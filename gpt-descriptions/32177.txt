Synopsis:
"A "Y" Girl in France: Letters of Katherine Shortall" is a captivating collection of letters written by Katherine Shortall during her time as a young volunteer in France during World War I. Through her heartfelt and vivid correspondence, Katherine provides a unique and intimate glimpse into the lives of soldiers, nurses, and civilians affected by the war. As she navigates the challenges and triumphs of her own experiences, Katherine's letters paint a vivid picture of the resilience, camaraderie, and sacrifices made during this tumultuous period in history.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Epistolary, War

Themes:
War and its impact, Personal growth and resilience, Human connection and compassion
