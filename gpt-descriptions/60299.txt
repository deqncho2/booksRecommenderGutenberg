Synopsis: In 'Team-Mates' by Ralph Henry Barbour, a group of college friends find themselves caught up in a thrilling adventure that tests their loyalty, courage, and teamwork. As they navigate through unexpected challenges and dangerous situations, their bond is put to the ultimate test. Will they be able to overcome their differences and work together to achieve their goals? This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of these extraordinary team-mates.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Friendship

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Teamwork
