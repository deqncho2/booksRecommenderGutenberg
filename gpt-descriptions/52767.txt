Synopsis: In "The Time of the End: or, the World, the Visible Church, and the People of God at the Advent of the Lord," Edward Hoare takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through biblical prophecies and their implications for the future. As the world faces uncertain times, Hoare explores the signs and events that may signal the approaching end of days. With a blend of historical analysis and spiritual insight, this book offers a captivating exploration of the mysteries surrounding the end times.

Genres: Christian literature, eschatology, prophecy

Themes: End times, biblical prophecy, faith, spiritual awakening
