Synopsis: In 'The Mintage: Being Ten Stories & One More' by Elbert Hubbard, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of eleven thought-provoking stories. Each tale explores the complexities of human nature, delving into themes of love, ambition, sacrifice, and the pursuit of happiness. From the struggles of a young artist to the unexpected consequences of a secret affair, these stories offer a glimpse into the depths of the human soul. With vivid storytelling and profound insights, Hubbard's work leaves readers pondering the intricacies of life and the choices we make.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Philosophy

Themes: Love, Ambition, Sacrifice, Happiness, Human Nature
