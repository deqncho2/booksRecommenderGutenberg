Synopsis: 
"The Wonders of the Invisible World: Being an Account of the Tryals of Several Witches Lately Executed in New-England, to which is added A Farther Account of the Tryals of the New-England Witches" is a gripping and chilling account of the infamous witch trials that took place in New England. Written by Cotton Mather and Increase Mather, this book delves into the dark and mysterious world of witchcraft, recounting the shocking trials and executions of those accused of practicing witchcraft. Through detailed descriptions and firsthand testimonies, the authors explore the events that unfolded during this dark period in history, leaving readers captivated by the eerie and unsettling atmosphere that surrounded the trials.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, True crime, Occult

Themes: Witchcraft, Hysteria, Justice, Religion
