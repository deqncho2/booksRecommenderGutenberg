Synopsis: In the seventh volume of "What Will He Do with It?" by Edward Bulwer Lytton, the story continues to follow the lives of the characters as they navigate through love, ambition, and the complexities of society. Secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and unexpected events unfold, leaving the reader eagerly turning the pages to discover the fate of the characters they have come to know and care for.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, drama

Themes: Love and relationships, social class and ambition, secrets and revelations
