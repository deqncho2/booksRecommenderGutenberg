Synopsis: 
In "The Heart of Rachael" by Kathleen Thompson Norris, a young woman named Rachael finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a complicated love triangle. As she navigates the challenges of her relationships, Rachael must confront her own fears and insecurities, ultimately discovering the true desires of her heart. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century America, this captivating novel explores the complexities of love, sacrifice, and self-discovery.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Duty and Responsibility, Self-Discovery
