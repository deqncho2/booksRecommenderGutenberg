Synopsis: In 'Memoirs of the Life and Labours of the Rev. Samuel Marsden, of Paramatta, Senior Chaplain of New South Wales; and of His Early Connexion with the Missions to New Zealand and Tahiti', Samuel Marsden takes us on a captivating journey through his remarkable life as a senior chaplain in New South Wales and his pivotal role in the missions to New Zealand and Tahiti. From his early struggles and triumphs to his encounters with indigenous cultures and his unwavering dedication to his faith, Marsden's memoirs offer a fascinating insight into the challenges and triumphs of a pioneering missionary.

Genres: Biography, History, Religious

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Colonialism, Cross-cultural encounters
