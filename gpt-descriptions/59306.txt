Synopsis: In "Homer: The Iliad; The Odyssey," William Lucas Collins presents a captivating retelling of two timeless epic poems. Delve into the world of ancient Greece as you follow the heroic journeys of Achilles and Odysseus. From the bloody battles of the Trojan War to the perilous adventures of Odysseus' long voyage home, this book brings to life the thrilling tales of love, war, and the human spirit. Prepare to be enthralled by the rich tapestry of mythology, heroism, and tragedy that unfolds within these pages.

Genres: Epic, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Loyalty, Love, Honor
