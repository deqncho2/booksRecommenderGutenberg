Synopsis: In "Predecessors of Cleopatra" by North, Leigh, a captivating historical mystery unfolds as a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon a hidden tomb that holds the secrets of Cleopatra's ancestors. As she delves deeper into the enigmatic past, she becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, ancient rituals, and forbidden love. With each discovery, the line between myth and reality blurs, leading her on a thrilling journey that challenges everything she thought she knew about the legendary queen.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Legacy and lineage, Power and politics, Love and sacrifice
