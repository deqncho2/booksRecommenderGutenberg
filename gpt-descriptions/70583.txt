Synopsis: In "Stopwatch on the World" by Daniel R. Gilgannon, a mysterious stopwatch falls into the hands of a young woman named Emily, changing her life forever. This stopwatch has the power to stop time, allowing Emily to explore the world in frozen moments and uncover hidden secrets. As she delves deeper into the stopwatch's origins, Emily discovers a dark conspiracy that threatens not only her own existence but the very fabric of time itself. With the help of a quirky group of allies, she must navigate through dangerous obstacles and make difficult choices to protect the world from falling into chaos.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Time manipulation, Identity, Power and responsibility
