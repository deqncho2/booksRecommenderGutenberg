Synopsis: 

In 'Ways of War and Peace' by Austrian author Delia, a young woman named Emma finds herself caught in the midst of a tumultuous war that threatens to tear her world apart. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of love, loss, and betrayal, Emma must make difficult choices that will shape not only her own destiny but also the fate of those she holds dear. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature and the enduring power of hope in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, war

Themes: Love and sacrifice, resilience, the impact of war on individuals and society
