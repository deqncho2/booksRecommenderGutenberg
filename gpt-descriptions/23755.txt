Synopsis: In 'Birds of the Indian Hills', Douglas Dewar takes us on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of the Indian hills, where he explores the fascinating world of birds. From the majestic Himalayas to the lush forests of Assam, Dewar's vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes bring to life the diverse avian species that inhabit these breathtaking regions. As he delves into the habits, behaviors, and unique characteristics of each bird, Dewar uncovers the hidden wonders of the Indian hills, offering a glimpse into the intricate tapestry of nature's beauty.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Travelogue

Themes: Biodiversity, Conservation, Natural History
