Synopsis: 
In 'Troubled Waters' by Raine, William MacLeod, a small coastal town is shaken by a mysterious disappearance that unravels a web of secrets and hidden motives. As the investigation unfolds, the protagonist finds themselves entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, the tension rises, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the truth behind the troubled waters.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Deception
