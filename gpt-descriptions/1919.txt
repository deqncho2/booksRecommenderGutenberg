Synopsis: In 'Grand'ther Baldwin's Thanksgiving, with Other Ballads and Poems' by Horatio Alger Jr., readers are transported to a small New England town where the humble and wise Grand'ther Baldwin resides. Through a collection of heartwarming ballads and poems, Alger weaves tales of love, family, and the power of gratitude. As the townspeople gather to celebrate Thanksgiving, Grand'ther Baldwin's stories touch the hearts of young and old, reminding them of the importance of cherishing the blessings in their lives. 

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Family

Themes: Gratitude, Love, Family, Tradition
