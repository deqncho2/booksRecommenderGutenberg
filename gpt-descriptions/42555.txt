Synopsis: 
'By the Barrow River, and Other Stories' by Edmund Leamy is a captivating collection of tales that transport readers to the enchanting landscapes of Ireland. From the mystical realms of fairies and leprechauns to the poignant stories of love and loss, this book weaves together a tapestry of emotions and experiences. Each story is a window into the rich folklore and traditions of Ireland, where ordinary lives are touched by extraordinary events. With its vivid imagery and compelling narratives, 'By the Barrow River, and Other Stories' invites readers on a journey through the heart and soul of Ireland.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Short Stories

Themes: 
Irish folklore, Love and loss, Magic and enchantment
