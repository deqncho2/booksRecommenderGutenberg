Synopsis: In 'Crimes of Charity' by Konrad Bercovici, a seemingly ordinary charity organization becomes entangled in a web of deceit, corruption, and unexpected twists. When a young journalist begins to investigate the organization's activities, she uncovers a dark underbelly of secrets and hidden agendas. As she delves deeper, she finds herself caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between good and evil becomes blurred. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the truth becomes more elusive. Will she be able to expose the crimes hidden behind the veil of charity, or will she become another victim of the very organization she sought to expose?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Deception, Morality
