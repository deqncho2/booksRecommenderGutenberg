Synopsis: In "Prose Idylls, New and Old," Charles Kingsley takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales that blend reality and imagination. From the picturesque landscapes of the English countryside to the mystical realms of ancient mythology, these idylls transport readers to a world where nature, love, and adventure intertwine. With each story, Kingsley weaves a tapestry of vivid imagery and profound insights, exploring the depths of human emotions and the timeless beauty of the natural world. Whether encountering mythical creatures or delving into the complexities of human relationships, these idylls offer a captivating exploration of the human spirit and the wonders that surround us.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Nature, Love, Adventure, Human emotions, Mythology
