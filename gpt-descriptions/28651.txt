Synopsis: 
'Our Bessie' by Rosa Nouchette Carey is a captivating novel that follows the life of Bessie Brooke, a young woman who finds herself torn between duty and desire. Set in Victorian England, Bessie navigates the complexities of love, family expectations, and societal norms, as she strives to find her own path in a world that seeks to confine her. With a cast of intriguing characters and unexpected twists, 'Our Bessie' is a tale of self-discovery, resilience, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: 
Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Love and Relationships, Gender Roles, Individuality, Social Expectations
