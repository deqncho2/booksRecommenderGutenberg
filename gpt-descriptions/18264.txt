Synopsis: 
Within The Enemy's Lines by Optic, Oliver is a thrilling adventure set during the American Civil War. The story follows a young Union soldier named Tom, who finds himself trapped behind enemy lines after a daring escape from a Confederate prison camp. As Tom navigates the treacherous landscape of the South, he must rely on his wits and courage to survive while evading capture. Along the way, he encounters unexpected allies and faces dangerous adversaries, all while desperately trying to make his way back to Union territory. With suspenseful twists and turns, Within The Enemy's Lines is a gripping tale of survival, loyalty, and the indomitable spirit of a young soldier.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Courage, Friendship
