Synopsis: 
'Home Life of Great Authors' takes readers on an intimate journey into the private lives of some of the most renowned literary figures in history. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Hattie Tyng Griswold offers a rare glimpse into the personal spaces, daily routines, and relationships of these literary giants. From the cozy study of Charles Dickens to the tranquil gardens of Jane Austen, this book unveils the hidden aspects of these authors' lives, shedding light on the inspirations and challenges that shaped their literary masterpieces.

Genres: Biography, Non-fiction, Literary Criticism

Themes: Creativity, Inspiration, Personal Relationships, Domesticity
