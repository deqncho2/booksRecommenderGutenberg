Synopsis: 

In "Life in the Shifting Dunes: A popular field guide to the natural history of Castle Neck, Ipswich, Massachusetts," Laurence B. White takes readers on an immersive journey through the captivating landscapes of Castle Neck. From the ever-changing dunes to the diverse wildlife that calls this place home, White's vivid descriptions and insightful observations bring the natural wonders of this coastal region to life. As readers delve into the pages of this field guide, they will uncover the hidden secrets of Castle Neck and gain a deeper understanding of the delicate balance between nature and human impact.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Field Guide

Themes: Environmental conservation, Biodiversity, Human-nature relationship
