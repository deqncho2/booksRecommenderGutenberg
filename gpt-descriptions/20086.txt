Synopsis: In 'Rome: A Sketch-Book' by Fred Richards, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting streets of Rome. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, the author invites readers to explore the city's hidden corners, ancient ruins, and bustling markets. From the grandeur of the Colosseum to the serenity of the Vatican, this sketchbook captures the essence of Rome's rich history and vibrant culture. As the pages turn, immerse yourself in the beauty and charm of this timeless city, discovering its secrets and experiencing its allure.

Genres: Travel, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, History, Culture
