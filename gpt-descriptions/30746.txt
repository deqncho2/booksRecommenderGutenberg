Synopsis: In 'The Last Straw' by William J. Smith, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious events that threaten to tear the community apart. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the residents must confront their darkest fears and confront the truth before it's too late. With a gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Small-town secrets, Community bonds, Facing fears
