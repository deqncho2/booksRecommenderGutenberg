Synopsis: In 'The Poisoned Pen' by Arthur B. Reeve, a renowned detective faces his most perplexing case yet when a mysterious poison pen begins targeting prominent figures in society. As the poison pen's letters become increasingly deadly, the detective must navigate a web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives to uncover the identity of the culprit before more lives are lost. With each twist and turn, the detective finds himself drawn deeper into a world of intrigue and danger, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Revenge, Power
