Synopsis: 
'Lost in the Backwoods: A Tale of the Canadian Forest' follows the gripping journey of a young woman named Mary as she finds herself stranded in the vast and treacherous Canadian wilderness. Separated from her family and with no knowledge of survival skills, Mary must navigate through the dense forest, facing numerous challenges and encountering unexpected allies and adversaries along the way. As she battles the harsh elements and her own fears, Mary's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will she find her way back to civilization or succumb to the unforgiving wilderness?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Survival

Themes: Resilience, Survival, Nature, Self-discovery
