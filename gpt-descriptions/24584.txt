Synopsis: In 'Man Overboard!' by Crawford, F. Marion, a wealthy and influential man mysteriously disappears from a luxurious ocean liner, leaving behind a trail of intrigue and suspicion. As the ship's passengers and crew scramble to uncover the truth, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the race to solve the mystery becomes a matter of life and death.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
