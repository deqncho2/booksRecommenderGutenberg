Synopsis: In 'The American Union Speaker' by John D. Philbrick, a captivating collection of speeches and orations from influential figures throughout American history is presented. From the stirring words of Abraham Lincoln to the impassioned speeches of Susan B. Anthony, this anthology takes readers on a journey through the power of rhetoric and its impact on shaping the nation. With each speech, the reader is transported to pivotal moments in American history, where the words spoken have the ability to inspire, unite, and ignite change. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Anthology

Themes: Power of words, Leadership, Social change
