Synopsis: In 'The Spoils of Poynton', a battle ensues over the ownership of a magnificent collection of furniture and art, as a strong-willed woman fights to protect her cherished possessions from falling into the wrong hands. As alliances shift and tensions rise, the story delves into the complexities of love, desire, and the power of material possessions.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Psychological

Themes: Materialism, Possession, Love and Desire, Power Dynamics
