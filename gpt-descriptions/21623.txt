Synopsis: In 'Usury: A Scriptural, Ethical and Economic View', Calvin Elliott delves into the controversial topic of usury, exploring its historical, religious, and economic implications. Through a thought-provoking analysis, he challenges conventional notions and offers a fresh perspective on this age-old practice. As he uncovers the intricate web of morality, finance, and human nature, readers will be captivated by the profound questions raised and the potential impact on society.

Genres: Non-fiction, Economics, Religious Studies

Themes: Usury, Morality, Religion, Economics, Ethics
