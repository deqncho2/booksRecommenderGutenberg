Synopsis: 

'Black Beauty' by Anna Sewell is a captivating and heartwarming tale that follows the life of a beautiful black horse named Black Beauty. From his early days as a carefree foal, to his experiences as a working horse in the bustling streets of London, and his encounters with various owners, Black Beauty's story is one of resilience, friendship, and the enduring bond between humans and animals. Through his eyes, we witness the joys and hardships of horse life, as well as the cruelty and compassion of those who interact with him. 

Genres: Fiction, Animal Fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: Animal welfare, empathy, social justice, human-animal relationships
