Synopsis: In "Captains of Industry; or, Men of Business Who Did Something Besides Making Money," James Parton takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of remarkable individuals who achieved great success in business while also making significant contributions to society. From the innovative minds behind the creation of the telegraph to the visionary leaders who revolutionized the steel industry, Parton unveils the untold stories of these extraordinary men and their lasting impact on the world. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the intersection between business acumen and social responsibility.

Genres: Biography, History, Business

Themes: Entrepreneurship, Social Responsibility, Innovation
