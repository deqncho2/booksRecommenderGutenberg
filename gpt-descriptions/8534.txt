Synopsis: In 'Daily Strength for Daily Needs', Mary Wilder Tileston presents a collection of inspiring daily readings that offer solace, guidance, and encouragement for navigating life's challenges. Each passage is carefully selected to provide a source of strength and inspiration, making this book a valuable companion for anyone seeking daily spiritual nourishment.

Genres: Devotional, Inspirational, Self-help

Themes: Faith, Resilience, Hope, Inner Strength
