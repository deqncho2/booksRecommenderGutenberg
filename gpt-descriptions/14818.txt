Synopsis: 
In 'The Daughter of Anderson Crow' by George Barr McCutcheon, the small town of Edgewood is turned upside down when a mysterious young woman arrives claiming to be the long-lost daughter of the beloved local constable, Anderson Crow. As the townspeople grapple with the truth behind her identity, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true meaning of family is explored. With a delightful blend of humor, intrigue, and heartwarming moments, this captivating tale takes readers on a journey of discovery and redemption in a charming small-town setting.

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Family Drama

Themes: Identity, Family, Community
