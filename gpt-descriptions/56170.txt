Synopsis: In 'The Surprise Book' by Beard and Patten, a mysterious package arrives on the doorstep of a young woman named Lily, containing a book with no title or author. As Lily delves into the pages, she becomes engrossed in a captivating story that seems to mirror her own life in unexpected ways. With each turn of the page, the lines between reality and fiction blur, leading Lily on a journey of self-discovery and revelation. As she unravels the secrets within the book, she must confront her past, make difficult choices, and ultimately find her own voice. 

Genres: Mystery, Fiction, Contemporary

Themes: Identity, Self-discovery, Fate, Literature
