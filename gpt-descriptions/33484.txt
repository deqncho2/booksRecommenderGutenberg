Synopsis: In the heart of ancient Rome, a young woman named Valeria finds herself caught in a web of danger and intrigue as she navigates the treacherous world of early Christianity. As she becomes entangled in a secret society of believers, Valeria must make difficult choices that could cost her everything she holds dear. With the catacombs as their refuge, Valeria and her fellow Christians face persecution, betrayal, and the constant threat of discovery. Will their faith be strong enough to withstand the trials that lie ahead?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Christian Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Faith and Persecution, Sacrifice and Redemption, Love and Loyalty
