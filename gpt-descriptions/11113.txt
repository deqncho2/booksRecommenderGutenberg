Synopsis: 
In 'Principal Cairns' by John Cairns, a small town is shaken by a series of mysterious events that lead to the sudden disappearance of the beloved principal of the local high school. As the community grapples with the unsettling absence, a determined group of students embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind their principal's vanishing act. With each clue they uncover, they find themselves drawn deeper into a web of secrets and unexpected revelations, ultimately leading them to a shocking discovery that will forever change their lives. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and deception, Friendship and loyalty, Coming of age
