Synopsis: In 'Harper's Electricity Book for Boys', Joseph H. Adams takes young readers on an electrifying journey through the fascinating world of electricity. Through engaging stories, experiments, and illustrations, this book introduces boys to the wonders of electricity, from its discovery to its practical applications. From building simple circuits to understanding the power of lightning, this book sparks curiosity and encourages hands-on exploration. With its captivating narrative and interactive approach, 'Harper's Electricity Book for Boys' is sure to ignite a lifelong interest in science and technology.

Genres: Educational, Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Electricity, Science education, Hands-on learning
