Synopsis: In "The Story of the American Legion: The Birth of the Legion," George Seay Wheat takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation of one of America's most influential organizations. From the aftermath of World War I to the struggles faced by returning veterans, this book delves into the challenges and triumphs that led to the birth of the American Legion. Through personal accounts, historical events, and the dedication of passionate individuals, the story unfolds, revealing the profound impact the Legion has had on American society. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Service, Post-war challenges, Advocacy
