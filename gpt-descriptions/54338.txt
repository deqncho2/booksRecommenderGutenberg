Synopsis: In 'Q-Ships and Their Story', E. Keble Chatterton takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of naval warfare during World War I. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Chatterton unveils the secret operations of Q-ships, seemingly innocent merchant vessels that were actually armed to the teeth and tasked with luring and destroying German U-boats. As the tension rises and the stakes become higher, readers will be captivated by the bravery and ingenuity of the sailors who risked their lives to protect their comrades and secure victory at sea.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Adventure

Themes: Naval warfare, Deception, Heroism, Sacrifice
