Synopsis: In 'Ancient Apostles', David O. McKay takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the early apostles of Jesus Christ. From their humble beginnings to their extraordinary experiences, McKay delves into the untold stories and profound teachings of these remarkable individuals. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the courage, faith, and unwavering dedication of these ancient apostles, leaving them inspired and eager to learn more about the foundations of Christianity.

Genres: Historical, Religious, Biography

Themes: Faith, Leadership, Sacrifice, Divine Calling
