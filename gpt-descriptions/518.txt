Synopsis: In 'The Enchanted Island of Yew: Whereon Prince Marvel Encountered the High Ki of Twi and Other Surprising People', follow the extraordinary adventures of Prince Marvel as he embarks on a journey to the mysterious Island of Yew. On this enchanted island, he encounters a host of fascinating characters, including the High Ki of Twi, who possesses incredible powers. As Prince Marvel navigates through this magical realm, he must face unexpected challenges and uncover the secrets that lie within the island's enchantment.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Magic and Enchantment, Heroism and Courage, Discovery and Exploration
