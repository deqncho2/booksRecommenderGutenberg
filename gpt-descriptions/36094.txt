Synopsis: 
'Our Boys, and Other Poems' by Alan L. Strang is a captivating collection of poems that delves into the complexities of human emotions and experiences. From heartfelt reflections on love and loss to thought-provoking explorations of societal issues, Strang's words paint vivid pictures and evoke powerful emotions. Each poem offers a unique perspective, inviting readers to contemplate the beauty and struggles of life through the lens of poetic expression.

Genres: Poetry, Contemporary Literature

Themes: Love, Loss, Society, Reflection, Emotion
