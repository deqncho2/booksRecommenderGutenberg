Synopsis: 
In 'Birthright' by April Smith, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her mysterious past. As she delves deeper into her family history, she uncovers a web of secrets and lies that threaten to unravel everything she thought she knew. With each revelation, Emily finds herself questioning her own identity and struggling to reconcile her present with her past. As she races against time to uncover the truth, she must confront the dark forces that will stop at nothing to keep her from discovering her birthright.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Family Secrets, Betrayal
