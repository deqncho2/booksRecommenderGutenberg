Synopsis: In "The Black Wolf's Breed," set in the reign of Louis XIV, readers are transported to both the Old World and the New as they follow the captivating story of a mysterious black wolf and the people whose lives it touches. From the grandeur of the French court to the untamed wilderness of the American colonies, this tale weaves together adventure, romance, and political intrigue. As secrets are revealed and loyalties are tested, the black wolf becomes a symbol of both danger and salvation, leading the characters on a thrilling journey that spans continents and cultures.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity, Power
