Synopsis: In "Old New England Traits," the reader is transported to the quaint and mysterious world of 19th-century New England. Through a collection of captivating stories, the author delves into the lives of the region's inhabitants, exploring their unique customs, superstitions, and eccentricities. From the enigmatic figures who roam the forests to the peculiar traditions that shape their daily lives, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a bygone era. With each tale, the reader is drawn deeper into the intricate web of New England's history, culture, and folklore, leaving them yearning for more.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Short Stories, Folklore

Themes: New England culture, superstition, eccentricity, tradition
