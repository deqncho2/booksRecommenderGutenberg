Synopsis: 

Join Bishop Nathaniel H. Bishop on an extraordinary adventure as he embarks on a daring journey of 2500 miles, from Quebec to the Gulf of Mexico, in a paper canoe. Set in the late 19th century, this captivating travelogue takes readers through breathtaking landscapes, treacherous waters, and encounters with fascinating characters along the way. As Bishop navigates the mighty rivers and explores the diverse cultures of North America, he unveils the hidden beauty and challenges of the continent, providing a unique perspective on the world around us.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Cultural Diversity
