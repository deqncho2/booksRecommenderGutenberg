Synopsis: In 'Infinity's Child' by Charles V. De Vet, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Richard Manning invents a groundbreaking device that has the power to alter the course of humanity. However, when his creation falls into the wrong hands, it becomes a dangerous weapon that could bring about catastrophic consequences. As Dr. Manning races against time to reclaim his invention, he finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue, betrayal, and a battle for the future of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Technology and its impact on society, Ethics and responsibility, Power and control
