Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's Setback; Or, True Pluck Welcomes Defeat' by Standish, Burt L., the indomitable Frank Merriwell faces a formidable challenge that threatens to shatter his confidence and dreams. As he navigates through unexpected setbacks and personal trials, Frank must summon his true pluck to overcome defeat and emerge stronger than ever. This thrilling tale takes readers on a rollercoaster of emotions, as they witness Frank's resilience and determination in the face of adversity.

Genres: Adventure, Sports, Coming-of-age

Themes: Perseverance, Resilience, Sportsmanship
