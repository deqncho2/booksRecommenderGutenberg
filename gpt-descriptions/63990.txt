Synopsis: In 'Palimpsest' by Roger D. Aycock, a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the key to a long-lost civilization. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and danger. With each discovery, the boundaries between past and present blur, leading him on a thrilling journey that challenges his beliefs and forces him to confront his own demons. As he races against time to unlock the secrets of the palimpsest, he must navigate treacherous alliances and face the consequences of his own actions. This gripping tale of adventure and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Archaeology, Secrets and Betrayal, Redemption
