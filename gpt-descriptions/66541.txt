Synopsis: 
'Facts and fancies for the curious from the harvest-fields of literature: A melange of excerpta' is a captivating collection of intriguing and thought-provoking snippets from various literary works. From ancient myths to modern novels, this book takes readers on a journey through time and across genres, offering glimpses into the fascinating worlds created by renowned authors. Each excerpt is carefully selected to pique the reader's curiosity, leaving them eager to explore the full stories behind these captivating snippets. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Anthology, Literary Criticism

Themes: Curiosity, Literature, Storytelling
