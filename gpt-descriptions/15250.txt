Synopsis:
In 'Myths and Legends of China', E. T. C. Werner takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Chinese mythology and folklore. From the creation of the world to the adventures of legendary heroes and the battles between gods and demons, this book unveils a world of enchantment and wonder. Discover the origins of the Chinese zodiac, the epic tales of the Monkey King, and the mystical powers of dragons. With vivid storytelling and intricate details, Werner brings to life the captivating myths and legends that have shaped Chinese culture for centuries.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Cultural Studies

Themes: Creation, Heroism, Supernatural Beings, Fate, Morality
