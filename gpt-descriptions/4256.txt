Synopsis: In 'Stammering, Its Cause and Cure' by Benjamin Nathaniel Bogue, embark on a captivating exploration of the enigmatic world of stammering. Delve into the depths of this speech disorder as Bogue unravels its mysterious origins and unveils a groundbreaking approach to its cure. With a blend of scientific research, personal anecdotes, and practical advice, this book offers a fresh perspective on stammering that challenges conventional beliefs. Prepare to be enlightened and inspired as you journey through the pages of this thought-provoking work.

Genres: Non-fiction, Psychology, Self-help

Themes: Speech disorders, Communication, Personal growth
