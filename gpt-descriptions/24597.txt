Synopsis: In 'Games for All Occasions' by Mary E. Blain, a seemingly ordinary family gathering takes an unexpected turn when a game of charades reveals long-held secrets and hidden desires. As the evening progresses, tensions rise and the line between reality and play becomes blurred, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the true nature of the games we play. 

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Family dynamics, Deception, Identity, Power dynamics
