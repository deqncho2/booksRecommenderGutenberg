Synopsis: In 'Each Man Kills' by Glad, Victoria, a small town is shaken by a series of mysterious deaths that seem to be connected. As the body count rises, a determined detective must unravel the dark secrets hidden within the community, while facing his own personal demons. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking truth is revealed.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Psychological suspense
