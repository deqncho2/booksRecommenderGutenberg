Synopsis: In 'Atomic!' by Kuttner, Henry, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the world forever. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of atomic energy, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of espionage, betrayal, and international intrigue. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous landscape where loyalties are tested and the line between good and evil becomes blurred. Will he be able to harness the power of the atom for the greater good, or will it fall into the wrong hands?

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Scientific discovery, Morality, Power and its consequences
