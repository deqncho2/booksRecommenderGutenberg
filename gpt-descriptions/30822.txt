Synopsis: In 'Campaign of Battery D, First Rhode Island Light Artillery', author Ezra Knight Parker takes readers on a gripping journey through the American Civil War. Through the eyes of Battery D, a group of courageous soldiers from Rhode Island, the story unfolds with vivid descriptions of battles, camaraderie, and the harsh realities of war. As they face the horrors of combat and the challenges of leadership, the soldiers of Battery D must navigate their own personal struggles and sacrifices. This captivating narrative offers a unique perspective on the Civil War, highlighting the bravery and resilience of those who fought on the front lines.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Military History

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Leadership, The Human Experience in War
