Synopsis: In "Lady Hollyhock and Her Friends: A Book of Nature Dolls and Others," Margaret Coulson introduces readers to a delightful world of enchanting nature dolls and their whimsical adventures. Join Lady Hollyhock and her friends as they embark on a journey filled with magic, friendship, and unexpected surprises. From the charming Flower Fairies to the mischievous Woodland Elves, this book is a celebration of the beauty and wonder of nature. With captivating illustrations and heartwarming stories, readers of all ages will be captivated by the enchanting world of Lady Hollyhock and her friends.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Picture Book

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Nature, Magic
