Synopsis: 

In 'Diary of Battery A, First Regiment Rhode Island Light Artillery', Theodore Reichardt takes us on a captivating journey through the American Civil War. Through the eyes of a young soldier, we witness the harsh realities of battle, the camaraderie among comrades, and the personal sacrifices made in the name of duty. From the chaos of the battlefield to the quiet moments of reflection, this diary offers a unique and intimate perspective on one of the most tumultuous periods in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, War

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, Brotherhood and camaraderie, Sacrifice and duty
