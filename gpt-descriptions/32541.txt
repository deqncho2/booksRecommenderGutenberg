Synopsis: In 'One Way' by Miriam Allen De Ford, a group of scientists embarks on a dangerous mission to a distant planet, but they soon discover that they are not alone. As they face unexpected challenges and encounter mysterious beings, the team must navigate a treacherous landscape and confront their own fears. With each step, the line between reality and illusion becomes blurred, leading to a thrilling and thought-provoking journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Identity, Perception
