Synopsis: 
In 'Through Spain to the Sahara', Matilda Betham-Edwards takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant landscapes of Spain and the mysterious Sahara desert. Through her vivid descriptions and encounters with fascinating characters, she unveils the hidden beauty and cultural richness of these enchanting regions. From the bustling streets of Madrid to the vast expanse of the Sahara, this travelogue offers a captivating exploration of the diverse landscapes, traditions, and people that make up these extraordinary places.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Natural beauty
