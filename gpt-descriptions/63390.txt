Synopsis: In 'Moslem and Frank;: or, Charles Martel and the rescue of Europe from the threatened yoke of the Saracens', Strauss takes us on a thrilling journey through the clash of civilizations during the 8th century. As the Saracens threaten to conquer Europe, the fate of the continent rests on the shoulders of two unlikely heroes: Moslem, a young Muslim warrior, and Frank, a courageous Frankish knight. Bound by a shared destiny, they must navigate treacherous alliances, face their own inner conflicts, and ultimately decide where their loyalties lie. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Strauss paints a vivid picture of a pivotal moment in history, where the fate of Europe hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Clash of civilizations, Loyalty, Identity, Courage
