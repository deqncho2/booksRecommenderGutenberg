Synopsis: In 'Stamped Caution' by Raymond Z. Gallun, a renowned scientist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of an ancient civilization. As he delves deeper into its origins, he becomes entangled in a dangerous race against time, facing treacherous adversaries and uncovering mind-bending truths that could reshape the course of humanity. This thrilling science fiction adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of knowledge and the consequences of unearthing forbidden knowledge.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of ancient civilizations, Forbidden knowledge, Consequences of discovery
