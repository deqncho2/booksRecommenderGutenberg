Synopsis: In "Anne Severn and the Fieldings" by May Sinclair, a young woman named Anne finds herself caught between two very different families, the Fieldings and the Severns. As she navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations, Anne must make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century England, this captivating novel explores the intricacies of family dynamics and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family, Love, Identity, Social expectations
