Synopsis: In 'Death to the Inquisitive! A story of sinful love' by Sheldon, Lurana, a mysterious and forbidden love affair unfolds in the heart of a small, conservative town. As secrets are unveiled and passions ignite, the consequences of their actions threaten to tear apart the lives of those involved. With a web of deceit and desire, this captivating tale explores the depths of human nature and the lengths people will go to protect their darkest secrets.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Betrayal, Secrets, Consequences
