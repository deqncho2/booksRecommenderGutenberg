Synopsis: In "Henry Ford's Own Story: How a Farmer Boy Rose to the Power that goes with Many Millions, Yet Never Lost Touch with Humanity," Rose Wilder Lane delves into the extraordinary life of Henry Ford, the man behind the iconic Ford Motor Company. From his humble beginnings as a farmer boy to his meteoric rise to immense wealth and power, Lane explores the fascinating journey of a man who revolutionized the automobile industry and forever changed the world. Through personal anecdotes and interviews, Lane paints a vivid portrait of Ford's relentless ambition, innovative spirit, and unwavering commitment to his vision. This captivating biography offers a glimpse into the mind of a genius and the impact he had on society, while also shedding light on the complexities of Ford's character and his enduring connection to humanity.

Genres: Biography, History, Business

Themes: Ambition, Innovation, Success, Social Impact
