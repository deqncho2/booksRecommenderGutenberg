Synopsis: In 'The Good Crow's Happy Shop', a mysterious shop appears in a small town, offering its customers the chance to purchase happiness. As the townspeople flock to the shop, they soon discover that happiness comes at a price they never expected. With secrets lurking behind the shop's doors, a young girl named Lily must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: The pursuit of happiness, the consequences of greed, the power of secrets
