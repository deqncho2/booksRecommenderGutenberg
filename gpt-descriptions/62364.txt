Synopsis: In 'The Origin of Tyranny', Ure, P. N. takes readers on a captivating journey through the depths of power and corruption. Set in a dystopian world, the story follows a young protagonist who uncovers a hidden truth that threatens to unravel the very fabric of society. As secrets are unveiled and alliances are tested, the reader is drawn into a suspenseful narrative that explores the dark origins of tyranny and the consequences of unchecked authority. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking revelations, this gripping tale will leave readers questioning the nature of power and the lengths some will go to maintain it.

Genres: Dystopian fiction, Political thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power and corruption, Rebellion, Truth and deception
