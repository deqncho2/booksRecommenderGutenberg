Synopsis: In 'Twilight Stories' by Catharine Shaw, prepare to be captivated by a collection of haunting and mysterious tales that will leave you questioning the boundaries between reality and the supernatural. From a small town plagued by unexplained disappearances to a forbidden love affair that defies time itself, each story in this anthology will transport you to a world where darkness and light collide, and where the line between dreams and nightmares becomes blurred. As you delve into the depths of these twilight stories, be prepared to confront your deepest fears and embrace the unknown.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, mystery

Themes: Love and loss, the power of the imagination, the thin veil between reality and the supernatural
