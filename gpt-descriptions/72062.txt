Synopsis:
'Electricity' by W.H. McCormick is a gripping and thought-provoking novel that follows the life of a young woman named Lily, who possesses a unique and mysterious ability. After a tragic accident leaves her orphaned and alone, Lily discovers that she has the power to control electricity. As she navigates through a world that fears and misunderstands her, Lily must learn to harness her extraordinary gift while facing dangerous adversaries and uncovering the truth about her past. With electrifying twists and turns, this captivating story explores the limits of human potential and the consequences of wielding immense power.

Genres:
Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes:
Identity, Power, Acceptance, Redemption
