Synopsis: 

In 'Twenty-Five Cent Dinners for Families of Six' by Juliet Corson, readers are taken on a captivating journey into the world of frugal cooking and resourcefulness. Set in a time when every penny counted, this book offers a unique perspective on how to provide nourishing meals for large families on a tight budget. Through a combination of practical tips, creative recipes, and insightful anecdotes, Corson presents a compelling argument for the power of ingenuity and the importance of making the most out of limited resources. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Cookbook, Historical

Themes: Frugality, Resourcefulness, Family, Cooking on a Budget
