Synopsis: In 'A Georgian Pageant' by Frank Frankfort Moore, readers are transported to the vibrant and captivating world of 18th-century Georgia. The story follows a diverse cast of characters, from aristocrats to commoners, as they navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. Amidst the backdrop of lavish balls, political intrigue, and scandalous secrets, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of passion and betrayal. As their lives intertwine, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns, where the pursuit of happiness and the pursuit of power collide in a dazzling display of Georgian society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Class and Ambition, Betrayal and Deception
