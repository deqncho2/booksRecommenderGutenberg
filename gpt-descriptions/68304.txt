Synopsis: In 'The Catspaw' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist named Tom is faced with a dangerous dilemma when he discovers a mysterious device that grants him the power to control minds. As he grapples with the ethical implications of this newfound ability, Tom becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and betrayal, where even the closest allies may have hidden agendas. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, Tom must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and decide how to wield his extraordinary power.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Betrayal
