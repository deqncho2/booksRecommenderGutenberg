Synopsis: In 'Getting Married' by Bernard Shaw, a group of unconventional characters gather for a wedding, but instead of celebrating love, they engage in a thought-provoking exploration of marriage, gender roles, and societal expectations. As the lively discussions unfold, the characters challenge traditional notions of matrimony, leading to unexpected revelations and a reevaluation of their own relationships. With Shaw's signature wit and sharp social commentary, 'Getting Married' offers a captivating and humorous examination of the institution of marriage and its impact on individuals and society.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Satire

Themes: Marriage, Gender roles, Society, Individualism, Tradition
