Synopsis: In "The Boy's King Arthur: Sir Thomas Malory's History of King Arthur and His Knights of the Round Table," Sir Thomas Malory brings to life the legendary tales of King Arthur and his noble knights. This captivating retelling follows the young Arthur as he discovers his destiny and embarks on a quest to unite the kingdom of Camelot. From the enchanting Lady of the Lake to the valiant Sir Lancelot, readers will be enthralled by the adventures, chivalry, and romance that unfold within the pages of this timeless classic.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Destiny, Loyalty
