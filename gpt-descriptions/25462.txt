Synopsis: In 'Little Folks' Handy Book', authors Adelia B. Beard and Lina Beard have created a delightful and practical guide for young readers, filled with a wide range of activities and projects to keep children entertained and engaged. From building a treehouse to making homemade toys and games, this book is a treasure trove of fun and educational ideas that will captivate the imagination of any child. With clear instructions and charming illustrations, 'Little Folks' Handy Book' is the perfect companion for young adventurers and creative minds.

Genres: Children's literature, Crafts and Hobbies, Activity Book

Themes: Creativity, Exploration, Self-sufficiency
