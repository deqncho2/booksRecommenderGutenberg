Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 136, April 17, 1915: The Man They Held Back', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving a man who possesses a mysterious power that others are desperate to control. As Carter delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of deceit, danger, and hidden agendas, leading him to question who can be trusted and who is manipulating the truth. With time running out, Carter must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and bring justice to those who deserve it.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Power and control, Deception and manipulation
