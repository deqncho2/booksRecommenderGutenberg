Synopsis: 
'Anna Christie' by Eugene O'Neill is a gripping drama that follows the life of a young woman named Anna, who is reunited with her estranged father after many years. As secrets from their past are revealed, Anna must confront her own demons and make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of the sea, this powerful play explores themes of redemption, forgiveness, and the search for identity.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Redemption, Family, Identity, Forgiveness
