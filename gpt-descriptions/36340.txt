Synopsis: 

'From Chart House to Bush Hut: Being the Record of a Sailor's 7 Years in the Queensland Bush' takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of Queensland, Australia. The story follows the adventures of a sailor who, after leaving his life at sea, finds himself immersed in the rugged and unpredictable world of the bush. From encounters with dangerous wildlife to forming unexpected friendships with the locals, the sailor's experiences shed light on the challenges and triumphs of adapting to a completely different way of life. As he navigates the harsh realities of survival, the sailor's resilience and determination are put to the test, leading to unexpected discoveries and personal growth.

Genres: Adventure, Memoir, Travel

Themes: Survival, Adaptation, Exploration
