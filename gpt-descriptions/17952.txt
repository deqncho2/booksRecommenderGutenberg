Synopsis: In 'Great Possessions' by Wilfrid Ward, Mrs., a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and intrigue when she inherits a mysterious estate from a distant relative. As she delves deeper into the history of the property, Emily uncovers a hidden world of forbidden love, family secrets, and long-buried scandals. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and must navigate the treacherous waters of her newfound inheritance. Will Emily be able to uncover the truth before it consumes her?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Betrayal, Love, Identity
