Synopsis: 
'Poems Every Child Should Know: The What-Every-Child-Should-Know-Library' is a delightful collection of timeless poems that will captivate both children and adults alike. From classic works by renowned poets to lesser-known gems, this anthology takes readers on a journey through the power of words and the beauty of language. With themes ranging from nature and imagination to love and friendship, these poems offer a glimpse into the rich tapestry of human emotions and experiences. Whether read aloud or savored in solitude, this collection is sure to inspire and ignite a lifelong love for poetry in readers of all ages.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature, Anthology
Themes: Nature, Imagination, Love, Friendship
