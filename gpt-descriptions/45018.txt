Synopsis:
In the second volume of "The works of Francis Maitland Balfour," titled "A treatise on comparative embryology: Invertebrata," Francis M. Balfour delves into the fascinating world of invertebrate embryology. Through meticulous research and detailed analysis, Balfour explores the development and evolution of various invertebrate species, shedding light on the intricate processes that shape their embryonic growth. From the early stages of fertilization to the formation of complex structures, Balfour's work uncovers the remarkable similarities and differences among different invertebrate groups. This volume serves as a comprehensive guide to understanding the diverse and captivating world of invertebrate embryology.

Genres: Science, Biology, Comparative Embryology

Themes: Evolution, Development, Invertebrate Biology
