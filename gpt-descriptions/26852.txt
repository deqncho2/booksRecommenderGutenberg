Synopsis: In 'The Blue Germ' by Maurice Nicoll, a mysterious and deadly epidemic sweeps through a small town, leaving its inhabitants in a state of fear and confusion. As the town's residents struggle to understand the origins and nature of the disease, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and desires. Amidst the chaos, a group of unlikely allies forms, determined to uncover the truth and find a way to stop the spread of the blue germ. With each passing day, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. Will they be able to unravel the enigma before it consumes them all?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Fear, Identity, Morality, Human Nature
