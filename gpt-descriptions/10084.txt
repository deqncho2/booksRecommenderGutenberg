Synopsis: In the remote wilderness of the Canadian North, a young man named Kazan finds himself caught between the call of his wild instincts and the bonds of human companionship. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters both danger and love, Kazan's true nature is tested, leading to a gripping tale of survival and self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Wilderness Fiction, Animal Fiction

Themes: Man vs. Nature, Loyalty, Identity, Redemption
