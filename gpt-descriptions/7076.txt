Synopsis: In "Prisoner for Blasphemy," a renowned journalist finds himself on trial for a crime that challenges the very foundations of society. As he defends his right to free thought and expression, he becomes a symbol of resistance against the oppressive forces of religious dogma. With his freedom hanging in the balance, he must confront the power of faith and the limits of tolerance in a gripping battle of ideas.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Political

Themes: Freedom of speech, Religious intolerance, Intellectual rebellion
