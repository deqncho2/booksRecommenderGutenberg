Synopsis: In 'Camp-fire and Wigwam' by Edward Sylvester Ellis, a group of adventurous young boys embark on a thrilling journey into the untamed wilderness of North America. As they navigate through dense forests, encounter dangerous wildlife, and face unexpected challenges, they learn valuable lessons about survival, friendship, and the power of the human spirit. This captivating tale of exploration and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to see what lies ahead for these brave young adventurers.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Exploration, Self-discovery
