Synopsis:
'Ave Roma Immortalis, Vol. 1: Studies from the Chronicles of Rome' takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient city of Rome. Filled with vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, this collection of stories immerses readers in the grandeur and intrigue of one of the greatest civilizations in history. From the rise and fall of emperors to the daily lives of ordinary citizens, the author weaves together a tapestry of tales that bring ancient Rome to life. Whether exploring the bustling streets of the city or delving into the political machinations of the ruling elite, each story offers a glimpse into a different aspect of Roman life, revealing the triumphs, tragedies, and enduring legacy of this remarkable civilization.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Anthology, Historical Non-Fiction

Themes: Ancient Rome, Power and Politics, Daily Life, Historical Events
