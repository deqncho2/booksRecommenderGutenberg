Synopsis: In 'My Literary Zoo' by Kate Sanborn, readers are transported into a whimsical world where characters from classic literature come to life. As the protagonist navigates this extraordinary zoo, they encounter beloved characters such as Alice from Wonderland, Sherlock Holmes, and even Dracula. With each encounter, the protagonist learns valuable lessons about life, love, and the power of storytelling. This enchanting tale is a delightful blend of fantasy, adventure, and literary homage that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Literary Fiction

Themes: Imagination, Literature, Life Lessons
