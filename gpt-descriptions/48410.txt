Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts of the Naval Reserve' by Robert Shaler, a group of young boys find themselves embarking on an unexpected adventure when they join the Naval Reserve. As they navigate the challenges of training and teamwork, they uncover a hidden secret that puts their skills and loyalty to the test. With danger lurking around every corner, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness and bravery to protect their country and each other.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Patriotism, Friendship, Courage
