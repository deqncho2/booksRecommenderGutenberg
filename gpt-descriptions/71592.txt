Synopsis: In 'Survival Factor' by Charles V. De Vet, a group of astronauts find themselves stranded on an alien planet after their spaceship crash-lands. As they struggle to survive in this hostile environment, they encounter bizarre creatures, face unexpected challenges, and must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to find a way back home. With each passing day, the line between friend and foe becomes blurred, and the true nature of survival is put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Teamwork, Identity
