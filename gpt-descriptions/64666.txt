Synopsis: 

'Patents and How to Get One: A Practical Handbook' is a comprehensive guide that unveils the secrets behind obtaining a patent, written by the United States Department of Commerce. This engaging handbook takes readers on a journey through the intricate world of patents, revealing the step-by-step process of securing intellectual property rights. From understanding the importance of innovation to navigating the legal complexities, this book equips aspiring inventors and entrepreneurs with the knowledge and tools necessary to protect their groundbreaking ideas. Filled with practical advice, real-life examples, and expert insights, 'Patents and How to Get One' is an indispensable resource for anyone seeking to turn their inventive dreams into reality.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Business

Themes: Intellectual property, Innovation, Entrepreneurship
