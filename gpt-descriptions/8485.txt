Synopsis: 

In "Books Fatal to Their Authors" by P. H. Ditchfield, embark on a captivating journey through literary history as the author explores the intriguing and often tragic fates of famous writers. From mysterious deaths to scandalous controversies, this book unveils the dark side of the literary world, revealing the untold stories behind the demise of renowned authors. Delve into the lives of these literary figures and discover the unexpected dangers that lurk within the pages of their own creations.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, Biography

Themes: Literary history, Tragedy, Controversy, Creativity, Mortality
