Synopsis: 
In 'Her Dark Inheritance' by Collins, E. Burke, Mrs., a young woman named Amelia discovers a hidden family secret that leads her on a thrilling journey through the dark corners of her family's history. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Amelia uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and supernatural forces that threaten to consume her. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and must confront her own inner demons to survive. Will Amelia be able to unravel the truth and break free from her dark inheritance, or will she succumb to the darkness that lurks within her bloodline?

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity, Supernatural forces
