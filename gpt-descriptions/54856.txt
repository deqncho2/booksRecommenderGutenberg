Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The New Abelard: A Romance', the captivating story of the forbidden love between Abelard and Heloise continues to unfold. As their relationship faces numerous challenges and obstacles, their passion and devotion are put to the ultimate test. Amidst the backdrop of medieval France, this tale of love, sacrifice, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the fate of these star-crossed lovers.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Forbidden Love, Sacrifice, Redemption
