Synopsis: 
'Box and Cox: A Romance of Real Life in One Act' is a delightful and humorous play that revolves around the lives of two men, Box and Cox, who unknowingly share the same apartment. As fate would have it, their paths never cross, until one day when circumstances force them to confront each other. What unfolds is a series of unexpected events, mistaken identities, and hilarious misunderstandings that will keep the audience on the edge of their seats. With witty dialogue and clever plot twists, 'Box and Cox' is a charming tale of coincidence and the unpredictable nature of life.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Mistaken identity, Coincidence, Humor
