Synopsis: In 'The Angel of the Gila: A Tale of Arizona' by Marsland, Cora, a young woman named Isabel finds herself caught in a web of danger and intrigue as she embarks on a journey through the rugged landscapes of Arizona. Drawn into a mysterious world of outlaws, hidden treasures, and forbidden love, Isabel must navigate the treacherous paths of the Wild West while uncovering the secrets of her own past. As she encounters a cast of captivating characters and faces numerous challenges, Isabel's courage and resilience are put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to unravel the truth and find her place in this untamed land?

Genres: Western, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Survival
