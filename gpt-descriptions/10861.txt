Synopsis: 

'Paris under the Commune: The Seventy-Three Days of the Second Siege; with Numerous Illustrations, Sketches Taken on the Spot, and Portraits' by John Leighton takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of the Paris Commune. Through vivid illustrations, sketches, and portraits, Leighton brings to life the harrowing experiences of the people of Paris during the Second Siege, providing a unique and immersive perspective on this historical event. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War, Art

Themes: Revolution, Resilience, Humanitarianism
