Synopsis: 

In "Historical Epochs of the French Revolution: With The Judgment And Execution Of Louis XVI., King Of France; And A List Of The Members Of The National Convention, Who Voted For And Against His Death," author Henry Goudemetz takes readers on a captivating journey through one of the most tumultuous periods in French history. This meticulously researched and gripping account delves into the events leading up to the judgment and execution of Louis XVI, the King of France, and provides a comprehensive list of the members of the National Convention who voted for and against his death. Goudemetz's narrative skillfully weaves together political intrigue, personal motivations, and the weight of historical consequences, leaving readers eager to uncover the dramatic twists and turns that shaped this pivotal moment in the French Revolution.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Revolution and rebellion, Power and monarchy, Political decision-making, Historical consequences
