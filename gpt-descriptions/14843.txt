Synopsis: 
In 'The Manor House of Lacolle', W. D. Lighthall takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the rich history and enchanting allure of the Manoir of the Seigniory de Beaujeu of Lacolle. From its origins as a French seigneurial estate to its transformation into a British military stronghold, this manor house holds secrets and stories that will leave readers spellbound. Lighthall's vivid descriptions and meticulous research bring the manor house and its inhabitants to life, painting a vivid picture of the past and the enduring legacy of this remarkable place.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Architecture, Canadian history

Themes: Heritage and preservation, Cultural identity, Historical significance
