Synopsis: In "Doctor Marigold" by Charles Dickens, readers are introduced to the charismatic and unconventional Doctor Marigold, a traveling salesman who also happens to be a self-taught doctor. Through his engaging storytelling, Doctor Marigold takes us on a journey filled with heartwarming encounters, unexpected twists, and profound reflections on the human condition. As he navigates the highs and lows of his profession, we witness the transformative power of compassion and the resilience of the human spirit. 

Genres: Fiction, Short Story, Social Commentary

Themes: Compassion, Resilience, Social Injustice
