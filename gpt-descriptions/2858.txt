Synopsis: In the small mining town of Red Gulch, a young woman named Cressy finds herself caught between the allure of the city and the simplicity of her rural upbringing. As she navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations, Cressy's journey takes unexpected turns that challenge her perceptions of herself and those around her. Set against the backdrop of the California Gold Rush, 'Cressy' is a captivating tale of self-discovery and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Social expectations, Love and relationships
