Synopsis: In 'The Minus Woman' by R. R. Winterbotham, a mysterious woman with an extraordinary ability finds herself caught in a web of danger and intrigue. As she navigates through a world filled with secrets and hidden agendas, she must uncover the truth about her own past and the powers she possesses. With each revelation, the stakes become higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Identity, Power, Deception
