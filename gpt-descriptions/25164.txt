Synopsis: 

"The Flag of Distress: A Story of the South Sea" by Mayne Reid is a thrilling adventure set in the exotic South Seas. The story follows the journey of a young sailor named Harry Blew, who finds himself caught in a series of dangerous and mysterious events while aboard a ship. As he navigates treacherous waters and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Harry must unravel the secrets that lie beneath the surface of the seemingly idyllic paradise. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Exploration
