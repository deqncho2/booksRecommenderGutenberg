Synopsis: In 'A Nine Days' Wonder' by B. M. Croker, a young woman named Muriel finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she inherits a grand estate in the English countryside. As she delves into the secrets of the house and its previous occupants, Muriel uncovers a hidden world of forbidden love, family secrets, and long-buried scandals. With each revelation, she becomes more entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. Will Muriel be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Love and betrayal, Identity and self-discovery
