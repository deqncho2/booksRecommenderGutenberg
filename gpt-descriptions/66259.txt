Synopsis: In 'The Cosmic Junkman' by Phillips, Rog, a young woman named Lily discovers a hidden world of intergalactic treasures and secrets when she stumbles upon a mysterious junkyard. As she delves deeper into this cosmic realm, she encounters eccentric characters, embarks on thrilling adventures, and uncovers the truth about her own origins. With danger lurking at every turn, Lily must navigate this extraordinary universe and confront her own destiny.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Discovery, Identity, Fate
