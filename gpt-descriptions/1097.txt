Synopsis: In "Mrs. Warren's Profession," a young woman named Vivie Warren is confronted with the shocking truth about her mother's source of wealth. As she delves deeper into her mother's past, Vivie must grapple with societal expectations, gender roles, and the complexities of morality. This thought-provoking play by Bernard Shaw explores the clash between personal ambition and societal norms, ultimately challenging the audience to question their own values and judgments.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Social Satire

Themes: Morality, Gender Roles, Social Class, Hypocrisy
