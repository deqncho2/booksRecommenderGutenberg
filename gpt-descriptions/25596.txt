Synopsis: In 'The Keepers of the Trail: A Story of the Great Woods', Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of the Great Woods. Set during the French and Indian War, the story follows a group of young frontiersmen who find themselves caught in the midst of a dangerous conflict between rival nations. As they navigate treacherous terrain, face deadly encounters, and forge unlikely alliances, the fate of their families, their land, and their very way of life hangs in the balance. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Altsheler weaves a tale of courage, loyalty, and the enduring spirit of those who call the wilderness their home.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Identity, Conflict
