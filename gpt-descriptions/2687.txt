Synopsis: In 'The Snare' by Sabatini, Rafael, a young and ambitious lawyer finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he takes on a seemingly straightforward case. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his career but also his life. With his reputation on the line, he must navigate through a treacherous maze of secrets and betrayal to uncover the truth and save himself from the snare that has been set.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Legal Drama

Themes: Deception, Ambition, Betrayal, Justice
