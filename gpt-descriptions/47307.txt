Synopsis: In 'Nancy Dale, Army Nurse' by Ruby Lorraine Radford, follow the gripping journey of Nancy Dale, a courageous and determined nurse serving in the army during World War II. As she faces the harsh realities of war, Nancy's strength and resilience are tested, and she must navigate through love, loss, and the horrors of the battlefield. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt emotions, this captivating novel takes readers on a captivating and unforgettable wartime adventure.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Romance

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Love, Resilience, Humanitarianism
