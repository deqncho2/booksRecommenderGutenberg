Synopsis:
In 'Snarleyyow; or, The Dog Fiend' by Frederick Marryat, a mischievous and cunning dog named Snarleyyow wreaks havoc on a British naval ship during the Napoleonic Wars. As the crew battles their enemies at sea, they also find themselves battling the unpredictable and treacherous nature of their four-legged companion. With a mix of humor, adventure, and suspense, this tale takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Satire

Themes:
Loyalty, Betrayal, Human-Animal Relationships
