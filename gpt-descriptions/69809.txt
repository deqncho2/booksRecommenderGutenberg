Synopsis: In the captivating sequel to "Why did he wed her?", Emma Dorothy Eliza Nevitte Southworth takes readers on a thrilling journey through the lives of the characters they have come to love. As secrets unravel and new challenges arise, the bonds of love and loyalty are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and turns, "For whose sake?" delves deeper into the complexities of relationships, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Betrayal and redemption, Family dynamics
