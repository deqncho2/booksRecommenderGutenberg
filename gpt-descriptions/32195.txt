Synopsis: In "The Battle of Blenheim" by Hilaire Belloc, a young boy named Tommy embarks on a journey to discover the truth behind a famous battle that took place in Blenheim. As he explores the historical site, he encounters unexpected characters and uncovers hidden secrets that shed light on the true nature of war. Through his adventures, Tommy learns valuable lessons about courage, sacrifice, and the impact of war on both individuals and society.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: War, History, Courage, Sacrifice
