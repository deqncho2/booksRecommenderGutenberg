Synopsis: In 'The Mormon Doctrine of Deity: The Roberts-Van Der Donckt Discussion', B.H. Roberts engages in a thought-provoking dialogue that delves into the complex and fascinating concept of deity within the Mormon faith. Through a series of discussions and debates with Van Der Donckt, Roberts explores the nature of God, the divinity of Jesus Christ, and presents a collection of authoritative Mormon teachings on these profound subjects. This enlightening work offers a deep exploration of Mormon theology and invites readers to contemplate the mysteries of the divine.

Genres: Religious Studies, Theology, Comparative Religion

Themes: Nature of God, Divinity, Mormon Doctrine, Religious Philosophy
