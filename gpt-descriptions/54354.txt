Synopsis: 
'Glasgow: A Sketch Book' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the vibrant streets of Glasgow, Scotland. Through the intricate and detailed sketches of artist John Nisbet, the city comes alive with its unique architecture, bustling markets, and diverse inhabitants. From the grandeur of historic landmarks to the hidden gems tucked away in narrow alleyways, this sketch book offers a fresh perspective on the city's rich history and contemporary charm. Each page invites readers to explore the essence of Glasgow, capturing its energy, character, and the stories that unfold within its streets.

Genres: 
Art, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Urban exploration, Cultural diversity, Architectural beauty
