Synopsis: In 'Survival of the Fittest' by Gene L. Henderson, a group of strangers find themselves stranded on a remote island after a catastrophic event. As they struggle to survive in this harsh and unforgiving environment, tensions rise and alliances are formed. With limited resources and the constant threat of danger, they must navigate through treacherous terrain and face their own inner demons. As the days turn into weeks, the true nature of humanity is put to the test, revealing both the darkest and most heroic aspects of human behavior. Will they succumb to their primal instincts or rise above the challenges that confront them?

Genres: Thriller, Survival, Psychological

Themes: Human nature, Morality, Resilience
