Synopsis: 
'Journal of Herbert Edward Pretyman written during his expedition to the Kittar Mountains, between Kenneh (on the Nile) and the Red Sea, 1891' takes readers on a captivating journey through the unexplored and treacherous Kittar Mountains. Herbert Edward Pretyman, an intrepid explorer, documents his thrilling expedition, encountering mysterious landscapes, indigenous tribes, and unexpected challenges. As he delves deeper into the heart of the mountains, Pretyman's journal unveils a world of breathtaking beauty, ancient secrets, and perilous encounters that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural Encounter
