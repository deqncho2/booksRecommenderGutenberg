Synopsis: 
In "Christmas at Sagamore Hill with Theodore Roosevelt," Helen Topping Miller takes readers on a delightful journey to the historic home of the 26th President of the United States. Set during the holiday season, this heartwarming tale follows a young girl named Lucy as she visits Sagamore Hill and experiences the magic of Christmas with the Roosevelt family. Through Lucy's eyes, readers are immersed in the festive atmosphere, filled with joy, laughter, and unexpected surprises. As Lucy explores the grand house and interacts with the Roosevelt children, she learns valuable lessons about friendship, family, and the true meaning of Christmas.

Genres: Historical fiction, Children's literature

Themes: Friendship, Family, Christmas spirit
