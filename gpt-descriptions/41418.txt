Synopsis: In 'Contraband; Or, A Losing Hazard', the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the dangerous world of smuggling in 19th century England. The story follows the adventures of a young nobleman who becomes entangled in a web of deceit, danger, and forbidden love. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the smuggling trade, he must confront his own moral compass and make choices that will determine his fate. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Morality, Deception, Forbidden Love, Loyalty
