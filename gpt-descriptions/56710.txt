Synopsis: 

In the "Venomous Arthropod Handbook: Envenomization Symptoms/Treatment, Identification, Biology and Control," Terry L. Biery delves into the captivating world of venomous arthropods, providing a comprehensive guide that explores their enigmatic nature. From the venomous bites of spiders and scorpions to the stings of bees and wasps, this handbook offers invaluable insights into the symptoms, treatment, identification, biology, and control of these fascinating creatures. With vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, Biery takes readers on a thrilling journey through the realm of venomous arthropods, shedding light on their unique characteristics and the potential dangers they pose. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Natural History

Themes: Wildlife, Conservation, Human-Animal Interaction
