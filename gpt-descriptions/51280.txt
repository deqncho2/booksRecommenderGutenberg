Synopsis: 

'Newfoundland to Cochin China: By the Golden Wave, New Nippon, and the Forbidden City' takes readers on an extraordinary journey across continents and cultures. Ethel Gwendoline Vincent recounts her captivating experiences as she travels from the rugged landscapes of Newfoundland to the exotic wonders of Cochin China. Along the way, she immerses herself in the vibrant cultures of New Nippon (Japan) and explores the enigmatic Forbidden City in China. Through her vivid descriptions and personal encounters, Vincent offers a unique perspective on the people, landscapes, and customs she encounters, creating a compelling narrative that will leave readers eager to discover more.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Cultural exploration, Personal growth, Discovery
