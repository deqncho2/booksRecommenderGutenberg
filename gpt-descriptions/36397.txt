Synopsis:
In "Ruth Fielding At Sunrise Farm; Or, What Became of the Raby Orphans," Ruth Fielding finds herself embarking on a new adventure as she is sent to live with her great-aunt and uncle on Sunrise Farm. As she settles into her new surroundings, Ruth discovers a mystery surrounding the Raby orphans, who disappeared years ago. Determined to uncover the truth, Ruth delves into the secrets of the past, encountering unexpected challenges and forming new friendships along the way.

Genres:
Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Family, Friendship, Identity, Resilience
