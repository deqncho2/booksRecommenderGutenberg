Synopsis: In 'The Grain Ship' by Robertson, Morgan, a mysterious grain ship arrives in a small coastal town, bringing with it a series of strange events that disrupt the peaceful lives of the townspeople. As the ship's secrets begin to unravel, the residents find themselves caught in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, the stakes rise higher, and the town's fate hangs in the balance. Will they uncover the truth behind the grain ship before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town dynamics, Survival
