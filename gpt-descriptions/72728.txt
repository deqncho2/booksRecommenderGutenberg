Synopsis: 

In 'Celtic Scotland: A history of ancient Alban. Volume 2 (of 3), Church and culture', author W. F. Skene delves into the captivating world of ancient Scotland, exploring the intricate relationship between the Church and the vibrant Celtic culture. This volume takes readers on a journey through the rich tapestry of Scotland's past, uncovering the fascinating interplay between religious beliefs, cultural practices, and the ever-evolving society of the time. From the enigmatic Picts to the influential Celtic saints, Skene's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life the captivating history of Celtic Scotland.

Genres: History, Cultural Studies, Religion

Themes: Celtic culture, Religious practices, Historical exploration
