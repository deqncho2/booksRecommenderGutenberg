Synopsis: In "An Address Delivered at the Interment of Mrs. Harriet Storrs," Reverend John Codman delivers a poignant and heartfelt eulogy for the late Mrs. Storrs, wife of Reverend Richard S. Storrs. Through his words, Codman explores the profound impact Mrs. Storrs had on her community and the legacy she leaves behind. With eloquence and emotion, he reflects on her virtues, her devotion to her family, and her unwavering faith. This address serves as a tribute to a remarkable woman whose life and character continue to inspire all who knew her.

Genres: Eulogy, Non-fiction

Themes: Legacy, Virtue, Faith
