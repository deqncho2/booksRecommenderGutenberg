Synopsis: In 'Dan, the Newsboy' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Dan Mordaunt, a young and determined newsboy struggling to make a living on the streets of New York City. Despite facing numerous challenges and setbacks, Dan's unwavering spirit and resourcefulness lead him on a remarkable journey of self-discovery and unexpected opportunities. As he navigates the bustling city and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Dan's resilience and determination are put to the test, ultimately shaping his future in ways he never could have imagined.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Social mobility, Self-reliance
