Synopsis: In 'The Kingdom of the Blind' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a young man named John Berrisford finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he inherits a vast fortune from a distant relative. As he delves deeper into his newfound wealth, he discovers that his inheritance comes with a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but also the lives of those around him. With the help of an enigmatic woman and a group of unlikely allies, John must navigate a treacherous world of deceit, betrayal, and hidden agendas to uncover the truth and protect what is rightfully his.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Betrayal, Identity
