Synopsis: In "Byron: The Last Phase" by Richard Edgcumbe, delve into the final chapter of the enigmatic poet Lord Byron's life. Set against the backdrop of the early 19th century, this captivating biography explores the tumultuous relationships, scandalous affairs, and literary brilliance that defined Byron's last years. As he navigates the complexities of love, fame, and personal demons, witness the transformation of a man who defied societal norms and left an indelible mark on the world of literature.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Love and Relationships, Fame and Notoriety, Personal Transformation
