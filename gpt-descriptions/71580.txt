Synopsis: In the enchanting world of ancient Persia, a young prince named Jamshyd embarks on a captivating journey through the courts of his kingdom. As he navigates the opulent palaces and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Jamshyd discovers secrets, faces challenges, and unravels the mysteries of his own destiny. With each court he visits, the prince's understanding of power, love, and the human condition deepens, leading him to question the very nature of his existence. Will Jamshyd find the answers he seeks, or will he be forever lost in the labyrinth of his own desires?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Relationships, Self-discovery
