Synopsis: 
In 'The Trap' by Betsy Curtis, a young woman named Emily finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter hidden in her deceased grandmother's belongings. As she delves deeper into the truth behind the letter, Emily becomes the target of a dangerous game, where trust is scarce and danger lurks around every corner. With her life on the line, Emily must navigate through a labyrinth of lies and betrayal to uncover the shocking truth that has been hidden for decades.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Betrayal
