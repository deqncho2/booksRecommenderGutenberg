Synopsis: In "The Jesus Problem: A Restatement of the Myth Theory," J.M. Robertson delves into the controversial topic of the historical existence of Jesus Christ. With meticulous research and thought-provoking analysis, Robertson challenges traditional beliefs and presents a compelling argument that challenges the very foundations of Christianity. As he explores the origins of the Jesus myth, the author raises intriguing questions about the nature of religious belief and the impact of mythology on human history. Prepare to have your assumptions challenged and your mind opened in this thought-provoking exploration of one of the most enduring figures in human history.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, Historical Analysis

Themes: Mythology, Religion, Historical Accuracy, Human Belief Systems
