Synopsis: In "The Rover Boys at College; Or, The Right Road and the Wrong," the adventurous Rover brothers find themselves embarking on a new chapter of their lives as they enter college. However, their journey takes an unexpected turn when they encounter a mysterious figure who seems determined to lead them astray. As they navigate the challenges of college life, the Rovers must unravel the secrets behind this enigmatic character while staying true to their values and making the right choices. Will they be able to overcome the obstacles and find their way to success?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Morality, Self-discovery
