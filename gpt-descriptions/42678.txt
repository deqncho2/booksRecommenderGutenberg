Synopsis:
In 'Fires - Book 2: The Ovens, and Other Tales' by Gibson, Wilfrid Wilson, readers are transported to a world where the boundaries between reality and imagination blur. This collection of captivating tales takes us on a journey through the mysterious and the supernatural, where ordinary lives are touched by extraordinary events. From a haunted house that holds dark secrets to a young girl's encounter with a mischievous spirit, each story is a mesmerizing exploration of the human psyche and the power of the unknown. As the characters navigate through these enigmatic experiences, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and desires, ultimately leading to unexpected revelations and profound transformations. 

Genres: Fiction, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: Reality vs. Imagination, Fear and Desire, Transformation
