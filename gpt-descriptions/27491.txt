Synopsis: 
In "Unthinkable" by Phillips, Rog, a renowned psychologist is faced with a perplexing case when a mysterious patient arrives at his office claiming to possess knowledge of an imminent catastrophic event. As the psychologist delves deeper into the patient's mind, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, manipulation, and moral dilemmas that challenge his own sanity. With time running out, he must navigate the blurred lines between right and wrong, truth and deception, in a race against the unthinkable.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Perception, Sanity
