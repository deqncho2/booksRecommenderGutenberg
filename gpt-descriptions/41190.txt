Synopsis:
'Chronicles of Dustypore: A Tale of Modern Anglo-Indian Society' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and complex world of Anglo-Indian society in Dustypore, a fictional town in colonial India. The story follows the lives of a diverse cast of characters, including British colonizers, Indian natives, and the Anglo-Indians caught between two cultures. As the narrative unfolds, secrets, scandals, and forbidden love affairs intertwine, revealing the intricate web of relationships and power dynamics within this unique community. Amidst the backdrop of social upheaval and political unrest, the characters navigate their personal struggles, challenging societal norms and confronting their own identities. With its richly detailed setting and compelling characters, this novel offers a thought-provoking exploration of the complexities of identity, cultural assimilation, and the clash of traditions in a rapidly changing world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Identity, Cultural Assimilation, Love and Relationships, Social Hierarchy, Colonialism
