Synopsis: In "The Black Feather: From "Mackinac And Lake Stories", 1899," Mary Hartwell Catherwood weaves a captivating tale set in the enchanting world of Mackinac Island. When a mysterious black feather is discovered, it sets off a chain of events that will test the bonds of friendship and loyalty. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling narrative, this story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Secrets, Intrigue
