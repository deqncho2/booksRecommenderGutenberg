Synopsis: 
In "Coquette" by Frank Swinnerton, we are introduced to the captivating and enigmatic character of Fanny, a young woman who possesses an irresistible charm that both intrigues and unsettles those around her. As Fanny navigates the complexities of love, desire, and societal expectations, her actions and choices have far-reaching consequences that challenge the conventional norms of her time. With a cast of compelling characters and a narrative that delves into the depths of human emotions, "Coquette" is a gripping exploration of passion, self-discovery, and the consequences of following one's heart.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, self-discovery
