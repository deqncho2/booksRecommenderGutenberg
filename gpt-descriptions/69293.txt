Synopsis: In "The Pleasure Age" by Joed Cahill, a mysterious new drug called Euphoria sweeps through the city, promising an unparalleled experience of pleasure. As the drug's popularity soars, it becomes clear that there are unforeseen consequences to indulging in this addictive substance. The story follows a diverse group of characters who find themselves entangled in the web of Euphoria, each with their own motivations and desires. As they navigate the dark underbelly of the city, they must confront their own inner demons and make choices that will determine their fate. With its gripping narrative and thought-provoking exploration of human desires, "The Pleasure Age" takes readers on a thrilling journey through a world where pleasure comes at a dangerous price.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Dystopian

Themes: Addiction, Morality, Consequences
