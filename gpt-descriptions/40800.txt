Synopsis: In 'An Imperial Marriage' by Arthur W. Marchmont, a young woman finds herself thrust into a world of political intrigue and forbidden love when she is chosen to marry a powerful emperor. As she navigates the treacherous court, she must decide between duty and her own desires, all while uncovering dark secrets that could change the course of an empire. With captivating characters and a gripping plot, this historical romance will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the ultimate fate of our heroine.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Intrigue

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Forbidden Love, Power and Politics
