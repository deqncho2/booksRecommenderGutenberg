Synopsis: In "Christopher Columbus (1440-1506): The First American Citizen (by Adoption)" by Alden, W. L., embark on a captivating journey through the life of the renowned explorer. Delve into the untold story of Columbus, as the author unveils surprising details about his origins, motivations, and the impact of his discoveries. This gripping narrative sheds light on the complex character of Columbus, challenging preconceived notions and inviting readers to reconsider the legacy of this historical figure. Prepare to be enthralled by the fascinating tale of a man who forever changed the course of history.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Exploration, Legacy, Identity
