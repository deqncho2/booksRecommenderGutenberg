Synopsis: 
In "My Three Years in Manipur: A Memoir and Escape from the Recent Mutiny," Ethel St. Clair Grimwood takes readers on a captivating journey through her experiences in Manipur, a remote region in India. As a British woman living in the late 19th century, Ethel finds herself immersed in a world vastly different from her own. She encounters the vibrant culture, the lush landscapes, and the complex political dynamics of Manipur. However, her idyllic life takes a dramatic turn when a mutiny erupts, threatening her safety and forcing her to embark on a daring escape. Gripping and filled with suspense, this memoir offers a unique perspective on a tumultuous period in history.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Cultural immersion, Political unrest, Survival
