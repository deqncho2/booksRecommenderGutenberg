Synopsis: In 'Return of a Legend' by Raymond Z. Gallun, a retired space explorer is unexpectedly called back into action when a mysterious threat emerges from the depths of the universe. With his experience and knowledge, he must navigate treacherous space and face unknown dangers to protect humanity from an ancient evil that could bring about the end of civilization as we know it. As he embarks on this perilous journey, he must confront his own past and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of the galaxy.

Genres: Science Fiction, Space Opera, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Redemption, Sacrifice, Exploration, Survival
