Synopsis: In 'Evening Incense' by John R. Macduff, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn into a world of ancient rituals and hidden secrets. As she delves deeper into the enigmatic world of incense, she discovers a power that could change her life forever. With each step she takes, Emily uncovers a web of intrigue and danger, leading her to question everything she thought she knew. Will she be able to navigate this treacherous path and unlock the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden knowledge, the power of rituals, self-discovery
