Synopsis: 
'Bib Ballads' by Lardner, Ring is a collection of witty and satirical poems that take a humorous look at various aspects of American life. From baseball to politics, love to social norms, Lardner's clever verses provide a fresh perspective on everyday situations. With his sharp wit and clever wordplay, Lardner invites readers to laugh and reflect on the absurdities of the world around them.

Genres: Poetry, Satire, Humor

Themes: American life, Social commentary, Satire
