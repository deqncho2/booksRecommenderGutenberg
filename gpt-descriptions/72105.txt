Synopsis: In 'Interference: A Novel, Vol. 2 (of 3)', the captivating story continues as the lives of two young women, separated by time and circumstance, become intertwined through a series of mysterious events. As secrets are unveiled and unexpected connections are made, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with suspense, romance, and the power of fate. Will these women be able to overcome the interference that threatens to unravel their lives?

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Fate and destiny, Secrets and revelations, Connection and interference
