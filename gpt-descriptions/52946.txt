Synopsis: In 'Three Sides of Paradise Green' by Augusta Huiell Seaman, a group of friends stumble upon a hidden treasure map that leads them on an exciting adventure. As they follow the clues, they encounter mysterious characters and face unexpected challenges that test their courage and friendship. With each twist and turn, the stakes get higher, and the friends must rely on their wits and determination to unravel the secrets of Paradise Green.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Treasure Hunting
