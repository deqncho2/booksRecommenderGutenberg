Synopsis: 
'Boche and Bolshevik: Experiences of an Englishman in the German Army and in Russian Prisons' follows the captivating journey of Hereward Thimbleby Price, an Englishman who finds himself caught in the midst of World War I and the Russian Revolution. From his unexpected enlistment in the German Army to his subsequent imprisonment in Russian jails, Price's firsthand account offers a unique perspective on the tumultuous events that shaped the early 20th century. Filled with gripping anecdotes, unexpected alliances, and harrowing escapes, this memoir takes readers on a thrilling and eye-opening adventure through two of the most significant conflicts of the era.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War Literature
Themes: War and Conflict, Identity and Nationality, Survival and Resilience
