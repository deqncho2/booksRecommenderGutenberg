Synopsis: In 'The Faithless Parrot' by Charles H. Bennett, a mysterious parrot named Jasper becomes the center of attention in a small coastal town. As the townspeople become captivated by Jasper's uncanny ability to mimic their deepest secrets, a web of intrigue and deception begins to unravel. With each revelation, the line between truth and lies becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning who can be trusted. As the story unfolds, the true nature of Jasper's power is revealed, leading to a shocking climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Deception, Trust, Power of Secrets
