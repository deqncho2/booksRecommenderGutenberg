Synopsis: In "The Motor Girls on the Coast; or, The Waif From the Sea," the adventurous and resourceful Motor Girls find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey along the coast. When they encounter a mysterious waif washed ashore, their curiosity is piqued, and they are determined to uncover the secrets surrounding her. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they face unexpected challenges, uncover hidden treasures, and form unbreakable bonds. This captivating tale of friendship, courage, and discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
