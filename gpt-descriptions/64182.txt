Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the rich history and captivating stories of the town and soke of Horncastle in the county of Lincoln. In this meticulously researched and beautifully written book, George Weir takes readers on a journey through time, uncovering the secrets and hidden gems of this fascinating region. From the ancient Roman settlements to the medieval castles and bustling marketplaces, Weir paints a vivid picture of Horncastle's past, bringing to life the people, events, and landmarks that have shaped its identity. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for storytelling, Weir invites readers to immerse themselves in the captivating history of this enchanting town and its surrounding areas.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Local history, Travelogue

Themes: History, Local culture, Architecture, Community
