Synopsis: In 'The Little Hunchback Zia' by Frances Hodgson Burnett, a young hunchback girl named Zia finds herself on a remarkable journey that takes her from the bustling streets of London to the enchanting landscapes of Italy. Along the way, she encounters a cast of colorful characters who help her discover her true worth and inner strength. As Zia navigates through adversity and unexpected friendships, she learns that beauty lies not in appearances, but in the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-acceptance, Inner beauty, Friendship, Overcoming adversity
