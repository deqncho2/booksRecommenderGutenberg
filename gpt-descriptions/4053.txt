Synopsis: In "Nuttie's Father" by Charlotte M. Yonge, a young girl named Nuttie embarks on a journey to discover the truth about her mysterious father. As she delves into her family's past, she uncovers secrets, encounters unexpected allies, and faces challenges that test her courage and resilience. Nuttie's quest for answers takes her on a captivating adventure filled with twists and turns, ultimately leading her to a revelation that will change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, Family Drama

Themes: Identity, Family, Secrets, Self-discovery
