Synopsis: In 'The Wheel O' Fortune' by Tracy, Louis, a mysterious antique shop appears in a small town, offering its customers a chance to spin a wheel that promises great fortune. As the townspeople become consumed by their desires, they soon discover that the wheel's rewards come at a high price. With each spin, the characters are forced to confront their deepest fears and make difficult choices that will change their lives forever. This gripping tale explores the consequences of greed, the power of choice, and the true nature of fortune.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Greed, Consequences, Choice, Fate
