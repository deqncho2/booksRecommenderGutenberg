Synopsis: 

In 'The Flags of Our Fighting Army: Including standards, guidons, colours and drum banners', Stanley C. Johnson takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and symbolism of military flags. From the battlefields of ancient civilizations to the modern era, this comprehensive guide explores the significance and stories behind these iconic symbols of honor and courage. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Johnson delves into the intricate details of each flag, revealing the tales of bravery, sacrifice, and unity that they represent. Whether it's the stirring tales of triumph or the poignant stories of loss, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of military flags and the men and women who fought under them.

Genres: History, Military, Non-fiction

Themes: Honor, Courage, Symbolism
