Synopsis: In the small town of Calumet, a mysterious figure known as 'K' arrives, stirring up curiosity and suspicion among the townspeople. As K's presence becomes more prominent, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the town's dark past is brought to light. With a blend of suspense, intrigue, and unexpected twists, Calumet 'K' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the complexities of human nature and the consequences of hidden truths.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption, Small-town dynamics
