Synopsis: In "Lee's Last Campaign" by J.C. Gorman, the reader is transported to the final days of the American Civil War, where General Robert E. Lee finds himself facing insurmountable odds. As Lee strategizes and leads his troops, the story delves into the complexities of war, the sacrifices made by soldiers, and the weight of leadership. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this historical novel takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous climax of the Civil War.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Military Fiction

Themes: Leadership, Sacrifice, Conflict, Loyalty
