Synopsis: 
In 'A Spring Harvest' by Geoffrey Bache Smith, a young man named Richard embarks on a journey of self-discovery and redemption after a tragic event changes the course of his life. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque English countryside, Richard encounters a cast of intriguing characters who challenge his beliefs and push him to confront his past. As he navigates through love, loss, and the complexities of human nature, Richard must ultimately decide whether he can find solace and forgiveness amidst the beauty of a spring harvest.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Self-discovery, Redemption, Love and Loss
