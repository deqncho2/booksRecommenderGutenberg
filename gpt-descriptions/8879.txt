Synopsis: In 'There & Back' by MacDonald, George, a young man named Cosmo embarks on a mysterious journey that takes him to fantastical realms and tests his courage and resilience. Along the way, he encounters enigmatic characters, faces daunting challenges, and discovers the true nature of his own identity. As Cosmo navigates through a world filled with wonder and danger, he must confront his deepest fears and make choices that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Courage, Self-discovery
