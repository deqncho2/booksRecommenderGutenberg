Synopsis: In 'Raw Material' by Dorothy Canfield Fisher, a young woman named Ellen is torn between her desire for independence and the expectations of her traditional family. When she embarks on a journey to a remote village in Vermont, she encounters a community that challenges her preconceived notions and forces her to confront her own identity. As Ellen navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and self-discovery, she must make difficult choices that will shape her future.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Romance

Themes: Identity, Independence, Tradition, Self-discovery
