Synopsis: In the charming town of Fredericton, Lady Rosamond leads a seemingly perfect life as a respected member of high society. However, beneath her elegant facade lies a secret that threatens to unravel her world. As she navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations, Lady Rosamond must confront the consequences of her hidden past. Set against the backdrop of a captivating romance, 'Lady Rosamond's Secret' takes readers on a thrilling journey of mystery and self-discovery.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and relationships, Society and class
