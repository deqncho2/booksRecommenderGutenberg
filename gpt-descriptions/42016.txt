Synopsis: In "The Purple Flame: A Mystery Story for Girls," a group of adventurous young girls stumble upon a mysterious purple flame that holds a secret power. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they find themselves entangled in a thrilling web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected twists. With their courage and resourcefulness, the girls must unravel the truth behind the purple flame before it falls into the wrong hands. Will they succeed in solving the mystery and protecting the world from its potential harm?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
