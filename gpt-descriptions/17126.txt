Synopsis: In 'Five Happy Weeks' by Margaret Elizabeth Munson Sangster, a young woman embarks on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected romance during a summer vacation. As she navigates new friendships and explores her passions, she finds herself questioning her priorities and reevaluating her future. With each passing week, she learns valuable lessons about love, happiness, and the importance of following one's dreams. Will these five weeks be enough to change her life forever?

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Pursuing dreams
