Synopsis: 
'Is He Popenjoy?' by Anthony Trollope is a captivating novel that revolves around the question of the true identity of a young boy named Popenjoy. As the story unfolds, readers are taken on a journey filled with family secrets, political intrigue, and the complexities of love and marriage. With unexpected twists and turns, the novel explores the consequences of deception and the lengths people will go to protect their own interests. 

Genres: 
Romance, Mystery, Victorian Literature

Themes: 
Identity, Deception, Family, Politics
