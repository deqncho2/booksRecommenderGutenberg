Synopsis:
In the small English village of Bryngelly, a respected doctor named Therne finds himself at the center of a scandalous controversy. As he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and deceit, his reputation and career hang in the balance. With the lives of his patients and the future of the village at stake, Doctor Therne must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he cares about.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Morality, Betrayal, Power, Redemption
