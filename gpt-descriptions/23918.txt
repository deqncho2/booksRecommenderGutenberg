Synopsis: In "The Little Quaker; or, the Triumph of Virtue. A Tale for the Instruction of Youth," Susanna Moodie weaves a captivating story that follows the life of a young Quaker boy named Samuel. Raised in a strict religious community, Samuel faces numerous challenges and temptations as he navigates the complexities of the world outside his sheltered upbringing. From encounters with deceitful individuals to moments of personal doubt, Samuel's unwavering commitment to his faith and the pursuit of virtue becomes a source of inspiration for those around him. As the story unfolds, readers are taken on a journey filled with moral dilemmas, unexpected friendships, and the triumph of virtue in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Religious Fiction

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Morality and ethics, Resilience and perseverance
