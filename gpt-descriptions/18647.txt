Synopsis: In 'Peeps at Many Lands: Egypt', join the adventurous journey of a group of young explorers as they immerse themselves in the rich history, captivating culture, and awe-inspiring landscapes of Egypt. From the bustling streets of Cairo to the majestic pyramids of Giza, the young explorers encounter ancient mysteries, encounter fascinating locals, and uncover hidden treasures. As they delve deeper into the heart of Egypt, they discover that the land of the pharaohs holds secrets that will forever change their perspective on history and the world.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Diversity, Ancient Civilizations
