Synopsis: In "Literary Boston as I Knew It," William Dean Howells takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant literary scene of Boston in the late 19th century. Through his personal experiences and encounters with renowned authors, Howells provides a vivid and intimate portrayal of the city's literary culture, its influential figures, and the intellectual debates that shaped the era. From lively discussions at the Saturday Club to the bohemian gatherings at the Old Corner Bookstore, Howells offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of literature and the vibrant personalities that inhabited it.

Genres: Memoir, Literary Criticism, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Literary culture, Intellectual debates, Boston's literary history
