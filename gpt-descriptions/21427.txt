Synopsis: In 'Comic History of the United States', Bill Nye takes readers on a hilarious and informative journey through the history of the United States, from its early days of exploration to the modern era. With his signature wit and humor, Nye presents historical events and figures in a way that is both entertaining and educational. From the founding fathers to the Wild West, and from the Civil War to the space race, Nye brings history to life with his unique storytelling style. This book is a delightful and engaging read for anyone interested in American history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Humor

Themes: American history, Education, Entertainment
