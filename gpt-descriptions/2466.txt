Synopsis: In 'Virgin Soil' by Ivan Turgenev, a young idealist named Nezhdanov finds himself caught between the allure of revolutionary ideas and the complexities of love and loyalty. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Russia, Nezhdanov becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal dilemmas as he navigates the treacherous path towards societal change. As he grapples with his own beliefs and desires, Nezhdanov must confront the harsh realities of a society on the brink of transformation.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Political Fiction

Themes: Revolution, Idealism, Love and Loyalty, Social Change
