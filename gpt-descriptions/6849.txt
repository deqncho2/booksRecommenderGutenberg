Synopsis: In the second volume of "The Prince of India; Or, Why Constantinople Fell," the captivating story continues as the enigmatic Prince of India, who possesses immense wealth and power, navigates the treacherous political landscape of Constantinople. As he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and betrayal, the Prince's true identity and motives remain shrouded in mystery. With the fate of an empire hanging in the balance, the Prince's actions will have far-reaching consequences that will test the limits of loyalty and honor.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Power and Politics, Identity and Deception, Loyalty and Betrayal
