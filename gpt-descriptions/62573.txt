Synopsis: In 'The Edge of the Desert' by Ianthe M. Dunbar, a young archaeologist embarks on a thrilling expedition to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization buried deep within the desert. As she delves into the mysteries of the past, she becomes entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and unexpected romance. With each discovery, she must navigate treacherous terrain and face formidable adversaries who will stop at nothing to protect the secrets of the desert. Will she unravel the truth before it consumes her?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Betrayal, Forbidden Love, Preservation of History
