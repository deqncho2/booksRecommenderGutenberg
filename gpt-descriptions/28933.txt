Synopsis: In 'One Out of Ten' by Ferlaine, J. Anthony, a seemingly ordinary high school student named Alex finds himself caught in a web of secrets and danger when he discovers a hidden society within his school. As he delves deeper into this mysterious world, Alex must navigate treacherous alliances, uncover the truth about his own identity, and confront the dark forces that threaten to destroy everything he holds dear. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, 'One Out of Ten' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Young Adult, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Betrayal, Survival
