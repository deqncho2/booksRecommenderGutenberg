Synopsis: In 'My Second Year of the War', Frederick Palmer takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous events of World War I. Through vivid and personal accounts, Palmer provides a unique perspective on the war, delving into the experiences of soldiers, the impact on civilians, and the ever-changing dynamics on the battlefield. As the war rages on, readers will be captivated by the bravery, resilience, and sacrifices of those involved, as well as the profound effects the war has on individuals and society as a whole.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War memoir

Themes: War, Courage, Sacrifice, Human resilience, Societal impact
