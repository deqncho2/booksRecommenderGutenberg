Synopsis: In "The Law of the Road; or, Wrongs and Rights of a Traveller," author R. Vashon Rogers takes readers on a thrilling journey through the complex world of travel and the legal implications that arise on the road. The story follows a traveler who finds himself entangled in a web of unexpected events and encounters, forcing him to navigate the treacherous terrain of justice and morality. As the protagonist grapples with the consequences of his actions, readers are drawn into a suspenseful narrative that explores the intricacies of the law and the choices we make while on our own personal journeys.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Justice, Morality, Consequences of actions
