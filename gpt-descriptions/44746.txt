Synopsis: In 'Household stories from the Land of Hofer; or, Popular Myths of Tirol', Rachel Harriette Busk takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting folklore of the Tirol region. From magical creatures and mystical encounters to tales of love, bravery, and the power of nature, this collection of popular myths and legends will transport readers to a world filled with wonder and adventure. With each story, Busk weaves a tapestry of imagination and folklore, inviting readers to explore the rich cultural heritage of Tirol and discover the timeless wisdom hidden within these captivating tales.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Mythology, Magic, Love, Bravery, Nature
