Synopsis: In "Innocent at Large" by Poul Anderson and Karen Anderson, a young boy named Timmy finds himself transported to a strange and magical world after a mysterious encounter. As he navigates this new realm, Timmy must rely on his wits and courage to unravel the secrets that lie within. With the help of newfound friends and facing dangerous adversaries, Timmy embarks on an extraordinary adventure that will test his strength and determination. Will he find a way back home, or will he be forever lost in this enchanting but treacherous land?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Coming of age, Friendship, Courage, Discovery
