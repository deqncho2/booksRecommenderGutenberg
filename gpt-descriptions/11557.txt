Synopsis: In 'On Compromise' by John Morley, a young politician finds himself torn between his personal beliefs and the compromises necessary for success in the political arena. As he navigates the treacherous waters of power, he must confront the moral dilemmas that arise when principles clash with pragmatism. With his reputation and relationships on the line, he must decide how far he is willing to compromise to achieve his goals. 

Genres: Political fiction, Drama, Psychological thriller

Themes: Morality, Power, Ambition, Integrity
