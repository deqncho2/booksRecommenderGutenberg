Synopsis: In 'Mildred', Mary Jane Holmes weaves a captivating tale of a young woman's journey through love, loss, and self-discovery. Set in the enchanting backdrop of 19th-century America, Mildred's life takes unexpected turns as she navigates the complexities of societal expectations, family secrets, and the pursuit of her own happiness. As she faces heartbreak and betrayal, Mildred's resilience and determination shine through, leading her on a path towards finding her true identity and the love she deserves. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Relationships, Identity and Self-Discovery, Society and Expectations
