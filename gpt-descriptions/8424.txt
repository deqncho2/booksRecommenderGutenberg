Synopsis: In 'Mohun; Or, the Last Days of Lee and His Paladins.: Final Memoirs of a Staff Officer Serving in Virginia. from the Mss. of Colonel Surry, of Eagle's Nest.', Colonel Surry recounts his experiences as a staff officer during the final days of the Civil War. Set in Virginia, the narrative follows the protagonist's encounters with General Robert E. Lee and his loyal soldiers, known as the Paladins. As the war reaches its climax, Surry finds himself torn between duty and personal beliefs, leading to a series of gripping and emotional events that will forever shape his perspective on honor, sacrifice, and the true nature of war.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Memoir

Themes: Honor, Sacrifice, Loyalty, Morality, War's impact on individuals and society
