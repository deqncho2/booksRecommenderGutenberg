Synopsis: In 'Signs of Change' by Morris, William, a young artist finds himself caught in the midst of a rapidly changing society, where the boundaries between reality and imagination blur. As he navigates through a world filled with political unrest, social inequality, and personal struggles, he discovers the power of art to challenge the status quo and ignite a revolution. With each stroke of his brush, he unveils a hidden truth that has the potential to transform not only his own life but also the fate of an entire nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Social Commentary

Themes: Artistic expression, Social justice, Revolution, Individualism
