Synopsis: 
"All Aboard: A Story for Girls" by Fannie E. Newberry takes readers on an exciting journey as they follow the adventures of a group of young girls aboard a steamboat. Set in the late 19th century, the story unfolds as the girls navigate through unexpected challenges, forge new friendships, and discover the power of determination and resilience. From encounters with mysterious passengers to thrilling escapades on the river, this captivating tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what lies ahead for these courageous girls.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Courage, Exploration
