Synopsis: In "King Arthur's Knights: The Tales Re-told for Boys & Girls," Gilbert Henry brings to life the legendary stories of King Arthur and his knights in a captivating and accessible way. From the valiant Sir Lancelot to the wise Merlin, readers will be enthralled by the adventures, quests, and battles that shape the Arthurian legend. This re-telling is filled with bravery, chivalry, and the timeless struggle between good and evil, making it a must-read for both young and old.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Destiny, Good vs. Evil
