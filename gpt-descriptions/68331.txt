Synopsis: In 'Planet of Sand' by Murray Leinster, a group of scientists embarks on a daring mission to explore a mysterious planet covered entirely in sand. As they delve deeper into the planet's secrets, they uncover ancient ruins, encounter strange creatures, and face unexpected dangers. With their survival hanging in the balance, the scientists must unravel the enigma of this peculiar world before it consumes them all.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Discovery
