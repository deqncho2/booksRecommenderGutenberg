Synopsis: 
In 'Bertha's Visit to Her Uncle in England; vol. 1 [of 3]', young Bertha embarks on a thrilling journey from her home in Switzerland to visit her uncle in England. As she navigates the unfamiliar landscapes and encounters new people, Bertha's curiosity and adventurous spirit lead her on a series of captivating escapades. From exploring ancient castles to unraveling family secrets, Bertha's visit becomes an unforgettable adventure filled with mystery, friendship, and self-discovery.

Genres: 
Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Curiosity, Adventure, Family, Self-discovery
