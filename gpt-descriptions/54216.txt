Synopsis: In 'The Mary Frances First Aid Book', young Mary Frances embarks on an exciting journey to learn about first aid and home remedies. Through engaging stories and interactive lessons, Mary Frances discovers the importance of being prepared for accidents and illnesses. With a ready reference list of ordinary mishaps and approved remedies, this book provides practical knowledge that can be applied in everyday life. Join Mary Frances as she navigates the world of first aid, empowering readers to become confident caregivers and problem solvers.

Genres: Children's literature, Educational, Health and Wellness

Themes: First aid, Home remedies, Empowerment, Preparedness
