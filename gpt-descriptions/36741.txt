Synopsis: 
In "Astronomy with an Opera-glass: A Popular Introduction to the Study of the Starry Heavens with the Simplest of Optical Instruments," Garrett Putman Serviss takes readers on a captivating journey through the night sky using nothing more than an opera-glass. With vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, Serviss unveils the wonders of the cosmos, revealing the hidden beauty and mysteries that lie beyond our reach. From the dazzling constellations to the enigmatic planets, readers will be enthralled by the celestial wonders that have captivated humanity for centuries. This book is a gateway to a world of awe-inspiring discoveries, inviting readers to explore the vastness of the universe and ignite their curiosity about the stars above.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Astronomy
Themes: Wonder and awe of the universe, Exploration and discovery
