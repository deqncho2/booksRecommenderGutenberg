Synopsis: 
In 'Tom Grogan' by Francis Hopkinson Smith, we are introduced to the charismatic and ambitious Tom Grogan, a self-made man who rises from humble beginnings to become a prominent figure in New York City. Through his determination and resourcefulness, Grogan navigates the treacherous world of business, politics, and love, facing numerous challenges and unexpected twists along the way. As his fortunes fluctuate, Grogan's resilience and unwavering spirit captivate the reader, making for a thrilling and inspiring tale of triumph against all odds.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: 
Ambition, Resilience, Social Mobility
