Synopsis: 

In 'Senate Resolution 6; 41st Congress, 1st Session: A Bill to provide stationery for Congress and the several departments, and for other purposes,' the United States Congress finds itself embroiled in a seemingly mundane yet surprisingly captivating debate over the allocation of stationery supplies. As tensions rise and alliances form, the fate of Congress and the several departments hangs in the balance. Will they be able to reach a consensus and secure the necessary stationery, or will this seemingly trivial matter lead to unexpected consequences?

Genres: Political drama, Satire

Themes: Bureaucracy, Power dynamics, Decision-making
