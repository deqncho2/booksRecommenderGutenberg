Synopsis: In 'Green Stockings: A Comedy in Three Acts', the lives of the Wetherell family are turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives on their doorstep. As secrets are revealed and hidden desires come to light, the family must navigate a web of misunderstandings, romantic entanglements, and hilarious mishaps. With witty dialogue and unexpected twists, this delightful comedy explores the complexities of love, loyalty, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Family dynamics, Identity and self-discovery
