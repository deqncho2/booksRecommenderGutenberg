Synopsis: 
In 'Girls of the True Blue', a group of young women find themselves entangled in a mysterious secret society at their prestigious boarding school. As they navigate the complexities of friendship, love, and loyalty, they uncover dark secrets that threaten to unravel their lives. With each twist and turn, the girls must confront their own fears and make difficult choices that will determine their fate. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Betrayal, Coming of Age
