Synopsis: In 'Warm' by Robert Sheckley, a man wakes up in a strange and unfamiliar world, where everything seems perfect and everyone is content. However, as he delves deeper into this seemingly idyllic society, he begins to uncover unsettling truths that challenge his perception of reality. With each revelation, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and find his way back home.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Conformity, Identity
