Synopsis: In the third volume of "When Valmond Came to Pontiac: The Story of a Lost Napoleon," the captivating tale of Valmond's arrival in the small Canadian town of Pontiac continues to unfold. As Valmond's influence grows, the townspeople find themselves torn between their loyalty to their own traditions and their fascination with this enigmatic figure. With secrets and betrayals lurking in the shadows, the fate of Pontiac hangs in the balance, and Valmond's true intentions remain shrouded in mystery. Will the town succumb to his charm, or will they uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Deception
