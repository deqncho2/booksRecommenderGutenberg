Synopsis: 
In 'Recollections of a Peninsular Veteran' by Joseph Jocelyn Anderson, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the Peninsular War. Through the eyes of a seasoned veteran, the narrative delves into the harrowing experiences, camaraderie, and personal growth that come with being on the frontlines of battle. From the adrenaline-fueled clashes to the quieter moments of reflection, this gripping account offers a vivid portrayal of the realities faced by soldiers during this historic conflict.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Memoir

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Survival
