Synopsis: 
For Yardley: A Story of Track and Field by Ralph Henry Barbour is a captivating coming-of-age novel that follows the journey of Yardley, a talented young athlete, as he navigates the world of track and field. Set against the backdrop of a prestigious boarding school, Yardley faces numerous challenges and obstacles that test his determination, resilience, and character. As he strives to achieve greatness in his sport, Yardley must confront personal doubts, rivalries, and unexpected friendships that will shape his path to success. With its compelling narrative and vivid portrayal of the competitive world of track and field, this novel is sure to keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: 
Sports Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Young Adult

Themes: 
Ambition, Perseverance, Friendship, Self-discovery
