Synopsis: 
In the small town of Willowbrook, a mysterious event leaves Tim with a unique ability that sets him apart from everyone else. As he navigates through the challenges of his newfound power, Tim discovers a hidden world filled with danger, secrets, and unexpected allies. With time running out, Tim must uncover the truth behind his abilities and confront the forces that threaten to destroy everything he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Power, Friendship, Coming of Age
