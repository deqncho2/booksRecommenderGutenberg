Synopsis: 

In 'The Newmarket, Bury, Thetford and Cromer Road: Sport and history on an East Anglian turnpike', Charles G. Harper takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich sporting and historical heritage of the East Anglian region. From the bustling horse racing scene in Newmarket to the ancient market towns of Bury St. Edmunds and Thetford, and the picturesque coastal town of Cromer, this book uncovers fascinating stories and anecdotes that intertwine the worlds of sport and history. Through vivid descriptions and engaging narratives, Harper brings to life the characters, events, and landmarks that have shaped this unique corner of England. 

Genres: History, Sports, Travel

Themes: East Anglian history, Horse racing, Sporting heritage, Local traditions, Cultural significance
