Synopsis: In 'The Great Mogul' by Tracy, Louis, a mysterious artifact is discovered, setting off a thrilling adventure that spans continents and centuries. As a group of unlikely allies races against time, they uncover hidden secrets and face dangerous adversaries, all while trying to unlock the true power of the artifact. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power of ancient artifacts, Unlikely alliances, Pursuit of knowledge
