Synopsis: In 'Turnover Point' by Alfred Coppel, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Martels embarks on a dangerous mission to a distant planet in order to uncover the truth behind a mysterious phenomenon that threatens the future of humanity. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters unexpected allies and enemies, Dr. Martels must confront his own fears and make difficult choices that could determine the fate of both Earth and the unknown world he explores.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Morality, Sacrifice
