Synopsis: 
In "Penny Nichols and the Knob Hill Mystery" by Clark, Joan, Penny Nichols, a young and ambitious journalist, finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and danger when she stumbles upon a mysterious disappearance in the affluent neighborhood of Knob Hill. As Penny delves deeper into the case, she uncovers a series of unexpected twists and turns that lead her to question the motives of those around her. With her determination and resourcefulness, Penny must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit and betrayal to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Determination
