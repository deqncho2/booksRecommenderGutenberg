Synopsis: 
'Gypsy Folk-Tales' by Francis Hindes Groome is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that delve into the mystical world of the Romani people. From ancient legends to whimsical anecdotes, this book takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the rich tapestry of Gypsy folklore. Each tale is filled with vivid imagery, colorful characters, and unexpected twists, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover the next captivating story. Whether it's a tale of love, adventure, or magic, 'Gypsy Folk-Tales' offers a delightful escape into a world where reality and myth intertwine.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural Studies

Themes: Romani culture, Magic and mysticism, Love and relationships
