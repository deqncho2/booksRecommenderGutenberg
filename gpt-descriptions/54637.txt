Synopsis: 

Step into the enchanting world of Cornwall as you embark on a journey through its rich history, traditions, and captivating folklore. In "Cornish Feasts and Folk-lore," Margaret Ann Courtney delves into the heart of this mystical land, uncovering tales of ancient rituals, mythical creatures, and mouthwatering feasts that have been passed down through generations. From the mysterious origins of the Cornish pasty to the legends of mermaids and giants that roam the rugged coastline, this book is a delightful exploration of the cultural tapestry that makes Cornwall truly unique. 

Genres: Folklore, History, Travel

Themes: Tradition, Mythology, Culinary Heritage
