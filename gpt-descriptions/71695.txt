Synopsis: 

In "Calvin Wilson Mateer, forty-five years a missionary in Shantung, China," author D.W. Fisher delves into the extraordinary life of Calvin Wilson Mateer, a dedicated missionary who spent nearly half a century in the remote region of Shantung, China. This captivating biography takes readers on a remarkable journey, chronicling Mateer's unwavering commitment to spreading the gospel, his encounters with the local community, and the challenges he faced in a foreign land. From his initial arrival in China to his enduring legacy, Fisher paints a vivid portrait of a man driven by faith and compassion, leaving readers inspired by his selfless devotion and the impact he made on the lives of countless individuals.

Genres: Biography, History, Religion

Themes: Missionary work, Faith, Cultural exchange
