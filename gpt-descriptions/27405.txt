Synopsis: 
'Ulf Van Yern, and Other Ballads' is a captivating collection of enchanting ballads that transport readers to a world of mythical creatures, epic battles, and forbidden love. From the haunting tale of Ulf Van Yern, a mysterious figure with a dark secret, to the epic ballads of heroes and heroines facing unimaginable challenges, this collection weaves together stories that will leave readers spellbound. Each ballad is a window into a different realm, filled with vivid imagery and lyrical storytelling that will ignite the imagination and keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Poetry, Folklore

Themes: 
Mythology, Love and Loss, Heroism, Fate and Destiny
