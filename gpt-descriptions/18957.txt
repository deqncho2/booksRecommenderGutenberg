Synopsis: In the small Irish village of Lisconnel, a mysterious group of strangers arrives, bringing with them secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to disrupt the peaceful community. As the villagers try to uncover the truth behind their arrival, they find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, love, and betrayal. With each passing day, tensions rise, and the once close-knit community begins to unravel. Will the villagers be able to uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Community
