Synopsis: 

In 'Ocean to Ocean on Horseback: Being the Story of a Tour in the Saddle from the Atlantic to the Pacific', Willard W. Glazier takes readers on an extraordinary journey across the United States, from the Atlantic Ocean to the Pacific. This captivating travelogue delves into the early history and development of cities and towns along the route, providing a fascinating glimpse into the growth of these regions beyond the Mississippi. Along the way, Glazier shares a multitude of thrilling incidents, intriguing anecdotes, and daring adventures that he encounters during his remarkable expedition.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, History

Themes: Exploration, American Frontier, Manifest Destiny
