Synopsis: In 'The Light of the Star: A Novel' by Garland, Hamlin, a young woman named Dorothy embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure. When she inherits a mysterious family heirloom, Dorothy's life takes an unexpected turn as she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden treasures. As she unravels the enigma surrounding the heirloom, Dorothy finds herself facing danger, romance, and the allure of the unknown. Set against the backdrop of the early 20th century, this captivating novel explores the transformative power of love, the pursuit of dreams, and the enduring quest for personal fulfillment.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Family secrets, Love and romance, Pursuit of dreams
