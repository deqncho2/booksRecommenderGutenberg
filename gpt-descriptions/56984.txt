Synopsis: In the picturesque countryside of Sussex, a fierce battle unfolds between two siblings, Reuben and Rachael, as they navigate the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations. Set against the backdrop of the early 20th century, this gripping tale explores the depths of human emotions and the lengths one will go to protect their own happiness. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, the siblings find themselves entangled in a web of passion, betrayal, and redemption that will leave readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, family drama, romance

Themes: Love and desire, family bonds, societal expectations, sacrifice
