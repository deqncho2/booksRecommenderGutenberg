Synopsis: In "Joan of Arc, the Warrior Maid" by Madison, Lucy Foster, follow the incredible journey of a young peasant girl who defies societal norms and rises to become a legendary figure in history. Joan, driven by her unwavering faith and determination, embarks on a mission that will test her courage, loyalty, and resilience. As she navigates the treacherous world of war and politics, Joan's unwavering spirit and unyielding belief in her divine purpose inspire those around her and challenge the very fabric of society. This captivating tale explores the extraordinary life of Joan of Arc, a true icon of strength and bravery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biographical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Faith and Spirituality, Gender Roles and Expectations, Heroism and Sacrifice
