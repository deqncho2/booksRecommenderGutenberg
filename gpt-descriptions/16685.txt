Synopsis: Private Peat is a gripping and deeply personal account of one soldier's harrowing experiences during World War I. Harold Reginald Peat, the author and protagonist, takes us on a journey through the trenches, vividly describing the horrors of war and the toll it takes on the human spirit. From the camaraderie among soldiers to the devastating impact of loss, Private Peat offers a raw and unflinching portrayal of the realities of battle, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: War and its effects on individuals, camaraderie and friendship in times of adversity, the human spirit's resilience in the face of trauma
