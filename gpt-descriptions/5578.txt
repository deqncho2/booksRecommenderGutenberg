Synopsis: In 'The Burgomaster's Wife - Volume 01', set in 17th century Holland, we are introduced to a young woman named Maria, who finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love. As the wife of the powerful burgomaster, Maria must navigate the treacherous waters of a society filled with secrets and hidden agendas. With her husband's life at stake, Maria must make difficult choices that will not only determine her own fate but also the fate of those she holds dear. As the plot unfolds, the reader is drawn into a world of suspense, romance, and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Power and Politics, Forbidden Love, Betrayal, Secrets and Intrigue
