Synopsis: 

'An Introduction to the Study of Meteorites: With a List of the Meteorites Represented in the Collection' takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of meteorites. Written by the British Museum (Natural History)'s Department of Mineralogy, this book provides a comprehensive overview of the study of meteorites, their origins, and their significance in understanding the universe. It also includes a detailed list of the meteorites represented in the museum's collection, offering a glimpse into the incredible diversity of these extraterrestrial objects. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Astronomy

Themes: Extraterrestrial objects, Origins of the universe, Scientific exploration
