Synopsis: 

"A Collection of State-papers, Relative to the First Acknowledgment of the Sovereignty of the United States of America, and the Reception of Their Minister Plenipotentiary, by Their High Mightinesses the States General of the United Netherlands" is a historical document that delves into the fascinating events surrounding the initial recognition of the United States of America as a sovereign nation by the United Netherlands. It provides a comprehensive account of the diplomatic efforts and negotiations that took place, shedding light on the political intricacies of the time. The book also includes an essay on Canon and Feudal Law, which explores the legal and societal systems prevalent during that era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political literature

Themes: Diplomacy, Sovereignty, International relations, Legal systems
