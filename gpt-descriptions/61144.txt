Synopsis: 

'From the Black Mountain to Waziristan' takes readers on a captivating journey through the border countries and the turbulent tribes controlled by the North-West Frontier Province. Written by H. C. Wylly, this gripping account delves into the military relations between these regions and the British Empire in the past. With vivid descriptions and firsthand experiences, the book offers a fascinating exploration of the challenges, conflicts, and alliances that shaped this volatile region.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Military

Themes: Borderlands, Tribal dynamics, Colonialism, Military strategy
