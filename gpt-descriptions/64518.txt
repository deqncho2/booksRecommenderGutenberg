Synopsis: 
In 'Short Stories. Early October, 1923', the reader is transported to a small town in the early 20th century, where a series of interconnected tales unfold. From a mysterious disappearance to a forbidden love affair, each story delves into the lives of the town's inhabitants, revealing secrets, desires, and the complexities of human nature. As the month progresses, the characters' lives intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to surprising and poignant revelations.

Genres: 
Mystery, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: 
Secrets and hidden truths, Love and desire, Interconnectedness of lives
