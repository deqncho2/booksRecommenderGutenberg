Synopsis: In "The Guns of Shiloh: A Story of the Great Western Campaign," Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on a gripping journey through the American Civil War. Set during the Battle of Shiloh, the story follows a young soldier named Harry Kenton as he navigates the chaos and brutality of war. As the battle rages on, Harry finds himself torn between loyalty to his comrades and his own personal beliefs. With vivid descriptions and intense action, Altsheler paints a vivid picture of the horrors and heroism of war, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Sacrifice, Coming of Age
