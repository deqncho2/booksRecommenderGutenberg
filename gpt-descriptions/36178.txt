Synopsis: In "The Little Dog Trusty; The Orange Man; and the Cherry Orchard," young readers are taken on a delightful journey through the adventures of a loyal little dog named Trusty. Along the way, they encounter an intriguing Orange Man and explore the enchanting Cherry Orchard. This tenth part of Early Lessons by Maria Edgeworth is filled with excitement, mystery, and valuable life lessons that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Moral tale

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Curiosity, Morality
