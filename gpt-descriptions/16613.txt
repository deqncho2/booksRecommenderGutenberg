Synopsis: In "Bolshevism: The Enemy of Political and Industrial Democracy," John Spargo delves into the heart of one of the most controversial political ideologies of the 20th century. Through meticulous research and insightful analysis, Spargo explores the origins, principles, and consequences of Bolshevism, shedding light on its impact on political and industrial democracy. With a captivating narrative, the author uncovers the hidden truths behind this radical movement, challenging conventional wisdom and offering a fresh perspective on its implications for society. This thought-provoking exploration is a must-read for anyone seeking a deeper understanding of the forces that shaped the modern world.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Science

Themes: Ideology, Democracy, Revolution, Socialism
