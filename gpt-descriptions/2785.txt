Synopsis: In "The Elusive Pimpernel" by Baroness Orczy, a daring and enigmatic hero known as the Scarlet Pimpernel continues his secret mission to rescue French aristocrats from the clutches of the guillotine during the French Revolution. As the French government becomes increasingly determined to capture and unmask the elusive Pimpernel, the stakes are raised, and the tension mounts. With danger lurking at every turn, the fate of countless lives hangs in the balance, and the Pimpernel's true identity remains a mystery that keeps readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Identity, Revolution
