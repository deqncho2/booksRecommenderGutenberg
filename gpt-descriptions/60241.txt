Synopsis: In 'Sheaves' by E. F. Benson, a young woman named Mary inherits a picturesque country estate from her late uncle. Excited to start a new chapter in her life, she soon discovers that the house holds a dark secret that threatens to unravel her sanity. As Mary delves deeper into the mysteries of the estate, she becomes entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and eerie encounters. With each passing day, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leaving Mary questioning her own sanity and the true nature of the house she now calls home.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural thriller, mystery

Themes: Haunting, psychological suspense, inheritance, the power of the past
