Synopsis: In 'A Woman In China' by Mary Gaunt, a young woman embarks on a journey to China, immersing herself in a captivating world of mystery and cultural exploration. As she navigates the complexities of a foreign land, she encounters unexpected friendships, dangerous secrets, and a love that defies all boundaries. With vivid descriptions and a compelling narrative, this novel takes readers on an unforgettable adventure through the heart of China.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Cultural exploration, Identity, Forbidden love
