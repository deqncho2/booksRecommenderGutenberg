Synopsis: 
'Contemporary Belgian Poetry: Selected and Translated by Jethro Bithell' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and diverse landscape of Belgian poetry. This anthology, carefully curated by Jethro Bithell, introduces a collection of powerful and evocative poems that explore the depths of human emotions, the complexities of love and loss, and the profound connections between nature and the human experience. From hauntingly beautiful verses to thought-provoking and introspective pieces, this anthology offers a glimpse into the vibrant world of Belgian poetry, inviting readers to immerse themselves in the lyrical beauty and profound insights of these talented poets.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology
Themes: Human emotions, Love and loss, Nature, Reflection
