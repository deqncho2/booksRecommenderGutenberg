Synopsis: 
In 'The Cuckoo Clock' by Molesworth, Mrs., we are introduced to a young girl named Griselda who receives a mysterious gift - a cuckoo clock that seems to have a life of its own. As Griselda delves into the secrets of the clock, she embarks on a whimsical and enchanting adventure that takes her to unexpected places and introduces her to peculiar characters. With each passing hour, the cuckoo clock reveals more about its magical powers, leading Griselda on a journey of self-discovery and bravery. 

Genres: 
Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: 
Magic, Friendship, Self-discovery
