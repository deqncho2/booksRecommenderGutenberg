Synopsis: In 'Shenanigans at Sugar Creek' by Hutchens, Paul, a group of adventurous kids find themselves caught up in a series of mysterious events that unfold in their small town of Sugar Creek. As they embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth, they encounter hidden treasures, secret codes, and unexpected allies. With each twist and turn, the children must rely on their wit, courage, and teamwork to solve the puzzle and bring justice to their community. Will they succeed in unraveling the shenanigans that have plagued Sugar Creek?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving, Community
