Synopsis: In 'Our Little French Cousin', we follow the enchanting journey of Jeanne, a young girl living in the picturesque French countryside. Through her eyes, we discover the beauty of French culture, from the charming villages to the bustling markets. Jeanne's adventures take her to Paris, where she encounters the wonders of the city, including the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum. Along the way, she learns about the importance of family, friendship, and embracing one's heritage. This heartwarming tale is filled with delightful surprises and captures the essence of French life.

Genres: Children's Literature, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cultural exploration, Family bonds, Friendship, Heritage
