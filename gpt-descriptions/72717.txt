Synopsis: In 'The Illiterate Digest' by Will Rogers, embark on a hilarious journey through the wit and wisdom of one of America's most beloved humorists. With his signature charm and sharp observations, Rogers takes readers on a rollicking ride through a wide range of topics, from politics and society to sports and entertainment. Through his unique perspective, he sheds light on the absurdities of everyday life, leaving readers laughing and pondering in equal measure.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Essays

Themes: Social commentary, American culture, Politics, Entertainment
