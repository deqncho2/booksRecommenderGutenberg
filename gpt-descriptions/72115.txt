Synopsis: 
In "Dogs as Home Companions," author A. F. Hochwalt delves into the fascinating world of dogs and their unique bond with humans. This captivating book explores the various roles dogs play in our lives, from loyal companions to therapy animals, and everything in between. Through heartwarming anecdotes and insightful research, Hochwalt uncovers the profound impact dogs have on our emotional well-being and the ways they enrich our daily lives. Whether you're a seasoned dog owner or simply a dog lover, this book is sure to deepen your understanding and appreciation for these incredible creatures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Studies, Pet Care

Themes: Human-animal bond, Emotional support, Pet ownership
