Synopsis: In 'The Junior Trophy' by Ralph Henry Barbour, a group of ambitious young athletes compete for the coveted junior trophy in a thrilling sports competition. As the competition heats up, friendships are tested, rivalries emerge, and unexpected obstacles challenge the participants' determination. With high stakes and intense rivalries, the athletes must push themselves to their limits to achieve their dreams and claim the ultimate prize.

Genres: Sports fiction, Young adult fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Competition, Friendship, Ambition, Perseverance
