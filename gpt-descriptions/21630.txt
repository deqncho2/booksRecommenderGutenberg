Synopsis: In 'Bibliomania in the Middle Ages' by F. Somner Merryweather, embark on a captivating journey through the medieval world of book lovers, where passion for literature knows no bounds. Follow the adventures of a young scholar as he uncovers hidden secrets, encounters eccentric characters, and delves into the mysterious realm of bibliomania. From ancient manuscripts to forbidden libraries, this enthralling tale will transport you to a time when books held immense power and ignited the imagination of all who dared to seek their treasures.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Love for books, Obsession, Knowledge and Power
