Synopsis: In 'Bert Wilson at the Wheel' by J. W. Duffield, follow the thrilling adventures of Bert Wilson as he embarks on a daring journey behind the wheel of a powerful automobile. From high-speed chases to unexpected encounters, Bert finds himself caught up in a web of mystery and danger. As he navigates treacherous roads and faces formidable adversaries, Bert's courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles in his path and emerge victorious?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Courage, Resourcefulness, Perseverance
