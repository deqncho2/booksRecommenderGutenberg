Synopsis: 

In "History of the Late Polish Revolution and the Events of the Campaign" by Joseph Hordynski, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of the Polish Revolution. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous historical research, Hordynski brings to life the gripping events and key figures that shaped this pivotal moment in Polish history. From the initial sparks of rebellion to the intense battles and political maneuverings, this book offers a comprehensive and enthralling account of the revolution and its aftermath. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political history, Military history

Themes: Revolution, National identity, Political upheaval, Heroism, Betrayal
