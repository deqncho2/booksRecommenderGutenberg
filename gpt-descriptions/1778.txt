Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Athens, a group of lovers, fairies, and amateur actors find themselves entangled in a whimsical tale of mistaken identities, magical spells, and mischievous antics. As the moonlit night unfolds, their lives become intertwined in a web of love, desire, and hilarious misunderstandings. With the mischievous Puck at the helm, chaos ensues, leading to a night filled with laughter, romance, and unexpected surprises.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Love and Desire, Illusion and Reality, Order and Chaos, Transformation and Metamorphosis
