Synopsis: In "A Thin Ghost and Others," James, M. R. presents a collection of chilling tales that will send shivers down your spine. From haunted houses to eerie encounters with the supernatural, each story is crafted with meticulous detail and a sense of mounting dread. As you delve into the pages, you'll find yourself drawn into a world where the line between the living and the dead becomes blurred, and where the darkest secrets of the past refuse to stay buried. Prepare to be captivated by these haunting tales that will leave you questioning what lies beyond the realm of the living.

Genres: Horror, Gothic fiction, Supernatural fiction

Themes: Ghosts, Hauntings, The supernatural, Secrets and mysteries, Fear and dread
