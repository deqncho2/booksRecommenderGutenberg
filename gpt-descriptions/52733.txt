Synopsis: In 'Denounced: A Romance' by Bloundelle-Burton, John, a forbidden love blossoms amidst the backdrop of a society filled with secrets and scandal. As the story unfolds, the protagonists find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a world where love and loyalty are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Betrayal, Society's expectations
