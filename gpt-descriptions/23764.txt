Synopsis: In 'The Bramble Bush' by Garrett, Randall, a brilliant and unconventional lawyer finds himself entangled in a complex murder case that challenges his beliefs and forces him to confront his own demons. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers shocking secrets and faces a moral dilemma that could jeopardize everything he holds dear. With suspenseful twists and thought-provoking revelations, this gripping legal thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Psychological drama

Themes: Morality, Justice, Redemption
