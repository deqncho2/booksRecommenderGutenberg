Synopsis: 
'The Second Class Passenger: Fifteen Stories' by Gibbon, Perceval is a collection of captivating and thought-provoking tales that delve into the lives of various individuals traveling together in the second class of a train. Each story presents a unique and intriguing character, their personal struggles, desires, and the unexpected connections they form during their journey. From a mysterious woman with a hidden past to a disillusioned artist seeking inspiration, these narratives intertwine to create a rich tapestry of human experiences, exploring themes of identity, love, loss, and the power of human connection.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Literary Fiction

Themes: Identity, Love, Loss, Human Connection
