Synopsis: 
'Round the Sofa' by Elizabeth Gaskell is a collection of short stories that takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of various characters who gather around a sofa to share their tales. From mysterious secrets to unexpected twists, each story offers a unique glimpse into the human experience, leaving readers eager to uncover the next surprising revelation.

Genres: 
Short stories, Mystery, Drama

Themes: 
Human connection, Secrets and revelations, Unexpected encounters
