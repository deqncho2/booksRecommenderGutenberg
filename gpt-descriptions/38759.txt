Synopsis: 
'Next Door Neighbours: A Comedy; In Three Acts' is a delightful and humorous play that revolves around the lives of two neighboring families. The story follows the comical misunderstandings, miscommunications, and mishaps that occur when the families become entangled in a series of amusing situations. As the neighbors navigate their daily lives, their interactions lead to uproarious laughter and unexpected surprises. With witty dialogue and clever plot twists, this comedy explores the hilarious consequences of living in close proximity to one another.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Neighborly relations, Miscommunication, Social satire
