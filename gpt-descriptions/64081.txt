Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Morley Ashton: A Story of the Sea', the captivating tale of adventure and romance continues as Morley Ashton finds himself entangled in a web of treachery and danger. Set against the backdrop of the vast and unpredictable sea, Morley must navigate through perilous waters, facing both external threats and internal conflicts. As secrets are revealed and alliances are tested, the stakes are raised, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of our courageous protagonist.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Betrayal, Survival, Love and Sacrifice
