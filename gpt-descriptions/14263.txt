Synopsis: In 'Katrine: A Novel' by Lane, Elinor Macartney, a young woman named Katrine finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits a mysterious estate in the Scottish Highlands. As she delves into the history of her family and the estate, she uncovers long-buried secrets that threaten to unravel her own life. With the help of a charming local historian, Katrine must navigate the treacherous waters of her family's past and confront the dark forces that still haunt the present.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and betrayal
