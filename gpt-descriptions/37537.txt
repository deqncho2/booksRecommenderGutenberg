Synopsis: 
'San Francisco in Ruins: A Pictorial History of Eight Score Photo-Views of the Earthquake Effects, Flames' Havoc, Ruins Everywhere, Relief Camps' takes readers on a visual journey through the aftermath of the devastating 1906 San Francisco earthquake. Through a collection of powerful photographs, this book captures the sheer destruction, chaos, and resilience of a city in ruins. From the haunting images of collapsed buildings and raging fires to the scenes of relief camps and the human spirit's triumph, this pictorial history offers a poignant and captivating glimpse into a defining moment in San Francisco's history.

Genres: Historical, Photography, Disaster

Themes: Destruction and Resilience, Human Spirit, Historical Tragedy
