Synopsis: In 'Overdue: The Story of a Missing Ship' by Collingwood, Harry, a renowned maritime historian delves into the mysterious disappearance of a ship that vanished without a trace. As he uncovers clues and interviews survivors, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and conspiracy that reaches far beyond the missing vessel. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling and unexpected conclusion that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Disappearance, Conspiracy, Secrets
