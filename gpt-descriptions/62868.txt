Synopsis: 

'Oriental tales, for the entertainment of youth: Selected from the most eminent English writers' is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that transport readers to the mystical and vibrant world of the Orient. Written by an anonymous author, this anthology features a selection of tales from renowned English writers, each filled with adventure, romance, and intrigue. From the bustling markets of ancient Persia to the serene gardens of Japan, readers will embark on a journey through exotic lands, encountering captivating characters and experiencing the wonders of Eastern culture. With each tale offering a unique and mesmerizing narrative, this collection promises to captivate readers of all ages, leaving them yearning for more tales from the Orient.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration of Eastern cultures, Quest for knowledge and self-discovery, Love and desire, Mysticism and magic
