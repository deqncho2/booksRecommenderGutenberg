Synopsis: 

Step into a world where magic and enchantment intertwine with the ordinary, where talking animals and mystical creatures roam freely. 'Household Tales by Brothers Grimm' is a captivating collection of timeless stories that will transport readers to far-off lands filled with adventure, danger, and unexpected twists. From the classic tales of Cinderella and Snow White to lesser-known gems like The Twelve Dancing Princesses and The Frog Prince, this anthology is a treasure trove of wonder and imagination. Prepare to be spellbound as you journey through these enchanting tales, where heroes and heroines face trials, triumph over adversity, and discover the true power of love and courage.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Transformation, Morality, Love and Courage
