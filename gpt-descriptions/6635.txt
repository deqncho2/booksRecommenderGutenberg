Synopsis: In the heart of a bustling city, a close-knit community of working-class families resides on Billy-Goat Hill. Among them is a young couple, Jenny and Bud, who dream of a better life beyond their humble surroundings. As they navigate the challenges of love, friendship, and societal expectations, they find themselves caught between the allure of ambition and the comfort of their roots. With the hill as their backdrop, their journey unfolds, revealing the power of hope, resilience, and the transformative nature of love.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social class, Ambition, Love and relationships, Community, Identity
