Synopsis: 
In 'The Gambler', we follow the story of Alexei Ivanovich, a young tutor who finds himself entangled in the world of gambling. As he becomes consumed by the thrill and obsession of risking everything, he navigates a treacherous path filled with love, betrayal, and the constant battle between fortune and misfortune. With high stakes and unexpected turns, this gripping tale explores the depths of human nature and the consequences of our desires.

Genres: 
Psychological fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: 
Addiction, Fate vs. Free Will, Love and Betrayal
