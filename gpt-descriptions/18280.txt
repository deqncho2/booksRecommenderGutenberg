Synopsis: 
In 'Enter Bridget' by Cobb, Thomas, a mysterious woman named Bridget arrives in a small town, capturing the attention and curiosity of its residents. As she becomes entangled in the lives of the locals, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the town's peaceful facade begins to crumble. With each passing day, Bridget's presence brings both hope and despair, leaving everyone wondering who she really is and what she truly wants. This gripping tale of intrigue and suspense will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Betrayal, Community
