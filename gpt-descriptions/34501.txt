Synopsis: 

In 'Wolf and Coyote Trapping: An Up-to-Date Wolf Hunter's Guide', A. R. Harding presents a comprehensive and insightful guide for those seeking to hunt and trap wolves and coyotes. Drawing from the expertise of experienced "wolfers," this book offers the most successful methods and techniques to effectively track, hunt, and trap these elusive animals. Alongside practical advice, Harding delves into the detailed habits and behaviors of wolves, providing readers with a deeper understanding of their prey. Whether you are an experienced hunter or a novice in the field, this guide is sure to captivate your interest and equip you with the knowledge needed to become a skilled wolf hunter.

Genres: Non-fiction, Outdoor and Wildlife, Hunting

Themes: Hunting techniques, Animal behavior, Wildlife conservation
