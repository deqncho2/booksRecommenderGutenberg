Synopsis:
In 'Crowded Out! and Other Sketches', Susie Frances Harrison takes readers on a delightful journey through a collection of charming and humorous sketches. From the misadventures of a young girl trying to find her place in a bustling city to the comical encounters of eccentric characters, each sketch offers a glimpse into the quirks and idiosyncrasies of everyday life. With her keen observations and witty storytelling, Harrison captures the essence of human nature and the absurdities of the world around us. Prepare to be entertained and amused by this delightful collection of sketches that will leave you with a smile on your face.

Genres: Humor, Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Everyday life, Human nature, Comedy
