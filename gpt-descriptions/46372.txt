Synopsis: 

Ocean's Story; or, Triumphs of Thirty Centuries: Maritime Adventures, Achievements, Explorations, Discoveries and Inventions; and of the Rise and Progress of Ship-Building and Ocean Navigation, from the Ark to the Iron Steamships is an enthralling journey through the history of maritime exploration and ship-building. From ancient times to the modern era, this book takes readers on a captivating voyage, uncovering the remarkable adventures, groundbreaking achievements, and fascinating discoveries that have shaped our understanding of the world's oceans.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Human ingenuity and innovation, Evolution of maritime technology
