Synopsis:
In 'Colonel Thorndyke's Secret', the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the mysterious world of espionage and international intrigue. Set against the backdrop of the Napoleonic Wars, the story follows the adventures of Colonel Thorndyke, a British officer with a hidden past. As he is assigned a dangerous mission to uncover a traitor within the ranks, Thorndyke must navigate a web of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the stakes grow higher, and the Colonel's secret becomes increasingly perilous. Will he be able to outwit his enemies and protect his country, or will his past catch up with him in the most dangerous way?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Espionage

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity, Patriotism
