Synopsis: 

In 'Report on the Migration of Birds in the Spring and Autumn of 1881. Third Report', a team of dedicated naturalists embark on a fascinating journey to document the awe-inspiring phenomenon of bird migration. Led by renowned ornithologists Cordeaux, Harvie-Brown, Kermode, Barrington, and More, this report takes readers on a captivating exploration of the birds' migratory patterns during the spring and autumn of 1881. From the intricate details of their flight paths to the challenges they face along the way, this meticulously researched report offers a unique glimpse into the world of avian migration.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Science

Themes: Migration, Ornithology, Natural History
