Synopsis: 

In 'Sarchedon: A Legend of the Great Queen', the reader is transported to ancient Assyria, where power, love, and betrayal intertwine in a captivating tale. The story follows the life of Sarchedon, a noble warrior who finds himself torn between his loyalty to the Great Queen Semiramis and his forbidden love for a beautiful captive. As political intrigue and war threaten to tear the empire apart, Sarchedon must navigate treacherous waters and make choices that will shape his destiny. With vivid descriptions of ancient civilizations, epic battles, and complex characters, this historical adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Betrayal, Loyalty and Duty
