Synopsis: In 'The Firing Line' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist discovers a groundbreaking invention that could change the course of warfare forever. As he navigates the treacherous waters of military politics and international espionage, he must decide whether to use his creation for the greater good or succumb to the temptations of power and destruction. With high-stakes action and unexpected twists, this gripping novel explores the moral dilemmas faced by those who hold the keys to technological advancements that could shape the world. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Political Drama

Themes: Ethics, Power, Consequences of Technological Advancements
