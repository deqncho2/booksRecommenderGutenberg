Synopsis: 

'Crusaders of New France: A Chronicle of the Fleur-de-Lis in the Wilderness' takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed lands of early North America. Set during the era of French colonization, this historical chronicle follows the lives of brave explorers, missionaries, and settlers who ventured into the wilderness in search of new opportunities and to spread their culture. From the bustling streets of Quebec to the vast expanses of the Great Lakes, the book weaves together tales of adventure, conflict, and resilience, showcasing the indomitable spirit of those who dared to forge a new world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Colonization, Cultural Identity, Resilience
