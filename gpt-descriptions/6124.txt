Synopsis:
Pamela, a young and virtuous maid, finds herself caught in a web of intrigue when her employer, Mr. B, becomes infatuated with her. As she navigates the treacherous waters of his advances, Pamela must rely on her wit, intelligence, and unwavering moral compass to protect her virtue and secure her future. Through a series of letters, Richardson weaves a tale of suspense, romance, and the triumph of virtue in the face of adversity.

Genres:
Epistolary novel, Romance, Psychological fiction

Themes:
Virtue and morality, Gender and power dynamics, Social class and hierarchy
