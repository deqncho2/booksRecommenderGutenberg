Synopsis: 
In 'To Leeward' by F. Marion Crawford, a young American artist named Paul Griggs finds himself on a journey that takes him from the bustling streets of New York City to the enchanting landscapes of Italy. Drawn into a web of mystery and intrigue, Paul becomes entangled with a captivating woman who holds a dark secret. As he delves deeper into her world, he must confront his own desires and the consequences of his actions. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, 'To Leeward' is a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of truth.

Genres: 
Romance, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: 
Love and passion, Deception and betrayal, Identity and self-discovery
