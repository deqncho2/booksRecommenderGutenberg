Synopsis: In the Days of Washington: A Story of the American Revolution takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. Set against the backdrop of the birth of a nation, this historical novel follows the life of a young protagonist who becomes entangled in the fight for freedom. From the early battles to the forging of alliances, the story weaves together personal struggles, political intrigue, and the sacrifices made by ordinary individuals in the pursuit of liberty. As the protagonist navigates the challenges of war, they must grapple with loyalty, honor, and the weight of their decisions in a time of great uncertainty.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Loyalty, Freedom
