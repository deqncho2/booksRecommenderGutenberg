Synopsis: 
In 'The Olden Time Series, Vol. 3: New-England Sunday', author Henry M. Brooks takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical newspapers of Boston and Salem, Massachusetts. Through a collection of fascinating gleanings, the author uncovers the unique and often surprising stories that unfolded on Sundays in New England during the early days of the nation. From religious controversies to scandalous events, this volume offers a glimpse into the lives and customs of the people who shaped the region's history. Prepare to be enthralled by the intriguing tales that lie within the pages of this remarkable collection.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Newspaper Archives, New England History

Themes: Religious Controversy, Social Customs, Early American Life
