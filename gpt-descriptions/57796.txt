Synopsis: In the May 9, 1882 edition of Harper's Young People, embark on a thrilling adventure through the pages of this illustrated weekly. Join a group of courageous young explorers as they journey to uncharted lands, encounter mysterious creatures, and unravel ancient secrets. From heart-pounding escapades to heartwarming tales, this captivating collection of stories will transport readers to a world filled with wonder and excitement.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Courage, Friendship
