Synopsis: 
In 'The Motor Boat Club at Nantucket; or, The Mystery of the Dunstan Heir', a group of adventurous young friends find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery while on a summer vacation in Nantucket. As they explore the island's picturesque landscapes and navigate the open waters on their motorboat, they stumble upon a perplexing secret that could change the course of someone's life forever. With danger lurking around every corner, the Motor Boat Club must use their wits and bravery to unravel the enigma and bring justice to those involved. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
