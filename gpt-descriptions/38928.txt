Synopsis: 

In 'Meteorology; or, Weather Explained', J. G. M'Pherson takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of weather. From the awe-inspiring power of thunderstorms to the delicate intricacies of cloud formations, this book unravels the mysteries of meteorology in an engaging and accessible manner. M'Pherson's vivid descriptions and insightful explanations will leave readers with a newfound appreciation for the forces that shape our daily lives. Whether you're a weather enthusiast or simply curious about the science behind the skies, this book is sure to captivate and enlighten.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Weather

Themes: Nature's power, Scientific exploration
