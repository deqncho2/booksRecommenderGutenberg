Synopsis: 
'Raemaekers' Cartoons: With Accompanying Notes by Well-known English Writers' is a captivating collection of political cartoons by Louis Raemaekers, accompanied by insightful commentary from prominent English writers. This thought-provoking compilation offers a powerful visual narrative that delves into the heart of the political landscape during World War I. Through his poignant illustrations, Raemaekers sheds light on the horrors of war, the impact of propaganda, and the resilience of the human spirit. The accompanying notes provide a deeper understanding of the historical context and the significance of each cartoon, making this book a compelling exploration of the power of art and its ability to shape public opinion.

Genres: Political commentary, Art, History

Themes: War, Propaganda, Human resilience
