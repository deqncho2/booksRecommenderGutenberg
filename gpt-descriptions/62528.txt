Synopsis: 
In 'To a Youth at School' by the Religious Tract Society, we follow the journey of a young student as he navigates the challenges and temptations of school life. From the pressures of academic success to the allure of peer influence, the protagonist must make difficult choices that will shape his future. As he grapples with moral dilemmas and seeks guidance, the story takes us on a thought-provoking exploration of the importance of character, integrity, and the pursuit of true wisdom.

Genres: 
Educational, Inspirational, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Moral development, Peer pressure, Personal growth
