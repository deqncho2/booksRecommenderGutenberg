Synopsis: 

In 'Quilts: Their Story and How to Make Them', Marie D. Webster takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and artistry of quilting. From the humble beginnings of quilting to the intricate designs and techniques used today, this book delves into the fascinating stories behind these cherished fabric creations. With detailed instructions and step-by-step guides, Webster also provides aspiring quilters with the tools and knowledge to create their own beautiful quilts. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Crafts

Themes: Artistry, Tradition, Creativity, Heritage
