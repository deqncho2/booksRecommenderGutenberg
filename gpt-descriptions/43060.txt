Synopsis: 

In the 11th edition of the renowned Encyclopaedia Britannica, Volume 17, Slice 3 takes readers on an intriguing journey from the life and presidency of William McKinley to the captivating realm of terrestrial magnetism. This volume delves into the fascinating details of McKinley's political career, his impact on American society, and the tragic events that marked his presidency. Moving on, it explores the enigmatic forces of terrestrial magnetism, unraveling the mysteries of Earth's magnetic field and its influence on various aspects of our planet. With a wealth of knowledge and captivating narratives, this volume offers a captivating exploration of history and science.

Genres: Reference, History, Science

Themes: Politics, Leadership, Geophysics, Natural Phenomena
