Synopsis: In 'The Blue Fairy Book' by Andrew Lang, embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales from around the world. From magical creatures and brave heroes to treacherous villains and unexpected twists, this book offers a delightful blend of fantasy, adventure, and wonder. Discover the secrets of a mysterious blue fairy and immerse yourself in a world where imagination knows no bounds. 

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Heroism, Morality, Transformation
