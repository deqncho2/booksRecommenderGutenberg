Synopsis: In "Over the Teacups," Holmes, Oliver Wendell takes readers on a delightful journey through his musings and observations during his regular teatime conversations with friends. From discussions on love and marriage to reflections on the passing of time and the pursuit of happiness, Holmes offers witty and insightful anecdotes that will captivate readers and leave them pondering life's deeper questions.

Genres: Essay, Memoir, Philosophy

Themes: Love, Marriage, Time, Happiness, Reflection
