Synopsis: In 'A Series of Lessons in Gnani Yoga: The Yoga of Wisdom', Atkinson takes readers on a transformative journey through the ancient practice of Gnani Yoga. Through a series of insightful lessons, he explores the profound wisdom and practical techniques that can help individuals unlock their true potential and achieve spiritual enlightenment. From understanding the power of thought to harnessing the energy of the mind, Atkinson's teachings offer a profound exploration of the human consciousness and its connection to the divine. This enlightening book is a must-read for those seeking to deepen their understanding of yoga and embark on a path of self-discovery and inner growth.

Genres: Self-help, Philosophy, Spirituality

Themes: Yoga, Wisdom, Self-realization, Mindfulness, Spiritual Enlightenment
