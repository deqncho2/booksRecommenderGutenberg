Synopsis: 

'Eleven Possible Cases' is a captivating collection of mystery stories written by a group of talented authors including Stockton, Frank R., Green, Anna Katharine, Thompson, Maurice, Munroe, Kirk, Harland, Henry, Miller, Joaquin, Fawcett, Edgar, Fyles, Franklin, Lockwood, Ingersoll, Smith, Brainard Gardner, and Wheeler, A. C. (Andrew Carpenter). Each story presents a unique and puzzling case that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly trying to unravel the mysteries alongside the brilliant detectives and investigators. From stolen jewels to unexplained disappearances, these stories offer a thrilling journey into the world of crime and intrigue.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Intrigue, Investigation, Puzzle-solving
