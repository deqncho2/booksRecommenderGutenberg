Synopsis: In 'The Moving Picture Girls at Rocky Ranch: Or, Great Days Among the Cowboys' by Laura Lee Hope, the adventurous and talented Moving Picture Girls find themselves in the heart of the Wild West. As they immerse themselves in the world of cowboys, cattle drives, and rodeos, they uncover a mysterious secret that puts their lives in danger. With their quick thinking and resourcefulness, the girls must navigate the treacherous landscape of the ranch while capturing the thrilling moments on film. Will they be able to solve the mystery and make it out unscathed?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Western

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
