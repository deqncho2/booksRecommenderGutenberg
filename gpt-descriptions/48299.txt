Synopsis: In 'The Story of a Hare' by J. C. Tregarthen, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the eyes of a curious and adventurous hare. This heartwarming tale follows the hare as it encounters various challenges and unexpected friendships, ultimately leading to a profound realization about the true meaning of freedom and belonging.

Genres: Children's Literature, Animal Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Freedom, Friendship, Self-discovery
