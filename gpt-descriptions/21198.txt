Synopsis: 
In 'Anecdotes of Painters, Engravers, Sculptors and Architects, and Curiosities of Art (Vol. 2 of 3)', Spooner, Shearjashub takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and works of renowned artists. From the eccentricities of Michelangelo to the mysterious disappearances of famous sculptures, this volume is filled with intriguing tales that shed light on the fascinating world of art. Delve into the secrets behind famous masterpieces, uncover the rivalries between artistic geniuses, and explore the untold stories of the creative minds that shaped the art world. With each anecdote, readers will be left craving for more captivating tales from the realm of art.

Genres: Biography, Art History, Anecdotes

Themes: Creativity, Eccentricity, Rivalry, Artistic Expression
