Synopsis: 

In "State of the Union Addresses (1790-2006)," readers are taken on a captivating journey through the history of the United States as seen through the eyes of its presidents. This remarkable collection brings together the powerful and influential speeches delivered by each president during their annual State of the Union addresses. From the founding fathers to modern-day leaders, this anthology offers a unique perspective on the nation's progress, challenges, and aspirations over more than two centuries. 

Genres: Historical, Political, Speeches

Themes: American history, Leadership, National progress, Challenges and aspirations
