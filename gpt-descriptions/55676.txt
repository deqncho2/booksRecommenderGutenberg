Synopsis: 
In "Told by Uncle Remus: New Stories of the Old Plantation," Joel Chandler Harris takes readers on a captivating journey back to the enchanting world of Uncle Remus and his beloved animal friends. Set in the old plantation, this collection of new stories weaves together heartwarming tales of adventure, wisdom, and the enduring power of friendship. From Brer Rabbit's mischievous escapades to Brer Fox's cunning schemes, each story is filled with humor, suspense, and valuable life lessons that will leave readers both entertained and inspired.

Genres: 
Fiction, Folklore, Children's Literature

Themes: 
Friendship, Wisdom, Morality, Adventure
