Synopsis: In 'Chickamauga. Useless, Disastrous Battle', Smith D. Atkins takes readers on a gripping journey through one of the most infamous battles of the American Civil War. Set against the backdrop of the Chickamauga Creek, the story follows a group of soldiers as they navigate the chaos and horrors of war. As the battle unfolds, the characters are faced with unimaginable challenges and must confront the devastating consequences of their actions. With vivid descriptions and a powerful narrative, Atkins paints a haunting portrait of the futility and tragedy of war.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Drama

Themes: The futility of war, Loss and sacrifice, The human cost of conflict
