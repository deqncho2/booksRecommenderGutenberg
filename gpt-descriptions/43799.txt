Synopsis: 

In "Gunnery in 1858: Being a Treatise on Rifles, Cannon, and Sporting Arms," William Greener takes readers on an enlightening journey into the world of firearms during the mid-19th century. This comprehensive treatise delves into the principles of the science of gunnery, providing a detailed explanation of rifles, cannon, and sporting arms. Greener also highlights the newest advancements in fire-arms, captivating readers with the latest improvements in weaponry technology.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Science

Themes: Technology advancements, Military history, Weaponry innovation
