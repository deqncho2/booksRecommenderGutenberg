Synopsis: 
In 'Breaking Away; or, The Fortunes of a Student' by Optic, Oliver, we follow the captivating journey of a young student named Paul Duncan. Tired of the monotonous routine of his everyday life, Paul yearns for adventure and a chance to break free from the constraints of society. When an unexpected opportunity arises, he seizes it with both hands, embarking on a thrilling and life-changing escapade. As Paul navigates through a series of exhilarating events, he discovers the true meaning of friendship, love, and self-discovery. Will he find the fulfillment he seeks, or will his pursuit of freedom lead him down a path he never anticipated?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Romance
Themes: Freedom, Self-discovery, Friendship
