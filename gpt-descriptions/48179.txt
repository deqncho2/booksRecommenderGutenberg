Synopsis: 
In 'A Manual of the Hand Lathe', Egbert P. Watson takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of working metals, ivory, bone, and precious woods. With concise directions and expert guidance, this manual unveils the secrets of the hand lathe, revealing the intricate techniques and skills required to transform raw materials into exquisite works of art. From the basics of tool selection to advanced turning methods, Watson's comprehensive instructions empower readers to unlock their creativity and master the art of craftsmanship. Whether you are a seasoned artisan or a curious beginner, this manual is sure to inspire and guide you on your own artistic journey.

Genres: 
Crafts and Hobbies, Reference, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Craftsmanship, Artistic Expression, Skill Development
