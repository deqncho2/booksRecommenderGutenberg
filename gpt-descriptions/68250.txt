Synopsis: In "Happy Ending," a young woman named Jane finds herself trapped in a mysterious and unsettling world where nothing is as it seems. As she navigates through a series of bizarre and surreal encounters, Jane must unravel the secrets of this strange realm and confront her own deepest fears. With each twist and turn, the story takes unexpected and mind-bending directions, keeping the reader on the edge of their seat until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Identity, Fear and Anxiety
