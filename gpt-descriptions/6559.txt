Synopsis: 

In 'Assyrian Historiography: A Source Study', A. T. Olmstead takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient world of Assyria, unraveling the mysteries of their historical writings. Delving into the rich tapestry of Assyrian culture, Olmstead explores the fascinating narratives and accounts that have survived the test of time. From epic battles to royal intrigues, this source study offers a unique glimpse into the lives and events that shaped the Assyrian civilization. 

Genres: Historical research, Archaeology, Ancient History

Themes: Assyrian culture, Historical narratives, Ancient civilizations
