Synopsis: In "Chaste as Ice, Pure as Snow: A Novel" by C. Despard, a young woman named Emma finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden desires when she is hired as a governess for a wealthy family in the English countryside. As she navigates the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and societal expectations, Emma discovers that the idyllic facade of her new surroundings hides a dark underbelly. With each twist and turn, she must confront her own desires and make choices that will forever alter her life and the lives of those around her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Forbidden love, societal expectations, secrets and deception
