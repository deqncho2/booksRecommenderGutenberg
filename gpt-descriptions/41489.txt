Synopsis: In 'One of Clive's Heroes: A Story of the Fight for India' by Strang, Herbert, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of British colonialism in India. The story follows a young and ambitious protagonist who finds himself caught in the midst of a fierce battle for power and control. As he navigates through treacherous alliances, political intrigue, and personal sacrifices, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the heart of India's struggle for independence. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this historical novel immerses readers in a world of adventure, bravery, and the fight for justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Political Drama

Themes: Colonialism, Power Struggles, Loyalty, Independence
