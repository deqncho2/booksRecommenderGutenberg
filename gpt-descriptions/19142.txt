Synopsis: In "The Devil Doctor" by Sax Rohmer, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he investigates a series of bizarre deaths. As he delves deeper into the case, he discovers a sinister figure known as the Devil Doctor, a master of disguise and manipulation. With his life on the line, the detective must navigate a treacherous world of secret societies, ancient rituals, and supernatural forces to uncover the truth and bring the Devil Doctor to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Deception, Occultism
