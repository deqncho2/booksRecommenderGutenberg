Synopsis: In 'Dave Porter and His Rivals; or, The Chums and Foes of Oak Hall', Dave Porter finds himself facing new challenges as he enters Oak Hall, a prestigious boarding school. Alongside his loyal friends, Dave must navigate the treacherous waters of rivalries, pranks, and unexpected alliances. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, Dave's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. Will he be able to overcome his rivals and prove himself worthy of the Oak Hall community?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Competition, Loyalty, Self-discovery
