Synopsis: In 'The Crystal Cup' by Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton, a young woman named Margaret is caught in a web of love, ambition, and betrayal as she navigates the glamorous world of high society in San Francisco. When she becomes entangled with a mysterious and enigmatic man, her life takes a thrilling turn, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and danger. As secrets unravel and loyalties are tested, Margaret must make choices that will shape her future and reveal the true nature of those around her.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and betrayal, Ambition, Self-discovery
