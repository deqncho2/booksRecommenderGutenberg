Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a young and determined girl, finds herself embarking on a thrilling adventure as she enters Briarwood Hall, a prestigious boarding school. However, her excitement quickly turns to intrigue when she stumbles upon a mysterious campus secret. Determined to uncover the truth, Ruth must navigate the complexities of school life while unraveling the enigma that surrounds Briarwood Hall. With her intelligence and resourcefulness, Ruth sets out to solve the campus mystery, all while forming lasting friendships and discovering her own strengths.

Genres: Mystery, Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Curiosity, Self-discovery
