Synopsis: 
'Peacock Pie, a Book of Rhymes' by Walter De la Mare is a captivating collection of whimsical and enchanting poems that transport readers to a world of imagination and wonder. From tales of mischievous fairies and talking animals to hauntingly beautiful landscapes and mysterious encounters, each rhyme is a delightful journey into the realms of fantasy and magic. With its lyrical language and vivid imagery, 'Peacock Pie' invites readers of all ages to explore the hidden corners of their imagination and embrace the joy of storytelling.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Children's Literature

Themes: Imagination, Nature, Childhood, Mystery
