Synopsis: In 'What is POSAT?' by Smith, Phyllis Sterling, a mysterious organization known as POSAT emerges, captivating the attention of a young journalist named Emma. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding POSAT, Emma finds herself entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and Emma must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth behind POSAT's existence. This gripping tale of suspense and conspiracy will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Power and control
