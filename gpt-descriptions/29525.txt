Synopsis: In 'The Leech' by Robert Sheckley, a man wakes up in a strange place with no memory of how he got there. As he tries to piece together his past, he discovers a shocking truth about his identity and the sinister forces at play. With each revelation, the protagonist is forced to confront his own morality and make difficult choices that will determine his fate. This gripping tale of mystery and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Morality, Memory
