Synopsis: In 'The Brother Avenged, and Other Ballads', prepare to be captivated by a collection of haunting and emotionally charged ballads that will transport you to a world of love, loss, and revenge. Each ballad tells a unique story, filled with vivid imagery and unforgettable characters, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets and fates of the protagonists. From tales of star-crossed lovers to tales of betrayal and redemption, this collection will take you on a rollercoaster of emotions, leaving you breathless and yearning for more.

Genres: Poetry, Folklore, Gothic

Themes: Love, Loss, Revenge, Betrayal, Redemption
