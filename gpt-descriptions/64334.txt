Synopsis: In 'Sales Talk' by Cente, H. F., a charismatic and enigmatic salesperson named Jack enters the lives of a struggling couple, promising them a life-changing opportunity. As they become entangled in Jack's web of persuasion, the couple must navigate a world of manipulation, ambition, and hidden agendas. With each twist and turn, the boundaries between truth and deception blur, leaving the reader questioning who can be trusted and what price they are willing to pay for success.

Genres: Psychological thriller, suspense, mystery

Themes: Manipulation, ambition, trust, morality
