Synopsis: In "My Lady Nobody: A Novel" by Maartens, Maarten, a young woman named Elma falls in love with a mysterious man who seems to have no past. As their relationship deepens, Elma becomes determined to uncover the truth about her enigmatic lover, leading her on a journey filled with secrets, deception, and unexpected revelations. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this captivating novel explores the complexities of love, identity, and the power of the past to shape our present.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Identity and Self-discovery, Deception and Secrets
