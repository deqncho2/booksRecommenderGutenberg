Synopsis: In 'A Good-For-Nothing: 1876' by Hjalmar Hjorth Boyesen, follow the captivating journey of a young man named Rolf, who finds himself caught between his desire for adventure and the expectations of society. Set in 19th-century America, Rolf's unconventional choices lead him on a path filled with unexpected encounters, dangerous situations, and a search for his true purpose. As he navigates through various trials and tribulations, Rolf's determination and resilience are put to the test, ultimately revealing the transformative power of self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Conformity, Self-Discovery
