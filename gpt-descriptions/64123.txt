Synopsis: 

In 'Frank Merriwell's Own Company; Or, Barnstorming in the Middle West', Frank Merriwell, the beloved hero of the popular series, embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him to the heart of the Middle West. When Frank and his friends decide to form their own company and take their show on the road, they find themselves facing unexpected challenges and dangerous encounters. From daring stunts to mysterious encounters, Frank and his companions must navigate through a world filled with excitement and uncertainty. Will they be able to overcome the obstacles that come their way and achieve success in their barnstorming venture?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Courage
