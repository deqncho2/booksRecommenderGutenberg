Synopsis: In the Book of Joel, a prophet delivers a powerful message from God, warning the people of a devastating locust invasion and urging them to repent. As the locusts wreak havoc on the land, the prophet calls for a solemn assembly and encourages the people to turn back to God, promising restoration and blessings for those who do. Through vivid imagery and poetic language, Joel's prophetic words convey a sense of urgency and the need for spiritual renewal.

Genres: Religious literature, prophecy, poetry

Themes: Repentance, divine judgment, restoration, faith
