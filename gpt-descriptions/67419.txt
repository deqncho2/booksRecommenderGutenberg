Synopsis: 

Step into the world of English homes from the reign of Charles I to George IV as J. Alfred Gotch takes you on a captivating journey through the architecture, decoration, and garden design of this fascinating era. From grand country estates to charming townhouses, this book unveils the secrets behind the exquisite craftsmanship and opulent interiors that defined the English home during this period. With stunning illustrations and insightful commentary, Gotch brings to life the stories of the people who inhabited these homes, their aspirations, and the societal changes that influenced their design choices. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Architecture

Themes: English home design, Historical interiors, Social history
