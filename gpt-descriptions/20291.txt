Synopsis: In 'Captain Mansana & Mother's Hands' by Bjørnstjerne Bjørnson, a young sailor named Captain Mansana finds himself in a small coastal village where he encounters a mysterious woman with extraordinary healing powers. As their paths intertwine, Mansana is faced with a life-altering decision that will test his beliefs and challenge the boundaries of love and sacrifice.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Magical Realism

Themes: Love, Sacrifice, Redemption, Supernatural Abilities
