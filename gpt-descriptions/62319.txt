Synopsis: 
In "Cosmic Castaway" by Carl Jacobi, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Jonathan Grant embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that goes awry, catapulting him into an unknown realm of space and time. Stranded in a mysterious dimension, he must navigate through strange and perilous worlds, encountering bizarre creatures and facing unimaginable challenges. As he fights to survive and find a way back home, Dr. Grant discovers shocking truths about the nature of the universe and the limits of human knowledge.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Survival, Existentialism
