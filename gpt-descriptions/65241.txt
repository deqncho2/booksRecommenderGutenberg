Synopsis: In 'Rescue Mission' by Robert Silverberg, a team of scientists embarks on a daring mission to save a group of stranded astronauts on a distant planet. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected challenges, the fate of the stranded crew hangs in the balance. With time running out, the team must push the boundaries of human ingenuity and courage to complete their rescue mission.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Teamwork, Human resilience
