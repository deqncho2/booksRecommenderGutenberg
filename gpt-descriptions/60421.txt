Synopsis: 
In 'Security' by Bryce Walton, a renowned security expert, Alex, finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue when he is hired to protect a mysterious client. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a dangerous conspiracy that threatens not only his own life but also the security of the entire nation. With time running out, Alex must navigate a treacherous landscape of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Espionage, Trust, Loyalty
