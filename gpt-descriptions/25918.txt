Synopsis: 
'Heads and Tales' is a captivating collection of anecdotes and stories that intertwine the lives of various animals with the histories of notable individuals. From quadrupeds to other fascinating beasts, this book delves into the intriguing incidents and connections they share with famous men. Each tale offers a unique perspective on the relationship between humans and animals, revealing unexpected parallels and shedding light on the extraordinary bond that can exist between them.

Genres: 
Historical fiction, Animal fiction, Short stories

Themes: 
Human-animal relationships, Historical events, Connection and empathy
