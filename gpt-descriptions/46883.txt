Synopsis: In "Goethe and Schiller: An Historical Romance," Mühlbach takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two of Germany's greatest literary figures, Johann Wolfgang von Goethe and Friedrich Schiller. Set against the backdrop of the tumultuous late 18th century, this historical romance delves into the complex friendship and rivalry between the two renowned poets, as they navigate love, politics, and the pursuit of artistic excellence. As their paths intertwine, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the boundaries of creativity are pushed to their limits. With vivid storytelling and rich historical detail, Mühlbach paints a vivid portrait of these iconic figures, offering a glimpse into the world of German literature during a time of great upheaval.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Biographical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Artistic Pursuit, Love, Politics, Rivalry
