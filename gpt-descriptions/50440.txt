Synopsis: In 'Leslie's Loyalty' by Charles Garvice, a young woman named Leslie finds herself torn between her loyalty to her family and her growing love for a mysterious stranger. As secrets unravel and danger looms, Leslie must navigate a treacherous path to protect those she holds dear. With unexpected twists and a captivating romance, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Love, Family, Secrets
