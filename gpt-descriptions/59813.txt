Synopsis: 
Embark on a captivating journey through the pages of 'The Footpath Way: An Anthology for Walkers', a collection of enchanting tales that will transport you to the hidden corners of the world. From the misty moors of Scotland to the bustling streets of Tokyo, each story invites you to explore the transformative power of walking and the profound connections it forges. Lose yourself in the footsteps of characters who embark on personal quests, encounter unexpected encounters, and discover the extraordinary within the ordinary. With each turn of the page, you'll find yourself yearning to lace up your shoes and set off on your own adventure.

Genres: Fiction, Anthology, Travel

Themes: Exploration, Transformation, Connection
