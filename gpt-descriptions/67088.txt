Synopsis: In 'Why go to College?' by Cooper, Clayton Sedgwick, embark on a thought-provoking journey that explores the significance of higher education and its impact on individuals and society. Through captivating anecdotes and insightful analysis, the author delves into the multifaceted reasons why attending college is a transformative experience. From personal growth and intellectual stimulation to career opportunities and social mobility, this book offers a compelling exploration of the value of higher education.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, Self-help

Themes: Personal development, Career advancement, Social mobility, Intellectual growth
