Synopsis: 
'Twas the Night before Christmas: A Visit from St. Nicholas' follows the enchanting tale of a family on Christmas Eve as they eagerly await the arrival of a mysterious visitor. As the night unfolds, the story takes readers on a magical journey filled with anticipation, wonder, and the joy of the holiday season. With vivid descriptions and delightful rhymes, this beloved classic captures the essence of Christmas and the spirit of giving.

Genres: 
Children's literature, Poetry, Holiday

Themes: 
Christmas, Family, Generosity, Tradition
