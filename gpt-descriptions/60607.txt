Synopsis: In 'A New Era of Thought' by Charles Howard Hinton, a brilliant scientist unveils a groundbreaking invention that has the potential to revolutionize the world. However, as the device gains popularity, it becomes clear that it also holds a dark secret that could have catastrophic consequences. As the protagonist delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the invention, they must navigate a web of intrigue, betrayal, and moral dilemmas. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning the nature of progress and the ethical implications of scientific advancements.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Ethics, Scientific Progress, Betrayal, Morality
