Synopsis: 
"The Warden of the Plains, and Other Stories of Life in the Canadian North-west" by John MacLean is a captivating collection of tales set in the vast and untamed wilderness of the Canadian North-west. From the gripping adventures of a fearless warden protecting the plains to the heartwarming encounters between settlers and indigenous peoples, these stories offer a glimpse into the challenges and triumphs of life in this rugged frontier. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, MacLean paints a vivid picture of the harsh beauty and unforgiving nature of the Canadian wilderness.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Nature's Power
