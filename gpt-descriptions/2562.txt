Synopsis: In 'The Clouds', Aristophanes takes us on a hilarious journey into the world of Socrates and his unconventional school, the Thinkery. As the protagonist, Strepsiades, seeks to escape his debts through the power of argumentation, he encounters a cast of eccentric characters and absurd situations that challenge his beliefs and the very nature of wisdom itself. With wit and satire, Aristophanes explores the clash between traditional values and the emerging intellectualism of ancient Athens, leaving the audience questioning the true nature of knowledge and the consequences of its pursuit.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Drama

Themes: Education, Intellectualism, Tradition vs. Innovation, Wisdom, Morality
