Synopsis: In 'Martyr's Flight' by Searls, Hank, a young woman named Lily finds herself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger when she stumbles upon a secret that could change the course of history. As she embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth, Lily must navigate through a treacherous world of spies, double agents, and hidden agendas. With her life on the line, she must decide who to trust and how far she is willing to go to protect the truth.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Courage, Truth
