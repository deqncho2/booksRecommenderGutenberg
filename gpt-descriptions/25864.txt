Synopsis: In 'Philosophy of Osteopathy', A. T. Still takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of alternative medicine. Through his personal experiences and profound insights, he challenges conventional medical practices and unveils a revolutionary approach to healing. As he delves into the philosophy behind osteopathy, Still explores the interconnectedness of the body, mind, and spirit, offering a fresh perspective on the nature of health and disease. With compelling anecdotes and thought-provoking theories, this book invites readers to question their understanding of medicine and embrace a new paradigm of holistic healing.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Philosophy

Themes: Alternative medicine, Holistic healing, Mind-body connection, Medical philosophy
