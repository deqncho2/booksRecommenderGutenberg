Synopsis: 
In the picturesque landscape of Romney Marsh, Joanna Godden, a fiercely independent and unconventional woman, inherits her father's farm. Determined to prove herself in a male-dominated world, she faces numerous challenges and makes bold decisions that defy societal expectations. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and the pursuit of her dreams, Joanna's journey becomes a captivating tale of resilience and self-discovery.

Genres: 
Drama, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: 
Gender roles, Independence, Love and relationships, Social expectations
