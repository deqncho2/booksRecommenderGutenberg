Synopsis: In 'A Great Success' by Ward, Humphry, Mrs., a young woman named Lucy embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment in the bustling city of London. As she navigates the challenges of love, ambition, and societal expectations, Lucy's determination and resilience are put to the test. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel explores the complexities of relationships and the pursuit of personal fulfillment.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Empowerment, Love, Ambition
