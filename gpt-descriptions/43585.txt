Synopsis: In 'Our Little Jewish Cousin', we follow the captivating journey of a young Jewish girl named Ruth as she navigates her daily life in a vibrant Jewish community. From celebrating holidays and traditions to facing challenges and embracing her heritage, Ruth's story offers a heartwarming glimpse into the rich culture and values of her family and community.

Genres: Children's literature, Historical fiction, Cultural exploration

Themes: Cultural identity, Family traditions, Tolerance and understanding
