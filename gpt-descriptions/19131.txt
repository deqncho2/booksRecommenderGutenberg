Synopsis: In 'Golden Lads', Arthur and Helen Hayes Gleason take readers on a captivating journey through the glamorous world of Hollywood during the Golden Age. Through their unique perspectives, they share intimate stories and anecdotes about the legendary stars they encountered, offering a behind-the-scenes glimpse into the lives of icons such as Charlie Chaplin, Marilyn Monroe, and Frank Sinatra. With wit, humor, and a touch of nostalgia, the Gleasons paint a vivid portrait of a bygone era, filled with both triumphs and heartaches.

Genres: Memoir, Biography, Film History

Themes: Hollywood glamour, Celebrity culture, Personal relationships, Nostalgia
