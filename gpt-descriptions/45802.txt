Synopsis: In 'Knock Three Times!' by Marion St. John Webb, a young girl named Lucy discovers a mysterious old house with a hidden secret. As she delves deeper into the house's history, she uncovers a series of puzzling clues that lead her on an exciting adventure. With the help of her friends, Lucy must unravel the mystery before it's too late. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the locked doors of the old house.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Discovery
