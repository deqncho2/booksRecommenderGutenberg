Synopsis: 
'Fables in Rhyme for Little Folks: From the French of La Fontaine' is a collection of charming and whimsical tales written by the renowned French author, Jean de La Fontaine. Each fable presents a moral lesson through the clever use of anthropomorphic animals as characters. From a cunning fox to a wise ant, these delightful stories captivate young readers with their engaging rhymes and timeless wisdom. Whether it's a lesson about the importance of hard work or the consequences of greed, La Fontaine's fables provide valuable insights into human nature and the world around us.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Poetry, Fables

Themes: 
Moral lessons, Human nature, Wisdom
