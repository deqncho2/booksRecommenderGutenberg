Synopsis: 

'Geneva: Painted by J. Hardwicke Lewis & May Hardwicke Lewis; Described by Francis Gribble' takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque city of Geneva. Through the vivid descriptions provided by Francis Gribble, readers are transported to the enchanting streets, historic landmarks, and breathtaking landscapes of this Swiss gem. From the bustling markets to the tranquil shores of Lake Geneva, the book offers a delightful exploration of the city's rich history, culture, and natural beauty. 

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Art

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Natural Beauty
