Synopsis: In 'The Eyes of the World' by Harold Bell Wright, two young artists, a painter and a sculptor, find themselves entangled in a web of love, ambition, and betrayal. As they navigate the vibrant art scene of New York City, they must confront their own desires and the sacrifices they are willing to make for their art. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel explores the power of art to both inspire and destroy.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Art

Themes: Love and passion, Ambition and sacrifice, Artistic expression
