Synopsis: In 'Afloat on the Flood' by Leslie, Lawrence J., a devastating flood engulfs a small town, forcing its residents to confront their deepest fears and secrets. As the waters rise, tensions escalate, and the characters find themselves navigating treacherous emotional currents. Amidst the chaos, unexpected alliances form, love blossoms, and the true nature of human resilience is revealed. This gripping tale explores the power of community, the fragility of human relationships, and the indomitable spirit that emerges in the face of adversity.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Natural disasters, Survival, Community, Secrets, Resilience
