Synopsis: 
'A New Century of Inventions: Being Designs & Descriptions of One Hundred Machines, Relating to Arts, Manufactures, & Domestic Life' takes readers on a captivating journey through the imaginative mind of James White, a civil engineer. In this remarkable collection, White presents one hundred innovative machines that revolutionize various aspects of arts, manufactures, and domestic life. From ingenious contraptions that enhance productivity in factories to clever devices that simplify household chores, each invention is meticulously described and accompanied by detailed designs. As readers delve into this treasure trove of ideas, they will be captivated by the boundless creativity and practicality of White's visionary mind.

Genres: Non-fiction, Engineering, Inventions

Themes: Innovation, Practicality, Technological advancement
