Synopsis: In 'The Wasted Generation' by Johnson Owen, a group of young adults find themselves caught in a web of secrets, lies, and self-destruction as they navigate the tumultuous world of post-college life. As they struggle to find their purpose and make sense of their place in society, they are faced with unexpected challenges that force them to confront their deepest fears and desires. With each character grappling with their own demons, the story delves into the dark underbelly of ambition, addiction, and the pursuit of happiness. Will they be able to break free from the cycle of self-destruction or will they succumb to the pressures of their wasted generation?

Genres: Contemporary Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Psychological Drama

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Addiction, Relationships, Self-discovery
