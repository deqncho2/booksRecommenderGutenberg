Synopsis: In "The Story of a Confederate Boy in the Civil War," we follow the gripping journey of a young boy named Johnny as he navigates the tumultuous and harrowing times of the American Civil War. From his idyllic life on a plantation to the chaos and destruction of war, Johnny's experiences offer a unique perspective on the conflict that tore a nation apart. As he faces the challenges of loyalty, loss, and the search for identity, Johnny's story becomes a poignant exploration of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, War

Themes: Loyalty, Loss, Identity, Resilience
