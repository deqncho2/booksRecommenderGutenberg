Synopsis: In 'Pattern for Conquest' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking invention that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his creation, he soon realizes that his invention has attracted the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess it. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, the scientist must navigate a treacherous web of intrigue, betrayal, and danger to protect his creation and ensure the survival of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Technological advancement, Power struggles, Ethics and responsibility
