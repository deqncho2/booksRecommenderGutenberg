Synopsis: In 'Primitive Culture, vol. 2', Edward B. Tylor takes readers on a captivating journey through the development of various aspects of human civilization. From the origins of mythology and philosophy to the evolution of religion, language, art, and custom, Tylor explores the fascinating connections between these fundamental elements of human culture. Through extensive research and analysis, he uncovers the underlying patterns and universal themes that have shaped human societies throughout history. This thought-provoking volume offers a comprehensive exploration of the origins and evolution of human culture, shedding light on the intricate tapestry of our shared human experience.

Genres: Anthropology, Cultural Studies, History

Themes: Cultural evolution, Human development, Universal patterns
