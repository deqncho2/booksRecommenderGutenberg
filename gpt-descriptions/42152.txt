Synopsis: 

"The Mormon Battalion, Its History and Achievements" is a captivating historical account that delves into the remarkable journey of a group of Mormon pioneers who were called to serve in the United States Army during the Mexican-American War. Led by their unwavering faith and determination, these men embarked on an arduous and perilous expedition across the vast American West, facing numerous challenges and forging a unique path in history. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, B. H. Roberts brings to life the trials, triumphs, and sacrifices of the Mormon Battalion, shedding light on a lesser-known chapter of American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Faith, Sacrifice, Perseverance, Identity, Manifest Destiny
