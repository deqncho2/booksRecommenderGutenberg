Synopsis: 
'Messalina of the suburbs' by Delafield, E. M. is a captivating novel that follows the life of a seemingly ordinary suburban housewife named Clarice. However, beneath her calm and composed exterior lies a woman with a secret passion and a desire for something more. As she navigates the challenges of domesticity and societal expectations, Clarice embarks on a journey of self-discovery that will test her boundaries and force her to confront the limitations placed upon her. With wit, humor, and a touch of scandal, this novel delves into the complexities of identity, desire, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Domestic Drama

Themes: Identity, Gender Roles, Societal Expectations, Self-Discovery
