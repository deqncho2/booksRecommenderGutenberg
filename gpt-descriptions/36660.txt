Synopsis: 
"The Journal of Countess Françoise Krasinska, Great Grandmother of Victor Emmanuel" takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a remarkable woman living in 18th-century Europe. Through her personal journal, Countess Françoise Krasinska shares her experiences as a member of the Polish nobility, her encounters with influential figures of the time, and her passionate love affairs. As the Countess navigates the complexities of her social status and the tumultuous political landscape, her journal becomes a testament to her resilience, ambition, and unwavering spirit.

Genres: Historical fiction, Memoir, Romance

Themes: Love and passion, Social status and class, Political intrigue, Female empowerment
