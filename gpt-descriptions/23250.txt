Synopsis: In "The Skeleton On Round Island," a mysterious discovery on a secluded island sets off a chain of events that will unravel a dark secret. As a group of friends embarks on a summer adventure, they stumble upon a skeleton that holds the key to a long-forgotten tragedy. With each clue they uncover, the friends find themselves drawn deeper into a web of intrigue and danger, where the past and present collide in unexpected ways. As they race against time to uncover the truth, they must confront their own fears and confront the consequences of their actions. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Friendship and loyalty, Consequences of actions
