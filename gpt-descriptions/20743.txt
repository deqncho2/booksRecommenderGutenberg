Synopsis: In 'The Marx He Knew' by John Spargo, a young journalist named David embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about the enigmatic philosopher Karl Marx. As he delves deeper into Marx's life and ideas, David becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal revelations that challenge his own beliefs. With each revelation, David's perception of Marx and his impact on society is transformed, leading him to question the very foundations of his own existence.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Philosophical Drama

Themes: Ideology, Revolution, Personal Identity
