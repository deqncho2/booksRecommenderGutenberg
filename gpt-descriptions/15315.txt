Synopsis: 
In 'Gladys, the Reaper' by Anne Beale, we are introduced to Gladys, an ordinary woman with an extraordinary secret. As a reaper, she is tasked with guiding souls to the afterlife. However, when she encounters a soul unlike any she has ever encountered before, Gladys finds herself questioning her own purpose and the nature of life and death. With a blend of humor, heart, and a touch of the supernatural, this captivating story takes readers on a thought-provoking journey that challenges our perceptions of mortality and the meaning of existence.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Comedy, Drama

Themes: 
Life and death, Identity, Purpose
