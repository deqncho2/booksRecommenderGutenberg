Synopsis: In 'The Constable's Move: Captains All, Book 4,' the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the streets of London as the clever and resourceful Constable Pettigrew finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. When a series of seemingly unrelated crimes occur, Pettigrew must rely on his sharp instincts and quick thinking to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice. As the plot thickens, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and Pettigrew's own life hangs in the balance. Will he be able to outsmart his adversaries and solve the case before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Historical Fiction

Themes: Justice, Deception, Loyalty, Survival
