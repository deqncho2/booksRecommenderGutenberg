Synopsis: In 'A Masque of Days: From the Last Essays of Elia: Newly Dressed & Decorated', Charles Lamb takes readers on a whimsical journey through a series of imaginative and enchanting masques. Each masque presents a unique story, filled with colorful characters and captivating settings, as Lamb weaves together elements of fantasy, history, and social commentary. From the magical realm of fairies to the bustling streets of London, this collection of masques offers a delightful escape into a world of wonder and imagination.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Imagination, Social Commentary, Escapism
