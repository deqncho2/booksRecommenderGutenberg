Synopsis: In "The Son of Don Juan," a gripping original drama in three acts, Echegaray draws inspiration from Ibsen's "Gengangere" to weave a tale of passion, secrets, and the consequences of past actions. Set in a small Spanish town, the story follows the enigmatic Don Juan, who returns after years of absence, only to face the haunting ghosts of his past. As the truth unravels, the characters are forced to confront their own desires, regrets, and the intricate web of relationships that binds them together. With each act, the tension builds, leading to a climactic finale that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Guilt and Redemption, Family Secrets, Love and Betrayal
