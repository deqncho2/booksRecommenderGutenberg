Synopsis: In 'La Fin Des Livres', Robida and Uzanne take us on a captivating journey to a future where books are on the brink of extinction. As the protagonists navigate this world, they encounter a mysterious group determined to preserve the power of literature. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking revelations, this novel explores the consequences of a society without books and the enduring impact of storytelling.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Mystery

Themes: The power of literature, Preservation of knowledge, Technological advancements, Resistance against societal changes
