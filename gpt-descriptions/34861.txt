Synopsis: 
In 'The Pursuit' by Savile, Frank, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse when a mysterious criminal mastermind emerges. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances that will test his skills and determination to the limit. With each twist and turn, the detective must stay one step ahead to bring the elusive culprit to justice before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Pursuit of truth, Betrayal, Redemption
