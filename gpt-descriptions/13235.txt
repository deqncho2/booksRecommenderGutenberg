Synopsis: 

In the Ranks of the C.I.V. takes readers on a thrilling journey through the eyes of a young British officer during the Boer War. As he joins the City Imperial Volunteers, a group of civilian soldiers, he finds himself thrust into the heart of the conflict in South Africa. From the harsh realities of battle to the camaraderie among comrades, this gripping tale explores the challenges and sacrifices faced by those who answered the call to serve their country.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Duty, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Coming of Age
