Synopsis: In this captivating lecture, Calthrop explores the fascinating connection between physical, mental, and spiritual development. Delivered before the American Institute of Instruction, he delves into the profound impact that physical well-being has on our overall growth and enlightenment. With thought-provoking insights and compelling examples, Calthrop sheds light on the intricate relationship between our physical, mental, and spiritual selves, leaving the audience eager to uncover the secrets to holistic development.

Genres: Non-fiction, Educational, Lecture

Themes: Physical development, Mental development, Spiritual development, Holistic growth
