Synopsis: In 'Tommy Remington's Battle' by Stevenson, Burton Egbert, a young man named Tommy finds himself thrust into a world of danger and adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. With the help of a mysterious guide, Tommy embarks on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As he faces numerous challenges and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Tommy must summon all his courage and wit to overcome the obstacles in his path. Will he be able to unravel the secrets of the map and claim the treasure that awaits him?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Discovery, Self-discovery
