Synopsis: In 'Grace Darling, Heroine of the Farne Islands', author Marianne Farningham takes us on a captivating journey into the life of Grace Darling, a young woman living on a remote island in the 19th century. Grace's ordinary existence is forever changed when a shipwreck occurs near her home, and she finds herself faced with a life-or-death decision. This gripping tale explores the bravery, resilience, and selflessness of a remarkable woman who becomes an unexpected hero in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Bravery, Heroism, Resilience, Selflessness
