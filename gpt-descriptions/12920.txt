Synopsis: In "The "Goldfish": Being the Confessions of a Successful Man," we are introduced to a man who seemingly has it all - wealth, power, and success. However, behind the facade of his perfect life lies a captivating tale of ambition, moral dilemmas, and the consequences of one's choices. As the protagonist delves into his past, he reveals the sacrifices he made to achieve his current status, the secrets he has kept hidden, and the unexpected twists that have shaped his journey. With each confession, the reader is drawn deeper into the intricate web of this man's life, questioning the true meaning of success and the price one must pay to attain it.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Consequences, Identity
