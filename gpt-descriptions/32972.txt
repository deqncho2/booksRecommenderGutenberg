Synopsis: Phileas Fogg, a wealthy and eccentric Englishman, makes a daring wager with his fellow club members that he can travel around the world in just eighty days. With his loyal French valet, Passepartout, by his side, Fogg embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected obstacles, breathtaking landscapes, and encounters with fascinating characters from different cultures. As the clock ticks, Fogg's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test, leading to a race against time that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Cultural Differences
