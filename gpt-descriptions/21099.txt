Synopsis: In 'More About Peggy' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, readers are introduced to Peggy, a young woman with a zest for life and a determination to make her own way in the world. As she navigates the challenges of love, friendship, and societal expectations, Peggy's journey takes unexpected turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Independence, Love and Relationships, Self-discovery
