Synopsis: 
'Little Dinners With the Sphinx, and Other Prose Fancies' by Le Gallienne, Richard is a collection of enchanting and thought-provoking prose pieces that transport readers to a world of imagination and introspection. From intimate conversations with the Sphinx to encounters with mythical creatures and musings on life's mysteries, each story invites readers to explore the hidden depths of their own minds. With a touch of whimsy and a dash of philosophy, Le Gallienne's prose fancies captivate and challenge, leaving readers yearning for more.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Literary Fiction, Philosophy

Themes: 
Imagination, Self-reflection, Mystery
