Synopsis: 

'Indian Birds: Being a Key to the Common Birds of the Plains of India' by Dewar, Douglas is a comprehensive guidebook that takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse avian world of India. Filled with vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, this book serves as a key to unlock the mysteries of the common birds found in the vast plains of India. From the vibrant colors of the Indian Roller to the majestic flight of the Brahminy Kite, readers will be enthralled by the beauty and diversity of the avifauna that grace the Indian skies. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Field Guide

Themes: Biodiversity, Wildlife Conservation, Natural History
