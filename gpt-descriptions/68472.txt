Synopsis: In "The Slave-Auction" by John Theophilus Kramer, a young woman named Amelia finds herself caught in the midst of a harrowing slave auction. As she witnesses the heart-wrenching sale of human lives, Amelia's own beliefs and values are put to the test. With each passing bid, she becomes determined to challenge the oppressive system and fight for justice. This gripping tale explores the complexities of morality, the power of empathy, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Social Justice

Themes: Slavery, Morality, Empathy, Social Injustice
