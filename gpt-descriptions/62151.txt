Synopsis: 
In 'Adele Doring of the Sunnyside Club' by Grace May North, readers are introduced to the charming and enigmatic Adele Doring. As a member of the prestigious Sunnyside Club, Adele finds herself caught in a web of secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances. With her quick wit and determination, she navigates the complex social dynamics of the club, uncovering hidden truths and challenging the status quo. As Adele delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the Sunnyside Club, she discovers that her own past may hold the key to unraveling the club's darkest secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Social Hierarchy, Female Empowerment
