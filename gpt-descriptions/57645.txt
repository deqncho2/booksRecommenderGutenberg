Synopsis: 
'Fresh Every Hour: Detailing the Adventures, Comic and Pathetic of One Jimmy Martin, Purveyor of Publicity, a Young Gentleman Possessing Sublime Nerve, Whimsical Imagination, Colossal Impudence, and, Withal, the Heart of a Child' follows the extraordinary escapades of Jimmy Martin, a young man with an audacious spirit and an imaginative mind. As a purveyor of publicity, Jimmy embarks on a series of adventures that are both comical and heart-wrenching. With his colossal impudence and childlike heart, Jimmy navigates through a world filled with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers captivated by his whimsical journey.

Genres: Comedy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Imagination, Audacity, Innocence, Self-discovery
