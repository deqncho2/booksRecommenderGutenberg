Synopsis:
In 'Red Spider, Volume 1 (of 2)' by S. Baring-Gould, a young artist named Frank Saxham finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he becomes the prime suspect in a murder investigation. As he delves deeper into the dark secrets of the small English village he calls home, Frank uncovers a sinister plot that reaches far beyond his imagination. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with hidden identities, unexpected alliances, and a relentless pursuit by a mysterious figure known as the Red Spider. Will Frank be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Deception, Justice
