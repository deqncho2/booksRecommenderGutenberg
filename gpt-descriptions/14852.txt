Synopsis:
In "The Younger Set" by Robert W. Chambers, a group of young artists and bohemians find themselves entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when they stumble upon a hidden secret in their New York City neighborhood. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they uncover a world filled with danger, romance, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes:
Artistic expression, Coming of age, Secrets and hidden identities
