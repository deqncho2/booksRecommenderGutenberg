Synopsis: In 'Sweet Mace: A Sussex Legend of the Iron Times', George Manville Fenn takes us on a thrilling journey through the ancient landscapes of Sussex. Set during the Iron Age, the story follows the adventures of Mace, a young warrior with a mysterious past. As he navigates the treacherous world of warring tribes and supernatural forces, Mace must uncover the truth about his heritage and fulfill his destiny. With its blend of historical fiction, folklore, and action-packed adventure, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Ancient Legends
