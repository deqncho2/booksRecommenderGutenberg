Synopsis: In the small New England town of Oldfields, a mysterious and enigmatic doctor arrives, captivating the townspeople with his unconventional methods and peculiar demeanor. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the locals, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of Doc. Gordon is slowly revealed. With a touch of suspense and a dash of romance, this captivating tale explores the complexities of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Community, Secrets
