Synopsis: 
In 'Three Days on the Ohio River', a group of friends embarks on a thrilling adventure down the Ohio River, unaware of the dangers that lie ahead. As they navigate the treacherous waters, they encounter unexpected obstacles and face life-threatening situations that test their courage and friendship. With each passing day, the tension rises, and the stakes become higher, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to discover the fate of these brave adventurers.

Genres: Adventure, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Perseverance
