Synopsis: 

In 'Tom and Some Other Girls: A Public School Story', readers are introduced to Tom, a spirited and adventurous young girl who finds herself in a new and unfamiliar environment - a public school. As Tom navigates the challenges and triumphs of school life, she forms unexpected friendships with a group of diverse and intriguing girls. Together, they embark on a series of mischievous escapades, pushing the boundaries of societal expectations and defying traditional gender roles. As the girls face various obstacles and learn valuable life lessons, Tom's resilience and determination shine through, leaving readers eager to discover how her journey unfolds.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, School story

Themes: Friendship, Gender roles, Adventure, Resilience
