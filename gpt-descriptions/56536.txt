Synopsis: In "A Life of Walt Whitman" by Henry Bryan Binns, embark on a captivating journey through the extraordinary life of one of America's most influential poets. From his humble beginnings to his controversial works, this biography delves into the enigmatic persona of Walt Whitman, exploring the profound impact he had on literature and society. Uncover the secrets behind his groundbreaking poetry and the struggles he faced in a society resistant to change. Discover the man behind the words and be inspired by his unwavering belief in the power of individuality and the beauty of the human spirit.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Individualism, Identity, Social Change, Poetry
