Synopsis: 

In 'The Myxomycetes of the Miami Valley, Ohio', Andrew Price Morgan takes readers on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of myxomycetes, commonly known as slime molds. Set in the picturesque Miami Valley, Ohio, this scientific exploration unveils the hidden beauty and complexity of these enigmatic organisms. As Morgan delves into their life cycles, habitats, and unique characteristics, he uncovers surprising connections between slime molds and the natural environment. With vivid descriptions and stunning photographs, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a lesser-known realm of nature that will leave readers in awe of the wonders that lie just beneath their feet.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Biodiversity, Ecological Interactions, Natural History
