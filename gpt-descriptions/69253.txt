Synopsis: 
In "Signalling across space without wires: being a description of the work of Hertz & his successors," Sir Oliver Lodge takes us on a captivating journey through the groundbreaking discoveries and advancements in wireless communication. From the pioneering experiments of Hertz to the remarkable achievements of his successors, Lodge unveils the fascinating world of wireless technology and its potential to revolutionize the way we connect and communicate. With vivid descriptions and insightful explanations, this book offers a glimpse into the awe-inspiring realm of wireless signals and their ability to traverse space without the need for physical wires. Prepare to be amazed by the wonders of this extraordinary scientific frontier.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Wireless communication, Technological advancements, Scientific discovery
