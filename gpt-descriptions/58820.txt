Synopsis: 
In 'Whose Body? A Lord Peter Wimsey Novel', a mysterious body is discovered in a bathtub, completely devoid of any identification. Lord Peter Wimsey, an aristocratic amateur detective, is called upon to solve the perplexing case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that lead him to question the true identity of the body and the motives behind the crime. With his sharp wit and keen intellect, Lord Peter Wimsey navigates through a labyrinth of clues and suspects, determined to uncover the truth and bring the perpetrator to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Class and Society
