Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The Oxonian in Thelemarken', Frederick Metcalfe takes us on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of south-western Norway. With his keen eye for detail and his insatiable curiosity, Metcalfe explores the region's legendary lore, delving into the rich tapestry of myths and folklore that have shaped the local culture. As he immerses himself in the customs and traditions of the people, he uncovers hidden gems and uncanny tales that transport the reader to a world of wonder and mystery. From encounters with mythical creatures to encounters with the supernatural, Metcalfe's travelogue is a captivating blend of adventure, folklore, and exploration.

Genres: Travelogue, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Mythology, Cultural Exploration, Supernatural Encounters
