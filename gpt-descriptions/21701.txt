Synopsis: In 'The Settler and the Savage' by R. M. Ballantyne, a young settler named Ralph finds himself stranded on a remote island after a shipwreck. Alone and desperate for survival, he encounters a mysterious savage who becomes both his greatest threat and his only hope for survival. As Ralph navigates the treacherous landscape and battles against the elements, he must also confront his own prejudices and preconceived notions about the savage. This thrilling adventure explores the complexities of human nature and the transformative power of unexpected friendships.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Prejudice
