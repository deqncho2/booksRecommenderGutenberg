Synopsis: In "Undo: A Novel" by Joe Hutsko, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Emma Collins discovers a groundbreaking technology that allows her to undo past events. As she delves deeper into the possibilities of this power, she becomes entangled in a web of moral dilemmas and personal consequences. With each undo, the line between right and wrong blurs, and Emma must confront the repercussions of altering the course of history. As the stakes rise, she must grapple with the question of whether some things are better left unchanged.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Morality, Consequences, Time Manipulation, Ethical Dilemmas
