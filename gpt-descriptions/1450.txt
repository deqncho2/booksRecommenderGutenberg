Synopsis:
Pollyanna, a young orphan, is sent to live with her strict and stern Aunt Polly in a small New England town. Despite the challenges she faces, Pollyanna possesses an extraordinary gift - the ability to find something to be glad about in every situation. Through her infectious optimism and unwavering belief in the power of positivity, Pollyanna begins to transform the lives of those around her, teaching them to see the beauty and joy in even the darkest of times. As she navigates the ups and downs of life, Pollyanna's genuine and heartfelt approach to living inspires everyone she encounters, ultimately leading to unexpected and heartwarming connections.

Genres: Children's literature, Fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Optimism, Gratitude, Empathy, Transformation
