Synopsis: 

'Preliminary Announcement & List of Members: Society for Pure English, Tract 01 (1919)' takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of language and its preservation. The Society for Pure English, a group of passionate individuals, unveils their ambitious mission to safeguard the purity and integrity of the English language. Through a comprehensive list of members, the tract introduces a diverse array of individuals who share a common love for the beauty and precision of English. As the reader delves deeper into the tract, they are immersed in the fascinating discussions and debates surrounding language usage, grammar, and the evolving nature of English. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Linguistics, Language Studies

Themes: Language preservation, Linguistic purity, Evolution of language
