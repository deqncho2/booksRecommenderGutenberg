Synopsis: In 'Dick Sand: A Captain at Fifteen' by Jules Verne, a young boy named Dick Sand finds himself thrust into a thrilling adventure when his ship is taken over by a group of ruthless pirates. With his quick thinking and determination, Dick must navigate treacherous waters, face dangerous foes, and prove himself as a capable leader. As he battles against the odds, Dick's journey becomes a test of courage, resilience, and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Leadership, Survival
