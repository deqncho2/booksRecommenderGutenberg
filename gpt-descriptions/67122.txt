Synopsis: In the Year Ten Thousand takes readers on a thrilling journey to a future where humanity has evolved beyond recognition. Set in a world of advanced technology and unimaginable wonders, the story follows a group of explorers who stumble upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of the past. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they uncover a shocking truth that challenges everything they thought they knew about their own existence. Filled with suspense, intrigue, and mind-bending revelations, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Evolution, Identity, Time, Human Potential
