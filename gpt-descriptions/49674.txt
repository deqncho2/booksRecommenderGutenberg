Synopsis: In 'The Stolen Cruiser' by Percy F. Westerman, a thrilling adventure unfolds as a group of young friends stumble upon a mysterious abandoned cruiser. Intrigued by its secrets, they embark on a daring mission to uncover the truth behind its disappearance. As they delve deeper into the cruiser's past, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, facing unexpected challenges and uncovering shocking revelations. Will they be able to solve the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
