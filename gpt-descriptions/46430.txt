Synopsis: 
In 'The Camp-life of the Third Regiment', Robert Thomas Kerlin takes readers on a captivating journey through the daily life of soldiers in the Third Regiment during the Civil War. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Kerlin provides a unique and intimate perspective on the challenges, camaraderie, and sacrifices experienced by these brave men. From the monotony of camp routines to the adrenaline-filled moments on the battlefield, this book offers a compelling glimpse into the realities of war and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Military history

Themes: War, Camaraderie, Sacrifice, Resilience
