Synopsis: In 'The Submarine Boys and the Middies: Or, the Prize Detail at Annapolis', the Submarine Boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure as they join the United States Naval Academy. As they navigate the challenges of their new environment, they uncover a mysterious plot that puts their skills and loyalty to the test. With danger lurking beneath the surface, the Submarine Boys must rely on their courage and resourcefulness to protect their comrades and uncover the truth.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Friendship, Intrigue
