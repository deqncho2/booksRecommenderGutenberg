Synopsis: In the second book of John from the King James version of the Bible, an anonymous author addresses a letter to a chosen lady and her children, warning them about deceivers who deny the divinity of Jesus Christ. The author emphasizes the importance of love and obedience to God's commandments, urging the recipients to remain steadfast in their faith and to avoid those who spread false teachings. This short but powerful letter serves as a reminder of the eternal truths and the need for discernment in a world filled with spiritual challenges.

Genres: Religious literature, Epistle, Biblical commentary

Themes: Faithfulness, Love, False teachings, Obedience, Discernment
