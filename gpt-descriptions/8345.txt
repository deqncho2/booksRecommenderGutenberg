Synopsis: 

In the Book of 1 Machabees, the Challoner Revision of the Douay-Rheims Bible takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of ancient Israel. This historical and religious text delves into the struggles faced by the Jewish people under the oppressive rule of foreign powers, and the heroic efforts of a family determined to defend their faith and reclaim their land. Filled with tales of bravery, political intrigue, and spiritual devotion, this book offers a gripping account of the Maccabean revolt and the fight for religious freedom.

Genres: Historical fiction, Religious literature, Epic

Themes: Faith and devotion, Political resistance, Family loyalty, Religious freedom
