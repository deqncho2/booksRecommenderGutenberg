Synopsis: In 'John Ronge; The Holy Coat of Treves; New German-Catholic Church', an anonymous author delves into the intriguing story of John Ronge, a controversial figure who challenges the established Catholic Church. Ronge's quest for religious reform takes an unexpected turn when he stumbles upon the Holy Coat of Treves, a relic believed to possess miraculous powers. As Ronge's influence grows, he faces opposition from both the Church and those who question his motives. This gripping tale explores the clash between tradition and progress, as well as the power of faith and the search for spiritual truth.

Genres: Historical fiction, religious fiction, mystery

Themes: Religious reform, faith, tradition vs. progress
