Synopsis: In "Edvard Grieg: The Story of the Boy Who Made Music in the Land of the Midnight Sun," author Thomas Tapper takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of the renowned Norwegian composer, Edvard Grieg. Set against the backdrop of the enchanting landscapes of Norway, this biographical tale follows Grieg's extraordinary musical journey from his humble beginnings as a young boy with a passion for music to his rise as one of the most celebrated composers of his time. Tapper skillfully weaves together Grieg's personal experiences, his encounters with influential figures, and the challenges he faced, creating a vivid portrait of a musical genius and the world that shaped him. With its rich descriptions and engaging narrative, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the life and artistry of Edvard Grieg.

Genres: Biography, Music, Historical Fiction

Themes: Passion for music, Artistic inspiration, Overcoming adversity, Cultural identity
