Synopsis:
Step back in time to ancient Babylon and discover the fascinating world of Hammurabi, the legendary King of Babylonia. In "The Oldest Code of Laws in the World," Hammurabi unveils his groundbreaking code of laws, a monumental achievement that has shaped the course of legal systems for centuries. Delve into the intricate details of this ancient legal framework, as Hammurabi navigates the complexities of justice, morality, and societal order. From the grandeur of the Babylonian empire to the everyday lives of its citizens, this captivating work offers a unique glimpse into the past, shedding light on the origins of law and its profound impact on civilization.

Genres: Historical, Legal, Non-fiction

Themes: Justice, Morality, Society, Legal Systems
