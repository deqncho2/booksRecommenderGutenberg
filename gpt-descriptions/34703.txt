Synopsis: In 'The Tower of Oblivion' by Oliver Onions, a renowned architect is commissioned to design a mysterious tower that holds a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the project, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, haunted by the tower's eerie history and the enigmatic figures that surround it. With each step closer to uncovering the truth, he risks losing his sanity and possibly even his life. This gripping tale of suspense and supernatural forces will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries between reality and the unknown.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Madness and obsession, The blurred line between reality and the supernatural
