Synopsis: 
In the midst of the women's suffrage movement, a group of passionate activists come together to create a revolutionary cookbook that not only provides recipes but also serves as a powerful tool for social change. Filled with mouthwatering dishes and thought-provoking essays, 'The Suffrage Cook Book' takes readers on a journey through the kitchens of these trailblazing women, showcasing their culinary skills and unwavering determination to fight for equality. As the recipes unfold, so does the story of their struggle, resilience, and the transformative power of food in shaping society.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Feminist Literature, Cookbook

Themes: 
Women's Rights, Activism, Empowerment, Culinary Heritage
