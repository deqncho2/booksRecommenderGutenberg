Synopsis:
'Cuore (Heart): An Italian Schoolboy's Journal' follows the life of Enrico Bottini, a young boy attending an Italian elementary school in the late 19th century. Through his journal entries, Enrico shares his experiences, friendships, and challenges as he navigates the ups and downs of school life. From heartwarming moments of camaraderie to heartbreaking encounters with poverty and injustice, Enrico's journal offers a poignant and insightful glimpse into the joys and struggles of childhood.

Genres:
Coming-of-age, Historical fiction, Epistolary

Themes:
Friendship, Empathy, Social inequality, Patriotism
