Synopsis: In 'Kiana: a Tradition of Hawaii', James Jackson Jarves takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Hawaii. Through the eyes of Kiana, a young Hawaiian girl, we witness her extraordinary adventures, encounters with mythical creatures, and the discovery of ancient traditions that hold the key to her destiny. As Kiana navigates the challenges of her world, she must learn to embrace her heritage and find the courage to protect the sacred traditions of her ancestors. This mesmerizing tale of love, courage, and self-discovery will transport readers to a world where the past and present intertwine, and where the power of tradition can shape the future.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Cultural Fiction

Themes: Cultural heritage, Tradition, Self-discovery
