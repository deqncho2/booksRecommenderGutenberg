Synopsis: In 'The Grafters' by Francis Lynde, a young engineer named Jack Howland finds himself entangled in a web of corruption and deceit when he takes on a job in a small mining town. As he uncovers the truth behind the town's shady dealings, Jack must navigate dangerous alliances and confront his own moral compass. With suspenseful twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of power and greed, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Corruption, Morality, Power
