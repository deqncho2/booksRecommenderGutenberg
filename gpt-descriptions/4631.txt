Synopsis: In "The Hand but Not the Heart; Or, The Life-Trials of Jessie Loring," we follow the captivating journey of Jessie Loring, a young woman who finds herself torn between duty and love. Set in the 19th century, this gripping tale explores the challenges and sacrifices Jessie must face as she navigates societal expectations, family secrets, and the complexities of her own heart. As she grapples with her choices, readers will be drawn into a world of forbidden desires, hidden motives, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Duty, Family Secrets, Self-Discovery
