Synopsis: 
'A Hundred and Sixty Books by Washington Authors: Some Other Writers Who are Contributors to Periodical Literature; Lines Worth Knowing by Heart' is a captivating collection of literary works by various Washington authors. From thought-provoking essays to captivating short stories, this anthology showcases the diverse talent and creativity of writers from the region. Each piece offers a unique perspective on life, love, and the human experience, leaving readers eager to explore the next page and discover the hidden gems within this literary treasure trove.

Genres: Anthology, Fiction, Non-fiction, Literature

Themes: Regional literature, Identity, Human experience
