Synopsis: In 'My Queen: A Weekly Journal for Young Women. Issue 3, October 13, 1900: Marion Marlowe's True Heart; or, How a Daughter Forgave' by Sheldon, Lurana, readers are transported to the early 20th century as they follow the captivating story of Marion Marlowe. Faced with a heart-wrenching betrayal, Marion must navigate the complexities of forgiveness and find the strength to mend broken bonds. As secrets unravel and emotions run high, readers will be captivated by Marion's journey of self-discovery and the power of love and forgiveness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Betrayal, Forgiveness, Family Dynamics, Love and Redemption
