Synopsis: In "The Campfire Girls of Roselawn: or, A Strange Message from the Air" by Margaret Penrose, a group of adventurous young girls find themselves embroiled in a mysterious and thrilling adventure. When a strange message is received from the air, the Campfire Girls must use their wit, courage, and teamwork to unravel the enigma and uncover the truth behind it. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they encounter unexpected twists and turns that test their friendship and resilience. Will they be able to solve the puzzle and bring justice to those involved?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving, Teamwork
