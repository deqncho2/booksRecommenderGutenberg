Synopsis: In 'Quickie' by Stephen Marlowe, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of danger and intrigue when he stumbles upon a mysterious briefcase. As he delves deeper into the secrets it holds, he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where nothing is as it seems and trust is a luxury he can't afford. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous world of deception and betrayal, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic briefcase.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Trust, Deception
