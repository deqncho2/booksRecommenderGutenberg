Synopsis: In 'Sea Scouts up-Channel' by Percy F. Westerman, a group of adventurous sea scouts embark on a thrilling journey along the treacherous English Channel. As they navigate through dangerous waters, encounter mysterious shipwrecks, and face unexpected challenges, the young scouts must rely on their skills, teamwork, and courage to overcome the perils that lie ahead. With each twist and turn, the story unfolds with gripping suspense, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies beyond the next wave.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Resilience
