Synopsis: In 'Over the Side: Captains All, Book 6,' W. W. Jacobs takes readers on a thrilling maritime adventure filled with mystery and suspense. Set aboard a ship sailing through treacherous waters, the story follows a group of captains who find themselves entangled in a series of unexpected events. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the captains must navigate not only the perils of the sea but also the dangers lurking within their own ranks. With its gripping plot and intriguing characters, this book will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Survival
