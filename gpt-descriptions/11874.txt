Synopsis: In 'Hodge and His Masters' by Richard Jefferies, follow the captivating journey of Hodge, a loyal and intelligent cat, as he navigates the complexities of human relationships and the ever-changing world around him. From his humble beginnings as a stray to his encounters with various masters, Hodge's adventures will keep readers enthralled, as he learns about love, loyalty, and the true meaning of home.

Genres: Fiction, Animal Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Human-Animal Relationships, Home
