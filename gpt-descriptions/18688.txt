Synopsis: In 'Daisy in the Field' by Susan Warner, a young woman named Daisy finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and personal growth, Daisy must make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque countryside, this captivating novel explores the power of self-discovery and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Independence, Love and Relationships
