Synopsis: 

In 'Writing & Illuminating, & Lettering', Edward Johnston takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of calligraphy and lettering. Through his expert guidance and insightful anecdotes, Johnston unveils the secrets behind the art of writing beautifully and illuminating manuscripts with exquisite designs. From the historical origins of calligraphy to the intricate techniques used by master scribes, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the craft. With stunning illustrations and practical tips, Johnston's work is a must-read for anyone interested in the timeless art of lettering.

Genres: Art, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Calligraphy, Lettering, Craftsmanship, Artistic Expression
