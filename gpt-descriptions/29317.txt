Synopsis: In 'There Will Be School Tomorrow' by Thiessen, V. E., a small town is plagued by a mysterious event that causes all the adults to disappear overnight. Left to their own devices, a group of resourceful children must navigate the challenges of survival, uncover the truth behind the disappearance, and find a way to bring their parents back. As they face unexpected dangers and form unlikely alliances, the children discover the strength within themselves and the power of friendship.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Science Fiction

Themes: Coming of age, Friendship, Resilience, Survival
