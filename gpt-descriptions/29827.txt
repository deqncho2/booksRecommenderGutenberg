Synopsis: In "The Life and Amours of the Beautiful, Gay and Dashing Kate Percival: The Belle of the Delaware," we are introduced to the captivating and enigmatic Kate Percival. From her humble beginnings in rural Delaware to her rise as a prominent figure in high society, Kate's journey is filled with scandal, romance, and unexpected twists. As we delve into her world, we witness her unyielding spirit, her pursuit of love, and the secrets she holds close to her heart. With each page, the reader is drawn deeper into Kate's captivating story, eagerly awaiting the next revelation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Class, Identity and Self-Discovery
