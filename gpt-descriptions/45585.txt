Synopsis: In "The Seven Curses of London" by James Greenwood, the dark underbelly of Victorian London is exposed through a series of interconnected tales. From the eerie depths of the Thames to the shadowy corners of the city's streets, this gripping collection delves into the haunting mysteries and sinister secrets that plague the capital. As each curse unfolds, readers are drawn into a world of crime, deception, and the supernatural, where the line between reality and nightmare becomes increasingly blurred. With vivid descriptions and a relentless pace, Greenwood paints a vivid portrait of a city teetering on the edge of darkness, where danger lurks around every corner.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Crime and punishment, Social inequality, Supernatural elements
