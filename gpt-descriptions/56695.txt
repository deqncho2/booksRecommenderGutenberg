Synopsis: In 'The Radio Boys with the Border Patrol' by Gerald Breckenridge, a group of adventurous young radio enthusiasts find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mission along the border. When they stumble upon a mysterious radio transmission, the boys are determined to uncover the truth behind it. As they join forces with the Border Patrol, they face danger, intrigue, and unexpected twists that will test their courage and resourcefulness. Will their radio skills be enough to solve the puzzle and protect their country?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Patriotism, Exploration
