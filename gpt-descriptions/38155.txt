Synopsis: 

In 'Norway' by Jungman, Beatrix, a young woman named Emma embarks on a journey to the breathtaking landscapes of Norway, seeking solace and a fresh start. As she immerses herself in the enchanting beauty of the country, Emma discovers a hidden world filled with ancient folklore, mysterious legends, and unexpected encounters. Along her path, she encounters a charismatic local guide, Henrik, who becomes her companion in unraveling the secrets that lie beneath Norway's surface. Together, they navigate through a captivating blend of adventure, romance, and self-discovery, as Emma's perception of reality is challenged and her life takes an unforeseen turn. 

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Folklore, Unexpected encounters
