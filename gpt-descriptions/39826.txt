Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, a shocking crime has taken place, and all eyes are on Callista Blake. Accused of a heinous act, Callista finds herself at the center of a gripping trial that will unravel dark secrets and challenge the very fabric of the community. As the courtroom drama unfolds, the truth becomes elusive, and the lines between guilt and innocence blur. With each revelation, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of suspense, questioning who can be trusted and what really happened that fateful night.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Legal Drama

Themes: Justice and injustice, Truth and deception, Community and betrayal
