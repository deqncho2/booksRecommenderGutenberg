Synopsis: In 'The Lost Treasure of Trevlyn: A Story of the Days of the Gunpowder Plot' by Everett-Green, Evelyn, a young girl named Maud finds herself caught up in a thrilling adventure when she discovers a hidden treasure map. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Maud uncovers secrets from the past that could change her life forever. With danger lurking around every corner, Maud must navigate treacherous paths and face unexpected challenges to uncover the truth behind the lost treasure of Trevlyn.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Treasure hunting, Secrets and hidden pasts, Courage and resilience
