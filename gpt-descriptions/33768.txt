Synopsis: 
In the Middle English poem, "Erthe Upon Erthe," the reader is transported to a mystical world where a knight embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about life and death. As he encounters various characters and landscapes, he is confronted with profound questions about the nature of existence and the fleetingness of human life. Through vivid imagery and thought-provoking symbolism, this poem takes readers on a captivating exploration of the human condition and the mysteries of the universe.

Genres: 
Medieval Poetry, Allegory, Philosophical Literature

Themes: 
Life and Death, Transience, Human Existence, Spiritual Enlightenment
