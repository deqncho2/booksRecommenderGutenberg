Synopsis: 

In 'Witchcraft & Second Sight in the Highlands & Islands of Scotland: Tales and Traditions Collected Entirely from Oral Sources', John Gregorson Campbell delves into the mystical and enchanting world of the Scottish Highlands and Islands. Through a collection of captivating tales and traditions, Campbell explores the ancient practices of witchcraft and the extraordinary gift of second sight, both deeply rooted in the folklore and oral history of the region. From encounters with witches and their dark magic to the extraordinary ability to see into the future, this book takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the supernatural realm of the Scottish Highlands and Islands.

Genres: Folklore, Supernatural, History

Themes: Witchcraft, Second Sight, Scottish folklore, Oral traditions
