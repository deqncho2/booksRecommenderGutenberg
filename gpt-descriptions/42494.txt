Synopsis: 

In 'The Paston Letters, A.D. 1422-1509. Volume 6 (of 6), Part 2 (Index): New Complete Library Edition', readers are transported to the captivating world of medieval England through a collection of letters that offer a unique glimpse into the lives of the Paston family. This final volume of the series delves into the intricate web of relationships, political intrigues, and personal dramas that unfold within the Paston household. As the family faces challenges and navigates through a rapidly changing society, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and unexpected alliances are formed. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Epistolary Novel, Biography

Themes: Family dynamics, social class, power struggles, love and marriage, loyalty and betrayal
