Synopsis: In 'The Vanishing of Tera' by Fergus Hume, a small coastal town is shaken when a young woman named Tera mysteriously disappears without a trace. As the community grapples with the unsettling event, a determined detective sets out to unravel the secrets hidden beneath the surface of this seemingly idyllic town. With each twist and turn, the investigation uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and dark desires, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town intrigue, The darkness beneath the surface
