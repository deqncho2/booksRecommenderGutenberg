Synopsis: In "King Alfred of England: Makers of History," Abbott, Jacob takes us on a captivating journey through the life of one of England's most legendary kings. From Alfred's early struggles against Viking invasions to his rise as a wise and just ruler, this historical account paints a vivid picture of a man who defied the odds and became a symbol of hope for his people. As we delve into the challenges and triumphs of King Alfred's reign, we witness his unwavering determination, his strategic brilliance, and his unwavering commitment to justice. This gripping narrative will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover the legacy of a true hero.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography

Themes: Leadership, Resilience, Justice
