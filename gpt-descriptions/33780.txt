Synopsis: 
In 'The Haunted Pajamas' by Elliott, Francis Perry, a young girl named Lily receives a mysterious pair of pajamas as a gift. Little does she know that these seemingly innocent sleepwear items hold a dark secret. As Lily starts wearing the pajamas, strange and unexplainable events begin to occur, leading her on a thrilling and chilling adventure. With each passing night, Lily becomes more entangled in the haunting mystery surrounding the pajamas, uncovering a hidden world she never knew existed. Will she be able to solve the enigma before it consumes her?

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Fantasy

Themes: Supernatural, Coming of Age, Courage
