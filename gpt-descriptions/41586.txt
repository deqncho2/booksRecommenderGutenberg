Synopsis: In 'The Red Dust' by Murray Leinster, a group of scientists embarks on a dangerous mission to investigate a mysterious red dust that has blanketed the Earth, causing chaos and destruction. As they delve deeper into the origins of the dust, they uncover a shocking secret that could change the fate of humanity forever. With time running out, they must race against the clock to find a solution before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Apocalyptic events, Scientific exploration, Survival
