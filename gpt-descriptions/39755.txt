Synopsis: In this enchanting retelling of the classic fairy play by Sir James Barrie, readers are transported to the magical world of Neverland, where the mischievous and forever-young Peter Pan leads a group of lost boys on thrilling adventures. Join Wendy, John, and Michael Darling as they embark on a journey filled with pirates, fairies, and mermaids, all while discovering the true meaning of friendship, bravery, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Childhood and growing up, the power of imagination, the importance of friendship and family, the allure of eternal youth.
