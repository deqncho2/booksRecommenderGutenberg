Synopsis: In "Recollections of Abraham Lincoln, 1847-1865," Ward Hill Lamon, a close friend and former law partner of the iconic president, offers a unique and intimate perspective on the life and character of Abraham Lincoln. Through a series of personal anecdotes and vivid recollections, Lamon takes readers on a journey that spans from Lincoln's early political career to his presidency during the tumultuous years of the Civil War. From humorous anecdotes to poignant moments, this book provides a captivating glimpse into the mind and actions of one of America's most revered leaders.

Genres: Biography, History, Memoir

Themes: Leadership, Friendship, Politics, Civil War
