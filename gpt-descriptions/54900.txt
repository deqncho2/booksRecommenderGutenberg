Synopsis: In 'The Return of the O'Mahony: A Novel' by Frederic, Harold, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small Irish village of Ballymore, setting off a chain of events that will uncover long-buried secrets and ignite a battle for justice. As the truth slowly unravels, the lives of the villagers become intertwined in a web of love, betrayal, and redemption. With its richly drawn characters and atmospheric setting, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family, Identity, Revenge, Redemption
