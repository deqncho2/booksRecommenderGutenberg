Synopsis: In 'A Boy's-eye View of the Arctic', Rawson, Kennett Longley takes readers on an exhilarating adventure through the icy landscapes of the Arctic, seen through the eyes of a young boy. As he navigates the treacherous terrain, encounters fascinating wildlife, and learns about the indigenous cultures, the boy's perspective offers a unique and captivating insight into this remote and mysterious region. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding moments, this book will transport readers to a world of frozen wonder and leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Cultural diversity
