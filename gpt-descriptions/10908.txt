Synopsis: 

In 'Manual for Noncommissioned Officers and Privates of Infantry of the Army of the United States, 1917', the United States War Department presents a comprehensive guide specifically designed for Engineer companies (dismounted) and Coast Artillery companies to enhance their infantry instruction and training. This manual delves into the intricacies of infantry tactics, weapons handling, and combat techniques, providing invaluable knowledge to soldiers on the front lines. With detailed instructions and illustrations, this manual equips soldiers with the necessary skills to navigate the challenges of warfare and emerge victorious.

Genres: Military, Instructional, Historical

Themes: Infantry tactics, Combat training, Military strategy
