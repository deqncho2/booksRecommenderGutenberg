Synopsis: In 'A Fascinating Traitor: An Anglo-Indian Story' by Richard Savage, a captivating tale unfolds as the protagonist, an Anglo-Indian individual, finds themselves torn between two worlds. Set against the backdrop of colonial India, this gripping narrative explores the complexities of identity, loyalty, and the struggle for self-discovery. As the story unravels, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the protagonist's journey becomes a thrilling exploration of love, betrayal, and the search for belonging. With its rich historical setting and compelling characters, 'A Fascinating Traitor' promises to take readers on an enthralling adventure through the intricate tapestry of Anglo-Indian society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Self-discovery, Love, Betrayal, Belonging
