Synopsis: In 'Natural Man' by Arthur B. Moss, a mysterious discovery in the heart of the Amazon rainforest sets off a thrilling adventure that combines elements of science fiction, mystery, and action. When a renowned anthropologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact with unimaginable powers, he becomes entangled in a race against time to unlock its secrets before it falls into the wrong hands. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic world of the artifact, he must confront his own beliefs and question the very nature of humanity. With danger lurking at every turn, the protagonist must navigate treacherous landscapes and face unexpected adversaries, all while grappling with the profound implications of the artifact's existence. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Action

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Power
