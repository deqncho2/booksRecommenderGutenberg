Synopsis: In 'Guns of the Gods: A Story of Yasmini's Youth', we are transported to the vibrant and mysterious world of India, where a young girl named Yasmini embarks on a thrilling journey of self-discovery. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of politics, love, and power, Yasmini finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and betrayals that will test her courage and resilience. With the backdrop of a tumultuous era, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Yasmini's extraordinary destiny.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Coming of Age, Identity, Power and Corruption
