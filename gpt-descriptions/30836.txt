Synopsis: 
Seven Keys to Baldpate is a thrilling mystery novel that follows a writer named Billy Magee who is given the opportunity to spend a week in an isolated mountain resort called Baldpate Inn. Little does he know, he is not the only one with a key to the inn. As a blizzard rages outside, a series of unexpected visitors arrive, each with their own motives and secrets. With danger lurking around every corner, Billy must unravel the mysteries of Baldpate Inn before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Identity
