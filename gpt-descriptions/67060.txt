Synopsis: In 'Making Good for Muley' by W. C. Tuttle, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Muley, bringing with him a dark secret and a troubled past. As the townspeople become entangled in his web of deception, they must confront their own demons and make difficult choices that will forever change their lives. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Identity
