Synopsis: 
In "Jules of the Great Heart: Free Trapper and Outlaw in the Hudson Bay Region in the Early Days," Lawrence Mott takes us on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of the Hudson Bay region. Set in the early days of the fur trade, the story follows the enigmatic Jules, a free trapper and outlaw with a mysterious past. As Jules navigates the treacherous landscapes and encounters various characters, he becomes entangled in a web of danger, adventure, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into Jules' world, where survival and freedom hang in the balance.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Wilderness survival, Identity, Redemption
