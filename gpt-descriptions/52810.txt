Synopsis: In 'The Border Boys in the Canadian Rockies' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of adventurous young men embark on a thrilling journey through the majestic Canadian Rockies. As they navigate treacherous terrain, encounter wild animals, and face unexpected challenges, they uncover a hidden secret that could change their lives forever. With danger lurking at every turn, the Border Boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Survival
