Synopsis: 
In 'Another Summer: The Yellowstone Park and Alaska', Charles J. Gillis takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the breathtaking landscapes of Yellowstone Park and Alaska. Through vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Gillis explores the wonders of these natural wonders, from the geysers and hot springs of Yellowstone to the majestic glaciers and wildlife of Alaska. As the author immerses readers in the beauty and grandeur of these destinations, he also delves into the history, culture, and unique experiences that make these places truly unforgettable. Whether it's encountering grizzly bears in the wild or witnessing the awe-inspiring beauty of the Northern Lights, 'Another Summer' is a thrilling exploration of two of America's most iconic destinations.

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Nature, Exploration, Wildlife, Cultural Heritage
