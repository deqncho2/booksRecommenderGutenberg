Synopsis: In 'Dark Windows' by Walton, Bryce, a young woman named Emily moves into an old Victorian house with a mysterious past. As she settles in, she begins to notice strange occurrences and eerie figures lurking in the shadows. Determined to uncover the truth, Emily delves into the house's dark history, unearthing secrets that threaten her sanity and safety. With each revelation, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leaving Emily questioning her own perception of the world around her.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Haunting, Secrets, Perception
