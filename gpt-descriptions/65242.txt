Synopsis: In 'Satellite of Death' by Randall Garrett, a brilliant scientist is found dead under mysterious circumstances, leaving behind a cryptic message that hints at a deadly plot involving a powerful satellite. As a team of investigators delves into the case, they uncover a web of international intrigue, high-stakes espionage, and a race against time to prevent a catastrophic event that could change the course of history. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Espionage, Technology, Conspiracy
