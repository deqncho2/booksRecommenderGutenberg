Synopsis: 

'Japan: A Record in Colour' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the vibrant and enchanting landscapes of Japan. Through the eyes of Menpes, a renowned artist, and the vivid descriptions of Dorothy Menpes, this book offers a unique and immersive experience of the country's rich culture, stunning architecture, and breathtaking natural beauty. From bustling city streets to serene temples, from traditional tea ceremonies to awe-inspiring cherry blossom festivals, this record in colour captures the essence of Japan in all its splendor. 

Genres: Travel, Art, Photography

Themes: Japanese culture, Beauty of nature, Artistic exploration
