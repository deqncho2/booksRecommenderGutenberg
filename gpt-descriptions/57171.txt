Synopsis: In "The Camp Fire Girls; Or, The Secret of an Old Mill," a group of adventurous young girls embark on a thrilling journey that leads them to uncover a mysterious secret hidden within an old mill. As they navigate through challenges and unexpected twists, the girls' determination and friendship are put to the test. With each clue they unravel, they find themselves drawn deeper into a captivating mystery that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
