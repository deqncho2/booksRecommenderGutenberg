Synopsis: 
'The Cambrian Sketch-Book: Tales, Scenes, and Legends of Wild Wales' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich folklore of Wales. Through a collection of tales, scenes, and legends, R. Rice Davies weaves together a tapestry of captivating stories that transport readers to a world of mystery, adventure, and wonder. From ancient myths and legends to encounters with supernatural beings, each story offers a glimpse into the unique cultural heritage of Wales. With vivid descriptions and compelling narratives, this book invites readers to explore the hidden depths of Wild Wales and discover the magic that lies within its rugged landscapes and ancient traditions.

Genres: Fiction, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Welsh culture and heritage, Mythology, Supernatural encounters
