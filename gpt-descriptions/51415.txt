Synopsis: 
'Poems' by Coleman, Jamie Harris is a captivating collection of verses that takes readers on an emotional journey through the depths of love, loss, and self-discovery. With each poem, the author skillfully weaves together vivid imagery and raw emotions, inviting readers to explore the complexities of the human experience. From the tender moments of joy to the haunting echoes of heartbreak, this collection offers a profound reflection on the beauty and fragility of life.

Genres: Poetry, Contemporary Literature

Themes: Love, Loss, Self-discovery
