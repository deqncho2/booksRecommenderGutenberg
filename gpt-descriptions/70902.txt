Synopsis: 
In 'Many happy returns of the day!' by Butler, Ellis Parker, we follow the misadventures of a man named Mr. Smith who finds himself in a series of hilarious and unexpected situations on his birthday. From mistaken identities to comical misunderstandings, this story takes readers on a rollercoaster ride of laughter and surprises. As Mr. Smith navigates through a day filled with chaos and confusion, he learns valuable lessons about life, love, and the importance of embracing the unexpected.

Genres: Humor, Comedy, Fiction

Themes: Birthday celebrations, Mistaken identities, Life lessons
