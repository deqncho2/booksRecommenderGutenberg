Synopsis: 
In 'A Handbook of the Cornish Language: chiefly in its latest stages with some account of its history and literature', Henry Jenner takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of the Cornish language. With a focus on its latest stages, Jenner delves into the history and literature surrounding this ancient language, shedding light on its revival and the challenges faced by its speakers. From the origins of Cornish to its modern usage, Jenner's handbook offers a comprehensive exploration of this fascinating linguistic heritage, making it a must-read for language enthusiasts and history buffs alike.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Linguistics, History

Themes: 
Language revival, Cultural heritage, Linguistic diversity
