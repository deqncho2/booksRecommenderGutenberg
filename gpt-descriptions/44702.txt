Synopsis: 
In "Reminiscences of a Prisoner of War and His Escape," Daniel Avery Langworthy takes readers on a gripping journey through his harrowing experiences as a prisoner of war and his daring escape. From the moment he is captured to the heart-pounding moments of his escape, Langworthy's vivid storytelling immerses readers in the brutal realities of war and the indomitable human spirit that refuses to be broken. With each page, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome of Langworthy's courageous quest for freedom.

Genres: Memoir, War, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Courage, Freedom
