Synopsis: In 'Tom Swift and His War Tank; Or, Doing His Bit for Uncle Sam', the brilliant young inventor Tom Swift takes on a new challenge as he creates a powerful war tank to aid the American forces during World War I. With his innovative technology and determination, Tom embarks on a thrilling adventure to test his creation and protect his country from enemy forces. As he faces danger and uncertainty, Tom's ingenuity and bravery are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Innovation, Heroism
