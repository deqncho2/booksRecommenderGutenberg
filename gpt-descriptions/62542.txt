Synopsis: 

'Infamous Day: Marines at Pearl Harbor, 7 December 1941' takes readers on a gripping journey through the eyes of the United States Marines stationed at Pearl Harbor during the devastating attack on December 7, 1941. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, authors Cressman and Wenger bring to life the harrowing experiences of these brave men as they face the chaos, destruction, and loss that unfolded that fateful day. From the initial shock of the surprise attack to the heroic acts of valor and resilience, this book offers a compelling and intimate account of the Marines' role in one of the most significant events in American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, World War II

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Resilience, Survival
