Synopsis: In 'Wild Roses: A Tale of the Rockies', Driggs takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged and untamed landscapes of the Rocky Mountains. The story follows the lives of two young women, Lily and Rose, who find themselves entangled in a web of love, adventure, and self-discovery. As they navigate the challenges of the wilderness, they must confront their own fears and desires, ultimately discovering the true meaning of freedom and the power of the human spirit. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, 'Wild Roses' is a gripping tale that will transport readers to the heart of the Rockies.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Nature and Wilderness, Self-Discovery, Love and Relationships
