Synopsis: 

In "South African Memories: Social, Warlike & Sporting from Diaries Written at the Time," Lady Sarah Isabella Augusta Wilson takes readers on a captivating journey through her personal diaries, offering a unique perspective on life in South Africa during a tumultuous period. From the vibrant social scene to the harrowing experiences of war and the thrill of sporting events, Lady Wilson's vivid descriptions and intimate anecdotes paint a vivid picture of a country in transition. As she navigates the complexities of colonial society, readers will be drawn into a world filled with intrigue, adventure, and unexpected twists. 

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Travelogue

Themes: Colonialism, Social Life, War, Sports, Cultural Transformation
