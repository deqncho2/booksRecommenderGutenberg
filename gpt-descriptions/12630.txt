Synopsis: In 'The Adventures of Old Mr. Toad', follow the captivating journey of a curious and mischievous toad named Mr. Toad as he embarks on a series of thrilling escapades in the enchanting world of the Green Forest. From daring escapes to unexpected encounters, Mr. Toad's adventures will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies around the next corner.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Curiosity, Friendship, Nature, Courage
