Synopsis:
In 'The Box with Broken Seals' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious box with broken seals arrives at the doorstep of a wealthy businessman. Intrigued by its contents, he embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the secrets hidden within. As he delves deeper into the box's origins, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the businessman must navigate a treacherous path to protect himself and those he holds dear. This gripping tale of mystery and suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Betrayal and trust, Power and manipulation
