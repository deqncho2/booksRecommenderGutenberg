Synopsis: 
"The Hudson: Three Centuries of History, Romance and Invention" takes readers on an enthralling journey through the captivating and ever-evolving history of the Hudson River. From the early days of exploration and settlement to the bustling industrial era and the birth of modern innovation, this book unveils the hidden stories, remarkable characters, and pivotal events that have shaped the Hudson's legacy. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Bruce Wallace paints a vivid picture of the river's role in shaping American history, while also exploring the enduring romance and inspiration it has provided to artists, writers, and dreamers throughout the centuries. 

Genres: History, Non-fiction, Travel

Themes: Exploration, Industrialization, Artistic Inspiration, Environmental Conservation
