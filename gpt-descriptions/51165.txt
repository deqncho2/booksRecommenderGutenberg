Synopsis: 

"The Regimental Records of the British Army" is a comprehensive historical résumé that takes readers on a captivating journey through the titles, campaigns, honours, uniforms, facings, badges, nicknames, and more of the British Army. Written by John Stephen Farmer and John S. Farmer, this meticulously arranged chronicle offers a fascinating glimpse into the rich and storied history of one of the world's most renowned military forces. From the early days of the British Army to the modern era, this book provides a captivating exploration of the various regiments, their achievements, and the unique characteristics that have shaped their identities.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military traditions, Regimental pride, Historical significance
