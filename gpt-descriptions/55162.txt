Synopsis: In 'Enchantment' by Harold MacGrath, a young woman named Zara finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she inherits a priceless necklace. As she delves into the necklace's history, Zara becomes entangled with a charming thief, a powerful secret society, and a centuries-old curse. With her life on the line, Zara must navigate a treacherous world of deception and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. Will she uncover the truth behind the necklace's enchantment before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Love and sacrifice
