Synopsis: In "The World Order of Bahá'u'lláh" by Shoghi Effendi, embark on a captivating journey through the visionary teachings of Bahá'u'lláh, the founder of the Bahá'í Faith. This profound work explores the transformative power of unity, the emergence of a global civilization, and the principles that can guide humanity towards a harmonious and prosperous future. Through eloquent prose and insightful analysis, Shoghi Effendi unveils the potential of Bahá'u'lláh's teachings to shape a new world order, one that embraces diversity, fosters justice, and promotes the spiritual and material well-being of all. 

Genres: Religious literature, Philosophy, Non-fiction

Themes: Unity, Global civilization, Social transformation, Justice, Spiritual growth
