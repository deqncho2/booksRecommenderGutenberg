Synopsis: In the February 1921 issue of The Beaver, delve into the captivating world of the Hudson's Bay Company as it unveils stories of adventure, exploration, and trade in the Canadian wilderness. Discover the untamed landscapes, encounter indigenous cultures, and witness the challenges faced by fur traders and explorers during this pivotal era. From thrilling accounts of daring expeditions to insightful articles on the fur trade, this issue of The Beaver offers a fascinating glimpse into the rich history of Canada's frontier.

Genres: Historical, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Wilderness, Indigenous cultures, Fur trade
