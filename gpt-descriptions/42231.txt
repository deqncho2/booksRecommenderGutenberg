Synopsis: 

Step into the enchanting world of Paris as you embark on a captivating journey through its rich history, vibrant people, and iconic places. In "Old and New Paris: Its History, Its People, and Its Places, v. 1," H. Sutherland Edwards invites readers to explore the hidden stories and lesser-known corners of this magnificent city. From the grandeur of Notre-Dame Cathedral to the bustling streets of Montmartre, Edwards paints a vivid picture of Paris, both past and present. Through his meticulous research and engaging storytelling, he unveils the secrets behind famous landmarks, introduces us to fascinating historical figures, and delves into the heart and soul of this timeless city. 

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Parisian culture, Historical preservation, Urban development
