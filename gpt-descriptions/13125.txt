Synopsis: In 'Deer Godchild' by Bernard, Marguerite and Serrell, Edith, a young girl named Sylvia discovers a mysterious connection to a magical deer in the forest near her home. As she embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind this enchanting creature, Sylvia finds herself caught in a web of secrets, ancient legends, and unexpected friendships. With each encounter, she unravels the extraordinary power of her own imagination and the transformative nature of love.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Magic and wonder, Identity and self-discovery, Friendship and loyalty
