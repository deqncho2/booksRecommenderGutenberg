Synopsis: In 'The Art of Ballet' by Perugini, Mark Edward, a talented young dancer named Isabella finds herself thrust into the competitive world of professional ballet. As she navigates the challenges of intense training, demanding teachers, and fierce rivalries, Isabella must discover her own unique voice and overcome the obstacles that stand in her way. With breathtaking descriptions of graceful movements and heart-pounding performances, this captivating novel takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the captivating world of ballet.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Pursuit of passion, Self-discovery, Competition, Perseverance
