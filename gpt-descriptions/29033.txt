Synopsis: In 'Critical Miscellanies (Vol. 3 of 3), Essay 10: Auguste Comte', John Morley delves into the life and ideas of the influential French philosopher, Auguste Comte. Morley explores Comte's groundbreaking theories on the development of human knowledge and society, shedding light on his unique perspective on the progression of civilization. Through a captivating analysis, Morley offers readers a fascinating glimpse into the mind of one of the most significant thinkers of the 19th century.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Philosophy

Themes: Philosophy of science, Sociology, Progress of civilization
