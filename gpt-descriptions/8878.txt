Synopsis: In 'The Mischief-Maker' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges, wreaking havoc and chaos in the lives of those around him. As his true intentions remain shrouded in secrecy, a web of intrigue and deception unfolds, leaving the reader captivated and eager to uncover the truth behind this mischievous character.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Manipulation, Identity
