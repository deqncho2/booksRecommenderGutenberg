Synopsis: In 'The Council of Dogs' by Anonymous, a group of dogs from different backgrounds and breeds come together to form a secret society with a mission to protect and serve their human companions. As they navigate the challenges of their canine lives, they uncover a hidden world filled with mystery, danger, and unexpected alliances. With their unique abilities and unwavering loyalty, these extraordinary dogs embark on a thrilling adventure that will test their courage and redefine the meaning of friendship.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Courage
