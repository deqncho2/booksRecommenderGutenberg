Synopsis: In 'The Girl at the Halfway House: A Story of the Plains' by Emerson Hough, a mysterious young woman finds herself caught between two worlds as she seeks refuge at a remote halfway house on the American frontier. As the residents of the house grapple with their own secrets and desires, tensions rise and alliances are tested. With danger lurking in the surrounding wilderness, the girl's presence becomes a catalyst for unexpected events that will forever change the lives of those around her.

Genres: Western, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Survival
