Synopsis: In "The Ragged Edge: A Tale of Ward Life & Politics," John T. McIntyre takes readers on a gripping journey through the gritty world of ward politics in a bustling city. The story follows a young and ambitious politician who finds himself entangled in a web of corruption, power struggles, and personal sacrifices. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of ward life, he must confront his own moral compass and make difficult choices that will shape his future and the fate of the community he serves. With vivid characters and a captivating narrative, this book offers a compelling exploration of the dark underbelly of politics and the human spirit.

Genres: Political fiction, Drama, Crime

Themes: Corruption, Power struggles, Morality, Personal sacrifice
