Synopsis: 

In 'Historic Tales: The Romance of Reality. Vol. 05 (of 15), German' by Charles Morris, embark on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of German history. From the legendary tales of ancient Germanic tribes to the rise of the Holy Roman Empire, this volume delves into the fascinating stories that have shaped Germany's past. Discover the heroic deeds of legendary figures like Hermann the Cheruscan and Charlemagne, and witness the struggles and triumphs of the German people throughout the ages. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Morris brings to life the captivating history of Germany, painting a vivid picture of a nation's evolution.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Germanic tribes, Holy Roman Empire, Heroism, National Identity
