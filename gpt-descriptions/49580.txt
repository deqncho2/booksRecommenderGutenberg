Synopsis:
'Wallace; or, the Life and Acts of Sir William Wallace, of Ellerslie' by Blind Hary is an epic poem that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the life of the legendary Scottish hero, Sir William Wallace. From his humble beginnings to his rise as a fearless leader, the poem delves into the trials and tribulations faced by Wallace as he fights for the freedom of his beloved Scotland. With vivid descriptions of battles, political intrigue, and personal sacrifices, 'Wallace' captures the essence of a man who became a symbol of resistance against oppression.

Genres:
Epic poetry, Historical fiction, Adventure

Themes:
Heroism, Patriotism, Freedom, Sacrifice
