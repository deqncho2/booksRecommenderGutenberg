Synopsis: In 'My Private Menagerie: from The Works of Theophile Gautier Volume 19', Gautier takes us on a captivating journey through his personal collection of exotic animals. From majestic tigers to graceful peacocks, he unveils the enchanting world of his private menagerie, filled with awe-inspiring creatures from all corners of the globe. As he shares his encounters and observations, Gautier's vivid descriptions transport us to a realm where the boundaries between man and beast blur, and where the beauty and mystery of the animal kingdom come alive.

Genres: Memoir, Natural History, Travelogue

Themes: Human-animal connection, Beauty of nature, Exploration and discovery
