Synopsis: 
In "Jean Craig Grows Up" by Kay Lyttleton, we follow the captivating journey of Jean Craig as she navigates the challenges and triumphs of adolescence. From her first love to her dreams of becoming a writer, Jean's coming-of-age story is filled with heartwarming moments, unexpected twists, and the timeless struggle of finding one's true self. As Jean faces the complexities of growing up, readers will be drawn into her world, eagerly turning the pages to see how she overcomes obstacles and discovers her own unique path in life.

Genres: 
Young Adult Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: 
Identity, Self-discovery, Love and Relationships
