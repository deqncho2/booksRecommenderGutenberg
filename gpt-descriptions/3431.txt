Synopsis: Set in 19th century Italy, 'The Gadfly' follows the life of Arthur Burton, a young Englishman who becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and revolution. As Arthur navigates through love, betrayal, and personal loss, he finds himself torn between his loyalty to his country and his desire for justice. With his sharp intellect and rebellious spirit, Arthur becomes a symbol of hope for the oppressed, leading him on a thrilling journey that will challenge his beliefs and change the course of his life forever.

Genres: Historical fiction, Political thriller, Romance

Themes: Revolution, Love and betrayal, Identity and personal growth
