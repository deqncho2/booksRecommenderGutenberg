Synopsis: In the second volume of the Warren Commission Hearings, the United States delves deeper into the investigation of the assassination of President John F. Kennedy. Through a series of testimonies and evidence, the commission seeks to uncover the truth behind this tragic event. As witnesses take the stand and provide their accounts, a web of conspiracy and intrigue begins to unravel, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the pages.

Genres: Non-fiction, True Crime, Historical

Themes: Assassination, Conspiracy, Government Investigation
