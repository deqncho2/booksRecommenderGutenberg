Synopsis: In 'The Altar at Midnight' by C. M. Kornbluth, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious discovery that could change the course of humanity. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between science and superstition blurs. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and confront the forces that seek to control his groundbreaking invention.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Scientific exploration, Power and control, Morality and ethics
