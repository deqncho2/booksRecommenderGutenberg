Synopsis: In "Molly Brown's Junior Days," follow the adventures of Molly Brown as she navigates the challenges and excitement of her junior year. From new friendships and unexpected romances to academic pressures and personal growth, Molly's journey is filled with laughter, tears, and unforgettable moments. Will Molly find her place in the world and discover what truly matters to her?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Young Adult, Contemporary

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Self-discovery
