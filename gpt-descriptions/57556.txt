Synopsis: In 'Doctor Rabbit and Ki-Yi Coyote' by Thomas C. Hinkle, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure through the animal kingdom. When Doctor Rabbit's peaceful life is disrupted by the mischievous Ki-Yi Coyote, chaos ensues as the two engage in a battle of wits and cunning. As the story unfolds, unexpected alliances are formed, secrets are revealed, and the true nature of friendship is put to the test. Will Doctor Rabbit be able to outsmart Ki-Yi Coyote and restore harmony to the animal kingdom?

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Problem-solving
