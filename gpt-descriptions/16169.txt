Synopsis: 
In "The Tragedy of Dido Queene of Carthage," Marlowe weaves a captivating tale of love, power, and betrayal. Set in the ancient city of Carthage, the story follows the passionate and ambitious Queen Dido as she navigates the complexities of ruling a kingdom while falling deeply in love with the Trojan hero Aeneas. As their relationship unfolds, Dido's desires clash with her responsibilities, leading to a tragic chain of events that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Power and Ambition, Fate and Destiny
