Synopsis: In "The Last Crash" by Kenneth Latour, a brilliant but troubled computer programmer, Alex, finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger when he stumbles upon a mysterious algorithm that has the power to predict catastrophic financial crashes. As he races against time to uncover the truth behind this powerful tool, Alex becomes entangled in a high-stakes game where the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. With his life on the line, Alex must navigate a treacherous world of deceit and manipulation, where the consequences of his actions could have far-reaching implications.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Techno-thriller

Themes: Financial manipulation, Technology's impact on society, Trust and betrayal
