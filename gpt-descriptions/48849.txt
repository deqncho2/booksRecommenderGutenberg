Synopsis: 
In the small town of Willow Creek, Frank Armstrong is known for his extraordinary talent as a drop kicker on the local football team. But when tragedy strikes and Frank's dreams are shattered, he finds himself facing a crossroads that will test his resilience and determination. As he navigates through unexpected challenges and encounters a mysterious mentor, Frank must summon the courage to redefine his identity and pursue a new path that will lead him to unexpected places. 'Frank Armstrong, Drop Kicker' is a captivating coming-of-age tale that explores the power of perseverance and the transformative journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Sports, Drama

Themes: Resilience, Identity, Self-discovery
