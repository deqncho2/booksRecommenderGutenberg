Synopsis: In 'The Floating Island of Madness' by Kirby, Jason, a group of explorers embarks on a perilous journey to a mysterious floating island that has suddenly appeared in the middle of the ocean. As they venture deeper into the island's dark secrets, they encounter unimaginable horrors and face their own sanity being tested. With each step, the line between reality and madness becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning what is truly lurking on the island.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Madness, Exploration, Survival
