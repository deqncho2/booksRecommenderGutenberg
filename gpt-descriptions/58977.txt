Synopsis: In 'The Confessions of Al Ghazzali', the renowned Islamic philosopher and theologian, Al Ghazzali, takes us on a profound journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. Through a series of introspective reflections, he delves into the depths of his own doubts, fears, and desires, ultimately seeking to reconcile his intellectual pursuits with his spiritual yearnings. As he grapples with the complexities of faith, reason, and the pursuit of truth, Al Ghazzali's confessions offer a captivating exploration of the human condition and the quest for meaning.

Genres: Autobiography, Philosophy, Spirituality

Themes: Self-reflection, Faith and Reason, Spiritual Awakening
