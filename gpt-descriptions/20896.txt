Synopsis: In 'Carry's Rose; or, the Magic of Kindness. A Tale for the Young', young Carry embarks on a magical journey after receiving a mysterious rose. As she navigates through enchanting lands and encounters various characters, Carry learns the transformative power of kindness and the importance of empathy. This heartwarming tale is filled with adventure, wonder, and valuable life lessons that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Kindness, Empathy, Transformation
