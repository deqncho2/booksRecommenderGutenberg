Synopsis: 
'Elfin land: and other poems' by Ball, Benjamin West is a captivating collection of enchanting verses that transport readers to a world of magic and wonder. From the mystical realm of elves to the ethereal beauty of nature, these poems explore the depths of imagination and evoke a sense of awe and fascination. With each turn of the page, readers will be swept away on a poetic journey that celebrates the power of dreams and the enchantment found in the smallest of things.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Magical Realism

Themes: Imagination, Nature, Wonder, Dreams
