Synopsis: In 'The French in Algiers: The Soldier of the Foreign Legion; and The Prisoners of Abd-el-Kader', Alby and Lamping take readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of French colonization in Algeria. Through the eyes of a soldier in the Foreign Legion, the story delves into the complexities of war, love, and loyalty amidst the backdrop of a fierce resistance led by the formidable Abd-el-Kader. As the soldier navigates the treacherous landscape of Algiers, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal dilemmas that will test his resolve and shape his destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Colonialism, Identity, Loyalty, Love and Betrayal
