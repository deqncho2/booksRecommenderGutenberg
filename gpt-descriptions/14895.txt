Synopsis: In 'All He Knew: A Story' by John Habberton, we follow the life of a young boy named Bertie, who is born blind and deaf. Despite his disabilities, Bertie's remarkable resilience and determination to experience the world around him captivate those who cross his path. As he grows older, Bertie's unique perspective challenges societal norms and forces those around him to question their own limitations. This heartwarming and thought-provoking tale explores the power of human connection, the resilience of the human spirit, and the boundless possibilities that exist within us all.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Resilience, Human connection, Overcoming adversity, Perception
