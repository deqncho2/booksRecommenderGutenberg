Synopsis: In 'The Conspiracy of Gianluigi Fieschi, or, Genoa in the sixteenth century,' Emanuele Celesia takes us on a thrilling journey through the political intrigue and power struggles of sixteenth-century Genoa. The story revolves around the ambitious and enigmatic Gianluigi Fieschi, who becomes entangled in a dangerous conspiracy that could change the course of history. As the plot thickens, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the fate of Genoa hangs in the balance. With its gripping narrative and complex characters, this historical novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the conspiracy.

Genres: Historical fiction, Political thriller

Themes: Power struggles, Conspiracy, Betrayal, Ambition
