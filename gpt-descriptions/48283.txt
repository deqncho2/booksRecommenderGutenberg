Synopsis: 
'Forest, Rock, and Stream: A series of twenty steel line-engravings' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the natural wonders of the world. Each engraving beautifully captures the essence of forests, rocks, and streams, inviting readers to immerse themselves in the serene and awe-inspiring landscapes. From lush green forests to majestic rocky cliffs and tranquil flowing streams, the engravings evoke a sense of wonder and appreciation for the beauty of nature. As readers explore each engraving, they are transported to different corners of the world, experiencing the diverse landscapes and the harmony between the elements. 

Genres: Art, Nature, Travel

Themes: Beauty of nature, Serenity, Appreciation for the natural world
