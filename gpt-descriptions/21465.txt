Synopsis: In 'Hurricane Hurry' by William Henry Giles Kingston, a young sailor named Jack finds himself caught in the midst of a powerful hurricane while on a perilous voyage. As the storm rages on, Jack must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to survive the treacherous waters and make it back to safety. Along the way, he encounters unexpected challenges and forms unlikely alliances that will test his character and determination. Will Jack be able to navigate through the chaos and find his way home?

Genres: Adventure, Action, Survival

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Survival
