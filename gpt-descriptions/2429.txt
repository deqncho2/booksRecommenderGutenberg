Synopsis: In the harsh and unforgiving wilderness of the Canadian North, a group of men find themselves entangled in a deadly game of survival and betrayal. As they face the imminent threat of death, their true natures are revealed, and the lengths they will go to in order to preserve their own lives become shockingly apparent. With each twist and turn, the tension rises, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, questioning who will emerge victorious in this battle of wits and cunning.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Short Story

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Human Nature
