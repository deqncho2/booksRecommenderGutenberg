Synopsis: In 'Abdu'l-Bahá's Tablet to Dr. Forel, a renowned Swiss psychiatrist receives a profound letter from 'Abdu'l-Bahá, the son of the founder of the Bahá'í Faith. This captivating correspondence delves into the nature of the human soul, the purpose of life, and the interconnectedness of all beings. As Dr. Forel grapples with these profound insights, he embarks on a transformative journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening.

Genres: Spiritual, Philosophical, Epistolary

Themes: Soul, Purpose of Life, Interconnectedness, Self-Discovery, Spiritual Awakening
