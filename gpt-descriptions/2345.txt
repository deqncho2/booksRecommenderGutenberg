Synopsis: In "The Adventure of the Red Circle," Sherlock Holmes and Dr. Watson are presented with a peculiar case involving a mysterious lodger and a secretive organization. As they delve deeper into the investigation, they uncover a web of intrigue and danger that threatens to unravel the lives of those involved. With their keen deductive skills and unwavering determination, Holmes and Watson must navigate through a complex puzzle to uncover the truth and protect the innocent. 

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Loyalty, Identity
