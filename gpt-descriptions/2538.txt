Synopsis: 
'Poems and Tales from Romania' is a captivating collection of poetry and stories that takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the rich cultural tapestry of Romania. From enchanting folk tales to poignant poems, this book offers a glimpse into the country's history, traditions, and the resilience of its people. With vivid imagery and lyrical prose, the authors transport readers to a world filled with mythical creatures, passionate love stories, and the struggles of everyday life. Whether it's a tale of bravery or a heartfelt poem, each piece in this collection is sure to leave readers spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Folklore

Themes: Romanian culture, Tradition, Love, Resilience
