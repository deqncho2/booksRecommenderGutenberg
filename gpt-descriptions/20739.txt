Synopsis: In "Rebels of the Red Planet" by Charles L. Fontenay, a group of rebels find themselves stranded on Mars, fighting against a tyrannical regime that seeks to control the planet's resources. As they navigate the treacherous Martian landscape, they must confront their own fears and forge unlikely alliances in order to bring about change. With thrilling action, unexpected twists, and a richly imagined world, this gripping science fiction adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Dystopian

Themes: Rebellion, Oppression, Survival
