Synopsis: 
In 'Evolution, Old & New: Or, the Theories of Buffon, Dr. Erasmus Darwin and Lamarck,; as compared with that of Charles Darwin,' Samuel Butler delves into the fascinating world of evolutionary theories. Through a captivating exploration of the ideas put forth by Buffon, Dr. Erasmus Darwin, Lamarck, and Charles Darwin, Butler presents a thought-provoking analysis of the evolution of life on Earth. With meticulous research and insightful observations, he compares and contrasts these theories, shedding light on the different perspectives that have shaped our understanding of the natural world. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Evolution, Scientific theories, Comparative analysis
