Synopsis: In 'The Pursuit of the House-Boat', the renowned detective Sherlock Holmes leads a group of famous literary characters on a thrilling adventure. When their beloved house-boat is stolen, they embark on a quest to recover it, encountering eccentric characters and facing unexpected challenges along the way. With Holmes at the helm, this humorous and imaginative tale takes readers on a wild ride through a world where fiction and reality collide.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Literary References
