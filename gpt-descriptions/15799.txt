Synopsis: In "Walter Harland: Or, Memories of the Past," Harriet S. Caswell takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Walter Harland, a man haunted by his past. As the story unfolds, secrets, betrayals, and unexpected connections are revealed, leading Walter on a quest for redemption and self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, this gripping tale explores the complexities of human relationships and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Redemption, Secrets, Betrayal, Self-discovery, Love
