Synopsis: In "Problems of Conduct: An Introductory Survey of Ethics," Drake and Durant take readers on a thought-provoking journey through the complex realm of ethics. Through engaging anecdotes and philosophical insights, they explore the various dilemmas and challenges that arise in our everyday lives. From examining the nature of moral responsibility to questioning the foundations of ethical theories, this book offers a captivating exploration of the intricacies of human conduct.

Genres: Philosophy, Ethics, Non-fiction

Themes: Moral responsibility, Ethical theories, Dilemmas of conduct
