Synopsis: In 'Regulations' by Leinster, Murray, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but the fate of the entire world. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and danger to protect his findings and prevent a catastrophic event from unfolding.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific ethics, Conspiracy, Survival
