Synopsis: In the quaint village of Nestleton Magna, a captivating tale unfolds, revealing the intricate web of relationships and the deep-rooted traditions of Yorkshire Methodism. As the story unravels, secrets are unveiled, love blossoms, and the strength of community is tested. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Yorkshire countryside, this heartwarming narrative explores the lives of the villagers and their unwavering faith in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Community bonds, Love and relationships, Tradition and change
