Synopsis: 

When five siblings stumble upon a mysterious creature called "It" during their summer holiday, they are granted one wish each day. However, they soon discover that their wishes don't always turn out as expected, leading to a series of misadventures and unexpected consequences. As they navigate through their magical encounters, the children learn valuable lessons about the power of wishes and the importance of appreciating what they already have.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Family, Contentment
