Synopsis: 
In the kingdom of Britain, an aging king named Lear decides to divide his kingdom among his three daughters based on their declarations of love for him. However, this seemingly innocent act sets off a chain of events that leads to betrayal, madness, and ultimately, tragedy. As Lear's power diminishes, he is forced to confront the consequences of his actions and question the true nature of love, loyalty, and the human condition.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Power and authority, Betrayal, Madness, Filial ingratitude, Justice and injustice
