Synopsis: 
In 'The Immortal; Or, One of the "Forty."', Alphonse Daudet takes us on a captivating journey through the streets of Paris, where we meet a mysterious group known as the "Forty." Among them is a man who claims to be immortal, having lived for centuries. As we delve into his extraordinary life, filled with love, betrayal, and secrets, we are left questioning the boundaries of mortality and the power of human connection. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn deeper into the enigmatic world of the "Forty" and the immortality they seek.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Immortality, Love and Betrayal, Secrets and Mystery
