Synopsis: In "The Witch Doctor and other Rhodesian Studies" by Frank Worthington, readers are transported to the captivating world of Rhodesia, where they will encounter a diverse cast of characters and witness the clash between traditional African beliefs and the influence of Western civilization. From the enigmatic witch doctor who possesses mysterious powers to the courageous settlers who navigate the challenges of a new land, this collection of stories offers a mesmerizing exploration of the human spirit and the complexities of cultural exchange.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Cultural Studies

Themes: Cultural clash, Colonialism, Supernatural beliefs, Identity, Resilience
