Synopsis: In 'The Paliser Case' by Edgar Saltus, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing murder mystery that takes him on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of society. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances, leading him to question the very nature of justice. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate a treacherous landscape of power, corruption, and moral ambiguity, all while racing against time to solve the enigmatic Paliser case.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Justice and morality, Power and corruption
