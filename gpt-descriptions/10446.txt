Synopsis: In 'The Green Flag, and Other Stories of War and Sport', Arthur Conan Doyle takes readers on a thrilling journey through tales of both war and sport. From the battlefields of the Boer War to the intense world of cricket, these stories captivate with their vivid descriptions and unexpected twists. Whether it's a soldier's harrowing escape or a cricket match filled with suspense, Doyle's storytelling prowess shines through, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Sports Fiction

Themes: Courage, Honor, Competition, Camaraderie
