Synopsis: 

"The United Empire Loyalists: A Chronicle of the Great Migration [1914 ed.]" by Wallace, W. Stewart is a captivating historical account that delves into the lives of the United Empire Loyalists during the American Revolution. This meticulously researched chronicle follows the incredible journey of these loyalists as they face unimaginable challenges and make the difficult decision to leave their homes and embark on a new life in British North America. Through vivid storytelling and compelling narratives, the author brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices of these brave individuals, shedding light on a lesser-known chapter of history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Migration

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Resilience, Historical events and their impact
