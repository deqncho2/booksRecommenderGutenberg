Synopsis: 
When a wealthy patriarch passes away, his family eagerly awaits the reading of his will. However, to their shock and dismay, the will mysteriously goes missing. As tensions rise and suspicions grow, a determined young lawyer takes it upon himself to uncover the truth behind the missing will. With a web of family secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected twists, Agatha Christie's "The Missing Will" will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Greed, Family dynamics, Deception
