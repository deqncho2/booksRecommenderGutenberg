Synopsis: 

Step back in time to 1863 and explore the vast network of post offices in Canada, as documented by the Canada Post Office Department. This comprehensive list unveils the names of the postmasters across the country, providing a fascinating glimpse into the individuals who played a crucial role in connecting communities during this era. From bustling cities to remote villages, this compilation offers a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes and people that shaped Canada's postal system. Discover the untold stories and hidden histories behind these post offices, as you immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of Canada's past.

Genres: Historical, Reference

Themes: Communication, Community, Canadian History
