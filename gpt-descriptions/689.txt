Synopsis: In "The Kreutzer Sonata and Other Stories," Leo Tolstoy presents a collection of captivating tales that delve into the complexities of human relationships and the moral dilemmas they entail. From a passionate love affair that leads to tragic consequences, to a man's struggle with his own desires and societal expectations, these stories explore the depths of human emotions and the consequences of our actions. Tolstoy's masterful storytelling and thought-provoking narratives will keep readers engrossed from beginning to end.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Literary Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Morality and ethics, Society and its constraints, Human nature and desires
