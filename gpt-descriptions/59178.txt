Synopsis: In 'Midwinter: Certain Travellers in Old England', John Buchan takes readers on a captivating journey through the wintry landscapes of old England. Set in the early 17th century, the story follows a group of diverse and enigmatic characters who find themselves thrown together by fate. As they navigate through treacherous terrains and encounter mysterious events, their lives become intertwined in ways they could never have imagined. With secrets to uncover and dangers to face, the travelers must rely on their wits and resilience to survive the harsh winter and the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Historical fiction, adventure, mystery

Themes: Fate and destiny, survival, the power of friendship, the allure of the unknown
