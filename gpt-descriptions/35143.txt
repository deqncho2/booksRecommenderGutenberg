Synopsis: In 'The Martins Of Cro' Martin, Vol. I (of II)' by Charles James Lever, we are introduced to the eccentric and captivating Martin family, who reside in the picturesque village of Cro' Martin. As the story unfolds, we follow the misadventures and romantic escapades of the Martins, including the charming but impulsive Roland, the vivacious and headstrong Kate, and the enigmatic and mysterious Sir Simon. From hilarious misunderstandings to unexpected twists of fate, this delightful tale takes us on a rollercoaster ride of laughter, love, and intrigue, leaving us eagerly anticipating the second volume.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Family dynamics, Love and relationships, Social class
