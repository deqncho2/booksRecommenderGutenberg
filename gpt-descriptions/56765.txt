Synopsis: 
"The Bashful Earthquake, & Other Fables and Verses" by Oliver Herford is a delightful collection of whimsical fables and verses that will transport readers to a world of imagination and wit. From the bashful earthquake to the adventures of a mischievous moonbeam, each story and poem is filled with clever wordplay and charming illustrations that will captivate readers of all ages. With its unique blend of humor and wisdom, this collection is sure to leave readers with a smile on their face and a renewed appreciation for the beauty of language.

Genres: Poetry, Fables, Humor

Themes: Imagination, Wit, Wordplay
