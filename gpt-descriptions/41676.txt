Synopsis: In 'Armour & Weapons', Ffoulkes takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of arms and armor, exploring the intricate craftsmanship and fascinating stories behind these iconic objects. From the battlefields of ancient civilizations to the medieval knights and Renaissance warriors, this book unveils the evolution of weaponry and the art of defense. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Ffoulkes brings to life the awe-inspiring world of armor and weapons, revealing the secrets and significance of these remarkable artifacts.

Genres: History, Art, Military

Themes: Warfare, Craftsmanship, Historical Significance
