Synopsis: 
In 'John Caldigate' by Anthony Trollope, a young Englishman named John Caldigate sets off on a journey to Australia in search of fortune during the gold rush. However, his life takes an unexpected turn when he becomes entangled in a scandalous love affair. As he navigates the challenges of love, loyalty, and societal expectations, John must confront the consequences of his actions and make difficult choices that will shape his future.

Genres: 
Romance, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Morality and Ethics, Social Expectations
