Synopsis: In "A New History of the United States: The greater republic," Charles Morris takes readers on a captivating journey through the growth and achievements of the United States, from its earliest days of discovery and settlement to the present. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Morris unveils the fascinating events, influential figures, and pivotal moments that have shaped the nation's history. From the struggles of the early colonists to the forging of a new nation, the book explores the triumphs and challenges faced by Americans throughout the centuries. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for bringing history to life, Morris paints a vivid picture of the United States' remarkable journey, making this book an essential read for anyone seeking a comprehensive understanding of America's past.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: American history, Exploration and settlement, Nation-building, Progress and achievements
