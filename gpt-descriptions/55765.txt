Synopsis: In 'The Faery Queen and Her Knights: Stories Retold from Edmund Spenser', Alfred John Church brings to life the enchanting world of Edmund Spenser's epic poem, 'The Faerie Queene'. This collection of stories follows the adventures of the noble knights and their encounters with mystical creatures, treacherous villains, and the captivating Faery Queen herself. From the valiant Redcrosse Knight to the courageous Britomart, each tale is filled with bravery, romance, and the timeless struggle between good and evil. Church's retelling captures the essence of Spenser's original work, transporting readers to a realm where chivalry, honor, and magic intertwine.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Poetry

Themes: Chivalry, Good vs. Evil, Romance, Heroism
