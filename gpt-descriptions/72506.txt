Synopsis: In "Listen, children ... listen!" by Wallace West, a small town is plagued by a mysterious phenomenon that leaves its residents in a state of fear and confusion. As the townspeople struggle to uncover the truth behind these unsettling occurrences, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and secrets. With each revelation, the line between reality and the supernatural becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of their own fears.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Secrets, Reality vs. Supernatural
