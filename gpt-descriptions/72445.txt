Synopsis: In 'Parents and Children' by Charlotte M. Mason, a seemingly ordinary family is thrown into turmoil when a long-lost relative unexpectedly arrives on their doorstep. As secrets are revealed and tensions rise, the family must confront their past and navigate the complexities of their relationships. With unexpected twists and emotional depth, this gripping tale explores the intricate dynamics between parents and children, and the profound impact that family secrets can have on our lives.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Family

Themes: Family relationships, Secrets and revelations, Identity, Forgiveness
