Synopsis: Under the Mikado's Flag takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the fascinating world of Japan, where a group of young soldiers of fortune find themselves embroiled in a thrilling adventure. As they navigate the treacherous landscapes and encounter unexpected allies and enemies, their loyalty and courage are put to the ultimate test. With danger lurking at every turn, the young soldiers must rely on their wits and skills to survive and fulfill their mission. This action-packed tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of these daring young heroes.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Exploration, Cultural Differences
