Synopsis: 
'Tales of the Punjab: Folklore of India' by Flora Annie Webster Steel is a captivating collection of traditional Indian folktales that transport readers to the enchanting world of Punjab. From magical creatures and brave heroes to cunning tricksters and beautiful princesses, this anthology weaves together a tapestry of captivating stories that have been passed down through generations. Each tale is filled with vivid imagery, rich cultural details, and timeless moral lessons, making it a delightful read for both children and adults alike. Prepare to be immersed in a world of wonder and adventure as you journey through the vibrant landscapes of Punjab and discover the hidden treasures of Indian folklore.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural

Themes: Heroism, Love and Sacrifice, Morality, Trickery and Deception
