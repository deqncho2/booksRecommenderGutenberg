Synopsis: In 'East in the Morning' by David E. Fisher, a young woman named Lily embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her mysterious past. As she delves deeper into her family history, she discovers a hidden world filled with ancient secrets, unexpected alliances, and a destiny she never could have imagined. With each step she takes, Lily becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where the line between good and evil becomes blurred. As she races against time to unlock the truth, Lily must confront her own inner demons and make choices that will shape not only her own fate but also the fate of those she loves.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Family, Destiny, Good vs. Evil
