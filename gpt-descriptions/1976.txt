Synopsis:
In "Peter Ruff and the Double Four," the enigmatic and resourceful Peter Ruff finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. When a notorious criminal organization known as the Double Four threatens the peace and security of London, Ruff is called upon to use his cunning and intelligence to bring them down. As he delves deeper into the dangerous underworld, Ruff must navigate a treacherous game of cat and mouse, where nothing is as it seems and betrayal lurks around every corner. With his quick wit and audacious charm, Ruff must outsmart his adversaries and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Crime, Deception, Heroism
