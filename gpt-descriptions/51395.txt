Synopsis: 
In 'Survival Type' by Jesse F. Bone, a man finds himself stranded on a deserted island with no hope of rescue. As he battles hunger, isolation, and the harsh elements, he is forced to make a series of unthinkable choices in order to survive. With each decision, his humanity is pushed to its limits, blurring the line between right and wrong. This gripping tale of survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the lengths they would go to in order to stay alive.

Genres: Thriller, Psychological Fiction, Survival Fiction

Themes: Morality, Survival, Human Nature
