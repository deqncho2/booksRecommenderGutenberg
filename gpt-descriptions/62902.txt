Synopsis: In 'The 13th District: A Story of a Candidate' by Whitlock Brand, a charismatic and ambitious candidate finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue as he runs for office in the highly competitive 13th district. As he navigates the treacherous world of politics, he must confront personal demons, make difficult choices, and uncover shocking secrets that could change the course of his campaign and his life forever.

Genres: Political thriller, drama, suspense

Themes: Ambition, power, corruption, personal sacrifice, redemption
