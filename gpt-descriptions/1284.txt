Synopsis: In 'Tom Swift and His Air Scout; Or, Uncle Sam's Mastery of the Sky' by Appleton, Victor, young inventor Tom Swift embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him high above the clouds. When a mysterious aircraft threatens national security, Tom is called upon to develop a cutting-edge air scout to aid Uncle Sam in maintaining control of the sky. As Tom faces dangerous challenges and encounters unexpected allies and enemies, he must use his ingenuity and resourcefulness to ensure the safety of his country and the success of his invention.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Invention and innovation, Patriotism, National security
