Synopsis:
In "Louis XIV.: Makers of History Series," John S. C. Abbott takes us on a captivating journey through the life and reign of one of history's most influential monarchs. From his ascension to the throne at the tender age of four to his transformation into the Sun King, Louis XIV's story is one of power, ambition, and the pursuit of absolute authority. As we delve into the intricacies of his court, we witness the political intrigues, the lavish lifestyles, and the grandeur that defined the era. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Abbott paints a vivid portrait of a ruler who shaped the destiny of France and left an indelible mark on European history.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power, Absolutism, Court Life, Political Intrigue
