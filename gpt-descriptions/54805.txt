Synopsis: In "A Servant of Satan: Romantic Career of Prado the Assassin" by Berard, Louis, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the life of Prado, a notorious assassin. Set in the backdrop of a dark and mysterious underworld, the story follows Prado's rise to power and his dangerous encounters with love, betrayal, and redemption. As Prado navigates through a web of deceit and violence, the reader is left questioning the boundaries between good and evil, and the lengths one will go to protect those they care about.

Genres: Crime fiction, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Morality, Love and obsession, Redemption
