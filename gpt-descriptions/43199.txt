Synopsis: In 'The Last Tenant' by B. L. Farjeon, a mysterious and reclusive tenant moves into a dilapidated London boarding house, arousing the curiosity and suspicion of his fellow residents. As the enigmatic tenant's presence becomes increasingly unsettling, the boarding house's inhabitants find themselves drawn into a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected twists. With each passing day, the tension escalates, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Suspicion, Intrigue
