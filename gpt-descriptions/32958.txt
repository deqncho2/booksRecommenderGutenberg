Synopsis: 

'Edge Hill: The Battle and Battlefield; With Notes on Banbury & Thereabout' by Edwin Alfred Walford takes readers on a captivating journey through history, delving into the details of the Battle of Edge Hill and the surrounding area. With meticulous research and vivid descriptions, Walford brings to life the events and characters that shaped this significant battle. From the strategic maneuvers to the personal stories of soldiers and civilians, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the battle and its impact on the region. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Local history

Themes: War and conflict, Historical preservation, Local heritage
