Synopsis: In 'Book of Etiquette, Volume II' by Lillian Eichler Watson, readers are invited into a world of refined manners and social graces. This comprehensive guide delves into the intricacies of etiquette, offering invaluable advice on everything from formal dining to proper introductions. With a focus on modern society, Watson's witty and insightful writing style makes this book an engaging and indispensable resource for anyone seeking to navigate social situations with elegance and confidence.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Reference

Themes: Etiquette, Social skills, Politeness
