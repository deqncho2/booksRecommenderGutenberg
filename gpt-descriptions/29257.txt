Synopsis: In the bustling streets of New York City, two young Arab immigrants, Khalid and Shakib, embark on a journey of self-discovery and cultural exploration. As they navigate the complexities of American society, their experiences and encounters shape their understanding of identity, love, and the pursuit of the American Dream. Through vivid descriptions and thought-provoking dialogues, 'The Book of Khalid' takes readers on a captivating journey that challenges societal norms and celebrates the beauty of diversity.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Cultural

Themes: Identity, Immigration, Cultural assimilation, Love and relationships
