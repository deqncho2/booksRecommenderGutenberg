Synopsis: 

Step back in time to April 1970 and immerse yourself in the world of Digital Equipment Corporation's 'Digital PDP15 Price List'. This captivating document takes readers on a journey through the technological landscape of the era, showcasing the cutting-edge offerings of the PDP15 computer system. From the excitement of new hardware to the anticipation of software advancements, this price list unveils a world of possibilities that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Historical, Technology, Business

Themes: Technological innovation, Market competition, Pricing strategies
