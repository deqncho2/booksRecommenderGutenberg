Synopsis: In "The Strange Story of Harper's Ferry, with Legends of the Surrounding Country," Joseph Barry takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious and eerie tales surrounding the historic town of Harper's Ferry. From ghostly apparitions to hidden treasures, the author weaves together a collection of legends and folklore that will leave readers both intrigued and unsettled. As the stories unfold, secrets are revealed, and the line between reality and myth becomes blurred. Prepare to be transported to a world where the past and present collide, and where the truth may be stranger than fiction.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Legends and folklore, History and its impact on the present, Mystery and intrigue
