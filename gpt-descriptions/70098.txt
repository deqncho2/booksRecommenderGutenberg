Synopsis: 
In the "Handbook of Old Burial Hill, Plymouth, Massachusetts," author Frank H. Perkins takes readers on a captivating journey through the history, famous inhabitants, and intriguing epitaphs of one of America's oldest cemeteries. From the Pilgrims who arrived on the Mayflower to notable figures of the Revolutionary War, this handbook unveils the stories and secrets buried within the hallowed grounds of Old Burial Hill. With vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, Perkins invites readers to explore the rich tapestry of Plymouth's past, uncovering the lives and legacies of those who rest beneath the ancient tombstones.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travel guide, Local history

Themes: History, Heritage, Commemoration, Legacy
