Synopsis: In the remote Arctic town of Fortitude, a gruesome murder sets off a chain of events that unravels the dark secrets hidden beneath the icy surface. As the tight-knit community grapples with the shocking crime, they soon realize that their idyllic town is not as innocent as it seems. With tensions rising and trust eroding, the residents of Fortitude must confront their own demons and face the chilling truth that lurks within their midst.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Isolation, Survival, Morality, Betrayal
