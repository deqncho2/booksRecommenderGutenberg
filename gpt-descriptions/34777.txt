Synopsis: 
'A Lame Dog's Diary' follows the journey of a witty and observant canine named Fido, who narrates his daily adventures and encounters with humans in a charming and humorous way. From his unique perspective, Fido provides a delightful and heartwarming account of his life, filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fiction, Humor, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Human-Animal Bond
