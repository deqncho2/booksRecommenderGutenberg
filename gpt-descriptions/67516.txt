Synopsis: 
In 'Object, Matrimony' by B. M. Bower, a young woman named Mary Thorne finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits a mysterious object from her late father. As she delves deeper into the truth behind the object, Mary becomes entangled in a dangerous game of love, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With her life on the line, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the object's true significance and protect those she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and betrayal
