Synopsis: In 'The Incubator Baby' by Butler, Ellis Parker, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious incubator baby arrives, captivating the community with its extraordinary abilities. As the townspeople become obsessed with the baby's potential, they are forced to confront their own desires, fears, and the consequences of their actions. With humor and wit, Butler weaves a tale that explores the power of curiosity and the lengths people will go to in order to fulfill their own ambitions.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Humor

Themes: Curiosity, Ambition, Consequences
