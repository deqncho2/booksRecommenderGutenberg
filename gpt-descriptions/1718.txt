Synopsis:
In 'Manalive', a mysterious and eccentric man named Innocent Smith bursts into the lives of a group of people, turning their world upside down. With his infectious energy and unconventional behavior, Smith challenges their preconceived notions of what it means to be alive. As the group tries to unravel the enigma that is Innocent Smith, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling and thought-provoking adventure that will make them question their own existence.

Genres:
Mystery, Comedy, Philosophical Fiction

Themes:
The meaning of life, the nature of happiness, the importance of living in the present moment
