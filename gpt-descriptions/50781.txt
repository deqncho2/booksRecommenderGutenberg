Synopsis: In 'The Mystery Ship: A Story of the 'Q' Ships During the Great War', Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a thrilling journey into the dangerous world of 'Q' ships, disguised vessels used to combat German U-boats during World War I. As the crew of the mystery ship embarks on their perilous mission, they must navigate treacherous waters, outsmart their enemies, and face the constant threat of being discovered. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Deception, Survival
