Synopsis: In 'The Jewel of Seven Stars' by Bram Stoker, a renowned Egyptologist becomes obsessed with an ancient Egyptian queen and her mysterious powers. As he delves deeper into her story, he unwittingly awakens a force that threatens to unleash chaos upon the world. With the help of a determined young woman, he must unravel the secrets of the past and confront the terrifying consequences of his actions.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, mystery

Themes: Ancient curses, obsession, the power of the supernatural
