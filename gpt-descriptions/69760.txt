Synopsis: In "Brothers and Sisters," Abbie Farwell Brown weaves a captivating tale of sibling bonds, secrets, and the power of family. Set in a small New England town, the story follows the lives of the four Abbott siblings as they navigate the complexities of love, loss, and the pursuit of their dreams. As their individual paths intertwine, a shocking revelation threatens to unravel the very foundation of their family. With richly drawn characters and a compelling narrative, this novel explores the enduring ties that bind brothers and sisters together, and the sacrifices they are willing to make for one another.

Genres: Fiction, Family Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Sibling Relationships, Family Secrets, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
