Synopsis: 
The 'Dictionary of Quotations from Ancient and Modern, English and Foreign Sources' is a comprehensive collection of quotes, phrases, maxims, proverbs, and sayings from wise men throughout history. This vast compilation covers a wide range of topics including life, literature, speculation, science, art, religion, and morals, with a particular focus on their modern aspects. Reverend James Wood has meticulously curated this dictionary, offering readers a treasure trove of wisdom and insight from various cultures and time periods. Whether you seek inspiration, guidance, or simply enjoy the beauty of language, this book is sure to captivate and enlighten.

Genres: Reference, Quotation Collection, Philosophy

Themes: Wisdom, Inspiration, Cultural Diversity, Human Experience
