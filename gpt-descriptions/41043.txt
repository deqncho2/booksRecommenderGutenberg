Synopsis: In "The Old First Massachusetts Coast Artillery in War and Peace," Frederick Morse Cutler takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of a remarkable military unit. From their early days as a volunteer militia to their crucial role in defending the coast during World War II, this book delves into the triumphs, sacrifices, and camaraderie of the soldiers who served in the Old First Massachusetts Coast Artillery. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Cutler brings to life the battles they fought, the challenges they faced, and the enduring legacy they left behind.

Genres: Military history, Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Military strategy, Historical legacy
