Synopsis: In 'Wild Life Near Home' by Dallas Lore Sharp, embark on a captivating journey through the hidden wonders of nature that exist right outside our doorstep. From the enchanting melodies of birdsong to the intricate dance of insects, this book unveils the extraordinary wildlife that thrives in our everyday surroundings. With vivid descriptions and insightful observations, Sharp invites readers to explore the intricate web of life that exists just beyond our windows, offering a fresh perspective on the beauty and complexity of the natural world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Environmental Literature

Themes: Nature appreciation, Biodiversity, Ecological interconnectedness
