Synopsis: In 'The Bashful Lover', Paul de Kock takes us on a delightful journey through the romantic misadventures of a timid young man named Charles. As Charles navigates the complexities of love and courtship, he finds himself entangled in a series of humorous and often embarrassing situations. From mistaken identities to comical misunderstandings, this charming novel explores the trials and tribulations of a bashful lover in 19th-century Paris. Will Charles overcome his shyness and find true love? Join him on this entertaining and heartwarming escapade to find out.

Genres: Romantic comedy, Historical fiction

Themes: Love and courtship, Social expectations, Humor
