Synopsis: 
In 'New Word-Analysis: Or, School Etymology of English Derivative Words', William Swinton takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins and meanings of English words. With a unique approach to etymology, Swinton unveils the fascinating stories behind the words we use every day, shedding light on their historical, cultural, and linguistic significance. From the humblest of nouns to the most complex of adjectives, this book delves into the intricate web of language, unraveling its secrets and inviting readers to explore the rich tapestry of English vocabulary. Prepare to be enthralled by the hidden tales behind the words we thought we knew.

Genres: Educational, Linguistics, Reference

Themes: Etymology, Language, History
