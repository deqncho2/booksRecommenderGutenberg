Synopsis: In 'Manners for Men' by Humphry, Mrs., a young man named Edward embarks on a journey of self-improvement as he navigates the complex world of social etiquette. From mastering the art of conversation to understanding proper attire, Edward must learn the rules of society in order to succeed in both his personal and professional life. Along the way, he encounters a colorful cast of characters who challenge his beliefs and push him to question the true meaning of manners. Will Edward be able to transform himself into a refined gentleman, or will he discover that true manners come from the heart?

Genres: Fiction, Self-help, Coming-of-age

Themes: Social etiquette, Personal growth, Identity, Relationships
