Synopsis: 
'Under the Andes' by Rex Stout is a thrilling adventure novel that takes readers on a journey deep beneath the majestic Andes Mountains. When two brothers, a scientist and an explorer, stumble upon a hidden civilization beneath the surface, they find themselves caught in a web of danger, mystery, and forbidden love. As they navigate through treacherous tunnels and encounter strange creatures, they must unravel the secrets of this underground world and fight for their survival. With unexpected twists and turns, 'Under the Andes' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Forbidden Love, Survival
