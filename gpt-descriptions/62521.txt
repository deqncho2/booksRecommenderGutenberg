Synopsis: In 'Links With the Past in the Plant World', A. C. Seward takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the fascinating connections between ancient plant species and their modern counterparts. From the discovery of prehistoric plant fossils to the intricate relationships between plants and their environment, Seward unveils the hidden stories that lie within the plant kingdom. With vivid descriptions and compelling anecdotes, this book offers a unique perspective on the rich history and evolution of plants, revealing the remarkable ways in which they have shaped our world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Natural History

Themes: Evolution, Botany, Environmental Science
