Synopsis: In 'Texas Week' by Hernhuter, Albert, a small town in Texas becomes the center of attention as a mysterious event unfolds, captivating the residents and leaving them questioning their own beliefs and values. As the week progresses, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the town's true colors are exposed. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot filled with unexpected twists, 'Texas Week' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of Texas, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Small-town dynamics, Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
