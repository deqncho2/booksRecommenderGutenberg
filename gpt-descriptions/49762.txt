Synopsis: 
In 'Diagnosis' by Ray Palmer, a renowned doctor finds himself entangled in a perplexing medical mystery when a series of unexplained illnesses plague a small town. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that threatens not only his reputation but also the lives of those he holds dear. With time running out, he must navigate through a labyrinth of medical anomalies and personal demons to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Medical Fiction

Themes: Medical ethics, Deception, Redemption
