Synopsis: In 'Peter Poodle, Toy Maker to the King' by Bradley Will, follow the enchanting journey of Peter Poodle, a talented toy maker who is summoned to create a special gift for the King. As Peter embarks on this extraordinary task, he encounters unexpected challenges and discovers the true power of imagination. With whimsical characters and a touch of magic, this heartwarming tale will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Creativity, Friendship, Overcoming Challenges
