Synopsis: In 'Eden: An Episode' by Edgar Saltus, a mysterious and captivating woman named Eden enters the lives of two men, igniting a passionate and dangerous love triangle. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the characters are forced to confront their deepest desires and the consequences of their actions. With its intoxicating blend of romance, suspense, and moral ambiguity, 'Eden: An Episode' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the complexities of love and the human psyche.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Forbidden love, Betrayal, Morality, Obsession
