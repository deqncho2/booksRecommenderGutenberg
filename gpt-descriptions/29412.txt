Synopsis: In 'The Phantom World', Augustin Calmet delves into the mysterious realm of spirits, apparitions, and supernatural phenomena. Through a series of captivating anecdotes and meticulous research, he explores the existence and nature of these otherworldly beings, challenging conventional beliefs and shedding light on the unexplained. From encounters with ghosts and haunted houses to discussions on the origins of vampires and werewolves, Calmet's work takes readers on a thrilling journey into the unknown, leaving them questioning the boundaries between the physical and spiritual realms.

Genres: Non-fiction, Paranormal, Philosophy

Themes: Supernatural, Metaphysics, Religion, Folklore
