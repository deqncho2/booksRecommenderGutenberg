Synopsis: In 'The Human Race' by Figuier, Louis, a group of scientists embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that takes them on an extraordinary journey through time. As they navigate through different eras, they encounter ancient civilizations, witness pivotal historical events, and face unexpected challenges that test their resilience and understanding of humanity. With each leap, they uncover profound truths about the human race and the interconnectedness of all civilizations throughout history. This thrilling and thought-provoking adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead for these intrepid explorers.

Genres: Science Fiction, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Human nature, Cultural evolution
