Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 154, August 21, 1915: The mask of death; or, Nick Carter's curious case,' renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing mystery when a series of bizarre deaths occur, all seemingly connected to a mysterious mask. As Carter delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden identities, leading him on a thrilling chase to unmask the truth before more lives are claimed.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Identity, Deception, Investigation
