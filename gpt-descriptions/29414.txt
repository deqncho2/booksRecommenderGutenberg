Synopsis: 

In 'An Inquiry into the Causes and Effects of the Variolae Vaccinae: A Disease Discovered in Some of the Western Counties of England, Particularly Gloucestershire, and Known by the Name of the Cow Pox', Edward Jenner takes readers on a fascinating journey into the discovery of a peculiar disease that has been spreading in the western counties of England. Through meticulous research and observation, Jenner unravels the mysterious origins and effects of this disease, which has come to be known as the Cow Pox. As he delves deeper into his investigation, Jenner uncovers surprising connections and potential solutions that could have far-reaching implications for the health and well-being of the population.

Genres: Medical research, Historical non-fiction

Themes: Disease discovery, Public health, Scientific inquiry
