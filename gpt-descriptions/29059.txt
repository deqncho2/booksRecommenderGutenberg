Synopsis: In 'The World Beyond' by Cummings, Ray, a young woman named Emily discovers a hidden portal that leads to a mysterious and enchanting realm. As she embarks on a thrilling adventure, she encounters fantastical creatures, uncovers ancient secrets, and learns that her destiny is intertwined with the fate of this extraordinary world. With danger lurking at every turn, Emily must summon her courage and unravel the truth before it's too late. 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Discovery, Destiny, Courage, Secrets
