Synopsis: 
In 'Stern' by Bruce Jay Friedman, meet Stern, a man who seems to have it all - a successful career, a beautiful wife, and a luxurious lifestyle. However, beneath the surface, Stern is plagued by a deep sense of dissatisfaction and a yearning for something more. As he embarks on a journey of self-discovery, Stern finds himself caught between the allure of his extravagant life and the desire for a simpler, more meaningful existence. With wit and humor, Friedman takes readers on a captivating exploration of one man's quest for fulfillment and the unexpected twists and turns that await him along the way.

Genres: 
Satire, Comedy, Literary Fiction

Themes: 
Existentialism, Materialism, Identity
