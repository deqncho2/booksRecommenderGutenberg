Synopsis: In "That House I Bought: A little leaf from life" by Henry Edward Warner, a seemingly ordinary house holds a mysterious secret that unravels a web of intrigue and suspense. As the protagonist delves deeper into the history of the house, they uncover dark secrets and unexpected connections that challenge their perception of reality. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn into a captivating tale of suspense and discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Perception and reality
