Synopsis: In 'Unwelcome Tenant' by Roger D. Aycock, a young couple moves into their dream home, only to discover that they are not alone. Strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena begin to plague their lives, leading them on a terrifying journey to uncover the dark secrets of their new home. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they realize that their lives are in danger, and they must confront the malevolent force that resides within their walls before it consumes them completely.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Hauntings, Secrets and Betrayal, Fear and Paranoia
