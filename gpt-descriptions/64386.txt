Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of Cleverly: A Story for Boys', readers are transported to the small town of Cleverly, where a group of adventurous boys stumble upon a perplexing mystery that has plagued their community for years. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they uncover hidden secrets, encounter dangerous obstacles, and form unbreakable bonds of friendship. With each twist and turn, the boys must rely on their wit, courage, and resourcefulness to unravel the truth behind the mystery of Cleverly.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
