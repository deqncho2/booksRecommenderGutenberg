Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell in Maine; Or, The Lure of 'Way Down East', Frank Merriwell, the beloved hero, finds himself embarking on a thrilling adventure in the picturesque state of Maine. As he explores the rugged wilderness and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Frank becomes entangled in a web of mystery and danger. With his quick wit, athletic prowess, and unwavering determination, Frank must navigate treacherous situations and uncover the truth behind the enigmatic allure of 'Way Down East'. This action-packed tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead for Frank Merriwell.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
