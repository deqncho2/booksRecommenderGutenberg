Synopsis: In 'We Didn't Do Anything Wrong, Hardly' by Roger Kuykendall, a group of friends find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected events after a seemingly innocent prank goes awry. As they navigate through the consequences of their actions, they uncover dark secrets and face the consequences of their choices. With their friendships tested and their lives forever changed, they must confront the truth and find redemption in the midst of chaos.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Consequences, Redemption
