Synopsis: 
In 'Afloat at Last: A Sailor Boy's Log of His Life at Sea', we follow the thrilling adventures of a young sailor as he embarks on a journey across the vast and treacherous oceans. From battling fierce storms to encountering exotic lands and encountering dangerous pirates, the sailor boy's log takes us on a captivating voyage filled with excitement, danger, and unexpected discoveries. As he navigates the challenges of life at sea, the sailor boy learns valuable lessons about courage, resilience, and the true meaning of friendship.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Maritime

Themes: Adventure, Exploration, Friendship, Resilience
