Synopsis: In 'The Great Round World And What Is Going On In It, Vol. 1, November 4, 1897, No. 52', readers are transported to a captivating world of adventure and discovery. This weekly magazine for boys and girls takes them on a thrilling journey through the latest news and events happening around the globe. From mysterious expeditions to scientific breakthroughs, this issue is filled with fascinating stories that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, hungry for more knowledge and excitement.

Genres: Adventure, Historical, Educational

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, Global Awareness
