Synopsis: 

'From Edinburgh to India & Burmah' takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of the adventurous author, Burn Murdoch. Set in the late 19th century, this travelogue recounts his remarkable experiences as he embarks on a voyage from Scotland to the exotic lands of India and Burma. From encountering diverse cultures and breathtaking landscapes to facing unexpected challenges and forging unlikely friendships, Murdoch's narrative is filled with vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes that transport readers to a world of wonder and discovery.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Exchange, Personal Growth
