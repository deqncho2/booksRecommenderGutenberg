Synopsis: In 'The Worth of the Soul: No. 179', a mysterious stranger arrives in a small village, captivating the attention of its inhabitants. As he interacts with the locals, he unveils profound truths about the value of each individual's soul, challenging their beliefs and forcing them to confront their own worth. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking encounters, this captivating tale explores the depths of human nature and the eternal significance of every soul.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Spiritual

Themes: Redemption, Self-worth, Spiritual awakening
