Synopsis: In 'A Translation of the New Testament from the original Greek: Humbly Attempted with a View to Assist the Unlearned with Clearer and More Explicit Views of the Mind of the Spirit in the Scriptures of Truth,' Thomas Haweis presents a fresh and accessible translation of the New Testament. With a sincere desire to help those who may struggle with understanding the original Greek text, Haweis offers a clearer and more explicit interpretation of the Scriptures, aiming to illuminate the profound wisdom and teachings of the Spirit. This work serves as a valuable resource for both scholars and the unlearned, providing a deeper understanding of the mind of the Spirit as revealed in the New Testament.

Genres: Religious literature, Biblical studies, Translation

Themes: Clarity of interpretation, Accessibility, Spiritual enlightenment
