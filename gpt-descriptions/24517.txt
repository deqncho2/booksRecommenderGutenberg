Synopsis: In 'Accidental Death' by Peter Baily, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger after witnessing a tragic incident. As he delves deeper into the circumstances surrounding the event, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel his own life. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous path, questioning his own sanity and the motives of those around him. This gripping tale of suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Consequences
