Synopsis: In "Dry fish and wet: Tales from a Norwegian seaport," Nilsen takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling streets and vibrant harbor of a Norwegian seaport. Through a collection of interconnected stories, we meet a diverse cast of characters, from hardworking fishermen to eccentric artists, each with their own unique tales to tell. As the narrative unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and unexpected connections are made, painting a vivid portrait of life in this enchanting coastal town. With its richly descriptive prose and compelling storytelling, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the heart and soul of a Norwegian seaport.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Cultural

Themes: Community, Identity, Tradition, Human Connection
