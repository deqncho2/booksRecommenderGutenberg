Synopsis: 
In 'The Three Choirs: A Handbook to the Cathedrals of Gloucester, Hereford, and Worcester', Richard John King takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history, stunning architecture, and fascinating biographies of the bishops associated with these three magnificent cathedrals. From the intricate details of the buildings to the stories of the influential figures who shaped these dioceses, King's comprehensive descriptions and engaging narrative bring these cathedrals to life. Whether you are a history enthusiast, an architecture lover, or simply curious about the religious heritage of England, this handbook is sure to captivate your imagination and deepen your understanding of these remarkable places.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Architecture, Biography

Themes: Religious heritage, Architectural significance, Historical figures, Diocesan history
