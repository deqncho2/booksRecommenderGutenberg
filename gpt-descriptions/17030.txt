Synopsis: In 'Super Man and the Bug Out' by Cory Doctorow, a young boy named Marcus is about to embark on an extraordinary adventure when he discovers a mysterious bug that grants him incredible powers. As Marcus learns to control his newfound abilities, he must navigate a world filled with danger, secrets, and unexpected allies. With the fate of his friends and family at stake, Marcus must decide how far he is willing to go to protect those he loves.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Power and Responsibility, Friendship, Coming of Age
