Synopsis: In "The Golden Fleece and The Heroes Who Lived Before Achilles," Padraic Colum takes readers on an epic journey through ancient Greek mythology. Follow the adventures of Jason and the Argonauts as they set sail in search of the legendary Golden Fleece, encountering mythical creatures, treacherous challenges, and unexpected allies along the way. This thrilling tale of heroism, bravery, and the pursuit of glory will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Mythology, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Quest for Glory, Mythical Creatures, Fate and Destiny
