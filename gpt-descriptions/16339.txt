Synopsis: In 'The Passenger from Calais' by Arthur Griffiths, a mysterious and enigmatic traveler boards a train from Calais to Paris, capturing the attention and curiosity of his fellow passengers. As the journey unfolds, secrets are revealed, tensions rise, and the true nature of the passenger's identity becomes increasingly uncertain. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale keeps readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the motives and intentions of each character until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Trust, Perception
