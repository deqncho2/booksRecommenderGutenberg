Synopsis: In the dark and mysterious streets of Victorian London, a respected and well-known doctor named Henry Jekyll becomes entangled in a sinister experiment that unleashes a terrifying alter ego named Edward Hyde. As Hyde's malevolent actions escalate, Jekyll struggles to control his dual nature and keep his dark secret hidden from the world. With the city gripped by fear and suspicion, the true nature of this strange case slowly unravels, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers questioning the depths of human nature.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, mystery

Themes: Duality of human nature, the consequences of repressed desires, the struggle between good and evil
