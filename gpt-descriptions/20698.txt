Synopsis: In 'The Story of Glass' by Sara Ware Bassett, a young woman named Ruth Thorne finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she inherits a beautiful glass vase. As she delves into the history of the vase, Ruth uncovers a hidden world of secrets, forbidden love, and unexpected connections that span generations. With each revelation, she becomes more entangled in the past, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of truth. Will Ruth be able to unravel the enigma surrounding the glass and find her own place in its captivating story?

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and loss
