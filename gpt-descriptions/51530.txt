Synopsis: In 'The Last Letter' by Fritz Leiber, a man finds himself in possession of a mysterious letter that seems to have been written by his deceased wife. As he delves deeper into the letter's contents, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, supernatural occurrences, and a haunting sense of loss. With each revelation, the man's perception of reality is challenged, leading him on a suspenseful journey to uncover the truth behind the letter's origins and its connection to his own life.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Grief and loss, Identity and perception, Supernatural phenomena
