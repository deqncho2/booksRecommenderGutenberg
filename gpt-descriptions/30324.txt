Synopsis: In 'The Pathless Trail' by Arthur O. Friel, a young man named Jack Hollister embarks on a treacherous journey through the rugged wilderness of the American West. Determined to prove his worth and find his true purpose, Jack faces numerous challenges, including dangerous encounters with outlaws and the harsh realities of survival. As he navigates the untamed landscape, Jack discovers unexpected allies and uncovers secrets that will forever change his life. This gripping tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Perseverance, Wilderness survival
