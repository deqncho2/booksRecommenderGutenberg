Synopsis: In "Dreadnoughts of the Dogger: A Story of the War on the North Sea," author Robert Leighton takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous waters of the North Sea during World War I. Set against the backdrop of intense naval warfare, the story follows a group of courageous sailors as they navigate the perils of the sea and face the relentless onslaught of enemy forces. As the battle rages on, alliances are tested, loyalties are questioned, and the true nature of heroism is revealed. With its gripping narrative and vivid descriptions, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the harrowing realities of war at sea.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Naval Fiction

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, The horrors of war
