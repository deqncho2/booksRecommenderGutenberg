Synopsis: 

'Narrative of a Second Expedition to the Shores of the Polar Sea, in the Years 1825, 1826, and 1827' takes readers on a thrilling journey to the uncharted and treacherous Polar Sea. Join explorers John Franklin and John Richardson as they navigate through icy waters, encounter indigenous peoples, and face the harsh realities of the Arctic wilderness. This gripping account of their expedition will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what challenges and discoveries await them in this unforgiving environment.

Genres: Adventure, Exploration, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Survival in extreme conditions, Cultural encounters
