Synopsis: 

'Origin Myths among the Mountain Peoples of the Philippines' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of folklore and legends that have shaped the cultural identity of the mountain peoples of the Philippines. From the mystical creation stories that explain the origins of the world to the enchanting tales of gods and goddesses, this book unveils a hidden world of ancient beliefs and traditions. As the author delves into the depths of these myths, readers will be transported to a realm where humans and supernatural beings coexist, where the forces of nature are personified, and where the boundaries between reality and myth blur. 

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Anthropology

Themes: Cultural identity, Creation, Supernatural beings, Nature, Tradition
