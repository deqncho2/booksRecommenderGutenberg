Synopsis:
In "Hemp" by Brittain B. Robinson, a small town is shaken by a mysterious event that leads to the discovery of a hidden secret. As the residents grapple with the consequences, they are forced to confront their own beliefs and question the boundaries of reality. With a blend of suspense, intrigue, and thought-provoking twists, this captivating novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the complexities of human nature and the power of the unknown.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes:
Secrets and hidden truths, Perception vs. reality, Human nature and morality
