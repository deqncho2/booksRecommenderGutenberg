Synopsis: In 'The Crimson Cryptogram: A Detective Story' by Fergus Hume, a renowned detective is faced with a perplexing case when a cryptic message written in blood is discovered at the scene of a gruesome murder. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that lead him down a twisted path. With each clue he unravels, the detective finds himself drawn into a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between truth and deception becomes increasingly blurred. Will he be able to decipher the cryptogram and catch the elusive killer before more lives are lost?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Investigation
