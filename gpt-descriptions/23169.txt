Synopsis: In 'The Diamond Lens' by O'Brien, Fitz James, a brilliant scientist becomes obsessed with a microscopic world that he discovers through a powerful lens. As he delves deeper into this hidden realm, he encounters a mysterious and captivating presence that challenges his understanding of reality. This gripping tale explores the boundaries of science, obsession, and the pursuit of knowledge.

Genres: Science fiction, mystery, psychological thriller

Themes: Scientific curiosity, obsession, the nature of reality
