Synopsis: In "The Amateur Gentleman" by Farnol, Jeffery, we follow the captivating journey of Barnabas Barty, a young man who finds himself entangled in a web of adventure, romance, and danger. Born into a humble background, Barnabas is determined to rise above his circumstances and prove himself worthy of the title "gentleman." As he navigates the treacherous world of high society, he becomes embroiled in a scandalous plot that threatens not only his reputation but also the lives of those he holds dear. With his quick wit, resourcefulness, and a touch of dashing charm, Barnabas must outsmart his adversaries and uncover the truth before it's too late. Set against the backdrop of 18th-century England, this thrilling tale of love, betrayal, and redemption will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Social class, Identity, Honor, Redemption
