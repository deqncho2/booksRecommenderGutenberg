Synopsis: 

'At the Point of the Bayonet: A Tale of the Mahratta War' by G. A. Henty is an enthralling historical adventure set during the Mahratta War in 18th-century India. The story follows the courageous and resourceful young protagonist, Dick Holland, as he embarks on a perilous journey through the treacherous landscapes of India. Amidst the chaos of war, Dick finds himself caught between conflicting loyalties, facing dangerous enemies and forging unexpected alliances. With his determination and quick thinking, he must navigate through battles, espionage, and political intrigue to fulfill his mission and bring honor to his name.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Honor, Conflict, Espionage
