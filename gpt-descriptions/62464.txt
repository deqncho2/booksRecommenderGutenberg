Synopsis: In 'The Prodigals and Their Inheritance; vol. 1', Mrs. Oliphant takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of two prodigal sons, each with their own unique struggles and desires. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, this novel explores the complexities of family dynamics, the pursuit of happiness, and the consequences of our choices. As secrets unravel and relationships are tested, readers will be drawn into a world filled with intrigue, love, and the timeless quest for self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Family Saga

Themes: Family, Identity, Redemption, Society, Coming of Age
