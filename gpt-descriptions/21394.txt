Synopsis: In 'Priscilla's Spies' by George A. Birmingham, a young woman named Priscilla finds herself caught in the midst of a dangerous espionage mission during World War I. As she navigates the treacherous world of spies and secret agents, Priscilla must rely on her wit, courage, and resourcefulness to uncover the truth and protect those she holds dear. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Espionage, Courage, Loyalty
