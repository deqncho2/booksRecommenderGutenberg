Synopsis: 
In 'A Madcap Cruise' by Bates, Oric, a group of eccentric characters embark on a seemingly ordinary cruise that quickly spirals into a hilarious and chaotic adventure. From mistaken identities to outrageous pranks, the passengers find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of madcap antics that will keep readers laughing and guessing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Humor, Friendship, Unexpected twists
