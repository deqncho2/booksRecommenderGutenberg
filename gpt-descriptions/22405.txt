Synopsis: 

Step back in time to the enchanting days of New England, where the spinning-wheel weaves tales of mystery and intrigue. In "The Olden Time Series: Vol. 2: The Days of the Spinning-Wheel in New England," author Henry M. Brooks delves into the forgotten stories of Boston and Salem, Massachusetts, as gleaned from old newspapers. Uncover the secrets of the past, as whispers of scandal, love, and adventure come to life through the yellowed pages of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Non-Fiction, Mystery

Themes: New England history, folklore, social customs, women's roles, community dynamics
