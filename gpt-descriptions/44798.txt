Synopsis: In 'The Book of Ballads: Eleventh Edition, 1870', Martin and Aytoun take readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting ballads. From tales of love and loss to stories of adventure and betrayal, this edition presents a diverse range of narratives that will leave readers spellbound. Each ballad is beautifully crafted, immersing the reader in vivid imagery and evoking a wide range of emotions. With its rich storytelling and lyrical prose, this collection is sure to captivate both fans of poetry and lovers of classic literature.

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Folklore

Themes: Love, Loss, Adventure, Betrayal, Emotion
