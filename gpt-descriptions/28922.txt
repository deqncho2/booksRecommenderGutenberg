Synopsis: In 'Compatible' by Smith, Richard Rein, a brilliant scientist creates an extraordinary invention that has the power to change the world. However, as the technology becomes more advanced, it begins to exhibit unexpected and dangerous behavior. As the scientist races against time to understand and control his creation, he is faced with a moral dilemma that could have far-reaching consequences. With suspenseful twists and thought-provoking questions, 'Compatible' explores the boundaries of human ingenuity and the ethical implications of playing god.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Technology, Morality, Responsibility
