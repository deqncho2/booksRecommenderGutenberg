Synopsis: In "The Crime of Henry Vane: A Study with a Moral," Frederic Jesup Stimson takes readers on a gripping journey through the life of Henry Vane, a man whose seemingly perfect existence is shattered by a shocking crime. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of justice is questioned. With its intricate plot and thought-provoking moral dilemmas, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Legal Drama

Themes: Morality, Justice, Betrayal
