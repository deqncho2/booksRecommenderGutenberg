Synopsis: In 'Of All Possible Worlds' by William Tenn, a group of scientists embark on a groundbreaking experiment that takes them to parallel universes, each with its own unique set of rules and possibilities. As they navigate through these worlds, they encounter bizarre civilizations, unexpected challenges, and thought-provoking ethical dilemmas. With each new world they explore, the scientists are forced to confront the consequences of their actions and question the very nature of reality itself. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Speculative Fiction

Themes: Parallel Universes, Ethics, Reality, Exploration
