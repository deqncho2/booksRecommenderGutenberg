Synopsis: 

In 'Two Wars: An Autobiography of General Samuel G. French', readers are taken on an extraordinary journey through the life of a remarkable military figure. General French recounts his experiences in the Mexican War, the War between the States, and the Reconstruction Period, providing a unique perspective on these pivotal moments in American history. Through his vivid diary entries, the reader gains insight into the challenges, triumphs, and personal sacrifices that General French faced during these tumultuous times. Alongside his personal accounts, the book also offers a collection of intriguing incidents and reminiscences that further illuminate the complexities of war and its aftermath. 

Genres: Autobiography, Military History, Memoir

Themes: War, Leadership, Personal Sacrifice, Historical Events
