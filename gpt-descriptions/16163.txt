Synopsis: In 'The Seven Great Monarchies Of The Ancient Eastern World, Vol 3: Media', George Rawlinson takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history, geography, and antiquities of Chaldaea, Assyria, Babylon, Media, Persia, Parthia, and the Sassanian or New Persian Empire. With the aid of maps and illustrations, Rawlinson paints a vivid picture of these ancient civilizations, exploring their rise and fall, their cultural achievements, and their enduring legacies. From the awe-inspiring architecture of Babylon to the military might of Persia, this volume offers a fascinating glimpse into the captivating world of the ancient Eastern empires.

Genres: History, Archaeology, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Power and conquest, Cultural heritage
