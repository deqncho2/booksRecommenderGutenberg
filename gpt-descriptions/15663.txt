Synopsis: 

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the captivating world of Ancient Egypt. In this enthralling book, Rawlinson and Gilman take readers on a mesmerizing journey through the mysterious and awe-inspiring civilization that flourished along the banks of the Nile. From the grandeur of the pyramids to the enigmatic rituals of the pharaohs, prepare to be spellbound by the secrets and wonders of this ancient land. Uncover the fascinating stories of legendary rulers, decipher the hieroglyphs that adorn ancient tombs, and explore the intricate beliefs and customs that shaped the lives of the Egyptian people. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, this book brings the past to life, transporting readers to a time of gods and goddesses, monumental architecture, and unparalleled beauty.

Genres: History, Archaeology, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Religion and mythology, Cultural heritage
