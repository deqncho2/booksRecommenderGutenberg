Synopsis: In "The Rough Riders," Theodore Roosevelt takes readers on a thrilling journey through his experiences as a member of the legendary volunteer cavalry unit during the Spanish-American War. From the intense battles in Cuba to the camaraderie forged among the diverse group of soldiers, Roosevelt's firsthand account offers a gripping tale of courage, sacrifice, and the pursuit of honor. As the Rough Riders face numerous challenges and confront their own fears, readers are transported to a pivotal moment in American history, witnessing the birth of a new era of heroism and patriotism.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War memoir, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Patriotism, Brotherhood, Leadership
