Synopsis: In 'Polly the Pagan: Her Lost Love Letters' by Isabel Anderson, a mysterious collection of love letters is discovered, revealing a passionate and forbidden romance that transcends time. As Polly delves into the past, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and desires, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and forbidden love. With each letter, the lines between reality and fantasy blur, leaving Polly questioning her own heart and the true nature of love.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Self-discovery, Secrets and deception
