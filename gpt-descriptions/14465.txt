Synopsis:
'Gods and Fighting Men: The story of the Tuatha de Danaan and of the Fianna of Ireland, arranged and put into English by Lady Gregory' is a captivating collection of ancient Irish myths and legends. Lady Gregory skillfully brings to life the enchanting tales of the Tuatha de Danaan, a mythical race of gods, and the heroic Fianna warriors of Ireland. From epic battles to magical encounters, this book takes readers on a journey through a world filled with gods, goddesses, heroes, and mystical creatures. With each story, the reader is transported to a time of ancient Ireland, where the line between myth and reality blurs, and the power of the supernatural intertwines with the bravery of mortal men.

Genres:
Mythology, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes:
Heroism, Mythical beings, Irish culture and history
