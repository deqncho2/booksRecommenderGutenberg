Synopsis: In 'Prospector's Special' by Robert Sheckley, a group of prospectors embarks on a dangerous journey to a distant planet in search of valuable minerals. However, they soon discover that they are not alone in their pursuit, as they encounter unexpected challenges and mysterious beings that test their resilience and resourcefulness. As tensions rise and the prospectors' survival hangs in the balance, they must navigate treacherous terrain and make difficult choices that will determine their fate.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human Nature
