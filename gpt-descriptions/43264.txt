Synopsis: In 'The Phantom Airman' by Walker, Rowland, a young pilot named Jack finds himself haunted by a mysterious figure from his past, leading him on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the phantom airman. As Jack delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and danger, where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the truth behind the phantom airman.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Guilt, Redemption
