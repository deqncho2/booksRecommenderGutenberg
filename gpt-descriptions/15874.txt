Synopsis: In "Old Testament Legends," James, M. R. delves into the lesser-known apocryphal books of the Old Testament to uncover captivating stories that have long been overlooked. From mysterious encounters with angels to ancient prophecies and hidden treasures, this collection of legends offers a fresh perspective on familiar biblical tales. As the reader delves into these forgotten narratives, they will be transported to a world of wonder and intrigue, where the boundaries between the divine and the mortal blur. With each turn of the page, James, M. R. weaves a tapestry of ancient myths and legends that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fiction, Religious, Historical

Themes: Mythology, Spirituality, Divine Intervention
