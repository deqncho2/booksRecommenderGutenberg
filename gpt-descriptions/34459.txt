Synopsis: 

In 'The Romance of War Inventions: A Description of Warships, Guns, Tanks, Rifles, Bombs, and Other Instruments and Munitions of Warfare, How They Were Invented & How They Are Employed', Thomas W. Corbin takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the fascinating world of war inventions. From the awe-inspiring warships that dominate the seas to the powerful guns, tanks, rifles, and bombs that shape the battlefield, this book delves into the origins and applications of these remarkable instruments of warfare. 

With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Corbin explores the ingenuity and innovation behind these inventions, revealing the minds and stories of the brilliant individuals who conceived them. From the early days of their creation to their modern-day employment, readers will gain a deeper understanding of the evolution and impact of these war machines.

Genres: Non-fiction, Military History, Technology

Themes: Invention and Innovation, Warfare, Historical Impact
