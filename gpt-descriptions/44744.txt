Synopsis: In 'Superstition in Medicine' by Magnus Hugo, a renowned doctor finds himself questioning the boundaries between science and superstition when he encounters a mysterious patient with an inexplicable illness. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a hidden world of ancient rituals, folklore, and unexplained phenomena that challenge everything he thought he knew about medicine. With his reputation on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path between reason and belief, ultimately leading him to question the very nature of reality itself.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Science vs. Superstition, Perception of Reality, Ancient Knowledge
