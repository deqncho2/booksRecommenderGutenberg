Synopsis: 

In 'Bruges and West Flanders', George W. T. Omond takes us on a captivating journey through the enchanting city of Bruges and the picturesque region of West Flanders. Through vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, Omond paints a vivid portrait of this hidden gem in Belgium, revealing its rich cultural heritage, architectural wonders, and fascinating legends. From the medieval charm of Bruges' cobblestone streets to the serene beauty of the countryside, this book offers a delightful exploration of a region that has preserved its timeless allure. 

Genres: Travel, History, Cultural

Themes: Cultural heritage, Architecture, Legends, Beauty of nature
