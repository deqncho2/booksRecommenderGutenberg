Synopsis: In 'The French Prisoners of Norman Cross: A Tale', Rev. Arthur Brown takes us on a captivating journey to the early 19th century, where we follow the lives of French prisoners held captive in the notorious Norman Cross prison camp. Amidst the harsh conditions and constant threat of war, a group of prisoners forms an unlikely bond, leading them to embark on a daring adventure that will test their courage and resilience. As their story unfolds, secrets are revealed, friendships are forged, and the true power of camaraderie is explored in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Survival, Loyalty
