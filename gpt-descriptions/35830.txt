Synopsis: 

The 2010 CIA World Factbook is a captivating and eye-opening reference guide that takes readers on a global journey, providing a comprehensive overview of the countries and territories around the world. From the bustling cities of Asia to the vast landscapes of Africa, this Factbook offers a wealth of information on each nation's geography, population, government, economy, and much more. Delve into the intricate details of each country's history, culture, and current affairs, as well as its relationships with other nations. With its wealth of data and engaging narrative, this Factbook is a must-read for anyone seeking to understand the complexities of our diverse world.

Genres: Reference, Non-fiction

Themes: Geography, Politics, Culture, International Relations
