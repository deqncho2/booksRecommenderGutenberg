Synopsis: 
In 'Wired Love: A Romance of Dots and Dashes', two strangers find themselves connected through the mysterious world of telegraphy. Nattie Rogers, a young woman working as a telegraph operator, becomes intrigued by a fellow operator known only as "C" who communicates with her solely through Morse code. As their relationship develops, Nattie becomes torn between her growing affection for "C" and the real-life complications that arise when their paths cross outside the telegraph office. With the backdrop of the bustling telegraph industry, this captivating tale explores the complexities of love and communication in the digital age of the late 19th century.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Technological Fiction

Themes: Love and connection, Communication, Technological advancements
