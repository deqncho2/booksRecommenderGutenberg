Synopsis: 

"The High School Failures: A Study of the School Records of Pupils Failing in Academic or Commercial High School Subjects" by F. P. O'Brien delves into the intriguing world of high school failures, examining the school records of students who struggled in academic or commercial subjects. Through a meticulous analysis of these records, the author uncovers a wealth of fascinating insights into the reasons behind their failures and the potential impact on their future prospects. This thought-provoking study sheds light on the challenges faced by these students and offers valuable lessons for educators, parents, and students alike.

Genres: Educational, Research, Non-fiction

Themes: Academic struggles, Educational system, Student performance, Personal development
