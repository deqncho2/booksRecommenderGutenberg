Synopsis: In 'Si Klegg, Book 6: Si and Shorty, with Their Boy Recruits, Enter on the Atlanta Campaign' by John McElroy, Si Klegg and his friend Shorty find themselves embarking on a new adventure as they join the Atlanta Campaign during the Civil War. As they lead a group of young recruits, Si and Shorty face the challenges of battle, camaraderie, and the harsh realities of war. With their unique blend of humor and determination, the duo navigates through the chaos and uncertainty, forming unbreakable bonds along the way.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Coming of Age, Sacrifice
