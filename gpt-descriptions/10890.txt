Synopsis: 

In Dio's Rome, Volume 5, Cassius Dio Cocceianus takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous reigns of Septimius Severus, Geta and Caracalla, Macrinus, Elagabalus, and Alexander Severus. Originally written in Greek, this historical narrative has been masterfully translated into English, allowing readers to immerse themselves in the fascinating world of ancient Rome. From political intrigue and military conquests to personal rivalries and the rise and fall of emperors, Dio's vivid storytelling brings to life the complex dynamics of power and ambition in this pivotal period of Roman history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Political Drama

Themes: Power and Ambition, Political Intrigue, Rise and Fall of Empires
