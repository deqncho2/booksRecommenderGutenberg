Synopsis: In "The Royal Book of Oz," the beloved Scarecrow embarks on a captivating journey to uncover his family roots, only to make a stunning discovery that he is the long-lost Emperor of the Silver Island. As he navigates through enchanting lands and encounters a host of whimsical characters, the Scarecrow's quest takes on a thrilling twist that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Identity, Family, Self-discovery
