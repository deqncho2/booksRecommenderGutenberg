Synopsis: In "Captain January" by Laura Elizabeth Howe Richards, we are introduced to the charming and spirited Captain January, a young girl who lives in a lighthouse with her guardian, Captain John. Their idyllic life takes an unexpected turn when a wealthy woman arrives, determined to separate them. As Captain January's world is thrown into turmoil, she must find the strength to fight for what she holds dear and discover the true meaning of family.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Family bonds, Resilience, Determination
