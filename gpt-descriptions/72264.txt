Synopsis: In the third volume of 'Don Sebastian: or, The House of the Braganza', Anna Maria Porter takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous world of 17th-century Portugal. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and love is put to the ultimate test. Amidst political intrigue, forbidden romance, and dangerous quests, the fate of Don Sebastian and the House of Braganza hangs in the balance. Will they be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead, or will their world crumble beneath the weight of their own desires?

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Power and politics, Loyalty and betrayal
