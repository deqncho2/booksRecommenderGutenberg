Synopsis: In 'A Fine Fix' by Ray C. Noll, a down-on-his-luck handyman named Jack finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he agrees to fix a seemingly simple plumbing issue for a wealthy client. As he delves deeper into the task, Jack uncovers a hidden world of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With his skills and resourcefulness put to the test, Jack must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he cares about.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Uncovering the truth
