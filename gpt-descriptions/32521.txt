Synopsis: 
'Keep-Well Stories for Little Folks' by Jones, May Farinholt is a delightful collection of enchanting tales that will captivate young readers. From magical adventures to heartwarming friendships, each story is filled with wonder and imagination. Join the lovable characters as they embark on exciting journeys, face challenges, and learn valuable life lessons along the way. With its charming storytelling and vibrant illustrations, this book is sure to spark the imagination and leave a lasting impression on young minds.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Life Lessons
