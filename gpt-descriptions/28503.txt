Synopsis: In 'The Wit of Women: Fourth Edition', author Kate Sanborn takes readers on a delightful journey through the clever and humorous observations of women from various walks of life. From witty one-liners to insightful anecdotes, this collection showcases the sharp minds and quick tongues of women throughout history. With a blend of wit, wisdom, and a touch of sass, Sanborn's compilation is sure to leave readers both entertained and inspired.

Genres: Non-fiction, Humor, Anthology

Themes: Wit, Femininity, Empowerment
