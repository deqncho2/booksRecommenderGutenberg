Synopsis: In 'Odette: A Fairy Tale for Weary People' by Ronald Firbank, readers are transported to a whimsical world where the enchanting Odette captures the hearts and imaginations of those around her. Set in a vibrant and eccentric society, the story follows Odette's journey as she navigates through a series of delightful encounters and unexpected twists. With Firbank's signature wit and charm, this fairy tale for grown-ups offers a delightful escape into a world of enchantment and wonder.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Satire

Themes: Love and desire, Beauty and appearance, Society and social conventions
