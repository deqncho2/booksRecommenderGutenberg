Synopsis: In 'Memoirs of Service Afloat, During the War Between the States', Semmes, Raphael takes readers on a captivating journey through his experiences as a naval officer during the American Civil War. From thrilling naval battles to daring escapes, Semmes provides a firsthand account of the challenges and triumphs he encountered while serving at sea. With vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, this memoir offers a unique perspective on the war and the role of the Confederate Navy.

Genres: Memoir, History, Military

Themes: War, Loyalty, Survival, Naval Strategy
