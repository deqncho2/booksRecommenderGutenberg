Synopsis: In 'The Gold Thread: A Story for the Young' by Macleod, Norman, a young boy named Jamie embarks on a magical adventure when he discovers a mysterious gold thread that grants him the power to see the hidden wonders of the world. With the thread as his guide, Jamie encounters enchanted forests, mythical creatures, and encounters challenges that test his courage and determination. As he unravels the secrets of the gold thread, Jamie learns valuable lessons about friendship, self-discovery, and the true meaning of bravery.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Magic, Friendship, Self-discovery, Bravery
