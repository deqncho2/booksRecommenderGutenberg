Synopsis: In 'Skyrider' by B. M. Bower, a young woman named Mary inherits a ranch in the rugged Montana wilderness. Determined to prove herself in a male-dominated world, she takes on the challenge of running the ranch and becomes an expert horsewoman. However, when a mysterious stranger arrives, Mary's life takes an unexpected turn. As danger looms and secrets unravel, Mary must navigate treacherous paths both on horseback and in her personal life. With breathtaking descriptions of the Montana landscape and heart-pounding action, 'Skyrider' is a thrilling tale of adventure, romance, and the pursuit of independence.

Genres: Western, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Gender roles, Independence, Wilderness survival
