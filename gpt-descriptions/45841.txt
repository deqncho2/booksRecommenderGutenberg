Synopsis: In 'The Ocean Wireless Boys on War Swept Seas' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of young radio operators find themselves caught in the midst of a treacherous war at sea. As they navigate through dangerous waters, they must rely on their skills and courage to overcome the perils that await them. With thrilling adventures and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
