Synopsis: In "The White Road to Verdun" by Kathleen Burke, a young woman named Kate embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her great-grandfather's experiences in World War I. As she delves into his past, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and sacrifice that spans generations. With each step closer to the truth, Kate finds herself drawn deeper into the haunting and devastating events of the war, ultimately leading her to a shocking revelation that will forever change her perception of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family legacy, the impact of war, the search for truth
