Synopsis: 

In "Occurrence of the Garter Snake, Thamnophis sirtalis, in the Great Plains and Rocky Mountains," Fitch and Maslin delve into the fascinating world of the garter snake, exploring its presence and behavior in the vast landscapes of the Great Plains and Rocky Mountains. Through meticulous research and captivating anecdotes, the authors shed light on the snake's adaptability, survival strategies, and its intricate relationship with its environment. From the snake's unique color variations to its intriguing mating rituals, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the life of this often misunderstood creature.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Wildlife

Themes: Adaptation, Ecology, Biodiversity
