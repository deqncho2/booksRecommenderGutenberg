Synopsis: In 'Other Worlds', Lena Jane Fry takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of American wealth and the struggle to secure it for its rightful owners. Through a compelling narrative, the author explores the intricate web of trusts and their impact on the lives of ordinary citizens. As the story unfolds, readers are drawn into a gripping tale of ambition, power, and the fight for economic justice. Will the American citizens be able to reclaim their wealth from the clutches of the trusts? Find out in this thought-provoking and timely exploration of wealth distribution in America.

Genres: Fiction, Political Thriller, Social Commentary

Themes: Wealth inequality, Economic justice, Power dynamics
