Synopsis: In 'More Cricket Songs' by Gale, Norman, readers are taken on a delightful journey through the world of cricket, where the sport becomes a metaphor for life's ups and downs. Through a collection of witty and insightful poems, the author captures the essence of the game, its players, and the emotions it evokes. From the thrill of victory to the agony of defeat, Gale's words paint a vivid picture of the cricketing world, leaving readers longing for more.

Genres: Poetry, Sports, Humor

Themes: Cricket, Life, Emotions
