Synopsis: In 'Mary Louise Stands the Test' by Emma Speed Sampson, Mary Louise, a spirited young girl, finds herself facing a challenging test that will put her courage and determination to the ultimate test. As she navigates through unexpected twists and turns, Mary Louise must rely on her wit and resourcefulness to overcome the obstacles that stand in her way. With the support of her loyal friends and a dash of adventure, Mary Louise embarks on a journey that will shape her character and reveal her true strength.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Self-discovery
