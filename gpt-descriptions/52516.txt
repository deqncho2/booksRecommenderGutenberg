Synopsis: 
In 'San Antonio: City of Missions' by Claude B. Aniol, readers are transported to the vibrant city of San Antonio, Texas, where a captivating tale unfolds. As the story unfolds, we follow the lives of a diverse group of individuals who find themselves intertwined in a web of secrets, love, and redemption. From the bustling streets of the city to the serene beauty of the historic missions, the characters embark on a journey that will test their courage, challenge their beliefs, and ultimately lead them to discover the true meaning of home.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Heritage, Redemption, Love, Secrets
