Synopsis: In the small town of Stillwater, secrets run deep and the past refuses to stay buried. When a mysterious newcomer arrives, the town's dark history is brought to the surface, threatening to unravel the lives of its residents. As tensions rise and long-held grudges resurface, the truth becomes a dangerous weapon that could destroy everything they hold dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Redemption, Revenge
