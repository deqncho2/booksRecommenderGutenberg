Synopsis: In 'Dan Carter-- Cub Scout' by Mildred A. Wirt, follow the thrilling adventures of Dan Carter, a young boy who joins the Cub Scouts and finds himself entangled in a mysterious case. As Dan and his fellow scouts embark on a camping trip, they stumble upon a hidden treasure map that leads them on a quest filled with danger and excitement. With their scout skills put to the test, Dan and his friends must unravel the clues, outsmart their adversaries, and uncover the truth behind the hidden treasure. Will they succeed in their mission, or will they fall victim to the perils that lie ahead?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
