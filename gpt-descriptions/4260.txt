Synopsis: In the fourth volume of "Andersonville: A Story of Rebel Military Prisons," author John McElroy delves deeper into the harrowing experiences of Union soldiers held captive in the notorious Confederate prison during the American Civil War. As the prisoners face unimaginable hardships and struggle to maintain their humanity, a glimmer of hope emerges in the form of a daring escape plan. With tension mounting and the odds stacked against them, the prisoners must summon every ounce of courage and resourcefulness to execute their audacious bid for freedom. Will they succeed or fall victim to the merciless conditions of Andersonville?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Camaraderie, Injustice
