Synopsis: In 'Lectures on Russian Literature: Pushkin, Gogol, Turgenef, Tolstoy', Ivan Panin takes readers on a captivating journey through the works of four iconic Russian authors. With insightful analysis and thought-provoking interpretations, Panin delves into the depths of their literary masterpieces, exploring the themes, characters, and social commentary that make these works timeless. From Pushkin's lyrical poetry to Gogol's satirical wit, Turgenef's introspective narratives, and Tolstoy's epic sagas, this book offers a fascinating exploration of Russian literature that will leave readers eager to dive into these classic works themselves.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Russian Literature, Non-fiction

Themes: Love and Passion, Social Critique, Human Nature, Existentialism
