Synopsis: In 'Nothing But the Truth' by Frederic Stewart Isham, a young man named Dick Livingstone finds himself caught in a web of secrets and deception when he stumbles upon a mysterious letter. As he delves deeper into the truth, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of intrigue and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. With his life on the line, Dick must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the shocking truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Truth
