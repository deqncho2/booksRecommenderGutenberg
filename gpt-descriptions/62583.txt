Synopsis: In "The Theory and Practice of Argumentation and Debate" by Victor Alvin Ketcham, readers are taken on a captivating journey into the art of persuasive communication. Through insightful analysis and practical examples, Ketcham explores the intricacies of constructing compelling arguments and engaging in spirited debates. From dissecting logical fallacies to mastering rhetorical techniques, this book equips readers with the tools necessary to navigate the complex world of argumentation. Whether you are a student, professional, or simply interested in the power of persuasion, this book will leave you inspired to engage in thoughtful discourse and armed with the skills to effectively convey your ideas.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, Communication

Themes: Argumentation, Debate, Persuasion, Critical Thinking
