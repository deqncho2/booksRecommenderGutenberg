Synopsis: 
'Old Peter's Russian Tales' by Arthur Ransome is a captivating collection of traditional Russian folktales that transport readers to a world filled with magical creatures, brave heroes, and enchanting adventures. From the mischievous antics of the Firebird to the heartwarming tale of the Snow Maiden, each story is beautifully woven with vivid imagery and rich cultural elements. As readers delve into these timeless tales, they will be swept away by the wonders of Russian folklore and the enduring power of storytelling.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: 
Magic, Heroism, Cultural Heritage
