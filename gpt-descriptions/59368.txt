Synopsis: 
In 'Juvenile Delinquent' by Edward W. Ludwig, we follow the gripping story of a troubled teenager named Jake who finds himself caught in a web of crime and violence. After a series of unfortunate events, Jake's life takes a dark turn as he becomes entangled with a dangerous gang. As he navigates the treacherous world of juvenile delinquency, Jake must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will determine his fate. With its intense narrative and unexpected twists, this novel delves into the complexities of youth, morality, and the consequences of one's actions.

Genres: Crime fiction, Coming-of-age, Thriller

Themes: Juvenile delinquency, Morality, Redemption
