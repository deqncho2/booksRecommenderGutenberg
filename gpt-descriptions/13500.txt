Synopsis: In "A Heroine of France: The Story of Joan of Arc," Everett-Green, Evelyn brings to life the captivating tale of one of history's most legendary figures. Set in medieval France, this gripping narrative follows the extraordinary journey of Joan of Arc, a young peasant girl who defies societal norms and rises to become a symbol of hope and courage. From her humble beginnings to her pivotal role in the Hundred Years' War, Joan's unwavering determination and unwavering faith in her divine mission will inspire readers as they witness her transformation from an ordinary girl to a fearless leader. As she faces numerous challenges and confronts the complexities of war, Joan's story becomes a testament to the power of conviction and the indomitable spirit of the human soul.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Faith, Gender Roles, War, Divine Intervention
