Synopsis: In 'Neal, the Miller: A Son of Liberty' by James Otis, follow the thrilling journey of Neal, a humble miller who becomes an unexpected hero during the American Revolution. As Neal finds himself caught up in the dangerous world of espionage and rebellion, he must navigate treacherous paths, face formidable enemies, and make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of a nation. With his courage and determination, Neal's actions will leave a lasting impact on the fight for freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Spy Thriller

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Courage, Freedom
