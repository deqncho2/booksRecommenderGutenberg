Synopsis: In "Hope Benham: A Story for Girls" by Nora Perry, readers are introduced to the spirited and determined Hope Benham. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows Hope as she navigates the challenges and expectations placed upon young women of her time. From her unconventional dreams and aspirations to her encounters with love and loss, Hope's journey is one of resilience, self-discovery, and the pursuit of her own happiness. As she defies societal norms and strives to find her place in the world, readers will be captivated by Hope's unwavering spirit and the choices she makes to shape her own destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Women's Literature

Themes: Gender roles, Independence, Self-determination, Love and Loss
