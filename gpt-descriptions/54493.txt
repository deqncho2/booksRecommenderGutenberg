Synopsis: 
'The Dungeons of Old Paris: Being the Story and Romance of the Most Celebrated Prisons of the Monarchy and the Revolution' takes readers on a captivating journey through the dark and mysterious underbelly of Paris. From the infamous Bastille to the lesser-known dungeons, this book delves into the secrets, scandals, and tragedies that unfolded within these walls. As the author uncovers the stories of both prisoners and jailers, a web of intrigue and suspense begins to unravel, revealing the true nature of power, corruption, and the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Power and Corruption, Survival, Redemption, Human Resilience
