Synopsis: In "How Ethel Hollister Became a Campfire Girl," Ethel finds herself in a new town, longing for adventure and friendship. When she discovers the Campfire Girls, a group of girls who embrace nature and camaraderie, Ethel is determined to join their ranks. However, she soon realizes that becoming a Campfire Girl is not as easy as she thought, and she must face challenges and prove herself worthy of the sisterhood. Will Ethel's determination and spirit be enough to earn her place among the Campfire Girls?

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Perseverance
