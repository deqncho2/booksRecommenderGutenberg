Synopsis: In 'The Perfume of Eros: A Fifth Avenue Incident' by Edgar Saltus, a mysterious and alluring woman enters the lives of the wealthy elite on Fifth Avenue, captivating them with her intoxicating presence. As she weaves her way through their lives, secrets are unveiled, desires are awakened, and the boundaries of love and obsession are tested. With its seductive atmosphere and enigmatic characters, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey into the depths of human desire and the power of attraction.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Seduction, Desire, Obsession, Secrets, Wealth and Privilege
