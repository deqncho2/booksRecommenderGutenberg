Synopsis: In "The Life and Times of Ulric Zwingli" by Johann Jakob Hottinger, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of the most influential figures of the Protestant Reformation. From his humble beginnings as a Swiss priest to his pivotal role in challenging the authority of the Catholic Church, Ulric Zwingli's story is one of courage, intellectual curiosity, and unwavering conviction. As he navigates the turbulent political and religious landscape of 16th-century Europe, Zwingli's ideas and actions will shape the course of history, forever altering the religious landscape of the Western world.

Genres: Biography, History, Religious Studies

Themes: Religious reform, Intellectual curiosity, Political and religious conflict
