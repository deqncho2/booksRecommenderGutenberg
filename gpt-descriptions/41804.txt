Synopsis: In 'A Legend of Reading Abbey' by Charles MacFarlane, a mysterious legend unfolds within the ancient walls of Reading Abbey, captivating the imagination of a young historian. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the abbey, he uncovers a hidden world of intrigue, betrayal, and lost treasures. With each discovery, the line between past and present blurs, leading him on a thrilling journey that will challenge his beliefs and change his life forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: History and its impact on the present, the power of legends and folklore, the pursuit of knowledge and truth
