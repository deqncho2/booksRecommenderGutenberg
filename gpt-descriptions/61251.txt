Synopsis: In "The Life of Abraham Lincoln for Young People, Told in Words of One Syllable," Harriet Putnam presents the remarkable story of one of America's greatest leaders in a unique and accessible way. This engaging biography takes readers on a journey through Lincoln's life, from his humble beginnings to his rise as the 16th President of the United States. Through simple language and captivating storytelling, Putnam brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and enduring legacy of this iconic figure. 

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Educational

Themes: Perseverance, Leadership, Equality, American History
