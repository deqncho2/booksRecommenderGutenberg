Synopsis: In 'The American Jew as Patriot, Soldier and Citizen' by Simon Wolf, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the experiences of American Jews who have served their country with unwavering loyalty and dedication. From the Revolutionary War to the Civil War and beyond, Wolf delves into the stories of these remarkable individuals, shedding light on their struggles, triumphs, and the unique challenges they faced as both Jews and patriots. Through vivid anecdotes and historical accounts, the book paints a vivid portrait of the American Jewish experience and the profound impact these individuals had on shaping the nation. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Jewish Studies

Themes: Patriotism, Identity, Religious Freedom, Assimilation, Discrimination
