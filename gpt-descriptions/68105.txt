Synopsis: 
In the bohemian streets of 1920s Rome, a group of eccentric artists and intellectuals form an enigmatic secret society known as "The Cabala." As they navigate the labyrinthine world of art, love, and spirituality, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, blurring the boundaries between reality and imagination. With its vivid characters and mysterious atmosphere, "The Cabala" takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the hidden depths of human desires and the power of artistic expression.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Artistic expression, Love and relationships, Spirituality, Identity
