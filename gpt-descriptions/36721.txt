Synopsis: In 'House of Torment: A Tale of the Remarkable Adventures of Mr. John Commendone, Gentleman to King Phillip II of Spain at the English Court' by Thorne, Guy, readers are transported to the intriguing world of the English court during the reign of King Phillip II of Spain. The story follows the remarkable adventures of Mr. John Commendone, a gentleman who finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and dangerous secrets. As he navigates the treacherous halls of power, Commendone must rely on his wit, courage, and loyalty to survive the House of Torment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Political intrigue, Forbidden love, Loyalty, Survival
