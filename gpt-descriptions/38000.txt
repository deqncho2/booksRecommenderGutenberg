Synopsis: 
In "Bridge; its Principles and Rules of Play: with Illustrative Hands and the Club Code of Bridge Laws," J. B. Elwell presents a comprehensive guide to the game of bridge, filled with insightful strategies and illustrative hands that will captivate both novice and experienced players alike. From the basic principles to advanced techniques, Elwell's expertise shines through as he delves into the intricacies of the game, providing valuable tips and examples to enhance the reader's understanding. With a focus on the club code of bridge laws, this book serves as an indispensable resource for players looking to improve their skills and knowledge of the game.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gaming, Reference

Themes: Strategy, Skill development, Rules and regulations
