Synopsis: 

In 'Denmark' by Thomson, M. Pearson, a young woman named Emma embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's mysterious past. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the walls of her ancestral home, Emma becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and long-buried family secrets. With each revelation, she finds herself questioning her own identity and the true nature of those she thought she knew. As the past and present collide, Emma must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the shocking truth that has been concealed for generations.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Betrayal, Deception
