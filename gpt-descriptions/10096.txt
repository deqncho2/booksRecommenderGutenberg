Synopsis: 
In the aftermath of the Trojan War, the once-proud city of Troy lies in ruins, and its women are left to face the devastating consequences. "The Trojan Women of Euripides" takes us into the lives of the women of Troy, who are now captives and face an uncertain future. Through their powerful voices, we witness their grief, resilience, and the indomitable spirit that emerges from the ashes of their fallen city. As they navigate the horrors of war and the loss of their loved ones, the women of Troy confront the timeless questions of fate, justice, and the true nature of humanity.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: War and its aftermath, the plight of women in times of conflict, the human capacity for resilience and hope
