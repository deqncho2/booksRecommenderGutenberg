Synopsis:
'Tar Heel Tales' by Bryant, H. E. C. is a collection of captivating stories set in the heart of North Carolina. From the mysterious legends of the Appalachian Mountains to the bustling streets of Raleigh, this anthology takes readers on a journey through the rich tapestry of Tar Heel State folklore. Encounter mythical creatures, encounter unsolved mysteries, and delve into the lives of ordinary people whose extraordinary experiences will leave you spellbound. Each tale is a window into the unique history and culture of North Carolina, weaving together elements of adventure, suspense, and the supernatural. Prepare to be enthralled by the enchanting world of 'Tar Heel Tales'.

Genres: Fiction, Folklore, Mystery

Themes: North Carolina history and culture, Mythology, Supernatural phenomena
