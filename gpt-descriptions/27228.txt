Synopsis: 
In 'Moon Lore' by Timothy Harley, embark on a captivating journey through the mystical world of lunar legends and folklore. From ancient civilizations to modern-day beliefs, this book unveils the enchanting tales and cultural significance surrounding Earth's celestial companion. Delve into the moon's influence on human emotions, its connection to supernatural phenomena, and the intriguing myths that have shaped our understanding of this celestial body. Prepare to be mesmerized by the captivating stories and intriguing insights that lie within the pages of 'Moon Lore'.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Astronomy

Themes: Lunar legends, Cultural significance, Supernatural phenomena
