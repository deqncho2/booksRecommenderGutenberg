Synopsis: In 'The Motor Maids' School Days' by Katherine Stokes, a group of adventurous young women embark on a thrilling journey filled with mystery, friendship, and unexpected challenges. As they attend a prestigious boarding school, the Motor Maids find themselves entangled in a series of puzzling events that test their courage and determination. With their unique skills and unwavering bond, they must unravel the secrets that lie within the school's walls and prove that they are more than just ordinary students. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
