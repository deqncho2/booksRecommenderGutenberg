Synopsis: In "The Hour Glass" by W.B. Yeats, a mysterious traveler arrives in a small village, bringing with him an ancient hourglass that possesses a strange power. As the hourglass is turned, time seems to stand still, revealing hidden truths and challenging the villagers' perceptions of reality. As the story unfolds, the traveler's intentions become increasingly enigmatic, leading to a captivating exploration of the human experience and the nature of existence.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Time, Perception, Truth, Existence
