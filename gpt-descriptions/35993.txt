Synopsis: 
"The History of Don Quixote de la Mancha" follows the adventures of an eccentric nobleman named Don Quixote who becomes obsessed with the idea of reviving chivalry and embarks on a series of comical and often absurd quests. Accompanied by his loyal squire, Sancho Panza, Don Quixote encounters a variety of characters and situations that test his idealistic beliefs and challenge the boundaries between reality and imagination. This timeless tale is a humorous and insightful exploration of the power of dreams and the complexities of human nature.

Genres: 
Satire, Adventure, Comedy

Themes: 
Idealism, Madness, Illusion vs Reality
