Synopsis: In "The Four Corners in Camp" by Amy Ella Blanchard, four adventurous friends embark on a thrilling summer camp experience that takes them on unexpected journeys, tests their friendship, and reveals hidden secrets. As they navigate through mysterious woods, encounter peculiar characters, and unravel puzzling clues, the campers find themselves caught up in a captivating adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Friendship

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Self-discovery
