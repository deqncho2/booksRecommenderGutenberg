Synopsis: In 'The Riddle of the Sands' by Childers, Erskine, two friends embark on a sailing adventure along the German coast, only to stumble upon a mysterious conspiracy that threatens their lives. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter suspicious characters, they must unravel a riddle that could have far-reaching consequences. With its gripping suspense and thrilling twists, this classic novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Espionage, National security, Friendship
