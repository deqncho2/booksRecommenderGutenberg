Synopsis: 
Against the Grain follows the life of Jean Des Esseintes, a wealthy and eccentric aristocrat who retreats from society to create his own secluded world of decadence and aestheticism. As he indulges in his extravagant desires and pursues his unique passions, Des Esseintes embarks on a journey of self-discovery and rebellion against societal norms. With vivid and detailed descriptions, this novel takes readers on a mesmerizing exploration of one man's quest for beauty and individuality.

Genres: 
Decadent literature, Symbolism, Psychological fiction

Themes: 
Aestheticism, Individualism, Alienation, Escapism
