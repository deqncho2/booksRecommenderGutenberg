Synopsis: In "How to Make Pottery" by Mary White, a young artist embarks on a transformative journey as she delves into the ancient art of pottery-making. Guided by a mysterious mentor, she discovers the secrets of clay, the power of creativity, and the profound connection between art and self-expression. As she navigates the challenges and triumphs of her artistic pursuit, she uncovers a hidden world of beauty and meaning that will forever change her perspective on life.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Art

Themes: Self-discovery, Creativity, Transformation
