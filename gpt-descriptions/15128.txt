Synopsis: 

In 'Narrative of the Life of J.D. Green, a Runaway Slave, from Kentucky: Containing an Account of His Three Escapes, in 1839, 1846, and 1848', we are taken on a gripping journey through the life of J.D. Green, a courageous slave who defied the odds and escaped from bondage not once, but three times. From the heart-wrenching accounts of his escapes to the harrowing experiences he faced along the way, Green's narrative sheds light on the resilience and determination of those who fought for their freedom during a dark period in American history.

Genres: Autobiography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Slavery, Freedom, Resilience
