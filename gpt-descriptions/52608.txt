Synopsis: In 'For His Country, and Grandmother and the Crow' by Saunders, Marshall, a young soldier finds himself torn between his duty to his country and his deep connection to his grandmother and a mysterious crow. As he navigates the complexities of war and family, he must confront the sacrifices he is willing to make and the true meaning of loyalty. 

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Magical realism

Themes: Duty, Family, Sacrifice, Loyalty
