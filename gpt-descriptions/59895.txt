Synopsis: In 'The Four Stragglers' by Frank L. Packard, a group of four unlikely companions find themselves thrown together in a thrilling adventure. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter dangerous adversaries, they must rely on their individual skills and forge an unbreakable bond to survive. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Overcoming adversity
