Synopsis: In Taunton town: a story of the rebellion of James Duke of Monmouth in 1685 is a captivating historical novel that takes readers back to the tumultuous events of the late 17th century. Set in the town of Taunton, England, the story follows the lives of various characters as they navigate the political and social upheaval caused by the rebellion of James Duke of Monmouth against his uncle, King James II. Amidst the chaos and uncertainty, secrets are revealed, loyalties are tested, and lives are forever changed. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling characters, this novel offers a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and the fight for freedom.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Rebellion, Loyalty, Betrayal, Love, Freedom
