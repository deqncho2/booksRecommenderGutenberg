Synopsis: 
In 'Flying Plover: His stories, told him by Squat-by-the-fire', Theodore Goodridge Roberts takes us on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and legends of the Mi'kmaq people. Through the eyes of Flying Plover, a young Mi'kmaq boy, we are transported to a world filled with magical creatures, ancient spirits, and extraordinary adventures. As Squat-by-the-fire shares his mesmerizing tales with Flying Plover, the young boy's imagination is ignited, and he learns valuable lessons about bravery, wisdom, and the importance of preserving his cultural heritage. 

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: 
Cultural heritage, Wisdom, Bravery, Imagination
