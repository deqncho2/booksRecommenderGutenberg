Synopsis: In 'The World Turned Upside Down', a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to a hidden world. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Emily must navigate treacherous alliances and face unexpected dangers, all while trying to uncover the truth about her own identity. With each twist and turn, the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, leaving Emily questioning everything she thought she knew.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Deception
