Synopsis: In 'Space Tug' by Murray Leinster, a group of space pioneers embark on a dangerous mission to salvage a stranded spaceship. As they face numerous challenges and unexpected dangers, they must rely on their wit, courage, and teamwork to overcome the odds and complete their mission. With thrilling action sequences and a suspenseful plot, 'Space Tug' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the vastness of space.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Space Opera

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Teamwork
