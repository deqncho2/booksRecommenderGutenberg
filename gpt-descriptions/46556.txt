Synopsis: In 'The Isle of Wight', a captivating tale by Geraldine Edith Mitton, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey to the picturesque Isle of Wight. Drawn to the island's enchanting beauty and rich history, she soon finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries that have been hidden for centuries. As Emily delves deeper into the island's past, she uncovers a hidden world filled with ancient legends, ghostly apparitions, and unexpected connections to her own life. With each discovery, Emily's curiosity grows, leading her on a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and challenge everything she thought she knew.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and mysteries, History and heritage, Personal discovery
