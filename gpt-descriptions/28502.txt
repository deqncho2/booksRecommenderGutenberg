Synopsis: 
"The Busted Ex-Texan, and Other Stories" is a captivating collection of short stories by W. H. H. Murray that takes readers on a journey through the wild and untamed landscapes of the American West. From the dusty plains of Texas to the rugged mountains of Colorado, each story introduces a unique and intriguing character facing their own trials and tribulations. Whether it's a former Texan seeking redemption, a young woman defying societal expectations, or a group of prospectors searching for gold, these tales are filled with adventure, suspense, and unexpected twists that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Western Fiction, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Redemption, Independence, Survival, Exploration
