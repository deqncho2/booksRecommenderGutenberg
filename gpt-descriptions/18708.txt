Synopsis: In 'Dr. Dumany's Wife' by Jókai, Mór, a young and beautiful woman named Ilonka marries the renowned and respected Dr. Dumany. However, as she settles into her new life, Ilonka begins to uncover dark secrets and mysterious occurrences within her husband's past. As she delves deeper into the truth, she finds herself caught in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden love. With each revelation, Ilonka's world is turned upside down, and she must navigate a treacherous path to protect herself and those she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and betrayal, Identity and self-discovery
