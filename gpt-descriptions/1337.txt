Synopsis: In 'Shelley' by Sydney Waterlow, a young woman named Shelley finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, Shelley must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth, all while trying to protect her own sanity and survival.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity, Psychological manipulation
