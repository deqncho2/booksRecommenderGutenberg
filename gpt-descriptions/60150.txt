Synopsis: In "Selling Latin America: A Problem in International Salesmanship," author William Edmund Aughinbaugh takes readers on a captivating journey through the challenges and strategies of selling in the Latin American market. With a keen eye for cultural nuances and business practices, Aughinbaugh explores the intricacies of what to sell and how to sell it in this dynamic region. Through real-life examples and insightful analysis, he uncovers the secrets to successful salesmanship in Latin America, offering invaluable advice to both seasoned professionals and aspiring entrepreneurs. This book is a must-read for anyone looking to navigate the complexities of international sales and tap into the vast potential of the Latin American market.

Genres: Business, Non-fiction

Themes: International sales, Cultural understanding, Business strategies
