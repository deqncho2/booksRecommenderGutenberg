Synopsis: In 'The Poor Gentleman' by Hendrik Conscience, a young man named Charles finds himself in dire financial straits after the sudden death of his father. Desperate to support his family and maintain his social standing, Charles embarks on a series of unexpected adventures that lead him to question the true value of wealth and the importance of love and friendship. As he navigates through the complexities of society, Charles discovers that true riches may not be measured in material possessions alone. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Wealth and poverty, Social class, Love and friendship
