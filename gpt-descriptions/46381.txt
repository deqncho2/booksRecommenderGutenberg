Synopsis: In 'The Lead of Honour' by Richardson Norval, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is assigned to investigate a high-profile murder case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a complex network of secrets, deceit, and betrayal that threatens to unravel everything he thought he knew. With time running out and the stakes higher than ever, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and confront his own demons to solve the case and bring the truth to light.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption, Justice
