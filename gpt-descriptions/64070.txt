Synopsis: In 'The Secret Chart; or, Treasure Hunting in Hayti' by Otis, James, a group of adventurous young friends stumble upon a mysterious chart that leads them on a thrilling treasure hunt in the exotic island of Hayti. As they navigate through dangerous landscapes and encounter unexpected allies and enemies, they must decipher clues and solve puzzles to uncover the hidden secrets of the chart. With each step closer to the treasure, the stakes rise higher, testing their courage, loyalty, and resourcefulness. Will they be able to outsmart their adversaries and claim the treasure that has been hidden for centuries?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Perseverance, Discovery
