Synopsis: In 'Hunted Down; or, Five Days in the Fog', young Granice finds himself in a terrifying situation when he becomes the target of a drunken and enraged mob. With his life hanging in the balance, Granice must navigate through a dense fog, evading his pursuers and fighting for his survival. This thrilling narrative takes readers on a heart-pounding journey as Granice's desperate escape unfolds over the course of five intense days.

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Action

Themes: Survival, Mob Mentality, Fear
