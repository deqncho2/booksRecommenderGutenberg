Synopsis: In "The Art of Drinking: A Historical Sketch," Gervinus takes readers on a captivating journey through the ages, exploring the fascinating relationship between humans and alcohol. From ancient civilizations to modern times, this engaging historical sketch delves into the cultural, social, and even political aspects of drinking. Gervinus uncovers intriguing anecdotes, explores the rituals and traditions associated with alcohol, and examines its impact on society. This thought-provoking exploration of the art of drinking is sure to leave readers with a newfound appreciation for the role of alcohol in human history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Alcohol, Culture, Society, Tradition, Human History
