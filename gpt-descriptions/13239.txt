Synopsis: In 'Grain and Chaff from an English Manor', Arthur Herbert Savory takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of the inhabitants of an English manor. From the enigmatic Lord of the manor to the humblest servant, each character's story intertwines in unexpected ways, revealing secrets, desires, and the complex dynamics of a society on the brink of change. As the plot unfolds, the reader is drawn deeper into the intricate web of relationships, scandals, and hidden motives that shape the fate of the manor and its inhabitants.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Social class, Power dynamics, Secrets and deception, Love and desire
