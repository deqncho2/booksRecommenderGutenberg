Synopsis: In 'Verses' by Susan Coolidge, a young woman named Katy Carr embarks on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. Through her experiences, she navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and the pursuit of her dreams. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, this captivating novel takes readers on a heartfelt exploration of life's joys and challenges.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Love and Relationships, Personal Growth
