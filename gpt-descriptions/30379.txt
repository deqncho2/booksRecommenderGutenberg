Synopsis: 
'Field Trip' by Hunter, Gene is a thrilling and suspenseful novel that follows a group of students on a seemingly ordinary field trip that quickly turns into a nightmare. As they venture into the wilderness, they soon discover that they are not alone and must fight for their lives against an unknown and deadly force. With each passing moment, the tension rises, and the students must rely on their wits and teamwork to survive. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind their terrifying ordeal.

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Horror

Themes: Survival, Teamwork, Mystery
