Synopsis: In "Hero From Yesterday," a mysterious figure from the past emerges in a modern world, captivating the attention of a young journalist. As she delves deeper into his enigmatic past, she uncovers a web of secrets and a connection that transcends time. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect the man who may hold the key to changing history forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Time travel, Identity, Love and sacrifice
