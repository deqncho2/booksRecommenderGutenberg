Synopsis: In "Rich Men's Children" by Geraldine Bonner, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deceit when she becomes entangled with a wealthy family. As she navigates the opulent world of the elite, Mary must uncover the truth behind the family's dark past and protect her own future. With unexpected twists and gripping suspense, this thrilling novel explores the lengths people will go to protect their wealth and the consequences that follow.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Drama

Themes: Wealth and power, Family secrets, Deception, Social class
