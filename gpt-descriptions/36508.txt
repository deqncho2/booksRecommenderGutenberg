Synopsis: 
'Eight Harvard Poets' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and poetic minds of eight talented individuals studying at Harvard University. As the story unfolds, we witness their struggles, triumphs, and the profound impact their art has on their personal lives and the world around them. Each poet brings a unique perspective and voice to the table, creating a tapestry of emotions, experiences, and poetic brilliance that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Artistic expression, Identity, Love, Ambition, Self-discovery
