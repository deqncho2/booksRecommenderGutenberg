Synopsis: In 'Doctor Cupid: A Novel' by Rhoda Broughton, a young and talented doctor finds herself caught between her professional duties and matters of the heart. As she navigates the complexities of love and relationships, she discovers that sometimes the cure for a broken heart can be found in the most unexpected places. With a touch of romance, humor, and a dash of medical drama, this captivating novel explores the intertwining of love and medicine in a delightful and heartwarming way.

Genres: Romance, Fiction, Medical Drama

Themes: Love, Professionalism, Self-discovery
