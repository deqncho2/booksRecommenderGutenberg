Synopsis: 
Step back in time and experience a day in the life of a colonial home through the eyes of the Prescott family. From the crackling of the fireplace to the aroma of freshly baked bread, this immersive journey takes readers on a captivating exploration of the daily routines, challenges, and joys of colonial living. Discover the intricate details of their home, the bustling activities in the kitchen, and the vibrant social interactions that shaped their world. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, 'A Day in a Colonial Home' offers a fascinating glimpse into a bygone era that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: Colonial life, Daily routines, Social interactions
