Synopsis: In 'Callista: a Tale of the Third Century', John Henry Newman takes us on a captivating journey to the ancient world, where a young Greek philosopher named Agellius finds his beliefs challenged when he encounters a mysterious Christian woman named Callista. As Agellius grapples with his own doubts and the allure of Callista's faith, their lives become intertwined in a tale of love, sacrifice, and the search for truth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Religious Fiction

Themes: Faith and Doubt, Conversion, Love and Sacrifice
