Synopsis: In 'Good for Evil: Rose Cottage', a young girl named Madeline finds herself in a mysterious and enchanting world when she is sent to live with her eccentric aunt in Rose Cottage. As Madeline explores the secrets of the cottage and its surrounding woods, she uncovers a hidden power within herself that could change everything. But with great power comes great danger, and Madeline must navigate a treacherous path to protect herself and those she loves. Filled with magic, adventure, and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Coming of age, Power and responsibility, Family bonds
