Synopsis: In 'Jose: Our Little Portuguese Cousin', we are transported to the vibrant and picturesque country of Portugal, where we meet the charming and curious young boy, Jose. Through his eyes, we embark on a delightful journey filled with captivating stories, cultural traditions, and heartwarming adventures. From exploring the bustling streets of Lisbon to discovering the secrets of the countryside, Jose's experiences offer a captivating glimpse into the rich heritage and everyday life of the Portuguese people. As we follow Jose's escapades, we witness the importance of family, friendship, and the power of embracing one's own unique identity. 

Genres: Children's Literature, Cultural Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Cultural heritage, Family, Friendship, Identity
