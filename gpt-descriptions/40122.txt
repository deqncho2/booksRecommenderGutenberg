Synopsis: In 'Glimpses into the Abyss' by Mary Higgs, a young woman named Emily finds herself inexplicably drawn to a mysterious antique shop in her small town. As she delves deeper into the shop's secrets, she uncovers a hidden world filled with dark magic and ancient curses. With each glimpse into the abyss, Emily's own life becomes entangled in a dangerous web of supernatural forces, forcing her to confront her own fears and make impossible choices. Will she be able to escape the clutches of the abyss, or will she be forever consumed by its darkness?

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Power of choice, Confronting fears, Supernatural forces
