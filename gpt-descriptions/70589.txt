Synopsis: In "Tarzan and the Lost Empire," Edgar Rice Burroughs takes readers on an exhilarating adventure as Tarzan embarks on a perilous journey to a hidden civilization deep within the African jungle. Drawn by the allure of lost treasure and ancient secrets, Tarzan finds himself facing treacherous enemies, navigating treacherous landscapes, and encountering a mysterious woman who holds the key to it all. As he fights to protect the lost empire from those who seek to exploit it, Tarzan must confront his own identity and make choices that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Action

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Legacy, Good vs. Evil
