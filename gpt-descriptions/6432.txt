Synopsis: 
In 'Betty Wales, Sophomore: A Story for Girls', we follow the journey of Betty Wales as she enters her second year at college. With new friendships, academic challenges, and unexpected adventures, Betty navigates the complexities of college life while discovering her true passions and values. As she faces various trials and triumphs, Betty's determination and resilience are put to the test, leading her to uncover hidden strengths and forge her own path towards success and self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Academic pursuits, Self-discovery
