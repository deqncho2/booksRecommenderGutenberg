Synopsis: In the rugged and lawless Canadian wilderness, a group of outlaws find themselves entangled in a deadly game of survival and revenge. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and face off against formidable adversaries, their loyalty and trust are put to the ultimate test. With each passing moment, the line between right and wrong becomes increasingly blurred, leaving them questioning their own morality and the true nature of justice.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Morality, Loyalty, Justice
