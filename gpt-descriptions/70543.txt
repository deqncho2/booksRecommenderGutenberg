Synopsis: 
In 'Color' by Countee Cullen, a young artist named Harold is faced with a life-altering decision when he discovers a hidden secret about his family's past. As he delves deeper into his ancestry, Harold uncovers a world filled with mystery, love, and betrayal. With each revelation, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets that threaten to unravel his own identity. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant Harlem Renaissance, 'Color' is a captivating tale of self-discovery and the power of embracing one's true heritage.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Family, Heritage, Secrets
