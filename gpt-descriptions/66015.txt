Synopsis: 

'History of the Forty-Eighth Regiment M. V. M. During the Civil War' by Albert Plummer takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of the Forty-Eighth Regiment during the tumultuous era of the American Civil War. From the regiment's formation to their involvement in key battles, this book offers a gripping account of the soldiers' bravery, sacrifices, and the challenges they faced on the battlefield. Through personal anecdotes and historical records, Plummer paints a vivid picture of the regiment's struggles and triumphs, providing a unique perspective on this pivotal period in American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Resilience
