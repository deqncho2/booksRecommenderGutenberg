Synopsis: In 'An Original Belle' by Edward Payson Roe, a young woman named Belle finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires for independence. Set in the late 19th century, Belle navigates the challenges of love, family, and personal growth in a world that often restricts women's choices. As she strives to break free from the confines of her upbringing, Belle discovers the power of self-discovery and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Gender roles, societal expectations, self-discovery, personal growth
