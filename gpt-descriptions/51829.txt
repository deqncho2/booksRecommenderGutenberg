Synopsis: In 'The Old Chelsea Bun-House: A Tale of the Last Century' by Manning, Anne, readers are transported to 18th century London, where they are introduced to the bustling world of the famous Chelsea Bun-House. This delightful tale follows the lives of the Bun-House's inhabitants, from the eccentric owner to the diverse group of customers who frequent the establishment. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, friendships are formed, and unexpected romances blossom, all against the backdrop of a rapidly changing city. With its vivid descriptions and charming characters, 'The Old Chelsea Bun-House' offers a captivating glimpse into a bygone era.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Friendship, Love, Social Change
