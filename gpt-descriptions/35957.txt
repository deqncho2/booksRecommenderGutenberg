Synopsis: In 'The Go Ahead Boys in the Island Camp' by Kay, Ross, a group of adventurous boys embark on a thrilling summer camp experience on a remote island. As they navigate the challenges of the wilderness, they stumble upon a mysterious secret that leads them on an unexpected journey filled with danger and excitement. With their resourcefulness and teamwork, the boys must unravel the enigma and face unforeseen obstacles to protect what they hold dear.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Courage, Problem-solving
