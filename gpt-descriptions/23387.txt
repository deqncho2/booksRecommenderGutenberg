Synopsis: In "Washed Ashore; Or, The Tower of Stormount Bay" by William Henry Giles Kingston, a young sailor named Jack is shipwrecked on a mysterious island. As he explores the island, he discovers a hidden tower that holds dark secrets and a connection to his own past. Jack must unravel the mysteries of the tower and confront the dangers that lie within, all while trying to find a way back home.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Identity, Redemption
