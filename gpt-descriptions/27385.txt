Synopsis: In 'The Conspirators; or, The Chevalier d'Harmental', Alexandre Dumas and Auguste Maquet weave a thrilling tale set in 18th century France. The story follows the adventures of the dashing Chevalier d'Harmental, a nobleman caught in a web of political intrigue, secret societies, and forbidden love. As he navigates the treacherous world of courtly politics, he becomes entangled in a plot that could change the course of history. With danger lurking at every turn, the Chevalier must rely on his wit, charm, and swordsmanship to survive and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Political intrigue, Loyalty, Love and sacrifice
