Synopsis: 

In 'By Sheer Pluck: A Tale of the Ashanti War', we follow the thrilling adventures of a young British boy named Dick, who finds himself in the midst of the Ashanti War in West Africa. Captured by the Ashanti warriors, Dick must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to survive in a hostile and unfamiliar environment. As he navigates through dangerous encounters, treacherous landscapes, and encounters with both friend and foe, Dick's determination and resilience are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to escape and make it back home?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Survival, Cultural Clash
