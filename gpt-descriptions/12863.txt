Synopsis: 

'Campaign of the Indus: In a Series of Letters from an Officer of the Bombay Division' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged landscapes and turbulent events of the Indus campaign. Through a series of letters, the book offers a unique perspective on the military operations, political intrigues, and personal experiences of an officer serving in the Bombay Division. As the narrative unfolds, the reader becomes immersed in the challenges and triumphs of the campaign, witnessing the clash of cultures, the strategic maneuvers, and the indomitable spirit of those involved. 

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Epistolary fiction

Themes: War and conflict, Cultural clashes, Personal resilience
