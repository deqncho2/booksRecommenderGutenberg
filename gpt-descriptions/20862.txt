Synopsis: In 'Jerry's Reward' by Evelyn Snead Barnett, a young boy named Jerry embarks on a thrilling adventure when he discovers a mysterious object in his backyard. As he unravels the secrets behind this extraordinary find, Jerry finds himself caught in a web of danger and intrigue. With the help of his friends, he must navigate through unexpected twists and turns to uncover the truth. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the ultimate reward that awaits Jerry.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
