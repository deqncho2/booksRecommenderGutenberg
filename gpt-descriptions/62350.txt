Synopsis: In 'Mutiny in the Void' by Charles R. Tanner, a group of space explorers find themselves stranded on a desolate planet after their ship is sabotaged. As tensions rise and trust begins to crumble, they must navigate the treacherous landscape and uncover the truth behind the mutiny that has left them stranded. With danger lurking at every turn, the survivors must confront their own demons and make difficult choices that will determine their fate.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Betrayal, Survival, Redemption
