Synopsis: In 'Snowball' by Anderson, Poul, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. However, as the world becomes aware of this incredible breakthrough, a dangerous race against time ensues, filled with political intrigue, high-stakes espionage, and unexpected alliances. As the protagonist navigates a treacherous landscape of power and deception, they must confront their own moral dilemmas and make choices that will determine the fate of the world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Political

Themes: Scientific advancement, Ethics, Power dynamics
