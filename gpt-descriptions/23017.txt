Synopsis: In 'Elsket: 1891' by Thomas Nelson Page, a young woman named Elsket finds herself caught between tradition and her own desires in the post-Civil War South. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Elsket must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, this captivating novel explores the power of love and the struggle for personal freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Tradition and Rebellion, Gender Roles and Expectations
