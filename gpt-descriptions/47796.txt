Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the hidden secrets of London's past in 'Bygone London' by Frederick Ross. This captivating book takes readers on a journey through the forgotten corners of the city, unearthing tales of intrigue, mystery, and forgotten history. From the shadowy alleyways of Victorian London to the opulent grandeur of the Tudor era, Ross delves into the lives of the city's inhabitants, both famous and obscure. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, 'Bygone London' paints a vivid picture of a city that has evolved and transformed over centuries, leaving behind a rich tapestry of stories waiting to be discovered.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travel, Biography

Themes: History, Urban development, Cultural heritage
