Synopsis: In 'A Matter of Protocol' by Jack Sharkey, a renowned diplomat finds himself caught in a web of international intrigue when he is assigned to negotiate a delicate peace treaty. As he navigates through political games and personal vendettas, he must unravel the truth behind a mysterious assassination plot that threatens to derail the entire negotiation process. With time running out and the stakes higher than ever, he must rely on his wit, charm, and diplomatic skills to save not only the treaty but also his own life.

Genres: Political thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Diplomacy, Betrayal, Power struggles, International relations
