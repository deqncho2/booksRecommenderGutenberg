Synopsis: In 'Knocking the Neighbors' by Ade George, a seemingly ordinary neighborhood is turned upside down when a mysterious newcomer moves in next door. As the residents become increasingly curious about their enigmatic neighbor, they soon discover that appearances can be deceiving. Secrets, lies, and unexpected connections unravel, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful journey that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Community dynamics, Intrigue
