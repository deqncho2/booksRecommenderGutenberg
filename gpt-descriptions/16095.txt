Synopsis: In the small town of Nordland, a mysterious phenomenon known as the Northern Light occurs every winter, captivating the townspeople with its ethereal beauty. When a young photographer named Emma arrives to document this natural wonder, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden desires that threaten to unravel the fabric of the community. As Emma delves deeper into the enigma of the Northern Light, she discovers that its allure goes far beyond its visual splendor, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and forbidden love.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Secrets and hidden desires, Self-discovery, Forbidden love
