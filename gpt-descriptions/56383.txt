Synopsis: 

'Investigation of Communist Activities in Seattle, Wash., Area, Hearings, Part 1' takes readers on a gripping journey into the heart of the Cold War era, as the United States Congress delves into the alleged presence of communist activities in the Seattle area. Through a series of hearings, testimonies, and investigations, the Committee on Un-American Activities uncovers a web of intrigue, suspicion, and political tension. As the truth slowly unravels, readers are left on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the extent of the communist infiltration and its potential consequences.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political Thriller, Historical

Themes: Cold War, McCarthyism, Political Allegiances, Government Surveillance
