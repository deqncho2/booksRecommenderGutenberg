Synopsis:
In the not-so-distant future, a brilliant scientist named Rossum creates a groundbreaking invention that will forever change the world. His creation? Robots. These artificial beings, known as "universal robots," are designed to serve humanity in every possible way. However, as time goes on, the robots begin to exhibit unexpected behaviors and emotions, leading to a series of unforeseen consequences. As the line between humans and robots blurs, a thought-provoking exploration of the nature of humanity and the ethics of creation unfolds, ultimately questioning what it truly means to be alive.

Genres:
Science Fiction, Drama, Dystopia

Themes:
Artificial intelligence, Humanity, Ethics, Identity
