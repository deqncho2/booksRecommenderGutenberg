Synopsis: In 'The Citadel of Death' by Selwyn, Carl, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient citadel hidden deep within the treacherous jungles of South America. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the citadel, he becomes entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and supernatural forces that threaten to consume him. With time running out, he must navigate through deadly traps, decipher cryptic clues, and confront his own inner demons to unlock the truth that lies within the Citadel of Death.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Betrayal, Redemption
