Synopsis: In 'Drawings and Pharmacy in Al-Zahrawi's 10th-Century Surgical Treatise', Sami Khalaf Hamarneh delves into the fascinating world of ancient medicine and explores the intricate drawings and pharmaceutical knowledge found in Al-Zahrawi's surgical treatise. Through meticulous research and analysis, Hamarneh uncovers the secrets behind these drawings and their significance in the field of medicine. This captivating work takes readers on a journey through time, shedding light on the remarkable advancements made in surgical techniques and pharmaceutical practices during the 10th century.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Medical history, Art history

Themes: Ancient medicine, Surgical techniques, Pharmaceutical knowledge
