Synopsis: 

"A Sketch of the Life of Elizabeth T. Stone and of Her Persecutions" is a gripping memoir that delves into the extraordinary life of Elizabeth T. Stone and the unimaginable hardships she endured. From her early years to her time in the Charlestown McLean Asylum, Stone recounts her experiences of persecution and the pretense of insanity that led to her confinement. This powerful narrative sheds light on the injustices faced by Stone and offers a glimpse into the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Memoir, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Persecution, Mental Health, Resilience
