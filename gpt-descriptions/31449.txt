Synopsis: 

'Across the Plains to California in 1852: Journal of Mrs. Lodisa Frizzell' takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the untamed American West during the mid-19th century. Through the eyes of Lodisa Frizzell, a courageous and determined woman, we witness the hardships, dangers, and triumphs of a group of pioneers as they make their way across the treacherous plains to seek a new life in California. From encounters with Native American tribes to facing the harsh realities of nature, this gripping journal offers a firsthand account of the challenges and resilience of those who dared to venture into the unknown.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Survival and resilience, Cultural encounters
