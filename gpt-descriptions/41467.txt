Synopsis: 
'The Everlasting Mercy' by John Masefield is a gripping narrative poem that follows the transformational journey of a troubled man named Saul Kane. After a series of life-altering events, Saul finds himself at a crossroads, torn between his dark past and the possibility of redemption. As he embarks on a soul-searching quest, he encounters love, loss, and the raw beauty of nature, ultimately leading him to a profound spiritual awakening. Masefield's vivid imagery and lyrical storytelling captivate readers, immersing them in Saul's tumultuous world and leaving them yearning to discover the outcome of his extraordinary journey.

Genres: Narrative poetry, spiritual fiction, coming-of-age

Themes: Redemption, spirituality, nature, self-discovery
