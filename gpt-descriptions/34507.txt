Synopsis: In 'The Heritage of the Hills' by Arthur Preston Hankins, a young man named David embarks on a journey to the Appalachian Mountains, seeking solace and a fresh start. As he immerses himself in the rugged beauty of the hills, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own secrets and desires. David soon finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and romance, as he uncovers the hidden heritage of the hills and discovers the true meaning of love and redemption.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Love, Secrets
