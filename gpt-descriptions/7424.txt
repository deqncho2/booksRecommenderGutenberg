Synopsis: In 'The Wishing-Ring Man' by Margaret Widdemer, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey to find the mysterious Wishing-Ring Man, who is said to possess a ring that can grant any wish. As Mary encounters various characters along the way, she discovers that the power of the ring comes with unexpected consequences. With each wish, Mary must confront the true desires of her heart and make choices that will shape her future. This enchanting tale explores the transformative power of wishes and the importance of self-discovery.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Adventure

Themes: The power of wishes, self-discovery, the consequences of choices
