Synopsis: 

Step into the enchanting world of West Cornwall as William Bottrell takes you on a captivating journey through its rich traditions and hearthside stories in the second series of his remarkable collection. From ancient legends of mermaids and piskies to eerie tales of haunted mines and spectral apparitions, this book unveils the hidden folklore and mysticism that permeates the rugged landscape. Delve into the lives of the local inhabitants as they navigate the supernatural realm, encountering both benevolent and malevolent forces that shape their destinies. With each story, Bottrell weaves a tapestry of wonder and intrigue, leaving readers spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Folklore, Supernatural, Historical

Themes: Tradition, Mythology, Supernatural encounters, Local legends
