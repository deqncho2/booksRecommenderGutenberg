Synopsis: In the remote Irish countryside, a blind couple named Martin and Mary Doul live in blissful ignorance of their own appearance. However, when a traveling saint arrives and miraculously restores their sight, their world is turned upside down. As they come face to face with their own reflection, they must grapple with the harsh realities of beauty and the true nature of happiness. Set against the backdrop of rural Ireland, this comedic play explores the profound impact of self-perception and the complexities of human desire.

Genres: Comedy, Drama

Themes: Appearance vs. reality, Self-perception, Happiness
