Synopsis: In "The Ocean Wireless Boys of the Iceberg Patrol" by John Henry Goldfrap, a group of young wireless operators find themselves on a thrilling adventure in the treacherous waters of the North Atlantic. Assigned to the iceberg patrol, they must navigate through dangerous ice fields, face unexpected challenges, and uncover a mysterious secret that could change everything. As they strive to fulfill their duty and protect the lives of those at sea, the boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and the power of communication to overcome the perils that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival, Communication
