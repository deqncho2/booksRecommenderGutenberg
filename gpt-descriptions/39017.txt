Synopsis: 
In "Eight Lectures on Theoretical Physics, Delivered at Columbia University in 1909," Max Planck takes readers on a captivating journey through the fundamental principles of physics. With his brilliant insights and engaging explanations, Planck explores the intricate world of atoms, energy, and the nature of matter. From the mysterious behavior of light to the revolutionary concept of quantum theory, this collection of lectures offers a fascinating exploration of the building blocks of the universe.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Science, Physics

Themes: 
Quantum theory, Atomic structure, Energy, Matter, Scientific discovery
