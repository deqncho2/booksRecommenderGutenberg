Synopsis: In 'A Round Dozen' by Susan Coolidge, twelve captivating short stories take readers on a journey through various settings and time periods. From the bustling streets of New York City to the tranquil countryside, each tale introduces a unique cast of characters facing unexpected challenges and heartwarming moments. With a blend of humor, emotion, and a touch of mystery, this collection promises to keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what surprises lie ahead.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Contemporary Literature

Themes: Human connection, resilience, the power of storytelling
