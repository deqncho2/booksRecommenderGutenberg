Synopsis: 

'American Life: A Narrative of Two Years' City and Country Residence in the United States' follows the captivating journey of Mrs. Felton as she immerses herself in the diverse landscapes and cultures of the United States. From bustling cities to serene countryside, she encounters a myriad of experiences and encounters that shape her understanding of American life. Through her keen observations and vivid storytelling, Mrs. Felton offers a unique perspective on the people, customs, and traditions she encounters during her two-year adventure.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Cultural diversity, Identity, Exploration, Social observations
