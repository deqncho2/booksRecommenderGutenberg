Synopsis: 

In 'Nursing as Caring: A Model for Transforming Practice', Boykin and Schoenhofer present a groundbreaking exploration of the role of caring in nursing. Through thought-provoking insights and real-life examples, they challenge traditional notions of healthcare and propose a transformative model that places caring at the center of nursing practice. This thoughtfully crafted book invites readers to reconsider the essence of nursing and its potential to bring about profound healing and connection.

Genres: Non-fiction, Healthcare, Nursing

Themes: Caring, Transformative Practice, Healing, Connection
