Synopsis: In "Daughters of the Revolution and Their Times: 1769 - 1776 A Historical Romance," Charles Carleton Coffin takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous years leading up to the American Revolution. Set against the backdrop of colonial America, this historical romance follows the lives of three young women from different backgrounds who find themselves caught up in the fervor of the revolutionary spirit. As they navigate love, loyalty, and the pursuit of freedom, their paths intertwine with key figures of the time, including George Washington and Benjamin Franklin. Through their personal struggles and triumphs, the novel offers a vivid portrayal of the challenges faced by women during this pivotal period in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Revolution, Women's Rights, Patriotism, Love and Sacrifice
