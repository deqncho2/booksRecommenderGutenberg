Synopsis: In 'Infiltration' by Algis Budrys, a renowned scientist is recruited by a secret organization to investigate a mysterious phenomenon that threatens the very fabric of reality. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a web of intrigue, deception, and mind-bending discoveries that challenge everything he thought he knew. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of hidden agendas and unexpected alliances to save humanity from an unimaginable fate.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Conspiracy, Human Potential
