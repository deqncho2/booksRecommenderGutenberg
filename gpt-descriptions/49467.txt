Synopsis: 

In "Radiation" by Phillips, Percy, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the world. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but also the fate of humanity. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal to uncover the truth and prevent a catastrophic event from unfolding.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific ethics, Consequences of discovery, Moral dilemmas
