Synopsis: In the small New England town of Websterbridge during the American Revolution, the lives of three individuals become intertwined in unexpected ways. Richard Dudgeon, a self-proclaimed "devil's disciple," finds himself caught up in a dangerous game of mistaken identity when he is mistaken for a local minister. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, Richard's unconventional beliefs and rebellious nature challenge the norms of society, leading to a series of surprising events that will forever change the lives of those involved.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Rebellion, Morality, Hypocrisy
