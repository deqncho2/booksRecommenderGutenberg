Synopsis: 

"The Torch Bearer: A Look Forward and Back at The Woman's Journal, the Organ of the Woman's Movement" by Agnes E. Ryan takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of The Woman's Journal, a prominent publication during the women's movement. Through a combination of historical analysis and personal anecdotes, Ryan delves into the struggles, triumphs, and influential figures that shaped the journal and the broader women's movement. From the suffrage movement to the fight for equal rights, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the challenges faced by women and the pivotal role played by The Woman's Journal in advocating for change.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Women's studies, Journalism

Themes: Women's rights, Feminism, Activism, Media influence
