Synopsis: 

'Across the Prairie in a Motor Caravan: A 3,000 Mile Tour by Two Englishwomen on Behalf of Religious Education' follows the incredible journey of Frances Hatton Eva Hasell and Iris Eugenie Sayle as they embark on a daring adventure across the vast prairies of America in a motor caravan. Their mission? To promote religious education and spread their message to the people they encounter along the way. As they navigate through unfamiliar landscapes, face unexpected challenges, and meet a diverse range of individuals, the two Englishwomen find themselves on a transformative journey that goes far beyond their initial purpose.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Religious education, Cultural exchange, Personal growth
