Synopsis: In 'Jan of the Windmill: A Story of the Plains' by Juliana Horatia Ewing, follow the captivating journey of Jan, a young boy living in a windmill on the vast plains. As Jan navigates the challenges of his humble life, unexpected events unfold, leading him on a path of self-discovery and resilience. With the windmill as a backdrop, Jan's story intertwines with the lives of those around him, revealing the power of determination and the importance of community. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Resilience, Community, Self-Discovery
