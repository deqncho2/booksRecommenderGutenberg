Synopsis: In 'The Wages of Virtue' by Percival Christopher Wren, a young man named Richard is thrust into a world of adventure and danger when he inherits a mysterious artifact. As he embarks on a quest to uncover the truth behind its origins, Richard finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each step, he must navigate treacherous landscapes and face formidable adversaries, all while questioning his own beliefs and the true nature of virtue. Will Richard's journey lead him to enlightenment or will he pay the ultimate price for his pursuit of truth?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Morality, Identity, Redemption
