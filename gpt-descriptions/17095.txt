Synopsis:
In "Bunny Brown and His Sister Sue on an Auto Tour," siblings Bunny and Sue embark on an exciting adventure with their family. As they set off on a cross-country journey in their new automobile, they encounter unexpected challenges and thrilling experiences along the way. From exploring new places to meeting interesting characters, Bunny and Sue's auto tour promises to be a delightful and unforgettable escapade.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Family

Themes: Exploration, Independence, Family bonds
