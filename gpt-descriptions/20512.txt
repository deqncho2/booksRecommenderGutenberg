Synopsis: 
In 'Man and Maid' by Elinor Glyn, a young and beautiful maid named Babette finds herself entangled in a passionate and forbidden love affair with her employer, a wealthy and powerful man. As their relationship intensifies, they must navigate the complexities of societal expectations, class differences, and the consequences of their actions. With secrets and desires lurking in the shadows, this captivating tale explores the boundaries of love and the sacrifices one is willing to make to be with the person they desire.

Genres: Romance, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Social class, Sacrifice
