Synopsis: In 'The Instant of Now' by Cox, Irving E., a brilliant scientist invents a groundbreaking technology that allows people to experience the present moment with unparalleled clarity. As the world becomes obsessed with this new invention, the protagonist finds themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between reality and perception begins to blur. With unexpected twists and a race against time, this gripping novel explores the consequences of living in the present and the power of perception.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Perception, Reality, Technology, Morality
