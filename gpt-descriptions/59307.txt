Synopsis: In 'The April Baby's Book of Tunes: with the story of how they came to be written', Elizabeth von Arnim takes us on a delightful journey through the whimsical world of a young girl named April. Through a collection of enchanting tunes, we discover the captivating stories behind each melody and the magical moments that inspired their creation. From mischievous adventures to heartwarming encounters, April's tales will transport readers to a world filled with imagination and wonder.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Music

Themes: Imagination, Creativity, Childhood, Adventure
