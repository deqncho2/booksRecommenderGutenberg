Synopsis: In "Churchwardens' Manual: their duties, powers, rights, and privileges," George Henry Sumner delves into the intriguing world of churchwardens, uncovering the hidden responsibilities, authority, and privileges that come with this often overlooked role. From managing church finances to overseeing the maintenance of the building, Sumner provides a comprehensive guide that sheds light on the fascinating and sometimes complex duties of churchwardens. With practical advice and historical anecdotes, this manual offers a captivating exploration of the inner workings of the church, revealing the untold stories behind these unsung heroes.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, History

Themes: Church administration, Religious leadership, Historical context
