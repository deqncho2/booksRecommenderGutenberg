Synopsis: 

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the captivating world of rural Canada fifty years ago. In this remarkable memoir, Haight, Canniff takes us on a nostalgic journey through his personal recollections and reminiscences as a sexagenarian. From the breathtaking landscapes to the charming anecdotes of daily life, this book paints a vivid picture of a bygone era. Discover the joys and challenges of country living, the close-knit communities, and the timeless wisdom passed down through generations. With heartfelt storytelling and a keen eye for detail, Haight, Canniff invites readers to experience the beauty and simplicity of a forgotten time.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Rural life, Nostalgia, Community, Wisdom
