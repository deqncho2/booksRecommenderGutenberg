Synopsis: In "The Everlasting Man," G.K. Chesterton takes readers on a captivating journey through history, philosophy, and theology, unraveling the mysteries of human existence. With his characteristic wit and profound insights, Chesterton explores the origins of civilization, the rise and fall of empires, and the profound impact of Christianity on the world. Through his unique perspective, he challenges conventional wisdom and invites readers to ponder the eternal questions that have shaped humanity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Theology

Themes: Human nature, History, Religion, Civilization
