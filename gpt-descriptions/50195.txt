Synopsis: In 'Practical Etiquette' by Cora C. Klein, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the intricacies of social decorum and the art of graceful living. From formal dinner parties to casual gatherings, this guide offers invaluable advice on navigating various social situations with finesse and charm. With a delightful blend of wit and wisdom, Klein explores the nuances of polite conversation, proper attire, and the subtle art of making a lasting impression. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will discover a treasure trove of practical tips and timeless etiquette that will empower them to navigate the complexities of modern society with confidence and grace.

Genres: Self-help, Lifestyle, Etiquette

Themes: Social decorum, Graceful living, Polite conversation, Making a lasting impression
