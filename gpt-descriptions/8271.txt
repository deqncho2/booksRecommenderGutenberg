Synopsis: 
In 'The World English Bible (WEB): Acts' by Anonymous, embark on a captivating journey through the early days of Christianity as the apostles spread the teachings of Jesus Christ. Follow the lives of Peter, Paul, and other key figures as they face persecution, perform miracles, and establish the foundations of the early Church. From the dramatic conversion of Saul to the thrilling encounters with Roman authorities, this book offers a gripping account of the trials and triumphs of the early Christian movement.

Genres: Religious, Historical, Drama

Themes: Faith and Conversion, Persecution and Resilience, Divine Intervention
