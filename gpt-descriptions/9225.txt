Synopsis: In "Monsieur du Miroir," a young artist named Walter is captivated by a mysterious mirror that seems to possess a supernatural power. As he delves deeper into its enchantment, he becomes entangled in a web of illusion and reality, questioning his own perception of the world around him. With each reflection, Walter's obsession grows, leading him down a path of self-discovery and unexpected consequences.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, psychological thriller

Themes: Perception vs. reality, the power of art, obsession, identity
