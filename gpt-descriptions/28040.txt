Synopsis: In 'Robin Tremayne: A Story of the Marian Persecution', Emily Sarah Holt takes us on a gripping journey through the tumultuous period of the Marian Persecution in England. The story follows Robin Tremayne, a young noblewoman who finds herself caught in the crossfire of religious conflict and political intrigue. As she navigates the dangerous landscape of betrayal and loyalty, Robin's courage and resilience are put to the ultimate test. With vivid historical detail and a captivating narrative, this tale of love, faith, and survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Religious persecution, Loyalty, Courage, Survival
