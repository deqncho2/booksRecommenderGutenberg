Synopsis: 
In 'Bob Strong's Holidays: Adrift in the Channel' by John C. Hutcheson, we follow the thrilling adventures of Bob Strong, a young sailor who finds himself in a perilous situation while on holiday. When his boat is caught in a violent storm, Bob is left adrift in the treacherous English Channel, fighting for survival against the elements and unexpected dangers. As he battles the forces of nature, Bob's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to navigate his way back to safety, or will he be forever lost at sea?

Genres: Adventure, Action, Survival

Themes: Resilience, Survival, Man vs. Nature
