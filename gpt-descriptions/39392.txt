Synopsis: In 'The Secrets of a Savoyard' by Henry A. Lytton, a renowned opera singer finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he receives a cryptic message from an anonymous sender. As he delves deeper into the secrets of his past, he uncovers a hidden world of scandal, betrayal, and forbidden love. With his career and reputation on the line, he must navigate the treacherous waters of the opera world and confront the demons of his past to uncover the truth. This gripping tale of passion, deception, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final curtain falls.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the power of art and music, the consequences of one's actions
