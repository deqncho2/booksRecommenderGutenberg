Synopsis:
'Colomba' by Mérimée, Prosper is a gripping tale set in Corsica during the early 19th century. The story follows a young man named Orso, who returns to his homeland after serving in the French army. Upon his arrival, he becomes entangled in a web of family feuds, vendettas, and political intrigue. As Orso navigates the treacherous landscape of Corsican society, he encounters a mysterious and captivating woman named Colomba, who holds the key to unraveling the secrets of his family's past. With its twists and turns, 'Colomba' keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the dark secrets that haunt the characters.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Family, Revenge, Honor, Loyalty
