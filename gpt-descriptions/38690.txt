Synopsis: In "What Shall We Do?" by Leo Tolstoy, a young woman named Masha finds herself torn between the expectations of society and her own desires for love and fulfillment. As she navigates the complexities of relationships and societal norms, Masha embarks on a journey of self-discovery that challenges her to question the very fabric of her existence. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Russia, this thought-provoking tale explores the timeless themes of love, duty, and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Psychological

Themes: Society and conformity, Individualism, Love and relationships
