Synopsis: 
"The Sweet Girl Graduates: A Farce in Three Acts and an Epilogue" follows the hilarious misadventures of a group of young women as they navigate the challenges of graduating from a prestigious finishing school. From mistaken identities to romantic entanglements, this uproarious farce takes the reader on a wild ride filled with laughter and unexpected twists. Will these sweet girls be able to overcome their obstacles and find their happily ever afters?

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Romance

Themes: Coming of age, Identity, Love and relationships
