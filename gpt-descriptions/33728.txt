Synopsis: In 'Believe You Me!' by Nina Wilcox Putnam, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Emily must navigate a world of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, she uncovers shocking truths that challenge everything she thought she knew about her own identity. Will Emily be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Family Secrets
