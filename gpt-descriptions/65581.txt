Synopsis: 
In "Jean Craig Finds Romance" by Kay Lyttleton, Jean Craig, a young and ambitious woman, embarks on a journey that takes her far from her small hometown. As she navigates the challenges of a new city and a demanding job, Jean finds herself caught in a whirlwind of unexpected events and encounters. Along the way, she discovers the complexities of love, friendship, and the pursuit of her dreams. With each twist and turn, Jean's determination and resilience are put to the test, leading her to question what truly matters in life.

Genres: 
Romance, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Ambition, Love and relationships
