Synopsis: 
In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 62, Feb 3, 1872', readers are transported to the vibrant and satirical world of Victorian England. This volume of the renowned magazine showcases a collection of witty and humorous articles, cartoons, and sketches that cleverly dissect the social and political issues of the time. From comical observations on fashion and etiquette to sharp critiques of the upper class and political figures, this edition of Punch offers a delightful and insightful glimpse into the Victorian era.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Magazine

Themes: Social commentary, Victorian society, Politics
