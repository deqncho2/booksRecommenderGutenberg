Synopsis: Agatha's Husband: A Novel follows the life of Agatha, a young woman who finds herself in an arranged marriage to a mysterious and enigmatic man. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, Agatha begins to uncover secrets and hidden truths that challenge her perception of love, loyalty, and identity. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this captivating novel explores the depths of human emotions and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Arranged Marriage, Identity, Secrets and Betrayal
