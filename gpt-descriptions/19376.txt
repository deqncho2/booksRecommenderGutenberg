Synopsis: In 'Mrs. Piper & the Society for Psychical Research' by Sage, Michael, a mysterious medium named Mrs. Piper captures the attention of the esteemed Society for Psychical Research. As they delve into her extraordinary abilities, they uncover a world of secrets, deception, and the blurred lines between the supernatural and reality. With each session, the boundaries of what is possible are pushed, leaving the Society questioning their own beliefs and the true nature of the human mind.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: The power of the mind, the search for truth, the exploration of the unknown
