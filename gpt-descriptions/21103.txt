Synopsis:
In 'Sisters Three' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, three sisters find themselves facing unexpected challenges when their father's sudden death leaves them in a precarious financial situation. As they navigate the complexities of their new circumstances, they must confront their own desires, ambitions, and the bonds that tie them together. With the arrival of a mysterious stranger, their lives take an intriguing turn, leading them on a journey of self-discovery, love, and the pursuit of happiness. 

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Family

Themes:
Sisterhood, Financial hardship, Love and relationships
