Synopsis: In 'Thoughts, Moods and Ideals: Crimes of Leisure' by W. D. Lighthall, a seemingly idyllic world is shattered when a series of mysterious crimes disrupt the tranquility of a small, affluent community. As the residents grapple with the shocking events, they are forced to confront their own dark secrets and hidden desires. With each twist and turn, the line between innocence and guilt becomes increasingly blurred, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind these crimes of leisure.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Morality, Human Nature
