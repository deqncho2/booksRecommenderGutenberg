Synopsis: 
In the Saddle: A Collection of Poems on Horseback-Riding takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of horseback-riding, where the rhythmic galloping of hooves echoes the beating of the human heart. From the exhilaration of a wild ride across open fields to the tender connection between rider and horse, these poems beautifully capture the essence of the equestrian experience. With vivid imagery and lyrical language, this collection explores the profound bond between humans and horses, delving into the emotions, challenges, and joys that come with being in the saddle.

Genres: Poetry, Sports, Nature

Themes: Connection between humans and animals, Freedom, Adventure, Love for nature
