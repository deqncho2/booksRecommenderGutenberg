Synopsis: 
"The Stranger in France" takes readers on a captivating journey from Devonshire to Paris, as seen through the eyes of the author, Sir John Carr. Through vivid aqua tint engravings, Carr brings to life the landscapes, architecture, and people he encounters along the way. As the reader follows Carr's travels, they are immersed in the beauty and charm of France, experiencing the culture, history, and unique moments that make this journey unforgettable.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Illustrated Book

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Exchange, Adventure
