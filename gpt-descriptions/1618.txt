Synopsis: 
In the remote Irish countryside, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of secrets and desires that threaten to unravel her world. As the mysterious stranger arrives at her doorstep, the boundaries between reality and illusion blur, leading to unexpected consequences. 'In the Shadow of the Glen' is a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and the power of hidden desires.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Irish Literature

Themes: Love and desire, Betrayal, Illusion and reality
