Synopsis: 
In "John Marshall" by James Bradley Thayer, a young lawyer named John Marshall finds himself thrust into the heart of a nation on the brink of change. As he navigates the treacherous waters of politics and law, Marshall becomes entangled in a high-stakes legal battle that will test his principles and shape the course of American history. With his reputation and the fate of the nation hanging in the balance, Marshall must summon all his wit, courage, and legal expertise to fight for justice and defend the Constitution.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Legal Drama, Political Thriller

Themes: Justice, Constitutional Law, Political Power, Personal Sacrifice
