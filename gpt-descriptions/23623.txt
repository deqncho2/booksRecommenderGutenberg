Synopsis: In 'The White Lady of Hazelwood: A Tale of the Fourteenth Century' by Emily Sarah Holt, a young woman named Maud finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she becomes the lady-in-waiting to Lady Isabel, the wife of a powerful nobleman. As Maud delves deeper into the secrets of Hazelwood Castle, she uncovers a haunting legend surrounding a ghostly white lady and a forbidden love affair that spans generations. With danger lurking around every corner, Maud must navigate treacherous alliances and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and betrayal, the power of secrets, the supernatural, loyalty and honor
