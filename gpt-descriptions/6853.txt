Synopsis: 
In 'Betty Gordon in Washington; Or, Strange Adventures in a Great City', Betty Gordon finds herself in the bustling city of Washington, D.C., where she embarks on a series of unexpected and thrilling adventures. From mysterious strangers to hidden secrets, Betty's curiosity leads her down a path filled with intrigue and danger. As she navigates the political landscape of the nation's capital, Betty must rely on her wit and resourcefulness to uncover the truth and protect those she cares about. Will she be able to unravel the mysteries that surround her, or will she become entangled in a web of deceit?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Curiosity, Independence, Friendship
