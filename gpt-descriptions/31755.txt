Synopsis: In 'Political Application' by John Victor Peterson, a young and ambitious political strategist finds himself caught in a web of power, manipulation, and moral dilemmas as he navigates the treacherous world of politics. With high stakes and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of political campaigns and the lengths one will go to achieve their goals. As secrets unravel and alliances shift, the protagonist must confront his own values and question the true cost of success. 

Genres: Political thriller, suspense, drama

Themes: Power, manipulation, morality, ambition
