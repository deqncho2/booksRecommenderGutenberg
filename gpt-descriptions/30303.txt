Synopsis: In 'The Passing of Ku Sui' by Gilmore, Anthony, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the past, he becomes entangled in a web of treachery, betrayal, and supernatural forces. With time running out, he must decipher cryptic clues and face unimaginable challenges to prevent a catastrophic event that could alter the course of history forever.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Archaeology, Betrayal, Ancient Civilizations, Fate
