Synopsis: In 'The Dash for Khartoum: A Tale of the Nile Expedition', G. A. Henty takes readers on an exhilarating adventure through the treacherous landscapes of Africa. Set during the late 19th century, the story follows a group of British soldiers as they embark on a daring mission to rescue General Gordon, who is trapped in the besieged city of Khartoum. Facing numerous obstacles, including hostile tribes, harsh terrain, and the mighty Nile River, the protagonists must navigate their way through danger and uncertainty to accomplish their mission. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Henty's tale captures the spirit of exploration and the courage of those who dared to venture into the unknown.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Bravery, Loyalty, Survival
