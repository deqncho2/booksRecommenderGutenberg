Synopsis: 
In 'Dorothy Dixon and the Double Cousin' by Wayne, Dorothy, a young and adventurous girl, finds herself caught up in a mysterious family secret when her long-lost cousin arrives unexpectedly. As Dorothy delves deeper into the enigma surrounding her cousin's sudden appearance, she uncovers a web of deceit, hidden identities, and dangerous secrets that threaten to tear her family apart. With her quick wit and determination, Dorothy must navigate through a series of thrilling twists and turns to uncover the truth and protect her loved ones. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Family Drama

Themes: Family bonds, Identity, Deception
