Synopsis: 
In 'A Fleet in Being: Notes of Two Trips With The Channel Squadron', Rudyard Kipling takes us on an exhilarating journey aboard the British Royal Navy's Channel Squadron. Through his vivid descriptions and personal experiences, Kipling offers a unique glimpse into the life of sailors and the power of a naval fleet. From the bustling ports to the thrilling naval exercises, the author captures the essence of life at sea and the awe-inspiring might of a fleet in being.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travelogue, Military History

Themes: Naval power, Adventure, Camaraderie, Patriotism
