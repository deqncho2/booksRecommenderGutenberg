Synopsis: In "The Red City: A Novel of the Second Administration of President Washington," Silas Weir Mitchell takes readers on a captivating journey through the political intrigue and personal struggles of President George Washington's second term. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing nation, the story follows a diverse cast of characters as they navigate the treacherous waters of power, loyalty, and love in the heart of the young United States. As tensions rise and secrets unravel, the fate of the nation hangs in the balance, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Romance

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Love and Sacrifice
