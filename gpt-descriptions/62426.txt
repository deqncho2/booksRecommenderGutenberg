Synopsis: In 'Black-out' by Farrell, Joseph, a city is plunged into darkness as a mysterious power outage strikes, leaving its inhabitants in a state of chaos and uncertainty. As the darkness persists, the lives of several individuals intertwine, revealing hidden secrets, unexpected connections, and the lengths people will go to survive. With each passing hour, tensions rise, alliances shift, and the true nature of humanity is put to the test. Will they find a way to overcome the darkness, or will it consume them all?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Survival, Human Nature, Secrets and Betrayal
