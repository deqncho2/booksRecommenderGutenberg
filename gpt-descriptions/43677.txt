Synopsis: In 'Whispers at Dawn; Or, The Eye' by Roy J. Snell, a young woman named Dawn finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden artifact with incredible powers. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the artifact, Dawn must navigate treacherous alliances, face supernatural forces, and uncover the truth about her own identity. With each whisper at dawn, the stakes rise higher, and Dawn's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Power, Secrets
