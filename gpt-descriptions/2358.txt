Synopsis: 
In "The After House" by Mary Roberts Rinehart, a young woman named Liddy Allen finds herself in a peculiar situation when she takes a job as a nurse on a remote island. As she settles into her new role, she becomes entangled in a web of mystery and suspense, surrounded by a cast of intriguing characters. Strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena begin to unfold, leading Liddy to question the secrets that lie within the eerie after house. With danger lurking around every corner, Liddy must navigate through the shadows of the past to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Supernatural elements, Psychological suspense
