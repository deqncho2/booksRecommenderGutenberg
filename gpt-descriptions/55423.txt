Synopsis: In 'Le Coq d'Or (The Golden Cock): An Opera in Three Acts', a magical golden cock becomes the center of a captivating tale filled with political intrigue, forbidden love, and supernatural forces. As the story unfolds, a powerful ruler becomes obsessed with possessing the enchanted bird, leading to a series of unexpected consequences that threaten the kingdom's stability. With its enchanting music and dramatic twists, this opera takes the audience on a thrilling journey through a world where power, desire, and destiny collide.

Genres: Opera, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Power and corruption, Love and desire, Fate and destiny
