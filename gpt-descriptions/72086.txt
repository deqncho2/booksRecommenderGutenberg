Synopsis: In 'Gay-Neck: The Story of a Pigeon' by Dhan Gopal Mukerji, embark on an extraordinary journey alongside a young Indian boy named Gay-Neck and his beloved pigeon. Set against the backdrop of World War I, this captivating tale follows Gay-Neck's transformation from a simple bird to a symbol of courage and loyalty. As they navigate through the perils of war and the challenges of their own identities, Gay-Neck and his young master forge an unbreakable bond that transcends boundaries and inspires hope.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Identity, Friendship
