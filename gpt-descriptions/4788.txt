Synopsis: In the midst of the Franco-Prussian War, a group of French officers find themselves stationed in a small town occupied by German soldiers. As tensions rise and the power dynamics shift, the officers become entangled in a dangerous game of pride, desire, and revenge. Amidst the chaos, a mysterious woman named Mademoiselle Fifi emerges, captivating both the French and German soldiers with her beauty and enigmatic nature. As the story unfolds, the characters are forced to confront their own prejudices and the consequences of their actions, leading to a shocking climax that challenges the boundaries of loyalty and honor.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Power dynamics, Prejudice, Desire, Revenge, Loyalty
