Synopsis: In "The Ladies of the White House; Or, in the Home of the Presidents," Laura C. Holloway takes readers on a captivating journey through the social and domestic lives of the Presidents of the United States, from George Washington to the present time of 1881. Delving into the private lives of these influential leaders, Holloway uncovers the untold stories of the remarkable women who stood beside them, offering a unique perspective on the history of the White House and the role of women in shaping the nation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Women's history

Themes: Women's empowerment, Presidential history, Domestic life, Social influence
