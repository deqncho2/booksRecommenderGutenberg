Synopsis: In the "Children's book of patriotic stories: The spirit of '76," young readers are transported back in time to the American Revolution, where they will embark on a thrilling journey alongside brave and determined characters. Through a collection of captivating tales, this book brings to life the spirit of patriotism, courage, and sacrifice that defined this pivotal period in history. From the stirring events of the Boston Tea Party to the heroic acts of ordinary citizens turned soldiers, these stories will inspire and ignite a sense of pride in young readers as they discover the true meaning of liberty and the power of unity.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Anthology

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Sacrifice, Unity
