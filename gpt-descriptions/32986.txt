Synopsis: 
In 'Thomas Stanley: His Original Lyrics, Complete, In Their Collated Readings of 1647, 1651, 1657', readers are invited into the captivating world of Thomas Stanley, a renowned poet of the 17th century. This collection presents a comprehensive compilation of Stanley's original lyrics, showcasing the evolution of his work through different editions. With an insightful introduction, textual notes, a list of editions, translations, and a portrait, this book offers a fascinating exploration of Stanley's poetic journey and the impact of his words on the literary landscape of his time.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Criticism, Biography

Themes: Artistic evolution, Historical context, Literary influence
