Synopsis: In 'Mount Royal: A Novel. Volume 2 of 3', the captivating story continues as the lives of the characters intertwine amidst the opulent backdrop of Mount Royal, a grand estate in the English countryside. Secrets are unveiled, forbidden desires are explored, and unexpected alliances are formed, leading to a series of dramatic events that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. As the plot thickens, the characters must navigate love, betrayal, and the consequences of their actions, all while trying to maintain their place in society. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this volume of 'Mount Royal' promises to be a thrilling and addictive read.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Class, Secrets and Betrayal
