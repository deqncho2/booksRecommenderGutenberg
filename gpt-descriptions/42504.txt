Synopsis: 
In "The Campers Out; Or, The Right Path and the Wrong" by Edward Sylvester Ellis, a group of adventurous young campers embark on a thrilling journey into the wilderness. As they navigate through the untamed landscapes, they encounter unexpected challenges and dangers that test their courage and resilience. Along the way, they must make difficult choices that will determine their fate and shape their characters. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding moments, this captivating tale takes readers on an unforgettable outdoor adventure.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Fiction

Themes: Courage, Decision-making, Nature, Friendship
