Synopsis: 
In 'Love Eternal' by Haggard, H. Rider, a young man named Leo Vincey embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of his ancestry. Guided by a mysterious letter left by his late father, Leo finds himself in a remote African kingdom where he encounters a beautiful and enigmatic woman named Ayesha. As Leo delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding Ayesha, he becomes entangled in a web of love, immortality, and ancient curses that will test the limits of his courage and devotion.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Love and obsession, the pursuit of immortality, fate and destiny
