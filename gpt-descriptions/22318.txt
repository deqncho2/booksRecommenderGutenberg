Synopsis:
In "Crocker's Hole: From "Slain By The Doones" By R. D. Blackmore," we are transported to the rugged and mysterious landscape of Exmoor, where a young man named Crocker finds himself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the notorious Doone clan, Crocker's life takes a thrilling turn, filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With vivid descriptions and a captivating narrative, this gripping tale explores the dark side of human nature and the lengths one will go to seek justice and redemption.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Revenge, Love and Betrayal, Justice
