Synopsis: 
In the vast and rugged landscape of the American Southwest, two Catholic priests embark on a remarkable journey to establish a diocese in the newly acquired territory of New Mexico. As they navigate the challenges of a foreign land, they encounter a rich tapestry of cultures, from the indigenous Pueblo people to the Spanish settlers and the Mexican ranchers. Through their tireless efforts, they strive to bring faith, compassion, and understanding to a region marked by hardship and isolation. Along the way, they face personal trials and witness the profound impact of their mission on the lives of those they serve. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Western, Religious fiction

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Cultural clash, Exploration of the American Southwest, Sacrifice and devotion
