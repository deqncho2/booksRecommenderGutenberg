Synopsis:
In 'The Time Machine', an eccentric inventor creates a remarkable device that allows him to travel through time. Venturing into the future, he encounters a world vastly different from his own, where humanity has evolved into two distinct species. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of time, he uncovers shocking truths about the fate of mankind and the consequences of its actions. This thrilling and thought-provoking tale takes readers on a captivating journey through time and the human condition.

Genres:
Science Fiction, Adventure, Dystopian

Themes:
Evolution, Social Class, Humanity's Future
