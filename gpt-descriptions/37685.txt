Synopsis: 
In 'The Brass Bottle: A Farcical Fantastic Play in Four Acts', a young architect named Horace Ventimore stumbles upon an ancient brass bottle that holds a mischievous and powerful genie. As Horace tries to navigate the chaos caused by the genie's unpredictable magic, he finds himself entangled in a series of hilarious and absurd situations. From mistaken identities to romantic entanglements, this play takes the reader on a whimsical journey filled with laughter and unexpected twists.

Genres: Comedy, Fantasy

Themes: Magic and its consequences, the power of wishes, the absurdity of human desires
