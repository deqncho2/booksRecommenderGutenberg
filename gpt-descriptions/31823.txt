Synopsis: In 'The Orpheus C. Kerr Papers, Series 3', the irreverent and satirical Orpheus C. Kerr returns with his witty observations and humorous anecdotes, taking readers on a hilarious journey through the absurdities of American society during the Civil War. From political shenanigans to social conventions, Kerr's sharp wit and biting commentary provide a unique perspective on the tumultuous times. As he navigates through the chaos, Kerr's misadventures and encounters with eccentric characters will keep readers entertained and laughing throughout.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Historical Fiction

Themes: Social commentary, Political satire, Absurdity of war
