Synopsis: In 'King Arthur in Cornwall', author W. Howship Dickinson takes readers on a captivating journey into the mystical world of King Arthur and his legendary knights. Set in the enchanting landscapes of Cornwall, this tale weaves together elements of history, mythology, and romance, as Arthur and his loyal companions face treacherous challenges and embark on a quest that will test their courage and honor. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are forged, and the fate of Camelot hangs in the balance. With vivid descriptions and a gripping narrative, 'King Arthur in Cornwall' offers a fresh and captivating take on the timeless legend of King Arthur.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Chivalry, Loyalty, Destiny, Betrayal, Quest
