Synopsis: 
In 'The Infant System: For Developing the Intellectual and Moral Powers of all Children, from One to Seven years of Age', Samuel Wilderspin takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of early childhood education. Through his innovative approach, Wilderspin introduces a revolutionary system that nurtures the intellectual and moral growth of children between the ages of one and seven. With a blend of practical advice, engaging anecdotes, and insightful observations, this book offers a fresh perspective on the importance of early education and the potential it holds for shaping young minds. 

Genres: Education, Non-fiction

Themes: Early childhood education, Moral development, Intellectual growth
