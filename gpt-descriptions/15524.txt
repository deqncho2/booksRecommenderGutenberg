Synopsis:
In the small town of Willow Creek, Digger Smith is known as the eccentric local handyman with a mysterious past. When a series of strange occurrences begin to plague the town, Digger finds himself at the center of it all. As he delves deeper into the secrets of Willow Creek, he uncovers a hidden world filled with ancient artifacts, supernatural forces, and a long-forgotten prophecy. With the help of a curious young girl named Lily, Digger must unravel the truth and confront the darkness that threatens to consume their town.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of friendship, the battle between light and darkness
