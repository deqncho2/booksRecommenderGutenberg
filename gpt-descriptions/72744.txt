Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Miss Cheyne of Essilmont', the captivating story continues as Miss Cheyne finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances. As she navigates the treacherous waters of society, Miss Cheyne must confront her own desires and make choices that will shape her future. With each twist and turn, the stakes grow higher, and the consequences become more dire. Will Miss Cheyne find the strength to overcome the challenges that lie ahead, or will she be forever trapped in a world of deceit and heartbreak?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Desire, Betrayal, Society and Class
