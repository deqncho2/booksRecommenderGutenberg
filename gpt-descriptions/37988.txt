Synopsis: In 'The Ladies' Guide to True Politeness and Perfect Manners: or, Miss Leslie's Behaviour Book', Eliza Leslie presents a comprehensive guide to etiquette and manners for women. Through a series of practical advice and anecdotes, Leslie offers invaluable tips on how to navigate social situations, present oneself with grace and charm, and cultivate genuine politeness. From proper introductions to appropriate conversation topics, from table manners to dress codes, this book serves as an essential manual for any woman aspiring to be a true lady. With wit and wisdom, Leslie's guidance is sure to captivate readers and inspire them to refine their behavior and embrace the art of politeness.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Etiquette

Themes: Etiquette, Manners, Social behavior, Self-improvement
