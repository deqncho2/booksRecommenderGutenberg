Synopsis: 
In 'Bees' by Phillips, Everett Franklin, a small town is plagued by a mysterious phenomenon that threatens the very fabric of their existence. As the townspeople grapple with the unexplained disappearance of bees, they are forced to confront their own fears, secrets, and desires. Amidst the chaos, a young woman named Lily embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the vanishing bees, leading her to unexpected revelations and a deeper understanding of the interconnectedness of all living beings.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Environmental Fiction

Themes: Nature's interconnectedness, Human impact on the environment, Secrets and revelations
