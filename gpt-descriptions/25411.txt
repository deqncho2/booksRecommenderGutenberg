Synopsis: In 'The Seven Wives of Bluebeard: 1920' by Anatole France, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as Bluebeard, a wealthy and enigmatic nobleman, takes on a series of wives, each meeting a tragic fate. As the story progresses, secrets are revealed, and the reader is drawn into a world of suspense, romance, and dark intrigue. With its intricate plot and richly developed characters, this gripping novel keeps readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Gothic Literature

Themes: Love and obsession, Power and control, Secrets and deception
