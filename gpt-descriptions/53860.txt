Synopsis: 

'Country Life in the Poetry of John Clare' by Coen, Mildred M. explores the enchanting world of John Clare's poetry, delving into the intricate tapestry of rural life that he weaves through his verses. Through an in-depth analysis of Clare's works, the book uncovers the profound connection between the poet and the natural world, capturing the essence of the countryside and its inhabitants in vivid detail. From the bustling fields to the serene meadows, Clare's poetry transports readers to a simpler time, where the beauty of nature intertwines with the joys and struggles of rural existence. 

Genres: Literary criticism, Poetry analysis, Biography

Themes: Nature, Rural life, Identity, Nostalgia
