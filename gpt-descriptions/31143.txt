Synopsis: In 'Talkers: With Illustrations' by John Bate, a mysterious group of individuals with extraordinary abilities emerges, captivating the world with their mesmerizing powers of persuasion. As their influence grows, society becomes divided between those who idolize and follow the Talkers, and those who fear their manipulative abilities. Amidst this turmoil, a young woman named Emma finds herself drawn into their world, uncovering dark secrets and discovering her own hidden potential. With stunning illustrations that bring the story to life, 'Talkers' is a thrilling and thought-provoking exploration of power, deception, and the blurred lines between truth and manipulation.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Power, Manipulation, Identity
