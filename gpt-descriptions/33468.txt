Synopsis: 
In 'Roland Cashel, Volume I (of II)' by Charles James Lever, we are introduced to the charismatic and enigmatic Roland Cashel, a young man of humble origins who finds himself thrust into the world of the Irish aristocracy. As Roland navigates the treacherous waters of high society, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and forbidden love. With his quick wit and resourcefulness, Roland must learn to navigate the complex social hierarchy while staying true to himself. As the plot unfolds, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns, as Roland's true character is tested and his destiny is revealed.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: 
Social class, Identity, Love and betrayal
