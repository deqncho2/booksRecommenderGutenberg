Synopsis: In 'The Duke Of Chimney Butte' by George W. Ogden, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Chimney Butte, captivating the locals with his enigmatic charm and extraordinary skills. As the townspeople become entangled in his web of secrets and hidden agendas, they soon discover that the Duke is not who he appears to be. With danger lurking around every corner, the residents of Chimney Butte must navigate a treacherous path of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. Will they uncover the truth about the Duke before it's too late?

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
