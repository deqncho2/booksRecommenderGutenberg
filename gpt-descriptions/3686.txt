Synopsis: In "The Army of the Cumberland" by Henry Martyn Cist, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the American Civil War, specifically focusing on the experiences and triumphs of the Army of the Cumberland. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Cist brings to life the struggles, sacrifices, and heroic acts of the soldiers who fought in this pivotal campaign. From the intense battles to the camaraderie forged in the face of adversity, this book offers a gripping account of the Army of the Cumberland's pivotal role in shaping the outcome of the war.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Leadership, Brotherhood, Resilience
