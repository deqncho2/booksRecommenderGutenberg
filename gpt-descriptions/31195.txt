Synopsis: 

'English Caricaturists and Graphic Humourists of the Nineteenth Century: How they Illustrated and Interpreted their Times' takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of English caricature and graphic humor during the 19th century. From the witty and satirical illustrations of renowned artists to the social and political commentary embedded within their works, this book explores the fascinating ways in which these artists captured and interpreted the spirit of their times. 

Genres: Art History, Cultural Studies, Biography

Themes: Satire, Social Commentary, Political Critique, Historical Representation
