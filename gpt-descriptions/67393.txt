Synopsis: 

In 'The Fourth Battalion, The King's Own (Royal Lancaster Regiment) and the Great War', authors Crossley and Wadham delve into the gripping and untold story of a remarkable battalion during World War I. Through meticulous research and firsthand accounts, they bring to life the experiences of these brave soldiers as they navigate the horrors and triumphs of the Great War. From the trenches of the Western Front to the battlefields of Gallipoli, this captivating narrative sheds light on the sacrifices, camaraderie, and resilience of the Fourth Battalion, offering a unique perspective on a pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, War memoir

Themes: Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Resilience, War experiences
