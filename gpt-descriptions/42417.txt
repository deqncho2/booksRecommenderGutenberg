Synopsis: In 'The Air Patrol: A Story of the North-west Frontier' by Herbert Strang, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure set in the rugged and treacherous terrain of the North-west Frontier. The story follows a group of courageous aviators who form an air patrol to protect the region from hostile forces. As they navigate through dangerous encounters, face unexpected challenges, and uncover hidden secrets, the fate of the frontier hangs in the balance. With its gripping narrative and vivid descriptions, this book promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the outcome of this high-stakes mission.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Exploration, Conflict
