Synopsis: In 'Death of a B.E.M.' by Livingston, Berkeley, a renowned scientist is found dead under mysterious circumstances, leaving the scientific community baffled. As the investigation unfolds, it becomes clear that there is more to this death than meets the eye. With a clever blend of science fiction and suspense, the story takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As the truth slowly unravels, the protagonist must confront their own beliefs and the ethical implications of their work, leading to a thought-provoking climax.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Ethics, Scientific Responsibility, Identity
