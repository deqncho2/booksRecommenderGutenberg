Synopsis: In 'The Daisy Chain, or Aspirations' by Charlotte M. Yonge, the lives of the May family are forever changed when they welcome a new governess into their home. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, friendships are tested, and unexpected events challenge the characters' aspirations. With its intricate web of relationships and captivating plot twists, this novel explores the complexities of family dynamics and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Domestic drama

Themes: Family, Friendship, Ambition, Secrets, Identity
