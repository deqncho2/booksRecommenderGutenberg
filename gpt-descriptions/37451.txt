Synopsis: 
In the small town of Dunport, Vermont, a young woman named Mary is determined to break free from the constraints of her traditional upbringing. With a fiery spirit and a thirst for adventure, Mary sets out on a journey that takes her from the rugged mountains of Vermont to the bustling streets of New York City. Along the way, she encounters a cast of colorful characters who challenge her beliefs and push her to confront her own desires and ambitions. As Mary navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and self-discovery, she must ultimately decide what truly matters to her and what she is willing to sacrifice to live life on her own terms.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Independence, Self-discovery, Gender roles, Social expectations
