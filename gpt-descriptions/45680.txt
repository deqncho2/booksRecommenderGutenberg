Synopsis: 
In "Flying the Coast Skyways; Or, Jack Ralston's Swift Patrol," Jack Ralston finds himself embarking on a thrilling adventure as he joins a swift patrol along the coast skyways. With danger lurking at every turn, Jack must navigate treacherous skies, encounter mysterious characters, and uncover a hidden secret that could change everything. Will Jack's courage and determination be enough to overcome the challenges he faces?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Aviation

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Friendship, Secrets
