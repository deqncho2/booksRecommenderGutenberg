Synopsis: In 'A World Apart' by Sam Merwin, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Jonathan Grant embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that unexpectedly transports him to a parallel universe. Struggling to navigate this strange new world, he encounters a society ruled by a powerful and enigmatic leader, where technology and magic coexist in ways he could never have imagined. As Dr. Grant searches for a way back home, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, dangerous alliances, and a mysterious prophecy that could change both worlds forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Parallel universes, Power and control, Identity and self-discovery
