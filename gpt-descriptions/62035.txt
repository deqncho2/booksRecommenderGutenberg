Synopsis: 
In 'A Planet for Your Thoughts' by Norman James, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything we know about the universe. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between reality and imagination blurs. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous journey to uncover the truth and protect his groundbreaking findings.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the power of knowledge, the fragility of reality
