Synopsis:
In 'Alter Ego: A Tale' by Walker, W. W., a seemingly ordinary man named John discovers a hidden power within himself that allows him to transform into a completely different person. As he delves deeper into his newfound ability, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and self-discovery. With each transformation, John uncovers shocking truths about his own identity and the world around him, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-exploration and redemption.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes:
Identity, Power, Redemption
