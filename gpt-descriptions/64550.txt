Synopsis: 

In 'Intelligence in Plants and Animals: Being a New Edition of the Author's Privately Issued "Soul and Immortality,"' Thomas G. Gentry delves into the fascinating world of intelligence beyond the human realm. Through a captivating exploration of scientific research and personal anecdotes, Gentry uncovers the remarkable cognitive abilities of plants and animals that challenge our traditional understanding of intelligence. From the intricate communication systems of plants to the problem-solving skills of animals, this thought-provoking book invites readers to reconsider the boundaries of consciousness and the interconnectedness of all living beings.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Intelligence, Consciousness, Interconnectedness
