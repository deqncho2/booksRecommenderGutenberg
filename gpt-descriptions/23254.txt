Synopsis: In "The Cobbler In The Devil's Kitchen," a small island community on Lake Michigan is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances. As the townspeople grow increasingly fearful, a cobbler with a reputation for being able to fix anything finds himself at the center of the mystery. With his unique skills and a touch of magic, he sets out to uncover the truth behind the strange occurrences, leading him on a thrilling and unexpected adventure.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Supernatural, Community, Courage
