Synopsis: 
In 'Manners and Rules of Good Society; or, Solecisms to be Avoided' by Anonymous, readers are transported to the refined world of high society, where every social interaction is governed by a complex set of rules and expectations. Through a series of witty anecdotes and practical advice, the author explores the delicate art of navigating social situations with grace and tact, while also shedding light on the consequences of committing social faux pas. From the intricacies of formal introductions to the proper way of accepting invitations, this guide offers invaluable insights into the world of etiquette, ensuring that readers will never be caught off guard in polite society.

Genres: Non-fiction, Etiquette, Self-help

Themes: Etiquette, Social norms, Politeness, Social class
