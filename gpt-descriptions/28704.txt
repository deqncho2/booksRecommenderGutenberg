Synopsis: In 'Free Ships: The Restoration of the American Carrying Trade', Codman, John takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous world of American shipping. Set against the backdrop of economic challenges and political tensions, this gripping narrative explores the efforts of a group of determined individuals who strive to revive the once-thriving American carrying trade. As they navigate treacherous waters, face fierce competition, and confront powerful adversaries, their quest for success becomes a thrilling tale of resilience, innovation, and the indomitable spirit of the American people.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Business, Adventure

Themes: Economic revival, Entrepreneurship, American identity
