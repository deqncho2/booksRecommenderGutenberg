Synopsis: 

In 'Farm Engines and How to Run Them: The Young Engineer's Guide', James H. Stephenson takes readers on an exciting journey into the world of farm engines and the art of operating them. This comprehensive guide is filled with practical advice, step-by-step instructions, and fascinating anecdotes that will captivate both young engineers and seasoned farmers alike. From understanding the inner workings of various engines to troubleshooting common issues, Stephenson's expertise shines through as he shares his wealth of knowledge in a clear and engaging manner. Whether you're a curious beginner or a seasoned professional, this book is sure to ignite your passion for farm engines and equip you with the skills needed to master them.

Genres: Non-fiction, Engineering, Agriculture

Themes: Farming, Mechanics, Education
