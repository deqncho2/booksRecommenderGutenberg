Synopsis: In the heart of San Francisco, a series of spine-chilling seances unfold, revealing a world beyond the living. As a group of curious individuals gather to communicate with the dead, they are thrust into a realm of inexplicable phenomena and eerie encounters. With each session, the boundaries between the living and the dead blur, leaving the participants questioning their own beliefs and sanity. As the seances become increasingly intense, the shocking truth behind these supernatural occurrences is unveiled, leaving readers breathless and questioning the nature of life and death.

Genres: Paranormal, Mystery, Non-fiction

Themes: Life after death, Supernatural phenomena, Belief and skepticism
