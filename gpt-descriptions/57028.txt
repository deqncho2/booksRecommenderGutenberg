Synopsis: In 'A Dominie's Log' by Alexander Sutherland Neill, embark on a captivating journey through the experiences of a schoolmaster in a remote Scottish village. Through his log entries, we witness the challenges, triumphs, and unexpected events that shape the lives of both the students and the teacher. From humorous anecdotes to heartwarming moments, this log offers a unique glimpse into the world of education and the power of human connection.

Genres: Autobiography, Education, Memoir

Themes: Education reform, Teacher-student relationships, Community dynamics
