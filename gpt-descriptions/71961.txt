Synopsis: 
'Journal of a second expedition into the interior of Africa, from the Bight of Benin to Soccatoo' follows the remarkable journey of Hugh Clapperton as he ventures into the heart of Africa. Facing treacherous landscapes, encounters with indigenous tribes, and the constant threat of danger, Clapperton's expedition takes readers on a thrilling and eye-opening adventure. Alongside Clapperton's journal, the book also includes the journal of Richard Lander, who explores a different route from Kano to the sea-coast. Together, these accounts provide a captivating exploration of the uncharted territories of Africa.

Genres: 
Travelogue, Adventure, Historical

Themes: 
Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Survival
