Synopsis: 
In 'Myth, Ritual And Religion, Vol. 2 (of 2)', Andrew Lang delves into the fascinating world of ancient myths, rituals, and religious practices from various cultures around the globe. From the enchanting tales of gods and heroes to the intricate ceremonies and beliefs that shaped civilizations, Lang uncovers the hidden meanings and universal truths behind these ancient traditions. With his insightful analysis and captivating storytelling, he invites readers on a journey through time and across cultures, unraveling the mysteries of human spirituality and the enduring power of myth and ritual.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Mythology, Anthropology

Themes: 
Mythology, Religion, Ritual, Cultural Studies
