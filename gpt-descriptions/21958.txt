Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's Races' by Burt L. Standish, readers are taken on an exhilarating journey alongside the charismatic and talented athlete, Frank Merriwell. As Frank faces a series of intense races, he must overcome various obstacles and rivals who will stop at nothing to claim victory. With his unwavering determination and remarkable skills, Frank captivates the attention of both friends and foes, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly anticipating the outcome of each race.

Genres: Sports, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Competition
