Synopsis: 

In 'The Geology of D.A.R. State Park, Mt. Philo State Forest Park, Sand Bar State Park', Harry W. Dodge takes readers on an awe-inspiring journey through three remarkable parks, unveiling the hidden secrets of their geological wonders. From the ancient rock formations to the breathtaking landscapes, Dodge's meticulous exploration of these parks will leave readers captivated and eager to discover the geological marvels that lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, Geology

Themes: Natural beauty, Environmental conservation, Geological history
