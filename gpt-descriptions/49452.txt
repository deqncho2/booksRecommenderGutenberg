Synopsis: In 'The Mentor: Benjamin Franklin', delve into the fascinating life of one of America's most influential Founding Fathers. This captivating volume takes readers on a journey through Benjamin Franklin's remarkable achievements as a scientist, inventor, writer, and diplomat. Discover the secrets behind his famous experiments with electricity, his groundbreaking inventions, and his pivotal role in shaping the United States. Uncover the lesser-known aspects of Franklin's life, including his love for literature and his tireless efforts to improve society. Through captivating storytelling and vivid illustrations, this Mentor issue offers a unique glimpse into the extraordinary life of Benjamin Franklin.

Genres: Biography, History, Education

Themes: American Revolution, Enlightenment, Scientific Inquiry, Intellectual Pursuits
