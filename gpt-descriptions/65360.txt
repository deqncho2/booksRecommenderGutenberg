Synopsis: 
In 'Charlie and His Puppy Bingo', young Charlie embarks on a heartwarming adventure when he discovers a stray puppy in his backyard. As Charlie and Bingo form an unbreakable bond, they navigate through various challenges and learn valuable life lessons along the way. With their unwavering friendship, they embark on a journey of self-discovery, teaching each other the true meaning of love, loyalty, and resilience.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Love, Loyalty, Resilience
