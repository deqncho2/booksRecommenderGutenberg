Synopsis: In "Life of Charles Darwin," author G. T. Bettany takes readers on a captivating journey through the extraordinary life of one of history's most influential scientists. From his adventurous voyage on the HMS Beagle to his groundbreaking theory of evolution, this biography delves into the personal and professional struggles that shaped Darwin's revolutionary ideas. With vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, Bettany paints a vivid portrait of a man driven by curiosity and a relentless pursuit of truth, ultimately changing our understanding of the natural world forever.

Genres: Biography, Science, History

Themes: Evolution, Scientific Inquiry, Personal Growth
