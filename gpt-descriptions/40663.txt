Synopsis: 
In 'Two on the Trail: A Story of Canada Snows', Edith Elise Cowper takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the breathtaking Canadian wilderness. The story follows two adventurous souls, who find themselves embarking on a perilous expedition across the snowy landscapes of Canada. As they face numerous challenges and encounter unexpected dangers, their bond is tested, and they must rely on their courage and resourcefulness to survive. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding moments, this captivating tale will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead for our intrepid protagonists.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Survival

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Resilience
