Synopsis: 

Embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting city of Charleston as Thomas Petigru Lesesne takes you on an incomparable stroll, revealing the hidden gems and fascinating history behind its iconic landmarks. From the majestic architecture of the historic district to the hauntingly beautiful plantations, Lesesne's vivid descriptions transport you to a bygone era, where tales of love, tragedy, and triumph intertwine with the city's rich heritage. As you wander through the cobblestone streets and immerse yourself in the vibrant culture, you'll uncover the secrets that make Charleston a truly unforgettable destination.

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: History and heritage, Architecture and landmarks, Cultural exploration
