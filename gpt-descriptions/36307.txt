Synopsis: In the heart of a poverty-stricken neighborhood, a diverse group of individuals struggle to survive amidst the harsh realities of life. As their paths intertwine, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of humanity is laid bare. Set against the backdrop of a bustling ghetto, this gripping drama explores the depths of human resilience, the power of compassion, and the lengths people will go to protect their loved ones.

Genres: Drama, Social Realism, Tragedy

Themes: Poverty, Survival, Community, Sacrifice
