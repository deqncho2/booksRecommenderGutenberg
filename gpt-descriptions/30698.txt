Synopsis: 

In 'Aces Up' by Clarke Covington, readers are introduced to a group of college students who are not only brilliant academically, but also possess an uncanny ability to outsmart their opponents in a high-stakes poker game. As they navigate the world of underground gambling, the stakes get higher and the risks become more dangerous. With their futures on the line, the group must rely on their wit, strategy, and a few unexpected alliances to come out on top.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Risk-taking, Deception, Ambition
