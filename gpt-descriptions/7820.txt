Synopsis: In 'Monitress Merle' by Angela Brazil, a young girl named Merle embarks on a thrilling adventure when she becomes a monitress at her boarding school. As she navigates the challenges of her new role, Merle discovers hidden secrets, forms unexpected friendships, and uncovers a mystery that could change everything. With courage and determination, she must unravel the truth and confront the consequences that come with it.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Coming of Age, Secrets and Discovery
