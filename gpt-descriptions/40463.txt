Synopsis: In "The Little Colonel's Holidays," the lovable and spirited Little Colonel embarks on a series of delightful adventures during her school break. From exploring a mysterious old house to organizing a festive Christmas pageant, the Little Colonel's holidays are filled with laughter, friendship, and heartwarming moments. As she navigates through new experiences and encounters unexpected challenges, the Little Colonel's indomitable spirit shines through, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to see what she will do next.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Adventure, Resilience
