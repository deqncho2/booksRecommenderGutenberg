Synopsis: In 'How Jack Mackenzie won his epaulettes' by Stables, Gordon, follow the thrilling journey of young Jack Mackenzie as he sets out to prove his worth and earn his epaulettes. Faced with numerous challenges and unexpected twists, Jack's determination and courage are put to the test. Will he succeed in his quest and prove himself worthy of the honor he seeks?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Perseverance, Bravery, Self-discovery
