Synopsis: In 'Sculpture in Spain', Calvert takes us on a captivating journey through the rich history and diverse styles of Spanish sculpture. From the ancient Roman influences to the intricate details of Gothic masterpieces, and the bold expressions of the modernist movement, this book unveils the hidden stories behind some of Spain's most iconic sculptures. With vivid descriptions and stunning visuals, Calvert brings to life the passion, creativity, and cultural significance of these remarkable works of art.

Genres: Art History, Non-fiction

Themes: Spanish culture, Artistic expression, Historical influences
