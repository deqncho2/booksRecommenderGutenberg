Synopsis: In "The Tale of the Flopsy Bunnies," Beatrix Potter takes readers on a delightful adventure with a mischievous group of bunnies. As they venture into Mr. McGregor's garden, they encounter unexpected challenges that test their cleverness and resourcefulness. With charming illustrations and a touch of suspense, this tale will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Mischief, Resourcefulness, Nature, Consequences
