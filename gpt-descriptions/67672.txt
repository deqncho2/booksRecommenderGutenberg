Synopsis: In "The Wright Brothers" by Fred C. Kelly, embark on a captivating journey into the lives of two ordinary men who dared to dream of flight. This gripping biography unveils the remarkable story of Orville and Wilbur Wright, brothers who defied the odds and revolutionized the world of aviation. From their humble beginnings in Ohio to their groundbreaking achievements in Kitty Hawk, North Carolina, witness the relentless determination, ingenious inventions, and unwavering passion that propelled these visionary brothers to conquer the skies. Discover the challenges they faced, the sacrifices they made, and the legacy they left behind, forever changing the course of history.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Innovation, Perseverance, Legacy
