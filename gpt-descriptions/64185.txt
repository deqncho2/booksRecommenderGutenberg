Synopsis: In 'A Body of Divinity, Vol. 3', Thomas Ridgley delves into the doctrines of the Christian religion, providing a comprehensive explanation and defense of these beliefs. Drawing from a series of lectures on the Assembly's Larger Catechism, Ridgley explores the fundamental principles of Christianity, shedding light on its core teachings and addressing common misconceptions. With a focus on clarity and accessibility, this volume offers readers a deeper understanding of the Christian faith and its significance in their lives.

Genres: Theology, Religious Studies, Non-fiction

Themes: Christian doctrine, Apologetics, Religious education
