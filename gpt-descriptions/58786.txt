Synopsis: In "The Queen's Reign and Its Commemoration," Walter Besant takes readers on a captivating journey through the Victorian era, exploring the literary and pictorial aspects that defined this transformative period. From the grandeur of Queen Victoria's reign to the cultural shifts and societal changes that occurred, Besant offers a rich and vivid review of this remarkable time. Through a combination of insightful analysis and stunning visuals, this book provides a comprehensive understanding of the Victorian era and its lasting impact.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Art history, Literary criticism

Themes: Victorian era, Queen Victoria, Cultural transformation, Literature and art, Societal changes
