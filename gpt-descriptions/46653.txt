Synopsis: In 'The Slave of the Mine; or, Jack Harkaway in 'Frisco' by Hemyng, Bracebridge, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as young Jack Harkaway finds himself caught up in the dangerous world of the San Francisco gold mines. With treacherous villains, daring escapes, and unexpected alliances, Jack must navigate the treacherous landscape of the mines to uncover a hidden secret that could change everything. Will he be able to outsmart his enemies and find his way back to safety?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Survival, Friendship, Betrayal
