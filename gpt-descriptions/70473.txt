Synopsis: 
Forrest House: $b A novel takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious and enchanting Forrest House. Set in a small town, the story follows a young woman named Emily who stumbles upon an old, abandoned mansion that holds secrets from the past. As she delves deeper into the house's history, Emily becomes entangled in a web of family secrets, hidden treasures, and unexpected connections. With each discovery, the line between reality and fantasy blurs, leaving Emily questioning her own sanity. As she unravels the mysteries of Forrest House, Emily must confront her own fears and desires, ultimately leading her to a life-altering decision.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Perception of reality, Transformation
