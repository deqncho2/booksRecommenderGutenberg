Synopsis: In "The Old Showmen and the Old London Fairs" by Thomas Frost, step back in time to the vibrant and bustling world of the old London fairs. Follow the lives of the showmen, the entertainers, and the visitors as they navigate the colorful chaos of these lively events. From the thrill of the rides to the wonders of the sideshows, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a bygone era of entertainment and spectacle.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, cultural history, social commentary

Themes: Nostalgia, entertainment industry, urban life, societal change
