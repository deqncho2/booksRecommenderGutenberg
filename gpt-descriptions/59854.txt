Synopsis: In 'The Cask' by Freeman Wills Crofts, a seemingly ordinary train journey takes a sinister turn when a passenger mysteriously disappears. As Inspector French investigates, he uncovers a web of secrets and deception, leading him to question everyone on board. With time running out, French must unravel the truth before the train reaches its final destination.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Investigation, Suspense
