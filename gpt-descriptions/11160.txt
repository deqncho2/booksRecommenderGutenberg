Synopsis: 
In 'A Cotswold Village; Or, Country Life and Pursuits in Gloucestershire', J. Arthur Gibbs takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes and charming villages of the Cotswolds. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, the author introduces us to the colorful characters and unique traditions that make this region so enchanting. From the bustling markets to the tranquil countryside, this delightful book offers a delightful glimpse into the idyllic country life of Gloucestershire.

Genres: Travel, Memoir, Non-fiction

Themes: Rural life, Community, Tradition
