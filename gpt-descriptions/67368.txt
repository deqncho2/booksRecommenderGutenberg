Synopsis: 
In 'Sam in the Suburbs', we follow the misadventures of Sam, a young man who finds himself unexpectedly thrust into the world of suburban living. As he navigates the peculiarities of his new neighborhood, Sam encounters a colorful cast of characters, from eccentric neighbors to conniving acquaintances. With his wit and charm, Sam must find a way to adapt to his surroundings and unravel the mysteries that lie beneath the seemingly idyllic surface of suburban life.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Social class, Conformity, Identity
