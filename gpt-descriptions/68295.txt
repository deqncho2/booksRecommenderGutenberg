Synopsis: In the final volume of the captivating 'Dulcie Carlyon' series, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the fate of our beloved protagonist hangs in the balance. As Dulcie navigates the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and redemption, she must confront her past and make difficult choices that will shape her future. With unexpected twists and heart-stopping revelations, this thrilling conclusion will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and sacrifice, redemption, the power of secrets
