Synopsis: In 'Cricket' by Lyttelton and Steel, a seemingly ordinary game of cricket takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious stranger arrives at the match. As the game progresses, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and the players find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger. With each ball bowled, the stakes become higher, and the outcome of the match becomes a matter of life and death. This gripping tale of suspense and deception will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final innings.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Sports

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Competition
