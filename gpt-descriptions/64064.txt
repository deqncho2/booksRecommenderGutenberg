Synopsis: In 'Blind Play' by Davis Chandler, a renowned pianist mysteriously loses his sight just days before a highly anticipated concert. As he struggles to come to terms with his sudden blindness, he embarks on a journey of self-discovery and finds solace in an unexpected friendship with a young girl who sees the world in a unique way. Together, they navigate the challenges of darkness and light, uncovering hidden truths and unlocking the power of music in the process.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Loss and resilience, Friendship, Perception and perspective
