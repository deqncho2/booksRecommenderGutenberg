Synopsis: In 'The Bible and Polygamy: Does the Bible Sanction Polygamy?', Newman, Pratt, Cannon, and Smith delve into the controversial topic of polygamy in the Bible. Through a comprehensive analysis of biblical texts, they explore the historical and cultural context surrounding polygamy, examining its prevalence and the various perspectives on its moral and religious implications. The authors present thought-provoking arguments and interpretations, challenging conventional beliefs and shedding light on the complex relationship between the Bible and polygamy.

Genres: Religious studies, Biblical interpretation, Theology

Themes: Polygamy, Marriage, Morality, Religious doctrine
