Synopsis: In 'Poisoned Air' by Meek, S. P. (Sterner St. Paul), a renowned scientist stumbles upon a shocking discovery that threatens the very fabric of society. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of conspiracy and deceit, where the line between truth and fiction becomes increasingly blurred. With time running out, he must race against the clock to expose the truth and save humanity from a deadly fate.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Scientific ethics, Government secrecy, Human survival
