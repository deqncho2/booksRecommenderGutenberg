Synopsis: In 'The Mating of Lydia' by Mrs. Humphry Ward, a young woman named Lydia finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires when she becomes entangled in a web of love, duty, and ambition. As she navigates the complexities of relationships and the constraints of her time, Lydia must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Duty, Social Expectations, Women's Agency
