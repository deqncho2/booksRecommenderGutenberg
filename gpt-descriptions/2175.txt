Synopsis: In "You Never Can Tell" by Bernard Shaw, a chance encounter between a family of independent and unconventional women and a mysterious stranger sets off a chain of events that challenges societal norms and explores the complexities of love, marriage, and the pursuit of happiness. As the characters navigate misunderstandings, mistaken identities, and unexpected revelations, they are forced to confront their own prejudices and reevaluate their beliefs. With Shaw's signature wit and sharp social commentary, this delightful comedy of manners takes readers on a humorous and thought-provoking journey through the intricacies of human relationships.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Satire

Themes: Love and romance, societal expectations, gender roles, class divisions, personal freedom
