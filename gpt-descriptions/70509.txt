Synopsis: 
"A caricature history of Canadian politics: $b events from the union of 1841, as illustrated by cartoons from 'Grip,' and various other sources" takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of Canadian politics. Through a collection of cartoons from the renowned publication "Grip" and other sources, author J. W. Bengough presents a unique and humorous perspective on the events that shaped Canadian history since the union of 1841. From political scandals to landmark decisions, this book offers a delightful and insightful exploration of the country's political landscape.
