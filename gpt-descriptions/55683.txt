Synopsis: In 'Go-Ahead; Or, The Fisher-Boy's Motto' by Castlemon, Harry, we follow the thrilling adventures of a young fisherman named Frank, who finds himself caught up in a dangerous mystery while out at sea. As he navigates treacherous waters and encounters unexpected allies and foes, Frank's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the suspenseful world of the fisher-boy, eagerly anticipating the outcome of his courageous journey.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Friendship, Perseverance
