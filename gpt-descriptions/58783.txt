Synopsis: 
"The Letters of S. Ambrose, Bishop of Milan" is a collection of personal letters written by Ambrose, the influential Bishop of Milan in the 4th century. These letters provide a fascinating glimpse into the life and thoughts of one of the most important figures in early Christianity. From addressing theological controversies to offering guidance on moral issues, Ambrose's letters showcase his wisdom, eloquence, and unwavering commitment to his faith. Through his correspondence with emperors, fellow bishops, and ordinary Christians, Ambrose navigates the complexities of his time, offering profound insights and inspiring words of encouragement. This collection of letters is a testament to the enduring impact of Ambrose's teachings and his dedication to the Church.

Genres: 
Epistolary, Religious, Historical

Themes: 
Faith, Morality, Leadership, Theology
