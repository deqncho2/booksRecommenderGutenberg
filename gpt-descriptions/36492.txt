Synopsis: In 'The Kidnapped President' by Boothby, Guy, a thrilling tale unfolds as the President of a powerful nation mysteriously disappears, leaving the entire country in chaos. As the authorities scramble to find him, a cunning and enigmatic criminal mastermind emerges, claiming responsibility for the abduction. With time running out and the fate of the nation hanging in the balance, a courageous and resourceful detective is tasked with unraveling the intricate web of clues and secrets to rescue the kidnapped President and bring the criminal to justice. Filled with unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Political intrigue, Crime and punishment
