Synopsis: In 'The Case of Richard Meynell' by Ward, Humphry, Mrs., a young and charismatic clergyman finds himself entangled in a complex web of love, duty, and religious beliefs. As he navigates the challenges of his position and the expectations of society, Richard Meynell must confront the consequences of his choices and the impact they have on those around him. With secrets, scandals, and moral dilemmas at every turn, this gripping tale explores the boundaries of faith, loyalty, and personal freedom.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and duty, Religion and morality, Social expectations
