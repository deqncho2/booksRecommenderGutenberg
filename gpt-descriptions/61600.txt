Synopsis: In 'The Castaways of the Flag: The Final Adventures of the Swiss Family Robinson' by Jules Verne, the Robinson family finds themselves once again stranded on a deserted island after a shipwreck. This time, however, they are not alone. Joined by a group of castaways from various backgrounds, they must work together to survive and find a way back home. As they face new challenges and encounter unexpected dangers, the Robinsons and their newfound companions embark on a thrilling and perilous adventure that will test their resilience and resourcefulness.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Survival

Themes: Family bonds, Resilience, Survival, Exploration
