Synopsis: 
In 'A Short History of a Long Travel from Babylon to Bethel', Crisp, Stephen takes readers on a captivating journey through time and space. The story follows a group of individuals who embark on a perilous expedition from the ancient city of Babylon to the sacred land of Bethel. As they traverse treacherous landscapes and encounter various obstacles, the travelers must confront their deepest fears and grapple with the complexities of faith, love, and redemption. With vivid descriptions and rich character development, Crisp, Stephen weaves a tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie ahead.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Spiritual

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Personal Growth
