Synopsis: In 'The Fasting Cure' by Sinclair, Upton, a young woman embarks on a radical journey to heal her body and mind through the power of fasting. As she delves deeper into this unconventional practice, she discovers unexpected challenges and transformative experiences that will forever change her perspective on health and well-being.

Genres: Fiction, Health and Wellness, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Self-discovery, Alternative Medicine, Personal Growth
