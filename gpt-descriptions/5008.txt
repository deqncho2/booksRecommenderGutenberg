Synopsis: In "Katherine's Sheaves," a young woman named Katherine finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is unexpectedly thrust into a world of wealth and privilege. As she navigates the complexities of high society, Katherine must confront her own ambitions and the sacrifices she is willing to make for love. With secrets, scandals, and a captivating cast of characters, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the intricacies of the human heart.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, social class and identity, personal growth and self-discovery
