Synopsis: In "A Traveler in Time" by August Derleth, a young girl named Susan finds herself mysteriously transported back in time to the 16th century, where she becomes embroiled in the dangerous world of religious conflict and political intrigue. As she navigates this unfamiliar era, Susan must grapple with her own identity and make difficult choices that could alter the course of history. With each passing day, she becomes more entangled in the lives of those around her, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical fiction, time travel, mystery

Themes: Identity, religious conflict, political intrigue
