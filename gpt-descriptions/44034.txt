Synopsis: 

'Juliette Drouet's Love-Letters to Victor Hugo: Edited with a Biography of Juliette Drouet' offers an intimate glimpse into the passionate and enduring love affair between Juliette Drouet and the renowned French writer, Victor Hugo. This collection of love letters, carefully edited and accompanied by a captivating biography of Juliette Drouet, unveils the depth of their emotional connection and the profound impact they had on each other's lives. Through their correspondence, we witness the intensity of their love, the challenges they faced, and the sacrifices they made for their relationship. 

Genres: Biography, Letters, Romance

Themes: Love, Passion, Artistic Inspiration
