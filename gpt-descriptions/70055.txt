Synopsis: In "Satan's Invisible World Discovered," George Sinclair presents a captivating collection of modern relations that provide undeniable evidence of the existence of devils, spirits, witches, and apparitions. Drawing from authentic records and the testimonies of credible witnesses, Sinclair challenges the atheists of his time by presenting a wealth of compelling evidence. Additionally, the book includes the astonishing history of Major Weir and his sister, as well as the tales of the witches of Bargarran, Pittenweem, Calder, and more. This work delves into the mysterious and supernatural, offering a fascinating exploration of the unseen world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Supernatural

Themes: Existence of the supernatural, Historical accounts, Witchcraft, Demonology
