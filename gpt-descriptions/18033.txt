Synopsis: 

In "The Boston Terrier and All About It: A Practical, Scientific, and Up to Date Guide to the Breeding of the American Dog," author Edward Axtell takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of Boston Terriers. This comprehensive guide provides a wealth of knowledge on the breeding, care, and characteristics of these beloved American dogs. From their origins and history to their unique physical traits and temperament, Axtell delves into every aspect of the Boston Terrier breed. With practical advice and scientific insights, this book is a must-read for both experienced breeders and dog enthusiasts alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Care, Breed Guide

Themes: Dog breeding, Canine genetics, Breed characteristics, Animal care
