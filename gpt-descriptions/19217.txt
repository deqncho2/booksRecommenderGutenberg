Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The History of England in Three Volumes', Tobias Smollett takes readers on a captivating journey through the reigns of William and Mary to the death of George II. From political intrigue and royal scandals to military conquests and cultural shifts, this volume delves into the fascinating events and personalities that shaped England during this period. With his signature wit and meticulous research, Smollett brings history to life, offering a rich and engaging narrative that will leave readers eager to uncover the next chapter of England's captivating past.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Monarchy, Politics, War, Society and culture
