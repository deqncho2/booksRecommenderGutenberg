Synopsis: 

In "Additional mounds of Duval and of Clay counties, Florida; Mound investigation on the east coast of Florida; Certain Florida coast mounds north of the St. Johns river," Clarence B. Moore takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious mounds of Florida's Duval and Clay counties. As he explores the east coast of Florida, Moore uncovers a wealth of fascinating information about the ancient civilizations that once inhabited these lands. From the enigmatic purpose of the mounds to the artifacts and relics discovered within them, this book offers a riveting account of Moore's archaeological investigations. 

Genres: Archaeology, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Archaeological exploration, Cultural heritage
