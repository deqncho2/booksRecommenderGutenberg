Synopsis: In 'Christmas in Sweden; or, A festival of light' by Sarah Gertrude Pomeroy, readers are transported to the enchanting world of Sweden during the holiday season. Follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Ingrid as she discovers the true meaning of Christmas through heartwarming traditions, magical encounters, and unexpected friendships. With the backdrop of snowy landscapes and the glow of candlelit celebrations, this delightful tale will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to experience the joy and wonder of Christmas in Sweden.

Genres: Children's Literature, Holiday Fiction, Cultural Fiction

Themes: Family, Tradition, Friendship, Cultural Identity, the Magic of Christmas
