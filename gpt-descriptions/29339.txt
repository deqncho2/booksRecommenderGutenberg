Synopsis: In 'The Practice of Autosuggestion', Brooks, C. Harry takes readers on a captivating journey into the power of the mind and its ability to shape our reality. Through a series of thought-provoking anecdotes and practical exercises, the author explores the concept of autosuggestion and its potential to transform our lives. As readers delve into the pages, they will discover the remarkable influence of self-suggestion on our thoughts, actions, and overall well-being. With a blend of scientific research and personal experiences, this book offers a unique perspective on harnessing the untapped potential of our subconscious minds.

Genres: Self-help, Psychology, Personal Development

Themes: Mind power, Self-improvement, Positive thinking, Subconscious mind
