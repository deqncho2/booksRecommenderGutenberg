Synopsis: 

In "With Grant at Fort Donelson, Shiloh and Vicksburg: and an appreciation of General U. S. Grant," Wilbur F. Crummer takes readers on a captivating journey through the American Civil War, specifically focusing on the pivotal battles at Fort Donelson, Shiloh, and Vicksburg. Through vivid and detailed accounts, Crummer provides a firsthand perspective of the bravery, strategy, and leadership of General Ulysses S. Grant, offering a unique appreciation for his role in shaping the outcome of the war. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be immersed in the intensity of the battles, the challenges faced by Grant and his troops, and the profound impact these events had on the course of history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Leadership, Bravery, Strategy, American Civil War, Historical significance
