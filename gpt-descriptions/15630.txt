Synopsis: Polly Oliver's Problem follows the captivating journey of a young girl named Polly who finds herself facing a unique predicament. Filled with determination and courage, Polly embarks on a daring adventure that challenges societal norms and tests her own identity. As she navigates through a world of secrets and unexpected discoveries, Polly must confront the complexities of love, loyalty, and self-acceptance. This heartwarming tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover Polly's ultimate resolution.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Gender identity, Self-discovery, Social expectations
