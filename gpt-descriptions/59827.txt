Synopsis: 
In 'My Life and Loves, Vol. 1 (of 4)', Harris, Frank takes us on a captivating journey through his scandalous and adventurous life. From his humble beginnings in rural England to his encounters with fascinating characters in the bohemian circles of Paris, Harris fearlessly explores the depths of his own desires and the complexities of human relationships. With vivid and explicit detail, he delves into the realms of love, passion, and the pursuit of pleasure, leaving readers both enthralled and scandalized by his audacious tales.

Genres: Autobiography, Erotic literature, Memoir

Themes: Love, Sexuality, Identity, Society, Artistic expression
