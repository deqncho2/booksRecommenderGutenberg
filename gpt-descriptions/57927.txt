Synopsis: In 'The Boy and His Gang' by Puffer, Joseph Adams, a young boy named Max finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a mysterious gang in his small town. As Max becomes entangled with the gang's activities, he discovers secrets that will challenge his loyalty and force him to question his own identity. With danger lurking around every corner, Max must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he cares about. This gripping tale of friendship, betrayal, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Identity
