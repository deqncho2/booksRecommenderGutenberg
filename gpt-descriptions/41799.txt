Synopsis: 
"The Old Pike: A History of the National Road, with Incidents, Accidents, and Anecdotes Thereon" takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating history of the National Road. From its humble beginnings as a dirt path to its transformation into a vital artery connecting the East and West, this book delves into the countless stories, both tragic and triumphant, that unfolded along its route. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, the author paints a vivid picture of the people, events, and challenges that shaped this iconic American roadway.

Genres: History, Non-fiction, Travel

Themes: American transportation history, Manifest Destiny, cultural and economic impact of infrastructure development
