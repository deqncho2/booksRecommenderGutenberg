Synopsis: 
In 'On the Construction of a Silvered Glass Telescope: Fifteen and a half inches in aperture, and its use in celestial photography', Draper takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of astronomy and the groundbreaking technology of telescopes. Through meticulous research and experimentation, he unveils the secrets behind constructing a remarkable silvered glass telescope with an impressive aperture. As he delves into the intricacies of celestial photography, Draper's work opens up new possibilities for capturing the wonders of the universe. With vivid descriptions and insightful observations, this book promises to ignite the reader's curiosity and deepen their understanding of the cosmos.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Astronomy, Technological Advancements, Photography
