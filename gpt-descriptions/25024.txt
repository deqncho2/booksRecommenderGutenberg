Synopsis: In 'The Night of the Long Knives' by Leiber, Fritz, a group of friends find themselves trapped in a mysterious and dangerous game that blurs the lines between reality and fantasy. As they navigate through a series of increasingly bizarre and terrifying challenges, they must unravel the secrets of the game and confront their own inner demons. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, wondering who will survive and what lies at the heart of this twisted game.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Horror, Mystery

Themes: Reality vs. fantasy, Identity, Survival
