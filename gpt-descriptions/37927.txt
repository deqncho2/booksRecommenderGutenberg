Synopsis: In 'The Boy Volunteers with the French Airmen' by Kenneth Ward, young Tom embarks on a thrilling adventure when he joins the French Airmen during World War I. As he navigates the dangers of the war-torn skies, Tom forms unbreakable bonds with his fellow volunteers and learns the true meaning of courage and sacrifice. With heart-pounding aerial battles and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will transport readers to the exhilarating world of early aviation and the bravery of those who took to the skies.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Sacrifice
