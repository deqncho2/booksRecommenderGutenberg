Synopsis: In 'The Ward of King Canute: A Romance of the Danish Conquest', we are transported to the tumultuous era of the Danish conquest of England. The story follows the life of Elfrida, a young Saxon noblewoman who finds herself caught in the midst of political intrigue and forbidden love. As she becomes the ward of King Canute, Elfrida must navigate the treacherous waters of power, loyalty, and her own desires. With vivid descriptions of medieval England and a cast of captivating characters, this historical romance will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Forbidden Love, Loyalty and Betrayal
