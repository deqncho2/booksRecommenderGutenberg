Synopsis: 
In 'The Lincoln Year Book: Axioms and Aphorisms from the Great Emancipator', delve into the profound wisdom and timeless insights of one of America's most revered leaders, Abraham Lincoln. This collection of quotes, anecdotes, and reflections offers a glimpse into the mind of the man who guided a nation through one of its most challenging periods. From his thoughts on leadership and justice to his musings on life and humanity, each page presents a nugget of wisdom that will inspire and provoke contemplation. Discover the essence of Lincoln's character and the enduring relevance of his words in this captivating compilation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Quotations, Biography

Themes: Leadership, Justice, Life, Humanity
