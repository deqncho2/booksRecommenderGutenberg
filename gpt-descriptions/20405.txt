Synopsis: In 'Grace Harlowe's Overland Riders Among the Kentucky Mountaineers', Grace and her adventurous friends embark on a thrilling journey through the rugged mountains of Kentucky. As they navigate treacherous trails and encounter mysterious locals, they find themselves caught up in a dangerous feud that threatens to tear apart the tight-knit community. With their courage and determination, the Overland Riders must unravel the secrets of the mountains and bring peace to the troubled land.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Community, Conflict
