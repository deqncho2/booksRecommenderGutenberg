Synopsis: In 'Mariposilla: A Novel' by Mary Stewart Daggett, a young woman named Mariposilla embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience in the vibrant landscapes of Mexico. Drawn to the allure of the country's rich culture and history, Mariposilla finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love. As she navigates through the complexities of her new surroundings, she must confront her own fears and confront the ghosts of her past. With each step she takes, Mariposilla uncovers hidden truths that challenge her perception of identity and the power of love.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Cultural exploration
