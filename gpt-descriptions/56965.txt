Synopsis: In 'The Wheels of Time' by Florence L. Barclay, a young woman named Sylvia finds herself torn between duty and desire when she meets a charismatic stranger who challenges everything she thought she knew about love and happiness. As their paths intertwine, Sylvia must navigate the complexities of societal expectations, personal growth, and the power of following one's heart. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century England, this captivating tale explores the choices we make and the consequences they hold, ultimately revealing the transformative nature of love and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Self-Discovery, Society and Expectations
