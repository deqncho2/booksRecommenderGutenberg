Synopsis: 
'Goops and How to Be Them' is a delightful and humorous children's book that introduces readers to a mischievous group of characters known as the Goops. Each Goop possesses a unique and often comical personality trait that leads to amusing situations and valuable life lessons. Through witty rhymes and charming illustrations, this book offers an entertaining exploration of the Goops' antics and provides young readers with valuable insights on manners, behavior, and the importance of kindness.

Genres: Children's literature, Poetry, Humor

Themes: Manners, Behavior, Kindness
