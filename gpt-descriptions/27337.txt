Synopsis: 
In 'From the Car Behind' by Eleanor M. Ingram, a chance encounter on a dark and stormy night leads to a mysterious and thrilling journey. When a young woman finds herself following a car with a suspicious driver, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and danger. As she delves deeper into the unknown, she must confront her own fears and uncover the truth before it's too late. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Trust, Deception, Courage
