Synopsis: In 'The Runaway Asteroid' by Michael D. Cooper, a brilliant astrophysicist discovers a shocking truth about an asteroid hurtling towards Earth. As the world races against time to prevent a catastrophic collision, a team of unlikely heroes must navigate through political intrigue, scientific breakthroughs, and personal sacrifices to save humanity. With gripping suspense and unexpected twists, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Sacrifice, Human Ingenuity
