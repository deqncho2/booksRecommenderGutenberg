Synopsis: In the heart of London, a mysterious tavern called "The Silver Flagon" becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and hidden secrets. As the lives of the tavern's patrons intertwine, a series of unexpected events unfold, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Intrigue, Deception, Loyalty, Redemption
