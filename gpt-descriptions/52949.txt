Synopsis: In 'Under Sail' by Felix Riesenberg, embark on a thrilling maritime adventure that takes you on a perilous journey across the vast oceans. Follow the protagonist as they navigate treacherous waters, encounter mysterious islands, and face unexpected challenges that test their courage and resilience. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this book will transport you to a world of high seas, where danger lurks at every turn and the power of the human spirit is put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Nautical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human Determination
