Synopsis: In this captivating volume, Mary Howitt takes readers on a thrilling journey through the history of the United States, from the moment of its discovery to the present day. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, she unveils the triumphs and struggles that have shaped the nation, delving into the lives of key figures and pivotal events that have left an indelible mark on American history. From the early explorers and settlers to the Revolutionary War, the Civil War, and the challenges of the modern era, Howitt paints a rich tapestry of the United States' past, offering a fresh perspective on its complex evolution.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: American history, Exploration, Revolution, Progress, Nation-building
