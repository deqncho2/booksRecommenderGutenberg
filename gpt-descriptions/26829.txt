Synopsis: In 'The Old Helmet, Volume I' by Susan Warner, a mysterious old helmet is discovered, setting off a chain of events that intertwines the lives of various characters. As the helmet's history is slowly unveiled, secrets, love, and unexpected connections come to light, leading to a captivating tale of intrigue and redemption. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Love and relationships, Redemption
