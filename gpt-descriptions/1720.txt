Synopsis: In 'The Man Who Knew Too Much', a seemingly ordinary man named Horne Fisher finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. As he navigates through a series of perplexing events, Fisher's unique ability to uncover hidden truths becomes both a blessing and a curse. With his sharp intellect and unconventional methods, he must unravel the secrets that threaten to shake the very foundations of society. As the plot thickens, Fisher's knowledge becomes a dangerous weapon, leading him down a path where he must confront the darkest corners of human nature. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Detective Fiction

Themes: Knowledge and its consequences, Morality and ethics, The nature of truth
