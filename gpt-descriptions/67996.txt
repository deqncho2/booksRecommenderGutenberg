Synopsis: In 'The Angel and the Demon: A Tale' by Arthur, T. S., a mysterious encounter between an angel and a demon sets off a chain of events that will challenge the boundaries of good and evil. As the angel and the demon navigate the human world, they find themselves drawn to a young woman who possesses a unique power. With their own agendas at play, the angel and the demon must decide whether to protect or exploit her abilities, leading to a thrilling battle of wits and morality.

Genres: Fantasy, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Morality, Power and Responsibility
