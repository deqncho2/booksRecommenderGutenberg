Synopsis: Polly: A New-Fashioned Girl follows the journey of a young girl named Polly Milton as she navigates the challenges of growing up in a rapidly changing world. With her unconventional ideas and independent spirit, Polly finds herself caught between the expectations of society and her own desires. As she encounters new friendships, unexpected love, and the pursuit of her dreams, Polly must learn to stay true to herself and embrace her own unique path.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Historical fiction, Romance

Themes: Individuality, Self-discovery, Society's expectations
