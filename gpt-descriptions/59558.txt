Synopsis: In 'Your Servant, Sir' by Boren, Sol, a young and ambitious servant named James finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue when he is hired by a mysterious and enigmatic nobleman. As James delves deeper into his master's world, he uncovers a hidden society filled with power struggles, forbidden desires, and dangerous alliances. With his loyalty tested and his own desires awakened, James must navigate a treacherous path where nothing is as it seems, and everyone has something to hide.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Power dynamics, Loyalty, Deception, Forbidden desires
