Synopsis: 

In 'For the Honor of Randall: A Story of College Athletics' by Chadwick, Lester, we are introduced to Randall, a talented and determined college athlete who faces numerous challenges and obstacles on his journey to success. As he navigates the competitive world of college sports, Randall must confront personal doubts, rivalries, and unexpected setbacks that test his resilience and character. With the support of his teammates and the guidance of a wise mentor, Randall must find the strength to overcome adversity and prove his worth on and off the field.

Genres: Sports, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Perseverance, Teamwork, Identity, Self-discovery
