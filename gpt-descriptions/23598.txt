Synopsis: In 'Little Bo-Peep: A Nursery Rhyme Picture Book', Brooke L. Leslie brings to life the beloved nursery rhyme of Little Bo-Peep and her lost sheep through captivating illustrations and a delightful retelling. Follow Little Bo-Peep on her quest to find her missing sheep, as she encounters various obstacles and learns valuable lessons along the way. This charming picture book is sure to enchant young readers and bring a fresh perspective to a timeless rhyme.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book

Themes: Responsibility, Perseverance, Problem-Solving
