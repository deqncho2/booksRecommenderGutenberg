Synopsis: 
In 'Psyche' by Louis Couperus, a young woman named Psyche finds herself caught in a web of passion, desire, and forbidden love. Set against the backdrop of a luxurious Dutch estate, Psyche becomes entangled in a complex love triangle that challenges her sense of self and forces her to confront the depths of her own desires. As she navigates the intricacies of her relationships, Psyche must grapple with the consequences of her choices and the power dynamics at play. With its lush descriptions and captivating characters, 'Psyche' is a mesmerizing tale of love, betrayal, and the complexities of the human heart.

Genres: 
Romance, Psychological Fiction, Gothic Fiction

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Identity and Self-discovery, Forbidden Love
