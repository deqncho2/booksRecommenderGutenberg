Synopsis: In 'Benefactor' by George H. Smith, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges, offering immense wealth and power to those who are willing to follow his lead. As the protagonist delves deeper into this world of opulence and influence, they soon discover that the benefactor's true intentions may not be as benevolent as they initially seemed. With secrets, intrigue, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the lengths people will go to attain power and the consequences that come with it.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Power, Morality, Deception
