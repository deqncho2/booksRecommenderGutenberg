Synopsis: In "Life of Elie Metchnikoff, 1845-1916," Olga Metchnikoff delves into the extraordinary life of her husband, Elie Metchnikoff, a renowned scientist and Nobel laureate. From his humble beginnings in Russia to his groundbreaking discoveries in immunology and aging, this captivating biography unveils the personal and professional struggles that shaped Metchnikoff's remarkable journey. With vivid anecdotes and intimate insights, Olga paints a vivid portrait of a man driven by curiosity, passion, and a relentless pursuit of knowledge. As the narrative unfolds, readers are transported through the fascinating world of scientific discovery, witnessing the triumphs and tribulations of one of history's most influential figures.

Genres: Biography, Science, History

Themes: Scientific inquiry, Personal growth, Legacy
