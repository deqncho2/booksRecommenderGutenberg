Synopsis: In "The School by the Sea" by Angela Brazil, a group of adventurous schoolgirls find themselves in a new and exciting environment when their boarding school is relocated to a picturesque seaside town. As they navigate the challenges of their new surroundings, they uncover hidden secrets, forge new friendships, and embark on thrilling adventures that will test their courage and resilience. With the backdrop of the beautiful seaside setting, the girls learn valuable life lessons and discover the true meaning of friendship and loyalty.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Adventure, Resilience, Self-discovery
