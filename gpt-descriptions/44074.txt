Synopsis: In 'Meccania, the Super-State' by Gregory, Owen, a young journalist named Oswald Bastable finds himself in a dystopian world ruled by a powerful and oppressive super-state known as Meccania. As he navigates through this totalitarian society, Bastable uncovers shocking secrets and becomes entangled in a dangerous rebellion against the regime. With his life on the line, he must decide whether to conform or fight for freedom, leading to a thrilling and thought-provoking journey that challenges the very essence of human nature.

Genres: Dystopian fiction, political thriller, speculative fiction

Themes: Totalitarianism, individualism vs. collectivism, resistance against oppression
