Synopsis: 
In 'The House of Sleep' by Elizabeth Bartlett, a group of insomniacs find themselves entangled in a mysterious and surreal world where dreams and reality blur. As they navigate through their sleepless nights, their lives become intertwined in unexpected ways, leading to a series of unpredictable and captivating events. With elements of romance, mystery, and psychological exploration, this novel takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the depths of the human mind.

Genres: 
Psychological fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: 
Insomnia, Dreams, Reality vs. Illusion
