Synopsis: In "Tarzan and the Lion Man," Edgar Rice Burroughs takes readers on another thrilling adventure with the iconic character Tarzan. This time, Tarzan finds himself in the heart of Hollywood, where he becomes involved in the making of a movie about his own life. As he navigates the glitz and glamour of the film industry, Tarzan encounters treacherous individuals and faces dangerous situations that test his strength, intelligence, and survival skills. With his primal instincts and unparalleled abilities, Tarzan must protect himself and those he cares about, all while unraveling the mysteries behind the scenes of the movie. 

Genres: Adventure, Action, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Survival, Exploration of the Wild
