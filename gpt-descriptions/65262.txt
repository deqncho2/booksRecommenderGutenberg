Synopsis: In 'The Fritz Strafers: A Story of the Great War', Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous times of World War I. The story follows a group of young British soldiers who find themselves facing the formidable German enemy, known as the Fritz Strafers. As they navigate the treacherous battlefields and encounter unexpected challenges, the soldiers must summon their courage and resourcefulness to survive. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Westerman paints a gripping portrait of the horrors and heroism of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Survival, Camaraderie, Sacrifice
