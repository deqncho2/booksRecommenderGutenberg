Synopsis: 
In 'Magic: In which are given clear and concise explanations of all the well-known illusions as well as many new ones,' Stanyon and Ellis delve into the captivating world of magic, revealing the secrets behind both classic and innovative illusions. From mind-bending card tricks to mind-reading feats, this book offers a comprehensive guide to the art of magic, providing readers with clear and concise explanations that will leave them in awe. Prepare to be amazed as you uncover the mysteries behind some of the most famous tricks, while also discovering new illusions that will challenge your perception of reality.

Genres: Non-fiction, Entertainment, Reference

Themes: Magic, Illusion, Performance, Deception
