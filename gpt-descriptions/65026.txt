Synopsis: 
"The Social Ladder: Drawings by Charles Dana Gibson" takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the intricate world of American society in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Through his iconic pen and ink illustrations, Charles Dana Gibson masterfully captures the nuances of social interactions, revealing the aspirations, struggles, and triumphs of individuals navigating their way up the social ladder. From elegant ballrooms to bustling city streets, Gibson's drawings provide a humorous and insightful commentary on the complexities of class, gender, and ambition during this transformative era.

Genres: Art, Social Commentary, Satire

Themes: Social class, Gender roles, Ambition, Society and culture
