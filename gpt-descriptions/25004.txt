Synopsis: 
"A Confederate Girl's Diary" is a captivating memoir written by Sarah Morgan Dawson, a young woman living in Louisiana during the American Civil War. Through her diary entries, Sarah provides a unique and intimate perspective on the daily struggles, heartaches, and triumphs experienced by those living in the Confederacy. As the war unfolds, Sarah's world is turned upside down, and she must navigate the complexities of love, loss, and loyalty in a time of great turmoil. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt emotions, this diary offers a poignant and personal account of a young girl's journey through one of the most tumultuous periods in American history.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: War, Love, Resilience, Loyalty
