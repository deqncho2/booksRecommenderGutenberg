Synopsis: 
Discover the captivating history and mysterious origins of the Mound State Monument in Moundville, Alabama. This intriguing work by the Alabama Museum of Natural History takes readers on a journey through time, exploring the ancient civilization that once thrived in this remarkable archaeological site. Uncover the secrets hidden within the mounds and delve into the lives of the people who built them, as you unravel the enigmatic story of Moundville.

Genres: Non-fiction, Archaeology, History

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Archaeological mysteries, Cultural heritage
