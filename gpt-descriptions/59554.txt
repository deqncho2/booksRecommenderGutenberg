Synopsis:
In 'House Flies' by Howard, L. O., a seemingly ordinary household pest becomes the catalyst for a series of unexpected events. As the protagonist delves deeper into the mysterious world of house flies, they uncover a hidden truth that challenges their perception of reality. With each revelation, the stakes rise, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes:
Perception, Reality, Intrigue
