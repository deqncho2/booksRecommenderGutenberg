Synopsis: 
'London at Night: A sketch-book' by Frederick Carter takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting streets of London after dark. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, the book unveils the hidden beauty and mysterious allure of the city's nocturnal landscape. From bustling markets to quiet alleyways, from iconic landmarks to hidden gems, the author's sketches and accompanying narratives paint a mesmerizing portrait of London's vibrant nightlife. As the night unfolds, readers are invited to explore the city's secrets, encounter intriguing characters, and witness the captivating interplay between light and darkness. 

Genres: 
Travel, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Urban exploration, Nightlife, Beauty in the ordinary
