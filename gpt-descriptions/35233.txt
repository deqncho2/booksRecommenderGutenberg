Synopsis: In 'The Streets of Ascalon: Episodes in the Unfinished Career of Richard Quarren, Esqre.', follow the enigmatic Richard Quarren as he navigates the treacherous streets of Ascalon, a city teeming with secrets and danger. As a series of mysterious events unfold, Quarren finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With his quick wit and resourcefulness, he must unravel the truth behind a sinister plot that threatens to plunge the city into chaos. Will Quarren's unfinished career reach its climax, or will he become just another casualty of the streets of Ascalon?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Intrigue, Betrayal, Survival
