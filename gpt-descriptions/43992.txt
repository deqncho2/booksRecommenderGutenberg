Synopsis: In 'A Hand-book to the Primates, Volume 2', Henry O. Forbes takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of primates. From the dense jungles of Africa to the remote islands of Southeast Asia, this comprehensive guide explores the diverse species of primates, their behaviors, habitats, and evolutionary history. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Forbes offers a unique perspective on these intelligent and complex creatures, shedding light on their social structures, communication methods, and remarkable adaptations. Whether you are a primate enthusiast or simply curious about the wonders of the animal kingdom, this book is sure to captivate and educate.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Science

Themes: Animal behavior, Biodiversity, Evolution
