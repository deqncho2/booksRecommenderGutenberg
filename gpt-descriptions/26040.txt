Synopsis: 

'Log-book of Timothy Boardman: Kept on Board the Privateer Oliver Cromwell, During a Cruise from New London, Ct., to Charleston, S. C., and Return, in 1778; Also, a Biographical Sketch of the Author.' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the eyes of Timothy Boardman, a young sailor aboard the privateer Oliver Cromwell during the American Revolutionary War. As Boardman recounts his experiences, readers are transported to the treacherous seas and witness the dangers, triumphs, and personal growth he encounters during this historic voyage. Alongside the log-book entries, the book also provides a captivating biographical sketch of the author, shedding light on his life and motivations.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Patriotism, Survival, Coming of Age
