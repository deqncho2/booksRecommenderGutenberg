Synopsis: 
In 'How to Do Chemical Tricks: Containing Over One Hundred Highly Amusing and Instructive Tricks With Chemicals', Anderson takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of chemical tricks. From creating colorful explosions to making objects disappear, this book offers a wide array of fascinating and entertaining experiments that will leave readers in awe. With clear instructions and explanations, Anderson ensures that even beginners can successfully perform these tricks, making it a perfect guide for both aspiring chemists and curious individuals looking to impress their friends. Get ready to unlock the secrets of chemical magic and embark on a thrilling adventure of scientific wonder!

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Science, Entertainment

Themes: 
Curiosity, Education, Amazement
