Synopsis: In 'The Vengeance of Toffee' by Farrell Henry, a seemingly ordinary confectionery shop owner named Toffee discovers a hidden power within himself that sets him on a thrilling journey of revenge. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of his town, Toffee uncovers a web of secrets, betrayal, and a sinister plot that threatens to destroy everything he holds dear. With unexpected allies and dangerous enemies at every turn, Toffee must harness his newfound abilities and confront his past to seek justice and protect those he loves.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Revenge, Power, Betrayal, Redemption
