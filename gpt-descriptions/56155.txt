Synopsis: In 'Fairy Tales from Gold Lands' by Wentworth, May, embark on a magical journey through enchanting realms filled with talking animals, brave heroes, and cunning villains. From the depths of the ocean to the highest mountaintops, these captivating tales will transport readers to a world where dreams come true and the impossible becomes possible. Prepare to be captivated by the whimsical adventures and timeless lessons that await within these pages.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Imagination, Courage, Morality
