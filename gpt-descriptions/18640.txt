Synopsis: 
In 'Phineas Redux' by Anthony Trollope, the reader is transported back to the political world of Phineas Finn, a young and ambitious Member of Parliament. After a period of exile, Phineas returns to London, hoping to revive his political career and find love. However, he soon becomes entangled in a web of scandal, intrigue, and personal vendettas that threaten to destroy everything he has worked for. As Phineas navigates the treacherous waters of politics and romance, he must confront his own moral compass and make difficult choices that will shape his future.

Genres: 
Political fiction, Romance, Victorian literature

Themes: 
Ambition, Morality, Love and relationships, Political corruption
