Synopsis: 

'The Blizzard in the West: Being a Record and Story of the Disastrous Storm which Raged Throughout Devon and Cornwall, and West Somerset, On the Night of March 9th, 1891' takes readers on a gripping journey through a catastrophic blizzard that wreaks havoc on the southwestern region of England. As the storm intensifies, the lives of the inhabitants are thrown into turmoil, and the struggle for survival becomes paramount. Amidst the chaos, the story follows a diverse cast of characters who must confront their deepest fears and find strength in the face of adversity. 

Genres: Historical fiction, disaster, survival

Themes: Resilience, community, the power of nature
