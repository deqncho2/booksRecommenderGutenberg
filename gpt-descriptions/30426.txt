Synopsis: 
In 'Rhymes Old and New: collected by M.E.S. Wright', prepare to embark on a delightful journey through the enchanting world of poetry. This collection brings together a diverse range of rhymes, from beloved classics to hidden gems waiting to be discovered. From whimsical tales of talking animals to heartfelt expressions of love and loss, these verses will captivate readers of all ages. Whether you're seeking a nostalgic trip down memory lane or a fresh exploration of the power of words, this anthology is sure to leave you spellbound.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature, Anthology

Themes: Imagination, Nostalgia, Emotions, Nature, Life's Journey
