Synopsis: In 'A Yellow Aster, Volume 1 (of 3)' by Caffyn, Mannington, Mrs., readers are transported to the vibrant world of the California gold rush. The story follows the lives of two young women, one from a wealthy family and the other an orphan, as they navigate the challenges and opportunities presented by this tumultuous era. As they encounter love, betrayal, and the pursuit of their dreams, their paths intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to a gripping tale of resilience and determination. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society, this captivating novel explores the complexities of class, gender, and the pursuit of happiness in a time of great upheaval.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Social class, Gender roles, Pursuit of happiness
