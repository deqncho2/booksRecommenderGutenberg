Synopsis:
In 'The House of Walderne: A Tale of the Cloister and the Forest in the Days of the Barons' Wars', the reader is transported to a tumultuous period in history, where the clash between the cloistered life and the chaos of the forest intertwine. Set during the Barons' Wars, this captivating tale follows the lives of the noble Walderne family as they navigate the treacherous political landscape and the secrets hidden within their own ancestral home. As the story unfolds, the reader is drawn into a world of intrigue, forbidden love, and the struggle for power, where loyalties are tested and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling characters, 'The House of Walderne' promises an enthralling journey through a time of conflict and transformation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Forbidden Love
