Synopsis: In "Konrad Wallenrod: An Historical Poem," Mickiewicz takes us on a captivating journey through medieval Lithuania, where a young warrior named Konrad Wallenrod finds himself torn between loyalty to his homeland and a secret mission that could change the course of history. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of political intrigue and personal sacrifice, Konrad's choices become increasingly complex, leading to a stunning climax that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Historical fiction, epic poetry, political drama

Themes: Identity, patriotism, betrayal, honor, sacrifice
