Synopsis: 
In 'Mignon; or, Bootles' Baby' by John Strange Winter, a mysterious baby is left on the doorstep of a bachelor's apartment, turning his life upside down. As he navigates the challenges of caring for an infant, he discovers unexpected connections and unravels a web of secrets that will change his life forever. With humor, heart, and a touch of suspense, this captivating story explores the transformative power of love and the bonds that can form in the most unlikely circumstances.

Genres: 
Romance, Mystery, Comedy

Themes: 
Love, Family, Identity
