Synopsis: In "The Lady from Nowhere: A Detective Story" by Fergus Hume, a mysterious woman appears out of thin air, seeking the help of a renowned detective to solve a perplexing case. As the detective delves deeper into the enigmatic woman's past, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden identities. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise, and the truth becomes more elusive. Will the detective be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Secrets
