Synopsis: In the small Scottish town of Thrums, a young boy named Gavin Dishart grows up under the watchful eyes of the town's colorful characters. As he navigates the challenges of life, love, and duty, Gavin's journey takes unexpected turns that will test his resilience and shape his destiny. Through the window of Thrums, readers are invited to witness the joys, sorrows, and secrets of a close-knit community that will leave them captivated until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Community, Identity, Love, Duty, Resilience
