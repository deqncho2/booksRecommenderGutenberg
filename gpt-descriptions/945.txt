Synopsis: In 'Dust' by Haldeman-Julius, E. and Haldeman-Julius, Marcet, a small town is plagued by a mysterious phenomenon that turns everything it touches into dust. As the townspeople struggle to understand and survive this strange occurrence, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and desires. With the threat of annihilation looming, the residents must band together and uncover the truth behind the dust before it consumes them all.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Existentialism, Human Nature, Survival
