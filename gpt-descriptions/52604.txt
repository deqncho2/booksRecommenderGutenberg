Synopsis: 
In 'Harper's Round Table, January 21, 1896', a group of young adventurers embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover a hidden treasure, encountering mysterious clues and dangerous obstacles along the way. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the past, they must rely on their wit and courage to unravel the enigma that could change their lives forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
