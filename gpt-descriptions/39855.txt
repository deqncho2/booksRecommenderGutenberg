Synopsis: 
In 'The Teaching of Epictetus: Being the 'Encheiridion of Epictetus,' with Selections from the 'Dissertations' and 'Fragments',' readers are introduced to the profound teachings of the Stoic philosopher Epictetus. This collection of his works offers a practical guide to living a virtuous life, filled with wisdom and insights that are as relevant today as they were in ancient times. Through his concise and powerful aphorisms, Epictetus explores the nature of human existence, the importance of self-discipline, and the pursuit of inner peace amidst the challenges of life. With his emphasis on personal responsibility and the power of the mind, Epictetus provides readers with a transformative philosophy that can help navigate the complexities of the modern world.

Genres: Philosophy, Self-help, Classics
Themes: Stoicism, Virtue, Self-discipline, Inner peace, Personal responsibility
