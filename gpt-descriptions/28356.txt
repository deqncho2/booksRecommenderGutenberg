Synopsis: 
In the rugged and untamed Montana wilderness, a young man named Chip Bennett finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden ranch known as the Wolverine. As Chip becomes entangled in the lives of the enigmatic ranch owner and his beautiful daughter, he soon discovers that the secrets of the Wolverine run deep, and that his own past may hold the key to unlocking its mysteries. With danger lurking at every turn, Chip must navigate a treacherous landscape of love, betrayal, and redemption, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred and the truth becomes a matter of life and death.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Family, Redemption
