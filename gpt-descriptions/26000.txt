Synopsis: 
In 'The Sailor's Word-Book: An Alphabetical Digest of Nautical Terms', author W. H. Smyth takes readers on a captivating journey through the vast and intricate world of nautical language. This comprehensive guide not only explores the terminology used by sailors, but also delves into military and scientific terms relevant to seafaring. Smyth's work also uncovers archaisms from early voyagers, providing a fascinating glimpse into the language of the past. From the familiar to the obscure, this book is a treasure trove of knowledge for anyone curious about the rich vocabulary of the sea.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Reference, Maritime

Themes: 
Language and Communication, Maritime History, Exploration
