Synopsis: 
In 'The Trail of a Sourdough: Life in Alaska', May Kellogg Sullivan takes us on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of Alaska. Through her vivid and heartfelt storytelling, she shares her experiences as a pioneer in this harsh and unforgiving land, where danger lurks at every turn. From encounters with wild animals to battling the elements, Sullivan's gripping narrative paints a picture of the resilience and determination required to survive in this rugged frontier. As she navigates the challenges of daily life, she also delves into the unique culture and traditions of the native Alaskans, providing a fascinating glimpse into a world few have ever known. 

Genres: Adventure, Memoir, Historical

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Cultural Exchange
