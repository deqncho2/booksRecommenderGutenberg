Synopsis: 

'Old English Patent Medicines in America' takes readers on a fascinating journey through the history of patent medicines and their impact on American society. From the early days of colonial America to the rise of the pharmaceutical industry, Griffenhagen and Young delve into the intriguing world of these popular remedies. Through meticulous research and captivating anecdotes, they explore the marketing strategies, ingredients, and controversies surrounding these medicines, shedding light on the cultural and medical landscape of the time. This comprehensive exploration of patent medicines in America is sure to captivate readers with its blend of history, science, and social commentary.

Genres: History, Medicine, Cultural Studies

Themes: Medical history, Marketing and advertising, Cultural impact of medicine
