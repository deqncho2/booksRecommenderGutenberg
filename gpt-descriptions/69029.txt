Synopsis: 
'Genevra; or, the history of a portrait' follows the captivating story of a mysterious portrait and its connection to the life of Genevra, a young woman living in Washington City. As the portrait's history unfolds, secrets are revealed, and Genevra finds herself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and intrigue. With each revelation, the portrait takes on a deeper significance, leading Genevra on a journey of self-discovery and transformation.

Genres: 
Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Art and its power to influence lives, Identity and self-discovery, Love and betrayal
