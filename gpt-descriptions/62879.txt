Synopsis: In "Gems in the Smithsonian Institution," author Paul E. Desautels takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden world of precious gemstones. From the breathtaking Hope Diamond to the mesmerizing Star of India, this book unveils the fascinating stories behind some of the most renowned gems housed in the Smithsonian Institution. With vivid descriptions and intriguing historical anecdotes, Desautels explores the allure, mystery, and extraordinary beauty of these gems, leaving readers spellbound and eager to discover more.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Gemology

Themes: Beauty and aesthetics, History and cultural significance of gemstones, Curiosity and exploration
