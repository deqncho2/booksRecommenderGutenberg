Synopsis: In 'Fish Stories' by Abbott, Henry, a small coastal town becomes the backdrop for a series of mysterious events that revolve around the town's fishing industry. As the residents grapple with strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena, they are forced to confront their own secrets and the dark underbelly of their seemingly idyllic community. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot that keeps you guessing until the very end, 'Fish Stories' is a gripping tale of suspense and intrigue that will leave you hooked.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Small-town dynamics, Supernatural elements
