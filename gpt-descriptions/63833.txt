Synopsis: In 'Jinx Ship to the Rescue' by Alfred Coppel, a daring and resourceful crew embarks on a high-stakes mission to save a stranded spaceship. As they navigate treacherous space, face unexpected challenges, and encounter mysterious forces, the crew must rely on their wits and teamwork to ensure the survival of all onboard. With heart-pounding action and unexpected twists, this thrilling science fiction adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Teamwork, Exploration
