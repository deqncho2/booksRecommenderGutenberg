Synopsis: In 'The Created Legend' by Fyodor Sologub, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small Russian village, captivating the townspeople with his enigmatic presence. As the stranger's influence grows, the villagers become entangled in a web of secrets, desires, and dark obsessions. With each passing day, the line between reality and illusion blurs, leading to a haunting climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of truth and the power of perception.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Mystery, Gothic literature

Themes: Identity, Deception, Madness, Power dynamics
