Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's Support; Or, A Triple Play' by Burt L. Standish, Frank Merriwell, the beloved college athlete, finds himself facing a series of unexpected challenges that test his character and determination. As he navigates through a web of deceit, betrayal, and personal struggles, Frank must rely on his unwavering support system to overcome the obstacles that threaten to derail his dreams. With his reputation on the line, Frank must summon all his strength and resilience to prove that he is not just a talented athlete, but also a true champion in life.

Genres: Sports, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Perseverance, Loyalty, Integrity
