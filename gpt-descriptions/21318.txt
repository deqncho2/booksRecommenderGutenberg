Synopsis: In 'The Lost Middy: Being the Secret of the Smugglers' Gap' by George Manville Fenn, a young sailor named Dick Persehouse finds himself caught up in a dangerous adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden secret in a remote coastal village. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he must navigate treacherous cliffs, encounter smugglers, and unravel the truth behind the enigmatic Smugglers' Gap. With his courage and resourcefulness tested, Dick must rely on his wits to uncover the secrets that lie within the gap and bring justice to those involved.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Secrets, Justice
