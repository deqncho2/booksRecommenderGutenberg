Synopsis: In 'The Angel of Death' by Johan Olof Wallin, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a series of mysterious murders that seem to be connected to a dark secret from his own past. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and revenge that threatens to consume him. With each new clue, the detective becomes more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means facing his own demons. As the body count rises, he must race against time to catch the elusive Angel of Death before more lives are lost.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Redemption, Revenge, Secrets, Identity
