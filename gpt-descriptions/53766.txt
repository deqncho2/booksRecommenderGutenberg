Synopsis: In 'Merry Tales', Ada M. Skinner and Eleanor L. Skinner take readers on a delightful journey through a collection of charming and humorous short stories. From mischievous children to eccentric neighbors, these tales are filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. With each story offering a unique and entertaining experience, 'Merry Tales' is a delightful escape into a world of laughter and joy.

Genres: Fiction, Humor, Short Stories

Themes: Humor, Childhood, Community
