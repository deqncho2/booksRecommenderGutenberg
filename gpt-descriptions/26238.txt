Synopsis: In 'Vixen, Volume III' by M. E. Braddon, the captivating and enigmatic Vixen returns, entangling herself in a web of scandal, secrets, and forbidden desires. As her past catches up with her, Vixen must navigate treacherous waters, where love and betrayal intertwine, and the consequences of her actions threaten to unravel her world. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind Vixen's mysterious past.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Forbidden Love, Redemption
