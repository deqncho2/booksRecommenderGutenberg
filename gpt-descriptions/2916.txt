Synopsis: In "The Foundations (An Extravagant Play)" by John Galsworthy, a wealthy family finds their lives turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives at their doorstep, claiming to be the rightful heir to their fortune. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the family must confront their own desires, ambitions, and the true meaning of wealth and happiness. This gripping play explores the complexities of family dynamics, the pursuit of power, and the consequences of greed.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Family

Themes: Wealth and power, Family dynamics, Greed, Identity
