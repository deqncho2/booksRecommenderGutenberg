Synopsis:
In the enchanting world of a mysterious country house, a group of strangers find themselves invited to a Midsummer's Eve gathering. As the clock strikes midnight, they are given a chance to revisit their lives and make different choices. With the guidance of the enigmatic host, they embark on a journey of self-discovery, exploring the paths not taken and the consequences of their decisions. But as the night unfolds, they soon realize that the house holds secrets far beyond their imagination, and the choices they make may have unforeseen and profound implications.

Genres:
Fantasy, Drama, Mystery

Themes:
Regret, Second chances, Fate, Identity
