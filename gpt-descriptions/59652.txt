Synopsis: In 'Cronus of the D. F. C.' by Biggle, Lloyd, Jr., a group of interstellar explorers embarks on a dangerous mission to uncover the secrets of a mysterious planet. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter enigmatic beings, they must confront their own fears and desires, leading to unexpected revelations that challenge their understanding of the universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Morality
