Synopsis: In 'The Woman from Outside: [On Swan River]' by Hulbert Footner, a mysterious woman arrives in a small town on the banks of Swan River, captivating the locals with her enigmatic presence. As the townspeople become increasingly intrigued by her origins and intentions, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden desires that threaten to unravel their peaceful existence. With each passing day, the woman's influence grows stronger, leaving the townsfolk questioning their own identities and the true nature of the world around them.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Desire, Perception
