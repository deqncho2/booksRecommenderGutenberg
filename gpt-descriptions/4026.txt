Synopsis: In 'The First Man' by Eugene O'Neill, we follow the journey of a young man named Yank as he navigates the harsh realities of life in early 20th century America. Raised in poverty and struggling to find his place in the world, Yank embarks on a quest for self-discovery that takes him from the depths of despair to moments of profound revelation. Through his encounters with various characters and his own introspection, Yank grapples with themes of identity, class struggle, and the search for meaning in a world that often seems indifferent to his existence.

Genres: Drama, Coming-of-age, Realism

Themes: Identity, Class struggle, Self-discovery, Existentialism
