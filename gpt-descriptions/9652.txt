Synopsis: 
'Miscellany of Poetry: 1919' is a captivating collection of poems that takes readers on a journey through the emotions and experiences of the year 1919. From the aftermath of World War I to the dawn of a new era, this anthology explores themes of love, loss, hope, and the resilience of the human spirit. Each poem offers a unique perspective, painting vivid images and evoking powerful emotions that will leave readers pondering the complexities of life and the beauty of language.

Genres: Poetry, Historical, Literary

Themes: Love, Loss, Hope, Resilience
