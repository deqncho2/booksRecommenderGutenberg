Synopsis: 
In 'Abbé Aubain and Mosaics' by Mérimée, Prosper, a young and ambitious artist, becomes captivated by the enigmatic Abbé Aubain, a renowned mosaicist. As Prosper delves deeper into the world of mosaics, he uncovers a hidden secret that threatens to unravel his own sanity. With each mosaic piece he creates, Prosper finds himself entangled in a web of obsession, desire, and the blurred lines between art and reality. As the story unfolds, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the dark and mysterious world of mosaic art, where secrets lie beneath the surface and the boundaries of creativity are pushed to their limits.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Art Fiction

Themes: Obsession, Artistic Expression, Identity
