Synopsis: 
'Jack Straw: A Farce in Three Acts' by W. Somerset Maugham is a hilarious and fast-paced comedy that follows the misadventures of Jack Straw, a charming but bumbling young man who finds himself caught up in a series of comical situations. From mistaken identities to romantic entanglements, Jack's journey takes him through a whirlwind of absurdity and laughter, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to see how he will extricate himself from each predicament.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Mistaken identity, Love and romance, Social class, Absurdity
