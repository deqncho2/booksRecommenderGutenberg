Synopsis: In "Medica Sacra: Or, A Commentary on the Most Remarkable Diseases Mentioned in the Holy Scriptures," Richard Mead delves into the intriguing world of ancient diseases mentioned in the Bible. From leprosy to demonic possession, Mead explores the historical context, symptoms, and possible explanations for these afflictions. Through his meticulous analysis, he sheds light on the medical knowledge and practices of the time, offering a fascinating glimpse into the intersection of faith and medicine in biblical times.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical History, Religious Studies

Themes: Ancient Medicine, Biblical Interpretation, Faith and Healing
