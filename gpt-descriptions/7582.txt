Synopsis:
'Widger's Quotes and Images from The Confession of a Child of the Century by Alfred de Musset: The French Immortals: Quotes and Images' is a collection of thought-provoking quotes and captivating images from Alfred de Musset's novel, 'The Confession of a Child of the Century.' The book offers a glimpse into the emotional journey of a young protagonist as he navigates love, heartbreak, and the complexities of life in 19th-century France. Through a series of poignant quotes and accompanying images, the reader is transported into the world of the protagonist, experiencing his joys, sorrows, and profound reflections on the human condition.

Genres:
Literary Fiction, Romance, Bildungsroman

Themes:
Love and Heartbreak, Coming of Age, Existentialism, Society and Morality
