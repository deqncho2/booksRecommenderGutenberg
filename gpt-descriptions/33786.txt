Synopsis: 
In 'Readings from Latin Verse; With Notes' by Curtis C. Bushnell, embark on a captivating journey through the rich and enchanting world of Latin poetry. This collection presents a carefully curated selection of Latin verse, accompanied by insightful notes that illuminate the historical and cultural context of each piece. From the passionate love poems of Catullus to the epic tales of Virgil's Aeneid, this book offers a delightful exploration of the beauty and power of Latin poetry. Whether you are a seasoned Latin scholar or a curious reader, this collection will transport you to a world of timeless elegance and lyrical brilliance.

Genres: Poetry, Classics, Literary Criticism

Themes: Love and desire, Mythology and legends, Historical and cultural context
