Synopsis: In 'A Cousin's Conspiracy; Or, A Boy's Struggle for an Inheritance' by Horatio Alger, Jr., a young boy named Harry is faced with a treacherous plot that threatens to strip him of his rightful inheritance. As he navigates through a web of deceit and betrayal, Harry must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to uncover the truth and reclaim what is rightfully his. This thrilling tale of intrigue and resilience will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the outcome.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Inheritance, Deception, Resilience
