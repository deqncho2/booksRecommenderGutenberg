Synopsis: 
In the August 1931 issue of Astounding Stories, prepare to embark on a thrilling journey through the vastness of space and the depths of the human imagination. Encounter a mysterious planet where time flows in reverse, witness a battle between humans and an alien race with unimaginable powers, and delve into the mind of a brilliant scientist who has discovered the secret to eternal life. These captivating tales will transport you to worlds beyond your wildest dreams, where the boundaries of science and fiction blur into one mesmerizing adventure.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Time manipulation, Extraterrestrial encounters, Immortality
