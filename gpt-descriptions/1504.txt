Synopsis: 
In the bustling city of Ephesus, two sets of identical twins, separated at birth, find themselves unknowingly entangled in a whirlwind of mistaken identities, hilarious misunderstandings, and uproarious chaos. As the confusion escalates, the townspeople become embroiled in the madness, leading to a series of comical encounters and absurd situations. With mistaken identities at every turn, this uproarious comedy takes the audience on a wild journey filled with laughter, love, and unexpected reunions.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Romance

Themes: Identity, Mistaken Identity, Love and Relationships, Family, Deception
