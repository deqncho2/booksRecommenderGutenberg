Synopsis: 
'Poems by Speranza' by Wilde, Lady is a collection of captivating and thought-provoking poems that delve into the depths of human emotions and societal norms. From exploring the complexities of love and desire to challenging the constraints imposed by society, these poems offer a unique perspective on life's most profound experiences. With each verse, Wilde, Lady invites readers on a journey of self-discovery and introspection, leaving them yearning for more.

Genres: Poetry, Romanticism, Social Commentary

Themes: Love, Desire, Society, Individuality, Rebellion
