Synopsis: In 'Making Life Worth While', Douglas Fairbanks takes readers on a captivating journey through the ups and downs of life, offering insightful perspectives and practical advice on how to find true happiness and fulfillment. Through a series of engaging anecdotes and personal experiences, Fairbanks explores the importance of embracing positivity, pursuing one's passions, and cherishing the simple joys that make life truly worth living. With his signature charm and wit, the author encourages readers to seize the day and discover the secrets to a meaningful and rewarding existence.

Genres: Self-help, Inspirational, Memoir

Themes: Happiness, Fulfillment, Positivity, Pursuit of Passions
