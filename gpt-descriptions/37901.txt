Synopsis: In 'Profitable Squab Breeding' by Dare, Carl, a young entrepreneur stumbles upon a unique and lucrative business opportunity that involves breeding a lesser-known bird species. As he delves deeper into the world of squab breeding, he encounters unexpected challenges and discovers the secrets behind this profitable venture. With high stakes and a race against time, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the outcome.

Genres: Business, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Entrepreneurship, Risk-taking, Unconventional business ventures
