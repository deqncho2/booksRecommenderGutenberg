Synopsis: In "Frank Allen and his motor boat; or, Racing to save a life" by Forbes, Graham B., readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as Frank Allen, a young and daring motorboat enthusiast, finds himself in a race against time to save a life. When a sudden and unexpected event puts someone's life in danger, Frank must rely on his skills, courage, and quick thinking to navigate treacherous waters and overcome various obstacles. As the tension rises, the story unfolds with heart-pounding action and unexpected twists, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Thriller

Themes: Courage, Heroism, Quick thinking, Perseverance
