Synopsis: In the fourteenth volume of "Historic Tales: The Romance of Reality," Sir Thomas Malory and Charles Morris delve deeper into the captivating legend of King Arthur. This installment continues the enthralling narrative of Arthur's reign, exploring his noble quests, the challenges he faces, and the enduring love that binds him to his queen, Guinevere. As the tale unfolds, readers will be transported to a world of chivalry, honor, and the eternal struggle between good and evil. Prepare to be swept away by the timeless allure of King Arthur's legendary saga.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Chivalry, Loyalty, Love, Good vs. Evil
