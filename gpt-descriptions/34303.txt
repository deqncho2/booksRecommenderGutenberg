Synopsis: In "A New Bat (Genus Myotis) From Mexico," Hall and Dalquest take readers on a captivating journey into the world of bats, unveiling the discovery of a previously unknown species in Mexico. As they delve into the scientific exploration and classification of this new bat, the authors provide fascinating insights into its unique characteristics and habitat. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this work offers a thrilling glimpse into the wonders of the natural world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Scientific literature, Natural history

Themes: Biodiversity, Species discovery, Wildlife conservation
