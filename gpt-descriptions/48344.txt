Synopsis: In "Benjamin of Ohio: A Story of the Settlement of Marietta," readers are transported to the early days of the American frontier, where Benjamin, a young and adventurous boy, embarks on a journey that will shape his destiny. Set against the backdrop of the settlement of Marietta, Ohio, this captivating tale follows Benjamin as he encounters Native American tribes, faces the challenges of survival in the wilderness, and forms unlikely friendships. As Benjamin navigates the untamed landscape, he discovers the true meaning of courage, resilience, and the power of community. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration and Discovery, Friendship, Cultural Exchange, Resilience
