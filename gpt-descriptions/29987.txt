Synopsis: In 'Join Our Gang?' by Lanier, Sterling E., a group of misfit teenagers stumble upon a mysterious invitation to join an exclusive secret society. Intrigued by the promise of adventure and belonging, they embark on a thrilling journey that takes them through hidden tunnels, ancient riddles, and unexpected dangers. As they navigate the challenges and uncover the truth behind the society, the teenagers must confront their own fears, forge unlikely alliances, and ultimately decide if the price of acceptance is worth the sacrifices they must make.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Belonging, Courage
