Synopsis: In "Who was the Commander at Bunker Hill?: With Remarks on Frothingham's History of the Battle," Samuel Swett delves into the historical mystery surrounding the Battle of Bunker Hill. With meticulous research and compelling arguments, Swett challenges the widely accepted account of the battle and raises intriguing questions about the true identity of the commander. As he unravels the complexities of this pivotal moment in American history, Swett takes readers on a captivating journey filled with surprising revelations and thought-provoking insights.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Historical revisionism, Leadership, Patriotism
