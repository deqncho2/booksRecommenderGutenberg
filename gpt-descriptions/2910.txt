Synopsis: In "The Little Dream: An Allegory in Six Scenes" by John Galsworthy, readers are transported to a dreamlike world where a young man named Jack embarks on a journey of self-discovery. As he encounters various characters and navigates through surreal landscapes, Jack's perceptions of reality and his own identity are challenged. With each scene, the boundaries between dreams and waking life blur, leaving readers captivated by the enigmatic nature of Jack's quest.

Genres: Allegory, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Identity, Perception, Reality, Self-discovery
