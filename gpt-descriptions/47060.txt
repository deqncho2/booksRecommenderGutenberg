Synopsis: 
In "Years of My Youth" by William Dean Howells, we are transported to a small New England town where we follow the life of a young protagonist named Bartley Hubbard. As Bartley navigates the challenges of love, ambition, and societal expectations, he finds himself torn between his desire for success and his longing for authenticity. With a cast of intriguing characters and a captivating narrative, this coming-of-age story explores the complexities of human nature and the pursuit of happiness in a rapidly changing world.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Realism

Themes: Ambition, Love, Authenticity, Societal Expectations
