Synopsis:
In the remote wilderness of the Canadian North, a young man named Glen embarks on a thrilling adventure that will test his courage, resilience, and love for the untamed land. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters dangerous wildlife, Glen must confront his own inner demons and discover the true meaning of survival. With every turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the breathtaking descriptions of the majestic wilderness and the heart-pounding moments of danger that await Glen in the high north.

Genres:
Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Exploration, Nature, Self-discovery
