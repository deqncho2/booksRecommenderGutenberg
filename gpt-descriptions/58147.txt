Synopsis: In 'Visits to Fields of Battle, in England, of the Fifteenth Century', Richard Brooke takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical battlefields of England. With vivid descriptions and meticulous attention to detail, Brooke transports us to the tumultuous fifteenth century, where we witness the remnants of epic conflicts and the echoes of long-forgotten warriors. Alongside these visits, the book also includes a collection of miscellaneous tracts and papers on archaeological subjects, adding further depth to the exploration of England's past. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Archaeology, Travelogue

Themes: History, Warfare, Archaeology, Cultural heritage
