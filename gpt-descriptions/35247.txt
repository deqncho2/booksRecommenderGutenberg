Synopsis: In 'That Affair at Elizabeth', a small town is shaken by a mysterious and scandalous event that threatens to unravel the tightly woven fabric of its community. As the truth slowly unravels, secrets are exposed, alliances are tested, and loyalties are questioned. With a cast of intriguing characters and a web of deceit, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Drama

Themes: Betrayal, Small-town dynamics, Secrets and lies
