Synopsis: 

In the 'American Red Cross Text-Book on Home Hygiene and Care of the Sick', the American National Red Cross provides a comprehensive guide to maintaining a healthy home environment and effectively caring for the sick. This essential resource covers a wide range of topics, including hygiene practices, nutrition, first aid, and the management of various illnesses. With practical advice and step-by-step instructions, this text-book equips readers with the knowledge and skills necessary to create a safe and nurturing environment for their loved ones.

Genres: Non-fiction, Health and Wellness, Reference

Themes: Home hygiene, Caregiving, Health education
