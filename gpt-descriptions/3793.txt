Synopsis: In "Joseph II. and His Court: An Historical Novel," Luise Mühlbach takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous court of Emperor Joseph II of Austria. Set in the 18th century, this gripping historical novel delves into the complex relationships, political intrigues, and personal struggles that shaped the reign of one of Europe's most enigmatic rulers. As Joseph II navigates the treacherous waters of power, he faces numerous challenges that test his ideals and force him to confront the consequences of his decisions. Amidst the opulence and grandeur of the court, secrets are unveiled, alliances are forged and broken, and love and betrayal intertwine in unexpected ways. With vivid storytelling and meticulous attention to historical detail, Mühlbach paints a vivid portrait of a ruler torn between his desire for reform and the constraints of tradition.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Romance

Themes: Power and Politics, Enlightenment and Reform, Love and Betrayal
