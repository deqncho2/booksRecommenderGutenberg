Synopsis: 

In "On the cattle plague: or, Contagious typhus in horned cattle. Its history, origin, description, and treatment," Bourguignon delves into the mysterious and devastating disease that has plagued cattle for centuries. With a meticulous examination of its history, origin, and description, the author uncovers the secrets behind this contagious typhus that has wreaked havoc on the livelihoods of farmers and the economy. Through his comprehensive research, Bourguignon also presents various treatment methods that have been employed throughout time, shedding light on the ongoing battle against this relentless plague. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Veterinary medicine, History

Themes: Animal diseases, Agricultural impact, Medical research
