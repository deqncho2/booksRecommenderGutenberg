Synopsis: 

In 'Stories about the Instinct of Animals, Their Characters, and Habits' by Thomas Bingley, embark on a captivating journey through the animal kingdom as the author delves into the fascinating world of animal instincts, characters, and habits. From the cunning strategies of predators to the remarkable intelligence of social animals, this collection of stories offers a glimpse into the remarkable lives of creatures both big and small. Discover the incredible survival skills of the animal kingdom and be amazed by the intricate web of behaviors that shape their lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Animal Behavior

Themes: Animal instincts, Survival strategies, Animal intelligence, Natural world exploration
