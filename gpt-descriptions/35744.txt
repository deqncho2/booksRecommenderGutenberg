Synopsis: In "The gradual acceptance of the Copernican theory of the universe," Dorothy Stimson takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period when the Copernican theory challenged the prevailing beliefs about the cosmos. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Stimson unveils the struggles, controversies, and triumphs that accompanied the gradual acceptance of this groundbreaking theory. From the initial skepticism to the eventual paradigm shift, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the intellectual and societal transformations that unfolded during this pivotal time in history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Scientific revolution, Paradigm shift, Intellectual progress
