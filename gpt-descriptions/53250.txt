Synopsis: In the heart of a children's hospital, a group of young patients form an extraordinary bond as they navigate the challenges of illness, friendship, and the bittersweet beauty of growing up. Set in the 1940s, 'The Golden Age' follows the lives of Frank and Elsa, two teenagers who find solace and hope in each other's company amidst the backdrop of a world at war. As they navigate the complexities of their own identities and the uncertainty of their futures, they discover the transformative power of love, resilience, and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Drama

Themes: Illness and Resilience, Friendship and Connection, Identity and Self-Discovery
