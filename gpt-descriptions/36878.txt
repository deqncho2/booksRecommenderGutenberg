Synopsis: 
In "Rambles by Land and Water; or, Notes of Travel in Cuba and Mexico," Benjamin Moore Norman takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the vibrant landscapes and captivating cultures of Cuba and Mexico. From the bustling streets of Havana to the ancient ruins of Chichen Itza, Norman's vivid descriptions and insightful observations paint a vivid picture of these enchanting destinations. As he encounters fascinating locals, explores hidden gems, and immerses himself in the rich history and traditions of these countries, the author's passion for adventure and discovery shines through every page. With a blend of humor, curiosity, and a deep appreciation for the beauty of the natural world, Norman invites readers to join him on this unforgettable exploration of two remarkable countries.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir
Themes: Cultural exploration, Natural beauty, Adventure, History
