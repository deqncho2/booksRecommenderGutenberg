Synopsis: In 'Cargoes for Crusoes' by Grant M. Overton, embark on a thrilling adventure as a group of castaways find themselves stranded on a deserted island. With limited resources and a desperate need for survival, they must rely on their ingenuity and teamwork to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. As they uncover the mysteries of the island and encounter unexpected dangers, the castaways must confront their own fears and discover the true meaning of resilience. Will they be able to find a way back home or will they be forever marooned on this treacherous island?

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Mystery

Themes: Resilience, Ingenuity, Teamwork
