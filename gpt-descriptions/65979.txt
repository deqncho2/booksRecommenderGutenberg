Synopsis: In 'The Incredible Life-Form' by Winston K. Marks, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything we know about life and the universe. As he delves deeper into his creation, he realizes that it possesses extraordinary abilities and potential, but also harbors a dark secret. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path of moral dilemmas and unforeseen consequences. This gripping tale explores the boundaries of science, the nature of existence, and the ethical implications of playing god.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Morality, Scientific Advancement, Existentialism
