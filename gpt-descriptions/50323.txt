Synopsis: In 'Dave Dashaway Around the World; or, A Young Yankee Aviator Among Many Nations', follow the thrilling adventures of Dave Dashaway, a young aviator with a passion for exploration and a knack for getting into trouble. When an opportunity arises to join a global race, Dave embarks on a whirlwind journey that takes him to exotic locations, introduces him to diverse cultures, and tests his skills as a pilot. From daring aerial stunts to unexpected encounters, Dave's determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test as he races against time and rivals from around the world.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
