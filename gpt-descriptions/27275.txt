Synopsis: 
"The Angel of Thought and Other Poems: Impressions from Old Masters" by Ethel Allen Murphy is a captivating collection of poems that takes readers on a journey through the vivid and enchanting world of Old Masters' paintings. Each poem is a unique impression, capturing the essence and emotions evoked by these timeless works of art. From the ethereal beauty of angels to the raw emotions of human subjects, Murphy's words breathe life into the canvases, inviting readers to see beyond the brushstrokes and delve into the depths of artistic expression.

Genres: Poetry, Art, Literary Criticism

Themes: Beauty, Emotion, Artistic Interpretation
