Synopsis: In 'Mary-'Gusta' by Joseph Crosby Lincoln, a young woman named Mary-'Gusta finds herself caught between tradition and her desire for independence in a small New England town. As she navigates the challenges of love, family, and societal expectations, Mary-'Gusta's journey becomes a captivating exploration of self-discovery and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Independence, Tradition, Love, Self-discovery
