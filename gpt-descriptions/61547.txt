Synopsis:
In 'The Undying Fire', H. G. Wells takes us on a captivating journey through time and space, as a young man named Harold Biffen discovers a mysterious and powerful force that could change the course of human history. As he delves deeper into this enigma, Biffen finds himself torn between the pursuit of knowledge and the consequences it may bring. With elements of science fiction, romance, and adventure, this contemporary novel explores the complexities of human ambition and the eternal quest for immortality.

Genres:
Science Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes:
Ambition, Immortality, Human Nature
