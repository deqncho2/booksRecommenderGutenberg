Synopsis: 

"The Wreck of the "Royal Charter"" is a gripping historical account that delves into the tragic events surrounding the sinking of the Royal Charter, a steam clipper ship, off the coast of Anglesey, Wales in 1859. Drawing from authentic sources and incorporating original material, author Frank Fowler paints a vivid picture of the ship's ill-fated voyage and the harrowing aftermath that ensued. As the story unfolds, readers are taken on a suspenseful journey filled with tales of bravery, survival, and the devastating power of nature. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, maritime literature, disaster narrative

Themes: Tragedy, resilience, human nature, the forces of nature
