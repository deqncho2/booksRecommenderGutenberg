Synopsis: In 'Contamination Crew' by Alan Edward Nourse, a team of highly skilled individuals known as the Contamination Crew is tasked with investigating and containing deadly outbreaks of unknown diseases. As they race against time to prevent the spread of these mysterious illnesses, they must navigate through treacherous environments and face unimaginable dangers. With each new case, the crew uncovers shocking secrets that challenge their understanding of the world and the very nature of life itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Disease outbreak, Medical ethics, Survival
