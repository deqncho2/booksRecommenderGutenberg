Synopsis: 
"The Female Soldier; Or, The Surprising Life and Adventures of Hannah Snell" follows the extraordinary journey of Hannah Snell, a young woman who defies societal expectations and disguises herself as a man to join the British army. As she navigates the perils of war, Hannah's courage and resilience are tested, leading her on a path of self-discovery and unexpected encounters. This captivating tale explores the complexities of identity, the pursuit of freedom, and the indomitable spirit of a woman determined to forge her own destiny.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Gender identity, Courage, Self-discovery, Freedom
