Synopsis: 
In "Baseball Joe on the School Nine; or, Pitching for the Blue Banner" by Chadwick, Lester, we follow the thrilling journey of Joe Matson, a talented young pitcher, as he joins his high school baseball team. With dreams of leading his team to victory and securing the coveted Blue Banner, Joe faces numerous challenges both on and off the field. From rivalries with other schools to personal setbacks, Joe must navigate the pressures of competition while staying true to himself and his love for the game. As the season unfolds, Joe's determination and skill are put to the test, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Sports fiction, Young adult fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Teamwork, Sportsmanship
