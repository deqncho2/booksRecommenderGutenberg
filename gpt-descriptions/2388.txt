Synopsis:
'The Song Celestial; Or, Bhagavad-Gîtâ' is a captivating philosophical dialogue between Arjuna, a prince of India, and the Supreme Being in the form of Krishna. As Arjuna faces a moral dilemma on the battlefield, Krishna imparts profound wisdom and guidance, revealing the secrets of life, duty, and the nature of the universe. This ancient text explores the depths of human existence and offers timeless insights into the complexities of the human condition.

Genres:
Philosophy, Religious Text, Epic Poetry

Themes:
Duty and Responsibility, Morality, Self-realization, Divine Guidance
