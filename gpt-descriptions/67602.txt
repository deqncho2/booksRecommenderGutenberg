Synopsis: In 'The Big Fix!' by Richard Wilson, a down-on-his-luck private investigator finds himself entangled in a web of corruption and deceit when he takes on a seemingly routine missing persons case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a dangerous conspiracy that reaches the highest levels of power. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous maze of lies and betrayal to uncover the truth and bring justice to those responsible.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Corruption, Betrayal, Justice
