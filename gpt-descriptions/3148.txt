Synopsis: In the small village of Ashbourne, a mysterious violin arrives, captivating the townspeople with its enchanting melodies. As the instrument changes hands, it brings both joy and turmoil to those who possess it, revealing the power of music to transform lives. Set against the backdrop of a quaint English village, 'A Village Stradivarius' weaves a tale of passion, ambition, and the enduring magic of music.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Music

Themes: Music and its transformative power, Pursuit of passion, Community and connection
