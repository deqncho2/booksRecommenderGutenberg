Synopsis: In 'Sandman's Goodnight Stories' by Abbie Phillips Walker, prepare to be whisked away to a world where dreams come alive and imagination knows no bounds. Follow the enchanting adventures of the Sandman as he weaves magical tales that will transport you to far-off lands, introduce you to extraordinary characters, and ignite your imagination. From talking animals to mythical creatures, each story is a delightful blend of whimsy and wonder that will captivate readers of all ages. Get ready to embark on a journey filled with bedtime stories that will leave you longing for just one more page.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Dreams, Storytelling
