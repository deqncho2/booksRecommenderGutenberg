Synopsis: In 'The Story of Ab: A Tale of the Time of the Cave Man' by Waterloo, Stanley, readers are transported back to the prehistoric era, where they follow the thrilling journey of Ab, a young cave man with an extraordinary destiny. Ab's life takes an unexpected turn when he encounters a mysterious object that holds the power to change his world forever. As he embarks on a perilous adventure, Ab must navigate treacherous landscapes, face dangerous creatures, and confront his own inner demons. This captivating tale explores the triumph of the human spirit, the pursuit of knowledge, and the timeless struggle between good and evil.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Coming of age, Survival, Quest for knowledge, Good vs. Evil
