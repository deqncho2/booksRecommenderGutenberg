Synopsis: In 'The Mayor's Wife' by Anna Katharine Green, a small town is thrown into chaos when the mayor's wife mysteriously disappears. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of the town's residents is exposed. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping mystery will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town politics
