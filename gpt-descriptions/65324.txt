Synopsis: In 'The Old Way' by Stephen Marlowe, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the past, he becomes entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and hidden agendas. With each step closer to the truth, he must confront his own demons and make choices that will shape the course of history. This gripping tale of adventure and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Archaeology, Betrayal, Redemption
