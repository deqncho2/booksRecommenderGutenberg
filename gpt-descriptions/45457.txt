Synopsis: Linda Carlton's Island Adventure follows the thrilling journey of a young girl named Linda, who finds herself stranded on a mysterious island after a shipwreck. As she navigates the unfamiliar terrain, Linda encounters a cast of intriguing characters and uncovers hidden secrets that will test her courage and resourcefulness. With danger lurking around every corner, Linda must rely on her wits and determination to survive and find a way back home.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Discovery
