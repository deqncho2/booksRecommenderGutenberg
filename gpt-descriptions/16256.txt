Synopsis: In "The Psychology of Management," Lillian Moller Gilbreth delves into the fascinating realm of human behavior and its impact on effective management. Drawing from her extensive research and experience, Gilbreth explores the intricate workings of the mind and how it influences decision-making, teaching methods, and the reduction of waste in various industries. Through captivating anecdotes and insightful analysis, she uncovers the hidden psychological factors that shape our approach to management and offers valuable strategies for optimizing productivity and efficiency. This thought-provoking exploration of the human mind in the context of management is sure to captivate readers and provide them with a fresh perspective on the art of leadership.

Genres: Psychology, Management, Business

Themes: Human behavior, Decision-making, Efficiency, Productivity
