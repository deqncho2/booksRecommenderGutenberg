Synopsis: 
"The Tragic Story of the Empress of Ireland, and Other Great Sea Disasters" by Marshall, Logan is a gripping account of the ill-fated voyage of the Empress of Ireland and other tragic maritime incidents. From the chilling details of the ship's sinking to the heroic tales of survival, this book takes readers on a heart-wrenching journey through some of history's most devastating sea disasters. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the author brings to life the harrowing experiences of those onboard, painting a haunting picture of the unforgiving power of the sea.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Maritime

Themes: Tragedy, Survival, Human Resilience
