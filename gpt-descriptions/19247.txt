Synopsis: 
In 'Dotty Dimple's Flyaway' by Sophie May, young Dotty Dimple embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes her far from home. When a mischievous bird steals her bonnet, Dotty finds herself chasing after it, leading her on a whimsical journey through unexpected places and encounters with peculiar characters. As she navigates through this enchanting escapade, Dotty learns valuable lessons about bravery, friendship, and the importance of embracing the unknown.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Bravery, Friendship, Exploration
