Synopsis: "Wee Tim'rous Beasties: Studies of Animal life and Character" takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of animals. Through a series of engaging and insightful studies, English Douglas explores the lives and behaviors of various creatures, revealing their unique personalities and characteristics. From the smallest insects to the largest mammals, this book offers a delightful exploration of the animal kingdom, shedding light on their intricate lives and the lessons they can teach us.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural history, Animal studies

Themes: Animal behavior, Nature's wonders, Interconnectedness of life
