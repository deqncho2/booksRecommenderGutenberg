Synopsis: 
In 'Grace Harlowe's Overland Riders in the Yellowstone National Park', Grace and her adventurous friends embark on a thrilling journey through the breathtaking landscapes of Yellowstone National Park. As they navigate treacherous trails, encounter wild animals, and uncover hidden secrets, they find themselves caught up in a dangerous mystery that could jeopardize their lives. With their courage and determination, the Overland Riders must unravel the enigma before it's too late. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
