Synopsis: 
'The Flower Beneath the Foot: Being a record of the early life of St. Laura de Nazianzi and the times in which she lived' by Ronald Firbank is a captivating novel that delves into the intriguing life of St. Laura de Nazianzi. Set in a time of great change and upheaval, the story follows St. Laura's journey from her humble beginnings to her eventual rise as a revered saint. Through vivid and poetic prose, Firbank paints a vivid picture of the world in which St. Laura lived, filled with political intrigue, religious fervor, and personal struggles. As the narrative unfolds, readers are drawn into a world of passion, devotion, and the complexities of faith, as St. Laura navigates the challenges and triumphs of her extraordinary life.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Religious Fiction

Themes: Faith, Identity, Power, Love, Sacrifice
