Synopsis: In 'The wrong letter' by Walter S. Masterman, a seemingly innocent mistake sets off a chain of events that unravels the lives of two strangers. When a letter meant for someone else ends up in the hands of a young woman named Emily, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and deception. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Emily discovers that the consequences of this mix-up are far more sinister than she could have ever imagined. With danger lurking around every corner, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect herself from those who will stop at nothing to keep it hidden.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Mistaken identity, Secrets and deception, Danger and survival
