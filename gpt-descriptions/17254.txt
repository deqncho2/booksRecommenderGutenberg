Synopsis: 
"The Slant Book" by Peter Newell is a delightful children's picture book that takes readers on a whimsical journey through a series of humorous and unexpected events. The story follows a young boy and his adventures with a unique mode of transportation, a slant board. As the boy travels through various landscapes, encountering amusing obstacles along the way, the book's clever design adds an extra layer of fun and surprise. With its imaginative illustrations and playful narrative, "The Slant Book" is sure to captivate young readers and leave them eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Children's literature, Picture book

Themes: Adventure, Imagination, Humor
