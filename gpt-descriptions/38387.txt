Synopsis: In 'Library Bookbinding' by Arthur Low Bailey, a young bookbinder named Emily discovers a hidden secret within the pages of an ancient book she is restoring. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, uncovering a centuries-old conspiracy that could change the course of history. With each turn of the page, Emily must navigate through hidden codes, cryptic clues, and unexpected alliances to unravel the truth before it's too late. This thrilling tale of suspense and discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last chapter.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden knowledge, the power of books, the pursuit of truth
