Synopsis: In "Lee and Longstreet at High Tide: Gettysburg in the Light of the Official Records," Helen Dortch Longstreet delves into the pivotal Battle of Gettysburg, offering a fresh perspective on the events that unfolded during those fateful days. Drawing from official records and personal accounts, Longstreet provides a captivating narrative that sheds light on the decisions made by General Robert E. Lee and General James Longstreet, revealing the complexities and challenges they faced. As the battle reaches its climax, the reader is taken on a gripping journey through the minds of these two military leaders, exploring their strategies, conflicts, and the consequences of their actions. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Leadership, Decision-making, Conflict, Consequences
