Synopsis: In "Man a Machine," a groundbreaking philosophical treatise, La Mettrie challenges traditional notions of human nature by arguing that humans are essentially complex machines. Through a series of thought-provoking arguments and examples, he explores the intricate workings of the human body and mind, delving into topics such as consciousness, emotions, and free will. As he dismantles the idea of a soul separate from the body, La Mettrie presents a bold and controversial perspective on what it means to be human.

Genres: Philosophy, Science, Enlightenment Literature

Themes: Human nature, Materialism, Determinism, Mind-body relationship
