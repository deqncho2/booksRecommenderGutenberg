Synopsis:
In the second volume of 'John Marchmont's Legacy', the story takes a thrilling turn as the secrets of the past begin to unravel. Lady Eversleigh, the enigmatic and captivating widow, finds herself entangled in a web of deceit and danger. As she delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding her late husband's legacy, she discovers shocking revelations that threaten to shatter her world. With unexpected alliances and unexpected enemies, Lady Eversleigh must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the truth and protect those she holds dear.

Genres:
Gothic Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes:
Inheritance, Deception, Betrayal, Love and Loss
