Synopsis: In 'Owen's Fortune; Or, "Durable Riches"', the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the life of Owen, a young man who unexpectedly inherits a vast fortune. As he navigates the complexities of newfound wealth, Owen's character is tested, and he must confront the true meaning of happiness and fulfillment. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century England, this novel explores the transformative power of wealth and the choices one must make when faced with life-altering circumstances.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Wealth and Happiness, Identity and Self-Discovery, Love and Relationships
