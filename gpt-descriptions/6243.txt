Synopsis: In 'The Right of Way - Volume 01', we are introduced to the enigmatic character of Jean Jacques Barbille, a man with a mysterious past and a reputation that precedes him. Set in the picturesque Canadian countryside, this gripping tale follows Barbille as he navigates through a web of secrets, love, and redemption. As his path intertwines with a diverse cast of characters, including a young priest, a beautiful widow, and a troubled artist, Barbille's journey becomes a captivating exploration of human nature and the choices we make in the face of adversity.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Love and Loss, Morality
