Synopsis: 
'At the back of the world: Wanderings over many lands and seas' takes readers on an extraordinary journey through various exotic locations, as experienced by the adventurous duo, George and Jennie Pugh. From the bustling streets of bustling cities to the remote corners of untouched wilderness, the Pughs encounter captivating cultures, breathtaking landscapes, and unexpected encounters. As they navigate through the unknown, their travels become a testament to the indomitable human spirit and the power of exploration.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Human resilience
