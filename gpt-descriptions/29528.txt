Synopsis: In "The Camp Fire Girls in the Mountains; Or, Bessie King's Strange Adventure" by Jane L. Stewart, Bessie King finds herself embarking on a thrilling journey with her fellow Camp Fire Girls. As they venture into the majestic mountains, they encounter unexpected challenges and mysteries that test their courage and resourcefulness. Bessie's strange adventure takes her on a path filled with danger, friendship, and self-discovery. Will she be able to overcome the obstacles that lie ahead and find her way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Self-discovery
