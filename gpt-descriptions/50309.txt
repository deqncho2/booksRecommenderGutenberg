Synopsis: In 'Dave Dawson in Libya' by Robert Sidney Bowen, young Dave Dawson finds himself thrust into the heart of World War II as he embarks on a dangerous mission in the deserts of Libya. As he navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters enemy forces, Dave must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to survive. With high-stakes action and unexpected twists, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Survival, Patriotism
