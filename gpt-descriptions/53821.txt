Synopsis:
In 'Julius, the Street Boy; or, Out West' by Horatio Alger, Jr., we follow the journey of Julius, a young boy who finds himself alone and homeless on the bustling streets of New York City. Determined to escape his harsh reality, Julius embarks on a daring adventure that takes him far away from the city and into the untamed wilderness of the American West. Along the way, he encounters a cast of colorful characters, faces numerous challenges, and discovers the true meaning of friendship and perseverance. Will Julius be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way and find a place to call home?

Genres:
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Perseverance, Friendship, Social mobility
