Synopsis: In 'The Book of All-Power' by Wallace, Edgar, a mysterious ancient book falls into the hands of a young scholar, setting off a thrilling adventure that spans continents and centuries. As he delves deeper into the book's secrets, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and deception, where the line between good and evil blurs. With each turn of the page, the protagonist uncovers hidden knowledge that could change the course of history forever. Will he unlock the book's true power or succumb to its dark allure?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power and its consequences, the pursuit of knowledge, the battle between good and evil
