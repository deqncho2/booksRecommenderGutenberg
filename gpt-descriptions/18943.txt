Synopsis: In "Tom Slade at Black Lake," Tom finds himself in a mysterious and thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. With his trusty friends, he embarks on a journey filled with danger, secrets, and unexpected twists. As they navigate through the treacherous Black Lake, Tom's courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will they uncover the hidden treasure, or will they fall victim to the perils that await them?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
