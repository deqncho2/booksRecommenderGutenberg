Synopsis: In "Socialism, Revolution and Internationalism," Gabriel Pierre Deville takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous history of socialism, exploring the profound impact of revolutionary movements and the power of international solidarity. From the fiery streets of Paris during the French Revolution to the rise of socialist ideologies across the globe, Deville delves into the complex interplay between social, political, and economic forces that have shaped the world we live in today. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, this thought-provoking work sheds light on the struggles, triumphs, and enduring ideals of socialism, leaving readers inspired to question the status quo and envision a more equitable future.

Genres: History, Political Science, Sociology

Themes: Socialism, Revolution, Internationalism, Ideology, Equality, Solidarity
