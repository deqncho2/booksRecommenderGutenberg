Synopsis: 
In 'The Prodigal Son' by Caine, Hall, Sir, a young man named James embarks on a journey of self-discovery and redemption after a series of unfortunate events. Struggling with his past mistakes and haunted by the consequences of his actions, James finds himself torn between the allure of his old life and the desire for a fresh start. As he navigates through a world filled with secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, James must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will ultimately shape his future. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Morality
