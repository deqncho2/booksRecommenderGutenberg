Synopsis: In 'The Englishing of French Words; the Dialectal Words in Blunden's Poems: Society for Pure English, Tract 05', the Society for Pure English delves into the fascinating world of language and poetry. Exploring the influence of French words and dialectal expressions in Edmund Blunden's poems, this tract offers a captivating analysis of the English language's evolution. Through meticulous research and insightful observations, the Society sheds light on the intricate relationship between languages and the impact they have on literature. This thought-provoking work invites readers to embark on a linguistic journey that unveils the hidden layers of meaning within poetry.

Genres: Linguistics, Literary Criticism, Poetry Analysis
Themes: Language evolution, Cultural influence, Literary interpretation
