Synopsis: In "The Young Ship-Builders of Elm Island" by Elijah Kellogg, a group of adventurous boys on a remote island embark on a thrilling journey of friendship, perseverance, and discovery. As they learn the art of ship-building from a wise old sailor, they face unexpected challenges and uncover hidden secrets that will test their courage and determination. With their newfound skills and unwavering bond, these young ship-builders set sail on a remarkable adventure that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical fiction

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Discovery
