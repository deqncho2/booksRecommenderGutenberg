Synopsis: 
In 'In Pawn' by Butler, Ellis Parker, a young man named Jack finds himself in a desperate situation when he pawns his most prized possession to make ends meet. Little does he know that this seemingly innocent transaction will set off a chain of events that will test his character and lead him down a dangerous path. As Jack navigates the treacherous world of pawnshops, he must confront his own moral dilemmas and make difficult choices that will determine his fate. With unexpected twists and turns, 'In Pawn' is a gripping tale of desperation, redemption, and the consequences of our actions.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Consequences of Actions, Redemption
