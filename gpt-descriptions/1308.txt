Synopsis: In 'A Florentine Tragedy; La Sainte Courtisane', Oscar Wilde takes us on a captivating journey through the streets of Florence, where a passionate love triangle unfolds. As the characters navigate the complexities of desire, power, and morality, their lives become entangled in a web of secrets and hidden motives. With Wilde's signature wit and poetic language, this gripping tale explores the depths of human nature and the consequences of our most forbidden desires.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Morality and Ethics, Power and Manipulation
