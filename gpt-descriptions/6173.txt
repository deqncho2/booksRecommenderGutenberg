Synopsis: 
"The Note-Books of Samuel Butler" takes readers on a captivating journey through the mind of the renowned Victorian author, Samuel Butler. This collection of his personal notebooks offers a unique glimpse into his thoughts, observations, and musings on a wide range of subjects. From art and literature to science and philosophy, Butler's sharp wit and insightful commentary provide a thought-provoking exploration of the world around him. As readers delve into the pages of these notebooks, they will be captivated by Butler's intellectual curiosity and his ability to challenge conventional wisdom. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Autobiography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Intellectual curiosity, Observations on society, Art and literature, Science and philosophy
