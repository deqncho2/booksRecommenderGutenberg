Synopsis: 

In 'The Story of the Living Machine: A Review of the Conclusions of Modern Biology in Regard to the Mechanism Which Controls the Phenomena of Living Activity', H.W. Conn takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate workings of living organisms. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for unraveling the mysteries of life, Conn explores the fascinating mechanisms that govern the activities of living beings. From the smallest cells to complex organisms, he delves into the inner workings of life, shedding light on the remarkable processes that sustain and drive living creatures. 

Genres: Science, Biology, Non-fiction

Themes: Mechanisms of life, Biological processes, Complexity of living organisms
