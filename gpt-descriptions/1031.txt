Synopsis: 
'Charmides, and Other Poems' is a captivating collection of poetry by Oscar Wilde that takes readers on a journey through various emotions and experiences. From the enchanting tale of Charmides to the exploration of love, beauty, and the human condition, each poem offers a unique perspective and a glimpse into the depths of Wilde's poetic genius. With its lyrical language and thought-provoking imagery, this collection is sure to captivate and inspire readers.

Genres: 
Poetry, Classic Literature, Romanticism

Themes: 
Love, Beauty, Human Condition, Morality
