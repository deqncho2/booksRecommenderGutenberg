Synopsis: 
In 'Gleanings in Buddha-Fields: Studies of Hand and Soul in the Far East', Lafcadio Hearn takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and vibrant cultures of the Far East. Through a series of beautifully written essays, Hearn delves into the intricate details of everyday life, exploring the profound connections between the physical world and the spiritual realm. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the serene temples of Kyoto, he uncovers the hidden beauty and wisdom that lie within the simplest of experiences. With his keen observations and poetic prose, Hearn invites readers to immerse themselves in the captivating tapestry of Eastern traditions and beliefs.

Genres: Travel literature, Cultural studies, Philosophy

Themes: Spirituality, Cultural exploration, Connection between the physical and spiritual realms
