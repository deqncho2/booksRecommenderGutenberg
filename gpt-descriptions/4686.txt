Synopsis: 
'Underground: Hacking, madness and obsession on the electronic frontier' takes readers on a thrilling journey into the hidden world of hackers, where boundaries blur between right and wrong, and the pursuit of knowledge becomes an all-consuming obsession. Through a series of captivating true stories, the book explores the lives of underground hackers and their audacious exploits, revealing the exhilarating highs and devastating lows of their clandestine activities. From the dark corners of the internet to the heart of global power structures, this gripping narrative sheds light on the untold stories of those who dared to challenge the status quo, ultimately raising profound questions about the nature of freedom, privacy, and the power of information.

Genres: Non-fiction, True Crime, Technology

Themes: Hacking, Obsession, Ethics, Power, Privacy
