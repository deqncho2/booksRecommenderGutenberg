Synopsis: 

'Nil Darpan; or, The Indigo Planting Mirror, A Drama.' is a captivating Bengali play translated by Dinabandhu Mitra. Set in colonial India during the 19th century, the story revolves around the exploitation and oppression faced by indigo farmers under the British rule. The play follows the journey of a young indigo farmer who becomes the voice of his community, exposing the harsh realities they endure. As the protagonist fights for justice and freedom, the narrative delves into the complexities of power dynamics, societal injustice, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Colonialism, Oppression, Social Justice
