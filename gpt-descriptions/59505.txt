Synopsis: In 'Sales Resistance' by Henry Still, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of deception and intrigue when he stumbles upon a mysterious sales pitch that promises to change his life forever. As he delves deeper into the world of salesmanship, he discovers that nothing is as it seems, and he must navigate a dangerous game of manipulation and persuasion to uncover the truth. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Manipulation, Identity
