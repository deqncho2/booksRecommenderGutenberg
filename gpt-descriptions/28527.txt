Synopsis: In "The Story of Nathan Hale" by Carlton, Henry Fisk, a young and ambitious schoolteacher named Nathan Hale finds himself caught in the midst of the American Revolution. As the war intensifies, Nathan is faced with a life-altering decision that will test his loyalty, courage, and patriotism. With danger lurking at every turn, he embarks on a perilous journey that will forever change the course of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Courage, Loyalty
