Synopsis: In 'Make or Break; or, The Rich Man's Daughter' by Optic, Oliver, a young woman named Grace finds herself torn between love and duty when she is forced to choose between two suitors from vastly different backgrounds. As she navigates the complexities of societal expectations and personal desires, Grace must confront the consequences of her decisions and ultimately discover what truly matters in life.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Social class and expectations, Self-discovery
