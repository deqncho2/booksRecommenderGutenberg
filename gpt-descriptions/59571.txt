Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Robert Burns' is a comprehensive collection of the literary works of the renowned Scottish poet, Robert Burns. This anthology includes a wide range of his poems, songs, and letters, showcasing his lyrical genius and his deep understanding of human emotions. From heartfelt love ballads to thought-provoking social commentaries, Burns' works capture the essence of life, love, and the struggles of the common man. This index serves as a gateway to explore the rich and diverse literary legacy of one of Scotland's most celebrated poets.

Genres: Poetry, Songwriting, Letters
Themes: Love, Nature, Social Commentary, Scottish Identity
