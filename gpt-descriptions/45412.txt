Synopsis: In "Broke," The Man Without the Dime, we follow the captivating journey of a man who finds himself stripped of everything he once had. From the bustling streets of New York City to the desolate landscapes of the American West, he embarks on a quest for redemption, searching for a way to rebuild his life from scratch. As he encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own struggles and dreams, he learns valuable lessons about the true meaning of wealth and the power of human connection. This poignant and thought-provoking tale explores the depths of despair and the resilience of the human spirit, leaving readers questioning their own definitions of success and happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Adventure

Themes: Redemption, Loss, Resilience, Human Connection
