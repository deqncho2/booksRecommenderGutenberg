Synopsis: In 'The Field of Ice: Part II of the Adventures of Captain Hatteras' by Jules Verne, the intrepid Captain Hatteras and his crew embark on a perilous journey to the Arctic, facing treacherous conditions and unexpected challenges. As they navigate through the icy landscape, they encounter mysterious phenomena and encounter a rival expedition, leading to a thrilling battle for survival. Will Captain Hatteras and his crew conquer the harsh Arctic environment, or will they succumb to its unforgiving nature?

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Rivalry, Survival
