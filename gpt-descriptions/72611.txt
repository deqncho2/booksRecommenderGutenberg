Synopsis: In 'Black April' by Julia Peterkin, a small Southern town is shaken by a mysterious event that leaves the community in turmoil. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, the lives of the townspeople become intertwined in unexpected ways. With vivid descriptions and complex characters, this gripping novel explores the dark underbelly of a seemingly idyllic town, where nothing is as it seems and everyone has something to hide.

Genres: Mystery, Southern Gothic, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Betrayal, Identity, Redemption
