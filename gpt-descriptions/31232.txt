Synopsis: In 'History of Morgan's Cavalry', Basil Wilson Duke takes us on a thrilling journey through the daring exploits of one of the most renowned cavalry units of the American Civil War. From their audacious raids behind enemy lines to their relentless pursuit of victory, Duke vividly recounts the exhilarating adventures and the personal sacrifices made by these fearless soldiers. As the battles unfold, the reader is immersed in the heart-pounding action and the strategic brilliance of Morgan's Cavalry, making this a captivating account of bravery, loyalty, and the indomitable spirit of the men who fought for their cause.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Bravery, Loyalty, Strategy, Sacrifice
