Synopsis: In "A Parody on 'Mary's Ghost; or, The Doctors and Body-snatchers,'" Thomas Hood presents a humorous and satirical take on the popular ballad "Mary's Ghost." The story follows the misadventures of a group of doctors and body-snatchers as they encounter a ghostly apparition. With clever wordplay and witty observations, Hood weaves a tale that will keep readers entertained and laughing throughout.

Genres: Parody, Satire, Humor

Themes: Death and the macabre, Social commentary
