Synopsis: 
Step back in time and immerse yourself in the captivating world of ancient Egypt. In "The Pharaohs and Their People: Scenes of old Egyptian life and history," author E. Berkley takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the rich tapestry of Egyptian civilization. From the bustling streets of Cairo to the majestic pyramids of Giza, this book unveils the hidden stories and secrets of the pharaohs and their people. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Berkley brings to life the daily rituals, cultural traditions, and monumental achievements of this extraordinary civilization. Discover the enigmatic allure of the Nile, the mysticism of the gods, and the awe-inspiring wonders of ancient Egypt in this captivating exploration of a bygone era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Archaeology, Ancient civilizations

Themes: Ancient Egyptian culture, Pharaohs and rulers, Daily life in ancient Egypt, Religion and mythology, Architectural wonders, Cultural traditions
