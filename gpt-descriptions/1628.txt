Synopsis: 
In 'My Lady's Money' by Wilkie Collins, a young woman named Isabel inherits a large sum of money from her late aunt. However, she soon discovers that her newfound wealth comes with a peculiar condition attached. As Isabel navigates the complexities of her aunt's will, she becomes entangled in a web of mystery, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With her fortune at stake, Isabel must unravel the truth behind her aunt's intentions and confront the dark secrets that lie hidden within her family's past.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Victorian Literature

Themes: Inheritance, Deception, Family Secrets
