Synopsis:
In "The Ink-Stain (Tache d'encre) — Complete" by Bazin, René, a mysterious ink stain appears on a young man's shirt, leading him on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected encounters. As he tries to unravel the meaning behind the stain, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and secrets that will challenge his perception of reality. With each clue he uncovers, the protagonist delves deeper into a world where nothing is as it seems, and the line between imagination and truth becomes blurred. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic ink stain.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Literary Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Reality vs. Illusion
