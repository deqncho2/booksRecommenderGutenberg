Synopsis: In 'The Anglican Friar, and the Fish which he Took by Hook and by Crook: A Comic Legend', Novice takes readers on a hilarious journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. The story revolves around an Anglican friar who embarks on a fishing adventure, encountering a series of comical mishaps along the way. As the friar's determination to catch a fish grows, so does the chaos that ensues. With a blend of wit, satire, and absurdity, Novice crafts a tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what hilarious predicament the friar will find himself in next.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Adventure

Themes: Perseverance, Absurdity, Human Foibles
