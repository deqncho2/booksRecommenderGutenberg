Synopsis: 

"The Story of Paper-making: An account of paper-making from its earliest known record down to the present time" by Frank O. Butler takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of paper-making. From ancient civilizations to modern techniques, this book explores the evolution of this essential medium, revealing the ingenuity and craftsmanship behind its creation. Discover the secrets of ancient paper-making methods, the impact of paper on society, and the innovations that have shaped its production over time. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Craft

Themes: Innovation, Cultural Impact, Technological Advancement
