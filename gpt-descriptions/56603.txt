Synopsis: 
'A Boy in the Peninsular War: The Services, Adventures and Experiences of Robert Blakeney' follows the gripping journey of young Robert Blakeney as he is thrust into the chaos and brutality of the Peninsular War. From his humble beginnings as a boy soldier, Robert navigates through the treacherous landscapes of Spain and Portugal, facing the horrors of battle, forging unlikely friendships, and discovering his own strength and resilience. As he witnesses the devastating consequences of war and grapples with his own moral dilemmas, Robert's coming-of-age story unfolds against the backdrop of one of history's most tumultuous conflicts.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, Friendship and camaraderie, Morality and ethical choices
