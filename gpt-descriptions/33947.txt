Synopsis: In 'The Nerve of Foley, and Other Railroad Stories' by Frank H. Spearman, embark on a thrilling journey through the world of railroads, where danger lurks around every bend and the courage of the men who run the trains is put to the ultimate test. From heart-stopping accidents to daring rescues, this collection of captivating stories will transport readers into the heart of the railroad industry, where the stakes are high and the adrenaline never stops flowing.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Danger, Camaraderie
