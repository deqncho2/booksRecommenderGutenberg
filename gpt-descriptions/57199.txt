Synopsis: In 'Mark Tidd's Citadel' by Clarence Budington Kelland, a group of adventurous boys stumble upon an abandoned house that holds a mysterious secret. As they embark on a quest to uncover the truth, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns. With their friendship and resourcefulness tested, the boys must rely on their wits and courage to solve the enigma of the citadel before it's too late.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
