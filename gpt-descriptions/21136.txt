Synopsis: In "For Fortune and Glory: A Story of the Soudan War," author Lewis Hough takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous landscapes of Sudan during the late 19th century. The story follows a group of courageous individuals who find themselves caught in the midst of a conflict between British forces and the formidable Mahdist army. As they navigate the harsh realities of war, the characters must confront their own fears, forge unlikely alliances, and make sacrifices in the pursuit of honor and glory.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Honor, Sacrifice, Survival
