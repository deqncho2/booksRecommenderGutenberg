Synopsis: 

In 'The Second Battalion Royal Dublin Fusiliers in the South African War: With a Description of the Operations in the Aden Hinterland', authors Mainwaring and Romer take readers on a gripping journey through the intense and harrowing experiences of the Second Battalion Royal Dublin Fusiliers during the South African War. From the adrenaline-fueled battles on the frontlines to the treacherous conditions in the Aden Hinterland, this book offers a vivid and captivating account of the battalion's heroic efforts and the challenges they faced.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War memoir

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Colonialism
