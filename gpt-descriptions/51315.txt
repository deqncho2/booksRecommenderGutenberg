Synopsis: In 'How? or, Spare Hours Made Profitable for Boys and Girls', Holbrook Kennedy presents a captivating collection of engaging activities and practical knowledge that will keep young readers entertained and educated. From learning how to make useful crafts to exploring the wonders of nature, this book offers a wealth of opportunities for children to make the most of their spare time. With step-by-step instructions and fascinating anecdotes, 'How?' encourages curiosity, creativity, and a sense of accomplishment in its young readers.

Genres: Educational, Activity, Non-fiction

Themes: Curiosity, Creativity, Skill-building
