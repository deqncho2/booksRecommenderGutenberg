Synopsis: 

In 'Dolæus upon the cure of the gout by milk-diet: To which is prefixed, an essay upon diet', Doläus and Stephens delve into the fascinating world of gout treatment through a unique and unexpected method: milk-diet. As they explore the potential benefits and drawbacks of this unconventional approach, they also provide an insightful essay on the importance of diet in maintaining overall health. With a blend of scientific research and historical anecdotes, this work offers a captivating exploration of a lesser-known remedy for a common ailment.

Genres: Medical literature, Historical non-fiction

Themes: Alternative medicine, Diet and nutrition, Health and wellness
