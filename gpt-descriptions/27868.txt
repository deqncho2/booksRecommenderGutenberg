Synopsis: In 'The Glow-Worm and Other Beetles' by Jean-Henri Fabre, embark on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of insects. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Fabre introduces us to the enchanting glow-worm and a myriad of other beetles, each with their own unique characteristics and behaviors. From the industrious dung beetle to the cunning tiger beetle, this book offers a delightful exploration of the insect kingdom, revealing the hidden wonders that lie beneath our feet.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Science

Themes: Nature's intricacies, Insect behavior, Curiosity and exploration
