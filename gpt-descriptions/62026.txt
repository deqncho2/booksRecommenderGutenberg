Synopsis: In "A Yankee Girl at Antietam" by Alice Turner Curtis, follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Sylvia as she finds herself caught in the midst of the historic Battle of Antietam during the American Civil War. As she navigates the chaos and danger surrounding her, Sylvia's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to survive the horrors of war and find her way back home?

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Identity, War
