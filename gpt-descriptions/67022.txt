Synopsis: 
'Lyrics & Legends of Christmas-Tide' by Scollard, Clinton is a captivating collection of enchanting poems and stories that transport readers into the magical world of Christmas. From heartwarming tales of love and friendship to whimsical adventures with Santa Claus and his reindeer, this book is a delightful celebration of the holiday season. With lyrical verses and captivating narratives, Scollard weaves together a tapestry of Christmas traditions and folklore that will leave readers filled with joy and wonder.

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Christmas, Tradition, Magic, Love, Friendship
