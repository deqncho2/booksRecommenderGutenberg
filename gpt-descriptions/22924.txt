Synopsis: In 'Pathfinder; or, The Missing Tenderfoot' by Douglas, Alan, Captain, a thrilling adventure unfolds as a group of explorers embark on a treacherous journey through uncharted territories. When a young and inexperienced member of the team goes missing, the remaining members must navigate through dangerous obstacles and face unexpected challenges to find their lost companion. As they delve deeper into the unknown, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of courage is put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Friendship, Bravery
