Synopsis: In 'What Need of Man?' by Harold Calin, a mysterious artifact is discovered in the depths of the Amazon rainforest, setting off a thrilling adventure that spans continents and centuries. As a team of archaeologists and scientists race against time to unlock the secrets of this ancient relic, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each new revelation, the boundaries of science and spirituality blur, leaving the characters questioning the very essence of humanity and the purpose of our existence.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Science Fiction

Themes: Exploration of human nature, the intersection of science and spirituality, the search for meaning and purpose in life
