Synopsis: In "The Connexion Between Taste and Morals: Two lectures," Mark Hopkins delves into the intriguing relationship between our aesthetic preferences and our moral values. Through thought-provoking lectures, he explores how our taste in art, literature, and music can shape our ethical judgments and influence our behavior. Hopkins challenges conventional notions and offers a fresh perspective on the profound impact that our aesthetic choices can have on our moral compass.

Genres: Philosophy, Ethics, Aesthetics

Themes: Aesthetic judgment, Moral values, Influence of art, Relationship between taste and behavior
