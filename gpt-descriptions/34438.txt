Synopsis: In 'The Troubles of Biddy: A Pretty Little Story', we follow the journey of Biddy, a young woman with a heart full of dreams and a spirit that refuses to be tamed. Set in the enchanting backdrop of a small village, Biddy's life takes unexpected turns as she navigates through love, loss, and the pursuit of her true self. With colorful characters and captivating twists, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within Biddy's troubled world.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Love and Loss
