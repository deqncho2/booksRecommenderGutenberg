Synopsis: In 'Tempest and Sunshine' by Mary Jane Holmes, two young girls, one fiery and impulsive, the other gentle and reserved, find themselves thrown together in a tumultuous journey through love, loss, and the pursuit of happiness. As they navigate the challenges of family, society, and their own personal desires, their bond is tested, secrets are revealed, and their lives are forever changed. Will they find the strength to weather the storms that come their way, or will they be swept away by the tempests of life?

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and friendship, societal expectations, personal growth
