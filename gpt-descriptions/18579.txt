Synopsis: In 'Taken by the Enemy' by Optic, Oliver, a young boy named Frank is unexpectedly thrust into the midst of a thrilling adventure when he finds himself captured by the enemy during the Civil War. As he navigates the dangerous world of espionage and secret missions, Frank must rely on his wit and courage to survive and make it back home. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Frank's capture and his ultimate fate.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Survival, Loyalty
