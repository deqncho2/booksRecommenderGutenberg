Synopsis: 

In Acton's Feud: A Public School Story, the reader is transported to the prestigious world of a British public school, where a bitter rivalry between two students takes center stage. As tensions rise and the feud escalates, the story delves into the complex dynamics of friendship, loyalty, and the pursuit of personal identity. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the lengths to which individuals will go to protect their pride and reputation, ultimately leading to a dramatic climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-Age, School Drama

Themes: Friendship, Rivalry, Identity, Loyalty
