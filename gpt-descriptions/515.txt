Synopsis: In 'Margret Howth: A Story of To-day', we are introduced to Margret, a young woman living in a small New England town. Struggling to make ends meet, she takes a job as a nursemaid for a wealthy family. As she becomes entangled in the complexities of their lives, Margret must navigate the boundaries of class, love, and personal identity. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel explores the challenges and sacrifices faced by individuals in a rapidly changing society.

Genres: Fiction, Realism, Social Commentary

Themes: Class struggle, Identity, Love and sacrifice
