Synopsis: In the vibrant city of Florence during the Renaissance, a young woman named Romola finds herself torn between duty and desire. As she navigates the complexities of love, politics, and personal growth, Romola becomes entangled in a web of secrets and betrayal that will test her strength and resilience. Set against a backdrop of historical events and richly detailed settings, this captivating novel explores the transformative power of love and the pursuit of personal freedom.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Bildungsroman

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Identity and self-discovery, Political and social upheaval
