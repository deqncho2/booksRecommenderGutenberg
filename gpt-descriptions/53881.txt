Synopsis: 
In 'Foster's Complete Hoyle: An Encyclopedia of Games', Robert Frederick Foster presents a comprehensive guide to all indoor games played in his time, offering suggestions for optimal play, illustrative hands, and the most up-to-date official laws. From classic card games like poker and bridge to lesser-known board games and parlor diversions, Foster's encyclopedia is a treasure trove of knowledge for game enthusiasts. With clear instructions and insightful tips, this book is sure to captivate readers and inspire them to explore the world of indoor games.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Gaming

Themes: Strategy, Entertainment, Recreation
