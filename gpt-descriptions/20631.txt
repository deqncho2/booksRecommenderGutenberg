Synopsis: In "Chosen Peoples," Israel Zangwill delivers a thought-provoking lecture that explores the concept of chosenness within the Jewish community. Drawing upon historical and biblical references, Zangwill delves into the complexities and implications of being a chosen people, challenging conventional beliefs and offering a fresh perspective on this age-old notion.

Genres: Non-fiction, Lecture, Jewish Studies

Themes: Chosenness, Identity, Religion, History, Belonging
