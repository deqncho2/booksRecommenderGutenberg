Synopsis: In 'Lotus Buds' by Amy Carmichael, a young woman named Grace finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is unexpectedly thrust into a world of beauty, danger, and forbidden love. As she navigates the complexities of her new surroundings, Grace must confront her own fears and make choices that will shape her destiny. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Love and sacrifice, societal expectations, personal growth
