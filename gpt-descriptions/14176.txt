Synopsis: In 'The Dweller on the Threshold' by Robert Hichens, a young artist named Maurice is drawn into a mysterious and haunting world when he encounters a beautiful woman who seems to possess an otherworldly power. As Maurice becomes entangled in her web, he must confront his own desires, fears, and the dark secrets that lie within himself. With elements of romance, mystery, and the supernatural, this gripping tale explores the boundaries between reality and illusion, and the consequences of delving into the unknown.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, psychological thriller

Themes: Identity, temptation, the power of the subconscious mind
