Synopsis: In 'The Bride of the Tomb, and Queenie's Terrible Secret' by Miller, Alex. McVeigh, Mrs., a young woman named Queenie finds herself entangled in a mysterious and haunting tale when she discovers a hidden secret within the depths of an ancient tomb. As she unravels the truth, Queenie must confront her own fears and make a choice that could change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, the power of choice, the supernatural, personal growth
