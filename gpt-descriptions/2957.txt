Synopsis: In the seventh volume of his captivating memoirs, Jacques Casanova takes us on a thrilling journey through the enchanting city of Venice. Amidst the opulent palaces and masked balls, Casanova finds himself entangled in a web of seduction, political intrigue, and dangerous liaisons. As he navigates the treacherous waters of love and power, Casanova's audacious escapades and irresistible charm will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets hidden within the canals of Venice.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and seduction, Power and politics, Intrigue and deception
