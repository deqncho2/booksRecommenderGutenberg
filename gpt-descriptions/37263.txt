Synopsis:
In "Coelebs: The Love Story of a Bachelor," we are introduced to Charles Coelebs, a wealthy and eligible bachelor who has managed to avoid the entanglements of love and marriage. However, his resolve is put to the test when he meets Lucilla Stanley, a charming and intelligent young woman. As their paths continue to cross, Coelebs finds himself torn between his desire for independence and his growing affection for Lucilla. Will he finally succumb to the power of love or will he remain a bachelor forever?

Genres:
Romance, Fiction, Drama

Themes:
Love and Marriage, Independence, Social Expectations
