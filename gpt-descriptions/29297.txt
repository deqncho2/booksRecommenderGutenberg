Synopsis: 
'Among the Brigands' by De Mille, James is a thrilling adventure set in the rugged landscapes of 19th-century Italy. The story follows a young American traveler who finds himself entangled with a notorious band of brigands, leading to a dangerous game of survival and deception. As he navigates the treacherous world of outlaws, he must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will test his courage and loyalty. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Identity, Morality
