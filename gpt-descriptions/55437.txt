Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Fifth Regiment of Foot, or Northumberland Fusiliers: Containing an Account of the Formation of the Regiment in 1674, and of Its Subsequent Services to 1837', Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of the renowned Northumberland Fusiliers. From its humble beginnings in 1674 to its remarkable achievements up until 1837, this book unveils the fascinating stories of the regiment's formation, battles fought, and the brave soldiers who served under its banner. Cannon's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life the triumphs, sacrifices, and enduring legacy of this esteemed regiment.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Military valor, Regimental history, Historical legacy
