Synopsis: In "Junius Unmasked: Or, Thomas Paine the author of the Letters of Junius and the Declaration of Independence," author Joel Moody delves into a captivating historical mystery surrounding the true identity of the enigmatic writer known as Junius. Through meticulous research and compelling analysis, Moody uncovers a startling connection between Junius and one of America's founding fathers, Thomas Paine. As the layers of secrecy are peeled back, readers are taken on a thrilling journey that challenges conventional wisdom and sheds new light on the origins of some of history's most influential writings.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Literary analysis

Themes: Identity, Political intrigue, Revolutionary ideas
