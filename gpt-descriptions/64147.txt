Synopsis: In 'A Stolen Name; Or, The Man Who Defied Nick Carter', the renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a web of deception and intrigue when a mysterious figure steals his identity. As he races against time to uncover the truth, Carter must navigate a treacherous underworld and outsmart his cunning adversary. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this thrilling mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Identity theft, Deception, Pursuit of justice
