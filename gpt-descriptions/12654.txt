Synopsis: 
In 'The Roll-Call' by Arnold Bennett, we follow the life of Denry Machin, a young man with an extraordinary ability to navigate the social ladder. From his humble beginnings in a small town, Denry's ambition and resourcefulness lead him to unexpected success and encounters with influential figures. As he rises through the ranks of society, Denry must navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and the consequences of his actions. With wit, charm, and a touch of mischief, 'The Roll-Call' takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a remarkable protagonist.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Social mobility, Love and relationships, Identity
