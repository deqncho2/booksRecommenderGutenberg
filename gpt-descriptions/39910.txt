Synopsis: In "Omphalos: An Attempt to Untie the Geological Knot," Philip Henry Gosse embarks on a fascinating exploration of the age-old debate between science and religion. With a unique blend of scientific knowledge and theological insights, Gosse delves into the question of Earth's true age, challenging conventional wisdom and proposing a thought-provoking theory that will leave readers questioning their own beliefs. As he navigates through the realms of geology, paleontology, and biblical interpretation, Gosse presents a captivating argument that seeks to reconcile the seemingly irreconcilable. 

Genres: Science, Religion, Philosophy

Themes: Science vs. Religion, Biblical Interpretation, The Nature of Time
