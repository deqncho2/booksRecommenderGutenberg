Synopsis: 
'Watch and Wait; or, The Young Fugitives' by Optic, Oliver is a thrilling adventure that follows the journey of two young siblings, Frank and Fanny, who find themselves on the run after a series of unexpected events. As they navigate through dangerous situations and encounter various characters, the siblings must rely on their wits and courage to survive. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome of their daring escape.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Survival, Family bonds, Courage, Resilience
