Synopsis: 
In 'The White Desert' by Courtney Ryley Cooper, a young woman finds herself stranded in the vast and treacherous expanse of the Arctic wilderness. As she battles the harsh elements and confronts her own fears, she must rely on her resilience and resourcefulness to survive. Along the way, she encounters unexpected allies and uncovers a hidden secret that could change her life forever. This gripping tale of survival and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Survival

Themes: Resilience, Self-discovery, Survival
