Synopsis: 
In 'Illustrated Catalogue of a Portion of the Collections Made During the Field Season of 1881', William Henry Holmes takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of ethnology. Through vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, Holmes showcases a portion of the collections he gathered during the field season of 1881, offering a glimpse into the diverse cultures and artifacts he encountered. From ancient pottery and tools to intricate jewelry and textiles, this catalogue provides a visual feast for the curious reader, inviting them to explore the rich tapestry of human history and creativity.

Genres: Ethnography, Anthropology, Art History

Themes: Cultural diversity, Material culture, Archaeology
