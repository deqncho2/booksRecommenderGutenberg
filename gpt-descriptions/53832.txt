Synopsis: In 'A Strike in Santa Land: A Play in One Act', the enchanting world of Santa Land is turned upside down when the elves decide to go on strike. As chaos ensues and the holiday season hangs in the balance, the audience is taken on a whimsical journey filled with laughter, heartwarming moments, and unexpected twists. Will Santa and his elves find a way to resolve their differences and save Christmas?

Genres: Comedy, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Teamwork, Conflict resolution, Holiday spirit
