Synopsis: 
In 'Samplers and Tapestry Embroideries: Second Edition', Marcus B. Huish takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate world of textile art. From the vibrant colors of ancient tapestries to the delicate stitches of samplers, this comprehensive guide explores the rich history and techniques behind these exquisite works. With stunning visuals and insightful commentary, Huish unveils the stories woven into each thread, revealing the artistry and skill of the talented individuals who created these masterpieces. Whether you are a seasoned collector or simply fascinated by the beauty of needlework, this book is sure to inspire and enchant.

Genres: Art, History, Craft

Themes: Textile art, Cultural heritage, Craftsmanship
