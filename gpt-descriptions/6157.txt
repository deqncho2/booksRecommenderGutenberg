Synopsis: In 'What Men Live By, and Other Tales', Tolstoy presents a collection of captivating short stories that delve into the depths of human nature and explore the mysteries of life. From a heartwarming tale of a shoemaker's encounter with an unexpected visitor to a thought-provoking story about the true meaning of love, these narratives are filled with profound insights and unexpected twists. Each story offers a glimpse into the complexities of the human experience, leaving readers pondering the profound questions that lie at the core of our existence.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Love, Redemption, Human Nature, Morality
