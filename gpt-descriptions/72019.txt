Synopsis: Frankie's dog Tony is a heartwarming tale that follows the extraordinary bond between a young girl named Frankie and her loyal canine companion, Tony. Set in a small town, their adventures take them on a journey of friendship, courage, and self-discovery. As Frankie navigates the challenges of growing up, Tony becomes her steadfast source of comfort and support. Together, they embark on a series of unforgettable escapades that will leave readers both laughing and reaching for tissues. This heartwarming story is a testament to the power of unconditional love and the incredible impact that animals can have on our lives.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Self-Discovery, Animal Companionship
