Synopsis: 
'Visions and Beliefs in the West of Ireland, First Series' takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical and enchanting landscapes of the West of Ireland. Filled with vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, this collection of folklore and supernatural tales offers a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage and deep-rooted beliefs of the region. From encounters with fairies and ghosts to the power of ancient rituals, this book unveils a world where the boundaries between the seen and unseen blur, leaving readers spellbound and eager for more.

Genres: Folklore, Supernatural, Cultural Studies

Themes: Irish mythology, Folk beliefs, Supernatural encounters, Cultural heritage
