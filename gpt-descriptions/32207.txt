Synopsis: In 'A Mixture of Genius' by Castle, Arnold, a renowned scientist stumbles upon a mysterious formula that has the potential to change the world. As he delves deeper into its secrets, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected consequences. With the clock ticking, he must navigate through a treacherous landscape of deception and betrayal, all while trying to protect his groundbreaking discovery from falling into the wrong hands. This thrilling tale of scientific discovery and its unforeseen ramifications will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Scientific exploration, Consequences of discovery
