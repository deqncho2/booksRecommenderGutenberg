Synopsis: In 'The Woman-Haters' by Joseph Crosby Lincoln, a group of men form a secret society dedicated to their shared disdain for women. However, their beliefs are challenged when they encounter a mysterious woman who defies their expectations and forces them to confront their own prejudices. As the men grapple with their conflicting emotions, they are forced to reevaluate their beliefs and question the true nature of love and companionship.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Satire

Themes: Gender roles, Prejudice, Love and companionship
