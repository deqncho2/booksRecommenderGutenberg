Synopsis: In "The Travellers: A Tale, Designed for Young People," Catharine Maria Sedgwick takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two young friends, Mary and Emily. As they embark on a series of adventures, the girls encounter various challenges and meet intriguing characters from different walks of life. From bustling cities to serene countryside, their travels offer a unique perspective on the world and its diverse inhabitants. Through their experiences, Sedgwick weaves a tale that explores the themes of friendship, personal growth, and the power of empathy.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical fiction

Themes: Friendship, Personal growth, Empathy
