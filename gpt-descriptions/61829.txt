Synopsis: 
In the small town of Kerry, a young woman named Peggy finds herself caught in a web of secrets and scandal that threatens to unravel her entire world. As she navigates through the complexities of love, betrayal, and family, Peggy must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge her sense of self. With vivid descriptions of the Irish countryside and a cast of intriguing characters, 'Peggy from Kerry' is a captivating tale of resilience and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and relationships, Identity, Family dynamics
