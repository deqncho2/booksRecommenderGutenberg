Synopsis: In the city of Semur, strange and unsettling events begin to unfold, blurring the line between reality and the supernatural. As the city becomes increasingly isolated and its inhabitants face a mysterious threat, a group of individuals must navigate their way through the chaos, uncovering hidden secrets and confronting their deepest fears. With the unseen forces at play, the fate of Semur hangs in the balance, and the true nature of the city's beleaguerment remains shrouded in uncertainty.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, mystery

Themes: Fear and paranoia, the power of the unknown, the struggle between good and evil
