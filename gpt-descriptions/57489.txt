Synopsis: In 'Omega: The Last Days of the World', Flammarion takes us on a mesmerizing journey into a future where the fate of humanity hangs in the balance. Through a series of vivid and prophetic visions, he paints a haunting picture of a world on the brink of destruction. As cataclysmic events unfold, the reader is left questioning the very essence of existence and the potential for redemption. This thought-provoking and gripping narrative will keep you on the edge of your seat until the final page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Apocalyptic Fiction

Themes: The nature of humanity, the fragility of civilization, the consequences of our actions, the search for meaning in a chaotic world
