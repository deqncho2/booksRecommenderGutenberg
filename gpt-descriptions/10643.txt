Synopsis: 
In 'The World's Greatest Books — Volume 02 — Fiction', embark on a captivating journey through a collection of timeless literary masterpieces. From the haunting tale of a man's descent into madness to the heart-wrenching story of a young woman's struggle for independence, this volume presents a diverse range of fiction that will leave readers spellbound. Delve into the depths of human emotions, explore the complexities of relationships, and witness the triumphs and tragedies that shape our lives. Each story is a window into the human experience, offering profound insights and unforgettable characters that will stay with you long after you turn the final page.

Genres: Fiction, Classic Literature, Short Stories

Themes: Madness, Independence, Human Emotions, Relationships, Triumphs and Tragedies
