Synopsis: 

'With Rifle and Bayonet: A Story of the Boer War' by F. S. Brereton is a thrilling adventure set during the tumultuous Boer War. The story follows a young British soldier named Jack Somerton who finds himself caught up in the brutal conflict between the British Empire and the Boer Republics in South Africa. As Jack navigates the harsh realities of war, he must confront not only the physical dangers of battle but also the moral dilemmas that arise in the face of violence and oppression. With his courage and determination, Jack becomes an unlikely hero, fighting for what he believes is right amidst the chaos and bloodshed of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Morality, Conflict, Heroism
