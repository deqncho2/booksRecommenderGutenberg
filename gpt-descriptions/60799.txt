Synopsis: In 'Ignatz' by Ron Goulart, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a famous artist is found dead under mysterious circumstances. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that lead him down a twisted path. With each clue he uncovers, the detective realizes that nothing is as it seems, and the truth may be more elusive than he ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Artistic Expression, Betrayal
