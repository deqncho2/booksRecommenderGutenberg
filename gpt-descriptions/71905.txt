Synopsis: In 'The discovery and decipherment of the trilingual cuneiform inscriptions', Arthur John Booth takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of ancient languages and the decipherment of mysterious inscriptions. From the unearthing of these enigmatic texts to the tireless efforts of scholars to unlock their secrets, Booth unveils the remarkable story behind the decipherment of cuneiform, a writing system used by ancient civilizations. With vivid descriptions and intriguing anecdotes, this book offers a glimpse into the thrilling quest to unravel the mysteries of the past.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Archaeology

Themes: Language and communication, Ancient civilizations, Historical discovery
