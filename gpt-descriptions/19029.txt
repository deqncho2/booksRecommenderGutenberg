Synopsis: In 'The Gifts of Asti' by Andre Norton, a young woman named Tanree is chosen to serve as the high priestess of the ancient city of Asti, a place shrouded in mystery and protected by powerful magic. As she navigates her new role, Tanree discovers that the city holds secrets and dangers beyond her imagination. With the help of a telepathic feline companion, she must unravel the mysteries of Asti and confront the forces that threaten its existence.

Genres: Fantasy, Science Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Power of ancient magic, Coming of age, Preservation of knowledge
