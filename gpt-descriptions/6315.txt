Synopsis: In the small town of Mercer, a tragic event sets off a chain of events that will forever change the life of Helena Richie. As she navigates through love, loss, and the complexities of society, Helena embarks on a journey of self-discovery and personal awakening. With secrets and hidden desires lurking beneath the surface, Helena must confront her own inner demons and make choices that will shape her future. Deland's poignant tale explores the depths of human emotions and the power of personal transformation.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Love and Loss, Society and Social Expectations
