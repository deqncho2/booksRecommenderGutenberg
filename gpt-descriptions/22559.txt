Synopsis: In 'The Day of the Boomer Dukes' by Frederik Pohl, a group of rebellious teenagers known as the Boomer Dukes take over a small town, turning it into their own lawless domain. As chaos ensues, the town's residents must confront their own fears and find the courage to stand up against the unruly youth. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this thrilling novel explores the clash between generations and the consequences of unchecked power.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Rebellion, Power dynamics, Generational conflict
