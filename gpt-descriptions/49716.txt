Synopsis: 
'Sonnets from Hafez & Other Verses' is a captivating collection of poetry by Hafiz, an influential Persian poet from the 14th century, and Daryush, Elizabeth Bridges. This anthology takes readers on a journey through the realms of love, spirituality, and the beauty of nature. With each verse, the poets skillfully weave together profound emotions, mystical imagery, and timeless wisdom, creating a tapestry of words that will leave readers enchanted and inspired.

Genres: Poetry, Spiritual, Love

Themes: Love, Spirituality, Nature
