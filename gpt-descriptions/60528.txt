Synopsis: In 'The Owls' House' by Garstin, Crosbie, a young woman named Alice finds herself drawn to a mysterious house in the countryside, inhabited by a reclusive family. As she becomes entangled in their secrets and dark past, Alice must confront her own fears and desires, leading to a thrilling and unexpected journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the power of fear, the search for self-identity
