Synopsis: 

'Geographic Variation in Red-backed Mice (Genus Clethrionomys) of the Southern Rocky Mountain Region' takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes of the Southern Rocky Mountain region, exploring the fascinating variations in red-backed mice populations. From the lush forests to the rugged mountains, this scientific study delves into the intricate details of these small creatures, uncovering the secrets of their adaptations and genetic diversity. As the researchers navigate through the vast wilderness, they encounter unexpected discoveries that challenge their preconceived notions and shed light on the intricate web of life in this unique ecosystem.

Genres: Scientific research, Natural history, Wildlife biology

Themes: Biodiversity, Adaptation, Ecosystem dynamics
