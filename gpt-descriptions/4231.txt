Synopsis: In "The Malady of the Century" by Max Simon Nordau, a renowned psychiatrist finds himself entangled in a mysterious case that challenges his beliefs and pushes the boundaries of his profession. As he delves deeper into the mind of a troubled patient, he uncovers a web of secrets and hidden desires that threaten to consume them both. With each revelation, the line between sanity and madness becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of the human psyche.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Mental illness, Identity, Morality
