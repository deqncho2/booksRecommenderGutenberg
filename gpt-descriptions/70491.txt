Synopsis: In 'Elfin Land' by Josephine Pollard, a young girl named Mabel discovers a hidden world filled with enchanting creatures and magical adventures. As she explores this mysterious realm, she encounters mischievous fairies, wise elves, and other fantastical beings. With each encounter, Mabel learns valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the power of imagination. But as she delves deeper into Elfin Land, she realizes that there may be more at stake than she initially thought. Will Mabel be able to navigate this enchanting world and uncover its secrets? Genres: Fantasy, Adventure Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Bravery
