Synopsis: In 'The Red House Mystery', a wealthy man invites his old friend to his country estate for a weekend getaway. However, when a mysterious murder occurs, the two friends find themselves caught up in a thrilling and perplexing whodunit. As they navigate through a web of secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected twists, they must unravel the truth before the killer strikes again.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Trust, Friendship, Justice
