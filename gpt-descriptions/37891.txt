Synopsis: 

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the rich history and captivating tales of Cumberland and Westmorland. In this enthralling book, Daniel Scott takes readers on a journey through the forgotten corners of these ancient counties, unearthing hidden stories and revealing the secrets of the past. From the haunting ruins of long-lost castles to the legendary figures who shaped the region, Scott paints a vivid picture of a bygone era. Prepare to be transported to a time of knights and battles, folklore and superstition, as you explore the fascinating tapestry of Cumberland and Westmorland's history.

Genres: History, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Local history, Folklore, Legends, Cultural heritage
