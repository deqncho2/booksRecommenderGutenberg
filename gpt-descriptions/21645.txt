Synopsis: In 'The Romance of Old New England Rooftrees', Mary Caroline Crawford takes readers on a captivating journey through the charming and mysterious rooftrees of old New England. From quaint cottages to grand mansions, each rooftop holds secrets, stories, and hidden treasures waiting to be discovered. As the author delves into the history and legends surrounding these rooftrees, she weaves together a tapestry of love, adventure, and intrigue that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and Relationships, History and Heritage, Secrets and Intrigue
