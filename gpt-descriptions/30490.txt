Synopsis: In 'The Fifth of November: A Romance of the Stuarts', Charles S. Bentley and Frank Kimball Scribner take readers on a thrilling journey through 17th century England. Set against the backdrop of the Gunpowder Plot, this historical romance follows the lives of two individuals whose paths intertwine amidst political intrigue, forbidden love, and the fight for freedom. As secrets are unveiled and loyalties are tested, the protagonists find themselves caught in a web of danger and passion that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Love, Freedom, Political Intrigue
