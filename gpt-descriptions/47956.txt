Synopsis: In 'Christmastide: Its History, Festivities, and Carols', William Sandys takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Christmas. From the origins of the holiday to the joyous celebrations and beloved carols, this book unravels the rich tapestry of Christmas traditions. Delving into the historical, cultural, and religious aspects, Sandys paints a vivid picture of the festive season, exploring the customs, games, and merriment that have shaped Christmas throughout the ages. With fascinating anecdotes and delightful insights, 'Christmastide' is a delightful exploration of the magic and wonder that surrounds this cherished holiday.

Genres: History, Cultural Studies, Music

Themes: Christmas traditions, Festivities, Cultural heritage
