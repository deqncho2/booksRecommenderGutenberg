Synopsis: In 'The Lost Ego' by Phillips, Rog, a renowned psychologist finds himself trapped in a mysterious experiment that challenges his understanding of identity and reality. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic world he's been thrust into, he must confront his own ego and unravel the secrets that lie within. With mind-bending twists and thought-provoking revelations, this gripping psychological thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Science fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Reality, Psychological exploration
