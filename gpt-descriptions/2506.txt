Synopsis: 
In 'The Sleeping-Car: A Farce' by William Dean Howells, chaos ensues when a group of eccentric passengers find themselves trapped in a malfunctioning sleeping car during a train journey. As they struggle to navigate the absurd situations that arise, from mistaken identities to unexpected romantic entanglements, the passengers must rely on their wit and resourcefulness to escape the hilarious predicaments they find themselves in. With a delightful blend of humor and wit, this farcical tale takes readers on a rollicking ride through the unpredictable world of train travel.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Absurdity, Mistaken Identity, Romantic Entanglements
