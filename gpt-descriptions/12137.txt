Synopsis: 
'La Légende des Siècles' by Victor Hugo is a captivating collection of poems that takes readers on a journey through time, exploring the grandeur and complexity of human existence. From ancient civilizations to the modern era, Hugo weaves together tales of love, war, heroism, and tragedy, painting a vivid picture of the human experience across the ages. Each poem is a window into a different epoch, filled with vivid imagery and profound insights that leave readers pondering the mysteries of life and the enduring power of the human spirit.

Genres: Poetry, Historical Fiction, Epic

Themes: Time, Human Nature, Love, War, Heroism, Tragedy
