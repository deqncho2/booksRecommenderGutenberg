Synopsis: In 'Under Padlock and Seal' by Avery, Harold, a mysterious package arrives at the doorstep of a struggling artist, setting off a chain of events that will challenge his perception of reality. As he delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the package, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the artist's world unravels, blurring the lines between imagination and truth, leaving him questioning everything he thought he knew.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Perception, Identity, Deception
