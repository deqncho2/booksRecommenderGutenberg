Synopsis: In 'The Stowaway Girl' by Tracy, Louis, a young girl named Amelia finds herself caught in a thrilling adventure when she stumbles upon a hidden secret aboard a mysterious ship. Determined to uncover the truth, Amelia must navigate treacherous waters, face unexpected dangers, and outsmart cunning adversaries. As she unravels the enigma surrounding the ship, Amelia discovers her own hidden strengths and the power of friendship. Will she be able to solve the puzzle before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Discovery, Friendship
