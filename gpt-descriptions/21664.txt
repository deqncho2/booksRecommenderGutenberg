Synopsis: 
In 'George at the Fort; Or, Life Among the Soldiers' by Castlemon, Harry, readers are transported to the rugged and thrilling world of the American frontier. George, a young and adventurous boy, finds himself immersed in the daily life of soldiers stationed at a fort. From witnessing intense battles with Native American tribes to forming unexpected friendships, George's journey is filled with danger, excitement, and personal growth. As he navigates the challenges of frontier life, George learns valuable lessons about courage, loyalty, and the true meaning of honor.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Identity, Honor
