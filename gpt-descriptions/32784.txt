Synopsis: In 'The Dark Goddess' by Richard S. Shaver, a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds a dark secret. As he delves deeper into its mysteries, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where ancient legends and modern science collide. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must race against time to uncover the truth and confront the malevolent forces that seek to unleash the power of the dark goddess upon the world.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Ancient mythology, Supernatural forces, Good vs. Evil
