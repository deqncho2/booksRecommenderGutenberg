Synopsis: In 'The Standardized Man' by Stephen Bartholomew, a seemingly ordinary man named John finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a hidden society that controls every aspect of human life. As he delves deeper into this clandestine world, John discovers shocking secrets that challenge his perception of reality and force him to question his own identity. With danger lurking at every turn, John must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and reclaim his freedom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Control, Conspiracy
