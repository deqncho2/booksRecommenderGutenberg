Synopsis: In 'Rollo in Rome' by Abbott, Jacob, young Rollo embarks on an exciting journey to the ancient city of Rome. As he explores the historical landmarks and encounters fascinating characters, Rollo's curiosity and sense of adventure lead him on unexpected paths. From the grandeur of the Colosseum to the mysteries of the Vatican, Rollo's experiences in Rome will captivate readers and leave them eager to discover what lies around each corner.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Juvenile Literature

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, History, Cultural Immersion
