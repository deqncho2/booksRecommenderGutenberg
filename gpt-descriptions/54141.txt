Synopsis: In 'A Party in Mother Goose Land: A One Act Play for Primary Children', the beloved characters from Mother Goose's nursery rhymes come to life in a delightful and whimsical party. As the characters gather together, they embark on a series of adventures and encounters that are both hilarious and heartwarming. From Little Bo Peep's search for her lost sheep to Humpty Dumpty's daring acrobatics, this enchanting play is filled with laughter, music, and surprises that will captivate young audiences.

Genres: Children's Literature, Drama, Fantasy

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Adventure
