Synopsis: In 'The Money Market' by F. Straker, a young and ambitious stockbroker finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and corruption as he navigates the cutthroat world of high finance. With his career and personal life hanging in the balance, he must uncover the truth behind a mysterious financial scandal before it consumes him completely. As the stakes rise and the danger intensifies, he must decide how far he is willing to go to protect his future and expose the truth.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Ambition, Corruption, Deception, Power
