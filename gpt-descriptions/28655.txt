Synopsis: 
Ralph, a young and ambitious engineer, embarks on a thrilling adventure aboard the Overland Express, a magnificent steam locomotive that traverses the vast American landscape. As he faces numerous challenges and triumphs, Ralph must navigate treacherous terrain, outsmart cunning adversaries, and confront his own fears. Along the way, he forms unexpected friendships, discovers hidden secrets, and learns valuable lessons about perseverance and the power of determination. Will Ralph's engineering skills and indomitable spirit be enough to overcome the obstacles that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Self-discovery
