Synopsis: 
In 'The Memoirs of Maria Stella (Lady Newborough)', we are transported to the captivating world of 18th-century Europe through the eyes of Maria Stella Petronilla, Baroness Ungern-Sternberg. As a woman of noble birth, Maria Stella's life is filled with opulence, scandal, and intrigue. From her privileged upbringing to her encounters with influential figures, she unveils the secrets and hidden desires of the aristocracy. Through her vivid and intimate recollections, we witness the highs and lows of her extraordinary journey, as she navigates love, betrayal, and the pursuit of personal freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Romance

Themes: Power and privilege, Love and betrayal, Personal freedom
