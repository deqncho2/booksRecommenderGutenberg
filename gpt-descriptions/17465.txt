Synopsis: In 'Great Singers, Second Series: Malibran To Titiens', George T. Ferris takes us on a captivating journey through the lives and careers of some of the most remarkable female opera singers of the 19th century. From the enigmatic and tragic Maria Malibran to the dazzling and influential Pauline Viardot-Garcia, Ferris delves into the personal stories, triumphs, and struggles of these extraordinary women who left an indelible mark on the world of music. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of these legendary performers, their artistic contributions, and the impact they had on the opera world.

Genres: Biography, Music History, Non-fiction

Themes: Female Empowerment, Artistic Expression, Legacy
