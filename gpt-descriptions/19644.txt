Synopsis: In 'Mary's Meadow, and Other Tales of Fields and Flowers', Juliana Horatia Ewing takes readers on a delightful journey through the enchanting world of meadows and flowers. From the heartwarming story of Mary and her special connection to a meadow, to the whimsical adventures of fairies and talking flowers, this collection of tales is sure to captivate readers of all ages. Each story is filled with wonder, friendship, and the beauty of nature, offering a glimpse into a world where imagination and reality intertwine.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Short Stories

Themes: Nature, Imagination, Friendship, Beauty
