Synopsis: In the small town of Beauchamp Row, a mysterious murder takes place on a stormy night in 1895. As the clock strikes midnight, the residents find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each passing hour, the tension rises, and the truth becomes more elusive. As the investigation unfolds, the dark underbelly of the seemingly idyllic town is exposed, revealing shocking revelations that will leave readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town secrets
