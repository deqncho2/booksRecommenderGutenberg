Synopsis: 
In 'How to Write Letters (Formerly The Book of Letters): A Complete Guide to Correct Business and Personal Correspondence', Mary Owens Crowther presents a comprehensive and indispensable manual for mastering the art of letter writing. With practical tips, examples, and step-by-step instructions, this guide takes readers on a journey through the intricacies of crafting effective and impactful letters for both professional and personal purposes. From formal business communications to heartfelt personal messages, Crowther's expertise shines through as she explores the nuances of tone, structure, and etiquette, ensuring that every letter leaves a lasting impression. Whether you're a seasoned writer or a novice penman, this book is a must-have resource for anyone seeking to enhance their communication skills and make a lasting impact through the written word.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Writing

Themes: Communication, Etiquette, Professionalism, Personal expression
