Synopsis: In "Tom Swift and His Electric Runabout; Or, The Speediest Car on the Road," young inventor Tom Swift embarks on a thrilling adventure as he unveils his latest creation, an electric runabout that promises to revolutionize the world of automobiles. However, Tom soon finds himself facing unexpected challenges and dangerous adversaries who will stop at nothing to get their hands on his groundbreaking invention. As he races against time, Tom must rely on his ingenuity and resourcefulness to protect his invention and ensure its success.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Invention and Innovation, Pursuit of Knowledge, Courage and Determination
