Synopsis: 
In "In a Steamer Chair, and Other Shipboard Stories" by Robert Barr, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of thrilling and mysterious tales set aboard various ships. From the opulent luxury liners of the upper class to the humble vessels of the working class, each story presents a unique and intriguing narrative that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Whether it's a case of mistaken identity, a deadly game of cat and mouse, or a shocking revelation, these shipboard stories are filled with unexpected twists and turns that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Intrigue, Survival
