Synopsis: In "Miracles of Our Lord" by George MacDonald, embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales that explore the extraordinary miracles performed by Jesus. From healing the sick to transforming lives, these stories delve into the profound impact of divine intervention on both individuals and communities. With each miraculous encounter, MacDonald weaves a tapestry of wonder, faith, and the power of love that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fiction, Christian literature, Short stories

Themes: Miracles, Faith, Redemption, Compassion, Divine intervention
