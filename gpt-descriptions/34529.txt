Synopsis:
In 'The Infidel; or, the Fall of Mexico. Vol. I.', Robert Montgomery Bird takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events surrounding the fall of Mexico. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue, war, and forbidden love, this gripping tale follows the life of a young American soldier who finds himself torn between loyalty and desire. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of war-torn Mexico, he must confront his own beliefs and question the boundaries of faith, honor, and love. With its rich historical detail and compelling characters, 'The Infidel' promises to be a thrilling and thought-provoking adventure.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Love and Desire, Faith and Belief
