Synopsis: In 'A Struggle for Rome, v. 2' by Felix Dahn, the gripping tale continues as the Roman Empire faces internal strife and external threats. Amidst political intrigue, military campaigns, and personal vendettas, a diverse cast of characters must navigate treacherous waters to secure their own ambitions and protect their loved ones. As the struggle for power intensifies, loyalties are tested, alliances are forged, and sacrifices must be made. Will Rome emerge stronger or crumble under the weight of its own turmoil?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Adventure

Themes: Power and Ambition, Loyalty and Betrayal, Survival and Sacrifice
