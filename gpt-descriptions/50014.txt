Synopsis: In 'The Trail of the Green Doll: A Judy Bolton Mystery' by Margaret Sutton, Judy Bolton finds herself embroiled in a perplexing case when a mysterious green doll appears in her possession. As she delves into the origins of the doll, Judy uncovers a web of secrets and hidden connections that lead her on a thrilling adventure. With danger lurking around every corner, Judy must use her wit and determination to solve the puzzle and bring the truth to light.

Genres: Mystery, Young Adult, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden connections, Determination and perseverance
