Synopsis: 
In 'Real Ghost Stories', W. T. Stead delves into the chilling world of the supernatural, recounting a collection of spine-tingling tales that will leave readers questioning the boundaries between the living and the dead. From haunted houses to ghostly apparitions, each story is meticulously crafted to send shivers down your spine and keep you on the edge of your seat. As the author explores the mysteries of the afterlife, he uncovers the dark secrets that lie hidden in the shadows, leaving readers captivated by the eerie and unexplained phenomena that haunt these pages.

Genres: 
Horror, Paranormal, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Supernatural encounters, Life after death, Fear and the unknown
