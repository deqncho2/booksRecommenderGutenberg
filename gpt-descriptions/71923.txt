Synopsis: In 'The Will to Live (Les Roquevillard)', Bordeaux, Henry takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the Roquevillard family. Set against the backdrop of a changing world, this novel explores the intricate dynamics of love, ambition, and the relentless pursuit of one's dreams. As the Roquevillards navigate through personal triumphs and tragedies, their resilience and determination to overcome adversity become the driving force behind their will to live.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Family Drama, Romance

Themes: Resilience, Ambition, Love, Adversity, Family Dynamics
