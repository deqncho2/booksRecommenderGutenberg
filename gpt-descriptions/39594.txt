Synopsis: In "The Little Colonel's Knight Comes Riding," the beloved characters of Lloyd Sherman and her friends embark on a thrilling adventure filled with mystery and danger. When a mysterious stranger arrives in town, the Little Colonel and her companions find themselves caught up in a web of secrets and intrigue. As they uncover the truth, they must confront their own fears and learn the importance of loyalty and bravery. Will they be able to solve the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Loyalty
