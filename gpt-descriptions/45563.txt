Synopsis: 

In "Deeds of a Great Railway: A record of the enterprise and achievements of the London and North-Western Railway company during the Great War," G. R. S. Darroch takes readers on a captivating journey through the remarkable accomplishments of the railway company during one of the most challenging periods in history. From transporting troops and supplies to aiding in the war effort, the London and North-Western Railway company played a pivotal role in the Great War, and this book unveils the untold stories of their heroic deeds. 

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, War

Themes: Heroism, Innovation, War Effort
