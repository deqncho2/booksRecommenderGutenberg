Synopsis: 
In the second volume of "The Collected Writings of Dougal Graham, 'Skellat' Bellman of Glasgow," readers are taken on a captivating journey through the streets of 18th-century Glasgow. Dougal Graham, a charismatic and observant bellman, shares his unique perspective on the city's bustling life, its colorful characters, and the hidden stories that lie behind its facades. From the lively markets to the dark alleyways, Dougal's writings offer a vivid portrayal of a city in transition, where tradition clashes with progress, and where the struggles and triumphs of its inhabitants are brought to life through his eloquent words.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Anthology
Themes: Urban life, Social change, Human resilience
