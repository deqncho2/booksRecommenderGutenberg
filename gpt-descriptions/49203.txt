Synopsis: In "My Visit to Tolstoy": Five Discourses, Joseph Krauskopf takes readers on a captivating journey as he recounts his personal encounter with the legendary Russian author, Leo Tolstoy. Through a series of five thought-provoking discourses, Krauskopf delves into the profound impact Tolstoy's writings had on his own life, exploring the complexities of human existence, the pursuit of truth, and the power of literature to inspire change. As he shares his intimate conversations with Tolstoy, Krauskopf invites readers to reflect on the timeless wisdom and enduring relevance of one of the greatest literary minds in history.

Genres: Memoir, Literary Criticism, Philosophy

Themes: Human existence, Truth, Literature's transformative power
