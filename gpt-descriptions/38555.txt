Synopsis: 
In "Dorothy Dale in the City" by Margaret Penrose, young Dorothy Dale finds herself in the bustling city, far from her quiet countryside home. As she navigates the unfamiliar streets and encounters new friends and unexpected challenges, Dorothy's adventurous spirit and determination shine through. With her loyal companions by her side, she embarks on a journey that will test her courage and resilience, leading her to discover the true meaning of friendship and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
