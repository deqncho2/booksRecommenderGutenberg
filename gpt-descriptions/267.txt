Synopsis: 
In the gripping novel "The Touchstone" by Edith Wharton, we follow the life of Stephen Glennard, a struggling writer who finds himself at a crossroads when he discovers a collection of letters that could potentially change his fortune. As he grapples with the moral implications of his actions, Glennard becomes entangled in a web of deceit, desire, and the consequences of his choices. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the intricate plot twists and the profound exploration of human nature that lies at the heart of this enthralling tale.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Morality, Ambition, Consequences of Actions
