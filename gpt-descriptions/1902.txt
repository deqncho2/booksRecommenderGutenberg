Synopsis: In the small town of Oldbury, a mysterious and neglected church pew becomes the center of attention during the Christmas season. As the townspeople gather to celebrate the holiday, the secrets and stories hidden within the old peabody pew begin to unravel, revealing unexpected connections and heartwarming surprises. This charming Christmas romance takes readers on a journey of love, forgiveness, and the power of community, all set against the backdrop of a quaint country church.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Christmas

Themes: Love, Forgiveness, Community
