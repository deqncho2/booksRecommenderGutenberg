Synopsis: In 'The World of Ice', a group of adventurous young men embark on a perilous journey to the Arctic, seeking to uncover the mysteries of the frozen wilderness. As they face treacherous landscapes, encounter indigenous peoples, and confront the harsh realities of survival, they discover that the Arctic holds more than just icy landscapes. With danger lurking at every turn, their courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will they conquer the challenges that lie ahead, or will the unforgiving world of ice consume them?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Survival

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Man vs. Nature
