Synopsis: 

In "Jewels and the woman: The romance, magic and art of feminine adornment," Marianne Ostier takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of jewelry and its profound connection to women. From ancient civilizations to modern times, Ostier explores the allure, symbolism, and transformative power of these exquisite adornments. Through enchanting stories and stunning visuals, she delves into the intimate relationship between women and their jewels, revealing the hidden stories and emotions they hold. This book is a celebration of femininity, beauty, and the timeless artistry of jewelry.

Genres: Art, Fashion, History

Themes: Femininity, Beauty, Symbolism, Personal Expression
