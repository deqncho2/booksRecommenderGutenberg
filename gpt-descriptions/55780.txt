Synopsis: In 'The Strife of the Sea' by T. Jenkins Hains, a young sailor named Jack finds himself caught in a treacherous web of mystery and danger as he embarks on a perilous journey across the vast ocean. With his shipmates, he must navigate through storms, encounter ruthless pirates, and uncover the secrets hidden within the depths of the sea. As the stakes rise and the tension mounts, Jack must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to survive the relentless strife of the sea.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Courage, Betrayal, Discovery
