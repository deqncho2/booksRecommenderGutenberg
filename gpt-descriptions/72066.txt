Synopsis: In 'Contraband' by Clarence Budington Kelland, a young man named Jimmie Dale finds himself caught in a dangerous web of smuggling and intrigue. When he stumbles upon a hidden secret, he must navigate a treacherous world of criminals, deceit, and unexpected alliances. As Jimmie races against time to uncover the truth, he discovers that nothing is as it seems and that his own life may be at stake.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Deception, Loyalty, Redemption
