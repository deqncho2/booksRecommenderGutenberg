Synopsis: In 'Our Little Cuban Cousin', readers are transported to the vibrant island of Cuba, where they meet Dolores, a spirited young girl with a deep love for her homeland. Through Dolores' eyes, we explore the rich culture, traditions, and natural beauty of Cuba, as well as the challenges and joys of everyday life. As Dolores navigates her world, she encounters unexpected friendships, exciting adventures, and important life lessons that will leave readers captivated and inspired.

Genres: Children's Literature, Historical Fiction, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Cultural identity, Family bonds, Adventure, Appreciation of nature
