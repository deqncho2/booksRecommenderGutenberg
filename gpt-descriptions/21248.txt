Synopsis: In "The Little Colonel: Maid of Honor," the beloved character Lloyd Sherman finds herself in the midst of a grand wedding celebration. As the maid of honor, she navigates through a series of unexpected events and encounters, all while learning valuable lessons about friendship, loyalty, and the true meaning of love. With her trademark charm and wit, Lloyd's adventures will captivate readers as they follow her journey towards self-discovery and personal growth.

Genres: Children's literature, Fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Love, Self-discovery
