Synopsis: 
'Home Again' by MacDonald, George is a captivating tale that follows the journey of a young woman named Janet as she navigates through a series of unexpected events that lead her back to her childhood home. Filled with mystery, romance, and a touch of the supernatural, this story takes readers on a thrilling adventure as Janet unravels the secrets of her past and discovers the true meaning of home.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Mystery, Romance

Themes: 
Identity, Family, Redemption
