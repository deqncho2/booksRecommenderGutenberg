Synopsis: In the small town of Bear Cat, a notorious bootlegger named Buck finds himself in a precarious situation when his secret moonshine operation is threatened by unexpected circumstances. As tensions rise and the town's residents become entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal, Buck must navigate a dangerous path to protect his livelihood and those he holds dear. With a cast of colorful characters and a gripping narrative, 'When 'Bear Cat' Went Dry' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of prohibition-era America.

Genres: Crime fiction, Historical fiction, Thriller

Themes: Prohibition, Loyalty, Survival
