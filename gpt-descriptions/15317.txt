Synopsis: In "The Baronet's Bride; Or, A Woman's Vengeance" by May Agnes Fleming, a young woman named Edith finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deceit when she becomes the bride of a wealthy baronet. As she navigates the opulent world of high society, Edith discovers that her new husband's past holds dark and dangerous secrets. Determined to uncover the truth, she embarks on a journey of revenge, where love, betrayal, and unexpected alliances await her at every turn.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Revenge, Secrets, Deception, Love and Betrayal
