Synopsis: In 'Americanism', Theodore Roosevelt takes readers on a captivating journey through the heart of America, exploring the essence of what it truly means to be an American. Through his insightful observations and personal experiences, Roosevelt delves into the unique qualities that define the American spirit, from the pioneering mindset to the unwavering commitment to freedom and democracy. With his characteristic eloquence and passion, he paints a vivid portrait of a nation built on resilience, diversity, and the pursuit of greatness. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Political, Historical

Themes: American identity, Patriotism, Democracy, Nationalism
