Synopsis: In 'Tuen, Slave and Empress' by Kathleen Gray Nelson, a young woman named Tuen finds herself torn from her peaceful life and thrust into a world of slavery and oppression. As she navigates the treacherous paths of power and deceit, Tuen discovers her own strength and resilience, ultimately rising to become an unexpected force to be reckoned with. Set against a backdrop of political intrigue and forbidden love, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover Tuen's fate.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and oppression, resilience and strength, forbidden love
