Synopsis: In 'The Philosophy of Evolution: Together With a Preliminary Essay on The Metaphysical Basis of Science', Stephen H. Carpenter delves into the fascinating world of evolution and its profound implications for our understanding of life. Through a thought-provoking exploration of the metaphysical foundations of science, Carpenter invites readers on a captivating journey that challenges conventional wisdom and offers new insights into the nature of existence. With a blend of scientific rigor and philosophical inquiry, this book promises to ignite the reader's curiosity and expand their understanding of the intricate tapestry of life.

Genres: Science, Philosophy

Themes: Evolution, Metaphysics, Scientific Inquiry
