Synopsis: 
In "The Adventures of Bobby Orde" by Stewart Edward White, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside young Bobby Orde as he navigates the challenges and excitement of his teenage years. From his mischievous escapades at school to his daring adventures in the great outdoors, Bobby's story is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As Bobby grows and matures, he learns valuable lessons about friendship, loyalty, and the importance of embracing life's adventures.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Adventure, Self-discovery
