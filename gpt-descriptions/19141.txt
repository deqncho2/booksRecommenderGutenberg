Synopsis: In "Edison's Conquest of Mars," Garrett Putman Serviss takes readers on an exhilarating journey to the Red Planet, where Thomas Edison and a group of intrepid adventurers embark on a daring mission to save Earth from a menacing Martian invasion. Filled with thrilling encounters, ingenious inventions, and epic battles, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats as they witness the clash between humanity and extraterrestrial forces.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Steampunk

Themes: Exploration, Innovation, Survival, Interplanetary Conflict
