Synopsis: In 'Out of the Primitive' by Robert Ames Bennet, a young American engineer finds himself stranded in the heart of the Amazon rainforest after a plane crash. As he navigates the treacherous jungle, he encounters a mysterious tribe and becomes entangled in their ancient rituals and customs. With danger lurking at every turn, he must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to survive and find a way back to civilization. This thrilling adventure takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness, where the line between civilization and savagery blurs.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Historical Fiction

Themes: Man vs. Nature, Cultural Exploration, Self-Discovery
