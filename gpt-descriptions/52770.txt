Synopsis: In "The Ancient City" by Constance Fenimore Woolson, a young woman named Hester travels to the ancient city of Rome, where she becomes captivated by its rich history and vibrant culture. As she immerses herself in the city's past, Hester discovers a hidden world of secrets and mysteries that will challenge her perceptions and change her life forever. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel takes readers on a journey through the enchanting streets of Rome, where the past and present intertwine in unexpected ways.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: History and its impact on the present, Identity and self-discovery, Love and passion
