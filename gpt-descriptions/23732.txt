Synopsis: In 'A Girl of the Klondike' by Cross, Victoria, we follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Clara as she embarks on a daring adventure to the Klondike during the gold rush era. Determined to prove her independence and find her own fortune, Clara faces numerous challenges and encounters a diverse cast of characters along the way. As she navigates the harsh wilderness and the treacherous world of gold mining, Clara's resilience and determination are put to the test, leading her to discover not only the true meaning of courage but also unexpected love and friendship. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Independence, Resilience, Self-discovery
