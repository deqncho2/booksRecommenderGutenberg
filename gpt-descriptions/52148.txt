Synopsis: In "Dorothy South: A Love Story of Virginia Just Before the War," George Cary Eggleston takes us on a captivating journey through the antebellum South, where the spirited and independent Dorothy South finds herself torn between duty and desire. Set against the backdrop of Virginia in the years leading up to the Civil War, this enthralling tale follows Dorothy as she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations. As she grapples with her own desires and the changing world around her, Dorothy's choices will have far-reaching consequences that will test her resilience and challenge the very fabric of her world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Duty, Social Expectations, Personal Freedom, Coming-of-Age
