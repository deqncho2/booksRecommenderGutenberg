Synopsis: In the midst of the Civil War, four sisters navigate the challenges of adolescence and womanhood, each with their own dreams and aspirations. Meg, Jo, Beth, and Amy March, guided by their loving mother, Marmee, learn the importance of family, friendship, and the pursuit of their individual passions. Through heartwarming and heart-wrenching experiences, they discover the true meaning of love, sacrifice, and the power of sisterhood.

Genres: Coming-of-age, family drama, historical fiction

Themes: Sisterhood, female empowerment, societal expectations, love and sacrifice
