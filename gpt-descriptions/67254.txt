Synopsis: 
In 'Out of the Woods' by Elisabeth Sanxay Holding, a young woman named Lily is caught in a web of mystery and danger when she agrees to help a stranger in need. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the man she is trying to assist, Lily finds herself entangled in a thrilling game of deception and betrayal. With her life on the line, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and escape the clutches of those who wish to silence her forever.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Survival
