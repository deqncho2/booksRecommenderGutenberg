Synopsis: In 'Musk-Ox, Bison, Sheep and Goat', three renowned authors embark on an adventurous journey through the untamed wilderness of the American West. As they encounter majestic musk-oxen, powerful bison, graceful sheep, and elusive goats, they delve into the captivating world of these remarkable creatures. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, the authors paint a vivid picture of the animals' behavior, habitat, and their significance in the natural ecosystem. This enthralling narrative takes readers on a thrilling expedition, filled with awe-inspiring encounters and a deeper understanding of the unique wildlife that roams the American frontier.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Adventure

Themes: Wildlife conservation, Exploration of the American West, Animal behavior and ecology
