Synopsis: 
In 'A Course In Wood Turning', Milton and Wohlers take readers on an immersive journey into the captivating world of wood turning. Through detailed instructions, step-by-step illustrations, and insightful tips, they guide beginners and experienced woodworkers alike in mastering the art of turning wood on a lathe. From selecting the right tools and materials to creating intricate designs and finishes, this book offers a comprehensive and engaging exploration of the craft. With a focus on both technique and creativity, readers will discover the joy of transforming raw wood into beautiful and functional objects.

Genres: 
Crafts, Hobbies, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Woodworking, Artistry, Skill Development
