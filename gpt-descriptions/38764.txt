Synopsis: In 'A Roving Commission; Or, Through the Black Insurrection at Hayti', follow the thrilling adventures of a young British naval officer, Frank Hallett, as he embarks on a dangerous mission to the Caribbean island of Hayti. Tasked with gathering intelligence on the brewing insurrection and the mysterious leader behind it, Hallett finds himself navigating treacherous waters, facing deadly enemies, and forging unexpected alliances. As he delves deeper into the heart of the rebellion, Hallett must confront his own beliefs and loyalties, ultimately questioning the true nature of freedom and justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Colonialism, Rebellion, Loyalty, Identity
