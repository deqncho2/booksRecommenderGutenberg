Synopsis: In 'People Like That: A Novel' by Kate Langley Bosher, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious newcomer arrives, stirring up secrets and scandal among the tight-knit community. As the townspeople grapple with their own hidden desires and long-held grudges, they must confront the consequences of their actions and face the truth about themselves. With its richly drawn characters and gripping plot, this novel explores the complexities of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Redemption, Human nature
