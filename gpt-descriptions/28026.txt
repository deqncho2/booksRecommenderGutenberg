Synopsis: In 'Great Pianists on Piano Playing: Study Talks with Foremost Virtuosos', James Francis Cooke takes readers on an enlightening journey into the minds of renowned piano masters. Through a series of personal educational conferences, Cooke delves into the most modern ideas on technic, interpretation, style, and expression. This captivating book offers a unique opportunity to gain insights from the greatest pianists of the time, providing invaluable knowledge for aspiring musicians and piano enthusiasts alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Biography

Themes: Music education, Artistic expression, Mastery of technique, Interpretation and style
