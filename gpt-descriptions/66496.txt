Synopsis: In "The Omnipotent Self, a Study in Self-Deception and Self-Cure," Bousfield, Paul takes readers on a captivating journey into the depths of human psychology. Through a series of thought-provoking anecdotes and insightful analysis, the author explores the intricate web of self-deception that we all weave, and the potential for self-cure that lies within us. As the pages turn, readers will be compelled to question their own perceptions of reality and confront the hidden truths that shape their lives. This gripping exploration of the human psyche will leave readers both enlightened and inspired.

Genres: Psychology, Self-help, Non-fiction

Themes: Self-deception, Self-awareness, Personal growth
