Synopsis: 
In 'Bell's Cathedrals: Southwark Cathedral', George Worley takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and architecture of the former Collegiate Church of St. Saviour, also known as St. Mary Overie. From its humble beginnings as a small priory to its transformation into a grand cathedral, Worley delves into the fascinating stories and legends surrounding this iconic London landmark. With vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, the author brings to life the intricate details of the cathedral's fabric, while also shedding light on the college and the see associated with it. This book is a must-read for history enthusiasts and architecture lovers alike, offering a unique glimpse into the rich heritage of Southwark Cathedral.

Genres: History, Architecture, Travelogue
Themes: Religious institutions, Cultural heritage, Urban history
