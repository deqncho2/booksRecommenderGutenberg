Synopsis: In "Dr. Bullivant," Hawthorne introduces us to a mysterious and enigmatic character who possesses an uncanny ability to heal the sick. As the story unfolds, we are drawn into the intriguing world of Dr. Bullivant, where his extraordinary powers and the secrets surrounding his past intertwine with the lives of those he encounters. With each patient he heals, questions arise about the source of his power and the true nature of his intentions. As the narrative unfolds, the reader is left wondering whether Dr. Bullivant is a savior or a harbinger of something more sinister.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Power and its consequences, the nature of good and evil, the limits of human understanding
