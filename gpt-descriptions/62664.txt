Synopsis: 
In "Three Little Lines: Silverton Railroad; Silverton, Gladstone and Northerly; Silverton Northern" by Josie Moore Crum, embark on a captivating journey through the history of the Silverton Railroad and its two sister lines, the Silverton, Gladstone and Northerly, and the Silverton Northern. Discover the challenges, triumphs, and remarkable stories of the people who built and operated these iconic railways, as well as the impact they had on the communities they served. From breathtaking mountain landscapes to daring engineering feats, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the rich heritage and enduring legacy of these three little lines.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Transportation, Local History

Themes: Railroad history, Community development, Engineering achievements
