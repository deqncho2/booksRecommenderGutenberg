Synopsis: In 'Tabby' by Winston K. Marks, a mysterious feline named Tabby enters the life of a struggling writer, bringing with her an enigmatic charm and a series of inexplicable events. As the writer becomes increasingly entangled in Tabby's world, he must confront the secrets she holds and the profound impact she has on his own existence. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn deeper into a captivating tale that blurs the lines between reality and imagination.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Perception, Fate, Supernatural
