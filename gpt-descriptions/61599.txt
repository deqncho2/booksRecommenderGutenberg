Synopsis: 
In 'The Gardens of the Sun: A naturalist's journal on the mountains and in the forests and swamps of Borneo and the Sulu Archipelago', Frederick William Burbidge takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the lush landscapes of Borneo and the Sulu Archipelago. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Burbidge explores the diverse flora and fauna that inhabit these remote regions, uncovering hidden wonders and encountering unexpected challenges along the way. From the towering mountains to the dense forests and mysterious swamps, this naturalist's journal offers a fascinating glimpse into the untamed beauty of these tropical paradises.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travelogue, Natural History

Themes: Exploration, Biodiversity, Conservation
