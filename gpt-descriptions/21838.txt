Synopsis: 
'Which? Or, Between Two Women' by Daudet, Ernest is a gripping tale that delves into the complex dynamics of love, desire, and loyalty. Set in 19th-century France, the story follows a young man named Ernest who finds himself torn between two captivating women. As he navigates the treacherous waters of passion and commitment, Ernest must confront his own desires and make a choice that will have far-reaching consequences. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this novel explores the depths of human emotions and the complexities of relationships.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Loyalty and betrayal
