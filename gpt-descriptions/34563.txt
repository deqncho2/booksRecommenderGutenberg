Synopsis: In 'Sober by Act of Parliament' by Fred A. McKenzie, a mysterious and enigmatic stranger arrives in a small town, bringing with him a proposal that could change the lives of its inhabitants forever. As the townspeople grapple with the consequences of this proposition, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human desires is revealed. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this novel explores the depths of human nature and the power of choice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Morality, Power, Redemption
