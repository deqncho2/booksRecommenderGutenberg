Synopsis: In 'The Convolvulus: A Comedy in Three Acts' by Norton Allen, a group of eccentric characters find themselves entangled in a series of hilarious misunderstandings and misadventures. From mistaken identities to secret romances, this uproarious comedy takes the audience on a wild ride filled with witty dialogue and unexpected twists. As the plot unfolds, the characters must navigate their way through a web of deception and confusion, leading to uproarious consequences that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Mistaken identity, Deception, Love and romance, Humor
