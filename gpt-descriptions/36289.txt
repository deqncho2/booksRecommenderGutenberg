Synopsis:
'Ormond; Or, The Secret Witness' is a gripping and mysterious novel that follows the life of Constantia Dudley, a young woman who finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception. Constantia's life takes a dramatic turn when she becomes the secret witness to a shocking event, forcing her to navigate a dangerous world filled with hidden identities, forbidden love, and unexpected alliances. As she delves deeper into the truth, Constantia must confront her own fears and make difficult choices that will shape her destiny. With its intricate plot and captivating characters, 'Ormond; Or, The Secret Witness' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity, Forbidden love
