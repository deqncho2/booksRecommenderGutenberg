Synopsis: 

Embark on a thrilling journey across the vast oceans as Frederic Stanhope Hill, an experienced sailor, shares his captivating tales in 'Twenty years at sea: Leaves from my old log-books'. From treacherous storms to encounters with exotic creatures, Hill's log-books offer a glimpse into the extraordinary adventures and challenges he faced during his two decades at sea. As he navigates through uncharted waters and explores distant lands, the reader is transported to a world filled with danger, excitement, and the beauty of the open sea. 

Genres: Adventure, Memoir, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human resilience
