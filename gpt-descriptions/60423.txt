Synopsis: In 'Harper's Round Table, January 12, 1897', a group of young adventurers embarks on a thrilling journey filled with mystery and danger. As they navigate through uncharted territories, they encounter unexpected allies and face formidable challenges that test their courage and resourcefulness. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly anticipating the outcome.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
