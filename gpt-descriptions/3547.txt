Synopsis: 

In "See America First," Herr and Hiestand take readers on an exhilarating journey across the United States, exploring the hidden gems and breathtaking landscapes that make up this vast and diverse country. From the towering peaks of the Rocky Mountains to the vibrant streets of New Orleans, the authors capture the essence of America's natural beauty and cultural richness through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes. As they traverse the nation, encountering both the familiar and the unexpected, readers are transported to a world of awe-inspiring wonders and unforgettable experiences. 

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, National identity, Appreciation of nature and culture
