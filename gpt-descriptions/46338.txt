Synopsis: 
'A Medley of Weather Lore' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of weather phenomena. From ancient myths and legends to modern scientific explanations, this book explores the rich tapestry of weather lore from around the globe. Through a collection of intriguing stories, captivating anecdotes, and surprising facts, readers will uncover the hidden secrets behind thunderstorms, rainbows, hurricanes, and more. Prepare to be enthralled by the captivating tales and gain a deeper understanding of the powerful forces that shape our weather.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Folklore

Themes: Weather phenomena, Mythology, Cultural beliefs, Scientific explanations
