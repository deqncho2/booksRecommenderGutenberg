Synopsis: 

'Underground Treasures: How and Where to Find Them: A Key for the Ready Determination of All the Useful Minerals Within the United States' by James Orton is a captivating guide that takes readers on an exhilarating journey into the depths of the Earth. Filled with intriguing anecdotes and practical advice, this book unveils the hidden world of underground treasures and reveals the secrets to locating valuable minerals across the United States. From shimmering gemstones to precious metals, Orton's comprehensive exploration offers a thrilling glimpse into the vast wealth that lies beneath our feet. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Geology, Mineralogy, Treasure hunting
