Synopsis: In 'Leila at Home: a continuation of Leila in England', we follow the captivating journey of Leila as she returns to her homeland after her adventures in England. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century India, this enthralling tale delves into Leila's struggles, triumphs, and the complexities of her relationships. As she navigates the societal expectations and cultural clashes, Leila's resilience and determination shine through, leading her on a path of self-discovery and personal growth. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this sequel promises to transport readers to a world filled with intrigue, passion, and the timeless pursuit of identity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Cultural identity, Gender roles, Love and relationships, Self-discovery
