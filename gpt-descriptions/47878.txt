Synopsis: 
In 'Spanish Arms and Armour: Being a Historical and Descriptive Account of the Royal Armoury of Madrid', Albert Frederick Calvert takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and exquisite craftsmanship of Spanish arms and armor. From the legendary swords of El Cid to the intricately designed suits of armor worn by Spanish knights, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the Royal Armoury of Madrid. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Calvert brings to life the stories behind these remarkable artifacts, shedding light on the cultural, artistic, and military significance of Spanish arms and armor throughout the centuries.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Art history, Military history

Themes: Spanish history, Cultural heritage, Artistic craftsmanship, Military technology
