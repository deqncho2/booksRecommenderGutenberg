Synopsis: In 'The Gold that Glitters: The Mistakes of Jenny Lavender' by Emily Sarah Holt, we are introduced to Jenny Lavender, a young woman with dreams of a better life. When an unexpected opportunity arises, Jenny finds herself caught up in a world of wealth, glamour, and deceit. As she navigates this new world, Jenny must confront the consequences of her choices and the true nature of those around her. With secrets lurking behind every corner, will Jenny be able to uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Identity
