Synopsis: In a world where manners are everything, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception as she navigates the treacherous waters of high society. As she strives to maintain her polite facade, she uncovers a hidden world of power struggles, forbidden love, and unexpected alliances. With each encounter, she must decide whether to conform to societal expectations or follow her own path, risking everything she holds dear. 'Be Polite to All' is a gripping tale of intrigue and self-discovery that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Social expectations, Identity, Power dynamics
