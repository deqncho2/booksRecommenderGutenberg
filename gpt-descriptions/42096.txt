Synopsis: In 'The King of the Mountains' by Edmond About, a young man named Jean Renaud embarks on a thrilling adventure in the Swiss Alps. After a chance encounter with a mysterious stranger, Jean finds himself caught up in a dangerous game of power and deception. As he navigates treacherous mountain paths and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Jean must uncover the truth behind the stranger's identity and his own destiny. With breathtaking landscapes and heart-pounding suspense, 'The King of the Mountains' is a captivating tale of courage, betrayal, and the pursuit of truth.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Power, Deception
