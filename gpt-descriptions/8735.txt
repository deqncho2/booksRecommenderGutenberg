Synopsis: In 'The Revolutions of Time' by Jonathan Dunn, a brilliant scientist discovers a mysterious artifact that has the power to manipulate time itself. As he delves deeper into its secrets, he becomes entangled in a thrilling race against time, facing unexpected dangers and uncovering shocking truths about the nature of reality. With each revolution of the artifact, the boundaries of time blur, leading to mind-bending consequences that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Time manipulation, Existentialism, Consequences of scientific discovery
