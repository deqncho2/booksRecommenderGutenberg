Synopsis: In 'The Place Where Chicago Was' by Jim Harmon, a mysterious event causes the entire city of Chicago to vanish overnight, leaving behind only an empty void. As the world grapples with the disappearance of one of its major cities, a group of unlikely heroes embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind this inexplicable phenomenon. With time running out and the fate of Chicago hanging in the balance, they must confront their own fears and confront the unimaginable forces at play.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Loss and grief, Identity, Conspiracy, Redemption
