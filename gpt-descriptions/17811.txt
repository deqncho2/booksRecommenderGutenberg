Synopsis: 
In 'Grace Harlowe's Junior Year at High School: Or, Fast Friends in the Sororities' by Josephine Chase, readers are taken on a thrilling journey as Grace Harlowe enters her junior year at high school. As Grace navigates the complexities of sorority life, she finds herself forming fast and unexpected friendships that will shape her future. With secrets, rivalries, and challenges at every turn, Grace must rely on her wit and determination to overcome the obstacles that come her way. Will she be able to maintain her integrity and loyalty amidst the pressures of high school society?

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Coming-of-Age, School Drama

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Identity, Social Hierarchy
