Synopsis: In 'The Unspeakable Perk' by Samuel Hopkins Adams, a young journalist named Jimmy Torrance finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a secret society with a sinister agenda. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of the city, Jimmy must navigate a treacherous world of corruption, deceit, and hidden identities. With his life on the line, he must uncover the truth before it's too late, all while grappling with his own personal demons and the consequences of his choices.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Identity, Morality
