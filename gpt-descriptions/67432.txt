Synopsis: In 'An Experiment in Altruism' by Margaret Pollock Sherwood, a brilliant scientist embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that challenges the very essence of human nature. As the experiment unfolds, unexpected consequences arise, leading to a thought-provoking exploration of the limits of selflessness and the complexities of human behavior. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning their own beliefs and pondering the true nature of altruism.

Genres: Science Fiction, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Altruism, Human Nature, Ethics, Morality
