Synopsis: 
In 'The Manoeuvring Mother (vol. 1 of 3)' by Charlotte Campbell, Lady Bury, readers are introduced to the intriguing world of Lady Juliana, a woman determined to secure advantageous marriages for her daughters. As she navigates the complex social hierarchy of Regency England, Lady Juliana employs various tactics and schemes to manipulate potential suitors and ensure her daughters' futures. However, her plans are not without consequences, and as the story unfolds, secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances emerge, threatening to unravel Lady Juliana's carefully crafted web of manipulation.

Genres: 
Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: 
Social climbing, Family dynamics, Love and marriage, Deception
