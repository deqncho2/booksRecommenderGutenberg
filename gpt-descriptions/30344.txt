Synopsis:
'The Fortunate Mistress (Parts 1 and 2): or a History of the Life of Mademoiselle de Beleau Known by the Name of the Lady Roxana' follows the captivating story of a young woman named Roxana, who finds herself navigating the treacherous world of 17th-century London. After a series of unfortunate events, Roxana is forced to make difficult choices in order to survive and secure her place in society. As she rises from poverty to wealth, she becomes entangled in a web of deceit, passion, and moral ambiguity. Will Roxana be able to maintain her fortune and reputation, or will her past catch up with her?

Genres:
Historical fiction, adventure, romance

Themes:
Identity, morality, social class, survival
