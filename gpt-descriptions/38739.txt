Synopsis: In 'The Wright Brothers' Engines and Their Design' by Leonard S. Hobbs, readers are taken on a captivating journey into the world of aviation and the groundbreaking engines that powered the Wright brothers' historic flights. From their early experiments to the development of their own engine, this book delves into the intricate details of their design process, the challenges they faced, and the innovative solutions they devised. With vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, Hobbs brings to life the relentless pursuit of the Wright brothers to conquer the skies and revolutionize the world of aviation.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Engineering

Themes: Innovation, Determination, Pioneering Spirit
