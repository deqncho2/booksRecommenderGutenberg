Synopsis: 
In 'The Motor Boys in Mexico; Or, The Secret of the Buried City' by Clarence Young, the adventurous trio of Jack, Mark, and Andy find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey to Mexico. When they stumble upon a mysterious map leading to a hidden city, they can't resist the temptation to uncover its secrets. As they navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter dangerous adversaries, and face unexpected challenges, the Motor Boys must rely on their wit, courage, and trust in each other to unravel the enigma of the buried city. Will they uncover the truth, or will their quest lead them into unforeseen danger?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
