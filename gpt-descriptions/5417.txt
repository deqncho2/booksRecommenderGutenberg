Synopsis: 
In 'Struggling Upward, or Luke Larkin's Luck' by Horatio Alger, Jr., we follow the journey of Luke Larkin, a young and ambitious boy who dreams of a better life. Luke faces numerous challenges and setbacks as he strives to overcome his humble beginnings and achieve success. With determination and a bit of luck, Luke's life takes unexpected turns, leading him on a path filled with adventure, friendship, and the pursuit of his dreams. Will Luke's luck hold out, or will he be forced to face the harsh realities of life? Discover the captivating story of Luke Larkin as he struggles to rise above his circumstances and find his place in the world.

Genres: 
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Rags-to-riches

Themes: 
Ambition, Perseverance, Social mobility
