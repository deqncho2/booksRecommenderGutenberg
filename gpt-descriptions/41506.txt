Synopsis: In "Dorothy Wordsworth: The Story of a Sister's Love," author Edmund Lee delves into the extraordinary bond between Dorothy Wordsworth and her brother, the renowned poet William Wordsworth. Through intimate letters, diaries, and personal accounts, Lee uncovers the depth of Dorothy's unwavering support, sacrifice, and love for her brother, as they navigate the challenges of life, creativity, and fame. From their idyllic childhood in the Lake District to their shared adventures and struggles, this captivating biography offers a unique perspective on the life of a remarkable woman who played an instrumental role in shaping one of the greatest literary minds of all time.

Genres: Biography, Non-fiction, Literary Criticism

Themes: Sibling love and devotion, Creative collaboration, The role of women in literary history
