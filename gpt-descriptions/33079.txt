Synopsis: 
In "My Trip Around the World: August, 1895-May, 1896" by Eleonora Hunt, embark on a captivating journey as the author recounts her extraordinary adventure across the globe. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene landscapes of Asia, Hunt's vivid descriptions transport readers to each destination, immersing them in the diverse cultures, breathtaking sights, and unexpected encounters she experienced along the way. As she navigates through both familiar and unfamiliar territories, Hunt's personal growth and self-discovery become intertwined with the mesmerizing tales of the people she meets, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover what lies ahead.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Self-discovery
