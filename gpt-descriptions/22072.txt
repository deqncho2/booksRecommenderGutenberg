Synopsis: 

'Folk-Lore and Legends: North American Indian' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Native American folklore and legends. From ancient creation myths to tales of heroic warriors and mystical creatures, this collection offers a glimpse into the vibrant oral traditions of various tribes across North America. Each story is filled with enchanting imagery, supernatural elements, and profound wisdom, transporting readers to a world where spirits roam the earth and the boundaries between reality and myth blur. As readers delve into these captivating narratives, they will discover the profound connection between nature, humanity, and the spiritual realm, gaining a deeper understanding of the Native American culture and their profound reverence for the natural world.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Native American Studies

Themes: Spirituality, Nature, Oral Tradition, Cultural Identity
