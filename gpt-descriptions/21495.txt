Synopsis: In 'To The West' by George Manville Fenn, a young boy named Tom embarks on a thrilling adventure across the American West in search of his missing father. As he faces numerous challenges and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Tom must rely on his wit, courage, and determination to unravel the mysteries that lie ahead. With danger lurking at every turn, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Tom's journey to uncover the truth.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Family, Courage, Survival
