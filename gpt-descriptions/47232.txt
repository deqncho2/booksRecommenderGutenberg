Synopsis: In 'The Book of Roses' by Parkman, Francis, a young botanist embarks on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of roses. As he delves into the history, symbolism, and cultivation of these exquisite flowers, he uncovers a hidden secret that could change the course of his life forever. With each turn of the page, the protagonist unravels the mysteries surrounding the power and allure of roses, leading him on a quest that intertwines love, beauty, and the fragility of existence.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and Passion, Nature and Beauty, Secrets and Discovery
