Synopsis: In 'Tarnished Silver' by Mary Frances Outram, a young woman named Emily finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits a mysterious silver locket from her late grandmother. As she delves into her family's past, Emily uncovers a hidden world of forbidden love, betrayal, and long-held grudges. With each revelation, she becomes more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means putting her own life at risk. Set against the backdrop of a quaint English village, this gripping tale of love, loss, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
