Synopsis: In 'Robert Hardy's Seven Days: A Dream and Its Consequences' by Sheldon, Charles M., readers are taken on a captivating journey through the life of Robert Hardy, a man who experiences a vivid dream that turns his world upside down. As the dream unfolds, Robert finds himself questioning the boundaries between reality and imagination, leading him on a quest to uncover the truth behind his dream and its profound consequences. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking revelations, this gripping tale explores the power of dreams and the impact they can have on our lives.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Dreams and Reality, Identity and Perception
