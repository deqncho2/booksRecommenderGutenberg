Synopsis: 

Step into the world of exquisite craftsmanship and timeless beauty with 'Ancient and Modern Furniture and Woodwork' by John Hungerford Pollen. This captivating book takes readers on a journey through the rich history of furniture and woodwork, exploring the intricate designs and techniques employed by master craftsmen throughout the ages. From ancient civilizations to the modern era, Pollen delves into the fascinating stories behind each piece, unveiling the secrets of their creation and the cultural significance they hold. With stunning illustrations and detailed descriptions, this book is a treasure trove for art enthusiasts, history buffs, and anyone who appreciates the artistry of woodwork.

Genres: Art History, Design, Craftsmanship

Themes: Cultural Significance, Craftsmanship, Historical Evolution
