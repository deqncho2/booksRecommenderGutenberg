Synopsis: In 'The Outbreak of Peace' by H. B. Fyfe, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in the midst of a global conflict that threatens to tear the world apart. As he navigates through the chaos and uncertainty, he discovers a hidden power within himself that could hold the key to restoring peace. With unexpected allies and dangerous adversaries, he embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth and bring about a much-needed resolution.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Power of the individual, Conflict and resolution
