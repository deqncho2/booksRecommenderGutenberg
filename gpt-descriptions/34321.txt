Synopsis: 

In 'Syndrome' by Hoover, Thomas, a brilliant neurologist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a groundbreaking medical discovery. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his career but also his life. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous path of deceit and betrayal to uncover the truth and save himself from the clutches of a powerful enemy.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Medical Fiction

Themes: Scientific ethics, Conspiracy, Redemption
