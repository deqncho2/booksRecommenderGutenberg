Synopsis: In 'Mary Christmas' by Chase, Mary Ellen, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a whirlwind of unexpected events during the holiday season. As she navigates through a series of mysterious encounters and heartwarming moments, Mary discovers the true meaning of Christmas and the power of love and forgiveness. With each passing day, she unravels secrets that will forever change her life and those around her. This heartwarming tale will keep readers captivated as they follow Mary's journey of self-discovery and the magic that can be found during the most wonderful time of the year.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Holiday

Themes: Love, Forgiveness, Self-discovery
