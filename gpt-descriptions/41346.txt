Synopsis: 
In 'Bellini' by Hay, George, a renowned art historian embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious painting by the famous Italian artist, Giovanni Bellini. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic world of art, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each clue he uncovers, the stakes rise higher, leading to a heart-pounding climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Art, Deception, Identity, Betrayal
