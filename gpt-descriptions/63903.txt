Synopsis: In "The Duke's Daughter; and, The Fugitives; vol. 3/3" by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), the captivating conclusion to the series unfolds as Lady Edith, the Duke's daughter, finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and danger. With her life at stake, she must navigate treacherous paths, unexpected alliances, and the complexities of her own heart. As the truth unravels, Lady Edith's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption, Love and Sacrifice
