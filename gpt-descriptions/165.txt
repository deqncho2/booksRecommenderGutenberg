Synopsis: In the bustling city of San Francisco, McTeague, a simple dentist, finds himself entangled in a web of love, greed, and obsession. As his life takes unexpected turns, McTeague's desires and ambitions clash with the realities of his surroundings, leading to a gripping tale of human nature and the consequences of unchecked desires.

Genres: Naturalism, Realism, Tragedy

Themes: Greed, Fate, Social Darwinism, Class Struggle
