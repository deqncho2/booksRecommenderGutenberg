Synopsis: In 'The Nameless Castle' by Jókai, Mór, a young nobleman inherits a mysterious castle that holds a dark secret. As he delves into the castle's history, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, romance, and supernatural occurrences. With each discovery, the protagonist uncovers hidden truths that challenge his beliefs and threaten his very existence. As he navigates the treacherous corridors of the castle, he must confront his own fears and make difficult choices that will determine his fate.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Historical fiction, Mystery

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Love and betrayal, Supernatural elements, Identity and self-discovery
