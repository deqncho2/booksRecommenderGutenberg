Synopsis:
In the second volume of "An Apology for the Life of Mr. Colley Cibber," the renowned actor and playwright, Colley Cibber, continues to regale readers with his captivating and often humorous anecdotes from his life in the theater. From his early struggles as a young actor to his rise to fame as the manager of the Theatre Royal, Cibber shares his experiences with wit and charm. This edition includes additional notes and a supplement that further enriches the narrative, providing a deeper understanding of the theatrical world of the time.

Genres:
Autobiography, Drama, Literary Criticism

Themes:
Theater and Performance, Success and Failure, The Art of Storytelling
