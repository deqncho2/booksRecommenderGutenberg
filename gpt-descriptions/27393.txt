Synopsis: In 'Medal of Honor' by Mack Reynolds, a decorated war veteran finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and conspiracy when he is unexpectedly awarded the highest military honor. As he delves deeper into the circumstances surrounding the medal, he uncovers a shocking truth that could change the course of history. With danger lurking at every turn, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of power and deception to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Thriller, Political Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Honor, Betrayal, Power, Truth
