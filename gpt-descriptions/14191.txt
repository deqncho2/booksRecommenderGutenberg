Synopsis: In "As We Are and As We May Be," Walter Besant takes readers on a captivating journey through the possibilities of human evolution and the potential for a utopian society. Through a series of thought-provoking essays, Besant explores the advancements in science, technology, and social structures that could shape a future where humanity thrives. From the potential of artificial intelligence to the reimagining of education and the role of women in society, Besant presents a compelling vision of a world transformed. As readers delve into this visionary work, they are invited to ponder the boundless potential of human progress and the profound impact it could have on our lives.

Genres: Science Fiction, Utopian Literature, Social Commentary

Themes: Human evolution, Technological advancements, Utopian society, Gender equality, Education reform
