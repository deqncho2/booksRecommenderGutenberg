Synopsis: In 'Unborn Tomorrow' by Reynolds, Mack, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but the fate of the entire world. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of deception and betrayal to protect his discovery and ensure a future that is not tainted by the shadows of the past.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific ethics, Consequences of knowledge, Betrayal
