Synopsis: In 'Here, There and Everywhere' by Hamilton, Frederic, Lord, a young woman embarks on a thrilling journey across time and space, encountering mysterious worlds and unexpected adventures. As she navigates through different dimensions, she discovers the power of love, the importance of self-discovery, and the boundless possibilities that exist beyond our own reality. With each new encounter, she unravels the secrets of the universe and learns that the true meaning of life lies in the connections we make along the way.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Love, Self-discovery, Multiverse, Existentialism
