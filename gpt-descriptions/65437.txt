Synopsis: In 'Beware, the Usurpers!' by Robert W. Krepps, a small coastal town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious group of individuals arrives, claiming to be the rightful rulers. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the townspeople must uncover the truth behind these usurpers before their peaceful community is torn apart. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Power struggles, Identity, Deception
