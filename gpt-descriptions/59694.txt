Synopsis: 

'American Masters of Painting: Being Brief Appreciations of Some American Painters' by Charles H. Caffin is a captivating exploration of the lives and works of renowned American painters. Through concise and insightful appreciations, Caffin delves into the artistic journeys of these masters, shedding light on their unique styles, inspirations, and contributions to the world of art. From the vibrant landscapes of Thomas Cole to the evocative portraits of John Singer Sargent, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the minds and talents of these influential artists. 

Genres: Art, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, American identity, Influence of environment on art
