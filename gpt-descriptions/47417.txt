Synopsis: In 'The Motor Boys After a Fortune; or, The Hut on Snake Island' by Clarence Young, the adventurous Motor Boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey to uncover a hidden fortune. When they stumble upon a mysterious hut on Snake Island, they become entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected twists. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter suspicious characters, the Motor Boys must rely on their wit, courage, and resourcefulness to solve the puzzle and claim the fortune that awaits them.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Treasure hunting, Friendship, Bravery
