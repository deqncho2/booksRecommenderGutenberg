Synopsis: 

'Sane Sex Life and Sane Sex Living: Some Things That All Sane People Ought to Know About Sex Nature and Sex Functioning; Its Place in the Economy of Life, Its Proper Training and Righteous Exercise' is a groundbreaking work that delves into the often taboo subject of human sexuality. Written by Harland William Long, this book explores the intricacies of sex nature and sex functioning, providing valuable insights into its place in the economy of life. With a focus on proper training and righteous exercise, the author offers a comprehensive guide that all sane individuals should know about. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Psychology

Themes: Human sexuality, Education, Personal development
