Synopsis: In "The Camp Fire Girls on a Yacht," a group of adventurous young girls embark on a thrilling journey aboard a luxurious yacht. As they set sail, they encounter unexpected challenges and mysteries that test their courage and teamwork. With their Camp Fire Girls skills and determination, they navigate through treacherous waters, uncover hidden secrets, and forge lifelong friendships. Will they be able to overcome the obstacles that come their way and make it back safely to shore?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Teamwork
