Synopsis: In 'Airplane Boys in the Black Woods' by Craine, E. J., two adventurous brothers find themselves in the midst of a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon a hidden secret in the dark and mysterious Black Woods. As they navigate through dangerous encounters and unexpected twists, the boys must rely on their wit and courage to unravel the truth and save the day. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets lurking within the Black Woods.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Brotherhood
