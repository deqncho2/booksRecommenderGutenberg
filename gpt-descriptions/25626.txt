Synopsis: In 'The Girl Scouts at Bellaire; Or, Maid Mary's Awakening' by Lilian Garis, a group of adventurous Girl Scouts find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey when they stumble upon a mysterious secret hidden within the walls of Bellaire. As they uncover the truth, they must confront their own fears and learn the importance of friendship, loyalty, and bravery. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Bravery, Self-discovery
