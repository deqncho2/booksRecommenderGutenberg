Synopsis: In 'The Velvet Glove' by Harry Harrison, a young man named Jerry Reed is thrust into a world of political intrigue and rebellion when he joins the ranks of the Peace Patrol. Tasked with maintaining order in a dystopian society, Jerry soon discovers that not everything is as it seems. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the oppressive regime, he must navigate a web of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances. With his life on the line, Jerry must decide where his loyalties truly lie and whether he has the courage to fight for what he believes in.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Political Thriller

Themes: Rebellion, Oppression, Loyalty, Deception
