Synopsis: In 'The Quest of the Silver Swan: A land and sea tale for boys', young Tom embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him from the bustling streets of London to the treacherous waters of the high seas. With the help of a mysterious map and a courageous crew, Tom sets out to uncover the secrets of the legendary Silver Swan, a mythical ship said to hold unimaginable treasures. As they face perilous challenges and encounter fascinating characters along the way, Tom learns the true meaning of friendship, bravery, and the power of perseverance.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Courage, Perseverance
