Synopsis: In 'Captain June' by Alice Caldwell Hegan Rice, a young woman named June finds herself thrust into the world of riverboat captains, challenging societal norms and expectations. As she navigates the treacherous waters of the Ohio River, June must confront her own fears and insecurities while proving her worth in a male-dominated profession. Along the way, she encounters unexpected allies and faces formidable adversaries, all while discovering the true meaning of courage and determination.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Gender Roles, Perseverance, Self-Discovery
