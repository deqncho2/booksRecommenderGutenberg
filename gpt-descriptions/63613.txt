Synopsis: In 'The Space Between' by Gilbert, Robert E., a mysterious phenomenon causes a small town to become trapped in a parallel dimension, where time and space are distorted. As the residents struggle to find a way back home, they uncover dark secrets and face unexpected challenges that test their courage and resilience. With each passing day, the line between reality and illusion blurs, leaving them questioning their own sanity. This gripping tale of survival and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the mysteries that lie within 'The Space Between'.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Parallel dimensions, Identity, Perception of reality
