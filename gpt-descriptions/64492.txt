Synopsis: In 'Last Run on Venus' by McKimmey, James, a group of skilled astronauts embarks on a dangerous mission to explore the mysterious and treacherous terrain of Venus. As they navigate through the planet's hostile environment, they encounter unexpected challenges and face the haunting remnants of a forgotten civilization. With time running out and their lives hanging in the balance, the astronauts must unravel the secrets of Venus before it consumes them all.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Ancient Civilizations
