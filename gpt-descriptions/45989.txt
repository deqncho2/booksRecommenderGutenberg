Synopsis: In 'Grace Harlowe's Overland Riders in the High Sierras', Grace and her adventurous friends embark on a thrilling journey through the majestic High Sierras. As they navigate treacherous trails, encounter mysterious strangers, and face unexpected dangers, the Overland Riders must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. With breathtaking landscapes and heart-pounding escapades, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to join Grace and her companions on their exhilarating expedition.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Western

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
