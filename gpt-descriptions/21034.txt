Synopsis: In "The Corner House Girls at School" by Grace Brooks Hill, the four spirited sisters find themselves embarking on a thrilling new adventure as they enter a prestigious boarding school. From navigating the complexities of friendships and rivalries to uncovering a mysterious secret, the girls must rely on their wit and resilience to overcome the challenges that come their way. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, this captivating story will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Discovery
