Synopsis: In "Mahatma Gandhi: The Man Who Became One With the Universal Being," Romain Rolland delves into the extraordinary life of one of the most influential figures of the 20th century. This captivating biography takes readers on a journey through Gandhi's transformation from a young lawyer to a spiritual leader who would inspire millions with his philosophy of nonviolence and civil disobedience. Rolland explores the challenges Gandhi faced, the sacrifices he made, and the profound impact he had on India's struggle for independence. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, this book offers a profound exploration of the man behind the legend.

Genres: Biography, History, Philosophy

Themes: Nonviolence, Civil Disobedience, Spiritual Awakening, Independence, Leadership
