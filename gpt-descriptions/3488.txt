Synopsis: In "Great Catherine (Whom Glory Still Adores)" by Bernard Shaw, a witty and audacious British envoy finds himself entangled in a hilarious and unexpected encounter with the formidable Catherine the Great of Russia. As their meeting unfolds, the clash of their personalities and the power dynamics between them create a captivating and comical exploration of politics, diplomacy, and the pursuit of personal desires.

Genres: Comedy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power dynamics, Politics, Personal ambition
