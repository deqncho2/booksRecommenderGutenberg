Synopsis: 
In 'Six Girls and the Tea Room' by Marion Ames Taggart, six young women from different walks of life find themselves brought together by a shared passion for tea and a desire for independence. As they navigate the challenges of running a tea room in a bustling city, they discover the power of friendship, the importance of following their dreams, and the strength that comes from embracing their individuality. With each girl bringing her unique talents and perspectives to the table, they create a haven where customers can find solace, inspiration, and a warm cup of tea. But as they face unexpected obstacles and personal struggles, their bond is put to the test, forcing them to confront their fears and redefine their own definitions of success.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Women's Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Pursuit of Dreams, Empowerment
