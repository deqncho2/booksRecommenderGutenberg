Synopsis: In 'Cosmic Tragedy' by Gardiner, Thomas S., a brilliant astrophysicist stumbles upon a shocking discovery that threatens to unravel the very fabric of the universe. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he becomes entangled in a web of cosmic secrets, ancient prophecies, and a race against time to prevent a catastrophic event. With mind-bending twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will leave readers questioning the boundaries of science and the fate of humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Existentialism, the nature of reality, the power of knowledge
