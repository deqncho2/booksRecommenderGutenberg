Synopsis: In the name of Time: A Tragedy by Field, Michael is a gripping tale that follows the life of a brilliant scientist, Dr. Jonathan Hartley, who stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of moral dilemmas, personal sacrifices, and unforeseen consequences. With time ticking away, Dr. Hartley must confront the dark side of his creation and make a choice that will determine the fate of not only his own existence but also the future of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Ethics, Time, Sacrifice, Consequences
