Synopsis: In the Days of the Comet takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a disillusioned man named William, who finds himself caught in the midst of a cosmic event that forever alters the course of humanity. As a mysterious comet passes by Earth, it releases a transformative gas that brings about profound changes in people's minds and hearts. William's personal struggles and the societal upheaval that follows the comet's visit intertwine, leading him to question the nature of love, power, and the true meaning of life. This thought-provoking novel explores the profound impact of a celestial event on human existence, offering a unique perspective on the potential for change and redemption.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Transformation, Love, Social Change, Human Nature
