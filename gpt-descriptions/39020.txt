Synopsis: 
In 'Bert Wilson's Fadeaway Ball' by Duffield, J. W., Bert Wilson, a talented young baseball player, discovers a mysterious old baseball that possesses an incredible power. As he learns to control the ball's unique ability, Bert's life takes an unexpected turn, leading him on a thrilling journey filled with challenges, rivalries, and unexpected friendships. With each pitch, Bert's fate hangs in the balance, and he must navigate the complexities of the game and his own personal growth to unlock the true potential of the fadeaway ball.

Genres: 
Sports, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: 
Ambition, Friendship, Self-discovery
