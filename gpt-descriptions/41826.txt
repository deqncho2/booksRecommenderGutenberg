Synopsis: In 'Wild Heather' by L. T. Meade, a young woman named Heather finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she inherits a remote estate in the Scottish Highlands. As she delves into the secrets of her family's past, Heather uncovers a hidden world of ancient legends, forbidden love, and dark secrets that threaten to consume her. With the help of a rugged and enigmatic local guide, she must navigate treacherous landscapes and confront her own fears to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Love and sacrifice, Supernatural elements
