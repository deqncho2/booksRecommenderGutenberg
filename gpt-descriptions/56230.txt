Synopsis: In 'The Amethyst Cross' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious amethyst cross becomes the center of a gripping tale filled with secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As the cross changes hands, it sets off a chain of events that will test the characters' loyalties and lead them on a thrilling journey through the underbelly of society. With each twist and turn, the true power and significance of the amethyst cross is revealed, leaving the reader captivated until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
