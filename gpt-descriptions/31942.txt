Synopsis: In 'A Christian But a Roman' by Jókai, Mór, a young Roman soldier finds himself torn between his Christian faith and his loyalty to the Roman Empire. As he navigates the treacherous political landscape and faces personal challenges, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. This gripping tale of faith, duty, and sacrifice will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Religion, Loyalty, Identity
