Synopsis: In 'The Double Four' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious secret society known as the Double Four emerges, threatening to disrupt the balance of power in Europe. As the protagonist, a British diplomat, delves deeper into the enigmatic organization, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, espionage, and danger. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous world of hidden agendas and unexpected alliances to uncover the truth behind the Double Four and prevent a catastrophic event that could change the course of history.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Secret societies, Power struggles, Betrayal, Intrigue
