Synopsis: In 'Westover of Wanalah: A story of love and life in Old Virginia', George Cary Eggleston takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Old Virginia. Set against the backdrop of the Civil War, this novel follows the lives of the Westover family as they navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations. As the Westovers face the challenges of war and the changing dynamics of their beloved homeland, they must confront their own desires and aspirations. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Eggleston weaves a tale that explores the power of love and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love, Loyalty, Society, War, Personal Growth
