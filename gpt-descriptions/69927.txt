Synopsis: In the remote village of Filaria, a mysterious illness begins to plague the inhabitants, causing strange and terrifying symptoms. Dr. Robertson, a renowned infectious disease specialist, is called upon to investigate the outbreak. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, ancient folklore, and a sinister presence lurking in the shadows. With time running out and lives at stake, Dr. Robertson must race against the clock to unravel the truth and find a cure for the enigmatic Filaria loa.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Medical Fiction

Themes: Medical ethics, Folklore and superstition, Human resilience
