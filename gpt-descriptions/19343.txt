Synopsis: In 'The Making of Mary' by Jean N. McIlwraith, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery and transformation. As she navigates through the challenges of love, loss, and personal growth, Mary's life takes unexpected turns that force her to confront her deepest fears and desires. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant and ever-changing city, this captivating novel explores the complexities of identity, the power of resilience, and the beauty of embracing one's true self.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Contemporary

Themes: Self-discovery, Transformation, Love and Loss, Identity, Resilience
