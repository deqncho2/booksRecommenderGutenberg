Synopsis: In 'The Story of the Duchess of Cicogne and of Monsieur de Boulingrin: 1920' by Anatole France, a charming and enigmatic duchess finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and scandal when she becomes the object of desire for Monsieur de Boulingrin, a man with a mysterious past. As their paths cross and their lives become intertwined, the story takes unexpected twists and turns, revealing the complexities of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of happiness in the midst of societal expectations and personal desires.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and desire, Social expectations, Deception and secrets
