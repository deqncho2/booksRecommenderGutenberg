Synopsis: In "Molly Brown's Orchard Home," a young woman named Molly Brown finds herself inheriting an enchanting orchard home in the countryside. As she settles into her new life, Molly discovers that the orchard holds secrets and mysteries waiting to be unraveled. With the help of new friends and unexpected allies, she embarks on a journey that will challenge her beliefs and change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Self-discovery, Friendship, Love, Secrets
