Synopsis: 

In 'Four Famous American Writers: Washington Irving, Edgar Allan Poe, James Russell Lowell, Bayard Taylor: A Book for Young Americans' by Cody, Sherwin, embark on a captivating journey through the lives and works of four iconic American writers. Discover the enchanting tales of Washington Irving, the master of American folklore, and delve into the haunting and mysterious world of Edgar Allan Poe. Explore the poetic brilliance of James Russell Lowell and the adventurous spirit of Bayard Taylor. This book offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives and imaginations of these literary legends, providing a rich tapestry of American literature that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Biography, Literature, History

Themes: American literature, creativity, imagination, cultural heritage
