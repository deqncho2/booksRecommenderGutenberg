Synopsis: 
'Over Strand and Field: A Record of Travel through Brittany' takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Brittany, as experienced by the renowned author Gustave Flaubert. Through vivid descriptions and insightful observations, Flaubert paints a vivid portrait of the region's natural beauty, its charming villages, and the unique customs and traditions of its people. As he immerses himself in the local culture, Flaubert encounters a variety of fascinating characters and encounters unexpected adventures along the way. This travelogue offers a delightful blend of personal anecdotes, historical insights, and poetic musings, making it a captivating read for anyone seeking to explore the enchanting region of Brittany.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Natural beauty, Local customs and traditions
