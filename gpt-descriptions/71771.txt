Synopsis: In "The Radio Girls of Roselawn: Or, A Strange Message from the Air" by Margaret Penrose, a group of young women find themselves caught up in a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon a mysterious message transmitted through the airwaves. As they delve deeper into the world of radio broadcasting, they uncover a web of secrets and danger that puts their lives at risk. With their quick thinking and determination, the radio girls must use their skills to unravel the truth and save the day.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Female empowerment, Friendship, Technology and its impact on society
