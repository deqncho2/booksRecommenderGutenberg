Synopsis: In "The Man Who Was Afraid" by Maksim Gorky, we follow the story of a man consumed by fear and anxiety. As he navigates through life, his constant state of apprehension leads him down a path of isolation and self-doubt. However, an unexpected encounter with a mysterious stranger forces him to confront his deepest fears and question the nature of his existence. This gripping tale explores the complexities of human emotions and the transformative power of facing one's fears.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Drama, Literary fiction

Themes: Fear, Anxiety, Isolation, Self-discovery
