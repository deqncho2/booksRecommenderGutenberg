Synopsis: In 'Wolf's Head: 1911' by Charles Egbert Craddock, a young man named Wolf embarks on a thrilling adventure through the rugged mountains of Tennessee. Faced with danger, mystery, and unexpected alliances, Wolf must navigate a treacherous landscape while uncovering the secrets of his own past. As he delves deeper into the wilderness, he discovers that the true test of his character lies not only in survival but also in the choices he makes along the way.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Nature vs. Civilization
