Synopsis: 
'A Catalogue of Books in English Later than 1700, Vol. 3: Forming a portion of the library of Robert Hoe' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vast collection of books owned by Robert Hoe. This volume specifically focuses on English literature published after 1700, offering a glimpse into the literary treasures that shaped the cultural landscape of the time. From rare editions to notable works, this catalogue provides a fascinating exploration of the written word and the profound impact it has had on society.

Genres: Non-fiction, Bibliography, Literary History

Themes: English literature, Book collecting, Cultural influence
