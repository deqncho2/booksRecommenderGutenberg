Synopsis: In 'Slavery' by James L. Baker, a gripping tale unfolds as a young woman named Sarah finds herself caught in the clutches of a brutal slave trade. As she navigates the treacherous world of plantation life, Sarah's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected alliances and heart-wrenching sacrifices, this powerful narrative explores the depths of human strength and the fight for freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Adventure

Themes: Oppression, Resilience, Freedom, Human Rights
