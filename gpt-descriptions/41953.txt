Synopsis: In 'Cyrus Hall McCormick: His Life and Work', Casson delves into the captivating story of a man whose invention revolutionized agriculture and forever changed the course of American history. From his humble beginnings on a Virginia farm to his relentless pursuit of perfection, McCormick's journey is one of determination, innovation, and the relentless pursuit of a dream. Casson's vivid storytelling brings to life the challenges, triumphs, and personal sacrifices that shaped McCormick's extraordinary legacy.

Genres: Biography, History, Technology

Themes: Innovation, Entrepreneurship, American Industrial Revolution
