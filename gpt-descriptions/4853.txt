Synopsis: In "History of the United Netherlands, 1587c," John Lothrop Motley takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events that shaped the Dutch struggle for independence during the late 16th century. From political intrigue and military campaigns to religious conflicts and diplomatic maneuvering, Motley paints a vivid picture of the Dutch Republic's fight against Spanish rule. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for storytelling, the author brings to life the key figures and pivotal moments that defined this crucial period in European history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Independence and resistance, Religious conflict, Diplomacy and international relations
