Synopsis:
In 'Windfalls' by A. G. Gardiner, a seemingly ordinary man named John Smith unexpectedly comes into a large sum of money. As he navigates the challenges and temptations that come with newfound wealth, Smith's life takes a dramatic turn. From extravagant purchases to unexpected encounters, the story explores the impact of money on relationships, identity, and personal values. With twists and turns that keep readers guessing, 'Windfalls' is a captivating tale that delves into the complexities of human nature and the allure of fortune.

Genres:
Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes:
Wealth and its consequences, Identity and self-discovery, Morality and values
