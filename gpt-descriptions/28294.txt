Synopsis: 

In "Pilgrimage from the Alps to the Tiber: or, The influence of Romanism on trade, justice, and knowledge," James Aitken Wylie takes readers on a captivating journey through history, uncovering the hidden influence of Romanism on various aspects of society. From the bustling trade routes to the halls of justice and the pursuit of knowledge, Wylie reveals the far-reaching impact of Roman Catholicism. With meticulous research and compelling storytelling, this book sheds light on a powerful force that has shaped the world in ways we may not have realized.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Religious studies, Cultural critique

Themes: Influence of religion, Historical impact, Societal power dynamics
