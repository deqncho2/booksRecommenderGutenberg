Synopsis:
In 'Cecilia: A Story of Modern Rome', we are transported to the vibrant city of Rome, where the lives of two young women, Cecilia and Agnes, intertwine in unexpected ways. Cecilia, a talented artist, finds herself caught between her passion for her craft and the societal expectations placed upon her. Meanwhile, Agnes, a spirited and independent woman, navigates the complexities of love and desire in a city known for its romantic allure. As their paths converge, secrets are revealed, friendships are tested, and the true nature of love is explored in this captivating tale set against the backdrop of modern Rome.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Artistic expression, Gender roles, Love and desire
