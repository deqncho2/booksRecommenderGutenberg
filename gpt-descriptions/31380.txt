Synopsis: In 'Bred of the Desert: A Horse and a Romance' by Charles M. Horton, a captivating tale unfolds as a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn to the mysterious and untamed beauty of the Arabian desert. When she encounters a magnificent Arabian horse, their connection becomes the catalyst for a thrilling adventure that will test their courage and resilience. As Emily delves deeper into the world of Arabian horses, she discovers a hidden world of passion, danger, and unexpected romance. Will she be able to navigate the challenges that lie ahead and find her true destiny?

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and connection, the power of nature, self-discovery
