Synopsis:
'Captain Scraggs; Or, The Green-Pea Pirates' by Peter B. Kyne is a thrilling adventure set on the high seas. The story follows the misadventures of Captain Scraggs, a bumbling yet lovable character who dreams of becoming a successful pirate. Alongside his loyal crew, Captain Scraggs embarks on a series of hilarious and unpredictable escapades, encountering treacherous waters, rival pirates, and unexpected challenges. As the crew navigates through their comical and often perilous journey, readers will be captivated by the humorous dialogue, colorful characters, and the sheer audacity of Captain Scraggs' ambitions.

Genres: Adventure, Comedy, Nautical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Friendship, Perseverance
