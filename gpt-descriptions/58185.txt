Synopsis: In 'The Crystal Palace and Other Legends', Marie Harriette Frary and Charles Maurice Stebbins take readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting legends. From the mysterious Crystal Palace to the haunting tale of the Lady of the Lake, each legend is filled with intrigue, magic, and unexpected twists. As readers delve into these captivating stories, they will be transported to fantastical worlds where mythical creatures roam and ordinary people are faced with extraordinary challenges. With each turn of the page, the authors weave a tapestry of wonder and imagination that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Mythology

Themes: Magic, Adventure, Transformation, Fate, Legends
