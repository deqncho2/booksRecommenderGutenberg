Synopsis: In 'The Story of a White Rocking Horse' by Laura Lee Hope, a young girl named Dorothy receives a beautiful white rocking horse as a gift. Little does she know that this seemingly ordinary toy holds a magical secret that will take her on an extraordinary adventure. As Dorothy embarks on a journey filled with mystery and enchantment, she discovers the true power of imagination and the importance of believing in oneself. Will Dorothy be able to unlock the secrets of the white rocking horse and find her own inner strength?

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Self-discovery, Magic
