Synopsis: 

In the Ypres Salient: The Story of a Fortnight's Canadian Fighting, June 2-16, 1916 takes readers on a gripping journey through the intense and harrowing experiences of Canadian soldiers during a crucial period of World War I. Through vivid and detailed accounts, the book explores the bravery, resilience, and sacrifices made by these soldiers as they face the horrors of war in the Ypres Salient. From the chaos of the battlefield to the camaraderie among comrades, this book offers a compelling narrative that captures the essence of the Canadian fighting spirit during this pivotal time.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War literature

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Human resilience
