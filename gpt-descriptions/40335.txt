Synopsis: In "The Bath Keepers; Or, Paris in Those Days, v.1," Paul de Kock takes us on a captivating journey through the bustling streets of Paris in the 19th century. The story revolves around a group of characters who find themselves entangled in a web of love, desire, and scandal within the city's famous bathhouses. As secrets are revealed and passions ignite, the lives of these individuals become increasingly intertwined, leading to unexpected consequences and thrilling encounters. With vivid descriptions and a keen eye for human nature, de Kock paints a vivid picture of a bygone era, exploring the complexities of relationships and the pursuit of pleasure in a rapidly changing society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Scandal and Intrigue, Social Change
