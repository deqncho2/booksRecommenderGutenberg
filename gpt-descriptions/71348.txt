Synopsis: In 'Easy Money' by Sinclair, Bertrand W., a down-on-his-luck college student stumbles upon a mysterious opportunity to make quick cash. Intrigued by the allure of easy money, he embarks on a thrilling journey that takes him into the dark underbelly of the city, where danger and temptation lurk at every corner. As he delves deeper into this world of fast riches, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit, betrayal, and moral ambiguity. With his future hanging in the balance, he must decide how far he is willing to go for a taste of the good life.

Genres: Thriller, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Greed, Morality, Temptation, Consequences
