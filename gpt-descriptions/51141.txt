Synopsis: In 'Charlie Bell, The Waif of Elm Island' by Kellogg, Elijah, a young boy named Charlie finds himself alone and abandoned on Elm Island. With no memory of his past, he must navigate the challenges of survival and uncover the secrets that surround him. As Charlie embarks on a journey of self-discovery, he encounters unexpected friendships, dangerous adversaries, and a hidden treasure that could change his life forever. Will Charlie unravel the mysteries of his past and find a place to call home?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Resilience
