Synopsis: In the small, mysterious town of Deadham Hard, a young woman named Alice finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden desires. As she navigates the dark underbelly of the town, she becomes entangled with a charismatic stranger who holds the key to unlocking the town's secrets. With danger lurking around every corner, Alice must confront her own past and make choices that will determine her fate and the fate of Deadham Hard itself.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Secrets, Forbidden Love, Redemption
