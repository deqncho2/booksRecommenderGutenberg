Synopsis: In 'Our Little Grecian Cousin' by Mary F. Nixon-Roulet, we are transported to ancient Greece, where we meet young Demetrius, a curious and adventurous boy. Join Demetrius as he embarks on a journey filled with fascinating myths, breathtaking landscapes, and encounters with legendary figures. From the bustling streets of Athens to the majestic temples of Olympia, Demetrius discovers the rich history and culture of his beloved homeland, while also learning valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the importance of family bonds.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: Greek Mythology, Adventure, Cultural Heritage, Family
