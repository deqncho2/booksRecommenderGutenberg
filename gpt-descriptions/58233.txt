Synopsis: In 'A Surgeon in Arms' by R.J. Manion, the reader is taken on a gripping journey through the life of a dedicated surgeon who finds himself thrust into the chaos of war. Driven by a deep sense of duty and compassion, the protagonist must navigate the horrors of the battlefield while grappling with the moral dilemmas that arise in the midst of conflict. As the story unfolds, the surgeon's skills are put to the ultimate test, forcing him to confront his own limits and question the true meaning of heroism.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Medical Drama

Themes: Duty and Sacrifice, Morality in War, Human Resilience
