Synopsis: 
'Goody Two Shoes' by Anonymous is a captivating tale that follows the life of a young orphan named Margery Meanwell. Despite facing numerous hardships, Margery's unwavering optimism and determination lead her on a remarkable journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As she navigates through various challenges, Margery's kind-hearted nature and resilience inspire those around her, leaving a lasting impact on the lives she touches. This heartwarming story is sure to keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what lies ahead for the endearing protagonist.

Genres: Children's literature, Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Perseverance, Kindness, Overcoming adversity
