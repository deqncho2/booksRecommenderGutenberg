Synopsis: 

'East of Paris: Sketches in the Gâtinais, Bourbonnais, and Champagne' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lesser-known regions of France. Matilda Betham-Edwards, a skilled writer and observer, paints vivid pictures of the landscapes, people, and traditions she encounters during her travels. From the picturesque villages of Gâtinais to the charming countryside of Bourbonnais and the sparkling vineyards of Champagne, each sketch offers a unique glimpse into the heart and soul of these regions. Through her keen eye for detail and her ability to capture the essence of a place, Betham-Edwards invites readers to join her in exploring the hidden gems of rural France.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Rural Life
