Synopsis: In 'Major Prophets of To-Day', Edwin E. Slosson takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and teachings of some of the most influential thinkers of the early 20th century. From philosophers to scientists, religious leaders to social reformers, Slosson delves into the minds of these remarkable individuals, exploring their ideas and the impact they had on society. Through vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the minds of these major prophets and the profound influence they continue to have on our world today.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, History

Themes: Intellectualism, Social Change, Philosophy, Religion
