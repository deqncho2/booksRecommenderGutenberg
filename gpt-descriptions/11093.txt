Synopsis: 
In 'Trailin'!' by Max Brand, a mysterious drifter named Anthony Bard finds himself entangled in a web of danger and deceit when he stumbles upon a small town plagued by corruption and violence. As Bard becomes embroiled in the town's troubles, he must confront his own haunted past and make a choice between seeking revenge or finding redemption. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, 'Trailin'!' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the Wild West, where loyalty and justice are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Western, Action, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Justice, Identity
