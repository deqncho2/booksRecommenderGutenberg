Synopsis: 
In 'Marion Harland's Complete Etiquette: A Young People's Guide to Every Social Occasion', Marion Harland and Virginia Terhune Van de Water provide a comprehensive guide to navigating the intricacies of social etiquette. From formal dinners to casual gatherings, this book offers practical advice and tips on how to conduct oneself with grace and poise in any social setting. With a focus on young people, the authors address topics such as introductions, conversation skills, table manners, and appropriate behavior at parties. Through relatable anecdotes and helpful suggestions, this guidebook aims to empower readers to confidently navigate the complexities of social interactions and make a positive impression in any situation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Etiquette

Themes: Social skills, Personal development, Manners, Confidence
