Synopsis: In 'Mary Louise at Dorfield' by Emma Speed Sampson, Mary Louise Burrows finds herself in a new town, Dorfield, where she uncovers a mysterious secret that threatens to disrupt the peace of the community. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Mary Louise must navigate through unexpected friendships, hidden agendas, and dangerous encounters, all while trying to protect those she cares about. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise, and Mary Louise must summon her courage and wit to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Secrets
