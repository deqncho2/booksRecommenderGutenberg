Synopsis: 

In "Elements of Show Card Writing: A Course of Instruction on How to Make Business-Building Show Cards," author John H. De Wild takes readers on a captivating journey into the art of creating eye-catching show cards that effectively promote businesses. Through a comprehensive and practical approach, De Wild shares invaluable techniques, tips, and examples that will inspire and empower readers to master the art of show card writing. From understanding the psychology of visual communication to mastering the use of typography and color, this book offers a wealth of knowledge that will help readers create show cards that captivate and persuade their target audience. Whether you are a seasoned designer or a novice, this book is a must-read for anyone looking to enhance their skills in the world of show card writing.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Business

Themes: Graphic design, Advertising, Marketing
