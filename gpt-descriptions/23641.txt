Synopsis: 
In 'The Forsaken Inn: A Novel', a young couple embarks on a journey to a remote inn nestled in the mountains. However, upon their arrival, they discover a mysterious and eerie atmosphere that surrounds the inn and its peculiar inhabitants. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the inn, they find themselves entangled in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected twists. With each passing night, the couple's sanity is tested, and they must uncover the truth before it consumes them entirely.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Psychological suspense
