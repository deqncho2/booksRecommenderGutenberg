Synopsis: In "Astronomy in a Nutshell," Garrett Putman Serviss presents a captivating exploration of the wonders of the universe, revealing the chief facts and principles of astronomy in an accessible and engaging manner. From the mesmerizing beauty of the night sky to the mind-boggling scale of celestial bodies, Serviss takes readers on a journey through the cosmos, unraveling the mysteries of stars, planets, galaxies, and more. With a blend of scientific knowledge and storytelling, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the vastness and complexity of our universe.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Astronomy

Themes: Exploration of the universe, Wonder and awe of the cosmos, Scientific knowledge and discovery
