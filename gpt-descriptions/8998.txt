Synopsis: 

'Paris as It Was and as It Is: A Sketch Of The French Capital, Illustrative Of The Effects Of The Revolution' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant city of Paris, both before and after the French Revolution. Blagdon's vivid descriptions and insightful observations provide a unique perspective on the transformation of the city and its people. From the opulent palaces and bustling streets of the past to the scars of revolution and the emergence of a new society, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the historical and cultural evolution of Paris.

Genres: Historical, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Revolution, Transformation, Cultural Evolution
