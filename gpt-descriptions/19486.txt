Synopsis: 
'Irish Wonders: The Ghosts, Giants, Pooka, Demons, Leprechauns, Banshees, Fairies, Witches, Widows, Old Maids, and other Marvels of the Emerald Isle' takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical and enchanting world of Irish folklore. From the haunting tales of ghosts and banshees to the mischievous tricks of leprechauns and fairies, this book unravels the rich tapestry of Irish mythology and legends. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, McAnally delves into the depths of Irish culture, revealing the hidden wonders and supernatural beings that have fascinated generations. Prepare to be spellbound by the extraordinary creatures and magical encounters that await within the pages of this captivating collection.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Supernatural

Themes: Irish culture, Legends and mythology, Supernatural beings
