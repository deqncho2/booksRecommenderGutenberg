Synopsis: 

In 'Through the Fray: A Tale of the Luddite Riots', George Alfred Henty takes us on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous period of the Luddite riots in early 19th century England. The story follows Ned Sankey, a young and courageous mill worker, who finds himself caught in the midst of the violent conflict between the Luddites and the mill owners. As Ned becomes entangled in the dangerous world of secret societies and industrial espionage, he must navigate treacherous alliances and face life-threatening challenges to protect his loved ones and fight for justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Social unrest, Industrial revolution, Loyalty, Courage
