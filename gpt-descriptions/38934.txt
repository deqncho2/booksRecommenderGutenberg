Synopsis: In 'The Camp Fire Girls' Larks and Pranks; Or, The House of the Open Door', a group of adventurous Camp Fire Girls embark on a summer filled with mischief, laughter, and unexpected discoveries. As they explore the mysterious House of the Open Door, they uncover secrets that will test their friendship and courage. With each prank and lark, the girls learn valuable lessons about trust, loyalty, and the power of unity. Will their mischievous escapades lead them to a greater understanding of themselves and the world around them?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
