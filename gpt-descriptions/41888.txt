Synopsis: 

In 'De Mortuis Nil Nisi Bona: Being a Series of Problems in Executorship Law and Accounts', authors Pegler and Spicer present a captivating collection of intricate legal puzzles and financial conundrums. Set in the realm of executorship law, this book takes readers on a journey through the complex world of estate administration, where unexpected twists and turns await at every corner. From disputed inheritances to hidden assets, the authors skillfully weave together a series of thought-provoking scenarios that challenge the reader's understanding of legal and financial matters. As the protagonists navigate through these perplexing cases, they must unravel the truth behind each situation, leading to surprising revelations and unforeseen outcomes. 

Genres: Legal fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Executorship law, Financial puzzles, Legal intricacies
