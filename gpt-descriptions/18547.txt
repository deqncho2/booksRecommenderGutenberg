Synopsis: In 'Madame Flirt: A Romance of 'The Beggar's Opera'' by Pearce, Charles Edward, readers are transported to the vibrant world of 18th-century London, where the notorious Madame Flirt reigns supreme. This captivating romance intertwines with the events of John Gay's famous play, 'The Beggar's Opera,' as Madame Flirt navigates a treacherous web of love, betrayal, and intrigue. As her secrets unravel and her past catches up with her, readers will be enthralled by the twists and turns of this thrilling tale.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Identity and Deception, Power and Corruption
