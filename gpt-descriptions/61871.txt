Synopsis: 
In "Spirit Slate Writing and Kindred Phenomena," Soo, Chung Ling takes readers on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of spiritualism and psychic phenomena. Through a series of intriguing anecdotes and mind-boggling demonstrations, the author explores the secrets behind spirit slate writing, a fascinating method used by mediums to communicate with the spirit realm. From inexplicable messages appearing on blank slates to uncanny predictions and unexplainable manifestations, this book delves into the enigmatic realm of the supernatural, leaving readers spellbound and questioning the boundaries of reality.

Genres: 
Occult, Paranormal, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Spiritualism, Psychic Phenomena, Communication with the Spirit Realm
