Synopsis: 
'Peak's Island: A Romance of Buccaneer Days' takes readers on a thrilling journey to the shores of a remote island, where the past collides with the present in unexpected ways. When a young woman named Emily discovers a hidden treasure map, she becomes entangled in a dangerous quest that leads her to encounter a mysterious pirate captain and his crew. As Emily navigates treacherous waters and faces perilous challenges, she must also confront her own desires and the complexities of love. With its blend of adventure, romance, and intrigue, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Treasure hunting, Love and desire, Identity and self-discovery
