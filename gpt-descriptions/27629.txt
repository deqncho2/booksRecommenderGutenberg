Synopsis: 
'Captain Desmond, V.C.' by Maud Diver is a captivating tale that follows the life of Captain Desmond, a decorated war hero haunted by the horrors of battle. As he navigates the challenges of post-war life, he finds himself torn between duty, love, and the pursuit of personal happiness. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century India, this gripping novel explores the complexities of loyalty, sacrifice, and the enduring power of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Romance

Themes: War and its aftermath, Duty and honor, Love and sacrifice
