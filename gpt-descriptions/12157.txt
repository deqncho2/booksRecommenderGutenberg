Synopsis: In 'The Unknown: Night Watches, Part 7,' the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the dark and mysterious streets of London. As the night unfolds, a series of strange and inexplicable events occur, leaving the protagonist questioning their own sanity. With each passing hour, the line between reality and the supernatural becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Supernatural

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Sanity, Supernatural Phenomena
