Synopsis: In the small New England town of Morgesons, young Cassandra Morgeson navigates the complexities of family, love, and societal expectations. As she grows from a curious child to a headstrong young woman, Cassandra's journey is marked by unconventional relationships, personal struggles, and a relentless pursuit of her own desires. Set against the backdrop of the mid-19th century, this captivating novel explores the inner world of a woman determined to break free from the constraints of her time.

Genres: Historical fiction, coming-of-age, family drama

Themes: Gender roles, societal expectations, self-discovery, unconventional relationships
