Synopsis: In 'Earth's Gone to the Dogs!' by William J. McClellan, a seemingly ordinary day takes a wild turn when dogs all around the world suddenly gain the ability to speak and think like humans. As chaos ensues and society grapples with this unprecedented phenomenon, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate through the newfound complexities of a world where dogs are no longer just man's best friend. With humor, heart, and a touch of adventure, this captivating tale explores the profound impact of communication, loyalty, and the unbreakable bond between humans and their furry companions.

Genres: Science Fiction, Comedy, Adventure

Themes: Communication, Loyalty, Human-Animal Bond
