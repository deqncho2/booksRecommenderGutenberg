Synopsis: In 'The Big Fix' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Hansen finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. As he races against time to protect his invention, he must navigate through a treacherous world of corporate greed, government conspiracies, and personal vendettas. With his life on the line, Dr. Hansen must outsmart his enemies and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific advancement, Corporate corruption, Government secrecy
