Synopsis: In 'The Affair of the Brains' by Gilmore, Anthony, a renowned neuroscientist is found dead under mysterious circumstances, leaving behind a trail of perplexing clues. As Detective Sarah Collins delves into the investigation, she uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and illusion becomes blurred, leading Sarah to question her own sanity. As she races against time to solve the case, she must navigate a world where nothing is as it seems and where the power of the mind can be both a blessing and a curse.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Deception, Perception, Mental Manipulation
