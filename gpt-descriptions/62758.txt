Synopsis: In 'The Clean Heart' by A. S. M. Hutchinson, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small English village, captivating the locals with his enigmatic charm and impeccable manners. As the villagers become increasingly infatuated with him, they soon discover that his presence brings about unexpected consequences, unraveling the secrets and desires hidden within their seemingly idyllic community. With each passing day, tensions rise, alliances shift, and the true nature of the stranger's intentions becomes more elusive. This gripping tale explores the depths of human nature, the power of manipulation, and the consequences of unchecked desires.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Deception, Identity, Morality, Human Nature
