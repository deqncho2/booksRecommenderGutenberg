Synopsis: In "The Little Immigrant" by Eva Stern, a young girl named Eva embarks on a journey that takes her from her small village in Eastern Europe to the bustling streets of New York City. As she navigates the challenges of being an immigrant in a new country, Eva's determination and resilience shine through. From facing language barriers to finding her place in a diverse society, Eva's experiences offer a heartfelt and inspiring tale of hope and adaptation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Immigration, Identity, Resilience, Cultural Adaptation
