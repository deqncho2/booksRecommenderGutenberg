Synopsis: In 'The Film of Fear' by Frederic Arnold Kummer, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious film reel arrives at his doorstep. As he delves into the secrets hidden within the film, he becomes immersed in a world of danger, deception, and unexpected twists. With each clue he uncovers, the detective's own life becomes increasingly endangered, leading to a thrilling race against time to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Crime, Intrigue, Betrayal
