Synopsis: In 'The Firm of Girdlestone' by Arthur Conan Doyle, a young medical student finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he becomes involved with a dubious business firm. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the firm, he uncovers a world of corruption, danger, and unexpected alliances. With his own life at stake, he must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Corruption, Loyalty, Morality
