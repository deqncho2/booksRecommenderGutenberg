Synopsis: In 'The Blind Spot' by Flint, Homer Eon and Hall, Austin, a brilliant scientist and a wealthy businessman join forces to explore a mysterious phenomenon that allows them to see into another dimension. As they delve deeper into this uncharted territory, they uncover astonishing secrets and encounter unimaginable dangers that threaten their very existence. With their lives hanging in the balance, they must navigate a treacherous journey through the blind spot, where reality and perception collide in mind-bending ways.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of alternate dimensions, Perception vs. reality, Consequences of scientific discovery
