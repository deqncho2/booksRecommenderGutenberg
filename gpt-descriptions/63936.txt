Synopsis: In 'Strange Exodus' by Robert Abernathy, a small town is suddenly plagued by a series of unexplained disappearances, leaving the community in a state of fear and confusion. As the mystery deepens, a group of unlikely allies must come together to uncover the truth behind these strange occurrences. With each revelation, they are drawn into a world they never knew existed, filled with secrets, danger, and unexpected twists. As they race against time to solve the puzzle, they soon realize that the fate of their town, and possibly even humanity, hangs in the balance.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Conspiracy, Identity, Survival
