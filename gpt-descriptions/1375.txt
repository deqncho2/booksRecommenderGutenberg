Synopsis: 
In the heartwarming sequel to "Rebecca of Sunnybrook Farm," we reunite with the spirited and imaginative Rebecca Randall as she embarks on new adventures in her beloved village. From organizing a grand pageant to facing unexpected challenges, Rebecca's indomitable spirit shines through as she navigates the joys and trials of growing up. With her infectious charm and unwavering determination, Rebecca's journey will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Children's literature, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: 
Friendship, Imagination, Resilience
