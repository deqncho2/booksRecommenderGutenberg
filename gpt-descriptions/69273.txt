Synopsis: In "The History of Silhouettes" by Emily Jackson, a mysterious art collector embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets behind the ancient art of silhouettes. As she delves deeper into the history and significance of these captivating shadow portraits, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, betrayal, and hidden identities. With each discovery, the collector's own life begins to mirror the enigmatic figures she seeks to understand, blurring the lines between art and reality. This captivating tale takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through time, art, and the power of shadows.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Art

Themes: Identity, Artistic Expression, Secrets
