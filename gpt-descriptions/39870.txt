Synopsis: 
In 'Bert Wilson, Marathon Winner' by J. W. Duffield, readers are introduced to Bert Wilson, a young and determined athlete with a passion for running. As he trains rigorously for the upcoming marathon, Bert faces numerous challenges and obstacles that test his physical and mental strength. With the support of his friends and family, Bert embarks on a journey that will not only push him to his limits but also reveal the true power of perseverance and determination. Will Bert be able to overcome the odds and emerge victorious in the marathon?

Genres: 
Sports, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Perseverance, Determination, Friendship
