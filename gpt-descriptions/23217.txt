Synopsis: In the remote coastal village of Troy, a mysterious shipwreck sets off a chain of events that will test the courage and loyalty of its inhabitants. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, the lives of the villagers become entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and redemption. With the fate of their community hanging in the balance, they must confront their darkest fears and make difficult choices that will shape their destinies forever.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Redemption, Community
