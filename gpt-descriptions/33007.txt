Synopsis: In 'Edelweiss: A Story' by Berthold Auerbach, a young woman named Edelweiss finds herself torn between duty and desire as she navigates the complexities of love and societal expectations. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque German village, Edelweiss's journey unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, leading her to question the true meaning of happiness and the sacrifices one must make for it.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Duty, Self-Discovery, Societal Expectations
