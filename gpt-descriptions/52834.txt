Synopsis: 

'Guernsey Folk Lore: a collection of popular superstitions, legendary tales, peculiar customs, proverbs, weather sayings, etc., of the people of that island' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and traditions of the island of Guernsey. From enchanting legends and mysterious superstitions to unique customs and intriguing proverbs, this collection offers a fascinating glimpse into the beliefs and practices of the island's inhabitants. Delve into the captivating world of Guernsey's folklore and uncover the hidden stories and wisdom passed down through generations.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Cultural Studies

Themes: Tradition, Belief Systems, Cultural Identity
