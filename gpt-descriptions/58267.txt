Synopsis: 
In 'The Delinquent (Vol. IV, No. 4), April, 1914', a mysterious figure emerges from the shadows, wreaking havoc on the unsuspecting residents of a small town. As the community grapples with fear and uncertainty, a determined detective sets out to uncover the truth behind the delinquent's actions. With each twist and turn, the plot thickens, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Crime and punishment, Justice and morality
