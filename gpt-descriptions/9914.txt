Synopsis: In 'The Babylonian Legends of the Creation', E. A. Wallis Budge takes readers on a captivating journey into the ancient world of Babylonian mythology. Through a collection of fascinating tales, Budge explores the creation of the universe, the birth of gods and goddesses, and the epic battles that shaped the cosmos. From the powerful god Marduk to the cunning goddess Tiamat, this book unveils a rich tapestry of mythical beings and their extraordinary feats. As readers delve into these legends, they will be transported to a time of divine power, cosmic struggles, and the eternal quest for order in a chaotic world.

Genres: Mythology, History, Religion

Themes: Creation, Gods and Goddesses, Cosmic Battles, Order and Chaos
