Synopsis: 
In 'The World's Greatest Books — Volume 12 — Modern History', embark on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events that shaped the modern world. From the rise and fall of empires to the struggles for independence and the birth of new nations, this volume offers a comprehensive exploration of the key moments and influential figures that have shaped our present. Delve into the gripping narratives of revolutions, wars, and social movements that have left an indelible mark on history. With vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, this volume brings to life the fascinating tapestry of modern history.

Genres: 
History, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Power and politics, Revolution and social change, Nationalism and identity, War and conflict
