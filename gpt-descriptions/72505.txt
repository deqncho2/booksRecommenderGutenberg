Synopsis: In this captivating biography, Roosevelt and Wilson delve into the life of one of America's most iconic presidents, Abraham Lincoln. From his humble beginnings to his rise to power, the authors paint a vivid portrait of a man who faced immense challenges and made history with his leadership. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, they explore Lincoln's personal struggles, his political journey, and the profound impact he had on the nation during one of its most tumultuous periods. This compelling narrative offers a fresh perspective on a legendary figure, revealing the complexities and enduring legacy of Abraham Lincoln.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Leadership, Politics, Civil War, Slavery, Legacy
