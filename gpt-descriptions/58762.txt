Synopsis: In 'The New-Year's Bargain' by Susan Coolidge, a young woman named Kitty embarks on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected romance. When she makes a daring decision to leave her comfortable life behind, she finds herself in a world filled with new experiences, challenges, and the possibility of finding true happiness. As Kitty navigates through the ups and downs of her new adventure, she learns valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the importance of following one's heart.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Adventure
