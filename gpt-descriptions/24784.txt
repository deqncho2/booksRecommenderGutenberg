Synopsis: In 'The Royal Pawn of Venice: A Romance of Cyprus' by Turnbull, Lawrence, Mrs., a young noblewoman finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love in the enchanting city of Venice. As she navigates the treacherous waters of power and deception, she must make choices that will determine not only her own fate but also the destiny of an entire kingdom. With its captivating blend of romance, mystery, and suspense, this tale transports readers to a world of opulence and danger, where loyalty and betrayal go hand in hand.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Power and Politics, Betrayal and Loyalty
