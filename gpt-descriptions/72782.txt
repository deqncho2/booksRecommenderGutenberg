Synopsis: In 'As a Thief in the Night' by R. Austin Freeman, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a valuable artifact mysteriously disappears from a locked room. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Intrigue, Deception, Deduction
