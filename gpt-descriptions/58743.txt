Synopsis: 
In 'Little Boy' by Bixby, Jerome, a young boy named Tommy discovers a mysterious power within himself that sets him apart from others. As he navigates through the challenges of growing up in a small town during World War II, Tommy's extraordinary ability becomes both a blessing and a burden. With the fate of his loved ones hanging in the balance, Tommy embarks on a journey that will test his courage, resilience, and the true meaning of sacrifice.

Genres: Science Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Power of belief, Innocence and loss, Heroism in unexpected places
