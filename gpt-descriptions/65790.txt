Synopsis: In 'Under the Tiger's Claws; Or, A Struggle for the Right' by Carter, Nicholas (House name), a young and ambitious journalist finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and danger as he uncovers a shocking conspiracy that threatens to tear apart a nation. With his life on the line, he must navigate through treacherous alliances and unexpected betrayals to expose the truth and fight for justice. This gripping tale of suspense and moral dilemmas will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Political Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Corruption, Power Struggles, Morality
