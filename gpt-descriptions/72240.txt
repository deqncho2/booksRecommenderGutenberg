Synopsis: 
"A Collection of Latin Maxims & Rules, in Law and Equity" is a comprehensive compilation of Latin maxims and rules selected from renowned authors in various fields of law, including civil, canon, feudal, English, and Scots law. The book provides an English translation of these maxims and rules, accompanied by an appendix that references the authorities from which they are derived. This invaluable resource offers readers a deep insight into the legal principles and concepts that have shaped the foundations of law across different jurisdictions.

Genres: Legal reference, Legal literature, Latin language

Themes: Legal principles, Jurisprudence, Comparative law
