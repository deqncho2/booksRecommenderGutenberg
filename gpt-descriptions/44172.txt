Synopsis: In 'Roy Blakeley's Motor Caravan', Roy and his friends embark on an exciting adventure as they set out on a cross-country journey in their newly acquired motor caravan. From encountering unexpected challenges to forming new friendships along the way, the boys navigate through thrilling escapades that test their resourcefulness and teamwork. As they explore the open road, they discover the true meaning of friendship and the importance of embracing the unknown.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Adventure, Friendship, Resourcefulness
