Synopsis: In "Ralph on the Engine; Or, The Young Fireman of the Limited Mail," we follow the thrilling journey of Ralph, a young and ambitious fireman on a fast-paced mail train. As he faces numerous challenges and dangers, Ralph must prove his courage and skill to protect the passengers and crew. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding moments, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover if Ralph can overcome the obstacles that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Young Adult

Themes: Bravery, Determination, Coming of Age
