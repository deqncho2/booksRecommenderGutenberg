Synopsis: 
In 'Westy Martin' by Percy Keese Fitzhugh, readers are introduced to Westy Martin, a young boy with a passion for adventure and a heart full of determination. When Westy's father is called away on a secret mission, Westy finds himself embarking on a thrilling journey that takes him from the bustling streets of New York City to the untamed wilderness of the Adirondack Mountains. Along the way, he encounters mysterious characters, faces unexpected challenges, and learns valuable lessons about courage, friendship, and the power of perseverance. Will Westy be able to overcome the obstacles in his path and fulfill his mission? Join him on this captivating adventure to find out!

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Perseverance
