Synopsis: 

'Phallic Worship' delves into the intriguing world of ancient sex worship, exploring the mysteries and rituals surrounding this taboo subject. From the history of the masculine cross to Hebrew phallicism, bacchic festivals, sexual rites, and the enigmatic ancient faiths, Hargrave Jennings provides a comprehensive account of primitive symbolism and the hidden practices of the past. This captivating work offers a fascinating glimpse into the secret world of ancient sexual worship and the profound impact it had on various cultures throughout history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Anthropology

Themes: Ancient religions, Taboo subjects, Symbolism, Rituals
