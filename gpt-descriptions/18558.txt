Synopsis: In 'The Good Shepherd: A Life of Christ for Children' by Anonymous, embark on a captivating journey through the life of Jesus Christ, as seen through the eyes of a child. Follow along as the young protagonist encounters the miracles, teachings, and profound love that defined Jesus' time on Earth. From the humble beginnings in Bethlehem to the triumphant resurrection, this beautifully written and illustrated book brings the story of Christ to life in a way that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Religious Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Faith, Love, Redemption, Miracles
