Synopsis: In 'Martine's Hand-book of Etiquette, and Guide to True Politeness' by Martine, Arthur, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the intricacies of social etiquette and the art of being truly polite. This engaging guide offers a wealth of practical advice and insightful observations on navigating various social situations, from formal gatherings to everyday interactions. With a delightful blend of wit and wisdom, Martine's handbook provides invaluable tips on manners, conversation, dress, and more, ensuring that readers will be well-equipped to navigate the complexities of social life with grace and charm.

Genres: Self-help, Etiquette, Non-fiction

Themes: Etiquette, Politeness, Social skills, Personal development
