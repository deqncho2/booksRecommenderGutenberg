Synopsis: In "Panther Eye" by Roy J. Snell, a young adventurer named Jack embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the secrets of a mysterious artifact known as the Panther Eye. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic world of ancient civilizations and hidden treasures, Jack finds himself entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each step closer to the truth, the stakes rise higher, and Jack must rely on his wit and courage to survive the perilous quest for the Panther Eye.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Ancient Mysteries, Survival
