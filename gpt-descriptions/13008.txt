Synopsis: In 'The Edda, Volume 2: The Heroic Mythology of the North', L. Winifred Faraday delves into the captivating world of Norse mythology, exploring the heroic tales and legends that have fascinated readers for centuries. From the epic adventures of gods and goddesses to the valiant deeds of legendary heroes, this volume offers a rich tapestry of Norse mythology that will transport readers to a realm of magic, bravery, and destiny. With meticulous research and a captivating narrative style, Faraday brings these ancient stories to life, unveiling the profound wisdom and timeless lessons hidden within their pages.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Romance

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Divine Intervention, Moral Lessons
