Synopsis: In "Dorothy Dixon Solves the Conway Case," Wayne introduces us to the brilliant and resourceful detective, Dorothy Dixon, as she takes on her most perplexing case yet. When a wealthy businessman mysteriously disappears, leaving behind a trail of cryptic clues, Dorothy must navigate a web of deceit, danger, and hidden motives to uncover the truth. As she delves deeper into the investigation, Dorothy finds herself entangled in a complex web of secrets and lies, where nothing is as it seems. With time running out and the stakes higher than ever, Dorothy must rely on her sharp intellect and unwavering determination to solve the Conway case and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Justice
