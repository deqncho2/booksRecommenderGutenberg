Synopsis: 

In 'Psychotherapy: Including the History of the Use of Mental Influence, Directly and Indirectly, in Healing and the Principles for the Application of Energies Derived from the Mind to the Treatment of Disease,' James J. Walsh delves into the fascinating world of psychotherapy, exploring its historical roots and the various ways in which mental influence has been used to heal. From ancient practices to modern techniques, Walsh uncovers the principles behind harnessing the power of the mind to treat disease, offering a captivating journey through the evolution of psychotherapy.

Genres: Non-fiction, Psychology, History

Themes: Healing through mental influence, Evolution of psychotherapy, Historical perspectives on mental health
