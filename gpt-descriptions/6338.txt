Synopsis: 
'Boy Scouts in the Coal Caverns; Or, The Light in Tunnel Six' follows a group of adventurous Boy Scouts who find themselves exploring the mysterious depths of a coal mine. As they venture deeper into the dark and treacherous tunnels, they stumble upon a hidden secret that could change their lives forever. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and bravery to overcome the challenges they face and uncover the truth hidden within the coal caverns.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Courage, Friendship, Discovery
