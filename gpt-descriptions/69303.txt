Synopsis: In "Michelangelo" by Edward C. Strutt, delve into the captivating life of one of the greatest artists in history. From his humble beginnings as a young apprentice to his rise as a renowned sculptor and painter, follow Michelangelo's journey as he navigates the complexities of art, religion, and politics in Renaissance Italy. Witness the creation of his iconic masterpieces, the struggles he faced in his personal life, and the profound impact he left on the world of art. This biography offers a fascinating exploration of the man behind the masterpieces, revealing the passion, determination, and genius that defined Michelangelo's extraordinary life.

Genres: Biography, Art History

Themes: Artistic genius, Renaissance culture, Personal struggles and triumphs
