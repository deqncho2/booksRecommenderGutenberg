Synopsis: In 'First on the Moon' by Sutton, Jeff, a team of astronauts embarks on a groundbreaking mission to become the first humans to set foot on the moon. As they face the challenges of space travel and the unknown, they discover something unexpected that could change the course of history forever. With suspenseful twists and turns, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Human resilience
