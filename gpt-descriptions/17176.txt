Synopsis: In 'The Ghost: A Modern Fantasy' by Arnold Bennett, a young man named James Briggerland finds himself haunted by a mysterious presence that seems to follow him wherever he goes. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a world of supernatural beings and hidden realms that challenge his perception of reality. With the help of an eccentric group of allies, James must navigate through a web of secrets and confront his own fears in order to unravel the truth behind the ghostly apparition.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Perception of Reality, Fear
