Synopsis: 
In 'The Submarine Boys on Duty: Life on a Diving Torpedo Boat' by Victor G. Durham, three adventurous young men find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey as they join the crew of a diving torpedo boat. As they navigate the treacherous waters of the Atlantic Ocean, they encounter dangerous missions, unexpected challenges, and the mysteries of the deep. With their courage and determination, the Submarine Boys must prove their worth and face the perils that lie beneath the surface. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
