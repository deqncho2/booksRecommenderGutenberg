Synopsis: In 'The Wizard's Son, Vol. 2 (of 3)' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), the captivating tale continues as the young wizard's son embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about his heritage and unlock his hidden powers. As he delves deeper into the magical realm, he encounters mysterious creatures, faces dangerous challenges, and discovers shocking secrets that will forever change his destiny. With each page, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly anticipating the final installment of this enchanting trilogy.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Coming of Age, Power and Responsibility
