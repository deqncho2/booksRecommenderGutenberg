Synopsis: In 'The Coast of Chance', two sisters, Esther and Lucia Chamberlain, embark on a journey that takes them to the breathtaking coast of California. As they navigate through the rugged landscapes and encounter a cast of intriguing characters, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, love, and self-discovery. With each twist and turn, the sisters uncover hidden truths about themselves and the world around them, ultimately leading them to question the very essence of their own identities.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Sisterhood, Self-discovery, Secrets
