Synopsis: 
In 'Savva and the Life of Man: Two plays by Leonid Andreyev', the reader is transported into the captivating world of two thought-provoking plays. The first play, 'Savva', follows the journey of a young man who embarks on a quest to find the meaning of life, encountering a series of enigmatic characters along the way. The second play, 'The Life of Man', delves into the existential struggles of a man as he navigates through the complexities of human existence. Both plays delve deep into the human psyche, exploring profound questions about the purpose of life and the nature of humanity.

Genres: Drama, Absurdist Fiction

Themes: Existentialism, Search for Meaning, Human Nature
