Synopsis: In "Shelley and the Marriage Question" by Todhunter, John, a young woman named Shelley finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love and marriage, she is faced with difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this captivating novel explores the struggles of a woman seeking independence and happiness in a world that seeks to confine her.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love, Marriage, Gender Roles, Independence
