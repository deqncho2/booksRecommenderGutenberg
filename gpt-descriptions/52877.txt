Synopsis: In "Subsidiary Notes as to the Introduction of Female Nursing into Military Hospitals in Peace and War," Florence Nightingale delves into the fascinating world of nursing in military hospitals, both during times of peace and war. Through her meticulous observations and insightful analysis, Nightingale sheds light on the challenges faced by female nurses and the impact they have on patient care. This thought-provoking work offers a unique perspective on the role of women in healthcare and the transformative power of compassion and dedication.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Medical

Themes: Gender roles, Healthcare, Compassion
