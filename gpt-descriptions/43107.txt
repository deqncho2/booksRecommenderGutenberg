Synopsis: In 'The Wood-Pigeons and Mary' by Molesworth, Mrs., a young girl named Mary discovers a hidden world of talking wood-pigeons in her grandmother's garden. As Mary befriends these enchanting creatures, she embarks on a magical adventure that will test her courage and teach her valuable life lessons. With the wood-pigeons as her guides, Mary learns the importance of friendship, empathy, and the power of imagination. 

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Empathy
