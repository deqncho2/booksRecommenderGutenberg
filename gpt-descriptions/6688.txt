Synopsis: In the small town of St. Ogg's, young Maggie Tulliver grows up with a strong-willed spirit and an insatiable thirst for knowledge. As she navigates the complexities of family dynamics and societal expectations, Maggie finds herself torn between her deep love for her brother Tom and her desire for personal freedom. Set against the backdrop of the tumultuous relationship between the Tulliver family and their wealthy relatives, 'The Mill on the Floss' explores the challenges of loyalty, societal constraints, and the pursuit of individual identity.

Genres: Fiction, Bildungsroman, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family dynamics, societal expectations, personal freedom, loyalty, identity
