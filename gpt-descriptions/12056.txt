Synopsis: In "Phases of Faith; Or, Passages from the History of My Creed," Francis William Newman takes readers on a captivating journey through his personal exploration of faith and the evolution of his beliefs. Through a series of thought-provoking passages, Newman delves into the depths of his own spiritual journey, questioning and challenging traditional religious doctrines along the way. With a candid and introspective narrative, he invites readers to reflect on their own beliefs and embark on a quest for truth and meaning.

Genres: Autobiography, Philosophy, Religious Studies

Themes: Faith and doubt, Religious skepticism, Personal spiritual growth, Intellectual exploration
