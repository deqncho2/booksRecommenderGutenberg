Synopsis: In 'The Seeker' by Harry Leon Wilson, a young man named John is on a quest to find his true purpose in life. Tired of the monotony of his everyday existence, he embarks on a journey that takes him across the country, encountering a colorful cast of characters along the way. As John delves deeper into his search, he discovers unexpected truths about himself and the world around him, leading to a transformative experience that will forever change his perspective.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Meaning of life
