Synopsis: In "An American Crusader at Verdun" by Rice, Philip Sidney, a young American soldier finds himself thrust into the heart of one of the bloodiest battles of World War I. As he navigates the treacherous trenches and faces the horrors of war, he must confront his own fears and discover the true meaning of courage. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, this novel takes readers on a harrowing journey through the chaos and devastation of Verdun.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Action/Adventure

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Survival, The human cost of war
