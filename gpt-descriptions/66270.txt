Synopsis: 

In 'How to Get Married, Although a Woman; or, The Art of Pleasing Men' by Irene W. Hartt, we are introduced to a young woman named Emily who is determined to find a suitable husband. However, she quickly realizes that the traditional methods of courtship and societal expectations are not working in her favor. Determined to take matters into her own hands, Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment, challenging the norms of her time and finding her own unique way to navigate the complex world of relationships and love.

Genres: Romance, Comedy, Feminist Literature

Themes: Gender roles, Self-empowerment, Love and relationships, Societal expectations
