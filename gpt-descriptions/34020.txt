Synopsis: 
In 'The Window at the White Cat', a young woman named Rachel Innes finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when she becomes the owner of a seemingly innocent antique shop. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the shop's walls, Rachel discovers that danger lurks around every corner, and she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth. With unexpected twists and a cast of intriguing characters, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, Danger and intrigue
