Synopsis: In 'Frank Allen at Gold Fork; or, Locating the lost claim' by Forbes, Graham B., readers are taken on an exhilarating adventure as Frank Allen, a young and determined prospector, embarks on a quest to find a legendary lost claim in the treacherous Gold Fork region. Facing numerous challenges and encountering a cast of intriguing characters, Frank must navigate the rugged wilderness and unravel the mysteries surrounding the lost claim. With danger lurking at every turn, the story unfolds with suspense, excitement, and unexpected twists, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Discovery
