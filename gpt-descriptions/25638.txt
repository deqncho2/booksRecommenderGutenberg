Synopsis: In 'The Hidden Power, and Other Papers upon Mental Science', T. Troward takes readers on a captivating journey into the realm of mental science, exploring the hidden powers of the mind and its potential to shape our reality. Through a series of thought-provoking essays, Troward delves into the mysteries of the subconscious mind, the power of visualization, and the profound influence of our thoughts on our daily lives. With compelling anecdotes and insightful analysis, this book offers a fresh perspective on the untapped potential within each of us, inviting readers to unlock their hidden power and transform their lives.

Genres: Self-help, Philosophy, Psychology

Themes: Mental science, Subconscious mind, Power of thought, Personal transformation
