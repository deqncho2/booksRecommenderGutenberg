Synopsis: In 'The Uninhabited House' by J.H. Riddell, a young woman named Mary inherits a mysterious house from her late aunt. Eager to uncover the secrets hidden within its walls, Mary moves into the house, only to find herself entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and eerie encounters. As she delves deeper into the house's dark history, Mary must confront her own fears and unravel the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural thriller, mystery

Themes: Haunting, inheritance, secrets, fear, the supernatural
