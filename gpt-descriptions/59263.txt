Synopsis: '88 Favourite Carols and Hymns for Christmas' is a delightful collection of timeless songs that will transport readers into the enchanting spirit of the holiday season. From traditional carols to uplifting hymns, this book offers a rich tapestry of melodies that will warm hearts and bring joy to all. Whether you're singing along or simply immersing yourself in the beautiful lyrics, this compilation is sure to create a magical atmosphere that captures the essence of Christmas.

Genres: Music, Holiday, Poetry

Themes: Christmas spirit, Tradition, Joy, Faith
