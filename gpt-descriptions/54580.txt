Synopsis: 
'The Forerunner, His Parables and Poems' by Kahlil Gibran is a captivating collection of thought-provoking parables and soul-stirring poems. Through his lyrical prose, Gibran takes readers on a profound journey of self-discovery, exploring the depths of human emotions, the complexities of relationships, and the mysteries of life. Each parable and poem is a window into the human condition, offering profound insights and inspiring reflections on love, loss, joy, and the eternal quest for meaning. With its timeless wisdom and exquisite imagery, this collection invites readers to delve into the depths of their own souls and discover the transformative power of Gibran's words.

Genres: Poetry, Parables, Philosophy

Themes: Love, Loss, Self-discovery, Spirituality, Human emotions
