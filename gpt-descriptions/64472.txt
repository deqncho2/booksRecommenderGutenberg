Synopsis: In this thought-provoking sermon delivered in 1870, John Stoughton explores the captivating world of Christian literature. Drawing from his extensive knowledge and experience, he delves into the profound impact that Christian writings have had on society, both past and present. Stoughton's sermon takes the reader on a journey through the power of words, the transformative nature of faith, and the enduring legacy of Christian literature. 

Genres: Sermon, Religious Literature, Non-fiction

Themes: The power of words, Faith and spirituality, The impact of literature on society
