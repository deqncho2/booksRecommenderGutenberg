Synopsis: 
In the Land of Dakota: A Little Book of North Dakota Verse takes readers on a poetic journey through the enchanting landscapes and captivating stories of North Dakota. Through vivid imagery and heartfelt words, Huldah Lucile Winsted paints a vibrant picture of the state's natural beauty, its people, and their way of life. From the rolling prairies to the mighty Missouri River, from the bustling cities to the quiet rural towns, this collection of verse captures the essence of North Dakota in all its glory. Whether it's a nostalgic reflection on the past or a celebration of the present, these poems will transport readers to the heart of the Dakotas, leaving them with a renewed appreciation for this remarkable corner of the world.

Genres: Poetry, Regional Literature

Themes: Nature, Identity, Nostalgia, Community
