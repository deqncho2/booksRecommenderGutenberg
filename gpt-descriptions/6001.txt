Synopsis: In "Polly of Pebbly Pit" by Lillian Elizabeth Roy, readers are introduced to Polly, a spirited young girl who finds herself in a world of adventure and mystery. When Polly's family moves to the remote Pebbly Pit, she discovers an old diary that leads her on a quest to uncover the secrets of the past. Along the way, she encounters hidden treasures, unexpected friendships, and a series of puzzling clues that test her courage and determination. As Polly delves deeper into the mysteries of Pebbly Pit, she learns valuable lessons about loyalty, perseverance, and the power of self-belief.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Self-discovery
