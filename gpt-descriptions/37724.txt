Synopsis: In the second volume of 'For Sceptre and Crown: A Romance of the Present Time,' the captivating story continues as the lives of the aristocratic and the working class intertwine in unexpected ways. Secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and love is put to the ultimate test. As the characters navigate through the complexities of power, ambition, and societal expectations, they must confront their own desires and make choices that will shape their futures. With its gripping narrative and intriguing characters, this volume delves deeper into the intricate web of relationships and challenges the boundaries of class and privilege.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Class, Power and Ambition
