Synopsis: In "When a Cobbler Ruled the King" by Augusta Huiell Seaman, a young girl named Peggy stumbles upon a mysterious old cobbler's shop and discovers a hidden secret that could change the course of history. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Peggy finds herself caught in a thrilling adventure filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected twists. With the fate of a kingdom hanging in the balance, Peggy must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and save the day.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Power of Knowledge
