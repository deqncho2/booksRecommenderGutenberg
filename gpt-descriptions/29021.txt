Synopsis: 
Step into a world of enchantment and wonder with 'The Fairy Tales of Charles Perrault' by Perrault, Charles. This captivating collection of timeless tales will transport you to far-off lands where magic reigns and dreams come true. From the daring adventures of Little Red Riding Hood to the enchanting love story of Cinderella, these beloved stories will ignite your imagination and leave you yearning for more. Discover the secrets hidden within the pages of this enchanting book and let yourself be swept away by the power of Perrault's storytelling.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Children's literature

Themes: Morality, Transformation, Love and Romance
