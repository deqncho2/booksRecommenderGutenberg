Synopsis: In 'The Derelict' by W. J. Matthews, a group of explorers embarks on a treacherous journey to uncover the secrets of a mysterious derelict ship floating in space. As they delve deeper into the vessel's dark corridors, they encounter unimaginable horrors that test their sanity and challenge their very existence. With each terrifying discovery, the line between reality and nightmare becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a heart-pounding climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Horror, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Psychological terror
