Synopsis: In 'A Princess of Mars', John Carter, a Confederate veteran of the American Civil War, mysteriously finds himself transported to the planet Mars. There, he discovers a world filled with strange creatures, warring civilizations, and a captivating princess in need of rescue. As Carter navigates this new and dangerous world, he must confront his own abilities and make choices that will shape the fate of Mars and his own destiny.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Heroism, Love and Sacrifice
