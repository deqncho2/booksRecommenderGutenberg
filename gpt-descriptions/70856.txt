Synopsis: In "William Tyndale" by James J. Ellis, embark on a captivating journey through the life of a remarkable man who defied the religious and political powers of his time. This gripping biography unveils the extraordinary story of William Tyndale, a brilliant scholar and translator who risked everything to bring the Bible to the English-speaking world. From his humble beginnings to his fateful encounter with King Henry VIII, Tyndale's unwavering determination and unwavering faith will inspire and captivate readers, as they witness his relentless pursuit of truth and his ultimate sacrifice for the sake of religious freedom.

Genres: Biography, History, Religious

Themes: Religious persecution, Translation and dissemination of the Bible, Courage and conviction
