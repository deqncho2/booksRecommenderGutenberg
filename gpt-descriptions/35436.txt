Synopsis: In 'Mr. Claghorn's Daughter' by Trent, Hilary, a mysterious letter arrives at the doorstep of a small town, revealing a long-hidden secret that threatens to unravel the lives of the Claghorn family. As the truth slowly emerges, the daughter finds herself caught in a web of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each page, the reader is drawn deeper into a gripping tale of family secrets and the lengths one will go to protect their loved ones.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Family

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Family bonds
