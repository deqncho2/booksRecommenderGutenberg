Synopsis: 
In 'Alone' by Marion Harland, a young woman finds herself in a remote cabin in the woods, cut off from civilization. As she grapples with her isolation, she begins to uncover dark secrets about the cabin's previous occupants. With each discovery, her sense of unease grows, and she must confront the terrifying truth that she may not be as alone as she thought.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Isolation, Secrets, Survival
