Synopsis: In "The Rescue of the Princess Winsome: A Fairy Play for Old and Young," a group of mischievous fairies embark on a daring adventure to save the beautiful Princess Winsome from an evil sorcerer. With the help of a brave young boy named Jack, the fairies must navigate through enchanted forests, solve riddles, and outsmart magical creatures to rescue the princess and restore peace to the kingdom. This delightful fairy play is filled with whimsy, humor, and heartwarming moments that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fantasy, Children's Literature, Drama

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Good vs. Evil, Magic
