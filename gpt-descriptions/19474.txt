Synopsis: In 'Uller Uprising' by H. Beam Piper, a group of colonists on the planet Uller find themselves caught in the middle of a rebellion against the oppressive Terran Federation. As tensions rise and violence escalates, a lone Terran agent must navigate the complex web of political intrigue and cultural clashes to prevent an all-out war. With unexpected alliances and a race against time, this thrilling science fiction novel explores the consequences of colonization and the struggle for freedom in a distant world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Political Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Colonialism, Rebellion, Cultural Conflict
