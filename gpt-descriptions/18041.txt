Synopsis: 
In "Celtic Religion in Pre-Christian Times," Edward Anwyl takes readers on a captivating journey into the mystical world of ancient Celtic beliefs. Delving into the rich tapestry of Celtic mythology, rituals, and deities, Anwyl uncovers the fascinating practices and spiritual traditions of the Celts before the arrival of Christianity. From the sacred groves and stone circles to the enigmatic druids and their powerful magic, this book offers a captivating exploration of a long-lost era that continues to intrigue and inspire.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, History, Mythology

Themes: 
Religion, Mythology, Ancient Cultures
