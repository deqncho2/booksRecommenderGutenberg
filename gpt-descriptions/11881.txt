Synopsis: In 'The Shadow of the North: A Story of Old New York and a Lost Campaign', Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on a thrilling journey through the bustling streets of old New York and the untamed wilderness of the North. Amidst political intrigue, secret societies, and a lost campaign, a young man finds himself caught in a web of danger and mystery. As he navigates through the treacherous landscape of power and deception, he must uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear. Will he succeed, or will the shadow of the North consume him?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Survival and Perseverance
