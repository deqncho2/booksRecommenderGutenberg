Synopsis: 
In 'The Underpup' by I. A. R. Wylie, we follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Judy, who finds herself navigating the complexities of life in a world that often underestimates her. From her humble beginnings in a small town to her unexpected rise in the big city, Judy's determination and resilience shine through as she faces numerous challenges and discovers the true power of her own voice. With a cast of colorful characters and unexpected twists, this coming-of-age tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, rooting for Judy every step of the way.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Drama, Fiction

Themes: 
Resilience, Self-discovery, Overcoming adversity
