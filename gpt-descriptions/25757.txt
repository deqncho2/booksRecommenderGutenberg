Synopsis: 
In 'Scarabs: The History, Manufacture and Symbolism of the Scarabæus in Ancient Egypt, Phoenicia, Sardinia, Etruria, etc.', Isaac Myer takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient civilizations that revered the scarab beetle. From the mystical rituals of ancient Egypt to the enigmatic practices of Phoenicia, Sardinia, and Etruria, this book unveils the fascinating history, intricate manufacture, and profound symbolism behind the scarabæus. Delve into the secrets of these ancient cultures as you uncover the hidden meanings and powers attributed to this sacred insect.

Genres: History, Archaeology, Symbolism

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Religious symbolism, Cultural practices
