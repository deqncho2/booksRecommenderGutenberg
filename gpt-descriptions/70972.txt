Synopsis: In 'The Man-Killers' by Coolidge Dane, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious deaths that leave the community in fear and suspicion. As the body count rises, a group of unlikely allies must uncover the truth behind these gruesome killings before they become the next victims. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking climax.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Fear, Suspicion, Uncovering the Truth
