Synopsis: 
'Little Lord Fauntleroy' tells the heartwarming story of a young boy named Cedric who unexpectedly becomes the heir to a grand estate and a title. As he navigates his new life of privilege and wealth, Cedric's genuine kindness and compassion touch the hearts of those around him, including his grumpy and estranged grandfather. With his innocent charm and unwavering belief in the goodness of people, Cedric teaches valuable lessons about love, family, and the true meaning of nobility.

Genres: Children's literature, Fiction, Historical fiction

Themes: Family, Kindness, Social class, Identity
