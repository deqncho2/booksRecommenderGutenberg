Synopsis: 

'Lives of Alchemystical Philosophers: To which is added a Bibliography of Alchemy and Hermetic Philosophy' delves into the mysterious world of alchemy and the lives of its most enigmatic practitioners. From ancient times to the Renaissance, this book uncovers the hidden lives and secret experiments of alchemists who sought to unlock the secrets of transmutation and immortality. With a comprehensive bibliography of alchemy and hermetic philosophy, this work serves as a gateway into the esoteric realm of alchemy, revealing the fascinating stories and pursuits of those who dedicated their lives to this mystical art.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Occult

Themes: Alchemy, Hermetic philosophy, Mysticism, Transmutation, Immortality
