Synopsis: In 'Her Father's Daughter' by Gene Stratton-Porter, a young woman named Linda embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience as she navigates the challenges of love, loss, and the pursuit of her dreams. Set against the backdrop of the early 20th century, Linda's determination to forge her own path in a world dominated by societal expectations and gender roles leads her on a captivating and emotional adventure that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Independence, Identity, Family, Love, Gender Roles
