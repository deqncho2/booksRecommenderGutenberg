Synopsis: In 'Palkkapiian päiväkirja: Romaaninovelli', the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the diary entries of a young woman named Palkkapiia. Through her intimate and introspective writings, we witness her struggles, dreams, and desires as she navigates the complexities of life. As Palkkapiia's story unfolds, the reader is drawn into a world filled with unexpected twists and turns, where secrets are revealed and choices must be made. This novella offers a unique glimpse into the inner thoughts and emotions of a remarkable protagonist, leaving the reader eager to uncover the truth behind Palkkapiia's captivating tale.

Genres: Fiction, Diary, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Self-discovery, Love, Social class
