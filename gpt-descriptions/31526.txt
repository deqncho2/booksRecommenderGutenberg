Synopsis: In 'Life of Wagner' by Ludwig Nohl, embark on a captivating journey through the tumultuous life of one of the most influential composers in history. From his humble beginnings to his rise to fame, witness the trials and triumphs that shaped Richard Wagner's extraordinary career. Uncover the secrets behind his groundbreaking operas, the controversies that surrounded him, and the profound impact he had on the world of music. As you delve into the depths of Wagner's life, be prepared to be enthralled by a tale of passion, ambition, and artistic genius.

Genres: Biography, Music, History

Themes: Artistic creativity, Ambition, Controversy, Influence
