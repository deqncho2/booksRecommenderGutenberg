Synopsis: In 'Atmâ: A Romance' by C. A. Frazer, a young woman named Atmâ embarks on a journey of self-discovery and love that takes her from the bustling streets of London to the enchanting landscapes of India. As she navigates through cultural differences, societal expectations, and personal desires, Atmâ finds herself torn between duty and passion, tradition and modernity. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this novel explores the complexities of identity, the power of love, and the pursuit of one's true purpose.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Self-discovery, Cultural identity, Love and desire, Tradition versus modernity
