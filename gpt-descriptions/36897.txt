Synopsis: 

In 'Benjamin Franklin; Self-Revealed, Volume 2 (of 2): A Biographical and Critical Study Based Mainly on his own Writings', author William Cabell Bruce delves into the fascinating life of one of America's most iconic figures. This second volume continues the exploration of Benjamin Franklin's personal writings, shedding light on his multifaceted character and the profound impact he had on the world. From his early struggles to his rise as a statesman, inventor, and philosopher, Franklin's own words provide a captivating glimpse into his remarkable journey. 

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Enlightenment, American Revolution, Intellectual curiosity
