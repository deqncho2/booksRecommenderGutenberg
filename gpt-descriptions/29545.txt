Synopsis: In 'The Spanish Jade' by Maurice Hewlett, a young Englishman named Francis entertains a mysterious Spanish woman, Doña Maria, who captivates him with her beauty and enigmatic charm. As their relationship deepens, Francis becomes entangled in a web of secrets and political intrigue, leading him on a thrilling journey through the dangerous streets of medieval Seville. With each twist and turn, Francis must navigate treacherous alliances and confront his own desires, all while uncovering the truth about Doña Maria and the priceless Spanish jade she possesses. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and obsession, Betrayal, Power and politics
