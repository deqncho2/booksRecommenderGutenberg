Synopsis: 

'A Book of Discovery: The History of the World's Exploration, From the Earliest Times to the Finding of the South Pole' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the captivating history of exploration. From the intrepid voyages of ancient civilizations to the daring expeditions of modern explorers, this book unveils the remarkable stories of those who pushed the boundaries of human knowledge and ventured into the unknown. With vivid descriptions and gripping narratives, Margaret Bertha Synge brings to life the triumphs, challenges, and sacrifices of these courageous individuals, painting a vivid picture of the world's exploration throughout history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, Human Spirit, Discovery
