Synopsis: 

In 'History of Chemistry, Volume 1 (of 2): From the earliest time to the middle of the nineteenth century,' Thorpe takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of chemistry, from its ancient origins to the groundbreaking discoveries of the nineteenth century. Delving into the lives and contributions of influential chemists, this volume unveils the fascinating stories behind the development of this fundamental science. From alchemy's mystical beginnings to the emergence of modern chemical theories, Thorpe's meticulous research and engaging narrative style make this book a captivating exploration of the rich history of chemistry.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Scientific progress, Evolution of ideas, Contributions of key figures
