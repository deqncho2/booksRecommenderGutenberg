Synopsis: 

'Autobiography of Frank G. Allen, Minister of the Gospel: and Selections from his Writings' takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and experiences of Frank Gibbs Allen, a renowned minister of the Gospel. From his humble beginnings to his rise as a prominent figure in the religious community, Allen's autobiography offers a unique perspective on faith, spirituality, and the challenges faced by those dedicated to spreading the word of God. This book also includes a selection of Allen's insightful writings, providing readers with a deeper understanding of his profound teachings and beliefs.

Genres: Autobiography, Religious, Inspirational

Themes: Faith, Spirituality, Personal Growth, Religious Leadership
