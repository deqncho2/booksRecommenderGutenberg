Synopsis:
'The Four Horsemen of the Apocalypse' by Blasco Ibáñez, Vicente is a gripping tale set during World War I. The story follows the lives of two wealthy families, the Desnoyers and the von Hartrotts, as they navigate the tumultuous events of the war. As the conflict unfolds, the characters find themselves torn between love and duty, loyalty and betrayal, and ultimately face the devastating consequences of their choices. With vivid descriptions and complex relationships, this novel explores the impact of war on individuals and society, painting a vivid picture of the human condition in times of crisis.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Family Drama

Themes:
War, Love, Loyalty, Betrayal, Consequences
