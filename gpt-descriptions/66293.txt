Synopsis: In 'Peril of the Starmen' by Neville Kris, a group of astronauts embarks on a daring mission to explore a distant planet. However, they soon discover that they are not alone in the vastness of space. As they encounter a mysterious race of beings known as the Starmen, the astronauts find themselves caught in a thrilling battle for survival, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this gripping sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Identity
