Synopsis: 
"Ladies in the Field: Sketches of Sport" takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of sports, but with a unique twist. Instead of focusing on the athletes themselves, this collection of sketches delves into the lives and experiences of the women who are often overlooked in the male-dominated sporting world. From the adrenaline-fueled excitement of the stadium to the quiet determination on the training field, these sketches offer a glimpse into the challenges, triumphs, and untold stories of these remarkable women. Each sketch is a vivid portrayal of their passion, resilience, and the indomitable spirit that drives them to excel in their chosen sports. 

Genres: Sports, Fiction, Women's Literature

Themes: Gender equality, Empowerment, Overcoming adversity, Passion for sports
