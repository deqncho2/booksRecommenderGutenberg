Synopsis: 

Baum's Complete Stamp Dealers Directory is a comprehensive guide that unveils a treasure trove of information about stamp dealers in the United States and Europe. Written by L. Frank Baum, renowned for his imaginative storytelling, this directory not only provides a complete list of dealers but also includes a compilation of philatelic publications. Whether you are a seasoned collector or a novice enthusiast, this directory promises to be an invaluable resource for discovering new dealers, expanding your collection, and delving deeper into the world of stamp collecting.

Genres: Reference, Collectibles, Philately

Themes: Collecting, Exploration, Information
