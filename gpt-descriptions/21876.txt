Synopsis: In 'Mary Louise and the Liberty Girls' by L. Frank Baum, a group of spirited young girls form a secret society during World War I to support their country and make a difference. As they navigate the challenges of war, they uncover hidden secrets and embark on thrilling adventures that test their courage and loyalty. With their unwavering determination, these Liberty Girls prove that even in the face of adversity, the power of friendship and patriotism can change the world.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Friendship, Courage, Female Empowerment
