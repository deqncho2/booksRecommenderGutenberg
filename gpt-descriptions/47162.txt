Synopsis: In "The Horse's Mouth, Showing the Age by the Teeth," Mayhew, Edward takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden world of horse racing. Through the eyes of an enigmatic protagonist, the story unravels the secrets, scandals, and triumphs that lie within the racing industry. As the protagonist navigates through the complex web of relationships and rivalries, readers are drawn into a thrilling tale of ambition, love, and the pursuit of greatness. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, this novel offers a unique glimpse into a world where fortunes are won and lost with every race.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Sports

Themes: Ambition, Love, Betrayal, Secrets, Society
