Synopsis: In 'The Story of Wool' by Sara Ware Bassett, a young woman named Ruth embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling city to the tranquil countryside, where she discovers a hidden family secret that will change her life forever. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Ruth uncovers a tale of love, betrayal, and resilience that spans generations. With each revelation, she becomes more entangled in the intricate web of her family's past, leading her to question her own identity and the choices she must make. Set against the backdrop of the wool industry in the early 20th century, this captivating novel weaves together history, romance, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Resilience
