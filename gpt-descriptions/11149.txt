Synopsis: 
'Little Folded Hands: Prayers for Children' is a heartwarming collection of prayers that will captivate both young and old readers alike. Filled with tender words and beautiful illustrations, this anonymous work takes readers on a journey of faith, hope, and gratitude. From morning prayers to bedtime blessings, each prayer is carefully crafted to inspire and uplift, reminding us of the power of prayer in our daily lives. Whether you are seeking solace, guidance, or simply a moment of reflection, 'Little Folded Hands' is a timeless treasure that will touch your heart and nourish your soul.

Genres: Devotional, Children's Literature

Themes: Faith, Gratitude, Guidance
