Synopsis: In 'Prue and I' by George William Curtis, the reader is transported to the vibrant streets of New York City in the mid-19th century. The story revolves around the lives of two young siblings, Prue and her brother, as they navigate the challenges and adventures of urban life. From encounters with colorful characters to unexpected twists of fate, the siblings' journey unfolds against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society. As they strive to find their place in the bustling city, Prue and her brother discover the power of resilience, friendship, and the enduring bond between siblings.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: Urban life, Resilience, Sibling relationships
