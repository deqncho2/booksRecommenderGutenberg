Synopsis: 
In 'The Tatler, Volume 4', Sir Richard Steele and Joseph Addison continue their witty and satirical exploration of 18th-century society through a collection of essays and fictional letters. This volume delves into various topics, including fashion, politics, love, and the arts, all presented with a sharp and humorous perspective. From the absurdities of courtship to the follies of high society, the authors skillfully navigate the complexities of human behavior, offering both entertainment and insightful social commentary.

Genres: Satire, Essay, Periodical

Themes: Social criticism, Morality, Human nature
