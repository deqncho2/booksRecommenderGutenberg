Synopsis: In 'The Engineer' by Pohl and Kornbluth, a brilliant engineer named Joe Davis finds himself caught in a web of corporate greed and political manipulation. As he navigates a dystopian future where technology is controlled by a powerful few, Joe must use his skills to fight for justice and reclaim his freedom. With thrilling twists and turns, this gripping sci-fi novel explores the consequences of unchecked power and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Technological Thriller

Themes: Corporate Greed, Political Manipulation, Technological Control, Justice, Freedom
