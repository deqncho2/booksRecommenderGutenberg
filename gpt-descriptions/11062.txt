Synopsis: In the small town of Lakerim, twelve strangers find themselves mysteriously drawn together, each harboring their own secrets and desires. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of their intertwined lives, they soon discover that their fates are inexplicably linked, and that the choices they make will have far-reaching consequences. With suspense and intrigue at every turn, 'The Dozen from Lakerim' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of a seemingly idyllic community.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Secrets, Fate, Consequences
