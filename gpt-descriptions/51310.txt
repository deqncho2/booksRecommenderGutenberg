Synopsis: In 'My Lady Greensleeves' by Frederik Pohl, a brilliant scientist named Dan Davis finds himself transported to a future where humanity has become enslaved by a mysterious alien race. With the help of a beautiful and enigmatic woman named My Lady Greensleeves, Dan embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the aliens' intentions and find a way to save humanity. As he navigates through a world filled with advanced technology and dangerous secrets, Dan must confront his own fears and discover the true power of love and sacrifice.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Time travel, Alien invasion, Freedom, Love and sacrifice
