Synopsis: In 'The Mosstrooper: A Legend of the Scottish Border', the reader is transported to the rugged and mysterious Scottish Borderlands, where a legendary figure known as the Mosstrooper roams. This thrilling tale follows the adventures of a young protagonist who becomes entangled in the Mosstrooper's world of danger, intrigue, and ancient folklore. As secrets are unveiled and loyalties are tested, the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the rich history and haunting landscapes of the Scottish Borderlands.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Folklore

Themes: Legends and Mythology, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
