Synopsis: In 'At His Gates: A Novel. Vol. 2 (of 3)', the captivating story continues as the lives of the characters intertwine in a web of secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances. As the plot thickens, the reader is drawn deeper into the world of high society, where appearances can be deceiving and loyalty is a rare commodity. With each turn of the page, the tension builds, leaving the reader eagerly anticipating the shocking revelations that lie just beyond the gates.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Social class, Betrayal, Identity
