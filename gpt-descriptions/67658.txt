Synopsis: In "White Cockades: An Incident of the 'Forty-Five'", Prime-Stevenson takes us on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Jacobite uprising in Scotland. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue and rebellion, the story follows the lives of two young men who find themselves caught up in a dangerous game of loyalty, love, and sacrifice. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of war and betrayal, their paths intertwine with those of historical figures, adding an extra layer of authenticity to this gripping tale. With its vivid descriptions and compelling characters, "White Cockades" is a captivating historical adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Love, Sacrifice
