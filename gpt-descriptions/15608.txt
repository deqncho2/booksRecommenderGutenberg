Synopsis: In the Days of Poor Richard takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Richard, a young man with dreams of success and adventure in colonial America. Set against the backdrop of the Revolutionary War, this enthralling tale follows Richard as he navigates love, friendship, and the pursuit of his dreams. With wit, charm, and a touch of mystery, this story paints a vivid picture of a bygone era and the indomitable spirit of its people.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Friendship, Love, Patriotism
