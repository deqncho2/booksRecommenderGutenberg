Synopsis: In 'The Creators: A Comedy' by Sinclair, May, a group of eccentric artists and writers come together to create a masterpiece, but their creative process takes an unexpected turn when their own lives become entangled in the drama they are trying to portray. As they navigate through love triangles, artistic rivalries, and existential crises, the line between reality and fiction blurs, leading to hilarious and thought-provoking situations that challenge their perceptions of art and life.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Satire

Themes: Artistic expression, Identity, Relationships, Reality vs. fiction
