Synopsis: In 'At Aboukir and Acre: A Story of Napoleon's Invasion of Egypt', follow the thrilling adventures of a young British officer, Ned Hearne, as he embarks on a perilous journey to Egypt during Napoleon's invasion. Amidst the chaos of war, Ned finds himself caught up in a web of espionage, treachery, and unexpected alliances. From the fierce battles of Aboukir to the legendary siege of Acre, Ned's courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to outwit his enemies and fulfill his mission? Prepare for a gripping tale of bravery, strategy, and the clash of empires.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Espionage, Conflict
