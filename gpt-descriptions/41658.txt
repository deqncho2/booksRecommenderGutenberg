Synopsis: In 'The Boss of Taroomba' by E. W. Hornung, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small Australian outback town of Taroomba, bringing with him an air of intrigue and danger. As the townspeople become captivated by his enigmatic presence, they soon find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of Taroomba hanging in the balance, the residents must navigate a treacherous landscape of loyalty and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Western

Themes: Identity, Power, Deception
