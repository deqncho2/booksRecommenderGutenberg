Synopsis: In 'The Needed Change in the Age of Consent: An Appeal For the Better Protection of Our Girls' by Arthur, Richard, a renowned advocate for children's rights delves into a thought-provoking exploration of the age of consent laws. Through compelling anecdotes and meticulous research, Arthur challenges societal norms and sheds light on the urgent need for change in order to safeguard the well-being of young girls. This eye-opening book presents a compelling argument that will leave readers questioning the current legal framework and advocating for a better future for our most vulnerable population.

Genres: Non-fiction, Social Justice, Advocacy

Themes: Age of Consent, Child Protection, Legal Reform, Gender Equality
