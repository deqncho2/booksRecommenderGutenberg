Synopsis: In 'Tales of the Covenanters' by Ellen Emma Guthrie, readers are transported to 17th century Scotland, a time of religious turmoil and persecution. Through a collection of captivating stories, the author introduces us to a group of courageous individuals who risked everything to uphold their beliefs and fight for religious freedom. From secret meetings in hidden caves to daring escapes from the clutches of their oppressors, these tales paint a vivid picture of the Covenanters' unwavering faith and unwavering determination. As we delve into the lives of these remarkable characters, we witness their struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices, ultimately leaving us inspired by their unwavering commitment to their cause.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Religious Fiction

Themes: Religious persecution, Faith, Courage, Freedom
