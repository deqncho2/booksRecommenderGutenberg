Synopsis: In 'The Moons of Mars' by Evans, Dean, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious discovery during a mission to Mars, leading to a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As secrets unravel and danger looms, the protagonist must navigate through a web of intrigue and deception to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic moons of Mars.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Betrayal, Scientific Advancement
