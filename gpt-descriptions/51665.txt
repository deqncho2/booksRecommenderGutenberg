Synopsis: In "Tippoo Sultaun: A tale of the Mysore war" by Taylor, Meadows, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the Mysore war, where the fate of nations hangs in the balance. Amidst the chaos, a young British officer finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden love. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of war, he must confront his own demons and make choices that will shape his destiny. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, this historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through a pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Loyalty and betrayal, Power and ambition
