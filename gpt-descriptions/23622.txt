Synopsis: In 'About Peggy Saville' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, readers are introduced to the charming and enigmatic Peggy Saville. Set in the early 20th century, the story follows Peggy's journey as she navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations. As she encounters various individuals who shape her life, Peggy's resilience and determination are put to the test. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the choices we make and the consequences they bring.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and relationships, Self-discovery, Social expectations
