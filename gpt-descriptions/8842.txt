Synopsis: In 'Weymouth New Testament in Modern Speech, 1 Timothy', Richard Francis Weymouth presents a fresh and contemporary translation of the biblical book of 1 Timothy. This powerful and thought-provoking work delves into the teachings and instructions of the apostle Paul to his young protégé Timothy, exploring themes of leadership, faith, and the challenges faced by early Christian communities. Through Weymouth's modern language, readers are invited to engage with the timeless wisdom and guidance found within this sacred text.

Genres: Religious literature, Biblical translation

Themes: Leadership, faith, Christian community, guidance
