Synopsis: In 'Invisible Links' by Selma Lagerlöf, a mysterious and enchanting collection of short stories, readers are transported to a world where the boundaries between reality and the supernatural blur. From a haunted forest to a cursed painting, each tale explores the hidden connections that exist between individuals and the unseen forces that shape their lives. As the characters navigate love, loss, and the inexplicable, Lagerlöf weaves a captivating tapestry of human emotions and the inexplicable mysteries that lie just beyond our grasp.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Supernatural

Themes: Fate and destiny, Love and relationships, The power of the supernatural, The interconnectedness of all things
