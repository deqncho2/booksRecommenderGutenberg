Synopsis: In 'A Little Traitor to the South: A War Time Comedy with a Tragic Interlude', the reader is transported to the American Civil War era, where a young Confederate soldier finds himself torn between loyalty to his cause and his growing friendship with a Union spy. As the war rages on, the soldier must navigate the complexities of love, honor, and betrayal, leading to a tragic turn of events that will forever change his life. This captivating tale explores the blurred lines of loyalty and the consequences of one's choices during times of conflict.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Drama, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Love, Betrayal, Consequences
