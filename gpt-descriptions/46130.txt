Synopsis: 
In 'Locke' by Fowler, Thomas, we are introduced to a mysterious protagonist named Locke, who possesses an extraordinary ability that allows him to access people's deepest secrets simply by touching them. As he navigates through a world filled with danger and intrigue, Locke finds himself entangled in a web of conspiracy and betrayal. With his unique power, he becomes both a sought-after asset and a target, forcing him to make difficult choices that will determine the fate of those around him. As the story unfolds, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and Locke's true identity is revealed, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Power and control, Identity and self-discovery, Betrayal and loyalty
