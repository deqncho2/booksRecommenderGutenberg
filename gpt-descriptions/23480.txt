Synopsis: In 'What Became of Them? and, The Conceited Little Pig' by Boare, G., readers are taken on a captivating journey through two interconnected tales. The first story follows a group of friends who embark on a mysterious adventure that leads them to unexpected places and tests their courage. In the second story, a conceited little pig learns a valuable lesson about humility and the importance of kindness. Both tales are filled with intrigue, humor, and heartwarming moments that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Humility, Kindness
