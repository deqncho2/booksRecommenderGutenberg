Synopsis: In 'Mark Manning's Mission: The Story of a Shoe Factory Boy' by Horatio Alger, Jr., follow the captivating journey of young Mark Manning as he navigates the challenges of working in a shoe factory. Mark's life takes an unexpected turn when he stumbles upon a mysterious mission that could change his fate forever. Filled with suspense, adventure, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Perseverance, Social class, Self-discovery
