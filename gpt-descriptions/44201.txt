Synopsis: 
"The Military Adventures of Johnny Newcome: With an Account of his Campaign on the Peninsula and in Pall Mall" follows the humorous and eventful journey of Johnny Newcome, a young and naive soldier, as he navigates the chaos and absurdity of war. From his experiences on the Peninsula during the Napoleonic Wars to his misadventures in the bustling streets of Pall Mall, Johnny's escapades are filled with mishaps, misunderstandings, and unexpected encounters. Through his eyes, readers are taken on a rollicking ride that combines satire, wit, and a touch of romance, offering a unique perspective on the realities of military life and the follies of society.

Genres: Satire, Historical Fiction, Comedy

Themes: War and its absurdities, Naivety and innocence, Social satire
