Synopsis: In 'Six Women and the Invasion' by Méléra, Marguerite-Yerta/ Yerta, Gabrielle, six women from different walks of life find themselves united in the face of an unexpected invasion. As they navigate the chaos and uncertainty, their individual strengths and vulnerabilities are put to the test. With their lives hanging in the balance, these women must confront their fears, forge unlikely alliances, and discover the power of resilience in the most challenging of circumstances.

Genres: Fiction, Thriller, Women's Fiction

Themes: Resilience, Female Empowerment, Unity in Adversity
