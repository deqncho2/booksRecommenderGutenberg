Synopsis: In 'The Hate Disease' by Murray Leinster, a mysterious illness spreads rapidly, causing people to become consumed by an overwhelming hatred towards others. As society crumbles under the weight of this newfound animosity, a small group of survivors must navigate the chaos and find a way to restore peace before humanity is completely destroyed. With tension, suspense, and unexpected twists, this gripping novel explores the darkest corners of human nature and the power of unity in the face of adversity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Hatred, Survival, Human Nature
