Synopsis: 
In 'Well, After All--' by Frank Frankfort Moore, a young woman named Muriel finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she becomes entangled with a mysterious stranger. As their relationship deepens, Muriel discovers that her own past holds unexpected connections to her enigmatic companion. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and Muriel must confront the truth about her own identity and the choices she has made. Set against the backdrop of a bustling city, this gripping tale of love, betrayal, and self-discovery will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Identity, Deception, Redemption
