Synopsis: 

In 'Fairy Tales from Brazil: How and Why Tales from Brazilian Folk-Lore', Elsie Spicer Eells takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Brazilian folklore. From mischievous fairies to brave heroes, this collection of tales introduces us to a rich tapestry of magical creatures and mythical beings. As we delve into the stories, we discover the secrets of the Amazon rainforest, encounter the spirits of the rivers, and witness the power of love and bravery in the face of adversity. With each tale, Eells weaves a spellbinding narrative that transports readers to a realm where imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Children's Literature

Themes: Brazilian folklore, Magic, Adventure, Courage
