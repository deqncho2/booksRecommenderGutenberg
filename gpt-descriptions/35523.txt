Synopsis: In 'Only One Love; or, Who Was the Heir' by Charles Garvice, a mysterious inheritance sets off a chain of events that will test the strength of love and loyalty. As secrets are unveiled and unexpected alliances are formed, the characters find themselves caught in a web of deceit and betrayal. With each twist and turn, the reader is left wondering who will ultimately claim the coveted inheritance and who can be trusted.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love and loyalty, Deception and betrayal
