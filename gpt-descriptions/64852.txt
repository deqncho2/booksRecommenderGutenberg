Synopsis: In the gripping historical novel "George Helm" by David Graham Phillips, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the American Civil War. Set against the backdrop of the Union Army's march through the South, the story follows the eponymous protagonist, George Helm, a young soldier with a mysterious past. As Helm navigates the horrors of war and the complexities of human relationships, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and betrayals that threaten to unravel his very existence. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this captivating tale explores the depths of loyalty, honor, and the enduring power of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity, Resilience
