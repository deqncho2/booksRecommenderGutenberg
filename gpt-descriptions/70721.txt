Synopsis: In 'The Cairn: A Gathering of Precious Stones from Many Hands' by Nicolas, Sarah Davison, Lady, a mysterious cairn appears in a small village, attracting the attention of its inhabitants. As each person adds their own precious stone to the pile, the cairn begins to reveal its true purpose, leading to unexpected consequences and revelations. This captivating tale explores the power of collective actions and the interconnectedness of individuals, as the villagers' lives intertwine through the enigmatic cairn.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Community, Fate, Transformation
