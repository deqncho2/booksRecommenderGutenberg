Synopsis: In "Septimius Felton, or, the Elixir of Life," Nathaniel Hawthorne weaves a captivating tale of a young man named Septimius Felton who becomes obsessed with the search for eternal life. Set in the backdrop of the American Revolution, Septimius embarks on a journey that takes him through mysterious encounters, hidden secrets, and profound philosophical reflections. As he delves deeper into his quest, Septimius grapples with the consequences of his desires and the sacrifices he must make to obtain the elixir of life.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Historical fiction, Philosophical fiction

Themes: Immortality, Mortality, Human desires, Sacrifice, Redemption
