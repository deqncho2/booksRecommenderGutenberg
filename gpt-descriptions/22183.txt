Synopsis: In 'Wilton School; or, Harry Campbell's Revenge' by F. E. Weatherly, a young boy named Harry Campbell finds himself at the prestigious Wilton School, where he is subjected to relentless bullying by his classmates. Determined to stand up for himself, Harry hatches a plan for revenge that will not only teach his tormentors a lesson but also challenge the oppressive hierarchy of the school. As Harry's plan unfolds, he discovers unexpected allies and uncovers dark secrets that threaten to unravel everything he has worked for. This gripping tale of resilience, justice, and the power of friendship will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, School Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Bullying, Revenge, Friendship, Justice
