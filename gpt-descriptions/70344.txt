Synopsis: In 'Odds and Ends' by B. M. Croker, a young woman named Mabel finds herself caught in a web of secrets and intrigue when she inherits a mysterious estate in the English countryside. As she delves deeper into the history of the property, she uncovers a series of hidden treasures and long-forgotten stories that will change her life forever. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, Mabel must navigate through a labyrinth of family secrets and unexpected twists to uncover the truth behind her inheritance.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Discovery
