Synopsis: In "Women for Votes" by Elizabeth Hughes, a group of courageous women band together to fight for their right to vote in a time when their voices were silenced. As they navigate the challenges of a male-dominated society, they discover the power of unity and the strength of their convictions. With determination and resilience, these women challenge the status quo and pave the way for future generations to come.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Women's Fiction, Social Justice

Themes: Women's Suffrage, Gender Equality, Activism, Empowerment
