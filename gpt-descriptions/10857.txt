Synopsis: 
In "My Native Land: The United States," James Cox takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders, beauties, and people of the United States. With vivid descriptions, character sketches, and a rich tapestry of folklore, traditions, legends, and history, Cox weaves together a tapestry of stories that both entertain and educate. From the majestic landscapes to the diverse cultures and fascinating individuals, this book offers a delightful exploration of the United States, appealing to both the young and the old.

Genres: Travelogue, Folklore, History

Themes: National identity, Cultural diversity, Natural beauty, Historical significance
