Synopsis: In 'The Dragon Painter' by Fenollosa, Mary McNeil, a mysterious and talented artist captures the attention of the art world with his extraordinary paintings of dragons. However, his obsession with finding a lost princess who he believes is trapped in the body of a dragon threatens to consume him. As he delves deeper into his art, his sanity and the line between reality and fantasy become blurred, leading to a captivating exploration of love, identity, and the power of art.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Artistic obsession, Love and longing, Identity and self-discovery
