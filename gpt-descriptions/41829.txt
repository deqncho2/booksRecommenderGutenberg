Synopsis: In 'The Serf' by Thorne, Guy, a young man named Ethan finds himself thrust into a world of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden society within his own town. As he delves deeper into this clandestine world, Ethan uncovers dark secrets that threaten to unravel everything he thought he knew about his own identity and the history of his community. With the help of a group of unlikely allies, Ethan must navigate treacherous alliances and face unimaginable challenges to uncover the truth and protect those he loves.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Betrayal, Power
