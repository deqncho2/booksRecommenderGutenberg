Synopsis: In 'King Horn, Floriz and Blauncheflur, The Assumption of Our Lady', embark on a captivating journey through three interconnected tales that span across time and kingdoms. Follow the adventures of King Horn as he faces treacherous challenges, witness the blossoming love between Floriz and Blauncheflur, and delve into the mystical realm of The Assumption of Our Lady. With unexpected twists and turns, this enchanting collection of stories will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within.

Genres: Medieval Romance, Fantasy, Religious Allegory

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty and Betrayal, Divine Intervention
