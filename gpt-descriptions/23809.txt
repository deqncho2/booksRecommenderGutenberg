Synopsis: In 'An Idyl of the East Side: 1891', Thomas A. Janvier takes us on a captivating journey through the vibrant streets of New York City's East Side. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows the lives of two young immigrants, Rosa and Luigi, as they navigate the challenges and opportunities of their new home. Amidst the bustling tenements and bustling markets, their paths intertwine with a cast of colorful characters, each with their own dreams and secrets. As Rosa and Luigi strive to carve out a better future for themselves, they find themselves caught in a web of love, betrayal, and unexpected twists that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Immigration, Social class, Love and sacrifice
