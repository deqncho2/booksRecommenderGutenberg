Synopsis: 
In "Bromide Printing and Enlarging," John A. Tennant takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of bromide prints and enlargements. This practical guide provides step-by-step instructions on creating stunning bromide prints through contact and enlarging techniques, using both natural and artificial light. Additionally, Tennant delves into the art of toning bromide prints and enlargements, adding a touch of creativity to the process. With detailed explanations and helpful tips, this book is a must-have for anyone interested in mastering the art of bromide printing and enlarging.

Genres: Non-fiction, Photography, Art

Themes: Photography techniques, Darkroom processes, Artistic expression
