Synopsis: In "Christmas Every Day and Other Stories," William Dean Howells takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of heartwarming and thought-provoking tales. From a magical Christmas that repeats itself endlessly to unexpected encounters with strangers, these stories explore the joys, challenges, and lessons of everyday life. With his signature wit and keen observation, Howells invites readers to reflect on the true meaning of love, family, and the spirit of the holiday season.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Holiday

Themes: Love, Family, Generosity, Reflection, Redemption
