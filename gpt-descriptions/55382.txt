Synopsis: 
'Field Book of Common Rocks and Minerals' is a comprehensive guide that takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse geological wonders of the United States. Written by Frederic Brewster Loomis, this book serves as an invaluable resource for identifying and understanding the origins and meanings behind various rocks and minerals found across the country. From the towering mountains of the Rockies to the sparkling shores of the Great Lakes, Loomis expertly unveils the hidden stories and geological marvels that lie beneath the surface. With vivid descriptions, detailed illustrations, and fascinating anecdotes, this field book is a must-have for anyone with a passion for geology and a desire to explore the natural wonders of America.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Geology, Natural History, Exploration
