Synopsis: In the midst of a dark and stormy night, a grieving man is visited by a mysterious raven that perches upon his chamber door. As the man engages in a haunting conversation with the bird, he becomes increasingly consumed by its presence and the foreboding message it brings. With each passing moment, the man's descent into madness intertwines with the raven's enigmatic nature, leading to a chilling climax that will leave readers questioning the boundaries between reality and the supernatural.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, poetry

Themes: Grief and loss, the human psyche, the supernatural, the power of language
