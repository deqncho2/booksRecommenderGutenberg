Synopsis: In "Possum" by Mary Grant Bruce, a young girl named Possum embarks on a thrilling adventure when she discovers a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she unravels the mystery, Possum encounters unexpected allies and faces dangerous challenges that test her courage and determination. With each step closer to the truth, Possum's world expands, revealing a hidden realm filled with magic and wonder. Will she be able to unlock the secrets of her family's past and save the future?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Family secrets, Courage, Discovery
