Synopsis: 
In "Grace Harlowe with the American Army on the Rhine," Grace finds herself in the midst of post-World War I Germany, serving as a Red Cross nurse. As she navigates the challenges of a war-torn country, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of espionage and intrigue. With her quick thinking and unwavering determination, Grace must uncover the truth and protect those she holds dear, all while facing the harsh realities of war.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Resilience, Espionage, Post-War Reconstruction
