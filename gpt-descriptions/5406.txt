Synopsis: 
In 'Afoot in England', W. H. Hudson takes us on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of England. With vivid descriptions and a keen eye for detail, the author immerses us in the beauty of the English countryside, from the rolling hills of the Cotswolds to the rugged cliffs of Cornwall. Along the way, he encounters fascinating characters, uncovers hidden historical treasures, and delves into the rich folklore and traditions of the land. As he wanders through quaint villages and explores ancient ruins, Hudson's love for nature and his deep appreciation for the English way of life shine through, making this travelogue a delightful and enchanting read.

Genres: Travelogue, Nature Writing, Memoir

Themes: Nature, English culture, History, Folklore
