Synopsis: In 'Si Klegg, Book 4: Experiences of Si and Shorty on the Great Tullahoma Campaign', Si Klegg and his comrade Shorty embark on a thrilling adventure during the Civil War. As they join the Union Army's campaign to capture Tullahoma, Tennessee, Si and Shorty face numerous challenges, from battling the enemy to enduring the harsh conditions of war. With their unique blend of humor and courage, the duo navigates through unexpected twists and turns, providing an entertaining and insightful glimpse into the life of soldiers during this tumultuous period.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Sacrifice
