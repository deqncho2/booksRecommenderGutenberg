Synopsis: In 'Victor Ollnee's Discipline' by Garland, Hamlin, a young man named Victor Ollnee finds himself caught between the allure of a carefree life and the expectations of society. As he navigates the challenges of love, ambition, and personal growth, Victor must confront the consequences of his choices and discover the true meaning of discipline. Set against the backdrop of a changing world, this captivating novel explores the complexities of self-discovery and the pursuit of one's passions.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Society's expectations, Personal growth
