Synopsis: 
In 'Write It Right: A Little Blacklist of Literary Faults', Ambrose Bierce takes on the role of a witty and sharp-tongued writing instructor, guiding readers through a collection of common writing mistakes and offering humorous yet insightful advice on how to avoid them. With his characteristic sarcasm and biting wit, Bierce dissects the flaws in grammar, punctuation, word usage, and other aspects of writing, providing both practical tips and entertaining anecdotes along the way. This little gem of a book is a must-read for aspiring writers and language enthusiasts alike, offering a delightful blend of instruction and entertainment.

Genres: Non-fiction, Writing, Language

Themes: Writing advice, Language usage, Humor
