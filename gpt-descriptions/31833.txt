Synopsis: In 'The Wedge' by H. B. Fyfe, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Endlich discovers a groundbreaking invention that has the potential to change the world. However, his creation attracts the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess it. As Dr. Endlich finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect his invention and ensure its potential is used for the greater good.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific discovery, Power struggles, Ethics
