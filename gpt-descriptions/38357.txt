Synopsis: In 'By Right of Sword' by Arthur W. Marchmont, a young man named Ronald Valletort finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he inherits a grand estate. As he delves into the secrets of his family's past, he uncovers a hidden world of political intrigue, forbidden love, and long-buried secrets. With his life on the line, Ronald must navigate treacherous alliances and confront his own inner demons to claim his rightful place. This gripping tale of suspense and adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Inheritance, Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
