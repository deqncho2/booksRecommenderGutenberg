Synopsis: In 'Within an Inch of His Life' by Emile Gaboriau, a prominent judge finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when a murder takes place within the walls of his own home. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers shocking secrets and unexpected connections that threaten to unravel his own life. With his reputation and personal safety at stake, the judge must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and bring the real culprit to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Justice and injustice, Betrayal, Deception, Power dynamics
