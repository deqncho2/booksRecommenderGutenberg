Synopsis: In 'The Mad Planet' by Murray Leinster, a catastrophic event has transformed Earth into a bizarre and dangerous world. As a lone survivor navigates this new environment, he encounters unimaginable creatures and faces perilous challenges. With his survival hanging in the balance, he must unravel the mysteries of this mad planet to find hope amidst the chaos.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Post-Apocalyptic

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Adaptation
