Synopsis: In 'The Prophetic Camera' by McGreevey, John, a young photographer stumbles upon an antique camera with a mysterious past. As he delves deeper into its history, he discovers that the camera has the uncanny ability to capture glimpses of the future. With each photograph, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and must navigate the consequences of altering destiny. As he grapples with the moral implications of his newfound power, he must decide how far he is willing to go to change the course of events.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fate vs. Free Will, Morality, Consequences of Actions
