Synopsis: In 'The Wiving of Lance Cleaverage' by Alice MacGowan, a young man named Lance Cleaverage finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and mystery as he navigates the complexities of marriage and relationships. Set against the backdrop of a small town, Lance's journey takes unexpected turns as he discovers the dark secrets hidden beneath the surface of seemingly idyllic lives. With captivating characters and a gripping plot, this novel explores the complexities of human emotions and the consequences of our choices.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and betrayal, Small-town secrets, Consequences of choices
