Synopsis: In "The Coming of the Friars" by Augustus Jessopp, a small English town is turned upside down when a group of mysterious friars arrive, bringing with them a new way of life and challenging the established order. As the townspeople grapple with the changes, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of faith and power is revealed. With its intriguing characters and unexpected twists, this captivating tale explores the clash between tradition and progress, and the profound impact of religious fervor on a community.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Religion, Power, Tradition vs. Progress
