Synopsis: 
In 'My Story' by Mills, Anson, a young man finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that threatens to unravel his entire existence. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he must confront his own past and make choices that will determine his future. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Consequences
