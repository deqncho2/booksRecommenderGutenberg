Synopsis: 
'Joseph Pennell's pictures of the Panama Canal' takes readers on a visual journey through the awe-inspiring landscapes and monumental engineering feat of the Panama Canal. Through a series of lithographs created by the artist during his time on the Isthmus of Panama, the book captures the grandeur and intricacies of this remarkable project. Accompanied by the artist's impressions and notes, the reader is transported to the vibrant and bustling atmosphere of the canal construction, gaining a unique perspective on its significance and impact.

Genres: Art, Travel, History

Themes: Engineering marvels, Human achievement, Cultural exploration
