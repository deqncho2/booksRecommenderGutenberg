Synopsis: In 'True to a Type, Vol. 1 (of 2)' by Cleland, Robert, we are introduced to a captivating world of mystery and intrigue. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows the enigmatic protagonist, Edward, as he navigates through a web of secrets and hidden identities. As Edward delves deeper into the dark underbelly of society, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning the true nature of the characters and their motives. Will Edward uncover the truth before it's too late? Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction. Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal.
