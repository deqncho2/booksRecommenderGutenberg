Synopsis: 
In 'Horace' by Theodore Martin, Sir, a young man named Horace finds himself caught in a web of secrets and deception as he navigates the treacherous world of high society in 19th century England. Drawn into a dangerous game of power and manipulation, Horace must uncover the truth behind a mysterious disappearance while facing unexpected challenges and unexpected allies. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of society, Horace discovers that nothing is as it seems, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. With his life hanging in the balance, Horace must rely on his wit and courage to unravel the truth and escape the clutches of those who seek to destroy him.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Power, Betrayal
