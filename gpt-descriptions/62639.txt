Synopsis:
In 'Frank Merriwell's Trust; Or, Never Say Die' by Standish, Burt L., we follow the thrilling adventures of Frank Merriwell, a young and talented athlete who finds himself caught up in a dangerous web of deceit and betrayal. When Frank's trust is shattered, he must rely on his determination, courage, and unwavering spirit to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way. As he navigates through treacherous situations and encounters unexpected allies and enemies, Frank's resilience and integrity are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to reclaim his trust and emerge victorious, or will he succumb to the forces working against him?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Sports

Themes: Trust, Betrayal, Perseverance
