Synopsis: In 'Wessex' by Charles G. Harper, a young archaeologist embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization buried deep within the mysterious landscapes of Wessex. As he delves into the forgotten history of this enigmatic region, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected romance. With each discovery, the protagonist unravels a complex tapestry of myths, legends, and hidden treasures, leading him to question his own identity and purpose. As the past and present collide, he must navigate treacherous alliances and confront his deepest fears to unlock the truth that lies beneath the surface of Wessex.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Archaeology, Identity, Mythology, Love and Romance
