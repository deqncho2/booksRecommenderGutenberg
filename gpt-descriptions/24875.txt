Synopsis: 
Step into the enchanting world of Lucy Maud Montgomery's short stories, 1904, where ordinary lives are transformed by extraordinary moments. From the whimsical adventures of mischievous children to the poignant tales of love and loss, this collection showcases Montgomery's unparalleled storytelling prowess. Delve into the lives of captivating characters as they navigate the complexities of friendship, family, and the pursuit of dreams. With each turn of the page, you'll be captivated by Montgomery's vivid imagination and her ability to evoke a range of emotions. Prepare to be transported to a world where ordinary moments become extraordinary, and where the power of storytelling knows no bounds.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Canadian Literature

Themes: Friendship, Family, Love, Loss, Dreams, Imagination
