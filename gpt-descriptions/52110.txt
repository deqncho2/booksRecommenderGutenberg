Synopsis: In 'The Key Note: A Novel' by Clara Louise Burnham, a young woman named Ruth finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. When she is unexpectedly thrust into the world of music, Ruth must navigate the challenges of pursuing her passion while facing the disapproval of those around her. As she strives to find her own voice, Ruth discovers the transformative power of music and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Pursuing one's passion, Overcoming societal expectations
