Synopsis:
'The Old Pincushion; or, Aunt Clotilda's Guests' by Molesworth, Mrs. is a delightful tale that follows the adventures of a group of children who visit their eccentric Aunt Clotilda. Set in a charming countryside estate, the story unfolds as the children encounter mysterious rooms, secret passages, and unexpected surprises. As they navigate through the enchanting house, they uncover hidden treasures and embark on a quest to solve a puzzling riddle. With each twist and turn, the children's bond grows stronger, and they learn valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Imagination
