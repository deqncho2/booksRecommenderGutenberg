Synopsis: 

In 'The Destroyers' by Randall Garrett, a young man named Jason Dinalt finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he is unexpectedly thrust into a world of secret societies, ancient artifacts, and deadly conspiracies. As he navigates the treacherous waters of power and deception, Jason must uncover the truth behind a mysterious organization known as the Destroyers, all while trying to stay one step ahead of those who would stop at nothing to protect their secrets. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Jason's life hangs in the balance. Will he be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Conspiracy, Power, Deception
