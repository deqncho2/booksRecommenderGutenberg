Synopsis: 
In 'Down the Snow Stairs; Or, From Good-Night to Good-Morning' by Alice Corkran, a young girl named Mabel embarks on a magical adventure after she falls asleep and finds herself in a mysterious snowy world. As she explores this enchanting realm, Mabel encounters talking animals, encounters unexpected challenges, and discovers the true power of friendship. With each step she takes, Mabel's journey becomes more captivating and unpredictable, leading her to uncover secrets that will change her life forever.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: 
Imagination, Friendship, Self-discovery
