Synopsis: In 'The Birds and the Bees' by David E. Fisher, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious event causes the local bird population to behave strangely. As the birds become increasingly aggressive, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden connection between the birds and her own family history. With the help of her eccentric grandfather and a group of unlikely allies, Lily must unravel the secrets of the birds and find a way to restore balance to their town before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Family secrets, Nature's power, Coming of age
