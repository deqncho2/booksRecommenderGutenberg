Synopsis: 
'Nearly Bedtime: Five Short Stories for the Little Ones' by Wilson, H. Mary is a delightful collection of bedtime stories that will captivate young readers and transport them to magical worlds. From a mischievous fairy who loves to play pranks to a brave little teddy bear on a quest, each story is filled with adventure, imagination, and heartwarming moments. With colorful illustrations and engaging storytelling, this book is perfect for creating a cozy and enchanting bedtime routine.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Bravery, Bedtime Routine
