Synopsis: 
In 'Twenty-three and a half hours' leave' by Mary Roberts Rinehart, a young woman named Betty finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she agrees to a seemingly innocent request from a stranger. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding her new acquaintance, Betty becomes entangled in a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and resilience. With each passing hour, the stakes rise higher, and Betty must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Trust, Perseverance
