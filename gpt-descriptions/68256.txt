Synopsis: In 'Rat Race' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the world forever. As he races against time to protect his invention, he finds himself caught in a dangerous web of corporate greed, government conspiracies, and personal vendettas. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, where trust is a luxury and betrayal lurks at every corner.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Technology and its consequences, Power struggles, Morality and ethics
