Synopsis: In 'Reign of the Telepuppets' by Daniel F. Galouye, a mysterious phenomenon has taken over the world, where people are controlled by unseen forces and forced to act against their will. As the protagonist delves deeper into this strange reality, they uncover a web of secrets and conspiracies that threaten to unravel their own sanity. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning the nature of free will and the power of manipulation.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Control, Identity, Reality vs. Illusion
