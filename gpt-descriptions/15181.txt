Synopsis: In 'My Mother's Rival: Everyday Life Library No. 4' by Charlotte M. Brame, a young woman named Edith finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when her mother's mysterious past resurfaces. As she delves deeper into her mother's history, Edith uncovers shocking revelations that threaten to tear her family apart. With her own future hanging in the balance, Edith must navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and forgiveness to uncover the truth and find her own path to happiness.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Family Drama

Themes: Secrets and deception, Family bonds, Love and forgiveness
