Synopsis: 

In 'Sketches and Tales Illustrative of Life in the Backwoods of New Brunswick', Mrs. Beavan takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of New Brunswick. Through her vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, she provides a unique glimpse into the challenges and triumphs of life in this fascinating colony. From encounters with wild animals to the struggles of establishing a home in the rugged backwoods, the author's experiences are both thrilling and heartwarming. With each sketch and tale, readers will be transported to a world filled with adventure, resilience, and the indomitable spirit of those who call the backwoods of New Brunswick their home.

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Wilderness survival, Colonial life, Resilience, Exploration
