Synopsis: Tristram of Blent follows the intriguing story of a young man named Tristram, who unexpectedly inherits an ancient house with a mysterious past. As he delves into the secrets of his ancestral home, Tristram becomes entangled in a web of family drama, hidden identities, and long-buried scandals. With each revelation, he uncovers shocking truths that challenge his own sense of identity and force him to question the loyalty of those closest to him. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque English countryside, this gripping tale of inheritance, betrayal, and redemption will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Inheritance, Secrets
