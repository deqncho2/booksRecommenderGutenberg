Synopsis: In this captivating issue of 'The Great Round World and What Is Going On In It', readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the latest news and events from around the world. From the mysterious disappearance of a famous explorer to the discovery of a hidden treasure, this magazine for boys and girls is filled with fascinating stories that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each turn of the page, young readers will uncover new and exciting adventures that will ignite their curiosity and leave them eager for more.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Curiosity
