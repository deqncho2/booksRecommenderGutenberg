Synopsis: In 'The Automobile Girls at Washington; Or, Checkmating the Plots of Foreign Spies', the adventurous and resourceful Automobile Girls find themselves embroiled in a dangerous game of espionage in the nation's capital. As they navigate the bustling streets and political intrigue, they uncover a web of secrets and must use their wit and courage to outsmart the foreign spies threatening the security of their country. Will their love for adventure and their trust in each other be enough to save the day?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Espionage, Patriotism, Friendship
