Synopsis: In 'The Seven Great Monarchies Of The Ancient Eastern World, Vol 1: Chaldaea', George Rawlinson takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient civilizations of Chaldaea, Assyria, Babylon, Media, Persia, Parthia, and the Sassanian Empire. With detailed descriptions, maps, and illustrations, Rawlinson unveils the rich history, geography, and antiquities of these remarkable empires. From the rise and fall of powerful rulers to the grandeur of their architectural wonders, this volume offers a fascinating exploration of the ancient Eastern world.

Genres: History, Archaeology, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Power and politics, Cultural heritage
