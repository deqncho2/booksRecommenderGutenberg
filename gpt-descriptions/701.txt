Synopsis: In "The King of the Golden River," young Gluck and his two wicked brothers live in a desolate valley plagued by drought and poverty. When a mysterious visitor arrives and offers Gluck a chance to change their fortunes, he embarks on a perilous journey to find the legendary King of the Golden River. Along the way, Gluck encounters magical creatures, faces moral dilemmas, and learns the true power of kindness and selflessness.

Genres: Fairy tale, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Greed, Redemption, Environmentalism
