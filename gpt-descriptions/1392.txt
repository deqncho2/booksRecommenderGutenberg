Synopsis: In 'The Seven Poor Travellers' by Charles Dickens, a group of destitute individuals find themselves unexpectedly welcomed into a charitable institution on Christmas Eve. As they gather around a festive dinner table, each traveler shares their unique and often heart-wrenching story, revealing the hardships they have endured. Amidst the warmth and generosity of their hosts, unexpected connections and acts of kindness unfold, leaving a lasting impact on both the travelers and those who have opened their doors to them.

Genres: Fiction, Short Story, Christmas Literature

Themes: Charity, Compassion, Redemption, Human Connection
