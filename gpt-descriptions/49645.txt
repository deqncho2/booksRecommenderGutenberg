Synopsis: In 'The Mentor: Belgium the Brave', the eighth volume of the series, author Ruth Kedzie Wood takes readers on a captivating journey through the courageous and resilient spirit of Belgium. Set in the aftermath of World War I, this installment delves into the untold stories of bravery, sacrifice, and determination that emerged from the war-torn nation. Through vivid descriptions and compelling narratives, Wood sheds light on the indomitable human spirit and the power of hope in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, War

Themes: Bravery, Resilience, Human Spirit, Hope
