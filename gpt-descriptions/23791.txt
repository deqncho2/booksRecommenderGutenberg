Synopsis: In the thrilling science fiction novella 'Scrimshaw' by Murray Leinster, a group of space explorers stumble upon a mysterious alien artifact that holds the key to unimaginable power. As they delve deeper into its secrets, they find themselves caught in a dangerous race against time, facing treacherous enemies and making difficult choices that could alter the fate of humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Power, Morality
