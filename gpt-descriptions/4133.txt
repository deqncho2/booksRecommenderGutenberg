Synopsis: In the fifteenth volume of his renowned diary, Samuel Pepys takes us on a captivating journey through the months of March and April in 1661-62. As Pepys navigates the bustling streets of London, he encounters a myriad of intriguing characters and finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, scandalous affairs, and personal triumphs. From his encounters with influential figures to his observations of the city's vibrant social scene, Pepys provides a vivid and intimate account of the tumultuous times he lived in.

Genres: Diary, Historical Fiction, Memoir

Themes: Politics, Society, Personal Relationships, Ambition, Intrigue
