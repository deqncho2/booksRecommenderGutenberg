Synopsis: In 'The Genius' by Pederson, Con, a brilliant but reclusive mathematician named Ethan finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of intellect when he receives a mysterious invitation to join an exclusive society of geniuses. As he delves deeper into this enigmatic world, Ethan must navigate a web of secrets, rivalries, and unexpected alliances, all while trying to protect his groundbreaking discoveries from falling into the wrong hands. With his own sanity and the fate of his work hanging in the balance, Ethan must use his exceptional mind to outwit those who seek to exploit his genius.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Genius and madness, Power and manipulation
