Synopsis: In "Rollo in Geneva" by Abbott, Jacob, young Rollo embarks on an exciting journey to the picturesque city of Geneva. As he explores the city's rich history, stunning landscapes, and encounters fascinating characters, Rollo finds himself caught up in unexpected adventures and learns valuable life lessons along the way. From unraveling mysteries to discovering hidden treasures, Rollo's time in Geneva becomes a thrilling and transformative experience.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Cultural immersion
