Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a young and adventurous girl, finds herself embarking on a thrilling journey down to the enchanting land of Dixie. As she explores the vibrant and captivating world of the cotton plantations, Ruth encounters a series of unexpected events and encounters that will test her courage and resilience. From mysterious secrets to heartwarming friendships, Ruth's time in Dixie becomes an unforgettable adventure that will change her life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Resilience
