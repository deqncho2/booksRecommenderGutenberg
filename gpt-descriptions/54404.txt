Synopsis: In the desolate and eerie landscape of the salt marshes, a young woman named Mehalah lives a solitary life, haunted by a dark secret from her past. As the tides of fate turn, Mehalah finds herself caught in a web of love, betrayal, and revenge that threatens to consume her. With the marshes as a backdrop, this gripping tale explores the depths of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Isolation, Secrets, Redemption
