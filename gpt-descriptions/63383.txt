Synopsis: In 'The Wonder Clock; or, four & twenty marvellous Tales: being one for each hour of the day', Howard Pyle and Katharine Pyle take readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales. From the mischievous antics of a clever cat to the adventures of a brave young knight, each story unfolds with whimsy and wonder, transporting readers to a world filled with magic and imagination. As the clock ticks away, the tales weave together to create a tapestry of captivating characters and thrilling adventures that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fantasy, Fairy Tales, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Imagination, Heroism, Morality
