Synopsis: 

'Family fare: $b food management and recipes' is a comprehensive guide that offers practical tips and delicious recipes to help families manage their food budget without sacrificing taste or nutrition. Written by the United States Agricultural Research Service and the Institute of Home Economics, this book provides valuable insights into meal planning, shopping strategies, and creative ways to stretch your food dollars. With a focus on healthy and affordable meals, this resource is a must-have for anyone looking to save money while still enjoying flavorful and nutritious dishes.

Genres: Cookbook, Budgeting, Nutrition

Themes: Food management, Meal planning, Budget-friendly cooking, Nutrition
