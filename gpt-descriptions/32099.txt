Synopsis: 
'The Titanic Disaster Poem' by McKenzie, J. H. is a gripping and poignant poetic account of one of the most tragic maritime disasters in history. Through vivid imagery and emotive language, the poem explores the harrowing events surrounding the sinking of the Titanic, capturing the bravery, despair, and loss experienced by those aboard the ill-fated ship. As the narrative unfolds, the reader is transported back in time to witness the unfolding tragedy, feeling the weight of the ocean's depths and the magnitude of human resilience in the face of unimaginable circumstances.

Genres: Poetry, Historical, Tragedy

Themes: Human resilience, Tragedy, Loss, Bravery
