Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Thirty-fourth, or the Cumberland Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this remarkable regiment from 1702 to 1844. From its humble beginnings to its pivotal role in historical events, Cannon's account unveils the triumphs, challenges, and sacrifices of the soldiers who served in this esteemed regiment. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the author paints a vivid picture of the regiment's evolution, highlighting the bravery and resilience of its members. As readers delve into the pages of this historical record, they will be transported to a time of great conflict and witness the indomitable spirit of the Cumberland Regiment of Foot.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Bravery, Resilience, Military service, Historical events
