Synopsis: In 'The Actress' Daughter: A Novel' by Fleming, May Agnes, we are introduced to the captivating world of the theater through the eyes of a young woman named Evelyn. Born to a famous actress, Evelyn finds herself torn between her desire to follow in her mother's footsteps and the expectations of society. As she navigates the challenges of love, ambition, and the pursuit of her own identity, Evelyn's journey takes her on a rollercoaster of emotions and unexpected twists. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel explores the complexities of family, the sacrifices we make for our dreams, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Family, Love, Society
