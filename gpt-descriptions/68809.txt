Synopsis: In 'The Londoners: An Absurdity' by Hichens, Robert, we are introduced to a group of eccentric characters living in the bustling city of London. From a mysterious fortune teller to a struggling artist, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to a series of absurd and hilarious situations. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, love blossoms, and the characters navigate the absurdities of city life with wit and charm. 

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Absurdity of city life, Human connection, Identity
