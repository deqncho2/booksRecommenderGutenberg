Synopsis: In 'The Miniature Menace' by Frank Belknap Long, a renowned scientist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the power to shrink living beings to miniature size. As he delves deeper into its secrets, he unwittingly unleashes a terrifying menace upon the world. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, a race against time ensues as the scientist and a group of unlikely allies must find a way to stop the miniature menace before it consumes everything in its path.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Scientific discovery, Unforeseen consequences, Survival
