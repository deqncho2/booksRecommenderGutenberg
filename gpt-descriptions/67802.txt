Synopsis: In 'Little Dog Ready: How He Lost Himself in the Big World' by Stryker, Mabel F., readers are taken on a heartwarming journey alongside a small and curious dog named Ready. When Ready finds himself separated from his beloved family, he embarks on an adventure through the vast and unfamiliar world. Along the way, he encounters a diverse cast of characters, both human and animal, who teach him valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the true meaning of home. As Ready navigates through various challenges and obstacles, he must find the strength within himself to overcome adversity and ultimately discover where he truly belongs.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Belonging, Home
