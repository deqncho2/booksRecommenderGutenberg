Synopsis: In 'The Pastor's Son' by William W. Walter, a small town is shaken when the pastor's son, Jacob, mysteriously disappears. As the community grapples with the shocking event, secrets and hidden truths begin to unravel, revealing a web of deception and betrayal. With each revelation, the town's faith and trust are put to the test, forcing them to confront their own demons and question the very foundation of their beliefs. As the search for Jacob intensifies, the lines between good and evil blur, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Faith, Betrayal, Deception, Community
