Synopsis: In "Through St. Dunstan's to Light" by James H. Rawlinson, a young artist named Edward embarks on a journey that takes him through the mysterious and enchanting St. Dunstan's Cathedral. As he delves deeper into the cathedral's secrets, Edward discovers a hidden world filled with ancient prophecies, mystical symbols, and unexpected allies. With each step, he unravels the truth about his own identity and the extraordinary power he possesses. This captivating tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the walls of St. Dunstan's Cathedral.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Power, Self-discovery
