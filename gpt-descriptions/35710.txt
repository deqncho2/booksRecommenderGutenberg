Synopsis: In 'Claimants to Royalty' by John Henry Ingram, a gripping tale unfolds as a series of individuals emerge, each claiming to be the rightful heir to a long-lost royal lineage. As their stories intertwine, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and the truth becomes a matter of life and death. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating narrative explores the lengths people will go to in order to claim their birthright and the consequences that follow.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Power, Deception
