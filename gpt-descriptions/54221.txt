Synopsis: 

'Induction Coils, How to Make, Use, and Repair Them' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the fascinating world of induction coils, exploring their creation, application, and maintenance. From the renowned Ruhmkorff and Tesla coils to the intriguing medical coils and Roentgen radiography, this book offers a wealth of knowledge on these electrical devices. With step-by-step instructions and insightful explanations, the author takes readers on a journey through the history, mechanics, and practical uses of induction coils. Whether you are a curious enthusiast or a professional in the field, this book is sure to captivate your interest and expand your understanding of these remarkable inventions.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Technology

Themes: Electrical engineering, Invention, Practical applications
