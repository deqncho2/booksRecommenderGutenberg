Synopsis: In 'The Woman Who Dared' by Sargent, Epes, a young woman named Amelia finds herself trapped in a suffocating society that stifles her dreams and ambitions. Determined to break free from the constraints of her time, she embarks on a daring journey that challenges societal norms and tests her own limits. As she navigates through love, loss, and self-discovery, Amelia's courage and resilience inspire those around her, igniting a spark of rebellion that threatens to shake the foundations of the world she knows. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Feminist Literature

Themes: Female Empowerment, Breaking Societal Norms, Self-Discovery
