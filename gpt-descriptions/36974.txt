Synopsis: 

'Old Trails on the Niagara Frontier' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and hidden stories of the Niagara Frontier. Frank H. Severance delves into the forgotten tales of early settlers, Native American tribes, and the struggles they faced in this fascinating region. From the legendary figures who shaped the land to the untold secrets buried beneath its surface, this book uncovers the mysteries and adventures that have shaped the Niagara Frontier into what it is today.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Local history

Themes: Exploration, Cultural heritage, Frontier life
