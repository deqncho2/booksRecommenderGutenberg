Synopsis: 
In "In Pastures New" by Ade, George, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure after a life-altering event. Leaving behind her familiar surroundings, she sets off to explore new landscapes, encounter intriguing characters, and confront unexpected challenges. As Emily navigates through the unknown, she learns valuable lessons about resilience, the power of human connection, and the transformative nature of embracing change. This captivating tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what lies ahead for Emily in her quest for a fresh start.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Transformation, Exploration
