Synopsis: 
'Once upon a time animal stories' by Carolyn Sherwin Bailey is a delightful collection of enchanting tales that transport readers to a world where animals possess extraordinary qualities and embark on captivating adventures. From clever foxes to wise owls, mischievous rabbits to courageous lions, these stories are filled with heartwarming moments, unexpected twists, and valuable life lessons. Each tale is beautifully crafted, capturing the imagination and leaving readers eager to discover what awaits the next animal protagonist. 

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: 
Animal intelligence, Courage, Friendship, Morality
