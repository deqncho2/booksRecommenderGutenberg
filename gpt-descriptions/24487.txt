Synopsis: In "The Story of Pocahontas and Captain John Smith," E. Boyd Smith delves into the captivating tale of the legendary Native American princess and the English explorer. Set against the backdrop of the early 17th century, this historical narrative takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with adventure, cultural clashes, and unexpected alliances. As Pocahontas and Captain John Smith navigate the complexities of their respective worlds, their lives become intertwined in ways that will forever shape the course of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Cultural exchange, Love and friendship across boundaries, Exploration and discovery
