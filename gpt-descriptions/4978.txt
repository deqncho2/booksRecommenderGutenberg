Synopsis: 

In "The March of Portola and the Discovery of the Bay of San Francisco," Eldredge and Molera take readers on a captivating journey back in time to the 18th century, when Spanish explorers embarked on a daring expedition to uncover the mysterious Bay of San Francisco. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors bring to life the challenges, triumphs, and encounters that shaped this historic voyage. As the expedition unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the breathtaking landscapes, the encounters with indigenous peoples, and the relentless pursuit of discovery. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Cultural encounters, Historical significance
