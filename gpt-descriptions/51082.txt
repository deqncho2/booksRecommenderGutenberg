Synopsis: In a world where dreams can be recorded and experienced by others, a struggling actress finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when she becomes the star of a mysterious and highly anticipated film. As the line between reality and fantasy blurs, she must navigate a treacherous landscape of ambition, manipulation, and hidden agendas, all while questioning her own identity and the nature of fame. With its gripping twists and turns, "Coming Attraction" is a thrilling exploration of the power of dreams and the price of success.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Reality vs. Illusion
