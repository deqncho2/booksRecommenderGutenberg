Synopsis: 
In 'The Rainbow Trail' by Zane Grey, a young man named John Shefford embarks on a journey to the wild and untamed West, seeking answers and redemption. As he travels through the rugged landscapes of Utah, he encounters a mysterious woman named Fay Larkin, who is on the run from a dangerous religious sect. Together, they navigate the treacherous terrain, facing perilous challenges and discovering unexpected allies. As their bond deepens, Shefford finds himself torn between his duty and his growing affection for Fay. Will they be able to escape the clutches of their pursuers and find solace in the vastness of the Rainbow Trail?

Genres: Western, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Redemption, Freedom, Love and Sacrifice
