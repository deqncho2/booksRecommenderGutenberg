Synopsis: In 'The Huntress' by Footner, Hulbert, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a thrilling cat-and-mouse game with a mysterious and elusive female criminal. As he delves deeper into her dark past, he becomes obsessed with capturing her, leading to a dangerous game of wits and a shocking revelation that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Obsession, Redemption, Identity
