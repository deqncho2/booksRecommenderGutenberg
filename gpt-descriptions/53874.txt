Synopsis: In 'Under the Red Dragon: A Novel' by James Grant, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is called to investigate a series of gruesome murders in Victorian London. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a sinister secret society and must navigate a treacherous world of deception and betrayal. With his reputation and life on the line, the detective must race against time to solve the puzzle before more lives are lost.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Crime and Justice, Secrets and Betrayal
