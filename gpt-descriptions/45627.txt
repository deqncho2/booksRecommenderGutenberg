Synopsis: In 'Wings Over the Rockies; Or, Jack Ralston's New Cloud Chaser', readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as young Jack Ralston embarks on a daring mission to conquer the skies. With his innovative invention, the Cloud Chaser, Jack aims to push the boundaries of aviation and explore the uncharted territories above the majestic Rockies. However, as he soars through the clouds, he soon discovers that his journey is not without its perils. From unexpected encounters to heart-stopping challenges, Jack's determination and courage are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that lie ahead and fulfill his dreams of conquering the skies?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Innovation, Courage
