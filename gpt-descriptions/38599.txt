Synopsis: In 'The Miracle Songs of Jesus' by MacDonald Wilson, a young musician named Sarah discovers a hidden collection of ancient songs that are said to possess miraculous powers. As she delves deeper into the origins and meanings of these songs, Sarah finds herself embarking on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. Along the way, she encounters a mysterious mentor who guides her through the challenges and revelations that come with unlocking the secrets of the songs. With each melody she uncovers, Sarah's life is transformed, leading her to question the boundaries of faith, love, and the extraordinary power of music.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Spiritual

Themes: Music, Faith, Self-discovery, Spirituality
