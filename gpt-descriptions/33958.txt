Synopsis: In 'A Sister's Love: A Novel' by Heimburg, W., the lives of two sisters, Emma and Sarah, are forever changed when a long-buried family secret resurfaces. As they delve into their family's past, they uncover a web of lies, betrayal, and hidden desires that threaten to tear them apart. With each revelation, the sisters must confront their own demons and make difficult choices that will test the limits of their love and loyalty. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque coastal town, this gripping tale explores the complexities of family dynamics, the power of forgiveness, and the unbreakable bond between sisters.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Family Saga

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Forgiveness, Sisterhood
