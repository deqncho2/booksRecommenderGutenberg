Synopsis: In 'Nine O'Clock Talks' by Frederic B. Kellogg, a mysterious and enigmatic speaker captivates a small town with his nightly talks, drawing in a diverse group of listeners who are eager to uncover the secrets he holds. As the speaker delves into a wide range of topics, from philosophy to spirituality, his words begin to challenge the beliefs and perceptions of those who attend. With each passing night, tensions rise, alliances form, and the true intentions of the speaker become increasingly unclear. As the town becomes consumed by the speaker's influence, the residents must grapple with their own desires for knowledge and power, leading to unexpected consequences.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Knowledge and Power, Perception and Reality, Manipulation
