Synopsis: In 'The Girls of Friendly Terrace; or, Peggy Raymond's Success', follow the captivating journey of Peggy Raymond as she navigates the challenges and triumphs of her teenage years. Set in the charming neighborhood of Friendly Terrace, Peggy and her friends embark on a series of adventures, uncovering secrets, forming lasting friendships, and discovering their own unique talents. As Peggy strives to achieve success in various aspects of her life, she learns valuable lessons about determination, resilience, and the power of community. Will Peggy's unwavering spirit lead her to the success she dreams of?

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Ambition, Community
