Synopsis: 
'Plays by August Strindberg, Second series' is a collection of captivating and thought-provoking plays by the renowned Swedish playwright, August Strindberg. This collection delves into the complexities of human relationships, exploring themes of love, power, and the struggle for identity. From intense psychological dramas to dark comedies, each play offers a unique and compelling exploration of the human condition. With richly developed characters and gripping narratives, Strindberg's plays in this collection will leave readers captivated and eager to delve deeper into the complexities of the human psyche.

Genres: Drama, Psychological Fiction, Dark Comedy

Themes: Love, Power, Identity, Human Relationships
