Synopsis: In 'The Minute Boys of York Town' by James Otis, a group of young boys find themselves caught up in the excitement and danger of the American Revolution. As the British army approaches their town, the boys must summon their courage and resourcefulness to protect their families and fight for their freedom. With thrilling adventures and unexpected twists, this historical novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Friendship, Coming of Age
