Synopsis: 

"The Fife and Forfar Yeomanry, and 14th (F. & F. Yeo.) Battn. R.H. 1914-1919" is a captivating historical account that takes readers on a remarkable journey through the experiences of the Fife and Forfar Yeomanry during World War I. From the trenches of the Western Front to the deserts of Egypt, this book delves into the trials, triumphs, and sacrifices of these brave soldiers. Through personal anecdotes, vivid descriptions, and meticulous research, author D. Douglas Ogilvie paints a vivid picture of the war and the indomitable spirit of the men who fought in it.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, War experiences
