Synopsis: In 'Practical Phrenology Simplified' by Theodore Foster, embark on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of phrenology, where the bumps on one's head hold the key to unlocking their true potential. As the protagonist delves deeper into this unconventional science, they uncover a web of secrets, hidden talents, and unexpected connections that challenge their perception of themselves and those around them. With each revelation, the line between science and superstition blurs, leaving the reader questioning the true nature of human identity and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Self-discovery, Science vs. Superstition
