Synopsis: 
In 'XXXII Ballades in Blue China', Lang, Andrew takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting ballads. Each ballad transports the reader to a different time and place, filled with vivid imagery and mysterious tales. From ancient China to medieval Europe, these mesmerizing poems explore themes of love, loss, and the power of storytelling. With each turn of the page, readers will find themselves immersed in a world of lyrical beauty and timeless emotions.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love, Loss, Storytelling
