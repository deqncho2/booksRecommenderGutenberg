Synopsis: 
'Jaufry the Knight and the Fair Brunissende: A Tale of the Times of King Arthur' follows the adventures of Jaufry, a valiant knight, and his love for the beautiful Brunissende. Set in the enchanting era of King Arthur, this captivating tale takes readers on a journey filled with chivalry, romance, and perilous quests. As Jaufry faces numerous challenges and encounters mythical creatures, he must prove his worthiness to win the heart of Brunissende. Will their love prevail against all odds?

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Chivalry, Love and Sacrifice
