Synopsis: 
In 'The Bride of the Nile - Volume 01', we are transported to ancient Egypt, where we follow the captivating story of a young Greek woman named Roxana. After being captured and sold into slavery, Roxana finds herself in the midst of a tumultuous world filled with political intrigue, forbidden love, and dangerous secrets. As she navigates the treacherous waters of the Nile, Roxana's strength and resilience are put to the test, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected alliances. Will she be able to overcome the challenges that fate has thrown her way and find her true place in this ancient civilization?

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Power and politics, Identity and self-discovery
