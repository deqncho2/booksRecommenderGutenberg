Synopsis: 
In "Washington Irving" by Charles Dudley Warner, embark on a captivating journey through the life and literary career of one of America's most beloved authors. From his humble beginnings in New York to his international acclaim, this biography delves into the fascinating world of Washington Irving, revealing the secrets behind his timeless tales and the man behind the pen. With vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, Warner paints a vivid portrait of Irving's life, exploring his friendships, travels, and the inspirations that shaped his iconic stories. Discover the captivating story of a literary genius who left an indelible mark on American literature.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: American literature, Romanticism, Cultural identity
