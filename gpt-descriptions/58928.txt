Synopsis: In 'Princess Kiku: A Japanese Romance. A Play for Girls', follow the captivating journey of Princess Kiku as she navigates the complexities of love, duty, and tradition in ancient Japan. As she faces unexpected challenges and encounters mysterious characters, Princess Kiku must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny. This enchanting play for girls will transport readers to a world of vibrant culture, breathtaking landscapes, and timeless romance.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Tradition and Modernity, Female Empowerment
