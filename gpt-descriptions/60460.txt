Synopsis: In 'The Raider' by Don Berry, a mysterious figure emerges from the shadows, wreaking havoc on a small coastal town in Oregon during the 19th century. As the townspeople struggle to uncover the identity of this enigmatic raider, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, the tension rises, and the stakes become higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Justice, Survival
