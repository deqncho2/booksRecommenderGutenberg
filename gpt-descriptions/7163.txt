Synopsis: 

In 'The History of Australian Exploration from 1788 to 1888' by Ernest Favenc, embark on a captivating journey through the untamed and mysterious landscapes of Australia. From the arrival of the First Fleet to the intrepid expeditions of explorers like Burke and Wills, this book delves into the triumphs, tragedies, and extraordinary tales of those who ventured into the unknown. With vivid descriptions and gripping narratives, Favenc brings to life the hardships, discoveries, and encounters with indigenous cultures that shaped the course of Australian exploration. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Indigenous cultures, Survival
