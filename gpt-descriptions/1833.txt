Synopsis: In 'The Case of the Registered Letter' by Auguste Groner, a seemingly ordinary registered letter sets off a chain of events that leads Detective Joseph Muller on a thrilling investigation. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Muller uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Muller must use his sharp intellect and intuition to solve the case before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Uncovering the Truth
