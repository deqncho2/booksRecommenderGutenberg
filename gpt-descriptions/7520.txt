Synopsis: In 'Snow-Blind' by Katharine Newlin Burt, a young woman named Judith embarks on a journey to a remote mountain village in search of her missing brother. As she delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding his disappearance, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, treachery, and forbidden love. With the harsh winter closing in, Judith must navigate the treacherous landscape of the human heart and confront the chilling truth that lies hidden beneath the snow.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family bonds, Betrayal, Survival, Forbidden love
