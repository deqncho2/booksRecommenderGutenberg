Synopsis: In "Zuleika Dobson; Or, An Oxford Love Story," the enchanting Zuleika arrives at Oxford University, captivating the hearts of all the male students. As they fall hopelessly in love with her, they become willing to do anything to win her affection, even if it means sacrificing their own lives. Amidst this frenzy of infatuation, Zuleika finds herself torn between her desire for love and her determination to remain independent. With wit, satire, and a touch of the supernatural, this novel explores the power of attraction and the consequences of unrequited love.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Love, Desire, Individuality, Social Expectations
