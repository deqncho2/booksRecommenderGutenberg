Synopsis: In "The Haunted House: A True Ghost Story," the reader is introduced to Esther Cox, a young girl who becomes the center of a chilling mystery that captivates an entire community. As strange and unexplained events unfold in her presence, Esther's life is turned upside down, and she becomes known as the great Amherst mystery. This gripping account delves into the mysterious manifestations that occur in the haunted house, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the supernatural occurrences.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Non-fiction

Themes: Supernatural phenomena, Possession, Community fascination
