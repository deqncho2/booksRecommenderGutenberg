Synopsis: In 'Return to Gone-Away', the charming and mysterious town of Gone-Away is once again the backdrop for an enchanting adventure. When Portia and her family return to the town they fell in love with years ago, they discover that Gone-Away has changed in unexpected ways. As they unravel the secrets of the town and its eccentric inhabitants, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling quest that will test their courage and bring them closer together. With its whimsical charm and delightful characters, 'Return to Gone-Away' is a heartwarming tale that will transport readers to a world where magic and adventure await.

Genres: Children's Literature, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Family, Friendship, Discovery, Courage
