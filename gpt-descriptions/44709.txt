Synopsis: In 'The Norwich Directory [1802]' by Thomas Peck, a mysterious stranger arrives in the bustling city of Norwich, bringing with him a secret that threatens to unravel the lives of its inhabitants. As the stranger's presence becomes increasingly ominous, a group of unlikely allies must navigate a web of deception, betrayal, and hidden agendas to uncover the truth before it's too late. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society, this gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of a city teeming with secrets and the lengths people will go to protect them.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, Betrayal, Society and its undercurrents
