Synopsis: In 'The Psychological Regulator' by C. M. Kornbluth, a brilliant scientist invents a groundbreaking device that can manipulate human emotions and behavior. As the protagonist delves deeper into the implications of his creation, he becomes entangled in a web of power, manipulation, and moral dilemmas. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous landscape where the line between good and evil blurs. This gripping tale explores the dark side of human nature and the consequences of playing god.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Ethics, Power, Morality, Human Nature
