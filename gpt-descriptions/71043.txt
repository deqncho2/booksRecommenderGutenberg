Synopsis: 
In "Around the Clock in Europe: A Travel Sequence," Charles Fish Howell takes readers on an exhilarating journey across Europe, filled with unexpected adventures, captivating encounters, and breathtaking landscapes. From the bustling streets of Paris to the tranquil canals of Venice, Howell's vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling transport readers to each destination, immersing them in the rich history, vibrant cultures, and diverse landscapes of the continent. As the clock ticks, the author's insatiable curiosity leads him to explore hidden gems, encounter fascinating locals, and delve into the heart and soul of each city. With every turn of the page, readers will find themselves eagerly anticipating the next destination, as Howell's narrative unfolds like a thrilling travelogue, capturing the essence of Europe's timeless allure.

Genres: Travel, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Wanderlust
