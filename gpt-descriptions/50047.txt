Synopsis: 

In "The Old Sixth Regiment, Its War Record, 1861-5" by Charles K. Cadwell, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the experiences of the renowned Old Sixth Regiment during the American Civil War. Through vivid storytelling and firsthand accounts, Cadwell brings to life the trials and triumphs of this courageous group of soldiers as they navigate the chaos and brutality of war. From their initial enlistment to their final battles, the Old Sixth Regiment's unwavering dedication and sacrifice will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of these brave men.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Resilience
