Synopsis: In 'The Girls of Rivercliff School; or, Beth Baldwin's Resolve' by Amy Bell Marlowe, readers are transported to the prestigious Rivercliff School, where Beth Baldwin finds herself navigating the complexities of friendship, rivalry, and personal growth. As Beth strives to find her place among the girls of Rivercliff, she uncovers secrets that threaten to unravel the very fabric of the school's reputation. With determination and resilience, Beth must summon the courage to confront the truth and make choices that will shape her future.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Betrayal, Self-discovery
