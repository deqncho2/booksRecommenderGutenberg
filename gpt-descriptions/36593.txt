Synopsis: In 'Fordham's Feud' by Mitford Bertram, a small town is torn apart by a bitter rivalry between two prominent families, the Fords and the Hams. As the feud escalates, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the town's dark past is revealed. Amidst the chaos, a forbidden love blossoms, threatening to further ignite the flames of hatred. With suspenseful twists and turns, 'Fordham's Feud' explores the lengths people will go to protect their family name and the devastating consequences that follow.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Family loyalty, Revenge, Forbidden love, Small-town dynamics
