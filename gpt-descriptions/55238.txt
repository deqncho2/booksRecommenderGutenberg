Synopsis: In 'Italian Yesterdays, vol. 1', Mrs. Hugh Fraser takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich history of Italy. Through her vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, she unveils the hidden gems and forgotten tales of this captivating country. From the bustling streets of Rome to the serene beauty of the Tuscan countryside, the author's love for Italy shines through every page, transporting readers to a bygone era filled with romance, intrigue, and cultural wonders.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Italian culture, History, Travel, Nostalgia
