Synopsis: In the final volume of Glenarvon, the captivating and scandalous tale reaches its climax as the characters face the consequences of their actions. Lady Calantha, torn between her duty and her desires, finds herself entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal. As secrets are revealed and alliances are tested, the fate of Glenarvon hangs in the balance. Will love conquer all, or will the darkness prevail?

Genres: Gothic fiction, Romance, Historical fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Betrayal, Duty and responsibility, Redemption
