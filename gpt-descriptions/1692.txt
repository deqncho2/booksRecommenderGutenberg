Synopsis: In '1492' by Mary Johnston, embark on a captivating historical adventure that transports readers to the era of Christopher Columbus and the Age of Exploration. Set against the backdrop of the late 15th century, this enthralling novel follows the journey of a young Spanish noblewoman, Isabel, who defies societal expectations and joins Columbus' expedition to the New World. As Isabel faces the perils of the unknown, encounters indigenous cultures, and navigates the complexities of love and loyalty, she discovers her own strength and resilience in the face of adversity. With vivid descriptions and rich historical detail, '1492' offers a gripping tale of exploration, discovery, and the indomitable spirit of a remarkable woman.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Courage, Love and Loyalty
