Synopsis: In 'Master of the Vineyard' by Reed, Myrtle, a young woman named Rosamund finds herself caught in a web of secrets and forbidden desires when she becomes the apprentice of a renowned winemaker. As she delves deeper into the world of winemaking, Rosamund uncovers a dark past that threatens to unravel everything she holds dear. With each sip of wine, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of love, betrayal, and redemption that will leave her questioning her own desires and the true nature of the vineyard.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Secrets and deception, Redemption
