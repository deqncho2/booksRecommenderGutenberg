Synopsis: In 'The Long Question' by David Mason, a renowned professor embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious ancient artifact. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each step closer to the answer, he realizes that the consequences of his pursuit may be far greater than he ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Quest for knowledge, Betrayal, Power of ancient artifacts
