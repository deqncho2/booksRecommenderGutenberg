Synopsis: In 'The Merriweather Girls and the Mystery of the Queen's Fan', the four adventurous Merriweather sisters find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon an ancient fan that once belonged to a queen. As they delve deeper into the fan's history, they uncover a web of secrets and intrigue that takes them on a journey through time and across continents. With danger lurking at every turn, the girls must use their wit and courage to solve the mystery before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family bonds, Friendship, Courage, History
