Synopsis: In 'Cost of Living' by Robert Sheckley, a man named Tom finds himself in a peculiar situation when he wakes up one day to discover that everything around him has a price tag. From the air he breathes to the ground he walks on, every aspect of his life now comes with a cost. As Tom navigates this bizarre world, he must confront the consequences of his actions and make difficult choices to survive. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking scenarios, 'Cost of Living' takes readers on a thrilling journey through a society where even the most basic necessities have a price.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Satire

Themes: Consumerism, Existentialism, Morality
