Synopsis: In "The Boy Travellers on the Congo," two adventurous youths embark on a thrilling journey through the heart of Africa with the renowned explorer Henry M. Stanley. As they navigate the treacherous Congo River and encounter dangerous wildlife, they uncover ancient civilizations, witness tribal conflicts, and face unexpected challenges. This gripping tale of exploration and discovery will captivate readers as they join these young travelers on their extraordinary expedition.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Cultural encounters
