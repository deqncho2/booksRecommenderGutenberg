Synopsis: In 'Helen in the Editor's Chair' by Wheeler, Ruthe S., follow the captivating journey of Helen, a young woman who unexpectedly finds herself in a position of power as the editor of a prestigious magazine. As she navigates the challenges of her new role, Helen must confront the pressures of the publishing industry, the demands of her staff, and the complexities of her personal life. With wit, determination, and a touch of romance, Helen strives to make her mark in the world of journalism while staying true to herself.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Women's Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Identity, Love and Relationships
