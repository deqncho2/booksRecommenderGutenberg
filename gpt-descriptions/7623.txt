Synopsis:
In the gripping novel "Pelham — Complete" by Edward Bulwer Lytton, Baron Lytton, a mysterious and enigmatic character named Pelham takes center stage. Pelham is a man of many talents and secrets, navigating the treacherous waters of high society while concealing his true identity. As the story unfolds, Pelham becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and danger, where his every move is watched and his every decision could have dire consequences. With its intricate plot and captivating characters, "Pelham — Complete" is a thrilling tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Identity, Deception, Power
