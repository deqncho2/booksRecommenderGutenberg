Synopsis: 
In 'Phaeton Rogers: A Novel of Boy Life', we follow the adventures of the mischievous and imaginative Phaeton Rogers as he navigates the ups and downs of boyhood in a small New England town. From building a homemade hot air balloon to uncovering a hidden treasure, Phaeton's escapades are filled with excitement, humor, and a touch of danger. Along the way, he forms lasting friendships, learns valuable life lessons, and discovers the power of imagination and perseverance. This heartwarming coming-of-age story will transport readers back to a simpler time and remind them of the joys and challenges of growing up.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Perseverance, Childhood
