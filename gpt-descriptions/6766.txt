Synopsis: In 'The Press-Gang Afloat and Ashore' by J.R. Hutchinson, a young sailor finds himself caught in the clutches of the notorious press-gang, a group known for forcibly recruiting men into the British Navy. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the press-gang's operations, he must confront the harsh realities of life at sea and the constant threat of danger. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the lengths one man will go to regain his freedom and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Naval Fiction

Themes: Freedom, Loyalty, Survival
