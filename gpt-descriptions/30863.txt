Synopsis: In 'A Castle in Spain: A Novel' by De Mille, James, a young American artist named Richard is drawn into a mysterious world of art, love, and intrigue when he receives an unexpected invitation to a secluded castle in Spain. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the castle and its enigmatic inhabitants, Richard finds himself entangled in a web of passion, betrayal, and hidden desires. With each twist and turn, he uncovers shocking revelations that challenge his perception of reality and force him to confront his own demons. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Spanish countryside, this captivating novel takes readers on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and forbidden love.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Art and creativity, Identity and self-discovery, Love and desire, Betrayal and secrets
