Synopsis: In 'Thunder in Space' by Del Rey, Lester, a team of astronauts embarks on a perilous mission to investigate a mysterious phenomenon in the depths of space. As they venture further into the unknown, they encounter unimaginable dangers and face a race against time to save humanity from an impending catastrophe. With thrilling twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Space Opera, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Humanity's Place in the Universe
