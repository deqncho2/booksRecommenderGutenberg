Synopsis: 

'12 Pies Husbands Like Best: Aunt Jenny's Recipe Book' is a delightful collection of mouthwatering pie recipes that are sure to please any husband's taste buds. From classic apple pie to decadent chocolate silk pie, this recipe book offers a wide variety of delicious options that will have readers rushing to their kitchens to try them out. Each recipe is accompanied by charming anecdotes and tips from Aunt Jenny, making this book not only a culinary treasure but also a heartwarming read. Whether you're a seasoned baker or a novice in the kitchen, this book is a must-have for anyone looking to impress their loved ones with delectable homemade pies.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink, Memoir

Themes: Family, Tradition, Culinary Arts
