Synopsis: 

'Edison: His Life and Inventions' is a captivating biography that delves into the extraordinary life of Thomas Edison, one of the greatest inventors in history. From his humble beginnings as a curious young boy to his relentless pursuit of innovation, this book takes readers on a fascinating journey through Edison's remarkable achievements and the challenges he faced along the way. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors provide a comprehensive account of Edison's life, shedding light on his groundbreaking inventions, his entrepreneurial spirit, and his enduring impact on the world.

Genres: Biography, History, Science

Themes: Innovation, Perseverance, Entrepreneurship
