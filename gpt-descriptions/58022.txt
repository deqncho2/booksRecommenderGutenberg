Synopsis: In 'My Queen: A Weekly Journal for Young Women. Issue 5, October 27, 1900: Marion Marlowe Entrapped; or, The Victim of Professional Jealousy' by Sheldon, Lurana, the captivating story revolves around Marion Marlowe, a talented young woman who finds herself ensnared in a web of professional jealousy. As she strives to pursue her dreams and succeed in her chosen career, Marion becomes the target of envy and manipulation from those around her. With her reputation and future at stake, Marion must navigate the treacherous waters of the entertainment industry while facing unexpected challenges and betrayals. This gripping tale explores the dark side of ambition and the lengths some will go to hinder the success of others.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Professional jealousy, Ambition, Betrayal
