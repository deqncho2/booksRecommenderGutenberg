Synopsis: 

'Voyage of H.M.S. Pandora: Despatched to Arrest the Mutineers of the 'Bounty' in the South Seas, 1790-1791' takes readers on a thrilling journey to the South Seas in the late 18th century. Captain Edward Edwards and surgeon George Hamilton embark on a perilous mission to capture the mutineers of the infamous 'Bounty' and bring them to justice. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter exotic islands, the crew of H.M.S. Pandora faces numerous challenges and unexpected twists that test their loyalty, courage, and determination. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Maritime

Themes: Loyalty, Justice, Survival
