Synopsis: In 'The Cost of Shelter' by Ellen H. Richards, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits an old Victorian mansion from a distant relative. As she delves into the history of the house, Emily uncovers a dark past and a series of mysterious events that threaten her sanity and safety. With each discovery, she becomes more entangled in the house's secrets, leading her to question her own identity and the true cost of finding shelter within its walls.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Deception, Sanity, Inheritance
