Synopsis: Ivanoff, a once respected and admired man, finds himself trapped in a web of personal and societal turmoil. As he struggles with his own inner demons and the pressures of his failing marriage, Ivanoff becomes entangled in a complex web of relationships with the people around him. With his reputation crumbling and his mental state deteriorating, Ivanoff must confront his past and make difficult choices that will shape his future.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Identity, Morality, Love and Betrayal
