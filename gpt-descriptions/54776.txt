Synopsis: In 'Ordeal by Battle', Oliver, Frederick Scott takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of soldiers during World War I. Through vivid and poignant storytelling, the author explores the physical and emotional toll of war, the bonds formed between comrades, and the resilience of the human spirit. As the narrative unfolds, readers are immersed in the brutal realities of battle, witnessing the sacrifices made by ordinary men in extraordinary circumstances. This powerful and thought-provoking account offers a unique perspective on the devastating impact of war and the indomitable courage of those who fought.

Genres: Historical fiction, War literature, Biographical fiction

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Resilience
