Synopsis: 
'Four Hundred Humorous Illustrations, Vol. 2 (of 2): With Portrait and Biographical Sketch' is a delightful collection of witty and amusing illustrations that will leave readers chuckling and grinning from ear to ear. This second volume continues to showcase a wide range of comical scenarios, clever wordplay, and humorous observations on everyday life. From hilarious mishaps to clever visual puns, this collection is sure to entertain and brighten anyone's day. Accompanied by a biographical sketch and a portrait, this book offers a glimpse into the life and work of the talented artist behind these delightful illustrations.

Genres: Humor, Art, Biography

Themes: Wit and humor, Everyday life, Artistic expression
