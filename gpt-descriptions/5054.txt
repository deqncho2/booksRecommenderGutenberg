Synopsis:
In 'The Dream Doctor' by Arthur B. Reeve, a brilliant young scientist named Dr. Kennedy embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that allows him to enter the dreams of his patients. As he delves deeper into the realm of the subconscious, he uncovers a series of mysterious and unsettling dreams that seem to be connected to a real-life crime. With his unique ability, Dr. Kennedy becomes entangled in a thrilling race against time to solve the puzzle and prevent a tragedy from occurring. Filled with suspense and unexpected twists, 'The Dream Doctor' takes readers on a captivating journey through the power of dreams and the dark secrets they hold.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Dreams, Psychology, Crime-solving
