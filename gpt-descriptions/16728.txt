Synopsis: 

In 'A Catechism of Familiar Things: Their History, and the Events Which Led to Their Discovery,' the anonymous author takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating history and discovery of everyday objects and natural phenomena. From the origins of common household items to the exploration of remarkable scientific phenomena, this revised edition provides a wealth of knowledge suitable for both schools and families. Delve into the intriguing stories behind familiar things and gain a deeper understanding of the world around us.

Genres: Non-fiction, Educational, Reference

Themes: History, Discovery, Science, Education
