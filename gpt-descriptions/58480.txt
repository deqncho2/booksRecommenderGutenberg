Synopsis: In 'Perry's Saints; Or, The Fighting Parson's Regiment in the War of the Rebellion', author James Moses Nichols takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous times of the American Civil War. The story follows the remarkable exploits of a regiment led by a charismatic and unconventional parson, as they navigate the horrors of battle, the complexities of loyalty, and the enduring power of faith. With vivid descriptions and unforgettable characters, this historical novel immerses readers in the heart-wrenching struggles and triumphs of those who fought for their beliefs.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Loyalty and duty, Sacrifice and bravery
