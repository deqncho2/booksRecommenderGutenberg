Synopsis: In 'The Queen's Maries: A Romance of Holyrood', the reader is transported to the captivating world of 16th-century Scotland, where the lives of Mary, Queen of Scots, and her loyal handmaidens, known as the Queen's Maries, unfold. Amidst the grandeur and treachery of the royal court, the Maries navigate a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and personal sacrifice. As their destinies become entwined with that of their beloved queen, they must summon courage and resilience to protect their loyalty and friendship in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Love, Betrayal, Power
