Synopsis: 
In 'The Romance of a Poor Young Man' by Octave Feuillet, we are introduced to a young man named Raoul, who finds himself caught between his love for a beautiful and mysterious woman and his duty to his family. As he navigates the complexities of society and the constraints of his own circumstances, Raoul's journey takes him on a path filled with passion, sacrifice, and unexpected twists. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in the way of his happiness, or will he be forever bound by the constraints of his poverty?

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Social class and societal expectations, Personal growth and self-discovery
