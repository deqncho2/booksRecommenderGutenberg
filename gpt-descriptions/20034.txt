Synopsis: 

Prepare to be chilled to the bone as you delve into the eerie world of 'Scottish Ghost Stories' by O'Donnell, Elliott. This captivating collection of supernatural tales will transport you to the misty landscapes of Scotland, where ancient castles, lonely moors, and haunted houses hold secrets that refuse to be forgotten. From vengeful spirits seeking justice to restless souls trapped between worlds, each story will leave you breathless with anticipation, wondering what lies beyond the veil of the living. With its rich folklore and hauntingly beautiful settings, 'Scottish Ghost Stories' is a must-read for anyone who craves spine-tingling tales that will linger long after the final page is turned.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Ghosts, Scottish folklore, Haunted places, Revenge, Mystery
