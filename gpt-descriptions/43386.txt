Synopsis: 

Macmillan & Co.'s Catalogue. November 1878: Of Works in Belles Lettres, Including Poetry, Fiction, Etc. is a captivating collection that offers a tantalizing glimpse into the literary world of the late 19th century. This catalogue showcases a wide range of works in various genres, from poetry to fiction, promising a diverse and enriching reading experience. With its carefully curated selection, it invites readers to explore the imaginative realms of renowned authors and discover new voices that will captivate their hearts and minds. 

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Belles Lettres

Themes: Literature, Imagination, Artistic Expression
