Synopsis: In 'A Bibliography of Bibliography; Or, a Handy Book About Books Which Relate to Books', Joseph Sabin takes readers on a fascinating journey through the world of books about books. This comprehensive bibliography showcases a vast collection of titles that explore various aspects of bibliographic history, including the evolution of printing, bookbinding techniques, and the art of book collecting. From ancient manuscripts to modern publications, Sabin's work provides a treasure trove of information for book lovers and scholars alike. Delve into the rich tapestry of bibliographic literature and discover the hidden stories behind the books we cherish.

Genres: Non-fiction, Bibliography, Reference

Themes: Bibliographic history, Book collecting, Printing technology
