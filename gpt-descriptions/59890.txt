Synopsis: 
In 'Our Union and Its Defenders: An Oration', J. Howard Pugh delivers a powerful speech to the citizens of Burlington, N.J., on the occasion of their celebration of Independence Day in 1862. With eloquence and passion, Pugh explores the significance of the Union and its defenders, delving into the challenges and sacrifices they face during a time of great turmoil. Through his words, he paints a vivid picture of the struggles and triumphs that have shaped the nation, leaving the reader inspired and reflective.

Genres: 
Historical, Speech, Patriotic

Themes: 
Patriotism, Unity, Sacrifice, National Identity
