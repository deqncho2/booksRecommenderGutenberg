Synopsis: 
In 'Kilo: being the love story of Eliph' Hewlitt, book agent', we follow the charming and witty Eliph' Hewlitt, a book agent who finds himself entangled in a whirlwind romance. When Eliph' meets the captivating Kilo, a mysterious woman with a passion for literature, his life takes an unexpected turn. As their love story unfolds, Eliph' must navigate the complexities of the publishing world, confront his own insecurities, and discover the true meaning of love and happiness. With humor, heart, and a touch of intrigue, 'Kilo' is a captivating tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Romance, Comedy, Fiction

Themes: Love, Self-discovery, Pursuit of Happiness
