Synopsis: In 'The Making of Arguments', Gardiner takes readers on a captivating journey through the art of constructing compelling arguments. With a blend of insightful analysis and engaging examples, he explores the intricacies of persuasive reasoning, revealing the secrets behind crafting effective and impactful arguments. From dissecting logical fallacies to unraveling the power of rhetoric, this book offers a comprehensive guide to mastering the art of persuasion. Whether you're a student, professional, or simply curious about the mechanics of argumentation, Gardiner's exploration of this essential skill will leave you inspired and equipped to navigate the world of discourse with confidence.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Communication

Themes: Argumentation, Persuasion, Critical Thinking
