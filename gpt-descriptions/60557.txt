Synopsis: 

In 'The Parochial History of Cornwall, Volume 3 (of 4)', the reader is transported to the captivating world of Cornwall, where ancient legends and historical events intertwine. This volume delves deeper into the rich tapestry of the region, uncovering hidden secrets and shedding light on the lives of its inhabitants. From tales of smugglers and shipwrecks to the enigmatic ruins of forgotten castles, the author paints a vivid picture of Cornwall's past, captivating readers with each turn of the page. As the narrative unfolds, the reader is immersed in a world of mystery, adventure, and the enduring spirit of the Cornish people.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: History, Legends, Identity, Resilience
