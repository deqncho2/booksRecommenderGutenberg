Synopsis: In "The True George Washington [10th Ed.]," Paul Leicester Ford delves into the life of one of America's most iconic figures, George Washington. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Ford uncovers the lesser-known aspects of Washington's character, shedding light on his personal struggles, triumphs, and the sacrifices he made for his country. From his early years as a surveyor to his pivotal role in the American Revolution and his presidency, Ford presents a comprehensive and intimate portrait of the man behind the legend. This compelling biography offers a fresh perspective on Washington's leadership, his unwavering commitment to honor and integrity, and the enduring legacy he left behind.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Leadership, Patriotism, Integrity
