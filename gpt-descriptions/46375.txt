Synopsis: In 'The Phantom Treasure' by Harriet Pyne Grove, a young archaeologist named Amelia embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization. As she delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding a legendary treasure, Amelia finds herself entangled in a web of danger, deception, and unexpected alliances. With each clue she uncovers, the stakes rise higher, leading her on a quest that will test her courage and determination. Will Amelia be able to unravel the truth behind the phantom treasure before it falls into the wrong hands?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Legacy, Betrayal, Redemption
