Synopsis: In this captivating historical account, Holinshed delves into the life and reign of Edward the First, also known as Longshanks, the eldest son of King Henry the Third. From his early struggles to secure his claim to the throne to his relentless efforts to expand his kingdom, Edward's story is one of ambition, power, and the complexities of ruling a nation. As he faces numerous challenges, both from within and outside his realm, Edward's determination and cunning are put to the test. This engrossing narrative sheds light on the life of one of England's most influential monarchs and the turbulent times in which he lived.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Power and ambition, Leadership and governance
