Synopsis: 

'Across the Continent by the Lincoln Highway' takes readers on a thrilling and adventurous journey across the United States, following the iconic Lincoln Highway. Effie Price Gladding recounts her remarkable experiences as she embarks on a cross-country road trip in 1915, capturing the essence of a rapidly changing America. From encountering breathtaking landscapes to facing unexpected challenges, Gladding's vivid storytelling paints a vivid picture of a nation on the brink of modernization. This captivating travelogue is a testament to the spirit of exploration and the enduring allure of the open road.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Adventure, American identity, Progress and change
