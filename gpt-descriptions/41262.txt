Synopsis: In 'Dan Carter, Cub Scout, and the River Camp' by Mildred A. Wirt, young Dan Carter embarks on an exciting adventure as he joins his fellow Cub Scouts on a camping trip near the river. However, what starts as a fun-filled outing soon takes an unexpected turn, leading Dan and his friends on a thrilling journey filled with mystery and danger. As they navigate through the wilderness, facing various challenges and uncovering hidden secrets, Dan must rely on his wit, courage, and the values he has learned as a Cub Scout to overcome the obstacles that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving, Nature exploration
