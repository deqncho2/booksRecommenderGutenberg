Synopsis: 

In 'Stories of Great Inventors: Fulton, Whitney, Morse, Cooper, Edison' by Hattie E. Macomber, embark on a captivating journey through the lives of some of history's most brilliant minds. From the visionary Robert Fulton, who revolutionized transportation with his steamboat, to the ingenious Thomas Edison, whose inventions transformed the world, this book delves into the fascinating stories of these great inventors. Discover the struggles they faced, the breakthroughs they achieved, and the lasting impact they left on society. 

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Innovation, Perseverance, Impact of Inventions
