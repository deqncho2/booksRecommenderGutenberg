Synopsis: In 'Uarda: a Romance of Ancient Egypt - Volume 10', the captivating tale continues as the lives of the characters intertwine amidst the political turmoil and religious conflicts of ancient Egypt. As secrets are revealed and loyalties are tested, the protagonists find themselves caught in a web of love, betrayal, and redemption. With the fate of their civilization hanging in the balance, they must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices that will shape their destinies forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Power and Politics, Religion and Belief
