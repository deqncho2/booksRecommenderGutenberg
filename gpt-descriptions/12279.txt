Synopsis: In 'The Maid-At-Arms: A Novel', Robert W. Chambers takes us on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. Set in colonial New York, the story follows the courageous and enigmatic character of Marya Zaleska, a young woman who disguises herself as a man to join the Continental Army. As Marya navigates the perils of war, she finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances. With danger lurking at every turn, Marya must summon all her strength and cunning to survive and protect those she holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice
