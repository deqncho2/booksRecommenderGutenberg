Synopsis: In 'Grace Harlowe's Overland Riders on the Old Apache Trail', Grace and her friends embark on an exciting adventure through the rugged terrain of the Old Apache Trail. As they navigate treacherous paths and encounter mysterious characters, they uncover a hidden secret that could change everything. With danger lurking at every turn, the Overland Riders must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to overcome the challenges they face.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
