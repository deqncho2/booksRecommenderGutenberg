Synopsis: In 'Little Meg's Children' by Hesba Stretton, follow the heartwarming journey of Meg, a young girl who finds herself responsible for her siblings after their mother's death. Meg's determination and resilience are put to the test as she navigates the challenges of poverty, loss, and the harsh realities of Victorian England. With the help of unexpected friendships and her unwavering love for her family, Meg embarks on a remarkable adventure that will touch the hearts of readers young and old.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: Resilience, Family, Poverty, Friendship
