Synopsis: 
In 'To Mesopotamia and Kurdistan in disguise', Ely Banister Soane takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious lands of Mesopotamia and Kurdistan. Disguised as a Kurdish merchant, Soane provides historical insights into the Kurdish tribes and the Chaldeans of Kurdistan, shedding light on their rich cultural heritage and the challenges they face. As the narrative unfolds, readers are immersed in the vibrant landscapes, encounter fascinating characters, and witness the complexities of the region's history. With vivid descriptions and intriguing anecdotes, this book offers a unique perspective on the hidden treasures and untold stories of Mesopotamia and Kurdistan.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction, Cultural Exploration
Themes: Identity, Cultural Heritage, History, Adventure
