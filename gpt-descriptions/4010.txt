Synopsis:
Set in 16th century Scotland, "Marmion: A Tale Of Flodden Field" follows the story of Lord Marmion, a charismatic and ambitious nobleman, as he navigates the treacherous world of court politics and romantic entanglements. As tensions rise between England and Scotland, Marmion finds himself caught in a web of deceit, love, and betrayal. With vivid descriptions of battlefields, secret trysts, and political intrigue, this epic tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through a tumultuous period in Scottish history.

Genres:
Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes:
Power and ambition, Love and betrayal, Loyalty and honor
