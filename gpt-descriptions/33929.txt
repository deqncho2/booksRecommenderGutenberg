Synopsis: In 'Dixmude: The epic of the French marines (October 17-November 10, 1914)', Charles Le Goffic takes us on a gripping journey through the intense battles and heroic acts of the French marines during a critical period of World War I. As the marines face overwhelming odds and unimaginable challenges, their unwavering courage and determination become the driving force behind their fight for survival. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Le Goffic paints a vivid picture of the harrowing events that unfolded in Dixmude, capturing the reader's attention from start to finish.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Resilience
