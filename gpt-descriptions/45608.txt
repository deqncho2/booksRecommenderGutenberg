Synopsis: 
In "Mr. Dide, His Vacation in Colorado" by France, Lewis B., readers are taken on a thrilling journey as Mr. Dide embarks on a much-needed vacation in the picturesque landscapes of Colorado. However, what starts as a peaceful getaway quickly turns into a series of unexpected events that challenge Mr. Dide's perception of reality. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding him, he uncovers secrets that could change his life forever. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth alongside Mr. Dide.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Perception, Reality, Secrets
