Synopsis: 
In 'Charlie Codman's Cruise: A Story for Boys', young Charlie Codman embarks on a thrilling adventure aboard a ship, where he encounters mysterious characters, faces dangerous challenges, and discovers the true meaning of courage. As he navigates through treacherous waters and unknown territories, Charlie's journey becomes a test of his resilience and determination. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that lie ahead and prove himself worthy of the title of a true sailor?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical fiction

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Self-discovery
