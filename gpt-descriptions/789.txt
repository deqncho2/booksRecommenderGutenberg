Synopsis: In 'The Gathering of Brother Hilarius' by Michael Fairless, a young monk named Hilarius embarks on a journey that takes him through enchanting landscapes and introduces him to a cast of intriguing characters. As he navigates the challenges of his spiritual path, Hilarius discovers profound truths about love, faith, and the power of human connection. This captivating tale explores the transformative journey of a young man seeking enlightenment and the profound impact it has on his life.

Genres: Fiction, Spiritual, Coming-of-age

Themes: Spiritual awakening, Love and compassion, Self-discovery
