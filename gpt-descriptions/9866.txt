Synopsis: In 'Freeland: A Social Anticipation', Hertzka takes us on a captivating journey to a utopian society where the traditional concept of work is completely transformed. The story follows a young protagonist who stumbles upon this remarkable community and becomes enthralled by its innovative principles and practices. As he delves deeper into the workings of Freeland, he begins to question the very foundations of his own society and the possibilities for a better future. With its thought-provoking narrative and unexpected twists, 'Freeland' offers a compelling exploration of a world where work, economics, and social structures are reimagined.

Genres: Utopian fiction, Science fiction, Social commentary

Themes: Utopia, Work, Economics, Social reform
