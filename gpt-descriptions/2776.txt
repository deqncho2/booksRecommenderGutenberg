Synopsis: 
In 'The Four Million' by O. Henry, readers are transported to the bustling streets of New York City, where a collection of captivating and heartwarming stories unfold. From a young couple's unexpected encounter on a park bench to a clever con artist's scheme to outwit the wealthy elite, each tale is filled with wit, charm, and unexpected twists. As the lives of ordinary people intersect in extraordinary ways, O. Henry masterfully captures the essence of human nature and the beauty found in the most unlikely of places.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Humor

Themes: Human connection, Serendipity, Social class
