Synopsis: In "Love and Tea: A comedy-drama of colonial times in two acts" by Anna Phillips See, the story takes us back to a charming colonial setting where love, secrets, and unexpected encounters intertwine. As the characters navigate the complexities of societal expectations and personal desires, their lives become entangled in a delightful web of humor, romance, and intrigue. With each sip of tea, the plot thickens, and the audience is left eagerly anticipating the next surprising twist.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and relationships, societal expectations, secrets and revelations
