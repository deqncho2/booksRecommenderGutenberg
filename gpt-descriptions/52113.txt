Synopsis: 
In 'Fifteen Days: An Extract from Edward Colvil's Journal' by Mary Lowell Putnam, readers are transported to the captivating world of Edward Colvil, a young man who embarks on a life-changing journey. Through his journal entries, we witness his transformation as he navigates through a series of unexpected events and encounters. As the days unfold, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and Colvil's perception of the world is forever altered. With each passing day, the suspense builds, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth behind Colvil's extraordinary experiences.

Genres: 
Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Secrets and Revelations, Transformation
