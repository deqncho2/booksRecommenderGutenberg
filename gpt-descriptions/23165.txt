Synopsis: In "The Man Who Stole A Meeting-House: 1878, From "Coupon Bonds"," a small New England town is thrown into chaos when their beloved meeting-house mysteriously disappears overnight. As the baffled community searches for answers, suspicions arise and secrets are revealed, leading to a thrilling chase to uncover the truth behind the audacious theft. This gripping tale of intrigue and deception will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Crime and punishment, Community, Deception and secrets
