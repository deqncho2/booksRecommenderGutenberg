Synopsis: In 'Selling Point' by Arkawy, Norman, a struggling salesman named Jack discovers a mysterious product that promises to change his life forever. As he delves deeper into the world of this extraordinary item, Jack becomes entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected consequences. With each sale, he uncovers a dark truth that challenges his perception of reality and forces him to confront the true cost of success. As the line between ambition and morality blurs, Jack must navigate a treacherous path to find redemption and reclaim his humanity.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Identity, Redemption
