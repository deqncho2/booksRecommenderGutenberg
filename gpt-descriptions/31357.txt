Synopsis: In 'The Ultroom Error' by Jerry Sohl, a brilliant scientist creates a revolutionary virtual reality system that allows users to experience their deepest desires. However, when a glitch in the system causes unexpected consequences, the line between reality and fantasy becomes dangerously blurred. As the protagonist delves deeper into the mysterious error, they must confront their own desires and face the consequences of their actions. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping sci-fi thriller explores the dark side of human desires and the consequences of playing with reality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Virtual reality, Human desires, Consequences of actions
