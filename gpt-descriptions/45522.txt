Synopsis: In 'The Gospel of Slavery: A Primer of Freedom', Thomas, Abel C. challenges the prevailing notions of slavery and its justification, presenting a thought-provoking exploration of the institution. Through a series of compelling arguments and historical analysis, the author delves into the complex relationship between slavery and religion, shedding light on the moral and ethical implications of this deeply ingrained system. With a captivating narrative and insightful observations, Thomas, Abel C. invites readers to question their own beliefs and confront the uncomfortable truths surrounding the institution of slavery.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Social Commentary

Themes: Slavery, Religion, Morality, Ethics, Social Justice
