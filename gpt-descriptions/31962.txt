Synopsis: In 'The Ethical Way' by Joseph Farrell, a renowned philosopher and ethicist, a young prodigy named Ethan embarks on a journey that challenges his beliefs and forces him to question the very essence of morality. As he delves into the mysterious world of ethical dilemmas, he encounters a secret society that holds the key to unlocking the true nature of ethics. With each revelation, Ethan finds himself torn between his desire for knowledge and the consequences of his actions. Will he be able to navigate the treacherous path of ethics and find his own moral compass, or will he succumb to the allure of power and manipulation?

Genres: Philosophical fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Ethics, Morality, Power, Knowledge
