Synopsis: In "The Eyes Have It" by Randall Garrett, a man finds himself on a train, engrossed in a book, when he notices a beautiful woman sitting across from him. As he tries to catch her attention, he realizes that something peculiar is happening with the words he reads and the people around him. With a mix of mystery and suspense, this short story takes readers on a thrilling journey as the protagonist unravels the truth behind the strange occurrences.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Science Fiction

Themes: Perception, Reality vs. Illusion
