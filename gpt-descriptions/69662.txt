Synopsis: In 'Watching on the Rhine' by Violet R. Markham, a young woman named Elise embarks on a journey to Germany during the tumultuous years leading up to World War I. As she navigates through a society on the brink of change, Elise becomes entangled in a web of secrets, political intrigue, and forbidden love. With the fate of nations hanging in the balance, Elise must make difficult choices that will shape her future and the world around her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty and Betrayal, Political Ideologies
