Synopsis: In 'Parlous Times: A Novel of Modern Diplomacy', David Dwight Wells takes readers on a thrilling journey through the intricate world of international politics and espionage. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing global landscape, the story follows a seasoned diplomat who finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, power struggles, and high-stakes negotiations. As he navigates treacherous waters, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that could alter the course of history. With its gripping narrative and intricate plot twists, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Political thriller, Espionage, Suspense

Themes: Diplomacy, Power struggles, Betrayal, Moral dilemmas
