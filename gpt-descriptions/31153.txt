Synopsis: In the thrilling novel 'Telempathy' by Simonds Vance, a young woman named Lily discovers a hidden ability that allows her to connect with others on a profound level. As she delves deeper into her newfound power, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and manipulation. With her life on the line, Lily must navigate a treacherous world where trust is scarce and betrayal lurks around every corner. Will she be able to harness her telempathic abilities to uncover the truth and save herself?

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Psychic abilities, Trust and betrayal, Identity
