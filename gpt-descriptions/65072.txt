Synopsis: In 'The Time Armada' by Holden, Fox B., a brilliant scientist discovers a way to manipulate time, leading to the creation of a powerful armada that can travel through different eras. As the armada embarks on a thrilling journey through history, the crew must navigate treacherous waters, encounter unexpected allies and enemies, and face the consequences of altering the past. With each leap through time, the stakes rise higher, and the fate of the world hangs in the balance. This gripping adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the ethics of time travel and pondering the impact of our actions across the ages.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Adventure

Themes: Ethics of Time Travel, Consequences of Actions, Power and Responsibility
