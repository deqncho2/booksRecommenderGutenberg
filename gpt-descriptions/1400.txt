Synopsis: 
Great Expectations is a captivating novel that follows the life of Pip, an orphaned boy who dreams of becoming a gentleman. Set in 19th-century England, the story takes us on a journey through Pip's encounters with eccentric characters, unexpected twists of fate, and the pursuit of love and identity. As Pip navigates the complexities of society, he discovers the true nature of wealth, the power of forgiveness, and the transformative effects of personal growth. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, Great Expectations is a timeless tale of ambition, redemption, and the pursuit of one's true self.

Genres: 
Bildungsroman, Historical Fiction, Gothic Fiction

Themes: 
Social class, Ambition, Identity, Love and Relationships, Redemption
