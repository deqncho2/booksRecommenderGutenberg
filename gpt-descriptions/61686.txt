Synopsis: In 'So Big' by Edna Ferber, we follow the life of Selina Peake DeJong, a strong-willed and determined woman who navigates the challenges of love, loss, and societal expectations. From her humble beginnings as a schoolteacher in a small farming community to her transformation into a successful and unconventional artist, Selina's journey is one of self-discovery and resilience. As she faces the complexities of motherhood and the pursuit of her artistic passion, Selina must confront the choices she has made and find her own definition of success.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Identity, Gender roles, Motherhood, Artistic expression
