Synopsis: In 'The Firefly of France' by Marion Polk Angellotti, a young American woman finds herself caught in the midst of World War I as she embarks on a dangerous mission in war-torn France. With her bravery and determination, she becomes an unexpected hero, facing unimaginable challenges and discovering the true meaning of sacrifice. As she navigates through the chaos of war, she must confront her own fears and find strength in the most unlikely places.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Resilience, Identity
