Synopsis: In 'Five Mice in a Mouse-trap, by the Man in the Moon,' Laura Elizabeth Howe Richards takes readers on a whimsical adventure as five mischievous mice find themselves in an unexpected predicament. With the help of the Man in the Moon, they embark on a journey filled with surprises, danger, and the possibility of finding their way back home. This enchanting tale will captivate readers of all ages, leaving them eager to discover the fate of these courageous little mice.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Imagination
