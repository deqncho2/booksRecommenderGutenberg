Synopsis: 
In 'A-B-C of Vegetable Gardening', Eben E. Rexford takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of vegetable gardening, offering a comprehensive guide filled with practical tips, expert advice, and insightful anecdotes. From selecting the perfect plot of land to nurturing and harvesting a bountiful crop, Rexford's engaging narrative and easy-to-follow instructions make this book a must-read for both novice and experienced gardeners alike. With a focus on sustainable practices and the joy of cultivating one's own food, 'A-B-C of Vegetable Gardening' is a delightful and informative exploration of the wonders and rewards of tending to a vegetable garden.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gardening, How-to

Themes: Sustainability, Self-sufficiency, Joy of gardening
