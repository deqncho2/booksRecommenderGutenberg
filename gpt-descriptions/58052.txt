Synopsis: In "The Self-Plumed Bishop Unplumed," Thomas Latham delivers a thought-provoking response to the profound erudition of Hugh Latimer, challenging his doctrine of endless punishment. Through a captivating exchange of ideas, Latham unveils a compelling argument that will leave readers questioning their beliefs and seeking a deeper understanding of the nature of punishment and redemption.

Genres: Theology, Religious Criticism, Philosophy

Themes: Punishment, Redemption, Religious Doctrine, Intellectual Debate
