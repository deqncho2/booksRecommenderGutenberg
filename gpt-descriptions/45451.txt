Synopsis: In "The Man Who Did the Right Thing: A Romance" by Harry Johnston, we are introduced to a man named Edward who finds himself at a crossroads in life. Torn between societal expectations and his own desires, Edward embarks on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. As he navigates through love, loss, and the complexities of human relationships, Edward must confront the consequences of his choices and ultimately decide what it truly means to do the right thing.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Morality, Self-discovery, Love and Relationships
