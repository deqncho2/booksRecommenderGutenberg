Synopsis: 
'Philippine Folk Tales' is a captivating collection of traditional stories that transport readers into the enchanting world of Philippine folklore. From mythical creatures and magical encounters to tales of love, bravery, and the power of nature, this book offers a rich tapestry of narratives that have been passed down through generations. Each story is filled with vivid imagery and explores the unique cultural heritage of the Philippines, captivating readers with its timeless charm and universal themes.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural

Themes: Mythology, Magic, Love, Bravery, Nature
