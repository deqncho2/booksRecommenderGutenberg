Synopsis: In 'The Problems of Psychical Research: Experiments and Theories in the Realm of the Supernormal', Hereward Carrington delves into the fascinating world of psychical research, exploring the mysteries of the supernatural and the unexplained. Through a series of captivating experiments and thought-provoking theories, Carrington takes readers on a journey that challenges conventional beliefs and invites them to question the boundaries of human perception. From telepathy and clairvoyance to mediumship and psychokinesis, this book offers a captivating exploration of the supernatural realm, leaving readers intrigued and eager to uncover the secrets that lie beyond our everyday reality.

Genres: Non-fiction, Paranormal, Occult

Themes: Supernatural phenomena, Human perception, Scientific exploration
