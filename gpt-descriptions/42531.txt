Synopsis: In 'The Honey-Pot' by Barcynska, Countess (Hélène), a mysterious woman named Eliza finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deceit when she is unexpectedly drawn into the world of espionage. As she navigates through a dangerous game of espionage and betrayal, Eliza must uncover the truth and protect her own life before it's too late. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
