Synopsis:
In this captivating adaptation of Shakespeare's play, 'The Merchant of Venice', Charles Kean brings the story to life on the stage of the Princess's Theatre. With his historical and explanatory notes, Kean provides a deeper understanding of the play's intricate plot and complex characters. Set in the vibrant city of Venice, the story follows the merchant Antonio, who finds himself entangled in a high-stakes loan agreement with the cunning Jewish moneylender, Shylock. As the play unfolds, themes of love, friendship, justice, and the consequences of prejudice are explored, leading to a dramatic climax that will keep the audience on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Tragedy

Themes: Love, Friendship, Justice, Prejudice
