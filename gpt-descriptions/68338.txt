Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 160, October 2, 1915: The Yellow Label; or, Nick Carter and the Society Looters,' renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when a series of high-profile thefts plague the upper echelons of society. As he delves deeper into the case, Carter uncovers a secret society of cunning criminals who operate under the guise of sophistication and elegance. With his keen intellect and relentless determination, he must navigate the treacherous world of the elite to bring the culprits to justice and restore order to the upper crust of society.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Crime and punishment, Social class and privilege, Deception and disguise
