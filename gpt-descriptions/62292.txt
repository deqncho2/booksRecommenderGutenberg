Synopsis: In "The Dogs and the Fleas: By One of the Dogs" by Scrimshaw, Frederic, readers are taken on a thrilling and humorous adventure through the eyes of a clever and mischievous dog. As the dog navigates the world of humans and their peculiar habits, he encounters a group of fleas who possess an unexpected secret. With a mix of wit, charm, and unexpected twists, this story will keep readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the mysterious fleas.

Genres: Adventure, Comedy, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Perception
