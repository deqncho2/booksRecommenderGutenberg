Synopsis: In 'Mad: A Story of Dust and Ashes' by George Manville Fenn, a young man named Jack embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious fire that destroyed his family's home. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the past, Jack finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and madness. With each revelation, he becomes more determined to seek justice and redemption, even if it means confronting his own demons. Set against the backdrop of a desolate landscape, this gripping tale explores the dark side of human nature and the lengths one will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Revenge, Betrayal, Redemption, Mental Illness
