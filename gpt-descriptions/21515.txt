Synopsis:
In the heart of 17th century Venice, a city of opulence and corruption, a group of conspirators forms an unlikely alliance to fight against the tyrannical rule of the Senate. Among them is Jaffier, a nobleman torn between his loyalty to his friend Pierre and his love for his wife, Belvidera. As their plot unfolds, secrets are revealed, loyalties are tested, and the consequences of their actions become increasingly dire. 'Venice Preserved: A Tragedy in Five Acts' takes readers on a thrilling journey through political intrigue, forbidden love, and the devastating consequences of power.

Genres:
Tragedy, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal, Love and sacrifice
