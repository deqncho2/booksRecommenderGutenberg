Synopsis: 
In 'Peggy Raymond's Vacation; Or, Friendly Terrace Transplanted', Peggy and her friends embark on an exciting summer adventure that takes them far from their familiar neighborhood. As they explore new surroundings, they encounter mysterious happenings and unexpected friendships. Peggy's vacation becomes a thrilling journey filled with secrets, surprises, and a touch of danger. Will they be able to unravel the mysteries that surround them and make it a summer to remember?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Independence
