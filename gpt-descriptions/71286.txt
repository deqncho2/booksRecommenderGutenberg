Synopsis: In 'Oliver Ellis: $b or, The Fusiliers' by Grant, James, we follow the captivating journey of Oliver Ellis, a young man who finds himself torn between duty and desire. Set against the backdrop of the tumultuous Napoleonic era, Oliver is faced with a series of choices that will test his loyalty, courage, and love. As he navigates through the treacherous world of war, politics, and romance, Oliver must confront his own inner demons and make decisions that will shape his destiny.
