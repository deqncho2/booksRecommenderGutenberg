Synopsis: In 'The White Cat: Captains All, Book 10,' the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the criminal underworld of London. The story follows a group of cunning thieves who are planning their biggest heist yet, targeting a wealthy aristocrat's mansion. However, their plans take an unexpected turn when they encounter a mysterious white cat that seems to possess supernatural powers. As the cat's influence grows, the thieves find themselves caught in a web of danger and intrigue, unsure of who they can trust. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Supernatural elements
