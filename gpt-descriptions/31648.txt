Synopsis: In 'My Fair Planet' by Smith, Evelyn E., a young woman named Lily finds herself transported to a parallel universe where the world is a utopia. As she navigates this new reality, she discovers that everything she thought she knew about her own world is challenged, and she must confront the consequences of her actions. With the help of unexpected allies, Lily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and redemption, all while trying to find her way back home.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Parallel universes, Utopia vs. Dystopia, Self-discovery, Redemption
