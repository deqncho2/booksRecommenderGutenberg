Synopsis: 
In 'The Victor' by Walton Bryce, a young prodigy named Victor finds himself caught in a web of secrets and deception when he is unexpectedly chosen to participate in a prestigious competition. As he delves deeper into the competition, Victor uncovers a hidden world filled with danger, betrayal, and unexpected allies. With his unique abilities and determination, Victor must navigate the treacherous path ahead, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the competition and his own mysterious past.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Identity, Power, Betrayal, Redemption
