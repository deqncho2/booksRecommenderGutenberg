Synopsis: In 'Grace Harlowe's Overland Riders in the Great North Woods', Grace and her friends embark on an exciting adventure through the rugged wilderness of the Great North Woods. As they navigate treacherous trails, encounter mysterious characters, and face unexpected challenges, the Overland Riders must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. With danger lurking at every turn, will they be able to unravel the secrets of the Great North Woods and emerge victorious?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
