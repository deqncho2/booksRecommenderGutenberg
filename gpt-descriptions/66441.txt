Synopsis: In 'Reputation' by W. C. Tuttle, a small town is shaken when a mysterious stranger arrives, stirring up secrets and hidden agendas. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the townspeople must confront their own dark pasts and make difficult choices that will forever alter their lives. With a gripping narrative and unexpected twists, 'Reputation' explores the power of secrets and the lengths people will go to protect their reputations.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Small-town dynamics
