Synopsis: In 'Bert Wilson on the Gridiron' by Duffield, J. W., follow the thrilling journey of Bert Wilson as he navigates the intense world of American football. As Bert faces challenges both on and off the field, he must summon his determination and skill to overcome adversity and prove himself as a formidable player. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding moments, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover Bert's fate.

Genres: Sports, Young Adult, Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Teamwork, Self-discovery
