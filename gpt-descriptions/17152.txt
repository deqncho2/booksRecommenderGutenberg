Synopsis: 
In 'Rosa's Quest: Or, The Way to the Beautiful Land' by Anna Potter Wright, readers are taken on a captivating journey alongside the courageous and determined Rosa. Faced with a mysterious prophecy and a daunting quest, Rosa embarks on an adventure that will test her strength, resilience, and belief in the power of love. As she encounters magical creatures, encounters unexpected allies, and faces formidable challenges, Rosa's determination to find the Beautiful Land becomes an inspiring tale of self-discovery and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Destiny, Love, Self-discovery
