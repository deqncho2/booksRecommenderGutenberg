Synopsis: In 'Perkins of Portland: Perkins The Great' by Butler, Ellis Parker, follow the extraordinary adventures of Perkins, a seemingly ordinary man with an extraordinary talent for turning the mundane into the extraordinary. From his humble beginnings in Portland, Perkins embarks on a series of hilarious and heartwarming escapades that will leave readers both in stitches and in awe. With his unique perspective on life and his uncanny ability to find joy in the simplest of things, Perkins proves that greatness can be found in the most unexpected places.

Genres: Humor, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Finding joy in everyday life, Embracing the extraordinary in the ordinary
