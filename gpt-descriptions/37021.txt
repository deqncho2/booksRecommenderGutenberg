Synopsis: 
In 'Four Afloat: Being the Adventures of the Big Four on the Water', four adventurous friends embark on a thrilling journey that takes them from the calm waters of their hometown to the treacherous seas of the Atlantic Ocean. As they navigate through storms, encounter mysterious islands, and face unexpected challenges, their bond is tested and their courage is pushed to the limit. Will they be able to overcome the perils that lie ahead and return home safely?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Action

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
