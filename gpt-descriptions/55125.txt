Synopsis: In 'Madam: A Novel', we are introduced to the enigmatic character of Madam, a woman of mystery and intrigue. Set in the bustling city of London, the story follows Madam's unconventional journey as she navigates through the complexities of society, challenging societal norms and expectations. As her true identity remains shrouded in secrecy, Madam's captivating presence draws people from all walks of life into her orbit, leaving them questioning their own beliefs and desires. With a cast of fascinating characters and a plot filled with unexpected twists and turns, 'Madam' is a compelling exploration of identity, power, and the blurred lines between reality and illusion.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Identity, Society, Power, Illusion
