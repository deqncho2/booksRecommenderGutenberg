Synopsis: 
In 'Sight Unseen' by Mary Roberts Rinehart, a renowned artist finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she witnesses a shocking crime while visiting a small coastal town. As she becomes the prime suspect, she must rely on her wit and resourcefulness to uncover the truth and clear her name. With unexpected twists and a captivating cast of characters, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Crime

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Identity, Trust
