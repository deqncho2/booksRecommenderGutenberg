Synopsis: In 'Houlihan's Equation' by Sheldon, Walter J., a brilliant mathematician named Dr. Benjamin Houlihan stumbles upon an enigmatic equation that has the potential to change the world as we know it. As he delves deeper into its complexities, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected consequences. With time running out, Houlihan must navigate a treacherous path filled with moral dilemmas and personal sacrifices, all while racing against a shadowy organization determined to possess the equation's power. This thrilling tale of mathematical discovery and its far-reaching implications will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Mathematical exploration, Ethical dilemmas, Pursuit of knowledge
