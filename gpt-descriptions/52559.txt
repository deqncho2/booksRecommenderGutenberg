Synopsis: 
In 'The Ballad of Ensign Joy' by E. W. Hornung, a young naval officer finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he becomes the target of a sinister plot. As Ensign Joy navigates through treacherous waters, he must uncover the truth behind a series of perplexing events and confront the dark forces that threaten his very existence. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Intrigue, Betrayal, Survival
