Synopsis: In 'Design for Doomsday' by Walton Bryce, a brilliant architect is tasked with designing a secret underground bunker to protect humanity from an impending global catastrophe. As he delves deeper into the project, he uncovers a web of conspiracy and must navigate a dangerous world of hidden agendas and personal sacrifices. With time running out, he must confront his own moral dilemmas and make choices that will determine the fate of humanity.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Ethics, Conspiracy
