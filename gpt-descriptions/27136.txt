Synopsis: 
In "Six Days on the Hurricane Deck of a Mule," Almira Stillwell Cole takes us on a captivating journey through the rugged landscapes of Honduras in 1891. With vivid descriptions and a keen eye for detail, Cole recounts her daring adventure on mule back, braving treacherous terrains and encountering fascinating characters along the way. From the bustling city of Tegucigalpa to the remote villages nestled in the mountains, this gripping account offers a unique glimpse into the untamed beauty and challenges of Central America during a time of great exploration.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Resilience, Cultural immersion
