Synopsis: In 'Christmas: A Story' by Gale, Zona, a young woman named Zona finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue during the holiday season. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding her family's Christmas traditions, she uncovers a hidden world filled with unexpected twists and turns. With each revelation, Zona's perception of Christmas is forever altered, leading her on a transformative journey of self-discovery and the true meaning of the holiday season.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Redemption
