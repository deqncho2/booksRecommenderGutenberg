Synopsis: In "The Trail of The Badger: A Story of the Colorado Border Thirty Years Ago," author Sidford F. Hamp takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rugged landscapes of Colorado, where danger lurks at every turn. Set thirty years ago, this gripping tale follows a group of individuals who find themselves entangled in a web of mystery, betrayal, and revenge. As they navigate treacherous trails and face formidable adversaries, they must uncover the truth behind a series of puzzling events that threaten to tear their lives apart. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, "The Trail of The Badger" will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Justice and vengeance, Survival in the wilderness, Loyalty and betrayal
