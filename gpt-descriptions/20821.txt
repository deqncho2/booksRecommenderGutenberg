Synopsis:
In "Betty Wales, Senior," the final installment of the Betty Wales series, our beloved protagonist finds herself navigating the challenges and triumphs of her senior year at Harding College. As Betty prepares to graduate, she must confront personal and academic obstacles, while also discovering the true meaning of friendship and loyalty. With her characteristic wit and determination, Betty faces unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages until the very end.

Genres:
Coming-of-age, Fiction, Young Adult

Themes:
Friendship, Growth, Identity, Perseverance
