Synopsis: 
'Selections from Aunt Sammy's Radio Recipes and USDA Favorites' is a delightful collection of recipes and culinary advice that were once broadcasted on the popular radio show, Aunt Sammy's Kitchen. This compilation offers a nostalgic glimpse into the past, featuring a wide range of delectable dishes and helpful tips for homemakers. From hearty main courses to mouthwatering desserts, these recipes are sure to inspire both seasoned cooks and kitchen novices alike. With its charming anecdotes and timeless recipes, this book is a treasure trove for anyone interested in exploring the culinary traditions of yesteryears.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink, History

Themes: Culinary traditions, Homemaking, Nostalgia
