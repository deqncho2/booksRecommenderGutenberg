Synopsis: In the harsh and unforgiving wilderness of the Klondike, a domesticated dog named Buck is abruptly thrust into a world of primal instincts and survival. Sold as a sled dog, Buck must adapt to the brutal conditions and learn to navigate the treacherous landscapes of the Yukon. As he faces the challenges of the wild, Buck's true nature emerges, and he embarks on a remarkable journey of self-discovery and transformation.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Animal Fiction

Themes: Survival, Nature vs. Nurture, Instinct, Freedom
