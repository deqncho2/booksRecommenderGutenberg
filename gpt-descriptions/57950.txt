Synopsis: In the secluded village of Wastwater, a mysterious figure known as the Grey Monk haunts the locals with his enigmatic presence. As the villagers become increasingly intrigued by his origins and purpose, a young artist named Walter Brune finds himself drawn into the Monk's world, uncovering a web of secrets and dark pasts that threaten to unravel the fabric of their community. With each encounter, Walter's perception of reality becomes blurred, leading him on a journey of self-discovery and the exploration of the human psyche. As the lines between truth and illusion blur, the Grey Monk's influence becomes both captivating and dangerous, leaving Walter questioning his own sanity and the nature of the world around him.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Secrets, Madness
