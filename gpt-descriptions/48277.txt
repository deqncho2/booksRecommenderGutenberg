Synopsis: In the small town of Oakdale, a mysterious newcomer named Ben Stone arrives, stirring up curiosity and suspicion among the locals. As Ben settles into his new life, he uncovers a web of secrets and dark pasts that threaten to unravel the town's peaceful facade. With each revelation, Ben becomes entangled in a dangerous game of trust and betrayal, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. As tensions rise and the truth is revealed, Ben must navigate the treacherous landscape of Oakdale to protect himself and those he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Trust, Small-town dynamics
