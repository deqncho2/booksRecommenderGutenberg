Synopsis: In 'A Romance of Two Worlds: A Novel' by Corelli, Marie, we are introduced to a young woman named Theos Alwyn, who possesses a unique ability to communicate with the spiritual realm. As she navigates the complexities of Victorian society, Theos finds herself torn between her spiritual calling and the conventional expectations of her time. Along her journey, she encounters a mysterious and enigmatic man named Heliobas, who becomes both her mentor and her love interest. Together, they embark on a captivating exploration of the supernatural, delving into the realms of mysticism, reincarnation, and the power of love. This enthralling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they follow Theos' quest for truth and self-discovery.

Genres: Supernatural fiction, Romance, Victorian literature

Themes: Spirituality, Love, Society's expectations, Reincarnation
