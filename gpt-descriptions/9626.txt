Synopsis: In 'Buried Cities, Volume 2: Olympia' by Jennie Hall, readers are transported to the ancient city of Olympia, where secrets lie hidden beneath its ruins. As archaeologist Emma embarks on a thrilling expedition, she uncovers a mysterious artifact that holds the key to a forgotten civilization. With danger lurking at every turn, Emma must navigate treacherous paths and decipher cryptic clues to unravel the truth about Olympia's past. Will she be able to unlock the city's secrets before they are lost forever?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Archaeology, Ancient Civilizations, Exploration, Secrets
