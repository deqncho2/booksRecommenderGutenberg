Synopsis: 

In 'Deserts: Geology and Resources', Alta Sharon Walker takes readers on an awe-inspiring journey through the mysterious and captivating world of deserts. From the scorching sands of the Sahara to the icy expanses of Antarctica, Walker delves into the geological wonders that shape these seemingly barren landscapes. With vivid descriptions and stunning photographs, she uncovers the hidden treasures and resources that lie beneath the surface, revealing a world teeming with life and untapped potential. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Geology, Natural History

Themes: Geology, Environmental Science, Exploration, Resource Management
