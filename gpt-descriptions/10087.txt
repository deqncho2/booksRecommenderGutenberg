Synopsis: In 'Old Lady Number 31' by Louise Forsslund, a seemingly ordinary old woman living in a small town becomes the center of attention when a mysterious event occurs. As the townspeople try to unravel the truth behind her enigmatic past, they uncover a web of secrets and unexpected connections that will leave them questioning everything they thought they knew. With each revelation, the story takes unexpected twists and turns, keeping the reader on the edge of their seat until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Small-town dynamics
