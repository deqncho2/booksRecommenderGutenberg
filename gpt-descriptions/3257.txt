Synopsis: In 'New Thought Pastels' by Ella Wheeler Wilcox, embark on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking and uplifting poems. From exploring the depths of human emotions to contemplating the mysteries of life, Wilcox's words paint vivid pictures that will leave readers inspired and introspective. With each turn of the page, discover a new perspective on love, happiness, and the power of positive thinking. 

Genres: Poetry, Self-help, Inspirational

Themes: Love, Happiness, Positive thinking
