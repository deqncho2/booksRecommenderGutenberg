Synopsis: In "Tom Strong, Lincoln's Scout: A Story of the United States in the Times That Tried Men's Souls" by Mason, Alfred Bishop, readers are transported to a tumultuous period in American history. Set during the American Civil War, the story follows Tom Strong, a skilled scout and loyal supporter of President Abraham Lincoln. As Strong embarks on dangerous missions behind enemy lines, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, face formidable adversaries, and confront his own inner demons. With vivid descriptions and gripping action, this historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through a divided nation, where bravery and sacrifice become the defining qualities of a true hero.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Bravery, Sacrifice, Divided Nation
