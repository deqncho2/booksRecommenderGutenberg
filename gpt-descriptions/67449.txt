Synopsis: 

In 'De Camp Genealogy: Laurent De Camp of New Utrecht, N.Y., 1664, and his descendants', George Austin Morrison delves into the fascinating history of the De Camp family, tracing their lineage from the arrival of Laurent De Camp in New Utrecht, N.Y. in 1664. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Morrison uncovers the triumphs, trials, and secrets that have shaped this remarkable family over the centuries. From their involvement in the American Revolution to their contributions in various fields, the De Camps have left an indelible mark on American history. This genealogical journey is filled with surprising discoveries, heartwarming anecdotes, and a deep exploration of the family's enduring legacy.

Genres: Genealogy, History, Biography

Themes: Family history, Legacy, American history
