Synopsis: In Volume 5 of "Clarissa Harlowe; or the history of a young lady," the captivating story of Clarissa Harlowe continues to unfold as she faces new challenges and heart-wrenching decisions. As her relationships with various characters become increasingly complex, Clarissa finds herself torn between duty and desire, loyalty and self-preservation. With unexpected twists and turns, this volume delves deeper into the emotional turmoil and moral dilemmas that Clarissa must navigate, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what fate has in store for her.

Genres: Epistolary novel, Psychological fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and passion, Morality and virtue, Gender roles and societal expectations
