Synopsis: In 'A Commentary' by John Galsworthy, a renowned author finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and deception when he becomes the target of a mysterious stalker. As he delves deeper into the twisted mind of his pursuer, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel his own sanity. With each turn of the page, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the truth behind the relentless pursuit. 

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Obsession, Identity, Psychological manipulation
