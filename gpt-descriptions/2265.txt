Synopsis: 
In the wake of his father's sudden death, Prince Hamlet returns to Denmark to find his world turned upside down. Haunted by his father's ghost and consumed by thoughts of revenge, Hamlet embarks on a journey of self-discovery and moral dilemma. As he navigates the treacherous waters of power, betrayal, and deceit, Hamlet must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape the destiny of an entire kingdom.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Revenge, Madness, Moral Ambiguity, Existentialism, Corruption
