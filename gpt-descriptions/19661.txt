Synopsis: 
In 'Tell Me Another Story: The Book of Story Programs', Carolyn Sherwin Bailey presents a delightful collection of story programs that will captivate both children and adults alike. From imaginative tales of talking animals to enchanting stories of faraway lands, this book offers a treasure trove of storytelling ideas that will bring joy and wonder to any audience. With detailed instructions on how to create and present each program, Bailey provides a valuable resource for educators, parents, and anyone who loves the magic of storytelling. Get ready to embark on a journey of imagination and discovery with 'Tell Me Another Story'.

Genres: Children's Literature, Education, Storytelling

Themes: Imagination, Creativity, Education, Entertainment
