Synopsis: In 'The Girls From Earth' by Robinson, Frank M., a group of young women from Earth find themselves on a thrilling intergalactic adventure when they are unexpectedly transported to a distant planet. As they navigate this strange new world, they must rely on their intelligence, courage, and resourcefulness to survive and find a way back home. Along the way, they encounter fascinating alien species, uncover hidden secrets, and form unexpected alliances. With each challenge they face, the girls discover their own inner strength and resilience, proving that they are more than capable of conquering the unknown.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Female Empowerment
