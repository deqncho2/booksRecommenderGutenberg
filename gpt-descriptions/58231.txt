Synopsis: 
In "Battles & Bivouacs: A French soldier's note-book" by Jacques Roujon, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the eyes of a French soldier during World War I. Through vivid and poignant descriptions, the author recounts the harrowing experiences, camaraderie, and sacrifices made on the front lines. From the trenches to the battlefields, from moments of fear and despair to acts of bravery and resilience, this soldier's note-book offers a unique and intimate perspective on the realities of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, War

Themes: War and its impact on individuals and society, camaraderie and brotherhood in times of adversity, the human spirit and resilience in the face of hardship.
