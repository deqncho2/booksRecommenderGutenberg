Synopsis: 
"The Bloody Theatre, or Martyrs Mirror of the Defenseless Christians" takes readers on a gripping journey through the history of Christianity, from the time of Christ to the year 1660. This monumental work by Thieleman J. van Braght chronicles the stories of those who were baptized upon confession of faith and who, in their unwavering devotion to Jesus, suffered and died as martyrs. With vivid and heart-wrenching accounts, the book unveils the untold sacrifices made by these brave individuals, shedding light on their unwavering faith and the persecution they endured.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Religious literature, Biography

Themes: Faith and devotion, Persecution and martyrdom, Religious history
