Synopsis: In 'The Spell of Belgium' by Isabel Anderson, a young American woman finds herself captivated by the enchanting country of Belgium during her travels. As she immerses herself in the rich history, vibrant culture, and picturesque landscapes, she becomes entangled in a web of unexpected friendships, romantic encounters, and mysterious secrets. From the bustling streets of Brussels to the tranquil countryside, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure, where the spellbinding allure of Belgium leaves an indelible mark on her heart.

Genres: Travelogue, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Cultural exploration, Identity, Love and relationships
