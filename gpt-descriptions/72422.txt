Synopsis: In "For Service Rendered" by Jesse F. Bone, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she takes a job as a secretary for a wealthy family. As she delves deeper into the family's affairs, she uncovers a dark and dangerous world that threatens to consume her. With her own life at stake, Evelyn must navigate a treacherous path of loyalty, betrayal, and unexpected alliances to survive.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Loyalty, Survival
