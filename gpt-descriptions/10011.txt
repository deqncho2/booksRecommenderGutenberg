Synopsis: 

'365 Foreign Dishes: A Foreign Dish for Every Day in the Year' takes readers on a culinary journey around the world, offering a unique and delicious dish for each day of the year. From mouthwatering appetizers to tantalizing main courses and delectable desserts, this book presents a diverse range of recipes that will awaken your taste buds and expand your culinary horizons. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, this book provides an opportunity to explore the flavors and traditions of different cultures, bringing the world's cuisines right to your dining table.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink, Travel

Themes: Culinary exploration, Cultural diversity, Global cuisine
