Synopsis: 

'Eikon Basilike: The Pourtracture of His Sacred Majestie, in His Solitudes and Sufferings' is a captivating and introspective work written by Charles I, King of England, and attributed to John Gauden. This book offers a unique glimpse into the personal thoughts, emotions, and experiences of the king during his times of solitude and suffering. Through a series of heartfelt reflections, the reader is invited to delve into the inner world of a monarch facing immense challenges and turmoil. 

Genres: Autobiography, Historical Fiction, Political Literature

Themes: Solitude, Suffering, Resilience, Leadership, Monarchy
