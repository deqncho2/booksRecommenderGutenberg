Synopsis: In 'The Story of Rolf and the Viking's Bow' by Allen French, readers are transported to the Viking Age, where they follow the thrilling adventures of Rolf, a young Viking boy. When Rolf's father is unjustly accused of murder, Rolf sets out on a dangerous quest to clear his father's name and retrieve a legendary Viking bow. Along the way, he encounters treacherous enemies, forms unexpected alliances, and discovers the true meaning of courage and honor. This captivating tale of bravery, loyalty, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Justice, Friendship, Coming of Age
