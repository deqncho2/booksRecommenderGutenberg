Synopsis: 
"The Works of Horace" is a collection of poetry by the renowned Roman poet Horace. This compilation showcases a diverse range of poetic styles and themes, including odes, satires, and epistles. Through his witty and insightful verses, Horace explores various aspects of human nature, society, and the pursuit of a meaningful life. From humorous observations on everyday life to profound reflections on love, friendship, and the passage of time, this collection offers a captivating glimpse into the mind of one of ancient Rome's most celebrated poets.

Genres: Poetry, Satire, Epistle

Themes: Human nature, Society, Love, Friendship, Time
