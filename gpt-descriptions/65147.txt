Synopsis: 
'The English Secretary; or, Methode of Writing Epistles and Letters' is a captivating guidebook written by Angel Day in 1599. This work offers readers a comprehensive and engaging exploration of the art of letter writing, providing valuable insights into the techniques, tropes, figures, and schemes that are essential for crafting eloquent and persuasive correspondence. With practical examples and clear instructions, Day's book serves as a valuable resource for anyone seeking to master the art of written communication.

Genres: 
Educational, Reference, Language

Themes: 
Art of letter writing, Rhetoric, Communication
