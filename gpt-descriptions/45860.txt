Synopsis: In 'The 116th Battalion in France' by Allen, E. P. S., a group of young soldiers from diverse backgrounds find themselves thrust into the chaos and horrors of World War I. As they navigate the trenches and battlefields of France, they form unbreakable bonds of camaraderie and face unimaginable challenges that test their courage and resilience. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, this novel takes readers on an emotional journey through the harrowing experiences of these brave soldiers.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Survival, Loss, Identity
