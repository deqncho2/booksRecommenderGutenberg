Synopsis: In "College prolongs infancy," Kallen, Horace Meyer delves into the intriguing notion that the college experience may inadvertently hinder the transition into adulthood. Through thought-provoking analysis and captivating anecdotes, the author challenges conventional wisdom and explores the impact of higher education on personal growth and maturity. This eye-opening exploration will leave readers questioning the true purpose and effects of the college years.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, Sociology

Themes: Adulthood, Personal Growth, Higher Education, Societal Expectations
