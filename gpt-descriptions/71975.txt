Synopsis: In 'A mediaeval burglary', Thomas Frederick Tout takes us on a captivating journey into the world of a daring burglary that took place during the medieval era. Set in the John Rylands Library, this lecture explores the audacious heist that unfolded on a fateful night in January 1915. With meticulous detail and historical context, Tout unravels the mystery behind this extraordinary event, leaving readers intrigued and eager to uncover the secrets hidden within the library's walls.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Crime, Mystery

Themes: Medieval history, Theft, Cultural preservation
