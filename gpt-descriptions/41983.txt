Synopsis: In 'The Book of Curiosities' by John Platts, embark on a thrilling journey through a hidden world of ancient artifacts, mysterious maps, and enigmatic riddles. When a young archaeologist stumbles upon a forgotten manuscript, he becomes entangled in a quest to uncover the secrets of a long-lost civilization. As he deciphers cryptic clues and navigates treacherous landscapes, he must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape the course of history. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of intrigue, where nothing is as it seems and every discovery brings new dangers and revelations. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Legacy, Identity
