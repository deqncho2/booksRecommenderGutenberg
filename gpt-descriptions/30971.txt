Synopsis: In 'Industrial Revolution' by Anderson, Poul, a brilliant inventor finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger as he unveils a groundbreaking technology that could change the world. As he navigates through a world of corporate espionage, political power plays, and personal vendettas, he must confront the consequences of his creation and make choices that will shape the future. With unexpected twists and a race against time, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Technological advancement, Power struggles, Morality
