Synopsis: In 'Baseball Joe of the Silver Stars; or, The Rivals of Riverside' by Chadwick, Lester, follow the thrilling journey of Joe Matson, a talented young baseball player, as he faces off against the formidable team from Riverside. As Joe and his teammates strive to outshine their rivals, they encounter unexpected challenges and must rely on their skills and determination to secure victory. With intense baseball action, gripping rivalries, and a dash of romance, this story will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Sports fiction, Young adult fiction, Adventure

Themes: Perseverance, Teamwork, Rivalry, Coming of age
