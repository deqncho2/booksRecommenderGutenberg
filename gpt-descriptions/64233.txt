Synopsis: 
'The postage stamps, envelopes, wrappers and post cards of the North American colonies of Great Britain' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of colonial postal history. This meticulously researched and beautifully illustrated book delves into the intricate details of the stamps, envelopes, wrappers, and postcards that were used in the North American colonies during the time they were under British rule. From the earliest postal systems to the evolution of postal services, this book provides a comprehensive exploration of the historical significance and artistic beauty of these postal artifacts. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Art

Themes: Colonial history, Postal systems, Artistic design
