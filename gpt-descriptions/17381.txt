Synopsis: In "What Timmy Did" by Marie Belloc Lowndes, a seemingly ordinary boy named Timmy finds himself caught up in a series of unexpected events that will challenge his innocence and force him to make difficult choices. As Timmy's actions have far-reaching consequences, the story takes a suspenseful turn, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what Timmy did and how it will all unfold.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Coming-of-age

Themes: Innocence, Morality, Consequences
