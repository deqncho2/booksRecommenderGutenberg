Synopsis: In 'The Shakespeare Garden' by Esther Singleton, a young woman named Alice discovers a hidden garden filled with enchanting flowers and plants inspired by the works of William Shakespeare. As she explores this magical space, Alice uncovers secrets and mysteries that intertwine with the Bard's timeless tales. With each step, she becomes more entangled in a world where reality and fiction blur, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected love.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Magic and enchantment, Literature and its influence, Self-discovery
