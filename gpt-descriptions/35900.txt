Synopsis: In 'The River-Names of Europe' by Robert Ferguson, embark on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Europe's river names. From the majestic Danube to the mysterious Volga, Ferguson delves into the origins, myths, and historical significance behind these enchanting names. As he uncovers the hidden stories and cultural connections woven into the fabric of Europe's rivers, the reader is transported to a world where geography and language intertwine, revealing a fascinating glimpse into the continent's past.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Linguistics

Themes: Cultural heritage, Language and identity, Historical geography
