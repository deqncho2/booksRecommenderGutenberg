Synopsis: 
In 'Harper's Round Table, August 18, 1896', a group of young adventurers embarks on a thrilling journey that takes them to uncharted territories, where they encounter mysterious creatures and face unexpected challenges. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes, their courage and resourcefulness are put to the test, leading to surprising discoveries and life-changing experiences. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within this extraordinary adventure.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Courage, Discovery
