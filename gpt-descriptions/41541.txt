Synopsis: In "Rubens" by S. L. Bensusan, a young artist named Rubens embarks on a journey that takes him from the vibrant streets of Paris to the enchanting landscapes of Italy. As he navigates the world of art, love, and ambition, Rubens finds himself torn between his passion for his craft and the allure of a forbidden romance. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this novel explores the complexities of art, the pursuit of dreams, and the sacrifices one must make for true fulfillment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Artistic expression, Love and desire, Ambition and sacrifice
