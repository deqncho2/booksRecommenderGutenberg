Synopsis: In 'The Story of Old Fort Dearborn', J. Seymour Currey takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of one of America's most iconic forts. Set in the early 19th century, the story follows the lives of the brave soldiers and settlers who inhabited Fort Dearborn, located in what is now modern-day Chicago. As tensions rise between Native American tribes and the encroaching settlers, the fort becomes a symbol of both hope and conflict. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Currey paints a vivid picture of the struggles, triumphs, and tragedies that unfolded within the walls of Old Fort Dearborn.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Conflict and coexistence between settlers and Native Americans, the resilience of the human spirit, the impact of historical events on individuals and communities.
