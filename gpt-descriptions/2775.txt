Synopsis: 
In the seemingly idyllic world of Edwardian England, two couples form a close friendship that appears to be the epitome of marital bliss. However, as the story unfolds, secrets, lies, and betrayals gradually unravel the facade of their seemingly perfect lives. Narrated by the enigmatic John Dowell, the reader is taken on a journey through the complexities of love, desire, and the destructive power of human relationships. As the layers of deception are peeled away, the true nature of these characters is revealed, leaving the reader questioning the very essence of trust and the fragility of human connections.

Genres: 
Psychological fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: 
Deception, Betrayal, Love, Marriage, Perception vs Reality
