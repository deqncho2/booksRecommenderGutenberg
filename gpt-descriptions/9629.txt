Synopsis: In 'Ghost Stories of an Antiquary Part 2: More Ghost Stories', James, M. R. takes readers on another chilling journey through the supernatural. This collection of eerie tales delves into haunted houses, mysterious apparitions, and ancient curses that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. From a cursed manuscript to a spectral figure haunting a country church, each story is masterfully crafted to send shivers down the reader's spine. Prepare to be captivated by the atmospheric settings, spine-tingling suspense, and the lingering sense of dread that permeates each page.

Genres: Horror, Gothic fiction, Supernatural fiction

Themes: Ghosts, Hauntings, Curses, Fear of the unknown, Psychological terror
