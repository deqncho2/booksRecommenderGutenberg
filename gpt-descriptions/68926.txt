Synopsis: 
In 'Stories and Ballads for Young Folks' by Ellen Tracy Alden, embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales and lyrical ballads that will transport readers to magical realms, ignite their imagination, and touch their hearts. From whimsical adventures to poignant stories of friendship and bravery, this book is a treasure trove of captivating narratives that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Adventure, Poetry

Themes: 
Imagination, Friendship, Bravery
