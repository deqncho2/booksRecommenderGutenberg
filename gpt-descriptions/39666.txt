Synopsis: In 'When It Was Dark: The Story of a Great Conspiracy' by Thorne, Guy, a small English village is thrown into chaos when a renowned scientist claims to have discovered irrefutable evidence that Jesus Christ did not rise from the dead. As the news spreads, the village becomes divided, with some embracing the scientist's findings while others fiercely defend their faith. Amidst the growing tension, a mysterious stranger arrives, determined to uncover the truth behind the conspiracy. With secrets, lies, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the power of belief and the lengths people will go to protect their convictions.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Faith and doubt, Truth and deception, Religious beliefs and skepticism
