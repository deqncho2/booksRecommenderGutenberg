Synopsis: 
'Floyd's Flowers; Or, Duty and Beauty for Colored Children: Being One Hundred Short Stories Gleaned from the Storehouse of Human Knowledge and Experience: Simple, Amusing, Elevating' is a collection of one hundred captivating short stories that will transport readers into a world of wonder and inspiration. From tales of bravery and kindness to stories that explore the power of imagination, each narrative is carefully crafted to engage and uplift young minds. Silas Xavier Floyd's masterful storytelling will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover the next enchanting tale.

Genres: Children's Literature, Short Stories, Fiction

Themes: Bravery, Kindness, Imagination, Inspiration
