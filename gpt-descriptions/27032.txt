Synopsis: 

'Interviews (1998-2001)' by Marie Lebert is a captivating collection of interviews that offers a unique glimpse into the lives and minds of various individuals from different walks of life. From renowned artists and writers to scientists and activists, each interview delves deep into their personal experiences, inspirations, and challenges. As the reader immerses themselves in these intimate conversations, they are taken on a thought-provoking journey that explores the complexities of human existence and the power of storytelling.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Interview

Themes: Identity, Creativity, Human Connection, Personal Growth
