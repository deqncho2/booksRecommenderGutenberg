Synopsis: In 'A System of Midwifery', Edward Rigby takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of midwifery, revealing the secrets and challenges faced by these unsung heroes. From the bustling streets of London to the tranquil countryside, Rigby's narrative weaves together the stories of midwives and their patients, showcasing the triumphs and tragedies that unfold within the birthing chambers. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt anecdotes, this book offers a unique glimpse into the art and science of midwifery, leaving readers both enlightened and deeply moved.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Historical

Themes: Women's health, Maternal care, Medical advancements, Gender roles, Empathy
