Synopsis: In 'The Clue of the Gold Coin' by Helen Wells, Nancy Drew finds herself embroiled in a thrilling mystery when she stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. As she follows the clues, Nancy uncovers a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. With her keen detective skills and unwavering determination, she must unravel the mystery of the gold coin before it falls into the wrong hands. Will Nancy be able to solve the puzzle and protect the treasure?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Treasure hunting, Friendship, Courage
