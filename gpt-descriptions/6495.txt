Synopsis: In 'Great Fortunes from Railroads', Gustavus Myers takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of railroad tycoons and the immense wealth they amassed. From the early days of railroading to the Gilded Age, Myers delves into the lives of these influential figures, exploring their cunning business strategies, cutthroat competition, and the societal impact of their fortunes. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, this book unveils the captivating stories behind the rise and fall of these larger-than-life characters, leaving readers enthralled by the power and ambition that shaped America's railroad industry.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Biography

Themes: Wealth and power, American industrialization, Entrepreneurship, Social impact of railroads
