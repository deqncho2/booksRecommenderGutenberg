Synopsis: In 'A Supplementary Report on the Results of a Special Inquiry into the Practice of Interment in Towns,' Edwin Chadwick delves into a thought-provoking investigation that uncovers the hidden truths behind the burial practices within urban areas. Through meticulous research and compelling evidence, Chadwick sheds light on the alarming consequences of interment in towns, revealing a shocking reality that challenges societal norms and raises crucial questions about public health and urban planning. As the report unfolds, readers are taken on a captivating journey that explores the dark underbelly of urban burial grounds, ultimately leaving them with a profound understanding of the urgent need for change.

Genres: Non-fiction, investigative journalism, social commentary

Themes: Public health, urban planning, societal norms, environmental impact
