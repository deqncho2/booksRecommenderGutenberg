Synopsis: In 'Cakewalk to Gloryanna' by Stecher, L. J., Jr., a young woman named Gloryanna embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience in the face of adversity. Set against the backdrop of the Great Depression, Gloryanna's life takes an unexpected turn when she finds herself caught between two worlds - the glamorous life of a Hollywood starlet and the humble existence of her small town roots. As she navigates the challenges of fame, love, and personal identity, Gloryanna must make difficult choices that will shape her future and reveal the true meaning of success.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Resilience, Love, Social Class
