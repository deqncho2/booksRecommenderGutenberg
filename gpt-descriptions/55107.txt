Synopsis: 
'Little Songs of Long Ago: More Old Nursery Rhymes' is a delightful collection of classic nursery rhymes that have been passed down through generations. From familiar favorites like "Twinkle, Twinkle, Little Star" to lesser-known gems, this book takes readers on a nostalgic journey through childhood. Each rhyme is accompanied by charming illustrations that bring the verses to life, making it a perfect read-aloud for both children and adults. Whether you're revisiting these beloved rhymes or discovering them for the first time, this collection is sure to evoke a sense of joy and wonder.

Genres: Children's Literature, Poetry, Picture Book

Themes: Nostalgia, Childhood, Tradition
