Synopsis: In 'The Magic Makers and the Bramble Bush Man' by Margaret Sutton, a group of young friends stumble upon a mysterious figure lurking in the shadows of their small town. As they delve deeper into the secrets surrounding the Bramble Bush Man, they uncover a world of enchantment and danger they never could have imagined. With their newfound magical abilities, the friends must navigate a treacherous journey to save their town from an ancient evil that threatens to consume everything they hold dear.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Good vs. Evil
