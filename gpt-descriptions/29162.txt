Synopsis: In 'The Traitors' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a young man named Felix is thrust into a world of espionage and danger when he stumbles upon a secret society plotting to overthrow the government. As he becomes entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal, Felix must navigate a treacherous landscape where loyalties are tested and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. With his life on the line, Felix must uncover the truth and expose the traitors before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Espionage, Mystery

Themes: Betrayal, Loyalty, Deception
