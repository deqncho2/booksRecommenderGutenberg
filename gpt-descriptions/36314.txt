Synopsis: In 'The Radio Boys Rescue the Lost Alaska Expedition' by Breckenridge, Gerald, a group of adventurous young radio enthusiasts embarks on a thrilling journey to save a stranded expedition in the treacherous Alaskan wilderness. As they face dangerous obstacles and unexpected twists, the Radio Boys must rely on their wit, resourcefulness, and their trusty radios to navigate through the unforgiving terrain and bring the lost explorers back to safety. This action-packed tale of friendship, courage, and survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival
