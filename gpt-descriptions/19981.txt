Synopsis: In the remote wilderness of Labrador, a young doctor named Luke struggles to provide medical care to the isolated communities scattered across the harsh landscape. As he battles against the unforgiving elements and the limitations of his resources, Luke finds himself facing not only physical challenges, but also the deep-rooted traditions and beliefs of the people he is trying to help. Amidst the rugged beauty of Labrador, Luke's determination and compassion are put to the ultimate test, forcing him to confront his own limitations and redefine his understanding of what it means to be a healer.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Medical Drama

Themes: Survival, Cultural Clash, Personal Growth
