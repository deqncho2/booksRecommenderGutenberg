Synopsis: In the enchanting world of high society and financial intrigue, Miss Dividends, a captivating debutante with a secret talent for numbers, finds herself entangled in a web of deceit and danger. As she navigates the treacherous waters of love and wealth, Miss Dividends must uncover the truth behind a mysterious inheritance and confront the dark forces that threaten to destroy everything she holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Wealth and power, Deception and betrayal, Love and sacrifice
