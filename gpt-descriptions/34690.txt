Synopsis: 
In 'Dorothy Payne, Quakeress: A Side-Light Upon the Career of 'Dolly' Madison', Ella K. Barnard delves into the fascinating life of Dorothy Payne, a Quaker woman who played a significant role in the political and social circles of early America. As the story unfolds, readers are taken on a captivating journey through Dorothy's unconventional upbringing, her encounters with influential figures, and her eventual transformation into the beloved First Lady, 'Dolly' Madison. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Barnard sheds light on the lesser-known aspects of Dolly Madison's life, offering a fresh perspective on this iconic historical figure.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction

Themes: Women's empowerment, Political intrigue, Social change
