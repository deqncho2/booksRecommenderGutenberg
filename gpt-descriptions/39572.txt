Synopsis: In 'West Of The Sun' by Edgar Pangborn, a young man named Johnny is haunted by vivid dreams of a mysterious world beyond the sun. As he grows older, these dreams become more intense, blurring the line between reality and fantasy. Determined to uncover the truth, Johnny embarks on a journey that takes him to the edge of the known universe, where he discovers a hidden realm filled with wonders and dangers beyond imagination. With each step closer to the truth, Johnny must confront his deepest fears and question the nature of his own existence.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Identity and self-discovery, The power of dreams and imagination
