Synopsis: In 'Musical Studies' by Newman, Ernest, a young prodigy embarks on a journey of self-discovery through the enchanting world of music. As he navigates the challenges of mastering various instruments and delves into the secrets of composition, he encounters unexpected friendships, bitter rivalries, and a profound connection to the power of sound. With each note he plays, the protagonist uncovers the transformative nature of music, ultimately leading him to a revelation that will forever change his perception of the art form.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Music

Themes: Passion for music, Self-discovery, Friendship, Rivalry, Transformation
