Synopsis: In 'Rose O'Paradise' by Grace Miller White, a young woman named Rose finds herself torn between duty and desire when she inherits a mysterious estate in the beautiful countryside. As she delves into the secrets of her family's past, Rose uncovers a web of hidden treasures, forbidden love, and long-buried betrayals. With each discovery, she becomes entangled in a captivating journey that will test her courage and challenge her perception of love and loyalty. Will Rose be able to navigate the complexities of her newfound inheritance, or will the past consume her present?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Love and sacrifice, Identity and self-discovery
