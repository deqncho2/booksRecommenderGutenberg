Synopsis: 
'Russian Fairy Tales from the Skazki of Polevoi' is a captivating collection of enchanting tales that transport readers to a world filled with magical creatures, brave heroes, and treacherous villains. From the depths of the mystical forests to the grand palaces of tsars, these timeless stories are woven with adventure, love, and the triumph of good over evil. Each tale unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers on the edge of their seats as they delve into the rich tapestry of Russian folklore.

Genres: 
Fairy Tale, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: 
Good vs. Evil, Heroism, Magic, Love and Sacrifice
