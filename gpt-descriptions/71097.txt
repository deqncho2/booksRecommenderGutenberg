Synopsis: In "The Unwelcome Child: Or, The Crime of an Undesigned and Undesired Maternity," Henry Clarke Wright takes readers on a gripping journey through the life of a child born into a world that never wanted them. Set in the 19th century, this powerful novel explores the consequences of an unplanned pregnancy and the societal pressures that surround it. As the story unfolds, readers are drawn into a web of secrets, lies, and the desperate struggle for survival in a world that seems determined to reject this innocent child. With each turn of the page, the reader is left questioning the true nature of love, family, and the lengths one will go to protect their own. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes: Unplanned Parenthood, Society's Expectations, Family Dynamics, Survival
