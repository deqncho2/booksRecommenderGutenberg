Synopsis: In 'The Chronicles of Newgate, vol. 1/2' by Arthur Griffiths, readers are transported to the dark underbelly of 19th-century London, where the notorious Newgate Prison stands as a symbol of both fear and fascination. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Griffiths unveils the gripping tales of the inmates, the wardens, and the intricate web of corruption that permeates the prison walls. From daring escapes to unexpected alliances, this enthralling volume delves into the lives of those trapped within Newgate's confines, painting a haunting portrait of a world where survival is a constant battle against both external and internal forces. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Crime, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Corruption, Redemption
