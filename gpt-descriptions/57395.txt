Synopsis: In 'Interim: Pilgrimage, Volume 5', the protagonist, Miriam Henderson, embarks on a transformative journey of self-discovery as she navigates the complexities of love, loss, and personal growth. Set against the backdrop of post-World War I England, Miriam's pilgrimage takes her through various encounters and experiences that challenge her beliefs and force her to confront her own desires and aspirations. As she grapples with the ever-changing landscape of her own emotions and the world around her, Miriam must find the strength to forge her own path and redefine her sense of identity.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Literary Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Love and Relationships, Identity, Personal Growth
