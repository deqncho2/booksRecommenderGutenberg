Synopsis: 
"Selected Speeches on British Foreign Policy 1738-1914" is a captivating collection of influential speeches that shaped the course of British history. From the stirring words of renowned statesmen to the impassioned pleas of diplomats, this anthology offers a unique insight into the evolution of British foreign policy over nearly two centuries. Delve into the eloquent speeches that address pivotal moments, diplomatic challenges, and the pursuit of national interests, providing a fascinating glimpse into the minds of those who shaped the destiny of a nation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political speeches, Diplomatic history

Themes: National identity, Imperialism, Diplomacy, War and peace, International relations
