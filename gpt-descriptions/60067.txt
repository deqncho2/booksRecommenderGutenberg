Synopsis:
'Leave it to Psmith' is a delightful comedic novel by P. G. Wodehouse that follows the misadventures of the charming and eccentric Psmith. When Psmith finds himself entangled in a case of mistaken identity, he takes on the role of a private detective and becomes embroiled in a series of hilarious and unpredictable situations. With his quick wit and unconventional methods, Psmith navigates the world of high society, love affairs, and secret societies, all while trying to maintain his composure and keep his true identity hidden. As the story unfolds, readers are taken on a whimsical journey filled with laughter, romance, and unexpected twists.

Genres:
Comedy, Mystery, Romance

Themes:
Identity, Love, Social class
