Synopsis: In 'The Day of Small Things' by Manning, Anne, a seemingly ordinary day takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious event occurs, altering the lives of a group of strangers forever. As they navigate through the aftermath, secrets are unveiled, relationships are tested, and the characters are forced to confront their deepest fears and desires. With a blend of suspense, romance, and a touch of the supernatural, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the day of small things.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and revelations, Fate and destiny, Human connection
