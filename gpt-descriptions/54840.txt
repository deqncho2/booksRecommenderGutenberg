Synopsis: In 'The New Optimism' by H. De Vere Stacpoole, a young artist named Julian embarks on a journey of self-discovery and transformation. Drawn to the vibrant streets of Paris, he encounters a mysterious group of individuals who introduce him to a new way of thinking and living. As Julian delves deeper into this world, he begins to question his own beliefs and finds himself torn between his old life and the allure of this new optimism. With each step he takes, Julian uncovers secrets that challenge his perception of reality and force him to confront his deepest desires. Will he embrace this newfound optimism or will he be consumed by its seductive power?

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Psychological

Themes: Self-discovery, Transformation, Belief systems, Desire, Reality
