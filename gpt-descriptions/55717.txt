Synopsis: In the Days of Queen Mary takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous reign of Queen Mary I of England. Set in the 16th century, this historical novel follows the lives of various characters as they navigate the treacherous political landscape, religious conflicts, and personal struggles of the time. Amidst the backdrop of religious persecution and the fight for power, the story weaves together themes of loyalty, betrayal, faith, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Political fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Religion, Power, Persecution
