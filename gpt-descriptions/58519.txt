Synopsis: In the final installment of the gripping trilogy, 'Kissing the Rod: A Novel', Edmund Yates takes readers on a thrilling journey through the lives of his complex characters. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and unexpected twists and turns keep readers on the edge of their seats. As the story reaches its climax, the characters must confront their pasts and make choices that will shape their futures. With its rich historical detail and compelling narrative, this novel is a captivating conclusion to an unforgettable saga.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Identity, Society's Expectations
