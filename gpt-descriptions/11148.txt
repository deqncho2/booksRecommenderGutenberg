Synopsis: 
In 'Aunt Harding's Keepsakes: Or, The Two Bibles' by Anonymous, a young woman named Emily discovers a hidden secret within her late aunt's belongings. As she delves deeper into her aunt's past, Emily uncovers a mysterious connection between two seemingly ordinary Bibles. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a web of family secrets, forbidden love, and unexpected alliances. As Emily unravels the truth, she must confront her own identity and make choices that will shape her future. This captivating tale of intrigue and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Forbidden love
