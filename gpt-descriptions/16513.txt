Synopsis: 
In "World's War Events $v Volume 3," embark on a riveting journey that begins with the departure of the first American destroyers for service abroad in April 1917. Witness the unfolding of a global conflict that will shape the course of history, as the world is thrust into chaos and uncertainty. From the trenches of Europe to the high seas, experience the bravery, sacrifice, and resilience of individuals caught in the midst of a war that tests the limits of humanity. As the narrative progresses, follow the intricate web of alliances, political maneuverings, and battles that ultimately lead to the treaties of peace in 1919. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War, Military History

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Diplomacy, Global Conflict
