Synopsis: In 'A Study of Poetry' by Bliss Perry, a renowned literary critic embarks on a captivating exploration of the power and beauty of poetry. Through insightful analysis and thought-provoking examples, Perry delves into the essence of poetry, unraveling its various forms and uncovering the profound impact it has on our emotions and understanding of the world. As he navigates through the works of renowned poets, Perry invites readers on a journey that celebrates the artistry and significance of poetry, leaving them with a newfound appreciation for this timeless form of expression.

Genres: Literary criticism, Poetry analysis, Non-fiction

Themes: The power of language, Artistic expression, Emotional resonance
