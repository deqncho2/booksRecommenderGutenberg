Synopsis: 
In "Kings in Exile" by Charles G. D. Roberts, Sir, a group of exiled kings find themselves stranded on a remote island, far away from their kingdoms and power. As they struggle to survive in this unfamiliar and hostile environment, they must confront their own personal demons and face the harsh realities of their past actions. With their once mighty crowns reduced to mere symbols, these kings must find a way to reclaim their lost glory or accept their fate in this new world.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Power and its consequences, Redemption, Identity
