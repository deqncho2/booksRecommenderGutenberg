Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Prince Edward Island, a group of cousins gather together for a summer filled with captivating stories and unforgettable adventures. Among them is the Story Girl, a young girl with a gift for spinning tales that transport her listeners to magical realms. As the summer unfolds, the cousins find themselves drawn into the Story Girl's captivating narratives, discovering the power of imagination and the beauty of friendship.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Family

Themes: Imagination, Storytelling, Friendship, Childhood, Family dynamics
