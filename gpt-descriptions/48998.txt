Synopsis: 
In the Village of Viger takes readers on a captivating journey to a small Canadian village in the late 19th century. The story revolves around the lives of two young women, one from a wealthy family and the other from a humble background, who find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden love, and societal expectations. As their paths cross, they must navigate the complexities of class, tradition, and personal desires, ultimately leading to unexpected consequences that will forever change their lives and the village they call home.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social class, Love and desire, Tradition and societal expectations
