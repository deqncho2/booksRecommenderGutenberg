Synopsis: 

'Climate and Health in Hot Countries and the Outlines of Tropical Climatology' is a captivating and informative treatise that delves into the fascinating world of personal hygiene and climate in hot regions. Written by George Michael James Giles, this popular work provides valuable insights into the challenges and benefits of living in hotter parts of the world, exploring the climates encountered within them. From practical advice on maintaining health and well-being to a deeper understanding of the unique climatic conditions, this treatise offers a comprehensive exploration of the topic.

Genres: Non-fiction, Health, Climate Science

Themes: Personal hygiene, Climate and health, Tropical climatology
