Synopsis: In 'Fix Bay'nets: The Regiment in the Hills' by George Manville Fenn, a young soldier named Tom is thrust into the heart of a treacherous mountainous region during a colonial war. As he navigates the rugged terrain and faces the challenges of battle, Tom must confront not only the enemy but also his own fears and doubts. With danger lurking at every turn, will Tom find the courage to survive and prove himself as a soldier?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Survival, Coming of Age
