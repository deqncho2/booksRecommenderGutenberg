Synopsis: In 'The Lilac Sunbonnet: A Love Story' by S.R. Crockett, a young woman named Lilias finds herself torn between duty and desire when she falls in love with a mysterious stranger. Set against the backdrop of the Scottish countryside, this captivating tale explores the complexities of love, sacrifice, and the power of fate. As Lilias navigates the challenges of her heart, she must confront the secrets that threaten to unravel her world and make a choice that will shape her future forever.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Love and Duty, Sacrifice, Secrets and Betrayal
