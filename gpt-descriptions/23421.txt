Synopsis: In 'The Collaborators: 1896' by Hichens, Robert, a renowned artist finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when he is commissioned to paint a portrait of a mysterious woman. As he delves deeper into her past, he uncovers a world of scandal, betrayal, and hidden identities. With each stroke of his brush, he unravels a captivating tale that will challenge his perception of art, love, and the true nature of collaboration.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Artistic expression, Identity, Betrayal, Secrets
