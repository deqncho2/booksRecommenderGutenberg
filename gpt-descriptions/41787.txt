Synopsis: 

In 'Reports of the Committee on the Conduct of the War: Fort Pillow Massacre. Returned Prisoners.', Daniel W. Gooch, along with the United States Congress Joint Committee on the Conduct of the War and Benjamin Franklin Wade, delves into a shocking incident known as the Fort Pillow Massacre. This report uncovers the harrowing details of the massacre and sheds light on the experiences of the returned prisoners. 

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, Military

Themes: War atrocities, Accountability, Prisoner experiences
