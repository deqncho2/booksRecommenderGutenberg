Synopsis: 

In 'The Great War and How It Arose' by Anonymous, a gripping tale unfolds as the author delves into the events leading up to one of the most devastating conflicts in history. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, the book uncovers the intricate web of political alliances, secret negotiations, and escalating tensions that ultimately ignited the flames of war. From the assassination of a prominent figure to the brinkmanship of world powers, the narrative unravels the complex tapestry of events that shaped the course of history. As the reader follows the author's exploration, they are drawn into a world of intrigue, power struggles, and the profound consequences of human decisions. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political thriller, War history

Themes: Power and politics, Diplomacy and negotiation, Consequences of actions
