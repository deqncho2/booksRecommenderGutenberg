Synopsis: In 'Lord Arthur Savile's Crime; The Portrait of Mr. W.H., and Other Stories' by Oscar Wilde, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing tales. From the misadventures of Lord Arthur Savile, who is determined to commit a crime before his impending marriage, to the mysterious portrait that holds a secret, and other captivating stories, Wilde weaves together a tapestry of wit, suspense, and unexpected twists. Each story presents a unique and thought-provoking exploration of human nature, morality, and the consequences of our actions. 

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Mystery

Themes: Morality, Consequences, Human Nature
