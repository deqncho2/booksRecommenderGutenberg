Synopsis: In the small village of Pabo, a young and enigmatic priest arrives, captivating the townspeople with his mysterious past and unconventional ways. As the villagers become increasingly drawn to him, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden desires that threaten to unravel their peaceful existence. With each passing day, the line between faith and sin blurs, leaving the community questioning their own beliefs and the true nature of their beloved priest.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Faith and doubt, Morality, Secrets and deception
