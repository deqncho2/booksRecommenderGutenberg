Synopsis: 
In the sixth volume of "Memoirs of the Court of Marie Antoinette," Madam Campan, the First Lady in Waiting to the Queen, continues her captivating account of life within the opulent and tumultuous court of Versailles. As the French Revolution looms on the horizon, Campan offers a unique perspective on the inner workings of the royal court, revealing the intricate web of alliances, rivalries, and secrets that shaped the destiny of the ill-fated queen. From the lavish balls and extravagant festivities to the political intrigues and personal dramas, this memoir provides a fascinating glimpse into the final years of Marie Antoinette's reign.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Court life, French Revolution, Royal intrigue, Personal sacrifice, Historical events
