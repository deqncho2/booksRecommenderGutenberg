Synopsis: In "Love-at-arms," the reader is transported to the captivating world of Urbino during the reign of the powerful Messer Guidobaldo da Montefeltro. Through the eyes of the narrator, we are introduced to a tale filled with intrigue, romance, and the complexities of courtly life. As the narrative unfolds, the reader becomes entangled in the lives of the noble inhabitants of Urbino, their ambitions, and the delicate dance of love and loyalty that binds them all. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Sabatini weaves a captivating story that keeps the reader eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Loyalty, Courtly Life, Ambition and Power
