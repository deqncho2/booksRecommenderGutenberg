Synopsis: In "The Motor Girls" by Margaret Penrose, a group of adventurous young women embark on a thrilling journey filled with mystery, danger, and unexpected twists. When a mysterious letter arrives, the girls find themselves entangled in a web of secrets that leads them on a cross-country adventure. As they navigate treacherous roads, encounter suspicious characters, and uncover hidden clues, the Motor Girls must rely on their wit, courage, and resourcefulness to solve the enigma before it's too late. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the thrilling escapades and the strong bond of friendship that binds these remarkable young women together.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Courage
