Synopsis: 

In 'Birds of Song and Story', Joseph and Elizabeth Grinnell take readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of birds. From the majestic eagle to the delicate hummingbird, the authors delve into the fascinating lives and behaviors of these feathered creatures, weaving together scientific knowledge and captivating anecdotes. Through their vivid descriptions and lyrical storytelling, the Grinnells invite readers to explore the intricate melodies of bird songs and the captivating tales that have been woven around these avian wonders for centuries. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Ornithology

Themes: Nature's Beauty, Animal Behavior, Cultural Significance of Birds
