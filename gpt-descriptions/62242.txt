Synopsis: In 'Doorway to Destruction' by Hatfield, Garold S., a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious doorway that leads to unimaginable power and destruction. As he delves deeper into the secrets of this doorway, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and manipulation, where the line between good and evil blurs. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to prevent the doorway from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power and its consequences, Morality and ethics
