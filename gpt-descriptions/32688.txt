Synopsis: In 'The Ordeal of Colonel Johns' by George H. Smith, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the life of Colonel Johns, a decorated war hero haunted by a dark secret from his past. As he navigates the treacherous waters of political intrigue and personal redemption, Colonel Johns finds himself entangled in a web of conspiracy that threatens to unravel everything he holds dear. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, forcing the Colonel to confront his demons and make impossible choices. Will he be able to overcome his past and emerge victorious, or will he succumb to the forces working against him?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Political Fiction

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Consequences of War
