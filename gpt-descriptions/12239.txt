Synopsis: In 'Dead Men's Money' by Fletcher, J.S., a mysterious death sets off a chain of events that lead to a thrilling investigation. When a wealthy man is found dead in his study, his nephew and a detective team up to uncover the truth behind his demise. As they delve deeper into the case, they encounter a web of secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected twists that keep them on their toes. With danger lurking around every corner, they must race against time to solve the puzzle and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Greed, Betrayal, Deception
