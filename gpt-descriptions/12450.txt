Synopsis: In 'The Reason Why' by Elinor Glyn, a young woman finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a scandalous love affair. Set against the backdrop of high society in early 20th century England, this captivating novel explores the complexities of passion, societal expectations, and the consequences of following one's heart.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Duty and Responsibility, Society and Class
