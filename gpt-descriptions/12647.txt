Synopsis: 
In 'J. S. Le Fanu's Ghostly Tales, Volume 4', Joseph Sheridan Le Fanu takes readers on a chilling journey through a collection of supernatural stories that will leave them questioning the boundaries between the living and the dead. From haunted houses to vengeful spirits, each tale is filled with suspense, mystery, and a sense of impending doom. As the characters navigate the eerie landscapes of their haunted existence, they must confront their deepest fears and confront the consequences of their actions. Prepare to be captivated by Le Fanu's masterful storytelling as he weaves together a tapestry of ghostly encounters that will send shivers down your spine.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, supernatural

Themes: The supernatural, the afterlife, guilt and redemption, the power of the unknown
