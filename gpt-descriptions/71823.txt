Synopsis: Percy's holidays take an unexpected turn when he finds himself entangled in a web of mysterious events and borrowed trouble. As he navigates through a series of misadventures, Percy discovers that sometimes the best way to solve a problem is to face it head-on, even if it means stepping out of his comfort zone. With each new challenge, Percy learns valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the importance of taking responsibility for one's actions. Will he be able to untangle the mess he's gotten himself into and make it a holiday to remember?

Genres: Children's Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Responsibility
