Synopsis: In 'Extraction of the Teeth' by Colyer, Frank, a renowned dentist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when a routine tooth extraction takes an unexpected turn. As he delves deeper into the patient's history, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his professional reputation but also his life. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Professional ethics
