Synopsis: In 'Captain Salt in Oz' by Ruth Plumly Thompson, a daring pirate captain named Salt finds himself transported to the magical land of Oz. With his loyal crew by his side, Captain Salt embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with enchanted islands, talking animals, and eccentric characters. As he navigates through the whimsical and unpredictable world of Oz, Captain Salt must face challenges, solve puzzles, and outwit cunning adversaries in order to find a way back home. Will he succeed in his quest or will he be forever lost in the enchanting realm of Oz?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Courage
