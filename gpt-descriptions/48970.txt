Synopsis: 

In 'Across Texas' by Edward Sylvester Ellis, a gripping tale unfolds as a group of adventurers embarks on a perilous journey across the vast and treacherous landscapes of Texas. Faced with numerous challenges and unexpected encounters, they must navigate through dangerous territories, confront ruthless outlaws, and unravel the mysteries that lie hidden within the untamed wilderness. As they push the boundaries of their physical and mental endurance, the characters find themselves tested in ways they never imagined, forging unbreakable bonds and discovering the true meaning of courage and resilience. 

Genres: Adventure, Western, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Friendship, Perseverance
