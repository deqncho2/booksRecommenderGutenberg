Synopsis: 
'Phil May: Sketches from "Punch"' is a captivating collection of humorous and satirical illustrations by the renowned artist Phil May. Filled with witty and clever sketches, this book offers a delightful glimpse into the world of late 19th-century British society. From comical characters to social commentary, May's illustrations provide a unique perspective on the everyday life and eccentricities of the time. Whether it's a humorous take on fashion trends or a playful jab at societal norms, each sketch is sure to entertain and leave readers wanting more.

Genres: 
Art, Humor, Satire

Themes: 
Social commentary, British society, Everyday life
