Synopsis: In 'The Mysterious Murder of Pearl Bryan, or: the Headless Horror,' a small town is shaken to its core when the body of a young woman is discovered, brutally decapitated. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, leading the townspeople down a dark and twisted path of obsession, betrayal, and revenge. With each revelation, the mystery deepens, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, desperate to uncover the truth behind this chilling crime.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Obsession, Justice and revenge
