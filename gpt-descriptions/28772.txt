Synopsis: 
'Ellen of Villenskov, and Other Ballads' is a collection of captivating and haunting ballads that transport readers to a world of love, betrayal, and mystery. Each ballad tells a unique story, filled with vivid imagery and unforgettable characters. From the tragic tale of Ellen, a young woman caught in a web of secrets, to the chilling legend of the ghostly figure haunting the village of Villenskov, these ballads will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind each haunting melody.

Genres: Poetry, Folklore, Gothic

Themes: Love and loss, Betrayal, Mystery, Supernatural elements
