Synopsis: 
'Conservation of marine birds of northern North America: papers from the international symposium held at the Seattle Hyatt House' takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of marine birds and the efforts being made to protect and preserve them. This collection of papers, presented at an international symposium, delves into the challenges faced by these magnificent creatures and the innovative strategies being employed to ensure their survival. From the breathtaking landscapes of the northern North American coastlines to the heartwarming stories of dedicated conservationists, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the intricate relationship between humans and marine birds.

Genres: Non-fiction, Conservation, Environmental Science

Themes: Wildlife conservation, Biodiversity, Human impact on ecosystems
