Synopsis: In the small college town of Churchton, a young and enigmatic man named Bertram Cope arrives, captivating the attention of both men and women alike. As the year unfolds, Bertram's presence becomes a catalyst for a series of unexpected events, stirring up desires, jealousies, and secret passions among the town's inhabitants. With his magnetic charm and mysterious allure, Bertram's impact on the community is profound, leading to a captivating exploration of love, identity, and the complexities of human relationships.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Identity, Desire, Love, Sexuality, Social dynamics
