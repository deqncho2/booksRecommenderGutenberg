Synopsis:
In 'Frank Merriwell, Junior's, Golden Trail; Or, The Fugitive Professor' by Standish, Burt L., Frank Merriwell Jr. finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when a renowned professor suddenly goes missing under mysterious circumstances. As Frank and his friends embark on a quest to uncover the truth, they encounter dangerous obstacles, hidden secrets, and unexpected allies. With time running out, Frank must use his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to solve the puzzle and bring the fugitive professor to justice.

Genres:
Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes:
Friendship, Loyalty, Perseverance, Justice
