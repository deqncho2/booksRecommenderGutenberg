Synopsis:
In "The Trossachs" by G. E. Mitton, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself drawn to the enchanting beauty of the Scottish Highlands. As she embarks on a journey through the picturesque landscapes of the Trossachs, she becomes entangled in a web of mystery and romance. Amidst the rugged mountains, shimmering lochs, and ancient forests, Evelyn discovers secrets that have been hidden for generations, and her own heart is captivated by a charismatic stranger. With each step she takes, the allure of the Trossachs grows stronger, and Evelyn must navigate the delicate balance between love and the haunting past that threatens to unravel it all.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Secrets and hidden histories, Connection to nature
