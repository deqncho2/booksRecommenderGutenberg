Synopsis: In 'Conjuror's House: A Romance of the Free Forest', a young man named John Gale finds himself entangled in a mysterious and dangerous world when he ventures into the remote Canadian wilderness. As he becomes acquainted with the enigmatic inhabitants of Conjuror's House, a secluded trading post, Gale discovers that there is more to this place than meets the eye. With secrets lurking in the shadows and a captivating woman at the center of it all, Gale must navigate a treacherous path filled with love, betrayal, and the supernatural forces that haunt the Free Forest.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Wilderness exploration, Supernatural elements, Love and betrayal
