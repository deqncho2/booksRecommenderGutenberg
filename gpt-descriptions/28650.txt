Synopsis: In 'Year of the Big Thaw' by Marion Zimmer Bradley, a devastating global catastrophe has left the world frozen in a perpetual winter. As survivors struggle to adapt to this harsh new reality, a group of scientists embarks on a dangerous mission to uncover the truth behind the cataclysm. Amidst the frozen landscapes and dwindling resources, they must confront their own fears and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Post-Apocalyptic, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Human resilience, Environmentalism
