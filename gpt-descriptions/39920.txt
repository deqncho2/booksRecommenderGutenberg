Synopsis: 

'Yarmouth Notes, 1830-1872. Collated from the File of the Norwich Mercury' by Palmer, Frederick Danby takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical events and intriguing stories that unfolded in the coastal town of Yarmouth during the years 1830 to 1872. Through a collection of notes and articles from the Norwich Mercury, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the people, the triumphs, the scandals, and the mysteries that shaped the town's history. From tales of shipwrecks and smuggling to political upheavals and social changes, this compilation paints a vivid picture of a vibrant community and the events that shaped its destiny.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Local history, Journalism

Themes: Community, History, Social change, Intrigue
