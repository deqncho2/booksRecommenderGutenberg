Synopsis: 

In "The Inquisition: A Political and Military Study of its Establishment," Nickerson and Hoffman delve into the dark and mysterious history of one of the most feared institutions in human history. Through meticulous research and analysis, they uncover the intricate web of political intrigue and military power that led to the establishment of the Inquisition. From its origins to its far-reaching consequences, this book offers a captivating exploration of the forces that shaped this infamous institution.

Genres: Historical, Political, Military

Themes: Power, Religion, Control, Fear
