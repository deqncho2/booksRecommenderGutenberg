Synopsis: In 'A Rogue's Tragedy' by Bernard Capes, a mysterious and enigmatic figure finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and revenge. Set against the backdrop of Victorian London, this gripping tale follows the protagonist as he navigates the treacherous underworld of thieves, con artists, and murderers. As secrets unravel and loyalties are tested, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will the rogue's past catch up with him, or will he manage to outwit his enemies and secure his own redemption?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
