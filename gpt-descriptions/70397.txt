Synopsis: 
In "How to Swim: A practical manual of swimming by a practical swimmer and a guide to the novice as well as the expert," James H. Sterrett presents a comprehensive guide to swimming, diving, and floating. With clear directions and helpful illustrations, this manual offers the easiest methods for learning various strokes, including the crawl and trudge-crawl strokes. Additionally, it includes a chapter on modern life-saving techniques and a special section dedicated to women and girl swimmers. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced swimmer, this practical guide will enhance your swimming skills and provide valuable insights into the world of swimming.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Self-help

Themes: Swimming techniques, Skill development, Safety, Gender inclusivity
