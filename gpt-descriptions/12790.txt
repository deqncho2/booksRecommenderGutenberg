Synopsis: In 'The True Woman: A Series of Discourses, to Which Is Added Woman vs. Ballot', Justin D. Fulton delves into the complex and controversial topic of women's roles in society. Through a series of thought-provoking discourses, Fulton challenges traditional notions and explores the true essence of womanhood. As he delves into the realms of education, marriage, and politics, he presents a compelling argument that will leave readers questioning their own beliefs and societal norms.

Genres: Non-fiction, Feminist literature, Social commentary

Themes: Gender roles, Women's rights, Femininity, Societal expectations
