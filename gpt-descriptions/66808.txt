Synopsis: In "Laura Everingham; or, The Highlanders of Glen Ora" by Grant, James, the reader is transported to the rugged and enchanting Scottish Highlands. Laura Everingham, a young Englishwoman, finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and romance as she becomes acquainted with the Highlanders of Glen Ora. As she delves deeper into their world, Laura discovers secrets that could change her life forever. With its captivating setting and intriguing characters, this novel promises an unforgettable journey filled with love, betrayal, and the allure of the Scottish landscape.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Cultural Identity, Forbidden Love, Family Secrets
