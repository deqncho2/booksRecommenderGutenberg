Synopsis: 
In 'Joseph Pennell's Pictures in the Land of Temples', the renowned artist takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the mesmerizing landscapes of the Land of Temples. Through a series of lithographs created during his stay from March to June 1913, Pennell captures the essence and grandeur of these ancient structures. Accompanied by his own impressions and notes, this book offers a unique glimpse into the artist's perspective and the profound impact these temples had on him.

Genres: Art, Travel, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Artistic Inspiration
