Synopsis: 
In "The Avenger" by Wallace Edgar, a mysterious figure emerges from the shadows, seeking justice for a series of heinous crimes. As the city trembles in fear, a relentless detective races against time to uncover the truth behind the avenger's motives and identity. With each twist and turn, the line between hero and villain becomes blurred, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking climax.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Vigilantism, Justice, Morality
