Synopsis: In 'The Kentish Coast' by Charles G. Harper, a mysterious disappearance sets off a chain of events that unravels the secrets hidden within the picturesque coastal town of Kent. As the protagonist delves deeper into the enigma, they uncover a web of deceit, betrayal, and long-buried family secrets that threaten to shatter the tranquility of the idyllic seaside community. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn into a captivating tale of suspense and intrigue, where nothing is as it seems and the truth lies just beyond the crashing waves.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Small-town intrigue
