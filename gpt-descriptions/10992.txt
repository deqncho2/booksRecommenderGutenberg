Synopsis: In 'Cleopatra' by Abbott, Jacob, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of history's most enigmatic and powerful women. From her rise to the throne of Egypt to her legendary relationships with Julius Caesar and Mark Antony, Cleopatra's story unfolds with intrigue, political maneuvering, and a touch of romance. As she navigates the treacherous world of ancient politics, Cleopatra's intelligence, charm, and determination become her greatest assets. Will she be able to secure her kingdom's independence and protect her people amidst the ever-shifting tides of power? Discover the captivating tale of Cleopatra, a woman who defied conventions and left an indelible mark on history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Romance

Themes: Power and politics, Love and relationships, Gender and societal expectations
