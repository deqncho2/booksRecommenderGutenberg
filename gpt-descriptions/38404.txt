Synopsis: In 'A Journey from Prince of Wales's Fort in Hudson's Bay to the Northern Ocean in the Years 1769, 1770, 1771, 1772', Samuel Hearne takes readers on a thrilling expedition through the uncharted wilderness of northern Canada. As he navigates treacherous terrain, encounters indigenous peoples, and battles the harsh elements, Hearne's account offers a captivating glimpse into the untamed beauty and dangers of the Arctic. This new edition includes an introduction, notes, and illustrations that enhance the reader's understanding and appreciation of Hearne's remarkable journey.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural Encounter
