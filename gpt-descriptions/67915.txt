Synopsis: In 'Sun' by D.H. Lawrence, a young woman named Alvina Houghton finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires. Set in the early 20th century, Alvina embarks on a journey of self-discovery as she navigates love, passion, and the constraints of her small English town. As she encounters various characters and experiences, Alvina must confront the complexities of her own identity and make choices that will shape her future. 

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Love, Society, Self-discovery
