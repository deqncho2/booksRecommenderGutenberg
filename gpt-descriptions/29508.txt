Synopsis: In 'The Ethics of Coöperation' by James Hayden Tufts, a renowned philosopher, a group of individuals find themselves entangled in a complex web of moral dilemmas and ethical choices. As they navigate through various situations, their beliefs and values are put to the test, forcing them to question the true nature of coöperation and its implications. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking scenarios, this gripping philosophical exploration challenges readers to examine their own principles and consider the consequences of their actions.

Genres: Philosophy, Fiction, Drama

Themes: Ethics, Morality, Decision-making, Consequences
