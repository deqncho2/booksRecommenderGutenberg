Synopsis: 
'A Book of Irish Verse: Selected from modern writers, with an introduction and notes by W. B. Yeats' is a captivating anthology that showcases the rich and diverse poetry of Ireland's modern writers. Curated by the renowned poet W. B. Yeats, this collection takes readers on a journey through the lyrical landscapes of Ireland, exploring themes of love, nature, mythology, and the complexities of Irish identity. From poignant ballads to stirring verses, this anthology offers a glimpse into the soul of Ireland through the power of words.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology

Themes: Love, Nature, Mythology, Irish Identity
