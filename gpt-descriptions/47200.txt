Synopsis: In 'Oracles from the Poets: A Fanciful Diversion for the Drawing Room', a group of eccentric poets gather in a secluded manor to unveil their mysterious and prophetic poems. As the evening unfolds, the guests find themselves immersed in a world of enchantment, where reality and imagination intertwine in unexpected ways. Secrets are revealed, desires are awakened, and the power of words takes on a whole new meaning. This whimsical tale will transport readers to a realm where the boundaries of time and space blur, leaving them captivated until the very last page.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Literary Fiction

Themes: Imagination, Fate, Secrets, Poetry
