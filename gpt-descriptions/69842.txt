Synopsis: In 'Ægle and the elf, a fantasy' by Toland, M. B. M., embark on a captivating journey through a mystical realm where a young girl named Ægle discovers a hidden world of enchantment. When she encounters an elf with a secret, their fates become intertwined, leading them on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, magic, and unexpected alliances. As Ægle and the elf navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter mythical creatures, they must uncover the truth behind their connection and the ancient prophecy that holds the key to their destinies.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Discovery, Friendship, Destiny, Magic
