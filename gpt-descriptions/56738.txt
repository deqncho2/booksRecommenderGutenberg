Synopsis: 

'Paint & Colour Mixing: A practical handbook for painters, decorators and all who have to mix colours, containing 72 samples of paint of various colours, including the principal graining grounds' by Arthur Seymour Jennings is a comprehensive guide that unlocks the secrets of paint and colour mixing. With 72 samples of paint in various hues, this handbook is an essential tool for painters and decorators seeking to create stunning and harmonious color combinations. From understanding the principles of colour theory to mastering the art of mixing pigments, this practical guide provides step-by-step instructions and expert advice to help readers achieve their desired results. Whether you are a professional painter or a DIY enthusiast, this handbook is sure to inspire and elevate your artistic endeavors.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Reference

Themes: Colour theory, Paint mixing, Artistic techniques
