Synopsis:
In the sweltering depths of the Louisiana bayou, a sinister force lurks, shrouded in ancient rituals and dark magic. When a young man from the North ventures into the heart of Black Canaan, he becomes entangled in a web of voodoo, forbidden desires, and the primal instincts of a forgotten civilization. As the line between reality and nightmare blurs, he must confront the horrors that lie within the shadows, and face the ultimate test of his own humanity.

Genres:
Horror, Dark Fantasy, Southern Gothic

Themes:
Supernatural forces, Cultural clash, Forbidden desires
