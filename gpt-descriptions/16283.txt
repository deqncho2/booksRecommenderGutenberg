Synopsis: In 'Idolatry: A Romance' by Hawthorne, Julian, a young artist named Adrian embarks on a journey of self-discovery and forbidden love. Set in the enchanting backdrop of 19th-century Italy, Adrian finds himself torn between his passion for art and his growing infatuation with a mysterious woman. As he delves deeper into the world of art and desire, Adrian must confront the consequences of his choices and the boundaries of his own creativity. With secrets, intrigue, and a touch of the supernatural, 'Idolatry: A Romance' promises to captivate readers with its exploration of love, art, and the pursuit of one's true identity.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Artistic expression, Identity, Temptation
