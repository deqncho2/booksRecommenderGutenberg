Synopsis: In 'Aunt Hannah and Seth' by Otis, James, a young boy named Seth is sent to live with his mysterious Aunt Hannah in a secluded village. As Seth explores his new surroundings, he discovers a hidden world filled with secrets, magic, and unexpected friendships. With each passing day, Seth unravels the truth about his own identity and the extraordinary powers that lie within him. But as dark forces threaten to destroy everything he holds dear, Seth must embrace his destiny and find the courage to protect his newfound home.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Friendship, Courage
