Synopsis:
Set in 16th century Scotland, "Marmion: A Tale of Flodden Field in Six Cantos" follows the story of Lord Marmion, a charismatic and ambitious nobleman, as he navigates a treacherous web of love, betrayal, and political intrigue. As tensions rise between England and Scotland, Marmion finds himself torn between his duty to his country and his forbidden desire for a beautiful woman. With vivid descriptions of battlefields, courtly affairs, and the rugged Scottish landscape, this epic tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through a tumultuous period in history.

Genres:
Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes:
Love and desire, Loyalty and betrayal, Power and ambition
