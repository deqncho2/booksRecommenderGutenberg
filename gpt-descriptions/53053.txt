Synopsis: 
'By the Aurelian Wall, and Other Elegies' by Carman, Bliss is a collection of poignant and evocative poems that explore the depths of human emotions and the beauty found within the natural world. From the haunting verses that depict the struggles of love and loss to the vivid imagery that transports readers to enchanting landscapes, this collection is a journey through the depths of the human soul. Each elegy is a reflection on life's fleeting moments, capturing the essence of both joy and sorrow in a delicate balance of words.

Genres: Poetry, Elegy, Romanticism

Themes: Love and loss, Nature and its beauty, Transience of life
