Synopsis: 
In 'Wood-Carving: Design and Workmanship', Jack George takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of wood-carving, unveiling the secrets behind this ancient craft. From the intricacies of design to the mastery of technique, this book offers a comprehensive guide that will inspire both beginners and experienced carvers alike. With stunning visuals and step-by-step instructions, George delves into the artistry and creativity required to transform a simple piece of wood into a true masterpiece. Whether you are interested in creating delicate sculptures or bold architectural elements, this book is a must-read for anyone passionate about the art of wood-carving.

Genres: 
Crafts, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Creativity, Skill development, Artistic expression
