Synopsis: In "The Protestants Plea for a Socinian," R. H. presents a compelling argument defending the controversial doctrine of Socinianism, while addressing accusations of heresy and schism. Through a meticulous examination of scripture and church authority, the author seeks to justify the compatibility of Socinian beliefs with the foundations of Protestantism. This thought-provoking work challenges conventional religious perspectives and invites readers to reconsider their understanding of doctrine and faith.

Genres: Theology, Religious Studies, Apologetics

Themes: Religious Controversy, Scriptural Interpretation, Church Authority, Heresy, Schism
