Synopsis: In the small village of Terriford, a mysterious event unfolds, leaving the residents bewildered and on edge. When a young woman disappears without a trace, the community becomes consumed by fear and suspicion. As the investigation deepens, dark secrets and hidden motives are gradually unearthed, revealing a web of deceit and betrayal that threatens to tear the village apart. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the enigma, eagerly trying to unravel the truth behind the Terriford mystery.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town intrigue, Betrayal, Fear and suspicion
