Synopsis: In 'The Pace That Kills: A Chronicle' by Edgar Saltus, we are introduced to a world of glamour, ambition, and dark secrets. Set in the bustling city of New York during the early 20th century, the story follows the lives of several individuals who are all driven by their insatiable desire for success and pleasure. As their paths intertwine, we witness the consequences of their relentless pursuit of power and the sacrifices they are willing to make to achieve it. From the dazzling heights of high society to the shadowy underbelly of the city, this gripping chronicle explores the price one must pay for living life at an exhilarating pace.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Ambition, Power, Morality, Society's Obsession with Success
