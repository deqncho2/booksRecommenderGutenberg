Synopsis: In 'Ancient and Modern Ships, Part 1: Wooden Sailing Ships', Sir George Charles Vincent Holmes takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and evolution of wooden sailing ships. From the ancient vessels of Egypt and Greece to the majestic warships of the 19th century, Holmes delves into the intricate details of shipbuilding techniques, navigational methods, and the fascinating stories behind these magnificent vessels. With vivid descriptions and illustrations, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the golden age of wooden sailing ships.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Maritime

Themes: Shipbuilding, Naval history, Technological advancements, Exploration
