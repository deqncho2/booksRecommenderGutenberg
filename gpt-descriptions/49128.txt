Synopsis: 
'Curiosities of Street Literature' takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden world of street literature, where tales of adventure, romance, and mystery come to life. From the bustling streets of Victorian London to the vibrant corners of modern-day cities, this collection of stories unveils the secrets and wonders found within the pages of these often overlooked publications. Delve into the lives of the authors, the readers, and the characters that inhabit these tales, as their stories intertwine and leave an indelible mark on the streets they call home.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Anthology

Themes: Urban culture, Literature and society, Human connection
