Synopsis: In 'The Menace of Prohibition' by Wightman, Lulu, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious substance starts spreading throughout the community, causing unexpected and dangerous consequences. As the townspeople struggle to uncover the truth behind this menace, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. Will they be able to overcome the menace before it consumes them all?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Power dynamics, Morality
