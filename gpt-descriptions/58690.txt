Synopsis: In 'For the Love of Lady Margaret: A Romance of the Lost Colony' by Wilson, William Thomas, a young Englishman named John finds himself captivated by the enigmatic Lady Margaret, a woman with a mysterious past. As John becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue, he embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about Lady Margaret and the lost colony she is connected to. Set against the backdrop of the untamed wilderness of the New World, this captivating romance explores the power of love, the pursuit of identity, and the enduring strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Desire, Identity and Self-discovery, Exploration and Adventure
