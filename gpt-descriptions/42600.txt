Synopsis: In 'Under St Paul's: A Romance' by Richard Dowling, a mysterious and enchanting tale unfolds beneath the iconic St Paul's Cathedral in London. As the protagonist delves into the hidden depths of the cathedral, he discovers a secret world filled with intrigue, forbidden love, and ancient secrets. With each step, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery that challenges his beliefs and forces him to confront his own desires. As the story unravels, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the hidden corridors and forgotten chambers of St Paul's, where nothing is as it seems and the past intertwines with the present in unexpected ways.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Forbidden love, Identity and self-discovery
