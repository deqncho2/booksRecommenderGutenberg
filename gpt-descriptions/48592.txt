Synopsis: In 'The Square Jaw', a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic charm and impeccable manners. As the townspeople become increasingly infatuated with him, they soon discover that his presence brings about a series of strange and unsettling events. With each passing day, the stranger's true intentions become more obscure, leaving the townspeople questioning their own sanity and the nature of reality itself.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Perception, Manipulation
