Synopsis: In "An Essay on the Origin of Language, Based on Modern Researches, and Especially on the Works of M. Renan," Farrar delves into the fascinating topic of language's origins, drawing from the groundbreaking research of M. Renan. Through a captivating exploration of linguistic evolution, Farrar offers thought-provoking insights into the development of human communication, shedding light on the mysteries of our earliest languages and their profound impact on society.

Genres: Non-fiction, Linguistics, Anthropology

Themes: Language evolution, Human communication, Societal impact
