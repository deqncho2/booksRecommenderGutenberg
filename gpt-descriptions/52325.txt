Synopsis: 

In 'The Geologic Story of Colorado National Monument: Revised Edition' by Stanley William Lohman, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the breathtaking landscapes of Colorado National Monument. This revised edition delves deep into the geological wonders that have shaped this remarkable region, unraveling the mysteries of its ancient rock formations, canyons, and unique ecosystems. Lohman's vivid descriptions and expert analysis bring to life the fascinating geological history of this natural wonder, leaving readers in awe of the forces that have shaped the land over millions of years. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Geology, Natural History

Themes: Geological formations, Natural wonders, Environmental conservation
