Synopsis: In "The Romance of a Great Store," Hungerford takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling world of a grand department store. The story revolves around the lives of two young women, one from a wealthy background and the other from a more modest upbringing, as they navigate the challenges and triumphs of working in this enchanting retail haven. As they encounter love, ambition, and unexpected twists of fate, the store becomes a backdrop for their personal and professional growth. With vivid descriptions and engaging characters, Hungerford weaves a tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what lies beyond the glamorous facade of the store.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, social class, ambition, personal growth
