Synopsis: 
In 'Jack O' Judgment' by Wallace Edgar, a mysterious figure known as Jack O' Judgment emerges in London, targeting criminals and dispensing his own brand of justice. As the police struggle to catch this elusive vigilante, a young journalist becomes entangled in the dangerous game of cat and mouse, uncovering shocking secrets along the way. With the city on edge and the stakes escalating, the race to unmask Jack O' Judgment becomes a thrilling battle of wits and a fight for justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Vigilantism, Justice, Morality
