Synopsis: In 'Death in Transit' by Jerry Sohl, a seemingly ordinary train journey takes a chilling turn when a passenger mysteriously dies on board. As the train hurtles through the night, the remaining passengers become entangled in a web of suspicion, secrets, and unexpected alliances. With each passing mile, tensions rise and the true nature of the deceased's demise becomes increasingly elusive. As the train races towards its destination, the passengers must confront their own fears and confront the possibility that the killer may still be among them.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Trust, Survival
