Synopsis: 
'Wine, Women, and Song: Mediaeval Latin Students' songs; Now first translated into English verse' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lively and often scandalous world of medieval Latin student songs. This collection of translated verses offers a glimpse into the raucous and humorous lives of students, their mischievous adventures, and their unabashed celebration of life's pleasures. From tales of love and romance to humorous anecdotes and witty observations, these songs provide a fascinating window into the vibrant culture of medieval student life.

Genres: 
Historical, Poetry, Anthology

Themes: 
Student life, Love and romance, Humor, Celebration of life's pleasures
