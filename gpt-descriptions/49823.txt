Synopsis: In 'Batik and Other Pattern Dyeing', authors Ida Strawn Baker and Walter Davis Baker take readers on a captivating journey into the world of fabric dyeing. From the ancient art of batik to modern techniques, this book explores the rich history and intricate processes behind creating stunning patterns on fabric. With step-by-step instructions and beautiful illustrations, the Bakers provide a comprehensive guide for both beginners and experienced artists, revealing the secrets of achieving vibrant colors and intricate designs. Whether you're a textile enthusiast or simply curious about the art of dyeing, this book will inspire and empower you to unleash your creativity and transform plain fabrics into works of art.

Genres: Art, Crafts, Textiles

Themes: Creativity, Cultural Heritage, Skill Development
