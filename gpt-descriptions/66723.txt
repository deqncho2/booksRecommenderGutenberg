Synopsis: 
In 'Stop, You're Killing Me!' by Stephen Marlowe, private detective Chester Drum finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he is hired to investigate the mysterious death of a wealthy businessman. As Drum delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a complex network of corruption, betrayal, and murder that reaches the highest levels of power. With his life on the line, Drum must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Corruption, Betrayal, Power
