Synopsis: In 'The Blue Star' by Pratt, Fletcher, a young man named Larry O'Keefe embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him from the bustling streets of New York City to the mystical realm of the Blue Star. As Larry becomes entangled in a web of love, danger, and ancient secrets, he must navigate a world where reality and fantasy intertwine, and where the fate of two worlds hangs in the balance.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Love and sacrifice, the power of imagination, the clash between reality and fantasy
