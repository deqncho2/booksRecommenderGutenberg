Synopsis: 

Step into the enchanting world of confectionery with 'The Candy Maker's Guide: A Collection of Choice Recipes for Sugar Boiling' by Fletcher Manufacturing Company. This delightful book takes readers on a mouthwatering journey through the art of candy making, revealing the secrets behind creating delectable treats that will captivate both young and old. From classic favorites like lollipops and caramels to innovative creations like flavored marshmallows and hand-pulled taffy, this guide offers a treasure trove of recipes that will inspire aspiring candy makers to unleash their creativity. With detailed instructions, helpful tips, and stunning illustrations, this book is a must-have for anyone with a sweet tooth and a passion for the culinary arts.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink, Non-fiction

Themes: Confectionery, Culinary Arts, Creativity
