Synopsis: In "The Sword of Antietam: A Story of the Nation's Crisis," Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on a gripping journey through the heart of the American Civil War. Set against the backdrop of the historic Battle of Antietam, the story follows a young Union soldier named Harry Kenton as he navigates the perils of war, grapples with his own personal demons, and discovers the true meaning of loyalty and honor. As the battle rages on, Harry's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test, leading him to make choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of the nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Courage, Personal Growth, Sacrifice
