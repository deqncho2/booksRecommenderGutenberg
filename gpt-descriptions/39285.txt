Synopsis: In "William Shakespeare, of Stratford-on-Avon: His Epitaph Unearthed, and the Author of the Plays run to Ground," author Scott F. Surtees delves into the mysterious life of the renowned playwright. Through meticulous research and compelling evidence, Surtees uncovers a hidden truth that challenges the traditional narrative surrounding Shakespeare's identity. As he unravels the enigma, the reader is taken on a captivating journey filled with intrigue, historical revelations, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Literary History, Conspiracy
