Synopsis: In 'Cousin Becky's Champions' by Eleanora H. Stooke, a young girl named Becky discovers a hidden talent for horseback riding when she visits her cousin's ranch for the summer. As she trains and competes in local horse shows, Becky forms unexpected friendships and faces challenges that test her determination and courage. With the support of her newfound champions, Becky embarks on a journey of self-discovery and learns valuable life lessons along the way.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Self-Discovery, Perseverance
