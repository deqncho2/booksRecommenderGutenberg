Synopsis: 
In 'Read-Aloud Plays' by Holley, Horace, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of engaging and thought-provoking plays. Each play presents a unique story, filled with intriguing characters and unexpected twists. From historical events to fantastical adventures, these read-aloud plays are sure to captivate readers of all ages. With vivid dialogue and imaginative settings, this collection brings the magic of theater right into the reader's hands. Get ready to be transported to different worlds and experience the power of storytelling through these captivating read-aloud plays.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Imagination, History, Identity, Morality
