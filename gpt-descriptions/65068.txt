Synopsis: 
In 'Terrible Tractoration, and Other Poems' by Fessenden, Thomas Green, prepare to embark on a whimsical journey through a collection of satirical and humorous poems. From the misadventures of a mischievous tractor to witty observations on society, Fessenden's clever verses will keep readers entertained and laughing throughout. With a delightful blend of wit, wordplay, and social commentary, this collection is sure to captivate and amuse poetry enthusiasts and humor lovers alike.

Genres: 
Satire, Humor, Poetry

Themes: 
Social commentary, Wit, Wordplay
