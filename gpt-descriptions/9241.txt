Synopsis: In "John Inglefield's Thanksgiving," Nathaniel Hawthorne weaves a captivating tale of a man haunted by a mysterious secret that threatens to unravel his life on Thanksgiving Day. As the day unfolds, John Inglefield finds himself torn between the joyous celebrations around him and the weight of his hidden burden. With a sense of impending doom, he must navigate through the festivities, desperately hoping to keep his secret hidden. This gripping story explores the complexities of guilt, redemption, and the power of truth.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, mystery

Themes: Guilt, redemption, secrets, the power of truth
