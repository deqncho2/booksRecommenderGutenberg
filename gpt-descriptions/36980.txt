Synopsis: 

'Papers from Overlook-House' by Beasley, Frederic W. is a gripping psychological thriller that follows the mysterious events surrounding a secluded mansion. When a young journalist stumbles upon a collection of old papers hidden within the walls of Overlook-House, she becomes obsessed with uncovering the truth behind the house's dark history. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the past, she finds herself entangled in a web of deception, danger, and supernatural occurrences. With each page turned, the tension builds, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to unravel the enigma that lies within the walls of Overlook-House.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Obsession, Supernatural phenomena
