Synopsis: In 'Little Visits with Great Americans, Vol. 2 (of 2): Or Success, Ideals and How to Attain Them', readers embark on a captivating journey through the lives of remarkable individuals who have achieved great success. From renowned inventors and artists to influential leaders and thinkers, this collection of inspiring anecdotes offers glimpses into the personal experiences and philosophies of these extraordinary figures. Through these encounters, readers are encouraged to reflect on their own aspirations and discover the keys to attaining their own ideals.

Genres: Biography, Self-help, Inspirational

Themes: Success, Achievement, Ideals, Personal Growth
