Synopsis: 
'White Mountain Trails: Tales of the Trails to the Summit of Mount Washington and other Summits of the White Hills' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the breathtaking landscapes of the White Mountains. Join the author, Winthrop Packard, as he recounts his thrilling adventures and encounters with nature's wonders while conquering the challenging trails leading to the summit of Mount Washington and other majestic peaks. From treacherous weather conditions to unexpected encounters with wildlife, Packard's tales will leave readers on the edge of their seats, yearning to experience the beauty and excitement of the White Mountains for themselves.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Memoir

Themes: Nature exploration, Outdoor adventure, Personal growth
