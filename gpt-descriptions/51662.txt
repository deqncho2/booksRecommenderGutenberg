Synopsis: 
In 'Breakdown' by Kastle, Herbert D., a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger when he witnesses a mysterious event that shakes the foundation of his reality. As he delves deeper into the truth, he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where nothing is as it seems and trust becomes a luxury he can't afford. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to uncover the shocking truth behind the breakdown of his world.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Conspiracy, Identity, Deception, Paranoia
