Synopsis: In 'Beyond the City' by Arthur Conan Doyle, a young woman named Violet Hunter finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she takes up a position as a governess in a seemingly idyllic country house. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the walls, she becomes entangled in a thrilling tale of love, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, Violet must navigate the treacherous path between truth and deception, ultimately discovering that not everything is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Deception, Love, Betrayal
