Synopsis: In 'Joseph the Dreamer' by Amy Steedman, readers are transported to ancient Egypt where they follow the captivating journey of Joseph, a young dreamer with a special gift. From his humble beginnings as a shepherd to his rise as a trusted advisor to the Pharaoh, Joseph's life is filled with unexpected twists and turns. As he navigates through trials and tribulations, Joseph's unwavering faith and determination become his guiding light. This enchanting tale of dreams, destiny, and resilience will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what lies ahead for Joseph.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biblical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Dreams and Prophecy, Faith and Belief, Overcoming Adversity
