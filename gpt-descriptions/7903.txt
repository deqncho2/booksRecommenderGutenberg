Synopsis: In 'American Historical and Literary Curiosities, Part 03', Smith and Watson take readers on a captivating journey through lesser-known stories and intriguing anecdotes from American history and literature. From the mysterious disappearance of a famous author to the surprising origins of iconic American symbols, this book uncovers fascinating curiosities that will leave readers both entertained and enlightened. Delve into the hidden corners of American history and literature, where unexpected tales and remarkable characters await.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Literature

Themes: American history, American literature, Curiosities, Hidden stories
