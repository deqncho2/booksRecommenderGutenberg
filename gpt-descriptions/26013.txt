Synopsis: 
Step into the world of the Northern Nut Growers Association as they gather for their 41st Annual Meeting in Pleasant Valley, New York in 1950. Delve into the fascinating discussions, presentations, and debates surrounding the cultivation and propagation of nut trees. From the latest advancements in hickory breeding to the challenges of walnut blight, this report offers a captivating glimpse into the world of nut growers and their tireless efforts to expand the possibilities of nut cultivation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Horticulture, Agriculture

Themes: Nut cultivation, Horticultural advancements, Agricultural challenges
