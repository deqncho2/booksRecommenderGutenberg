Synopsis: In 'The Abandoned of Yan' by Donald F. Daley, a young archaeologist named Emily embarks on a thrilling expedition to the remote and mysterious region of Yan. As she delves deeper into the ancient ruins, she uncovers a dark secret that has been buried for centuries. With danger lurking at every turn, Emily must navigate treacherous terrain and outsmart ruthless adversaries to unravel the truth and protect the ancient knowledge that lies within the abandoned city.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Secrets and Betrayal, Preservation of History
