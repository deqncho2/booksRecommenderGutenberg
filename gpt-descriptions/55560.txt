Synopsis: In 'The Mercer Boys' Mystery Case' by Wyckoff, Capwell, the Mercer brothers find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon a hidden treasure map. As they follow the clues, they encounter dangerous obstacles, unexpected allies, and a web of secrets that could change their lives forever. With time running out, the Mercer boys must use their wits and bravery to unravel the mystery and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Treasure hunting, Brotherhood, Courage
