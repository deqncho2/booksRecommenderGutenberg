Synopsis: In 'The Radio Gunner' by Forbes, Alexander, a young man finds himself thrust into the heart of World War II as a radio gunner on a bomber plane. As he navigates the treacherous skies, he must confront the harsh realities of war, the bonds of brotherhood, and the sacrifices that come with duty. With each mission, he becomes more entangled in a web of danger and uncertainty, testing his courage and resilience. Will he survive the relentless onslaught of enemy fire and make it back home?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: War, Courage, Brotherhood, Sacrifice
