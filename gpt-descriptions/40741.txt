Synopsis: In 'Paul and His Dog, v.1', Paul de Kock takes readers on a delightful journey through the streets of Paris, where a charming and mischievous dog named Fido becomes the center of attention. As Fido's antics bring joy and chaos to Paul's life, the story unfolds with humor, heartwarming moments, and unexpected twists. This first volume of the series introduces readers to the endearing bond between Paul and Fido, as they navigate the bustling city and encounter a colorful cast of characters. With its lively narrative and lovable canine protagonist, 'Paul and His Dog, v.1' promises an entertaining and heartwarming tale that will leave readers eagerly awaiting the next installment.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, City Life
