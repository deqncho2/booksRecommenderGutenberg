Synopsis: 
In 'Trails of the Pathfinders', George Bird Grinnell takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of North America. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, he delves into the lives of legendary explorers and pioneers who braved the unknown, facing perilous challenges and forging new paths. From the rugged landscapes of the Rocky Mountains to the vast expanses of the Great Plains, this enthralling narrative unveils the triumphs and tragedies of those who shaped the history of the American frontier.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: 
Exploration, Survival, Frontier life
