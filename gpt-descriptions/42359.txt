Synopsis: 
'Tales and Legends of the English Lakes' by Armistead Wilson is a captivating collection of folklore and mythical stories set in the enchanting landscapes of the English Lakes. From ancient legends of mystical creatures to haunting tales of lost love and tragedy, this book takes readers on a journey through the rich tapestry of folklore that has shaped the region for centuries. As the stories unfold, readers will be transported to a world where reality and fantasy intertwine, where the beauty of nature hides secrets and where the past echoes in the present. 

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Mythology, Nature, Love and Loss
