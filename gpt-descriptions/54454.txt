Synopsis: 

In "Dirty Dustbins and Sloppy Streets: A Practical Treatise on the Scavenging and Cleansing of Cities and Towns," author H. Percy Boulnois takes readers on a fascinating journey into the hidden world of urban cleanliness. Through vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, Boulnois uncovers the secrets behind the management of waste and the maintenance of cleanliness in bustling cities and towns. From the bustling streets of London to the narrow alleyways of Paris, this book offers a unique perspective on the unsung heroes who work tirelessly to keep our urban environments clean and hygienic. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Urban Studies, Environmental Studies

Themes: Urban sanitation, Waste management, Public health
