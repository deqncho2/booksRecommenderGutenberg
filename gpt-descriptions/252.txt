Synopsis: 

Delve into the intricate world of copyright law with the comprehensive guide, 'Copyright Law of the United States of America: Contained in Title 17 of the United States Code.' Authored by the United States Library of Congress, Copyright Office, this enlightening work explores the legal framework that governs intellectual property rights in the United States. From the protection of creative works to the enforcement of copyright infringement, this book provides a detailed examination of the laws and regulations that shape the creative landscape. Discover the fascinating interplay between artistic expression and legal protection, as this essential resource navigates the complexities of copyright law.

Genres: Legal, Reference

Themes: Intellectual property, Creative expression, Legal protection
