Synopsis: In 'A Sheaf' by John Galsworthy, a seemingly ordinary man named Richard Larch is faced with a life-altering decision that will challenge his values and test the boundaries of his relationships. As he grapples with the consequences of his choices, the story delves into the complexities of human nature and the power of personal conviction. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking dilemmas, 'A Sheaf' takes readers on a captivating journey of self-discovery and moral introspection.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Morality, Personal Responsibility, Consequences of Actions
