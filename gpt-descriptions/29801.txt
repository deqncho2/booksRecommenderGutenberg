Synopsis: In 'A Short History of eBooks' by Marie Lebert, embark on a captivating journey through time as the author delves into the fascinating evolution of electronic books. From the humble beginnings of digitized texts to the revolutionary impact of e-readers and online platforms, Lebert uncovers the untold stories behind the rise of this modern literary phenomenon. Discover the pioneers, the challenges faced, and the transformative power of eBooks that has forever changed the way we read and access knowledge.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Technological advancements, Publishing industry, Accessibility of knowledge
