Synopsis: 

'Narcissism Book of Quotes: A Selection of Quotes from the Collective Wisdom of over 12,000 Individual Discussions' is a captivating compilation that delves into the intricate world of narcissism. Through a carefully curated selection of quotes, this book offers a unique insight into the minds of individuals who have experienced and discussed narcissism firsthand. From poignant reflections to thought-provoking revelations, these quotes provide a glimpse into the complexities of narcissistic behavior and its impact on both the narcissist and those around them. 

Genres: Self-help, Psychology, Non-fiction

Themes: Narcissism, Personal growth, Relationships, Self-awareness
