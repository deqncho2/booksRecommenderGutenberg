Synopsis: 
In 'Lefty Locke, pitcher-manager' by Standish, Burt L., follow the thrilling journey of Lefty Locke, a talented baseball player who finds himself thrust into the role of both pitcher and manager. As he navigates the challenges of leading his team to victory, Lefty must confront personal demons, face unexpected obstacles, and make difficult decisions that will shape his future in the game. With high-stakes games, intense rivalries, and a cast of colorful characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final pitch.

Genres: Sports fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Leadership, Perseverance, Identity, Teamwork
