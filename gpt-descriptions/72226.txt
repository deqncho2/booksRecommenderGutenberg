Synopsis: In "The First American King" by Hastings, George Gordon, a young and ambitious lawyer named Benjamin finds himself thrust into a world of power, intrigue, and danger when he is unexpectedly chosen to serve as the advisor to the newly crowned American king. As Benjamin navigates the treacherous waters of politics and uncovers dark secrets that threaten to tear the nation apart, he must make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of a nation and test his own loyalty. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale explores the price of power and the sacrifices one must make to protect what they hold dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Drama

Themes: Power, Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity, Sacrifice
