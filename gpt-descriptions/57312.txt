Synopsis: In 'The Nether Millstone' by Fred M. White, a mysterious murder takes place in a small village, leaving the residents shocked and fearful. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, and the dark underbelly of the seemingly idyllic community is exposed. With twists and turns at every corner, the reader is drawn into a web of suspense and intrigue, wondering who can be trusted and what lies beneath the surface of this picturesque town.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Small-town secrets, Deception, Betrayal
