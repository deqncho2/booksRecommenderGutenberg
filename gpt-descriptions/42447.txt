Synopsis: 
In 'The Heart of the White Mountains, Their Legend and Scenery: Tourist's Edition', Samuel Adams Drake takes readers on a captivating journey through the majestic White Mountains. With vivid descriptions and intriguing legends, Drake paints a breathtaking picture of this enchanting region, enticing readers to explore its hidden wonders. From the awe-inspiring peaks to the mysterious tales that have been passed down through generations, this book is a must-read for anyone seeking adventure and a deeper understanding of the White Mountains' allure.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Nature's beauty, Exploration, Folklore and legends
