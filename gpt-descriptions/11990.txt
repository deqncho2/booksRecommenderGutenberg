Synopsis: In 'Shakespeare's Insomnia, and the Causes Thereof' by Franklin H. Head, delve into the mysterious and enigmatic world of William Shakespeare's sleepless nights. This captivating work explores the haunting reasons behind the Bard's insomnia, taking readers on a journey through his troubled mind and the secrets that lie within. As the pages unfold, a web of intrigue and hidden truths emerges, leaving readers eager to uncover the dark forces that keep Shakespeare awake at night.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Literary Analysis

Themes: Sleeplessness, Creativity, Psychological Turmoil
