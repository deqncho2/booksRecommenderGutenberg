Synopsis: 
In this captivating and informative illustrated edition, J. S. Rarey shares his revolutionary techniques for taming horses, drawing from his renowned lectures at the Round House. With additional chapters on horsemanship and hunting, this book is a must-read for both the young and timid equestrians. Rarey's expertise and insights will captivate readers as they delve into the art of horse taming and the intricacies of horsemanship.

Genres: Non-fiction, Instructional, Biography

Themes: Animal training, Horsemanship, Personal growth
