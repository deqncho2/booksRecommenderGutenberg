Synopsis:
In "Cappy Ricks; Or, the Subjugation of Matt Peasley," the ambitious and determined Matt Peasley finds himself caught in a whirlwind of adventure and unexpected challenges when he joins the Blue Star Navigation Company. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the shipping industry, Matt must contend with the eccentric and cunning Cappy Ricks, the company's founder and his new boss. With Cappy's unorthodox methods and relentless pursuit of success, Matt is pushed to his limits and forced to confront his own values and ambitions. Will Matt be able to prove himself and earn Cappy's respect, or will he be forever subjugated by the wily old sea captain?

Genres: Adventure, Maritime Fiction, Business Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Mentorship, Ethics, Success
