Synopsis: In 'Satan Absolved: A Victorian Mystery' by Wilfrid Scawen Blunt, a renowned poet and diplomat, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges in Victorian London. As the city grapples with a series of unexplained deaths and strange occurrences, a detective is drawn into a web of intrigue and darkness. With each clue leading deeper into the heart of the occult, the detective must confront his own beliefs and face the possibility that evil forces may be at work. Blending elements of mystery, supernatural, and historical fiction, this gripping tale explores the battle between good and evil in the backdrop of Victorian society.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Historical Fiction

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Occult, Victorian Society
