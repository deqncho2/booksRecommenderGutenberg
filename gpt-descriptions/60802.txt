Synopsis:
In 'Colin' by E. F. Benson, a young man named Colin embarks on a journey that takes him from the bustling streets of London to the enchanting landscapes of Italy. Along the way, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own secrets and desires. As Colin delves deeper into this world of mystery and intrigue, he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and unexpected twists. Will he be able to navigate the treacherous path before him and uncover the truth that lies beneath the surface?

Genres:
Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes:
Love and betrayal, Secrets and deception, Self-discovery
