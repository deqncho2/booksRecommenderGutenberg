Synopsis: In 'The Courtship of Morrice Buckler: A Romance', Morrice Buckler, a young and ambitious lawyer, finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and romance when he becomes the legal advisor to a wealthy and eccentric family. As he delves deeper into their affairs, he uncovers long-held secrets, hidden desires, and a love that defies societal expectations. With danger lurking around every corner, Morrice must navigate the treacherous waters of love and loyalty, all while trying to protect his own heart.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Family secrets, Society's expectations
