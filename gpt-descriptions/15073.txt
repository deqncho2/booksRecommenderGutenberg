Synopsis: In 'The Colossus: A Novel' by Read, Opie Percival, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges from the depths of the ocean, captivating the attention of the world. As the colossal being wreaks havoc and destruction, a group of unlikely heroes must come together to uncover the truth behind its existence and find a way to stop it. With thrilling action, unexpected twists, and thought-provoking revelations, this gripping novel takes readers on a thrilling journey that explores the depths of human nature and the power of unity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Humanity's relationship with nature, the consequences of unchecked power, the strength of collective action
