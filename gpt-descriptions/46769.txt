Synopsis: In "Flower o' the Heather: A Story of the Killing Times" by Robert William MacKenna, a young woman named Kirsty finds herself caught in the midst of the turbulent Killing Times in 17th century Scotland. As she navigates the dangerous political and religious landscape, Kirsty's life becomes intertwined with a group of rebels fighting for their freedom. With her courage and determination, Kirsty must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and the fate of those she loves.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Religious persecution, Rebellion, Love and sacrifice
