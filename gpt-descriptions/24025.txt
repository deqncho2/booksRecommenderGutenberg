Synopsis: 
"The New Girl at St. Chad's: A Story of School Life" by Angela Brazil follows the journey of a young girl named Monica as she enters the prestigious St. Chad's boarding school. As Monica navigates the challenges of fitting in and making friends, she discovers a hidden world of secrets, rivalries, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, Monica's determination and resilience are put to the test, as she learns valuable lessons about loyalty, self-discovery, and the true meaning of friendship.

Genres: School life, coming-of-age, mystery

Themes: Friendship, identity, resilience, loyalty
