Synopsis: In 'The Protector' by Harold Bindloss, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small Canadian town, catching the attention of the locals. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the townspeople, secrets from the past begin to resurface, leading to unexpected consequences. With elements of suspense and romance, this gripping tale explores the complexities of trust, loyalty, and the lengths one will go to protect those they care about.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Trust, Loyalty, Protection
