Synopsis: 
Step into the world of the Northern Nut Growers Association as they gather for their second annual meeting in Ithaca, New York in 1911. Delve into the fascinating discussions and presentations on various nut-growing topics, from the cultivation of rare and exotic nut trees to the latest advancements in nut processing techniques. Discover the passion and dedication of these nut enthusiasts as they share their knowledge and experiences, and witness the birth of a community united by their love for nuts and their desire to promote their cultivation. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Horticulture, Agriculture

Themes: Nut cultivation, Horticultural advancements, Community building
