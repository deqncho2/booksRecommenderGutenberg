Synopsis: In 'Amour, Amour, Dear Planet!' by Clutter, Mark, a young astronomer named Alex embarks on a thrilling journey across the galaxy to save Earth from an impending cosmic disaster. As he encounters strange alien civilizations, battles interstellar forces, and unravels the mysteries of the universe, Alex discovers the true power of love and the interconnectedness of all beings. With heart-pounding action, mind-bending twists, and a touch of romance, this captivating sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Love, Exploration, Interconnectedness
