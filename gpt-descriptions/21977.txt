Synopsis: 
In 'The Narrative of a Blockade-Runner' by Wilkinson, J., readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the dangerous world of smuggling during the American Civil War. The story follows the daring exploits of a blockade-runner, a man who risks his life to transport goods through enemy lines. As he navigates treacherous waters and evades capture, the protagonist must confront the moral dilemmas and personal sacrifices that come with his chosen profession. With each high-stakes mission, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, wondering if he will succeed or face the dire consequences of his actions.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Sacrifice, Survival
