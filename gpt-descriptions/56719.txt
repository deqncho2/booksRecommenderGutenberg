Synopsis: In 'Mimi's Marriage' by Veselitskaia, Lidiia Ivanovna, we follow the captivating journey of Mimi, a young woman who finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires. Set in a vibrant and changing world, Mimi navigates the complexities of love, family, and personal growth, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. As Mimi's story unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the true meaning of marriage is explored in a way that will leave readers questioning their own beliefs and choices.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, societal expectations, personal growth, family dynamics
