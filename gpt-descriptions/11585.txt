Synopsis: In "The Young Emigrants; Madelaine Tube; the Boy and the Book; and Crystal Palace," Susan Anne Livingston Ridley Sedgwick takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of young emigrants, a mysterious girl named Madelaine Tube, a boy and his magical book, and the grandeur of the Crystal Palace. As these interconnected stories unfold, secrets are revealed, friendships are formed, and unexpected adventures await. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to different worlds and immersed in the lives of these intriguing characters.

Genres: Historical fiction, adventure, mystery

Themes: Coming of age, friendship, discovery, resilience
