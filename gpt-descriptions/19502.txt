Synopsis:
In 'Frank Merriwell's Chums' by Burt L. Standish, Frank Merriwell, the charismatic and talented athlete, finds himself facing a new set of challenges as he enters college. Alongside his loyal group of friends, Frank navigates the complexities of campus life, including academic pressures, romantic entanglements, and unexpected rivalries. As they strive to maintain their integrity and uphold their values, Frank and his chums must confront personal demons and make difficult choices that will shape their futures. With a mix of thrilling sports competitions, intriguing mysteries, and heartfelt friendships, this captivating novel takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the trials and triumphs of young adulthood.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Integrity, Personal growth
