Synopsis: In the heart of 18th century Japan, a young woman finds herself thrust into a world of political intrigue and danger when she becomes the target of a ruthless assassin. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of feudal Japan, she must uncover the truth about her own identity and the secrets that could change the course of history. With the fate of an empire hanging in the balance, she must summon the strength and courage to protect herself and those she loves, even if it means defying tradition and challenging the most powerful forces in the land.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Power, Loyalty, Tradition, Courage
