Synopsis: In 'A Modern Wizard' by Ottolengui, Rodrigues, a brilliant and enigmatic magician named Victor embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient artifact. As he delves deeper into the world of magic, he must confront his own demons and navigate a web of intrigue and danger. With a mix of illusion, mystery, and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Magic, Identity, Redemption
