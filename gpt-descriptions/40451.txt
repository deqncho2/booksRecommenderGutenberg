Synopsis: In 'Under One Flag' by Richard Marsh, a mysterious and enigmatic figure arrives in a small English village, captivating the residents with his charm and charisma. As the villagers become increasingly infatuated with him, they soon discover that his true intentions are far from benevolent. With secrets and hidden agendas unraveling, the once peaceful community is thrown into chaos, and the residents must confront their darkest fears to protect their way of life.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Manipulation, Community Dynamics
