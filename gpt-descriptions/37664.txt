Synopsis: In "The Sins of the Children: A Novel" by Hamilton Cosmo, a small town is rocked by a shocking crime that exposes the dark secrets hidden within seemingly perfect families. As the truth unravels, the lives of the town's residents become entangled in a web of betrayal, guilt, and redemption. With each chapter, the reader is drawn deeper into the complex relationships and haunting pasts of the characters, leading to a gripping climax that will leave them questioning the nature of sin and the lengths people will go to protect their loved ones.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Guilt, Redemption
