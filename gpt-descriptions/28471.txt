Synopsis: In 'Man And His Ancestor: A Study In Evolution', Charles Morris takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of human evolution. From the earliest hominids to modern humans, Morris explores the fascinating discoveries and theories that have shaped our understanding of our ancestors. Through engaging storytelling and meticulous research, he delves into the complex web of evolutionary processes that have shaped the human species. This thought-provoking exploration of our ancestral roots will leave readers with a deeper appreciation for the remarkable journey that has led to the existence of humankind.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Anthropology

Themes: Evolution, Human Origins, Scientific Inquiry
