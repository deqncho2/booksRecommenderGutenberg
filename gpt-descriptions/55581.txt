Synopsis: 

In 'Trial of the Major War Criminals Before the International Military Tribunal, Nuremberg, 14 November 1945-1 October 1946, Volume 07', the courtroom becomes a stage for justice as the most notorious figures of the Nazi regime face trial for their heinous crimes. As the proceedings unfold, shocking revelations and gripping testimonies shed light on the extent of the atrocities committed during World War II. With the world watching, the trial becomes a pivotal moment in history, challenging the notion of accountability and the pursuit of justice in the face of unimaginable evil.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Legal drama, True crime

Themes: War crimes, Justice, Accountability, Human rights, Historical memory
