Synopsis: In 'Miss Billy: A Neighborhood Story', readers are introduced to Billy Neil, a young woman who finds herself suddenly orphaned and alone. Determined to make her own way in the world, Billy moves to a new neighborhood and begins to build a life for herself. Along the way, she encounters a colorful cast of characters, including a charming musician, a wise old woman, and a mischievous little boy. As Billy navigates the ups and downs of her new life, she discovers the power of friendship, love, and the importance of embracing the unexpected.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Independence, Community, Love, Self-discovery
