Synopsis: In 'The Combined Maze' by Sinclair, May, a group of strangers find themselves trapped in a mysterious maze that defies logic and challenges their very existence. As they navigate through its ever-shifting corridors, they must confront their deepest fears and unravel the secrets of the maze before it consumes them all. With each twist and turn, the characters are forced to question their reality and the true nature of the maze itself. Will they find a way out or become forever lost in this enigmatic labyrinth?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Survival
