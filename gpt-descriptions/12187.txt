Synopsis: 
In 'The Mystery of 31 New Inn', a young lawyer named Mr. John Bellingham is found dead under mysterious circumstances, leaving behind a perplexing puzzle for his friend, Mr. Paul Harley, to unravel. As Harley delves deeper into the case, he discovers a web of secrets, hidden identities, and a sinister plot that reaches far beyond the confines of 31 New Inn. With each twist and turn, the truth becomes more elusive, and the danger more palpable. Will Harley be able to solve the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Intrigue
