Synopsis: In 'The Alternate Plan' by Maddren Gerry, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden world of secrets and conspiracies, forcing him to question everything he thought he knew. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with danger and betrayal, as he races to unveil the truth and protect his invention from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific advancement, Conspiracy, Betrayal
