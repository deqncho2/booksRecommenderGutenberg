Synopsis: In 'Do Unto Others' by Mark Clifton, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, offering the residents a chance to fulfill their deepest desires. However, as they quickly discover, every wish comes with a price. As the town's darkest secrets are exposed, the residents must confront their own morality and the consequences of their actions. With a gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this thought-provoking tale explores the lengths people will go to for personal gain and the power of redemption.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Morality, Desire, Consequences
