Synopsis: In 'The Belgian Twins' by Lucy Fitch Perkins, we follow the captivating journey of Jan and Marie, two adventurous twins living in Belgium during World War I. As they navigate the challenges and dangers of war, they encounter unexpected friendships, face personal growth, and discover the power of resilience. With their unwavering spirit and determination, the Belgian Twins embark on a remarkable adventure that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War, Friendship, Resilience, Personal Growth
