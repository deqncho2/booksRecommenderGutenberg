Synopsis: In 'Modern Cosmogonies', Agnes M. Clerke takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of cosmology. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, she explores the various theories and explanations that scientists have proposed to understand the origins and structure of the universe. From ancient myths to cutting-edge scientific discoveries, this book unveils the awe-inspiring mysteries of the cosmos, leaving readers with a profound sense of wonder and curiosity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Astronomy

Themes: Origins of the universe, Scientific theories, Cosmology, Human curiosity
