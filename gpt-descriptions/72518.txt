Synopsis: In "The Minister Had to Wait" by Roger D. Aycock, a small-town minister finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries when a stranger arrives in town, seeking redemption. As the minister's faith is tested and his past comes back to haunt him, he must navigate the complexities of love, forgiveness, and the power of second chances. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the captivating world of this suspenseful and thought-provoking tale.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Drama

Themes: Redemption, Faith, Secrets, Forgiveness
