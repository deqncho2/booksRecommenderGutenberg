Synopsis: 
In "Grace Harlowe's Senior Year at High School" by Josephine Chase, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside Grace Harlowe as she navigates her final year of high school. Faced with new challenges, unexpected friendships, and a series of mysterious events, Grace must summon her courage and determination to uncover the truth. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, Grace's senior year becomes a rollercoaster of emotions, tests of loyalty, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Identity, Trust
