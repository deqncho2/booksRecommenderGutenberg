Synopsis: In 'The Flower Princess' by Abbie Farwell Brown, a young girl named Rosamond discovers a hidden world of enchantment when she stumbles upon a magical flower. As she embarks on a journey filled with wonder and danger, Rosamond must navigate through a realm where flowers come to life and secrets are waiting to be unraveled. With the fate of the flower kingdom hanging in the balance, Rosamond must summon her courage and embrace her destiny to save the ones she loves.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Magic and enchantment, Courage and destiny, Love and sacrifice
