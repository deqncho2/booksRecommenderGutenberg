Synopsis: In "The Maker of Rainbows, and Other Fairy-tales and Fables" by Le Gallienne, Richard, embark on a magical journey through a collection of enchanting fairy-tales and fables. From a mysterious rainbow-maker to a mischievous talking cat, these captivating stories will transport readers to whimsical worlds filled with wonder and imagination. Each tale holds a hidden message, inviting readers to reflect on the power of love, the importance of kindness, and the beauty of embracing one's true self. Prepare to be spellbound by this delightful collection that will leave you believing in the magic of storytelling.

Genres: Fantasy, Fairy-tales, Fables

Themes: Love, Kindness, Self-discovery
