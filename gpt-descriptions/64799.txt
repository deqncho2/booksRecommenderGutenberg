Synopsis: In 'Equation for Time' by R. R. Winterbotham, a brilliant mathematician named Dr. Emily Carter stumbles upon a mysterious equation that seems to hold the key to manipulating time itself. As she delves deeper into her research, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and conspiracies, where the line between science and fiction blurs. With time running out, Dr. Carter must race against the clock to unlock the equation's true potential and protect it from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Time manipulation, Scientific discovery, Conspiracy
