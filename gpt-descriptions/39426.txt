Synopsis: In "Rupert, Prince Palatine" by Eva Scott, a young prince finds himself torn between duty and desire when he is unexpectedly thrust into a world of political intrigue and forbidden love. Set against the backdrop of 17th century Europe, Rupert must navigate treacherous alliances and dangerous secrets as he fights for his own happiness and the future of his kingdom. As he grapples with his own identity and the sacrifices he must make, Rupert's journey becomes a thrilling tale of passion, power, and the enduring strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Intrigue

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Identity, Sacrifice, Power
