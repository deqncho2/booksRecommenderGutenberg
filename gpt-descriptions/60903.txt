Synopsis: In 'The Bradys' Race for Life; or, Rounding Up a Tough Trio: A Detective Story of Life' by Francis Worcester Doughty, the Bradys find themselves embroiled in a thrilling race against time. When a mysterious trio threatens the life of a prominent figure, the Bradys must use their detective skills to uncover the truth and prevent a tragedy. As they delve deeper into the case, they encounter unexpected twists and turns, leading them on a suspenseful journey through the underbelly of society. With danger lurking at every corner, the Bradys must rely on their wit and resourcefulness to solve the mystery before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Crime and Justice, Loyalty and Betrayal, Perseverance
