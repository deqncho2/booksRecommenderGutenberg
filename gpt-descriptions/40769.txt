Synopsis: In the 11th edition of the renowned Encyclopaedia Britannica, journey through Volume 15, Slice 7 as it delves into the fascinating world of "Kelly, Edward" to "Kite". This captivating section takes readers on a whirlwind exploration of the life and achievements of Edward Kelly, a mysterious figure from the Elizabethan era, and then seamlessly transitions to unravel the history, construction, and cultural significance of kites. From the enigmatic alchemist to the soaring kite, this volume offers a captivating blend of historical intrigue and scientific wonder, leaving readers eager to uncover the secrets and stories behind these two vastly different subjects.

Genres: Reference, History, Science

Themes: Biography, Exploration, Invention, Cultural Significance
