Synopsis: 

'Our Legal Heritage: King AEthelbert - King George III, 600 A.D. - 1776: June 2011 (Sixth) Edition' by Reilly, S. A. takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of English law from the time of King AEthelbert to King George III. This comprehensive and meticulously researched book explores the legal systems, customs, and landmark cases that shaped the legal landscape of England over centuries. From the establishment of common law to the struggles for individual rights and the influence of the Magna Carta, this book delves into the fascinating history of English law, shedding light on the pivotal moments and influential figures that have shaped the legal system we know today.

Genres: Legal history, Non-fiction, Historical

Themes: Evolution of law, Legal systems, Individual rights, Historical influence
