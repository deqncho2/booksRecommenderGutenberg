Synopsis: In the small town of Old Chester, Miss Gibbie Gault is a beloved figure known for her kindness and generosity. But beneath her gentle exterior lies a secret that threatens to unravel the community she holds dear. As the town grapples with the consequences of Miss Gibbie's past, friendships are tested, secrets are revealed, and the true power of forgiveness is put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Redemption, Community, Secrets, Forgiveness
