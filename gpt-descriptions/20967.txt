Synopsis: 

'A Guide to Peterborough Cathedral' is a captivating exploration of the rich history and architectural wonders of one of England's most iconic cathedrals. Drawing from the works of renowned historians and original documents, author George S. Phillips takes readers on a journey through time, from the monastery's humble beginnings to its present-day grandeur. With vivid descriptions and meticulous attention to detail, the book unveils the cathedral's unique architectural features and highlights the recent improvements that have enhanced its beauty. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Architecture

Themes: History, Architecture, Religious Heritage
