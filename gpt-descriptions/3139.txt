Synopsis: In 'The Dove in the Eagle's Nest', Charlotte M. Yonge takes us on a captivating journey to medieval Germany, where a young English girl named Thekla finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the struggle for power. As she navigates the treacherous world of knights, castles, and courtly manners, Thekla must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and the fate of those around her. With its rich historical backdrop and complex characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Sacrifice, Identity and Self-discovery
