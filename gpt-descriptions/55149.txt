Synopsis: 
'Michigan's Copper Country in Early Photos' by Tyler, B. E. takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the historic Copper Country region of Michigan. Through a collection of early photographs, this book offers a unique glimpse into the lives of the people who lived and worked in this bustling mining community during the late 19th and early 20th centuries. From the towering mine shafts to the vibrant streets, the photographs vividly depict the challenges, triumphs, and everyday moments of a bygone era. This book is a fascinating exploration of a forgotten time and place, inviting readers to step back in time and discover the rich history of Michigan's Copper Country.

Genres: Historical photography, Local history, Americana

Themes: Industrialization, Community, Everyday life
