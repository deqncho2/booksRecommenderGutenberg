Synopsis: In "Arab and Druze at Home," William Ewing takes readers on a captivating journey through the lands east of the Jordan River, delving into the lives and cultures of the Arab and Druze peoples. Through his vivid descriptions and personal encounters, Ewing offers a unique perspective on the region, its landscapes, traditions, and the fascinating individuals he meets along the way. From the bustling markets of Damascus to the remote villages nestled in the mountains, this travelogue provides an immersive experience that will leave readers yearning to explore the enchanting East.

Genres: Travelogue, Cultural Exploration, Memoir

Themes: Cross-cultural encounters, Exploration of Middle Eastern cultures, Adventure and discovery
