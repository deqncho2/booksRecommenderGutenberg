Synopsis: In "The Hungry Stones, and Other Stories" by Rabindranath Tagore, readers are transported to a world where reality and fantasy intertwine. From a mysterious palace that holds a haunting secret to a man's encounter with a supernatural being, these stories delve into the depths of human emotions and desires. As the characters navigate through their own personal journeys, they are confronted with the power of love, the complexities of identity, and the blurred boundaries between the seen and the unseen. Each tale is a captivating exploration of the human spirit, leaving readers spellbound and questioning the nature of their own existence.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Magical Realism

Themes: Love, Identity, Reality vs. Illusion
