Synopsis: In 'The Common Law' by Oliver Wendell Holmes, a young woman named Sally finds herself entangled in a complex legal battle that challenges societal norms and questions the very essence of justice. As she navigates the intricacies of the legal system, Sally must confront her own desires and ambitions, ultimately leading her to question the true meaning of love and happiness. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking dilemmas, this gripping novel explores the complexities of human relationships and the ever-evolving nature of the law.

Genres: Legal fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Justice, Individualism, Love and Relationships
