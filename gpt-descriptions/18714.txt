Synopsis: 
In 'Abe and Mawruss: Being Further Adventures of Potash and Perlmutter', the dynamic duo of Abe and Mawruss return with their humorous and shrewd business escapades. This time, they find themselves navigating the treacherous waters of the garment industry, facing unexpected challenges and hilarious misadventures. As they strive to outwit their competitors and maintain their success, Abe and Mawruss must rely on their wit, resourcefulness, and unbreakable friendship.

Genres: Humor, Business, Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Entrepreneurship, Competition
