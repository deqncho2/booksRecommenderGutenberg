Synopsis: 
In 'Marcella' by Ward, Humphry, Mrs., a young woman named Marcella finds herself torn between duty and desire as she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations. Set in the late 19th century, Marcella's journey takes her from the opulent halls of London's high society to the rugged landscapes of the Scottish Highlands. As she grapples with her own desires and the expectations placed upon her, Marcella must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Duty and Responsibility, Societal Expectations
