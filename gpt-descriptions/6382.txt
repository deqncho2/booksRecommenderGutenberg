Synopsis: In the heart of Africa, a mysterious figure known as "Bat Wing" emerges, wreaking havoc and instilling fear among the locals. When a young American detective arrives to investigate, he finds himself entangled in a web of deception, danger, and a love triangle that could cost him everything. As he delves deeper into the secrets of Bat Wing, he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his own life but the fate of an entire nation.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Colonialism, Identity, Betrayal
