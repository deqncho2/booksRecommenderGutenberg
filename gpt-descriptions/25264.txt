Synopsis: 

In "Newfoundland and the Jingoes: An Appeal to England's Honor" by John Fretwell, a gripping tale unfolds as the author delves into the complex relationship between Newfoundland and England. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue and international tensions, this thought-provoking work explores the struggles faced by the people of Newfoundland and their fight for self-determination. Fretwell's vivid storytelling and meticulous research bring to life the untold stories of courage, sacrifice, and the pursuit of justice. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be captivated by the unexpected twists and turns that shape the destiny of a nation hanging in the balance.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Adventure

Themes: National identity, Colonialism, Independence, Loyalty, Justice
