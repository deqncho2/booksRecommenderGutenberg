Synopsis: 
In 'A Young Mutineer' by L. T. Meade, a young and rebellious protagonist finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger. As he navigates through a world of secrets and hidden agendas, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Rebellion, Loyalty, Identity
