Synopsis: In 'Pigs is Pigs' by Butler, Ellis Parker, a seemingly simple situation spirals out of control when a bureaucratic mix-up leads to a hilarious and chaotic series of events. The story follows a station agent who finds himself caught in a battle of wills with a determined customer over the classification of guinea pigs. As the dispute escalates, the station agent's attempts to resolve the issue only seem to make matters worse, resulting in a comical and unexpected outcome.

Genres: Humor, Satire

Themes: Bureaucracy, Miscommunication
