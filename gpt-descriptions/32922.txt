Synopsis: In 'A Veldt Official: A Novel of Circumstance' by Mitford, Bertram, a young and ambitious government official finds himself stationed in a remote African outpost. As he navigates the challenges of his new environment, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and personal discovery. With vivid descriptions of the African landscape and a cast of intriguing characters, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the complexities of colonial life and the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Colonialism, Ambition, Forbidden Love, Identity
