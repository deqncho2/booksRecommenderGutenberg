Synopsis: In "Janice Meredith: A Story of the American Revolution," the reader is transported to the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. Set in New Jersey, the story follows the spirited and independent Janice Meredith as she navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and patriotism. Amidst the backdrop of war, Janice finds herself torn between two suitors, one a British officer and the other a passionate American rebel. As the Revolution unfolds, Janice's choices become increasingly difficult, and she must confront the consequences of her decisions. This captivating historical novel offers a compelling blend of romance, adventure, and political intrigue, immersing the reader in a pivotal period of American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Loyalty, Patriotism, Choices and Consequences
