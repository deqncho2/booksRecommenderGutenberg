Synopsis: In 'Chats on Autographs', Alexander Meyrick Broadley takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of autographs, sharing intriguing stories and fascinating insights into the lives of famous individuals. From historical figures to literary giants, Broadley delves into the secrets behind these handwritten treasures, exploring the art of collecting and the thrill of uncovering rare signatures. With a blend of historical anecdotes and personal experiences, 'Chats on Autographs' offers a delightful exploration of the captivating world of autograph collecting.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, History

Themes: Collecting, History, Personalities, Significance of Handwritten Artifacts
