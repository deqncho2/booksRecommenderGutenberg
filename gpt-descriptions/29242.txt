Synopsis: In 'Made in Tanganyika' by Carl Jacobi, a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the key to a long-lost civilization. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where ancient secrets collide with modern ambitions. With each discovery, the protagonist uncovers a hidden world filled with treacherous alliances, forbidden knowledge, and a race against time to protect the truth from falling into the wrong hands. This thrilling adventure takes readers on a captivating journey through history, mythology, and the human desire for power and immortality.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Archaeology, Ancient Civilizations, Power and Ambition
