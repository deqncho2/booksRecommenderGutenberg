Synopsis: In 'The Seventh Noon' by Frederick Orin Bartlett, a small town is plagued by a mysterious phenomenon that occurs every day at noon. As the townspeople struggle to understand and cope with this inexplicable event, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to a gripping climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With its intriguing premise and compelling characters, 'The Seventh Noon' is a thrilling exploration of human nature and the power of the unknown.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Mystery, Fear of the Unknown, Community Dynamics, Human Nature
