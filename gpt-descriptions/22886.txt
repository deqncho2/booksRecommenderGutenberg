Synopsis: 
'Cinderella in the South: Twenty-Five South African Tales' by Arthur Shearly Cripps takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and diverse landscapes of South Africa. This collection of twenty-five enchanting tales weaves together elements of folklore, mythology, and everyday life, transporting readers into a world where magic and reality intertwine. From the dusty plains of the Karoo to the lush forests of the Garden Route, these stories introduce us to a cast of unforgettable characters, each with their own unique struggles and triumphs. As we follow their journeys, we discover the power of love, the resilience of the human spirit, and the enduring magic that lies within the heart of South Africa.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Short Stories

Themes: Love, Resilience, Magic, Cultural Identity
