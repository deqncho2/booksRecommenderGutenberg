Synopsis: In 'The Monctons: A Novel. Volume 1 (of 2)', Susanna Moodie takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the Moncton family. Set in the early 19th century, this historical fiction follows the Monctons as they navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. As secrets unravel and relationships are tested, the Monctons find themselves entangled in a web of scandal and intrigue. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Moodie paints a vivid portrait of a family on the brink of transformation. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Expectations, Family Dynamics
