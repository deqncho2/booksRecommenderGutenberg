Synopsis: 
In 'A Day at a Time, and Other Talks on Life and Religion', Alexander, Archibald takes readers on a captivating journey through various aspects of life and religion. Through a collection of insightful talks, he explores the complexities of human existence, delving into topics such as faith, purpose, and the pursuit of happiness. With his eloquent prose and thought-provoking anecdotes, Alexander offers a unique perspective on the challenges and joys of everyday life, encouraging readers to embrace each day as a precious gift. This collection of talks is sure to captivate and inspire readers, inviting them to reflect on their own beliefs and find solace in the beauty of the present moment.

Genres: Spirituality, Philosophy, Self-help
Themes: Faith, Purpose, Happiness, Reflection, Gratitude
