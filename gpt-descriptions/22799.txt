Synopsis: Step back in time and embark on a captivating journey to Ancient Egypt, where the mysteries of the pharaohs and the wonders of the Nile await. In "Peeps at Many Lands: Ancient Egypt," James Baikie takes readers on an immersive exploration of this ancient civilization, revealing its rich history, remarkable architecture, and intriguing customs. From the awe-inspiring pyramids to the bustling markets of Cairo, this vividly descriptive account brings the vibrant world of Ancient Egypt to life, offering a glimpse into the daily lives of its people and the enduring legacy they left behind.

Genres: Historical, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Archaeology, Cultural heritage
