Synopsis: In 'Crusoes of the Frozen North' by Gordon Stables, two young adventurers find themselves stranded in the icy wilderness of the Arctic. With only their wits and limited supplies, they must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter wild animals, and face the harsh realities of survival. As they strive to find their way back home, they discover the true meaning of friendship, resilience, and the indomitable human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Survival
