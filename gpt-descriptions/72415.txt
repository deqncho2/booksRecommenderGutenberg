Synopsis: In 'The Spirit of Toffee' by Farrell Henry, a mysterious antique shop appears overnight in the small town of Willowbrook, captivating the curiosity of its residents. When a young girl named Lily discovers a magical toffee that grants her extraordinary powers, she embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets behind the shop and its enigmatic owner. As Lily's powers grow, she must navigate the fine line between good and evil, facing unexpected challenges and making difficult choices along the way. With its blend of enchantment, suspense, and unexpected twists, 'The Spirit of Toffee' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Power, Morality
