Synopsis: In 'Blind Policy' by George Manville Fenn, a young man named Jack is thrust into a world of mystery and danger when he becomes entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal. As he navigates through a series of unexpected events, Jack must rely on his wits and courage to uncover the truth and protect those he cares about. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the ultimate outcome.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Deception, Loyalty, Courage
