Synopsis: In 'Curiosities of Olden Times', Baring-Gould takes us on a captivating journey through history, unearthing fascinating tales of forgotten civilizations, mysterious artifacts, and enigmatic legends. From ancient Egypt to medieval Europe, each chapter unveils a new and intriguing story that will leave readers spellbound. Delving into the depths of the past, this book offers a glimpse into the extraordinary and often bizarre aspects of human history, shedding light on the peculiarities and curiosities that have shaped our world.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Anthropology

Themes: Curiosity, Exploration, Cultural Heritage
