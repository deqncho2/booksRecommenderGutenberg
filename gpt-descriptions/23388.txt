Synopsis: In 'Wrecked but not Ruined' by R. M. Ballantyne, a young man named Ralph finds himself stranded on a deserted island after a shipwreck. Alone and with limited resources, he must navigate the challenges of survival, including finding food, building shelter, and avoiding dangerous wildlife. As time passes, Ralph's resilience and ingenuity are put to the test, and he discovers unexpected allies and adversaries in his quest to escape the island and return to civilization. Will he be able to overcome the odds and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Coming-of-age

Themes: Resilience, Ingenuity, Friendship, Man vs. Nature
