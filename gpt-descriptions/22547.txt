Synopsis: The Rover Boys on Treasure Isle; or, The Strange Cruise of the Steam Yacht takes readers on an exhilarating adventure as the Rover boys find themselves embarking on a mysterious journey to a remote island. When their father's steam yacht is stolen, the boys set out to recover it, only to stumble upon a hidden treasure and a series of unexpected challenges. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter a cast of intriguing characters, the Rover boys must rely on their wit, courage, and brotherly bond to unravel the secrets of Treasure Isle.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Brotherhood, Courage, Treasure Hunting
