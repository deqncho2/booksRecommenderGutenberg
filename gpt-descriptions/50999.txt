Synopsis: In 'Med Ship Man' by Murray Leinster, a skilled medical officer named Calhoun travels through space to remote planets, providing vital medical aid to those in need. But when he arrives on a seemingly deserted planet, he discovers a mysterious and deadly disease that threatens not only the inhabitants but also his own life. As Calhoun races against time to find a cure, he uncovers a shocking secret that could have far-reaching consequences for the entire galaxy.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Medical ethics, Exploration, Survival
