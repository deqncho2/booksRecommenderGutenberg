Synopsis: 

The Harvard Classics Volume 38: Scientific Papers (Physiology, Medicine, Surgery, Geology) is a captivating collection of groundbreaking scientific papers that will transport readers through the realms of physiology, medicine, surgery, and geology. From the exploration of the human body to the study of the Earth's geological wonders, this volume presents a diverse range of scientific discoveries and theories that have shaped our understanding of the world. Delve into the intricate workings of the human body, witness the advancements in medical knowledge, and embark on a journey to uncover the secrets hidden within the Earth's geological formations. This anthology of scientific papers offers a fascinating glimpse into the minds of the brilliant scientists who have revolutionized their respective fields.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction, Anthology

Themes: Scientific discovery, Human anatomy, Medical advancements, Geological exploration
