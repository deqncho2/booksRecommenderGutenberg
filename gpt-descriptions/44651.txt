Synopsis: In "With the Swamp Fox: A Story of General Marion's Young Spies," author James Otis takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the American Revolution. Set in the swamps of South Carolina, this gripping historical fiction follows a group of courageous young spies who risk their lives to aid General Francis Marion, known as the Swamp Fox. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter dangerous enemies, these young patriots must rely on their wit, bravery, and loyalty to help secure victory for the American cause. With thrilling adventures and unexpected twists, this captivating tale brings to life the untold stories of the unsung heroes of the Revolution.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice
