Synopsis: In 'The Great God Gold' by William Le Queux, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young man named Jack discovers a hidden secret that could change his life forever. Drawn into a world of intrigue and danger, Jack embarks on a thrilling journey across continents, encountering enigmatic characters and facing unexpected challenges. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Jack must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the elusive "Great God Gold." With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the enigma that lies at the heart of this gripping story.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Greed, Betrayal, Pursuit of Wealth, Secrets
