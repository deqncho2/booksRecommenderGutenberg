Synopsis: In 'Brother and Sister' by Josephine Lawrence, a young woman named Mary finds herself torn between her loyalty to her brother and her desire for independence. As she navigates the complexities of family dynamics and societal expectations, Mary must make difficult choices that will shape her future. This captivating story explores the bonds of siblinghood, the pursuit of personal freedom, and the sacrifices one is willing to make for love.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family, Independence, Sacrifice
