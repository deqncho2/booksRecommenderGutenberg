Synopsis: In 'Under the Mendips: A Tale' by Emma Marshall, a young woman named Lucy finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries when she moves to the picturesque countryside of Somerset. As she delves deeper into the history of the area, Lucy uncovers a hidden world filled with ancient legends, hidden treasures, and unexpected connections to her own past. With each revelation, she becomes more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means putting herself in danger. Set against the backdrop of the stunning Mendip Hills, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of the past, self-discovery
