Synopsis: In "A Woman who went to Alaska," May Kellogg Sullivan takes readers on a captivating journey as she recounts her adventurous and unconventional life in the untamed wilderness of Alaska. From facing the harsh realities of survival to encountering fascinating characters and unexpected challenges, Sullivan's memoir offers a unique perspective on the rugged beauty and unforgiving nature of the Last Frontier.

Genres: Memoir, Adventure, Travel

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Nature's power, Cultural exploration
