Synopsis: In 'Our Little Lady: Six Hundred Years Ago' by Emily Sarah Holt, a young girl named Lady Margaret finds herself caught in the midst of political intrigue and danger during the tumultuous times of the Wars of the Roses. As she navigates through treacherous alliances and secret plots, Lady Margaret must rely on her wit, courage, and the unexpected friendships she forms along the way. With the fate of her family and the kingdom hanging in the balance, will Lady Margaret be able to protect those she loves and secure a better future for all?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Power, Love, Resilience
