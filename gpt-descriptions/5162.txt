Synopsis: In the small town of Sutherland, a murder has taken place in the mysterious and eerie house of Agatha Webb. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unraveled, and the residents of Sutherland find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, suspicion, and hidden motives. With a clever detective determined to uncover the truth, the story takes unexpected twists and turns, keeping the reader on the edge of their seat until the shocking conclusion.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Secrets, Justice
