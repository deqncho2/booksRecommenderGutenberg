Synopsis: In 'A Study In Shadows' by Locke, William John, a brilliant detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when a series of bizarre murders occur in the heart of London. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel the very fabric of society. With danger lurking at every turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and deception to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Society's Underbelly
