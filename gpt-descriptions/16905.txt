Synopsis: In 'The Great Red Frog' by Sidney Ransom, a small town is captivated by the mysterious appearance of a vibrant red frog. As the townspeople become obsessed with finding the frog, they uncover secrets that challenge their beliefs and force them to confront their own desires and fears. With a touch of magical realism and a dash of suspense, this captivating tale explores the power of obsession and the consequences of unchecked curiosity.

Genres: Magical realism, mystery, suspense

Themes: Obsession, curiosity, secrets, self-discovery
