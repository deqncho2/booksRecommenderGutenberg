Synopsis: In 'The Magic World' by Edith Nesbit, prepare to be transported to a world where wishes come true, toys come to life, and extraordinary adventures await. From a mischievous sand-fairy granting wishes with unexpected consequences, to a magical ring that grants invisibility, each enchanting tale will captivate readers of all ages. Join a group of siblings as they navigate the whimsical and sometimes perilous realms of magic, learning valuable lessons along the way. With its delightful blend of fantasy and reality, 'The Magic World' is a collection of enchanting stories that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fantasy, Children's Literature, Short Stories

Themes: Magic, Imagination, Morality, Sibling Relationships
