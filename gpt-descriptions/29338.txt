Synopsis: In 'England and Germany' by Dillon, Emile Joseph, a young Englishman finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love as he navigates the complex relationship between England and Germany during a time of great tension. As he delves deeper into the secrets and hidden agendas of both nations, he must make difficult choices that will not only shape his own destiny but also have far-reaching consequences for the future of Europe. With its captivating blend of romance, suspense, and historical backdrop, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Romance

Themes: National identity, Loyalty, Betrayal, Love and sacrifice
