Synopsis: In "The Heart of Princess Osra," the captivating Princess Osra finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of political intrigue and forbidden love. As she navigates the treacherous world of royalty, Osra must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and the fate of her kingdom. With secrets, romance, and unexpected twists, this enchanting tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Duty and Responsibility, Love and Sacrifice, Power and Politics
