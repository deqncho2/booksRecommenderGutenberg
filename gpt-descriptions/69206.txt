Synopsis: In "Winning his Game" by Ralph Henry Barbour, a young man named Jack finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a hidden secret while attending a prestigious boarding school. As he delves deeper into the enigma, Jack must navigate a dangerous game of wits and deception, where the stakes are higher than he could have ever imagined. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Coming of age, Deception and manipulation
