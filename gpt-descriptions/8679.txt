Synopsis: In 'By England's Aid; Or, the Freeing of the Netherlands, 1585-1604', G. A. Henty takes readers on a thrilling journey through the historical events that unfolded during the Dutch War of Independence. Set in the late 16th century, the story follows the adventures of Ned Martin, an English lad who finds himself caught up in the conflict between the Dutch rebels and the powerful Spanish Empire. As Ned becomes embroiled in espionage, naval battles, and daring rescues, he must navigate the treacherous waters of war and loyalty, all while striving to fulfill his duty to England and its allies.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Courage, War and Conflict, Historical Events
