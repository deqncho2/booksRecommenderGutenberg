Synopsis: In 'The Dodge Club; Or, Italy in MDCCCLIX', De Mille takes readers on a thrilling journey through Italy in the year 1859. The story follows a group of American tourists who find themselves caught up in a web of political intrigue, secret societies, and forbidden love. As they navigate the stunning landscapes and historical landmarks of Italy, they become entangled in a dangerous game that could change the course of history. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale keeps readers on the edge of their seats, wondering who can be trusted and what secrets lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Political intrigue, Secret societies, Forbidden love, Trust and betrayal
