Synopsis: 

'Gardening for the Million' by Alfred Pink is a captivating and informative guide that takes readers on a journey through the world of gardening. Filled with practical tips, expert advice, and beautiful illustrations, this book is a must-read for both novice and experienced gardeners alike. From selecting the perfect plants to creating stunning landscapes, Pink's comprehensive guide covers every aspect of gardening, making it accessible to all. As readers delve into the pages of 'Gardening for the Million,' they will discover a wealth of knowledge that will inspire them to transform their own outdoor spaces into breathtaking gardens.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gardening, Reference

Themes: Gardening techniques, Plant selection, Landscape design, Horticultural knowledge
