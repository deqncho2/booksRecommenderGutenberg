Synopsis: In "The Red Year: A Story of the Indian Mutiny" by Louis Tracy, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the Indian Mutiny. Amidst the backdrop of rebellion and political unrest, the lives of two individuals from different worlds intertwine in unexpected ways. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of war and betrayal, their paths converge, leading to a gripping tale of love, sacrifice, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: War and Conflict, Identity and Belonging, Love and Sacrifice
