Synopsis: In 'Happy Ending', a renowned author and a struggling writer find themselves entangled in a mysterious game that blurs the lines between reality and fiction. As they navigate through a series of unexpected twists and turns, they must confront their own demons and question the nature of storytelling itself. With each page, the boundaries between the characters' lives and the story they are creating become increasingly blurred, leading to a mind-bending climax that will leave readers questioning the power of imagination.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Reality vs. Fiction, Identity, Power of Imagination
