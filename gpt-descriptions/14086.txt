Synopsis: 
'Carry On: Letters in War-Time' by Dawson, Coningsby is a collection of heartfelt letters exchanged between soldiers and their loved ones during World War I. Through these letters, the reader is transported to the frontlines and given a glimpse into the emotional struggles, camaraderie, and resilience of those affected by the war. As the correspondence unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the power of love and hope in the face of adversity shines through.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Epistolary Novel, War Literature

Themes: 
Love and Loss, Friendship and Loyalty, Resilience and Hope
