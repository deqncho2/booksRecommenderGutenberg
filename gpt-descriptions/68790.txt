Synopsis: 
In "A Haunted Life" by Grant James, a young woman named Emily moves into an old Victorian house, hoping for a fresh start. However, she soon discovers that the house holds dark secrets and is plagued by supernatural occurrences. As Emily delves deeper into the house's history, she becomes entangled in a web of mystery and danger, unsure of who she can trust. With each passing day, the haunting intensifies, and Emily must confront her own fears and unravel the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: Hauntings, Secrets, Trust, Fear
