Synopsis: In "The Poet Li Po, A.D. 701-762," Arthur Waley delves into the life and works of the renowned Chinese poet Li Po. Through a collection of translated poems and insightful commentary, Waley paints a vivid picture of Li Po's extraordinary talent, his adventurous spirit, and his deep connection with nature. From his humble beginnings to his encounters with emperors and fellow poets, Li Po's journey unfolds, revealing the profound impact he had on Chinese literature and his enduring legacy as one of the greatest poets in history.

Genres: Biography, Poetry, Literary Criticism

Themes: Nature, Spirituality, Artistic Expression, Adventure
