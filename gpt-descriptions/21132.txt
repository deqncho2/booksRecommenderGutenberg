Synopsis: In 'Frank Oldfield: Lost and Found' by Wilson, Theodore P., readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the life of Frank Oldfield, a man who mysteriously disappears and then reappears years later with no memory of what happened. As Frank struggles to piece together the fragments of his past, he uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that threatens to unravel his entire existence. With each revelation, the stakes are raised, and Frank must confront his own identity and the truth behind his disappearance. This gripping tale of mystery and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Memory, Deception
