Synopsis: In 'Tales of a Poultry Farm' by Clara Dillingham Pierson, readers are transported to a charming farm filled with delightful and mischievous poultry. Follow the adventures of a clever rooster, a curious hen, and their feathered friends as they navigate the ups and downs of farm life. From daring escapes to unexpected friendships, this heartwarming collection of stories will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Animal fiction, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resilience
