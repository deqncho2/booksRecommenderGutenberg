Synopsis: In the midst of the Trojan War, Troilus, a young and valiant knight, falls deeply in love with the beautiful Criseyde. Their passionate and forbidden romance unfolds against the backdrop of political intrigue, betrayal, and the fickle nature of fate. As Troilus and Criseyde navigate the complexities of love and loyalty, their story becomes a captivating exploration of human desires, the power of fate, and the consequences of our choices.

Genres: Romance, Epic Poetry, Medieval Literature

Themes: Love and Desire, Loyalty and Betrayal, Fate and Free Will
