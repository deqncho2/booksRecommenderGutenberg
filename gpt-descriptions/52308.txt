Synopsis: In 'The Tunnellers of Holzminden (with a side-issue)' by H. G. Durnford, a group of British prisoners of war find themselves trapped in a seemingly impenetrable German camp during World War I. Faced with harsh conditions and constant surveillance, they devise a daring plan to escape through a network of secret tunnels. As they dig deeper into the earth, tensions rise and loyalties are tested, leading to unexpected consequences that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Camaraderie, Survival
