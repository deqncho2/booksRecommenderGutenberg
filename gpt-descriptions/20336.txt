Synopsis: In 'The Miraculous Revenge' by Shaw, Bernard, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, bringing with him a series of inexplicable events that turn the lives of the townspeople upside down. As the stranger's true intentions become clearer, the residents find themselves caught in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected consequences. With each twist and turn, the story delves into the depths of human nature, exploring the power of revenge and the lengths one might go to seek justice.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Revenge, Justice, Morality
