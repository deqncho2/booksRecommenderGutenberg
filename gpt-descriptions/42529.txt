Synopsis:
In the picturesque countryside of Surrey, a young and mysterious woman named Dariel captures the attention of all who encounter her. With her ethereal beauty and enigmatic past, she becomes the object of desire for many suitors, including the wealthy and influential Sir Richard. As Dariel navigates the complexities of love and society, secrets from her past threaten to unravel her present happiness. Set against the backdrop of stunning landscapes and intricate social dynamics, 'Dariel: A Romance of Surrey' is a captivating tale of passion, intrigue, and the power of love.

Genres:
Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Love and desire, Identity and self-discovery, Social class and societal expectations
