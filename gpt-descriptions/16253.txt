Synopsis: 
In 'Madge Morton, Captain of the Merry Maid' by Amy D. V. Chalmers, readers are introduced to the adventurous and spirited Madge Morton. When Madge's father purchases a yacht, she seizes the opportunity to embark on thrilling sea voyages with her friends. However, their journeys take an unexpected turn when they stumble upon a mysterious message in a bottle. Determined to solve the puzzle, Madge and her friends find themselves caught up in a thrilling and dangerous adventure that will test their courage and resourcefulness.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
