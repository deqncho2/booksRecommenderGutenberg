Synopsis: 
'Folk-Lore and Legends: English' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of English folklore and legends. From ancient myths and mystical creatures to ghostly apparitions and enchanting tales, this collection brings to life the captivating stories that have been passed down through generations. Delve into the magical world of English folklore and discover the hidden depths of its cultural heritage.

Genres: Folklore, Legends, Fantasy

Themes: Mythology, Supernatural, Cultural Heritage
