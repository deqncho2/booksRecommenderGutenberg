Synopsis: In 'The Boy in the Bush', a young couple from England, Kate and Jack, embark on a journey to Australia in search of a new life. Set in the rugged and untamed Australian outback, their adventure takes an unexpected turn when they encounter a mysterious boy living alone in the bush. As they navigate the challenges of their new surroundings, they find themselves drawn into the boy's enigmatic world, leading to a series of events that will test their love, resilience, and understanding of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the Australian outback, Cultural clash, Survival, Coming of age
