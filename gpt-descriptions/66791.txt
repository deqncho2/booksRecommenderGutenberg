Synopsis: 
In 'The City of the Saints, and Across the Rocky Mountains to California', Sir Richard Francis Burton takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the American West during the mid-19th century. From the bustling streets of Salt Lake City to the treacherous trails of the Rocky Mountains, Burton's vivid descriptions and encounters with various characters provide a captivating account of his adventures. As he delves deeper into the heart of the untamed wilderness, the reader is immersed in a world of danger, discovery, and unexpected encounters that will keep them on the edge of their seat.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Cultural clash, Survival
