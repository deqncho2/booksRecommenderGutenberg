Synopsis: In 'The Nurse in Greek Life' by Mary Rosaria, Sister, a young nurse finds herself thrust into the world of Greek life when she takes a job at a prestigious university. As she navigates the complexities of sorority culture, she uncovers dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel the very fabric of sisterhood. With her own life at stake, she must decide how far she is willing to go to protect the truth and the bonds of sisterhood.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Sisterhood and Loyalty
