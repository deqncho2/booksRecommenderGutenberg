Synopsis: In "Letter to the Right Honourable Lord Viscount Melbourne on the Cause of the Higher Average Price of Grain in Britain than on the Continent," Grant Suttie, George, Sir delves into the intriguing mystery behind the disparity in grain prices between Britain and the Continent. With meticulous research and insightful analysis, the author uncovers the hidden factors that contribute to this economic phenomenon, shedding light on a complex issue that has puzzled economists for years. As Suttie presents his findings, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the intricacies of the grain market, uncovering surprising connections and uncovering the truth behind this perplexing disparity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Economics, History

Themes: Economic disparity, Market forces, Agricultural economics
