Synopsis: 
In 'How Music Developed: A Critical and Explanatory Account of the Growth of Modern Music', author W. J. Henderson takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of music, from its earliest origins to the modern era. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of the subject matter, Henderson delves into the historical, cultural, and technological factors that shaped the development of music as we know it today. From the ancient melodies of early civilizations to the complex compositions of classical masters, and finally to the groundbreaking innovations of contemporary musicians, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the growth and transformation of music throughout the ages.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Evolution of Music, Historical Context, Cultural Influences, Technological Advancements
