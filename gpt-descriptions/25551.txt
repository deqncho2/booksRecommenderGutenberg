Synopsis: In 'Six Girls: A Home Story' by Fannie Belle Irving, six young girls find themselves thrown together in a unique living arrangement. As they navigate the challenges of their new home, they form an unbreakable bond that will shape their lives forever. With each girl bringing her own distinct personality and background to the mix, their shared experiences lead to unexpected adventures, heartwarming moments, and valuable life lessons. This heartwarming tale explores the power of friendship, resilience, and the importance of finding a place to call home.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Family

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Belonging
