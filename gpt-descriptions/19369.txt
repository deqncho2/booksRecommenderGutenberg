Synopsis: In 'The Triumphs of Eugène Valmont', readers are introduced to the witty and astute French detective, Eugène Valmont, as he navigates a series of perplexing and often humorous cases. From a stolen diamond necklace to a mysterious disappearance, Valmont's sharp intellect and unconventional methods lead him to uncover the truth behind each enigma. Set against the backdrop of late 19th-century Europe, this collection of interconnected stories takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deduction, Deception, Justice, Cultural Differences
