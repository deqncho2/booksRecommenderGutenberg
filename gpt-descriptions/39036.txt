Synopsis: 

In 'Essays In Pastoral Medicine', Walsh and O'Malley delve into the fascinating world of medicine and spirituality, exploring the intricate relationship between the two. Through a collection of thought-provoking essays, they share captivating stories of patients and their journeys towards healing, offering profound insights into the human condition. From the challenges faced by healthcare professionals to the transformative power of faith, this book takes readers on a captivating exploration of the intersection between medicine and the soul.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical literature, Spirituality

Themes: Medicine and spirituality, Healing, Human condition
