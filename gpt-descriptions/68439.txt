Synopsis: In "Neva's Choice: A Sequel to 'Neva's Three Lovers'," the captivating story continues as Neva finds herself torn between her past and her future. As she navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and self-discovery, Neva must make a decision that will shape her destiny. With unexpected twists and emotional revelations, this sequel delves deeper into Neva's journey, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Love and Relationships, Identity and Self-Discovery, Choices and Consequences
