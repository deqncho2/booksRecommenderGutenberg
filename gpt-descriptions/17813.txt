Synopsis: 
'At Ypres with Best-Dunkley' takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing trenches of World War I. As the protagonist, Thomas Hope, finds himself thrust into the chaos and brutality of battle, he forms an unlikely bond with his comrade, Best-Dunkley. Together, they navigate the horrors of war, facing unimaginable challenges and witnessing the true cost of conflict. This poignant and suspenseful tale explores the resilience of the human spirit amidst the darkest of circumstances.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Sacrifice, Humanity
