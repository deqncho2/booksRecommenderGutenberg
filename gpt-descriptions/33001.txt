Synopsis: 

In 'My Diary in Serbia: April 1, 1915-Nov. 1, 1915' by Monica M. Stanley, the reader is transported to the tumultuous period of World War I. Through the intimate and personal lens of the author's diary, we witness her journey as a nurse in war-torn Serbia. As she navigates the horrors of the battlefield, the resilience of the Serbian people, and the complexities of human nature, Stanley's diary offers a unique perspective on the devastating consequences of war.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War Literature

Themes: War and its impact on individuals and societies, resilience and strength in the face of adversity, the human experience in times of conflict
