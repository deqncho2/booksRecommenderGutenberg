Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Adventures of an Aide-de-Camp; or, A Campaign in Calabria', the thrilling escapades of Captain Charles O'Hara continue as he navigates the treacherous landscape of war-torn Calabria. As tensions rise and alliances shift, O'Hara finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, where loyalty is tested and betrayal lurks at every turn. With vivid descriptions of battles, daring missions, and unexpected alliances, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of Captain O'Hara and the outcome of the campaign.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Courage, Survival
