Synopsis: 
In 'A Trip to Paris in July and August 1792', Richard Twiss takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant streets of Paris during a tumultuous period in history. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Twiss provides a unique perspective on the city's social and political climate, as well as its cultural treasures. As he navigates the bustling markets, encounters intriguing characters, and witnesses the unfolding events, the reader is transported to a time of revolution and uncertainty. With each page, the suspense builds, leaving readers eager to discover what lies ahead in this captivating travelogue.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Revolution, Cultural Exploration
