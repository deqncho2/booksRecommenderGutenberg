Synopsis: 
In 'Dorothy Dale's Queer Holidays' by Margaret Penrose, Dorothy and her friends embark on a series of unexpected adventures during their holiday break. From a mysterious disappearance to a thrilling treasure hunt, the girls find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of excitement and intrigue. As they navigate through these unusual circumstances, Dorothy and her friends must rely on their wit, courage, and friendship to unravel the mysteries that surround them. Will they be able to solve the puzzles and uncover the truth? Join Dorothy and her companions on this thrilling holiday journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
