Synopsis: In 'Ferragus, Chief of the Dévorants' by Honoré de Balzac, a young married couple finds themselves entangled in a web of mystery and danger when they become the target of a secret society led by the enigmatic Ferragus. As they delve deeper into the dark underbelly of Parisian society, they uncover shocking secrets and face life-threatening challenges that test the limits of their love and loyalty.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Love and betrayal, societal corruption, the power of secrets, the struggle between good and evil
