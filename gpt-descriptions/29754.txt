Synopsis: 
'Whittier-land: A Handbook of North Essex, Containing Many Anecdotes of and Poems by John Greenleaf Whittier Never Before Collected' takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and works of renowned poet John Greenleaf Whittier. Samuel T. Pickard delves into the rich history and enchanting landscapes of North Essex, providing readers with fascinating anecdotes and previously unpublished poems by Whittier. As readers explore the pages of this handbook, they will uncover hidden gems and gain a deeper understanding of the poet's life and the inspiration behind his timeless works.

Genres: Biography, Poetry, Travelogue

Themes: History, Nature, Literary Exploration
