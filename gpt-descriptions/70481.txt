Synopsis: In "A Man Made of Money" by Douglas William Jerrold, a mysterious man with an extraordinary ability to create money out of thin air enters the lives of a struggling family. As they become entangled in his web of wealth and deception, they must navigate the moral dilemmas and consequences that arise from their newfound prosperity. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the corrupting power of money and the lengths people will go to attain it.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Greed, Morality, Deception
