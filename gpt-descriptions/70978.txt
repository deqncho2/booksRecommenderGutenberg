Synopsis: In 'The Little Gentleman' by Eleanora H. Stooke, a young boy named Oliver finds himself transported to a magical world where he must navigate through a series of enchanting and perilous adventures. Along the way, he encounters talking animals, mythical creatures, and uncovers the truth about his own identity. As Oliver's journey unfolds, he learns valuable lessons about courage, friendship, and the power of believing in oneself.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Courage, Friendship, Self-belief
