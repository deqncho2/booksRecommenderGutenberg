Synopsis: In 'Egyptian Ideas of the Future Life', E. A. Wallis Budge takes readers on a captivating journey into the ancient Egyptian beliefs about the afterlife. Through a meticulous exploration of hieroglyphics, rituals, and funerary practices, Budge unveils a fascinating world where the dead embark on a complex and awe-inspiring journey to the realm of eternity. From the weighing of the heart to encounters with gods and demons, this book offers a mesmerizing glimpse into the rich tapestry of Egyptian mythology and the profound significance of death in their culture.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Mythology

Themes: Afterlife, Mythology, Religion, Rituals
