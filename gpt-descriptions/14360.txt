Synopsis: In 'The Dawn and the Day; Or, The Buddha and the Christ, Part I', Henry Thayer Niles takes readers on a captivating journey through time and space, intertwining the lives of two iconic figures - the Buddha and the Christ. As the story unfolds, we witness their individual paths of enlightenment and the profound impact they have on the world around them. Through vivid descriptions and thought-provoking encounters, Niles explores the depths of human spirituality and the universal quest for truth and meaning.

Genres: Historical fiction, Religious fiction, Philosophical fiction

Themes: Spirituality, Enlightenment, Interconnectedness, Universal truths
