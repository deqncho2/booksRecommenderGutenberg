Synopsis: 

'With the Doughboy in France: A Few Chapters of an American Effort' takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of Edward Hungerford, an American soldier serving in France during World War I. In this gripping account, Hungerford vividly describes his experiences on the front lines, the camaraderie among his fellow soldiers, and the challenges they faced in the midst of war. From the trenches to the battlefields, from the horrors of combat to the moments of respite, Hungerford's narrative offers a unique perspective on the American effort in France during this tumultuous time.

Genres: Memoir, War, History

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Human resilience
