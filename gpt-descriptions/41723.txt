Synopsis: In "The Duck-footed Hound" by Jim Kjelgaard, a young boy named Dave finds himself drawn to a peculiar and mysterious dog with an unusual physical trait. As Dave forms a bond with the hound, he embarks on an adventure that will test his courage and loyalty. Together, they navigate the challenges of the wilderness, encountering both danger and unexpected allies. This heartwarming tale explores the power of friendship and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Animal fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Loyalty
