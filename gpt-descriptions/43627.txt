Synopsis: 
Step into a world where the line between reality and fantasy blurs, and supernatural beings lurk in the shadows. 'Strange Stories from a Chinese Studio, Vol. 1' by Pu Songling is a captivating collection of enchanting tales that will transport you to ancient China. From mischievous ghosts and vengeful spirits to forbidden love and magical encounters, these stories will leave you spellbound. Delve into the mysterious and unpredictable realm of Chinese folklore, where ordinary lives intertwine with extraordinary events, and where the supernatural is just a breath away.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Folklore, Short Stories

Themes: 
Supernatural encounters, Love and desire, Moral dilemmas
