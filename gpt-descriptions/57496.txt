Synopsis: In 'The Wanderers' by Mary Johnston, readers are transported to the early 17th century, where they follow the captivating journey of two young English women who embark on a perilous adventure across the Atlantic Ocean. Faced with treacherous seas, encounters with indigenous tribes, and the constant threat of danger, these courageous women must navigate a world filled with uncertainty and discover the true meaning of survival. As their paths intertwine with historical figures and events, the story unfolds with gripping intensity, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Exploration, Female Empowerment, Resilience
