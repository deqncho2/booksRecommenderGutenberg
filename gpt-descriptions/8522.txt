Synopsis: In the small town of Salem, a group of Puritans live their lives strictly adhering to their religious beliefs. However, when a mysterious newcomer arrives, their faith and unity are put to the test. As secrets are unveiled and tensions rise, the community becomes engulfed in a web of suspicion and fear. With lives hanging in the balance, the Puritans must confront their own demons and make choices that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Religion and faith, Morality, Conformity and individuality, Guilt and redemption
