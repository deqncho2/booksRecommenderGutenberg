Synopsis: 
'Not Pretty, but Precious; And Other Short Stories' is a captivating collection of short stories that delves into the complexities of human emotions and experiences. From the haunting tale of a forgotten love to the unexpected bond between strangers, each story offers a unique perspective on life's triumphs and tribulations. With its richly drawn characters and thought-provoking narratives, this collection will keep readers engrossed from beginning to end.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Contemporary

Themes: Love and loss, Redemption, Human connection, Identity, Resilience
