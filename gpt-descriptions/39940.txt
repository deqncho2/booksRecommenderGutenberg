Synopsis: 

In 'Ashton-Kirk, Secret Agent' by John T. McIntyre, readers are introduced to the enigmatic and brilliant detective, Ashton-Kirk. When a prominent politician is found dead under mysterious circumstances, Ashton-Kirk is called upon to unravel the truth behind the crime. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of intrigue, espionage, and hidden identities that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With his sharp intellect and keen observation skills, Ashton-Kirk navigates a world of danger and deception, determined to bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Intrigue, Espionage, Deception, Justice
