Synopsis: 
In 'A Parisian Sultana, Vol. 1', Adolphe Belot takes readers on a captivating journey through the glamorous and mysterious world of 19th-century Paris. The story revolves around a young and enigmatic woman named Zora, who possesses an irresistible allure that captivates all who encounter her. As Zora navigates the intricate web of Parisian society, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of love, power, and secrets. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into Zora's world, where passion and betrayal lurk around every corner. Will Zora's charm be her ultimate downfall, or will she emerge as the true queen of Paris?

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Seduction, Power, Deception, Society, Identity
