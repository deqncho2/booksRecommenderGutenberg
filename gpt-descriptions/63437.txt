Synopsis: In 'The Seven Plaits of Nettles, and other stories' by Vredenburg, Edric, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting and mysterious tales. From a young girl's encounter with a mischievous fairy to a man's quest to uncover the truth behind a haunted painting, each story is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. With a blend of fantasy, suspense, and a touch of the supernatural, this collection offers a delightful escape into a world where anything is possible.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Magic, Transformation, Secrets, Fate
