Synopsis: In 'The Evil Eye, Thanatology, and Other Essays', Park, Roswell delves into the mysterious and often overlooked aspects of human existence. From exploring the ancient belief in the evil eye to examining the profound subject of thanatology, the author takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through various essays. With a blend of historical research, personal anecdotes, and philosophical musings, Park invites readers to question their own perceptions of life, death, and the supernatural. This collection of essays offers a captivating exploration of the unknown, leaving readers with a renewed sense of curiosity and a deeper understanding of the human experience.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Occult

Themes: Supernatural, Death, Human Perception
