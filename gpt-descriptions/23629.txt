Synopsis: In 'The Riddle of the Rocks: 1895' by Charles Egbert Craddock, a mysterious discovery in the Appalachian Mountains sets off a chain of events that will test the limits of friendship, loyalty, and the human spirit. As a group of friends embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic rocks, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each step closer to unraveling the riddle, they must confront their own fears and confront the darkness that lies within. This gripping tale of adventure and mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Discovery, Courage
