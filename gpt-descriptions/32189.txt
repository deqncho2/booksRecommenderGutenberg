Synopsis: In "The Raising and Care of Guinea Pigs," author Allen Christian Smith provides a comprehensive guide to all aspects of breeding, feeding, housing, exhibiting, and marketing of cavies. From the basics of selecting the right guinea pig to advanced techniques for breeding and showing, this book covers it all. With detailed instructions and practical tips, Smith offers invaluable advice for both beginners and experienced guinea pig enthusiasts. Whether you are interested in starting your own cavy breeding program or simply want to ensure the best care for your beloved pets, this guide is a must-read for all guinea pig lovers.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Care, Reference

Themes: Animal husbandry, Breeding techniques, Pet care, Exhibition and competition, Marketing and sales
