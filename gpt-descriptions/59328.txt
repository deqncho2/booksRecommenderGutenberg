Synopsis: 

In 'Modern Copper Smelting', Donald M. Levy presents a comprehensive and engaging exploration of the fascinating world of copper smelting. Drawing from his lectures delivered at Birmingham University, Levy delves into the history, uses, and properties of copper, providing a captivating introduction to the subject. With extensive expansion and adaptation, this book offers valuable insights into the intricate processes involved in modern copper smelting, captivating readers with its informative and accessible approach.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Technology

Themes: History of copper, Industrial processes, Material properties, Scientific advancements
