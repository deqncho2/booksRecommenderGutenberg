Synopsis: 
In 'The Young Collector's Handbook of Ants, Bees, Dragon-flies, Earwigs, Crickets, and Flies', author W. Harcourt Bath takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of insects. This comprehensive guide provides a wealth of information on various species, their behavior, habitats, and unique characteristics. From the intricate societies of ants and bees to the mesmerizing flight patterns of dragonflies and the mysterious allure of earwigs, crickets, and flies, this book offers a captivating exploration of the insect kingdom. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Bath's handbook is a must-read for young collectors and nature enthusiasts alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Field Guide

Themes: Insect biology, Nature exploration, Collecting and studying insects
