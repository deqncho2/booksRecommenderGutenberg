Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts on the Trail' by Durston, George, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts embark on a thrilling journey through the wilderness. As they navigate treacherous terrain, encounter mysterious creatures, and face unexpected challenges, the Scouts must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and survival skills to overcome the obstacles that lie ahead. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must prove their courage and determination in order to complete their mission and return home safely.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival
