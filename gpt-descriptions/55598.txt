Synopsis: In 'Shifting For Himself; or, Gilbert Greyson's Fortunes', follow the captivating journey of Gilbert Greyson, a young man who finds himself thrust into the harsh realities of the world. With his family's fortune lost, Gilbert must navigate the treacherous waters of society, facing adversity, deceit, and unexpected friendships along the way. As he strives to make a name for himself and reclaim his place in the world, Gilbert's resilience and determination will be put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Resilience, Self-discovery, Social mobility
