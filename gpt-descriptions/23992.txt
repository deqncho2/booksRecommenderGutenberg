Synopsis: In 'A Missionary Twig' by Emma L. Burnett, a young woman named Sarah embarks on a life-changing journey to a remote village in Africa. As she immerses herself in the local culture and faces unexpected challenges, Sarah's beliefs and convictions are put to the test. With the fate of the village hanging in the balance, she must find the strength to overcome her doubts and fulfill her mission.

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Inspirational

Themes: Self-discovery, Cultural immersion, Faith, Resilience
