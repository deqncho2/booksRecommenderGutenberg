Synopsis: In "The Water Ghost and Others," John Kendrick Bangs takes readers on a delightful journey through a collection of humorous and imaginative ghost stories. From mischievous spirits to haunted houses, each tale presents a unique and entertaining twist on the supernatural. With wit and charm, Bangs weaves together a series of captivating narratives that will leave readers both amused and intrigued.

Genres: Fiction, Humor, Supernatural

Themes: Ghosts, Humor, Imagination
