Synopsis: In "Old Friends and New Fancies: An Imaginary Sequel to the Novels of Jane Austen," Sybil G. Brinton takes readers on a delightful journey through the world of Jane Austen's beloved characters. Set in the early 19th century, the story intertwines the lives of familiar faces from Austen's novels, such as Elizabeth Bennet and Mr. Darcy, with a host of new characters. As old friends reunite and new acquaintances are made, the plot unfolds with romance, intrigue, and unexpected twists. Brinton's skillful writing captures the essence of Austen's style, making this sequel a delightful continuation of the Austen universe.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Literary Fiction

Themes: Love and Marriage, Social Class, Friendship, Identity
