Synopsis: 
In 'Betty Gordon at Boarding School; Or, The Treasure of Indian Chasm', Betty Gordon finds herself embarking on a thrilling adventure as she starts her new life at boarding school. Little does she know that her time at school will lead her to uncover a hidden treasure and unravel a mysterious secret. With her determination and curiosity, Betty must navigate through unexpected challenges and unexpected friendships, all while trying to solve the enigma of Indian Chasm. This captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Betty's journey of discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Curiosity
