Synopsis:
In "Arabella Stuart: A Romance from English History," James, G. P. R. takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous life of Arabella Stuart, a woman caught in the midst of political intrigue and forbidden love. Set against the backdrop of 16th-century England, this historical romance follows Arabella as she navigates the treacherous waters of courtly life, torn between her duty to her family and her own desires. As she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of power struggles and secret alliances, Arabella must summon all her strength and cunning to survive in a world where loyalty is a scarce commodity and love is a dangerous game.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Intrigue

Themes:
Power and Politics, Forbidden Love, Loyalty and Betrayal
