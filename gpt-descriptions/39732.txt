Synopsis: 
In 'Budd Boyd's Triumph; or, The Boy-Firm of Fox Island', young Budd Boyd finds himself thrust into a world of adventure and entrepreneurship when he inherits a small island off the coast of Maine. With the help of his friends, Budd sets out to transform the island into a thriving business venture, facing numerous challenges and unexpected twists along the way. As they navigate the complexities of running a business, the group learns valuable lessons about friendship, determination, and the power of teamwork.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Entrepreneurship, Overcoming challenges
