Synopsis: 
'Little Masterpieces of Science: Invention and Discovery' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of scientific breakthroughs and ingenious inventions. From the discovery of electricity to the invention of the telephone, this collection of short stories delves into the brilliant minds behind some of the most significant scientific advancements in history. Each narrative unveils the struggles, triumphs, and unexpected twists that led to these groundbreaking discoveries, leaving readers in awe of the incredible human capacity for innovation and curiosity. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Invention, Discovery, Human Ingenuity
