Synopsis: In 'Prelude to Space' by Robert W. Haseltine, humanity is on the brink of a monumental achievement - the first manned mission to the moon. As the world watches with bated breath, a team of brilliant scientists and engineers must overcome numerous obstacles and personal conflicts to make this historic journey a reality. With the fate of the mission hanging in the balance, the characters must confront their own fears and doubts, pushing the boundaries of human potential and paving the way for a new era of space exploration.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Human ambition, Exploration, Teamwork, Overcoming adversity
