Synopsis: In 'Historic Tales: The Romance of Reality. Vol. 07 (of 15), Spanish', Charles Morris takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of Spain. From the legendary tales of El Cid to the dramatic events of the Spanish Inquisition, this volume delves into the fascinating stories that shaped the nation. With vivid descriptions and engaging narratives, Morris brings to life the heroes, conquerors, and rulers who left an indelible mark on Spanish history. From the glory of the Moors to the rise of the Catholic Monarchs, this volume offers a captivating exploration of Spain's past.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Spanish history, Heroism, Power and Politics
