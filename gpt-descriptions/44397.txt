Synopsis: 

'Haunted Places in England' takes readers on a spine-chilling journey through the most haunted locations in England. From ancient castles to eerie manor houses, this book uncovers the dark secrets and ghostly tales that have plagued these sites for centuries. O'Donnell and Elliott delve into the history and legends surrounding each haunted place, providing a captivating exploration of the supernatural. Prepare to be captivated by the mysterious and haunted side of England.

Genres: Non-fiction, Paranormal, Travel

Themes: Ghosts, Legends, History, Supernatural
