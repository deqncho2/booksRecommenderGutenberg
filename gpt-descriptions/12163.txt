Synopsis:
In "The Sleeper Awakes: A Revised Edition of When the Sleeper Wakes," a man named Graham falls into a deep sleep in the late 19th century and wakes up in a dystopian future where he is hailed as a messianic figure. As he navigates this strange new world, Graham becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, social unrest, and personal struggles. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must confront the consequences of his awakening and decide whether to embrace his role as a savior or fight against the oppressive forces that control society.

Genres:
Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Political Thriller

Themes:
Power and Control, Social Inequality, Individualism vs. Collectivism
