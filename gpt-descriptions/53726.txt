Synopsis: In 'Cædwalla; or, The Saxons in the Isle of Wight: A Tale', Cowper, Frank takes us on a captivating journey to the Isle of Wight during the time of the Saxons. The story follows the adventures of Cædwalla, a young Saxon warrior, as he navigates the treacherous landscapes of love, loyalty, and power. As Cædwalla's path intertwines with the lives of the island's inhabitants, he must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape the destiny of his people. Filled with gripping battles, forbidden romance, and unexpected alliances, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Power and Ambition, Identity and Belonging
