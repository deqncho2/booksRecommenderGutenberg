Synopsis: 
In the remote and mysterious Hurricane Island, a group of strangers find themselves trapped in a deadly game of survival. As a hurricane approaches, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, forcing the characters to confront their darkest fears and make impossible choices. With each passing moment, the island becomes more treacherous, and the line between friend and foe blurs. Will they find a way to escape the island's clutches, or will Hurricane Island claim them all?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Identity, Trust
