Synopsis: 

'From the Bottom Up: The Life Story of Alexander Irvine' takes readers on a captivating journey through the extraordinary life of Alexander Irvine. Born into poverty and facing numerous hardships, Irvine's determination and resilience propel him forward, leading him to unexpected places and encounters. From his humble beginnings in a small Irish village to his adventures in America, Irvine's story is one of triumph over adversity, love, loss, and the pursuit of one's dreams. As the pages turn, readers will be enthralled by the twists and turns of Irvine's life, eagerly following his path to see where it ultimately leads.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Historical Fiction

Themes: Overcoming adversity, Pursuit of dreams, Resilience, Love and loss
