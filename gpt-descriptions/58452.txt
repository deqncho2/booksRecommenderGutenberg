Synopsis: 
'Fences, Gates and Bridges: A Practical Manual' takes readers on a captivating journey through the art of building and designing structures that connect and divide. From the intricate craftsmanship of fences to the grandeur of bridges, this manual delves into the practical aspects of construction while exploring the deeper symbolism behind these architectural marvels. With vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, the author invites readers to discover the hidden stories and secrets that lie within these structures, ultimately revealing the profound impact they have on our lives and communities.

Genres: Non-fiction, Architecture, Design

Themes: Connection, Division, Symbolism, Community
