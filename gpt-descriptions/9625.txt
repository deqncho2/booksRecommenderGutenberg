Synopsis: 

In 'Buried Cities, Volume 1: Pompeii' by Jennie Hall, readers are transported back in time to the ancient city of Pompeii, just days before the catastrophic eruption of Mount Vesuvius. Through the eyes of a young protagonist, we witness the bustling streets, vibrant markets, and intricate social dynamics of this Roman city. As the impending disaster looms, tension builds, secrets are revealed, and lives are forever changed. Will our protagonist find a way to survive amidst the chaos and destruction?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Human Resilience, Natural Disasters
