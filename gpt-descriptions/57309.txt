Synopsis: In 'Aunt Jo's Scrap-Bag, Volume 3: Cupid and Chow-chow, etc.', Louisa May Alcott presents a delightful collection of short stories that will captivate readers of all ages. From mischievous Cupid causing chaos in a household to the heartwarming tale of a loyal dog named Chow-chow, each story is filled with humor, adventure, and valuable life lessons. Join Aunt Jo as she weaves these enchanting tales that will leave you eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what surprises await.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Children's Literature

Themes: Love, Loyalty, Family, Morality
