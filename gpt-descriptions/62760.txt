Synopsis: In the enchanting city of Florence, a young American artist finds herself drawn into a web of secrets and intrigue. As she delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the city's art and history, she uncovers a hidden world of forbidden love, political conspiracies, and long-buried secrets. With each discovery, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game where the line between reality and illusion blurs. As she navigates the labyrinthine streets of Florence, she must unravel the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Art and Beauty, Love and Betrayal, Secrets and Deception
