Synopsis: 
In "John Knox and the Reformation," Andrew Lang takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and times of one of the most influential figures in Scottish history. This gripping biography delves into the tumultuous era of religious and political upheaval, as John Knox emerges as a fearless leader and champion of the Protestant Reformation. From his early struggles to his pivotal role in shaping the destiny of Scotland, Lang paints a vivid portrait of a man driven by unwavering conviction and an unyielding desire for religious freedom. As the story unfolds, readers are transported to a world of intrigue, conflict, and the relentless pursuit of truth.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Religious reform, Political turmoil, Leadership, Religious freedom
