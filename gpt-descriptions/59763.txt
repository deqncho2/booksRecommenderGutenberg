Synopsis: In "The Tariff in Our Times," Ida M. Tarbell takes readers on a captivating journey through the complex world of tariffs, exploring their impact on society, economy, and politics. Through meticulous research and insightful analysis, Tarbell sheds light on the historical context, controversies, and consequences surrounding this crucial economic policy. With a keen eye for detail and a compelling narrative style, she unravels the intricate web of interests and power struggles that have shaped the tariff system, making this book a must-read for anyone seeking to understand the far-reaching implications of this often overlooked subject.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Economics

Themes: Economic policy, Politics, Society, Power dynamics
