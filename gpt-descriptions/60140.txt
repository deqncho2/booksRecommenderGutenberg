Synopsis: In 'New Paths through Old Palestine' by Margaret Slattery, a young archaeologist embarks on a thrilling journey through the ancient land of Palestine, unearthing hidden secrets and encountering unexpected dangers. As she delves deeper into the mysteries of the past, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that spans centuries, forcing her to question her own beliefs and confront the consequences of her discoveries. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding suspense, this captivating tale will transport readers to a world where the past and present collide, and where the search for truth comes at a great cost.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Archaeology, Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
