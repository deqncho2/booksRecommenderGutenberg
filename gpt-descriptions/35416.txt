Synopsis: In 'The Romance of Biography', Mrs. Jameson delves into the captivating lives of women who have been loved and celebrated by poets throughout history. From the enchanting days of the Troubadours to the present age, this volume explores the extraordinary stories of these remarkable women, their relationships with poets, and the impact they had on the world of literature. With rich historical detail and poetic flair, Mrs. Jameson invites readers on a journey through time, uncovering the hidden passions, triumphs, and tragedies of these inspiring women.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, History

Themes: Love and Romance, Women's Empowerment, Influence of Literature
