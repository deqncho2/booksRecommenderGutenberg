Synopsis: In 'All (Frightfully Unofficial) About an Old Friend of Mine: What He Most Probably Was. What He Most Certainly Will Be, and Who Has Done This? Why the Cat.', author Thomas C. Gash takes readers on a thrilling journey as they uncover the mysterious past and uncertain future of an enigmatic old friend. Through a series of unexpected events, the protagonist becomes entangled in a web of secrets, leading them to question the true identity of their friend and the forces at play. As the plot unfolds, the role of a peculiar cat becomes increasingly significant, leaving readers eager to discover its connection to the unfolding mystery. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Betrayal
