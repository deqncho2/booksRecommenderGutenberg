Synopsis: In 'How to Sing' by Luisa Tetrazzini, a renowned opera singer shares her secrets and techniques for achieving vocal mastery. Through personal anecdotes and practical advice, Tetrazzini takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and artistic growth, revealing the transformative power of music and the human voice. From breath control to interpretation, she delves into the intricacies of vocal technique, offering invaluable insights for aspiring singers and music enthusiasts alike. With passion and expertise, Tetrazzini's guide unlocks the potential within every voice, inspiring readers to embrace their own unique musical expression.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Self-help

Themes: Artistic expression, Personal growth, Mastery of craft
