Synopsis: In 'The Medici Balls: Seven little journeys in Tuscany', Newell and Sheldon take readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting region of Tuscany, where they uncover the hidden secrets and intriguing stories behind the Medici family. Through seven unique and enthralling tales, the authors delve into the opulent world of the Medici balls, where lavish costumes, scandalous affairs, and political intrigue intertwine. From the grandeur of the Renaissance to the tumultuous times of the Medici dynasty, this book offers a mesmerizing glimpse into the lives of the powerful and influential Medici family.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Betrayal, Renaissance Art and Culture
