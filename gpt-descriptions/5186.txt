Synopsis: 
'Kalevala: The Epic Poem of Finland - Complete' takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient Finnish mythology and folklore. This epic poem weaves together tales of heroes, gods, and mythical creatures, as they navigate a world filled with magic, adventure, and the eternal struggle between good and evil. From the creation of the world to the epic battles fought by legendary figures, this epic poem immerses readers in a rich tapestry of Finnish culture and tradition.

Genres: Epic Poetry, Mythology, Folklore

Themes: Heroism, Creation, Good vs. Evil, Magic, Tradition
