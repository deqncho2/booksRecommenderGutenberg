Synopsis: In 'The Master of St. Benedict's, Vol. 1 (of 2)' by St. Aubyn, Alan, a mysterious and enigmatic figure arrives at St. Benedict's, a prestigious boarding school with a dark past. As he assumes the role of the new headmaster, his unconventional methods and unorthodox teachings begin to captivate the students, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and rebellion against the traditional norms of the institution. However, as the secrets of St. Benedict's start to unravel, the students find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden desires that threaten to shatter their world.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Power and authority, Rebellion, Secrets and deception
