Synopsis: 

In "Trial of the Major War Criminals Before the International Military Tribunal, Nuremberg 14 November 1945-1 October 1946, Volume 08," the courtroom becomes a stage for justice as the most notorious figures of the Nazi regime face their day of reckoning. As the trial unfolds, shocking revelations and gripping testimonies shed light on the extent of the atrocities committed during World War II. With the world watching, the prosecution and defense engage in a battle of wits, seeking to expose the truth and deliver justice to the victims of the Holocaust. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Legal drama, True crime

Themes: Justice, Accountability, Human rights, Holocaust, World War II
