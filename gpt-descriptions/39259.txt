Synopsis: In 'Was the Beginning Day of the Maya Month Numbered Zero (or Twenty) or One?' Charles P. Bowditch delves into the fascinating world of the ancient Maya civilization and their complex calendar system. Through meticulous research and analysis, Bowditch explores the debate surrounding the numbering of the Maya month's beginning day, presenting compelling evidence that challenges conventional beliefs. As he unravels the mysteries of the Maya calendar, readers are taken on a captivating journey through history, mathematics, and cultural interpretation. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Archaeology, Anthropology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Calendar systems, Cultural interpretation
