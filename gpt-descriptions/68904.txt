Synopsis: In 'One of Three' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a shocking truth that challenges everything he thought he knew. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path of deception, betrayal, and moral dilemmas. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of science and the consequences of playing god.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethical dilemmas, Scientific advancement, Betrayal
