Synopsis: In "How to Use Your Mind: A Psychology of Study," author Harry Dexter Kitson presents a groundbreaking manual that explores the intricate workings of the human mind and provides invaluable insights into effective study techniques. With a focus on both students and teachers, this book offers practical advice on how to optimize the learning process and enhance academic performance. Through a comprehensive examination of psychological principles and strategies, Kitson empowers readers to unlock their full intellectual potential and achieve success in their educational pursuits.

Genres: Educational, Psychology, Self-help

Themes: Learning strategies, Cognitive psychology, Academic success
