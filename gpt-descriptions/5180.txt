Synopsis: 

In the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Pennsylvania, a groundbreaking ruling takes place that challenges the boundaries of internet access for children. The Children's Internet Protection Act (CIPA) is put to the test as the court grapples with the delicate balance between protecting children from harmful content and preserving their freedom of expression. As the case unfolds, unexpected alliances are formed, secrets are revealed, and the future of online safety hangs in the balance.

Genres: Legal drama, political thriller, courtroom suspense

Themes: Internet regulation, freedom of speech, child protection, privacy
