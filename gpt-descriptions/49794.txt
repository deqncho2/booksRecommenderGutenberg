Synopsis: 
In 'Personal Sketches of His Own Times, Vol. 3', Sir Jonah Barrington takes us on a captivating journey through his personal experiences and encounters with notable figures of his era. From his encounters with royalty and politicians to his adventures in the legal profession, Barrington's anecdotes are filled with wit, humor, and a keen eye for detail. As he navigates the social and political landscape of his time, the reader is transported to a world of intrigue, scandal, and unexpected twists. This final volume of Barrington's memoirs is a delightful blend of history, memoir, and entertainment that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Memoir, Historical, Biography

Themes: Social and political commentary, Personal experiences, Historical events
