Synopsis: In the thrilling tale of 'Jack the Giant Killer' by Leigh, Percival, a young and courageous hero named Jack embarks on a perilous journey to save his kingdom from the clutches of menacing giants. Armed with his wit, bravery, and a magical weapon, Jack must face treacherous landscapes, outsmart cunning adversaries, and confront his own fears. As he battles against towering giants and uncovers dark secrets, Jack's determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the odds and emerge victorious, or will the giants prove to be too formidable? 

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Action

Themes: Heroism, Courage, Good vs. Evil
