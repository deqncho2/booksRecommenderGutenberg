Synopsis: 
In 'Harry Milvaine; Or, The Wanderings of a Wayward Boy', we follow the captivating journey of young Harry Milvaine as he embarks on a series of thrilling adventures. From his mischievous escapades at school to his encounters with pirates and explorers, Harry's life takes unexpected turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As he navigates through danger and uncertainty, Harry learns valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the importance of staying true to oneself. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
