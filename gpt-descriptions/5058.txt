Synopsis: 
In 'Plays: Fourth Series' by John Galsworthy, the reader is taken on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking plays. From the complexities of family dynamics to the struggles of societal expectations, Galsworthy's masterful storytelling delves into the lives of diverse characters facing moral dilemmas, love, and the pursuit of justice. Each play presents a unique and compelling narrative that explores the depths of human nature, leaving the reader eagerly turning the pages to discover the outcomes of these gripping tales.

Genres: Drama, Social Realism, Tragedy

Themes: Family, Morality, Love, Justice, Society
