Synopsis: In 'Metzerott, Shoemaker' by Katharine Pearson Woods, a young shoemaker named Metzerott finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a hidden secret in his small town. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a dark conspiracy that threatens not only his own life but the lives of those he holds dear. With danger lurking at every turn, Metzerott must use his wit and resourcefulness to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Betrayal and trust, Courage and resilience
