Synopsis: 
'Uncanny Tales' is a collection of gripping and eerie short stories that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. From haunted houses to mysterious disappearances, each tale delves into the realm of the supernatural, exploring the dark corners of the human psyche. As the characters navigate through chilling encounters and unexplained phenomena, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and question the boundaries of reality. With unexpected twists and haunting atmospheres, 'Uncanny Tales' is a captivating journey into the unknown.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Supernatural, Fear, Perception
