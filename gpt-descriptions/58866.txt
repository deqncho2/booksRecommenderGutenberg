Synopsis: 
When renowned detective Hercule Poirot receives a desperate plea for help from a wealthy man in France, he finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and murder. Arriving at the scene of the crime, Poirot discovers a lifeless body lying in an open grave on a golf course, with no shortage of suspects and a myriad of secrets waiting to be unraveled. As he delves deeper into the case, Poirot must navigate a complex maze of clues, red herrings, and unexpected twists, all while racing against time to catch a cunning killer before they strike again.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Revenge, Justice
