Synopsis: 
"The Swindler and Other Stories" is a captivating collection of short stories by Ethel M. Dell that will transport readers into a world of mystery, romance, and unexpected twists. From a cunning con artist to a forbidden love affair, each story presents a unique and thrilling narrative that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With vivid characters and intricate plots, Dell weaves tales that explore the complexities of human nature and the consequences of our choices. Prepare to be enthralled by these gripping stories that will leave you wanting more.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Love, Morality
