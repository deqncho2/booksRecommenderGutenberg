Synopsis: In 'The Heart of the Hills' by John Fox, Jr., a young woman named June Tolliver finds herself torn between two worlds - the rugged beauty of the Appalachian Mountains and the allure of city life. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, June must make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of the enchanting hills, this captivating tale explores the power of self-discovery and the enduring bonds of heritage.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Tradition, Love and Sacrifice
