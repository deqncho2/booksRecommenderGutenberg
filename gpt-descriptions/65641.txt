Synopsis: 
In 'Dad' by Albert Payson Terhune, a heartwarming tale unfolds as a young boy named Larry forms an unbreakable bond with his loyal and intelligent collie, Lad. Together, they navigate the challenges of life, including a mysterious stranger who threatens their peaceful existence. As Larry grows up, he learns valuable lessons about love, loyalty, and the enduring power of family. This captivating story explores the unbreakable bond between humans and animals, and the profound impact they can have on each other's lives.

Genres: 
Fiction, Animal Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Love and loyalty, Family bonds, Growth and maturity
