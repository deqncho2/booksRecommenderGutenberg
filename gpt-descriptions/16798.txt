Synopsis: In 'Elster's Folly: A Novel' by Wood, Henry, Mrs., a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as Elster, a wealthy and enigmatic man, becomes the center of attention in a small English village. As the villagers try to uncover the secrets behind Elster's past and his peculiar behavior, they find themselves drawn into a web of intrigue, romance, and unexpected twists. With each revelation, the true nature of Elster's folly is gradually unveiled, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and desire, Society and class
