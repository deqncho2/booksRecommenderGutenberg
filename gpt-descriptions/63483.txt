Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's Chase; Or, Exciting Times Afloat', Frank Merriwell finds himself embarking on a thrilling adventure at sea. As he sets sail, he encounters treacherous waters, mysterious characters, and unexpected challenges that test his courage and resourcefulness. With danger lurking at every turn, Frank must rely on his wits and determination to navigate through the high-stakes chase that unfolds on the open ocean.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Courage, Resourcefulness, Perseverance
