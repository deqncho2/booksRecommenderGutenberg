Synopsis: In 'The Brighton Boys with the Flying Corps', a group of adventurous young men from Brighton find themselves thrust into the thrilling world of aviation during World War I. As they join the Flying Corps, they face danger, camaraderie, and the exhilaration of soaring through the skies in their biplanes. With their lives on the line, they must navigate the challenges of aerial combat and prove their mettle in the face of adversity. Will they emerge as heroes or succumb to the perils of war?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Sacrifice
