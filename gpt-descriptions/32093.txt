Synopsis: 

In 'Loyal to the School' by Angela Brazil, the story revolves around a group of students who are fiercely devoted to their beloved school. As they navigate the challenges of adolescence, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, rivalries, and unexpected friendships. Amidst the backdrop of school competitions, academic pressures, and personal growth, the students must confront their own fears and prejudices, ultimately learning the true meaning of loyalty and the power of unity.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, School Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Identity, Acceptance, Personal Growth
