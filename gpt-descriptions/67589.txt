Synopsis: In "A Message From Our Sponsor" by Henry Slesar, a struggling writer receives an unexpected offer that promises to change his life forever. As he delves deeper into this mysterious opportunity, he soon realizes that there is much more at stake than he could have ever imagined. With each twist and turn, the protagonist is forced to confront his own desires and the consequences of his choices, leading to a thrilling and thought-provoking climax.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Manipulation
