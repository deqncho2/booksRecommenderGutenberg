Synopsis: 
In 'At Plattsburg' by Allen French, a group of young men from a small New England town find themselves caught up in the excitement and challenges of World War I. As they train at the Plattsburg Military Camp, they face physical and mental tests that push them to their limits. Amidst the camaraderie and competition, friendships are forged, love is discovered, and the true meaning of courage is revealed. Will these young men rise to the occasion and prove themselves on the battlefield?

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Patriotism, Friendship, Sacrifice, Growth, Courage
