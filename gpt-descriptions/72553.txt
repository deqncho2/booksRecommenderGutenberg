Synopsis: In "A Long Way Back" by Ben Bova, a renowned scientist embarks on a perilous journey through time to save humanity from a catastrophic future. As he navigates the complexities of time travel, he must confront personal demons and make difficult choices that could alter the course of history. With each leap through time, the stakes grow higher, and the line between past, present, and future becomes increasingly blurred. This gripping tale of adventure and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Sacrifice, Consequences of Actions
