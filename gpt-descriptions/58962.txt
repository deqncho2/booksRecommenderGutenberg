Synopsis: In 'Making Home Profitable', Kate V. Saint Maur takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of home economics, revealing unconventional strategies to turn any household into a thriving business. With a blend of practical advice and inspiring anecdotes, this book offers a fresh perspective on how to maximize the potential of your home and transform it into a profitable venture. From innovative ways to monetize your hobbies to leveraging the power of social media, Saint Maur provides a roadmap for success that will leave readers inspired and motivated to unlock the hidden potential within their own homes.

Genres: Self-help, Business, Home Economics

Themes: Entrepreneurship, Financial Independence, Creativity
