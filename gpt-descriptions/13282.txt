Synopsis: In 'The Island of Faith' by Margaret E. Sangster, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey to a remote island, seeking solace and answers to her deepest questions. As she immerses herself in the island's unique culture and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Emily's faith is tested and transformed in unexpected ways. With each passing day, she unravels the mysteries of the island and discovers the power of belief and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Spiritual

Themes: Faith, Identity, Redemption, Cultural Exploration
