Synopsis: In 'The Haunted Hotel: A Mystery of Modern Venice' by Wilkie Collins, a mysterious hotel in Venice becomes the setting for a series of eerie events. When a young bride mysteriously disappears on her wedding night, her husband becomes obsessed with uncovering the truth behind her vanishing. As the story unfolds, secrets, deceit, and supernatural occurrences intertwine, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful journey through the dark underbelly of Venice.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Obsession, Supernatural, Betrayal
