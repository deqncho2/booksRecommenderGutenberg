Synopsis: 
In "Samuel Brohl and Company" by Victor Cherbuliez, the reader is introduced to Samuel Brohl, a man of humble origins who unexpectedly finds himself in a position of power and influence. As he navigates the treacherous waters of high society, Brohl must grapple with the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot filled with unexpected twists and turns, this novel takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of human nature and the pursuit of success.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Satire

Themes: 
Social climbing, Ambition, Love and relationships
