Synopsis: 
"Co. Aytch," Maury Grays, First Tennessee Regiment: Or, A Side Show of the Big Show is a captivating memoir that takes readers on a journey through the American Civil War. Written by Samuel R. Watkins, a soldier who fought in the war, this book offers a unique and personal perspective on the experiences and hardships faced by the soldiers of the Maury Grays. From the camaraderie and humor shared among the troops to the brutal realities of battle, Watkins vividly recounts his time on the front lines, providing a gripping and emotional account of the war that shaped a nation.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: War and its impact on individuals and society, camaraderie and brotherhood in times of adversity, the human experience in the face of conflict
