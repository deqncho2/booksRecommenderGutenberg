Synopsis: 
In 'Added Upon: A Story' by Nephi Anderson, readers are transported to a celestial realm where they witness the lives of two souls, Lyle and Marcia, as they navigate the complexities of mortal existence and the eternal consequences of their choices. As their paths intertwine, the story delves into themes of love, sacrifice, and the profound impact of our actions on both our earthly and heavenly lives. With unexpected twists and a thought-provoking narrative, 'Added Upon' invites readers to ponder the eternal significance of the choices we make and the ultimate purpose of our existence.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Religious

Themes: Love, Choice, Eternal Consequences
