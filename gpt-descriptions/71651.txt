Synopsis: 

Embark on an exhilarating journey from Cairo to Kisumu as Frank Carpenter takes readers on a captivating exploration of Egypt, The Sudan, and Kenya Colony. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Carpenter paints a vivid picture of the diverse landscapes, rich cultures, and fascinating people encountered along the way. From the bustling streets of Cairo to the serene beauty of the Nile, from the ancient wonders of Luxor to the vibrant markets of Khartoum, and finally to the breathtaking landscapes of Kenya, this travelogue is a thrilling adventure that will leave readers yearning for their own African odyssey.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Natural beauty, Historical significance
