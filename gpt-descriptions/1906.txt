Synopsis: In 'Erewhon; Or, Over the Range', the protagonist embarks on a journey to a mysterious land where everything seems strangely familiar yet utterly different. As he explores this enigmatic society, he uncovers a series of bizarre customs and beliefs that challenge his own understanding of civilization. With wit and satire, Butler takes readers on a thought-provoking adventure that blurs the lines between reality and illusion.

Genres: Satire, Science Fiction, Utopian Fiction

Themes: Critique of Victorian society, Hypocrisy, Evolution of civilization
