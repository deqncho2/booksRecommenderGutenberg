Synopsis: In "The Sister Years," Hawthorne weaves a haunting tale of four sisters who personify the four seasons, each with their own distinct personalities and powers. As they navigate the complexities of their relationships and the changing world around them, a mysterious event threatens to disrupt their delicate balance. With lyrical prose and a touch of the supernatural, Hawthorne explores the bonds of sisterhood and the eternal cycle of life and death.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural fiction, allegory

Themes: Sisterhood, nature, mortality, the passage of time
