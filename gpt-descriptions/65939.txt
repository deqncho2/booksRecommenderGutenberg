Synopsis: In "Fortune's Fool" by Sabatini, Rafael, a young nobleman finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love when he is mistaken for a notorious outlaw. As he navigates the treacherous world of 17th-century Europe, he must rely on his wit and cunning to survive, all while trying to uncover the truth behind his mistaken identity. With danger lurking at every turn, the young nobleman's fate hangs in the balance, and he must make choices that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Deception, Love and Betrayal
