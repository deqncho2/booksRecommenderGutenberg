Synopsis: In 'The Garden Without Walls' by Dawson Coningsby, a mysterious garden appears overnight in the heart of a bustling city, captivating the attention of its inhabitants. As they explore its enchanting beauty, they soon discover that the garden holds secrets that challenge their perceptions of reality and ignite a journey of self-discovery. With each step deeper into the garden, the characters are confronted with profound questions about love, identity, and the nature of existence. As they navigate through the garden's hidden wonders and unexpected dangers, they must confront their deepest fears and desires, ultimately leading them to a transformative understanding of themselves and the world around them.

Genres: Magical realism, Mystery, Literary fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Perception, Love, Identity, Existence
