Synopsis: 

In 'Wild Beasts: A study of the characters and habits of the elephant, lion, leopard, panther, jaguar, tiger, puma, wolf, and grizzly bear,' J. Hampden Porter takes readers on a thrilling journey into the untamed world of some of nature's most majestic creatures. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Porter delves into the lives of these wild beasts, revealing their unique personalities, hunting techniques, and social dynamics. From the awe-inspiring strength of the elephant to the stealthy prowess of the leopard, each chapter offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of these extraordinary animals. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural history, Wildlife

Themes: Animal behavior, Wildlife conservation, Human-animal interaction
