Synopsis: 
In "The Under Dog" by Francis Hopkinson Smith, a young artist named Tom Van Dorn finds himself caught between his passion for painting and the expectations of his wealthy family. Determined to prove himself, Tom embarks on a journey to the picturesque village of Pont-Aven in France, where he encounters a colorful cast of characters and becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and artistic rivalry. As Tom navigates the challenges of pursuing his dreams, he must confront his own insecurities and discover the true meaning of success.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Artistic expression, Identity, Social class
