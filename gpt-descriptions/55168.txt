Synopsis: 
In 'Fossils: A Story of the Rocks and Their Record of Prehistoric Life', Markman takes readers on an enthralling journey through the fascinating world of fossils. From the discovery of the first fossil to the intricate process of fossilization, this book unveils the secrets hidden within these ancient remnants. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Markman explores the diverse forms of life that once roamed the Earth, from colossal dinosaurs to microscopic organisms. Delving into the methods used by paleontologists to decipher the past, this book offers a glimpse into the remarkable history of our planet and the incredible creatures that inhabited it.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Paleontology

Themes: Prehistoric life, Evolution, Earth's history
