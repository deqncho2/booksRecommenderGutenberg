Synopsis: In 'The Two Treaties; or, Hope for Jerusalem' by Edward Hoare, a mysterious set of ancient treaties is discovered, revealing a hidden history of Jerusalem. As the protagonist delves deeper into the secrets of these treaties, they uncover a web of intrigue, betrayal, and hope that spans centuries. With each revelation, the reader is drawn into a captivating journey through time, where the fate of Jerusalem hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: History, Identity, Faith, Redemption
