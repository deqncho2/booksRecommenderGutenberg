Synopsis: In 'The City of Comrades' by Basil King, a young artist named John Strickland finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and mystery when he moves to New York City. As he navigates the bustling streets and encounters a diverse cast of characters, John becomes entangled in a secret society that challenges his perceptions of art, love, and the meaning of life. With each twist and turn, he must confront his own desires and ambitions, ultimately leading him to question the very fabric of society itself.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Literary Fiction

Themes: Art and creativity, Identity and self-discovery, Society and its conventions
