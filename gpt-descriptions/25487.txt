Synopsis: In 'Peck's Bad Boy and His Pa: 1883', George W. Peck takes us on a hilarious and mischievous journey through the antics of a young troublemaker and his exasperated father. Set in the late 19th century, this delightful tale follows the escapades of Peck's Bad Boy as he navigates school, friendships, and family life, leaving chaos in his wake. From pranks and practical jokes to unexpected twists, this heartwarming and humorous story will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what mischief the Bad Boy will get into next.

Genres: Humor, Fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family dynamics, Childhood mischief, Parent-child relationships
