Synopsis: In "Granny's Chapters" (on scriptural subjects): The New Testament, with a Sketch of the Subsequent History of the Jews, Mary, Lady Ross takes readers on a captivating journey through the New Testament, exploring its teachings and delving into the fascinating history of the Jewish people that followed. Through her unique perspective, Lady Ross offers a fresh and insightful look into the scriptures, shedding light on their significance and relevance in our lives today.

Genres: Religious, Historical, Educational

Themes: Scripture interpretation, Jewish history, Faith, Redemption
