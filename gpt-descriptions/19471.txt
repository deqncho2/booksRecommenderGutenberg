Synopsis: In a future society where medical advancements have eliminated most diseases, Dr. Daniel Feldman finds himself branded with a "Badge of Infamy" for a crime he did not commit. Forced to live on the fringes of society, he becomes a reluctant hero, fighting against a corrupt system and uncovering shocking truths that could change everything.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Medical Thriller

Themes: Social injustice, Redemption, Ethics in medicine
