Synopsis: 

In 'Rat Proofing Buildings and Premises', authors Betts, Crouch, and Silver delve into the fascinating world of rat prevention and control. This comprehensive guide offers practical advice and innovative techniques to protect buildings and premises from these cunning creatures. From identifying potential entry points to implementing effective deterrents, the authors provide invaluable insights into the art of rat proofing. With real-life case studies and expert tips, this book is a must-read for anyone seeking to outsmart these resilient rodents.

Genres: Non-fiction, Home Improvement, Pest Control

Themes: Rat prevention, Building maintenance, Pest management
