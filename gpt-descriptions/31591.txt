Synopsis: In "Language of Flowers" by Kate Greenaway, a young girl named Flora discovers the enchanting world of flowers and their secret meanings. Through her journey, she learns to communicate her emotions and desires using the language of flowers, unlocking a hidden realm of beauty and symbolism. As Flora navigates the complexities of human relationships, she finds solace and understanding in the language of nature, ultimately discovering the transformative power of love and self-expression.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Communication, Nature, Emotions, Love, Self-expression
