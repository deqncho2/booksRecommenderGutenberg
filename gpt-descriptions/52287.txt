Synopsis: In 'A Struggle for a Fortune' by Castlemon, Harry, a young and ambitious protagonist finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he unexpectedly inherits a vast fortune. As he navigates through a world of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, Harry must uncover the truth behind his newfound wealth and fight to protect what is rightfully his. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Harry's determination is put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the challenges that come with his fortune, or will he lose everything he holds dear?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Inheritance, Deception, Loyalty, Ambition
