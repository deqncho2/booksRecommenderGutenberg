Synopsis: 
'Folklore of the Santal Parganas' by Cecil Henry Bompas is a captivating collection of enchanting tales that transport readers into the mystical world of the Santal people. Through vivid storytelling, Bompas delves into the rich cultural heritage of this indigenous community, unveiling a tapestry of myths, legends, and folktales that are both mesmerizing and thought-provoking. From tales of shape-shifting spirits to epic battles between gods and demons, this anthology offers a glimpse into a realm where the supernatural intertwines with everyday life. As readers immerse themselves in these captivating narratives, they will discover the profound wisdom, moral lessons, and timeless truths hidden within the folklore of the Santal Parganas.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Anthropology

Themes: Cultural heritage, Supernatural beliefs, Moral lessons
