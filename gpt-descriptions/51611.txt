Synopsis: In 'The Silent Call' by Edwin Milton Royle, a mysterious and haunting tale unfolds as a young woman named Marion finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception. When she receives a cryptic message from an unknown sender, Marion embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind her family's dark past. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Marion becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between reality and illusion blurs. With each step closer to the truth, Marion must confront her own fears and confront the silent call that beckons her towards an unimaginable destiny.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Perception vs. Reality
