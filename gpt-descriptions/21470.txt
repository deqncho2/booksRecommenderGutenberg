Synopsis: In "The Missing Ship: The Log of the "Ouzel" Galley" by William Henry Giles Kingston, a young sailor named Jack ventures on a perilous journey aboard the "Ouzel" galley. As the ship sets sail, it mysteriously disappears, leaving Jack and his crewmates stranded in uncharted waters. Determined to uncover the truth behind the ship's disappearance, Jack embarks on a thrilling quest filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will he be able to solve the mystery and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Courage
