Synopsis: "My Merry Rockhurst" is a captivating historical novel that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the life of Viscount Rockhurst, a close friend of King Charles II and former Constable of the Tower of London. Set in the vibrant and scandalous court of the Restoration era, the story follows Rockhurst as he navigates the treacherous political landscape, engages in daring adventures, and experiences passionate love affairs. With its richly detailed historical backdrop and a cast of intriguing characters, this novel promises to transport readers to a time of opulence, intrigue, and danger.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Power, Love, Intrigue
