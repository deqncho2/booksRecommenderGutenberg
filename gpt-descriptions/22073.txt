Synopsis: In a futuristic world where advanced technology is the norm, Bob is a skilled repairman who specializes in fixing malfunctioning machines. However, when he receives a mysterious request to repair a device that is said to possess unimaginable power, Bob finds himself embarking on a dangerous journey that will challenge his skills and beliefs. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the enigmatic device, Bob must navigate a treacherous landscape of corporate greed, political intrigue, and the blurred lines between man and machine.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Technology, Power, Morality
