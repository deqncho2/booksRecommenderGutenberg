Synopsis: In 'Willing to Die: A Novel' by Joseph Sheridan Le Fanu, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she becomes the target of a sinister plot. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding her family, she must navigate a treacherous world of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With her life hanging in the balance, Evelyn must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity, Survival
