Synopsis: In 'A Hoodoo Machine; or, The Motor Boys' Runabout No. 1313.: Brave and Bold Weekly No. 363', the Motor Boys find themselves entangled in a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a mysterious contraption with supernatural powers. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the hoodoo machine, they must navigate treacherous obstacles, outsmart cunning adversaries, and uncover the truth behind its enigmatic origins. With danger lurking at every turn, the Motor Boys must summon their courage and resourcefulness to solve the puzzle before it's too late.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
