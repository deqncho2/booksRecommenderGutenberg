Synopsis: 
In 'The Henchman' by Luther, Mark Lee, we are introduced to a world of secret organizations, high-stakes missions, and a protagonist torn between loyalty and personal growth. The story follows a skilled henchman who has always been content with his role as the right-hand man to a powerful villain. However, when he is assigned to protect a mysterious woman with a hidden agenda, his loyalties are tested, and he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit, danger, and unexpected alliances. As the henchman delves deeper into the secrets of his own past and the true nature of his employer, he must decide where his true allegiance lies and whether he can break free from the chains that bind him.

Genres: Action, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Redemption
