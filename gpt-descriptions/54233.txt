Synopsis: 

In 'The Vivisectors' Directory', readers are taken on a captivating journey through the world of vivisection and physiology, as they explore a comprehensive list of licensed vivisectors in the United Kingdom, along with the leading physiologists in foreign laboratories. This intriguing directory unveils the hidden world of scientific experimentation, shedding light on the individuals who push the boundaries of medical knowledge and ethical dilemmas they face. From the cutting-edge research facilities in the UK to the groundbreaking work happening abroad, this directory offers a unique glimpse into the lives and work of these pioneering scientists.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Medical

Themes: Ethics, Scientific Advancement, Morality, Animal Rights
