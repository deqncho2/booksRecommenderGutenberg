Synopsis: 

'Westminster' is a captivating historical novel that takes readers on a journey through the bustling streets of London in the 19th century. Set against the backdrop of the iconic Westminster Abbey, the story follows the lives of several intriguing characters whose paths intertwine in unexpected ways. From a young artist struggling to make a name for himself to a mysterious woman with a hidden past, the novel delves into the secrets, scandals, and ambitions that lie within the heart of the city. As the characters navigate the treacherous waters of love, power, and betrayal, they must confront their own desires and face the consequences of their actions. With vivid descriptions and a richly detailed setting, 'Westminster' immerses readers in a world of intrigue, passion, and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Ambition, Secrets, Betrayal, Love, Social Class
