Synopsis: In 'The Ranch Girls and Their Heart's Desire' by Margaret Vandercook, four spirited sisters find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a hidden treasure map. As they navigate the rugged terrain of their family's ranch, they encounter unexpected challenges, forge new friendships, and uncover secrets that will change their lives forever. With determination and courage, the Ranch Girls set out to fulfill their heart's desire, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and unforgettable experiences.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family bonds, Friendship, Pursuit of dreams, Self-discovery
