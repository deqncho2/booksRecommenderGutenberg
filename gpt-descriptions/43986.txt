Synopsis: In 'The Criminal & the Community' by Devon James, a notorious criminal finds himself in an unexpected situation when he is forced to interact with the very community he has been preying upon. As he navigates this unfamiliar territory, he begins to question his own actions and the consequences they have on those around him. With unexpected alliances and surprising revelations, this gripping tale explores the complexities of redemption, forgiveness, and the power of human connection.

Genres: Crime fiction, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Morality, Community, Consequences
