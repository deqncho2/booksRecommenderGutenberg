Synopsis: In 'Active Service' by Stephen Crane, a group of American tourists embarks on a journey to the war-torn Balkans, seeking adventure and excitement. As they navigate through the chaos and danger of the conflict, they are confronted with unexpected challenges that test their courage and resilience. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, Crane takes readers on a thrilling and thought-provoking exploration of the human spirit in the face of war.

Genres: Adventure, War Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Survival, Exploration of Human Nature
