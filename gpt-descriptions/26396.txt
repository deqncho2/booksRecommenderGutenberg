Synopsis: 
When a wealthy businessman mysteriously disappears, his bumbling assistant finds himself thrust into the role of detective. With no experience and a knack for stumbling into trouble, he must navigate a web of clues, red herrings, and eccentric suspects to solve the perplexing case. As the assistant delves deeper into the mystery, he uncovers shocking secrets and unexpected twists that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Crime

Themes: Amateur detective, Unexpected heroes, Unraveling secrets
