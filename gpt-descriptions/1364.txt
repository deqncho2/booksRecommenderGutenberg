Synopsis: In "Tom Swift and His Electric Locomotive; Or, Two Miles a Minute on the Rails," the brilliant young inventor Tom Swift embarks on a thrilling adventure to revolutionize the world of transportation. When a powerful and mysterious corporation threatens to monopolize the railway industry, Tom and his friends must race against time to develop an electric locomotive capable of reaching unprecedented speeds. As they face numerous obstacles and encounter unexpected allies, Tom's ingenuity and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he succeed in his mission to transform the future of rail travel?

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Innovation, Competition, Friendship
