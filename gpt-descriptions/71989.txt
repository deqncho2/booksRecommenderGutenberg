Synopsis: 

In "The Salabue Stradivari: A history and critical description of the famous violin, commonly called 'le Messie'", W.E. Hill & Sons take readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of one of the most renowned violins in history. This meticulously researched and beautifully written book delves into the origins, craftsmanship, and mysterious history surrounding the Salabue Stradivari, also known as "le Messie". From its creation by the legendary Antonio Stradivari to its rumored curse and the enigmatic figures who have owned it, this book unveils the secrets and allure of this extraordinary instrument. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, History

Themes: Artistry, Legacy, Mystery
