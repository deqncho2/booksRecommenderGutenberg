Synopsis:
In "Afghanistan and the Anglo-Russian Dispute," Rodenbough delves into the complex and intriguing history of the power struggle between Afghanistan, Great Britain, and Russia. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, he uncovers the hidden motives, political maneuverings, and military strategies that shaped the region's destiny. As tensions rise and alliances shift, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the treacherous landscapes of diplomacy and warfare, where the fate of nations hangs in the balance.

Genres:
Historical non-fiction, Political thriller, Military history

Themes:
Power struggles, Geopolitics, Diplomacy, War
