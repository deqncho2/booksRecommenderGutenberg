Synopsis: In 'Cursed by a Fortune' by George Manville Fenn, a young man named Jack is unexpectedly thrust into a world of mystery and danger when he inherits a fortune from a distant relative. As he delves deeper into his newfound wealth, Jack discovers that his inheritance comes with a dark secret that threatens to unravel his life. With the help of a resourceful friend, he embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the curse that haunts his family. As Jack navigates treacherous paths and encounters enigmatic characters, he must confront his own fears and make difficult choices that will determine his fate.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Fate, Courage
