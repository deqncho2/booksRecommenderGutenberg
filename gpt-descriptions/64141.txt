Synopsis: In 'Slay-Ride' by Winston K. Marks, a seemingly idyllic winter getaway turns into a chilling nightmare when a group of friends find themselves trapped in a remote cabin during a snowstorm. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, they must confront their deepest fears and fight for their lives against an unknown and relentless enemy. With each passing moment, the line between friend and foe becomes blurred, and survival becomes the ultimate game.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Survival, Trust, Psychological Warfare
