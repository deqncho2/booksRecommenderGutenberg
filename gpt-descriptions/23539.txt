Synopsis: "Where Angels Fear to Tread" and Other Stories of the Sea is a captivating collection of tales that will transport readers to the vast and mysterious world of the ocean. From treacherous shipwrecks to haunting encounters with mythical creatures, each story delves into the depths of human emotions and the untamed power of the sea. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, Robertson Morgan weaves together a tapestry of thrilling adventures that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human Nature
