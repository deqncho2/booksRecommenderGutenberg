Synopsis: 
In 'Psichopath' by Garrett Randall, a renowned criminal profiler finds himself entangled in a twisted game of cat and mouse with a cunning serial killer. As the body count rises and the stakes become increasingly personal, the profiler must delve into the darkest corners of the human psyche to outsmart the psychopath before it's too late. With unexpected twists and psychological suspense, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Serial killers, Criminal profiling, Psychological manipulation
