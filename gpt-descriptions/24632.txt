Synopsis: 
In 'The Fifth Form at Saint Dominic's: A School Story', the students of Saint Dominic's face new challenges as they enter their fifth year at the prestigious boarding school. With the arrival of a new headmaster and the introduction of a mysterious new student, tensions rise and friendships are tested. As the boys navigate through academic pressures, sporting rivalries, and the complexities of adolescence, they must also confront a series of unexpected events that threaten to disrupt the harmony of their school life. Will they be able to overcome these obstacles and prove their loyalty to Saint Dominic's?

Genres: School story, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Identity, Resilience
