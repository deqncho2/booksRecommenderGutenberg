Synopsis: In 'The Gnome King of Oz' by Ruth Plumly Thompson, a mischievous gnome named Klik embarks on a quest to become the ruler of Oz. With the help of his magical powers and a group of eccentric companions, Klik sets out to challenge the current ruler, Princess Ozma. As they journey through enchanting lands and encounter peculiar creatures, Klik's determination and cunning are put to the test. Will he succeed in his quest for power, or will he discover that true leadership lies in something far greater?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Power and Leadership, Friendship, Self-discovery
