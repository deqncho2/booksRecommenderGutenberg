Synopsis: 
'Jeanne d'Arc, Maid of Orleans, Deliverer of France: Being the Story of Her Life, Her Achievements, and Her Death, as Attested on Oath and Set Forth in the Original Documents' takes readers on a captivating journey through the extraordinary life of Jeanne d'Arc, a young woman who defied societal norms and led an army to liberate her country. This meticulously researched account unveils the trials and triumphs of a legendary figure, revealing the untold details of her remarkable achievements and tragic fate. As the story unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the courage, determination, and unwavering faith of this iconic historical figure.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Heroism, Faith, Gender roles, National identity
