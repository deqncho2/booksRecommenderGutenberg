Synopsis: In 'The Evolution of the Idea of God: An Inquiry Into the Origins of Religions', Allen Grant takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of religious beliefs. From ancient civilizations to modern societies, this thought-provoking exploration delves into the origins and development of the concept of God. Through meticulous research and compelling analysis, Allen Grant uncovers the fascinating evolution of religious ideas, shedding light on the diverse ways in which humans have sought to understand and connect with the divine.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, History

Themes: Religion, Evolution of Beliefs, Human Spirituality
