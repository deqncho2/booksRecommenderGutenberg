Synopsis: 
In 'Tracked by Wireless' by William Le Queux, a young inventor named Gilbert Larose finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a secret wireless communication system. As he delves deeper into the clandestine world of espionage, Larose becomes the target of a sinister organization determined to silence him at any cost. With his life on the line, Larose must use his wits and technological prowess to outsmart his pursuers and uncover the truth behind the wireless conspiracy.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Technology, Espionage, Intrigue
