Synopsis: In the second volume of "Prince Zilah," the captivating story continues as the eponymous character, Prince Zilah, finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and personal redemption. As he navigates the treacherous waters of power and passion, Zilah's loyalty and honor are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and turns, this volume delves deeper into the complex world of Prince Zilah, revealing hidden secrets and challenging his very identity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Power and Corruption, Love and Betrayal, Identity and Redemption
