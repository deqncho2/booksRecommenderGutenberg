Synopsis: 
In "Slovenly Betsy" by Heinrich Hoffmann, we are introduced to a young girl named Betsy who seems to have an uncanny ability to find herself in all sorts of messy situations. From refusing to wash her hands to neglecting her chores, Betsy's slovenly behavior leads to a series of comical and sometimes disastrous consequences. As we follow Betsy's misadventures, we can't help but wonder if she will ever learn her lesson and discover the importance of cleanliness and responsibility.

Genres: Children's literature, Comedy, Morality tale

Themes: Cleanliness, Responsibility, Consequences
