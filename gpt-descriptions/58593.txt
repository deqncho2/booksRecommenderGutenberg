Synopsis: In 'The Garden of God' by H. De Vere Stacpoole, a young man embarks on a journey to a remote island in the South Pacific, where he encounters a hidden paradise untouched by civilization. As he explores the lush landscapes and encounters the island's inhabitants, he becomes entangled in a web of love, desire, and forbidden secrets. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this novel takes readers on a mesmerizing adventure that will leave them yearning for their own escape to the Garden of God.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of untouched nature, Forbidden love, Quest for paradise
