Synopsis: In "The Whirl: A Romance of Washington Society," Davis Foxcroft takes readers on a captivating journey through the glamorous and scandalous world of Washington, D.C.'s elite. Amidst the backdrop of political power plays and social intrigue, the story follows the lives of two young women, one born into privilege and the other striving to climb the social ladder. As they navigate the treacherous waters of love, ambition, and betrayal, secrets are unveiled and alliances are tested, ultimately leading to unexpected consequences that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Political Drama

Themes: Social class, Ambition, Betrayal, Power dynamics
