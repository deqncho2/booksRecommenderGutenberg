Synopsis: In 'Hilaria. The Festive Board', a mysterious invitation leads a group of strangers to a grand celebration filled with enchantment and intrigue. As the night unfolds, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the true nature of the gathering is revealed. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a world where nothing is as it seems, and where the line between reality and fantasy becomes blurred.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Perception
