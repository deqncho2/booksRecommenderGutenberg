Synopsis: 

In 'Trial of the Major War Criminals Before the International Military Tribunal, Nuremberg, 14 November 1945-1 October 1946, Volume 12', readers are taken inside the historic courtroom where the most notorious figures of the Nazi regime are brought to justice. This gripping volume presents a detailed account of the trial proceedings, revealing the shocking testimonies, legal arguments, and dramatic confrontations that unfolded during this landmark event. As the prosecution seeks to prove the guilt of the accused, the defense fights to undermine the credibility of the trial itself. With the world watching, the outcome of this trial will have far-reaching consequences for the future of international justice.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Legal drama, World War II history

Themes: Justice, Accountability, Human rights, War crimes, Historical memory
