Synopsis: In 'Motor Matt's Triumph; or, Three Speeds Forward' by Stanley R. Matthews, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside the daring and resourceful Motor Matt. When a mysterious gang threatens the safety of a small town, Motor Matt must rely on his quick thinking, mechanical skills, and unwavering determination to uncover the truth and bring justice to those in need. With high-speed chases, unexpected twists, and a race against time, this action-packed adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Justice
