Synopsis: In 'Katia' by Leo Tolstoy, a young woman named Katia finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a passionate love affair. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Russia, this captivating novella explores the complexities of love, societal expectations, and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and desire, Duty and societal expectations
