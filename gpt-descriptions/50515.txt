Synopsis: In 'The Sack of Monte Carlo: An Adventure of To-day' by Frith, Walter, a group of audacious individuals embarks on a daring heist in the glamorous city of Monte Carlo. As they meticulously plan their operation, the stakes rise higher than ever before, leading to a thrilling and unpredictable adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the lengths people will go to for wealth and the consequences that follow.

Genres: Thriller, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Greed, Risk-taking, Consequences
