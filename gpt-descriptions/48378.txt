Synopsis: 

In 'Fireplaces and Chimneys', Miller and Senner take readers on a captivating journey through the history and evolution of fireplaces and chimneys. From ancient civilizations to modern times, the authors explore the cultural significance, architectural marvels, and technological advancements surrounding these essential elements of our homes. Through vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, they delve into the artistry and craftsmanship behind fireplaces and chimneys, revealing their profound impact on society and the way we live. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Architecture

Themes: Cultural significance, Technological advancements, Architectural craftsmanship
