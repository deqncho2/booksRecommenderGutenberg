Synopsis: In "Frank Merriwell at Yale; Or, Freshman Against Freshman," Frank Merriwell, a talented athlete and scholar, begins his journey as a freshman at Yale University. As he navigates the challenges of college life, he finds himself caught in a web of rivalries, mysteries, and unexpected friendships. With his unwavering determination and exceptional skills, Frank must face off against his fellow freshmen in a series of intense competitions, both on and off the field. As the stakes rise, Frank's character is tested, and he must prove himself not only as a formidable athlete but also as a true leader. Will Frank be able to overcome the obstacles and emerge victorious in this thrilling tale of freshman rivalry?

Genres: Adventure, Sports, Mystery

Themes: Rivalry, Friendship, Leadership, Determination
