Synopsis: 
'Six Short Plays' by John Galsworthy is a collection of thought-provoking and emotionally charged plays that delve into the complexities of human relationships and societal norms. From tales of forbidden love to the struggles of the working class, each play presents a unique and captivating story that will leave readers questioning their own beliefs and values. With a mix of drama, romance, and social commentary, Galsworthy's collection offers a compelling exploration of the human condition.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Love and Desire, Class and Society, Morality and Ethics
