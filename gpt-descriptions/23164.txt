Synopsis: In the depths of Cleveland, a mysterious creature lurks, terrorizing the city and leaving a trail of destruction in its wake. As the residents grapple with the fear and chaos, a group of unlikely heroes emerges, determined to uncover the truth behind the creature's origins and put an end to its reign of terror. With each step closer to the truth, they find themselves drawn into a dark and dangerous world they never could have imagined.

Genres: Horror, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Fear, Exploration of the Unknown, Human Nature
