Synopsis: 

'Popular lessons in astronomy, on a new plan' takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders of the universe. Written by Francis J. Grund, this book presents the principles of astronomy in a unique and engaging way, using vivid comparisons instead of complex numerical explanations. From the mesmerizing dance of celestial bodies to the awe-inspiring beauty of distant galaxies, Grund's work offers a fresh perspective on the mysteries of the cosmos. Prepare to be enthralled as you explore the vastness of space and unlock the secrets of the stars.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction, Education

Themes: Astronomy, Universe, Comparative Illustrations
