Synopsis: In 'The Golden Magnet' by George Manville Fenn, a young sailor named Dick Coppinger embarks on a perilous journey across the seas, driven by a mysterious golden magnet that possesses an uncanny power. As he faces treacherous waters, encounters with pirates, and the allure of hidden treasure, Dick must navigate the fine line between his desires and the consequences they may bring. With danger lurking at every turn, the story unfolds with thrilling adventures and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Temptation, Fate
