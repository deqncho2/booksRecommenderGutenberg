Synopsis: In 'The Secret of Heroism: A Memoir of Henry Albert Harper', author William Lyon Mackenzie King delves into the extraordinary life of Henry Albert Harper, a man whose acts of heroism remain shrouded in mystery. As King uncovers the truth behind Harper's enigmatic persona, he takes readers on a captivating journey through war-torn landscapes, secret missions, and the depths of human courage. With each revelation, the reader becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, questioning the true nature of heroism and the sacrifices one must make in the face of adversity.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Identity, War
