Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 156, September 4, 1915: Blood Will Tell; or, Nick Carter's Play in Politics', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue when he is hired to investigate a mysterious murder that threatens to disrupt the upcoming election. As he delves deeper into the case, Carter uncovers a dangerous conspiracy that reaches the highest levels of power, forcing him to navigate treacherous waters where loyalty and betrayal blur the lines. With time running out and the stakes higher than ever, Nick Carter must use all his cunning and resourcefulness to unravel the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Political intrigue, Conspiracy, Power struggle
