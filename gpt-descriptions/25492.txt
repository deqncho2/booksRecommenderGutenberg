Synopsis: In 'How Private George W. Peck Put Down the Rebellion: or, The Funny Experiences of a Raw Recruit', George W. Peck takes us on a hilarious journey through his time as a raw recruit during the Civil War. From comical mishaps to unexpected encounters, Peck's witty storytelling will keep readers entertained as they follow his adventures on the battlefield. 

Genres: Memoir, Humor, Historical

Themes: War, Humor, Coming-of-age
