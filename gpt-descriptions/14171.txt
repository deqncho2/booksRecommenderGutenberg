Synopsis: In 'A Man Four-Square' by William MacLeod Raine, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small western town, capturing the attention and curiosity of the locals. As his true identity and purpose slowly unravel, the town becomes embroiled in a web of secrets, revenge, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the stranger's past and the town's future become intertwined in a thrilling tale of redemption and justice.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Revenge, Redemption
