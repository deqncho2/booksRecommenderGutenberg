Synopsis: In 'The Dark Tower' by Bottome, Phyllis, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling streets of London to the mysterious and enchanting landscapes of the Scottish Highlands. Drawn into a web of secrets and hidden identities, Mary finds herself torn between love and duty, as she uncovers the truth about her own past and the dark forces that threaten her future. With its captivating blend of romance, suspense, and supernatural elements, 'The Dark Tower' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Love and Duty
