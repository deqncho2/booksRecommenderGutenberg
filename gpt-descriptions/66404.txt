Synopsis: 

In "Historic Litchfield: address delivered at the bi-centennial celebration of the town of Litchfield, August 1, 1920," Morris Woodruff Seymour takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of Litchfield, a town with a fascinating past. From its humble beginnings to its significant role in the American Revolution, Seymour paints a vivid picture of the town's evolution, highlighting the remarkable individuals and events that shaped its destiny. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, this address offers a captivating glimpse into the captivating history of Litchfield, leaving readers eager to uncover the secrets and triumphs that lie within its storied past.

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: Local history, American Revolution, Community development
