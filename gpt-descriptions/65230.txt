Synopsis: In 'The Mistake of Christopher Columbus' by Archer, Jules, a renowned historian stumbles upon a hidden journal that challenges everything we thought we knew about Christopher Columbus. As he delves deeper into the mysterious contents, he uncovers a shocking secret that could rewrite history. With each revelation, the line between truth and fiction becomes blurred, leading him on a thrilling journey of discovery and self-reflection.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Truth and deception, Exploration and discovery
