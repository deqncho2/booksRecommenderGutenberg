Synopsis: 
'Overland Tales' by Josephine Clifford takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed American West. Set against the backdrop of the late 1800s, this collection of interconnected stories introduces a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets, desires, and ambitions. From rugged cowboys to fearless outlaws, from determined pioneers to mysterious Native Americans, the paths of these individuals intertwine as they navigate the treacherous landscapes and face the challenges of survival, love, and redemption. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, 'Overland Tales' paints a vivid portrait of a time and place where danger lurks around every corner and where the human spirit is tested to its limits.

Genres: Western, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Love, Redemption, Exploration of the American West
