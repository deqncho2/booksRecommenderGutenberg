Synopsis: In the remote and mysterious Dartmoor region of England, a young woman named Mary meets a captivating stranger who seems to possess an uncanny connection to the ancient landscape. As their paths intertwine, Mary becomes entangled in a web of secrets, folklore, and hidden desires that will challenge her perception of reality and lead her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Identity, Nature vs. Civilization, Supernatural, Love and Desire
