Synopsis: In 'Unbegotten Child' by Winston K. Marks, a renowned scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges the very fabric of human existence. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a secret that could change the course of humanity forever. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with moral dilemmas, unexpected alliances, and a race against time. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of science and the essence of what it means to be human.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Identity, Scientific Responsibility
