Synopsis: 

In "The Origin of Species by Means of Natural Selection: Or, the Preservation of Favoured Races in the Struggle for Life, 6th Edition," Charles Darwin takes readers on a captivating journey through the natural world, unveiling a groundbreaking theory that challenges traditional beliefs about the origins of life. Through meticulous observations and compelling evidence, Darwin presents his revolutionary concept of natural selection, where species evolve and adapt to their environments over time. This edition delves deeper into the intricate mechanisms of evolution, shedding light on the diverse forms of life and the intricate web of interconnections that shape our planet. 

Genres: Science, Non-fiction, Evolutionary Biology

Themes: Evolution, Adaptation, Survival of the Fittest, Biodiversity, Natural Selection
