Synopsis: In "The House in the Mist" by Anna Katharine Green, a young woman named Violet Strange finds herself drawn to a mysterious house shrouded in fog. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within its walls, she becomes entangled in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected romance. With each twist and turn, Violet must navigate the treacherous path to uncover the truth, all while facing her own fears and insecurities.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the power of perception, the blurred line between reality and illusion
