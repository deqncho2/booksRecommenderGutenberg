Synopsis: 

In "The Battle of Gettysburg: The Country, the Contestants, the Results" by W. C. Storrick, readers are transported to the heart of one of the most pivotal moments in American history. This gripping account delves into the events leading up to the Battle of Gettysburg, the key players involved, and the dramatic consequences that unfolded on the battlefield. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Storrick brings to life the bravery, sacrifice, and strategic maneuvers that shaped the outcome of this historic clash.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: War and its impact, Leadership and decision-making, National identity and unity
