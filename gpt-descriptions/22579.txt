Synopsis: In "Bread Overhead" by Fritz Leiber, a seemingly ordinary morning commute takes an unexpected turn when a man finds himself caught in a bizarre and hilarious situation involving a loaf of bread. As the events unfold, the protagonist must navigate through a series of absurd and comical encounters, leading to a surprising and thought-provoking conclusion. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Satire, Comedy

Themes: Absurdity of bureaucracy, Consumerism, Individuality
