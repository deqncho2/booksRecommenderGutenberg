Synopsis: 

'Chinese literature: Comprising the Analects of Confucius, the Sayings of Mencius, the Shi-King, the Travels of Fâ-Hien, and the Sorrows of Han' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Chinese literature. This collection includes the profound teachings of Confucius and Mencius, the timeless poetry of the Shi-King, the remarkable travelogue of Fâ-Hien, and the poignant tale of the Sorrows of Han. Each work offers unique insights into the wisdom, culture, and history of ancient China, providing a fascinating glimpse into a civilization that has shaped the world.

Genres: Philosophy, Poetry, Travelogue, Historical Fiction

Themes: Wisdom, Morality, Human Nature, Cultural Identity, Historical Events
