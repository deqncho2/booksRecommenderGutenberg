Synopsis: In the small town of Greenfield, a farmer named John finds himself at the center of a mysterious event that shakes his community to its core. As he navigates through the complexities of rural life, John discovers hidden secrets, unexpected alliances, and the power of unity. With the fate of his farm and the well-being of his neighbors hanging in the balance, John must confront his own fears and make a choice that will forever change the course of his life and the community he holds dear.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Fiction

Themes: Community, Identity, Resilience, Secrets
