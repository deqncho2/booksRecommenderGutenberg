Synopsis: 
In 'Ave Roma Immortalis, Vol. 2: Studies from the Chronicles of Rome', F. Marion Crawford takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient city of Rome. Through a series of interconnected stories, Crawford delves into the lives of various characters, both historical and fictional, who have left their mark on the eternal city. From the grandeur of the Colosseum to the intrigue of the Roman Senate, this volume offers a vivid and immersive exploration of Rome's rich history and culture. As the tales unfold, readers will find themselves enthralled by the triumphs and tragedies of these captivating individuals, and will gain a deeper understanding of the enduring legacy of Rome.

Genres: Historical fiction, Anthology, Roman literature

Themes: History, Power and politics, Cultural heritage
