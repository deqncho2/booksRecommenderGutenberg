Synopsis: In 'The Keeper of the Door' by Ethel M. Dell, a young woman named Joan Lowrie finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she becomes entangled with a secretive and enigmatic man. As their paths intertwine, Joan is faced with a series of unexpected events that challenge her beliefs and force her to confront her deepest fears. With each twist and turn, the story takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with suspense, romance, and the quest for truth.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and sacrifice
