Synopsis: 
In 'Mary, Help of Christians, and the Fourteen Saints Invoked as Holy Helpers', readers are invited into a world of spiritual guidance and devotion. This comprehensive book offers instructions, novenas, and prayers for every day of the year, accompanied by profound thoughts from the saints. As readers delve into the pages, they will discover a wealth of wisdom and inspiration, exploring the power of Mary and the fourteen holy helpers in their lives. Additionally, an appendix explores the reasonableness of Catholic ceremonies and practices, shedding light on the deeper meaning behind these rituals. This book is a treasure trove for those seeking spiritual growth and a deeper connection with their faith.

Genres: Religious, Devotional, Spiritual

Themes: Catholicism, Devotion, Spiritual Guidance, Faith, Prayer
