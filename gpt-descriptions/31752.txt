Synopsis: In 'The Gold Sickle; Or, Hena, The Virgin of The Isle of Sen', we are transported to the mystical world of Druid Gaul, where the young and enigmatic Hena resides. Raised in seclusion on the Isle of Sen, Hena possesses a secret power that sets her apart from others. As she embarks on a journey to discover her true identity and purpose, she becomes entangled in a web of ancient prophecies, forbidden love, and the clash between the old ways and the encroaching Roman Empire. With danger lurking at every turn, Hena must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of her people.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Forbidden Love, Power Struggles, Ancient Mysteries
