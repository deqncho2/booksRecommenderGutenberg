Synopsis: 
'The New Penelope, and Other Stories and Poems' by Victor, Frances Fuller is a captivating collection of tales that will transport readers to different worlds and evoke a range of emotions. From the enchanting story of a young girl's journey through a magical forest to the haunting poem about lost love, each piece in this collection is a unique and thought-provoking exploration of the human experience. With vivid imagery and lyrical prose, Victor, Frances Fuller invites readers to delve into the depths of imagination and discover the beauty and complexity of life.

Genres: Fiction, Poetry, Fantasy

Themes: Imagination, Love, Loss, Transformation
