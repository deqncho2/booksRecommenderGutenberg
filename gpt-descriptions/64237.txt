Synopsis:
In "Old Age Deferred: The causes of old age and its postponement by hygienic and therapeutic measures," Arnold Lorand delves into the fascinating realm of aging and explores the various factors that contribute to the process of growing old. Through a meticulous examination of hygienic and therapeutic measures, Lorand uncovers the secrets to postponing old age and unlocking the potential for a longer, healthier life. With a blend of scientific research and practical advice, this book offers a captivating exploration of the mysteries surrounding aging and provides valuable insights into how we can delay its effects.

Genres: Non-fiction, Health, Science

Themes: Aging, Longevity, Health and Wellness
