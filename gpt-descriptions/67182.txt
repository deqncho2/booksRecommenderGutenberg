Synopsis: 

In 'A History of Epidemic Pestilences: From the Earliest Ages, 1495 Years Before the Birth of our Saviour to 1848: With Researches into Their Nature, Causes, and Prophylaxis,' Edward Bascome takes readers on a captivating journey through centuries of devastating epidemics. From ancient times to the mid-19th century, Bascome delves into the origins, nature, and prevention of these deadly diseases, offering a comprehensive exploration of the human struggle against pestilences. With meticulous research and compelling narratives, this book sheds light on the profound impact of epidemics on societies throughout history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Medical

Themes: Epidemics, Disease, Public Health, Historical Impact
