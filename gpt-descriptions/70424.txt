Synopsis: 
'Old Nurse's Book of Rhymes, Jingles and Ditties' takes readers on a whimsical journey through a collection of enchanting verses and playful melodies. From lullabies that soothe the soul to riddles that tickle the mind, this delightful book is a treasure trove of timeless rhymes that have been passed down through generations. As readers explore the pages, they will encounter mischievous characters, magical creatures, and heartwarming tales, all woven together with the lyrical charm of the old nurse's words. Each rhyme and jingle holds a secret, inviting readers to unravel the hidden meanings and discover the joy that lies within. 

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature, Fantasy

Themes: Imagination, Tradition, Childhood, Wonder
