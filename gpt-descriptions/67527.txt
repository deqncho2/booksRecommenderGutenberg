Synopsis: In "The Foot-prints of the Creator: or, The Asterolepis of Stromness," Hugh Miller takes us on a captivating journey through the ancient landscapes of Scotland, where he uncovers a remarkable fossil that challenges the prevailing beliefs of his time. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of this prehistoric creature, Miller's quest for truth leads him to question the very foundations of science and religion, ultimately revealing a profound connection between the natural world and the divine.

Genres: Historical fiction, Science fiction, Mystery

Themes: Evolution, Faith and reason, Nature and spirituality
