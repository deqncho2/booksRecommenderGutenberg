Synopsis: In 'Sight Gag' by Janifer, Laurence M., a renowned illusionist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when a seemingly impossible trick goes horribly wrong. As he delves deeper into the secrets behind the illusion, he uncovers a dark conspiracy that threatens not only his career but also his life. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous world of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth and save himself from a sinister fate.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Illusion and reality, Deception, Betrayal
