Synopsis: In 'Attila and His Conquerors: A Story of the Days of St. Patrick and St. Leo the Great', Charles, Elizabeth Rundle takes readers on a captivating journey back to the days of ancient Rome and the fearsome Hunnic Empire. Amidst the clash of empires and the rise of Christianity, the lives of two extraordinary men, St. Patrick and St. Leo the Great, intertwine with the legendary figure of Attila the Hun. As the paths of these three powerful figures converge, a tale of courage, faith, and destiny unfolds, revealing the impact of their choices on the fate of nations.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Christian Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Power and leadership, Clash of cultures
