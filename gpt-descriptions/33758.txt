Synopsis: 
In 'Derby Day in the Yukon, and Other Poems of the "Northland"', Kate Simpson Hayes takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged and enchanting landscapes of the Yukon. From the exhilarating atmosphere of the annual Derby Day race to the haunting tales of gold rush pioneers, Hayes weaves together a collection of poems that celebrate the spirit of the Northland. Through vivid imagery and lyrical storytelling, she invites readers to experience the untamed beauty and the indomitable human spirit that thrives in this remote corner of the world.

Genres: Poetry, Adventure, Nature

Themes: Exploration, Resilience, Connection with Nature
