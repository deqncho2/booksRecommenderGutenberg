Synopsis: In 'The Story of a Monkey on a Stick' by Laura Lee Hope, a young girl named Lily discovers a mysterious monkey on a stick hidden in her grandmother's attic. Little does she know that this seemingly innocent toy holds a secret that will take her on an extraordinary adventure. As Lily unravels the truth behind the monkey on a stick, she finds herself caught in a web of danger, magic, and unexpected friendships. With each twist and turn, Lily must navigate through a world where nothing is as it seems, and where the fate of both the monkey and herself hang in the balance.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Discovery, Friendship, Magic, Courage
