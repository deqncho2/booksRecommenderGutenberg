Synopsis: 
In 'Short story classics (Foreign), Vol. 2, Italian and Scandinavian', embark on a captivating journey through the rich literary traditions of Italy and Scandinavia. This anthology brings together a collection of enchanting short stories that will transport readers to distant lands, immersing them in tales of love, mystery, and the complexities of human nature. From the sun-soaked streets of Rome to the haunting landscapes of the Nordic countries, these stories will captivate and intrigue, leaving readers yearning for more.

Genres: 
Literary Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: 
Love and Relationships, Identity, Cultural Traditions
