Synopsis: 

In 'U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1968 July - December', the Library of Congress Copyright Office takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of intellectual property. This compilation unveils a treasure trove of renewed copyrights, revealing the creative endeavors and artistic masterpieces that captivated the minds of the American public during the latter half of 1968. From literature to music, from film to photography, this collection showcases the diverse range of works that have left an indelible mark on American culture. Delve into the pages of this book and discover the hidden stories behind the copyrights that have shaped our artistic landscape.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Intellectual property, American culture, Artistic expression
