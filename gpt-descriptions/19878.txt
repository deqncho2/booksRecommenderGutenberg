Synopsis: In 'The Passaic Flood of 1903' by Marshall Ora Leighton, a small town is suddenly thrust into chaos when a devastating flood strikes, leaving its residents fighting for survival. As the waters rise and the community is pushed to its limits, the story follows a diverse cast of characters who must confront their deepest fears, forge unlikely alliances, and find strength in the face of unimaginable adversity. With gripping suspense and heart-wrenching moments, this historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through one of the most catastrophic natural disasters in American history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Adventure

Themes: Resilience, Community, Survival, Human spirit
