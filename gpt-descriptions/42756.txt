Synopsis: In 'An Isle of Surrey: A Novel' by Richard Dowling, a mysterious island off the coast of England becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of love, secrets, and self-discovery. When a young woman named Emma stumbles upon the island, she is drawn into a web of intrigue that spans generations. As she delves deeper into the island's history, Emma uncovers long-buried secrets that threaten to unravel her own life. With its lush descriptions and compelling characters, this novel takes readers on a journey that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Identity, Redemption
