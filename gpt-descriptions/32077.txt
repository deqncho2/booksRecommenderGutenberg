Synopsis: In 'Breeder Reaction' by Winston K. Marks, a renowned scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As the implications of his findings become clear, he finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between progress and ethical boundaries blurs. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect his discovery and confront the consequences of his actions.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Ethics, Scientific Responsibility, Consequences of Progress
