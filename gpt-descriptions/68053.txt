Synopsis: In 'The Frugal Life: A Paradox' by Landi, Ortensio, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, captivating its residents with his unconventional lifestyle. As he challenges their materialistic values and introduces them to the art of frugality, the townspeople find themselves torn between their desire for wealth and the allure of a simpler existence. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking encounters, this captivating tale explores the paradoxical nature of frugality and its impact on human happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Philosophical

Themes: Materialism, Simplicity, Happiness, Identity
