Synopsis: In 'Bengal Dacoits and Tigers' by Sunity Devee, Maharani of Cooch Behar, embark on a thrilling journey through the dense jungles of Bengal, where danger lurks at every turn. Follow the courageous Maharani as she unravels the mysteries surrounding the notorious dacoits and encounters the majestic tigers that roam the wilderness. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding moments, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of the Maharani and the secrets of the jungle.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Survival, Exploration
