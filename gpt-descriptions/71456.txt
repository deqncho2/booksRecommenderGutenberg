Synopsis: 

"The Literature of Kissing" takes readers on a captivating journey through history, poetry, fiction, and anecdote to explore the art and significance of the intimate act of kissing. From ancient civilizations to modern times, Charles C. Bombaugh delves into the rich tapestry of literature to uncover the various meanings, emotions, and cultural contexts associated with this universal expression of love and desire. Through a collection of enchanting stories, poems, and historical accounts, the book offers a delightful exploration of the power and beauty of the kiss, leaving readers with a newfound appreciation for this timeless gesture of affection.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, History

Themes: Love, Intimacy, Cultural Significance
