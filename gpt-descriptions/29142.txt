Synopsis: In 'Keep Out' by Fredric Brown, a small town is plagued by a mysterious force that compels its residents to stay indoors after dark. As the tension rises and fear takes hold, a group of unlikely allies must uncover the truth behind this sinister phenomenon before it consumes them all. With its gripping suspense and unexpected twists, 'Keep Out' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Fear, Survival, Community
