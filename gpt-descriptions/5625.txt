Synopsis: 
'Flint and Feather: Collected Verse' by Johnson, E. Pauline is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through the rich tapestry of Indigenous Canadian history and culture. Through her eloquent and evocative verses, Johnson explores themes of identity, love, loss, and resilience, painting a vivid portrait of the Indigenous experience. From the haunting beauty of nature to the struggles faced by Indigenous peoples, this collection offers a powerful and thought-provoking exploration of the human spirit. 

Genres: Poetry, Historical, Cultural

Themes: Indigenous culture, Identity, Love, Loss, Resilience
