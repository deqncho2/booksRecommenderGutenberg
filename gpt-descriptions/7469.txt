Synopsis: In the midst of Victorian society, a young man named Daniel Deronda finds himself torn between two worlds: the aristocratic circles he was raised in and the mysterious Jewish community he becomes drawn to. As he navigates his own identity and purpose, he becomes entangled in the lives of two captivating women, each with their own secrets and desires. With themes of love, self-discovery, and the search for meaning, 'Daniel Deronda' is a compelling tale that explores the complexities of society and the choices that shape our lives.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Bildungsroman

Themes: Identity, Social class, Religion, Love and relationships
