Synopsis: In 'Style in Singing' by Haslam, W. E., a renowned vocal coach takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of singing, exploring the diverse styles and techniques that have shaped the art of vocal performance. From classical opera to contemporary pop, this book delves into the secrets behind the mesmerizing voices that have captivated audiences for centuries. With insightful anecdotes and practical advice, the author unveils the hidden nuances of each singing style, offering a fresh perspective on the power of vocal expression. Prepare to be inspired and enlightened as you embark on this fascinating exploration of the art of singing.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Performing Arts

Themes: Vocal technique, Musical interpretation, Artistic expression
