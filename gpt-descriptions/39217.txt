Synopsis: In 'The King's Stratagem, and Other Stories' by Stanley John Weyman, readers are transported to a world of intrigue, adventure, and unexpected twists. From the cunning stratagems of a king to the daring escapades of a young soldier, this collection of stories will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Whether it's a tale of love, betrayal, or redemption, each story is filled with captivating characters and gripping narratives that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Love and Sacrifice
