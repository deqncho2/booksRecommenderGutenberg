Synopsis: In 'The Perilous Seat' by Caroline Dale Snedeker, a young noblewoman named Eleanor finds herself caught in the midst of a dangerous power struggle during the tumultuous times of King Henry II's reign. As she navigates treacherous alliances, secret plots, and forbidden love, Eleanor must decide where her loyalties truly lie and how far she is willing to go to protect those she holds dear. With vivid historical detail and gripping suspense, this captivating tale will transport readers to a world of political intrigue and personal sacrifice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Power, Love, Sacrifice
