Synopsis: In "Tom Swift Circling the Globe; or, The Daring Cruise of the Air Monarch," Tom Swift embarks on an exhilarating adventure that takes him across the globe in his remarkable airship, the Air Monarch. As he encounters various challenges and unexpected dangers, Tom's ingenuity and resourcefulness are put to the test. With his loyal friends by his side, Tom must navigate treacherous landscapes, outsmart cunning adversaries, and unravel the mysteries that lie ahead. This thrilling tale of exploration, courage, and innovation will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Ingenuity, Friendship
