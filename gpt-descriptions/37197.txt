Synopsis: In 'Problems in Periclean Buildings', Elderkin takes readers on a captivating journey through the architectural wonders of ancient Athens. From the majestic Parthenon to the intricate details of lesser-known structures, he unveils the hidden challenges faced by the master builders of the time. As he delves into the mysteries behind these architectural marvels, Elderkin uncovers a web of secrets, rivalries, and unexpected discoveries that will leave readers enthralled. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the complexities and triumphs of Periclean architecture.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Architecture, Ancient History

Themes: Architecture and society, Ancient Greek culture, Artistic expression, Historical preservation
