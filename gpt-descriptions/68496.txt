Synopsis: In 'Dwala: A Romance' by Calderon, George, a young artist named Ethan finds himself captivated by the enigmatic Dwala, a woman with a mysterious past and an irresistible allure. As their paths intertwine, Ethan is drawn into a world of passion, secrets, and unexpected twists that challenge his perception of love and identity. With each encounter, their connection deepens, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and emotional awakening. Will their love withstand the trials they face, or will the truth tear them apart?

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Identity and self-discovery, Secrets and deception
