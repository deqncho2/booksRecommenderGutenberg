Synopsis: 

In 'Shan Folk Lore Stories from the Hill and Water Country', author William Charles Griggs takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Shan folklore. From mythical creatures and magical beings to tales of love, bravery, and betrayal, this collection of stories offers a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of the Shan people. As readers delve into the pages, they will be transported to a realm where spirits roam, where ancient traditions intertwine with everyday life, and where the line between reality and fantasy becomes blurred. With each story, Griggs weaves a tapestry of wonder and intrigue, leaving readers eager to uncover the secrets hidden within the hills and waters of the Shan country.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural

Themes: Mythology, Tradition, Supernatural, Love and Betrayal
