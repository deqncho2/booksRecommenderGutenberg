Synopsis: In "The Century of Columbus" by James J. Walsh, embark on a captivating journey through the 15th century, a time of exploration, discovery, and the rise of one of history's most influential figures. This meticulously researched and engaging narrative delves into the life and adventures of Christopher Columbus, shedding light on the challenges he faced, the discoveries he made, and the impact he had on the world. From the bustling streets of Genoa to the uncharted territories of the New World, this book paints a vivid picture of an era that forever changed the course of history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Legacy
