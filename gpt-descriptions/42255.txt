Synopsis: In 'A Little Fleet' by Yeats, Jack B., a group of unlikely companions embarks on a perilous journey across treacherous waters, facing danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As they navigate through the unknown, they discover hidden truths about themselves and the world they thought they knew. With each passing challenge, their bonds are tested, and they must decide what they are willing to sacrifice for the greater good. This gripping tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
