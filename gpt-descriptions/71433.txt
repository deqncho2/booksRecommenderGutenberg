Synopsis: 
In 'The complete works of John Gower, volume 3: The English works', readers will be captivated by the eloquent and thought-provoking writings of John Gower, a prominent medieval poet. This volume showcases Gower's English works, which delve into various aspects of human nature, society, and morality. Through his compelling narratives and insightful observations, Gower explores the complexities of love, power, and the pursuit of virtue. From tales of chivalry and romance to moralistic allegories, this collection offers a fascinating glimpse into the medieval mind and the timeless themes that continue to resonate with readers today.

Genres: Poetry, Medieval Literature, Moralistic Fiction
Themes: Love, Morality, Power, Virtue
