Synopsis: In 'The Gods of Mars' by Edgar Rice Burroughs, the thrilling sequel to 'A Princess of Mars,' John Carter finds himself transported back to the mysterious and dangerous planet of Barsoom. As he reunites with old allies and faces new enemies, Carter embarks on a perilous journey to rescue his beloved Dejah Thoris from the clutches of the ancient and powerful gods of Mars. Filled with epic battles, daring escapes, and unexpected alliances, this action-packed adventure takes readers on a thrilling ride through the fantastical world of Barsoom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Love and Sacrifice, Exploration of Other Worlds
