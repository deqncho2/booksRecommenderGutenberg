Synopsis: In 'The Lady of Loyalty House: A Novel', secrets and scandals abound within the walls of Loyalty House, a grand estate shrouded in mystery. As the enigmatic Lady of the house, Isabella, navigates the treacherous waters of high society, she becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and forbidden love. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the dark secrets that threaten to unravel the very fabric of Loyalty House.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Forbidden Love, Social Class
