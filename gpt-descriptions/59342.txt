Synopsis: 

'Amphibians and Reptiles in Captivity' takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of these often misunderstood creatures. From the vibrant colors of dart frogs to the majestic movements of snakes, this book provides a comprehensive guide to caring for and understanding amphibians and reptiles in captivity. With stunning photographs and expert advice, the authors delve into the intricacies of creating the perfect habitat, feeding and breeding these unique creatures, and addressing common challenges that arise. Whether you are a seasoned herpetologist or a curious beginner, this book will leave you enthralled by the beauty and diversity of amphibians and reptiles.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Pet Care

Themes: Animal husbandry, Conservation, Education
