Synopsis: In 'The Haunted Mine' by Castlemon, Harry, a young boy named Frank is sent to live with his uncle in a small mining town. As he explores the mysterious mine nearby, Frank uncovers a series of eerie occurrences that lead him to believe the mine is haunted. Determined to solve the mystery, Frank embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with suspense, danger, and unexpected twists. Will he uncover the truth behind the haunted mine?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
