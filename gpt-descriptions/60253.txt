Synopsis: 
In 'The Noble Rogue' by Baroness Orczy, a mysterious and charming gentleman thief known as the Scarlet Pimpernel takes center stage. With his cunning disguises and daring rescues, he outwits the authorities and saves innocent lives during the French Revolution. As the tension rises and the stakes become higher, the Scarlet Pimpernel's true identity remains a closely guarded secret, captivating readers with each twist and turn. Will his noble cause prevail, or will he be caught in the web of danger that surrounds him?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Identity
