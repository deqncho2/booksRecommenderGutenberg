Synopsis: In 'Perfection City' by Adela E. Orpen, a young woman named Alice finds herself in a seemingly idyllic city where everything appears perfect. However, as she delves deeper into the city's secrets, she uncovers a dark underbelly that challenges her perception of perfection and forces her to question the true nature of the world she thought she knew.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Dystopian

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Conformity, Individuality
