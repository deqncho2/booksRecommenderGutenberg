Synopsis: 

'Legends of the Pike's Peak Region; The Sacred Myths of the Manitou' takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical and enchanting tales of the Manitou, the ancient spirits that dwell in the Pike's Peak region. As the authors, Alexander and Whitney, delve into the rich folklore and legends of this sacred land, they uncover a world filled with mythical creatures, heroic quests, and powerful forces of nature. From the origins of the Manitou to the epic battles between good and evil, this book weaves together a tapestry of captivating stories that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Spirituality, Nature, Good vs. Evil
