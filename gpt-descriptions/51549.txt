Synopsis: In 'The Big Engine' by Leiber, Fritz, a group of engineers embark on a daring mission to harness the power of an enigmatic and colossal engine. As they delve deeper into the mysteries surrounding this technological marvel, they uncover a world of danger, intrigue, and unexpected consequences. With their lives and the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, the engineers must navigate a treacherous path filled with moral dilemmas and unforeseen challenges. Will they succeed in taming the power of the big engine, or will their ambitions lead to their downfall?

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Technological advancement, Morality, Consequences of human ambition
