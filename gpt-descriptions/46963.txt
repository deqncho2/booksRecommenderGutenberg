Synopsis: 

In 'Astrology: How to Make and Read Your Own Horoscope', Sepharial takes readers on an enlightening journey through the intricate world of astrology. With a unique blend of ancient wisdom and modern insights, this book offers a comprehensive guide to understanding and interpreting horoscopes. From deciphering the positions of the planets to uncovering the hidden meanings behind zodiac signs, Sepharial provides practical techniques and step-by-step instructions for creating and analyzing personal horoscopes. Whether you're a seasoned astrologer or a curious beginner, this captivating book will empower you to unlock the secrets of the stars and gain a deeper understanding of yourself and others.

Genres: Astrology, Self-help, Reference

Themes: Astrological interpretation, Personal growth, Self-discovery
