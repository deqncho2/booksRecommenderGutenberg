Synopsis: In 'The Tailor and the Crow: An Old Rhyme with New Drawings', Brooke L. Leslie brings to life a classic rhyme with captivating illustrations that will enchant readers of all ages. Follow the mischievous adventures of a clever tailor and a cunning crow as they engage in a battle of wits and outsmart each other at every turn. With vibrant and imaginative drawings, this delightful tale will leave readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book, Poetry

Themes: Wit and cleverness, Trickery and deception
