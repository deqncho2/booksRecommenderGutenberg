Synopsis: 
'Hurlbut's Life of Christ For Young and Old' is a captivating and accessible retelling of the life of Jesus Christ, written in simple language and based on the Gospel narrative. This engaging book takes readers on a journey through the significant events and teachings of Jesus, from his birth in Bethlehem to his crucifixion and resurrection. With vivid descriptions and relatable storytelling, Jesse Lyman Hurlbut brings the timeless story of Christ to life, making it accessible to readers of all ages.

Genres: Religious, Biography, Historical

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Love, Sacrifice
