Synopsis: 
'Prisoners of Chance: The Story of What Befell Geoffrey Benteen, Borderman, through His Love for a Lady of France' by Parrish, Randall is a captivating historical romance set in the tumultuous times of the 16th century. The story follows Geoffrey Benteen, a brave borderman, who finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of love and loyalty when he falls for a captivating Lady of France. As their forbidden love blossoms, Geoffrey is faced with treacherous challenges and unexpected betrayals that threaten to tear them apart. Will their love conquer all, or will they become prisoners of chance?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Forbidden Love, Loyalty, Betrayal, Courage
