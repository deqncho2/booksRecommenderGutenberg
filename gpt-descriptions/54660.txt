Synopsis: In 'The Disagreeable Woman: A Social Mystery', a young woman named Marion finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when she becomes the target of a mysterious and manipulative woman. As Marion tries to uncover the truth behind this enigmatic figure, she discovers shocking revelations that challenge her perception of trust and loyalty. With each twist and turn, the tension escalates, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the mystery alongside Marion.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity, Power Dynamics
