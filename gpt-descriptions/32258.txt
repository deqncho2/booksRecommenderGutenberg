Synopsis: In "Joseph K. F. Mansfield, Brigadier General of the U.S. Army," author John Mead Gould takes readers on a gripping journey through the events leading up to the mortal wounding of General Mansfield during the Battle of Antietam. Set against the backdrop of the American Civil War, this narrative delves into the bravery, sacrifice, and strategic decisions that shaped one of the bloodiest battles in history. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Gould brings to life the harrowing experiences of Mansfield and his comrades, offering a unique perspective on this pivotal moment in American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Leadership, Strategy, American Civil War
