Synopsis: In the final volume of the "Worlds Within Worlds" series, Isaac Asimov delves into the captivating world of nuclear energy, exploring the fascinating concepts of nuclear fission, nuclear fusion, and the possibilities that lie beyond fusion. With his signature clarity and engaging storytelling, Asimov takes readers on a journey through the history, science, and potential future of nuclear energy, unraveling the mysteries behind these powerful forces that have shaped our world and continue to hold immense promise for the future.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction, Popular Science

Themes: Nuclear energy, Scientific discovery, Technological advancements, Future possibilities
