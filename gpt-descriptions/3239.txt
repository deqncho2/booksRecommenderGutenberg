Synopsis: In 'The Puppet Crown' by Harold MacGrath, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love as she becomes entangled in a dangerous plot to overthrow a kingdom. Set in a captivating world of royalty, spies, and secret societies, this thrilling tale follows the protagonist's journey as she navigates treacherous alliances and uncovers shocking secrets that could change the course of history. With twists and turns at every corner, 'The Puppet Crown' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Power struggles, Betrayal, Love and loyalty
