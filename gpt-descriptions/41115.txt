Synopsis: 
In 'Solomon and Solomonic Literature', Conway, Moncure Daniel takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and legacy of the legendary King Solomon. From his rise to power and wisdom to his enigmatic love affairs and the construction of the magnificent Temple, this book unveils the fascinating tales surrounding one of history's most iconic figures. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the secrets, mysteries, and profound wisdom that Solomon's story holds, making this a must-read for anyone intrigued by ancient history and the power of human intellect.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Religious literature

Themes: Wisdom, Power, Love, Spirituality
