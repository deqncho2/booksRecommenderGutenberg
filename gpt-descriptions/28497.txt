Synopsis: 
In 'Myths of the Norsemen: From the Eddas and Sagas', H. A. Guerber takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich and enchanting world of Norse mythology. From the creation of the world to the epic battles of gods and giants, this collection of myths and legends brings to life the fascinating tales of Odin, Thor, Loki, and other iconic figures from Norse folklore. With vivid storytelling and intricate details, Guerber paints a vivid picture of a mythical realm filled with gods, goddesses, heroes, and monsters. From the chilling adventures of the Valkyries to the gripping tales of Ragnarok, this book offers a mesmerizing exploration of the Norse mythological universe.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Creation, Heroism, Fate, Good vs. Evil
