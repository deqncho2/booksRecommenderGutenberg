Synopsis: 
In 'Poor Relations' by MacKenzie, Compton, we are introduced to a group of eccentric and down-on-their-luck characters who find themselves living together in a dilapidated London boarding house. From the aspiring poet who can't seem to finish a single poem to the former actress who dreams of a comeback, each resident has their own unique story and struggles. As they navigate their daily lives, unexpected events and surprising connections bring both joy and heartache, ultimately leading them to question the true meaning of friendship and family.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Drama

Themes: Friendship, Family, Identity, Pursuit of Dreams
