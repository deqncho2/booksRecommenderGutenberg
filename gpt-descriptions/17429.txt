Synopsis: In 'The Story of Dago' by Annie F. Johnston, readers are transported to the enchanting world of a young Italian boy named Dago. Living in the picturesque countryside of Italy, Dago's life takes an unexpected turn when he encounters a mysterious stranger who offers him a chance at a new beginning. As Dago embarks on a journey filled with adventure, friendship, and self-discovery, he must navigate the challenges of his past and embrace the possibilities of his future. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt storytelling, 'The Story of Dago' is a captivating tale that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Friendship
