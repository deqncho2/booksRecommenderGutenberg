Synopsis: In the small town of Lorrane, a series of mysterious events begins to unfold when an unusual flock of birds descends upon the area. As the townspeople grapple with the inexplicable behavior of the birds, tensions rise and secrets are unearthed, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Nature's unpredictability, Small-town secrets, Survival
