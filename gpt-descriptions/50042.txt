Synopsis:
In 'The Forgery; or, Best Intentions,' a young artist finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he unwittingly becomes involved in a high-stakes forgery scheme. As he navigates the treacherous world of art forgery, he must confront his own moral compass and make difficult choices that could have far-reaching consequences. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of art and the lengths people will go to protect their secrets.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Deception, Morality, Art and Authenticity
