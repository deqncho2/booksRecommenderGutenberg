Synopsis: In "The Master of Appleby: A Novel Tale Concerning Itself in Part with the Great Struggle in the Two Carolinas; but Chiefly with the Adventures Therein of Two Gentlemen Who Loved One and the Same Lady," author Lynde weaves a captivating story set against the backdrop of the intense conflict in the Carolinas. The narrative primarily revolves around the entangled lives of two gentlemen who find themselves vying for the affections of the same woman. As their love triangle unfolds, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with adventure, passion, and unexpected twists. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Loyalty and Betrayal, Conflict and Struggle
