Synopsis: In "What Every Mother Should Know; or, How Six Little Children Were Taught The Truth," Margaret Sanger takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of six children and their mother, as they navigate the challenges of growing up in a society that often shies away from discussing important topics. As the children encounter various situations and questions, their mother courageously guides them towards the truth, empowering them with knowledge and understanding. This heartwarming and thought-provoking tale explores the power of open communication, the importance of education, and the resilience of a mother's love.

Genres: Fiction, Family, Coming-of-age

Themes: Parenthood, Education, Communication, Empowerment
