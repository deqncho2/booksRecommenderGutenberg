Synopsis: In 'The Invisible Enemy' by Castle, Arnold, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where nothing is as it seems. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with hidden agendas, unexpected allies, and a mysterious enemy that lurks in the shadows. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of science and the true nature of power.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Scientific ethics, Conspiracy, Identity
