Synopsis: In "The Ladies' Book of Etiquette, and Manual of Politeness: A Complete Hand Book for the Use of the Lady in Polite Society," Florence Hartley presents a comprehensive guide to navigating the intricacies of social etiquette for women. From proper introductions and conversation skills to hosting guests and attending social events, this book offers invaluable advice on how to conduct oneself with grace and charm in every situation. With a focus on cultivating good manners and refining one's character, Hartley's manual provides a roadmap for women to excel in polite society and leave a lasting impression.

Genres: Self-help, Etiquette, Non-fiction

Themes: Social etiquette, Politeness, Personal development
