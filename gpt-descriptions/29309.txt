Synopsis: In 'The Death-Traps of FX-31' by Wright, Sewell Peaslee, a brilliant scientist invents a revolutionary machine that can alter the fabric of reality itself. However, when his creation falls into the wrong hands, it becomes a deadly weapon capable of unimaginable destruction. As the world teeters on the brink of chaos, a group of unlikely heroes must race against time to stop the impending catastrophe and save humanity from the clutches of this terrifying device.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Technology gone awry, Heroism, Moral responsibility
