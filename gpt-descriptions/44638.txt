Synopsis: 

'Plant Lore, Legends, and Lyrics: Embracing the Myths, Traditions, Superstitions, and Folk-Lore of the Plant Kingdom' by Folkard, Richard is a captivating exploration of the rich tapestry of stories and beliefs surrounding plants. From ancient myths to traditional folklore, this book delves into the fascinating world of plant lore, revealing the hidden meanings and magical properties attributed to various plants throughout history. With enchanting tales and lyrical descriptions, Folkard takes readers on a journey through time and culture, uncovering the deep-rooted connections between humans and the plant kingdom.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Botany

Themes: Nature's symbolism, Cultural traditions, Human-plant relationships
