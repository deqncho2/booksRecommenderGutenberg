Synopsis: 
In "The Pony Rider Boys in the Rockies; Or, The Secret of the Lost Claim," the adventurous group of young riders embarks on a thrilling journey through the majestic Rocky Mountains. As they navigate treacherous terrains and encounter mysterious characters, the boys find themselves entangled in a quest to uncover the secret behind a lost claim. With danger lurking at every turn, the Pony Rider Boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to solve the enigma and emerge victorious.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
