Synopsis: 
In 'Ballads of Lost Haven: A Book of the Sea' by Carman, Bliss, embark on a captivating journey through the mystical and treacherous world of the sea. This collection of ballads weaves together tales of love, adventure, and the haunting beauty of the ocean. From the enchanting sirens that lure sailors to their doom, to the courageous souls who dare to challenge the wrath of the sea, each ballad transports the reader to a realm where danger and desire collide. With vivid imagery and lyrical storytelling, Carman invites readers to explore the depths of the human spirit and the power of the sea's timeless allure.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Adventure
Themes: Love, Adventure, Nature, Human Spirit
