Synopsis: In Old Madras takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling streets of colonial Madras, where secrets, scandals, and forbidden love intertwine. As the story unfolds, we follow the lives of a diverse cast of characters, each with their own ambitions, desires, and hidden pasts. From the opulent mansions of the British elite to the vibrant bazaars of the local population, the novel paints a vivid picture of a city on the brink of change. Amidst the backdrop of political unrest and cultural clashes, the characters navigate their way through a web of intrigue, romance, and betrayal, ultimately leading to unexpected consequences that will forever alter their lives.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Colonialism, Identity, Forbidden Love, Social Class
