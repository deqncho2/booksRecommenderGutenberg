Synopsis: In "Pragmatism: A New Name for Some Old Ways of Thinking," James, William explores a revolutionary philosophical movement that challenges traditional beliefs and offers a fresh perspective on truth, knowledge, and reality. Through engaging anecdotes and thought-provoking arguments, James presents a compelling case for embracing pragmatism as a practical and effective approach to navigating the complexities of life. This captivating work invites readers to question their own assumptions and embark on a journey of intellectual exploration.

Genres: Philosophy, Non-fiction

Themes: Pragmatism, Truth, Knowledge, Reality, Intellectual Exploration
