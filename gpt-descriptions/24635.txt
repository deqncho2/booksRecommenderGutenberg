Synopsis: 
In the small town of Exeter, a series of mysterious events begins to unfold, leaving the residents questioning their own sanity. As the protagonist, a young journalist named Alice, delves deeper into the secrets of Exeter, she uncovers a dark history that has been buried for decades. With each revelation, Alice finds herself drawn into a web of intrigue and danger, where nothing is as it seems. As she races against time to uncover the truth, Alice must confront her own fears and confront the sinister forces that threaten to consume Exeter.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of perception, the thin line between reality and illusion
