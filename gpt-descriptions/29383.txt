Synopsis: 

Discover the hidden treasures of the enchanting Hawaiian Islands in this captivating book by the Hawaii Department of Foreign Affairs. Delve into the rich tapestry of resources, agriculture, commerce, and finance that make these islands a true paradise. From the lush landscapes and diverse flora to the bustling markets and thriving industries, this comprehensive guide unveils the secrets behind Hawaii's remarkable success. Explore the unique blend of traditional and modern practices that have shaped the islands' economy, and gain a deeper understanding of the challenges and triumphs faced by the people of Hawaii. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, Economics

Themes: Natural resources, Agriculture, Commerce, Finance, Cultural heritage
