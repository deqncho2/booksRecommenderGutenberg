Synopsis: In 'King Penda's Captain: A Romance of Fighting in the Days of the Anglo-Saxons', the reader is transported to a tumultuous era of ancient England, where the valiant Captain Harold finds himself torn between loyalty to his king and his forbidden love for a noblewoman. As battles rage and alliances shift, Harold must navigate treacherous political landscapes and face the consequences of his choices. With vivid descriptions of epic battles, passionate romance, and the clash of cultures, this captivating historical romance will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Forbidden Love, Honor, Political Intrigue
