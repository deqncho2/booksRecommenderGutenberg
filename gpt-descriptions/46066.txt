Synopsis: 
In 'Manual of Taxidermy: A Complete Guide in Collecting and Preserving Birds and Mammals', Maynard, C. J. takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of taxidermy. This comprehensive guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to collect, prepare, and preserve birds and mammals, offering invaluable insights into the art and science of taxidermy. From selecting the right tools and materials to mastering the techniques of skinning, mounting, and finishing, this book is a treasure trove of knowledge for both beginners and experienced taxidermists. With stunning illustrations and detailed explanations, Maynard's manual is a must-have for anyone interested in the preservation of wildlife.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Natural History

Themes: Taxidermy, Wildlife Preservation, Art and Science
