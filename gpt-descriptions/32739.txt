Synopsis: In 'Probability' by Trimble, Louis, a brilliant mathematician named Ethan discovers a mysterious equation that seems to predict future events with uncanny accuracy. As he delves deeper into the world of probability, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game where the line between reality and prediction blurs. With his life on the line, Ethan must navigate a web of intrigue and deception to uncover the truth behind the equation and its implications for the world.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Fate vs. Free Will, the Power of Knowledge, the Consequences of Playing God
