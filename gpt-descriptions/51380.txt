Synopsis: In "Time In the Round" by Fritz Leiber, a group of scientists stumble upon a mysterious artifact that allows them to travel through time. As they explore different eras, they encounter unexpected challenges and must confront the consequences of their actions. With each leap, the boundaries of time become blurred, leading to a thrilling and mind-bending adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Adventure

Themes: Consequences of Actions, Exploration, Blurring of Time
