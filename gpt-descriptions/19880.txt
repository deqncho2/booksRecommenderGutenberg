Synopsis: 

In 'Voice production in singing and speaking, based on scientific principles' by Mills, Wesley, embark on a fascinating journey into the intricate world of vocalization. Delve into the secrets behind the art of singing and speaking, as the author unveils the scientific principles that underlie these captivating forms of expression. From the mechanics of breath control to the nuances of resonance, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the vocal apparatus and its potential. Prepare to be captivated by the author's insightful analysis and practical exercises, as you discover the key to unlocking your own vocal prowess.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Science

Themes: Voice production, Singing, Speaking, Scientific principles
