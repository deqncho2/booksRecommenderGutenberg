Synopsis: In 'Carnac's Folly, Volume 2' by Gilbert Parker, the captivating story continues as the enigmatic Carnac faces new challenges and uncovers hidden secrets. As he navigates through a web of political intrigue, personal vendettas, and forbidden love, Carnac's determination to uncover the truth becomes more perilous than ever. With his life hanging in the balance, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the lives of those around him. Will Carnac's relentless pursuit of justice lead to triumph or tragedy?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption, Love and Sacrifice
