Synopsis:
In 'The Defence of Lucknow: A Diary Recording the Daily Events during the Siege of the European Residency, from 31st May to 25th September, 1857', T. F. Wilson takes us on a gripping journey through the harrowing events of the Siege of Lucknow. Through the eyes of a diarist, we witness the relentless attacks, the bravery of the defenders, and the desperate struggle for survival. As the siege intensifies, tensions rise, alliances are tested, and the resilience of the human spirit is put to the ultimate test. Will the defenders of Lucknow be able to withstand the onslaught and emerge victorious?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Diary

Themes: Courage, Survival, Loyalty, Resilience
