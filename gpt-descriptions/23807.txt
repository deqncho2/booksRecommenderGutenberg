Synopsis: In 'The Uncle of an Angel: 1891' by Thomas A. Janvier, a mysterious uncle arrives in a small town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic charm and peculiar habits. As the townspeople become increasingly intrigued by his presence, they uncover a series of unexpected events that challenge their perceptions of reality and test the boundaries of their beliefs. With each revelation, the uncle's true nature becomes more elusive, leaving the reader questioning the line between the ordinary and the extraordinary.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Belief, Mystery
