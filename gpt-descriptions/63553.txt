Synopsis: Join Israel C. Russell on a thrilling adventure as he leads an expedition to conquer the majestic Mount St. Elias in Alaska. From treacherous terrain to unpredictable weather conditions, Russell and his team face numerous challenges that test their physical and mental strength. As they push the boundaries of human endurance, the expedition becomes a gripping tale of determination, camaraderie, and the relentless pursuit of conquering nature's greatest heights.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Exploration

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Human resilience, Nature's power and beauty
