Synopsis: In 'The Cubies' ABC', join a group of adorable and mischievous creatures called the Cubies as they embark on a whimsical adventure through the alphabet. Each letter brings a new and exciting experience for the Cubies, from flying on a magical carpet to exploring a hidden treasure cave. With vibrant illustrations and playful rhymes, this delightful picture book will captivate young readers and ignite their imagination.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book, Alphabet Book

Themes: Adventure, Creativity, Learning
