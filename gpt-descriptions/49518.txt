Synopsis: In 'A Few More Verses' by Susan Coolidge, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth as she navigates the complexities of love, loss, and the pursuit of her dreams. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, Emily's story unfolds through a series of beautifully crafted poems and prose, capturing the essence of her emotions and experiences. As she grapples with the challenges of her time, Emily's journey becomes a testament to the resilience of the human spirit and the power of finding one's voice.

Genres: Poetry, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Loss, Pursuit of Dreams
