Synopsis: In the Yule-Log Glow, Book II: Christmas Tales from 'Round the World' takes readers on a captivating journey through various cultures and traditions, exploring heartwarming and enchanting Christmas tales from different corners of the globe. From the snowy landscapes of Scandinavia to the bustling streets of Mexico City, this collection of stories brings to life the magic and wonder of the holiday season, filled with unexpected twists, memorable characters, and messages of love, hope, and the power of giving. 

Genres: Fiction, Anthology, Holiday

Themes: Christmas traditions, Cultural diversity, Love and compassion, The joy of giving
