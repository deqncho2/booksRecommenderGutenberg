Synopsis: In the remote wilderness of the Canadian North, a young man named Philip Marsh embarks on a treacherous journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious disappearance. As he navigates through the harsh landscapes and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Philip becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden motives. With each step closer to the truth, he finds himself questioning his own beliefs and the true nature of humanity. 'Northern Lights' is a gripping tale of adventure, suspense, and self-discovery that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Morality
