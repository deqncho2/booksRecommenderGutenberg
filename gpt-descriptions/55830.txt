Synopsis: In "Peggy on the Road" by Virginia Hughes, we follow the captivating journey of Peggy, a young woman who embarks on a road trip across the country. As she travels through various cities and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Peggy's adventure becomes a transformative experience that challenges her perceptions and leads her to unexpected discoveries. With each new destination, Peggy unravels the layers of her own identity and learns valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the power of self-discovery. This heartwarming and thought-provoking tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to join Peggy on her remarkable journey.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Contemporary Fiction, Travel

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Friendship, Love
