Synopsis: In 'The Sequel: What the Great War will mean to Australia', George A. Taylor takes readers on a captivating journey through the aftermath of World War I, exploring the profound impact it will have on Australia. Through a combination of historical analysis and personal anecdotes, Taylor paints a vivid picture of the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead for the nation. From the struggles of returning soldiers to the social and political changes that will shape the future, this thought-provoking sequel offers a compelling exploration of Australia's post-war transformation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War literature, Social commentary

Themes: Post-war reconstruction, National identity, Social change, Veterans' experiences
