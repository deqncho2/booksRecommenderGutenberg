Synopsis: In 'Ned in the Block-House: A Tale of Early Days in the West', follow the thrilling adventures of young Ned as he navigates the untamed wilderness of the American West. When Ned's family is captured by a group of ruthless outlaws, he must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to rescue them. Along the way, he encounters Native American tribes, treacherous landscapes, and unexpected allies, all while facing the harsh realities of survival in the wild. This gripping tale of bravery, resilience, and the indomitable spirit of the pioneers will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Courage, Survival, Frontier Life
