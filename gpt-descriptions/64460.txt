Synopsis: In 'The Armenians' by Dixon-Johnson, C. F., a young journalist named Emily embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious disappearance in the Armenian community. As she delves deeper into the secrets and complexities of this ancient culture, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden love. With each revelation, Emily's determination to uncover the truth grows stronger, but so does the danger that surrounds her. Will she be able to navigate the treacherous path and bring justice to those who have been wronged?

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Cultural Heritage, Love and Betrayal
