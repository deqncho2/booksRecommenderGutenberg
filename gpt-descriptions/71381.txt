Synopsis: In "Daisy of 'Old Meadow'" by Agnes Giberne, a young girl named Daisy finds herself in a mysterious and enchanting world filled with unexpected adventures. As she explores the captivating Old Meadow, Daisy encounters talking animals, magical creatures, and uncovers secrets that will change her life forever. With each new discovery, Daisy's courage and determination are put to the test, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and friendship.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Self-discovery
