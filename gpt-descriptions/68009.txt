Synopsis: In the vibrant streets of Italian New York, a young woman named Rosa finds herself caught between tradition and the allure of the modern world. As she navigates the challenges of love, family, and identity, Rosa becomes the last hope for preserving the rich heritage of her community. With secrets and sacrifices at every turn, this captivating tale explores the power of heritage and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Cultural

Themes: Tradition vs. Modernity, Identity, Family, Heritage
