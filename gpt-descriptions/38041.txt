Synopsis: 
'Old Celtic Romances' takes readers on a captivating journey through ancient Celtic lands, where mythical creatures, heroic warriors, and enchanting love stories intertwine. From the mystical forests of Ireland to the rugged landscapes of Scotland, this collection of tales transports readers to a world filled with adventure, magic, and passionate romance. Each story unveils the trials and tribulations faced by the protagonists as they navigate through treacherous quests, encounter supernatural beings, and ultimately discover the power of love and destiny. With its rich storytelling and vivid imagery, 'Old Celtic Romances' offers a mesmerizing glimpse into the captivating world of ancient Celtic mythology.

Genres: Mythology, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Love and destiny, Heroism and bravery, Supernatural beings, Quests and adventures
