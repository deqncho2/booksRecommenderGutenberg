Synopsis:
'Barford Abbey, a Novel: In a Series of Letters' by Gunning, Mrs. (Susannah) is an epistolary novel that takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of its characters. The story unfolds through a series of letters exchanged between various individuals, revealing their deepest secrets, desires, and struggles. As the letters pass from hand to hand, a web of intrigue, romance, and mystery begins to unravel, drawing readers into a world filled with hidden motives and unexpected twists. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Barford Abbey, this novel explores the complexities of human relationships and the consequences of our choices. 

Genres: Epistolary fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and desire, Deception and secrets, Fate and consequences
