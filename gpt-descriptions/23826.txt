Synopsis: 
"A Sketch of the Life of the late Henry Cooper: Barrister-at-Law, of the Norfolk Circuit; as also, of his Father" takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Henry Cooper, a renowned barrister from the Norfolk Circuit. Written by his son, William Cooper, this memoir delves into the fascinating world of the legal profession, offering glimpses into the challenges, triumphs, and personal sacrifices that shaped Henry's life. From his humble beginnings to his rise as a respected lawyer, this book paints a vivid portrait of a man who dedicated his life to the pursuit of justice. Alongside Henry's story, the author also explores the life of his father, adding depth and complexity to the narrative. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling characters, this memoir offers a captivating exploration of the legal profession and the personal journey of a remarkable individual.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Legal Drama

Themes: Pursuit of justice, Personal sacrifice, Family legacy
