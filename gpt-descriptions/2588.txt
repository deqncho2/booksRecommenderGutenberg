Synopsis: 

'Stories by English Authors: Scotland (Selected by Scribners)' is a captivating collection of short stories that takes readers on a journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Scotland. From the bustling streets of Edinburgh to the remote Highlands, this anthology presents a diverse range of tales that delve into the lives of intriguing characters and explore the complexities of human nature. Each story offers a unique perspective on Scotland, its history, traditions, and the timeless struggles faced by its people. Whether it's a haunting ghost story, a thrilling adventure, or a poignant exploration of love and loss, these narratives will transport readers to the heart of Scotland and leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Anthology

Themes: Scottish culture, History, Human nature, Love and loss, Adventure, Ghost stories
