Synopsis: In 'Coco Bolo: King of the Floating Islands' by Hamp, Sidford F., embark on an exhilarating adventure through a fantastical world of floating islands, where a young boy named Coco Bolo discovers his extraordinary destiny. As Coco navigates treacherous challenges and encounters peculiar creatures, he must unravel the secrets of his past and embrace his true identity. With breathtaking imagery and heart-pounding action, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the mysteries that lie within the floating islands.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Courage, Discovery
