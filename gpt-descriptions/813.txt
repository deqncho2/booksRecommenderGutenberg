Synopsis: In "Reminiscences of Tolstoy, by His Son," Ilia Lvovich Tolstoi offers a captivating and intimate account of his father, the legendary Russian writer Leo Tolstoy. Through personal anecdotes and heartfelt recollections, Ilia provides a unique perspective on the life and inner world of one of literature's greatest figures. From Leo Tolstoy's creative process and philosophical musings to his complex relationships and spiritual journey, this memoir delves into the depths of a literary genius, shedding light on the man behind the masterpieces.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Literary Nonfiction

Themes: Artistic creativity, Family dynamics, Philosophy, Spirituality
