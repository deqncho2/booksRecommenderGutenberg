Synopsis: In 'The Garden of Survival' by Algernon Blackwood, a group of friends embarks on a seemingly innocent adventure to explore a remote garden. However, as they delve deeper into the mysterious landscape, they soon discover that the garden holds secrets far beyond their imagination. With each step, they are confronted with strange occurrences and unsettling encounters that challenge their perception of reality. As they struggle to survive the garden's enigmatic forces, they must confront their deepest fears and unravel the truth hidden within its lush foliage.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the power of nature, the fragility of human perception
