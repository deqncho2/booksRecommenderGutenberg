Synopsis: 

In 'Momentum' by Dye, Charles, a brilliant physicist named Dr. Emily Parker finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when she stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of science forever. As she races against time to protect her research and uncover the truth, she becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. With her life on the line, Dr. Parker must navigate a treacherous world of secrets and deception, where every step forward brings her closer to the edge.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Scientific breakthroughs, Betrayal, Survival
