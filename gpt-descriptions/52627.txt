Synopsis: In 'Library Ideals' by Henry Eduard Legler, a mysterious librarian embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets hidden within the walls of a forgotten library. As he delves deeper into the labyrinthine stacks, he encounters enigmatic books, ancient manuscripts, and cryptic clues that lead him on a thrilling adventure. With each discovery, the librarian's perception of reality becomes blurred, and he must confront his own beliefs and the true purpose of libraries. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the mysteries that lie within the pages of 'Library Ideals'.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Knowledge and discovery, Perception and reality
