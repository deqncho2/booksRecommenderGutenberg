Synopsis:
In 'The Sea Monarch' by Percy F. Westerman, a thrilling adventure unfolds as a group of young friends embark on a perilous journey aboard a mysterious ship. As they set sail, they soon discover that the vessel holds secrets beyond their wildest imagination. With danger lurking at every turn, the friends must navigate treacherous waters, encounter strange creatures, and unravel the enigma of the Sea Monarch. Will they uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres:
Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes:
Friendship, Exploration, Courage
