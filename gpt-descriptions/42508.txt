Synopsis: In 'Curious Creatures in Zoology' by John Ashton, embark on a captivating journey through the animal kingdom as the author unveils the most extraordinary and enigmatic creatures ever discovered. From mythical beasts to bizarre hybrids, this book delves into the realm of zoological wonders, presenting a collection of astonishing creatures that will leave readers in awe. With vivid descriptions and captivating illustrations, Ashton's exploration of these curious creatures will ignite the reader's imagination and curiosity, revealing the hidden mysteries of the natural world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Zoology

Themes: Curiosity, Wonder, Biodiversity
