Synopsis: In 'The Marriage of Elinor' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman named Elinor finds herself caught between duty and desire when she is forced into an arranged marriage. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, Elinor discovers hidden secrets and unexpected alliances that challenge her perception of love and happiness. With a cast of intriguing characters and a captivating plot, this novel explores the complexities of marriage and the power of personal choice.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Victorian Literature

Themes: Love and duty, societal expectations, personal agency
