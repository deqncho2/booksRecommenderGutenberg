Synopsis: 
'The Magician' by W. Somerset Maugham is a gripping tale that follows the life of Oliver Haddo, a mysterious and enigmatic magician. When Arthur Burdon, a young doctor, becomes entangled in Haddo's web of dark secrets and supernatural powers, he must navigate a treacherous journey that will test his beliefs and challenge his understanding of reality. As the story unfolds, Burdon finds himself drawn deeper into Haddo's world, where the line between illusion and truth becomes increasingly blurred.

Genres:
Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller, Occult fiction

Themes:
The nature of evil, The power of manipulation, The search for truth
