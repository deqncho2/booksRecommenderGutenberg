Synopsis:
'Bealby; A Holiday' by H.G. Wells is a delightful and whimsical tale that follows the misadventures of a young boy named Bealby. When Bealby's life takes an unexpected turn, he finds himself embarking on a thrilling journey filled with eccentric characters, humorous escapades, and unexpected discoveries. From mistaken identities to secret societies, Bealby's holiday becomes a whirlwind of excitement and intrigue. Will he be able to navigate this extraordinary adventure and find his way back home?

Genres:
Adventure, Comedy, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Identity, Exploration, Resilience
