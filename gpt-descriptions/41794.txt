Synopsis: In 'The Last Miracle' by M. P. Shiel, a renowned scientist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that possesses an extraordinary power. As he delves deeper into its origins and capabilities, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, pursued by those who seek to control this miraculous object. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect the artifact and unlock its true potential.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Power and its consequences, the pursuit of knowledge, the nature of miracles
