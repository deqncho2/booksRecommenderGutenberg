Synopsis: In 'The Monkey's Frolic: A Humorous Tale, in Verse' by Anonymous, readers are taken on a delightful journey through the mischievous antics of a group of playful monkeys. From their hilarious escapades to their clever tricks, this whimsical tale is sure to captivate readers of all ages. As the monkeys frolic and cause chaos, the story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to see what mischief the monkeys will get up to next. With its witty rhymes and charming illustrations, 'The Monkey's Frolic' is a delightful and entertaining read that will leave readers laughing and wanting more.

Genres: Children's Literature, Poetry, Humor

Themes: Playfulness, Mischief, Adventure
