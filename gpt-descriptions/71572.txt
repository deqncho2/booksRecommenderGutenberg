Synopsis: 

In "The Gold Seekers of '49," author Kimball Webster takes readers on a thrilling journey through the overland trail and the wild adventures that awaited those who sought fortune in California and Oregon during the years 1849 to 1854. Through his personal narrative, Webster vividly recounts the challenges, dangers, and triumphs of the gold rush era, providing a captivating glimpse into the lives of those who risked everything in pursuit of their dreams.

Genres: Adventure, Historical, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Pursuit of Wealth, Frontier Life
