Synopsis: The Bobbsey Twins, Nan and Bert, embark on a thrilling adventure when they visit their cousins at Meadow Brook. As they explore the beautiful countryside, they stumble upon a mysterious secret that leads them on a quest to uncover the truth. With their quick thinking and determination, the Bobbsey Twins must solve the puzzle before it's too late. Will they succeed in unraveling the enigma that lies hidden in Meadow Brook?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Family bonds, Curiosity and exploration, Problem-solving
