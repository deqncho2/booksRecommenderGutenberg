Synopsis: In 'The Uncalled: A Novel' by Paul Laurence Dunbar, a group of friends find themselves entangled in a mysterious and thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a hidden secret that has been buried for centuries. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they must confront their own fears and confront the consequences of their actions. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Consequences
