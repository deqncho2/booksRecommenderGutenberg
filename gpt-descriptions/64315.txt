Synopsis: In 'The Girl Scouts' Captain' by Lavell, Edith, a group of young girls embarks on an extraordinary adventure that will test their courage, teamwork, and leadership skills. When their beloved camp is threatened, they must band together to uncover a hidden treasure and save the day. Along the way, they encounter mysterious clues, face unexpected challenges, and learn valuable life lessons that will shape their futures. This heartwarming and thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, cheering for these brave young girls as they navigate through a world of secrets and surprises.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Empowerment, Resilience
