Synopsis: 
In the small town of Edgewater, the bumbling and lovable Anderson Crow finds himself unexpectedly thrust into the role of detective. With his comical antics and unconventional methods, Crow sets out to solve a perplexing mystery that has the whole town on edge. As he navigates through a web of secrets, hidden identities, and unexpected twists, Crow's determination and wit are put to the test. Will he be able to crack the case and bring the culprit to justice, or will his clumsy nature lead to more chaos and confusion?

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Crime

Themes: Amateur detective, Small-town charm, Unconventional methods
