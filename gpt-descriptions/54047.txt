Synopsis: 
In "Best Lincoln Stories, tersely told," J. E. Gallaher presents a collection of captivating anecdotes and tales about the legendary President Abraham Lincoln. From humorous incidents to profound encounters, these concise stories shed light on Lincoln's wit, wisdom, and humanity. Delve into the fascinating world of one of America's greatest leaders through these engaging and thought-provoking narratives.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Leadership, Humor, Wisdom, Humanity
