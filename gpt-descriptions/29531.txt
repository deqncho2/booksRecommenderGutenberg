Synopsis: In 'Silhouettes' by Arthur Symons, a mysterious woman enters the life of a struggling artist, captivating him with her enigmatic allure. As their paths intertwine, the artist becomes consumed by his obsession with capturing her essence on canvas, blurring the lines between reality and imagination. Set against the backdrop of bohemian Paris, this atmospheric tale explores the depths of desire, the power of art, and the enigmatic nature of human connection.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Obsession, Identity, Artistic Expression
