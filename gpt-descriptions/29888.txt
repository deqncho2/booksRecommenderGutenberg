Synopsis: In "The Hunting of the Snark: An Agony, in Eight Fits," Lewis Carroll takes readers on a whimsical and mysterious journey as a group of adventurers set out to hunt a mythical creature known as the Snark. Along the way, they encounter peculiar characters, face unexpected challenges, and navigate through a surreal and nonsensical world. As they strive to unravel the enigma of the Snark, the adventurers find themselves questioning the nature of reality and the meaning of their quest. With Carroll's signature wit and wordplay, this delightful and thought-provoking tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Poetry, Adventure

Themes: Quest for the unknown, Perception of reality, Nonsense and absurdity
