Synopsis: In 'Fort Lafayette or, Love and Secession: A Novel' by Benjamin Wood, a gripping tale unfolds against the backdrop of the American Civil War. The story follows the lives of two individuals, a Union soldier and a Southern belle, whose paths unexpectedly cross in the midst of the conflict. As they navigate the treacherous waters of love and loyalty, their fates become intertwined in ways they could never have imagined. With its rich historical setting and complex characters, this novel explores the profound impact of war on personal relationships and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and Loyalty, Conflict and Consequences
