Synopsis: In 'The Last Rebel' by Joseph A. Altsheler, a young man named Harry Kenton finds himself torn between loyalty to his family and his desire for adventure during the American Civil War. As he navigates the treacherous battlefields and encounters both friends and foes, Harry's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. With the fate of a nation hanging in the balance, will Harry choose to follow his heart or fulfill his duty?

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Sacrifice
