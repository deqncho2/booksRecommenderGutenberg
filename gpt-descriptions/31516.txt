Synopsis: In "The Eyes Have It" by Philip K. Dick, a man on a train journey becomes intrigued by a fellow passenger who is engrossed in a book. As he tries to catch a glimpse of the book's title, he discovers something peculiar about the woman's eyes. This chance encounter leads him down a mysterious and thought-provoking path, questioning the nature of perception and reality itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Perception, Reality, Identity
