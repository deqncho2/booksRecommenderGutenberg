Synopsis: In 'The Hitch Hikers' by McCain, Vernon L., a chance encounter on a desolate highway leads to a thrilling and unexpected journey for two strangers. As they embark on an adventure filled with danger, mystery, and unexpected twists, they must rely on each other to survive. With every mile they travel, secrets are revealed and the stakes become higher, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Trust, Survival, Redemption
