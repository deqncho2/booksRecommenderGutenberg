Synopsis: In "The Achievement of the British Navy in the World-War" by John Leyland, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the remarkable accomplishments of the British Navy during the tumultuous World War. From daring naval battles to covert operations, Leyland unveils the untold stories of heroism, strategy, and sacrifice that shaped the outcome of the war. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, this book sheds light on the pivotal role played by the British Navy in securing victory for the Allied forces. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, War memoir

Themes: Heroism, Strategy, Sacrifice, Naval warfare, World War I
