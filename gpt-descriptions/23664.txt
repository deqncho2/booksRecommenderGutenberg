Synopsis: In the small town of Flamsted, a mysterious secret lies hidden within the depths of the quarries. As the townspeople go about their daily lives, unaware of the danger lurking beneath their feet, a young woman named Ruth embarks on a journey to uncover the truth. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, she delves into the dark history of the quarries, unearthing long-buried secrets and facing unexpected challenges along the way. As Ruth's investigation intensifies, she must confront her own fears and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of Flamsted and its inhabitants.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Courage and self-discovery, Small-town dynamics
