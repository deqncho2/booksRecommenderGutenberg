Synopsis: In 'A Catalogue of Books in English Later than 1700, Vol. 2: Forming a portion of the library of Robert Hoe', readers are transported into the vast world of rare and fascinating books. This meticulously curated catalogue showcases a diverse collection of literary treasures, ranging from classic novels to obscure manuscripts. As readers peruse the pages, they will uncover hidden gems and forgotten masterpieces, each with its own unique story and historical significance. From the works of renowned authors to obscure literary curiosities, this catalogue offers a tantalizing glimpse into the literary landscape of the past.

Genres: Non-fiction, Bibliography, Collectibles

Themes: Literary history, Rare books, Cultural preservation
