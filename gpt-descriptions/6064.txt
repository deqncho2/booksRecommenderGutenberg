Synopsis: 
In 'Memoirs of Lady Fanshawe: Wife of Sir Richard Fanshawe, Bt., Ambassador from Charles II to the Courts of Portugal and Madrid,' Ann Fanshawe takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous world of 17th-century European diplomacy. As the wife of a prominent ambassador, Lady Fanshawe provides a unique perspective on the political intrigues, lavish court life, and personal challenges she faced during her husband's diplomatic missions. From her encounters with powerful monarchs to her own struggles with love and loss, Lady Fanshawe's memoirs offer a fascinating glimpse into a remarkable era.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Diplomacy, Court Life, Personal Challenges, Love and Loss
