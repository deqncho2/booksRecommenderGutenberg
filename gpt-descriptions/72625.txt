Synopsis: 
Step into the enchanting world of Christmas with "Fifty Christmas Poems for Children." This delightful anthology, carefully selected by Florence B. Hyett, brings together a collection of heartwarming and whimsical poems that capture the magic and joy of the holiday season. From tales of Santa's mischievous elves to the beauty of a snowy winter's night, each poem is a delightful journey that will captivate both young and old. Whether read aloud by a crackling fire or shared with loved ones, these fifty poems will transport readers to a world filled with wonder and the true spirit of Christmas.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: Christmas, Magic, Joy, Tradition
