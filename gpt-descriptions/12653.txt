Synopsis: In the second volume of his acclaimed biography, Henry Cabot Lodge delves deeper into the life and legacy of George Washington, revealing the challenges and triumphs that shaped the first President of the United States. From his pivotal role in the Revolutionary War to his efforts in establishing a new nation, Lodge paints a vivid portrait of Washington's leadership, character, and unwavering commitment to the ideals of liberty and democracy. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, this volume offers a captivating exploration of the man behind the legend, shedding light on the complexities and contradictions that made George Washington an enduring figure in American history.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Leadership, Patriotism, Nation-building
