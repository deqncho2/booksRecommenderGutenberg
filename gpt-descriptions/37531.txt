Synopsis: In 'Theology and the Social Consciousness: A Study of the Relations of the Social Consciousness to Theology (2nd ed.)', Henry Churchill King delves into the intricate connection between theology and the social consciousness. Through thought-provoking analysis and compelling arguments, King explores how our understanding of theology is shaped by our awareness of the social world around us. This revised edition offers fresh insights and perspectives on the dynamic interplay between faith and society, challenging readers to reconsider their own beliefs and the impact they have on the world.

Genres: Theology, Philosophy, Social Sciences

Themes: The relationship between theology and society, the influence of social consciousness on religious beliefs, the role of faith in shaping social change.
