Synopsis: In 'A Prince of Dreamers' by Flora Annie Steel, a young prince discovers a hidden world beyond his wildest imagination, filled with enchanting creatures and magical adventures. As he embarks on a quest to save his kingdom, he must confront his deepest fears and learn the true power of dreams. This captivating tale takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through a realm where reality and fantasy intertwine, leaving them eager to uncover the secrets that lie within.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Imagination, Courage, Self-discovery
