Synopsis: 

In 'Children Above 180 IQ Stanford-Binet: Origin and Development' by Leta Stetter Hollingworth, embark on a fascinating journey into the world of exceptionally gifted children. This groundbreaking work explores the origins and development of the Stanford-Binet Intelligence Scale, shedding light on the unique challenges and opportunities faced by children with extraordinary intellectual abilities. Through meticulous research and insightful analysis, Hollingworth delves into the lives of these remarkable individuals, uncovering the complexities of their cognitive abilities and the impact it has on their personal and educational experiences. 

Genres: Psychology, Education, Biography

Themes: Giftedness, Intelligence, Education, Individuality
