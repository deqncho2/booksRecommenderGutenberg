Synopsis: 
'Clan Traditions and Popular Tales of the Western Highlands and Islands' is a captivating collection of folklore and legends that takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the rich cultural heritage of the Scottish Highlands and Islands. From ancient clan traditions to enchanting tales of mythical creatures and heroic deeds, this book unveils a world of mystery and wonder. Delve into the lives of brave warriors, cunning witches, and noble chieftains as you explore the rugged landscapes and delve into the depths of the supernatural. With each story, the reader is transported to a realm where ancient traditions and fantastical tales intertwine, leaving them spellbound and eager for more.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Fantasy

Themes: Scottish culture and heritage, Supernatural beings, Heroism, Tradition and folklore
