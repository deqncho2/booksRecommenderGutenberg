Synopsis:
The Coral Island: A Tale of the Pacific Ocean follows the thrilling adventures of three young boys, Ralph, Jack, and Peterkin, who find themselves stranded on a deserted island in the Pacific Ocean after their ship is wrecked during a storm. As they navigate their new surroundings, they must learn to survive in the wild, facing various challenges and encountering both friendly and hostile inhabitants of the island. Together, they embark on a journey of exploration, friendship, and self-discovery, uncovering the secrets of the island and testing their courage and resourcefulness in the face of danger.

Genres:
Adventure, Survival, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Friendship, Exploration, Survival, Nature, Morality
