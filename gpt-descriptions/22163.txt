Synopsis: The Rover Boys on the Farm; or, Last Days at Putnam Hall is an exciting adventure novel that follows the thrilling escapades of the Rover brothers as they find themselves embroiled in a mysterious plot during their final days at Putnam Hall. When a series of strange events occur on the farm, the brothers must use their wit, courage, and resourcefulness to uncover the truth and protect their friends. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Coming of Age
