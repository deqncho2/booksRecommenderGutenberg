Synopsis: 
In 'Little Journeys to the Homes of the Great - Volume 10: Little Journeys To The Homes Of Great Teachers', Elbert Hubbard takes readers on a captivating journey to explore the lives and homes of some of history's most influential educators. From Socrates to Confucius, from Plato to Emerson, Hubbard delves into the personal stories and philosophies of these great teachers, shedding light on their profound impact on the world. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, this volume offers a unique glimpse into the lives of these remarkable individuals, inspiring readers to reflect on the power of education and the enduring legacy of great teachers.

Genres: Biography, History, Philosophy

Themes: Education, Wisdom, Influence
