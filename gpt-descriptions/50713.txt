Synopsis: In 'One Against the Moon' by Donald A. Wollheim, a brilliant scientist embarks on a daring mission to the moon, but soon finds himself facing unimaginable dangers and a mysterious force that threatens his very existence. As he battles against the odds, he must confront his own fears and make a choice that could change the course of humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human Determination
