Synopsis: In 'Subject to Change' by Ron Goulart, a young man named Robin Hudson finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a secret society that can alter reality itself. As he delves deeper into this hidden world, Robin must navigate treacherous alliances, mind-bending illusions, and a race against time to uncover the truth and save himself. With unexpected twists and a cast of eccentric characters, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Reality manipulation, Secret societies, Identity
