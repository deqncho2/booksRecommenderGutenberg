Synopsis: In "A Lost Leader" by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a charismatic political figure mysteriously disappears, leaving his followers and loved ones in shock and confusion. As the search for him unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of power and loyalty is explored. This gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the world of politics, intrigue, and personal sacrifice.

Genres: Mystery, Political Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Leadership, Betrayal, Power, Loyalty
