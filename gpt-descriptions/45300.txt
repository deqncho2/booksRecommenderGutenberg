Synopsis: 
'Once Upon a Time, and Other Child-Verses' by Mary Eleanor Wilkins Freeman is a delightful collection of whimsical and enchanting poems that transport readers to a world of imagination and wonder. From tales of talking animals to magical adventures, each verse captures the essence of childhood and the joy of storytelling. With its lyrical language and vivid imagery, this collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages and ignite their imagination.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: Imagination, Childhood, Storytelling
