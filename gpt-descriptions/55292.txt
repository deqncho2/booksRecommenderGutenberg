Synopsis: In 'The Scaring off of Teddy Dawson: A Comedy in One Act' by Harold Brighouse, the story revolves around Teddy Dawson, a young man who finds himself in a series of hilarious and unexpected situations. As Teddy navigates through misunderstandings, mistaken identities, and comical mishaps, the audience is taken on a rollercoaster ride of laughter and amusement. With each twist and turn, the play keeps the reader guessing and eagerly anticipating what will happen next.

Genres: Comedy, Farce

Themes: Miscommunication, Identity
