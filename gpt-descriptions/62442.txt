Synopsis: 
In the small coastal town of Greycliff, a mysterious new family moves into the old mansion on the hill. As the townspeople become increasingly intrigued by the family's peculiar habits and nocturnal activities, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden secret that could change everything. With the help of her friends, Lily embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic Greycliff Wings.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Friendship and loyalty
