Synopsis: In 'The Hour and the Man, An Historical Romance' by Harriet Martineau, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the French Revolution. The story follows the life of a young man named Gilbert Marguerite, who finds himself caught up in the revolutionary fervor and becomes a key figure in the fight for liberty and justice. As Gilbert navigates the treacherous political landscape and faces personal challenges, the novel explores themes of heroism, sacrifice, and the power of the individual to shape history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Revolution, Heroism, Personal Sacrifice
