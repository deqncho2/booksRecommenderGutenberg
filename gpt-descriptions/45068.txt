Synopsis: In "The Truth About Jesus: Is He a Myth?: Illustrated," Mangasarian delves into the controversial topic of the historical existence of Jesus. Through a meticulous examination of ancient texts, archaeological evidence, and logical reasoning, the author challenges the widely accepted narrative surrounding Jesus' life and teachings. With thought-provoking arguments and captivating illustrations, Mangasarian invites readers on a journey of intellectual exploration, questioning the origins and authenticity of one of the most influential figures in human history.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Religious Studies

Themes: Historical accuracy, Religious skepticism, Critical thinking
