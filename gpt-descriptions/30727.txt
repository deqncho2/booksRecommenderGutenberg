Synopsis: In "Motor Boat Boys Down the Coast; or, Through Storm and Stress to Florida" by Louis Arundel, a group of adventurous young men embark on a thrilling journey along the coast to Florida. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter unexpected challenges, and face the wrath of a powerful storm, their bond is tested and their courage is pushed to the limit. With danger lurking at every turn, the Motor Boat Boys must rely on their resourcefulness and determination to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Survival
