Synopsis: In a future world ravaged by a devastating plague, a small group of survivors must navigate the remnants of society and confront the existential questions that arise in the face of their own mortality. As they grapple with love, loss, and the fragility of human existence, they are forced to confront the ultimate question: what does it mean to be the last man standing?

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Romanticism

Themes: Mortality, Isolation, Human Nature, Existentialism
