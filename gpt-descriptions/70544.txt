Synopsis: 
'Parodies of the works of English & American authors, vol. II' is a collection of clever and humorous parodies that pay homage to some of the most renowned English and American authors. From Charles Dickens to Mark Twain, this volume takes readers on a delightful journey through various literary styles and themes. Each parody captures the essence of the original author's work while adding a unique twist, resulting in a collection that is both entertaining and thought-provoking. Whether you are a fan of classic literature or simply enjoy a good laugh, this book is sure to captivate and amuse.

Genres: 
Satire, Parody, Humor

Themes: 
Literary homage, Comedy, Subversion of literary conventions
