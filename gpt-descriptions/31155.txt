Synopsis: 

'The Academy Keeper: Or Variety of useful Directions Concerning the Management of an Academy, The Terms, Diet, Lodging, Recreation, Discipline, and Instruction of Young Gentlemen. With the Proper Methods of addressing Parents and Guardians of all Ranks and Conditions' is a comprehensive guidebook that offers invaluable insights into the inner workings of an academy and the proper methods of educating and nurturing young gentlemen. Written by an anonymous author, this enlightening work delves into the intricacies of running an academy, including topics such as terms, diet, lodging, recreation, discipline, and instruction. 

Genres: Educational, Self-help, Non-fiction

Themes: Education, Discipline, Parental Guidance
