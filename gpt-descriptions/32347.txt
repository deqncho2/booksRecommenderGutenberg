Synopsis: In 'Time Fuze' by Randall Garrett, a brilliant scientist invents a device that can manipulate time, but soon finds himself caught in a dangerous web of intrigue and betrayal. As he races against time itself, he must navigate through a world of secret societies, government conspiracies, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the line between past, present, and future becomes blurred, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Time travel, Power and control, Betrayal
