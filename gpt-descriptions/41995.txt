Synopsis: 
In 'The Scout and Ranger: Being the Personal Adventures of Corporal Pike of the Fourth Ohio cavalry', readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the American Civil War as experienced by Corporal Pike. From dangerous scouting missions to unexpected encounters with enemy soldiers, Pike's personal adventures provide a gripping and immersive account of the war. As he navigates the treacherous landscapes and faces the harsh realities of battle, Pike's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the life of a soldier during one of the most tumultuous periods in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Survival, Loyalty, Camaraderie
