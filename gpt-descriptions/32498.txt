Synopsis: 
In 'The Brain' by Hamilton, Edmond, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but the fate of the entire world. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal, all while trying to protect his groundbreaking invention from falling into the wrong hands. This gripping tale of scientific intrigue and high-stakes suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific advancement, Ethics, Betrayal
