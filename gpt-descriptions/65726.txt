Synopsis: In 'Day of the Comet' by Ivar Jorgensen, a small town is thrown into chaos when a comet unexpectedly passes by Earth, leaving behind a mysterious substance that alters the behavior of its inhabitants. As the town's residents grapple with their newfound powers and desires, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate the ensuing chaos and uncover the truth behind the comet's strange effects. With suspenseful twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the dark side of human nature and the consequences of unchecked power.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power and its corrupting influence, Human nature and morality, Unpredictability of the universe
