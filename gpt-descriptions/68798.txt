Synopsis: "Strange Stories of the Civil War" is a captivating collection of tales that takes readers on a journey through the lesser-known and mysterious aspects of the American Civil War. From eerie encounters with supernatural beings to unexplained phenomena on the battlefield, this book delves into the strange and unexplored corners of one of history's most tumultuous periods. Each story offers a unique perspective, shedding light on the extraordinary events that unfolded during this pivotal time in American history.

Genres: Historical fiction, supernatural, mystery

Themes: War, the supernatural, human resilience, the unknown
