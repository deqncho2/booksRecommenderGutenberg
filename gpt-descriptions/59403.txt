Synopsis: In 'Jekyll-Hyde Planet' by Jack Lewis, a team of scientists embarks on a groundbreaking mission to explore a distant planet. However, as they delve deeper into the planet's mysterious and dangerous environment, they soon discover that it holds a dark secret. The planet seems to have a split personality, with contrasting landscapes and bizarre creatures that challenge their understanding of reality. As the team grapples with the planet's dual nature, they must confront their own inner demons and make difficult choices that will determine their fate.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Dualism, Identity, Morality, Exploration
