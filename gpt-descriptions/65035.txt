Synopsis: 
In 'Inheritance' by Ludwig, Edward W., a young woman named Emma unexpectedly inherits a mysterious family estate after the death of her estranged grandmother. As she delves into the secrets of her family's past, Emma uncovers a web of hidden treasures, long-lost love letters, and a dark family curse that threatens to unravel her own future. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, Emma must navigate through the twists and turns of her inheritance, all while discovering the true meaning of family and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Legacy, Redemption
