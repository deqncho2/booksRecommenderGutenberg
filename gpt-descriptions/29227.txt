Synopsis: In 'The Politician Out-Witted' by Samuel Low, a cunning and ambitious politician finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and manipulation. As he navigates the treacherous world of politics, he soon realizes that he is not the only one with a hidden agenda. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning who will come out on top in this high-stakes game of power and deception.

Genres: Political thriller, suspense, mystery

Themes: Ambition, manipulation, power struggle
