Synopsis: 
In 'Aunt Olive in Bohemia' by LM (Leslie Moore), a young woman named Olive embarks on a journey to Bohemia, a vibrant and unconventional artistic community. As she immerses herself in this bohemian world, Olive encounters a cast of eccentric characters, indulges in wild parties, and discovers a hidden talent that sets her apart. However, as she delves deeper into this unconventional lifestyle, Olive must confront the clash between her newfound freedom and the expectations of her conservative upbringing. With secrets, scandals, and unexpected twists, 'Aunt Olive in Bohemia' takes readers on a captivating exploration of self-discovery and the pursuit of artistic passion.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Self-discovery, Artistic expression, Rebellion against societal norms
