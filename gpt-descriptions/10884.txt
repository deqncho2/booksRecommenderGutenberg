Synopsis: 

"A Publisher and His Friends: Memoir and Correspondence of John Murray; with an Account of the Origin and Progress of the House, 1768-1843" by Samuel Smiles takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and career of John Murray, a prominent publisher during the late 18th and early 19th centuries. Through a collection of memoirs and correspondence, Smiles provides a fascinating glimpse into the world of publishing, shedding light on the challenges, triumphs, and friendships that shaped Murray's life and the renowned publishing house he established.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, History

Themes: Publishing industry, Friendship, Entrepreneurship, Literary history
