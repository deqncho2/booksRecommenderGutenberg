Synopsis: In 'Apron-Strings' by Eleanor Gates, a young woman named Mary finds herself torn between her duty to her family and her desire for independence. As she navigates the challenges of love, societal expectations, and personal growth, Mary must make difficult choices that will shape her future. This captivating story explores the complexities of family dynamics and the pursuit of personal freedom, leaving readers eager to discover the outcome.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family, Independence, Love, Self-discovery
