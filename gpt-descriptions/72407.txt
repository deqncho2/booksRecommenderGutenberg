Synopsis: In 'The god on the 36th floor' by Kastle, Herbert D., a mysterious and enigmatic figure takes residence on the 36th floor of a luxurious high-rise building. As the residents become increasingly intrigued by this god-like presence, they are drawn into a web of secrets, desires, and hidden agendas. With each encounter, the god challenges their beliefs, pushing them to question their own existence and the nature of power. As tensions rise and the line between reality and illusion blurs, the residents must confront their deepest fears and confront the consequences of their actions.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Power and Control, Identity and Self-Discovery, Perception vs. Reality
