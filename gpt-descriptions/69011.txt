Synopsis: 
'Australian Fairy Tales' by Hume-Cook, James takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Australian folklore. From mystical creatures lurking in the depths of the Outback to magical encounters in the bustling cities, this collection of fairy tales offers a unique blend of Australian culture and fantastical storytelling. Prepare to be transported to a land where dreams come true and the extraordinary is just a step away. 

Genres: 
Fairy Tale, Fantasy, Australian Literature 

Themes: 
Australian folklore, Magic, Adventure, Cultural identity
