Synopsis: 
In 'Historical Mysteries', Lang, Andrew takes readers on a captivating journey through time, unraveling enigmatic events and unsolved puzzles from various historical periods. From the mysterious disappearance of the Princes in the Tower to the perplexing riddles surrounding the identity of Jack the Ripper, Lang delves into the depths of history to shed light on these intriguing mysteries. With meticulous research and a keen eye for detail, he presents a collection of gripping tales that will leave readers questioning what they thought they knew about the past.

Genres: 
Historical Non-fiction, Mystery, True Crime

Themes: 
Unsolved mysteries, Historical enigmas, Intrigue
