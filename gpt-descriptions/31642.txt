Synopsis: 
In 'Eyes Like the Sea: A Novel' by Jókai, Mór, a young and mysterious woman named Róza appears in a small coastal village, captivating the hearts of all who encounter her. As the villagers become entangled in her enigmatic charm, secrets from the past resurface, leading to a series of unexpected events that will test their loyalties and challenge their perceptions of love, betrayal, and redemption. Set against the backdrop of the mesmerizing sea, this gripping tale explores the depths of human emotions and the power of destiny.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and passion, Betrayal and forgiveness, Fate and destiny
