Synopsis: 

'Arts and Crafts Essays: by Members of the Arts and Crafts Exhibition Society' is a captivating collection of essays that delves into the philosophy and principles of the Arts and Crafts movement. Written by various members of the Arts and Crafts Exhibition Society, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the relationship between art, craft, and society during the late 19th and early 20th centuries. From discussions on the importance of handmade objects to the revival of traditional craftsmanship, each essay presents a unique perspective on the role of art in everyday life. 

Genres: Art, Craft, Essay

Themes: Arts and Crafts movement, Handmade objects, Traditional craftsmanship, Art and society
