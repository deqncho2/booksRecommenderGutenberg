Synopsis: 
In 'A Girl in Ten Thousand' by L. T. Meade, a young woman named Muriel is thrust into a world of mystery and danger when she receives a cryptic message from a stranger. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding her family's past, Muriel finds herself caught in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected romance. With each twist and turn, she must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the truth and protect those she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Love and sacrifice
