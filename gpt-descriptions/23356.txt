Synopsis: In 'A Struggle For Life' by Thomas Bailey Aldrich, a young artist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he becomes the target of a sinister plot. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding him, he must navigate a treacherous world of deception and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. With his life hanging in the balance, he must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Deception
