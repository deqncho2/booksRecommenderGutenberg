Synopsis: 
In 'The Man Who Killed the World' by Cummings, Ray, a mysterious man finds himself burdened with a haunting secret that threatens to unravel his entire existence. As he navigates through a web of deception and betrayal, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between reality and illusion blurs. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must confront his own demons and make choices that will determine the fate of not only himself but the world around him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Guilt, Perception, Consequences
