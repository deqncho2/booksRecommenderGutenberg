Synopsis: In 'Crito' by Plato, Socrates finds himself facing a difficult decision as he awaits his impending execution. His loyal friend, Crito, visits him in prison and presents a compelling argument for escape. As they engage in a thought-provoking dialogue, Socrates contemplates the nature of justice, the role of the individual in society, and the importance of moral integrity. Will Socrates choose to defy the law or remain steadfast in his principles?

Genres: Philosophy, Drama

Themes: Justice, Loyalty, Moral Integrity
