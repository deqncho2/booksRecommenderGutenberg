Synopsis: In 'They Also Serve' by Donald E. Westlake, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he stumbles upon a secret that could change the course of history. As he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, he must navigate a treacherous world of spies, double-crosses, and unexpected alliances. With his life on the line, he must uncover the truth and outsmart those who will stop at nothing to protect their secrets.

Genres: Thriller, Espionage, Mystery

Themes: Betrayal, Identity, Power
