Synopsis: In 'The Lawton Girl' by Frederic, Harold, a young woman named Mary Lawton finds herself caught between the expectations of her conservative family and her own desires for independence. As she navigates the complexities of love, societal norms, and personal growth, Mary must make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century America, this captivating novel explores the challenges faced by women in a rapidly changing society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Gender roles, societal expectations, self-discovery, love and sacrifice
