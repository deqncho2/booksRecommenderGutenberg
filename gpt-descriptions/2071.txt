Synopsis: 

'Stories by English Authors: Germany (Selected by Scribners)' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and diverse literary landscape of Germany. This anthology features a collection of gripping short stories written by English authors, each set against the backdrop of Germany. From tales of love and betrayal to mysteries and supernatural encounters, these stories offer a unique perspective on the country and its people. Whether it's exploring the haunting streets of Berlin or delving into the secrets of a Bavarian castle, this anthology promises to transport readers to a world where history, culture, and imagination intertwine.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Cultural Identity, History and Memory, Supernatural Encounters
