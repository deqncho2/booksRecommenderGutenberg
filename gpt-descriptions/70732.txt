Synopsis: In "The Old Vicarage," a captivating novel by Catherine-Anne Austen Hubback, secrets and scandals unravel in a quaint English village. When a mysterious stranger arrives at the vicarage, the lives of the residents are forever changed. As the past intertwines with the present, love, betrayal, and redemption take center stage, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption, Love, Small-town Life
