Synopsis: In 'The Honour of the Clintons' by Marshall, Archibald, a mysterious secret threatens to unravel the lives of the esteemed Clinton family. As the story unfolds, the reader is drawn into a web of scandal, betrayal, and hidden identities. With each twist and turn, the characters must confront their pasts and make difficult choices that will determine their future. Set against the backdrop of high society and political intrigue, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Morality, Power dynamics
