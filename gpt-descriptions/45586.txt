Synopsis: 
"Auld Lang Syne: Selections from the Papers of the 'Pen and Pencil Club'" takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and experiences of the members of the renowned club. From the bustling streets of London to the enchanting landscapes of the Scottish Highlands, this collection of stories and essays offers a glimpse into the joys, sorrows, and secrets of a group of talented individuals. As the club's members navigate love, friendship, and the pursuit of their artistic passions, they uncover hidden truths and confront the complexities of life in the late 19th century.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Anthology

Themes: Friendship, Artistic Expression, Love and Relationships, Identity, Society and Culture
