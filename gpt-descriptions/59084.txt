Synopsis: In 'A Modern Legionary' by John Patrick Le Poer, a young man named Marcus finds himself at a crossroads in life, searching for purpose and meaning. In a twist of fate, he is drawn into the world of an ancient order, the Legionaries, who are tasked with protecting humanity from supernatural threats. As Marcus undergoes rigorous training and faces unimaginable challenges, he discovers hidden truths about himself and the world around him. With danger lurking at every turn, Marcus must navigate a treacherous path filled with mystery, betrayal, and self-discovery.

Genres: Fantasy, Action, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Sacrifice, Redemption
