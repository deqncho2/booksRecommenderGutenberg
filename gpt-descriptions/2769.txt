Synopsis:
In "Cleopatra" by H. Rider Haggard, the reader is transported back to ancient Egypt, where the captivating and enigmatic Queen Cleopatra reigns. As the story unfolds, a young Greek scholar named Harmachis finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the pursuit of power. With the fate of Egypt hanging in the balance, Harmachis must navigate treacherous waters and make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of a nation and the woman he loves.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes:
Power and Politics, Love and Betrayal, Fate and Destiny
