Synopsis: In 'What a Man Wills' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of secrets and unexpected circumstances when she inherits a mysterious fortune from a distant relative. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, she must confront the challenges of love, betrayal, and the true nature of her own desires. With each twist and turn, the protagonist is forced to question her own identity and make difficult choices that will shape her future.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Inheritance, Identity, Love and Betrayal
