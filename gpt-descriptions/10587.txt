Synopsis: 
"The Poetical Works of Addison; Gay's Fables; and Somerville's Chase: With Memoirs and Critical Dissertations" is a captivating collection of poetry by renowned authors John Gay, Joseph Addison, and William Somerville. This anthology, compiled by the Rev. George Gilfillan, presents a diverse range of poetic styles and themes. From Addison's elegant and reflective verses to Gay's witty and satirical fables, and Somerville's vivid descriptions of the hunt, this collection offers a delightful journey through the world of poetry. With accompanying memoirs and critical dissertations, readers will gain a deeper understanding of the poets' lives and the significance of their works.

Genres: Poetry, Memoir, Literary Criticism
Themes: Reflection, Satire, Nature, Life
