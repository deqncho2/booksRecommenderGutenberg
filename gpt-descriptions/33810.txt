Synopsis: 
"On the magnet, magnetick bodies also, and on the great magnet the earth: a new physiology, demonstrated by many arguments & experiments" is a captivating scientific work that delves into the mysterious world of magnets and their influence on our planet. Through a series of intriguing arguments and experiments, the author explores the hidden powers of magnetism and its connection to the Earth. From uncovering the secrets of magnetic bodies to unraveling the enigma of the Earth's magnetic field, this book takes readers on a fascinating journey of discovery and wonder.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Magnetism, Earth's magnetic field, Scientific exploration
