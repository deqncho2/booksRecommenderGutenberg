Synopsis: 
'Knotting and splicing ropes and cordage' is a comprehensive guide that takes readers on a captivating journey into the intricate world of rope manipulation. Filled with numerous engravings and diagrams, this book unveils the secrets of knotting and splicing techniques, revealing the artistry and practicality behind these age-old skills. From basic knots to complex splices, readers will be enthralled by the step-by-step instructions and the fascinating history behind each technique. Whether you're an aspiring sailor, outdoor enthusiast, or simply curious about the art of rope manipulation, this book is sure to captivate and inspire.

Genres: Non-fiction, How-to, Crafts

Themes: Skill acquisition, Practicality, History
