Synopsis: In the Forbidden Land is a gripping account of Arnold Henry Savage Landor's extraordinary journey into Tibet, where he finds himself captured by Tibetan lamas and soldiers. Imprisoned, tortured, and facing an uncertain fate, Landor's harrowing experiences unfold as he navigates the treacherous landscape of Tibet. This captivating narrative takes readers on a thrilling adventure, filled with danger, suspense, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural Clash
