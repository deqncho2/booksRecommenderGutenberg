Synopsis: 

In 'Oriental Rugs, Antique and Modern' by W. A. Hawley, embark on a captivating journey through the rich history and intricate beauty of Oriental rugs. From the bustling bazaars of the Middle East to the elegant parlors of Europe, this book unveils the secrets and stories woven into these remarkable works of art. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Hawley delves into the origins, craftsmanship, and symbolism behind each rug, transporting readers to a world of cultural exchange and artistic mastery. Whether you are an avid collector or simply fascinated by the allure of Oriental rugs, this book will leave you spellbound and yearning to explore the hidden treasures that lie beneath your feet.

Genres: Art, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Cultural exchange, Artistic craftsmanship, Symbolism
