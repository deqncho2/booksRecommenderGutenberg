Synopsis: 
In 'Cupid's Middleman' by Edward B. Lent, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges in the small town of Willowbrook, offering his services as a middleman for those seeking love. As he orchestrates unexpected encounters and manipulates the course of romance, the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of desire, secrets, and unexpected consequences. With each new connection he creates, the middleman's true intentions become increasingly unclear, leaving the reader questioning his motives and the ultimate price of love.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Love, Manipulation, Secrets, Consequences
