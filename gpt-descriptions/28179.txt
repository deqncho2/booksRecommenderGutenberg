Synopsis: In 'The Inglises; Or, How the Way Opened', Margaret M. Robertson weaves a captivating tale of the Inglis family, who find themselves facing unexpected challenges and opportunities. As they navigate through the complexities of life, their paths intertwine with a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets and desires. With twists and turns at every corner, the Inglises must confront their past, confront their fears, and ultimately discover the true meaning of family and love.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Family, Identity, Redemption, Love, Secrets
