Synopsis:
In "Hunted Down: The Detective Stories of Charles Dickens," the renowned author takes on a new role as a master of mystery and suspense. This collection of gripping tales introduces readers to a variety of intriguing characters, each entangled in their own web of secrets and deception. From a cunning blackmailer to a mysterious disappearance, Dickens weaves together intricate plots that keep readers on the edge of their seats. As the stories unfold, the reader is drawn deeper into the dark underbelly of Victorian society, where justice is sought and secrets are unraveled. With his signature wit and keen observation, Dickens presents a captivating exploration of the human psyche and the lengths people will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Crime

Themes: Deception, Justice, Morality
