Synopsis: 

In "Lameness of the Horse: Veterinary Practitioners' Series, No. 1" by John Victor Lacroix, readers are taken on a captivating journey into the world of equine medicine. Dr. Lacroix, a renowned veterinarian, shares his expertise and experiences in diagnosing and treating lameness in horses, unraveling the mysteries behind this common yet complex issue. Through a combination of real-life case studies, practical advice, and insightful anecdotes, the author delves into the intricate art of identifying and resolving lameness, providing a valuable resource for both aspiring and seasoned veterinary practitioners. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Veterinary Medicine, Animal Care

Themes: Equine health, Lameness diagnosis and treatment, Veterinary practice
