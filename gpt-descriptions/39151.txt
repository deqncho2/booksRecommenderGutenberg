Synopsis: In "Swift and Sure: The Story of a Hydroplane" by Herbert Strang, embark on an exhilarating adventure that takes you into the heart of the thrilling world of hydroplane racing. Follow the journey of a young and determined protagonist as he navigates the treacherous waters of competition, facing both personal and professional challenges along the way. With high stakes, unexpected twists, and a race against time, this gripping tale will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Sports, Young Adult

Themes: Perseverance, Ambition, Overcoming obstacles
