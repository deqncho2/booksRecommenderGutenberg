Synopsis: In "The Wonderful "One-Hoss-Shay", and Other Poems," Oliver Wendell Holmes takes readers on a delightful journey through a collection of witty and humorous poems. From the tale of a perfectly constructed carriage to the musings on life's fleeting moments, Holmes captivates with his clever wordplay and insightful observations. Each poem offers a unique perspective on various aspects of human existence, leaving readers both entertained and contemplative.

Genres: Poetry, Humor, Satire

Themes: Mortality, Time, Human Nature
