Synopsis: In "Tyburn Tree: Its History and Annals" by Alfred Marks, embark on a captivating journey through the dark and intriguing history of London's infamous Tyburn Tree. Uncover the secrets and stories behind this notorious gallows, where countless criminals met their fate. From notorious highwaymen to political rebels, this book delves into the lives and crimes of those who faced the Tyburn Tree's deadly embrace. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Marks brings to life the chilling atmosphere of this historical landmark, leaving readers spellbound by the tales of justice, betrayal, and redemption that unfolded beneath its branches.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, True Crime, British History

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Social History, Notorious Figures
