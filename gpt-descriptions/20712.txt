Synopsis: 
In 'Trail's End' by George W. Ogden, a young adventurer named Jack embarks on a perilous journey through the untamed wilderness of the American West. As he faces treacherous landscapes, encounters with outlaws, and unexpected alliances, Jack's determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. With each step, he unravels a web of secrets that could change his life forever. Will Jack find the answers he seeks, or will the trail lead him to a different destination altogether?

Genres: Adventure, Western, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Identity
