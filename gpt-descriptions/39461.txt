Synopsis: In 'I've Been Thinking; or, the Secret of Success', a young man named John embarks on a journey to uncover the secret to achieving success in life. Along the way, he encounters a mysterious mentor who guides him through a series of thought-provoking experiences and challenges. As John delves deeper into the realms of self-reflection and personal growth, he begins to unravel the hidden truths that lie within himself and the world around him. This captivating tale explores the power of mindset, determination, and the pursuit of one's dreams, leaving readers inspired to unlock their own potential.

Genres: Self-help, Fiction, Inspirational

Themes: Personal growth, Success, Self-discovery
