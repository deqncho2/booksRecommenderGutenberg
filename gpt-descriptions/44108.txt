Synopsis: 
'The Inferno' by August Strindberg is a gripping and intense psychological drama that delves into the depths of the human mind. The story follows a man named Carl, who finds himself trapped in a nightmarish world of his own creation. As he navigates through a series of surreal and haunting encounters, Carl is forced to confront his deepest fears and confront the demons that reside within him. With each twist and turn, the boundaries between reality and illusion blur, leaving the reader questioning what is truly happening and what is merely a figment of Carl's tormented imagination.

Genres: Drama, Psychological Fiction, Surrealism

Themes: Madness, Identity, Self-Reflection, Descent into Darkness
