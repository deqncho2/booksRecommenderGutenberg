Synopsis: In 'Young Peoples' History of the War with Spain' by Holmes Prescott, embark on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Spanish-American War. Follow the lives of courageous young individuals who find themselves caught up in the conflict, as they navigate the perils of war, forge unlikely alliances, and discover the true meaning of bravery. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this historical adventure will transport readers to a time of intense struggle and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War, Courage, Friendship, Sacrifice
