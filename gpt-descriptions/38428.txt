Synopsis: 
"Through a Microscope: Something of the Science, Together with many Curious Observations Indoor and Out and Directions for a Home-made Microscope" takes readers on a fascinating journey into the microscopic world. Written by Frederick Leroy Sargent, Mary Treat, and Samuel Wells, this book explores the wonders that can be discovered through the lens of a microscope. From observing tiny organisms to uncovering hidden details in everyday objects, the authors provide a captivating exploration of the unseen world around us. With step-by-step instructions for creating a homemade microscope, readers are encouraged to embark on their own scientific adventures. 

Genres: Science, Non-fiction, Educational

Themes: Microscopic exploration, Scientific curiosity, DIY science
