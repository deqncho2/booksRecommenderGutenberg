Synopsis: In 'ABC Butter Making: A Hand-Book for the Beginner', Frederick S. Burch takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of butter making. Through a delightful blend of storytelling and practical advice, Burch introduces the reader to the art and science of creating delicious homemade butter. From the history of butter making to step-by-step instructions, this book is a comprehensive guide that will leave readers inspired to embark on their own butter-making adventures. With charming anecdotes and helpful tips, Burch's book is a must-read for anyone curious about the magic behind this beloved dairy product.

Genres: Non-fiction, Food and Drink, How-to

Themes: Culinary arts, Self-sufficiency, Historical traditions
