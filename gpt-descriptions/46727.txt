Synopsis: In "An Epitome of the History of Medicine," Park, Roswell takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of medicine, from ancient civilizations to modern times. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Park explores the groundbreaking discoveries, influential figures, and societal impact of medical advancements. From the ancient Egyptians' innovative practices to the Renaissance's revolutionary ideas, this book delves into the triumphs and challenges that have shaped the field of medicine. With a focus on the human aspect of healing, Park highlights the perseverance and dedication of those who have dedicated their lives to improving the well-being of others. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Medical

Themes: Progress and innovation in medicine, the impact of medical discoveries on society, the human aspect of healing and healthcare.
