Synopsis: In 'The Lost Guidon: 1911' by Charles Egbert Craddock, a young woman named Judith embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious disappearance during the Civil War. As she delves deeper into the past, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With danger lurking at every turn, Judith must navigate treacherous landscapes and confront her own demons to find the lost guidon and bring closure to a haunting chapter of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Redemption, Historical Legacy
