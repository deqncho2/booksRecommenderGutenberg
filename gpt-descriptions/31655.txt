Synopsis: In 'A Daughter of the Forest' by Raymond Evelyn, a young woman named Eliza finds herself torn between two worlds when she discovers a hidden secret about her ancestry. As she delves deeper into her family's history, she uncovers a mystical connection to the forest that holds both danger and enchantment. With the fate of her loved ones hanging in the balance, Eliza must navigate a treacherous path filled with ancient magic, forbidden love, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Sacrifice, Nature vs. Civilization
