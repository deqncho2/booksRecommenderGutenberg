Synopsis: In 'Jan Vedder's Wife' by Amelia E. Barr, a young woman named Jan Vedder finds herself caught between duty and desire when she marries a man she does not love. As she navigates the challenges of her new life, Jan discovers unexpected strength within herself and must make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, this captivating tale explores the complexities of love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Marriage, Self-discovery, Social Expectations
