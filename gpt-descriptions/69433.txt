Synopsis: In 'Smugglers' Island and the Devil Fires of San Moros', Clarissa A. Kneeland takes readers on a thrilling adventure to a mysterious island off the coast of California. When a group of friends stumble upon an ancient map, they embark on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets hidden on Smugglers' Island. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter dangerous smugglers, and face the wrath of the devil fires, they must rely on their wits and courage to survive. Will they unravel the island's mysteries or fall victim to its deadly secrets?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Survival
