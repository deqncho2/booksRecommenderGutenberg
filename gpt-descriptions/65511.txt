Synopsis: 
In "This Then is Upland Pastures," Adeline Knapp takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting beauty of spring and summer. Through a series of out-door essays, she explores the wonders and delights that these seasons bring, immersing readers in the vibrant colors, fragrant scents, and melodious sounds of nature. From the delicate blossoms of flowers to the graceful flight of birds, Knapp's vivid descriptions transport readers to idyllic landscapes, inviting them to experience the joy and serenity found in the natural world. With her lyrical prose and keen observations, she captures the essence of these seasons, leaving readers with a renewed appreciation for the simple yet profound beauty that surrounds us.

Genres: Nature writing, Essay collection, Non-fiction

Themes: Beauty of nature, Seasonal changes, Appreciation for the natural world
