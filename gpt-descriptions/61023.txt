Synopsis: In "A Letter to Lord Fielding," George Luther Stone presents a gripping tale inspired by the unsettling events that unfolded at the New Church in Pantasa. As the story unfolds through a series of letters, a mysterious and sinister atmosphere takes hold, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat. With each letter, the plot thickens, revealing shocking secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel the very fabric of society. As Lord Fielding delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the New Church, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and supernatural occurrences that will test his beliefs and challenge his understanding of the world. This captivating work will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Religion and Belief, Supernatural Phenomena
