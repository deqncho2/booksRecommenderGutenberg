Synopsis: 
In 'Three Wonder Plays' by Gregory, Lady, prepare to be transported to a world where mythical creatures, enchanting landscapes, and extraordinary events come to life. This collection of plays takes readers on a captivating journey through three distinct wonder-filled stories. From the mysterious depths of the sea to the magical realms of the forest, each play unveils a unique and mesmerizing tale that will leave readers spellbound. With vivid imagery, compelling characters, and unexpected twists, 'Three Wonder Plays' is a literary masterpiece that will ignite the imagination and leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Mythology, Transformation, Quest for Truth
