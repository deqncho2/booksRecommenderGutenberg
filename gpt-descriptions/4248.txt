Synopsis: In 'The Problem of the Ohio Mounds', Thomas Cyrus takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious ancient mounds of Ohio. As an archaeologist, Cyrus delves into the enigmatic history and purpose of these intriguing structures, unearthing secrets that have remained hidden for centuries. With each discovery, he becomes increasingly entangled in a web of historical puzzles and unexplained phenomena, leading him to question the very foundations of our understanding of the past. As he races against time to unravel the truth, Cyrus finds himself facing unexpected dangers and encountering individuals who will stop at nothing to protect the secrets of the Ohio mounds.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Archaeological Thriller

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Historical mysteries, Quest for knowledge
