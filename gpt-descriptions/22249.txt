Synopsis: 
In "Shorty McCabe" by Ford, Sewell, follow the hilarious misadventures of Shorty McCabe, a former boxing champion turned detective, as he navigates the world of crime and intrigue in early 20th century New York City. When a wealthy socialite's necklace goes missing, Shorty is hired to crack the case, leading him on a wild journey filled with colorful characters, unexpected twists, and plenty of laughs. With his quick wit and street smarts, Shorty must outsmart both the criminals and the police to solve the mystery and restore order. 

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Crime

Themes: Crime and justice, Humor, Social class
