Synopsis: 

'Peeps at many lands: Wales' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Wales. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, the author paints a vibrant picture of this fascinating country, exploring its history, traditions, and natural wonders. From the bustling streets of Cardiff to the breathtaking beauty of Snowdonia, readers will be transported to the heart of Wales, discovering its unique charm and discovering the secrets that lie within its ancient castles and picturesque villages. 

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Welsh culture, History, Natural beauty, Tradition
