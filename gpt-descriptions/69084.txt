Synopsis: 

In "Engineers and their Triumphs: The Story of the Locomotive, the Steamship, Bridge Building, Tunnel Making," author F. M. Holmes takes readers on a captivating journey through the remarkable achievements of engineers. From the invention of the locomotive to the construction of awe-inspiring bridges and tunnels, this book delves into the fascinating world of engineering and the triumphs that have shaped our modern society. With vivid storytelling and insightful anecdotes, Holmes brings to life the challenges faced by these brilliant minds and the groundbreaking solutions they devised. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Engineering

Themes: Innovation, Human Ingenuity, Technological Advancement
