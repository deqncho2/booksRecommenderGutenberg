Synopsis: In 'Rodney, the Partisan' by Castlemon, Harry, follow the thrilling adventures of Rodney, a young boy who finds himself caught up in the dangerous world of espionage during the American Civil War. As he navigates through treacherous territory and encounters unexpected allies and enemies, Rodney must rely on his wit and courage to fulfill his mission and protect those he holds dear. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Bravery, Sacrifice
