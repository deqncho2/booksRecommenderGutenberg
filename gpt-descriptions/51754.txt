Synopsis: 
In 'The Road to En-Dor: Being an Account of How Two Prisoners of War at Yozgad in Turkey Won Their Way to Freedom', the reader is taken on a thrilling journey alongside two prisoners of war who find themselves trapped in a Turkish camp during World War I. As they devise a daring plan to escape, they must navigate treacherous landscapes, outsmart their captors, and rely on their wits to survive. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding moments, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Resilience
