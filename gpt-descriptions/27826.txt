Synopsis: 
In 'The Olive Fairy Book', readers will embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales from various cultures. From the mysterious lands of the East to the magical realms of the West, this book presents a delightful assortment of stories filled with brave heroes, cunning tricksters, and mythical creatures. Each tale is woven with vivid imagery and unexpected twists, transporting readers to far-off lands where anything is possible. From the heartwarming to the spine-chilling, 'The Olive Fairy Book' is a treasure trove of timeless tales that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fairy tales, folklore, fantasy

Themes: Adventure, heroism, magic, cultural diversity
