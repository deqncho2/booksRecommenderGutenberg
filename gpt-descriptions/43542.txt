Synopsis: In 'Beginners Luck' by Emily Hahn, a young woman named Lucy embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure as she leaves behind her predictable life to pursue her passion for photography. Set in the vibrant city of New York, Lucy finds herself navigating the complexities of love, friendship, and the pursuit of her dreams. As she immerses herself in the world of photography, she encounters unexpected challenges and encounters that test her resilience and determination. With each new experience, Lucy learns valuable lessons about life, love, and the power of following one's heart.

Genres: Contemporary Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Self-discovery, Pursuit of Passion, Love and Relationships
