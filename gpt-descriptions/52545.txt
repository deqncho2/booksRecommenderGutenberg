Synopsis: In 'The Princess Nobody: A Tale of Fairyland' by Lang, Andrew, we are transported to a magical realm where a young princess finds herself in a peculiar predicament. Born without a name or identity, she embarks on a captivating journey to discover her true self. Along the way, she encounters enchanting creatures, faces unexpected challenges, and unravels the secrets of her past. This whimsical tale of self-discovery and the power of identity will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fairy tale, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Self-discovery, Magic
