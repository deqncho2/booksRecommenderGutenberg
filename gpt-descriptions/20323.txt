Synopsis: In 'That Stick' by Charlotte M. Yonge, a seemingly ordinary stick becomes the catalyst for a series of unexpected events in the lives of two young siblings. As they embark on a thrilling adventure, they discover the stick's mysterious powers and encounter a world filled with magic and danger. With each twist and turn, the siblings must navigate through challenges and make difficult choices that will ultimately shape their destinies. 'That Stick' is a captivating tale that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to uncover the secrets hidden within the ordinary.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Magic, Sibling bonds, Destiny
