Synopsis: In 'The Builders' by Ellen Anderson Gholson Glasgow, a small Southern town becomes the backdrop for a gripping tale of ambition, love, and betrayal. As the lives of its inhabitants intertwine, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human desires is exposed. With vivid characters and a captivating plot, this novel explores the depths of human nature and the consequences of our choices.

Genres: Southern Gothic, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Love, Betrayal, Secrets, Human Nature
