Synopsis: 
In "Nights With Uncle Remus," Joel Chandler Harris takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of African-American folklore. Set in the post-Civil War South, this collection of stories follows the adventures of Uncle Remus, a wise and beloved storyteller, as he shares his timeless tales with a young boy named Johnny. Through these captivating narratives, readers are transported to a world filled with talking animals, clever tricksters, and valuable life lessons. As Uncle Remus weaves his magical stories, Johnny learns about the power of friendship, the importance of kindness, and the wisdom of the past. 

Genres: 
Folklore, Children's Literature, African-American Literature

Themes: 
Friendship, Wisdom, Morality, Cultural Heritage
