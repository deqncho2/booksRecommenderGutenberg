Synopsis: In "The Sunken Isthmus; or, Frank Reade, Jr., in the Yucatan Channel," Frank Reade Jr., a brilliant inventor, embarks on a thrilling adventure in the Yucatan Channel. When a mysterious sunken isthmus is discovered, Frank Reade Jr. and his team set out to explore its secrets. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter dangerous creatures, they uncover a hidden world filled with ancient civilizations and untold wonders. With their innovative inventions and unwavering determination, they must overcome numerous challenges to unravel the mysteries of the sunken isthmus.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Invention, Ancient Civilizations
