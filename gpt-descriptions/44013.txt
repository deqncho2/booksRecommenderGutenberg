Synopsis: 

In 'Tom Fairfield at Sea; or, The Wreck of the Silver Star', Tom Fairfield finds himself embarking on a thrilling maritime adventure aboard the ill-fated ship, the Silver Star. As the ship encounters treacherous waters and faces the threat of a devastating wreck, Tom must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to survive. Along the way, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters and uncovers a mystery that could change the course of his life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Survival, Courage, Mystery, Coming of Age
