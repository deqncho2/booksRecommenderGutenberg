Synopsis: In 'The Law and the Word', T. Troward takes readers on a captivating journey into the power of thought and the influence it has on our lives. Through a series of thought-provoking essays, Troward explores the connection between our thoughts, the universal laws that govern the world, and the manifestation of our desires. With profound insights and practical examples, he reveals how understanding and aligning with these laws can unlock our true potential and lead to a life of abundance, success, and fulfillment.

Genres: Self-help, Philosophy, Spirituality

Themes: Power of thought, Universal laws, Manifestation, Personal growth, Success
