Synopsis: In "Under the absolute Amir" by Frank A. Martin, a young American journalist finds himself immersed in the captivating world of Afghanistan during the reign of the absolute Amir. As he navigates the complex political landscape and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and betrayals that could have far-reaching consequences. With danger lurking at every turn, he must uncover the truth while grappling with his own personal demons. This gripping tale of adventure and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Power and Corruption, Identity and Self-discovery, Loyalty and Betrayal
