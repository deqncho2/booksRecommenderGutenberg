Synopsis: In 'The Socialist' by Thorne, Guy, a young idealistic journalist finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue when he uncovers a secret society with a radical agenda. As he delves deeper into the world of socialism, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and betrayal, where the lines between friend and foe blur. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of hidden agendas and unexpected alliances, all while questioning his own beliefs and the true nature of socialism.

Genres: Political thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Political ideology, Power struggles, Betrayal, Idealism vs. reality
