Synopsis: In "Aria da Capo" by Edna St. Vincent Millay, two rival performers find themselves trapped in a surreal world where their comedic play takes an unexpected turn. As the story unfolds, the characters' true natures are revealed, leading to a thought-provoking exploration of the human condition and the consequences of our actions.

Genres: Drama, Absurdist, Satire

Themes: Illusion vs. Reality, Power dynamics, Social commentary
