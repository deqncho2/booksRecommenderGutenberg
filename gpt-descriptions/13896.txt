Synopsis: In "Jacques Bonneval; Or, The Days of the Dragonnades" by Manning, Anne, readers are transported to 17th century France, where religious tensions are at their peak. Jacques Bonneval, a young Huguenot, finds himself caught in the midst of the brutal persecution of Protestants known as the Dragonnades. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of betrayal, love, and survival, Jacques must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of his fellow Huguenots. This gripping historical novel delves into the harrowing events of the Dragonnades, painting a vivid picture of a time filled with danger, courage, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Religious Persecution, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
