Synopsis: 
In 'A Little Country Girl' by Susan Coolidge, we follow the journey of a young girl named Cicely, who is sent to live with her wealthy relatives in the city after the death of her parents. As Cicely navigates the unfamiliar world of privilege and sophistication, she must learn to adapt to her new surroundings while staying true to her humble roots. Along the way, she encounters unexpected friendships, faces challenges, and discovers the power of resilience and self-discovery. 

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: 
Identity, Social Class, Resilience, Friendship
