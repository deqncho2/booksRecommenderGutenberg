Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The Red Court Farm', secrets and scandals continue to unravel in the idyllic English countryside. As the lives of the residents intertwine, a mysterious newcomer arrives, stirring up old wounds and igniting forbidden passions. With the fate of the beloved farm hanging in the balance, the characters must navigate treacherous alliances and unexpected betrayals. Will they be able to overcome their pasts and find redemption, or will the dark secrets of the Red Court Farm tear them apart forever?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Redemption, Betrayal
