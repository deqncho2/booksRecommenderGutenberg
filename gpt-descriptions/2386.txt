Synopsis: In "Theodore Roosevelt: An Intimate Biography," William Roscoe Thayer delves into the extraordinary life of one of America's most captivating presidents. From his childhood struggles with illness to his adventures as a Rough Rider and his transformative presidency, Thayer paints a vivid portrait of Theodore Roosevelt's indomitable spirit and unwavering dedication to public service. Through personal anecdotes, letters, and interviews, the author offers readers an intimate glimpse into the complex personality and influential leadership of this larger-than-life figure. This biography is a captivating exploration of Roosevelt's life, revealing the man behind the legend.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Leadership, Public Service, Perseverance, Adventure
