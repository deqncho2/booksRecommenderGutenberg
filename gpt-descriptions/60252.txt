Synopsis: 
In 'Advice: A Book of Poems' by Maxwell Bodenheim, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking and emotionally charged poems. Each poem offers a unique perspective on life, love, and the human experience, inviting readers to reflect on their own existence and find solace in the power of words. From the depths of despair to the heights of joy, Bodenheim's poems explore the complexities of the human soul, leaving readers both moved and inspired.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Fiction

Themes: Life, Love, Human Experience, Reflection, Emotions
