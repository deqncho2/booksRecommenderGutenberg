Synopsis: In 'The World Peril of 1910' by George Chetwynd Griffith, a brilliant scientist unveils a groundbreaking invention that has the potential to change the course of history. However, as the world marvels at this technological marvel, a sinister plot unfolds, threatening the very fabric of society. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, a group of courageous individuals must race against time to uncover the truth and prevent a catastrophe of unimaginable proportions.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Technological advancement, Conspiracy, Heroism
