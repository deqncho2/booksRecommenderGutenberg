Synopsis: In "The Belt" by West, Wallace, a young engineer named Alex finds himself on a remote space station, tasked with repairing a malfunctioning asteroid mining belt. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the station, he uncovers a web of secrets and encounters unexpected dangers that challenge his sanity and survival. With time running out, Alex must navigate the treacherous corridors of the station and confront the enigmatic forces that lurk within. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Isolation, Exploration, Survival, Psychological Tension
