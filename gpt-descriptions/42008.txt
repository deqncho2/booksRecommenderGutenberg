Synopsis: In 'Telling Fortunes by Cards: A Symposium of the Several Ancient and Modern Methods as Practiced by Arab Seers and Sibyls and the Romany Gypsies', Ali, Mohammed delves into the captivating world of fortune-telling through cards. This intriguing book explores the various techniques employed by Arab seers, sibyls, and Romany Gypsies, revealing the secrets behind their mystical practices. From ancient divination methods to modern interpretations, the author takes readers on a journey through the fascinating art of card reading, offering insights into the symbolism, rituals, and predictions that have captivated cultures throughout history.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Divination

Themes: Fortune-telling, Mysticism, Cultural Traditions
