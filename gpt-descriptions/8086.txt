Synopsis: In a future where death has been conquered and the world is run by ad-hocracies, Jules, a former Disney World cast member, finds himself embroiled in a power struggle within the Magic Kingdom. As he navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and the pursuit of immortality, Jules must confront the dark side of utopia and question the very essence of what it means to be human.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Cyberpunk

Themes: Immortality, Post-scarcity society, Power dynamics, Identity
