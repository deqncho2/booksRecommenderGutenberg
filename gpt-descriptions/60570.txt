Synopsis: 
In 'Court Life From Within' by Eulalia, Infanta of Spain, readers are transported into the captivating world of the Spanish royal court. Through the eyes of the author, who herself is a member of the royal family, we are given a rare glimpse into the opulent and secretive lives of the Spanish monarchy. From lavish balls and extravagant ceremonies to political intrigues and forbidden romances, this memoir unveils the hidden truths and untold stories that lie behind the grandeur of the court. As Eulalia navigates the complex web of courtly etiquette and familial obligations, she must also grapple with her own desires and aspirations, ultimately challenging the very foundations of the monarchy. 

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Courtly life, Intrigue, Forbidden love, Identity, Power dynamics
