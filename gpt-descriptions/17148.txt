Synopsis: 

In 'Cessions of Land by Indian Tribes to the United States: Illustrated by Those in the State of Indiana', Charles C. Royce delves into the complex history of land cessions by Native American tribes to the United States government, with a particular focus on the state of Indiana. Through meticulous research and detailed analysis, Royce uncovers the intricate negotiations, treaties, and agreements that shaped the land ownership landscape in the region. This comprehensive report provides a fascinating glimpse into the historical interactions between Native American tribes and the United States, shedding light on the profound impact these land cessions had on both parties involved.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Anthropology, Native American studies

Themes: Land ownership, Native American history, Government treaties and agreements
