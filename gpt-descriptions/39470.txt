Synopsis:
In "The March to Magdala," the reader is taken on an exhilarating journey through the rugged terrains of Abyssinia (modern-day Ethiopia) during the 19th century. The story follows the adventures of a young British officer, Harry Feversham, who finds himself caught in the midst of a perilous mission to rescue British captives held by the Abyssinian emperor. As Feversham and his comrades face treacherous landscapes, hostile tribes, and the formidable Abyssinian army, they must summon their courage and resourcefulness to overcome seemingly insurmountable obstacles. Along the way, Feversham's character is tested, and he must confront his own fears and doubts, ultimately discovering the true meaning of loyalty, honor, and sacrifice.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes:
Courage, Loyalty, Redemption, Exploration of Abyssinian culture and history
