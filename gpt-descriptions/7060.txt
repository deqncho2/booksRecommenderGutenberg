Synopsis: 

In 'At Agincourt' by G. A. Henty, the reader is transported back to the 15th century, amidst the tumultuous events leading up to the famous Battle of Agincourt. The story follows the adventures of a young English archer named Ned, who finds himself caught in the midst of the fierce conflict between the English and the French. As Ned navigates the treacherous world of medieval warfare, he must overcome personal challenges, forge unlikely alliances, and prove his worth on the battlefield. With vivid descriptions of the era's weaponry, tactics, and political intrigue, this gripping historical novel offers a thrilling and immersive journey into one of the most significant battles in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Honor, Conflict, Survival
