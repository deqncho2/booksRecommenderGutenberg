Synopsis: In 'The Poet Assassinated', a renowned poet is found dead under mysterious circumstances, leaving the literary world in shock. As the investigation unfolds, a web of secrets, betrayals, and hidden desires is revealed, leading to unexpected twists and turns. With each revelation, the line between reality and fiction becomes blurred, leaving the reader questioning the true nature of art and the power of words.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological thriller, Surrealism

Themes: Art and creativity, Identity and perception, Truth and deception
