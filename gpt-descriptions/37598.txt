Synopsis: 
In 'Denis Dent: A Novel' by E. W. Hornung, readers are introduced to the enigmatic protagonist, Denis Dent, a man with a mysterious past and a talent for getting into trouble. Dent finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he becomes embroiled in a high-stakes game of espionage. As he navigates through a world of secret societies, hidden agendas, and unexpected alliances, Dent must rely on his wit, resourcefulness, and a few unlikely allies to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover Dent's fate.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Deception, Loyalty, Intrigue
