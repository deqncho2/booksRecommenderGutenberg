Synopsis: In 'The Hour of the Dragon', Conan the Cimmerian, the legendary barbarian king, finds himself dethroned and exiled from his kingdom. But when an ancient sorcerer resurrects him, Conan embarks on a perilous journey to reclaim his throne and save his people from a dark and malevolent force. Filled with epic battles, treacherous alliances, and a quest for redemption, this thrilling tale takes readers on a gripping adventure through a world of sorcery, swordplay, and political intrigue.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Sword and Sorcery

Themes: Power and Kingship, Redemption, Loyalty, Magic and Sorcery
