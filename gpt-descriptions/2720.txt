Synopsis: In 'The Pension Beaurepas' by Henry James, a young American artist named John Barrington visits a picturesque pension in Geneva, Switzerland, where he becomes captivated by the eccentricities and secrets of the other guests. As he delves deeper into their lives, he uncovers a web of hidden desires, unrequited love, and unexpected connections that challenge his own perceptions of art and human nature. With each encounter, Barrington finds himself drawn into a world of intrigue and mystery, where the boundaries between reality and illusion blur. 

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Drama

Themes: Identity, Perception, Artistic Expression, Human Relationships
