Synopsis: In "A Farmer's Wife: The Story of Ruth," we follow the journey of Ruth, a young woman who marries a farmer and finds herself navigating the challenges of rural life in the early 20th century. As she adapts to her new role as a wife and farmer's partner, Ruth encounters unexpected hardships, forms unlikely friendships, and discovers her own strength and resilience. This heartwarming tale explores the triumphs and tribulations of a woman's journey towards self-discovery and empowerment in a time of great change.

Genres: Historical fiction, Women's fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and marriage, Gender roles and expectations, Resilience and personal growth
