Synopsis: In "The Ambulance Made Two Trips" by Murray Leinster, a seemingly routine ambulance call takes a bizarre turn when the ambulance makes two trips to the same location. As the story unfolds, the protagonist finds themselves caught in a mind-bending mystery that challenges their perception of reality and tests their sanity. With each trip, the stakes rise, leading to a thrilling and unexpected conclusion.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Perception, Reality, Sanity, Mystery
