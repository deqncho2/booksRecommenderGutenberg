Synopsis: In 'A Cabinet Secret' by Boothby, Guy, a young and ambitious politician finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and scandal when he stumbles upon a mysterious secret hidden within the highest echelons of power. As he delves deeper into the dangerous world of politics, he must navigate treacherous alliances, deceitful adversaries, and his own moral compass to uncover the truth and protect his own future. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of power and the lengths people will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Political thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal
