Synopsis: In 'The Girl at Central' by Geraldine Bonner, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in the midst of a dangerous conspiracy when she takes a job as a telephone operator at Central Exchange. As she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and deceit, Mary must navigate the treacherous world of espionage and betrayal, all while trying to uncover the truth behind a mysterious murder. With her life on the line, Mary must rely on her wit and courage to survive and bring justice to those responsible.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Espionage, Betrayal, Justice
