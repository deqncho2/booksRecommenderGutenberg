Synopsis: In "Sevastopol," Tolstoy takes us on a gripping journey through the harrowing realities of war, as seen through the eyes of soldiers and civilians in the besieged city of Sevastopol during the Crimean War. Through a series of interconnected stories, Tolstoy explores the human spirit, the complexities of love and loyalty, and the profound impact of war on individuals and society.

Genres: Historical fiction, War literature, Realism

Themes: War, Heroism, Love and relationships, Morality, Human resilience
