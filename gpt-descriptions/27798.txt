Synopsis: In 'The Silver Lining: A Guernsey Story' by Roussel, John, a young woman named Emma finds herself drawn to the enchanting island of Guernsey, where she uncovers a hidden secret that has been buried for decades. As she delves deeper into the island's history, Emma becomes entangled in a web of mystery, love, and betrayal, leading her on a journey that will change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, the power of love and forgiveness, the resilience of the human spirit
