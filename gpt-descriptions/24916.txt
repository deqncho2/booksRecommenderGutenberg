Synopsis: In 'Crown and Anchor: Under the Pen'ant' by John C. Hutcheson, a young sailor named Tom finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. As he embarks on a thrilling adventure across the high seas, Tom must navigate treacherous waters, outsmart cunning adversaries, and uncover the truth behind the enigmatic Pen'ant. With his life hanging in the balance, Tom's determination and resourcefulness will be put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Treasure hunting, Survival, Betrayal
