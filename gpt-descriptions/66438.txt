Synopsis: In 'Vengeance From the Past' by Robert W. Krepps, a retired detective is haunted by a cold case that has resurfaced, forcing him to confront his own demons and unravel a web of secrets that spans decades. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he discovers that the truth is far more sinister than he could have ever imagined, leading him on a thrilling and dangerous journey to seek justice and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Revenge, Justice, Redemption
