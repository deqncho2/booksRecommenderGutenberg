Synopsis: In 'The Short Cut' by Gregory Jackson, a young woman named Emily stumbles upon a mysterious shortcut that promises to save her time and energy. Intrigued by the allure of this hidden path, she embarks on a journey that takes her to unexpected places and introduces her to a cast of intriguing characters. As Emily delves deeper into the secrets of the shortcut, she soon realizes that there is more to this seemingly innocent pathway than meets the eye. With each step she takes, the line between reality and fantasy becomes blurred, leading her to question her own perception of the world around her. As Emily races against time to uncover the truth, she must confront her deepest fears and make choices that will forever alter her destiny.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Self-Discovery, Consequences of Choices
