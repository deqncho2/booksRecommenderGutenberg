Synopsis: In 'The Flight of the Eagle' by Alfred Coppel, a daring pilot finds himself caught in a web of international intrigue when he is unexpectedly recruited for a top-secret mission. As he embarks on a dangerous journey across continents, he must navigate through a world of spies, double-crosses, and hidden agendas. With his skills and resourcefulness put to the ultimate test, he must uncover the truth and outsmart his enemies before it's too late. Will he succeed in his mission, or will he become just another pawn in a deadly game?

Genres: Thriller, Espionage, Adventure

Themes: Betrayal, Courage, Deception, Survival
