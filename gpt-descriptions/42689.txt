Synopsis: In 'The Young Cavalier: A Story of the Civil Wars', follow the thrilling journey of a young and courageous cavalier named Ralph Conway as he navigates the treacherous landscape of the English Civil Wars. Set against the backdrop of political unrest and divided loyalties, Ralph finds himself torn between his duty to his family and his unwavering commitment to the cause he believes in. As battles rage and alliances shift, Ralph's bravery and determination are put to the ultimate test, leading him on a path that will forever shape his destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Identity, Conflict
