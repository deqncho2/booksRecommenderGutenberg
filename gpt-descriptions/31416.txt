Synopsis: In 'The Immortal Moment: The Story of Kitty Tailleur' by Sinclair, May, we are introduced to Kitty Tailleur, a young woman with an extraordinary ability to freeze time. As she navigates through the challenges of her newfound power, Kitty discovers a hidden world of secrets and dangers that threaten to unravel her very existence. With the help of a mysterious mentor, she must learn to control her gift and uncover the truth behind her extraordinary abilities. This captivating tale takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with suspense, adventure, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Power and control, Identity and self-discovery, Secrets and deception
