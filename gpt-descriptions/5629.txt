Synopsis:
In "Dorothy Dale: A Girl of To-Day" by Margaret Penrose, we are introduced to the spirited and adventurous Dorothy Dale. Set in a small town, Dorothy finds herself caught up in a mysterious series of events that lead her on a thrilling journey. Alongside her loyal friends, she unravels secrets, faces unexpected challenges, and discovers the power of friendship and determination. As Dorothy navigates through the twists and turns of her adventure, readers will be captivated by her courage and resourcefulness, eagerly turning the pages to see how she overcomes each obstacle.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Determination
