Synopsis: 
'Among the Night People' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of nocturnal creatures. As the sun sets and darkness descends, the author introduces us to a diverse cast of characters, from mischievous fireflies to wise old owls, each with their own unique stories and secrets. Through vivid descriptions and charming anecdotes, Clara Dillingham Pierson invites us to explore the hidden wonders of the night and discover the extraordinary lives of these nocturnal beings. 

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Nature Writing, Animal Fiction

Themes: 
Nature's Mysteries, Nocturnal Life, Animal Behavior
