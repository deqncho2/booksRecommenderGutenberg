Synopsis: In 'Many Kingdoms' by Elizabeth Jordan, a young woman named Sarah discovers a hidden world beyond her imagination when she stumbles upon an ancient artifact. As she delves deeper into this mysterious realm, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, magic, and political intrigue. With the fate of multiple kingdoms hanging in the balance, Sarah must navigate treacherous alliances and confront her own inner demons to save those she loves. This captivating tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Discovery, Power, Identity
