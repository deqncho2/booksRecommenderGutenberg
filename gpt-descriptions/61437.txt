Synopsis: In "Set Down in Malice: A Book of Reminiscences" by Gerald Cumberland, readers are transported to a small, idyllic town where secrets lurk beneath the surface. The story follows the enigmatic protagonist, who finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue as he delves into the dark history of the town and its inhabitants. As he uncovers long-buried secrets, the line between truth and deception becomes increasingly blurred, leading to shocking revelations that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town intrigue, Unraveling the past
