Synopsis: In 'Critical Miscellanies (Vol. 3 of 3), Essay 7: W.R. Greg: A Sketch' by Morley, John, readers are introduced to the intriguing life and ideas of W.R. Greg, a prominent Victorian writer and philosopher. Through a captivating narrative, Morley delves into Greg's controversial views on marriage, women's rights, and the role of religion in society. As the essay unfolds, readers are taken on a thought-provoking journey that challenges conventional beliefs and sheds light on the complexities of Victorian society.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Social Commentary

Themes: Gender roles, Marriage, Religion, Social norms
