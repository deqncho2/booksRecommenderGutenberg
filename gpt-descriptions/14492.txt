Synopsis: 

In 'Notable Women of Modern China', Margaret E. Burton takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of extraordinary women who defied societal norms and made significant contributions to the shaping of modern China. From political activists and writers to educators and artists, these women challenged the traditional roles assigned to them and left an indelible mark on Chinese history. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Burton brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and resilience of these remarkable individuals, shedding light on their immense impact on the country's cultural, social, and political landscape. 

Genres: Biography, History, Women's Studies

Themes: Gender roles and expectations, Women's empowerment, Social change, Cultural preservation
