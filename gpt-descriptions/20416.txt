Synopsis: 
'The Annual Catalogue (1737): Or, A New and Compleat List of All The New Books, New Editions of Books, Pamphlets, &c.' is a comprehensive catalog that showcases the latest literary offerings of the year 1737. Compiled by Warner and Worrall, this catalogue provides a tantalizing glimpse into the diverse range of books, pamphlets, and other publications that were available during this period. From works of fiction to scientific treatises, from political pamphlets to religious texts, this catalogue presents a treasure trove of knowledge and entertainment for readers of all interests.

Genres: 
Catalog, Reference

Themes: 
Literature, Knowledge, Publishing
