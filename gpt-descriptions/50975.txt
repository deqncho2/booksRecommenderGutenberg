Synopsis: In 'Motor Matt's Prize; or, The Pluck That Wins', Stanley R. Matthews takes readers on an exhilarating journey alongside the daring and resourceful Motor Matt. When a mysterious prize is up for grabs, Motor Matt finds himself entangled in a high-stakes race against time, facing treacherous obstacles and unexpected adversaries. With his quick thinking and unwavering determination, Motor Matt must rely on his wits and mechanical skills to secure the ultimate prize. Will he emerge victorious or be left in the dust?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Perseverance, Resourcefulness, Competition
