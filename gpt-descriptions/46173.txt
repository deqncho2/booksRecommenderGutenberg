Synopsis: In 'Tourmalin's Time Cheques' by F. Anstey, a young man named Tourmalin discovers a mysterious set of time cheques that allow him to travel through time. As he explores different eras, he encounters various challenges and adventures, all while trying to navigate the consequences of altering history. With each journey, Tourmalin learns valuable lessons about the nature of time and the importance of making the right choices.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Consequences of actions, Morality
