Synopsis: 

In "Trial of the Major War Criminals Before the International Military Tribunal, Nuremberg, 14 November 1945-1 October 1946, Volume 03," the gripping courtroom drama unfolds as the major war criminals of World War II face justice for their heinous crimes. This volume delves into the intense cross-examinations, shocking revelations, and emotional testimonies that shed light on the darkest period in human history. As the trial progresses, the reader is drawn into the complex web of evidence, legal arguments, and moral dilemmas that challenge the very foundations of justice.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Legal drama, World War II

Themes: War crimes, Justice, Accountability, Human rights
