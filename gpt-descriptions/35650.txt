Synopsis: 
In 'A Treatise on the Police of the Metropolis', Patrick Colquhoun delves into the dark underbelly of the city, exposing the multitude of crimes and misdemeanors that threaten public and private property, as well as the safety of its inhabitants. With meticulous detail, he explores the various methods employed by criminals to injure and endanger the security of the metropolis. However, Colquhoun does not stop at merely highlighting the problems; he also offers insightful remedies for their prevention, providing a glimmer of hope in the face of rampant criminality.

Genres: Non-fiction, Crime, Social Commentary

Themes: Crime prevention, Public safety, Social order
