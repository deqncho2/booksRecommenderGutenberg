Synopsis: 

Join Annie Brassey on an extraordinary adventure as she embarks on a daring voyage to India and Australia aboard the magnificent yacht, Sunbeam. From the bustling streets of Bombay to the exotic landscapes of the Great Barrier Reef, this captivating travelogue takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with breathtaking sights, fascinating encounters, and unexpected challenges. As Annie immerses herself in the vibrant cultures and natural wonders of these distant lands, she provides a vivid and intimate account of her experiences, offering a unique perspective on the people, places, and customs she encounters along the way. 

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Immersion, Resilience
