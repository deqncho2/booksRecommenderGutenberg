Synopsis: In the small town of Pineville, a mysterious woman named Lydia Orr arrives with a unique talent - the ability to spin golden thread from ordinary flax. As the townspeople become captivated by her enchanting gift, Lydia's presence begins to unravel secrets, ignite desires, and challenge the boundaries of love and sacrifice. With her spinning wheel as the catalyst, the lives of Pineville's residents intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to a series of events that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Magic and the supernatural, Love and sacrifice, Secrets and hidden desires
