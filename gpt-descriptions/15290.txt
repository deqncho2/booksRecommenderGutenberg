Synopsis: In 'Why the Chimes Rang: A Play in One Act', the audience is transported to a grand cathedral on Christmas Eve, where a mysterious event is about to unfold. As the chimes in the tower hold a legendary power, the play follows the journey of a young boy who embarks on a quest to discover the true meaning of selflessness and the magic that lies within the chimes. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, this enchanting play captures the essence of the holiday spirit and the transformative power of generosity.

Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Holiday

Themes: Selflessness, Generosity, Faith, Magic
