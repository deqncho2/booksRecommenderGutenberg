Synopsis: 
At Hotel On-de-Blink: An entertainment in two parts takes readers on a thrilling journey through the mysterious and enchanting world of a peculiar hotel. As guests arrive at this seemingly ordinary establishment, they soon discover that nothing is as it seems. From peculiar staff members to unexpected encounters, each room holds a unique and captivating story waiting to be unraveled. With each part of the entertainment, the intrigue deepens, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets hidden within the walls of Hotel On-de-Blink.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Transformation, Unpredictability
