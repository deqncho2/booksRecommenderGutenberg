Synopsis: 
The Complete Plays of John Galsworthy is a captivating collection of thought-provoking dramas that delve into the complexities of human relationships and societal issues. From the struggles of the working class to the conflicts within the upper echelons of society, Galsworthy's plays offer a profound exploration of the human condition. With compelling characters and intricate plotlines, this collection takes readers on a journey through the triumphs and tragedies of life, leaving them pondering the intricacies of morality, justice, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Drama, Social Realism, Tragedy

Themes: Social class, Morality, Justice, Love and relationships, Family dynamics
