Synopsis: In 'Knole and the Sackvilles', Victoria Sackville-West takes us on a captivating journey through the history of her ancestral home, Knole, and the remarkable family that has inhabited it for centuries. From scandalous affairs to hidden secrets, this enthralling narrative unveils the captivating lives of the Sackvilles, weaving together a tapestry of love, ambition, and the enduring power of heritage.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction, Family Saga

Themes: Family legacy, Identity, Love and relationships, Historical preservation
