Synopsis: In 'Homo: 1909' by Smith, Francis Hopkinson, a mysterious discovery in an ancient Egyptian tomb sets off a chain of events that will challenge the boundaries of science and human understanding. As a renowned archaeologist delves deeper into the secrets of this extraordinary find, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden knowledge. With each revelation, the line between myth and reality blurs, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the very nature of humanity.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Science Fiction

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the limits of human knowledge, the nature of existence
