Synopsis: 

'Makers of British Botany; a collection of biographies by living botanists' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and achievements of some of the most influential figures in British botany. From their humble beginnings to their groundbreaking discoveries, this collection offers a unique insight into the passion, dedication, and struggles that shaped these remarkable individuals. Each biography is a testament to the power of curiosity and the profound impact that botanical science has had on our understanding of the natural world. 

Genres: Biography, Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Botanical exploration, Scientific discovery, Personal perseverance
