Synopsis: In "The Girl in the Mirror" by Elizabeth Garver Jordan, a young woman named Alice discovers a mysterious mirror that holds a captivating secret. As she delves deeper into its enchanting powers, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. With each reflection, Alice uncovers shocking truths about her own identity and the dark forces that seek to control her. As she fights to break free from the mirror's grasp, Alice must confront her deepest fears and make a choice that will change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Power, Self-discovery
