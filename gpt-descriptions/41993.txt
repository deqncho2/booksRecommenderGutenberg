Synopsis: In 'The Sabbath at Home' by Silas M. Andrews, a seemingly ordinary Sabbath day takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious visitor arrives at the protagonist's doorstep. As the day unfolds, secrets are revealed, tensions rise, and the characters are forced to confront their deepest fears and desires. With its gripping narrative and unpredictable twists, this book will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the visitor's arrival.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Moral dilemmas, Confronting the past
