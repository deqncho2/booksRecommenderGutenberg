Synopsis: In 'Preview of Peril' by Alfred Coppel, a renowned photojournalist finds himself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue when he stumbles upon a mysterious photograph. As he delves deeper into the secrets behind the image, he becomes the target of a relentless pursuit, forcing him to confront his own past and make choices that could change his life forever. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
