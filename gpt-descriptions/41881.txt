Synopsis: 
The Corsican Brothers is a thrilling tale of two brothers, Lucien and Louis, who are separated at birth but share an extraordinary bond. Raised in different environments, they grow up unaware of each other's existence until a series of mysterious events brings them together. As they uncover the truth about their past and the dark secrets surrounding their family, they embark on a dangerous journey filled with revenge, love, and betrayal. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Corsica, this gripping novel explores the power of brotherhood and the lengths one will go to protect their loved ones.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Family bonds, Revenge, Identity, Loyalty
