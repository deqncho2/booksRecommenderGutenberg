Synopsis: 
In 'Sisters' by Kathleen Thompson Norris, two sisters, Ellen and Julia, find themselves navigating the complexities of love, marriage, and family in early 20th century America. Ellen, the older sister, is a strong-willed and independent woman who is determined to follow her own path, even if it means defying societal expectations. Julia, on the other hand, is more traditional and struggles to find her own voice amidst the pressures of conformity. As they face various challenges and heartbreaks, the bond between the sisters is tested, forcing them to confront their own desires and the sacrifices they are willing to make for love and happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Sisterhood, Love, Independence, Societal Expectations
