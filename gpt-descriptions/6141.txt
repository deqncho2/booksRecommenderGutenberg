Synopsis: In 'Peck's Bad Boy with the Cowboys' by George W. Peck, follow the mischievous adventures of Peck's Bad Boy as he finds himself in the wild west, surrounded by cowboys, outlaws, and the untamed frontier. From causing chaos at the local saloon to getting involved in a high-stakes cattle drive, this young troublemaker brings his unique brand of mischief to the rugged world of the cowboys. With humor, excitement, and unexpected twists, this story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what Peck's Bad Boy will do next.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Humor

Themes: Mischief, Coming of Age, Frontier Life
