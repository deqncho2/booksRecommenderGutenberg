Synopsis: 
The Fallen Leaves by Wilkie Collins is a gripping tale that follows the lives of two sisters, Clara and Lucy, who find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and forbidden love. As they navigate through a series of unexpected events, their lives take a dramatic turn, leading them to question their identities and the true nature of those around them. With a mysterious disappearance and a shocking revelation, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Forbidden Love, Secrets
