Synopsis: In the aftermath of the American Civil War, Anne Douglas, a spirited and independent young woman, finds herself caught between the expectations of society and her own desires for freedom and self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Mackinac Island, Anne's journey unfolds as she navigates the complexities of love, family, and the pursuit of her own dreams. As she grapples with the challenges of her time, Anne's story becomes a captivating exploration of identity, resilience, and the power of personal choice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Gender roles, societal expectations, self-discovery, love and relationships, personal freedom
