Synopsis: In 'The Royal End: A Romance' by Henry Harland, a young woman finds herself entangled in a forbidden love affair with a member of the royal family. As their passionate relationship unfolds, secrets, betrayals, and unexpected twists threaten to tear them apart. Set against the backdrop of opulent palaces and societal expectations, this captivating tale explores the lengths one will go to for love and the sacrifices that must be made to protect it.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Sacrifice, Betrayal, Society's expectations
