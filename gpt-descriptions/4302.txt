Synopsis:
In the bustling streets of Victorian London, Thyrza Trent, a young and spirited girl, finds herself caught between the harsh realities of poverty and the allure of a better life. As she navigates the challenges of her circumstances, Thyrza's resilience and determination are tested, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of her dreams. Along the way, she encounters a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own secrets and desires, who shape her path in unexpected ways. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society, 'Thyrza' is a captivating tale of love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Social Realism

Themes:
Poverty, Social class, Ambition, Love, Identity
