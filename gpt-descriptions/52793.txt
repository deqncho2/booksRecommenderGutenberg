Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Seventeenth, or the Leicestershire Regiment of Foot', Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of the renowned regiment from 1688 to 1848. From its humble beginnings to its involvement in significant historical events, Cannon's account offers a fascinating insight into the regiment's trials, triumphs, and the brave men who served within its ranks. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military service, Regimental history, Historical events
