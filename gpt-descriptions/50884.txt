Synopsis: In 'Today is Forever' by Roger D. Aycock, a young woman named Emily discovers a mysterious journal that transports her back in time to the 19th century. As she navigates this unfamiliar world, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, romance, and danger. With each journey, Emily uncovers shocking truths about her own family history and the power of love that transcends time. Will she find a way to reconcile the past with the present, or will her newfound knowledge forever alter her future?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Time Travel, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Love transcending time, Identity and self-discovery
