Synopsis: 
'Under the Shadow of Etna: Sicilian Stories from the Italian of Giovanni Verga' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and complex world of Sicily. From the bustling streets of Catania to the quiet countryside, these stories delve into the lives of ordinary Sicilian people, exploring their dreams, desires, and struggles. Through vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Verga paints a vivid picture of the island's rich culture and traditions, while also shedding light on the harsh realities of poverty and social inequality. With each story, the reader is transported to a different corner of Sicily, where they witness the power of love, the weight of tradition, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Regional Literature

Themes: Sicilian culture, Social inequality, Love and relationships, Tradition and modernity
