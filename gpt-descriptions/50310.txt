Synopsis: 
In "Mother Goose for Grown-ups," Guy Wetmore Carryl takes the beloved nursery rhymes of Mother Goose and gives them a hilarious and irreverent twist. From Little Miss Muffet's unexpected encounter with a spider to Jack and Jill's misadventures up the hill, Carryl's witty and satirical reimagining of these classic tales will have readers laughing out loud. With clever wordplay and unexpected plot twists, this collection of humorous poems is sure to entertain and surprise.

Genres: 
Satire, Poetry, Humor

Themes: 
Humor, Satire, Parody
