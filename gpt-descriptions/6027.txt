Synopsis: In "In the Closed Room" by Frances Hodgson Burnett, a young woman named Rosalie finds herself drawn to a mysterious locked room in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within, she uncovers a haunting tale of love, loss, and the power of the human spirit. With each revelation, Rosalie's own life becomes intertwined with the past, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Historical fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Redemption, Self-discovery
