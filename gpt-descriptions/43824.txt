Synopsis: Join Lewis R. Freeman on an exhilarating yachting adventure as he embarks on a fourteen thousand mile journey to the enchanting islands of Hawaii, Marquesas, Societies, Samoas, and Fijis. From the breathtaking landscapes to the captivating cultures, Freeman's vivid account takes readers on a thrilling voyage filled with unexpected encounters, daring escapades, and unforgettable moments. As he navigates the vast Pacific Ocean, Freeman's narrative paints a mesmerizing picture of the beauty and allure of these remote island paradises, leaving readers yearning to set sail on their own extraordinary adventure.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Natural beauty, Personal growth
