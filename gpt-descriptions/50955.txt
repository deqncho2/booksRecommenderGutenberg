Synopsis: In 'The Cities of the Sun: Stories of Ancient America founded on historical incidents in the Book of Mormon', Elizabeth Cannon Porter takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient civilizations of America, as inspired by the Book of Mormon. Through a collection of stories, Porter weaves together historical incidents and vivid characters to bring to life the rich tapestry of ancient American cultures. From the bustling cities of the Nephites to the mysterious lands of the Lamanites, readers will be immersed in a world of adventure, spirituality, and the struggles of humanity. As the stories unfold, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the fate of these ancient civilizations hangs in the balance. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Spirituality

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Faith, Identity, Conflict, Redemption
