Synopsis: In 'Josiah's Alarm, and Abel Perry's Funeral' by Holley, Marietta, a small town is shaken by a mysterious alarm that disrupts the peaceful lives of its residents. As the townspeople try to unravel the source of the alarm, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden connections that will change their lives forever. Meanwhile, the unexpected death of Abel Perry, a beloved member of the community, brings grief and reflection to the town, forcing its inhabitants to confront their own mortality and the fragility of human relationships. This captivating story weaves together mystery, intrigue, and profound emotions, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth behind the alarm and the impact of Abel Perry's funeral.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Community, Secrets, Mortality, Relationships
