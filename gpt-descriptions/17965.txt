Synopsis: 
In 'Boy Woodburn: A Story of the Sussex Downs', we are transported to the picturesque landscapes of the Sussex Downs, where a young boy named Boy Woodburn embarks on a remarkable journey of self-discovery. Raised in a humble village, Boy's life takes an unexpected turn when he encounters a mysterious stranger who reveals a hidden family secret. As Boy delves deeper into his ancestry, he uncovers a world filled with adventure, danger, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of his family and the Sussex Downs hanging in the balance, Boy must summon all his courage and determination to face the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Family, Heritage, Courage
