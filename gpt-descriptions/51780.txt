Synopsis:
In 'The Olive' by K. G. Bitting, a young woman named Olive finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter hidden in her deceased grandmother's belongings. As she delves deeper into her family's history, Olive uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to unravel everything she thought she knew about her own identity. With each revelation, Olive becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception, where the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. As she races against time to uncover the truth, Olive must confront her own past and make difficult choices that will determine her future.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes:
Family secrets, Identity, Deception
