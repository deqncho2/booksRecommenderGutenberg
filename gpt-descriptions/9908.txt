Synopsis: In 'The False Faces: Further Adventures from the History of the Lone Wolf', the notorious gentleman thief, Michael Lanyard, finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue and danger as he navigates the treacherous world of espionage. With his quick wit and cunning, Lanyard must outsmart his adversaries, uncover hidden secrets, and protect those he holds dear. As he delves deeper into the shadows, he discovers that not everything is as it seems, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. Will the Lone Wolf prevail, or will he become just another pawn in a deadly game?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Espionage, Deception, Loyalty
