Synopsis: 

In 'Certain Success' by Norval A. Hawkins, a young and ambitious entrepreneur named Alex finds himself at the center of a high-stakes business deal that could make or break his career. As he navigates the cutthroat world of corporate politics and ruthless competition, Alex must confront his own insecurities and make difficult choices that will test his integrity and determination. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the price of success and the lengths one is willing to go to achieve it.

Genres: Business fiction, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Corporate intrigue
