Synopsis: 
In 'Skiddoo!' by George V. Hobart, follow the hilarious misadventures of a down-on-his-luck actor named Jimmy, who finds himself caught up in a whirlwind of mistaken identities, eccentric characters, and outrageous situations. From mistaken marriages to mistaken identities, Jimmy's life takes an unexpected turn when he stumbles upon a secret society that will change his life forever. With witty dialogue, slapstick humor, and unexpected twists, 'Skiddoo!' is a delightful comedy that will keep readers laughing from start to finish.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Mistaken identity, Theatrical life, Absurdity, Social satire
