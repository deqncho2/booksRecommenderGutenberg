Synopsis: In 'But the Patient Lived' by Harry Warner, a brilliant surgeon finds himself facing a life-or-death situation when a routine surgery takes an unexpected turn. As he battles against time and unforeseen complications, the doctor must navigate the complexities of the medical field, confront his own limitations, and make difficult decisions that could determine the fate of his patient. This gripping and suspenseful tale explores the high-stakes world of medicine, the resilience of the human spirit, and the profound impact of one doctor's unwavering determination.

Genres: Medical thriller, Drama, Suspense

Themes: Life and death, Perseverance, Ethical dilemmas
