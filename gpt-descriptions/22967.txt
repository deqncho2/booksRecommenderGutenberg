Synopsis: In 'The Stoker and the Stars' by Algis Budrys, a young stoker named Peter realizes that his monotonous life aboard a spaceship is about to change when he stumbles upon a mysterious object. As he delves deeper into its secrets, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger that could alter the fate of humanity. With his newfound knowledge, Peter must navigate the treacherous waters of power and deception, all while grappling with his own identity and purpose in the vastness of space.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration of identity, Power dynamics, Humanity's place in the universe
