Synopsis: In 'A Trace of Memory' by Keith Laumer, a man wakes up in a strange place with no memory of who he is or how he got there. As he embarks on a journey to uncover his identity, he discovers a world filled with advanced technology, dangerous adversaries, and a mysterious organization that will stop at nothing to keep their secrets hidden. With each clue he uncovers, he becomes closer to the truth, but also closer to danger. Will he be able to reclaim his past and survive the perilous path he's on?

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Memory, Conspiracy
