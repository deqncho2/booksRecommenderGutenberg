Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Glenloch Girls, a prestigious boarding school nestled in the Scottish Highlands, secrets and mysteries abound. As new student Emily arrives, she quickly becomes entangled in a web of friendships, rivalries, and hidden pasts. With each passing day, the girls of Glenloch uncover dark secrets that threaten to unravel their lives and the very fabric of the school itself. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, Emily must navigate the treacherous waters of Glenloch Girls if she hopes to survive and uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Betrayal, Secrets, Coming-of-age
