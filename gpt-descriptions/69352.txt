Synopsis: In 'The X Bar X boys on Whirlpool River' by James Cody Ferris, a group of adventurous young cowboys find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon a hidden treasure map. As they navigate the treacherous Whirlpool River, they encounter dangerous outlaws, unexpected allies, and face numerous challenges that test their courage and resourcefulness. With their lives on the line, the X Bar X boys must rely on their wits and teamwork to unravel the secrets of the map and claim the treasure before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Treasure hunting
