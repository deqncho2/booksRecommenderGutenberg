Synopsis: In 'Time Crime' by H. Beam Piper, a brilliant scientist accidentally discovers a way to travel through time, leading to a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of time travel, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and conspiracies that could alter the course of history forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Time travel, Consequences of scientific discovery, Manipulation of history
