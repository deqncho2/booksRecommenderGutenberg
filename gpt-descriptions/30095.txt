Synopsis: In 'At the Crossroads' by Harriet T. Comstock, a young woman named Mary finds herself at a pivotal moment in her life when she is faced with a difficult decision that could change her future forever. As she navigates the challenges and uncertainties of love, family, and personal growth, Mary must confront her deepest desires and confront the consequences of her choices. Set against the backdrop of a small town in the early 20th century, this captivating novel explores the complexities of human relationships and the transformative power of self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and relationships, Self-discovery, Choices and consequences
