Synopsis: In 'The True Story of the American Flag' by Fow, John Henry, embark on a captivating journey through the history of the iconic American flag. From its humble beginnings to its evolution as a symbol of unity and freedom, this book unveils the untold stories behind the stars and stripes. Discover the remarkable individuals who played a pivotal role in shaping the flag's design and significance, and delve into the controversies and triumphs that have surrounded it throughout history. With vivid storytelling and fascinating anecdotes, this book brings to life the extraordinary tale of the American flag, reminding us of the enduring power of patriotism and the values it represents.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, American history

Themes: Patriotism, National identity, Symbolism
