Synopsis: In "The Twilight Years" by Drussai, Garen/Drussai, Kirk, a retired detective and a young journalist unexpectedly find themselves entangled in a mysterious series of events that lead them to uncover dark secrets from the past. As they delve deeper into the shadows, they must confront their own demons and navigate a treacherous web of lies and deceit. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and they must race against time to expose the truth before it consumes them both.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Identity
