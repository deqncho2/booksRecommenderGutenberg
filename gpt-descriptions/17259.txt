Synopsis: In 'His Second Wife' by Ernest Poole, a successful businessman finds himself torn between his loyalty to his first wife and the allure of a passionate affair with a captivating woman. As he navigates the complexities of love, desire, and commitment, he must confront the consequences of his choices and the impact they have on those around him. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century New York City, this gripping tale explores the depths of human emotions and the complexities of relationships.

Genres: Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and betrayal, loyalty and temptation, the complexities of marriage and relationships
