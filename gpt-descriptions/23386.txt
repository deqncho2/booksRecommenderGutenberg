Synopsis: In the King's Name: The Cruise of the "Kestrel" takes readers on a thrilling maritime adventure set in the 18th century. When young Lieutenant Leigh is given command of the "Kestrel," a swift and powerful warship, he embarks on a dangerous mission to protect the interests of the British Empire. As he navigates treacherous waters, battles enemy ships, and faces the perils of the high seas, Leigh must also confront personal challenges that test his loyalty and courage. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Naval Fiction

Themes: Duty and Honor, Loyalty, Coming of Age
