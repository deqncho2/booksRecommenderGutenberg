Synopsis: In the bustling streets of New York City, a young doctor named Felix enters the world of faith healing, captivating the attention of both believers and skeptics alike. As he navigates the complexities of his newfound profession, Felix finds himself torn between his scientific training and the inexplicable miracles he witnesses. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society, 'The Faith Doctor: A Story of New York' explores the clash between reason and faith, the power of belief, and the search for meaning in an ever-evolving world.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Faith and reason, Science and spirituality, Belief and skepticism, Society and progress
