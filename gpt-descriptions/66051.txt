Synopsis: In 'The Millbank Case: A Maine Mystery of To-day', a small town in Maine is shaken by a mysterious disappearance that leaves the community in a state of fear and suspicion. When a prominent local figure vanishes without a trace, it falls upon a determined detective to unravel the truth behind the enigmatic Millbank case. As the investigation unfolds, dark secrets and hidden motives begin to surface, leading the detective down a twisted path of deception and betrayal. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the detective must race against time to solve the puzzle before more lives are put at risk. This gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the shocking truth that lies beneath the surface.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Small-town dynamics
