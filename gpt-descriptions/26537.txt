Synopsis: In the enchanting world of The Windy Hill, young Merry and her family embark on a summer adventure that takes them to a mysterious island. As they explore the island's secrets, they encounter a cast of intriguing characters and uncover a hidden treasure that holds the key to their future. With elements of mystery, adventure, and a touch of magic, this captivating tale will transport readers to a world where anything is possible.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Family bonds, Discovery, Courage
