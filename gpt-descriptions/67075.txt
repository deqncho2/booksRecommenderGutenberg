Synopsis: In 'A Whirl Asunder' by Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton, a young woman named Margaret finds herself caught in a whirlwind of passion, betrayal, and societal expectations in the vibrant city of San Francisco during the late 19th century. As she navigates the treacherous waters of love and ambition, Margaret must confront the consequences of her choices and the limitations imposed upon her by a society that seeks to confine her. With its vivid portrayal of the era's social dynamics and the complexities of human relationships, 'A Whirl Asunder' is a captivating tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Gender Roles and Expectations, Social Class and Ambition
