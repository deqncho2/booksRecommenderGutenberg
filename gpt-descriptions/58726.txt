Synopsis: In 'Small Talk at Wreyland. First Series' by Cecil Torr, a group of friends gather at the idyllic Wreyland estate for a series of intriguing conversations. From discussions on art and literature to debates on philosophy and politics, the characters delve into the depths of human nature and the complexities of society. As the conversations unfold, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and unexpected connections are made, leading to a captivating exploration of the human condition.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Literary Fiction

Themes: Human nature, Society, Philosophy, Relationships
