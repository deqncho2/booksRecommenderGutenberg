Synopsis: 
In "Sulamith: A Romance of Antiquity," the renowned Russian author Aleksandr Kuprin takes us on a captivating journey to the ancient world of Babylon. The story revolves around Sulamith, a beautiful and enigmatic woman who captures the hearts of those around her, including the powerful King Nebuchadnezzar. As the narrative unfolds, we are immersed in a world of forbidden love, political intrigue, and the clash between personal desires and societal expectations. Kuprin masterfully weaves together a tale of passion, sacrifice, and the timeless struggle for freedom and self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Power and Politics, Freedom and Self-Discovery
