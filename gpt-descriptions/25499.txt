Synopsis: In 'The Great White Queen: A Tale of Treasure and Treason' by William Le Queux, a thrilling adventure unfolds as a young woman finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and hidden treasures. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century Europe, the story follows the protagonist's quest to uncover the truth behind her family's mysterious past and the valuable artifact they possess. As she navigates through treacherous alliances, espionage, and unexpected romance, she must stay one step ahead of those who would stop at nothing to claim the treasure for themselves. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leading to a climactic showdown that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Pursuit of treasure, Love and romance, Intrigue
