Synopsis: In 'The Coming Wave; Or, The Hidden Treasure of High Rock' by Optic, Oliver, a group of adventurous friends stumble upon a mysterious treasure map that leads them on a thrilling journey to uncover a hidden treasure. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter dangerous obstacles, and face unexpected challenges, the friends must rely on their courage, teamwork, and quick thinking to unravel the secrets of High Rock. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies at the heart of the hidden treasure.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Courage, Discovery
