Synopsis: 
"Society's Child (lyrics)" by Janis Ian is a powerful and thought-provoking song that delves into the complexities of societal norms and the consequences of going against them. Through poignant and evocative lyrics, the song explores the struggles of a young woman who finds herself caught between her own desires and the expectations of those around her. As she navigates the challenges of love, prejudice, and social judgment, she must confront the harsh realities of a society that refuses to accept her choices. With its raw emotion and compelling storytelling, "Society's Child (lyrics)" offers a profound reflection on the human experience and the courage it takes to challenge the status quo.

Genres: Folk, Pop, Singer-songwriter

Themes: Social norms, Identity, Prejudice, Love, Rebellion
