Synopsis: In a world where ordinary people possess extraordinary abilities, a group of reluctant heroes must come together to save humanity from a looming threat. As they navigate their own personal struggles and insecurities, they are forced to confront their fears and embrace their true potential. With high-stakes action, unexpected alliances, and a race against time, 'The Reluctant Heroes' takes readers on an exhilarating journey filled with twists and turns that will keep them on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Action, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Identity, Overcoming adversity
