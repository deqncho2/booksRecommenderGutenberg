Synopsis: The Every-day Book and Table Book. v. 2 (of 3): or Everlasting Calendar of Popular Amusements, Sports, Pastimes, Ceremonies, Manners, Customs and Events, Incident to Each of the Three Hundred and Sixty-five Days, in past and Present Times; Forming a Complete History of the Year, Month, and Seasons, and a Perpetual Key to the Almanac by William Hone is a fascinating exploration of the daily activities, traditions, and events that have shaped each day of the year. From ancient customs to modern pastimes, this book provides a comprehensive history of the year, month, and seasons, offering a perpetual key to the almanac. Delve into the rich tapestry of popular amusements, sports, ceremonies, and manners that have colored our lives throughout history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Reference

Themes: Cultural traditions, Daily life, Seasonal celebrations
