Synopsis: In the midst of political turmoil and social unrest, a renowned Roman general named Coriolanus finds himself torn between his duty to his country and his own personal desires. As he navigates the treacherous world of power and manipulation, Coriolanus must confront his own flaws and confront the consequences of his actions. With betrayal, revenge, and the struggle for identity at its core, "The Tragedy of Coriolanus" is a gripping tale of ambition and the complexities of human nature.

Genres: Tragedy, Historical Drama

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Reflection
