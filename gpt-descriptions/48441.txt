Synopsis: In 'Putnam's Phrase Book', Edwin Hamlin Carr presents a comprehensive guide to social letter writing and engaging conversation. With over 100 model social letters and 6000 of the world's best English phrases, this book equips readers with the tools to express themselves eloquently and confidently in various social situations. Whether it's crafting a heartfelt letter or engaging in captivating conversation, Carr's book provides invaluable guidance and inspiration. 

Genres: Self-help, Language and Communication, Reference

Themes: Effective communication, Social etiquette, Language mastery
