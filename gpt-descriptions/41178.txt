Synopsis: 
"I Believe" and other essays by Thorne, Guy is a collection of thought-provoking and introspective essays that delve into the complexities of human existence. From exploring the nature of belief and the power of imagination to examining the intricacies of love and the pursuit of happiness, Thorne takes readers on a captivating journey through the depths of the human psyche. With a blend of personal anecdotes, philosophical musings, and insightful observations, this collection offers a unique perspective on life's most profound questions.

Genres: 
Essay, Philosophy, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Belief, Imagination, Love, Happiness, Human Existence
