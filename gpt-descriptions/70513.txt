Synopsis: 
In "The Non-Stop Stowaway: The Story of a Long Distance Flight," author Clayton Knight takes readers on a thrilling journey aboard a transatlantic flight. As the plane soars through the skies, a mysterious stowaway is discovered, setting off a chain of events that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the lengths one person will go to in order to achieve their goals, and the consequences that follow. Will the crew be able to handle the situation and ensure the safety of all passengers? Find out in this heart-pounding aviation adventure.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Perseverance, Consequences, Morality
