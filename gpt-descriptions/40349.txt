Synopsis: In 'The Chase of the Ruby' by Richard Marsh, a priceless ruby is stolen from a wealthy aristocrat's mansion, setting off a thrilling chase across London. As the cunning thief eludes the authorities, a determined detective and a resourceful journalist join forces to track down the elusive culprit. With each twist and turn, the chase becomes more perilous, leading to a climactic showdown that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Pursuit of justice, Deception and disguise, Cat-and-mouse game
