Synopsis: In 'Catalysis' by Poul Anderson, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Martels discovers a groundbreaking formula that has the potential to change the world. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but also the fate of humanity. With time running out, Martels must navigate a treacherous web of intrigue, betrayal, and scientific ethics to protect his discovery and ensure the survival of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific advancement, Morality, Conspiracy
