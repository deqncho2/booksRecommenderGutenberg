Synopsis: 

'George Morland: Sixteen examples in colour of the artist's work' by E. D. Cuming showcases a stunning collection of sixteen vibrant and captivating paintings by the renowned artist George Morland. Each artwork is accompanied by insightful commentary, shedding light on Morland's artistic techniques and the stories behind his creations. From picturesque landscapes to lively scenes of rural life, this book offers a glimpse into the talent and imagination of one of the most celebrated painters of his time.

Genres: Art, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, Rural life, Landscape, Historical context
