Synopsis: In 'Try Again; Or, the Trials and Triumphs of Harry West. A Story for Young Folks', we follow the journey of Harry West, a young boy who faces numerous challenges and setbacks. From his struggles with school and friendships to his determination to overcome adversity, Harry's story is one of resilience and growth. As he navigates through various trials, Harry learns valuable life lessons and discovers the true meaning of perseverance. Will he be able to rise above his difficulties and find triumph in the face of adversity?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Resilience, Perseverance, Growth
