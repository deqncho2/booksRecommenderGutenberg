Synopsis: In 'Common Sense in the Household: A Manual of Practical Housewifery', Marion Harland offers a comprehensive guide to managing a household with efficiency and grace. From cooking and cleaning to budgeting and entertaining, Harland provides practical advice and insightful tips that will revolutionize the way you run your home. With a focus on simplicity and common sense, this manual is a must-read for anyone seeking to create a harmonious and well-organized household.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Home and Garden

Themes: Household management, Efficiency, Practicality
