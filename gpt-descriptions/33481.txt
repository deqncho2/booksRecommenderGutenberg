Synopsis: In 'The Guns of Europe' by Joseph A. Altsheler, the world is on the brink of war as tensions rise between the great powers of Europe. Amidst this backdrop, a group of young American friends find themselves caught up in the chaos and danger of the conflict. From the trenches of the Western Front to the treacherous seas, they must navigate the horrors of war while facing personal trials and forging unbreakable bonds. As the battle lines are drawn and the guns of Europe roar, their lives will be forever changed in ways they could never have imagined.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Sacrifice, Coming of Age
