Synopsis: In 'Sand Dunes Story' by Bessken, Donna P., a young woman named Lily embarks on a journey to a remote coastal town, seeking solace and a fresh start. As she settles into her new surroundings, she discovers a hidden world of secrets and mysteries buried beneath the shifting sands. With each step she takes, Lily unravels the enigmatic history of the town and its inhabitants, leading her to question her own identity and purpose. As the past and present collide, she must confront her deepest fears and make choices that will shape her future. 

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Redemption, Self-discovery
