Synopsis: 
In 'The Next Time We Die' by Robert Moore Williams, a renowned scientist discovers a groundbreaking invention that allows humans to experience past lives. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of reincarnation, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel the fabric of reality itself. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal to save humanity from an unimaginable fate.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Reincarnation, Identity, Morality
