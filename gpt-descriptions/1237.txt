Synopsis: In the heart of Paris, a boarding house becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of love, ambition, and betrayal. Father Goriot, an elderly widower, resides in this house alongside a colorful cast of characters, each with their own secrets and desires. As the story unfolds, the lives of these individuals become intricately intertwined, leading to unexpected alliances and devastating consequences. Balzac's masterful storytelling delves into the depths of human nature, exploring themes of social hierarchy, greed, and the sacrifices one is willing to make for love and success.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Realism

Themes: Social hierarchy, Love, Ambition, Betrayal, Sacrifice
