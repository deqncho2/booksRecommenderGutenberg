Synopsis: In "Origin of the Anglo-Boer War Revealed: The Conspiracy of the 19th Century Unmasked," author C. H. Thomas delves into a captivating investigation that uncovers a hidden web of intrigue behind one of the most significant conflicts in South African history. Through meticulous research and compelling evidence, Thomas unveils a shocking conspiracy that challenges the conventional understanding of the Anglo-Boer War. As the layers of deception are peeled back, readers are taken on a thrilling journey that exposes the true origins of the war and the powerful forces at play. This revised edition offers new insights and revelations, shedding light on a century-old mystery that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Conspiracy theory, Military history

Themes: Power and manipulation, Historical revisionism, Nationalism, Colonialism
