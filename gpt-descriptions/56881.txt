Synopsis: In 'Forged Egyptian Antiquities' by T. G. Wakeling, a renowned archaeologist finds himself entangled in a web of deception and mystery when a series of ancient Egyptian artifacts turn out to be clever forgeries. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a dark underworld of art forgery and illicit trade, where the line between authenticity and deception becomes blurred. With his reputation on the line and his life in danger, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and expose those responsible.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Authenticity, Art forgery, Archaeology
