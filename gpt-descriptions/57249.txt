Synopsis: In 'My Queen: A Weekly Journal for Young Women. Issue 1. September 29, 1900.: From Farm to Fortune; or Only a Farmer's Daughter' by Sheldon, Lurana, we are introduced to the captivating story of a young woman's journey from a humble farm to a life of unexpected wealth and opportunity. The protagonist, a farmer's daughter, finds herself caught between the simplicity of her upbringing and the allure of a world filled with glamour and luxury. As she navigates the challenges and choices that come her way, she must grapple with the question of whether her newfound fortune can truly bring her happiness and fulfillment. This first issue sets the stage for an enthralling tale of self-discovery, love, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Social class, Ambition, Identity, Love and relationships
