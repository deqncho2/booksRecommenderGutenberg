Synopsis: 

In 'The Retreat from Mons: By one who shared in it', Arthur Corbett-Smith takes us on a gripping journey through the harrowing events of the early days of World War I. Through his vivid and personal account, we witness the chaos, fear, and camaraderie experienced by soldiers as they face the relentless German advance during the retreat from Mons. Corbett-Smith's narrative captures the intensity of the battles, the resilience of the troops, and the profound impact of war on the human spirit. 

Genres: Historical fiction, War memoir

Themes: War, Courage, Survival, Brotherhood
