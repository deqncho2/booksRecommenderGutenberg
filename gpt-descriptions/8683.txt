Synopsis: In 'The Columbiad: A Poem', Barlow takes readers on an epic journey through time and space, exploring the birth and growth of a new nation. Through vivid imagery and poetic language, he weaves together the stories of heroes, battles, and triumphs that shaped the United States. From the founding fathers to the struggles for independence, this poem captures the spirit of a nation's quest for freedom and progress.

Genres: Epic poetry, Historical fiction, National literature

Themes: Patriotism, Nation-building, Heroism, Progress, Freedom
