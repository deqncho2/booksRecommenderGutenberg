Synopsis: 
In 'Le Cocu', Paul de Kock takes us on a captivating journey through the streets of Paris, where we follow the lives of a group of colorful characters. The story revolves around the misadventures of a young man named Charles, who finds himself entangled in a web of love, deceit, and scandal. As Charles navigates the complexities of his relationships, he becomes embroiled in a series of hilarious and unexpected situations that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. With its witty dialogue, lively descriptions, and a cast of unforgettable characters, 'Le Cocu' is a delightful and entertaining tale that offers a glimpse into the vibrant world of 19th-century Paris.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Satire
Themes: Love and infidelity, Social class, Parisian society
