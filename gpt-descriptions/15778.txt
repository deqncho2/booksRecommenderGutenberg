Synopsis: In the quaint town of Fairleigh, the arrival of the Honorable Miss Evelyn St. John brings a wave of excitement and intrigue. With her old-fashioned charm and mysterious past, Miss St. John captivates the townspeople, including the dashing young doctor, Dr. Richard Mayne. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, the Honorable Miss finds herself at the center of a scandal that threatens to shake the foundations of the town. Will she be able to maintain her reputation and find true love amidst the chaos?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Reputation, Love, Secrets, Small-town dynamics
