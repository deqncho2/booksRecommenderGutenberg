Synopsis: 
In 'Tom Cringle's Log' by Michael Scott, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure set in the early 19th century. The story follows Tom Cringle, a young man who embarks on a journey as a midshipman in the Royal Navy. As he sails the treacherous waters of the Caribbean, Tom encounters pirates, battles, and unexpected alliances. Through his vivid and captivating log entries, we witness his growth from an inexperienced sailor to a courageous leader. With danger lurking at every turn, Tom must navigate not only the perils of the sea but also the complexities of loyalty and honor. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Nautical Fiction

Themes: Coming of Age, Loyalty, Honor, Survival
