Synopsis: In a small provincial town, a group of radical intellectuals becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and moral decay. As the town's inhabitants are consumed by their own desires and obsessions, a charismatic figure emerges, leading them down a path of destruction and madness. Filled with dark humor and psychological depth, 'The Possessed' explores the complexities of human nature and the consequences of unchecked ideology.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Political fiction, Satire

Themes: Ideology, Morality, Power, Madness
