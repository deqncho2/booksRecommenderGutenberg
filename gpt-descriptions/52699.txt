Synopsis: In 'The Chinese Coat' by Jennette Lee, a young American woman finds herself captivated by a mysterious antique coat she discovers in a small shop in Shanghai. As she delves deeper into the history of the coat, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue that spans generations and continents. With each clue she uncovers, she is drawn closer to a truth that could change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Cultural Heritage, Family Secrets
