Synopsis: In 'The Hazeley Family' by A.E. Johnson, secrets and scandals unravel as the Hazeley family navigates the complexities of their privileged lives. As the story unfolds, readers are drawn into a web of deceit, forbidden love, and unexpected alliances. With each chapter, the tension builds, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth behind the family's dark past and uncertain future.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family dynamics, Betrayal, Class divide, Love and desire
