Synopsis: In 'Dealings with the Dead, Volume 2 (of 2)', Sargent, Lucius M. delves deeper into the mysterious world of the supernatural, presenting a collection of chilling tales that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. From encounters with vengeful spirits to unexplained phenomena, this volume explores the haunting and eerie experiences of individuals who have crossed paths with the dead. As the stories unfold, the line between the living and the deceased becomes increasingly blurred, leading to spine-tingling revelations that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Ghosts and Spirits, Life and Death, The Unknown
