Synopsis: In "The Story of the "9th King's" in France" by Roberts, Enos Herbert Glynne, a group of soldiers from the 9th King's regiment find themselves thrust into the heart of the intense and brutal battles of World War I. As they navigate the treacherous landscapes of France, they form unbreakable bonds of camaraderie and face unimaginable challenges that test their courage and resilience. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, this book takes readers on a captivating journey through the harrowing experiences of these brave soldiers.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Survival, The horrors of war
