Synopsis:
In 'Road Scrapings: Coaches and Coaching' by Haworth, M. E., embark on a captivating journey through the world of coaching as seen through the eyes of the author. With a unique blend of personal anecdotes, insightful observations, and thought-provoking reflections, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the highs and lows, triumphs and challenges, and the transformative power of coaching. From the author's encounters with legendary coaches to his own experiences as a coach, this narrative takes readers on a captivating ride that will leave them inspired and eager to delve deeper into the art of coaching.

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Self-help

Themes: Coaching, Personal growth, Leadership, Mentorship
