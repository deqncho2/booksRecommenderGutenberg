Synopsis: 
In 'Letters of Franz Liszt -- Volume 2: from Rome to the End', the renowned composer and pianist Franz Liszt takes us on a captivating journey through his personal and artistic life. Through a collection of his intimate letters, we witness Liszt's experiences and reflections as he travels from Rome to various European cities, encountering fellow musicians, aristocrats, and even political figures along the way. From his passionate love affairs to his struggles with fame and artistic expression, Liszt's letters offer a rare glimpse into the mind of a musical genius.

Genres: Biography, Music, Letters

Themes: Artistic expression, Love and passion, Fame and celebrity, Travel and exploration
