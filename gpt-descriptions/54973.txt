Synopsis: In 'The Brighton Boys in Transatlantic Flight' by James R. Driscoll, a group of adventurous young men from Brighton embark on a daring mission to conquer the skies. As they navigate the treacherous challenges of aviation, they find themselves facing unexpected dangers and testing the limits of their courage and determination. With their dreams soaring high, the Brighton Boys must overcome obstacles that threaten to ground their ambitions and prove that they have what it takes to conquer the transatlantic flight.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Pursuit of Dreams
