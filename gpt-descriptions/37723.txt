Synopsis: 

In 'For Sceptre and Crown: A Romance of the Present Time. Vol. 1', Gregor Samarow weaves a captivating tale set in the modern world. The story follows the lives of two individuals from vastly different backgrounds, whose paths unexpectedly cross, leading them on a journey of love, ambition, and intrigue. As they navigate the complexities of power, wealth, and societal expectations, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and hidden agendas. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the characters must confront their own desires and the consequences of their actions. 

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love and passion, Ambition and power, Deception and betrayal
