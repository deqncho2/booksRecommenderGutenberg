Synopsis: In 'A Canadian Farm Mystery; Or, Pam the Pioneer', a young girl named Pam finds herself caught up in a thrilling mystery when strange occurrences begin happening on her family's farm in rural Canada. As she delves deeper into the puzzling events, Pam uncovers hidden secrets and unexpected dangers that test her courage and resourcefulness. With the help of her loyal friends, she must unravel the mystery before it's too late. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth alongside Pam.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Discovery
