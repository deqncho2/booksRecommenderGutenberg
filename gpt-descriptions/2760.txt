Synopsis: 
In 'Celebrated Crimes (Complete)', Alexandre Dumas takes readers on a thrilling journey through history, exploring some of the most notorious crimes and criminals of the past. From the dark secrets of the Borgias to the treacherous deeds of the Marquise de Brinvilliers, Dumas weaves together a collection of captivating tales that delve into the minds of those who committed these heinous acts. With his signature storytelling prowess, Dumas brings to life the intricate details of each crime, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind these celebrated crimes.

Genres: Historical fiction, True crime, Mystery

Themes: Crime and punishment, Power and corruption, Morality and justice
