Synopsis: 

In "Tutankhamen and the Discovery of His Tomb," the Late Earl of Carnarvon and Mr. Howard Carter take us on an extraordinary journey into the heart of ancient Egypt. Set against the backdrop of the early 20th century, this captivating account unveils the thrilling tale of the duo's relentless pursuit to uncover the secrets hidden within the tomb of the legendary pharaoh Tutankhamen. As they navigate treacherous landscapes, face unexpected challenges, and unravel the mysteries of the past, the reader is transported into a world of intrigue, adventure, and awe-inspiring discoveries.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Archaeology, Adventure

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Exploration, Legacy, Obsession
