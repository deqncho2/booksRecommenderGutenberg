Synopsis: In 'Dross' by Henry Seton Merriman, a young man named Richard Larchester finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a secret society with dangerous intentions. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of society, Richard must navigate treacherous alliances and face unexpected betrayals, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic organization. With his life on the line, Richard must make difficult choices that will test his courage and integrity, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Loyalty and betrayal, Courage and integrity
