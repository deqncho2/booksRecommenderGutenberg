Synopsis: 
In 'Fran' by Ellis, J. Breckenridge, a young woman named Fran finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter that leads her on a thrilling journey. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Fran discovers shocking truths about her own identity and the people she thought she knew. With danger lurking around every corner, Fran must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those she loves.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal
