Synopsis: 

'Danger! A True History of a Great City's Wiles and Temptations: The Veil Lifted, and Light Thrown on Crime and its Causes, and Criminals and their Haunts. Facts and Disclosures' takes readers on a captivating journey through the dark underbelly of a bustling city. Written by William F. Howe and Abraham H. Hummel, this gripping work unveils the hidden truths behind crime, delving into the causes and consequences that plague society. 

Through a series of shocking revelations and eye-opening anecdotes, the authors expose the intricate web of deceit, temptation, and corruption that permeates the city's streets. From the shadowy haunts of criminals to the manipulative tactics employed by those in power, 'Danger!' offers a riveting exploration of the city's most dangerous elements.

As readers delve deeper into the narrative, they are confronted with the harsh realities of crime and its impact on both individuals and society as a whole. The authors shed light on the underlying causes that drive individuals to a life of crime, challenging preconceived notions and offering a fresh perspective on the complex issue.

Genres: True Crime, Non-fiction, Historical

Themes: Crime and its causes, Corruption, Temptation, Societal impact
