Synopsis: In 'Are these Things So? (1740) The Great Man's Answer to Are These things So: (1740)', James Miller takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a mysterious and enigmatic figure. Set in the backdrop of the 18th century, this gripping tale unravels the secrets and complexities surrounding the protagonist, leaving readers questioning the nature of truth and the power of perception. As the story unfolds, unexpected twists and turns keep readers on the edge of their seats, delving into the depths of human nature and the consequences of one's actions. With its rich historical setting and thought-provoking narrative, this book is sure to captivate readers from start to finish.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Psychological thriller

Themes: Truth and perception, Identity, Morality, Consequences of actions
