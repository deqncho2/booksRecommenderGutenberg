Synopsis: 
'Behind the Veil at the Russian Court' takes readers on a captivating journey through the opulent and mysterious world of the Russian court. Written by Catherine Radziwill, a princess with unparalleled access to the inner workings of the court, this book offers a rare glimpse into the lives of the Russian elite. From scandalous affairs to political intrigue, Radziwill's firsthand accounts paint a vivid picture of the hidden truths and secrets that lie behind the grandeur of the court. As the reader delves deeper into this captivating narrative, they will be left questioning the true nature of power and the sacrifices made to maintain it.

Genres: Biography, History, Memoir

Themes: Power, Intrigue, Secrecy, Society
