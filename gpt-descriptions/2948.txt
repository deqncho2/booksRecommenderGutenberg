Synopsis: In the small town of Monteriano, a young widow named Lilia Herriton embarks on a journey to Italy, accompanied by her unconventional sister-in-law, Caroline Abbott. As they immerse themselves in the vibrant Italian culture, they encounter a passionate young man named Gino, who becomes entangled in their lives. What unfolds is a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and the clash between English propriety and Italian passion.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Cultural clash, Love and desire, Social conventions
