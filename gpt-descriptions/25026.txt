Synopsis: In 'Bristol Bells: A Story of the Eighteenth Century' by Emma Marshall, readers are transported to the bustling city of Bristol in the 18th century. The story follows the lives of two young women, Lucy and Emily, who come from different backgrounds but share a deep bond of friendship. As they navigate the challenges and opportunities of their time, their lives become intertwined with the vibrant social scene, the struggles of the working class, and the secrets that lie within the city's historic bells. With its rich historical backdrop and captivating characters, 'Bristol Bells' is a tale of friendship, love, and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Social Class, Secrets, Ambition
