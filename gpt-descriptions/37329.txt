Synopsis: In 'The Domestic Cat' by Gordon Stables, embark on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of our beloved feline companions. From their ancient origins to their modern-day roles as both cherished pets and enigmatic creatures, this book delves into the captivating history, behavior, and unique characteristics of domestic cats. With fascinating anecdotes and captivating insights, Stables explores the intricate bond between humans and cats, revealing the secrets behind their captivating allure and their enduring place in our hearts.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Animal Studies

Themes: Animal-human relationship, Evolution, Behavior
