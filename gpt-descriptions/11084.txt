Synopsis: In "Sonny, a Christmas Guest," a young boy named Sonny unexpectedly arrives at the doorstep of a family on Christmas Eve, bringing with him a mysterious past and a heartwarming message. As the family welcomes Sonny into their home, they embark on a journey of love, forgiveness, and the true meaning of Christmas.

Genres: Fiction, Holiday, Family

Themes: Love, Forgiveness, Redemption, Generosity, Christmas Spirit
