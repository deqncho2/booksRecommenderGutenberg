Synopsis: 
In Joseph Conrad's "The Secret Sharer," a young captain finds himself facing a moral dilemma when he encounters a mysterious stranger who seeks refuge on his ship. As the captain navigates the treacherous waters of his own conscience, he must grapple with the consequences of his actions and the boundaries of loyalty and identity. With tension mounting and secrets unraveling, this gripping tale explores the depths of human nature and the complexities of personal integrity.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Adventure fiction, Maritime fiction

Themes: Identity, Morality, Loyalty, Isolation
