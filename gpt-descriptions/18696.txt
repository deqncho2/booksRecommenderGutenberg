Synopsis: 
Step back in time and explore the literary curiosities of Boston and Salem, Massachusetts through the pages of old newspapers. In this captivating volume, Henry M. Brooks delves into the forgotten stories, anecdotes, and peculiarities that once graced the headlines of these historic cities. From tales of eccentric characters to bizarre events and forgotten customs, Literary Curiosities offers a fascinating glimpse into the past, unearthing hidden gems that will leave readers both entertained and enlightened.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Journalism, Local History

Themes: History, Culture, Journalism, Curiosity
