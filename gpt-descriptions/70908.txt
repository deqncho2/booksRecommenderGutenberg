Synopsis: In 'The Undertakers' Manual' by Auguste Renouard, a young apprentice embarks on a journey into the mysterious world of funeral directors. As he delves deeper into the profession, he uncovers dark secrets and encounters peculiar characters that challenge his perception of life and death. With each funeral he assists, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and must navigate through a labyrinth of emotions and moral dilemmas. This gripping tale explores the hidden side of death and the profound impact it has on the living.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Mortality, Identity, Morality
