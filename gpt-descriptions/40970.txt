Synopsis: In 'Exploiter's End' by James Causey, a brilliant hacker named Alex finds himself caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse when he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens to destabilize the world. As he races against time to expose the truth, Alex must navigate a treacherous web of deception, betrayal, and high-stakes technology. With his life on the line, he must outsmart his adversaries and confront the dark forces that seek to exploit the vulnerabilities of the modern world. 

Genres: Techno-thriller, Cyberpunk, Suspense

Themes: Technology, Power, Morality, Redemption
