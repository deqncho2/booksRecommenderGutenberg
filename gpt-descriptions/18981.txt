Synopsis: In 'Dick the Bank Boy; Or, A Missing Fortune' by Frank V. Webster, readers are introduced to Dick, a young and ambitious bank employee who finds himself caught up in a thrilling mystery. When a large sum of money goes missing from the bank, Dick becomes determined to uncover the truth and clear his name. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he encounters unexpected twists and turns, leading him on a suspenseful journey filled with danger and intrigue. Will Dick be able to solve the mystery and prove his innocence? Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense Themes: Crime and Investigation, Trust and Betrayal
