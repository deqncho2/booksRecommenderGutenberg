Synopsis: 
'Anecdotes of Painters, Engravers, Sculptors and Architects, and Curiosities of Art (Vol. 1 of 3)' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and works of renowned artists, shedding light on their fascinating anecdotes and the curious aspects of their craft. From the eccentricities of famous painters to the secrets behind iconic sculptures, this volume offers a delightful exploration of the art world's hidden gems. With vivid storytelling and intriguing details, Spooner, Shearjashub invites readers to discover the untold stories behind some of history's most celebrated artistic masterpieces.

Genres: Biography, Art History, Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic eccentricities, Creative process, Historical context
