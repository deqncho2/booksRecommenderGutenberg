Synopsis: In 'Broken to Harness: A Story of English Domestic Life' by Edmund Yates, we are introduced to the captivating world of Victorian England, where the lives of two families become intricately intertwined. As secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances unfold, the reader is taken on a journey through the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. With a cast of compelling characters and a plot filled with twists and turns, this novel explores the delicate balance between duty and desire, and the lengths one will go to protect their reputation and secure their future.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Social Class and Expectations, Family Dynamics, Ambition and Sacrifice
