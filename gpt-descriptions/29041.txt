Synopsis: In 'The Education of Eric Lane' by Stephen McKenna, we follow the journey of Eric Lane, a young man who embarks on a quest for knowledge and self-discovery. Set in the early 20th century, Eric's pursuit takes him from the hallowed halls of Oxford to the vibrant streets of Paris, where he encounters a colorful cast of characters and experiences that challenge his beliefs and shape his destiny. As Eric navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and intellectual growth, he must confront the choices that will define his future and ultimately find his place in the world.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Education, Self-discovery, Love and Relationships, Identity
