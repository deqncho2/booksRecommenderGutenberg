Synopsis: 
'Wanderings in North Africa' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the captivating landscapes and vibrant cultures of North Africa. Join James Hamilton, an intrepid traveler, as he embarks on a series of thrilling adventures, encountering mysterious ruins, bustling markets, and fascinating locals along the way. From the bustling streets of Marrakech to the vast Sahara Desert, Hamilton's vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling will transport readers to a world filled with wonder and intrigue.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Discovery
