Synopsis: In 'Hidden Water' by Coolidge Dane, a young woman named Lily embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's mysterious past. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within her ancestral home, she becomes entangled in a web of deception, betrayal, and long-buried desires. With each revelation, Lily's perception of her own identity is challenged, leading her on a thrilling and dangerous path towards self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Betrayal, Self-discovery
