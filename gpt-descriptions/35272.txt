Synopsis: 
'A Catalogue of Books in English Later than 1700, Vol. 1: Forming a portion of the library of Robert Hoe' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vast collection of books owned by Robert Hoe. This meticulously curated catalogue showcases a diverse range of English books published after 1700, offering a glimpse into the literary treasures that have shaped the world of literature. From rare editions to notable works, this volume presents a fascinating exploration of the written word, inviting readers to discover hidden gems and delve into the rich tapestry of English literature.

Genres: Non-fiction, Bibliography, Literary History

Themes: Literature, Book Collecting, Cultural Influence
