Synopsis: In "The Temptation of St. Anthony," Gustave Flaubert takes us on a mesmerizing journey into the tormented mind of the legendary saint. Set in the desolate Egyptian desert, the story follows St. Anthony as he battles against a relentless onslaught of temptations, both physical and spiritual. As he grapples with his inner demons, the boundaries between reality and illusion blur, leading to a surreal exploration of the human psyche. Through vivid and haunting imagery, Flaubert delves into the depths of human desires, fears, and the eternal struggle between good and evil.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Historical fiction, Surrealism

Themes: Temptation, Religion, Madness, Human nature
