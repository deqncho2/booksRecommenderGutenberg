Synopsis: In 'Weymouth New Testament in Modern Speech, 1 John', Richard Francis Weymouth presents a contemporary translation of the biblical book of 1 John. This powerful and thought-provoking work delves into the themes of love, faith, and the importance of living a righteous life. Through his modern language and vivid descriptions, Weymouth brings to life the timeless teachings of this ancient text, inviting readers to explore the depths of their own spirituality and discover the true meaning of Christian love.

Genres: Religious literature, Biblical translation

Themes: Love, Faith, Righteousness
