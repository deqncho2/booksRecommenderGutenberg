Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Trebarwith, a mysterious group known as "The Wreckers" has been causing havoc for years, luring ships to their doom and plundering the wreckage. When a young sailor named John Penrose arrives in town, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and danger as he uncovers the truth behind the treacherous activities of The Wreckers. With his life on the line, John must navigate the treacherous waters of betrayal and deceit to bring justice to the town and put an end to The Wreckers' reign of terror.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Justice, Survival
