Synopsis: In "Our People: From the Collection of 'Mr. Punch'," Keene, Charles takes us on a captivating journey through a collection of vivid and eccentric characters. From mischievous jesters to noble knights, from cunning witches to lovable rogues, this anthology of illustrations brings to life a diverse cast of individuals. Each page unveils a new personality, inviting readers to delve into the whimsical and sometimes dark world of "Mr. Punch." With intricate details and a touch of humor, Keene's artwork captures the essence of these captivating figures, leaving readers eager to discover the stories behind each character.

Genres: Art, Illustration, Anthology

Themes: Character exploration, Whimsy, Diversity
