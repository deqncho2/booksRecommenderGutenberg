Synopsis: In the midst of the English Civil War, the Beverley children find themselves orphaned and forced to flee their ancestral home. Seeking refuge in the New Forest, they must adapt to a life of survival and secrecy. As they navigate the challenges of their new surroundings, they uncover hidden secrets and form unexpected alliances. With danger lurking at every turn, the children must rely on their resilience and resourcefulness to protect their family and find a way to reclaim their rightful place in society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Resilience, Family, Loyalty, Survival
