Synopsis:
'The Power of Sympathy: or, The Triumph of Nature. Founded in Truth.' is a captivating novel that delves into the complexities of human emotions and the power of empathy. Set in 18th-century New England, the story follows the lives of two young individuals, Harriot and Harrington, who find themselves entangled in a web of forbidden desires and societal expectations. As their lives intertwine, secrets are revealed, and the consequences of their actions become increasingly dire. This gripping tale explores the depths of human nature, the consequences of societal constraints, and the transformative power of sympathy.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Forbidden love, societal expectations, human nature, empathy
