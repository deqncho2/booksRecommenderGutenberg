Synopsis: In 'Afloat and Ashore: A Sea Tale' by James Fenimore Cooper, we embark on a thrilling maritime adventure with Miles Wallingford, a young man who sets sail on a voyage that will test his courage, resilience, and loyalty. From treacherous storms to encounters with pirates, Miles must navigate the perils of the sea while also grappling with matters of the heart. As he faces the challenges of life at sea, Miles discovers the true meaning of honor, friendship, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Coming of Age, Survival, Love and Sacrifice
