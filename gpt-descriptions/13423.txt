Synopsis: In 'Zarlah the Martian' by R. Norman Grisewood, a mysterious Martian arrives on Earth with a mission that could change the fate of both planets. As he navigates the complexities of human society, he encounters unexpected allies and formidable adversaries, all while trying to fulfill his purpose. With a blend of adventure, intrigue, and thought-provoking ideas, this captivating tale takes readers on an interplanetary journey that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Extraterrestrial life, Identity, Exploration
