Synopsis: In "Same old Bill, eh Mable!" we follow the hilarious misadventures of Bill, a young soldier stationed overseas during World War I, as he navigates the challenges of military life and tries to maintain a long-distance relationship with his beloved Mable. From comical encounters with fellow soldiers to unexpected encounters with enemy forces, Bill's journey is filled with laughter, heartwarming moments, and a touch of romance. Will Bill and Mable's love withstand the trials of war? Find out in this delightful and entertaining tale.

Genres: Humor, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and relationships, Friendship, Resilience, War
