Synopsis: 
In 'Frank Allen at Old Moose Lake; or, The trail in the snow', Frank Allen finds himself in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a mysterious trail in the snow during a winter vacation at Old Moose Lake. As he follows the trail, Frank uncovers a series of clues that lead him deeper into a hidden world of secrets and danger. With each step, the stakes rise, and Frank must rely on his wit and courage to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Bravery, Friendship, Discovery
