Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Mount Music, a mysterious boarding school nestled in the Scottish Highlands, a group of talented young musicians embark on a journey that will challenge their skills, friendships, and even their perception of reality. As they delve into the secrets of the school and its eccentric staff, they uncover a hidden power that could change their lives forever. With music as their guide, they must navigate through a web of intrigue, magic, and self-discovery, all while facing the ultimate test of their musical abilities. Will they find harmony or discord in their quest for truth?

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Music, Friendship, Identity, Magic, Coming-of-age
