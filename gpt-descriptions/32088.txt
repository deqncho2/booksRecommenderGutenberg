Synopsis: In 'Instant of Decision' by Randall Garrett, a brilliant scientist invents a device that can predict the future with astonishing accuracy. However, when he discovers a shocking event that will change the course of history, he faces a moral dilemma that could have dire consequences. As he grapples with the weight of his discovery, he must navigate a treacherous web of power, secrecy, and personal sacrifice. This gripping tale of suspense and ethical quandaries will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Consequences
