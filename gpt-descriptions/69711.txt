Synopsis: In 'The Star Dreamer: A Romance' by Castle, Egerton and Castle, Agnes, a young woman named Amelia finds herself captivated by the enigmatic and charming Vincent, a renowned astronomer. As their paths intertwine, Amelia is drawn into a world of celestial wonders and forbidden love. Together, they embark on a journey that challenges societal norms and tests the boundaries of their own desires. Amidst the backdrop of starry nights and whispered secrets, Amelia must navigate the complexities of love, passion, and sacrifice, ultimately discovering the true meaning of following one's dreams.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Forbidden love, Pursuit of dreams, Society's expectations
