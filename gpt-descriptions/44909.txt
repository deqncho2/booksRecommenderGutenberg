Synopsis: In 'The New Forest' by Elizabeth Godfrey, a young woman named Lucy finds herself drawn to the enchanting beauty of the New Forest, a mysterious and ancient woodland. As she explores its depths, she uncovers a hidden world filled with secrets, mythical creatures, and unexpected friendships. But as Lucy delves deeper into the forest's secrets, she soon realizes that there is a dark force at play, threatening to destroy everything she holds dear. With the help of her newfound allies, Lucy must embark on a perilous journey to save the forest and uncover her own destiny.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Nature and its connection to the human spirit, the power of friendship, the battle between good and evil
