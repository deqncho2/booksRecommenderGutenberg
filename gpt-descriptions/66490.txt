Synopsis: 
In 'The Collected Works of Ambrose Bierce, Volume 09: Tangential Views', Ambrose Bierce takes readers on a captivating journey through his unique and thought-provoking perspectives. This collection of essays, satirical pieces, and short stories showcases Bierce's sharp wit, biting humor, and incisive observations on a wide range of subjects. From politics and society to human nature and the absurdities of everyday life, Bierce's tangential views offer a refreshing and often controversial take on the world around us. With his signature style, he challenges conventional wisdom and invites readers to question their own beliefs, leaving them both entertained and intellectually stimulated.

Genres: Satire, Essay, Short Story

Themes: Social commentary, Human nature, Satire
