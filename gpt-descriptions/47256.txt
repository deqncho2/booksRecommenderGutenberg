Synopsis: 
In 'Days in the Open' by Lathan A. Crandall, a young artist named Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure as she leaves behind her comfortable life in the city to spend a summer in a remote mountain town. As she immerses herself in the breathtaking landscapes and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Emily's perspective on life and art is transformed. With each passing day, she uncovers hidden truths about herself and the world around her, leading her to question the boundaries of creativity and the power of human connection. 

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Adventure, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Nature, Artistic expression, Human connection
