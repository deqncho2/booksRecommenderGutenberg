Synopsis: In "The Pleasures of a Single Life, Or, The Miseries of Matrimony," the author explores the contrasting experiences of single life and married life, shedding light on the recent divorces granted by Parliament. With a touch of satire and wit, the book also delves into the joys of a country-life, offering a dedicated message to the fashionable beaus. 

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Social Commentary

Themes: Marriage, Divorce, Single Life, Country Living, Satire
