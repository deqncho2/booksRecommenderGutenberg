Synopsis: Owen Clancy's Run of Luck; or, The Motor Wizard in the Garage follows the thrilling adventures of Owen Clancy, a young mechanic with an extraordinary talent for fixing cars. When a mysterious stranger arrives in town with a high-stakes challenge, Owen's skills are put to the ultimate test. As he delves deeper into the world of fast cars and dangerous secrets, Owen must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will his run of luck continue, or will he find himself in over his head?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Skill and talent, Risk and reward, Secrets and deception
