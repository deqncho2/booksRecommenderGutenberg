Synopsis: 

'With an Ambulance During the Franco-German War: Personal Experiences and Adventures with Both Armies, 1870-1871' by Charles Edward Ryan takes readers on a gripping journey through the Franco-German War, offering a unique perspective from the frontlines of the conflict. The author, serving as an ambulance worker, recounts his personal experiences and thrilling adventures while tending to the wounded soldiers from both sides of the war. From the chaos of the battlefield to the harrowing moments of saving lives, Ryan's narrative provides a vivid and intimate portrayal of the war's impact on individuals and the human spirit.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War memoir

Themes: War and its consequences, Human resilience and compassion
