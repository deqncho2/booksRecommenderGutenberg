Synopsis: In 'The Brand of Silence: A Detective Story', a mysterious crime syndicate known as The Black Hundred is wreaking havoc in the city, leaving a trail of violence and fear in its wake. When a prominent businessman is found dead under suspicious circumstances, Detective Jimmie Dale is determined to uncover the truth behind the organization and bring them to justice. As he delves deeper into the dangerous underworld, Dale must navigate a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden identities, all while trying to protect his own secrets. With each step closer to the truth, the stakes rise higher, and Dale must rely on his wit, courage, and the brand of silence to survive.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Crime and justice, Identity and secrecy
