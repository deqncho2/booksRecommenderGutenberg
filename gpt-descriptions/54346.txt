Synopsis: In 'Queens of the Renaissance', author Ryley M. Beresford takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of remarkable women who defied societal norms and left an indelible mark on history. From the powerful and influential queens of Europe to the lesser-known but equally fascinating figures, this book unveils the untold stories of these extraordinary women who shaped the Renaissance era. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Beresford brings to life the triumphs, struggles, and enduring legacies of these queens, offering a fresh perspective on a pivotal period in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Women's history

Themes: Female empowerment, Renaissance culture, Political intrigue, Gender roles, Legacy
