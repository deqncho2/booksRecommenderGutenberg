Synopsis: In 'Shameless Wayne: A Romance of the last Feud of Wayne and Ratcliffe', the reader is transported to the rugged and lawless American West, where two notorious families, the Waynes and the Ratcliffes, are locked in a bitter and deadly feud. Amidst the chaos and violence, a forbidden love blossoms between the headstrong and fearless Wayne daughter and the enigmatic and dangerous Ratcliffe son. As their love defies the boundaries of loyalty and tradition, they must navigate treacherous terrain, facing betrayal, revenge, and the ever-present threat of death. Will their love conquer all, or will it be consumed by the flames of their feuding families?

Genres: Western, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Family feuds, Loyalty, Revenge
