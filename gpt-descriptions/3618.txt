Synopsis:
In "Arms and the Man," a young woman named Raina finds herself torn between two suitors: the dashing war hero Sergius and the pragmatic and unconventional Captain Bluntschli. Set during the Serbo-Bulgarian War, this witty and satirical play explores the absurdity of romanticized notions of love and war. As the characters navigate their desires and societal expectations, unexpected alliances and revelations emerge, challenging their preconceived notions of heroism and true love.

Genres:
Comedy, Satire, Romance

Themes:
Idealism vs. Realism, Love and Marriage, Class and Social Expectations
