Synopsis: In 'Nameless River' by Vingie E. Roe, a young woman named Ruth embarks on a perilous journey through the untamed wilderness of Alaska in search of her missing brother. As she navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters mysterious characters, Ruth must confront her own fears and discover the true meaning of family. With each step closer to her brother, she unravels a web of secrets that will change her life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Family, Self-discovery
