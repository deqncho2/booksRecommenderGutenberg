Synopsis:
In the dark and treacherous world of Renaissance Italy, a young and defiant duchess defies the expectations of her society by secretly marrying her steward. As her powerful and malevolent brothers seek to control her every move, the duchess must navigate a web of deceit, betrayal, and violence in order to protect her love and secure her own freedom. Filled with passion, intrigue, and a haunting sense of impending doom, 'The Duchess of Malfi' is a gripping tale of love, power, and the lengths one woman will go to defy the constraints of her time.

Genres:
Tragedy, Drama, Revenge

Themes:
Love and Desire, Power and Control, Gender and Identity
