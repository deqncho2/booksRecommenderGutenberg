Synopsis: 
In 'The Radio Boys at Ocean Point; Or, The Message that Saved the Ship' by Chapman, Allen, a group of adventurous young radio enthusiasts find themselves caught in a thrilling mystery while on vacation at Ocean Point. When they intercept a mysterious distress signal, the boys embark on a dangerous mission to uncover the truth behind it. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter suspicious characters, they must rely on their wit, courage, and radio skills to save the day. Will their message be the key to saving a ship in peril? Join the Radio Boys on an exhilarating journey filled with suspense, friendship, and the power of communication.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Communication
