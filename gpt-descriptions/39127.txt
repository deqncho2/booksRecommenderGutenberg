Synopsis: 

In the 11th edition of the renowned Encyclopaedia Britannica, Volume 13, Slice 6 takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and accomplishments of notable individuals, from the enigmatic Daniel Home to the influential Quintus Hortensius. This volume delves into the fascinating stories and contributions of these historical figures, offering a glimpse into their unique experiences and the impact they had on their respective fields. From the mysterious world of spiritualism to the political landscape of ancient Rome, this volume is sure to captivate readers with its rich tapestry of knowledge and intrigue.

Genres: Reference, Biography, History

Themes: Spirituality, Politics, Influence
