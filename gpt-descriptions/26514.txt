Synopsis: In 'The Iron Pirate: A Plain Tale of Strange Happenings on the Sea' by Pemberton, Max, a young sailor named Jack finds himself aboard a mysterious ship with a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the ship's history, he uncovers a web of intrigue, danger, and supernatural occurrences that threaten to consume him and his fellow crewmates. With each passing day, Jack must navigate treacherous waters, both literal and metaphorical, as he races against time to unravel the truth and save himself from a fate worse than death.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Exploration, Betrayal, Redemption, the Power of Knowledge
