Synopsis: 
In "Hildegarde's Holiday: A Story for Girls," young Hildegarde finds herself embarking on a delightful adventure during her summer vacation. Leaving behind the confines of her city life, she travels to a charming seaside town where she encounters new friends, explores the beauty of nature, and discovers the joy of independence. As Hildegarde immerses herself in the wonders of her holiday, she learns valuable lessons about self-discovery, friendship, and the importance of embracing new experiences.

Genres: Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Embracing new experiences
