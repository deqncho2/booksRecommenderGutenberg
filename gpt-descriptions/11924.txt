Synopsis: In 'The Loves of Krishna in Indian Painting and Poetry', Archer delves into the captivating world of Indian art and literature to explore the enchanting tales of Krishna's romantic escapades. From his playful encounters with the cowherd maidens to his passionate love for Radha, the book unveils the rich tapestry of Krishna's amorous adventures through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations. As Archer unravels the intricate symbolism and poetic nuances, readers are transported to a realm where divine love and human desire intertwine, leaving them spellbound by the timeless allure of Krishna's loves.

Genres: Art, Poetry, Cultural Studies

Themes: Love and Desire, Mythology and Religion, Symbolism
