Synopsis: In 'Santa Claus's Partner' by Page, Thomas Nelson, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden world of magic and adventure when she becomes Santa Claus's unexpected partner. As they embark on a thrilling journey to save Christmas, Lily learns the true meaning of friendship, courage, and the power of believing. With enchanting characters and heartwarming moments, this story will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Christmas spirit, Friendship, Belief, Courage
