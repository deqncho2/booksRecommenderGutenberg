Synopsis: In 'The S.S. Glory' by Frederick Niven, a mysterious ship sets sail on a perilous journey across the treacherous seas, carrying a diverse group of passengers with their own hidden agendas. As the ship battles against the forces of nature and encounters unexpected challenges, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful climax. Will the passengers survive the dangers that lie ahead, or will they succumb to the mysteries of the deep?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Redemption
