Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Norway, a woman named Ellida struggles with her inner desires and the constraints of her marriage. As the mysterious stranger from her past resurfaces, Ellida is faced with a life-altering decision that could either set her free or bind her forever. With elements of romance, mystery, and psychological drama, "The Lady from the Sea" takes readers on a captivating journey of self-discovery and the complexities of human relationships.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Freedom and personal fulfillment, the conflict between duty and desire, the power of the past on the present
