Synopsis: 
In the roaring 1920s, Flaming Youth takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of a group of young adults navigating the exhilarating and often scandalous world of post-World War I America. As they embrace newfound freedoms and challenge societal norms, their lives intertwine in a whirlwind of love, ambition, and self-discovery. From glamorous parties to secret affairs, this gripping tale explores the complexities of youth, the pursuit of happiness, and the consequences of living life on the edge.

Genres: 
Drama, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Youth rebellion, societal expectations, love and desire, self-discovery
