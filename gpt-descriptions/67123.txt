Synopsis: 

'Show Boat' by Edna Ferber is a captivating novel that takes readers on a journey through the lives of the performers and crew aboard the Cotton Blossom, a Mississippi River show boat. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th and early 20th centuries, this story delves into the complexities of love, race, and societal norms. As the characters navigate their personal struggles and dreams, the Cotton Blossom becomes a microcosm of a changing America, challenging the status quo and pushing the boundaries of acceptance. With a rich tapestry of characters and a compelling narrative, 'Show Boat' offers a glimpse into the world of show business and the profound impact it has on the lives it touches.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Racism, Love, Social change, Identity, Ambition
