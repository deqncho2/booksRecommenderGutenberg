Synopsis: In 'Prevailing Prayer: What Hinders It?' Dwight Lyman Moody delves into the power and potential of prayer, exploring the obstacles that often hinder its effectiveness. Through personal anecdotes and biblical teachings, Moody uncovers the secrets to unlocking the full potential of prayer, revealing how it can transform lives and bring about remarkable change. With his characteristic passion and wisdom, Moody invites readers on a journey to discover the true power of prayer and how to overcome the hindrances that stand in its way.

Genres: Christian literature, spiritual growth, self-help

Themes: Prayer, faith, spiritual warfare, overcoming obstacles
