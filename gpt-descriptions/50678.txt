Synopsis: In 'Snug Harbor; or, The Champlain Mechanics' by Optic, Oliver, a group of young mechanics embark on a thrilling adventure that takes them to the picturesque shores of Lake Champlain. As they navigate the challenges of their journey, they encounter mysterious characters, uncover hidden secrets, and find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue. With danger lurking at every turn, the mechanics must rely on their wit, courage, and teamwork to unravel the mysteries that surround Snug Harbor.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Self-discovery
