Synopsis: 
In 'The Sandman's Hour: Stories for Bedtime', Abbie Phillips Walker weaves a collection of enchanting tales that transport readers to a world of imagination and wonder. From mischievous fairies to talking animals, each story is filled with delightful characters and unexpected twists that will captivate both young and old. Whether it's a magical adventure or a heartwarming tale, these stories are perfect for bedtime reading, leaving readers with a sense of joy and anticipation for the dreams that await them. 

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Fantasy, Short Stories

Themes: 
Imagination, Wonder, Bedtime, Dreams
