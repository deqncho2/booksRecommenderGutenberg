Synopsis: 
In "Large Fees and How to Get Them: A book for the private use of physicians" by Albert V. Harmon, readers are taken on a captivating journey into the world of medical professionals seeking to maximize their earnings. Through a series of insightful anecdotes and practical advice, Harmon delves into the strategies and mindset necessary for physicians to secure substantial fees for their services. From negotiating with patients to leveraging one's expertise, this book offers a unique perspective on the business side of medicine, shedding light on the untapped potential for financial success within the field. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Business, Self-help

Themes: Financial success, Negotiation strategies, Professional development
