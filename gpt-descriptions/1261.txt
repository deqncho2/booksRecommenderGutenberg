Synopsis: In the midst of the American Revolution, Betty Zane finds herself caught in a dangerous and thrilling adventure. As the settlers of Fort Henry face the threat of Native American attacks, Betty's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. With her unwavering spirit and a secret that could change everything, Betty becomes a symbol of hope and resilience in a time of chaos and uncertainty.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Courage, Survival, Love, Patriotism
