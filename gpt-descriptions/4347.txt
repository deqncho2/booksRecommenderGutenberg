Synopsis: In 'My Young Alcides: A Faded Photograph' by Charlotte M. Yonge, a mysterious photograph sets off a chain of events that unravels a family's hidden secrets. As the protagonist delves into the past, she uncovers a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and redemption. With each revelation, the lines between reality and fantasy blur, leaving the reader questioning the true nature of the photograph and its significance. This gripping narrative takes readers on a journey through time, exploring the power of memory and the enduring impact of the past.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Secrets and Lies, Identity, Memory and Perception
