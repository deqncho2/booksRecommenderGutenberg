Synopsis: In "The Eyes Have It" by McKimmey, James, a young woman named Lily discovers a mysterious pair of glasses that allow her to see things that others cannot. As she delves deeper into this newfound ability, she uncovers a hidden world filled with secrets and danger. With each glimpse through the lenses, Lily becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that could change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Perception, Identity, Power
