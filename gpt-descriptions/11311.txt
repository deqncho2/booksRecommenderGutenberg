Synopsis: In 'The Masters of the Peaks: A Story of the Great North Woods', Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of the Great North Woods. Set in the late 18th century, the story follows a young frontiersman named Robert Lennox as he navigates the treacherous terrain, encounters Native American tribes, and faces the challenges of survival in the harsh wilderness. As Lennox embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets of the mysterious peaks, he becomes entangled in a web of danger, adventure, and unexpected alliances. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Altsheler paints a captivating portrait of the untamed frontier and the individuals who dare to conquer it.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Wilderness

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Native American culture, Frontier life
