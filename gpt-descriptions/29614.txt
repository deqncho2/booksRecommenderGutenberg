Synopsis: In "The Game of Rat and Dragon," humanity is engaged in a fierce battle against invisible creatures that lurk in the depths of space. These creatures, known as "dragons," attack spaceships and their crews, causing immense destruction. To combat this threat, specially trained telepaths called "cats" are paired with human pilots to form a unique and deadly team. Together, they enter the dangerous game of rat and dragon, fighting to protect humanity from the unseen enemy.

Genres: Science Fiction, Space Opera, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Warfare, Telepathy, Human-Animal Bond, Sacrifice
