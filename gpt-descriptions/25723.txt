Synopsis: 
In "The Voyage of the Oregon from San Francisco to Santiago in 1898," author R. Cross takes readers on a thrilling journey aboard the USS Oregon during the Spanish-American War. As tensions rise and the crew faces numerous challenges, including treacherous waters and enemy encounters, the fate of the ship and its crew hangs in the balance. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Cross paints a vivid picture of the dangers and triumphs experienced during this historic voyage.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Patriotism, Camaraderie, Survival
