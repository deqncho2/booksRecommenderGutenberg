Synopsis: In the remote Appalachian Mountains, a young girl named Mary grows up in isolation, surrounded by the beauty and harshness of nature. As she navigates the challenges of her unconventional upbringing, Mary's life takes an unexpected turn when she encounters a mysterious stranger who awakens her curiosity about the world beyond her mountain home. With her determination and resilience, Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery, facing both the wonders and dangers that await her beyond the familiar peaks. 

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Isolation, Nature, Self-discovery
