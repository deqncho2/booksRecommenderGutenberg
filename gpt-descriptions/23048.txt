Synopsis: 
In 'Adrift in a Boat' by William Henry Giles Kingston, a group of young adventurers find themselves in a perilous situation when their boat is swept away by a powerful current. Stranded in the vast ocean, they must navigate treacherous waters, face unexpected challenges, and rely on their resourcefulness to survive. As they struggle to find their way back home, they discover the true strength of friendship and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Survival
