Synopsis: In 'Physiology of the Opera' by Scrici, readers are taken on a captivating journey behind the scenes of the opera world. Through the eyes of a passionate opera enthusiast, the story unravels the intricate web of relationships, rivalries, and secrets that exist within the opera house. As the protagonist delves deeper into this captivating world, they discover the power of music, the complexities of human emotions, and the sacrifices made in the pursuit of artistic perfection. With each turn of the page, readers will be enthralled by the drama, romance, and intrigue that unfolds within the grandeur of the opera house.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Passion for art, Human emotions, Pursuit of perfection
