Synopsis: 
'History of the Fifty-Seventh Regiment, Pennsylvania Veteran Volunteer Infantry: First Brigade, First Division, Third Corps and Second Brigade, Third Division, Second Corps, Army of the Potomac' takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of the soldiers who fought in the American Civil War. This meticulously researched and vividly written account delves into the battles, hardships, and triumphs of the Fifty-Seventh Regiment, offering a unique perspective on the war that shaped a nation.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: 
War and its impact, Sacrifice and bravery, Camaraderie and brotherhood
