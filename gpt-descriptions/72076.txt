Synopsis: 
In "A Rolling Stone" by B. M. Croker, a young woman named Muriel is thrust into a world of wealth and privilege when she marries a wealthy man. However, she soon discovers that the glittering facade hides a web of secrets and lies. As Muriel navigates the treacherous waters of high society, she must confront her own desires and make difficult choices that will shape her future. With its captivating characters and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Social class, Deception, Self-discovery
