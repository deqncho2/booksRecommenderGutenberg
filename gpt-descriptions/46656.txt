Synopsis: In 'The Ghost of Mystery Airport' by Powell, Van, a group of friends embarks on a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon an abandoned airport with a mysterious past. As they uncover the secrets hidden within its walls, they encounter strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena that lead them to question the very nature of reality. With each twist and turn, the friends must confront their fears and unravel the enigma of the ghostly presence that haunts the airport. Will they be able to solve the mystery before it consumes them?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, the Unknown
