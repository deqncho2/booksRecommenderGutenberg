Synopsis: In "When a Witch is Young: A Historical Novel," Philip Verrill Mighels takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a young witch in a historical setting. Set against the backdrop of a tumultuous era, the story follows the protagonist as she navigates the challenges of her unique abilities, societal expectations, and the pursuit of her true self. As secrets unravel and alliances form, the young witch must confront her own destiny and the forces that seek to control her. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel weaves together history, magic, and personal growth in a spellbinding tale that will leave readers eager for more.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Power, Rebellion, Love and Sacrifice
