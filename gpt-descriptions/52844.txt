Synopsis: In 'The Long Remembered Thunder' by Keith Laumer, a young man named Jim Endicott finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to a hidden power. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the artifact, Jim becomes entangled in a battle between ancient forces and modern technology, where the fate of the world hangs in the balance. With unexpected allies and formidable enemies, Jim must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and harness the power of the long remembered thunder.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Ancient mysteries, Power and its consequences, Good vs. Evil
