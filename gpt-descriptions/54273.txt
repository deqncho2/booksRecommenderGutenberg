Synopsis: 
'Locked Doors' by Mary Roberts Rinehart is a gripping mystery novel that follows the life of a young woman named Kit, who finds herself trapped in a secluded mansion with a group of strangers during a snowstorm. As the storm rages outside, tensions rise within the house, and Kit soon realizes that each person harbors their own secrets and motives. With no way to escape and a murderer on the loose, Kit must unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Trust, Survival
