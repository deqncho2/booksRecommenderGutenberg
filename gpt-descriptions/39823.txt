Synopsis: In "History of the National Flag of the United States of America," Hamilton Schuyler takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of the iconic American flag. From its humble beginnings to its current design, this book unveils the fascinating stories behind each modification and the individuals who played a pivotal role in shaping the flag's symbolism. With vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, Schuyler paints a vivid picture of the flag's significance, making it a must-read for anyone curious about the rich history and enduring power of this beloved national symbol.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, American history, Cultural studies

Themes: Patriotism, National identity, Symbolism
