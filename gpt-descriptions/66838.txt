Synopsis: In 'Coasting Bohemia' by J. Comyns Carr, a group of artists and bohemians embark on a journey along the picturesque coast of England, seeking inspiration and adventure. As they navigate the idyllic landscapes and encounter a cast of eccentric characters, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to both joyous and tumultuous experiences. With its vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this novel takes readers on a captivating exploration of art, love, and the pursuit of a bohemian lifestyle.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Artistic expression, Bohemianism, Love and relationships, Nature and landscapes
