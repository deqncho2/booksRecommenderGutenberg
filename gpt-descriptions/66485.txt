Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 134, April 3, 1915; The Secret of Shangore; Or, Nick Carter Among the Spearmen', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself embroiled in a mysterious case that takes him deep into the heart of Africa. When a wealthy explorer goes missing in the treacherous jungles of Shangore, Carter must navigate dangerous terrain, face deadly spearmen, and unravel a web of secrets to uncover the truth. As he delves deeper into the heart of darkness, Carter's skills and wit are put to the ultimate test, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Deception, Cultural clashes
