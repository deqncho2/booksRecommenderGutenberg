Synopsis: In 'The Chosen People: A Compendium of Sacred and Church History for School-Children', Charlotte M. Yonge takes young readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating history of the chosen people of God. From the ancient Israelites to the early Christian Church, this compendium explores the trials, triumphs, and enduring faith of those who were called to fulfill a divine purpose. Through engaging storytelling and vivid descriptions, Yonge brings to life the heroes, prophets, and saints who shaped the course of religious history. This educational and inspiring book is a must-read for young readers seeking to deepen their understanding of the sacred and church history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Religious Literature, Educational

Themes: Faith, Divine Purpose, Religious History, Heroism
