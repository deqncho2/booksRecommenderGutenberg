Synopsis: In 'The Trials of the Soldier's Wife: A Tale of the Second American Revolution' by Abrams, Alex. St. Clair, follow the gripping journey of Sarah, a devoted wife and mother, as she navigates the tumultuous landscape of a war-torn America. As her husband fights on the front lines, Sarah finds herself facing unimaginable challenges and heart-wrenching choices that will test her strength and resilience. With danger lurking at every turn, she must summon the courage to protect her family and fight for the future they deserve.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Drama

Themes: Love and sacrifice, resilience in the face of adversity, the impact of war on families
