Synopsis: 

'Stories by English Authors: London (Selected by Scribners)' is a captivating collection of short stories that takes readers on a journey through the bustling streets of London. From the grandeur of aristocratic mansions to the grimy back alleys, this anthology showcases the diverse lives and experiences of the city's inhabitants. Each story offers a unique perspective, delving into the hidden secrets, desires, and struggles of the characters who call London their home. With tales of love, mystery, betrayal, and redemption, this anthology paints a vivid portrait of the vibrant and ever-changing city that is London.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Anthology

Themes: Urban life, Social class, Human nature, Secrets, Transformation
