Synopsis: 

In 'The Expositor's Bible: The Epistles of St. Peter', J. Rawson Lumby delves into the profound teachings of the Apostle Peter, offering a comprehensive and enlightening analysis of his epistles. Lumby's meticulous examination of the text brings to light the timeless wisdom and guidance contained within these letters, providing readers with a deeper understanding of Peter's teachings and their relevance to our lives today. With his insightful commentary and scholarly approach, Lumby invites readers on a thought-provoking journey through the Epistles of St. Peter, uncovering profound insights and inspiring reflections along the way.

Genres: Religious literature, Biblical commentary, Theology

Themes: Faith and perseverance, Christian living, Spiritual growth, The role of suffering, The importance of love and unity in the Christian community
