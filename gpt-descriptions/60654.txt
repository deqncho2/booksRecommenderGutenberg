Synopsis: In 'Love and Moondogs' by Richard McKenna, a young musician named Jake finds himself caught in a whirlwind of love, music, and self-discovery when he meets a mysterious woman named Luna. As their passionate relationship unfolds, Jake is drawn into a world of late-night jam sessions, underground clubs, and the enchanting power of the moon. But as their connection deepens, Jake must confront his own fears and insecurities, ultimately learning that love and music have the ability to heal even the deepest wounds.

Genres: Romance, Music, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love, Self-discovery, Passion, Healing
