Synopsis: 
'The Sunken Garden, and Other Poems' by Walter De la Mare is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through enchanting landscapes, mysterious encounters, and introspective musings. From the depths of a hidden garden to the ethereal realms of dreams, De la Mare's words paint vivid pictures and evoke a sense of wonder and curiosity. Each poem is a window into the human experience, exploring themes of nature, beauty, love, and the enigmatic nature of existence. This collection is a treasure trove of lyrical gems that will leave readers mesmerized and inspired.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Nature, Beauty, Love, Existence, Mystery
