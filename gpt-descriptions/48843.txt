Synopsis: 
In "Philochristus: Memoirs of a Disciple of the Lord," Edwin Abbott takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a disciple who walked alongside Jesus Christ. Through the eyes of Philochristus, we witness the miracles, teachings, and profound impact of the Messiah on the lives of those around him. As Philochristus recounts his experiences, we are immersed in a world of faith, doubt, and unwavering devotion, where the boundaries between the divine and the human blur. This thought-provoking memoir offers a unique perspective on the life of Jesus and the transformative power of belief.

Genres: Historical fiction, Religious fiction, Memoir

Themes: Faith, Doubt, Devotion, Transformation
