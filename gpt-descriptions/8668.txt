Synopsis: 
In 'Revenge!' by Barr, Robert, a seemingly ordinary man named John finds himself caught in a web of deceit and betrayal after a tragic incident changes his life forever. Fueled by a burning desire for justice, John embarks on a relentless quest for revenge, uncovering dark secrets and facing dangerous adversaries along the way. As the line between right and wrong blurs, John must confront his own demons and make choices that will determine the course of his life. Gripping and suspenseful, 'Revenge!' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Revenge, Justice, Betrayal, Morality
