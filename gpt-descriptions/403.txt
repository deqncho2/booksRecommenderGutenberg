Synopsis: 
In 'Soldiers of Fortune' by Davis, Richard Harding, a group of wealthy and adventurous individuals embark on a perilous journey to a remote and war-torn country. Drawn by the promise of excitement and riches, they soon find themselves caught in a web of political intrigue, danger, and unexpected alliances. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and face life-threatening challenges, their true characters are tested, and they must confront their own motivations and loyalties. With twists and turns at every corner, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Political Fiction

Themes: Wealth and privilege, Loyalty and betrayal, Morality in times of conflict
