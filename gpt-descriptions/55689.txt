Synopsis: In 'The Clock Struck One' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious murder takes place in a small English village, leaving the residents baffled and fearful. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, and the true nature of the village's inhabitants is revealed. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town intrigue, Uncovering the truth
