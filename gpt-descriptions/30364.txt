Synopsis: 
'An Account of The Kingdom of Nepal: And of the Territories Annexed to this Dominion by the House of Gorkha' by Francis Hamilton takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious and enchanting Kingdom of Nepal. Through vivid descriptions and firsthand experiences, Hamilton unveils the rich history, culture, and landscapes of this ancient land. From the majestic Himalayan peaks to the bustling markets of Kathmandu, the reader is immersed in a world of breathtaking beauty and intriguing customs. As Hamilton delves deeper into the political landscape, he uncovers the fascinating story of the House of Gorkha and their conquests, shedding light on the rise of this powerful dynasty. With its blend of adventure, history, and cultural exploration, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the hidden treasures of Nepal.

Genres: Travelogue, History, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Power and Politics, Cultural Identity
