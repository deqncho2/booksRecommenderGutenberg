Synopsis: 
In 'Roy Blakeley's Funny-bone Hike', Roy and his friends embark on an exciting adventure filled with laughter, mishaps, and unexpected encounters. As they set out on a hike, they stumble upon a mysterious old house that holds a secret. With their quick wit and mischievous nature, Roy and his friends find themselves in hilarious situations that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Adventure, Humor, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Adventure, Problem-solving
