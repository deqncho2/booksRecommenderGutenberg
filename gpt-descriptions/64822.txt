Synopsis: 
"The Alhambra: being a brief record of the Arabian conquest of the Peninsula with a particular account of the Mohammedan architecture and decoration" takes readers on a captivating journey through the Arabian conquest of the Iberian Peninsula, focusing on the magnificent Mohammedan architecture and decoration found in the Alhambra. Albert Frederick Calvert provides a vivid and detailed account of the historical events surrounding the conquest, while also delving into the intricate beauty and cultural significance of the Alhambra's architectural wonders. From the intricate tilework to the mesmerizing calligraphy, readers will be transported to a world of opulence and artistic mastery. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Architecture, Cultural exploration
Themes: Arabian conquest, Islamic architecture, Cultural heritage
