Synopsis: 

In 'True Tales of Arctic Heroism in the New World', A. W. Greely takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous Arctic landscapes, recounting incredible stories of bravery and survival. From daring expeditions to harrowing encounters with nature's fury, this collection of true tales will leave readers on the edge of their seats, marveling at the indomitable spirit of these Arctic heroes. 

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Bravery, Survival, Exploration
