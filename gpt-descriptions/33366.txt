Synopsis: In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 98, June 14 1890', readers are transported to the vibrant and satirical world of Victorian England. This edition of the renowned magazine presents a collection of witty and humorous articles, cartoons, and social commentaries that cleverly dissect the absurdities and idiosyncrasies of the era. From political satire to social observations, this volume offers a delightful glimpse into the comical and often bizarre aspects of Victorian society.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Periodical

Themes: Social commentary, Victorian society, Political satire
