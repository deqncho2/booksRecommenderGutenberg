Synopsis: In 'Peggy Plays Off-Broadway' by Virginia Hughes, follow the captivating journey of Peggy, a talented young actress who dreams of making it big on the Broadway stage. As she navigates the cutthroat world of auditions, rehearsals, and backstage drama, Peggy's determination and resilience are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover if Peggy's dreams will become a reality.

Genres: Drama, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Pursuing dreams, Overcoming obstacles, Self-discovery
