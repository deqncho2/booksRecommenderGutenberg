Synopsis: In 'A Handful of Stars: Texts That Have Moved Great Minds', Frank Boreham takes readers on a captivating journey through the power of words. From ancient philosophers to modern thinkers, Boreham explores the profound impact that certain texts have had on the minds of great individuals. Through his eloquent storytelling, he delves into the transformative nature of literature and the ways in which it can shape our perspectives, inspire us, and ignite our imaginations. With each chapter, Boreham unveils a new text that has left an indelible mark on the minds of those who have encountered it, inviting readers to reflect on the profound beauty and wisdom that can be found within the written word.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, Inspirational

Themes: The power of literature, transformative texts, the impact of words, inspiration, personal growth
