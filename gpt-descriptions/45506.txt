Synopsis: In the world of Flatland, where everything exists in only two dimensions, a humble Square finds his perspective forever changed when he encounters a mysterious visitor from a higher dimension. As the Square navigates the complexities of his geometric society, he embarks on a mind-bending journey that challenges his beliefs and forces him to question the limitations of his reality. With wit and imagination, 'Flatland: A Romance of Many Dimensions' takes readers on a captivating exploration of geometry, social hierarchy, and the nature of perception.

Genres: Science Fiction, Satire, Mathematical Fiction

Themes: Perception, Social Critique, Dimensions, Reality
