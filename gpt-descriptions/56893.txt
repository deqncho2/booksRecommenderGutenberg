Synopsis: In "Nervous Ills, Their Cause and Cure" by Boris Sidis, delve into the fascinating exploration of the human mind and the intricate connection between our emotions and physical well-being. Through captivating case studies and insightful analysis, Sidis uncovers the hidden causes behind various nervous disorders and offers a groundbreaking approach to their treatment. This thought-provoking work will challenge your understanding of the mind-body connection and leave you with a newfound appreciation for the power of the human psyche.

Genres: Psychology, Self-help, Medical

Themes: Mind-body connection, Emotional well-being, Mental health
