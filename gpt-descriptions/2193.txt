Synopsis: In 'A Ward of the Golden Gate' by Bret Harte, a young girl named Yerba Buena is left orphaned and alone in the bustling city of San Francisco during the Gold Rush era. Taken in by a mysterious benefactor, she is thrust into a world of wealth, intrigue, and danger. As Yerba navigates the treacherous waters of high society, she must uncover the secrets of her past and confront the dark forces that threaten her future. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing city, this gripping tale explores the complexities of identity, love, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Social Class, Love and Relationships
