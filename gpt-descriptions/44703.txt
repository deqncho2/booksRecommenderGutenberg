Synopsis: In 'The Eighteen Christian Centuries', James White takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of Christianity, exploring the triumphs, struggles, and transformations that have shaped the faith over the course of eighteen centuries. From the early days of the Church to the challenges faced by Christians in the modern world, White delves into the lives of key figures, the impact of major events, and the evolution of Christian thought. With a blend of historical analysis and engaging storytelling, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the enduring legacy of Christianity.

Genres: History, Religion, Non-fiction

Themes: Faith and belief, Historical development, Influence and impact
