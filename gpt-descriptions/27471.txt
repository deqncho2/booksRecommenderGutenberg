Synopsis: In 'The Wall Between' by Sara Ware Bassett, a young woman named Ruth finds herself torn between two worlds when she inherits a grand estate with a mysterious past. As she delves into the secrets hidden within the walls, she uncovers a web of deceit, love, and betrayal that threatens to consume her. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, Ruth must navigate the treacherous path between truth and illusion, all while trying to protect her own heart. Will she be able to break down the wall that separates her from the truth, or will she become trapped in a world of lies?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and betrayal, Identity and self-discovery
