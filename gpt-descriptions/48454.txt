Synopsis: 

In "Inventors at Work, with Chapters on Discovery" by George Iles, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the lives and minds of some of history's greatest inventors. From the brilliant minds of Thomas Edison and Alexander Graham Bell to the lesser-known but equally remarkable inventors, this book delves into the fascinating stories behind their groundbreaking discoveries. Each chapter unveils the struggles, triumphs, and moments of inspiration that shaped these inventors' lives, offering a glimpse into the creative process and the relentless pursuit of innovation. With vivid storytelling and insightful anecdotes, "Inventors at Work" celebrates the ingenuity and determination that have forever changed the world.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Innovation, Creativity, Perseverance, Scientific Discovery
