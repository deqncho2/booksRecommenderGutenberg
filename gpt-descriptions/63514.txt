Synopsis: 
'Boys and Girls: The Verses of James W. Foley' is a captivating collection of poetry that delves into the complexities of life, love, and the human experience. Through his eloquent verses, James W. Foley explores the depths of emotions, the beauty of nature, and the power of self-discovery. From poignant reflections on heartbreak to uplifting odes to the wonders of the world, this collection takes readers on a journey of introspection and inspiration. With each poem, Foley invites readers to connect with their own emotions and find solace in the power of words.

Genres: Poetry, Contemporary Literature

Themes: Life, Love, Nature, Self-discovery
