Synopsis: 
In 'Rimrock Trail' by J. Allan Dunn, a young geologist named Jack is sent on a perilous expedition to the treacherous Rimrock Mountains. As he navigates through the rugged terrain, he encounters a mysterious tribe, a beautiful but enigmatic woman, and a hidden treasure that could change his life forever. With danger lurking at every turn, Jack must rely on his wits and courage to survive and unravel the secrets of the Rimrock Trail.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Love and Betrayal
