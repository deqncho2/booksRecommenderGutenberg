Synopsis: In 'Machine-Gun Tactics' by R. V. K. Applin, a young soldier finds himself thrust into the chaos of World War I, where he must navigate the treacherous trenches and deadly battlefields. As he witnesses the devastating power of machine guns, he becomes determined to uncover a way to outsmart and overcome this formidable weapon. With the lives of his comrades hanging in the balance, he embarks on a daring mission that will test his courage, resourcefulness, and loyalty. Will he succeed in his quest to turn the tide of war?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Action

Themes: Courage, Survival, Innovation
