Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts of Lenox; Or, The Hike Over Big Bear Mountain' by Frank V. Webster, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts embarks on a thrilling journey through the rugged wilderness of Big Bear Mountain. As they navigate treacherous terrains, encounter unexpected challenges, and uncover hidden secrets, the scouts must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and determination to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. With each step they take, the boys not only test their physical limits but also learn valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the power of perseverance. Will they successfully conquer Big Bear Mountain and return home with newfound wisdom and unforgettable memories?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Perseverance
