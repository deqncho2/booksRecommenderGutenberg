Synopsis: In 'The Parables of Our Lord' by William Arnot, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the timeless and thought-provoking parables told by Jesus. From the familiar stories of the Good Samaritan and the Prodigal Son to lesser-known parables, Arnot skillfully explores the deeper meanings and spiritual lessons hidden within these tales. With vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, this book offers a fresh perspective on the teachings of Jesus, inviting readers to reflect on their own lives and discover profound truths that transcend time and culture.

Genres: Christian literature, Biblical studies, Spiritual growth

Themes: Faith and redemption, Compassion and forgiveness, The power of storytelling
