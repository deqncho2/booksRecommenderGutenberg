Synopsis: 

In 'Seeking His Fortune, and Other Dialogues', Alger and Cheney present a collection of captivating dialogues that delve into the lives of individuals striving to make their mark in the world. From ambitious young men seeking success in the business world to determined women pursuing their dreams against societal expectations, these dialogues explore the challenges, sacrifices, and triumphs that come with the pursuit of fortune and fulfillment. Through thought-provoking conversations and relatable characters, the authors offer a glimpse into the complexities of ambition, determination, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Success, Determination, Societal Expectations, Personal Fulfillment
