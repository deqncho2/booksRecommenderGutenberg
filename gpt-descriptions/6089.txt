Synopsis: 
In 'The Follies of Love' by Jean-François Regnard, a young and naive nobleman named Lélio finds himself entangled in a web of love, deceit, and mistaken identities. As he navigates through a series of hilarious and absurd situations, Lélio must learn to distinguish between true love and superficial infatuation. With a cast of eccentric characters and a plot filled with unexpected twists and turns, this comedic masterpiece takes readers on a delightful journey through the follies of love.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Love and infatuation, Deception and mistaken identities, Social class and hierarchy
