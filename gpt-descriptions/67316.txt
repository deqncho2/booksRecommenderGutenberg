Synopsis: In 'The First' by Ludwig, Edward W., a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything we know about human history. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden truth that could change the course of humanity forever. With the clock ticking and powerful forces determined to keep the secret buried, he must navigate a treacherous path of deception and danger to reveal the truth to the world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Human origins, Conspiracy, Scientific discovery
