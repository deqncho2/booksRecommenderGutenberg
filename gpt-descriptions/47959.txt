Synopsis: 
'From Paper-mill to Pressroom' takes readers on a captivating journey through the transformation of the paper industry and the birth of modern printing. William Bond Wheelwright, a renowned historian, delves into the fascinating history of paper-making and its profound impact on the dissemination of knowledge and ideas. From the humble beginnings of paper production in ancient China to the revolutionary advancements in printing technology, this book unveils the remarkable story behind the evolution of the written word. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Wheelwright paints a vivid picture of the individuals, inventions, and societal changes that shaped the paper-mill industry and paved the way for the modern pressroom.

Genres: History, Non-fiction, Technology

Themes: Innovation, Communication, Industrial Revolution
