Synopsis: In 'Weymouth New Testament in Modern Speech, John', Richard Francis Weymouth presents a fresh and contemporary translation of the Gospel of John. This rendition brings to life the profound teachings and miracles of Jesus Christ, as well as the conflicts and controversies he faced during his ministry. Through vivid language and a modern perspective, Weymouth invites readers to delve into the timeless wisdom and spiritual journey found within the pages of this beloved biblical text.

Genres: Religious, Spiritual, Translation

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Divine Love, Spiritual Enlightenment
