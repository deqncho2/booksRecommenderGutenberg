Synopsis: 
'Farthest North: The Life and Explorations of Lieutenant James Booth Lockwood, of the Greely Arctic Expedition' takes readers on a thrilling journey into the icy depths of the Arctic. This gripping biography follows the remarkable life of Lieutenant James Booth Lockwood, a key member of the ill-fated Greely Arctic Expedition. From his early days as a determined young explorer to his harrowing experiences in the treacherous Arctic wilderness, Lockwood's story is one of courage, resilience, and the relentless pursuit of discovery. As readers delve into the pages of this captivating account, they will be transported to a world of frozen landscapes, perilous encounters, and the indomitable spirit of exploration.

Genres: Biography, Adventure, History

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Perseverance
