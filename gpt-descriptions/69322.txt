Synopsis: In "The Leading Lady" by Geraldine Bonner, a talented and ambitious actress named Marion embarks on a journey to make a name for herself in the male-dominated world of theater during the early 20th century. Facing numerous challenges and societal expectations, Marion must navigate through love, betrayal, and the pursuit of her dreams. As she rises to prominence, she discovers the true cost of fame and the sacrifices required to maintain her position in the spotlight. This captivating tale explores the complexities of gender roles, the pursuit of success, and the price of ambition.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Gender inequality, Ambition, Love and Betrayal
