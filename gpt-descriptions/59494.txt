Synopsis: In the heart of a bustling city, a mysterious night court operates under the cover of darkness, presided over by a judge with a hidden past. As the court deals with a series of enigmatic cases, the judge finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, corruption, and unexpected alliances. With each case, the boundaries between justice and darkness blur, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning the true nature of the night court.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Legal Drama

Themes: Justice, Corruption, Redemption, Identity
