Synopsis: 
In "An ivory trader in North Kenia: The record of an expedition through Kikuyu to Galla-land in east equatorial Africa; with an account of the Rendili and Burkeneji tribes," Alfred Arkell-Hardwick takes readers on a captivating journey through the heart of Africa. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows the adventures of an ivory trader as he navigates the treacherous landscapes and encounters the diverse tribes of North Kenya. From the vibrant Kikuyu people to the enigmatic Rendili and Burkeneji tribes, the trader's expedition unveils the rich cultural tapestry of the region. As he delves deeper into the unknown, the trader must confront the moral complexities of his trade and grapple with the consequences of his actions. With vivid descriptions and gripping encounters, this book offers a thrilling exploration of Africa's untamed wilderness and the human spirit that thrives within it.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Diversity, Morality, Consequences
