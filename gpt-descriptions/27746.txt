Synopsis: 

In 'Schedule of Salaries for Teachers, members of the Supervising staff and others: January 1-August 31, 1920, inclusive', the Boston School Committee unveils a comprehensive guide to the salaries of teachers and staff members during a pivotal period in history. This intriguing document sheds light on the financial landscape of the education system, offering a glimpse into the challenges and rewards faced by those dedicated to shaping young minds. As the reader delves into the pages of this unique publication, they will uncover the intricate details of compensation and the evolving role of educators in society.

Genres: Historical, Reference

Themes: Education, Compensation, Social History
