Synopsis: In "Remarks Concerning Stones Said to Have Fallen from the Clouds, Both in These Days, and in Antient Times," King Edward delves into the fascinating phenomenon of stones falling from the sky. Through meticulous research and captivating anecdotes, he explores both modern and ancient accounts of these mysterious occurrences. From the awe-inspiring tales of witnesses to the scientific explanations proposed by experts, this work offers a thought-provoking exploration of a phenomenon that has puzzled humanity for centuries.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Natural phenomena, Curiosity, Scientific inquiry
