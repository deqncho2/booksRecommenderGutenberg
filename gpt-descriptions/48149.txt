Synopsis: In 'Fundamental Philosophy, Vol. 1 (of 2)', Jaime Luciano Balmes takes readers on a captivating intellectual journey, exploring the fundamental questions of human existence and the nature of reality. Through a thought-provoking blend of philosophy, theology, and science, Balmes challenges conventional wisdom and invites readers to question their own beliefs. As the pages turn, readers will find themselves immersed in a world of profound ideas and profound insights, leaving them eager to delve into the second volume.

Genres: Philosophy, Theology, Science

Themes: Existence, Reality, Belief, Knowledge
