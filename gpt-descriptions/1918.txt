Synopsis: In 'Long Odds' by H. Rider Haggard, a young man named Frank Muller finds himself caught in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. With the promise of unimaginable wealth, Frank embarks on a perilous journey through treacherous landscapes and encounters dangerous adversaries. As he faces numerous challenges and tests his courage, Frank must rely on his wits and determination to survive and claim the treasure that lies at the end of the long odds.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Treasure hunting, Perseverance, Survival
