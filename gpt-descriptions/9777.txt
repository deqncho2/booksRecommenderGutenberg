Synopsis: 
'Riley Child-Rhymes' is a delightful collection of whimsical and imaginative poems that will transport readers back to the enchanting world of childhood. From mischievous fairies and talking animals to playful adventures and heartwarming friendships, James Whitcomb Riley weaves together a tapestry of captivating rhymes that will leave readers of all ages smiling and reminiscing about the magic of youth.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: Childhood, Imagination, Playfulness
