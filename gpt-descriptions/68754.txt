Synopsis:
In Glenarvon, Volume 1, Caroline, Lady Lamb takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous world of love, passion, and betrayal. Set against the backdrop of the early 19th century, the story follows the young and beautiful Calantha, who finds herself irresistibly drawn to the enigmatic Lord Glenarvon. As their relationship intensifies, Calantha becomes entangled in a web of scandal, political intrigue, and dark secrets that threaten to consume her. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this gripping tale explores the complexities of desire, the power of obsession, and the consequences of forbidden love.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Romance, Historical fiction

Themes: Love and desire, Betrayal, Power dynamics, Social expectations
