Synopsis: 
In "Chain of Command" by Arr, Stephen, a high-ranking military officer finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and betrayal when he uncovers a shocking secret that threatens to destabilize the entire nation. As he navigates through a dangerous maze of power struggles and hidden agendas, he must make difficult choices that will not only determine his own fate but also the future of his country. With pulse-pounding action and unexpected twists, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Political Fiction

Themes: Power, Betrayal, Loyalty, Conspiracy
