Synopsis: In 'The Climbers' by Bates, Lizzie, a group of adventurous individuals embark on a treacherous journey to conquer the world's highest peak. As they face the unforgiving challenges of the mountain, their personal struggles and hidden secrets threaten to unravel their unity. With each step closer to the summit, the climbers must confront their deepest fears and make life-altering decisions that will test their limits and redefine their understanding of courage and sacrifice.

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Suspense

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Self-discovery
