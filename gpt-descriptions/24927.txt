Synopsis: In 'A Matter of Magnitude' by Al Sevcik, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As the world grapples with the implications of this newfound power, a thrilling race against time ensues, filled with suspense, intrigue, and unexpected twists. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, the characters must navigate moral dilemmas and confront their own personal demons. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Consequences
