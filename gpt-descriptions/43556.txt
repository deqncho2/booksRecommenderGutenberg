Synopsis: 

In 'I, Mary MacLane: A Diary of Human Days', we are invited into the intimate world of Mary MacLane, a young woman living in Butte, Montana, at the turn of the 20th century. Through her raw and unfiltered diary entries, Mary bares her soul, exploring her deepest desires, frustrations, and ambitions. With a fierce determination to break free from societal expectations, Mary challenges the norms of her time, delving into her own sexuality, spirituality, and the complexities of her relationships. As we journey alongside Mary, we witness her unapologetic self-expression and her relentless pursuit of personal freedom.

Genres: Autobiography, Diary, Feminist Literature

Themes: Self-discovery, Rebellion, Female Empowerment
