Synopsis: 

'Infantry Drill Regulations, United States Army, 1911: Corrected to April 15, 1917 (Changes Nos. 1 to 19)' is a comprehensive guide that takes readers into the world of military training and tactics during a critical period in American history. This meticulously corrected edition provides a fascinating glimpse into the evolution of infantry drill regulations, offering valuable insights into the strategies and techniques employed by the United States Army during World War I. From the precise execution of formations to the intricacies of combat maneuvers, this book serves as a vital resource for understanding the discipline and precision required of soldiers during this era.

Genres: Military history, Reference

Themes: Military training, Tactical strategies, Discipline
