Synopsis: 

In "The British Woodlice: Being a Monograph of the Terrestrial Isopod Crustacea Occurring in the British Islands," authors Webb and Sillem delve into the fascinating world of woodlice, uncovering their hidden secrets and shedding light on their unique characteristics. From their habitats to their behavior, this monograph provides a comprehensive exploration of these often overlooked creatures. Through meticulous research and captivating descriptions, the authors invite readers to embark on a journey of discovery, unveiling the intricate lives of woodlice and their significance within the British Isles.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Zoology

Themes: Biodiversity, Ecology, Species Interaction
