Synopsis: 
Step into the opulent world of Jacobean furniture and English styles in oak and walnut, as Helen Churchill Candee takes you on a captivating journey through the intricate craftsmanship and lavish designs of this remarkable era. From grand four-poster beds to intricately carved cabinets, this book unveils the secrets behind the exquisite pieces that adorned the homes of the Jacobean period. Delve into the fascinating history, explore the influences of European design, and discover the stories behind the master craftsmen who brought these stunning creations to life. With stunning photographs and detailed descriptions, this book is a treasure trove for furniture enthusiasts and history lovers alike.

Genres: 
Art History, Interior Design, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: 
Craftsmanship, Design Influences, Historical Aesthetics
