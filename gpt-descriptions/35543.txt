Synopsis: In 'My Little Boy' by Carl Ewald, a young boy named Peter embarks on a captivating adventure when he discovers a mysterious creature in his backyard. As Peter forms an unlikely bond with this extraordinary being, he must navigate a world filled with wonder, danger, and unexpected twists. With each passing day, Peter's life becomes intertwined with the creature's, leading to a heartwarming and thought-provoking journey that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Self-discovery
