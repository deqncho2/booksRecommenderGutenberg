Synopsis: In 'The Lost Land of King Arthur' by John Cuming Walters, a renowned historian embarks on a captivating journey to uncover the truth behind the legendary King Arthur. As he delves into ancient texts, explores mystical landscapes, and encounters enigmatic characters, he unravels a web of secrets that could change history forever. With each revelation, the line between myth and reality becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a thrilling quest for the lost land of King Arthur.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Legends and Mythology, Historical Truth, Quest for Identity
