Synopsis: In 'The Emeryville Shellmound', archaeologist Max Uhle embarks on a captivating journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient Native American burial site in Emeryville, California. As he delves deeper into the excavation, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery, intrigue, and unexpected discoveries that challenge his understanding of history and the human spirit. With each artifact unearthed, Uhle's quest takes on a personal dimension, leading him to question the true nature of the past and its impact on the present.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Archaeology, Cultural Heritage, Identity, Legacy
