Synopsis: In 'The Radio Boys in the Thousand Islands; Or, The Yankee-Canadian Wireless Trail' by Frank Honeywell, a group of adventurous young radio enthusiasts find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery while exploring the picturesque Thousand Islands region. As they navigate the treacherous waters and encounter suspicious characters, the boys must use their radio skills to uncover a secret that could have far-reaching consequences. With danger lurking at every turn, their determination and resourcefulness will be put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Technology, Courage
