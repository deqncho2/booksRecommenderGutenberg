Synopsis: In 'The Disentanglers' by Lang, Andrew, a group of skilled individuals known as "The Disentanglers" are brought together to solve a series of perplexing mysteries. From uncovering hidden secrets to untangling complex webs of deceit, this thrilling collection of interconnected stories takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of crime and intrigue. As the Disentanglers navigate through a variety of puzzling cases, they must rely on their wit, intelligence, and unique set of skills to unravel the truth and bring justice to those in need.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Investigation, Problem-solving, Justice
