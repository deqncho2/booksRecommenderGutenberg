Synopsis: 

'Outwitting Our Nerves: A Primer of Psychotherapy' takes readers on a captivating journey into the realm of psychotherapy, exploring the intricate workings of the human mind and the power of self-discovery. Written by Josephine A. Jackson and Helen M. Salisbury, this enlightening book delves into the depths of our nerves, unraveling the mysteries of anxiety, fear, and stress, while providing practical techniques to overcome these challenges. Through a combination of personal anecdotes, scientific research, and therapeutic insights, the authors offer a roadmap to understanding and outwitting our own nerves, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling and balanced life.

Genres: Psychology, Self-help, Non-fiction

Themes: Psychotherapy, Mental Health, Self-discovery, Overcoming Anxiety
