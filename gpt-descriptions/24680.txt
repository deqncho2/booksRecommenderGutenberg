Synopsis: In the heart of ancient Rome, a young Christian woman named Irene finds herself caught in a web of danger and intrigue as she navigates the treacherous catacombs beneath the city. With her faith tested and her life on the line, Irene must make a choice that could change the course of history. As she faces persecution and betrayal, she discovers the true meaning of sacrifice and the power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Christian Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Faith, Persecution, Sacrifice, Love
