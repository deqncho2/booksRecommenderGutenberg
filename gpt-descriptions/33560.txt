Synopsis: In 'A New Genus of Pennsylvanian Fish (Crossopterygii, Coelacanthiformes) from Kansas', Echols, Joan takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of paleontology, unveiling the discovery of a previously unknown genus of fish from the Pennsylvanian era in Kansas. As the story unfolds, readers are immersed in the excitement of scientific exploration, as researchers uncover clues about the ancient past and the evolution of life on Earth. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Echols brings to life the remarkable story of this new genus of fish, shedding light on the mysteries of our planet's history.

Genres: Non-fiction, Paleontology, Natural History

Themes: Scientific discovery, Evolution, Ancient life, Paleontological research
