Synopsis: 

In 'Diary Written in the Provincial Lunatic Asylum', Mary Huestis Pengilly takes us on a captivating journey through the inner workings of a 19th-century asylum. Through the eyes of a patient, we witness the daily struggles, the haunting secrets, and the fragile line between sanity and madness. As the diary unfolds, we are drawn into a world of mystery, where the boundaries of reality and imagination blur, leaving us questioning our own perceptions. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Psychological Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Mental illness, Perception, Identity, Power dynamics
