Synopsis: 
In 'The God of Love' by Justin H. McCarthy, a young artist named Maurice Desmond finds himself caught in a web of love, desire, and betrayal in the vibrant city of Paris. As he navigates the bohemian lifestyle of the late 19th century, Maurice becomes entangled with a captivating woman who challenges his beliefs and pushes him to explore the depths of his own passions. Set against the backdrop of artistic revolution and societal constraints, this gripping tale explores the complexities of love and the sacrifices one must make to follow their heart.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Artistic Expression, Betrayal, Societal Expectations
