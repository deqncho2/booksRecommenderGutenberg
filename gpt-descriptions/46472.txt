Synopsis: 
Inventors & Inventions takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of innovation and discovery. From the ancient civilizations to the modern era, this book unveils the remarkable stories behind some of the most groundbreaking inventions that have shaped our world. Delve into the minds of brilliant inventors, witness their struggles and triumphs, and uncover the unexpected connections between seemingly unrelated inventions. With vivid descriptions and intriguing anecdotes, Robinson, Henry brings to life the incredible ingenuity and creativity that have propelled humanity forward.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Innovation, Creativity, Human Ingenuity
