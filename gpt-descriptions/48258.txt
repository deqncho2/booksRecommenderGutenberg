Synopsis: In 'The Old Dominion' by Mary Johnston, a young woman named Judith finds herself caught in the midst of the American Revolution, torn between her loyalty to her family and her desire for freedom. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of war, Judith must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and challenge the conventions of her time. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous era of the American Revolution.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Freedom, Identity, Courage
