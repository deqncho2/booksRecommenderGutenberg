Synopsis:
In "Helena" by Ward, Humphry, Mrs., a young woman named Helena finds herself caught between duty and desire when she is forced into an arranged marriage. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, Helena discovers a hidden world of secrets and intrigue that threatens to unravel everything she holds dear. With her wit and determination, she must uncover the truth and make choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century England, this captivating novel explores the power of love, the constraints of society, and the strength of the human spirit.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes:
Love and Duty, Social Expectations, Secrets and Betrayal
