Synopsis: Valancy Stirling, a timid and oppressed young woman, lives a monotonous life under the strict rule of her overbearing family. But when she receives devastating news about her health, Valancy decides to break free from societal expectations and live life on her own terms. In her newfound freedom, she discovers a hidden strength within herself and embarks on a journey of self-discovery, love, and adventure. Set against the backdrop of the breathtaking Blue Castle, this enchanting novel takes readers on a captivating and transformative journey that will leave them yearning for their own moments of liberation.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Freedom, Love, Defying societal expectations
