Synopsis: In 'The Boy Pilot of the Lakes; Or, Nat Morton's Perils' by Frank V. Webster, readers are introduced to Nat Morton, a young and adventurous boy who dreams of becoming a pilot. When an unexpected opportunity arises, Nat finds himself navigating the treacherous waters of the Great Lakes, facing numerous perils and challenges along the way. From dangerous storms to encounters with ruthless criminals, Nat's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome these obstacles and fulfill his dream of becoming a pilot?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Action

Themes: Courage, Determination, Pursuit of Dreams
