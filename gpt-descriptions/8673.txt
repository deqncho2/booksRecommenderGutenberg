Synopsis: In 'A Columbus of Space' by Garrett Putman Serviss, embark on a thrilling interstellar adventure as a young astronomer discovers a mysterious celestial body that defies all known laws of physics. As he sets out to unravel the enigma, he finds himself caught in a web of danger, intrigue, and the possibility of encountering extraterrestrial life. With each twist and turn, the boundaries of human knowledge and the nature of the universe are pushed to their limits, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, The Unknown, Human Curiosity
