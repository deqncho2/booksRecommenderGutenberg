Synopsis: In 'The Tribes and Castes of the Central Provinces of India, Volume 4', Russell, R. V. delves into the rich cultural tapestry of the Central Provinces of India, exploring the diverse tribes and castes that inhabit the region. From the Bhils, Gonds, and Korkus to the Ahirs, Banjaras, and Kolams, this comprehensive volume provides a fascinating glimpse into the customs, traditions, and social structures of these communities. Through meticulous research and vivid descriptions, Russell, R. V. paints a vivid picture of the vibrant and complex society that thrives in this part of India.

Genres: Anthropology, Ethnography, Cultural Studies

Themes: Cultural diversity, Social structures, Traditional customs and practices
