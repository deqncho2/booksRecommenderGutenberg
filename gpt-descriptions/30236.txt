Synopsis: In the captivating novel "Pepita Ximenez" by Juan Valera, a young seminarian named Luis de Vargas finds himself torn between his religious vocation and an unexpected attraction to the beautiful and enigmatic Pepita Ximenez. Set in 19th-century Spain, this tale of forbidden love and moral dilemmas takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and the complexities of desire. As Luis grapples with his conflicting emotions, he must navigate the boundaries of societal expectations and confront the consequences of his choices. Will he choose the path of duty or surrender to the intoxicating allure of passion?

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Historical fiction

Themes: Love and desire, Morality and ethics, Religious conflict
