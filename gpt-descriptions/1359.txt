Synopsis: 
'A Guide to the Best Historical Novels and Tales' by Jonathan Nield is a captivating exploration of the most remarkable historical novels and tales ever written. From ancient civilizations to modern times, Nield takes readers on a journey through time, introducing them to unforgettable characters and immersive settings. With insightful analysis and recommendations, this guide is a treasure trove for both avid readers and history enthusiasts, offering a curated selection of the finest historical fiction ever penned.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Historical Fiction, Non-Fiction

Themes: History, Literature, Storytelling
