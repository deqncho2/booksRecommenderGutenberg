Synopsis: 
The Koran (Al-Qur'an) is a profound and influential religious text that serves as the central scripture of Islam. It presents a collection of divine revelations received by the Prophet Muhammad over a period of 23 years. This sacred book delves into the fundamental beliefs and practices of Islam, exploring themes of faith, morality, guidance, and the relationship between humanity and the divine. Through its poetic and thought-provoking verses, The Koran offers spiritual insights, ethical teachings, and a comprehensive worldview that continues to shape the lives of millions around the world.

Genres: Religious text, Philosophy, Poetry

Themes: Faith, Morality, Guidance, Humanity and the Divine
