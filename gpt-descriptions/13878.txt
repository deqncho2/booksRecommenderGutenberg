Synopsis: In 'The English Orphans; Or, A Home in the New World', Mary Jane Holmes takes readers on a captivating journey across the Atlantic, where two young English siblings find themselves orphaned and alone in a foreign land. As they navigate the challenges of their new life, they encounter unexpected friendships, face heart-wrenching trials, and discover the true meaning of family. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant New World, this tale of resilience, love, and hope will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: Resilience, Identity, Friendship, Love, Home
