Synopsis: 

In 'Turkey Raising' by Lee, Alfred R./ Marsden, Stanley J., readers are taken on a captivating journey into the world of turkey farming. Set against the backdrop of a small rural town, the story follows the lives of two unlikely friends who embark on a daring adventure to save their struggling turkey farm. As they face numerous challenges and unexpected obstacles, their determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. With vivid descriptions and a touch of humor, this heartwarming tale explores the bond between humans and animals, the resilience of the human spirit, and the power of friendship.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Animal-human connection
