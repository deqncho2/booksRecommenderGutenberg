Synopsis: 

In 'The High Deeds of Finn and other Bardic Romances of Ancient Ireland', T. W. Rolleston takes us on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of ancient Ireland. This collection of bardic romances introduces us to the legendary hero Finn and his band of warriors, as they embark on thrilling adventures filled with magic, battles, and quests. From encounters with mythical creatures to encounters with love and betrayal, these tales transport us to a time of bravery and honor, where the line between reality and myth blurs.

Genres: Mythology, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Fate, Love, Mythical Creatures
