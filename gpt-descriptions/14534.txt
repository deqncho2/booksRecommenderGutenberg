Synopsis: In 'Christmas with Grandma Elsie', the beloved Elsie Dinsmore series continues as the family gathers for a heartwarming holiday celebration. As Grandma Elsie's wisdom and love guide them through the joys and challenges of the season, unexpected events and cherished traditions intertwine, creating a memorable Christmas that will touch the reader's heart.

Genres: Fiction, Christian Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family, Love, Faith, Tradition
