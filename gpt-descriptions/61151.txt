Synopsis:
In 'Good Form and Christian Etiquette' by Henry, S. M. I., readers are introduced to a world where manners and social graces are of utmost importance. The story follows a young woman named Emily as she navigates the intricacies of high society, striving to maintain her reputation and adhere to the strict rules of etiquette. However, as Emily delves deeper into this world, she begins to question the true meaning behind these societal expectations and the impact they have on her own happiness. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the fine line between conformity and authenticity, and the power of individuality in a world obsessed with appearances.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Etiquette, Society, Identity, Individuality
