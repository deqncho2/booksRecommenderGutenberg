Synopsis: In "The Book-Bills of Narcissus: An Account Rendered," Richard Le Gallienne takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of book collecting and the fascinating characters he encounters along the way. Through his witty and insightful observations, Le Gallienne explores the allure of rare books, the thrill of the hunt, and the profound connections that can be forged through a shared love of literature. As he delves into the lives of eccentric collectors, passionate booksellers, and the stories behind treasured volumes, Le Gallienne invites readers to ponder the power of books to shape our lives and the enduring magic of the written word.

Genres: Memoir, Literary Criticism, Biography

Themes: Book collecting, Literature, Human connection, Passion for books
