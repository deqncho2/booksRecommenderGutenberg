Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts in the Blue Ridge; Or, Marooned Among the Moonshiners', a group of adventurous Boy Scouts find themselves in a thrilling and dangerous situation when they become stranded in the mysterious Blue Ridge Mountains. As they navigate through the treacherous terrain, they stumble upon a hidden world of moonshiners and must use their scouting skills to survive and uncover the truth behind the moonshining operation. With danger lurking at every turn, the Boy Scouts must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and bravery to make it out alive.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Survival, Courage, Camaraderie
