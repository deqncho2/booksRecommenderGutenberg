Synopsis: In Henrik Ibsen's "Little Eyolf," a married couple, Alfred and Rita, struggle to come to terms with the tragic loss of their young son, Eyolf. As they navigate their grief, their relationship becomes strained, revealing deep-seated resentments and unfulfilled desires. The arrival of a mysterious visitor further complicates their already fragile emotional state, leading to a series of intense confrontations and revelations. This gripping drama explores the complexities of love, guilt, and the pursuit of personal fulfillment, ultimately questioning the nature of human responsibility and the consequences of our choices.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Grief and loss, Marriage and family dynamics, Desire and fulfillment, Responsibility and consequences
