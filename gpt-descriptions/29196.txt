Synopsis: In 'Mutineer' by Robert Shea, a young naval officer finds himself torn between loyalty and justice when he uncovers a dark secret aboard his ship. As he navigates the treacherous waters of mutiny and betrayal, he must make difficult choices that will test his integrity and challenge his beliefs. With unexpected twists and high-stakes action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Justice, Betrayal
