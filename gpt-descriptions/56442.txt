Synopsis: 

In 'Romantic Cities of Provence', Mona Caird takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting cities of Provence, where love, passion, and beauty intertwine. From the picturesque streets of Aix-en-Provence to the vibrant markets of Avignon, Caird's vivid descriptions transport readers to a world of romance and intrigue. As she explores the history, art, and culture of each city, Caird weaves together tales of forbidden love, secret affairs, and the enduring power of the human heart. With each turn of the page, readers will find themselves falling in love with the charm and allure of Provence, and perhaps even discovering a newfound appreciation for the complexities of love itself.

Genres: Travel, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love, Passion, Beauty, History, Art, Culture
