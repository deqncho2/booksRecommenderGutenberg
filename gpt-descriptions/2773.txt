Synopsis: In 'Fraternity' by John Galsworthy, a chance encounter between two strangers leads to a deep bond that transcends societal barriers. As their friendship grows, they navigate the complexities of class, loyalty, and the pursuit of justice in a world divided by privilege and prejudice. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century England, this gripping tale explores the power of human connection and the lengths one will go to protect those they hold dear.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes: Friendship, Social Inequality, Justice
