Synopsis: In 'Captain Sam: The Boy Scouts of 1814' by George Cary Eggleston, readers are transported to the early 19th century, where they follow the adventures of young Sam, a courageous and resourceful boy scout. Set against the backdrop of the War of 1812, Sam finds himself caught up in a dangerous mission that could change the course of history. With his unwavering determination and the skills he has learned as a scout, Sam must navigate treacherous waters, outsmart enemy spies, and face unexpected challenges. As the tension rises, readers will be captivated by Sam's bravery and quick thinking, eagerly turning the pages to see if he can fulfill his mission and save the day.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Patriotism, Resourcefulness
