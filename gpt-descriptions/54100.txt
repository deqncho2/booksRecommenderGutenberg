Synopsis: In 'Wayfaring Men: A Novel' by Edna Lyall, a group of young men from different walks of life find themselves drawn together by a shared desire for adventure and purpose. As they embark on a journey that takes them across continents and through various trials, they discover the true meaning of friendship, love, and the pursuit of one's dreams. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this captivating novel explores the transformative power of self-discovery and the enduring bonds that can be formed in the most unexpected of circumstances.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Pursuit of dreams
