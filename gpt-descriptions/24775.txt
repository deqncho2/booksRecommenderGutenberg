Synopsis: 
In 'Up the River; or, Yachting on the Mississippi' by Optic, Oliver, embark on an exhilarating adventure along the mighty Mississippi River. Join a group of young friends as they set sail on a luxurious yacht, encountering thrilling challenges and unexpected encounters along the way. From breathtaking landscapes to encounters with mysterious characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie up the river.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Coming of Age
