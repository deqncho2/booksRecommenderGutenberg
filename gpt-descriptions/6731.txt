Synopsis:
'The Duenna: A Comic Opera' by Richard Brinsley Sheridan is a delightful tale filled with mistaken identities, secret romances, and hilarious misunderstandings. Set in 18th century Spain, the story follows the adventures of a young couple, Louisa and Antonio, who are desperately in love but face numerous obstacles in their path to happiness. With the help of a clever duenna, a wise old woman, they navigate through a series of comical situations, all while trying to outwit their disapproving parents and find true love. This opera is a delightful blend of wit, charm, and uproarious laughter that will keep readers entertained from beginning to end.

Genres: Comedy, Opera, Romance

Themes: Love and romance, Deception and disguise, Social class and expectations
