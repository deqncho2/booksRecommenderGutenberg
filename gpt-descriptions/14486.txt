Synopsis: In the remote wilderness of Montana, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change her life forever. As she delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the legendary Thunder Bird, she must navigate treacherous paths, face unexpected alliances, and confront her own inner demons. With each step closer to the truth, the stakes rise higher, and she must decide how far she is willing to go to uncover the secrets that lie within the shadows.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, self-discovery, the power of legends and folklore
