Synopsis: In 'Tales from the German. Volume II.', author C. F. van der Velde presents a collection of captivating and mysterious stories that will transport readers to the enchanting world of German folklore. From haunted castles and vengeful spirits to forbidden love and supernatural encounters, each tale is filled with suspense, intrigue, and unexpected twists. As the pages turn, readers will find themselves immersed in a realm where reality and fantasy intertwine, leaving them eager to uncover the secrets that lie within each story.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: Mystery, Supernatural, Love and Loss
