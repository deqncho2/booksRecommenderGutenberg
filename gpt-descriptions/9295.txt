Synopsis: In 'A Night Out' by Peple, Edward, a group of friends embarks on a seemingly ordinary night out in the city. However, as the evening progresses, they find themselves caught in a web of unexpected events and encounters that challenge their perceptions of reality. With each twist and turn, the friends must navigate through mysterious occurrences, hidden secrets, and personal revelations, all while trying to unravel the truth behind their extraordinary night out.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Friendship, Secrets and Revelations
