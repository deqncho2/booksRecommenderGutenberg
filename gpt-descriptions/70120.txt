Synopsis: In 'The Blackguard' by Roger Pocock, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges from the shadows, captivating the reader with his dark past and questionable motives. Set against the backdrop of a tumultuous era, this gripping tale follows the Blackguard as he navigates a treacherous world filled with danger, deceit, and unexpected alliances. As secrets unravel and loyalties are tested, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of intrigue, unsure of who to trust and what lies ahead. With each twist and turn, the Blackguard's true nature is slowly revealed, leaving the reader questioning their own perceptions of right and wrong. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Morality, Betrayal
