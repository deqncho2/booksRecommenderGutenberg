Synopsis: In the midst of Scotland's struggle for independence, a young warrior named William Wallace emerges as a symbol of hope and defiance against English tyranny. As he rallies his fellow Scots to fight for their freedom, Wallace faces treachery, love, and unimaginable loss, ultimately becoming a legendary figure in Scottish history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Betrayal, Love, Freedom
