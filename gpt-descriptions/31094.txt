Synopsis: In 'Bear Trap' by Alan Edward Nourse, a group of scientists embarks on a dangerous expedition to a remote island in search of a legendary creature. As they delve deeper into the island's mysteries, they soon find themselves trapped in a deadly game of survival, where the line between predator and prey becomes blurred. With each passing moment, the tension rises, and the true nature of the island's inhabitants is revealed. Will they be able to escape the clutches of the bear trap that awaits them?

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Man vs. Nature
