Synopsis: In 'Milton' by John Cann Bailey, a mysterious and enigmatic character named Milton takes center stage, captivating the reader with his intriguing personality and hidden past. As the story unfolds, Milton's actions and motivations become increasingly puzzling, leaving the reader questioning his true nature and the secrets he holds. Set against a backdrop of suspense and uncertainty, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth about Milton.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Redemption
