Synopsis: 
'They Who Knock at Our Gates: A Complete Gospel of Immigration' by Mary Antin is a captivating exploration of the immigrant experience in America. Through a series of poignant and personal anecdotes, Antin takes readers on a journey that delves into the challenges, triumphs, and complexities faced by those who leave their homelands in search of a better life. From the initial excitement of arrival to the struggles of assimilation, this book offers a unique perspective on the immigrant narrative, shedding light on the hopes, dreams, and sacrifices that shape the lives of newcomers to a foreign land.

Genres: Autobiography, Memoir, Social Commentary

Themes: Immigration, Identity, Assimilation, Cultural Diversity
