Synopsis: In "Looking Back" by Sir Seymour John Fortescue, a renowned historian takes us on a captivating journey through time, delving into the hidden secrets and untold stories of the past. From ancient civilizations to pivotal moments in history, this enthralling collection of essays offers a fresh perspective on well-known events and introduces lesser-known tales that will leave readers spellbound. As Fortescue weaves together historical facts, personal anecdotes, and thought-provoking analysis, he invites us to reflect on the impact of the past on our present and future. Prepare to be transported to different eras and discover the fascinating intricacies that shaped our world.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Essay collection

Themes: History, Memory, Perspective, Legacy
