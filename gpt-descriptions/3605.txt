Synopsis: 
In 'On the Firing Line', a young journalist named Betty is determined to make a name for herself in the male-dominated world of newspaper reporting. When she is assigned to cover a high-profile murder trial, Betty finds herself caught in a web of corruption, deceit, and danger. As she digs deeper into the case, she uncovers shocking secrets that could not only jeopardize her career but also put her life at risk. With each twist and turn, Betty must navigate the treacherous waters of journalism and confront the dark underbelly of society, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the murder. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Gender inequality, Corruption, Pursuit of truth
