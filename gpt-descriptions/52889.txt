Synopsis: 

In the fourth volume of "The Chronicles of Enguerrand de Monstrelet," readers are transported to a tumultuous period in French history. The narrative delves into the brutal civil wars between the houses of Orleans and Burgundy, as well as the English occupation of Paris and Normandy. Enguerrand de Monstrelet vividly recounts the expulsion of the English from these territories and chronicles other significant events that unfolded in the kingdom of France and beyond. With a keen eye for detail and a captivating storytelling style, Monstrelet offers a riveting account of this pivotal era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Chronicle, Memoir

Themes: War and conflict, Political power struggles, National identity, Historical events and their impact
