Synopsis: In the Second Edition of A Discovery Concerning Ghosts: With a Rap at the "Spirit-Rappers," George Cruikshank takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of the supernatural. Through a series of intriguing anecdotes and illustrations, Cruikshank explores the phenomenon of ghosts and challenges the claims of "spirit-rappers." As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the spirit world, he uncovers surprising truths and presents a thought-provoking perspective on the existence of ghosts. This engaging and witty work will leave readers questioning their beliefs and craving for more.

Genres: Non-fiction, Supernatural, Humor

Themes: Ghosts, Supernatural phenomena, Skepticism, Belief
