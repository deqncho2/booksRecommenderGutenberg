Synopsis: 

'Games and Songs of American Children' takes readers on a delightful journey through the playful world of American children. From skipping rope rhymes to hand-clapping games, this captivating collection explores the cherished traditions and imaginative games that have been passed down through generations. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to a world filled with laughter, creativity, and the joy of childhood. 

Genres: Children's Literature, Folklore, Cultural Studies

Themes: Tradition, Playfulness, Cultural Heritage
