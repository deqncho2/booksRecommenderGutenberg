Synopsis: In "Clara A. Swain, M.D." by Hoskins, Robert, Mrs., readers are introduced to the remarkable life of Clara A. Swain, a pioneering female physician in the late 19th century. This captivating biography takes us on a journey through Swain's struggles, triumphs, and the barriers she faced as she pursued her passion for medicine. From her humble beginnings to her groundbreaking achievements, the story of Clara A. Swain is one of determination, resilience, and the relentless pursuit of her dreams.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Gender equality, Perseverance, Medical advancements
