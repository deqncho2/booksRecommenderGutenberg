Synopsis: In "The Arm-Chair at the Inn," a group of international travelers gather at a quaint inn in a small French village, sharing their captivating stories and experiences from around the world. From a daring escape from a prison in Siberia to a mysterious love affair in the heart of Africa, each tale unfolds with intrigue, adventure, and unexpected twists. As the travelers bond over their shared love for exploration and the human spirit, they discover that the true essence of life lies in the connections we make and the stories we tell.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Human connection, Storytelling
