Synopsis: In 'The Parochial History of Cornwall, Volume 2 (of 4)', the reader is transported to the captivating world of Cornwall, where ancient legends, mysterious ruins, and hidden treasures await. This volume delves deeper into the rich history and folklore of the region, unveiling tales of love, betrayal, and redemption. From the haunting legends of lost souls to the secrets buried within the rugged landscapes, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through the hidden corners of Cornwall. As the stories unfold, the reader becomes immersed in the lives of the characters, their struggles, and their triumphs, all set against the backdrop of this enchanting land.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Folklore

Themes: History and heritage, Legends and folklore, Secrets and mysteries
