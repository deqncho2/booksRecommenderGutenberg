Synopsis: In 'The Secret of the Sundial: Madge Sterling Series, #3', Madge Sterling finds herself embroiled in a mysterious adventure when she discovers an ancient sundial with a hidden secret. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Madge uncovers a web of secrets, codes, and hidden treasures that lead her on a thrilling journey through time and danger. With the help of her loyal friends, Madge must decipher the clues and unravel the truth before it's too late. Will she be able to unlock the secret of the sundial and save the day?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden mysteries, Friendship and loyalty, Time travel and exploration
