Synopsis: In the enchanting novel "Summer of Love" by Joyce Kilmer, a young woman named Lily embarks on a transformative journey during a summer filled with unexpected adventures and profound self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque coastal town, Lily finds herself caught between the allure of a mysterious stranger and the comfort of her familiar life. As she navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and personal growth, Lily's summer becomes a pivotal turning point that will forever change her perspective on life and the power of following one's heart.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Self-discovery, Embracing change, Friendship, Nostalgia
