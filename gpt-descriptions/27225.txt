Synopsis: In 'A Woman's Will' by Anne Warner, a young woman named Elizabeth finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. When she inherits a substantial fortune, she is faced with the choice of conforming to the traditional role of a woman or forging her own path. As Elizabeth navigates the complexities of love, family, and independence, she must confront the limitations placed upon her by society and ultimately discover what it truly means to follow her own will.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Gender roles, Independence, Love and relationships
