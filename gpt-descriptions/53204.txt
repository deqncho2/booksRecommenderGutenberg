Synopsis: 

Incaland: A Story of Adventure in the Interior of Peru and the Closing Chapters of the War with Chile takes readers on a thrilling journey through the unexplored landscapes of Peru. Set during the closing chapters of the War with Chile, the story follows a group of adventurers who find themselves in the heart of the Peruvian jungle, facing treacherous terrain, ancient mysteries, and unexpected dangers. As they navigate through the lush wilderness, they uncover hidden secrets that could change the course of the war and alter the fate of nations. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Loyalty, War
