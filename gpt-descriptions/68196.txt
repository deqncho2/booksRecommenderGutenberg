Synopsis: In 'Alien' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a shocking truth that challenges everything he thought he knew about the universe. With the fate of mankind hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous web of secrets and deception to protect his discovery from those who would use it for nefarious purposes.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Ethical dilemmas, Human ambition and its consequences
