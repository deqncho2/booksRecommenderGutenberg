Synopsis: In 'Monopoly' by Phillips, Vic and Roberts, Scott, a group of friends stumble upon a mysterious board game that promises unimaginable wealth and power. As they delve deeper into the game, they soon realize that it holds a dark secret, and their lives are forever changed. With high stakes and unexpected twists, 'Monopoly' takes readers on a thrilling journey where greed, manipulation, and the pursuit of power collide.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Greed, Power, Manipulation
