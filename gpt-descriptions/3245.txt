Synopsis: In the remote wilderness of the Selkirks, a group of friends embarks on a thrilling adventure that will test their courage, loyalty, and survival skills. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected dangers, they must confront their deepest fears and rely on each other to make it out alive. With breathtaking descriptions of the rugged landscape and heart-pounding action, "Black Rock: A Tale of the Selkirks" is a gripping tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Nature's Power
