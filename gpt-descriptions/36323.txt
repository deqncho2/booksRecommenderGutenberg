Synopsis: In 'Two Boys of the Battleship; Or, For the Honor of Uncle Sam' by Frank V. Webster, two young boys find themselves on an unexpected adventure aboard a battleship. As they navigate the challenges of life at sea, they uncover a secret that could have far-reaching consequences. With danger lurking around every corner, the boys must rely on their courage and resourcefulness to protect the honor of Uncle Sam.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Patriotism, Friendship, Loyalty
