Synopsis: In 'Mr. World and Miss Church-Member: A Twentieth Century Allegory', the lives of two seemingly ordinary individuals collide, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. As Mr. World, a charismatic and enigmatic figure, challenges Miss Church-Member's beliefs and values, she is forced to confront the complexities of her faith and the true nature of the world around her. With thought-provoking twists and turns, this allegorical tale explores the power of faith, the search for truth, and the transformative potential of unexpected encounters.

Genres: Allegory, Fiction, Spiritual

Themes: Faith, Truth, Transformation
