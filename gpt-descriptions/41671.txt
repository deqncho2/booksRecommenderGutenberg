Synopsis: In 'Double Challenge' by Jim Kjelgaard, a young boy named Dave faces a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. With his loyal dog, he embarks on a dangerous journey through the wilderness, encountering unexpected obstacles and formidable adversaries. As Dave's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test, he must rely on his wits and courage to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Resilience
