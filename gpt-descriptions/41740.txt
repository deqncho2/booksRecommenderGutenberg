Synopsis: In "Ralph Wilton's Weird" by Alexander, Mrs., a young man named Ralph Wilton finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a strange and enigmatic secret society. As he delves deeper into their world, Ralph becomes entangled in a series of bizarre events and encounters eccentric characters who challenge his perception of reality. With each twist and turn, Ralph's journey takes him closer to uncovering the truth behind the society's hidden agenda, but at what cost?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden societies, Perception vs. reality
