Synopsis: 
'The Art & Practice of Typography: A Manual of American Printing' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the fascinating world of typography, providing a historical overview and showcasing the work of early masters of the craft. Edmund Gress takes readers on a journey through the evolution of typography, from its humble beginnings to the modern era, offering practical discussions and extensive demonstrations of typefaces and methods of arrangement. This manual serves as an invaluable resource for anyone interested in the art of printing and the power of typography.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Design

Themes: Typography, Printing history, Craftsmanship
