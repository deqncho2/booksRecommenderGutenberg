Synopsis: In 'The House of Defence v. 1' by E. F. Benson, a mysterious and opulent mansion becomes the setting for a thrilling tale of secrets, intrigue, and unexpected alliances. As the inhabitants of the house navigate their complex relationships and hidden agendas, a series of strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena begin to unfold, leaving the reader questioning the true nature of the house and its inhabitants. With its atmospheric setting and gripping narrative, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the mysteries that lie within the House of Defence.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets, Intrigue, Supernatural, Identity
