Synopsis: In 'The Lifeboat' by R. M. Ballantyne, a group of survivors find themselves stranded on a lifeboat after their ship sinks in the middle of the ocean. As they struggle to survive with limited resources and face the harsh realities of their situation, tensions rise and alliances are tested. With danger lurking in every wave, the passengers must confront their deepest fears and make difficult choices that will determine their fate.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Morality, Human Nature, Sacrifice
