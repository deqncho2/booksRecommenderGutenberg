Synopsis: 
In 'Little Jack Rabbit and Mr. Wicked Wolf' by Cory, David, a mischievous rabbit named Jack finds himself face to face with the notorious Mr. Wicked Wolf. As Jack tries to outsmart the cunning wolf, a thrilling game of cat and mouse ensues, filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will Jack Rabbit be able to outwit his dangerous adversary, or will he fall victim to Mr. Wicked Wolf's sinister plans?

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Bravery
