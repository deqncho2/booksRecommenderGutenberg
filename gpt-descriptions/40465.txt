Synopsis: In 'Just Irish' by Charles Battell Loomis, a young American man finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and romance during his travels in Ireland. As he delves deeper into the country's rich history and folklore, he uncovers secrets that could change his life forever. With captivating characters and a captivating setting, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Heritage, Adventure
