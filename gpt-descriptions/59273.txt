Synopsis: In "Discovering "Evelina": An Old-fashioned Romance," author Frank Frankfort Moore takes readers on a captivating journey through the pages of a forgotten novel. As the protagonist delves into the world of "Evelina," a classic romance, he becomes enthralled by the story and its characters. However, as he uncovers the secrets behind the novel's creation, he finds himself drawn into a web of mystery and intrigue. With each turn of the page, the lines between reality and fiction blur, leading to unexpected revelations and a deeper understanding of love, passion, and the power of storytelling.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love, Identity, Literature, Secrets
