Synopsis: 
In 'Aunt Jane's Nieces', three young girls find themselves unexpectedly inheriting a fortune from their wealthy Aunt Jane. As they navigate their newfound wealth and the challenges that come with it, they embark on a journey of self-discovery, friendship, and adventure. With their unique personalities and individual dreams, the girls must learn to navigate the complexities of their new lives while staying true to themselves and the values instilled in them by their beloved aunt. 

Genres: 
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Family

Themes: 
Inheritance, Self-discovery, Friendship
