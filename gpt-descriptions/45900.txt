Synopsis: 

'Record of Medals of Honor issued to the officers and enlisted men of the United States Navy, Marine Corps and Coast Guard, 1862-1923' is a comprehensive compilation that unveils the extraordinary acts of bravery and heroism displayed by the men serving in the United States Navy, Marine Corps, and Coast Guard during a significant period in American history. This meticulously documented record showcases the awe-inspiring stories of individuals who went above and beyond the call of duty, risking their lives to protect their country and fellow servicemen. From daring rescues at sea to valiant battles on land, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the remarkable valor exhibited by these unsung heroes.

Genres: Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Bravery, Heroism, Sacrifice
