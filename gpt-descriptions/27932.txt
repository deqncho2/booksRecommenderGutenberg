Synopsis: In "History of the Division of Medical Sciences," Sami Khalaf Hamarneh takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of medical sciences in the United States. From the early days of the United States National Museum to the advancements made in the field of medicine, this bulletin sheds light on the fascinating history of medical research and its impact on society. Hamarneh's meticulous research and engaging storytelling make this a must-read for anyone interested in the development of medical sciences.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Science history

Themes: Evolution of medical sciences, Impact of medical research on society
