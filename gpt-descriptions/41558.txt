Synopsis: 
In 'Dorothy Dale's Great Secret' by Margaret Penrose, Dorothy and her friends find themselves caught up in a mysterious secret that could change their lives forever. As they embark on a thrilling adventure, they must navigate through unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the great secret. With danger lurking around every corner, Dorothy and her friends must rely on their courage and resourcefulness to solve the puzzle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Secrets
