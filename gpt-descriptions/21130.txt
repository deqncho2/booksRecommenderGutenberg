Synopsis: 
'Book of Wise Sayings: Selected Largely from Eastern Sources' is a captivating collection of timeless wisdom and profound insights gathered from various Eastern cultures. In this thought-provoking compilation, W. A. Clouston presents a treasure trove of ancient proverbs, fables, and anecdotes that offer invaluable guidance for navigating the complexities of life. From the teachings of Confucius to the wisdom of Sufi mystics, this book takes readers on a journey through the rich tapestry of Eastern philosophy, illuminating the path to self-discovery and enlightenment.

Genres: 
Philosophy, Self-help, Cultural Studies

Themes: 
Wisdom, Life Lessons, Cultural Diversity
