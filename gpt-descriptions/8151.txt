Synopsis: 
In 'Miss Merivale's Mistake' by Clarke, Henry, Mrs., a young woman named Miss Merivale finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she becomes entangled with a mysterious stranger. As she navigates through a world of hidden motives and unexpected twists, Miss Merivale must confront her own desires and make choices that will determine her future. With its captivating narrative and intriguing characters, this novel will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and betrayal, Identity and self-discovery
