Synopsis: In 'Brotherly House' by Grace S. Richmond, a young woman named Eleanor finds herself caught in a web of family secrets and hidden desires when she moves into her deceased uncle's house. As she delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding her uncle's life, Eleanor uncovers shocking revelations that challenge her perception of her own family. With each discovery, she becomes entangled in a complex web of love, betrayal, and redemption that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Hidden desires, Redemption
