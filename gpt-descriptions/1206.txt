Synopsis: 
When Andy Green arrives at the Flying U Ranch, he expects a peaceful summer of cowpunching and camaraderie. Little does he know that his arrival will set off a chain of events that will test his mettle and challenge the very fabric of the ranch. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, Andy must navigate the treacherous landscape of loyalty, love, and betrayal. With danger lurking around every corner, the Flying U Ranch becomes a battleground where friendships are forged and broken, and where the true nature of the human spirit is put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Betrayal, Survival
