Synopsis: 
'Anecdotes about Authors, and Artists' by Timbs, John is a captivating collection of intriguing and lesser-known stories about famous authors and artists. From the eccentric habits of renowned writers to the unexpected inspirations behind famous paintings, this book delves into the fascinating lives and creative processes of these influential figures. Each anecdote offers a glimpse into the personal and professional lives of these literary and artistic icons, leaving readers eager to discover more about their hidden quirks and untold stories.

Genres: Biography, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Creativity, Inspiration, Quirks, Artistic Process
