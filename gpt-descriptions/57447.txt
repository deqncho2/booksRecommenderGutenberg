Synopsis: In 'The Romance of a Shop' by Amy Levy, two sisters, Rachel and Fanny, embark on a daring adventure as they open their own shop in Victorian London. As they navigate the challenges of running a business in a male-dominated society, they find themselves entangled in a web of love, ambition, and societal expectations. With their determination and resilience, the sisters must confront their own desires and the sacrifices they are willing to make for success and happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Feminist Literature

Themes: Gender roles, Ambition, Love and Relationships, Societal Expectations
