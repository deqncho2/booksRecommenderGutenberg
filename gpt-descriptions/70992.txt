Synopsis: 

'Escape from East Tennessee to the federal lines' is a captivating memoir written by Captain R. A. Ragan, recounting his personal experiences during the American Civil War from 1861 to 1864. The story follows Captain Ragan's daring escape from East Tennessee, a region deeply divided by the war, to seek refuge with the federal forces. Faced with numerous challenges and dangers, the captain's journey becomes a thrilling tale of survival, resilience, and determination. As he navigates through enemy territory, encounters with Confederate soldiers, and treacherous landscapes, Captain Ragan's story offers a unique perspective on the war and the individual struggles faced by those caught in its midst.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: War and Conflict, Survival, Bravery, Loyalty
