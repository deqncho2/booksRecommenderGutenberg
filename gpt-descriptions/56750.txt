Synopsis: In "Tarry thou till I come; or, Salathiel, the wandering Jew," George Croly takes us on a captivating journey through time and history. The story follows Salathiel, a man cursed with immortality, condemned to wander the earth until the second coming of Christ. From ancient Jerusalem to the Roman Empire, from the Crusades to the French Revolution, Salathiel witnesses the rise and fall of empires, the triumphs and tragedies of humanity, and the enduring power of hope. As he navigates through the ages, Salathiel grapples with his own immortality, the weight of his past, and the ultimate purpose of his existence.

Genres: Historical fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Immortality, Redemption, Faith, Human nature, Historical events
