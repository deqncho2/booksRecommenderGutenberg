Synopsis: In 'Pink Gods and Blue Demons' by Cynthia Stockley, a young woman named Amelia finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden world of ancient gods and supernatural beings. As she delves deeper into this realm, Amelia must navigate treacherous alliances, uncover long-buried secrets, and confront her own inner demons. With the fate of both worlds hanging in the balance, Amelia must harness her own hidden powers and make a choice that will change her life forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Power, Redemption
