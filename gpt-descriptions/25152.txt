Synopsis: In 'All for a Scrap of Paper: A Romance of the Present War', Joseph Hocking takes readers on a thrilling journey through the chaos and uncertainty of war. Set during the tumultuous period of the First World War, the story follows the lives of two individuals from different backgrounds who find themselves caught up in the conflict. As they navigate the horrors of war, their paths intertwine, leading to unexpected alliances, heart-wrenching sacrifices, and a love that defies all odds. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, Hocking explores the profound impact of war on individuals and society, highlighting the resilience of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and sacrifice, the human cost of war, resilience and hope
