Synopsis: 
'Old English Poems: Translated into the Original Meter Together with Short Selections from Old English Prose' is a captivating collection that brings to life the beauty and richness of Old English literature. This anthology features a selection of Old English poems and prose, skillfully translated into the original meter, allowing readers to experience the lyrical and rhythmic qualities of the language. From epic tales of heroic warriors to poignant elegies and enchanting riddles, these ancient texts offer a glimpse into the cultural and literary heritage of the Anglo-Saxon period. Immerse yourself in the world of Old English poetry and prose, and discover the timeless themes and captivating narratives that have influenced literature for centuries.

Genres: Poetry, Prose, Translation

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Love, Nature, Riddles, Cultural Heritage
