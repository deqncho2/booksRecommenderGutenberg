Synopsis: In 'Friends' by Gibson, Wilfrid Wilson, a chance encounter between two strangers sparks an unexpected friendship that transcends societal boundaries. As their bond deepens, they navigate through the complexities of life, love, and personal growth, ultimately discovering the transformative power of true friendship. Set against the backdrop of a bustling city, this captivating tale explores the profound impact that connections with others can have on our lives.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Social class, Personal growth
