Synopsis: In "Roy Blakeley: His Story" by Percy Keese Fitzhugh, readers are introduced to the mischievous and adventurous Roy Blakeley. As he recounts his thrilling escapades with his friends in the East Side Boys' Club, Roy's storytelling takes readers on a rollercoaster of laughter, suspense, and unexpected twists. From secret missions to solving mysteries, Roy's witty and humorous narrative will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what hilarious and daring adventure awaits next.

Genres: Adventure, Humor, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Imagination
