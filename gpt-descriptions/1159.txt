Synopsis: In 'Fire-Tongue' by Rohmer, Sax, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is called upon to investigate a series of bizarre and seemingly supernatural events. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a sinister secret society and a powerful figure known only as "Fire-Tongue." With his wit and intellect pushed to the limit, Sax must navigate a treacherous world of deception and intrigue to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Secret societies, Supernatural elements, Detective work
