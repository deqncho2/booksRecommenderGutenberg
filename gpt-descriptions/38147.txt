Synopsis: In 'St. Peter, His Name and His Office, as Set Forth in Holy Scripture', T. W. Allies delves into the intriguing and often misunderstood figure of St. Peter, exploring his name, his role, and his significance as portrayed in the pages of the Holy Scripture. Through meticulous analysis and compelling arguments, Allies sheds light on the profound impact that St. Peter had on the early Christian community, unraveling the layers of his character and the depth of his authority. This thought-provoking work invites readers to reconsider their understanding of St. Peter and his pivotal role in shaping the foundations of Christianity.

Genres: Religious Studies, Biblical Studies, Theology

Themes: Authority, Leadership, Apostolic Succession, Early Christianity
