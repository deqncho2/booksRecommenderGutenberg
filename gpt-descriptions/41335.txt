Synopsis: In "Automatic Pistol Shooting: Together with Information on Handling the Duelling Pistol and Revolver," Walter Winans takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of firearms, exploring the art and science of shooting with precision and skill. From the intricacies of handling different types of pistols to mastering the techniques of duelling and revolver shooting, Winans provides a comprehensive guide that will captivate both novice and experienced shooters alike. With a blend of historical anecdotes, practical advice, and expert insights, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the art of pistol shooting.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, History

Themes: Firearms, Shooting techniques, Skill development
