Synopsis: In 'The Englishman from Paris' by Arthur Murphy, a mysterious Englishman arrives in Paris, captivating the locals with his charm and enigmatic past. As he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue, he must navigate the treacherous world of 18th-century France, where danger lurks around every corner. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal
