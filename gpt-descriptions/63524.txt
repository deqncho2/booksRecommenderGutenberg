Synopsis: In 'The Silver Plague' by Albert dePina, a mysterious and deadly disease sweeps across the world, leaving chaos and devastation in its wake. As the protagonist, Dr. Emily Carter, races against time to find a cure, she uncovers a shocking conspiracy that threatens not only her own life but the fate of humanity. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Medical Ethics
