Synopsis: 
'Finger plays for nursery and kindergarten' by Emilie Poulsson is a delightful collection of interactive rhymes and finger plays that will captivate young children and ignite their imagination. From counting games to animal adventures, this book offers a wide range of finger plays that encourage active participation and promote early learning. Each finger play is accompanied by charming illustrations that bring the rhymes to life, making it a perfect resource for parents, teachers, and caregivers to engage and entertain little ones. 

Genres: Children's Literature, Poetry, Education

Themes: Early Learning, Imagination, Interactive Play
