Synopsis: 
In "An Almond for a Parrot: Being a reply to Martin Mar-Prelate," the reader is transported to 17th century England, where Tully Truegood finds herself imprisoned for murder. As she awaits her fate, Tully recounts her extraordinary life filled with passion, desire, and secrets. From her upbringing in a brothel to her involvement in the occult, Tully's story is a captivating blend of eroticism, mystery, and intrigue that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical fiction, Erotica, Mystery

Themes: Sexuality, Power, Deception
