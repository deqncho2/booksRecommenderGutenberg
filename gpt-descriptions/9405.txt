Synopsis: 
'The Book of Old English Ballads' by George Wharton Edwards is a captivating collection of traditional English ballads that transport readers to a bygone era. From tales of love and betrayal to stories of heroism and adventure, this anthology brings to life the rich tapestry of Old English folklore. Each ballad is beautifully illustrated, enhancing the enchanting experience of diving into these timeless narratives. Whether it's the haunting melody of a tragic romance or the thrilling exploits of legendary figures, this book offers a delightful journey into the heart of Old English culture.

Genres: Poetry, Folklore, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Heroism and Adventure, Tradition and Culture
