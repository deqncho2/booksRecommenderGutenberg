Synopsis: 
'A British Rifle Man: The Journals and Correspondence of Major George Simmons, Rifle Brigade, During the Peninsular War and the Campaign of Waterloo' offers a captivating firsthand account of Major George Simmons' experiences as a soldier during the Peninsular War and the Campaign of Waterloo. Through his journals and correspondence, Simmons provides a vivid and intimate portrayal of the challenges, triumphs, and camaraderie that defined his time on the battlefield. From the brutal realities of war to the moments of courage and resilience, this book offers a unique perspective on the Napoleonic era and the indomitable spirit of the British Rifle Brigade.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History, Memoir

Themes: War and Conflict, Heroism, Camaraderie, Resilience
