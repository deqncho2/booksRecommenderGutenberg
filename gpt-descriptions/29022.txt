Synopsis: 
In 'Mr. Punch Awheel: The Humours of Motoring and Cycling', embark on a delightful journey through the early days of motoring and cycling, as seen through the mischievous eyes of Mr. Punch. This collection of humorous and satirical sketches takes readers on a rollicking adventure filled with comical mishaps, eccentric characters, and unexpected twists. From chaotic road trips to hilarious encounters with peculiar cyclists, this book offers a whimsical and entertaining exploration of the joys and follies of these popular pastimes.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Adventure

Themes: Early motoring and cycling, Comedy, Social commentary
