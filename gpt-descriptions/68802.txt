Synopsis: In 'Invincible Minnie' by Elisabeth Sanxay Holding, a young woman named Minnie finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she becomes the sole witness to a shocking crime. As she tries to navigate the treacherous path of truth and deception, Minnie's determination and resilience are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and a relentless pursuit of justice, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Perseverance, Justice, Deception
