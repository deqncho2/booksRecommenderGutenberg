Synopsis: In "Samantha at the World's Fair" by Marietta Holley, Samantha, a witty and independent woman, embarks on a thrilling adventure at the World's Fair. As she navigates through the grand exhibits and encounters fascinating characters, Samantha's quick wit and sharp observations bring a unique perspective to the wonders of the fair. With her trademark humor and charm, Samantha's journey unfolds, filled with unexpected twists and delightful surprises.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Humor

Themes: Adventure, Independence, Feminism
