Synopsis: In the second volume of the captivating Miser Farebrother series, the enigmatic and wealthy protagonist finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and deceit. As his past begins to unravel, Miser Farebrother must navigate treacherous waters to protect his fortune and reputation. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Wealth and power, Deception and betrayal, Redemption
