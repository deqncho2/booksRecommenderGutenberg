Synopsis: In 'A Thane of Wessex: Being a Story of the Great Viking Raids into Somerset', Charles W. Whistler takes readers on a thrilling journey back to the time of the Viking raids in Somerset. The story follows the life of a brave thane, who finds himself caught in the midst of the violent clashes between the Vikings and the people of Wessex. As the raids intensify, the thane must navigate treacherous alliances, face personal sacrifices, and confront his own inner demons. With vivid descriptions of battles, political intrigue, and the clash of cultures, this historical adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Bravery, Loyalty, Cultural Conflict, Survival
