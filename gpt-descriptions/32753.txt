Synopsis: 
In the exclusive world of the Newport Croquet Club, where the elite gather to play the refined sport of croquet, a scandalous secret threatens to unravel the carefully constructed facade of high society. As the members compete for the coveted championship title, hidden alliances, forbidden romances, and shocking betrayals come to light, revealing the dark underbelly of this seemingly innocent game. With each swing of the mallet, the stakes rise higher, and the players must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and ambition. Will the truth be exposed, or will the secrets of the Newport Croquet Club remain buried forever?

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Deception, Power, Class divide
