Synopsis: 

In 'On the Construction of Catalogues of Libraries and Their Publication by Means of Separate, Stereotyped Titles: With Rules and Examples', Charles C. Jewett delves into the fascinating world of library catalogues and their publication. This comprehensive guide explores the intricacies of creating catalogues, providing valuable insights and practical examples for librarians and bibliophiles alike. Jewett's meticulous attention to detail and his innovative approach to organizing information make this work an indispensable resource for anyone interested in the art of cataloguing.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Library Science

Themes: Cataloguing, Information Organization, Library Management
