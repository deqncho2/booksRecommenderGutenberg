Synopsis: In 'Flight Through Tomorrow', a brilliant scientist invents a revolutionary time-travel device that propels a group of individuals into a future filled with astonishing technological advancements and unforeseen dangers. As they navigate this unfamiliar world, they must confront the ethical implications of their newfound power and grapple with the consequences of altering the course of history. With thrilling twists and thought-provoking dilemmas, this captivating science fiction novel takes readers on an exhilarating journey through time and space.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Adventure

Themes: Ethics, Technological Advancement, Consequences of Actions
