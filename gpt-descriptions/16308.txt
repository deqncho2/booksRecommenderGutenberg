Synopsis: In 'How Deacon Tubman and Parson Whitney Kept New Year's: And Other Stories', Murray takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of heartwarming and humorous tales. From the misadventures of Deacon Tubman and Parson Whitney on New Year's Eve to the unexpected encounters of various characters, each story is filled with delightful surprises and valuable life lessons. With wit and charm, Murray explores the complexities of human nature, the power of friendship, and the importance of embracing the joy and humor in everyday life.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Human Nature, Joy and Humor
