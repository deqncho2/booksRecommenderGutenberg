Synopsis: 
Godfrey Morgan, a young and wealthy Californian, embarks on a seemingly ordinary voyage aboard the Tartar. However, when a series of unexpected events unfold, Godfrey finds himself stranded on a deserted island with his French valet, Tartlet. Together, they must navigate the challenges of survival, encounter peculiar creatures, and unravel the mysteries that surround the island. As they strive to find a way back home, Godfrey's courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test in this thrilling Californian mystery.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Survival

Themes: Exploration, Resilience, Discovery
