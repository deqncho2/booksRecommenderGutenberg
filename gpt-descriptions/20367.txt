Synopsis: In 'The Coming of the King' by Babcock, Bernie, a young man named Alex finds himself thrust into a world of ancient prophecies and hidden powers when he discovers a mysterious artifact. As he embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about his own identity, Alex must navigate a treacherous landscape filled with mythical creatures, dark forces, and unexpected allies. With the fate of the kingdom hanging in the balance, Alex must embrace his destiny and face the ultimate test of courage and sacrifice.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Good vs. Evil, Coming of Age
