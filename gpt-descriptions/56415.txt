Synopsis: In 'Making the Nine' by Albertus T. Dudley, a young prodigy named Ethan embarks on a thrilling journey to join the prestigious Nine, a group of elite warriors who protect the realm from dark forces. As Ethan trains under the guidance of a wise mentor, he discovers hidden powers within himself and uncovers a dangerous conspiracy that threatens the very existence of the Nine. Filled with heart-pounding action, unexpected twists, and a touch of magic, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Coming of age, Power and destiny, Loyalty and betrayal
