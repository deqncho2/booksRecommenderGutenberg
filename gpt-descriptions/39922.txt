Synopsis: In 'Juggernaut: A Veiled Record', a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young woman named Veiled Record embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind her forgotten past. As she delves deeper into her own history, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, lies, and hidden identities. With each revelation, Veiled Record's world is turned upside down, and she must navigate a treacherous path filled with danger and betrayal. As the pieces of her past slowly come together, she discovers that the truth may be more shocking than she ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Memory, Deception, Betrayal
