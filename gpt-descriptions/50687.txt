Synopsis: In 'The History of the Twenty-ninth Regiment of Massachusetts Volunteer Infantry: in the Late War of the Rebellion' by William H. Osborne, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the experiences of a regiment during the American Civil War. Through vivid storytelling and firsthand accounts, the author brings to life the trials and triumphs of these brave soldiers as they navigate the chaos and horrors of war. From the initial enlistment to the final battles, this gripping narrative sheds light on the sacrifices made by these men and the impact they had on the outcome of the war.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Resilience
