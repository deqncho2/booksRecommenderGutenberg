Synopsis: 

In 'Australia at War: A Winter Record Made by Will Dyson on the Somme and at Ypres, During the Campaigns of 1916 and 1917', Will Dyson takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing battlefields of World War I. Through his vivid and poignant illustrations, Dyson captures the raw emotions and experiences of Australian soldiers fighting on the Somme and at Ypres during the brutal winter campaigns of 1916 and 1917. From the trenches to the front lines, Dyson's powerful imagery and accompanying commentary provide a unique and intimate perspective on the horrors of war.

Genres: War, History, Art

Themes: War experiences, Human resilience, The impact of war on individuals and society
