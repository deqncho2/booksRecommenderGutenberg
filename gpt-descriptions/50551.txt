Synopsis: 
'History of Brighthelmston; or, Brighton as I View it and Others Knew It: With a Chronological Table of Local Events' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and ever-evolving town of Brighton. Written by John Ackerson Erredge, this historical account offers a unique perspective on the city, combining personal observations with the recollections of others who have experienced its transformation. From its humble beginnings as a fishing village to its rise as a fashionable seaside resort, the book delves into the fascinating stories, events, and characters that have shaped Brighton's rich history. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Erredge paints a vivid picture of a town that has captivated the hearts and imaginations of generations.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Local history
Themes: Urban development, Cultural transformation, Community identity
