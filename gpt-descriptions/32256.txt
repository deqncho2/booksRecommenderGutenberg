Synopsis: In "The Big Time" by Fritz Leiber, a group of time-traveling entertainers find themselves trapped in a mysterious war outside of time. As they navigate through different eras and dimensions, they must unravel the secrets of their captors and the true nature of the conflict they are caught in. With twists and turns at every corner, this thrilling science fiction novel explores the complexities of time travel and the consequences of meddling with the fabric of reality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Mystery

Themes: War, Identity, Reality vs. Illusion
