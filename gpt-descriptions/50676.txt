Synopsis: In 'The Infidel: A Story of the Great Revival', a young woman named Hester falls in love with a mysterious and enigmatic man who challenges her beliefs and tests the boundaries of her faith. As their relationship unfolds amidst the backdrop of a religious revival, Hester finds herself torn between her devotion to her religion and her growing affection for this captivating stranger. With secrets and hidden agendas at play, the story takes unexpected twists and turns, keeping the reader on the edge of their seat until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Faith and doubt, Love and temptation, Identity and deception
