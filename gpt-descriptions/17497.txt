Synopsis: 
In 'Ole Mammy's Torment' by Annie F. Johnston, readers are transported to the post-Civil War South, where the lives of two young girls, Mary and Betty, are forever changed when they are sent to live with their strict and mysterious Aunt Serena. As they uncover the secrets of their family's past, they find themselves caught in a web of deception, betrayal, and forbidden love. With each revelation, the girls must navigate the complexities of their newfound identities and confront the painful truths that threaten to tear their family apart.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Southern Gothic, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and betrayal
