Synopsis: In 'The Hero of Garside School' by J. Harwood Panting, a mysterious new student arrives at Garside School, capturing the attention of the entire student body. As the protagonist, Tom, befriends this enigmatic figure, he soon discovers that there is more to him than meets the eye. Together, they embark on a thrilling adventure that will test their courage and loyalty, ultimately revealing the true meaning of heroism.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Identity
