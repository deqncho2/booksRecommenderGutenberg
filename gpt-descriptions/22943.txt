Synopsis: In the small English town of Cullerne, a mysterious and ancient coat of arms becomes the center of a gripping tale of secrets, scandal, and suspense. As the town's inhabitants become entangled in a web of deceit and hidden motives, a young architect finds himself drawn into a dangerous game of power and manipulation. With each revelation, the true nature of the nebulous coat is slowly unveiled, leading to shocking consequences that will forever change the lives of those involved.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Historical fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Power and manipulation, Identity and heritage
