Synopsis: In 'Brother Against Brother; or, The Tompkins Mystery: A Story of the Great American Rebellion,' John R. Musick takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous times of the American Civil War. The story revolves around the Tompkins family, torn apart by conflicting loyalties and a mysterious secret that threatens to unravel their lives. As the war rages on, brothers find themselves on opposing sides, leading to heart-wrenching confrontations and unexpected alliances. Amidst the chaos, a gripping mystery unfolds, intertwining the fate of the Tompkins family with the fate of a nation divided.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, War

Themes: Loyalty, Family, Betrayal, Identity, Honor
