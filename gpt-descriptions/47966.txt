Synopsis: Frank Armstrong, a young and ambitious student, embarks on a thrilling journey as he enters college. Faced with new friendships, academic challenges, and unexpected obstacles, Frank must navigate the complexities of campus life while discovering his true passions and purpose. As he delves deeper into the college experience, Frank's journey becomes a transformative exploration of self-discovery, personal growth, and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Bildungsroman

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Friendship, Self-discovery, Personal growth
