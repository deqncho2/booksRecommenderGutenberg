Synopsis: 

In 'Religious Experience and Journal of Mrs. Jarena Lee: Giving an Account of Her Call to Preach the Gospel', we are introduced to the remarkable life of Jarena Lee, an African American woman who defied societal norms and became one of the first female preachers in the early 19th century. Through her captivating narrative, Lee takes us on a journey of self-discovery, faith, and determination as she navigates the challenges and triumphs of her calling to spread the Gospel.

Genres: Autobiography, Religious Literature, African American Literature

Themes: Faith and Spirituality, Gender and Equality, Racial Identity, Social Justice
