Synopsis: In 'Glass' by Edward Dillon, a mysterious artifact is discovered, capable of granting unimaginable power to its possessor. As a group of unlikely allies embarks on a perilous journey to protect it, they must confront their own inner demons and face the consequences of their choices. With twists and turns at every corner, 'Glass' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of power and the lengths one will go to obtain it.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Power, Morality, Redemption
