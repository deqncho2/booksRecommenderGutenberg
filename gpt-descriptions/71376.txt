Synopsis: In "The Strength of Love: Love is Lord of All" by Miller, Alex McVeigh, a young woman finds herself torn between two suitors who represent different paths in life. As she navigates the complexities of love, she discovers the true power and resilience of the human heart. This captivating story explores the depths of emotions, the choices we make, and the transformative nature of love.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and its complexities, Choices and consequences, Self-discovery
