Synopsis: In 'Letter of the Law' by Alan Edward Nourse, a brilliant young lawyer finds himself caught in a moral dilemma when he is assigned to defend a notorious criminal. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers shocking secrets that challenge his own beliefs and force him to question the true meaning of justice. With his reputation and personal values on the line, he must navigate a treacherous legal system and make a decision that could change his life forever.

Genres: Legal thriller, suspense, drama

Themes: Morality, justice, ethics, personal integrity
