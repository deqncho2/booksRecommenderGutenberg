Synopsis: In "Vicksburg" by J. Frank Hanly, a gripping tale unfolds against the backdrop of the American Civil War. Set in the besieged city of Vicksburg, Mississippi, the story follows a diverse group of individuals who find themselves caught in the crossfire of a brutal conflict. As the Union Army closes in, tensions rise, alliances are tested, and the characters must navigate the treacherous landscape of war, facing personal sacrifices and unexpected choices. With vivid descriptions and compelling storytelling, "Vicksburg" takes readers on a journey through the harrowing experiences of those who lived through one of the most pivotal moments in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Survival, Sacrifice, Identity, Resilience
