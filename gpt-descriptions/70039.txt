Synopsis: In 'First Harvests: An Episode in the Life of Mrs. Levison Gower: A Satire Without a Moral', we are introduced to the intriguing world of Mrs. Levison Gower, a woman of high society who finds herself entangled in a web of scandal and deception. As she navigates through the complexities of her social circle, Mrs. Gower's true character is revealed, and the consequences of her actions become increasingly dire. With a cast of colorful characters and a plot filled with unexpected twists and turns, this satirical novel promises to captivate readers with its witty commentary on the hypocrisy and superficiality of the upper class.

Genres: Satire, Drama, Comedy

Themes: Social class, Hypocrisy, Deception
