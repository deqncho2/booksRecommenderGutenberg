Synopsis: In 'The Annals of Willenhall' by Frederick William Hackwood, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious events that threaten to unravel its peaceful existence. As the townspeople grapple with the unexplained occurrences, a young historian delves into the town's dark past, unearthing long-buried secrets and uncovering a sinister conspiracy that spans generations. With each revelation, the historian becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, racing against time to save the town from its own dark history.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of knowledge, the consequences of the past
