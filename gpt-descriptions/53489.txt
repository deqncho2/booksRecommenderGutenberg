Synopsis:
'The Life of Lazarillo de Tormes: His Fortunes & Adversities; with a Notice of the Mendoza Family, a Short Life of the Author, Don Diego Hurtado De Mendoza, a Notice of the Work, and Some Remarks on the Character of Lazarillo de Tormes' is a captivating and thought-provoking novel that takes readers on a journey through the life of a young boy named Lazarillo. Set in 16th-century Spain, this anonymous work follows Lazarillo as he navigates a world filled with poverty, deceit, and unexpected opportunities. Through a series of misfortunes and encounters with various masters, Lazarillo learns to adapt and survive in a society that often overlooks the struggles of the lower class. As the story unfolds, readers are introduced to the influential Mendoza family and gain insight into the life of the author, Don Diego Hurtado De Mendoza. With its richly detailed narrative and complex characters, this novel offers a unique perspective on social hierarchy, morality, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Picaresque novel, Satire, Realism

Themes: Social class, Morality, Survival
