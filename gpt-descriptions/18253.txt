Synopsis: In 'Discovery of Witches: The Wonderfull Discoverie of Witches in the Countie of Lancaster,' Thomas Potts takes us on a gripping journey through the dark and mysterious world of witchcraft in 17th century England. Set in the county of Lancaster, this captivating account follows the shocking events surrounding the infamous Pendle Witch Trials. As Potts delves into the lives of those accused, he uncovers a web of secrets, betrayal, and supernatural powers that will leave readers spellbound. With its vivid descriptions and compelling narrative, this historical document offers a fascinating glimpse into a time when fear and superstition ruled the land.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, True crime, Occult

Themes: Witchcraft, Fear and superstition, Betrayal, Power dynamics
