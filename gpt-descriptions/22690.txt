Synopsis: 
In "Rembrandt and His Works," John Burnet takes readers on a captivating journey into the life and artistic genius of the renowned Dutch painter, Rembrandt. Through a combination of insightful analysis and stunning examples from Rembrandt's etchings, Burnet explores the principles and techniques that made Rembrandt a master of design, light, shade, and color. This book offers a unique opportunity to delve into the mind of one of history's greatest artists and gain a deeper understanding of his artistic process.

Genres: Art History, Biography, Criticism

Themes: Artistic principles, Mastery of technique, Exploration of light and shadow
