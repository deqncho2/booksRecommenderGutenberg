Synopsis: In "Katharine Frensham: A Novel," we follow the journey of a young woman named Katharine as she navigates the complexities of life, love, and self-discovery. Set in the early 20th century, Katharine finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires, leading her on a path of personal growth and unexpected encounters. As she encounters various individuals who challenge her beliefs and values, Katharine must confront her own limitations and redefine her place in the world. With its captivating narrative and compelling characters, this novel takes readers on a thought-provoking exploration of identity, independence, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Self-discovery, Societal expectations, Love, Personal growth
