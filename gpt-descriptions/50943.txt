Synopsis:
In "Rose D'Albret; or, Troublous Times," the reader is transported to 16th century France, a time of political unrest and religious conflict. The story revolves around the captivating Rose D'Albret, a young woman caught in the midst of these turbulent times. As she navigates through a web of treachery, romance, and danger, Rose's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. With her life hanging in the balance, she must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and the fate of those she holds dear.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes:
Political Intrigue, Love and Sacrifice, Religious Conflict
