Synopsis:
In "Benjamin Franklin, Self-Revealed, Volume 1 (of 2): A Biographical and Critical Study Based Mainly on his own Writings," author William Cabell Bruce delves into the life and mind of one of America's most iconic figures. Through an exploration of Franklin's own writings, Bruce uncovers the fascinating and often surprising aspects of Franklin's character, shedding new light on his achievements and motivations. This captivating biography offers readers a unique opportunity to understand the man behind the legend, as Franklin's own words provide a window into his thoughts, struggles, and triumphs.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Enlightenment, American Revolution, Intellectual curiosity
