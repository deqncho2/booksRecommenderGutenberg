Synopsis: 
In 'The Rhesus of Euripides' by Euripides, the story unfolds on the eve of a pivotal battle during the Trojan War. As the Greek army prepares for the ultimate clash, a mysterious and enigmatic figure enters the scene. Rhesus, the valiant and fearless Thracian king, arrives with his army, ready to join the Trojans in their fight against the Greeks. However, his presence raises questions and suspicions among both sides, leading to a series of unexpected events and dramatic confrontations. Amidst the chaos and uncertainty, alliances are tested, loyalties are questioned, and the true nature of heroism is explored. 

Genres: 
Tragedy, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
War, Heroism, Loyalty, Betrayal
