Synopsis: 
In 'The Twin Cousins' by May, Sophie, two cousins, Lily and Emma, discover a hidden family secret that takes them on a thrilling adventure. As they delve deeper into their family's history, they uncover a mysterious connection to a long-lost treasure. With danger lurking around every corner, the cousins must rely on their wit and bravery to unravel the clues and outsmart their adversaries. Along the way, they learn the importance of trust, loyalty, and the power of family bonds. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Family

Themes: Family secrets, Treasure hunting, Bravery, Trust
