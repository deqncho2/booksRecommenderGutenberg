Synopsis: In 'Commodore Barney's Young Spies: A Boy's Story of the Burning of the City of Washington,' author James Otis takes readers on a thrilling journey through the War of 1812. Set in the midst of the British invasion of Washington D.C., the story follows a group of courageous young spies who risk their lives to protect their city and uncover a treacherous plot. As they navigate dangerous missions and encounter unexpected allies and enemies, the young spies must rely on their wit, bravery, and loyalty to save their beloved capital from destruction. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Friendship, Loyalty
