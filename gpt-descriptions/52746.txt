Synopsis: 
In 'Harper's Round Table, January 28, 1896', readers are transported back in time to the late 19th century, where they join a group of young adventurers on a thrilling journey filled with mystery, danger, and unexpected discoveries. As they embark on a quest that takes them across continents and through various challenges, the characters must rely on their wit, courage, and teamwork to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the vivid descriptions, heart-pounding action, and the sense of wonder that permeates this enthralling tale.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
