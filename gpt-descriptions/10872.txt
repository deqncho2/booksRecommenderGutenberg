Synopsis: In 'At Sunwich Port, Part 2', the story continues with the eccentric and humorous adventures of the residents of Sunwich Port. From a mysterious disappearance to a scandalous love affair, the small seaside town is buzzing with excitement. As the characters navigate through unexpected twists and turns, secrets are revealed and relationships are tested. With its delightful blend of wit, charm, and unexpected surprises, this second part of the series promises to keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Humor, Mystery, Fiction

Themes: Small-town life, Love and relationships, Secrets and revelations
