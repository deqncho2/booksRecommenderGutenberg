Synopsis: 
In 'Homesick' by Lyn Venable, a young woman named Emily finds herself trapped in a mysterious house with no memory of how she got there. As she explores the eerie rooms and uncovers hidden secrets, Emily becomes increasingly desperate to escape. With each passing day, the house seems to exert a strange power over her, blurring the lines between reality and illusion. As Emily races against time to unravel the truth, she must confront her deepest fears and confront the darkness that lurks within the walls of the house.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Memory, Fear, Perception
