Synopsis: In 'The Flag' by Greene, Homer, a mysterious flag is discovered in a small town, stirring up a whirlwind of emotions and secrets among the townspeople. As the flag's significance becomes clearer, the characters find themselves caught in a web of loyalty, betrayal, and redemption. With each passing chapter, the reader is drawn deeper into the intricate tapestry of the town's history, where the flag serves as a catalyst for both personal and collective transformation.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Patriotism, Truth, Redemption
