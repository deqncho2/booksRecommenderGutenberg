Synopsis: 
'Jungle Folk: Indian Natural History Sketches' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lush landscapes of India, where the author, Douglas Dewar, unveils the hidden wonders of its diverse wildlife. From the majestic tigers prowling through dense forests to the elusive birds soaring above the treetops, Dewar's vivid descriptions and personal encounters with these creatures bring the Indian natural world to life. Through his keen observations and anecdotes, he not only introduces readers to the fascinating creatures that inhabit the jungles but also sheds light on the delicate balance between humans and nature. 

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Natural History, Travelogue

Themes: 
Wildlife conservation, Biodiversity, Human-nature relationship
