Synopsis: In 'The Meadow-Brook Girls Afloat; Or, the Stormy Cruise of the Red Rover', the adventurous Meadow-Brook Girls embark on a thrilling journey aboard the Red Rover, a magnificent yacht. As they set sail, they encounter unexpected challenges and face a storm that tests their courage and resilience. With their friendship and resourcefulness, the girls navigate through treacherous waters, uncovering secrets and forming new alliances along the way. Will they be able to overcome the storm and find their way back to safety?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resilience
