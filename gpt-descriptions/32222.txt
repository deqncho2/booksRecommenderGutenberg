Synopsis: 

In 'Felicitas: A Tale of the German Migrations: A.D. 476', Felix Dahn takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of the German migrations in the 5th century. The story follows the life of Felicitas, a young Germanic woman who finds herself caught between the clash of cultures and the struggle for power in a rapidly changing world. As she navigates through love, loss, and the complexities of her own identity, Felicitas becomes a witness to the epic events that shape the destiny of her people.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Cultural Clash, Power Struggles
