Synopsis: 
In 'Tales of the supernatural: Six romantic stories' by Platt, James, prepare to be captivated by a collection of enchanting tales that blend romance with the mysterious and otherworldly. From haunted houses to ghostly encounters, each story takes readers on a thrilling journey where love and the supernatural intertwine. Delve into the lives of intriguing characters as they navigate the complexities of relationships while facing the inexplicable forces that surround them. With unexpected twists and turns, these six stories will leave you spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Romance, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: Love and the supernatural, Fate and destiny, Hauntings and ghosts
