Synopsis: 

'From Wealth to Poverty; Or, the Tricks of the Traffic. A Story of the Drink Curse' follows the life of a wealthy and successful individual who, through a series of unfortunate events, finds themselves spiraling into poverty. As they navigate the treacherous world of addiction and the consequences it brings, they must confront the tricks and traps that come with the allure of the drink curse. This gripping tale explores the dark side of society and the devastating effects of addiction, leaving readers on the edge of their seats as they witness the protagonist's journey from wealth to poverty.

Genres: Drama, Thriller, Social Commentary

Themes: Addiction, Social Class, Redemption
