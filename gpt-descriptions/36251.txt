Synopsis: In "The Corner House Girls on a Tour: Where they went, what they saw, and what they found," the adventurous Corner House Girls embark on an exciting journey that takes them to various destinations. From bustling cities to serene countryside, they encounter unexpected challenges, make new friends, and stumble upon intriguing mysteries. As they navigate through unfamiliar territories, the girls uncover hidden secrets and unravel the truth behind puzzling events. With their wit, courage, and determination, the Corner House Girls prove that even on a tour, there is no shortage of thrilling adventures waiting to be discovered.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Problem-solving
