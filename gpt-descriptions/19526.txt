Synopsis: In "Stand by for Mars!" by Rockwell, Carey, a group of young cadets embarks on an extraordinary journey to the red planet. As they face the challenges of space travel, they must rely on their skills, teamwork, and determination to overcome unexpected obstacles. With thrilling adventures and unexpected discoveries, this captivating story will transport readers to the vast wonders of the cosmos.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance, Discovery
