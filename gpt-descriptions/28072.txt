Synopsis: 

In 'A History of Art in Chaldæa & Assyria, Vol. 1 (of 2)', Chipiez and Perrot take readers on a captivating journey through the ancient civilizations of Chaldæa and Assyria. Delving into the rich history and artistic achievements of these remarkable cultures, the authors unveil a world of awe-inspiring architecture, intricate sculptures, and mesmerizing reliefs. From the grandeur of the palaces to the intricacies of everyday life, this volume offers a comprehensive exploration of the art and culture that flourished in these ancient lands.

Genres: Art History, Archaeology, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Artistic achievements, Cultural heritage
