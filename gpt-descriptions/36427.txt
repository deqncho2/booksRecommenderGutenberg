Synopsis: In 'Art Principles with Special Reference to Painting: Together with Notes on the Illusions Produced by the Painter', Ernest Govett takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of art, exploring the principles and techniques that make paintings come alive. With a keen eye for detail, Govett delves into the illusions created by painters, unraveling the secrets behind their ability to transport viewers into different realms. From the interplay of light and shadow to the use of color and perspective, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the art of painting, providing valuable insights for both artists and art enthusiasts alike.

Genres: Art, Non-fiction

Themes: Principles of art, Illusions in painting, Techniques of painting
