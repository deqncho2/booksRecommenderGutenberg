Synopsis: In "Lord, Teach Us To Pray," Andrew Murray takes readers on a transformative journey into the depths of prayer, unveiling the power and beauty that lies within this ancient practice. Through insightful teachings and personal anecdotes, Murray explores the various aspects of prayer, guiding readers to a deeper understanding of its purpose and significance. With profound wisdom and practical advice, this book offers a fresh perspective on prayer that will inspire and empower readers to cultivate a more intimate and meaningful connection with God.

Genres: Christian literature, spiritual growth, self-help

Themes: Prayer, spiritual intimacy, faith, personal transformation
