Synopsis: In "A Boy's Adventures Round the World" by John Andrew Higginson, follow the thrilling journey of a young boy as he embarks on a global adventure filled with danger, discovery, and unexpected encounters. From the bustling streets of New York City to the exotic landscapes of faraway lands, this captivating tale will transport readers to a world of excitement and wonder. As the boy navigates through various cultures and encounters both friend and foe, he learns valuable lessons about courage, resilience, and the power of friendship. Will he be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and return home safely? Find out in this exhilarating adventure that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Friendship, Self-discovery
