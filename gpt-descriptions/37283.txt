Synopsis: 

In 'The Whole Secret Laid Open, Or the Complete Art of Making the Chemical Fulminating Objects,' an anonymous author unveils a mysterious and dangerous world of hidden objects that possess explosive potential. From seemingly innocent lace and letters to everyday items like balls, garters, and chairs, this clandestine guide reveals the art of creating these volatile objects. As readers delve into the secrets of making fulminating spiders, segars, and even boots and shoes, they are drawn into a thrilling journey of intrigue and danger. With each page, the author skillfully builds suspense, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the next explosive revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Non-fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden knowledge, Danger and risk
