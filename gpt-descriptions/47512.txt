Synopsis: In 'Chats on Old Miniatures', Foster takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of antique miniatures, uncovering the hidden stories and intricate details behind these tiny treasures. From delicate portraits to exquisite furniture, each miniature holds a rich history waiting to be discovered. With a blend of historical anecdotes, expert insights, and captivating descriptions, Foster invites readers to explore the enchanting world of old miniatures and the fascinating characters who created and collected them.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, History

Themes: Art appreciation, Historical preservation, Collecting and curating
