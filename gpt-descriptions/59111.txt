Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Lewis Carroll' is a comprehensive collection of the literary works of Lewis Carroll, the renowned author of Alice's Adventures in Wonderland. This compilation includes a wide range of Carroll's writings, from his beloved Alice stories to his lesser-known works such as poetry, puzzles, and mathematical treatises. With its extensive index, readers can easily navigate through Carroll's imaginative and whimsical world, discovering the depth and diversity of his literary genius.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Children's Literature, Poetry, Mathematics, Puzzles

Themes: 
Imagination, Nonsense, Logic, Wordplay, Mathematical Concepts
