Synopsis: In 'Crestlands: A Centennial Story of Cane Ridge' by Mary Addams Bayne, a small town's secrets and mysteries are unearthed as the community prepares to celebrate its centennial. As the townspeople delve into the past, long-buried secrets resurface, threatening to unravel the fabric of their close-knit community. With each revelation, the characters are forced to confront their own demons and make difficult choices that will shape the future of Crestlands forever. This captivating tale weaves together the lives of multiple generations, painting a vivid portrait of love, loss, and the enduring power of community.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Family, Identity, Community, Secrets, Legacy
