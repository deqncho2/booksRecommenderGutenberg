Synopsis: 

'Chronicle and Romance: Froissart, Malory, Holinshed' is a captivating collection of historical chronicles and romantic tales that transport readers to different eras and realms. From the vivid accounts of medieval battles and courtly intrigues by Jean Froissart, to the legendary adventures of King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table as recounted by Sir Thomas Malory, and the comprehensive historical narratives of Raphael Holinshed, this anthology offers a rich tapestry of stories that will enthrall and transport readers to bygone times. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Epic

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Power, Love, Fate, Historical Events
