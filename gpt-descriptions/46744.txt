Synopsis: In 'The Camp of Refuge: A Tale of the Conquest of the Isle of Ely', MacFarlane takes us on a thrilling journey to the Isle of Ely during a time of political turmoil. Amidst the chaos, a group of brave individuals find themselves seeking refuge in a hidden camp, where they must navigate treacherous landscapes and face formidable enemies. As they fight for their survival and freedom, they discover the true strength of unity and the power of hope.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Resilience, Loyalty, Freedom, Unity
