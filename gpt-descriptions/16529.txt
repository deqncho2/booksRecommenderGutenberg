Synopsis: In 'Lost Leaders' by Lang, Andrew, a mysterious group of individuals known as the Lost Leaders emerge, wielding an uncanny power to influence the course of history. As they navigate through time and space, their actions have far-reaching consequences, leading to unexpected alliances, dangerous encounters, and a race against time to uncover the truth behind their existence. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a world where nothing is as it seems, and the line between hero and villain becomes blurred.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Power and manipulation, Identity and self-discovery, Consequences of actions
