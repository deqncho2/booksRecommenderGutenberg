Synopsis: 
In the heart of the American Southwest, a colossal engineering feat stands as a testament to human ingenuity and the pursuit of progress. 'Glen Canyon Dam' takes readers on a captivating journey through the construction and impact of one of the world's most iconic dams. From the audacious visionaries who conceived it to the awe-inspiring landscapes it transformed, this book unveils the untold stories behind the creation of this monumental structure. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the challenges faced, the triumphs achieved, and the profound consequences that forever altered the natural and cultural heritage of the region.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Engineering

Themes: Human ambition and innovation, Environmental impact and conservation
