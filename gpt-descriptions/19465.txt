Synopsis: In 'Draining for Profit, and Draining for Health', George E. Waring takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of drainage systems. From the bustling streets of cities to the serene landscapes of rural areas, Waring explores the intricate network of pipes and channels that keep our communities functioning. With a blend of historical anecdotes, scientific explanations, and personal experiences, this book uncovers the hidden secrets and surprising benefits of proper drainage. Whether you're a curious reader or a professional in the field, prepare to be enthralled by the untold stories behind the unsung heroes of urban infrastructure.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Engineering

Themes: Urban development, Environmental sustainability, Public health
