Synopsis:
In "Red Dynamite: A Mystery Story for Boys" by Roy J. Snell, a thrilling adventure unfolds as a group of young boys stumble upon a mysterious red dynamite box hidden in an old abandoned house. Intrigued by its contents, they embark on a dangerous journey to uncover the truth behind the box and its connection to a series of puzzling events in their small town. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they encounter unexpected twists, secret codes, and a web of deceit that puts their lives at risk. With time running out, the boys must use their wits and courage to solve the puzzle and bring justice to their community.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
