Synopsis:
In "Gloriana; or, the revolution of 1900" by Florence Dixie, Lady, we are transported to a captivating alternate reality where the year is 1900, and a revolution is brewing. Gloriana, a strong-willed and fearless young woman, finds herself at the center of this tumultuous time. As she navigates through political intrigue, secret societies, and unexpected alliances, Gloriana's journey becomes a thrilling adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With its richly imagined world and complex characters, this novel offers a unique blend of historical fiction, political thriller, and feminist literature.

Genres: Historical fiction, Political thriller, Feminist literature
Themes: Revolution, Power dynamics, Gender roles, Social change
