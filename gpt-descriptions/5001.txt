Synopsis: In "Relativity: The Special and General Theory," Albert Einstein takes readers on a mind-bending journey through the fundamental principles that govern the universe. With his characteristic clarity and brilliance, Einstein explores the nature of space, time, and gravity, challenging our preconceived notions and revolutionizing our understanding of the cosmos. From the intriguing concept of time dilation to the mind-boggling implications of the equivalence principle, this book offers a captivating exploration of the mysteries of the universe that will leave readers in awe of Einstein's genius.

Genres: Science, Physics, Non-fiction

Themes: Relativity, Space and Time, Gravity, Scientific Revolution
