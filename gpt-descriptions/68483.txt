Synopsis: In "The Time-Raider" by Edmond Hamilton, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Foster invents a groundbreaking time-travel device that allows him to explore different eras of history. However, when he stumbles upon a mysterious artifact from the future, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and conspiracies. As Dr. Foster races against time to uncover the truth, he must confront his own moral dilemmas and face the consequences of tampering with the fabric of time itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Mystery

Themes: Morality, Consequences of Actions, Secrets and Conspiracies
