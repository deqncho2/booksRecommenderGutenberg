Synopsis: In 'The Legends of San Francisco', George W. Caldwell takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and folklore of the iconic city. From the mysterious tales of haunted houses and hidden treasures to the legendary figures who shaped the city's destiny, this book unveils the hidden secrets and enchanting stories that have become an integral part of San Francisco's identity. As readers delve into the pages, they will be transported to a world where reality and myth intertwine, leaving them eager to explore the streets of San Francisco with a newfound sense of wonder.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Folklore

Themes: Urban legends, Historical figures, Cultural identity
