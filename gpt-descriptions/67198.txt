Synopsis: In "Constantino Brumidi, Michelangelo of the United States Capitol," author Myrtle Cheney Murdock takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and artistic achievements of the renowned Italian-American painter. From his humble beginnings in Rome to his iconic contributions to the United States Capitol, Brumidi's story is one of determination, creativity, and the power of art to shape history. Murdock's vivid storytelling and meticulous research bring to life the fascinating world of Brumidi, revealing the untold stories behind his masterpieces and the lasting impact he had on American art and culture.

Genres: Biography, Art History, American History

Themes: Artistic legacy, Immigrant experience, Historical preservation
