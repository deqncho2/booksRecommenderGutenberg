Synopsis: 
In the September 19, 1882 issue of Harper's Young People, readers are transported to a captivating world filled with adventure, mystery, and imagination. Through a collection of beautifully illustrated stories, this weekly publication takes young readers on thrilling journeys across different landscapes and time periods. From a daring escape from a treacherous pirate ship to a heartwarming tale of friendship between a boy and his loyal dog, each story is sure to captivate and inspire young minds. With its vivid illustrations and engaging narratives, Harper's Young People offers a delightful reading experience that will leave readers eagerly anticipating the next issue.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Imagination
