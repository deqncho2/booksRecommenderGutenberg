Synopsis: 
'With Carson and Frémont' is an enthralling historical adventure that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed American West in the mid-19th century. The story follows the daring exploits of Scout Christopher Carson and Lieutenant John Charles Frémont, as they lead a courageous company, including the young Oliver, on a treacherous trail from the eastern Rockies to the western Sierras. Facing perilous mountains and unforgiving deserts, the group must navigate through unknown territories, encountering Native American tribes, wild animals, and the harsh realities of survival. As they forge ahead, Carson and Frémont's unwavering determination and resourcefulness are put to the test, making for a gripping and unforgettable tale.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Friendship, Native American culture
