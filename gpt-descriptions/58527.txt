Synopsis: In 'Eric's Book of Beasts' by David Starr Jordan, young Eric discovers a mysterious book that holds the secrets of extraordinary creatures. As he delves into its pages, he embarks on a thrilling adventure, encountering mythical beasts and uncovering hidden truths about the world around him. With each turn of the page, Eric's understanding of the natural world expands, leading him to question his own place within it. As he navigates through this enchanting journey, Eric learns valuable lessons about courage, friendship, and the power of knowledge.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Identity, Friendship
