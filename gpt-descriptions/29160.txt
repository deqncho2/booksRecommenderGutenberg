Synopsis: In 'Operation Lorelie' by William P. Salton, a brilliant and enigmatic hacker named Lorelie is recruited by a covert government agency to carry out a dangerous mission. As she delves deeper into the world of espionage and cyber warfare, Lorelie must navigate a web of deceit, betrayal, and high-stakes action. With her exceptional skills and a mysterious past, she becomes a key player in a game where the line between friend and foe is blurred. As the operation unfolds, the tension rises, and the fate of nations hangs in the balance.

Genres: Thriller, Espionage, Cyberpunk

Themes: Technology and its impact on society, Trust and betrayal, Identity and self-discovery
