Synopsis: In 'Overshadowed: A Novel', the lives of two individuals from vastly different backgrounds intertwine in a captivating tale of love, ambition, and the pursuit of identity. As secrets are unveiled and unexpected connections are revealed, the characters are forced to confront their pasts and make choices that will shape their futures. With its richly developed characters and intricate plot, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with twists and turns that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Ambition, Love, Redemption
