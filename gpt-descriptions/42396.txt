Synopsis: In 'Grit A-Plenty: A Tale of the Labrador Wild' by Wallace, Dillon, a group of adventurers embarks on a treacherous journey through the unforgiving wilderness of Labrador. Battling against the harsh elements and their own limitations, they face unimaginable challenges that test their resilience and determination. As they push the boundaries of human endurance, their gripping tale of survival unfolds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Historical Fiction

Themes: Resilience, Determination, Human Spirit
