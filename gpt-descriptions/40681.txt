Synopsis: In the final installment of 'The Greville Memoirs', Charles Greville takes us on a captivating journey through the reign of Queen Victoria from 1852 to 1860. Through his insightful journal entries, Greville provides a unique perspective on the political and social landscape of the time, offering a behind-the-scenes look at the inner workings of the royal court and the challenges faced by the Queen and her government. From political scandals to international affairs, Greville's memoirs paint a vivid picture of a pivotal period in British history.

Genres: Memoir, History, Biography

Themes: Monarchy, Politics, Society, Power, Intrigue
