Synopsis: In 'The Immortals' by David Duncan, a group of scientists embarks on a groundbreaking experiment to unlock the secret of immortality. As they delve deeper into their research, they discover the unimaginable consequences of their actions, leading to a thrilling race against time to save humanity from the very thing they sought to conquer. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking ethical dilemmas, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Immortality, Morality, Human Nature
