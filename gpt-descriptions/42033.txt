Synopsis: 

In "Assyria: Its Princes, Priests and People: By-Paths of Bible Knowledge VII," A.H. Sayce takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient civilization of Assyria. Delving into the lives of its princes, priests, and people, Sayce uncovers the secrets and mysteries of this once-mighty empire. From the grandeur of the royal palaces to the rituals of the priests, the reader is transported to a world filled with power, intrigue, and religious fervor. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Sayce paints a vivid picture of the rise and fall of Assyria, shedding light on its influence on biblical history and the wider ancient world. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Archaeology, Ancient History

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Power and politics, Religion and mythology
