Synopsis: In "What Happened to Me," Pickett, La Salle Corbell takes readers on a captivating journey through her extraordinary life, filled with unexpected twists and turns. From her early years as a young Southern belle to her experiences during the Civil War, she shares intimate details of her encounters with famous figures and the challenges she faced as a woman in a male-dominated society. As the narrative unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the reader is left wondering what truly happened to her. This gripping memoir will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Biography

Themes: Identity, Gender Roles, Historical Events, Personal Growth
