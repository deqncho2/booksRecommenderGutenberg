Synopsis: In "Pottery Decoration Under the Glaze," McLaughlin takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of pottery, exploring the intricate art of decorating ceramics beneath the glaze. Through vivid descriptions and expert insights, the author delves into the history, techniques, and secrets behind this mesmerizing craft. From ancient traditions to modern innovations, this book unveils the beauty and complexity of pottery decoration under the glaze, leaving readers inspired and eager to discover the wonders that lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Art, Crafts, Non-fiction

Themes: Pottery, Decoration, Artistry, Tradition, Innovation
