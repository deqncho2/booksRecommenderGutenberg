Synopsis: In "The Pilgrims' First Christmas" by Josephine Pittman Scribner, readers are transported back in time to the early days of the Pilgrims' settlement in Plymouth, Massachusetts. As the harsh winter sets in, the Pilgrims face numerous challenges and uncertainties. However, amidst the struggles, they come together to celebrate their first Christmas in the New World, finding hope, resilience, and the true meaning of the holiday season.

Genres: Historical fiction, Christian fiction, Holiday fiction

Themes: Perseverance, faith, community, cultural adaptation
