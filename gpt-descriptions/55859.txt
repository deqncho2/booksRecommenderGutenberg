Synopsis: 

In 'The West Indies' by Henderson, John, a young archaeologist embarks on a thrilling expedition to the enchanting islands of the West Indies. As he delves into the rich history and hidden secrets of this captivating region, he uncovers a series of mysterious artifacts that hold the key to a long-lost civilization. With danger lurking at every turn, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter intriguing characters, and decipher cryptic clues to unravel the truth behind this ancient civilization. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Discovery
