Synopsis: 
The Sorrows of Young Werther follows the emotional journey of a young artist named Werther, who becomes infatuated with a beautiful and unattainable woman named Lotte. As Werther's passion intensifies, he finds himself caught in a whirlwind of love, desire, and despair. Through a series of letters, Werther pours out his soul, exploring the depths of his emotions and the complexities of human relationships. This poignant and deeply introspective novel delves into the profound struggles of unrequited love and the devastating consequences it can have on one's psyche.

Genres: 
Romantic novel, Epistolary novel

Themes: 
Unrequited love, Passion, Despair, Individualism
