Synopsis: 
In 'In the Van; or, The Builders' by Price-Brown, John, a group of skilled craftsmen embark on a journey that takes them across the country in a unique mode of transportation - a van. As they travel from town to town, they encounter a series of unexpected challenges and form an unbreakable bond. With each new project they undertake, they not only build physical structures but also discover the power of friendship, resilience, and the importance of following one's passion.

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Pursuit of Passion
