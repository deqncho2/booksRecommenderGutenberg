Synopsis: In 'The Lunatic at Large' by J. Storer Clouston, a mysterious lunatic escapes from an asylum and embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him through the dark and twisted alleys of Victorian London. As he encounters a cast of eccentric characters, the line between sanity and madness becomes blurred, leaving the reader questioning who the true lunatic really is. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Madness, Identity, Perception
