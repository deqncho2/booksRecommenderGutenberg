Synopsis: In 'The Sun of Saratoga: A Romance of Burgoyne's Surrender', Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on a captivating journey through the American Revolutionary War. Set against the backdrop of the pivotal Battle of Saratoga, the story follows the life of a young American soldier named Robert Lennox. As Lennox becomes entangled in the conflict, he finds himself torn between love, loyalty, and the pursuit of freedom. With vivid descriptions and gripping action, Altsheler weaves a tale of courage, sacrifice, and the indomitable spirit of those who fought for independence.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Romance

Themes: Patriotism, Love and Sacrifice, Coming of Age, Freedom and Independence
