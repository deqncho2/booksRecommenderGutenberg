Synopsis: In 'The Sky Trap' by Frank Belknap Long, a group of scientists embarks on a perilous journey to a remote island in the Pacific Ocean, where they stumble upon a mysterious phenomenon that defies all scientific understanding. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the island, they find themselves trapped in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred. With their lives hanging in the balance, they must unravel the enigma of the sky trap before it consumes them all.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the limits of human perception, the power of illusion
