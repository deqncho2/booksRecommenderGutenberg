Synopsis: In the bustling streets of 18th-century Quebec City, a mysterious golden dog becomes the center of attention, captivating the hearts and minds of the locals. As the dog's presence intertwines with the lives of various characters, a web of secrets, love, and betrayal begins to unravel. From the lavish mansions of the French aristocracy to the gritty underworld of smugglers, this gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the vibrant tapestry of colonial Canada.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Loyalty and Betrayal, Social Class and Power
