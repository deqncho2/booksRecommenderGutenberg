Synopsis: 
In 'Ruth Erskine's Crosses' by Pansy, we follow the journey of Ruth Erskine, a young woman who faces numerous challenges and crosses in her life. From family conflicts to personal struggles, Ruth's faith is tested as she navigates through difficult circumstances. As she encounters unexpected friendships and unexpected love, Ruth must find the strength to overcome her crosses and discover the true meaning of forgiveness and redemption.

Genres: Fiction, Christian literature, Romance

Themes: Faith, Forgiveness, Redemption
