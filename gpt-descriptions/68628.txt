Synopsis: 
Caroline, a young hospice worker, takes a job caring for an elderly man in a remote Louisiana plantation. As she explores the house, she discovers a hidden attic filled with mysterious artifacts and a collection of skeleton keys. Intrigued by the secrets the keys may unlock, Caroline delves into the dark history of the house, uncovering a chilling connection to her own past. With each key she uses, Caroline unwittingly opens the door to a world of supernatural forces and malevolent spirits, leading her on a terrifying journey to uncover the truth and save herself from a sinister fate.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of belief and superstition, the thin line between reality and the supernatural
