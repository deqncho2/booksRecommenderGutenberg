Synopsis: 
'Fables in Slang' by Ade, George is a collection of witty and humorous short stories that take traditional fables and give them a modern twist. Through the use of slang and colloquial language, Ade presents a fresh and entertaining take on timeless moral lessons. From talking animals to clever tricksters, these fables explore the complexities of human nature and offer insightful commentary on society. Each story is filled with clever wordplay and unexpected twists, making it a delightful read for those who enjoy a blend of humor and wisdom.

Genres: 
Satire, Humor, Fiction

Themes: 
Human nature, Morality, Society
