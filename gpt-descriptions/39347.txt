Synopsis: 
'Caricature and Other Comic Art in All Times and Many Lands' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of comic art, exploring its rich history and diverse forms across different cultures and eras. From ancient civilizations to modern times, this book delves into the origins and evolution of caricature, cartoons, and other forms of visual humor, showcasing the incredible creativity and wit of artists throughout history. With vivid illustrations and engaging anecdotes, Parton invites readers to discover the power of laughter and satire as they explore the universal language of comic art.

Genres: Art History, Cultural Studies, Humor

Themes: Satire, Creativity, Cultural Expression
