Synopsis: 

In 'Picked up at Sea: The Gold Miners of Minturne Creek', John C. Hutcheson takes readers on a thrilling adventure set in the rugged wilderness of the gold rush era. When a group of gold miners find themselves stranded on a desolate island after a shipwreck, they must rely on their resourcefulness and determination to survive. As they navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter dangerous wildlife, and face unexpected challenges, the bond between these men is tested to its limits. Will they be able to overcome the odds and find their way back to civilization, or will they be forever lost in the unforgiving wilderness?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Camaraderie, Perseverance
