Synopsis: In the dark underbelly of New York City, a notorious gang of smugglers known as the "Dock Rats" rule the docks with an iron fist. But when a mysterious stranger arrives, determined to bring them down, the gang finds themselves facing their greatest challenge yet. As secrets are revealed and alliances are tested, the battle for control of the waterfront reaches a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Crime and punishment, Loyalty and betrayal
