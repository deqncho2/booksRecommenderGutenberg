Synopsis: In 'Stay off the Moon!' by Raymond F. Jones, a group of scientists embarks on a groundbreaking mission to the moon, only to discover a shocking secret that could change the course of humanity forever. As they grapple with the implications of their discovery, they must confront their own fears and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of the world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Scientific Ethics, Consequences of Discovery
