Synopsis: 
In 'The Survivor' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious man with a dark past finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue and danger when he becomes the sole survivor of a tragic event. As he tries to uncover the truth behind the incident, he must navigate through a world of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With his life on the line, he must rely on his wits and resourcefulness to survive and bring justice to those responsible.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Revenge
