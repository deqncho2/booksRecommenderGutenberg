Synopsis: In 'A New Aristocracy' by Alice E. Bartlett, a young woman named Emily finds herself thrust into a world of privilege and power when she unexpectedly inherits a grand estate. As she navigates the complexities of her new aristocratic life, Emily discovers dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel everything she holds dear. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, she must uncover the truth and decide where her loyalties truly lie. This gripping tale of wealth, deception, and the pursuit of justice will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Social class, Power dynamics, Identity
