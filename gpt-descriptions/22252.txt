Synopsis: In "Rollo on the Atlantic" by Abbott, Jacob, young Rollo embarks on a thrilling journey across the vast Atlantic Ocean. Join him as he encounters fascinating characters, faces unexpected challenges, and discovers the wonders of the deep blue sea. Will Rollo's adventurous spirit and determination be enough to overcome the perils that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Juvenile Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Independence, Resilience
