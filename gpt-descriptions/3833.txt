Synopsis: 

'Australian Legendary Tales: folk-lore of the Noongahburrahs as told to the Piccaninnies' is a captivating collection of Aboriginal folk tales, passed down through generations and now shared with the world. Through the enchanting storytelling of Katie Langloh Parker, readers are transported to the rich and vibrant world of the Noongahburrahs, where mythical creatures, heroic figures, and ancient traditions come to life. From the creation of the land to epic battles and tales of love and loss, these stories offer a unique glimpse into the cultural heritage of the Australian Aboriginal people.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Cultural Heritage

Themes: Aboriginal culture, Creation myths, Heroism, Tradition, Love and loss
