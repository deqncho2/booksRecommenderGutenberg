Synopsis: 
In 'Fenwick's Career' by Ward, Humphry, Mrs., we follow the journey of a young man named Fenwick as he navigates the complexities of life and love in Victorian England. From his humble beginnings as a clerk in a London office to his rise in society, Fenwick's ambition and determination drive him forward. Along the way, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, faces moral dilemmas, and grapples with the consequences of his choices. As Fenwick's career progresses, the reader is drawn into a captivating tale of ambition, love, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Ambition, Love, Morality, Social Class
