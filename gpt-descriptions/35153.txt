Synopsis: In 'Far Off Things' by Arthur Machen, a young man named Dyson embarks on a journey through the bustling streets of London, where he encounters a series of peculiar and mysterious events. As he delves deeper into the hidden corners of the city, Dyson becomes entangled in a web of secrets, occultism, and the supernatural. With each encounter, he is drawn closer to a truth that will challenge his perception of reality and force him to confront the unknown lurking just beyond the surface of everyday life.

Genres: Mystery, Occult Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: The blurred line between reality and the supernatural, the hidden mysteries of urban environments, the exploration of the occult and esoteric knowledge.
