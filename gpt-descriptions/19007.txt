Synopsis: 

In 'Danger Signals: Remarkable, Exciting and Unique Examples of the Bravery, Daring and Stoicism in the Midst of Danger of Train Dispatchers and Railroad Engineers', authors Brady and Hill delve into the thrilling world of train dispatchers and railroad engineers, uncovering extraordinary tales of bravery and stoicism in the face of danger. From heart-stopping near misses to daring rescues, this book takes readers on a captivating journey through the high-stakes world of the railroad industry. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Bravery, Danger, Resilience, Heroism
