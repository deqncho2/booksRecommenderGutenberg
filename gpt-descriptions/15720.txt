Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a young aspiring actress, embarks on a thrilling adventure in the Great Northwest. When she encounters an Indian girl with a remarkable talent for acting, Ruth's world is turned upside down. Together, they navigate the challenges of the movie industry, facing unexpected obstacles and discovering the power of friendship. As Ruth's dreams of stardom collide with the beauty of the wilderness, she must find her own path and embrace the unexpected opportunities that come her way.

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Pursuing dreams, Friendship, Cultural diversity
