Synopsis: 
In "The Bodleian Library at Oxford," Madan, Falconer takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and hidden treasures of one of the world's most renowned libraries. From ancient manuscripts to secret passageways, this book unveils the fascinating stories and mysteries that lie within the walls of the Bodleian Library. As readers delve into the depths of this literary sanctuary, they will be enthralled by the tales of scholars, librarians, and the countless individuals who have been touched by the magic of this extraordinary institution.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Biography

Themes: Knowledge and discovery, Preservation of cultural heritage, Intellectual pursuit
