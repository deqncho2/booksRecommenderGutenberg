Synopsis: In 'Historia Amoris: A History of Love, Ancient and Modern', Edgar Saltus takes readers on a captivating journey through the ages, exploring the multifaceted nature of love. From the passionate romances of ancient Greece to the scandalous affairs of the Renaissance, Saltus delves into the lives of historical figures and their extraordinary love stories. With his eloquent prose and meticulous research, he unveils the secrets, desires, and tragedies that have shaped the course of love throughout history. This enthralling exploration of love's complexities will leave readers spellbound, questioning the very essence of this universal emotion.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Romance, Literary Criticism

Themes: Love, Desire, Tragedy, Human Nature
