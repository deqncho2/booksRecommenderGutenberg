Synopsis: 

'Curiosities of History: Boston, September Seventeenth, 1630-1880' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and often overlooked history of Boston, Massachusetts. From the city's founding in 1630 to the late 19th century, author William W. Wheildon delves into the lesser-known stories, events, and personalities that have shaped Boston's unique character. Through a collection of intriguing anecdotes, Wheildon uncovers hidden gems and uncanny occurrences that have left their mark on the city's history. From political scandals to remarkable acts of bravery, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the captivating tapestry of Boston's past.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, local history, cultural exploration

Themes: City development, social dynamics, historical significance, cultural heritage
