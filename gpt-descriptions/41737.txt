Synopsis: In 'Burton of the Flying Corps' by Herbert Strang, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside Lieutenant Burton, a young pilot in the Royal Flying Corps during World War I. As he faces the dangers of aerial combat and the challenges of life on the front lines, Burton's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. With vivid descriptions of dogfights and the camaraderie among the pilots, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the fate of Burton and his fellow aviators.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Coming of Age
