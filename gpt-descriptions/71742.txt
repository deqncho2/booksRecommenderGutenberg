Synopsis: In 'The Prince of the Pin Elves' by Charles Lee Sleight, a young boy named Jack discovers a hidden world of magical creatures living in his own backyard. When he befriends a mischievous pin elf named Pippin, Jack is thrust into a thrilling adventure filled with danger, friendship, and the power of imagination. As Jack and Pippin navigate through the enchanted realm, they must uncover the truth about Jack's own heritage and the destiny that awaits him. Will Jack embrace his newfound role as the Prince of the Pin Elves, or will he succumb to the darkness that threatens to consume their world?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Middle Grade

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Coming of Age
