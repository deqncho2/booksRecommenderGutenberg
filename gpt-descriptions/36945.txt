Synopsis: In 'A Tatter of Scarlet: Adventurous Episodes of the Commune in the Midi 1871', author S. R. Crockett takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Paris Commune. Set in the Midi region of France, this gripping tale follows a group of courageous individuals who find themselves caught up in the chaos and danger of the Commune. As they navigate through treacherous streets and face unexpected challenges, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to heart-stopping adventures and surprising alliances. With its vivid descriptions and fast-paced narrative, 'A Tatter of Scarlet' offers a captivating glimpse into a pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Revolution, Loyalty, Survival
