Synopsis: 
In "Junior Achievement" by William M. Lee, a young and ambitious entrepreneur named Alex embarks on a journey to create his own business empire. Faced with numerous challenges and setbacks, Alex must navigate the cutthroat world of corporate competition, while also dealing with personal relationships and the sacrifices required for success. As he rises through the ranks, Alex discovers the true cost of ambition and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: 
Drama, Business, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Ambition, Success, Personal Growth, Sacrifice
