Synopsis: In 'Who Crosses Storm Mountain?: 1911' by Charles Egbert Craddock, a group of individuals embarks on a treacherous journey through the rugged terrain of Storm Mountain. As they face the perils of nature and the unknown, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, revealing secrets, desires, and the true nature of human resilience. With each step, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, wondering who will make it to the other side of Storm Mountain alive.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Human Nature
