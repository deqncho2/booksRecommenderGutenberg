Synopsis: In 'The Hand' by Jerry Sohl, a seemingly ordinary man wakes up one day to find that his left hand has developed a mind of its own. As he struggles to regain control over his rebellious appendage, he embarks on a journey that takes him deep into the mysteries of the human mind and the nature of identity. With his sanity hanging in the balance, he must confront the sinister forces that seek to exploit his newfound power. This gripping psychological thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of self-control and the true nature of free will.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Science fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Control, Sanity, Free will
