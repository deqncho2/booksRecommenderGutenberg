Synopsis: 
In "The Hittites: The story of a Forgotten Empire," A.H. Sayce takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient world, uncovering the mysteries of a long-lost civilization. From their rise to power in Anatolia to their encounters with other great empires, the Hittites' story is one of intrigue, conquest, and cultural exchange. As Sayce delves into their religious beliefs, military strategies, and diplomatic relations, he paints a vivid picture of a forgotten empire that once shaped the course of history. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, this book brings to life the fascinating world of the Hittites, shedding light on a civilization that has long been overshadowed by its more famous counterparts.

Genres: History, Archaeology, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Cultural exchange, Power and conquest
