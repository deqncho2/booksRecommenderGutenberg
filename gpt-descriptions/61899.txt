Synopsis: In 'The Pirate Submarine' by Percy F. Westerman, a thrilling adventure unfolds as a group of young friends stumble upon a mysterious submarine hidden in a secluded cove. Little do they know, this is no ordinary vessel, but a pirate submarine with a dangerous secret. As they embark on a perilous journey, they must outsmart the cunning pirates and uncover the truth behind the submarine's true purpose. With heart-pounding action and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
