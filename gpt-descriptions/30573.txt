Synopsis: In 'Why I Preach the Second Coming', Haldeman, Isaac Massey takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through his personal experiences and theological reflections that have led him to passionately advocate for the belief in the Second Coming of Christ. Through vivid storytelling and compelling arguments, the author explores the significance of this event and its implications for humanity, challenging readers to reconsider their own beliefs and embrace the hope and transformation it offers. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Christian literature, Theology

Themes: Second Coming of Christ, Faith, Hope, Redemption, Eschatology
