Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Port Tidings, a mysterious fog rolls in, shrouding the community in an eerie atmosphere. As the fog thickens, strange occurrences begin to unfold, revealing long-buried secrets and hidden desires. Amidst the fog's enigmatic presence, the lives of the townspeople intertwine, leading to unexpected alliances and devastating consequences. Pelley's gripping novel explores the power of secrets, the fragility of human connections, and the haunting nature of the unknown.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Identity, Redemption
