Synopsis: In 'Modern Magic' by Schele de Vere, M., a young man stumbles upon a hidden world of enchantment and sorcery, where ancient spells and mystical creatures coexist with the modern world. As he delves deeper into this realm, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel the delicate balance between magic and reality. With danger lurking at every turn, he must navigate treacherous alliances and harness his own latent powers to save both worlds from impending doom.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Magic, Dual Worlds, Power and Responsibility
