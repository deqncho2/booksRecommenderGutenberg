Synopsis: In "Phoenix" by Marion Zimmer Bradley and Ted White, a young woman named Shebat finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love in a futuristic society. As she navigates through a world of power struggles and hidden agendas, Shebat must make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of her people. With its captivating blend of science fiction and romance, "Phoenix" takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with secrets, betrayal, and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Science Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Power struggles, Forbidden love, Destiny
