Synopsis: In 'The White Shield' by Bertram Mitford, a young Englishman finds himself embroiled in a thrilling adventure in the untamed African wilderness. Drawn into a dangerous conflict between rival tribes, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, face deadly wildlife, and confront the mysteries of an ancient artifact known as the White Shield. As he delves deeper into the heart of Africa, he discovers secrets that could change the course of history. With unexpected allies and formidable enemies, he must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to survive and protect what he holds dear.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural Conflict, Ancient Mysteries
