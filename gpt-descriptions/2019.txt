Synopsis: 
In the small town of Galesburg, a notorious criminal known as "The Bat" strikes fear into the hearts of its residents. When a wealthy woman is found murdered in her mansion, chaos ensues as everyone becomes a suspect. As the police investigate, a series of unexpected twists and turns lead to a thrilling and suspenseful hunt for the elusive Bat. With danger lurking around every corner, the residents must confront their deepest fears and secrets to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Fear, Suspicion, Secrets
