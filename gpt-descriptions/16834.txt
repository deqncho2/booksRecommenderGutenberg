Synopsis: In 'The Harris-Ingram Experiment' by Charles E. Bolton, a brilliant scientist and a skeptical journalist team up to investigate a mysterious experiment that promises to unlock the secrets of the human mind. As they delve deeper into the experiment, they uncover a web of intrigue and danger that challenges their beliefs and puts their lives at risk. With each revelation, the boundaries between reality and illusion blur, leaving them questioning their own sanity. Will they uncover the truth behind the experiment, or will they become trapped in a mind-bending labyrinth of deception?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Ethics of Scientific Experimentation, Psychological Manipulation
