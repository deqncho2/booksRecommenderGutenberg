Synopsis: In 'The Minoans' by George Glasgow, a young archaeologist embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization lost to time. As she delves deeper into the mysterious world of the Minoans, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden love. With each discovery, she unravels the enigmatic past of a civilization that once thrived on the island of Crete, but ultimately met a tragic fate. Will she be able to piece together the puzzle before it's too late?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Archaeology, Forbidden love, Intrigue, Tragedy
