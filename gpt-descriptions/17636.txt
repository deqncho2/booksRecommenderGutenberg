Synopsis: In 'The Mystery at Putnam Hall: The School Chums' Strange Discovery' by Stratemeyer, Edward, a group of school friends stumble upon a mysterious secret hidden within the walls of their prestigious boarding school. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling adventure that tests their courage and friendship. With unexpected twists and turns, the young protagonists must unravel the truth behind the strange discovery before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Secrets
