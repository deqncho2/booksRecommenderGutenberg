Synopsis: In 'Brother Jacques', Paul de Kock takes us on a captivating journey through the streets of Paris, where we follow the life of the charming and mischievous Brother Jacques. From his humble beginnings as a street urchin to his unexpected rise in society, Jacques encounters a colorful cast of characters, navigates love and betrayal, and finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and scandal. As the story unfolds, the reader is drawn into a world of intrigue, romance, and unexpected twists that will keep them eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Social class, Love and relationships, Morality
