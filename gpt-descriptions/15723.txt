Synopsis: The Rover Boys on Treasure Isle; Or, The Strange Cruise of the Steam Yacht takes readers on an exhilarating adventure as the Rover boys find themselves embarking on a mysterious journey to a remote island. When their steam yacht is unexpectedly hijacked, the brothers must navigate treacherous waters, encounter dangerous foes, and unravel the secrets of the island. With their courage and resourcefulness tested to the limit, the Rover boys must rely on their wits and teamwork to survive and uncover the truth hidden on Treasure Isle.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Brotherhood, Perseverance
