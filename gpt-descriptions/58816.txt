Synopsis: In "Simla Village Tales; Or, Folk Tales from the Himalayas," Dracott, Alice takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of the Himalayan villages. Through a collection of folk tales, the author weaves together stories of love, loss, bravery, and the supernatural. From mystical creatures lurking in the mountains to the resilience of the human spirit, these tales transport readers to a realm where reality and myth intertwine. With each story, Dracott invites readers to explore the rich cultural heritage and traditions of the Himalayan region, while also delving into the universal themes of hope, redemption, and the power of storytelling.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural Fiction

Themes: Love, Loss, Bravery, Supernatural, Cultural Heritage, Resilience, Hope, Redemption, Power of Storytelling
