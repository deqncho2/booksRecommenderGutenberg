Synopsis: In 'The Bread and Biscuit Baker's and Sugar-Boiler's Assistant: Including a Large Variety of Modern Recipes' by Robert Wells, readers are taken on a delightful culinary journey filled with mouthwatering recipes and expert baking techniques. This comprehensive guide offers a wide range of modern recipes for bread, biscuits, and sugary treats, providing aspiring bakers with the knowledge and skills to create delectable delights. From fluffy loaves of bread to perfectly golden biscuits, and from sweet sugary confections to savory treats, this book is a treasure trove of baking secrets that will leave readers eager to roll up their sleeves and get baking.

Genres: Cookbook, Culinary, Non-fiction

Themes: Baking, Culinary Arts, Recipe Collection
