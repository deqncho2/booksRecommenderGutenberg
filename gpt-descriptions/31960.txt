Synopsis: In 'The Covenanters of Damascus; A Hitherto Unknown Jewish Sect', George Foot Moore takes us on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of a hidden Jewish sect in Damascus. Set against the backdrop of the early 20th century, this enthralling narrative follows a young scholar who stumbles upon a secret society with ancient traditions and beliefs that challenge everything he thought he knew about Judaism. As he delves deeper into their enigmatic practices, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden love. Moore's vivid descriptions and meticulous research bring this clandestine community to life, leaving readers spellbound until the very last page.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Religious fiction

Themes: Identity, Tradition, Faith, Forbidden knowledge
