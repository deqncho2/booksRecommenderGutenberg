Synopsis: In 'Hand and Ring' by Anna Katharine Green, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young woman finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deceit. When a valuable ring goes missing, suspicion falls on those closest to her, and she must navigate a labyrinth of clues and hidden motives to uncover the truth. As the plot thickens, unexpected alliances are formed, and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. With each twist and turn, the reader is kept on the edge of their seat, eagerly anticipating the shocking revelations that lie ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Trust
