Synopsis:
In "Roy: A Tale in the Days of Sir John Moore" by Agnes Giberne, readers are transported to the early 19th century, amidst the backdrop of the Peninsular War. The story revolves around Roy, a young boy who finds himself caught up in the chaos and uncertainty of war. As he navigates through the challenges and dangers of the battlefield, Roy's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. Along the way, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own secrets and motivations. As the war rages on, Roy's journey becomes a gripping tale of survival, loyalty, and the indomitable human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival, Coming of Age
