Synopsis: 

In "History of the Beef Cattle Industry in Illinois," author Frank Webster Farley takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and often overlooked history of the beef cattle industry in the state of Illinois. From the early days of settlement to the modern era, Farley delves into the triumphs and challenges faced by those who dedicated their lives to raising and nurturing these magnificent animals. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the author sheds light on the pioneers, the innovations, and the economic impact that shaped the industry into what it is today. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Agriculture

Themes: Agricultural development, Economic impact, Pioneering spirit
