Synopsis: In "The Art of Kissing: Curiously, Historically, Humorously, Poetically Considered," Rossiter, Will takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of kissing. From ancient civilizations to modern times, this book explores the various cultural, historical, and even humorous aspects of this intimate act. With a blend of wit, curiosity, and poetic insight, Rossiter delves into the artistry and significance of kissing, leaving readers intrigued and eager to discover more.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Humor, Poetry

Themes: Intimacy, Cultural practices, Human connection, Historical evolution
