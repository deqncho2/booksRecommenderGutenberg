Synopsis: In "The Sex Side of Life: An Explanation for Young People," Mary Ware Dennett fearlessly explores the often-taboo topic of sex, providing a comprehensive and enlightening guide for young readers. Through a series of candid discussions and thought-provoking anecdotes, Dennett navigates the complexities of human sexuality, addressing questions and concerns that many young people may have. With a compassionate and informative approach, this book aims to empower and educate, offering a refreshing perspective on an essential aspect of life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, Self-help

Themes: Sexuality, Education, Empowerment
