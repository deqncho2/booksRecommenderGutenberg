Synopsis: 
"Heimatlos: Two stories for children, and for those who love children" by Johanna Spyri is a captivating collection of two heartwarming tales that will transport readers into the lives of two young protagonists. In the first story, a young girl named Verena finds herself in a difficult situation when she is separated from her family and must navigate the challenges of being alone in a new environment. The second story follows the adventures of a young boy named Toni, who embarks on a journey to find his true home. Both stories beautifully depict the resilience, courage, and determination of these young characters as they face adversity and search for a sense of belonging.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Family, Home, Resilience, Belonging
