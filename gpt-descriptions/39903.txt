Synopsis: In 'Star of Mercia: Historical Tales of Wales and the Marches', Devereux, Blanche takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of Wales and the Marches. From the legendary tales of King Arthur to the struggles of the Welsh princes against English domination, this collection of historical tales weaves together a tapestry of adventure, romance, and political intrigue. Through the eyes of courageous warriors, cunning spies, and resilient heroines, the reader is transported to a time of knights, castles, and epic battles. As the stories unfold, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the fate of nations hangs in the balance. With vivid descriptions and meticulous attention to historical detail, Devereux, Blanche brings the past to life, immersing readers in a world of chivalry, honor, and the enduring spirit of the Welsh people.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Heroism, Identity, Power Struggles, Love and Loyalty
