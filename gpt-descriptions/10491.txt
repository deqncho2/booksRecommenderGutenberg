Synopsis: 
In 'Practice Book, Leland Powers School' by Leland Todd Powers, a group of talented students at the prestigious Leland Powers School embark on a journey of self-discovery and growth as they navigate the challenges of honing their artistic skills. From intense acting exercises to vocal training and improvisation, the students face both personal and professional obstacles that push them to their limits. As they strive to become the best versions of themselves, they form deep bonds and learn valuable life lessons that extend far beyond the walls of the school. 

Genres: Drama, Coming-of-Age, Education

Themes: Self-discovery, Artistic growth, Friendship, Perseverance
