Synopsis: 
In "The Golden House," a mysterious and enigmatic family moves into a luxurious mansion in a prestigious neighborhood in New York City. The patriarch, Nero Golden, and his three adult sons captivate the attention of their neighbors and the media, who are eager to uncover the secrets behind their wealth and past. As the story unfolds, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the lives of the Goldens, filled with love, betrayal, tragedy, and redemption. With each revelation, the true nature of the family and their hidden agenda becomes increasingly apparent, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the boundaries of power and the lengths people will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Literary Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Power, Family, Secrets, Redemption
