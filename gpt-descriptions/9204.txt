Synopsis: In "The Prophetic Pictures," a young artist is commissioned to paint a series of portraits that mysteriously reveal the future. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic nature of his subjects, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden meanings. With each stroke of his brush, the artist uncovers a world of prophecy and fate, leading him to question the boundaries between art and reality. This captivating tale explores the power of art to transcend time and the consequences of peering into the unknown.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, mystery

Themes: Fate and destiny, the power of art, the blurred line between reality and illusion
