Synopsis: 
In '1001 Questions and Answers on Orthography and Reading' by B. A. Hathaway, readers are taken on an enlightening journey through the intricacies of language and the art of reading. This captivating book presents a collection of thought-provoking questions and their insightful answers, covering a wide range of topics related to orthography and reading. From the origins of language to the nuances of pronunciation, from the rules of grammar to the secrets of comprehension, Hathaway's book offers a treasure trove of knowledge that will leave readers with a newfound appreciation for the written word. Whether you're a language enthusiast, a student, or simply curious about the inner workings of our linguistic world, this book is sure to captivate and educate.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, Linguistics

Themes: Language, Orthography, Reading, Education
