Synopsis: In 'The Twickenham Peerage' by Richard Marsh, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young woman named Maud is unexpectedly thrust into the world of aristocracy. When she inherits a title and a grand estate, Maud must navigate the treacherous waters of high society, facing deception, scandal, and hidden secrets. As she delves deeper into her newfound position, Maud discovers that not everything is as it seems, and she must unravel the truth before it consumes her. With its intriguing plot twists and complex characters, 'The Twickenham Peerage' promises a thrilling journey through the upper echelons of society, where appearances can be deceiving and power can corrupt.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Power, Social Class
