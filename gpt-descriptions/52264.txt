Synopsis: In the gripping true crime novel 'Trial of William Palmer', a seemingly respectable doctor finds himself at the center of a scandalous murder trial that captivated Victorian England. As the evidence unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the truth becomes increasingly elusive. With its shocking twists and turns, this suspenseful account of a high-profile trial will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: True Crime, Historical Fiction, Legal Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice, Morality
