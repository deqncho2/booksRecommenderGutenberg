Synopsis: In "Isabella Orsini: A Historical Novel of the Fifteenth Century," Francesco Domenico Guerrazzi takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous world of 15th-century Italy. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue, power struggles, and forbidden love, this gripping historical novel follows the life of Isabella Orsini, a strong-willed and enigmatic woman who defies societal norms to fight for her family's honor and her own destiny. As Isabella navigates the treacherous landscape of Renaissance Italy, readers are immersed in a tale of passion, betrayal, and the indomitable spirit of a remarkable woman.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Power and Politics, Gender Roles and Expectations, Family Honor, Forbidden Love
