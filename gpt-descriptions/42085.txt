Synopsis: In 'A Bachelor Husband' by Ruby M. Ayres, a young woman finds herself in a precarious situation when she agrees to marry a man she barely knows. As she navigates the challenges of married life, secrets from her husband's past begin to unravel, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected romance. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating novel explores the complexities of love, trust, and the choices we make in the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Love and marriage, Secrets and deception, Self-discovery
