Synopsis: 
'Boswelliana: The Commonplace Book of James Boswell, with a Memoir and Annotations' offers a captivating glimpse into the mind of the renowned 18th-century Scottish writer and biographer, James Boswell. This collection of personal writings, anecdotes, and reflections provides an intimate portrait of Boswell's thoughts, experiences, and encounters with some of the most influential figures of his time. Through his witty observations and insightful musings, Boswell takes readers on a journey through his life, offering a unique perspective on the social, cultural, and political landscape of the era.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Literary Nonfiction

Themes: Personal introspection, Intellectual curiosity, Historical context
