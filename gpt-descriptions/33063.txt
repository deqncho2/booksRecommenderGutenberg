Synopsis: 
In 'Plane Geometry' by G. A. Wentworth, embark on a captivating journey through the world of shapes, lines, and angles. This engaging book takes readers on a visual exploration of the fundamental principles of plane geometry, unraveling the mysteries of triangles, circles, and polygons. With clear explanations and numerous examples, Wentworth invites readers to unlock the secrets of geometric proofs and discover the beauty and elegance of this timeless mathematical discipline. Prepare to be enthralled by the intricate patterns and symmetries that lie within the pages of 'Plane Geometry'.

Genres: Mathematics, Educational, Non-fiction

Themes: Geometry, Mathematical principles, Visual learning
