Synopsis: 
In 'The Hoofer' by Miller, Walter M., a down-on-his-luck tap dancer named Eddie is given a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to revive his career when he is offered a chance to perform in a prestigious Broadway show. As Eddie navigates the cutthroat world of show business, he must confront his own insecurities and face the challenges that come with fame and success. With captivating dance sequences and a heartfelt exploration of dreams and redemption, 'The Hoofer' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the glitz and glamour of the entertainment industry.

Genres: Drama, Dance, Fiction

Themes: Pursuit of dreams, Redemption, Self-discovery
