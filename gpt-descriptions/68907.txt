Synopsis: 
'The true prophecies or prognostications of Michael Nostradamus, physician to Henry II. Francis II. and Charles IX. Kings of France, and one of the best astronomers that ever were.' is a fascinating work that delves into the prophetic visions of the renowned physician and astronomer, Michael Nostradamus. Translated and commented by Theophilus de Garencieres, this book offers a captivating exploration of Nostradamus' predictions, providing a glimpse into the future as foreseen by this remarkable figure. 

Genres: 
Historical, Prophetic, Astrology

Themes: 
Prophecy, Fate, Future, Astrological Interpretation
