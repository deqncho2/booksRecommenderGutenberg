Synopsis: In "Boys of the Light Brigade: A Story of Spain and the Peninsular War," Herbert Strang takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the tumultuous era of the Peninsular War. Set against the backdrop of the early 19th century, this gripping historical novel follows the adventures of two young British soldiers, Jack and Tom, as they find themselves thrust into the heart of the conflict in Spain. From the chaos of battlefields to the intrigue of espionage, the boys must navigate the treacherous landscape of war, facing danger, camaraderie, and the harsh realities of life on the front lines. As they grow and mature amidst the chaos, their loyalty and bravery are put to the ultimate test. Will they emerge unscathed, or will the horrors of war forever change them?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Coming of Age, Sacrifice
