Synopsis: 

"Ladies on Horseback: Learning, Park-riding, and Hunting, with Hints Upon Costume, and Numerous Anecdotes" takes readers on an exhilarating journey into the world of equestrianism, offering a comprehensive guide for women seeking to master the art of horse riding, from basic skills to the thrill of hunting. Filled with captivating anecdotes and valuable tips on proper attire, this book provides a fascinating glimpse into the lives of women who fearlessly embrace the freedom and challenges that come with riding horses.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Memoir

Themes: Empowerment, Adventure, Gender roles, Fashion, Skill development
