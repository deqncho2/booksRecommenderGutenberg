Synopsis: In 'The Mystery at Dark Cedars' by Edith Lavell, a young woman named Amelia finds herself drawn into a web of secrets and intrigue when she inherits a grand estate known as Dark Cedars. As she delves deeper into the history of the mansion, Amelia uncovers a series of mysterious events that have plagued the previous owners. With the help of a charming but enigmatic stranger, she must unravel the truth behind the dark secrets that haunt Dark Cedars before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Romance

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
