Synopsis: In 'My Sword's My Fortune: A Story of Old France' by Hayens, Herbert, we are transported to the enchanting world of medieval France, where chivalry, honor, and romance intertwine. The story follows the journey of a young and valiant knight, whose fate becomes entangled with a mysterious lady of noble birth. As he navigates treacherous battles, political intrigue, and forbidden love, our protagonist must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape his destiny. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this tale of adventure and passion will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Love, Identity, Destiny
