Synopsis: 
In 'Apology' by Plato, the renowned philosopher Socrates finds himself on trial for his life, accused of corrupting the youth and challenging the gods of Athens. As he defends himself against these charges, Socrates takes the reader on a thought-provoking journey, questioning the nature of wisdom, the pursuit of truth, and the meaning of a just life. Through his eloquent and unyielding defense, Socrates challenges the very foundations of society, leaving the reader to ponder the true nature of justice and the consequences of questioning authority.

Genres: Philosophy, Drama

Themes: Justice, Wisdom, Truth, Morality, Authority
