Synopsis: In 'A History of Babylon, from the Foundation of the Monarchy to the Persian Conquest', King L. W. takes readers on a captivating journey through the rise and fall of one of the most legendary ancient civilizations. From the awe-inspiring Hanging Gardens to the enigmatic Tower of Babel, this meticulously researched account unveils the secrets and grandeur of Babylon's golden age. As the narrative unfolds, readers will witness the power struggles, cultural achievements, and epic battles that shaped the destiny of this remarkable city. With vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, King L. W. brings to life the fascinating characters and events that defined Babylon's place in history.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Archaeology, Ancient Civilizations

Themes: Power and Politics, Cultural Achievements, Rise and Fall of Empires
