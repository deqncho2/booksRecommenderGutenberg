Synopsis: 
In the midst of a tempestuous storm, a powerful sorcerer named Prospero and his daughter Miranda find themselves stranded on a remote island. As they navigate their new surroundings, they encounter a host of intriguing characters, including a mischievous spirit named Ariel and a deformed creature called Caliban. Prospero, seeking revenge on those who wronged him in the past, uses his magical abilities to manipulate events and bring his enemies to the island. However, as the story unfolds, the boundaries between reality and illusion become blurred, and the true nature of forgiveness and redemption is explored.

Genres: 
Drama, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: 
Power and control, Revenge, Forgiveness, Illusion and reality
