Synopsis: 

"Eighteen months in the war zone: The record of a woman's work on the western front" takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of Kate John Finzi, a courageous woman who dedicated herself to serving on the front lines during World War I. Through her vivid and heartfelt accounts, we witness the challenges, triumphs, and heartbreaks she encounters while working tirelessly to provide aid and support to soldiers and civilians alike. As the war rages on, Finzi's determination and resilience are put to the test, revealing the true strength of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War Literature

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Humanitarianism, Gender Roles, Resilience
