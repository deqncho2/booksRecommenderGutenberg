Synopsis: 

'Practical Methods of Sewage Disposal for Residences, Hotels and Institutions' delves into the fascinating world of sewage disposal, offering innovative and efficient techniques for managing waste in various settings. Written by Henry N. Ogden and H. Burdett Cleveland, this comprehensive guide explores the challenges and solutions surrounding the disposal of sewage, providing practical advice and step-by-step instructions for implementing effective systems. From residential homes to large institutions, this book offers invaluable insights into the often overlooked but essential aspect of waste management. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Environmental Science, Engineering

Themes: Waste management, Environmental sustainability, Public health
