Synopsis: In 'The History of Modern Painting, Volume 3 (of 4): Revised edition continued by the author to the end of the XIX century', Richard Muther takes us on a captivating journey through the vibrant world of art in the 19th century. From the rise of Romanticism to the birth of Impressionism, Muther delves into the lives and works of renowned artists such as Delacroix, Turner, Courbet, and Monet. With meticulous research and insightful analysis, he explores the revolutionary techniques, evolving styles, and societal influences that shaped this transformative era in painting. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the artistic triumphs, controversies, and personal struggles that defined the artists of this period, ultimately leading to the dawn of modern art.

Genres: Art History, Non-fiction

Themes: Evolution of artistic styles, Societal influences on art, Lives and works of renowned artists
