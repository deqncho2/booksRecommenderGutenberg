Synopsis: In 'Some Reflections Upon Marriage: With additions,' Mary Astell delves into the complexities of marriage and challenges the traditional notions surrounding it. Through her insightful observations and thought-provoking arguments, she explores the power dynamics, societal expectations, and personal fulfillment within the institution of marriage. Astell's reflections offer a fresh perspective on the subject, inviting readers to question and reevaluate their own beliefs and experiences.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Feminist literature

Themes: Marriage, Gender roles, Power dynamics, Personal fulfillment
