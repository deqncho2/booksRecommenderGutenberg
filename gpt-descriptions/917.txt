Synopsis:
In the midst of the tumultuous events of the Gordon Riots in 1780s London, a young man named Barnaby Rudge finds himself caught up in a web of intrigue, mystery, and danger. As the riots escalate and the city descends into chaos, Barnaby becomes entangled with a diverse cast of characters, including a mysterious stranger, a beautiful young woman, and a group of rebels seeking justice. With secrets and betrayals lurking around every corner, Barnaby must navigate the treacherous landscape of the riots while uncovering the truth about his own past. Will he be able to survive the riots and find redemption, or will he be consumed by the violence and chaos that surrounds him?

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Social unrest, Identity, Redemption
