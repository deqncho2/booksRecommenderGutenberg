Synopsis: 
In "Apu Ollantay: A Drama of the Time of the Incas," Sir Clements R. Markham takes readers on a captivating journey to the ancient Inca civilization. Set against the backdrop of the majestic Andes Mountains, this dramatic tale follows the forbidden love between Apu Ollantay, a noble warrior, and Cusi Coyllur, a beautiful princess. As their love blossoms, they face numerous challenges and must navigate the treacherous political landscape of the Inca Empire. Filled with intrigue, passion, and sacrifice, this gripping drama explores the clash between tradition and personal desires, ultimately leading to a climactic confrontation that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Forbidden love, Power and politics, Tradition and rebellion
