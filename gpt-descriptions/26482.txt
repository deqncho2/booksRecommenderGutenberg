Synopsis: 
In "Madeline Payne, the Detective's Daughter" by Lynch, Lawrence L., Madeline Payne finds herself thrust into the world of crime-solving when her father, a renowned detective, mysteriously disappears. Determined to uncover the truth, Madeline embarks on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As she delves deeper into her father's disappearance, she uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that threatens to unravel everything she thought she knew. With her sharp wit and keen intuition, Madeline must navigate a dangerous underworld to bring her father home and uncover the truth behind his vanishing act.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Family bonds, Identity, Deception
