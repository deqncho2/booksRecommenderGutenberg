Synopsis: In 'An Encounter in Atlanta' by Howdershelt, Ed, a chance meeting between two strangers in the bustling city of Atlanta sets off a chain of events that will test their courage, loyalty, and ultimately, their very lives. As they navigate through a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances, the protagonists find themselves entangled in a thrilling race against time, where every decision could mean the difference between survival and destruction. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Trust, Betrayal, Survival
