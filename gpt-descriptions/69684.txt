Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, a mysterious red blight begins to spread, infecting the minds and bodies of its residents. As the town descends into chaos, a group of unlikely heroes must uncover the truth behind the blight and find a way to stop it before it consumes them all. With secrets, suspense, and unexpected twists, "Red Blight" takes readers on a thrilling journey through the darkness that lies within the human soul.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Corruption, Survival, Betrayal
