Synopsis: In 'Myths and Dreams', Edward Clodd takes readers on a captivating journey through the realms of ancient mythology and the mysterious world of dreams. Delving into the rich tapestry of human imagination, Clodd explores the origins and significance of various myths from different cultures, unraveling their hidden meanings and shedding light on the universal human experience. From the enchanting tales of gods and goddesses to the enigmatic symbolism of dreams, this book offers a thought-provoking exploration of the human psyche and the power of storytelling.

Genres: Mythology, Psychology, Literary Criticism

Themes: Mythology, Dreams, Symbolism, Human Psychology
