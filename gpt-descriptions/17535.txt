Synopsis: In 'The Jester of St. Timothy's' by Pier, Arthur Stanwood, we are introduced to a mischievous and quick-witted young boy named Jack, who finds himself in the midst of a thrilling adventure at St. Timothy's boarding school. Jack's clever pranks and humorous escapades keep the students and faculty on their toes, but when a mysterious treasure hunt begins, Jack's skills are put to the ultimate test. As he navigates through secret passages, hidden clues, and unexpected alliances, Jack discovers that there is more to this treasure hunt than meets the eye. With danger lurking around every corner, Jack must use his wit and cunning to unravel the secrets of St. Timothy's and uncover the truth behind the treasure hunt.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
