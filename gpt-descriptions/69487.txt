Synopsis: 
'The Complete Servant' is a practical guide that provides invaluable advice and guidance to servants of all kinds, from housekeepers to foot-boys. Written by Samuel Adams, a seasoned servant, and Sarah Adams, his wife, this comprehensive manual covers the peculiar duties and responsibilities of each role within a household. Filled with useful receipts and tables, this book is an essential resource for anyone seeking to excel in their service. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Household management

Themes: Servitude, Household management, Professionalism
