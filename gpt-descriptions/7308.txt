Synopsis:
"The History of Mr. Polly" follows the life of Alfred Polly, a man who feels trapped in a monotonous existence and yearns for something more. Faced with a series of disappointments and setbacks, he embarks on a journey of self-discovery, seeking to break free from the constraints of society and find his true purpose. As he navigates through various adventures and encounters, Mr. Polly's story takes unexpected turns, leading him to question the meaning of life and the choices he has made. This captivating novel explores the complexities of human nature and the pursuit of happiness, offering a poignant reflection on the universal desire for personal fulfillment.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Existentialism, Individualism, Escape from Conformity
