Synopsis: In 'The Making of Species', Dewar and Finn take readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of evolutionary biology. With a blend of scientific rigor and engaging storytelling, they unveil the secrets behind the origin and diversification of species. From the intricate mechanisms of natural selection to the astonishing adaptations that have shaped life on Earth, this book offers a thought-provoking exploration of the forces that have driven the evolution of all living organisms. Prepare to be enthralled by the wonders of the natural world and the remarkable processes that have shaped its incredible biodiversity.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction, Biology

Themes: Evolution, Natural selection, Biodiversity
