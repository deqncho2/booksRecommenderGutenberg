Synopsis: In 'Swords Reluctant' by Pemberton, Max, a young blacksmith named Ethan finds himself thrust into a world of danger and adventure when he discovers an ancient sword with a mysterious power. Reluctantly accepting his newfound destiny, Ethan embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind the sword's origins and unlock his own hidden potential. Along the way, he encounters formidable enemies, forms unlikely alliances, and learns that sometimes, the greatest strength lies within oneself.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Destiny, Self-discovery, Courage, Friendship
