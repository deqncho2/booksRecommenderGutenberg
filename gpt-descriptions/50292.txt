Synopsis: In "The Laughter of Peterkin: A retelling of old tales of the Celtic Wonderworld," William Sharp takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting realm of Celtic folklore. Through the eyes of Peterkin, a mischievous and curious young boy, we are transported to a world filled with magical creatures, ancient legends, and extraordinary adventures. As Peterkin encounters mythical beings such as fairies, selkies, and leprechauns, he learns valuable lessons about bravery, friendship, and the power of laughter. With each tale, Sharp weaves a tapestry of wonder and imagination, inviting readers to rediscover the timeless allure of Celtic mythology.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Friendship, Courage, Imagination
