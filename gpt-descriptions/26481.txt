Synopsis: In 'The Romance of Mathematics', we are introduced to a Lady Professor at Girtham College who embarks on a journey of original research in polemical science. Along the way, she explores the intriguing social properties of a conic, delves into equations that relate to brain waves, uncovers the hidden forces that shape society, and unravels the laws of political motion. This captivating tale takes readers on a thought-provoking adventure, where the boundaries between mathematics, science, and society blur, leaving us questioning the very fabric of our world.

Genres: Fiction, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Mathematics, Science, Society, Power, Exploration
