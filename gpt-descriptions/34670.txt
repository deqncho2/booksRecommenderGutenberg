Synopsis: In 'Camping at Cherry Pond' by Abbott, Henry, a group of friends embarks on a seemingly ordinary camping trip that quickly turns into a thrilling adventure. As they navigate through the dense forest and encounter unexpected challenges, they uncover a hidden secret that could change their lives forever. With danger lurking at every corner, the friends must rely on their wit, courage, and friendship to survive the wilderness and unravel the mystery that awaits them at Cherry Pond.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Discovery
