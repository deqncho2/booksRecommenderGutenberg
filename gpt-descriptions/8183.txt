Synopsis: 
In "Time and the Gods" by Lord Dunsany, embark on a mesmerizing journey through a realm where gods and mortals coexist, and time itself is a malleable force. From the creation of the world to the whimsical adventures of deities, this collection of interconnected stories weaves a tapestry of enchantment and wonder. Encounter ancient gods who shape the destiny of mankind, witness the clash between immortals and mortals, and explore the boundaries of reality in this captivating exploration of myth and imagination.

Genres: Fantasy, Mythology, Short Stories

Themes: Time, Divinity, Fate, Mortality
