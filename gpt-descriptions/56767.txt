Synopsis: In 'Those Other Animals' by G.A. Henty, a young boy named Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden world filled with extraordinary creatures. As he befriends these mysterious animals, Jack discovers that they possess incredible abilities and secrets that could change his life forever. However, danger lurks in the shadows, and Jack must navigate treacherous obstacles to protect his newfound friends and uncover the truth behind their existence.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Discovery, Courage, Conservation
