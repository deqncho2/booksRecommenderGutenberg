Synopsis: 
In 'When the Sea Gives Up Its Dead: A Thrilling Detective Story', a seasoned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious body washes ashore. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that lead him down a dangerous path. With each clue he uncovers, the detective becomes more determined to solve the case, even if it means putting his own life at risk. As the story unfolds, the tension builds, keeping readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic corpse and the dark forces at play.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption, Justice
