Synopsis: In 'The Banshee' by O'Donnell, Elliott, a small Irish village becomes plagued by a mysterious and chilling presence that haunts the locals. As the townspeople grapple with their fear and superstitions, a young woman named Maeve finds herself drawn into the heart of the mystery. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, she must uncover the truth behind the banshee's haunting cries before it claims more lives. This gripping tale of suspense and folklore will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Superstition, Identity
