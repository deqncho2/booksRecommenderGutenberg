Synopsis: 
'Parsons on the Rose: A Treatise on the Propagation, Culture and History of the Rose' by Samuel Browne Parsons is a captivating exploration of the world of roses. This comprehensive guide takes readers on a journey through the fascinating history, cultivation techniques, and propagation methods of these beloved flowers. From the ancient civilizations that revered roses to the modern-day enthusiasts who dedicate their lives to their care, Parsons delves into the rich tapestry of rose culture. With vivid descriptions and practical advice, this treatise is a must-read for anyone seeking to deepen their understanding and appreciation of these timeless blooms.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gardening, History

Themes: Horticulture, Botany, Cultural significance
