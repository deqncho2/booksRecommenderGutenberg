Synopsis: 

In 'Uncle Sam's Boys with Pershing's Troops: Or, Dick Prescott at Grips with the Boche', author H. Irving Hancock takes readers on an exhilarating journey alongside Dick Prescott, a young American soldier, as he faces the challenges of war on the frontlines. As part of General Pershing's troops, Dick finds himself in the midst of intense battles against the Boche, the German forces. With his unwavering determination and resourcefulness, Dick must navigate the treacherous landscapes of war, relying on his courage and quick thinking to outwit the enemy and protect his fellow soldiers. 

Genres: Adventure, War, Historical Fiction

Themes: Patriotism, Bravery, Camaraderie, Survival
