Synopsis: In 'Somewhere in France' by Davis, Richard Harding, a young American journalist finds himself caught in the midst of World War I as he embarks on a dangerous mission to uncover the truth behind a mysterious disappearance. As he navigates the treacherous landscapes of war-torn France, he encounters unexpected allies and uncovers shocking secrets that will test his courage and determination. With each step closer to the truth, he becomes entangled in a web of espionage, romance, and betrayal, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, War

Themes: Love and sacrifice, the impact of war on individuals, the pursuit of truth and justice
