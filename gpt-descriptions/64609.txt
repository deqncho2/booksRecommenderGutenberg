Synopsis: In 'A West Point Treasure; Or, Mark Mallory's Strange Find', Mark Mallory, a cadet at West Point, stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to a hidden treasure. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the treasure, Mark finds himself caught in a web of danger and intrigue, where his loyalty and courage will be tested. With the help of his friends, Mark embarks on a thrilling adventure that will challenge his wits and lead him to unexpected discoveries.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Treasure hunting, Friendship, Loyalty, Courage
