Synopsis: 
"The Works of Guy de Maupassant, Vol. 1: Boule de Suif and Other Stories" is a captivating collection of short stories by the renowned French author, Guy de Maupassant. This volume introduces readers to the unforgettable character of Boule de Suif, a prostitute with a mysterious past, and takes them on a journey through a variety of intriguing tales. From tales of love and betrayal to stories of social class and morality, each narrative offers a unique glimpse into the complexities of human nature. With Maupassant's masterful storytelling and vivid descriptions, readers will be transported to a world filled with passion, suspense, and unexpected twists.

Genres: 
Short stories, Fiction, Literary Fiction

Themes: 
Love and betrayal, Social class, Morality, Human nature
