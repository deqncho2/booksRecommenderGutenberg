Synopsis: In 'Autobiography of Matthew Scott, Jumbo's Keeper; Also Jumbo's Biography, by the same Author', Matthew Scott takes us on a captivating journey through his life as the keeper of the legendary elephant, Jumbo. From their first encounter to the incredible adventures they shared, Scott's memoir offers a unique perspective on the bond between man and beast. As he delves into Jumbo's biography, Scott uncovers surprising secrets and sheds light on the true nature of this magnificent creature. Prepare to be enthralled by this heartfelt tale of friendship, loyalty, and the extraordinary life of Jumbo.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Animal-Human Connection
