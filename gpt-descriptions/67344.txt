Synopsis: 

Embark on a captivating journey through the rich mythologies of China and Japan in "Myths of China and Japan: with illustrations in colour & monochrome after paintings and photographs" by Donald A. Mackenzie. This enchanting book delves into the ancient tales and legends that have shaped the cultural fabric of these two fascinating countries. From celestial dragons and mischievous fox spirits to noble warriors and wise sages, the pages come alive with vivid illustrations that bring these mythical beings to life. Discover the captivating stories of gods and goddesses, heroes and heroines, and the timeless battles between good and evil. 

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Art

Themes: Cultural heritage, Legends and folklore, Supernatural beings
