Synopsis: 

In "Reminiscences of the Great Mutiny 1857-59," William Forbes-Mitchell takes us on a gripping journey through one of the most tumultuous periods in Indian history. Through his vivid and personal accounts, we witness the harrowing events of the Indian Rebellion of 1857, including the relief, siege, and capture of Lucknow, as well as the campaigns in Rohilcund and Oude. As Forbes-Mitchell recounts his experiences as a participant in these historic events, the reader is transported to a time of intense conflict, bravery, and resilience.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Military history

Themes: Rebellion, Colonialism, Heroism, Survival
