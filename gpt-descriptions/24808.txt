Synopsis: 

In 'All Afloat: A Chronicle of Craft and Waterways', William Wood takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of boats and waterways. From ancient times to the modern era, Wood explores the rich history, diverse types, and remarkable stories behind various watercrafts. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, he unveils the secrets of shipbuilding, the thrill of sailing, and the significance of water transportation in shaping human civilization. Whether it's the majestic tall ships of the past or the sleek yachts of the present, this chronicle immerses readers in the captivating world of boats and their enduring allure.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Maritime

Themes: Water transportation, Shipbuilding, Human civilization
