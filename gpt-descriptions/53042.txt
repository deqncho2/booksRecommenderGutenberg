Synopsis: In 'A Hitch in Space' by Leiber, Fritz, a group of space travelers find themselves stranded on a mysterious planet after their ship is damaged. As they explore their new surroundings, they encounter strange creatures, ancient ruins, and a hidden power that could change the fate of the universe. With their survival at stake, they must unravel the secrets of this enigmatic world and find a way back home.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Discovery, The Unknown
