Synopsis: In "Shakespeare and Precious Stones," George Frederick Kunz delves into the fascinating world of Shakespeare's works, uncovering the hidden references to precious stones. With meticulous research, Kunz explores the origins of Shakespeare's material, shedding light on the poet's knowledge and fascination with these gemstones. From diamonds to rubies, emeralds to pearls, this book uncovers the secrets behind the precious stones of Shakespeare's time and the significance they held in his works.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Historical Analysis, Gemology

Themes: Symbolism, Material Culture, Renaissance Literature
