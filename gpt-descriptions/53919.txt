Synopsis: In 'Sister Gertrude: A Tale of the West Riding', the reader is transported to the captivating world of a small village in the West Riding of Yorkshire. The story revolves around Sister Gertrude, a mysterious and enigmatic woman who arrives in the village, bringing with her a sense of intrigue and curiosity. As the villagers become increasingly fascinated by her presence, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden pasts that will forever change their lives. With its richly drawn characters and atmospheric setting, this tale explores the complexities of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Redemption, Human nature
