Synopsis: In the distant future, Earth has been conquered by an alien race known as the "Wolves," who have transformed the planet into a desolate wasteland. Amidst this bleak existence, a group of rebels known as the "Wolvesbane" rise up to challenge their oppressors. As they embark on a dangerous mission to reclaim their world, they uncover shocking secrets about the true nature of the Wolves and the fate of humanity. With thrilling action and thought-provoking twists, "Wolfbane" takes readers on an epic journey of resistance and discovery.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Adventure

Themes: Rebellion, Survival, Identity, Power
