Synopsis: 
En Route by J.-K. Huysmans takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Durtal, a writer who embarks on a spiritual quest that leads him to explore the realms of faith, art, and the occult. As Durtal delves deeper into his search for meaning, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters and finds himself entangled in a web of mysticism, decadence, and forbidden desires. With vivid descriptions and thought-provoking encounters, En Route offers a mesmerizing exploration of the human soul and the eternal struggle between the sacred and the profane.

Genres: Fiction, Spiritual, Gothic

Themes: Spiritual quest, Art and aesthetics, Mysticism, Decadence, Forbidden desires
