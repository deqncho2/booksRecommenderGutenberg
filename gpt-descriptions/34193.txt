Synopsis: In "The True Benjamin Franklin," Sydney George Fisher delves into the life of one of America's most iconic founding fathers, revealing a side of Benjamin Franklin that is often overlooked. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Fisher uncovers the lesser-known aspects of Franklin's life, from his early struggles to his rise as a prominent statesman and inventor. With surprising anecdotes and a fresh perspective, this biography offers a captivating exploration of the man behind the legend.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: American Revolution, Enlightenment ideals, Personal growth
