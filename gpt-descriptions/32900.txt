Synopsis: In 'Rats in the Belfry' by O'Brien, David Wright, a small town is plagued by a mysterious infestation of rats that seem to be causing chaos everywhere they go. As the townspeople struggle to find a solution, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden secret that could hold the key to stopping the rat invasion. With the help of her friends, Lily embarks on a thrilling adventure to save her town from the relentless rodents, uncovering surprising truths along the way.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Overcoming adversity
