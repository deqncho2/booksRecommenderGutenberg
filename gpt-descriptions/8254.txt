Synopsis: In 'The World English Bible (WEB): Daniel' by Anonymous, a young man named Daniel finds himself thrust into a world of political intrigue and divine visions. As he navigates the treacherous court of King Nebuchadnezzar, Daniel's unwavering faith and wisdom are put to the ultimate test. With the fate of nations hanging in the balance, Daniel must decipher cryptic dreams and face daunting challenges that will shape the course of history. This captivating biblical tale explores the power of faith, the struggle between good and evil, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Biblical fiction, Historical fiction, Religious literature

Themes: Faith, Wisdom, Divine intervention, Prophecy, Courage
