Synopsis: In "The House Boat Boys; Or, Drifting Down to the Sunny South," a group of adventurous boys embark on a thrilling journey down the Mississippi River on a houseboat. As they navigate the treacherous waters, encounter mysterious characters, and face unexpected challenges, they find themselves immersed in a world of excitement and danger. With each twist and turn, the boys uncover secrets and unravel a captivating mystery that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Independence, Courage
