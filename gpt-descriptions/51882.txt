Synopsis: 

In 'Trial of the Major War Criminals Before the International Military Tribunal, Nuremberg, 14 November 1945-1 October 1946, Volume 02', the gripping continuation of the historic Nuremberg Trials unfolds. This volume delves into the intricate details of the prosecution's case against the major war criminals, revealing shocking testimonies and evidence that shed light on the atrocities committed during World War II. As the trial progresses, tensions rise, secrets are unveiled, and the true extent of the defendants' involvement in the Holocaust and other war crimes is exposed. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Legal drama, True crime

Themes: Justice, Accountability, Human rights, Holocaust, World War II
