Synopsis: In 'The Merry-go-round', W. Somerset Maugham takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of a group of individuals whose paths intertwine in unexpected ways. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant and glamorous world of 1920s London, the story delves into the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness. As the characters navigate the highs and lows of their relationships and careers, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and lives are forever changed. With Maugham's signature wit and keen observation, 'The Merry-go-round' offers a compelling exploration of human desires and the consequences of our choices.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and relationships, Ambition, Society and class, Secrets and deception
