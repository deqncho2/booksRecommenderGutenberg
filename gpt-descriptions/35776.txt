Synopsis: In 'The Way of the Strong' by Cullum Ridgwell, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small western town, bringing with him a dark past and a deadly secret. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and revenge, they must confront their own inner demons and make choices that will determine their fate. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Justice, Morality
