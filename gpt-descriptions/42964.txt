Synopsis: 

In 'Bible Animals: Being a Description of Every Living Creature Mentioned in the Scripture, from the Ape to the Coral,' J. G. Wood takes readers on a captivating journey through the animal kingdom as described in the Bible. From the majestic lion to the humble ant, Wood explores the fascinating creatures that played a role in biblical stories and teachings. With vivid descriptions and historical context, this book offers a unique perspective on the animals that have shaped religious narratives for centuries.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Religious Studies

Themes: Biblical references, Animal symbolism, Religious teachings
