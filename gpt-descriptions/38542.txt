Synopsis: 

'A Century of Sail and Steam on the Niagara River' takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the rich history and dramatic transformations that have occurred along the Niagara River. From the majestic era of sailboats to the advent of steam-powered vessels, the book delves into the stories of the people, the ships, and the communities that thrived along this iconic waterway. With vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, the narrative unveils the triumphs, challenges, and secrets hidden within the river's currents. As the pages turn, readers will be transported to a bygone era, where the clash between tradition and progress unfolds against the backdrop of one of North America's most awe-inspiring natural wonders.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Maritime history, Local history

Themes: Transformation, Progress, Community, Tradition
