Synopsis:
In 'The Pigeon Pie', a young woman named Lucilla is thrust into a world of intrigue and mystery when she becomes entangled in a case of mistaken identity. As she navigates through a web of secrets and hidden agendas, Lucilla must rely on her wit and resourcefulness to uncover the truth and protect her loved ones. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres:
Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes:
Identity, Deception, Love and Loyalty
