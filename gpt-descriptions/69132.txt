Synopsis: 
In "Betty Wales on the Campus," Betty Wales returns to college for her sophomore year, ready to take on new challenges and make lasting friendships. As she navigates the complexities of campus life, Betty finds herself caught up in a whirlwind of social events, academic pressures, and unexpected romantic entanglements. With her wit, determination, and unwavering spirit, Betty must learn to balance her personal ambitions with the demands of her studies and the ever-changing dynamics of college life.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Friendship, Ambition, Self-discovery
