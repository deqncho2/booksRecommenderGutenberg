Synopsis: In 'The Motor Girls at Camp Surprise; Or, The Cave in the Mountains' by Margaret Penrose, a group of adventurous young women embark on a thrilling summer camp adventure. When they stumble upon a mysterious cave hidden deep in the mountains, they find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected events that test their courage and resourcefulness. As they navigate through dangerous situations and unravel the secrets of the cave, the Motor Girls must rely on their friendship and determination to overcome the challenges they face.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
