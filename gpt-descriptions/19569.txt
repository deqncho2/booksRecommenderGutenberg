Synopsis: In "The Guests of Hercules," a mysterious invitation leads a group of strangers to a secluded island, where they find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger. As they navigate the treacherous landscape, they must unravel the secrets of the island and confront their own hidden pasts. With each twist and turn, the guests are forced to question their own motives and alliances, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
