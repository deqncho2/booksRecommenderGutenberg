Synopsis: In 'The Highest Treason' by Randall Garrett, the year is 2100 and Earth is under the rule of the Unity, a totalitarian government that controls every aspect of people's lives. When a brilliant scientist discovers a shocking secret that could shatter the Unity's grip on power, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of espionage and betrayal. As he navigates a treacherous world of political intrigue and hidden agendas, he must decide whether to risk everything for the truth or succumb to the oppressive regime. With twists and turns at every corner, 'The Highest Treason' is a thrilling tale of rebellion and the fight for freedom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Dystopian

Themes: Totalitarianism, Resistance, Truth and Deception
