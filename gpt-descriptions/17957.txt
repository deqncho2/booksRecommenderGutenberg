Synopsis: 
In 'On the Sublime', Longinus takes readers on a captivating journey through the power of language and the art of rhetoric. Through a series of thought-provoking discussions and examples, he explores the concept of the sublime and its ability to evoke awe, inspiration, and even fear in the hearts of its audience. Longinus delves into the techniques and qualities that elevate writing to the realm of the sublime, examining the works of renowned authors from the past and present. With eloquence and passion, he challenges readers to embrace the transformative potential of the sublime and to harness its force in their own writing.

Genres: 
Philosophy, Literary Criticism

Themes: 
The power of language, the art of rhetoric, the sublime as a transformative force
