Synopsis: In 'The Cat and Fiddle Book: Eight Dramatised Nursery Rhymes for Nursery Performers', Lady Bell and Lady Richmond bring beloved nursery rhymes to life through delightful and imaginative dramatizations. From the mischievous adventures of Jack and Jill to the enchanting world of Humpty Dumpty, this book invites young performers to step into the shoes of their favorite nursery rhyme characters and bring their stories to the stage. With lively dialogue, catchy songs, and colorful costumes, this collection is sure to captivate both children and adults alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Drama, Musical

Themes: Imagination, Creativity, Playfulness
