Synopsis: In 'The Fairies and the Christmas Child' by Lilian Gask, a young girl named Mabel discovers a hidden world of enchantment when she stumbles upon a group of mischievous fairies. As Christmas approaches, Mabel becomes entangled in a magical adventure that will test her courage and belief in the power of love and kindness. With the help of her newfound fairy friends, she learns valuable lessons about the true meaning of Christmas and the importance of embracing the wonder and joy of the holiday season.

Genres: Fantasy, Children's Literature

Themes: Magic, Friendship, Christmas Spirit
