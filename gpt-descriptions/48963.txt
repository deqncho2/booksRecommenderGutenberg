Synopsis: In 'The Viper of Milan: A Romance of Lombardy', the reader is transported to 14th century Italy, where political intrigue, forbidden love, and a mysterious masked figure known as the Viper intertwine to create a captivating tale. As the city of Milan is torn apart by rival factions and foreign powers, a young noblewoman finds herself caught in the midst of the chaos. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate treacherous alliances and make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of her beloved city. Will she succumb to the Viper's seductive charm or will she find the strength to resist? This historical romance is filled with suspense, passion, and a vivid portrayal of a tumultuous era.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Power Struggles, Identity and Masks
