Synopsis: 
'Combat lessons, no. 1, rank and file in combat: What they are doing, how they do it' by United States. War Department is an insightful guide that delves into the world of combat, providing a captivating exploration of the actions and strategies employed by the rank and file soldiers. Through vivid descriptions and real-life examples, this book offers a unique perspective on the intricacies of combat, shedding light on the crucial role played by the soldiers on the front lines. From tactical maneuvers to the psychology of warfare, readers will gain a deeper understanding of the challenges faced by these brave individuals. 

Genres: Military, Non-fiction, Historical

Themes: Combat strategies, Soldier psychology, Military tactics
