Synopsis: 
In 'A Prisoner in Turkey' by John Still, a young British archaeologist finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger while on a dig in Turkey. When he stumbles upon a mysterious artifact, he becomes the target of a powerful secret society that will stop at nothing to possess it. As he fights for his life and tries to unravel the secrets surrounding the artifact, he discovers a hidden world of ancient secrets, betrayal, and a love that could either save him or destroy him. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous landscape where nothing is as it seems, and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Archaeology, Secret societies, Betrayal, Love and loyalty
