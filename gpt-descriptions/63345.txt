Synopsis: In 'Lessons in Wireless Telegraphy' by Alfred Powell Morgan, a young inventor embarks on a thrilling journey into the world of wireless communication. As he delves deeper into the secrets of this groundbreaking technology, he uncovers a hidden world of espionage, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each lesson, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that challenges his skills and tests his courage. Will he be able to navigate this treacherous landscape and unlock the true potential of wireless telegraphy?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Invention and Innovation, Espionage and Intrigue
