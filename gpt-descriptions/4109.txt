Synopsis: In the second volume of "Christopher Columbus and the New World of His Discovery," Filson Young continues his captivating exploration of the legendary explorer's journey to the New World. As Columbus and his crew face numerous challenges and encounters with indigenous peoples, the narrative delves deeper into the complexities of their interactions and the impact of their discoveries. Young's vivid storytelling brings to life the triumphs and tribulations of Columbus, shedding light on the untold aspects of his historic voyage.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Cultural encounters, Legacy
