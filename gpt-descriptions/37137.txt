Synopsis: In 'A Hind Let Loose', Alexander Shields presents a captivating historical representation of the testimonies of the Church of Scotland for the interest of Christ. Through a meticulous exploration of the true state of the church in various periods, Shields unveils a compelling narrative that will leave readers enthralled. This work delves into the struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices of the church, shedding light on its unwavering commitment to Christ's cause. As the story unfolds, readers will be drawn into a world of religious fervor, political intrigue, and the enduring power of faith.

Genres: Historical, Religious, Non-fiction

Themes: Church history, Religious persecution, Faith and devotion
