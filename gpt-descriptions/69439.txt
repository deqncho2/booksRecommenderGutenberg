Synopsis: In 'Physiological economy in nutrition, with special reference to the minimal proteid requirement of the healthy man: an experimental study', Chittenden delves into a groundbreaking investigation that challenges conventional wisdom about the human body's protein needs. Through meticulous experimentation, he uncovers surprising findings that could revolutionize our understanding of nutrition and its impact on overall health. This thought-provoking study offers a fresh perspective on the minimal proteid requirement of the healthy man, leaving readers eager to explore the implications of Chittenden's research.

Genres: Scientific research, Nutrition, Health

Themes: Human physiology, Dietary requirements, Scientific experimentation
