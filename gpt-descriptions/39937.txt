Synopsis: In 'The Long Dim Trail' by Forrestine C. Hooker, a young woman named Mary embarks on a perilous journey through the rugged wilderness of the American West. Faced with treacherous landscapes, dangerous outlaws, and unexpected allies, Mary must summon her courage and resilience to survive. As she navigates the untamed frontier, she discovers secrets about her own past that could change her life forever.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Survival, Courage, Redemption
