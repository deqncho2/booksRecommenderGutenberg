Synopsis: In the second volume of "The Shaving of Shagpat; an Arabian entertainment," the enchanting tale continues as the protagonist, Shibli Bagarag, embarks on a perilous journey to rescue his beloved, the beautiful Noorna. Along the way, he encounters a series of fantastical adventures, including encounters with powerful sorcerers, treacherous djinns, and cunning thieves. As Shibli's determination and wit are tested, he must navigate a world filled with magic, deception, and unexpected twists. Will he succeed in his quest and win the heart of his true love? 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Love and devotion, Hero's journey, Magic and enchantment
