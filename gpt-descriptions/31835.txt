Synopsis: 
In 'Prisoners of Conscience' by Amelia E. Barr, a gripping tale unfolds as two individuals find themselves entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal sacrifice. Set against the backdrop of a tumultuous era, their paths converge in a fight for justice and freedom. As secrets are unveiled and loyalties are tested, the protagonists must navigate a treacherous landscape where the line between right and wrong becomes blurred. With each page, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the ultimate fate of these prisoners of conscience.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Drama

Themes: Political oppression, Moral dilemmas, Personal sacrifice
