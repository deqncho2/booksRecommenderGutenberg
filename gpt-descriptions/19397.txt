Synopsis: 

In 'History of Rationalism Embracing a Survey of the Present State of Protestant Theology', J.F. Hurst takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of rationalism and its impact on Protestant theology. From the early days of the Reformation to the modern era, Hurst explores the intellectual and philosophical movements that have shaped religious thought. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for storytelling, he delves into the lives of influential thinkers and theologians, revealing the conflicts and controversies that have shaped the landscape of Protestantism. As the narrative unfolds, readers are drawn into a world of intellectual exploration, religious upheaval, and the quest for truth. 

Genres: History, Theology, Non-fiction

Themes: Rationalism, Protestantism, Intellectual History, Religious Thought
