Synopsis: In 'King--of the Khyber Rifles: A Romance of Adventure' by Talbot Mundy, we follow the thrilling journey of Captain Athelstan King, a British officer stationed in the treacherous Khyber Pass. Tasked with maintaining peace in the region, King finds himself caught between loyalty to his country and the allure of the mysterious and captivating Princess Yasmini. As he navigates the dangerous landscape of espionage, betrayal, and political intrigue, King must make difficult choices that will test his courage and honor. Will he be able to fulfill his duty while also following his heart?

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Duty, Love, Cultural Clash
