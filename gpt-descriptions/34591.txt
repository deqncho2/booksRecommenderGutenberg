Synopsis: In 'Clue of the Silken Ladder' by Mildred A. Wirt, Nancy Drew finds herself entangled in a mysterious case involving a missing heirloom and a secret society. As she follows the trail of clues, Nancy must navigate a web of deception, danger, and hidden identities. With her keen detective skills and unwavering determination, she races against time to unravel the truth and bring justice to those involved. Will Nancy be able to solve the puzzle before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Trust and betrayal, Courage and perseverance
