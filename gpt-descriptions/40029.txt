Synopsis: 

In "The Gunpowder Plot and Lord Mounteagle's Letter, Being a Proof, with Moral Certitude, of the Authorship of the Document: Together with Some Account of the Whole Thirteen Gunpowder Conspirators, Including Guy Fawkes," author Henry Spink delves into the infamous Gunpowder Plot of 1605. This historical account explores the events leading up to the plot, the involvement of Lord Mounteagle, and provides an in-depth look at the thirteen conspirators, including the notorious Guy Fawkes. Spink presents evidence that sheds light on the authorship of the letter that ultimately exposed the plot, offering a compelling argument that challenges previous assumptions.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, True crime, Biography

Themes: Conspiracy, Betrayal, Loyalty, Political intrigue
