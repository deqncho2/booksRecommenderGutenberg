Synopsis: In 'The Mystery Crash: Sky Scout Series, #1' by Powell, Van, a group of young friends stumble upon a mysterious crash site in the woods near their small town. As they investigate the wreckage, they uncover clues that lead them on a thrilling adventure, filled with unexpected twists and turns. With each discovery, they find themselves drawn deeper into a web of secrets and danger, where nothing is as it seems. As they race against time to unravel the truth, they must rely on their courage, intelligence, and teamwork to solve the mystery before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Discovery
