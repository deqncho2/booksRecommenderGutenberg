Synopsis: In 'The Lady's Knitting-Book: Containing eighty clear and easy patterns of useful and ornamental knitting', Elvina Mary Corbould presents a delightful collection of knitting patterns that will captivate both beginners and experienced knitters alike. From intricate lace shawls to cozy winter accessories, this book offers a wide range of designs that are both practical and beautiful. With detailed instructions and helpful tips, Corbould guides readers through each pattern, ensuring a rewarding knitting experience. Whether you're looking to create a stunning gift or simply indulge in a relaxing hobby, 'The Lady's Knitting-Book' is a must-have for any knitting enthusiast.

Genres: Craft, Non-fiction

Themes: Knitting, Creativity, Self-expression
