Synopsis: 
'Colonel Edward Buncombe, Fifth North Carolina Continental Regiment: His Life, Military Career, and Death While a Wounded Prisoner in Philadelphia During the War of the Revolution' delves into the captivating story of Colonel Edward Buncombe, a prominent figure in the American Revolution. From his early life to his valiant military service, the book explores the triumphs and challenges that shaped Buncombe's journey. However, the narrative takes a dramatic turn when Buncombe becomes a wounded prisoner in Philadelphia, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Resilience
