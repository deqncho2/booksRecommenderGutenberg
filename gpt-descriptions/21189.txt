Synopsis: In 'Aesop, in Rhyme: Old Friends in a New Dress', Aesop's timeless fables are given a fresh twist as they are presented in delightful rhymes. From the clever tortoise and the boastful hare to the wise ant and the lazy grasshopper, these classic tales come alive in a new and engaging way. With colorful illustrations and memorable characters, this collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Poetry, Fables

Themes: Morality, Wisdom, Life Lessons
