Synopsis: In 'Jack Hardy: A Story of English Smugglers in the Days of Napoleon' by Strang, Herbert, readers are transported to a thrilling world of adventure and danger. Set during the time of Napoleon, the story follows the daring exploits of Jack Hardy, a young English smuggler who finds himself caught up in a web of intrigue and treachery. As Jack navigates the treacherous waters of the English Channel, he must outwit both the authorities and rival smugglers to protect his livelihood and the secrets he holds. With its gripping plot and vivid descriptions, this tale of smugglers and spies will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Smuggling, Espionage, Loyalty, Survival
