Synopsis: In 'My American Diary' by Sheridan, Clare, a young woman embarks on a life-changing journey to the United States, leaving behind her familiar world in search of adventure, self-discovery, and the pursuit of her dreams. Through her captivating diary entries, she takes us on a whirlwind exploration of the vibrant American landscape, encountering a diverse cast of characters and experiencing both the highs and lows of her new life. As she navigates the challenges of adapting to a foreign culture, she discovers unexpected friendships, faces personal obstacles, and ultimately finds herself on a transformative path of growth and resilience. This poignant and captivating diary offers a unique perspective on the American experience, as seen through the eyes of an intrepid traveler.

Genres: Travelogue, Coming-of-age, Memoir

Themes: Adventure, Self-discovery, Cultural exploration
