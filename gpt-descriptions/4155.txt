Synopsis: In the thirty-fourth volume of his renowned diary, Samuel Pepys takes us on a captivating journey through the months of March and April in 1664-65. As Pepys navigates the bustling streets of London, he encounters a myriad of intriguing characters and finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, scandalous affairs, and personal triumphs. From his encounters with influential figures to his own personal struggles, Pepys provides a vivid and intimate account of the tumultuous events that shape his world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Diary

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Relationships, Personal Growth
