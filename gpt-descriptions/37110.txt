Synopsis: In 'Frontier Folk' by Booth, George, a group of unlikely companions embarks on a perilous journey through the untamed wilderness of the American frontier. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter various challenges, they must rely on their wits, courage, and the bonds they form along the way. Secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances add layers of intrigue to their quest, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Redemption, Betrayal
