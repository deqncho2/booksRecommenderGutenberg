Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Fourteenth, or the King's, Regiment of Light Dragoons," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment. From its early beginnings to its involvement in significant historical events, Cannon's account offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of these brave soldiers. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the author paints a vivid picture of the regiment's triumphs and challenges, leaving readers eager to uncover the untold stories of these valiant men.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Military strategy, Historical events
