Synopsis: In "Beyond the Lines; Or, A Yankee Prisoner Loose in Dixie," John James Geer takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of the American Civil War. The story follows the daring escape of a Union soldier from a Confederate prison camp, as he navigates the treacherous landscapes of the South, encountering danger, deception, and unexpected allies along the way. As he fights for his freedom, he also grapples with the moral complexities of war and the true meaning of loyalty.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Morality, Freedom
