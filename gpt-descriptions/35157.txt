Synopsis: In "Handel: The Story of a Little Boy who Practiced in an Attic" by Tapper, Thomas, readers are transported to the world of a young boy named Handel who discovers his passion for music. Set in the 18th century, this heartwarming tale follows Handel's journey as he faces numerous challenges and obstacles in pursuit of his dreams. From his humble beginnings in an attic to his encounters with influential figures, Handel's determination and talent shine through, captivating readers with every turn of the page. This inspiring story explores the power of perseverance, the transformative nature of music, and the indomitable spirit of a young prodigy.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: Pursuit of Passion, Overcoming Adversity, Music and Inspiration
