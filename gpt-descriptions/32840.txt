Synopsis: In 'One Of Them' by Charles James Lever, a young man named Arthur finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he becomes acquainted with a group of enigmatic individuals. As he delves deeper into their world, Arthur discovers that nothing is as it seems, and he must navigate a dangerous game of secrets and deception to uncover the truth. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, 'One Of Them' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning who can be trusted and what lies beneath the surface.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Trust
