Synopsis: In 'Memoirs of an American Prima Donna', Clara Louise Kellogg takes us on a captivating journey through her extraordinary life as a renowned opera singer. From her humble beginnings in Connecticut to her dazzling performances on the grand stages of Europe, Kellogg shares the triumphs, challenges, and secrets of her illustrious career. With vivid descriptions of the opera world, her encounters with famous composers and fellow singers, and the sacrifices she made for her art, this memoir offers a fascinating glimpse into the captivating world of a prima donna.

Genres: Memoir, Biography, Music

Themes: Pursuit of passion, Artistic dedication, Personal sacrifice, Success and fame
