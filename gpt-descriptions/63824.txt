Synopsis: In 'The Man the Sun-Gods Made' by Gardner F. Fox, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Wilson embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that takes him on an extraordinary journey through time and space. As he travels to different dimensions and encounters ancient civilizations, Wilson must confront his own identity and grapple with the consequences of his actions. With each new encounter, the line between science and mythology blurs, leading to a thrilling and thought-provoking exploration of the nature of humanity and the power of the unknown.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Time travel, Identity, Mythology, Human nature
