Synopsis: In "Peter's Mother" by Mrs. Henry De La Pasture, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she becomes the governess to a wealthy family. As she delves deeper into the family's history, she uncovers shocking truths that threaten to unravel their lives. With her own past haunting her, Mary must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and betrayal in order to protect those she cares about. 

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Deception, Love and loyalty
