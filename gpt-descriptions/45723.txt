Synopsis: 
In 'Myths & Legends of Japan', F. Hadland Davis takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Japanese folklore and mythology. From the creation of the world to the tales of gods, heroes, and supernatural creatures, this collection offers a mesmerizing glimpse into the ancient and mystical realm of Japan. Discover the epic battles, tragic love stories, and extraordinary adventures that have shaped the cultural heritage of this fascinating country. With vivid storytelling and enchanting illustrations, Davis brings these timeless tales to life, immersing readers in a world where gods walk among mortals and the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur.

Genres: 
Folklore, Mythology, Cultural Studies

Themes: 
Creation, Heroism, Love and Sacrifice, Supernatural Beings, Cultural Identity
