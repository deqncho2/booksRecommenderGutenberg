Synopsis: In 'The Appetite of Tyranny: Including Letters to an Old Garibaldian', G.K. Chesterton delves into the complex nature of power and its insidious effects on society. Through a series of thought-provoking essays and letters, Chesterton explores the dangers of unchecked authority and the subtle ways in which tyranny can infiltrate our lives. With his characteristic wit and insight, he challenges conventional wisdom and offers a fresh perspective on the age-old struggle between freedom and oppression.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political commentary, Essay collection

Themes: Power, Tyranny, Freedom, Authority, Society
