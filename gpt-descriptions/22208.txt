Synopsis: In 'Cruel Barbara Allen: From Coals Of Fire And Other Stories, Volume II.', Murray, David Christie weaves a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and redemption. Set in a small village, the story follows the enigmatic Barbara Allen, whose heart is as cold as ice. When a young man named William falls deeply in love with her, he is determined to melt her icy demeanor and win her heart. However, as their relationship unfolds, secrets from the past come to light, threatening to tear them apart. With its twists and turns, this gripping narrative explores the depths of human emotions and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and redemption, Betrayal and forgiveness, Transformation and self-discovery
