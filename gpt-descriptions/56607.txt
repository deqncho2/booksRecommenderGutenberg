Synopsis: In 'Dauber: A Poem' by John Masefield, a young artist named Dauber embarks on a journey that takes him from the bustling city to the tranquil countryside. Along the way, he encounters a cast of colorful characters and experiences the transformative power of art. As Dauber navigates the challenges and triumphs of his artistic pursuit, he discovers the true essence of creativity and the profound impact it can have on both the artist and the world.

Genres: Poetry, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Artistic expression, Self-discovery, Transformation
