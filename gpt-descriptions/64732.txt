Synopsis: In 'Bible-Burning', join John C. Miller as he delivers a powerful sermon that challenges the very foundations of faith and ignites a controversial debate. Set in St. Martin's Church in Birmingham, this thought-provoking sermon explores the burning question of whether the Bible should be revered or questioned. As Miller delves into the complexities of religious interpretation, he unveils shocking truths that will leave the congregation questioning their own beliefs. Brace yourself for a sermon that will challenge your faith and ignite a firestorm of controversy.

Genres: Sermon, Religious Literature

Themes: Faith, Interpretation of Scripture, Controversy
