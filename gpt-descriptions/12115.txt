Synopsis: 

In 'Discoveries in Australia, Volume 1', John Lort Stokes takes readers on a captivating journey through the uncharted coasts and rivers of Australia during the mid-19th century. As a member of the renowned HMS Beagle expedition, Stokes recounts the thrilling adventures and discoveries made during the seven-year voyage commanded by the Lords Commissioners of the Admiralty. Alongside this account, the book also includes a fascinating narrative of Captain Owen Stanley's visits to the islands in the Arafura Sea. 

Genres: Exploration, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration and Discovery, Natural History, Indigenous Cultures
