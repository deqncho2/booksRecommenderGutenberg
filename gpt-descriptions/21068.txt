Synopsis: 
'Under the Meteor Flag: Log of a Midshipman during the French Revolutionary War' follows the thrilling adventures of a young midshipman as he navigates the treacherous waters of the French Revolutionary War. From daring naval battles to encounters with pirates and spies, the protagonist's journey is filled with danger, excitement, and unexpected twists. As he rises through the ranks, he must confront his own fears and test his loyalty to his country and comrades. Set against the backdrop of a turbulent era, this gripping tale offers a vivid portrayal of life at sea during a time of great upheaval.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Coming of Age, Patriotism
