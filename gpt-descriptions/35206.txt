Synopsis: In 'Brother Against Brother; Or, The War on the Border' by Optic, Oliver, a gripping tale unfolds as two brothers find themselves on opposite sides of a conflict that threatens to tear their family apart. Set against the backdrop of the tumultuous American Civil War, this captivating story delves into the complexities of loyalty, sacrifice, and the enduring bonds of brotherhood. As the war rages on, the brothers must navigate treacherous terrain, face moral dilemmas, and confront the harsh realities of battle. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this historical novel takes readers on an emotional journey through a divided nation, where the line between right and wrong becomes blurred, and the ultimate price of war is revealed.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Brotherhood, Morality, Sacrifice, Conflict
