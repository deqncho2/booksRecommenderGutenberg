Synopsis: In 'Parables of the Cross', I. Lilias Trotter takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking parables that explore the profound mysteries of the cross. Through vivid storytelling and rich imagery, Trotter invites readers to delve into the depths of spiritual truths and discover the transformative power of the cross in their own lives. Each parable offers a unique perspective on the cross, challenging readers to reflect on their own understanding of faith, sacrifice, and redemption. With its enchanting narratives and profound insights, 'Parables of the Cross' is a captivating exploration of the timeless significance of the cross.

Genres: Christian literature, allegory, spiritual fiction

Themes: Faith, sacrifice, redemption, spiritual growth
