Synopsis: In the picturesque coastal town of Portsmouth, a young artist named Tom finds himself captivated by the enchanting beauty and rich history of the old town. As he immerses himself in the vibrant community, he encounters a cast of colorful characters, each with their own secrets and desires. From the mysterious woman who haunts the abandoned lighthouse to the eccentric old sea captain who spins tales of adventure, Tom's journey through this old town by the sea becomes a captivating exploration of love, loss, and the enduring power of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and longing, History and heritage, Community and connection
