Synopsis: In 'When Day is Done' by Castle, Arnold, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a series of mysterious deaths occur in a small, seemingly idyllic town. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that threaten to unravel the fabric of the community. With time running out and the body count rising, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and confront his own demons to solve the puzzle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Redemption
