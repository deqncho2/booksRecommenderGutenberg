Synopsis: In the heart of the Canadian wilderness, a young man finds himself entangled in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change the course of his life forever. As he navigates through the treacherous flaming forest, he must confront his deepest fears and make difficult choices that will test his courage and resilience. With danger lurking at every turn, he must rely on his wits and the help of unexpected allies to survive the perils of the untamed wilderness.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Love and Sacrifice, Man vs. Nature
