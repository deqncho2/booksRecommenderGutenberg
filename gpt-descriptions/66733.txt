Synopsis: In 'The Obedient Servant' by Tenneshaw, S. M., a young woman named Amelia finds herself thrust into a world of intrigue and danger when she becomes the personal servant to a mysterious and powerful nobleman. As Amelia navigates the treacherous waters of high society, she uncovers dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten not only her own life but also the fate of the entire kingdom. With her wit, intelligence, and unwavering determination, Amelia must unravel the web of deceit and betrayal before it's too late.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Power and manipulation, Identity and self-discovery, Love and sacrifice
