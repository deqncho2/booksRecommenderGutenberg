Synopsis: In 'Beyond the Black Waters' by A. L. O. E., a young orphan named Grace finds herself embarking on a perilous journey across treacherous waters to an unknown land. Alongside a diverse group of companions, she must navigate through mysterious challenges and face unexpected dangers. As they venture deeper into the unknown, Grace discovers hidden truths about herself and the world she thought she knew, ultimately leading her to question the very essence of her existence.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Self-discovery
