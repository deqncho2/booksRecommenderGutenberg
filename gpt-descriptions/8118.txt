Synopsis: 

'Redburn. His First Voyage: Being the Sailor Boy Confessions and Reminiscences of the Son-Of-A-Gentleman in the Merchant Navy' follows the journey of a young sailor named Wellingborough Redburn as he embarks on his first voyage at sea. As the son of a gentleman, Redburn's transition from a sheltered life to the harsh realities of life on a merchant ship is filled with challenges, adventures, and unexpected encounters. Through his vivid and introspective narrative, Redburn provides a unique perspective on the complexities of life at sea, the diverse characters he encounters, and the moral dilemmas he faces along the way.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Maritime fiction

Themes: Identity, Social class, Morality, Self-discovery
