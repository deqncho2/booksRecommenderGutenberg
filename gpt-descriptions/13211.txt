Synopsis: In 'The Pearl: A Middle English Poem, A Modern Version in the Metre of the Original' by Sophie Jewett, readers are transported to a world of enchantment and mystery. This captivating tale follows the journey of a young protagonist who embarks on a quest to find a precious pearl. As the story unfolds, the reader is drawn into a realm filled with magical creatures, hidden treasures, and unexpected twists. With each step closer to the pearl, the protagonist must confront their own fears and desires, ultimately leading to a profound revelation. 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Poetry

Themes: Quest for self-discovery, the power of dreams, the allure of the unknown
