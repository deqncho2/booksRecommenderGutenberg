Synopsis: In 'Brownlows: A Novel' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a mysterious inheritance leads the Brownlow family on a journey of self-discovery and hidden secrets. As they navigate through the complexities of their newfound wealth, they must confront their own desires, ambitions, and the consequences of their actions. With a cast of intriguing characters and a captivating plot, this novel explores the depths of human nature and the power of money.

Genres: Mystery, Family Drama, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Inheritance, Identity, Morality, Wealth and Power
