Synopsis: 
'Drawing for Printers: A practical treatise on the art of designing and illustrating in connection with typography' is a comprehensive guide that explores the art of drawing in relation to typography. Written by Ernest Knaufft, this book provides complete instruction and illustrations for both beginners and advanced students. It covers various techniques and methods for creating visually appealing designs and illustrations that complement typography. Whether you are new to drawing or looking to enhance your skills, this practical treatise offers valuable insights and guidance to help you master the art of drawing in connection with typography.

Genres: Art, Design, Typography

Themes: Drawing techniques, Illustration, Typography, Skill development
