Synopsis: 
In 'Making Money' by Johnson, Owen, we follow the story of a young entrepreneur named Alex who stumbles upon a mysterious opportunity to make a fortune. As he delves deeper into the world of high-stakes investments and cutthroat business deals, he finds himself caught in a web of deceit, power struggles, and unexpected alliances. With his wit and determination, Alex must navigate through a treacherous landscape where money is both a means to success and a dangerous weapon. Will he be able to outsmart his rivals and secure his place in the ruthless world of finance?

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Ambition, Greed, Betrayal
