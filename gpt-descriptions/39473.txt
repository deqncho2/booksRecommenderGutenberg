Synopsis: In 'The Young Sharpshooter at Antietam' by Tomlinson, Everett T., readers are transported to the battlefields of the American Civil War. Follow the gripping journey of a young sharpshooter as he navigates the chaos and brutality of the historic Battle of Antietam. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this book explores the harrowing experiences of a soldier caught in the midst of one of the bloodiest battles in American history. As the young sharpshooter faces the horrors of war, he must grapple with questions of duty, honor, and the true cost of freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Identity, Loss, Morality
