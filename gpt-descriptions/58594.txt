Synopsis: In the small town of Clearwater, a mysterious moonshine operation has taken hold, captivating the locals with its potent and illicit concoctions. When a young and ambitious journalist, Sarah, arrives to investigate, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets, corruption, and forbidden desires. As she delves deeper into the world of white lightning, Sarah must navigate treacherous alliances and confront her own demons in order to uncover the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Corruption, Secrets, Forbidden Desires
