Synopsis: In 'The Candidate: A Political Romance' by Joseph A. Altsheler, a young and ambitious lawyer named John Strickland finds himself thrust into the world of politics when he is chosen as the candidate for a major political party. As he navigates the treacherous waters of campaign trails, power struggles, and personal relationships, John must confront his own ideals and make difficult choices that will shape not only his own future but also the destiny of the nation. With a backdrop of political intrigue and a touch of romance, this gripping tale explores the sacrifices and compromises one must make in the pursuit of power and the consequences that follow.

Genres: Political fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Power, Idealism, Sacrifice
