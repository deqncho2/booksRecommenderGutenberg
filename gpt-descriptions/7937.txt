Synopsis: 

'Journal of an African Cruiser: Comprising Sketches of the Canaries, the Cape De Verds, Liberia, Madeira, Sierra Leone, and Other Places of Interest on the West Coast of Africa' by Bridge, Horatio takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and diverse landscapes of West Africa. Through vivid sketches and personal anecdotes, the author provides a unique perspective on the Canaries, the Cape De Verds, Liberia, Madeira, Sierra Leone, and other intriguing locations along the coast. From the bustling markets to the breathtaking natural wonders, this journal offers a rich and immersive experience of the region.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Diversity, Colonialism
