Synopsis: In 'Prince and Rover of Cloverfield Farm' by Helen Fuller Orton, a young boy named Prince discovers a mysterious secret hidden within the walls of his family's farm. With the help of his loyal dog Rover, Prince embarks on an extraordinary adventure that will test their courage and friendship. As they unravel the farm's secrets, they encounter unexpected challenges and meet fascinating characters along the way. This heartwarming tale explores the power of determination, the importance of family, and the magic that can be found in the most unexpected places.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Family, Discovery
