Synopsis:
In the picturesque countryside of Bannisdale, a passionate and unconventional love story unfolds. Laura Fountain, a strong-willed and independent woman, finds herself irresistibly drawn to the enigmatic Catholic recluse, Alan Helbeck. As their paths intertwine, they navigate the complexities of faith, duty, and desire, challenging societal norms and facing the consequences of their choices. Set against the backdrop of a changing Victorian England, 'Helbeck of Bannisdale' is a captivating tale of forbidden love and the power of personal conviction.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes:
Forbidden love, Religion and spirituality, Individualism, Social expectations
