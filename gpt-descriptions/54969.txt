Synopsis: In 'Sound' by John Tyndall, a brilliant scientist embarks on a groundbreaking journey to unravel the mysteries of sound. As he delves deeper into his research, he discovers astonishing connections between sound waves and the natural world, leading him to question the very fabric of reality. With each revelation, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the power of sound holds the key to both salvation and destruction. This captivating tale takes readers on a thrilling exploration of the hidden forces that shape our perception of the world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Scientific discovery, Perception, Power of sound
