Synopsis: In the Palace of the King: A Love Story of Old Madrid takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant streets of 16th century Madrid. Amidst the opulence and intrigue of the Spanish court, a forbidden love blossoms between a young Englishman and a beautiful Spanish noblewoman. As their love faces countless obstacles and dangers, the reader is swept away in a tale of passion, sacrifice, and the power of true love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Forbidden Love, Cultural Differences, Power and Intrigue
