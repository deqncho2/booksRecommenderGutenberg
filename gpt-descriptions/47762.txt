Synopsis: 

In 'Records of Steam Boiler Explosions' by Marten, Edward Bindon, readers are taken on a gripping journey through a collection of true stories that will leave them on the edge of their seats. From the harrowing accounts of catastrophic boiler explosions to the investigations that followed, this book delves into the dangerous world of steam power and the devastating consequences that can occur when things go wrong. With each page, readers will be captivated by the suspenseful narratives and the meticulous details surrounding these explosive incidents. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Engineering

Themes: Industrial accidents, Technological advancements, Safety precautions
