Synopsis: In "The True Story of Our National Calamity of Flood, Fire and Tornado" by Marshall, Logan, embark on a gripping journey through the devastating disasters that have struck our nation. From the heart-wrenching accounts of floods sweeping away entire towns to the harrowing tales of fires engulfing cities, and the terrifying force of tornadoes tearing through communities, this book unveils the raw power of nature and the resilience of the human spirit in the face of unimaginable tragedy. Through vivid storytelling and firsthand testimonies, the author paints a vivid picture of these catastrophic events, leaving readers captivated and in awe of the indomitable human will to survive.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Natural Disasters

Themes: Resilience, Human Spirit, Natural Forces, Survival
