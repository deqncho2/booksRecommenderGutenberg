Synopsis: In "Adrift on an Ice-Pan," a gripping true story by Sir Wilfred Thomason Grenfell, a young boy finds himself stranded on a small ice floe in the middle of the freezing Labrador Sea. With no hope of rescue in sight, he must rely on his wits and resilience to survive the harsh elements and the ever-present threat of the icy waters below. As the hours turn into days, his struggle for survival becomes a testament to the indomitable human spirit and the power of hope.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Memoir

Themes: Resilience, Survival, Human Spirit
