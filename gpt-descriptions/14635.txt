Synopsis: 
In 'Ruth Fielding in Moving Pictures; Or, Helping the Dormitory Fund', Ruth Fielding finds herself immersed in the captivating world of moving pictures. When a mysterious opportunity arises for Ruth to work in the film industry, she eagerly embraces the chance to explore this new and exciting realm. As she delves deeper into the world of cinema, Ruth uncovers secrets, faces unexpected challenges, and forms unlikely friendships. With her determination and resourcefulness, Ruth strives to make a difference and help those in need, all while navigating the thrilling and unpredictable world of moving pictures.

Genres: 
Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Ambition, Friendship, Empowerment
