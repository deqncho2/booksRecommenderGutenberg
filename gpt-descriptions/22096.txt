Synopsis: 

In 'Stories the Iroquois Tell Their Children', Mabel Powers takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich oral tradition of the Iroquois people. Through a collection of enchanting tales passed down from generation to generation, readers will be transported to a world filled with mythical creatures, brave heroes, and timeless wisdom. From the creation of the world to the adventures of legendary figures, these stories offer a glimpse into the cultural heritage and values of the Iroquois people. With each story, Powers weaves a tapestry of imagination and folklore that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Children's Literature

Themes: Cultural heritage, Wisdom, Heroism, Creation, Tradition
