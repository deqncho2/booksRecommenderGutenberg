Synopsis: In 'For Every Man A Reason' by Patrick Wilkins, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he is hired to investigate a mysterious disappearance. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a shocking conspiracy that threatens to unravel everything he thought he knew. With his own life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path of secrets and betrayal to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Conspiracy
