Synopsis: In 'Good Times with the Juniors' by Heath, Lilian M., a group of misfit teenagers find themselves thrown together in an unexpected adventure that will test their friendship and courage. As they navigate through a series of thrilling and sometimes dangerous escapades, they discover hidden talents, face their fears, and learn the true meaning of loyalty. With each challenge they overcome, the bond between them grows stronger, and they realize that together, they can accomplish anything. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
