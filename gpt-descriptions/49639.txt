Synopsis: In 'The Mentor: Game Animals of America, Vol. 4, Num. 13, Serial No. 113, August 15, 1916', William T. Hornaday takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse and awe-inspiring world of game animals in America. From the majestic elk roaming the Rocky Mountains to the elusive mountain lion prowling the wilderness, this volume delves into the fascinating lives and habitats of these remarkable creatures. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Hornaday brings the reader face-to-face with the beauty and power of these game animals, while also shedding light on the challenges they face in an ever-changing environment. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Wildlife

Themes: Conservation, Wildlife Preservation, Biodiversity
