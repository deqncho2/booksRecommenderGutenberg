Synopsis: 
In "Louis Agassiz as a Teacher; illustrative extracts on his method of instruction," Cooper, Lane takes us on a captivating journey into the world of renowned scientist and educator, Louis Agassiz. Through a series of illustrative extracts, the author unveils Agassiz's unique and unconventional teaching methods, which captivated and transformed the minds of his students. From dissecting fish to studying glaciers, Agassiz's hands-on approach and emphasis on observation and critical thinking challenged his students to see the world in a whole new light. As we delve into the anecdotes and experiences shared by Agassiz's former pupils, we are left inspired and intrigued by the profound impact this extraordinary teacher had on their lives.

Genres: Biography, Education, Science

Themes: Teaching methods, Observation, Critical thinking
