Synopsis: 

In 'Agricultural Implements and Machines in the Collection of the National Museum of History and Technology', author John T. Schlebecker takes readers on a fascinating journey through the evolution of agricultural technology. From the humble beginnings of hand tools to the complex machinery of the modern era, this book showcases the vast collection of agricultural implements and machines housed in the National Museum of History and Technology. Through detailed descriptions and captivating photographs, Schlebecker explores the ingenuity and innovation that have shaped the way we cultivate the land.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Agricultural innovation, Technological advancements, Historical preservation
