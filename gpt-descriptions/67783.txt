Synopsis: In the heart of Siena, Italy, a young horse named Gaudenzia possesses an extraordinary talent that could change her life forever. As she prepares to compete in the legendary Palio horse race, Gaudenzia must overcome numerous obstacles and prove herself against fierce competition. With determination, courage, and a deep bond with her rider, Gaudenzia embarks on a thrilling journey that will captivate readers and leave them on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Identity
