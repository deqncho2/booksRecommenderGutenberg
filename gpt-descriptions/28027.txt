Synopsis: In 'Dwellers in Arcady: The Story of an Abandoned Farm', Albert Bigelow Paine takes us on a captivating journey to a forgotten farm nestled in the picturesque countryside. As the author explores the history and secrets of this abandoned place, he uncovers a tale of resilience, love, and the enduring connection between humans and nature. Through vivid descriptions and heartfelt anecdotes, Paine invites readers to discover the hidden treasures and untold stories that lie within the forgotten corners of our world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Nature Writing

Themes: Resilience, Connection with Nature, History
