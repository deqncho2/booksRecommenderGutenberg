Synopsis:
In 'Cerise: A Tale of the Last Century', the reader is transported to the vibrant and tumultuous world of 18th-century France. The story follows the captivating journey of Cerise, a young woman with a mysterious past and an indomitable spirit. As she navigates the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and political intrigue, Cerise finds herself entangled in a web of secrets that could change the course of history. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes:
Identity, Love and Betrayal, Political Intrigue
