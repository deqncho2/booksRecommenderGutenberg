Synopsis: In 'The Convict: A Tale' by James, G. P. R., a mysterious convict escapes from prison and embarks on a thrilling journey filled with danger, deception, and unexpected alliances. As he navigates through a treacherous world, the convict's true identity and the secrets he holds become the center of a gripping narrative that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Betrayal
