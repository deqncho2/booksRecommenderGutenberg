Synopsis: 

In "The Geologic Story of Mount Rainier: A look at the geologic past of one of America's most scenic volcanoes," Dwight R. Crandell takes readers on an awe-inspiring journey through the captivating history of Mount Rainier. From its fiery origins to its majestic present, this book unveils the secrets hidden within the layers of this iconic volcano. With vivid descriptions and stunning photographs, Crandell paints a vivid picture of the geological forces that have shaped this natural wonder, leaving readers in awe of the immense power and beauty of Mount Rainier.

Genres: Non-fiction, Geology, Natural History

Themes: Volcanic activity, Geological processes, Natural beauty
