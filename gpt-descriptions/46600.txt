Synopsis: In 'By the World Forgot: A Double Romance of the East and West', Cyrus Townsend Brady takes readers on a captivating journey that intertwines two passionate love stories set against the backdrop of the exotic East and the bustling West. As the characters navigate cultural differences, societal expectations, and personal sacrifices, their lives become entangled in a web of secrets, betrayals, and unexpected twists. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel explores the power of love, the clash of cultures, and the enduring strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Cultural Differences, Secrets and Betrayal
