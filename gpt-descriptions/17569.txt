Synopsis: 
Step back in time and explore the fascinating world of the New York Subway, as revealed by the Interborough Rapid Transit Company. Delve into the intricate details of its construction and marvel at the innovative equipment that made this engineering marvel possible. From the underground tunnels to the electrifying power systems, this book takes readers on a captivating journey through the birth and evolution of one of the world's most iconic transportation systems. Discover the challenges faced, the triumphs achieved, and the untold stories of the people who made it all happen.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Engineering

Themes: Urban development, Transportation, Technological innovation
