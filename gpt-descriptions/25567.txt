Synopsis: In 'Impact' by Cox, Irving E., a renowned scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the course of humanity. However, as the implications of his findings become clear, he finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger. With his life and the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect his discovery and those he holds dear.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific breakthrough, Conspiracy, Ethics, Sacrifice
