Synopsis: In 'The Case Against Spirit Photographs', Vincent and Whately delve into the mysterious world of spirit photography, where the line between reality and the supernatural becomes blurred. As they investigate a series of haunting images, they uncover a web of deception, manipulation, and hidden agendas. With each revelation, the authors question the authenticity of these photographs and challenge the very existence of spirits. As they navigate through a labyrinth of secrets, they must confront their own beliefs and confront the unsettling truth that lies beneath the surface.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Historical

Themes: Deception, Belief, Reality vs. Illusion
