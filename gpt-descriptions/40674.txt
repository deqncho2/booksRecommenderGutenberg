Synopsis: In 'Sawn Off: A Tale of a Family Tree' by George Manville Fenn, a mysterious family tree holds the key to a hidden treasure, setting off a thrilling adventure filled with secrets, danger, and unexpected twists. As the protagonist delves deeper into their family's past, they uncover a web of deceit and betrayal that spans generations. With each revelation, the stakes grow higher, and the race to uncover the truth becomes a matter of life and death. Will they be able to unravel the secrets of the family tree before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Treasure hunting
