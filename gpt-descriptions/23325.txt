Synopsis: In "Seth" by Frances Hodgson Burnett, a young boy named Seth finds himself caught between two worlds when he discovers a hidden secret that changes his life forever. As he navigates through the challenges of his newfound reality, Seth must confront his own fears and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With unexpected twists and a touch of magic, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth alongside Seth.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Courage, Self-discovery
