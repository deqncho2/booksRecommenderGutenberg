Synopsis: 
'Off duty: A dozen yarns for soldiers and sailors' is a collection of thrilling and captivating short stories that take readers on a journey through the lives of soldiers and sailors during their moments of respite. From tales of adventure and heroism to unexpected encounters and mysterious occurrences, each story offers a unique glimpse into the lives of these brave men and women. With twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, this collection promises to entertain and engage with its vivid storytelling and unforgettable characters.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Camaraderie, Unexpected Encounters
