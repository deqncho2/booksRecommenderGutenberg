Synopsis: In 'The Long Voyage' by Carl Jacobi, a group of explorers embarks on a perilous journey to a remote and mysterious island. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter strange phenomena, they soon realize that their voyage is not just a physical one, but a journey into the depths of their own fears and desires. With each passing day, the line between reality and illusion becomes blurred, leading to a thrilling and thought-provoking climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of perception and the limits of human exploration.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Perception, Identity, Fear
