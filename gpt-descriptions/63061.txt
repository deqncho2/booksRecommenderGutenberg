Synopsis: In 'Sir Francis Drake: His Voyage, 1595: Together with the Spanish account of Drake's attack on Puerto Rico', Maynarde takes us on a thrilling journey alongside the legendary explorer Sir Francis Drake. Set in the late 16th century, this gripping historical account unveils the daring exploits of Drake and his crew as they embark on a perilous voyage to the Caribbean. With vivid descriptions and meticulous attention to detail, Maynarde brings to life the dramatic events surrounding Drake's audacious attack on Puerto Rico, revealing the high stakes and intense conflicts that unfolded during this pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Warfare, Colonialism
