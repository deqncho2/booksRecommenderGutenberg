Synopsis: 
'Evenings at Donaldson Manor; Or, The Christmas Guest' by Maria J. McIntosh is a captivating tale set during the holiday season. The story revolves around the mysterious arrival of a Christmas guest at Donaldson Manor, a grand estate filled with secrets and hidden pasts. As the guest's presence disrupts the lives of the inhabitants, unexpected events unfold, leading to revelations that will forever change their lives. With its intriguing plot and richly developed characters, this novel promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Family dynamics, Redemption
