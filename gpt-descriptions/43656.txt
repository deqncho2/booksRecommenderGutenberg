Synopsis: 

In 'The Works of Samuel Johnson, LL.D. in Nine Volumes, Volume 02: The Rambler, Volume I', Samuel Johnson takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of essays that explore various aspects of human nature and society. With his eloquent prose and insightful observations, Johnson delves into the complexities of life, offering thought-provoking reflections on topics such as love, friendship, morality, and the pursuit of happiness. Through his engaging storytelling and profound analysis, Johnson invites readers to ponder the intricacies of the human condition and to gain a deeper understanding of the world around them.

Genres: Essay, Non-fiction, Literature

Themes: Human nature, Society, Morality, Happiness
