Synopsis: In 'A Knight of the Nets' by Amelia E. Barr, a young woman named Edith embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment in the male-dominated world of technology and innovation. Set in the late 19th century, Edith defies societal expectations and becomes a pioneer in the field of telegraphy, facing numerous challenges and obstacles along the way. As she navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of her dreams, Edith's determination and resilience shine through, making her a true knight of the nets.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Women's Literature

Themes: Gender roles, Ambition, Self-discovery
