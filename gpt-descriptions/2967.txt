Synopsis: In the seventeenth volume of his captivating memoirs, Jacques Casanova de Seingalt takes us on a thrilling journey as he returns to his beloved Italy. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant cities of Venice, Rome, and Naples, Casanova's adventures unfold with a mix of passion, intrigue, and danger. From his encounters with powerful nobles and seductive courtesans to his involvement in political conspiracies, Casanova's return to Italy is a rollercoaster ride of emotions and escapades. As he navigates the treacherous world of high society and indulges in his insatiable appetite for pleasure, Casanova's memoirs offer a fascinating glimpse into the life of one of history's most notorious figures.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Power and Politics, Identity and Self-Discovery
