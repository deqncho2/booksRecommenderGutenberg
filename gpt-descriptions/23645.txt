Synopsis: In 'The Motor Maids at Sunrise Camp' by Katherine Stokes, a group of adventurous young women embark on a thrilling journey to a picturesque campsite. As they navigate through unexpected challenges and encounter mysterious characters, their bond is tested and their courage shines through. With their trusty motorbikes as their companions, the Motor Maids embark on an unforgettable adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Exploration
