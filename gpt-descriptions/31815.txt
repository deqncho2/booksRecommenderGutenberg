Synopsis:
In 'Student Body' by Wallace, F. L., a prestigious university becomes the backdrop for a thrilling mystery when a series of suspicious deaths occur on campus. As the body count rises, a group of students must navigate the treacherous world of academia, uncovering dark secrets and hidden motives. With each twist and turn, the tension mounts, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the sinister events plaguing the student body.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes:
Academic pressure, Secrets and lies, Power dynamics
