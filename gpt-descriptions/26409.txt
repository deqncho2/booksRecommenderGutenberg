Synopsis: In 'The Children of Wilton Chase' by L. T. Meade, a group of adventurous children stumble upon a hidden secret within the walls of an old mansion. As they uncover clues and face unexpected challenges, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling mystery that will test their courage and friendship. With danger lurking around every corner, the children must rely on their wits and bravery to unravel the truth and bring justice to those who have been wronged.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Justice
