Synopsis: 
In "Skinner's Dress Suit" by Henry Irving Dodge, we follow the life of Mr. Skinner, a hardworking office clerk who dreams of a better life. One day, he decides to splurge on a new suit, hoping it will bring him the success and respect he craves. However, as he dons his new attire, Mr. Skinner's life takes an unexpected turn, leading him on a journey of self-discovery and challenging societal norms. This heartwarming and thought-provoking tale explores the power of appearances and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Drama

Themes: Identity, Social expectations, Self-worth
