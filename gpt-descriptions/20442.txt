Synopsis: 
In "A Catalogue of Early Pennsylvania and Other Firearms and Edged Weapons at "Restless Oaks", McElhattan, Pa.", Henry W. Shoemaker takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of firearms and edged weapons in Pennsylvania. From the earliest settlers to the present day, Shoemaker meticulously catalogues and describes a wide array of fascinating weapons, each with its own unique story and significance. As readers delve into the pages of this catalogue, they will uncover the secrets behind these weapons, their craftsmanship, and the individuals who wielded them. With vivid descriptions and historical context, Shoemaker brings these weapons to life, transporting readers to a time when these tools were essential for survival and protection. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Pennsylvania history, Weaponry, Craftsmanship
