Synopsis: In 'The White People' by Frances Hodgson Burnett, a young girl named Betty discovers a hidden world filled with secrets and mysteries. As she delves deeper into this enchanting realm, she encounters strange and ethereal beings known as the White People. With each encounter, Betty's understanding of the world is challenged, and she must navigate a delicate balance between reality and fantasy. As the story unfolds, readers are drawn into a captivating tale of imagination, wonder, and the power of belief.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Imagination, Perception, Reality vs. Fantasy
