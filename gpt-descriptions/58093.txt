Synopsis: In 'Round the Sofa; vol. 2', Elizabeth Gaskell takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of short stories that delve into the lives of various characters. From tales of love and loss to mysteries and unexpected twists, each story offers a unique glimpse into the human experience. As the characters gather around the sofa, their stories unfold, revealing the complexities of human nature and the power of human connection.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Victorian Literature

Themes: Love and Relationships, Human Nature, Mystery and Intrigue
