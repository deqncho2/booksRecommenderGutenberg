Synopsis: 
'Our Artist in Cuba: Fifty drawings on wood. Leaves from the Sketch-book of a traveler, During the Winter of 1864-5' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the vibrant landscapes and rich culture of Cuba. Through the eyes of the artist, we explore the bustling streets of Havana, the serene beauty of the countryside, and the fascinating lives of the Cuban people. With each intricately detailed drawing on wood, the artist captures the essence of this captivating island, inviting readers to immerse themselves in the colors, textures, and stories that unfold within each page.

Genres: Travelogue, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Artistic expression
