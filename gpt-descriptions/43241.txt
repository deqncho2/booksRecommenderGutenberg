Synopsis: In 'The Adventures of François: Foundling, Thief, Juggler, and Fencing-Master during the French Revolution', follow the captivating journey of François, a young orphan who navigates the treacherous streets of Paris during the tumultuous French Revolution. From his humble beginnings as a foundling, François embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected alliances. As he becomes entangled in the world of thievery, juggling, and even fencing, François must rely on his wit, skill, and resilience to survive the chaos of revolution and find his true purpose.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Survival, Revolution, Loyalty
