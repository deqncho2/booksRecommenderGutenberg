Synopsis: 
Step into the enchanting world of ancient Rome as Rachel Harriette Busk takes you on a captivating journey through the fables and folk-lore of this legendary city. From the mythical origins of Rome to the heroic tales of its legendary founders, Romulus and Remus, this collection of Roman legends will transport you to a time of gods, goddesses, and extraordinary feats. Discover the stories of brave warriors, cunning gods, and cunning mortals as they navigate the intricate web of Roman mythology. With each tale, Busk weaves a tapestry of adventure, love, and betrayal that will leave you spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Historical Fiction

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Power, Love, Betrayal
