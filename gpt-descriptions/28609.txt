Synopsis: 
In the picturesque coastal town of St. Merryn, a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him an air of intrigue and a dark secret. As the townspeople become captivated by his enigmatic presence, they soon find themselves entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. Against the backdrop of the stunning Cornish Riviera, this gripping tale explores the depths of human desires and the consequences of hidden pasts.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and relationships, Small-town dynamics
