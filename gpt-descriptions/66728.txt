Synopsis: In 'The Kingmakers' by Stevenson, Burton Egbert, a mysterious group of individuals known as the Kingmakers holds the power to shape the destiny of nations. As they navigate through political intrigue, secret societies, and hidden agendas, the protagonists find themselves entangled in a dangerous game where the stakes are nothing less than the fate of entire countries. With unexpected alliances, thrilling plot twists, and a race against time, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Political thriller, Mystery, Historical fiction

Themes: Power and manipulation, Conspiracy, Loyalty and betrayal
