Synopsis: 
In the small fishing village of Tuckamore Bay, young Billy Topsail dreams of becoming a great sailor like his father. When a mysterious storm hits the coast, Billy finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure that takes him far beyond his wildest dreams. Alongside his loyal dog, Billy must navigate treacherous waters, encounter strange creatures, and face unexpected challenges in order to uncover the truth behind the storm and save his village. With courage and determination, Billy Topsail sets sail on an unforgettable journey that will test his skills, his friendships, and his belief in himself.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Self-discovery
