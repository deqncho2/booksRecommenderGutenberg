Synopsis: In 'The Story of Sugar' by Sara Ware Bassett, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey that takes her from a small New England town to the bustling city of Boston. Drawn into the world of sugar refining, Mary finds herself caught between tradition and progress, as she navigates the challenges of love, ambition, and societal expectations. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the sugar industry, Mary discovers a hidden world filled with intrigue and danger, where the sweetest things can sometimes have the most bitter consequences.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Ambition, Tradition vs. Progress, Love and Relationships, Secrets and Intrigue
