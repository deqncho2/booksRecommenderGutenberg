Synopsis: In 'New House That Jack Built. An Original American Version', follow the captivating journey of Jack as he embarks on a quest to build his dream house. From the bustling streets of New York City to the untamed wilderness of the American frontier, Jack encounters a colorful cast of characters and faces numerous challenges along the way. As he navigates through the trials and tribulations of construction, Jack discovers that building a house is not just about bricks and mortar, but also about finding a place to call home.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Perseverance, Identity, Belonging
