Synopsis:
In the small English town of Polchester, Mark Sabre's life takes an unexpected turn when he falls in love with a mysterious woman named Mabel. As their relationship blossoms, Mark finds himself caught in a web of secrets, lies, and societal expectations. Set against the backdrop of World War I, 'If Winter Comes' explores the complexities of love, the consequences of one's actions, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Love and relationships, societal expectations, personal growth, resilience
