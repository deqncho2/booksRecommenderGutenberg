Synopsis: 

'An Illustrated Account of St. Bartholomew's Priory Church, Smithfield: With a Sketch of Bartholomew Fair, St. Bartholomew's Hospital, and the Prior's Country Seat, Canonbury Tower, Islington' by Normanus takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and fascinating landmarks of St. Bartholomew's Priory Church. From the bustling Bartholomew Fair to the renowned St. Bartholomew's Hospital and the enchanting Canonbury Tower in Islington, this book offers a vivid exploration of the priory's past and its enduring significance. With stunning illustrations and detailed descriptions, Normanus invites readers to uncover the secrets and hidden stories behind these iconic locations, painting a vivid picture of the priory's historical and cultural importance.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travel guide, Art book

Themes: History, Architecture, Cultural heritage
