Synopsis: In 'Harlequin and Columbine' by Booth Tarkington, a chance encounter between two strangers sparks a whirlwind romance that transcends societal expectations. As their relationship blossoms, they navigate the complexities of love, identity, and the pursuit of happiness. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the power of connection and the transformative nature of love.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love, Identity, Society, Happiness
