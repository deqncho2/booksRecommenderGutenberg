Synopsis: In 'Light On the Child's Path' by Bixler, William Allen, a young girl named Lily embarks on a mysterious journey that takes her to a hidden world filled with enchantment and danger. As she navigates through this magical realm, Lily discovers her own inner strength and uncovers a long-lost secret that could change everything. With the help of newfound friends and facing unexpected challenges, Lily must find the courage to fulfill her destiny and bring light to the child's path.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Courage, Destiny
