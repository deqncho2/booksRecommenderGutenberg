Synopsis: John Marcher, a man haunted by a mysterious premonition, forms a unique bond with May Bartram. As their lives intertwine over the years, Marcher becomes consumed by the belief that a profound event awaits him in the future. This gripping tale explores the complexities of human relationships and the haunting power of anticipation.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Gothic fiction, Tragedy

Themes: Fate and destiny, Fear and anticipation, Loneliness and isolation
