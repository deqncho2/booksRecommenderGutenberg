Synopsis: 
In 'Personal Sketches of His Own Times, Vol. 2 (of 3)', Sir Jonah Barrington takes us on a captivating journey through his eventful life as a prominent figure in 18th-century Ireland. From his encounters with notorious criminals to his involvement in high-stakes political intrigue, Barrington's memoirs offer a thrilling glimpse into a world of danger, scandal, and adventure. As he navigates the treacherous waters of Irish society, Barrington's wit and charm shine through, making this memoir a delightful and engrossing read.

Genres: Memoir, Historical, Adventure

Themes: Personal experiences, Political intrigue, Society and culture
