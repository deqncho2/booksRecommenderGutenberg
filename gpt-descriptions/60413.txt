Synopsis: In 'The Stars Incline' by Jeanne Judson, a young astrologer named Amelia discovers a hidden celestial secret that could change the course of her life forever. As she delves deeper into the mysteries of the stars, Amelia finds herself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and ancient prophecies. With her newfound knowledge, she must navigate a dangerous path to uncover the truth and fulfill her destiny. This captivating tale of astrology, romance, and destiny will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Fate and destiny, Secrets and revelations
