Synopsis: 

In 'Civilization and Beyond: Learning from History', Nearing, Scott takes readers on a captivating journey through the rise and fall of civilizations, exploring the patterns and lessons that can be learned from our collective past. From ancient empires to modern societies, this thought-provoking book delves into the complexities of human progress, examining the factors that contribute to the success or downfall of civilizations. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of historical events, Nearing, Scott offers a fresh perspective on the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead for humanity. 

Genres: History, Sociology, Philosophy

Themes: Rise and fall of civilizations, Human progress, Lessons from history
