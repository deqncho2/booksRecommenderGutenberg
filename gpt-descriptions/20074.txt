Synopsis: 

"The Story of the Cambrian: A Biography of a Railway" takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of the Cambrian Railway, from its humble beginnings to its remarkable expansion. Charles Penrhyn Gasquoine delves into the fascinating world of railways, exploring the challenges faced by the Cambrian Railway and the individuals who played pivotal roles in its development. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Gasquoine paints a vivid picture of the railway's impact on the communities it served and the transformative power of this remarkable mode of transportation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Transport

Themes: Industrialization, Community development, Technological advancements
