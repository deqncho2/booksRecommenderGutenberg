Synopsis: In 'The Motor Boat Club off Long Island', a group of adventurous young men embark on a thrilling marine game that takes them on a high-speed racing adventure off the coast of Long Island. As they navigate treacherous waters, face unexpected challenges, and encounter rival boat clubs, the boys must rely on their courage, skill, and teamwork to come out on top. With heart-pounding action and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Competition
