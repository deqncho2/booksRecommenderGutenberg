Synopsis: In "A Straight Deal; Or, The Ancient Grudge" by Owen Wister, a young man named Jack Orde embarks on a journey to the Wild West, seeking adventure and fortune. However, he soon finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and a long-standing feud that threatens to consume him. As Jack navigates the treacherous landscape of the West, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Redemption, Loyalty, Revenge, Identity
