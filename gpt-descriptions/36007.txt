Synopsis: Ethel Morton and the Christmas Ship follows the adventures of Ethel Morton and her friends as they embark on a thrilling journey during the holiday season. When a mysterious ship arrives in their small town, the group of friends becomes determined to uncover its secrets and bring joy to those in need. As they navigate through unexpected challenges and heartwarming surprises, Ethel and her friends learn the true meaning of Christmas and the power of friendship.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Generosity, Holiday spirit
