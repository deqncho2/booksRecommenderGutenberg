Synopsis: In 'A Republic Without a President, and Other Stories', Herbert D. Ward takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking tales. From a mysterious disappearance in a small town to a surprising twist in a political election, each story presents a unique and unexpected narrative. With vivid storytelling and intriguing characters, Ward explores the complexities of human nature, the consequences of our choices, and the power of resilience. Prepare to be captivated by these gripping and imaginative stories that will leave you pondering long after you turn the last page.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Human nature, Consequences of choices, Resilience
