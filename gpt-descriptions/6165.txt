Synopsis: In 'Cowboy Dave; Or, The Round-up at Rolling River' by Frank V. Webster, readers are transported to the rugged and wild world of the American West. The story follows the adventures of Cowboy Dave, a fearless and skilled cowboy, as he embarks on a thrilling round-up at Rolling River Ranch. As Dave faces various challenges, including treacherous terrain, unpredictable weather, and a mysterious cattle rustler, the stakes are raised, and the excitement intensifies. With vivid descriptions of the breathtaking landscapes and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Action

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Loyalty
