Synopsis: 
'Burgundy: The Splendid Duchy. Stories and Sketches in South Burgundy' by Allen, Percy takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting region of South Burgundy. Through a collection of stories and sketches, the book unveils the hidden gems, rich history, and vibrant culture of this splendid duchy. From picturesque vineyards and charming villages to ancient castles and bustling markets, the author's vivid descriptions and engaging narratives transport readers to the heart of Burgundy. With each tale, readers will uncover the secrets and mysteries that lie within the region, immersing themselves in its beauty and allure.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Fiction, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Heritage, Beauty of Nature, Cultural Identity
