Synopsis: In 'Whatsoever a Man Soweth' by William Le Queux, a mysterious murder takes place in a quiet English village, leaving the residents shocked and fearful. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, and the true nature of the seemingly idyllic community is revealed. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the motives and actions of each character.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town secrets
