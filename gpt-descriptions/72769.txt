Synopsis: In "Half a Dozen Boys: An Every-Day Story," author Anna Chapin Ray takes readers on a captivating journey into the lives of six young boys. Set in a small town, the story follows the adventures, friendships, and challenges faced by these spirited boys as they navigate the joys and tribulations of childhood. From mischievous pranks to heartfelt moments of camaraderie, this heartwarming tale explores the bonds of friendship and the transformative power of youthful innocence.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Childhood, Innocence, Adventure
