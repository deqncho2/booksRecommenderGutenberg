Synopsis:
In "Meet Mr Mulliner," P.G. Wodehouse introduces readers to the eccentric and entertaining world of Mr Mulliner, a genial raconteur who frequents the Angler's Rest pub. With each visit, Mr Mulliner regales the patrons with his extraordinary family tales, filled with love, adventure, and hilarity. From a romance involving a stolen cow to a golfing mishap that leads to unexpected consequences, these stories are sure to captivate and amuse readers. As the tales unfold, the reader becomes immersed in the delightful and often absurd escapades of the Mulliner clan, leaving them eagerly awaiting the next installment.

Genres: Humor, Fiction, Short Stories
Themes: Family, Love, Adventure, Comedy
