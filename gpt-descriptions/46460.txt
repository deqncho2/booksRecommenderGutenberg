Synopsis: In 'Gunboat and Gun-runner: A Tale of the Persian Gulf', author T. T. Jeans takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous waters of the Persian Gulf. Set against the backdrop of political unrest and international intrigue, the story follows a daring gunboat captain and a cunning gun-runner as they navigate the dangerous waters of the Gulf, facing perilous encounters, unexpected alliances, and a race against time. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Courage, Betrayal, Survival, Political Intrigue
