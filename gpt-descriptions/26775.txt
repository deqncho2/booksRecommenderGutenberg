Synopsis: 

In "Lewis and Clark: Meriwether Lewis and William Clark," author William R. Lighton takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed American wilderness. This gripping narrative follows the extraordinary expedition led by Meriwether Lewis and William Clark, as they venture into uncharted territories, facing treacherous landscapes, encounters with Native American tribes, and the constant threat of danger. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Lighton brings to life the awe-inspiring courage and determination of these two iconic explorers, shedding light on their triumphs, struggles, and the lasting impact of their historic expedition.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Courage, Native American relations, Wilderness survival
