Synopsis: In "The Minister of Evil: The Secret History of Rasputin's Betrayal of Russia," author William Le Queux delves into the mysterious life of Rasputin, the enigmatic figure who held immense power over the Russian royal family. Through a captivating narrative, Le Queux uncovers the hidden truths behind Rasputin's rise to influence and his ultimate betrayal of the Russian empire. As the secrets unravel, readers are taken on a thrilling journey filled with political intrigue, scandal, and the dark forces that shaped the destiny of a nation.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Biography

Themes: Power and manipulation, Loyalty and betrayal, Historical events and figures
