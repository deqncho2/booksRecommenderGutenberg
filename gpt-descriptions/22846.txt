Synopsis: In 'The Ground-Ash' by Mary Russell Mitford, a young woman named Lucy finds herself drawn to the mysterious and enchanting world of the supernatural. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the ground-ash tree, she uncovers a hidden realm filled with ancient powers and dark forces. With her life hanging in the balance, Lucy must navigate a treacherous path of love, betrayal, and sacrifice to protect those she holds dear. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the ground-ash's power.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, romance

Themes: Mystery, the supernatural, love and sacrifice
