Synopsis: In 'The Old Room' by Carl Ewald, a mysterious room hidden within an ancient manor holds secrets that have been locked away for centuries. When a young woman stumbles upon this forgotten chamber, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. As she delves deeper into the room's history, she uncovers a haunting tale of love, betrayal, and the power of the human spirit. With each revelation, the boundaries between past and present blur, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Gothic

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the passage of time, the resilience of the human spirit
