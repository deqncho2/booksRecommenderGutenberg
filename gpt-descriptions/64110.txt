Synopsis: In the quaint village of Foxden, a mysterious inn called "The Fox" becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of love, secrets, and unexpected alliances. As the innkeeper's daughter, Rosamond, navigates the complexities of her own heart, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that spans generations. With the arrival of a charming stranger, the secrets of the inn and its inhabitants begin to unravel, leading Rosamond on a journey of self-discovery and forbidden love. Set against the backdrop of the English countryside, "At the Sign of the Fox: A Romance" is a captivating tale that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and desire, Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery
