Synopsis: In 'Dough or Dynamite' by W. C. Tuttle, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives with a secret that could change everything. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, they must confront their own desires and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of their community. With unexpected twists and a thrilling climax, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Small-town dynamics, Secrets and deception, Moral dilemmas
