Synopsis: In "Tom the telephone boy; or, The mystery of a message" by Frank V. Webster, readers are introduced to Tom, a young and curious telephone operator who stumbles upon a cryptic message that leads him on an unexpected adventure. As Tom delves deeper into the mystery, he finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, Tom's determination to uncover the truth grows stronger, taking him on a thrilling journey that will test his courage and resourcefulness. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Tom's quest to unravel the mystery behind the enigmatic message.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Curiosity, Determination, Friendship, Secrets
