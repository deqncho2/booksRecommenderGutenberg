Synopsis: In 'Dumps - A Plain Girl' by L. T. Meade, readers are introduced to Dumps, a young girl who is often overlooked due to her plain appearance. However, when a mysterious stranger arrives in her small village, Dumps finds herself caught up in a thrilling adventure that will challenge her perception of herself and the world around her. As she embarks on a journey filled with secrets, danger, and unexpected friendships, Dumps discovers her own inner strength and the power of embracing one's true self.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Friendship
