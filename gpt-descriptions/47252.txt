Synopsis: In 'Mother Earth's Children: The Frolics of the Fruits and Vegetables' by Gordon, Elizabeth, embark on a whimsical journey into the secret lives of fruits and vegetables. As the sun sets and the moon rises, these lively characters come alive, revealing their hidden talents and engaging in delightful adventures. From mischievous strawberries to wise broccoli, this enchanting tale will transport readers to a world where the wonders of nature are celebrated in the most delightful way.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Nature, Imagination, Friendship
