Synopsis: In Volume 03 of 'The Works of Samuel Johnson, LL.D.', readers delve into the second volume of 'The Rambler', a collection of essays that captivate with their profound insights and thought-provoking musings. Samuel Johnson, a master of the English language, takes readers on a journey through various topics, exploring the complexities of human nature, society, morality, and the pursuit of happiness. With his eloquent prose and sharp wit, Johnson invites readers to ponder the intricacies of life and offers timeless wisdom that remains relevant to this day.

Genres: Essay, Non-fiction, Literature

Themes: Human nature, Society, Morality, Happiness
