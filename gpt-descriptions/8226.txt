Synopsis: In 'Fairy Tales, Their Origin and Meaning; With Some Account of Dwellers in Fairyland', John Thackray Bunce delves into the enchanting world of fairy tales, exploring their origins and uncovering the hidden meanings behind these beloved stories. From the magical realms of fairies, elves, and goblins to the captivating narratives of princesses, witches, and talking animals, Bunce takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of fairyland. With fascinating insights and historical context, this book offers a fresh perspective on the timeless tales that have captivated generations.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, Folklore

Themes: Fairy tales, Mythology, Symbolism
