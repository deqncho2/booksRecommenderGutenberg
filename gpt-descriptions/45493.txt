Synopsis: 
"On Yacht Sailing: A Simple Treatise for Beginners upon the Art of Handling Small Yachts and Boats" by Thomas Fleming Day is a comprehensive guide that takes readers on an exciting journey into the world of yacht sailing. From the basics of boat handling to advanced techniques, this book provides a wealth of knowledge and practical advice for beginners looking to embark on their own sailing adventures. With vivid descriptions and helpful illustrations, Day's treatise offers a captivating exploration of the art of yacht sailing, making it an essential companion for anyone eager to set sail and navigate the open waters.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Adventure

Themes: Yacht sailing, Boat handling, Beginner's guide, Adventure, Skill development
