Synopsis: In 'Star-land: Being Talks With Young People About the Wonders of the Heavens', Robert S. Ball takes readers on a captivating journey through the vastness of the universe. Through a series of engaging conversations, Ball explores the mysteries of the stars, planets, and galaxies, unraveling the secrets of the cosmos in a way that is both educational and awe-inspiring. From the birth of stars to the possibility of extraterrestrial life, this book delves into the wonders of the heavens, igniting curiosity and wonder in readers of all ages.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Astronomy

Themes: Wonder and curiosity, Exploration of the universe, Education and learning
