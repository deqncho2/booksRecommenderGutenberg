Synopsis: 
'Peterkin' by Molesworth, Mrs. is a captivating coming-of-age novel that follows the adventures of a young boy named Peterkin. Sent away to boarding school, Peterkin must navigate the challenges of fitting in, making friends, and discovering his own identity. As he encounters various characters and experiences both triumphs and setbacks, Peterkin's journey becomes a heartwarming tale of self-discovery and resilience. With its delightful blend of humor, charm, and relatable moments, 'Peterkin' is a story that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres:
Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes:
Identity, Friendship, Resilience
