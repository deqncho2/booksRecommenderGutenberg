Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Abington Abbey, secrets lurk behind every elegant facade. When a mysterious letter arrives, it sets in motion a chain of events that will unravel the dark past of the aristocratic Montague family. As the secrets of the Abbey begin to surface, the lives of its inhabitants are forever changed. From forbidden love to scandalous betrayals, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth that lies within the walls of Abington Abbey.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Betrayal, Social class
