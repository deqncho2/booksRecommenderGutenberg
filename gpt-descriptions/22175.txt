Synopsis: 
In 'Stories from the Ballads, Told to the Children', Mary Macgregor weaves a captivating collection of tales inspired by traditional ballads. From brave knights and fair maidens to treacherous villains and magical creatures, these enchanting stories transport readers to a world of adventure and romance. Each narrative is filled with suspense, mystery, and unexpected twists, making it impossible to put the book down. Whether it's the haunting tale of a ghostly lover or the thrilling account of a daring rescue, these timeless ballads come alive in Macgregor's skilled storytelling, captivating readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: 
Love and Romance, Heroism, Fate and Destiny
