Synopsis: In 'Dick Merriwell's Pranks; Or, Lively Times in the Orient' by Standish, Burt L., follow the thrilling adventures of Dick Merriwell and his friends as they embark on a journey to the exotic Orient. From mischievous pranks to unexpected encounters, this action-packed tale takes readers on a whirlwind ride through bustling cities, ancient temples, and treacherous landscapes. As Dick and his companions navigate the challenges and mysteries of the Orient, they must rely on their wit, courage, and resourcefulness to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Cultural Differences
