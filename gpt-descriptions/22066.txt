Synopsis: In the midst of the American Civil War, a young Confederate soldier finds himself torn between loyalty to his cause and the bonds of friendship he forms with his Union adversaries. As he navigates the chaos and brutality of war, he must confront his own beliefs and question the true meaning of honor and sacrifice. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, 'The Long Roll' takes readers on an emotional journey through the heart of a divided nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Morality, Identity, Honor
