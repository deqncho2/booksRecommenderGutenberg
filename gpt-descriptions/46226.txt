Synopsis: 
'Niobe, All Smiles: A Farcical Comedy in Three Acts' follows the misadventures of Niobe, a charming and vivacious young woman who finds herself entangled in a series of hilarious and absurd situations. From mistaken identities to romantic entanglements, Niobe's journey is filled with laughter, wit, and unexpected twists. As the story unfolds, the audience is taken on a rollercoaster ride of comedic mishaps and misunderstandings, all leading to a climactic finale that will leave them in stitches.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Mistaken identity, Love and romance, Absurdity, Humor
