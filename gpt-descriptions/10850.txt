Synopsis: In the kingdom of Sicily, a love triangle unfolds between the noble Prince Philaster, the beautiful Princess Arethusa, and the cunning courtier Bellario. As their passions intertwine, secrets are revealed, loyalties are tested, and the pursuit of love becomes a dangerous game of deception and betrayal. Set against a backdrop of political intrigue and power struggles, 'Philaster; Or, Love Lies a Bleeding' is a captivating tale of love, honor, and the lengths one will go to protect their heart.

Genres: Tragedy, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Loyalty and betrayal, Power and politics
