Synopsis:
In "Flip's Islands of Providence" by Annie F. Johnston, readers are transported to the enchanting world of the Providence Islands, where adventure and mystery await. Flip, a spirited young girl, finds herself embarking on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As she explores the islands and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Flip uncovers secrets that will change her life forever. With each page, the reader is drawn deeper into the captivating world of Providence, eager to discover what lies around the next corner.

Genres:
Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Self-discovery, Friendship, Courage
