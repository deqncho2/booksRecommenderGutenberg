Synopsis: 

In 'All about Ferrets and Rats: A Complete History of Ferrets, Rats, and Rat Extermination from Personal Experiences and Study. Also a Practical Hand-Book on the Ferret,' author Adolph Isaacsen takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of ferrets and rats. Drawing from personal experiences and extensive research, Isaacsen delves into the history, behavior, and extermination methods of these intriguing creatures. From the ancient Egyptians' reverence for ferrets to the role of rats in spreading diseases, this comprehensive book offers a wealth of knowledge and practical advice for anyone interested in these animals. With vivid anecdotes and practical tips, Isaacsen's work is a must-read for both enthusiasts and those seeking to understand the complex relationship between humans and these often misunderstood creatures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Animal Care

Themes: Animal behavior, Human-animal interaction, Pest control
