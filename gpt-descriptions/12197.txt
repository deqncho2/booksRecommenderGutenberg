Synopsis: In 'Mr. Scraggs' by Phillips, Henry Wallace, follow the misadventures of a bumbling yet endearing protagonist as he stumbles into a series of comical and unexpected situations. From failed business ventures to hilarious encounters with eccentric characters, Mr. Scraggs' journey is filled with laughter, surprises, and valuable life lessons. Will he ever find success or will his charming ineptitude continue to lead him astray?

Genres: Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Ambition, Self-discovery
