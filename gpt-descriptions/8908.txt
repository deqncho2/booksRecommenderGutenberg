Synopsis: In 'The Ignatian Epistles Entirely Spurious: A Reply to the Right Rev. Dr. Lightfoot', author W. D. Killen delves into a controversial debate surrounding the authenticity of the Ignatian Epistles. With meticulous research and compelling arguments, Killen challenges the widely accepted views of Dr. Lightfoot, shedding light on the intriguing mystery surrounding these ancient texts. As the battle of scholarly minds unfolds, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the world of biblical scholarship, where truth and deception intertwine.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biblical Studies, Historical Criticism

Themes: Authenticity, Biblical Interpretation, Scholarly Debate
