Synopsis: 
In "The Big Four" by Agatha Christie, the world-renowned detective Hercule Poirot finds himself entangled in a web of international intrigue and danger. As he investigates a series of seemingly unrelated crimes, Poirot discovers a shadowy organization known as the Big Four, whose sinister influence reaches far and wide. With his brilliant deductive skills and the help of his loyal companion Captain Hastings, Poirot must unravel the mystery and outwit his most formidable adversaries yet.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: International conspiracy, Deception, Power struggle
