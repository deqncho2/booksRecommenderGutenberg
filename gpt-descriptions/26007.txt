Synopsis: 
'From Paris to New York by Land' follows the incredible journey of Harry De Windt as he embarks on a daring expedition from Paris to New York, traversing vast landscapes, encountering diverse cultures, and facing numerous challenges along the way. From treacherous mountain passes to unforgiving deserts, De Windt's gripping narrative takes readers on a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns. As he navigates through unfamiliar territories, De Windt's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test, making this an exhilarating tale of exploration and survival.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Resilience, Cultural diversity
