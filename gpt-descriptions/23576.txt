Synopsis: 
In 'Quadrupeds, What They Are and Where Found: A Book of Zoology for Boys', Reid Mayne takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the animal kingdom. From the fierce predators of the African savannah to the mysterious creatures lurking in the depths of the ocean, this captivating book introduces young readers to a wide array of fascinating quadrupeds. With vivid descriptions and captivating illustrations, Mayne brings these creatures to life, revealing their unique characteristics, habitats, and behaviors. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will embark on an adventure filled with awe-inspiring discoveries and gain a deeper understanding of the diverse world of quadrupeds.

Genres: Non-fiction, Zoology, Children's Literature
Themes: Animal diversity, Exploration, Education
