Synopsis:
In "Buffalo Roost: A Story of a Young Men's Christian Association Boys' Department," Frank H. Cheley takes readers on a captivating journey into the lives of a group of young boys who find themselves at the heart of an unexpected adventure. Set in the vibrant world of the Young Men's Christian Association (YMCA), the story follows the boys as they navigate the challenges of growing up, forming lasting friendships, and discovering their own strengths and potential. As they face various trials and tribulations, the boys learn valuable life lessons and experience the transformative power of community and faith. With its blend of excitement, camaraderie, and personal growth, "Buffalo Roost" is a heartwarming tale that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Christian Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Personal growth, Faith, Community
