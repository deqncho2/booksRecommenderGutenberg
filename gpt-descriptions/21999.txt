Synopsis: In 'The Wind Bloweth' by Byrne, Donn, a small coastal town is plagued by a series of mysterious events that leave its residents on edge. As the wind howls through the streets, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of the town's inhabitants is revealed. With each gust of wind, the tension builds, leading to a climactic conclusion that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town dynamics, Unpredictability of human nature
