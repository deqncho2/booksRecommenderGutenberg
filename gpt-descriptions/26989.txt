Synopsis: In 'B. C. 30,000' by S. P. Meek, embark on a thrilling journey to a distant future where a group of scientists discover a hidden civilization that challenges everything they thought they knew about human history. As they delve deeper into this mysterious society, they uncover ancient secrets and face unimaginable dangers that could alter the course of humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Evolution, Ancient Civilizations
