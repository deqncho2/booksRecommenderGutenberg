Synopsis: In 'The Running Fight' by William Hamilton Osborne, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse when she witnesses a crime that puts her life at risk. With the help of a mysterious stranger, she embarks on a thrilling journey filled with suspense, unexpected twists, and a relentless pursuit that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Trust, Redemption
