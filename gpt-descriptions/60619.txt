Synopsis: In "Astronomy Explained Upon Sir Isaac Newton's Principles: And made easy to those who have not studied mathematics," James Ferguson takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders of the universe. With a clear and accessible approach, Ferguson unveils the secrets of astronomy, revealing the beauty and complexity of celestial bodies, from planets and stars to galaxies and beyond. Through vivid descriptions and engaging explanations, he brings to life the principles of Sir Isaac Newton, making this fascinating subject accessible to those without a background in mathematics. Prepare to be enthralled as Ferguson unravels the mysteries of the cosmos, leaving readers with a newfound appreciation for the vastness and intricacy of the universe.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction, Astronomy

Themes: Astronomy, Scientific principles, Accessibility
