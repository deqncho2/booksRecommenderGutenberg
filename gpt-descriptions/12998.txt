Synopsis: In 'Thankful Rest' by Swan, Annie S., a young woman named Emily finds herself in a small, mysterious town after a tragic event. As she navigates through the town's peculiarities, she discovers a hidden world filled with secrets and unexpected connections. With each encounter, Emily's perception of reality is challenged, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and healing. As she unravels the town's enigmatic past, Emily must confront her own demons and learn to find solace in the most unexpected places.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Grief, Redemption, Perception
