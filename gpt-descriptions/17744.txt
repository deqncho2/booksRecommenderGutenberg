Synopsis: In 'The Moving Picture Boys on the War Front: Or, The Hunt for the Stolen Army Films' by Appleton, Victor, the adventurous trio of Joe, Tom, and Frank find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mission during World War I. When a crucial set of army films goes missing, the boys must navigate treacherous battlefields, enemy spies, and unexpected allies to recover the stolen footage. As they race against time, their courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test, leading to a heart-pounding climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Espionage, World War I
