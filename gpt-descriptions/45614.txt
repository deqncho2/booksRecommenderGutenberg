Synopsis: In 'Chronicles of England, Scotland and Ireland (3 of 6): England (5 of 9): The History of Edward the Fift and King Richard the Third Unfinished', Holinshed delves into the captivating and mysterious history of two English monarchs, Edward V and Richard III. This historical account takes readers on a journey through the tumultuous events surrounding their reigns, revealing the complex web of political intrigue, power struggles, and the enigmatic disappearance of the young princes. As the narrative unfolds, Holinshed skillfully weaves together a tapestry of historical events, shedding light on the fascinating and often controversial figures that shaped England's past.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Power and ambition, Loyalty and betrayal, Historical interpretation
