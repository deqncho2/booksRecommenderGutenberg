Synopsis: In the second volume of "Donovan Pasha, and Some People of Egypt," the captivating story continues as Donovan Pasha, a British officer stationed in Egypt, finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and personal vendettas. As he navigates the complex world of Egyptian society, Donovan must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of those around him. With vivid descriptions of the exotic landscapes and rich cultural tapestry of Egypt, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Colonialism, Identity, Love and Betrayal
