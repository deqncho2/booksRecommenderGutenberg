Synopsis: 

In this intriguing and informative guide, Mr. G. Calver, a renowned expert in the field, presents a new edition of his groundbreaking work on silver-glass reflecting telescopes. Filled with invaluable hints and practical advice, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the manufacturing process, along with detailed instructions for silvering and adjusting these remarkable instruments. As readers delve into the pages, they will uncover the secrets behind the creation of these telescopes, gaining a deeper understanding of their inner workings and the intricate craftsmanship required to produce them. With its wealth of knowledge and expert guidance, this edition is a must-read for anyone fascinated by the world of telescopes and the wonders they reveal.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Technology

Themes: Telescope manufacturing, Silvering techniques, Telescope adjustment, Expert craftsmanship
