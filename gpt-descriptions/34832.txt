Synopsis: 
In 'Voice from the Cave' by Mildred A. Wirt, a young journalist named Penny Parker finds herself embroiled in a mysterious case when she stumbles upon a hidden cave during a hiking trip. Intrigued by the eerie voice that emanates from within, Penny becomes determined to uncover the secrets that lie hidden in the depths of the cave. As she delves deeper into the investigation, she soon realizes that the voice holds the key to a long-forgotten tragedy, and she must race against time to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Determination and perseverance
