Synopsis: In 'The God of Civilization: A Romance' by Pittock, M. A., Mrs., a young woman finds herself entangled in a mysterious world where ancient gods and modern society collide. As she delves deeper into this hidden realm, she uncovers secrets that challenge her perception of reality and her own identity. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and ultimately decide the fate of civilization itself.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Reality vs. Illusion, Power and Responsibility
