Synopsis: In "Sketch of Handel and Beethoven: Two Lectures," Thomas Hanly Ball takes us on a captivating journey through the lives and music of two legendary composers. Through his insightful lectures, delivered in the intimate setting of the Wimbledon Village Club, Ball unveils the fascinating stories behind Handel and Beethoven, shedding light on their artistic genius, personal struggles, and the profound impact they had on the world of music. As Ball delves into their compositions, he invites us to explore the depths of their emotions, the evolution of their styles, and the enduring legacy they left behind. This enlightening and engaging exploration of Handel and Beethoven is a must-read for music enthusiasts and history lovers alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Biography

Themes: Artistic genius, Personal struggles, Musical legacy
