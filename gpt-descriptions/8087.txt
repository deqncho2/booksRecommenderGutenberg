Synopsis: In 'A Fountain Sealed' by Anne Douglas Sedgwick, a young woman named Mary is torn between her duty to her family and her desire for personal freedom. As she navigates the complexities of love and societal expectations, Mary finds herself facing a difficult choice that could change her life forever. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century England, this captivating novel explores the timeless themes of love, duty, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Love, Duty, Personal Freedom
