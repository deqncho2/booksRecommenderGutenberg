Synopsis: In 'The Old Blood' by Frederick Palmer, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious deaths that seem to be connected to an ancient curse. As the townspeople struggle to uncover the truth, they find themselves facing dark secrets, supernatural forces, and a race against time to break the curse before it claims more lives. With a blend of suspense, horror, and a touch of the supernatural, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: Curses, Small-town Secrets, Supernatural Forces
