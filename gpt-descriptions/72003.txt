Synopsis: In "Ending the Depression through Planned Obsolescence," Bernard London presents a groundbreaking solution to the economic crisis that has plagued society. With a unique perspective on consumerism and innovation, London proposes a radical idea that could potentially transform the world. This thought-provoking book challenges conventional wisdom and offers a fresh approach to tackling the depression, leaving readers eager to explore the possibilities it presents.

Genres: Non-fiction, Economics, Social Commentary

Themes: Economic crisis, Consumerism, Innovation
