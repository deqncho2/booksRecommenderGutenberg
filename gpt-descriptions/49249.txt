Synopsis: 
'Folk-Lore and Legends: Russian and Polish' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Russian and Polish folklore. From enchanting tales of mythical creatures and magical beings to haunting legends of love, betrayal, and redemption, this collection offers a glimpse into the captivating world of Slavic folklore. Delve into the mysterious depths of Baba Yaga's forest, witness the power of the Firebird, and unravel the secrets of the elusive Rusalka. With each story, readers will be transported to a realm where reality and fantasy intertwine, leaving them spellbound until the very last page.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Magic, Transformation, Love and Betrayal, Redemption
