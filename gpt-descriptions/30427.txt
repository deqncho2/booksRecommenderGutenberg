Synopsis: In 'The Lost Kafoozalum' by Pauline Ashwell, a young archaeologist named Amelia embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization. When she stumbles upon a mysterious artifact, Amelia becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, facing treacherous obstacles and unexpected allies along the way. As she delves deeper into the enigma of the lost Kafoozalum, Amelia discovers that the fate of the world may rest in her hands. Will she be able to unlock the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Legacy, Redemption
