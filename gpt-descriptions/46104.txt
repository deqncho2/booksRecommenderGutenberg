Synopsis: In 'The Girl in Industry' by Dorothy Josephine Collier, a young woman named Emily finds herself thrust into the male-dominated world of industry during the early 20th century. As she navigates the challenges and prejudices of the time, Emily's determination and resilience shine through, propelling her on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel explores the complexities of gender roles, societal expectations, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Women's Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Gender Equality, Ambition, Resilience, Self-Discovery
