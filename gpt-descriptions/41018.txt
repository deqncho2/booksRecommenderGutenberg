Synopsis: In the second volume of 'John Leech, His Life and Work', William Powell Frith delves deeper into the fascinating life and artistic career of the renowned Victorian caricaturist. From his early struggles to his rise to fame, this biography uncovers the untold stories behind Leech's iconic illustrations and provides a captivating glimpse into the world of 19th-century London. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, Frith paints a vivid portrait of a man whose art captured the essence of his time and left an indelible mark on the art world.

Genres: Biography, Art, History

Themes: Artistic expression, Victorian society, Cultural impact
