Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts on War Trails in Belgium; Or, Caught Between Hostile Armies' by Herbert Carter, a group of brave Boy Scouts find themselves in the midst of the chaos and danger of war-torn Belgium. As they navigate through the treacherous landscapes and encounter unexpected challenges, their loyalty, resourcefulness, and determination are put to the ultimate test. With their lives hanging in the balance, the Boy Scouts must rely on their training and camaraderie to survive the perils of being caught between hostile armies.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Survival, Friendship, Loyalty
