Synopsis: In "A Devotee: An Episode in the Life of a Butterfly" by Mary Cholmondeley, readers are introduced to the intriguing world of a young woman named Cicely. As she navigates the complexities of love, duty, and societal expectations, Cicely finds herself torn between two very different suitors. With her heart torn and her future hanging in the balance, Cicely must make a choice that will shape her destiny. This captivating tale explores the depths of human emotions and the consequences of following one's heart.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and passion, Duty and societal expectations
