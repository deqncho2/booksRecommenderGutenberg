Synopsis: 

In "Thoughts I Met on the Highway: Words of Friendly Cheer From "The Life Books"," Ralph Waldo Trine takes readers on a captivating journey filled with profound insights and uplifting messages. As the author encounters various individuals on his travels, he shares their stories and imparts wisdom that resonates deeply with the reader. Through these encounters, Trine explores the power of positive thinking, the importance of self-belief, and the transformative nature of gratitude. This collection of thought-provoking and inspiring anecdotes will leave readers feeling motivated and empowered to embrace the beauty of life.

Genres: Self-help, Inspirational, Philosophy

Themes: Positive thinking, Self-belief, Gratitude
