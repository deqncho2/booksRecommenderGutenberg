Synopsis: 
In 'The Three Just Men' by Wallace Edgar, a secret society of three brilliant individuals emerges, dedicated to delivering justice where the law fails. When a prominent politician is targeted for assassination, the three men embark on a relentless pursuit to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice. As the tension rises and the clock ticks, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns, where the line between right and wrong becomes blurred.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Vigilantism, Justice, Morality
