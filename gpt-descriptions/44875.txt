Synopsis: 
In 'The Cruise of the Midge', the reader is taken on a thrilling adventure across the high seas, as the young protagonist embarks on a perilous journey filled with treacherous pirates, hidden treasures, and unexpected alliances. As the crew of the Midge battles against the forces of nature and the dangers lurking in the depths, they must also confront their own inner demons and face the consequences of their choices. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to discover what fate has in store for the crew of the Midge.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Nautical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Morality, Loyalty
