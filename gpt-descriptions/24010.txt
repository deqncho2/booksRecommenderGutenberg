Synopsis: In 'The Gods are Athirst' by Anatole France, a young artist named Évariste Gamelin finds himself caught up in the tumultuous events of the French Revolution. As he becomes increasingly radicalized, Évariste's passion for justice and his desire to create a new society lead him down a dangerous path. Amidst the chaos and violence, he must confront the moral dilemmas and personal sacrifices that come with his newfound power. Will Évariste's ideals survive the test of revolution, or will he be consumed by the very forces he sought to overthrow?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Revolution, Idealism, Corruption, Morality, Power
