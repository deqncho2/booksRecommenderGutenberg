Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Miguel de Cervantes Saavedra' is a comprehensive collection of the literary works by the renowned Spanish author, Miguel de Cervantes Saavedra. This index includes some of his most famous works, such as "Don Quixote," "Novelas Exemplares," and "The Trials of Persiles and Sigismunda," among others. Each work showcases Cervantes' masterful storytelling, wit, and exploration of the human condition. Whether it's the adventures of the delusional knight-errant, Don Quixote, or the intricate tales of love, honor, and deception, Cervantes' works continue to captivate readers with their timeless themes and rich characters.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama, Comedy

Themes: Chivalry, Madness, Love, Honor, Deception, Identity, Social Critique
