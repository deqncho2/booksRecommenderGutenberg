Synopsis: In 'The Alligator and Its Allies' by Reese, A. M., a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances, leaving the residents in a state of fear and confusion. As the investigation unfolds, a peculiar alliance forms between an unlikely group of individuals, including a retired detective, a quirky librarian, and a reclusive herpetologist. Together, they delve into the dark secrets of the town, uncovering a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As they race against time to solve the mystery, they soon realize that the true danger lies not only in the alligator-infested swamps but also within the hearts of those closest to them.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Unlikely alliances, Small-town dynamics
