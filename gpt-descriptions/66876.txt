Synopsis: In "In Naaman's House" by Marian MacLean Finney, a young woman named Ruth finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries when she takes a job as a governess in the grand estate of the wealthy Naaman family. As she delves deeper into the family's history, she uncovers dark secrets and hidden desires that threaten to unravel everything she thought she knew. With danger lurking around every corner, Ruth must navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and redemption to uncover the truth and save herself and those she cares about.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and betrayal, Redemption and forgiveness
