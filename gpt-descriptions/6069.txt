Synopsis: In "The Pony Rider Boys in the Ozarks; Or, The Secret of Ruby Mountain," the adventurous group of young riders embarks on a thrilling journey through the picturesque Ozark Mountains. As they explore the rugged terrain, they stumble upon a hidden secret that leads them on a quest filled with danger, mystery, and unexpected encounters. With their courage and resourcefulness, the Pony Rider Boys must unravel the enigma of Ruby Mountain before it's too late.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Courage, Discovery
