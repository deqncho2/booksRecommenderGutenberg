Synopsis: In "Polly in the Southwest" by Lillian Elizabeth Roy, a spirited young girl named Polly embarks on a thrilling adventure in the captivating landscapes of the American Southwest. As she explores the vast deserts, encounters mysterious Native American tribes, and unravels the secrets of ancient ruins, Polly's courage and determination are put to the test. With unexpected friendships and unexpected dangers lurking around every corner, Polly's journey becomes a captivating tale of self-discovery and the power of embracing the unknown.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Cultural Diversity
