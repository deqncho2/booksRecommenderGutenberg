Synopsis: In 'Chitra, a Play in One Act' by Rabindranath Tagore, the reader is transported to a mythical kingdom where a princess named Chitra struggles with her identity and societal expectations. When a prince arrives seeking her hand in marriage, Chitra must confront her inner conflicts and make a choice that will shape her destiny. This captivating play explores the complexities of self-discovery, love, and the power of personal transformation.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mythology

Themes: Identity, Self-discovery, Love, Gender roles
