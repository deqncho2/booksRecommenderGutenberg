Synopsis: 
In the small town of Whistler, a mysterious series of events unfolds when a young woman named Emily discovers an old diary hidden in her attic. As she delves deeper into the diary's secrets, she becomes entangled in a web of dark family history, long-buried secrets, and a chilling presence that seems to haunt the town. With each page turned, Emily uncovers shocking revelations that threaten to unravel her own sanity and the lives of those around her. As she races against time to uncover the truth, she must confront her own fears and confront the malevolent force that lurks in the shadows of Whistler.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Family secrets, Psychological suspense, Supernatural presence
