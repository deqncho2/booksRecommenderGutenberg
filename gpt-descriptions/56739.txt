Synopsis: In "High Society: Advice as to Social Campaigning, and Hints on the Management of Dowagers, Dinners, Debutantes, Dances, and the Thousand and One Diversions of Persons of Quality," authors Chappell, Parker, and Crowninshield take readers on a captivating journey into the glamorous world of high society. Through witty anecdotes, practical advice, and insightful observations, they delve into the intricacies of social campaigning, the art of managing dowagers, the secrets behind successful dinners, the allure of debutantes, the enchantment of dances, and the myriad of diversions that captivate persons of quality. This delightful guide offers a glimpse into the opulent and sometimes scandalous lives of the elite, providing readers with a fascinating exploration of the customs, rituals, and etiquette that shape high society.

Genres: Non-fiction, Social commentary, Lifestyle

Themes: High society, Etiquette, Social hierarchy, Glamour, Scandal
