Synopsis: In "Held by Chinese Brigands" by Charles Gilson, a thrilling tale unfolds as a group of unsuspecting travelers find themselves at the mercy of ruthless brigands in the heart of China. As they navigate the treacherous landscape and face unimaginable dangers, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats. Will they be able to escape the clutches of their captors and find their way back to safety? This gripping adventure takes readers on a rollercoaster ride through the unknown, filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Courage, Cultural Clash
