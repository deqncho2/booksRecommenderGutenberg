Synopsis: In 'The Shadow of the Czar' by John R. Carling, a young American journalist finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and danger while on assignment in Russia. As he delves deeper into the mysterious circumstances surrounding the ruling regime, he uncovers shocking secrets that could change the course of history. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of deception, betrayal, and power struggles, all while trying to protect the truth from those who would do anything to keep it hidden.

Genres: Political thriller, Historical fiction, Mystery

Themes: Power and corruption, Truth and deception, Loyalty and betrayal
