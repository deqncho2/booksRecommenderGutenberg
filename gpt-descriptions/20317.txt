Synopsis: In "A Treatise on Staff Making and Pivoting," Eugene Edward Hall takes readers on a fascinating journey into the intricate world of staff making. With meticulous attention to detail, Hall provides complete directions for crafting and fitting new staffs from raw materials. From selecting the right wood to shaping and pivoting, this comprehensive guide offers invaluable insights into the art of staff making. Whether you are a seasoned craftsman or a curious novice, this book will captivate your imagination and inspire you to explore the hidden secrets of this ancient craft.

Genres: Non-fiction, Craftsmanship, How-to

Themes: Craftsmanship, Precision, Tradition
