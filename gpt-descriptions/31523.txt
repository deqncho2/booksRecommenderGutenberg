Synopsis: In 'The Women-Stealers of Thrayx' by Holden, Fox B., a group of women mysteriously vanish from the planet Thrayx, leaving the remaining inhabitants in a state of fear and confusion. As the search for the missing women intensifies, a young and determined investigator uncovers a shocking secret that threatens to unravel the very fabric of their society. With time running out, she must navigate a treacherous web of deception and betrayal to save not only the missing women but also the future of Thrayx itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Gender roles, Power dynamics, Societal control
