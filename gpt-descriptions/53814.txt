Synopsis: In "A Searchlight on Germany: Germany's Blunders, Crimes and Punishment," William T. Hornaday takes readers on a captivating journey through the dark underbelly of Germany, exposing its blunders, crimes, and the consequences that await. With meticulous research and compelling storytelling, Hornaday sheds light on the hidden truths and shocking revelations that will leave readers both fascinated and appalled. From political corruption to wartime atrocities, this book uncovers the depths of Germany's transgressions and the price it must pay.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Commentary

Themes: War crimes, Accountability, National identity
