Synopsis: 
'Beasts and Super-Beasts' by Saki is a collection of witty and darkly humorous short stories that delve into the eccentricities and absurdities of Edwardian society. From mischievous children and conniving animals to cunning social climbers and unexpected twists, each story presents a unique and entertaining glimpse into the peculiarities of human nature. With Saki's signature blend of satire and irony, this collection is sure to captivate readers with its clever observations and unexpected turns.

Genres: Satire, Dark Comedy, Short Stories

Themes: Social satire, Irony, Human nature, Class divisions
