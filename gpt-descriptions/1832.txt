Synopsis: In 'The Case of the Lamp That Went Out' by Auguste Groner, a renowned detective is called upon to solve a perplexing mystery surrounding a seemingly ordinary lamp that mysteriously goes out, leading to a series of unexpected events. As the detective delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives, all while racing against time to prevent further tragedy. With each twist and turn, the detective must rely on his keen observation skills and sharp intuition to unravel the truth behind the lamp that went out.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Investigation, Time Pressure
