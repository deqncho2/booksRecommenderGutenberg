Synopsis: In 'The Husband's Story: A Novel' by David Graham Phillips, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of mystery and deception when his wife suddenly disappears without a trace. As he delves deeper into her disappearance, he uncovers shocking secrets that challenge everything he thought he knew about their marriage. With each twist and turn, the husband must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will ultimately determine the fate of his relationship and his own sanity.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Marriage, Deception, Identity, Betrayal
