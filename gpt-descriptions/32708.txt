Synopsis: In 'The Golden Age in Transylvania' by Jókai, Mór, a young nobleman finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love during the prosperous era of Transylvania. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of power and passion, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of his beloved homeland.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Political turmoil, Social class, Identity and self-discovery
