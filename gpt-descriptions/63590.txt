Synopsis: 
In 'Midshipman Merrill' by Henry Harrison Lewis, readers are transported to the thrilling world of the United States Navy during the early 19th century. The story follows the journey of young Midshipman Merrill as he embarks on a perilous voyage filled with adventure, danger, and unexpected twists. As Merrill navigates the treacherous waters of the high seas, he must confront his own fears, prove his worth, and face the challenges that come with life on a naval vessel. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this novel takes readers on an unforgettable journey through the trials and triumphs of a young midshipman.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Honor, Perseverance, Identity
