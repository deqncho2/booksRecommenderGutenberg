Synopsis: 

In the captivating "Diary of Anna Green Winslow, a Boston School Girl of 1771," we are transported back in time to the tumultuous era leading up to the American Revolution. Through the intimate and heartfelt diary entries of young Anna Green Winslow, we witness the challenges and joys of her daily life as a schoolgirl in Boston. From her encounters with friends and family to her observations of the political tensions brewing in the city, Anna's diary offers a unique perspective on a pivotal moment in history. As we follow Anna's journey, we become immersed in her world, eagerly turning the pages to discover how she navigates the complexities of her time.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Epistolary

Themes: Identity, Patriotism, Family, Independence
