Synopsis: In 'The Third Circle' by Frank Norris, we are introduced to a young artist named Curtis Jadwin, who becomes obsessed with the world of wheat trading in 19th century Chicago. As he delves deeper into this cutthroat industry, he finds himself entangled in a web of greed, ambition, and love. With his marriage at stake and his fortune hanging in the balance, Jadwin must navigate the treacherous third circle of the wheat market, where fortunes are made and lost in an instant. Will he succumb to the allure of wealth and power, or will he find redemption amidst the chaos?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Greed, Ambition, Love, Morality
