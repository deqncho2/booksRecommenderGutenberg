Synopsis: In 'The Upton Letters' by Arthur Christopher Benson, a series of mysterious letters are discovered, revealing a hidden world of secrets, desires, and forbidden love. As the letters unfold, the reader is drawn into a captivating web of intrigue, where the lives of the characters become intertwined in unexpected ways. With each letter, the tension builds, leaving the reader eager to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic correspondence.

Genres: Mystery, Epistolary Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Forbidden love, Identity and self-discovery
