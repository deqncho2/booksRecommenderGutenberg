Synopsis: In 'The Moon is Green' by Fritz Leiber, a young woman finds herself in a post-apocalyptic world where the moon has turned green. As she navigates this strange new reality, she must confront the challenges of survival, love, and the consequences of humanity's actions. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this story will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Romance

Themes: Post-apocalyptic survival, Environmental consequences, Love and relationships
