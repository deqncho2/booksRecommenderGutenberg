Synopsis: 
In "Peggy Owen, Patriot: A Story for Girls" by Lucy Foster Madison, readers are transported to the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. The story follows the spirited and courageous Peggy Owen, a young girl who finds herself caught up in the fight for independence. As Peggy navigates the challenges of war, she discovers her own strength and determination, becoming an unexpected hero in the fight for freedom. With danger lurking at every turn, Peggy's journey is filled with suspense, adventure, and the power of patriotism.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Independence, Gender Roles
