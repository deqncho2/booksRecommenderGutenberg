Synopsis: 
In 'Literary Landmarks of Venice', Laurence Hutton takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting city of Venice, exploring its rich literary history and the famous authors who found inspiration within its labyrinthine streets. From the works of Shakespeare to the musings of Lord Byron, Hutton delves into the lives and stories behind these literary giants, uncovering the secrets and hidden gems that have shaped Venice's literary landscape. With vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, this book is a must-read for both literature enthusiasts and travel lovers alike, offering a unique perspective on the city that has captivated the imaginations of countless writers throughout history.

Genres: Travel, Literary Criticism, History

Themes: Literary inspiration, Venice's cultural heritage, the influence of place on creativity
