Synopsis: In "The Dreamers" by Kella Lu, a mysterious sleeping sickness sweeps through a small college town, causing its victims to fall into a deep slumber from which they cannot wake. As the epidemic spreads, a group of students find themselves trapped in a quarantined dormitory, forced to confront their deepest fears and desires while the world outside descends into chaos. Amidst the uncertainty and isolation, they form unexpected connections and discover the power of dreams. Will they find a way to break free from the grip of this enigmatic illness?

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Dreams and reality, isolation and connection, fear and desire
