Synopsis: 
'From Fort Henry to Corinth' takes readers on a gripping journey through the American Civil War, following the experiences of a young soldier named Ned Fulton. As Ned navigates the brutal realities of war, he must confront his own fears, forge unlikely alliances, and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. From the chaos of the battlefield to the camaraderie of the camp, this captivating novel immerses readers in the harrowing and poignant world of the Civil War.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Identity, Brotherhood
