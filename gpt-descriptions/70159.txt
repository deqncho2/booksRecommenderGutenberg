Synopsis: In "The Little Barefoot," Auerbach takes us on a captivating journey through the life of a young girl named Nanni. Born into poverty and facing numerous hardships, Nanni's resilience and determination become her guiding light. As she navigates the challenges of her circumstances, she discovers unexpected friendships, uncovers hidden secrets, and finds solace in the power of love and hope. Auerbach's vivid storytelling and rich character development make this tale a heartwarming and thought-provoking read.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Poverty, Resilience, Friendship, Love, Hope
