Synopsis: In "The Kingdom of God is Within You / Christianity and Patriotism / Miscellanies," Tolstoy explores the profound connection between spirituality and social justice. Through a series of essays and reflections, he delves into the essence of Christianity, challenging conventional interpretations and advocating for a radical transformation of society. With thought-provoking insights and personal anecdotes, Tolstoy invites readers to question their own beliefs and consider the true meaning of faith and patriotism.

Genres: Philosophy, Religious Literature, Social Criticism

Themes: Spirituality, Nonviolence, Social Justice, Individualism, Moral Responsibility
