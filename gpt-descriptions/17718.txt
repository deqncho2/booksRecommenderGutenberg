Synopsis:
In the gripping novel 'Infelice' by Augusta J. Evans, the reader is transported to the antebellum South, where the lives of two young women, Infelice and Rosalind, become intertwined in a tale of love, betrayal, and redemption. Infelice, a spirited and independent woman, finds herself torn between her duty to her family and her desire for true love. As she navigates the complexities of society and grapples with her own inner demons, Infelice's journey takes her on a path filled with unexpected twists and turns. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, 'Infelice' is a captivating exploration of the human spirit and the power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Southern Gothic

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Social Expectations, Redemption
