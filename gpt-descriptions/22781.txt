Synopsis: In '32 Caliber' by Donald McGibeny, a mysterious murder takes place in a small town, leaving the residents shocked and fearful. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the search for the truth becomes a race against time. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Crime

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town intrigue, Justice and truth
