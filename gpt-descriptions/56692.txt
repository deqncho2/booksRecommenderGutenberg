Synopsis: In 'Editha's Burglar: A Story for Children', a young girl named Editha finds herself face to face with an unexpected visitor in the middle of the night. As she navigates this mysterious encounter, Editha's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. Will she be able to uncover the truth behind the burglar's presence and find a way to help him? This captivating story takes readers on a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Children's Literature, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Resourcefulness, Empathy
