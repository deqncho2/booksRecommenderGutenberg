Synopsis: In 'Modern literature: a novel, Volume 1 (of 3)', Bisset takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of a diverse group of characters, each grappling with their own personal struggles and desires. From the bustling streets of a vibrant city to the tranquil countryside, this novel weaves together multiple narratives that explore the complexities of human relationships, the pursuit of identity, and the power of literature to shape our lives. As secrets are unveiled and unexpected connections are forged, readers will be drawn into a rich tapestry of emotions and experiences that will leave them eagerly anticipating the next volumes.

Genres: Literary fiction, Contemporary fiction, Multinarrative

Themes: Identity, Relationships, Literature's impact on life
