Synopsis: In 'The Black Tide' by Arthur G. Stangland, a small coastal town is plagued by a mysterious and deadly phenomenon that emerges from the depths of the ocean. As the black tide engulfs the community, a group of unlikely heroes must uncover the truth behind its origins and find a way to stop it before it consumes everything they hold dear. With suspenseful twists and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Horror

Themes: Survival, Sacrifice, the Power of Nature
