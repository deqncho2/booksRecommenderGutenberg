Synopsis: In 'Chimera World' by Wilbur S. Peacock, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Evelyn Hartman discovers a groundbreaking technology that allows her to create a virtual reality world where people can live out their wildest dreams. As the popularity of Chimera World grows, Evelyn becomes increasingly obsessed with perfecting the experience, blurring the lines between reality and fantasy. However, as the boundaries between the virtual and real worlds begin to crumble, Evelyn must confront the consequences of her creation and the ethical dilemmas it poses.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Virtual reality, Ethics, Obsession, Identity
