Synopsis: In 'The Orange Fairy Book' by Lang, Andrew, readers will embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales from various cultures around the world. From magical creatures and brave heroes to cunning tricksters and forbidden love, this anthology offers a delightful blend of adventure, mystery, and wonder. Each story is carefully selected to transport readers to distant lands and immerse them in the rich tapestry of folklore and imagination. Prepare to be spellbound by the timeless tales that lie within the pages of 'The Orange Fairy Book'.

Genres: Fairy tales, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Adventure, Magic, Morality, Love and Sacrifice
