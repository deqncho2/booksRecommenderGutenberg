Synopsis: In this enchanting tale, a wooden puppet named Pinocchio embarks on a series of extraordinary adventures, guided by his conscience and the desire to become a real boy. From encounters with talking animals to encounters with treacherous characters, Pinocchio's journey is filled with both whimsical and perilous moments. As he navigates through a world of magic and wonder, Pinocchio learns valuable lessons about honesty, bravery, and the true meaning of family.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Coming-of-age, Morality, Identity, Family
