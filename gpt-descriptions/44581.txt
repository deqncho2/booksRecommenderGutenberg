Synopsis: In 'The Stoneground Ghost Tales', the Reverend Roland Batchel shares a collection of eerie and spine-chilling stories that have haunted the small parish he serves. From ghostly apparitions to mysterious disappearances, each tale is more unsettling than the last, leaving readers on the edge of their seats. As the Reverend delves into the dark secrets of Stoneground, he uncovers a web of supernatural occurrences that will test his faith and challenge his understanding of the world beyond. 

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Ghosts, Hauntings, Faith, Secrets
