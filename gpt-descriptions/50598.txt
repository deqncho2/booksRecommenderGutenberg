Synopsis: In the midst of the 17th century, a young orphan named Philip Marsham finds himself aboard a mysterious and notorious pirate ship, The Dark Frigate. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the high seas, Philip must confront the dangers of piracy, betrayal, and the haunting secrets that lie within the ship's dark past. With his life hanging in the balance, Philip must make choices that will test his courage and challenge his understanding of loyalty and honor.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Morality, Redemption
