Synopsis: In 'Sabbath Defence Tactics: a manual' by Bridges, James, a mysterious and enigmatic guide unveils a collection of unconventional strategies to protect oneself during the sacred day of rest. Filled with unexpected twists and surprising revelations, this gripping manual takes readers on a thrilling journey through the hidden world of Sabbath defence. As readers delve deeper into the secrets within, they will discover a wealth of knowledge that challenges their perception of the Sabbath and forces them to question the true nature of their beliefs. With each turn of the page, Bridges, James masterfully crafts a narrative that keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the ancient art of Sabbath defence.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Spiritual

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Secrets and hidden knowledge
