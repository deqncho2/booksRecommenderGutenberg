Synopsis: In 'Christmas Light' by Phillips, Ethel Calvert, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn to a mysterious antique shop during the holiday season. As she explores the enchanting store, she discovers a hidden world filled with magical Christmas lights that have the power to grant wishes. With each wish she makes, Emily's life takes unexpected turns, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and the true meaning of Christmas.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Holiday

Themes: Magic, Hope, Redemption, Self-discovery
