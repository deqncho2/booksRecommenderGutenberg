Synopsis: In 'The Silicon Jungle' by David H. Rothman, a brilliant computer programmer finds himself caught in a web of corporate espionage and high-stakes technology battles. As he delves deeper into the world of Silicon Valley, he uncovers shocking secrets that could change the course of the tech industry forever. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of power, greed, and betrayal to protect his groundbreaking invention and expose the truth.

Genres: Techno-thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Corporate espionage, Technology, Power struggle
