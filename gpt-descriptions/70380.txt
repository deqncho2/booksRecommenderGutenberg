Synopsis: 
"The "Land & Water" edition of Raemaekers' cartoons, volume 1" by Louis Raemaekers is a captivating collection of political cartoons that provide a powerful commentary on the events of World War I. Through his thought-provoking illustrations, Raemaekers sheds light on the horrors of war, the resilience of the human spirit, and the importance of standing up against injustice. This volume takes readers on a visual journey, offering a unique perspective on the war and its impact on society.

Genres: Political cartoons, Historical commentary, Art

Themes: War, Human resilience, Social justice
