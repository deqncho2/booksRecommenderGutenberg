Synopsis: 
'International Short Stories: American' is a captivating collection of diverse and thought-provoking tales that transport readers across the vast landscapes of America. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene countryside of the Midwest, these stories delve into the lives of intriguing characters, each facing their own unique challenges and desires. Through vivid storytelling and richly crafted narratives, this anthology explores the complexities of human relationships, the pursuit of dreams, and the ever-present clash between tradition and modernity. Prepare to be enthralled by this captivating journey through the heart and soul of America.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Literary Fiction

Themes: Identity, Love and Relationships, Cultural Diversity, Coming of Age, Social Issues
