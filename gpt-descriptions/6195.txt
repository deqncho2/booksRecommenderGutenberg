Synopsis: In 'Cumner's Son and Other South Sea Folk - Volume 01', Gilbert Parker takes readers on a captivating journey to the enchanting South Seas. Set against the backdrop of the Pacific islands, this collection of stories introduces us to a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique tales to tell. From the mysterious Cumner's Son, whose origins are shrouded in secrecy, to the courageous and resourceful South Sea Islanders, these narratives weave together themes of love, adventure, and the clash of cultures. As the stories unfold, readers will be transported to a world of exotic landscapes, where danger lurks beneath the surface and unexpected connections are forged. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Short Stories
Themes: Love, Identity, Cultural Conflict
