Synopsis: In 'Thin Edge' by Randall Garrett, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Endlich discovers a groundbreaking technology that could change the world forever. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but also the fate of humanity. With time running out, Dr. Endlich must navigate a treacherous web of conspiracy and betrayal to protect his invention and ensure the survival of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Survival
