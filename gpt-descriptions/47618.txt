Synopsis: In 'At His Gates: A Novel. Vol. 1 (of 3)', we are transported to the vibrant and scandalous world of Victorian London. The story revolves around the enigmatic and charismatic Lord Courtland, a man with a mysterious past and a reputation that precedes him. As the city's elite vie for his attention, a young and ambitious journalist, Emily, becomes determined to uncover the truth behind Lord Courtland's allure. However, her investigation leads her down a treacherous path, filled with secrets, betrayal, and unexpected romance. With each revelation, Emily finds herself drawn deeper into a web of intrigue that threatens to unravel everything she thought she knew about love and society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, societal expectations, the power of love and desire
