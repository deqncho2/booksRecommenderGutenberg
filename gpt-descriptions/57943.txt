Synopsis: 

'A Treatyse of Fysshynge wyth an Angle' takes readers on a captivating journey back in time to 1496 England, where the art of fishing was just beginning to be explored. This facsimile reproduction of the first book on fishing printed in England by Wynkyn de Worde at Westminster offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of angling as seen through the eyes of Juliana Berners. As readers delve into the pages of this historical gem, they will discover the secrets of fishing with an angle, uncovering the techniques, equipment, and strategies employed by anglers of the past. 

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: Fishing, History, Nature
