Synopsis: 
'Rural Architecture: Being a Complete Description of Farm Houses, Cottages, and Out Buildings' by Allen, Lewis Falley is a comprehensive guide that takes readers on a journey through the fascinating world of rural architecture. From charming farmhouses to cozy cottages and practical outbuildings, this book provides a detailed description of various structures found in the countryside. With beautiful illustrations and insightful explanations, the author explores the unique design elements, construction techniques, and functional aspects of these rural buildings. Whether you are an architecture enthusiast, a homeowner, or simply curious about the charm of rural living, this book offers a captivating exploration of the architectural wonders found in the countryside.

Genres: Non-fiction, Architecture, Home Improvement

Themes: Rural living, Architectural design, Construction techniques
