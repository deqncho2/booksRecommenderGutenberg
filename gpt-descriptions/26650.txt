Synopsis: In 'The Ducks and Frogs, a Tale of the Bogs' by Fire-Fly, Fanny, a mysterious event occurs in the peaceful bogs where the ducks and frogs reside. As tensions rise and alliances are tested, the creatures must navigate through unexpected challenges and uncover the truth behind the disturbance. With danger lurking in every corner, the fate of the bogs hangs in the balance, and the ducks and frogs must find a way to restore harmony before it's too late.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Environmentalism
