Synopsis: In 'Old Church Lore' by Andrews, William, a young historian stumbles upon a hidden diary that reveals a dark secret lurking within an ancient church. As he delves deeper into the mysterious past, he becomes entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and unexplained phenomena. With each revelation, the historian's sanity is tested, and he must confront the chilling truth before it consumes him entirely.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Supernatural forces, Psychological suspense
