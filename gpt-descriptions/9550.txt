Synopsis: 
In the second edition of "Manual of Gardening," renowned horticulturist Liberty Hyde Bailey provides a comprehensive and practical guide to creating beautiful home grounds and cultivating a variety of flowers, fruits, and vegetables. With detailed instructions and insightful tips, Bailey takes readers on a journey through the art of gardening, offering valuable advice on soil preparation, plant selection, propagation, and maintenance. Whether you are a novice or an experienced gardener, this book is a treasure trove of knowledge that will inspire and empower you to transform your own backyard into a flourishing oasis.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gardening, Reference

Themes: Horticulture, Home gardening, Plant cultivation, Landscape design
