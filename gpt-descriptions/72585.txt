Synopsis: In 'The Viaduct Murder' by Ronald Arbuthnott Knox, a seemingly ordinary train journey takes a sinister turn when a murder occurs on a viaduct. As the investigation unfolds, the detective must navigate a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected twists to uncover the truth behind this perplexing crime. With an array of intriguing characters and a cleverly crafted plot, this gripping mystery will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice, Perception
