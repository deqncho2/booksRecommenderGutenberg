Synopsis: In "Young Glory and the Spanish cruiser: A Brave Fight Against Odds," Walter Fenton Mott takes readers on a thrilling adventure set during the Spanish-American War. The story follows the courageous Young Glory as he finds himself facing a formidable Spanish cruiser, leading to a high-stakes battle that will test his bravery and determination. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome of this daring confrontation.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Bravery, Determination, Patriotism
