Synopsis: In "Boy Scouts in Glacier Park: The Adventures of Two Young Easterners in the Heart of the High Rockies" by Walter Prichard Eaton, two young boys from the East embark on an unforgettable journey to Glacier National Park. As they navigate the rugged terrain and encounter various challenges, they discover the true meaning of friendship, courage, and self-discovery. With breathtaking descriptions of the majestic landscapes and thrilling adventures, this book takes readers on an exhilarating expedition through the heart of the High Rockies.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Nature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
