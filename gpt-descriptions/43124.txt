Synopsis: In 'The Flaming Sword in Serbia and Elsewhere', Mabel Annie Stobart takes us on a captivating journey through war-torn Serbia during World War I. As a fearless and determined nurse, Stobart provides a firsthand account of her experiences in the midst of chaos and destruction. From the frontlines to the makeshift hospitals, she encounters both the horrors of war and the resilience of the human spirit. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt anecdotes, Stobart paints a vivid picture of the people she meets and the challenges she faces, ultimately revealing the true cost of war.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War Literature

Themes: War, Courage, Humanitarianism, Resilience
