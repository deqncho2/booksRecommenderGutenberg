Synopsis: 

In the Book of Obadiah, a short but powerful prophetic book from the Bible, the reader is taken on a journey through the divine message delivered to the nation of Edom. Through vivid imagery and poetic language, the book explores the consequences of pride, betrayal, and the ultimate justice that awaits those who oppress others. As the reader delves into the depths of this ancient text, they will witness the unfolding of a timeless message that resonates with the human experience.

Genres: Religious literature, Prophetic literature, Poetry

Themes: Pride and its consequences, Betrayal, Divine justice
