Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Old Virginia and Her Neighbours', John Fiske delves deeper into the captivating history of the early American colonies. From the struggles of the Jamestown settlement to the rise of the Carolinas and the conflicts with Native American tribes, Fiske paints a vivid picture of the challenges and triumphs faced by the early settlers. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, he explores the complex relationships between the English, French, and Spanish colonies, shedding light on the fascinating interactions that shaped the future of America. This volume is a captivating journey through the untold stories of the early American colonies, filled with intrigue, adventure, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Colonialism, Exploration, Cultural Exchange
