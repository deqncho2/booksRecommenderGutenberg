Synopsis: In 'The Coryston Family: A Novel', Mrs. Ward takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the Coryston family, a prominent English aristocratic clan. Set against the backdrop of political and social upheaval, the story follows the family's struggles, ambitions, and secrets, as they navigate the complexities of love, power, and duty. As the Corystons grapple with their own desires and the expectations of society, their choices have far-reaching consequences that will test their loyalties and challenge their very identities. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this novel delves into the intricate web of family dynamics and the clash between tradition and progress.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Family Saga, Social Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Power and Duty, Identity and Self-discovery
