Synopsis:
In Molière's Tartuffe; Or, The Hypocrite, a seemingly pious and virtuous man named Tartuffe enters the household of a wealthy family, quickly gaining their trust and admiration. However, as his influence grows, it becomes clear that Tartuffe's true intentions may not be as noble as they appear. With his cunning manipulation and religious façade, Tartuffe threatens to tear apart the family's harmony and expose their vulnerabilities. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the family must navigate a treacherous path to protect their own interests and confront the hypocrisy that lies within their midst.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Drama

Themes: Hypocrisy, Deception, Morality, Family, Power
