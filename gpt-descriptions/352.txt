Synopsis: 
In 'Buttered Side Down: Stories', Edna Ferber takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of short stories that delve into the lives of ordinary people facing extraordinary circumstances. From the bustling streets of New York City to the quiet corners of small-town America, Ferber's vivid storytelling brings to life a diverse cast of characters, each grappling with their own hopes, dreams, and challenges. Through tales of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness, Ferber explores the complexities of human nature and the unexpected twists and turns that life often presents. Prepare to be enthralled by these poignant and thought-provoking narratives that will leave you pondering the intricacies of the human experience.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Human nature, Ambition, Love, Life's challenges
