Synopsis: 
In 'Summer' by Romain Rolland, we are transported to a small French village where a young man named Alexis finds himself caught between his passion for music and his duty to his family. As he navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations, Alexis embarks on a journey of self-discovery that will challenge his beliefs and shape his future. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant summer, this captivating novel explores the transformative power of art and the pursuit of one's true calling.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Artistic expression, Love and duty
