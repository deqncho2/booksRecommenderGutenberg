Synopsis: In 'Life's Minor Collisions', the Warner sisters take readers on a captivating journey through a collection of short stories that explore the unexpected collisions that shape our lives. From chance encounters to serendipitous moments, each story delves into the intricate web of human connections and the profound impact they can have. With richly drawn characters and poignant narratives, this book offers a heartfelt exploration of the beauty and complexity of life's small but significant collisions.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Contemporary Literature

Themes: Human connections, Serendipity, Life's surprises, Meaningful encounters
