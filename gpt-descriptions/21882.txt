Synopsis: 
In 'The House of Torchy' by Ford, Sewell, we follow the misadventures of Torchy, a lovable troublemaker who finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a secret society. As Torchy navigates through a series of hilarious and dangerous situations, he must use his wit and cunning to uncover the truth and save the day. With unexpected twists and turns, this fast-paced and humorous tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden societies, Loyalty and friendship
