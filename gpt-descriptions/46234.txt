Synopsis: In 'Guingamor, Lanval, Tyolet, Bisclaveret: Four lais rendered into English prose', Marie de France takes us on a captivating journey through four enchanting tales. From the forbidden love between a knight and a fairy, to the extraordinary adventures of a knight who can transform into a wolf, each story is filled with intrigue, romance, and unexpected twists. As we delve into the lives of these courageous characters, we are transported to a world where chivalry, honor, and the supernatural collide. 

Genres: Medieval literature, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Love and desire, Loyalty and betrayal, Transformation and identity
