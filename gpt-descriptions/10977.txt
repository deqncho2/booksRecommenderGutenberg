Synopsis: In the rugged wilderness of the Canadian Rockies, a legendary grizzly bear known as "The Grizzly King" reigns supreme. This thrilling adventure follows the intertwined lives of a young man named Thorpe and a fearless female journalist named Neewa, as they embark on a quest to uncover the truth behind the myth of The Grizzly King. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter both human and animal adversaries, their journey becomes a test of courage, loyalty, and the indomitable spirit of the wild.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Nature

Themes: Man vs. Nature, Survival, Love and Loyalty
