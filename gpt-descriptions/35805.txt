Synopsis: In the small town of Dutcher's Coolly, Rose, a young and spirited girl, finds herself caught between the allure of the city and the simplicity of her rural life. As she navigates the challenges of love, family, and societal expectations, Rose's journey becomes a captivating exploration of self-discovery and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Identity, Independence, Rural vs Urban life
