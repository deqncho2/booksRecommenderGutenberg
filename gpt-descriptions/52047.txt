Synopsis: In 'A Little Maid in Toyland' by Sutton, Adah Louise, follow the enchanting journey of a young girl named Mary as she finds herself transported to a magical world filled with talking toys and whimsical adventures. As Mary navigates through Toyland, she encounters a cast of colorful characters, including the mischievous Raggedy Andy and the wise old Toymaker. Together, they embark on a quest to save Toyland from an impending threat, all while Mary learns valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Imagination
