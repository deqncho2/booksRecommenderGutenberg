Synopsis: In 'The Last Shot' by Frederick Palmer, a renowned investigative journalist, Jack Donovan, finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger when he stumbles upon a shocking secret that threatens to shake the foundations of power. As he races against time to uncover the truth, Donovan must navigate a treacherous landscape of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, all while trying to stay one step ahead of those who will stop at nothing to protect their dark secrets. With his career and life on the line, Donovan must make the ultimate decision: to expose the truth or succumb to the forces that seek to silence him forever.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Power, Truth
