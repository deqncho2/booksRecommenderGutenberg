Synopsis: 

In 'Owindia: a true tale of the MacKenzie River Indians, North-West America', Charlotte Selina Bompas takes readers on a captivating journey into the heart of the MacKenzie River Indians' world. Set in the vast wilderness of North-West America, this true tale follows the life of Owindia, a young indigenous girl who embarks on a remarkable adventure filled with danger, discovery, and self-discovery. As Owindia navigates the challenges of her environment and encounters various characters along the way, readers are transported to a world rich in cultural traditions, natural beauty, and the resilience of the human spirit. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Indigenous culture, Survival, Identity, Nature
