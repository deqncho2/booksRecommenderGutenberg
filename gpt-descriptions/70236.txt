Synopsis: In "The Secret of the Old Mill," the Hardy Boys find themselves embroiled in a mysterious case involving a hidden mill and a dangerous criminal organization. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the old mill, the brothers must use their detective skills and bravery to uncover the truth and bring the criminals to justice. With thrilling twists and turns, this action-packed adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Crime-solving
