Synopsis: In 'The War Terror' by Arthur B. Reeve, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of international intrigue and espionage as he investigates a series of mysterious bombings that threaten to plunge the world into chaos. With each explosive event, the stakes rise higher, and the detective must race against time to uncover the truth behind the war terror before it's too late. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers shocking secrets and unexpected alliances, leading him down a dangerous path where trust is scarce and betrayal lurks at every turn.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: International intrigue, Terrorism, Betrayal
