Synopsis: 
In 'Sanders' Union Fourth Reader', Charles W. Sanders presents a comprehensive guide to the art of rhetorical reading. This reader is filled with a wide range of exercises, including prose and poetry, carefully designed to enhance reading skills and engage students of all levels. With its diverse selection of styles and themes, this book is a valuable resource for teaching in schools of every grade.

Genres: Educational, Instructional, Literature

Themes: Rhetorical reading, Reading comprehension, Language skills development
