Synopsis: 
'Legends of the Gods: The Egyptian Texts, edited with Translations' takes readers on a captivating journey into the ancient world of Egyptian mythology. In this remarkable collection, E. A. Wallis Budge presents a compilation of ancient Egyptian texts, providing translations and insights into the fascinating stories of gods, goddesses, and mythical beings. From the creation of the world to epic battles between deities, this book unveils the rich tapestry of Egyptian mythology, offering a glimpse into the beliefs and legends that shaped one of the world's most intriguing civilizations.

Genres: Mythology, History, Religion

Themes: Creation, Deities, Mythical Beings, Ancient Civilization
