Synopsis: 

'Lancashire Idylls' takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Lancashire, England. Set in the late 19th century, this collection of stories by Mather, Marshall introduces us to a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique tales to tell. From the bustling streets of industrial towns to the tranquil beauty of the countryside, the author weaves together a tapestry of human experiences, exploring themes of love, loss, resilience, and the enduring spirit of the Lancashire people. Through vivid descriptions and heartfelt narratives, 'Lancashire Idylls' offers a glimpse into a bygone era, immersing readers in the rich tapestry of life in this enchanting region.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Love, Loss, Resilience, Community, Nature
