Synopsis: In 'A Butterfly Chase' by Stahl, P.-J., a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden world of magic and adventure when she stumbles upon a mysterious butterfly. As she embarks on a thrilling chase to catch the elusive creature, Lily finds herself drawn into a captivating journey that will test her courage and reveal secrets she never could have imagined. With each twist and turn, the butterfly leads Lily deeper into a realm where dreams come to life and the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur. Will Lily be able to unravel the butterfly's secrets and unlock her own hidden potential?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Discovery, Courage, Imagination
