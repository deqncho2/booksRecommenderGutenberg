Synopsis: In "The Sacred Tree; or, the tree in religion and myth," Mrs. J. H. Philpot delves into the fascinating world of trees and their significance in various religions and myths. From ancient civilizations to modern-day beliefs, this captivating exploration uncovers the deep-rooted connections between trees and spirituality. Through captivating anecdotes and extensive research, the author unveils the symbolic and sacred roles trees have played throughout history, shedding light on their mystical allure and the profound impact they have had on human culture. This thought-provoking book invites readers on a journey through time and across cultures, revealing the profound and often overlooked significance of the humble tree.

Genres: Non-fiction, Mythology, Religion

Themes: Symbolism, Spirituality, Cultural significance
