Synopsis: In 'Mr. Fortune's Practice' by H. C. Bailey, readers are introduced to the enigmatic and unconventional detective, Mr. Reggie Fortune. When a wealthy man is found dead in his locked study, seemingly from natural causes, Mr. Fortune is called upon to investigate the peculiar circumstances surrounding the death. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected connections that lead him down a path filled with danger and intrigue. With his sharp wit and unorthodox methods, Mr. Fortune must navigate through a maze of clues to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Unconventional Investigation
