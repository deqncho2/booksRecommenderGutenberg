Synopsis: Thalaba the Destroyer by Robert Southey is an epic poem that follows the extraordinary journey of a young hero named Thalaba. Born with a prophetic destiny, Thalaba embarks on a perilous quest to avenge the murder of his family and rid the world of evil. Along his path, he encounters mythical creatures, battles supernatural forces, and faces unimaginable challenges. As Thalaba's powers grow, so does his determination to fulfill his destiny, leading him to confront the ultimate test of his strength and courage. 

Genres: Epic poetry, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Destiny, Heroism, Good vs. Evil
