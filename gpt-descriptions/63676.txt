Synopsis: In 'The Pluto Lamp' by Charles A. Stearns, a mysterious antique lamp with a hidden secret is discovered by a young woman named Emily. As she delves deeper into the lamp's history, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, uncovering a world of hidden treasures and dark secrets. With each clue she uncovers, Emily finds herself drawn closer to the truth, but also closer to the sinister forces that will stop at nothing to possess the lamp's power. Will she be able to unlock the lamp's secrets before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden treasures, the power of objects, the pursuit of knowledge
