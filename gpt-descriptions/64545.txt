Synopsis: In 'Marguerite; or, The Isle of Demons and Other Poems', George Martin takes readers on a captivating journey to a mysterious island filled with dark secrets and haunting beauty. As Marguerite, a young woman with a troubled past, finds herself stranded on this enigmatic isle, she must confront her fears and unravel the island's ancient mysteries. Through a series of mesmerizing poems, Martin weaves a tale of love, loss, and redemption that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Poetry, Gothic, Fantasy

Themes: Love, Loss, Redemption, Mystery
