Synopsis:
In 'The School-Girls in Number 40; or, Principle Put to the Test' by Anonymous, we are introduced to a group of schoolgirls residing in Number 40, a boarding house filled with secrets and mysteries. As the girls navigate their daily lives, they soon find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and unexpected events. With each passing day, their friendships are tested, alliances are formed, and secrets are unveiled, leading them on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and resilience. Will they be able to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic Number 40, or will they become mere pawns in a much larger game?

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Resilience
