Synopsis: 
'Southern War Songs: Camp-Fire, Patriotic and Sentimental' takes readers on a captivating journey through the heart and soul of the American South during a tumultuous period of history. This collection of songs, carefully curated and accompanied by poignant stories, offers a glimpse into the emotions, struggles, and resilience of the Southern people during the war. From rousing patriotic anthems to tender sentimental ballads, these songs serve as a powerful testament to the indomitable spirit of a region torn apart by conflict. 

Genres: 
Music, History, Anthology

Themes: 
Patriotism, Resilience, Emotion, Identity
