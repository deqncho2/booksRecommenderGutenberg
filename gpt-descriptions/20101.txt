Synopsis: In 'Under Fire' by Charles King, a renowned journalist finds himself thrust into the heart of a dangerous conflict as he investigates a mysterious series of events. As he delves deeper into the truth, he uncovers a web of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate the treacherous landscape of war, risking everything to uncover the truth and bring justice to those involved.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, War

Themes: Truth and deception, Loyalty and betrayal
