Synopsis: 

In 'Cyropaedia: The Education of Cyrus', Xenophon takes us on a captivating journey through the life of Cyrus the Great, the founder of the Persian Empire. This historical novel follows Cyrus from his humble beginnings as a young prince to his rise as a powerful and wise ruler. Through a series of adventures, battles, and political maneuverings, we witness Cyrus' transformation into a legendary leader, admired for his military prowess, diplomatic skills, and benevolent rule.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Leadership, Education, Morality, Power, War and Peace
