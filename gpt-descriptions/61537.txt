Synopsis: In "The Charitable Pestmaster; Or, The Cure of the Plague," Thomas Sherwood presents a concise and essential guide on how to protect oneself from the deadly plague and offers remedies for those already infected. Additionally, he includes a treatise on the cure of smallpox. This work provides valuable instructions and insights into preserving the body from infection and combating these devastating diseases.

Genres: Medical literature, self-help, historical

Themes: Disease prevention, medical remedies, public health
