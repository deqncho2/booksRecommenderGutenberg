Synopsis: In 'Dangerous Dilemmas: Startling but True' by Peddie, James Anderson, prepare to be captivated by a collection of gripping and shocking true stories that will keep you on the edge of your seat. From heart-stopping encounters with dangerous animals to spine-chilling tales of survival against all odds, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through some of the most perilous situations imaginable. With each page turn, you'll be left wondering how these individuals managed to escape their dangerous dilemmas. Brace yourself for a rollercoaster ride of adrenaline-pumping narratives that will leave you breathless.

Genres: Non-fiction, True Crime, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Perilous Situations, Human Resilience
