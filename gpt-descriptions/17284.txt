Synopsis: In 'Rome in 1860', Dicey, Edward takes us on a captivating journey through the vibrant streets of Rome during a pivotal year in history. As political tensions rise and the city undergoes significant changes, we follow a diverse cast of characters whose lives intertwine amidst the backdrop of revolution and intrigue. From the opulent palaces of the aristocracy to the bustling markets of the common folk, this immersive tale offers a glimpse into the heart of a city on the brink of transformation. With secrets to uncover and alliances to forge, 'Rome in 1860' is a gripping historical novel that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Mystery

Themes: Revolution, Identity, Power Dynamics
