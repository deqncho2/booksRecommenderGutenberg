Synopsis: In "Ginevra: or, The Old Oak Chest, a Christmas Story," a mysterious old oak chest holds the key to a captivating tale of love, loss, and redemption. As the chest's secrets are slowly unveiled, a young woman named Ginevra finds herself drawn into a world of enchantment and discovery. With each revelation, Ginevra's life intertwines with the past, leading her on a journey that will forever change her perception of love and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love, Loss, Redemption, Family, Forgiveness
