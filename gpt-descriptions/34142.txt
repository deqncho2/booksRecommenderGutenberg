Synopsis: In 'By Birth a Lady' by George Manville Fenn, a young woman named Maud is thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when she discovers a hidden family secret. As she delves deeper into her family's history, she uncovers a web of lies, betrayal, and forbidden love that spans generations. With danger lurking around every corner, Maud must navigate the treacherous waters of high society while trying to uncover the truth about her own identity. Will she be able to unravel the secrets of her past before it's too late?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Family Secrets, Betrayal, Forbidden Love
