Synopsis: 
'War's Embers, and Other Verses' by Ivor Gurney is a collection of poignant and evocative poems that delve into the depths of human emotions and experiences. From the haunting memories of war to the beauty of nature, Gurney's verses transport readers to a world where love, loss, and longing intertwine. Through his lyrical and introspective style, he captures the essence of the human condition, exploring themes of nostalgia, resilience, and the enduring power of art.

Genres: Poetry, War Literature, Nature Writing

Themes: War, Love, Loss, Nature, Nostalgia, Resilience, Art
