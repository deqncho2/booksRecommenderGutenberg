Synopsis: In this retelling of Miguel de Cervantes' classic novel, 'Don Quixote of the Mancha', Judge Parry brings to life the timeless tale of an eccentric knight-errant and his loyal squire as they embark on a series of misadventures in pursuit of chivalry and honor. With a touch of humor and a deep understanding of human nature, this retelling captures the essence of Don Quixote's delusions and the impact they have on those around him. From tilting at windmills to rescuing damsels in distress, the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the imaginative mind of Don Quixote and the world he creates.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Satire

Themes: Madness and Sanity, Illusion and Reality, Idealism and Pragmatism
