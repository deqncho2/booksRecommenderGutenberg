Synopsis: In 'His Little Royal Highness' by Ruth Ogden, a young boy named Henry discovers a hidden secret that changes his life forever. When he stumbles upon a mysterious letter, he embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him to a world he never knew existed. As Henry navigates through this enchanting realm, he must confront his fears, make new friends, and uncover the truth about his own identity. With danger lurking at every turn, Henry's journey becomes a race against time to save not only himself but also the fate of an entire kingdom.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Courage, Friendship, Destiny
