Synopsis: In 'The Shadow of a Crime: A Cumbrian Romance' by Caine, Hall, Sir, a mysterious crime shrouded in secrecy unfolds in the picturesque landscapes of Cumbria. As the story unravels, a web of deceit, love, and betrayal emerges, leaving the reader captivated by the intricate connections between the characters. With each twist and turn, the shadow of the crime looms larger, keeping the reader on the edge of their seat until the shocking truth is finally revealed.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and betrayal, Justice and redemption
