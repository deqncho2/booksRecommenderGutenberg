Synopsis: In the midst of the devastating Thirty Years' War, a group of young carpenters in the town of Freiberg find themselves caught up in a dangerous web of political intrigue and personal vendettas. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of war-torn Germany, they must rely on their skills, resourcefulness, and unwavering loyalty to protect their loved ones and fight for justice. With each passing day, their courage is tested, and they discover the true meaning of sacrifice and resilience in the face of unimaginable adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Survival, Justice
