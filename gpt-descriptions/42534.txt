Synopsis: In 'The Narrow House' by Evelyn Scott, a young woman named Mary finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries when she moves into a peculiar house with a dark past. As she delves deeper into the history of the house and its previous inhabitants, Mary becomes haunted by strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena. With each discovery, she uncovers a hidden world filled with secrets, betrayal, and a chilling presence that threatens her sanity. As Mary races against time to unravel the truth, she must confront her own fears and confront the malevolent force that lurks within the narrow walls of the house.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Haunting and Supernatural, Psychological Tension
