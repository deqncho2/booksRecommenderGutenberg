Synopsis: In "The Child at Home: The Principles of Filial Duty, Familiarly Illustrated," John S. C. Abbott delves into the complex dynamics of family relationships, exploring the fundamental principles of filial duty through captivating anecdotes and relatable examples. This thought-provoking work takes readers on a journey through the joys, challenges, and responsibilities of being a child within the home, shedding light on the importance of respect, gratitude, and love in fostering harmonious family connections. With its engaging storytelling and insightful reflections, Abbott's book offers a valuable guide for both parents and children, encouraging them to cultivate strong bonds and navigate the intricacies of family life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Parenting, Self-help

Themes: Filial duty, Family relationships, Respect, Gratitude, Love
