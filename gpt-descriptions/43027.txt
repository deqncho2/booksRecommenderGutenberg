Synopsis: 

In 'Getting Gold: A Gold-Mining Handbook for Practical Men', Joseph Colin Francis Johnson takes readers on an exhilarating journey into the world of gold mining. With a wealth of practical knowledge and firsthand experience, Johnson provides invaluable insights and techniques for those seeking to strike it rich in the goldfields. From the intricacies of prospecting to the art of extracting gold from the earth, this handbook is a treasure trove of information that will captivate both aspiring miners and curious adventurers alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Mining, Adventure

Themes: Gold mining, Prospecting, Practical techniques, Adventure, Exploration
