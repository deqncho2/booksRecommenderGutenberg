Synopsis: In 'The Best Man' by Harold MacGrath, a young man named John Sherwood finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and danger when he becomes the target of a powerful secret society. As he navigates through a world of hidden agendas, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, John must uncover the truth behind a mysterious manuscript that holds the key to his survival. With his life hanging in the balance, he must outwit his enemies and protect those he holds dear, all while trying to unravel the secrets of the enigmatic best man.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Political intrigue, Betrayal, Survival
