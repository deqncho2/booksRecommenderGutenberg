Synopsis: 

'Annals, Anecdotes and Legends: A Chronicle of Life Assurance' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of life assurance. Written by John Francis, a distinguished member of the Bank of England, this book delves into the annals of history, uncovering intriguing anecdotes and legendary tales surrounding the industry. From the early days of life assurance to the modern practices that shape our present, Francis weaves together a tapestry of stories that will leave readers spellbound. 

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: History, Finance, Insurance
