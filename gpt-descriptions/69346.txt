Synopsis: 
In 'Dishes made without meat', Mrs. Peel takes readers on a culinary journey through a world of delectable vegetarian recipes that are sure to tantalize the taste buds. From vibrant salads bursting with fresh flavors to hearty main courses that will leave you feeling satisfied, this cookbook offers a diverse range of meat-free dishes that are both nutritious and delicious. With easy-to-follow instructions and helpful tips, Mrs. Peel invites readers to explore the endless possibilities of vegetarian cooking, proving that meatless meals can be just as exciting and flavorful as their carnivorous counterparts.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink, Vegetarian Cuisine

Themes: Vegetarianism, Healthy Eating, Culinary Exploration
