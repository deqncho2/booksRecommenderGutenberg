Synopsis: In "Imaginary Interviews" by William Dean Howells, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a series of fictional interviews with famous historical figures. From Socrates to Shakespeare, Napoleon to Lincoln, and many more, Howells skillfully imagines conversations with these iconic personalities, shedding light on their thoughts, motivations, and the impact they had on the world. Through these engaging dialogues, the reader gains a deeper understanding of history and the complex individuals who shaped it.

Genres: Historical fiction, Literary fiction

Themes: History, Human nature, Influence
