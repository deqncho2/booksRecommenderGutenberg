Synopsis: 
'Lincoln & other poems' by Markham, Edwin is a collection of powerful and thought-provoking poems that delve into the life and legacy of Abraham Lincoln, as well as other significant historical figures and events. Through vivid imagery and lyrical language, Markham captures the essence of these individuals and moments, inviting readers to reflect on the complexities of human nature, the pursuit of justice, and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: Poetry, Historical, Biographical

Themes: Leadership, Justice, Legacy, Hope
