Synopsis:
In "John Law of Lauriston: Financier and Statesman, Founder of the Bank of France, Originator of the Mississippi Scheme, Etc.", A. W. Wiston-Glynn delves into the captivating life of John Law, a man whose financial genius and ambition shaped the course of history. From his humble beginnings to his rise as a prominent figure in the world of finance, Law's story is one of intrigue, scandal, and the pursuit of power. As the founder of the Bank of France and the mastermind behind the Mississippi Scheme, Law's innovative ideas and risky ventures forever changed the economic landscape of Europe. This meticulously researched biography offers a fascinating exploration of Law's life, shedding light on the complex man behind the legend.

Genres: Biography, History, Finance

Themes: Ambition, Power, Financial Innovation
