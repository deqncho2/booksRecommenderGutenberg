Synopsis: In 'The Firebrand' by S.R. Crockett, a young and ambitious journalist named John Burnet finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love in the midst of a turbulent Scottish rebellion. As he uncovers dark secrets and faces dangerous adversaries, John must navigate the treacherous waters of loyalty, betrayal, and personal sacrifice. Set against the backdrop of a nation on the brink of revolution, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Rebellion, Love and Sacrifice, Political Intrigue
