Synopsis: In 'The Golden Flood' by Edwin Lefevre, a young and ambitious stockbroker finds himself caught in the whirlwind of the financial world during the early 20th century. As he navigates the treacherous waters of Wall Street, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, power struggles, and unexpected alliances. With his career and personal life hanging in the balance, he must make difficult choices that will shape his future and reveal the true nature of those around him. This gripping tale of ambition, greed, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Ambition, Greed, Power, Morality
