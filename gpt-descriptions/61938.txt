Synopsis: In 'The Burston School Strike', Casey takes us on a captivating journey to a small English village in the early 20th century, where a seemingly ordinary school becomes the epicenter of a remarkable rebellion. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the students and their passionate teachers find themselves at the forefront of a battle for justice and equality. With unexpected twists and heartfelt moments, this gripping tale explores the power of unity and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Social justice

Themes: Activism, Education, Social inequality
