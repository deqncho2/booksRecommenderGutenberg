Synopsis: In 'Hayslope Grange: A Tale of the Civil War' by Leslie, Emma, a young woman named Clara finds herself caught in the midst of the Civil War, as her family's ancestral home becomes a refuge for wounded soldiers from both sides. As Clara navigates the complexities of war, she forms unexpected alliances and discovers the true meaning of courage and sacrifice. With danger lurking around every corner, Clara's journey will test her resilience and challenge her beliefs, ultimately leading her to uncover secrets that could change the course of the war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Romance

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Loyalty, Identity
