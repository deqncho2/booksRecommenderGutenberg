Synopsis:
In "With Frederick the Great: A Story of the Seven Years' War," George Alfred Henty takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the tumultuous period of the Seven Years' War. The story follows the adventures of a young Englishman named Harry, who finds himself caught up in the epic conflict as he joins the Prussian army under the legendary Frederick the Great. From thrilling battles and daring escapes to political intrigue and personal growth, Harry's experiences provide a captivating glimpse into the life of a soldier during this pivotal time in European history.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes:
Courage, Loyalty, Leadership, Friendship, Patriotism
