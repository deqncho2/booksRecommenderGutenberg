Synopsis: 
In 'Babylon, Volume 1' by Allen Grant, we are introduced to a mysterious and enigmatic character named Lucian, who possesses an uncanny ability to predict the rise and fall of stocks. Lucian, along with his partner, John, embarks on a thrilling journey to create a secret society that aims to revolutionize the world of finance. As they navigate the treacherous waters of Wall Street, they encounter powerful adversaries, unexpected alliances, and a web of deceit that threatens to unravel their plans. With high-stakes financial maneuvers, gripping suspense, and a touch of romance, 'Babylon, Volume 1' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets of this captivating world.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Finance

Themes: Power and corruption, Wealth and ambition, Deception and manipulation
