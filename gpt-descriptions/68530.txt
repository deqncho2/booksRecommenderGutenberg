Synopsis:
In 'Crystals' by A. E. H. Tutton, a young girl named Lucy discovers a hidden world within the crystals she collects. As she delves deeper into this enchanting realm, she encounters magical creatures, uncovers ancient secrets, and embarks on a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and determination. With each crystal she encounters, Lucy's understanding of herself and the world around her expands, leading her on a transformative journey that will leave readers captivated until the very end.

Genres:
Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes:
Discovery, Self-discovery, Magic, Courage
