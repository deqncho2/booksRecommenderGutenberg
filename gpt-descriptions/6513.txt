Synopsis: In 'The suppressed Gospels and Epistles of the original New Testament of Jesus the Christ, Volume 7, Barnabas', William Wake uncovers a hidden treasure of ancient texts that shed light on the life and teachings of Jesus Christ. This volume focuses on the Gospel of Barnabas, a captivating account that challenges traditional beliefs and offers a fresh perspective on the early Christian movement. As readers delve into the pages of this suppressed gospel, they will encounter a narrative filled with intrigue, mystery, and profound spiritual insights that have been kept hidden for centuries.

Genres: Religious literature, Historical fiction, Biblical studies

Themes: Alternative perspectives on Jesus Christ, Early Christian teachings, Hidden knowledge and secrets
