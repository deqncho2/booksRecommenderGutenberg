Synopsis: In 'How the Piano Came to Be' by Glover, Ellye Howell, embark on a captivating journey through time and discover the fascinating origins of one of the most beloved musical instruments in history. From ancient civilizations to the Renaissance, witness the evolution of the piano and the remarkable individuals who contributed to its creation. Uncover the secrets behind its intricate design and the profound impact it had on the world of music. This enchanting tale will leave you with a newfound appreciation for the instrument that has touched the hearts of millions.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Music, Biography

Themes: Innovation, Artistic Expression, Cultural Influence
