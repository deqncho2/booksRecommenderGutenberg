Synopsis: In "The Bride of Fort Edward: Founded on an Incident of the Revolution," Delia Salter Bacon takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. The story revolves around a young woman named Mary, who finds herself caught between loyalty to her family and her love for a British officer. As the war rages on, Mary's choices become increasingly difficult, leading her to face heart-wrenching decisions that will shape her destiny. Filled with romance, intrigue, and the backdrop of a nation at war, this historical novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and Loyalty, Conflict and Choices, The Impact of War
