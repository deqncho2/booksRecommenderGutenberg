Synopsis: In 'Rebellion' by Patterson, Joseph Medill, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious group of rebels emerges, challenging the established order. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the protagonist finds themselves caught in the middle of a dangerous power struggle. With secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Betrayal
