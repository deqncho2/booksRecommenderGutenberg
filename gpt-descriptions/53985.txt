Synopsis: 

In 'The Celtic Christianity of Cornwall: Divers Sketches and Studies' by Thomas Taylor, embark on a captivating journey through the rich history and mystical traditions of Cornwall. Delve into the enigmatic world of Celtic Christianity, as the author uncovers hidden tales, explores ancient ruins, and reveals the unique spiritual practices of this fascinating region. From the legends of King Arthur to the mysterious standing stones, Taylor's vivid descriptions and meticulous research bring Cornwall's Celtic heritage to life, leaving readers spellbound and eager to uncover more.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Religion

Themes: Celtic spirituality, Mythology, Cultural heritage
