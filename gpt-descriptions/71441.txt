Synopsis: In "Mating Center" by Frank Belknap Long, a young man finds himself in a mysterious and alluring world where love and desire take on a whole new meaning. Drawn into a hidden society, he must navigate a complex web of relationships and emotions, where the boundaries of love and lust blur. As he delves deeper into this captivating world, he uncovers shocking secrets that challenge his understanding of human connection and the true nature of desire.

Genres: Science Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love, Desire, Human Connection, Identity
