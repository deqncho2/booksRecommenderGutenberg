Synopsis: In 'The Little House in the Fairy Wood' by Ethel Cook Eliot, a young girl named Betty discovers a hidden house in the woods that seems to be straight out of a fairy tale. As she explores the magical dwelling, she encounters enchanting creatures and embarks on a whimsical adventure. But as Betty delves deeper into the secrets of the house, she realizes that there is more to this magical place than meets the eye. With each new discovery, Betty's world expands, and she learns valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Discovery
