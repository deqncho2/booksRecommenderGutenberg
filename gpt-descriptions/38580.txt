Synopsis: 
In 'True Heart/Mind' by Sengcan, a young monk embarks on a transformative journey to discover the true nature of his heart and mind. As he encounters various challenges and encounters, he must confront his deepest fears and desires, ultimately leading him to a profound realization. This captivating tale explores the depths of human consciousness and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: 
Philosophical fiction, spiritual literature, allegory

Themes: 
Self-discovery, mindfulness, the nature of reality, inner peace
