Synopsis: In 'Flower of the Dusk' by Reed, Myrtle, a young woman named Zara finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is forced into an arranged marriage. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, Zara discovers a hidden world of secrets and forbidden love that threatens to unravel everything she holds dear. With each twist and turn, she must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and challenge societal norms. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant and exotic setting, this captivating tale explores the power of love, the strength of the human spirit, and the lengths one will go to follow their heart.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Identity and Self-Discovery, Tradition and Rebellion
