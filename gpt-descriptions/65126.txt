Synopsis: In 'An Eel by the Tail' by Lang, Allen Kim, a young woman named Lily finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious eel-shaped pendant. As she delves deeper into the pendant's origins, she becomes embroiled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, Lily must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Identity
