Synopsis: In 'Doctor Izard' by Anna Katharine Green, a renowned physician is found dead under mysterious circumstances, leaving the small town in shock. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, and the residents become entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden motives. With the help of an astute detective, the truth behind the doctor's death slowly comes to light, revealing a shocking twist that no one saw coming.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Secrets, Justice
