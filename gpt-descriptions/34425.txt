Synopsis: In 'Johnstone of the Border' by Harold Bindloss, a young man named Johnstone finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger as he embarks on a journey to the rugged borderlands. Drawn by the allure of hidden treasure and the promise of adventure, Johnstone must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter enigmatic characters, and confront his own inner demons. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the border, he discovers that the line between friend and foe is blurred, and that the true test of his character lies in his ability to trust and survive.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Betrayal, Redemption
