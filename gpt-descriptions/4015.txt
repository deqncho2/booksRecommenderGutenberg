Synopsis: In 'The Hairy Ape' by Eugene O'Neill, we follow the journey of Yank, a brutish and powerful stoker on a transatlantic ocean liner. When he encounters the upper-class world, Yank's perception of himself and his place in society is shattered, leading him on a quest for identity and belonging. As he grapples with his own humanity and the forces that shape it, Yank's journey takes unexpected turns, challenging the audience to question the nature of power, class, and the human condition.

Genres: Drama, Expressionism

Themes: Identity, Class struggle, Alienation
