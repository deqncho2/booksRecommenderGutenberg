Synopsis: 

In "The History of Bannock County, Idaho," author A. C. Saunders takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and often overlooked history of this fascinating region. From the early Native American tribes that inhabited the land to the arrival of European settlers and the subsequent development of the county, Saunders weaves together a compelling narrative that brings the past to life. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, the author uncovers hidden tales of triumph, tragedy, and resilience, shedding light on the struggles and triumphs of the people who shaped Bannock County. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Local history, Biography

Themes: Frontier life, Cultural heritage, Community development
