Synopsis: In the bustling streets of eighteenth-century Williamsburg, a skilled bookbinder named Thomas K. Ford weaves his way through a world of secrets, intrigue, and craftsmanship. As he navigates the complexities of his trade, he becomes entangled in a web of unexpected events that will challenge his skills and test his loyalty. From the grand libraries of the wealthy to the hidden corners of the city, Ford's journey unveils a captivating tale of love, ambition, and the power of books.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Craftsmanship, Secrets, Ambition, Love, Loyalty
