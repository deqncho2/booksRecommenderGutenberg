Synopsis: In "The Ghost Breaker: A Novel Based Upon the Play," a young man finds himself embroiled in a mysterious and haunted castle while on a journey to save a damsel in distress. As he unravels the secrets of the castle's dark past, he must confront supernatural forces and face his own fears. With suspenseful twists and unexpected alliances, this thrilling novel takes readers on a gripping adventure through the realms of the supernatural.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Adventure

Themes: Supernatural, Fear, Redemption
