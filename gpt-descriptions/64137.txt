Synopsis: 

In 'History of Early Steamboat Navigation on the Missouri River, Volume 2 (of 2): Life and Adventures of Joseph La Barge', Hiram Martin Chittenden delves into the captivating life of Joseph La Barge, a prominent figure in the early steamboat navigation on the Missouri River. This volume takes readers on a thrilling journey through La Barge's remarkable experiences, from his early days as a deckhand to his rise as a successful steamboat captain. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the dangers and challenges faced by La Barge, including treacherous river conditions, encounters with Native American tribes, and the constant struggle to navigate the ever-changing river channels. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous historical research, Chittenden paints a vivid picture of the steamboat era and the indomitable spirit of those who braved the untamed Missouri River.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Exploration and adventure, Pioneering spirit, River navigation, Historical significance
