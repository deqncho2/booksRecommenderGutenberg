Synopsis: In 'The Jade God' by Alan Sullivan, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the fabled Jade God, he becomes entangled in a web of treachery, betrayal, and supernatural forces. With time running out, he must decipher cryptic clues and face unimaginable challenges to prevent a cataclysmic event that could reshape the world as we know it. This thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they are transported to exotic locations and immersed in a world of ancient artifacts and hidden powers.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Archaeology, Mythology, Power and Corruption
