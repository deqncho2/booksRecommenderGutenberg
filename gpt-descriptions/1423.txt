Synopsis: 
In 'No Thoroughfare', two young men, Walter Wilding and George Vendale, find themselves unexpectedly connected through a mysterious inheritance. As they embark on a journey to uncover the truth behind their shared past, they encounter a web of secrets, deception, and danger. Along the way, they encounter a captivating and enigmatic woman named Marguerite, whose presence adds an intriguing twist to their quest. With their lives at stake, Walter and George must navigate through a series of unexpected events and make choices that will determine their fate. Will they be able to unravel the truth and find their way to a brighter future?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Inheritance, Identity, Deception, Redemption
