Synopsis: 
In 'Newshound' by Stephen Marlowe, a seasoned journalist finds himself entangled in a web of corruption and danger when he stumbles upon a high-profile scandal. As he digs deeper into the story, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to expose powerful figures and put his own life at risk. With his career and reputation on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path of deceit, betrayal, and redemption, all while trying to uncover the truth that could change everything.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Corruption, Journalism, Power
