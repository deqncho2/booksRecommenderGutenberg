Synopsis: In 'What Great Men Have Said About Women: Ten Cent Pocket Series No. 77', readers are taken on a captivating journey through the profound and often controversial thoughts of influential men regarding women. From renowned philosophers to celebrated writers, this collection unveils a diverse range of perspectives, offering both praise and criticism. As the pages turn, readers will be intrigued by the timeless wisdom, surprising insights, and thought-provoking anecdotes shared by these great minds. Delve into this enlightening compilation and discover the complex and ever-evolving relationship between men and women.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Anthology

Themes: Gender dynamics, Feminism, Perception of women
