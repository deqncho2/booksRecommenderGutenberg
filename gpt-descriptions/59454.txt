Synopsis: In the vine country, a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and secrets, takes readers on a journey through the picturesque vineyards of France. When a young American woman inherits a vineyard from her estranged French grandmother, she embarks on a quest to uncover the truth about her family's past. As she delves deeper into the vine country's rich history, she becomes entangled in a web of scandal and intrigue that threatens to unravel everything she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Cultural heritage
