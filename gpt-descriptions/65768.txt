Synopsis: In 'Trouble on Sun-side' by Tenneshaw, S. M., a young engineer named Alex finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he is unexpectedly assigned to a top-secret project on a distant planet. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the project, Alex must navigate a treacherous landscape of political conspiracies, technological advancements, and personal betrayals. With his life on the line, he must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Betrayal, Conspiracy, Technological Advancements
