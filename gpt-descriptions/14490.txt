Synopsis: In 'A Daughter of To-Day', we follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Winifred, who defies societal expectations and embarks on a quest for independence and self-discovery. Set in the late 19th century, Winifred's unconventional choices challenge the norms of her time, leading her on a path filled with love, ambition, and unexpected obstacles. As she navigates through the complexities of relationships and societal pressures, Winifred's determination and resilience shine through, making her a truly inspiring protagonist.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Independence, Gender Roles, Love and Relationships, Societal Expectations
