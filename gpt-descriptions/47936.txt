Synopsis: 

'Jersey City and Its Historic Sites' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and hidden gems of Jersey City. From the bustling waterfront to the charming neighborhoods, author Harriet Phillips Eaton unveils the captivating stories behind the city's most iconic landmarks and lesser-known treasures. As readers delve into the pages, they will uncover the secrets of Jersey City's past, immersing themselves in tales of triumph, tragedy, and the enduring spirit of its people. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: Local history, Architecture, Cultural heritage
