Synopsis: 
In "Edgar Huntly; or, Memoirs of a Sleep-Walker," the protagonist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and psychological turmoil. Edgar Huntly, a young man living in rural Pennsylvania, becomes plagued by sleepwalking episodes that lead him to uncover dark secrets and confront his own inner demons. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic events surrounding his friend's death, Huntly's perception of reality becomes increasingly blurred, blurring the lines between dreams and waking life. With each sleepwalking episode, he uncovers more unsettling truths, pushing him to the brink of madness. Will Huntly be able to unravel the truth before it consumes him entirely?

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, mystery

Themes: Identity, guilt, the subconscious mind, the nature of reality, the power of the mind
