Synopsis: 

In "The Stock Exchange from Within," Van Antwerp takes readers on a captivating journey into the heart of the stock market, revealing the inner workings and secrets of this complex financial world. Through vivid anecdotes and insider knowledge, he unveils the hidden dynamics that drive the market's highs and lows, exposing the intense competition, greed, and manipulation that often go unnoticed by the average investor. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for storytelling, Van Antwerp offers a rare glimpse into the thrilling and sometimes treacherous realm of stock trading.

Genres: Non-fiction, Finance, Business

Themes: Market manipulation, Competition, Greed, Financial transparency
