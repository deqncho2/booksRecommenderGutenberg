Synopsis: In 'The Submarine Boys and the Middies: The Prize Detail at Annapolis', the Submarine Boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure as they join the Naval Academy's prize detail. Assigned to protect a valuable trophy, they soon discover that their mission is far from ordinary. As they navigate the challenges of life at Annapolis, they must also face unexpected dangers and uncover a mysterious plot that could jeopardize the security of the entire fleet. With their courage and resourcefulness put to the test, the Submarine Boys must rely on their training and teamwork to unravel the truth and protect their beloved Navy.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Friendship, Naval life
