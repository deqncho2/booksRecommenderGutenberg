Synopsis: In 'A Man of Honor' by George Cary Eggleston, a young lawyer named Philip Kent finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he takes on a case defending a notorious criminal. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel his own life and reputation. With his career and personal relationships at stake, Philip must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and uphold his sense of honor.

Genres: Mystery, Legal Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Justice and Morality, Loyalty and Betrayal
