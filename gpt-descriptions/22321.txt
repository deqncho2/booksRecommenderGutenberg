Synopsis: In the rugged and captivating Scottish Highlands, John Splendid, a poor gentleman, finds himself caught in the midst of the Little Wars of Lorn. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of clan rivalries and political turmoil, John must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With vivid descriptions of the breathtaking scenery and the fierce battles that unfold, this tale of adventure, love, and honor will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Conflict, Honor
