Synopsis: In the remote Scottish countryside, a mysterious figure known as the Black Dwarf lurks in the shadows, feared and misunderstood by the local villagers. When a young nobleman arrives in the area, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, revenge, and forbidden love, all while uncovering the truth behind the enigmatic Black Dwarf. As the story unfolds, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the dark and treacherous landscapes of Scotland, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Gothic Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Prejudice, Revenge, Love and Betrayal
