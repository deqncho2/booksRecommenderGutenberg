Synopsis: In 'Silver Cross' by Mary Johnston, a young woman named Judith embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about her family's mysterious past. As she delves deeper into the secrets of her ancestors, she becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and revenge. With each revelation, Judith's determination grows, leading her on a path that will test her courage and challenge everything she thought she knew about herself and her heritage.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and betrayal
