Synopsis: In 'The Boys of Old Monmouth: A Story of Washington's Campaign in New Jersey in 1778', author Everett T. Tomlinson takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the American Revolution. Set in New Jersey during 1778, the story follows a group of young boys who find themselves caught up in the midst of General George Washington's campaign against the British forces. As they navigate the dangers of war, these brave boys must learn to rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and loyalty to survive. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Tomlinson brings to life the untold stories of the unsung heroes of the American Revolution.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Resourcefulness, Coming of Age
