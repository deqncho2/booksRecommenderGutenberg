Synopsis: 
'Poems of West & East' by V. Sackville-West is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through contrasting landscapes and cultures. From the lush English countryside to the vibrant streets of the East, the poems explore themes of love, longing, and the complexities of human emotions. With vivid imagery and lyrical language, Sackville-West invites readers to immerse themselves in the beauty and diversity of the world, while delving into the depths of the human soul.

Genres: Poetry, Travel, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Love, Longing, Nature, Cultural Identity
