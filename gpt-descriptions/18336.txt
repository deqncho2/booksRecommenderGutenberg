Synopsis: In 'The Lighted Match' by Charles Neville Buck, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, igniting a chain of events that will challenge the community's values and test the limits of human nature. As the stranger's true intentions remain shrouded in secrecy, the townspeople find themselves drawn into a web of intrigue, deception, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, tensions rise, and the once peaceful town becomes a powder keg ready to explode. Will the lighted match bring destruction or illuminate the truth?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Morality, Trust, Power dynamics
