Synopsis: 
In the small town of Willowbrook, a mysterious stranger arrives on a stormy night, bringing with him an air of danger and temptation. As the night unfolds, secrets are revealed, desires are awakened, and the residents of Willowbrook find themselves caught in a web of seduction and betrayal. With each passing hour, the line between right and wrong becomes blurred, and the consequences of their choices become increasingly dire. Will they succumb to their darkest desires or find the strength to resist the allure of the night?

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Temptation, Betrayal, Morality
