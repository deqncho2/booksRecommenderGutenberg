Synopsis: 
Step into a world of wonder and fascination as you embark on a captivating journey through the pages of 'Ten Thousand Wonderful Things: Comprising whatever is marvellous and rare, curious, eccentric and extraordinary in all ages and nations'. This extraordinary collection of tales and anecdotes will transport you to the far corners of the globe, unveiling the most astonishing and peculiar aspects of human history and culture. From ancient civilizations to modern marvels, from bizarre inventions to extraordinary feats, this book is a treasure trove of captivating stories that will leave you in awe of the incredible diversity and ingenuity of the world we inhabit.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Curiosities

Themes: Wonder, Curiosity, Human Ingenuity
