Synopsis: Lena Rivers, a young and innocent girl, finds herself caught between two worlds after the death of her mother. Sent to live with her strict and unloving aunt, Lena longs for the warmth and affection she never received. When she discovers the truth about her parentage, Lena embarks on a journey to find her true identity and the love she deserves. Along the way, she encounters unexpected friendships, heartbreak, and the challenges of navigating societal expectations. Will Lena be able to overcome the obstacles in her path and find her place in the world?

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Identity, Love, Family, Social class
