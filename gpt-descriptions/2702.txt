Synopsis: In 'The Lion's Skin' by Sabatini, Rafael, a young nobleman finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and danger when he is mistaken for a notorious outlaw. Forced to assume the identity of the outlaw, he must navigate a treacherous world of deception and betrayal, all while trying to protect his true identity and uncover the truth behind the conspiracy that threatens his life.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Deception, Power, Loyalty
