Synopsis: 
In 'Confidence' by Henry James, a young American artist named Bernard Longueville finds himself captivated by a mysterious and alluring woman named Angela Vivian. As their paths cross in various European cities, Bernard becomes increasingly infatuated with Angela, but soon realizes that her charm and beauty may be hiding a darker secret. As he delves deeper into Angela's world, Bernard must confront his own desires and the consequences of his actions. With its intricate plot and complex characters, 'Confidence' is a gripping tale of love, deception, and the pursuit of personal identity.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and desire, Identity and self-discovery, Deception and manipulation
