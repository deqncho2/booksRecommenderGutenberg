Synopsis:
In the second volume of "The Life of the Rev. George Whitefield," author Luke Tyerman delves deeper into the extraordinary life of this influential preacher. As Whitefield's fame spreads across continents, his tireless efforts to spread the Gospel take him on a remarkable journey filled with triumphs, controversies, and personal struggles. From his electrifying sermons that captivate thousands to his encounters with prominent figures of his time, this biography offers a captivating exploration of a man who left an indelible mark on religious history.

Genres:
Biography, History, Religion

Themes:
Faith and spirituality, Evangelism, Influence and legacy
