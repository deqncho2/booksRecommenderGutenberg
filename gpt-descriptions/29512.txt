Synopsis: In 'Olive in Italy' by Dalton Moray, a young woman named Olive embarks on a life-changing journey to Italy, where she discovers a hidden family secret that takes her on a captivating adventure. As she delves deeper into her family's history, Olive finds herself entangled in a web of mystery, romance, and self-discovery. With picturesque descriptions of the Italian countryside and a cast of intriguing characters, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth alongside Olive.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Adventure
