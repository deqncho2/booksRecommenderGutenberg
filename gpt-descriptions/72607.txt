Synopsis: In "Uncle Wiggily's Silk Hat," Uncle Wiggily finds himself in a stylish yet practical adventure as he discovers the many uses of his tall silk hat. In another escapade, Uncle Wiggily brings home unexpected company without informing Nurse Jane, leading to a delightful and humorous situation. Lastly, Uncle Wiggily attempts to make salt water taffy, resulting in a sweet and entertaining endeavor. This collection of stories by Howard Roger Garis is filled with whimsical adventures that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Humor

Themes: Resourcefulness, Unexpected surprises, Creativity
