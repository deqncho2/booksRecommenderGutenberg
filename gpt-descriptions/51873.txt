Synopsis: In this captivating and informative guide, Tom Hood delves into the art of English versification, offering a compendious dictionary of rhymes and exploring classical measures. With a keen eye for humor, Hood also examines burlesque and comic verse, as well as the delightful realms of vers de société and song-writing. Whether you're a budding poet or simply a lover of language, this practical guide will entertain and enlighten, revealing the secrets behind the rhythmic beauty of English poetry.

Genres: Non-fiction, Poetry, Language

Themes: Versification, Rhyme, Classical Measures, Humor, Poetry Writing
