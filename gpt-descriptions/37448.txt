Synopsis: In 'Comet's Burial' by Raymond Z. Gallun, a group of scientists embarks on a daring mission to explore a mysterious comet hurtling towards Earth. As they venture into the unknown, they encounter unimaginable wonders and face the terrifying consequences of their own curiosity. With time running out, they must confront the ultimate question: what sacrifices are they willing to make to ensure the survival of humanity?

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Sacrifice
