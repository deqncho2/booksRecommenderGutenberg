Synopsis: 
'With the French in France and Salonika' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events of World War I, as experienced by the renowned American journalist Richard Harding Davis. From the trenches of France to the exotic landscapes of Salonika, Davis provides a vivid and firsthand account of the bravery, camaraderie, and sacrifices made by the French soldiers and their allies. Through his immersive storytelling, Davis offers a unique perspective on the war, shedding light on the human spirit amidst the chaos and highlighting the indomitable will to fight for freedom.

Genres: 
War memoir, Historical non-fiction, Journalism

Themes: 
War, Heroism, Camaraderie, Sacrifice
