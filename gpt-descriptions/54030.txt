Synopsis: In 'Ben o' Bill's, the Luddite: A Yorkshire Tale', readers are transported to the heart of Yorkshire during the Industrial Revolution. The story revolves around Ben, a young and curious lad, who finds himself caught up in the tumultuous world of the Luddites, a group of textile workers protesting against the rapid mechanization of their trade. As Ben navigates the dangerous and secretive world of the Luddites, he uncovers hidden truths and faces difficult choices that will shape his future and the fate of his community.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Resistance to change, Loyalty, Identity, Socioeconomic inequality
