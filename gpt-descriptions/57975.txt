Synopsis: In 'Excavating a Husband' by Ella Bell Wallis, a woman embarks on a captivating journey to uncover the truth about her husband's mysterious past. As she delves deeper into his secrets, she discovers shocking revelations that challenge everything she thought she knew about their relationship. With each revelation, the stakes rise, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery
