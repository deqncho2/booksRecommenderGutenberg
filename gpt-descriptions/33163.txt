Synopsis: In 'Joseph in the Snow, and The Clockmaker. In Three Volumes. Vol. II.', Auerbach takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two intriguing characters. Joseph, a young man with a mysterious past, finds himself in a snowy village where he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and unexpected encounters. Meanwhile, the clockmaker, a master craftsman with a hidden talent, grapples with the consequences of his creations. As their paths intertwine, the story unfolds with suspense, romance, and a touch of the supernatural, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth behind these enigmatic characters.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Fate, Secrets
