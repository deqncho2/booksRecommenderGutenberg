Synopsis: 
'Frontier Humor in Verse, Prose and Picture' by Cox, Palmer is a delightful collection of humorous tales and illustrations that transport readers to the wild and untamed American frontier. From mischievous pranks to absurd situations, this book offers a glimpse into the lighter side of life on the frontier, filled with eccentric characters and hilarious anecdotes. With its witty writing and charming illustrations, this collection is sure to leave readers laughing and longing for more.

Genres: 
Humor, Western, Illustrated

Themes: 
Frontier life, Comedy, Eccentricity
