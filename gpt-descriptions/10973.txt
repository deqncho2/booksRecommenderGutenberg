Synopsis: In "The Late Mrs. Null" by Frank R. Stockton, the seemingly ordinary life of Mr. and Mrs. Null takes an unexpected turn when Mrs. Null suddenly passes away. Left to navigate the complexities of life on his own, Mr. Null finds himself entangled in a series of comical and absurd situations. As he tries to make sense of his new reality, he discovers that his late wife's absence may not be as straightforward as it seems. With a cast of eccentric characters and unexpected twists, this delightful tale explores the humorous side of life's unexpected turns.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Absurdity of everyday life, Unexpected consequences
