Synopsis: 
'A Selection from the Comedies of Marivaux' is a collection of witty and charming plays by the renowned French playwright, Marivaux. This selection includes a variety of his most celebrated comedies, each filled with intricate plots, mistaken identities, and clever wordplay. From tales of love and courtship to stories of deception and social class, Marivaux's comedies explore the complexities of human relationships and the follies of society. With his sharp observations and delightful characters, Marivaux invites readers into a world of laughter, romance, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and courtship, Deception and disguise, Social class and hierarchy
