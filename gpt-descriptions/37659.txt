Synopsis: In Vondel's Lucifer, the celestial realm is thrown into chaos as Lucifer, the once beloved angel, rebels against God's authority. As the battle between good and evil unfolds, the fate of humanity hangs in the balance. With its poetic language and dramatic intensity, this epic play explores the depths of human ambition, the consequences of pride, and the eternal struggle between light and darkness.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Poetry

Themes: Rebellion, Pride, Good vs. Evil, Divine Justice
