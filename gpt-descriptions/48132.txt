Synopsis: In 'Essays Upon Heredity and Kindred Biological Problems: Authorised Translation', August Weismann delves into the fascinating world of heredity and explores the intricate mechanisms that shape life itself. Through a series of thought-provoking essays, Weismann challenges conventional beliefs and presents groundbreaking theories that shed light on the mysteries of inheritance. From the role of natural selection to the influence of environment, this captivating work takes readers on a journey of discovery, unraveling the secrets of life's blueprint.

Genres: Science, Biology, Non-fiction

Themes: Heredity, Evolution, Natural Selection, Genetics
