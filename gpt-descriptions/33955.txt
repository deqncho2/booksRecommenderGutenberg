Synopsis: In 'Remodeled Farmhouses', Mary Harrod Northend takes readers on a captivating journey through the transformation of old farmhouses into stunning modern homes. From quaint cottages to sprawling estates, this book showcases the remarkable stories of homeowners who have breathed new life into these historic structures. With vivid descriptions and beautiful photographs, Northend explores the challenges, triumphs, and creative solutions that arise when blending the charm of the past with the comforts of the present. Discover the secrets behind these remarkable renovations and be inspired by the timeless beauty that can be found within these remodeled farmhouses.

Genres: Home Improvement, Architecture, Interior Design

Themes: Preservation of History, Creativity in Design
