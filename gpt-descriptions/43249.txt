Synopsis: In 'Our Little Canadian Cousin', we follow the adventures of a young girl named Edith as she explores the beautiful landscapes and vibrant culture of Canada. From the bustling city of Montreal to the serene countryside, Edith encounters friendly locals, learns about Canadian history, and discovers the importance of family and friendship. As she immerses herself in the Canadian way of life, Edith's journey becomes a delightful exploration of the wonders that Canada has to offer.

Genres: Children's literature, Historical fiction, Cultural exploration

Themes: Cultural diversity, Family, Friendship, Adventure
