Synopsis: 

In "The Christian Church in These Islands before the Coming of Augustine: Three Lectures Delivered at St. Paul's in January 1894," George Forrest Browne takes readers on a captivating journey through the early history of Christianity in the British Isles. Drawing from historical records and archaeological findings, Browne sheds light on the fascinating and often overlooked period before the arrival of Augustine, the famous Christian missionary. Through three engaging lectures, he explores the struggles, triumphs, and unique characteristics of the Christian Church in these islands, offering a fresh perspective on a crucial era in religious history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Religious studies, Archaeology

Themes: Early Christianity, Religious conversion, Cultural exchange, Historical exploration
