Synopsis: In Volume 2 of 'The Persian Literature', Sadi takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Persian culture and history. This collection includes four timeless masterpieces: 'The Shah Nameh', an epic poem chronicling the heroic tales of Persian kings; 'The Rubaiyat', a collection of poignant and thought-provoking quatrains; 'The Divan', a compilation of lyrical and passionate poetry; and 'The Gulistan', a collection of insightful and moralistic stories. From grand battles to profound philosophical musings, Sadi's words transport readers to a world of beauty, wisdom, and enchantment.

Genres: Epic poetry, Quatrains, Poetry, Short stories

Themes: Heroism, Philosophy, Love, Morality
