Synopsis: 

In 'Americana Ebrietatis: The Favorite Tipple of our Forefathers and the Laws and Customs Relating Thereto', author Hewson L. Peeke delves into the fascinating world of the drinks that shaped America's history. From the early colonial period to the birth of the nation, this captivating book uncovers the beloved tipples that our forefathers indulged in, exploring their cultural significance and the laws surrounding them. With intriguing anecdotes and historical insights, Peeke takes readers on a journey through time, revealing the hidden stories behind the drinks that played a pivotal role in shaping American society.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Culinary history, Cultural history

Themes: American history, Alcohol and society, Cultural traditions
