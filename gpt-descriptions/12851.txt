Synopsis: 
'Folk Tales from the Russian' by De Blumenthal, Kalamatiano/ Xenophontovna, Verra is a captivating collection of enchanting Russian folk tales. From magical creatures and brave heroes to cunning witches and treacherous villains, these stories transport readers to a world filled with wonder and adventure. Each tale is beautifully woven with vivid imagery and rich cultural elements, offering a glimpse into the folklore and traditions of Russia. Whether it's a tale of love and sacrifice or a lesson in morality, these timeless stories are sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: 
Love and sacrifice, Morality, Russian culture and traditions
