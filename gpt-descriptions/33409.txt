Synopsis: In 'The Ranch Girls at Rainbow Lodge' by Margaret Vandercook, four adventurous sisters find themselves embarking on a thrilling summer adventure at a picturesque lodge in the mountains. As they explore the breathtaking wilderness, encounter mysterious guests, and uncover hidden secrets, the girls must rely on their wit, courage, and sisterly bond to navigate the challenges that lie ahead. With each passing day, their journey becomes more captivating, filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Sisterhood, Courage, Exploration, Secrets
