Synopsis: 

In 'Modern French Prisons: Bicêtre; St. Pélagie; St. Lazare; La Force; The Conciergerie; La Grande and La Petite Roquettes; Mazas; La Santé', Arthur Griffiths takes readers on a captivating journey through the infamous prisons of France. From the dark and mysterious walls of Bicêtre to the haunting cells of La Santé, this book delves into the history, architecture, and stories behind each prison. Griffiths uncovers the secrets and scandals that unfolded within these walls, shedding light on the lives of both prisoners and guards. With vivid descriptions and compelling narratives, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of French prisons.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, True Crime

Themes: Incarceration, Justice, Human Rights
