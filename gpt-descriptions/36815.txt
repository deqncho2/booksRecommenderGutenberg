Synopsis: 
In 'Advanced Toy Making for Schools', author David M. Mitchell takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of toy making, revealing the secrets behind creating intricate and imaginative toys. From designing and sculpting to painting and assembling, this book provides step-by-step instructions and expert tips to help readers master the art of toy making. With stunning photographs showcasing the finished creations, Mitchell's passion for the craft shines through, inspiring readers to unleash their creativity and bring their own unique toys to life.

Genres: 
Crafts, Education, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Creativity, Education, Artistic Expression
