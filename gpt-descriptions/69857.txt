Synopsis: 

In "Pride and Passion: Robert Burns, 1759-1796" by J. De Lancey Ferguson, readers are transported to the tumultuous life of one of Scotland's most beloved poets, Robert Burns. This captivating biography delves into the complex world of Burns, exploring his humble beginnings, his rise to literary fame, and the passionate relationships that shaped his life. From his enchanting poetry to his fiery love affairs, this book offers a vivid portrayal of a man whose words continue to resonate with readers centuries later. 

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Love and Desire, Artistic Expression, Social and Political Commentary
