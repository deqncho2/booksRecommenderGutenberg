Synopsis: 

Step back in time to the early 20th century and immerse yourself in the captivating world of the second National Conservation Congress. Held in Saint Paul in 1910, this historic event brings together a diverse group of individuals passionate about the preservation and sustainable use of our natural resources. From influential politicians and scientists to dedicated activists and concerned citizens, the Congress becomes a melting pot of ideas, debates, and groundbreaking proposals. As the attendees navigate through the complex web of environmental challenges, they strive to find innovative solutions that will shape the future of conservation in America. 

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, Environmental

Themes: Conservation, Sustainability, Environmentalism
