Synopsis: In 'The Orchard Secret: Arden Blake Mystery Series #1' by Cleo F. Garis, young Arden Blake finds herself caught up in a thrilling mystery when she discovers a hidden secret in her family's orchard. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Arden uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected connections that will keep readers guessing until the very end. With danger lurking around every corner, Arden must use her wit and courage to unravel the truth and protect those she loves.

Genres: Mystery, Young Adult, Adventure

Themes: Family secrets, Coming of age, Courage, Friendship
