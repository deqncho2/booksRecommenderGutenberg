Synopsis: In 'The Great Green Blight' by Robert Emmett McDowell, a small town is suddenly plagued by a mysterious and deadly green substance that begins to consume everything in its path. As the blight spreads, the townspeople must confront their deepest fears and secrets, while desperately searching for a way to stop the unstoppable. With a blend of suspense, horror, and psychological exploration, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Fear, Secrets, Survival
