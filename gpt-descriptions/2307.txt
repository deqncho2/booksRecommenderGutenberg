Synopsis: In 'The Depot Master' by Joseph Crosby Lincoln, a small-town railway station becomes the backdrop for a mysterious disappearance that sends shockwaves through the community. As the depot master, John Flint, delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a web of secrets and unexpected connections that challenge his perception of the people he thought he knew. With each revelation, the tension rises, and Flint finds himself caught in a race against time to unravel the truth before it consumes him and those around him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town dynamics, Identity and perception
