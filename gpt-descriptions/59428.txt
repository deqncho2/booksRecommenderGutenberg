Synopsis: In 'The Florentine Dagger: A Novel for Amateur Detectives' by Ben Hecht, a mysterious and ancient dagger becomes the center of a thrilling murder investigation in 1920s Chicago. When a wealthy art collector is found dead with the dagger in his chest, a group of amateur detectives, including a newspaper reporter, a professor, and a young socialite, join forces to unravel the secrets behind this puzzling crime. As they delve deeper into the case, they uncover a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden motives that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Historical Fiction

Themes: Murder, Intrigue, Secrets, Investigation
