Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Saltville, a mysterious stranger arrives with a peculiar proposition that challenges the very fabric of the community. As the townspeople grapple with their own desires and secrets, they find themselves entangled in a web of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, the line between right and wrong becomes increasingly blurred, leaving everyone to question their own moral compass. 'Salt ... or No Salt ...' is a gripping tale of intrigue and suspense that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Morality, Deception, Community
