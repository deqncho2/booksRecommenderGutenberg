Synopsis: In "Ball's Bluff: An Episode and Its Consequences to Some of Us," Charles Lawrence Peirson takes us on a gripping journey through a little-known event in American history. Set during the Civil War, the story follows a group of soldiers who find themselves caught in a treacherous battle on the banks of the Potomac River. As the chaos unfolds, friendships are tested, loyalties are questioned, and the consequences of one fateful decision reverberate through the lives of those involved. With vivid descriptions and a keen eye for detail, Peirson brings this forgotten episode to life, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Consequences of Actions, Survival
