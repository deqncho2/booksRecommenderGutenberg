Synopsis: In 'The Highflyers' by Clarence Budington Kelland, a group of ambitious young men set out to conquer the world of aviation in the early 20th century. As they navigate the challenges of building and flying their own planes, they find themselves entangled in a web of love, rivalry, and betrayal. With their dreams soaring high, they must confront their own limitations and make difficult choices that will shape their destinies forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Friendship, Love, Betrayal, Pursuit of Dreams
