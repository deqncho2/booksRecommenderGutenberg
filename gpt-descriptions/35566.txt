Synopsis: In "History of the Postage Stamps of the United States of America," John K. Tiffany takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of American postage stamps. From the earliest designs to the modern masterpieces, Tiffany delves into the stories behind each stamp, revealing the intricate details and hidden symbolism that make them truly remarkable. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, this book offers a unique perspective on the evolution of American culture and history through the lens of postage stamps.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Art

Themes: American history, Philately (stamp collecting), Cultural symbolism
