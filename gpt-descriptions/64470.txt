Synopsis: In 'Biblical Revision, its duties and conditions', Henry Alford delivers a thought-provoking sermon at St. Paul's Cathedral, exploring the crucial topic of revising the Bible. With eloquence and passion, Alford delves into the duties and conditions surrounding this monumental task, shedding light on the challenges and responsibilities faced by those entrusted with such a sacred endeavor. This sermon, delivered in 1870, offers a unique perspective on the importance of biblical revision and its implications for the faithful.

Genres: Sermon, Religious Literature

Themes: Biblical revision, Sacred responsibility, Faith, Interpretation
