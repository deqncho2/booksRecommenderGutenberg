Synopsis: 
In Search of the Castaways; Or, The Children of Captain Grant is an enthralling adventure novel that follows the journey of Lord Glenarvan and his crew as they embark on a perilous quest to find the missing Captain Grant. When a message in a bottle is discovered, containing the captain's last known coordinates, Glenarvan sets out on a daring expedition across the globe, encountering treacherous landscapes, hostile natives, and unexpected allies. With the captain's two children, Mary and Robert, by their side, the search becomes a race against time, filled with suspense, danger, and unexpected twists. Will they be able to unravel the mystery and rescue Captain Grant?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Travel

Themes: Exploration, Family bonds, Perseverance
