Synopsis: 

"The Jefferson-Lemen Compact: The Relations of Thomas Jefferson and James Lemen in the Exclusion of Slavery from Illinois and Northern Territory with Related Documents 1781-1818" explores the fascinating and little-known alliance between Thomas Jefferson and James Lemen in their efforts to prevent the spread of slavery in Illinois and the Northern Territory. Through a collection of carefully curated documents, author Willard C. MacNaul sheds light on the strategic collaboration and political maneuvering that took place during this critical period in American history. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political history, Biography

Themes: Slavery, Abolitionism, Political activism, Collaboration
