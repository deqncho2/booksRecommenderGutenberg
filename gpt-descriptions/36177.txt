Synopsis: In "Mr. Punch on Tour: The Humour of Travel at Home and Abroad," embark on a whimsical journey alongside the mischievous and iconic puppet, Mr. Punch. As he travels both near and far, this delightful collection of stories takes readers on a hilarious exploration of the joys and absurdities of travel. From comical encounters with eccentric characters to unexpected mishaps in exotic locations, each tale is filled with wit, charm, and a touch of whimsy. Whether it's a chaotic family vacation or a solo adventure gone awry, Mr. Punch's escapades will leave readers laughing out loud and longing for their own travel adventures.

Genres: Humor, Travel, Fiction

Themes: Adventure, Comedy, Cultural exploration
