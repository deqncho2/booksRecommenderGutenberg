Synopsis: In 'The Crux: A Novel' by Charlotte Perkins Gilman, we follow the life of a young woman named Vivian Lane who finds herself trapped in a suffocating marriage. As she struggles to break free from societal expectations and the confines of her own mind, Vivian embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment. Along the way, she encounters a mysterious group of women who challenge her beliefs and offer her a chance at liberation. With its gripping narrative and thought-provoking exploration of gender roles and personal freedom, 'The Crux' is a captivating tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Fiction, Feminist Literature, Psychological Drama

Themes: Gender roles, Marriage, Personal freedom, Self-discovery
