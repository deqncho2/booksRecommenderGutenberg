Synopsis: 
In 'Riding and Driving for Women' by Belle Beach, embark on a thrilling journey alongside a courageous young woman who defies societal norms to pursue her passion for equestrian sports. As she navigates the challenges of a male-dominated world, she discovers not only her own strength and determination but also the transformative power of following one's dreams. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding adventures, this captivating tale will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, rooting for our fearless heroine every step of the way.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Women's Fiction

Themes: Gender roles, Empowerment, Pursuit of passion
