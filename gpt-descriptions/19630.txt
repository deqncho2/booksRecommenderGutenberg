Synopsis: 
'Maha-bharata: The Epic of Ancient India Condensed into English Verse' is a captivating retelling of the legendary Indian epic, Maha-bharata, in a condensed and poetic form. This epic tale follows the lives of two warring families, the Pandavas and the Kauravas, as they navigate through love, loyalty, betrayal, and the ultimate battle for power. Filled with complex characters, divine interventions, and moral dilemmas, this condensed version brings to life the timeless story of honor, duty, and the consequences of one's actions.

Genres: Epic, Poetry, Mythology

Themes: Honor, Duty, Loyalty, Betrayal, Power, Morality
