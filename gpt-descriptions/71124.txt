Synopsis: In the small town of Oakwood, a mysterious pool filled with glowing radium water becomes the center of attention for the locals. As the townspeople become obsessed with the pool's magical properties, they soon discover that its allure comes at a dangerous cost. With secrets, betrayals, and unexpected consequences, "The Radium Pool" takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark side of human desire and the power of obsession.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Obsession, Power, Consequences
