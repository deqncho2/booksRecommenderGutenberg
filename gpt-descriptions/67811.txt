Synopsis: 
In "Roosevelt in the Kansas City Star: War-time Editorials," Theodore Roosevelt takes readers on a captivating journey through his thought-provoking editorials during a crucial period in American history. As an influential figure and editor of the Kansas City Star, Roosevelt fearlessly tackles pressing issues of the time, offering insightful perspectives on war, patriotism, and the role of the media. Through his eloquent and passionate writing, Roosevelt's editorials shed light on the challenges faced by a nation at war and the importance of unity and sacrifice. This collection of editorials provides a unique glimpse into the mind of one of America's most iconic leaders, showcasing his unwavering dedication to his country and his unwavering commitment to truth and justice.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Commentary

Themes: Patriotism, War, Media Influence, Leadership
