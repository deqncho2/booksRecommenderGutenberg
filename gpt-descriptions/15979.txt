Synopsis: In 'Miss Caprice' by Rathborne, St. George, a mysterious and captivating young woman named Caprice arrives in a small town, instantly captivating the attention of the locals. As she weaves her way into the lives of the townspeople, secrets begin to unravel, and the true nature of Miss Caprice becomes increasingly enigmatic. With her charm and enigmatic allure, she sets off a chain of events that will forever change the lives of those around her. Prepare to be enthralled by the captivating tale of Miss Caprice and the secrets she holds.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Identity, Deception, Love, Secrets
