Synopsis: In 'Goblin Tales of Lancashire' by James Bowker, prepare to be transported to a world where mischievous goblins roam the enchanting landscapes of Lancashire. These captivating tales will introduce you to a host of whimsical characters, from cunning tricksters to lovable rogues, as they embark on thrilling adventures filled with magic, humor, and unexpected twists. From ancient legends to modern-day encounters, these tales will leave you spellbound and eager to uncover the secrets of the goblin realm.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Mythical creatures, Trickery, Transformation
