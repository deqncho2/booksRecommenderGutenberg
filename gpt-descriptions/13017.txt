Synopsis: 
In 'Five Nights: A Novel' by Cross, Victoria, a renowned author, Emily finds herself trapped in a luxurious mansion for five consecutive nights. As the nights unfold, she discovers that the mansion holds dark secrets and is haunted by a malevolent presence. With each passing night, Emily must unravel the mysteries of the mansion and confront her deepest fears to survive. The suspense builds as the clock ticks, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, questioning what is real and what is merely a figment of Emily's imagination.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Haunting, Psychological suspense, Survival
