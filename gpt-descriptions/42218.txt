Synopsis: 
In 'Maori Religion and Mythology: Illustrated by Translations of Traditions, Karakia, &c., to Which Are Added Notes on Maori Tenure of Land', Edward Shortland delves into the captivating world of Maori religion and mythology. Through his translations of traditions and karakia (prayers), Shortland offers readers a fascinating glimpse into the spiritual beliefs and practices of the Maori people. Alongside these translations, he also provides insightful notes on Maori tenure of land, shedding light on the cultural significance of land ownership and its connection to their religious beliefs. This comprehensive exploration of Maori religion and mythology is sure to captivate readers with its rich cultural insights and intriguing narratives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Mythology, Anthropology

Themes: Maori culture, Religion, Mythology, Land ownership
