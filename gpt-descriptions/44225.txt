Synopsis: 
In 'The Glory of the Coming', Irvin S. Cobb takes readers on a captivating journey through the remarkable actions and endeavors of Americans during a pivotal year. From the bustling streets of New York City to the battlefields of Europe, Cobb's vivid storytelling brings to life the courage, resilience, and ingenuity of the American people. Through a series of captivating anecdotes and firsthand accounts, he paints a portrait of a nation united in the face of adversity, showcasing the indomitable spirit that defines the American character.

Genres: 
Historical, Non-fiction, War

Themes: 
Patriotism, Heroism, Unity
