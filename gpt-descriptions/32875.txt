Synopsis: 

'Ten Years' Captivity in the Mahdi's Camp 1882-1892' is a gripping memoir written by Josef Ohrwalder, recounting his harrowing experience of being held captive by the Mahdi's forces for a decade. Ohrwalder provides a firsthand account of the brutal conditions, the constant threat of violence, and the psychological toll of his captivity. As he navigates the complexities of survival and the ever-present hope for freedom, Ohrwalder's story takes readers on a captivating journey through the heart of Sudan during a tumultuous period in history.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Captivity, Cultural Clash
