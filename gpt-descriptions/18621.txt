Synopsis: In the small, picturesque town of Cooperstown, a mysterious event unfolds, shrouding the community in a web of secrets and intrigue. As the townspeople grapple with the consequences of this event, they are forced to confront their own hidden desires, fears, and pasts. With each passing day, the tension rises, and the truth becomes more elusive. Will the truth be uncovered, or will Cooperstown forever be haunted by its secrets?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption, Small-town dynamics
