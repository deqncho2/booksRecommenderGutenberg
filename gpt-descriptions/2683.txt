Synopsis: In 'Saint's Progress' by John Galsworthy, a young woman named Dinny Cherrell finds herself caught between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a scandalous love affair. As she navigates the complexities of societal expectations and personal freedom, Dinny must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Love and passion, societal expectations, personal freedom, moral dilemmas
