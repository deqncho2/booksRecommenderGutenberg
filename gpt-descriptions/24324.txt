Synopsis: In the bustling city of New York in 1906, a young woman named Evelyn discovers a mysterious device that allows her to hear the thoughts of those around her. As she delves deeper into this newfound power, she uncovers a web of secrets, scandals, and hidden desires that threaten to unravel the very fabric of society. With danger lurking at every corner, Evelyn must navigate a world of deception and intrigue, all while trying to maintain her own sanity and protect those she holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Power of secrets, Society's facade, Identity and self-discovery
