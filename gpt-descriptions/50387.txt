Synopsis: In 'The Texican' by Dane Coolidge, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small Texas town, stirring up trouble and challenging the status quo. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With danger lurking around every corner, the stranger's true identity and motives remain shrouded in mystery, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat until the very end.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Justice, Loyalty
