Synopsis: In 'Klytia: A Story of Heidelberg Castle', Hausrath takes us on a captivating journey through the enchanting Heidelberg Castle, where secrets, love, and tragedy intertwine. As the story unfolds, we are introduced to Klytia, a young woman with a mysterious past, who finds herself caught in a web of forbidden desires and dangerous alliances. Set against the backdrop of the majestic castle, this tale of passion and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth that lies hidden within the castle's walls.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Forbidden love, Betrayal, Secrets, Power dynamics
