Synopsis: In "A Modern Cinderella; Or, The Little Old Shoe, and Other Stories," Louisa May Alcott weaves a collection of enchanting tales that transport readers into a world of magic, love, and unexpected twists. From a young girl's journey to find her true identity to a mysterious old shoe that holds the key to a young woman's destiny, these stories will captivate readers with their timeless charm and heartwarming messages. Prepare to be swept away by Alcott's imaginative storytelling and discover the hidden wonders that lie within the ordinary.

Genres: Fiction, Fairy Tales, Romance

Themes: Identity, Love, Fate, Transformation
