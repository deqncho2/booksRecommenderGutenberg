Synopsis: In "The Princess of the School" by Angela Brazil, readers are introduced to Avice Carrington, a spirited and independent young girl who finds herself navigating the challenges of a prestigious boarding school. As Avice strives to fit in and make friends, she encounters a variety of colorful characters, faces unexpected obstacles, and discovers the true meaning of loyalty and friendship. With her determination and resilience, Avice embarks on a journey of self-discovery and growth, ultimately leaving a lasting impact on those around her.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Coming-of-Age, School Story

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Resilience, Self-Discovery
