Synopsis: 
Step back in time and immerse yourself in the captivating world of California's old missions. In 'Old Mission Stories of California' by Charles Franklin Carter, embark on a journey through the rich history and enchanting tales of the state's iconic missions. From the founding of the first mission to the struggles and triumphs of the Franciscan friars, this collection of stories will transport you to a bygone era filled with adventure, faith, and the enduring spirit of the Golden State.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: History, Religion, Exploration
