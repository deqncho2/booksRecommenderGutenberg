Synopsis: 
In a secluded Russian estate, a group of artists and intellectuals gather for a summer retreat. Among them is the aspiring playwright Konstantin, who is desperately seeking recognition for his work. When he presents his new play to the group, it is met with indifference and ridicule, leading to a series of events that unravel the complex relationships and unfulfilled desires of the characters. Love, jealousy, and the pursuit of artistic success intertwine in this poignant tale of unrequited dreams and the search for meaning.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Comedy

Themes: Artistic ambition, Unrequited love, Disillusionment, The nature of creativity
