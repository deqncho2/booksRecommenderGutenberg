Synopsis:
In 'The Wedge of Gold' by C. C. Goodwin, a mysterious artifact with unimaginable power is discovered, setting off a thrilling race against time and a dangerous pursuit across continents. As the protagonist delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the wedge of gold, they uncover a web of ancient legends, hidden societies, and treacherous alliances. With each step closer to the truth, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. Will they unlock the true potential of the wedge of gold, or will its power fall into the wrong hands, leading to catastrophic consequences?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Power and its consequences, Ancient legends and mythology, Loyalty and betrayal
