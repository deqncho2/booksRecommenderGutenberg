Synopsis: 
In 'Glimpses of Unfamiliar Japan: Second Series', Lafcadio Hearn takes readers on a captivating journey through the lesser-known aspects of Japan. Through his vivid descriptions and keen observations, Hearn delves into the mysterious world of Japanese folklore, exploring the supernatural and the spiritual. From encounters with ghostly apparitions to encounters with ancient rituals and customs, Hearn's narrative paints a mesmerizing portrait of a Japan that is both enchanting and haunting. As he immerses himself in the country's rich cultural tapestry, Hearn uncovers the hidden depths of Japan's traditions and beliefs, offering readers a unique glimpse into a world that is both familiar and unfamiliar.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Japanese folklore, Supernatural, Cultural traditions, Spirituality
