Synopsis: 
In 'The Light of the Western Stars', Madeline Hammond, a strong-willed and independent woman, finds herself in the rugged and lawless land of the Wild West. Seeking refuge from her troubled past, she becomes the owner of a ranch and must navigate the treacherous world of cattle rustlers, outlaws, and a mysterious cowboy named Gene Stewart. As danger looms and secrets unravel, Madeline must confront her own fears and desires, ultimately discovering the true meaning of love and redemption in the untamed frontier.

Genres: Western, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Redemption, Independence, Love, Survival
