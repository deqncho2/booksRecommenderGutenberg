Synopsis: In 'Mary Minds Her Business' by Weston, George, we are introduced to Mary, a seemingly ordinary woman with an extraordinary talent for uncovering secrets. When a mysterious client hires her to investigate a high-profile scandal, Mary's life takes an unexpected turn. As she delves deeper into the case, she discovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and danger that threatens to unravel everything she holds dear. With her quick wit and sharp instincts, Mary must navigate a treacherous world where nothing is as it seems, and trust is a luxury she can't afford. Will she be able to untangle the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Trust, Power
