Synopsis: 

In 'Native Life in South Africa: Before and Since the European War and the Boer Rebellion', Solomon Tshekisho Plaatje takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and complex world of South Africa before and after the European War and the Boer Rebellion. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Plaatje offers a unique perspective on the social, political, and cultural landscape of his homeland, shedding light on the struggles and triumphs of the native people.

Genres: Autobiography, Historical Non-fiction, Cultural Studies

Themes: Colonialism, Identity, Resistance, Cultural Preservation
