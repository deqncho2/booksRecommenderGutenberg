Synopsis: 

'Widger's Quotes and Images from The Red Lily by Anatole France: The French Immortals: Quotes and Images' is a captivating collection that offers a glimpse into the enchanting world of Anatole France's novel, The Red Lily. Through carefully selected quotes and accompanying images, this book invites readers to explore the intricate web of love, desire, and tragedy that unfolds within the pages of France's masterpiece. From the passionate encounters of its characters to the societal constraints that shape their destinies, this collection captures the essence of The Red Lily, leaving readers eager to delve deeper into the novel's secrets.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Constraints, Tragedy
