Synopsis: In 'Two Years Ago, Volume I' by Charles Kingsley, a mysterious event from the past resurfaces, setting off a chain of events that will challenge the characters' beliefs and reshape their lives. As secrets are unveiled and unexpected connections are made, the reader is drawn into a world of intrigue, romance, and moral dilemmas. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Kingsley takes us on a journey through the complexities of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Redemption, Morality, Identity, Love and Loss
