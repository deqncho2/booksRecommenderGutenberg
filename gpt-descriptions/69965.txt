Synopsis: In "Horrors of vaccination exposed and illustrated: $b Petition to the President to abolish compulsory vaccination in Army and Navy," Charles Michael Higgins delves into the controversial topic of compulsory vaccination in the military. Through a compelling narrative, the author exposes the dark side of this medical practice, shedding light on the potential dangers and consequences. With shocking revelations and thought-provoking arguments, Higgins presents a compelling case against mandatory vaccination, urging the President to reconsider its implementation. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Political

Themes: Medical ethics, Government control, Individual rights
