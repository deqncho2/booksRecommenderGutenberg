Synopsis: In 'Mayan Nomenclature', Charles P. Bowditch takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate world of Mayan names. Delving into the rich history and cultural significance of Mayan naming practices, Bowditch uncovers fascinating stories behind the names of individuals, places, and even deities. From deciphering the hidden meanings to unraveling the complex naming patterns, this book offers a mesmerizing exploration of the Mayan civilization through the lens of nomenclature. Prepare to be enthralled by the secrets and symbolism that lie within the names of the ancient Mayans.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Anthropology

Themes: Mayan civilization, Naming practices, Cultural significance
