Synopsis: In "A Survivor's Recollections of the Whitman Massacre," Matilda Sager shares her harrowing account of the infamous Whitman Massacre, a tragic event that forever changed the lives of the settlers in the Oregon Territory. As a survivor of the massacre, Matilda's recollections provide a gripping and intimate perspective on the brutal attack and its aftermath, shedding light on the resilience and strength of the human spirit in the face of unimaginable tragedy.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Biography

Themes: Survival, Tragedy, Resilience, Cultural clash
