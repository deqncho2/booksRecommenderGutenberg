Synopsis: 
In 'The Unknown; A Play in Three Acts', W. Somerset Maugham takes us on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of a renowned artist, Arnold Champion-Cheney. As the play unfolds, we are introduced to a series of enigmatic characters who are entangled in a web of secrets, desires, and hidden identities. With each act, the plot thickens, revealing unexpected twists and turns that keep the audience on the edge of their seats. As the truth gradually unravels, the characters are forced to confront their own fears and confront the unknown that lies within themselves.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Artistic Expression, Human Nature
