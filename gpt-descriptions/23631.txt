Synopsis: In the secluded Appalachian mountains of Hoho-hebee Falls, a group of moonshiners lead a clandestine operation to produce the finest and most sought-after moonshine in 1895. As the local law enforcement closes in on their operation, the moonshiners must navigate treacherous terrain, evade capture, and protect their secret recipe at all costs. Amidst the danger and suspense, unexpected alliances are formed, and the moonshiners find themselves facing moral dilemmas that challenge their loyalty and integrity. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Morality, Pursuit of Freedom
