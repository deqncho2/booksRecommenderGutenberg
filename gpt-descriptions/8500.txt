Synopsis:
In "Comrades," a group of friends reunite after years apart, but their reunion takes a dark turn as secrets and betrayals come to light.

In "Facing Death," a doctor is faced with a moral dilemma when he must decide whether to save the life of a patient or adhere to his own principles.

In "Pariah," a young woman struggles to find her place in society as she is shunned and ostracized by those around her.

In "Easter," a family gathers for the holiday, but tensions rise as long-held resentments and hidden desires bubble to the surface.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Psychological

Themes: Betrayal, Morality, Social alienation, Family dynamics
