Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Clevedon, a mysterious murder takes place, leaving the community in shock and suspicion. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unraveled, and the residents find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden motives. With each twist and turn, the truth becomes more elusive, and the stakes higher than ever. Will the truth be uncovered, or will Clevedon's dark secrets remain buried forever?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town secrets
