Synopsis: In 'People Minus X' by Raymond Z. Gallun, a brilliant scientist invents a machine that can transport people to parallel dimensions. However, when he accidentally sends himself to a world devoid of human life, he must navigate the eerie landscapes and encounter strange creatures in order to find a way back home. As he delves deeper into these alternate realities, he uncovers shocking secrets that challenge his understanding of existence itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Parallel dimensions, Existentialism, Exploration
