Synopsis: In 'The Great Painters' Gospel: Pictures Representing Scenes and Incidents in the Life of Our Lord Jesus Christ', Henry Turner Bailey takes readers on a visual journey through the life of Jesus Christ, showcasing stunning paintings that depict key moments and stories from the New Testament. From the birth of Jesus to his crucifixion and resurrection, this book offers a unique and captivating way to experience the life and teachings of the central figure of Christianity. With each painting accompanied by insightful commentary, readers will gain a deeper understanding of the significance and symbolism behind these masterpieces. 

Genres: Art, Religion, Biography

Themes: Christianity, Life of Jesus, Symbolism
