Synopsis: In 'Flood Tide' by Sara Ware Bassett, a young woman named Ruth Thorne finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she inherits a dilapidated mansion on the coast of Maine. As she delves into the secrets of the house and its previous owners, Ruth uncovers a hidden treasure map that leads her on a thrilling adventure. With the help of a charming stranger, she must navigate treacherous waters, face unexpected obstacles, and confront her own fears. Will Ruth unlock the secrets of the past and find the treasure she seeks, or will the flood tide of danger sweep her away?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Courage
