Synopsis: In "The Dock and the Scaffold: The Manchester Tragedy and the Cruise of the Jacknell," T. D. Sullivan takes readers on a gripping journey through the dark underbelly of Manchester's criminal underworld. Set against the backdrop of a tragic event, the story follows a group of individuals whose lives become intertwined in unexpected ways. As secrets are revealed and loyalties tested, the narrative unfolds with suspense and intrigue, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, Crime fiction, Mystery

Themes: Justice and retribution, Loyalty and betrayal, Social inequality
