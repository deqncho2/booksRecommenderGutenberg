Synopsis: In the second volume of "History of the Reformation in Europe in the Time of Calvin," Merle d'Aubigné delves deeper into the tumultuous era of religious reform. As the Reformation gains momentum, we witness the rise of influential figures like John Calvin and the profound impact they have on the religious and political landscape of Europe. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, d'Aubigné takes us on a captivating journey filled with religious conflicts, political intrigue, and the struggle for religious freedom. This volume offers a fascinating exploration of the Reformation's expansion and its profound consequences for European society.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Religious history

Themes: Religious reform, Political upheaval, Religious freedom, Influence of key figures, Impact on European society
