Synopsis: In 'The Riverman' by Stewart Edward White, a young man named Kent Denman embarks on a thrilling adventure to the remote wilderness of British Columbia. Drawn by the allure of the untamed river and the mysterious riverman who resides there, Kent finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of survival and self-discovery. As he navigates treacherous rapids, encounters enigmatic characters, and confronts his own inner demons, Kent's journey becomes a test of courage, resilience, and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Wilderness exploration, Self-discovery, Survival
