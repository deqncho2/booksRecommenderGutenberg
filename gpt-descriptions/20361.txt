Synopsis: In "The Voice of the Machines: An Introduction to the Twentieth Century" by Gerald Stanley Lee, embark on a captivating journey through the technological advancements and societal changes that defined the twentieth century. From the invention of the telephone to the rise of the internet, Lee explores the profound impact of machines on human civilization, delving into the intricate relationship between man and technology. Through thought-provoking anecdotes and insightful analysis, this book offers a compelling exploration of the transformative power of machines and their influence on the course of history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Technological advancements, Societal change, Human-machine interaction
