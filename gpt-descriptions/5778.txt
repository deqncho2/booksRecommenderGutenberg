Synopsis: In 'Try and Trust; Or, Abner Holden's Bound Boy' by Horatio Alger Jr., readers are introduced to Abner Holden, a hardworking and determined young boy who finds himself in a challenging situation. Abner's life takes an unexpected turn when he becomes a bound boy, working for a wealthy family. As he navigates the complexities of his new life, Abner must rely on his resilience and resourcefulness to overcome the obstacles that come his way. This captivating story explores themes of perseverance, self-reliance, and the power of trust.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Perseverance, Self-reliance, Trust
