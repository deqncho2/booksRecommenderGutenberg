Synopsis: 
In 'Strive and Succeed; or, The Progress of Walter Conrad' by Horatio Alger, Jr., we follow the journey of Walter Conrad, a young man who faces numerous challenges and setbacks as he strives to achieve success. From humble beginnings to unexpected opportunities, Walter's determination and resilience are put to the test as he navigates the complexities of the world around him. With each obstacle he encounters, Walter learns valuable lessons about hard work, integrity, and the true meaning of success. Will he be able to overcome the odds and achieve his dreams?

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman, Adventure

Themes: 
Perseverance, Social mobility, Moral development
