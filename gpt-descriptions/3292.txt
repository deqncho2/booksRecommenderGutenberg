Synopsis:
"The Clever Woman of the Family" follows the life of Rachel Curtis, a young woman with a sharp mind and a strong sense of duty. As she navigates the challenges of Victorian society, Rachel's intelligence and determination set her apart from her peers. With her quick wit and strategic thinking, she becomes a force to be reckoned with, making her mark on her family and the world around her. But as Rachel strives to find her place in a world that often underestimates her, she must confront the sacrifices and compromises that come with being a clever woman.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes:
Gender roles, Family dynamics, Intellectualism, Societal expectations
