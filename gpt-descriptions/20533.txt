Synopsis: 
In "Jill the Reckless," we follow the misadventures of Jill Mariner, a young woman who finds herself in a series of comical and unpredictable situations. After a failed engagement, Jill sets out to make a fresh start in New York, where she becomes entangled in a whirlwind of romantic entanglements, mistaken identities, and unexpected encounters. As she navigates the ups and downs of her new life, Jill must learn to trust her instincts and find the courage to follow her heart.

Genres: Comedy, Romance

Themes: Love and relationships, self-discovery, resilience
