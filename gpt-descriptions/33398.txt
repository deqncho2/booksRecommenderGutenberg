Synopsis: In 'A Select Collection of Old English Plays, Volume 14', readers will be transported to the vibrant world of Elizabethan theater, where they will encounter a captivating assortment of plays. From tales of love and betrayal to stories of revenge and redemption, this collection offers a diverse range of dramatic narratives that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Each play presents a unique and compelling exploration of human emotions and the complexities of human nature, making this volume a must-read for fans of classic English literature.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Revenge, Redemption, Human Nature
