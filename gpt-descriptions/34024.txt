Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a young and adventurous girl, finds herself on a thrilling journey when she stumbles upon a mysterious castaway named Nita. As Ruth and Nita form an unlikely friendship, they uncover secrets and face unexpected challenges at Lighthouse Point. With danger lurking around every corner, Ruth must rely on her wit and determination to unravel the mysteries of the island and protect her newfound friend. Will Ruth be able to navigate the treacherous waters and find the truth hidden within Lighthouse Point?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Discovery
