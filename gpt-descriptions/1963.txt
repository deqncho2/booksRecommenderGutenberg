Synopsis: 
In 'The Confession' by Mary Roberts Rinehart, a young woman named Louise is haunted by a dark secret from her past. When she receives an unexpected visitor who claims to know the truth, Louise's world is turned upside down. As she grapples with the consequences of her actions, she must confront her own guilt and make a difficult decision that could change her life forever. With suspenseful twists and a gripping narrative, 'The Confession' delves into the complexities of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Guilt, Secrets, Redemption
