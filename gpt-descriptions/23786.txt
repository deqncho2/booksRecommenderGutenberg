Synopsis: In 'A Little Girl in Old Boston', we follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Emily as she navigates the bustling streets of 19th century Boston. From her encounters with historical figures to her adventures in the city's vibrant neighborhoods, Emily's story is filled with charm, mystery, and unexpected twists. As she immerses herself in the rich culture and traditions of the era, Emily discovers secrets that will forever change her perception of the world around her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Family, History, Adventure
