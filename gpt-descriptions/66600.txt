Synopsis: In 'Short Story-Writing: An Art or a Trade?' by Fagin, N. Bryllion, readers are taken on a captivating journey into the world of short story writing. Through the eyes of the protagonist, we witness the struggles and triumphs of a writer striving to find their place in the literary world. As the story unfolds, unexpected challenges and surprising revelations test the protagonist's determination and creativity. This thought-provoking tale explores the delicate balance between artistic expression and commercial success, leaving readers pondering the true nature of the craft.

Genres: Fiction, Literary Fiction, Writing

Themes: Artistic expression, Commercialism, Identity, Creativity
