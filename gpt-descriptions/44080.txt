Synopsis:
In 'Madonna Mary' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman named Mary finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and duty, Mary's journey takes her through unexpected twists and turns, challenging her to make difficult choices that will shape her future. With vivid characters and a captivating narrative, 'Madonna Mary' explores the complexities of womanhood and the power of self-discovery.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Love and sacrifice, societal expectations, self-discovery
