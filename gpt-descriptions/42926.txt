Synopsis: In 'The Last of Their Race' by Annie S. Swan, a mysterious family secret is unearthed when a young woman inherits an ancestral home in the Scottish Highlands. As she delves into her family's history, she discovers a hidden world filled with ancient traditions, forbidden love, and a curse that has haunted her bloodline for generations. With the help of a local historian, she must unravel the truth before it's too late, and the last remnants of her family's legacy are lost forever.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Heritage and ancestry, Forbidden love, Supernatural elements
