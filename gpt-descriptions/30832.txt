Synopsis: In 'Nor Iron Bars a Cage...' by Randall Garrett, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Endlich invents a groundbreaking device that allows him to travel through time. However, when he tests his invention, something goes terribly wrong, and he finds himself trapped in a mysterious and dangerous future world. As he navigates this unfamiliar landscape, Dr. Endlich must confront unexpected challenges and make difficult choices in order to find a way back home.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Adventure

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Survival, Consequences of scientific advancements
