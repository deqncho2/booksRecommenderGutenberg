Synopsis: 

'Japhet in Search of a Father' follows the journey of a young boy named Japhet, who sets out on a quest to find his missing father. Along the way, he encounters a variety of colorful characters and experiences numerous adventures that test his courage and determination. As Japhet navigates through the challenges of his search, he learns valuable lessons about family, identity, and the true meaning of fatherhood. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman

Themes: Family, Identity, Fatherhood
