Synopsis: In 'The Putnam Hall Rebellion; or, The Rival Runaways', a group of adventurous boys find themselves caught up in a thrilling rebellion against the strict rules of their boarding school. When two rival students decide to run away, they embark on a daring journey filled with unexpected challenges and surprising alliances. As they navigate through the wilderness, they must confront their own fears and learn the true meaning of friendship and loyalty. Will they succeed in their rebellion or will they be caught and face the consequences?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Rebellion, Friendship, Loyalty
