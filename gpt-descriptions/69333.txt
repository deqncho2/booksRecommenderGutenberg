Synopsis: In '1812: A Tale of Cape Cod' by Michael Fitzgerald, a young boy named Seth finds himself caught in the midst of the War of 1812, as British forces threaten to invade his beloved Cape Cod. As Seth embarks on a dangerous journey to warn his community, he encounters unexpected allies and faces numerous challenges that test his courage and resilience. Set against the backdrop of a tumultuous historical period, this captivating tale explores the power of friendship, the strength of community, and the indomitable spirit of a young hero.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Patriotism, Community
