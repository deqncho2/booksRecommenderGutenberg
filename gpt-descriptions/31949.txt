Synopsis: In 'The Bartlett Mystery' by Tracy, Louis, a renowned detective is called upon to solve a perplexing case involving a wealthy family with a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of lies, betrayal, and hidden motives that threaten to unravel the entire community. With each clue leading to more questions than answers, the detective must navigate through a maze of deceit to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Family dynamics
