Synopsis: In "For the Honor of the School: A Story of School Life and Interscholastic Sport," author Ralph Henry Barbour takes readers on a thrilling journey through the ups and downs of school life and the intense world of interscholastic sports. Follow the protagonist as he navigates the challenges of friendship, rivalry, and personal growth, all while striving to bring honor to his beloved school. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Sports Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Competition, Perseverance, Identity
