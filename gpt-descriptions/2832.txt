Synopsis: 
In 'Myth, Ritual and Religion, Vol. 1 (of 2)', Andrew Lang delves into the fascinating world of mythology, exploring the ancient tales and rituals that have shaped human civilization. From the myths of ancient Greece to the legends of the Native Americans, Lang uncovers the common threads that connect these diverse cultures and their beliefs. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for storytelling, he takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins of religious practices and the significance of rituals in different societies. As he unravels the mysteries of the past, Lang invites readers to question their own beliefs and ponder the enduring power of myth and religion.

Genres: Mythology, Anthropology, History

Themes: Mythology, Religion, Ritual, Cultural Diversity
