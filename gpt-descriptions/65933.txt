Synopsis: 
In 'All Wool' by W.C. Tuttle, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives with a secret that threatens to unravel the fabric of their community. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of deception and betrayal, they must confront their own hidden desires and face the consequences of their actions. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Moral ambiguity
