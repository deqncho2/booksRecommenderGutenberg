Synopsis: In a world where alcohol has been banned, two friends embark on a hilarious and daring adventure to keep the spirit of the old English inn alive. With a pub on wheels, they travel the countryside, spreading mirth, challenging authority, and defending the importance of tradition. Along the way, they encounter eccentric characters, engage in witty debates, and find themselves caught up in a web of political intrigue. Will they succeed in their mission to bring back the joy of the tavern, or will they be crushed by the forces of prohibition?

Genres: Satire, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Tradition, Freedom, Resistance to Authority
