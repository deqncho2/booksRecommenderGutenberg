Synopsis: 

In 'Mere Mortals: Medico-historical essays', author C. MacLaurin takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of medical history. From ancient civilizations to modern times, this collection of essays delves into the lives of remarkable individuals who have shaped the field of medicine. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, MacLaurin uncovers the triumphs, struggles, and groundbreaking discoveries that have shaped our understanding of the human body and the practice of medicine. From the mysterious practices of ancient healers to the revolutionary breakthroughs of modern medicine, 'Mere Mortals' offers a captivating exploration of the human quest for health and healing.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Medicine

Themes: Medical history, Human body, Healing, Scientific discovery
