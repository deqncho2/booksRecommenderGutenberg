Synopsis: 

In 'Nitro-Explosives: A Practical Treatise', Sanford, P. Gerald takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of explosive materials. From the very first page, this captivating book delves into the intricate science behind nitro-explosives, exploring their creation, properties, and potential applications. With vivid descriptions and real-life examples, the author unveils the secrets of these powerful substances, offering a fascinating insight into their history, development, and the dangers they pose. As readers delve deeper into the pages, they will be enthralled by the author's meticulous attention to detail and the gripping anecdotes that bring the subject matter to life. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Technology

Themes: Chemistry, Engineering, History, Safety
