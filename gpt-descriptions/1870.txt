Synopsis: 
In 'Reginald in Russia, and Other Sketches', Saki takes readers on a delightful journey through a collection of witty and satirical short stories. From the misadventures of the mischievous Reginald in Russia to the absurdities of high society and the peculiarities of human nature, each sketch offers a humorous and insightful glimpse into the world of Edwardian England. With his signature blend of sharp wit and clever observations, Saki invites readers to laugh, ponder, and question the idiosyncrasies of society and the foibles of its inhabitants.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Short Stories

Themes: Social satire, Irony, Critique of societal norms
