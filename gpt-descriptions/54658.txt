Synopsis: 
"The Agony Column of the 'Times' 1800-1870" takes readers on a captivating journey through the pages of one of the most influential newspapers of the 19th century. This collection of anonymous letters, known as the "agony column," reveals the hidden desires, secrets, and scandals of the era. From tales of forbidden love to mysterious disappearances, the column becomes a window into the lives of ordinary people and the extraordinary events that shaped their world. As readers delve into the captivating narratives, they are transported to a time of societal upheaval, where the power of the written word holds the potential to change lives forever.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Epistolary literature

Themes: Love and desire, Secrets and scandals, Social change, Power of the written word
