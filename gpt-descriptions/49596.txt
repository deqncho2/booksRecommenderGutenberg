Synopsis: In "The Sorrows of Belgium: A Play in Six Scenes," Andreyev takes us on a gripping journey through the tumultuous history of Belgium. Set against the backdrop of political unrest and social upheaval, the play follows a diverse cast of characters as they navigate love, betrayal, and the search for identity in a nation on the brink of change. From the passionate revolutionary to the disillusioned aristocrat, each character's story intertwines to create a powerful exploration of the human condition and the complexities of national identity.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Political Theatre

Themes: Nationalism, Revolution, Identity, Betrayal, Social Injustice
