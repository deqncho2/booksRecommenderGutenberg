Synopsis: In "Through a Pocket Lens" by Henry Scherren, a young photographer stumbles upon a mysterious antique camera that has the power to capture glimpses of the past. As he delves deeper into the camera's history, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden treasures, leading him on a thrilling adventure across time and continents. With each photograph he takes, the photographer uncovers a new piece of the puzzle, but also risks altering the course of history itself. As he races against time, he must navigate the blurred lines between reality and illusion, and ultimately discover the true power of the pocket lens.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Time travel, Secrets and hidden treasures, Perception and reality
