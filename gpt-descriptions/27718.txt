Synopsis: In 'Parables from Flowers' by Gertrude P. Dyer, readers are transported into a world where flowers come to life and share their wisdom through enchanting parables. Each flower has a unique story to tell, revealing profound insights about love, resilience, and the beauty of the natural world. As readers delve into this captivating collection, they will be captivated by the transformative power of nature and the timeless lessons it holds.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Inspirational

Themes: Nature's wisdom, Love, Resilience
