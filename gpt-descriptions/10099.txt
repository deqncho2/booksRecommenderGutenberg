Synopsis: 
'Towards the Goal' by Ward, Humphry, Mrs. is a captivating novel that follows the journey of a young woman named Lucy, who finds herself torn between duty and desire. Set in the picturesque English countryside, Lucy's life takes an unexpected turn when she encounters a mysterious stranger who challenges her beliefs and pushes her to question the path she has always followed. As she embarks on a quest for self-discovery, Lucy must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and the pursuit of personal fulfillment. With its richly drawn characters and beautifully evoked settings, this novel is a compelling exploration of the choices we make and the transformative power of following our true passions.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Duty, Personal fulfillment
