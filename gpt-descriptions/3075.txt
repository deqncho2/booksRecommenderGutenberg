Synopsis: In 'The Return' by Walter De la Mare, a young man named Richard is haunted by a recurring dream that leads him on a mysterious journey. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic world of his dreams, Richard becomes entangled in a web of secrets and supernatural occurrences. With each step he takes, the line between reality and fantasy blurs, leaving him questioning his own sanity. As Richard unravels the truth behind his dreams, he discovers a shocking revelation that will forever change his perception of the world around him.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Dreams and Reality, Identity and Perception, Mystery and Intrigue
