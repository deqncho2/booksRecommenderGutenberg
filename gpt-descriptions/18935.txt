Synopsis: In "Vanity, All Is Vanity: A Lecture on Tobacco and its effects," the enigmatic lecturer takes the audience on a captivating journey through the dark and alluring world of tobacco. With a mix of historical anecdotes, scientific revelations, and personal experiences, the lecturer delves into the addictive nature of tobacco and its profound impact on individuals and society. As the lecture unfolds, the audience is left questioning their own relationship with this seemingly harmless vice, and the consequences it may hold. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Lecture, Psychology

Themes: Addiction, Self-deception, Societal influence
