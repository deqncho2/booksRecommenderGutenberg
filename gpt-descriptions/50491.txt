Synopsis: In 'Darnley; or, The Field of the Cloth of Gold', James, G. P. R. takes us on a thrilling journey through the opulent courts of Europe, where political intrigue, forbidden love, and treacherous alliances intertwine. Set against the backdrop of the extravagant Field of the Cloth of Gold, this gripping tale follows the fate of Darnley, a young nobleman caught in a web of power struggles and dangerous secrets. As he navigates the treacherous waters of courtly life, Darnley must make choices that will not only determine his own destiny but also shape the course of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Betrayal, Loyalty and Honor
