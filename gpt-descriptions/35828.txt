Synopsis: In 'By Wit of Woman' by Arthur W. Marchmont, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she becomes the target of a sinister plot. With her quick wit and resourcefulness, Evelyn must navigate through a world of secrets and deception, relying on her intelligence and courage to uncover the truth and protect herself from those who wish her harm.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Female empowerment, Deception, Survival
