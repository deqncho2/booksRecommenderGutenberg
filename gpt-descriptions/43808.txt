Synopsis: In 'Mary's Little Lamb: A Picture Guessing Story for Little Children', author Edith Francis Foster takes young readers on an exciting adventure as they follow Mary and her beloved lamb through a series of captivating illustrations. Each page presents a delightful scene that encourages children to guess what will happen next, fostering their imagination and critical thinking skills. With vibrant and engaging artwork, this interactive picture book is sure to captivate young minds and keep them eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Problem-solving
