Synopsis: 

In 'Reminiscences of the Military Life and Sufferings of Col. Timothy Bigelow', author Charles Hersey takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of Col. Timothy Bigelow, a commander in the Continental Army during the War of the Revolution. Through vivid and detailed accounts, Hersey paints a vivid picture of the challenges, sacrifices, and triumphs that Bigelow and his regiment faced on the battlefield. From the intense battles to the harsh conditions endured by the soldiers, this book offers a gripping and personal perspective on the realities of war.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Leadership, War experiences
