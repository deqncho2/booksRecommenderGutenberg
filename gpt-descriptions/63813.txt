Synopsis: 
In "In His Image" by Bryce Walton, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Paul Cameron makes a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but also the fate of the entire world. With time running out, Dr. Cameron must navigate a treacherous web of deception and betrayal to protect his discovery and ensure the survival of humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics of scientific advancement, Human nature and identity, Consequences of playing god
