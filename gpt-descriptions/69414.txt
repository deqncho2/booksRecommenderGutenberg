Synopsis: 
"To Arms for Liberty: A Pageant of the War for Schools and Societies" by Catherine T. Bryce is a captivating historical drama that takes readers on a journey through the tumultuous times of war. Set against the backdrop of a nation divided, this pageant brings to life the struggles, sacrifices, and triumphs of those who fought for freedom. Through vivid storytelling and compelling characters, the author paints a vivid picture of the human spirit and the power of unity in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Educational

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Unity, Freedom
