Synopsis: 
Step into the enchanting world of Japanese folklore with 'Japanese Fairy World: Stories from the Wonder-Lore of Japan' by William Elliot Griffis. This captivating collection of tales takes readers on a journey through ancient Japan, where mythical creatures, mischievous spirits, and courageous heroes come to life. From the heartwarming story of a humble fisherman's encounter with a magical turtle to the thrilling adventures of a brave warrior battling supernatural forces, each tale is filled with wonder, adventure, and valuable life lessons. Delve into the rich tapestry of Japanese culture and immerse yourself in the captivating realm of Japanese fairy tales.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: 
Mythology, Morality, Courage, Supernatural
