Synopsis: In 'The Handbook of Conundrums' by Edith B. Ordway, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of perplexing puzzles, enigmatic riddles, and mind-bending conundrums. From ancient mysteries to modern-day brainteasers, this book challenges the reader's intellect and problem-solving skills, offering a delightful blend of entertainment and mental stimulation. As the pages turn, the reader becomes engrossed in a world of intricate puzzles, each one more puzzling than the last. With every solution discovered, a new layer of mystery unfolds, leaving the reader eager to unravel the next enigma. 

Genres: Puzzle, Mystery, Non-fiction

Themes: Intellectual challenge, Problem-solving, Curiosity
