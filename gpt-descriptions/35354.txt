Synopsis: In "Moral Theology: A Complete Course Based on St. Thomas Aquinas and the Best Modern Authorities," authors Callan and McHugh delve into the complex world of moral theology, offering a comprehensive exploration of ethical principles and their application in modern society. Through a blend of traditional teachings and contemporary insights, this enlightening course takes readers on a thought-provoking journey, challenging them to examine their own moral beliefs and guiding them towards a deeper understanding of virtue, conscience, and the pursuit of the good life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Theology, Philosophy

Themes: Moral principles, Ethics, Virtue, Conscience, Human nature
