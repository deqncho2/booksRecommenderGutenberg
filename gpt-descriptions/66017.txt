Synopsis: 
In "Jean Craig, Graduate Nurse" by Kay Lyttleton, follow the captivating journey of Jean Craig as she embarks on her career as a nurse. From the bustling city hospital to the tranquil countryside, Jean encounters a myriad of challenges and triumphs that test her resilience and determination. As she navigates the complexities of medical emergencies, personal relationships, and the demands of her profession, Jean's unwavering passion for nursing shines through. With each patient she cares for, Jean learns valuable lessons about compassion, sacrifice, and the true meaning of healing. Will Jean find her place in the world of medicine and discover her own path to happiness?

Genres: Fiction, Medical Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Nursing, Personal Growth, Resilience, Compassion
