Synopsis: 

In 'The Great Frozen Sea: A Personal Narrative of the Voyage of the "Alert"', Sir Albert Hastings Markham takes us on an extraordinary journey through the treacherous Arctic waters. Join him aboard the "Alert" as he recounts the gripping tale of their harrowing expedition, battling against the unforgiving elements and facing unimaginable challenges. From encounters with polar bears to the awe-inspiring beauty of the frozen landscape, this personal narrative will transport you to a world of danger and wonder, where the human spirit is tested to its limits.

Genres: Adventure, Memoir, Exploration

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Human resilience
