Synopsis: In 'A Message to Garcia: Being a Preachment', Elbert Hubbard presents a captivating tale that explores the power of determination and the importance of taking initiative. Set in the backdrop of the Spanish-American War, the story follows a young soldier named Rowan who is entrusted with a seemingly impossible mission: to deliver a message to General Garcia, deep in the heart of enemy territory. With no questions asked, Rowan embarks on a perilous journey, facing numerous obstacles and challenges along the way. This inspiring narrative serves as a timeless reminder of the value of perseverance, resourcefulness, and unwavering commitment to duty.

Genres: Inspirational, Historical Fiction

Themes: Determination, Initiative, Duty, Perseverance
