Synopsis: In "The Night-Side of Nature; Or, Ghosts and Ghost-Seers," Catherine Crowe delves into the mysterious world of the supernatural, exploring the chilling tales of ghosts and the individuals who have encountered them. From haunted houses to spectral apparitions, this collection of true stories will captivate readers with its spine-tingling accounts of the paranormal. As Crowe investigates the nature of these ghostly encounters, she uncovers the hidden depths of human perception and the enduring fascination with the unknown.

Genres: Non-fiction, Paranormal, Occult

Themes: Supernatural, Perception, Fear, Mystery
