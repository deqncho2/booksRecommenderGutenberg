Synopsis: 
'Lovey Mary' by Alice Caldwell Hegan Rice is a heartwarming and poignant story that follows the journey of a young girl named Mary, who is abandoned by her mother and left to navigate the harsh realities of life in an orphanage. Despite the challenges she faces, Mary's resilience and determination shine through as she forms unexpected friendships and discovers the power of love and compassion. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century America, this captivating tale explores the transformative power of kindness and the enduring strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Resilience, Friendship, Compassion, Overcoming Adversity
