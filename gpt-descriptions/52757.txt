Synopsis: In 'The Proportions of Truth' by Edward Hoare, a renowned mathematician stumbles upon a mysterious equation that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of the universe. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: The pursuit of knowledge, the power of truth, the consequences of discovery
