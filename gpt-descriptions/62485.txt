Synopsis: 
'Edgings: crocheted, tatted, hair pin lace' by American Thread Company is a captivating guide that unveils the art of creating intricate and beautiful edgings through various techniques such as crocheting, tatting, and hair pin lace. This comprehensive book showcases a wide range of patterns and designs, providing step-by-step instructions and inspiration for both beginners and experienced crafters. From delicate lace borders to charming embellishments, 'Edgings' offers endless possibilities to add a touch of elegance to any project. 

Genres: Crafts, Needlework, DIY

Themes: Creativity, Beauty, Handmade crafts
