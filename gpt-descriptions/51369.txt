Synopsis: In "A Queen of Tears, vol. 2 of 2: Caroline Matilda, Queen of Denmark and Norway and Princess of Great Britain and Ireland," author W. H. Wilkins delves into the captivating life of Caroline Matilda, a princess who becomes a queen and faces a tumultuous journey filled with love, betrayal, and political intrigue. As she navigates the treacherous waters of the Danish court, Caroline Matilda's resilience and determination shine through, making her a truly unforgettable historical figure.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Drama

Themes: Royalty, Love and betrayal, Political intrigue, Resilience
