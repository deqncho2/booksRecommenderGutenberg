Synopsis: In "For the Master's Sake: A Story of the Days of Queen Mary," Emily Sarah Holt takes us back to the tumultuous era of Queen Mary's reign. The story follows the lives of two young siblings, Richard and Dorothy, who find themselves caught in the crossfire of religious persecution and political intrigue. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of Tudor England, their loyalty to their faith and their unwavering determination to protect each other will be tested. With danger lurking at every corner, the siblings must make difficult choices that will shape their destinies and challenge their beliefs. Will they be able to survive the reign of Queen Mary and find a way to stay true to themselves? 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Religious persecution, Loyalty, Family bonds
