Synopsis: In 'Pharos, The Egyptian: A Romance' by Boothby, Guy, a young Englishman named Cyril Forrester embarks on a thrilling adventure when he inherits a mysterious artifact from his late uncle. This artifact leads him to Egypt, where he becomes entangled in a web of ancient secrets, forbidden love, and perilous encounters with a shadowy cult. As Cyril delves deeper into the enigmatic world of ancient Egypt, he must navigate treacherous alliances and face the consequences of his own desires. With danger lurking at every turn, will Cyril uncover the truth behind the artifact and find his own destiny amidst the sands of time?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Forbidden love, Fate and destiny
