Synopsis: In "The Outdoor Girls at Wild Rose Lodge; or, the Hermit of Moonlight Falls" by Laura Lee Hope, the adventurous Outdoor Girls find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey to Wild Rose Lodge. As they settle into their new surroundings, they stumble upon a mysterious hermit living in the nearby Moonlight Falls. Determined to uncover the hermit's secrets, the girls find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected events that test their courage and friendship. Will they be able to solve the mystery of the hermit and bring peace to Moonlight Falls?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
