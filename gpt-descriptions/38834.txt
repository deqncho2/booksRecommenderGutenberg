Synopsis: 
In 'The Twins in the South' by Dorothy Whitehill, two identical twin sisters, Lily and Rose, embark on a journey to the enchanting landscapes of the South. As they immerse themselves in the vibrant culture and captivating history, they uncover a long-held family secret that threatens to unravel their lives. With their bond tested and their identities at stake, the twins must navigate a web of mystery, romance, and betrayal, all while discovering the true meaning of sisterhood.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Sisterhood
