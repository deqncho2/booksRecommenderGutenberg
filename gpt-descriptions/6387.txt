Synopsis: In 'The Lives of the Twelve Caesars, Volume 02: Augustus', Suetonius delves into the life of one of history's most influential figures, Augustus. From his rise to power after the assassination of Julius Caesar to his transformation of Rome into an empire, this captivating biography explores the complex personality and political genius of Augustus. Through vivid anecdotes and meticulous research, Suetonius paints a vivid portrait of a man who shaped the destiny of an entire civilization.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Power and politics, Leadership, Legacy
