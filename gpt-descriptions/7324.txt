Synopsis: In 'Tropic Days', E. J. Banfield takes us on a captivating journey to Dunk Island, a tropical paradise off the coast of Australia. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, he introduces us to the unique flora and fauna, the vibrant local community, and the challenges of living in such a remote and untamed environment. From encounters with deadly snakes to heartwarming interactions with the island's inhabitants, Banfield's memoir offers a fascinating glimpse into a world few have experienced. 

Genres: Memoir, Travelogue, Natural History

Themes: Adventure, Nature, Community, Survival
