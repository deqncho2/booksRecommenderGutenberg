Synopsis: In 'The Palace in the Garden' by Molesworth, Mrs., a young girl named Rosamund discovers a hidden palace in her family's garden. As she explores the enchanting palace, she encounters mysterious characters and uncovers secrets that will change her life forever. With each new discovery, Rosamund's imagination and courage are put to the test, leading her on a thrilling adventure filled with magic and wonder.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Discovery, Courage, Magic
