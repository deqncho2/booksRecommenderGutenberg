Synopsis: In 'The Chariot of the Flesh' by Peek, Hedley, a mysterious antique shop appears in a small town, offering its customers the chance to fulfill their deepest desires. As the townspeople succumb to their temptations, they soon discover that their wishes come with a heavy price. With each desire granted, the line between reality and illusion blurs, leading to unexpected consequences and a thrilling battle for redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Temptation, Illusion vs Reality, Redemption
