Synopsis: In 'Carry On!' by Virna Sheard, a young woman named Anne finds herself caught in the midst of World War I, torn between her duty to her country and her desire for love and freedom. As she navigates the challenges of war, Anne discovers unexpected friendships, faces heart-wrenching choices, and learns the true meaning of courage. With vivid descriptions and emotional depth, this captivating novel takes readers on a journey of love, sacrifice, and resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Courage and resilience, Duty and freedom
