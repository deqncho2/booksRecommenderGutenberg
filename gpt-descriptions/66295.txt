Synopsis: 

In the enchanting city of Venice, two women from different backgrounds find their lives unexpectedly intertwined. Violetta, a talented opera singer, is drawn into a world of passion, secrets, and betrayal when she becomes the muse of a renowned composer. Meanwhile, Lina, a young Englishwoman, arrives in Venice seeking solace after a devastating loss, only to discover a hidden family connection that will change her life forever. As their paths converge, both women must navigate the treacherous waters of love, ambition, and the haunting beauty of Venice itself.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and desire, Identity and self-discovery, Art and creativity, Family and heritage
