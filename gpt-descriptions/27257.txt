Synopsis: 

In 'Electricity for the farm: Light, heat and power by inexpensive methods from the water wheel or farm engine', Frederick Irving Anderson takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of electricity on the farm. With a focus on providing affordable and sustainable methods, Anderson explores the potential of harnessing the power of water wheels and farm engines to generate electricity for various needs. From illuminating the darkest corners of the farm to powering essential machinery, this book offers practical and innovative solutions that will revolutionize farming practices.

Genres: Non-fiction, Technology, Agriculture

Themes: Sustainable energy, Farming innovation, Cost-effective solutions
