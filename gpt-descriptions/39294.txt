Synopsis: In 'The Great House' by Stanley John Weyman, a young man named Anthony Gell is unexpectedly thrust into a world of mystery and danger when he inherits an ancient and grand estate. As he delves into the history of the house, he uncovers a hidden secret that has been guarded for centuries. With the help of a captivating woman, Anthony must navigate treacherous alliances and face his own inner demons to protect the legacy of the great house.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Redemption
