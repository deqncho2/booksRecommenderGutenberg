Synopsis: In 'The Rose-Jar' by Thomas S. Jones, a young woman named Mary discovers a mysterious jar filled with roses that possess an enchanting power. As she delves deeper into the jar's secrets, Mary finds herself caught in a web of love, desire, and the consequences of her choices. With each rose she plucks, her life takes unexpected turns, leading her on a captivating journey of self-discovery and transformation.

Genres: Romance, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Love, Fate, Personal growth
