Synopsis: In "Arthur Machen: A Novelist of Ecstasy and Sin," Vincent Starrett delves into the life and works of the enigmatic author Arthur Machen, known for his captivating tales of mystery and the supernatural. Starrett explores Machen's literary career, his unique writing style, and the controversies surrounding his provocative themes. Alongside this insightful analysis, the book includes two previously uncollected poems by Machen, offering readers a glimpse into the mind of this extraordinary writer. 

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Poetry

Themes: Mystery, Supernatural, Controversy
