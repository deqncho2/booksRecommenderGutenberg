Synopsis: 
In 'Second Plays' by A. A. Milne, the reader is taken on a delightful journey through a collection of witty and charming plays. From a whimsical tale of a mischievous ghost to a hilarious comedy of errors set in a country house, each play is filled with clever dialogue and unexpected twists. With a cast of endearing characters and a touch of whimsy, 'Second Plays' is sure to captivate readers with its delightful blend of humor and heart.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Fantasy

Themes: Love and relationships, Identity and self-discovery, The power of imagination
