Synopsis: In "Reminiscences of Forts Sumter and Moultrie in 1860-'61," Abner Doubleday takes us on a captivating journey back to the tumultuous period leading up to the American Civil War. Through his vivid recollections, we witness the escalating tensions and political maneuverings that ultimately culminate in the fateful events at Fort Sumter. Doubleday's personal experiences as a military officer provide a unique perspective, offering a gripping account of the pivotal moments that shaped the course of history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Military history

Themes: American Civil War, Political tensions, Military strategy, Personal sacrifice
