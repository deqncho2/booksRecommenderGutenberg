Synopsis: In 'Historic Tales: The Romance of Reality. Vol. 02 (of 15), American (2)', Charles Morris takes readers on a captivating journey through the early days of American history. From the daring exploits of explorers to the struggles of the early settlers, this volume delves into the fascinating stories that shaped the nation. Discover the untold tales of famous figures like Pocahontas and John Smith, as well as lesser-known heroes who played pivotal roles in America's past. With vivid descriptions and engaging narratives, Morris brings history to life, transporting readers to a time of adventure, conflict, and triumph.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Settlement, Heroism, Cultural Exchange
