Synopsis: 
In 'Bell's Cathedrals: The Cathedral Church of Durham: A Description of Its Fabric and A Brief History of the Episcopal See', Joseph E. Bygate takes readers on a captivating journey through the magnificent Cathedral Church of Durham. With meticulous attention to detail, Bygate unveils the architectural wonders and historical significance of this iconic structure. From its awe-inspiring Norman architecture to its breathtaking stained glass windows, readers will be transported to a world of grandeur and spirituality. Alongside the architectural exploration, Bygate delves into the rich history of the Episcopal See, shedding light on the influential figures and events that shaped Durham Cathedral into the remarkable symbol of faith it is today. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Architecture, History

Themes: Architecture and design, Religious history, Cultural heritage
