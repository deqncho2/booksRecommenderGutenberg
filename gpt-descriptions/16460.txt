Synopsis: 

'From Aldershot to Pretoria: A Story of Christian Work among Our Troops in South Africa' by William E. Sellers takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of Christian workers amidst the chaos of the South African War. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, this gripping narrative follows the lives of dedicated individuals who selflessly provide spiritual support and guidance to soldiers on the frontlines. As the story unfolds, readers are immersed in the challenges, triumphs, and heart-wrenching moments that shape the lives of both the soldiers and the Christian workers. From the bustling military camps to the battlefields of Pretoria, this book offers a unique perspective on the power of faith and compassion in the midst of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Christian Fiction

Themes: Faith, Compassion, Sacrifice, War, Humanitarianism
