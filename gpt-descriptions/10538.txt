Synopsis: In 'Hyacinth' by George A. Birmingham, a mysterious woman named Hyacinth arrives in a small Irish village, captivating the locals with her enigmatic charm. As the villagers become entangled in her web, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the idyllic community is forever changed. With its intriguing characters and unexpected twists, 'Hyacinth' delves into the depths of human nature, exploring the power of manipulation and the consequences of hidden desires.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity, Morality
