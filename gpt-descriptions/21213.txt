Synopsis:
In "New Ideals in Rural Schools," George Herbert Betts takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of rural education. Through vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, Betts explores the challenges faced by rural schools and presents innovative ideas to transform them into vibrant centers of learning. From the struggles of underfunded schools to the importance of community involvement, this book offers a fresh perspective on the potential of rural education. As Betts delves into the lives of students, teachers, and administrators, he uncovers the untapped potential and hidden treasures within rural communities. With a blend of practical advice and inspiring anecdotes, "New Ideals in Rural Schools" is a must-read for anyone passionate about education and the power of change.

Genres: Education, Non-fiction

Themes: Rural education, Community involvement, Educational reform
