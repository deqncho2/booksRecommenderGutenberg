Synopsis: In 'A Traveller in Little Things', W. H. Hudson takes readers on a captivating journey through the seemingly ordinary and overlooked aspects of everyday life. From the enchanting beauty of a spider's web to the mysterious allure of a moth, Hudson's keen observations and lyrical prose transport readers to a world where the smallest details hold profound significance. Through his encounters with nature and the human world, the author invites readers to reflect on the interconnectedness of all things and the hidden wonders that surround us. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Travelogue

Themes: Nature, Observation, Interconnectedness, Beauty, Wonder
