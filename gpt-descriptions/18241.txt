Synopsis: In "Tea-Cup Reading and Fortune-Telling by Tea Leaves, by a Highland Seer," embark on a mystical journey into the world of tea leaves and divination. Follow the guidance of a wise Highland Seer as they unravel the secrets hidden within the delicate patterns formed by tea leaves in a cup. Discover the ancient art of tea-cup reading and explore the fascinating realm of fortune-telling through this enchanting and insightful guide. Delve into the hidden meanings and symbols that lie within the tea leaves, and learn how to interpret them to gain glimpses into the past, present, and future. This captivating book will leave you eager to unlock the mysteries that lie within your own tea cup.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Divination

Themes: Mysticism, Intuition, Symbolism
