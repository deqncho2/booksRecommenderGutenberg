Synopsis: In 'The Common Man' by Reynolds, Mack, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger when he stumbles upon a mysterious artifact. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the artifact, he becomes entangled in a thrilling race against time, facing unexpected allies and formidable enemies. With his life on the line, the common man must uncover the truth and confront his own hidden potential before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Action

Themes: Identity, Power, Redemption
