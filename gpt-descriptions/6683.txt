Synopsis: 
In 'The Little Nugget', a wealthy American couple hires a private detective to keep an eye on their mischievous son, Ogden Ford, who has a knack for getting into trouble. However, when Ogden is kidnapped by a notorious gang, chaos ensues as the detective, along with a cast of eccentric characters, embarks on a hilarious and unpredictable journey to rescue the little nugget.

Genres:
Comedy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes:
Parenting, Wealth and Privilege, Redemption
