Synopsis: In this groundbreaking work, Charles Darwin delves into the intricate world of human evolution and the role of sexual selection. Building upon his previous theories, Darwin explores the fascinating connections between the descent of man and the complex dynamics of sexual relationships. Through meticulous observations and compelling evidence, he uncovers the hidden forces that shape our physical and behavioral traits, challenging conventional notions of gender and shedding light on the origins of human diversity. This thought-provoking volume offers a captivating exploration of the mysteries of human evolution and the intricate interplay between sex and selection.

Genres: Science, Evolutionary Biology, Anthropology

Themes: Human evolution, Sexual selection, Gender, Diversity
