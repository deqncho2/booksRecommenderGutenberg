Synopsis: In 'The Brownie Scouts in the Cherry Festival' by Mildred A. Wirt, a group of adventurous Brownie Scouts find themselves in the midst of a thrilling mystery during their visit to the annual Cherry Festival. As they explore the festival grounds, they stumble upon a series of puzzling clues that lead them on a quest to uncover the truth behind a mysterious disappearance. With their determination and resourcefulness, the Brownie Scouts embark on a thrilling adventure that tests their teamwork and problem-solving skills. Will they be able to solve the mystery before the festival comes to an end?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Problem-solving
