Synopsis: In 'The Story of a Picture' by Sherley Douglass, a mysterious photograph is discovered, revealing a captivating tale that spans generations. As the protagonist delves deeper into the picture's secrets, they uncover a web of hidden connections, long-lost love, and a haunting mystery that refuses to be forgotten. With each revelation, the reader is drawn into a world where past and present collide, and the power of a single image holds the key to unlocking the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and loss
