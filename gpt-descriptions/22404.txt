Synopsis: In the small coastal town of White-Rock Cove, a mysterious series of events unfolds, shrouding the community in an eerie atmosphere. As the townspeople grapple with unexplained occurrences and unsettling secrets, they must confront their deepest fears and unravel the enigma that haunts their beloved home. With each twist and turn, the story delves into the dark underbelly of human nature, testing the limits of trust, loyalty, and the lengths one will go to protect their own. Prepare to be captivated by this gripping tale of suspense and intrigue that will keep you guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Human nature
