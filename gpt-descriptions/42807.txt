Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of the Clasped Hands: A Novel' by Boothby, Guy, a young woman named Elsie is haunted by a chilling secret that threatens to unravel her life. As she delves into the mysterious circumstances surrounding her father's death, she uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden identities. With each clue she uncovers, Elsie finds herself drawn deeper into a dangerous world where nothing is as it seems. As she races against time to uncover the truth, she must confront her own past and make choices that will determine her future. Will Elsie be able to solve the mystery before it consumes her?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Identity, Betrayal
