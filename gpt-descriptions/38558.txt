Synopsis: In 'The White Crystals: Being an Account of the Adventures of Two Boys', readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside two young boys who stumble upon a mysterious discovery. As they delve deeper into the secrets surrounding the white crystals, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger and intrigue. With each twist and turn, the boys must rely on their wits and bravery to navigate through a world filled with unexpected challenges and unexpected allies. Will they uncover the truth behind the white crystals, or will their curiosity lead them into perilous territory?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Exploration, Perseverance
