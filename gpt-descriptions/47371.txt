Synopsis: In 'First Lessons in the Maori Language, with a Short Vocabulary', author W. L. Williams takes readers on an immersive journey into the rich and fascinating world of the Maori language. Through a series of engaging lessons, readers will discover the beauty and intricacies of this indigenous language, gaining a deeper understanding of Maori culture and heritage along the way. With its practical approach and insightful explanations, this book serves as an invaluable resource for anyone interested in learning the Maori language and connecting with the vibrant Maori community.

Genres: Language Learning, Cultural Studies, Indigenous Studies

Themes: Language Preservation, Cultural Identity, Education
