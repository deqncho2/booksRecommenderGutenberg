Synopsis: 

In "The Nineteenth and Their Times: Being an Account of the Four Cavalry Regiments in the British Army That Have Borne the Number Nineteen and of the Campaigns in Which They Served," author J. Biddulph takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of four cavalry regiments in the British Army. From their formation to their involvement in various campaigns, this book provides a detailed account of the brave soldiers who served in these regiments and the challenges they faced on the battlefield. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Military strategy, Historical significance
