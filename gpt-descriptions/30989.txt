Synopsis: 
In 'Mystery Ranch' by Arthur Chapman, a group of adventurous young boys stumble upon an abandoned ranch in the heart of the Wild West. Intrigued by the secrets it holds, they embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the ranch's mysterious past. As they delve deeper into the hidden chambers and forgotten corridors, they encounter unexpected challenges and face dangerous adversaries. With each clue they unravel, the boys find themselves entangled in a web of suspense and intrigue, leading them to a shocking revelation that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Western

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Courage
