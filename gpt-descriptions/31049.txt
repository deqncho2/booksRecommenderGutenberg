Synopsis: 
Bugle Blasts is a captivating collection of essays and speeches delivered by William E. Crane before the Ohio Commandery of the Military Order of the Loyal Legion of the United States. Through his powerful and thought-provoking words, Crane takes readers on a journey through the tumultuous times of the American Civil War and its aftermath. From the battlefields to the home front, he explores the sacrifices, heroism, and resilience of the soldiers and civilians who shaped the nation's history. With a blend of historical anecdotes, personal reflections, and stirring rhetoric, Crane's Bugle Blasts offers a unique perspective on the war and its lasting impact.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Speeches

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Resilience, Historical memory
