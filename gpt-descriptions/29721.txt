Synopsis: 
In 'Philo Gubb, Correspondence-School Detective', we follow the misadventures of Philo Gubb, a bumbling and eccentric detective-in-training. Armed with his trusty correspondence-school detective kit, Gubb sets out to solve a series of puzzling cases that take him from small-town America to the bustling streets of New York City. Along the way, he encounters a colorful cast of characters, including a mysterious femme fatale, a cunning con artist, and a gang of ruthless criminals. With his unique blend of naivety, determination, and sheer luck, Gubb stumbles his way through each case, leaving chaos and hilarity in his wake.

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Amateur detective, Absurdity, Irony
