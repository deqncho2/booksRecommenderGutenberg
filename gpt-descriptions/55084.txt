Synopsis: 

In 'The Homing Pigeon', the United States War Department and Army Signal Corps present a captivating account that delves into the extraordinary role of homing pigeons during World War I. This gripping narrative follows the journey of these remarkable birds as they navigate treacherous skies, carrying vital messages across enemy lines. From the trenches to the front lines, the pigeons become unsung heroes, defying danger and delivering critical information that could change the course of the war. As the conflict intensifies, the bond between the soldiers and their feathered companions grows stronger, showcasing the indomitable spirit and resilience of both man and bird.

Genres: Historical, War, Non-fiction

Themes: Heroism, Communication, Loyalty
