Synopsis: In 'Campfire Girls' Outing; Or, Ethel Hollister's Second Summer in Camp', Ethel Hollister returns to Camp Keewaydin for another thrilling summer adventure with her fellow Campfire Girls. As they embark on a series of exciting outings and face unexpected challenges, Ethel discovers the true meaning of friendship, courage, and the importance of embracing new experiences. With the campfire as their guiding light, the girls navigate through a summer filled with laughter, growth, and unforgettable memories.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Adventure, Self-discovery
