Synopsis: Etheldreda the Ready: A School Story follows the adventures of Etheldreda, a spirited young girl who finds herself in a new and exciting environment when she is sent to boarding school. As she navigates the challenges of making friends, facing strict teachers, and discovering her own strengths, Etheldreda's quick wit and determination shine through. With a touch of mystery and plenty of heartwarming moments, this delightful tale captures the joys and trials of school life.

Genres: Children's Literature, School Story, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Self-discovery
