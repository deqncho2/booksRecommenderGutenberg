Synopsis: In "The Mind in the Making: The Relation of Intelligence to Social Reform," James Harvey Robinson explores the fascinating connection between intelligence and social progress. Through thought-provoking analysis and historical examples, Robinson delves into the ways in which human intelligence has shaped and continues to shape society. From examining the impact of education and critical thinking to questioning the role of tradition and authority, this book offers a captivating exploration of the power of the human mind in driving social change.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Sociology

Themes: Intelligence, Social reform, Education, Critical thinking, Tradition, Authority
