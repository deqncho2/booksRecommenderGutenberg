Synopsis: In 'The Lost Mountain: A Tale of Sonora' by Mayne Reid, a group of adventurers embarks on a perilous journey through the rugged landscapes of Sonora, Mexico, in search of a legendary lost mountain. As they face treacherous terrain, encounters with bandits, and the mysteries of an ancient civilization, they must confront their own fears and desires. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Redemption
