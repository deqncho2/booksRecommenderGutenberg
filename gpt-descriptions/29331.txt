Synopsis: In the small town of Crestwood, a mysterious crevice appears overnight, causing fear and curiosity among the townspeople. As the crevice grows larger and more dangerous, a group of unlikely allies must come together to uncover its origins and find a way to close it before it consumes the entire town. With secrets, suspense, and unexpected twists, 'The Crevice' takes readers on a thrilling journey into the unknown.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fear of the unknown, Community, Collaboration
