Synopsis: In 'Hospital Sketches', Peabody, Robert Swain takes us on a captivating journey through the experiences of a nurse during the American Civil War. Through vivid and poignant sketches, the author provides a unique and intimate glimpse into the lives of the wounded soldiers and the challenges faced by those caring for them. With a blend of compassion, humor, and raw emotion, this collection of sketches offers a powerful portrayal of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical fiction, Memoir, War literature

Themes: Compassion, Resilience, Sacrifice, Human connection
