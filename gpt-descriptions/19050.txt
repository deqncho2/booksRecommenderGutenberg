Synopsis: 
In the eighth annual meeting of the Northern Nut Growers Association, held in Stamford, Connecticut in 1917, a diverse group of horticulturists, botanists, and enthusiasts gather to discuss the fascinating world of nut cultivation. From the exploration of new varieties and hybridization techniques to the challenges faced by nut growers, this report unveils a wealth of knowledge and insights into the art and science of nut growing. With engaging discussions, practical advice, and intriguing discoveries, this meeting promises to captivate readers with its exploration of an often overlooked but essential aspect of agriculture.

Genres: Non-fiction, Horticulture, Botany

Themes: Nut cultivation, Hybridization, Horticultural challenges, Agricultural innovation
