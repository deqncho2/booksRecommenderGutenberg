Synopsis: 

'Additional Records and Extensions of Known Ranges of Mammals from Utah' takes readers on an exciting journey through the diverse landscapes of Utah, uncovering new records and expanding our understanding of the mammal species that inhabit this region. From the majestic mountains to the arid deserts, this comprehensive study presents fascinating discoveries and unexpected findings that will captivate both wildlife enthusiasts and scientific researchers alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Wildlife Research

Themes: Biodiversity, Conservation, Species Distribution
