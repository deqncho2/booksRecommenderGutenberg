Synopsis: 
In "Apis Mellifica; or, The Poison of the Honey-Bee, Considered as a Therapeutic Agent," author C. W. Wolf delves into the fascinating world of honey bees and their venom, exploring its potential as a therapeutic remedy. Through meticulous research and captivating anecdotes, Wolf uncovers the hidden healing properties of this natural substance, shedding light on its potential to treat a wide range of ailments. From ancient civilizations to modern medicine, this book takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and science behind the honey bee's venom, revealing its remarkable potential as a therapeutic agent.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Medicine

Themes: Natural remedies, Alternative medicine, History of medicine
