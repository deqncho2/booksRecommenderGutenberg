Synopsis: 

In the town of Mobile, a mysterious and devastating sickness has struck, causing an unprecedented number of deaths. In response, a committee is formed to investigate the causes and extent of this extraordinary outbreak. As they delve into the heart of the matter, they uncover shocking revelations and unexpected connections that challenge their understanding of the situation. With each revelation, the committee's determination to uncover the truth grows stronger, leading them down a path filled with danger and intrigue. Will they be able to uncover the hidden secrets behind the sickness and mortality in Mobile before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Historical, Medical

Themes: Investigation, Disease, Conspiracy
