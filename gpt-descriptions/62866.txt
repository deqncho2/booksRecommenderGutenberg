Synopsis: In 'The Young Game-Warden' by Castlemon, Harry, a young and adventurous protagonist finds himself thrust into the world of wildlife conservation as a game warden. Assigned to a remote and untamed region, he must navigate the challenges of poaching, dangerous animals, and the complexities of human nature. As he strives to protect the natural beauty around him, Harry discovers that the true battle lies not only in preserving the wilderness but also in preserving his own integrity and sense of justice.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Wildlife Conservation

Themes: Conservation, Morality, Courage, Wilderness Preservation
