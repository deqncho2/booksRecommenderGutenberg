Synopsis: 

In 'Report on the Migration of Birds in the Autumn of 1879. [First Report]', Cordeaux and Harvie-Brown embark on a fascinating exploration of the mysterious phenomenon of bird migration. Through meticulous observations and scientific analysis, they delve into the intricate patterns and behaviors of various bird species during the autumn season of 1879. From the awe-inspiring journeys of migratory birds to the intricate details of their habitats and feeding habits, this report offers a captivating glimpse into the world of avian migration.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Ornithology

Themes: Migration, Nature's Rhythms, Animal Behavior
