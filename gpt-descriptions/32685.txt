Synopsis: In 'Cold Ghost' by Chester S. Geier, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious deaths that leave the residents terrified and searching for answers. As the body count rises, a determined detective must unravel the dark secrets hidden within the community, leading him on a chilling journey into the unknown. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the cold ghost haunting the town.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Secrets and lies, Supernatural phenomena
