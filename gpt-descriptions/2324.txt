Synopsis: In 'A House to Let', a mysterious advertisement leads four strangers to rent a peculiar house. As they uncover the secrets hidden within its walls, they become entangled in a web of intrigue, love, and tragedy. From a missing bride to a haunted room, each character's story intertwines in unexpected ways, revealing the true nature of the house and its inhabitants. This collaborative work by Charles Dickens, Wilkie Collins, Elizabeth Cleghorn Gaskell, and Adelaide Anne Procter will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the mysteries that lie within the house.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Victorian Literature

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, supernatural elements, the power of love and redemption
