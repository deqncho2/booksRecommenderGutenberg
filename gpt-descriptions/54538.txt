Synopsis: In 'Miss Esperance and Mr Wycherly' by L. Allen Harker, a young woman named Miss Esperance finds herself drawn to the mysterious Mr Wycherly, a man with a dark past and a hidden secret. As their paths intertwine, they embark on a journey filled with intrigue, romance, and unexpected twists. Will Miss Esperance uncover the truth about Mr Wycherly, or will she become entangled in a web of deception?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Love and trust, Redemption
