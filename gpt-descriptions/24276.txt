Synopsis: In 'The Coffin Cure' by Alan Edward Nourse, a mysterious disease is spreading rapidly, causing people to fall into a deep coma from which they cannot awaken. As the medical community struggles to find a cure, a brilliant doctor embarks on a dangerous journey to uncover the truth behind this baffling illness. With time running out, he must navigate through a web of conspiracy and face unimaginable challenges to save humanity from this deadly epidemic.

Genres: Science Fiction, Medical Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Medical ethics, Human resilience, Conspiracy
