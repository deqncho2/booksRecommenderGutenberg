Synopsis: In 'Frank and Fearless; or, The Fortunes of Jasper Kent' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Jasper Kent, a young man who finds himself in a series of unexpected adventures and challenges. From facing dangerous situations to navigating the complexities of relationships, Jasper's determination and courage are put to the test. As he strives to overcome obstacles and make a name for himself, readers will be captivated by the twists and turns that shape his journey.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Self-discovery
