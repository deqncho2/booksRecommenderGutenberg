Synopsis: In 'The Path to Honour' by Sydney C. Grier, a young and ambitious protagonist finds themselves thrust into a world of political intrigue and dangerous secrets. As they navigate through treacherous alliances and unexpected betrayals, they must make difficult choices that will test their loyalty and integrity. With each step they take, the path to honour becomes increasingly elusive, forcing them to question their own values and the true meaning of righteousness. Will they be able to stay true to themselves and find their way back to honour, or will they be consumed by the darkness that surrounds them?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Morality, Identity
