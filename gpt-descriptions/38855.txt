Synopsis: In 'The Iron Furnace; or, Slavery and Secession', John H. Aughey takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous era of slavery and secession in the United States. Set against the backdrop of the Civil War, this powerful narrative follows the lives of individuals caught in the iron grip of slavery, as they navigate the complexities of loyalty, freedom, and the pursuit of justice. Aughey's vivid storytelling and compelling characters shed light on the harsh realities of a divided nation, leaving readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes: Slavery, Civil War, Loyalty, Freedom, Justice
