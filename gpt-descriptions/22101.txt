Synopsis: In 'Wood Folk at School' by William J. Long, a group of woodland creatures embarks on an extraordinary adventure when they decide to attend school alongside human children. From mischievous squirrels to wise old owls, these enchanting creatures bring their unique perspectives to the classroom, teaching valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the wonders of the natural world. As they navigate the challenges of school life, the wood folk discover that their presence has a profound impact on both the students and the teachers, leading to unexpected and heartwarming outcomes.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Nature, Education, Acceptance
