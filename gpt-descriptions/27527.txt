Synopsis: 

Step back in time to the year 1861 and enter the mysterious world of the Insane Asylum of California. In this intriguing document, the Resident Physician unveils the rules and regulations that govern the lives of the patients within its walls. From the strict daily routines to the peculiar treatments, this glimpse into the inner workings of the asylum will leave readers captivated and curious about the lives of those deemed insane. 

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction 

Themes: Mental health, Institutionalization, Medical practices
