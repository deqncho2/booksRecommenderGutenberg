Synopsis: In "Belgians Under the German Eagle" by Jean Massart, a gripping tale unfolds as a group of Belgian civilians find themselves caught in the midst of World War I. As the German forces occupy their homeland, the characters navigate through a web of danger, resistance, and personal sacrifice. With their lives forever changed, they must confront the harsh realities of war and make difficult choices that will shape their futures. This captivating narrative explores the resilience of the human spirit and the enduring power of hope in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Drama

Themes: Resilience, Sacrifice, Occupation, Survival
