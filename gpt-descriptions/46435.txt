Synopsis: 
'Practical Instruction for Detectives: A Complete Course in Secret Service Study' takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of secret service investigation. Written by the renowned detective Manning, Emmerson Wain, this comprehensive guide offers invaluable insights and techniques for aspiring detectives. From deciphering cryptic codes to mastering disguise, readers will be captivated by the intricate methods employed by the world's top sleuths. As the pages turn, readers will find themselves immersed in a series of gripping case studies that showcase the application of these techniques in real-life scenarios. With each chapter, the stakes rise higher, leading to a climactic finale that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Non-fiction

Themes: Secret service investigation, Detective techniques, Crime-solving methods
