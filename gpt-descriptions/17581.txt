Synopsis: In "The Romancers: A Comedy in Three Acts," two young lovers, Sylvette and Percinet, are kept apart by their feuding fathers who pretend to be enemies. The young couple, however, secretly meet in a garden and plan to elope. As their plan unfolds, they encounter a series of hilarious and unexpected obstacles that test their love and determination. Will their love conquer all, or will their fathers' feud tear them apart forever?

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Farce

Themes: Love, Deception, Parental Influence
