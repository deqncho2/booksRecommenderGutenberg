Synopsis: In 'The Man Who Was Thursday: A Nightmare', a poet named Gabriel Syme is recruited by a secret anti-anarchist organization. As he delves deeper into the mysterious world of the anarchists, he finds himself caught in a web of intrigue, deception, and unexpected twists. With each revelation, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Satire

Themes: Anarchy, Identity, Truth, Deception
