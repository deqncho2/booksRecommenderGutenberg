Synopsis: In "Her Country" by Mary Raymond Shipman Andrews, a young woman named Helen finds herself torn between her duty to her family and her love for her country during a time of war. As she navigates the challenges and sacrifices that come with patriotism, Helen's loyalty is put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to reconcile her personal desires with her commitment to her country?

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, war

Themes: Patriotism, duty, sacrifice, love
