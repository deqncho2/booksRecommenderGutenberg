Synopsis: In the sixth volume of "Harold: the Last of the Saxon Kings," the story takes a dramatic turn as the fate of England hangs in the balance. As Harold, the last Saxon king, faces numerous challenges to his rule, including internal conflicts and external threats, he must navigate treacherous political alliances and make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of his kingdom. With the Norman invasion looming, Harold's strength and determination are put to the ultimate test, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical fiction, political drama, adventure

Themes: Power struggles, loyalty, destiny, the clash of cultures
