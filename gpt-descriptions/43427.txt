Synopsis: 

In the 17th volume of the renowned Encyclopaedia Britannica, 11th Edition, readers will embark on a fascinating journey from "Lord Chamberlain" to "Luqman." This captivating volume delves into a wide range of subjects, from the influential role of the Lord Chamberlain in British history to the enigmatic figure of Luqman, a wise man mentioned in Islamic tradition. Each entry offers a wealth of knowledge, providing readers with a deeper understanding of these intriguing topics.

Genres: Reference, Non-fiction

Themes: British history, Islamic tradition, Wisdom
