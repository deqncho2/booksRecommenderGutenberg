Synopsis: In "What Shall I Be? A Chat With Young People" by Francis Bernard Cassilly, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a series of conversations that explore the dreams, aspirations, and challenges faced by young individuals. Through thought-provoking dialogues, the author delves into various career paths, personal growth, and the importance of finding one's true calling. With each conversation, the reader is invited to reflect on their own passions and ambitions, ultimately inspiring them to embrace their unique potential and make meaningful choices for their future.

Genres: Self-help, Inspirational, Education

Themes: Career exploration, Personal growth, Self-discovery
