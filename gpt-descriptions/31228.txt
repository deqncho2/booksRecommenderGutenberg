Synopsis: 
In "The House by the Lock," a mysterious house holds dark secrets that have haunted the surrounding village for years. When a young man inherits the property, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, as he uncovers the truth behind the house's eerie reputation. As he delves deeper into the house's history, he discovers a series of chilling events that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning what lies behind the locked doors. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Haunting and supernatural elements
