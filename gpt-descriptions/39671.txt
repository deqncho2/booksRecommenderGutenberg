Synopsis: In 'Handel' by Romain Rolland, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of the greatest composers in history. This biographical novel delves into the struggles, triumphs, and inner world of George Frideric Handel, a musical genius whose melodies continue to enchant audiences to this day. From his early years in Germany to his rise to fame in England, follow Handel's relentless pursuit of his artistic vision, his encounters with influential figures of his time, and the profound impact his music had on society. With vivid descriptions and a deep exploration of the human spirit, 'Handel' is a mesmerizing tribute to a remarkable artist.

Genres: Biographical fiction, Historical fiction

Themes: Artistic passion, Perseverance, Legacy
