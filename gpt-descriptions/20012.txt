Synopsis: 
In 'The Hawarden Visitors' Hand-Book: Revised Edition, 1890', Gladstone, W. H. takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque village of Hawarden. This revised edition serves as a comprehensive guide, offering a wealth of information about the village's history, landmarks, and notable figures. From the enchanting Hawarden Castle to the charming local shops and eateries, readers will be transported to a bygone era, discovering the hidden gems and fascinating anecdotes that make Hawarden a truly unique destination. As readers delve into the pages of this hand-book, they will uncover the secrets and stories that have shaped the village, leaving them eager to explore its wonders for themselves.

Genres: Travel, History, Guidebook
Themes: Local history, Landmarks, Cultural heritage
