Synopsis: 
'Peerless Prestidigitation: Being a collection of entirely new ideas and effects in the fascinating art of modern magic' by De Caston, Herbert is a captivating book that unveils a treasure trove of innovative magic tricks and illusions. From mind-bending card tricks to mind-reading feats, this collection promises to astound and amaze even the most seasoned magician. With step-by-step instructions and detailed illustrations, De Caston takes readers on a journey through the world of modern magic, revealing secrets that will leave audiences spellbound. Prepare to be dazzled by the ingenuity and creativity of De Caston's magical masterpieces.

Genres: 
Magic, Entertainment, How-to

Themes: 
Ingenuity, Creativity, Illusion
