Synopsis: In 'That Fortune' by Charles Dudley Warner, a young man named Ned embarks on a journey to seek his fortune in the bustling city of New York. Faced with numerous challenges and unexpected encounters, Ned's determination and wit are put to the test as he navigates the complexities of urban life. Along the way, he discovers the true meaning of success and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Identity, Self-discovery
