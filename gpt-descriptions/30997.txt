Synopsis: In 'A New Bat (Genus Leptonycteris) From Coahuila' by Stains, Howard J., a team of biologists embarks on a thrilling expedition to the remote region of Coahuila, Mexico, in search of a new species of bat. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected challenges, they uncover a remarkable discovery that could have significant implications for the field of biology. With suspenseful twists and turns, this scientific adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to learn more about this fascinating new bat species.

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, Natural History

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Biodiversity
