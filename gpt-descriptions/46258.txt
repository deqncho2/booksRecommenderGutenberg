Synopsis: 
In the quaint village of Thorley Weir, a mysterious and enchanting presence begins to captivate the townspeople. As the villagers become increasingly drawn to this enigmatic force, they find themselves embarking on a journey of self-discovery, love, and the exploration of the supernatural. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, Thorley Weir becomes a place where reality and fantasy intertwine, leaving the reader questioning what is real and what is merely a figment of the imagination.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Supernatural

Themes: Mystery, Love, Identity, Perception
