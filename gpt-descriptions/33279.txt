Synopsis: In 'The Riddle of the Spinning Wheel', a mysterious murder takes place in a small village, leaving the residents baffled and fearful. As the investigation unfolds, a web of secrets and hidden motives is revealed, leading the reader on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the crime. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Justice
