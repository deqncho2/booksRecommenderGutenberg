Synopsis: 
"The Autobiography of a Quack, and The Case of George Dedlow" is a captivating novel that delves into the life of a charismatic and enigmatic doctor, Dr. X. Through his own words, we are taken on a journey that explores the complexities of his medical career, his unorthodox methods, and the mysterious case of George Dedlow. As the story unfolds, we are left questioning the true nature of Dr. X's intentions and the ethical boundaries he is willing to cross.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Medical Drama

Themes: Morality, Ethics, Power and Manipulation
