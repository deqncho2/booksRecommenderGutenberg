Synopsis: 
'As A Chinaman Saw Us: Passages from His Letters to a Friend at Home' takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of a Chinese immigrant living in the United States. Through a series of heartfelt and thought-provoking letters, the protagonist shares his observations, experiences, and reflections on American society, culture, and the complexities of assimilation. From the bustling streets of New York City to the vast landscapes of the American West, this poignant narrative offers a unique perspective on the challenges and triumphs of navigating a new world.

Genres: Fiction, Epistolary, Cultural Commentary

Themes: Immigration, Identity, Cultural Differences, Assimilation, Friendship
