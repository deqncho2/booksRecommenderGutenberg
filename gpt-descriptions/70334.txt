Synopsis: In "The Moat House: or Sir Jasper's Favourite Niece," secrets and mysteries surround the grand estate of the Moat House. When Sir Jasper's niece, Evelyn, arrives to stay, she becomes entangled in a web of family secrets, hidden treasures, and unexpected romance. As she delves deeper into the history of the Moat House, Evelyn uncovers dark secrets that threaten to unravel the very foundation of her family's legacy.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Hidden treasures, Love and betrayal
