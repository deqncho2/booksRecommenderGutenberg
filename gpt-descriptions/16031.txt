Synopsis: In this captivating issue of 'The Great Round World and What Is Going On In It', readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the latest news and events from around the world. From mysterious disappearances to daring adventures, this weekly magazine for boys and girls is filled with fascinating stories that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. Join the young protagonists as they uncover secrets, solve puzzles, and navigate through the ever-changing landscape of the late 19th century. Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, Problem-solving
