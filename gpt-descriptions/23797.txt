Synopsis: In 'A True Friend: A Novel' by Sergeant, Adeline, a chance encounter between two strangers leads to an unexpected friendship that will test their loyalty, trust, and resilience. As they navigate through life's challenges together, they discover the true meaning of friendship and the power it holds to heal and transform. With secrets, betrayals, and heart-wrenching choices, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the ultimate truth.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Trust, Loyalty, Redemption
