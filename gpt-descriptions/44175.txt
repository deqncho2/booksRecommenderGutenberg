Synopsis: In 'Nautical Charts' by G.R. Putnam, a mysterious discovery on an old shipwreck sets off a thrilling adventure that spans across treacherous seas and uncharted territories. As a team of explorers delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the nautical charts, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and ancient legends. With each step closer to the truth, the stakes rise higher, and the race against time becomes more perilous. Will they unlock the mysteries of the charts before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Secrets and Hidden Knowledge, Perseverance
