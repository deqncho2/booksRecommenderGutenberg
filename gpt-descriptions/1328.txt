Synopsis: In 'The Tinker's Wedding', a spirited young woman named Sarah Casey defies societal expectations and falls in love with a charismatic tinker named Michael Byrne. As they navigate the challenges of their unconventional relationship, they must confront the clash between tradition and personal freedom. With humor and wit, this play explores the complexities of love, marriage, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Comedy, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, rebellion against tradition
