Synopsis: In 'Exiles of the Three Red Moons' by Selwyn Carl, a group of unlikely companions find themselves stranded on a mysterious planet with three red moons. As they navigate the treacherous landscapes and encounter strange creatures, they must unravel the secrets of the moons and their own pasts to survive. With each step, they uncover a web of ancient prophecies, hidden powers, and a looming threat that could change the fate of the entire universe. This thrilling sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the truth behind the Three Red Moons.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Destiny
