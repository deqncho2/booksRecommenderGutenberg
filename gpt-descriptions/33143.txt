Synopsis: In 'East Angels: A Novel' by Constance Fenimore Woolson, a young woman named Cecilia finds herself torn between duty and desire as she navigates the complex social dynamics of post-Civil War America. Set in the picturesque town of East Angels, the story unfolds against a backdrop of societal expectations, forbidden love, and the struggle for independence. As Cecilia grapples with her own desires and the expectations placed upon her, she must make choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Social Expectations, Independence
