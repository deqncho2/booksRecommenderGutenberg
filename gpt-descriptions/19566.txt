Synopsis: In 'Fables of Infidelity and Facts of Faith: Being an Examination of the Evidences of Infidelity', Patterson, Robert takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the realms of doubt and belief. Through a series of captivating stories and compelling arguments, the author challenges the foundations of infidelity and explores the enduring power of faith. With each page, readers will find themselves questioning their own beliefs and pondering the true nature of truth and conviction.

Genres: Philosophy, Religious Studies, Non-fiction

Themes: Doubt, Belief, Faith, Skepticism, Truth
