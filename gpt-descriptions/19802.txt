Synopsis: In 'Cobwebs and Cables' by Stretton, Hesba, a young woman named Mabel finds herself caught between the allure of a glamorous city life and the simplicity of her rural upbringing. As she navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations, Mabel must make difficult choices that will shape her future. With secrets, unexpected twists, and a cast of intriguing characters, this captivating novel explores the delicate balance between dreams and reality.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Choices, Social expectations
