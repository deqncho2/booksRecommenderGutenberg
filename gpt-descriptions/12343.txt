Synopsis: In 'The Art of Iugling or Legerdemaine', Samuel Rid delves into the world of magic and deception, unraveling the secrets behind various tricks and illusions. From card and dice cheating to exposing the tricks of alchemy and foolish cons, Rid provides a comprehensive guide to the art of legerdemain. This book offers a delightful exploration of the techniques and practices of magic, aimed at entertaining and enlightening those who wish to delve into the world of illusion.

Genres: Non-fiction, Magic, Entertainment

Themes: Deception, Entertainment, Skill acquisition
