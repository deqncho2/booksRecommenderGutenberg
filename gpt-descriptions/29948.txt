Synopsis: 
In 'Two Timer' by Fredric Brown, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of deceit and danger when he becomes entangled with two women who are more than they appear. As he navigates a treacherous path of love, betrayal, and unexpected twists, the protagonist must uncover the truth before it's too late. With its gripping narrative and unpredictable plot, 'Two Timer' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Trust
