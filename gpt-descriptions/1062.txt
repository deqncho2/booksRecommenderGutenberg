Synopsis: 
The First Project Gutenberg Collection of Edgar Allan Poe is a captivating compilation of some of Poe's most haunting and mysterious tales. From the chilling depths of "The Tell-Tale Heart" to the enigmatic allure of "The Masque of the Red Death," this collection immerses readers in a world of suspense, horror, and psychological exploration. Each story delves into the darkest corners of the human psyche, leaving readers questioning their own sanity and the boundaries of reality. With its masterful storytelling and atmospheric prose, this collection is a must-read for anyone seeking to experience the macabre genius of Edgar Allan Poe.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, mystery

Themes: Madness, death, the supernatural, psychological exploration
