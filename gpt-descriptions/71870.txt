Synopsis: In "A Pound of Prevention" by Edmondson, G. C., a brilliant scientist named Dr. Amelia Grant finds herself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger when her groundbreaking invention attracts the attention of powerful forces. As she races against time to protect her creation and uncover the truth, Dr. Grant must navigate a treacherous world where nothing is as it seems and trust becomes a luxury she can't afford.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Technology and its consequences, Betrayal and deception
