Synopsis: 
'Garside's Career: A Comedy in Four Acts' follows the life of Arthur Garside, a middle-aged man who finds himself at a crossroads in his career. Faced with the prospect of losing his job, Garside embarks on a series of comical misadventures as he tries to navigate the ever-changing world of work. From hilarious encounters with eccentric colleagues to unexpected opportunities that test his resolve, Garside's journey is filled with laughter, surprises, and a touch of romance. Will he be able to reinvent himself and find success in a new career path, or will he be forever stuck in the rut of his old job?

Genres: Comedy, Drama

Themes: Career, Identity, Change
