Synopsis: In 'Squib and His Friends' by Evelyn Everett-Green, a young boy named Squib finds himself in a world of adventure and mystery when he befriends a group of extraordinary individuals. Together, they embark on a thrilling journey filled with secrets, danger, and unexpected twists. As Squib and his friends navigate through challenges and uncover hidden truths, they discover the power of friendship and the importance of embracing one's unique abilities.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Courage
