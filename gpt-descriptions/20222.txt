Synopsis: In "An Ethical Problem; or, Sidelights upon Scientific Experimentation on Man and Animals," Leffingwell delves into the dark and controversial world of scientific experimentation. The story follows a young scientist who becomes entangled in a web of moral dilemmas as he uncovers shocking secrets within his own laboratory. As he grapples with the ethical implications of his work, he must confront the consequences of his actions and make difficult choices that will shape the future of scientific research.

Genres: Science fiction, thriller, ethical fiction

Themes: Ethics, morality, scientific responsibility, the limits of experimentation
