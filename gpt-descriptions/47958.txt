Synopsis: In 'The Minister's Wooing', a young woman finds herself torn between two suitors - a charismatic, worldly doctor and a reserved, pious minister. Set in the early 19th century, this captivating novel explores the complexities of love, faith, and societal expectations, as the protagonist navigates the challenges of choosing between passion and duty.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Religious fiction

Themes: Love and duty, Faith and spirituality, Social expectations
