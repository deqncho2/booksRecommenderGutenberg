Synopsis:
In 'Vixen, Volume II' by M. E. Braddon, the captivating story continues as the enigmatic Vixen finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and forbidden desires. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, Vixen's journey takes her through the opulent ballrooms of London society, the treacherous streets of Paris, and the mysterious depths of her own heart. As she navigates the complexities of love, betrayal, and self-discovery, Vixen must confront the consequences of her past choices and make decisions that will shape her future. With each twist and turn, the reader is left eagerly anticipating the next revelation, wondering if Vixen will find redemption or succumb to the darkness that threatens to consume her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Desire, Identity and Self-Discovery, Morality and Consequences
