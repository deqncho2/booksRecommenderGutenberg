Synopsis: 

'Brave Old Salt; or, Life on the Quarter Deck: A Story of the Great Rebellion' by Optic, Oliver is a thrilling adventure set during the time of the American Civil War. The story follows the journey of a young sailor named Jack Somers, who finds himself caught up in the chaos and danger of the war at sea. As Jack navigates the treacherous waters of battle, he must confront his own fears and prove his bravery in the face of adversity. With vivid descriptions of naval battles, daring escapes, and unexpected alliances, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Bravery, Loyalty, Survival
