Synopsis: 
In 'The New Guide to Knitting & Crochet' by Marie Jane Cooper, a young woman named Emma discovers a hidden treasure trove of knitting and crochet patterns in her grandmother's attic. As she delves into the world of yarn and needles, Emma embarks on a journey of creativity, self-discovery, and connection with her family's past. With each stitch, she unravels the secrets of her grandmother's life and finds solace in the rhythmic art of knitting and crochet. Along the way, Emma encounters a diverse community of fellow crafters who teach her valuable lessons about friendship, perseverance, and the power of handmade creations. 

Genres: Fiction, Women's Fiction, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Family, Heritage, Creativity, Self-discovery, Friendship
