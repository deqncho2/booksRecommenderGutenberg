Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell, Jr., in Arizona; or, Clearing a Rival's Record' by Standish, Burt L., Frank Merriwell Jr. finds himself in the rugged and dangerous landscapes of Arizona. As he navigates through treacherous situations and encounters a rival with a tarnished reputation, Frank must use his wit, courage, and athletic prowess to clear his rival's name and uncover the truth. With unexpected twists and thrilling adventures, this story takes readers on a captivating journey through the Wild West.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Western

Themes: Friendship, Justice, Redemption
