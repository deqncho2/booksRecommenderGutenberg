Synopsis: In a future world where humanity has lost its connection with nature, a struggling writer named Gary discovers a mysterious green cat that seems to possess extraordinary powers. As he delves deeper into the cat's origins, Gary becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, leading him on a thrilling adventure that challenges his perception of reality and the true nature of the green millennium.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Environmentalism, Humanity's relationship with nature, Perception of reality
