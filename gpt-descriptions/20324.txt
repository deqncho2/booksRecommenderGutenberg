Synopsis: The Outdoor Girls at Bluff Point; Or a Wreck and a Rescue follows the thrilling adventures of a group of young girls as they find themselves caught up in a dangerous situation during their summer vacation. When a wreck occurs near their campsite, the girls must use their resourcefulness and bravery to come to the rescue. As they navigate through treacherous waters and face unexpected challenges, the girls learn the importance of teamwork, courage, and quick thinking. Will they be able to save the day and make it out unscathed?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resourcefulness
