Synopsis:
In the small English village of High Eldersham, a series of mysterious deaths occur, leaving the community in a state of fear and suspicion. When Scotland Yard detective Inspector Holly arrives to investigate, he finds himself entangled in a web of dark secrets and ancient rituals. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to unravel the very fabric of reality itself.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes:
Secrets and deception, Supernatural forces, Small-town intrigue
