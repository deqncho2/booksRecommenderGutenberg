Synopsis: In 'Criminal Man, According to the Classification of Cesare Lombroso', Lombroso, Gina delves into the fascinating world of criminal psychology, exploring the mind of the criminal and the factors that contribute to criminal behavior. Through meticulous research and analysis, Lombroso presents a groundbreaking classification system that challenges conventional notions of criminality. As the pages turn, readers are taken on a thought-provoking journey that unveils the complex nature of criminality and raises profound questions about the origins of deviant behavior.

Genres: Non-fiction, Psychology, Criminology

Themes: Criminal psychology, Deviant behavior, Nature vs. Nurture
