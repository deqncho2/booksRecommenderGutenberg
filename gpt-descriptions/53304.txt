Synopsis: 

In 'The Gourmet's Guide to London', Lieut.-Col. Newnham-Davis takes readers on a delectable journey through the culinary wonders of London. From the grandest restaurants to the hidden gems tucked away in the city's streets, this guide offers a tantalizing exploration of the capital's gastronomic delights. With vivid descriptions of mouthwatering dishes and insightful anecdotes about the city's culinary history, readers will be enticed to embark on their own culinary adventures in London.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, Food and Drink

Themes: Culinary exploration, London's gastronomy, Food culture
