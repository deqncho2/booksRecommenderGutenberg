Synopsis: In 'Pussy and Her Language', Clark and Grimaldi take readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious world of feline communication. Through their extensive research and personal experiences, they delve into the intricate language of cats, exploring the various sounds, gestures, and behaviors that cats use to communicate with humans and each other. From the subtle flick of a tail to the mesmerizing purrs, this book unveils the secrets behind the enigmatic language of our beloved feline companions. Prepare to be amazed and enlightened as you discover the hidden depths of Pussy's language.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Behavior, Communication

Themes: Animal-human connection, Understanding non-verbal communication
