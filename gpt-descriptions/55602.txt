Synopsis: 

'The Curiosities of Ale & Beer: An Entertaining History: (Illustrated with over Fifty Quaint Cuts)' takes readers on a fascinating journey through the rich and captivating history of ale and beer. From ancient civilizations to modern brewing techniques, this book delves into the intriguing stories, anecdotes, and curiosities surrounding these beloved beverages. With over fifty charming illustrations, readers will be transported to different eras and cultures, discovering the secrets and traditions behind the world's favorite drinks.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Culinary

Themes: Brewing history, Cultural significance of alcohol, Evolution of brewing techniques
