Synopsis: 

In 'Life and Sport in China: Second Edition' by Oliver George Ready, embark on a captivating journey through the vibrant world of China, where ancient traditions seamlessly blend with modernity. Through his immersive experiences, Ready unveils the fascinating intersection of life and sport in this diverse nation. From the bustling streets of Beijing to the serene landscapes of rural China, the author delves into the heart of Chinese culture, exploring the profound impact of sports on society, the pursuit of excellence, and the unbreakable spirit of its people. 

Genres: Travel, Sports, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Chinese culture, Sports and society, Pursuit of excellence
