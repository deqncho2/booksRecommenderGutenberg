Synopsis: In 'Siege of Washington, D.C., written expressly for little people', join a group of brave young friends as they navigate through a thrilling adventure set during the Civil War. As the capital city faces a dangerous siege, the children find themselves caught up in a secret mission that could change the course of history. With danger lurking at every corner, they must rely on their courage, wit, and friendship to overcome the challenges they encounter. Will they be able to save Washington, D.C. from the impending threat?

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Friendship, History, Patriotism
