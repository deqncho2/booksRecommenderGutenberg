Synopsis: In the 'Funny Alphabet: Uncle Franks' Series' by Edward P. Cogger, embark on a whimsical journey through the alphabet with Uncle Franks and his hilarious adventures. From mischievous monkeys to zany zebras, each letter comes to life in a delightful and unexpected way. With vibrant illustrations and clever wordplay, this series is sure to captivate readers of all ages and leave them eagerly anticipating the next letter.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book, Humor

Themes: Alphabet, Imagination, Playfulness
