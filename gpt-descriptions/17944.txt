Synopsis: In "The House of Rimmon: A Drama in Four Acts," Van Dyke takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of a group of individuals bound by love, secrets, and the pursuit of redemption. Set against the backdrop of a grand estate, the story unfolds as the characters navigate their complex relationships, hidden desires, and the consequences of their choices. As the drama unfolds, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of intrigue, passion, and ultimately, the search for personal salvation.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and betrayal, Redemption, Secrets and their consequences
