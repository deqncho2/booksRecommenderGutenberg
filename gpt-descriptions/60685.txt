Synopsis: In 'Downstream' by Siwertz, Sigfrid, a young woman named Eva finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the past, Eva uncovers a series of dark secrets that threaten to unravel her present and change her future forever. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. As Eva races against time to uncover the truth, she must confront her own fears and make choices that will determine her ultimate fate.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity, Redemption
