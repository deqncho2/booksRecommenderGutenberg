Synopsis: 

"The Flow of Time in the Connecticut Valley: Geological Imprints" takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient history of the Connecticut Valley, uncovering the secrets hidden within its geological formations. As the authors, George W. Bain and Howard A. Meyerhoff, delve into the depths of time, they reveal a fascinating narrative of the valley's formation, evolution, and the forces that shaped its breathtaking landscapes. From the remnants of ancient oceans to the dramatic impact of glaciers, this book unveils the remarkable story written in stone, inviting readers to explore the mysteries of the Earth's past.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Geology

Themes: Geological history, Natural landscapes, Environmental change
