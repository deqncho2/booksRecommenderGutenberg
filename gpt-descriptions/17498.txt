Synopsis: 
When Knighthood Was in Flower: or, the Love Story of Charles Brandon and Mary Tudor the King's Sister, and Happening in the Reign of His August Majesty King Henry the Eighth, is a captivating historical romance set in the tumultuous era of King Henry VIII. The story follows the forbidden love affair between Charles Brandon, a dashing knight, and Mary Tudor, the sister of the king. As their passionate relationship unfolds, they must navigate the treacherous court politics and face the wrath of a powerful monarch. Filled with intrigue, romance, and political drama, this tale transports readers to the opulent world of the Tudor court, where love and loyalty are tested in the face of power and ambition.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Forbidden Love, Court Intrigue, Power and Ambition
