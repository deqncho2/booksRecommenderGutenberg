Synopsis: In the haunting tale of 'Kerfol: 1916' by Edith Wharton, a young American woman finds herself drawn to the mysterious and eerie Kerfol estate in rural France. As she delves into the history of the property, she uncovers a series of chilling secrets and encounters the ghostly presence of its former inhabitants. With each revelation, the reader is left questioning the true nature of love, loyalty, and justice in this atmospheric and suspenseful story.

Genres: Gothic fiction, mystery, supernatural

Themes: Love and desire, justice and injustice, the power of the past
