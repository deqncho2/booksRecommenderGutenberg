Synopsis: In "Joan of Arc" by Laura Elizabeth Howe Richards, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of history's most enigmatic figures. This enthralling biography delves into the extraordinary tale of Joan of Arc, a young French peasant girl who defied societal norms and rose to become a symbol of courage and resilience. From her humble beginnings to her pivotal role in the Hundred Years' War, Richards skillfully weaves together a narrative that explores Joan's unwavering faith, her unwavering determination, and the profound impact she had on the world. Discover the remarkable story of a young woman who dared to challenge the status quo and became a legendary icon.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Faith, Resilience
