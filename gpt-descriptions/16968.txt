Synopsis: In 'The Bad Man: A Novel', Browne and Towne weave a gripping tale of mystery and suspense that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Set in a small town, the story follows a young woman named Emily who becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse with a notorious criminal known as "The Bad Man." As Emily delves deeper into the dark secrets of her town, she must confront her own fears and uncover the truth before it's too late. With unexpected twists and a relentless pace, this novel explores the depths of human nature and the lengths one will go to protect their loved ones.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Crime and justice, Fear and courage, Secrets and deception
