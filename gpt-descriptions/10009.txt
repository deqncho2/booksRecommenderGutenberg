Synopsis: 
In 'Wild Northern Scenes; Or, Sporting Adventures with the Rifle and the Rod', join the author on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of the North. From thrilling encounters with majestic wildlife to heart-pounding adventures in pursuit of game, this captivating narrative immerses readers in the beauty and danger of the great outdoors. With vivid descriptions and a keen eye for detail, Hammond paints a vivid picture of the rugged landscapes and the challenges faced by those who dare to explore them. Prepare to be transported to a world where nature reigns supreme and every step brings a new and unexpected adventure.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Nature Writing

Themes: Exploration, Wildlife, Survival
