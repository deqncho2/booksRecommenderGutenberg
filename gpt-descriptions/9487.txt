Synopsis: 

In the quaint English village of Slowbridge, the arrival of a young American girl named Bettina Vanderpoel turns the lives of the locals upside down. With her unconventional manners and refreshing outlook on life, Bettina captivates the hearts of everyone she meets, including the reserved Lord Mount Dunstan. As Bettina's presence challenges the traditional norms of the village, she brings about unexpected changes and sparks a delightful whirlwind of romance, humor, and cultural clashes.

Genres: Romance, Comedy, Fiction

Themes: Cultural differences, Love and relationships, Breaking societal norms
