Synopsis: In 'The Last Laugh' by Walton, Bryce, a renowned comedian finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when his jokes start coming true, leading him on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind this bizarre phenomenon. As he delves deeper into the dark secrets of his own past, he must confront the consequences of his words and the power of laughter itself. With unexpected twists and a race against time, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Comedy

Themes: Identity, Consequences of Actions, Power of Words
