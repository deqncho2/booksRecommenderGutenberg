Synopsis: In "The Strife of the Roses and Days of the Tudors in the West," author W. H. Hamilton takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of the Wars of the Roses and the rise of the Tudor dynasty. Set in the West of England, this historical account unveils the gripping tales of power struggles, political intrigue, and personal ambitions that shaped the destiny of England. From the fierce battles on the battlefield to the clandestine plots within the royal courts, Hamilton's narrative brings to life the captivating characters and events that defined this pivotal era in English history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Power struggles, Political intrigue, Loyalty, Ambition, Fate
