Synopsis: In the third volume of his captivating diary, Samuel Pepys takes us on a journey through the tumultuous month of February, from 1659 to 1660. As Pepys navigates the political landscape of Restoration England, we witness his encounters with influential figures, his secret affairs, and his insatiable curiosity for the world around him. From scandalous gossip to personal triumphs, this volume offers a fascinating glimpse into the life of one of history's most intriguing diarists.

Genres: Historical, Biography, Memoir

Themes: Politics, Personal Relationships, Curiosity
