Synopsis: 
'For Treasure Bound' by Collingwood, Harry is an exhilarating adventure novel that follows the daring exploits of a group of sailors who embark on a treacherous journey in search of hidden treasure. Set against the backdrop of the vast ocean, the story unfolds as the crew faces perilous challenges, encounters mysterious islands, and battles against ruthless pirates. As they navigate through uncharted waters, the crew's loyalty, courage, and determination are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, 'For Treasure Bound' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Maritime Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Loyalty, Survival
