Synopsis: In 'The Cities of Refuge: or, The Name of Jesus', author John R. Macduff takes young readers on a captivating journey through the biblical concept of cities of refuge. Through a series of engaging stories and thought-provoking lessons, Macduff explores the significance of these cities as a symbol of God's mercy and grace. As the young protagonists navigate their way through various challenges and dangers, they discover the power and protection found in the name of Jesus. This Sunday book for the young is filled with adventure, faith, and valuable life lessons that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Christian literature, Children's literature, Adventure

Themes: Mercy, Grace, Redemption, Faith, Protection
