Synopsis: In 'Turned Adrift' by Collingwood, Harry, a young sailor finds himself shipwrecked on a remote island after a violent storm. Alone and with limited resources, he must navigate the treacherous terrain and unravel the mysteries that lie within. As he explores the island, he encounters strange creatures, ancient ruins, and a hidden society that holds the key to his survival. With danger lurking at every turn, he must rely on his wits and courage to uncover the truth and find a way back home.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Discovery
