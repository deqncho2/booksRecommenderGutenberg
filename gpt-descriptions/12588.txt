Synopsis: 

In 'Cavour' by Martinengo-Cesaresco, Evelyn Lilian Hazeldine Carrington, contessa, readers are transported to 19th century Italy, where they follow the captivating life of Count Camillo di Cavour. From his humble beginnings as a young lawyer to his rise as a prominent statesman, Cavour's journey is filled with political intrigue, personal sacrifices, and a relentless pursuit of his vision for a unified Italy. As he navigates the complex web of alliances, rivalries, and power struggles, Cavour's determination and strategic brilliance become evident, making him a key figure in the unification of Italy. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Political drama

Themes: Ambition, Power, Nationalism, Diplomacy
