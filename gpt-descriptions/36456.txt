Synopsis: 
In 'The Autobiography of an Electron', the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the life of an electron, from its birth in a distant star to its role in the world of science. Written in a unique and engaging style, this autobiography explores the fascinating scientific ideas of the present time, presenting them in a way that is both informative and entertaining. As the electron recounts its experiences and encounters with various scientists, the reader gains a deeper understanding of the intricate workings of the universe and the fundamental principles that govern it. 

Genres: Science fiction, Biography, Popular science

Themes: Scientific discovery, Nature of reality, Evolution of scientific ideas
