Synopsis: In 'The Kiltartan History Book', Gregory, Lady takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and colorful history of the Kiltartan region. From ancient legends and mythical tales to the struggles and triumphs of its people, this book unveils the hidden stories that have shaped the land and its inhabitants. As the pages turn, readers will uncover fascinating anecdotes, historical events, and the unique customs that have defined Kiltartan throughout the ages. With a blend of fact and folklore, this captivating narrative paints a vivid picture of a community deeply rooted in its past, offering a captivating glimpse into a world that is both familiar and enchanting.

Genres: Historical fiction, Folklore, Non-fiction

Themes: Cultural heritage, Identity, Tradition
