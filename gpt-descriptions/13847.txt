Synopsis: 

'Slave Narratives: A Folk History of Slavery in the United States from Interviews with Former Slaves. Administrative Files: Selected Records Bearing on the History of the Slave Narratives' is a captivating and eye-opening collection of firsthand accounts from former slaves in the United States. This work, compiled by the United States Work Projects Administration, delves into the personal stories and experiences of those who endured the horrors of slavery. Through interviews and selected records, the book offers a unique perspective on the lives of these individuals, shedding light on the harsh realities they faced and the resilience they displayed. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Oral History

Themes: Slavery, Human resilience, Historical documentation
