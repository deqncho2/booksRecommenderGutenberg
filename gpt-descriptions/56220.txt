Synopsis: 

In "Historical record of the Seventh Regiment, or the Royal Fusiliers: Containing an Account of the Formation of the Regiment in 1685, and of Its Subsequent Services to 1846," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of the renowned Seventh Regiment. From its humble beginnings in 1685 to its remarkable achievements up until 1846, this book unveils the fascinating story of a regiment that has stood the test of time. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Cannon delves into the regiment's formation, its battles, and the brave men who served under its banner. This historical record is a testament to the indomitable spirit and unwavering dedication of the Royal Fusiliers.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military valor, Regimental history, Historical legacy
