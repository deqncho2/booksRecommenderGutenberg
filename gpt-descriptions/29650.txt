Synopsis: In 'The Greater Power' by Harold Bindloss, a mysterious and powerful force emerges in a small Canadian town, captivating the residents and stirring up a whirlwind of emotions and desires. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of secrets and hidden agendas, they must confront their own inner demons and make choices that will shape their lives forever. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this novel explores the depths of human nature and the consequences of power.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Power and its corrupting influence, Secrets and their consequences, Moral dilemmas
