Synopsis: 
In 'The Half-Hearted' by John Buchan, we follow the life of Lewis Haystoun, a young man torn between duty and desire. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, Lewis finds himself caught in a web of family expectations, political intrigue, and a forbidden love that threatens to consume him. As he navigates the treacherous waters of loyalty and passion, Lewis must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape his destiny. With secrets lurking around every corner, 'The Half-Hearted' is a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and the struggle to find one's true path.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Duty vs Desire, Family Expectations, Forbidden Love, Identity, Political Intrigue
