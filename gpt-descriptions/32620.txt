Synopsis:
In the enchanting world of the Mulla-mulgars, three peculiar creatures embark on a thrilling adventure to find their lost homeland. As they journey through mystical landscapes and encounter extraordinary beings, they must overcome numerous challenges and face their deepest fears. With their unique abilities and unwavering determination, the Mulla-mulgars discover the true meaning of family, friendship, and the power of hope.

Genres:
Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes:
Identity, Belonging, Courage, Resilience
