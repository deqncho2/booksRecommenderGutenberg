Synopsis: 
In "Recollections of a Confederate Staff Officer," G. Moxley Sorrel takes us on a captivating journey through his experiences as a staff officer during the American Civil War. From the early days of the conflict to the final moments of the Confederacy, Sorrel provides a unique perspective on the inner workings of the Confederate army, the strategies employed, and the personalities he encountered. With vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Sorrel offers a firsthand account of the triumphs, tragedies, and complexities of war, leaving readers eager to uncover the untold stories of this pivotal period in American history.

Genres: Historical memoir, Military history, Biography

Themes: War and its impact, Loyalty and duty, Leadership and strategy
