Synopsis: In 'The Square Book of Animals' by Arthur Waugh, embark on a whimsical journey through the animal kingdom like never before. This delightful collection of short stories introduces us to a variety of fascinating creatures, each with their own unique quirks and adventures. From mischievous monkeys to wise old elephants, from cunning foxes to graceful swans, these enchanting tales will captivate readers of all ages. With vivid descriptions and charming illustrations, 'The Square Book of Animals' is a delightful celebration of the wonders of the animal world.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Animal behavior, Imagination, Friendship
