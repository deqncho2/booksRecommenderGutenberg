Synopsis: In 'Weymouth New Testament in Modern Speech, 1 Peter', Richard Francis Weymouth presents a fresh and contemporary translation of the biblical book of 1 Peter. This rendition brings to life the timeless wisdom and teachings of Peter, one of Jesus' closest disciples, in a language that resonates with modern readers. Through his eloquent and accessible translation, Weymouth invites readers to delve into the profound messages of faith, perseverance, and hope that Peter imparts to early Christians facing persecution and challenges. This edition serves as a bridge between ancient wisdom and contemporary understanding, making the teachings of 1 Peter relevant and relatable to readers today.

Genres: Religious literature, Biblical translation, Christian non-fiction

Themes: Faith, Perseverance, Hope, Christian living
