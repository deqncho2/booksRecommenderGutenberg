Synopsis:
In "The Scarecrow of Oz," join Dorothy and her friends as they embark on a thrilling adventure to rescue a beloved character from the clutches of an evil sorceress. Along the way, they encounter enchanting creatures, magical lands, and face unexpected challenges that test their courage and loyalty. With danger lurking at every turn, will they be able to overcome the sorceress's wicked plans and bring peace back to the Land of Oz?

Genres:
Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes:
Friendship, Bravery, Good vs. Evil
