Synopsis: In 'Esmeralda' by Frances Hodgson Burnett, a young girl named Esmeralda finds herself caught between two worlds when she is taken from her humble home in Mexico and brought to England. As she navigates the unfamiliar customs and expectations of her new life, Esmeralda's spirit and resilience shine through, captivating those around her. With her unique perspective and unwavering determination, Esmeralda's journey becomes a testament to the power of identity, belonging, and the strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Identity, Belonging, Cultural differences, Resilience
