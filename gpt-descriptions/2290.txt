Synopsis: 
In the enchanting world of 'Twenty-Two Goblins', a collection of ancient Indian fairy tales, readers are transported to a realm where goblins, demons, and magical creatures roam freely. Each tale presents a unique and captivating story, filled with unexpected twists and turns that will leave readers spellbound. From clever tricksters to courageous heroes, these goblins bring to life a world of adventure, mystery, and wonder that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Folklore, Short Stories

Themes: 
Magic, Trickery, Morality, Courage
