Synopsis: 
In the heartwarming novel, 'Mrs. Wiggs of the Cabbage Patch' by Alice Caldwell Hegan Rice, readers are introduced to the lovable and resilient Mrs. Wiggs, a widowed mother struggling to make ends meet in the poverty-stricken Cabbage Patch. Despite the hardships she faces, Mrs. Wiggs remains determined to provide for her children and create a better life for them. Alongside her colorful and unforgettable neighbors, Mrs. Wiggs embarks on a journey filled with laughter, tears, and unexpected surprises that will touch the hearts of readers.

Genres: 
Drama, Fiction, Humor

Themes: 
Family, Resilience, Poverty, Community
