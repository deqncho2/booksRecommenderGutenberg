Synopsis: In "Mordred and Hildebrand: A Book of Tragedies" by Wilfred Campbell, readers are transported to a world of ancient legends and epic battles. This collection of tragedies delves into the lives of two iconic characters, Mordred and Hildebrand, exploring their complex journeys filled with love, betrayal, and the pursuit of power. As their destinies intertwine, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the consequences of their actions reverberate throughout the kingdom. With vivid imagery and poetic prose, Campbell weaves a captivating tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the fates of these legendary figures.

Genres: Poetry, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Power and Ambition, Fate and Destiny
