Synopsis: In 'Prisoners in Devil's Bog: A Skippy Dare Mystery Story', Skippy Dare finds himself in the midst of a thrilling adventure when he and his friends stumble upon a hidden secret in the treacherous Devil's Bog. As they uncover clues and face dangerous obstacles, Skippy must use his wit and bravery to solve the mystery and save the day. With suspenseful twists and unexpected turns, this captivating mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
