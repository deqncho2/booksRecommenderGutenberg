Synopsis: In 'Our Pirate Hoard: 1891' by Thomas A. Janvier, a thrilling tale unfolds as a group of adventurers embarks on a perilous journey to uncover a hidden treasure buried by notorious pirates. Set in the late 19th century, this captivating story takes readers on a thrilling quest filled with danger, mystery, and unexpected twists. As the characters navigate treacherous waters and encounter a host of challenges, they must rely on their wit and courage to outsmart their adversaries and claim the coveted pirate hoard. Will they succeed in their quest, or will the secrets of the past remain buried forever?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Treasure Hunting, Exploration, Bravery, Betrayal
