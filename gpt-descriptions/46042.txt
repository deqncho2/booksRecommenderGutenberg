Synopsis: In 'Our Little Arabian Cousin', we follow the captivating journey of a young Arabian girl named Suleima. Living in the enchanting land of Arabia, Suleima's life is filled with vibrant colors, rich traditions, and exciting adventures. From exploring bustling marketplaces to witnessing breathtaking desert landscapes, Suleima's world is a tapestry of beauty and wonder. As she navigates the complexities of her culture and encounters unexpected challenges, Suleima's resilience and determination shine through. Through her eyes, we gain a deeper understanding of the Arabian way of life and the importance of family, friendship, and embracing one's heritage.

Genres: Children's Literature, Cultural Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Cultural identity, Family, Friendship, Adventure
