Synopsis: 

In "Lafayette, We Come!: The Story of How a Young Frenchman Fought for Liberty in America and How America Now Fights for Liberty in France" by Rupert Sargent Holland, embark on a captivating historical journey that intertwines the lives of two nations fighting for freedom. This gripping narrative follows the remarkable story of a young Frenchman, Lafayette, who played a pivotal role in the American Revolution, and the subsequent reciprocal support between America and France during times of turmoil. As the pages turn, readers will be immersed in the thrilling accounts of battles, political intrigue, and the enduring bond between these two nations.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Biography, War

Themes: Freedom, Patriotism, Alliance, Revolution
