Synopsis: In "Light and Peace: Instructions for devout souls to dispel their doubts and allay their fears," Carlo Giuseppe Quadrupani offers a profound and enlightening guide for those seeking solace and spiritual growth. Through a series of insightful instructions, the author explores the doubts and fears that often plague devout souls, providing practical advice and encouragement to overcome them. With a gentle and compassionate tone, Quadrupani invites readers on a transformative journey towards finding inner peace and deepening their faith.

Genres: Spiritual, Self-help, Inspirational

Themes: Faith, Doubt, Fear, Inner peace, Spiritual growth
