Synopsis: In 'Home Rule: Second Edition' by Harold Spender, a mysterious manuscript is discovered, revealing a hidden world of political intrigue and personal secrets. As the protagonist delves deeper into the manuscript's contents, they become entangled in a web of power struggles, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each turn of the page, the boundaries between reality and fiction blur, leaving the reader questioning the true nature of the manuscript and its implications for the present. This gripping tale of discovery and deception will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Politics, Secrets, Identity
