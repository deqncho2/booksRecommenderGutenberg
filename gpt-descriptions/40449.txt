Synopsis: In 'The Woman with One Hand, and Mr. Ely's Engagement' by Richard Marsh, a mysterious woman with a missing hand captures the attention of Mr. Ely, a wealthy and eligible bachelor. As their paths intertwine, secrets are revealed and unexpected events unfold, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful climax. This gripping tale explores the complexities of love, identity, and the power of appearances.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Romance

Themes: Identity, Deception, Love and Desire
