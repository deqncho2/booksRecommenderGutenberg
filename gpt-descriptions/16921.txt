Synopsis: In 'Plague Ship' by Andre Norton, a deadly disease threatens to wipe out an entire planet, and a desperate race against time begins. When a group of interstellar traders unknowingly becomes carriers of the deadly plague, they must find a way to contain the outbreak and save themselves and the inhabitants of the planet they inadvertently infected. As they navigate treacherous waters and face unexpected challenges, the crew must confront their own fears and make difficult choices to prevent the spread of the disease and find a cure. With danger lurking at every turn, 'Plague Ship' is a thrilling tale of survival, sacrifice, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Epidemic, Survival, Morality
