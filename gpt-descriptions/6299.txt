Synopsis: In 'Carnac's Folly, Complete' by Parker Gilbert, a young man named Carnac is faced with a life-altering decision that could either lead him to greatness or utter ruin. As he navigates through a web of secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances, Carnac's journey takes him from the bustling streets of Paris to the treacherous landscapes of the Canadian wilderness. With his reputation and future hanging in the balance, Carnac must confront his own demons and make choices that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Ambition, Redemption, Identity
