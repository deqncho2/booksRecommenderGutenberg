Synopsis: In 'More Cargoes: 1897', W.W. Jacobs takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of short stories that explore the unexpected twists and turns of life. From the bustling streets of London to the treacherous seas, each tale presents a unique and intriguing scenario that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. With his signature wit and skillful storytelling, Jacobs delves into the lives of ordinary people facing extraordinary circumstances, revealing the surprising outcomes that await them. Prepare to be enthralled by this delightful collection of tales that will leave you pondering the unpredictability of fate.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Fate and destiny, Unexpected consequences, Everyday life
