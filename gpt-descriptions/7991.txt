Synopsis: In the small town of Kalinov, a storm is brewing, both literally and figuratively. As the rain pours down, the lives of the townspeople become entangled in a web of secrets, desires, and forbidden love. Amidst the chaos, a young woman named Katerina finds herself torn between duty and passion, leading to a series of events that will forever change the lives of those involved.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Tragedy

Themes: Forbidden love, societal expectations, moral dilemmas, the power of desire
