Synopsis: In "The Jolliest Term on Record: A Story of School Life" by Angela Brazil, readers are transported to a lively and eventful term at a boarding school. Follow the adventures of a group of spirited students as they navigate friendships, rivalries, and unexpected challenges. From secret societies to daring escapades, this delightful tale captures the essence of school life and the bonds that form within its walls.

Genres: Fiction, Young Adult, School Stories

Themes: Friendship, Adventure, Coming of Age
