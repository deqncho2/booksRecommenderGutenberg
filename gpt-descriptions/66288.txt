Synopsis: In 'The Fifty-Fourth of July' by Alan Edward Nourse, a young boy named Johnny wakes up on the morning of July 4th to find that everyone in his town has mysteriously vanished. With no adults around, Johnny and his friends embark on an adventure to uncover the truth behind the disappearance and save their town from an unexpected threat. As they navigate through empty streets and encounter strange occurrences, they must rely on their resourcefulness and courage to unravel the mystery and bring back their loved ones.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Independence, Friendship, Courage, Survival
