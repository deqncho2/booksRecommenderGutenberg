Synopsis: In "Burgoyne's Invasion of 1777: With an outline sketch of the American Invasion of Canada, 1775-76," Samuel Adams Drake takes readers on a captivating journey through the lesser-known events of the American Revolution. With meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Drake delves into the audacious British invasion led by General John Burgoyne and the daring American counteroffensive in Canada. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the strategic maneuvers, unexpected alliances, and the resilience of both sides in this pivotal chapter of history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, American Revolution

Themes: War and conflict, Strategy and tactics, National identity, Resilience and determination
