Synopsis: In 'The New York Obelisk: Cleopatra's Needle', Charles E. Moldenke takes readers on a captivating journey through the history, erection, uses, and significance of obelisks, with a particular focus on the iconic Cleopatra's Needle in New York. Moldenke delves into the fascinating story behind the transportation of this ancient Egyptian artifact to the United States, shedding light on the challenges faced and the mysteries surrounding its origins. Through a preliminary sketch, the author unveils the symbolic and cultural importance of obelisks throughout history, offering readers a deeper understanding of these enigmatic structures and their enduring allure.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Archaeology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Cultural significance, Symbolism
