Synopsis: In 'The Dreadnought Boys on a Submarine' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey aboard a mysterious submarine. As they navigate the depths of the ocean, they encounter unexpected dangers and uncover a hidden secret that could change the course of history. With their courage and resourcefulness put to the test, the boys must work together to overcome the perils that lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Bravery
