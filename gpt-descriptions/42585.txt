Synopsis: 
'The Ballads & Songs of Derbyshire: With Illustrative Notes, and Examples of the Original Music, etc.' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich musical heritage of Derbyshire. This collection of ballads and songs, accompanied by insightful notes and examples of the original music, offers a fascinating glimpse into the cultural tapestry of the region. From tales of love and loss to stories of local legends and historical events, each piece carries the essence of Derbyshire's past, resonating with the emotions and experiences of its people. Whether you are a lover of folk music, a history enthusiast, or simply curious about the traditions of Derbyshire, this book promises to transport you to a world of captivating melodies and captivating stories.

Genres: Folklore, Music, History
Themes: Cultural heritage, Local legends, Historical events
