Synopsis: 
In 'Australian Writers' by Byrne, Desmond, embark on a captivating journey through the rich and diverse literary landscape of Australia. From the bustling streets of Sydney to the vast outback, this book introduces readers to a collection of talented Australian writers who have shaped the nation's literary identity. Through their stories, poems, and essays, these writers explore themes of identity, belonging, and the complex relationship between humans and the Australian landscape. Discover the hidden gems of Australian literature and gain a deeper understanding of the country's cultural heritage.

Genres: 
Literary criticism, Australian literature, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Identity, Belonging, Nature and landscape
