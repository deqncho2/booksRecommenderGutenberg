Synopsis: In 'The Cosmic Deflector', a brilliant scientist discovers a mysterious device that has the power to alter the course of celestial bodies. As he delves deeper into its secrets, he becomes entangled in a thrilling race against time to prevent a catastrophic event that could change the fate of the entire universe. With unexpected twists and mind-bending revelations, this gripping science fiction adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Time manipulation, Existentialism, Consequences of scientific discovery
