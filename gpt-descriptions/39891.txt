Synopsis: In 'Jewel Mysteries, from a Dealer's Note Book' by Pemberton, Max, a renowned gem dealer finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue and danger when he stumbles upon a rare and mysterious jewel. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the gem, he must navigate a treacherous world of thieves, con artists, and rival collectors, all vying for the priceless treasure. With his reputation and life on the line, the dealer must use his expertise and cunning to solve the enigma of the jewel before it consumes him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Greed, Deception, Intrigue
