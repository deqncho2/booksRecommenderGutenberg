Synopsis: In 'By Violence' by John Trevena, a small, idyllic village is shaken to its core when a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a dark secret from the past. As the villagers become entangled in a web of deceit, suspicion, and violence, they must confront their own demons and make choices that will forever change their lives. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
