Synopsis: In the remote and hauntingly beautiful landscape of Dartmoor, a young woman named Urith finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger. As she delves into the secrets of her past, she uncovers a dark family history that threatens to consume her. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, Urith must navigate treacherous paths and face her deepest fears in order to uncover the truth and reclaim her own destiny.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Historical fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Redemption, Supernatural elements
