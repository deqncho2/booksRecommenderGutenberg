Synopsis: In the town of Hamelin, plagued by a relentless infestation of rats, a mysterious stranger arrives with a promise to rid the town of its vermin problem. However, when the townspeople refuse to fulfill their end of the bargain, they soon discover the dire consequences of their actions. This haunting and enigmatic tale explores the power of promises, the consequences of betrayal, and the price of greed.

Genres: Poetry, Fairy Tale, Allegory

Themes: Betrayal, Consequences, Greed
