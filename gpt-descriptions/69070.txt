Synopsis: In 'The Isle of Retribution' by Marshall Edison, a group of strangers find themselves stranded on a mysterious island after a shipwreck. As they struggle to survive, they soon discover that the island holds dark secrets and a malevolent force that seeks retribution. With danger lurking at every turn, the survivors must unravel the island's mysteries and confront their own pasts before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Survival, Redemption, Revenge
