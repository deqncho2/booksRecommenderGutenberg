Synopsis: In 'Angelot: A Story of the First Empire', Eleanor C. Price takes readers on a captivating journey through a world of magic, adventure, and political intrigue. Set in the First Empire, the story follows Angelot, a young orphan with a mysterious past, as he navigates the treacherous landscape of power-hungry rulers, ancient prophecies, and a secret society that holds the key to his destiny. As Angelot discovers his own extraordinary abilities, he must confront his inner demons and make choices that will shape the fate of the empire. With vivid world-building and compelling characters, this epic tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Political Intrigue

Themes: Destiny, Power, Identity, Redemption
