Synopsis: In 'The Coxon Fund' by Henry James, a wealthy and eccentric American woman decides to establish a fund to support a brilliant young man, but her intentions and the consequences of her actions take unexpected turns. As the story unfolds, the characters navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and the power dynamics that arise when money is involved. With James' signature psychological depth and intricate storytelling, 'The Coxon Fund' explores the intricacies of human relationships and the unpredictable nature of human desires.

Genres: Fiction, Psychological Fiction, Literary Fiction

Themes: Wealth and Power, Love and Relationships, Ambition and Success
