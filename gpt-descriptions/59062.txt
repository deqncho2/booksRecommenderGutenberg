Synopsis: 
'Peru as It Is, Volume 1' is a captivating travelogue written by Archibald Smith, chronicling his experiences during his residence in Lima and other parts of the Peruvian Republic. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Smith provides a fascinating account of the social and physical features of Peru, offering readers a unique glimpse into the country's culture, landscapes, and people.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Natural beauty, Social observations
