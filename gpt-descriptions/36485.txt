Synopsis: In 'The Camp Fire Girls on the Open Road; Or, Glorify Work' by Hildegard G. Frey, a group of adventurous Camp Fire Girls embark on a thrilling journey across the country. As they travel from one destination to another, they encounter unexpected challenges, make new friends, and learn valuable life lessons. From camping in the wilderness to exploring bustling cities, the girls discover the true meaning of hard work, perseverance, and the importance of embracing new experiences. Will their bond as Camp Fire Girls be enough to overcome the obstacles they face along the open road?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Work ethic
