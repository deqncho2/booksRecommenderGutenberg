Synopsis: 

In 'Diary of Richard Cocks, Volume 1: Cape-Merchant in the English Factory in Japan, 1615-1622, with Correspondence', we are transported back to the early 17th century, where Richard Cocks, an English merchant, finds himself in the midst of a fascinating and unfamiliar world. Through his detailed diary entries and correspondence, we witness his encounters with the Japanese culture, their customs, and the intricate web of political and economic relationships that shape his experiences. From navigating the complexities of trade to forming unlikely friendships, Cocks' diary offers a captivating glimpse into a time and place where East meets West.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Memoir

Themes: Cultural exchange, Trade and commerce, Exploration and discovery
