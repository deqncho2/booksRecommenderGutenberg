Synopsis: In "The Jesus of History," T.R. Glover delves into the enigmatic life of Jesus Christ, exploring the historical context and shedding light on the man behind the religious figure. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Glover presents a compelling narrative that challenges conventional beliefs and invites readers to reconsider their understanding of Jesus' life and teachings. From his humble beginnings to his transformative impact on society, this thought-provoking exploration offers a fresh perspective on one of history's most influential figures.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Religious studies

Themes: Historical context, Religious interpretation, Transformation
