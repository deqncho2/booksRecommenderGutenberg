Synopsis: 
"The Backwash of War: The Human Wreckage of the Battlefield as Witnessed by an American Hospital Nurse" takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of an American nurse stationed in a military hospital during World War I. Through vivid and poignant anecdotes, the nurse provides a raw and unfiltered account of the physical and emotional toll of war on soldiers and civilians alike. From the chaos of the battlefield to the struggles of medical personnel, this book offers a unique perspective on the devastating consequences of war.

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, War literature

Themes: War, Human suffering, Medical care, Psychological trauma
