Synopsis: The Outdoor Girls at Rainbow Lake; Or, The Stirring Cruise of the Motor Boat Gem takes readers on an exciting adventure with a group of young girls as they embark on a thrilling journey on Rainbow Lake. When the girls acquire a motor boat called Gem, they set out to explore the picturesque lake, unaware of the mysteries and dangers that await them. As they navigate through treacherous waters and encounter unexpected obstacles, the girls must rely on their resourcefulness, courage, and teamwork to overcome the challenges that come their way. Will they be able to unravel the secrets of Rainbow Lake and make it back safely?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Independence, Courage
