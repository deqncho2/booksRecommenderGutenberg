Synopsis: In "The Salving of the "Fusi Yama": A Post-War Story of the Sea," Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a thrilling maritime adventure set in the aftermath of a devastating war. When the crew of the salvage vessel "Fusi Yama" embarks on a dangerous mission to recover a sunken treasure, they soon find themselves facing unexpected challenges and life-threatening situations. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter mysterious adversaries, the crew must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and teamwork to survive. Will they be able to salvage the treasure and overcome the perils that lie in their path?

Genres: Adventure, Maritime Fiction, Post-War Fiction

Themes: Courage, Survival, Camaraderie, Redemption
