Synopsis: In 'When the Birds Begin to Sing' by Winifred Graham, a mysterious event shatters the tranquility of a small English village, leaving its residents questioning their reality. As the birds start to sing an eerie melody, strange occurrences unfold, and the community becomes consumed by fear and suspicion. Amidst the chaos, a young woman named Alice finds herself at the center of the unfolding mystery, desperately seeking answers and discovering secrets that will change her life forever. This gripping tale explores the boundaries of perception, the power of secrets, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Secrets and their Consequences, Resilience
