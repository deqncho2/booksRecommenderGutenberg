Synopsis: In 'Camp Fire Girls in War and Peace' by Isabel Hornibrook, a group of young girls find themselves caught up in the tumultuous events of World War I. As they navigate the challenges of war, they also discover the power of friendship, bravery, and the importance of making a difference. From aiding soldiers on the front lines to supporting their community back home, these Camp Fire Girls prove that even in the darkest times, hope and resilience can prevail.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Community, Resilience, Making a Difference
