Synopsis: In "Affairs of State: Being an Account of Certain Surprising Adventures Which Befell an American Family in the Land of Windmills," the Stevenson family embarks on a journey to the enchanting land of windmills, where they find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected and thrilling adventures. From secret societies to hidden treasures, the family's encounters with eccentric characters and mysterious events will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As they navigate through the unfamiliar landscapes and unravel the secrets of this foreign land, the Stevensons discover the true meaning of courage, resilience, and the power of family bonds.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Family

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Differences, Resilience, Family Bonds
