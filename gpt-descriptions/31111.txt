Synopsis: 

'Across Asia on a Bicycle' is an exhilarating true story that follows the incredible journey of two American students, Allen and Sachtleben, as they embark on a daring adventure from Constantinople to Peking. Fueled by their thirst for exploration and a desire to experience the world beyond their comfort zones, the duo sets out on an epic bicycle ride across the vast and diverse landscapes of Asia. From traversing treacherous mountain ranges to navigating through bustling cities, they encounter a myriad of challenges and unforgettable encounters along the way. This gripping narrative captures the spirit of adventure, resilience, and the unbreakable bond of friendship that propels them forward, making it a captivating read for all.

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Resilience
