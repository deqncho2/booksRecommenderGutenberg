Synopsis: 

In 'Report of Mr. W. E. Cormack's journey in search of the Red Indians in Newfoundland,' William Eppes Cormack takes us on a thrilling expedition through the uncharted wilderness of Newfoundland in the early 19th century. Driven by a deep curiosity and a desire to understand the indigenous people of the region, Cormack embarks on a perilous quest to find the elusive Red Indians. As he navigates treacherous terrains, encounters unexpected dangers, and delves into the rich cultural heritage of the indigenous communities, Cormack's journey becomes a captivating tale of exploration, discovery, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Indigenous cultures, Survival
