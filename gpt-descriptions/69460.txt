Synopsis: In "Rulers of Kings," Atherton weaves a captivating tale of power, love, and betrayal set in the opulent world of European royalty. As the young and ambitious Princess Ziska navigates the treacherous waters of court politics, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and seduction. With her intelligence and cunning, she must outmaneuver her rivals and secure her place as the ruler of her own destiny. But as secrets unravel and loyalties are tested, Ziska must confront the ultimate question: What price is she willing to pay for the throne?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Intrigue

Themes: Power, Ambition, Love, Betrayal
