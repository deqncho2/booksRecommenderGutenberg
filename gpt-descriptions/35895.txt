Synopsis: In 'The Gospel of Buddha, Compiled from Ancient Records', Carus, Paul takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and teachings of the revered spiritual leader, Siddhartha Gautama. Drawing from ancient records, this enlightening compilation unveils the profound wisdom and transformative experiences that shaped the Buddha's path to enlightenment. From his humble beginnings as a prince to his encounters with various individuals seeking truth, the narrative delves into the Buddha's teachings on compassion, self-discovery, and the nature of existence. Through vivid storytelling and thought-provoking anecdotes, Carus invites readers to explore the timeless teachings of the Buddha and discover the universal truths that lie within.

Genres: Religious literature, Philosophy, Biography

Themes: Enlightenment, Compassion, Self-discovery, Spiritual teachings
