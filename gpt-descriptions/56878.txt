Synopsis: 
'First Love, and Other Stories' by Ivan Turgenev is a collection of poignant and evocative tales that explore the complexities of human emotions and relationships. From the innocence and turbulence of first love to the bittersweet nostalgia of lost opportunities, Turgenev weaves together a tapestry of captivating stories that delve into the depths of the human soul. Through vivid characters and vivid settings, the reader is transported to a world where love, longing, and the passage of time intertwine to create a rich and unforgettable reading experience.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Literary Fiction

Themes: Love, Loss, Coming of Age, Regret, Nostalgia
