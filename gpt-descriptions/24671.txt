Synopsis: 
In 'Making a Garden of Perennials', Egan takes readers on a captivating journey through the art of creating a stunning perennial garden. With vivid descriptions and practical advice, he unveils the secrets to transforming any outdoor space into a vibrant and ever-blooming oasis. From selecting the perfect plants to designing harmonious color schemes, Egan's expertise shines through every page, inspiring both novice and experienced gardeners alike. As the seasons unfold, readers will witness the magic of nature as their own perennial garden flourishes and evolves into a breathtaking masterpiece.

Genres: Gardening, Non-fiction

Themes: Horticulture, Landscape design, Nature's beauty
