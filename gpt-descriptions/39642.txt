Synopsis: 
'Kashmir, described by Sir Francis Younghusband, painted by Major E. Molyneux' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of Kashmir. Sir Francis Younghusband, a renowned explorer, provides vivid descriptions of the region's breathtaking beauty, while Major E. Molyneux's stunning paintings bring these descriptions to life. From the majestic Himalayan peaks to the serene Dal Lake, the book immerses readers in the awe-inspiring charm of Kashmir. As the narrative unfolds, readers will discover the rich cultural heritage, the vibrant markets, and the warm hospitality of the Kashmiri people. This book is a visual and literary masterpiece that transports readers to a land of wonder and leaves them yearning to experience the magic of Kashmir firsthand.

Genres: Travelogue, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Natural beauty, Cultural heritage
