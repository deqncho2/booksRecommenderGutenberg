Synopsis: In 'The Inner Flame: A Novel' by Clara Louise Burnham, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. Set in the early 20th century, Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery as she navigates the complexities of love, family, and personal fulfillment. As she grapples with the choices before her, Emily must confront the inner flame that burns within her, guiding her towards a path that challenges convention and embraces her true self.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Love and Relationships, Societal Expectations
