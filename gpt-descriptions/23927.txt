Synopsis: In 'A Tame Surrender, A Story of The Chicago Strike' by Charles King, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the Chicago Strike. Amidst the chaos and tension, the lives of two individuals from different social backgrounds intertwine, leading them on a journey of self-discovery, love, and sacrifice. As the strike escalates, they find themselves caught between loyalty to their respective classes and the desire for a better future. Will they succumb to the pressures of society or will they find the courage to challenge the status quo?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Class struggle, Love and sacrifice, Social justice
