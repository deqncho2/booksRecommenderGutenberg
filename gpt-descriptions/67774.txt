Synopsis:
In the small village of Feversham, a mysterious young woman named Anne arrives, captivating the townspeople with her beauty and enigmatic presence. As she becomes entangled in the lives of the villagers, secrets from the past begin to unravel, leading to unexpected consequences. With its richly drawn characters and a touch of romance, 'Anne Feversham' takes readers on a journey of intrigue and self-discovery that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres:
Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes:
Identity, Secrets, Redemption
