Synopsis: In "The River Motor Boat Boys on the Colorado; Or, The Clue in the Rocks" by Harry Gordon, a group of adventurous boys embarks on a thrilling journey along the Colorado River. As they navigate the treacherous waters, they stumble upon a mysterious clue hidden within the rocks. Determined to uncover the truth, they find themselves entangled in a dangerous pursuit that will test their courage and resourcefulness. Will they be able to solve the puzzle before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Perseverance
