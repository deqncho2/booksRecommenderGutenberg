Synopsis: In 'A Chicago Princess' by Barr, Robert, a young woman named Amelia finds herself torn between her duty as a princess and her desire for freedom. Born into a life of privilege and luxury, Amelia is expected to follow a predetermined path, but her rebellious spirit yearns for something more. As she navigates the bustling streets of Chicago, Amelia discovers a world filled with unexpected friendships, thrilling adventures, and the possibility of true love. Will she have the courage to break free from her royal obligations and forge her own destiny?

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Identity, Freedom, Love, Duty
