Synopsis: 
"The Complete Plays of Gilbert and Sullivan" is a delightful collection of witty and whimsical theatrical works by the renowned duo, Gilbert and Sullivan. From the hilarious antics of the Pirates of Penzance to the satirical social commentary of The Mikado, this compilation takes readers on a journey through a series of captivating and comical musical plays. Each play is filled with memorable characters, catchy tunes, and clever wordplay, making it a true feast for the senses. Whether it's the absurdity of mistaken identities or the absurdity of societal norms, these plays are sure to entertain and leave readers with a smile on their face.

Genres: Comedy, Musical, Satire

Themes: Social commentary, Love and romance, Identity and mistaken identity
