Synopsis: On the planet Pyrrus, life is a constant battle for survival. The planet's hostile environment has shaped its inhabitants into fierce warriors, capable of taking on any threat. When a renowned gambler named Jason dinAlt arrives on Pyrrus, he finds himself caught in a deadly game where the stakes are higher than he could have ever imagined. As he navigates the treacherous landscape and encounters the planet's deadly creatures, Jason must use his wits and cunning to stay alive and uncover the secrets of Pyrrus.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Evolution, Human Nature
