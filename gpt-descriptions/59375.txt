Synopsis: In the small town of Ethica, a group of ordinary citizens find themselves caught in a web of secrets and moral dilemmas when a mysterious newcomer arrives. As they navigate the complexities of truth, justice, and personal ethics, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to a shocking revelation that will challenge everything they thought they knew about themselves and their community.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Morality, Truth, Consequences, Community
