Synopsis: 

In 'Dead Men Tell No Tales' by E. W. Hornung, a mysterious and enigmatic character named Jack Rattenbury finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and danger. As a former sailor turned smuggler, Rattenbury's past catches up with him when he becomes the target of a relentless pursuit. With his life on the line, he must navigate treacherous waters, outsmart his pursuers, and uncover the truth behind a hidden treasure. As the stakes rise, Rattenbury's cunning and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Survival
