Synopsis: 
In 'Bliss, and Other Stories', Katherine Mansfield takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of short stories that explore the complexities of human emotions and relationships. From a woman's unexpected realization about her own happiness to a chance encounter that leads to a life-changing decision, each story delves into the depths of human experiences, unveiling the hidden desires, fears, and vulnerabilities that lie beneath the surface. With her exquisite prose and keen observations, Mansfield invites readers to witness the intricacies of everyday life and the profound impact that seemingly ordinary moments can have on one's sense of self and understanding of the world.

Genres: 
Literary Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: 
Human emotions, Relationships, Self-discovery, Everyday life
