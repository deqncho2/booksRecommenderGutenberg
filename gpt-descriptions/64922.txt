Synopsis: 

'Pyrotechnics: The History and Art of Firework Making' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of fireworks. From ancient civilizations to modern-day spectacles, author Alan St. H. Brock delves into the rich history, intricate techniques, and awe-inspiring artistry behind the creation of these explosive displays. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, the book explores the evolution of pyrotechnics, revealing the secrets behind the mesmerizing colors, shapes, and patterns that light up the night sky. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Art

Themes: Creativity, Cultural traditions, Technological advancements
