Synopsis: In 'The Kingdom of the Yellow Robe: Being Sketches of the Domestic and Religious Rites and Ceremonies of the Siamese', Ernest Young takes readers on a captivating journey into the heart of Siamese culture. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Young explores the intricate tapestry of domestic and religious life in Siam, offering a unique glimpse into the customs, traditions, and beliefs of its people. From elaborate ceremonies to everyday rituals, this book paints a rich and immersive portrait of a fascinating kingdom.

Genres: Travelogue, Cultural Exploration, Anthropology

Themes: Cultural diversity, Religious practices, Traditional customs
