Synopsis: 

In "The Battle of New Orleans: including the Previous Engagements between the Americans; and the British, the Indians and the Spanish which led to; the Final Conflict on the 8th of January, 1815," author Z. F. Smith takes readers on a thrilling journey through the events that culminated in one of the most significant battles in American history. From the early clashes between American settlers and Native tribes to the tense encounters between the British and Spanish forces, the stage is set for a dramatic showdown in New Orleans. As the final conflict approaches, tensions rise, alliances are tested, and the fate of a nation hangs in the balance. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, American history

Themes: War and conflict, National identity, Leadership and strategy
