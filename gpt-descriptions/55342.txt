Synopsis: 
'Pine Tree Ballads: Rhymed Stories of Unplaned Human Natur' up in Maine' by Day, Holman is a collection of captivating and humorous tales set in the picturesque state of Maine. From the misadventures of eccentric characters to the unexpected twists and turns of everyday life, this book takes readers on a delightful journey through the quirks and idiosyncrasies of human nature. Each rhymed story offers a unique glimpse into the lives of the people who call Maine home, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover what unexpected surprises await them next.

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Humor

Themes: Human nature, Quirkiness, Everyday life
