Synopsis: In "A Tree with a Bird in it: A Symposium of Contemporary American Poets on Being Shown a Pear-tree on Which Sat a Grackle," Margaret Widdemer takes us on a poetic journey through the perspectives of various American poets as they encounter a pear-tree with a grackle perched on it. Each poet brings their unique voice and interpretation to this simple yet captivating scene, offering profound insights into nature, beauty, and the human experience. As we delve into the symposium, we are invited to explore the depths of our own perceptions and contemplate the interconnectedness of all things.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology

Themes: Nature, Beauty, Perception, Interconnectedness
