Synopsis: In 'Boy Scouts on the Open Plains; Or, The Round-Up Not Ordered' by Ralphson, G. Harvey, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey across the vast open plains. As they navigate through treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected challenges, the Scouts must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and survival skills to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. With danger lurking at every turn, the Scouts must prove their mettle and demonstrate the true meaning of bravery and resilience. Will they be able to conquer the open plains and complete their mission? Find out in this action-packed tale of courage and determination.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Action

Themes: Bravery, Survival, Teamwork
