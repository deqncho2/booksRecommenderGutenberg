Synopsis: 
'Over the Rocky Mountains: Wandering Will in the Land of the Redskin' takes readers on an exhilarating adventure through the untamed wilderness of the American West. Follow the courageous protagonist, Will, as he navigates treacherous landscapes, encounters Native American tribes, and faces unexpected challenges. With danger lurking at every turn, Will's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. This thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover what lies beyond the next mountain peak.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural Encounter
