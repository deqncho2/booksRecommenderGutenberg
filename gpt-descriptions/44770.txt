Synopsis:
In 'Daisy's Work: The Third Commandment' by Joanna H. Mathews, readers are introduced to Daisy, a young woman with a mysterious past who finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deceit. As she delves deeper into her work, Daisy uncovers shocking revelations that threaten to unravel her world and those around her. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Daisy's enigmatic existence.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes:
Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
