Synopsis: In 'A treatise of the cohabitacyon of the faithfull with the vnfaithfull', Bullinger and Vermigli explore the complex dynamics of living alongside those who do not share the same faith. They delve into the challenges, conflicts, and potential resolutions that arise when believers and non-believers coexist. Additionally, the authors present a sermon on the importance of confessing Christ and his gospel, as well as the consequences of denying them. This thought-provoking work offers insights into the intricacies of faith and its impact on interpersonal relationships.

Genres: Theological treatise, Sermon

Themes: Faith and doubt, Interfaith relationships, Confession and denial of Christ, Religious tolerance
