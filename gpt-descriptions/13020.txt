Synopsis: In 'The Boy Allies at Verdun; Or, Saving France from the Enemy', the young heroes Jack and Frank find themselves in the midst of the intense battle of Verdun during World War I. As they navigate the treacherous trenches and face the constant threat of enemy forces, they must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to fulfill their mission and help turn the tide of the war. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys' determination to save France becomes a thrilling adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Heroism
