Synopsis: 
'Folk-Tales of the Khasis' by Rafy, Mrs. is a captivating collection of traditional stories from the Khasi tribe, an indigenous community in Northeast India. Through enchanting narratives and vivid imagery, the book takes readers on a journey into a world filled with mythical creatures, magical encounters, and timeless wisdom. From tales of brave warriors and cunning tricksters to stories of forbidden love and supernatural beings, each story unveils the rich cultural heritage and deep-rooted beliefs of the Khasi people. With its blend of folklore, adventure, and moral lessons, this collection promises to transport readers to a realm where imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Cultural heritage, Wisdom, Supernatural beings
