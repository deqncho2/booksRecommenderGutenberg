Synopsis: In 'The Blind Beggar of Jericho' by Mortimer, Favell Lee, a blind beggar named Bartimaeus finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure. As he embarks on a thrilling journey through the ancient city of Jericho, Bartimaeus must rely on his wits and the help of unexpected allies to uncover the truth behind the treasure and protect it from those who would stop at nothing to possess it. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Redemption, Friendship
