Synopsis: In 'Spies Die Hard!' by Marmor, Arnold, a retired spy finds himself pulled back into the dangerous world of espionage when a long-lost enemy resurfaces with a deadly plan. With his skills and wit put to the ultimate test, he must navigate a web of deceit, betrayal, and high-stakes action to save not only his own life but also the lives of countless innocent people. As the clock ticks and the tension rises, the line between friend and foe becomes blurred, leaving our protagonist questioning who he can truly trust.

Genres: Thriller, Action, Mystery

Themes: Espionage, Betrayal, Trust
