Synopsis: In 'Room Number 3, and Other Detective Stories' by Anna Katharine Green, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through a collection of captivating detective stories. From a mysterious disappearance in a hotel room to a perplexing case of stolen jewels, each story presents a unique puzzle that will keep readers guessing until the very end. With clever twists and unexpected turns, Green's masterful storytelling will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to uncover the truth behind each enigma.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Crime Fiction

Themes: Investigation, Deception, Justice
