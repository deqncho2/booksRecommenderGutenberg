Synopsis: 

'Geographic Range of the Hooded Skunk, Mephitis macroura: With Description of a New Subspecies from Mexico' takes readers on an intriguing journey into the world of the hooded skunk, a fascinating creature found in various regions. The authors, E. Raymond Hall and Walter Woelber Dalquest, present a detailed exploration of the skunk's geographic range, shedding light on its distribution and habitat. Along the way, they unveil the discovery of a new subspecies in Mexico, adding an element of excitement and novelty to the narrative. 

Genres: Scientific research, zoology, natural history

Themes: Biodiversity, animal classification, species discovery
