Synopsis: In 'The Trade Union Woman' by Alice Henry, we follow the journey of a determined young woman named Mary who finds herself at the forefront of the labor movement in early 20th century America. As she navigates the challenges of organizing workers and fighting for their rights, Mary must also confront personal sacrifices and societal expectations. With her unwavering spirit and resilience, she becomes a symbol of hope and empowerment for women in the labor movement.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Social Justice, Feminism

Themes: Gender Equality, Labor Rights, Activism, Sacrifice
