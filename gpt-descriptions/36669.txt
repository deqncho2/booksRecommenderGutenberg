Synopsis: 
'Knitting, Crochet, and Netting, with Twelve Illustrations' by Riego de la Branchardière, Eléonore is a captivating tale that follows the life of Eléonore, a young woman with a passion for needlework. As she navigates the intricacies of her craft, Eléonore discovers that her skills hold the power to weave together not only beautiful garments but also the threads of her own destiny. Through her journey, she encounters unexpected challenges, finds solace in the company of fellow artisans, and uncovers the transformative nature of creativity. With twelve stunning illustrations, this book invites readers into a world where artistry and self-discovery intertwine.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Craft and Hobby

Themes: Artistic expression, Self-discovery, Community, Perseverance
