Synopsis: 
In 'How to Make Electrical Machines', R.A.R. Bennett takes readers on an electrifying journey into the world of electrical engineering. This comprehensive guide provides step-by-step instructions for constructing a wide range of electrical machines, from induction coils to dynamos, as well as intriguing toys powered by electricity. With clear directions and detailed diagrams, Bennett empowers readers to delve into the fascinating realm of electrical engineering and create their own functional devices. Whether you're a curious hobbyist or an aspiring engineer, this book will spark your imagination and ignite your passion for all things electrical.

Genres: Non-fiction, DIY, Engineering

Themes: Electrical engineering, Creativity, Scientific exploration
