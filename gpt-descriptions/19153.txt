Synopsis: 
In 'A New System; or, an Analysis of Antient Mythology. Volume 1', Jacob Bryant takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient world, unraveling the mysteries of mythology and shedding light on the origins of various legends. From the gods and goddesses of Greece and Rome to the ancient civilizations of Egypt and Persia, Bryant's meticulous analysis and insightful interpretations bring these ancient tales to life. As readers delve into the pages of this volume, they will discover a wealth of fascinating information, connecting the dots between different mythologies and uncovering the underlying truths hidden within these ancient stories. With each chapter, Bryant's narrative becomes more enthralling, leaving readers eager to explore the subsequent volumes and delve deeper into the captivating world of ancient mythology.

Genres: Mythology, History, Analysis

Themes: Origins of mythology, Comparative mythology, Ancient civilizations
