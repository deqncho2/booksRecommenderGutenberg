Synopsis: 
'Prairie Smoke, a Collection of Lore of the Prairies' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vast and enchanting prairies. In this collection, Melvin R. Gilmore weaves together a tapestry of captivating stories, myths, and legends that have been passed down through generations. From tales of mysterious creatures lurking in the shadows to the extraordinary adventures of brave pioneers, this book offers a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of the prairies. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to a world where the boundaries between reality and folklore blur, leaving them spellbound and eager to uncover the secrets hidden within the prairie's depths.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cultural heritage, Legends and myths, Exploration and adventure
