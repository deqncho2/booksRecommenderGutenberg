Synopsis:
In 'Lawrence' by S. L. Bensusan, a mysterious and enigmatic man named Lawrence enters the lives of a small English village, captivating the residents with his charm and intelligence. As the villagers become increasingly fascinated by Lawrence, they soon discover that there is much more to him than meets the eye. With secrets and hidden motives, Lawrence's presence in the village sets off a chain of events that will challenge the perceptions and beliefs of everyone involved.

Genres:
Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes:
Identity, Deception, Manipulation
