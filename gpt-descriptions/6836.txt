Synopsis:
In 'Three Men and a Maid', three bachelors find their lives turned upside down when a mysterious baby girl is left on their doorstep. As they struggle to navigate the challenges of parenthood, their lives become entangled with a beautiful and enigmatic young woman. Hilarity ensues as misunderstandings, mistaken identities, and romantic entanglements abound, leading to a series of uproarious and unpredictable events.

Genres:
Comedy, Romance, Farce

Themes:
Love and relationships, Parenthood, Friendship
