Synopsis: In "The Friendly Daemon, or the Generous Apparition," the renowned Dr. Duncan Campbel, who is both deaf and dumb, experiences a miraculous cure through the intervention of a mysterious familiar spirit. This spirit appears to him in the form of a cathedral singing boy, wearing a white surplice. As the story unfolds, the reader is taken on a journey of wonder and astonishment as the spirit's presence brings about a transformation in Dr. Campbel's life. 

Genres: Fiction, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: Miracles, Communication, Transformation
