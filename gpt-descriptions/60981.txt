Synopsis: In 'The Useless Bugbreeders' by Stamers, James, a group of misfit teenagers discover a hidden talent for breeding and training bugs with extraordinary abilities. As they delve deeper into this secret world, they uncover a dark conspiracy that threatens not only their newfound skills but also the entire bugbreeding community. With danger lurking at every turn, the teenagers must navigate treacherous alliances, face their own insecurities, and harness the power of their bugbreeding abilities to save themselves and those they care about.

Genres: Young Adult, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Coming of age, Friendship, Overcoming adversity, Identity, Power and responsibility
