Synopsis: 

In 'Cattle and Their Diseases: Embracing Their History and Breeds, Crossing and Breeding, and Feeding and Management; with the Diseases to Which They Are Subject, and the Remedies Best Adapted to Their Cure', author Robert Jennings delves into the fascinating world of cattle, exploring their history, breeds, and the various challenges they face. From the intricacies of crossing and breeding to the essential aspects of feeding and management, this comprehensive guide offers valuable insights into the world of cattle farming. Moreover, Jennings provides an in-depth examination of the diseases that afflict these animals and offers remedies that are best suited to their cure. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Agriculture, Veterinary Medicine

Themes: Animal husbandry, Livestock management, Disease prevention and treatment
