Synopsis: In 'An Ideal Husband' by Oscar Wilde, a seemingly perfect politician's life is thrown into turmoil when a mysterious woman threatens to expose a dark secret from his past. As the story unfolds, the characters navigate a web of deceit, blackmail, and moral dilemmas, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Satire

Themes: Morality, Corruption, Marriage, Appearance vs. Reality
