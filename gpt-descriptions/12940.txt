Synopsis: In 'The Chums of Scranton High out for the Pennant: or, In the Three Town League' by Ferguson, Donald, a group of determined high school friends from Scranton find themselves in a thrilling baseball adventure as they compete in the intense Three Town League. As they face formidable opponents and encounter unexpected challenges, the chums must rely on their teamwork, resilience, and unwavering spirit to pursue their dreams of winning the pennant. With each game, the stakes get higher, and the bonds between the friends grow stronger. Will they be able to overcome the odds and emerge victorious?

Genres: Sports, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Competition
