Synopsis: In 'The Gayton Scholarship: A School Story' by Hayens, Herbert, a group of students at Gayton School find themselves embroiled in a mysterious competition for a prestigious scholarship. As they navigate the challenges and secrets that surround the scholarship, friendships are tested, alliances are formed, and unexpected twists and turns keep the reader guessing until the very end. This captivating school story explores the lengths people will go to achieve their goals and the power of determination and teamwork.

Genres: Mystery, Young Adult, School

Themes: Competition, Friendship, Secrets, Determination
