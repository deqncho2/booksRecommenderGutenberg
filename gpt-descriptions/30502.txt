Synopsis: In 'The Gorgeous Isle: A Romance; Scene-- Nevis, B.W.I. 1842', Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton takes readers on a captivating journey to the beautiful island of Nevis in the year 1842. Amidst the lush landscapes and vibrant culture, a tale of love, mystery, and intrigue unfolds. As the story unravels, secrets are revealed, and the characters find themselves entangled in a web of passion and danger. With its vivid descriptions and compelling narrative, this novel promises to transport readers to a world filled with romance and suspense.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Love and Desire, Secrets and Betrayal, Cultural Identity
