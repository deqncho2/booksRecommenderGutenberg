Synopsis: 

'Victorian Worthies: Sixteen Biographies' by George Henry Blore takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of sixteen remarkable individuals who shaped the Victorian era. From influential politicians and pioneering scientists to renowned artists and social reformers, this collection of biographies offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives and achievements of these extraordinary figures. Each biography delves into the personal struggles, triumphs, and contributions of these Victorian worthies, painting a vivid portrait of their impact on society and their enduring legacies. 

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Achievement, Influence, Legacy
