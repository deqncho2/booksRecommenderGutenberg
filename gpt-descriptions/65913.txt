Synopsis: 

In "Tirant lo Blanch; a study of its authorship, principal sources and historical setting," Joseph Anthony Vaeth delves into the fascinating world of one of the most renowned works of medieval literature. This comprehensive study explores the origins, influences, and historical context surrounding the creation of "Tirant lo Blanch," captivating readers with its intriguing revelations and meticulous research. Vaeth uncovers the enigmatic authorship of this epic tale, shedding light on the principal sources that inspired its creation. From the political landscape of 15th-century Europe to the chivalric traditions of the time, this study provides a captivating exploration of the historical setting that shaped the narrative. 

Genres: Literary analysis, historical research, medieval studies

Themes: Authorship, historical context, chivalry, political intrigue
