Synopsis:
In 'The Adventures of Dr. Thorndyke: (The Singing Bone)', renowned detective Dr. Thorndyke finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious bone with a haunting melody is discovered. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that will challenge his intellect and put his life at risk. With each twist and turn, Dr. Thorndyke must use his keen observation skills and scientific expertise to unravel the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres:
Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes:
Deception, Deduction, Justice
