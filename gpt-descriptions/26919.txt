Synopsis: In 'The New Education: A Review of Progressive Educational Movements of the Day', Nearing, Scott takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolving landscape of education in 1915. From the bustling classrooms of urban schools to the serene settings of rural learning institutions, Nearing explores the innovative approaches and philosophies that are reshaping the way we educate our youth. With vivid descriptions and thought-provoking analysis, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the future of education, challenging traditional methods and inspiring readers to reimagine the possibilities of learning.

Genres: Education, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Progressive education, Educational reform, Innovation in teaching
