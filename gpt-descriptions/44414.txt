Synopsis: In "The Mormon Prophet and His Harem: Or, An Authentic History of Brigham Young, His Numerous Wives and Children," author C. V. Waite delves into the intriguing and controversial life of Brigham Young, the second president of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. This captivating account unveils the hidden world of Young's polygamous relationships, shedding light on the complex dynamics within his harem and the challenges faced by his many wives and children. Through meticulous research and compelling storytelling, Waite offers a fascinating exploration of a man who held immense power and influence, while also grappling with the complexities of love, faith, and personal ambition.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Polygamy, Religion, Power and Influence, Gender Roles, Family Dynamics
