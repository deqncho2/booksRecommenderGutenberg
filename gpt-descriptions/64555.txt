Synopsis: In 'Lodore, Vol. 1', Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of a remarkable mother and daughter. As we delve into their intertwined stories, we witness the struggles they face in a society that restricts their ambitions and desires. From the picturesque landscapes of England to the political turmoil of America, this novel explores the complexities of love, family, and the pursuit of personal freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Bildungsroman

Themes: Gender roles, Social expectations, Individualism, Motherhood
