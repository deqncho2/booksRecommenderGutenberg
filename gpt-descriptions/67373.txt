Synopsis: 
In 'The Worst Joke in the World' by Elisabeth Sanxay Holding, a seemingly harmless prank takes a dark turn when it leads to unexpected consequences. As a group of friends gather for a weekend getaway, they decide to play a practical joke on one of their own. However, the joke quickly spirals out of control, revealing hidden secrets and testing the limits of friendship. With tension mounting and trust shattered, the group must confront the consequences of their actions and face the truth that some jokes can have devastating effects.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Consequences of Actions, Trust, Friendship
