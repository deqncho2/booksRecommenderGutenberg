Synopsis: In 'The Aeroplane', Grahame-White and Harper take us on a thrilling journey through the skies, exploring the exhilarating world of aviation. From daring aerial stunts to heart-stopping races, this captivating tale follows the protagonist's quest to conquer the skies and push the boundaries of human flight. As the story unfolds, unexpected challenges and personal sacrifices test the limits of their passion for aviation, leading to a breathtaking climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Ambition, Courage, Innovation, Risk-taking
