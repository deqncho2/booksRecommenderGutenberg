Synopsis: In 'Myths and Legends of Christmastide' by Bertha F. Herrick, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting tales and folklore surrounding the holiday season. From ancient customs and traditions to mythical creatures and magical beings, this book unveils the hidden stories that have shaped the celebration of Christmas throughout history. Delve into the origins of beloved traditions like Santa Claus and the Christmas tree, and discover the mystical legends that have been passed down through generations. With each page, the reader is transported to a world where the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, leaving them spellbound by the rich tapestry of myths and legends that have come to define Christmastide.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, History

Themes: Tradition, Magic, Cultural Heritage
