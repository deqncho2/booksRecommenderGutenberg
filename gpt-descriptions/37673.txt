Synopsis: In 'Ned Wilding's Disappearance; or, The Darewell Chums in the City' by Chapman, Allen, a group of adventurous friends find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery when their companion, Ned Wilding, suddenly vanishes in the bustling city. Determined to uncover the truth, the Darewell Chums navigate through a web of clues, unexpected encounters, and dangerous situations, all while facing their own fears and insecurities. As they delve deeper into the mystery, the Chums discover that Ned's disappearance is just the tip of the iceberg, leading them on a suspenseful journey that will test their friendship and courage.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Mystery-solving, Coming of Age
