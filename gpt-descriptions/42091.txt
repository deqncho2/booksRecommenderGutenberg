Synopsis: In 'A Floating Home', a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young couple embarks on a journey to find a new beginning. Their search leads them to a unique and enchanting floating home, where they soon discover that the house holds secrets and a history that intertwines with their own lives. As they delve deeper into the mysteries of the floating home, they are faced with unexpected challenges and must confront their own fears and desires. With each twist and turn, the couple becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that will test their love and resilience. 

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Love and relationships, Self-discovery
