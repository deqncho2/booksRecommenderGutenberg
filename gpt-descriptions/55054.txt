Synopsis: 
'The Commerce and Navigation of the Erythræan Sea' takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient trade routes of the Erythræan Sea, as described by an anonymous writer in the Periplus Maris Erythræi. This translation also includes Arrian's account of the voyage of Nearkhos, from the mouth of the Indus to the head of the Persian Gulf. Through vivid descriptions and detailed accounts, the book unveils the fascinating world of maritime commerce, exotic destinations, and the challenges faced by ancient seafarers. From bustling ports to treacherous waters, readers will be transported to a time when the Erythræan Sea was a hub of trade and cultural exchange.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Maritime literature
Themes: Trade and commerce, Exploration, Ancient civilizations
