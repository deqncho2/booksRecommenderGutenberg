Synopsis: In 'The Republic of Costa Rica' by Gustavo Niederlein, a young journalist embarks on a journey to uncover the hidden secrets of a small Central American country. As he delves deeper into the heart of Costa Rica, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, corruption, and personal discovery. With each revelation, the journalist finds himself questioning his own beliefs and values, ultimately leading him to confront the dark underbelly of power and the true meaning of justice.

Genres: Mystery, Political Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Corruption, Power, Identity, Justice
