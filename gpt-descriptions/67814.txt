Synopsis: 

In 'Mother Goose in Gridiron Rhyme: A collection of alphabets, rhymes, tales and jingles: With 80 illustrations', the Gridiron Club from Washington, D.C. presents a delightful and humorous twist on the classic Mother Goose nursery rhymes. This collection features a clever adaptation of beloved rhymes, tales, and jingles, all infused with a unique gridiron theme that will leave readers chuckling and entertained. With 80 illustrations accompanying the witty verses, this book offers a fresh and playful take on traditional nursery rhymes that will captivate both children and adults alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Poetry, Humor

Themes: Nursery Rhymes, Gridiron (Football) Theme, Humor
