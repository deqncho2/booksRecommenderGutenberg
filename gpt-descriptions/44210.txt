Synopsis: 
In 'The Angel of Pain' by E. F. Benson, a mysterious and alluring woman arrives in a small English village, captivating the hearts and minds of the locals. As her presence becomes more prominent, the villagers find themselves drawn into a web of secrets, desires, and forbidden passions. With each passing day, the line between reality and fantasy blurs, leading to unexpected consequences that will forever change the lives of those involved.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Forbidden love, Deception, Obsession, Small-town secrets
