Synopsis: In 'The Young Castellan: A Tale of the English Civil War' by George Manville Fenn, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the English Civil War. The story revolves around a young castellan, or castle governor, named Cyril, who finds himself caught in the midst of the conflict. As the war rages on, Cyril must navigate treacherous alliances, face personal challenges, and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With danger lurking at every turn, the young castellan's courage and resilience are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Bravery, Identity, Conflict
