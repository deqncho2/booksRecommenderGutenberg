Synopsis: In 'How to Camp Out' by Gould, John Mead, a group of adventurous friends embarks on a camping trip that takes an unexpected turn. As they navigate through the wilderness, they encounter thrilling challenges and mysterious encounters that test their survival skills and deepen their bond. With each passing day, they uncover hidden secrets and learn valuable lessons about nature, friendship, and the resilience of the human spirit. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Self-discovery
