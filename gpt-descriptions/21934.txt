Synopsis: In "The Gold Of Fairnilee" by Andrew Lang, a young man named Walter Scott embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient treasure hidden in the Scottish Borders. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he encounters a series of enchanting and perilous adventures, including encounters with mythical creatures and encounters with love. With each step, Walter's determination grows, but so does the danger that surrounds him. Will he be able to unlock the secrets of Fairnilee and claim the gold that lies within?

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Quest for treasure, Mythology and folklore, Love and sacrifice
