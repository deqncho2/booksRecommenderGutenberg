Synopsis: In 'Seekers in Sicily: Being a Quest for Persephone by Jane and Peripatetica', two adventurous women embark on a captivating journey to the enchanting island of Sicily. Fueled by their shared love for Greek mythology, they find themselves entangled in a mysterious quest to uncover the truth about the mythological figure Persephone. As they delve deeper into the island's rich history and encounter unexpected challenges, their quest takes on a life of its own, leading them to discover hidden secrets and confront their own personal demons. With vivid descriptions of Sicily's breathtaking landscapes and a touch of magic, this captivating tale will transport readers into a world where ancient myths and modern-day adventures intertwine.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Mythology

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Mythology, Personal growth
