Synopsis: 
In 'Inhibition' by James Causey, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but the fate of the entire world. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal to protect his invention and those he holds dear. This gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of scientific progress and the lengths some will go to control it.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Scientific ethics, Conspiracy, Power struggle
