Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 133, March 27, 1915: Won by Magic; or, Nick Carter's Mysterious Ear,' renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving a mysterious ear. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Carter uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected twists that challenge his deductive skills and push him to the limits. With magic and intrigue at every turn, this thrilling story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Detective work, Deception, Intrigue
