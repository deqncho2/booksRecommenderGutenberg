Synopsis: In 'Moth and Rust; Together with Geoffrey's Wife and The Pitfall', Mary Cholmondeley weaves together four captivating tales that explore the complexities of human relationships and the consequences of our choices. From a forbidden love affair to a mysterious pitfall that threatens to unravel a family, these interconnected stories delve into the depths of human emotions and the unexpected twists that life can bring. As secrets are revealed and lives are forever changed, Cholmondeley's masterful storytelling keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Love and betrayal, Consequences of actions, Family dynamics, Secrets and revelations
