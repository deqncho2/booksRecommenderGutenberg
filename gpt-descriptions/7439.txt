Synopsis: 
'English Fairy Tales' is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that will transport readers to a world filled with magical creatures, brave heroes, and treacherous villains. From mischievous fairies to cunning witches, this book presents a delightful array of tales that will ignite the imagination and leave readers eagerly turning the pages. Each story is a delightful blend of adventure, mystery, and whimsy, promising to captivate readers of all ages and take them on a journey through the rich tapestry of English folklore.

Genres:
Fairy tales, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes:
Magic, Good vs. Evil, Heroism
