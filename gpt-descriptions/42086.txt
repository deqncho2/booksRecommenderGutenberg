Synopsis: In "The Boy Scouts at the Panama-Pacific Exposition" by John Henry Goldfrap, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery while attending the grand Panama-Pacific Exposition in San Francisco. As they explore the wonders of the exposition, they stumble upon a series of puzzling events that lead them on a quest to uncover the truth behind a hidden secret. With their resourcefulness, bravery, and determination, the Boy Scouts must navigate through a web of intrigue and danger to solve the mystery and protect the exposition from a potential threat.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Problem-solving, Patriotism
