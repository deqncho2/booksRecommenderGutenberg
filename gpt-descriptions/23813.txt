Synopsis: In 'A Newly Discovered System of Electrical Medication' by Clark, Daniel, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking method of healing using electricity. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden world of possibilities and dangers that could change the course of medicine forever. With his life and reputation on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path of discovery, facing unexpected consequences and moral dilemmas along the way. This electrifying tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of science and the power of innovation.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Medical Drama

Themes: Scientific discovery, Morality, Ethical dilemmas
