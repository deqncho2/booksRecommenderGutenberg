Synopsis: In the small, seemingly ordinary town of Muskrat City, a series of mysterious events begins to unfold, turning the lives of its residents upside down. As secrets are revealed and tensions rise, the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and deception. With each passing day, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving everyone questioning their own sanity. As the truth slowly unravels, the fate of Muskrat City hangs in the balance, and its inhabitants must confront their darkest fears in order to survive.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery, Reality vs. illusion
