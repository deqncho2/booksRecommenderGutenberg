Synopsis: In 'Raw Gold: A Novel' by Sinclair, Bertrand W., a young and ambitious geologist named Emma embarks on a treacherous journey to the heart of the Amazon rainforest in search of a legendary gold mine. As she delves deeper into the lush and dangerous wilderness, Emma encounters a cast of intriguing characters, including a mysterious guide and a charismatic explorer, each with their own hidden agendas. As the stakes rise and tensions mount, Emma must navigate the treacherous landscape of greed, betrayal, and survival, all while facing her own inner demons. 'Raw Gold' is a gripping adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of humanity and the lengths one will go to for wealth and power.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Greed, Betrayal, Survival
