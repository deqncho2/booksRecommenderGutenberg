Synopsis: In 'Meeting of the Minds' by Robert Sheckley, a group of individuals find themselves trapped in a mysterious and surreal world where their thoughts and desires manifest into reality. As they navigate this bizarre realm, they must confront their deepest fears and confront the consequences of their own imaginations. With each encounter, the line between reality and fantasy blurs, leading to unexpected and mind-bending revelations.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Perception of reality, Power of the mind, Confronting fears, Existentialism
