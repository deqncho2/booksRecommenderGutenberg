Synopsis: In "The Web, a Multilingual Encyclopedia" by Marie Lebert, embark on a captivating journey through the vast expanse of the internet, where languages intertwine and knowledge knows no boundaries. This enthralling work explores the fascinating world of online encyclopedias, delving into the challenges faced by those who strive to create a multilingual platform that unites people from diverse cultures and backgrounds. As the story unfolds, readers will be captivated by the intricate web of human connections, the power of collaboration, and the ever-evolving nature of information in the digital age.

Genres: Non-fiction, Technology, Linguistics

Themes: Multilingualism, Cultural diversity, Collaboration, Information sharing
