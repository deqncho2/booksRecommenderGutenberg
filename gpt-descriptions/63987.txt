Synopsis: In 'Wreck Off Triton' by Alfred Coppel, a group of survivors find themselves stranded on a remote island after their ship is wrecked during a violent storm. As they struggle to survive in this harsh and unforgiving environment, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to a thrilling battle for survival against both the elements and each other. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Survival

Themes: Human nature, Survival instincts, Betrayal
