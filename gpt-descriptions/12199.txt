Synopsis: In "The Chase of Saint-Castin and Other Stories of the French in the New World," Mary Hartwell Catherwood takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of North America during the French colonial period. From the thrilling pursuit of a notorious French-Indian leader to the mysterious encounters with Native American tribes, this collection of stories offers a vivid portrayal of the challenges and adventures faced by the French settlers in the New World. With each tale, Catherwood weaves together elements of history, romance, and suspense, transporting readers to a time when the clash of cultures and the pursuit of dreams shaped the destiny of a continent.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration and colonization, Cultural clashes, Survival and resilience
