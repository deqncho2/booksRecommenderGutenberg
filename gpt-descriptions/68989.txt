Synopsis: 
Step back in time and explore the hidden treasures of London through a captivating collection of early drawings and pictures. Delve into the rich history of the city as you uncover the intricate details of its iconic landmarks and bustling streets. But this collection holds more than just artistic depictions of London; it also offers a glimpse into the world of contemporary furniture, showcasing the evolving tastes and styles of the time. As you peruse the pages of this catalogue, prepare to be transported to a bygone era, where art and design intertwine to tell the story of a vibrant city. 

Genres: Art, History, Catalogue

Themes: London history, Artistic representation, Evolution of furniture design
