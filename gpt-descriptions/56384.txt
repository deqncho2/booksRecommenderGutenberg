Synopsis: 

In "Investigation of Communist activities in Seattle, Wash., Area, Hearings, Part 2," the United States Congress House Committee on Un-American Activities delves into a gripping investigation that uncovers a web of clandestine activities in the heart of Seattle. As the hearings progress, shocking revelations emerge, exposing the hidden agendas and covert operations of individuals suspected of communist affiliations. With each testimony, the committee uncovers a complex network of intrigue, betrayal, and ideological conflict that threatens the very fabric of American society. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Political Thriller, Historical

Themes: Cold War, McCarthyism, Ideological Conflict, Betrayal, Government Surveillance
