Synopsis: 
In 'Spices and How to Know Them', Walter M. Gibbs takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of spices. From the exotic markets of India to the ancient spice routes of the Middle East, Gibbs explores the history, origins, and uses of a wide variety of spices. Through vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, he reveals the secrets behind these aromatic treasures, delving into their cultural significance and the impact they have had on global trade and cuisine. With mouth-watering recipes and practical tips, this book is a sensory delight that will leave readers eager to explore the vibrant world of spices.

Genres: Non-fiction, Food and Drink, Travel

Themes: Culinary exploration, Cultural significance, Global trade
