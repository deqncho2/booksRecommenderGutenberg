Synopsis: 
Heart of Darkness by Joseph Conrad takes readers on a captivating journey into the depths of the African Congo, where the protagonist, Marlow, embarks on a mission to find the enigmatic ivory trader, Kurtz. As Marlow navigates the treacherous river, he encounters the darkness that resides within the human soul, confronting the horrors of colonialism and the fragility of civilization. Through vivid descriptions and haunting encounters, Conrad explores the depths of human nature and the moral ambiguity that arises when confronted with the unknown.

Genres: 
Adventure, Psychological Fiction, Colonial Literature

Themes: 
The darkness within human nature, the destructive nature of imperialism, the struggle between civilization and savagery, the exploration of moral ambiguity.
