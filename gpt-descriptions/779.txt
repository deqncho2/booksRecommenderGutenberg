Synopsis: In 'The Tragical History of Doctor Faustus: From the Quarto of 1604', we are introduced to the brilliant and ambitious Doctor Faustus, a scholar who becomes disillusioned with the limitations of human knowledge. Desperate for power and eternal knowledge, he makes a dangerous pact with the devil, Mephistopheles. As Faustus delves deeper into the dark arts, he experiences both the exhilaration of his newfound abilities and the torment of his impending damnation. This gripping tale explores the consequences of unchecked ambition and the eternal struggle between good and evil.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama, Supernatural

Themes: Ambition, Knowledge, Morality, Good vs. Evil
