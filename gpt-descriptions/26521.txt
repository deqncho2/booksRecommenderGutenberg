Synopsis: In 'Earthmen Bearing Gifts' by Fredric Brown, a mysterious alien arrives on Earth with a peculiar gift for humanity. As the gift spreads across the globe, it brings both wonder and chaos, forcing humanity to confront their deepest desires and fears. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, a small group of individuals must unravel the truth behind the alien's intentions before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Alien encounters, Human nature, Consequences of power
