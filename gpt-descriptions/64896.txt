Synopsis: 
"The Men of the Merchant Service: Being the polity of the mercantile marine for 'longshore readers'" takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of the merchant navy. Through a series of engaging stories and anecdotes, Frank Thomas Bullen provides a vivid and insightful portrayal of the men who navigate the vast oceans, facing the perils and challenges of life at sea. From thrilling adventures to heartwarming tales of camaraderie, this book offers a unique glimpse into the lives of those who dedicate themselves to the merchant service.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Maritime literature

Themes: Life at sea, Camaraderie, Perseverance, Adventure, Human resilience
