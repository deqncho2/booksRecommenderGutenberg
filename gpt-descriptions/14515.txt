Synopsis: In 'The Nervous Child' by Hector Charles Cameron, a young boy named Oliver finds himself plagued by unexplainable fears and anxieties that threaten to consume him. As his parents struggle to understand his condition, they embark on a journey to uncover the truth behind Oliver's nervousness. With each revelation, the family is forced to confront their own fears and insecurities, ultimately leading them to a profound understanding of the power of love and resilience. 

Genres: Psychological thriller, family drama, coming-of-age

Themes: Mental health, family dynamics, resilience
