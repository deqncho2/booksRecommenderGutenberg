Synopsis: 

In 'Is Tomorrow Hitler's? 200 Questions on the Battle of Mankind', H. R. Knickerbocker delves into the depths of human history and poses thought-provoking questions that challenge our understanding of the past, present, and future. From exploring the rise and fall of empires to examining the impact of technology on society, this book takes readers on a captivating journey through time, raising crucial questions about the nature of power, the fragility of civilization, and the potential for both progress and destruction. With each question, Knickerbocker invites readers to reflect on the lessons of history and consider the implications for our own uncertain future.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Philosophy

Themes: Power and its consequences, Human nature, Historical cycles, Technological advancements, Societal progress and decline
