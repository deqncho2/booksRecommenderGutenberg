Synopsis: In 'Hearts of Controversy' by Alice Meynell, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of secrets and scandal when she becomes entangled with a mysterious group of intellectuals in Victorian London. As she delves deeper into their world, she must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and the pursuit of truth, all while facing the consequences of her own choices. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must confront the blurred lines between right and wrong, and ultimately decide where her heart truly lies.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and Scandal, Love and Loyalty, Morality and Ethics
