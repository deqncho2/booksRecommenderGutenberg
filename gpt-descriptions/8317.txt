Synopsis: In the seventeenth book of the Douay-Rheims Bible, Tobias embarks on a perilous journey to fulfill a sacred duty. Guided by an enigmatic companion, he faces numerous trials and encounters unexpected allies along the way. As Tobias navigates through treacherous lands and faces supernatural challenges, he learns valuable lessons about faith, love, and the power of divine intervention. This captivating tale of adventure and spiritual enlightenment will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the ultimate outcome of Tobias' quest.

Genres: Biblical fiction, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Faith, Love, Divine Providence
