Synopsis: 
'Holidays at the Grange; or, A Week's Delight: Games and Stories for Parlor and Fireside' takes readers on a delightful journey to the Grange, a charming country estate where a group of friends gather for a week of merriment and amusement. As the days unfold, the guests engage in a variety of entertaining games and share captivating stories that captivate both young and old. From thrilling treasure hunts to heartwarming tales, this collection offers a perfect blend of excitement and enchantment, making it an ideal companion for cozy evenings by the fire or lively gatherings with loved ones.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Games

Themes: Friendship, Adventure, Imagination, Tradition
