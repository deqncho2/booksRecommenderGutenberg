Synopsis: In 'The Crimson Conquest: A Romance of Pizarro and Peru', Charles B. Hudson takes readers on a captivating journey to the heart of the Inca Empire during the Spanish conquest. Amidst the lush landscapes and ancient ruins, the story follows the fateful encounter between the ambitious conquistador Francisco Pizarro and the spirited Inca princess, blending history, adventure, and romance in a tale that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration and Conquest, Clash of Cultures, Love and Betrayal
