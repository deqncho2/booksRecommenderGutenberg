Synopsis: In 'The Silent Shore: A Romance' by Bloundelle-Burton, John, a young woman named Muriel finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she inherits a secluded estate on the rugged Cornish coast. As she delves into the secrets of the house and its enigmatic former owner, Muriel becomes entangled in a forbidden love affair that spans generations. With each discovery, she uncovers a haunting tale of lost love, betrayal, and redemption that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Secrets and hidden pasts, Redemption and forgiveness
