Synopsis: 
In 'Village Folk-Tales of Ceylon, Volume 3 (of 3)', author H. Parker takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Ceylonese folklore. This final volume of the series presents a collection of intriguing and magical tales that transport readers to a time when mythical creatures roamed the villages of Ceylon. From mischievous demons and cunning witches to brave heroes and clever animals, these folk tales are filled with adventure, suspense, and valuable life lessons. As readers delve into the rich tapestry of Ceylonese culture, they will be captivated by the vivid storytelling and the timeless wisdom embedded within each tale.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure
Themes: Mythical creatures, Heroism, Life lessons
