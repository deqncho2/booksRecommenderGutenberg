Synopsis: In 'Bakemono Yashiki (The Haunted House), Retold from the Japanese Originals: Tales of the Tokugawa, Volume 2', James S. De Benneville takes readers on a chilling journey through the eerie and supernatural. Set in feudal Japan, this collection of ghostly tales unveils the mysteries lurking within a haunted house, where the line between the living and the dead becomes blurred. As the characters navigate through the shadows of the past, they must confront their deepest fears and unravel the secrets that bind them to the spectral realm. With each story more spine-tingling than the last, this retelling of traditional Japanese folklore will leave readers captivated and haunted by its haunting beauty.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural, Folklore

Themes: Ghosts, Fear, Mystery, Cultural Traditions
