Synopsis: In 'Campaign of the First Troop Philadelphia City Cavalry: April 25-November 11, 1898', Cooper, James takes readers on a thrilling journey through the intense and pivotal events of the Spanish-American War. Through the eyes of the brave soldiers of the First Troop Philadelphia City Cavalry, the reader is immersed in the chaos and danger of battle, as well as the camaraderie and resilience of these remarkable men. As they face the challenges of war, unexpected alliances are formed, personal sacrifices are made, and the true meaning of patriotism is tested. This gripping account of a forgotten chapter in American history will leave readers captivated until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Brotherhood, Sacrifice
