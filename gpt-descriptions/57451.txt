Synopsis: In 'Woodcock's Little Game: A Comedy-Farce, In Two Acts' by John Maddison Morton, chaos ensues when the eccentric Mr. Woodcock decides to play a mischievous game with his unsuspecting friends and family. As the game unfolds, secrets are revealed, misunderstandings abound, and hilarity ensues. With a cast of colorful characters and a series of unexpected twists, this comedy-farce will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Farce

Themes: Deception, Miscommunication, Family Dynamics
