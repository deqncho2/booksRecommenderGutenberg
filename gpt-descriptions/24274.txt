Synopsis: In 'The Native Soil' by Alan Edward Nourse, a young doctor named David returns to his hometown after years of studying medicine in the city. However, he soon discovers that his idyllic rural community is plagued by a mysterious illness that is spreading rapidly. As David investigates the cause of the outbreak, he uncovers dark secrets and faces a moral dilemma that challenges his medical ethics. With the lives of his loved ones at stake, David must navigate through treacherous terrain to find a cure and save his community.

Genres: Medical thriller, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Medical ethics, Community, Sacrifice
