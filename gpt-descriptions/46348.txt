Synopsis: 

'Over There with the Canadians at Vimy Ridge' by G. Harvey Ralphson takes readers on a gripping journey to the battlefields of World War I. Through the eyes of a young Canadian soldier, the story unfolds with heart-pounding action, intense camaraderie, and the indomitable spirit of those who fought for their country. As the soldiers face the horrors of war, they also discover the true meaning of bravery, sacrifice, and the unbreakable bonds of brotherhood.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Resilience
