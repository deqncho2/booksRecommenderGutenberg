Synopsis: In 'The Radio Boys Trailing a Voice; Or, Solving a Wireless Mystery' by Chapman, Allen, a group of adventurous young radio enthusiasts stumble upon a mysterious voice transmission that leads them on a thrilling journey. As they follow the trail of clues, they encounter danger, deception, and unexpected allies, all while using their knowledge of radio technology to unravel the enigma. With each step closer to the truth, the boys find themselves entangled in a web of secrets that could change their lives forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Technology, Exploration
