Synopsis: In a future where Earth is ruled by a powerful corporation, a young man named Ross finds himself caught in a web of secrets and lies. When he stumbles upon a hidden message from the past, he embarks on a dangerous journey to uncover the truth about the company's true intentions and the fate of humanity. As Ross delves deeper into the mystery, he must navigate a treacherous world of corporate espionage, interstellar politics, and the ever-present threat of a dystopian future. With the help of a mysterious woman, he must decide whether to trust his instincts or succumb to the control of those in power.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Corporate power, Government control, Rebellion, Human resilience
