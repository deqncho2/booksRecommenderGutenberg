Synopsis: 
In 'Game Preserve' by Phillips, Rog, a group of friends embarks on a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a hidden game preserve deep in the wilderness. As they explore the preserve, they soon realize that it is not just an ordinary hunting ground, but a place where danger lurks at every turn. With their survival skills put to the test, the friends must navigate through treacherous terrain, outsmart cunning adversaries, and uncover the dark secrets of the preserve before it's too late.

Genres: Action, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Mystery
