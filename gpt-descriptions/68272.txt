Synopsis: 
In 'Trouble' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he unknowingly attracts the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess his invention. Caught in a web of intrigue and danger, the scientist must navigate a treacherous path to protect his creation and ensure the future of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific advancement, Power struggles, Ethical dilemmas
