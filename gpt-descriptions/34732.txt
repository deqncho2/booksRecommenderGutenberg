Synopsis: In "Max Carrados" by Ernest Bramah, readers are introduced to the enigmatic and brilliant detective, Max Carrados. Despite being blind, Carrados possesses an extraordinary ability to solve complex mysteries using his heightened senses and astute deductive reasoning. This collection of short stories follows Carrados as he unravels baffling cases, outwits criminals, and uncovers hidden truths. From stolen jewels to art forgeries, each story presents a unique challenge that Carrados fearlessly takes on, leaving readers captivated by his remarkable skills and unconventional methods.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Perception, Intelligence, Crime-solving, Disability
