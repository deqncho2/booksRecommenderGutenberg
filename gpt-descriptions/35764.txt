Synopsis: 
In 'The Chronicle of the Discovery and Conquest of Guinea. Vol. II', Gomes Eannes de Zurara continues his captivating account of the Portuguese exploration of the African coast. As the expedition ventures further into unknown territories, they encounter new cultures, face treacherous challenges, and make astonishing discoveries. From encounters with powerful African kingdoms to encounters with mythical creatures, this volume is filled with thrilling adventures that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As the Portuguese strive to expand their empire, they must navigate the complexities of diplomacy, trade, and warfare, all while grappling with their own moral dilemmas. 

Genres: 
Historical fiction, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: 
Exploration and discovery, Cultural encounters, Morality and ethics
