Synopsis: In 'The Christmas Peace: 1908' by Thomas Nelson Page, a small town in Virginia is preparing for the holiday season amidst the backdrop of racial tensions and social divisions. As the community comes together to celebrate Christmas, unexpected events unfold, challenging their beliefs and forcing them to confront their prejudices. With heartwarming moments and thought-provoking encounters, this captivating tale explores the power of forgiveness, unity, and the true meaning of Christmas.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Holiday

Themes: Racial tensions, Social divisions, Forgiveness, Unity, Christmas spirit
