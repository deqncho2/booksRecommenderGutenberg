Synopsis: In 'Physiology', Sir Michael Foster takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate workings of the human body. From the beating of the heart to the firing of neurons, Foster explores the fascinating mechanisms that allow us to function and thrive. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, he unveils the hidden wonders of our physiology, shedding light on the mysteries that lie beneath our skin. Prepare to be amazed as Foster unravels the secrets of our bodies, leaving you with a newfound appreciation for the incredible machine that is the human form.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Human anatomy, Biological processes, Health and well-being
