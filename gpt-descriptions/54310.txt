Synopsis: In 'Kitty Alone: A Story of Three Fires', the reader is transported to the picturesque village of Lew Trenchard, where a series of mysterious fires have been plaguing the community. As the flames continue to consume the homes of innocent villagers, a young girl named Kitty finds herself at the center of the unfolding events. With her quick wit and determination, Kitty embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the fires and protect her loved ones from the looming danger. As the flames grow higher and the secrets of the village unravel, Kitty must navigate through a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances to save her community from destruction.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Community, Secrets, Resilience
