Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Wellmouth, a mysterious woman named Keziah Coffin arrives, captivating the townspeople with her enigmatic presence. As the community becomes increasingly fascinated by her, they uncover a web of secrets and dark pasts that threaten to unravel their peaceful existence. Keziah Coffin is a gripping tale of intrigue, suspense, and the power of secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Identity, Small-town dynamics
