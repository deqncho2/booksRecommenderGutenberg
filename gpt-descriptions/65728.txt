Synopsis: In a world where technology has advanced to the point of allowing people to relive their past lives, a renowned Inquisitor is tasked with investigating a mysterious case of a man who claims to have lived multiple lives as historical figures. As the Inquisitor delves deeper into the man's memories, he uncovers a web of secrets and hidden agendas that challenge his own beliefs and force him to question the nature of identity and truth.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Memory, Truth, Ethics
