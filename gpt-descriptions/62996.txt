Synopsis: In the city of Bas, a mysterious and powerful artifact known as the Jewel holds the key to unimaginable power. When a daring thief named Eric John Stark becomes entangled in a deadly game of politics and treachery, he must navigate a dangerous world of warring factions and ancient secrets to uncover the truth behind the Jewel of Bas. As Stark's journey takes him from the depths of the city's underworld to the heights of its ruling elite, he must confront his own past and make choices that will determine the fate of Bas itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Power and corruption, Identity and self-discovery, Betrayal and loyalty
