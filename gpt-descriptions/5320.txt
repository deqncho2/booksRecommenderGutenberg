Synopsis: In 'Taken Alive' by Roe, Edward Payson, a young woman named Grace finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she becomes the target of a sinister plot. As she fights to uncover the truth and protect her loved ones, Grace is forced to confront her own inner strength and resilience. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Identity
