Synopsis: In 'The Day Before Yesterday' by Middleton, Richard, a mysterious event sends the world into chaos, erasing the memories of everyone on Earth. As the protagonist wakes up with no recollection of their past, they embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the forgotten past and the enigmatic cause of the memory loss. With each step, they encounter unexpected allies, dangerous adversaries, and shocking revelations that challenge their perception of reality. As time ticks away, they must race against the clock to reclaim their memories and prevent a catastrophe that could reshape the world forever.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Memory, Identity, Conspiracy, Perception of Reality
