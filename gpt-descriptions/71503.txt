Synopsis: In "The gods of Mexico" by Spence, Lewis, a young archaeologist embarks on a thrilling journey through the heart of Mexico, uncovering ancient secrets and encountering powerful deities that still hold sway over the land. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the Aztec and Mayan civilizations, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and betrayal, where the line between myth and reality begins to blur. With each step, he unravels the enigmatic connection between the gods of Mexico and the fate of humanity itself.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mythology

Themes: Mythology, Power, Betrayal, Fate
