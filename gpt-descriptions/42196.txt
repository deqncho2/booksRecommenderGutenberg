Synopsis:
In the city of Zamboula, Conan the Cimmerian finds himself entangled in a web of dark secrets and ancient sorcery. When a beautiful woman seeks his help, Conan becomes embroiled in a deadly game of survival, facing off against sinister forces that lurk in the shadows. As he navigates the treacherous streets and uncovers the city's dark underbelly, Conan must rely on his cunning and strength to unravel the mysteries that threaten to consume him.

Genres:
Fantasy, Adventure, Sword and Sorcery

Themes:
Power and corruption, Survival in a hostile world
