Synopsis: In 'The Hybrids, An Epi-comic Satire' by An M. D., a group of peculiar characters find themselves entangled in a series of absurd and hilarious situations. From a talking cat with a penchant for philosophy to a mad scientist with questionable ethics, this epi-comic satire takes readers on a wild and unpredictable ride. As the story unfolds, the characters navigate themes of identity, societal norms, and the blurred lines between reality and fantasy.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Society, Reality vs. Fantasy
