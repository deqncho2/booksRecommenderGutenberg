Synopsis: In 'An Unofficial Patriot' by Helen H. Gardener, a young woman named Marion finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and espionage during the height of World War I. As she becomes entangled with a mysterious group of activists, Marion must navigate a dangerous world of secrets and betrayal, all while grappling with her own loyalties and the true meaning of patriotism. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving Marion to question who she can trust and what sacrifices she is willing to make for her beliefs.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity
