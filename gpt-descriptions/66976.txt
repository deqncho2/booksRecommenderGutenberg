Synopsis: In 'Upside Down or Backwards' by W. C. Tuttle, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a series of bizarre events and unexpected twists. As the townspeople try to unravel the truth behind the stranger's arrival, they find themselves questioning their own perceptions and facing the consequences of their actions. With a blend of suspense, humor, and a touch of the supernatural, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Perception, Consequences, Identity
