Synopsis: 
'A Wodehouse Miscellany: Articles & Stories' is a delightful collection of humorous and witty tales by the renowned author, P. G. Wodehouse. From the misadventures of bumbling aristocrats to the escapades of clever butler Jeeves, this anthology offers a diverse range of stories that will leave readers chuckling and eagerly turning the pages. With a mix of hilarious anecdotes, clever wordplay, and eccentric characters, Wodehouse's writing showcases his unparalleled talent for comedic storytelling.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Social class, Love and romance, Wit and wordplay
