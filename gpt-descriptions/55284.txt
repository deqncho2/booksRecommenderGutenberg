Synopsis: In "Reminiscences of Leo Nicolayevitch Tolstoi," Maksim Gorky offers a captivating and intimate portrait of one of the greatest literary figures of all time. Through his personal encounters and conversations with Tolstoi, Gorky delves into the complex mind and turbulent life of the renowned Russian writer. From their initial meeting to their deepening friendship, Gorky provides a unique perspective on Tolstoi's inner struggles, his artistic genius, and his relentless pursuit of truth and justice. As Gorky delves into the depths of Tolstoi's soul, readers are transported into a world of profound introspection and profound human connection.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Literary Criticism

Themes: Artistic genius, Inner struggles, Pursuit of truth and justice
