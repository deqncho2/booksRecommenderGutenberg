Synopsis: 
In 'My Wayward Pardner; or, My Trials with Josiah, America, the Widow Bump, and Etcetery', Marietta Holley takes us on a hilarious and heartwarming journey through the trials and tribulations of her unconventional friendship with Josiah, a lovable but mischievous character. From their misadventures in the small town of Jonesville to their encounters with the eccentric Widow Bump and other colorful personalities, this book is a delightful tale of friendship, love, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Humor, Fiction, Satire

Themes: Friendship, Love, Small-town life, Social commentary
