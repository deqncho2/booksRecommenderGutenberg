Synopsis: In 'A Master's Degree' by Margaret Hill McCarter, a young woman named Ruth embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment as she defies societal expectations and pursues higher education. Set in the early 20th century, Ruth faces numerous challenges and obstacles, but her determination and resilience drive her to overcome adversity and achieve her dreams. As she navigates the complexities of academia and confronts the limitations placed on women, Ruth's story becomes a testament to the power of education and the pursuit of one's passions.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Women's Literature

Themes: Gender Equality, Education, Self-empowerment, Societal Expectations
