Synopsis: In 'Life of Johnson, Volume 4: 1780-1784', Boswell continues his captivating account of the renowned English writer and lexicographer, Samuel Johnson. This volume delves into the fascinating period of Johnson's life, exploring his literary achievements, personal struggles, and his interactions with notable figures of the time. From his involvement in the creation of the first English dictionary to his profound insights on various subjects, Boswell paints a vivid portrait of Johnson's complex character and his enduring impact on the literary world.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Literary Criticism

Themes: Intellectual pursuits, Friendship, Literary legacy
