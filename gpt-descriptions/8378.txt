Synopsis: 
In 'Selected Polish Tales', embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Polish folklore. This collection of tales introduces readers to a variety of intriguing characters, from mischievous spirits and mythical creatures to brave heroes and cunning tricksters. Each story is filled with unexpected twists and turns, transporting readers to a realm where magic and reality intertwine. From the depths of haunted forests to the bustling streets of medieval towns, these tales offer a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of Poland, blending elements of fantasy, adventure, and mystery.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: 
Magic, Heroism, Trickery, Cultural Heritage
