Synopsis: In 'The Unwilling Professor' by Arthur Porges, a renowned professor finds himself caught in a perplexing predicament when he is unexpectedly transported to a parallel universe. Struggling to adapt to this strange new world, he must navigate through a series of mind-bending challenges and unravel the mysteries that surround him. As he delves deeper into the secrets of this alternate reality, he discovers shocking truths that challenge his perception of reality and force him to question the very nature of existence itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Parallel universes, Perception of reality, Existentialism
