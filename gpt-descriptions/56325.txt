Synopsis:
In "Sea Scouts Abroad: Further Adventures of the 'Olivette'", the young Sea Scouts find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey across the vast oceans. Join them as they encounter treacherous storms, mysterious islands, and unexpected dangers that test their courage and resourcefulness. With their trusty ship, the "Olivette", as their home, the Sea Scouts navigate through uncharted territories, forming unbreakable bonds and learning valuable life lessons along the way. Will they conquer the perils that lie ahead and return home victorious?

Genres:
Adventure, Young Adult, Nautical

Themes:
Courage, Friendship, Exploration
