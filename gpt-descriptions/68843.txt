Synopsis: In 'The Quest for Santa Claus' by Atkinson, Frank, a group of adventurous children embarks on a thrilling journey to find the elusive Santa Claus. Faced with numerous challenges and unexpected twists, they must rely on their courage, wit, and teamwork to overcome obstacles and uncover the truth behind the legend of Santa Claus. As they delve deeper into their quest, the children discover the power of belief, the importance of kindness, and the magic that lies within the holiday season.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Belief, Kindness, Magic
