Synopsis: In 'The Rubaiyat of Ohow Dryyam: With Apologies to Omar', author J. L. Duff presents a captivating collection of quatrains that pay homage to the timeless poetry of Omar Khayyam. Through a unique blend of wit, humor, and introspection, Duff explores the complexities of life, love, and the human condition. Each quatrain offers a thought-provoking glimpse into the universal truths that connect us all, inviting readers on a poetic journey of self-discovery and contemplation. With a modern twist on a classic form, this collection is sure to captivate poetry enthusiasts and newcomers alike.

Genres: Poetry, Philosophy, Humor

Themes: Life's transience, Love and relationships, Existentialism, Reflection on human nature
