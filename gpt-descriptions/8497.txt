Synopsis: 
"The Poems and Prose of Ernest Dowson, With a Memoir by Arthur Symons" is a captivating collection that delves into the profound emotions and melancholic musings of Ernest Dowson. Through his evocative poetry and prose, Dowson explores themes of love, loss, beauty, and the fleeting nature of life. With a memoir by Arthur Symons, this compilation offers a glimpse into the life and artistic journey of a talented and enigmatic writer.

Genres: Poetry, Prose, Memoir

Themes: Love, Loss, Beauty, Transience of Life
