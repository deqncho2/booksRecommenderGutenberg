Synopsis: In 'The Man Who Couldn't Sleep' by Arthur Stringer, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a wealthy recluse is found dead in his locked mansion. As the detective delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and a mysterious condition that prevents the prime suspect from ever sleeping. With time running out, the detective must unravel the truth before the killer strikes again, all while battling his own personal demons.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Insomnia, Deception, Psychological Warfare
