Synopsis: 

'Privateering and Piracy in the Colonial Period: Illustrative Documents' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of privateering and piracy during the colonial era. Through a collection of illustrative documents, this book unveils the hidden stories of daring adventurers, treacherous sea battles, and the pursuit of wealth and power on the high seas. From the Caribbean to the Indian Ocean, readers will encounter notorious figures such as Blackbeard, Anne Bonny, and William Kidd, as well as lesser-known but equally intriguing characters. As the pages turn, readers will be immersed in tales of cunning strategies, unexpected alliances, and the constant struggle between law and lawlessness. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Adventure, Maritime History

Themes: Exploration and Expansion, Crime and Justice, Power and Authority
