Synopsis: In 'The Dream' by Émile Zola, we are introduced to Angélique, a young woman with a vivid imagination and an insatiable desire for love and adventure. As she navigates the complexities of her own desires and the societal expectations placed upon her, Angélique finds herself torn between the pursuit of her dreams and the harsh realities of life. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century France, this captivating tale explores the power of imagination, the constraints of societal norms, and the eternal quest for fulfillment.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Imagination, Love, Society, Fulfillment
