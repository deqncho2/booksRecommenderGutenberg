Synopsis: In 'Friday, the Thirteenth: A Novel', Lawson, Thomas William takes readers on a thrilling journey through a small town plagued by a series of mysterious and sinister events. As the residents of Elmwood Falls find themselves trapped in a web of secrets and superstitions, they must confront their deepest fears and unravel the truth behind the curse that haunts their lives. With each passing Friday the thirteenth, the tension rises, and the line between reality and the supernatural blurs. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the shocking secrets hidden within the town's dark past.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Superstition, Fear, Secrets, Small-town dynamics
