Synopsis: In 'Prehistoric Structures of Central America: Who Erected Them?' Townsend delves into the mysterious origins of ancient structures found in Central America, captivating readers with intriguing theories and evidence. From the enigmatic Mayan ruins to the awe-inspiring pyramids, this book takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the fascinating question of who built these remarkable structures and why. As Townsend uncovers clues and examines various hypotheses, readers are left pondering the secrets of these ancient civilizations and the profound impact they had on the region.

Genres: Archaeology, History, Anthropology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Mystery, Cultural heritage
