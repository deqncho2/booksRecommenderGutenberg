Synopsis: In 'The Peacock 'At Home:': A Sequel to the Butterfly's Ball', Catherine Ann Turner Dorset takes readers on a whimsical journey through the animal kingdom as they gather for a grand celebration hosted by the majestic Peacock. From the enchanting forest to the vibrant meadow, the animals come together to revel in music, dance, and delightful conversations. As the night unfolds, unexpected encounters and surprising revelations add a touch of mystery to the festivities. This charming sequel to 'The Butterfly's Ball' invites readers to immerse themselves in a world of imagination and wonder.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Poetry

Themes: Friendship, Celebration, Nature, Curiosity
