Synopsis: 

'Kept in the Dark' by Anthony Trollope is a captivating novel that follows the lives of two couples, Cecilia and Sir Francis Geraldine, and Cecilia's former lover, Captain George Western. As secrets and misunderstandings unravel, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of deception and emotional turmoil. With unexpected twists and turns, the story explores the complexities of love, loyalty, and the consequences of keeping secrets.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and betrayal, Deception and secrets, Consequences of actions
