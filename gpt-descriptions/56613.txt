Synopsis: 

In "History of English Literature from 'Beowulf' to Swinburne," Andrew Lang takes readers on an enthralling journey through the evolution of English literature, spanning from the ancient epic poem "Beowulf" to the works of the renowned poet Algernon Charles Swinburne. With his characteristic wit and insightful analysis, Lang delves into the lives and works of influential writers, shedding light on the cultural, social, and historical contexts that shaped their creations. From the medieval tales of chivalry to the romantic poetry of the Victorian era, this captivating exploration offers a comprehensive overview of the rich tapestry of English literature.

Genres: Literary Criticism, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Evolution of Literature, Cultural and Historical Context, Influence of Writers
