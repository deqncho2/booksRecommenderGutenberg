Synopsis: 
In the small town of Carlinville, a shocking murder takes place, and all eyes turn to the esteemed Judge Oliver Garland. As the investigation unfolds, secrets from the past resurface, threatening to unravel the judge's carefully constructed life. With his son, a defense attorney, by his side, Judge Garland must confront his own demons and navigate a web of deceit to uncover the truth. As the trial approaches, the judge's reputation and legacy hang in the balance, forcing him to confront the ultimate question: can justice truly be served?

Genres: Mystery, Legal Drama, Family Saga

Themes: Truth and deception, the weight of responsibility, the complexities of family dynamics, the pursuit of justice
