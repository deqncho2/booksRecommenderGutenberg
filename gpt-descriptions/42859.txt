Synopsis: In "Lachmi Bai, Rani of Jhansi: The Jeanne D'Arc of India" by Michael White, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of India's most iconic and fearless female leaders. Set against the backdrop of British colonial rule, this gripping biography unveils the extraordinary story of Lachmi Bai, a warrior queen who defied societal norms and led her people in a valiant fight for independence. From her early years as a precocious princess to her pivotal role in the Indian Rebellion of 1857, witness the indomitable spirit and unwavering determination of a true heroine who became a symbol of resistance and inspiration for generations to come.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Female empowerment, Colonialism, Resistance, Leadership
