Synopsis: In 'The Lenni Lenape, or Delaware Indians' by Edwin Robert Walker, embark on a captivating journey through the rich history and culture of the Lenni Lenape tribe. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Walker unveils the fascinating traditions, struggles, and triumphs of this Native American community. From their ancient origins to their encounters with European settlers, this book offers a captivating exploration of the Lenni Lenape's enduring legacy.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Cultural studies, Native American history

Themes: Indigenous identity, Colonialism, Cultural preservation
