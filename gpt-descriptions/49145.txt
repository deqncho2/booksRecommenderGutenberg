Synopsis: In this thought-provoking lecture, Mayow Wynell Mayow delves into the controversial First Report of the Royal Commission on Ritual, exploring its implications for the integrity of the Book of Common Prayer. With insightful analysis and compelling arguments, Mayow challenges the Commission's findings, shedding light on the intricate relationship between ritual and the cherished liturgical text. This lecture is a captivating exploration of the tensions surrounding religious practices and their impact on the Church of England.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, Historical Analysis

Themes: Ritual, Integrity, Liturgy, Religious Controversy
