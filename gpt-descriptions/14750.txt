Synopsis: 

In "Bowdoin Boys in Labrador," join Prof. Leslie A. Lee and a group of adventurous Bowdoin College students as they embark on a scientific expedition to the remote and untamed region of Labrador. Facing treacherous landscapes, harsh weather conditions, and encounters with indigenous peoples, the Bowdoin Boys push the boundaries of exploration and knowledge. As they delve deeper into the mysteries of this uncharted territory, they uncover remarkable discoveries that challenge their understanding of the natural world and their place within it.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Exploration

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Nature and the environment, Cultural encounters
