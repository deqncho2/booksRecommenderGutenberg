Synopsis: 
'Philippine Folklore Stories' by Miller, John Maurice is a captivating collection of enchanting tales that transport readers into the rich and vibrant world of Philippine folklore. From mythical creatures and legendary heroes to supernatural beings and magical realms, this book unveils a treasure trove of captivating stories that have been passed down through generations. Each story is filled with adventure, mystery, and a touch of the supernatural, making it impossible to put down. Whether it's the tale of a brave warrior battling a fearsome creature or a young girl discovering her hidden powers, these stories will leave readers spellbound and eager for more.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: 
Mythology, Heroism, Magic, Cultural Heritage
