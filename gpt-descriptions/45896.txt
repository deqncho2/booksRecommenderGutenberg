Synopsis: In 'The World of Homer' by Lang, Andrew, embark on a captivating journey through the ancient world of Homer's epic poems. Delve into the rich tapestry of Greek mythology, as Lang expertly weaves together tales of gods, heroes, and epic battles. From the legendary Trojan War to the adventures of Odysseus, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the timeless stories that have captivated readers for centuries. Prepare to be transported to a world of adventure, heroism, and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Mythology, Literary Criticism, History

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Gods and Mortals, Epic storytelling
