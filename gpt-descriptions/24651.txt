Synopsis: In "Old Times at Otterbourne," Charlotte M. Yonge takes readers on a captivating journey to the quaint village of Otterbourne, where secrets, love, and family ties intertwine. As the story unfolds, a mysterious stranger arrives, stirring up long-buried memories and challenging the status quo. With its richly drawn characters and atmospheric setting, this novel explores the complexities of human relationships and the power of the past to shape the present.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family, Secrets, Memory, Identity
