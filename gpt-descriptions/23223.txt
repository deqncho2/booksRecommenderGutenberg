Synopsis: In 'Good Blood' by Ernst von Wildenbruch, a young man named Friedrich finds himself torn between his duty to his family and his desire for personal freedom. Set in 19th century Germany, Friedrich must navigate the complex web of societal expectations, family honor, and his own ambitions. As he grapples with his choices, he becomes entangled in a love triangle that threatens to unravel everything he holds dear. With secrets, betrayals, and unexpected twists, 'Good Blood' is a gripping tale of love, loyalty, and the struggle for self-fulfillment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Duty vs. Personal Freedom, Family Honor, Love and Betrayal
