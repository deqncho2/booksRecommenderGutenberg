Synopsis: 
In 'A Boy Trooper with Sheridan' by Allen, Stanton P., readers are taken on an exhilarating journey through the American Civil War as seen through the eyes of a young boy named Charlie. As he joins the Union Army and becomes a trooper under the legendary General Sheridan, Charlie experiences the harsh realities of war, the camaraderie among soldiers, and the thrill of battle. From daring cavalry charges to covert missions behind enemy lines, this gripping tale follows Charlie's transformation from an innocent boy to a brave soldier, all while providing a unique perspective on one of the most pivotal periods in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice, the Horrors of War
