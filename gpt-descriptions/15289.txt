Synopsis: In 'The Truce of God: A Tale of the Eleventh Century', George H. Miles takes us on a captivating journey to the tumultuous eleventh century. Amidst the chaos of war, political intrigue, and religious fervor, we follow the life of a young noblewoman, Isabelle, as she navigates the treacherous landscape of medieval Europe. From the grand castles of France to the battlefields of the Crusades, Isabelle's story intertwines with the lives of knights, monks, and kings, revealing secrets and forging unexpected alliances. As she grapples with love, loyalty, and the pursuit of peace, Isabelle's choices will have far-reaching consequences that could shape the destiny of nations.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: War and Conflict, Love and Relationships, Power and Politics
