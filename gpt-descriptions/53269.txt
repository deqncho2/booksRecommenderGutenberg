Synopsis: In 'Atom Mystery [Young Atom Detective]' by Charles Ira Coombs, a brilliant young scientist named Atom finds himself caught up in a thrilling mystery when a valuable invention goes missing from his laboratory. With his keen intellect and scientific knowledge, Atom embarks on a quest to uncover the truth, facing danger and unexpected twists along the way. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Atom must use his scientific skills to decipher clues and outsmart the cunning culprits. Will he be able to solve the case and recover the stolen invention before it falls into the wrong hands?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Science Fiction

Themes: Scientific inquiry, Problem-solving, Courage
