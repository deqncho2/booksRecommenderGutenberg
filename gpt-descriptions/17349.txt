Synopsis: In 'Frank among the Rancheros' by Castlemon, Harry, a young and adventurous protagonist named Frank embarks on a thrilling journey to the wild and untamed lands of the Rancheros. As he immerses himself in the vibrant culture and traditions of the Mexican ranchers, Frank finds himself caught in a web of danger, mystery, and unexpected friendships. With his courage and resourcefulness tested at every turn, Frank must navigate through treacherous landscapes, encounter formidable foes, and uncover the secrets that lie hidden within the Rancheros. Will he be able to survive the challenges that await him and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Western, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Friendship, Survival
