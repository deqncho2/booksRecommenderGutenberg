Synopsis: 
In "Moon of Israel: A Tale of the Exodus," H. Rider Haggard takes readers on a captivating journey back to ancient Egypt, where the fate of a young Hebrew slave named Ana is intertwined with the epic story of the Exodus. As Ana finds herself caught between the powerful Pharaoh and the enigmatic Moses, she must navigate a treacherous world of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the quest for freedom. With vivid descriptions and a gripping narrative, Haggard brings to life the trials and triumphs of a people seeking liberation from oppression.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Freedom, Oppression, Love and Sacrifice
