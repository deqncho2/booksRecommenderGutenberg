Synopsis: In 'The Midnight Passenger: A Novel' by Richard Savage, a mysterious stranger boards a train late at night, setting off a chain of events that will test the limits of trust, loyalty, and the human spirit. As the passengers find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and deception, they must confront their own pasts and make life-altering choices. With each passing mile, the tension rises, and the true nature of the midnight passenger becomes increasingly elusive. Will they uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
