Synopsis: 

'Armor and Arms: An elementary handbook and guide to the collection in the City Art Museum of St. Louis, Missouri, U.S.A.' by Hoopes, Thomas T. takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of armor and arms. This comprehensive handbook provides a detailed exploration of the collection housed in the City Art Museum, offering insights into the historical significance, craftsmanship, and cultural context of each piece. From intricately designed suits of armor to finely crafted weapons, this guide immerses readers in the rich history and artistry of these remarkable artifacts.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, History

Themes: Historical significance, Artistry, Cultural context
