Synopsis: In 'Learning to Be a Schoolmaster', Thomas R. Cole takes us on a captivating journey through his experiences as a young teacher in a rural community. From the challenges of managing a rowdy classroom to the heartwarming moments of connecting with his students, Cole's memoir offers a poignant and insightful reflection on the transformative power of education. As he navigates the complexities of teaching, he discovers valuable lessons about patience, resilience, and the profound impact a dedicated educator can have on the lives of their students.

Genres: Memoir, Education, Coming-of-age

Themes: Education, Teaching, Personal growth, Resilience
