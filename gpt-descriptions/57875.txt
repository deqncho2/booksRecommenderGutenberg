Synopsis: 

In "Anne Hereford: A Novel" by Wood, Henry, Mrs., readers are introduced to the captivating story of Anne Hereford, a young woman who finds herself caught between love and duty. As the daughter of a wealthy landowner, Anne is expected to marry well and secure her family's social standing. However, her heart yearns for a forbidden love that threatens to upend her carefully planned future. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this novel explores the complexities of societal expectations, the power of love, and the sacrifices one must make to find true happiness.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Duty, Social Expectations, Forbidden Love
