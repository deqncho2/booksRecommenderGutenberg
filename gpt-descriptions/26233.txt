Synopsis: In 'The Indifference of Juliet' by Grace S. Richmond, Juliet is a young woman who seems to have it all - beauty, wealth, and a loving family. However, beneath her seemingly perfect exterior lies a deep sense of indifference towards life and those around her. When a mysterious stranger enters her life, Juliet's world is turned upside down, forcing her to confront her own apathy and discover the true meaning of love and happiness. As secrets are revealed and unexpected connections are made, Juliet embarks on a journey of self-discovery that will challenge everything she thought she knew.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Indifference, Self-discovery, Love, Happiness
