Synopsis: 
In "Tish: The Chronicle of Her Escapades and Excursions," Mary Roberts Rinehart introduces us to the delightful and mischievous character of Tish Carberry. Tish, a spirited and adventurous woman in her sixties, embarks on a series of unexpected escapades that take her on thrilling journeys and hilarious encounters. From solving mysteries to outwitting criminals, Tish's unconventional approach to life leads her on a whirlwind of adventures that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Mystery, Humor, Adventure

Themes: Aging with grace and humor, Embracing adventure and spontaneity
