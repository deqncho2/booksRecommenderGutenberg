Synopsis: In 'Misrule' by Robert Scott, a small town is shaken by a series of mysterious events that threaten to unravel the fabric of their community. As the townspeople grapple with the unsettling occurrences, they must confront their deepest fears and secrets, leading to unexpected alliances and shocking revelations. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the web of intrigue, wondering who can be trusted and what lies beneath the surface of this seemingly idyllic town.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Small-town secrets, Betrayal, Trust, Identity
