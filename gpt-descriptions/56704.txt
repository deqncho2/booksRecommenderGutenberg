Synopsis: In 'Sanctuary: A Bird Masque' by Percy MacKaye, a mysterious and enchanting world unfolds as a group of birds embark on a captivating journey. Filled with vibrant imagery and poetic language, this masque takes readers on a mesmerizing exploration of nature, freedom, and the power of the human spirit. As the birds navigate through various challenges and encounters, they discover the true meaning of sanctuary and the transformative potential it holds. With its lyrical prose and imaginative storytelling, 'Sanctuary: A Bird Masque' invites readers to soar alongside these extraordinary creatures and discover the magic that lies within the natural world.

Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Poetry

Themes: Nature, Freedom, Transformation
