Synopsis: In 'Frank Hunter's Peril' by Alger, Horatio, Jr., we follow the thrilling journey of Frank Hunter, a young man who finds himself caught in a web of danger and deceit. When Frank's father mysteriously disappears, he embarks on a quest to uncover the truth, facing treacherous obstacles and unexpected allies along the way. As Frank delves deeper into the mystery, he discovers shocking secrets that threaten not only his own life but also the lives of those he holds dear. Will Frank be able to navigate the perilous path ahead and unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Perseverance, Betrayal, Family bonds
