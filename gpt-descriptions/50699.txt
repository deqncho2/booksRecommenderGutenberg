Synopsis: In 'Baled Hay: A Drier Book than Walt Whitman's "Leaves o' Grass"', Bill Nye takes readers on a hilarious and thought-provoking journey through the mundane world of hay bales. With his signature wit and charm, Nye explores the unexpected adventures and peculiarities of hay baling, revealing the hidden humor and absurdity in the most ordinary of tasks. From the perils of hay fever to the art of stacking bales, this book is a delightful exploration of the often overlooked world of hay. 

Genres: Humor, Non-fiction

Themes: Everyday absurdity, Finding humor in the ordinary
