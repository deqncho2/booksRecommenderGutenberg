Synopsis: In the remote mountains of Tibet, a group of explorers stumble upon a mysterious and ancient civilization. As they delve deeper into the secrets of this hidden world, they encounter a terrifying and seemingly invincible metal monster. With their lives at stake, they must uncover the truth behind this monstrous creation and find a way to escape its deadly grasp.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Ancient civilizations, Technology, Survival
