Synopsis: In "The Bravest Girl in School" by Ethel Talbot, follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Lily as she navigates the challenges of a new school. Lily's determination and resilience are put to the test as she faces bullies, academic pressures, and the struggle to fit in. With the support of her friends and her own inner strength, Lily discovers the true meaning of bravery and the power of standing up for oneself.

Genres: Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, School Life

Themes: Bravery, Friendship, Bullying, Self-Confidence
