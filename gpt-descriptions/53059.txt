Synopsis: In 'To Save Earth' by Ludwig, Edward W., a brilliant scientist discovers a shocking truth about the impending destruction of Earth. With time running out, he embarks on a perilous journey to find a solution that could save humanity from its own demise. As he faces unimaginable challenges and confronts his own inner demons, the fate of the planet hangs in the balance.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Environmentalism, Human resilience, Sacrifice
