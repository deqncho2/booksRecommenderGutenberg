Synopsis: In 'Crawford's Defeat: A Tale of the Frontier in 1812', the Public Library of Fort Wayne and Allen County takes readers on a thrilling journey to the untamed American frontier during the War of 1812. Set against the backdrop of a brutal conflict, this gripping tale follows the courageous Crawford family as they navigate the treacherous wilderness, facing danger at every turn. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the family's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will they be able to survive the perils of the frontier and overcome the challenges that lie ahead?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Survival, Family, Loyalty, Conflict
