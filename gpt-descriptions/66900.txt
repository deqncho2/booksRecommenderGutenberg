Synopsis: 

'Life and Confession of Ann Walters, the Female Murderess!!: Also the Execution of Enos G. Dudley, at Haverhill, N. H., May 23, 1849. To Which Is Added the Confession of Mary Runkle, Who Was Executed for Murder.' is a gripping and chilling true crime account that delves into the lives of two notorious murderers. The book explores the shocking crimes committed by Ann Walters and Enos G. Dudley, as well as the subsequent execution of Mary Runkle. Through detailed confessions and accounts, the reader is taken on a dark journey into the minds of these individuals, unraveling the motives behind their heinous acts. 

Genres: True Crime, Biography, Historical

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Morality, Psychological Exploration
