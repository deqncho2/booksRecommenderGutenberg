Synopsis: In 'White Magic: A Novel' by David Graham Phillips, a young woman named Evelyn discovers a hidden world of enchantment and mystery when she stumbles upon an ancient book of spells. As she delves deeper into the realm of white magic, Evelyn must navigate the dangerous consequences that come with wielding such power. With dark forces lurking in the shadows, she must decide how far she is willing to go to protect herself and those she loves. This captivating tale explores the boundaries of magic, the complexities of morality, and the transformative power of self-discovery.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Magic, Morality, Self-discovery
