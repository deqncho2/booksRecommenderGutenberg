Synopsis: In 'The Claw' by Cynthia Stockley, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden artifact with a dark secret. As she delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the ancient relic, Emily becomes entangled in a thrilling adventure that takes her across continents and tests her courage and resilience. With each step closer to the truth, she uncovers shocking revelations that challenge her perception of history and her own identity. Will Emily be able to unravel the secrets of 'The Claw' before it consumes her?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Identity and self-discovery
