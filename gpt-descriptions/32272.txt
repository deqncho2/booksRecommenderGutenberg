Synopsis: 
In 'Insidekick' by Jesse F. Bone, a young prodigy named Alex finds himself thrust into a secret world of virtual reality where he becomes the sidekick to a legendary superhero. As Alex navigates this new reality, he must uncover the truth behind the disappearance of his father and confront the dark forces that threaten to destroy both the virtual and real worlds. With thrilling action, unexpected twists, and a captivating blend of technology and fantasy, 'Insidekick' takes readers on an exhilarating journey that will keep them on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Action, Adventure

Themes: Virtual reality, Heroism, Family, Identity
