Synopsis: In 'Parrot & Co.' by Harold MacGrath, a mysterious parrot named Polly becomes the center of attention when it starts repeating a series of numbers that hold a secret. As various individuals and organizations try to decipher the meaning behind Polly's words, a thrilling adventure unfolds, filled with danger, romance, and unexpected twists. With the fate of nations hanging in the balance, the race to unlock the parrot's secret becomes a matter of life and death.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden messages, Loyalty and betrayal
