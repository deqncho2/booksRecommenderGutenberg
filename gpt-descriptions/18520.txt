Synopsis: In 'Sabotage in Space' by Rockwell, Carey, a team of astronauts embarks on a perilous mission to explore the mysteries of the universe. However, they soon discover that they are not alone in the vastness of space, as a series of unexplained incidents begin to occur onboard their spacecraft. As tensions rise and trust is tested, the crew must uncover the truth behind the sabotage before it's too late. With heart-pounding suspense and unexpected twists, this thrilling space adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Betrayal, Survival
