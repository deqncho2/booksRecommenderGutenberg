Synopsis: 
'Bath and Wells: A Sketch-Book' takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque cities of Bath and Wells, as seen through the eyes of the talented artist, Douglas S. Andrews. Through his vivid sketches and insightful observations, Andrews brings to life the stunning architecture, charming streets, and hidden gems of these historic English cities. From the bustling markets to the tranquil gardens, readers will be transported to a world of beauty and intrigue, discovering the rich history and unique character of Bath and Wells.

Genres: Travel, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: Beauty of architecture, Cultural exploration, Historical significance
