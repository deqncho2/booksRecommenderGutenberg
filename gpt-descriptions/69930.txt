Synopsis: 

In 'The unconscious: The fundamentals of human personality, normal and abnormal', Morton Prince delves into the mysterious depths of the human mind, exploring the hidden realms of the unconscious. Through captivating case studies and insightful analysis, Prince unravels the enigmatic workings of the unconscious, shedding light on its profound influence on our thoughts, emotions, and behaviors. As he navigates the intricate landscapes of dreams, memories, and desires, Prince uncovers the intricate tapestry that shapes our very identities. With a blend of scientific rigor and compelling storytelling, this book takes readers on a captivating journey into the depths of the human psyche.

Genres: Psychology, Non-fiction

Themes: Unconscious mind, Personality, Abnormal psychology, Identity
