Synopsis: In 'Songs of Love and Empire' by E. Nesbit, a group of friends stumble upon a mysterious artifact that transports them back in time to ancient Rome. As they navigate the treacherous world of emperors and gladiators, they must find a way to return home while unraveling the secrets of love and power that transcend time. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of these courageous adventurers.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Friendship, Love, Power
