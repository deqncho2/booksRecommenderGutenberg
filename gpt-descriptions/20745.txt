Synopsis: In 'An Outcast; Or, Virtue and Faith', follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Edith as she navigates the complexities of society and grapples with the consequences of her choices. Set in the 19th century, this gripping tale explores themes of love, betrayal, redemption, and the power of faith. Will Edith find solace and acceptance in a world that has cast her aside, or will she forever remain an outcast?

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love, Betrayal, Redemption, Faith
