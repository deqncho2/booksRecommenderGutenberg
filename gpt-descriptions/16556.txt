Synopsis: 
In 'Short Story Classics (American) Vol. 2', embark on a captivating journey through a collection of timeless American short stories. From the haunting tales of Edgar Allan Poe to the thought-provoking narratives of Mark Twain, this anthology showcases the brilliance of American literature. Delve into the depths of human nature, explore the complexities of relationships, and experience the power of storytelling in this captivating compilation.

Genres: 
Fiction, Gothic, Satire, Realism, Adventure

Themes: 
Human nature, Relationships, Morality, Social commentary, Identity
