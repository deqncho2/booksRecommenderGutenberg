Synopsis: In "A Maker of History" by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges from the shadows to shape the course of history. As the protagonist navigates a world of political intrigue, espionage, and power struggles, their true identity remains shrouded in secrecy. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale keeps readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the motives and actions of every character. Will the maker of history succeed in their mission, or will their secrets be their downfall?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power, Identity, Manipulation
