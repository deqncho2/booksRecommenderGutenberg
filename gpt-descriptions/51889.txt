Synopsis: In "Robert Emmet: A Survey of His Rebellion and of His Romance," Louise Imogen Guiney delves into the captivating life of Robert Emmet, a young Irish revolutionary who fought for his country's independence in the late 18th century. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Guiney paints a vivid portrait of Emmet's rebellious spirit and his passionate love affair, intertwining history and romance in a mesmerizing narrative. As the reader follows Emmet's journey, they are transported to a tumultuous era filled with political intrigue, secret societies, and forbidden love, making this biography a captivating exploration of one man's fight for freedom and the sacrifices he made along the way.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Patriotism, Rebellion, Love and Sacrifice
