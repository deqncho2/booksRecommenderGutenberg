Synopsis: In 'Green Doors' by Ethel Cook Eliot, a young woman named Alice finds herself drawn to a mysterious house with green doors that seems to hold secrets from her past. As she delves deeper into the house's history, she uncovers a web of hidden connections and unexpected revelations that challenge her perception of reality. With each door she opens, Alice is propelled into a captivating journey of self-discovery and the exploration of the human psyche.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Memory, Perception, Secrets, Transformation
