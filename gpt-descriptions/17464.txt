Synopsis: 

'Great Singers, First Series: Faustina Bordoni To Henrietta Sontag' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and careers of some of the most remarkable female singers in history. From the enchanting performances of Faustina Bordoni to the mesmerizing talent of Henrietta Sontag, this book delves into the triumphs, struggles, and extraordinary voices that shaped the world of opera. With vivid storytelling and insightful anecdotes, Ferris paints a vivid portrait of these legendary singers, their rise to fame, and the impact they had on the music industry. 

Genres: Biography, Music, History

Themes: Talent and dedication, the power of music, the challenges of fame
