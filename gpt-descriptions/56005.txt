Synopsis: In 'Marsena, and Other Stories of the Wartime', Frederic Harold takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of poignant and gripping tales set during wartime. From the trenches of World War I to the battlefields of the American Civil War, these stories delve into the lives of soldiers and civilians, exploring the profound impact of war on individuals and society. Through vivid storytelling and rich character development, the author paints a vivid picture of the human experience in times of conflict, revealing the resilience, sacrifice, and unexpected moments of hope that emerge amidst the chaos of war.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Short stories

Themes: War and its consequences, Human resilience, Sacrifice, Hope in adversity
