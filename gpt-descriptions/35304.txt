Synopsis: In "The Last Stroke: A Detective Story" by Lynch, Lawrence L., a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a wealthy art collector is found dead under mysterious circumstances. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that lead him down a twisted path. With time running out and the pressure mounting, the detective must rely on his keen instincts and sharp intellect to solve the puzzle before the last stroke of the clock.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Betrayal
