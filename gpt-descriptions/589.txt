Synopsis: 
In "Catriona" by Robert Louis Stevenson, the story continues from where "Kidnapped" left off, following the adventures of David Balfour as he navigates the treacherous political landscape of 18th-century Scotland. When David becomes entangled with a mysterious and captivating young woman named Catriona, his life takes an unexpected turn. As he becomes embroiled in a dangerous game of intrigue and betrayal, David must rely on his wit and courage to navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and honor. Set against the backdrop of Jacobite uprisings and political unrest, "Catriona" is a thrilling tale of adventure, romance, and the pursuit of justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
