Synopsis: In 'World Beyond Pluto' by Stephen Marlowe, a renowned astrophysicist embarks on a groundbreaking mission to explore the uncharted depths of space. As he ventures further than any human has ever gone, he encounters mysterious phenomena and encounters that challenge his understanding of the universe. With each discovery, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, forcing him to question the very fabric of reality. This gripping tale takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the cosmos, filled with unexpected twists and mind-bending revelations.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Existentialism, Perception of Reality
