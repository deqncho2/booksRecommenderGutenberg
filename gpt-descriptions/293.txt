Synopsis: In 'Paul Prescott's Charge' by Horatio Alger, Jr., a young and ambitious orphan named Paul Prescott finds himself thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when he is entrusted with a secret mission. As Paul navigates through unexpected challenges and encounters a cast of colorful characters, he must rely on his wit, determination, and the lessons he has learned along the way to fulfill his charge. Will Paul succeed in his mission and uncover the truth that lies beneath the surface?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Perseverance, Self-discovery, Loyalty
