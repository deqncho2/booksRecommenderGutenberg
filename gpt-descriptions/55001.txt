Synopsis: In 'In a Toy Shop: A Christmas Play for Small Children' by Effa E. Preston, a magical toy shop comes to life on Christmas Eve, captivating the imagination of young children. As the toys come alive and embark on a whimsical adventure, they learn valuable lessons about friendship, kindness, and the true meaning of Christmas. With enchanting characters and delightful surprises, this heartwarming play is sure to captivate both children and adults alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Christmas Play, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Kindness, Christmas Spirit
