Synopsis: In 'The Bread-winners: A Social Study' by John Hay, readers are transported to the bustling streets of New York City in the late 19th century. The story revolves around the lives of two brothers, one a successful businessman and the other a struggling artist, as they navigate the complexities of society and the pursuit of their dreams. As their paths diverge, they find themselves entangled in a web of love, ambition, and societal expectations, leading to unexpected consequences that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Social class, Ambition, Family dynamics
