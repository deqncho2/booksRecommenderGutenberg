Synopsis: 
In 'Dave Dashaway and His Hydroplane; Or, Daring Adventures over the Great Lake', we follow the thrilling escapades of young Dave Dashaway as he embarks on a daring journey over the vast Great Lake. With his newly invented hydroplane, Dave faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected dangers that test his courage and resourcefulness. As he navigates through treacherous waters and battles against the forces of nature, Dave's determination and quick thinking are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way and achieve his dreams of becoming a skilled aviator?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Invention, Perseverance
