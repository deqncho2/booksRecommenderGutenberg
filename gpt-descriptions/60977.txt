Synopsis: In 'The Girl Scouts at Singing Sands' by Mildred A. Wirt, a group of adventurous Girl Scouts embark on a thrilling summer camp experience at Singing Sands, a picturesque beachside location. As they immerse themselves in various activities and challenges, the girls stumble upon a mysterious secret that leads them on a captivating journey filled with danger and intrigue. With their resourcefulness and teamwork, the Girl Scouts must unravel the enigma and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving, Discovery
