Synopsis: 

In 'Manners and Rules of Good Society; Or, Solecisms to be Avoided' by Anonymous, readers are transported to the elegant world of high society, where every social interaction is governed by a complex set of rules and expectations. Through a series of witty anecdotes and insightful observations, the author explores the various faux pas and blunders that can befall even the most well-intentioned individuals. From improper introductions to inappropriate conversation topics, this guide offers invaluable advice on how to navigate the treacherous waters of polite society and avoid committing social solecisms. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Etiquette, Self-help

Themes: Social etiquette, Politeness, Social class
