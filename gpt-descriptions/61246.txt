Synopsis: In 'The Prisoners of Hartling' by J.D. Beresford, a group of strangers find themselves trapped in a mysterious and foreboding mansion. As they struggle to uncover the secrets of their confinement, they must confront their own fears and pasts, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax. With each page, the tension builds, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Survival
