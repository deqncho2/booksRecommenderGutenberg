Synopsis: In "A Maid at King Alfred's Court: A Story for Girls" by Lucy Foster Madison, young Emma finds herself thrust into the bustling world of King Alfred's court as a maid. As she navigates the intrigues and challenges of court life, Emma discovers hidden secrets and unexpected friendships that will shape her destiny. With danger lurking in the shadows, Emma must summon her courage and wit to protect those she loves and uncover the truth that could change everything.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Identity, Loyalty
