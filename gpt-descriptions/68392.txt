Synopsis: In "Reminiscences of the Civil War, 1861-1865," Preston Lafayette Ledford takes readers on a captivating journey through his personal experiences during one of the most tumultuous periods in American history. From his enlistment as a young soldier to the harrowing battles he witnessed, Ledford's vivid recollections provide a unique perspective on the Civil War. Through his heartfelt storytelling, readers are transported to the front lines, where they witness the camaraderie, sacrifice, and resilience of the soldiers who fought for their beliefs.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War Literature

Themes: Patriotism, Brotherhood, Loss and Sacrifice, Resilience, Historical Significance
