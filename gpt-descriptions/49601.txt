Synopsis: 

'A History of Deerpark in Orange County, N. Y.' by Peter E. Gumaer takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and often overlooked history of Deerpark, a small town in Orange County, New York. From its humble beginnings as a Dutch settlement in the 17th century to its role in the American Revolution and beyond, Gumaer delves into the fascinating stories of the people, events, and landmarks that have shaped Deerpark into what it is today. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, the author uncovers hidden tales of triumph, tragedy, and resilience, shedding light on the town's unique heritage and the individuals who have left an indelible mark on its history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Local history

Themes: Community identity, Historical preservation, Cultural heritage
