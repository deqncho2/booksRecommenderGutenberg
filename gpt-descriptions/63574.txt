Synopsis: In "The Rise of Universities," Charles Homer Haskins takes readers on a captivating journey through the medieval world, unveiling the fascinating origins and development of universities. From the bustling streets of Bologna to the hallowed halls of Oxford, Haskins explores the intellectual, social, and cultural forces that led to the birth of these renowned institutions. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, he reveals the struggles, triumphs, and transformative impact of universities on society, shedding light on the remarkable individuals who shaped their history. This engrossing narrative offers a captivating glimpse into the birth of higher education and the enduring legacy it has left behind.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Education, Intellectualism, Social Transformation
