Synopsis: In 'Signing the Contract, and What It Cost' by Martha Finley, a young woman finds herself faced with a life-altering decision when she is presented with a contract that promises to fulfill her deepest desires. As she delves into the terms and conditions, she soon realizes that the cost of her dreams may be higher than she ever imagined. With suspense and intrigue, this gripping tale explores the lengths one is willing to go to achieve their desires and the consequences that may follow.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Temptation, Morality, Sacrifice
