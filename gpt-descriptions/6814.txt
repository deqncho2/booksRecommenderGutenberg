Synopsis: The Curlytops, a lively and adventurous group of siblings, embark on a thrilling journey to their Uncle Frank's ranch. Excitement fills the air as they explore the vast landscapes, encounter wild animals, and learn to ride ponies. But when a mysterious event occurs, the Curlytops find themselves caught up in a captivating mystery that tests their courage and resourcefulness. Will they be able to solve the puzzle and save the day? Join the Curlytops on this delightful and suspenseful adventure at Uncle Frank's Ranch!

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Family bonds, Exploration, Problem-solving
