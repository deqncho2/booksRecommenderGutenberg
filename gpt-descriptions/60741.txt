Synopsis: In "Jenny: A Novel" by Sigrid Undset, readers are introduced to Jenny Winge, a young woman living in early 20th century Norway. Jenny's life takes an unexpected turn when she becomes entangled in a passionate love affair that challenges societal norms and forces her to confront her own desires and ambitions. As she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and personal fulfillment, Jenny must make difficult choices that will shape her future and test her resilience. Set against a backdrop of a changing society, this captivating novel explores the complexities of human relationships and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Desire, Societal Expectations, Personal Freedom
