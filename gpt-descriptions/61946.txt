Synopsis: In 'Jesse James' Desperate Game; Or, The Robbery of the Ste. Genevieve Bank', William Ward takes readers on a thrilling journey into the heart of the Wild West. Set in the late 1800s, this gripping tale follows the notorious outlaw Jesse James as he hatches a daring plan to rob the Ste. Genevieve Bank. As the tension builds, readers are drawn into a world of danger, deception, and unexpected twists that will keep them on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Historical Fiction, Crime

Themes: Outlawry, Justice, Loyalty
