Synopsis: In 'The Child Wife' by Mayne Reid, a young girl named Ellen finds herself thrust into an arranged marriage at a tender age. As she navigates the challenges of her new life, she discovers hidden secrets, faces unexpected dangers, and must summon her inner strength to overcome adversity. This gripping tale takes readers on a journey filled with suspense, romance, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Coming of age, Love and sacrifice, Resilience
