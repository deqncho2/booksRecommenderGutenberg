Synopsis: In 'India for Indians: Enlarged Edition', Chitta Ranjan Das takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of India's history, culture, and diversity. From the ancient Indus Valley civilization to the struggles of the freedom movement, Das weaves together a compelling narrative that explores the essence of being Indian. With vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, the book delves into the country's vibrant traditions, languages, religions, and social customs, shedding light on the complexities and beauty of India's identity. This enlarged edition offers an even more comprehensive exploration of the nation, providing readers with a deeper understanding of India's past, present, and future.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Indian identity, Cultural diversity, Historical significance, Nationalism
