Synopsis: In 'A Little Maid of Old Philadelphia', follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Betty as she navigates the bustling streets of colonial Philadelphia. From her encounters with historical figures to her involvement in secret missions, Betty's adventures will transport readers to a time of revolution and intrigue. Will she be able to fulfill her important role in the fight for independence? Find out in this thrilling tale of courage, loyalty, and patriotism.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Independence, Coming of age
