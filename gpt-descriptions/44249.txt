Synopsis: In 'Detectives, Inc.: A Mystery Story for Boys', readers are introduced to two young boys, Jack and Tom, who stumble upon a mysterious case that leads them into a world of intrigue and danger. As they delve deeper into the investigation, they uncover a web of secrets and unexpected twists that keep them on their toes. With their quick thinking and determination, the boys must navigate through a series of clues and suspects to solve the perplexing mystery. Will they be able to crack the case and bring the truth to light?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Problem-solving
