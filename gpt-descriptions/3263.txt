Synopsis: In 'The Portygee' by Joseph Crosby Lincoln, a young Portuguese fisherman named Manuel Silva finds himself torn between his love for the sea and his desire to provide a better life for his family. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the fishing industry and faces unexpected challenges, Manuel's resilience and determination are put to the test. Set against the backdrop of a small New England fishing community, this captivating tale explores the sacrifices one must make in pursuit of their dreams and the power of family bonds.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Pursuit of dreams, Sacrifice, Family bonds
