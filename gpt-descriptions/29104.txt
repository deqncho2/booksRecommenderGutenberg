Synopsis: In 'The Web of the Golden Spider' by Frederick Orin Bartlett, a young American engineer finds himself entangled in a thrilling adventure when he is sent to Central America to oversee the construction of a railroad. As he navigates through treacherous jungles, encounters dangerous wildlife, and faces unexpected challenges, he becomes entwined in a web of mystery and intrigue. With the fate of the project and his own life hanging in the balance, he must unravel the secrets hidden within the golden spider's web before it's too late.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Deception, Courage
