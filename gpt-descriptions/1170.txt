Synopsis: 
Anabasis by Xenophon is a thrilling and captivating tale that follows a group of Greek mercenaries who find themselves stranded deep in enemy territory after their leader is betrayed. With no hope of rescue, they must navigate treacherous landscapes, face hostile armies, and endure unimaginable hardships in their desperate struggle to return home. As they battle against overwhelming odds, the mercenaries are forced to confront their own fears, test their loyalty, and discover the true meaning of courage and brotherhood.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Bravery, Brotherhood
