Synopsis: In the third volume of "The Life and Times of Queen Victoria," author Robert Wilson delves into the captivating and tumultuous period of Queen Victoria's reign. As the Queen navigates the complexities of her personal life and the challenges of ruling a vast empire, readers are transported to a world of political intrigue, social change, and royal scandals. From the rise of the suffragette movement to the tensions of international diplomacy, this volume offers a rich and immersive exploration of the era that shaped Queen Victoria's legacy.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Monarchy, Power and Politics, Social Change
