Synopsis: In "The Rejuvenation of Aunt Mary," a mysterious elixir promises to turn back the clock and restore youth to Aunt Mary, a woman in her seventies. As she embarks on this extraordinary journey, unexpected consequences and hidden secrets begin to unravel, leading to a captivating exploration of the complexities of aging, identity, and the pursuit of eternal youth.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Aging, Identity, Self-discovery, Deception, Mortality
