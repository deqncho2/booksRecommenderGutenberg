Synopsis: In "Agnes Sorel: A Novel" by James, G. P. R., readers are transported to the captivating world of 15th-century France, where the enigmatic Agnes Sorel rises from obscurity to become the most influential woman in the court of King Charles VII. As Agnes navigates the treacherous waters of power, love, and betrayal, she must confront her own desires and ambitions while facing the ever-present threat of political intrigue and war. With vivid historical detail and a cast of compelling characters, this novel offers a mesmerizing glimpse into the life of a woman who defied convention and left an indelible mark on history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Power and Influence, Love and Desire, Betrayal and Loyalty
