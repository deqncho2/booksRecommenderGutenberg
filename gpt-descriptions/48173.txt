Synopsis: 

In 'Practical Carriage and Wagon Painting: A Treatise on the Painting of Carriages, Wagons and Sleighs,' author M. C. Hillick takes readers on a comprehensive journey into the art of painting and decorating various types of vehicles. This detailed guide provides explicit directions for executing all kinds of work, including painting factory work, lettering, scrolling, ornamenting, and varnishing. With the inclusion of tested recipes and formulas, this treatise offers invaluable insights and techniques for both aspiring and experienced painters in the carriage and wagon industry.

Genres: Non-fiction, How-to, Crafts

Themes: Artistry, Craftsmanship, Technical Expertise
