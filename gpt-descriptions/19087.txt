Synopsis: In "The King Nobody Wanted" by Langford, Norman F., a young boy named David finds himself in a peculiar situation when he is unexpectedly chosen to become the king of a faraway land. With no prior knowledge or desire for such a role, David embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected challenges, magical creatures, and a quest to discover his true destiny. As he navigates through this enchanting world, David learns valuable lessons about courage, self-discovery, and the power of embracing one's unique abilities.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Courage, Self-discovery
