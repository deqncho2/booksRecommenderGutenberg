Synopsis: In 'The Philippine Islands' by Lala, Ramon Reyes, a young woman named Maria embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets of her family's past. As she delves into the history of the Philippine Islands, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, romance, and danger. With each revelation, Maria's understanding of her own identity is challenged, leading her to question everything she thought she knew. Set against the backdrop of the stunning Philippine landscape, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Family, History, Secrets
