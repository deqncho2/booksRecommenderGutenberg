Synopsis: In "The Real Gladstone: An Anecdotal Biography," J. Ewing Ritchie takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of one of Britain's most influential political figures, William Ewart Gladstone. Through a collection of fascinating anecdotes and personal accounts, Ritchie paints a vivid portrait of Gladstone's character, his political career, and the impact he had on Victorian society. From his early years as a prodigious student to his rise as a formidable statesman, this biography offers a unique and intimate glimpse into the life of a man who shaped the course of British history.

Genres: Biography, History, Politics

Themes: Political leadership, Victorian society, Personal character
