Synopsis: 

In 'Salt Water: The Sea Life and Adventures of Neil D'Arcy the Midshipman', we follow the thrilling journey of young Neil D'Arcy as he embarks on a life at sea. From his humble beginnings as a midshipman, Neil quickly finds himself immersed in a world of danger, excitement, and unexpected encounters. As he navigates treacherous waters, battles fierce enemies, and forms unlikely alliances, Neil's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he prove himself worthy of the sea and all its mysteries?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Friendship, Identity
