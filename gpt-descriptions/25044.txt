Synopsis: In 'The Eugenic Marriage, Volume 3', author W. Grant Hague delves into the fascinating world of eugenics, offering readers a personal guide to the new science of better living and better babies. Through a combination of scientific research, personal anecdotes, and practical advice, Hague explores the importance of selecting the right partner for marriage and procreation, emphasizing the impact it can have on future generations. With thought-provoking insights and a compelling narrative, this volume provides readers with a unique perspective on the potential for improving the human race through intentional breeding and genetic selection.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Self-help

Themes: Eugenics, Marriage, Genetics, Human improvement
