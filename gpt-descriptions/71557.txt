Synopsis: In "The Secret History of the Court of Spain During the Last Century," Rachel Challice takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden corridors of power, scandal, and intrigue within the Spanish court. Set against the backdrop of the 18th century, this gripping narrative unveils the untold stories of the royal family, their loyal subjects, and the dark secrets that threaten to unravel the very fabric of the kingdom. As the reader delves deeper into the clandestine world of the court, they will uncover shocking revelations, forbidden romances, and political machinations that will keep them on the edge of their seat until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Power and Corruption, Loyalty and Betrayal, Love and Forbidden Desires
