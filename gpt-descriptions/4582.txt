Synopsis: 
In "Björnstjerne Björnson, 1832-1910" by William Morton Payne, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the life of one of Norway's most influential literary figures. This biography delves into the complexities of Björnstjerne Björnson's life, exploring his rise to fame as a writer, his political activism, and his personal struggles. From his humble beginnings to his international acclaim, this book paints a vivid portrait of a man who left an indelible mark on the literary world.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Literary legacy, Political activism, Personal struggles
