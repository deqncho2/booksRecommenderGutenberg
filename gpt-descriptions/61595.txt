Synopsis: In "Jack Straw, Lighthouse Builder" by Crump, Irving, follow the captivating journey of Jack Straw, a young and ambitious man who embarks on a daring mission to construct a lighthouse in a treacherous and mysterious location. As Jack faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected allies and adversaries, the story unfolds with suspense, adventure, and a touch of romance. Will Jack's determination and resourcefulness be enough to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Perseverance, Ambition, Love and Sacrifice
