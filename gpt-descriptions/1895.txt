Synopsis:
'Armadale' by Wilkie Collins is a gripping Victorian novel that follows the intertwined lives of two young men, Allan Armadale and Ozias Midwinter. The story begins with a mysterious curse that haunts the Armadale family, leading to a series of unexpected events and dark secrets. As the plot unfolds, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with deception, identity confusion, and a web of interconnected relationships. With its intricate plot and complex characters, 'Armadale' keeps the reader guessing until the very end.

Genres:
Mystery, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes:
Fate and destiny, Identity and inheritance, Secrets and deception
