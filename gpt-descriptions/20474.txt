Synopsis: In 'Grace Harlowe's Fourth Year at Overton College', Grace and her friends return for their final year at college, ready to face new challenges and adventures. As they navigate through academic pressures, romantic entanglements, and unexpected friendships, they find themselves caught up in a mysterious secret that threatens to disrupt their lives. With determination and resilience, Grace and her friends must unravel the truth and protect their futures before it's too late.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Identity, Self-discovery
