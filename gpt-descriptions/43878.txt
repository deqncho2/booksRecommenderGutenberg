Synopsis: In 'The Grim House' by Molesworth, Mrs., a young girl named Griselda finds herself in a mysterious and eerie house filled with secrets and dark history. As she explores the house, she uncovers hidden rooms, strange occurrences, and encounters with peculiar characters. With each discovery, Griselda becomes entangled in a web of suspense and danger, unsure of who she can trust. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the Grim House, Griselda must summon her courage and unravel the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Courage and self-discovery
