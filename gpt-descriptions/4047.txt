Synopsis: In 'The Leavenworth Case' by Anna Katharine Green, a wealthy and respected man is found dead in his mansion, with all evidence pointing towards murder. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, motives are questioned, and the line between truth and deception becomes increasingly blurred. With a web of complex characters and a gripping plot, this thrilling mystery will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice, Wealth and Power
