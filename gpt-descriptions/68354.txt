Synopsis: In 'The Undamned' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist discovers a groundbreaking technology that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his invention, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but the fate of the entire world. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of intrigue and betrayal to protect his creation and ensure the survival of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Technological advancement, Morality, Conspiracy
