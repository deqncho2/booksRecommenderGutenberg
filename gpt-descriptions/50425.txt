Synopsis: 

'Four-Fifty Miles to Freedom' is a gripping historical novel set during the height of the American Civil War. The story follows two unlikely companions, Maurice and Kenneth, as they embark on a perilous journey to escape slavery and find freedom. As they traverse the treacherous landscapes of the South, facing constant danger and uncertainty, their bond grows stronger, and they discover the true meaning of courage and resilience. With every step they take, they inch closer to their ultimate goal, but will they be able to overcome the numerous obstacles that stand in their way?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Freedom, Friendship, Resilience, Social Injustice
