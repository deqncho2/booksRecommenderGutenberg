Synopsis: In "The Priest's Tale - Père Etienne: From The New Decameron, Volume III," we are introduced to Père Etienne, a young and idealistic priest who finds himself torn between his duty to the church and his desire for love and companionship. As he navigates the complexities of his faith and the temptations of the world, Père Etienne's journey takes unexpected turns, leading him to question his beliefs and ultimately forcing him to make a difficult choice that will shape his future.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Faith and Doubt, Love and Sacrifice, Moral Dilemmas
