Synopsis: 
In "Tar-Heel Tales in Vernacular Verse," J. E. P. Doyle takes readers on a delightful journey through the charming and humorous world of North Carolina. Through a collection of vernacular verse, Doyle weaves together tales of colorful characters, amusing anecdotes, and unexpected twists that will leave readers both laughing and reflecting on the quirks of life. From mischievous pranks to heartwarming moments, this collection captures the essence of the Tar Heel State in a truly unique and entertaining way.

Genres: Poetry, Humor, Regional Literature

Themes: North Carolina culture, Humor, Everyday life
