Synopsis: 
'Emelian the Fool: a tale' is a captivating story that follows the life of Emelian, a seemingly simple-minded fool who possesses an extraordinary gift. Set in a medieval world filled with political intrigue, magical elements, and unexpected twists, Emelian's journey takes him from the lowest ranks of society to the heart of power. As he navigates through a web of secrets and manipulations, Emelian's true nature and purpose gradually unfold, challenging the reader's assumptions and leaving them eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Fantasy, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Deception, Redemption
