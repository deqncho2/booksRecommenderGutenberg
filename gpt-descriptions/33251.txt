Synopsis: In the small town of Hempfield, secrets run deep and the past refuses to stay buried. When a mysterious newcomer arrives, the lives of the town's residents are thrown into turmoil, as long-held grudges and hidden desires come to the surface. As the tension escalates, the line between truth and deception becomes blurred, and the consequences of their actions threaten to tear the community apart.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Small-town dynamics, Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Redemption
