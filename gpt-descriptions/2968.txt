Synopsis: In "The Memoirs of Jacques Casanova de Seingalt, 1725-1798. Volume 18: Return to Naples," Casanova finds himself back in the vibrant city of Naples, where he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love affairs, and dangerous alliances. As he navigates the treacherous waters of Naples' elite society, Casanova must rely on his wit, charm, and cunning to survive. With each encounter, he uncovers shocking secrets and faces life-threatening challenges that test his resilience and resourcefulness. Will Casanova's return to Naples be his downfall or his greatest triumph?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Power and Politics, Survival and Resilience
