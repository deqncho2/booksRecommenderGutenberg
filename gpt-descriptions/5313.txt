Synopsis:
In 'The Herd Boy and His Hermit' by Charlotte M. Yonge, a young boy named Wilfred finds himself drawn to the mysterious hermit who lives in the woods near his village. As their unlikely friendship develops, Wilfred discovers that the hermit holds a secret that could change his life forever. With themes of friendship, self-discovery, and the power of secrets, this enchanting tale takes readers on a captivating journey through the depths of human connection and the transformative power of compassion.

Genres:
Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Friendship, Self-discovery, Secrets
