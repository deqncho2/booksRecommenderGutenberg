Synopsis: 
In 'More "Short Sixes"', H. C. Bunner presents a collection of captivating short stories that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. From tales of love and betrayal to stories of mystery and suspense, Bunner weaves together a tapestry of intriguing narratives that explore the complexities of human nature. Each story is filled with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers eager to uncover the secrets that lie within. With his masterful storytelling and vivid characters, Bunner invites readers into a world where nothing is as it seems, and where the unexpected is always just around the corner.

Genres: 
Fiction, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: 
Love and Betrayal, Human Nature, Secrets and Surprises
