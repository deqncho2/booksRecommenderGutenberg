Synopsis: In 'Footlights' by Rita Weiman, a talented young actress named Lily finds herself thrust into the glamorous world of Broadway, where dreams are made and broken. As she navigates the cutthroat competition, passionate romances, and unexpected betrayals, Lily must discover the true cost of fame and the sacrifices she is willing to make to achieve her dreams. With a backdrop of dazzling performances and behind-the-scenes drama, 'Footlights' takes readers on a captivating journey through the highs and lows of the theater world.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Love and Betrayal, Pursuit of Dreams
