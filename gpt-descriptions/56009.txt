Synopsis: In 'Marjorie in Command' by Carolyn Wells, Marjorie is a young woman who unexpectedly finds herself in a position of power and authority. As she navigates the challenges and responsibilities that come with her newfound command, Marjorie's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. With a touch of mystery and a dash of romance, this captivating story follows Marjorie's journey as she learns to lead and discovers the true strength within herself.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Power and authority, Self-discovery, Resilience
