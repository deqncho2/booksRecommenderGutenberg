Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Lucy Maud Montgomery' is a comprehensive collection of the works of the beloved Canadian author, Lucy Maud Montgomery. This index includes her famous novels such as "Anne of Green Gables," "Emily of New Moon," and "The Story Girl," as well as her lesser-known works, short stories, and poetry. Dive into the enchanting world of Montgomery's imagination, where vivid characters, picturesque landscapes, and heartfelt emotions come together to create timeless stories that have captivated readers for generations.

Genres: Fiction, Children's Literature, Romance, Poetry

Themes: Coming-of-age, Friendship, Love, Nature, Imagination
