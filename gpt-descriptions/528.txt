Synopsis: 
In 'Joe the Hotel Boy; Or, Winning out by Pluck' by Horatio Alger, Jr., we follow the journey of Joe, a young and ambitious hotel boy who dreams of a better life. Despite facing numerous challenges and setbacks, Joe's determination and resourcefulness propel him forward as he navigates the world of hospitality. Along the way, he encounters a cast of colorful characters, both friend and foe, who test his resolve and shape his character. Will Joe's pluck and perseverance be enough to overcome the obstacles in his path and achieve his dreams?

Genres: 
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Rags-to-riches

Themes: 
Ambition, Hard work, Resilience, Social mobility
