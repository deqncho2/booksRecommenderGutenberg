Synopsis: In "A New Voyage Round the World by a Course Never Sailed Before," embark on an extraordinary journey with the adventurous protagonist as he sets sail on a daring expedition to uncharted territories. Filled with unexpected encounters, perilous challenges, and fascinating discoveries, this gripping tale will transport readers to the far corners of the globe, unveiling the wonders and mysteries of the unknown. Brace yourself for a thrilling adventure that will leave you captivated until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, Survival
