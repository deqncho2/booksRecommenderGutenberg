Synopsis: 
'The Best Short Stories of 1921, and the Yearbook of the American Short Story' is a captivating collection of diverse and thought-provoking tales that will transport readers to different worlds and evoke a range of emotions. From tales of love and loss to stories of mystery and adventure, this anthology showcases the finest examples of storytelling from the year 1921. Each story is carefully selected to captivate and engage readers, leaving them eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead. With a blend of well-known authors and emerging talents, this collection promises to be a literary journey like no other.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Anthology

Themes: Love, Loss, Mystery, Adventure, Human Nature
