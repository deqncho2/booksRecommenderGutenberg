Synopsis: In 'As a Matter of Course', Annie Payson Call takes readers on a captivating journey of self-discovery and personal growth. Through her own experiences and observations, she explores the power of mindset and the importance of cultivating a positive attitude in the face of life's challenges. With wit and wisdom, Call offers practical advice on how to navigate the complexities of daily life, find inner peace, and achieve true happiness. This thought-provoking book is a must-read for anyone seeking to transform their perspective and live a more fulfilling life.

Genres: Self-help, Personal Development, Psychology

Themes: Mindset, Positive Thinking, Personal Growth, Happiness
