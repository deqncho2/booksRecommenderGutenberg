Synopsis: 
'Frontier Ballads' by Joseph Mills Hanson takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed American frontier. This collection of ballads weaves together tales of love, loss, adventure, and resilience, painting a vivid picture of life on the edge of civilization. From the dusty trails of the Wild West to the rugged landscapes of the Rocky Mountains, each ballad transports readers to a different time and place, immersing them in the struggles and triumphs of the pioneers who shaped the American West. With its lyrical storytelling and rich imagery, 'Frontier Ballads' is a captivating homage to the spirit of the frontier.

Genres: Poetry, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Love and Loss, Resilience
