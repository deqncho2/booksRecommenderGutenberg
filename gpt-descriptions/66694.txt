Synopsis: In volume 10 of "The Historians' History of the World," delve into the captivating history of Spain and Portugal. From the legendary tales of conquistadors to the intricate web of political intrigue, this volume unveils the rich tapestry of these Iberian nations. Discover the rise and fall of empires, the clash of cultures, and the exploration of new worlds that shaped the destiny of these influential countries. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, this volume offers a fascinating journey through the captivating history of Spain and Portugal.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Cultural studies

Themes: Exploration, Colonialism, Power dynamics, Cultural exchange
