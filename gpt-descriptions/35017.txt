Synopsis: 
In 'Half-Hours with the Idiot', John Kendrick Bangs takes readers on a hilarious and absurd journey through the mind of an eccentric and clueless character. From his misadventures in love to his comical encounters with society, the idiot's antics will leave readers in stitches. With each chapter presenting a new escapade, this collection of short stories is a delightful and entertaining exploration of the absurdities of human behavior.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Absurdity, Social Commentary, Miscommunication
