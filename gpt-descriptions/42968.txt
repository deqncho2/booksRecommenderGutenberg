Synopsis: In "The Riddle of the Universe at the close of the nineteenth century," Ernst Haeckel takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysteries of existence. With a blend of scientific inquiry and philosophical exploration, Haeckel delves into the fundamental questions that have puzzled humanity for centuries. From the origins of life to the nature of consciousness, he presents a thought-provoking analysis that challenges conventional beliefs and offers new perspectives on the enigmatic universe we inhabit. As Haeckel unravels the intricate web of existence, readers are left pondering the profound implications of his revelations.

Genres: Science, Philosophy, Non-fiction

Themes: Evolution, Cosmology, Consciousness, Existentialism
