Synopsis: 
In the United States Declaration of Independence, Thomas Jefferson presents a powerful and thought-provoking argument for the separation of the American colonies from British rule. Through eloquent prose and compelling reasoning, Jefferson explores the fundamental rights of all individuals and the inherent duty to challenge oppressive governments. This seminal document not only lays the groundwork for a new nation but also serves as a timeless reminder of the importance of liberty and self-determination.

Genres: Political philosophy, Historical document

Themes: Freedom, Independence, Individual rights, Government oppression
