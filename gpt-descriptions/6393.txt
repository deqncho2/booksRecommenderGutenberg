Synopsis: In "The Lives of the Twelve Caesars, Volume 08: Otho," Suetonius delves into the captivating life of Otho, a Roman emperor whose reign was marked by ambition, political intrigue, and unexpected twists. From his rise to power to his tragic downfall, this gripping account unveils the complex personality and tumultuous journey of a man driven by his desire for glory and love. As Otho navigates the treacherous world of Roman politics, readers are taken on a thrilling journey filled with power struggles, betrayals, and the ultimate quest for self-preservation.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Ambition, Power, Political Intrigue, Love, Betrayal
