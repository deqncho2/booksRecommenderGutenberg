Synopsis: 

"A libell of Spanish lies: found at the sacke of Cales, discoursing the fight in the West Indies, twixt the English navie being fourteene ships and pinasses, and a fleete of twentie saile of the king of Spaines, and of the death of Sir Francis Drake" is a captivating historical account written by Captain Henry Savile. The book delves into the intense battle that took place in the West Indies between the English navy, led by Sir Francis Drake, and a formidable fleet of twenty ships commanded by the king of Spain. Amidst the chaos and danger, the narrative also explores the tragic death of Sir Francis Drake, a renowned English explorer and naval officer. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Courage and bravery, Exploration and conquest, Betrayal and deception
