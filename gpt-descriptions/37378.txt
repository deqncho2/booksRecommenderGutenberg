Synopsis: In 'The Secret Toll' by Thorne, Mabel and Thorne, Paul, a small town is plagued by a mysterious toll that exacts a heavy price from its residents. As the toll's dark secrets begin to unravel, a group of unlikely allies must uncover the truth and confront the malevolent force behind it. With each passing day, the toll grows stronger, threatening to consume everything in its path. Will they be able to break the curse before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, the power of unity, the battle between good and evil
