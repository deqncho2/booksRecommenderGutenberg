Synopsis: In 'War, 52 Carey Cartoons', embark on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking cartoons that explore the complexities and consequences of war. Each cartoon presents a unique perspective, inviting readers to reflect on the impact of conflict on individuals and society as a whole. From poignant moments of humanity to satirical critiques, this collection offers a powerful and engaging exploration of the multifaceted nature of war.

Genres: Graphic Novel, Political Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: War, Conflict, Humanity, Consequences, Satire
