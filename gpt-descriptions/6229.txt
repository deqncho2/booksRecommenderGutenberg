Synopsis: 
Set in the backdrop of 18th century Quebec, 'The Seats of the Mighty, Complete' follows the gripping tale of a young British officer, Robert Moray, who finds himself caught in the midst of political intrigue, espionage, and a dangerous love affair. As tensions rise between the British and French forces, Moray must navigate a treacherous web of loyalties and secrets, where every decision could mean life or death. With the fate of a nation hanging in the balance, Moray's courage and cunning will be put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Power, Love, War
