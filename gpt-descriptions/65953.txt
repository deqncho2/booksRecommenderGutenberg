Synopsis: In 'Our Irish Theatre: A chapter of autobiography', Lady Gregory takes us on a captivating journey through the vibrant world of Irish theatre. With her sharp wit and insightful observations, she shares her personal experiences and encounters with some of the greatest playwrights and actors of her time. From the struggles and triumphs of the Abbey Theatre to the cultural and political backdrop of Ireland, Lady Gregory's memoir offers a fascinating glimpse into the evolution of Irish theatre and the indomitable spirit of its people.

Genres: Autobiography, Theatre, History

Themes: Irish identity, Cultural revival, Artistic expression, Nationalism
