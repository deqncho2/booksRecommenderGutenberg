Synopsis:
In "True to the Old Flag: A Tale of the American War of Independence," follow the thrilling adventures of young Harry Sandwith as he joins the British army during the American Revolution. From the Battle of Bunker Hill to the Siege of Charleston, Harry finds himself in the midst of intense battles, daring escapes, and encounters with historical figures like George Washington and Benjamin Franklin. As he navigates the complexities of war and loyalty, Harry's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he remain true to his country or follow his own path?

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes:
Patriotism, Loyalty, Courage, Identity
