Synopsis: In 'A Village of Vagabonds' by F. Berkeley Smith, a mysterious group of wanderers descends upon a small, unsuspecting village, forever changing the lives of its inhabitants. As the villagers grapple with the arrival of these enigmatic strangers, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of humanity is put to the ultimate test. With its captivating blend of suspense, intrigue, and unexpected twists, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the complexities of human nature and the power of community.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Community, Morality, Secrets
