Synopsis: 

"The Popham Colony: A Discussion of its Historical Claims, with a Bibliography of the Subject" delves into the intriguing history of the Popham Colony, a short-lived English settlement in the early 17th century. Authors Poole, Kidder, and Ballard present a comprehensive analysis of the colony's historical significance, exploring its origins, challenges, and ultimate demise. Through meticulous research and engaging discussions, the book sheds light on the mysteries surrounding this forgotten chapter of American history, captivating readers with its exploration of the colony's impact on early English colonization efforts in North America.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, American history, Colonial history

Themes: Exploration and colonization, Historical significance, Forgotten history
