Synopsis: In "Under Blanco's Eye; or, Hal Maynard among the Cuban insurgents" by Douglas Wells, readers are transported to the tumultuous world of Cuba during the late 19th century. Hal Maynard, a young American journalist, finds himself immersed in the dangerous and thrilling world of the Cuban insurgents fighting for independence against Spanish rule. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of war, Maynard becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, romance, and personal growth. With each passing page, the stakes rise higher, and Maynard must confront his own beliefs and loyalties in the face of a nation's struggle for freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: War and Revolution, Identity and Belonging, Love and Sacrifice
