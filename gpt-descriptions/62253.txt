Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Panadur, a young girl named Luna discovers a mysterious star that holds the power to change her life forever. As Luna embarks on a thrilling adventure to protect the star from falling into the wrong hands, she encounters magical creatures, uncovers ancient secrets, and learns the true meaning of courage and friendship. With danger lurking at every turn, Luna must summon all her strength to fulfill her destiny and save Panadur from darkness.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Destiny, Friendship, Courage, Magic
