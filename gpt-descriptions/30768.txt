Synopsis: In "Sowing and Reaping" by Dwight Lyman Moody, a young man named Harry Graham finds himself at a crossroads in life after a series of unfortunate events. Struggling with the consequences of his choices, Harry embarks on a journey of self-discovery and redemption. As he encounters various characters and experiences, he begins to understand the power of sowing good seeds and reaping the rewards of righteousness. Will Harry be able to turn his life around and find true purpose and fulfillment?

Genres: Christian fiction, Inspirational, Self-help

Themes: Redemption, Consequences of choices, Spiritual growth
