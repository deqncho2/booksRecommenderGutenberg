Synopsis: 
In 'Dave Porter and the Runaways; Or, Last Days at Oak Hall' by Edward Stratemeyer, Dave Porter finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure as he tries to uncover the truth behind a mysterious disappearance at his boarding school, Oak Hall. When two of his classmates suddenly vanish without a trace, Dave takes it upon himself to investigate, leading him on a dangerous journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Dave discovers shocking secrets that could change everything he thought he knew about his friends and the school. Will he be able to solve the puzzle before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Coming of Age
