Synopsis: In Volume 28 of his captivating memoirs, Jacques Casanova de Seingalt takes us on a thrilling journey through the enchanting city of Rome. Set in the 18th century, this volume delves into Casanova's encounters with influential figures, his passionate love affairs, and his daring escapades in the heart of the Eternal City. From secret societies to scandalous liaisons, Casanova's vivid storytelling transports readers to a world of intrigue, desire, and danger.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Power and Influence, Society and Morality
