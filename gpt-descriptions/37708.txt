Synopsis: In 'The Magic Bed: A Book of East Indian Fairy-Tales', embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of East Indian folklore. Discover tales of brave heroes, cunning tricksters, and magical creatures as they navigate through challenges and seek their destinies. From mystical forests to royal palaces, these timeless stories will transport readers to a realm filled with wonder and adventure. 

Genres: Fairy Tales, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Magic, Destiny, Morality
