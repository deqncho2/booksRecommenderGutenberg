Synopsis: In 'Lister's Great Adventure' by Harold Bindloss, a young and ambitious engineer named Lister embarks on a thrilling journey to a remote and untamed region. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters unexpected dangers, Lister's determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Along the way, he forms unlikely alliances and discovers hidden secrets that could change his life forever. This gripping tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Redemption
