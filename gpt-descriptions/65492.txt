Synopsis: In "The Goose-step: A Study of American Education," Upton Sinclair takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the American education system. Through his meticulous research and insightful analysis, Sinclair uncovers the hidden forces shaping the minds of young students and the impact it has on society as a whole. From the influence of corporate interests to the suppression of critical thinking, Sinclair exposes the alarming truths behind the education system, leaving readers questioning the very foundations of their own education.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, Social Criticism

Themes: Education system, Corporate influence, Critical thinking, Social control
