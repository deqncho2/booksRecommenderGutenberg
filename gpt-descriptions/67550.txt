Synopsis: In 'Troubled Waters' by Sinclair, Bertrand W., a small coastal town is shaken by a mysterious series of events that threaten to unravel the community's peaceful existence. As the townspeople grapple with the mounting tensions and secrets that surface, they must confront their own demons and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of their beloved town. With its gripping narrative and complex characters, 'Troubled Waters' delves into the dark undercurrents of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Secrets and lies, Community, Redemption
