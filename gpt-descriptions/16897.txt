Synopsis: In 'The Tables Turned; or, Nupkins Awakened. A Socialist Interlude' by Morris, William, a seemingly ordinary man named Nupkins finds himself caught in a world turned upside down. As he embarks on a journey of self-discovery, he encounters a group of individuals who challenge his beliefs and open his eyes to a new way of life. With each encounter, Nupkins is forced to question the societal norms he has always accepted, leading him down a path of enlightenment and revolution.

Genres: Political fiction, Social commentary, Satire

Themes: Social inequality, Revolution, Individual awakening
