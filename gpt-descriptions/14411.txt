Synopsis: In 'The Children's Six Minutes', a small town is rocked by a shocking event when six children mysteriously vanish without a trace. As the community grapples with fear and uncertainty, a determined detective and a grieving mother join forces to uncover the truth behind the disappearances. With each passing minute, the urgency to find the children intensifies, leading them down a dark and twisted path filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will they be able to unravel the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Loss and grief, Community solidarity, Parental instincts, The power of determination
