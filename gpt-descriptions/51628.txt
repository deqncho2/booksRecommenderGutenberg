Synopsis: In 'Delaware; or, The Ruined Family. Vol. 1', James, G. P. R. takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the Delaware family. Set in the early 19th century, this gripping tale follows the family's struggles, secrets, and the consequences of their actions. As the story unfolds, readers are drawn into a web of mystery, romance, and betrayal, as the Delawares navigate their way through a world filled with danger and intrigue. With each page, the plot thickens, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth behind the family's ruin.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
