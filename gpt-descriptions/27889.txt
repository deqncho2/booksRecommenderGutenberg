Synopsis: 
'Familiar Quotations: A Collection of Passages, Phrases, and Proverbs Traced to Their Sources in Ancient and Modern Literature' is a captivating compilation that takes readers on a journey through the vast world of literature. This meticulously curated collection unveils the origins of well-known quotes, phrases, and proverbs, revealing the hidden stories behind their creation. From ancient texts to contemporary works, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the literary influences that have shaped our language and culture.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Literary Criticism

Themes: Language and Communication, Literary Influence, Cultural Significance
