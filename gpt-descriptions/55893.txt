Synopsis: In 'Peeps at Many Lands: Portugal', Agnes M. Goodall takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and diverse country of Portugal. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, the author introduces readers to the rich history, stunning landscapes, and unique cultural traditions of this enchanting land. From the bustling streets of Lisbon to the picturesque vineyards of the Douro Valley, readers will be transported to the heart of Portugal, discovering its hidden gems and immersing themselves in its captivating atmosphere. With a focus on both the past and the present, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the country's traditions, architecture, cuisine, and way of life. Whether you are a seasoned traveler or simply curious about the world, 'Peeps at Many Lands: Portugal' is sure to ignite your wanderlust and leave you yearning to explore this captivating country.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Cultural Exploration

Themes: History, Culture, Geography, Traditions
