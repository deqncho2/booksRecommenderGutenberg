Synopsis: In "Molly McDonald: A Tale of the Old Frontier," we follow the courageous journey of Molly McDonald, a young woman who finds herself thrust into the untamed wilderness of the Old Frontier. As she navigates the challenges of survival, Molly must confront her own inner demons and discover the strength within her to overcome the hardships that come her way. With danger lurking at every turn, Molly's resilience and determination will be put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to conquer the frontier and find her place in this rugged world?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Survival, Self-discovery, Resilience
