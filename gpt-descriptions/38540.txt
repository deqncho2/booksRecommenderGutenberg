Synopsis: In 'A Scout of To-day' by Isabel Hornibrook, follow the thrilling journey of a young scout named Tom as he embarks on a daring adventure that will test his courage, resourcefulness, and loyalty. As Tom navigates through the wilderness, facing unexpected challenges and encountering mysterious characters, he must rely on his scout training and instincts to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way. With each step, Tom discovers the true meaning of bravery and the importance of community, ultimately transforming into a remarkable scout of his time.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Resourcefulness, Loyalty, Community
