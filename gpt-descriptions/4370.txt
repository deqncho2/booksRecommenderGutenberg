Synopsis: 
"The Deeds of God Through the Franks" by Guibert, Abbot of Nogent-sous-Coucy is a captivating historical account that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous times of the Crusades. Through vivid storytelling and personal anecdotes, Guibert paints a vivid picture of the bravery, treachery, and religious fervor that defined this era. From the battles fought on distant lands to the inner struggles faced by the Crusaders, this chronicle offers a unique perspective on the clash of cultures and the profound impact it had on both the Franks and the Muslim world.

Genres: Historical fiction, Memoir, Religious literature

Themes: Religious conflict, Heroism, Cultural exchange
