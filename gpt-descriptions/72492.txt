Synopsis: 
'Christmas Carols: Old English Carols for Christmas and Other Festivals' is a captivating collection of traditional carols that transport readers to the enchanting world of festive celebrations. From joyful songs of merriment to heartfelt melodies of reflection, this anthology showcases the rich heritage of Old English carols, offering a delightful journey through the holiday season and beyond. With each carol, readers will be immersed in the spirit of Christmas, experiencing the warmth, love, and joy that this special time of year brings.

Genres: 
Music, Poetry, Folklore

Themes: 
Christmas, Festivals, Tradition, Joy, Reflection
