Synopsis: In 'The Malefactor' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious and enigmatic man named Felix is released from prison after serving a long sentence for a crime he claims he did not commit. Determined to uncover the truth and seek justice, Felix embarks on a thrilling journey that takes him across Europe, encountering danger, romance, and unexpected allies along the way. As he delves deeper into the web of deceit and betrayal, Felix must confront his own past and make difficult choices that will ultimately determine his fate.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Innocence and guilt, Redemption, Betrayal, Justice
