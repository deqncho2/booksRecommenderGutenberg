Synopsis: In 'The Lash' by Olin L. Lyman, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, bringing with him a dark secret and a hidden agenda. As the townspeople become entangled in his web of deceit, they must confront their own demons and make difficult choices that will test their loyalty and integrity. With its gripping suspense and unexpected twists, 'The Lash' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Morality, Consequences
