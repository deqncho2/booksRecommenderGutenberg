Synopsis: In "The Crimp" by Leverage, Henry, a skilled con artist, finds himself caught in a high-stakes game of deception when he is recruited by a mysterious organization. As he delves deeper into the world of cons and scams, Henry must navigate a web of lies, betrayal, and unexpected alliances to survive. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving Henry questioning his own identity and motives. This gripping tale of manipulation and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Crime

Themes: Deception, Identity, Betrayal
