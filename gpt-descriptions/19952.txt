Synopsis: In 'To the Front: A Sequel to Cadet Days' by Charles King, the story follows the adventures of a young cadet who finds himself thrust into the heart of a dangerous conflict. As he navigates the challenges of war, he must confront his own fears and discover the true meaning of courage. With vivid descriptions and gripping action, this sequel takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Coming of Age, Patriotism
