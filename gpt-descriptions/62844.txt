Synopsis: 

In 'The Fair Dominion: A Record of Canadian Impressions', author R. E. Vernède takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes and vibrant cities of Canada. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Vernède paints a vivid picture of the country's natural beauty, cultural richness, and the unique experiences he encounters during his travels. From the majestic Rocky Mountains to the bustling streets of Montreal, the author's observations and encounters with the people and places of Canada offer a fascinating glimpse into the nation's identity and character.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Natural beauty, National identity
