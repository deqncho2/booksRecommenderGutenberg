Synopsis: In "In a Syrian Saddle," the adventurous Ada Goodrich-Freer takes us on a captivating journey through the heart of Syria, immersing us in the rich tapestry of its landscapes, people, and customs. From traversing the vast desert on horseback to encountering mysterious Bedouin tribes, Goodrich-Freer's vivid descriptions transport us to a world of exotic beauty and intrigue. As she delves deeper into the Syrian culture, she uncovers hidden secrets and encounters unexpected challenges that test her resilience and determination. This gripping travelogue is a captivating exploration of a land steeped in history and a testament to the indomitable spirit of adventure.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Resilience
