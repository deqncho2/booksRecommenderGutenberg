Synopsis: In "A Lady of Quality," Mr. Isaac Bickerstaff unveils a captivating and previously unknown history that has remained hidden from the world of fashion. This enthralling tale, not presented in The Tatler, is now revealed for the first time. Set in the vibrant society of 17th-century England, the story follows the remarkable journey of a woman who defies societal expectations and challenges the constraints placed upon her. With secrets, scandals, and unexpected twists, this historical novel takes readers on a thrilling exploration of love, power, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Gender roles, Social class, Independence, Love and relationships
