Synopsis: 
In "Mrs. Raffles: Being the Adventures of an Amateur Crackswoman," the enigmatic and audacious Mrs. Raffles takes center stage as she embarks on a series of thrilling escapades. With her quick wit, cunning, and a penchant for mischief, she becomes an amateur detective, cracking cases that baffle the authorities. From daring heists to solving perplexing mysteries, Mrs. Raffles proves to be a force to be reckoned with, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following her every move.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Female empowerment, wit and intelligence, the thrill of the chase
