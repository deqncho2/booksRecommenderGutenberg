Synopsis: In 'In Luck at Last' by Besant, Walter, a down-on-his-luck protagonist finds himself in a series of unexpected and fortunate events that completely transform his life. From chance encounters to unforeseen opportunities, this captivating tale takes readers on a rollercoaster ride of luck and destiny. As the protagonist navigates through the twists and turns of his newfound fortune, he must grapple with the consequences of his choices and the true meaning of luck.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Luck, Fate, Redemption
