Synopsis: 

In 'Placebo' by David Mason, a renowned psychiatrist finds himself caught in a web of deception and manipulation when he agrees to participate in a groundbreaking medical experiment. As he delves deeper into the study, he begins to question the true nature of reality and the ethical boundaries of science. With his own sanity at stake, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and shocking revelations.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Suspense, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Perception, Morality, Ethics
