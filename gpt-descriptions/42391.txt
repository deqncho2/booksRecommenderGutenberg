Synopsis: 

'Our National Forests: A Short Popular Account of the Work of the United States Forest Service on the National Forests' takes readers on a captivating journey through the remarkable efforts of the United States Forest Service in preserving and managing the national forests. Richard H. D. Boerker provides a fascinating and accessible account of the agency's work, highlighting the challenges faced and the incredible impact achieved in safeguarding these precious natural resources. From the awe-inspiring landscapes to the dedicated individuals behind the scenes, this book offers a compelling exploration of the United States Forest Service's vital role in protecting our national forests.

Genres: Non-fiction, Environmental Science, Conservation

Themes: Conservation, Environmental Stewardship, Natural Resource Management
