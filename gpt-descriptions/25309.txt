Synopsis: In 'Child's Story of the Bible', Mary A. Lathbury takes young readers on a captivating journey through the timeless stories of the Bible. From the creation of the world to the miracles of Jesus, this beautifully written and illustrated book brings the Bible to life in a way that is accessible and engaging for children. Through vivid storytelling and colorful illustrations, Lathbury introduces young readers to the key figures and events of the Bible, inspiring them to explore the teachings and lessons within these sacred stories.

Genres: Children's Literature, Religious Literature, Historical Fiction

Themes: Faith, Morality, Redemption, Divine Intervention
