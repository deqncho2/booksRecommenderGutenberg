Synopsis: In 'Wild Animals at Home', Ernest Thompson Seton takes readers on a captivating journey into the untamed world of wildlife. Through vivid storytelling and detailed observations, Seton introduces us to a diverse array of creatures, from cunning foxes to majestic eagles, and from mischievous raccoons to elusive mountain lions. With each encounter, he unveils the hidden lives of these animals, shedding light on their behaviors, habitats, and the challenges they face in their struggle for survival. Seton's remarkable ability to bring these wild animals to life on the page will leave readers spellbound, fostering a deep appreciation for the beauty and complexity of the natural world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Adventure

Themes: Wildlife conservation, Animal behavior, Human-animal interactions
