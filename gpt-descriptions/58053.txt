Synopsis: In 'A Correct Account of the Horrible Occurrence Which Took Place at a Public-House in St. James's Market', an anonymous author unveils a scandalous event involving the Right Rev. Father in God, the Bishop of Clogher. Recently transferred from the Bishopric of Ferns, the Bishop finds himself entangled in a shocking incident with a common soldier at a public-house. This revelation not only brings disgrace upon the clergy and the Board of Education, of which the Bishop is a commissioner, but also tarnishes the reputation of the nation that gave him birth. The story delves into the scandalous actions of a prominent figure, leaving readers eager to uncover the details and consequences of this shocking event.

Genres: Historical fiction, scandalous literature, political satire
Themes: Morality, hypocrisy, social status, scandal, power dynamics
