Synopsis: In "A Voice from Waterloo: A History of the Battle Fought on the 18th June, 1815," Edward Cotton takes readers on a gripping journey back to one of the most significant battles in history. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Cotton brings to life the events of the Battle of Waterloo, revealing the bravery, strategies, and sacrifices made by both sides. From the chaos of the battlefield to the personal stories of soldiers and commanders, this book offers a captivating account of a pivotal moment in European history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: War and conflict, Leadership, Heroism, Historical significance
