Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Samuel Whiskers; Or, The Roly-Poly Pudding', Beatrix Potter takes readers on a thrilling adventure as Tom Kitten finds himself in a precarious situation. When he ventures into the home of Samuel Whiskers, a cunning rat, Tom must rely on his wit and bravery to escape. With suspenseful twists and turns, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering if Tom will make it out alive.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Bravery, Survival, Ingenuity
