Synopsis: In this thought-provoking sermon, delivered in 1868, Reverend Charles John Vaughan explores the future prospects of the Church of England. Addressing the controversial issue of church rates, he delves into the significance of the first offertory as an alternative means of funding the church. With eloquence and passion, Vaughan examines the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead for the Church of England, leaving the reader eager to discover his insights and predictions.

Genres: Sermon, Religious Literature

Themes: Church funding, Future of the Church of England, Controversial issues in religion
