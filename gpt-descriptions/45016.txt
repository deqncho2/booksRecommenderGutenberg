Synopsis: 

'With Rod and Line in Colorado Waters' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Colorado, as the author, Lewis B. France, recounts his adventures in pursuit of the elusive trout. From the rushing rivers to the serene mountain lakes, France shares his passion for fishing and the natural beauty that surrounds him. As he navigates the challenges of angling in these pristine waters, he also delves into the rich history and folklore of the region, providing a captivating blend of personal anecdotes and informative insights. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Outdoor Literature

Themes: Nature appreciation, Fishing, Adventure, Conservation
