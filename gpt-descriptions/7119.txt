Synopsis: In the small New England town of Eliot, a mysterious family arrives, captivating the townspeople with their enigmatic past and peculiar behavior. As the Dolliver family's story unfolds, secrets are revealed, love is tested, and the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur. With its rich symbolism and intricate character development, "The Dolliver Romance" takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the complexities of human nature and the power of imagination.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Psychological

Themes: Identity, Love and Desire, Illusion and Reality
