Synopsis: In 'The Missing Pocket-Book; Or, Tom Mason's Luck' by Castlemon, Harry, a young boy named Tom Mason finds himself in possession of a mysterious pocket-book that holds secrets and dangers beyond his imagination. As he embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the pocket-book, Tom must navigate treacherous situations, encounter unexpected allies, and face formidable adversaries. With each twist and turn, Tom's luck is tested, and he must rely on his wit and courage to survive. Will he unravel the mystery and emerge victorious, or will the pocket-book's secrets consume him?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Discovery
