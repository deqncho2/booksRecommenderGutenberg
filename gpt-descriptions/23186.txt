Synopsis: In 'The Handbook to English Heraldry', Charles Boutell takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of heraldry, unraveling the secrets and symbolism behind the ancient art of coats of arms. From the majestic lions and fierce dragons to the intricate patterns and vibrant colors, Boutell delves into the fascinating history and significance of each element, revealing the stories of the noble families and their ancestral lineages. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, this handbook serves as a captivating guide for both heraldry enthusiasts and curious readers alike, unlocking the hidden meanings and tales behind these timeless symbols.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Art

Themes: Symbolism, Heritage, Nobility
