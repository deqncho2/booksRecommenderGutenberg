Synopsis: 
"The Complete Project Gutenberg Works of Jane Austen: A Linked Index of all PG Editions of Jane Austen" is a comprehensive collection of the beloved author's works, including all of her novels, letters, and unfinished manuscripts. This indexed edition allows readers to easily navigate through Austen's captivating stories, exploring the intricate lives of her memorable characters and immersing themselves in the world of Regency England. From the witty social commentary to the timeless themes of love, marriage, and societal expectations, this collection offers a delightful journey through Austen's literary genius.

Genres: 
Classic Literature, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Love and Marriage, Social Class and Society, Female Agency and Independence
