Synopsis: In "Matter, Ether, and Motion: The Factors and Relations of Physical Science," Amos Emerson Dolbear takes readers on a captivating journey through the fundamental principles of physical science. This revised and expanded edition delves into the intricate connections between matter, ether, and motion, unraveling the mysteries of the universe in a thought-provoking and accessible manner. Dolbear's exploration of these scientific concepts will leave readers with a newfound appreciation for the intricate workings of the world around them.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Physics

Themes: Scientific inquiry, Fundamental principles, Universal connections
