Synopsis: In 'Kindred of the Dust' by Peter B. Kyne, a young man named John Cardigan inherits his father's lumber empire in the rugged redwoods of California. As he navigates the challenges of running the business, he becomes entangled in a bitter feud with a rival timber baron. Amidst the backdrop of a changing world and the looming threat of war, John must confront his own demons and fight for what he believes is right, even if it means sacrificing everything he holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Family legacy, Love and loss, Morality and ethics, Class struggle
