Synopsis: 

In the second volume of "The Chronicles of Enguerrand de Monstrelet," readers are transported to a tumultuous period in French history. The narrative delves into the brutal civil wars between the houses of Orleans and Burgundy, as well as the English occupation of Paris and Normandy. Enguerrand de Monstrelet vividly recounts the expulsion of the English from these territories and chronicles other significant events that unfolded not only in France but also in neighboring countries. With a keen eye for detail and a captivating storytelling style, Monstrelet offers a gripping account of this pivotal era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Chronicle, Memoir

Themes: War and conflict, Political power struggles, Historical events and their impact
