Synopsis: 

In "History of the Scottish expedition to Norway in 1612," Thomas Michell takes us on a thrilling journey back in time to a little-known chapter in Scottish history. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue and international tensions, this gripping account follows a group of Scottish adventurers as they embark on a daring expedition to Norway. Filled with unexpected twists and turns, the story unfolds with a mix of bravery, treachery, and unexpected alliances. As the expedition faces numerous challenges and encounters, the reader is left on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to discover the fate of these intrepid explorers.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, adventure, political intrigue

Themes: Exploration, national identity, diplomacy, loyalty, survival
