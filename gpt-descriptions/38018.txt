Synopsis: In "Girl Scouts in the Rockies" by Lillian Elizabeth Roy, a group of adventurous Girl Scouts embark on a thrilling journey to the majestic Rocky Mountains. As they navigate through the breathtaking landscapes, they encounter unexpected challenges and forge lifelong friendships. With each new obstacle they face, the girls learn valuable lessons about courage, resilience, and the power of teamwork. This heartwarming tale of adventure and self-discovery will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Teamwork
