Synopsis: 

In Volume 2 of "Narrative of the Voyage of H.M.S. Rattlesnake," John Macgillivray takes readers on an enthralling journey through the years 1846-1850, as he recounts the discoveries and surveys made by the ship in New Guinea and the Louisiade Archipelago. Alongside these fascinating explorations, Macgillivray also includes the account of Mr. E.B. Kennedy's expedition, which aimed to explore the Cape York Peninsula. As the naturalist to the expedition, Macgillivray provides vivid descriptions of the landscapes, encounters with indigenous peoples, and the challenges faced by the crew during their voyage.

Genres: Exploration, Travelogue, Natural History

Themes: Adventure, Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Natural Sciences
