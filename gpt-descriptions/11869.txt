Synopsis:
In the enchanting world of Regency England, a young and spirited Venetia finds herself caught between the allure of society's expectations and the yearning for true love. With her sharp wit and unconventional charm, she captivates the hearts of those around her, including the enigmatic Lord Cadurcis. As their lives intertwine, secrets are unveiled, passions ignite, and Venetia must navigate the treacherous waters of love and betrayal. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant and decadent society, 'Venetia' is a captivating tale of romance, intrigue, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes:
Love and Desire, Society and Conformity, Betrayal and Redemption
