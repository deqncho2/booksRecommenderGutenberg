Synopsis: In 'Dairy Disagreeables Busy the Bacteriologists', four brilliant bacteriologists find themselves embroiled in a mysterious case involving contaminated dairy products. As they delve deeper into their investigation, they uncover a web of deceit, sabotage, and unexpected alliances. With time running out, they must race against the clock to uncover the truth and save the reputation of the dairy industry. This thrilling and suspenseful tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Science and technology, Deception and betrayal, Justice and truth
