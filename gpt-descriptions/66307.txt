Synopsis: In the second volume of her memoirs, Bertha von Suttner continues to captivate readers with her extraordinary life experiences. From her involvement in the peace movement to her encounters with influential figures, she takes us on a journey through the challenges and triumphs that shaped her eventful life. As she navigates the complexities of love, politics, and personal growth, Bertha's story unfolds with gripping intensity, leaving readers eager to discover how she overcomes the obstacles that come her way.

Genres: Memoir, Biography, Historical

Themes: Peace activism, Love and relationships, Personal growth, Social change
