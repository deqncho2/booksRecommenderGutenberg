Synopsis: 

In "Narrative of the Life of Moses Grandy, Late a Slave in the United States of America," Moses Grandy shares his remarkable journey from being a slave to gaining his freedom. Through vivid and poignant storytelling, Grandy takes readers on a harrowing and eye-opening exploration of the brutal realities of slavery, the resilience of the human spirit, and the pursuit of freedom against all odds. From his early days as a young boy on a plantation to his experiences as a skilled waterman and his eventual escape, Grandy's narrative offers a unique perspective on the institution of slavery and the indomitable will to overcome it.

Genres: Autobiography, Slave narrative, Historical non-fiction

Themes: Slavery, Freedom, Resilience, Injustice, Human spirit
