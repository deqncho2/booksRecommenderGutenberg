Synopsis: In 'The Black Bear' by William H. Wright, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious and brutal attacks, leaving the residents terrified and searching for answers. As the tension rises, a group of unlikely allies must come together to uncover the truth behind these horrifying events. With each revelation, they find themselves drawn deeper into a dark and dangerous world they never knew existed. Will they be able to stop the relentless terror before it consumes them all?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Fear, Survival, Community
