Synopsis: In the picturesque coastal town of Orr's Island, young Mara Lincoln grows up surrounded by the beauty of nature and the strength of her community. As she navigates the challenges of life, Mara's journey intertwines with the lives of those around her, including the enigmatic Captain Kit, the wise Aunt Roxy, and the mysterious Mrs. Snow. Through love, loss, and the pursuit of dreams, Mara discovers the power of resilience and the enduring bonds of friendship.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: Resilience, Community, Nature, Love, Loss
