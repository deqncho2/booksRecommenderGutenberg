Synopsis: In 'The Marooner' by Charles A. Stearns, a young sailor finds himself stranded on a deserted island after a treacherous shipwreck. As he struggles to survive in this harsh and unforgiving environment, he discovers that he is not alone. The island is inhabited by a mysterious marooner, a man with a dark past and a hidden agenda. As their paths intertwine, the sailor must navigate a dangerous game of trust and betrayal, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. Will he be able to escape the clutches of the marooner and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Trust, Betrayal
