Synopsis: In "The World As I Have Found It: Sequel to Incidents in the Life of a Blind Girl," Mary L. Day continues her captivating memoir, delving deeper into her experiences as a blind woman navigating a sighted world. From her struggles with societal expectations to her triumphs in education and love, Day's story is a testament to the resilience of the human spirit. As she encounters new challenges and encounters, the reader is drawn into her world, witnessing the power of determination and the beauty of human connection.

Genres: Memoir, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Disability, Resilience, Education, Love, Society
