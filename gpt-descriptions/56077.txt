Synopsis: 
In 'Love Insurance' by Earl Derr Biggers, a wealthy American businessman is determined to protect his daughter from fortune hunters. To ensure her happiness, he devises an ingenious plan involving a fake marriage and a hefty insurance policy. However, when a charming British aristocrat enters the picture, the lines between love and deception become blurred, leading to a series of unexpected twists and turns. As the stakes rise, the characters must navigate a web of secrets and hidden agendas, all while questioning the true nature of love and the lengths one will go to protect it.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Love and deception, Wealth and social status, Family dynamics
