Synopsis: In this captivating address, Honorable Franklin K. Lane, Secretary of the Interior, delivers a powerful speech at the Conference of Regional Chairmen of the Highways Transport Committee Council of National Defense. With eloquence and passion, Lane discusses the crucial role of transportation in national defense and the importance of efficient highways. He explores the challenges faced by the United States during a time of war and emphasizes the need for a well-connected and robust transportation system. Lane's address serves as a call to action, inspiring the audience to prioritize and invest in the development of highways for the greater good of the nation.

Genres: Speech, Historical, Political

Themes: National defense, Transportation infrastructure, Patriotism
