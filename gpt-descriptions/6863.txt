Synopsis: In "The Passing of New France: a Chronicle of Montcalm" by William Wood, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the French and Indian War. Through the eyes of the valiant General Louis-Joseph de Montcalm, the narrative delves into the intense battles, political intrigues, and personal sacrifices that shaped the destiny of New France. As Montcalm navigates the treacherous landscape of war, readers are immersed in a gripping tale of honor, loyalty, and the inevitable clash of empires.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Biographical fiction

Themes: War and its consequences, Loyalty and sacrifice, Clash of empires
