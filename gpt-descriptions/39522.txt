Synopsis: In 'The Rulers of the Mediterranean' by Richard Harding Davis, a group of wealthy and influential individuals embark on a luxurious yacht trip across the Mediterranean Sea. As they sail through picturesque landscapes and visit exotic destinations, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, power struggles, and forbidden desires. With each passing day, the tension among the passengers grows, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. This thrilling tale explores the dark underbelly of wealth and power, revealing the lengths people will go to protect their status and the consequences that follow.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Power, Wealth, Intrigue, Betrayal
