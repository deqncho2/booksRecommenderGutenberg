Synopsis: 
In a small village, a young woman named Belle finds herself trapped in a mysterious castle with a fearsome Beast. As she begins to unravel the secrets of the castle and the curse that binds its inhabitants, Belle discovers that there is more to the Beast than meets the eye. Through their unlikely friendship, Belle learns the power of compassion, inner beauty, and the true nature of love.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Romance, Fairy Tale

Themes: 
Beauty and appearance, Inner beauty, Love and sacrifice
