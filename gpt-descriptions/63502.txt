Synopsis: In the picturesque Welsh village of Torn Sails, secrets lie buried beneath the surface of its idyllic facade. When a mysterious newcomer arrives, the lives of the villagers are thrown into disarray, as long-held grudges and hidden desires come to light. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the village must confront its past and face the consequences of their actions. 'Torn Sails: A Tale of a Welsh Village' is a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and redemption that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Secrets, Forgiveness, Community
