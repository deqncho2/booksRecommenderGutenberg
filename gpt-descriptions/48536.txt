Synopsis: In 'The Motor Boys on the Border; Or, Sixty Nuggets of Gold', the adventurous trio of Jack, Mark, and Andy find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey to the Mexican border. Their mission? To uncover a hidden treasure trove of sixty gold nuggets. As they navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter dangerous outlaws, and face unexpected challenges, the Motor Boys must rely on their wit, courage, and trust in each other to succeed in their quest. Will they be able to outsmart their adversaries and claim the golden prize?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Treasure Hunting
