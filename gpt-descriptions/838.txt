Synopsis: In "Jasmin: Barber, Poet, Philanthropist" by Samuel Smiles, we are introduced to the remarkable life of Jasmin, a humble barber who possesses an extraordinary talent for poetry. As he navigates the challenges of his profession and the societal constraints of his time, Jasmin's poetic gift becomes a powerful tool for social change and philanthropy. Through his verses, he captures the hearts of the people, inspiring them to question the status quo and strive for a better world. This captivating biography takes us on a journey through Jasmin's life, revealing the transformative power of art and the indomitable spirit of a man determined to make a difference.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Poetry

Themes: Artistic expression, Social change, Overcoming adversity
