Synopsis: In "The Heiress of Greenhurst: An Autobiography," Ann S. Stephens takes us on a captivating journey through her extraordinary life. From her humble beginnings to unexpected fortune, she shares the trials and triumphs that shaped her into the remarkable woman she became. With secrets, scandals, and a touch of romance, this autobiography unveils a world of privilege and privilege lost, leaving readers eager to uncover the mysteries that lie within the pages.

Genres: Autobiography, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Wealth and Privilege, Identity and Self-Discovery, Love and Betrayal
