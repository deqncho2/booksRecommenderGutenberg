Synopsis: 
In 'Short Studies in Ethics: An Elementary Text-Book for Schools', John Ormsby Miller presents a captivating exploration of ethical principles and moral dilemmas. Through a series of thought-provoking scenarios and engaging discussions, Miller challenges readers to question their own beliefs and consider the consequences of their actions. From examining the complexities of truth and honesty to delving into the nature of justice and fairness, this book offers a stimulating journey into the realm of ethics that will leave readers pondering the choices they make in their own lives.

Genres: Educational, Philosophy, Ethics

Themes: Moral dilemmas, Ethical principles, Personal responsibility
