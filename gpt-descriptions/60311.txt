Synopsis: In 'Clipped Wings' by Percy F. Westerman, a young aviator named Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him from the skies of England to the treacherous landscapes of war-torn Europe. As he navigates through dangerous missions and encounters unexpected allies and enemies, Jack's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. With each flight, he uncovers secrets that could change the course of history, leading him to question his own loyalties and the true nature of heroism.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Identity, Heroism
