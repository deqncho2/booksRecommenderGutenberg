Synopsis: In the small, isolated town of Starkfield, Massachusetts, a tragic love triangle unfolds between the brooding and mysterious Ethan Frome, his sickly wife Zeena, and their vivacious cousin Mattie Silver. As the story delves into the past, secrets are revealed, and the consequences of forbidden desires become increasingly dire. Edith Wharton's haunting tale explores the depths of human emotions and the devastating consequences of unfulfilled dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Tragedy, Romance

Themes: Forbidden love, Isolation, Sacrifice
