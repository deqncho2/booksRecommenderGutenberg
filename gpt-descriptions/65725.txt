Synopsis: In 'Centauri Vengeance' by Stephen Marlowe, a renowned intergalactic detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and treachery when he is hired to investigate a mysterious murder on the planet Centauri V. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a dark conspiracy that threatens not only his own life but the delicate balance of power in the entire galaxy. With danger lurking at every turn, our protagonist must navigate through a labyrinth of secrets and betrayal to uncover the truth and exact his own brand of justice.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Corruption, Revenge, Power Struggles
