Synopsis: In "Is the Morality of Jesus Sound?: A Lecture Delivered Before the Independent Religious Society," Mangasarian challenges conventional beliefs by examining the moral teachings of Jesus. With thought-provoking arguments and a critical analysis, he delves into the contradictions and inconsistencies found within the teachings of the Christian faith. This lecture offers a fresh perspective on the morality of Jesus, inviting readers to question long-held beliefs and explore the true nature of his teachings.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Religious Criticism

Themes: Morality, Religion, Critical Thinking
