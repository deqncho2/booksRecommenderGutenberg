Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the captivating world of prehistoric villages, castles, and towers in the enchanting landscapes of southwestern Colorado. In this enthralling work by Jesse Walter Fewkes, embark on a journey that unveils the mysteries of ancient civilizations and their remarkable architectural achievements. From the intricate designs of ancestral Puebloan dwellings to the majestic fortresses perched atop towering cliffs, Fewkes takes readers on a mesmerizing exploration of the region's rich history. Discover the secrets hidden within these ancient structures and delve into the lives of the people who once called them home. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Archaeology, History

Themes: Prehistoric civilizations, Architecture, Cultural heritage
