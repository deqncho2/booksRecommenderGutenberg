Synopsis: 
In 'The Comedies of Terence', Terence takes us on a delightful journey through the world of ancient Roman comedy. With his sharp wit and clever wordplay, Terence presents a collection of humorous and entertaining plays that explore the complexities of love, relationships, and social class. From mistaken identities to romantic entanglements, each comedy is filled with hilarious situations and memorable characters that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. 

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Roman Literature

Themes: Love and relationships, Social class, Identity
