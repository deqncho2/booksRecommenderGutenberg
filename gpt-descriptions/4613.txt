Synopsis: In 'At Large' by Arthur Christopher Benson, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges in a small English village, captivating the attention of its inhabitants. As rumors and speculations spread like wildfire, the villagers find themselves drawn into a web of intrigue and suspense. With each passing day, the tension escalates, leading to unexpected consequences that will forever change their lives. This gripping tale explores the dark undercurrents of human nature and the power of secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Intrigue, Secrets, Human Nature
