Synopsis:
'Across America; Or, The Great West and the Pacific Coast' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the vast landscapes of the United States, from the bustling cities of the East to the untamed wilderness of the West. Join James Fowler Rusling as he embarks on a remarkable adventure, encountering fascinating people, breathtaking scenery, and unexpected challenges along the way. From the majestic Rocky Mountains to the awe-inspiring beauty of the Pacific Coast, this captivating travelogue offers a vivid and immersive exploration of America's diverse and captivating landscapes.

Genres:
Travelogue, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes:
Exploration, American identity, Natural beauty
