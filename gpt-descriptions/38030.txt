Synopsis: In 'The Girl Scouts at Camp Comalong; Or, Peg of Tamarack Hills' by Lilian Garis, a group of adventurous Girl Scouts embark on a thrilling summer camp experience at Camp Comalong. Among them is Peg, a spirited and resourceful young girl who quickly becomes the center of attention. As the girls navigate the challenges of camp life, they uncover a mysterious secret that puts their friendship and bravery to the test. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
