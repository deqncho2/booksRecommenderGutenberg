Synopsis: In 'The Measure of a Man' by Randall Garrett, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Wilson finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. As he races against time to protect his invention, he must navigate a treacherous world of corporate greed, government secrets, and personal vendettas. With his life and the fate of his invention hanging in the balance, Dr. Wilson must confront the true measure of his own character and make choices that will shape the future.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Identity
