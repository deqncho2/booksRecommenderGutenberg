Synopsis: 
In 'My Tropic Isle' by Banfield, E. J., embark on a captivating journey to a remote tropical island in the Great Barrier Reef. The author, Edmund James Banfield, shares his extraordinary experiences and encounters with the unique flora, fauna, and inhabitants of this enchanting paradise. From the mesmerizing beauty of the coral reefs to the intriguing stories of the island's inhabitants, this memoir paints a vivid picture of life in a secluded tropical paradise. As Banfield immerses himself in the island's natural wonders, he also reflects on the profound impact of solitude and the delicate balance between man and nature. 

Genres: Memoir, Travelogue, Natural History

Themes: Solitude, Nature, Environmentalism
