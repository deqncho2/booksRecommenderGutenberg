Synopsis: In 'Black is White' by George Barr McCutcheon, a young man named Jack discovers a mysterious artifact that has the power to turn his world upside down. As he grapples with the consequences of this newfound ability, Jack finds himself caught in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the line between truth and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving Jack questioning his own identity and the nature of reality itself.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Perception, Deception
