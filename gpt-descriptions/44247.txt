Synopsis: In the second volume of "Anne of Geierstein; Or, The Maiden of the Mist," the captivating tale continues as Anne, the mysterious maiden with a secret, finds herself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and supernatural forces. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of medieval Europe, Anne's fate becomes intertwined with the fate of nations, and she must summon all her courage and wit to protect those she holds dear. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling conclusion will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Power, Love, Supernatural
