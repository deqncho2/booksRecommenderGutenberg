Synopsis: In Stephen H. Branch's Alligator, Vol. 1 no. 09, June 19, 1858, a mysterious event unfolds in a small town, leaving its residents bewildered and on edge. As the townspeople grapple with the unexplainable, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human behavior is revealed. With a gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Human nature and morality
