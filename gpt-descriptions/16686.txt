Synopsis: 
'Verses for Children, and Songs for Music' by Juliana Horatia Ewing is a delightful collection of poems and songs that will captivate both children and adults alike. From whimsical tales of talking animals to heartfelt verses about love and friendship, this enchanting book takes readers on a journey through the imaginative world of childhood. With its lyrical language and charming illustrations, Ewing's work is sure to spark the imagination and leave a lasting impression.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature, Music

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Love, Nature
