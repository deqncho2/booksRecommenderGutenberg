Synopsis: In 'Maida's Little Shop' by Gillmore, Inez Haynes, a young girl named Maida embarks on a captivating adventure when she opens a little shop in her own backyard. As customers flock to her shop, Maida's creativity and resourcefulness are put to the test, leading to unexpected encounters and heartwarming moments. With each new day, Maida's little shop becomes a hub of excitement and surprises, leaving readers eager to discover what awaits her next.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Imagination, Entrepreneurship, Friendship
