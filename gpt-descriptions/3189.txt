Synopsis: 
In 'Sketches New and Old', Mark Twain takes readers on a delightful journey through a collection of humorous and satirical sketches. From absurd anecdotes to witty observations, Twain's sharp wit and clever storytelling captivate readers as he explores various aspects of American society and human nature. With each sketch, Twain offers a unique perspective on everyday life, shedding light on the absurdities and contradictions that often go unnoticed. From the misadventures of a bumbling traveler to the comical mishaps of a small-town newspaper, 'Sketches New and Old' is a delightful and entertaining read that will leave readers chuckling and pondering the quirks of the world around them.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Short Stories

Themes: American society, Human nature, Absurdity
