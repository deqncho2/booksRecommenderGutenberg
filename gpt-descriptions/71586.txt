Synopsis: In 'Deadline' by Walter L. Kleine, a renowned investigative journalist, Jack Turner, finds himself entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger when he stumbles upon a mysterious document that could expose a high-level government cover-up. As he delves deeper into the truth, Jack must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, risking everything to uncover the shocking secrets hidden beneath the surface. With time running out, he must race against the clock to bring the truth to light before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Government conspiracy, Journalism ethics, Truth and deception
