Synopsis: In 'The Ralstons' by Crawford, F. Marion, a wealthy and influential family finds themselves entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and forbidden love. As the patriarch's health deteriorates, long-held grudges and hidden desires threaten to tear the family apart. With each member harboring their own dark secrets, the Ralstons must confront their past and face the consequences of their actions before it's too late. Will they be able to overcome their differences and find redemption, or will their family be forever fractured?

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Family dynamics, Secrets and lies, Redemption
