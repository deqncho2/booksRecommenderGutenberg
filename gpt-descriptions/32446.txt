Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Waldfried, a small village nestled in the German countryside, a young woman named Walpurga embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Walpurga finds herself torn between tradition and her own desires. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, this captivating novel explores the intricate web of relationships and the profound impact they have on shaping one's identity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Identity, Tradition, Love and Relationships
