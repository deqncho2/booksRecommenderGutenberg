Synopsis: In 'The Guinea Stamp: A Tale of Modern Glasgow' by Annie S. Swan, readers are transported to the bustling streets of Glasgow, where a mysterious guinea stamp sets off a chain of events that will change the lives of several individuals. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and unexpected alliances are formed. With its intriguing plot and vivid portrayal of the city, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic guinea stamp.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Social class and inequality, Redemption and forgiveness
