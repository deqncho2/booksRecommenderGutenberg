Synopsis: 
In 'The Bright Face of Danger: Being an Account of Some Adventures of Henri de Launay, Son of the Sieur de la Tournoire', Henri de Launay finds himself thrust into a world of danger and intrigue as he embarks on a quest to uncover the truth behind his father's mysterious disappearance. With his loyal companions by his side, Henri must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter cunning adversaries, and unravel a web of secrets that could change the course of his life forever. As he delves deeper into the unknown, Henri discovers that the bright face of danger may hold the key to his own destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Family, Loyalty, Identity, Coming of Age
