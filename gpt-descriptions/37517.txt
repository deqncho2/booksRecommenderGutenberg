Synopsis: In "The Notorious Impostor (1692); Diego Redivivus (1692)" by Elkanah Settle, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the life of a mysterious and enigmatic character. This captivating tale follows the exploits of an impostor who skillfully assumes various identities, leaving a trail of intrigue and deception in his wake. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of this enigmatic figure is gradually unveiled. Set against the backdrop of 17th-century England, this gripping narrative explores the depths of human identity and the power of illusion.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Power
