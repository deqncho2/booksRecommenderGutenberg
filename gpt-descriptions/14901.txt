Synopsis: 

In 'Epilepsy, Hysteria, and Neurasthenia: Their Causes, Symptoms, & Treatment', author Isaac George Briggs delves into the fascinating world of neurological disorders and mental health conditions. Through meticulous research and insightful analysis, Briggs explores the intricate web of causes, symptoms, and potential treatments for these enigmatic conditions. From the ancient beliefs surrounding epilepsy and hysteria to the modern understanding of neurasthenia, this book offers a captivating journey through the history and evolution of these disorders. With a blend of scientific knowledge and compelling anecdotes, Briggs presents a comprehensive exploration of these conditions that will leave readers both informed and intrigued.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, History

Themes: Neurological disorders, Mental health, Medical history
