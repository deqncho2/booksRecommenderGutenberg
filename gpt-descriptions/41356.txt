Synopsis: Letty and the Twins is a heartwarming tale that follows Letty, a young girl with a vivid imagination, as she embarks on an extraordinary adventure with her newfound friends. When Letty discovers a hidden door in her grandmother's attic, she is transported to a magical world where she encounters mischievous twins who are in desperate need of her help. Together, they must navigate through enchanting landscapes, face thrilling challenges, and unravel the secrets of this mysterious realm. As Letty's courage and determination are put to the test, she learns valuable lessons about friendship, self-discovery, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Self-discovery
