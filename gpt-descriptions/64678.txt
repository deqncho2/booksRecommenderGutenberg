Synopsis: In 'The First Man on the Moon' by Alfred Coppel, a brilliant astronaut named John Cargraves finds himself at the center of a thrilling race to be the first person to set foot on the moon. As he faces numerous challenges and dangers, Cargraves must navigate the treacherous politics of the space program and confront his own personal demons. With high-stakes action and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Ambition, Sacrifice
