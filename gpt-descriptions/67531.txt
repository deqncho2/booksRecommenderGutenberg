Synopsis: In 'The Amateur Inn' by Albert Payson Terhune, a young couple embarks on a journey to transform a dilapidated inn into a thriving business. As they face numerous challenges, including skeptical locals and unexpected setbacks, they must rely on their determination, resourcefulness, and the support of their newfound community. Amidst the trials and triumphs, they discover the true meaning of perseverance and the power of following one's dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Pursuit of dreams, Overcoming obstacles, Community support
