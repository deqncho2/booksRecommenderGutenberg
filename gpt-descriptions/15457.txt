Synopsis: In this captivating issue of 'The Great Round World And What Is Going On In It', readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the latest news and events happening around the world. From the mysterious disappearance of a famous explorer to the discovery of a hidden treasure, this magazine keeps its young audience on the edge of their seats. Packed with fascinating stories, intriguing mysteries, and exciting adventures, this issue is sure to captivate the minds of boys and girls alike.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical
