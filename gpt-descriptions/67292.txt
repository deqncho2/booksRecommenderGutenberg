Synopsis: In 'The Man Higher Up', a gripping political thriller by Balmer and MacHarg, a young and ambitious lawyer finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of corruption and power. When he is offered a prestigious position in a high-profile law firm, he soon discovers that the firm's success is built on a foundation of deceit and manipulation. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of the legal world, he must navigate treacherous alliances and make difficult choices that could cost him everything. With suspenseful twists and turns, this thrilling novel explores the lengths one man will go to uncover the truth and fight for justice.

Genres: Political thriller, Legal drama, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Power, Morality, Justice
