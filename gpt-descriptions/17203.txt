Synopsis: 

'Witchcraft and Devil Lore in the Channel Islands' delves into the mysterious and dark history of the Guernsey Royal Court, presenting transcripts from the official records that document cases of witchcraft and devil worship. With an English translation and a captivating historical introduction, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the beliefs, superstitions, and trials that plagued the Channel Islands during a time of fear and paranoia. From accusations of witchcraft to encounters with the devil himself, these records provide a chilling account of the supernatural forces that gripped the islanders' imaginations.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Occult, Folklore

Themes: Witchcraft, Devil worship, Superstition, Fear, Historical beliefs
