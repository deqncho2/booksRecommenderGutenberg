Synopsis: 

In "The Dyer's Guide," Thomas Packer presents a comprehensive compendium of the art of dyeing, covering a wide range of materials such as linen, cotton, silk, wool, muslin, dresses, and furniture. This guide not only provides detailed instructions on dyeing techniques but also delves into various related processes like scouring wool, bleaching cotton, ungumming silk, and whitening and sulphuring silk and wool. Packer also includes an introductory epitome of the leading facts in chemistry, highlighting their connection to the art of dyeing. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Craft, Reference

Themes: Artistry, Chemistry, Textile Techniques
