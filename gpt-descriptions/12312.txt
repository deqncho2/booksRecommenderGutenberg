Synopsis: 
In 'A King, and No King', the lives of two brothers take an unexpected turn when they find themselves in a complex love triangle. As they navigate the treacherous waters of power, desire, and loyalty, their bond is put to the ultimate test. With a mix of humor, drama, and intrigue, this play explores the boundaries of love and the consequences of forbidden desires.

Genres: 
Comedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: 
Love and desire, Loyalty and betrayal
