Synopsis: In the small town of Oakwood, Michael McGrath leads a seemingly ordinary life as the local postmaster. However, when a mysterious package arrives at the post office, Michael's world is turned upside down. As he delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the package, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each clue he uncovers, Michael's own past comes back to haunt him, forcing him to confront his own demons and make choices that will determine the fate of not only himself but also the entire town.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Redemption and self-discovery, Small-town dynamics and community bonds
