Synopsis: 
In 'By Earthlight' by Bryce Walton, a mysterious object crash-lands on Earth, setting off a chain of events that will challenge the boundaries of human understanding. As scientists and government officials race to uncover the truth behind the object's origin and purpose, they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, the characters must confront their own fears and make difficult choices that will shape the future of the planet.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the power of knowledge, the resilience of the human spirit
