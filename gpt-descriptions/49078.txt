Synopsis: In 'The Shire Horse in Peace and War' by J. Albert Frost, follow the remarkable journey of a majestic Shire horse named Thunder as he navigates through the tumultuous times of peace and war. From the idyllic countryside to the battlefields of World War I, Thunder's strength and loyalty are tested, and he becomes an unlikely hero in the face of adversity. This heartwarming tale explores the bond between humans and animals, the resilience of the human spirit, and the power of hope.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Animal Fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Resilience, Friendship, Humanity
