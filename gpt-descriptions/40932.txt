Synopsis: In "Washington's Road (Nemacolin's Path) the First Chapter of the Old French War," Archer Butler Hulbert takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of 18th-century America. This meticulously researched historical account follows the young George Washington as he embarks on a perilous expedition to secure the Ohio River Valley. As Washington navigates treacherous terrain, encounters Native American tribes, and faces the looming threat of war, readers are transported to a time of great uncertainty and adventure. Hulbert's vivid storytelling and attention to detail bring to life the challenges and triumphs of Washington's early military career, setting the stage for the epic conflict that would shape the destiny of a nation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Conflict and war, Leadership and perseverance
