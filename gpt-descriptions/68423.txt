Synopsis: In "The Story of Abraham Lincoln," Mary Agnes Hamilton takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of one of America's most iconic figures. From his humble beginnings in a log cabin to his rise as the 16th President of the United States, this biography delves into the trials, triumphs, and personal struggles that shaped Lincoln's character. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Hamilton paints a vivid portrait of a man who faced adversity with unwavering determination and ultimately left an indelible mark on history.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Leadership, Equality, Legacy
