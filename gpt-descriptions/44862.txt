Synopsis: 
In 'Linda Carlton, Air Pilot' by Edith Lavell, follow the thrilling journey of Linda Carlton, a young and determined woman who defies societal expectations to pursue her dream of becoming an air pilot. Set in the early 20th century, Linda faces numerous challenges and obstacles as she navigates a male-dominated industry, proving her worth and skill in the face of adversity. As she soars through the skies, Linda's courage and determination are put to the test, leading her on a path of self-discovery and empowerment. Will Linda Carlton conquer the skies and break through the barriers that hold her back?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Women's Fiction

Themes: Gender equality, Pursuit of dreams, Overcoming obstacles
