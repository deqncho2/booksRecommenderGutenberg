Synopsis: In 'A Hazard of New Fortunes — Volume 1', a group of diverse characters find themselves entangled in the bustling world of late 19th-century New York City. As they navigate the complexities of social class, ambition, and the pursuit of the American Dream, their lives become intertwined in unexpected ways. From the struggles of a struggling writer to the challenges faced by an immigrant family, this novel explores the clash between old traditions and the rapidly changing modern world. With its vivid portrayal of the city's vibrant atmosphere and its exploration of the human condition, 'A Hazard of New Fortunes — Volume 1' offers a captivating glimpse into the lives of those seeking their fortunes in the urban jungle.

Genres: Fiction, Realism, Social Commentary

Themes: Social class, Immigration, Ambition, American Dream, Cultural clash
