Synopsis: 
'Lessons in the Art of Illuminating' takes readers on a captivating journey through the exquisite world of illuminated manuscripts. Drawing from renowned collections, such as the British Museum and Lambeth Palace Library, this book presents a series of stunning examples that showcase the intricate beauty and skill of this ancient art form. With practical instructions and a fascinating sketch of the art's history, readers are invited to explore the techniques and secrets behind these masterpieces, unlocking a world of creativity and inspiration.

Genres: Art, History, Instructional

Themes: Illuminated manuscripts, Artistic techniques, Historical significance
