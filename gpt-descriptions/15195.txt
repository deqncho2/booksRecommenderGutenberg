Synopsis: In the small town of Old Harpeth, a young woman named Rose finds herself caught in a web of secrets, forbidden love, and societal expectations. As she navigates the complexities of her relationships and the challenges of her time, Rose must make difficult choices that will shape her future and the lives of those around her. Set against the backdrop of the post-Civil War era, 'Rose of Old Harpeth' is a captivating tale of love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Expectations, Personal Freedom
