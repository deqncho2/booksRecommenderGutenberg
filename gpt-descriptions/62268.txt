Synopsis: In "Money-making men; or, how to grow rich," James Ewing Ritchie takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of wealth accumulation. Through a series of intriguing anecdotes and practical advice, the author explores the lives of individuals who have mastered the art of making money. From cunning entrepreneurs to shrewd investors, this book delves into the secrets and strategies employed by these money-making men. With a blend of wit, wisdom, and a touch of humor, Ritchie offers valuable insights into the mindset and actions required to achieve financial success. Genres: Self-help, Business, Non-fiction. Themes: Wealth accumulation, Entrepreneurship, Financial strategies.
