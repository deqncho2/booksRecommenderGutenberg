Synopsis: 
'Broad Grins: Comprising, With New Additional Tales in Verse, Those; Formerly Publish'd Under the Title "My Night-Gown and; Slippers."' is a collection of humorous and witty tales in verse written by George Colman. This delightful compilation includes a variety of amusing anecdotes and comical situations that will keep readers entertained from start to finish. From mischievous pranks to clever wordplay, Colman's tales are sure to bring a smile to your face and leave you wanting more.

Genres: 
Humor, Poetry

Themes: 
Comedy, Satire
