Synopsis: 
In 'Memoirs of the Duchesse de Dino (Afterwards Duchesse de Talleyrand et de Sagan), 1831-1835', Dorothée, the Duchess of Dino, takes us on a captivating journey through the glamorous and tumultuous world of 19th-century European aristocracy. From her privileged upbringing to her encounters with influential figures such as Napoleon Bonaparte and Prince Metternich, the Duchess shares her intimate experiences and provides a unique perspective on the political and social landscape of the time. With her sharp wit and keen observations, she unveils the secrets, scandals, and intrigues that lie behind the elegant façade of high society.

Genres: Memoir, Historical, Biography

Themes: Aristocracy, Politics, Social Etiquette, Intrigue
