Synopsis: In 'Cartouche' by Frances Mary Peard, a young woman named Hester finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she inherits a mysterious artifact from her late uncle. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the artifact, Hester becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where her life and the lives of those she cares about hang in the balance. With twists and turns at every corner, 'Cartouche' takes readers on a thrilling journey through hidden treasures, ancient curses, and unexpected alliances.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the pursuit of knowledge and truth, the power of friendship and loyalty
