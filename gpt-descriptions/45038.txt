Synopsis:
In the picturesque village of Perlycross, nestled among the enchanting Western Hills, a young woman named Ruth Josselin finds herself caught in a web of secrets and mysteries. As she delves deeper into the history of her family and the village, she uncovers a hidden world of forbidden love, betrayal, and tragedy. With each revelation, Ruth's own life becomes entangled in the past, and she must navigate the treacherous path of love and loyalty to uncover the truth that has been buried for generations.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes:
Family secrets, Forbidden love, Betrayal, Tragedy
