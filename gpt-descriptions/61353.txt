Synopsis: In 'Saline Solution' by Keith Laumer, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Jameson makes a groundbreaking discovery that could revolutionize the medical field. However, his invention attracts the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess it. As Dr. Jameson finds himself caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, he must navigate a treacherous world of corporate espionage and betrayal to protect his creation and his own life.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Scientific advancement, Corporate greed, Betrayal
