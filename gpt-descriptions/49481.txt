Synopsis: 
In 'Twice Killed: A Farce in One Act', the audience is taken on a hilarious journey filled with mistaken identities, absurd situations, and a touch of the supernatural. As the plot unfolds, the characters find themselves caught in a web of confusion, leading to a series of comical misunderstandings and unexpected twists. With each passing moment, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the audience guessing until the very end.

Genres: Farce, Comedy, Supernatural

Themes: Mistaken identity, Illusion vs. reality
