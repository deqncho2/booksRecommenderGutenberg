Synopsis: 
In 'Double Identity' by Farrell, Henry, thirteen-year-old Bethany is sent to live with her aunt and uncle in a small town after her parents mysteriously disappear. As she settles into her new life, Bethany discovers a hidden room in her aunt and uncle's house that holds clues to her true identity. With the help of a new friend, she embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth about her past, leading her to a shocking revelation that will change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Family, Secrets
