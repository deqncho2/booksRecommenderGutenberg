Synopsis: 

In 'Westward with the Prince of Wales', author W. Douglas Newton takes readers on an exhilarating journey across the Atlantic, as he accompanies the Prince of Wales on his historic visit to North America. From the bustling streets of New York City to the untamed wilderness of Canada, Newton provides a captivating firsthand account of the prince's adventures, encounters with influential figures, and the cultural exchange that unfolds during this remarkable expedition. As the entourage navigates through various landscapes and social settings, unexpected challenges and thrilling escapades await, making this a truly unforgettable tale of exploration and diplomacy.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Biography

Themes: Royalty and diplomacy, Cultural exchange, Adventure and exploration
