Synopsis: In 'The Visionary: Pictures From Nordland' by Jonas Lie, a young artist named Johan Borg embarks on a journey to the remote and mysterious region of Nordland in Norway. Drawn by the allure of its breathtaking landscapes and the stories of its enigmatic inhabitants, Johan becomes captivated by the beauty and darkness that coexist in this isolated land. As he delves deeper into Nordland's secrets, Johan's perception of reality begins to blur, leading him on a transformative and haunting exploration of art, love, and the human psyche.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Mystery, Nordic literature

Themes: Art and creativity, Nature and its influence on the human spirit, Identity and self-discovery
