Synopsis: In 'The Land That Time Forgot' by Edgar Rice Burroughs, a group of survivors from a torpedoed ship find themselves stranded on a mysterious and dangerous island. As they struggle to survive, they soon discover that the island is home to prehistoric creatures and a primitive human civilization. With danger lurking at every turn, the survivors must navigate treacherous landscapes and face unimaginable challenges in their quest for escape. 

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Evolution
