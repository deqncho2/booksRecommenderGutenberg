Synopsis: In 'Blackfeet Tales of Glacier National Park', James Willard Schultz takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and legends of the Blackfeet Native American tribe. Through vivid storytelling, Schultz brings to life the enchanting tales of the Blackfeet people, weaving together elements of nature, spirituality, and the supernatural. From mythical creatures lurking in the mountains to the profound connection between humans and the natural world, this collection of stories offers a glimpse into the cultural heritage and deep wisdom of the Blackfeet tribe.

Genres: Folklore, Native American literature, Historical fiction

Themes: Nature, Spirituality, Cultural heritage
