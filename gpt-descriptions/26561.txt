Synopsis: In "The Story of a Common Soldier of Army Life in the Civil War, 1861-1865," Leander Stillwell takes us on a gripping journey through the American Civil War, sharing his personal experiences as a soldier on the front lines. From the excitement and camaraderie of enlistment to the horrors of battle and the heart-wrenching moments of loss, Stillwell's vivid and honest account immerses readers in the harsh realities of war. Through his eyes, we witness the resilience and courage of ordinary men thrust into extraordinary circumstances, as they navigate the complexities of loyalty, sacrifice, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War Literature
Themes: War, Patriotism, Brotherhood, Loss, Survival
