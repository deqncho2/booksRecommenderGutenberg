Synopsis: In 'The Great Inquiry' by Hilaire Belloc, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case that challenges his intellect and tests his moral compass. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each clue leading him closer to the truth, the detective must navigate a treacherous path filled with danger and intrigue. Will he be able to solve the mystery before it consumes him?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Truth and deception, Morality and ethics
