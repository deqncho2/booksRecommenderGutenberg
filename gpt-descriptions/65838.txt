Synopsis: In 'Final Examination' by Robert Sheckley, a group of students find themselves trapped in a mysterious and deadly game where their intelligence and survival skills are put to the ultimate test. As they navigate through a series of mind-bending challenges, they must unravel the secrets behind their predicament and discover the true purpose of their final examination. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, forcing them to confront their fears and make life-or-death decisions. Will they be able to outsmart their captors and escape this deadly game?

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Intelligence, Identity, Morality
