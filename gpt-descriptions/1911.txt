Synopsis: In 'Concerning Christian Liberty; with Letter of Martin Luther to Pope Leo X.', Martin Luther challenges the traditional beliefs and practices of the Catholic Church, sparking a revolution that would forever change the course of Christianity. Through a powerful letter addressed to Pope Leo X, Luther delves into the essence of true Christian freedom and the role of faith in salvation. This thought-provoking work explores the tension between religious authority and individual conscience, igniting a theological debate that would shape the Reformation movement.

Genres: Religious literature, Historical non-fiction

Themes: Religious authority, Individual conscience, Faith and salvation, Reformation movement
