Synopsis: In 'The Berserker' by Charles V. De Vet, a group of scientists on a distant planet must confront a deadly and relentless enemy known as the Berserker. As they struggle to survive, they uncover shocking secrets about the origins and purpose of these destructive machines. With time running out, they must find a way to outsmart the Berserker and save themselves from annihilation.

Genres: Science Fiction, Action, Thriller

Themes: Artificial Intelligence, Survival, Morality
