Synopsis: In 'Monte-Cristo's Daughter' by Edmund Flagg, a young woman named Zuleika finds herself caught in a web of mystery and revenge as she uncovers the secrets of her father's past. With her father's infamous reputation as the Count of Monte-Cristo, Zuleika embarks on a thrilling journey filled with danger, romance, and unexpected twists. As she delves deeper into her family's dark history, Zuleika must confront her own identity and make choices that will shape her future. Will she be able to break free from the shadows of her father's legacy, or will she become a pawn in a game of vengeance?

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Revenge, Identity, Family Secrets
