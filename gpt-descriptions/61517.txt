Synopsis: In 'Bread and Circuses' by Helen Parry Eden, a mysterious invitation leads a young woman on a thrilling journey into the heart of a secret society. As she delves deeper into this hidden world, she uncovers dark secrets and dangerous alliances that threaten to unravel everything she thought she knew. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Power
