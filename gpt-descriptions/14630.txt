Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a young and adventurous girl, finds herself on Cliff Island, a place filled with mystery and hidden treasures. As she explores the island, she stumbles upon an old hunter's treasure box, setting off a thrilling journey of discovery and danger. With her determination and resourcefulness, Ruth must unravel the secrets of the treasure box and navigate the challenges that come her way. Will she uncover the truth behind the old hunter's treasure and what it means for her future?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Courage, Self-discovery
