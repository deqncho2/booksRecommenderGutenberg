Synopsis: In 'Kodaks and Kodak Supplies, 1914' by Canadian Kodak Company, embark on a captivating journey through the world of photography as it was in the early 20th century. This beautifully illustrated book takes readers on a visual exploration of the innovative Kodak cameras and the wide range of supplies available at the time. Discover the fascinating history behind the development of photography and witness the evolution of Kodak's groundbreaking technology. From the simplicity of point-and-shoot cameras to the intricacies of darkroom equipment, this book offers a glimpse into the art and science of capturing moments in time.

Genres: Historical, Art, Technology

Themes: Innovation, Photography, Progress
