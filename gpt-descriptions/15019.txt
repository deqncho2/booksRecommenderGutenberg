Synopsis: 

In 'A Queens Delight: The Art of Preserving, Conserving and Candying. As also, A right; Knowledge of making Perfumes, and Distilling the most Excellent; Waters.', the anonymous author takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of preserving, conserving, and candying. This delightful guide not only reveals the secrets of creating mouthwatering treats but also provides invaluable knowledge on crafting exquisite perfumes and distilling the most exceptional waters. With its detailed instructions and fascinating anecdotes, this book offers a glimpse into the artistry and science behind these ancient crafts, leaving readers eager to explore the wonders of these forgotten arts.

Genres: Non-fiction, Cookbook, History

Themes: Culinary arts, Craftsmanship, Historical preservation
