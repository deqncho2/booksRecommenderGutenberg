Synopsis: In the vibrant streets of 1920s Shanghai, a young woman named Iris embarks on a journey of self-discovery and forbidden love. Caught between tradition and modernity, Iris finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and desires, as she navigates the complexities of a society on the brink of change. As her world unravels, Iris must confront the consequences of her choices and the power of societal expectations. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Love and Desire, Societal Expectations
