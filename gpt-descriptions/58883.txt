Synopsis: In the small town of Boru, a group of men find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected alliances. As their lives intertwine, they must navigate through the complexities of friendship, love, and betrayal, all while facing the consequences of their past actions. With each passing day, the men of Boru discover that their choices have far-reaching consequences, and that the bonds they thought were unbreakable may be more fragile than they ever imagined.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Betrayal, Redemption, Consequences
