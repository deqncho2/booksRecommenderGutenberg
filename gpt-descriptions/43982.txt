Synopsis: In 'Stories of the Old World' by Alfred John Church, embark on a captivating journey through ancient civilizations and mythical realms. From the epic tales of Greek gods and heroes to the enchanting legends of Arthurian Britain, this collection of stories will transport you to a time of adventure, magic, and wonder. Discover the triumphs and tragedies of legendary figures such as Hercules, Perseus, King Arthur, and many more, as they navigate through love, war, and destiny. With vivid storytelling and rich historical detail, Church brings these timeless tales to life, captivating readers of all ages.

Genres: Mythology, Historical Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Love and Sacrifice
