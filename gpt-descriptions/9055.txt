Synopsis: In 'Bad Medicine' by Robert Sheckley, a desperate man seeks a cure for his mysterious illness, leading him on a bizarre and perilous journey through a twisted world of medical malpractice. As he encounters eccentric doctors, questionable treatments, and a series of strange and hilarious mishaps, he must confront the true nature of his affliction and the sinister forces at play. With a blend of dark humor and suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Comedy, Mystery

Themes: Medical ethics, Identity, Absurdity
