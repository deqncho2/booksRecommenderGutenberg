Synopsis: In "The Hoe Cake of Appomattox" by Spear, Ellis, a mysterious artifact from the Civil War era is discovered, leading a young historian on a thrilling journey to uncover its secrets. As she delves deeper into the past, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected romance. With each clue she uncovers, the stakes rise higher, and she must race against time to solve the puzzle before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: History and its impact on the present, the power of artifacts, love and sacrifice
