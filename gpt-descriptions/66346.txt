Synopsis: In 'The Lighter Classics in Music', David Ewen takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of musical masterworks, exploring the lighter side of classical compositions. With a comprehensive guide featuring works by 187 composers, Ewen delves into the stories behind these enchanting pieces, revealing the hidden humor, whimsy, and playfulness within. From lively symphonies to charming ballets, this book uncovers the delightful nuances and lighter tones that often go unnoticed in classical music, offering a fresh perspective on these timeless works.

Genres: Music, Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Light-heartedness, Humor, Whimsy, Playfulness
