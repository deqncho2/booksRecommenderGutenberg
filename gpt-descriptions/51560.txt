Synopsis: In 'The Mentor: American Novelists, Vol. 1, No. 25', Mabie, Hamilton Wright takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and works of some of America's most celebrated novelists. From the mysterious tales of Edgar Allan Poe to the heartwarming stories of Louisa May Alcott, this volume delves into the fascinating minds behind these literary masterpieces. Through insightful analysis and engaging anecdotes, Mabie offers a unique glimpse into the creative process and the personal struggles that shaped these iconic authors. Discover the secrets and inspirations that led to the creation of some of America's most beloved novels in this captivating exploration of the lives of American novelists.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Biography

Themes: Creativity, Personal Struggles, Literary Influence
