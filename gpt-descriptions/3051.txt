Synopsis: In the charming town of Saratoga, a group of friends find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and hidden agendas. As they navigate through the social scene of the summer resort, they uncover a conspiracy that threatens to shatter their idyllic world. With their eyes wide open, they must decide whether to expose the truth or protect their own interests. 

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Satire

Themes: Deception, Social class, Morality
