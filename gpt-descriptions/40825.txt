Synopsis: 

In 'Fictitious & Symbolic Creatures in Art: With Special Reference to Their Use in British Heraldry', John Vinycomb takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of mythical creatures and their significance in British heraldry. From majestic dragons and fierce griffins to enchanting unicorns and mythical sea creatures, Vinycomb explores the rich history and symbolism behind these fantastical beings. Through detailed descriptions and stunning illustrations, he unveils the hidden meanings and cultural contexts that have shaped the use of these creatures in art and heraldry. This book is a treasure trove for art enthusiasts, history buffs, and anyone intrigued by the allure of mythical creatures.

Genres: Art History, Heraldry, Mythology

Themes: Symbolism, Cultural Significance, Artistic Representation
