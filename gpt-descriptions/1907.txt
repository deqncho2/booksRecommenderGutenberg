Synopsis: In 'Rowdy of the Cross L', a young cowboy named Rowdy Vaughan finds himself caught in a web of danger and deceit when he stumbles upon a mysterious secret hidden deep within the rugged landscape of the Cross L Ranch. As Rowdy embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth, he must confront his own demons and navigate the treacherous world of outlaws and lawmen. With his courage and determination tested at every turn, Rowdy must decide where his loyalties lie and what he is willing to sacrifice to protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Redemption
