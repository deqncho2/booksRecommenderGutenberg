Synopsis: In the distant future, space pilot John Greylorn finds himself caught in a deadly game of power and survival. Tasked with a dangerous mission to rescue a kidnapped scientist, Greylorn must navigate treacherous alliances, interstellar politics, and a mysterious alien artifact that holds the key to humanity's future. As he battles against formidable enemies and uncovers shocking secrets, Greylorn's courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he succeed in his mission and save not only the scientist but also the fate of the entire galaxy?

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Space Opera

Themes: Exploration, Loyalty, Survival, Political Intrigue
