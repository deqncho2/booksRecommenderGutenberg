Synopsis: 
In 'Rhymes of a Red Cross Man', Robert W. Service takes readers on a poignant journey through the experiences of a Red Cross worker during World War I. Through a collection of powerful and emotive poems, Service captures the raw emotions, sacrifices, and resilience of those involved in the war effort. From the trenches to the hospitals, from the camaraderie to the heart-wrenching losses, these verses paint a vivid picture of the human spirit amidst the chaos and devastation of war.

Genres: Poetry, War Literature

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Resilience, Human Spirit
