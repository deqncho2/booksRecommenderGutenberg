Synopsis: In 'The Ultimate Salient' by Bond, Nelson S., a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger, forcing him to confront his own moral compass and make impossible choices. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of power-hungry individuals and hidden agendas, all while trying to protect his groundbreaking invention from falling into the wrong hands. This gripping and thought-provoking thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Power, Conspiracy, Ethics
