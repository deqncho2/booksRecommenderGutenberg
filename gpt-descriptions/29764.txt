Synopsis:
In the gritty world of 1920s Chicago, a young and street-smart newsboy named Kid Scanlan finds himself caught up in a dangerous game of power and corruption. When he witnesses a high-profile murder, Kid becomes a target for both the mob and the police. With his quick wit and resourcefulness, he must navigate the treacherous streets and outsmart his pursuers to uncover the truth and protect himself. As the stakes rise and the danger intensifies, Kid Scanlan's journey becomes a thrilling and suspenseful tale of survival in a city ruled by crime.

Genres:
Crime fiction, Historical fiction, Thriller

Themes:
Power and corruption, Survival, Loyalty, Justice
