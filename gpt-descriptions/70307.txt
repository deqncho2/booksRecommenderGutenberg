Synopsis: In 'Torwood's Trust', the final installment of the captivating trilogy by Evelyn Everett-Green, secrets and mysteries unravel as the lives of the Torwood family take unexpected turns. As the family estate faces financial ruin, a long-lost relative emerges, bringing with them a glimmer of hope. However, their arrival also stirs up a whirlwind of suspicion and intrigue, leaving the Torwoods questioning who they can truly trust. With the fate of their beloved home hanging in the balance, the family must navigate treacherous alliances and confront their own past mistakes. Will they be able to restore their fortune and find solace in the truth, or will their trust be shattered forever?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Family Drama

Themes: Trust, Family, Secrets, Redemption
