Synopsis: In 'Victor's Triumph: Sequel to A Beautiful Fiend', Emma Dorothy Eliza Nevitte Southworth continues the thrilling story of Victor, a complex and enigmatic character who finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and revenge. As Victor navigates the treacherous waters of society, he must confront his past and make difficult choices that will determine his future. With unexpected twists and turns, this sequel delves deeper into Victor's psyche, unraveling the mysteries that surround him and leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and obsession, Identity and self-discovery, Revenge and redemption
