Synopsis: In 'Dealings with the Dead, Volume 1 (of 2)', Lucius M. Sargent takes readers on a chilling journey through a collection of eerie and supernatural tales. From encounters with vengeful spirits to mysterious apparitions, this volume delves into the realm of the paranormal, leaving readers on the edge of their seats. As the stories unfold, the line between the living and the dead becomes blurred, and the characters must confront their deepest fears and darkest secrets. Prepare to be captivated by these haunting tales that will keep you guessing until the very end.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: Ghosts, Death, Fear, Secrets
