Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Thirty-first, or, the Huntingdonshire Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of the regiment from 1702 to 1850. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, this historical record unveils the untold stories of bravery, sacrifice, and resilience that shaped the regiment's legacy. Cannon's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life the battles, campaigns, and pivotal moments that defined the regiment's place in history. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Resilience, Military strategy, Historical legacy
