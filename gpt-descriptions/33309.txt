Synopsis: 
In 'Through the Postern Gate: A Romance in Seven Days' by Florence L. Barclay, a mysterious letter sets off a chain of events that will test the strength of love and loyalty. As the story unfolds over the course of seven days, secrets are revealed, hearts are broken, and unexpected alliances are formed. With its captivating characters and a plot filled with twists and turns, this romance novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love and betrayal, Secrets and deception, Redemption and forgiveness
