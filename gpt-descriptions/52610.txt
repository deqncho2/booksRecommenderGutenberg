Synopsis:
In 'Ward Hill, the Senior' by Tomlinson, Everett T., readers are introduced to the enigmatic protagonist, Ward Hill, a man with a mysterious past and a reputation that precedes him. As the story unfolds, Ward Hill finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning the true nature of Ward Hill and the motives of those around him. Set against the backdrop of a small, close-knit community, this gripping tale explores the depths of human nature and the lengths one will go to protect their own interests.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes:
Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
