Synopsis: 
'Salt-Water Ballads' by John Masefield is a collection of captivating poems that transport readers to the vast and unpredictable world of the sea. Through vivid imagery and lyrical storytelling, Masefield explores the lives of sailors, the beauty and danger of the ocean, and the timeless themes of love, loss, and the human spirit. From thrilling tales of adventure to poignant reflections on the power of nature, this collection is sure to leave readers spellbound.

Genres:
Poetry, Adventure, Maritime

Themes:
The sea, Sailor's life, Love, Loss, Nature, Human spirit
