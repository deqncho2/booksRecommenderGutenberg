Synopsis: In 'The Secret Way' by Gale, Zona, a young woman named Emily embarks on a mysterious journey that takes her through hidden realms and ancient secrets. As she unravels the enigmatic clues left behind by her late grandmother, Emily discovers a hidden world filled with magic, danger, and unexpected allies. With each step she takes, Emily becomes more entangled in a web of intrigue and must confront her own inner demons to unlock the truth. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within 'The Secret Way'.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Family legacy, Self-discovery, Magic and mysticism
