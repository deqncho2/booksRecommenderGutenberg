Synopsis: In 'Lost in the Wilds: A Canadian Story' by Eleanor Stredder, a group of adventurous teenagers embarks on a thrilling journey through the untamed Canadian wilderness. When their expedition takes an unexpected turn, they find themselves facing dangerous challenges and uncovering hidden secrets that test their courage and resilience. As they navigate through the rugged landscapes, the group must rely on their wits and teamwork to survive and find their way back home. This gripping tale of survival and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Exploration
