Synopsis: In 'French and English: A Story of the Struggle in America' by Evelyn Everett-Green, two families from different backgrounds find themselves caught in the midst of a fierce conflict in colonial America. As tensions rise between the French and English settlers, secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances emerge, testing the characters' loyalties and challenging their beliefs. Amidst the backdrop of war and political turmoil, the lives of these individuals intertwine, leading to a gripping tale of love, sacrifice, and the struggle for survival.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Conflict and Struggle, Identity and Belonging, Loyalty and Betrayal
