Synopsis: 

"The History of Battery H First Regiment Rhode Island Light Artillery in the War to Preserve the Union 1861-1865" by Fenner, Earl is a captivating historical account that delves into the experiences of Battery H, a regiment of Rhode Island Light Artillery, during the American Civil War. Through meticulous research and firsthand accounts, the author paints a vivid picture of the regiment's journey, from their initial enlistment to their involvement in major battles and their ultimate contribution to preserving the Union. This gripping narrative offers a unique perspective on the war, highlighting the bravery, sacrifices, and camaraderie of the soldiers who fought to uphold their beliefs.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Historical significance
