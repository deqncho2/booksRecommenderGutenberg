Synopsis: In this retelling of Lewis Carroll's classic tale, "Alice in Wonderland," the story unfolds using only words of one syllable. Follow Alice as she falls down a rabbit hole and enters a whimsical world filled with peculiar characters and nonsensical adventures. From the Mad Hatter's tea party to the Queen of Hearts' croquet game, Alice must navigate through this strange land, encountering both enchanting and perplexing situations. With its simplified language, this adaptation offers a fresh perspective on a beloved story, making it accessible to readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Curiosity, Absurdity, Identity
