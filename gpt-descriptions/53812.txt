Synopsis: In 'The Book of Clever Beasts: Studies in Unnatural History' by Reed, Myrtle, embark on a captivating journey through a world where animals possess extraordinary abilities and exhibit peculiar behaviors. From the cunning foxes who outsmart their human counterparts to the enigmatic creatures lurking in the depths of the ocean, this collection of stories unveils the hidden wonders of the animal kingdom. Prepare to be enthralled by tales of adventure, mystery, and the unexpected as you delve into the pages of this mesmerizing book.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Animal intelligence, Human-animal relationships, Exploration of the unknown
