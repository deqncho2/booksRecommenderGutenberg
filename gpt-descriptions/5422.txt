Synopsis: 
In 'The Masquerader' by Katherine Cecil Thurston, a young man named John Chilcote finds himself caught in a web of deception and intrigue. When circumstances force him to assume the identity of another man, he must navigate the treacherous waters of high society while concealing his true self. As he delves deeper into this masquerade, secrets unravel, and the consequences of his actions become increasingly dire. With his true identity hanging in the balance, John must confront the choices he has made and the price he is willing to pay to maintain his charade.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Identity, Deception, Consequences
