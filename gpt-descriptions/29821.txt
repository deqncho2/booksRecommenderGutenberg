Synopsis: 
'Shakespeare Jest-Books: Reprints of the Early and Very Rare Jest-Books Supposed to Have Been Used by Shakespeare' takes readers on a delightful journey through the humorous and witty world of jest-books that were believed to have influenced Shakespeare's plays. This collection of reprints showcases the clever jests, comical anecdotes, and playful stories that would have entertained audiences during Shakespeare's time. From mischievous pranks to clever wordplay, these jest-books offer a glimpse into the comedic influences that shaped Shakespeare's iconic works.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Literary Criticism

Themes: Humor, Wit, Influence of Literature
