Synopsis: 
'Alone in London' by Hesba Stretton is a gripping tale that follows the life of a young girl named Nellie, who finds herself alone and destitute on the streets of Victorian London. With no family or friends to turn to, Nellie must navigate the harsh realities of poverty, exploitation, and survival in a city that can be both cruel and kind. As she encounters various characters, some benevolent and others sinister, Nellie's resilience and determination are put to the test. Will she find a way to rise above her circumstances and forge a better future for herself?

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Social Realism

Themes: 
Poverty, Resilience, Survival, Social Injustice
