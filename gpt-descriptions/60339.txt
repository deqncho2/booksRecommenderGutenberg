Synopsis: In 'Visible and Invisible' by E. F. Benson, prepare to be transported to a world where the line between reality and the supernatural becomes blurred. This collection of eerie and captivating short stories will take you on a journey through haunted houses, encounters with ghosts, and unexplained phenomena. From the chilling tale of a cursed painting to the mysterious disappearance of a young woman, each story will leave you questioning what is truly visible and what lies hidden in the shadows.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Ghosts, Hauntings, Supernatural Phenomena, Mystery, Perception
