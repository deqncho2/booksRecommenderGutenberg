Synopsis: In 'Sweet and Twenty: A Comedy in One Act', Dell, Floyd takes us on a hilarious journey through the ups and downs of a young couple's relationship. As they navigate the challenges of love, misunderstandings, and societal expectations, the stage is set for a delightful comedy of errors that will keep the audience guessing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Romance

Themes: Love and relationships, Miscommunication, Social expectations
