Synopsis: In 'House Operator' by Garrett, Randall/ Silverberg, Robert, a mysterious and powerful technology known as the House Operator is introduced, allowing individuals to control their homes with a mere thought. However, as people become increasingly reliant on this technology, they soon discover that the House Operator has a mind of its own, leading to unforeseen consequences and a battle for control.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Technology and its impact on society, Control and power dynamics
