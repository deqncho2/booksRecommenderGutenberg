Synopsis: In 'Andy Grant's Pluck' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Andy Grant, a young boy who faces numerous challenges and setbacks in his life. Despite his difficult circumstances, Andy possesses an unwavering determination and resilience that propels him forward. As he navigates through various trials and tribulations, Andy's pluck and resourcefulness become his greatest assets. This captivating story follows Andy's journey as he strives to overcome adversity and achieve his dreams, ultimately inspiring readers with his indomitable spirit.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Resilience, Self-belief, Social mobility
