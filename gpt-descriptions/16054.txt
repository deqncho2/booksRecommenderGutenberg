Synopsis: In the small town of Dorsetville, secrets lurk behind the closed doors of the mysterious Palace of Darkened Windows. When a young woman named Judith arrives, she becomes entangled in a web of deception, forbidden love, and long-buried family secrets. As she delves deeper into the dark history of the palace, Judith must confront her own past and make choices that will forever change her life.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Forbidden love, Family legacy, Personal transformation
