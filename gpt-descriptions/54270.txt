Synopsis: In 'The Human Boy' by Eden Phillpotts, a mysterious and enchanting tale unfolds as a young boy named David embarks on a remarkable journey. Drawn into a world of magic and wonder, David encounters extraordinary creatures and faces unexpected challenges that test his courage and resilience. As he navigates through this captivating realm, David's innocence and curiosity become his greatest strengths, leading him to uncover secrets that will forever change his life. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to a realm where imagination knows no bounds and where the line between reality and fantasy becomes beautifully blurred.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Imagination, Courage, Discovery
