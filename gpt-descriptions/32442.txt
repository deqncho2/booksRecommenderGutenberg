Synopsis: In 'Gertrude's Marriage' by Heimburg, W., a young woman named Gertrude finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires when she is forced into an arranged marriage. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, Gertrude must confront the consequences of her choices and discover the true meaning of love and happiness.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and duty, societal expectations, personal freedom
