Synopsis: 
'Half-hours with the Telescope: Being a Popular Guide to the Use of the Telescope as a Means of Amusement and Instruction' takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders of the universe. Written by Richard A. Proctor, this book serves as a comprehensive guide to using a telescope for both entertainment and education. From exploring the moon's craters to observing distant galaxies, Proctor provides fascinating insights and practical advice for amateur astronomers. With vivid descriptions and helpful illustrations, this book is sure to ignite the reader's curiosity and inspire them to embark on their own celestial adventures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Astronomy, Science

Themes: Astronomy, Education, Exploration
