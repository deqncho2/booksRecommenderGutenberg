Synopsis: In 'The Labor Divide' by Vaknin, Samuel, a seemingly ordinary office worker stumbles upon a hidden world beneath the surface of his workplace. As he delves deeper into this mysterious realm, he uncovers a shocking truth that challenges everything he thought he knew about his job and the people around him. With each revelation, the protagonist finds himself torn between loyalty and self-preservation, leading to a thrilling and thought-provoking exploration of power, deception, and the blurred lines between reality and illusion.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Science fiction

Themes: Corporate manipulation, Identity, Perception vs. reality
