Synopsis: In 'The Peddler Spy; or, Dutchmen and Yankees. A Tale of the Capture of Good Hope', the reader is transported to the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. Amidst the chaos, a young peddler finds himself caught between two worlds, as he becomes entangled in a web of espionage and intrigue. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of war, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of those around him. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this historical novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets hidden within.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Spy Thriller

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity, Courage
