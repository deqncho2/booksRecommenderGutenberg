Synopsis: 
In 'Fur Farming: A book of Information about Fur Bearing Animals, Enclosures, Habits, Care, etc.', author A. R. Harding delves into the fascinating world of fur-bearing animals, providing a comprehensive guide on their enclosures, habits, and care. From the elusive mink to the cunning fox, Harding explores the intricacies of these creatures, offering valuable insights into their behavior and needs. With practical advice and detailed information, this book is a must-read for anyone interested in fur farming or simply curious about these captivating animals.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Care, Reference

Themes: Animal husbandry, Wildlife conservation, Ethical considerations
