Synopsis: In 'A Manual of Toy Dogs: How to breed, rear, and feed them', Mrs. Leslie Williams takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of toy dogs. With her wealth of knowledge and expertise, she provides invaluable insights into the art of breeding, rearing, and feeding these delightful companions. From selecting the perfect breed to understanding their unique needs, this manual offers practical advice and tips for toy dog enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned breeder or a first-time owner, this book is a must-read for anyone looking to enhance their understanding and care for these beloved pets.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Care, Reference

Themes: Dog breeding, Pet care, Animal welfare
