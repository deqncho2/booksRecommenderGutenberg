Synopsis: 
In 'Milk Run' by Robert Donald Locke, a young and ambitious pilot named Jack finds himself caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse when he agrees to transport a mysterious cargo across treacherous skies. As he navigates through unforeseen challenges and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Jack's skills and courage are put to the ultimate test. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Action, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Risk-taking, Survival, Loyalty
