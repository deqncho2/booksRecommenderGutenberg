Synopsis: In "Rasputin the Rascal Monk: Disclosing the Secret Scandal of the Betrayal of Russia by the Mock-Monk Grichka and the Consequent Ruin of the Romanoffs. With official documents revealed and recorded for the first time," Le Queux delves into the captivating and scandalous story of Grichka Rasputin, the enigmatic monk who wielded immense influence over the Russian royal family. Through a meticulous examination of official documents, Le Queux uncovers the shocking betrayal that led to the downfall of the Romanoff dynasty. This gripping account unveils the hidden secrets and manipulations that took place behind the scenes, ultimately leading to the tragic fate of the Russian monarchy.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political intrigue

Themes: Betrayal, Power and influence, Royal scandals, Historical events
