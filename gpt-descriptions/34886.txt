Synopsis: In "St. George for England: A Tale of Cressy and Poitiers," G. A. Henty takes us on an exhilarating journey through the Hundred Years' War, following the adventures of a young English archer named Ned. As Ned rises through the ranks of the English army, he finds himself at the heart of two pivotal battles: the Battle of Cressy and the Battle of Poitiers. Along the way, he encounters love, loyalty, and the harsh realities of war, all while striving to uphold the honor of his country and the chivalric ideals of St. George.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Patriotism, Chivalry, Loyalty, Love, Honor
