Synopsis: In 'The Trysting Place: A Farce in One Act' by Tarkington, Booth, a case of mistaken identity leads to a hilarious series of events when two couples find themselves in the wrong place at the wrong time. As secrets are revealed and chaos ensues, the characters must navigate their way through a web of misunderstandings and comedic situations, all while trying to maintain their dignity. Will they be able to untangle the mess before it's too late?

Genres: Comedy, Farce

Themes: Mistaken identity, Deception, Love and relationships
