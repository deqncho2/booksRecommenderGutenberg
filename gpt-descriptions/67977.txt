Synopsis: In 'Not Under the Law' by Grace Livingston Hill, a young woman named Marcia finds herself caught between duty and desire when she is unexpectedly thrust into a world of mystery and danger. As she navigates through a web of secrets and deceit, Marcia must confront her own fears and make choices that will determine her future. Will she find the strength to follow her heart, or will she succumb to the pressures of society?

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Love and sacrifice, societal expectations, personal growth
