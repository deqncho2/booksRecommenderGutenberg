Synopsis: In 'Daughters of Belgravia; vol. 1 of 3' by Fraser, Alexander, Mrs., the lives of two young women from different social classes intertwine in the glamorous world of 19th century London. As secrets, scandals, and unexpected connections come to light, the protagonists must navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this novel takes readers on a captivating journey through the opulent streets of Belgravia.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social class, Family secrets, Love and relationships, Ambition
