Synopsis: 
In "Mud and Khaki: Sketches from Flanders and France," Bartlett, Vernon takes us on a captivating journey through the trenches of World War I. Through a series of vivid sketches, he brings to life the harrowing experiences of soldiers on the front lines, capturing both the camaraderie and the horrors of war. From the mud-soaked trenches to the desolate landscapes of Flanders and France, Bartlett's sketches offer a poignant and intimate glimpse into the lives of those who fought and sacrificed during this tumultuous time.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Literature, Memoir

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Human resilience
