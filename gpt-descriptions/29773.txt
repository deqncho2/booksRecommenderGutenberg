Synopsis: In 'Legends of the Wailuku' by Hapai, Charlotte, a young woman named Leilani embarks on a journey to uncover the ancient legends and mysteries surrounding the Wailuku River. As she delves deeper into the secrets of her ancestors, she discovers a hidden world filled with mythical creatures, powerful spirits, and a long-lost prophecy that could change her life forever. With the help of a mysterious guide, Leilani must navigate through treacherous landscapes and face her own fears in order to fulfill her destiny and protect her people. This captivating tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the Legends of the Wailuku.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mythology

Themes: Identity, Heritage, Courage, Destiny
