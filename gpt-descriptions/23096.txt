Synopsis: 
In 'And Judas Iscariot: Together with other evangelistic addresses', J. Wilbur Chapman delves into the enigmatic character of Judas Iscariot, exploring the depths of his betrayal and the profound lessons it holds for humanity. Through a series of captivating evangelistic addresses, Chapman invites readers to reflect on the complexities of human nature, the power of redemption, and the transformative potential of faith. With thought-provoking insights and a compelling narrative, this book offers a fresh perspective on one of the most infamous figures in history.

Genres: Religious, Christian Literature, Non-fiction

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Faith, Human Nature
