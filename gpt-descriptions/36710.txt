Synopsis: In 'The Black Opal' by Katharine Susannah Prichard, a mysterious black opal holds the power to change lives in the small Australian town of Yarloo. As the opal passes through the hands of various characters, it becomes a catalyst for greed, desire, and unexpected alliances. With its captivating allure, the black opal reveals the true nature of those who possess it, leading to a series of thrilling and unpredictable events that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Greed, Power, Morality
