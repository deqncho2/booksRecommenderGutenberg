Synopsis: In 'The Title Market' by Emily Post, a young woman stumbles upon a mysterious shop that sells titles. Intrigued by the possibilities, she decides to purchase a title for herself, unaware of the unexpected consequences that await her. As she delves deeper into the world of titles, she must navigate the complexities of identity, power, and the true value of a name. With each new title she acquires, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and desires, forcing her to question her own sense of self. Will she be able to find her true identity amidst the chaos of the title market?

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Identity, Power, Self-discovery
