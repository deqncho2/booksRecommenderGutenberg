Synopsis: In "The Head Girl at the Gables," Angela Brazil takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a young girl named Monica, who finds herself thrust into the role of head girl at her boarding school, the Gables. As Monica navigates the challenges of leadership, she must also navigate the complexities of friendship, loyalty, and personal growth. With unexpected twists and turns, Monica's journey at the Gables is filled with excitement, mystery, and self-discovery.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, School Story, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Leadership, Friendship, Personal Growth, Loyalty
