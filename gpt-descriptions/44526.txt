Synopsis: 

In "Report on the Radiolaria Collected by H.M.S. Challenger During the Years 1873-1876, Second Part: Subclass Osculosa; Index: Report on the Scientific Results of the Voyage of H.M.S. Challenger During the Years 1873-76, Vol. XVIII," Ernst Haeckel takes readers on a fascinating journey into the microscopic world of radiolaria. This second part of the report focuses on the subclass Osculosa, providing detailed descriptions and illustrations of these intricate marine organisms. Haeckel's meticulous observations and scientific analysis shed light on the diversity and complexity of these tiny creatures, offering a glimpse into the wonders of the natural world.

Genres: Scientific literature, Biology, Marine biology

Themes: Microscopic life, Biodiversity, Scientific exploration
