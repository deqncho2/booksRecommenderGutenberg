Synopsis: In 'Dust: A Novel' by Julian Hawthorne, a mysterious substance known as "dust" emerges, possessing the power to alter reality and challenge the boundaries of human existence. As the world grapples with the implications of this enigmatic substance, a diverse group of characters find themselves drawn into a web of intrigue, danger, and self-discovery. From a brilliant scientist to a cunning detective, each individual must navigate the treacherous landscape of power, morality, and the unknown, as they uncover the secrets behind the dust and its profound impact on humanity. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking revelations, 'Dust' is a captivating exploration of the human condition and the limitless possibilities of the universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Power and Morality, Human Potential
