Synopsis: The Rover Boys on Sunset Trail; or, The old miner's mysterious message is an exciting adventure novel that follows the thrilling journey of the Rover boys as they uncover a cryptic message left by an old miner. When the boys stumble upon a hidden treasure map, they embark on a perilous quest filled with danger, suspense, and unexpected twists. As they navigate treacherous trails and encounter a cast of intriguing characters, the Rover boys must use their wits and bravery to solve the mystery and claim the hidden riches. Will they succeed in unraveling the old miner's message and outsmarting their adversaries, or will they fall victim to the dangers lurking on Sunset Trail?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Treasure hunting, Friendship, Courage, Perseverance
