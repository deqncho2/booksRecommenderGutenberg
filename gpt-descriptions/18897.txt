Synopsis: 
In "The Epic of Gilgamish: A Fragment of the Gilgamish Legend in Old-Babylonian Cuneiform," Stephen Langdon takes readers on a captivating journey through ancient Mesopotamia. This fragmentary epic follows the adventures of Gilgamish, a legendary king who embarks on a quest for immortality. Along the way, he encounters mythical creatures, battles formidable foes, and grapples with the complexities of human existence. As the story unfolds, readers are transported to a world of gods and mortals, where themes of friendship, power, love, and the search for meaning intertwine.

Genres: 
Epic, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: 
Friendship, Power, Love, The Search for Meaning
