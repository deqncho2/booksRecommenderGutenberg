Synopsis: 
'Comediettas and Farces' by John Maddison Morton is a collection of hilarious and entertaining short plays that will leave readers in stitches. From mistaken identities to absurd situations, each play presents a unique and uproarious comedy of errors. Follow the misadventures of eccentric characters as they navigate through comical misunderstandings and outrageous predicaments. With witty dialogue and clever plot twists, this collection promises to keep readers thoroughly entertained from start to finish.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Mistaken identity, Absurdity, Humor
