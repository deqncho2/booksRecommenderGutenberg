Synopsis: In "Ships that pass in the night" by Beatrice Harraden, two strangers find themselves aboard a ship, each carrying their own secrets and burdens. As fate intertwines their paths, they embark on a journey that will challenge their beliefs, test their resilience, and ultimately lead them to question the true meaning of love and sacrifice. With unexpected twists and poignant moments, this captivating tale explores the profound connections we can form with those we meet fleetingly, and the profound impact they can have on our lives.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Fate and destiny, Love and sacrifice, The power of human connection
