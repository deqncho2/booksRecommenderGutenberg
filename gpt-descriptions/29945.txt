Synopsis: Jan, a young and ambitious woman, finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when she takes on a new job at a mysterious company. As she delves deeper into her role, Jan uncovers dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten not only her career but also her life. With each twist and turn, she must navigate a treacherous world of deception and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. Will Jan be able to survive the dangerous game she has unwittingly become a part of?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Betrayal
