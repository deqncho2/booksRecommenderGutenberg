Synopsis: In the small town of Black Spring, a seventeenth-century witch named Katherine van Wyler haunts the residents, cursed to roam the streets and homes of the town. The townspeople have learned to live with her presence, abiding by a set of strict rules to keep her contained. However, when a group of teenagers decides to break the rules and expose the witch to the world, they unleash a series of terrifying events that threaten to tear the town apart.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: Witchcraft, Fear, Community, Consequences
