Synopsis: In 'The Seventh Regiment: A Record', George L. Wood takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of the renowned Seventh Regiment of the New York National Guard. From its formation in 1806 to its involvement in major conflicts such as the Civil War and World War I, this book delves into the triumphs, sacrifices, and camaraderie of the soldiers who served in this esteemed regiment. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Wood brings to life the battles, the heroes, and the indomitable spirit that defined the Seventh Regiment.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Duty, Brotherhood, Sacrifice
