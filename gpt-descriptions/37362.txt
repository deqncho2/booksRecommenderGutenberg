Synopsis: 
In 'Beautiful Bulbous Plants for the Open Air', Weathers, John takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of bulbous plants, revealing their stunning beauty and the secrets behind their cultivation. From vibrant tulips to exotic lilies, this book showcases a wide array of these fascinating plants, providing practical tips and expert advice for both novice and experienced gardeners. As readers delve into the pages, they will be enchanted by the vivid descriptions and breathtaking photographs that bring these bulbous plants to life, inspiring them to create their own vibrant outdoor oasis. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Gardening, Nature

Themes: Botany, Horticulture, Beauty of Nature
