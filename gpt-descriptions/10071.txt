Synopsis:
'By-Ways of Bombay' takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling streets of Bombay, offering a glimpse into the hidden corners and untold stories of the city. From the opulent mansions of the wealthy to the crowded bazaars teeming with life, the author weaves together a tapestry of vivid characters and intriguing encounters. As the narrative unfolds, secrets are unveiled, mysteries are unraveled, and the reader becomes immersed in the vibrant tapestry of Bombay's diverse society. With each turn of the page, the city's rich history, cultural nuances, and the complexities of human nature are brought to life, leaving readers yearning for more.

Genres:
Fiction, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Exploration of urban life, Cultural diversity, Secrets and mysteries
