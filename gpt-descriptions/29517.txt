Synopsis: In 'John and Betty's History Visit' by Margaret Williamson, a couple embarks on a thrilling journey through time, exploring various historical eras and encountering unexpected challenges along the way. As they navigate through different periods, their bond is tested, and they must rely on their wit and resourcefulness to overcome the obstacles that arise. With each new adventure, John and Betty uncover hidden secrets and gain a deeper understanding of themselves and the world around them. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what awaits the couple in their next historical escapade.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Time travel, Love and relationships, Self-discovery
