Synopsis:
In 'Carpets' by R.S. Brinton, a mysterious antique carpet becomes the centerpiece of a captivating tale that spans generations. As the carpet weaves its way through different time periods and continents, it holds secrets that have the power to change lives. From the bustling bazaars of Istanbul to the elegant drawing rooms of London, the characters are drawn into a web of intrigue, love, and betrayal. As they unravel the enigma of the carpet's origins, they discover that its true value lies not in its monetary worth, but in the stories it carries and the connections it forges between people.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes:
Legacy, Identity, Connection
