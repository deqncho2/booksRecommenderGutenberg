Synopsis: 
In "Jasper's Old Shed, and How the Light Shone In" by A. M. Coker, a mysterious old shed becomes the focal point of a young boy's curiosity. As Jasper ventures inside, he discovers a hidden world filled with secrets and unexpected wonders. With each visit, the shed reveals more about its past and its connection to Jasper's own life. The story takes readers on a captivating journey of exploration, friendship, and the power of imagination.

Genres: 
Children's Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: 
Curiosity, Discovery, Friendship, Imagination
