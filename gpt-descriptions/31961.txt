Synopsis: 
In 'Joy Ride' by Meadows, Mark, three friends embark on a thrilling road trip across the country, seeking adventure and freedom. Little do they know that their journey will take an unexpected turn when they encounter a mysterious stranger on the road. As their innocent joy ride turns into a dangerous game of cat and mouse, the friends must rely on their wits and courage to survive the nightmarish ordeal that awaits them.

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Trust, Survival
