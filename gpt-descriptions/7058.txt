Synopsis:
In "Mankind in the Making," H.G. Wells takes us on a captivating journey through the evolution of human society, exploring the forces that shape our progress and the potential for a brighter future. From the origins of civilization to the complexities of modern life, Wells delves into the intricate web of human nature, examining the interplay between individuality and collective progress. With his characteristic blend of insightful analysis and thought-provoking speculation, Wells offers a compelling vision of the possibilities that lie ahead for mankind.

Genres:
Non-fiction, Social Science, Philosophy

Themes:
Evolution of society, Human nature, Progress, Individuality, Collective progress
