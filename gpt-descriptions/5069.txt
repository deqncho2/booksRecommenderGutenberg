Synopsis: In 'The Doctor's Dilemma: Preface on Doctors' by Bernard Shaw, the reader is introduced to a thought-provoking exploration of the medical profession. Shaw delves into the complex ethical and moral dilemmas faced by doctors, challenging conventional notions of medical practice. Through a series of witty and insightful observations, Shaw presents a compelling argument that will leave readers questioning the true motivations and responsibilities of those in the medical field.

Genres: Drama, Satire, Comedy

Themes: Ethics, Morality, Medical Responsibility
