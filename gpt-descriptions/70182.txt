Synopsis: In "Tom Swift and his Flying Boat; or, The Castaways of the Giant Iceberg," Tom Swift embarks on a thrilling adventure when he sets out to rescue a group of castaways stranded on a massive iceberg. Using his innovative flying boat, Tom faces treacherous weather conditions, encounters mysterious creatures, and uncovers a hidden secret that could change the course of history. As he races against time to save the castaways, Tom's courage and ingenuity are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Invention
