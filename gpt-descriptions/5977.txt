Synopsis: In 'Bound to Rise; Or, Up the Ladder' by Horatio Alger, Jr., we follow the journey of a young protagonist who faces numerous challenges and setbacks in his pursuit of success. From humble beginnings, he navigates through a world filled with adversity, deceit, and unexpected opportunities. With determination and resilience, he strives to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way, all while discovering the true meaning of ambition and the power of self-belief.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Rags-to-riches

Themes: Perseverance, Social mobility, Integrity
