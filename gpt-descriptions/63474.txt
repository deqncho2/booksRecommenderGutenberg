Synopsis: In 'Alien Equivalent' by Smith, Richard Rein, a group of scientists embarks on a groundbreaking mission to a distant planet, only to discover a shocking secret that challenges everything they thought they knew about extraterrestrial life. As they delve deeper into the mysteries of this alien world, they must confront their own fears and grapple with the ethical implications of their discoveries. With unexpected twists and a sense of impending danger, 'Alien Equivalent' takes readers on a thrilling journey that will leave them questioning the boundaries of humanity and the vastness of the universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Morality, Existentialism
