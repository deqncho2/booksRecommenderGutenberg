Synopsis: In 'Our Little Austrian Cousin', we follow the captivating journey of a young Austrian girl named Gretl as she navigates the enchanting city of Vienna. From exploring the grand palaces and attending the famous Vienna Boys' Choir performances to encountering the vibrant culture and traditions of her homeland, Gretl's adventures are filled with excitement and discovery. Along the way, she forms unexpected friendships and learns valuable lessons about loyalty, courage, and the importance of family. This delightful tale offers readers a charming glimpse into the life of a young Austrian girl and the rich heritage that surrounds her.

Genres: Children's Literature, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cultural exploration, Friendship, Family values
