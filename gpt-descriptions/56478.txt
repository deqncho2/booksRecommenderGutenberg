Synopsis: 

In "Old Mines of Southern California: Desert-Mountain-Coastal Areas Including the Calico-Salton Sea Colorado River Districts and Southern Counties," authors Harold W. Fairbanks and William H. Storms take readers on an intriguing journey through the hidden history of Southern California's old mines. From the rugged desert landscapes to the majestic mountains and picturesque coastal areas, this comprehensive guide uncovers the secrets of the region's rich mining heritage. Delving into the Calico-Salton Sea Colorado River Districts and Southern Counties, the authors provide a captivating exploration of the forgotten mines that once shaped the landscape and the lives of those who sought their fortunes within. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: Mining history, Southern California, Exploration, Heritage preservation
