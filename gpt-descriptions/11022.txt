Synopsis: In 'Sowing and Reaping: A Temperance Story' by Frances Ellen Watkins Harper, we follow the life of a young woman named Ruth who finds herself caught in the destructive grip of alcoholism. As she navigates the challenges of her addiction, Ruth's journey takes her through heartbreak, redemption, and the power of personal transformation. Set against the backdrop of the temperance movement, this gripping tale explores the consequences of alcohol abuse and the strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: Alcoholism, Redemption, Personal Transformation, Social Issues
