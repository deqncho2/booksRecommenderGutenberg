Synopsis: In "The Theory of Psychoanalysis," renowned psychologist Carl Gustav Jung delves into the depths of the human psyche, exploring the hidden realms of the unconscious mind. Through a captivating blend of case studies, personal anecdotes, and theoretical analysis, Jung unveils the intricate workings of the human psyche, shedding light on the mysteries of dreams, symbols, and the collective unconscious. As he challenges the prevailing theories of his time, Jung invites readers on a transformative journey of self-discovery and understanding, ultimately revealing the profound impact of the unconscious on our everyday lives.

Genres: Psychology, Philosophy, Non-fiction

Themes: Unconscious mind, Dreams, Symbols, Self-discovery, Collective unconscious
