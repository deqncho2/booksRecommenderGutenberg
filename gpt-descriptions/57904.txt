Synopsis: In 'The Doom of London', Fred M. White takes readers on a thrilling journey through a series of interconnected stories that explore the potential catastrophes that could befall the city of London. From deadly diseases to devastating natural disasters, this gripping collection of tales delves into the dark and unpredictable forces that threaten the metropolis. As the city faces one peril after another, the characters must navigate the chaos and find ways to survive against all odds. With each story more gripping than the last, 'The Doom of London' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what disaster will strike next.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Catastrophe, Survival, Human Resilience
