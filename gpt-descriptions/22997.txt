Synopsis: In 'Second Sight' by Alan Edward Nourse, a brilliant young doctor named Tom is faced with a perplexing medical mystery when a series of patients arrive at his clinic with unexplainable symptoms. As he delves deeper into their cases, he discovers a shocking connection that challenges everything he thought he knew about medicine and the human mind. With time running out, Tom must navigate a treacherous path of scientific ethics, personal sacrifice, and the blurred boundaries between science and superstition to uncover the truth and save his patients.

Genres: Medical thriller, Mystery, Science fiction

Themes: Medical ethics, Supernatural phenomena, Human consciousness
