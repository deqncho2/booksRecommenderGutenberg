Synopsis: In the 18th volume of his captivating diary, Samuel Pepys takes us on a journey through the vibrant streets of London during September and October 1662. As a high-ranking naval administrator, Pepys encounters a myriad of intriguing characters and experiences, from attending grand social events to navigating the complexities of his personal relationships. Amidst the backdrop of a bustling city, Pepys provides a vivid and intimate account of his daily life, filled with scandalous gossip, political intrigue, and his own personal triumphs and tribulations.

Genres: Diary, Historical Fiction, Memoir

Themes: Social life, Politics, Personal relationships, Intrigue, Historical events
