Synopsis: 
'Poems of Giosuè Carducci, Translated with two introductory essays: I. Giosuè Carducci and the Hellenic reaction in Italy. II. Carducci and the classic realism' is a collection of poems by Giosuè Carducci, a renowned Italian poet. The book includes a selection of his poems translated into English, along with two introductory essays that provide insights into Carducci's literary influences and his approach to classic realism. The collection showcases Carducci's mastery of poetic expression and his deep appreciation for the Hellenic tradition, while also exploring the themes of national identity, cultural heritage, and the power of language.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Criticism, Translation
Themes: National identity, Cultural heritage, Language and expression
