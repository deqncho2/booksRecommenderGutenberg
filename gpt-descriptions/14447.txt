Synopsis:
In "Letters to "The Times" upon War and Neutrality (1881-1920)" by Thomas Erskine Holland, readers are transported to a tumultuous period in history through a collection of thought-provoking letters. Spanning four decades, these letters offer a unique perspective on the complex issues of war and neutrality, as seen through the eyes of a keen observer. From the rise of nationalism to the devastating impact of global conflicts, Holland's letters provide a captivating glimpse into the political and moral dilemmas faced by nations during this transformative era.

Genres:
Historical non-fiction, Epistolary

Themes:
War, Neutrality, Nationalism, Ethics
