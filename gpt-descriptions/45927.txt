Synopsis: In "Seets i' Paris," Sammywell Grimes embarks on a thrilling adventure with his old friend Billy Baccus to the vibrant city of Paris. As they immerse themselves in the French culture, Sammywell shares his candid opinions about the French people, their customs, and their way of life. However, the story takes an unexpected turn when Sammywell's exhibition of himself becomes the talk of the town, capturing the attention of both locals and tourists alike. Through Hartley's masterful storytelling, the reader is left intrigued to discover how Sammywell's exhibition unfolds and the impact it has on his perception of the French, as well as their opinion of him.

Genres: Fiction, Travelogue, Comedy

Themes: Cultural differences, Self-discovery, Perception
