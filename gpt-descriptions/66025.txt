Synopsis: In 'The Lady Poverty: A XIII. Century Allegory' by Giovanni da Parma, readers are transported to a captivating world where a mysterious lady named Poverty takes center stage. As the story unfolds, we follow Poverty's journey through various encounters and challenges, witnessing her impact on the lives of those she encounters. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking moments, this allegorical tale explores the complexities of human nature and the profound lessons that can be learned from embracing the unexpected. 

Genres: Allegory, Fiction, Historical

Themes: Poverty, Human Nature, Transformation
