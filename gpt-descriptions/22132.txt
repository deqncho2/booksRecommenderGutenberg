Synopsis: In 'Giants on the Earth' by S. P. Meek, a team of scientists embarks on a daring expedition to a remote island in the Pacific Ocean. As they uncover ancient artifacts and encounter mysterious phenomena, they soon realize that they are not alone. The island holds a secret that could change the course of human history forever. With danger lurking at every turn, the team must navigate treacherous terrain and face unimaginable challenges in their quest for knowledge and survival.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Ancient Civilizations, Survival
