Synopsis: 
'A Dog's Tale' by Mark Twain is a heartwarming and thought-provoking novella that follows the life of a loyal and intelligent dog named Aileen. From her innocent puppyhood to her experiences as a beloved family pet, Aileen's story takes unexpected turns, revealing the complexities of human nature and the enduring bond between humans and animals. Through Aileen's eyes, readers witness the joys, sorrows, and challenges of life, ultimately questioning the true meaning of loyalty and the power of unconditional love.

Genres: Fiction, Animal Fiction, Satire

Themes: Loyalty, Human-Animal Relationships, Innocence, Social Critique
