Synopsis: In 'The True Citizen: How to Become One', Markwick and Smith take readers on a captivating journey through the complexities of citizenship. Through thought-provoking anecdotes and insightful analysis, they explore the qualities that define a true citizen and provide practical guidance on how to cultivate these attributes. From the importance of civic engagement to the power of empathy, this book offers a compelling exploration of what it truly means to be a citizen in today's world.

Genres: Self-help, Philosophy, Social Sciences

Themes: Citizenship, Civic Engagement, Personal Development, Empathy, Social Responsibility
