Synopsis: In 'The Quest' by Pío Baroja, a young man named Andrés embarks on a journey that takes him through the vibrant streets of Madrid, the rugged landscapes of the Basque Country, and the mystical world of the Pyrenees. Fueled by a desire for adventure and a thirst for knowledge, Andrés encounters a cast of intriguing characters who challenge his beliefs and push him to his limits. As he navigates through love, loss, and self-discovery, Andrés must confront the complexities of life and the existential questions that haunt him. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Literary Fiction

Themes: Quest for meaning, Identity, Exploration of the human condition
