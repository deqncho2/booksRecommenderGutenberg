Synopsis: 
In 'Seven Short Plays' by Gregory, Lady, readers are taken on a captivating journey through seven distinct and thought-provoking stories. From a mysterious encounter in a secluded garden to a tense confrontation between two former lovers, each play presents a unique and compelling narrative that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. With unexpected twists and moments of profound introspection, these short plays explore the complexities of human relationships, the power of secrets, and the transformative nature of love and loss.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Relationships, Secrets, Love and Loss
