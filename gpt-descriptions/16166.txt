Synopsis: 

In 'The Seven Great Monarchies Of The Ancient Eastern World, Vol 6: Parthia', George Rawlinson takes readers on a captivating journey through the history, geography, and antiquities of the ancient Eastern empires. Focusing on Chaldaea, Assyria, Babylon, Media, Persia, Parthia, and the Sassanian or New Persian Empire, Rawlinson weaves together a rich tapestry of knowledge, bringing to life the fascinating civilizations that once thrived in these lands. With the aid of maps and illustrations, the author paints a vivid picture of the rise and fall of these great empires, exploring their political, cultural, and military achievements. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Archaeology, Ancient History

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Empire building, Cultural heritage, Historical exploration
