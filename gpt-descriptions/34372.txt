Synopsis: In "Fra Angelico: A Sketch" by Jennie Ellis Keysor, readers are transported to the vibrant world of Renaissance Italy, where they follow the life and artistic journey of the renowned painter Fra Angelico. Through vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, the author delves into the artist's struggles, triumphs, and the profound impact of his work on the world of art. From his humble beginnings as a Dominican friar to his encounters with influential figures of the time, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the life of a true master. 

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Art

Themes: Artistic inspiration, Faith and spirituality, Renaissance culture
