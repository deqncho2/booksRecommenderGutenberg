Synopsis: 
In the small town of St. Malo, Madame Claire, a mysterious and enigmatic woman, arrives and opens a quaint antique shop. As the townspeople become captivated by her charm and the allure of her treasures, they soon discover that Madame Claire possesses an uncanny ability to uncover the hidden stories and emotions behind each object she sells. Through her interactions with the townspeople, Madame Claire not only transforms their lives but also reveals the profound impact that the past can have on the present. As secrets are unveiled and long-forgotten memories resurface, the residents of St. Malo find themselves on a journey of self-discovery and healing, all under the watchful eye of Madame Claire.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Magical Realism

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of objects and memories, self-discovery and healing
