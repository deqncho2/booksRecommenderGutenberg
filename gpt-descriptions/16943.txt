Synopsis: 
In "Paris: With Pen and Pencil: Its People and Literature, Its Life and Business," author David W. Bartlett takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting streets of Paris. Through vivid descriptions and charming illustrations, Bartlett paints a vibrant picture of the city's bustling life, its rich literary heritage, and its thriving business scene. From the iconic landmarks to the hidden gems, readers will be transported to the heart of Paris, discovering its unique charm and the fascinating stories that lie within its streets. 

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Art

Themes: City life, Literature, Cultural heritage
