Synopsis: In "François the waif" by George Sand, a young boy named François finds himself caught between the harsh realities of poverty and the allure of a life filled with adventure. As he navigates the streets of Paris, he encounters a cast of colorful characters who both help and hinder his journey. With his quick wit and determination, François must learn to survive in a world that often seems stacked against him. This captivating tale explores the resilience of the human spirit and the power of hope in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical fiction, coming-of-age, adventure

Themes: Poverty, resilience, survival, hope
