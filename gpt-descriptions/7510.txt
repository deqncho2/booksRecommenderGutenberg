Synopsis: In 'Keith of the Border: A Tale of the Plains' by Parrish, Randall, follow the thrilling journey of Keith, a young cowboy with a mysterious past, as he navigates the treacherous landscapes of the Wild West. Filled with action, danger, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as Keith's courage and resilience are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Survival
