Synopsis:
In "A Century of Roundels," Algernon Charles Swinburne takes readers on a poetic journey through a collection of one hundred roundel poems. Each roundel is a compact and exquisite piece of verse, capturing moments of love, beauty, and longing. Swinburne's words dance across the pages, painting vivid images and evoking powerful emotions. From the depths of despair to the heights of ecstasy, these roundels explore the complexities of human emotions and the fleeting nature of life. With each poem, Swinburne invites readers to immerse themselves in the beauty of language and the power of poetic expression.

Genres: Poetry, Classic Literature

Themes: Love, Beauty, Emotions, Transience
