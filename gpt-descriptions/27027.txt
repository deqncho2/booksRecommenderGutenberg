Synopsis: 

'A Chronicle of London from 1089 to 1483: Written in the Fifteenth Century, and for the First Time Printed from MSS. in the British Museum' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of London, spanning almost four centuries. This anonymous chronicle, finally brought to light from manuscripts in the British Museum, offers a unique perspective on the city's evolution, its notable events, and the lives of its inhabitants. From political upheavals and royal intrigues to social customs and cultural transformations, this chronicle unveils the hidden stories that shaped London's destiny. 

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, Chronicle

Themes: History, Urban Development, Social Change
