Synopsis: In "An Introduction to the History of Science" by Libby Walter, embark on a captivating journey through the annals of scientific discovery. From ancient civilizations to modern breakthroughs, this book unveils the fascinating stories behind the greatest scientific minds and their groundbreaking achievements. Delve into the mysteries of the universe, witness the clash of ideas, and explore the profound impact of science on society. With vivid narratives and engaging anecdotes, this book offers a compelling exploration of the evolution of scientific thought that will leave readers inspired and in awe of the wonders of the natural world.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Scientific discovery, Intellectual curiosity, Societal impact
