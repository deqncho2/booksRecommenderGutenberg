Synopsis: In 'The Awful Australian' by Desmond, Valerie, a young woman named Emily embarks on a thrilling adventure to the land down under. Drawn by the allure of the unknown and the promise of self-discovery, she finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected romance. As Emily delves deeper into the mysteries of Australia, she uncovers a hidden world filled with ancient legends, treacherous landscapes, and a sinister plot that could change the course of history. With each step she takes, Emily must confront her fears, trust her instincts, and navigate the treacherous path that leads to the truth. Will she unravel the enigma that lies at the heart of the Australian outback, or will she become another victim of its dark secrets?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Exploration, Secrets, Danger
