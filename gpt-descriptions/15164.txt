Synopsis: 
'Folk Tales Every Child Should Know' is a captivating collection of timeless stories that will transport readers to enchanting worlds filled with magical creatures, brave heroes, and cunning villains. From the depths of the sea to the highest mountaintops, these folk tales will ignite the imagination and leave readers eagerly turning the pages to discover the next captivating adventure. With tales of love, bravery, and the triumph of good over evil, this collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages and inspire a love for the rich tradition of folklore.

Genres: 
Fairy tales, folklore, fantasy

Themes: 
Love, bravery, good vs. evil
