Synopsis: 

"The Story of Anna Kingsford and Edward Maitland and of the new Gospel of Interpretation" is a captivating non-fiction account that delves into the extraordinary lives of Anna Kingsford and Edward Maitland. This enthralling narrative takes readers on a journey through their unique spiritual experiences, their profound collaboration, and the creation of a groundbreaking interpretation of the Gospel. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, mysteries are unraveled, and the reader is left with a sense of awe and wonder.

Genres: Biography, Spiritual, Philosophy

Themes: Spiritual awakening, Mysticism, Collaboration, Interpretation of religious texts
