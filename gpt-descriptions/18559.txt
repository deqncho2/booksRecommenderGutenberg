Synopsis: In 'The Child's Day' by Hutchinson Woods, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden world within her own imagination. As she embarks on a magical adventure, she encounters mysterious creatures, uncovers long-lost secrets, and learns the power of self-belief. With each passing day, Lily's journey becomes more enchanting and perilous, leading her to question the boundaries between reality and fantasy. This captivating tale will transport readers to a realm where dreams come alive and imagination knows no limits.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Imagination, Self-discovery, Belief in oneself
