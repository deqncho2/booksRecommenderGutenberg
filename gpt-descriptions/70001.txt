Synopsis: In 'Metamorphosis' by Curry, Mike, a seemingly ordinary man wakes up one morning to find himself transformed into a bizarre creature. As he grapples with his new form, he must navigate the challenges of his altered existence and the reactions of those around him. This gripping tale explores the profound impact of transformation on identity, relationships, and the human condition.

Genres: Science Fiction, Psychological Thriller, Literary Fiction

Themes: Identity, Isolation, Alienation, Existentialism
