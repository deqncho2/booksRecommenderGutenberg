Synopsis: 

In 'Seaward Sussex: The South Downs from End to End', Edric Holmes takes readers on an enchanting journey through the picturesque landscapes of the South Downs. From the rolling hills and ancient woodlands to the breathtaking cliffs and serene coastal paths, this book is a captivating exploration of one of England's most stunning regions. Through vivid descriptions and immersive storytelling, Holmes invites readers to discover the hidden gems, historical landmarks, and fascinating wildlife that make the South Downs a true natural wonder. 

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Nature Writing

Themes: Nature appreciation, Cultural heritage, Adventure, Environmental conservation
