Synopsis: In 'A Fortune Hunter; Or, The Old Stone Corral: A Tale of the Santa Fe Trail', we follow the thrilling journey of a young man named John Dunloe as he embarks on a treacherous expedition along the Santa Fe Trail in search of wealth and adventure. As he faces the perils of the untamed wilderness, encounters with dangerous outlaws, and unexpected alliances, John's determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he find the fortune he seeks, or will he discover something even more valuable along the way?

Genres: Adventure, Western, Historical Fiction

Themes: Pursuit of wealth, Survival in the wilderness, Unexpected discoveries
