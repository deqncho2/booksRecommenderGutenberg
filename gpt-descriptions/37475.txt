Synopsis: 

'Peeps at Many Lands: Turkey' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and diverse country of Turkey. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, the author, Julius R. Van Millingen, offers a unique glimpse into the rich history, cultural traditions, and breathtaking landscapes of this fascinating land. From the bustling streets of Istanbul to the ancient ruins of Ephesus, from the serene beauty of Cappadocia to the picturesque shores of the Mediterranean, this book immerses readers in the enchanting tapestry of Turkish life. With a keen eye for detail and a deep appreciation for the country's heritage, Van Millingen paints a vivid picture of Turkey, leaving readers eager to explore its wonders for themselves.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Cultural

Themes: History, Culture, Exploration
