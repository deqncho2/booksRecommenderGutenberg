Synopsis: In "Clever Hans (The Horse of Mr. Von Osten): A contribution to experimental animal and human psychology," Oskar Pfungst delves into the fascinating story of Clever Hans, a horse who seemingly possessed the ability to perform complex mathematical calculations and answer questions through hoof tapping. As the world marveled at this extraordinary talent, Pfungst embarks on a scientific investigation to uncover the truth behind Hans' abilities. Through meticulous experiments and observations, he uncovers a surprising explanation that challenges our understanding of animal intelligence and human psychology.

Genres: Non-fiction, Psychology, Animal Studies

Themes: Animal cognition, Human-animal communication, Scientific inquiry
