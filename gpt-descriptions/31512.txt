Synopsis: In 'The Coming of the Law' by Charles Alden Seltzer, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small western town, stirring up trouble and challenging the corrupt local law enforcement. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the townspeople must decide whether to embrace justice or succumb to fear. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Justice, Corruption, Redemption
