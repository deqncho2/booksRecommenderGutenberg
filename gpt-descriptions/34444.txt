Synopsis: 

In 'The Story of the Great War, Volume 8: Victory with the Allies; Armistice; Peace Congress; Canada's War Organizations and vast War Industries; Canadian Battles Overseas', readers are taken on a captivating journey through the final stages of World War I and its aftermath. This volume focuses on the pivotal role played by Canada in the war effort, highlighting their war organizations, vast war industries, and the courageous battles fought by Canadian soldiers overseas. From the armistice negotiations to the Peace Congress, this book provides a comprehensive account of the events that led to victory and the subsequent efforts to establish lasting peace.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, World War I

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, International diplomacy, War and peace
