Synopsis: 
'A List of Philippine Baptismal Names' is a captivating collection of short stories that delves into the rich cultural tapestry of the Philippines through the lens of baptismal names. Each story explores the unique and often surprising meanings behind these names, revealing the complexities of Filipino identity and the diverse experiences of its people. From tales of love and loss to stories of resilience and redemption, this book takes readers on a journey that celebrates the power of names to shape lives and connect generations.

Genres: Fiction, Cultural, Short Stories

Themes: Identity, Tradition, Family, Heritage
