Synopsis: 
'A Guide to the Exhibition of English Medals' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of English medals. Written by the British Museum's Department of Coins and Medals, and Herbert A. Grueber, this guide provides a comprehensive overview of the historical and artistic significance of English medals. From the intricate designs to the stories behind each medal, readers will discover a treasure trove of information that brings these pieces of history to life. Whether you are a history enthusiast, an art lover, or simply curious about the hidden stories behind these medals, this guide is sure to captivate your imagination.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, History, Art

Themes: 
Historical significance, Artistic interpretation, Cultural heritage
