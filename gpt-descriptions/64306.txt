Synopsis: In "Artificial Fire-works: Improved to the Modern Practice, from the Minutest to the Highest Branches," Robert Jones takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of pyrotechnics. From the humblest beginnings to the most awe-inspiring displays, this book unveils the secrets behind creating dazzling firework spectacles. With detailed instructions and historical anecdotes, Jones explores the art and science of pyrotechnics, leaving readers eager to discover the magic and beauty that can be ignited in the night sky.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, How-to

Themes: Pyrotechnics, Artistry, Historical Evolution
