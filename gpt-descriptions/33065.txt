Synopsis: In the heart of the American Southwest, a mysterious Indian drum holds a power that can change the course of history. When a young archaeologist stumbles upon this ancient artifact, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of greed, betrayal, and supernatural forces. As he races against time to unlock the secrets of the drum, he must confront his own inner demons and make a choice that could alter the fate of humanity forever.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Power of ancient artifacts, Good vs. evil, Redemption
