Synopsis: In 'The Play-day Book: New Stories for Little Folks' by Fern, Fanny, embark on a delightful journey through a collection of enchanting tales that will captivate the imagination of young readers. From magical adventures to heartwarming friendships, each story is filled with wonder and excitement. Join the lovable characters as they navigate through challenges, discover hidden treasures, and learn valuable life lessons along the way. With its charming narratives and engaging storytelling, this book is sure to become a cherished favorite for children and adults alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Life Lessons
