Synopsis: In the gripping novel "The Port of Missing Men" by Nicholson, Meredith, a young man named Jack Merrill finds himself entangled in a mysterious disappearance that leads him to the treacherous underworld of San Francisco's waterfront. As he delves deeper into the dark secrets of the city, Jack becomes entwined with a captivating woman who holds the key to the truth. With danger lurking at every turn, Jack must navigate a web of deceit and betrayal to uncover the shocking reality behind the port's missing men.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Crime and corruption, Identity and deception
