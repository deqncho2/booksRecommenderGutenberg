Synopsis: 
In 'Eliza' by Pain, Barry, a young woman named Eliza finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter that leads her on a thrilling journey. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Eliza must navigate through a world of hidden identities, unexpected alliances, and dangerous encounters. With each revelation, she becomes more entangled in a dangerous game where trust is a luxury she cannot afford. Will Eliza uncover the truth before it's too late, or will she become another casualty in this treacherous game of cat and mouse?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Trust
