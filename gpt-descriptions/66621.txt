Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Fifty-sixth, or the West Essex Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment from 1755 to 1844. From the regiment's humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, Cannon delves into the triumphs and challenges faced by the West Essex Regiment of Foot, offering a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the soldiers who served.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military service, Regimental history, Historical events
