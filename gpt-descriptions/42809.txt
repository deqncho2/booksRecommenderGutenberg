Synopsis: 
In 'A Treatise on the Incubus, or Night-Mare, Disturbed Sleep, Terrific Dreams and Nocturnal Visions' by John Augustine Waller, a renowned scholar delves into the mysterious realm of the supernatural, exploring the haunting phenomenon of the incubus. Through a captivating blend of historical accounts, scientific analysis, and personal experiences, Waller takes readers on a chilling journey into the dark recesses of the human psyche, where nightmares come alive and dreams turn into terrifying realities. As he unravels the enigma of the incubus, Waller uncovers a web of secrets and hidden truths that will leave readers questioning the boundaries between the real and the imagined.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Psychology

Themes: Supernatural phenomena, Sleep disorders, Human consciousness
