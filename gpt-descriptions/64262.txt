Synopsis: In "Buffalo Bill's Big Surprise; Or, The Biggest Stampede on Record" by Ingraham, Prentiss, the legendary Buffalo Bill finds himself facing an unexpected challenge that threatens to disrupt the Wild West show he has worked so hard to build. As he embarks on a daring adventure to overcome this obstacle, Bill must rely on his wit, courage, and the loyalty of his friends to save the day. With thrilling twists and turns, this action-packed tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Historical Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Overcoming Challenges
