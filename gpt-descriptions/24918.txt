Synopsis: In 'Hollowdell Grange: Holiday Hours in a Country Home' by George Manville Fenn, a group of friends embarks on a much-needed vacation to the idyllic Hollowdell Grange. However, their peaceful retreat takes an unexpected turn when they stumble upon a mysterious secret hidden within the walls of the old country home. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, testing their friendships and challenging their perceptions of reality. With each passing hour, the tension rises, and the true nature of Hollowdell Grange begins to unravel, leaving the characters questioning their own sanity and the dark forces at play.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Friendship and loyalty, Perception and reality
