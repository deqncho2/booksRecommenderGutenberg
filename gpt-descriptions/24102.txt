Synopsis: Owen Clancy's Happy Trail; Or, The Motor Wizard in California follows the thrilling adventures of Owen Clancy, a young and talented mechanic, as he embarks on a journey to California. Drawn by the allure of the Golden State, Owen finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger, encountering eccentric characters and uncovering secrets that could change his life forever. As he navigates through the picturesque landscapes and treacherous obstacles, Owen's skills as a motor wizard are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and find his own version of happiness?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Pursuit of dreams, Self-discovery, Resilience
