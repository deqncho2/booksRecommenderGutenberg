Synopsis: In 'Opera Stories from Wagner' by Florence Akin, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Richard Wagner's operas. From the mythical realm of gods and heroes in "The Ring of the Nibelung" to the tragic love story of "Tristan and Isolde," this collection brings to life the timeless tales of passion, betrayal, and redemption. Through vivid storytelling, Akin transports readers to the grand stages of Wagner's masterpieces, where they will witness the power of music and the depths of human emotions. 

Genres: Music, Drama, Fantasy

Themes: Love and Desire, Fate and Destiny, Mythology
