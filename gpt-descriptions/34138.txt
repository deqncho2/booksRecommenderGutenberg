Synopsis: In 'Lady Maude's Mania' by George Manville Fenn, a young woman named Lady Maude finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she becomes obsessed with a peculiar hobby. As her mania takes hold, Lady Maude's life takes a thrilling turn as she uncovers secrets, encounters danger, and discovers unexpected allies. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into Lady Maude's world, eagerly following her journey to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Obsession, Secrets, Adventure
