Synopsis: 
In 'The complete works of John Gower, volume 2: The English works', readers will delve into the captivating world of John Gower's literary genius. This volume showcases Gower's remarkable English works, including his renowned poem 'Confessio Amantis' and his lesser-known but equally compelling works. From tales of love and romance to moral lessons and social commentary, Gower's writings offer a fascinating glimpse into the medieval world. With his eloquent language and thought-provoking narratives, Gower's works continue to captivate readers, transporting them to a bygone era filled with intrigue and wisdom.

Genres: Poetry, Medieval Literature, Moral Literature

Themes: Love, Morality, Social Commentary
