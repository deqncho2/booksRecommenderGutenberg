Synopsis: In Castle Hohenwald, a captivating romance unfolds within the walls of a majestic castle. As secrets are revealed and forbidden desires are explored, the lives of the inhabitants become entangled in a web of passion and intrigue. With its lush descriptions and complex characters, this novel takes readers on a journey through love, betrayal, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Secrets and deception, Power and control
