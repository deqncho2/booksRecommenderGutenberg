Synopsis: In 'The Love of Frank Nineteen' by David C. Knight, readers are introduced to Frank, a seemingly ordinary young man who discovers a hidden world of love and passion. As he navigates through a series of unexpected encounters and heart-wrenching choices, Frank's life takes an exhilarating turn that challenges his beliefs and forces him to question the nature of love itself. With each twist and turn, the story delves into the complexities of human relationships, exploring the depths of desire, sacrifice, and the power of connection.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love, Identity, Self-discovery
