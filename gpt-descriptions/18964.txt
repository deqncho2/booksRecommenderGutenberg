Synopsis: In 'Wolf Breed' by Gregory Jackson, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances, leaving the community in fear and confusion. As the investigation unfolds, a young detective uncovers a dark secret that connects the missing individuals to a hidden world of supernatural creatures. With time running out, she must navigate the treacherous path of trust and betrayal to save her town from an ancient evil that threatens to consume them all.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Good vs. Evil, Sacrifice and redemption
