Synopsis: 

'Primer of Ohio Archaeology: The Mound Builders and the Indians' by H. C. Shetrone takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of Ohio's ancient civilizations. From the mysterious mound builders to the indigenous tribes that inhabited the region, this book unveils the secrets buried beneath the Ohio soil. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Shetrone paints a vivid picture of the people who once called this land home, their customs, and the incredible structures they left behind. As readers delve into the pages of this primer, they will uncover the fascinating world of Ohio archaeology and gain a deeper understanding of the complex tapestry of human history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Archaeology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Indigenous cultures, Archaeological discoveries
