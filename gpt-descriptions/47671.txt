Synopsis: In 'The Heart of the Ancient Wood', a young boy named Lionel embarks on a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden portal that leads him into a magical and mysterious ancient wood. As Lionel explores this enchanted realm, he encounters fantastical creatures, uncovers long-lost secrets, and must confront his own fears and doubts. With the fate of the ancient wood hanging in the balance, Lionel must summon his courage and embark on a quest to save this extraordinary world from impending doom.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Discovery, Nature, Self-discovery
