Synopsis: In "The Outdoor Girls in Army Service; Or, Doing Their Bit for the Soldier Boys" by Laura Lee Hope, the adventurous Outdoor Girls find themselves embarking on a new mission as they join the army service during World War I. With their determination and resourcefulness, they face challenges, uncover secrets, and prove their loyalty to the soldier boys. Will they succeed in their mission and make a difference in the war effort?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Patriotism, Friendship, Courage, Women's Empowerment
