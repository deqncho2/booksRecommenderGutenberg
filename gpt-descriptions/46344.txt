Synopsis: In 'Eighty-fourth Regiment, Pennsylvania Volunteers (Infantry),' Thomas Edward Merchant takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of a regiment during the American Civil War. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Merchant brings to life the trials and triumphs of the soldiers, their camaraderie, and the harrowing battles they face. From the initial enlistment to the final days of the war, this gripping account offers a unique perspective on the sacrifices made by these brave men and the impact of war on their lives.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Resilience
