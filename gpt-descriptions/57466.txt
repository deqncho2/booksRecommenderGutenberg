Synopsis: In 'Jacqueline of the Carrier Pigeons' by Augusta Huiell Seaman, a young girl named Jacqueline discovers a hidden secret in her family's old house that leads her on a thrilling adventure. With the help of carrier pigeons, she unravels a mystery that has been buried for years, encountering danger and unexpected allies along the way. As Jacqueline delves deeper into the past, she must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices to uncover the truth and protect those she loves.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Courage, Friendship, Loyalty
