Synopsis: 
'Practical Italian Recipes for American Kitchens: Sold to aid the Families of Italian Soldiers' is a delightful cookbook that takes readers on a culinary journey through the flavors of Italy. Written by the talented Julia Lovejoy Cuniberti, this book offers a collection of authentic Italian recipes adapted for American kitchens. From mouthwatering pasta dishes to delectable desserts, each recipe is carefully crafted to bring the taste of Italy into the homes of American families. As readers explore the pages, they will not only discover the secrets behind traditional Italian cooking but also learn about the noble cause behind the book's creation. 

Genres: Cookbook, Non-fiction, Cultural

Themes: Italian cuisine, Family, Philanthropy
