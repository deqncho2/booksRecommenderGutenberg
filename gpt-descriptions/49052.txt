Synopsis: In Stephen H. Branch's Alligator, Vol. 1 no. 08, June 12, 1858, a mysterious event unfolds in a small town, leaving its residents bewildered and on edge. As the townspeople grapple with the unexplained phenomenon, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human nature is revealed. With a gripping narrative and a cast of intriguing characters, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Human nature, Small-town dynamics
