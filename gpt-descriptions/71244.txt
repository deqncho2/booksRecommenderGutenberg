Synopsis: In 'Nelly: $b or, The best inheritance,' Lucy Ellen Guernsey takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Nelly, a young woman who unexpectedly inherits a vast fortune. As Nelly navigates the complexities of newfound wealth, she must confront the challenges and temptations that come with it. With her heart torn between love and the allure of material possessions, Nelly's choices will shape her future and reveal the true meaning of happiness.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Wealth and its impact on relationships, Love versus materialism, Self-discovery and personal growth
