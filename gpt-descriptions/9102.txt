Synopsis: 
In 'Run to Earth: A Novel' by M. E. Braddon, a mysterious young woman named Amelius is found wandering the countryside, with no memory of her past or identity. Taken in by a kind-hearted doctor and his family, Amelius begins to rebuild her life, but her past soon catches up with her in unexpected ways. As secrets unravel and hidden connections are revealed, Amelius finds herself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and danger. With each twist and turn, the reader is left wondering who Amelius really is and what secrets lie buried in her past.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Memory, Deception, Redemption
