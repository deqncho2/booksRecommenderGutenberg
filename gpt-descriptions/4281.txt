Synopsis: 
In 'Helen's Babies' by John Habberton, the lives of two bachelors are turned upside down when they are unexpectedly entrusted with the care of two mischievous and precocious children. As they navigate the challenges of parenthood, hilarity ensues, and they find themselves in a series of comical and chaotic situations. From disastrous outings to unconventional parenting methods, the bachelors' lives are forever changed by the presence of these adorable yet troublesome little ones.

Genres: Humor, Fiction, Family

Themes: Parenthood, Responsibility, Friendship
