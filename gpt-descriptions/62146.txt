Synopsis: In "The Janitor's Boy, and Other Poems" by Nathalia Crane, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of poems that explore the hidden depths of everyday life. From the perspective of a young boy, the poems delve into the joys, struggles, and unexpected moments of his world, offering a unique and refreshing perspective on the ordinary. With vivid imagery and a touch of whimsy, Crane's poems invite readers to see the beauty and complexity that can be found in the most unexpected places.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: Childhood, Perspective, Finding Beauty in the Ordinary
