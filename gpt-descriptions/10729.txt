Synopsis: In 'Jack's Ward; Or, The Boy Guardian' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Jack, a young orphan who finds himself under the care of a wealthy guardian. However, as Jack navigates his new life, he soon discovers that his guardian's intentions may not be as benevolent as they initially seemed. With determination and resourcefulness, Jack must uncover the truth and find a way to secure his own future.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Orphanhood, Trust, Self-reliance
