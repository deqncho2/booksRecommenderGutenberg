Synopsis: 

'Gems Gathered in Haste: A New Year's Gift for Sunday Schools' is a captivating collection of stories that will transport readers into a world of wonder and inspiration. From tales of courage and resilience to heartwarming anecdotes of kindness and compassion, this anonymous work offers a treasure trove of captivating narratives that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. Each story is carefully crafted to touch the hearts of both young and old, reminding us of the power of faith, hope, and love. 

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Inspirational

Themes: Faith, Hope, Love, Courage, Kindness
