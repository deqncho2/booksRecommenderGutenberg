Synopsis: 

In 'Bombers' Training, and Application of Same in Trench Warfare' by Ferris, J. R., Lieutenant, readers are taken on a gripping journey into the world of trench warfare during a time of intense conflict. The author delves into the intricate training and application of bombers, individuals who possess a unique skill set that can turn the tide of battle. As the story unfolds, readers are introduced to a diverse group of characters who must navigate the harsh realities of war while honing their abilities as bombers. With each page, the tension builds, and the stakes become higher, as the fate of their comrades and the success of their missions hang in the balance. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Action

Themes: Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Survival
