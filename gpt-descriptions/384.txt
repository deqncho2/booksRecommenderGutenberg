Synopsis: In 'The Lost Prince' by Frances Hodgson Burnett, a young boy named Marco lives a seemingly ordinary life in London, until he encounters a mysterious and enigmatic fellow named The Rat. Together, they embark on a thrilling adventure that takes them through the bustling streets of the city, uncovering secrets and hidden truths that will change their lives forever. As Marco discovers his own hidden potential, he must navigate a world filled with danger, loyalty, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Power of Imagination
