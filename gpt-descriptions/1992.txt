Synopsis: 

In "Travels in England during the reign of Queen Elizabeth; with Fragmenta regalia: or, Observations on Queen Elizabeth, her times and favourites," Hentzner and Naunton take readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and intriguing world of Queen Elizabeth I's reign. Through their vivid descriptions and keen observations, they provide a unique glimpse into the life and times of one of England's most iconic monarchs. From the bustling streets of London to the opulent court of the Queen, the authors paint a vivid picture of the political, social, and cultural landscape of Elizabethan England. As they delve into the lives of the Queen's favorites and the inner workings of her court, readers are transported to a time of power, intrigue, and grandeur. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Biography

Themes: Elizabethan England, Monarchy, Court life, Politics, Society
