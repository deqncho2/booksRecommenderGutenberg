Synopsis: In "The Step on the Stair" by Anna Katharine Green, a young woman named Rosamund is haunted by a mysterious sound she hears every night on the stair of her family's ancestral home. Determined to uncover the truth behind this eerie occurrence, Rosamund embarks on a thrilling journey that leads her to uncover dark family secrets and confront her own fears. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she becomes entangled in a web of deception, betrayal, and danger, where nothing is as it seems. Will Rosamund be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Fear and paranoia, Deception and betrayal
