Synopsis: In "Salome's Burden: $b or, The Shadow on the Homes," Eleanora H. Stooke weaves a captivating tale that follows the life of Salome, a young woman burdened by a mysterious shadow that haunts her family's home. As Salome delves deeper into the secrets of her ancestors, she uncovers a web of dark secrets and hidden truths that threaten to unravel her world. With each revelation, Salome must confront her own fears and make choices that will determine the fate of her family and the legacy they carry.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction
