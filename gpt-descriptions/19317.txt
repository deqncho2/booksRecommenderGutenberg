Synopsis: 
In 'Gallipoli Diary, Volume 1', Ian Hamilton takes us on a gripping journey through the harrowing events of the Gallipoli campaign during World War I. Through his vivid and personal diary entries, we witness the intense battles, the camaraderie among soldiers, and the devastating losses that shaped this historic conflict. As Hamilton navigates the treacherous terrain and faces the horrors of war, his diary becomes a testament to the resilience and courage of those who fought in this brutal campaign.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War memoir

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Resilience, Camaraderie
