Synopsis: 

In "Edinburgh Sketches & Memories," David Masson takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting city of Edinburgh. Through a series of vivid sketches and personal memories, Masson paints a rich and immersive portrait of the city's history, culture, and people. From the bustling streets of the Old Town to the serene beauty of Arthur's Seat, Masson's evocative descriptions bring Edinburgh to life, capturing its unique atmosphere and charm. As he delves into the city's past, Masson uncovers fascinating tales of famous figures, literary legends, and historical events that have shaped Edinburgh into the captivating place it is today. 

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, History

Themes: City life, Scottish culture, Historical significance
