Synopsis: 

In "Human Bullets: A Soldier's Story of Port Arthur," Sakurai, Tadayoshi takes us on a gripping journey through the harrowing events of the Battle of Port Arthur during the Russo-Japanese War. Through the eyes of a young soldier, we witness the brutal realities of war, the unyielding determination of the Japanese troops, and the sacrifices made in the pursuit of victory. As the siege intensifies and the soldiers face unimaginable challenges, the story unfolds with heart-pounding action and emotional depth, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Action

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Resilience, Camaraderie
