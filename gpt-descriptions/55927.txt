Synopsis: In 'Derelicts' by William John Locke, a mysterious letter sets off a chain of events that leads a young man on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected encounters. As he delves into the secrets of his family's past, he uncovers a web of deceit, love, and betrayal that spans generations. With each revelation, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. As the stakes rise, he must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity
