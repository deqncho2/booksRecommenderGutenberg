Synopsis: In "Ralph of the Roundhouse; Or, Bound to Become a Railroad Man," we follow the thrilling journey of Ralph, a young and ambitious protagonist who dreams of becoming a railroad man. From his humble beginnings as a telegraph operator to his daring adventures on the tracks, Ralph's determination and courage are put to the test as he faces numerous challenges and unexpected twists. As he navigates the world of railroads, encounters with dangerous criminals, and even a love interest, Ralph's journey becomes a captivating tale of ambition, perseverance, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Pursuit of Dreams
