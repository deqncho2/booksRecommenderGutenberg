Synopsis: In 'Danger at the Drawbridge' by Mildred A. Wirt, a young and adventurous girl named Penny Parker finds herself caught up in a thrilling mystery when she stumbles upon a suspicious incident at a drawbridge. As she delves deeper into the case, Penny uncovers a web of secrets and danger that puts her own life at risk. With her quick thinking and determination, she must unravel the truth before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Justice
