Synopsis: In "The Ordeal of Richard Feverel," we follow the tumultuous journey of Richard Feverel, a young man raised under the strict principles of his father, Sir Austin Feverel. As Richard navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations, he finds himself torn between his father's teachings and his own desires. With a cast of intriguing characters and a series of unexpected events, this novel explores the consequences of rigid upbringing and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Bildungsroman, Romance, Satire

Themes: Coming of age, Love and marriage, Parental influence, Society and its expectations
