Synopsis: In "A Little Girl in Old Quebec" by Amanda M. Douglas, readers are transported to the enchanting world of 19th-century Quebec through the eyes of a young girl named Jeanne. As Jeanne navigates the bustling streets, encounters intriguing characters, and unravels family secrets, she discovers the power of friendship, the importance of heritage, and the beauty of embracing one's true self. This heartwarming tale takes readers on a captivating journey through history, culture, and the timeless bonds that shape our lives.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: Heritage, Identity, Friendship, Secrets
