Synopsis: In 'That Awful Letter: A Comedy for Girls' by Edna I. MacKenzie, a young girl named Emily receives a mysterious letter that turns her world upside down. As she embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the letter, she finds herself caught in a web of secrets, misunderstandings, and unexpected friendships. With humor and wit, this delightful comedy takes readers on a rollercoaster of emotions as Emily navigates the challenges of adolescence and discovers the power of forgiveness and self-discovery.

Genres: Comedy, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Forgiveness, Self-discovery
