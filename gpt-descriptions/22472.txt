Synopsis: In 'The Book of the Damned', Charles Fort delves into a collection of bizarre and unexplained phenomena that have been dismissed or ignored by mainstream science. From strange rains of frogs and fish to mysterious disappearances and inexplicable celestial events, Fort presents a captivating exploration of the unknown. As he challenges the boundaries of scientific knowledge, he invites readers to question the limitations of human understanding and embrace the mysteries that lie beyond.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Paranormal

Themes: Unexplained phenomena, Scientific skepticism, Human limitations, Mystery
