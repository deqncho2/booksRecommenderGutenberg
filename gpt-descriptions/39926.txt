Synopsis: In "Puffing Billy" and the Prize "Rocket": or, the story of the Stephensons and our Railways, Helen C. Knight takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of the Stephensons and the birth of railways. This historical narrative follows the lives of George and Robert Stephenson, two brilliant engineers who revolutionized transportation with their groundbreaking inventions. From their humble beginnings to their triumphs and challenges, the story unfolds with vivid descriptions of their inventions, the fierce competition they faced, and the impact their work had on society. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be captivated by the thrilling race between the "Puffing Billy" and the "Prize Rocket," which ultimately shaped the future of railways.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Innovation, Competition, Industrial Revolution, Progress
