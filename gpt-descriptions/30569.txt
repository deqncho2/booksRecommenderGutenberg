Synopsis: In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 98, 19 April 1890', readers are transported to the vibrant and satirical world of Victorian London. This edition of the renowned magazine presents a collection of witty and humorous articles, cartoons, and social commentaries that offer a delightful glimpse into the absurdities and eccentricities of the era. From political satire to comical observations on everyday life, this volume promises to entertain and amuse readers with its clever insights and cleverly crafted humor.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Social Commentary

Themes: Victorian society, Politics, Everyday life
