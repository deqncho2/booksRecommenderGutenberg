Synopsis: In 'The Secret of Life, Death and Immortality', author Henry Fleetwood presents a thought-provoking proposition that challenges conventional beliefs about life, death, and the possibility of immortality. With a chapter dedicated to mental therapeutics and detailed instructions for self-healing, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the power of the mind and its potential to transform our lives. As readers delve into the pages, they will be captivated by the author's unique perspective and find themselves questioning long-held assumptions about the nature of existence.

Genres: Philosophy, Self-help, Metaphysics

Themes: Life and death, Immortality, Mind-body connection, Self-transformation
