Synopsis: In 'Shenac's Work at Home', Margaret M. Robertson takes readers on a captivating journey into the life of Shenac, a young woman who finds herself unexpectedly working from home. As she navigates the challenges and joys of this new lifestyle, Shenac discovers hidden talents, unexpected friendships, and a sense of purpose she never thought possible. With a touch of mystery and a dash of romance, this heartwarming tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what lies ahead for Shenac and her unconventional work-at-home adventure.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Adaptation to change, Finding purpose in unexpected places
