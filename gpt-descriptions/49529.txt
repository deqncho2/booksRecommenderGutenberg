Synopsis: In 'General Nelson's Scout' by Byron A. Dunn, a young scout named Nelson finds himself caught in the midst of the Civil War, torn between his loyalty to his country and his desire for justice. As he embarks on a dangerous mission behind enemy lines, Nelson must navigate treacherous landscapes, face unexpected challenges, and confront the moral complexities of war. With each step, he uncovers secrets that could change the course of history, leading him to question everything he thought he knew. This gripping tale of courage, sacrifice, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Justice, Morality, Courage, Redemption
