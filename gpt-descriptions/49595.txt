Synopsis: 
'When the King Loses His Head, and Other Stories' is a captivating collection of short stories by Leonid Andreyev that takes readers on a journey through a variety of intriguing and thought-provoking narratives. From tales of love and loss to explorations of the human psyche, each story presents a unique and gripping experience. Whether it's a king facing the consequences of his actions or a character grappling with their own inner demons, Andreyev's masterful storytelling keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the next twist and turn.

Genres: 
Fiction, Short Stories, Psychological Thriller

Themes: 
Love, Loss, Consequences, Human Nature
