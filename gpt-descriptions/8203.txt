Synopsis: In 'A Modern Instance' by William Dean Howells, a young couple, Bartley and Marcia, embark on a seemingly idyllic marriage. However, as they navigate the challenges of societal expectations, personal ambitions, and the complexities of love, their relationship becomes a battleground of conflicting desires and hidden secrets. Set against the backdrop of late 19th-century New England, this gripping novel explores the delicate balance between tradition and progress, the consequences of personal choices, and the enduring power of human emotions.

Genres: Realistic fiction, Romance, Social commentary

Themes: Marriage and its complexities, societal expectations, personal ambition, love and desire, tradition versus progress
