Synopsis: 
'Visual Signaling' by United States. Army. Signal Corps is a captivating guide that unveils the fascinating world of visual communication used by the military. Through vivid descriptions and illustrations, this book explores the intricate techniques and codes employed by soldiers to convey messages across vast distances. From flag signals to semaphore and heliograph systems, readers will be enthralled by the ingenuity and precision of these visual signaling methods. Whether you're a history enthusiast or simply curious about the hidden language of the armed forces, this book offers a captivating journey into the world of visual signaling.

Genres: Military History, Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Communication, Military Tactics, Innovation
