Synopsis: In 'Earth Is Missing!' by Selwyn Carl, a renowned astrophysicist makes a shocking discovery that sends the world into a frenzy. As the title suggests, Earth mysteriously vanishes from its orbit, leaving humanity in a state of panic and confusion. With the fate of the planet hanging in the balance, a team of brilliant scientists and unlikely heroes embark on a thrilling journey across the universe to uncover the truth behind Earth's disappearance. Along the way, they encounter mind-bending phenomena, encounter extraterrestrial civilizations, and face unimaginable challenges that test their courage and resilience. Will they be able to solve the cosmic puzzle and bring Earth back home?

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Existentialism, Exploration, Survival
