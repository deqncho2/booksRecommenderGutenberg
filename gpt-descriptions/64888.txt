Synopsis: 
In Trust: The Story of a Lady and Her Lover by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret) is a captivating tale that follows the life of Lady Jane, a woman of high society who finds herself entangled in a forbidden love affair. Set in the Victorian era, this novel explores the complexities of passion, duty, and the consequences of societal expectations. As Lady Jane navigates the treacherous waters of love and loyalty, she must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres: 
Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: 
Forbidden love, Duty and responsibility, Societal expectations
