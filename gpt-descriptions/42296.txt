Synopsis: In 'The Duchess of Trajetto' by Manning, Anne, a young woman named Juliette finds herself thrust into a world of political intrigue and forbidden love when she is forced into a marriage of convenience with a powerful duke. As she navigates the treacherous waters of courtly life, Juliette must learn to navigate the delicate balance between duty and desire, all while uncovering the secrets that threaten to tear her world apart.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and duty, power and politics, secrets and betrayal
