Synopsis: 

In "Spanish John: Being a Memoir," readers are transported to the early life and thrilling adventures of Colonel John McDonell, also known as "Spanish John." The memoir, now published in complete form, delves into the captivating experiences of McDonell when he served as a lieutenant in the Company of St. James of the Regiment Irlandia, in the service of the King of Spain operating in Italy. 

As the narrative unfolds, readers are taken on a journey through the tumultuous landscapes of war-torn Italy, where McDonell's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. From daring escapades on the battlefield to encounters with intriguing characters, the memoir offers a vivid portrayal of the challenges and triumphs that shaped McDonell's early life.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: War and Conflict, Identity and Self-Discovery, Loyalty and Honor
