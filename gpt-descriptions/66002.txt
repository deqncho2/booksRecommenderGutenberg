Synopsis: 
'An Epic of Women, and Other Poems' by Arthur William Edgar O'Shaughnessy is a captivating collection of poems that explores the complexities of women's lives and their profound impact on society. From the enchanting tales of mythical heroines to the poignant reflections on love and loss, O'Shaughnessy weaves together a tapestry of emotions and experiences that will leave readers spellbound. Through his lyrical and evocative verses, the poet delves into the depths of the female spirit, celebrating their strength, resilience, and the transformative power they possess. Each poem is a mesmerizing journey that unveils the multifaceted nature of women, inviting readers to ponder upon the mysteries and wonders of femininity.

Genres: Poetry, Feminist Literature, Mythology

Themes: Women's empowerment, Love and relationships, Mythology and folklore
