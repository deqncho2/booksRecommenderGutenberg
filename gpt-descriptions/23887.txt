Synopsis: 
In 'Strange Stories from History for Young People', George Cary Eggleston takes readers on a captivating journey through time, uncovering fascinating and lesser-known tales from the past. From the mysterious disappearance of a famous explorer to the daring exploits of a cunning spy, each story is filled with intrigue, adventure, and unexpected twists. Whether it's a legendary pirate's hidden treasure or a secret society's ancient rituals, these strange stories will leave young readers eager to learn more about the mysteries of history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Intrigue, Curiosity
