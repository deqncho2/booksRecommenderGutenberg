Synopsis: 

'Nova Scotia's Part in the Great War' takes readers on a captivating journey through the untold stories of Nova Scotians who played a significant role in World War I. From the bustling streets of Halifax to the trenches of Europe, this book unveils the extraordinary sacrifices, heroism, and resilience of the men and women who left their homes to fight for their country. Through personal accounts, historical documents, and vivid descriptions, the author paints a vivid picture of the challenges faced by Nova Scotians during this tumultuous period. As the war unfolds, readers will be drawn into the lives of these brave individuals, experiencing their triumphs, heartaches, and the indomitable spirit that emerged from the ashes of war.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Resilience, Identity, Community
