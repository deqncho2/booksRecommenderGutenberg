Synopsis: In "The Outdoor Girls in the Saddle; Or, The Girl Miner of Gold Run" by Laura Lee Hope, the adventurous Outdoor Girls find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey to the gold mines of Gold Run. As they navigate the rugged terrain and face unexpected challenges, they uncover a mysterious secret that could change everything. With their determination and resourcefulness, the girls must unravel the truth and overcome the obstacles that stand in their way.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
