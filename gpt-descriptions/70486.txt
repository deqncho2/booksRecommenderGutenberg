Synopsis: In the isolated region of the Catskill Mountains, a dark and ancient terror haunts the inhabitants, striking fear into their hearts. As a brave investigator delves into the chilling mysteries surrounding the area, he uncovers a horrifying truth that threatens to consume him and all those who dare to uncover the lurking fear.

Genres: Horror, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Fear, Madness, Supernatural terror, Isolation
