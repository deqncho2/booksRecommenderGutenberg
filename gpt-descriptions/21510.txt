Synopsis: In 'Legacy' by James H. Schmitz, a young woman named Andrea discovers a hidden family secret that thrusts her into a dangerous world of intrigue and adventure. As she unravels the mysteries of her family's past, Andrea must navigate treacherous alliances and confront powerful enemies who will stop at nothing to protect their own interests. With her life on the line, Andrea must decide how far she is willing to go to uncover the truth and protect her family's legacy.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity, Courage
