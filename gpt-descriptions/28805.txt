Synopsis: 
In 'Dorothy's House Party' by Raymond, Evelyn, a seemingly ordinary house party takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious guest arrives, bringing with them a series of strange occurrences and unsettling secrets. As the night unfolds, Dorothy and her friends find themselves caught in a web of suspense and intrigue, where nothing is as it seems. With each passing hour, the tension rises, and the guests must confront their own fears and desires in order to survive the night.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Secrets, Identity
