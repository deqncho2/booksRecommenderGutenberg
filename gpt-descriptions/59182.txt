Synopsis: In 'Top o' the World: A Once Upon a Time Tale' by Swan, Mark, a young woman named Emma finds herself transported to a magical world where fairy tales come to life. As she navigates through this enchanting realm, she discovers that her own destiny is intertwined with the fate of the beloved characters she grew up reading about. With danger lurking at every turn, Emma must uncover the truth about her past and embrace her true identity to save both herself and the fairy tale world from an impending darkness.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Good vs. Evil
