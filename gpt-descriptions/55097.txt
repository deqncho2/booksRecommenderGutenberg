Synopsis: In 'A Book of Nimble Beasts: Bunny Rabbit, Squirrel, Toad, and "Those Sort of People"', English, Douglas takes readers on a whimsical journey through the lives of four fascinating creatures. From the mischievous Bunny Rabbit to the wise and observant Toad, each character brings their unique perspective to the world around them. As they encounter "Those Sort of People," the story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers captivated by the charm and wit of these nimble beasts.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Acceptance, Curiosity, Self-discovery
