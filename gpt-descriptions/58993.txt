Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of John Burroughs' is a comprehensive collection of the works of John Burroughs, a renowned American naturalist and essayist. This index provides readers with easy access to Burroughs' captivating writings, which explore the beauty of nature, the wonders of the animal kingdom, and the profound connections between humans and the natural world. From his observations of birds and flowers to his reflections on the importance of solitude and the simplicity of life, Burroughs' works offer a delightful journey into the wonders of the natural environment.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Essays

Themes: Nature, Wildlife, Environmentalism, Solitude, Simplicity
