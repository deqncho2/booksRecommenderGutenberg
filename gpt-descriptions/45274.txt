Synopsis: In 'Ye Book of Copperheads', the reader is transported to the tumultuous era of the American Civil War, where a secret society known as the Copperheads threatens to undermine the Union's efforts. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, a young journalist finds himself entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger, uncovering a plot that could change the course of history. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping historical thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Conspiracy, Patriotism
