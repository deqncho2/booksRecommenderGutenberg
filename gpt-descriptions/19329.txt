Synopsis: 
"The Sunny Side of Ireland: How to see it by the Great Southern and Western Railway" takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Ireland. Written by John O'Mahony, this travel guide offers a unique perspective on exploring the sunny side of the country, showcasing the hidden gems and breathtaking sights that can be discovered along the Great Southern and Western Railway. From charming villages to majestic castles, readers will be enchanted by the beauty and rich history of Ireland as they follow O'Mahony's expert advice on the best routes and attractions to visit. With vivid descriptions and helpful tips, this book is a must-read for anyone seeking to experience the sunny side of Ireland.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Beauty of Nature, Cultural Heritage
