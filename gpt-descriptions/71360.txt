Synopsis: In "Nobody's Fault" by Netta Syrett, a young woman named Joan finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and independence, Joan must confront the consequences of her choices and the limitations placed upon her by a rigid society. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century England, this captivating novel explores the struggles and sacrifices of a woman determined to forge her own path.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, coming-of-age

Themes: Gender roles, societal expectations, self-discovery, love and marriage
