Synopsis: 
In 'The Come Back' by Carolyn Wells, a renowned actress mysteriously disappears from the stage during a performance, leaving the audience bewildered and the theater in chaos. Years later, a young woman with an uncanny resemblance to the missing actress emerges, claiming to be her long-lost daughter. As she delves into the secrets of her past, she becomes entangled in a web of deception, betrayal, and unexpected twists that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Family Secrets
