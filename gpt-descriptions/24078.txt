Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Acadian Louisiana, a young man named Bonaventure finds himself torn between his love for a beautiful Creole girl and his duty to his family. As he navigates the complexities of love, honor, and tradition, Bonaventure becomes entangled in a web of secrets and betrayals that threaten to shatter his world. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant and diverse Acadian culture, this captivating prose pastoral takes readers on a journey of passion, resilience, and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Southern Literature

Themes: Love and Desire, Family and Duty, Cultural Identity, Betrayal and Secrets
