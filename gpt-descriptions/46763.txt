Synopsis: In "Michael Faraday, Man of Science" by Walter Jerrold, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of history's most brilliant minds. From his humble beginnings as a bookbinder's apprentice to his groundbreaking discoveries in the field of electromagnetism, Faraday's story is one of determination, curiosity, and relentless pursuit of knowledge. Delve into the world of scientific exploration as you witness Faraday's experiments, his encounters with influential figures of his time, and the profound impact he had on the world of science. This biography offers a fascinating glimpse into the life of a true scientific genius, leaving readers inspired by Faraday's unwavering passion for discovery.

Genres: Biography, Science, History

Themes: Curiosity, Determination, Scientific Exploration
