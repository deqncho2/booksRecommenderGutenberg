Synopsis: 
'A Simple Story' by Mrs. Inchbald is a captivating novel that follows the lives of two individuals, Miss Milner and Dorriforth, as they navigate the complexities of love, duty, and societal expectations. Miss Milner, a spirited and independent young woman, finds herself torn between her desires and the constraints placed upon her by society. Dorriforth, a noble and honorable man, is faced with the challenge of reconciling his duty to his family with his own personal happiness. As their paths intertwine, their choices and actions have far-reaching consequences that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Bildungsroman

Themes: 
Love and passion, Duty and responsibility, Social expectations
