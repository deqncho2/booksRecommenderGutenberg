Synopsis: 

In "Liége on the Line of March: An American Girl's Experiences When the Germans Came Through Belgium," Glenna Lindsley Bigelow takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events of World War I. Set in the Belgian city of Liége, the story follows the experiences of an American girl as she witnesses the arrival of the German army and the subsequent impact on the local population. Through her eyes, we gain a unique perspective on the horrors of war, the resilience of the human spirit, and the power of compassion in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War and its consequences, Resilience, Compassion
