Synopsis: 
Ramsey Milholland, a young and ambitious lawyer, finds himself torn between his desire for success and his growing disillusionment with the corrupt world of politics and business. As he navigates the treacherous waters of power and influence, Ramsey must confront his own moral compass and make difficult choices that will shape his future. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century America, this gripping novel explores the complexities of ambition, integrity, and the pursuit of the American Dream.

Genres: 
Drama, Historical Fiction, Political Fiction

Themes: 
Ambition, Corruption, Morality, Integrity, American Dream
