Synopsis: In "The Young Continentals at Bunker Hill" by John T. McIntyre, a group of young patriots find themselves caught up in the intense and pivotal battle of Bunker Hill during the American Revolution. As they navigate the chaos and danger of war, they must confront their own fears, forge unlikely alliances, and discover the true meaning of courage. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this historical adventure will transport readers back in time to witness the bravery and sacrifice of those who fought for freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Friendship, Sacrifice
