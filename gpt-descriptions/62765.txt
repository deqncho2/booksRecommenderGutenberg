Synopsis: In 'The Star Guardsman' by Albert dePina, a young soldier finds himself thrust into a dangerous intergalactic conflict when he is chosen to join an elite group of warriors known as the Star Guardsmen. As he navigates through treacherous battles and encounters strange alien species, he must confront his own fears and discover the true meaning of courage. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this thrilling science fiction adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Action, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Identity, Interstellar Conflict
