Synopsis: 
'Ancient Ballads and Legends of Hindustan' by Toru Dutt is a captivating collection of enchanting tales and mythical stories from the rich cultural heritage of India. Through vivid and poetic storytelling, Dutt transports readers to a world of ancient kingdoms, valiant warriors, and mystical creatures. From the tragic love story of a prince and a celestial nymph to the epic battles fought by legendary heroes, each ballad and legend unveils the beauty, complexity, and timeless wisdom of Indian folklore. With its lyrical prose and evocative imagery, this collection offers a mesmerizing journey into the heart of Hindustan's mythical past.

Genres: Poetry, Folklore, Mythology
Themes: Love and Romance, Heroism, Fate and Destiny, Cultural Identity
