Synopsis: 

In "Old-Time Makers of Medicine: The Story of The Students And Teachers of the Sciences Related to Medicine During the Middle Ages," James J. Walsh takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of medicine during the Middle Ages. From the renowned scholars and their groundbreaking discoveries to the challenges they faced in a time of limited resources and superstitions, this book unveils the untold stories of the pioneers who shaped the field of medicine as we know it today. With vivid descriptions and engaging narratives, Walsh brings to life the struggles and triumphs of these old-time makers of medicine, offering a unique perspective on the evolution of medical knowledge and practice.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Biography

Themes: Medical history, Scientific progress, Intellectual curiosity, Human resilience
