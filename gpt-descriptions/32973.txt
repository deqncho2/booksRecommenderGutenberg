Synopsis: In 'Elements of Plane Trigonometry', Hugh Blackburn takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of mathematics. This comprehensive guide is specifically designed for the junior class of mathematics at the University of Glasgow, offering a clear and concise exploration of plane trigonometry. With a focus on practical applications and real-world examples, Blackburn's work not only equips students with the necessary tools to solve complex trigonometric problems, but also instills a sense of wonder and curiosity about the beauty of mathematics.

Genres: Educational, Mathematics, Reference

Themes: Mathematical principles, Problem-solving, Practical applications
