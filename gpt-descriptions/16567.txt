Synopsis: In 'Aunt Jane's Nieces in the Red Cross', three young and spirited nieces find themselves embarking on a journey of compassion and bravery as they join the Red Cross during World War I. As they navigate the challenges of war-torn Europe, they discover the true meaning of sacrifice, resilience, and the power of unity. With their unwavering determination, the girls become beacons of hope in a time of darkness, proving that even in the face of adversity, love and compassion can prevail.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: War, Compassion, Bravery, Unity
