Synopsis: In "A Husband for My Wife" by William W. Stuart, a seemingly perfect marriage takes an unexpected turn when a wife decides to play matchmaker for her husband. As she sets out on a mission to find him a new partner, she discovers secrets and desires that challenge the very foundation of their relationship. With twists and turns that keep the reader guessing, this captivating story explores the complexities of love, trust, and the lengths we go to find happiness.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Marriage, Identity, Self-discovery
