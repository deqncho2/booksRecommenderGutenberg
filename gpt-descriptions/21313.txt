Synopsis: In Honour's Cause: A Tale of the Days of George the First is a thrilling historical adventure set in the early 18th century. The story follows the young protagonist, who finds himself caught up in a dangerous plot that could change the course of history. As he navigates through treacherous political intrigues, secret societies, and unexpected alliances, he must make difficult choices to protect his honor and the ones he loves. With its gripping narrative and vivid portrayal of the era, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Honor, Loyalty, Betrayal, Political Intrigue
