Synopsis: In "Her Royal Highness: A Romance of the Chancelleries of Europe," Le Queux takes readers on a thrilling journey through the intricate world of European politics and royal intrigue. When an unexpected turn of events leads to a young woman ascending to the throne, she must navigate the treacherous waters of power, love, and betrayal. As she grapples with her newfound responsibilities, she discovers shocking secrets that could change the course of history. With danger lurking at every corner, she must rely on her wit and courage to protect herself and her kingdom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Romance

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Betrayal, Identity and Responsibility
