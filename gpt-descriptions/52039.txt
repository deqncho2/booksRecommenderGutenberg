Synopsis: In 'The Jesuits' by Edward Hoare, a young and ambitious journalist, John, stumbles upon a mysterious secret society that has been operating in the shadows for centuries. As he delves deeper into their world, he uncovers a web of intrigue, power struggles, and hidden agendas. With his life on the line, John must navigate a treacherous path, balancing his desire for the truth with the dangers that come with exposing the secrets of the Jesuits.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Conspiracy, Power, Religion, Loyalty
