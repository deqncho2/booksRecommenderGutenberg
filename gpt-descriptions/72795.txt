Synopsis: In 'Weapon' by Jesse F. Bone, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of warfare forever. As he races against time to protect his invention from falling into the wrong hands, he finds himself caught in a web of intrigue, danger, and betrayal. With the fate of nations hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous landscape where trust is scarce and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. This gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of power and the lengths people will go to obtain it.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Suspense

Themes: Power, Betrayal, Ethics, Technological Advancement
