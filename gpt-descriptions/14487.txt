Synopsis: In 'The Lion's Share' by Arnold Bennett, a young and ambitious artist named Henry Leek embarks on a journey to achieve fame and fortune in the competitive world of art. As he navigates the challenges of the art scene, he encounters unexpected obstacles, complex relationships, and a moral dilemma that could jeopardize everything he has worked for. With his talent and determination put to the test, Henry must make difficult choices that will shape his future and reveal the true nature of success.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Artistic integrity, Morality, Success, Relationships
