Synopsis: 

In "Umbrellas and Their History" by William Sangster, embark on a fascinating journey through time as the author unravels the captivating story behind one of the most ubiquitous objects in our daily lives. From its humble beginnings as a simple shelter from the rain to its evolution into a symbol of elegance and status, this book delves into the intriguing history of umbrellas, revealing surprising anecdotes and little-known facts along the way. With vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, Sangster takes readers on a delightful exploration of the cultural, social, and technological aspects that have shaped the umbrella's evolution throughout the centuries.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Evolution of technology, Cultural symbolism, Everyday objects
