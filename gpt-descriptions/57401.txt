Synopsis: In 'The King's Scapegoat' by Drummond Hamilton, a young peasant named William finds himself thrust into a dangerous game of political intrigue when he is chosen as the scapegoat for a crime he did not commit. As he navigates the treacherous world of the royal court, William must uncover the truth behind the conspiracy and prove his innocence before it's too late. With unexpected allies and formidable enemies, this thrilling tale of deception and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Power and corruption, Identity and self-discovery
