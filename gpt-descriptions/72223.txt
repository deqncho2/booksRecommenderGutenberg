Synopsis:
When Lolly Willowes, a single woman in her forties, decides to break free from societal expectations and move to the countryside, she discovers a world filled with unexpected enchantments and secrets. As she embraces her newfound independence, Lolly finds herself drawn to a mysterious figure known as the loving huntsman. But as their connection deepens, Lolly begins to question the true nature of her desires and the price she may have to pay for her freedom.

Genres:
Fiction, Fantasy, Feminist Literature

Themes:
Female empowerment, societal expectations, freedom, self-discovery
