Synopsis: In 'The Executor: Blackwood's Edinburgh Magazine vol. LXXXIX' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman finds herself unexpectedly thrust into the role of executor for a mysterious and wealthy man. As she delves into his affairs, she uncovers a web of secrets and intrigue that threatens to unravel her own life. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and deception, where nothing is as it seems. Will she be able to navigate the treacherous path laid before her, or will she become just another pawn in this deadly game?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Power, Deception, Secrets, Identity
