Synopsis: 

'Fishing Grounds of the Gulf of Maine' takes readers on an immersive journey through the captivating world of commercial fishing in the Gulf of Maine. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, author Walter H. Rich delves into the challenges, triumphs, and mysteries that surround this unique industry. From the treacherous waters to the camaraderie among fishermen, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a way of life that is both exhilarating and perilous. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Maritime, Adventure

Themes: Fishing, Maritime culture, Survival
