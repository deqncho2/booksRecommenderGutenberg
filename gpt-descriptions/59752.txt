Synopsis: In 'The Moon Maid' by Edgar Rice Burroughs, a thrilling adventure unfolds as Julian, a young American soldier, finds himself transported to a future Earth where the Moon has been colonized by a mysterious race. As he navigates this strange new world, Julian becomes entangled in a dangerous love triangle and must confront the enigmatic Moon Men who threaten the existence of humanity. Filled with action, romance, and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Love and Desire, Survival
