Synopsis: 
In 'Flora' by A. L. O. E., a young girl named Flora finds herself caught in a web of secrets and mysteries when she is sent to live with her distant relatives in a secluded mansion. As she explores the eerie surroundings, Flora uncovers hidden rooms, cryptic messages, and encounters strange occurrences that challenge her perception of reality. With the help of an enigmatic young boy, Flora embarks on a thrilling journey to unravel the truth behind the mansion's dark past and the secrets that lie within its walls.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Perception and reality, Coming of age
