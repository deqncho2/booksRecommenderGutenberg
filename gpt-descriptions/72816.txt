Synopsis: In "The Case with Nine Solutions" by Connington, J. J., a renowned detective is faced with a perplexing mystery when a wealthy man is found dead in his locked study. As the investigation unfolds, the detective discovers a series of baffling clues that seem to point in multiple directions, leading to nine possible solutions. With each solution presenting a different motive and suspect, the detective must navigate through a web of deception and misdirection to uncover the truth behind the murder.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Misdirection, Multiple Perspectives
