Synopsis: A charming and audacious gentleman, A.J. Raffles, leads a double life as a respected member of society by day and a skilled thief by night. With his loyal accomplice, Bunny Manders, they embark on a series of daring heists that keep the police and high society on their toes. But as their exploits become increasingly risky, Raffles finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse that could jeopardize everything he holds dear.

Genres: Crime fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Morality, Loyalty
