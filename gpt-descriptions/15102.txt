Synopsis: In 'Proposals for Building, in Every County, a Working-Alms-House or Hospital: as the Best Expedient to Perfect the Trade and Manufactory of Linnen Cloth', Richard Haines presents a groundbreaking idea that could revolutionize the textile industry. Through his innovative proposals, Haines aims to enhance the production of linen cloth by establishing working-alms-houses or hospitals in every county. By delving into the intricacies of this concept, Haines offers a fresh perspective on how to improve the trade and manufactory of linen cloth, captivating readers with his visionary ideas.

Genres: Non-fiction, Economics, Social Commentary

Themes: Industrial development, Social welfare, Economic progress
