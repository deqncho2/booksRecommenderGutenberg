Synopsis: In the heart of the majestic Jackson Hole, a group of friends gather around a crackling campfire to share spine-chilling tales that blur the line between reality and the supernatural. As the night unfolds, these captivating stories weave together a tapestry of mystery, suspense, and unexpected twists, leaving the listeners questioning what is real and what is merely a figment of their imagination. From haunted cabins to ancient legends, each tale takes the reader on a thrilling journey through the dark corners of Jackson Hole's history, where secrets and eerie encounters lurk in the shadows.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Legends and folklore, the power of storytelling, the thin line between reality and the supernatural
