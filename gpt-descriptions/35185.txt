Synopsis: 
'The Buddha's Path of Virtue: A Translation of the Dhammapada' takes readers on a profound journey through the teachings of the Buddha, offering timeless wisdom and guidance for living a virtuous life. This translation of the Dhammapada, one of the most revered texts in Buddhism, presents the Buddha's teachings in a clear and accessible manner, exploring themes of compassion, mindfulness, and the pursuit of inner peace. Through its verses, the book invites readers to reflect on the nature of existence, the power of self-discipline, and the transformative potential of embracing virtue. 

Genres: Philosophy, Religion, Self-Help

Themes: Virtue, Compassion, Mindfulness, Inner Peace
