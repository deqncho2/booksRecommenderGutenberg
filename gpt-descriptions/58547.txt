Synopsis: In this thought-provoking sermon, preacher J. Church explores the unwavering confidence of believers in a faithful God, while delving into the perplexing triumph of His enemies. Set in the Surrey Tabernacle, Church's sermon takes place on a significant Lord's Day morning, November 23, 1817, at the early hour of seven o'clock. Through his powerful words, Church challenges the reader to consider the unshakeable faith of believers and the mysterious ways in which God's enemies seem to prevail. This sermon is sure to captivate and inspire readers as they reflect on the complexities of faith and the ultimate triumph of God's plan.

Genres: Sermon, Religious Literature

Themes: Faith, God's Faithfulness, Triumph, Spiritual Warfare
