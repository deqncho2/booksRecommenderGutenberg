Synopsis:
In "Maurice Tiernay, Soldier of Fortune," we follow the captivating journey of Maurice Tiernay, a young Irishman who finds himself caught up in the tumultuous events of the French Revolution. From his humble beginnings as a lowly servant, Maurice's life takes an unexpected turn when he is thrust into the world of espionage and adventure. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Maurice must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to survive. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Maurice's loyalties are put to the ultimate test. Will he emerge as a hero or succumb to the perils that surround him?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Espionage

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Survival
