Synopsis: In 'Experimental Determination of the Velocity of Light: Made at the U.S. Naval Academy, Annapolis', Albert A. Michelson takes us on a captivating scientific journey as he sets out to measure the speed of light. With meticulous detail and ingenious experiments, Michelson's groundbreaking research challenges the boundaries of human knowledge and unveils the secrets of one of nature's most fundamental phenomena. As he navigates through the complexities of his experiments, the reader is left in awe of the sheer brilliance and determination of this pioneering scientist.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Scientific inquiry, Exploration of natural phenomena
