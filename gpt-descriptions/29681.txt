Synopsis: 
In "A Mountain Boyhood," Joe Mills takes us on a captivating journey through his childhood in the Appalachian Mountains. From his encounters with wild animals to his adventures in the untamed wilderness, Mills paints a vivid picture of a boyhood filled with both wonder and danger. With his unique storytelling style, he transports readers to a world where nature is both friend and foe, and where the bonds of family and community are tested against the backdrop of a rugged landscape. As Mills navigates the challenges of growing up in the mountains, he learns valuable lessons about resilience, self-reliance, and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Memoir, Adventure, Nature Writing

Themes: Coming of Age, Survival, Connection with Nature
