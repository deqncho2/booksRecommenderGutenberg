Synopsis: In 'Red and White: A Tale of the Wars of the Roses' by Emily Sarah Holt, the reader is transported to the tumultuous period of the Wars of the Roses in 15th century England. Amidst the backdrop of political intrigue, power struggles, and shifting loyalties, the lives of two young protagonists, one from the House of Lancaster and the other from the House of York, become intertwined. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of war and love, they must confront their own personal demons and make choices that will shape their destinies. With vivid historical detail and compelling characters, this gripping tale explores the complexities of loyalty, honor, and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Loyalty, Power Struggles, Love and Sacrifice
