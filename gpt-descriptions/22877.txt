Synopsis:
'Lavengro: The Scholar - The Gypsy - The Priest, Vol. 1' follows the captivating journey of a young scholar named Lavengro, who finds himself entangled in a world of gypsies, priests, and mysterious encounters. As he navigates through various adventures and encounters with intriguing characters, Lavengro's thirst for knowledge and his unconventional approach to life lead him down a path of self-discovery and unexpected revelations. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century England, this enthralling tale explores the complexities of identity, the power of language, and the clash between tradition and individuality.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Bildungsroman

Themes:
Identity, Knowledge and Learning, Cultural Clash
