Synopsis: In 'History of Egypt From 330 B.C. To the Present Time, Volume 10 (of 12)', A. S. Rappoport takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous history of Egypt, from the Hellenistic period to the modern era. This volume delves into the fascinating events and personalities that shaped Egypt's destiny, including the rise and fall of powerful dynasties, the influence of foreign powers, and the struggles for independence. From the grandeur of ancient pharaohs to the political upheavals of the 19th and 20th centuries, this volume offers a comprehensive and enthralling account of Egypt's rich historical tapestry.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and politics, Cultural heritage, National identity
