Synopsis: 
'Bygone Beliefs: Being a Series of Excursions in the Byways of Thought' takes readers on a captivating journey through the forgotten realms of ancient beliefs and superstitions. From exploring the mystical practices of ancient civilizations to delving into the esoteric traditions of forgotten cultures, this book uncovers the fascinating and often bizarre beliefs that have shaped human history. With a blend of historical research and personal anecdotes, Redgrove invites readers to question their own beliefs and ponder the mysteries of the past.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Anthropology

Themes: Belief systems, Superstition, Cultural heritage, Human spirituality
