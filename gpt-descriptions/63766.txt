Synopsis: In 'Man nth' by Fox, Gardner F., a brilliant scientist named Dr. Jonathan Reed makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges the very fabric of reality. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden world of infinite possibilities, where time and space become malleable. However, his newfound power attracts the attention of a shadowy organization that will stop at nothing to control his extraordinary abilities. Driven by a desire to protect his discovery and those he holds dear, Dr. Reed must navigate a treacherous web of intrigue, betrayal, and mind-bending paradoxes. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Power and Control, Ethical Dilemmas
