Synopsis: In 'Atom Drive' by Charles L. Fontenay, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Endlich invents a revolutionary propulsion system that could change the course of space exploration forever. However, his groundbreaking creation attracts the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess this technology. As Dr. Endlich races against time to protect his invention, he finds himself caught in a dangerous web of intrigue, betrayal, and high-stakes adventure that takes him to the far reaches of the galaxy.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Technological advancement, Power struggles, Ethical dilemmas
