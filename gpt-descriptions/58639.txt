Synopsis: In 'Welcome, Martians!' by Evan Hunter, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious spaceship lands in their midst. As the townspeople grapple with their fear and curiosity, they must confront their own prejudices and preconceived notions about the unknown. With the arrival of the Martians, the town's secrets and hidden desires are brought to the surface, leading to unexpected consequences and a thrilling exploration of human nature.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Fear of the unknown, Prejudice, Human nature
