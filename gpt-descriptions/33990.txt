Synopsis: In 'The Red Cross Girls with Pershing to Victory', the brave and determined Red Cross Girls embark on a thrilling journey alongside General Pershing during World War I. As they navigate the dangers of war-torn Europe, they face unexpected challenges, forge unbreakable bonds, and discover the true meaning of courage and sacrifice. With their unwavering dedication to helping others, the Red Cross Girls become an integral part of the war effort, leaving a lasting impact on those they encounter.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Friendship, Humanitarianism
