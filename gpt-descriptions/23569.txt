Synopsis: In 'Christmas Holidays at Merryvale: The Merryvale Boys' by Alice Hale Burnett, a group of adventurous boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey during their Christmas break. As they explore the enchanting Merryvale estate, they stumble upon a hidden secret that leads them on a quest filled with mystery, friendship, and unexpected discoveries. With each twist and turn, the boys uncover clues that challenge their courage and ingenuity, making this holiday season one they will never forget.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Curiosity, Holiday Spirit
