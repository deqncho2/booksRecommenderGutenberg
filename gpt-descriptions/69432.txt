Synopsis: 
In "David Thompson, the explorer" by Charles Norris Cochrane, embark on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of North America alongside the enigmatic explorer, David Thompson. From his humble beginnings as a young apprentice in the fur trade to his remarkable achievements as a cartographer and surveyor, Thompson's life is a testament to the indomitable human spirit and the insatiable thirst for discovery. As he navigates treacherous rivers, encounters indigenous tribes, and battles against the harsh elements, Thompson's unwavering determination and unwavering commitment to his mission will leave readers captivated and inspired. 

Genres: Biography, Adventure, History

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Cultural Encounter
