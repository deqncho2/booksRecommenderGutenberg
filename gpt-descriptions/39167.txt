Synopsis: In 'Fairies Afield' by Molesworth, Mrs., a young girl named Primrose discovers a hidden world of enchantment and adventure when she stumbles upon a group of mischievous fairies. As Primrose becomes entangled in their magical escapades, she must navigate the challenges and dangers that arise, all while trying to keep the existence of fairies a secret. With each encounter, Primrose's understanding of friendship, bravery, and the power of imagination is tested, leading her on a captivating journey that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Bravery, Secrets
