Synopsis: In 'The Sorceress, v. 2 of 3' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), the captivating tale continues as the mysterious sorceress, with her enigmatic powers and dark secrets, weaves her spellbinding influence over the lives of those around her. As her true intentions slowly unravel, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and supernatural forces, leading to unexpected twists and shocking revelations. With each page, the reader is drawn deeper into a world where magic and reality collide, leaving them eagerly anticipating the final installment.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Power of manipulation, Secrets and deception, Love and obsession
