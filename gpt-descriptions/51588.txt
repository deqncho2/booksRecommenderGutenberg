Synopsis: In 'Security Plan' by Farrell, Joseph, a brilliant computer programmer finds himself caught in a web of intrigue when he is unexpectedly recruited by a secretive government agency. As he delves deeper into the world of cybersecurity, he uncovers a dangerous conspiracy that threatens not only his own life but also the security of the entire nation. With his skills put to the ultimate test, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of deception, betrayal, and high-stakes technology to uncover the truth and protect what he holds dear.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Techno-thriller

Themes: Espionage, Technology, Government Conspiracy
