Synopsis: 
"The Spell of Flanders: An Outline of the History, Legends and Art of Belgium's Famous Northern Provinces" takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting region of Flanders. Edward Neville Vose delves into the rich tapestry of Flanders' history, exploring its legends and art that have shaped this renowned northern province. From the medieval cities of Bruges and Ghent to the picturesque countryside dotted with windmills and canals, Vose paints a vivid picture of Flanders' cultural heritage. Discover the allure of Flanders as you immerse yourself in its captivating tales and artistic wonders.

Genres: History, Travel, Art

Themes: Cultural heritage, Legends and folklore, Artistic expression
