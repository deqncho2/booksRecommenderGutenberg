Synopsis: In 'The Cross-Cut' by Courtney Ryley Cooper, a small town is shaken by a mysterious murder that leaves the community in shock. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the residents find themselves questioning their trust in one another. With suspense building at every turn, the truth behind the murder becomes increasingly elusive, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Small-town dynamics
