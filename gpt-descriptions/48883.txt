Synopsis: In 'Sea-gift: A Novel' by Edwin W. Fuller, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure as she sets sail on a whaling ship in the 19th century. As she navigates the treacherous waters of the sea and the complexities of human relationships, Mary's resilience and determination are tested, leading her to uncover hidden truths about herself and the world around her. With vivid descriptions of life at sea and a captivating cast of characters, this novel takes readers on an unforgettable voyage filled with danger, love, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Freedom
