Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Gustave Doré' is a comprehensive collection of the works of the renowned French artist Gustave Doré. This compilation showcases Doré's exceptional talent and includes a wide range of his illustrations, engravings, and paintings. From his iconic depictions of biblical scenes to his imaginative interpretations of classic literature, this index offers a captivating glimpse into the artistic genius of Gustave Doré.

Genres: Art, Illustration, Engraving

Themes: Biblical narratives, Classic literature, Visual storytelling
