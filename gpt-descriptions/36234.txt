Synopsis: In 'The Stolen Heiress; or, The Salamanca Doctor Outplotted', the lives of two young women are turned upside down when they are mistaken for each other. As they navigate a web of mistaken identities, secret plots, and unexpected alliances, they must rely on their wit and resourcefulness to unravel the truth and reclaim their rightful places. This delightful comedy by Susanna Centlivre is filled with hilarious misunderstandings, clever disguises, and a dash of romance, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Romance

Themes: Mistaken identity, Deception, Love and marriage
