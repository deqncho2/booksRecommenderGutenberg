Synopsis: 
In 'Jonah' by Louis Stone, a young man named Jonah finds himself caught in a web of secrets and lies as he navigates the dark underbelly of his city. Drawn into a dangerous world of crime and corruption, Jonah must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will determine his fate. As he delves deeper into the shadows, Jonah discovers shocking truths that will challenge everything he thought he knew about himself and those around him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Identity, Morality, Betrayal
