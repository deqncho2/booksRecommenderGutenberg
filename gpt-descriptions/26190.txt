Synopsis: In 'A Choice of Miracles' by James A. Cox, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Emma Collins discovers a groundbreaking technology that has the potential to change the world. However, as she delves deeper into her research, she uncovers a dark secret that puts her life and the fate of humanity at risk. With time running out, Emma must make a choice that will determine the course of history and challenge her own beliefs.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Morality, Power of Choice
