Synopsis: In "The Story of the Airship (Non-rigid): A Study of One of America's Lesser Known Defense Weapons," author Hugh Allen takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of non-rigid airships. From their humble beginnings to their crucial role in defense, this book unveils the untold story of these lesser-known aerial marvels. With vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, Allen explores the triumphs and challenges faced by the pioneers of airship technology, shedding light on their impact on American defense. As readers delve into the pages of this captivating study, they will discover the remarkable ingenuity and resilience that shaped the destiny of these magnificent flying machines.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Military

Themes: Technological innovation, Military strategy, American history
