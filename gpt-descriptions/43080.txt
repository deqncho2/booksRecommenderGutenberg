Synopsis: In 'Devota' by Augusta J. Evans, a young woman named Devota embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience in the face of adversity. Set in the post-Civil War era, Devota's life takes an unexpected turn when she finds herself caught between her duty to her family and her desire for personal freedom. As she navigates through love, loss, and societal expectations, Devota must confront her own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape her future. With its captivating narrative and richly drawn characters, 'Devota' is a compelling tale of strength, passion, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Freedom, Love and Sacrifice
