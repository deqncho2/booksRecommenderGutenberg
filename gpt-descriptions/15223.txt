Synopsis: In 'Doctor Claudius, A True Story' by F. Marion Crawford, a mysterious and enigmatic doctor arrives in a small Italian village, captivating the locals with his unconventional methods and uncanny abilities. As the doctor's true intentions and past are slowly revealed, the village becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and tragedy. With its atmospheric setting and intriguing characters, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Identity, Power, Love and Obsession
