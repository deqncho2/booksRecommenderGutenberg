Synopsis:
In Richard Wagner's "The Flying Dutchman," a young woman named Senta becomes captivated by the legend of a cursed sea captain doomed to sail the oceans for eternity. When the mysterious captain arrives in her coastal village, Senta's fascination turns into a dangerous obsession that challenges societal norms and tests the boundaries of love and redemption. As their destinies intertwine, the power of faith and the possibility of salvation take center stage in this captivating tale of passion and sacrifice.

Genres: Romantic opera, Drama, Fantasy

Themes: Love and obsession, Redemption and salvation, Fate and destiny
