Synopsis: In 'The Hidden Servants and Other Very Old Stories', Alexander Francesca weaves together a collection of enchanting tales that have been passed down through generations. From mysterious creatures lurking in the shadows to forgotten realms hidden within everyday objects, these stories transport readers to a world where magic and wonder abound. As each story unfolds, readers will be captivated by the unexpected twists and turns, and the timeless lessons they hold. Prepare to be transported to a realm where imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Short Stories

Themes: Magic, Transformation, Morality, Adventure
