Synopsis: In "Roy Blakeley: Lost, Strayed or Stolen" by Percy Keese Fitzhugh, the beloved character Roy Blakeley finds himself in a thrilling adventure when he becomes entangled in a mysterious case of a missing object. As Roy and his friends embark on a quest to solve the puzzle, they encounter unexpected twists and turns that test their courage and resourcefulness. With humor, wit, and a dash of danger, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Problem-solving, Bravery
