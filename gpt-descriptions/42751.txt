Synopsis: In 'Tempest-Driven: A Romance (Vol. 2 of 3)' by Richard Dowling, the tempestuous love affair between the enigmatic artist, Sebastian, and the spirited young heiress, Isabella, reaches new heights of passion and danger. As their relationship intensifies, they find themselves caught in a web of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With the stormy backdrop of a coastal town, their love is tested by external forces that threaten to tear them apart. Will their love survive the tempest that rages around them, or will it be swept away by the tumultuous winds of fate?

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Betrayal, Secrets, Redemption
