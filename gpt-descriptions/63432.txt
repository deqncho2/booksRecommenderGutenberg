Synopsis: In 'Colony of the Unfit' by Carter, Manfred A., a group of individuals deemed unfit by society find themselves exiled to a mysterious and isolated colony. As they navigate the challenges of their new environment, they uncover dark secrets that threaten their very existence. With danger lurking at every corner, they must band together to survive and reclaim their freedom. This gripping tale explores the resilience of the human spirit and the lengths people will go to in order to find redemption.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Social exclusion, Survival, Redemption
