Synopsis: In 'Beside Still Waters' by Arthur Christopher Benson, a group of friends reunite at their childhood home for a summer holiday, only to find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, desires, and unspoken emotions. As they navigate the complexities of their relationships, the idyllic setting becomes a backdrop for introspection, self-discovery, and the unraveling of long-held mysteries. With each passing day, the bonds between them are tested, and they must confront the choices they have made and the paths they have taken. Will they find solace and forgiveness in the tranquil waters that surround them, or will the past continue to haunt their present?

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Self-discovery, Forgiveness
