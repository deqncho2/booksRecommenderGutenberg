Synopsis: 

The War History of the 1st/4th Battalion, the Loyal North Lancashire Regiment: now the Loyal Regiment (North Lancashire), 1914-1918 takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of a battalion during World War I. Written by an anonymous author, this historical account delves into the battalion's formation, training, and their involvement in some of the most significant battles of the war. From the trenches of the Western Front to the intense battles of the Somme and Ypres, the book vividly portrays the bravery, sacrifice, and camaraderie of the soldiers as they face the horrors of war.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Bravery
