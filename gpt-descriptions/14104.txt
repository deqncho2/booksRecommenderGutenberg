Synopsis: In 'Our Changing Constitution', Charles W. Pierson takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of the United States Constitution. From its inception to the present day, Pierson explores the dynamic nature of this foundational document, shedding light on the pivotal moments and influential figures that have shaped its interpretation and application. Through engaging storytelling and insightful analysis, Pierson reveals the intricate web of political, social, and cultural forces that have continuously molded the Constitution, making it a living and breathing entity that adapts to the ever-changing needs of a nation. This thought-provoking exploration of the Constitution's transformation will leave readers with a deeper understanding of the delicate balance between tradition and progress that lies at the heart of American democracy.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Science

Themes: Constitutional law, American history, Political philosophy
