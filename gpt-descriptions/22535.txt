Synopsis: 

"Rubáiyát of Omar Khayyám, and Salámán and Absál: Together with a Life of Edward Fitzgerald and an Essay on Persian Poetry" is a captivating collection that combines the poetic brilliance of Omar Khayyám and Jami, with the insightful commentary of Ralph Waldo Emerson. This work takes readers on a journey through the enchanting world of Persian poetry, exploring the themes of love, mortality, and the pursuit of wisdom. 

Genres: Poetry, Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Love, Mortality, Wisdom
