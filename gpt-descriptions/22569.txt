Synopsis: 
In 'The Listeners and Other Poems', Walter De la Mare takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of haunting and mysterious poems. From the eerie encounters of spectral beings to the enchanting beauty of nature, each poem is a window into a world where the ordinary becomes extraordinary. With his lyrical and evocative language, De la Mare invites readers to explore the depths of human emotions, the power of imagination, and the enigmatic nature of existence. This collection is sure to leave readers spellbound and pondering the mysteries of life.

Genres: Poetry, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Mystery, Supernatural, Nature, Imagination
