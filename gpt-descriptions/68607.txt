Synopsis: In 'Don Sebastian: or, The House of the Braganza', Anna Maria Porter weaves a captivating historical romance that takes readers on a journey through the tumultuous times of 17th-century Portugal. The story revolves around the enigmatic Don Sebastian, a nobleman haunted by a dark secret, and the mysterious Lady Agnes, whose arrival in Lisbon sets off a chain of events that will test their loyalties and challenge their hearts. As political intrigue, forbidden love, and treacherous alliances unfold, the fate of the House of Braganza hangs in the balance. Will Don Sebastian's past catch up with him, or will love conquer all?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Intrigue

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Forbidden Love, Political Intrigue
