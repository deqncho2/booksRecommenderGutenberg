Synopsis: 
"The House on the Borderland" by William Hope Hodgson is a gripping and eerie tale that follows two friends who stumble upon an ancient manuscript detailing the terrifying experiences of a recluse living in a remote house. As they delve into the manuscript, they are transported to a realm of cosmic horror, where they witness unimaginable terrors and face a battle for their sanity and survival. With its haunting atmosphere and mind-bending narrative, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of reality and the nature of fear.

Genres: Horror, Science Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Cosmic horror, Time and space, Madness, Existentialism
