Synopsis: In 'Flowering Evil' by Margaret St. Clair, a small, idyllic town becomes the setting for a series of mysterious and gruesome murders. As the body count rises, the townspeople are left in a state of fear and paranoia, unsure of who among them could be the killer. With each murder, the clues become more perplexing, leading the protagonist on a twisted journey to uncover the truth behind the evil that has taken root in their community. As the investigation unfolds, dark secrets are revealed, and the line between good and evil becomes blurred. Will the protagonist be able to stop the flowering evil before it consumes them all?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Small-town Secrets, Psychological Suspense
