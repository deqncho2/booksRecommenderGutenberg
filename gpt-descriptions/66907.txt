Synopsis: In 'Broken Barriers' by Nicholson Meredith, a young woman named Emma finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies when she stumbles upon a hidden letter that unravels a dark family history. As she delves deeper into the past, Emma must confront the consequences of her ancestors' actions and make difficult choices that will shape her future. With each revelation, the barriers that have kept her from the truth begin to crumble, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Redemption
