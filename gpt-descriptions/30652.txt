Synopsis: 
In "In The Seven Woods: Being Poems Chiefly of the Irish Heroic Age," W.B. Yeats takes readers on a captivating journey through the mythical and heroic age of Ireland. Through his lyrical and evocative poetry, Yeats transports us to a world of ancient warriors, mystical landscapes, and timeless tales of love and loss. From the stirring adventures of legendary heroes to the haunting beauty of Celtic mythology, this collection of poems immerses readers in the rich tapestry of Irish folklore and history. With each verse, Yeats weaves a spellbinding narrative that explores the complexities of human nature, the power of destiny, and the enduring legacy of Ireland's heroic past.

Genres: Poetry, Mythology, Historical Fiction
Themes: Irish folklore, Heroism, Love and Loss, Destiny, Mythology
