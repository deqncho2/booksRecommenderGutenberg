Synopsis: In "Commentary on the Epistle to the Galatians," Martin Luther delves into the heart of the Christian faith, challenging traditional beliefs and advocating for a radical understanding of salvation. Through his passionate and thought-provoking analysis of the biblical text, Luther unveils a powerful message that challenges the status quo and calls for a return to the true essence of Christianity. As he navigates through the Epistle to the Galatians, Luther's commentary offers a fresh perspective on the nature of faith, grace, and the role of the law in the life of a believer. 

Genres: Religious commentary, theological analysis, historical literature

Themes: Faith and works, grace and law, justification by faith, religious authority, freedom in Christ
