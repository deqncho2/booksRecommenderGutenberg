Synopsis: 
In 'Modern Billiards: A Complete Text-Book of the Game', Benjamin Garno presents a comprehensive guide to mastering the art of billiards. With clear and practical instructions, this book offers readers the opportunity to delve into the world of this scientific amusement and acquire the skills necessary to become a skilled player. From basic techniques to advanced strategies, Garno's expertise shines through as he explores the intricacies of the game, making it an essential read for both beginners and experienced players alike.

Genres: Sports, Instructional, Non-fiction

Themes: Skill acquisition, Strategy, Recreation
