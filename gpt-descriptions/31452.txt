Synopsis: In 'Roy Blakeley in the Haunted Camp', Roy and his friends embark on a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a mysterious campsite rumored to be haunted. As they uncover eerie secrets and encounter strange occurrences, they must summon their courage to solve the camp's haunting mystery. With suspenseful twists and unexpected turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Overcoming Fear
