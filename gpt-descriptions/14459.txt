Synopsis: 

In 'Proportional Representation Applied to Party Government: A New Electoral System', Ashworth presents a groundbreaking exploration of electoral systems and their impact on party governance. Through meticulous research and analysis, he proposes a new approach to proportional representation that challenges traditional notions of democracy and political power. As he delves into the intricacies of party government, Ashworth uncovers surprising insights and offers thought-provoking solutions to the flaws inherent in current electoral systems. This thought-provoking work is a must-read for anyone interested in the future of democracy and the potential for transformative change in political systems.

Genres: Political Science, Government, Electoral Systems

Themes: Democracy, Political Power, Electoral Reform
