Synopsis: In 'The Luck of the Kid' by Cullum, Ridgwell, a young cowboy named Kid Wolf finds himself caught in a web of danger and deceit when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Kid must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to survive. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Kid's luck becomes both his greatest ally and his most dangerous adversary.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Luck, Survival, Betrayal
