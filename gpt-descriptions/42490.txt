Synopsis: 

In 'Outwitting the Hun: My Escape from a German Prison Camp', Pat O'Brien takes readers on a thrilling journey through his harrowing escape from a German prison camp during World War II. As a prisoner of war, O'Brien faces unimaginable challenges and risks as he devises a daring plan to outsmart his captors and regain his freedom. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover if O'Brien's audacious escape plan will succeed.

Genres: Memoir, War, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Survival
