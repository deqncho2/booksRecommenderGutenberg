Synopsis: In 'A Houseful of Girls' by Sarah Tytler, a charming and mysterious stranger arrives at the doorstep of the lively and unconventional Marchmont family. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the four Marchmont sisters, secrets are unveiled, passions are ignited, and the bonds of sisterhood are put to the test. With its blend of romance, suspense, and family dynamics, this captivating novel takes readers on a journey of love, betrayal, and self-discovery.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Family Drama

Themes: Sisterhood, Secrets, Identity
