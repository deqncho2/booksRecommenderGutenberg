Synopsis: In 'A Woman of No Importance' by Oscar Wilde, a seemingly ordinary gathering of high society individuals takes a dramatic turn when a mysterious woman arrives. As secrets are unveiled and hidden pasts are exposed, the characters are forced to confront their own moral values and societal expectations. With sharp wit and biting social commentary, Wilde explores the complexities of love, class, and the double standards faced by women in Victorian society.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Satire

Themes: Society and Class, Morality and Hypocrisy, Gender Roles and Expectations
