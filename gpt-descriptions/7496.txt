Synopsis: 
In 'Jack Ranger's Western Trip; Or, from Boarding School to Ranch and Range', young Jack embarks on an exciting adventure that takes him from the confines of his boarding school to the vast and untamed landscapes of the American West. As he navigates the challenges of ranch life and encounters a colorful cast of characters, Jack discovers the true meaning of friendship, courage, and self-discovery. With thrilling escapades and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will transport readers to a world of cowboys, outlaws, and the boundless spirit of the Wild West.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
