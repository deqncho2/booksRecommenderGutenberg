Synopsis:
In "Betty Gordon in the Land of Oil; Or, The Farm That Was Worth a Fortune," Betty Gordon finds herself embarking on an exciting adventure when she inherits a farm in a land rich with oil. As she navigates the challenges and mysteries surrounding her newfound property, Betty's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. With the help of her friends, she uncovers secrets, encounters danger, and discovers the true value of friendship and perseverance.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Inheritance, Friendship, Resourcefulness
