Synopsis: In 'The Boy Allies in Great Peril; Or, With the Italian Army in the Alps', author Clair W. Hayes takes readers on an exhilarating journey alongside two young American boys who find themselves in the midst of a treacherous war zone. As they join forces with the Italian Army in the rugged Alps, they face danger, espionage, and unexpected alliances. With their courage and resourcefulness tested to the limit, the boys must navigate through perilous situations to fulfill their mission and ensure victory for their newfound comrades.

Genres: Adventure, War, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Loyalty
