Synopsis: 
In 'Two on the Trail: A Story of the Far Northwest', two adventurous souls embark on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of the Far Northwest. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter dangerous wildlife, they must rely on their wits and resilience to survive. Along the way, they uncover hidden secrets and face unexpected challenges that test the limits of their friendship. This gripping tale of adventure and survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Exploration
