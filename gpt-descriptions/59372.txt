Synopsis: In "The Alfred Jewel: An Historical Essay," John Earle delves into the fascinating story behind a mysterious and exquisite artifact known as the Alfred Jewel. This essay takes readers on a captivating journey through history, exploring the origins, significance, and enigmatic nature of this precious object. As Earle unravels the secrets surrounding the Alfred Jewel, he uncovers a tale that intertwines power, artistry, and the enduring legacy of a legendary ruler. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Art history, Archaeology

Themes: Power and authority, Art and craftsmanship, Historical legacy
