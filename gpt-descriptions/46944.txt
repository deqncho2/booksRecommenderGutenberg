Synopsis: 
In 'The Golden Maiden, and other folk tales and fairy stories told in Armenia', A. K. Seklemian takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Armenian folklore. From magical creatures and heroic quests to tales of love and betrayal, this collection of stories is sure to captivate readers of all ages. Discover the secrets of the Golden Maiden, follow the adventures of brave warriors, and unravel the mysteries of ancient Armenia. Each story is filled with vivid imagery and rich cultural details, transporting readers to a realm where anything is possible.

Genres: Folklore, Fairy Tales, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Love and Betrayal, Cultural Identity
