Synopsis: 
In 'The Junior Classics, Volume 4: Heroes and heroines of chivalry', embark on a thrilling journey through the tales of legendary heroes and heroines who embody the noble ideals of chivalry. From the valiant knights of King Arthur's Round Table to the courageous maidens who defy societal expectations, this collection of stories will transport readers to a world of honor, bravery, and adventure. Encounter epic battles, quests for justice, and the triumph of love and loyalty as these timeless tales unfold. 

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Bravery, Love, Loyalty
