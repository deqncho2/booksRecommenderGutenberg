Synopsis: 
'Diaries of Court Ladies of Old Japan' is a captivating collection of diaries written by three remarkable women from different periods of Japanese history. Izumi Shikibu, Murasaki Shikibu, and Sugawara no Takasue no Musume offer a unique glimpse into the lives of court ladies, their intricate relationships, and the challenges they face in a society bound by tradition and hierarchy. Through their intimate and poetic accounts, the reader is transported to a world of forbidden love, political intrigue, and personal growth, where these women navigate the complexities of court life while striving to find their own voice and purpose.

Genres: Historical fiction, memoir, poetry

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, personal growth, female empowerment
