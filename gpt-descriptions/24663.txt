Synopsis: In 'The Rich Little Poor Boy' by Eleanor Gates, a young boy named Jack finds himself caught between two worlds when he discovers a shocking secret about his true identity. As he navigates the complexities of his newfound wealth and privilege, Jack must confront the challenges of loyalty, self-discovery, and the true meaning of family. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Jack's extraordinary journey.

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, Family Drama

Themes: Identity, Wealth and Privilege, Loyalty, Self-discovery
