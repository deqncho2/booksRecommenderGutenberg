Synopsis: In 'Frank Reade, Jr., With His New Steam Man in Central America', readers are taken on an exhilarating adventure as Frank Reade, Jr. embarks on a daring expedition through the heart of Central America. Equipped with his remarkable invention, the Steam Man, Frank faces treacherous landscapes, encounters with dangerous wildlife, and unexpected encounters with local inhabitants. As he navigates through the unknown, Frank's ingenuity and courage are put to the ultimate test, making this a thrilling and suspenseful tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Steampunk

Themes: Exploration, Invention, Survival
