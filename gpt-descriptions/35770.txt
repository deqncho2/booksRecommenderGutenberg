Synopsis: In 'The Hero' by Elaine Wilber, a retired detective is drawn back into the world of crime-solving when a mysterious murder case resurfaces, forcing him to confront his past and face the consequences of his actions. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that threatens to unravel everything he holds dear. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with danger and betrayal to uncover the truth and bring justice to those who deserve it. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Redemption, Consequences of Actions, Betrayal
