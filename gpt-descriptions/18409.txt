Synopsis: In 'By the Roadside' by Katherine M. Yates, a chance encounter between two strangers on a desolate roadside sets off a chain of events that will challenge their beliefs, test their resilience, and ultimately lead them on a journey of self-discovery. As they navigate through unexpected twists and turns, their lives become intertwined in ways they could never have imagined. This gripping tale explores the power of human connection, the complexities of personal identity, and the profound impact of choices made along life's unpredictable path.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Fate, Human Connection
