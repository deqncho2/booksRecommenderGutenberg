Synopsis: In 'The Snowball Effect' by Katherine MacLean, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious snowstorm blankets the area, leaving behind a trail of unexpected consequences. As the snowball effect takes hold, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the residents must confront their deepest fears. With each passing day, the snow becomes a metaphor for the characters' lives, slowly building up and threatening to bury them all. Will they find a way to break free from the icy grip of their pasts, or will they be forever trapped in the snowstorm of their own making?

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and lies, Consequences of actions, Redemption, Personal growth
