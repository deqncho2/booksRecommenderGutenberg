Synopsis: In 'A Modern Cinderella' by Amanda M. Douglas, follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Ella as she navigates the challenges of life in the bustling city. Struggling to make ends meet, Ella finds solace in her passion for writing and dreams of a better future. Unexpectedly, she crosses paths with a mysterious stranger who may hold the key to her dreams coming true. As Ella's life takes an unexpected turn, she must confront her fears, embrace her inner strength, and discover the true meaning of love and happiness.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Overcoming adversity, Pursuing dreams, Self-discovery
