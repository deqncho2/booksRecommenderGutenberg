Synopsis: In "The Boss, and How He Came to Rule New York" by Alfred Henry Lewis, delve into the captivating world of New York City's underworld and witness the rise of a powerful figure who will stop at nothing to control the city. This gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark alleys, corrupt politics, and dangerous alliances that shape the destiny of one man and the entire city. As secrets are unveiled and loyalties are tested, prepare to be enthralled by the gripping narrative that explores the lengths one person will go to in order to achieve ultimate power.

Genres: Crime fiction, Political thriller, Historical fiction

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal, Ambition and ambition
