Synopsis: In 'The 32nd Mersenne Prime: Predicted by Mersenne', mathematician David Slowinski takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of prime numbers. When a mysterious prediction about the discovery of the 32nd Mersenne Prime emerges, Slowinski finds himself caught in a race against time to uncover the truth. As he delves deeper into the secrets of prime numbers, he encounters unexpected challenges and uncovers a hidden world of intrigue and deception. Will Slowinski be able to solve the puzzle and find the elusive prime number before it falls into the wrong hands?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Mathematics

Themes: Mathematics, Cryptography, Intellectual Pursuit
