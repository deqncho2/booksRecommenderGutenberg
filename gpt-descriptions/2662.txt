Synopsis: In the idyllic village of Mellstock, a group of talented musicians known as the Mellstock Quire play their music in the local church. However, their peaceful existence is disrupted when a new organist arrives, threatening to replace their traditional music with a modern instrument. As the villagers rally to protect their beloved quire, a charming love story unfolds, filled with tender moments and unexpected twists. Set against the backdrop of the beautiful English countryside, 'Under the Greenwood Tree' is a delightful tale that explores the clash between tradition and progress, and the power of music to bring people together.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Tradition vs. Modernity, Love and Relationships, Community and Identity
