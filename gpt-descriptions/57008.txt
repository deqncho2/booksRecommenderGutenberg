Synopsis: In 'The Haunted Hangar: Sky Scouts/Air Mystery series #3' by Powell, Van, the Sky Scouts find themselves in a spine-chilling adventure when they stumble upon an abandoned hangar with a dark secret. As they delve deeper into the mysteries of the haunted hangar, they uncover a series of eerie occurrences that defy explanation. With their courage and resourcefulness put to the test, the Sky Scouts must unravel the truth behind the haunting before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Paranormal

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Supernatural phenomena
