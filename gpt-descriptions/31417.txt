Synopsis: 

'Psychic Phenomena: A Brief Account of the Physical Manifestations Observed in Psychical Research' by Edward T. Bennett delves into the intriguing world of psychical research, exploring the various physical manifestations that have been observed and documented. From telekinesis to levitation, the book presents a captivating overview of these extraordinary phenomena, offering a glimpse into the unexplained and mysterious aspects of the human mind. As the author delves deeper into the subject matter, readers will find themselves engrossed in the fascinating world of psychic phenomena, questioning the boundaries of what is considered possible.

Genres: Non-fiction, Paranormal, Science

Themes: Psychic abilities, Unexplained phenomena, Human consciousness
