Synopsis: 
'Before and after Waterloo: Letters from Edward Stanley, sometime Bishop of Norwich' is a captivating collection of personal letters written by Edward Stanley, offering a unique perspective on the events leading up to and following the Battle of Waterloo. Through his correspondence, Stanley provides intimate insights into the political climate, the military strategies, and the personal experiences of those involved in this pivotal moment in history. As the reader delves into Stanley's letters, they will be transported back in time, witnessing the anticipation, the chaos, and the aftermath of one of the most significant battles in European history.

Genres: Historical, Epistolary

Themes: War, Politics, Personal experiences
