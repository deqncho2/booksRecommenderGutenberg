Synopsis: In "Legends of the City of Mexico," Janvier takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and enchanting tales of Mexico's vibrant capital. From ancient Aztec legends to haunting ghost stories, this collection of narratives weaves together the mystical and the historical, painting a vivid picture of the city's cultural tapestry. As readers delve into the pages, they will encounter mythical creatures, heroic figures, and supernatural occurrences that have shaped the identity of Mexico City. With each story, Janvier invites readers to explore the hidden corners of this captivating metropolis and discover the enduring power of its legends.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cultural identity, Supernatural, History, Tradition
