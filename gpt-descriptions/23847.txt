Synopsis: 
In "The Peacock and Parrot, on their Tour to Discover the Author of 'The Peacock At Home'," two colorful and curious birds embark on an adventurous journey to uncover the identity of the mysterious author behind a popular book. As they travel through enchanting landscapes and encounter a variety of eccentric characters, the Peacock and Parrot find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and surprises. With each clue they uncover, the birds become more determined to solve the puzzle, leading them on a thrilling and unpredictable quest. Will they finally unveil the truth behind the enigmatic author?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Curiosity, Exploration
