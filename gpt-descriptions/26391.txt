Synopsis: 
In 'The Unknown Wrestler' by Cody, H. A., a mysterious newcomer arrives in a small town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic presence. As he steps into the wrestling ring, his extraordinary skills and unconventional techniques leave the audience in awe. But behind the mask lies a secret that could change everything. As the town becomes consumed by the unknown wrestler's allure, a thrilling journey of self-discovery and redemption unfolds, revealing the power of determination and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: 
Sports, Mystery, Drama

Themes: 
Identity, Redemption, Perseverance
