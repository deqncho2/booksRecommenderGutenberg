Synopsis: 
In 'Probable Sons' by Amy Le Feuvre, a young boy named Dickie finds himself caught between two worlds when he is unexpectedly taken in by a wealthy family. As he adjusts to his new life, Dickie must navigate the complexities of social class, family secrets, and the true meaning of love and acceptance. With twists and turns that keep readers guessing, this heartwarming tale explores the power of compassion and the transformative nature of unexpected connections.

Genres: 
Drama, Coming-of-age, Family

Themes: 
Social class, Identity, Love and acceptance
