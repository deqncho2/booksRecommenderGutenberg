Synopsis: In 'The Life of Columbus' by Arthur Helps, embark on a captivating journey through the life of the renowned explorer Christopher Columbus. This enthralling biography delves into the trials and triumphs of Columbus, from his humble beginnings to his audacious voyages across the Atlantic Ocean. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be transported to a world of uncharted territories, political intrigue, and the relentless pursuit of dreams. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Helps paints a vivid portrait of Columbus, revealing the complexities of his character and the impact of his discoveries on the course of history.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Ambition, Legacy
