Synopsis: 
In 'A Smaller History of Greece: from the Earliest Times to the Roman Conquest' by William Smith, embark on a captivating journey through the rich and vibrant history of Greece. From the legendary tales of gods and heroes to the rise and fall of powerful city-states, this book offers a concise yet enthralling account of Greece's fascinating past. Delve into the birth of democracy, the epic battles of the Persian Wars, and the conquests of Alexander the Great, all while exploring the cultural and intellectual achievements that shaped Western civilization. With vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, Smith brings to life the triumphs and struggles of the Greek people, painting a vivid picture of this extraordinary ancient civilization.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Democracy, War and conquest, Cultural and intellectual achievements
