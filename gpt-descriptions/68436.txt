Synopsis: In "Only a Farm Boy; or, Dan Hardy's Rise in Life" by Frank V. Webster, follow the captivating journey of Dan Hardy, a young farm boy with dreams bigger than his rural surroundings. As he navigates the challenges and opportunities that come his way, Dan's determination and resilience propel him towards a life he never thought possible. From humble beginnings to unexpected encounters, this inspiring tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering just how far Dan's rise in life will take him.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Inspirational

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Self-discovery
