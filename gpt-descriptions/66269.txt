Synopsis: 
'At the North of Bearcamp Water: Chronicles of a Stroller in New England from July to December' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of New England. Frank Bolles, an avid stroller, immerses himself in the beauty of nature as he explores the region from July to December. From the majestic mountains to the serene lakes, Bolles encounters a myriad of fascinating characters and experiences unexpected adventures along the way. As he delves deeper into the heart of New England, he discovers secrets and hidden treasures that will leave readers yearning to embark on their own exploration of this captivating region.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Nature Writing

Themes: Nature's beauty, Exploration, Serendipitous encounters
