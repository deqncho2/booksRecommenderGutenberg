Synopsis: 
In "The Confessions of Arsène Lupin," the notorious gentleman thief Arsène Lupin takes center stage as he reveals his most daring exploits and uncovers hidden secrets. From his audacious heists to his clever disguises, Lupin's confessions captivate readers with their thrilling twists and turns. As the lines between right and wrong blur, readers are left questioning their own moral compasses. With each confession, Lupin challenges societal norms and leaves readers wondering if there is more to this charming thief than meets the eye.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Morality, Identity, Deception
