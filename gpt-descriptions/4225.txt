Synopsis: In 'First and Last Things: A Confession of Faith and Rule of Life', H. G. Wells takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the realms of philosophy, science, and spirituality. Through a series of essays, Wells explores the fundamental questions of existence, delving into the nature of reality, the purpose of life, and the mysteries of the universe. With his characteristic wit and intellectual prowess, Wells challenges conventional beliefs and offers a fresh perspective on the eternal quest for truth.

Genres: Philosophy, Non-fiction, Essay

Themes: Existentialism, Science and Religion, Meaning of Life
