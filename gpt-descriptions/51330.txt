Synopsis: In 'I Am a Nucleus' by Barr, Stephen, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Emily Collins finds herself at the center of a groundbreaking experiment that could change the course of humanity. As she delves deeper into her research, she uncovers a web of secrets and conspiracies that threaten not only her work but also her very existence. With time running out, Dr. Collins must navigate a treacherous world of power struggles and moral dilemmas, all while trying to protect her groundbreaking discovery. This gripping and thought-provoking novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific ethics, Power struggles, Conspiracy
