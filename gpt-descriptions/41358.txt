Synopsis: 
In 'Practical Mind-Reading: A Course of Lessons on Thought-Transference, Telepathy, Mental-Currents, Mental Rapport, &c.', Atkinson delves into the fascinating world of mind-reading and telepathy. Through a series of lessons, he explores the power of thought-transference and the ability to connect with others on a deep mental level. With practical exercises and insightful explanations, Atkinson guides readers on a journey to unlock their own latent psychic abilities. Prepare to be captivated by the mysteries of the mind and the potential for extraordinary connections.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Self-help, Occult

Themes: 
Mind-reading, Telepathy, Psychic abilities, Mental connection, Personal development
