Synopsis: 
'A Chesterton Calendar: Compiled from the writings of 'G.K.C.' both in verse and in prose. With a section apart for the moveable feasts.' is a captivating collection of writings by G.K. Chesterton, encompassing both poetry and prose. This calendar-like compilation offers a diverse range of thought-provoking and insightful pieces, capturing the essence of Chesterton's wit, wisdom, and unique perspective on life. From profound reflections on faith and philosophy to humorous observations on everyday occurrences, this anthology invites readers to delve into the mind of one of the most celebrated writers of the 20th century.

Genres: 
Poetry, Prose, Anthology

Themes: 
Faith, Philosophy, Humor, Observations of Life
