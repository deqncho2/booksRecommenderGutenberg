Synopsis: In 'Through Space to Mars; Or, the Longest Journey on Record' by Rockwood, Roy, a group of adventurous scientists embark on an extraordinary mission to Mars, facing countless challenges and unexpected discoveries along the way. As they navigate through the vastness of space, their determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will they succeed in reaching the red planet and unravel its mysteries?

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Scientific Curiosity
