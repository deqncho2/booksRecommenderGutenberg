Synopsis: In 'Pluck on the Long Trail; Or, Boy Scouts in the Rockies', a group of adventurous Boy Scouts embark on a thrilling journey through the majestic Rockies. Led by their fearless leader, Pluck, the boys face numerous challenges and dangers as they navigate treacherous terrains, encounter wild animals, and uncover hidden secrets. With their survival skills and determination, they must rely on teamwork and resourcefulness to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. This action-packed tale of courage, friendship, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival, Exploration
