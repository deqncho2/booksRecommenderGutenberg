Synopsis: In 'The Purple Pennant' by Ralph Henry Barbour, a talented young athlete named Jack is faced with a difficult decision that could change the course of his life. As he navigates the challenges of high school, friendships, and the pressures of success, Jack finds himself torn between loyalty and ambition. With the fate of his team and his own future hanging in the balance, Jack must make a choice that will test his character and define his legacy.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Sports Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Ambition, Friendship, Integrity
