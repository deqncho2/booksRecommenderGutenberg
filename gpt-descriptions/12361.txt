Synopsis: In 'The Mother's Recompense, Volume 1: A Sequel to Home Influence', Grace Aguilar takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two families, exploring the complexities of love, duty, and sacrifice. Set in the 19th century, this compelling novel delves into the challenges faced by mothers and daughters, as well as the consequences of societal expectations and personal choices. As secrets unravel and relationships are tested, the characters must navigate the delicate balance between personal happiness and fulfilling their responsibilities. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this sequel to 'Home Influence' promises to captivate readers with its emotional depth and thought-provoking themes.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Family Drama, Romance

Themes: Motherhood, Duty, Sacrifice, Society's Expectations, Personal Happiness
