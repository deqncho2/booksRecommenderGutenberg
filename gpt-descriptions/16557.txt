Synopsis: In 'The Deserter' by Charles King, a decorated military officer mysteriously disappears during a crucial mission, leaving his comrades and superiors baffled. As they delve deeper into the investigation, they uncover a web of secrets, betrayal, and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel the entire military operation. With time running out, the search for the deserter becomes a race against the clock, where loyalties are tested and the true nature of honor is questioned.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Military Fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Honor, Duty
