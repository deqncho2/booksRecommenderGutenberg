Synopsis: 

Step into the fascinating world of the wives of the Prime Ministers from 1844 to 1906, as authors Elizabeth Lee and Lucy Masterman unveil the untold stories of these remarkable women. From the elegant and influential to the unconventional and controversial, this captivating book delves into the lives of these women who stood beside the most powerful men in the country. Discover the secrets, scandals, and triumphs that shaped their lives, as they navigated the complexities of politics, society, and their own personal ambitions. 

Genres: Biography, History, Women's Studies

Themes: Power and Influence, Gender Roles, Political Intrigue
