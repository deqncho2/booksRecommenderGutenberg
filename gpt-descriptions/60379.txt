Synopsis: In 'The Camel and the Needle's Eye', Arthur Ponsonby takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two individuals from vastly different backgrounds. As their paths intertwine, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and unexpected connections are formed. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, this gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Social class, Identity, Redemption
