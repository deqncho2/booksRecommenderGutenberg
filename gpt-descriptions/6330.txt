Synopsis: In 'Amanda: A Daughter of the Mennonites', Anna Balmer Myers takes us on a captivating journey into the life of Amanda, a young woman raised in a strict Mennonite community. As Amanda navigates the challenges of her sheltered upbringing, she begins to question the traditions and beliefs that have shaped her existence. With the arrival of an unexpected visitor, Amanda's world is turned upside down, forcing her to confront her deepest desires and make choices that will forever alter her path. This poignant and thought-provoking tale explores the complexities of identity, faith, and the pursuit of personal freedom.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Religious

Themes: Identity, Tradition, Freedom, Faith
