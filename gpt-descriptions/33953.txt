Synopsis: 
"The Boke of Noblesse: Addressed to King Edward the Fourth on His Invasion of France in 1475" is a captivating historical work that takes readers on a journey through the tumultuous events surrounding King Edward IV's invasion of France. Through a series of letters and narratives, the book delves into the intricate web of political alliances, personal ambitions, and the timeless struggle for power that defined this pivotal moment in history. As the story unfolds, readers are introduced to a cast of fascinating characters, each with their own motivations and secrets, as they navigate the treacherous landscape of war and diplomacy. With its rich historical detail and compelling storytelling, this book offers a unique perspective on a crucial chapter in European history.

Genres: Historical fiction, political drama, epistolary novel

Themes: Power and ambition, loyalty and betrayal, the consequences of war
