Synopsis: In 'Lay Down Your Arms: The Autobiography of Martha von Tilling', we are taken on a captivating journey through the life of Martha, a strong-willed and determined woman who defies societal expectations and fights for peace in a world torn apart by war. From her humble beginnings to her encounters with influential figures, Martha's story is one of resilience, love, and the power of conviction. As she navigates through personal struggles and the horrors of war, Martha's unwavering commitment to peace becomes a beacon of hope in a turbulent era.

Genres: Autobiography, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Peace activism, Feminism, Social justice
