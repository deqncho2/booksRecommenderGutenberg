Synopsis: 

"The Master Builder" by Henrik Ibsen is a captivating play that delves into the complex psyche of Halvard Solness, a renowned architect who is haunted by his past and consumed by his ambitions. As Solness navigates his professional and personal life, he becomes entangled in a web of relationships that challenge his beliefs and force him to confront his deepest fears. With the arrival of a young woman named Hilda, who claims to have a mysterious connection to Solness, the play takes a thrilling turn, exploring themes of power, desire, and the pursuit of greatness.

Genres: Drama, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Guilt, Identity, Power Dynamics
