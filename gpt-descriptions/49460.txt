Synopsis: 

In 'Dividing Waters' by I. A. R. Wylie, a gripping tale unfolds as a young woman finds herself torn between duty and desire in the midst of a treacherous love triangle. Set against the backdrop of a small coastal town, secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances emerge, leading to a series of shocking events that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love and passion, Betrayal, Secrets and lies
