Synopsis: In 'The Roof Tree' by Charles Neville Buck, a young woman named Mary finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is forced to make a difficult decision that could change her life forever. Set against the backdrop of a small New England town, this captivating novel explores the complexities of love, family, and the pursuit of happiness. As Mary navigates the challenges of her circumstances, she must confront her own desires and confront the expectations of society. Will she choose the path that is expected of her or follow her heart's true desires?

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Love and Sacrifice, Societal Expectations
