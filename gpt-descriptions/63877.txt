Synopsis: In 'Never Fire First: A Canadian Northwest Mounted Story' by James French Dorrance, a young and determined Mountie finds himself in a treacherous and mysterious land, where danger lurks at every turn. As he navigates through the rugged wilderness, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will test his loyalty and honor. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Duty, Redemption
