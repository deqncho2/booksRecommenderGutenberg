Synopsis: In 'Doctor' by Murray Leinster, a brilliant scientist discovers a groundbreaking medical technology that has the potential to revolutionize the world of medicine. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that could have catastrophic consequences. With lives at stake, he must navigate a treacherous path of moral dilemmas and ethical choices, all while trying to protect his groundbreaking invention from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Medical Drama

Themes: Ethical dilemmas, Scientific advancements, Morality, Consequences of technology
