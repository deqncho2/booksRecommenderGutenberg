Synopsis: In 'A Little Girl in Old San Francisco', we follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Maria as she navigates the vibrant streets of 19th century San Francisco. From the bustling markets to the opulent mansions, Maria's adventures take her through a city filled with colorful characters and hidden secrets. As she uncovers the mysteries of her own past, Maria discovers that the city she loves holds both danger and possibility, and that her own resilience and determination will be tested in ways she never imagined.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Family, Resilience, Secrets
