Synopsis: In 'The Worn Doorstep' by Margaret Pollock Sherwood, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn to a mysterious old house with a worn doorstep. As she delves into the history of the house and its previous inhabitants, she uncovers a web of secrets and hidden connections that will change her life forever. With each step closer to the truth, Emily becomes entangled in a haunting tale of love, loss, and redemption that spans generations.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Redemption
