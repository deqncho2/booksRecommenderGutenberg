Synopsis: 
In 'Bart Stirling's Road to Success; Or, The Young Express Agent' by Chapman, Allen, we follow the journey of Bart Stirling, a determined and ambitious young man who dreams of making a name for himself in the world. When an unexpected opportunity arises, Bart seizes it with both hands, embarking on a thrilling adventure that will test his courage, resilience, and resourcefulness. As he navigates the challenges of the fast-paced express industry, Bart must confront both external obstacles and his own inner doubts. Will he be able to overcome the odds and achieve the success he so desperately desires?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Self-discovery
