Synopsis: In 'Revolving Lights: Pilgrimage, Volume 7' by Dorothy M. Richardson, the protagonist, Miriam Henderson, embarks on a transformative journey as she navigates the bustling streets of London and encounters a diverse cast of characters. As Miriam grapples with her own desires and ambitions, she becomes entangled in a web of relationships and experiences that challenge her perceptions of love, identity, and the pursuit of happiness. With vivid prose and introspective narration, Richardson takes readers on a captivating exploration of self-discovery and the complexities of human connection.

Genres: Modernist literature, coming-of-age, psychological fiction

Themes: Identity, self-discovery, love and relationships, societal expectations
