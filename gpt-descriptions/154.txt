Synopsis: 
In 'The Rise of Silas Lapham', we follow the journey of a self-made businessman who finds himself torn between his desire for social acceptance and his commitment to his humble roots. Silas Lapham, a successful paint manufacturer, is determined to climb the social ladder and secure a place among Boston's elite. However, as he navigates the treacherous world of high society, he must confront the consequences of his choices and the true meaning of success. Will Silas be able to maintain his integrity and find happiness amidst the pressures of wealth and status?

Genres: 
Realistic fiction, Satire, Social commentary

Themes: 
Social mobility, Materialism, Morality, Identity
