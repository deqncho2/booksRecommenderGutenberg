Synopsis: In 'The Unsolved Riddle of Social Justice', Stephen Leacock delves into the complex and perplexing issue of social justice, exploring its various facets and presenting thought-provoking arguments. Through a combination of wit, humor, and insightful analysis, Leacock challenges conventional wisdom and offers a fresh perspective on this enduring enigma. With his characteristic storytelling style, he takes readers on a journey of intellectual exploration, shedding light on the intricacies of social justice and leaving them pondering the unsolved riddle.

Genres: Non-fiction, Social Commentary, Philosophy

Themes: Social justice, Equality, Morality, Economic systems
