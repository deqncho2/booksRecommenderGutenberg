Synopsis: In "How to thought-read: A manual of instruction in the strange and mystic in daily life, psychic phenomena," James Coates, Ph.D., F.A.S. delves into the fascinating world of psychic abilities and explores various techniques such as hypnotism, mind reading, thought transference, and clairvoyance. This comprehensive manual offers practical instructions and insights into unlocking the hidden potential of the mind, providing readers with a captivating journey into the realm of psychic phenomena.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Self-help

Themes: Psychic abilities, Mind exploration, Mysticism
