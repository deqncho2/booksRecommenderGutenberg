Synopsis: In "The Real Shelley. New Views of the Poet's Life. Vol. 2," John Cordy Jeaffreson delves into the captivating life of the renowned poet, Percy Bysshe Shelley. Uncovering previously unknown details and shedding light on the enigmatic figure, this volume takes readers on a journey through Shelley's tumultuous relationships, his literary pursuits, and the profound impact he had on the Romantic movement. From his scandalous elopement to his passionate friendships with fellow poets, Jeaffreson paints a vivid portrait of Shelley's complex character, revealing the man behind the poetry.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Love and Relationships, Artistic Expression, Rebellion against societal norms
