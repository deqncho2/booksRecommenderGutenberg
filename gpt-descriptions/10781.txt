Synopsis: In 'Deserted: Sailor's Knots, Part 1,' the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the mysterious world of sailors and their intriguing tales. Set against the backdrop of the sea, this captivating collection of stories introduces a cast of colorful characters, each with their own secrets and desires. From the hauntingly beautiful to the downright bizarre, these tales will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly awaiting the next twist and turn.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Survival, Human nature
