Synopsis: In 'Gypsy Breynton' by Elizabeth Stuart Phelps, a young woman named Gypsy finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires for independence. As she navigates the challenges of love, family, and personal growth, Gypsy's journey takes unexpected turns that will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Freedom, Self-discovery
