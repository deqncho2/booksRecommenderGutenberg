Synopsis: In 'The Bishop's Shadow' by Ida Treadwell Thurston, a young boy named Teddy finds himself caught up in a mysterious and thrilling adventure when he becomes the shadow of a prominent bishop. As Teddy navigates the world of high society and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, he discovers secrets that could change everything. With danger lurking around every corner, Teddy must rely on his wit and courage to unravel the truth and protect those he cares about.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Coming of Age
