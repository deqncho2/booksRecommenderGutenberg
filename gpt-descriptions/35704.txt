Synopsis: 
'Wonder Stories: The Best Myths for Boys and Girls' by Carolyn Sherwin Bailey is a captivating collection of timeless myths and legends that will transport readers to enchanting worlds filled with gods, heroes, and magical creatures. From the adventures of Hercules to the tales of King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table, this book brings together a diverse range of myths from different cultures around the world. Each story is beautifully retold, capturing the imagination and wonder of readers of all ages. Whether it's the epic battles of gods and monsters or the inspiring journeys of brave heroes, this collection is sure to ignite a love for mythology and leave readers longing for more.

Genres: Mythology, Fantasy, Children's Literature
Themes: Heroism, Adventure, Mythical Creatures, Cultural Diversity
