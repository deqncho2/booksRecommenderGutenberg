Synopsis: 

In 'Life Sentence' by McConnell, James V., a young woman named Emma finds herself facing a devastating diagnosis that changes the course of her life forever. As she navigates the challenges of her new reality, she discovers unexpected friendships, unearths long-buried secrets, and learns the true meaning of love and forgiveness. With each passing day, Emma's strength and resilience are put to the test, forcing her to confront her deepest fears and make difficult choices that will shape her future. 

Genres: Drama, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Terminal illness, Friendship, Secrets, Love, Forgiveness
