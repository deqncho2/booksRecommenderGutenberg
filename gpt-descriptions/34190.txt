Synopsis: In the thrilling naval adventure 'H.M.S. ----' by John Graham Bower, a young officer finds himself aboard a mysterious ship with a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the ship's history, he uncovers a web of intrigue and danger that threatens not only his own life but the fate of the entire crew. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Courage and Honor
