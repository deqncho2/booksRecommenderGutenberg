Synopsis: 
In 'Snowbound for Christmas' by Edna I. MacKenzie, a young woman finds herself unexpectedly stranded in a remote cabin during a snowstorm. With no means of communication and no one to rely on but herself, she must navigate the challenges of survival and confront the secrets that lie hidden within the cabin's walls. As the days pass and the snow continues to fall, she discovers that she is not alone, and that the cabin holds more than just shelter from the storm. With danger lurking outside and mysteries unraveling inside, she must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Survival, Secrets, Isolation
