Synopsis: 
In 'Vicky Van' by Wells, Carolyn, a young woman named Vicky Van finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she inherits a luxurious yacht from a distant relative. As she embarks on a thrilling adventure, Vicky discovers that the yacht holds more secrets than she could have ever imagined. With danger lurking around every corner, she must navigate through a world of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth. Will Vicky be able to unravel the mysteries of the yacht before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Deception
