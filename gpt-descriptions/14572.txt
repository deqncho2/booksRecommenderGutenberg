Synopsis: 
In the small town of Fairview, a mysterious and enchanting presence disrupts the usual holiday routine. When a young lawyer arrives to settle the estate of a deceased client, he finds himself drawn to a beautiful woman who seems to be trapped between the realms of the living and the dead. As he delves deeper into her story, he uncovers a centuries-old curse that must be broken before Christmas Eve. With time running out, he must unravel the secrets of the past and find a way to save her before it's too late.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Redemption, Supernatural forces
