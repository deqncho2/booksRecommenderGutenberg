Synopsis: 
In 'The North Devon Coast', Charles G. Harper takes readers on a captivating journey along the stunning coastline of North Devon. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Harper explores the hidden gems, historical landmarks, and natural wonders that make this coastal region truly remarkable. From picturesque fishing villages to rugged cliffs and sandy beaches, the author paints a vivid picture of the area's beauty and charm. Along the way, he delves into the rich history, folklore, and legends that have shaped the North Devon Coast, offering readers a fascinating glimpse into the past. Whether you are a nature lover, history enthusiast, or simply seeking inspiration, this book will transport you to a world of breathtaking landscapes and captivating stories.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Coastal beauty, Local history, Folklore and legends
