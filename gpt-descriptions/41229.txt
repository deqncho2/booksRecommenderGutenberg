Synopsis: In the second volume of "Guy Deverell," the mysterious events surrounding the young lawyer's life continue to unfold. As Guy finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and deceit, he must navigate through a series of unexpected twists and turns that threaten to unravel his very existence. With the stakes higher than ever, the truth behind Guy's past and the sinister forces at play become increasingly elusive, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the final revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal, Supernatural elements
