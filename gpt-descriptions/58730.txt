Synopsis: In 'Miracle by Price' by Cox, Irving E., a small town is captivated by the arrival of a mysterious stranger who possesses an extraordinary ability. As the townspeople grapple with their own doubts and fears, they are forced to confront the power of belief and the potential for miracles. With a blend of suspense and heartwarming moments, this captivating story explores the transformative impact of faith and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Belief, Faith, Redemption, Community
