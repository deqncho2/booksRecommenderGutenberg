Synopsis: In 'Poker!' by Zora Neale Hurston, a group of friends gathers for a lively game of poker, but the stakes quickly escalate beyond money. As the night unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of each player is laid bare. With sharp wit and unexpected twists, Hurston takes readers on a thrilling journey through the highs and lows of this intense game of chance.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Deception, Power dynamics, Identity
