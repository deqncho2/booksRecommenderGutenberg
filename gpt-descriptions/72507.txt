Synopsis: In 'The Maugham Obsession' by Derleth, August, a young writer becomes captivated by the mysterious life and works of the renowned author W. Somerset Maugham. As he delves deeper into Maugham's world, he uncovers a series of enigmatic events and encounters that blur the line between reality and fiction. With each revelation, the writer's obsession intensifies, leading him down a path of intrigue, danger, and self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Literary Fiction

Themes: Obsession, Identity, Artistic Inspiration, Reality vs. Illusion
