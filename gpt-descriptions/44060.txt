Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of Carlitos: Mexican Mystery Stories #2' by Helen Randolph, a young detective named Carlitos finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a valuable artifact goes missing from a local museum. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Carlitos uncovers a web of secrets and hidden motives, leading him on a thrilling journey through the vibrant streets of Mexico. With danger lurking at every turn, Carlitos must use his wit and courage to solve the mystery before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Detective work, Cultural exploration, Courage
