Synopsis: In the midst of the Great Depression, the Joad family is forced to leave their Oklahoma farm and embark on a treacherous journey to California in search of a better life. Along the way, they encounter poverty, injustice, and the harsh realities of the American Dream. As they struggle to survive, the Joads find strength in their unity and resilience, facing both the cruelty of human nature and the power of compassion.

Genres: Historical fiction, Social commentary, Family drama

Themes: Poverty and inequality, Resilience and survival, The human spirit, Social justice
