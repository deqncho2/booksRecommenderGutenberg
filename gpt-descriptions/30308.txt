Synopsis: In 'Hanging by a Thread' by Garrett Randall, a young woman named Lily finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and danger when she discovers an ancient tapestry that holds a mysterious power. As she delves deeper into the tapestry's history, Lily becomes the target of a sinister organization determined to possess its hidden abilities. With her life hanging by a thread, Lily must unravel the truth behind the tapestry before it consumes her completely.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Secrets and hidden knowledge, Power and its consequences
