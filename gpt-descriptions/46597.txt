Synopsis: In Search of the Castaways is a thrilling adventure novel by Jules Verne that follows the journey of Captain Grant's children and their friends as they embark on a daring mission to find their missing father. With the help of a determined geographer, they set off on a perilous voyage across the globe, encountering treacherous landscapes, mysterious clues, and unexpected allies along the way. As they face countless challenges and dangers, the group's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test, leading them closer to the truth about Captain Grant's disappearance. Will they succeed in their quest and reunite with their beloved father?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Travel

Themes: Family bonds, Perseverance, Exploration
