Synopsis: 
In the vast and untamed wilderness of the American frontier, a diverse group of individuals find themselves bound together by fate and circumstance. As they navigate the treacherous prairie, they encounter danger, adventure, and unexpected alliances. Amidst the breathtaking landscapes and the clash of cultures, they must confront their own inner demons and grapple with the harsh realities of survival. With each step they take, the mysteries of the prairie unravel, revealing secrets that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Exploration of the American frontier, Survival, Cultural clashes, Identity, Nature's power and beauty
