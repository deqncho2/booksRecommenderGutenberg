Synopsis: In 'A Little Knowledge' by R. R. Winterbotham, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a shocking discovery that could change the course of history. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and conspiracies, where the line between knowledge and power blurs. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect his findings and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Knowledge and power, Conspiracy, Ethics
