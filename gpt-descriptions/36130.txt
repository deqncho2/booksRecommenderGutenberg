Synopsis: In 'The Campfire Girls on Station Island; Or, The Wireless from the Steam Yacht', a group of adventurous Campfire Girls find themselves on an island with a mysterious steam yacht. As they explore the island and uncover its secrets, they stumble upon a hidden wireless station that holds the key to a thrilling adventure. With danger lurking around every corner, the Campfire Girls must use their resourcefulness and teamwork to unravel the mysteries of the island and save the day.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Problem-solving
