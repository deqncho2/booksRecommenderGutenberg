Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Port Argent, secrets lie beneath the surface, waiting to be unearthed. When a mysterious newcomer arrives, the town's residents find themselves drawn into a web of intrigue, romance, and danger. As the past collides with the present, alliances are tested, and loyalties are questioned. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the truth becomes more elusive. Port Argent is a captivating tale that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
