Synopsis: In "Hubert's Wife: A Story for You" by Lee, Minnie Mary, a seemingly ordinary woman named Hubert's wife finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception. As she unravels the truth about her husband's mysterious past, she must confront her own inner demons and make difficult choices that will change her life forever. This gripping tale of love, betrayal, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Identity, Redemption
