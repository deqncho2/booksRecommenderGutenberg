Synopsis: In 'The Roman Question' by Edmond About, a young French journalist finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love when he is sent to Rome to report on the delicate balance between the Vatican and the Italian government. As he delves deeper into the heart of the Eternal City, he uncovers secrets that could shake the foundations of both Church and State. With danger lurking at every corner, he must navigate a treacherous landscape where loyalty and betrayal blur the lines of morality. Will he be able to uncover the truth without sacrificing his own principles?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Romance

Themes: Power struggles, Religion and politics, Love and sacrifice
