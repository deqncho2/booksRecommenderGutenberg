Synopsis: In 'Battling the Bighorn; or, The Aeroplane in the Rockies', a group of adventurous aviators embark on a daring expedition through the majestic Rocky Mountains. As they navigate treacherous terrain and face unexpected challenges, they must rely on their courage, ingenuity, and the power of their aeroplane to overcome the perils that await them. With breathtaking aerial stunts, heart-pounding action, and a race against time, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Bravery, Survival
