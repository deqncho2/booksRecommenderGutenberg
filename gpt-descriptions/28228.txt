Synopsis: In 'The Battle with the Slum', Jacob A. Riis takes readers on a gripping journey through the dark underbelly of New York City's slums. Through vivid descriptions and powerful storytelling, Riis exposes the harsh realities faced by the impoverished residents and the tireless efforts of those fighting to bring about change. From the overcrowded tenements to the corrupt political system, this eye-opening account sheds light on the desperate struggle for survival and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Non-fiction, Social documentary, Journalism

Themes: Poverty, Social inequality, Urbanization, Activism
