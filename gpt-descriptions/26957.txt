Synopsis: In 'Star Mother' by Robert F. Young, a young boy named David embarks on a thrilling adventure when he discovers a mysterious star-shaped pendant that grants him the ability to communicate with an extraterrestrial being. As David delves deeper into the secrets of the pendant, he finds himself caught in a cosmic battle between good and evil, where he must make difficult choices that will determine the fate of not only his own world but also the entire universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Coming of Age, Interstellar Communication
