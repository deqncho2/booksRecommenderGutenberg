Synopsis: 
In 'A Bold Stroke for a Husband: A Comedy in Five Acts', the spirited and clever heroine, Anne Lovely, finds herself in a predicament when her father arranges a marriage for her against her will. Determined to take control of her own destiny, Anne hatches a daring plan that involves disguises, mistaken identities, and a series of hilarious and unexpected events. As the plot unfolds, the audience is taken on a delightful journey filled with wit, romance, and a touch of mischief.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Theatre

Themes: Love and Marriage, Gender Roles, Independence
