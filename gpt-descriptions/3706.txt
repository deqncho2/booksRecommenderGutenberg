Synopsis: In 'The Valiant Runaways' by Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton, a young woman named Rosalie embarks on a daring adventure that takes her from the bustling streets of San Francisco to the rugged wilderness of the Sierra Nevada mountains. Faced with danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, Rosalie must summon her courage and resourcefulness to survive. As she navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters a cast of colorful characters, Rosalie's journey becomes a thrilling tale of resilience, self-discovery, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Survival, Independence, Identity
