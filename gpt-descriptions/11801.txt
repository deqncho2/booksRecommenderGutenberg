Synopsis: 

Step into the world of U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1950 January - June, a captivating compilation by the Library of Congress. This enthralling collection takes readers on a journey through the first half of 1950, exploring the fascinating world of copyright renewals in the United States. From literary masterpieces to iconic films, this book unveils the hidden stories behind the works that shaped the cultural landscape of the time. Delve into the pages of this remarkable archive and uncover the secrets and surprises that lie within.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Reference

Themes: Intellectual property, Cultural heritage, Creative expression
