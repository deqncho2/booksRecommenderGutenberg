Synopsis: In 'Chico: the Story of a Homing Pigeon' by Blanchard, Lucy Mansfield Blanchard, readers are taken on an extraordinary journey through the eyes of Chico, a remarkable homing pigeon. From his humble beginnings as a young chick, Chico's life is filled with adventure, danger, and unexpected friendships. As he navigates through various challenges, Chico's determination and loyalty are put to the test, ultimately leading him to discover the true meaning of home.

Genres: Adventure, Animal Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Finding Home
