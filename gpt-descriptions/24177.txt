Synopsis: In 'The Right Knock: A Story' by Van-Anderson, Helen, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, bringing with him a secret that threatens to unravel the lives of its inhabitants. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of deception and betrayal, they must confront their own dark pasts and make choices that will determine their future. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the power of secrets and the lengths people will go to protect them.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
