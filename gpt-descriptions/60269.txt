Synopsis: 
'Along the Mohawk Trail; Or, Boy Scouts on Lake Champlain' follows a group of adventurous Boy Scouts as they embark on a thrilling journey along the historic Mohawk Trail and explore the picturesque Lake Champlain. As they navigate through the wilderness, the Scouts encounter unexpected challenges, forge new friendships, and uncover hidden secrets that test their courage and resourcefulness. With danger lurking around every corner, the boys must rely on their scouting skills and teamwork to overcome obstacles and protect the natural beauty they hold dear.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Conservation
