Synopsis: 
In 'A practical treatise on coach-building historical and descriptive', James W. Burgess takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of coach-building. This comprehensive guide provides a wealth of information on the various trades and processes involved in creating these magnificent carriages. From the intricate details of design and construction to the proper maintenance and care of carriages, Burgess offers valuable insights and hints to enthusiasts and professionals alike. With historical anecdotes and descriptive passages, this treatise captures the essence of coach-building, captivating readers with its rich and detailed exploration of this timeless craft.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, History, Crafts and Hobbies

Themes: 
Craftsmanship, Historical preservation, Transportation
