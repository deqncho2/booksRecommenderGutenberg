Synopsis: In 'Bumper the White Rabbit in the Woods' by Walsh, George Ethelbert, follow the enchanting journey of Bumper, a curious white rabbit, as he ventures into the mysterious woods. Filled with unexpected encounters, magical creatures, and thrilling adventures, this heartwarming tale will captivate readers of all ages. Will Bumper find his way back home or discover a new world beyond the woods? Join him on this whimsical escapade to find out!

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Curiosity, Friendship, Exploration
