Synopsis: In 'You Can't Buy Eternity!' by Dwight V. Swain, a mysterious antique shop appears in a small town, offering its customers the chance to purchase items that promise eternal life. As the townspeople become obsessed with the idea of immortality, they soon discover that the consequences of their desires are far more sinister than they could have ever imagined. With the fate of the entire town hanging in the balance, a group of unlikely heroes must uncover the truth behind the shop and find a way to break the deadly cycle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Temptation, Mortality, Consequences
