Synopsis: 
'Japanese Fairy Tales' by Yei Theodora Ozaki is a captivating collection of enchanting and magical stories that transport readers to the mystical world of ancient Japan. From brave warriors and mischievous spirits to beautiful princesses and cunning animals, these timeless tales are filled with adventure, love, and the power of the human spirit. Each story unveils a unique aspect of Japanese culture and folklore, weaving together a tapestry of wonder and imagination that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: 
Fairy Tales, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: 
Courage, Love, Wisdom, Transformation
