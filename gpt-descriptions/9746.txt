Synopsis: In 'The Ashiel Mystery: A Detective Story', a small town is shaken by the sudden disappearance of a prominent resident. As the investigation unfolds, Detective Charles Bryce finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected twists. With each clue leading to more questions than answers, Bryce must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit to uncover the truth behind the Ashiel mystery.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Unraveling the Truth
