Synopsis: In 'The Ivory Snuff Box' by Frederic Arnold Kummer, a mysterious ivory snuff box becomes the center of a thrilling tale of love, betrayal, and international intrigue. When a young American woman inherits the snuff box from her late father, she soon discovers that it holds a secret that could change the course of history. As she delves deeper into the box's origins, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of deception and must navigate a treacherous world of spies and double agents. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and she must decide whom she can trust before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Love and betrayal, Espionage and international intrigue
