Synopsis: 
'America First: Patriotic Readings' is a captivating collection of essays, speeches, and historical documents that delve into the heart and soul of America. From the stirring words of founding fathers to the powerful orations of influential leaders, this anthology offers a profound exploration of the nation's history, values, and aspirations. Through thought-provoking narratives and insightful analysis, the book paints a vivid picture of the American spirit, inspiring readers to reflect on their own patriotism and the enduring principles that shape the United States.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Science

Themes: Patriotism, American identity, Nationalism, Civic duty
