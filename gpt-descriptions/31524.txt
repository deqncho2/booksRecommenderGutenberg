Synopsis: In 'The Price of the Prairie: A Story of Kansas' by Margaret Hill McCarter, readers are transported to the vast and untamed prairies of Kansas in the late 1800s. The story follows the lives of two young women, Ruth and Eleanor, who find themselves caught in the midst of the tumultuous events that shaped the state's history. As they navigate the challenges of love, loss, and the pursuit of their dreams, they must also confront the harsh realities of frontier life and the sacrifices it demands. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this captivating tale explores the price one must pay for the pursuit of a better future.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Western, Romance

Themes: Frontier life, Sacrifice, Pursuit of dreams
