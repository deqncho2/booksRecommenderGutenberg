Synopsis: In 'Recollections', David Christie Murray takes readers on a captivating journey through his own life, sharing intimate moments and encounters with literary giants such as George Meredith and Robert Louis Stevenson. Through a collection of original letters, including facsimiles, Murray offers a unique glimpse into the lives and minds of these renowned authors. As the pages turn, readers will be drawn into a world of literary brilliance, personal anecdotes, and the profound impact of these friendships. 

Genres: Autobiography, Literary Nonfiction, Biography

Themes: Friendship, Literary Influence, Personal Reflection, Intellectual Exchange
