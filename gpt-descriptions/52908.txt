Synopsis: In 'Clever Betsy: A Novel', we follow the captivating journey of Betsy, a young woman with an extraordinary talent for problem-solving and a determination to overcome the limitations imposed on her by society. Set in the late 19th century, Betsy's intelligence and resourcefulness lead her on a path filled with unexpected twists and turns, as she navigates through a world that often underestimates her abilities. From her humble beginnings to her encounters with love, betrayal, and personal growth, Betsy's story is a testament to the power of resilience and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Women's Fiction

Themes: Gender roles and expectations, societal constraints, self-discovery, resilience
