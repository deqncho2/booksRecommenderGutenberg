Synopsis:
In 'Love in '76: An Incident of the Revolution' by Oliver Bell Bunce, readers are transported back to the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. The story revolves around a young couple, whose love is tested amidst the chaos and uncertainty of war. As they navigate the treacherous waters of loyalty, sacrifice, and patriotism, their relationship becomes a microcosm of the larger struggle for freedom and independence. With its gripping narrative and vivid portrayal of historical events, this play offers a captivating glimpse into a pivotal moment in American history.

Genres: Historical drama, romance, war

Themes: Love and sacrifice, patriotism, the American Revolution
