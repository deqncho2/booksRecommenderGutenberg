Synopsis: In 'The Story of Fifty-Seven Cents' by Robert Shackleton, a seemingly insignificant amount of money becomes the catalyst for a captivating journey. When a young boy discovers a mysterious coin, he embarks on an adventure that takes him through unexpected twists and turns. As he delves deeper into the origins of the coin, he uncovers a hidden world filled with secrets, danger, and the power of human connection. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the mysteries that lie within the humble fifty-seven cents.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Discovery, Identity, Friendship
