Synopsis: In 'Back to Billabong' by Mary Grant Bruce, a young girl named Norah is sent to live with her aunt and uncle in the Australian outback. As she adjusts to her new surroundings, Norah discovers a hidden world of adventure, friendship, and the untamed beauty of the land. With the help of her newfound companions, she embarks on a journey that will test her courage and resilience, leading her to uncover secrets that will change her life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration of nature, Friendship, Self-discovery
