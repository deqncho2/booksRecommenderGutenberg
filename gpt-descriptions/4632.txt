Synopsis:
In 'The Good Time Coming' by Arthur, T. S. (Timothy Shay), a small town is torn apart by the impending Civil War, as families and friends find themselves on opposing sides. Amidst the chaos, a young girl named Hester must navigate the challenges of loyalty, love, and loss, as she grapples with the harsh realities of war. As the conflict escalates, Hester's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and courage that will leave readers captivated until the very end.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, War

Themes:
Loyalty, Love, Loss, Resilience, Self-Discovery
