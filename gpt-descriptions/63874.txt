Synopsis: In 'The Creatures That Time Forgot' by Ray Bradbury, a group of scientists embarks on a daring expedition to a remote island, where they stumble upon a hidden world filled with ancient and mysterious creatures. As they delve deeper into this prehistoric realm, they must confront not only the dangers of the past but also the ethical dilemmas that arise when faced with the power to control time itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Time travel, Evolution, Morality, Human hubris
