Synopsis: In 'A City Near Centaurus' by William R. Doede, a group of astronauts embarks on a mission to explore a distant planet named Centaurus. However, as they approach their destination, they encounter a mysterious city that challenges their understanding of reality and tests the limits of their sanity. As they delve deeper into the secrets of this enigmatic city, they must confront their own fears and desires, leading to a thrilling and thought-provoking journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Reality vs. Illusion, Human Nature
