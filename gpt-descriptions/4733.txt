Synopsis: In 'When Egypt Went Broke: A Novel' by Day, Holman, a captivating tale unfolds as a young archaeologist stumbles upon a hidden artifact that holds the key to Egypt's ancient secrets. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she becomes entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and a race against time. With vivid descriptions of the Egyptian landscape and a cast of intriguing characters, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Secrets and hidden knowledge, Betrayal, Survival
