Synopsis: 
In 'The Mystery of the Iron Box: A Ken Holt Mystery' by Campbell, Bruce, Ken Holt finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when he stumbles upon an iron box containing a cryptic message. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Ken discovers a web of secrets, hidden identities, and dangerous alliances. With the help of his trusty sidekick, Sandy Allen, Ken must unravel the clues and outsmart the cunning criminals before it's too late. This thrilling detective story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Crime and deception
