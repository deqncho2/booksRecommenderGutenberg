Synopsis: In 'The Indians' Last Fight; Or, The Dull Knife Raid' by Collins, Dennis, a gripping tale unfolds as a group of Native American warriors embark on a daring raid against their oppressors. Filled with heart-pounding action and unexpected twists, this story delves into the complexities of identity, justice, and the resilience of the human spirit. As the battle rages on, the characters are forced to confront their deepest fears and make choices that will shape their destinies forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Identity, Justice, Resilience
