Synopsis: In 'The Opium Habit' by Day, Horace B., a young doctor finds himself entangled in a dangerous world of addiction and deception when he becomes obsessed with a mysterious patient who suffers from an opium habit. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of society, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that could have dire consequences. This gripping tale of obsession and moral dilemmas will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Addiction, Morality, Deception
