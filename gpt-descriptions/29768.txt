Synopsis: In the August 1930 edition of 'Astounding Stories of Super-Science', prepare to be transported to a world where incredible inventions and mind-bending scientific discoveries await. From a thrilling tale of a man who gains extraordinary powers to a gripping adventure set on a distant planet, this collection of stories will keep you on the edge of your seat. Brace yourself for a journey through the realms of super-science that will leave you in awe and wonder.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Extraordinary abilities, Exploration, Technological advancements
