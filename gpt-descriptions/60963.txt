Synopsis: In 'The Impersonator' by Robert Wicks, a talented young actress named Lily is given the opportunity of a lifetime when she is asked to impersonate a famous celebrity. As she delves deeper into her role, Lily becomes entangled in a web of secrets, lies, and danger. With her own identity at stake, she must navigate the treacherous world of impersonation and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal
