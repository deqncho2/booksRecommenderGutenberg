Synopsis: 

'Prehistoric Indians of the Southwest' takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient civilizations that once thrived in the Southwest region of the United States. From the mysterious cliff dwellings of Mesa Verde to the intricate pottery of the Anasazi, this book delves into the rich history and cultural achievements of the prehistoric Indians who inhabited this fascinating landscape. Through meticulous research and vivid descriptions, Wormington paints a vivid picture of the daily lives, customs, and beliefs of these ancient peoples, offering a glimpse into a world that existed long before our own.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Archaeology

Themes: Prehistoric civilizations, Native American culture, Southwest history, Archaeological discoveries
