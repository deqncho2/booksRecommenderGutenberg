Synopsis: 

'Australasia Triumphant!: With the Australians and New Zealanders in the Great War on Land and Sea' by Arthur St. John Adcock is a gripping historical account that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the experiences of the brave soldiers from Australia and New Zealand during World War I. From the trenches of the Western Front to the treacherous waters of the Pacific, this book vividly captures the heroism, sacrifice, and resilience of these remarkable men. Through personal anecdotes, letters, and official records, Adcock paints a vivid picture of the horrors of war and the indomitable spirit of the ANZACs. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War literature, Military history

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Resilience, Camaraderie, National identity
