Synopsis: In "The Return of Peter Grimm: Novelised From the Play," the enigmatic Peter Grimm, a wealthy and influential man, passes away, leaving behind a web of secrets and unresolved conflicts. However, his unexpected return from the afterlife brings forth a series of supernatural events that challenge the beliefs and relationships of those closest to him. As the truth slowly unravels, the characters find themselves caught in a haunting tale of love, betrayal, and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Life after death, Family dynamics, Redemption, Love and forgiveness
