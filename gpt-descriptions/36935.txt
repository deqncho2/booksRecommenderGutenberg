Synopsis: In the small town of Barkhamsted, Connecticut, a mysterious light has been appearing in the night sky for centuries, captivating the locals and sparking countless legends. As the town's residents delve into the history of the light, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden connections that stretch back through generations. With each revelation, the line between reality and myth becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a thrilling journey of discovery and self-discovery for the town's inhabitants.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Legends and folklore, Family secrets, Identity and self-discovery
