Synopsis: In 'Watch (First 25,000 words)' by Robert J. Sawyer, a brilliant scientist named Caitlin Decter is given the extraordinary ability to see the World Wide Web as a three-dimensional space. As she explores this new virtual realm, she stumbles upon a mysterious presence that seems to be watching her every move. With the help of her friends and a team of experts, Caitlin embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind this enigmatic entity and its intentions. As she delves deeper into the digital world, she must confront the ethical dilemmas and potential dangers that arise from her newfound power.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Virtual reality, Artificial intelligence, Ethics, Identity
