Synopsis: 

In "Leyte: The Return to the Philippines" by Cannon M. Hamlin, readers are taken on a gripping journey through the harrowing events of World War II. Set against the backdrop of the Pacific theater, this historical account follows a group of soldiers as they embark on a daring mission to liberate the Philippines from Japanese occupation. As they face unimaginable challenges and witness the devastating consequences of war, the soldiers must summon their courage and resilience to fight for freedom and survival. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this book offers a captivating exploration of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Action/Adventure

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Sacrifice, Liberation, Survival
