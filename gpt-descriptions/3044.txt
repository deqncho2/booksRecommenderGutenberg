Synopsis: In the small town of Budmouth, a young woman named Cytherea Graye finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and forbidden love. As she navigates through a series of unexpected events, she becomes entwined with a mysterious man who holds a dark past. With her life hanging in the balance, Cytherea must make desperate choices to protect herself and those she holds dear, leading to a thrilling and unpredictable climax.

Genres: Gothic fiction, mystery, romance

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, the consequences of secrets and lies
