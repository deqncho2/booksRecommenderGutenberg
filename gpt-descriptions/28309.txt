Synopsis: In 'Christopher Hibbault, Roadmaker' by Marguerite Bryant, a young man named Christopher embarks on a journey that takes him through treacherous landscapes and tests his resilience. As he navigates the challenges of roadmaking, Christopher encounters a cast of intriguing characters and faces unexpected dangers. With each step, he discovers the true power of determination and the transformative nature of the road. This captivating tale explores the depths of human spirit and the pursuit of one's destiny.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Perseverance, Self-discovery, Fate
