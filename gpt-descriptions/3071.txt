Synopsis: In "The Golden Slipper, and Other Problems for Violet Strange," the enigmatic and resourceful Violet Strange finds herself entangled in a series of perplexing cases. From a missing slipper that holds a secret to a mysterious disappearance during a masquerade ball, Violet must use her keen intellect and intuition to solve these puzzling mysteries. As she delves deeper into each case, she uncovers a web of deceit, hidden motives, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Crime Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the power of observation and deduction, the complexities of human nature
