Synopsis: In 'Christmas in Ritual and Tradition, Christian and Pagan', Clement A. Miles delves into the fascinating history and origins of Christmas, exploring its roots in both Christian and pagan traditions. From ancient Roman festivals to medieval customs, Miles uncovers the rich tapestry of rituals and practices that have shaped the modern celebration of Christmas. This captivating exploration offers a fresh perspective on the holiday season, revealing the surprising connections between ancient beliefs and contemporary customs.

Genres: History, Anthropology, Folklore

Themes: Cultural traditions, Religious syncretism, Festivals and celebrations
