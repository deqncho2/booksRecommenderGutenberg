Synopsis: 
In 'The Erie Train Boy' by Horatio Alger, Jr., we follow the thrilling journey of a young boy named Mark Manning who finds himself caught up in a dangerous adventure. When Mark's father mysteriously disappears, leaving his family in financial distress, Mark takes it upon himself to find a way to support his mother and sister. With his quick thinking and determination, Mark secures a job as a train boy on the Erie Railroad. Little does he know, this job will lead him on a series of unexpected encounters, including encounters with a notorious gang of thieves. As Mark navigates the treacherous world of the railroad, he must rely on his wits and courage to overcome the challenges that come his way.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Perseverance, Courage, Family, Self-reliance
