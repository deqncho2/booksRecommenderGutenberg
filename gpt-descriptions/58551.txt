Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Lodrix, a young lake dweller named Lodrix embarks on a thrilling adventure to save his home from an ancient curse. With the help of his loyal friends, Lodrix must navigate treacherous waters, face mythical creatures, and uncover the secrets of his own identity. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of his world, Lodrix discovers that his destiny is intertwined with the fate of the entire lake. Will he be able to break the curse and restore peace to his beloved home?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Courage, Environmentalism
