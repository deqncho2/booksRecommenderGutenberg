Synopsis: 
In "Socrates" by Voltaire, the reader is transported to ancient Athens, where the renowned philosopher Socrates finds himself at the center of a gripping tale. As Socrates navigates the complexities of his time, he encounters a myriad of fascinating characters, engages in thought-provoking dialogues, and faces unexpected challenges that test his unwavering commitment to truth and wisdom. With wit, humor, and a touch of suspense, Voltaire masterfully weaves a captivating narrative that explores the profound impact of Socrates' teachings on society and the enduring power of intellectual inquiry.

Genres: 
Historical fiction, Philosophical fiction, Satire

Themes: 
Philosophy, Truth, Wisdom, Morality, Society
