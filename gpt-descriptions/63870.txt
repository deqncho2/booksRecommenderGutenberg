Synopsis: 
In 'A Southern Woman's Story' by Pember, Phoebe Yates, we are transported to the antebellum South, where we follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Katherine. Born into a privileged plantation family, Katherine's life takes an unexpected turn when she finds herself torn between her duty to her family and her desire for independence. As the Civil War looms, Katherine must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations, ultimately discovering her own strength and resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Gender Roles, Social Class, Resilience
