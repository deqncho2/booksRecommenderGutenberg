Synopsis: 

In 'Strive and Thrive; or, Stories for the Example and Encouragement of the Young' by Anonymous, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of inspiring stories. Each tale follows the lives of young individuals who face various challenges and obstacles, but ultimately find the strength and determination to overcome them. From tales of perseverance and resilience to stories of friendship and self-discovery, this book offers a rich tapestry of narratives that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to see how each protagonist's journey unfolds.

Genres: Fiction, Inspirational, Coming-of-age

Themes: Perseverance, Resilience, Friendship, Self-discovery
