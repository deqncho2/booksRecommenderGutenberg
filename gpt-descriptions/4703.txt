Synopsis: 
In 'Flower of the North: A Modern Romance' by James Oliver Curwood, a young woman named Joan is thrust into a thrilling adventure when she finds herself stranded in the Canadian wilderness. As she navigates the harsh and unforgiving landscape, she encounters a mysterious man named Philip, who becomes her unlikely companion. Together, they must face treacherous obstacles and confront their own inner demons, all while a powerful and dangerous secret threatens to tear them apart. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this modern romance will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Love and Sacrifice, Redemption
