Synopsis: In 'A Sheaf of Roses' by Elizabeth Gordon, a mysterious package arrives on the doorstep of a young woman named Emily, containing a collection of dried roses. As she delves into the history behind these flowers, Emily uncovers a captivating tale of love, loss, and secrets that spans generations. With each rose revealing a new chapter of the past, Emily becomes entangled in a web of family secrets and hidden desires, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected connections.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Love and loss, Self-discovery
