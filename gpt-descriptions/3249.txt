Synopsis: 
In 'The Major' by Connor Ralph, a retired military officer finds himself entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger when he is unexpectedly drawn back into the world of espionage. As he navigates through a treacherous landscape of deceit and betrayal, he must confront his own demons and make choices that will determine the fate of not only himself but also those he holds dear. With pulse-pounding action and unexpected twists, 'The Major' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Espionage

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Duty, Identity
