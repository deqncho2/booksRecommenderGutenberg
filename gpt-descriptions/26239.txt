Synopsis: In "The Forester's Daughter: A Romance of the Bear-Tooth Range," the rugged and untamed beauty of the Bear-Tooth Range serves as the backdrop for a captivating love story. Set in the late 19th century, the novel follows the life of Nellie, the spirited and independent daughter of a forester. As she navigates the challenges of living in the wilderness, Nellie finds herself torn between two suitors: the charismatic and adventurous cowboy, Jack, and the sophisticated and wealthy city-dweller, Arthur. Amidst the breathtaking landscapes and the clash of social classes, Nellie must make a choice that will shape her destiny and reveal the true nature of love.

Genres: Romance, Western, Adventure

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Nature and wilderness, Social class and identity
