Synopsis: 
'Building and Flying an Aeroplane: A practical handbook covering the design, construction, and operation of aeroplanes and gliders' is an engaging and comprehensive guide that takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of aviation. Written by Charles B. Hayward, an experienced aviator, this book provides step-by-step instructions and expert advice on designing, building, and piloting aeroplanes and gliders. From understanding the principles of flight to constructing a sturdy aircraft, Hayward's practical handbook equips readers with the knowledge and skills needed to take to the skies. Filled with captivating anecdotes and insightful tips, this book is a must-read for aviation enthusiasts, aspiring pilots, and anyone fascinated by the wonders of flight.

Genres: Non-fiction, Aviation, How-to

Themes: Aviation technology, Flight mechanics, Pilot training
