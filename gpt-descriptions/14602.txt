Synopsis: In 'War Brides: A Play in One Act', Wentworth, Marion Craig takes us on an emotional journey as we meet a group of women who have married soldiers during wartime. Set in a small town, the play explores the challenges and sacrifices these women face as they navigate their new lives and relationships. As secrets are revealed and tensions rise, the women must confront their fears and find strength in each other. With poignant dialogue and powerful moments of vulnerability, 'War Brides' offers a glimpse into the resilience and resilience of these remarkable women.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Female camaraderie, Resilience in the face of adversity
