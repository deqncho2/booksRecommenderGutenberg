Synopsis: In "Helen of Troy" by Andrew Lang, the captivating tale of the legendary beauty Helen unfolds, revealing the intricate web of love, power, and destiny that surrounds her. Set against the backdrop of ancient Greece, this enthralling narrative explores the fateful events that lead to the Trojan War, as well as the profound impact Helen's allure has on the lives of those around her. As the story unfolds, readers are transported into a world of passion, betrayal, and the timeless struggle between duty and desire. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mythology, Romance

Themes: Beauty and its consequences, Fate and destiny, Love and desire, Power and politics
