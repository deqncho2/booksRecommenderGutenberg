Synopsis: In 'The Dominant Strain' by Anna Chapin Ray, a young woman named Ruth Thorne finds herself caught between two worlds - the bustling city life of New York and the tranquil beauty of a small New England town. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Ruth must confront her own desires and make difficult choices that will shape her future. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this captivating novel explores the clash between tradition and modernity, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Love and Relationships, Societal Expectations
