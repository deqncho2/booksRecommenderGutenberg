Synopsis: In 'The Club at Crow's Corner' by Otis James, a group of misfit kids stumble upon a hidden clubhouse in the woods, where they embark on a thrilling adventure filled with secrets, mysteries, and unexpected friendships. As they uncover the clubhouse's past and its connection to their own lives, they must confront their fears and work together to protect their newfound sanctuary. With each page, the story unfolds with suspense, humor, and heartwarming moments that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Friendship

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
