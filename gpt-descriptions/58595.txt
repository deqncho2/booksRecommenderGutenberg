Synopsis: In 'The Golden Boys Rescued by Radio', a group of adventurous boys stumble upon a mysterious radio signal that leads them on a thrilling journey filled with danger and intrigue. As they unravel the secrets behind the signal, they find themselves caught up in a race against time to save something precious. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding moments, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
