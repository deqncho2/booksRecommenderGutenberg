Synopsis: 
In 'Into the Sun' by John L. Chapman, a brilliant astrophysicist named Dr. Amelia Brooks discovers a mysterious anomaly in the sun's behavior that could have catastrophic consequences for Earth. As she delves deeper into her research, she uncovers a shocking secret that puts her life in danger and forces her to make a difficult choice. With time running out, Dr. Brooks must race against the clock to save humanity from an unimaginable disaster.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific discovery, Sacrifice, Survival
