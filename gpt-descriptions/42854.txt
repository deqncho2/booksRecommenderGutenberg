Synopsis: 

In Volume 17, Slice 4 of the renowned Encyclopaedia Britannica, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of "Magnetite" to "Malt." This volume delves into the intricate properties and uses of magnetite, a mineral with remarkable magnetic qualities, before exploring the art of malting and its significance in the brewing industry. From the exploration of magnetism to the secrets behind the perfect malt, this volume offers a wealth of knowledge and insights that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Reference, Science, History

Themes: Magnetism, Minerals, Brewing, Industrial Processes
