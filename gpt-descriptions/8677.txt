Synopsis:
In "Behind a Mask; or, a Woman's Power" by Louisa May Alcott, a mysterious and captivating woman named Jean Muir enters the wealthy Coventry family's household as a governess. With her cunning intelligence and beguiling charm, Jean quickly becomes the center of attention, captivating the hearts of the family members one by one. However, as her true intentions slowly unravel, the Coventrys find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, manipulation, and hidden desires. This gripping tale explores the power dynamics between men and women, the masks we wear to hide our true selves, and the consequences of unchecked ambition.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, romance

Themes: Power and manipulation, gender roles and expectations, identity and deception
