Synopsis: In 'Earth Alert!' by Neville Kris, a renowned scientist stumbles upon a shocking discovery that threatens the very existence of humanity. As he races against time to unravel the truth, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy, danger, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate treacherous terrain and make unimaginable sacrifices to save the planet from impending doom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Environmentalism, Survival, Moral Responsibility
