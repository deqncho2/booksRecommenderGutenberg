Synopsis: 

"The Escape of a Princess Pat" is a gripping true story that recounts the capture and imprisonment of Corporal Edwards, a member of the Princess Patricia's Canadian Light Infantry, during World War I. The book takes readers on a thrilling journey as Edwards shares his harrowing experiences of being held captive for fifteen long months in Germany. Faced with unimaginable challenges and constant danger, Edwards eventually devises a daring plan to escape and make his way to freedom in Holland. This captivating account of courage, resilience, and determination will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome of Edwards' incredible escape.

Genres: Memoir, War, History

Themes: Survival, Bravery, Resilience, Escape
