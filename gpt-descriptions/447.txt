Synopsis: In the gritty streets of New York City, Maggie, a young girl living in poverty, struggles to find her place in a world filled with despair and hardship. As she navigates through the harsh realities of her environment, Maggie is faced with difficult choices that will shape her future. Stephen Crane's powerful novel delves into the dark corners of society, exploring the human condition and the consequences of societal constraints.

Genres: Realism, Naturalism, Social Commentary

Themes: Poverty, Morality, Social Inequality, Survival
