Synopsis: In 'A Lincoln Conscript' by Homer Greene, a young man named John is faced with a life-altering decision when he is conscripted into the Union Army during the American Civil War. As he grapples with the consequences of his choices, John embarks on a journey that will test his loyalty, courage, and sense of self. With the backdrop of a nation torn apart by war, this gripping tale explores the complexities of duty, sacrifice, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Duty, Sacrifice, Identity, Freedom
