Synopsis: In 'Booby Prize' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist invents a groundbreaking device that could revolutionize the world. However, when his invention falls into the wrong hands, it becomes a dangerous weapon with the potential to cause widespread destruction. As the scientist races against time to reclaim his creation, he must navigate a treacherous web of intrigue, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, the stakes have never been higher.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Technology, Power, Morality
