Synopsis: In "Snowed Up; or, The Sportman's Club in the Mountains" by Castlemon, Harry, a group of adventurous friends embark on a winter hunting trip in the mountains. However, their journey takes an unexpected turn when they find themselves trapped by a sudden snowstorm. As they struggle to survive in the harsh conditions, they must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and determination to make it out alive. With danger lurking at every turn, the friends must face their fears and push their limits to overcome the challenges that nature throws their way.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Action

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Nature's Power
