Synopsis: 

"Iron Making in the Olden Times: as instanced in the Ancient Mines, Forges, and Furnaces of The Forest of Dean" takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical iron-making industry in the Forest of Dean. Author H. G. Nicholls delves into the fascinating world of ancient mines, forges, and furnaces, uncovering the secrets and techniques employed by the ironworkers of old. From the discovery of iron ore to the intricate process of smelting and forging, this book offers a vivid portrayal of the challenges and triumphs faced by those involved in the iron-making trade. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Nicholls brings to life the rich history and technological advancements that shaped the Forest of Dean's iron industry.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Industrial history, Archaeology

Themes: Industrialization, Technological advancements, Historical preservation
