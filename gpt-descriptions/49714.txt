Synopsis: 
In 'At Bay' by Alexander, Mrs., a young woman finds herself trapped in a secluded mansion with a mysterious and dangerous man. As she desperately tries to escape, she uncovers dark secrets that threaten her life and sanity. With each twist and turn, the tension builds, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, wondering who will emerge victorious in this deadly game of cat and mouse.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Deception, Psychological manipulation
