Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter weekly No. 186, July 21, 1900: Nick Carter rescues a daughter; or, The junior partner's strange behavior,' renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when the daughter of a wealthy businessman goes missing. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Carter discovers that his own junior partner's behavior holds the key to unraveling the mystery. With time running out, Carter must navigate a web of deceit and danger to rescue the missing daughter and uncover the truth behind his partner's strange actions.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Loyalty, Deception, Family Dynamics
