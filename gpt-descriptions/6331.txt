Synopsis: 
In 'The Pillars of the House; Or, Under Wode, Under Rode, Vol. 1', Charlotte M. Yonge takes us on a captivating journey into the lives of the Morville family. Set in the idyllic English countryside, the story follows the trials and triumphs of the Morvilles as they navigate the complexities of family, love, and duty. As secrets unravel and unexpected events unfold, the reader is drawn deeper into the lives of these fascinating characters, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth that lies beneath the surface.

Genres: 
Romance, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: 
Family dynamics, Duty and responsibility, Love and sacrifice
