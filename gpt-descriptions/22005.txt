Synopsis: In 'How to Add Ten Years to your Life and to Double Its Satisfactions', author S. S. Curry presents a captivating guide that unveils the secrets to not only extending one's lifespan but also enhancing the quality of life. Through a combination of practical advice, insightful anecdotes, and thought-provoking exercises, Curry offers a unique perspective on how to unlock the true potential of our minds and bodies. This enlightening journey explores various aspects of personal growth, from cultivating positive habits to fostering meaningful relationships, ultimately empowering readers to live a longer, more fulfilling life.

Genres: Self-help, Personal Development, Health and Wellness

Themes: Longevity, Personal Growth, Happiness, Mind-Body Connection
