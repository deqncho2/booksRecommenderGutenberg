Synopsis: 
In "Different Girls," a group of seemingly ordinary high school girls find themselves drawn together by a mysterious force that reveals their extraordinary abilities. As they navigate the challenges of adolescence, they must also learn to harness their unique powers and uncover the truth behind their shared destiny. With secrets, unexpected alliances, and thrilling adventures, this captivating story explores the complexities of friendship, identity, and the power within oneself.

Genres: Young Adult, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Power, Destiny
