Synopsis: In 'The Secret of Toni' by Molly Elliot Seawell, a young woman named Toni finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change her life forever. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Toni must navigate a dangerous world of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With her determination and courage, she embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth, all while facing personal challenges that test her resilience and strength. Will Toni unravel the secret before it consumes her, or will she become entangled in a dangerous game with no way out?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Betrayal and trust, Personal growth and resilience
