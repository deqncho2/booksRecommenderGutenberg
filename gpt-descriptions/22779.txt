Synopsis: In 'The False Chevalier: or, The Lifeguard of Marie Antoinette', a mysterious and captivating stranger arrives at the court of Marie Antoinette, claiming to be a nobleman and a loyal supporter of the queen. As he gains the trust and admiration of the royal court, whispers of his true identity begin to circulate, leading to a thrilling tale of deception, loyalty, and the power of appearances. Set against the backdrop of the French Revolution, this historical novel takes readers on a suspenseful journey through the opulent halls of Versailles and the dangerous streets of Paris.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Deception, Power
