Synopsis:
'Liza of Lambeth' is a captivating novel that takes readers into the gritty and vibrant world of Lambeth, a working-class neighborhood in London. The story revolves around Liza Kemp, a young and spirited girl who dreams of escaping her mundane life and finding true love. As she navigates the challenges of poverty, family dynamics, and societal expectations, Liza's journey becomes a compelling exploration of human desires, sacrifices, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres:
Realistic fiction, Coming-of-age, Social commentary

Themes:
Love and relationships, Social class and inequality, Personal freedom and self-discovery
