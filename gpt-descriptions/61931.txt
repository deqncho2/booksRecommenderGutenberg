Synopsis: 

In "Narrative of the Most Extraordinary and Distressing Shipwreck of the Whale-ship Essex," Owen Chase recounts a harrowing tale of survival and tragedy on the high seas. Set in the early 19th century, the story follows the ill-fated journey of the whale-ship Essex, which is attacked and destroyed by a massive sperm whale in the Pacific Ocean. As the ship sinks, the captain and crew find themselves stranded in open boats, facing unimaginable challenges and enduring unparalleled suffering for a grueling ninety-three days at sea. 

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Non-fiction

Themes: Man vs. Nature, Resilience, Human Spirit, Morality
