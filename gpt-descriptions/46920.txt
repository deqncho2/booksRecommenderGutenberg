Synopsis: 
In "The Pony Rider Boys on the Blue Ridge; or, A Lucky Find in the Carolina Mountains," the adventurous group of friends embarks on a thrilling journey through the picturesque Carolina Mountains. As they navigate treacherous trails and encounter unexpected challenges, the boys stumble upon a remarkable discovery that leads them on a quest filled with mystery and excitement. With their unwavering determination and resourcefulness, the Pony Rider Boys must unravel the secrets hidden within the mountains and overcome the obstacles that stand in their way.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Perseverance
