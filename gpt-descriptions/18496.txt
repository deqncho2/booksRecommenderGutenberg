Synopsis: 
In 'Big Brother' by Annie F. Johnston, a young girl named Betty finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when her older brother, Jack, suddenly disappears. Determined to uncover the truth, Betty embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes her through secret passageways, hidden rooms, and unexpected alliances. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Betty discovers shocking secrets about her family and the true nature of her brother's disappearance. With danger lurking around every corner, Betty must rely on her wit and courage to unravel the truth and bring her brother home.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Family

Themes: Family bonds, Loyalty, Courage, Secrets
