Synopsis: 

In 'Memorials of the Sea: My Father', William Scoresby Jr. delves into the extraordinary life of his late father, William Scoresby Sr., a renowned adventurer and seafarer from Whitby. Through a collection of records and anecdotes, the younger Scoresby paints a vivid portrait of his father's thrilling experiences at sea, including his daring encounters with treacherous storms, encounters with exotic wildlife, and his relentless pursuit of scientific knowledge. From the icy waters of the Arctic to the vast expanses of the Atlantic, readers will be captivated by the gripping tales of danger, discovery, and resilience that shaped the life of this remarkable man.

Genres: Biography, Adventure, Maritime

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Scientific Inquiry
