Synopsis: 
'Party Sweets' by Blake, Mary is a tantalizing tale that follows the life of Sarah, a young woman with a passion for baking. When she is unexpectedly invited to cater a high-profile party, Sarah's world is turned upside down as she navigates the glamorous and cutthroat world of the elite. As she strives to create the perfect desserts and impress her demanding clients, Sarah finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, rivalries, and unexpected romance. With each delectable treat she creates, Sarah's journey becomes a delectable blend of ambition, love, and the pursuit of sweet success.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Love, Success, Secrets
