Synopsis: 
In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 147, November 4, 1914', readers are transported to the vibrant and satirical world of early 20th-century London. This volume of the renowned magazine offers a collection of witty and humorous articles, cartoons, and illustrations that provide a unique perspective on the social and political issues of the time. From clever commentary on the war to amusing observations on everyday life, this edition of Punch promises to entertain and enlighten readers with its sharp wit and clever insights.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Magazine

Themes: Social commentary, Politics, Everyday life
