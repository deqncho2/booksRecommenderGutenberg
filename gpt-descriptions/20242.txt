Synopsis: 

Left on Labrador; or, The cruise of the Schooner-yacht "Curlew": As Recorded by "Wash" is an enthralling adventure novel that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous waters of Labrador. The story follows the daring exploits of the crew aboard the Schooner-yacht "Curlew" as they navigate through dangerous storms, encounter mysterious islands, and face unexpected challenges. Told through the eyes of "Wash," a young sailor on board, the narrative is filled with suspense, unexpected twists, and moments of heart-pounding excitement. As the crew delves deeper into the unknown, they uncover secrets that will test their courage and resilience. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Nautical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Camaraderie, Discovery
