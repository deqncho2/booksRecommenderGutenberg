Synopsis: In "The Story of Rome, From the Earliest Times to the Death of Augustus, Told to Boys and Girls," Mary Macgregor takes readers on a captivating journey through the rise and fall of one of the greatest civilizations in history. From the legendary founding of Rome by Romulus and Remus to the grandeur of the Roman Empire under Augustus, this book brings to life the fascinating stories of emperors, warriors, and everyday citizens who shaped the destiny of Rome. Through vivid storytelling and historical anecdotes, Macgregor paints a vivid picture of the triumphs, struggles, and enduring legacy of the Roman civilization.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: Power and Politics, Heroism and Sacrifice, Legacy and Influence
