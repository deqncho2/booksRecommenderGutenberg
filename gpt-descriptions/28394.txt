Synopsis: In "William Penn" by Hodges, George, embark on a captivating journey through the life of a remarkable man who defied societal norms and fought for religious freedom. This gripping biography delves into the trials and triumphs of William Penn, a visionary leader who founded Pennsylvania and left an indelible mark on American history. From his early struggles to his pivotal role in shaping a new nation, this book unveils the extraordinary life of a true pioneer.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Religious freedom, Leadership, Social justice
