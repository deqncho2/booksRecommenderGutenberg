Synopsis: In 'Pemrose Lorry, Radio Amateur' by Isabel Hornibrook, a young woman named Pemrose discovers a hidden talent for amateur radio during a time when women's roles were strictly defined. As she delves deeper into the world of radio communication, Pemrose finds herself caught up in a thrilling adventure that challenges societal norms and tests her courage. With each transmission, she uncovers secrets and forms unexpected connections, ultimately leading her to question the boundaries of her own potential. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Gender roles, Self-discovery, Breaking societal norms
