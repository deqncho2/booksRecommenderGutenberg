Synopsis: 
In the small town of Tours, the arrival of a new vicar named Félix de Vandenesse causes quite a stir among the local residents. The charming and handsome vicar quickly becomes the center of attention, captivating both men and women alike with his charismatic personality. As the townspeople become increasingly infatuated with Félix, they begin to reveal their true selves, exposing their hidden desires, ambitions, and secrets. Amidst the chaos and scandal that ensues, Félix finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and deceit, ultimately leading to unexpected consequences for everyone involved.

Genres: 
Drama, Satire, Psychological Fiction

Themes: 
Desire, Ambition, Hypocrisy, Social Status
