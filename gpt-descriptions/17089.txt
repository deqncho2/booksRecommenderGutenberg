Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Mrs. Tittlemouse' by Beatrix Potter, readers are introduced to a charming little mouse named Mrs. Tittlemouse who lives in a cozy home in the countryside. However, her peaceful life is constantly disrupted by uninvited guests who make a mess of her tidy abode. Determined to maintain order, Mrs. Tittlemouse embarks on a delightful adventure to reclaim her home from these unwanted intruders. Along the way, she encounters a variety of quirky characters and faces unexpected challenges that test her patience and resourcefulness. Will Mrs. Tittlemouse succeed in restoring harmony to her humble dwelling?

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Animal fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Home and belonging
