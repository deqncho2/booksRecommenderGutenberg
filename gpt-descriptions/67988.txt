Synopsis: 
In 'The Vortex: A Play in Three Acts', we are introduced to a glamorous and decadent world of the 1920s, where a mother and son find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, desires, and destructive relationships. As the characters navigate through their own personal struggles, the play delves into the complexities of love, addiction, and societal expectations, ultimately leading to a shocking climax that will leave the audience questioning the nature of human relationships.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Love and Desire, Addiction, Social Expectations, Family Dynamics
