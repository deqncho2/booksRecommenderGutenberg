Synopsis: In 'Demos' by George Gissing, a young man named Richard Mutimer finds himself caught between his aspirations for a better life and the harsh realities of his working-class existence. As he navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and social class, Richard becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal sacrifice. Set against the backdrop of late 19th-century England, this gripping tale explores the struggles and triumphs of individuals striving for social justice and personal fulfillment.

Genres: Fiction, Social Realism, Historical Fiction

Themes: Social class, Politics, Love and relationships, Personal ambition, Social justice
