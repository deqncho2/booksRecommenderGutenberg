Synopsis: In "In the Bishop's Carriage," a young woman named Sally is thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when she becomes the personal secretary to a wealthy bishop. As she delves deeper into her employer's affairs, Sally uncovers a web of secrets and hidden motives that could have far-reaching consequences. With danger lurking around every corner, Sally must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those she cares about.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Power, Redemption
