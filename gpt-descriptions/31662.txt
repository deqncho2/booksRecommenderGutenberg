Synopsis: In 'Their Son; The Necklace' by Eduardo Zamacois, a seemingly ordinary family is torn apart when a mysterious necklace enters their lives. As the necklace's power begins to unravel their deepest secrets, the family is forced to confront their past and make difficult choices that will forever change their future. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this thrilling tale explores the complexities of family dynamics and the consequences of hidden desires.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity, Redemption
