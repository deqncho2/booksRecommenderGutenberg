Synopsis: 

In 'Travels through the states of North America, and the provinces of Upper and Lower Canada, during the years 1795, 1796, and 1797', Isaac Weld takes readers on an enthralling journey through the diverse landscapes and vibrant cultures of North America. As he traverses the vast territories, Weld encounters fascinating characters, witnesses breathtaking natural wonders, and delves into the social and political fabric of the time. From bustling cities to remote wilderness, this captivating travelogue offers a vivid and immersive exploration of the continent during a pivotal period in its history.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Diversity, Social and Political Observations
