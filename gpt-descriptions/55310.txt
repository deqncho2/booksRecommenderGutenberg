Synopsis: In 'The Lone Inn: A Mystery' by Fergus Hume, a young woman mysteriously disappears after checking into a remote inn. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, suspects emerge, and the truth becomes increasingly elusive. With a web of intrigue and a cast of enigmatic characters, this gripping mystery will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity, Secrets
