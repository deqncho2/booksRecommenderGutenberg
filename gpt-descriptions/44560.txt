Synopsis: In 'Three Courses and a Dessert: Comprising Three Sets of Tales, West Country, Irish, and Legal; and a Melange', William Clarke takes readers on a captivating journey through three distinct sets of tales. From the enchanting landscapes of the West Country to the lively anecdotes of the Irish, and the intriguing world of the legal system, this collection offers a delightful blend of storytelling. With each course, readers will be treated to a delectable dessert, a melange of tales that will leave them craving for more. 

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Anthology

Themes: Regional culture, Legal system, Human nature
