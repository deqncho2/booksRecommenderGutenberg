Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 135. April 10, 1915; Straight to the Goal; Or, Nick Carter's Queer Challenge', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when he receives a mysterious challenge. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Carter uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With time running out, he must navigate through a series of twists and turns to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Justice
