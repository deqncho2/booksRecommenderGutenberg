Synopsis: In 'Running the Gauntlet: A Novel' by Edmund Yates, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of Victorian London. The story follows the life of a young protagonist who finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of secrets, deceit, and betrayal. As he navigates through the treacherous streets, he must confront his own demons and make choices that will determine his fate. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Survival
