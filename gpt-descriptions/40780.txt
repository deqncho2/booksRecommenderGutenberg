Synopsis: 

'Our Legal Heritage: King AEthelbert - King George III, 600 A.D. - 1776' by Reilly, S. A. takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of English law from the time of King AEthelbert to King George III. This meticulously researched and engagingly written book explores the fascinating legal developments, landmark cases, and influential figures that shaped the legal system of England over the course of nearly twelve centuries. From the establishment of the earliest legal codes to the struggles for justice and the triumphs of legal reform, this book offers a comprehensive and enlightening exploration of the legal heritage that has had a profound impact on the modern world.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Legal History

Themes: Legal evolution, Justice, Legal reform
