Synopsis: In 'Perez the Mouse' by Coloma, Luis, follow the captivating journey of a small and curious mouse named Perez as he embarks on an extraordinary adventure. Perez finds himself in a world filled with unexpected challenges and encounters with fascinating characters. As he navigates through this whimsical and enchanting tale, Perez discovers the true meaning of friendship, courage, and the power of believing in oneself.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-belief
