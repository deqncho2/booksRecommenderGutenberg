Synopsis: In 'The Laurel Bush: An Old-Fashioned Love Story', we are transported to the enchanting English countryside where we follow the lives of two young women, Lucy and Phyllis, as they navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations. As their paths intertwine with a cast of intriguing characters, including the enigmatic Mr. Fordyce, secrets are revealed, hearts are tested, and true love is put to the ultimate challenge. With its captivating storytelling and richly drawn characters, this timeless tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, yearning to uncover the fate of these unforgettable characters.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Social Expectations, Female Empowerment
