Synopsis: In "King Arthur and His Knights" by Warren, Maude Radford, embark on a captivating journey into the legendary world of King Arthur and his valiant knights. Follow the young Arthur as he pulls the sword from the stone, proving his destiny as the rightful king of Britain. Witness the formation of the Knights of the Round Table, a fellowship bound by honor and chivalry. Experience thrilling adventures, epic battles, and encounters with enchanting characters such as Merlin the wizard and the enchantress Morgan le Fay. Discover the timeless tales of bravery, love, and betrayal that shape the legend of King Arthur and his knights.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Destiny, Love, Betrayal
