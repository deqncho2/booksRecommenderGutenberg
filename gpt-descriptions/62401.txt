Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the bustling city of Norwich through the pages of Mason's Norwich General and Commercial Directory & Handbook. This meticulously crafted guide takes readers on a journey through the vibrant hamlets of Earlham, Eaton, Heigham, Hellesdon, Lakenham, Pockthorpe, Thorpe, Trowse, Carrow, and Bracondale. Discover the hidden gems, local businesses, and historical landmarks that shaped the city's identity during the time of its publication. From the charming streets to the thriving commercial scene, this directory offers a fascinating glimpse into the past, providing a valuable resource for both locals and visitors alike.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Local History, Reference

Themes: Local commerce, Urban development, Historical preservation
