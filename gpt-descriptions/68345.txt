Synopsis: 
"On the Anzac Trail: Being extracts from the diary of a New Zealand sapper" takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of a New Zealand sapper during World War I. Through vivid and heartfelt diary entries, the reader is transported to the battlefields of Gallipoli and the Western Front, experiencing the camaraderie, hardships, and sacrifices of the soldiers. As the sapper faces the brutal realities of war, the diary entries also reveal moments of hope, resilience, and the unbreakable spirit of the Anzacs.

Genres: Historical fiction, War literature, Memoir

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Resilience, Human spirit
