Synopsis:
In the second volume of "Margery (Gred): A Tale Of Old Nuremberg," the captivating story continues as Margery, a young and spirited girl, faces new challenges and adventures in the medieval city of Nuremberg. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Margery finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and betrayals that threaten to unravel her world. With the backdrop of a vibrant and bustling city, this volume delves deeper into Margery's journey of self-discovery and the choices she must make to protect those she holds dear.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes:
Love and Sacrifice, Social Expectations, Betrayal and Redemption
