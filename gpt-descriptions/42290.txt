Synopsis: 
'A Hundred and Seventy Chinese Poems' is a captivating collection of ancient Chinese poetry that takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the rich tapestry of Chinese culture and history. From the lyrical verses of renowned poets to the hidden gems of lesser-known writers, this anthology offers a diverse range of emotions, landscapes, and philosophical musings. Each poem is a window into a different era, revealing the beauty, wisdom, and complexities of Chinese society. Whether exploring themes of love, nature, or the human condition, these poems evoke a sense of timelessness and invite readers to immerse themselves in the enchanting world of Chinese literature.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology, Cultural

Themes: Love, Nature, Philosophy, History, Human Condition
