Synopsis: In "The Old Miracle Plays of England," Syrett takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of medieval English drama. From the awe-inspiring biblical stories to the lively and humorous depictions of everyday life, these ancient plays come alive once again. Witness the dramatic retelling of Adam and Eve's fall from grace, the epic battles of saints and demons, and the miraculous tales of redemption. Syrett's meticulous research and insightful commentary provide a fascinating glimpse into the religious and cultural traditions of the time, making this collection a true treasure trove for both scholars and enthusiasts of medieval literature.

Genres: Drama, History, Literature

Themes: Religion, Morality, Redemption, Human Nature
