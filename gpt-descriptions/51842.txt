Synopsis: In 'Beyond Bedlam' by Guin Wyman, a renowned psychiatrist finds himself drawn into a mysterious world of madness and supernatural occurrences when he takes on a new patient with a disturbing secret. As he delves deeper into the patient's twisted psyche, he uncovers a dark conspiracy that threatens to unleash chaos upon the world. With his own sanity hanging in the balance, the psychiatrist must navigate a treacherous path between reality and the supernatural, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Supernatural horror, Mystery

Themes: Madness, Sanity, Conspiracy, Supernatural forces
