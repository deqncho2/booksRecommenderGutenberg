Synopsis: In 'The Tenants: An Episode of the '80s', Mary S. Watts takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of a diverse group of tenants living in a New York City apartment building during the 1980s. From struggling artists to ambitious professionals, each tenant's story intertwines, revealing the complexities of their relationships, dreams, and secrets. As the characters navigate the challenges of love, ambition, and identity, their lives become increasingly entangled, leading to unexpected consequences that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Contemporary

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Relationships, Secrets
