Synopsis: 
In 'The Spider' by Fergus Hume, a renowned detective is called upon to solve a perplexing case involving a mysterious disappearance and a web of deceit. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a complex network of secrets and hidden motives, leading him down a dangerous path where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate the treacherous web spun by the enigmatic "Spider" to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Intrigue
