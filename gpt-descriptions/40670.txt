Synopsis: 

In "Makers of Modern Agriculture" by William Macdonald, embark on a captivating journey through the lives of visionary individuals who revolutionized the world of farming. From humble beginnings to groundbreaking innovations, this book unveils the untold stories of these agricultural pioneers who transformed the way we cultivate the land and feed the world. Discover the triumphs, challenges, and extraordinary contributions of these unsung heroes, whose relentless pursuit of progress forever changed the face of agriculture.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, History

Themes: Innovation, Progress, Agricultural Revolution
