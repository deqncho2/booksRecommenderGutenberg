Synopsis: 
In 'Bird Guide: Land Birds East of the Rockies, from Parrots to Bluebirds', author Chester A. Reed takes readers on an enchanting journey through the diverse world of land birds found east of the Rockies. From the vibrant colors of parrots to the delicate beauty of bluebirds, this comprehensive guide provides detailed descriptions and stunning illustrations that bring these feathered creatures to life. Whether you are an avid birdwatcher or simply curious about the avian species that inhabit this region, Reed's guide is an invaluable resource that will captivate and inspire.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Guide, Field Guide

Themes: Birdwatching, Biodiversity, Conservation
