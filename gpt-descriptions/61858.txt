Synopsis: In 'Revolt on Io' by Nelson S. Bond, a group of colonists on the moon of Io find themselves caught in a dangerous power struggle that threatens their very existence. As tensions rise and alliances shift, the colonists must navigate a treacherous landscape of political intrigue and rebellion. With the fate of their community hanging in the balance, they must decide how far they are willing to go to fight for their freedom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Political Thriller

Themes: Power Struggles, Rebellion, Survival
