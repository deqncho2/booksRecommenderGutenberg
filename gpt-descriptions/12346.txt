Synopsis:
In 'A Roman Singer' by F. Marion Crawford, a young American artist finds himself captivated by the enchanting voice of a mysterious Roman singer. As he becomes more entangled in her world, he discovers a web of secrets and hidden desires that threaten to consume them both. Set against the backdrop of Rome's vibrant artistic scene, this tale of passion and obsession will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres:
Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Love and desire, Art and creativity, Secrets and deception
