Synopsis: In 'The Story of Alexander' by Robert Steele, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the life of one of history's most legendary figures. From his humble beginnings as a young prince to his rise as a formidable conqueror, Alexander the Great's story is brought to life with vivid detail and captivating storytelling. As he navigates treacherous battles, political intrigue, and personal relationships, Alexander's ambition and determination are put to the ultimate test. With each triumph and setback, the reader is left wondering what sacrifices Alexander will make in his quest for greatness and how his legacy will shape the world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Power, Leadership, Legacy
