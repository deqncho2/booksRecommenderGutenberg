Synopsis: 
In 'The White Feather' by P. G. Wodehouse, a young man named Bill West is faced with a difficult decision that could change the course of his life forever. When he receives a mysterious white feather, a symbol of cowardice, he is determined to prove his bravery and redeem himself. With a cast of eccentric characters and a series of hilarious misadventures, Bill embarks on a journey that will test his courage, challenge his beliefs, and lead him to unexpected discoveries.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Adventure

Themes: Bravery, Redemption, Identity
