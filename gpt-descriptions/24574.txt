Synopsis: 

In 'Loss and Gain: The Story of a Convert' by John Henry Newman, follow the captivating journey of Charles Reding, a young man searching for truth and meaning in his life. Set in the backdrop of 19th-century England, this thought-provoking novel takes readers on a rollercoaster of emotions as Charles navigates the complexities of faith, friendship, and love. As he encounters various intellectual and spiritual challenges, Charles must confront his own doubts and fears, ultimately leading him to make a life-altering decision. 

Genres: Fiction, Religious, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Conversion, Faith, Doubt, Friendship, Love
