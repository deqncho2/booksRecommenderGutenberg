Synopsis: In the third volume of "A Hazard of New Fortunes," the lives of the characters continue to intertwine amidst the bustling city of New York. As the city grapples with social and political changes, the protagonists find themselves caught in a web of personal and professional challenges. From the struggles of a young writer trying to make a name for himself to the complexities of love and marriage, this volume delves deeper into the intricate tapestry of human relationships. With unexpected twists and turns, the characters navigate the ever-changing landscape of ambition, morality, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Realism, Social Commentary

Themes: Social class, Ambition, Love and marriage, Morality, Urban life
