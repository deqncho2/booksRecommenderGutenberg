Synopsis: 
In 'De Turkey and De Law: A Comedy in Three Acts', Zora Neale Hurston takes us on a hilarious journey through the small town of Eatonville, Florida. The story revolves around the lively characters and their misadventures as they navigate the complexities of love, marriage, and the law. As the townspeople prepare for a highly anticipated turkey shoot, unexpected twists and turns keep everyone on their toes, leading to uproarious situations and unexpected revelations. Hurston's witty dialogue and vibrant storytelling bring this comedic tale to life, leaving readers eagerly anticipating the next laugh-out-loud moment.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Satire

Themes: Love and relationships, Small-town dynamics, Social commentary
