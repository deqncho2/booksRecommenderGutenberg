Synopsis: 
In "Elusive Isabel" by Jacques Futrelle, a wealthy young American man named Lawrence Blakeley finds himself entangled in a thrilling adventure when he encounters the enigmatic and captivating Isabel Thorne. As he becomes infatuated with her, he soon discovers that Isabel is not who she appears to be, and her true identity is shrouded in mystery. Determined to uncover the truth, Lawrence embarks on a journey filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected twists, as he tries to unravel the secrets surrounding Isabel's past and present. Will he be able to solve the puzzle and win Isabel's heart, or will the elusive Isabel remain forever out of his reach?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Deception, Love and Obsession
