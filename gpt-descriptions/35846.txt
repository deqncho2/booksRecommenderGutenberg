Synopsis:
Set in the tenth century, 'Ekkehard: A Tale of the Tenth Century' follows the journey of a young monk named Ekkehard who finds himself torn between his monastic duties and his passion for literature. When he is unexpectedly sent to the castle of the powerful Duke of Swabia, Ekkehard becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and dangerous secrets. As he navigates the treacherous world of the medieval court, Ekkehard must confront his own desires and make choices that will shape his destiny.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes:
Love and Desire, Duty and Sacrifice, Power and Politics
