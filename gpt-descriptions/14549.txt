Synopsis: 
In "Rule a Wife, and Have a Wife," Fletcher presents a captivating tale of love, power, and deception. Set in a world where men rule and women are expected to obey, the story follows the lives of two couples whose relationships are put to the test. As secrets unravel and hidden desires come to light, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of manipulation and unexpected alliances. With its witty dialogue and intricate plot twists, this play explores the complexities of marriage, gender roles, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Romance
Themes: Gender roles, Marriage, Deception
