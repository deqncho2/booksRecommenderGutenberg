Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of Space: A Study of the Hyperspace Movement in the Light of the Evolution of New Psychic Faculties and an Inquiry into the Genesis and Essential Nature of Space' by Robert T. Browne, embark on a mind-bending exploration of the unknown as the author delves into the enigmatic realm of hyperspace. Through a captivating blend of scientific inquiry and metaphysical exploration, Browne takes readers on a journey to unravel the mysteries of space, uncovering the evolution of new psychic faculties and questioning the very essence of our understanding of space itself. Prepare to have your perceptions challenged and your imagination ignited as Browne delves into the uncharted territories of the cosmos.

Genres: Science Fiction, Metaphysics, Philosophy

Themes: Hyperspace, Psychic Abilities, Evolution, Perception, Existence
