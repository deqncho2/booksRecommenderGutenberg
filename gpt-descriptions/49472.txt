Synopsis: In 'The Old Dominion' by James, G. P. R., a young woman named Virginia finds herself caught in a web of secrets and intrigue when she inherits a mysterious estate in the heart of Virginia. As she delves into the history of her family and the land, she uncovers a hidden world of forbidden love, political conspiracies, and long-buried secrets that threaten to unravel her own life. With danger lurking around every corner, Virginia must navigate through a treacherous landscape of deceit and betrayal to uncover the truth and reclaim her legacy.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Political intrigue
