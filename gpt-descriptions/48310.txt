Synopsis: In Stephen H. Branch's Alligator, Vol. 1 no. 01, April 24, 1858, a mysterious publication emerges in the heart of the antebellum South, captivating readers with its audacious and controversial content. As the first issue of this enigmatic newspaper hits the streets, it sets off a chain of events that will challenge societal norms, ignite passionate debates, and ultimately reveal hidden truths that could shake the very foundations of the era. With its gripping storytelling and thought-provoking articles, Alligator promises to be a captivating journey into a world where secrets lurk beneath the surface and the power of words can change everything.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Political drama

Themes: Social justice, Freedom of speech, Power dynamics
