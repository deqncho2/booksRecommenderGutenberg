Synopsis: 
"The poetical works of Janet Little, the Scotch milkmaid" takes readers on a captivating journey through the poetic musings of Janet Little, a humble milkmaid from Scotland. This collection of poems offers a glimpse into the life and experiences of a woman who defied societal expectations to express her thoughts and emotions through her lyrical verses. From the beauty of nature to the complexities of love and the struggles of everyday life, Little's poems resonate with authenticity and a deep sense of humanity. Through her words, she invites readers to explore the power of poetry as a means of self-expression and connection with the world around us.

Genres: Poetry, Autobiography, Scottish Literature

Themes: Nature, Love, Identity, Social Expectations
