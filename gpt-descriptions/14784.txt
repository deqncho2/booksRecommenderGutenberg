Synopsis: In 'Timid Hare: The Little Captive' by Mary Hazelton Blanchard Wade, follow the captivating journey of a timid hare who finds himself in a perilous situation. As he faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected allies, the hare must summon his courage to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way. With each twist and turn, the story unfolds into a heartwarming tale of bravery, friendship, and the power of self-belief.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Bravery, Friendship, Self-belief
