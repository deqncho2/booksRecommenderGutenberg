Synopsis: 

In 'Health on the Farm: A Manual of Rural Sanitation and Hygiene', author H. F. Harris delves into the often overlooked world of rural health and hygiene practices. Through a combination of practical advice, insightful anecdotes, and scientific research, Harris uncovers the hidden dangers that lurk in the countryside and provides invaluable guidance on how to maintain a healthy and sanitary environment. From the proper disposal of waste to the prevention of waterborne diseases, this manual offers a comprehensive and eye-opening exploration of the challenges faced by those living and working on farms. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Health and Wellness, Agriculture

Themes: Rural sanitation, Hygiene practices, Disease prevention, Farm life
