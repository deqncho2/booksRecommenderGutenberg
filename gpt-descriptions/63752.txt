Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell on the Boulevards; Or, Astonishing the Europeans', Frank Merriwell, the charismatic and talented athlete, embarks on a thrilling adventure across Europe. As he travels through the bustling streets of Paris, the picturesque canals of Venice, and the historic landmarks of Rome, Frank finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. With his quick wit and remarkable skills, he must navigate through unexpected challenges, uncover hidden secrets, and outsmart his adversaries. Along the way, Frank encounters fascinating characters, experiences the rich cultures of Europe, and learns valuable life lessons that will shape his future. Will Frank Merriwell astonish the Europeans with his extraordinary abilities? Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult Themes: Courage, Friendship, Cultural Exploration
