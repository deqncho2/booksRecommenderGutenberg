Synopsis: 

'The Children of France: A Book of Stories of the Heroism and Self-sacrifice of Youthful Patriots of France During the Great War' by Ruth Royce is a collection of captivating stories that highlight the extraordinary bravery and sacrifice of young patriots in France during World War I. 

Through a series of inspiring narratives, the book delves into the lives of these courageous children who, despite their tender age, displayed unwavering determination and resilience in the face of adversity. From secret missions to acts of defiance, each story unveils the remarkable feats accomplished by these youthful heroes, shedding light on their unwavering commitment to their country and their unwavering spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Young Adult

Themes: Heroism, Self-sacrifice, Patriotism, Resilience
