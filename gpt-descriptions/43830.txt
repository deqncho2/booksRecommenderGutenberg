Synopsis: In 'White Wings: A Yachting Romance, Volume III' by William Black, the captivating story continues as the characters navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and adventure. Set against the backdrop of the stunning Mediterranean, the lives of the wealthy and the working class intertwine, revealing secrets and desires that threaten to shatter their world. As the tension rises and the stakes become higher, unexpected alliances are formed and sacrifices are made, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Love and passion, Betrayal, Social class dynamics
