Synopsis: In "The Chinese Lantern," a captivating play by Laurence Housman, a mysterious lantern holds the power to reveal hidden truths and desires. As the lantern passes from person to person, it unveils a web of secrets, love, and betrayal, ultimately leading to unexpected consequences. Set against the backdrop of a small village, this enthralling tale explores the complexities of human relationships and the consequences of our deepest desires.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets, Love, Betrayal, Desire
