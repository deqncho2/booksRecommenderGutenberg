Synopsis: 
In 'Under the Skin' by Perri, Leslie, a mysterious woman named Isserley spends her days driving through the Scottish Highlands, picking up hitchhikers. But there's something peculiar about these encounters, as Isserley's true intentions remain hidden beneath her enigmatic facade. As the story unfolds, the reader is taken on a thrilling and thought-provoking journey that explores the depths of human nature and the blurred lines between predator and prey.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Morality, Power Dynamics
