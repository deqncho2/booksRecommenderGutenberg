Synopsis: In "Knock at a Venture" by Eden Phillpotts, a young man named John Dart returns to his hometown after a long absence, only to find himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. As he delves deeper into the secrets of his past, he uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to unravel everything he thought he knew. With each twist and turn, Dart must navigate a treacherous path filled with danger and deception, all while trying to protect those he holds dear. Will he be able to uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Betrayal
