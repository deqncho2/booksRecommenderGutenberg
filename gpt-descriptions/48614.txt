Synopsis: 

In 'Women's Suffrage: A Short History of a Great Movement', Millicent Garrett Fawcett, Dame takes readers on a captivating journey through the remarkable history of the women's suffrage movement. From the early pioneers who fought tirelessly for equality to the groundbreaking moments that led to the eventual triumph, Fawcett's narrative is filled with inspiring stories of courage, determination, and resilience. This concise yet powerful account sheds light on the struggles and triumphs of countless women who fought for their right to vote, ultimately shaping the course of history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Women's Studies

Themes: Gender equality, Activism, Social change
