Synopsis: 
'Lord Kilgobbin' by Charles James Lever is a captivating novel set in Ireland during the 19th century. The story follows the eccentric Lord Kilgobbin and his family as they navigate the complexities of their aristocratic life amidst political turmoil and personal challenges. With a mix of humor, romance, and political intrigue, the novel takes readers on a journey filled with unexpected twists and turns, as the characters strive to protect their heritage and find happiness in a rapidly changing world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Family dynamics, Political unrest, Social change
