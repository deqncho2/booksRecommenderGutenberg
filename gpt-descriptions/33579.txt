Synopsis: In 'Last Words' by Stephen Crane, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic presence and cryptic conversations. As the townspeople become increasingly obsessed with unraveling the stranger's true identity, they are drawn into a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected revelations. With each passing day, tensions rise, and the line between reality and illusion blurs, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of truth and the power of perception.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Literary Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Truth, Deception
