Synopsis: 
In this captivating anthology, Leo Wiener takes readers on a journey through the rich and diverse world of Russian literature, spanning from the tenth century to the close of the eighteenth century. From epic poems to lyrical ballads, from historical chronicles to philosophical treatises, Wiener carefully selects and presents a collection of literary gems that showcase the evolution of Russian literature over the centuries. Through the works of renowned authors and lesser-known voices, readers will be transported to different eras, exploring the complexities of Russian society, the struggles of its people, and the profound insights into the human condition that these writings offer.

Genres: 
Anthology, Literature, History

Themes: 
Russian culture and society, Human condition, Evolution of literature
