Synopsis: In 'Goddess of the Moon' by Reynolds, John Murray, a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the key to a long-lost civilization. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and a race against time to uncover the truth before it falls into the wrong hands. With each discovery, the line between myth and reality blurs, leading her on a thrilling adventure that spans across continents and centuries.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Mythology, Archaeology, Power of Knowledge
