Synopsis: In 'The History of Modern Painting, Volume 2', Richard Muther takes us on a captivating journey through the vibrant world of art in the 19th century. From the rise of Romanticism to the birth of Impressionism, Muther explores the revolutionary movements, influential artists, and groundbreaking techniques that shaped the course of modern painting. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, he delves into the lives and works of renowned painters such as Delacroix, Turner, Courbet, and Manet, revealing the struggles, triumphs, and controversies that surrounded their art. This revised edition, continued by the author, brings us to the end of the 19th century, providing a comprehensive and enthralling account of this transformative period in art history.

Genres: Art History, Non-fiction

Themes: Evolution of artistic movements, Impact of individual artists, Societal and cultural influences on art
