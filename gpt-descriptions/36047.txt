Synopsis: In 'The Red Debt: Echoes from Kentucky' by MacDonald, Everett, a small town in Kentucky becomes the backdrop for a gripping tale of family secrets, forbidden love, and the haunting power of the past. When a mysterious stranger arrives in town, he sets off a chain of events that will unearth long-buried secrets and force the residents to confront the dark history that binds them together. As the truth slowly unravels, the characters find themselves caught in a web of betrayal, revenge, and redemption, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Redemption, Revenge
