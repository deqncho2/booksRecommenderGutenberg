Synopsis: 
In 'Syd Belton: The Boy Who Would Not Go to Sea' by George Manville Fenn, we follow the story of Syd, a young boy who dreams of adventure but is terrified of the sea. Despite his father's wishes for him to become a sailor, Syd's fear holds him back. However, when an unexpected turn of events forces him to confront his fears head-on, Syd embarks on a journey that will test his courage and determination. Will he overcome his fear and find the adventure he has always longed for?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Facing fears, Pursuing dreams, Self-discovery
