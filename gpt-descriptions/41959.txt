Synopsis: 

'Philippine Progress Prior to 1898: A Source Book of Philippine History to Supply a Fairer View of Filipino Participation and Supplement the Defective Spanish Accounts' is a comprehensive historical work that aims to provide a more accurate and balanced perspective on the progress and participation of Filipinos in the Philippines before 1898. Written by Austin Craig and Conrado O. Benitez, this source book delves into the often overlooked aspects of Filipino history, shedding light on the contributions and achievements of the Filipino people during this crucial period. 

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Filipino history, Colonialism, National identity, Historical perspective
