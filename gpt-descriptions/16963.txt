Synopsis: In 'The Golden Bird' by Maria Thompson Daviess, a young woman named Dorinda finds herself caught in a web of mystery and romance when she stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. As she embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets of the past, Dorinda must navigate through unexpected twists and turns, all while discovering the true meaning of love and loyalty. With danger lurking at every corner, will she be able to unlock the secrets of the golden bird?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Treasure hunting, Love and loyalty
