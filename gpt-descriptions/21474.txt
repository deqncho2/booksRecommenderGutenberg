Synopsis: In 'Peter the Whaler' by William Henry Giles Kingston, follow the thrilling adventures of young Peter as he sets sail on a whaling ship. From battling treacherous storms to encountering dangerous sea creatures, Peter's journey is filled with excitement and danger. Along the way, he learns valuable lessons about bravery, perseverance, and the power of friendship. Will Peter prove himself as a true whaler and overcome the challenges that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Bravery, Perseverance, Friendship
