Synopsis: In "The unknown life of Jesus Christ: the original text of Nicolas Notovitch's 1877 discovery," Nicolas Notovitch takes readers on a captivating journey through a hidden chapter of Jesus Christ's life. Through his meticulous research and analysis, Notovitch unveils a remarkable account of Jesus' travels to the East during his early years, shedding light on his spiritual teachings and encounters with various cultures and religions. This groundbreaking discovery challenges conventional beliefs and offers a fresh perspective on the life of one of history's most influential figures.

Genres: Historical fiction, Religious literature, Biography

Themes: Spirituality, Cultural exchange, Religious exploration
