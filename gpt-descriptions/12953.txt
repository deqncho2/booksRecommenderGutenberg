Synopsis: 
'Sketches of Western North Carolina, Historical and Biographical' takes readers on a captivating journey through the Revolutionary Period of Mecklenburg, Rowan, Lincoln, and adjoining counties. Written by C. L. Hunter, this book offers a unique blend of historical and biographical accounts, accompanied by a wealth of miscellaneous information that has never been published before. From the untold stories of courageous individuals to the intriguing events that shaped the region, this book paints a vivid picture of Western North Carolina's rich past.

Genres: Historical, Biographical, Non-fiction

Themes: Revolutionary Period, Courage, Regional History
