Synopsis: 
In 'Spoilers of the Spaceways' by Martin, W. Bradford, a group of intergalactic smugglers find themselves caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse as they navigate treacherous spaceways and encounter mysterious adversaries. With their lives on the line, they must rely on their wit, cunning, and a few unexpected allies to survive. As secrets unravel and alliances shift, the crew must confront their own pasts and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of not only themselves but the entire galaxy.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Survival
