Synopsis: In 'The Plow-Woman' by Eleanor Gates, a young woman named Mary defies societal expectations and takes on the role of a plow-woman, challenging the traditional gender roles of her time. As she navigates the hardships and prejudices of her chosen profession, Mary discovers her own strength and resilience, inspiring those around her and proving that determination knows no bounds. This captivating story explores the power of breaking free from societal constraints and the importance of pursuing one's true passion.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Feminist Literature

Themes: Gender roles, Empowerment, Self-discovery
