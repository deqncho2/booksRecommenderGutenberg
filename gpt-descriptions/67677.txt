Synopsis: 
In 'Rainbow Landing: An Adventure Story' by Pollock, Frank Lillie, readers are transported to the enchanting world of Rainbow Landing, a hidden island filled with mystical creatures and breathtaking landscapes. When twelve-year-old Emily stumbles upon a mysterious map, she embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the island's secrets and fulfill a long-lost prophecy. Alongside her newfound friends, Emily must navigate treacherous waters, solve riddles, and face unexpected challenges that test her courage and determination. As the adventure unfolds, Emily discovers that Rainbow Landing holds not only the key to her own destiny but also the fate of the entire island. Will she be able to unlock the island's secrets and save Rainbow Landing before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Destiny, Friendship, Courage, Discovery
