Synopsis: In 'Trouble Times Two' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist accidentally creates a device that has the power to change the world. However, when his invention falls into the wrong hands, it becomes a dangerous weapon that could bring about catastrophic consequences. As the scientist races against time to reclaim his creation, he finds himself caught in a web of intrigue, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous landscape where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Technology and its consequences, Morality and ethics in scientific advancements
