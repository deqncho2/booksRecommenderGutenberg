Synopsis:
In "The Silent Bullet" by Arthur B. Reeve, a renowned detective faces his most perplexing case yet when a seemingly impossible murder takes place. A brilliant scientist is found dead in his locked laboratory, with no signs of forced entry or any weapon nearby. As the detective delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, hidden motives, and scientific marvels that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each clue leading to more questions than answers, the detective must rely on his keen intellect and deductive reasoning to solve the baffling mystery before another life is claimed.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Scientific innovation, Deductive reasoning, Hidden motives
