Synopsis: In 'The Romance of the Red Triangle', Arthur K. Yapp takes us on a captivating journey into the heart of World War I, where the Y.M.C.A. plays a crucial role in providing support and solace to the brave sailors and soldiers of the British Empire. Through vivid storytelling and firsthand accounts, Yapp unveils the extraordinary service rendered by the Y.M.C.A., revealing the untold stories of sacrifice, camaraderie, and resilience amidst the chaos of war. As the red triangle becomes a symbol of hope and compassion, this gripping narrative explores the profound impact of the Y.M.C.A. on the lives of those who fought for their country.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, War Literature, Biography

Themes: War and its effects, Humanitarianism, Brotherhood and camaraderie
