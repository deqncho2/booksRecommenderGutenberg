Synopsis: In 'Pirate Princes and Yankee Jacks', follow the thrilling adventures of David Forsyth as he embarks on a perilous journey across the treacherous seas and scorching deserts. From battling the notorious buccaneer princes of Barbary to delving into the mysteries of an ancient tomb buried beneath the Sahara, Forsyth's quest for treasure and survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Will he conquer the ruthless pirates and uncover the secrets of the desert? Find out in this action-packed tale of high-stakes adventure.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Treasure Hunting, Survival
