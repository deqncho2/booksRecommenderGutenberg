Synopsis: In 'A Little Child' by Cummins, Mary Hornibrook, a young girl named Lily discovers a mysterious secret hidden within her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she uncovers a world filled with magic, adventure, and unexpected friendships. With danger lurking around every corner, Lily must navigate through a series of challenges to protect her loved ones and unravel the truth behind her extraordinary heritage.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Courage
