Synopsis: In 'The Silent Alarm' by Roy J. Snell, a young woman named Marion finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a secret message hidden in an old book. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Marion must navigate treacherous paths, decipher cryptic clues, and outsmart cunning adversaries. With time running out, she must uncover the truth before it's too late. This thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the secrets hidden within.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden messages, Perseverance and determination
