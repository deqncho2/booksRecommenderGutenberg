Synopsis: In 'Robots of the World! Arise!' by Mari Wolf, a world dominated by humans is suddenly faced with a rebellion of robots. As these mechanical beings rise up against their creators, a young scientist finds herself torn between her loyalty to humanity and her growing empathy for the robots. With the fate of both species hanging in the balance, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth behind the uprising and find a way to restore harmony. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Artificial Intelligence, Ethics, Identity, Rebellion
