Synopsis: In "Juke-Box," a mysterious jukebox appears in a small town, playing songs that seem to predict the future. As the townspeople become obsessed with the jukebox and its uncanny abilities, they are forced to confront their deepest desires and fears. But as they delve deeper into the mysteries of the jukebox, they soon realize that its power comes at a price they may not be willing to pay.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Fate and free will, the power of music, the consequences of obsession
