Synopsis: In 'The Return of Clubfoot' by Valentine Williams, a notorious criminal mastermind thought to be dead resurfaces, sending shockwaves through the criminal underworld. As the authorities scramble to capture him, a retired detective is drawn back into the dangerous game of cat and mouse, determined to bring Clubfoot to justice once and for all. With thrilling twists and turns, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Redemption, Revenge, Pursuit of Justice
