Synopsis: 
'The Journal of Joachim Hane' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous period of Oliver Cromwell's rule in France. Joachim Hane, a young and ambitious man, finds himself employed by Cromwell and embarks on a series of daring escapes and harrowing sufferings. From the treacherous streets of Paris to the battlefields of war-torn France, Hane's journal chronicles his encounters with danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As he navigates the complex web of political intrigue and personal survival, Hane's loyalty and resilience are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Political Thriller

Themes: Loyalty, Survival, Betrayal, Political Intrigue
