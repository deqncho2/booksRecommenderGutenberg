Synopsis: In 'The Seven Great Monarchies Of The Ancient Eastern World, Vol 2: Assyria', George Rawlinson takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history, geography, and antiquities of the ancient civilizations of Chaldaea, Assyria, Babylon, Media, Persia, Parthia, and the Sassanian or New Persian Empire. With detailed maps and illustrations, Rawlinson paints a vivid picture of these once-mighty empires, exploring their rise and fall, their cultural achievements, and their impact on the world. From the grandeur of Babylon to the military might of Assyria, this volume offers a fascinating glimpse into the ancient Eastern world.

Genres: History, Archaeology, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Power and empire, Cultural heritage
