Synopsis: 

In "The Spiritual Exercises of St. Ignatius: Adapted to an Eight Days Retreat and Six Triduums in Preparation for the Semi-Annual Renovation of the Vows, All for the Use of Jesuits Only," Charles Coppens presents a comprehensive guide to the spiritual exercises practiced by the Jesuits. This book serves as a practical manual for Jesuits to deepen their spiritual journey and strengthen their commitment to their vows.

Genres: Religious, Spiritual, Self-help

Themes: Spiritual growth, Discernment, Vows and commitment
