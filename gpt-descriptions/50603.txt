Synopsis: 
In 'Minute Mysteries [Detectograms]' by H. A. Ripley, readers are invited to put their detective skills to the test with a collection of intriguing and mind-boggling mysteries. Each story presents a seemingly impossible crime or puzzling situation, challenging readers to unravel the truth within a minute. From baffling disappearances to perplexing alibis, these bite-sized mysteries will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly trying to crack the case before the solution is revealed. Prepare to be captivated by these clever and cunning tales that will leave you guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Short Stories

Themes: Deduction, Problem-solving, Crime-solving
