Synopsis: 
In "Pee-wee Harris: Fixer" by Percy Keese Fitzhugh, young Pee-wee Harris finds himself in yet another thrilling adventure. When a mysterious problem arises in his neighborhood, Pee-wee takes it upon himself to become the ultimate fixer. With his boundless energy and clever thinking, Pee-wee sets out to solve the puzzle, uncovering unexpected secrets along the way. As he navigates through challenges and encounters a colorful cast of characters, Pee-wee's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. Will he be able to fix everything and save the day?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Problem-solving, Friendship, Curiosity
