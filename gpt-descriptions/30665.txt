Synopsis: In 'Gossip in the First Decade of Victoria's Reign' by John Ashton, readers are transported to the vibrant and scandalous world of early Victorian society. Through a collection of intriguing anecdotes and juicy tales, Ashton unveils the hidden secrets, scandals, and intrigues that captivated the upper echelons of society during Queen Victoria's first ten years on the throne. From royal scandals to illicit affairs, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the lives of the rich and famous, revealing the power of gossip in shaping public perception and social dynamics. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Social history

Themes: Scandal, Power dynamics, Social perception, Victorian society
