Synopsis: 
'The Annual Catalogue: Numb. II. (1738): Or, A new and compleat List of All The New Books, New Editions of Books, Pamphlets, &c.' is a comprehensive catalogue that presents an extensive collection of newly published books, updated editions, and thought-provoking pamphlets from the year 1738. This meticulously curated list offers readers a glimpse into the literary landscape of the time, showcasing a wide range of subjects and genres. From thrilling adventures to philosophical musings, historical accounts to scientific discoveries, this catalogue promises to captivate bibliophiles and ignite their curiosity about the literary treasures of the era.

Genres: Catalogue, Reference, Literature
Themes: Literature, Knowledge, Cultural Trends
