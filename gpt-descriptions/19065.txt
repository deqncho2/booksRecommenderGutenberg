Synopsis: 

'Swimming Scientifically Taught: A Practical Manual for Young and Old' is an engaging and comprehensive guide that dives into the art of swimming. Written by Frank Eugen Dalton and Louis C. Dalton, this book offers a wealth of knowledge and practical advice for swimmers of all ages and skill levels. From mastering the basic strokes to perfecting advanced techniques, the authors provide step-by-step instructions and insightful tips to help readers become confident and efficient swimmers. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Education

Themes: Skill development, Physical fitness, Water safety
