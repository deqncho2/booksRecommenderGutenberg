Synopsis: In 'The Island Home' by Richard Archer, a young woman named Emily finds herself mysteriously transported to a remote island with no memory of how she got there. As she explores the island and uncovers its secrets, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. With the help of a charismatic stranger, Emily must navigate treacherous waters and confront her own past in order to uncover the truth about the island and find her way back home.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Memory, Survival
