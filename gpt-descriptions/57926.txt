Synopsis: 

'Gleanings from the Works of George Fox' is a captivating collection of writings by the influential Quaker leader, George Fox. Compiled by Dorothy M. Richardson, this book offers a unique glimpse into the profound thoughts and experiences of Fox, who played a significant role in shaping the Quaker movement. Through his powerful words, readers are taken on a spiritual journey that explores themes of faith, divine revelation, and the pursuit of truth. This collection serves as a testament to the enduring impact of George Fox's teachings and provides a valuable resource for those seeking spiritual enlightenment.

Genres: Religious literature, Philosophy, Biography

Themes: Faith, Divine revelation, Truth-seeking
