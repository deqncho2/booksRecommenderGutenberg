Synopsis: In 'Cadets of Gascony: Two stories of old France', Stevenson, Burton Egbert takes us on a thrilling journey to the enchanting world of old France. Through two captivating tales, we follow the adventures of young cadets as they navigate the treacherous landscapes of honor, love, and loyalty. From daring duels to secret romances, these stories transport us to a time of chivalry and bravery, where the bonds of friendship are tested and the pursuit of justice knows no bounds. Get ready to be swept away by the charm and excitement of these timeless tales set in the heart of Gascony.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Honor, Friendship, Love, Loyalty
