Synopsis: In 'Redevelopment' by George O. Smith, a brilliant architect finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he is tasked with designing a groundbreaking new city. As he delves deeper into the project, he uncovers dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten not only his career but also his life. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of corruption and betrayal to save himself and the future of urban development.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Corruption, Betrayal, Urban Development
