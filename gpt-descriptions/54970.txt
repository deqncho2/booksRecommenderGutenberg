Synopsis: In 'Everglades Wildguide: Handbook 143', George and Jean Craighead take readers on an immersive journey through the captivating and mysterious Everglades. Through vivid descriptions and expert knowledge, they unveil the hidden wonders of this unique ecosystem, from its diverse wildlife to its delicate balance of land and water. As readers explore the Everglades' lush landscapes and encounter its fascinating inhabitants, they will gain a deeper understanding of the importance of preserving this fragile wilderness. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Adventure

Themes: Environmental conservation, Biodiversity, Exploration
