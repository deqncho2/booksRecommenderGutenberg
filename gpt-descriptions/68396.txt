Synopsis: 
In "Dr. Courtney's Guide to Happy Marriage," renowned relationship expert Dr. Courtney A. B. shares her invaluable insights and practical advice on how to build and maintain a fulfilling and lasting marriage. Through a combination of personal anecdotes, scientific research, and relatable examples, Dr. Courtney offers a refreshing perspective on the complexities of marriage and provides readers with the tools they need to navigate the ups and downs of married life. With her warm and engaging writing style, she explores various aspects of a successful marriage, including effective communication, conflict resolution, intimacy, and the importance of self-care. Whether you're newly married or have been together for decades, this guide is a must-read for anyone seeking to create a joyful and harmonious partnership.

Genres: Self-help, Relationships, Marriage

Themes: Communication, Conflict resolution, Intimacy, Self-care
