Synopsis: 
'Every Man in His Humour' is a hilarious and witty play that takes place in the bustling streets of London. The story revolves around a group of eccentric characters, each with their own peculiarities and quirks. As they navigate through misunderstandings, deceptions, and unexpected encounters, their lives become intertwined in a series of comical and absurd situations. With mistaken identities, secret love affairs, and a touch of mischief, this play promises to keep readers entertained from start to finish.

Genres: Comedy, Drama

Themes: Human nature, Deception, Social class
