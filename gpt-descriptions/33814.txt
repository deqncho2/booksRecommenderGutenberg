Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of Evelin Delorme: A Hypnotic Story' by Albert Bigelow Paine, a young woman named Evelin Delorme finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and deception when she becomes the subject of a mysterious hypnotist's experiments. As Evelin delves deeper into the world of hypnotism, she uncovers dark secrets and must confront her own fears and desires. With its gripping plot and unexpected twists, this hypnotic tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Deception, Identity, Power
