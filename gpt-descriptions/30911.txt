Synopsis: In 'The Demi-Urge' by Thomas M. Disch, a renowned scientist named Dr. Daniel Weinreb embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that blurs the lines between reality and imagination. As his creation, the demi-urge, gains sentience and begins to manipulate the world around him, Dr. Weinreb finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and existential questions. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must confront the consequences of playing god and grapple with the nature of free will.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Artificial Intelligence, Ethics, Reality vs. Illusion
