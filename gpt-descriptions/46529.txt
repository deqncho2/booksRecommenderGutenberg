Synopsis: 

In 'Cornish Worthies: Sketches of Some Eminent Cornish Men and Families, Volume 1', Walter H. Tregellas takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of remarkable individuals and influential families from Cornwall. From renowned artists and writers to pioneering engineers and courageous explorers, this collection of biographical sketches unveils the extraordinary achievements and contributions of these Cornish worthies. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Tregellas paints a vivid picture of the rich history and cultural heritage of Cornwall, shedding light on the lives of these unsung heroes who have left an indelible mark on the world.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Cornish history and culture, Individual achievement, Family legacies
