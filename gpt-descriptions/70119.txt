Synopsis: In "Concerning the bi-literal cypher of Francis Bacon discovered in his works," Elizabeth Wells Gallup delves into the mysterious world of Francis Bacon's writings, uncovering a hidden code that has remained unnoticed for centuries. As she unravels the secrets hidden within Bacon's texts, Gallup takes readers on a captivating journey through history, literature, and cryptography, revealing a hidden layer of meaning that challenges conventional interpretations. With each revelation, the enigmatic figure of Francis Bacon becomes even more intriguing, leaving readers eager to explore the depths of his encoded messages.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Cryptography

Themes: Hidden knowledge, Secret codes, Literary analysis
