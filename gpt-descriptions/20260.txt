Synopsis: In 'Daybreak: A Story for Girls' by Florence Alice Sitwell, readers are transported to the enchanting world of a young girl named Muriel. Living in a small village, Muriel's life takes an unexpected turn when she discovers a hidden secret that unlocks a world of adventure and self-discovery. As she embarks on a journey filled with mystery and wonder, Muriel encounters fascinating characters and faces challenges that test her courage and resilience. With each step she takes, Muriel learns valuable lessons about friendship, determination, and the power of embracing one's true self. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Courage
