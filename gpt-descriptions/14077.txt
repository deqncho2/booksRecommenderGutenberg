Synopsis: In 'A Frog He Would A-Wooing Go', join a brave and adventurous frog as he embarks on a perilous journey to win the heart of his beloved lady frog. Along the way, he encounters a host of eccentric characters and faces unexpected challenges that test his determination and wit. Will the frog's wooing efforts be successful, or will he learn valuable lessons about love and acceptance? This delightful tale is filled with humor, whimsy, and surprising twists that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Children's literature, Picture book, Folklore

Themes: Love and courtship, Adventure and bravery, Lessons in life
