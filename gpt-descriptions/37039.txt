Synopsis: In 'The Red Room' by August Strindberg, a young artist finds himself trapped in a mysterious room, haunted by a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the room's enigmatic history, he becomes entangled in a web of psychological suspense and supernatural occurrences. With each passing moment, the boundaries between reality and illusion blur, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of truth and perception.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Gothic fiction, Supernatural mystery

Themes: Madness, Isolation, Perception, Supernatural phenomena
