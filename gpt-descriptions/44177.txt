Synopsis: In 'The Myths and Fables of To-Day' by Samuel Adams Drake, embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales that blur the lines between reality and imagination. From mythical creatures lurking in the shadows to ordinary people discovering extraordinary abilities, these stories will transport you to a world where the extraordinary becomes ordinary and the ordinary becomes extraordinary. Prepare to be spellbound as you delve into the depths of these modern myths and fables, where the boundaries of possibility are pushed to their limits.

Genres: Fantasy, Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Imagination, Transformation, Perception, Reality
