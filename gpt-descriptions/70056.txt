Synopsis: 

In "Historical record of the 71st Regiment Highland Light Infantry," author Henry John Thoroton Hildyard takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and evolution of the renowned 71st Regiment, also known as the Highland Light Infantry. From its humble beginnings as the 73rd, or McLeod's Highlanders, in 1777, the regiment's story unfolds with gripping tales of battles, triumphs, and the unwavering spirit of its soldiers. Spanning nearly a century, this historical record offers a fascinating glimpse into the regiment's experiences, challenges, and contributions to military history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Patriotism, Bravery, Military camaraderie
