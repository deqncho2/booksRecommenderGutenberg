Synopsis: 
'The Negro in the United States; a selected bibliography' is a comprehensive compilation by Dorothy B. Porter that delves into the rich history and experiences of African Americans in the United States. This meticulously curated bibliography offers a wide range of resources, including books, articles, and other publications, that explore various aspects of the African American experience. From the struggles of slavery and the Civil Rights Movement to the achievements and contributions of African Americans in art, literature, and politics, this bibliography provides a valuable resource for anyone seeking to deepen their understanding of the African American journey in America.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Reference, History

Themes: 
African American history, Civil Rights Movement, Slavery, African American contributions, Social justice
