Synopsis: In "Young Folks' History of England," Charlotte M. Yonge takes readers on an engaging journey through the captivating history of England. From the legendary tales of King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table to the tumultuous reigns of famous monarchs, this book brings history to life with its vivid storytelling and fascinating anecdotes. Through the eyes of young protagonists, readers will witness the rise and fall of kingdoms, the struggles for power, and the triumphs of the English people. With its blend of adventure, drama, and historical accuracy, this book is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: History, Power and Politics, Heroism, Identity
