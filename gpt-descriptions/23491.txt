Synopsis: 
In 'The Castaways' by Collingwood, Harry, a group of strangers find themselves stranded on a remote island after a catastrophic event. As they struggle to survive and find a way back home, they must confront their deepest fears, forge unlikely alliances, and unravel the mysteries of the island. With each passing day, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and the true nature of humanity is put to the test. Will they be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead, or will they succumb to the unforgiving forces of nature and their own inner demons?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Human nature, Redemption
