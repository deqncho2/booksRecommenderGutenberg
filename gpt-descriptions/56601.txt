Synopsis: 

In 'A Text-book of Tanning: A treatise on the conversion of skins into leather, both practical and theoretical,' author H. R. Procter delves into the fascinating world of tanning, offering a comprehensive guide that combines practical techniques with insightful theories. From the ancient art of transforming animal hides into durable leather to the modern advancements in the industry, this book takes readers on a captivating journey through the history, science, and artistry of tanning. With detailed instructions, illustrations, and anecdotes, Procter provides a wealth of knowledge that will intrigue both aspiring tanners and curious readers alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Craftsmanship

Themes: Leatherworking, Historical Techniques, Scientific Principles
