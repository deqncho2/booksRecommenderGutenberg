Synopsis: 
In 'The Old Inns of Old England, Volume 2', Charles G. Harper takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of ancient English inns. From the cozy taverns nestled in picturesque villages to the grand coaching inns that once welcomed weary travelers, Harper's vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes bring these storied hostelries to life. With charming illustrations and fascinating tales of famous guests and local legends, this volume is a delightful exploration of the rich history and unique character of England's old inns.

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: English heritage, Hospitality, Cultural preservation
