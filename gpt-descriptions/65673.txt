Synopsis: 

'Putnam's Automobile Handbook: The Care and Management of the Modern Motor-Car' is a comprehensive guide that takes readers on a journey through the intricacies of owning and maintaining a motor-car. Written by H. Clifford Brokaw and Charles A. Starr, this handbook covers everything from the basics of automobile mechanics to advanced troubleshooting techniques. With detailed instructions, practical tips, and insightful advice, this book equips readers with the knowledge and skills necessary to ensure their motor-car runs smoothly and efficiently. Whether you are a seasoned car enthusiast or a novice driver, 'Putnam's Automobile Handbook' is an indispensable resource that will empower you to take control of your vehicle's care and management.

Genres: Non-fiction, Automotive, Reference

Themes: Automobile maintenance, Car troubleshooting, Vehicle care, Automotive knowledge
