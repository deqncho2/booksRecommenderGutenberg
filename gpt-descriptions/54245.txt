Synopsis: 

Step into the captivating world of 'Bygone Scotland: Historical and Social' by David Maxwell, where the rich tapestry of Scotland's past comes alive. Delve into the forgotten tales and hidden corners of this ancient land, as Maxwell expertly weaves together historical events, social customs, and intriguing anecdotes. From the fierce battles of the clans to the mysterious folklore that still lingers in the misty Highlands, this book offers a fascinating journey through Scotland's captivating history. Discover the untold stories of remarkable individuals, explore the breathtaking landscapes that have shaped the nation, and unravel the complex tapestry of Scottish society. 

Genres: History, Non-fiction, Cultural Studies

Themes: Scottish history, Social customs, Folklore, Cultural identity
