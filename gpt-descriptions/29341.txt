Synopsis: 
In 'The Story of the Great War, Volume 5: Battle of Jutland Bank; Russian Offensive; Kut-El-Amara; East Africa; Verdun; The Great Somme Drive; United States and Belligerents; Summary of Two Years' War', readers are taken on a gripping journey through some of the most significant events of World War I. From the intense Battle of Jutland Bank to the Russian Offensive, the struggles in Kut-El-Amara, the conflicts in East Africa, the infamous battle of Verdun, and the monumental Great Somme Drive, this volume provides a comprehensive account of the war's major turning points. It also delves into the involvement of the United States and other belligerents, offering a summary of the war's first two years. 

Genres: History, Military History, Non-fiction

Themes: War, Strategy, Global Conflict, Human Resilience
