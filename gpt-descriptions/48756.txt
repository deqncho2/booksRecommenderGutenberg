Synopsis: In 'By Blow and Kiss: The Love Story of a Man with a Bad Name' (published serially under the title 'Unstable as Water'), readers are introduced to a man burdened with a name that carries a dark reputation. As he navigates through life, he unexpectedly finds himself entangled in a passionate love affair that challenges his beliefs and forces him to confront his past. This gripping tale explores the complexities of love, redemption, and the power of personal transformation.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Love and redemption, Identity and self-discovery, Overcoming past traumas
