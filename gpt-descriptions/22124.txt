Synopsis: In 'The Golden Shoemaker: or 'Cobbler' Horn' by Keyworth, J. W., a humble cobbler named Cobbler Horn discovers a mysterious golden thread that possesses magical powers. As he embarks on a journey to uncover the origins and purpose of this enchanted thread, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters and faces unexpected challenges. With each step, Cobbler Horn's life is transformed, leading him to question the true nature of his own identity and the power of his dreams.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Transformation, Dreams
