Synopsis: In the small village of Wantley, a fearsome dragon terrorizes the townspeople, devouring their livestock and causing havoc. When a brave knight arrives to slay the beast, he finds himself facing a challenge unlike any other. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale takes readers on a captivating journey filled with danger, courage, and unexpected alliances.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Comedy

Themes: Heroism, Overcoming Challenges, Unlikely Alliances
