Synopsis: In 'War in the Garden of Eden' by Roosevelt, Kermit, a gripping tale unfolds as a group of adventurers embark on a perilous journey through the untamed wilderness of Africa. Faced with treacherous landscapes, dangerous wildlife, and the constant threat of conflict, they must navigate their way through a land teetering on the brink of chaos. As they delve deeper into the heart of the continent, they discover not only the beauty and wonder of Africa, but also the dark secrets that lie hidden within its depths. With each step, the stakes grow higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. Will they survive the war that rages in the Garden of Eden?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Conflict, Betrayal
