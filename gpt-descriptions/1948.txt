Synopsis: 
'The Story of a Bad Boy' follows the mischievous adventures of a young boy named Tom Bailey as he navigates the challenges and joys of growing up in a small New England town. From his escapades with friends to his encounters with authority figures, Tom's rebellious spirit and zest for life make for an entertaining and relatable coming-of-age tale.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Fiction, Humor

Themes: 
Youthful rebellion, Friendship, Self-discovery
