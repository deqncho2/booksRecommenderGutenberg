Synopsis: 
In the fast-paced world of Wall Street, a young and ambitious woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of power, greed, and deception. As she navigates the cutthroat environment of high finance, Emily must make difficult choices that will test her integrity and challenge her personal relationships. With the stakes higher than ever, she must decide how far she is willing to go to succeed in a world dominated by men. 

Genres: Drama, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Gender inequality, Morality, Betrayal
