Synopsis: In 'The Son of Monte-Cristo' by Jules Lermina, a young man named Roland discovers a hidden secret that connects him to the legendary Count of Monte-Cristo. As he delves deeper into his mysterious lineage, Roland finds himself entangled in a web of revenge, betrayal, and redemption. With the help of a loyal friend and a newfound love interest, he embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth and reclaim his rightful place in society. Filled with unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale of adventure and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Revenge, Identity, Redemption
