Synopsis: In 'Ti-Ti-Pu: A Boy of Red River', follow the captivating journey of Ti-Ti-Pu, a young boy living in the Red River settlement during the early 19th century. As he navigates the challenges of his unique cultural heritage and the rapidly changing world around him, Ti-Ti-Pu discovers the power of friendship, the importance of family, and the resilience of the human spirit. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt storytelling, this historical novel takes readers on an unforgettable adventure through the Canadian frontier.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Cultural Identity, Friendship, Family, Resilience
