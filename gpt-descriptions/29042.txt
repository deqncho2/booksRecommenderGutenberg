Synopsis: In 'A Tangled Tale' by Lewis Carroll, a collection of intriguing mathematical puzzles and paradoxes awaits the reader. As the characters navigate through a series of perplexing situations, the solutions to these puzzles become intertwined with their own personal journeys. With each twist and turn, the reader is invited to unravel the mysteries and discover the unexpected connections hidden within this captivating tale.

Genres: Mathematical fiction, Puzzle, Fantasy

Themes: Logic and reasoning, Problem-solving, Perception of reality
