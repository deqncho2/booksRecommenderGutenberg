Synopsis: 
'Journalism for Women: A Practical Guide' by Bennett, Arnold is a captivating and empowering book that takes readers on a journey through the world of journalism from a female perspective. Through insightful anecdotes and practical advice, the author explores the challenges and triumphs faced by women in the field, offering invaluable guidance on how to navigate the industry and make a lasting impact. From breaking barriers to finding one's voice, this guide is a must-read for aspiring female journalists seeking to make their mark in a male-dominated profession.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Journalism

Themes: Gender equality, Empowerment, Professional development
