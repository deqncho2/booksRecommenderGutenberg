Synopsis: In 'A Romany of the Snows, vol. 1', Gilbert Parker takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged landscapes of the Canadian wilderness. This continuation of the beloved "Pierre and His People" series introduces us to new characters and their enthralling personal histories. As the last existing records of the enigmatic Pretty Pierre are revealed, secrets and mysteries unfold, intertwining the lives of these fascinating individuals. With vivid descriptions and compelling storytelling, Parker paints a vivid picture of the harsh yet beautiful world of the snows.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Survival, Redemption
