Synopsis: In "Peter and Alexis: The Romance of Peter the Great," Dmitry Sergeyevich Merezhkovsky takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous life of Peter the Great, the legendary Russian tsar. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue, war, and personal struggles, the novel delves into the complex relationship between Peter and his son Alexis, exploring the profound impact it had on both their lives. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, loyalties are tested, and the characters are forced to confront their deepest desires and fears. Merezhkovsky's vivid storytelling and meticulous historical research bring to life the grandeur and brutality of 18th-century Russia, making this a compelling tale of love, power, and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Biographical fiction

Themes: Father-son relationships, Power and ambition, Duty and sacrifice, Love and betrayal
