Synopsis:
In 'Camping For Boys' by H. W. Gibson, a group of adventurous boys embark on a thrilling camping trip in the wilderness. As they navigate through the challenges of nature, they encounter unexpected obstacles and forge lifelong friendships. With each passing day, they learn valuable lessons about survival, teamwork, and the importance of self-discovery. This captivating tale of outdoor exploration will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to see what awaits these brave young adventurers.

Genres:
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Outdoor

Themes:
Friendship, Self-discovery, Survival
