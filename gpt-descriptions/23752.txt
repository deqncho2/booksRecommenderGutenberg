Synopsis: In 'The Flaw in the Sapphire' by Charles M. Snyder, a renowned gemologist, Dr. Amelia Hart, is presented with a rare and mysterious sapphire that holds a secret that could change the course of history. As she delves deeper into the gem's origins, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of deceit, betrayal, and international intrigue. With her life on the line, Dr. Hart must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth behind the flaw in the sapphire before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, the pursuit of knowledge, the consequences of greed and power
