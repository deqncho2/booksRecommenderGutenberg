Synopsis: 
Phileas Fogg, a wealthy and eccentric Englishman, makes a daring wager with his fellow club members that he can travel around the world in just eighty days. With his loyal French valet, Passepartout, by his side, Fogg embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected obstacles, breathtaking landscapes, and encounters with fascinating characters from different cultures. As they race against time, Fogg's meticulous planning and unwavering determination are put to the test, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following their journey across continents and oceans.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Cultural Differences
