Synopsis: In 'The Voyage of the Hoppergrass' by Edmund Lester Pearson, a group of adventurers embarks on a thrilling journey aboard the Hoppergrass, a mysterious ship with a hidden purpose. As they sail through uncharted waters, they encounter treacherous obstacles, encounter strange creatures, and uncover ancient secrets that could change the course of history. With danger lurking at every turn, the crew must rely on their wit, courage, and camaraderie to survive and fulfill their mission. 

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Discovery
