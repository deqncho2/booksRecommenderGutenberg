Synopsis: In "Don Miguel Lehumada: discoverer of liquid from the sun's rays: an occult romance of Mexico and the United States" by Sue Greenleaf, a mysterious Mexican scientist named Don Miguel Lehumada embarks on a groundbreaking discovery that could change the world. As he navigates the realms of science and the occult, his journey takes him from the vibrant streets of Mexico to the enigmatic landscapes of the United States. Along the way, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own hidden agendas and desires. As the plot unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the boundaries between reality and the supernatural blur. This occult romance promises a thrilling adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Occult fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Science and the supernatural, Exploration of hidden knowledge, Love and desire
