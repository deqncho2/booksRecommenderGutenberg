Synopsis: In 'Peru as It Is, Volume 2', Archibald Smith takes readers on a captivating journey through Lima and various parts of the Peruvian Republic. Through vivid descriptions and personal experiences, Smith provides a fascinating account of the social and physical aspects of this enchanting country. From the bustling streets of Lima to the breathtaking landscapes, readers will be transported to a world filled with vibrant culture, historical richness, and natural wonders. As Smith delves into the heart of Peru, he uncovers hidden gems and encounters diverse characters, offering readers a unique perspective on this captivating nation.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Natural beauty, Historical significance
