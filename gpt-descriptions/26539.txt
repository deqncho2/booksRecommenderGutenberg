Synopsis: The Rover Boys at Big Horn Ranch follows the thrilling adventures of the three Rover brothers as they embark on a journey to the wild and untamed Big Horn Ranch. When the boys arrive, they find themselves caught up in a web of mystery and danger, as they uncover a plot that threatens the very existence of the ranch. With their courage and resourcefulness, the Rover boys must navigate treacherous terrain, outsmart cunning adversaries, and unravel the secrets of Big Horn Ranch before it's too late.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Survival
