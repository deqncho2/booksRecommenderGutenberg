Synopsis: In "The Real Shelley. New Views of the Poet's Life. Vol. 1," John Cordy Jeaffreson delves into the enigmatic life of the renowned poet, Percy Bysshe Shelley. Through meticulous research and previously undiscovered documents, Jeaffreson uncovers a captivating narrative that challenges the conventional understanding of Shelley's life and sheds light on the untold stories and relationships that shaped his poetic genius. From his tumultuous childhood to his scandalous love affairs, this volume offers a fresh perspective on one of the most influential figures in English literature.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Romanticism, Love and Relationships, Artistic Expression, Social and Political Activism
