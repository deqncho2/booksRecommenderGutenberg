Synopsis: In 'The Lawhorn Site' by John Moselage, a team of archaeologists embarks on a thrilling expedition to uncover the secrets of an ancient Native American burial ground. As they delve deeper into the site, they encounter mysterious artifacts and unexplained phenomena that challenge their understanding of history and the boundaries of science. With each discovery, the team becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, leading them to question their own beliefs and the true nature of the human past.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Archaeology, Native American history, Supernatural phenomena, Human curiosity and exploration
