Synopsis: 
In "The Holiday Round," a group of friends embark on a delightful adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns during their summer vacation. From a mysterious island to a mischievous game of hide-and-seek, this enchanting tale takes readers on a journey of friendship, discovery, and the joy of living in the moment.

Genres: 
Children's literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: 
Friendship, Adventure, Imagination
