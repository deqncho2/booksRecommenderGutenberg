Synopsis: 
In 'Break a Leg' by Jim Harmon, a struggling actor named Max finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he takes on a role in a highly anticipated Broadway production. As Max delves deeper into the world of theater, he uncovers dark secrets and encounters a series of bizarre incidents that blur the line between reality and fiction. With his life and career on the line, Max must navigate through a labyrinth of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Identity
