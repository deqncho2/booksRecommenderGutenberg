Synopsis: In 'Silverthorns' by Molesworth, Mrs., a young girl named Cicely embarks on a thrilling adventure when she discovers a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she unravels the mysteries of Silverthorns, Cicely encounters enchanted creatures, magical realms, and unexpected allies. With danger lurking at every turn, she must summon her courage and wit to protect her loved ones and uncover the truth. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to join Cicely on her extraordinary journey.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Family legacy, Courage, Discovery
