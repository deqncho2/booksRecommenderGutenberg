Synopsis: In 'The Mandarin's Fan' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious fan becomes the center of a captivating tale set in the exotic backdrop of China. As the fan changes hands, it brings with it a series of unexpected events, secrets, and hidden desires. With a cast of intriguing characters, including a determined detective and a seductive femme fatale, the story unfolds with twists and turns that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Cultural clashes, Power dynamics
