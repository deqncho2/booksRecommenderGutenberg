Synopsis: In 'Uther and Igraine' by Deeping, Warwick, a tale of love, betrayal, and destiny unfolds as the legendary King Uther Pendragon and the beautiful Igraine find themselves entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden desires. Set against the backdrop of ancient Britain, this captivating story follows their tumultuous journey as they navigate the treacherous waters of power, honor, and sacrifice. As their fates become intertwined, the reader is drawn into a world of passion, magic, and the timeless struggle between duty and desire.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Love and Desire, Power and Politics, Fate and Destiny
