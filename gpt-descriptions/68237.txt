Synopsis: In 'Unravelled Knots' by Emmuska Orczy, Baroness Orczy, a mysterious woman with a hidden past finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception. As she navigates through a world of intrigue and danger, she must unravel the knots of her own identity and confront the truth that lies beneath the surface. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Redemption
