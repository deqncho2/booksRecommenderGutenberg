Synopsis: In "Marianson: From "Mackinac And Lake Stories", 1899," Mary Hartwell Catherwood takes us on a captivating journey to the enchanting Mackinac Island. As the story unfolds, we are introduced to the mysterious and alluring character of Marianson, a young woman with a secret past. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque island, the narrative weaves together elements of romance, suspense, and the supernatural, as Marianson's fate becomes intertwined with the lives of those around her. With unexpected twists and turns, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Marianson's enigmatic persona.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and Identity, Love and Betrayal, Supernatural Elements
