Synopsis: In old Narragansett; romances and realities takes readers on a captivating journey through the charming and mysterious world of Narragansett, a historic town in Rhode Island. Through a collection of enchanting stories and vivid descriptions, Alice Morse Earle paints a vivid picture of the town's rich history, filled with romance, intrigue, and unexpected realities. From tales of forbidden love to hidden treasures and secret societies, this book unveils the hidden layers of Narragansett's past, leaving readers eager to uncover its secrets.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and passion, History and heritage, Secrets and hidden truths
