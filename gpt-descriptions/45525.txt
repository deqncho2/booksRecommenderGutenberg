Synopsis:
In 'The Betrayal of John Fordham' by B.L. Farjeon, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and treachery when he is hired to investigate a mysterious disappearance. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers shocking secrets and unexpected alliances, leading him to question everything he thought he knew. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the truth becomes increasingly elusive. Will he be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes:
Betrayal, Deception, Trust, Redemption
