Synopsis: In 'Gawayne and the Green Knight: A Fairy Tale', a brave knight named Gawayne finds himself entangled in a mysterious and perilous quest. When a strange and enigmatic Green Knight challenges the court of King Arthur, Gawayne steps forward to accept the challenge. As he embarks on a journey filled with treacherous obstacles and unexpected encounters, Gawayne must prove his honor and loyalty while facing his own inner demons. With each step, the knight is tested physically, mentally, and morally, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Medieval Literature

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Loyalty, Morality
