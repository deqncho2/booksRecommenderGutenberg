Synopsis: In 'The Secret Martians' by Sharkey, Jack, a group of ordinary teenagers stumble upon a hidden Martian civilization while exploring an abandoned mine. As they uncover the truth about the Martians' existence, they become entangled in a thrilling adventure filled with mystery, danger, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of both Earth and Mars hanging in the balance, the teenagers must use their wits and courage to navigate a world beyond their wildest imagination.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Survival, Identity
