Synopsis: 

In 'History of the 1/4th Battalion Duke of Wellington's (West Riding) Regiment, 1914-1919,' author P. G. Bales takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of a battalion during World War I. From the initial mobilization to the final armistice, this book provides a detailed account of the battalion's actions, struggles, and triumphs on the Western Front. Through personal anecdotes, official records, and historical analysis, Bales paints a vivid picture of the soldiers' daily lives, the challenges they faced, and the impact of war on both individuals and the collective unit. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: War and its effects, Camaraderie and brotherhood, Sacrifice and resilience
