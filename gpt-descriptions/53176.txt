Synopsis:
In the remote wilderness of Hudson Bay, a young Inuit boy named Kasba embarks on a remarkable journey that will test his courage, resilience, and connection to his ancestral roots. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters both friend and foe, Kasba must confront the harsh realities of survival and the delicate balance between tradition and progress. With the fate of his people hanging in the balance, Kasba's extraordinary adventure becomes a testament to the enduring power of hope and the strength of the human spirit.

Genres:
Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Identity, Tradition vs. Modernity, Survival
