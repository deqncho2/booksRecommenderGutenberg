Synopsis: In "The Making of a Saint," W. Somerset Maugham takes us on a captivating journey through the life of a young man named Martin, who embarks on a spiritual quest that leads him to unexpected places. From his humble beginnings in a small English village to his encounters with fascinating characters in far-flung corners of the world, Martin's search for meaning and purpose takes him on a transformative path filled with love, loss, and profound self-discovery. As he grapples with his own beliefs and the complexities of human nature, Martin's journey challenges conventional notions of faith and offers a thought-provoking exploration of the nature of spirituality.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Spiritual

Themes: Identity, Faith, Self-discovery, Belief, Transformation
