Synopsis: In 'The Primrose Path: A Chapter in the Annals of the Kingdom of Fife', Mrs. Oliphant weaves a captivating tale set in the picturesque landscapes of Scotland's Kingdom of Fife. The story follows the lives of two young women, Isabella and Lilias, who find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal. As they navigate the complexities of society and the constraints placed upon them, their paths diverge, leading them on separate journeys of self-discovery and resilience. With vivid descriptions and rich character development, this novel explores the depths of human emotions and the consequences of choices made in the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Society and Class, Women's Independence, Love and Betrayal
