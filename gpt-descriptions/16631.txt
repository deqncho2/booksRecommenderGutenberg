Synopsis: In "The Skipper and the Skipped: Being the Shore Log of Cap'n Aaron Sproul," embark on a thrilling maritime adventure as Cap'n Aaron Sproul, a seasoned sailor, finds himself entangled in a mysterious and dangerous situation. When a young stowaway named Jack unexpectedly appears on his ship, Cap'n Sproul must navigate treacherous waters, face unforeseen challenges, and uncover the truth behind Jack's presence. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the fate of both captain and stowaway hangs in the balance.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Redemption
