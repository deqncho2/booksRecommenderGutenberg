Synopsis: In 'The Phantom Yacht' by Carol Norton, a mysterious yacht appears out of nowhere, captivating the attention of a group of friends on a summer vacation. As they embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets behind the phantom yacht, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger, intrigue, and unexpected twists. With each clue they uncover, the friends are drawn deeper into a world of suspense and deception, where nothing is as it seems. Will they be able to solve the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and deception, Friendship and loyalty
