Synopsis: In "The Narrative of Sojourner Truth," Olive Gilbert captures the remarkable life story of Sojourner Truth, an African-American woman who overcame immense adversity and fought for freedom and equality. From her early years as a slave to her eventual escape and transformation into a prominent abolitionist and women's rights activist, Sojourner Truth's journey is one of resilience, courage, and unwavering determination. Through her powerful narrative, readers are invited to witness the strength of the human spirit and the enduring fight for justice and equality.

Genres: Autobiography, Historical Non-fiction, African-American Literature

Themes: Slavery, Freedom, Identity, Women's Rights, Social Justice
