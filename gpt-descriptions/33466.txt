Synopsis: In 'The Social Gangster' by Arthur B. Reeve, a notorious criminal mastermind emerges from the shadows, captivating the city with his audacious heists and enigmatic persona. As the police struggle to catch him, a young detective becomes obsessed with unraveling the truth behind the socialite facade of this cunning gangster. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale explores the dark underbelly of society and the lengths one will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Power
