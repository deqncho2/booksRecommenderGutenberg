Synopsis: 
In 'Essays: Scientific, Political, & Speculative; Vol. 2 of 3: Library Edition', Herbert Spencer presents a collection of thought-provoking essays that delve into various subjects not commonly explored. This volume includes seven essays that have never been published before, offering readers a fresh perspective on scientific, political, and speculative topics. From examining the principles of biology and psychology to discussing the role of government and the nature of society, Spencer's essays challenge conventional wisdom and invite readers to question their own beliefs. With a blend of intellectual rigor and captivating prose, this collection promises to ignite curiosity and stimulate critical thinking.

Genres: 
Philosophy, Science, Politics

Themes: 
- Evolution and the principles of biology
- Psychology and the nature of the mind
- Government and the role of the state
- Society and its organization
