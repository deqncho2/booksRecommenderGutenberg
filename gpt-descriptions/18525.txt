Synopsis: 
In 'On the Trail: An Outdoor Book for Girls', Adelia B. Beard and Lina Beard take readers on an exciting journey into the great outdoors. Filled with practical advice, fascinating stories, and engaging activities, this book is a must-read for adventurous girls who want to explore nature, learn survival skills, and discover the wonders of the wilderness. From camping and hiking to identifying plants and animals, the authors provide a comprehensive guide that will inspire young readers to embrace the beauty and challenges of the natural world.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Outdoor Education

Themes: Nature exploration, Survival skills, Empowerment
