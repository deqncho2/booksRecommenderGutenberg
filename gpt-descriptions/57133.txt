Synopsis: 
In 'The Motor Routes of France: To the Châteaux of Touraine, Biarritz, the Pyrenees, the Riviera, & the Rhone Valley', Gordon Home takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the picturesque landscapes of France. With detailed descriptions of the motor routes, Home guides travelers to the enchanting châteaux of Touraine, the sun-kissed beaches of Biarritz, the majestic Pyrenees, the glamorous Riviera, and the breathtaking Rhone Valley. Along the way, he uncovers hidden gems, shares fascinating historical anecdotes, and provides practical advice for navigating the roads. This travelogue is a captivating invitation to explore the diverse beauty and rich cultural heritage of France.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural heritage, Adventure
