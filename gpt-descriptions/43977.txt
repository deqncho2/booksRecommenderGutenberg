Synopsis: In 'The Seven Darlings' by Morris, Gouverneur, a wealthy and eccentric bachelor unexpectedly becomes the guardian of seven orphaned sisters. As he navigates the challenges of raising the girls, he discovers the transformative power of love, family, and the importance of embracing one's responsibilities. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century New York City, this heartwarming tale explores the bonds of sisterhood, the complexities of fatherhood, and the joys and trials of unconventional family dynamics.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Love, Family, Responsibility, Sisterhood, Parenthood
