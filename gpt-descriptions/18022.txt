Synopsis: In 'Betty at Fort Blizzard' by Molly Elliot Seawell, young Betty finds herself in the midst of a thrilling adventure when she is sent to Fort Blizzard, a remote outpost in the snowy wilderness. As she navigates the challenges of the harsh environment and encounters mysterious characters, Betty's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. With secrets lurking in the shadows and danger lurking around every corner, Betty must uncover the truth and save the day. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Survival, Discovery
