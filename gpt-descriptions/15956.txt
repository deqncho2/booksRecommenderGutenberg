Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Vellenaux, a young woman named Elara finds herself caught in a web of secrets and ancient prophecies. As she embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about her own identity, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and betrayal. With the fate of Vellenaux hanging in the balance, Elara must navigate treacherous alliances and face her own inner demons to fulfill her destiny and save her world.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Betrayal, Destiny
