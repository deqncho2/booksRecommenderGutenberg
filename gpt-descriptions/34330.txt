Synopsis: In the small town of Belhaven, secrets are hard to keep and gossip spreads like wildfire. When a scandalous rumor about a prominent family surfaces, it sets off a chain reaction that unravels the lives of the town's residents. As the truth becomes increasingly elusive, friendships are tested, alliances are formed, and the power of gossip threatens to destroy everything in its path.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Small-town dynamics, the consequences of gossip, the fragility of relationships
