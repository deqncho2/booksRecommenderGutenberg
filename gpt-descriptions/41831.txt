Synopsis: In "Betty Leicester's Christmas," a young girl named Betty finds herself in a new town during the holiday season. As she navigates the challenges of fitting in and making new friends, Betty discovers the true meaning of Christmas and the power of kindness and generosity. With heartwarming moments and unexpected surprises, this story captures the magic of the holiday season and the joy of finding one's place in a new community.

Genres: Fiction, Children's Literature, Holiday

Themes: Friendship, Kindness, Belonging, Generosity, Christmas Spirit
