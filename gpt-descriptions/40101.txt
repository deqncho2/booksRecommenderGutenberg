Synopsis: In 'Popular Technology; or, Professions and Trades. Vol. 2 (of 2)', author Edward Hazen takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of professions and trades. From the intricate workings of steam engines to the secrets of glassblowing, Hazen delves into the inner workings of various industries, shedding light on the skills and techniques that drive them forward. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, this volume offers a glimpse into the diverse and often overlooked professions that shape our modern world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Technology

Themes: Exploration of professions, Industrial advancements, Skill and craftsmanship
