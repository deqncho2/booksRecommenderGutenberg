Synopsis: In 'Jack Harkaway's Boy Tinker Among The Turks', the fifteenth book in the Jack Harkaway series, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as young Tinker finds himself immersed in the exotic world of the Turks. From the bustling streets of Constantinople to the treacherous deserts of Arabia, Tinker must navigate dangerous encounters, uncover hidden secrets, and face unexpected challenges. As he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and espionage, Tinker's resourcefulness and courage are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to outwit his adversaries and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Cultural Differences
