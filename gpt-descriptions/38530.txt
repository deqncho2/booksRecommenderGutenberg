Synopsis: 
In "Legends & Romances of Spain," Lewis Spence takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Spanish folklore and mythology. From tales of valiant knights and beautiful princesses to stories of enchantment and betrayal, this collection brings to life the vibrant and magical world of Spanish legends. Discover the legendary origins of famous cities, encounter mythical creatures, and delve into the passionate romances that have shaped Spanish culture. With each story, Spence weaves a spellbinding narrative that will transport readers to a realm where honor, love, and destiny intertwine.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Historical Fiction

Themes: Heroism, Love and Romance, Fate and Destiny
