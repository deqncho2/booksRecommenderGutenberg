Synopsis: In 'Rational Horse-Shoeing' by John E. Russell, embark on a fascinating journey into the world of equine hoof care. This comprehensive guide takes readers on a captivating exploration of the art and science behind horse-shoeing, revealing the secrets to maintaining healthy hooves and enhancing a horse's performance. With vivid anecdotes and practical advice, Russell offers a fresh perspective on this ancient craft, challenging traditional methods and presenting innovative techniques that will leave readers eager to learn more. Discover the intricate balance between tradition and innovation in the world of horse-shoeing, and unlock the key to unlocking a horse's true potential.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Care, Sports

Themes: Equine health, Traditional vs. modern techniques, Performance enhancement
