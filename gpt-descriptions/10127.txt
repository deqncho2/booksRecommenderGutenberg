Synopsis: In 'Abducted to Oz', a young girl named Emily finds herself mysteriously transported to the magical land of Oz. With the help of new friends, she embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind her abduction and find a way back home. As Emily navigates through the enchanting yet treacherous world of Oz, she discovers hidden secrets, encounters dangerous creatures, and learns the power of friendship and bravery. Will she be able to unravel the mystery and return to her own world?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Identity, Discovery
