Synopsis: Betty Lee, a bright and ambitious sophomore, finds herself caught in a web of secrets and intrigue when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter hidden in the school library. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Betty Lee uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to unravel the very fabric of her small town. With her friends by her side, she must navigate through a labyrinth of deception and danger, determined to uncover the truth and protect those she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Secrets and lies, Friendship, Courage, Identity
