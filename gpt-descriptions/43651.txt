Synopsis: 
In 'Irish Witchcraft and Demonology', Seymour, St. John D. delves into the mysterious world of witchcraft and demonology in Ireland, unearthing chilling tales of supernatural encounters and dark practices. From ancient Celtic beliefs to the infamous witch trials, this captivating book explores the hidden corners of Irish folklore and the terrifying forces that have haunted the land for centuries. With detailed accounts of witchcraft rituals, demonic possessions, and encounters with otherworldly beings, this intriguing work will leave readers spellbound and questioning the boundaries between myth and reality.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Occult, Folklore

Themes: 
Supernatural, Witchcraft, Folklore, Irish History
