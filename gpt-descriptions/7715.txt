Synopsis:
In "The Last of the Barons - Volume 01," Edward Bulwer Lytton takes us back to the tumultuous times of the Wars of the Roses in 15th-century England. Amidst the power struggles and political intrigues, we follow the life of Warwick the Kingmaker, a formidable nobleman who wields great influence over the fate of the nation. As Warwick navigates the treacherous waters of courtly politics, he must confront his own personal ambitions and desires, testing the limits of loyalty and honor. With vivid historical detail and captivating characters, this gripping tale immerses readers in a world of power, betrayal, and the pursuit of greatness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Romance
Themes: Power and Ambition, Loyalty and Betrayal, Love and Desire
