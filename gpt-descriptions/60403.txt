Synopsis: 

In "The Japan Expedition: Japan and Around the World," join J. Willett Spalding on an extraordinary journey as he recounts his three visits to the enigmatic Japanese empire. Along the way, he takes us on a captivating exploration of various exotic locations, including Madeira, St. Helena, the Cape of Good Hope, Mauritius, Ceylon, Singapore, China, and Loo-Choo. Through vivid sketches and detailed descriptions, Spalding paints a vivid picture of these diverse landscapes, cultures, and people, offering a unique perspective on the world.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Exchange, Discovery
