Synopsis: In 'The Green Beret' by Tom Purdom, a former Special Forces soldier finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger when he is recruited for a top-secret mission. As he navigates through treacherous terrain and faces formidable adversaries, he must rely on his training, instincts, and the support of his team to survive. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Action, Military

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Survival
