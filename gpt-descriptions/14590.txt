Synopsis: In 'A New Hochelagan Burying-ground Discovered at Westmount on the Western Spur of Mount Royal, Montreal, July-September, 1898', author W. D. Lighthall takes readers on a captivating journey through the unearthing of a mysterious burial ground in Montreal. As the excavation progresses, the author uncovers a wealth of artifacts and clues that shed light on the ancient Hochelagan civilization. With each discovery, the reader is drawn deeper into the secrets and history of this long-lost culture, leaving them eager to uncover the truth behind this fascinating archaeological find.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Archaeology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Cultural heritage, Exploration
