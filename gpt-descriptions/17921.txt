Synopsis: 

In the sixth edition of 'Manual of Surgery Volume First: General Surgery', Miles and Thomson present a comprehensive guide that delves into the intricate world of surgical procedures. From the basics of surgical techniques to the latest advancements in the field, this book serves as an indispensable resource for both aspiring surgeons and seasoned professionals. With detailed illustrations and step-by-step instructions, the authors provide a captivating exploration of the art and science of surgery, offering valuable insights into the challenges and triumphs that surgeons face in the operating room. 

Genres: Medical, Reference, Educational

Themes: Surgical techniques, Medical advancements, Professional challenges
