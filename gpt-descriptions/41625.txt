Synopsis: 

'Curious Facts in the History of Insects; Including Spiders and Scorpions' takes readers on a captivating journey through the intriguing world of insects. From legends and superstitions to their uses in medicine, art, and even as food, this comprehensive collection explores the fascinating connections between humans and these tiny creatures. Delving into the remarkable injuries and appearances of insects, the book uncovers a wealth of curious facts that will leave readers amazed and eager to learn more.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Folklore

Themes: Human-insect relationships, Cultural beliefs and superstitions, Medical and artistic uses of insects
