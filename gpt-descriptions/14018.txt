Synopsis: In 'Marie' by Laura Elizabeth Howe Richards, a young woman named Marie finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. As she navigates the challenges of love, family, and personal growth, Marie must make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, this captivating novel explores the complexities of identity, the pursuit of happiness, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Identity, Love and Relationships, Self-Discovery
