Synopsis: In 'The Wish: A Novel' by Hermann Sudermann, a young woman named Helene finds herself torn between duty and desire when she falls in love with a man who is not her husband. As she navigates the complexities of her emotions, Helene must confront the consequences of her choices and the societal expectations that threaten to suffocate her. Set against the backdrop of a small German town, this captivating tale explores the power of love, the constraints of tradition, and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Duty and responsibility, Societal expectations
