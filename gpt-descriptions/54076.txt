Synopsis: 

In 'Essays: Scientific, Political, & Speculative; Vol. 3 of 3: Library Edition', Herbert Spencer presents a collection of thought-provoking essays that delve into various subjects not commonly explored. This volume includes seven essays that have never been republished before, offering readers a fresh perspective on Spencer's ideas. From examining the principles of biology and psychology to discussing the role of government and the nature of society, these essays provide a captivating exploration of scientific, political, and speculative topics. With his characteristic analytical approach, Spencer challenges conventional wisdom and invites readers to question their own beliefs and assumptions.

Genres: Philosophy, Science, Politics

Themes: Evolution, Individualism, Social Order, Government, Psychology
