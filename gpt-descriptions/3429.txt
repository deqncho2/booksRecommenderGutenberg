Synopsis: In "St. George for England" by G. A. Henty, follow the thrilling adventures of Ned Eldon, a young Englishman who finds himself caught up in the tumultuous events of the 16th century. As he joins Sir Francis Drake on his daring voyages, battles against the Spanish Armada, and encounters treacherous plots, Ned must prove his loyalty to his country and his unwavering determination to defend England against its enemies. With danger lurking at every turn, Ned's courage and resourcefulness will be put to the ultimate test in this gripping historical tale.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Courage, Exploration
