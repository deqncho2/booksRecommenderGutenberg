Synopsis: 
'Paper-Cutting Machines: A Primer of Information about Paper and Card Trimmers, Hand-Lever Cutters, Power Cutters and Other Automatic Machines for Cutting Paper' by Gray, Niel, Jr. takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of paper-cutting machines. From the intricate hand-lever cutters to the powerful automatic machines, this primer provides a comprehensive overview of the various tools used for cutting paper and card. With detailed explanations and illustrations, the book delves into the history, mechanics, and practical applications of these machines, offering a captivating exploration of a lesser-known aspect of the printing industry.

Genres: Non-fiction, Technology, Reference

Themes: Printing technology, Industrial machinery, Efficiency
