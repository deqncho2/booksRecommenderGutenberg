Synopsis: In 'The Homesteader: A Novel' by Oscar Micheaux, we follow the journey of Jean Baptiste, a young African American man who leaves his small town in the South to pursue a new life in the bustling city of Chicago. Facing numerous challenges and encountering unexpected love, Jean must navigate the complexities of race, identity, and the pursuit of the American Dream in a society that is often unforgiving. As he strives to build a better future for himself and his community, Jean's determination and resilience are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Racial identity, Social inequality, Love and sacrifice
