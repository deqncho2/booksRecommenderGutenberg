Synopsis: 
'Glimpses of King William IV. and Queen Adelaide: In Letters of the Late Miss Clitherow, of Boston House, Middlesex. With a Brief Account of Boston House and the Clitherow Family' offers a captivating collection of letters written by Miss Clitherow, providing a unique and intimate perspective on the lives of King William IV and Queen Adelaide. Set against the backdrop of Boston House and the Clitherow family, this book takes readers on a journey through the intriguing world of royalty and the personal experiences of those who were close to them.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Royalty, Personal Accounts, Historical Context
