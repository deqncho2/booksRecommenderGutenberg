Synopsis: In 'World of Mockery' by Sam Moskowitz, a mysterious carnival arrives in a small town, promising to grant the deepest desires of its visitors. As the townspeople indulge in their fantasies, they soon discover that the carnival's offerings come at a steep price. With each wish fulfilled, the line between reality and illusion blurs, leading to unexpected consequences and a battle for the town's very soul. This gripping tale explores the dark side of human desires and the dangers of succumbing to temptation.

Genres: Fantasy, Horror, Mystery

Themes: Temptation, Illusion vs. Reality, Consequences of Desires
