Synopsis: In 'The Wolf Patrol: A Tale of Baden-Powell's Boy Scouts' by Finnemore, John, a group of young scouts embarks on an exciting adventure that takes them deep into the heart of the wilderness. Led by their fearless leader, they must navigate treacherous terrain, face unexpected challenges, and uncover a hidden secret that could change their lives forever. As they test their skills and bond as a team, the scouts learn the true meaning of courage, loyalty, and the importance of standing up for what is right.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Teamwork, Wilderness Survival
