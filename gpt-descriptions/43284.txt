Synopsis: 
In "Wilderness, A Journal of Quiet Adventure in Alaska," Rockwell Kent takes readers on an awe-inspiring journey through the untamed landscapes of Alaska. Through vivid descriptions and captivating illustrations, Kent immerses us in the beauty and challenges of living in the wilderness. From encounters with majestic wildlife to the solitude and self-discovery found in nature, this journal offers a unique perspective on the raw power and serenity of Alaska's wilderness.

Genres: Adventure, Memoir, Nature Writing

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Connection with Nature
