Synopsis: 
Join George Palmer Putnam on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of the Oregon Country, as he explores the breathtaking landscapes of Oregon, Washington, and California. Along the way, he weaves together captivating legendary lore and provides glimpses into the modern West in the making. From majestic mountains to roaring rivers, from dense forests to vast prairies, this book is a thrilling adventure that will transport you to the heart of the wild and untamed West.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Legends and Folklore
