Synopsis: 
In 'The Ghost Ship: A Mystery of the Sea' by John C. Hutcheson, a young sailor named Jack finds himself aboard a mysterious ship with a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the ship's history, he uncovers a series of eerie events and unexplained disappearances that have plagued the vessel for years. With each revelation, Jack becomes more entangled in the ship's haunting past, leading him on a thrilling and suspenseful journey to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Supernatural, Secrets and Betrayal, Survival
