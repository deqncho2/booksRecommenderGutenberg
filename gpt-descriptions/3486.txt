Synopsis: In "The Inca of Perusalem: An Almost Historical Comedietta," Shaw takes us on a whimsical journey to the fictional land of Perusalem, where a power-hungry Inca ruler finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, love triangles, and absurd situations. As the Inca's thirst for power grows, he must navigate the treacherous waters of diplomacy, war, and the unpredictable nature of his own subjects. With Shaw's signature wit and satire, this comedic tale unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers eagerly anticipating what will happen next.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Desire, Absurdity of Human Nature
