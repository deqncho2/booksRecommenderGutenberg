Synopsis: 
In 'The Tatler, Volume 2', the witty and observant Mr. Bickerstaff continues to regale readers with his satirical and humorous essays on the social and cultural happenings of early 18th-century London. Through his sharp commentary and fictional correspondences, he delves into the world of fashion, politics, love, and scandal, shedding light on the idiosyncrasies and foibles of the city's inhabitants. From the absurdities of courtship to the peculiarities of human behavior, this collection of essays offers a delightful and insightful glimpse into the society of the time.

Genres: Satire, Essay, Periodical

Themes: Social criticism, Human nature, Morality
