Synopsis: 

In 'History of the Origin, Formation, and Adoption of the Constitution of the United States, Vol. 1: With Notices of Its Principle Framers', George Ticknor Curtis takes readers on a captivating journey through the birth of the United States Constitution. Delving into the intricate details of its origin, formation, and adoption, Curtis sheds light on the key figures who played a pivotal role in shaping this historic document. From the intense debates and compromises to the challenges faced by the framers, this volume offers a fascinating exploration of the early days of American democracy.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political history, Biography

Themes: Constitutional law, Founding Fathers, American democracy, Political philosophy
