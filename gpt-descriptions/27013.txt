Synopsis: In "Hellhounds of the Cosmos" by Clifford D. Simak, a group of astronauts embarks on a dangerous mission to explore a distant planet. However, they soon discover that they are not alone in the vastness of space. As they encounter mysterious and deadly creatures known as the Hellhounds, the astronauts must confront their own fears and make unimaginable sacrifices to survive.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Humanity's Place in the Universe
