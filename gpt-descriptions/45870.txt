Synopsis: In 'Gold, Gold, in Cariboo! A Story of Adventure in British Columbia' by Phillipps-Wolley, Clive, readers are transported to the rugged and untamed wilderness of British Columbia during the gold rush era. The story follows a group of determined prospectors as they embark on a treacherous journey in search of fortune and adventure. As they face numerous challenges and encounter a diverse cast of characters, the stakes rise higher than ever. Will they strike it rich or succumb to the dangers lurking in the Cariboo region?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Greed, Camaraderie
