Synopsis: 
In 'Myths & Legends of our New Possessions & Protectorate', Charles M. Skinner takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and enchanting tales of the newly acquired territories and protectorates of the United States. From the tropical allure of Hawaii to the mysterious depths of Puerto Rico, Skinner weaves together a tapestry of myths and legends that will transport readers to a world of magic, adventure, and cultural exploration. Discover the legends of ancient gods and goddesses, encounter mythical creatures, and delve into the fascinating folklore that has shaped the identity of these diverse regions. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Skinner brings these captivating tales to life, offering readers a unique glimpse into the cultural heritage of these new possessions and protectorates.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, History

Themes: Cultural identity, Exploration, Mythical creatures
