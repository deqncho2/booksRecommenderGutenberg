Synopsis: In "The Beckoning Hand, and Other Stories" by Allen Grant, prepare to be captivated by a collection of thrilling and mysterious tales that will keep you on the edge of your seat. From a chilling encounter with a ghostly apparition to a perplexing case of mistaken identity, each story presents a unique and unexpected twist that will leave you questioning reality. Delve into the depths of the human psyche as you navigate through these gripping narratives, where nothing is as it seems and the truth is often shrouded in darkness. Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Supernatural Themes: Deception, Identity, Paranormal
