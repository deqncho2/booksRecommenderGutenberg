Synopsis: In 'Master Race' by Richard Ashby, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a shocking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of conspiracy and power struggles, where the line between good and evil becomes increasingly blurred. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Identity
