Synopsis: In 'The Rocket Book' by Newell, Peter, embark on an exhilarating adventure as a mischievous young boy discovers a rocket and sets off on an unexpected journey. Filled with humor, suspense, and unexpected twists, this delightful picture book will keep readers of all ages on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to see where the rocket will take them next.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Imagination, Curiosity, Exploration
