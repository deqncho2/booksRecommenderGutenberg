Synopsis: In 'A note on the position and extent of the great temple enclosure of Tenochtitlan, and the position, structure and orientation of the Teocolli of Huitzilopochtli,' Maudslay delves into the fascinating world of ancient Tenochtitlan, the capital of the Aztec Empire. Through meticulous research and analysis, he uncovers the secrets of the great temple enclosure and the Teocolli of Huitzilopochtli, shedding light on their positions, structures, and orientations. This scholarly work offers a captivating exploration of the architectural wonders of Tenochtitlan, providing valuable insights into the ancient civilization that once thrived there.

Genres: Historical research, Archaeology, Anthropology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Architecture, Aztec Empire
