Synopsis: In 'Mother West Wind "When" Stories' by Thornton W. Burgess, readers are transported to the enchanting world of the Green Forest, where Mother West Wind and her merry band of animal friends embark on a series of delightful adventures. From the mischievous antics of Peter Rabbit to the clever escapades of Sammy Jay, each story is filled with excitement, humor, and valuable life lessons. As the animals navigate through various challenges and encounters, young readers will be captivated by the charm and wisdom of these timeless tales.

Genres: Children's literature, Animal fiction, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Morality, Nature appreciation
