Synopsis:
In 'Eastern Nights - and Flights: A Record of Oriental Adventure,' Alan Bott takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and vibrant cultures of the East. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the serene temples of Kyoto, and from the mystical deserts of Rajasthan to the ancient ruins of Angkor Wat, Bott's vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes transport readers to a world filled with exotic wonders and unexpected encounters. As he immerses himself in the local customs and traditions, Bott's narrative weaves together tales of thrilling adventures, heartwarming friendships, and profound self-discovery. With each page, readers will find themselves yearning to explore the hidden corners of the East and experience the magic that lies beyond their own horizons.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Self-discovery
