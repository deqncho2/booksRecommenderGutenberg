Synopsis: 
Translations of Shakuntala and Other Works by Kalidasa is a captivating collection of ancient Indian plays and poems that transport readers to a world of love, passion, and divine intervention. From the enchanting tale of Shakuntala, a beautiful maiden whose destiny takes an unexpected turn, to the lyrical verses that explore the complexities of human emotions, this anthology showcases the timeless brilliance of Kalidasa's literary genius. With each translation capturing the essence of the original Sanskrit texts, readers will be immersed in a rich tapestry of mythical narratives and profound philosophical insights.

Genres: Drama, Poetry, Mythology

Themes: Love, Fate, Identity, Nature, Spirituality
