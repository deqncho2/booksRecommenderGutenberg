Synopsis: In 'The Aquarian Gospel of Jesus the Christ', Levi takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and teachings of Jesus Christ, revealing a side of his story that has been largely overlooked. Drawing from ancient texts and esoteric wisdom, Levi presents a thought-provoking and enlightening account of Jesus' travels, encounters, and spiritual insights. This unique perspective sheds new light on the profound impact Jesus had on the world and offers a fresh understanding of his teachings for the modern age.

Genres: Spiritual, Historical, Philosophy

Themes: Spiritual enlightenment, Universal love, Divine wisdom, Mysticism
