Synopsis: In 'Dan Carter and the Haunted Castle', young detective Dan Carter finds himself in a thrilling adventure when he is called upon to investigate a mysterious haunting at a centuries-old castle. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the castle, Dan uncovers a web of hidden passages, ancient legends, and a ghostly presence that seems determined to keep its secrets buried. With his quick wit and keen observation skills, Dan must unravel the truth behind the haunting before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Courage, Friendship, the Power of Knowledge
