Synopsis: 

Step into the enchanting world of Celtic mythology and immerse yourself in the captivating tales of gods, heroes, and mystical creatures. In 'Myths & Legends of the Celtic Race', T. W. Rolleston weaves together a tapestry of ancient stories that will transport you to a time of magic and wonder. From the epic battles of legendary warriors to the tragic love stories of immortal beings, this collection unveils the rich and vibrant folklore of the Celtic people. Discover the origins of the Tuatha Dé Danann, the heroic deeds of Cú Chulainn, and the haunting beauty of the Sidhe. Delve into a realm where the boundaries between the mortal and the divine blur, and where the power of myth shapes the destiny of an entire race.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Love and Tragedy, Supernatural Beings, Cultural Identity
