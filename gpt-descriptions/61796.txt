Synopsis: 
'For the Birds' takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of avian conservation. Through vivid storytelling and stunning imagery, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service unveils the untold stories of dedicated individuals who work tirelessly to protect and preserve our feathered friends. From the remote corners of the world to our own backyards, this book offers a glimpse into the challenges and triumphs of those who strive to ensure the survival of these magnificent creatures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Conservation

Themes: Wildlife preservation, Environmental stewardship, Human impact on nature
