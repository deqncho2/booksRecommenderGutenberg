Synopsis: 
In 'Seventeen: A Tale of Youth and Summer Time and the Baxter Family, Especially William', we follow the misadventures of William Sylvanus Baxter, a seventeen-year-old boy who is convinced that he is on the brink of adulthood. As he navigates the complexities of first love, social expectations, and the challenges of growing up, William finds himself in a series of hilarious and often embarrassing situations. From failed attempts at impressing girls to comical misunderstandings with his family, William's journey through the summer becomes a coming-of-age tale filled with laughter, heartache, and self-discovery.

Genres: 
Humor, Coming-of-Age, Fiction

Themes: 
Adolescence, Identity, Family dynamics
