Synopsis: In "A Story of the Sawdust: The Pathetic History of 'Old Props' Darling' by Anonymous," readers are taken on a captivating journey into the world of a forgotten theater troupe. The story revolves around the enigmatic character known as "Old Props' Darling," whose tragic past and mysterious present intertwine with the lives of the troupe members. As secrets are unveiled and the line between reality and illusion blurs, the reader is left questioning the true nature of love, loyalty, and the power of storytelling.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption, Theatricality
