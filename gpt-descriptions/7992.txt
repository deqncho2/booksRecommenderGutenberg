Synopsis: 

Step back in time to 1922 and explore the fascinating world of American homes with "Better Homes in America: Plan Book for Demonstration Week October 9 to 14, 1922" by Marie Mattingly Meloney. This captivating book takes readers on a journey through a week-long demonstration event that showcases the latest trends and innovations in home design and construction. From charming bungalows to elegant mansions, this plan book offers a glimpse into the diverse and evolving landscape of American homes during the early 20th century. Discover the architectural marvels, interior design inspirations, and practical tips that shaped the way people lived and built their homes in this transformative era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Architecture, Interior Design

Themes: Home design and construction, American lifestyle, Evolution of housing
