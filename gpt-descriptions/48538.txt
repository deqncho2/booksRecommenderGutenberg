Synopsis: In 'Comrades: A Drama in Three Acts', George M. Baker takes us on a gripping journey through the lives of two friends, John and Robert, who find themselves on opposite sides of a political conflict. Set against the backdrop of a tumultuous era, their friendship is tested as they navigate the complexities of loyalty, betrayal, and the pursuit of justice. As the drama unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are shattered, and the characters are forced to confront the consequences of their choices. Will their bond withstand the trials they face, or will it crumble under the weight of their convictions?

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Politics, Loyalty, Betrayal
