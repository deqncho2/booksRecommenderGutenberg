Synopsis: 

In 'Under Handicap: A Novel' by Gregory Jackson, we are introduced to a world where appearances can be deceiving. The story follows the life of Sarah, a young woman who seems to have it all - beauty, wealth, and a loving family. However, beneath her seemingly perfect exterior lies a secret that threatens to unravel her entire existence. As Sarah navigates through a web of lies, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, she must confront the true nature of those around her and discover the strength within herself to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Power dynamics
