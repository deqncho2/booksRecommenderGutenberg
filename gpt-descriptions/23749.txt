Synopsis: In 'The Mythological Zoo' by Oliver Herford, readers are taken on a whimsical journey through a zoo unlike any other. As they explore the exhibits, they encounter a delightful array of mythical creatures from various cultures and legends. From the majestic phoenix to the mischievous kitsune, each creature comes to life through Herford's witty and imaginative illustrations. This enchanting book invites readers to immerse themselves in the captivating world of mythology, where imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Art

Themes: Mythology, Imagination, Cultural Diversity
