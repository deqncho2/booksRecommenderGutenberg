Synopsis: In 'The Byzantines' by Thomas Caldecot Chubb, a young historian stumbles upon a hidden manuscript that reveals a secret society of Byzantine descendants living in modern-day Istanbul. As he delves deeper into their world, he uncovers a web of intrigue, power struggles, and ancient mysteries that threaten to unravel the fabric of society. With the fate of the Byzantines hanging in the balance, he must navigate treacherous alliances and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Power, Betrayal, Cultural Heritage
