Synopsis: 
'The Hero' by W. Somerset Maugham is a captivating tale that follows the life of a young man named Martin Harvey. Martin, a struggling artist, finds himself caught between his desire for success and his moral compass. As he navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations, Martin is faced with difficult choices that will shape his future. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century London, this novel explores the inner turmoil of a man torn between his dreams and the realities of life.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Love, Society
