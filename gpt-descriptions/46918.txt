Synopsis: 
In 'Single Life: A Comedy, in Three Acts', follow the hilarious misadventures of a group of single individuals as they navigate the treacherous waters of love, friendship, and societal expectations. From disastrous blind dates to comical misunderstandings, this uproarious play takes a lighthearted yet insightful look at the complexities of modern relationships. As the characters grapple with their own desires and insecurities, they learn valuable lessons about the true meaning of happiness and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Self-discovery, Society's expectations
