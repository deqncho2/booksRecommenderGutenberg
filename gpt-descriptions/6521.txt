Synopsis: In "The King of the Dark Chamber" by Rabindranath Tagore, a young woman named Nandini finds herself married to a mysterious man who never leaves his dark chamber. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, she discovers hidden truths and embarks on a journey of self-discovery that challenges societal norms and explores the depths of human emotions.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Freedom and confinement, Identity and self-discovery
