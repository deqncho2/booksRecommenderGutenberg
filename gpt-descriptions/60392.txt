Synopsis: In 'A Treatise on Electricity', Francis Penrose delves into the fascinating world of electricity, exploring its various phenomena and providing an explanation for the attraction and gravitation of solids. However, this treatise goes beyond the realm of science, as Penrose also delves into the intriguing topic of how electrical effluvia act upon the human body. He offers insights into the potential applications of electricity in treating certain disorders, while cautioning against its misuse. This work is a captivating exploration of electricity's mysteries and its potential impact on both the physical and biological realms.

Genres: Science, Medicine, Philosophy

Themes: Electricity, Natural Phenomena, Human Physiology, Medical Applications
