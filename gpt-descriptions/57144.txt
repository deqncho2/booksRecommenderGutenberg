Synopsis: 
'Ypres to Verdun: A Collection of Photographs of the War areas in France & Flanders' takes readers on a visually stunning journey through the haunting landscapes of the First World War. Through a collection of powerful photographs, author Alex B. W. Kennedy captures the devastating impact of war on the regions of Ypres and Verdun. From the crumbling ruins of once-thriving towns to the solemn memorials that stand as a testament to the fallen, this book offers a poignant and thought-provoking exploration of the war-torn areas. 

Genres: 
Photography, History, War

Themes: 
War and its aftermath, Destruction and resilience, Commemoration and remembrance
