Synopsis: 
In 'Basutoland: Its Legends and Customs', Minnie Martin takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and cultural traditions of Basutoland, a region nestled in the heart of southern Africa. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Martin unveils a tapestry of mythical legends, enchanting rituals, and intriguing customs that have shaped the lives of the Basotho people for generations. From the mesmerizing tales of ancestral spirits to the vibrant celebrations of harvest festivals, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a world where folklore and reality intertwine, leaving readers spellbound and eager to explore the depths of Basutoland's heritage.

Genres: Folklore, Cultural Studies, Non-fiction

Themes: Mythology, Tradition, Cultural Identity
