Synopsis: In 'The Barton Experiment' by John Habberton, a mysterious experiment takes place in the small town of Barton, captivating the residents and leaving them questioning the boundaries of science and human nature. As the experiment unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the town becomes a stage for a thrilling exploration of the human psyche. With unexpected twists and turns, 'The Barton Experiment' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind this captivating experiment.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Human nature, Ethics, Scientific exploration
