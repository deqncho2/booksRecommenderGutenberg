Synopsis: Two friends embark on a canoe trip along the Danube River, but their journey takes a sinister turn when they encounter an eerie and inexplicable force lurking within the ancient willow trees that line the riverbanks. As they struggle to comprehend the malevolent presence surrounding them, their sanity is tested, and they find themselves at the mercy of an otherworldly power that threatens to consume them. With each passing moment, the willows become more than just trees, becoming a gateway to a realm beyond human understanding.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural, Psychological

Themes: The power of nature, the fragility of the human mind, the unknown and the unknowable
