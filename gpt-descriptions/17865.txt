Synopsis: In 'The Meadow-Brook Girls in the Hills; Or, The Missing Pilot of the White Mountains' by Janet Aldridge, a group of adventurous young girls find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey in the picturesque White Mountains. When a pilot mysteriously disappears, the Meadow-Brook Girls take it upon themselves to uncover the truth and solve the perplexing mystery. As they navigate through the hills, encountering unexpected challenges and uncovering hidden secrets, the girls' courage and determination are put to the test. Will they be able to find the missing pilot and unravel the enigma that surrounds him?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
