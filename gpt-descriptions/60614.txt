Synopsis: In "Rat in the Skull" by Phillips, Rog, a seasoned detective finds himself in a high-stakes interrogation room, facing a suspected IRA member. As the intense questioning unfolds, secrets, lies, and personal demons come to light, blurring the lines between truth and manipulation. With each revelation, the detective's own beliefs and convictions are put to the test, leading to a gripping psychological battle that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Crime, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Power, Justice
