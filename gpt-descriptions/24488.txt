Synopsis: In "Rollo in Naples" by Abbott, Jacob, young Rollo embarks on an exciting adventure in the vibrant city of Naples. As he explores the bustling streets, encounters fascinating historical sites, and interacts with the locals, Rollo's curiosity and thirst for knowledge are ignited. Along the way, he learns valuable life lessons, discovers the importance of cultural understanding, and experiences the beauty of friendship. This captivating tale takes readers on a journey filled with discovery, growth, and the wonders of a new city.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Understanding, Friendship
