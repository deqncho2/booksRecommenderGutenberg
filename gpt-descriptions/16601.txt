Synopsis: In 'The Death-Wake: or Lunacy; a Necromaunt in Three Chimeras', Stoddart takes readers on a haunting journey through the realms of life, death, and the supernatural. Set in a mysterious and eerie landscape, the story follows a protagonist who becomes entangled in a series of bizarre and otherworldly events. As the boundaries between reality and the supernatural blur, the protagonist must confront their own fears and unravel the dark secrets that lie hidden within the shadows.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, horror

Themes: Life and death, the supernatural, fear and the unknown
