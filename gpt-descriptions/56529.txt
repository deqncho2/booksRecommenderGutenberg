Synopsis: 
Join Louis Legrand Noble on an unforgettable summer voyage to the rugged landscapes of Labrador and Newfoundland. As a painter, Noble embarks on a journey filled with breathtaking scenery, treacherous icebergs, and encounters with the local Inuit people. Through his vivid descriptions and captivating artwork, Noble transports readers to a world of untamed beauty and adventure. From the thrill of whale sightings to the challenges of navigating icy waters, 'After Icebergs with a Painter' is a captivating exploration of the Canadian wilderness that will leave readers yearning for their own expedition.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Art

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Cultural Encounter
