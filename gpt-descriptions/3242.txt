Synopsis: In 'The Doctor: A Tale of the Rockies' by Connor Ralph, a young doctor finds himself in a remote town nestled in the majestic Rockies, where he must navigate the treacherous terrain and face the challenges of a tight-knit community. As he delves deeper into the lives of the townspeople, he uncovers dark secrets and hidden truths that threaten to unravel everything he thought he knew. With danger lurking at every turn, the doctor must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will test his courage and integrity. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Western

Themes: Redemption, Community, Secrets
