Synopsis: In "Through Stained Glass: A Novel" by George Agnew Chamberlain, a young artist named Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and redemption as she uncovers a hidden secret within the walls of an ancient cathedral. As she delves deeper into the mysteries of the stained glass windows, Emily's life becomes intertwined with the enigmatic characters she encounters, leading her to question her own beliefs and confront the darkness that lies within. With each brushstroke and revelation, Emily's artistic talent becomes a powerful tool in her quest for truth and healing. This captivating tale of art, spirituality, and the power of forgiveness will keep readers enthralled until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Spiritual Fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, Redemption, Faith, Secrets, Forgiveness
