Synopsis: In "Studies of Contemporary Poets" by Mary Sturgeon, embark on a captivating journey through the minds and lives of some of the most influential poets of our time. From the enigmatic recluse who weaves magic with words to the fiery revolutionary who challenges societal norms, each poet's story unfolds with intrigue and passion. As Sturgeon delves into their works, she uncovers the hidden depths of their artistry, exploring the profound impact their words have on the human experience. Through intimate interviews and meticulous analysis, this book offers a unique glimpse into the inner workings of these contemporary poets, leaving readers inspired and hungry for more.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Biography, Poetry

Themes: Artistic expression, Identity, Social commentary
