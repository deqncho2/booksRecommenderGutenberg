Synopsis: 

'Highways and Byways in the Border: Illustrated' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich history of the Scottish-English border region. Written by Andrew Lang and John Lang, this beautifully illustrated book invites readers to explore the hidden gems, picturesque villages, and ancient castles that dot the borderlands. From thrilling tales of border reivers to the legends of mythical creatures, this travelogue offers a delightful blend of history, folklore, and personal anecdotes. As the authors traverse the winding roads and delve into the region's past, they uncover the unique cultural heritage and enduring spirit of the borderlands.

Genres: Travelogue, History, Folklore

Themes: Cultural heritage, Border history, Folklore and legends
