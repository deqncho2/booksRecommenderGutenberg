Synopsis: In "The Life and Letters of the Rev. George Mortimer, M.A.: Rector of Thornhill, in the Diocese of Toronto, Canada West," author John Armstrong delves into the captivating life of Reverend George Mortimer. From his humble beginnings to his influential role as a rector, Mortimer's journey is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. Through a collection of personal letters and insightful anecdotes, Armstrong paints a vivid portrait of a man whose unwavering faith and dedication to his community leave a lasting impact.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Community service, Personal growth
