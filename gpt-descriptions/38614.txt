Synopsis: 

In "Cornwall" by G. E. Mitton, a captivating tale unfolds against the stunning backdrop of the picturesque county of Cornwall. The story follows a young woman named Alice, who finds herself drawn to the rugged beauty and mysterious history of the region. As she delves deeper into Cornwall's secrets, she becomes entangled in a web of family drama, hidden treasures, and long-buried secrets. With each twist and turn, Alice's journey takes her closer to uncovering the truth, but also puts her in danger she never could have imagined. 

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Treasure hunting, Love and betrayal
