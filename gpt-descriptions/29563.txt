Synopsis: 

'Geographic Variation in the Harvest Mouse, Reithrodontomys megalotis, On the Central Great Plains And in Adjacent Regions' explores the fascinating world of the harvest mouse and its unique adaptations to different environments. Through meticulous research and analysis, Jones and Mursaloglu delve into the various factors that contribute to the geographic variation in this small but resilient creature. From the grasslands of the Central Great Plains to the adjacent regions, the authors uncover the secrets of the harvest mouse's survival and evolution, shedding light on the intricate relationship between species and their habitats.

Genres: Scientific research, biology, ecology

Themes: Adaptation, biodiversity, habitat diversity
