Synopsis: 
'An account of some of the principal slave insurrections, and others, which have occurred, or been attempted, in the United States and elsewhere, during the last two centuries' delves into the often untold stories of slave insurrections and uprisings that have taken place throughout history. From the United States to other parts of the world, author Joshua Coffin sheds light on these significant events, providing a captivating exploration of the struggles and resistance faced by enslaved individuals. Through vivid accounts and historical analysis, this book uncovers the courage and determination of those who fought against the oppressive system of slavery.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, African American studies, Social justice

Themes: 
Slavery, Resistance, Oppression, Courage, History
