Synopsis: In "Harding's Luck" by E. Nesbit, a young boy named Harding finds himself transported back in time to ancient Egypt. As he navigates this unfamiliar world, he encounters mysterious characters, uncovers hidden secrets, and embarks on a thrilling adventure to find his way back home. With each twist and turn, Harding's luck takes unexpected and exciting turns, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Time travel, Identity, Resilience
