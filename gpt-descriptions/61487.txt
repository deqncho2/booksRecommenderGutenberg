Synopsis: In '133 Quicker Ways to Homemade, with Bisquick' by Crocker, Betty, discover a treasure trove of mouthwatering recipes that can be whipped up in no time using the versatile Bisquick mix. From savory breakfast dishes to delectable desserts, this cookbook offers a wide range of quick and easy recipes that will satisfy any craving. With helpful tips and tricks, Betty Crocker shows readers how to create delicious homemade meals without spending hours in the kitchen. Get ready to impress your family and friends with these simple yet impressive dishes!

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink

Themes: Quick and easy cooking, Homemade meals, Versatile ingredients
