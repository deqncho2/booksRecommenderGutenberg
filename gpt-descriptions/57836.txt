Synopsis: 
'Jinny the Carrier' by Zangwill, Israel is a captivating tale set in the bustling streets of London. The story follows Jinny, a young and spirited girl who finds herself thrust into a world of mystery and adventure when she becomes a carrier for a secret organization. As Jinny navigates the treacherous paths of the city, she encounters a cast of intriguing characters and uncovers hidden truths that will challenge her perception of the world around her. With danger lurking at every corner, Jinny must rely on her wit and courage to fulfill her mission and protect those she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Coming of age, Identity, Loyalty, Social justice
