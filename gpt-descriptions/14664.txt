Synopsis: In 'Things To Make' by Archibald Williams, embark on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing and unconventional creations. From peculiar contraptions to ingenious gadgets, this book unveils a world of endless possibilities and sparks the reader's imagination. Each chapter presents a new and unexpected project, inviting readers to explore the realm of DIY innovation. With detailed instructions and whimsical illustrations, 'Things To Make' is a delightful and inspiring read that will leave readers eager to unleash their own creativity.

Genres: Non-fiction, DIY, Crafts

Themes: Creativity, Innovation, Imagination
