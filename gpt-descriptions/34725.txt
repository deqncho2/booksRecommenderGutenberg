Synopsis: In 'The Hidden Force: A Story of Modern Java' by Louis Couperus, a Dutch colonial family finds themselves entangled in a web of mysterious occurrences and supernatural forces while living in Java. As tensions rise between the native Javanese population and the Dutch colonizers, the family's relationships and sanity are put to the test. With a blend of psychological suspense and cultural exploration, this captivating novel delves into the complexities of power, desire, and the clash of cultures in a mesmerizing setting.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Historical fiction, Supernatural fiction

Themes: Colonialism, Cultural clash, Power dynamics, Supernatural forces
