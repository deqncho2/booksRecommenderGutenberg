Synopsis: In 'The Vagrant Duke' by Gibbs, George, a mysterious and enigmatic nobleman arrives in a small, secluded town, captivating the locals with his charm and charisma. As he becomes entangled in the town's affairs, secrets from his past begin to unravel, revealing a complex web of intrigue, betrayal, and hidden agendas. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the Duke must navigate a treacherous path to protect his true identity and the people he has come to care for. This gripping tale of deception and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
