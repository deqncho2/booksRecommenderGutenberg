Synopsis: 

Step into the fascinating world of George Selwyn, a prominent figure in 18th-century England, through his captivating letters and the intriguing events of his life. From his close friendships with influential individuals to his involvement in scandalous affairs, Selwyn's letters provide a unique glimpse into the political and social landscape of his time. As you delve into his correspondence, you will uncover a world filled with wit, charm, and a touch of scandal that will keep you eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-Fiction, Epistolary

Themes: Politics and Power, Social Relationships, Scandal and Intrigue
