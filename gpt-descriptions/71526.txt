Synopsis: 
In the second issue of Dixie: A monthly magazine, readers are transported to the vibrant world of the American South in February 1899. This captivating collection of stories, articles, and poetry delves into the lives of Southern men and women, exploring their struggles, triumphs, and the unique charm of their culture. From tales of love and loss to insightful reflections on the changing times, this issue of Dixie offers a captivating glimpse into the heart and soul of the South.

Genres: Fiction, Non-fiction, Poetry

Themes: Southern culture, Love and relationships, Social change
