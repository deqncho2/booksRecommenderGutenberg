Synopsis: In 'Night and Morning, Complete' by Edward Bulwer Lytton, the lives of two men from different social backgrounds intertwine in a gripping tale of love, ambition, and redemption. As they navigate through the shadows of the night and the challenges of the morning, their paths converge in unexpected ways, leading to a series of dramatic events that will test their character and resilience. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this captivating novel explores the complexities of human nature and the power of second chances.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Social class, Redemption, Love and sacrifice
