Synopsis: In "Slingshot" by Lande, Irving W., a brilliant young inventor named Max is on a mission to revolutionize space travel. With the help of his friends, Max builds a remarkable slingshot that propels objects into space at incredible speeds. As their invention gains attention, they find themselves caught in a web of corporate greed, government interference, and personal rivalries. Max must navigate these challenges while staying true to his vision of making space accessible to all. Will he succeed in his quest or will his dreams be crushed by those who seek to control the future of space exploration?

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Innovation, Ambition, Power Struggles, Friendship
