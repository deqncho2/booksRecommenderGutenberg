Synopsis: 
In 'Jed's Boy: A Story of Adventures in the Great World War' by Goss, Warren Lee, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the eyes of Jed's young son. As the world is engulfed in the chaos of the Great World War, the boy finds himself caught up in a series of unexpected and dangerous adventures. From secret missions to encounters with enemy soldiers, he must navigate the treacherous landscape of war while trying to uncover the truth about his father's mysterious disappearance. With each twist and turn, the boy's courage and resilience are put to the test, leading him to discover the true meaning of bravery and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Family, Sacrifice
