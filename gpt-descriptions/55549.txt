Synopsis: In "My Memory of Gladstone" by Smith, Goldwin, a young journalist uncovers a hidden secret that threatens to unravel the legacy of one of the most influential political figures of the time. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic life of Gladstone, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, personal sacrifices, and moral dilemmas. With each revelation, the journalist's perception of Gladstone is challenged, forcing him to question his own beliefs and confront the complexities of power and idealism. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Political Thriller

Themes: Power and Influence, Morality and Ethics, Personal Sacrifice
