Synopsis: In 'Washington the Model of Character for American Youth', J. N. M'Jilton delivers a powerful address to the boys of the public schools, exploring the life and virtues of one of America's greatest leaders. Through captivating anecdotes and inspiring examples, M'Jilton reveals how George Washington's character and principles can serve as a guiding light for the youth of America. This address is a call to action, urging young readers to embrace the qualities that made Washington a true model of character and integrity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Self-help

Themes: Character development, Leadership, Virtues, Role models
