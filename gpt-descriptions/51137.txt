Synopsis: In 'Cause of Death' by Tadlock, Max, a renowned forensic pathologist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is called to investigate a series of bizarre deaths. As he delves deeper into the cases, Max uncovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his own life but also the lives of those closest to him. With time running out, Max must use his expertise and intuition to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Crime investigation, Danger, Betrayal
