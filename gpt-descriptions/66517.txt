Synopsis: 
In 'A Tragic Idyl' by Paul Bourget, a chance encounter between two strangers on a train leads to a captivating and forbidden love affair. As their relationship unfolds, the characters find themselves torn between their desires and the societal expectations that threaten to tear them apart. With its intricate exploration of passion, morality, and the complexities of human relationships, this gripping tale takes readers on an emotional rollercoaster that will leave them questioning the boundaries of love and the consequences of following one's heart.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Psychological Fiction

Themes: 
Forbidden love, Morality, Society's expectations, Passion, Human relationships
