Synopsis: In 'The Two-Gun Man' by Charles Alden Seltzer, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small western town, his reputation preceding him as a skilled gunslinger. As he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden agendas, the town's residents are forced to confront their own dark secrets. With danger lurking at every corner, the two-gun man must navigate treacherous alliances and face his own demons to bring justice to the town.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Action

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Morality
