Synopsis: 
'Contigo Pan y Cebolla' by Manuel Eduardo de Gorostiza is a captivating play that takes place in 19th century Mexico. The story revolves around a young couple, Luis and Ana, who are deeply in love but face numerous obstacles that threaten to tear them apart. As they navigate through societal expectations, family pressures, and personal sacrifices, their relationship is put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and turns, the play explores the complexities of love, loyalty, and the lengths one is willing to go for the person they cherish the most.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love, Sacrifice, Social Expectations
