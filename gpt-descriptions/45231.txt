Synopsis: 
'Motion Picture Operation, Stage Electrics and Illusions: A Practical Hand-book and Guide for Theater Electricians, Motion Picture Operators and Managers of Theaters and Productions' is a comprehensive guide that takes readers behind the scenes of the captivating world of theater and motion pictures. Written by Horstmann and Tousley, this practical handbook offers valuable insights and techniques for theater electricians, motion picture operators, and theater managers. From understanding the intricacies of stage electrics to creating mesmerizing illusions, this guide equips readers with the knowledge and skills necessary to bring the magic of the theater to life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Performing Arts, Technical Guide

Themes: Theater production, Motion picture operation, Stage electrics, Illusion techniques
