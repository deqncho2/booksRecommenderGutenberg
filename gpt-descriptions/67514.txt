Synopsis:
In the quaint village of Chiltern Green, a mysterious murder takes place, leaving the local police baffled. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, and the truth becomes more elusive than ever. With a cast of intriguing characters and a web of deceit, 'The House of the Arrow' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the shocking truth behind the murder.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes:
Deception, Betrayal, Secrets
