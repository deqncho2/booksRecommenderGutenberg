Synopsis: 

In the remote countryside of England, a charming old house named "Running Water" becomes the setting for a mysterious and thrilling tale. When a young couple, Richard and Mary, decide to make the house their new home, they soon discover that it holds a dark secret. As they delve deeper into the history of the house, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, hidden passages, and unexplained occurrences. With each passing day, the couple becomes more determined to uncover the truth, but they soon realize that some secrets are better left undisturbed.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Supernatural elements, Psychological suspense
