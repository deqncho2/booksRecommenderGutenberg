Synopsis: In 'A Jolly Fellowship' by Frank R. Stockton, a group of friends embarks on a seemingly innocent adventure that takes an unexpected turn. As they navigate through a series of peculiar events, their bond is tested, secrets are revealed, and the true nature of their fellowship is put to the ultimate test. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the mysteries that lie within.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Betrayal
