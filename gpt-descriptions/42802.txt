Synopsis: In 'The Riddle of the Mysterious Light', a renowned detective couple, Mr. and Mrs. Van Dusen, find themselves entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious light appears in the night sky, followed by a series of inexplicable events. As they delve deeper into the investigation, they uncover a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that lead them on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic light.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Intrigue, Deception, Unraveling the Unknown
