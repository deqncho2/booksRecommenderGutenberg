Synopsis: 
'Paris: A Sketch Book' by Eugène Béjot takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the enchanting streets of Paris. Through a collection of beautifully illustrated sketches, Béjot captures the essence and charm of the city, showcasing its iconic landmarks, bustling neighborhoods, and the everyday life of its inhabitants. From the grandeur of the Eiffel Tower to the quaint cafes of Montmartre, this sketchbook offers a unique perspective of Paris, inviting readers to immerse themselves in its timeless beauty and vibrant atmosphere.

Genres: Art, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: City life, Architecture, Cultural heritage
