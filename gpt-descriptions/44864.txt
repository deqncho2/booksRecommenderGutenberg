Synopsis:
'Coaching Days & Ways' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of coaching in the 19th century. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, the author, E. D. Cuming, paints a vivid picture of the golden age of coaching, where horse-drawn carriages ruled the roads and the thrill of travel was at its peak. From the bustling coaching inns to the daring coachmen and the diverse passengers they transported, this book offers a delightful glimpse into a bygone era. With a mix of historical facts, personal stories, and humorous observations, 'Coaching Days & Ways' is a charming and informative read that will transport readers back in time.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travel literature, Memoir

Themes: Transportation, Travel, History, Nostalgia
