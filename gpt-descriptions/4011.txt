Synopsis: 
In 'Epicoene; Or, The Silent Woman', a group of friends concoct a plan to teach one of their own a lesson about the dangers of marriage. They introduce him to a seemingly perfect woman named Epicoene, who is known for her silence. However, as the plot unfolds, the friends find themselves caught in a web of deceit, mistaken identities, and hilarious misunderstandings. As the truth begins to unravel, the characters must confront their own desires and the consequences of their actions.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Farce

Themes: Deception, Gender roles, Marriage, Social satire
