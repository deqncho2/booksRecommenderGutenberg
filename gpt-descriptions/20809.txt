Synopsis: 
In 'Archie's Mistake' by Wyatt, G. E., Archie, a young and ambitious architect, finds himself caught in a web of deceit and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret during a routine renovation project. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Archie's life takes a thrilling turn, filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With his reputation and life at stake, Archie must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
