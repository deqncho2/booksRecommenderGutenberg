Synopsis: 
In 'A Love Crime' by Paul Bourget, a seemingly ordinary love affair takes a dark and twisted turn when a young woman becomes entangled in a web of deceit, manipulation, and obsession. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the line between love and madness becomes blurred. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this psychological thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Love and obsession, Deception and manipulation, Morality and ethics
