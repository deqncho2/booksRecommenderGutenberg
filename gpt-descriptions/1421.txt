Synopsis: 
In 'Mrs. Lirriper's Legacy', we are introduced to the delightful Mrs. Lirriper, a warm-hearted and observant landlady who has seen it all. When a mysterious young woman arrives at her boarding house, Mrs. Lirriper's life takes an unexpected turn. As she unravels the secrets and dramas surrounding her new tenant, she finds herself drawn into a world of intrigue and danger. With her wit and compassion, Mrs. Lirriper becomes an unlikely heroine, navigating through the complexities of love, betrayal, and redemption. 

Genres: 
Drama, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Love and friendship, Secrets and deception, Redemption
