Synopsis: 

In 'Narrative and Critical History of America, Vol. 1 (of 8): Aboriginal America', embark on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of the Americas before the arrival of European settlers. Delve into the fascinating world of the indigenous peoples, their diverse cultures, and the intricate civilizations that flourished across the continent. From the ancient Mayans and Aztecs to the mysterious mound builders of North America, this volume unveils the awe-inspiring achievements and complex histories of the original inhabitants of the Americas.

Genres: History, Anthropology, Archaeology

Themes: Indigenous cultures, Pre-Columbian civilizations, Exploration and discovery
