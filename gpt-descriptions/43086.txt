Synopsis: 
In "My Own Affairs" by Louise, Princess of Belgium, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the life of a royal princess who defies societal expectations and embraces her own desires. From her early years as a rebellious teenager to her tumultuous love affairs and political involvement, Louise's story is one of passion, independence, and the pursuit of personal happiness. As she navigates the complexities of her royal status and the constraints placed upon her, readers will be enthralled by her determination to forge her own path, even at the risk of scandal and heartbreak. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling narrative, "My Own Affairs" offers a fascinating glimpse into the hidden world of a princess who dared to defy convention.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Independence, Love, Society's Expectations
