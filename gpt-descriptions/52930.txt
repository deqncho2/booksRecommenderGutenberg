Synopsis: 

'Names: and Their Meaning; A Book for the Curious' by Leopold Wagner is a captivating exploration of the fascinating world of names and their significance. Delving into the origins, history, and hidden meanings behind a wide range of names, this book takes readers on a journey through the rich tapestry of human nomenclature. From ancient civilizations to modern times, from common names to rare gems, Wagner uncovers the stories and symbolism that lie within each name, offering a thought-provoking and enlightening read for anyone curious about the power of words.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Linguistics

Themes: Etymology, Cultural Significance, Personal Identity
