Synopsis: 

'With Spurs of Gold: Heroes of Chivalry and their Deeds' takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of knights and chivalry. From the legendary tales of King Arthur and his Knights of the Round Table to the lesser-known but equally inspiring stories of medieval heroes, this book delves into the lives and adventures of these noble warriors. With vivid descriptions and thrilling narratives, the authors bring to life the courage, honor, and sacrifice that defined the age of chivalry. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Courage, Heroism
