Synopsis: 
In 'Mildred Keith' by Martha Finley, follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Mildred as she navigates the challenges of life in the mid-19th century. From her idyllic childhood on a farm to her adventures in a bustling city, Mildred's story is filled with heartwarming friendships, unexpected twists, and moments of profound self-discovery. As she faces both joy and sorrow, Mildred's resilience and determination shine through, making her an inspiring protagonist that readers will root for until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: Resilience, Friendship, Self-discovery
