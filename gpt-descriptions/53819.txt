Synopsis: In 'The Coil of Carne' by John Oxenham, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young man finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and hidden desires. Set against the backdrop of a remote coastal village, the protagonist's journey takes him through a labyrinth of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of truth. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic Coil of Carne, he discovers that nothing is as it seems, and the consequences of his choices may be more far-reaching than he ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Redemption
