Synopsis: In 'Billy Whiskers at the Circus' by Montgomery, Frances Trego, follow the adventures of Billy Whiskers, a mischievous and clever goat, as he finds himself in the midst of the exciting world of the circus. From daring acrobats to fierce lions, Billy Whiskers encounters a variety of colorful characters and thrilling acts. Will Billy be able to navigate the chaos and find his way back home? This delightful tale is filled with humor, excitement, and unexpected twists that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
