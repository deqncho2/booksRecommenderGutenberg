Synopsis: In 'The Sport of the Gods' by Paul Laurence Dunbar, a family's life takes a dramatic turn when they are uprooted from their comfortable home in the South and forced to start anew in the bustling city of New York. As they navigate the challenges of urban life, they encounter betrayal, tragedy, and the harsh realities of racial discrimination. Amidst the turmoil, they must find the strength to rise above their circumstances and redefine their own destinies.

Genres: Fiction, African American literature, Social realism

Themes: Racial discrimination, Family bonds, Resilience, Social inequality
