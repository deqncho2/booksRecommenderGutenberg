Synopsis: In "The Story of John Wesley, Told to Boys and Girls" by Marianne Kirlew, readers are transported to the fascinating life of John Wesley, a remarkable figure who played a pivotal role in the history of Christianity. Through captivating storytelling, the book delves into Wesley's journey from a young boy with a strong sense of justice to becoming one of the most influential religious leaders of his time. As readers follow Wesley's adventures, they will witness his struggles, triumphs, and unwavering dedication to his faith. This inspiring tale is filled with adventure, self-discovery, and the power of perseverance.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Religious Literature

Themes: Faith and spirituality, personal growth, social justice
