Synopsis: In 'Out of the North' by Howard V. Sutherland, a young woman named Evelyn embarks on a journey to the remote and mysterious North, seeking answers to the secrets of her past. As she navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters enigmatic characters, Evelyn's quest becomes intertwined with a larger battle between ancient forces. With each step closer to the truth, she discovers that her own destiny is intricately connected to the fate of an entire world.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Fate, Redemption
