Synopsis: 
In 'Jack of the Pony Express; Or, The Young Rider of the Mountain Trails' by Frank V. Webster, readers are transported to the thrilling world of the Pony Express, where danger lurks around every bend. Follow the courageous Jack as he embarks on a perilous journey through treacherous mountain trails, facing wild animals, hostile outlaws, and unpredictable weather conditions. With his determination and unwavering spirit, Jack must overcome numerous obstacles to deliver the mail and fulfill his duty as a Pony Express rider. Will he succeed in his mission and prove himself as a true hero?

Genres: Adventure, Western, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Coming of Age
