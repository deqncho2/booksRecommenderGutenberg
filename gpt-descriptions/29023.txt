Synopsis: In 'Treasure Valley' by Mary Esther Miller MacGregor, a young woman named Emma embarks on a journey to the rugged and mysterious Treasure Valley, where she uncovers a hidden family secret that will change her life forever. As she delves deeper into the valley's secrets, Emma finds herself torn between loyalty to her family and the desire for personal freedom. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate treacherous landscapes and confront her own fears to uncover the truth that lies within Treasure Valley.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Personal freedom, Courage, Self-discovery
