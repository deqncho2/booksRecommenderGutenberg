Synopsis: 

In 'A Short Treatise on the Section of the Prostate Gland in Lithotomy', C. Aston Key presents a groundbreaking method for conducting a delicate surgical procedure. With a focus on the principles of Cheselden, Key unveils a safe and easy approach to operating on the prostate gland. This concise treatise offers valuable insights into a complex medical procedure, providing a glimpse into the advancements of surgical techniques during the time.

Genres: Medical literature, Surgical techniques, Historical non-fiction

Themes: Medical innovation, Surgical advancements, Historical medical practices
