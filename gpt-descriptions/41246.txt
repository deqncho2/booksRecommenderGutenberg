Synopsis: 
Embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting cities of Crete, Constantinople, Naples, and Florence as Annie Vivanti shares her three-month adventure abroad. From the vibrant streets of Constantinople to the breathtaking landscapes of Crete, Annie's vivid descriptions transport readers to each destination, immersing them in the rich history, culture, and beauty of these remarkable places. As she encounters fascinating characters, navigates unexpected challenges, and discovers hidden treasures, Annie's journey becomes a transformative experience that will leave readers yearning for their own adventure.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Immersion, Self-Discovery
