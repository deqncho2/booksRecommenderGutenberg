Synopsis: In the small New England town of Bylow Hill, a mysterious and haunting secret lies buried beneath the surface. As the townspeople grapple with their own personal demons, a series of unexplained events begins to unravel the truth, leading to a shocking revelation that will forever change their lives. With its atmospheric setting and complex characters, 'Bylow Hill' delves into the dark corners of human nature, exploring the depths of guilt, redemption, and the power of secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Guilt, Redemption, Small-town dynamics
