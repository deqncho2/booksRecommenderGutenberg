Synopsis: In 'Surprise House' by Abbie Farwell Brown, a young girl named Dorothy discovers a mysterious house hidden deep within the woods. As she explores its enchanting rooms, she uncovers secrets that will change her life forever. With each new discovery, Dorothy's curiosity grows, leading her on a captivating journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will she uncover the truth behind the Surprise House and its inhabitants?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Curiosity, Discovery, Secrets
