Synopsis: In 'The Romantic' by Sinclair, May, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a whirlwind of passion and desire when she meets a mysterious and enigmatic stranger. As their relationship intensifies, Emily is drawn into a world of forbidden love and dangerous secrets, where the line between reality and fantasy becomes blurred. With each encounter, she becomes more entangled in a web of intrigue, leading her to question her own desires and the true nature of love. As the story unfolds, Emily must navigate the complexities of her heart and make choices that will forever alter her life.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Forbidden love, Identity, Desire, Secrets, Reality vs. Fantasy
