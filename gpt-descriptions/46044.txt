Synopsis: In 'A Little Change: A Farce in One Scene' by Sydney Grundy, chaos ensues when a case of mistaken identity leads to a hilarious series of events. As the characters navigate through misunderstandings, miscommunications, and unexpected surprises, the audience is taken on a rollercoaster ride of laughter and confusion. With witty dialogue and clever plot twists, this farce guarantees an evening of uproarious entertainment.

Genres: Comedy, Farce

Themes: Mistaken identity, Miscommunication, Humor
