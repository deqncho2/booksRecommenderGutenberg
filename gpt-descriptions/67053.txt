Synopsis: 
In 'Janet: A Stock-Farm Scout' by Lillian Elizabeth Roy, follow the captivating journey of Janet, a young woman with an unyielding spirit and a deep love for the land. Set in the rugged and untamed landscapes of the American West, Janet embarks on a daring adventure as a stock-farm scout, defying societal expectations and facing numerous challenges along the way. With her unwavering determination and a profound connection to nature, Janet's story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, revealing the resilience of the human spirit and the power of following one's true calling.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Independence, Gender Roles, Connection to Nature
