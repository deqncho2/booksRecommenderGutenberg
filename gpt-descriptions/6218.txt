Synopsis: In the small village of St. Placide, a mysterious stranger arrives at the Sign of the Eagle inn, bringing with him a dark secret and a troubled past. As the villagers become entangled in his enigmatic presence, their lives are forever changed. Secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and love is put to the ultimate test. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century rural Quebec, "At the Sign of the Eagle" is a gripping tale of intrigue, redemption, and the power of human connection.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Redemption, Love and sacrifice
