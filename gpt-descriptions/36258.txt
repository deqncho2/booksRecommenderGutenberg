Synopsis: In 'The Beautiful People' by Beaumont, Charles, a mysterious invitation leads a struggling artist into the glamorous and enigmatic world of the elite. As he becomes entangled in their seductive lifestyle, he discovers that beneath their flawless exteriors lies a dark secret that threatens to consume them all. With his own sanity at stake, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit, power, and desire to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Social Class, Obsession
