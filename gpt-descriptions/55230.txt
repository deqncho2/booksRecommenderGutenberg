Synopsis: In 'The Day After Death; Or, Our Future Life According to Science (New Edition)', Figuier takes readers on a captivating journey beyond the realm of the living. Through a scientific lens, he explores what lies ahead after death, delving into the mysteries of the afterlife and the potential for an existence beyond our earthly realm. With a blend of scientific research, philosophical ponderings, and imaginative speculation, Figuier presents a thought-provoking exploration of what may await us in the great unknown.

Genres: Science Fiction, Philosophy, Speculative Non-Fiction

Themes: Afterlife, Science and Spirituality, Existentialism
