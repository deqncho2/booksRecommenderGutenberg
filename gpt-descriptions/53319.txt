Synopsis: In 'Initiative in Evolution' by Kidd, Walter, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything we know about the origins of life. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger, forcing him to question his own beliefs and confront the ethical implications of his work. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect his discovery from those who would use it for nefarious purposes.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Evolution, Ethics, Conspiracy, Scientific Discovery
