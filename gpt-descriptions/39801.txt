Synopsis: 
In 'Fighting with French: A Tale of the New Army' by Herbert Strang, readers are transported to the tumultuous times of World War I. The story follows the journey of a young British soldier, Jack Meredith, who finds himself fighting alongside the French army. As Jack navigates the horrors of war, he forms unexpected friendships, faces unimaginable challenges, and discovers the true meaning of courage and sacrifice. With vivid descriptions and heart-wrenching moments, this gripping tale takes readers on an unforgettable adventure through the trenches of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Sacrifice, Survival
