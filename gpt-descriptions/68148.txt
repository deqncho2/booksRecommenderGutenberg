Synopsis: In 'Tea and the effects of tea drinking', W. Scott Tebb takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of tea, exploring its origins, cultural significance, and the profound impact it has on our health and well-being. From the ancient tea ceremonies of China to the bustling tea houses of England, Tebb delves into the secrets and rituals surrounding this beloved beverage. With a blend of historical anecdotes, scientific research, and personal experiences, this book offers a refreshing perspective on the multifaceted effects of tea drinking, leaving readers eager to savor every sip.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Health

Themes: Cultural traditions, Wellness, Historical significance
