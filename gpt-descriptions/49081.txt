Synopsis: In 'Reube Dare's Shad Boat: A Tale of the Tide Country', readers are transported to the enchanting world of the Tide Country, where the protagonist, Reube Dare, embarks on a captivating adventure. Set against the backdrop of a small coastal community, Reube's life takes an unexpected turn when he inherits a shad boat, a traditional fishing vessel with a rich history. As he navigates the challenges of mastering the boat and facing the perils of the sea, Reube discovers the true essence of courage, resilience, and the indomitable spirit of the Tide Country.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Heritage and Tradition, Courage and Resilience, Connection to Nature
