Synopsis: 
In 'The Standard Light Operas, Their Plots and Their Music', George P. Upton takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of light operas. From the lively melodies of Gilbert and Sullivan to the romantic tales of Offenbach and Strauss, Upton delves into the plots and music of these beloved theatrical productions. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, he brings to life the whimsical characters, humorous situations, and heartfelt emotions that make these operas so memorable. Whether you are a seasoned opera enthusiast or a newcomer to the genre, this book is sure to ignite your curiosity and leave you eager to experience the magic of these timeless performances.

Genres: Music, Theatre, Non-fiction

Themes: Love and romance, Comedy and humor, Musical composition and performance
