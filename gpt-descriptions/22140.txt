Synopsis: 
In 'The Wrong Woman' by Charles D. Stewart, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of deception and danger when she is mistaken for someone else. As she tries to unravel the truth, she becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where nothing is as it seems. With her life on the line, she must navigate through a maze of secrets and lies to uncover the shocking truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Danger
