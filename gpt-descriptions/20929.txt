Synopsis: 
In "Little Novels of Italy," Maurice Hewlett takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of Italy. Through a collection of interconnected short stories, he introduces us to a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique tales of love, passion, and adventure. From the bustling streets of Florence to the serene countryside of Tuscany, these little novels paint a vivid picture of Italian life, filled with romance, intrigue, and unexpected twists. As we delve into the lives of these captivating individuals, we are transported to a world where passion and desire intertwine with tradition and societal expectations, creating a tapestry of emotions that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Italian culture and traditions, Society and societal expectations
