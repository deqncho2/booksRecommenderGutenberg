Synopsis: 
In 'The Love-Story of Aliette Brunton' by Frankau, Gilbert, we are introduced to Aliette, a young and spirited woman who finds herself torn between duty and desire. Set against the backdrop of post-World War I England, Aliette's journey takes her through the complexities of love, societal expectations, and the pursuit of personal happiness. As she navigates the challenges of her relationships, Aliette must confront her own desires and make difficult choices that will shape her future. With its captivating characters and richly detailed setting, this novel explores the depths of human emotions and the power of love.

Genres: 
Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Duty and Sacrifice, Society and Expectations
