Synopsis: In "You Never Know Your Luck; being the story of a matrimonial deserter. Volume 1," we follow the captivating journey of a man who, driven by a series of unexpected events, finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and adventure. As he navigates through the complexities of his own choices, he discovers that fate has a way of leading us down unexpected paths, and that the consequences of our actions can be both thrilling and devastating. With each twist and turn, the protagonist's life takes on new dimensions, leaving readers eagerly anticipating what lies ahead in Volume 2.

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and relationships, Fate and destiny, Consequences of one's actions
