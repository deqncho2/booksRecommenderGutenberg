Synopsis: In 'The Confession: A Novel' by Maksim Gorky, a young man named Alexei finds himself caught in a web of secrets and lies when he becomes entangled with a mysterious woman. As their relationship deepens, Alexei is forced to confront his own past and make a life-altering decision that will test his loyalty and integrity. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century Russia, this gripping tale explores the complexities of love, betrayal, and the search for personal identity.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Psychological Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Identity and Self-discovery, Morality and Ethics
