Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Thirty-ninth, or the Dorsetshire Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of the regiment from 1702 to 1853. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, this historical record unveils the untold stories of bravery, sacrifice, and resilience that shaped the regiment's legacy. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will be transported to pivotal moments in history, witnessing the regiment's involvement in significant battles and campaigns. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Cannon brings to life the triumphs and challenges faced by the Dorsetshire Regiment of Foot, leaving readers eager to uncover the remarkable tales that lie within its pages.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Resilience, Legacy
