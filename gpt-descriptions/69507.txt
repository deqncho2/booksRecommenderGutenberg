Synopsis: 
'Color mixing guide: For artists, painters, decorators, printing pressmen, show card writers, sign painters, color mixers. Gives color mixtures by parts' by John L. King is a comprehensive guide that unlocks the secrets of color mixing for various creative professionals. Whether you are an artist, painter, decorator, printing pressman, show card writer, or sign painter, this book provides invaluable insights into achieving the perfect color combinations. With detailed instructions and color mixtures by parts, King's guide offers practical advice and techniques to enhance your artistic endeavors and bring your creations to life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Reference

Themes: Color theory, Creative techniques, Professional development
