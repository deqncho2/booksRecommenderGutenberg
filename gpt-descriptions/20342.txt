Synopsis: In 'Grace Harlowe's Problem' by Josephine Chase, Grace finds herself facing a perplexing situation that tests her loyalty, courage, and determination. As she navigates through unexpected challenges, she must rely on her wit and resourcefulness to unravel the mystery that threatens to disrupt her life and the lives of those she holds dear. With each twist and turn, Grace's problem becomes more complex, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and resilience.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Determination, Self-discovery
