Synopsis: In 'Further Foolishness' by Stephen Leacock, prepare to be entertained by a collection of hilarious and absurd stories that will leave you in stitches. From a mischievous parrot causing chaos to a man who becomes a human magnet, Leacock's wit and humor shine through in this delightful collection. Each story is filled with unexpected twists and turns, showcasing the author's unique ability to find humor in the most ordinary situations. Get ready for a rollicking ride through Leacock's world of foolishness and laughter.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Short Stories

Themes: Absurdity, Irony, Social Commentary
