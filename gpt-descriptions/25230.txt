Synopsis: In 'The Return of Blue Pete' by Allan, Luke, a retired detective is drawn back into the world of crime-solving when an old nemesis resurfaces, threatening to unravel the peaceful life he has built. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets and deception that forces him to confront his own past and make difficult choices. With suspenseful twists and turns, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Justice
