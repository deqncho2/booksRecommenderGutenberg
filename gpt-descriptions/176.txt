Synopsis: In "Roderick Hudson," a talented young sculptor named Roderick Hudson captures the attention of a wealthy American art patron who becomes his benefactor. As Roderick's career takes off, he embarks on a journey to Europe, where he encounters love, passion, and the complexities of artistic ambition. However, his relationships and personal struggles threaten to overshadow his artistic genius, leading to a captivating exploration of the human spirit and the pursuit of artistic perfection.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Psychological Drama

Themes: Artistic ambition, Love and passion, Personal struggles, Human spirit
