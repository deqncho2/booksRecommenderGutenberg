Synopsis: In the midst of the Black-Hawk Uprising, a group of border riflemen find themselves entangled in a web of mystery and danger. As they navigate the treacherous forest, they encounter a sinister figure known as the Forest Fiend, whose presence threatens their very existence. With their lives hanging in the balance, the riflemen must unravel the secrets of the uprising and confront the enigmatic Forest Fiend before it's too late.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Betrayal, Survival
