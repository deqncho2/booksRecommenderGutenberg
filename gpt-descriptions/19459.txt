Synopsis: In 'Born Again' by Alfred W. Lawson, a mysterious event takes place that transforms the lives of ordinary people, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. As the characters navigate through unexpected twists and turns, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and question their beliefs. With elements of mystery and suspense, this captivating novel explores the power of redemption and the possibility of finding a new purpose in life.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Spiritual Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Redemption, Belief, Transformation
