Synopsis: In 'The Red Cockade' by Stanley John Weyman, a young French nobleman finds himself caught up in the tumultuous events of the French Revolution. As he navigates the treacherous political landscape, he becomes entangled in a dangerous love affair and is forced to make difficult choices that will determine his fate. With its gripping plot and vivid historical backdrop, this thrilling tale of love, loyalty, and betrayal will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Revolution, Loyalty, Betrayal
