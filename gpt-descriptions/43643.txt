Synopsis: 
In 'Foods and Culinary Utensils of the Ancients', Martyn Charles takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the fascinating world of ancient cuisine and the tools used to prepare it. From the exotic spices of the Silk Road to the ingenious inventions of the Roman Empire, this book unveils the secrets of the past, revealing how our ancestors transformed simple ingredients into culinary masterpieces. With vivid descriptions and intriguing anecdotes, Charles paints a vivid picture of the ancient culinary landscape, leaving readers hungry for more.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Food and Drink

Themes: Culinary history, Ancient civilizations, Food culture
