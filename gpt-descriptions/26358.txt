Synopsis: 
'Eastern Tales by Many Story Tellers' is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that transport readers to the mystical and vibrant world of the East. From ancient legends to modern fables, this anthology weaves together tales of love, adventure, and the supernatural, each one more captivating than the last. As readers delve into the pages, they will encounter cunning tricksters, wise sages, and courageous heroes, all navigating a rich tapestry of cultural traditions and timeless wisdom. With each story, the reader is drawn deeper into the captivating allure of the East, where imagination knows no bounds and the power of storytelling reigns supreme.

Genres: 
Fiction, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: 
Cultural diversity, Wisdom, Adventure
