Synopsis: 

In 'History of English Humour, Vol. 1: With an Introduction upon Ancient Humour', A. G. K. L'Estrange takes readers on a delightful journey through the evolution of English humor. From ancient times to the 18th century, L'Estrange explores the origins and development of comedy, showcasing the wit and humor of various eras and cultures. With a keen eye for detail and a witty writing style, L'Estrange presents a comprehensive and entertaining account of the history of English humor, offering fascinating insights into the comedic traditions that have shaped our understanding of laughter.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Humor

Themes: Evolution of humor, Cultural influences, Comedy as a reflection of society
