Synopsis: 
In 'The Prodigal Pro Tem' by Frederick Orin Bartlett, a young lawyer named John finds himself caught in a web of deceit and corruption when he takes on a high-profile case. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to destroy everything he holds dear. With his career and personal life hanging in the balance, John must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and seek justice, all while facing unexpected challenges and betrayals.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Betrayal, Justice, Redemption
