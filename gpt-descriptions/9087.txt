Synopsis: 
In the gripping novel 'Eleanor' by Ward, Humphry, Mrs., a young woman named Eleanor finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she is unexpectedly thrust into a world of wealth and privilege. As she navigates the treacherous waters of high society, Eleanor must unravel the mysteries surrounding her own identity and confront the dark forces that threaten to destroy everything she holds dear. With its twists and turns, 'Eleanor' is a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and the lengths one will go to protect their true self.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Social Class
