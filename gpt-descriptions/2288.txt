Synopsis: In 'Through Russia', Gorky takes us on a captivating journey through the vast and diverse landscapes of Russia. Through his vivid descriptions and encounters with various characters, he unveils the hidden layers of Russian society, from the bustling cities to the remote countryside. As he travels, Gorky delves into the complexities of human nature, exploring themes of poverty, inequality, and the resilience of the human spirit. This travelogue offers a unique perspective on the Russian people and their struggles, painting a rich and compelling portrait of a nation in transition.

Genres: Travelogue, Autobiography, Social Commentary

Themes: Poverty, Inequality, Resilience, Social Change
