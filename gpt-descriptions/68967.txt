Synopsis: 
Step into the world of exquisite craftsmanship and timeless elegance with 'Stoles and Shawls: Hairpin Lace, Knitted, Crocheted' by American Thread Company. This captivating book takes you on a journey through the art of creating stunning stoles and shawls using various techniques such as hairpin lace, knitting, and crocheting. From delicate lace patterns to intricate stitches, each design is a testament to the beauty and versatility of these accessories. With detailed instructions and inspiring photographs, this book is a treasure trove for both beginners and experienced crafters, offering endless possibilities to create unique and stylish pieces that will be cherished for generations to come.

Genres: Craft, Fashion, Design

Themes: Creativity, Beauty, Tradition
