Synopsis: In 'Patience' by James W. Alexander, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, captivating the residents with his enigmatic charm and uncanny abilities. As the townspeople become increasingly infatuated with him, they soon discover that his presence brings both blessings and curses, leading to a series of unexpected events that will test their faith, loyalty, and ultimately, their patience.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Drama

Themes: Faith, Loyalty, Temptation, Human Nature
