Synopsis: In 'Heiress of Haddon' by W. E. Doubleday, a young woman named Emily finds herself unexpectedly inheriting a grand estate in the English countryside. As she navigates the challenges of her new role, she uncovers long-held family secrets and must confront the ghosts of the past. With the help of a mysterious stranger, Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and love, all while trying to protect her newfound inheritance from those who would do anything to claim it for themselves.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Inheritance, Love and self-discovery
