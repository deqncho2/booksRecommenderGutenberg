Synopsis: In 'The Lost Continent', a young adventurer embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the mysteries of a forgotten land. Set in the early 20th century, our protagonist finds himself in a world filled with treacherous landscapes, ancient civilizations, and hidden dangers. As he delves deeper into this uncharted territory, he encounters strange creatures, encounters unexpected allies, and unravels the secrets that have been lost to time. With each step, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and must confront his own fears and desires. Will he be able to survive the perils of the lost continent and uncover its hidden truths?

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Discovery, Identity
