Synopsis: In 'The Prior Claim' by Eleanor Mercein Kelly, a young woman named Amelia finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits a mysterious estate from a distant relative. As she delves into the history of the property, Amelia uncovers a dark family legacy that threatens to unravel her own life. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, she must navigate through hidden motives and unexpected alliances to uncover the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Inheritance, Betrayal, Identity
