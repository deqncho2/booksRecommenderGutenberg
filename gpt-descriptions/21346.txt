Synopsis: 
'The Sot-weed Factor: or, A Voyage to Maryland. A Satyr.: In which is Describ'd The Laws, Government, Courts and Constitutions of the Country, and also the Buildings, Feasts, Frolicks, Entertainments and Drunken Humours of the Inhabitants of that Part of America. In Burlesque Verse.' follows the hilarious misadventures of a young Englishman named Ebenezer Cooke, who is sent to the colony of Maryland in the late 17th century. As he navigates the unfamiliar land, he encounters a colorful cast of characters, absurd situations, and outrageous events. From courtrooms to drunken revelries, Cooke's journey is filled with satire, wit, and a keen observation of the human condition. 

Genres: Satire, Burlesque, Historical Fiction

Themes: Colonialism, Identity, Social Critique
