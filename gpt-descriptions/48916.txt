Synopsis: In 'Outings at Odd Times' by Charles C. Abbott, a group of friends embarks on a series of spontaneous adventures that take them to unexpected places and push the boundaries of their comfort zones. From secret midnight escapades to impromptu road trips, the characters find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of thrilling and sometimes dangerous situations. As they navigate through these unconventional outings, they discover hidden truths about themselves and the world around them, ultimately leading to profound personal growth and self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Spontaneity, Exploration, Self-discovery
