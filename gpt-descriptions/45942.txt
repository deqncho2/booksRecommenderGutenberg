Synopsis: In 'Manpower' by Lincoln C. Andrews, a young engineer named John finds himself thrust into a high-stakes battle against corporate greed and corruption. As he navigates the cutthroat world of big business, John must confront his own moral compass and make difficult choices that could change the course of his career and his life. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Corporate Fiction, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Power and Corruption
