Synopsis: 

'Half hours on the quarter-deck: The Spanish Armada to Sir Cloudesley Shovel 1670' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events of the Spanish Armada and the heroic exploits of Sir Cloudesley Shovel. Written by an anonymous author, this historical work offers a vivid and immersive account of the naval battles, political intrigues, and personal sacrifices that shaped this pivotal period in British history. From the grandeur of the Armada's ill-fated voyage to the resilience and bravery of Sir Cloudesley Shovel, this book paints a compelling portrait of the triumphs and tragedies of the era. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Heroism, Naval warfare, National identity, Leadership
