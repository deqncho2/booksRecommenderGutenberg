Synopsis: In 'A Lively Bit of the Front: A Tale of the New Zealand Rifles on the Western Front', author Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a gripping journey to the battlefields of World War I. Follow the courageous soldiers of the New Zealand Rifles as they navigate the treacherous trenches, face the horrors of war, and forge unbreakable bonds of camaraderie amidst the chaos. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this tale immerses readers in the harrowing experiences of these brave men, capturing both the brutality and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Survival, Sacrifice
