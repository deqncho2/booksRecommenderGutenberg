Synopsis: In 'A Question of Marriage' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, a young woman finds herself torn between two suitors, each offering a different path in life. As she navigates the complexities of love, duty, and societal expectations, she must make a decision that will shape her future. With engaging characters and unexpected twists, this novel explores the challenges of choosing between passion and stability, and the consequences of one's choices.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Victorian Fiction

Themes: Love and duty, societal expectations, choices and consequences
