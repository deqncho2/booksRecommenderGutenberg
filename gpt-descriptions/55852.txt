Synopsis: In 'The Children of Cupa' by Mary Ellen Mannix, a small town is plagued by a mysterious curse that has haunted its residents for generations. When a group of teenagers stumble upon an ancient artifact, they unwittingly unleash a powerful force that threatens to consume them and their entire community. As they race against time to unravel the secrets of their town's dark past, they must confront their deepest fears and make unimaginable sacrifices to break the curse before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Young Adult

Themes: Legacy, Friendship, Sacrifice
