Synopsis: In 'A Dash for a Throne' by Arthur W. Marchmont, a young and ambitious protagonist finds themselves thrust into a world of political intrigue and power struggles. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of courtly life, they must make difficult choices and form unlikely alliances to secure their place on the throne. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale keeps readers on the edge of their seats, wondering who will ultimately claim the coveted throne.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Power, Betrayal, Loyalty
