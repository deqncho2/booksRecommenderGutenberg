Synopsis: In 'Our Little Korean Cousin', readers are transported to the fascinating world of Korea through the eyes of a young Korean girl named Sunia. Join Sunia as she takes you on a journey through her daily life, filled with traditional customs, beautiful landscapes, and heartwarming family moments. From exploring bustling markets to participating in traditional ceremonies, Sunia's story offers a captivating glimpse into the rich culture and heritage of Korea.

Genres: Children's Literature, Cultural Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cultural identity, Family bonds, Tradition and customs
