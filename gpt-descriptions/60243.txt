Synopsis: In 'A Historical Sketch of Company "B," Eighteenth Regiment Ohio Volunteer Infantry: Three Months Service', Palmer and Jewett take readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of Company "B" during their three months of service in the Civil War. From the excitement of enlistment to the harsh realities of battle, this historical sketch offers a vivid and intimate portrayal of the soldiers' lives, their camaraderie, and the challenges they faced on the front lines. Through personal anecdotes and detailed accounts, the authors bring to life the bravery, sacrifice, and resilience of these ordinary men turned heroes.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Resilience
