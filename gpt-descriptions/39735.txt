Synopsis: In 'Ti-Ping Tien-Kwoh: The History of the Ti-Ping Revolution (Volume II)' by Augustus F. Lindley, the enthralling continuation of the Ti-Ping Revolution unfolds, delving deeper into the epic struggle for power and freedom in 19th-century China. As the Ti-Ping Heavenly Kingdom faces internal conflicts and external threats, the reader is taken on a captivating journey through battles, political intrigue, and the personal stories of key figures. Lindley's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life this pivotal period in Chinese history, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of the Ti-Ping Revolution.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Political Drama

Themes: Revolution, Power Struggles, Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity
