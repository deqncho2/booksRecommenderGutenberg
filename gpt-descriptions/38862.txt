Synopsis: In 'My Lords of Strogue, Vol. 2 (of 3): A Chronicle of Ireland, from the Convention to the Union' by Wingfield, Lewis, the captivating saga of Ireland continues as political intrigue, forbidden love, and the struggle for independence intertwine. As the Convention debates the future of the nation, the Lords of Strogue find themselves caught in a web of treachery and loyalty, where their choices will shape the destiny of Ireland. Amidst the backdrop of a changing society, the characters navigate personal and political challenges, testing their resolve and forcing them to confront their deepest desires. With each page, the stakes rise higher, and the reader is left eagerly anticipating the final installment of this enthralling historical epic.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Betrayal, National Identity
