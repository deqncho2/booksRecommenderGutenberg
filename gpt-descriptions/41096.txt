Synopsis: In 'All-Hallow Eve; or, The Test of Futurity,' Curtis takes readers on a thrilling journey through time and space. The story revolves around a mysterious event that occurs on Halloween night, where a group of individuals find themselves transported to different eras and dimensions. As they navigate through these unfamiliar worlds, they must confront their deepest fears and make life-altering choices. With each leap, the stakes grow higher, and the characters must unravel the secrets of their own destinies. This captivating tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Time Travel

Themes: Fate and Free Will, Identity, Confronting Fear
