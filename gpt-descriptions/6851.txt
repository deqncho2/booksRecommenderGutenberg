Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a young and adventurous girl, finds herself embarking on a thrilling journey to Snow Camp. However, her excitement quickly turns to fear when she becomes lost in the vast and treacherous backwoods. With only her determination and resourcefulness to rely on, Ruth must navigate through the wilderness, facing unexpected challenges and discovering hidden secrets along the way. Will she find her way back to safety, or will she be forever lost in the backwoods?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Survival, Courage, Self-discovery
