Synopsis: In 'The Delegate from Venus' by Henry Slesar, a mysterious visitor from another planet arrives on Earth, claiming to be a delegate from Venus. As he interacts with humans, his extraordinary abilities and enigmatic nature captivate those around him. However, as his true intentions become unclear, the world is left questioning whether this delegate is a savior or a harbinger of destruction.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Extraterrestrial life, Power and manipulation, Human nature
