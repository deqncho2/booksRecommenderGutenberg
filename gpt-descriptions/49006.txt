Synopsis: In 'On the apostolical succession: Parochial lectures, second series', William J. Irons delves into the intriguing and controversial topic of apostolic succession within the Christian Church. Through a series of thought-provoking lectures, Irons explores the historical, theological, and spiritual significance of this doctrine, shedding light on its implications for the present-day Church. As he navigates through the complexities of this subject, Irons captivates readers with his insightful analysis and compelling arguments, leaving them eager to uncover the secrets and mysteries surrounding apostolic succession.

Genres: Theological discourse, Religious studies, Christian literature

Themes: Apostolic succession, Church history, The authority of the clergy, The continuity of the Church
