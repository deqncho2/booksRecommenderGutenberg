Synopsis: 

In "Selections from American poetry, with special reference to Poe, Longfellow, Lowell and Whittier," readers are taken on a captivating journey through the works of four iconic American poets. This anthology showcases the hauntingly beautiful verses of Edgar Allan Poe, the lyrical storytelling of Henry Wadsworth Longfellow, the thought-provoking social commentary of James Russell Lowell, and the heartfelt expressions of John Greenleaf Whittier. From tales of love and loss to reflections on nature and society, these carefully selected poems offer a glimpse into the rich tapestry of American literature.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology

Themes: Love, Loss, Nature, Society, Social Commentary
