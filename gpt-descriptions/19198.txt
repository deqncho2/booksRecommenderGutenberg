Synopsis: In 'Aliens or Americans?' by Howard B. Grose, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious group of individuals arrives, claiming to be from another planet. As the townspeople grapple with their own beliefs and fears, tensions rise and lines are drawn. With the fate of their community hanging in the balance, the townspeople must confront their own prejudices and decide whether to embrace these newcomers or reject them. This thought-provoking and suspenseful novel explores the complexities of human nature and the power of acceptance.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Prejudice, Acceptance
