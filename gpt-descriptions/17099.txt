Synopsis: In 'The Meadow-Brook Girls by the Sea; Or, The Loss of The Lonesome Bar', the adventurous Meadow-Brook Girls embark on a thrilling seaside vacation that takes an unexpected turn. As they encounter mysterious events and face unforeseen challenges, their bond is tested and their courage shines through. Will they be able to solve the perplexing puzzle and overcome the loss of The Lonesome Bar?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
