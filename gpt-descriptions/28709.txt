Synopsis: In 'Practical Education, Volume II', Maria and Richard Lovell Edgeworth continue their exploration of education through a series of captivating and thought-provoking stories. From the challenges faced by a young girl in a boarding school to the adventures of a group of siblings in the countryside, this volume delves into the complexities of character development, moral dilemmas, and the importance of practical knowledge. With their unique blend of storytelling and educational philosophy, the Edgeworths offer readers a compelling and enlightening journey into the world of practical education.

Genres: Educational fiction, Moral tales, Coming-of-age stories

Themes: Education, Character development, Moral dilemmas, Practical knowledge
