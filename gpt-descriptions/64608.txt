Synopsis: 
'From the Land of the Snow-Pearls: Tales from Puget Sound' by Ella Higginson is a captivating collection of stories set in the enchanting landscapes of Puget Sound. Each tale takes readers on a journey through the lives of diverse characters, from rugged fishermen to mysterious island dwellers, as they navigate the beauty and challenges of this unique region. With vivid descriptions and rich storytelling, Higginson weaves together a tapestry of emotions, secrets, and unexpected connections that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Regional Literature

Themes: Nature and the environment, human connection, the power of storytelling
