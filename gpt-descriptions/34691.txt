Synopsis: In 'Hoofbeats on the Turnpike', a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling city to the tranquil countryside. Along the way, she encounters a mysterious stranger who seems to hold the key to her past and future. As Mary delves deeper into her own history, she uncovers secrets that will challenge her beliefs and change the course of her life forever. With its blend of mystery, romance, and self-discovery, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Family, Secrets
