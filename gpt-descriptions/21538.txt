Synopsis: 
In "Souvenir of the George Borrow Celebration: Norwich, July 5th, 1913," James Hooper takes readers on a captivating journey through the streets of Norwich during a celebration honoring the renowned author George Borrow. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Hooper paints a vibrant picture of the festivities, capturing the essence of the event and the spirit of the city. From the bustling crowds to the enchanting historical landmarks, this souvenir offers a delightful glimpse into a bygone era and the enduring legacy of a literary icon.

Genres: 
Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: 
Literary celebration, Cultural heritage, Historical preservation
