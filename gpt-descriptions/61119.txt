Synopsis: 
In 'Dangerous Quarry' by Jim Harmon, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he is hired to investigate a mysterious disappearance. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a sinister plot that reaches far beyond what he initially anticipated. With his own life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of secrets and betrayal to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Danger
