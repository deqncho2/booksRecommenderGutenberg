Synopsis: In 'The Portion of Labor' by Mary Eleanor Wilkins Freeman, a young woman named Sarah finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. As she navigates the challenges of love, marriage, and motherhood, Sarah must confront the limitations placed upon her by a rigid society. Set in a small New England town, this captivating story explores the complexities of gender roles and the sacrifices women make in pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Gender roles, societal expectations, love and sacrifice
