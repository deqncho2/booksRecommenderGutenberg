Synopsis: In 'The Feeding of School Children', Mildred Emily Bulkley takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of school nutrition. Through vivid storytelling and insightful observations, she explores the challenges faced by educators and policymakers in providing nourishment to young minds. From the bustling kitchens to the bustling classrooms, this book sheds light on the impact of proper nutrition on academic performance and the well-being of students. As the author delves into the intricacies of school feeding programs, readers will be left pondering the importance of this often overlooked aspect of education.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, Health

Themes: School nutrition, Academic performance, Childhood development
