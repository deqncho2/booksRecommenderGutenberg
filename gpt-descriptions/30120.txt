Synopsis: In "The Happy Prince, and Other Tales," Oscar Wilde weaves a collection of enchanting and thought-provoking stories that transport readers to a world where statues come to life, nightingales sing with unmatched beauty, and selfish giants learn the power of compassion. From the tragic tale of a devoted swallow to the whimsical adventures of a remarkable rocket, these stories explore the complexities of human nature, the pursuit of beauty, and the true meaning of love and sacrifice.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Allegory

Themes: Compassion, Beauty, Selflessness, Sacrifice
