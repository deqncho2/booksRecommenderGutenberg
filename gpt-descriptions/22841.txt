Synopsis: In 'Mr. Joseph Hanson, The Haberdasher' by Mary Russell Mitford, a seemingly ordinary haberdasher named Joseph Hanson finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue when a mysterious customer enters his shop. As Joseph delves deeper into the enigmatic world of his new acquaintance, he discovers that appearances can be deceiving and that his own life may be at stake. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Identity, Betrayal
