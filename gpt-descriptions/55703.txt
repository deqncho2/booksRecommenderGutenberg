Synopsis: 
In "In Our Convent Days" by Agnes Repplier, the reader is transported to a time of innocence and camaraderie within the walls of a convent school. Through a series of charming and humorous anecdotes, the author shares her experiences and memories of the nuns, her fellow students, and the daily routines that shaped her formative years. From mischievous pranks to heartfelt friendships, this delightful memoir offers a glimpse into a world that is both enchanting and relatable.

Genres: Memoir, Autobiography, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Education, Friendship, Innocence, Female Empowerment
