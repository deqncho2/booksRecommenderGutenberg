Synopsis: 
In "The Personal History of David Copperfield," we follow the life of the titular character as he navigates through the ups and downs of his journey from childhood to adulthood. From his early years as an orphan to his encounters with eccentric and memorable characters, David's story is one of resilience, self-discovery, and the pursuit of happiness. As he faces various trials and tribulations, including love, loss, and betrayal, David's personal growth and determination to find his place in the world make for a captivating and heartwarming tale.

Genres: 
Bildungsroman, Victorian Literature, Satire

Themes: 
Identity, Social Class, Family, Friendship, Love, Resilience
