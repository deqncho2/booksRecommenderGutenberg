Synopsis: 

In "Twelve Months with the Eighth Massachusetts Infantry in the Service of the United States," Harry Endicott Webber takes readers on a captivating journey through the trials and triumphs of the soldiers of the Eighth Massachusetts Infantry during their year-long service in the American Civil War. Through vivid and personal accounts, Webber provides a unique perspective on the daily life, camaraderie, and sacrifices made by these brave men as they navigate the harsh realities of war. From the front lines to the home front, this gripping narrative offers a compelling glimpse into the experiences and emotions of those who fought for their country.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Resilience
