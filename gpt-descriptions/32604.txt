Synopsis: In 'The Story of the Raising and Organization of a Regiment of Volunteers in 1862', Spear, Ellis takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of the American Civil War. Through vivid storytelling and firsthand accounts, the author delves into the challenges and triumphs of raising and organizing a regiment of volunteers during this pivotal period in history. From the initial recruitment efforts to the formation of a cohesive unit, this gripping narrative sheds light on the sacrifices, camaraderie, and determination that shaped the fate of these brave soldiers.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Leadership, Camaraderie, Civil War history
