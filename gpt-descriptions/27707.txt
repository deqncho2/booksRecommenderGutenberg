Synopsis: 
In "Saint Athanasius, the Father of Orthodoxy" by Forbes, F. A., embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of the most influential figures in early Christianity. This gripping biography delves into the struggles, triumphs, and unwavering faith of Saint Athanasius, a man who stood against the tide of heresy and fought to preserve the true teachings of the Church. From his early years as a devoted disciple to his pivotal role in the Council of Nicaea, this book paints a vivid portrait of a man whose unwavering dedication to his beliefs shaped the course of Christian history.

Genres: Biography, History, Religion

Themes: Faith, Perseverance, Theological Controversy
