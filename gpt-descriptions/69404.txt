Synopsis: 
'You Can't Win' by Jack Black is a gripping memoir that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the criminal underworld of early 20th century America. The story follows the author's experiences as a professional thief, hobo, and drug addict, as he navigates the dangerous and unpredictable world of crime. From daring heists to narrow escapes, Black's narrative is filled with suspense, unexpected twists, and a raw portrayal of life on the fringes of society. As he delves deeper into the criminal underworld, the author's introspective reflections shed light on the complexities of human nature and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Memoir, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Crime and punishment, Survival, Identity, Freedom
