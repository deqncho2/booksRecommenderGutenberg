Synopsis: In "The Lifted Veil" by George Eliot, we are introduced to a protagonist named Latimer who possesses a unique ability to see into the future. However, this extraordinary gift becomes a curse as he begins to unravel the dark and unsettling truths that lie ahead. As Latimer's visions become increasingly haunting, he finds himself trapped in a web of fate and desire, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the boundaries of perception and reality.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, supernatural

Themes: Predestination, isolation, the limitations of human perception
