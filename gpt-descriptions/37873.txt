Synopsis: 
'Alone with the Hairy Ainu: or, 3,800 miles on a pack saddle in Yezo and a cruise to the Kurile Islands' takes readers on an extraordinary adventure through the remote and captivating landscapes of Yezo and the Kurile Islands. Join Arnold Henry Savage Landor as he embarks on a daring journey, traversing thousands of miles on a pack saddle, encountering the enigmatic Ainu people, and exploring the mysterious Kurile Islands. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Landor offers a unique glimpse into the rich cultural heritage and untamed beauty of these little-known regions. Prepare to be enthralled by the breathtaking landscapes, intrigued by the encounters with the Ainu, and captivated by the spirit of exploration that permeates every page of this remarkable travelogue.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Anthropology

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Natural Beauty
