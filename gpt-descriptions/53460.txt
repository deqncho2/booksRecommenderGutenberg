Synopsis: In 'Dick Hamilton's Steam Yacht; Or, A Young Millionaire and the Kidnappers', young millionaire Dick Hamilton finds himself embroiled in a thrilling adventure when he sets sail on his steam yacht. As he navigates treacherous waters and encounters a group of dangerous kidnappers, Dick must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to outsmart his adversaries and save the day. This action-packed tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of Dick and his companions.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Resourcefulness, Overcoming Adversity
