Synopsis: 
In 'The Wonder Island Boys: Treasures of the Islands' by Roger T. Finlay, a group of adventurous boys find themselves on a remote island filled with mystery and danger. As they explore the island's hidden treasures, they encounter unexpected challenges and must rely on their wits and teamwork to survive. With each discovery, they uncover secrets that could change their lives forever. Will they be able to unravel the island's mysteries and find the ultimate treasure?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Survival
