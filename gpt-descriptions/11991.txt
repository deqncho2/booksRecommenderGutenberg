Synopsis: In 'Po-No-Kah: An Indian Tale of Long Ago', Mary Mapes Dodge takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of Native American folklore. Set in a time when the land was untouched by modern civilization, the story follows the adventures of Po-No-Kah, a young Native American girl with a special gift. As she navigates the challenges of her tribe and encounters mystical creatures, Po-No-Kah's courage and determination are put to the test. With vivid descriptions and rich storytelling, Dodge weaves a tale that explores the power of nature, the importance of tradition, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Native American culture, Nature, Coming of age, Tradition, Resilience
