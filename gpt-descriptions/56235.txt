Synopsis: In 'Picked up Adrift: Illustrated' by De Mille, James, a young artist named Emily finds herself stranded on a mysterious island after a shipwreck. As she explores her surroundings, she discovers a hidden world filled with enchanting creatures and breathtaking landscapes. With the help of a newfound friend, Emily embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets of the island and find her way back home. This beautifully illustrated tale will captivate readers with its vivid imagery and imaginative storytelling.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Illustrated

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Resilience
