Synopsis: 
'Little Eve Edgarton' is a captivating novel that follows the life of Eve Edgarton, a young woman with a unique perspective on the world. From her unconventional upbringing to her adventures in the bustling city, Eve's journey is filled with unexpected twists and turns. As she navigates love, friendship, and the pursuit of her dreams, Eve's vibrant personality and indomitable spirit shine through. This heartwarming and thought-provoking story will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what lies ahead for Little Eve Edgarton.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Individuality, Self-discovery, Love and Relationships
