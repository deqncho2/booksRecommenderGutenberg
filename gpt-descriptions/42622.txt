Synopsis: 
'The Book of Christmas' takes readers on a delightful journey through the enchanting world of Christmas, exploring the customs, ceremonies, traditions, superstitions, and festivities that make this holiday season so special. From the joyous fun and heartwarming feelings to the intriguing historical origins and magical folklore, Thomas K. Hervey paints a vivid picture of the Christmas season, captivating readers with his descriptive and engaging writing style. This book is a treasure trove of knowledge and anecdotes, offering a comprehensive and fascinating exploration of the rich tapestry of Christmas traditions.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Folklore

Themes: Christmas traditions, Festivities, Cultural heritage
