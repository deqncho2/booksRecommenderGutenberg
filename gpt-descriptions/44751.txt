Synopsis: In 'The Search Party' by George A. Birmingham, a small Irish village is thrown into chaos when a young woman mysteriously disappears. As the community bands together to search for her, secrets are unearthed, tensions rise, and the true nature of the village is revealed. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Community, Secrets, Identity
