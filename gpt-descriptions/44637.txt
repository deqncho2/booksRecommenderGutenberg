Synopsis: In 'Brother Billy' by Frances Margaret Fox, a young woman named Lily finds herself drawn into a mysterious world when she discovers a hidden letter from her long-lost brother. As she embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's past, Lily becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, she must confront her own identity and make choices that will shape her future. This gripping tale of family, love, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Redemption
