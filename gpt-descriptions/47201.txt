Synopsis: 

In "Kentucky's Famous Feuds and Tragedies: Authentic History of the World Renowned Vendettas of the Dark and Bloody Ground," Charles Gustavus Mutzenberg delves into the captivating and often brutal history of Kentucky's infamous feuds and tragedies. From the Hatfield-McCoy feud to the Rowan County War, this book uncovers the dark secrets and violent clashes that have shaped the state's past. With meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Mutzenberg brings to life the gripping tales of revenge, betrayal, and honor that have left an indelible mark on Kentucky's history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, True Crime

Themes: Feuds, Revenge, Honor, Violence, Betrayal
