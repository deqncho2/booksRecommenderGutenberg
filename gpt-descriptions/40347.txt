Synopsis: In 'The Coward Behind the Curtain' by Richard Marsh, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he discovers that appearances can be deceiving, and the true nature of courage lies in unexpected places. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must confront his own fears and confront the coward lurking within. Will he find the strength to face his demons and uncover the truth, or will he succumb to the shadows that threaten to consume him?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Courage, Identity, Deception, Fear
