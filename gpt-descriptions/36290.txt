Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Ormond; Or, The Secret Witness', the gripping tale continues as the protagonist, Constantia Dudley, finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception. As she delves deeper into the mysterious circumstances surrounding her father's death, Constantia uncovers shocking revelations that threaten to unravel her world. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate treacherous alliances and confront her own inner demons to uncover the truth. Will Constantia be able to unravel the secrets that haunt her family, or will she become another victim of the dark forces at play?

Genres: Gothic fiction, mystery, psychological thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, family legacy, the pursuit of truth, psychological turmoil
