Synopsis: 
In "The Two Hundredth Anniversary of the Settlement of the Town of New Milford, Conn. June 17th, 1907: Address Delivered by Daniel Davenport, of Bridgeport, Conn.", Daniel Davenport takes the reader on a captivating journey through the history of New Milford, Connecticut. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Davenport explores the town's humble beginnings, its growth and development, and the remarkable individuals who shaped its destiny. From the struggles of the early settlers to the triumphs of the present day, this address offers a fascinating glimpse into the rich heritage of New Milford.

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: History, Community, Progress
