Synopsis: 

In 'With Cavalry in 1915: The British Trooper in the Trench Line, Through the Second Battle of Ypres', author Frederic Coleman takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of a British trooper during the Second Battle of Ypres in 1915. Through vivid and detailed accounts, the reader is transported to the front lines, where the trooper faces the brutal realities of trench warfare and the constant threat of enemy attacks. As the battle unfolds, the trooper's courage and resilience are put to the ultimate test, providing a unique perspective on the horrors of war and the indomitable spirit of those who fought.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Military History

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Sacrifice, Horrors of War
