Synopsis: In 'Games for Hallow-e'en' by Mary E. Blain, a group of friends gathers on Halloween night to partake in a series of mysterious and thrilling games. As the night progresses, they find themselves immersed in a world of eerie riddles, hidden secrets, and spine-chilling challenges. With each game they play, the stakes become higher, and the line between reality and fantasy begins to blur. As the clock strikes midnight, they must confront their deepest fears and unravel the enigma that lies at the heart of these haunting games.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Thriller

Themes: Halloween, Friendship, Fear, Mystery
