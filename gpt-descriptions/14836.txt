Synopsis: 
'A Voyage in the 'Sunbeam', Our Home on the Ocean for Eleven Months' takes readers on an extraordinary journey across the seas, as Annie Brassey recounts her family's captivating adventure aboard their luxurious yacht. From exploring exotic lands to encountering diverse cultures, the Brassey family's voyage is filled with thrilling escapades and unexpected encounters. As they navigate the vast oceans, readers will be swept away by the beauty of the world and the incredible experiences that await them.

Genres:
Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes:
Exploration, Cultural diversity, Family bonds
