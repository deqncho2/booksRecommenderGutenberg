Synopsis: In 'The Khedive's Country' by George Manville Fenn, a young Englishman finds himself embroiled in a thrilling adventure in Egypt. Drawn into a web of intrigue and danger, he must navigate the treacherous landscape of political unrest, ancient secrets, and personal vendettas. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the Khedive's country, he uncovers shocking truths that could change the course of history. With unexpected allies and formidable enemies, he must rely on his wit and courage to survive and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Political intrigue, Cultural clash, Loyalty and betrayal
