Synopsis: 
In 'A Practical Handbook on the Distillation of Alcohol from Farm Products', author F. B. Wright takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of alcohol distillation. From the humble beginnings of farm products to the intricate process of transforming them into spirits, this handbook provides a comprehensive guide for those interested in the art of distilling. With detailed instructions, insightful anecdotes, and practical tips, Wright's book offers a captivating exploration of the history, science, and techniques behind alcohol production. Whether you're a curious hobbyist or an aspiring distiller, this handbook is sure to ignite your passion for the craft.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Food and Drink

Themes: Alcohol production, Farming, Craftsmanship
