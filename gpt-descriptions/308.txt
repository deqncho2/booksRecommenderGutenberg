Synopsis: 
Three Men in a Boat (To Say Nothing of the Dog) follows the hilarious misadventures of three friends as they embark on a boating trip along the Thames River. From their comical encounters with eccentric characters to their mishaps with camping equipment, the trio finds themselves in one uproarious situation after another. Along the way, they also uncover surprising historical facts and engage in witty banter, making for a delightful and entertaining read.

Genres: 
Humor, Adventure, Travelogue

Themes: 
Friendship, Exploration, British culture
