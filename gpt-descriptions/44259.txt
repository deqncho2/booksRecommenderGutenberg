Synopsis:
In "William Dwight Whitney" by Thomas D. Seymour, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of the most influential linguists of the 19th century. This biographical work delves into the fascinating world of language and academia, as it explores the remarkable achievements and struggles of William Dwight Whitney. From his early years as a precocious student to his groundbreaking contributions to the field of linguistics, Whitney's story is one of intellectual curiosity, perseverance, and the pursuit of knowledge. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the intricate web of linguistic theories, cultural encounters, and personal triumphs that shaped Whitney's life and legacy. 

Genres: Biography, Linguistics, Academic

Themes: Intellectual curiosity, Perseverance, Pursuit of knowledge
