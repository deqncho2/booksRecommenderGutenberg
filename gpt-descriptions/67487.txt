Synopsis: In 'The Stilled Patter' by James E. Gunn, a renowned scientist discovers a mysterious phenomenon that threatens to unravel the fabric of reality itself. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between science and the supernatural blurs. With time running out, he must confront his own beliefs and confront the unimaginable consequences of his discovery.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Perception, Scientific Ethics, Existentialism
