Synopsis: In "The Problem of 'Edwin Drood': A Study in the Methods of Dickens," W. Robertson Nicoll delves into the mysterious and unfinished novel by Charles Dickens, "The Mystery of Edwin Drood." Nicoll explores the various theories and speculations surrounding the unresolved plot, shedding light on Dickens' writing techniques and the possible outcomes of the story. With a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of Dickens' style, Nicoll presents a captivating analysis that will leave readers intrigued and eager to uncover the secrets of Edwin Drood.

Genres: Literary criticism, Non-fiction

Themes: Mystery, Unfinished works, Writing techniques
