Synopsis: 
Step into the captivating world of Methuen and Co. Ltd. as they unveil their latest selection of books published in June 1919. From thrilling adventures to thought-provoking dramas, this collection promises to transport readers to diverse realms and ignite their imagination. Discover tales of love and betrayal, explore the mysteries of the past, and embark on exhilarating journeys that will leave you yearning for more. With an array of genres and themes, this anthology is a literary treasure trove waiting to be explored.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance, Adventure, Drama, Historical

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Mystery and Intrigue, Exploration and Adventure, Historical Context
