Synopsis: In 'Operation Terror' by Murray Leinster, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a shocking discovery that could change the course of history. As he races against time to prevent his groundbreaking invention from falling into the wrong hands, he becomes entangled in a web of espionage, danger, and international intrigue. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous landscape where trust is scarce and betrayal lurks at every turn.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Espionage

Themes: Technology, Power, Trust, Betrayal
