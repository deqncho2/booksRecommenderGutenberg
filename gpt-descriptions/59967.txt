Synopsis: In 'On Angels' Wings' by Mrs. Greene, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Emily discovers a world of supernatural forces and ancient prophecies that threaten to unravel her reality. With the help of a charismatic stranger, she embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth and confront the darkness that lurks within her own bloodline.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Destiny, Redemption
