Synopsis: 

In "Dress and care of the feet," John Lord Peck delves into the fascinating world of foot health and fashion. This comprehensive guide explores the natural shape and construction of feet, contrasting them with the often distorted conditions caused by various deformities such as corns, bunions, and flat feet. Peck provides valuable instructions for both preventing and curing these ailments, offering practical advice for maintaining healthy and comfortable feet. Moreover, he offers insightful tips for dressing the feet with elegance, catering to both wearers and makers of foot-coverings. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Health, Fashion

Themes: Foot health, Deformities, Prevention, Fashion, Comfort
