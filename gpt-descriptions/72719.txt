Synopsis: 
In "The Greene Murder Case" by S. S. Van Dine, a wealthy and influential family finds themselves at the center of a perplexing murder mystery. When the patriarch of the Greene family is found dead in his study, detective Philo Vance is called upon to unravel the intricate web of secrets and motives that surround the case. As Vance delves deeper into the lives of the family members and their close associates, he uncovers a series of shocking revelations that challenge his deductive skills and lead him to question the true nature of the crime. With time running out and the pressure mounting, Vance must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit and deception to uncover the truth behind the Greene murder case.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Wealth and Power, Family Secrets, Deception and Betrayal
