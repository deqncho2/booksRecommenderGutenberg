Synopsis: In 'The Innocent Adventuress' by Mary Hastings Bradley, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she becomes entangled with a group of enigmatic strangers. As she navigates through a world of secrets and hidden agendas, Evelyn must rely on her wit and resourcefulness to uncover the truth and protect herself from danger. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Deception, Identity, Trust
