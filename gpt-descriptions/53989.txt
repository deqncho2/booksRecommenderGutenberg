Synopsis: 
In 'The Double Life' by Gaston Leroux, a young man named Raoul finds himself captivated by the enigmatic and alluring Christine, a talented singer at the Paris Opera House. As their relationship deepens, Raoul becomes entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries surrounding Christine's past and her connection to the Opera House. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of the theater, Raoul discovers a shocking truth that will challenge everything he thought he knew about love, loyalty, and the power of obsession.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Love and obsession, Identity and duality, Secrets and deception
