Synopsis: In 'Motor Boat Boys' River Chase; or, Six Chums Afloat and Ashore' by Arundel, Louis, six adventurous friends embark on an exhilarating journey along the river, encountering thrilling challenges and unexpected dangers. As they navigate treacherous waters and face mysterious adversaries, their bond is tested, and they must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to overcome the obstacles that lie ahead. With heart-pounding action and a captivating storyline, this book will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what awaits the Motor Boat Boys.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
