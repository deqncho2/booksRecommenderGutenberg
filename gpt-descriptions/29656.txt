Synopsis: In 'The Mountain Divide' by Frank H. Spearman, a young engineer named John Cameron finds himself caught in a dangerous race against time as he battles treacherous terrain, ruthless competitors, and his own inner demons to complete a monumental railway project. As he navigates the rugged mountains and faces unexpected challenges, Cameron must confront his past and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of the project and his own future. With high stakes and gripping suspense, 'The Mountain Divide' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of the American West.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Perseverance, Redemption, Man vs. Nature
