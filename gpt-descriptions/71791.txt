Synopsis: 
When the Bunker family arrives at Farmer Joel's farm for a summer vacation, they have no idea what adventures await them. From exploring mysterious caves to solving a puzzling mystery, the six little Bunkers find themselves in the midst of thrilling escapades that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each new day, the Bunkers discover the true meaning of friendship, bravery, and the importance of family bonds. 

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Family, Friendship, Bravery, Adventure
