Synopsis: 

In "Korea's Fight for Freedom" by Fred A. McKenzie, embark on a gripping journey through the tumultuous history of Korea as it struggles for independence against oppressive forces. This captivating narrative follows the lives of courageous individuals who risk everything to fight for their nation's sovereignty, weaving together personal stories of sacrifice, resilience, and unwavering determination. From the Japanese occupation to the Korean War, this book unveils the untold stories of heroism and the indomitable spirit of the Korean people in their quest for freedom.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Biography, War

Themes: Independence, Resistance, National Identity, Courage, Sacrifice
