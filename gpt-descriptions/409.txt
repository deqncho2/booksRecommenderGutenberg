Synopsis: 
'Poems on various subjects, religious and moral' by Wheatley, Phillis is a collection of thought-provoking and deeply moving poems that explore a wide range of subjects. From reflections on faith and spirituality to social justice and the human condition, Wheatley's words captivate the reader's imagination and stir their emotions. Through her eloquent verses, she delves into the complexities of life, offering profound insights and challenging societal norms. This collection is a testament to Wheatley's exceptional talent and her ability to use poetry as a powerful tool for self-expression and social commentary.

Genres:
Poetry, Religious Literature, Social Commentary

Themes:
Faith, Morality, Social Justice, Human Condition
