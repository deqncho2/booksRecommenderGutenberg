Synopsis: 

'Friars and Filipinos: An Abridged Translation of Dr. Jose Rizal's Tagalog Novel, 'Noli Me Tangere'' is a captivating and thought-provoking literary masterpiece that delves into the complex social and political landscape of 19th-century Philippines. Set against the backdrop of Spanish colonial rule, the story follows the life of Juan Crisostomo Ibarra, a young Filipino who returns to his homeland after studying in Europe. As he navigates through a web of corruption, oppression, and religious hypocrisy, Ibarra becomes entangled in a dangerous quest for justice and liberation. Through vivid characters and gripping plot twists, Rizal's novel sheds light on the struggles faced by the Filipino people and the impact of colonization on their identity and culture.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Social Commentary

Themes: Colonialism, Oppression, Corruption, Identity, Religion
