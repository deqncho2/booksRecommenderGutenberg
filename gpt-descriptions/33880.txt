Synopsis: In 'The Land of Lost Toys' by Juliana Horatia Ewing, a young girl named Dolly finds herself transported to a magical world where forgotten toys come to life. As she navigates this enchanting realm, Dolly encounters a cast of unique characters and embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets of the Land of Lost Toys. With whimsical adventures and unexpected twists, this captivating tale explores the power of imagination and the importance of cherishing childhood memories.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Nostalgia, Friendship, Childhood
