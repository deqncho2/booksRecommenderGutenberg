Synopsis: In 'The Plot That Failed; or, When Men Conspire' by Carter, Nicholas, a group of individuals with hidden agendas find themselves entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal. As their carefully crafted plans begin to unravel, the consequences become increasingly dire, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Conspiracy, Betrayal, Deception
