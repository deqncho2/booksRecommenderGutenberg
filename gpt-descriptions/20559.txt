Synopsis: In 'R. Holmes & Co.: Being the Remarkable Adventures of Raffles Holmes, Esq., Detective and Amateur Cracksman by Birth', John Kendrick Bangs introduces readers to a unique and captivating blend of two iconic characters - Sherlock Holmes and A.J. Raffles. Set in the late 19th century, this thrilling detective novel follows the adventures of Raffles Holmes, a brilliant detective with a penchant for crime. As he solves perplexing cases, Raffles Holmes must navigate the fine line between justice and his own criminal tendencies, making for a suspenseful and unpredictable read.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Dual identities, Morality, Justice
