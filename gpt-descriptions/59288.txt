Synopsis: 
Step into the vibrant world of 19th-century French painting as Evans and Grose take you on a captivating journey through the masterpieces housed in the National Gallery of Art. From the romantic landscapes of Corot to the dazzling brushstrokes of Monet, this book unveils the secrets behind each stroke of the brush, revealing the stories and emotions that lie within these iconic works. With stunning illustrations and insightful commentary, 'French Painting of the 19th Century in the National Gallery of Art' offers a mesmerizing exploration of a pivotal era in art history.

Genres: Art History, Non-fiction

Themes: Romanticism, Impressionism, Realism, Symbolism, Artistic Expression
