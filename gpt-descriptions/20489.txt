Synopsis: In 'Henry of Monmouth, Volume 2: Or, Memoirs of the Life and Character of Henry the Fifth, as Prince of Wales and King of England', James Endell Tyler delves into the captivating life of Henry V, exploring his journey from a young prince to a revered king. This volume focuses on the challenges and triumphs Henry faces as he ascends to the throne, revealing the complexities of his character and the impact of his reign on England. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Tyler brings to life the remarkable story of one of England's most legendary monarchs.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Leadership, Power, National Identity
