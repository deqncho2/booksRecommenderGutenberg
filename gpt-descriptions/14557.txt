Synopsis: In 'The Conundrums of Psychology', Vaknin, Samuel takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate workings of the human mind. Through a series of thought-provoking anecdotes and insightful analysis, Vaknin explores the enigmatic nature of psychology, unraveling the complexities that lie beneath the surface. From the exploration of subconscious desires to the impact of childhood experiences, this book delves into the depths of the human psyche, challenging conventional wisdom and offering fresh perspectives on the mysteries of the mind.

Genres: Psychology, Non-fiction, Self-help

Themes: Human behavior, Mental processes, Emotional intelligence, Self-discovery
