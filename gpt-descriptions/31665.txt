Synopsis: In 'Skin Game' by Fritch, Charles E., a down-on-his-luck private investigator finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal when he takes on a seemingly routine missing person case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to consume him. With his own life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous world of crime and corruption to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Corruption
