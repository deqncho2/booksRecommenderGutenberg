Synopsis: 
'Last winter in the United States' takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes of the late Southern Confederation, the Far West, the Rocky Mountains, and more. Through the lens of F. Barham Zincke, the author, we are immersed in a collection of table talk, capturing the essence of conversations and experiences during his tour. From the intriguing encounters with locals to the breathtaking natural wonders, this book offers a unique perspective on the United States during a pivotal time. 

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Exchange, American Identity
