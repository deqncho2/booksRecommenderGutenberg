Synopsis: In 'Favorite Dishes: a Columbian Autograph Souvenir Cookery Book', author Carrie V. Shuman takes readers on a culinary journey through the vibrant and diverse cuisine of Colombia. Filled with authentic recipes, captivating anecdotes, and stunning photographs, this book offers a tantalizing glimpse into the rich flavors and cultural heritage of Colombian cooking. From hearty stews and savory empanadas to exotic tropical fruits and indulgent desserts, each dish is a celebration of the country's culinary traditions. Whether you are a seasoned chef or a curious food lover, this book will inspire you to explore the vibrant world of Colombian cuisine and create unforgettable meals in your own kitchen.

Genres: Cookbook, Travel, Food and Drink

Themes: Culinary traditions, Cultural heritage, Exploration
