Synopsis: In 'The Guardians' by Cox, Irving E., a group of ordinary individuals with extraordinary abilities are brought together to protect the world from a looming threat. As they navigate through a web of secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances, they must confront their own inner demons and make sacrifices for the greater good. With heart-pounding action and mind-bending twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Superhuman abilities, Teamwork, Sacrifice, Morality
