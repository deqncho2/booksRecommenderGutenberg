Synopsis: In 'Supermind', a brilliant scientist creates a groundbreaking invention that allows him to tap into the limitless power of the human mind. However, as he delves deeper into the mysteries of the supermind, he discovers a dark and dangerous side that threatens to consume him and everything he holds dear. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path of moral dilemmas and unexpected consequences. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Human potential, Ethics, Power and responsibility
