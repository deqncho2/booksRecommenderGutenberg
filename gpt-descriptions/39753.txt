Synopsis: In 'The Misfit Christmas Puddings' by Consolation Club, a group of unlikely friends embarks on a heartwarming adventure to save Christmas. When a batch of magical Christmas puddings goes awry, causing chaos and confusion, this diverse group must come together to fix the mess before it's too late. With unexpected twists and turns, the story takes readers on a delightful journey filled with laughter, friendship, and the true spirit of the holiday season.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Christmas Spirit
