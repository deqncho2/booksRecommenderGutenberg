Synopsis: In 'Sunday Under Three Heads', Charles Dickens takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of three very different families, all intertwined by a single event that unfolds on a Sunday. From the bustling streets of London to the quiet countryside, we witness the struggles, joys, and secrets that lie within each household. As the day progresses, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and unexpected connections are made, leaving us eagerly turning the pages to uncover the ultimate outcome of this eventful Sunday.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes: Family dynamics, Social class, Morality, Secrets and revelations
