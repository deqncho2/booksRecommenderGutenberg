Synopsis: In 'Young Stowaways in Space', a group of adventurous teenagers find themselves unexpectedly aboard a spaceship bound for the outer reaches of the galaxy. As they navigate the vastness of space, they encounter thrilling challenges and unexpected allies, all while trying to uncover the secrets behind their mysterious journey. With danger lurking at every turn, the young stowaways must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and courage to survive and find their way back home.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Coming of Age
