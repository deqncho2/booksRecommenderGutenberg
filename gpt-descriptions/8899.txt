Synopsis: 
In 'Three Weeks' by Elinor Glyn, a young and innocent English girl finds herself caught in a whirlwind of passion and desire during a three-week vacation in France. As she becomes entangled in a forbidden love affair with a charismatic older man, she must navigate the complexities of societal expectations, personal growth, and the power of her own desires. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, 'Three Weeks' takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and forbidden love that will leave them breathless.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Forbidden love, Sexual awakening, Society's expectations
