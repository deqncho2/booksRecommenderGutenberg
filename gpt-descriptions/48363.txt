Synopsis: In 'The Little Grey House' by Marion Ames Taggart, a young woman named Mary finds herself drawn to a mysterious abandoned house in her small town. As she delves deeper into the house's history, she uncovers secrets that have been hidden for decades. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery and redemption, as she unravels the truth behind the little grey house and its former inhabitants.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, the power of forgiveness, the search for identity
