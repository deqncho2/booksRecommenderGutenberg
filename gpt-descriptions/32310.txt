Synopsis: 
In 'Dorothy at Oak Knowe' by Evelyn Raymond, a young girl named Dorothy finds herself transported to a mysterious and enchanting world called Oak Knowe. As she explores this magical realm, she encounters talking animals, mythical creatures, and uncovers long-lost secrets. With the help of newfound friends, Dorothy embarks on a thrilling adventure to save Oak Knowe from an impending danger. Filled with wonder, suspense, and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
