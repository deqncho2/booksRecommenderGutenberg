Synopsis: In 'Radio Boys in the Flying Service; or, Held For Ransom by Mexican Bandits' by J. W. Duffield, a group of adventurous radio boys find themselves caught in a thrilling and dangerous situation when they are kidnapped by Mexican bandits. As they are held captive, they must rely on their resourcefulness and bravery to escape and bring the criminals to justice. With the backdrop of the aviation world and the suspenseful plot, this story takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
