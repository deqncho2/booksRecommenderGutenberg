Synopsis: In 'Reject' by Johnson, John, a young artist named Emma finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden society that rejects societal norms. As she delves deeper into this underground world, Emma must confront her own fears and insecurities, ultimately leading her to question the very fabric of her reality. With unexpected twists and turns, 'Reject' takes readers on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and rebellion.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Conformity, Rebellion
