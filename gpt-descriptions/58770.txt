Synopsis: In "Beryl of the Biplane: Being the Romance of an Air-Woman of To-Day," we are introduced to the fearless and audacious Beryl, a young woman who defies societal norms to pursue her passion for aviation. Set in the early 20th century, this thrilling tale follows Beryl's journey as she navigates the male-dominated world of aviation, facing numerous challenges and dangers along the way. As she soars through the skies, Beryl's determination and courage are put to the test, leading her on a path filled with adventure, romance, and unexpected twists. Will she conquer the skies and find her place in a world that tries to hold her back?

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Gender equality, Pursuit of passion, Overcoming societal barriers
