Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Dickie Deer Mouse' by Bailey, Arthur Scott, follow the thrilling adventures of Dickie, a curious and courageous deer mouse, as he navigates the dangers of the forest and encounters a cast of fascinating woodland creatures. From daring escapes to unexpected friendships, Dickie's journey will captivate readers and leave them eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead for this brave little mouse.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
