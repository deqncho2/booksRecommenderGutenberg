Synopsis: In "The Caesars," De Quincey takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the Roman emperors, revealing their hidden secrets, scandals, and the dark underbelly of power. From the rise of Julius Caesar to the fall of Nero, this gripping narrative explores the complex personalities and Machiavellian tactics of these iconic figures, shedding light on the tumultuous history of the Roman Empire.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Literary criticism

Themes: Power and corruption, Ambition, Historical legacy
