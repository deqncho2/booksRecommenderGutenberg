Synopsis:
In "Shorter Novels, Eighteenth Century," three captivating tales from the 18th century come together to transport readers to different worlds. "The History of Rasselas, Prince of Abyssinia" follows the journey of a young prince seeking the true meaning of happiness. In "The Castle of Otranto, a Gothic Story," a mysterious castle holds dark secrets that threaten the lives of its inhabitants. Lastly, "Vathek, an Arabian Tale" takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the exotic lands of the East, where a powerful and ambitious caliph embarks on a dangerous quest for ultimate power. These three novels, each unique in its own way, promise to captivate readers with their rich storytelling and imaginative settings.

Genres: 
Adventure, Gothic, Fantasy

Themes:
The pursuit of happiness, the supernatural, power and ambition
