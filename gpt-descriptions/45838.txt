Synopsis: 
In 'The ABC of Cooking' by Adelin Balch Coit, embark on a culinary adventure like no other as the author takes you on a journey through the alphabet, revealing the secrets of cooking from A to Z. From appetizers to zesty desserts, this delightful cookbook is filled with mouthwatering recipes, helpful tips, and charming anecdotes that will inspire both novice and experienced cooks alike. With its engaging storytelling and easy-to-follow instructions, 'The ABC of Cooking' is a must-have for anyone looking to expand their culinary repertoire and create delicious meals that will impress family and friends.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink, Non-fiction

Themes: Culinary exploration, Cooking techniques, Recipe collection
