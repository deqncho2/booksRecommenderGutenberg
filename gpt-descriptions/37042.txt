Synopsis: In 'The Coming of the King' by Joseph Hocking, a young man named David embarks on a journey that will change his life forever. Drawn into a world of ancient prophecies and hidden secrets, David finds himself at the center of a battle between good and evil. As he uncovers the truth about his own identity and destiny, he must confront his fears and make choices that will determine the fate of an entire kingdom. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Good vs. Evil
