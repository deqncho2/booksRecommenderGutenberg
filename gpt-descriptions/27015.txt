Synopsis: In "More Science from an Easy Chair," Sir Edwin Ray Lankester takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders of science, exploring a wide range of fascinating topics from the comfort of his easy chair. From the mysteries of the deep sea to the intricacies of the human body, Lankester's engaging and accessible writing style brings complex scientific concepts to life, making them accessible to readers of all backgrounds. With a blend of wit, curiosity, and profound knowledge, this collection of essays invites readers to delve into the captivating world of science and discover the extraordinary wonders that surround us every day.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Essays

Themes: Wonder of the natural world, Scientific exploration, Popular science
