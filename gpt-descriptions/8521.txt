Synopsis: 

In 'Maintaining Health (Formerly Health and Efficiency)' by Alsaker, Rasmus Larssen, we are introduced to a world where the pursuit of physical and mental well-being has become an obsession. The story follows the lives of three individuals who find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and hidden agendas within the health and fitness industry. As they navigate through a society that values perfection above all else, they must confront the dark side of maintaining an ideal image and the sacrifices it demands. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Obsession, Deception, Identity, Society's Expectations
