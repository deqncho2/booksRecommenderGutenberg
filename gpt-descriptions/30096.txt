Synopsis: In 'The Camera Fiend' by E. W. Hornung, a seemingly innocent hobby takes a dark turn when a young man becomes obsessed with capturing the perfect photograph. As he delves deeper into his passion, he discovers the dangerous lengths he is willing to go to in order to achieve his artistic vision. With each click of the camera, the line between art and obsession blurs, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Obsession, Artistic pursuit, Morality
