Synopsis: 
In the small town of Hampton, the eccentric and witty T. Haviland Hicks, Senior finds himself caught up in a series of mysterious events. From a missing diamond necklace to a suspicious death, Hicks becomes the unlikely detective determined to uncover the truth. With his sharp intellect and unconventional methods, he navigates through a web of secrets and deceit, all while keeping the townspeople entertained with his humorous antics. As the plot thickens, Hicks must rely on his wit and charm to solve the perplexing mysteries that unfold in this delightful and engaging tale.

Genres: Mystery, Humor, Cozy Mystery

Themes: Detective work, Small-town life, Wit and humor
