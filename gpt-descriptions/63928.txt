Synopsis: 

'Arabia: The Cradle of Islam: Studies in the Geography, People and Politics of the Peninsula, with an Account of Islam and Mission-Work' by Samuel Marinus Zwemer takes readers on a captivating journey through the heart of Arabia, exploring its rich history, diverse people, and complex politics. From the birthplace of Islam to the challenges faced by missionaries, this book offers a comprehensive and enlightening account of the Arabian Peninsula.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Religion

Themes: Arabian history, Islamic culture, Missionary work
