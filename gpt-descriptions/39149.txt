Synopsis: In 'Ciphers For the Little Folks: A Method of Teaching the Greatest Work of Sir Francis Bacon,' Dorothy Crain presents a captivating and mysterious tale that unravels the secrets hidden within the works of the renowned philosopher. As a group of young friends embarks on a thrilling adventure, they discover a series of cryptic ciphers that hold the key to unlocking Bacon's greatest masterpiece. With each clue they decipher, the children are drawn deeper into a world of intrigue and intellectual discovery, where the boundaries between reality and imagination blur. As they race against time to solve the puzzles, they uncover a hidden legacy that could change the course of history forever.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Intellectual curiosity, Friendship, Legacy
