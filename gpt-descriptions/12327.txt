Synopsis: 
In 'The Jewish Manual: Practical Information in Jewish and Modern Cookery with a Collection of Valuable Recipes & Hints Relating to the Toilette', Judith Cohen, Lady Montefiore takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of Jewish and modern cookery. This comprehensive guide not only provides a wealth of valuable recipes but also offers insightful hints relating to the art of personal grooming. From traditional Jewish dishes to contemporary culinary delights, this book is a treasure trove of gastronomic knowledge and beauty secrets that will leave readers eager to explore the rich tapestry of Jewish cuisine and enhance their personal care routines.

Genres: Cookbook, Lifestyle, Cultural

Themes: Jewish culture, Culinary traditions, Personal grooming, Beauty tips
