Synopsis: 
In "Adrift in the Ice-Fields" by Charles Winslow Hall, a gripping tale of survival unfolds as a group of explorers find themselves trapped in the treacherous Arctic ice. As they battle against the unforgiving elements and face unimaginable dangers, their will to survive is tested to its limits. With each passing day, the line between sanity and madness becomes blurred, and the true nature of human resilience is revealed. Will they find a way to escape the icy grip of the Arctic, or will they succumb to the merciless forces of nature?

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Man vs. Nature, Resilience, Madness
