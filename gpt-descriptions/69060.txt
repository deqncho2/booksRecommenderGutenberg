Synopsis: In "The Angel of His Presence; and Gabriel the Acadian," Grace Livingston Hill and Edith M. Nicholl weave a captivating tale of love, faith, and redemption. Set in the picturesque Acadian region, the story follows Gabriel, a young man burdened by his past, and the mysterious Angel who enters his life. As Gabriel grapples with his inner demons and seeks forgiveness, the Angel becomes a guiding presence, leading him on a transformative journey. Along the way, Gabriel encounters unexpected friendships, faces moral dilemmas, and discovers the power of love and faith in overcoming adversity. This heartwarming and thought-provoking novel will keep readers engrossed until the very end.

Genres: Christian fiction, Romance, Historical fiction

Themes: Redemption, Forgiveness, Faith, Love, Personal growth
