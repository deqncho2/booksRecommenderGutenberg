Synopsis: In 'Our Little English Cousin' by Blanche McManus, we follow the adventures of a young American girl named Betty as she travels to England to visit her English relatives. From exploring the bustling streets of London to experiencing the charm of the English countryside, Betty immerses herself in the rich culture and traditions of her English cousins. Along the way, she encounters new friends, encounters unexpected challenges, and learns valuable lessons about friendship, family, and the importance of embracing different cultures.

Genres: Children's Literature, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cultural exchange, Family, Friendship
