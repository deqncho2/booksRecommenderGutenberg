Synopsis:
'Alroy: The Prince of the Captivity' by Benjamin Disraeli, Earl of Beaconsfield, is a captivating historical novel set in the 12th century. The story follows the life of David Alroy, a young Jewish prince who embarks on a remarkable journey to reclaim his people's freedom and establish a new kingdom. Filled with political intrigue, religious conflicts, and forbidden love, Alroy's quest takes him through exotic lands and introduces him to a cast of intriguing characters. As he navigates treacherous alliances and battles against formidable enemies, Alroy's determination and courage are put to the ultimate test. Will he succeed in his mission and fulfill his destiny as the Prince of the Captivity?

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Power, Faith, Love, Freedom
