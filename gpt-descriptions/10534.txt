Synopsis:
In 'The Double Traitor' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious and enigmatic man named Felix is caught in a web of espionage and intrigue during World War I. As he navigates the dangerous world of spies and secret agents, Felix finds himself torn between loyalty and betrayal, unsure of who to trust. With his life on the line, he must make difficult choices that will have far-reaching consequences. This thrilling tale of espionage and double-crossing will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres:
Thriller, Espionage, Mystery

Themes:
Betrayal, Loyalty, Trust
