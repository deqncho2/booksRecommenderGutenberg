Synopsis: 
In 'Host and Guest: a book about dinners, dinner-giving, wines, and desserts' by A. V. Kirwan, embark on a delightful culinary journey filled with sumptuous meals, exquisite wines, and delectable desserts. This captivating book takes readers into the world of hosting and being a guest, exploring the art of entertaining and the joy of sharing a meal with loved ones. From elegant dinner parties to intimate gatherings, Kirwan's vivid descriptions and mouthwatering recipes will transport you to a world of gastronomic delights. Discover the secrets of creating unforgettable dining experiences and learn how to pair the perfect wine with each course. With its enticing blend of food, wine, and hospitality, 'Host and Guest' is a feast for the senses that will leave readers hungry for more.

Genres: Culinary, Non-fiction, Lifestyle

Themes: Entertaining, Hospitality, Food and Wine
