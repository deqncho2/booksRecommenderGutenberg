Synopsis: In 'Hypolympia; Or, The Gods in the Island, an Ironic Fantasy', Edmund Gosse takes readers on a whimsical journey to a mysterious island inhabited by ancient gods. As the gods navigate the complexities of human life, their interactions with mortals lead to unexpected consequences and humorous situations. This ironic fantasy explores the clash between divine power and human desires, offering a thought-provoking and entertaining exploration of the nature of gods and the follies of humanity.

Genres: Fantasy, Satire, Comedy

Themes: Power and its consequences, Human nature and folly, Clash of divine and mortal realms
