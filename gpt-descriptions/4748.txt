Synopsis: In the remote wilderness of the Canadian North, a young wolf-dog named Baree embarks on a thrilling journey of survival and self-discovery. Born to a wild wolf father and a domesticated dog mother, Baree must navigate the treacherous landscapes, encounter dangerous predators, and face the challenges of his dual nature. As he grows, Baree forms unexpected alliances and learns valuable lessons about loyalty, love, and the indomitable spirit of the wild. Will he find his place in the unforgiving wilderness or succumb to the forces that threaten his existence?

Genres: Adventure, Nature, Animal Fiction

Themes: Identity, Nature vs. Nurture, Survival
