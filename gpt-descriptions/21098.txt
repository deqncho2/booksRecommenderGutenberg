Synopsis: In 'The Independence of Claire' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, readers are introduced to Claire, a young woman who finds herself at a crossroads in life. Torn between societal expectations and her own desires, Claire embarks on a journey of self-discovery and independence. As she navigates the challenges and obstacles that come her way, Claire must confront her own fears and make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century England, this captivating novel explores themes of love, identity, and the pursuit of personal freedom.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Independence, Self-discovery, Societal Expectations, Love, Identity
