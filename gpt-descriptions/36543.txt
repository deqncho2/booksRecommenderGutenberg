Synopsis: In 'The Motley Muse (Rhymes for the Times)' by Harry Graham, readers are taken on a whimsical journey through a collection of witty and satirical poems that cleverly comment on various aspects of society. From politics and love to everyday absurdities, Graham's verses offer a refreshing and humorous perspective on the world around us. With each rhyme, the author invites readers to ponder, laugh, and perhaps even question their own beliefs. This delightful collection is sure to entertain and leave readers with a newfound appreciation for the power of words.

Genres: Poetry, Satire, Humor

Themes: Society, Politics, Love, Absurdity
