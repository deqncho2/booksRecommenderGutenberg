Synopsis: 
'Unleavened Bread' by Grant, Robert is a gripping psychological thriller that follows the life of a young woman named Hester, who finds herself entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal. As Hester's seemingly perfect life begins to unravel, she must navigate through a series of unexpected events that challenge her perception of reality and force her to confront the dark secrets that lie beneath the surface. With each twist and turn, the tension builds, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
