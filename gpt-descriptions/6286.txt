Synopsis: In 'You Never Know Your Luck; being the story of a matrimonial deserter. Volume 2.', the captivating tale continues as the protagonist, a matrimonial deserter, finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and unexpected encounters. As he navigates through the complexities of his past choices, he must confront the consequences of his actions and make difficult decisions that will shape his future. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the protagonist's journey, eagerly anticipating the resolution of his tumultuous predicament.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and betrayal, Consequences of one's actions, Redemption
