Synopsis: 
In 'Star-Dust: A Story of an American Girl' by Fannie Hurst, we follow the captivating journey of a young American girl named Selina Peake. Born into poverty, Selina possesses an extraordinary talent that sets her apart from her peers. As she navigates the challenges of her upbringing and strives to fulfill her dreams, Selina's path intertwines with various characters who shape her destiny. From the bustling streets of New York City to the glamour of Hollywood, this tale explores the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Poverty, Talent, Ambition, Love, Identity
