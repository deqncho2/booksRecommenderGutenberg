Synopsis:
"Daddy Long-Legs: A Comedy in Four Acts" follows the story of an orphaned girl named Jerusha Abbott, who unexpectedly receives a life-changing opportunity. A mysterious benefactor, whom she affectionately calls "Daddy Long-Legs," offers to fund her college education on one condition: that she writes him regular letters about her experiences. As Jerusha embarks on her journey, she discovers a world of knowledge, friendship, and self-discovery, all while trying to uncover the identity of her enigmatic benefactor. Through her witty and heartfelt letters, Jerusha's life takes unexpected turns, leading her to question her own desires and the meaning of family.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Education, Identity, Independence, Love, Social Class
