Synopsis: 

'Armenia, a martyr nation: A historical sketch of the Armenian people from traditional times to the present tragic days' by Gabrielian, Mugurdich Chojhauji is a captivating historical account that takes readers on a journey through the rich and tumultuous history of the Armenian people. From ancient times to the present, the book delves into the struggles, triumphs, and tragedies that have shaped the nation, offering a poignant exploration of their enduring spirit and resilience. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Cultural studies, Biography

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Historical injustice, Cultural heritage
