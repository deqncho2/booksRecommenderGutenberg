Synopsis: In 'The Lost Charm' by Norton Roy, a young archaeologist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to a forgotten ancient civilization. As she delves deeper into the artifact's secrets, she becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, racing against time to uncover the truth before it falls into the wrong hands. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of history and the power of lost knowledge.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Ancient Civilizations, Secrets and Discovery
