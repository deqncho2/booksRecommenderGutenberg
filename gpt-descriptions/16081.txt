Synopsis: 
"The Anti-Slavery Alphabet" by Anonymous is a captivating and thought-provoking children's book that takes readers on a journey through the alphabet while shedding light on the horrors of slavery. Each letter is accompanied by a powerful illustration and a corresponding verse that challenges young minds to question the injustice of human bondage. From A for "Auction Block" to Z for "Zeal for Freedom," this alphabet book serves as a poignant reminder of the importance of equality and the fight against oppression.

Genres: Children's Literature, Historical Fiction, Social Justice

Themes: Slavery, Equality, Activism
