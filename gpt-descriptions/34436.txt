Synopsis: In 'Hints to Husbands: A Revelation of the Man-Midwife's Mysteries', Morant, George takes readers on a captivating journey into the secretive world of man-midwifery. Through a series of intriguing anecdotes and revelations, the author sheds light on the hidden practices and challenges faced by these unconventional practitioners. From scandalous encounters to unexpected twists, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a profession shrouded in mystery and controversy. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Medical Drama, Mystery

Themes: Gender Roles, Medical Ethics, Social Taboos
