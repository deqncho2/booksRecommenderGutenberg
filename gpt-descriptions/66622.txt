Synopsis: In 'Fiander's Widow: A Novel' by Francis, M. E., a young widow finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and danger when she inherits her late husband's estate. As she delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding her husband's death, she uncovers shocking revelations that threaten to upend her entire world. With each twist and turn, she must navigate treacherous alliances and unexpected alliances to uncover the truth and protect her own life. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Identity
