Synopsis: In 'The Retreat from Mons' by George Stuart Gordon, readers are taken on a gripping journey through the harrowing events of World War I. Set against the backdrop of the Battle of Mons, the story follows a group of soldiers as they navigate the treacherous retreat from the advancing German forces. With vivid descriptions and heart-wrenching accounts, the novel explores the physical and emotional toll of war, the bonds formed in the face of adversity, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Adventure

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Survival
