Synopsis: In 'I Was a Teen-Age Secret Weapon' by Sabia, Richard, a seemingly ordinary teenager named Jake discovers a hidden power within himself that could change the course of a secret war between supernatural beings. As he navigates through a world filled with danger, mystery, and unexpected alliances, Jake must learn to harness his newfound abilities and uncover the truth about his own identity. With thrilling twists and turns, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Young Adult, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Power, Coming-of-age
