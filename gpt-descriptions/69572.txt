Synopsis: In "Fundamental Ideas and Problems of the Theory of Relativity," Albert Einstein takes readers on a mind-bending journey through the revolutionary concepts that reshaped our understanding of space, time, and gravity. With his characteristic clarity and brilliance, Einstein explores the perplexing puzzles and paradoxes that arise when we challenge our conventional notions of the universe. From the nature of light to the curvature of spacetime, this captivating exploration of the theory of relativity will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew about the fundamental laws of physics.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Space and time, Gravity, Paradigm shifts in scientific thinking
