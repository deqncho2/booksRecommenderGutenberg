Synopsis: 

In 'Conservation Archaeology of the Richland/Chambers Dam and Reservoir', Moir and Raab take readers on an intriguing journey through the hidden history of a dam and reservoir project. Through meticulous archaeological research, they uncover the secrets of the past, revealing a fascinating narrative that intertwines human civilization with the forces of nature. From ancient artifacts to forgotten settlements, this book unveils a captivating tale that will leave readers in awe of the rich heritage lying beneath the surface.

Genres: Archaeology, History, Conservation

Themes: Human impact on the environment, Cultural heritage preservation, Uncovering hidden histories
