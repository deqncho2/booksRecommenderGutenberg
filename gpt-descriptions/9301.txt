Synopsis: 
In "Ranald Bannerman's Boyhood" by George MacDonald, follow the captivating journey of young Ranald as he navigates the enchanting landscapes of Scotland. From his humble beginnings in a small village to his encounters with mysterious characters and thrilling adventures, Ranald's boyhood is filled with wonder, courage, and self-discovery. As he explores the beauty of nature, encounters the supernatural, and learns valuable life lessons, Ranald's coming-of-age story will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Self-discovery, Nature, Courage
