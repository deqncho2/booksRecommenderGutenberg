Synopsis: 

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the opulent and intriguing world of Old Court Life in Spain. In this captivating two-volume work, Frances Minto Dickinson Elliot takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the lavish courts of Spanish royalty. From the grand palaces to the intricate web of political alliances, this book offers a rare glimpse into the lives of the nobility, their scandals, and their secrets. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Elliot paints a vivid picture of the splendor and decadence that characterized the Spanish court during this fascinating era. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Memoir

Themes: Court intrigue, Power dynamics, Royal life, Spanish history
