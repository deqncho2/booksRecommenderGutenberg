Synopsis: In 'Prince Jan, St. Bernard' by Forrestine C. Hooker, a young boy named Jan embarks on a thrilling adventure in the Swiss Alps alongside his loyal and courageous St. Bernard dog. Together, they face treacherous mountain passes, encounter mysterious strangers, and uncover a hidden secret that could change their lives forever. This heartwarming tale of friendship, bravery, and the power of the human-animal bond will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Adventure, Animal Fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Loyalty, Nature
