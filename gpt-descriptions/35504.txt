Synopsis: 
In 'Miss Maitland, Private Secretary' by Geraldine Bonner, we are introduced to the intelligent and resourceful Miss Maitland, who finds herself in a thrilling and unexpected adventure. When she takes on the role of private secretary to a wealthy and mysterious businessman, she soon discovers that there is more to her new employer than meets the eye. As she delves deeper into his world, Miss Maitland becomes entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and romance. With her quick wit and determination, she must navigate treacherous waters and uncover the truth before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Independence
