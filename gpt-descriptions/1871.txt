Synopsis: 
In the small town of Arcis-sur-Aube, a scandalous election takes place, revealing the intricate web of political corruption and personal ambitions. As the deputy of Arcis, Simon Giguet, faces a formidable opponent, he must navigate through a world of deceit, secret alliances, and hidden agendas. With the fate of the town hanging in the balance, Giguet finds himself entangled in a complex game of power and manipulation, where loyalty and betrayal blur the lines of morality.

Genres: 
Political fiction, Satire, Drama

Themes: 
Political corruption, Ambition, Morality
