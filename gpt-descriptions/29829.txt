Synopsis: 
In 'Hair Breadth Escapes: Perilous incidents in the lives of sailors and travelers in Japan, Cuba, East Indies, etc., etc.', Arthur T. S. takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous adventures of sailors and travelers in exotic locations. From the heart-pounding encounters with pirates in the East Indies to the life-threatening situations faced in Japan and Cuba, this book is a gripping collection of hair-raising escapes that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to a world filled with danger, suspense, and unexpected twists, as they follow the characters' desperate attempts to survive against all odds.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Courage, Exploration
