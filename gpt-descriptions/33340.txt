Synopsis: In 'Weighed and Wanting: Addresses on the Ten Commandments', Dwight Lyman Moody delivers a thought-provoking series of addresses that explore the timeless relevance of the Ten Commandments in our modern lives. With his characteristic passion and clarity, Moody delves into the profound impact these commandments can have on our relationships, our character, and our spiritual journey. Through engaging anecdotes and powerful insights, he challenges readers to examine their own lives and consider the weight of their actions in light of these commandments. This collection of addresses serves as a compelling reminder of the moral compass provided by the Ten Commandments and their potential to transform our lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Christian literature, Sermons

Themes: Morality, Personal transformation, Spiritual guidance
