Synopsis: 
In 'Breakfast, Luncheon and Tea' by Marion Harland, readers are transported to the elegant world of 19th-century society, where the lives of three remarkable women intertwine in unexpected ways. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, friendships are tested, and love blossoms amidst the backdrop of lavish meals and social gatherings. With its captivating characters and intricate plot, this novel takes readers on a journey filled with passion, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Friendship, Love, Society, Secrets, Ambition
