Synopsis: In 'Meddler's Moon' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious phenomenon that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his discovery, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected consequences. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with secrets, betrayal, and a race against time. Will he be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Scientific discovery, Consequences of meddling with nature, Ethical dilemmas
