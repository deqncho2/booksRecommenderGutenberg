Synopsis: In 'How old are fossils?' Roy, Sharat Kumar takes readers on a captivating journey through time, unraveling the mysteries of ancient life forms. From the depths of the ocean to the peaks of mountains, this book explores the fascinating world of fossils and the incredible stories they tell. Prepare to be amazed as you discover the secrets hidden within these ancient relics, and embark on a thrilling adventure that will leave you questioning everything you thought you knew about the history of our planet.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Paleontology

Themes: Evolution, Geology, Natural History
