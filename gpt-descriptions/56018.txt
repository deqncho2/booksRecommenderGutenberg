Synopsis: 

In 'Business Administration: Theory, Practice and Application. [Vol. 1] Business Economics', embark on a captivating journey through the intricate world of business economics. This volume delves into the fundamental principles and practical applications that drive the economic decisions within organizations. From analyzing market dynamics to understanding supply and demand, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the economic forces that shape the business landscape. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Business, Economics

Themes: Economic principles, Market dynamics, Decision-making, Organizational behavior
