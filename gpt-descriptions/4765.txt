Synopsis: In 'Loyalties' by John Galsworthy, a seemingly ordinary dinner party among friends takes an unexpected turn when a scandalous secret is revealed. As the characters grapple with their loyalties and the consequences of their actions, the delicate balance of their relationships is put to the test. With gripping twists and thought-provoking moral dilemmas, this captivating drama explores the complexities of loyalty, trust, and the consequences of keeping secrets.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Morality, Consequences
