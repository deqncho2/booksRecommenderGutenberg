Synopsis: In 'The Native Born; or, the Rajah's People' by I. A. R. Wylie, a young Englishwoman finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and forbidden love in the exotic setting of British India. As she navigates the complex social dynamics and cultural clashes, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game that could cost her everything. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this gripping tale explores the clash between tradition and modernity, love and duty, and the power struggles that shape lives in a colonial society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Colonialism, Cultural Identity, Forbidden Love, Power Dynamics
