Synopsis: In the small Icelandic town of Hadda Pada, a mysterious event occurs that shakes the community to its core. As the townspeople grapple with the aftermath, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of human nature is revealed. Told through multiple perspectives, 'Hadda Pada' is a gripping and atmospheric tale that explores the dark underbelly of a seemingly idyllic town.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Small-town secrets, Human nature, Betrayal, Redemption
