Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Pole Moor and the Greenfield Hills, a young girl named Miriam embarks on a captivating journey filled with mystery and adventure. As she navigates through the lush landscapes and encounters peculiar characters, Miriam discovers hidden secrets that will change her life forever. With each twist and turn, the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, leaving readers spellbound until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Coming of age, Identity, Magic, Friendship
