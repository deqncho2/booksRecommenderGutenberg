Synopsis: In 'The Fugitives' by Malcolm B. Morehart, a group of unlikely companions find themselves on the run from a powerful and mysterious organization. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter dangerous adversaries, they must rely on their wits and newfound abilities to survive. With each step, they uncover shocking secrets that challenge their perception of reality and force them to question their own identities. As the pursuit intensifies, the fugitives must decide who they can trust and what sacrifices they are willing to make to uncover the truth and reclaim their freedom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Survival
