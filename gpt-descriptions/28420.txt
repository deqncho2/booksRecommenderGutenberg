Synopsis: 

In 'Lives of the Most Eminent Painters Sculptors and Architects, Vol. 04 (of 10): Filippino Lippi to Domenico Puligo', Giorgio Vasari takes us on a captivating journey through the lives and works of renowned artists from Filippino Lippi to Domenico Puligo. From the struggles and triumphs of these talented individuals to the fascinating stories behind their masterpieces, Vasari's vivid descriptions and anecdotes bring the art world of the Renaissance to life. 

Genres: Biography, Art History, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic genius, Renaissance culture, the lives of artists
