Synopsis: In the fourth volume of the Warren Commission Hearings, the investigation into the assassination of President John F. Kennedy intensifies as key witnesses take the stand. This gripping installment delves into the testimonies of individuals who were present during the tragic event, shedding light on the events leading up to and following the assassination. As the commission seeks to uncover the truth, tensions rise and shocking revelations emerge, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, True Crime

Themes: Assassination, Conspiracy, Government Investigation
