Synopsis: 
'A Household Book of English Poetry: Selected and Arranged with Notes' is a captivating anthology that takes readers on a poetic journey through the rich tapestry of English literature. From the enchanting verses of Shakespeare to the profound musings of Wordsworth, this collection offers a diverse range of poems that will transport readers to different eras and evoke a myriad of emotions. With insightful notes accompanying each poem, readers will gain a deeper understanding of the historical context and literary techniques employed by the poets. Whether you are a poetry enthusiast or a newcomer to the genre, this anthology promises to be a delightful exploration of the beauty and power of English poetry.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology, Literary Criticism

Themes: Beauty of language, Historical context, Literary analysis
