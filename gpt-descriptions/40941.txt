Synopsis: In "The Wreck of the Red Bird: A Story of the Carolina Coast," George Cary Eggleston takes readers on a thrilling journey along the treacherous Carolina coast. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows the lives of a diverse group of characters whose paths intertwine after a devastating shipwreck. As they struggle to survive in the harsh coastal environment, they must confront their own fears, navigate through dangerous situations, and find strength in unexpected alliances. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding moments, this gripping tale explores the resilience of the human spirit and the power of community in the face of adversity.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Survival

Themes: Resilience, Community, Survival
