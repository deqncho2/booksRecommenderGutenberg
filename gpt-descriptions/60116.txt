Synopsis: In 'West Side Studies: Boyhood and Lawlessness; The Neglected Girl', the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the gritty streets of the West Side. This collection of stories delves into the lives of young boys caught in the clutches of lawlessness, and the neglected girl who struggles to find her place in a harsh world. As the characters navigate the complexities of their surroundings, their paths intertwine, leading to unexpected consequences that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Crime fiction, Coming-of-age, Social realism

Themes: Juvenile delinquency, Poverty, Gender inequality, Identity
