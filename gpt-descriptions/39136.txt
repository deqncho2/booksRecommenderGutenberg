Synopsis:
In "Historical Romances: Under the Red Robe, Count Hannibal, A Gentleman of France" by Stanley John Weyman, readers are transported to the captivating world of 17th-century France. The collection includes three thrilling tales that intertwine love, adventure, and political intrigue. "Under the Red Robe" follows the story of a young Frenchman torn between his loyalty to the king and his forbidden love for a beautiful woman. In "Count Hannibal," a nobleman finds himself entangled in a dangerous plot against the monarchy, testing his courage and honor. Lastly, "A Gentleman of France" introduces readers to a charismatic soldier who becomes embroiled in a treacherous mission to protect the queen. Each story is filled with suspense, romance, and the rich historical backdrop of France during this tumultuous era.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Love, Political Intrigue
