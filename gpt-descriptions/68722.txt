Synopsis: In "In the twinkling of an eye," a mysterious event suddenly causes millions of people to disappear without a trace, leaving the world in chaos and confusion. As the remaining survivors grapple with the aftermath, they must confront their deepest fears, question their beliefs, and search for answers in a world turned upside down. Amidst the turmoil, a group of individuals find themselves drawn together, forming unlikely alliances and embarking on a journey that will test their resilience and challenge everything they thought they knew about life, faith, and the nature of reality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Existentialism, Faith, Identity, Survival
