Synopsis: In 'Life and Times of Washington, Volume 2: Revised, Enlarged, and Enriched', Lossing and Schroeder delve into the captivating life of George Washington, exploring his remarkable achievements and the challenges he faced. This revised edition offers a deeper understanding of Washington's character, his leadership during the Revolutionary War, and his pivotal role in shaping the United States. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors bring to life the trials and triumphs of one of America's most revered figures.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Leadership, American Revolution, Legacy
