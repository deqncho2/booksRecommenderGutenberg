Synopsis: 
In "Barlasch of the Guard" by Henry Seton Merriman, we are introduced to Barlasch, a grizzled veteran of the French Imperial Guard, who finds himself caught up in a web of intrigue and danger. Set against the backdrop of the Franco-Prussian War, Barlasch's loyalty and courage are put to the ultimate test as he navigates through treacherous alliances and personal sacrifices. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through war-torn Europe.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Sacrifice, Betrayal, Honor
