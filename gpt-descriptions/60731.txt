Synopsis: 

In "G. A. Selwyn, D.D.: Bishop of New Zealand and Lichfield," author Louise Creighton delves into the extraordinary life of George Augustus Selwyn, a man whose journey takes him from the far reaches of New Zealand to the esteemed position of Bishop in Lichfield. This captivating biography explores Selwyn's tireless efforts to spread Christianity, his encounters with indigenous cultures, and the challenges he faces in his quest for spiritual enlightenment. From his early days as a missionary to his eventual rise as a prominent figure in the Church of England, Selwyn's story is one of resilience, faith, and the pursuit of a higher calling.

Genres: Biography, History, Religion

Themes: Missionary work, Cultural encounters, Spiritual growth
