Synopsis:
In "Forty Minutes Late: 1909" by Smith, Francis Hopkinson, a seemingly ordinary train ride takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious passenger boards the train forty minutes behind schedule. As the journey unfolds, the passengers find themselves caught in a web of intrigue, secrets, and unexpected connections. With each passing minute, tensions rise, and the true nature of the late arrival becomes increasingly enigmatic. Will the passengers uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres:
Mystery, Suspense, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Deception, Fate, Coincidence
