Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Thirteenth Regiment of Light Dragoons', Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment from 1715 to 1842. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, Cannon delves into the fascinating history of the Thirteenth Regiment, offering a detailed account of their triumphs and challenges. This enthralling narrative sheds light on the regiment's involvement in significant events and conflicts, providing a vivid portrayal of the soldiers' bravery, camaraderie, and unwavering dedication. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military valor, Regimental history, Historical events
