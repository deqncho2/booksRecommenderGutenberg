Synopsis: 
'Poems & Ballads (First Series)' by Algernon Charles Swinburne is a collection of captivating and provocative poems that explore the depths of human emotions and desires. From passionate love to dark and forbidden themes, Swinburne's lyrical verses take readers on a journey through the complexities of the human experience. With vivid imagery and evocative language, this collection delves into the realms of love, beauty, and the darker aspects of life, leaving readers mesmerized by the power of Swinburne's words.

Genres: Poetry, Romanticism, Symbolism

Themes: Love, Beauty, Desire, Mortality, Taboo
