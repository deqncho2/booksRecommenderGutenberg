Synopsis: In "The Genius" by Theodore Dreiser, we follow the life of Eugene Witla, a talented artist with a burning desire for success. Set in the vibrant art scene of early 20th century America, the story takes us on a journey through Eugene's struggles, ambitions, and the sacrifices he must make to achieve his dreams. As he navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations, we witness the transformative power of art and the price one must pay for true genius.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Artistic expression, Love and relationships, Society's expectations
