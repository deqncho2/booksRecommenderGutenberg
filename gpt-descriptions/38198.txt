Synopsis:
In "Frederica and her Guardians; Or, The Perils of Orphanhood" by Margaret M. Robertson, a young girl named Frederica finds herself thrust into a world of uncertainty and danger after the sudden loss of her parents. Left in the care of her guardians, she must navigate the treacherous waters of deceit, betrayal, and hidden secrets. As Frederica unravels the mysteries surrounding her parents' death, she discovers that her fate is intertwined with a powerful secret society. With the help of unexpected allies, she embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth and reclaim her rightful place in society.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Orphanhood, Family, Secrets, Betrayal, Identity
