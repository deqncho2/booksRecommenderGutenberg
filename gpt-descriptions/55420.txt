Synopsis: 
In 'For the Defence' by Fergus Hume, a renowned defense lawyer finds himself entangled in a perplexing murder case that challenges his skills and beliefs. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that could shatter his reputation and jeopardize his own life. With time running out, he must navigate through a maze of lies and manipulation to uncover the truth and save his client from a life behind bars.

Genres: Mystery, Legal Thriller, Crime Fiction

Themes: Justice and injustice, the power of truth, the complexities of the legal system
