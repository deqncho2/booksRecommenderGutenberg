Synopsis:
In 'Havoc' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious and enigmatic woman named Madame de Medici arrives in London, capturing the attention of both the upper class and the criminal underworld. As her true identity and motives remain shrouded in secrecy, a young journalist becomes determined to uncover the truth behind her captivating allure. With danger lurking at every turn, the journalist finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As the stakes rise, he must navigate a treacherous world where nothing is as it seems, and where the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Identity
