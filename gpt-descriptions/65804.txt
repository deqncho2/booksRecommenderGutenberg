Synopsis: In 'What Every Housewife Should Know About Electric Cooking (1945)', General Motors Corporation's Frigidaire Division takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of electric cooking. This informative guide unveils the secrets and wonders of this modern culinary revolution, offering practical tips, delicious recipes, and expert advice. From the convenience of electric stoves to the art of perfect baking, this book promises to empower housewives with the knowledge and skills to master the electric kitchen. With its engaging narrative and insightful content, readers will discover a whole new realm of possibilities in the realm of cooking.

Genres: Non-fiction, Cooking, Home Improvement

Themes: Modernization, Convenience, Empowerment
