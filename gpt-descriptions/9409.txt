Synopsis: 
In 'Five Thousand Dollars Reward' by A. Frank Pinkerton, a wealthy businessman's daughter is kidnapped, and he offers a substantial reward for her safe return. As the investigation unfolds, a determined detective is assigned to the case, uncovering a web of secrets, unexpected alliances, and dangerous encounters. With time running out, the detective must navigate through a maze of deceit and betrayal to bring the kidnappers to justice and rescue the young woman. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Kidnapping, Investigation, Betrayal, Justice
