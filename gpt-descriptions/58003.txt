Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Bertha's Visit to Her Uncle in England', Bertha embarks on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected encounters and mysterious secrets. As she explores the enchanting landscapes of England, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that tests her courage and determination. With each new revelation, Bertha's understanding of herself and her place in the world is challenged, leading her on a path of self-discovery and growth. Will she uncover the truth behind her uncle's peculiar behavior? And what hidden dangers lie ahead? Join Bertha on this captivating adventure that will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Courage, Family secrets
