Synopsis: 

'Representative Plays by American Dramatists: 1765-1819' takes readers on a captivating journey through the early years of American theater, showcasing a collection of influential plays that shaped the nation's dramatic landscape. From the stirring political dramas to the heartfelt comedies, this anthology offers a glimpse into the rich tapestry of American playwriting during a pivotal period in history. Each play presents a unique perspective on the challenges and triumphs of the time, exploring themes of revolution, identity, love, and the pursuit of freedom. 

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Revolution, Identity, Love, Freedom
