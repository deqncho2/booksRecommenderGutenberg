Synopsis: 

Step back in time to January 1915 and explore the fascinating world of school and college textbooks with the 'Complete Classified Price List of School & College Textbooks'. Published by the American Book Company, this comprehensive guide offers a glimpse into the educational materials available during this era. From literature and history to mathematics and science, this price list provides a captivating snapshot of the educational landscape of the time. Discover the diverse range of textbooks, their prices, and the subjects that shaped the minds of students in the early 20th century.

Genres: Historical reference, educational literature

Themes: Education, curriculum, publishing industry
