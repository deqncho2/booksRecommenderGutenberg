Synopsis: In 'Gladiator' by Philip Wylie, a mysterious and powerful figure emerges from the shadows to challenge the corrupt and oppressive regime that rules over a dystopian society. As this enigmatic gladiator rises to prominence, he becomes a symbol of hope for the oppressed masses, inspiring them to rise up against their oppressors. Filled with thrilling action, unexpected twists, and thought-provoking moral dilemmas, 'Gladiator' takes readers on a gripping journey through a world on the brink of revolution.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Action

Themes: Oppression, Revolution, Morality
