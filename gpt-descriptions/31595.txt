Synopsis: In 'The Return of the Prodigal' by Sinclair, May, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and redemption after a mysterious event forces her to confront her past. As she delves into the secrets that have haunted her family for years, Emily must navigate a web of lies, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, she becomes more determined to uncover the truth and find her place in a world that has always seemed just out of reach.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Redemption, Identity
