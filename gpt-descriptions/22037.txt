Synopsis: 
"The Life, Public Services and Select Speeches of Rutherford B. Hayes" delves into the fascinating journey of Rutherford B. Hayes, the 19th President of the United States. From his humble beginnings to his rise in politics, this biography offers a captivating exploration of Hayes' life, his accomplishments, and the challenges he faced along the way. Through a collection of select speeches, readers gain insight into Hayes' leadership style and his dedication to the principles of justice and equality. This book provides a compelling portrait of a man who played a significant role in shaping American history.

Genres: Biography, History, Politics

Themes: Leadership, Justice, Equality
