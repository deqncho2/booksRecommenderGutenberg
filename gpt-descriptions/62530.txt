Synopsis: 
In 'Adventures in Thrift' by Richardson, Anna Steese, we follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Emily as she embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets of thrift and frugality. Faced with financial struggles and societal pressures, Emily finds herself drawn into a world of unexpected adventures and valuable life lessons. From thrift stores to hidden treasures, she discovers that the true value of thrift lies not only in saving money, but also in the joy of simplicity and the power of resourcefulness. As Emily's journey unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the twists and turns that await her, ultimately leaving them inspired to embrace thrift in their own lives.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Thrift, Frugality, Self-discovery
