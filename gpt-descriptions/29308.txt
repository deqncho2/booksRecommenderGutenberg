Synopsis: In 'Small World' by William F. Nolan, a group of strangers find themselves trapped in a mysterious and seemingly endless underground labyrinth. As they navigate the treacherous maze, they must confront their deepest fears and darkest secrets, all while trying to unravel the enigma that binds them together. With each twist and turn, the characters are forced to confront their own mortality and question the nature of reality itself. As tensions rise and alliances shift, the group must find a way to escape before they lose themselves completely.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Horror

Themes: Identity, Fear, Survival
