Synopsis: 
In 'An Arkansas Planter' by Read, Opie Percival, we are introduced to a wealthy and influential planter in the heart of Arkansas. As the story unfolds, we witness the planter's life take an unexpected turn when a mysterious stranger arrives in town. With secrets and hidden agendas, the stranger's presence threatens to unravel the planter's carefully constructed world. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the planter must navigate a treacherous path to protect his family and preserve his legacy.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Power and Influence, Secrets and Betrayal, Family and Legacy
