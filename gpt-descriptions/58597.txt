Synopsis: In 'The Meredith Mystery' by Natalie Sumner Lincoln, a young woman named Meredith finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits a mysterious estate from a distant relative. As she delves deeper into the history of the estate, Meredith uncovers a series of puzzling clues that lead her on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth. With danger lurking around every corner, she must navigate through hidden passageways, decipher cryptic messages, and confront her own family's dark past. Will Meredith be able to solve the enigma before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Historical Fiction

Themes: Inheritance, Family Secrets, Intrigue
