Synopsis: 

'Character' by Samuel Smiles is a captivating novel that follows the life of a young protagonist as he navigates through various challenges and experiences, ultimately shaping his character. From humble beginnings to unexpected encounters, the story takes readers on a journey filled with personal growth, moral dilemmas, and the pursuit of self-discovery. As the protagonist faces both triumphs and setbacks, he must confront the choices he makes and the impact they have on his character. With its compelling narrative and thought-provoking situations, 'Character' offers a profound exploration of the human spirit and the power of individual choices.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Bildungsroman

Themes: Personal growth, Moral dilemmas, Self-discovery
