Synopsis: 
In 'Basque Legends; With an Essay on the Basque Language', Webster, Wentworth takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and enchanting legends of the Basque people. From mythical creatures and heroic figures to tales of love and betrayal, this collection of stories immerses readers in the mystical world of the Basque culture. Alongside these captivating legends, Webster, Wentworth also delves into the intricacies of the Basque language, shedding light on its unique characteristics and historical significance. This book is a treasure trove of captivating narratives and linguistic exploration that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Linguistics

Themes: Cultural heritage, Mythical beings, Language preservation
