Synopsis: In "Friar Tuck: Being the Chronicles of the Reverend John Carmichael, of Wyoming, U. S. A.", Reverend John Carmichael finds himself in a small town in Wyoming, where he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the town, he discovers a hidden world of corruption, deception, and unexpected alliances. With the help of an enigmatic figure known as Friar Tuck, Reverend Carmichael must navigate through treacherous waters to uncover the truth and bring justice to the town.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Corruption, Deception, Justice
