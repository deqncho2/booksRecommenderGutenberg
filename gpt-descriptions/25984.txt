Synopsis: 
In 'The Big Drum: A Comedy in Four Acts', the eccentric and wealthy Sir William Cheshire decides to host a grand masquerade ball at his country estate. As the guests arrive, they find themselves entangled in a web of mistaken identities, secret romances, and hilarious misunderstandings. With each passing act, the chaos and hilarity escalate, leading to a climactic finale that will leave the audience in stitches.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Mistaken identity, Love and romance, Social class, Deception
