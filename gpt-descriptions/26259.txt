Synopsis: In "Her Mother's Secret" by Emma Dorothy Eliza Nevitte Southworth, a young woman named Edna embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her mother's mysterious past. As she delves deeper into her family's history, she uncovers shocking secrets and hidden betrayals that threaten to unravel her own life. With each revelation, Edna must confront the consequences of her mother's choices and navigate a web of deceit and love that spans generations. This gripping tale of family secrets and forbidden love will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Love and sacrifice
