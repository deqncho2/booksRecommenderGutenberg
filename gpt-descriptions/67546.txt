Synopsis: In the Name of a Woman: A Romance by Marchmont, Arthur W. is a captivating tale that follows the life of a mysterious woman named Evelyn. As she navigates through the complexities of love, betrayal, and secrets, the reader is drawn into a web of intrigue and suspense. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque English countryside, this novel explores the depths of human emotions and the lengths one will go to protect their true identity.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Love, Betrayal, Secrets
