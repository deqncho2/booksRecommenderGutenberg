Synopsis: In "From the Print Media to the Internet," Marie Lebert takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of journalism in the digital age. From the early days of newspapers to the rise of online news platforms, Lebert explores the profound impact of technology on the way we consume and produce news. Through insightful anecdotes and interviews with industry experts, she uncovers the challenges faced by journalists in adapting to this new landscape, while also highlighting the exciting opportunities that arise from the convergence of print media and the internet. This thought-provoking exploration of the changing face of journalism will leave readers with a deeper understanding of the power and potential of the digital revolution.

Genres: Non-fiction, Journalism, Technology

Themes: Media evolution, Technological disruption, Journalism ethics, Digital transformation
