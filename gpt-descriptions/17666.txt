Synopsis: In the enchanting countryside of Italy, Lucia Rudini finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret that has been buried for decades. As she delves deeper into the past, Lucia uncovers a series of clues that lead her on a thrilling journey across the picturesque landscapes of Italy, encountering unexpected allies and dangerous adversaries along the way. With time running out, Lucia must unravel the truth before it is lost forever, and in doing so, she discovers a strength within herself that she never knew existed.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, self-discovery, the power of determination
