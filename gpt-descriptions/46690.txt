Synopsis: 

In 'Cornish Saints & Sinners' by J. Henry Harris, a small coastal town in Cornwall becomes the backdrop for a gripping tale of secrets, betrayal, and redemption. When a mysterious stranger arrives in town, the lives of the locals are thrown into disarray as long-buried secrets are unearthed. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the residents of this close-knit community must confront their pasts and face the consequences of their actions. With its richly drawn characters and atmospheric setting, this captivating novel will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption, Community
