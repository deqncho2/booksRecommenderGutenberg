Synopsis: 
In 'Forget Me Nearly' by Wallace, F. L., a young woman wakes up in a hospital bed with no memory of who she is or how she got there. As she embarks on a journey to uncover her past, she discovers a web of secrets and lies that threaten to unravel her newfound life. With each clue she uncovers, she becomes more entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal. As the truth slowly unravels, she must confront her own identity and decide who she can trust before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Memory, Deception, Betrayal
