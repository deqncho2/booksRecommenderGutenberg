Synopsis: In "The Son of a Servant" by August Strindberg, we follow the life of a young man named Gustav who grows up in poverty and faces numerous challenges. From his humble beginnings as the son of a servant, Gustav navigates through a world filled with social hierarchies, love, betrayal, and personal growth. As he strives to overcome the limitations imposed by his birth, Gustav's journey takes unexpected turns, revealing the complexities of human nature and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Drama, Bildungsroman, Realism

Themes: Social class, Identity, Love and relationships, Ambition, Self-discovery
