Synopsis: 
'Aucassin & Nicolette, and Other Mediaeval Romances and Legends' is a captivating collection of medieval tales that transport readers to a world of chivalry, romance, and adventure. From the forbidden love between Aucassin and Nicolette to the heroic exploits of knights and the enchanting tales of mythical creatures, this anthology offers a rich tapestry of medieval storytelling. Each story is filled with vivid imagery, lyrical prose, and unexpected twists that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. 

Genres: 
Romance, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: 
Forbidden love, Chivalry, Heroism, Mythology
