Synopsis:
In "The Emerald City of Oz," Dorothy and her friends embark on a thrilling adventure to the magical Emerald City. However, they soon discover that the city is in danger and must join forces with new allies to save it. Along the way, they encounter enchanting creatures, face unexpected challenges, and uncover secrets that will change their lives forever. Will Dorothy and her friends be able to protect the Emerald City and find their way back home?

Genres:
Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes:
Friendship, Courage, Loyalty, Home
