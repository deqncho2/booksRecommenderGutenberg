Synopsis: In 'The Girl Aviators and the Phantom Airship' by Margaret Burnham, a group of adventurous young aviators find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey when they encounter a mysterious phantom airship. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they must use their flying skills and quick thinking to uncover the truth behind the phantom airship and the secrets it holds. With danger lurking at every turn, the girl aviators must rely on their courage and teamwork to solve the puzzle and save the day.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Problem-solving
