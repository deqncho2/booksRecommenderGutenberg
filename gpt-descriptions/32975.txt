Synopsis: 

In the fifth volume of the renowned Encyclopaedia Britannica, 11th Edition, we delve into a fascinating range of topics, from the influential American statesman John C. Calhoun to the enigmatic Portuguese poet Luís de Camões. This volume takes us on a captivating journey through history, politics, literature, and culture, offering a comprehensive exploration of the lives and contributions of these remarkable individuals. From Calhoun's pivotal role in shaping American politics to Camões' poetic genius that left an indelible mark on Portuguese literature, this volume presents a rich tapestry of knowledge and insight. 

Genres: Reference, Biography, History, Literature

Themes: Politics, Literature, Cultural Influence
