Synopsis:
Emily Starr, a young and imaginative girl, finds herself uprooted from her beloved home and sent to live with her strict and unyielding relatives on Prince Edward Island. As she navigates the challenges of her new life, Emily discovers a hidden talent for writing and a deep connection to the natural beauty that surrounds her. With the support of a few kindred spirits, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, embracing her dreams and defying societal expectations. Along the way, Emily encounters love, loss, and the complexities of family, all while striving to find her own voice and place in the world.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Fiction, Drama

Themes: Identity, Creativity, Family, Nature, Self-discovery
