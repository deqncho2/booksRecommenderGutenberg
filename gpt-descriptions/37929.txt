Synopsis: In 'Fenn Masterson's Discovery; or, The Darewell Chums on a Cruise' by Chapman, Allen, a group of adventurous friends embark on a thrilling cruise that takes them to exotic locations and unexpected encounters. As they navigate through mysterious islands and encounter dangerous situations, Fenn Masterson stumbles upon a remarkable discovery that could change their lives forever. With their friendship and courage tested, the Darewell Chums must work together to unravel the secrets that lie within their extraordinary journey.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Friendship

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Discovery
