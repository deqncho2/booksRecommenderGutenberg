Synopsis: In the heart of Tokyo, a mysterious building stands tall, captivating the curiosity of all who pass by. Its dark history and enigmatic inhabitants draw a young journalist, Hiroshi, into a web of secrets and intrigue. As he delves deeper into the building's past, Hiroshi uncovers a haunting tale of love, betrayal, and the supernatural. With each revelation, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game where the line between reality and illusion blurs. Will Hiroshi uncover the truth before it consumes him?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Love, Illusion
