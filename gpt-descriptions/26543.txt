Synopsis: 
In 'Clematis' by Bertha B. Cobb and Ernest Cobb, a mysterious woman named Clematis arrives in a small town, captivating the residents with her enigmatic charm. As the townspeople become increasingly infatuated with her, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden desires that threaten to unravel their lives. With each passing day, Clematis's presence becomes more intoxicating, leading to unexpected consequences that will leave readers questioning the true nature of love and obsession.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets, Desire, Obsession
