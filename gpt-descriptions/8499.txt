Synopsis:
In "Plays: The Father; Countess Julie; The Outlaw; The Stronger," August Strindberg presents a collection of captivating plays that delve into the complexities of human relationships and societal norms. "The Father" explores the power dynamics within a family, while "Countess Julie" delves into the forbidden desires and class struggles of a young aristocrat. "The Outlaw" takes us on a journey of love and betrayal, while "The Stronger" delves into the intricacies of female rivalry. Each play offers a unique and thought-provoking exploration of the human condition.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Naturalism

Themes: Power dynamics, Class struggles, Love and betrayal, Female rivalry
