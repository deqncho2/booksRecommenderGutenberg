Synopsis: 

'Out for Business; or, Robert Frost's Strange Career' follows the journey of Robert Frost, a young and ambitious businessman who sets out to make his mark in the business world. However, as he climbs the ladder of success, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With his reputation and future on the line, Frost must navigate through a series of thrilling twists and turns, all while trying to uncover the truth behind his strange and mysterious career.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Betrayal
