Synopsis: In 'The Seven Sleuths' Club' by Carol Norton, a group of seven friends with a knack for solving mysteries form their own detective club. When a puzzling case arises in their small town, they embark on an exciting adventure to uncover the truth. As they follow clues, unravel secrets, and face unexpected challenges, the young sleuths must rely on their unique skills and teamwork to crack the case. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the final revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Friendship

Themes: Teamwork, Problem-solving, Friendship
