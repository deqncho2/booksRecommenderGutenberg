Synopsis: 

In 'Greenmantle' by John Buchan, Richard Hannay finds himself embroiled in a thrilling adventure that takes him from the streets of London to the treacherous landscapes of the Middle East during World War I. Tasked with a dangerous mission by the British government, Hannay must navigate a web of espionage, deception, and political intrigue as he races against time to uncover a sinister plot that could change the course of history. With his life on the line and enemies lurking at every turn, Hannay must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to unravel the mysteries of 'Greenmantle'.

Genres: Thriller, Espionage, Historical Fiction

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Identity
