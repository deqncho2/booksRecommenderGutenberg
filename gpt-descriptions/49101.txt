Synopsis: In 'A Middy's Recollections, 1853-1860', Montagu, Victor Alexander takes us on a captivating journey through his experiences as a young midshipman in the Royal Navy during the mid-19th century. From thrilling naval battles to encounters with exotic cultures, Montagu's recollections offer a vivid and personal account of life at sea during a time of great historical significance. As he navigates the challenges of his role, Montagu also grapples with questions of identity, duty, and the complexities of human relationships. Through his engaging storytelling, readers are transported to a bygone era filled with adventure, danger, and self-discovery.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Coming of Age, Duty and Honor, Exploration and Discovery
