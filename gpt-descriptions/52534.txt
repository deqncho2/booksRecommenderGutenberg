Synopsis: In 'True Manliness: From the Writings of Thomas Hughes', readers are taken on a captivating journey through the writings of Thomas Hughes, exploring the essence of true manliness. Through a collection of thought-provoking essays and stories, Hughes delves into the complexities of masculinity, challenging societal norms and redefining what it means to be a man. From tales of courage and honor to reflections on character and integrity, this book offers a profound exploration of manhood that will leave readers inspired and questioning their own understanding of masculinity.

Genres: Essay, Fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Masculinity, Identity, Honor, Courage, Integrity
