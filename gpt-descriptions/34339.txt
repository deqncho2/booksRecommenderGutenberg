Synopsis: In "The Princess and the Goblin," young Princess Irene discovers a secret world beneath her castle, inhabited by mysterious creatures and guarded by a brave miner boy named Curdie. As Irene and Curdie embark on a perilous adventure, they must confront the goblins who seek to harm the princess and uncover the truth about their own destinies. This enchanting tale weaves together elements of fantasy, adventure, and mystery, captivating readers with its magical atmosphere and unexpected twists.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Good vs. Evil
