Synopsis: In 'The Day Time Stopped Moving' by Repp, Ed Earl, a young scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges the very fabric of reality. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where time itself becomes a formidable adversary. With each passing moment, the line between past, present, and future blurs, leaving the protagonist questioning his own sanity and the consequences of tampering with the laws of nature.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Time manipulation, Existentialism, Consequences of scientific advancement
