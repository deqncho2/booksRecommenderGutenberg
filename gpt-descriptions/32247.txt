Synopsis: In 'The Battle of Allatoona, October 5th, 1864' by Ludlow, William, readers are transported to the heart of the American Civil War as they witness a pivotal clash between Union and Confederate forces. Amidst the chaos and violence, the story follows a diverse cast of characters who must navigate the treacherous battlefield and confront their own personal demons. As the battle rages on, alliances are tested, loyalties are questioned, and the true cost of war becomes painfully clear. This gripping historical novel offers a vivid and immersive portrayal of a crucial moment in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Sacrifice, The Human Cost of War
