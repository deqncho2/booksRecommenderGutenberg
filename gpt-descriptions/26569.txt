Synopsis: In 'Monkey On His Back' by Charles V. De Vet, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Jonathan Crane finds himself entangled in a dangerous experiment that goes horribly wrong. As he struggles to undo the catastrophic consequences of his creation, he becomes the target of a relentless pursuit by a mysterious organization. With time running out, Dr. Crane must confront his own demons and make a choice that could change the course of humanity forever.

Genres: Science fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Responsibility, Redemption
