Synopsis: 

In "Dealings with the Inquisition; Or, Papal Rome, Her Priests, and Her Jesuits," Giacinto Achilli takes readers on a gripping journey through his personal experiences with the Inquisition and the inner workings of Papal Rome. As a former Catholic priest and a convert to Protestantism, Achilli unveils the dark secrets and manipulative tactics employed by the Roman Catholic Church and its powerful Jesuit order. From his encounters with corrupt priests to his daring escape from the clutches of the Inquisition, Achilli's account is filled with suspense, intrigue, and a relentless pursuit of truth. 

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Religious Critique

Themes: Religious oppression, Corruption, Conversion, Freedom of thought
