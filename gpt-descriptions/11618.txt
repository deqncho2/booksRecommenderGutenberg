Synopsis: 
'From Chaucer to Tennyson: With Twenty-Nine Portraits and Selections from Thirty Authors' takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of English literature, spanning from the medieval era to the Victorian age. This anthology, curated by Henry A. Beers, offers a delightful collection of excerpts from renowned authors such as Chaucer, Shakespeare, Milton, and Tennyson, among others. Each selection is accompanied by a vivid portrait of the respective author, adding a visual element to the exploration of their literary contributions. Through this anthology, readers can immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of English literature, discovering the diverse voices and styles that have shaped the written word over centuries.

Genres: Literary Anthology, Literary Criticism, Biography

Themes: Evolution of English Literature, Influence of Great Authors, Artistic Expression, Historical Context
