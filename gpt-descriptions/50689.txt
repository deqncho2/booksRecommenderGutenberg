Synopsis: In 'One in a Thousand; or, The Days of Henri Quatre', James, G. P. R. takes us on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous times of King Henri IV of France. Amidst political intrigue, religious conflicts, and personal vendettas, a young nobleman finds himself caught in a web of danger and romance. As he navigates the treacherous court of Henri Quatre, he must make choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of the kingdom. With vivid historical detail and captivating storytelling, this novel immerses readers in a world of power, passion, and betrayal.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Ambition, Love and Sacrifice
