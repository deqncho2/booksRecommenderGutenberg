Synopsis: In the May 1886 issue of St. Nicholas Magazine for Boys and Girls, young readers will embark on a thrilling adventure filled with captivating illustrations. This illustrated magazine for young folks features a collection of exciting stories, fascinating articles, and delightful poems that will transport readers to different worlds and ignite their imagination. From tales of daring explorers to heartwarming stories of friendship, this volume promises to captivate and entertain young readers with its diverse range of content.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Fiction, Poetry

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Imagination, Education
