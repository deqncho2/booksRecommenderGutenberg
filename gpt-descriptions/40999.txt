Synopsis: In 'Stolen Souls' by William Le Queux, a young woman named Muriel Chetwynd finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she receives a cryptic message from her missing father. Determined to uncover the truth, Muriel embarks on a thrilling journey across Europe, encountering enigmatic characters, secret societies, and unexpected twists at every turn. As she delves deeper into the secrets of her father's past, Muriel must confront her own courage and resilience to save not only her father but also herself from the clutches of a sinister conspiracy.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Family secrets, Conspiracy, Courage, Resilience
