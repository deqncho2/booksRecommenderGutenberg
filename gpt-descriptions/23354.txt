Synopsis: 

In 'Vegetable Teratology: An Account of the Principal Deviations from the Usual Construction of Plants', Maxwell T. Masters delves into the fascinating world of plant abnormalities and deviations from the norm. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Masters uncovers the peculiar and often bizarre anomalies that can occur in the plant kingdom. From twisted stems to peculiar growth patterns, this captivating account explores the mysteries and wonders of plant teratology, shedding light on the extraordinary diversity of nature's creations.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Botany

Themes: Plant abnormalities, Biodiversity, Natural variations
