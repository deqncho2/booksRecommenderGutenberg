Synopsis: In "The Lady of the Forest: A Story for Girls," the reader is transported to the enchanting world of the English countryside. The story revolves around a young girl named Rosamund, who discovers a hidden secret within the depths of the forest near her home. As she unravels the mysteries of the forest, Rosamund embarks on a journey of self-discovery, friendship, and courage. With each step she takes, she becomes more entwined in the magical realm of the Lady of the Forest, where her destiny awaits.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Nature and its connection to the human spirit, the power of friendship, self-discovery and personal growth
