Synopsis: 

Step back in time and embark on a captivating journey through the charming and mysterious world of the Old Country Inns of England. In this enchanting book, authors Gregory and Maskell invite readers to explore the hidden stories and secrets that lie within these historic establishments. From the quaint village pubs nestled in picturesque landscapes to the grand coaching inns that once welcomed weary travelers, each inn holds a unique tale waiting to be discovered. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, the authors paint a vivid picture of the rich history, fascinating characters, and timeless allure of these beloved establishments. Whether you are a history enthusiast, a lover of architecture, or simply seeking a delightful escape, 'Old Country Inns of England' promises to transport you to a bygone era and leave you yearning to experience the warmth and charm of these remarkable inns for yourself.

Genres: Travel, History, Architecture

Themes: English heritage, Cultural preservation, Nostalgia
