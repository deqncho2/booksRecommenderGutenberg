Synopsis: 
In 'Thrilling Stories Of The Ocean', author Marmaduke Park takes readers on a captivating journey through the vast and mysterious world of the ocean. Through a collection of authentic accounts from modern voyagers and travelers, this book offers a thrilling and educational experience for young readers. From tales of daring explorations to encounters with fascinating marine creatures, each story is filled with excitement and wonder. Dive into the depths of the ocean and embark on unforgettable adventures that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Marine life, Courage, Discovery
