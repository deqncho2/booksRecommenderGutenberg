Synopsis: In 'The Eugenic Marriage, Volume 2', author W. Grant Hague delves deeper into the fascinating world of eugenics, offering a personal guide to achieving a better life and better babies. Through a combination of scientific research, practical advice, and thought-provoking anecdotes, Hague explores the potential of eugenics to shape the future of humanity. From the importance of selecting the right partner to the significance of heredity and environment, this volume provides valuable insights into the principles and practices of eugenic living. As readers embark on this enlightening journey, they will discover a wealth of knowledge that challenges conventional beliefs and encourages them to consider the possibilities of a more genetically advanced society.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Self-help

Themes: Eugenics, Genetics, Human improvement, Personal development
