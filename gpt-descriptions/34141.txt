Synopsis: In 'The Parson O' Dumford' by George Manville Fenn, a young parson finds himself in a small village filled with secrets and mysteries. As he delves deeper into the lives of the villagers, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden desires. With his faith and determination, the parson must navigate through the darkness to bring light and justice to Dumford.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption, Faith
