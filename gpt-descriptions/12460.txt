Synopsis: 
In 'Pomona's Travels: A Series of Letters to the Mistress of Rudder Grange from her Former Handmaiden' by Frank R. Stockton, readers are taken on a delightful journey through the witty and adventurous correspondence between Pomona, a former handmaiden, and her former mistress. As Pomona embarks on a series of unexpected travels, she shares her humorous and often unconventional experiences through her letters, providing a unique perspective on the world. From encounters with eccentric characters to unexpected twists and turns, this epistolary novel promises to captivate readers with its charm and wit.

Genres: 
Epistolary fiction, Adventure, Humor

Themes: 
Friendship, Independence, Exploration
