Synopsis: In 'The English Rogue: Continued in the Life of Meriton Latroon, and Other Extravagants: The Second Part', we follow the daring and audacious adventures of Meriton Latroon, a notorious rogue and master of deception. From his encounters with wealthy aristocrats to his escapades in the criminal underworld, Meriton's cunning and charm know no bounds. As he navigates through a world filled with danger and intrigue, he must constantly adapt and outwit his adversaries to survive. This thrilling sequel to 'The English Rogue' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what outrageous exploits Meriton will undertake next.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Picaresque

Themes: Deception, Survival, Morality
