Synopsis: 

'Bringing up the Boy: A Message to Fathers and Mothers from a Boy of Yesterday Concerning the Men of To-morrow' by Carl Avery Werner is a thought-provoking and insightful book that offers a unique perspective on parenting and the future of our society. Through the eyes of a boy from the past, the author shares his wisdom and experiences, providing valuable lessons for both fathers and mothers as they navigate the challenges of raising children in the modern world. This book delves into the complexities of parenthood, exploring the impact that parents have on shaping the men and women of tomorrow.

Genres: Self-help, Parenting, Memoir

Themes: Parenting, Childhood, Personal growth, Social impact
