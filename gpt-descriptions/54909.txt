Synopsis: In 'Harry's Island' by Ralph Henry Barbour, a young man named Harry finds himself stranded on a remote island after a shipwreck. As he navigates the challenges of survival, he discovers hidden secrets and unexpected allies that will change his life forever. With danger lurking around every corner, Harry must summon his courage and resourcefulness to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way. This thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the mysteries of Harry's Island.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Survival

Themes: Survival, Discovery, Courage
