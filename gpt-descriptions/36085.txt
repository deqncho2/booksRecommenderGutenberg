Synopsis: In 'Turns about Town' by Holliday, Robert Cortes, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a series of unexpected events and secrets that threaten to unravel the community. As the townspeople navigate through deception, betrayal, and hidden desires, they must confront their own demons and make choices that will forever change their lives. With its gripping plot and intriguing characters, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Small-town secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
