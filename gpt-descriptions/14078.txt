Synopsis: 

In 'The Liberation of Italy, 1815-1870' by Martinengo-Cesaresco, Evelyn Lilian Hazeldine Carrington, contessa, readers are transported to a tumultuous period in Italian history. This captivating work delves into the struggles and triumphs of the Italian people as they strive for independence and unity. Through a rich tapestry of historical events, political intrigue, and personal narratives, the author paints a vivid picture of the arduous journey towards liberation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, political history, biography

Themes: Nationalism, revolution, identity, political upheaval, cultural transformation
