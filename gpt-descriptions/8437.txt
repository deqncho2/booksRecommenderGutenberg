Synopsis: In 'The Path of Life' by Stijn Streuvels, a young man named Felix embarks on a journey that takes him through the picturesque countryside of Flanders. As he navigates the challenges and encounters the diverse characters along his path, Felix's perspective on life and his own identity undergoes a profound transformation. Through vivid descriptions and introspective moments, the reader is drawn into Felix's captivating journey of self-discovery and the exploration of the human condition.

Genres: Literary fiction, Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Nature, Human condition
