Synopsis: 
In "Maiwa's Revenge; Or, The War of the Little Hand," H. Rider Haggard takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the treacherous landscapes of South Africa. The story follows the courageous Maiwa, a young African woman seeking vengeance for the atrocities committed against her people. As she embarks on a perilous journey, she encounters danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With her unwavering determination and resourcefulness, Maiwa becomes a force to be reckoned with, challenging the oppressive forces that have plagued her homeland. This gripping tale of revenge and resilience will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Revenge, Justice, Colonialism, Female Empowerment
