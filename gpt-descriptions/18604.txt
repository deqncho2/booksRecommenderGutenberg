Synopsis: In "The Ice-Maiden: and Other Tales" by Hans Christian Andersen, readers are transported to a world of enchantment and wonder. This collection of tales includes stories of love, sacrifice, and the power of the human spirit. From a young girl's encounter with a mysterious ice-maiden to a brave tin soldier's journey through adversity, each story captivates with its magical elements and heartfelt messages. As readers delve into these tales, they will be swept away by the beauty of Andersen's storytelling and the timeless themes that resonate throughout.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Children's literature

Themes: Love, Sacrifice, Resilience, Transformation
