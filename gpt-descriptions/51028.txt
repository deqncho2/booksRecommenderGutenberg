Synopsis: 
In 'The Protector' by Curtis, Betsy, a young woman named Emily discovers a hidden world of supernatural beings living among humans. When she becomes the target of a powerful and dangerous entity, she must rely on a mysterious and enigmatic protector to keep her safe. As Emily delves deeper into this hidden world, she uncovers dark secrets and uncanny abilities within herself, leading her to question her own identity and the true nature of those around her. With danger lurking at every turn, Emily must navigate a treacherous path of trust, betrayal, and self-discovery to survive.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Paranormal, Romance

Themes: 
Identity, Trust, Power
