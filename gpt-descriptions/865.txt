Synopsis: In "Passing of the Third Floor Back," a mysterious stranger moves into a boarding house filled with a motley crew of characters. As he interacts with each resident, he brings out their hidden desires, challenges their prejudices, and inspires them to become better versions of themselves. But who is this enigmatic figure, and what is his true purpose in the house?

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Fantasy

Themes: Redemption, Transformation, Human Nature
