Synopsis: In "According to Plato" by Frank Frankfort Moore, a renowned philosopher mysteriously disappears, leaving behind a cryptic message that leads a group of curious individuals on a thrilling quest for truth and enlightenment. As they delve into the ancient teachings of Plato, they uncover hidden secrets, encounter unexpected allies and adversaries, and embark on a journey that challenges their beliefs and perceptions. With each revelation, the line between reality and illusion becomes blurred, leaving the reader questioning the nature of knowledge and the power of philosophy.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Philosophy

Themes: Truth and deception, the pursuit of knowledge, the nature of reality
