Synopsis: In 'Return Engagement' by Margaret St. Clair, a renowned scientist discovers a mysterious artifact that holds the key to unlocking humanity's greatest potential. As she delves deeper into its secrets, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where ancient powers clash with modern ambitions. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, she must navigate treacherous alliances and confront her own inner demons to ensure a future where humanity can thrive. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Power of knowledge, Ethical dilemmas, Redemption
