Synopsis: In 'Longhead: The Story of the First Fire', Robinson, C. H. takes readers on a captivating journey back to prehistoric times. Follow the adventures of Longhead, a young cave dweller who embarks on a dangerous quest to discover the secret of fire. Along the way, he encounters treacherous landscapes, encounters with wild animals, and encounters with rival tribes. Will Longhead succeed in his mission and bring the gift of fire to his people? This thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the outcome.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Prehistoric Fiction

Themes: Survival, Discovery, Human Ingenuity
