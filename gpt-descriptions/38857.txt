Synopsis: In 'Irradiations; Sand and Spray' by John Gould Fletcher, a mesmerizing collection of poems takes readers on a journey through vivid landscapes, exploring the interplay between nature and the human experience. From the ethereal beauty of sand dunes to the relentless power of ocean spray, Fletcher's words transport us to a realm where the boundaries between the physical and the metaphysical blur. With each poem, the reader is invited to contemplate the profound mysteries of existence and the transformative power of the natural world. 

Genres: Poetry, Modernist literature

Themes: Nature, Transcendence, Human experience
