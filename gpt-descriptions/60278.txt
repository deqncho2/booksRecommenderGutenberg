Synopsis: In 'The Thirteen Travellers' by Hugh Walpole, a diverse group of individuals find themselves stranded in a remote country inn during a snowstorm. As they wait for the weather to clear, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to unexpected alliances and dangerous confrontations. Each character harbors their own dark past and hidden motives, making the atmosphere increasingly suspenseful and unpredictable. As the storm rages on, the true nature of these thirteen travelers is gradually unveiled, leaving the reader questioning who can be trusted and what will happen next.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity, Redemption
