Synopsis: In "The Lifted Bandage," a young woman named Margaret finds herself in a peculiar situation when she discovers a mysterious bandage on her husband's arm. As she delves deeper into the truth behind the bandage, Margaret uncovers a web of secrets and deception that threatens to unravel her entire world. With each revelation, she must confront the complexities of love, trust, and the lengths one will go to protect those they hold dear.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Betrayal, Secrets, Trust, Love, Deception
