Synopsis: In "Young Wild West at "Forbidden Pass": and, How Arietta Paid the Toll" by Old Scout, Young Wild West and his loyal companions find themselves facing a treacherous journey through the Forbidden Pass. As they navigate the dangerous terrain, they encounter a mysterious toll collector named Arietta, who holds a secret that could change their lives forever. With thrilling adventures, unexpected twists, and a race against time, this action-packed tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Redemption
