Synopsis:
In 'Half-hours with the Highwaymen - Vol 1: Picturesque Biographies and Traditions of the "Knights of the Road",' Charles G. Harper delves into the captivating world of highwaymen, exploring their lives, adventures, and the legends that surround them. From the infamous Dick Turpin to lesser-known figures, Harper paints vivid portraits of these daring outlaws, their audacious exploits, and the romanticized allure of their criminal lifestyles. Through engaging narratives and historical anecdotes, the author takes readers on a thrilling journey through the highways and byways of England, where danger and excitement lurked around every bend. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to a bygone era, where the line between hero and villain was blurred, and the highwaymen reigned supreme.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Crime and punishment, Legends and folklore, Romanticized outlaws
