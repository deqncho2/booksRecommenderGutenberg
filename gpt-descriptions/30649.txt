Synopsis: In 'Last Resort' by Stephen Bartholomew, a group of strangers find themselves trapped on a secluded island resort with no way to escape. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, they must confront their darkest fears and work together to survive. With each passing day, the line between reality and nightmare becomes blurred, leaving the characters questioning their own sanity. As they uncover the island's dark history, they realize that their only hope for survival lies in unraveling the mysteries that surround them. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Survival, Trust, Identity, Psychological Manipulation
