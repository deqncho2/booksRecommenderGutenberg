Synopsis: In "Secrets of Radar," Roy J. Snell takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of radar technology during World War II. As the war rages on, a group of brilliant scientists and engineers are tasked with developing a top-secret radar system that could change the course of the war. Amidst the chaos and danger, they must navigate personal sacrifices, unexpected challenges, and the constant threat of enemy infiltration. With lives hanging in the balance, the race to unlock the secrets of radar becomes a gripping tale of innovation, espionage, and heroism.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Technological Thriller

Themes: Innovation, Espionage, Sacrifice, Heroism
