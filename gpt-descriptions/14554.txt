Synopsis: 
In 'Baltimore Catechism, No. 4: An Explanation of the Baltimore Catechism of Christian Doctrine for the Use of Sunday-School Teachers and Advanced Classes,' Thomas L. Kinkead delves into the depths of Christian doctrine, providing a comprehensive explanation that is both enlightening and accessible. With a focus on Sunday-school teachers and advanced classes, this book serves as a valuable resource for those seeking a deeper understanding of their faith. Kinkead's insightful explanations and thought-provoking insights make this an essential guide for anyone looking to expand their knowledge of Christian doctrine.

Genres: Religious, Educational, Reference

Themes: Christian doctrine, Faith, Education
