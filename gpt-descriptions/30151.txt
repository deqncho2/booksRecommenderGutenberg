Synopsis: In 'Macedonia: A Nation at a Crossroads', Vaknin, Samuel takes readers on a captivating journey through the complex history, cultural diversity, and political challenges of Macedonia. From the ancient kingdom of Alexander the Great to the modern struggles for identity and recognition, this book unveils the untold stories and hidden truths of a nation caught between tradition and progress. As the country grapples with its past and navigates its future, Vaknin's insightful analysis sheds light on the intricate web of influences that have shaped Macedonia's destiny. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Politics

Themes: National identity, Cultural heritage, Political turmoil, Geopolitics
