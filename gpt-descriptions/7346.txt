Synopsis: 
'Among Malay Pirates: a Tale of Adventure and Peril' follows the thrilling journey of young Frank, a British boy who finds himself caught up in the dangerous world of pirates in the Malay Archipelago. Captured by a notorious pirate leader, Frank must navigate treacherous waters, face deadly enemies, and rely on his wit and courage to survive. As he forms unexpected alliances and discovers hidden secrets, Frank's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to escape the clutches of the pirates and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Survival, Friendship, Exploration
