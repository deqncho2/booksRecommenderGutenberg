Synopsis: In "The Bell in the Fog and Other Stories," Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton weaves a collection of captivating tales that delve into the depths of human emotions and the mysteries of the supernatural. From a haunting love story set in the foggy streets of London to a chilling encounter with a vengeful spirit in the California wilderness, these stories will transport readers to a world where the line between reality and the supernatural is blurred. With each story, Atherton masterfully explores the complexities of human nature and the power of fate, leaving readers spellbound until the very last page.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural fiction, mystery

Themes: Love and obsession, the supernatural, fate and destiny
