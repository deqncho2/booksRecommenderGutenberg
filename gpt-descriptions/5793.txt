Synopsis: In "Stories of Red Hanrahan," follow the captivating journey of Red Hanrahan, a wandering poet and dreamer, as he encounters mystical beings and embarks on extraordinary adventures. From encounters with fairies and witches to his search for love and spiritual enlightenment, Red Hanrahan's tales will transport you to a world where reality and fantasy intertwine, leaving you spellbound until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Irish Literature

Themes: Mythology, Love, Spirituality, Identity
