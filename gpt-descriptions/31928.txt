Synopsis: In 'A Golfing Idyll; Or, The Skipper's Round with the Deil On the Links of St. Andrews' by Violet Flint, a group of friends embark on a golfing adventure that takes an unexpected turn when they encounter a mysterious figure on the legendary links of St. Andrews. As they navigate the challenges of the course, they find themselves entangled in a thrilling and otherworldly game that tests their skills and their courage. With each swing of the club, secrets are revealed and friendships are put to the ultimate test. Will they be able to conquer the course and escape the clutches of the Deil? This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome of this extraordinary golfing journey.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Adventure, Supernatural
