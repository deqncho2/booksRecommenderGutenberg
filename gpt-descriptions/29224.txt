Synopsis: In 'The Group: A Farce' by Mercy Otis Warren, a group of eccentric characters find themselves entangled in a series of hilarious misadventures. From mistaken identities to secret romances, this uproarious farce takes readers on a wild ride filled with witty dialogue and unexpected twists. As the group navigates their way through a web of misunderstandings and comical situations, they learn valuable lessons about friendship, love, and the importance of embracing one's true self.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Love, Self-discovery
