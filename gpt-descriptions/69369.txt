Synopsis: In "Deep Channel" by Margaret Prescott Montague, a young woman named Alice finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her coastal town. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she becomes entangled with a group of intriguing characters, each with their own hidden motives. With her life on the line, Alice must navigate treacherous waters and uncover the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Betrayal and trust, Survival and self-discovery
