Synopsis: In 'Married' by August Strindberg, a seemingly happy couple's marriage is put to the test when they are confronted with unexpected challenges and secrets from their past. As tensions rise and emotions run high, the couple must navigate the complexities of love, trust, and societal expectations. With each revelation, the characters are forced to confront their own desires and confront the consequences of their actions. Will their marriage survive the trials they face, or will it crumble under the weight of their secrets?

Genres: Drama, Psychological Fiction, Realism

Themes: Marriage, Love, Betrayal, Gender Roles, Social Expectations
