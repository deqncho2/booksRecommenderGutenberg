Synopsis: In 'The Primus Curse' by Wesley Bill, a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds a dark secret. As she delves deeper into its history, she becomes entangled in a web of mystery and danger, where ancient curses and supernatural forces threaten her very existence. With time running out, she must uncover the truth behind the curse before it consumes her and those she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Ancient curses, Archaeology, Supernatural forces, Mystery, Danger
