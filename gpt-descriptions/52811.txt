Synopsis: In "Anarchy and Anarchists: A History of the Red Terror and the Social Revolution in America and Europe; Communism, Socialism, and Nihilism in Doctrine and in Deed; The Chicago Haymarket Conspiracy and the Detection and Trial of the Conspirators," Michael J. Schaack delves into the captivating world of anarchist movements and their impact on society. From the Red Terror to the Chicago Haymarket Conspiracy, this book uncovers the thrilling history of these radical ideologies and the individuals who championed them. Through meticulous research and gripping storytelling, Schaack explores the rise of anarchism, its violent manifestations, and the subsequent efforts to bring the conspirators to justice.

Genres: History, True Crime, Political Science

Themes: Anarchism, Radicalism, Social Revolution, Justice, Ideology
