Synopsis: In 'The Man Thou Gavest' by Harriet T. Comstock, a young woman named Ruth embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience in the face of adversity. Set against the backdrop of the early 20th century, Ruth's life takes an unexpected turn when she finds herself caught between duty and desire. As she navigates through love, loss, and the pursuit of her dreams, Ruth must confront the complexities of her own identity and the sacrifices she is willing to make. With vivid storytelling and compelling characters, this novel explores the depths of human emotions and the power of personal growth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Love and Sacrifice, Resilience
