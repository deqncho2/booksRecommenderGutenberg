Synopsis: In 'The Pre-Columbian Discovery of America by the Northmen: Illustrated by Translations from Icelandic Sagas', Benjamin Franklin DeCosta delves into the fascinating world of the Vikings and their possible discovery of America long before Columbus. Through meticulous research and captivating translations of Icelandic sagas, DeCosta presents a compelling argument that challenges conventional historical narratives. This thought-provoking exploration takes readers on a journey through ancient Norse legends and explores the possibility of a forgotten chapter in American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Exploration, Norse mythology

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Cultural exchange, Historical revisionism
