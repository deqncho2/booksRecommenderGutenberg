Synopsis: In the second volume of "Quintus Claudius: A Romance of Imperial Rome," the captivating story of Quintus Claudius continues as he navigates the treacherous world of ancient Rome. Filled with political intrigue, forbidden love, and unexpected alliances, this gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of the Roman Empire. As Quintus faces new challenges and confronts his own demons, the fate of Rome hangs in the balance, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. Will Quintus be able to protect those he loves and secure his place in the empire, or will he be consumed by the darkness that lurks within?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Power and ambition, Loyalty and betrayal, Love and sacrifice
