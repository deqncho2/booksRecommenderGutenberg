Synopsis: In 'New Bodies for Old' by Maurice Renard, a brilliant scientist discovers a groundbreaking method to transfer human consciousness into new bodies. As the experiment unfolds, unexpected consequences arise, leading to a thrilling exploration of identity, morality, and the limits of scientific progress. This gripping tale delves into the ethical dilemmas and existential questions that arise when humanity tamper with the very essence of life itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Morality, Scientific Progress, Existentialism
