Synopsis: In 'True to a Type, Vol. 2 (of 2)' by Cleland, Robert, the captivating story continues as the lives of the intriguing characters intertwine, secrets are revealed, and unexpected alliances are formed. As the plot thickens, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with suspense, romance, and unexpected twists. Will the characters stay true to their nature or will they surprise us all? Find out in this gripping conclusion to the series.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Loyalty, Redemption
