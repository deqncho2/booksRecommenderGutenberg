Synopsis: In 'The Big Blue Soldier' by Grace Livingston Hill, a young woman named Phyllis finds herself caught between duty and love when she encounters a mysterious soldier with a secret past. As their paths intertwine, Phyllis must navigate the complexities of loyalty, sacrifice, and the power of forgiveness. Will she choose the safe path or follow her heart into the unknown?

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Christian Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Redemption, Faith and forgiveness
