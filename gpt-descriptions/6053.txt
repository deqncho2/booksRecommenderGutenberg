Synopsis: 
Evelina, Or, the History of a Young Lady's Entrance into the World follows the journey of a young and innocent girl named Evelina as she navigates the treacherous waters of high society in 18th century England. Through a series of humorous and often embarrassing encounters, Evelina learns the complexities of love, friendship, and social etiquette. As she encounters various suitors, manipulative relatives, and scandalous secrets, Evelina must find her own voice and identity in a world that constantly tries to shape her. Will she succumb to the pressures of society or forge her own path?

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Epistolary Novel, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Love and Romance, Social Etiquette, Identity and Self-Discovery
