Synopsis: In the twelfth volume of his captivating memoirs, Jacques Casanova de Seingalt returns to the vibrant city of Paris, where he once again finds himself entangled in a web of scandal, intrigue, and forbidden desires. As he navigates the treacherous world of the French aristocracy, Casanova encounters a cast of captivating characters, from seductive courtesans to powerful nobles, each with their own secrets and agendas. Amidst the opulence and decadence of 18th-century Paris, Casanova must rely on his wit, charm, and cunning to survive and pursue his insatiable appetite for pleasure and adventure.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Erotic Literature

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Class and Status, Deception and Manipulation
