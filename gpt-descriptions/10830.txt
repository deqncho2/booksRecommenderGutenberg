Synopsis: In Hewet's enchanting retelling of the classic fairy tale, 'Cinderella', readers are transported to a world where magic, love, and destiny intertwine. Follow the journey of a young girl named Cinderella, who, despite her humble beginnings, possesses an extraordinary spirit and resilience. As she navigates the challenges of her life, Cinderella discovers that true beauty lies within, and that dreams can indeed come true. With a touch of whimsy and a dash of romance, this timeless tale will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fairy Tale, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Transformation, Inner Beauty, Love and Destiny
