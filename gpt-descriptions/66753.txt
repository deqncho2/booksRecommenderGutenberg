Synopsis: In 'Traitor's Choice' by Paul W. Fairman, a high-ranking government official finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and deception when he is unexpectedly accused of treason. As he races against time to clear his name, he must navigate a treacherous world of political conspiracies, secret alliances, and hidden agendas. With his reputation and life on the line, he must make difficult choices and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Political Fiction

Themes: Betrayal, Loyalty, Power Struggles, Redemption
