Synopsis: In 'The Gland Stealers' by Gayton, Bertram, a renowned surgeon finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he discovers a secret society that steals human glands for their own nefarious purposes. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of this secret world, he must navigate treacherous alliances, face deadly adversaries, and confront the ethical dilemmas that arise from the pursuit of scientific advancement. With pulse-pounding action and unexpected twists, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Medical Fiction

Themes: Ethics, Scientific Advancement, Conspiracy
