Synopsis: In 'The Man Who Hated Mars' by Randall Garrett, a renowned detective is sent to Mars to solve a mysterious murder case that seems impossible to crack. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, political intrigue, and a conspiracy that could change the fate of Mars forever. With his wit and determination, he must navigate through a hostile environment and outsmart those who will stop at nothing to keep the truth hidden.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Corruption, Power Struggles, Identity
