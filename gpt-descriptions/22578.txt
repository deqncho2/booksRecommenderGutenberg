Synopsis: In 'The Girl and the Kingdom: Learning to Teach' by Kate Douglas Smith Wiggin, a young woman named Mary finds herself thrust into a new role as a teacher in a small, isolated kingdom. As she navigates the challenges of teaching and connecting with her students, Mary discovers the power of education to transform lives and the unexpected ways in which she herself is transformed. With a cast of colorful characters and a captivating setting, this heartwarming tale explores the joys and struggles of teaching, the importance of empathy and understanding, and the transformative power of education.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Education, Transformation, Empathy, Personal growth
