Synopsis: In "Frontier Boys on the Coast; Or, In the Pirate's Power" by Roosevelt, Wyn, a group of adventurous boys find themselves embroiled in a thrilling and dangerous quest when they stumble upon a hidden treasure map. Determined to uncover the secrets of the pirate's power, they set sail on a perilous journey along the treacherous coast, facing unexpected challenges and encountering a cast of intriguing characters. As they navigate through the mysteries of the sea, the boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and teamwork to outwit the pirates and claim the treasure that lies at the heart of their adventure.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Exploration
