Synopsis:
'The Mad Pranks of Tom Tram, Son-in-law to Mother Winter: To Which Are Added His Merry Jests, Odd Conceits, and Pleasant Tales' is a delightful collection of humorous anecdotes and mischievous adventures. Tom Tram, a mischievous and quick-witted character, embarks on a series of hilarious pranks and jests that will leave readers laughing out loud. From his clever tricks to his witty banter, Tom Tram's escapades are sure to entertain and amuse. This collection also includes a variety of amusing tales and odd conceits that add to the overall enjoyment of the book. 

Genres:
Comedy, Satire, Fiction

Themes:
Humor, Mischief, Wit
