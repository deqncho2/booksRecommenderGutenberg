Synopsis: In 'How and When to Be Your Own Doctor', Solomon and Moser present a groundbreaking guide that empowers readers to take control of their own health and well-being. Through a combination of personal anecdotes, scientific research, and practical advice, the authors explore the untapped potential of self-healing and offer valuable insights into alternative medicine. From natural remedies to holistic approaches, this book provides a comprehensive roadmap for individuals seeking to optimize their health and make informed decisions about their own bodies. Prepare to embark on a transformative journey towards self-care and discover the incredible healing power that lies within you.

Genres: Self-help, Health, Alternative Medicine

Themes: Self-empowerment, Holistic healing, Personal responsibility, Natural remedies
