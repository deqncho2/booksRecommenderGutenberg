Synopsis: In 'A Mountain Woman' by Elia Wilkinson Peattie, a young woman named Mary finds herself torn between the allure of the city and the rugged beauty of the mountains. As she navigates the challenges of love, family, and societal expectations, Mary must make a choice that will shape her future and define her identity. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this captivating novel explores the complexities of self-discovery and the power of nature's influence.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Nature, Love and Relationships
