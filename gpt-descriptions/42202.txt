Synopsis: 

'Under the Red Crescent: Adventures of an English Surgeon with the Turkish Army at Plevna and Erzeroum 1877-1878' takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of an English surgeon who finds himself amidst the chaos and bravery of the Turkish Army during the Russo-Turkish War of 1877-1878. From the intense battles at Plevna to the harsh conditions in Erzeroum, the surgeon's experiences provide a unique perspective on the horrors of war, the resilience of the human spirit, and the bonds forged in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: War and its impact on individuals and societies, camaraderie and friendship in times of crisis, the resilience of the human spirit.
