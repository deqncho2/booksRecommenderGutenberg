Synopsis: In "The Last of the Foresters: Or, Humors on the Border; A story of the Old Virginia Frontier," John Esten Cooke takes readers on a captivating journey to the untamed Virginia frontier. Set during the tumultuous years leading up to the American Revolution, this historical novel follows the life of a young frontiersman named Ralph, who finds himself caught between loyalty to his family and his desire for adventure. As Ralph navigates the dangers of the wilderness and the complexities of love, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and rivalries that threaten to tear his world apart. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Cooke paints a vivid picture of a bygone era, immersing readers in the thrilling and treacherous world of the Old Virginia Frontier.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Adventure, Love, Family, Secrets
