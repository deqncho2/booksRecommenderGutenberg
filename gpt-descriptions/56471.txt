Synopsis: 
'Corporal Tikitanu, V.C.' follows the gripping journey of Corporal Tikitanu, a decorated war hero, as he navigates the treacherous landscapes of World War I. From the trenches of the Western Front to the battlefields of Gallipoli, Tikitanu's courage and resilience are tested to their limits. As the war rages on, he must confront not only the physical horrors of combat but also the moral dilemmas that arise in the face of war. With vivid descriptions and heart-wrenching moments, this captivating novel explores the sacrifices made by soldiers and the profound impact of war on the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Morality, Resilience
