Synopsis: In the enchanting region of Camargue, a young man named Martial finds himself torn between his love for a beautiful gypsy girl and his duty to protect his family's ancestral land. As he navigates the treacherous waters of love, honor, and tradition, Martial becomes entangled in a web of secrets and betrayals that threaten to tear his world apart. With the rugged landscape of Camargue as its backdrop, "King of Camargue" is a captivating tale of passion, loyalty, and the timeless struggle between duty and desire.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Tradition and Modernity, Family and Heritage
