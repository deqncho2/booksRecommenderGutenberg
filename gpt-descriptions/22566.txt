Synopsis: 
In "Dorothy and the Wizard in Oz," Dorothy finds herself once again transported to the magical land of Oz, but this time she is accompanied by her faithful dog Toto and her new friend Zeb. As they journey through the strange and captivating landscapes of Oz, they encounter peculiar creatures and face thrilling challenges. Together, they must rely on their wit and courage to navigate through the mysterious underground kingdom of the Mangaboos and find their way back home. Will Dorothy and her companions be able to overcome the obstacles and return safely to Kansas?

Genres: 
Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: 
Friendship, Courage, Exploration
