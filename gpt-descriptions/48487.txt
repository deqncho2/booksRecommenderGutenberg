Synopsis: 
In 'Young Earnest: The Romance of a Bad Start in Life' by Cannan, Gilbert, we are introduced to the captivating story of Earnest, a young man who has faced numerous challenges and setbacks in his life. From a troubled childhood to a series of unfortunate events, Earnest's journey is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As he navigates through love, loss, and self-discovery, Earnest's resilience and determination shine through, making this a truly unforgettable tale.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Resilience, Overcoming adversity, Self-discovery
