Synopsis: In 'The Four Corners in California' by Blanchard, Amy Ella, a group of four friends embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets hidden within the mysterious Four Corners region of California. As they delve deeper into the unknown, they encounter ancient legends, unexplained phenomena, and a race against time to protect the ancient treasures that lie within. With danger lurking at every turn, the friends must rely on their wit, courage, and unwavering friendship to unravel the enigma that has captivated them. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Ancient Legends
