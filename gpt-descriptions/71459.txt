Synopsis: In 'The G-man's son at Porpoise Island' by Warren F. Robinson, a young boy named Tim embarks on a thrilling adventure when he discovers a hidden treasure map. With the help of his friends, Tim sets off to uncover the secrets of Porpoise Island, encountering danger, mystery, and unexpected allies along the way. As they navigate through treacherous waters and face formidable challenges, Tim learns valuable lessons about courage, friendship, and the power of perseverance. Will they be able to unravel the mysteries of the island and find the long-lost treasure?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Perseverance
