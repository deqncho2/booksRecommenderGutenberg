Synopsis: In 'Hugh Gwyeth: A Roundhead Cavalier' by Dix, Beulah Marie, follow the captivating journey of Hugh Gwyeth, a young man torn between his loyalty to the Parliamentarians and his love for a Royalist woman. Set against the backdrop of the English Civil War, this historical novel delves into the complexities of war, love, and the struggle to find one's true identity. As Hugh navigates the treacherous political landscape, he must confront his own beliefs and make choices that will shape his destiny. Will he remain true to his principles or succumb to the allure of forbidden love?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Love and Conflict
