Synopsis: In 'The Unwilling Vestal' by Edward Lucas White, a young woman named Helen finds herself thrust into a world of ancient rituals and dark secrets when she is chosen as the next Vestal Virgin. As she navigates the treacherous politics of Rome and uncovers the truth behind her selection, Helen must decide whether to embrace her destiny or defy the gods. With its blend of historical fiction, mystery, and supernatural elements, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Fate and Destiny, Power and Corruption
