Synopsis: 
In 'The Border Boys Across the Frontier' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of adventurous young boys embark on a thrilling journey that takes them across the untamed American frontier. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes, encounter dangerous outlaws, and face unexpected challenges, their courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. With each step they take, the boys uncover secrets that will change their lives forever, leading them to question their own identities and the true meaning of friendship.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Identity, Exploration
