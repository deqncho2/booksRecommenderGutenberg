Synopsis:
'The Star' by H. G. Wells takes readers on a thrilling journey through the cosmos as a mysterious celestial object hurtles towards Earth, causing widespread panic and upheaval. As scientists and astronomers scramble to understand the nature of this impending disaster, the fate of humanity hangs in the balance. With vivid descriptions and a sense of impending doom, Wells masterfully explores the human response to an existential threat from the vastness of space.

Genres:
Science Fiction, Apocalyptic Fiction, Adventure

Themes:
Existentialism, Human Nature, Catastrophe, Scientific Inquiry
