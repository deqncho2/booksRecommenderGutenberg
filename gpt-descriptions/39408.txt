Synopsis: In "The Grateful Dead: The History of a Folk Story," Gordon Hall Gerould delves into the fascinating origins and evolution of the timeless folk tale of the Grateful Dead. From its ancient roots in various cultures to its modern interpretations, Gerould uncovers the captivating journey of this enduring narrative. Exploring the diverse versions and adaptations of the story, the author reveals the profound impact it has had on literature, music, and popular culture throughout history. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Gerould invites readers to unravel the mystery and significance behind the Grateful Dead, making this book a must-read for folklore enthusiasts and curious minds alike.

Genres: Folklore, Literary Criticism, Cultural Studies

Themes: Mythology, Oral Tradition, Cultural Transmission
