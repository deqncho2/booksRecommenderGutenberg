Synopsis: 
In 'A Matter of Taste' by Stecher, L. J., Jr., a renowned chef finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is invited to a secluded island to create a once-in-a-lifetime dining experience for a secretive group of elite individuals. As he delves deeper into the world of haute cuisine, he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his reputation but also his life. With his culinary skills and wit put to the ultimate test, he must navigate through a treacherous game of cat and mouse, where the stakes are higher than he could have ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Culinary arts, Secrets and deception, Survival
