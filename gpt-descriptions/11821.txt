Synopsis: 

Step into the world of creativity and innovation as the Library of Congress takes you on a captivating journey through the U.S. Copyright Renewals of 1960, January to June. This meticulously curated collection unveils a treasure trove of artistic endeavors, from literature to music, painting to photography, and everything in between. Discover the hidden stories behind the works that shaped the cultural landscape of the time, as the Copyright Office unveils a captivating tapestry of imagination and originality. Delve into the minds of the creators, feel their passion, and witness the birth of timeless masterpieces.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Art

Themes: Creativity, Intellectual Property, Cultural Heritage
