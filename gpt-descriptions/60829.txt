Synopsis: In 'The Upside-Down Captain' by Jim Harmon, a seasoned captain finds himself in a bizarre predicament when he wakes up one morning to discover that gravity has reversed itself. As he navigates this topsy-turvy world, he must unravel the mystery behind this strange phenomenon and find a way to restore order. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the very laws of physics.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Perception of reality, Adaptation to change, Problem-solving
