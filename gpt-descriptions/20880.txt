Synopsis: In 'The Story of Russia', Van Bergen takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and tumultuous history of Russia, from its early beginnings to the modern era. From the rise of the powerful tsars to the struggles of the Russian Revolution, this book delves into the fascinating stories of the people, events, and cultural developments that shaped this vast and enigmatic nation. With vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, Van Bergen paints a vivid picture of Russia's complex past, offering a compelling narrative that will leave readers eager to uncover more about this captivating country.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and politics, Revolution and change, Cultural identity
