Synopsis: In Tower of Ivory, Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton weaves a captivating tale of love, ambition, and betrayal set against the backdrop of San Francisco's high society in the early 20th century. The story follows the lives of two women, one born into privilege and the other a self-made social climber, as they navigate the treacherous world of power and wealth. As their paths intertwine, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human desires is exposed. Tower of Ivory is a gripping novel that explores the complexities of human relationships and the lengths people will go to fulfill their ambitions.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Social class, Ambition, Betrayal, Identity
