Synopsis: 
In 'Starlight Ranch, and Other Stories of Army Life on the Frontier', Charles King takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged and untamed American frontier. From the dusty plains to the treacherous canyons, this collection of stories delves into the lives of soldiers stationed at Starlight Ranch, a remote outpost on the edge of civilization. Through tales of bravery, love, and sacrifice, King paints a vivid picture of the challenges and triumphs faced by these soldiers as they navigate the harsh realities of army life in the Wild West.

Genres: Western, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Duty and Honor, Survival in the Wilderness, Camaraderie and Brotherhood
