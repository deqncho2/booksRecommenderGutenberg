Synopsis: 
In 'Wild Flowers Worth Knowing', Blanchan, Neltje takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of wildflowers. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, she introduces us to a diverse array of flowers, from delicate woodland blooms to vibrant meadow blossoms. As we delve into the pages, we uncover fascinating facts about each flower's unique characteristics, medicinal properties, and folklore. From the elusive Lady's Slipper to the cheerful Sunflower, this book is a treasure trove of knowledge that will ignite a newfound appreciation for the beauty and wonder of nature's floral creations.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Reference

Themes: Botany, Natural History, Environmental Conservation
