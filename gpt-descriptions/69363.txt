Synopsis: In 'Mutiny of the Bounty and story of Pitcairn Island, 1790-1894', Rosalind Amelia Young delves into the captivating tale of the infamous mutiny on the HMS Bounty and the subsequent settlement on Pitcairn Island. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Young uncovers the untold secrets, conflicts, and triumphs of the mutineers and their descendants, painting a vivid picture of life on the remote island. From the dramatic events that led to the mutiny to the challenges faced by the settlers, this book offers a fascinating exploration of a little-known chapter in maritime history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Mutiny, Survival, Isolation, Cultural assimilation
