Synopsis: In 'The Sea Bride' by Ben Ames Williams, a young man named David falls in love with a mysterious woman who emerges from the sea. As their relationship deepens, David becomes entangled in a web of secrets and supernatural forces that threaten to tear them apart. With elements of romance, mystery, and the supernatural, this captivating tale explores the power of love and the lengths one will go to protect it.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Love, Secrets, Fate
