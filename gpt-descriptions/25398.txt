Synopsis: In 'The Little French Lawyer: A Comedy', a cunning and charismatic French lawyer arrives in London, ready to use his wit and charm to manipulate the English legal system. As he takes on a high-profile case, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, mistaken identities, and romantic entanglements. With its clever wordplay, hilarious misunderstandings, and unexpected plot twists, this comedy will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Deception, Identity, Love and Romance
