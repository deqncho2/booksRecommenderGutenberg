Synopsis: In 'A District Messenger Boy, and A Necktie Party' by Otis, James, a young messenger boy finds himself caught up in a mysterious adventure when he stumbles upon a secret society planning a dangerous event. As he becomes entangled in their plans, he must navigate a web of intrigue and deception, all while trying to protect himself and those he cares about. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden societies, Loyalty and friendship, Coming of age
