Synopsis: In the small town of Klamugra, a mysterious machine suddenly appears, captivating the curiosity of the townspeople. As they delve deeper into its secrets, they uncover a world of wonder and danger that will challenge their beliefs and change their lives forever. With unexpected twists and a cast of intriguing characters, 'Machine of Klamugra' takes readers on a thrilling journey of discovery and self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Curiosity, Exploration, Identity, Transformation
