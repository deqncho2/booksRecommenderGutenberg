Synopsis: In 'The Library of Work and Play: Electricity and Its Everyday Uses', John F. Woodhull takes readers on an illuminating journey into the world of electricity and its practical applications. From the wonders of electric lighting to the marvels of telegraphy and telephony, this captivating book explores the transformative power of electricity in everyday life. Through engaging anecdotes and informative explanations, Woodhull unveils the mysteries behind this revolutionary force, making it accessible to readers of all ages. Discover the fascinating history, science, and practical uses of electricity in this enlightening and thought-provoking read.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Technological advancements, Practical applications, Scientific exploration
