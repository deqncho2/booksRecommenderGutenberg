Synopsis: In "And they thought we wouldn't fight," Gibbons, Floyd Phillips takes readers on a gripping journey through the lives of a group of individuals who defy expectations and prove their resilience in the face of adversity. Set against the backdrop of a tumultuous era, this captivating story explores the unyielding spirit of human determination and the power of unity. As the characters navigate through personal challenges and societal pressures, they discover the true meaning of courage and the lengths they are willing to go to fight for what they believe in.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Drama

Themes: Resilience, Unity, Courage, Societal expectations
