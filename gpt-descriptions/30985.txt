Synopsis: In 'The Mark of the Knife' by Clayton H. Ernst, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a series of gruesome murders occur in a small, seemingly peaceful town. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives that challenge his own beliefs and force him to confront his darkest fears. With each new revelation, the detective realizes that the true mark of the knife goes far beyond the physical wounds, leaving an indelible scar on the souls of both the victims and the perpetrator.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption, Psychological exploration
