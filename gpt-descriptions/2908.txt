Synopsis: In the midst of a bitter labor dispute, the lives of workers and management become entangled in a web of power, ambition, and moral dilemmas. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the play delves into the complex dynamics of class struggle and the pursuit of justice, ultimately leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Drama, Social Realism

Themes: Class struggle, Power dynamics, Morality, Justice
