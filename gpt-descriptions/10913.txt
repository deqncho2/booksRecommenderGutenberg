Synopsis: 
'English Poems' by Le Gallienne, Richard is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through the beauty and complexity of the English language. From heartfelt expressions of love and longing to profound reflections on life's mysteries, this anthology explores a wide range of emotions and experiences. With each poem, Le Gallienne's lyrical and evocative language transports readers to different landscapes and moments in time, leaving them with a renewed appreciation for the power of words and the human spirit.

Genres: Poetry, Literature

Themes: Love, Nature, Life's Mysteries
