Synopsis: In "The Official Chaperon" by Natalie Sumner Lincoln, a young woman finds herself thrust into the glamorous world of high society as she takes on the role of a chaperon. As she navigates the complexities of social etiquette and the intrigues of the elite, she discovers that her own heart may be at stake. With secrets, scandals, and unexpected romance, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, personal growth
