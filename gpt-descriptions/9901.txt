Synopsis: Grace Harlowe's Return to Overton Campus follows the captivating journey of Grace Harlowe as she returns to her beloved college campus after a year-long absence. As she reunites with her friends and navigates the challenges of academic life, Grace finds herself caught up in a series of mysterious events that threaten to disrupt the peace and harmony of Overton. With her determination and resourcefulness, Grace must unravel the secrets and protect her friends from danger, all while facing personal trials that test her resilience and character. This thrilling and heartwarming tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the campus mysteries.

Genres: Mystery, Young Adult, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Identity, Coming of Age
