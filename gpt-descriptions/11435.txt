Synopsis: In 'Small Means and Great Ends', a seemingly ordinary individual embarks on a journey that will challenge their perception of reality and lead them to uncover hidden truths about themselves and the world around them. As they navigate through unexpected twists and turns, they must confront their deepest fears and make choices that will have far-reaching consequences. This gripping tale explores the power of resilience, the complexities of human nature, and the profound impact that small actions can have on shaping our destinies.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Consequences, Resilience
