Synopsis: In the small town of Fieu Dayol, a group of young girls embarks on a mysterious adventure that will change their lives forever. As they uncover hidden secrets and encounter supernatural forces, their bond is tested, and they must rely on their courage and friendship to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. With each twist and turn, the girls discover the true power within themselves and the importance of embracing their unique abilities.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Empowerment
