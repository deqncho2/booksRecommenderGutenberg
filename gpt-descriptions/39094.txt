Synopsis: In 'Two Boy Gold Miners; Or, Lost in the Mountains' by Frank V. Webster, two adventurous boys embark on a thrilling journey to strike it rich in the gold mines. However, their dreams take an unexpected turn when they find themselves lost in the treacherous mountains. As they face numerous challenges and encounter mysterious characters, the boys must rely on their wits and determination to survive and find their way back home. Will they uncover the secrets hidden within the mountains, or will they be forever lost in the wilderness?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Exploration
