Synopsis: In 'From Whose Bourne' by Robert Barr, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges from the shadows, seeking revenge against those who have wronged him. As he embarks on a relentless pursuit, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of society, filled with unexpected twists and turns. With each step closer to his targets, the protagonist's true identity and motivations become increasingly intriguing, leaving the reader captivated until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Revenge, Identity, Justice
