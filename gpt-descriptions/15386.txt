From mysterious disappearances to daring adventures, this weekly magazine for boys and girls is filled with fascinating stories that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. Delve into the pages of this volume to uncover the secrets of the world and discover the incredible tales that await.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical
