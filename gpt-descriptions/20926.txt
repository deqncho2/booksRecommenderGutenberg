Synopsis: In the small village of Ratlinghope, a mysterious register is discovered, containing the names of every resident, past and present. As the villagers delve into its secrets, they uncover a web of hidden connections, long-buried secrets, and a dark history that threatens to unravel their lives. With each revelation, the line between reality and myth blurs, and the residents of Ratlinghope must confront their own demons and face the consequences of their actions. As the register's power grows, the village becomes a battleground for the forces of good and evil, where the past and present collide in a thrilling and suspenseful tale.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, the power of history and memory, the battle between good and evil, the impact of choices and actions
