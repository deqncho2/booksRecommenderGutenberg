Synopsis: In 'The Gospel of the Hereafter' by J. Paterson Smyth, a renowned theologian embarks on a journey to uncover the mysteries of the afterlife. As he delves into ancient texts and encounters enigmatic figures, he begins to question his own beliefs and grapples with the profound implications of what he discovers. This thought-provoking tale takes readers on a captivating exploration of faith, spirituality, and the eternal nature of the human soul.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Spiritual

Themes: Afterlife, Faith, Existentialism, Redemption
