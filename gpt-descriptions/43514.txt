Synopsis: In 'Chinese Diamonds for the King of Kings', Goforth, Rosalind takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious world of ancient China. The story follows a young adventurer who embarks on a perilous quest to find the legendary Chinese diamonds, rumored to possess unimaginable power. As the protagonist delves deeper into the secrets of the past, they encounter treacherous obstacles, unexpected allies, and a web of deceit that threatens to unravel everything they hold dear. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leading to a breathtaking climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Power and greed, Loyalty and betrayal, Fate and destiny
