Synopsis: In "Oscar Wilde, Art and Morality: A Defence of 'The Picture of Dorian Gray'," delve into the fascinating world of Oscar Wilde as he passionately defends his controversial novel, "The Picture of Dorian Gray." Through a thought-provoking exploration of art, beauty, and morality, Wilde challenges societal norms and offers a compelling defense of his masterpiece. Uncover the secrets behind the creation of this iconic novel and gain a deeper understanding of Wilde's artistic vision.

Genres: Literary criticism, biography, cultural studies

Themes: Art and aesthetics, morality and ethics, societal conventions
