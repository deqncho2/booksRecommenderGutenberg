Synopsis: In 'Captain Mary Miller', Harriet Jane Hanson Robinson takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a remarkable woman. Mary Miller defies societal expectations and becomes a captain, navigating the challenges of a male-dominated world. As she faces personal and professional obstacles, Mary's determination and resilience shine through, making her an inspiring figure. This drama explores themes of gender equality, perseverance, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Gender equality, Perseverance, Pursuit of dreams
