Synopsis: In the heart of Liverpool, a city known for its rich history and vibrant culture, a group of individuals find their lives intertwined in unexpected ways. From the bustling streets of the city center to the hidden corners of its iconic landmarks, secrets are unveiled, relationships are tested, and the true essence of Liverpool is revealed. As the characters navigate through love, loss, and the pursuit of their dreams, they discover that the city they call home has a profound impact on their identities and the paths they choose to follow.

Genres: Contemporary Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Identity, Community, Love and Relationships, Pursuit of Dreams
