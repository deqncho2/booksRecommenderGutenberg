Synopsis: In 'The Store Boy' by Horatio Alger, Jr., a young boy named Frank is determined to escape his life of poverty and make a better future for himself. When an opportunity arises for him to work at a prestigious store, Frank seizes the chance, but soon finds himself facing numerous challenges and obstacles. As he navigates the world of retail, Frank must learn valuable lessons about hard work, perseverance, and the true meaning of success.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Poverty, Ambition, Determination, Social mobility
