Synopsis: 
'Lovers' Vows' follows the story of a young woman named Amelia who finds herself torn between her duty to her family and her passionate love for a man of lower social status. As secrets are revealed and societal expectations clash with personal desires, Amelia must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal norms in order to find her own happiness.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Love and duty, social class, societal expectations, personal freedom
