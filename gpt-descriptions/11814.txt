Synopsis: 

Step into the world of U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1956 July - December, a captivating compilation by the Library of Congress. This intriguing collection unveils a treasure trove of forgotten literary gems, offering a glimpse into the creative landscape of mid-20th century America. From beloved classics to hidden masterpieces, this volume showcases the diverse range of works that sought to secure their place in history. Delve into the pages of this remarkable archive and discover the stories that once captivated the imaginations of readers across the nation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Literature

Themes: Intellectual property, Cultural heritage, Literary history
