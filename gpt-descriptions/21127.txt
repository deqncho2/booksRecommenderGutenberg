Synopsis: In 'The Mystics: A Novel' by Katherine Cecil Thurston, a group of enigmatic individuals with extraordinary abilities find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, power, and forbidden love. As they navigate a world filled with mysticism and danger, their paths converge, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Supernatural

Themes: Mysticism, Power, Forbidden Love
