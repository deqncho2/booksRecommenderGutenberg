Synopsis: In 'The Friendly Killers' by Tenneshaw, S. M., a group of seemingly ordinary individuals find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and deception when they are recruited as assassins for a mysterious organization. As they navigate the dangerous world of contract killing, they must confront their own moral compasses and question the true nature of friendship and loyalty. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Loyalty, Deception
