Synopsis: In "Nobody saw the ship" by Murray Leinster, a mysterious and unexplained event takes place when a massive spaceship suddenly appears in the sky, but nobody seems to have witnessed its arrival. As scientists and government officials scramble to uncover the truth behind this perplexing phenomenon, they soon realize that the ship's presence holds far-reaching implications for humanity. With suspense and intrigue, the story delves into the race against time to unravel the secrets of the ship and its enigmatic purpose.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Extraterrestrial life, Government secrecy, Human curiosity, Technological advancements
