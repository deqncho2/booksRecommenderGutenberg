Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of the Fifteen Sounds' by Powell, Van, a renowned linguist is drawn into a perplexing case when a series of mysterious sounds begin to haunt a small town. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, ancient languages, and hidden meanings that could change the course of history. With time running out, he must decipher the enigmatic sounds before they unleash a power beyond imagination.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Language and communication, Secrets and hidden knowledge
