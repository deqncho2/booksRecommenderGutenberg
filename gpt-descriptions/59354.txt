Synopsis: In 'Gloria: A Girl and Her Dad' by Lilian Garis, readers are introduced to the spirited and imaginative Gloria, a young girl who shares a special bond with her father. As they embark on a series of heartwarming adventures together, Gloria's curiosity and determination lead her to unexpected discoveries and valuable life lessons. From exploring hidden treasures to unraveling mysteries, this delightful tale captures the essence of a father-daughter relationship filled with love, laughter, and unforgettable moments.

Genres: Children's Literature, Family, Adventure

Themes: Father-Daughter Bond, Imagination, Curiosity, Life Lessons
