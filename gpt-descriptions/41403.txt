Synopsis: In 'At the Relton Arms' by Evelyn Sharp, a seemingly ordinary gathering at a country inn takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a secret that threatens to unravel the lives of the inn's inhabitants. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the characters find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and deception, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Intrigue
