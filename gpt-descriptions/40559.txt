Synopsis: In 'The Güegüence; A Comedy Ballet in the Nahuatl-Spanish Dialect of Nicaragua', we are transported to the vibrant world of colonial Nicaragua, where a colorful cast of characters come together to tell a tale of love, rebellion, and cultural identity. Through lively dances, witty dialogues, and captivating music, the story unfolds, revealing the struggles and triumphs of the people as they navigate the complexities of their society. As the plot thickens, unexpected alliances are formed, secrets are unveiled, and the power of unity is put to the test. This comedic ballet is a celebration of the rich cultural heritage of Nicaragua, blending indigenous traditions with Spanish influences, and leaving the audience enchanted and inspired.

Genres: Comedy, Ballet, Cultural

Themes: Cultural identity, Love and relationships, Rebellion, Unity
