Synopsis: In 'The House That Grew' by Molesworth, Mrs., a young girl named Griselda discovers a mysterious house that seems to have a life of its own. As she explores its ever-expanding rooms and secret passages, Griselda uncovers hidden treasures and encounters peculiar characters. With each new discovery, the house reveals its magical and enchanting nature, leading Griselda on an extraordinary adventure that will change her life forever.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Discovery, Transformation
