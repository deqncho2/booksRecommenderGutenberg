Synopsis: In 'Dick Hamilton's Cadet Days; Or, The Handicap of a Millionaire's Son', readers are introduced to Dick Hamilton, a young boy who seems to have it all - wealth, privilege, and a bright future. However, when his father decides to send him to a military academy, Dick's life takes an unexpected turn. Struggling to fit in among his fellow cadets, Dick must navigate the challenges of discipline, friendship, and self-discovery. As he faces the trials and tribulations of cadet life, Dick learns valuable lessons about humility, perseverance, and the true meaning of success.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: Identity, Social class, Friendship, Self-discovery
