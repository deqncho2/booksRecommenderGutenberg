Synopsis: 

In the sixth volume of "The Chronicles of Enguerrand de Monstrelet," readers are transported to a tumultuous period in French history. The narrative revolves around the brutal civil wars between the houses of Orleans and Burgundy, as well as the English occupation of Paris and Normandy. Enguerrand de Monstrelet vividly recounts the power struggles, political intrigues, and military campaigns that shaped the kingdom of France during this time. From the expulsion of the English to other significant events occurring in various countries, this volume offers a captivating account of historical events that will leave readers eager to delve deeper into the chronicles.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Chronicle

Themes: Power struggles, Political intrigue, Military campaigns, National identity, Historical events
