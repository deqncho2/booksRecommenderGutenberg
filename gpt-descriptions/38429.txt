Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's Alarm; Or, Doing His Best' by Standish, Burt L., Frank Merriwell, the beloved hero, finds himself facing a series of unexpected challenges that put his reputation and friendships at stake. As he navigates through a web of deceit, danger, and personal struggles, Frank must summon all his courage and determination to overcome the obstacles that threaten to derail his dreams. With his unwavering spirit and unwavering loyalty, Frank Merriwell proves that sometimes, doing your best means going above and beyond what is expected.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Integrity
