Synopsis: In "Ruth Fielding Down East; Or, The Hermit of Beach Plum Point," Ruth Fielding finds herself embarking on a thrilling adventure when she visits her friend Helen in a quaint coastal town. As Ruth explores the picturesque landscapes and encounters mysterious characters, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden treasures. With her determination and resourcefulness, Ruth sets out to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic hermit of Beach Plum Point, leading her on a journey that will test her courage and reveal unexpected surprises.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Self-discovery
