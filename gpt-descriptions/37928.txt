Synopsis: 

'Gas Burners Old and New: A historical and descriptive treatise on the progress of invention in gas lighting, embracing an account of the theory of luminous combustion' takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of gas lighting. From ancient times to the modern era, author Owen Merriman delves into the fascinating world of gas burners, exploring their development, theories, and practical applications. This comprehensive work offers a unique blend of historical anecdotes, scientific explanations, and descriptive accounts, providing readers with a deeper understanding of the illuminating power of gas.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Technological progress, Innovation, Historical development of lighting
