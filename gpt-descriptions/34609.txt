Synopsis: In 'King of the Castle' by George Manville Fenn, a young boy named Dick is unexpectedly thrust into a world of mystery and danger when he discovers a hidden treasure map. With the help of his loyal dog, he embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with secret passages, hidden clues, and unexpected allies. As Dick delves deeper into the mystery, he must outsmart a cunning villain who will stop at nothing to claim the treasure for himself. Will Dick be able to unravel the secrets of the map and become the true king of the castle?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Treasure hunting, Friendship, Bravery
