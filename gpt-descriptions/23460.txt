Synopsis: 
Abroad is a thrilling and suspenseful novel that follows the journey of a young woman named Sophie who embarks on a study abroad program in a picturesque European city. However, her dream quickly turns into a nightmare when she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and deception. As Sophie navigates through the unfamiliar territory, she must uncover the truth behind the mysterious occurrences and protect herself from the sinister forces that lurk in the shadows. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the shocking truth that lies beneath the surface.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Danger, Identity
