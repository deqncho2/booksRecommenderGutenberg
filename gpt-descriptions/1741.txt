Synopsis:
In "The White Moll" by Frank L. Packard, we are introduced to a mysterious and captivating character known as the White Moll. She is a master of disguise, a femme fatale who navigates the dangerous underworld of crime in early 20th century New York City. As she becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and murder, the White Moll must use all her cunning and resourcefulness to survive and outsmart those who seek to bring her down. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the White Moll's enigmatic persona.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Survival
