Synopsis: 
In "Itinerarium Curiosum (Centuria I): or, An account of the antiquities, and remarkable curiosities in nature or art, observed in travels through Great Britain," William Stukeley takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden wonders of Great Britain. From ancient ruins to natural marvels, Stukeley's meticulous observations and vivid descriptions transport readers to a world of awe-inspiring curiosities. As he explores the country's rich history and diverse landscapes, Stukeley uncovers fascinating secrets and uncanny connections between the past and the present. With each page, readers are left eager to discover more about the extraordinary wonders that lie hidden within their own surroundings.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction, Natural History

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, Connection between past and present
