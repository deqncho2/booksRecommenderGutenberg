Synopsis: In 'The Strong Arm' by Robert Barr, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a series of seemingly unrelated crimes occur across the city. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden motives that lead him down a dangerous path. With time running out, the detective must rely on his sharp instincts and unwavering determination to solve the puzzle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Determination
