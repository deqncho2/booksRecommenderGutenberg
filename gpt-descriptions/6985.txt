Synopsis: 
In 'A Prefect's Uncle', young Pongo Twistleton finds himself in a predicament when his mischievous and unpredictable uncle, Fred, arrives at his boarding school. Uncle Fred's eccentricity and penchant for causing chaos quickly turn Pongo's life upside down, leading to a series of hilarious and unexpected events. As Pongo tries to navigate the chaos caused by his uncle, he learns valuable lessons about friendship, loyalty, and the importance of embracing one's true self.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family dynamics, Identity, Friendship, Acceptance
