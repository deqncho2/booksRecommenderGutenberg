Synopsis: In "History of the Sabbath and first day of the week," John Nevins Andrews delves into the fascinating origins and evolution of the Sabbath, exploring its significance across different cultures and religions. From ancient civilizations to modern times, Andrews uncovers surprising historical facts and uncanny connections that shed light on the true meaning of this sacred day. As he unravels the mysteries surrounding the Sabbath, readers will be captivated by the rich tapestry of traditions, beliefs, and controversies that have shaped its existence. Prepare to embark on a thought-provoking journey through time, where the past and present converge to reveal the profound impact of the Sabbath on humanity.

Genres: History, Religion, Cultural Studies

Themes: Sabbath observance, Religious traditions, Historical significance
