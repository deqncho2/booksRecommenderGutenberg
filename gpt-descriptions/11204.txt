Synopsis: 

In 'Diseases of the Horse's Foot', H. Caulton Reeks takes readers on a captivating journey into the intricate world of equine health. With a meticulous blend of scientific knowledge and real-life anecdotes, Reeks delves into the mysterious ailments that afflict horses' feet, unraveling the complexities of diagnosis, treatment, and prevention. From the subtlest lameness to the most severe hoof conditions, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the challenges faced by both horse owners and veterinarians. As readers delve into the pages, they will be captivated by the author's passion for these majestic creatures and his unwavering dedication to their well-being.

Genres: Non-fiction, Veterinary Medicine, Animal Health

Themes: Equine health, Veterinary care, Animal welfare
