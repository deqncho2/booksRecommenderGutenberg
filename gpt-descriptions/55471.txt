Synopsis: In the remote village of Ravenswood, a mysterious inn with a dark past becomes the center of a series of inexplicable events. As the villagers uncover the secrets hidden within the walls of The Black Lion Inn, they find themselves entangled in a web of suspense, betrayal, and supernatural occurrences. With each passing night, the line between reality and nightmare blurs, leaving the characters questioning their own sanity and the true nature of the inn.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, the thin line between reality and illusion, the power of fear and the unknown
