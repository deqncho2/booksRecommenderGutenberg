Synopsis: In "A Beacon for the Blind: Being a Life of Henry Fawcett, the Blind Postmaster-General," author Winifred Holt delves into the extraordinary life of Henry Fawcett, a man who defied all odds and became a prominent figure in British politics despite his blindness. From his early struggles to his remarkable achievements, this captivating biography explores Fawcett's unwavering determination, his unwavering commitment to social justice, and the lasting impact he made on society. As readers follow Fawcett's journey, they will be inspired by his resilience and touched by the profound influence he had on the lives of others.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Overcoming adversity, Social justice, Perseverance
