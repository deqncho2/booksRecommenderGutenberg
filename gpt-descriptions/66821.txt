Synopsis: In 'The Dead-Line' by W. C. Tuttle, a small town is gripped by fear when a mysterious deadline is set, threatening the lives of its residents. As the clock ticks down, a determined journalist races against time to uncover the truth behind the deadly game and save the innocent. With suspenseful twists and unexpected turns, this thrilling mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Fear, Time, Journalism
