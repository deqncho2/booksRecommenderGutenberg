Synopsis: In 'Angels in Art', Clara Erskine Clement Waters takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of art, exploring the depiction of angels throughout history. From the ethereal and serene to the powerful and awe-inspiring, Waters delves into the symbolism, mythology, and religious significance behind these celestial beings. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, she unveils the various artistic interpretations of angels, revealing the profound impact they have had on human imagination and spirituality.

Genres: Art History, Religious Studies, Cultural Studies

Themes: Symbolism, Spirituality, Mythology
