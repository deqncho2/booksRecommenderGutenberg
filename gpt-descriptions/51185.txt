Synopsis: In 'All Jackson's Children' by Daniel F. Galouye, a post-apocalyptic world is inhabited by a small group of survivors who have adapted to a life without sight. Among them is Jackson, a blind man who possesses a unique ability to see through the eyes of others. When a mysterious stranger arrives, claiming to be from a place where everyone can see, Jackson's world is turned upside down. As he embarks on a dangerous journey to uncover the truth, he must confront his own beliefs and the secrets that lie within the community he thought he knew.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Mystery

Themes: Perception, Identity, Survival
