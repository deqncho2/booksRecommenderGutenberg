Synopsis: In 'Charles I: Makers of History' by Abbott, Jacob, embark on a captivating journey through the tumultuous reign of one of England's most controversial kings. From political intrigue to religious conflicts, this gripping historical account unveils the complex character of Charles I and the profound impact he had on the nation. As the stakes rise and tensions escalate, readers will be enthralled by the power struggles, personal sacrifices, and ultimate fate of this enigmatic monarch.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Political Drama

Themes: Power and Authority, Religion and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal
