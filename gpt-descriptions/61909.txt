Synopsis: 

In "History of the Ordinance of 1787 and the Old Northwest Territory: A Supplemental Text for School Use," readers are taken on a captivating journey through the fascinating events surrounding the creation of the Ordinance of 1787 and the establishment of the Old Northwest Territory. This meticulously researched and engagingly written book delves into the political, social, and economic factors that shaped this pivotal moment in American history. From the struggles of early settlers to the debates among influential figures, the narrative unfolds with gripping detail, shedding light on the challenges and triumphs that paved the way for the expansion of the United States.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Educational, Biography

Themes: American history, Westward expansion, Governance and legislation
