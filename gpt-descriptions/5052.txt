Synopsis: In 'Absalom's Hair' by Bjørnstjerne Bjørnson, a young man named Absalom embarks on a journey of self-discovery and rebellion against societal norms. As he grows out his long, flowing hair, Absalom becomes a symbol of defiance and freedom, challenging the expectations placed upon him by his family and community. Set against the backdrop of a small Norwegian village, this captivating tale explores the power of individuality and the consequences of breaking away from tradition.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Individuality, Rebellion, Tradition, Identity
