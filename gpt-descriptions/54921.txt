Synopsis: In 'The Romance of War; or, The Highlanders in France and Belgium', the thrilling sequel to 'The Highlanders in Spain', author James Grant takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the battlefields of France and Belgium during the Napoleonic Wars. As the Highlanders face new challenges and forge unbreakable bonds, they must navigate the treacherous landscapes of war, love, and honor. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Grant paints a captivating portrait of the soldiers' bravery and resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Love, Honor
