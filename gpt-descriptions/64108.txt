Synopsis: In 'Christmas in Spain; or, Mariquita's Day of Rejoicing' by Sarah Gertrude Pomeroy, readers are transported to the vibrant streets of Spain during the holiday season. Mariquita, a young girl full of curiosity and wonder, embarks on a magical adventure that will forever change her perspective on Christmas. As she immerses herself in the rich traditions and customs of Spain, Mariquita discovers the true meaning of joy, love, and the power of community. This heartwarming tale captures the essence of Christmas in a unique and enchanting way, leaving readers captivated until the very last page.

Genres: Children's Literature, Holiday Fiction, Cultural Fiction
Themes: Christmas Spirit, Cultural Identity, Family and Community
