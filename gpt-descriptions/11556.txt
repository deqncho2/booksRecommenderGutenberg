Synopsis: In "Facing the Flag" by Jules Verne, a brilliant scientist mysteriously disappears, leaving behind a cryptic message and a dangerous invention. As a group of adventurers embarks on a thrilling quest to uncover the truth, they find themselves entangled in a web of international intrigue, high-stakes espionage, and a race against time. With the fate of nations hanging in the balance, they must confront the ethical dilemmas posed by scientific advancements and grapple with the consequences of their actions. Will they be able to unravel the mystery and prevent a catastrophic disaster?

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Scientific ethics, Patriotism, Consequences of technological advancements
