Synopsis: In the enchanting Scottish Highlands, a young woman named Fiona discovers a hidden world of magic and mystery when she stumbles upon an ancient book of spells. As she delves deeper into her newfound powers, Fiona must navigate a treacherous path filled with dark secrets, dangerous alliances, and a centuries-old curse that threatens to consume her. With the help of a wise old witch and a charming but enigmatic stranger, Fiona must embrace her destiny as the Witch of the Glens and uncover the truth that lies within her own heart.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Magic, Self-discovery, Fate, Love and Sacrifice
