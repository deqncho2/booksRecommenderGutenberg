Synopsis: In 'The Love That Prevailed', a young woman finds herself torn between duty and desire when she falls in love with a man from a different social class. As their forbidden love blossoms, they must navigate the challenges of societal expectations and family secrets that threaten to tear them apart. Will their love be strong enough to overcome the obstacles in their path?

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Social class, Family secrets, Sacrifice
