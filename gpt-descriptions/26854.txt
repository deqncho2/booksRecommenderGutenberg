Synopsis:
"The Trembling of a Leaf: Little Stories of the South Sea Islands" by W. Somerset Maugham is a collection of short stories set in the exotic and captivating South Sea Islands. Each story delves into the lives of different characters, exploring their encounters with love, desire, betrayal, and the clash of cultures. From a young woman torn between her duty and her heart's desires to a man who finds himself caught in a web of deceit, these tales offer a glimpse into the complexities of human nature and the allure of the South Seas.

Genres:
Fiction, Short Stories, Adventure

Themes:
Love and Desire, Cultural Clash, Betrayal, Human Nature
