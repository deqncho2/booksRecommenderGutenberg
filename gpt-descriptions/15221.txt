Synopsis: In "A New Conscience and an Ancient Evil," Jane Addams takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the dark underbelly of society, exposing an ancient evil that continues to plague humanity. Through a series of compelling narratives and insightful analysis, Addams explores the profound impact of this evil on individuals and communities, challenging readers to confront their own complicity and consider the urgent need for a new conscience. With a blend of personal anecdotes, historical context, and social commentary, Addams offers a powerful call to action, urging readers to join her in the fight against this pervasive evil.

Genres: Non-fiction, Social Commentary, Sociology

Themes: Social justice, Morality, Civic responsibility, Human nature
