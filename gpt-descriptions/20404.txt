Synopsis: In 'All Round the Year', join a group of adventurous children as they embark on a series of thrilling and magical escapades that take place throughout the seasons. From encountering mysterious creatures in the depths of winter to uncovering hidden treasures in the vibrant springtime, each chapter brings a new and exciting adventure. With their boundless imagination and unwavering friendship, these young explorers will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Nature, Exploration
