Synopsis: 
In "Robinson Crusoe, Jr.: A story for little folks," young Jack Hazard embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him to a remote island. After a shipwreck separates him from his family, Jack must rely on his resourcefulness and ingenuity to survive in this unfamiliar and challenging environment. With the help of newfound friends and his unwavering determination, Jack discovers the true meaning of resilience and the power of friendship. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats as they follow Jack's extraordinary journey of survival and self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Survival, Resourcefulness, Friendship
