Synopsis: In 'Mutual Aid: A Factor of Evolution', Kropotkin explores the fascinating concept of mutual aid and its role in shaping the evolution of species. Through a captivating blend of scientific analysis and historical examples, Kropotkin challenges the prevailing notion of "survival of the fittest" and presents a compelling argument for the power of cooperation and solidarity in the natural world. This thought-provoking work sheds light on the intricate dynamics of social organization and offers a fresh perspective on the true nature of evolution.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Philosophy

Themes: Mutual aid, Evolution, Cooperation, Social organization
