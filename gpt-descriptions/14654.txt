Synopsis: In the rugged and treacherous wilderness of the Yukon, Frona Welse, a strong-willed and independent young woman, finds herself caught in a thrilling adventure that tests her resilience and challenges societal norms. As she navigates through the harsh landscapes and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Frona must confront her own desires, face danger head-on, and ultimately discover her true self. With its gripping narrative and vivid descriptions, 'A Daughter of the Snows' takes readers on a captivating journey of self-discovery and survival in the unforgiving Alaskan frontier.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Independence, Gender Roles, Wilderness Survival
