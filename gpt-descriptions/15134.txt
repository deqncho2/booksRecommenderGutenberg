Synopsis: 
In 'Supplement to Animal Sanctuaries in Labrador', Lt.-Colonel William Wood takes us on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of Labrador, uncovering the hidden secrets of its animal sanctuaries. As he presents his findings before the Commission of Conservation, Wood's account reveals astonishing discoveries and challenges our understanding of the region's wildlife. With vivid descriptions and intriguing anecdotes, this supplement promises to transport readers to the heart of Labrador's animal sanctuaries, leaving them eager to explore the wonders that lie within.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Exploration

Themes: Wildlife conservation, Environmental preservation, Exploration and discovery
