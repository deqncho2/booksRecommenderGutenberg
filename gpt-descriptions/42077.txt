Synopsis: In "The Boy Scouts at the Panama Canal" by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts embark on a thrilling journey to the iconic Panama Canal. As they navigate through the lush jungles and encounter unexpected challenges, the Scouts must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and courage to overcome the obstacles that lie ahead. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys find themselves in a race against time to complete their mission and prove their worth. Will they succeed in their quest and emerge as true heroes?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Resilience
