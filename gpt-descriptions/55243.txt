Synopsis: In 'The Puzzle in the Pond: A Judy Bolton Mystery' by Margaret Sutton, Judy Bolton finds herself entangled in a perplexing mystery when a valuable necklace goes missing during a summer vacation at a picturesque lake. As Judy delves deeper into the case, she uncovers a web of secrets and hidden motives, leading her to question the trustworthiness of those around her. With her keen intuition and determination, Judy must navigate through a maze of clues and red herrings to solve the puzzle and bring the truth to light.

Genres: Mystery, Young Adult Fiction

Themes: Trust, Deception, Investigation
