Synopsis: In 'Two Gallant Sons of Devon: A Tale of the Days of Queen Bess' by Collingwood, Harry, readers are transported to the thrilling era of Queen Elizabeth I. The story follows two courageous sons of Devon, who find themselves embroiled in a dangerous adventure that tests their loyalty, bravery, and honor. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter formidable enemies, and face unexpected challenges, the brothers must rely on their wits and resourcefulness to protect their loved ones and defend their homeland. This gripping tale of adventure, loyalty, and sacrifice will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Bravery, Honor, Family, Patriotism
