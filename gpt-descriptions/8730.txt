Synopsis: 
In 'A Little Bush Maid' by Mary Grant Bruce, we follow the spirited and adventurous Norah Linton as she navigates the rugged Australian bush. When her family moves to the countryside, Norah finds herself in a world of excitement and challenges, from taming wild horses to facing dangerous bushfires. Alongside her loyal dog, Bobs, Norah's determination and bravery are put to the test as she learns the ways of the land and discovers the true meaning of friendship.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Independence, Connection with nature
