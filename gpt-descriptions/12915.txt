Synopsis: In the dark and treacherous world of Renaissance Italy, where power and deceit reign supreme, 'The White Devil' takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with revenge, passion, and betrayal. As the lives of the characters intertwine, a web of secrets and lies is spun, leading to a dramatic climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Tragedy, Revenge Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Corruption, Ambition, Love and Lust, Morality, Manipulation
