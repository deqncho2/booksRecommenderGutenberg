Synopsis: In 'Lincoln and the Sleeping Sentinel: The True Story', author Lucius Eugene Chittenden uncovers a little-known incident from Abraham Lincoln's life that reveals the depth of his compassion and sense of justice. Set during the American Civil War, this captivating narrative follows the extraordinary events surrounding a young Union soldier who falls asleep on duty and faces the severe consequences of his actions. As the story unfolds, Chittenden masterfully weaves together historical facts and personal accounts, shedding light on Lincoln's remarkable decision that would forever change the lives of those involved. This gripping tale offers a unique perspective on one of America's greatest leaders and the moral dilemmas he faced during a time of turmoil.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Leadership, Compassion, Justice
