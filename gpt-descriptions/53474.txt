Synopsis: In 'Prejudices, third series', H. L. Mencken takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through his sharp and unapologetic observations of American society. With his signature wit and biting humor, Mencken delves into various topics, from politics and religion to literature and culture, challenging conventional wisdom and exposing the flaws and contradictions of the human condition. Through a collection of insightful essays, he offers a fresh perspective on the complexities of American life, leaving readers both entertained and enlightened.

Genres: Essay, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Prejudice, American society, Intellectualism, Cultural criticism
