Synopsis: In 'Our Little German Cousin', we follow the adventures of Gretchen, a young girl living in Germany. From exploring the enchanting Black Forest to experiencing the vibrant traditions of Oktoberfest, Gretchen's journey is filled with cultural discoveries and heartwarming friendships. As she navigates the joys and challenges of her everyday life, readers will be captivated by the rich tapestry of German customs and traditions that unfold before their eyes.

Genres: Children's Literature, Historical Fiction, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Cultural Identity, Friendship, Adventure
