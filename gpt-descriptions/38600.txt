Synopsis: In 'The World's Sixteen Crucified Saviors; Or, Christianity Before Christ', Graves, Kersey explores the fascinating and controversial concept of multiple crucified saviors throughout history. Through meticulous research and compelling evidence, the author presents a thought-provoking examination of the parallels between the life stories of these revered figures and the story of Jesus Christ. Delving into ancient myths, legends, and religious texts from various cultures, Graves challenges conventional beliefs and invites readers to question the origins and foundations of Christianity.

Genres: Historical, Religious, Comparative Religion

Themes: Mythology, Religious Syncretism, Historical Analysis
