Synopsis: In 'The Lord of Dynevor: A Tale of the Times of Edward the First', the reader is transported to the medieval era, where the young hero, Owen, finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the pursuit of justice. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of medieval Wales, Owen must confront his own identity and make choices that will shape the destiny of his family and his land. With vivid descriptions of battles, courtly romance, and the clash of cultures, this captivating tale keeps the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the walls of Dynevor Castle.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Power, Love, Justice
