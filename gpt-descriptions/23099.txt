Synopsis: In 'The Fourth Invasion' by Robert W. Lowndes, a small town is suddenly thrust into chaos when a mysterious object crash-lands nearby. As the townspeople investigate, they uncover a shocking truth that threatens not only their lives but the fate of the entire world. With time running out, they must band together to confront an otherworldly force that is far more powerful than they could have ever imagined.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Alien invasion, Survival, Human resilience
