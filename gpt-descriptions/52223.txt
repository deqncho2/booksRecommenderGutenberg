Synopsis: 

"The Campaigns of the 124th Regiment, Ohio Volunteer Infantry, with Roster and Roll of Honor" takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of the brave soldiers of the 124th Regiment during the American Civil War. From the regiment's formation to their involvement in significant battles, this book provides a vivid and detailed account of their struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices. Through personal anecdotes, historical records, and firsthand narratives, author G.W. Lewis paints a compelling picture of the regiment's courage, resilience, and unwavering dedication to their cause.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Resilience
