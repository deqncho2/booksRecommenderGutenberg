Synopsis: In 'The World English Bible (WEB): Amos' by Anonymous, a prophet named Amos is called upon by God to deliver a powerful message to the people of Israel. As he confronts the nation's leaders and exposes their corruption, Amos faces opposition and danger, but remains steadfast in his mission to bring about justice and righteousness. With vivid imagery and poetic language, this book explores the consequences of societal injustice and the importance of staying true to one's convictions.

Genres: Religious fiction, Biblical literature, Poetry

Themes: Social justice, Corruption, Faith and conviction
