Synopsis: 
In 'The Men of the Moss-Hags: Being a history of adventure taken from the papers of William Gordon of Earlstoun in Galloway', the reader is transported to the rugged and mysterious landscape of Galloway, Scotland. Through the papers of William Gordon, a man of great courage and resilience, we are immersed in a thrilling tale of adventure, danger, and the pursuit of justice. As Gordon's story unfolds, we are introduced to a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own secrets and motivations. From treacherous journeys through treacherous terrain to encounters with notorious outlaws, this gripping historical narrative will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the men of the moss-hags.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Justice, Secrets, Resilience
