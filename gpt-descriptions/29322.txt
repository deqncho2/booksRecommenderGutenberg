Synopsis: In 'When the Sleepers Woke' by Arthur Leo Zagat, a group of people mysteriously awaken from a deep sleep to find themselves in a world vastly different from the one they knew. As they navigate through this strange new reality, they uncover shocking secrets and face unexpected challenges that force them to question their own identities and the nature of their existence. With each revelation, the line between dreams and reality blurs, leading to a thrilling and thought-provoking journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Reality vs. Illusion, Existentialism
