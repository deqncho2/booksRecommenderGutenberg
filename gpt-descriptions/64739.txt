Synopsis: In "Paula Monti; or, The Hôtel Lambert," the reader is transported to the enchanting world of 19th-century Paris, where the young and beautiful Paula Monti finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden desires. As she becomes a resident of the prestigious Hôtel Lambert, Paula discovers that the opulent facade hides a dark underbelly of deceit, passion, and treachery. With each twist and turn, Paula's journey takes her deeper into a world where love and betrayal collide, forcing her to question her own desires and the true nature of those around her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Deception, Forbidden Love, Identity, Power Dynamics
