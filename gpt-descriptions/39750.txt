Synopsis: 
In 'To Your Dog and to My Dog', two strangers find themselves unexpectedly connected through a series of heartwarming and mysterious events. As they navigate their separate lives, their paths intersect in unexpected ways, leading to a profound exploration of love, loss, and the unbreakable bond between humans and their furry companions. This poignant and uplifting story will leave readers questioning the extraordinary connections that exist in the world around us.

Genres: Fiction, Contemporary, Magical Realism

Themes: Love, Loss, Connection, Fate, Animal Companionship
