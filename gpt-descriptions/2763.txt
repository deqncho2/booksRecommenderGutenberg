Synopsis: In "The World's Desire," a young Greek warrior named Odysseus embarks on a perilous journey to find the legendary Helen of Troy, whose beauty has captivated the hearts of men across the ancient world. As Odysseus travels through treacherous lands and encounters mythical creatures, he must confront his own desires and the consequences of his actions. With the fate of nations hanging in the balance, Odysseus must navigate a world filled with love, betrayal, and the pursuit of immortality.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mythology

Themes: Love and Desire, Heroism, Fate and Destiny
