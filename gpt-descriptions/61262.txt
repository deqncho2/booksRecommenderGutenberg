Synopsis: 
In "Poirot Investigates," the brilliant detective Hercule Poirot takes on a series of intriguing cases that range from a mysterious suicide to a perplexing jewel theft. With his sharp mind and keen eye for detail, Poirot unravels each puzzle, uncovering hidden motives and surprising twists along the way. From the bustling streets of London to the opulent settings of the upper class, this collection of short stories will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Justice, Human Nature
