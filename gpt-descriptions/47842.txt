Synopsis: In 'Queen Moo's Talisman: The Fall of the Maya Empire', Alice D. Le Plongeon takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient Maya civilization. As the story unfolds, we follow the adventures of Queen Moo, a powerful and enigmatic ruler, and her quest to protect her people and their sacred talisman. Filled with mystery, intrigue, and a touch of romance, this historical fiction novel delves into the secrets of the Maya Empire and the events that led to its downfall.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Power and Leadership, Ancient Civilizations, Fate and Destiny
