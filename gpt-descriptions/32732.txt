Synopsis:
In 'Jacob's Ladder' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious and enigmatic man named Jacob Harbord appears out of nowhere, claiming to have lost his memory. As he embarks on a journey to uncover his past, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and danger. With each step he takes, the truth becomes more elusive, and Jacob must confront his own identity and the sinister forces that seek to control him. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes:
Identity, Memory, Deception
