Synopsis: In "The Boys in White: The Experience of a Hospital Agent in and around Washington," Julia S. Wheelock takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of hospital agents during the Civil War. Through the eyes of the protagonist, we witness the challenges, triumphs, and heartbreaks faced by these unsung heroes as they navigate the chaotic and often devastating landscape of war-torn Washington. From the bustling hospitals to the battlefields, Wheelock's vivid storytelling immerses readers in the lives of these dedicated individuals, shedding light on their sacrifices and the profound impact they had on the wounded soldiers they cared for.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Medical fiction

Themes: Sacrifice, Compassion, Resilience, Human connection
