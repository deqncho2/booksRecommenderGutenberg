Synopsis: 
'Favourite Fables in Prose and Verse' is a captivating collection of enchanting tales that will transport readers to a world filled with talking animals, magical creatures, and moral lessons. From clever foxes to wise owls, each fable presents a unique and thought-provoking story that will leave readers pondering the deeper meanings hidden within. With its delightful blend of prose and verse, this collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages and ignite their imagination.

Genres: 
Fiction, Fantasy, Poetry

Themes: 
Morality, Wisdom, Animal Behavior
