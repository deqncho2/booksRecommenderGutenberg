Synopsis: In 'Dawn' by Adams, H. A., Mrs., a young woman finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change her life forever. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to uncover the truth and protect those she holds dear. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies beyond the next dawn.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Danger and suspense, Personal sacrifice
