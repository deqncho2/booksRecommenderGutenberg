Synopsis: In "Where the Phph Pebbles Go" by Miriam Allen De Ford, a young girl named Phph discovers a mysterious portal that leads her to a hidden world filled with enchanting creatures and magical adventures. As she embarks on a quest to uncover the truth behind the disappearing pebbles, Phph must navigate through treacherous landscapes and face unexpected challenges. With the help of newfound friends and her own determination, she unravels a secret that could change her life forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Discovery, Friendship, Courage
