Synopsis: In "Adonijah: A Tale of the Jewish Dispersion," Jane Margaret Strickland takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of a Jewish family living in exile. Set against the backdrop of the Jewish dispersion, the story follows Adonijah, a young man torn between his love for his family and his desire for freedom. As he navigates the challenges of assimilation, prejudice, and the search for identity, Adonijah's journey becomes a poignant exploration of the human spirit and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Cultural

Themes: Identity, Family, Resilience, Prejudice
