Synopsis: 
In 'Miss Billy', a young woman named Billy Neilson finds herself suddenly orphaned and alone in the world. With no family or prospects, she is taken in by her late father's old friend, a wealthy bachelor named Bertram Henshaw. As Billy adjusts to her new life, she discovers a passion for music and a talent for bringing joy to those around her. But when a charming stranger enters her life, Billy must navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and the choices that will shape her future. 

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Self-discovery, Family bonds
