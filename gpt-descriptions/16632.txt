Synopsis: In 'Over Here' by Edgar A. Guest, a small-town newspaper editor finds himself caught in the midst of a gripping mystery that threatens to unravel the fabric of his community. As he delves deeper into the secrets and scandals hidden behind closed doors, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that could change everything. With its intriguing plot twists and complex characters, 'Over Here' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Small-town secrets, Moral dilemmas, Redemption
