Synopsis: 

In 'Home Pork Making' by A. W. Fulton, embark on a fascinating journey into the world of pork processing and discover the secrets to creating delicious homemade pork products. From curing and smoking to sausage making and ham preparation, this comprehensive guide provides step-by-step instructions and expert tips that will leave you eager to try your hand at this age-old culinary tradition. Delve into the art of preserving pork and unlock the knowledge that has been passed down through generations, ensuring that you can savor the flavors of your own homemade pork creations.

Genres: Non-fiction, Cookbook, Food and Drink

Themes: Culinary traditions, Food preservation, Homemade delicacies
