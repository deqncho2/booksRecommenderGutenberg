Synopsis: In the small town of Raymond, a group of individuals are challenged to ask themselves a simple yet profound question: "What would Jesus do?" As they embark on a journey to live their lives in accordance with this question, they find themselves facing unexpected trials, making difficult choices, and experiencing the transformative power of faith. With each decision they make, they discover the profound impact that following in Jesus' footsteps can have on their own lives and the lives of those around them.

Genres: Christian fiction, Inspirational fiction, Social commentary

Themes: Faith, Social justice, Personal transformation
