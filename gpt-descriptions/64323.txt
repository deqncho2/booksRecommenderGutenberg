Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, a mysterious yellow fog descends, trapping the residents and cutting them off from the outside world. As the fog thickens, strange occurrences begin to unfold, revealing hidden secrets and long-forgotten memories. Amidst the chaos, a group of unlikely allies must unravel the truth behind the fog and confront their own fears before it consumes them all.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Secrets, Memory, Survival
