Synopsis: 
In 'Linda Carlton's Perilous Summer' by Lavell, Edith, Linda Carlton finds herself embarking on a thrilling and dangerous adventure during her summer vacation. When Linda's family moves to a small coastal town, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries that threaten her safety and the safety of those she cares about. As she unravels the truth, Linda must navigate treacherous waters, both literal and metaphorical, and confront the darkness lurking beneath the idyllic surface of her new home. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, 'Linda Carlton's Perilous Summer' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets, Danger, Coming of Age
