Synopsis: In 'The Highest Mountain' by Walton Bryce, a young mountaineer named Alex embarks on a treacherous journey to conquer the world's tallest peak. Battling against the elements and his own inner demons, Alex must push himself to the limits of human endurance. As he ascends higher, he discovers that the mountain holds secrets that could change his life forever. With danger lurking at every turn, Alex must decide how far he is willing to go to reach the summit and what sacrifices he is willing to make.

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Perseverance, Self-discovery, Sacrifice
