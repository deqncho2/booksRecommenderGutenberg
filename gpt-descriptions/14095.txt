Synopsis: In "The Thirteenth Chair: A Play in Three Acts," a murder mystery unfolds within the confines of a séance room, where a group of individuals gather to communicate with the spirit world. As the lights dim and the séance begins, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to a shocking climax that will keep the audience on the edge of their seats. With each character harboring their own motives and suspicions, the play delves into the intricate web of deception and betrayal, leaving the audience guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Supernatural, Justice
