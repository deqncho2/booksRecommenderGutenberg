Synopsis: In 'No. 13 Washington Square', a young woman named Emily moves into a grand old house in New York City, unaware of the dark secrets that lie within its walls. As she delves deeper into the history of the house and its previous occupants, Emily becomes entangled in a web of mystery, romance, and supernatural occurrences. With each revelation, the line between reality and illusion blurs, leaving Emily questioning her own sanity and the true nature of the house she now calls home.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Perception vs. reality, Madness and sanity
