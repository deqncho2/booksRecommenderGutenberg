Synopsis: 
In 'My Life' by Flynt, Josiah, a young man finds himself caught in a web of secrets and deception when he stumbles upon a mysterious journal that seems to hold the key to his own identity. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic pages, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between reality and fiction blurs. With each revelation, he uncovers shocking truths about his past and the people around him, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Redemption
