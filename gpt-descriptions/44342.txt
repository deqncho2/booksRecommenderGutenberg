Synopsis: 
'Classical Enigmas, Adapted to Every Month in the Year' is a captivating and educational collection of enigmas and puzzles designed to entertain and challenge readers of all ages. Composed from various sources such as English and Roman histories, heathen mythology, and the names of famous writers, this book aims to engage young minds while also testing their memory and knowledge. Each month presents a new set of enigmas, inviting readers to solve riddles and uncover hidden meanings. With its clever and thought-provoking content, this book promises to provide hours of amusement and mental stimulation.

Genres: Puzzle book, Educational, Entertainment

Themes: Memory, Knowledge, History, Mythology, Literature
