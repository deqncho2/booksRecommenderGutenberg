Synopsis: In 'Wood Rangers: The Trappers of Sonora' by Reid, Mayne, a group of young adventurers embarks on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of Sonora, Mexico. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter dangerous wildlife, they must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to survive. Along the way, they uncover a hidden secret that could change their lives forever. This action-packed tale of courage, friendship, and discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Young Adult

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Friendship
