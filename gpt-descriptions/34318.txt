Synopsis: In 'Life Blood' by Hoover, Thomas, a young woman named Emma discovers a hidden world of supernatural beings and becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and desire. As she navigates this treacherous new reality, Emma must confront her own inner demons and make choices that will determine not only her fate but the fate of those she loves. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, 'Life Blood' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within.

Genres: Paranormal, Urban Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Identity, Power, Love and Sacrifice
