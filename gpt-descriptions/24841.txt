Synopsis: In 'Friendly Visiting among the Poor: A Handbook for Charity Workers', Mary Ellen Richmond provides a comprehensive guide for those seeking to make a positive impact on the lives of the less fortunate. Drawing from her own experiences as a social worker, Richmond offers practical advice, heartfelt anecdotes, and valuable insights into the challenges faced by both the visitors and the visited. Through her engaging storytelling and compassionate approach, she explores the complexities of poverty, the importance of empathy, and the transformative power of human connection. This handbook serves as a beacon of hope, inspiring readers to become agents of change in their communities.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Social Work

Themes: Poverty, Empathy, Community, Social Justice
