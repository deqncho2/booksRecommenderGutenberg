Synopsis: In 'The Mentor: Uncle Sam, Vol. 7, Num. 11, Serial No. 183, July 15, 1919' by Albert Bushnell Hart, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the history and significance of Uncle Sam. This edition of The Mentor magazine delves into the origins of the iconic American symbol, exploring the evolution of Uncle Sam from a mere cartoon character to a powerful representation of the nation. Through vivid storytelling and historical analysis, Hart sheds light on the cultural and political impact of Uncle Sam, revealing surprising insights that will leave readers fascinated and enlightened.

Genres: Historical, Educational, Magazine

Themes: American identity, National symbols, Cultural significance
