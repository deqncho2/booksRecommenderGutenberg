Synopsis: In 'Household Organization' by Caddy, Florence, a seemingly ordinary woman named Emma discovers a hidden secret within her new home that leads her on a thrilling journey of mystery and self-discovery. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Emma uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that will challenge everything she thought she knew about her own life. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and Emma must navigate a dangerous path to uncover the truth before it consumes her. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Self-discovery
