Synopsis: In 'The Student's Companion to Latin Authors', Middleton and Mills take readers on a captivating journey through the world of Latin literature. From the epic tales of Virgil to the philosophical musings of Cicero, this comprehensive guide provides a fascinating exploration of the works of various Latin authors. With insightful analysis and helpful explanations, the authors bring these ancient texts to life, shedding light on their historical context and literary significance. Whether you are a student of Latin or simply a lover of classical literature, this companion is sure to deepen your understanding and appreciation of these timeless works.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Classical Studies, Reference

Themes: Latin literature, Ancient Rome, Literary analysis
