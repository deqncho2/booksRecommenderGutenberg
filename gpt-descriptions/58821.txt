Synopsis: 
In 'The Strenuous Life: The Works of Theodore Roosevelt, Volume 12 (of 14)', Theodore Roosevelt takes readers on a captivating journey through his experiences as a statesman, adventurer, and conservationist. From his time as President of the United States to his expeditions in the wilderness, Roosevelt shares his insights on leadership, perseverance, and the importance of embracing challenges. This volume offers a collection of his speeches, essays, and letters, providing a unique glimpse into the mind of one of America's most influential figures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Political Science

Themes: Leadership, Perseverance, Conservation, American History
