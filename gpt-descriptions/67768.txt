Synopsis: 

In 'Hand-book of Sanitary Information for Householders', Tracy, Roger Sherman provides a comprehensive guide filled with essential facts and practical suggestions on various aspects of maintaining a healthy home. From ventilation and drainage to the care of contagious diseases, disinfection, food, and water, this book covers it all. With additional appendices on disinfectants and plumbers' materials, readers will find valuable information to ensure the well-being of their households. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Health, Home Improvement

Themes: Sanitation, Disease Prevention, Household Maintenance
