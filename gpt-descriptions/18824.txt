Synopsis: 

In 'Fairies and Folk of Ireland', William Henry Frost takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Irish folklore. From mischievous fairies to legendary creatures, this collection of tales unveils the hidden wonders and magical beings that have long fascinated the people of Ireland. With each story, Frost weaves a spellbinding narrative that transports readers to a realm where ancient traditions and supernatural beings come to life. Prepare to be captivated by the whimsical adventures, mysterious encounters, and timeless wisdom that lie within the pages of this enchanting book.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Irish culture and traditions, Supernatural beings, Magic and enchantment
