Synopsis: 
In 'Seneca Myths and Folk Tales', Arthur C. Parker delves into the rich tapestry of Seneca mythology and folklore, transporting readers to a world filled with enchanting legends and captivating tales. From the creation of the world to the adventures of legendary heroes, this collection offers a mesmerizing glimpse into the ancient Seneca culture. Discover the origins of the Seneca people, encounter mythical creatures, and witness the power of ancient gods and goddesses. With each story, Parker weaves a spellbinding narrative that will leave readers spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Cultural Studies

Themes: Creation, Heroism, Supernatural Beings, Cultural Identity
