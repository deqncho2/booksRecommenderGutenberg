Synopsis: In "The Stolen White Elephant," Mark Twain takes readers on a thrilling journey as a rare and valuable white elephant mysteriously disappears during its transportation from India to Britain. As the investigation unfolds, a series of unexpected events and comical mishaps occur, leading to a captivating and unpredictable tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Satire

Themes: Crime and Investigation, Absurdity and Irony
