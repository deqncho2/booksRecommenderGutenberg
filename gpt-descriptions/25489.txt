Synopsis: 
In "Peck's Bad Boy Abroad," follow the misadventures of the mischievous Peck and his exasperated father as they embark on a series of comical escapades across foreign lands. From causing chaos in Paris to getting into trouble in Rome, Peck's antics know no bounds. With his quick wit and knack for trouble, Peck manages to turn every situation into a hilarious disaster, leaving readers in stitches as they join him on his unforgettable journey.

Genres: Humor, Travel, Adventure

Themes: Mischievousness, Cultural differences, Father-son relationships
