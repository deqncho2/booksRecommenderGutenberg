Synopsis: In "The Orange Girl" by Walter Besant, a young man named Jack Redgrave finds himself captivated by a mysterious woman he encounters on the streets of London. Drawn to her vibrant personality and the enigmatic orange she carries, Jack embarks on a quest to uncover the truth behind her identity. As he delves deeper into her past, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and unexpected connections that will challenge his perception of reality and lead him on a journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love, Identity, Fate, Redemption
