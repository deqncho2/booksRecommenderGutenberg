Synopsis: In "Thomas Carlyle" by G.K. Chesterton and J.E. Hodder-Williams, delve into the fascinating life and mind of one of the most influential thinkers of the 19th century. This biography takes readers on a captivating journey through Carlyle's unconventional upbringing, his literary achievements, and his profound impact on society. From his passionate defense of heroes to his unyielding critique of modernity, Carlyle's ideas continue to resonate today. Discover the man behind the words and explore the profound legacy he left behind.

Genres: Biography, History, Literary Criticism

Themes: Heroism, Individualism, Social Critique
