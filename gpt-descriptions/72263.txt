Synopsis: 

In "Rusticus: Or, the Future of the Countryside," Martin S. Briggs takes readers on a captivating journey through a rural landscape that is on the brink of transformation. Set in a not-so-distant future, the story follows a young protagonist named Rusticus, who finds himself caught between the allure of technological progress and the preservation of traditional values. As he navigates through a world where nature and machines collide, Rusticus must confront the consequences of his choices and the impact they have on the future of the countryside.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Technology vs. Tradition, Environmentalism, Identity and Belonging
