Synopsis: In "The Oxonian in Thelemarken, volume 1," Frederick Metcalfe takes us on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of south-western Norway. Through his vivid and detailed travel notes, we are transported to the summers of 1856 and 1857, where we explore the stunning beauty of the region and delve into the intriguing legendary lore that surrounds it. As the Oxonian immerses himself in the local culture and folklore, he uncovers hidden secrets and encounters fascinating characters, all while experiencing the awe-inspiring wonders of Norway's natural wonders.

Genres: Travelogue, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Immersion, Mythology, Natural Beauty
