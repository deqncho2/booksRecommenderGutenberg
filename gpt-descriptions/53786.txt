Synopsis: In 'The Dolls on Dress Parade' by Effa E. Preston, a young girl named Marjorie receives a mysterious package containing a set of exquisite dolls. Little does she know that these dolls hold a secret that will transport her into a world of enchantment and adventure. As Marjorie embarks on a thrilling journey, she must navigate through challenges and uncover the truth behind the dolls' magical powers. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will leave readers eagerly turning the pages to discover the fate of Marjorie and her newfound companions.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Discovery
