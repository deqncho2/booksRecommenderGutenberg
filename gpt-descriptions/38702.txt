Synopsis: In 'The Maker of Opportunities' by Gibbs, George, a young and ambitious artist named Alex embarks on a journey that takes him from the bustling streets of New York City to the enchanting landscapes of Europe. Faced with numerous challenges and unexpected encounters, Alex discovers that the key to success lies not only in his talent but also in his ability to seize the opportunities that come his way. As he navigates through the world of art, love, and self-discovery, Alex learns valuable lessons about perseverance, the power of dreams, and the importance of taking risks.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Pursuing one's passion, Embracing opportunities, Self-discovery
