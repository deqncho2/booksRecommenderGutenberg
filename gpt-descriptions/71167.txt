Synopsis: 
In "Through the Gates of the Silver Key," Randolph Carter, a recurring character in Lovecraft's works, embarks on a mind-bending journey that transcends time, space, and even existence itself. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the universe, Carter discovers unimaginable realms and encounters beings beyond human comprehension. With each revelation, the boundaries of reality blur, leading him to question the very nature of his own existence and the cosmic forces that govern the universe.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Horror, Science Fiction

Themes: 
Existentialism, Cosmic Horror, Metaphysics
