Synopsis:
In the rugged and untamed landscape of Wind River, a mysterious rustler emerges, wreaking havoc on the local ranchers. As tensions rise and suspicion spreads, a young cowboy named Ben Wade finds himself caught in the middle of the escalating conflict. With his loyalty and courage tested, Ben must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth behind the rustler's identity and put an end to the chaos that threatens to tear apart the community.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Justice and morality, Loyalty and betrayal
