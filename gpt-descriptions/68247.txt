Synopsis: In 'Vocation' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden truth that challenges everything he thought he knew, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and moral dilemmas. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking revelations, 'Vocation' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Scientific advancement, Morality, Identity
