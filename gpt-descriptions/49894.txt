Synopsis: "Chicago to the Sea" is a comprehensive guidebook that takes readers on an exciting journey through the principal eastern summer resorts. From the majestic Niagara Falls to the picturesque White Mountains, from the enchanting Saint Lawrence and Saguenay Rivers to the vibrant cities of Montreal and Quebec, and finally to the serene New England sea beaches, this guidebook provides all the necessary information on how and when to enjoy these remarkable destinations. Packed with detailed descriptions, practical tips, and captivating anecdotes, "Chicago to the Sea" is the ultimate companion for anyone seeking an unforgettable summer adventure in the eastern United States and Canada.

Genres: Travel Guide, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Adventure, Vacation Planning
