Synopsis: In 'Gas and Flame in Modern Warfare' by Samuel James Manson Auld, a gripping exploration of the devastating impact of chemical warfare unfolds. Set against the backdrop of World War I, this captivating account delves into the sinister world of gas attacks and the harrowing experiences of soldiers on the front lines. As the protagonist navigates the horrors of war, unexpected alliances and personal sacrifices emerge, challenging the boundaries of loyalty and humanity. With vivid descriptions and heart-wrenching moments, this book offers a haunting portrayal of the destructive power of gas and flame in the modern battlefield.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Warfare, Survival, Morality, Sacrifice
