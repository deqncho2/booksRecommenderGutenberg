Synopsis: In 'Vocabulum; or The Rogue's Lexicon: Compiled from the Most Authentic Sources', George W. Matsell takes readers on a captivating journey into the secret language of rogues and criminals. This intriguing lexicon unveils the hidden meanings behind their coded words and phrases, shedding light on the underworld of 19th-century New York City. From thieves to swindlers, Matsell's compilation provides a fascinating glimpse into the criminal subculture, revealing the cunning tactics and clever disguises employed by these rogues. Delve into this captivating lexicon and unlock the secrets of the city's most notorious characters.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, History

Themes: Criminal subculture, Language and communication, 19th-century New York City
