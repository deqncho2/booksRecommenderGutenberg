Synopsis: In 'A Thousand Francs Reward; and, Military Sketches' by Emile Gaboriau, a young man named Lecoq finds himself entangled in a mysterious case involving a missing person and a substantial reward. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Lecoq uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Alongside this thrilling main story, the book also presents a collection of military sketches, providing a fascinating glimpse into the world of soldiers and their experiences. 

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Investigation, Loyalty, Military Life
