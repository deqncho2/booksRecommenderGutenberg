Synopsis: In 'Brothers of Peril: A Story of old Newfoundland', Theodore Goodridge Roberts takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rugged landscapes and treacherous waters of Newfoundland. Set in the early 19th century, the story follows two brothers, John and Tom, as they navigate the dangerous world of smuggling, piracy, and political intrigue. As they become entangled in a web of secrets and betrayal, the brothers must rely on their wit, courage, and the bonds of brotherhood to survive. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Roberts paints a vivid picture of a bygone era and the challenges faced by those who dared to defy the law.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Brotherhood, Survival, Betrayal, Courage
