Synopsis: In 'The Scott Country' by John Geddie, a young woman named Emma finds herself drawn into a mysterious world when she inherits a remote Scottish estate from a distant relative. As she delves into the history of the estate, Emma uncovers dark secrets and encounters supernatural forces that threaten her very existence. With the help of a local historian and a charming but enigmatic stranger, she must unravel the truth behind the estate's haunting past before it consumes her. This gripping tale of suspense and the supernatural will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Family secrets, Haunting, Identity
