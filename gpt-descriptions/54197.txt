Synopsis: 
'Cambridge: A Sketch-Book' takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque city of Cambridge, England. Through vivid descriptions and charming illustrations, the author invites us to explore the historic streets, iconic landmarks, and hidden corners of this renowned academic hub. From the bustling student life to the tranquil beauty of the River Cam, this sketchbook captures the essence of Cambridge, offering a delightful glimpse into its rich history and vibrant culture.

Genres: Travel, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, History, Culture
