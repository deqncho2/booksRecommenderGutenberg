Synopsis: 

'With Roberts to Pretoria: A Tale of The South African War' follows the thrilling journey of a young British soldier named Ned, who finds himself caught up in the tumultuous events of the South African War. As Ned joins the British forces under the command of General Roberts, he embarks on a dangerous mission to capture the capital city of Pretoria, facing numerous challenges and encounters along the way. With his courage and determination tested to the limit, Ned must navigate the treacherous landscapes of war, forge unlikely alliances, and confront the harsh realities of battle.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Camaraderie, Conflict, Survival
