Synopsis: In 'Confession; Or, The Blind Heart. A Domestic Story' by William Gilmore Simms, a young woman named Edith finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love. As she navigates the complexities of her heart, Edith must confront the consequences of her choices and the power of forgiveness. Set against the backdrop of a turbulent society, this gripping tale explores the depths of human emotions and the complexities of relationships.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Duty and Responsibility, Secrets and Betrayal
