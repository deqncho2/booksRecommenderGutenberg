Synopsis: In 'De L'Orme', G. P. R. James weaves a captivating tale of mystery and romance set against the backdrop of 17th-century France. The story follows the enigmatic protagonist, De L'Orme, as he navigates the treacherous world of court intrigue, secret societies, and forbidden love. As De L'Orme's past is slowly revealed, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of deception and betrayal, where loyalties are tested and alliances are shattered. With its rich historical detail and compelling characters, 'De L'Orme' is a thrilling journey through a tumultuous era, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Intrigue, Betrayal, Forbidden Love, Identity
