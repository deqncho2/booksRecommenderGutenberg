Synopsis: 
'The Millennium, and Other Poems: To Which is Annexed, a Treatise on the Regeneration and Eternal Duration of Matter' is a captivating collection of poems and a thought-provoking treatise by Parley P. Pratt. Through his eloquent verses, Pratt explores the concept of the Millennium, a period of peace and harmony prophesied in religious texts. Alongside the poems, he presents a treatise that delves into the regeneration and eternal duration of matter, offering a unique perspective on the nature of existence. This work is a fascinating blend of poetry and philosophical inquiry that will leave readers pondering the mysteries of life and the possibilities of the future.

Genres: Poetry, Philosophy, Religious Literature
Themes: The Millennium, Regeneration, Eternal Duration of Matter, Peace, Harmony, Existence
