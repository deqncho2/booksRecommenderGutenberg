Synopsis: 
In 'Astounding Stories of Super-Science July 1930', prepare to be transported to a world where scientific marvels and extraordinary adventures await. From a thrilling encounter with a mysterious alien race to a mind-bending exploration of time and space, this collection of stories will keep you on the edge of your seat. Brace yourself for a journey through the realms of super-science that will challenge your imagination and leave you craving for more.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Extraterrestrial life, Time and space travel
