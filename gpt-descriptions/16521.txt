Synopsis: 
In 'Fanny Goes to War' by Washington, Pat Beauchamp, we follow the captivating journey of Fanny, a young woman who defies societal expectations and enlists in the British Army during World War II. As Fanny navigates the challenges of war, she discovers her own strength, resilience, and determination, all while forging unexpected friendships and facing heart-wrenching choices. With a blend of adventure, romance, and historical accuracy, this novel takes readers on an unforgettable wartime odyssey that will leave them eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Gender roles, Courage, Friendship, Sacrifice
