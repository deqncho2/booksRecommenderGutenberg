Synopsis: In 'Face to Face with Kaiserism', Gerard takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of World War I, as he recounts his personal encounters with the enigmatic Kaiser Wilhelm II. From the trenches of the Western Front to the opulent palaces of Germany, Gerard's gripping narrative unveils the true face of Kaiserism, revealing the depths of its power and the consequences it holds for the world. As Gerard navigates the treacherous landscape of war and diplomacy, he must confront the Kaiser's charismatic charm and cunning, leading to a confrontation that will shape the course of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Political Thriller

Themes: Power and Influence, Loyalty and Betrayal, Consequences of War
