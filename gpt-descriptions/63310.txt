Synopsis: In the small, secluded town of Willowbrook, a mysterious chapel stands atop a hill, shrouded in secrets and whispers. When a young journalist arrives to investigate the chapel's enigmatic history, she becomes entangled in a web of dark pasts, hidden desires, and supernatural occurrences. As she delves deeper into the chapel's mysteries, she uncovers a haunting tale that will challenge her beliefs and force her to confront her own demons. With each revelation, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leaving her questioning what is truly lurking within the Chapel on the Hill.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets, Redemption, Supernatural forces, Faith and belief
