Synopsis: In 'Slow and Sure: The Story of Paul Hoffman the Young Street-Merchant', follow the journey of Paul Hoffman, a determined and ambitious young man who sets out to make his mark in the bustling streets of New York City. Facing numerous challenges and setbacks, Paul must navigate the cutthroat world of business, learn valuable life lessons, and discover the true meaning of success. Will he be able to overcome the odds and achieve his dreams?

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Self-discovery
