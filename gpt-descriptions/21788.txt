Synopsis: 

In "Held Fast For England: A Tale of the Siege of Gibraltar (1779-83)" by G. A. Henty, readers are transported to the dramatic events surrounding the legendary Siege of Gibraltar during the late 18th century. The story follows the adventures of Ned, a young British officer, who finds himself caught in the midst of the intense battle for control of the strategic fortress. As the Spanish and French forces relentlessly attack, Ned must navigate the treacherous waters of war, facing danger, betrayal, and personal sacrifice. With the fate of Gibraltar hanging in the balance, Ned's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Patriotism, Survival
