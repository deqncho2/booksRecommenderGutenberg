Synopsis:
In the eighth volume of "The Caxtons: A Family Picture," the Caxton family finds themselves embroiled in a web of secrets and intrigue. As the patriarch Austin Caxton delves deeper into his research on ancient manuscripts, he uncovers a shocking revelation that threatens to upend everything the family holds dear. Meanwhile, his daughter, the spirited and independent Pisistratus, finds herself torn between two suitors, each with their own hidden agendas. With tensions rising and the family's reputation at stake, the Caxtons must navigate treacherous waters to protect their legacy and uncover the truth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family dynamics, Secrets and deception, Love and loyalty
