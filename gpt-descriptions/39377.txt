Synopsis: In the second volume of "Mildred Arkell: A Novel," the captivating story continues as Mildred finds herself torn between duty and desire. As she navigates the complexities of love and societal expectations, her world is filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will Mildred be able to follow her heart, or will she succumb to the pressures of society? This volume delves deeper into the characters' lives, revealing secrets, and exploring the consequences of their choices.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and duty, societal expectations, personal sacrifice, secrets and consequences
