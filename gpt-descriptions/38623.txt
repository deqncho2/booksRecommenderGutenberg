Synopsis: In 'The Story of Charles Strange: A Novel. Vol. 1 (of 3)', we are introduced to the enigmatic and captivating character of Charles Strange. Set in the backdrop of Victorian England, this gripping tale follows Charles as he navigates through a web of secrets, scandals, and unexpected twists. From his mysterious origins to his rise in society, the reader is drawn into a world of intrigue and suspense, where nothing is as it seems. As Charles delves deeper into his own past, he uncovers shocking revelations that threaten to unravel his carefully constructed life. With each page, the reader is left wondering what lies ahead for Charles Strange and how his story will unfold.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
