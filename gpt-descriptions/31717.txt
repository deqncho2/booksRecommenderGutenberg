Synopsis: In the enchanting world of 19th century New York, three extraordinary women run a tea shop that is much more than it seems. Eleanor, Adelaide, and Beatrice possess a secret knowledge of the occult and use their powers to help the women who seek their guidance. As they navigate the bustling city, they encounter a wide array of characters, from wealthy socialites to struggling immigrants, all with their own unique desires and secrets. But when a series of mysterious events begins to unfold, the witches find themselves facing a dangerous adversary who threatens to expose their hidden world. With their powers tested and their lives at stake, the witches must uncover the truth and protect their beloved city from the forces of darkness.

Genres: Historical fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Female empowerment, Magic and the occult, Social inequality
