Synopsis: In 'A Little Union Scout' by Joel Chandler Harris, a young boy named Johnny embarks on a thrilling adventure during the Civil War. When he stumbles upon a secret mission, Johnny must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to navigate dangerous territory and make a difficult decision that could change the course of history. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Morality, Identity
