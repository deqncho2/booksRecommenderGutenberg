Synopsis: In 'The Goblins' Christmas' by Elizabeth Anderson, a group of mischievous goblins decides to pay a visit to a human family on Christmas Eve. With their magical powers and playful nature, the goblins bring unexpected joy and wonder to the household, but also a touch of chaos. As the family navigates through this enchanting and unpredictable night, they learn valuable lessons about the true meaning of Christmas and the importance of love and togetherness.

Genres: Fantasy, Christmas, Children's Literature

Themes: Magic, Family, Joy, Love, Christmas Spirit
