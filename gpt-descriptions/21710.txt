Synopsis: In 'The Crew of the Water Wagtail' by R. M. Ballantyne, a group of adventurous young boys embark on a thrilling sailing expedition along the coast of Scotland. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter mysterious islands, and face unexpected challenges, their bond is tested and their courage is pushed to the limit. With danger lurking at every turn, the crew of the Water Wagtail must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to overcome the perils that await them.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Nautical

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
