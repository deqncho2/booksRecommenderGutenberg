Synopsis: In 'The Beasts in the Void' by Paul W. Fairman, a group of astronauts embarks on a perilous mission to explore an uncharted region of space. As they venture deeper into the unknown, they encounter strange and terrifying creatures that challenge their understanding of the universe. With their lives hanging in the balance, the crew must unravel the mysteries of the void before it consumes them all.

Genres: Science Fiction, Horror, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Existentialism
