Synopsis: In "Christianity and Problems of To-day," a collection of lectures delivered before Lake Forest College, five distinguished scholars explore the pressing issues facing Christianity in the modern world. From the challenges of science and philosophy to the moral dilemmas of society, these thought-provoking lectures delve into the complexities of faith and its relevance in today's rapidly changing landscape. With each speaker offering unique perspectives and insights, this book offers a compelling exploration of the enduring questions that confront Christianity in the face of contemporary challenges.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, Philosophy

Themes: Faith and Reason, Moral Dilemmas, Relevance of Christianity in Modern Society
