Synopsis: In the midst of the Great World War, a group of courageous women rise above societal expectations and join the fight for their countries. Mabel Potter Daggett, a trailblazing journalist, unveils the untold stories of these remarkable women who defied conventions and faced unimaginable challenges on the front lines. With blood red letters on the horizon, this gripping narrative explores the indomitable spirit and sacrifices made by these unsung heroes.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Women's Literature

Themes: Gender roles, Courage, Resilience, Sacrifice
