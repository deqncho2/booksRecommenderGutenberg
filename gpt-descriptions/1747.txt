Synopsis: In 'The Red Seal' by Natalie Sumner Lincoln, a young woman named Ruth Thorne finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she inherits a mysterious red seal. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the seal, Ruth becomes the target of a sinister plot that threatens her life and those she holds dear. With the help of a charming detective, she must navigate a treacherous path filled with deception, betrayal, and unexpected twists to uncover the truth behind the red seal before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Danger and suspense, Love and trust
