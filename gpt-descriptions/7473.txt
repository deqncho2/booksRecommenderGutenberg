Synopsis: 
'Lost on the Moon; Or, in Quest of the Field of Diamonds' by Rockwood, Roy is an exhilarating adventure that follows a group of young explorers who find themselves stranded on the moon after a catastrophic event. As they navigate the treacherous lunar landscape, they stumble upon a mysterious field of diamonds, sparking a thrilling quest filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected discoveries. Will they be able to survive and find their way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Discovery
