Synopsis: In 'Four Girls and a Compact' by Annie Hamilton Donnell, four young girls stumble upon a mysterious compact that holds a secret power. As they embark on a thrilling adventure, they discover that the compact has the ability to transport them to different places and times. With each journey, they encounter new challenges and learn valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the importance of embracing their individual strengths. As they navigate through the unknown, the girls must rely on their wits and teamwork to overcome obstacles and unlock the compact's true purpose. 

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
