Synopsis: In "The Southern Cross: A Play in Four Acts," Daingerfield Foxhall takes us on a captivating journey through the deep South, where secrets, love, and betrayal intertwine. Set against the backdrop of a grand plantation, the story follows a wealthy family torn apart by their own desires and the changing times. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the characters must confront their pasts and make choices that will shape their futures. With its richly drawn characters and dramatic twists, this play explores the complexities of human relationships and the consequences of our actions.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family dynamics, societal change, love and passion, betrayal, personal growth
