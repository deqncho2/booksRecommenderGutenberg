Synopsis: 
'For Jacinta' by Bindloss, Harold is a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and redemption set against the backdrop of the rugged Canadian wilderness. When a young man named Jack embarks on a treacherous journey to find his lost love, Jacinta, he must confront his own demons and face the harsh realities of the unforgiving landscape. As Jack's determination grows, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and danger, where every decision could mean life or death. Will Jack's relentless pursuit of Jacinta lead him to salvation or to his ultimate downfall?

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Redemption, Survival
