Synopsis: 
In "Who Cares? A Story of Adolescence" by Hamilton Cosmo, we are introduced to a group of teenagers navigating the complexities of adolescence in a small town. As they grapple with the pressures of school, relationships, and societal expectations, they find solace in their shared experiences and the bonds they form. However, when a mysterious event shakes their community, they are forced to confront their own fears and secrets, ultimately leading them on a journey of self-discovery and resilience. With its compelling characters and unexpected twists, this coming-of-age tale will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: 
Young Adult Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: 
Adolescence, Friendship, Identity, Secrets, Resilience
