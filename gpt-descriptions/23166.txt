Synopsis: In "Who Was She?: From "The Atlantic Monthly" for September, 1874," Taylor, Bayard takes readers on a mysterious journey as a young man becomes captivated by a woman he encounters on a train. Drawn to her enigmatic presence, he embarks on a quest to uncover the truth about her identity, leading him down a path filled with intrigue and unexpected revelations. As the young man delves deeper into the mystery, he discovers that the woman's past holds secrets that could change his life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Love, Secrets
