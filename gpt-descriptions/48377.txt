Synopsis: In 'Bill Bruce on Forest Patrol' by Arnold, Henry Harley, follow the thrilling adventures of Bill Bruce, a courageous forest ranger, as he embarks on a dangerous mission to protect the wilderness from a mysterious threat. With his trusty companions by his side, Bill must navigate treacherous terrain, face unexpected challenges, and unravel a puzzling mystery that could have devastating consequences. As the tension rises and the stakes get higher, readers will be captivated by the heart-pounding action and suspense that unfolds in this gripping tale.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Conservation, Bravery, Environmentalism
