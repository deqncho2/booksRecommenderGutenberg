Synopsis: In 'Fra Angelico' by James Mason, a young artist named Fra Angelico embarks on a journey that will test his faith, challenge his artistic abilities, and lead him to uncover a hidden secret. Set against the backdrop of Renaissance Italy, this captivating tale follows Fra Angelico as he navigates the complexities of art, religion, and love, ultimately discovering the true power of his brushstroke.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Art, Mystery

Themes: Faith and spirituality, the pursuit of artistic excellence, the intersection of art and religion
