Synopsis: 

In 'Traditions of Lancashire, Volume 2', Roby, John takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and history of Lancashire. From ancient legends and ghostly tales to local customs and superstitions, this volume delves deeper into the enchanting traditions that have shaped the region. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Roby paints a vivid picture of Lancashire's past, intertwining fact and fiction to create a tapestry of captivating narratives that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Historical fiction, Folklore, Supernatural

Themes: Local traditions, Legends and myths, Cultural heritage
