Synopsis: 

In 'Manassas (Bull Run) National Battlefield Park [1953]' by United States. National Park Service, readers are transported to the historic battlegrounds of the American Civil War. This immersive account takes us on a journey through the events that unfolded during the Battle of Bull Run, also known as the First Battle of Manassas. Through vivid descriptions and historical insights, the book brings to life the intense struggles, the bravery of soldiers, and the impact of this pivotal battle on the course of American history. 

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: War, Courage, Historical Preservation
