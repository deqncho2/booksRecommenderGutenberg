Synopsis: 

'English Book Collectors' by Fletcher, William Younger is a captivating exploration of the fascinating world of book collecting in England. From the dusty shelves of ancient libraries to the secretive auctions of rare manuscripts, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through the lives and obsessions of passionate collectors. Uncovering hidden treasures, encountering eccentric characters, and delving into the history of literature, this narrative offers a glimpse into the captivating world of those who dedicate their lives to the pursuit of rare and valuable books.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Biography

Themes: Book collecting, Obsession, Literary history
