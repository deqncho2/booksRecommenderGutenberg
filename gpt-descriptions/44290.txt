Synopsis: In "A King of Tyre: A Tale of the Times of Ezra and Nehemiah," James M. Ludlow takes readers on a captivating journey to the ancient city of Tyre during the tumultuous times of Ezra and Nehemiah. Amidst political intrigue, religious conflicts, and personal struggles, the story follows the life of a young king who must navigate the treacherous waters of power, love, and faith. As he grapples with his own identity and the challenges of leadership, the king's choices will have far-reaching consequences for his kingdom and the people he holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biblical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Power and Leadership, Identity and Self-discovery, Faith and Religion
