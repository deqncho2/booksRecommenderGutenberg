Synopsis: In Volume 2 of "Clarissa Harlowe; or the history of a young lady," the captivating story of Clarissa Harlowe continues as she faces new challenges and heart-wrenching decisions. As her family's pressure to marry a man she despises intensifies, Clarissa finds herself torn between her own desires and societal expectations. With her reputation and happiness at stake, she must navigate a treacherous path filled with deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. Will Clarissa be able to assert her independence and find true love, or will she succumb to the pressures of a rigid society?

Genres: Epistolary novel, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Social expectations and constraints, Morality and virtue
