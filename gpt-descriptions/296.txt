Synopsis:
In 'The Cash Boy' by Horatio Alger, Jr., we follow the story of Frank Fowler, a young boy who finds himself thrust into the bustling world of New York City. After a series of unfortunate events, Frank is left to fend for himself, taking on odd jobs and facing numerous challenges along the way. As he navigates the streets of the city, Frank encounters both kind-hearted individuals who offer him a helping hand and cunning individuals who seek to exploit him. With determination and resilience, Frank strives to overcome adversity and carve out a better future for himself. Will he be able to rise above his circumstances and find success in the face of adversity?

Genres: 
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Rags-to-riches

Themes:
Perseverance, Social mobility, Morality
