Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 122, January 9, 1915: The suicide; or, Nick Carter and the lost head', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving a mysterious suicide and a missing head. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Carter uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that leads him on a thrilling chase to uncover the truth. With danger lurking at every turn, Nick Carter must use his unparalleled skills to solve the puzzle and bring justice to those involved.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Investigation, Justice
