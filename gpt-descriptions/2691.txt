Synopsis: 
In 'Nan Sherwood at Pine Camp; Or, The Old Lumberman's Secret', Nan Sherwood finds herself embarking on a thrilling adventure when she visits Pine Camp, a place known for its mysterious secrets. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the old lumberman, Nan uncovers a hidden treasure that could change everything. With her friends by her side, Nan must navigate through unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to keep the treasure out of the wrong hands. Will Nan be able to solve the puzzle and protect the secret, or will she become entangled in a dangerous game of greed and deceit?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Treasure Hunting
