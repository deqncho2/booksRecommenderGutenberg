Synopsis: In 'Carry On! A Story of the Fight for Bagdad' by Herbert Strang, readers are transported to the heart of the Middle East during World War I. Amidst the chaos and danger, a group of British soldiers embarks on a daring mission to capture the city of Baghdad from the Ottoman Empire. As they face treacherous landscapes, enemy forces, and internal conflicts, the soldiers must summon their courage and determination to overcome seemingly insurmountable odds. With vivid descriptions and gripping action, this thrilling tale explores the resilience of the human spirit and the sacrifices made in the pursuit of victory.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Resilience
