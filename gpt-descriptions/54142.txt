Synopsis: In 'English Lands, Letters and Kings, vol. 2: From Elizabeth to Anne', Mitchell, Donald Grant takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of English history from the reign of Queen Elizabeth I to Queen Anne. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the author delves into the lives of influential figures such as Shakespeare, Bacon, and Newton, while also exploring the political and cultural developments that shaped the nation. From the intrigue of courtly affairs to the scientific advancements of the time, this volume offers a fascinating glimpse into the rich tapestry of English history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Literary analysis

Themes: Power and politics, Cultural and intellectual movements, Historical figures and their impact
