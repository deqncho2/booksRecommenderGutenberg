Synopsis: 

In 'The castles and abbeys of England; Vol. 2 of 2: from the national records, early chronicles, and other standard authors', author William Beattie takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and architectural wonders of England's castles and abbeys. This volume delves into the fascinating stories behind these majestic structures, drawing from national records, early chronicles, and other esteemed sources. From the towering castles that witnessed epic battles to the serene abbeys that housed devoted monks, Beattie's meticulous research and vivid descriptions bring these historical landmarks to life. Discover the secrets hidden within their walls, the legends that surround them, and the impact they had on shaping England's past.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Architecture

Themes: History, Architecture, Cultural heritage
