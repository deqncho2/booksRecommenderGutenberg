Synopsis: In 'The Laughing Prince: Jugoslav Folk and Fairy Tales', Fillmore Parker takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Yugoslav folk and fairy tales. From mischievous princes and cunning witches to magical creatures and epic quests, this collection of stories is filled with adventure, humor, and unexpected twists. Each tale is beautifully woven with vivid imagery and rich cultural elements, offering a glimpse into the folklore and traditions of the region. As readers delve into these timeless tales, they will be transported to a realm where laughter, bravery, and the power of love prevail.

Genres: Folklore, Fairy Tales, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Love, Magic, Cultural Traditions
