Synopsis: In 'Stolen Treasure' by Howard Pyle, a young boy named Tom embarks on a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. With the help of his loyal friends, Tom sets off on a dangerous journey filled with pirates, secret caves, and unexpected twists. As they navigate through treacherous waters and face numerous challenges, Tom and his companions must outsmart their adversaries and uncover the truth behind the stolen treasure.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Treasure Hunting
