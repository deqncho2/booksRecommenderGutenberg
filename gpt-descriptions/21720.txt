Synopsis:
In 'Charlie to the Rescue' by R. M. Ballantyne, a young boy named Charlie finds himself in a perilous situation when he becomes stranded on a deserted island. With only his wits and determination, Charlie must navigate the treacherous landscape, face dangerous creatures, and overcome numerous challenges in order to survive and find his way back home. Along the way, he forms unexpected alliances and discovers the true strength of friendship. Will Charlie's bravery and resourcefulness be enough to save him?

Genres:
Adventure, Survival, Children's Literature

Themes:
Courage, Friendship, Resilience
