Synopsis: 
'Bearslayer: A free translation from the unrhymed Latvian into English heroic verse' by Pumpurs, Andrejs is an epic tale that follows the adventures of a brave warrior named Bearslayer. Set in the mystical and dangerous world of ancient Latvia, Bearslayer embarks on a perilous journey to protect his people from a fearsome bear that threatens their existence. As he faces numerous challenges and battles, Bearslayer must confront his own inner demons and discover the true meaning of heroism.

Genres: Epic, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Courage, Identity, Mythology
