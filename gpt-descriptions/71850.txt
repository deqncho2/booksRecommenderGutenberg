Synopsis: 

"The Inquisition Revealed" by Thomas Timpson takes readers on a gripping journey through the dark and haunting history of the Inquisition. From its origins to its policies, cruelties, and the stories of its victims, this book delves into the depths of this infamous institution. Timpson explores the Inquisition's reach across various countries, including France, Spain, Portugal, Italy, England, and even India. Through detailed accounts and memoirs, the author sheds light on the horrors endured by those who fell victim to the Inquisition's relentless pursuit of power and control. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, True Crime, Biography

Themes: Religious persecution, Abuse of power, Human resilience
