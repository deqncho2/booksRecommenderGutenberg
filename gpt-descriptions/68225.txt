Synopsis: 

'Australian Fairy Tales' by Westbury Atha is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that transport readers to the magical world of Australia's folklore. From mischievous fairies to talking animals, these tales are filled with wonder, adventure, and unexpected twists. Journey through the vast Australian landscapes and discover the hidden secrets of the Outback, as well as the unique creatures that inhabit this mystical land. Each story is a delightful blend of imagination and cultural heritage, showcasing the rich tapestry of Australian folklore. 

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Nature, Cultural Heritage
