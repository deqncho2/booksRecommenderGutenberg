Synopsis: 

In "The Handbook to the Rivers and Broads of Norfolk & Suffolk," author G. Christopher Davies takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque waterways of Norfolk and Suffolk. With vivid descriptions and detailed maps, Davies unveils the hidden gems and enchanting landscapes that make these regions a boater's paradise. From quaint villages nestled along the riverbanks to the rich wildlife that thrives in these waters, this handbook is a treasure trove of information for anyone seeking to explore the beauty of Norfolk and Suffolk's rivers and broads.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Guidebook

Themes: Nature exploration, Boating, Local history and culture
