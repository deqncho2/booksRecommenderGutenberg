Synopsis: In 'The Imprudence of Prue' by Sophie Fisher, Prue, a young and spirited woman, finds herself caught in a web of secrets and scandal when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter. As she delves deeper into the intrigue, Prue must navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and societal expectations. With her reputation and future at stake, Prue must make daring choices that could either lead to her downfall or set her free.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and passion, societal expectations, secrets and betrayal
