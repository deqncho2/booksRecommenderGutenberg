Synopsis: In 'Nancy Brandon's Mystery' by Garis, Lilian, a young and curious Nancy Brandon finds herself embroiled in a perplexing mystery when she stumbles upon a hidden room in her family's old mansion. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the past, Nancy uncovers a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected connections that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each clue she uncovers, Nancy's determination to solve the mystery grows, leading her on a thrilling adventure filled with twists and turns.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Curiosity, Secrets, Family Legacy
