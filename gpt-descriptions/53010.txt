Synopsis: In 'The Last Abbot of Glastonbury: A Tale of the Dissolution of the Monasteries', author A. D. Crake takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of the Dissolution of the Monasteries in 16th century England. Set in the historic town of Glastonbury, the story follows the life of the last abbot, a man torn between his loyalty to his faith and his duty to protect his beloved abbey from destruction. As political and religious tensions rise, the abbot finds himself caught in a web of intrigue, betrayal, and forbidden love, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Faith and Loyalty, Power and Corruption, Love and Sacrifice
