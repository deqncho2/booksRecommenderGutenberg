Synopsis: In 'Seductio Ad Absurdum: The Principles & Practices of Seduction, A Beginner's Handbook' by Hahn, Emily, embark on a captivating journey into the art of seduction like never before. This unconventional guide takes readers on a wild ride through the absurd and unexpected, revealing the secrets to mastering the art of seduction in the most unconventional ways. From bizarre anecdotes to outlandish techniques, this handbook challenges conventional wisdom and offers a fresh perspective on the age-old game of attraction. Prepare to be both amused and enlightened as you delve into the world of seduction through a lens of absurdity.

Genres: Self-help, Humor, Non-fiction

Themes: Seduction, Relationships, Unconventional Wisdom
