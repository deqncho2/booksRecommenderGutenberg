Synopsis:
In "Alice Sit-By-The-Fire," the eccentric Grey family finds themselves in a whirlwind of chaos when their absent-minded father returns home after a long absence. As the family tries to adjust to his presence, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and unexpected alliances are formed. With witty dialogue and humorous situations, this play explores the complexities of family dynamics and the importance of communication and understanding.

Genres:
Comedy, Drama

Themes:
Family, Communication, Relationships
