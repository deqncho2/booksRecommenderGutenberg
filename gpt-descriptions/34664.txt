Synopsis: In 'The Mynns' Mystery' by George Manville Fenn, a young boy named Dick Mynn finds himself entangled in a thrilling mystery when he discovers a hidden room in his family's ancestral home. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the Mynns family, Dick uncovers a web of intrigue, deceit, and long-buried secrets that threaten to unravel his own life. With the help of his loyal friends, Dick must navigate through a series of puzzling clues and dangerous encounters to uncover the truth behind the Mynns' mysterious past.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Friendship, Courage
