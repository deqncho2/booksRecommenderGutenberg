Synopsis: In 'Putois: 1907' by France Anatole, we are introduced to the enigmatic character of Putois, a man with a peculiar talent for transforming himself into various animals. As he navigates through the streets of Paris, Putois encounters a colorful cast of characters, each with their own secrets and desires. With his extraordinary ability, Putois becomes entangled in a web of mystery, love, and deception, leading to unexpected consequences that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Transformation, Human Nature
