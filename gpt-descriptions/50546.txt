Synopsis: 

'Royal Winchester: Wanderings in and about the Ancient Capital of England' takes readers on a captivating journey through the historic city of Winchester. Written by A. G. K. L'Estrange, this book offers a delightful exploration of the city's rich heritage, hidden gems, and intriguing anecdotes. From the majestic Winchester Cathedral to the charming streets and landmarks, L'Estrange's vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling bring the city's past and present to life. As readers wander through the pages, they will uncover fascinating tales, discover lesser-known corners, and gain a deeper appreciation for the significance of Winchester in English history.

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Heritage, Exploration, Architecture, English History
