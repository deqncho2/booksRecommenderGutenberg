Synopsis: In the second volume of the thrilling Donald Ross of Heimra series by William Black, the captivating story continues as Donald Ross finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue on the remote Scottish island of Heimra. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the island's inhabitants, he uncovers shocking revelations that threaten to shatter the tranquility of Heimra forever. With his own life at stake, Donald must navigate treacherous waters and confront the dark forces that lurk beneath the surface. Will he be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Identity and Self-discovery, Power and Corruption
