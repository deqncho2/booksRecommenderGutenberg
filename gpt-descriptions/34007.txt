Synopsis: In 'The Postage Stamp in War' by Frederick John Melville, a seemingly ordinary postage stamp becomes the catalyst for a thrilling adventure that spans across war-torn landscapes and secret intelligence operations. As a young collector stumbles upon a rare stamp with a hidden message, he is unwittingly drawn into a dangerous world of espionage and intrigue. With his life on the line, he must navigate through a web of deception and betrayal, all while trying to unravel the mysteries behind the stamp and its significance in the midst of war.

Genres: Thriller, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Espionage, Identity, Sacrifice
