Synopsis: In the Fog by Davis, Richard Harding is a gripping mystery novel set in the foggy streets of London. When a wealthy businessman mysteriously disappears, his wife enlists the help of a renowned detective to uncover the truth. As the investigation unfolds, dark secrets and unexpected twists emerge, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
