Synopsis: 

In "Daisy Ashford: Her Book," we are transported into the imaginative world of a young girl named Daisy. Through her innocent and charming storytelling, we are introduced to a delightful cast of characters and their whimsical adventures. From mischievous fairies to talking animals, Daisy's vivid imagination knows no bounds. As we follow her journey, we are reminded of the magic and wonder that can be found in the simplest of things. 

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Imagination, Innocence, Wonder
