Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The Broken Font: A Story of the Civil War', the gripping tale continues as the characters navigate the treacherous landscape of the war-torn United States. Amidst the chaos and uncertainty, friendships are tested, loyalties are questioned, and sacrifices must be made. As the conflict reaches its climax, the characters find themselves facing impossible choices that will shape their futures and the fate of the nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Sacrifice, Friendship, Identity, Resilience
