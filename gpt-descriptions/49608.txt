Synopsis: 
In this captivating collection, William Butler Yeats showcases his poetic genius through a diverse range of lyrical and narrative poems. From enchanting love ballads to haunting tales of the supernatural, Yeats takes readers on a journey through the depths of human emotions and the mysteries of the world. With his evocative imagery and profound insights, he explores themes of love, loss, mythology, and the complexities of the human condition. This volume is a testament to Yeats' mastery of language and his ability to transport readers to a realm where beauty and truth intertwine.

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Mythology

Themes: Love, Loss, Mythology, Human emotions, Supernatural
