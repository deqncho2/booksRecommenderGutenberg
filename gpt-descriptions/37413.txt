Synopsis: In 'The Duke Decides' by Hill, Headon, a powerful and enigmatic duke finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and deception as he navigates the treacherous world of high society. With his reputation and fortune at stake, the duke must make a series of difficult decisions that will not only determine his own fate but also the future of those around him. As secrets unravel and alliances shift, the duke's true character is put to the test, revealing unexpected depths and surprising consequences.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Power and Responsibility, Betrayal, Identity
