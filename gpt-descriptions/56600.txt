Synopsis: 

In "Lewis Arundel; Or, The Railroad Of Life" by Frank E. Smedley, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the life of the titular character, Lewis Arundel. From his humble beginnings as an orphan to his rise in the bustling world of railway engineering, Arundel's story is one of ambition, love, and the pursuit of success. As he navigates the challenges and triumphs of his career, Arundel finds himself entangled in a web of personal and professional relationships that will test his character and resilience. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the complexities of human nature and the choices we make in the pursuit of our dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Love, Success, Personal Growth
