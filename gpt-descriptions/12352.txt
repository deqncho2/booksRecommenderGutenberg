Synopsis: In 'Iola Leroy; Or, Shadows Uplifted', we follow the life of Iola Leroy, a young woman of mixed race who is determined to find her place in a society deeply divided by race and prejudice. From her childhood in the South to her education in the North, Iola faces numerous challenges and obstacles, but she remains steadfast in her pursuit of justice and equality. As she navigates through love, loss, and the complexities of identity, Iola Leroy becomes a symbol of resilience and hope in a world that seeks to keep her in the shadows.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Bildungsroman, Social Commentary

Themes: Racial identity, Social justice, Resilience, Education, Love and relationships
