Synopsis: In 'The Green God' by Frederic Arnold Kummer, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization hidden deep within the Amazon rainforest. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of this lost world, he encounters treacherous landscapes, deadly creatures, and a powerful deity worshipped by the indigenous people. With danger lurking at every turn, the archaeologist must navigate through a web of intrigue and betrayal to unlock the truth that could change the course of history.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Ancient civilizations, Survival
