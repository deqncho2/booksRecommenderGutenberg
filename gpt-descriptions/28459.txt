Synopsis: 
In 'In Brief Authority' by F. Anstey, a seemingly ordinary man suddenly finds himself in a position of great power and authority. As he navigates the challenges and temptations that come with his newfound status, he must grapple with the consequences of his actions and the impact they have on those around him. With humor and wit, Anstey explores the complexities of human nature and the allure of authority, leaving readers questioning the true nature of power and its effects on individuals and society.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Power, Authority, Human Nature, Consequences
