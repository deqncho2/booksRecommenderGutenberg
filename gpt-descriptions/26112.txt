Synopsis: In 'A Tar-Heel Baron' by Mabell S. C. Smith, a young man named John finds himself unexpectedly inheriting a vast fortune and a grand estate in the heart of North Carolina. As he navigates the challenges of his newfound wealth and status, John becomes entangled in a web of family secrets, forbidden love, and the dark history of his ancestral home. With each twist and turn, John must confront his own identity and make difficult choices that will shape his future. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Tar Heel State, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Inheritance, Identity, Love and Betrayal, Family Secrets
