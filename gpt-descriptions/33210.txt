Synopsis: In 'The Frontier Boys in the Grand Canyon; Or, A Search for Treasure', the adventurous Frontier Boys embark on a thrilling journey to the majestic Grand Canyon. Drawn by the allure of hidden treasure, they face perilous challenges, encounter mysterious characters, and uncover secrets that will test their courage and loyalty. As they navigate the treacherous terrain, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to unravel the enigma of the Grand Canyon and its hidden riches.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Bravery, Discovery
