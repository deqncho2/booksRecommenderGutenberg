Synopsis: 
In 'Patty's Success' by Carolyn Wells, readers are introduced to Patty Fairfield, a young woman with dreams of achieving success in her chosen career. As she navigates the challenges and opportunities that come her way, Patty's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. With a cast of intriguing characters and unexpected twists, this story takes readers on a journey filled with ambition, romance, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Determination, Love, Self-discovery
