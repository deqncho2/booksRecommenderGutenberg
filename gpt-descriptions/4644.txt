Synopsis: 
In "The Adventures of Mr. Verdant Green" by Bede, Cuthbert, we follow the misadventures of the eponymous Mr. Verdant Green, a naive and inexperienced university student. As he navigates the unfamiliar world of academia, he finds himself entangled in a series of humorous and often embarrassing situations. From awkward social encounters to comical mishaps, Mr. Verdant Green's journey is filled with laughter, self-discovery, and unexpected friendships. Will he manage to survive the trials and tribulations of university life with his dignity intact?

Genres: 
Comedy, Coming-of-age, Satire

Themes: 
Naivety, Social awkwardness, Friendship, Self-discovery
