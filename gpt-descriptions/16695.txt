Synopsis: 

'Fighting Instructions, 1530-1816: Publications of the Navy Records Society Vol. XXIX' by Julian Stafford Corbett takes readers on a captivating journey through centuries of naval warfare. This volume presents a collection of historical documents that provide invaluable insights into the strategies, tactics, and instructions employed by naval commanders during this period. From the early days of sail to the age of gunpowder, readers will discover the evolution of naval warfare and the challenges faced by sailors and officers alike. 

Genres: History, Military, Non-fiction

Themes: Naval warfare, Strategy and tactics, Historical documents
