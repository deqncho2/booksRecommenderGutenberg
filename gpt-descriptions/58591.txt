Synopsis: In 'Hygiene: a manual of personal and public health (New Edition)', Sir Arthur Newsholme delves into the fascinating world of hygiene, offering a comprehensive guide to maintaining personal well-being and promoting public health. From the importance of cleanliness to the prevention of disease, this manual covers a wide range of topics, providing practical advice and scientific insights. With engaging anecdotes and thought-provoking examples, Newsholme explores the intricate relationship between individual hygiene and the overall well-being of society. This new edition is a must-read for anyone seeking to understand the vital role hygiene plays in our daily lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Health, Science

Themes: Personal hygiene, Public health, Disease prevention, Well-being
