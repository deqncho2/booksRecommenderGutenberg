Synopsis: In 'Vendetta: A Story of One Forgotten' by Marie Corelli, a mysterious and enigmatic woman named Vendetta enters the lives of two young lovers, forever altering their destinies. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, passions ignite, and a haunting past threatens to consume them all. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this tale of love, revenge, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Gothic fiction, romance, mystery

Themes: Love and obsession, revenge, the power of secrets
