Synopsis: 
In 'Ginger-Snaps' by Fern, Fanny, a young woman named Ginger finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious photograph. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Ginger's life takes a thrilling turn, leading her on a dangerous journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. With each revelation, Ginger must confront her own past and make choices that will determine her future. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Identity
