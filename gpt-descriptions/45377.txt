Synopsis: 
In 'All About Dogs: A Book for Doggy People' by Lane, Charles Henry, embark on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of dogs. From their ancient origins to their unique behaviors and remarkable abilities, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of our beloved canine companions. Discover the secrets behind their unwavering loyalty, their incredible senses, and the deep bond they share with humans. Filled with heartwarming anecdotes, intriguing facts, and stunning photographs, this book is a must-read for anyone who has ever been touched by the magic of dogs.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Studies, Pet Care

Themes: Canine companionship, Animal intelligence, Human-animal bond
