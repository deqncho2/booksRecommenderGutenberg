Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Fergus Hume' is a collection of gripping and mysterious tales penned by the renowned author Fergus Hume. This compilation includes a variety of intriguing stories that will captivate readers with their suspenseful plots, unexpected twists, and complex characters. From murder mysteries to thrilling adventures, each narrative takes readers on a thrilling journey through the depths of human nature and the intricacies of crime. With Hume's masterful storytelling, this collection promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind each enigma.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Murder, Deception, Intrigue
