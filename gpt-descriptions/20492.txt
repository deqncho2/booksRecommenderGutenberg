Synopsis: In "Terry; Or, She ought to have been a Boy," we follow the captivating journey of Terry, a spirited young girl who defies societal expectations and challenges gender norms in Victorian-era Ireland. With her adventurous spirit and determination, Terry embarks on a series of thrilling escapades that will test her resilience and challenge the traditional roles assigned to women. As she navigates through a world dominated by men, Terry's story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover her fate.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Gender Roles, Identity, Independence, Societal Expectations
