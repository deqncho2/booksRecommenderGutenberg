Synopsis: 
In the remote future, a group of brilliant scientists and engineers operate a space station called Venus Equilateral, located at the point in space where the gravitational forces of the Sun and Venus balance perfectly. As they navigate the challenges of maintaining communication between Earth and the outer planets, they encounter a mysterious signal that could change the course of human history. With tensions rising and the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, the crew of Venus Equilateral must unravel the enigma before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of outer space, Communication and technology, Human ingenuity and collaboration
