Synopsis: In 'The Motor Boys on Road and River; Or, Racing To Save a Life' by Clarence Young, three adventurous friends find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey that takes them from the open road to treacherous river rapids. When a mysterious message leads them to believe that a life is in danger, the Motor Boys must rely on their wits, courage, and their trusty motorboat to navigate dangerous obstacles and solve a perplexing mystery. With each twist and turn, the boys face exhilarating challenges that test their friendship and push them to their limits. Will they be able to save a life before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Perseverance
