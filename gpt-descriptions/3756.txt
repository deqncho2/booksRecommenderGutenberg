Synopsis:
'Indiscretions of Archie' follows the misadventures of Archie Moffam, a well-meaning but perpetually bumbling young man who finds himself entangled in a series of hilarious and increasingly absurd situations. From mistaken identities to romantic entanglements, Archie's life becomes a whirlwind of chaos as he tries to navigate the complexities of love, family, and social expectations. With a cast of eccentric characters and Wodehouse's signature wit, this delightful comedy of errors will keep readers laughing from start to finish.

Genres:
Comedy, Satire, Romance

Themes:
Love and relationships, Social expectations, Mistaken identities
