Synopsis: In "How John Became a Man: Life Story of a Motherless Boy," we follow the journey of John, a young boy who is forced to navigate the challenges of life without a mother. From his early years filled with heartache and loss to his determination to find his place in the world, John's story is one of resilience, self-discovery, and the power of love. As he encounters various obstacles and forms unexpected connections, John's transformation into a man is a testament to the strength of the human spirit and the enduring bonds of family.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Family

Themes: Loss and grief, Resilience, Self-discovery, Family bonds
