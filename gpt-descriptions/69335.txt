Synopsis: On a snowy Christmas Eve, the quaint Irish pub, Mulligan's, becomes the backdrop for a heartwarming tale of love, forgiveness, and second chances. As the night unfolds, a diverse group of patrons find themselves drawn together by fate, each carrying their own burdens and secrets. Amidst the laughter, tears, and a touch of magic, they discover the true meaning of Christmas and the power of human connection.

Genres: Contemporary Fiction, Romance, Holiday

Themes: Love, Forgiveness, Redemption, Community, Christmas Spirit
