Synopsis: In "Rejected of Men: A Story of To-day" by Howard Pyle, we are introduced to a young man named John, who finds himself caught between the allure of wealth and the pursuit of true happiness. As he navigates the complexities of society, John becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a world where appearances can be deceiving and the consequences of one's choices can be life-altering. Will John find redemption and discover the true meaning of success, or will he be forever rejected by those he once held dear?

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Wealth and Happiness, Deception and Betrayal, Redemption and Self-discovery
