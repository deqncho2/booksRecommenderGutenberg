Synopsis: 
'The Rogerenes: some hitherto unpublished annals belonging to the colonial history of Connecticut' delves into the captivating and untold story of the Rogerenes, a religious sect that emerged during the colonial era in Connecticut. Through a collection of unpublished annals, the book uncovers the intriguing lives and beliefs of this enigmatic group, shedding light on their struggles, conflicts, and ultimate impact on the history of the region. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be drawn into a world of religious dissent, social upheaval, and the quest for individual freedom, making this a compelling exploration of a forgotten chapter in American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Religious studies

Themes: Religious dissent, Social conflict, Individual freedom, Colonial history
