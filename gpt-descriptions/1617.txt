Synopsis: In 'The Wind in the Rose-Bush, and Other Stories of the Supernatural', Mary Eleanor Wilkins Freeman weaves a collection of eerie and captivating tales that will send shivers down your spine. From haunted houses and ghostly apparitions to mysterious curses and unexplained phenomena, each story delves into the realm of the supernatural, leaving readers on the edge of their seats. With her masterful storytelling and vivid descriptions, Freeman explores the darker side of human nature and the inexplicable forces that lurk in the shadows. Prepare to be enthralled and unsettled as you journey through these haunting tales.

Genres: Supernatural fiction, Horror, Mystery

Themes: Ghosts and spirits, Psychological suspense, The power of the unknown
