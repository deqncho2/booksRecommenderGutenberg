Synopsis: In 'Poppy Ott's pedigreed pickles' by Edwards, Leo, a young boy named Poppy Ott stumbles upon a mysterious secret recipe for pickles that could change his life forever. With the help of his friends, Poppy embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns, as he tries to protect the recipe from falling into the wrong hands. As the stakes rise, Poppy must rely on his wit, courage, and the power of friendship to overcome the challenges that come his way.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Secrets
