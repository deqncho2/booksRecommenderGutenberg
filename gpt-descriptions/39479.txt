Synopsis: In the small town of Killsbury, the residents eagerly prepare for their annual Independence Day celebration. However, this year's festivities take a terrifying turn when a series of mysterious and gruesome events unfold, turning the town into a nightmare. As the residents struggle to survive, they uncover dark secrets and face unimaginable horrors that threaten to consume them all.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Secrets, Small-town terror
