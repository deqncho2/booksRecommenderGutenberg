Synopsis: In "Old Kensington," Anne Thackeray Ritchie takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of the Kensington family. Set in the elegant streets of Victorian London, the story revolves around the struggles and secrets of a widowed mother and her two adult children. As the family grapples with societal expectations, love, and the pursuit of happiness, they are forced to confront their own desires and the consequences of their choices. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, "Old Kensington" offers a poignant exploration of family dynamics, societal pressures, and the pursuit of personal fulfillment.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Family relationships, societal expectations, love and marriage, personal fulfillment
