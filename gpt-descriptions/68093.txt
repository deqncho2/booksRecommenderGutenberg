Synopsis: 

In "Keeping one cow: Being the experience of a number of practical writers, in a clear and condensed form, upon the management of a single milch cow," readers are taken on a fascinating journey into the world of dairy farming. This concise and practical guide brings together the collective wisdom of experienced writers, offering valuable insights and advice on the art of managing a single milch cow. From selecting the right breed to ensuring proper nutrition and health, this book covers all aspects of cow care, making it an indispensable resource for both novice and seasoned farmers. With its engaging anecdotes and practical tips, "Keeping one cow" is a captivating exploration of the joys and challenges of tending to these gentle creatures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Agriculture, Animal Husbandry

Themes: Farming, Livestock Management, Self-sufficiency
