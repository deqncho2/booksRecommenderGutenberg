Synopsis: In "The Half-Back: A Story of School, Football, and Golf," we follow the journey of a young athlete named Jack, who finds himself torn between his passion for football and his unexpected talent for golf. As he navigates the challenges of high school, friendships, and the pressures of sports, Jack must make difficult choices that will shape his future. With a captivating blend of sportsmanship, coming-of-age, and unexpected twists, this story takes readers on a thrilling ride through the world of competitive athletics.

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Pursuing one's passion, Friendship, Identity, Overcoming obstacles
