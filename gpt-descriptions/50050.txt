Synopsis: 

In 'The Sea-girt Fortress: A Story of Heligoland', Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a thrilling adventure set in the treacherous waters surrounding the island of Heligoland. When a young boy named Jack finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous secret, he must navigate through perilous situations and face unexpected challenges. As he unravels the mysteries of the sea-girt fortress, Jack discovers the true power of courage and loyalty, while uncovering a hidden world that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Secrets, Exploration
