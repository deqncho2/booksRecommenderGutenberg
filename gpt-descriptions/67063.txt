Synopsis: 

In "Cottage Scenes During the Cholera: Being Extracts from a Diary Written in July and August, 1832," William Isaac Coppard takes us on a gripping journey through the harrowing days of a cholera outbreak. Through the eyes of a diary writer, we witness the devastating impact of the disease on a small village and its inhabitants. As fear and panic spread, the diary entries reveal the resilience and courage of the community as they face the horrors of the epidemic. With vivid descriptions and emotional depth, this poignant account offers a unique perspective on a dark chapter in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Diary, Medical Drama

Themes: Disease Outbreak, Community Resilience, Human Spirit
