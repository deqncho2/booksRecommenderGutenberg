Synopsis: In 'Tom, The Bootblack; or, The Road to Success' by Horatio Alger, Jr., we follow the journey of Tom, a young bootblack with big dreams. Tom's life takes an unexpected turn when he encounters a mysterious stranger who offers him an opportunity that could change his life forever. As Tom navigates the challenges and obstacles that come his way, he must learn to trust his instincts and make difficult choices that will shape his future. Will Tom be able to overcome adversity and find his path to success?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Rags-to-riches

Themes: Perseverance, Self-belief, Social mobility
