Synopsis: In "An Elementary Treatise on Electricity," James Clerk Maxwell takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious world of electricity. With a blend of scientific rigor and engaging storytelling, Maxwell unravels the secrets of this powerful force, exploring its origins, properties, and practical applications. From the electrifying experiments of ancient civilizations to the cutting-edge discoveries of his own time, Maxwell's treatise offers a comprehensive and accessible exploration of electricity that will leave readers electrified with knowledge.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction, Educational

Themes: Electricity, Scientific inquiry, Historical development of electricity, Practical applications of electricity
