Synopsis: In 'The Hollow of Her Hand' by George Barr McCutcheon, a young woman named Kate is caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she unexpectedly inherits a fortune. As she navigates the complexities of her newfound wealth, she becomes entangled with a charming con artist, a cunning detective, and a cast of colorful characters. With her life hanging in the balance, Kate must rely on her wit and intuition to uncover the truth and protect her inheritance.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Deception, Wealth and Power, Identity
