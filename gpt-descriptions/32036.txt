Synopsis: In 'The Unprotected Species' by Sturgis, Melvin, a renowned wildlife photographer embarks on a dangerous expedition to capture the elusive and endangered Snow Leopard in the treacherous mountains of the Himalayas. As he navigates through the harsh terrain and faces unexpected challenges, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue that goes beyond the realm of wildlife conservation. With each step closer to the Snow Leopard, he unravels a dark secret that could change the course of his career and the fate of the endangered species forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Wildlife conservation, Environmentalism, Human greed
