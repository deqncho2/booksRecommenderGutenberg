Synopsis: 

'Legends of Saints & Sinners. Collected and Translated from the Irish' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and mythology of Ireland. This collection of enchanting tales unveils the extraordinary lives of saints and sinners, weaving together stories of bravery, redemption, and the eternal struggle between good and evil. From the mystical landscapes of ancient Ireland to the bustling streets of modern-day Dublin, these legends transport readers to a world where mythical creatures roam, miracles happen, and the boundaries between the mortal and the divine blur. With each story, the reader is drawn deeper into the intricate tapestry of Irish mythology, discovering the hidden truths and timeless wisdom that lie within. 

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Fantasy

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Redemption, Mythical Creatures, Irish Culture and History
