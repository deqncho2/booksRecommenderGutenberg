Synopsis: In 'Back to Methuselah: A Metabiological Pentateuch', Shaw takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the potential of human evolution and the consequences of immortality. From the Garden of Eden to a distant future, the story unfolds with intriguing characters and thought-provoking dialogues, challenging our understanding of life, love, and the pursuit of knowledge. As the narrative unfolds, Shaw weaves together a tapestry of ideas that will leave readers questioning the very nature of existence.

Genres: Science Fiction, Drama, Satire

Themes: Evolution, Immortality, Human Nature, Knowledge, Love
