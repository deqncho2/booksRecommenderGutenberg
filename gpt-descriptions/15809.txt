Synopsis: In 'A Apple Pie' by Greenaway, Kate, embark on a delightful journey through the alphabet as you follow the adventures of a little girl and her apple pie. From the bustling streets of a charming village to the cozy comforts of home, each letter of the alphabet is beautifully illustrated with whimsical scenes that will captivate readers of all ages. Discover the surprising twists and turns that await as the little girl's apple pie becomes the center of attention in this enchanting tale.

Genres: Children's literature, Picture book

Themes: Alphabet, Adventure, Everyday objects
