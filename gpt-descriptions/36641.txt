Synopsis: 

'Women Novelists of Queen Victoria's Reign: A Book of Appreciations' is a captivating collection of essays written by prominent female authors of the Victorian era. In this anthology, Edna Lyall, Charlotte M. Yonge, Mrs. Margaret Oliphant, E. Lynn Linton, Mrs. Alexander, Adeline Sergeant, Emma Marshall, Katharine S. Macquoid, and Louisa Parr pay tribute to the remarkable women novelists who flourished during Queen Victoria's reign. Through insightful analysis and heartfelt appreciation, these authors shed light on the literary achievements and societal impact of their female counterparts, offering a fascinating glimpse into the world of Victorian literature.

Genres: Literary criticism, Biography, History

Themes: Women's literature, Victorian society, Female empowerment
