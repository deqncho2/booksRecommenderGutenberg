Synopsis: In 'The Story of Great Inventions' by Burns, Elmer Ellsworth, embark on a captivating journey through time as you discover the fascinating stories behind some of the world's most groundbreaking inventions. From the invention of the wheel to the creation of electricity, this book unveils the ingenuity and perseverance of inventors who changed the course of history. Delve into the trials and triumphs of these brilliant minds as they revolutionize the way we live, work, and communicate. Prepare to be inspired by the incredible stories that shaped our modern world.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Biography

Themes: Innovation, Human Ingenuity, Technological Advancement
