Synopsis: 
In 'A Master Of Craft' by W.W. Jacobs, a young man named Dick Stapleton finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he takes on a job at a seemingly ordinary London office. As he delves deeper into the secrets of his enigmatic employer, Stapleton becomes embroiled in a dangerous game of deception and manipulation. With his life hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, where nothing is as it seems. Will Stapleton uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Manipulation, Identity
