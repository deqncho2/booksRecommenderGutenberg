Synopsis: In 'The Young Guard' by E. W. Hornung, a group of young friends find themselves entangled in a dangerous web of espionage and intrigue during World War I. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of war-torn Europe, they must rely on their wit, courage, and loyalty to protect their country and each other. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Friendship, Sacrifice
