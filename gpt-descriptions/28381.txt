Synopsis: In 'Ben, the Luggage Boy; Or, Among the Wharves', follow the captivating journey of young Ben as he navigates the bustling world of the wharves. From his humble beginnings as a luggage boy, Ben encounters a cast of intriguing characters and faces numerous challenges that test his resilience and determination. As he strives to rise above his circumstances, Ben's story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Social mobility, Friendship
