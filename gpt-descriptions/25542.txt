Synopsis: In the bustling world of early 20th century New York, a mysterious woman appears, defying the boundaries of time and age. As she captivates the city's elite with her youthful beauty and enigmatic charm, questions arise about her true identity and the secret behind her eternal youth. Set against a backdrop of love, ambition, and societal expectations, "Black Oxen" delves into the complexities of human desire and the pursuit of eternal youth.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Aging and mortality, Love and desire, Identity and self-discovery
