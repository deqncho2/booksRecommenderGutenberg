Synopsis: 

'English Dialects From the Eighth Century to the Present Day' by Walter W. Skeat takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of English dialects, spanning from the eighth century to modern times. Delving into the rich tapestry of linguistic variations across different regions and time periods, Skeat explores the fascinating origins, influences, and transformations that have shaped the English language as we know it today. From the Old English dialects of the Anglo-Saxon era to the diverse accents found in contemporary English-speaking communities, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the ever-changing nature of language.

Genres: Linguistics, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Language evolution, Regional identity, Historical linguistics
