Synopsis: 
In "Principles of Home Decoration, With Practical Examples" by Candace Wheeler, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the art of home decoration. With a blend of insightful advice and practical examples, Wheeler offers a fresh perspective on transforming living spaces into havens of beauty and comfort. From selecting color schemes to arranging furniture, this book provides a wealth of inspiration and guidance for creating a truly enchanting home. Whether you're a seasoned decorator or a novice looking to enhance your living space, Wheeler's expertise and creativity will leave you eager to embark on your own home decoration projects.

Genres: Home improvement, Interior design, Non-fiction

Themes: Beauty in the home, Creativity in decoration, Practicality in design
