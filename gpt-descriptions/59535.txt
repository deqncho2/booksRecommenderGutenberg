Synopsis: In 'Project Hi-Psi' by Riley Frank, a brilliant scientist embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that pushes the boundaries of human potential. As the project unfolds, unexpected consequences arise, leading to a thrilling journey of discovery, danger, and moral dilemmas. With high stakes and a race against time, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the limits of science and the power of the human mind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Human potential, Ethical dilemmas, Scientific exploration
