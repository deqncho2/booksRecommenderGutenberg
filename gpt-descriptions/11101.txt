Synopsis: In Coleridge's Ancient Mariner and Select Poems, embark on a mesmerizing journey through the depths of the human soul and the vastness of the natural world. From the haunting tale of a cursed mariner to the enchanting verses that explore love, nature, and the mysteries of existence, this collection of poems will transport you to a realm where imagination and reality intertwine. Prepare to be captivated by Coleridge's lyrical prowess and his ability to evoke profound emotions through his words.

Genres: Poetry, Gothic literature, Romanticism

Themes: Nature, spirituality, guilt, redemption, the supernatural
