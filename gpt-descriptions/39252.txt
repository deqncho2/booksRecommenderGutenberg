Synopsis: In "The Near East: Dalmatia, Greece and Constantinople," Hichens takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and vibrant cultures of the Near East. From the sun-soaked shores of Dalmatia to the ancient ruins of Greece and the bustling streets of Constantinople, the author weaves a tapestry of vivid descriptions, intriguing encounters, and unexpected twists. As the protagonist immerses themselves in the region's rich history, they become entangled in a web of secrets, love affairs, and political intrigue, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical fiction, Travelogue, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Intrigue, Love and passion
