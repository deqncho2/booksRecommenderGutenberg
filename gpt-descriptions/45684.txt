Synopsis: In 'My Mamie Rose: The Story of My Regeneration' by Kildare, Owen, we are introduced to the captivating life of Mamie Rose, a woman who defies societal norms and embarks on a journey of self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of a changing world, Mamie Rose's story takes us through her trials and triumphs, as she navigates love, loss, and the pursuit of her true identity. With vivid storytelling and unforgettable characters, this novel explores the depths of human resilience and the power of personal transformation.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Love and Loss, Societal Expectations
