Synopsis: 
In 'Lessons in Sabre, Singlestick, Sabre & Bayonet, and Sword Feats: or, How to use a cut and thrust sword', author J. M. Waite takes readers on an exhilarating journey into the world of swordsmanship. Through detailed instructions and captivating anecdotes, Waite unveils the secrets of mastering the art of combat with a cut and thrust sword. From the elegant finesse of the sabre to the swift and deadly movements of the bayonet, this book offers a comprehensive guide to wielding these weapons with skill and precision. Whether you are a seasoned swordsman or a curious novice, this enthralling manual will transport you to a realm of bravery, strategy, and honor.

Genres: Non-fiction, Martial Arts, Historical

Themes: Swordsmanship, Combat Techniques, Historical Warfare
