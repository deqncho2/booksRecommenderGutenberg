Synopsis:
In "The Old Countess; or, The Two Proposals" by Ann S. Stephens, a mysterious and enigmatic old countess finds herself at the center of two unexpected proposals. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the countess's true identity becomes a matter of great intrigue. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot filled with twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres:
Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Identity, Love and Betrayal, Secrets and Deception
