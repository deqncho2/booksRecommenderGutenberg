Synopsis: In 'The Way to Peace' by Margaret Wade Campbell Deland, a young woman named Mary finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is forced to choose between two very different suitors. As she navigates the complexities of love and societal expectations, Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery that will challenge her beliefs and ultimately lead her to a path she never expected.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and sacrifice, societal expectations, self-discovery
