Synopsis: 
In the small coastal town of Pelican Pool, secrets run deep and the past refuses to stay buried. When a mysterious newcomer arrives, the lives of the town's residents are thrown into disarray, as long-held grudges and hidden desires come to the surface. As tensions rise and the truth threatens to unravel, the inhabitants of Pelican Pool must confront their own demons and make choices that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, redemption, the power of forgiveness, the consequences of one's actions
