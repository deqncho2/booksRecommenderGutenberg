Synopsis: In the enchanting world of 18th-century France, Mademoiselle Olympe Zabriski, a captivating and mysterious young woman, finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, romance, and intrigue. As she navigates the glittering halls of Versailles and the shadowy corners of Paris, Olympe's journey takes her from the heights of society to the depths of her own heart. With her wit, charm, and determination, she must unravel the mysteries that surround her and discover her true destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Love, Power, Deception
