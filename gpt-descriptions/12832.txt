Synopsis: In 'Said the Observer' by Louis J. Stellman, a renowned journalist finds himself entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger when he stumbles upon a mysterious photograph. As he delves deeper into the secrets it holds, he becomes a target, racing against time to uncover the truth before it consumes him. With unexpected twists and a relentless pursuit, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Conspiracy, Journalism, Truth
