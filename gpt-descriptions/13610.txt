Synopsis: 

In 'Studies in the Psychology of Sex, Volume 1: The Evolution of Modesty; The Phenomena of Sexual Periodicity; Auto-Erotism', Havelock Ellis delves into the intriguing and often misunderstood aspects of human sexuality. Exploring the evolution of modesty, the author uncovers the origins and development of this complex social construct. Additionally, Ellis examines the fascinating phenomena of sexual periodicity, shedding light on the cyclical nature of human desire. Lastly, the book delves into the realm of auto-erotism, exploring the intricacies of self-pleasure and its psychological implications. 

Genres: Psychology, Sexuality, Non-fiction

Themes: Human sexuality, Modesty, Sexual desire, Self-pleasure
