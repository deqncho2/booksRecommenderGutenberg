Synopsis: In 'Con-Fen' by James R. Adams, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Evelyn Foster discovers a groundbreaking technology that allows her to enter the dreams of others. As she delves deeper into this mysterious realm, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and deception, where nothing is as it seems. With each dream she enters, Dr. Foster uncovers shocking secrets and hidden agendas, forcing her to question her own sanity and the true nature of reality. As the line between dreams and reality blurs, she must navigate a dangerous game of manipulation and betrayal to uncover the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Dreams and reality, Identity, Deception, Psychological exploration
