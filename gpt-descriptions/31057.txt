Synopsis: In 'The Wishing Moon' by Louise Elizabeth Dutton, a young woman named Lily discovers a mysterious moon-shaped pendant that grants her deepest desires. As she explores the power of the pendant, she realizes that every wish comes with a consequence, leading her on a thrilling and dangerous journey. With each wish, Lily must confront the moral implications of her desires and make choices that will shape her future. As the line between fantasy and reality blurs, she must navigate the consequences of her actions and discover the true nature of her own desires.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Power of wishes, Morality, Self-discovery
