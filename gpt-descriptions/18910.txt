Synopsis: 

In 'Diary of a Nursing Sister on the Western Front, 1914-1915', an anonymous nursing sister takes us on a gripping and emotional journey through the harrowing realities of war. Through her vivid and heartfelt diary entries, we witness the daily struggles, triumphs, and heartbreaks experienced by the brave nurses on the front lines of World War I. From the chaos of the battlefield to the makeshift hospitals, the nursing sister's account offers a unique perspective on the toll of war on both the soldiers and those who cared for them.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War Literature

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, Sacrifice and bravery, Human resilience and compassion
