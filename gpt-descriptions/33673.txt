Synopsis: In "The King of the Golden River," two wicked brothers, Hans and Schwartz, live a life of greed and cruelty in the beautiful Stirian Alps. When they encounter a mysterious visitor who offers them a chance at redemption, they embark on a perilous journey to find the legendary King of the Golden River. Along the way, they face numerous challenges and learn valuable lessons about the power of kindness, selflessness, and the consequences of their actions.

Genres: Fairy tale, fantasy, allegory

Themes: Redemption, greed, kindness, selflessness, consequences of actions
