Synopsis: In this thought-provoking dialogue, John and Thomas engage in a passionate discussion on the pressing issues of their time, including the controversial Corn Laws, the Charter, Teetotalism, and the potential solution to the current distresses. As they delve into these topics, their contrasting perspectives and lively arguments shed light on the complexities of society and the search for remedies. 

Genres: Political discourse, social commentary, philosophical dialogue

Themes: Economic inequality, political reform, social justice, moral responsibility
