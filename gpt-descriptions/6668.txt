Synopsis: In 'Annette, the Metis Spy: A Heroine of the N.W. Rebellion', we are introduced to Annette, a courageous and resourceful young woman who finds herself caught in the midst of the North-West Rebellion. As tensions rise and the battle for land and rights escalates, Annette becomes a vital asset to the Metis resistance, using her intelligence and wit to gather crucial information for their cause. But as she delves deeper into the dangerous world of espionage, Annette must navigate treacherous alliances and face personal sacrifices that will test her loyalty and determination. This gripping historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through a pivotal moment in Canadian history, showcasing the bravery and resilience of a remarkable heroine.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Spy Thriller

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Identity, Resistance
