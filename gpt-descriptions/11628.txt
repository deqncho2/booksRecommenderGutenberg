Synopsis: In "Gossip in a Library" by Edmund Gosse, readers are invited into the enchanting world of literature as the author takes them on a journey through various libraries, sharing intriguing anecdotes and captivating stories about the books and their authors. From the hidden secrets of rare manuscripts to the scandalous lives of famous writers, Gosse's witty and insightful observations bring these literary treasures to life, making this a delightful exploration for book lovers and history enthusiasts alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, Biography

Themes: Literature, History, Anecdotes, Authorship
