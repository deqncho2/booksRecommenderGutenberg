Synopsis: In the small coastal town of King's Cove, a mysterious smuggler lurks in the shadows, operating under the cover of darkness. When a young boy stumbles upon an old chapel with a hidden secret, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he uncovers a plot that could change the fate of the entire town. With the help of his friends, he must navigate treacherous waters and outsmart the cunning smuggler before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Friendship and loyalty, Courage and bravery
