Synopsis: In the small town of Badger, located in the Southwest Borderland, a new sheriff takes charge, bringing with him a mysterious past and a determination to restore law and order. As he delves into the dark underbelly of the town, he uncovers a web of corruption, smuggling, and violence that threatens to tear the community apart. With danger lurking at every turn, the sheriff must navigate treacherous alliances and confront his own demons to protect the town and its inhabitants. 

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Justice, Redemption, Corruption
