Synopsis: 
In 'Swatty: A Story of Real Boys' by Butler, Ellis Parker, readers are introduced to a group of mischievous boys who embark on a series of thrilling adventures in their small town. Led by the charismatic and fearless Swatty, the boys find themselves caught up in a web of mystery and danger as they uncover hidden secrets and face unexpected challenges. From daring escapades to unexpected friendships, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to see what happens next.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
