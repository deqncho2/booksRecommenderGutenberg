Synopsis: In "Ran away from the Dutch: Or, Borneo from South to North," follow the thrilling journey of a young adventurer who escapes the clutches of the Dutch colonial rule in Borneo. Filled with danger, intrigue, and breathtaking landscapes, this gripping tale takes readers on a wild ride through the heart of the island, as our protagonist encounters indigenous tribes, battles treacherous terrain, and uncovers hidden secrets. Will he find freedom or succumb to the perils that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Travelogue

Themes: Colonialism, Survival, Exploration
