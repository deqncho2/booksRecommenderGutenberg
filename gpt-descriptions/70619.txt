Synopsis: In 'A History of Criticism and Literary Taste in Europe, Volume 1', George Saintsbury takes readers on a captivating journey through the classical and medieval periods of European literature. From the earliest texts to the present day, Saintsbury explores the evolution of literary criticism and the ever-changing tastes of readers. Delving into the works of renowned writers and thinkers, he uncovers the fascinating debates and controversies that have shaped the way we perceive and appreciate literature. This volume serves as a comprehensive introduction to the rich tapestry of European literary criticism, offering insights into the minds of both critics and authors throughout history.

Genres: Literary criticism, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Evolution of literary criticism, Changing literary tastes, Influence of classical and medieval literature, Debates and controversies in literary criticism
