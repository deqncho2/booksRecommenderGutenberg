Synopsis: In 'German Atrocities: A Record of Shameless Deeds', Le Queux delves into the dark underbelly of World War I, exposing the horrifying acts committed by the German forces. Through a series of gripping narratives and eyewitness accounts, the author uncovers the shocking truth behind the German atrocities, revealing a side of war that is often hidden from public view. From the brutal treatment of civilians to the merciless tactics employed on the battlefield, this book sheds light on the depths of human cruelty and the devastating consequences of war.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War literature, Investigative journalism

Themes: War crimes, Human rights, Propaganda, Morality, Consequences of war
