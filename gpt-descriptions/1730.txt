Synopsis: In "Michael, Brother of Jerry" by Jack London, the story follows the adventures of Michael, a courageous and intelligent dog, as he navigates the harsh realities of life in the city. From his early days as a puppy to his encounters with other animals and humans, Michael's journey is filled with both heartwarming moments and intense challenges. As he forms a unique bond with his brother Jerry, the two face numerous trials that test their loyalty, resilience, and determination. Set against the backdrop of a bustling metropolis, this captivating tale explores the complexities of survival, the power of companionship, and the indomitable spirit of a remarkable canine.

Genres: Adventure, Animal Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Companionship
