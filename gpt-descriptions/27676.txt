Synopsis: 

The 1999 CIA World Factbook takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse nations of the world, providing a comprehensive and intriguing collection of information about each country. From the smallest island nations to the largest global powers, this factbook offers a wealth of knowledge about geography, population, government, economy, and much more. Delve into the fascinating details of each nation's history, culture, and unique characteristics, as well as their relationships with other countries. With its wealth of data and captivating insights, this factbook serves as an indispensable resource for anyone seeking to understand the world we live in.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Geography

Themes: Global diversity, International relations, Cultural exploration
