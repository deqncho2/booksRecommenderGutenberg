Synopsis: 

'Ontario Teachers' Manuals: Household Science in Rural Schools' is a captivating educational guide that takes readers on a journey through the fascinating world of household science. Filled with practical tips, engaging activities, and insightful lessons, this manual offers a unique perspective on the importance of domestic skills in rural communities. From learning about nutrition and meal planning to exploring the art of sewing and home management, this comprehensive guide equips teachers with the tools to empower their students with essential life skills. As readers delve into the pages of this manual, they will discover a wealth of knowledge that not only enhances their understanding of household science but also inspires them to embrace the beauty and significance of rural living.

Genres: Educational, Non-fiction

Themes: Domestic skills, Rural living, Life skills, Education
