Synopsis: In 'A Poor Wise Man' by Mary Roberts Rinehart, a young man named John Gilman rises from poverty to become a successful lawyer, but his past threatens to unravel his newfound success. As he navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and the corrupt world of politics, John must make difficult choices that will determine his future and the lives of those around him.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social class, Ambition, Love and sacrifice
