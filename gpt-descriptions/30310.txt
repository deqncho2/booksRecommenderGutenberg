Synopsis: 

In "Special Report on Diseases of Cattle," a team of experts from the United States Bureau of Animal Industry delves into the intricate world of cattle diseases. Led by Leonard Pearson, this comprehensive report explores various aspects of bovine health, including the identification, prevention, and treatment of diseases that affect cattle. With contributions from esteemed professionals such as Dr. William Dickson, Dr. Benjamin Tilghman Woodward, and many others, this report provides valuable insights into the challenges faced by the cattle industry and offers potential solutions to mitigate the impact of these diseases. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Veterinary Medicine, Agriculture

Themes: Animal health, Disease prevention, Livestock management
