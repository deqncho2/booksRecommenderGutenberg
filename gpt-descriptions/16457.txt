Synopsis: 
In 'All Around the Moon' by Jules Verne, a group of intrepid adventurers embark on a daring mission to the moon. As they journey through space, they encounter unexpected challenges and face the possibility of never returning to Earth. With their lives hanging in the balance, they must rely on their resourcefulness and ingenuity to navigate the mysteries of the lunar landscape. This thrilling tale of exploration and survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human Ingenuity
