Synopsis: In "Lion Loose" by James H. Schmitz, a mysterious and powerful creature escapes from a secret laboratory, wreaking havoc and instilling fear in its wake. As chaos ensues, a group of unlikely heroes must band together to confront the creature and prevent further destruction. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this thrilling sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Action, Thriller

Themes: Power and Responsibility, Survival, Unlikely Heroes
