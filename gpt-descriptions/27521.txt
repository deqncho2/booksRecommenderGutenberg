Synopsis: In 'The Watchers of the Plains: A Tale of the Western Prairies' by Cullum, Ridgwell, a young Englishman finds himself embroiled in a thrilling adventure on the vast and untamed Western Prairies of America. As he navigates the treacherous landscape, he encounters a cast of colorful characters, dangerous outlaws, and a mysterious group known as the Watchers. With danger lurking at every turn, our protagonist must uncover the secrets of the prairies and confront his own inner demons to survive.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the American West, Survival, Identity
