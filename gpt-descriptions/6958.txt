Synopsis: In 'Frank on the Lower Mississippi' by Castlemon, Harry, readers are taken on an exhilarating adventure down the mighty Mississippi River. Frank, a young and daring protagonist, finds himself embarking on a journey filled with danger, mystery, and unexpected encounters. As he navigates the treacherous waters, Frank must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. With each twist and turn, the story unfolds, keeping readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what awaits Frank on his thrilling expedition.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Exploration
