Synopsis: In 'The Motion Picture Chums at Seaside Park: Or, The Rival Photo Theatres of the Boardwalk' by Appleton, Victor, a group of adventurous friends find themselves caught in the middle of a fierce competition between two photo theatres on the bustling boardwalk of Seaside Park. As they navigate the thrilling world of early motion pictures, the Chums uncover a web of secrets and sabotage that threatens to unravel their summer fun. With their quick thinking and determination, they must outsmart the rivals and save the day before the final reel rolls. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Competition, Innovation
