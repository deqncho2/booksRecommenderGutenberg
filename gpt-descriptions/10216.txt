Synopsis: 
'Poems' by Holley, Marietta is a collection of heartfelt and thought-provoking verses that explore the depths of human emotions and experiences. From love and loss to self-discovery and resilience, these poems take readers on a journey through the complexities of life. With vivid imagery and lyrical language, the author invites readers to reflect on their own lives and find solace in the power of words.

Genres: Poetry, Contemporary Literature

Themes: Love, Loss, Self-discovery, Resilience
