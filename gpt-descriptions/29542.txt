Synopsis: In 'The Valor of Cappen Varra' by Poul Anderson, a young and ambitious soldier named Cappen Varra finds himself caught in the midst of a treacherous war. As he navigates through a world filled with political intrigue, ancient legends, and powerful enemies, Cappen must summon all his courage and cunning to survive. With unexpected allies and dangerous adversaries at every turn, the fate of Cappen and his comrades hangs in the balance, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Military

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Survival
