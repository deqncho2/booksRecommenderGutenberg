Synopsis: In 'The Boy Aviators in Record Flight; Or, The Rival Aeroplane' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of young aviators embarks on a thrilling adventure to break a record and claim victory in a high-stakes competition. As they navigate the skies, they encounter unexpected challenges and face off against a rival aeroplane, testing their skills and determination to the limit. Will they be able to achieve their goal and emerge triumphant in this exhilarating race against time?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Competition
