Synopsis: 
In Goethe's "Iphigenia in Tauris," a gripping tale unfolds as Iphigenia, the daughter of Agamemnon, finds herself trapped in a foreign land. Struggling to reconcile her past and her present, she is faced with a difficult decision that could change her life forever. As she navigates the treacherous waters of love, loyalty, and sacrifice, Iphigenia's journey takes unexpected turns, leading to a climactic confrontation that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Romanticism

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Fate, Family, Love
