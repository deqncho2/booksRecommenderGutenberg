Synopsis: In "Forward, March": A Tale of the Spanish-American War, Munroe Kirk takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Spanish-American War. The story follows a group of young soldiers who find themselves thrust into the heart of battle, facing not only the horrors of war but also the challenges of camaraderie, loyalty, and sacrifice. As they navigate the treacherous landscapes of Cuba and the Philippines, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, testing their resolve and shaping their destinies. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, "Forward, March" offers a captivating portrayal of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: War, Friendship, Courage, Sacrifice
