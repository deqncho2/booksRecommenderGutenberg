Synopsis: 
In "Baseball Joe in the Big League; or, A Young Pitcher's Hardest Struggles" by Chadwick, Lester, follow the thrilling journey of Joe Matson, a talented young pitcher, as he faces the biggest challenges of his baseball career. From the intense pressure of playing in the major leagues to the unexpected obstacles that come his way, Joe must navigate through a series of hardships that test his determination and resilience. Will he be able to overcome these struggles and prove himself as a true baseball star?

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Perseverance, Ambition, Teamwork
