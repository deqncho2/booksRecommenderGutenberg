Synopsis: In 'The Star Lord' by Ellanby Boyd, a young orphan named Alex discovers a mysterious amulet that grants him extraordinary powers and transports him to a distant galaxy. There, he becomes embroiled in an epic battle between warring alien factions, where he must navigate treacherous alliances, uncover ancient secrets, and ultimately embrace his destiny as the Star Lord. With breathtaking action, unexpected twists, and a captivating cast of characters, this thrilling science fiction adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Coming of age, Power and responsibility, Identity, Redemption
