Synopsis: In 'The Lost Heir' by G. A. Henty, a young English boy named Edgar is unexpectedly thrust into a world of adventure and danger when he discovers a hidden secret about his ancestry. As he embarks on a journey to claim his rightful inheritance, Edgar must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter fascinating characters, and face numerous challenges that test his courage and resilience. With his loyal companions by his side, Edgar's quest for truth and justice takes him on a thrilling and unpredictable ride that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Courage, Justice
