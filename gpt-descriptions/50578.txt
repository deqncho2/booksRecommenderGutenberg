Synopsis: 

In 'A Sub. of the R.N.R.: A Story of the Great War', author Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous waters of World War I. The story follows the adventures of young Jack Somers, who joins the Royal Naval Reserve as a submarine officer. As Jack faces the perils of the deep sea and the horrors of war, he must navigate not only the treacherous waters but also the challenges of leadership and loyalty. With danger lurking at every turn, Jack's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Leadership, Perseverance
