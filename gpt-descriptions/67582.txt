Synopsis: In "Jerry Todd and the Oak Island Treasure" by Edwards, Leo, a group of adventurous friends stumble upon a mysterious map that leads them to the legendary Oak Island. As they embark on a thrilling treasure hunt, they encounter hidden clues, dangerous obstacles, and unexpected allies. With their determination and wit, they must unravel the secrets of Oak Island before it falls into the wrong hands. Will they uncover the long-lost treasure or will their quest lead them to an even greater discovery?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Perseverance
