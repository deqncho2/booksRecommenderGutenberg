Synopsis: 

'Fairy Circles: Tales and Legends of Giants, Dwarfs, Fairies, Water-Sprites, and Hobgoblins' by Villamaria is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that will transport readers into a world of mythical creatures and magical realms. From the towering giants to the mischievous fairies, and the elusive water-sprites to the playful hobgoblins, this book weaves together a tapestry of folklore and legends that will leave readers spellbound. Each tale is filled with wonder, adventure, and unexpected twists, as the characters navigate through the realms of fantasy, encountering both peril and beauty along the way. 

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Mythology

Themes: Magic, Adventure, Mythical Creatures, Legends, Transformation
