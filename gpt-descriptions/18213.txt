Synopsis:
In Volume III of "The Story of the Great War," the stage is set for an epic conflict as the war begins and the invasion of Belgium unfolds. The reader is taken on a gripping journey through the intense Battle of the Marne, where the fate of nations hangs in the balance. With vivid descriptions and meticulous attention to historical detail, this volume brings to life the early stages of World War I, showcasing the bravery, sacrifice, and strategic brilliance of both sides. As the war escalates, alliances are tested, and the world is forever changed.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Military History

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Strategy, Consequences of War
