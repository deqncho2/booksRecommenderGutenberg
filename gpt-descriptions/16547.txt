Synopsis: 
In 'Sequential Problem Solving: A Student Handbook with Checklists for Successful Critical Thinking', author Fredric B. Lozo presents a comprehensive guide to developing effective problem-solving skills. This handbook offers practical strategies and step-by-step checklists that empower students to approach any problem with confidence and clarity. Lozo's engaging writing style and real-life examples make this book an invaluable resource for students of all ages and disciplines. Whether you're tackling a complex math equation or facing a challenging decision, this handbook will equip you with the tools to think critically and solve problems effectively.

Genres: 
Educational, Self-help, Reference

Themes: 
Critical thinking, Problem-solving, Decision-making
