Synopsis: In 'The Story of Creation as Told By Theology and By Science', T. S. Ackland takes readers on a captivating journey that explores the age-old question of how the universe came into existence. Through a thought-provoking blend of theology and science, Ackland delves into the contrasting perspectives of these two disciplines, offering a unique and enlightening exploration of the origins of our world. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be captivated by the intriguing parallels and divergences between theological beliefs and scientific theories, ultimately leading to a deeper understanding of the complexities surrounding the creation of our universe.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Theology, Science

Themes: Creation, Origins, Religion and Science, Philosophy of Science
