Synopsis: In 'The Geste of Duke Jocelyn' by Farnol, Jeffery, readers are transported to medieval England, where the valiant Duke Jocelyn embarks on a perilous journey to reclaim his rightful title and win back the heart of his beloved lady. Filled with swashbuckling adventures, treacherous betrayals, and unexpected alliances, this epic tale follows Duke Jocelyn as he faces daunting challenges and fights for honor, love, and redemption.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Chivalry, Loyalty, Redemption
