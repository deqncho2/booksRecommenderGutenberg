Synopsis: In the harsh and unforgiving wilderness of the Yukon, a young wolf-dog named White Fang must navigate the treacherous world of humans and animals alike. From his early days as a wild and fierce predator to his eventual encounters with kind-hearted individuals, White Fang's journey is a gripping tale of survival, loyalty, and the eternal struggle between nature and civilization.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Animal Fiction

Themes: Nature vs. Nurture, Loyalty, Survival of the Fittest
