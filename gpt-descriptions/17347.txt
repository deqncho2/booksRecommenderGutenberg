Synopsis: 
In 'Sonnets, and Sonnets on English Dramatic Poets (1590-1650): Taken from The Collected Poetical Works of Algernon Charles Swinburne, Vol V.', Algernon Charles Swinburne presents a collection of sonnets that pay homage to the great English dramatic poets of the late 16th and early 17th centuries. From Shakespeare to Jonson, Swinburne's sonnets capture the essence of these influential figures and their works, exploring their genius, their impact on the literary world, and the timeless themes they tackled. With his lyrical and evocative language, Swinburne invites readers to delve into the world of these renowned poets and discover the beauty and power of their words.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Criticism

Themes: Literary influence, Artistic appreciation, Timelessness of literature
