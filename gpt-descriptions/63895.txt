Synopsis: 
In 'Severn & Somme' by Ivor Gurney, the reader is transported to the battlefields of World War I through the poignant and evocative poetry of a soldier. Gurney's words paint a vivid picture of the horrors and beauty he witnessed, capturing the essence of the war and the human spirit that endured it. Through his verses, he explores the profound impact of war on the individual, the longing for home, and the enduring power of art to provide solace amidst chaos.

Genres: Poetry, War Literature

Themes: War, Human Spirit, Longing, Art
