Synopsis: In the small town of Old Fort Garland, secrets lie buried beneath the surface, waiting to be unearthed. When a mysterious stranger arrives, the town's dark past is brought to light, unraveling a web of deceit, betrayal, and long-held grudges. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the residents of Old Fort Garland must confront their own demons and face the consequences of their actions. With each revelation, the town's fragile peace hangs in the balance, and the truth becomes a dangerous weapon.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Redemption, Small-town dynamics
