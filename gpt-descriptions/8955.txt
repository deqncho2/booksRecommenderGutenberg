Synopsis: In 'Far Above Rubies' by George MacDonald, a young woman named Ruby embarks on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. As she navigates through the challenges of life, she encounters a mysterious stranger who leads her on a path of unexpected encounters and profound revelations. With each encounter, Ruby's perception of herself and the world around her is transformed, ultimately leading her to question the true nature of love, faith, and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Spiritual

Themes: Self-discovery, Spiritual awakening, Love, Faith
