Synopsis: In 'Sweet Clover: A Romance of the White City', Clara Louise Burnham takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of the 1893 Chicago World's Fair. The story follows the life of a young woman named Sweet Clover, who finds herself caught between the allure of the fair and the complexities of love. As she navigates the bustling fairgrounds, she encounters a cast of intriguing characters and experiences unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. This historical romance beautifully captures the spirit of the White City and the transformative power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love, Ambition, Transformation
