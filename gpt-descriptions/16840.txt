Synopsis: In 'Stories of the Wagner Opera', Hélène Adeline Guerber takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Richard Wagner's operas. From the mythical realm of gods and heroes in 'The Ring of the Nibelung' to the tragic love story of 'Tristan and Isolde', this collection of stories brings to life the epic tales and unforgettable characters that have made Wagner's operas timeless classics. With vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, Guerber invites readers to immerse themselves in the dramatic and emotional landscapes of these iconic works, exploring themes of love, destiny, power, and redemption.

Genres: Music, Opera, Mythology, Fiction

Themes: Love, Destiny, Power, Redemption
