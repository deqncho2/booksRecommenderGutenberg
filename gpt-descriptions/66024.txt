Synopsis: In 'Bearly Reasonable' by W. C. Tuttle, a small town is thrown into chaos when a grizzly bear starts wreaking havoc on the community. As the townspeople scramble to find a solution, a mysterious stranger arrives with a surprising proposition that could change everything. With a mix of humor, suspense, and unexpected twists, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Western, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Community, Unexpected alliances
