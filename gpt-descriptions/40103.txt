Synopsis: 
In 'Denry the Audacious' by Arnold Bennett, follow the captivating journey of Denry Machin, a young man with an extraordinary knack for turning every situation to his advantage. From humble beginnings in a small English town, Denry's audacious and cunning nature propels him into the glamorous world of high society, where he becomes a master of reinvention. With his quick wit and charm, Denry navigates through various social circles, encountering love, scandal, and unexpected opportunities along the way. As he rises to prominence, Denry's audacity is put to the ultimate test, forcing him to make daring choices that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Social mobility, Identity
