Synopsis: In 'Ordeal in Space' by Ralph Sloan, a group of astronauts embarks on a perilous mission to explore the mysteries of the universe. As they venture further into the unknown, they encounter unexpected dangers and face life-threatening challenges that test their courage and resilience. With their lives hanging in the balance, the astronauts must rely on their ingenuity and teamwork to survive the treacherous journey through the vastness of space.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human Ingenuity
