Synopsis: 
'Boy Scouts at Crater Lake: A Story of Crater Lake National Park and the High Cascades' by Walter Prichard Eaton takes readers on an exciting adventure with a group of Boy Scouts exploring the breathtaking landscapes of Crater Lake National Park and the High Cascades. As they navigate through the rugged terrain, the Scouts encounter unexpected challenges, uncover hidden secrets, and forge lifelong friendships. With danger lurking in the wilderness, they must rely on their skills, resourcefulness, and teamwork to overcome obstacles and protect the natural wonders they have come to cherish. This thrilling tale captures the spirit of exploration, the beauty of nature, and the resilience of young adventurers.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Nature

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Conservation
