Synopsis:
'World's End: A Story in Three Books' by Richard Jefferies is a captivating tale that takes readers on a journey through the English countryside. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows the lives of three individuals who find themselves drawn to the mysterious and enchanting World's End estate. As they explore the sprawling landscape, they uncover secrets that intertwine their fates and challenge their perceptions of reality. With vivid descriptions and a touch of the supernatural, Jefferies weaves a mesmerizing narrative that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres:
Mystery, Historical Fiction, Supernatural

Themes:
Nature, Identity, Perception
