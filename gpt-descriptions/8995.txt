Synopsis: In 'What Katy Did Next', we follow the spirited and adventurous Katy Carr as she embarks on a journey across Europe. From the bustling streets of Paris to the picturesque landscapes of Germany, Katy encounters new friends, faces unexpected challenges, and discovers the true meaning of independence. As she navigates through the highs and lows of her travels, Katy's resilience and determination shine through, making this a captivating tale of self-discovery and personal growth.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Travel

Themes: Independence, Resilience, Self-discovery
