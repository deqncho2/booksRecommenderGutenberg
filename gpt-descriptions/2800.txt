Synopsis: The Koran (Al-Qur'an) is a profound and influential religious text that delves into the teachings and revelations of Islam's central figure, the Prophet Muhammad. This sacred scripture encompasses a wide range of topics, including theology, morality, guidance for personal conduct, and the nature of God. Through its poetic verses and captivating narratives, The Koran offers a unique perspective on the relationship between humanity and the divine, exploring themes of faith, justice, compassion, and the ultimate purpose of life.

Genres: Religious text, Philosophy, Poetry

Themes: Faith, Morality, Divine guidance, Justice, Compassion, Purpose of life
