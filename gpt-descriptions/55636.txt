Synopsis: In 'Orlóff and His Wife: Tales of the Barefoot Brigade' by Maksim Gorky, we are introduced to the intriguing world of Orlóff, a charismatic and enigmatic character who leads a group of impoverished individuals known as the Barefoot Brigade. Through a series of captivating tales, we witness the struggles, triumphs, and unexpected turns of fate that befall Orlóff and his wife as they navigate the harsh realities of life in early 20th-century Russia. With vivid storytelling and a touch of mystery, Gorky invites readers to delve into the lives of these captivating characters and discover the hidden depths of their humanity.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Poverty, Resilience, Social Injustice, Human Spirit
