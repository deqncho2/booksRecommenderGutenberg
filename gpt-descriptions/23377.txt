Synopsis: In 'The Lively Poll: A Tale of the North Sea', follow the thrilling adventures of a young sailor named Jack, who finds himself caught in a treacherous storm while aboard the ship Lively Poll. As the crew battles the raging sea, Jack must confront his deepest fears and summon his courage to survive the perils of the North Sea. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Maritime Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Survival, Overcoming Adversity
