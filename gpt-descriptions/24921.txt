Synopsis: In "It's like this, cat" by Emily Neville, we follow the story of Dave Mitchell, a fourteen-year-old boy living in New York City. Dave's life takes an unexpected turn when he befriends a stray cat named Cat. Through their adventures together, Dave discovers the complexities of friendship, family, and the vibrant city that surrounds him. As he navigates the challenges of adolescence, Dave learns valuable life lessons and finds solace in the unbreakable bond between a boy and his cat.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Family, Identity, Urban life
