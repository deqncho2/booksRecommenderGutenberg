Synopsis: 

In 'Sexual Neuroses', James Tyler Kent delves into the complex and often misunderstood world of human sexuality. Through a series of captivating case studies, he explores the intricate web of desires, fears, and anxieties that shape our sexual experiences. From the repressed desires of a young woman to the unconventional fantasies of a middle-aged man, Kent uncovers the hidden layers of the human psyche, challenging societal norms and shedding light on the intricacies of sexual neuroses. As the stories unfold, readers are taken on a thought-provoking journey that explores the depths of human sexuality and the profound impact it has on our lives.

Genres: Psychology, Case Studies, Non-fiction

Themes: Human sexuality, Repression, Desire, Anxiety, Psychological exploration
