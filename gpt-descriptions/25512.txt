Synopsis: 
In 'The Fables of Phædrus: Literally translated into English prose with notes', Phaedrus takes us on a captivating journey through a collection of timeless fables. From cunning foxes to wise ants, these fables are filled with wit, wisdom, and unexpected twists. Each story presents a moral lesson that is as relevant today as it was in ancient times. As we delve into the world of Phædrus, we are invited to reflect on the complexities of human nature and the consequences of our actions. With its engaging narratives and thought-provoking insights, this collection of fables is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Fiction, Fables, Classics

Themes: 
Moral lessons, Human nature, Consequences of actions
