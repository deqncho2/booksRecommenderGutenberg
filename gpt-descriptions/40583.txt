Synopsis: In 'The God in the Car: A Novel' by Hope, Anthony, a mysterious and enigmatic man named Felix is thrust into the world of politics and power. With his charm, intelligence, and uncanny ability to manipulate those around him, Felix becomes a force to be reckoned with. As he navigates the treacherous waters of high society, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and dangerous alliances. With each move he makes, Felix's true intentions remain shrouded in mystery, leaving the reader captivated and eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Political Fiction

Themes: Power and manipulation, Secrets and deception, Ambition and ambition
