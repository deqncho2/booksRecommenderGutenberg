Synopsis: In 'The Big Night' by Henry Kuttner, a renowned scientist discovers a mysterious formula that has the power to change the world. As he delves deeper into its secrets, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, pursued by shadowy figures who will stop at nothing to possess the formula. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, leading to a climactic showdown that will determine the fate of humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Scientific discovery, Power and its consequences, Pursuit of knowledge
