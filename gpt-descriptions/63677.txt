Synopsis: 
In 'The Recluse' by Curry, Mike, a mysterious recluse living in a secluded cabin becomes the center of attention when a series of unexplained events occur in the surrounding town. As the townspeople grow increasingly curious about the recluse's past and the secrets he holds, they embark on a journey that will challenge their beliefs and force them to confront their own demons. With each revelation, the line between reality and imagination blurs, leaving the reader questioning the true nature of the recluse and the darkness that lurks within.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the power of perception, the duality of human nature
