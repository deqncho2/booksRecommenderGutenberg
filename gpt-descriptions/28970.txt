Synopsis: 

In 'Works of George W. Peck: A Linked Index to the Project Gutenberg Editions of the "Bad Boy" Series and Others', embark on a hilarious and mischievous journey through the witty and entertaining writings of George W. Peck. This collection features the beloved "Bad Boy" series, along with other humorous tales that will leave you laughing out loud. From the misadventures of a mischievous young boy to the satirical observations of everyday life, Peck's works are sure to captivate readers with their clever humor and relatable characters. 

Genres: Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Mischievousness, Satire, Everyday Life
