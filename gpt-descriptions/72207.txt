Synopsis: In "Bomba the jungle boy at the giant cataract: Or, Chief Nascanora and his captives," Bomba finds himself in a thrilling adventure as he encounters a powerful chief and his captives near a massive waterfall. As Bomba navigates the treacherous jungle, he must outwit his enemies and uncover the secrets hidden within the giant cataract. With danger lurking at every turn, Bomba's courage and resourcefulness will be put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Bravery, Exploration
