Synopsis: In C. Sallusti Crispi De Bello Catilinario Et Jugurthino, Sallust takes us on a captivating journey through two pivotal moments in Roman history. Through his vivid storytelling, we witness the rise and fall of two ambitious men, Catiline and Jugurtha, as they navigate the treacherous political landscape of ancient Rome. With intrigue, betrayal, and political machinations at every turn, Sallust's narrative keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the fate of these captivating characters.

Genres: Historical fiction, political thriller

Themes: Ambition, corruption, power struggles, loyalty, morality
