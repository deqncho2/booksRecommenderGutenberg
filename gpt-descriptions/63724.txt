Synopsis: In "Death disarmed of its sting: A tribute to the memory of the Hon. Roger Minott Sherman," Atwater delivers a powerful discourse at the funeral of a prominent figure. Through heartfelt words and poignant reflections, he explores the impact of death and the legacy left behind by the Hon. Roger Minott Sherman. This tribute serves as a reminder of the fragility of life and the enduring influence one can have even after death.

Genres: Eulogy, Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Mortality, Legacy, Remembrance
