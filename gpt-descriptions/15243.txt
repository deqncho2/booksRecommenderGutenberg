Synopsis: 

In 'Over Paradise Ridge: A Romance' by Maria Thompson Daviess, a young woman named Betty Malroy finds herself caught between two suitors in the picturesque town of Paradise Ridge. As she navigates the complexities of love and friendship, Betty discovers that the path to true happiness is not always straightforward. With its charming setting and a cast of endearing characters, this captivating romance will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Friendship, Self-discovery
