Synopsis:
In "Life and Conduct" by J. Cameron Lees, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, captivating the residents with his enigmatic charm and unconventional lifestyle. As the townspeople become increasingly intrigued by his presence, they are forced to confront their own beliefs and question the boundaries of societal norms. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, this thought-provoking tale explores the complexities of human behavior and the power of individual choices.

Genres:
Mystery, Drama, Psychological Fiction

Themes:
Identity, Morality, Conformity, Self-discovery
