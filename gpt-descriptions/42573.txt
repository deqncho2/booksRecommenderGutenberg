Synopsis: 

In "Ingres" by A. J. Finberg, embark on a captivating journey through the life and art of one of the most influential painters of the 19th century. This biographical work delves into the enigmatic world of Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres, a master of neoclassical painting, whose artistic genius and controversial personality left an indelible mark on the art world. From his early struggles and rejections to his rise as a celebrated artist, this book unveils the untold stories behind Ingres' iconic works, revealing the passion, ambition, and inner turmoil that shaped his artistic vision. 

Genres: Biography, Art History

Themes: Artistic genius, Ambition, Struggle, Controversy
