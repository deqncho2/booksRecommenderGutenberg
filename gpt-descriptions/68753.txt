Synopsis: In 'Forgotten Danger' by Joseph Samachson, a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds a mysterious power. As he delves deeper into its origins, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and deceit. With time running out, he must race against a powerful organization to unlock the artifact's true potential and prevent a catastrophe of epic proportions. This thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they uncover the truth behind the forgotten danger.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Ancient mysteries, Power and corruption
