Synopsis: 
In 'Through the Land of the Serb' by M. E. Durham, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey through the captivating landscapes of Serbia. As she immerses herself in the rich culture and history of the region, she encounters unexpected adventures, encounters with intriguing characters, and a deepening understanding of the complexities of love and loyalty. From the bustling streets of Belgrade to the serene monasteries of the countryside, Mary's exploration of Serbia becomes a transformative experience that will forever change her perspective on life.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Cultural exploration, Personal growth, Love and loyalty
