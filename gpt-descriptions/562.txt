Synopsis: In 'The Go Ahead Boys and the Racing Motor-Boat' by Kay, Ross, a group of adventurous boys stumble upon a mysterious motor-boat and find themselves caught up in a thrilling race against time. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter unexpected obstacles, the boys must rely on their wit and teamwork to outsmart their competitors and uncover the boat's secrets. With heart-pounding action and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
