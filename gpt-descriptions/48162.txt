Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The Pennycomequicks' by S. Baring-Gould, the eccentric Pennycomequick family continues to navigate the challenges of their unconventional lives. As the family members face unexpected twists and turns, their relationships are tested, secrets are revealed, and new alliances are formed. Amidst the backdrop of a changing society, the Pennycomequicks must confront their past and make difficult choices that will shape their future. With its blend of humor, drama, and mystery, this captivating novel delves deeper into the lives of its colorful characters, leaving readers eagerly anticipating the final installment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Family Saga, Mystery

Themes: Family dynamics, societal change, secrets and revelations
