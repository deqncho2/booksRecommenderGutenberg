Synopsis: 

'London Signs and Inscriptions' by Philip Norman takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling streets of London, uncovering the hidden stories behind the city's signs and inscriptions. From faded advertisements on brick walls to ornate plaques on historic buildings, Norman delves into the rich history and intriguing anecdotes that lie behind these often overlooked details. As he explores the labyrinthine streets, the author reveals the secrets, mysteries, and forgotten tales that have shaped the vibrant tapestry of London's urban landscape. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: Urban exploration, Cultural heritage, Historical preservation
