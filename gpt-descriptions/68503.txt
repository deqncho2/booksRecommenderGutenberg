Synopsis: In the small village of Brede, the once powerful and respected Alard family is on the brink of collapse. As secrets, scandals, and long-held grudges come to light, the family's legacy hangs in the balance. With the arrival of a mysterious stranger, the Alards must confront their past and make difficult choices that will determine the future of their once grand house.

Genres: Historical fiction, family drama, mystery

Themes: Family legacy, secrets and betrayal, redemption
