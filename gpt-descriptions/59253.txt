Synopsis: In "Attack of Fortified Places: Including Siege-works, Mining, and Demolitions," James Mercur provides a comprehensive guide for the cadets of the United States Military Academy on the art of besieging and capturing fortified places. From the intricacies of constructing siege-works to the strategic use of mining and demolitions, this book delves into the tactics and techniques necessary to overcome even the most formidable fortifications. With detailed illustrations and historical examples, Mercur's work offers invaluable insights into the art of siege warfare.

Genres: Military strategy, Historical reference, Instructional

Themes: Siege warfare, Military tactics, Engineering
