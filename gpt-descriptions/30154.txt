Synopsis: In 'Essays on God and Freud', Vaknin, Samuel delves into the complex relationship between religion and psychology, exploring the profound impact of Sigmund Freud's theories on our understanding of God and spirituality. Through a thought-provoking collection of essays, Vaknin challenges conventional beliefs and offers a fresh perspective on the age-old questions of faith, the unconscious mind, and the nature of human existence. With a blend of intellectual rigor and personal introspection, this book invites readers on a captivating journey that will challenge their preconceptions and ignite a deeper exploration of the human psyche.

Genres: Philosophy, Psychology, Religion

Themes: Religion and spirituality, Psychology and the unconscious mind, Faith and belief systems
