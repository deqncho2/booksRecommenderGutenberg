Synopsis: 

In 'Lyman's History of old Walla Walla County, Vol. 2: Embracing Walla Walla, Columbia, Garfield and Asotin counties', author William Denison Lyman takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of these four counties. From the early days of settlement to the challenges faced by pioneers, this volume delves into the fascinating stories of the people, places, and events that shaped the region. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Lyman paints a vivid picture of the struggles and triumphs that unfolded in this corner of the Pacific Northwest.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Local history, Biography

Themes: Pioneer life, Settlement and development, Regional history
