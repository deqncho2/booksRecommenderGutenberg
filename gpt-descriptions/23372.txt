Synopsis: In 'The Buffalo Runners: A Tale of the Red River Plains', follow the thrilling adventures of two young brothers, Hugh and Donald, as they navigate the treacherous and untamed Red River Plains in search of their missing father. As they encounter hostile Native American tribes, dangerous wildlife, and the harsh elements of the Canadian wilderness, the brothers must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and the help of unexpected allies to survive. With each step closer to their father, they uncover secrets that will change their lives forever, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and resilience.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Survival, Family, Friendship, Exploration
