Synopsis: In 'The Prophet of Berkeley Square' by Hichens, Robert, a mysterious and enigmatic figure appears in the heart of London, captivating the attention of the city's inhabitants. This prophet, with his uncanny ability to predict the future, becomes a sensation, drawing both admiration and skepticism from the public. As his prophecies unfold, the lives of those around him are forever changed, leading to unexpected consequences and revelations. With its intriguing blend of mysticism and human nature, this novel takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling streets of London, exploring the power of belief and the complexities of fate.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Prophecy, Belief, Fate, Human Nature
