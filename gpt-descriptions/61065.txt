Synopsis: 
In 'Modern American Drinks: How to Mix and Serve All Kinds of Cups and Drinks', George J. Kappeler takes readers on a delightful journey through the art of mixology. From classic cocktails to innovative concoctions, this comprehensive guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to create and present a wide variety of drinks. With a focus on American flavors and techniques, Kappeler's expertise shines through as he shares his secrets for crafting the perfect libation. Whether you're a seasoned bartender or an aspiring home mixologist, this book is sure to inspire and elevate your cocktail game.

Genres: Non-fiction, Cookbook, Mixology

Themes: Mixology, Bartending techniques, American flavors
