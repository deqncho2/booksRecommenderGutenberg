Synopsis: In 'Patrins: To Which Is Added an Inquirendo Into the Wit & Other Good Parts of His Late Majesty King Charles the Second', Louise Imogen Guiney takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and wit of King Charles II. Through a series of intriguing anecdotes and historical accounts, Guiney explores the enigmatic personality of the king, shedding light on his wit, charm, and the impact he had on the society of his time. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will uncover the hidden depths of a monarch who was both revered and controversial, leaving them with a newfound appreciation for the complexities of history.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Wit and humor, Monarchy and power, Historical context
