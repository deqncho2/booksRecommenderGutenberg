Synopsis: 
'California, 1849-1913; Or, The Rambling Sketches and Experiences of Sixty-four Years' Residence in that State' takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and development of California, as seen through the eyes of Lell Hawley Woolley. From the Gold Rush to the rise of industry, Woolley's personal anecdotes and observations provide a unique perspective on the transformation of the state over six decades. Through vivid storytelling and colorful characters, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the trials, triumphs, and ever-changing landscape of California.

Genres: 
Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Travelogue

Themes: 
California history, Personal experiences, Transformation and development
