Synopsis: In 'The Middy and the Moors: An Algerine Story', young Jack Rogers finds himself caught in a thrilling adventure when his ship is captured by Algerian pirates. Separated from his crewmates, Jack must navigate the treacherous moors of North Africa, facing danger at every turn. Along the way, he encounters a mysterious girl who holds the key to his survival and the fate of his fellow sailors. Will Jack be able to outsmart his captors and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Survival, Friendship, Coming of Age
