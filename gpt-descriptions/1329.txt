Synopsis: In 'A Voyage to Arcturus' by David Lindsay, a young man named Maskull embarks on a mysterious journey to the planet Arcturus. As he explores this strange world, he encounters bizarre creatures, undergoes profound transformations, and grapples with the nature of good and evil. With each step, Maskull's perception of reality is challenged, leading him on a mind-bending odyssey that will leave readers questioning their own understanding of the universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Existentialism, Metaphysics, Morality, Perception
