Synopsis: In 'All's for the Best' by Arthur, T. S. (Timothy Shay), a young woman named Grace finds herself caught between the expectations of society and her own desires for independence. As she navigates the challenges of love, family, and societal pressures, Grace must make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century America, this captivating novel explores the complexities of personal freedom and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Independence, Love, Societal Expectations
