Synopsis: 
In 'Uncle Terry: A Story of the Maine Coast' by Charles Clark Munn, a young boy named Terry embarks on a thrilling adventure along the rugged Maine coast. When Terry's uncle, a seasoned sailor, mysteriously disappears, Terry sets out on a quest to uncover the truth. Along the way, he encounters treacherous storms, encounters with dangerous wildlife, and unexpected friendships that will change his life forever. As Terry delves deeper into the secrets of the coast, he discovers a hidden world filled with danger and wonder, where the line between reality and myth becomes blurred.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family bonds, Courage, Exploration of nature
