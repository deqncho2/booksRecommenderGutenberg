Synopsis: 

In 'All in It: K(1) Carries On' by Hay, Ian, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the experiences of a young soldier during World War I. Set against the backdrop of the Great War, the story follows the protagonist as he faces the harsh realities of battle, forms deep bonds with his comrades, and navigates the complexities of love and loss. With vivid descriptions and a gripping narrative, the novel captures the raw emotions and sacrifices made by those who fought in the war, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War, Friendship, Love, Sacrifice
