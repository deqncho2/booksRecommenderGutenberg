Synopsis:
In 'Corianton: A Nephite Story', B.H. Roberts takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient world of the Nephites. The story follows Corianton, a young and impulsive Nephite who finds himself entangled in a web of forbidden desires, dangerous temptations, and personal redemption. As Corianton navigates the complexities of love, faith, and the consequences of his actions, readers are drawn into a tale of moral dilemmas, spiritual growth, and the power of forgiveness. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Roberts weaves a gripping narrative that explores the depths of human nature and the eternal struggle between good and evil.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Religious Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Morality, Redemption, Temptation, Faith, Forgiveness
