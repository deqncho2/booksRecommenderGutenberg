Synopsis: In 'Blottentots, and How to Make Them' by Carmel, John Prosper, a young inventor named Max stumbles upon a mysterious recipe that promises to bring to life a unique creature called a Blottentot. With the help of his friends, Max embarks on an extraordinary adventure to create these magical beings, but soon discovers that their existence comes with unexpected consequences. As Max delves deeper into the secrets of the Blottentots, he must confront his own desires and the ethical implications of playing with life itself.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Science Fiction

Themes: Creation and responsibility, Friendship, Morality
