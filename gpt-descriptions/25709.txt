Synopsis: In 'A Practical View of the Prevailing Religious System of Professed Christians, in the Middle and Higher Classes in this Country, Contrasted with Real Christianity,' Wilberforce delves into the stark contrast between the religious practices of the middle and higher classes and the true essence of Christianity. Through thought-provoking analysis and compelling arguments, he challenges the prevailing religious system and offers a fresh perspective on what it means to be a true follower of Christ.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious, Social Commentary

Themes: Hypocrisy, Authenticity, Religious Practices, Social Class
