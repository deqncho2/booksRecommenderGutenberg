Synopsis: 
In "Motion Picture Directing: The Facts and Theories of the Newest Art," Peter Milne takes readers on an exhilarating journey into the world of film directing. Through captivating anecdotes and insightful analysis, Milne explores the intricacies of this evolving art form, shedding light on the techniques and theories that shape the creation of unforgettable movies. From the challenges faced by directors to the secrets behind their creative decisions, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the behind-the-scenes world of filmmaking. Whether you're an aspiring director or simply a lover of cinema, "Motion Picture Directing" is a must-read that will leave you with a newfound appreciation for the magic that happens behind the camera.

Genres: Non-fiction, Film Studies, Art

Themes: Film directing, Creative process, Filmmaking techniques
