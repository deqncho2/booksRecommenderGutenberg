Synopsis: In Stephen H. Branch's Alligator, Vol. 1 no. 20, September 4, 1858, a mysterious event unfolds in the heart of a small town, leaving its residents bewildered and on edge. As the townspeople grapple with the unexplainable, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human nature is revealed. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the suspense and intrigue that permeates this gripping tale.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Human nature, Small-town dynamics
