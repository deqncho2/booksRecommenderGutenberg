Synopsis: 
In the Morning of Time takes readers on a captivating journey through prehistoric Britain, where ancient tribes clash, mystical forces awaken, and a young warrior named Oona embarks on a quest to save her people. As Oona navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters enigmatic beings, she discovers her own hidden powers and must confront the ultimate test of courage and sacrifice. With vivid descriptions and a richly imagined world, this epic tale of adventure and destiny will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Coming of age, Power and destiny, Tribal conflicts, Mythology
