Synopsis: 
In the picturesque countryside of Kentucky, a young man named John Gray embarks on a journey of self-discovery and love. As he navigates the complexities of rural life, he encounters a captivating cardinal that becomes a symbol of hope and transformation. Through his encounters with the cardinal and the people he meets along the way, John's perspective on life and his own aspirations are forever changed. 

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Nature, Love, Self-discovery
