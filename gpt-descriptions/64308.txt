Synopsis: In 'The Ethic of the Assassin' by Howard Hayden, a renowned assassin finds himself torn between his duty and his conscience when he is assigned a target that challenges his moral code. As he delves deeper into the world of political intrigue and power struggles, he must confront the consequences of his actions and make a decision that could change everything. With unexpected twists and a complex protagonist, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Action

Themes: Morality, Conscience, Power
