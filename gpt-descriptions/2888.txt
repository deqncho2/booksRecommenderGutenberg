Synopsis: 
Step into the rich tapestry of Yorkshire's history and culture through the enchanting verses of Moorman's 'Yorkshire Dialect Poems (1673-1915) and traditional poems'. From the bustling streets of York to the serene landscapes of the Dales, this collection takes readers on a poetic journey spanning over two centuries. Immerse yourself in the unique dialect and vivid imagery that brings the spirit of Yorkshire to life, capturing the essence of its people, traditions, and landscapes. Discover the hidden gems of Yorkshire's literary heritage and experience the power of poetry to transport you to another time and place.

Genres: Poetry, Regional Literature, Historical Literature

Themes: Yorkshire culture and identity, Nature and landscapes, Tradition and heritage
