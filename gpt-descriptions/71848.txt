Synopsis: In "Rogues and Vagabonds" by Compton MacKenzie, a captivating tale unfolds as a group of eccentric characters find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and mischief. Set against the backdrop of a charming coastal town, this delightful novel takes readers on a journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As the lives of these rogues and vagabonds intertwine, their pasts and hidden desires come to light, revealing the complexities of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Comedy

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Secrets, Human nature
