Synopsis: In 'Pandemic' by Jesse F. Bone, a deadly virus sweeps across the globe, leaving chaos and uncertainty in its wake. As the world struggles to contain the outbreak, a group of unlikely heroes emerges, each with their own secrets and motivations. With time running out and the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, they must navigate a treacherous landscape of fear, betrayal, and sacrifice. Will they find a cure and save the world, or will the pandemic consume everything they hold dear?

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Morality, Sacrifice
