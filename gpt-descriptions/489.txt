Synopsis: In 'One Basket' by Edna Ferber, a collection of short stories takes readers on a captivating journey through various settings and characters. From the bustling streets of New York City to the vast plains of the Midwest, Ferber weaves tales of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness. Each story offers a glimpse into the lives of ordinary people facing extraordinary circumstances, leaving readers eager to uncover the outcomes of their trials and tribulations.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Love, Ambition, Pursuit of Happiness
