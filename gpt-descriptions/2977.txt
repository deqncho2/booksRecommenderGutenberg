Synopsis: In "The Memoirs of Jacques Casanova de Seingalt, 1725-1798. Volume 27: Expelled from Spain," the infamous adventurer finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love in the vibrant city of Madrid. As Casanova navigates the treacherous world of Spanish nobility, he becomes embroiled in a dangerous game of secrets and betrayal that threatens to unravel his carefully constructed life. With his wit, charm, and cunning, Casanova must outmaneuver his enemies and protect the woman he loves, all while facing the consequences of his own reckless actions. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Intrigue, Forbidden Love, Betrayal, Identity
