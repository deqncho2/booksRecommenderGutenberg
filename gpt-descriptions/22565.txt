Synopsis: In 'From Farm to Fortune; or, Nat Nason's Strange Experience' by Horatio Alger, Jr., follow the captivating journey of Nat Nason, a young farm boy with dreams of a better life. When an unexpected turn of events leads him away from his humble beginnings, Nat finds himself facing numerous challenges and encountering a cast of intriguing characters. As he navigates the twists and turns of his newfound fortune, Nat must grapple with the true meaning of success and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Rags-to-riches

Themes: Ambition, Hard work, Integrity, Self-discovery
