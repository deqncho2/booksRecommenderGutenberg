Synopsis: In 'Remember the 4th!' by Loomis, Noel M., a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious event occurs on the Fourth of July, leaving the residents questioning their reality and their own memories. As the protagonist delves deeper into the enigma, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to tear the community apart. With time running out, they must race against the clock to uncover the truth and save their town from impending disaster.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Memory, Identity, Community
