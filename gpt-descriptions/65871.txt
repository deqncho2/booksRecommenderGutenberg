Synopsis: In 'The Rejuvenation of Miss Semaphore: A Farcical Novel', eccentric Miss Semaphore embarks on a hilarious journey to reclaim her youth and find love in the most unconventional ways. With a cast of quirky characters and unexpected twists, this uproarious tale will keep readers laughing and guessing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Satire

Themes: Aging, Identity, Love and Relationships
