Synopsis: 
In 'A Pioneer Railway of the West', Maude Ward Lafferty takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed American West, where a group of determined pioneers set out to build a railway that will connect the vast wilderness. As they face countless challenges, from treacherous terrain to hostile Native American tribes, the pioneers must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering spirit to overcome the odds and achieve their ambitious goal. Along the way, they encounter unexpected friendships, heart-wrenching sacrifices, and moments of triumph that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Perseverance, Exploration, Friendship, Overcoming Adversity
