Synopsis: 
In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 147, October 14, 1914', readers are transported to the vibrant and satirical world of early 20th-century London. This volume of the renowned magazine showcases a collection of witty and humorous cartoons, articles, and poems that provide a unique perspective on the social and political issues of the time. From clever commentary on the ongoing World War I to amusing observations on everyday life, this edition of Punch offers a delightful and thought-provoking glimpse into the past.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Magazine

Themes: Social commentary, Politics, Everyday life
