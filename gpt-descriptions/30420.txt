Synopsis: 
'The Poets' Lincoln: Tributes in Verse to the Martyred President' is a collection of heartfelt and poignant poems dedicated to the memory of Abraham Lincoln, the beloved President of the United States who was tragically assassinated. This anthology brings together the works of various renowned poets who were deeply moved by Lincoln's life, leadership, and untimely death. Through their eloquent verses, they pay homage to his legacy, exploring themes of grief, loss, patriotism, and the enduring impact of Lincoln's ideals on the nation.

Genres: Poetry, Historical, Biography

Themes: Grief, Loss, Patriotism, Legacy, Leadership
