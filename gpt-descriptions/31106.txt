Synopsis: In 'Brooke's Daughter: A Novel' by Sergeant, Adeline, a young woman named Brooke discovers a long-hidden family secret that turns her world upside down. As she delves deeper into her mother's mysterious past, Brooke uncovers a web of lies, betrayal, and unexpected connections that threaten to shatter everything she thought she knew about her own identity. With each revelation, she must confront the dark truths that have shaped her family's history and find the strength to forge her own path. This gripping tale of secrets and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Family Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Family Secrets, Betrayal, Self-Discovery
