Synopsis: In 'The Wars of the Roses; or, Stories of the Struggle of York and Lancaster', Edgar, John G. takes readers on a captivating journey through one of the most tumultuous periods in English history. Set during the Wars of the Roses, this gripping tale follows the intertwined fates of the rival houses of York and Lancaster as they vie for power and control of the English throne. Amidst the backdrop of political intrigue, epic battles, and personal betrayals, the lives of ordinary individuals are forever changed by the consequences of their choices. As the conflict escalates, loyalties are tested, alliances are forged and broken, and the true cost of power becomes painfully clear. With its rich historical detail and compelling characters, this enthralling narrative brings to life the dramatic events that shaped the destiny of a nation.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Drama

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Betrayal, Fate, Consequences
