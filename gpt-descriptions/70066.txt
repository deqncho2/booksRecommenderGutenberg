Synopsis: In "Twenty years a fakir" by Weldon, S. James, a mysterious fakir with extraordinary powers emerges in a small village, captivating the locals with his mesmerizing tricks and enigmatic presence. As the fakir's fame spreads, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden agendas, leading to a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. This gripping tale explores the blurred lines between reality and illusion, the power of belief, and the consequences of deception.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Illusion vs. Reality, Power of Belief, Deception
