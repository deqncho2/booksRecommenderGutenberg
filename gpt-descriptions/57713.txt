Synopsis: 

"The Animal Parasites of Man" takes readers on a fascinating journey into the hidden world of parasites that inhabit the human body. From microscopic organisms to larger creatures, this book explores the diverse range of parasites that can affect humans and the intriguing ways in which they interact with their hosts. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, the authors shed light on the complex relationships between parasites and their human hosts, revealing the surprising ways in which these tiny creatures can shape our lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Medical

Themes: Parasitology, Human health, Coevolution
