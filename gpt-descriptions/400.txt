Synopsis: 
'Helen of Troy, and Other Poems' by Sara Teasdale is a captivating collection of poetry that explores the complexities of love, desire, and the human experience. Through vivid imagery and lyrical language, Teasdale delves into the mythological tale of Helen of Troy, weaving it with her own personal reflections on love and longing. This collection takes readers on a journey through the depths of passion, the pain of heartbreak, and the beauty of the natural world, leaving them spellbound by the power of Teasdale's words.

Genres: Poetry, Mythology, Romance

Themes: Love, Desire, Beauty, Mythology, Nature
