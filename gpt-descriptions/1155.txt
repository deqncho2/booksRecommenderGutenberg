Synopsis: 

In "The Secret Adversary" by Agatha Christie, two young adventurers, Tommy and Tuppence, find themselves entangled in a web of mystery and danger. Set in post-World War I London, the story begins with a chance encounter that leads them to a thrilling mission involving stolen documents, a mysterious organization, and a race against time. As they navigate through a series of twists and turns, Tommy and Tuppence must rely on their wit, courage, and quick thinking to uncover the truth and outsmart their secret adversary.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Espionage, Trust, Deception, Post-War Society
