Synopsis:
In 'The Yotsuya Kwaidan or O'Iwa Inari: Tales of the Tokugawa, Volume 1', James S. De Benneville takes us on a captivating journey through the dark and mysterious world of feudal Japan. Set during the Tokugawa period, this collection of chilling tales introduces us to a cast of intriguing characters, including a vengeful ghost, a tormented samurai, and a cunning fox spirit. As the stories unfold, secrets are revealed, curses are unleashed, and the line between the living and the dead becomes blurred. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of supernatural intrigue and suspense, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Horror, Historical Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Revenge, Betrayal, Supernatural forces, Moral corruption
