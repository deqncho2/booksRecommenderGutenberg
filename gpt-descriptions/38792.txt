Synopsis: In 'For the Major: A Novelette' by Constance Fenimore Woolson, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of secrets and forbidden desires when she becomes entangled with a mysterious Major. As their relationship deepens, Mary must navigate the complexities of love, duty, and societal expectations, leading to unexpected consequences that will challenge her understanding of loyalty and sacrifice.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, societal expectations, loyalty, sacrifice
