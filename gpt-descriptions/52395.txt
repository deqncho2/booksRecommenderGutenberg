Synopsis: In "Journal of my journey over the mountains," George Washington takes us on a captivating adventure through the untamed wilderness of Virginia in the mid-18th century. As he surveys the land for Lord Thomas Fairfax, he encounters breathtaking landscapes, treacherous obstacles, and unexpected encounters with Native American tribes. Through his vivid descriptions and personal reflections, Washington provides a unique glimpse into the challenges and wonders of exploring uncharted territories.

Genres: Historical, Travelogue, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Wilderness, Cultural Encounter
