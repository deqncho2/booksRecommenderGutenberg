Synopsis: In 'Millennium' by Cole, Everett B., a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of time travel. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a dangerous race against time, facing unforeseen consequences and a powerful organization determined to control this newfound power. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with thrilling twists and unexpected alliances.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Time travel, Power and control, Ethical dilemmas
