Synopsis: In "The Red Cross girls with the Stars and Stripes," a group of courageous young women join the Red Cross during World War I and find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure overseas. As they navigate the challenges of war-torn Europe, they encounter danger, romance, and unexpected friendships that will test their resilience and determination. With the Stars and Stripes as their emblem, these brave Red Cross girls will face unimaginable obstacles and make sacrifices that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Friendship, Love, Resilience
