Synopsis: 
In 'Georgina's Service Stars' by Annie F. Johnston, readers are introduced to Georgina, a young girl with a heart full of compassion and a desire to make a difference in the world. When she discovers a unique way to serve others, Georgina's life takes an unexpected turn as she embarks on a journey filled with challenges, friendships, and personal growth. As she navigates through various service projects, Georgina learns valuable lessons about empathy, perseverance, and the power of kindness. Will her dedication to helping others lead her to find her true purpose?

Genres: Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, Inspirational

Themes: Service, Empathy, Personal Growth
