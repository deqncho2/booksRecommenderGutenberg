Synopsis: 
In "Pee-wee Harris on the Trail" by Percy Keese Fitzhugh, young Pee-wee Harris embarks on an exciting adventure that takes him deep into the wilderness. Joined by his loyal friends, Pee-wee sets out to solve a mystery and uncover hidden treasures. As they navigate through treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected obstacles, the group's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. With humor, suspense, and a touch of danger, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Pee-wee's every step.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
