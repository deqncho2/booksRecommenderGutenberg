Synopsis: Agnes Grey, a young governess, embarks on a journey to educate and shape the minds of unruly children from wealthy families. As she navigates the challenges of her profession, Agnes encounters a world filled with arrogance, indifference, and cruelty. However, amidst the hardships, she remains steadfast in her determination to instill kindness and virtue in her pupils. Along the way, Agnes also grapples with her own desires for love and independence, leading her to question societal expectations and the role of women in Victorian England.

Genres: Bildungsroman, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Education, Morality, Gender Roles, Social Class
