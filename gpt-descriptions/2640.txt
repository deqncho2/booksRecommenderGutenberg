Synopsis: 
In the enchanting setting of the French Riviera, the young and ambitious Andre-Louis Moreau finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the aristocratic society, Andre-Louis must confront his own principles and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With its captivating blend of romance, adventure, and suspense, 'St. Martin's Summer' takes readers on a thrilling journey through a world where honor and passion collide.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Social class, Love and sacrifice, Political upheaval
