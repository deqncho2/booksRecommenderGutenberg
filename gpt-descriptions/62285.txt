Synopsis: 
In 'The Art and the Romance of Indian Basketry: Clark Field Collection, Philbrook Art Center, Tulsa, 1964', Clark Field takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate world of Indian basketry. With a collection that spans centuries and tribes, Field delves into the rich history, cultural significance, and artistic beauty of these masterpieces. From the meticulous craftsmanship to the stories woven within each basket, this book offers a mesmerizing exploration of a timeless art form.

Genres: Art, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Indigenous culture, Craftsmanship, Artistic expression
