Synopsis: 
In 'Big Baby' by Sharkey, Jack, a mysterious and enigmatic baby is discovered in a small town, captivating the entire community. As the baby grows at an astonishing rate, strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena begin to unfold, leaving the townspeople questioning the true nature of this extraordinary child. With a blend of suspense, humor, and a touch of the supernatural, 'Big Baby' takes readers on a thrilling journey that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Humor

Themes: Identity, Power of Perception
