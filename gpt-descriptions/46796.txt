Synopsis: In "The Pioneer Boys of the Mississippi; or, The Homestead in the Wilderness" by St. George Rathborne, readers are transported to the untamed wilderness of the Mississippi River. Follow the thrilling adventures of a group of courageous young boys as they navigate treacherous waters, encounter dangerous wildlife, and face unexpected challenges. As they strive to establish a homestead in the midst of the wilderness, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and unwavering determination. Will they succeed in their quest for a new life, or will the perils of the wild prove too much to overcome?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Friendship, Resilience
