Synopsis: 

Step back in time to the enchanting world of Florence Hanemann's Dance Revue, set in Central School, Glen Rock, New Jersey, on June 9, 1950. This captivating tale follows a group of talented young dancers as they prepare for their highly anticipated performance. Amidst the excitement and nerves, friendships are tested, secrets are revealed, and dreams are pursued. As the big day approaches, the dancers must overcome obstacles and find the courage to shine on stage. With vibrant costumes, toe-tapping music, and unforgettable performances, Florence Hanemann's Dance Revue is a heartwarming story that will transport readers to a bygone era filled with passion, determination, and the magic of dance.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Performing Arts

Themes: Friendship, Pursuit of Dreams, Overcoming Challenges
