Synopsis: In 'A Book of Golden Deeds', Charlotte M. Yonge presents a collection of inspiring true stories that showcase the extraordinary acts of courage, selflessness, and kindness performed by individuals throughout history. From ancient times to the present day, these tales of heroism and sacrifice will captivate readers and remind them of the power of compassion and the potential for greatness that lies within each of us.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, History

Themes: Heroism, Courage, Selflessness, Kindness, Inspiration
