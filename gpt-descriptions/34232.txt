Synopsis: In 'From Kingdom to Colony' by Mary Devereux, a young princess finds herself torn between duty and desire as she navigates the treacherous world of politics and power. Set in a time of shifting alliances and secret plots, the princess must make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of her kingdom. With danger lurking at every turn, she must rely on her wit, courage, and the unexpected alliances she forms along the way. As the stakes rise and the kingdom hangs in the balance, the princess discovers the true meaning of sacrifice and the strength that lies within her. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Intrigue

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Power and Politics, Sacrifice
