Synopsis: 

In 'Studies in the Psychology of Sex, Volume 5: Erotic Symbolism; The Mechanism of Detumescence; The Psychic State in Pregnancy,' Havelock Ellis delves into the intriguing and often misunderstood aspects of human sexuality. Exploring the depths of erotic symbolism, Ellis uncovers the hidden meanings behind various sexual expressions and their psychological significance. Additionally, he delves into the intricate mechanisms of detumescence, shedding light on the psychological and physiological processes that occur after sexual arousal. Lastly, Ellis examines the unique psychic state experienced during pregnancy, unraveling the complex emotions and psychological changes that accompany this transformative period.

Genres: Psychology, Sexuality Studies, Non-fiction

Themes: Human sexuality, Symbolism, Psychological processes, Pregnancy, Emotional transformation
