Synopsis: In the midst of a gathering at the eccentric Captain Shotover's country house, a group of diverse characters find themselves entangled in a web of love, desire, and disillusionment. As the night unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of the heartbreak house is exposed. With wit and satire, Shaw explores the complexities of human relationships and the illusions we create to protect ourselves from the harsh realities of life.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Satire

Themes: Love and desire, Illusion and reality, Social class and inequality, War and its consequences
