Synopsis:
In 'The Outcaste' by Penny, F. E., a young woman named Alice finds herself caught between two worlds - the rigid social hierarchy of Victorian England and the exotic allure of India. When she is unexpectedly thrust into a forbidden romance, Alice must navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and societal expectations. As she grapples with her own identity and the consequences of her choices, Alice is forced to confront the true meaning of freedom and the power of self-discovery.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Identity, Love and Betrayal, Societal Expectations
