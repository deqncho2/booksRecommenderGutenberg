Synopsis: In "The Farmer's Bride" by Charlotte Mew, a farmer recounts the story of his troubled marriage to a young woman who seems to be haunted by a mysterious past. As he delves into the complexities of their relationship, the reader is drawn into a haunting tale of love, loss, and the human psyche. With each turn of the page, the secrets and struggles of the farmer's bride are slowly unveiled, leaving the reader captivated and eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Poetry, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Mental Health, Isolation, Gender Roles
