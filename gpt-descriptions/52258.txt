Synopsis: 

In 'History of the Royal Regiment of Artillery, Vol. 1: Compiled from the Original Records', Francis Duncan takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and storied past of the Royal Regiment of Artillery. From its humble beginnings to its pivotal role in shaping military strategies and technological advancements, this meticulously compiled volume unveils the untold stories and remarkable achievements of the regiment. With vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, Duncan paints a vivid picture of the challenges faced by the artillerymen and the triumphs they achieved on the battlefield. As readers delve into this engrossing narrative, they will discover the secrets behind the regiment's success and the enduring legacy it has left on the world of warfare.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Military strategy, Technological advancements, Historical legacy
