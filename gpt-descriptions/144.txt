Synopsis: 
In 'The Voyage Out', Rachel Vinrace embarks on a journey that will forever change her perception of the world. Set against the backdrop of a captivating ocean voyage, Rachel encounters a diverse cast of characters, each grappling with their own desires, fears, and secrets. As the ship sails through exotic landscapes and encounters unexpected challenges, Rachel's own inner journey of self-discovery unfolds, leading her to question societal norms and the limitations placed upon women. With its richly drawn characters and evocative prose, 'The Voyage Out' is a mesmerizing exploration of love, identity, and the complexities of human connection.

Genres: Fiction, Modernist Literature, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Self-discovery, Gender roles, Society and its constraints, Love and relationships
