Synopsis: In 'The Ocean Wireless Boys on the Pacific' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of adventurous young men embark on a thrilling journey across the vast Pacific Ocean. Their mission? To test the limits of the newly developed wireless technology and establish communication with a remote island. As they face treacherous weather, encounters with dangerous wildlife, and unexpected challenges, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. Will they succeed in their mission and make history? Join the Ocean Wireless Boys on this exhilarating voyage to find out!

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Technology, Friendship
