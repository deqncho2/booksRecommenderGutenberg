Synopsis: In 'Asa Holmes; or, At the Cross-Roads', Annie F. Johnston takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Asa Holmes, a young man at a pivotal point in his life. Asa finds himself at a crossroads, torn between the expectations of his family and his own desires for independence and self-discovery. Along the way, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, faces unexpected challenges, and navigates the complexities of love and friendship. This heartwarming and thought-provoking tale explores the choices we make and the paths we take, ultimately leading to a profound understanding of oneself and the power of personal growth.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Fiction, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Independence, Family expectations, Love, Friendship
