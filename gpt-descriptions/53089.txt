Synopsis: In 'The Creature Inside' by Sharkey, Jack, a young woman named Lily discovers a mysterious journal hidden in her deceased grandmother's attic. As she delves into the pages, she becomes consumed by a dark and haunting secret that threatens to consume her sanity. With each revelation, Lily's reality begins to blur, and she must confront the terrifying truth lurking within her own mind. This gripping psychological thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning what is real and what is merely a figment of Lily's imagination.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Horror

Themes: Identity, Sanity, Family secrets
