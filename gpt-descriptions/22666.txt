Synopsis: In 'The Rainy Day Railroad War' by Day Holman, a small town is thrown into chaos when two rival railroad companies clash over the rights to build their tracks through the area. As tensions rise and the rain pours down, the townspeople find themselves caught in the middle of a fierce battle for power and control. With unexpected alliances forming and secrets being revealed, the fate of the town hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Community, Secrets
