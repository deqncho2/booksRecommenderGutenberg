Synopsis:
In the remote and mysterious land of Nagaland, a young British officer finds himself immersed in a world of ancient traditions, tribal rivalries, and a hidden treasure that holds the key to a long-lost civilization. As he navigates the treacherous terrain and encounters the enigmatic Lhota Nagas, he becomes entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and forbidden love. With each step closer to uncovering the truth, he must confront his own beliefs and question the boundaries of loyalty and honor. Will he unlock the secrets of the Lhota Nagas, or will the past remain buried forever?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Cultural exploration, Identity, Forbidden love, Betrayal
