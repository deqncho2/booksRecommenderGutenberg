Synopsis: 
In 'Six Feet Four' by Gregory Jackson, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, capturing the attention and curiosity of the locals. As the stranger's true identity and purpose slowly unravel, the town becomes embroiled in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected connections. With each revelation, the tension escalates, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Small-town dynamics
