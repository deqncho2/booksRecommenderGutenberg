Synopsis: In 'Three Hats: A Farcical Comedy in Three Acts' by Shirley Arthur, chaos ensues when three strangers accidentally swap hats at a crowded train station. As they try to retrieve their own hats, they find themselves caught up in a series of hilarious misunderstandings and mistaken identities. With each passing act, the situation becomes increasingly absurd, leading to a riotous climax that will leave readers in stitches.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Mistaken identity, Absurdity, Chaos
