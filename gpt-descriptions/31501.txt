Synopsis: In 'The Sensitive Man' by Anderson, Poul, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Smith embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that could change the course of humanity. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he discovers a hidden world of secrets and conspiracies that threaten not only his work but also his very existence. With time running out, Dr. Smith must navigate a treacherous web of deception and betrayal, all while grappling with his own inner demons. This gripping tale of science, mystery, and personal struggle will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Scientific ethics, Identity, Conspiracy
