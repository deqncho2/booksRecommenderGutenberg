Synopsis: In 'Confiscation; An Outline' by William Greenwood, a mysterious artifact is discovered, setting off a thrilling chain of events that spans across time and space. As the protagonist delves deeper into the artifact's secrets, they uncover a hidden world filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected allies. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, leading to a climactic showdown that will determine the fate of not only the protagonist but also the entire universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Power and corruption, Redemption
