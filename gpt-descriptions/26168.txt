Synopsis: In 'The Success Machine' by Henry Slesar, a brilliant inventor creates a revolutionary device that promises to change the world. However, as the machine gains popularity and people start achieving unprecedented success, a dark and unexpected consequence emerges. As the protagonist delves deeper into the mystery, they must confront the ethical implications of their creation and the true nature of success itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Consequences of Technology
