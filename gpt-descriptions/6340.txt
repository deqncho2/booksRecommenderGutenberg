Synopsis: 
In 'Literary Lapses', Stephen Leacock takes readers on a delightful journey through a collection of humorous and satirical short stories. From the absurdities of everyday life to the comical mishaps of eccentric characters, Leacock's wit and clever observations will leave readers chuckling and pondering the quirks of human nature. Whether it's a misadventure at a summer resort, a hilarious encounter with a pompous professor, or a satirical take on the literary world, Leacock's stories are sure to entertain and amuse.

Genres: 
Humor, Satire, Short Stories

Themes: 
Human nature, Absurdity, Social commentary
