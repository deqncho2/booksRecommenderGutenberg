Synopsis: In 'Ancestors: A Novel', a young woman named Mary inherits a mysterious family estate after the sudden death of her parents. As she delves into the history of her ancestors, Mary uncovers a web of secrets, hidden treasures, and long-lost love letters that lead her on a captivating journey through time. With each discovery, Mary becomes more entangled in the lives of her ancestors, ultimately questioning her own identity and the choices she must make to honor her family's legacy.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family legacy, Identity, Secrets and hidden histories
