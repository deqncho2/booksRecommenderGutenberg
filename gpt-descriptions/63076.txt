Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Old Harbour, a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a tale that captivates the entire community. As the townspeople gather to listen, they are transported to a world filled with adventure, love, and secrets that have long been buried. With each thread of the story, the stranger weaves a tapestry of intrigue and suspense, leaving the townsfolk yearning for more. But as the yarn unravels, the boundaries between reality and fiction begin to blur, and the true nature of the stranger's intentions becomes uncertain. Will the people of Old Harbour uncover the truth hidden within the tale, or will they be forever entangled in its enigmatic web?

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Storytelling, Community, Truth and Perception
