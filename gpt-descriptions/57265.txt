Synopsis: 
In 'The Rāmāyana, Volume 1. Bālakāndam and Ayodhyākāndam' by Valmiki, embark on an epic journey through ancient India as the story unfolds with captivating characters and extraordinary events. Follow the life of Prince Rama, the embodiment of virtue and righteousness, as he faces trials and tribulations that test his strength, loyalty, and devotion. From his idyllic childhood in the kingdom of Ayodhya to his exile in the forest, witness the unfolding of a timeless tale filled with love, honor, and the eternal battle between good and evil.

Genres: Epic, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Virtue and righteousness, Loyalty, Devotion, Love, Good vs. Evil
