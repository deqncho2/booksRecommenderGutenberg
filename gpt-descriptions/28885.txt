Synopsis: 
In this enchanting tale, young Alice finds herself tumbling down a rabbit hole into a whimsical world filled with peculiar characters and nonsensical situations. From the grinning Cheshire Cat to the tyrannical Queen of Hearts, Alice encounters a host of unforgettable personalities as she navigates through this topsy-turvy realm. With the stunning illustrations by Arthur Rackham bringing the story to life, readers will be captivated by the imaginative and surreal adventures that unfold in Wonderland.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Children's Literature, Classic

Themes: 
Imagination, Identity, Absurdity
