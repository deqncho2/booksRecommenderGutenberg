Synopsis: 
'Fun for the Household: A Book of Games' by Gray, Emma J. is a delightful collection of interactive games and activities that will bring joy and laughter to any household. From classic board games to creative challenges, this book offers a wide range of entertainment options for all ages. Whether you're looking for a fun way to bond with your family or planning a memorable game night with friends, 'Fun for the Household' has something for everyone. Get ready to embark on a journey of laughter, competition, and unforgettable moments!

Genres: Non-fiction, Entertainment, Family

Themes: Bonding, Creativity, Fun
