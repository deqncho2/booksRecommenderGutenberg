Synopsis: 

In "Extracts from "Sikes on Parochial Communion": relating to Episcopacy, and the sin of Schism," Thomas Sikes delves into the controversial topics of Episcopacy and Schism within the context of parochial communion. Through a series of extracts, Sikes presents thought-provoking arguments and insights that challenge traditional beliefs and shed light on the consequences of division within the church. As the reader delves into these extracts, they will be captivated by the intellectual discourse and the exploration of the moral implications surrounding these contentious issues.

Genres: Religious, Theological, Historical

Themes: Episcopacy, Schism, Parochial Communion, Morality
