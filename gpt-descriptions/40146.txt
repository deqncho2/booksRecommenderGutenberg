Synopsis: In 'The City of Masks' by George Barr McCutcheon, a young American traveler finds himself in the enchanting city of Venice, where he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. As he navigates the labyrinthine streets and canals, he discovers a secret society that wears masks to conceal their true identities. Drawn deeper into their world, he must uncover the truth behind the masks and confront the consequences of his own choices.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Deception, Cultural Exploration
