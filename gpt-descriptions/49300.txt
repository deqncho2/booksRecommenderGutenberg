Synopsis: 

Step into the enchanting world of Biscayne Bay, Dade Co., Florida, where a treasure trove of information awaits. This complete manual unveils the secrets of the region's climate, soil, and abundant products, offering a captivating exploration of the lands bordering the bay. Discover the allure of this picturesque paradise, nestled between the 25th and 26th degrees of latitude, as you delve into its rich history and vibrant ecosystem. From the captivating tales of early settlers to the bountiful harvests that flourish in this fertile land, this guide will transport you to a place where beauty and opportunity intertwine. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, Guidebook

Themes: Climate, Geography, Agriculture, History, Exploration
