Synopsis: In "Romances of the old town of Edinburgh," Leighton takes us on a captivating journey through the winding streets and hidden corners of the historic Scottish city. Through a series of interconnected tales, we encounter a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own secrets, desires, and ambitions. From forbidden love affairs to mysterious disappearances, the old town of Edinburgh becomes a backdrop for passion, intrigue, and unexpected twists. As the stories unfold, the reader is transported to a bygone era, where the past and present collide, and the city itself becomes a character in its own right.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and desire, Secrets and hidden identities, Fate and destiny, City as a character
