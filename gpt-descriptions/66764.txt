Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 140, May 15, 1915: The Melting-Pot', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is called upon to investigate a series of bizarre disappearances. As he delves deeper into the case, Carter uncovers a sinister plot that threatens to plunge the city into chaos. With time running out, he must use his unparalleled deductive skills to unravel the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Crime-solving, Danger, Deception
