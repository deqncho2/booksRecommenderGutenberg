Synopsis: In "Under Wolfe's Flag; or, The Fight for the Canadas" by Walker, Rowland, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the French and Indian War. Amidst the backdrop of political intrigue and military conflict, a young British officer finds himself caught in a web of loyalty, love, and betrayal. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of war, he must make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of the Canadas. With vivid descriptions and gripping action, this historical adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the outcome of this epic struggle.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Love, Betrayal, Conflict, Identity
