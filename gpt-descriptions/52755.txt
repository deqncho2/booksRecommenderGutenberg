Synopsis: In 'Uncle Sam's Boys on Field Duty; or, Winning Corporal's Chevrons', follow the thrilling adventures of a group of young soldiers as they navigate the challenges of military life during World War I. From the trenches to the battlefields, these brave boys face danger, camaraderie, and the test of their loyalty to their country. As they strive to earn their corporal's chevrons, they must prove their mettle and learn the true meaning of honor and sacrifice.

Genres: Adventure, War, Historical Fiction

Themes: Patriotism, Brotherhood, Courage, Sacrifice
