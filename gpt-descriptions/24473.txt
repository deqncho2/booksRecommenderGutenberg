Synopsis: In 'The Cat and the Mouse: A Book of Persian Fairy Tales', embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Persian folklore. This collection of tales weaves together magical adventures, cunning animals, and wise sages, transporting readers to a realm where cats and mice hold secrets, and unexpected friendships are formed. From the bustling bazaars of ancient Persia to the mystical landscapes of the desert, each story unfolds with delightful surprises and valuable lessons, leaving readers spellbound until the very last page.

Genres: Fairy Tales, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Wisdom, Adventure, Morality
