Synopsis: In 'Riallaro: The Archipelago of Exiles' by Sweven Godfrey, a group of diverse individuals find themselves stranded on a mysterious archipelago after a catastrophic event. As they navigate the treacherous landscapes and encounter enigmatic creatures, they must unravel the secrets of the island and confront their own inner demons. With each character possessing unique abilities and motivations, alliances are formed and broken, leading to unexpected twists and turns. This gripping tale of survival, adventure, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Identity, Redemption
