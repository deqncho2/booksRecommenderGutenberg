Synopsis: In 'Our Little Russian Cousin', we follow the captivating journey of a young Russian girl named Natasha as she navigates the enchanting world of her homeland. From the bustling streets of St. Petersburg to the serene countryside, Natasha's adventures introduce us to the rich culture, traditions, and customs of Russia. As she encounters new friends and experiences, Natasha's curiosity and resilience shine through, making for a delightful and heartwarming tale.

Genres: Children's Literature, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cultural exploration, Friendship, Resilience
