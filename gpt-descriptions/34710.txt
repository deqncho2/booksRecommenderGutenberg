Synopsis: In 'One of My Sons' by Anna Katharine Green, a wealthy and respected family is torn apart when a shocking secret is revealed. As the patriarch struggles to protect his family's reputation, he must confront the devastating consequences of his past actions. With suspenseful twists and turns, this gripping novel explores the lengths one will go to protect their loved ones and the moral dilemmas that arise when faced with the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Family Drama

Themes: Family secrets, Morality, Reputation
