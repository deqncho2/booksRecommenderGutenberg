Synopsis: In "Strangers and Wayfarers" by Sarah Orne Jewett, a young woman named Joanna embarks on a journey to a remote coastal village in Maine. As she becomes acquainted with the locals, Joanna discovers a community filled with unique characters and hidden stories. Through her encounters with these strangers and wayfarers, Joanna's own perspective on life and love is challenged, leading her to question her own place in the world.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Community, Self-discovery
