Synopsis: In 'History of Egypt From 330 B.C. To the Present Time, Volume 11 (of 12)', A. S. Rappoport takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and complex history of Egypt. From the conquest of Alexander the Great to the modern era, this volume delves into the political, cultural, and social developments that have shaped Egypt over centuries. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Rappoport uncovers the rise and fall of dynasties, the influence of foreign powers, and the struggles for independence. From the grandeur of ancient pharaohs to the tumultuous events of the 19th and 20th centuries, this volume offers a comprehensive exploration of Egypt's fascinating past.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and politics, Cultural transformation, National identity, Colonialism, Independence struggles
