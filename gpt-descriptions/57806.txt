Synopsis: In 'Maiolica', a captivating tale by C. Drury E. Fortnum, a young artist named Robert embarks on a journey that takes him from the bustling streets of London to the enchanting landscapes of Italy. Drawn to the art of maiolica, a vibrant form of pottery, Robert becomes immersed in a world of beauty, mystery, and hidden secrets. As he delves deeper into the history and techniques of this ancient craft, he uncovers a web of intrigue that spans centuries, connecting him to a long-lost legacy. With each piece of pottery he creates, Robert unravels the enigmatic past and discovers the transformative power of art.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Art

Themes: Artistic expression, Identity, History, Secrets
