Synopsis: In 'A Modern Slavery' by Nevinson, Henry Woodd, a young journalist uncovers a shocking secret that leads him down a dangerous path of corruption and deceit. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of society, he must confront his own moral compass and make difficult choices that could have far-reaching consequences. With each twist and turn, the protagonist becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that threatens to consume him entirely.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Corruption, Morality, Power
