Synopsis: In the small town of Santa Rosa, a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a dark and enigmatic past. As the townspeople become captivated by his charm and charisma, they soon discover that he is not what he seems. Secrets unravel, alliances are tested, and the line between good and evil becomes blurred. With a thrilling blend of suspense, romance, and supernatural elements, "El Diablo" takes readers on a gripping journey through the shadows of human nature.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Deception, Redemption, Moral ambiguity
