Synopsis: In "Betty Leicester: A Story For Girls," we follow the journey of Betty, a young girl who moves to a new town and finds herself navigating the challenges of making friends, discovering her own identity, and facing unexpected adventures. As Betty immerses herself in the community, she encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets and stories. Through her experiences, Betty learns valuable lessons about loyalty, courage, and the power of friendship. Will Betty find her place in this new world and uncover the truth behind the mysteries that surround her?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Courage, Community
