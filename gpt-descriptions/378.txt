Synopsis: In 'The White Knight: Tirant Lo Blanc', we are transported to the captivating world of chivalry, romance, and adventure. Set in the 15th century, the story follows the valiant knight Tirant Lo Blanc as he embarks on a series of daring quests and battles, encountering love, treachery, and political intrigue along the way. As Tirant navigates the complex web of relationships and alliances, he must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape the course of his destiny. With its richly detailed setting and compelling characters, this epic tale will keep readers enthralled until the very last page.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Chivalry, Love and Desire, Honor and Duty
