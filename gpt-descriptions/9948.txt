Synopsis: In 'The Banner Boy Scouts Afloat; or, The Secret of Cedar Island' by George A. Warren, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts embark on a thrilling summer adventure on Cedar Island. As they set sail, they stumble upon a mysterious secret that leads them on a dangerous quest, testing their courage, teamwork, and resourcefulness. With unexpected twists and turns, the scouts must navigate treacherous waters and face unexpected challenges to uncover the truth hidden on Cedar Island.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Teamwork, Exploration, Mystery
