Synopsis: In 'The White Chief of the Ottawa' by Carr-Harris, Bertha, a young woman finds herself torn between two worlds when she falls in love with a powerful Native American chief. As she navigates the complexities of cultural differences and societal expectations, Bertha must make a choice that will forever change her life. Set against the backdrop of the stunning Ottawa Valley, this captivating tale explores the depths of love, loyalty, and the clash of cultures.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Cultural

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Cultural Identity, Forbidden Relationships
