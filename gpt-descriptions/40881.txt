Synopsis: 

'Workhouse Characters, and other sketches of the life of the poor' by Margaret Wynne Nevinson takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of various individuals living in poverty. From the heart-wrenching stories of those confined within the walls of the workhouse to the struggles faced by families on the brink of destitution, this collection of sketches offers a poignant and eye-opening glimpse into the harsh realities of poverty-stricken communities. Through vivid descriptions and compelling narratives, Nevinson sheds light on the resilience, strength, and humanity that can be found amidst the most challenging circumstances.

Genres: Non-fiction, Social commentary, Historical

Themes: Poverty, Social inequality, Resilience, Human dignity
