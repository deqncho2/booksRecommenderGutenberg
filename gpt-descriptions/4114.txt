Synopsis: In Volume 7 of "Christopher Columbus and the New World of His Discovery," Young, Filson takes readers on a captivating journey through the final years of Columbus' life. As the explorer faces numerous challenges and encounters with indigenous peoples, the narrative delves into the complexities of his character and the impact of his discoveries. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the book sheds light on the untold stories of Columbus' later expeditions, revealing surprising twists and turning points that shaped the course of history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Legacy, Cultural encounters, Human resilience
