Synopsis: In 'The 1893 Duryea Automobile In the Museum of History and Technology', Berkebile takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating history of the first gasoline-powered automobile in the United States. From the initial concept to its groundbreaking debut, the author delves into the challenges faced by the Duryea brothers and the impact their invention had on the future of transportation. With vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, this book offers a captivating exploration of a pivotal moment in automotive history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Innovation, Transportation, American History
