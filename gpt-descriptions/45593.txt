Synopsis:
In the third book of the Chronicles of England, Scotland and Ireland, we delve into the captivating story of Edward the Fourth, the Earl of March and the son and heir to Richard Duke of York. Set in the tumultuous period of the Wars of the Roses, this historical account takes us through Edward's rise to power, his struggles to maintain his throne, and the complex web of alliances and betrayals that surround him. As Edward navigates the treacherous political landscape, he must confront his own personal demons and make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of England.

Genres:
Historical fiction, Biography, Political drama

Themes:
Power and ambition, Loyalty and betrayal, Fate and destiny
