Synopsis: In 'First Book in Physiology and Hygiene', John Harvey Kellogg takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate workings of the human body and the importance of maintaining good health. From exploring the mysteries of digestion to unraveling the secrets of the nervous system, Kellogg's engaging narrative and vivid illustrations bring the science of physiology to life. With a focus on practical knowledge and everyday applications, this book serves as an indispensable guide to understanding our bodies and taking control of our well-being.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Health

Themes: Human anatomy, Health education, Disease prevention
