Synopsis: In 'New Nick Carter weekly; No. 28. July 10, 1897; Nick Carter at the track; or, How he became a dead game sport,' the renowned detective finds himself immersed in the thrilling world of horse racing. As he delves into a mysterious case at the track, Nick Carter must navigate a web of deceit, danger, and high-stakes gambling. With his keen intellect and relentless determination, he uncovers shocking secrets that could cost him his life. Will Nick Carter emerge victorious in this deadly game of wits?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Detective work, Deception, Gambling
