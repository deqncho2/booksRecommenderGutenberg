Synopsis: In 'To Mars via the Moon: An Astronomical Story' by Mark Wicks, a group of brilliant scientists embark on a daring mission to explore the mysteries of Mars. However, their journey takes an unexpected turn when they encounter a series of perplexing phenomena on the Moon. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they uncover secrets that could change the course of human history. With suspenseful twists and mind-bending discoveries, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Scientific Discovery, Human Potential
