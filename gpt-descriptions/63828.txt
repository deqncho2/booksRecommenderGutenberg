Synopsis: In 'The Burnt Planet' by William J. Brittain, a young scientist named Dr. Emily Carter embarks on a thrilling journey to a distant planet that has been ravaged by a catastrophic event. As she explores the desolate landscape, she uncovers shocking secrets that challenge her understanding of the universe and the very nature of humanity. With danger lurking at every turn, Dr. Carter must navigate treacherous terrain and confront her own inner demons in order to unravel the mysteries of the burnt planet.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Identity, Environmentalism
