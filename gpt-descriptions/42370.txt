Synopsis: In 'Round the Corner in Gay Street', the reader is transported to the charming and mysterious world of Gay Street, where a group of friends stumble upon a hidden secret that will change their lives forever. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected romance. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth that lies just around the corner.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Friendship and loyalty, Love and sacrifice
