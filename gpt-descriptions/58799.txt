Synopsis: 

In "History of the Australian Bushrangers" by George Boxall, embark on a thrilling journey through the untamed and lawless Australian outback. This captivating non-fiction work delves into the lives of notorious bushrangers, the daring outlaws who roamed the vast wilderness during the 19th century. From the infamous Ned Kelly to the audacious Captain Thunderbolt, the book uncovers the hidden stories, audacious escapades, and heart-stopping encounters that shaped the legends of these fearless bandits. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Boxall paints a vivid picture of the harsh realities and wild adventures that defined the era of the Australian bushrangers.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Biography

Themes: Outlaw culture, Survival in the wilderness, Social rebellion, Law and order
