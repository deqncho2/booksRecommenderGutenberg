Synopsis: In 'Electricity for Boys' by Zerbe, James Slough, a young boy named Alex embarks on an electrifying adventure when he stumbles upon a mysterious device that grants him the power to control electricity. As he delves deeper into the secrets of this newfound ability, Alex must navigate the dangerous consequences that come with such immense power. With thrilling twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Alex's electrifying journey.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Power and Responsibility, Discovery and Exploration
