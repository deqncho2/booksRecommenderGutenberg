Synopsis: 
In the remote Scottish countryside lies Deep Moat Grange, a mysterious and enchanting estate with a dark past. When a young artist named Ronald West inherits the property, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, hidden treasures, and unexpected alliances. As he delves deeper into the history of Deep Moat Grange, Ronald discovers that the estate holds more than just its fair share of ghosts and legends. With danger lurking around every corner, he must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit and betrayal to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the power of the past, the supernatural, love and loyalty
