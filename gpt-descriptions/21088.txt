Synopsis:
"The White Squall: A Story of the Sargasso Sea" takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the treacherous waters of the Sargasso Sea. When a group of young sailors find themselves stranded in this mysterious and dangerous region, they must navigate not only the physical challenges of the sea but also the psychological and emotional turmoil that arises. As they battle against the elements and face unexpected encounters, the true nature of human resilience and the power of friendship are put to the ultimate test.

Genres:
Adventure, Mystery, Survival

Themes:
Perseverance, Friendship, Man vs. Nature
