Synopsis: In 'The Hawaiian Romance Of Laieikawai' by Haleole, S. N., a young chiefess named Laieikawai is born with a divine destiny that sets her apart from others. As she grows older, she finds herself caught between the desires of her family and the call of her own heart. With the help of a mysterious prophet, Laieikawai embarks on a journey of self-discovery, encountering love, betrayal, and the power of her own inner strength. Set against the backdrop of ancient Hawaii, this captivating tale weaves together elements of romance, adventure, and the supernatural, leaving readers eager to uncover the secrets that lie within Laieikawai's extraordinary life.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Destiny, Love, Identity, Power
