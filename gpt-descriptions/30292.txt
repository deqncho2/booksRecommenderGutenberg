Synopsis: 
In 'How to Tie Flies' by Gregg, Ellery Clark, a young aspiring fly fisherman stumbles upon a hidden world of intricate fly tying techniques. As he delves deeper into this captivating art form, he discovers that there is more to tying flies than just creating beautiful imitations of insects. With the guidance of a mysterious mentor, he embarks on a transformative journey that not only teaches him the secrets of fly tying, but also reveals profound insights about life, passion, and the interconnectedness of nature. 

Genres: 
Adventure, Coming-of-Age, Mystery 

Themes: 
The pursuit of mastery, the power of mentorship, the beauty of nature, self-discovery
