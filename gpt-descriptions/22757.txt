Synopsis: In 'Debts of Honor' by Jókai, Mór, a young nobleman named Béla finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, betrayals, and forbidden love. As he navigates through the treacherous world of Hungarian politics and society, Béla must confront his own past and make difficult choices that will determine his future. With its captivating characters and intricate plot, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with suspense, romance, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Honor, Loyalty, Social class, Love and sacrifice
