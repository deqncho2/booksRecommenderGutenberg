Synopsis: In 'How it Works', Archibald Williams takes readers on an enlightening journey through the fascinating world of steam, electricity, light, heat, sound, hydraulics, optics, and more. With a focus on simplicity and practicality, this book explores the inner workings of these fundamental concepts and their applications in everyday devices. From the mechanics of steam engines to the wonders of electric circuits, Williams provides clear explanations and real-life examples that will captivate readers and deepen their understanding of the world around them.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Education

Themes: Technology, Engineering, Physics, Practical Applications
