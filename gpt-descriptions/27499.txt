Synopsis: 

'Folk-lore and Legends: German' by Anonymous takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of German folklore and legends. From enchanting tales of mythical creatures to haunting stories of supernatural beings, this collection offers a glimpse into the mystical world of German folklore. Delve into the depths of the Black Forest, where ancient spirits roam, and witness the power of love and sacrifice in the face of adversity. With each story, readers will be transported to a realm where magic and reality intertwine, leaving them spellbound until the very last page.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Mythical creatures, Supernatural beings, Love and sacrifice
