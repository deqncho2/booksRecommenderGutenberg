Synopsis: 
'Poems, translated and original' by Ellet, E. F. is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through various emotions and experiences. From heartfelt love poems to thought-provoking reflections on nature and life, this anthology showcases the author's talent for capturing the essence of human emotions in beautifully crafted verses. With a mix of translated works and original compositions, each poem offers a unique perspective and lyrical beauty that will leave readers enchanted and inspired.

Genres: Poetry, Translations, Literature

Themes: Love, Nature, Life, Emotions
