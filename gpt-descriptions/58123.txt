Synopsis: In 'The Unfinished Programme of Democracy' by Richard Roberts, a renowned political scientist uncovers a hidden conspiracy that threatens to dismantle the very foundations of democracy. As he delves deeper into the shadows, he finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, power struggles, and moral dilemmas. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of corruption and betrayal to expose the truth and save democracy from its impending demise.

Genres: Political thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Power struggles, Morality, Betrayal
