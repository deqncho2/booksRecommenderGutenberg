Synopsis: 
"The Geologic Story of Arches National Park: Geological Survey Bulletin 1393" takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating geological history of Arches National Park. Written by Stanley William Lohman, this comprehensive guide explores the formation of the park's iconic arches, towering cliffs, and breathtaking landscapes. From ancient seas to powerful forces of erosion, Lohman unveils the secrets behind the park's unique geological features, providing a deeper understanding of the natural wonders that have captivated visitors for centuries.

Genres: Non-fiction, Geology, Travel

Themes: Geological formations, Natural wonders, Erosion, Environmental preservation
