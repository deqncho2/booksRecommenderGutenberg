Synopsis: In "Risen from the Ranks; Or, Harry Walton's Success" by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Harry Walton, a young and determined protagonist who faces numerous challenges and setbacks in his quest for success. From humble beginnings, Harry must navigate through a world filled with adversity, deceit, and unexpected opportunities. As he strives to rise above his circumstances, Harry's resilience and unwavering determination become his greatest assets. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles in his path and achieve the success he dreams of?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Social mobility, Morality
