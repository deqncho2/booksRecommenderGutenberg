Synopsis: 
In "The Motor Boys on a Ranch; or, Ned, Bob and Jerry Among the Cowboys" by Clarence Young, the adventurous trio of Ned, Bob, and Jerry find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey to a ranch in the Wild West. As they immerse themselves in the cowboy lifestyle, they encounter mysterious happenings and unexpected dangers that test their courage and resourcefulness. With their trusty motorbikes as their companions, the Motor Boys navigate through thrilling escapades, uncovering secrets and forming unexpected alliances along the way. Will they be able to solve the mysteries that surround the ranch and prove their mettle as true cowboys?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Western

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
