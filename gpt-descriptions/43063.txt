Synopsis: 
In 'A Case in Camera' by Oliver Onions, a renowned photographer is summoned to a remote country estate to capture a mysterious event on film. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic circumstances surrounding the event, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and supernatural occurrences. With each photograph he takes, the photographer uncovers unsettling truths that challenge his perception of reality and push the boundaries of his own sanity. As the tension mounts, he must confront the terrifying consequences of his pursuit for the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Secrets and Deception, Sanity and Madness
