Synopsis: 

In "Report on the Department of Ports and Harbours for the Year 1890-91," the Queensland Department of Ports and Harbours takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling maritime world of late 19th-century Australia. Through meticulous documentation and insightful analysis, this report unveils the challenges, triumphs, and innovations that shaped the ports and harbours during this pivotal year. From the construction of new infrastructure to the implementation of cutting-edge technologies, the report offers a fascinating glimpse into the dynamic and ever-evolving world of maritime transportation.

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, Government Report

Themes: Infrastructure development, Technological advancements, Maritime industry, Economic growth
