Synopsis: 
'Contemporary American Composers' by Rupert Hughes is a captivating study that delves into the world of music in America, exploring its current state and future prospects. Filled with critical evaluations, biographies of prominent living composers, and a wealth of portraits, facsimile musical autographs, and compositions, this book offers a comprehensive and engaging look into the vibrant landscape of American music.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Biography

Themes: American music, contemporary composers, artistic expression, cultural identity
