Synopsis: 
In "Bomba the jungle boy on Jaguar Island: Adrift on the river of mystery," Bomba finds himself on a thrilling adventure when he becomes stranded on a mysterious island inhabited by dangerous jaguars. As he navigates the treacherous jungle and encounters various perils, Bomba must rely on his survival skills and resourcefulness to uncover the island's secrets and find a way back home. With suspenseful twists and unexpected discoveries, this action-packed tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Jungle Fiction

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Courage
