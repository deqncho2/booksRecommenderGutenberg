Synopsis: In 'Familiar Animals' by Anonymous, a seemingly ordinary family moves into a charming old house in a small town. However, as they settle in, they begin to notice strange occurrences involving the local wildlife. Birds behaving oddly, squirrels with unusual abilities, and cats that seem to possess an uncanny intelligence. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they uncover a hidden world where animals are not what they seem, and their presence in the town is no coincidence. With danger lurking around every corner, the family must unravel the secrets of these familiar animals before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden worlds, the power of nature, the bond between humans and animals
