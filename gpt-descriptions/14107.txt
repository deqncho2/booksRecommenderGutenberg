Synopsis: In 'The Lost Stradivarius', a young man named John Maltravers becomes obsessed with a mysterious and hauntingly beautiful violin. As he delves deeper into the instrument's history, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to consume him. With its eerie atmosphere and gripping narrative, this gothic tale explores the power of music and the consequences of obsession.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, mystery

Themes: Obsession, the power of music, the supernatural, the past haunting the present
