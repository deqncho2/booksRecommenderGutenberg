Synopsis:
In 'Cousin Mary' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman named Mary finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. When her cousin unexpectedly arrives, Mary's life takes an intriguing turn as she navigates the complexities of family, love, and personal growth. As secrets unravel and relationships are tested, Mary must make difficult choices that will shape her future. This captivating tale explores the intricacies of human relationships and the pursuit of happiness in a world bound by tradition and duty.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Family dynamics, Self-discovery, Societal expectations
