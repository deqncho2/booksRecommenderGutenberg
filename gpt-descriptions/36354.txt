Synopsis: 

In 'Problems in Greek History', J. P. Mahaffy takes readers on a captivating journey through the complexities and mysteries of ancient Greece. From the rise of the city-states to the enigmatic Oracle of Delphi, Mahaffy delves into the fascinating world of Greek history, shedding light on the intriguing problems that have puzzled historians for centuries. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, he explores the enigmatic figures of Greek mythology, the intricacies of political systems, and the profound impact of Greek culture on the Western world. As readers navigate through the pages, they will uncover hidden truths, encounter unexpected twists, and gain a deeper understanding of the rich tapestry that is Greek history.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Mythology, Politics, Cultural influence
