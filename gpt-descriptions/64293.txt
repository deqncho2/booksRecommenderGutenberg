Synopsis: In 'Assignment in the Dawn' by Bryce Walton, a renowned scientist is mysteriously transported to a parallel universe where he must navigate a dangerous and unfamiliar world. As he struggles to find a way back home, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and must confront his own beliefs and values. With unexpected allies and formidable enemies, he embarks on a thrilling adventure that will test his resilience and challenge everything he thought he knew.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Parallel universes, Identity, Morality, Political intrigue
