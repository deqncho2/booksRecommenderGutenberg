Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts on the Trail; or, Scouting through the Big Game Country' by Herbert Carter, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts embark on a thrilling expedition through the untamed wilderness. As they navigate through the treacherous terrain, they encounter dangerous animals, face unexpected challenges, and uncover a mysterious secret that could change everything. With their survival skills and teamwork put to the test, the Boy Scouts must rely on their courage and resourcefulness to overcome the perils that await them in the big game country.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Friendship
