Synopsis: In 'Feudal tyrants; or, The Counts of Carlsheim and Sargans, volume 1', Benedikte Naubert takes us on a captivating journey through the treacherous world of feudal lords and their thirst for power. Set in the medieval era, this gripping tale follows the lives of two noble families, the Counts of Carlsheim and Sargans, as they navigate a web of deceit, betrayal, and political intrigue. As the power struggle intensifies, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the characters find themselves torn between loyalty and self-interest. With its rich historical backdrop and complex characters, this first volume sets the stage for an epic saga that will leave readers eagerly anticipating the next installment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Family Dynamics
