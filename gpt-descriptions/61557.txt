Synopsis: In "The Red Reign: The True Story of an Adventurous Year in Russia," Durland and Kellogg take readers on a captivating journey through the heart of Russia during a tumultuous time. Through their vivid storytelling, they recount their experiences and encounters with the Russian people, their customs, and the political climate that shaped their year-long adventure. From the bustling streets of Moscow to the serene landscapes of Siberia, the authors provide a unique perspective on the country's rich history and its complex present. As they navigate through the challenges and surprises that come their way, readers will be enthralled by the captivating narrative and the fascinating insights into Russian culture and society.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Non-fiction

Themes: Adventure, Cultural exploration, Political climate, Russian history and society
