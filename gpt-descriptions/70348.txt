Synopsis: In "Imitation of Death" by Del Rey, Lester, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he is hired to investigate a mysterious death that may not be what it seems. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a series of shocking secrets and uncanny imitations that challenge his perception of reality. With time running out, Lester must navigate a treacherous path of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth before it consumes him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Perception
