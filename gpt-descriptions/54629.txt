Synopsis: In 'Frank Reade, Jr., and His Electric Ice Ship; or, Driven Adrift in the Frozen Sky,' the brilliant inventor Frank Reade, Jr. embarks on a thrilling adventure aboard his latest creation, an electric ice ship. As he sets out to explore the uncharted frozen skies, he encounters treacherous obstacles, mysterious creatures, and unexpected allies. With danger lurking at every turn, Frank Reade, Jr. must rely on his wit, courage, and innovative technology to navigate through this icy world and uncover its secrets.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Steampunk

Themes: Exploration, Invention, Survival
