Synopsis: 
In "An Old Maid," Balzac introduces us to the life of a woman named Mademoiselle Cormon, who is known as the "Old Maid" in her small provincial town. Despite her wealth and beauty, she remains unmarried, causing much speculation and gossip among the townspeople. As suitors come and go, Mademoiselle Cormon's secrets and desires are gradually revealed, leading to unexpected twists and turns in her quest for love and happiness.

Genres: 
Realism, Satire, Comedy

Themes: 
Love and Marriage, Social Expectations, Deception, Desire
