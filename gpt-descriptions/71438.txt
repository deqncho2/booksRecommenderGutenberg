Synopsis: Discover the enchanting world of fine crochet and tatting in this captivating guide by Spool Cotton Co. Unveiling the secrets of delicate needlework, this book takes you on a journey through intricate patterns and exquisite designs that will leave you in awe. From dainty doilies to elegant edgings, each project is a testament to the artistry and skill of these timeless crafts. Whether you are a seasoned needleworker or a beginner, this book will inspire you to create stunning pieces that will be cherished for generations to come.

Genres: Crafts, Needlework, Art

Themes: Creativity, Tradition, Beauty
