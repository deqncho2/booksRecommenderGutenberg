Synopsis: In 'A Treatise on Grain Stacking', John N. De Lamater delves into the art of properly stacking various types of grain to ensure optimal preservation for threshing and market. With detailed instructions and expert advice, this guide offers invaluable insights into the techniques and methods necessary to maintain the quality and value of harvested grain. Whether you are a farmer, a grain enthusiast, or simply curious about the intricacies of grain preservation, this treatise will captivate your interest and provide you with practical knowledge to enhance your agricultural practices.

Genres: Non-fiction, Agricultural Literature, How-to Guide

Themes: Agricultural preservation, Farming techniques, Grain storage
