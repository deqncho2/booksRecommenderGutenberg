Synopsis: In 'The Old Man; or, Ravings and Ramblings round Conistone', the reader is transported to the picturesque village of Conistone, where an enigmatic old man captivates the townsfolk with his eccentric behavior and cryptic ramblings. As the villagers try to unravel the mysteries surrounding the old man, they are drawn into a web of secrets, folklore, and hidden truths that will challenge their perceptions of reality. With each revelation, the line between sanity and madness becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning the nature of truth and the power of the human mind.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Perception of reality, Madness, Secrets and hidden truths
