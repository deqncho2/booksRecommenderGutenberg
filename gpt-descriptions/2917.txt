Synopsis: In "The Skin Game (A Tragi-Comedy)" by John Galsworthy, two families from different social classes clash over land and morality. As the battle escalates, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the consequences become increasingly dire. This gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature and the destructive power of greed, ultimately leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the true cost of winning.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Comedy

Themes: Class conflict, Morality, Greed, Family dynamics
