Synopsis: In 'The entertaining story of King Brondé, his Lily and his Rosebud' by Diaz, Abby Morton, readers are transported to a whimsical kingdom where King Brondé finds himself torn between two captivating women, Lily and Rosebud. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the fate of the kingdom hangs in the balance. With its delightful blend of romance, intrigue, and humor, this enchanting tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Fantasy, Comedy

Themes: Love and desire, Loyalty and betrayal
