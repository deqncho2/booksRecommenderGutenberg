Synopsis:
Ernest Maltravers is a captivating tale that follows the life of the eponymous protagonist, a brilliant and enigmatic young man. From his unconventional upbringing to his encounters with love, ambition, and the pursuit of knowledge, Maltravers embarks on a journey that will test his character and shape his destiny. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century England, this novel delves into the complexities of human nature, exploring themes of passion, self-discovery, and the consequences of one's choices.

Genres:
Romantic fiction, Bildungsroman, Psychological fiction

Themes:
Love and desire, Ambition and success, Identity and self-discovery
