Synopsis: In 'An I.D.B. in South Africa' by Sheldon, Louise Vescelius, a young woman embarks on a journey to South Africa, where she becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the country, she uncovers shocking truths that challenge her perception of reality. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate through a labyrinth of deception and betrayal to uncover the ultimate truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Corruption, Power
