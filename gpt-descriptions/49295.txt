Synopsis: 
In the gripping novel 'After Taps' by George M. Baker and Rachel Baker Gale, a young soldier named Jack finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous secret mission during World War II. As he navigates the treacherous waters of espionage and betrayal, Jack must rely on his wit, courage, and the unexpected help of a mysterious woman to survive. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Jack's loyalty is put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to uncover the truth and make it out alive?

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: 
Espionage, Loyalty, Betrayal, Survival
