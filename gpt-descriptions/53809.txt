Synopsis: 

In "Saint John Chrysostom, His Life and Times: A sketch of the church and the empire in the fourth century," author W. R. W. Stephens takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and era of one of the most influential figures in early Christianity. This insightful biography delves into the fascinating world of Saint John Chrysostom, a renowned preacher and bishop, while also providing a vivid portrayal of the church and the empire during the fourth century. From his humble beginnings to his rise as a powerful spiritual leader, Chrysostom's story is filled with intrigue, challenges, and profound insights that continue to resonate today. 

Genres: Biography, History, Religion

Themes: Early Christianity, Church History, Spiritual Leadership
