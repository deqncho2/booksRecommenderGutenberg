Synopsis:
In the remote hills of Northeast India, a young British officer finds himself immersed in a world of ancient traditions and mysterious rituals. As he delves deeper into the lives of the indigenous Khasi people, he uncovers a web of secrets and hidden desires that threaten to unravel the very fabric of their society. With tensions rising and loyalties tested, the officer must navigate a treacherous path to protect the Khasis from external forces seeking to exploit their land and culture. But in doing so, he risks losing everything he holds dear, including his own identity.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Mystery, Cultural Exploration

Themes:
Cultural clash, Identity, Tradition vs. Modernity
