Synopsis:
'At Large' by E. W. Hornung is a thrilling mystery novel that follows the cunning and audacious adventures of A. J. Raffles, a gentleman thief with a penchant for danger. When a priceless artifact goes missing from a prestigious museum, Raffles finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, pursued by both the authorities and a mysterious rival. As he navigates the treacherous world of crime and deception, Raffles must outwit his adversaries and stay one step ahead to protect his reputation and freedom.

Genres:
Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes:
Deception, Morality, Identity
