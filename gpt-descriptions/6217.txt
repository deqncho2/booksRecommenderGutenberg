Synopsis: In the small French-Canadian village of St. Saviour, a scandalous secret threatens to unravel the prestigious Lavilette family. As the patriarch, Honore Lavilette, struggles to maintain his family's reputation, his daughter, Elise, finds herself torn between duty and desire. With the arrival of a mysterious stranger, the Lavilettes' carefully constructed facade begins to crumble, leading to a series of shocking events that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Family honor, societal expectations, forbidden love, secrets and betrayal
