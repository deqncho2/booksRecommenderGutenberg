Synopsis: 
'Captivating Bible Stories for Young People, Written in Simple Language' by Charlotte M. Yonge is a collection of timeless biblical tales retold in a captivating and accessible manner. From the creation of the world to the adventures of Moses and the miracles of Jesus, this book brings the stories of the Bible to life for young readers. With its engaging narrative style and beautiful illustrations, it is sure to captivate the hearts and minds of children, fostering a love for the Bible and its teachings.

Genres: Children's Literature, Religious Literature, Educational

Themes: Faith, Morality, Redemption, Miracles
