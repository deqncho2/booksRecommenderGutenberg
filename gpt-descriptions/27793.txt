Synopsis: In 'Child and Country: A Book of the Younger Generation', Will Levington Comfort takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of young individuals as they navigate the challenges and complexities of growing up in early 20th century America. Through a series of interconnected stories, the book explores the joys, sorrows, dreams, and aspirations of these vibrant characters, painting a vivid portrait of youth and the pursuit of happiness. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene countryside, this poignant collection of tales captures the essence of a generation on the brink of change.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Youth, Identity, Social change, Dreams and aspirations
