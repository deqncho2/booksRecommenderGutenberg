Synopsis: In 'The Glebe 1914/01 (Vol. 1, No. 4): Love of One's Neighbor' by Andreyev, Leonid, a small village is shaken by a mysterious event that tests the limits of human compassion. As the villagers grapple with their own desires and fears, a profound question arises: how far will one go to help their neighbor in times of crisis? This gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature and the power of empathy in the face of adversity.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Compassion, Morality, Human Nature
