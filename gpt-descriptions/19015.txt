Synopsis: 
In 'Jane Allen, Right Guard' by Edith Bancroft, readers are introduced to the spirited and determined Jane Allen, a young woman who defies societal expectations and challenges the status quo. As Jane navigates her way through a world dominated by men, she finds herself drawn to a cause that will test her courage and resilience. With her unwavering determination and a touch of audacity, Jane becomes an unexpected force to be reckoned with, fighting for justice and equality in a world that often dismisses her. As she faces numerous obstacles and confronts her own fears, Jane's journey becomes a captivating tale of empowerment and self-discovery.

Genres: 
Adventure, Historical Fiction, Feminist Literature

Themes: 
Gender equality, Courage, Empowerment
