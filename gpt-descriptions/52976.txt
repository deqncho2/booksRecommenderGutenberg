Synopsis: 
"The Great Lakes: The Vessels That Plough Them" takes readers on a captivating journey through the vast and treacherous waters of the Great Lakes. James Oliver Curwood delves into the fascinating world of the ships that navigate these inland seas, exploring the lives of their owners, sailors, and the valuable cargoes they transport. Alongside this exploration, Curwood provides a brief history of the Great Lakes, shedding light on the challenges and triumphs that have shaped these remarkable bodies of water. From thrilling tales of shipwrecks to the resilience of those who call the Great Lakes their home, this book offers a captivating glimpse into a world few have experienced.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Maritime

Themes: Exploration, Adventure, Resilience, Maritime history
