Synopsis: 

In 'Commander Lawless V.C.: Being the Further Adventures of Frank H. Lawless, Until Recently a Lieutenant in His Majesty's Navy' by Bennett, Rolf, readers are taken on an exhilarating journey alongside the courageous Commander Frank H. Lawless. After his promotion to the rank of Commander, Lawless finds himself embarking on a series of thrilling and dangerous missions that test his skills, bravery, and loyalty to the crown. From treacherous sea battles to covert operations behind enemy lines, Lawless must navigate the complexities of war while facing personal challenges that push him to his limits. As the story unfolds, readers will be captivated by Lawless' unwavering determination and the unexpected twists and turns that await him.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Perseverance, Sacrifice
