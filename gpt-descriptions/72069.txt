Synopsis: In "Go to sleep, my darling" by Winston K. Marks, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a series of mysterious deaths occur in a small, isolated town. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers dark secrets and uncanny occurrences that challenge his rationality and push the boundaries of his own sanity. With each twist and turn, the detective must confront his own fears and confront the malevolent force that lurks in the shadows, threatening to consume him and the town. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Darkness and light, Sanity and madness, Uncovering hidden truths
