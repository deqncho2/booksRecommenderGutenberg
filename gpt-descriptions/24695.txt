Synopsis: In 'The Snowshoe Trail' by Marshall Edison, a young woman embarks on a winter adventure through the breathtaking Canadian wilderness. As she navigates the treacherous snow-covered terrain, she discovers hidden secrets and encounters unexpected dangers that test her resilience and determination. With each step, she unravels a mystery that will change her life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Discovery, Resilience
