Synopsis: In "King of the Jews: A story of Christ's last days on Earth," author W. T. Stead takes readers on a captivating journey through the final days of Jesus Christ. Set against the backdrop of ancient Jerusalem, this gripping narrative explores the political and religious tensions of the time, as well as the profound impact of Christ's teachings on those who encountered him. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous historical research, Stead brings to life the events leading up to the crucifixion, shedding new light on the complex dynamics between Jesus, his disciples, and the ruling powers of the day. As the story unfolds, readers are drawn into a world of intrigue, betrayal, and ultimate redemption, leaving them with a deeper understanding of the profound significance of Christ's sacrifice.

Genres: Historical fiction, Religious fiction, Drama

Themes: Faith, Sacrifice, Redemption, Power dynamics, Betrayal
