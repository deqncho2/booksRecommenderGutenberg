Synopsis: 
In 'Fanny Lambert: A Novel', we are introduced to the enigmatic and captivating Fanny Lambert, a young woman with a mysterious past and an irresistible allure. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows Fanny as she navigates through the complexities of love, betrayal, and societal expectations. As her secrets slowly unravel, Fanny finds herself torn between the pursuit of her own happiness and the constraints of a rigid society. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, this novel takes readers on a journey filled with passion, intrigue, and the timeless quest for self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Love and Desire, Social Conventions
