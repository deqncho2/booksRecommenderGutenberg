Synopsis: In 'A Country Gentleman and His Family' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), follow the captivating journey of a wealthy landowner and his family as they navigate the complexities of rural life in Victorian England. From the idyllic countryside to the bustling city, the family's fortunes and relationships are tested, revealing hidden secrets and unexpected alliances. With a cast of intriguing characters and a richly detailed setting, this novel explores the delicate balance between tradition and progress, love and duty, and the enduring power of family bonds.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Family Drama, Romance

Themes: Social class, Tradition vs. modernity, Family dynamics, Love and duty
