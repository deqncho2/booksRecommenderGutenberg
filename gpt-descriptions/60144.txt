Synopsis: 

'Under Six Flags: The Story of Texas' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of Texas, from its early days as a Spanish colony to its eventual independence and beyond. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, author M.E.M. Davis brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and colorful characters that shaped the Lone Star State. From the Alamo to the oil boom, from cattle drives to the birth of the Texas Rangers, this book offers a comprehensive and engaging exploration of Texas' unique heritage. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Texan history

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Cultural diversity, Frontier spirit
