Synopsis: In the lively comedy of manners, "The Beaux-Stratagem," two young gentlemen, Aimwell and Archer, embark on a mischievous adventure in the countryside. Disguised as master and servant, they set out to seduce wealthy women and secure their fortunes. However, their plans take an unexpected turn when they encounter a variety of eccentric characters, including a cunning innkeeper, a flirtatious widow, and a beautiful heiress. As their deceptions unravel, Aimwell and Archer find themselves entangled in a web of love, deceit, and unexpected alliances. Will they succeed in their cunning schemes or will they be outwitted by their own desires?

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Restoration Drama

Themes: Love and romance, Deception and disguise, Social class and wealth
