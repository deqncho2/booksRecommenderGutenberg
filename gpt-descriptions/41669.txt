Synopsis: 
In 'Home-made Toys for Girls and Boys: Wooden and Cardboard Toys, Mechanical and Electric Toys', author A. Neely Hall takes readers on a delightful journey into the world of DIY toys. Filled with detailed instructions and illustrations, this book offers a treasure trove of ideas for creating unique and imaginative toys using simple materials like wood and cardboard. From classic wooden puzzles and spinning tops to intricate mechanical and electric toys, Hall's guide provides endless inspiration for both children and adults alike. Discover the joy of crafting your own toys and watch as your creations come to life!

Genres: DIY, Crafts, Children's Literature

Themes: Creativity, Imagination, Hands-on Learning
