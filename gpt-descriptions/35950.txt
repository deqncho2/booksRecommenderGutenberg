Synopsis: 

In 'Lives of Poor Boys Who Became Famous' by Sarah Knowles Bolton, embark on a captivating journey through the lives of remarkable individuals who defied the odds and rose from humble beginnings to achieve greatness. From artists to inventors, writers to scientists, this collection of inspiring biographies unveils the extraordinary stories of these once-poor boys who left an indelible mark on history. Discover the trials they faced, the obstacles they overcame, and the unwavering determination that propelled them towards their dreams. 

Genres: Biography, Non-fiction, Inspirational

Themes: Perseverance, Ambition, Overcoming adversity
