Synopsis: In 'The Debit Account' by Oliver Onions, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, offering the residents a chance to fulfill their deepest desires. However, as they indulge in their newfound pleasures, they soon discover that there is a heavy price to pay. As the town becomes consumed by their own desires, a young woman must uncover the truth behind the stranger's intentions before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Temptation, Consequences, Morality
