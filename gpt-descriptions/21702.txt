Synopsis: In 'Shifting Winds: A Tough Yarn' by R. M. Ballantyne, a young sailor named Jack finds himself caught in a treacherous storm while aboard a ship bound for the South Seas. Shipwrecked on a remote island, Jack must navigate the challenges of survival, encountering mysterious creatures and unexpected allies along the way. As he strives to find a way back home, Jack's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test in this thrilling adventure.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Young Adult

Themes: Resilience, Survival, Friendship, Exploration
