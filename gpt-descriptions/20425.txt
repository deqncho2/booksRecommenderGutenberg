Synopsis: In 'The Peace Egg and Other Tales', Juliana Horatia Ewing takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting and thought-provoking stories. From the magical adventures of a talking doll to the heartwarming tale of a young girl's determination to save her family, each story is filled with wonder, imagination, and valuable life lessons. With its delightful blend of fantasy, adventure, and moral teachings, this collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Moral Tales

Themes: Imagination, Determination, Morality, Family, Friendship
