Synopsis: 
In 'Imprudence' by F. E. Mills Young, a young woman named Sylvia finds herself caught in a web of secrets and scandal when she becomes entangled with a mysterious and alluring stranger. As Sylvia delves deeper into his world, she must navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and the consequences of her own imprudent actions. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Love and passion, Deception and betrayal
