Synopsis: 
'Florence on a Certain Night, and Other Poems' by Dawson, Coningsby is a collection of captivating and evocative poems that transport readers to the enchanting city of Florence. Through vivid imagery and lyrical language, the poems explore various themes such as love, loss, longing, and the beauty of the natural world. From moonlit streets to ancient architecture, the collection paints a mesmerizing portrait of Florence and its profound impact on the human spirit.

Genres: Poetry, Travel, Romance

Themes: Love, Loss, Longing, Beauty of Nature
