Synopsis: In 'The Yellow Flag: A Novel. Volume 1 (of 3)' by Edmund Yates, a mysterious yellow flag becomes the symbol of a hidden secret that threatens to unravel the lives of the characters. Set in the vibrant streets of Victorian London, this gripping tale follows the intertwined destinies of a diverse cast of characters, including a young artist, a beautiful actress, and a cunning detective. As they navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and deception, they soon discover that the yellow flag holds the key to a dark and dangerous truth that could change their lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Love and betrayal, Society and class dynamics
