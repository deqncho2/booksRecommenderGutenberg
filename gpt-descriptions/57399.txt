Synopsis: 
'Village Folk-Tales of Ceylon, Volume 2 (of 3)' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Ceylonese folklore. This collection of folk tales, passed down through generations, unveils a tapestry of magical creatures, mythical beings, and extraordinary adventures. From cunning tricksters to benevolent spirits, each story is a testament to the rich cultural heritage of Ceylon. As readers delve into these captivating tales, they will be transported to a realm where imagination knows no bounds and where the line between reality and fantasy becomes beautifully blurred.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Cultural heritage, Mythology, Magic
