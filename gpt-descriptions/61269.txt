Synopsis: 
In 'The Sea Road to the East, Gibraltar to Wei-hai-wei', A. J. Sargent takes readers on a captivating journey along the ancient sea route connecting Gibraltar to Wei-hai-wei. Through six engaging lectures, Sargent explores the rich history, diverse cultures, and breathtaking landscapes encountered along this remarkable maritime path. From bustling ports to remote islands, readers will be transported to a world of exotic destinations, encountering fascinating characters and witnessing the challenges faced by those who traversed these treacherous waters. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Sargent paints a vivid picture of the sea road to the East, leaving readers eager to embark on their own imaginary voyage.

Genres: Travelogue, History, Cultural Exploration
Themes: Maritime history, Cultural exchange, Exploration
