Synopsis: In the thrilling conclusion of the Mr. Jervis series, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the fate of our beloved protagonist hangs in the balance. As Mr. Jervis navigates a treacherous web of deceit and danger, he must confront his past and make difficult choices that will determine the course of his future. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Identity
