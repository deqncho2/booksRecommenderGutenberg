Synopsis: In the Mayor's Parlour by Fletcher, J.S. takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of a small English town. When a mysterious murder occurs, the mayor finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, corruption, and scandal. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel the very fabric of the community. With danger lurking around every corner, the mayor must navigate treacherous alliances and confront his own demons to bring justice to the town he loves.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Corruption, Betrayal, Power dynamics, Justice
