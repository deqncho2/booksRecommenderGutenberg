Synopsis: In 'Hauntings: Fantastic Stories' by Lee, Vernon, prepare to be transported to a world where the line between the living and the dead is blurred. From eerie encounters in haunted houses to chilling encounters with vengeful spirits, this collection of supernatural tales will leave you questioning what lies beyond the realm of the living. With each story more spine-tingling than the last, 'Hauntings' will keep you on the edge of your seat, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the mysteries that lurk in the shadows.

Genres: Horror, Gothic Fiction, Supernatural Fiction

Themes: Ghosts, Hauntings, Death, Fear, Mystery
