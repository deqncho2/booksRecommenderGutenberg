Synopsis: In "The Motor Maids Across the Continent," a group of adventurous young women embark on a thrilling cross-country journey in their trusty automobiles. As they navigate through various landscapes and encounter unexpected challenges, they form unbreakable bonds and discover the true meaning of friendship. With each mile they conquer, the Motor Maids prove that determination and courage can lead to extraordinary adventures.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Female Empowerment
