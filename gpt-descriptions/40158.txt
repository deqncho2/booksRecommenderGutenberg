Synopsis: In 'Manners: A Novel, Vol 1' by Panache, Madame, readers are transported to the elegant and scandalous world of 18th-century Paris. The story revolves around the enigmatic Madame de Manners, a woman of mysterious origins and impeccable manners, who captivates the high society with her charm and wit. As the narrative unfolds, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the boundaries of societal norms are pushed to their limits. With a cast of intriguing characters and a backdrop of lavish balls and clandestine affairs, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the intricate web of French aristocracy, where appearances can be deceiving and manners can hide a multitude of sins.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Deception, Social Class, Power
