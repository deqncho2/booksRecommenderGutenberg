Synopsis: In "The Quilt of Happiness; Creeping Jenny; and Other New England Stories," Kate Douglas Smith Wiggin takes readers on a captivating journey through the charming landscapes of New England. From heartwarming tales of love and friendship to unexpected twists and turns, this collection of stories will transport readers to a world filled with joy, sorrow, and the enduring spirit of the region. Whether it's the tale of a mysterious quilt that holds the key to happiness or the story of Creeping Jenny, a young girl with a unique gift, each narrative is sure to captivate and leave readers wanting more.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Regional Literature

Themes: Love, Friendship, Community, New England Life
