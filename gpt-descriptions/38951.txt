Synopsis: In 'The Curiosities of Heraldry' by Mark Antony Lower, embark on a captivating journey through the intricate world of heraldry, where coats of arms hold secrets, symbols, and stories waiting to be unraveled. From the origins of heraldry to the fascinating tales behind each emblem, this book unveils the hidden meanings and historical significance behind these ancient symbols. Delve into the pages and discover the captivating stories of knights, noble families, and their unique coats of arms, as well as the intriguing connections between heraldry and mythology. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, 'The Curiosities of Heraldry' is a captivating exploration of a timeless art form that continues to intrigue and inspire.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Art

Themes: Symbolism, History, Identity
