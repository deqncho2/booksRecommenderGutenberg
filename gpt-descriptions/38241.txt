Synopsis: In "Uncle's Dream; and The Permanent Husband," Dostoyevsky takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of two very different protagonists. In "Uncle's Dream," we follow the story of a cunning and ambitious uncle who will stop at nothing to achieve his desires. Meanwhile, in "The Permanent Husband," we delve into the complex dynamics of a troubled marriage and the unexpected consequences that arise. These two novellas intertwine themes of ambition, deception, love, and the human capacity for self-deception.

Genres: Fiction, Psychological Thriller, Satire

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Love, Marriage, Self-deception
