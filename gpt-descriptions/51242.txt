Synopsis: 

In "Electricity in Locomotion: An Account of Its Mechanism, Its Achievements, and Its Prospects," author Adam Gowens takes readers on an electrifying journey through the fascinating world of electric transportation. From the early experiments with electric vehicles to the cutting-edge advancements in electric propulsion, this book explores the remarkable history and future potential of electricity in locomotion. With captivating anecdotes and insightful analysis, Gowens delves into the mechanisms behind electric propulsion, highlighting the achievements and breakthroughs that have revolutionized the way we move. As the reader delves deeper into the pages, they will be left in awe of the incredible possibilities that lie ahead in the realm of electric transportation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Technology

Themes: Electric propulsion, Transportation history, Technological advancements
