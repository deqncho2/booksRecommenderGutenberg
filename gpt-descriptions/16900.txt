Synopsis: 
In 'The First Book of Farming', author C. L. Goodrich takes readers on an immersive journey into the world of farming, offering a comprehensive guide filled with practical advice, insightful anecdotes, and fascinating historical context. From the basics of soil preparation and crop selection to the intricacies of animal husbandry and sustainable farming practices, this book serves as an invaluable resource for both novice and experienced farmers alike. With vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, Goodrich paints a vivid picture of the joys and challenges of life on the farm, inspiring readers to connect with the land and embrace the timeless art of agriculture.

Genres: Non-fiction, Agriculture, Farming

Themes: Sustainability, Self-sufficiency, Connection to nature
