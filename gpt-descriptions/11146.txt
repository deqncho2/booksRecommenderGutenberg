Synopsis: 

"The Expedition of the Donner Party and its Tragic Fate" by Eliza Poor Donner Houghton is a gripping and harrowing account of one of the most infamous journeys in American history. Set in the mid-19th century, this true story follows a group of pioneers as they embark on a treacherous expedition across the Sierra Nevada Mountains to reach the promised land of California. However, as they face numerous challenges and setbacks along the way, their journey takes a dark and tragic turn that will test their resilience and humanity to the core.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Perseverance, Human Nature, Tragedy
