Synopsis: In "Cursory Observations on the Poems Attributed to Thomas Rowley," Edmond Malone delves into the mysterious world of ancient manuscripts and literary forgery. With meticulous research and critical analysis, Malone investigates the authenticity of the poems attributed to the enigmatic poet Thomas Rowley. As he unravels the layers of history and deception, Malone uncovers startling revelations that challenge the established literary canon. This captivating exploration of literary intrigue will leave readers questioning the boundaries between truth and fiction.

Genres: Literary criticism, historical analysis, detective work

Themes: Authenticity, literary forgery, historical context
