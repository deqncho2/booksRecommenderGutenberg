Synopsis: In "Economic Sophisms," Frédéric Bastiat takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the world of economics, challenging commonly held misconceptions and exposing the fallacies that hinder economic progress. Through a series of engaging and witty essays, Bastiat dismantles popular economic sophisms, revealing the hidden truths behind them and shedding light on the importance of free trade, individual liberty, and the unintended consequences of government intervention. With his sharp intellect and persuasive arguments, Bastiat encourages readers to question prevailing economic dogmas and embrace the principles of economic freedom.

Genres: Economics, Political Philosophy, Satire

Themes: Free trade, Individual liberty, Government intervention, Economic fallacies, Unintended consequences
