Synopsis: 
'Twentieth Century Culture and Deportment' is a comprehensive guidebook that offers a wealth of knowledge on etiquette and self-culture for both men and women. From social occasions like calls, parties, weddings, and dinners, to navigating public spaces and mastering the art of conversation, this book covers it all. It also delves into topics such as courtship, letter-writing, and artistic home decorations, providing readers with a complete guide to refined living in the twentieth century.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Etiquette guide

Themes: Etiquette, Self-improvement, Social norms
