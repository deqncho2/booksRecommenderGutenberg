Synopsis: In 'The Lords of the Wild: A Story of the Old New York Border', Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on a thrilling journey to the untamed wilderness of the New York border. Set during the French and Indian War, the story follows a young frontiersman named Robert Lennox as he navigates the treacherous landscape, facing danger, intrigue, and unexpected alliances. As Lennox becomes entangled in the conflict between the British and French forces, he must rely on his courage, resourcefulness, and loyalty to survive. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Altsheler paints a vivid picture of a forgotten era and the clash of empires that shaped the American frontier.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Conflict, Exploration
