Synopsis: In the bustling city of Agrabah, a young street urchin named Aladdin embarks on a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a magical lamp containing a powerful genie. With the help of his newfound friend, Aladdin must navigate through treacherous obstacles, face an evil sorcerer, and ultimately discover the true meaning of love and self-worth.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Overcoming adversity
