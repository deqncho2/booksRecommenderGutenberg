Synopsis: In 'Ladies Must Live' by Alice Duer Miller, a young woman named Grace finds herself at a crossroads when her life takes an unexpected turn. Faced with societal expectations and personal desires, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment. As Grace navigates the challenges of love, independence, and identity, she must confront the limitations placed upon women in the early 20th century. Will she find the courage to break free from convention and forge her own path?

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Gender roles, Self-determination, Societal expectations
