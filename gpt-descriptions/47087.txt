Synopsis: In 'Motor Matt's Race; or, The Last Flight of the Comet', Motor Matt, a skilled young racer, finds himself entangled in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a mysterious race that promises a grand prize. As he competes against formidable opponents, he soon discovers that there is more at stake than just winning the race. With danger lurking at every turn, Motor Matt must rely on his wit, courage, and racing skills to uncover the truth and save the day.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Courage, Determination, Friendship, Pursuit of Truth
