Synopsis: In 'The Orange Fairy Book', readers will embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales from various cultures around the world. From magical creatures and brave heroes to cunning tricksters and forbidden love, this anthology offers a delightful blend of adventure, mystery, and wonder. Each story is beautifully woven with vivid imagery and unexpected twists, leaving readers spellbound and eager to discover what lies beyond the next page. 

Genres: Fairy tales, folklore, fantasy

Themes: Adventure, magic, love, morality, cultural diversity
