Synopsis: 

'Political Women, Vol. 1' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of influential women who defied societal norms and made their mark in the political arena. From the corridors of power to the battlefields of change, this book unveils the untold stories of these remarkable women who challenged the status quo and fought for their beliefs. With gripping narratives and intriguing anecdotes, 'Political Women, Vol. 1' sheds light on the struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices of these trailblazers, leaving readers inspired and eager to discover more about the unsung heroines of history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Women's Studies

Themes: Gender equality, Political activism, Social change
