Synopsis: The Bobbsey Twins, Nan and Bert, embark on a thrilling seaside adventure filled with mysterious clues, hidden treasures, and unexpected encounters. As they explore the sandy shores, they stumble upon a secret that could change their lives forever. With their quick thinking and determination, the Bobbsey Twins must unravel the puzzle before it's too late. Will they be able to solve the mystery and save the day?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Family, Friendship, Curiosity, Problem-solving
