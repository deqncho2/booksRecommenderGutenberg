Synopsis: In 'The Vitamine Manual', Eddy, Walter Hollis takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of vitamins, unraveling their fascinating history, exploring their various types, and revealing their incredible impact on our health and well-being. From the discovery of the first vitamin to the latest scientific breakthroughs, this comprehensive manual provides a wealth of knowledge and practical advice on how to optimize our vitamin intake for optimal health. Packed with intriguing anecdotes, surprising facts, and expert insights, this book is a must-read for anyone seeking to unlock the secrets of vitamins and harness their incredible potential.

Genres: Non-fiction, Health, Science

Themes: Nutrition, Wellness, Medical advancements
