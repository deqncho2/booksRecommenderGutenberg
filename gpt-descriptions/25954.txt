Synopsis: In 'The Opened Shutters: A Novel' by Clara Louise Burnham, a young woman named Mary is faced with a life-altering decision when she discovers a hidden secret within her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Mary finds herself torn between loyalty to her family and the pursuit of her own happiness. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, this captivating novel explores the complexities of love, duty, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Independence, Societal expectations
