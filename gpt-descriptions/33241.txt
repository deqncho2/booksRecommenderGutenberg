Synopsis: 
'Letters of a Lunatic: A Brief Exposition of My University Life, During the Years 1853-54' takes readers on a captivating journey through the mind of a young university student named George. Through a series of letters, George shares his experiences, struggles, and triumphs as he navigates the complexities of university life in the mid-19th century. From his encounters with eccentric professors to his passionate pursuit of knowledge, George's letters offer a unique and intimate glimpse into the world of academia during a transformative period in history.

Genres: 
Epistolary fiction, Historical fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Education, Mental health, Identity, Society
