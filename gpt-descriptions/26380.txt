Synopsis: 

In 'Complete Story of the San Francisco Horror', White, Trumbull, Fallows, Samuel, and Linthicum take readers on a gripping journey through the devastating events of the 1906 San Francisco earthquake and subsequent fire. Through vivid descriptions and firsthand accounts, the authors paint a harrowing picture of the chaos, destruction, and resilience that unfolded during this catastrophic event. From the initial tremors to the city's remarkable recovery, this book offers a comprehensive and captivating narrative that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Disaster

Themes: Natural disasters, Resilience, Human spirit
