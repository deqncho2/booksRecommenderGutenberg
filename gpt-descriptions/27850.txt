Synopsis: 
In "The Young Alaskans in the Rockies" by Hough, Emerson, four adventurous friends find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey through the majestic Rocky Mountains. As they navigate treacherous terrain, encounter wild animals, and face unexpected challenges, their bond is tested and their courage is pushed to the limit. With danger lurking at every turn, the young Alaskans must rely on their resourcefulness and determination to survive and achieve their ultimate goal. 

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Action

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Exploration
