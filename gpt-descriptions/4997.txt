Synopsis: In 'Two Boys and a Fortune; Or, The Tyler Will' by Matthew White, two young boys find themselves unexpectedly entangled in a mysterious inheritance. As they embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the fortune, they encounter unexpected allies and face dangerous adversaries. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following the boys' journey to unlock the secrets of the Tyler Will.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Family, Inheritance, Discovery
