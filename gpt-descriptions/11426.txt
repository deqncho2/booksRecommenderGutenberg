Synopsis: In 'The Call of the North' by Stewart Edward White, a young man named Jack is drawn to the untamed wilderness of the Canadian North. Leaving behind his comfortable life, he embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, survival, and unexpected encounters. As Jack navigates through the harsh landscapes and encounters the indigenous people of the region, he discovers a profound connection to nature and a deeper understanding of himself. Will he succumb to the call of the wild or find his way back to the life he left behind?

Genres: Adventure, Wilderness Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Man vs. Nature
