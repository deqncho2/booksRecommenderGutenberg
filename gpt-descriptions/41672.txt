Synopsis: In 'A Changed Heart: A Novel' by Fleming, May Agnes, a young woman named Edith is torn between duty and desire when she finds herself caught in a web of secrets and forbidden love. Set in the 19th century, this captivating tale follows Edith's journey as she navigates the complexities of societal expectations, personal growth, and the transformative power of love. As her heart undergoes a profound change, Edith must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Forbidden Love, Personal Growth, Societal Expectations
