Synopsis: In 'Parodies of the works of English & American authors, vol. III', prepare to embark on a hilarious journey through the literary world as renowned authors are playfully spoofed and their iconic works turned upside down. From the misadventures of a bumbling detective to the whimsical tales of a peculiar tea party, this collection of parodies will have you laughing out loud and marveling at the clever wit and satire on display. Get ready to see beloved characters and famous stories in a whole new light, as this volume takes you on a delightful literary rollercoaster ride.

Genres: Parody, Satire, Humor

Themes: Literary criticism, Subversion of expectations
