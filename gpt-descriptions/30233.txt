Synopsis: 
'Folk-lore in Borneo: a sketch' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Borneo's folklore. Written by William Henry Furness, this book delves into the rich tapestry of myths, legends, and traditional tales that have been passed down through generations in this mystical land. From ancient spirits and mythical creatures to heroic adventures and forbidden love, the stories within this collection transport readers to a realm where imagination knows no bounds. With vivid descriptions and captivating narratives, 'Folk-lore in Borneo: a sketch' offers a glimpse into the cultural heritage and fantastical beliefs of the people of Borneo.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Travelogue

Themes: Cultural heritage, Mythical creatures, Adventure, Love and romance
