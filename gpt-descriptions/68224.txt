Synopsis: 

In "Synthetic resins and their raw materials," the United States Tariff Commission delves into a comprehensive exploration of the types and uses of synthetic resins, the industry's organization, and the trade in resins and raw materials. With a particular focus on factors crucial to tariff consideration, this survey offers valuable insights into the intricate world of synthetic resins and their economic implications. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Economics

Themes: Industrial organization, Trade regulations, Tariff considerations
