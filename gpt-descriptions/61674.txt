Synopsis: In 'War-Lords of the Moon' by Davies, Linton, a group of astronauts embarks on a perilous mission to the moon, only to discover a hidden civilization ruled by powerful warlords. As they navigate the treacherous lunar landscape, they must confront ancient secrets, face deadly challenges, and make difficult choices that could determine the fate of both Earth and the moon. With its thrilling blend of science fiction, adventure, and mystery, this captivating novel takes readers on an unforgettable journey to a world beyond our own.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Power and Control, Survival
