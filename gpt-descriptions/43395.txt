Synopsis: In "The Washer of the Ford: Legendary moralities and barbaric tales" by William Sharp, readers are transported to a world of ancient legends and captivating tales. This collection of stories takes us on a journey through the realms of myth and folklore, where heroes and heroines face extraordinary challenges and make fateful choices. From the hauntingly beautiful tale of a mysterious washerwoman to the thrilling adventures of warriors and kings, each story is a mesmerizing blend of morality and barbarism. With vivid imagery and lyrical prose, Sharp weaves together a tapestry of legends that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fantasy, Mythology, Folklore

Themes: Morality, Heroism, Fate, Transformation
