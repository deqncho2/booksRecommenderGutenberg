Synopsis: 

'Wigwam Evenings: Sioux Folk Tales Retold' takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich oral tradition of the Sioux people. Through a collection of enchanting folk tales, Charles A. Eastman and Elaine Goodale Eastman bring to life the vibrant culture, wisdom, and spirituality of the Sioux tribe. From tales of brave warriors and cunning tricksters to stories of love, loss, and the power of nature, this book offers a mesmerizing glimpse into the heart and soul of the Sioux people. 

Genres: Folklore, Native American Literature, Cultural Studies

Themes: Native American spirituality, Wisdom of oral tradition, Cultural preservation
