Synopsis:
In "Lady William" by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman named Lady William finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she marries a man she barely knows. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, she discovers shocking truths about her husband and the people around her. With her world turned upside down, Lady William must unravel the mysteries that surround her and find the strength to forge her own path.

Genres:
Mystery, Romance, Victorian Literature

Themes:
Deception, Identity, Independence
