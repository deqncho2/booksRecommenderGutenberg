Synopsis:
In "Ruth Arnold: or, the Country Cousin," the reader is introduced to Ruth, a young woman from the countryside who finds herself thrust into the bustling city life. As she navigates the challenges and adventures that come her way, Ruth's innocence and charm captivate those around her, including a dashing young gentleman who becomes enamored with her. However, Ruth's country upbringing and the expectations of society threaten to keep them apart. Will Ruth be able to find her place in this new world and follow her heart?

Genres:
Romance, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Love and sacrifice, Social class and expectations, Self-discovery
