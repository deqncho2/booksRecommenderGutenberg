Synopsis: 
In "To Make a Hero" by Randall Garrett, a renowned scientist named Dr. John Wilson embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that has the potential to change the course of humanity. As he delves deeper into his research, he discovers a hidden world of power and manipulation, where the line between hero and villain becomes blurred. With the fate of society hanging in the balance, Dr. Wilson must navigate a treacherous path of moral dilemmas and unexpected alliances to uncover the truth and determine the destiny of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Morality, Power, Manipulation
