Synopsis: In 'The Disappearance of Kimball Webb' by Wright Rowland, a small town is left in shock when a beloved high school teacher mysteriously vanishes without a trace. As the community grapples with the unsettling absence, secrets begin to unravel, revealing a web of deception, hidden desires, and unexpected connections. With each revelation, the search for Kimball Webb takes unexpected turns, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Disappearance, Secrets, Deception, Community, Identity
