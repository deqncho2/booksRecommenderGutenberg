Synopsis: In 'Dick Hamilton's Football Team; Or, A Young Millionaire On The Gridiron' by Howard Roger Garis, readers are introduced to Dick Hamilton, a young millionaire who decides to form his own football team. As the team faces various challenges and obstacles, Dick's determination and leadership skills are put to the test. With a mix of thrilling football action, unexpected twists, and a cast of diverse characters, this story takes readers on an exciting journey both on and off the gridiron.

Genres: Sports, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Determination, Leadership, Teamwork
