Synopsis: In 'The Arabian Art of Taming and Training Wild & Vicious Horses', Kincaid and Stutzman delve into the captivating world of Arabian horsemanship, revealing ancient techniques and wisdom that have been passed down through generations. This comprehensive guide takes readers on a journey through the Arabian Peninsula, exploring the unique bond between humans and horses and the extraordinary methods used to tame and train these magnificent creatures. Filled with captivating anecdotes and practical advice, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the art of horse training, showcasing the remarkable skills and deep understanding of the Arabian horsemen. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Training, Equestrian

Themes: Horsemanship, Arabian culture, Animal-human connection
