Synopsis: In 'Macaria' by Augusta J. Evans, a young woman named Macaria finds herself caught in the midst of the American Civil War. As she navigates the chaos and uncertainty of the times, Macaria becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and sacrifice. With her courage and determination, she must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and the lives of those around her. Set against the backdrop of war-torn America, 'Macaria' is a captivating tale of resilience, loyalty, and the power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, Love and sacrifice, Strength and resilience in the face of adversity
