Synopsis: In the small Scottish village of Thrums, a collection of charming and humorous stories unfold, showcasing the lives and quirks of the Auld Lichts, a strict religious sect. From the misadventures of Tammas Haggart, the village bully, to the romantic escapades of Davie Lunn, a young man torn between love and duty, these idyllic tales offer a delightful glimpse into the colorful characters and traditions of a bygone era.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Humor

Themes: Small-town life, Religion, Love and romance, Community dynamics
