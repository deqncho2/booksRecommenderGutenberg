Synopsis: 

"The Book of the Pearl: The history, art, science, and industry of the queen of gems" takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of pearls. From ancient civilizations to modern times, this book delves into the rich history, intricate artistry, scientific marvels, and thriving industry surrounding these exquisite gems. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, the authors unveil the secrets behind the allure and mystique of pearls, revealing their significance in various cultures and their impact on the global market. Whether you are a lover of history, art, science, or simply intrigued by the beauty of pearls, this book promises to be an enlightening and enchanting exploration.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Art, Science

Themes: Beauty and aesthetics, Cultural significance, Economic impact
