Synopsis: In 'A Pair of Clogs' by Amy Walton, two young sisters embark on a journey that will change their lives forever. Leaving their small village in England, they find themselves in a bustling city, facing challenges and discovering unexpected opportunities. As they navigate the complexities of their new lives, the sisters learn valuable lessons about friendship, perseverance, and the power of dreams.

Genres: Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Pursuit of Dreams
