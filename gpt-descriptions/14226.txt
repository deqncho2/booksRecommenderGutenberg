Synopsis: 
In 'Lobo, Rag and Vixen: Being The Personal Histories Of Lobo, Redruff, Raggylug & Vixen', Ernest Thompson Seton takes us on a captivating journey into the lives of four remarkable animals. Lobo, the cunning and fearless wolf; Rag, the mischievous and adventurous young rabbit; Raggylug, the brave and determined rabbit; and Vixen, the clever and resourceful fox. Through their individual stories, we witness the struggles, triumphs, and unexpected friendships that shape their lives in the wild. Set against the backdrop of the untamed wilderness, this enthralling tale explores the intricate dynamics of the animal kingdom and the enduring spirit of survival.

Genres: Adventure, Animal Fiction, Natural History

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Nature's Hierarchy
