Synopsis: In 'Lost in the Fog' by De Mille, James, a young woman named Emily finds herself trapped in a mysterious fog that transports her to a parallel world. As she navigates this strange and dangerous realm, she encounters a group of rebels fighting against an oppressive regime. With the help of a charismatic leader, Emily must uncover the truth about her own identity and the power she possesses. As she battles both external and internal conflicts, Emily's journey becomes a thrilling quest for freedom and self-discovery.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Rebellion, Power, Self-discovery
