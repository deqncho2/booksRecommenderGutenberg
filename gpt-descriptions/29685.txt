Synopsis: 

In "Submarine Warfare of To-day," Charles W. Domville-Fife takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of underwater warfare. Through vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, the author reveals how the menacing submarine threat was met and ultimately defeated. From the ingenious inventions and devices used to the deployment of fast boats, mystery ships, nets, and aircraft, Domville-Fife explores the relentless efforts to counter the submarine menace. Additionally, he delves into the selection and training of the vast personnel involved in this new branch of the navy. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Military History, Technology

Themes: Naval warfare, Innovation, Human resource management
