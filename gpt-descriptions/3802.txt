Synopsis: In 'The Lerouge Case' by Emile Gaboriau, a small village is shaken by a shocking murder that leaves everyone baffled. The victim, a wealthy widow, is found brutally slain in her own home, and suspicion falls on those closest to her. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, hidden motives come to light, and unexpected alliances are formed. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Justice
