Synopsis: In the third volume of "The Shaving of Shagpat; an Arabian entertainment," the enchanting tale continues as the protagonist, Shibli Bagarag, embarks on a perilous journey to rescue his beloved, the beautiful Noorna. Along the way, he encounters a series of fantastical creatures, treacherous sorcerers, and cunning adversaries, all while navigating the intricate web of destiny and the power of love. As the stakes rise and the challenges become increasingly daunting, Shibli must summon all his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to overcome the obstacles that stand in his path. Will he succeed in his quest and reunite with his true love, or will he be forever trapped in the clutches of fate?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Destiny, Love, Heroism
