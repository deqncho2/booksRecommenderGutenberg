Synopsis: In 'Love under Fire' by Parrish Randall, a passionate love story unfolds amidst the chaos of war. Set against the backdrop of World War II, this gripping tale follows the lives of two individuals who find solace, hope, and unexpected love in the midst of destruction. As they navigate the challenges of war, their bond grows stronger, but they soon realize that their love will be tested in ways they never imagined. With danger lurking at every turn, they must make difficult choices that could either tear them apart or bind them forever. Will their love survive the flames of war?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and resilience in times of adversity, the impact of war on personal relationships, the strength of the human spirit.
