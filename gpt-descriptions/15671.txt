Synopsis: In 'A Splendid Hazard' by Harold MacGrath, a young woman named Mary Ogden finds herself caught in a dangerous game of espionage and romance during the American Civil War. As she becomes entangled with both Union and Confederate spies, Mary must navigate a treacherous path filled with secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances. With her life hanging in the balance, she must decide whom to trust and how far she is willing to go to protect her country and the ones she loves.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Espionage, Loyalty, Sacrifice
