Synopsis: In Desert and Wilderness is an enthralling adventure novel that follows the thrilling journey of two young friends, Stas and Nel, as they find themselves stranded in the heart of Africa. Separated from their families and faced with numerous challenges, the duo must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and the help of unexpected allies to navigate the treacherous desert and wilderness. As they encounter dangerous animals, encounter different cultures, and face life-threatening situations, Stas and Nel's bond is tested, and they discover the true meaning of friendship and resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Cultural Differences, Resilience
