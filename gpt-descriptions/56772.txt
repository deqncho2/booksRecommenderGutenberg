Synopsis: In 'The Land of Riddles (Russia of To-day)' by Hugo Ganz, embark on a thrilling journey through the enigmatic and captivating world of Russia. As the protagonist delves into the heart of this mysterious land, they encounter a series of perplexing riddles that hold the key to unlocking the secrets of the country. From hidden treasures to ancient legends, the reader is taken on a suspenseful adventure that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Identity, Intrigue
