Synopsis: 
In "A Concise Chronicle of Events of the Great War," author R. P. P. Rowe takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous years of the First World War. Through a meticulous compilation of historical accounts, personal testimonies, and vivid descriptions, Rowe paints a vivid picture of the war's major battles, political intrigues, and the human stories that unfolded amidst the chaos. From the trenches of the Western Front to the diplomatic negotiations behind closed doors, this chronicle offers a comprehensive and captivating overview of one of the most significant conflicts in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War literature, Military history

Themes: War and its impact on society, Human resilience and sacrifice, Political and diplomatic maneuvering
