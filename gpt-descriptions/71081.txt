Synopsis: In "An Account of a Useful Discovery," Stephen Hales presents an intriguing exploration of distilling sea-water to obtain double the usual quantity. He also delves into the significant benefits of ventilators in preserving the health and lives of people, particularly in slave and transport ships. Additionally, Hales reveals a fascinating method of improving the taste of milk by blowing showers of air through it. This work captivates readers with its innovative ideas and practical applications.

Genres: Scientific literature, Experimental research, Historical documentation

Themes: Innovation, Health and well-being, Practical solutions
