Synopsis: In 'The Star of Gettysburg: A Story of Southern High Tide' by Joseph A. Altsheler, the reader is transported to the heart of the American Civil War. Amidst the chaos and bloodshed, a young Confederate soldier named Harry Kenton finds himself caught up in a series of thrilling adventures. From the battlefields of Virginia to the historic clash at Gettysburg, Harry's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of war, Harry must confront his own beliefs and grapple with the harsh realities of loyalty, honor, and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Honor, Sacrifice, Coming of Age
