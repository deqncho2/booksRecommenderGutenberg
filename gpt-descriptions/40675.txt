Synopsis: In 'Nurse Elisia' by George Manville Fenn, a young nurse named Elisia finds herself embroiled in a mysterious and dangerous situation when she takes on a new patient. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding her patient's illness, Elisia becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected romance. With her life on the line, she must navigate treacherous waters and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Love and Sacrifice
