Synopsis: In 'The Armourer's Prentices' by Charlotte M. Yonge, two young apprentices, Ned and Stephen, find themselves caught up in the tumultuous events of the Wars of the Roses. As they navigate the dangerous world of 15th-century England, they must learn to trust each other and face the challenges that come their way. With their lives intertwined with those of nobles, knights, and even royalty, Ned and Stephen's journey is filled with adventure, loyalty, and the pursuit of justice.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Justice, Social class, Historical events
