Synopsis: 
In 'Exit From Asteroid 60', a group of astronauts find themselves stranded on an asteroid after a catastrophic event. As they struggle to survive in the harsh conditions of space, they uncover a mysterious secret that could change the fate of humanity forever. With time running out, they must navigate treacherous obstacles and make difficult choices in order to find a way back home. This thrilling sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Sacrifice
