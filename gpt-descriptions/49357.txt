Synopsis: 

'Outlines of Mormon Philosophy: Or the Answers Given by the Gospel, as Revealed Through the Prophet Joseph Smith, to the Questions of Life' takes readers on a thought-provoking journey into the depths of Mormon philosophy. Lycurgus A. Wilson explores the profound answers provided by the Gospel, as revealed through the Prophet Joseph Smith, to the fundamental questions that shape our existence. From the nature of God and the purpose of life to the eternal destiny of the soul, this book delves into the profound teachings that have captivated millions.

Genres: Religious, Philosophy, Non-fiction

Themes: Mormonism, Faith, Life's Purpose, Divine Revelation
