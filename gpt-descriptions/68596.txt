Synopsis: In "Eustace Marchmont: A friend of the people" by Everett-Green, Evelyn, we follow the captivating journey of Eustace Marchmont, a man whose life takes an unexpected turn when he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and social unrest. As he navigates through the complexities of power, love, and loyalty, Eustace finds himself torn between his own ambitions and the greater good of the people he has come to care for. With secrets lurking in every corner and unexpected alliances forming, Eustace's choices will have far-reaching consequences that could change the course of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Romance

Themes: Power and Corruption, Social Justice, Loyalty and Betrayal
