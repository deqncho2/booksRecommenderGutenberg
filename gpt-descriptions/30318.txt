Synopsis: In 'Money Magic: A Novel' by Garland, Hamlin, we are introduced to a young man named Joe, who finds himself caught in the whirlwind of Wall Street's financial frenzy. As he navigates the treacherous world of money and power, Joe becomes entangled in a web of deceit, ambition, and unexpected alliances. With his future hanging in the balance, Joe must confront the dark side of wealth and discover the true meaning of success.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Ambition, Wealth, Morality, Power
