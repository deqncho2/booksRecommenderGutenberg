Synopsis: In 'The Peddler's Boy; Or, I'll Be Somebody' by Francis C. Woodworth, follow the captivating journey of a young boy named Frank, who starts his life as a humble peddler's assistant. As Frank encounters various challenges and opportunities, he embarks on a quest to rise above his circumstances and become somebody. With determination and resilience, Frank navigates the complexities of society, facing both triumphs and setbacks along the way. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in his path and achieve his dreams?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Social Mobility
