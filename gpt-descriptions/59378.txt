Synopsis: 

In "Carpet Beetles and Their Control," author E. A. Back delves into the fascinating world of these tiny yet destructive creatures that lurk within our homes. With a blend of scientific research and practical advice, Back takes readers on a journey to understand the behavior, habits, and life cycle of carpet beetles. From their insidious ability to damage fabrics and furnishings to the challenges they pose in eradication, this book offers a comprehensive guide to controlling and preventing infestations. Packed with intriguing anecdotes and expert insights, "Carpet Beetles and Their Control" is a must-read for anyone seeking to protect their homes from these elusive pests.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Home Improvement

Themes: Pest control, Home maintenance, Entomology
