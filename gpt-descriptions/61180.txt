Synopsis: In the final volume of 'The Yellow Flag: A Novel', the gripping tale reaches its climax as the protagonist, John, finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the yellow flag, a symbol of treachery and deceit, John's life is at stake, and he must navigate a labyrinth of betrayal and unexpected alliances. With each page, the tension rises, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the yellow flag.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Identity
