Synopsis: In 'Stranded in Arcady' by Lynde, Francis, a young man named Jack finds himself unexpectedly stranded in a remote and mysterious town called Arcady. As he tries to unravel the secrets of this peculiar place, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, romance, and danger. With each passing day, Jack discovers that Arcady holds more than meets the eye, and he must navigate its treacherous paths to uncover the truth and find a way back home.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Love and relationships, Identity and self-discovery
