Synopsis: In 'Nan of the Gypsies' by Grace May North, a young woman named Nan finds herself drawn into the captivating world of the gypsies. As she becomes immersed in their vibrant culture and traditions, Nan discovers a hidden family secret that will change her life forever. With danger lurking around every corner, Nan must navigate a treacherous path filled with love, betrayal, and self-discovery. Will she be able to uncover the truth and find her place among the gypsies, or will she be forever torn between two worlds?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Family, Cultural Exploration
