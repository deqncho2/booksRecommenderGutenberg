Synopsis: In "The Woodman: A Romance of the Times of Richard III," the reader is transported to the tumultuous era of Richard III's reign. Amidst the political intrigue and power struggles, a young woodman named Robin Hood emerges as a symbol of hope for the oppressed. As Robin navigates the treacherous landscape of medieval England, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and loyalty. With his band of merry men, Robin Hood fights for justice and challenges the corrupt forces that threaten the kingdom. This captivating historical romance will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they follow Robin's journey to become a legendary figure.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and Corruption, Justice and Rebellion, Love and Loyalty
