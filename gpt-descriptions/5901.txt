Synopsis: In 'Dyke Darrel the Railroad Detective; Or, The Crime of the Midnight Express' by A. Frank Pinkerton, follow the thrilling adventures of Dyke Darrel, a fearless detective with a knack for solving mysteries. When a heinous crime takes place aboard the infamous Midnight Express, Dyke Darrel must use his wit, courage, and keen observation skills to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice. As he delves deeper into the case, he encounters a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Justice, Deception, Perseverance
