Synopsis: In 'The Tree of Appomattox' by Joseph A. Altsheler, the reader is transported to the final days of the American Civil War. As the Confederate Army faces its ultimate defeat, a small group of soldiers find themselves caught in the midst of the chaos. Amidst the smoke and gunfire, friendships are tested, loyalties are questioned, and the true meaning of honor is revealed. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, Altsheler paints a poignant picture of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Friendship, Adversity, Human Spirit
