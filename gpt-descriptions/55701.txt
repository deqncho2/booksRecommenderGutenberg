Synopsis: In 'Under Many Flags', Singmaster and Cronk take readers on a captivating journey through history, following the lives of individuals from various countries who find themselves united under one flag. From the American Revolution to the World Wars, this collection of stories explores the triumphs, struggles, and sacrifices of these diverse characters as they navigate the complexities of war, love, and identity. With rich historical detail and compelling narratives, 'Under Many Flags' offers a poignant and thought-provoking exploration of the human experience in times of conflict.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Anthology

Themes: Identity, Patriotism, Love and loss, Unity in diversity
