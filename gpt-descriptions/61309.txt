Synopsis: 
In 'Road Stop' by David Mason, a young couple embarks on a road trip through the vast and desolate American Southwest. As they make a pit stop at a seemingly ordinary roadside diner, they soon find themselves entangled in a web of mystery and danger. With each passing hour, the couple's journey takes a dark and unexpected turn, forcing them to confront their deepest fears and question the very nature of reality. As they struggle to escape the clutches of an enigmatic force, they must rely on their wits and resilience to survive the nightmarish ordeal that awaits them.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Horror

Themes: Identity, Perception, Survival
