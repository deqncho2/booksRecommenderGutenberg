Synopsis: In 'Police!!!' by Robert W. Chambers, a young artist named Philip is drawn into a mysterious world of crime and danger when he witnesses a shocking event while sketching in Central Park. As he becomes entangled in a web of deceit and corruption, Philip must navigate the treacherous streets of New York City and uncover the truth before it's too late. With unexpected twists and a thrilling chase, this gripping novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Crime and corruption, Pursuit of truth, Danger in unexpected places
