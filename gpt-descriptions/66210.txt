Synopsis: 
In 'Repeat Performance' by Phillips, Rog, a successful actress named Sheila is given a chance to relive the past year of her life after a tragic event on New Year's Eve. With the knowledge of what is to come, she embarks on a journey to change her fate and prevent the mistakes that led to her downfall. As she navigates through the glitz and glamour of the entertainment industry, Sheila must confront her own flaws and make difficult choices that will determine her future. With each repeated performance, the stakes get higher, and Sheila must find a way to break the cycle before it consumes her completely.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Time Travel

Themes: Second chances, Fate vs. Free will, Redemption
