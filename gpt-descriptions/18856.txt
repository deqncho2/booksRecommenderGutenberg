Synopsis: In the small town of Sage, a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a dark secret that threatens to unravel the peaceful existence of its inhabitants. As the stranger's presence becomes increasingly unsettling, the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of suspicion, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With tensions rising and the truth lurking just beneath the surface, the settling of Sage will forever change the lives of its residents.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Western

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Moral ambiguity
