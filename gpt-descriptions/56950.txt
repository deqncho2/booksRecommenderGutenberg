Synopsis: In 'Uncle Wiggily's Squirt Gun; Or, Jack Frost Icicle Maker: And, Uncle Wiggily's Queer Umbrellas, also, Uncle Wiggily's Lemonade Stand', follow the adventures of Uncle Wiggily, a lovable rabbit, as he embarks on a series of whimsical escapades. From battling Jack Frost with his trusty squirt gun to encountering peculiar umbrellas and setting up a lemonade stand, Uncle Wiggily's misadventures are sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Problem-solving
