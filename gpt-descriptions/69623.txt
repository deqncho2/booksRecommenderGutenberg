Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Feudal tyrants; or, The Counts of Carlsheim and Sargans', the gripping saga continues as the power struggle between two noble families intensifies. As the Counts of Carlsheim and Sargans vie for control over their territories, dark secrets, treachery, and forbidden love threaten to tear them apart. Amidst the backdrop of a medieval world filled with political intrigue and simmering tensions, the characters must navigate a web of deceit and betrayal to protect their legacies and find redemption. Will the Counts be able to maintain their grip on power, or will their own actions lead to their downfall?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Power struggles, Family legacies, Betrayal, Forbidden love, Redemption
