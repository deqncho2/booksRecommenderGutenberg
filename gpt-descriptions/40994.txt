Synopsis: In 'Zoraida: A Romance of the Harem and the Great Sahara', Le Queux takes readers on a captivating journey through the exotic world of the harem and the vast Sahara desert. The story follows the adventures of a young Englishman who finds himself entangled in a web of love, danger, and intrigue when he encounters the beautiful and enigmatic Zoraida. As he delves deeper into her mysterious past, he becomes embroiled in a perilous quest that will test his courage and loyalty. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, this thrilling romance will transport readers to a world filled with passion, secrets, and the allure of the unknown.

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Desire, Cultural Exploration, Intrigue and Mystery
