Synopsis: 
In "The Country Doctor," a dedicated physician finds himself facing a series of challenging situations that test his skills and compassion. As he navigates through the complexities of rural life, the doctor must confront the harsh realities of human suffering and the limitations of his profession. Balzac's vivid portrayal of the doctor's struggles and the moral dilemmas he encounters will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: 
Realism, Fiction, Short Story

Themes: 
Duty and Sacrifice, Human Frailty, Social Inequality
