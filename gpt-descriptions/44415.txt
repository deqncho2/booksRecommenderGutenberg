Synopsis: In 'Robber and Hero: The Story of the Raid on the First National Bank of Northfield, Minnesota, by the James-Younger Band of Robbers, in 1876,' author George Huntington takes readers on a thrilling journey back in time to one of the most audacious bank robberies in American history. Set in the Wild West, this gripping tale follows the notorious James-Younger gang as they meticulously plan and execute their daring heist, only to face unexpected challenges and a heroic resistance. As the story unfolds, readers will be captivated by the high-stakes action, the complex characters, and the moral dilemmas that arise in the face of crime and justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Western, Crime

Themes: Crime and Justice, Morality, Heroism
