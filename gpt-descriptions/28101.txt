Synopsis: In 'The Van Dwellers: A Strenuous Quest for a Home', Albert Bigelow Paine takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of a group of unconventional individuals who have chosen to live on the road. As they navigate the challenges and joys of van dwelling, they encounter unexpected friendships, face societal prejudices, and embark on a quest to find a place they can truly call home. With humor, insight, and a touch of adventure, Paine invites readers to explore the fascinating world of the van dwellers and discover the true meaning of belonging.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Social Commentary

Themes: Homelessness, Community, Identity, Freedom
