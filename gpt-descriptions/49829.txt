Synopsis: 

"The Geological History of Fossil Butte National Monument and Fossil Basin" takes readers on an awe-inspiring journey through time, unraveling the mysteries of an ancient landscape. Casilliano and McGrew expertly guide us through the geological wonders of Fossil Butte National Monument and Fossil Basin, revealing the fascinating story of how these remarkable fossil-rich areas came to be. From the formation of the ancient lake that once covered the region to the preservation of countless prehistoric creatures, this book offers a captivating exploration of the deep past.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Natural History

Themes: Geological evolution, Paleontology, Environmental preservation
