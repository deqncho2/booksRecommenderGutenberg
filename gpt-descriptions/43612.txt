Synopsis: In 'Katydid's Poems' by Kate Slaughter McKinney, a young girl named Katydid discovers a hidden talent for poetry that allows her to express her deepest thoughts and emotions. As she navigates the challenges of growing up in a small town, Katydid's poems become a source of solace, empowerment, and connection with others. Through her heartfelt verses, she explores themes of self-discovery, friendship, love, and the power of words to heal and inspire. 

Genres: Poetry, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Self-expression, Identity, Friendship, Love, Empowerment
