Synopsis: 

In 'The Cambridge, Ely, and King's Lynn Road: The Great Fenland Highway', Charles G. Harper takes readers on a captivating journey through the historic road that connects three iconic English towns. From the bustling streets of Cambridge to the serene landscapes of Ely and the charming coastal town of King's Lynn, Harper delves into the rich history, hidden stories, and fascinating landmarks that line this ancient highway. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, the author paints a vivid picture of the road's significance, revealing the secrets and legends that have shaped its character over the centuries. 

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: English heritage, Road networks, Local legends and folklore
