Synopsis: In the first volume of 'Trevethlan: A Cornish Story', we are transported to the rugged and enchanting landscapes of Cornwall, where the lives of the wealthy Trevethlan family and their humble neighbors intertwine in unexpected ways. As secrets, scandals, and forbidden love unfold, the reader is drawn into a captivating tale of ambition, betrayal, and redemption. Amidst the backdrop of breathtaking scenery and the clash of social classes, the characters navigate their desires and ambitions, ultimately leading to a series of dramatic events that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social class, Family dynamics, Love and passion, Secrets and betrayal
