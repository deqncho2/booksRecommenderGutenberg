Synopsis: 
In 'Roaming Through the West Indies', Harry Alverson Franck takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the vibrant and diverse landscapes of the Caribbean islands. From the bustling streets of Havana to the tranquil beaches of Jamaica, Franck's vivid descriptions and captivating encounters with locals paint a vivid picture of the region's rich history, cultural traditions, and natural wonders. As he immerses himself in the local customs and explores hidden gems off the beaten path, Franck's narrative unfolds as a captivating travelogue that will leave readers yearning to embark on their own adventure through the West Indies.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Natural beauty
