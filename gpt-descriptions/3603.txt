Synopsis: 

'Widger's Quotations from the Project Gutenberg Editions of the Works of Guy de Maupassant' is a captivating collection of quotes from the renowned French author's literary masterpieces. This compilation offers a glimpse into the profound and thought-provoking world created by Maupassant, showcasing his exceptional storytelling and keen observations of human nature. From tales of love and passion to stories of tragedy and despair, these carefully selected quotes provide a tantalizing taste of Maupassant's genius, leaving readers eager to explore his works in their entirety.

Genres: Literary Fiction, Short Stories, Classic Literature

Themes: Human nature, Love and passion, Tragedy and despair
