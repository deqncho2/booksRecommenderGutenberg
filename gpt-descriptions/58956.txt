Synopsis: In "The Woman and the Car: A Chatty Little Handbook for All Women Who Motor or Who Want to Motor," Dorothy Levitt takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the world of early 20th-century motoring. With wit and charm, Levitt provides practical advice, humorous anecdotes, and insightful observations, making this handbook an essential companion for any woman venturing into the world of automobiles. From choosing the right car to mastering driving techniques, navigating road etiquette, and even fashion tips for motoring, Levitt's guide is a delightful blend of practicality and entertainment that will captivate readers from start to finish.

Genres: Non-fiction, Automotive, Women's Literature

Themes: Women's empowerment, Independence, Adventure, Fashion, Social change
