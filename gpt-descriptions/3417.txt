Synopsis: In "The Fortunes of Oliver Horn" by Francis Hopkinson Smith, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the life of Oliver Horn, a young man with a mysterious past and an uncertain future. From his humble beginnings as an orphan in a small New England town, Oliver's life takes unexpected turns as he navigates the treacherous waters of love, ambition, and self-discovery. As he encounters a colorful cast of characters and faces numerous challenges, Oliver's resilience and determination are put to the test. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way and find his true place in the world?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Love and Relationships, Self-Discovery
