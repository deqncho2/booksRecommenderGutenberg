Synopsis: In 'Out of Death's Shadow; Or, A Case Without a Precedent' by Carter, Nicholas, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing murder mystery when a seemingly impossible crime takes place in a secluded mansion. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances, leading him to question everything he thought he knew. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate the treacherous shadows of the past to uncover the truth and bring the elusive killer to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Redemption, Uncovering the truth
