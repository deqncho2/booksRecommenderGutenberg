Synopsis: 

'List of Post Offices in Canada, with the Names of the Postmasters ... 1866' is a comprehensive record compiled by the Canada Post Office Department, providing a fascinating glimpse into the postal system of Canada during the year 1866. This meticulously detailed list includes the names of post offices across the country, along with the names of the postmasters responsible for each location. 

Genres: Historical, Reference

Themes: Canadian history, Postal system, Administration
