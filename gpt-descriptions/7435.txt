Synopsis:
Set during the tumultuous times of the Great War, 'Springhaven: A Tale of the Great War' takes readers on a captivating journey to the coastal village of Springhaven. Amidst the backdrop of war, the lives of two families become intertwined, as secrets, love, and sacrifice unfold. As the war rages on, the characters must navigate through personal trials and tribulations, testing their resilience and challenging their beliefs. With vivid descriptions and compelling storytelling, 'Springhaven' offers a poignant exploration of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Romance

Themes:
War and its impact on individuals and communities, Love and sacrifice, Resilience and personal growth
