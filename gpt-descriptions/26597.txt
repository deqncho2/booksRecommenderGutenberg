Synopsis: In "How to Behave: A Pocket Manual of Republican Etiquette, and Guide to Correct Personal Habits," Samuel R. Wells presents a comprehensive guide to good manners and proper behavior. Covering a wide range of topics including personal care, social interactions, and public conduct, this manual offers practical advice and useful hints on how to navigate various situations with grace and decorum. With illustrative anecdotes and rules of order for debating societies, Wells provides readers with a valuable resource for cultivating self-culture and refining their behavior in all aspects of life.

Genres: Self-help, Etiquette, Reference

Themes: Good manners, Personal development, Social etiquette
