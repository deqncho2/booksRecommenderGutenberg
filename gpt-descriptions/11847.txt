Synopsis: 

Step into the world of U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1973 January - June, a captivating compilation by the Library of Congress. This enthralling collection takes readers on a journey through the first half of 1973, exploring the diverse range of creative works that were renewed for copyright protection during this period. From literature to music, art to film, this book unveils a treasure trove of intellectual property that shaped the cultural landscape of the time. Delve into the pages of U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1973 January - June and discover the hidden gems that have left an indelible mark on American creativity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Intellectual property, Cultural heritage
