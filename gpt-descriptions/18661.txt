Synopsis: 
Step back in time to the year 1911 and immerse yourself in the enchanting world of 'The Empire Annual for Girls'. This captivating collection of stories, articles, and illustrations is a delightful window into the lives of young girls during the early 20th century. From thrilling adventures to heartwarming tales of friendship, this annual offers a glimpse into a bygone era filled with innocence, courage, and the boundless possibilities of girlhood. Discover the secrets of the past and be transported to a time of elegance and wonder in 'The Empire Annual for Girls, 1911'.

Genres: Historical fiction, Anthology, Children's literature

Themes: Coming of age, Friendship, Adventure, Gender roles, Nostalgia
