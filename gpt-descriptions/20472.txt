Synopsis: In "Grace Harlowe's Plebe Year at High School: The Merry Doings of the Oakdale Freshmen Girls," readers are introduced to Grace Harlowe and her group of friends as they navigate their first year at Oakdale High School. From forming new friendships to facing unexpected challenges, the girls find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of adventures and misadventures. As they strive to find their place in the high school hierarchy, Grace and her friends learn valuable lessons about loyalty, perseverance, and the power of friendship.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Coming-of-Age, School Life

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Resilience, Growth
