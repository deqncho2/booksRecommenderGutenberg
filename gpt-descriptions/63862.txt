Synopsis: In 'Stalemate in Space' by Charles L. Harness, a brilliant scientist discovers a mysterious anomaly in space that threatens the delicate balance of power between nations. As tensions rise and the world teeters on the brink of war, a team of unlikely heroes must embark on a perilous mission to unravel the secrets of this cosmic enigma and prevent a catastrophic conflict that could change the course of history forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Power struggles, Diplomacy, Exploration, Technological advancements
