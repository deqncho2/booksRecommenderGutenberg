Synopsis: In 'The Methodist: A Poem' by Lloyd Evan, a mysterious figure emerges from the shadows, captivating the attention of a small town. As the townspeople become enthralled by his charismatic presence, they are drawn into a web of secrets and hidden desires. With each passing day, the Methodist's influence grows stronger, leaving the community questioning their own beliefs and values. Told through vivid imagery and lyrical prose, this captivating poem explores the power of persuasion and the consequences of blind faith.

Genres: Poetry, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Manipulation, Identity, Morality
