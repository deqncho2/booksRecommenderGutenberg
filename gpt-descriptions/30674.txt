Synopsis: 

'Famous Firesides of French Canada' by Mary Wilson Alloway takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and folklore of French Canada. From the chilling tales of haunted houses to the heartwarming stories of love and resilience, this collection of fireside stories will transport readers to a world filled with mystery, adventure, and the enduring spirit of the Canadian people. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Alloway weaves together a tapestry of captivating narratives that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to uncover the secrets and legends of French Canada.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Folklore, Mystery

Themes: Canadian history, cultural heritage, supernatural folklore
