Synopsis: In "The Admiral's Walk," a retired admiral finds himself embarking on a mysterious journey that takes him across different dimensions and time periods. As he navigates through these extraordinary realms, he encounters enigmatic characters and uncovers hidden truths about his own past. With each step, the admiral becomes entangled in a web of secrets and must confront the ultimate question of his existence.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Time, Reality, Redemption
