Synopsis: 
'Awd Isaac, The Steeple Chase, and Other Poems: With a glossary of the Yorkshire Dialect' by Castillo, John is a collection of captivating and humorous poems that transport readers to the charming world of Yorkshire. From the misadventures of Awd Isaac to the thrilling steeple chase, these poems offer a delightful glimpse into the lives and dialect of the Yorkshire people. With vivid imagery and a touch of wit, Castillo's poems celebrate the beauty of the Yorkshire countryside and the resilience of its inhabitants.

Genres: Poetry, Regional Literature, Humor

Themes: Yorkshire culture, Rural life, Resilience
