Synopsis: In the small town of Leatherwood, a charismatic preacher named Elder Fairley arrives, captivating the community with his mesmerizing sermons and mysterious healing powers. As the townspeople become increasingly devoted to the enigmatic preacher, a young journalist named John Carruthers is sent to investigate the truth behind Elder Fairley's miracles. However, as John delves deeper into the secrets of Leatherwood, he uncovers a web of deception, manipulation, and dark desires that threaten to consume the entire town.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Religious fanaticism, Power and manipulation, Truth and deception
