Synopsis: In 'Raising P. V. Squabs for Profit' by John S. Trecartin, a young entrepreneur named Alex embarks on a unique and lucrative business venture. With the help of his eccentric mentor, he dives into the world of raising P. V. squabs, a mysterious and highly sought-after species. As Alex navigates the challenges of breeding and selling these enigmatic creatures, he discovers that there is more to his venture than meets the eye. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story explores the lengths one will go to achieve success and the consequences that come with it.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Discovery, Consequences
