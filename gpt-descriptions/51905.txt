Synopsis: In "The Invasion of 1910, with a full account of the siege of London," Le Queux takes readers on a thrilling journey through a meticulously planned invasion of England by a foreign power. As the city of London becomes the epicenter of a devastating siege, the lives of ordinary citizens and key political figures intertwine in a desperate struggle for survival. With a blend of suspense, espionage, and political intrigue, this gripping novel keeps readers on the edge of their seats, wondering if London will succumb to the invaders or if a heroic resistance will emerge.

Genres: Thriller, Historical Fiction, Espionage

Themes: Invasion, War, Patriotism, Survival
