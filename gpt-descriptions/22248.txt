Synopsis: 
In 'The Indian Fairy Book: From the Original Legends', Mathews, Cornelius takes readers on a captivating journey into the enchanting world of Native American folklore. This collection of Native American legends and fairy tales unveils a rich tapestry of magical creatures, heroic deeds, and timeless wisdom. From the mischievous trickster figures to the noble spirits of nature, each story offers a glimpse into the vibrant mythology and cultural heritage of the indigenous peoples. With its vivid storytelling and captivating characters, this book invites readers to immerse themselves in the captivating realm of Native American folklore.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Cultural heritage, Nature spirits, Trickster figures, Heroic deeds
