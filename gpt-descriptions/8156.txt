Synopsis: In 'Twilight and Dawn; Or, Simple Talks on the Six Days of Creation', Caroline Pridham takes readers on a captivating journey through the six days of creation, exploring the wonders of the natural world and the divine design behind it. Through engaging conversations and thought-provoking discussions, Pridham delves into the intricate details of each day, unraveling the mysteries of creation and shedding light on the profound beauty and purpose behind it all. With a blend of scientific knowledge and spiritual insights, this book offers a unique perspective on the origins of life and the interconnectedness of all living things.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Spirituality

Themes: Creation, Nature, Spirituality, Science, Divine Design
