Synopsis: 

'Lessons on Manners for School and Home Use' by Edith E. Wiggin is a delightful and insightful guide that takes readers on a journey through the importance of manners in both school and home settings. Through a series of engaging stories and practical lessons, Wiggin explores the impact of good manners on personal relationships, social interactions, and overall character development. From the classroom to the dining table, this book offers valuable lessons on etiquette, kindness, and respect, providing readers with the tools to navigate various social situations with grace and confidence. 

Genres: Educational, Self-help, Etiquette

Themes: Manners, Etiquette, Character Development, Social Skills
