Synopsis: In 'A Matter of Honor: A Terran Empire novel' by Wilson, Ann, a young cadet named Alex finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and personal challenges as she navigates the treacherous world of the Terran Empire's military academy. With her loyalty and determination tested at every turn, Alex must make difficult choices that will not only shape her own destiny but also the fate of the empire itself. As secrets unravel and alliances shift, she discovers that honor can be a double-edged sword, and the true measure of a person lies in their ability to rise above adversity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Military Fiction, Political Thriller

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Personal Growth, Power Dynamics
