Synopsis: In 'The Bacillus of Beauty: A Romance of To-day', Harriet Stark takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of beauty and its profound impact on society. The story revolves around a mysterious bacillus that possesses the power to transform ordinary individuals into extraordinary beauties. As this bacillus spreads, it sets off a chain of events that challenges societal norms, tests the boundaries of love, and explores the true nature of beauty. With unexpected twists and turns, the novel delves into the complex relationships between appearance and identity, the pursuit of perfection, and the consequences of our obsession with beauty.

Genres: Romance, Science Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: Beauty and identity, societal expectations, the power of appearance, love and relationships, the consequences of obsession
