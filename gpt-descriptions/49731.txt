Synopsis: In 'Tamawaca Folks: A Summer Comedy', the charming and eccentric residents of Tamawaca, a small lakeside town, find themselves entangled in a series of hilarious and unexpected events during one unforgettable summer. From mistaken identities to romantic entanglements, this delightful comedy takes readers on a whimsical journey filled with laughter, love, and the joy of community.

Genres: Comedy, Fiction, Humor

Themes: Small-town life, Community, Love and relationships
