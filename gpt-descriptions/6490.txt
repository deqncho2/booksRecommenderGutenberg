Synopsis: In "The Betrothed" by Walter Scott, the reader is transported to 16th-century Scotland, where the young and beautiful Lady Eveline finds herself torn between duty and love. When her father forces her into an arranged marriage with an older nobleman, Eveline's heart yearns for the dashing and mysterious Highland chieftain, Lord Angus. As political tensions rise and danger lurks in every corner, Eveline must navigate treacherous waters to follow her heart's desire. Will she defy societal expectations and risk everything for true love?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Duty, Social Expectations, Political Intrigue
