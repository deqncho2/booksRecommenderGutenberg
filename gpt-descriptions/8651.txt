Synopsis: In 'With Moore at Corunna', follow the thrilling adventures of a young British officer, Terence O'Connor, as he joins the British army during the Peninsular War. From the treacherous battles and daring escapades to the unexpected friendships and heart-wrenching sacrifices, Terence's journey takes him through the rugged landscapes of Spain and Portugal. As he faces the perils of war and the challenges of leadership, Terence must find the strength to persevere and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Coming of Age
