Synopsis: In 'The Sleeping Beauty Picture Book', a young princess is cursed to sleep for a hundred years, and only true love's kiss can awaken her. As the years pass, the kingdom becomes overgrown and forgotten, until a brave prince embarks on a perilous journey to find the sleeping princess and break the curse. Filled with enchantment, adventure, and unexpected twists, this timeless tale will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fairy tale, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: True love, Courage, Destiny
