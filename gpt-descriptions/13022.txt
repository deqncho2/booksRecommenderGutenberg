Synopsis: 
In 'My Book of Indoor Games' by Squareman, Clarence, get ready to embark on a thrilling journey through a collection of mind-bending puzzles, challenging riddles, and exciting games that will keep you on the edge of your seat. From solving intricate mazes to deciphering cryptic codes, this book is a treasure trove of indoor entertainment that will test your wits and push your problem-solving skills to the limit. With each turn of the page, you'll uncover new and unexpected challenges that will captivate your imagination and leave you craving for more. Get ready to dive into a world of endless fun and excitement!

Genres: Puzzle, Riddle, Game

Themes: Problem-solving, Entertainment
