Synopsis: 

In "Fifteen Years Among the Top-Knots; Or, Life in Korea," Lillias H. Underwood takes readers on a captivating journey through her experiences living in Korea for fifteen years. From the bustling streets of Seoul to the serene countryside, Underwood provides a vivid and intimate portrayal of Korean culture, customs, and traditions. Through her encounters with the local people, she unveils the complexities of Korean society, offering a unique perspective on the country's history, religion, and social dynamics. As she navigates the challenges and joys of daily life, Underwood's narrative is filled with heartwarming anecdotes, unexpected adventures, and profound insights into the human spirit. 

Genres: Memoir, Travelogue, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Cross-cultural experiences, Identity, Resilience, Exploration of Korean society and customs
