Synopsis: In the small, picturesque town of East Lupton, Vermont, a mysterious and malevolent force begins to haunt the residents, spreading fear and chaos. As the townspeople grapple with the unexplainable occurrences, a group of unlikely allies must uncover the truth behind the evil that has taken hold of their beloved community. With secrets, suspense, and a touch of the supernatural, 'The Devil of East Lupton, Vermont' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Small-town Secrets, Unlikely Heroes
