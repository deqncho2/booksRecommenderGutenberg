Synopsis: 
In 'A Compilation of the Messages and Papers of the Presidents. Volume 7, part 2: Rutherford B. Hayes', readers are transported back to the late 19th century to explore the presidency of Rutherford B. Hayes. This captivating compilation delves into the challenges and triumphs faced by Hayes during his time in office, shedding light on the political landscape and the pressing issues of the era. From the aftermath of the Civil War to the fight for civil rights, this volume offers a fascinating glimpse into the life and presidency of one of America's lesser-known leaders.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, political biography, government documents

Themes: 
Presidential leadership, post-Civil War reconstruction, civil rights, political challenges
