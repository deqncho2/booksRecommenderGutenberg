Synopsis: In 'The Manoeuvring Mother (vol. 3 of 3)' by Charlotte Campbell, Lady Bury, the captivating conclusion to the series unfolds as Lady Emily navigates the treacherous waters of high society, facing unexpected challenges and making daring choices. As her secrets threaten to unravel, she must rely on her wit and cunning to protect her family's reputation and secure their future. With scandal, romance, and intrigue at every turn, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social class, Family dynamics, Deception, Ambition
