Synopsis: In the enchanting world of King Arthur's court, a mysterious and imposing Green Knight challenges the noble Sir Gawayne to a perilous game. As Gawayne embarks on a treacherous journey to fulfill his end of the bargain, he encounters a series of tests that will push his courage and honor to their limits. With each step, the line between reality and fantasy blurs, and Gawayne must confront his deepest fears and desires. Will he emerge victorious or succumb to the seductive allure of the Green Knight's game?

Genres: Medieval Romance, Arthurian Literature, Epic Poetry

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Temptation, The Supernatural
