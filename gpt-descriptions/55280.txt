Synopsis: In "An Enquiry into the Life and Legend of Michael Scot," James Wood delves into the mysterious life of a forgotten medieval figure, Michael Scot. Known for his extraordinary intellect and rumored magical abilities, Scot's story takes readers on a captivating journey through the realms of science, philosophy, and the occult. As Wood unravels the enigma surrounding Scot, he uncovers a web of intrigue, power struggles, and hidden knowledge that spans centuries. This gripping exploration of a forgotten genius will leave readers spellbound, questioning the boundaries of human potential and the true nature of reality.

Genres: Biography, History, Mystery

Themes: Intellectual curiosity, Power and knowledge, Mysticism and the occult
