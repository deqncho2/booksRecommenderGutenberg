Synopsis: In 'The White Wampum' by Johnson, E. Pauline, a mysterious artifact with a dark history resurfaces, setting off a chain of events that will challenge the protagonist's beliefs and force them to confront their own past. As they delve deeper into the secrets surrounding the white wampum, they find themselves entangled in a web of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With danger lurking at every turn, the protagonist must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the truth and protect what they hold dear.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Power
