Synopsis: In 'The Princess Idleways: A Fairy Story' by Hays, Helen Ashe, we are introduced to Princess Isabella, a young royal who has a penchant for daydreaming and avoiding her royal duties. However, when a mysterious curse befalls the kingdom, Isabella must embark on a magical adventure to save her people and discover the true power within herself. With the help of a mischievous fairy and a brave young knight, Isabella must navigate enchanted forests, face dangerous creatures, and confront her own fears. Will she find the courage to fulfill her destiny and become the princess her kingdom needs?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Fairy Tale

Themes: Self-discovery, Courage, Duty, Magic
