Synopsis: In a distant future where Earth is a forgotten memory, a group of interstellar adventurers known as the Starmen embark on a perilous mission to a mysterious planet. Led by the enigmatic Eric John Stark, they must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter strange alien civilizations, and confront their own inner demons. As they delve deeper into the secrets of this uncharted world, they discover that their journey holds the key to unlocking the ultimate power in the universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Space Opera

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Redemption
