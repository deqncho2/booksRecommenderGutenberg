Synopsis: 
'365 Bedtime Stories' by Mary Graham Bonner is a delightful collection of short stories that will transport readers into a world of imagination and wonder. Each story is carefully crafted to captivate young minds and make bedtime a magical experience. From tales of talking animals to adventures in faraway lands, this book offers a different story for every night of the year. With its charming characters and enchanting narratives, '365 Bedtime Stories' is sure to become a cherished favorite for children and parents alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Morality
