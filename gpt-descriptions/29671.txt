Synopsis: 
In 'Nobody' by Vance, Louis Joseph, a seemingly ordinary man named John wakes up one day to find that he has become invisible to everyone around him. As he navigates this newfound isolation, John discovers a hidden world of secrets and conspiracies that threaten not only his own existence but also the lives of those he holds dear. With time running out, John must unravel the mystery behind his invisibility and find a way to reclaim his place in the visible world.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Identity, Isolation, Conspiracy
