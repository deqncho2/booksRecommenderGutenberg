Synopsis: 
In 'To Each His Own' by Jack Sharkey, a seemingly ordinary man named John finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change the course of history. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled with a group of eccentric characters, each with their own motives and secrets. With his life on the line, John must navigate a treacherous path of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances to uncover the truth and protect what he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Betrayal, Identity and self-discovery
