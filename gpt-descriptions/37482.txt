Synopsis: In 'The Postmaster' by Joseph Crosby Lincoln, a young postmaster is transferred to a remote village in rural India, where he encounters a young girl named Ratan. As the postmaster and Ratan develop an unlikely friendship, they navigate the complexities of life, love, and longing in a world filled with cultural differences and societal expectations. Through their interactions, the postmaster begins to question his own beliefs and finds solace in the simplicity and innocence of Ratan's perspective. 

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Cultural

Themes: Friendship, Cultural clash, Self-discovery
