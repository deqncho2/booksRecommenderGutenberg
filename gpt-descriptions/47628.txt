Synopsis: In 'A Corner of Spain' by Walter Wood, a young artist named Emma embarks on a journey to a remote village in Spain, seeking inspiration for her paintings. As she immerses herself in the vibrant culture and picturesque landscapes, Emma discovers a hidden secret that intertwines the lives of the villagers and challenges her perception of art and identity. With each stroke of her brush, Emma uncovers the power of self-expression and the transformative nature of embracing one's true self.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Artistic inspiration, Cultural exploration, Self-discovery
