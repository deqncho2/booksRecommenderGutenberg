Synopsis: In the small New England town of Oldfields, Madelon is a young woman with a mysterious past and a captivating presence. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Madelon's unconventional choices and enigmatic nature draw the attention and curiosity of the townspeople. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, this novel explores the intricacies of human relationships, the power of secrets, and the pursuit of personal freedom.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Love and Desire, Social Expectations, Secrets and Mystery
