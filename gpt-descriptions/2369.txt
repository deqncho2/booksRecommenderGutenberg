Synopsis: 
In 'One of Ours' by Willa Cather, we follow the life of Claude Wheeler, a young man living in a small Nebraska town. Feeling trapped by his mundane existence, Claude yearns for something more meaningful. As he navigates through love, war, and self-discovery, he embarks on a journey that will challenge his beliefs and reshape his understanding of the world. 

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, War

Themes: 
Identity, Purpose, Sacrifice, Ambition
