Synopsis: In 'A Commentary on Herodotus', Wells and How delve into the fascinating world of ancient history, offering a fresh perspective on the renowned historian's work. Through meticulous analysis and insightful commentary, they unravel the mysteries and controversies surrounding Herodotus' accounts, shedding light on the truth behind his narratives. As they navigate through the intricate web of historical events, the authors provide thought-provoking interpretations and challenge conventional beliefs, making this commentary an engaging and enlightening read for history enthusiasts and scholars alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Commentary

Themes: Historical interpretation, Ancient civilizations, Narrative analysis
