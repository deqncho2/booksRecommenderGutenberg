Synopsis: In 'Told in the East', Talbot Mundy takes readers on a thrilling journey through the mystical and enigmatic lands of the East. The story follows the adventures of a young British officer who finds himself caught in a web of intrigue, danger, and ancient secrets. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters fascinating characters, he must unravel the mysteries that lie hidden in the heart of the East. With its captivating blend of adventure, mystery, and exotic settings, 'Told in the East' promises to transport readers to a world filled with suspense and wonder.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration of Eastern cultures, Quest for knowledge and truth, Clash of civilizations
