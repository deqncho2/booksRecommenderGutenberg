Synopsis: 

'Captain Cuellar's Adventures in Connaught & Ulster A.D. 1588.' is a captivating historical account that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Spanish Armada and its aftermath. The book features an introduction and a complete translation of Captain Cuellar's own narrative, recounting his incredible adventures in Ireland.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Cultural clash, Exploration
