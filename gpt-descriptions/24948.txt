Synopsis: 

'Finnish Legends for English Children' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore of Finland. From mythical creatures lurking in the depths of lakes to brave heroes embarking on extraordinary quests, this collection of enchanting tales brings to life the magic and wonder of Finnish mythology. Each story is filled with adventure, mystery, and a touch of whimsy, transporting readers to a world where the ordinary and the extraordinary intertwine. Whether it's a mischievous forest spirit or a courageous young girl facing a daunting challenge, these legends are sure to captivate readers of all ages and ignite their imagination.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Mythology, Heroism, Magic, Nature
