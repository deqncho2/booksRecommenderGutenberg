Synopsis: In 'History of the Washington National Monument and of the Washington National Monument Society', Frederick L. Harvey delves into the captivating story behind the iconic Washington Monument. From its conception to its completion, Harvey uncovers the challenges, controversies, and triumphs that shaped this monumental structure. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, he reveals the untold tales of the individuals who dedicated their lives to the creation of this enduring symbol of American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Architecture

Themes: American patriotism, Historical preservation, Symbolism
