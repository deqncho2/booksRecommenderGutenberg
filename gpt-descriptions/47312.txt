Synopsis: In the dark underbelly of 19th-century London, a web of intrigue, crime, and secrets unfolds. Follow the lives of various characters as they navigate the treacherous streets, encountering mysterious figures, dangerous plots, and unexpected alliances. From the opulent mansions of the elite to the squalid slums, this gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the hidden corners of the city, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Crime and punishment, Social inequality, Deception and disguise
