Synopsis: In 'Airplane Boys at Platinum River' by E. J. Craine, two adventurous brothers, Dave and Freddy, find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey when they stumble upon a mysterious clue that leads them to an abandoned airfield. As they uncover the secrets hidden within the airfield, they become entangled in a dangerous mission that takes them across the globe, encountering unexpected allies and formidable adversaries. With their courage and resourcefulness, the brothers must navigate treacherous situations and solve perplexing puzzles to unravel the truth behind the airfield's enigma. Will they succeed in their quest and uncover the hidden treasures that lie at Platinum River?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Brotherhood, Courage, Discovery
