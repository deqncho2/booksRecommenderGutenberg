Synopsis: In 'Forest Days: A Romance of Old Times' by James, G. P. R., a young noblewoman finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love during a tumultuous period in history. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of power and passion, she must make choices that will shape her destiny and the fate of those she holds dear. Set against the backdrop of a lush forest, this captivating tale will transport readers to a world of secrets, danger, and the enduring strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Political Intrigue, Coming of Age
