Synopsis: In "The Shadow of the Rope" by E. W. Hornung, a young lawyer named Harold Quaritch finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he becomes involved in a high-profile murder case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers shocking secrets and faces dangerous adversaries who will stop at nothing to protect their own interests. With his reputation and life on the line, Quaritch must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and bring justice to those who deserve it.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Justice and morality, Deception and betrayal
