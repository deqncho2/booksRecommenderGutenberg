Synopsis: 

'English Costume' by Dion Clayton Calthrop takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of fashion in England. From the medieval period to the early 20th century, Calthrop explores the evolution of clothing styles, revealing the fascinating stories behind each era's attire. Through vivid descriptions and beautiful illustrations, the author brings to life the intricate details of garments, accessories, and the social context in which they were worn. Whether it's the opulence of Tudor fashion or the simplicity of Victorian dress, 'English Costume' offers a delightful exploration of the ever-changing world of English fashion.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Fashion, Art

Themes: Fashion history, Social history, Cultural identity
