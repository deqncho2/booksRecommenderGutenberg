Synopsis: In "Mercia, the astronomer royal: A romance" by A. Garland Mears, readers are transported to a world where science and love intertwine. Mercia, a brilliant astronomer, finds herself torn between her passion for the stars and a forbidden romance that challenges everything she knows. As she navigates the complexities of her personal and professional life, Mercia must confront the consequences of her choices and the power of following one's heart. This captivating tale explores the delicate balance between ambition and love, and the sacrifices one must make in pursuit of both.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Science Fiction

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Pursuit of Passion, Conflict between Duty and Desire
