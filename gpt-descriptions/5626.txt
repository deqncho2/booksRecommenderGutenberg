Synopsis: In the small town of Riverton, a group of misfit teenagers form an unlikely alliance and embark on a thrilling adventure that will test their courage, friendship, and love for all things fast and furious. As they uncover a hidden secret about their town's racing history, they must navigate dangerous challenges, rival racers, and their own personal demons to prove that they are the ultimate Motormaniacs.

Genres: Young Adult, Adventure, Sports

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Identity, Overcoming Challenges
