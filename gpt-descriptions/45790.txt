Synopsis: In 'Winged Wheels in France' by Shoemaker, Michael Myers, a young American cyclist embarks on a thrilling adventure through the picturesque landscapes of France. As he navigates the challenging terrain and encounters unexpected obstacles, he discovers the true meaning of perseverance, friendship, and the transformative power of chasing one's dreams. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding moments, this captivating tale will transport readers into the world of competitive cycling and leave them yearning for their own exhilarating journey.

Genres: Sports, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Pursuit of Dreams
