Synopsis: In 'The Harvester' by Gene Stratton-Porter, a mysterious man known as the Harvester lives a solitary life in the heart of the wilderness, where he cultivates medicinal plants and studies the secrets of nature. When a chance encounter with a beautiful woman named Ruth opens his heart to the possibility of love, the Harvester must navigate the complexities of their budding relationship while facing unexpected challenges that threaten to tear them apart. As their bond deepens, the Harvester's past and present collide, forcing him to confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will shape their future. 

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Love and sacrifice, the healing power of nature, self-discovery and redemption
