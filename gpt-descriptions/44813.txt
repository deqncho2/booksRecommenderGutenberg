Synopsis: In "Sally Scott of the WAVES" by Roy J. Snell, a young woman named Sally Scott finds herself thrust into the world of the Women Accepted for Volunteer Emergency Service (WAVES) during World War II. As she navigates the challenges of military life, Sally discovers hidden secrets and uncovers a dangerous plot that could have far-reaching consequences. With her determination and resourcefulness, Sally becomes an unexpected heroine, fighting not only for her own survival but also for the safety of her fellow servicewomen.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Patriotism, Female Empowerment, Espionage
