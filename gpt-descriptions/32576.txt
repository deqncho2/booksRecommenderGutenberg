Synopsis: In "A Novelist on Novels," George, Walter Lionel takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of literature, offering insightful and thought-provoking analysis of various novels. From classic works to contemporary masterpieces, George delves into the minds of renowned authors, exploring the intricacies of their storytelling techniques, character development, and underlying themes. With his eloquent prose and deep understanding of the craft, George invites readers to see novels in a new light, igniting a passion for literature and inspiring aspiring writers to hone their skills. 

Genres: Literary criticism, non-fiction

Themes: The art of storytelling, character development, the power of literature
