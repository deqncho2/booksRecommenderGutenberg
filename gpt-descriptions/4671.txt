Synopsis: 
In 'The Story of the Pony Express', Glenn D. Bradley takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed American West, where a group of daring young riders risked their lives to deliver mail at unprecedented speed. From the founding of the Pony Express to its legendary riders and the challenges they faced, this captivating account sheds light on the remarkable history of this iconic mail delivery service. With tales of danger, adventure, and the indomitable spirit of the riders, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a pivotal chapter of American history.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Innovation
