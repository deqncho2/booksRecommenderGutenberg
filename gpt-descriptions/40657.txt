Synopsis: In 'The Quest: The authorized translation from the Dutch of De kleine Johannes', we follow the enchanting journey of a young boy named Johannes who discovers a hidden world filled with mystical creatures and profound wisdom. As he embarks on a quest to find the true meaning of life, Johannes encounters a series of captivating adventures and encounters with both light and darkness. Through his encounters, Johannes learns valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the complexities of the human spirit. This beautifully written tale takes readers on a thought-provoking exploration of the mysteries of existence and the pursuit of inner truth.

Genres: Fantasy, Coming-of-age, Philosophical fiction

Themes: Quest for meaning, Nature of reality, Friendship, Love, Self-discovery
