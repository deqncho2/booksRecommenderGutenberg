Synopsis: 
In 'Linda Carlton's Hollywood Flight' by Lavell, Edith, Linda Carlton, a talented young actress, finds herself on a thrilling journey to Hollywood in pursuit of her dreams. As she navigates the glitz and glamour of the entertainment industry, Linda encounters unexpected challenges, heartwarming friendships, and a whirlwind romance that could change her life forever. With each twist and turn, Linda must make difficult choices that will test her resilience and determination. Will she be able to overcome the obstacles and achieve her ultimate goal of stardom?

Genres: Drama, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Pursuit of dreams, Friendship, Love and sacrifice
