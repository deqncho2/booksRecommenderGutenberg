Synopsis: In 'Redemption' by Robert F. Young, a disillusioned detective finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and lies when he is assigned to investigate a mysterious murder case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a dark underworld filled with corruption, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With his own troubled past haunting him, the detective must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and find redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Corruption
