Synopsis: In "The Unwelcome Man," a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, unsettling its residents with his enigmatic presence. As the townspeople grapple with their curiosity and suspicion, they soon discover that this man holds a dark secret that threatens to unravel their lives. With each passing day, the tension escalates, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Small-town dynamics, Suspicion and paranoia
