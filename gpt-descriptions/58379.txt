Synopsis: In "Montessori Children" by Carolyn Sherwin Bailey, embark on a captivating journey into the world of Montessori education, where children are encouraged to explore, learn, and grow at their own pace. Follow the experiences of young students as they engage in hands-on activities, develop their independence, and discover the joy of self-directed learning. From the enchanting Montessori classroom to the transformative impact on children's lives, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a revolutionary educational approach that empowers young minds.

Genres: Educational, Non-fiction, Parenting

Themes: Montessori education, Child development, Independent learning, Hands-on learning, Self-directed learning
