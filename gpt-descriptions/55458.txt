Synopsis: In 'The Curse of Kehama, Volume 1', Robert Southey takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical and treacherous landscapes of ancient India. As the story unfolds, a young hero named Ladurlad finds himself entangled in a web of dark secrets, forbidden love, and supernatural forces. With the fate of his beloved at stake, Ladurlad must navigate a world filled with sorcery, curses, and divine intervention. As he battles against formidable enemies and uncovers the truth behind the curse of Kehama, Ladurlad's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Epic Poetry, Fantasy, Romanticism

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Good vs. Evil, Fate and Destiny
