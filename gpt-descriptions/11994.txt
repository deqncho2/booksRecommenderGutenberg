Synopsis: In "A Residence in France During the Years 1792, 1793, 1794 and 1795, Part III., 1794," we follow the captivating journey of an English lady as she navigates the tumultuous political landscape of France during the French Revolution. Through a series of letters, she provides a vivid and intimate account of her experiences, offering a unique perspective on the French character and manners during this transformative period. As she immerses herself in the society and culture of France, the reader is drawn into a world of intrigue, danger, and unexpected alliances. Will she be able to navigate the treacherous waters of revolution and find her place in this new reality?

Genres: Historical fiction, Epistolary novel

Themes: Revolution, Identity, Cultural clash
