Synopsis: 
In 'Intra Muros' by Rebecca Ruter Springer, a young woman named Ruth Thorne embarks on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. After a series of unexpected events, Ruth finds herself in a mysterious and enchanting realm known as Intra Muros. As she navigates this captivating world, she encounters fascinating characters and experiences profound moments of introspection. Through her encounters and adventures, Ruth begins to unravel the secrets of Intra Muros and discovers the true purpose of her own existence.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Spiritual Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Spiritual Awakening, Purpose in Life
