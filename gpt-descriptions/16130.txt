Synopsis: 

'Judgments of the Court of Appeal of New Zealand on Proceedings to Review Aspects of the Report of the Royal Commission of Inquiry into the Mount Erebus Aircraft Disaster: C.A. 95/81' delves into a gripping legal battle that follows the aftermath of a tragic aircraft disaster. The Court of Appeal of New Zealand is tasked with reviewing aspects of the Royal Commission of Inquiry's report, leading to a complex and thought-provoking exploration of accountability, justice, and the pursuit of truth. 

Genres: Legal, Non-fiction, True Crime

Themes: Accountability, Justice, Truth-seeking
