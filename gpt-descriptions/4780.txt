Synopsis: In 'Terrorists and Freedom Fighters' by Samuel Vaknin, a gripping exploration of the complex world of terrorism and insurgency unfolds. Through a series of thought-provoking essays, Vaknin delves into the minds of those who resort to violence in the name of their cause, examining their motivations, tactics, and the impact they have on society. From the psychological profiles of terrorists to the strategies employed by freedom fighters, this book offers a unique perspective on a topic that continues to shape our world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political Science, Psychology

Themes: Terrorism, Insurgency, Ideology, Violence, Society
