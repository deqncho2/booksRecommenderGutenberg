Synopsis: In 'Mildred at Roselands: A Sequel to Mildred Keith', we follow the captivating journey of Mildred Keith as she navigates the challenges of adolescence and discovers the true meaning of family and faith. Set in the picturesque Roselands, this heartwarming sequel delves into Mildred's relationships, her pursuit of personal growth, and the unexpected twists that life throws her way. As she faces both joyous moments and heart-wrenching trials, Mildred's resilience and determination shine through, captivating readers with her unwavering spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Historical

Themes: Family, Faith, Resilience, Personal Growth
