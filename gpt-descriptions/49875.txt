Synopsis: 

In 'The History of the 2/6th (Rifle) Battalion, "the King's" (Liverpool Regiment) 1914-1919', author C. E. Wurtzburg takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of a remarkable battalion during World War I. From their formation in 1914 to their final days in 1919, the battalion's story unfolds with gripping detail, offering a unique perspective on the war and the soldiers who fought in it. Through personal accounts, historical records, and vivid descriptions, Wurtzburg brings to life the challenges, triumphs, and sacrifices of these brave men, shedding light on the untold stories of their heroism and resilience. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: War and conflict, Heroism, Camaraderie, Sacrifice
