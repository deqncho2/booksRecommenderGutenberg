Synopsis: 

'Powder-Post Beetles in Buildings: What to Do About Them' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the mysterious world of these destructive insects and provides practical solutions for dealing with their presence in buildings. Written by McIntyre and St. George, this informative work explores the various types of powder-post beetles, their behavior, and the damage they can cause to wooden structures. From identifying infestations to implementing effective eradication methods, the authors offer invaluable advice to homeowners, contractors, and pest control professionals alike. With a focus on prevention and preservation, this book equips readers with the knowledge and tools necessary to protect their homes and buildings from these tiny yet formidable adversaries.

Genres: Non-fiction, Home Improvement, Pest Control

Themes: Insect infestation, Building maintenance, Preservation
