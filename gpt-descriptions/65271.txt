Synopsis: In 'Gloria at Boarding School' by Lilian Garis, follow the captivating journey of Gloria, a spirited young girl who finds herself navigating the challenges and adventures of boarding school life. From forming unexpected friendships to facing unexpected obstacles, Gloria's determination and resilience shine through as she discovers the true meaning of independence and self-discovery. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by Gloria's journey of growth and the surprises that await her at every corner.

Genres: Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Independence, Friendship, Self-Discovery
