Synopsis: In 'More about Pixie' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, readers are taken on a delightful journey as they follow the adventures of Pixie, a spirited and mischievous young girl. Set in a charming English village, Pixie's escapades and encounters with various characters bring laughter, surprises, and heartwarming moments. As Pixie navigates through her world, readers will be captivated by her infectious energy and the unexpected twists and turns that unfold.

Genres: Fiction, Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Adventure, Friendship, Growing Up
