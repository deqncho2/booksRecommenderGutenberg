Synopsis: 
In "The Fortunes and Misfortunes of the Famous Moll Flanders," we follow the captivating life of Moll Flanders, a woman who navigates through the turbulent world of 17th-century England. From her humble beginnings as an orphan, Moll embarks on a journey filled with love, loss, deception, and survival. As she encounters various characters and faces numerous challenges, Moll's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the test. Will she be able to overcome the obstacles that life throws her way, or will she succumb to the misfortunes that seem to follow her at every turn?

Genres: 
Historical fiction, adventure, romance

Themes: 
Identity, morality, social class, survival
