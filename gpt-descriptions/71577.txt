Synopsis: In the heart of revolutionary Boston, a young patriot lad finds himself caught up in a dangerous game of espionage and intrigue. As tensions rise and the fight for independence intensifies, he must navigate a treacherous path, risking everything for the cause he believes in. With secrets to uncover and a city on the brink of revolution, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Courage, Freedom
