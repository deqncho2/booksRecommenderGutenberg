Synopsis: In "Personal Recollections of the War of 1861: As Private, Sergeant and Lieutenant in the Sixty-First Regiment, New York Volunteer Infantry," Charles A. Fuller takes us on a gripping journey through his firsthand experiences as a soldier during the American Civil War. From his humble beginnings as a private to his rise in rank as a sergeant and lieutenant, Fuller vividly recounts the trials and tribulations, the camaraderie and conflicts, and the moments of courage and despair that he and his fellow soldiers faced on the battlefield. Through his personal recollections, the reader gains a unique perspective on the realities of war and the profound impact it had on the lives of those who fought.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: War, Patriotism, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Survival
