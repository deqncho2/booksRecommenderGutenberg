Synopsis: 
In "Correggio: A Collection of Fifteen Pictures and a Portrait of the Painter with Introduction and Interpretation," Estelle M. Hurll takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and art of the renowned Italian painter, Correggio. Through vivid descriptions and insightful interpretations, Hurll brings to life fifteen of Correggio's most iconic paintings, showcasing his mastery of light, perspective, and emotion. From religious scenes to mythological tales, each artwork is explored in detail, revealing the artist's unique style and innovative techniques. Accompanied by a portrait of Correggio himself, this collection offers a fascinating glimpse into the mind of a true artistic genius.

Genres: Art History, Biography, Interpretation

Themes: Artistic genius, Innovation, Emotion, Religious and mythological symbolism
