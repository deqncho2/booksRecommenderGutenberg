Synopsis: In 'School-Room Humour' by T. J. Macnamara, prepare to embark on a hilarious journey through the mischievous and uproarious world of a school classroom. Follow the witty and cunning antics of the students as they navigate their way through pranks, practical jokes, and clever schemes that will leave you laughing out loud. From the ingenious ways they outsmart their teachers to the unexpected twists and turns, this book is a delightful and entertaining exploration of the lighter side of education.

Genres: Humor, Fiction, Satire

Themes: School life, Pranks, Wit and cleverness
