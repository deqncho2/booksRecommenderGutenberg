Synopsis: In this intriguing collection, Marat delves into two distinct medical topics that have long puzzled scholars and practitioners alike. In the first tract, he explores the enigmatic condition known as gleets, offering a thought-provoking essay that challenges conventional wisdom and sheds new light on its nature and treatment. The second tract delves into a peculiar disease of the eyes, delving into its origins, causes, and potential cures. Marat's meticulous research and innovative ideas make this reprint a captivating read for those interested in the mysteries of medical science.

Genres: Medical literature, scientific research, historical non-fiction

Themes: Medical mysteries, unconventional theories, scientific inquiry
