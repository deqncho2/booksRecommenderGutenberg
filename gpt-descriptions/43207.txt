Synopsis: 

'Scenes and Characters from the Works of Charles Dickens: Being Eight Hundred and Sixty-six Pictures Printed from the Original Wood Blocks' is a captivating collection of illustrations that brings to life the vivid and diverse world created by the renowned author Charles Dickens. From the bustling streets of Victorian London to the idyllic countryside, this book showcases the memorable characters and iconic scenes from Dickens' beloved novels. Each woodblock print captures the essence of Dickens' storytelling, inviting readers to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of his literary universe.

Genres: Art, Literature, Biography

Themes: Victorian society, social injustice, human resilience, the power of storytelling
