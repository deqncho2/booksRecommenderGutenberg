Synopsis: In 'Bobby and Betty with the workers' by Dopp, Katharine Elizabeth, two young siblings embark on a thrilling adventure that takes them deep into the world of the working class. As they navigate through the challenges and mysteries of this unfamiliar realm, Bobby and Betty uncover secrets that will change their lives forever. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Social class, Family bonds, Discovery
