Synopsis: 
In 'The Sixth Sense: A Novel' by Stephen McKenna, a young woman named Emily discovers an extraordinary ability that sets her apart from others. As she navigates through a world where reality and perception intertwine, she must unravel the mysteries surrounding her newfound power. With the help of a mysterious mentor, Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery, uncovering hidden truths and confronting her deepest fears. As the lines between the seen and the unseen blur, the novel takes readers on a thrilling and thought-provoking exploration of the human mind and the power of perception.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Perception, Identity, Reality vs. Illusion
