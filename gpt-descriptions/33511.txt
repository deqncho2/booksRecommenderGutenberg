Synopsis: 
'Tales of Passed Times' by Perrault, Charles is a collection of enchanting fairy tales that have captivated readers for centuries. From the classic stories of Cinderella and Sleeping Beauty to lesser-known tales like Bluebeard and Puss in Boots, this collection transports readers to a world of magic, adventure, and moral lessons. Each story is filled with memorable characters, unexpected twists, and timeless messages that continue to resonate with readers of all ages. Whether it's the triumph of good over evil or the power of love and kindness, these tales are sure to ignite the imagination and leave readers longing for more.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Children's literature

Themes: Morality, Transformation, Love and kindness
