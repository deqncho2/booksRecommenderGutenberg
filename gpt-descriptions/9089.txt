Synopsis: In "Handel" by Edward J. Dent, embark on a captivating journey through the life and music of one of the greatest composers of all time. From his humble beginnings in Germany to his rise to fame in England, follow Handel's extraordinary career as he overcomes adversity, navigates the complexities of the music industry, and leaves an indelible mark on the world of classical music. With vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, Dent paints a vivid portrait of Handel's genius, his triumphs, and the profound impact he had on the musical landscape of his time.

Genres: Biography, Music, History

Themes: Creativity and inspiration, Perseverance and resilience, Artistic legacy
