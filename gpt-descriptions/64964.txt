Synopsis: In exitu Israel is a captivating historical novel that takes readers on a thrilling journey through ancient Israel. Set in the tumultuous times of the Judges, the story follows the lives of two young Israelites, Gideon and Deborah, as they navigate the challenges of their era. From battles against enemy nations to internal conflicts within their own tribes, the protagonists find themselves caught in a web of political intrigue, personal struggles, and divine intervention. As they strive to fulfill their destinies and lead their people, Gideon and Deborah must confront their own doubts, face formidable adversaries, and make difficult choices that will shape the future of Israel.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Biblical fiction

Themes: Leadership, Faith, Courage, Divine intervention, Political intrigue
