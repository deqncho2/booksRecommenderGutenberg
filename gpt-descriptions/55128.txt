Synopsis: In "Extempore Speech: How to Acquire and Practice It" by Pittenger, William, readers are taken on a captivating journey into the art of impromptu speaking. Through insightful guidance and practical tips, the author unveils the secrets to mastering the skill of delivering compelling speeches on the spot. From conquering stage fright to crafting persuasive arguments, this book offers a comprehensive roadmap for anyone seeking to excel in extemporaneous speaking. Whether you aspire to become a confident public speaker or simply wish to enhance your communication skills, Pittenger's expertise will inspire and empower you to embrace the spontaneity of extempore speech.

Genres: Self-help, Communication, Public Speaking

Themes: Confidence, Improvisation, Effective Communication
