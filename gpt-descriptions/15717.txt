Synopsis: In 'Books and Persons; Being Comments on a Past Epoch, 1908-1911', Arnold Bennett takes readers on a captivating journey through the literary landscape of a bygone era. Through his insightful and thought-provoking commentary, Bennett explores the works of various authors, delving into their impact on society and the human condition. From the exploration of classic novels to the examination of contemporary literature, this collection of essays offers a fascinating glimpse into the literary world of the early 20th century.

Genres: Literary criticism, essay collection, historical commentary

Themes: Literature and society, the evolution of the novel, cultural criticism
