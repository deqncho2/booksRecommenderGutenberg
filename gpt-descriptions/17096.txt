Synopsis: Bunny Brown and his sister Sue are excited to spend their summer at Camp Rest-A-While, a beautiful lakeside retreat. As they embark on various adventures, they make new friends, explore the surrounding nature, and learn valuable life lessons. From sailing on the lake to participating in campfire sing-alongs, the siblings discover the joys of outdoor activities and the importance of teamwork and friendship. But when a mysterious event occurs, Bunny and Sue find themselves caught up in a thrilling mystery that will test their courage and problem-solving skills. Will they be able to unravel the secrets of Camp Rest-A-While?

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Outdoor Exploration, Problem-Solving
