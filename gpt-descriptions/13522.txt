Synopsis: In 'Superseded' by Sinclair, May, a young woman named Lily discovers a hidden world of supernatural beings living among humans. As she delves deeper into this mysterious realm, she uncovers dark secrets and finds herself caught in a dangerous power struggle. With her own life at stake, Lily must navigate treacherous alliances and confront her own hidden potential to survive in this thrilling urban fantasy.

Genres: Urban Fantasy, Paranormal Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Betrayal, Hidden Realities
