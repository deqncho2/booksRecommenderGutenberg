Synopsis: 
In the picturesque Basque country of France, a young shepherd named Ramuntcho finds himself torn between his love for the beautiful Gracieuse and his loyalty to his homeland. As he navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and duty, Ramuntcho becomes entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden desires that threaten to shatter his world. Set against the backdrop of stunning landscapes and cultural traditions, this captivating tale explores the timeless themes of passion, identity, and the clash between tradition and modernity.

Genres: 
Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Cultural Identity, Tradition versus Modernity
