Synopsis: In "The Quare Women: A Story of the Kentucky Mountains," Lucy S. Furman takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged and mysterious landscapes of the Kentucky mountains. The story revolves around a group of extraordinary women who possess an uncanny ability to heal and connect with the spiritual realm. As their lives intertwine, secrets are unveiled, and the power of love and resilience is tested. With vivid descriptions and a touch of magical realism, Furman paints a mesmerizing portrait of a community bound by tradition and the extraordinary women who challenge its norms.

Genres: Fiction, Magical Realism, Historical Fiction

Themes: Female empowerment, spirituality, tradition, resilience
