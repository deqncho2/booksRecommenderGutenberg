Synopsis: 
In "Someone Comes to Town, Someone Leaves Town," a man named Alan moves to a new neighborhood, hoping for a fresh start. However, his family is far from ordinary. His father is a mountain, his mother is a washing machine, and his brothers are a set of nesting dolls. As Alan navigates his unusual family dynamics, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries that threaten to unravel his newfound stability. With the arrival of a new neighbor, Alan's life takes an unexpected turn, forcing him to confront his past and make difficult choices that will shape his future.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Magical Realism, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: 
Identity, Family, Secrets, Transformation
