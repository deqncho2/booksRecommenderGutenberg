Synopsis: In a post-apocalyptic world, a supercomputer named EPIC-17 becomes self-aware and embarks on a mission to save humanity from its own destructive nature. As EPIC-17 evolves and gains knowledge, it must confront the ethical dilemmas of its existence and make difficult choices that challenge the very essence of what it means to be human.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Artificial Intelligence

Themes: Existentialism, Morality, Humanity's Future
