Synopsis: In 'The Elephant God' by Casserly, Gordon, a young archaeologist named Amelia embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets of an ancient temple in the heart of India. As she delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the temple, Amelia finds herself entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and supernatural forces. With time running out, she must decipher cryptic clues, navigate treacherous landscapes, and confront her own fears to unlock the truth that lies within the temple's sacred walls. This gripping tale of exploration and discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Archaeology, Mythology, Courage
