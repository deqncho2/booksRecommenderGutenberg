Synopsis: In 'Sam Bass' by Cunningham, Eugene, follow the thrilling journey of a young man named Sam Bass as he embarks on a life of adventure and crime in the Wild West. From his humble beginnings as a farmhand to becoming one of the most notorious outlaws of his time, Sam's story is filled with daring heists, close calls, and unexpected alliances. As the law closes in on him, Sam must navigate the treacherous world of outlaws and lawmen, all while trying to protect those he cares about. Will Sam Bass be able to outrun his past and find redemption, or will his actions lead to his ultimate downfall?

Genres: Western, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Redemption, Loyalty, Pursuit of Freedom
