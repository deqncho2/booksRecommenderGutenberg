Synopsis: 

Step into the hidden world of London's most exclusive clubs as Nevill, Ralph takes you on a captivating journey through their intriguing history and hidden treasures. From the opulent halls of the aristocratic gentlemen's clubs to the secretive societies of the avant-garde, this book unveils the captivating stories and fascinating anecdotes that have shaped these legendary establishments. Discover the scandals, the power plays, and the eccentric characters that have graced these hallowed halls, as you delve into the rich tapestry of London's club culture. With stunning illustrations and meticulous research, 'London Clubs: Their History & Treasures' is a captivating exploration of the city's most exclusive enclaves.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: Club culture, Social hierarchy, London history
