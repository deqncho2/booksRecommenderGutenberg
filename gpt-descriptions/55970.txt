Synopsis: 

In "The Panama Canal" by Harry Clow Boardman, embark on a thrilling journey through the fascinating history and monumental engineering feat that is the Panama Canal. This gripping account takes readers on a captivating exploration of the challenges, triumphs, and controversies surrounding the construction of one of the world's most remarkable waterways. From the audacious vision of its creators to the relentless struggles faced by the workers, this book unveils the untold stories and hidden secrets behind the creation of this iconic marvel of human ingenuity.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Engineering

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Globalization, Environmental impact
