Synopsis: In 'Captain Jim' by Bruce, Mary Grant, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn to the enigmatic Captain Jim, a sailor with a mysterious past. As she delves deeper into his world, Emily uncovers secrets that challenge her perception of love, loyalty, and the power of forgiveness. With danger lurking at every turn, Emily must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the truth and find her own path to happiness.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Redemption, Identity and self-discovery
