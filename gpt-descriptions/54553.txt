Synopsis: 

In 'Zone therapy; or, Relieving pain at home', authors Edwin F. Bowers and Wm. H. Fitzgerald delve into the fascinating world of alternative medicine and the power of touch. This comprehensive guide takes readers on a journey through the zones of the body, revealing the hidden connections between specific areas and the relief they can bring. With practical techniques and step-by-step instructions, this book empowers readers to take control of their own pain management and well-being, all from the comfort of their own homes. 

Genres: Self-help, Alternative Medicine, Health and Wellness

Themes: Pain relief, Holistic healing, Self-empowerment
