Synopsis:
In "Christina Alberta's Father," H.G. Wells takes us on a captivating journey into the life of a young woman named Christina Alberta. Raised in a small English village, Christina's life takes an unexpected turn when she discovers a shocking secret about her father. As she delves deeper into her family's history, she becomes entangled in a web of mystery, scandal, and hidden identities. With each revelation, Christina's world is turned upside down, forcing her to question her own identity and the true nature of her relationships. This gripping tale explores the complexities of family, love, and the search for personal truth.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Family, Secrets, Love, Personal Truth
