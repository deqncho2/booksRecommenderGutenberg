Synopsis: 
In 'Andy Gordon; Or, The Fortunes of A Young Janitor' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Andy, a young janitor who dreams of a better life. Despite his humble beginnings, Andy possesses a strong work ethic and determination to overcome the challenges that come his way. As he navigates through various obstacles and encounters unexpected opportunities, Andy's journey takes him on a path filled with adventure, self-discovery, and the pursuit of his dreams. Will Andy be able to rise above his circumstances and achieve the success he desires?

Genres: 
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Rags-to-riches

Themes: 
Perseverance, Social mobility, Self-improvement
