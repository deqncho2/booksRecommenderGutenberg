Synopsis: 

In the midst of the American Revolutionary War, Diary of Ezra Green, M.D. takes us on a captivating journey through the eyes of Dr. Ezra Green, a physician serving in the Continental Army. From November 1, 1777, to September 27, 1778, we witness the daily struggles, triumphs, and heartaches of a man dedicated to healing amidst the chaos of war. Through his diary entries, we delve into the lives of soldiers, civilians, and the medical challenges they face, all while experiencing the personal growth and transformation of Dr. Green himself. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Diary

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Healing, Personal Growth
