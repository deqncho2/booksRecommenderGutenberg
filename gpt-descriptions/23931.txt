Synopsis: 

In 'Instinct' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges the very essence of human nature. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden world of primal instincts and uncanny abilities that could change the course of humanity forever. However, his groundbreaking findings also attract the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to control this newfound power. With danger lurking at every turn, the scientist must navigate a treacherous path to protect his discovery and safeguard the future of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Human nature, Evolution, Power struggle
