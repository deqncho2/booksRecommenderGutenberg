Synopsis: 
In 'American Historical and Literary Curiosities, Part 05', Smith and Watson take readers on a captivating journey through lesser-known stories and intriguing anecdotes from American history and literature. From the mysterious disappearance of a famous author to the surprising origins of a beloved American holiday, this collection of curiosities will leave readers both entertained and enlightened. Delve into the hidden corners of American history and literature as Smith and Watson uncover fascinating tales that have been overlooked or forgotten. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Literature

Themes: American history, American literature, Curiosities, Forgotten stories
