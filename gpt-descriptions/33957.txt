Synopsis: In 'The Cabinet Minister: A farce in four acts', the charismatic and ambitious Cabinet Minister finds himself entangled in a web of hilarious misunderstandings and mistaken identities. As he desperately tries to maintain his reputation and navigate the chaos surrounding him, the Minister's political career hangs in the balance. With witty dialogue, clever disguises, and unexpected twists, this uproarious farce takes readers on a rollercoaster ride of laughter and intrigue.

Genres: Farce, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Politics, Deception, Identity, Ambition
