Synopsis:
'Mind Amongst the Spindles. A Miscellany, Wholly Composed by the Factory Girls' is a captivating collection of writings by the factory girls themselves. Through a series of poems, stories, and personal reflections, this work offers a unique glimpse into the lives and minds of these young women working in the factories of the 19th century. From tales of love and loss to musings on the harsh realities of their daily lives, the factory girls' voices come alive, painting a vivid picture of their hopes, dreams, and struggles. This anthology is a testament to the power of creativity and resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres:
Poetry, Short Stories, Memoir

Themes:
Working-class life, Female empowerment, Creativity, Social commentary
