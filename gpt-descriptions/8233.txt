Synopsis: 

In 'The World English Bible (WEB): Joshua' by Anonymous, embark on an epic journey through the pages of history as the Israelites, led by the valiant Joshua, face numerous trials and tribulations in their quest to claim the Promised Land. From miraculous victories to heart-wrenching defeats, this gripping tale of conquest and faith will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning each page to discover what lies ahead for the chosen people.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Religious Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Faith and Belief, Leadership and Courage, Divine Intervention, Conquest and Redemption
