Synopsis: 

'Camp-Fire and Cotton-Field: Southern Adventure in Time of War: Life with the Union Armies, and Residence on a Louisiana Plantation' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of the American Civil War. Written by Thomas Wallace Knox, this memoir offers a unique perspective as it explores the author's experiences living among the Union armies and his time spent on a Louisiana plantation. From the camaraderie around campfires to the complexities of plantation life, Knox's vivid storytelling transports readers to a world filled with adventure, danger, and unexpected encounters. 

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War Literature

Themes: American Civil War, Union Army, Plantation Life, Adventure, Survival
