Synopsis: In "Istar of Babylon: A Phantasy" by Margaret Horton Potter, a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that transports her back in time to the legendary city of Babylon. As she navigates the opulent and treacherous world of ancient Mesopotamia, she becomes entangled in a web of power, love, and betrayal. With the fate of an empire hanging in the balance, she must uncover the secrets of the past to shape the future.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Ancient civilizations, Power and politics, Love and betrayal
