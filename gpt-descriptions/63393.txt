Synopsis: In 'Mind Stealers of Pluto' by Farrell, Joseph, a team of scientists embarks on a dangerous mission to the mysterious and distant planet of Pluto. As they uncover a hidden civilization and encounter mind-altering technology, they must navigate a treacherous web of deception and betrayal. With their own sanity at stake, the team races against time to unravel the truth and save humanity from a sinister force lurking in the depths of space.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Manipulation of the mind, Betrayal and trust
