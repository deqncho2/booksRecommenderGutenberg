Synopsis: 
In 'Ten Great Events in History' by James Johonnot, embark on a captivating journey through time as the author delves into ten pivotal moments that shaped the course of human civilization. From the rise and fall of empires to groundbreaking scientific discoveries, each event is brought to life with vivid descriptions and engaging narratives. Discover the untold stories behind these historical turning points and gain a deeper understanding of the impact they had on the world we live in today.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, Narrative non-fiction, Educational

Themes: 
Historical significance, Human progress, Cause and effect
