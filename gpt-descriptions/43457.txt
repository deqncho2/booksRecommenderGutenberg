Synopsis: In "The Popular Story of Blue Beard," Perrault weaves a captivating tale of a wealthy and mysterious nobleman who captures the attention of young women with his charm and riches. However, behind his enigmatic facade lies a dark secret that sends shivers down the spine. As the story unfolds, the reader is drawn into a suspenseful journey filled with forbidden rooms, hidden keys, and the haunting question of what lies behind the locked doors. Will the courageous protagonist uncover the truth, or will she too fall victim to the chilling mystery that surrounds Blue Beard?

Genres: Fairy tale, Gothic fiction, Mystery

Themes: Curiosity, Power and control, Female agency
