Synopsis: In 'The Advanced-Guard' by Sydney C. Grier, a group of mysterious individuals known as the Advanced-Guard arrive in a small town, bringing with them a series of inexplicable events and unsettling occurrences. As the townspeople try to uncover the truth behind the Advanced-Guard's presence, they are drawn into a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. Will the townspeople be able to unravel the enigma of the Advanced-Guard before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Intrigue, Identity, Trust, Power
