Synopsis: In 'The Viking Age. Volume 2', Du Chaillu delves into the captivating early history, manners, and customs of the ancestors of the English-speaking nations. From the fierce battles and legendary voyages of the Vikings to their intriguing social structures and cultural practices, this volume offers a fascinating exploration of a pivotal era in history. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Du Chaillu brings to life the world of the Vikings, shedding light on their impact on the development of the English-speaking nations.

Genres: History, Anthropology, Cultural Studies

Themes: Viking culture, Exploration, Influence on English-speaking nations
