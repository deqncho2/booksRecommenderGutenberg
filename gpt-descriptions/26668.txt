Synopsis: In 'The Strange Adventures of Captain Dangerous, Vol. 2: Who was a sailor, a soldier, a merchant, a spy, a slave; among the moors...', George Augustus Sala takes readers on a thrilling journey through the life of Captain Dangerous. From his daring exploits as a sailor and soldier to his unexpected encounters as a merchant, spy, and even a slave among the moors, Captain Dangerous's life is filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected twists. As the story unfolds, readers will be captivated by the unpredictable adventures that await this enigmatic protagonist.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Survival, Exploration
