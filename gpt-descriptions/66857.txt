Synopsis: In 'A Prevaricated Parade' by W. C. Tuttle, a small town is thrown into chaos when a charismatic stranger arrives, promising to bring excitement and prosperity. As the townspeople become enthralled by his charm, they soon discover that his intentions may not be as noble as they initially believed. With secrets, deception, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Manipulation, Small-town dynamics
