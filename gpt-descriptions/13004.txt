Synopsis: 
In 'More Goops and How Not to Be Them: A Manual of Manners for Impolite Infants', Burgess, Gelett introduces a delightful collection of mischievous characters who exemplify the worst of manners. From the messy eater to the inconsiderate playmate, these Goops provide humorous and relatable examples of what not to do. Through witty rhymes and charming illustrations, the author encourages young readers to learn from the Goops' mistakes and strive for better behavior. This entertaining and educational book is sure to captivate children and teach them valuable lessons in etiquette.

Genres: Children's Literature, Humor, Poetry

Themes: Manners, Etiquette, Behavior
