Synopsis: 
In 'A List To Starboard: 1909' by Smith, Francis Hopkinson, a group of wealthy and eccentric passengers embark on a luxurious ocean liner, unaware of the unexpected events that await them. As the ship sets sail, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and the passengers find themselves caught in a thrilling and dangerous adventure. With a mix of humor, mystery, and suspense, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Wealth and privilege, Intrigue and deception
