Synopsis: 
In 'Following a Chance Clew; Or, Nick Carter's Lucky Find', the renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when he stumbles upon a seemingly insignificant clue. As he follows this chance lead, Carter is thrust into a web of danger, deceit, and unexpected twists that will test his skills to the limit. With time running out, he must rely on his sharp intellect and resourcefulness to unravel the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Detective work, Perseverance, Deception
