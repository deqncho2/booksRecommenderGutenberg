Synopsis: In 'Alcatraz of the Starways', a group of prisoners find themselves trapped on a remote and mysterious planet, forced to confront their pasts and fight for their survival. As they navigate the treacherous landscape and face unimaginable challenges, they must uncover the secrets of their captors and find a way to escape before it's too late. This gripping science fiction adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the nature of freedom and the lengths one will go to regain it.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Freedom, Redemption, Survival
