Synopsis: In 'Success and How He Won It' by Werner, E., we follow the journey of a young and ambitious protagonist who is determined to achieve greatness. Faced with numerous challenges and setbacks, the protagonist must navigate through a world filled with competition, betrayal, and unexpected opportunities. As the story unfolds, readers are taken on a thrilling ride, witnessing the protagonist's relentless pursuit of success and the sacrifices made along the way. Will the protagonist's unwavering determination be enough to conquer the obstacles in their path and achieve their dreams?

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Sacrifice, Competition
