Synopsis: In 'Once a Greech' by Evelyn E. Smith, a young woman named Lila finds herself in a peculiar situation when she wakes up one morning to discover that she has transformed into a Greech, a small and mischievous creature from another world. As Lila navigates her new life as a Greech, she must learn to adapt to her surroundings and find a way to reverse the transformation. Along the way, she encounters a cast of eccentric characters and embarks on a whimsical adventure filled with humor, mystery, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Comedy

Themes: Identity, Transformation, Acceptance
