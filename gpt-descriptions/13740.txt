Synopsis: In 'Mischievous Maid Faynie' by Laura Jean Libbey, a spirited young woman named Faynie finds herself in a series of amusing and unpredictable situations as she navigates the challenges of being a maid. From mistaken identities to romantic entanglements, Faynie's mischievous nature leads her on a delightful adventure filled with laughter, love, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Romance, Comedy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Love, Adventure
