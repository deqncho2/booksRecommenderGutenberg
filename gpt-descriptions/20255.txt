Synopsis: In 'The Unruly Sprite: A Partial Fairy Tale' by Van Dyke, Henry, a mischievous sprite wreaks havoc in a small village, leaving the townspeople bewildered and frustrated. As the sprite's pranks escalate, a young girl named Lily takes it upon herself to uncover the truth behind the sprite's actions. With each encounter, Lily discovers that there is more to this unruly sprite than meets the eye, leading her on a whimsical and enchanting adventure that will change her life forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Magic and its consequences, the power of perception, the importance of empathy
