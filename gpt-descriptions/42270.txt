Synopsis: In 'The Retrospect' by Cambridge, Ada, a young woman named Emily discovers a mysterious journal hidden in her grandmother's attic. As she delves into the pages, she becomes captivated by the vivid stories of love, loss, and adventure that unfold before her. With each entry, Emily finds herself drawn deeper into the lives of the journal's author and the secrets they hold. As she unravels the truth behind the journal, Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and understanding, ultimately leading her to question her own place in the world.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Personal growth
