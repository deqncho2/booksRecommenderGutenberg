Synopsis: In "Roland Graeme: Knight. A Novel of Our Time" by Agnes Maule Machar, readers are transported to a captivating world where the protagonist, Roland Graeme, embarks on a thrilling journey filled with love, loyalty, and the pursuit of justice. Set in a tumultuous era, Roland finds himself torn between his duty as a knight and his desire for personal happiness. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters intriguing characters, Roland's courage and integrity are put to the test. This gripping tale explores the complexities of honor, sacrifice, and the timeless struggle between good and evil.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Chivalry, Identity, Morality, Love and Duty
