Synopsis:
In 'Tales of English Minsters: Hereford', Elizabeth W. Grierson takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and hidden secrets of Hereford Cathedral. From the mysterious legends surrounding the building's construction to the intriguing stories of the people who have walked its hallowed halls, Grierson weaves a tapestry of intrigue, romance, and spiritual discovery. As the tales unfold, readers will be transported to a world where ancient relics hold unimaginable power, where love and betrayal intertwine, and where the past and present collide in unexpected ways. Grierson's vivid descriptions and meticulous research bring the majestic cathedral to life, making it a character in its own right. Prepare to be enthralled by the enchanting stories that lie within the walls of Hereford Cathedral.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: History, Religion, Secrets, Love and Betrayal
