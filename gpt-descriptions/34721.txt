Synopsis: In 'The Lighter Side of School Life' by Hay, Ian, follow the hilarious and heartwarming adventures of a group of mischievous students as they navigate the ups and downs of school life. From pranks and escapades to unexpected friendships and life lessons, this delightful tale will keep you laughing and reminiscing about your own school days.

Genres: Humor, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Adolescence, Education
