Synopsis: In 'Last Enemy' by H. Beam Piper, a brilliant scientist named Conn Maxwell finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and interstellar conflict. As he navigates through a complex world of secret societies and hidden agendas, Conn must uncover the truth behind a mysterious alien artifact that holds the key to humanity's survival. With his life on the line, he must outwit his enemies and make alliances with unlikely allies to prevent a catastrophic war that could destroy everything he holds dear.

Genres: Science Fiction, Political Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power struggles, Betrayal, Survival, Exploration of alien civilizations
