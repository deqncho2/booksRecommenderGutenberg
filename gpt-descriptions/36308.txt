Synopsis: 
Songs From Alice in Wonderland and Through the Looking-Glass is a whimsical collection of poems and songs that accompany Lewis Carroll's beloved tales. From the curious adventures of Alice in Wonderland to her journey through the Looking-Glass, these enchanting verses bring to life the imaginative world of Wonderland. Filled with clever wordplay, nonsensical rhymes, and delightful characters, this collection invites readers to join Alice on her extraordinary escapades and discover the magic that lies within Carroll's lyrical imagination.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Poetry, Fantasy

Themes: 
Imagination, Curiosity, Nonsense
