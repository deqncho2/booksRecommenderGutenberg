Synopsis: In 'The Christmas Angel' by Abbie Farwell Brown, a young girl named Effie embarks on a magical journey when she discovers a mysterious angel ornament on her Christmas tree. As the angel comes to life, Effie is transported to different time periods and encounters various characters who teach her valuable lessons about love, kindness, and the true meaning of Christmas. With each adventure, Effie's belief in the power of miracles grows, leading her to a heartwarming revelation that will touch the hearts of readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Holiday

Themes: Magic, Miracles, Love, Kindness, Christmas Spirit
