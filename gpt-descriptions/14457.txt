Synopsis: In "Kings, Queens and Pawns: An American Woman at the Front," Mary Roberts Rinehart takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous landscape of World War I. As an American woman, she defies societal norms and ventures to the frontlines, where she encounters danger, intrigue, and unexpected romance. With her sharp wit and fearless spirit, Rinehart provides a unique perspective on the war, shedding light on the sacrifices made by both soldiers and civilians. As she navigates the complexities of war, Rinehart's experiences will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the battlefield.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Love and Sacrifice, Gender Roles, Espionage
