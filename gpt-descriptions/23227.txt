Synopsis: 
'Aucassin and Nicolette: translated from the Old French' is a captivating medieval romance that follows the forbidden love between Aucassin, a young nobleman, and Nicolette, a beautiful and mysterious maiden. Set in a world of chivalry and courtly love, their passionate and tumultuous relationship is tested by societal expectations, family feuds, and a series of perilous adventures. As they navigate through a world filled with knights, troubadours, and enchantments, Aucassin and Nicolette must fight against all odds to be together, defying the conventions of their time.

Genres: Romance, Medieval Literature, Adventure

Themes: Forbidden Love, Chivalry, Courtly Love, Society and Expectations
