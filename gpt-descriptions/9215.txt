Synopsis: In "Chippings with a Chisel," a young sculptor named Kenyon embarks on a journey to create a masterpiece that will capture the essence of beauty and perfection. As he delves into his work, he encounters unexpected challenges and discovers the transformative power of art. Through his artistic endeavors, Kenyon explores the depths of his own soul and the complexities of human nature, ultimately leading him to a profound realization.

Genres: Fiction, Short Story, Gothic Literature

Themes: Art and Creativity, Beauty and Perfection, Self-Discovery, Human Nature
