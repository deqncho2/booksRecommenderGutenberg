Synopsis: In 'Heart' by Henry Slesar, a renowned surgeon finds himself in a moral dilemma when he receives an unusual request from a mysterious patient. As he delves deeper into the patient's background, he uncovers a shocking secret that challenges his beliefs and forces him to question the boundaries of medical ethics. With each twist and turn, the story takes readers on a suspenseful journey that explores the complexities of human nature and the consequences of our choices.

Genres: Psychological thriller, suspense, medical fiction

Themes: Morality, ethics, identity, the power of secrets
