Synopsis: 

'Beautiful Gardens in America' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of American gardens. From meticulously manicured landscapes to wild and untamed natural wonders, author Louise Shelton unveils a collection of breathtaking gardens that will leave readers in awe. With vivid descriptions and stunning photographs, this book showcases the diversity and beauty of gardens across the United States, revealing hidden gems and well-known treasures alike. Whether exploring historic estates, urban oases, or botanical wonders, 'Beautiful Gardens in America' is a celebration of the artistry and passion that goes into creating these living works of art.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, Photography

Themes: Beauty of nature, Artistic expression, Cultural heritage
