Synopsis: In 'The Deadly Dust' by Murray Leinster, a mysterious and deadly substance is unleashed upon the world, causing chaos and destruction. As scientists race against time to find a solution, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate through a post-apocalyptic landscape, facing unimaginable dangers and uncovering shocking secrets along the way. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, they must confront their own fears and make sacrifices in order to save what remains of civilization.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Post-Apocalyptic

Themes: Survival, Sacrifice, Human Ingenuity
