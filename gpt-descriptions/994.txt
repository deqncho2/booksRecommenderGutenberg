Synopsis:
In the remote Aran Islands off the west coast of Ireland, a family grapples with the harsh realities of life and death. Maurya, an aging widow, has already lost her husband and five of her sons to the treacherous sea. Now, as her last remaining son prepares to set sail, she must confront her own fears and the relentless power of the ocean. With hauntingly beautiful language and a poignant exploration of grief, "Riders to the Sea" takes readers on a journey of loss, resilience, and the indomitable spirit of the human heart.

Genres:
Drama, Tragedy

Themes:
Grief, Loss, Fate, Resilience
