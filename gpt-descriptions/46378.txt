Synopsis: 
"A Plain and Faithful Narrative of the Original Design, Rise, Progress and Present State of the Indian Charity-School at Lebanon, in Connecticut" is a captivating non-fiction work by Eleazar Wheelock. The book takes readers on a remarkable journey through the establishment and development of an Indian charity-school in Lebanon, Connecticut. Through vivid storytelling and firsthand accounts, Wheelock paints a vivid picture of the challenges, triumphs, and transformations that occurred within the school and its students. From the initial vision to the current state of the institution, this narrative offers a compelling exploration of the power of education and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Education

Themes: Education, Cultural Exchange, Empowerment
