Synopsis: 
In 'Back to Life' by Gibbs, Philip, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Emily Parker makes a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the world. However, her life takes an unexpected turn when she becomes the target of a powerful organization determined to control her invention. As she fights to protect her creation and stay alive, Dr. Parker finds herself caught in a web of intrigue, danger, and betrayal. With the help of an enigmatic ally, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and reclaim her life.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Technology and its consequences, Power struggles, Redemption
