Synopsis: In 'The Land of Look Behind' by Brown, Paul Cameron, a young archaeologist embarks on a thrilling expedition to Jamaica, where he stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change history forever. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the island, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of intrigue, betrayal, and ancient folklore. With each step closer to the truth, he must confront his own fears and navigate treacherous paths, all while trying to protect the knowledge that could reshape the world. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they unravel the enigma of 'The Land of Look Behind'.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Secrets and Hidden Knowledge, Cultural Mythology
