Synopsis: In 'Peeps at Many Lands: Belgium', join the young protagonist on an exciting journey through the enchanting country of Belgium. From the bustling streets of Brussels to the picturesque canals of Bruges, the reader will be captivated by the rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning landscapes of this European gem. Along the way, the protagonist encounters friendly locals, indulges in mouthwatering Belgian chocolates, and discovers the secrets behind Belgium's famous lace-making industry. With each turn of the page, the reader will be transported to a land filled with charm, beauty, and unexpected surprises.

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Cultural diversity, History, Exploration
