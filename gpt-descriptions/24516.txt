Synopsis: In 'The Crystal Hunters: A Boy's Adventures in the Higher Alps', young Tom and his adventurous uncle set off on a thrilling expedition to the majestic Higher Alps. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter mysterious characters, they become entangled in a quest for rare and precious crystals. With danger lurking at every turn, Tom must summon his courage and resourcefulness to uncover the secrets hidden within the mountains. Will he succeed in his quest and emerge unscathed from this perilous adventure?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Bravery, Discovery
