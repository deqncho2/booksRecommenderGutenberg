Synopsis: 

"The United States Marine Corps in the World War" is a captivating historical account that delves into the experiences and heroic actions of the United States Marine Corps during World War I. Written by Edwin North McClellan, a former Marine Corps officer, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through the battles, challenges, and triumphs faced by the Marines on the front lines of the war. From the trenches of France to the intense battles in Belleau Wood and beyond, the book offers a vivid and detailed portrayal of the Marines' unwavering dedication, bravery, and sacrifice in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, War memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Heroism, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Warfare
