Synopsis: In 'The Caged Lion' by Charlotte M. Yonge, a young woman named Ethel May is thrust into a world of secrets and intrigue when she becomes the governess to a wealthy family. As she delves deeper into the family's history, she uncovers a web of lies, betrayal, and hidden desires that threaten to tear them apart. Ethel must navigate the treacherous waters of love and loyalty, all while trying to protect her own heart. With each twist and turn, the tension builds, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and loyalty, Betrayal and redemption
