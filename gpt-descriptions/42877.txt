Synopsis: 

'One Hundred Books Famous in English Literature: With Facsimiles of the Title-Pages' by Grolier Club takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of English literature. This beautifully illustrated book showcases a curated selection of one hundred iconic books, each accompanied by a facsimile of its title page. From timeless classics to lesser-known gems, this collection offers a glimpse into the literary masterpieces that have shaped the English language and influenced generations of readers. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, Art

Themes: Literary history, Book design, Cultural impact
