Synopsis: In 'Two-Legs' by Carl Ewald, a mysterious creature with two legs suddenly appears in a small village, captivating the townspeople with its peculiar behavior and enigmatic origins. As the villagers grapple with their curiosity and fear, they embark on a journey of self-discovery, challenging their preconceived notions and questioning the boundaries of humanity. With each encounter, the enigmatic creature forces the villagers to confront their own desires, secrets, and vulnerabilities, ultimately leading them to question what it truly means to be human.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Magical Realism

Themes: Identity, Otherness, Human Nature
