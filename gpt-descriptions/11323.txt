Synopsis:
Caleb Williams; Or, Things as They Are is a gripping and suspenseful novel that follows the life of Caleb Williams, a young man who becomes entangled in a web of secrets and injustice. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding his employer, the enigmatic Falkland, Caleb finds himself caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse. With each revelation, Caleb's life is turned upside down, forcing him to confront the dark underbelly of society and question the nature of power and authority. Will Caleb be able to uncover the truth and escape the clutches of his relentless pursuer?

Genres:
Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller, Mystery

Themes:
Injustice, Power and authority, Social class, Individual freedom
