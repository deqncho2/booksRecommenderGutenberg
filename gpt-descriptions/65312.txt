Synopsis: In "The Divine Lady: A Romance of Nelson and Emma Hamilton," Lily Adams Beck weaves a captivating tale of love, passion, and political intrigue set against the backdrop of the Napoleonic Wars. The story follows the extraordinary life of Emma Hamilton, a beautiful and charismatic woman who captures the hearts of both the powerful and the ordinary. From her humble beginnings as a maid to her rise as the mistress of a British ambassador, Emma's journey takes her from the glittering courts of Europe to the treacherous seas where she encounters the legendary Admiral Horatio Nelson. As their forbidden love blossoms, Emma finds herself torn between her loyalty to her country and her devotion to the man who ignites her soul. With vivid historical detail and a cast of unforgettable characters, "The Divine Lady" is a sweeping romance that will transport readers to a time of passion, war, and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Biographical Fiction

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Ambition and Power, Loyalty and Betrayal
