Synopsis: 

'Fragments of Earth Lore: Sketches & Addresses Geological and Geographical' takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders of our planet. Geikie, James, a renowned geologist and geographer, presents a collection of sketches and addresses that delve into the fascinating world of Earth's geological and geographical features. From the majestic landscapes shaped by ancient forces to the intricate processes that have shaped our planet over millions of years, this book offers a captivating exploration of the Earth's lore. Through vivid descriptions and insightful observations, Geikie invites readers to marvel at the mysteries and complexities of our world, leaving them with a deeper appreciation for the forces that have shaped our existence.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Geology, Geography, Natural History, Earth's Formation
