Synopsis: In "The Mission of Janice Day" by Helen Beecher Long, readers are introduced to Janice Day, a young girl with a strong sense of adventure and a desire to make a difference in the world. When an unexpected opportunity arises, Janice embarks on a mission that takes her on a journey of self-discovery, friendship, and unexpected challenges. As she navigates through various obstacles, Janice learns valuable lessons about courage, determination, and the power of following one's dreams.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Courage, Determination, Pursuit of Dreams
