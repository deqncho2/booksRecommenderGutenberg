Synopsis: In "The Red-headed Man" by Fergus Hume, a mysterious stranger with fiery red hair arrives in a small English village, capturing the attention and curiosity of the locals. As his presence becomes increasingly unsettling, the villagers find themselves drawn into a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected twists. With each revelation, the true nature of the red-headed man becomes more enigmatic, leaving the reader eager to uncover the truth behind his arrival and the impact he will have on the village.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Small-town dynamics
