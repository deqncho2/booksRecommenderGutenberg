Synopsis: In 'The Weans at Rowallan' by Kathleen Fitzpatrick, a group of mischievous children embark on a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a hidden secret in the old mansion they are staying at. As they uncover clues and face unexpected challenges, their bond grows stronger and they learn valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the power of imagination. With twists and turns at every corner, this heartwarming tale will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Imagination
