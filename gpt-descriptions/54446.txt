Synopsis: In 'The Radio Boys Seek the Lost Atlantis' by Breckenridge, Gerald, a group of adventurous young radio enthusiasts embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the mysteries of the lost city of Atlantis. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter ancient artifacts, and decode cryptic messages, the Radio Boys find themselves caught in a race against time to unlock the secrets of this legendary civilization. With danger lurking at every turn, their quest for Atlantis becomes a test of courage, friendship, and the power of knowledge.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Ancient civilizations, Friendship, Quest for knowledge
