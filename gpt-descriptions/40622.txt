Synopsis: In 'Stories from Virgil' by Alfred John Church, embark on a captivating journey through the ancient world of Rome as the renowned poet Virgil weaves tales of heroism, love, and destiny. From the epic adventures of Aeneas to the enchanting romance of Dido, these stories bring to life the rich tapestry of characters and events from Virgil's timeless masterpiece, the Aeneid. Immerse yourself in the vivid landscapes, mythical creatures, and dramatic conflicts that shape the destiny of nations and individuals alike. With Church's masterful retelling, discover the enduring power and beauty of Virgil's epic poetry.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mythology, Classic Literature
Themes: Heroism, Fate, Love, Destiny
