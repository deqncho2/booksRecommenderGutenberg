Synopsis: In the remote jungles of Malaysia, a young man finds himself stranded and alone after a shipwreck. As he struggles to survive in this harsh and unfamiliar environment, he encounters a mysterious and captivating young woman who has been raised by a tribe of primitive cave dwellers. Drawn to her wild beauty and untamed spirit, he embarks on a journey of self-discovery and forbidden love, navigating the complexities of culture clash and the primal instincts that lie within us all.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Love, Cultural Differences, Human Nature
