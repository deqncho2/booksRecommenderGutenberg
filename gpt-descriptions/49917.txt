Synopsis: In 'St John Damascene on Holy Images', John of Damascus passionately defends the use of holy images against those who criticize and attack them. He presents a compelling argument, drawing from both theological and philosophical perspectives, to support the veneration of these sacred representations. Following this, 'Three Sermons on the Assumption' explores the belief in the bodily assumption of the Virgin Mary into heaven. John of Damascus delves into the significance of this event, offering profound insights and spiritual reflections.

Genres: Theological treatise, Apologetics, Sermons

Themes: Defense of holy images, Veneration of the Virgin Mary, Theological reflections on the Assumption
