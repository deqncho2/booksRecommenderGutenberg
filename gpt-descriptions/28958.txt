Synopsis: In 'The Road to Frontenac' by Samuel Merwin, a young man embarks on a journey that takes him through the rugged wilderness of Canada in search of his missing father. Along the way, he encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets and motivations. As he delves deeper into the unknown, he must confront his own fears and discover the truth about his family's past. This gripping tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family, Identity, Exploration
