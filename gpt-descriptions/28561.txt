Synopsis: In "Richard III: Makers of History" by Abbott, Jacob, embark on a captivating journey through the tumultuous life of one of England's most infamous kings. This gripping historical narrative delves into the complex character of Richard III, exploring his rise to power, his cunning strategies, and the dark secrets that shroud his reign. As the plot unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the web of political intrigue, betrayal, and ambition that surrounds Richard III, making for a truly riveting read.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Political Drama

Themes: Power and Ambition, Betrayal and Loyalty, Manipulation and Deception
