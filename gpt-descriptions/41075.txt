Synopsis: In 'The Curiosities and Law of Wills' by Proffatt, John, a renowned lawyer delves into the intriguing world of wills, uncovering a web of secrets, unexpected twists, and hidden motives. As he navigates through complex legal cases, he encounters eccentric characters, puzzling bequests, and shocking revelations that challenge his own beliefs. With each chapter, the reader is drawn deeper into the captivating realm of wills, where nothing is as it seems and the truth is often stranger than fiction.

Genres: Mystery, Legal Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Inheritance, Justice
