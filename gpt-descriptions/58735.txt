Synopsis: In 'Peace' by Arkawy and Henig, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, bringing with him an air of tranquility and harmony. As the townspeople become enamored with his presence, they soon discover that his peaceful demeanor hides a dark secret that threatens to unravel their lives. With suspense and intrigue, this gripping tale explores the lengths people will go to maintain peace, and the consequences that follow.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Deception, Power, Morality
