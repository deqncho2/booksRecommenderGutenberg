Synopsis: In 'Evolution Social and Organic', Arthur M. Lewis takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate web of human evolution, exploring the fascinating interplay between social and organic factors. From the origins of our species to the complex societies we have built, Lewis delves into the profound impact that both biological and cultural forces have had on shaping our present-day existence. Through a blend of scientific research, historical analysis, and thought-provoking insights, this book offers a fresh perspective on the ever-evolving nature of humanity.

Genres: Science, Anthropology, Sociology

Themes: Evolution, Social dynamics, Cultural influences, Human nature
