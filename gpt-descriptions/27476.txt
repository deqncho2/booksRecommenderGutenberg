Synopsis: In 'The Sign of the Spider' by Bertram Mitford, a young Englishman finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger as he ventures into the heart of Africa. Drawn by the allure of hidden treasure and the promise of adventure, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter enigmatic characters, and confront the sinister forces that lurk in the shadows. With each step closer to the truth, he becomes entwined in a deadly game where betrayal and survival go hand in hand. Will he unravel the secrets of the spider's sign before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Greed, Betrayal, Survival
