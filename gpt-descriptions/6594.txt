Synopsis: In "Schwatka's Search: Sledging in the Arctic in Quest of the Franklin Records," William H. Gilder takes us on a thrilling expedition to the Arctic in search of the lost records of Sir John Franklin's ill-fated expedition. Join Lieutenant Frederick Schwatka and his team as they navigate treacherous icy landscapes, encounter indigenous peoples, and face the harsh realities of survival in one of the most unforgiving environments on Earth. As they delve deeper into the mysteries of the Arctic, the quest for the Franklin records becomes a race against time, filled with danger, suspense, and unexpected discoveries.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Historical mysteries
