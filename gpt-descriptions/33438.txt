Synopsis: In 'Sea-Hounds' by Lewis R. Freeman, a thrilling maritime adventure unfolds as a young sailor finds himself entangled in a dangerous quest for hidden treasure. Set against the backdrop of the vast and treacherous ocean, the protagonist must navigate through perilous waters, encounter mysterious characters, and face unexpected challenges. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of courage and loyalty is put to the ultimate test. Will the young sailor emerge victorious, or will he be consumed by the depths of the sea?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Treasure hunting, Survival, Betrayal, Coming of age
