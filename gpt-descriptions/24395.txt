Synopsis: In "The Winds of Time" by James H. Schmitz, a young woman named Telzey Amberdon discovers a hidden power within herself that allows her to manipulate the minds of others. As she navigates a dangerous world filled with political intrigue and alien threats, Telzey must learn to harness her abilities and uncover the truth behind a mysterious conspiracy. With her quick wit and resourcefulness, Telzey becomes a formidable force to be reckoned with, but will she be able to outsmart her enemies and protect those she cares about?

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Psychic abilities, Power and control, Conspiracy
