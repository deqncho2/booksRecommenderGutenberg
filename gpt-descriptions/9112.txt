Synopsis: In 'The Dare Boys of 1776', a group of young patriots embark on a thrilling adventure during the American Revolution. Join the Dare Boys as they navigate dangerous missions, encounter unexpected allies, and face treacherous enemies in their quest for freedom. With their courage and determination, these daring boys become an integral part of the fight for independence, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Bravery, Friendship
