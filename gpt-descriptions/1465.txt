Synopsis: In 'The Wreck of the Golden Mary' by Charles Dickens, a diverse group of passengers find themselves stranded on a deserted island after their ship, the Golden Mary, is wrecked during a treacherous storm. As they struggle to survive and await rescue, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to unexpected alliances and heartbreaking betrayals. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this gripping tale explores the depths of human nature and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Survival, Morality, Betrayal
