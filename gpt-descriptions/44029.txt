Synopsis: In 'Mind and Body; or, Mental States and Physical Conditions', Atkinson delves into the fascinating relationship between the mind and the body, exploring the intricate connection between our mental states and physical well-being. Through thought-provoking insights and compelling anecdotes, he uncovers the profound impact our thoughts and emotions have on our overall health and vitality. As he navigates the realms of psychology, physiology, and metaphysics, Atkinson invites readers on a captivating journey of self-discovery and empowerment, revealing the extraordinary potential that lies within the harmonious integration of mind and body.

Genres: Self-help, Psychology, Philosophy

Themes: Mind-body connection, Mental and physical health, Self-empowerment
