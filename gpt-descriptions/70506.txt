Synopsis: In 'The Family at Misrule' by Ethel Turner, readers are introduced to the lively and mischievous Ralston family. Set in the charming Australian countryside, the story follows the adventures of the six Ralston siblings as they navigate the ups and downs of family life. From secret midnight feasts to daring escapades, the Ralstons bring chaos and laughter wherever they go. But when a mysterious stranger arrives in their small town, the family's world is turned upside down, and they must band together to uncover the truth. With its heartwarming humor and unexpected twists, 'The Family at Misrule' is a delightful tale that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Family, Adventure

Themes: Sibling bonds, Coming of age, Mystery
