Synopsis: 
In 'The Khaki Kook Book: A Collection of a Hundred Cheap and Practical Recipes; Mostly from Hindustan', Mary Kennedy Core takes readers on a culinary journey through the vibrant flavors of Hindustan. Filled with a hundred budget-friendly and easy-to-follow recipes, this book offers a delightful blend of traditional Indian dishes and Western influences. From aromatic curries to delectable desserts, Core's collection showcases the versatility and richness of Hindustani cuisine. With its practical approach and emphasis on affordability, 'The Khaki Kook Book' is a must-have for both seasoned cooks and beginners looking to explore the exotic tastes of Hindustan.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink, Cultural

Themes: Culinary exploration, Budget-friendly cooking, Cultural fusion
