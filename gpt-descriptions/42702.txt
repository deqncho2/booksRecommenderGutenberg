Synopsis: 
'Passing By' by Maurice Baring is a captivating novel that follows the life of a young man named Michael, who embarks on a journey of self-discovery and love. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century Europe, Michael encounters a series of intriguing characters and experiences that challenge his beliefs and shape his destiny. As he navigates through the complexities of relationships and societal expectations, Michael must confront his own desires and make difficult choices that will ultimately define his path. With its richly drawn characters and evocative prose, 'Passing By' is a compelling exploration of identity, passion, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Love, Self-discovery
