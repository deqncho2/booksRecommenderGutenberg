Synopsis: In 'The Boys' Nelson' by Wheeler, Harold, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious newcomer arrives, bringing with him a dark secret that threatens to unravel the fabric of their close-knit community. As the truth slowly unravels, the residents find themselves caught in a web of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the shocking truth that lies at the heart of this gripping tale.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Betrayal, Redemption
