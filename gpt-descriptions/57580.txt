Synopsis: 

Join author Thomas Dowler Murphy on a thrilling journey through the picturesque landscapes of California in "On Sunset Highways: A Book of Motor Rambles in California." As he embarks on a series of motor rambles, Murphy takes readers on a captivating exploration of the state's hidden gems, from its stunning coastlines to its majestic mountains and everything in between. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, this travelogue paints a vivid picture of the diverse beauty and rich history of California, offering a unique perspective on the wonders that lie just beyond the well-trodden path.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Natural beauty, History
