Synopsis: In "The Autobiography of a Slander," the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the life of a malicious rumor. From its inception to its devastating consequences, this captivating narrative explores the power of words and the destructive nature of gossip. As the slanderous tale spreads like wildfire, it weaves its way through the lives of various individuals, leaving a trail of chaos and heartbreak in its wake. With unexpected twists and turns, this thought-provoking story delves into the dark side of human nature and the consequences of unchecked rumors.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Power of words, Consequences of gossip, Human nature, Morality
