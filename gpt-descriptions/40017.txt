Synopsis: In 'The Stampeder' by Samuel Alexander White, a young and ambitious prospector named Jack embarks on a treacherous journey to strike it rich during the Klondike Gold Rush. As he faces the harsh realities of the unforgiving wilderness, Jack must navigate through treacherous landscapes, encounter dangerous characters, and confront his own inner demons. With each step closer to his dream, he becomes entangled in a web of greed, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. Will Jack's determination and resilience be enough to survive the brutal wilderness and achieve his ultimate goal?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Ambition, Survival, Betrayal, Redemption
