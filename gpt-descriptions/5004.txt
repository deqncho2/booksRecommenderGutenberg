Synopsis: In 'The Motor Boys on the Pacific; Or, the Young Derelict Hunters', the adventurous trio of the Motor Boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey across the vast Pacific Ocean. When they stumble upon a mysterious derelict ship, they become entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and intrigue. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter unexpected obstacles, the Motor Boys must rely on their wit, courage, and resourcefulness to unravel the enigma surrounding the derelict ship and its hidden treasures.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
