Synopsis: 
'Consequences' by E. M. Delafield is a gripping and thought-provoking novel that follows the life of a young woman named Alex Clare. Set in early 20th century England, the story takes us on a journey through Alex's tumultuous relationships, societal expectations, and the consequences of her choices. As she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and motherhood, Alex must confront the repercussions of her actions and the impact they have on her own happiness and the lives of those around her. With its compelling narrative and rich character development, 'Consequences' explores the complexities of personal responsibility and the enduring power of one's decisions.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and relationships, societal expectations, personal responsibility, consequences of choices
