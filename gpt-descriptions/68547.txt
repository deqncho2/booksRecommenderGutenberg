Synopsis:
In 'He' by H. P. Lovecraft, a young artist named Howard Phillips is drawn into a mysterious and haunting world when he receives a peculiar painting from an unknown sender. As he delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the artwork, Howard's perception of reality begins to unravel, leading him on a journey that blurs the line between dreams and nightmares. With each revelation, he becomes entangled in a web of cosmic horror and ancient secrets that threaten to consume his very existence.

Genres:
Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes:
Cosmic horror, Madness, Art and its power, Existential dread
