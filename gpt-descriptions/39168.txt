Synopsis: In 'Sophia: A Romance' by Stanley John Weyman, a young French nobleman finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love during the tumultuous times of the French Revolution. As he navigates through dangerous alliances and secret societies, he becomes captivated by the enigmatic Sophia, a woman with a mysterious past and a dangerous secret. Their forbidden romance takes them on a thrilling journey filled with suspense, betrayal, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Political Intrigue, Revolution and Rebellion
