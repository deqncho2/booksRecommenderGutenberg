Synopsis: In 'Nonsense Novels', Stephen Leacock takes readers on a hilarious and absurd journey through a collection of satirical short stories. From a man who becomes invisible to a town plagued by a mysterious epidemic of laughter, each tale is filled with witty wordplay, clever twists, and outrageous characters. Prepare to be entertained and delighted as Leacock's wit and imagination run wild in this whimsical and nonsensical literary adventure.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Fiction

Themes: Absurdity, Satire of Society, Wordplay
