Synopsis: On a perilous voyage across the Pacific Ocean, the Chancellor, a British ship, faces a series of unimaginable challenges that test the limits of human endurance. As the crew and passengers battle against the forces of nature, their own fears and desires threaten to tear them apart. With tension mounting and hope dwindling, the survivors must confront their darkest secrets and make impossible choices in order to stay alive. Will they find the strength to endure, or will they succumb to the unforgiving sea?

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Human resilience, Morality, Survival instincts
