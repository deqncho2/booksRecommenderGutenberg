Synopsis: 
In 'Old Man Curry: Race Track Stories', Charles E. Van Loan takes readers on a thrilling journey through the world of horse racing. Set against the backdrop of the race track, this collection of stories introduces us to the enigmatic character of Old Man Curry, a seasoned gambler with a knack for picking winners. From the heart-pounding excitement of the racetrack to the behind-the-scenes drama, each story offers a unique glimpse into the high-stakes world of horse racing. With colorful characters, unexpected twists, and a touch of humor, 'Old Man Curry: Race Track Stories' is a captivating read that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Fiction, Sports, Short Stories

Themes: Gambling, Luck, Determination, Human nature
