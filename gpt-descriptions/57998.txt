Synopsis: 

In 'On Sameness and Identity: A Psychological Study: Being a Contribution to the Foundations of a Theory of Knowledge', George Stuart Fullerton delves into the intricate workings of the human mind, exploring the fascinating concepts of sameness and identity. Through a thought-provoking analysis, Fullerton challenges conventional notions and offers a fresh perspective on how we perceive ourselves and others. As he navigates the complex terrain of psychology, the reader is taken on a captivating journey of self-discovery and introspection, uncovering the hidden depths of our understanding of identity.

Genres: Psychology, Philosophy

Themes: Identity, Perception, Self-discovery
