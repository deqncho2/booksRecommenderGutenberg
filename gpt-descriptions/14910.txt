Synopsis: Elsie at the World's Fair follows the adventures of Elsie Dinsmore as she travels to the grand World's Fair in Chicago. Amidst the bustling crowds and dazzling exhibits, Elsie encounters unexpected challenges and forms new friendships. As she navigates through the wonders of the fair, Elsie's faith and character are tested, leading her to discover the true meaning of courage and resilience.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Christian Fiction

Themes: Faith, Friendship, Resilience, Adventure
