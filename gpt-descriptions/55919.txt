Synopsis:
In "William Caxton" by E. Gordon Duff, embark on a captivating journey through the life of the man who brought the printing press to England. This biography delves into the fascinating world of William Caxton, a merchant turned printer, as he navigates the political and cultural landscape of 15th-century Europe. From his humble beginnings to his encounters with influential figures, witness the challenges and triumphs that shaped Caxton's pioneering efforts in revolutionizing the dissemination of knowledge.

Genres:
Biography, History

Themes:
Innovation, Cultural Transformation
