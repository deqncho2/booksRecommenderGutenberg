Synopsis: In "Dorothy Dale to the Rescue" by Margaret Penrose, the spirited and resourceful Dorothy finds herself caught up in a thrilling adventure when she stumbles upon a mysterious message. Determined to uncover the truth, she embarks on a daring mission that takes her to unexpected places and introduces her to a cast of intriguing characters. As Dorothy races against time to solve the puzzle, she must rely on her wit, courage, and loyal friends to unravel the secrets that lie beneath the surface. This captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead for Dorothy Dale.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
