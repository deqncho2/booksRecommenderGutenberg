Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of Lost River Canyon' by Castlemon, Harry, a group of adventurous friends stumble upon a hidden canyon that holds a dark secret. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they uncover clues that lead them on a thrilling journey filled with danger, suspense, and unexpected twists. With each step closer to the truth, they must confront their fears and rely on their wits to unravel the enigma of Lost River Canyon.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Courage, Secrets
