Synopsis: 
'Christmas in Legend and Story: A Book for Boys and Girls' by Elva Sophronia Smith is a captivating collection of Christmas tales that will transport readers into the enchanting world of holiday legends and traditions. From ancient folklore to heartwarming stories, this book explores the rich history and magical allure of Christmas through a variety of captivating narratives. Delve into the origins of beloved customs, encounter mythical characters, and experience the joy and wonder of the holiday season through the eyes of children and adults alike. This delightful compilation is sure to ignite the spirit of Christmas in readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Children's Literature

Themes: 
Christmas traditions, Legends and folklore, Joy and wonder of the holiday season
