Synopsis: 
'A History of the Comstock Silver Lode & Mines: Nevada and the Great Basin Region; Lake Tahoe and the High Sierras' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating history of the Comstock Silver Lode and the mines that shaped Nevada and the Great Basin Region. From the discovery of the rich silver deposits to the boom and bust cycles that followed, this book delves into the stories of the miners, entrepreneurs, and adventurers who sought their fortunes in the rugged landscapes of the High Sierras and the shimmering waters of Lake Tahoe. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, De Quille brings to life the triumphs and tragedies of this legendary era, revealing the untold tales of the men and women who shaped the destiny of the American West.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Mining, Exploration, American West, Economic Boom and Bust, Human Resilience
