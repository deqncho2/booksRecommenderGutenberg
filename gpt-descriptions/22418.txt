Synopsis: In 'Dollars and Sense' by William Crosbie Hunter, a young and ambitious stockbroker finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal as he navigates the cutthroat world of Wall Street. With his career on the line and his personal life in shambles, he must uncover the truth behind a mysterious financial scandal before it consumes him completely. As the stakes rise and the danger intensifies, he must confront his own moral compass and make choices that will determine his fate.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Morality, Financial Scandals
