Synopsis: In "The Plastic Age" by Percy Marks, we follow the journey of Hugh Carver, a young college student who finds himself caught between the allure of campus life and the challenges of adulthood. As he navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and ambition, Hugh is faced with difficult choices that will shape his future. Set against the backdrop of the roaring 1920s, this coming-of-age novel explores the exhilaration and pitfalls of youth, as well as the timeless quest for self-discovery.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Romance, Drama

Themes: Youthful idealism, Love and relationships, Self-discovery
