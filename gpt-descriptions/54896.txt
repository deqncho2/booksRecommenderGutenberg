Synopsis: 
In 'My Adventure in the Flying Scotsman; A Romance of London and North-Western Railway Shares', the protagonist finds himself embroiled in a thrilling and unexpected journey aboard the iconic Flying Scotsman train. As he navigates the intricate world of railway shares and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and romance that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Love and relationships, Intrigue and suspense, Exploration of the railway industry
