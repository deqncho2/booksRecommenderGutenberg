Synopsis: 

In 'A Popular History of the United States of America, Vol. 1', Mary Howitt takes readers on an enthralling journey through the captivating story of America, from its discovery to the present day. With vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, Howitt delves into the triumphs and struggles that have shaped the nation, revealing the remarkable individuals and events that have left an indelible mark on American history. From the early explorers and settlers to the Revolutionary War and the birth of a new nation, this volume offers a comprehensive and captivating account of America's past.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: American history, Exploration, Revolution, Nation-building
