Synopsis: In "A Hitch in Time" by Frederik Pohl, a brilliant scientist accidentally invents a time machine, but soon realizes that altering the past can have disastrous consequences. As he navigates the complexities of time travel, he becomes entangled in a web of paradoxes and must race against time to fix the damage he has caused. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this thrilling science fiction novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Thriller

Themes: Consequences of Time Travel, Paradoxes, Ethical Dilemmas
