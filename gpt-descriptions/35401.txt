Synopsis: 
In 'Friend Island' by Stevens, Francis, a group of strangers find themselves stranded on a mysterious island after a boating accident. As they struggle to survive and find a way back home, they soon discover that the island is not what it seems. With each passing day, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and friendships are tested. As they uncover the island's dark secrets, they must confront their own pasts and make difficult choices that will determine their fate. 'Friend Island' is a gripping and suspenseful tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Betrayal, Redemption
