Synopsis: 
'The Little Demon' by Fyodor Sologub is a haunting and psychologically intense novel that delves into the dark recesses of the human mind. Set in a small Russian town, the story follows the life of a seemingly innocent young girl named Alena, who possesses a mysterious and unsettling power. As Alena's true nature gradually unfolds, the town becomes entranced by her enigmatic presence, leading to a series of chilling and unpredictable events. With its eerie atmosphere and complex characters, 'The Little Demon' explores the depths of human desires and the destructive forces that can lurk within us all.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Gothic fiction, Supernatural fiction

Themes: Identity, Power, Morality, Human nature
