Synopsis: In "Belshazzar: A Tale of the Fall of Babylon," William Stearns Davis takes readers on a captivating journey to ancient Babylon, where the mighty empire is on the brink of collapse. Amidst political intrigue, forbidden love, and impending doom, the fate of Babylon rests in the hands of a courageous young prince. As the city's walls crumble and its people face their darkest hour, the prince must navigate treacherous alliances and make unimaginable sacrifices to save his beloved homeland. This gripping tale of power, love, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Power and Corruption, Loyalty and Betrayal, Love and Sacrifice
