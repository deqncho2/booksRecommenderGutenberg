Synopsis: In 'The Black Buccaneer' by Stephen W. Meader, a young boy named Tom finds himself transported back in time to the 18th century, where he becomes embroiled in the thrilling world of pirates and adventure. As Tom navigates the treacherous waters of the Caribbean, he must outwit dangerous foes, uncover hidden secrets, and ultimately discover his own courage and resilience. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this captivating tale will transport readers to a world of swashbuckling excitement and daring escapades.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Identity, Time Travel
