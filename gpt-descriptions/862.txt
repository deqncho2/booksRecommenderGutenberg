Synopsis: In 'Philosophy 4: A Story of Harvard University' by Owen Wister, a young and ambitious student named John embarks on a journey of self-discovery and intellectual exploration at the prestigious Harvard University. As he delves into the world of philosophy, he encounters a captivating professor who challenges his beliefs and pushes him to question the very essence of existence. Alongside his fellow classmates, John navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and the pursuit of knowledge, all while grappling with the profound ideas that shape his understanding of the world. This coming-of-age tale takes readers on a thought-provoking and transformative journey through the hallowed halls of Harvard.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Fiction, Philosophical

Themes: Self-discovery, Intellectual exploration, Love, Friendship, Pursuit of knowledge
