Synopsis: In 'The Picture Alphabet' by Oliver Spafford, embark on a whimsical journey through the alphabet as each letter comes to life through vibrant illustrations and clever rhymes. From adventurous animals to zany objects, this delightful picture book will captivate young readers and ignite their imagination. As they explore each page, children will discover a world of wonder and learn the alphabet in a fun and engaging way.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book

Themes: Learning, Imagination, Creativity
