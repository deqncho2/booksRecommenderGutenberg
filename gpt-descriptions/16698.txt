Synopsis: In 'The King's Arrow: A Tale of the United Empire Loyalists', follow the gripping journey of young Jonathan as he navigates the treacherous world of espionage and loyalty during the American Revolution. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, Jonathan finds himself torn between his duty to the crown and his desire for freedom. With danger lurking at every turn, will he be able to make the right choices and protect those he holds dear?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Espionage

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Freedom, Sacrifice
