Synopsis: In the fourth volume of his memoirs, Charles-Lewis, Baron de Pollnitz takes us on a captivating journey through various European countries, sharing his observations and experiences in a series of letters to his friend. From Prussia to Germany, Italy, France, Flanders, Holland, England, and more, Pollnitz provides a vivid account of the present state of the chief cities and towns he encounters, while also delving into the intriguing characters he encounters at the different courts. With his keen eye for detail and sharp wit, Pollnitz offers a unique perspective on the social and political landscapes of Europe during his travels.

Genres: Memoir, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Court Life, Social Observation, Cultural Differences
