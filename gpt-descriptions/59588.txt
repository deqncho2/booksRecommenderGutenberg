Synopsis: In 'The Happy Herd' by Walton Bryce, a group of misfit animals embark on a heartwarming adventure to save their beloved home, a struggling animal sanctuary. Led by a determined donkey named Daisy, the eclectic group must overcome their differences and work together to outsmart a conniving developer who wants to tear down their sanctuary. Along the way, they discover the true meaning of friendship, courage, and the power of unity. Will the Happy Herd be able to save their home and inspire others to stand up for what they believe in?

Genres: Children's Fiction, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Unity, Conservation
