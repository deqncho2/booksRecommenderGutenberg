Synopsis: In 'The Mahabharata of Krishna-Dwaipayana Vyasa Translated into English Prose: Sabha Parva', the epic tale of the Mahabharata continues with the Sabha Parva, which delves into the political intrigues and power struggles of the Kuru dynasty. As the story unfolds, the reader is drawn into a world of elaborate court politics, cunning strategies, and complex relationships. Amidst the grandeur of the royal court, the characters navigate through alliances, rivalries, and personal ambitions, setting the stage for the epic battle that lies ahead. 

Genres: Epic, Mythology, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Family and Duty
