Synopsis: In 'How Janice Day Won' by Helen Beecher Long, follow the captivating journey of Janice Day, a young girl with a determined spirit and a heart full of dreams. As she navigates the challenges of life, Janice discovers unexpected friendships, faces daunting obstacles, and learns the power of perseverance. With each step she takes, Janice's courage and resilience shine through, leading her on a path towards triumph and self-discovery.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Fiction, Inspirational

Themes: Determination, Friendship, Self-discovery
