Synopsis: 
In "Tom Brown at Oxford," the sequel to the beloved classic "Tom Brown's School Days," we follow the adventures of Tom Brown as he navigates the prestigious university. Set in the vibrant and competitive world of Oxford, Tom finds himself entangled in a web of friendships, rivalries, and romantic entanglements. As he strives to excel academically, he also becomes embroiled in the politics and traditions of the university, facing challenges that test his character and resilience. With its richly drawn characters and captivating narrative, "Tom Brown at Oxford" offers a compelling exploration of the complexities of university life and the pursuit of personal growth.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: 
Friendship, Rivalry, Academic Excellence, Tradition, Personal Growth
