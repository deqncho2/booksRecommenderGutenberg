Synopsis: In 'Urania' by Camille Flammarion, a renowned astronomer, a mysterious celestial phenomenon captures the attention of the scientific community and the world at large. As the enigma unfolds, a young woman named Urania emerges, possessing an extraordinary connection to the cosmos. Through her eyes, we embark on a mesmerizing journey that intertwines science, spirituality, and the profound mysteries of the universe. With each revelation, the boundaries of human understanding are pushed to their limits, leaving us questioning our place in the vastness of space and time.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Astronomy, Existentialism, Human connection with the universe
