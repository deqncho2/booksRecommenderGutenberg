Synopsis: In 'Rough and Ready; Or, Life Among the New York Newsboys', follow the gripping tale of a young newsboy named Frank Fowler as he navigates the bustling streets of New York City in the late 19th century. From his encounters with rival newsboys to his unexpected friendship with a wealthy benefactor, Frank's journey is filled with adventure, challenges, and the pursuit of a better life. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way and find his place in the world?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Social class and inequality, Friendship, Perseverance, Self-discovery
