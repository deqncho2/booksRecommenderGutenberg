Synopsis: 
'By Forest Ways in New Zealand' takes readers on a captivating journey through the breathtaking landscapes of New Zealand. Filled with adventure, mystery, and unexpected encounters, this travelogue follows the author as he explores the country's hidden gems, encounters fascinating wildlife, and delves into the rich cultural heritage of the Maori people. From traversing dense forests to navigating treacherous mountain passes, the author's vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling transport readers to the heart of New Zealand's natural wonders. As the journey unfolds, unexpected twists and turns keep readers on the edge of their seats, making this a truly unforgettable exploration of a land like no other.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Nature exploration, Cultural immersion, Wildlife encounters
