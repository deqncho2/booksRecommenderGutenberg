Synopsis: 
In the quaint English countryside, a wealthy widow, Emily Inglethorp, is found dead under mysterious circumstances. As suspicions arise and secrets unravel, the brilliant detective Hercule Poirot is called upon to solve the perplexing case. With a diverse cast of characters, each harboring their own motives and secrets, Poirot must navigate through a web of lies and deceit to uncover the truth behind this puzzling murder.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice, Redemption
