Synopsis: In 'The Minister's Wife' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a small town is thrown into turmoil when a new minister and his mysterious wife arrive. As the townspeople become increasingly intrigued by the enigmatic woman, secrets and scandals begin to unravel, threatening to shatter the community's fragile peace. With a web of deceit and hidden desires, this gripping tale explores the consequences of curiosity and the power of perception.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Perception and judgment
