Synopsis: In 'Out of the Ashes' by Ethel Watts Mumford Grant, a young woman named Helen finds herself caught in the aftermath of a devastating fire that destroys her family's home and leaves her with nothing. Determined to rebuild her life, Helen embarks on a journey of self-discovery, resilience, and unexpected love. As she navigates the challenges of starting over, Helen learns the power of hope, the importance of community, and the strength that can arise from the ashes of tragedy.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Resilience, Rebuilding, Love, Community
