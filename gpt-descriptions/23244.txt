Synopsis: 
In the heart of the Wild West, a spirited young woman named Nellie Grayson finds herself unexpectedly employed as a "dude wrangler" at a remote ranch. With a group of eccentric guests arriving, Nellie must navigate the challenges of taming both the unruly horses and the even more unpredictable humans. As she delves deeper into the world of cowboys and outlaws, Nellie discovers a hidden strength within herself and a love that defies all expectations.

Genres: Western, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Self-discovery, Love and relationships, Challenging societal norms
