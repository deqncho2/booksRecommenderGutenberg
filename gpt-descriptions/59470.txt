Synopsis: In 'Laboratory' by Jerome Bixby, a brilliant scientist embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that pushes the boundaries of human knowledge and morality. As he delves deeper into his research, he discovers shocking and unforeseen consequences that challenge his own understanding of the world. This gripping tale explores the fine line between scientific progress and ethical dilemmas, leaving readers questioning the limits of human ambition and the potential dangers that lie within the realm of scientific discovery.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Morality, Scientific Ethics, Human Ambition
