Synopsis: In 'Death and Taxes' by Hartzell, H. A., a brilliant and enigmatic tax auditor named Max finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he uncovers a mysterious conspiracy within the government. As he delves deeper into the secrets hidden behind the numbers, Max must navigate a treacherous landscape of corruption, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With his life on the line, Max must use his intellect and cunning to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Government corruption, Conspiracy, Betrayal
