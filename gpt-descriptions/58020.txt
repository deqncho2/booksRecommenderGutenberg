Synopsis: 
'The Trial and Conviction of John Church: The Preacher of the Surrey Tabernacle' is a gripping true crime story set in 19th century England. The book follows the shocking trial of John Church, a respected preacher, who stands accused of an assault with intent to commit an unnatural crime. As the trial unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the truth becomes increasingly elusive. With its scandalous subject matter and courtroom drama, this captivating narrative will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: True Crime, Historical Fiction, Legal Drama

Themes: Morality, Hypocrisy, Justice, Betrayal
