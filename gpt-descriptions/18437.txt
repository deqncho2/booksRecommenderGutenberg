Synopsis: In 'Troublesome Comforts: A Story for Children', author G.R. Glasgow weaves a captivating tale that follows the adventures of a young girl named Emily. When Emily's family moves to a new town, she finds herself feeling out of place and longing for the familiar comforts of her old home. However, as she embarks on a journey filled with unexpected friendships, magical encounters, and valuable life lessons, Emily discovers that sometimes, the greatest comforts can be found in the most unexpected places.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Adaptation, Finding Comfort in Change
