Synopsis: 
In "Diary from March 4, 1861, to November 12, 1862," Adam Gurowski offers a captivating firsthand account of a tumultuous period in American history. Through his diary entries, Gurowski provides a unique perspective on the events leading up to and during the American Civil War. From political intrigue and personal reflections to vivid descriptions of battles and societal upheaval, this diary offers an intimate glimpse into a nation on the brink of transformation.

Genres: Historical, Memoir, Political

Themes: American Civil War, Politics, Personal Reflection, Societal Transformation
