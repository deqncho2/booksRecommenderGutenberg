Synopsis: In 'The Barrier: A Novel' by French Allen, a mysterious and impenetrable force field suddenly appears, dividing a small town into two distinct halves. As the residents grapple with the physical and psychological effects of this inexplicable barrier, tensions rise and secrets are unearthed. Amidst the chaos, a group of unlikely allies emerges, determined to uncover the truth behind the barrier and find a way to reunite their fractured community. With its gripping narrative and thought-provoking exploration of human nature, 'The Barrier' is a captivating tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Community, Power dynamics, Survival
