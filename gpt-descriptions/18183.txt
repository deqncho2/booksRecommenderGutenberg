Synopsis: 

'Trees, Fruits and Flowers of Minnesota, 1916' takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of horticulture in Minnesota during the year 1916. This volume encompasses the fascinating transactions of the Minnesota State Horticultural Society, offering a comprehensive look at the advancements, challenges, and discoveries in the field. From December 1, 1915, to December 1, 1916, readers will delve into the twelve issues of "The Minnesota Horticulturist" for 1916, uncovering a wealth of knowledge about trees, fruits, and flowers specific to the region. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Horticulture, History

Themes: Horticultural advancements, Challenges in horticulture, Regional flora and fauna
