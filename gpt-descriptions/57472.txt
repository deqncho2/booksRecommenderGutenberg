Synopsis: 
In "Fanny Burney and Her Friends: Select Passages from Her Diary and Other Writings," we delve into the captivating world of Fanny Burney, a talented and witty writer of the 18th century. Through her personal diary entries and other writings, we are transported to the vibrant social circles of Georgian England, where Burney encounters a colorful cast of characters, navigates the complexities of love and friendship, and grapples with the challenges of being a female writer in a male-dominated society. With her sharp observations and engaging storytelling, Burney offers a fascinating glimpse into the joys, sorrows, and triumphs of her life and those around her.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Historical Fiction

Themes: Gender roles, Friendship, Love, Society and class, Writing and creativity
