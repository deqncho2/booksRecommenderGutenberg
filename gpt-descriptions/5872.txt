Synopsis: In "Cashel Byron's Profession" by Bernard Shaw, we are introduced to Cashel Byron, a young and talented boxer who rises to fame in the world of prizefighting. However, his life takes an unexpected turn when he meets a beautiful and intelligent woman named Lydia Carew. As their paths intertwine, Cashel is faced with a moral dilemma that challenges his beliefs and forces him to question the societal norms of his time. This thought-provoking and captivating novel explores the complexities of love, class, and the pursuit of personal fulfillment.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Love and Relationships, Class and Society, Morality and Ethics
