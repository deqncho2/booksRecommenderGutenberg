Synopsis: In Connection with the De Willoughby Claim is a captivating novel that follows the lives of two families, the De Willoughbys and the Carews, as they become entangled in a mysterious legal dispute. Set in the late 19th century, the story unfolds with secrets, hidden identities, and unexpected alliances, all leading to a thrilling courtroom showdown. As the truth gradually unravels, the characters must confront their pasts and make difficult choices that will forever alter their lives.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Legal Drama

Themes: Identity, Family, Justice, Secrets
