Synopsis: 
'Visions and Beliefs in the West of Ireland, Second Series' takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical and enchanting landscapes of the West of Ireland. Drawing upon the rich folklore and oral traditions of the region, this collection of stories and accounts explores the deep-rooted beliefs, supernatural encounters, and extraordinary visions that have shaped the lives of the local inhabitants. From encounters with fairies and spirits to tales of prophetic dreams and miraculous healings, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a world where the boundaries between the seen and unseen are blurred. With each page, readers will be transported to a realm where ancient traditions and mystical experiences intertwine, leaving them spellbound and eager to uncover the secrets hidden within the West of Ireland.

Genres: Folklore, Supernatural, Non-fiction

Themes: Belief systems, Oral traditions, Supernatural encounters, Cultural heritage
