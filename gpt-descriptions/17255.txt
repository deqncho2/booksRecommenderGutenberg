Synopsis: In 'The Wings of Icarus: Being the Life of one Emilia Fletcher', we are transported to the vibrant world of 19th-century London, where we follow the extraordinary journey of Emilia Fletcher. Born into a life of privilege, Emilia's path takes an unexpected turn when she becomes captivated by the world of art and the bohemian lifestyle. As she navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations, Emilia's determination to spread her wings and soar to new heights becomes both her greatest strength and her greatest vulnerability. Set against a backdrop of artistic revolution and societal constraints, this captivating novel explores the power of passion, the pursuit of dreams, and the sacrifices one must make to find true fulfillment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Artistic expression, societal expectations, love and sacrifice
