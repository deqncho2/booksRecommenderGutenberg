Synopsis: In "The Scarecrow of Oz," join Dorothy and her friends as they embark on a thrilling adventure to rescue a beloved character from the clutches of an evil sorceress. Along the way, they encounter enchanting creatures, magical lands, and face unexpected challenges that test their courage and loyalty. Will they succeed in their mission and bring happiness back to the Land of Oz?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Good vs. Evil
