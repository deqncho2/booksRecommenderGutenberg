Synopsis: In 'The Weird Orient: Nine Mystic Tales' by Iliowizi, Henry, embark on a mesmerizing journey through the enigmatic and mystical landscapes of the Orient. From ancient temples to hidden realms, this collection of nine tales will transport readers to a world where reality intertwines with the supernatural. Encounter vengeful spirits, cunning sorcerers, and forbidden love as the characters navigate the intricate web of Eastern folklore and tradition. Each story unveils a new layer of mystery, leaving readers captivated and eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the exotic and enchanting Orient.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Eastern folklore, Tradition, Supernatural encounters
