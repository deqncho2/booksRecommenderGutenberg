Synopsis: 

'A Laodicean: A Story of To-day' by Thomas Hardy is a captivating novel that follows the life of Paula Power, a young woman who inherits a grand estate from her late father. As Paula navigates the complexities of her newfound wealth and status, she becomes entangled in a love triangle that challenges her beliefs and desires. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this novel explores the clash between tradition and modernity, as well as the consequences of indecisiveness and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Love and desire, Class and social status, Tradition versus modernity
