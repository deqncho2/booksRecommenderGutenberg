Synopsis: In the rugged and lawless American West, a saloon owner named Minnie finds herself caught in a dangerous love triangle between a bandit and a lawman. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, Minnie must navigate the treacherous landscape of desire, loyalty, and survival. Will she be able to protect her heart and make the right choices in a world where trust is scarce and danger lurks at every turn?

Genres: Drama, Western, Romance

Themes: Love and sacrifice, morality and redemption, the clash between law and lawlessness
