Synopsis: In 'Frank Reynolds, R.I.', a renowned artist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a hidden secret within his own artwork. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes embroiled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred. With his reputation and sanity on the line, Frank must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it consumes him entirely.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Artistic obsession, Identity, Perception vs. reality
