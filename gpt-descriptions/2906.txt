Synopsis: 
In 'The Silver Box: A Comedy in Three Acts', John Galsworthy weaves a captivating tale that explores the stark contrast between social classes and the consequences of a single impulsive act. The story revolves around a stolen silver cigarette box and the lives it intertwines, from the wealthy elite to the struggling working class. As the characters navigate their way through scandal, prejudice, and moral dilemmas, the audience is taken on a rollercoaster of emotions, questioning the true nature of justice and the power dynamics at play in society.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes: Social class, Morality, Justice, Prejudice
