Synopsis: 

In "First Offensive: The Marine Campaign for Guadalcanal," Henry I. Shaw takes readers on a gripping journey through one of the most significant battles of World War II. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Shaw delves into the intense and harrowing experiences of the United States Marine Corps as they launch their first major offensive against the Japanese forces on the island of Guadalcanal. From the initial landing to the grueling jungle warfare, the book provides a riveting account of the Marines' relentless determination, sacrifice, and bravery in the face of overwhelming odds.

Genres: Military history, War narrative, Historical non-fiction

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Resilience, Camaraderie, Strategic warfare
