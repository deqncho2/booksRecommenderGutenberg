Synopsis: 
"Rubáiyát of Omar Khayyam, Rendered into English Verse" is a collection of poetic quatrains that delve into the timeless themes of love, mortality, and the pursuit of meaning in life. Through the mesmerizing verses of Omar Khayyam, readers are transported to a world of philosophical contemplation, where the beauty of nature intertwines with the complexities of human existence. This enchanting work invites readers to reflect on the fleeting nature of time and the importance of embracing the present moment.

Genres: Poetry, Philosophy, Classic Literature

Themes: Love, Mortality, Existentialism, Nature, Carpe Diem
