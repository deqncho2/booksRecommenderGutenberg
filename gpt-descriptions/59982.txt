Synopsis: In 'The Human Element' by Kelley, Leo P., a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the course of humanity. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but also the fate of the entire world. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal to protect his discovery and ensure the survival of the human race.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Survival
