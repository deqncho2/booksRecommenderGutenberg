Synopsis: In 'Patience Wins: War in the Works' by George Manville Fenn, a young and determined protagonist finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous conspiracy. As he unravels the secrets surrounding a mysterious war project, he must navigate treacherous alliances and face unexpected challenges. With his wit and resourcefulness, he strives to outsmart his adversaries and bring justice to those who deserve it. This thrilling tale of espionage and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Espionage, Determination, Justice
