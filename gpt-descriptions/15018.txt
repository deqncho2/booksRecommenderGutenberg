Synopsis: 

In 'Elements of Civil Government: A Text-Book for Use in Public Schools, High Schools and Normal Schools and a Manual of Reference for Teachers', Alexander L. Peterman presents a comprehensive guide to understanding the intricate workings of civil government. This engaging textbook explores the fundamental principles and structures of government, shedding light on the rights and responsibilities of citizens. Through vivid examples and thought-provoking discussions, Peterman delves into the various branches of government, the electoral process, and the importance of civic participation. With its accessible language and practical approach, this book serves as an invaluable resource for students and teachers alike, fostering a deeper understanding of the foundations of civil government.

Genres: Educational, Reference, Non-fiction

Themes: Government, Citizenship, Civic Education
