Synopsis: In 'To Nuremberg and Back: A Girl's Holiday' by Neally, Amy, a young woman embarks on a spontaneous trip to Nuremberg, Germany, seeking adventure and self-discovery. As she immerses herself in the vibrant culture and explores the city's hidden gems, she encounters unexpected friendships, romantic encounters, and thought-provoking encounters with history. Through her journey, she learns valuable lessons about love, resilience, and the power of embracing the unknown.

Genres: Travel, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Adventure, Self-discovery, Cultural immersion, Friendship, Love
