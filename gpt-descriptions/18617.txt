Synopsis: 
In 'On The Blockade' by Optic, Oliver, a young sailor named Paul Duncan finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous blockade during the American Civil War. As he navigates treacherous waters and encounters unexpected challenges, Paul must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to survive. Along the way, he forms unlikely alliances and discovers the true meaning of loyalty and sacrifice. Will Paul's determination be enough to overcome the perils of war and make it back home?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival
