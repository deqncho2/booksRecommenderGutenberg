Synopsis: 
In 'The Cable Game: The Adventures of an American Press-Boat in Turkish Waters During the Russian Revolution', Washburn, Stanley takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous waters of the Russian Revolution. Set against the backdrop of the conflict, the story follows the daring exploits of an American press-boat as it navigates the treacherous Turkish waters. As the crew faces danger at every turn, they must rely on their wit, resourcefulness, and courage to survive. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale offers a unique perspective on a pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Courage, Journalism, War
