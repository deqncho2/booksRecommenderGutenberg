Synopsis: 
In "Medium Boiled" by Burtis Thomson, a down-on-his-luck private investigator finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he takes on a seemingly routine missing persons case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a dark underworld filled with eccentric characters, hidden secrets, and unexpected twists. With his wit and resourcefulness, the detective must navigate through a maze of clues and confront his own demons to solve the case and save himself.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Noir

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Identity
