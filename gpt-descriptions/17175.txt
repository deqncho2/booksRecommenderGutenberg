Synopsis: In 'The Tapestry Room: A Child's Romance' by Molesworth, Mrs., a young girl named Rosamund discovers a hidden room in her family's ancestral home. As she explores the room and its mysterious tapestries, she becomes entangled in a captivating world of imagination and adventure. With the help of newfound friends, Rosamund embarks on a journey that will test her courage and reveal secrets about her family's past. This enchanting tale will transport readers to a world where dreams and reality intertwine, leaving them eager to uncover the secrets of the tapestry room.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Imagination, Adventure, Family Secrets
