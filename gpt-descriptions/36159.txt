Synopsis: In 'The Letter of Credit' by Susan Warner, a young woman named Grace finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when she receives an unexpected letter that holds the key to her family's past. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the letter, Grace embarks on a journey that will test her courage, challenge her beliefs, and ultimately lead her to uncover a truth she never could have imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Redemption
