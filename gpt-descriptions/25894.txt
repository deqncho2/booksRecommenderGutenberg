Synopsis: In "The Spirit Proper to the Times: A Sermon preached in King's Chapel, Boston, Sunday, May 12, 1861," James Walker delivers a powerful sermon that captivates the congregation with its timely message. As tensions rise during the American Civil War, Walker explores the essence of the spirit needed to navigate these challenging times. With thought-provoking insights and a call to action, this sermon offers a profound reflection on the role of individuals in shaping the course of history.

Genres: Sermon, Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: Patriotism, Morality, Social Responsibility
