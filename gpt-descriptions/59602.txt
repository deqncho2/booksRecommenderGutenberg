Synopsis: In 'The Chasm' by Walton, Bryce, a young woman named Emily finds herself trapped in a mysterious and dangerous world after falling into a hidden chasm. As she navigates through this treacherous realm, she encounters strange creatures, uncovers ancient secrets, and discovers her own hidden powers. With time running out, Emily must unravel the mysteries of the chasm and find a way back home before it's too late.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Overcoming adversity
