Synopsis: In "Opening the West With Lewis and Clark," author Edwin L. Sabin takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the uncharted territories of the New United States. Join young Captain Lewis, known as the Long Knife, and his friend Captain Clark, the Red Head Chief, as they lead their brave band of men on a daring expedition. With the aid of Sacajawea, the Birdwoman, they navigate the treacherous Great River Missouri, traverse the formidable Stony Mountains, and ultimately reach the vast Pacific. This gripping tale of exploration and adventure will captivate readers as they witness the trials and triumphs of these courageous pioneers.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Native American culture, Perseverance
