Synopsis: In "Rupert of Hentzau: From The Memoirs of Fritz Von Tarlenheim," the thrilling sequel to "The Prisoner of Zenda," the reader is transported back to the kingdom of Ruritania. Set against a backdrop of political intrigue, forbidden love, and daring escapades, the story follows the charismatic and enigmatic Rupert of Hentzau. As he becomes entangled in a web of deception and power struggles, Rupert's actions have far-reaching consequences that threaten to unravel the very fabric of the kingdom. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Honor, Love, Power
