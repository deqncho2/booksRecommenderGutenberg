Synopsis: In 'Pythias' by Frederik Pohl, a brilliant scientist named Pythias is on the verge of a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. However, his work attracts the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess his invention. As Pythias finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, he must navigate a treacherous world where trust is scarce and betrayal lurks around every corner. With the fate of his invention and possibly the world hanging in the balance, Pythias must make difficult choices that will test his loyalty, courage, and the limits of his own humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Scientific discovery, Power and control, Betrayal, Morality, Ethics
