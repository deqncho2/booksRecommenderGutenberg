Synopsis: In 'Round the World in Seven Days' by Herbert Strang, embark on a thrilling adventure as a group of intrepid explorers set out on a daring mission to circumnavigate the globe in just one week. From the bustling streets of London to the exotic landscapes of far-flung destinations, join them as they encounter unexpected challenges, encounter fascinating cultures, and unravel a mystery that could change the course of history. With danger lurking at every turn, will they succeed in their quest or will their ambitious journey prove to be their ultimate downfall?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Cultural Diversity
