Synopsis: In 'Zenobia; or, the Fall of Palmyra', readers are transported to the ancient city of Palmyra, where the captivating and enigmatic Zenobia rises to power. As she navigates the treacherous world of politics and warfare, Zenobia's strength and determination become legendary. However, her ambitions and the fate of Palmyra hang in the balance as she faces formidable enemies and grapples with her own desires. This gripping tale of power, love, and sacrifice will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Power, Love, Sacrifice
