Synopsis:
In "The Origin of Species: From 'The Westminster Review', April 1860," Thomas Henry Huxley presents a thought-provoking exploration of the origins of species and the mechanisms behind evolution. Through a captivating analysis of scientific evidence, Huxley challenges prevailing beliefs and offers a compelling argument that will leave readers questioning their understanding of the natural world.

Genres:
Science, Non-fiction, Philosophy

Themes:
Evolution, Natural selection, Scientific inquiry
