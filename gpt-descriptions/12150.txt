Synopsis: In 'North, South and Over the Sea' by Francis, M. E., a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling streets of London to the enchanting landscapes of Scotland and beyond. As she navigates through unexpected twists and turns, Emily discovers hidden family secrets, encounters mysterious characters, and finds herself torn between love and duty. With each step she takes, Emily unravels a captivating tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Adventure, Love and duty
