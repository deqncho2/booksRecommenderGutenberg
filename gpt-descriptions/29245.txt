Synopsis: 
In 'A Breath of Prairie and other stories', Will Lillibridge takes readers on a captivating journey through the vast prairies of the American Midwest. From tales of love and loss to stories of resilience and redemption, this collection of short stories explores the lives of ordinary people facing extraordinary circumstances. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Lillibridge paints a vivid picture of the prairie landscape and the human spirit that thrives within it. Whether it's a young couple struggling to make a life on the frontier or a lonely farmer finding solace in the beauty of nature, each story in this collection offers a glimpse into the heart and soul of the prairie.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Love and Loss, Resilience, Redemption, Nature, Frontier Life
