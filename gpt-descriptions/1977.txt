Synopsis:
In Racine's tragic masterpiece, 'Phaedra', a forbidden love ignites a chain of events that leads to devastating consequences. Set in ancient Greece, the story revolves around Phaedra, the wife of Theseus, who finds herself consumed by a passionate and illicit desire for her stepson, Hippolytus. As her inner turmoil intensifies, Phaedra must navigate the treacherous waters of love, honor, and duty, ultimately leading to a heart-wrenching climax that explores the depths of human emotions and the consequences of unchecked desires.

Genres:
Tragedy, Drama

Themes:
Forbidden love, Honor, Duty, Guilt, Fate
