Synopsis: In 'Alamo Ranch: A Story of New Mexico' by Sarah Warner Brooks, a young woman named Elena embarks on a journey to the Alamo Ranch, a remote and mysterious place in the heart of New Mexico. As she delves into the secrets of the ranch, she uncovers a web of family history, hidden treasures, and long-lost love. With each discovery, Elena becomes entangled in a captivating tale that spans generations, blurring the lines between past and present. As she unravels the mysteries of Alamo Ranch, Elena must confront her own desires, fears, and the power of destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Heritage, Love and loss, Fate
