Synopsis: In 'Typhoon' by Joseph Conrad, Captain MacWhirr finds himself at the helm of a steamship caught in the midst of a powerful typhoon. As the storm intensifies, the crew faces unimaginable challenges and the ship's fate hangs in the balance. Amidst the chaos, Conrad weaves a gripping tale of human resilience, the clash between nature and man, and the unpredictable forces that shape our lives.

Genres: Adventure, Sea fiction, Psychological fiction

Themes: Man vs. Nature, Human Resilience, Fate and Determination
