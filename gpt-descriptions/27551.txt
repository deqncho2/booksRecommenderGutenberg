Synopsis: In 'Hannibal: Makers of History' by Abbott, Jacob, embark on a thrilling journey through the life of one of history's most enigmatic figures, Hannibal. From his early years in Carthage to his audacious military campaigns, this captivating biography unveils the strategic genius and relentless determination that made Hannibal a legend. Witness his epic battles against Rome, his daring tactics, and the personal sacrifices he made in his quest for victory. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the fate of empires hangs in the balance. Prepare to be enthralled by the extraordinary life of Hannibal, a man who defied the odds and left an indelible mark on history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Leadership, Ambition, Warfare, Loyalty
