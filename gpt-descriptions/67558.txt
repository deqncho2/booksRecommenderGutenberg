Synopsis: 

In 'The Origin of Property in Land: With an introductory chapter on the English manor', Fustel de Coulanges takes readers on a captivating journey through history to uncover the fascinating origins of property ownership. Delving into the depths of ancient civilizations and exploring the evolution of land ownership, this thought-provoking work sheds light on the complex relationship between humans and the land they inhabit. With meticulous research and insightful analysis, Coulanges challenges conventional notions of property and invites readers to question the very foundations of our modern society.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Anthropology, Sociology

Themes: Property ownership, Land rights, Evolution of society
