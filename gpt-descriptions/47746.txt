Synopsis: 

'Campfire and Battlefield: An Illustrated History of the Campaigns and Conflicts of the Great Civil War' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous era of the American Civil War. Through vivid illustrations and compelling narratives, authors Ridpath, Connor, Gordon, Howard, Johnson, and Morgan bring to life the epic battles, the courageous soldiers, and the profound impact of this historic conflict. From the blood-soaked fields of Gettysburg to the hallowed grounds of Antietam, this book offers a comprehensive and visually stunning exploration of the Civil War, shedding light on the strategies, sacrifices, and struggles that shaped the destiny of a nation.

Genres: History, Military, Illustrated

Themes: War, Leadership, Sacrifice, National Identity
