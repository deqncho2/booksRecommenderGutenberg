Synopsis: In 'Tattered Tom; or, The Story of a Street Arab' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Tom, a young boy living on the streets of New York City. Tom's life is a constant struggle as he navigates the harsh realities of poverty, homelessness, and the dangers of the city. However, a chance encounter with a kind-hearted stranger sets Tom on a path that could change his life forever. Will Tom be able to overcome the obstacles in his path and find a better future?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Social Realism

Themes: Poverty, Resilience, Redemption
