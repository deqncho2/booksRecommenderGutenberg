Synopsis: In "The Truth about the Titanic," Gracie and Archibald delve into the untold secrets and mysteries surrounding the infamous sinking of the Titanic. Through meticulous research and shocking revelations, they uncover a web of deceit, hidden agendas, and unexpected alliances that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew about this tragic event. As they navigate through historical documents, personal testimonies, and long-lost artifacts, Gracie and Archibald embark on a thrilling journey that will challenge their beliefs and change the way they view one of the most iconic disasters in history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Truth and deception, Human resilience, Historical significance
