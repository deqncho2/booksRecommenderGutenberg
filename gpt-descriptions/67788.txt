Synopsis: 

Step back in time and journey through the colonial doorways of America with Anne Hollingsworth Wharton. In this captivating collection of historical vignettes, Wharton brings to life the untold stories of the men and women who shaped the early days of the nation. From the bustling streets of Philadelphia to the quiet plantations of Virginia, each chapter unveils a new tale of love, ambition, and sacrifice. Through the eyes of Wharton, we witness the struggles and triumphs of these remarkable individuals as they navigate the complexities of colonial life. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, 'Through Colonial Doorways' offers a captivating glimpse into a bygone era that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Anthology

Themes: Colonial America, Love and Relationships, Social Class, Ambition, Sacrifice
