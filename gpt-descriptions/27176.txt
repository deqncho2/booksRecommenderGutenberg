Synopsis: 
In 'More Beasts (For Worse Children)' by Belloc, Hilaire, prepare to embark on a delightfully wicked journey through a collection of cautionary tales that will leave you both amused and slightly disturbed. From the misadventures of a disobedient boy who encounters a peculiar creature, to the consequences faced by a gluttonous child who indulges in forbidden treats, each story is filled with dark humor and unexpected twists. With its clever rhymes and whimsical illustrations, this book is sure to captivate readers of all ages, offering a unique blend of entertainment and moral lessons.

Genres: Children's Literature, Poetry, Dark Humor

Themes: Morality, Consequences, Mischievousness
