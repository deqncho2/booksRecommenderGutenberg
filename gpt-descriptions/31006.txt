Synopsis: 

In 'The Author's Printing and Publishing Assistant', Frederick Saunders takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of printing and publishing. This comprehensive guide provides explanations of the intricate process of printing, from the preparation and calculation of manuscripts to the selection of paper, type, binding, illustrations, and even advertising. Alongside these explanations, Saunders offers a detailed description of the typographical marks used in the correction of the press, providing invaluable insights into the behind-the-scenes work of creating a printed masterpiece. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Publishing

Themes: Printing techniques, Manuscript preparation, Publishing industry
