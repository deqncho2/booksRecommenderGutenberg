Synopsis: 
In 'The Game-Birds of the Coasts and Lakes of the Northern States of America', Robert Barnwell Roosevelt takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the diverse landscapes of America's sea-shores and inland waters. With a keen focus on the art of sporting, Roosevelt provides a comprehensive account of the various game-birds that inhabit these regions, while also delving into the debate between breech-loaders and muzzle-loaders. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, the author invites readers to explore the thrilling world of hunting and the natural beauty of America's northern states.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Nature

Themes: Hunting, Wildlife, Sporting, Gun technology
