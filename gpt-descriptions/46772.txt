Synopsis: 
'Across Iceland' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the stunning landscapes of Iceland, as the author, W. Bisiker, embarks on a daring adventure. From traversing treacherous glaciers to encountering mystical creatures, this travel memoir is filled with captivating anecdotes and breathtaking descriptions that will leave readers yearning to explore the enchanting beauty of Iceland themselves.

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Cultural immersion
