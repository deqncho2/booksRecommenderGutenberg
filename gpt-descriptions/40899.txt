Synopsis: In the small town of Willow Creek, a group of women form an unlikely bond through their shared love for gardening. As they come together to create a beautiful community garden, they discover that their individual struggles and secrets are not so different after all. With each passing season, the Morning Glory Club becomes a sanctuary where friendships bloom, secrets are unearthed, and healing begins. But when a mysterious newcomer arrives, the women must confront their pasts and face the challenges that threaten to uproot their newfound harmony.

Genres: Women's Fiction, Contemporary Fiction, Romance

Themes: Friendship, Healing, Community, Secrets, Growth
