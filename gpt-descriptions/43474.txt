Synopsis: 
In the November 30, 1880 issue of Harper's Young People, readers are transported to a captivating world filled with adventure, mystery, and imagination. Through a collection of beautifully illustrated stories, this monthly publication takes young readers on thrilling journeys to far-off lands, introduces them to fascinating characters, and immerses them in tales that will leave them eagerly turning the pages. From tales of daring explorers to heartwarming stories of friendship and bravery, this issue of Harper's Young People promises to captivate and inspire young minds.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Bravery
