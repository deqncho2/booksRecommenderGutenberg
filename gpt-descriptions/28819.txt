Synopsis: 
In the small town of Maplewood, four high school girls find themselves unexpectedly thrown together as they vie for the coveted title of School Queen. As they navigate the treacherous waters of popularity, friendships are tested, secrets are revealed, and alliances are formed. With each girl harboring her own ambitions and insecurities, the competition becomes more than just a popularity contest. As tensions rise and the stakes get higher, the girls must confront their own desires and the lengths they are willing to go to achieve their dreams. 'The School Queens' is a gripping tale of ambition, friendship, and the dark side of popularity.

Genres: Young Adult, Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Ambition, Friendship, Identity, Peer Pressure
