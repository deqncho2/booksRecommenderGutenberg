Synopsis: In "The Prisoner at the Bar: Sidelights on the Administration of Criminal Justice," Arthur Cheney Train takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of the criminal justice system. Through a series of compelling anecdotes and insightful observations, Train sheds light on the fascinating world of courtroom drama, exploring the complexities of legal proceedings, the strategies employed by lawyers, and the impact of justice on both the accused and the accuser. With each story, the reader is drawn deeper into the suspenseful and thought-provoking realm of criminal trials, where truth and justice hang in the balance.

Genres: Non-fiction, Legal Thriller, True Crime

Themes: Criminal justice, Legal system, Courtroom drama, Ethics, Truth and justice
