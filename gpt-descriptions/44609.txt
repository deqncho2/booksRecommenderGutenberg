Synopsis: In "The Northfield Tragedy; or, the Robber's Raid," author J.H. Hanson presents a thrilling narrative that recounts the audacious attempt to rob a bank in Northfield, Minnesota. This gripping tale delves into the cold-blooded murder of a brave cashier and an innocent citizen, as well as the subsequent slaying of two of the notorious brigands. The story also vividly describes the incredible pursuit and capture of the robbers, providing biographies of the victims, captors, and the infamous Younger and James Gang of desperadoes. 

Genres: Historical fiction, True crime, Adventure

Themes: Crime and punishment, Heroism, Justice, Survival
