Synopsis: 
In 'Jack Harkaway in New York; or, The Adventures of the Travelers' Club', readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside the daring and resourceful Jack Harkaway as he finds himself in the bustling streets of New York City. As a member of the prestigious Travelers' Club, Jack becomes entangled in a web of mystery and danger, encountering eccentric characters, uncovering hidden secrets, and facing numerous challenges that test his courage and wit. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this captivating adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
