Synopsis: In 'The Gentle Art of Cooking Wives' by Elizabeth Strong Worthington, a seemingly perfect suburban neighborhood is turned upside down when a mysterious cooking club is formed. As the wives gather to share their culinary secrets, hidden desires, rivalries, and secrets begin to simmer beneath the surface. With each recipe, the women's lives become more entangled, leading to unexpected consequences that will leave readers hungry for more.

Genres: Fiction, Women's Fiction, Domestic Drama

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Female Relationships, Identity and Self-Discovery
