Synopsis: 
In 'The White Linen Nurse' by Eleanor Hallowell Abbott, a young nurse named Linda finds herself in a challenging situation when she is assigned to care for a difficult patient in a wealthy household. As Linda navigates the complexities of her job, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden desires, and unexpected alliances. With her determination and compassion, Linda must confront the consequences of her choices and find her own path in a world filled with societal expectations and personal sacrifices.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Duty and responsibility, Love and sacrifice, Social class and expectations
