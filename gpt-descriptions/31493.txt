Synopsis: In "The Daughter of a Republican" by Bernie Babcock, a young woman finds herself torn between her conservative upbringing and her own desires for independence and self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of a politically charged era, she embarks on a journey of personal growth, challenging societal expectations and questioning her own beliefs. As she navigates the complexities of family, love, and identity, she must confront the consequences of her choices and ultimately decide where her true allegiance lies.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Political Drama

Themes: Identity, Family, Politics, Independence, Self-discovery
