Synopsis: In 'The Green Rust' by Wallace Edgar, a brilliant chemist stumbles upon a mysterious substance that holds the power to change the world. As he delves deeper into its secrets, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and international espionage. With lives at stake and a race against time, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth behind the green rust and prevent its catastrophic consequences.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Scientific discovery, Power and its consequences, Espionage
