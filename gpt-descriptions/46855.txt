Synopsis: In 'Under the Flag of France: A Tale of Bertrand du Guesclin' by Ker, David, readers are transported to medieval France, where they follow the captivating journey of Bertrand du Guesclin, a legendary knight and military leader. Set against the backdrop of the Hundred Years' War, this enthralling tale delves into du Guesclin's rise from humble beginnings to becoming one of the most renowned figures in French history. As he navigates treacherous political landscapes, engages in epic battles, and forms unexpected alliances, du Guesclin's unwavering loyalty to his country and his unwavering determination to defend it against all odds shines through. With vivid descriptions, gripping action, and a cast of memorable characters, this historical novel brings to life the tumultuous era of medieval France and the indomitable spirit of a true hero.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Heroism, National Identity
