Synopsis: In 'Tom Swift and His Giant Cannon; Or, The Longest Shots on Record', the brilliant young inventor Tom Swift embarks on an extraordinary mission to create a revolutionary cannon capable of firing projectiles unimaginable distances. As he faces numerous challenges and adversaries, Tom's determination and ingenuity are put to the ultimate test. With high stakes and thrilling adventures, this book takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of cutting-edge technology and daring exploits.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Invention and innovation, Courage and perseverance
