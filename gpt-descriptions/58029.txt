Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Oliver Goldsmith' is a comprehensive collection of the literary works of Oliver Goldsmith, a renowned 18th-century Irish writer. This compilation includes a wide range of his works, such as novels, plays, essays, and poems. From the beloved novel "The Vicar of Wakefield" to the witty play "She Stoops to Conquer," readers will be captivated by Goldsmith's masterful storytelling and his ability to depict both the humor and the complexities of human nature. This index serves as a gateway to explore the diverse literary contributions of Oliver Goldsmith, offering a delightful journey through his timeless works.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Poetry, Essays
Themes: Human nature, Social satire, Comedy, Morality
