Synopsis: In the Name of the People by Arthur W. Marchmont is a gripping political thriller that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of power and corruption. Set in a fictional country, the story follows a young journalist who stumbles upon a dangerous conspiracy that reaches the highest levels of government. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and danger, where the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. With his life on the line, he must navigate treacherous waters and make difficult choices in order to expose the truth and bring justice to those who have been oppressed. 

Genres: Political thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Power and corruption, Betrayal, Justice
