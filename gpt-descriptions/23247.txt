Synopsis: In "The Cursed Patois: From "Mackinac And Lake Stories", 1899," Mary Hartwell Catherwood takes readers on a captivating journey to the enchanting Mackinac Island. As the story unfolds, a mysterious curse begins to haunt the island's inhabitants, leading to a series of unexplained events and unexpected encounters. Amidst the backdrop of this supernatural phenomenon, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, love, and the struggle to break free from the curse's grip. With its rich descriptions and intriguing plot, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the curse.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Historical Fiction

Themes: Curses and superstitions, Love and relationships, Secrets and deception
