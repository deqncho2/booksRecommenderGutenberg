Synopsis: In "Katharine von Bora: Dr. Martin Luther's Wife" by Armin Stein, delve into the captivating life of Katharine von Bora, a remarkable woman who defied societal norms and became the wife of one of history's most influential figures, Martin Luther. From her daring escape from a convent to her unexpected marriage to Luther, this biographical account unveils the untold story of a woman who played a pivotal role in the Reformation. With intrigue, passion, and resilience, Katharine's journey will leave readers inspired and eager to discover the impact she had on her husband and the world.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Marriage, Religious Reformation, Gender Roles
