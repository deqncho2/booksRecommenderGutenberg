Synopsis: 

'Pilgrim Trails: A Plymouth-to-Provincetown Sketchbook' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the historic towns of Plymouth and Provincetown. Through the eyes of the talented artist Frances Lester Warner, this sketchbook offers a unique perspective on the landscapes, architecture, and people that make these places so special. From the charming streets of Plymouth to the vibrant art scene of Provincetown, each sketch captures the essence and beauty of these iconic New England destinations. 

Genres: Art, Travel, History

Themes: New England history, Cultural heritage, Artistic expression
