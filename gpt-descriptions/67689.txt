Synopsis: 
In 'Cousin Lucy at Play: By the Author of the Rollo Books', readers are introduced to the lively and adventurous character of Lucy, a young girl who embarks on a series of exciting escapades. From exploring hidden caves to encountering unexpected challenges, Lucy's playful spirit leads her on a journey filled with curiosity and discovery. As she navigates through various situations, Lucy learns valuable lessons about friendship, perseverance, and the importance of embracing one's imagination. Join Lucy on her thrilling adventures and witness the joy and wonder that comes with being a child.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Perseverance
