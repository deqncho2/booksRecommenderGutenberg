Synopsis: In "The Old Homestead" by Ann S. Stephens, a young woman named Mary finds herself torn between duty and desire when she inherits her family's ancestral home. As she navigates the challenges of maintaining the old homestead, Mary becomes entangled in a web of family secrets, forbidden love, and the haunting presence of the past. With each discovery, she must confront her own identity and make difficult choices that will shape her future. This captivating tale of love, loss, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Family legacy, Identity, Forbidden love, Secrets
