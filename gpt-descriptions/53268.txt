Synopsis: In 'Murder at Large' by Lesley Frost, a small, picturesque town is shaken to its core when a brutal murder takes place during a local festival. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, and the residents find themselves questioning the true nature of their seemingly idyllic community. With a web of suspects and a detective determined to uncover the truth, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking conclusion.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Small-town secrets, Deception, Betrayal
