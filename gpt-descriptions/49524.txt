Synopsis: In "Knowledge for the Time: A Manual of Reading, Reference, and Conversation on Subjects of Living Interest, Useful Curiosity, and Amusing Research," Timbs, John takes readers on a captivating journey through a vast array of fascinating topics. From exploring the wonders of nature to delving into the mysteries of history and science, this manual serves as a treasure trove of knowledge and curiosity. With engaging anecdotes, thought-provoking discussions, and intriguing facts, Timbs invites readers to expand their understanding of the world around them and ignite their thirst for learning. Whether you're seeking to impress friends with captivating conversation or simply looking to satisfy your own intellectual curiosity, this book is a must-read for anyone eager to explore the depths of human knowledge.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Education

Themes: Intellectual curiosity, Lifelong learning, Broadening knowledge
