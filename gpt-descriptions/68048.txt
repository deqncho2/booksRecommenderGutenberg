Synopsis: In 'The Big Mogul' by Joseph Crosby Lincoln, a young and ambitious entrepreneur named Jack finds himself caught in a web of power, greed, and deception as he navigates the cutthroat world of corporate business. With his dreams of success on the line, Jack must make difficult choices and face unexpected challenges that threaten to unravel everything he has worked so hard to achieve. As the stakes rise and the tension mounts, readers will be captivated by the thrilling twists and turns that unfold in this gripping tale of ambition and betrayal.

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Power, Deception
