Synopsis: In 'Murder in the Gunroom' by H. Beam Piper, a wealthy gun collector is found dead in his gunroom, surrounded by his prized firearms. As the investigation unfolds, it becomes clear that this is no ordinary murder case. With a cast of eccentric characters, a web of secrets, and a trail of clues hidden within the world of guns, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of deception to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Greed, Obsession, Betrayal
