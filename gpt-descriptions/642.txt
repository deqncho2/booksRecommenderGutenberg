Synopsis: In "The Altar of the Dead" by Henry James, a man becomes consumed by his obsession with remembering the dead. As he creates a personal shrine in his home, he finds solace in the company of departed souls. However, his devotion to the deceased begins to isolate him from the living, leading to a profound exploration of love, loss, and the power of remembrance.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological fiction, ghost story

Themes: Death, grief, obsession, isolation, memory
