Synopsis: In 'The Slipper Point Mystery' by Augusta Huiell Seaman, a group of friends stumble upon an old mansion with a mysterious past while on vacation. As they explore the house, they uncover hidden clues and secret passages that lead them on a thrilling adventure to solve a long-forgotten mystery. With danger lurking around every corner, the friends must use their wits and bravery to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Secrets, Courage
