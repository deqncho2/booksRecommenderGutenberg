Synopsis: 

In 'The Coinages of the Channel Islands' by Lowsley, B., embark on a fascinating journey through the rich history and intricate world of numismatics in the Channel Islands. From the ancient Celtic coins to the medieval and modern coinages, this comprehensive guide unveils the hidden stories behind each coin, shedding light on the economic, political, and cultural developments of these enchanting islands. Delve into the mysteries of rare coins, discover the secrets of their designs, and unravel the tales of the people who once held them in their hands. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Numismatics

Themes: History, Culture, Economics, Collecting
