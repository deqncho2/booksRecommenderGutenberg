Synopsis: In 'War Days in Brittany' by Elsie Deming Jarves, a young American woman finds herself caught in the midst of World War II while visiting the picturesque region of Brittany, France. As the conflict escalates and the German occupation tightens its grip, she must navigate the treacherous landscape of war, facing danger, loss, and unexpected alliances. Through her eyes, we witness the resilience and courage of the local population as they fight to preserve their way of life amidst the chaos and uncertainty of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Resilience, Courage, Survival, Identity, Human Connection
