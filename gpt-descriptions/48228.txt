Synopsis: In 'Averil' by Rosa Nouchette Carey, a young woman named Averil finds herself caught between duty and desire as she navigates the complexities of Victorian society. With her strong-willed nature and a secret that threatens to unravel her world, Averil must make choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time. As she grapples with love, loyalty, and the pursuit of personal happiness, Averil's journey takes unexpected turns, leading her to question the expectations placed upon her. Will she find the courage to forge her own path, or will she succumb to the pressures of society?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Society's Expectations, Personal Freedom
