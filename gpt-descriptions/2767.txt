Synopsis:
In 'The Devil's Paw' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious and alluring woman named Madame Koluchy arrives in a small English village, capturing the attention of the locals. As her true intentions remain shrouded in secrecy, a young lawyer named Francis Ledsam becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. With his reputation and life on the line, Ledsam must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, leading him to question his own beliefs and alliances. As the truth slowly unravels, the fate of not only Ledsam but the entire village hangs in the balance.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
