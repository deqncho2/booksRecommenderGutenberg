Synopsis: In 'Natalie: A Garden Scout' by Lillian Elizabeth Roy, a young girl named Natalie discovers a hidden world within her own backyard. As she embarks on a thrilling adventure, she encounters magical creatures, uncovers ancient secrets, and learns the true power of nature. With each step, Natalie's courage and determination are put to the test, leading her to unexpected friendships and a deeper understanding of herself. This enchanting tale will transport readers to a world where imagination and nature intertwine, leaving them eager to explore their own surroundings.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Nature, Friendship, Self-discovery
