Synopsis: 
In 'Weatherby's Inning: A Story of College Life and Baseball' by Ralph Henry Barbour, readers are transported to the exciting world of college life and baseball. The story revolves around Weatherby, a talented college baseball player who faces numerous challenges both on and off the field. As he navigates the pressures of academics, friendships, and love, Weatherby's passion for the game is put to the test. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the triumphs and tribulations of college athletes, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover Weatherby's fate.

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Pursuit of passion, Friendship, Overcoming adversity
