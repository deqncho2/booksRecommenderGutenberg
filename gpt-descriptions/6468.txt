Synopsis: 
In 'On a Torn-Away World; Or, the Captives of the Great Earthquake', a group of adventurers find themselves stranded on a mysterious and treacherous planet after a catastrophic earthquake tears their world apart. As they navigate through perilous landscapes and encounter strange creatures, they must unravel the secrets of this new world and find a way back home. With danger lurking at every turn, their survival depends on their resourcefulness, courage, and the bonds they form along the way.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Friendship, Resilience
