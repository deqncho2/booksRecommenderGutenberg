Synopsis: 
In 'On Canada's Frontier: Sketches of History, Sport, and Adventure and of the Indians, Missionaries, Fur-traders, and Newer Settlers of Western Canada', Ralph Julian takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of Western Canada. Through a series of vivid sketches, he introduces us to a diverse cast of characters, including indigenous peoples, missionaries, fur-traders, and the brave pioneers who ventured into this rugged land. From thrilling hunting expeditions to heartwarming tales of cultural exchange, Julian's narratives paint a rich and immersive picture of life on Canada's frontier. 

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Cultural studies

Themes: 
Exploration and discovery, Cultural diversity, Survival and resilience
