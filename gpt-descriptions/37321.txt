Synopsis: In "Remarks on a Pamphlet Lately published by the Rev. Mr. Maskelyne, Under the Authority of the Board of Longitude," John Harrison challenges the claims made by Mr. Maskelyne regarding the accuracy of his timekeeping invention. As a renowned horologist, Harrison presents a compelling argument that questions the authority and credibility of the Board of Longitude. With meticulous detail and a touch of controversy, this work delves into the world of scientific rivalry and the pursuit of recognition.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Scientific

Themes: Scientific rivalry, Authority and credibility, Pursuit of recognition
