Synopsis: In "Bismarck: Some Secret Pages of His History," Dr. Moritz Busch shares his personal diary chronicling his official and private interactions with the renowned Chancellor, Otto von Bismarck, over a span of twenty-five years. As an intimate observer, Busch unveils intriguing and undisclosed aspects of Bismarck's life, shedding light on the enigmatic figure behind the scenes of German politics. Through his vivid and captivating entries, Busch offers a unique perspective on the man who shaped the course of European history.

Genres: Biography, History, Memoir

Themes: Politics, Power, Diplomacy, Intrigue
