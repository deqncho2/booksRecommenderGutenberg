Synopsis: 

In "Last of the Great Scouts: The Life Story of William F. Cody ["Buffalo Bill"]", Helen Cody Wetmore takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the extraordinary life of one of America's most legendary figures. From his early days as a young scout and buffalo hunter to his rise as a renowned showman and Wild West icon, this captivating biography unveils the thrilling adventures and remarkable achievements of Buffalo Bill. With vivid storytelling and intimate anecdotes, Wetmore paints a vivid portrait of a man who became a symbol of the American frontier and captured the imagination of millions.

Genres: Biography, History, Adventure

Themes: American frontier, Wild West, Showmanship, Legends and myths
