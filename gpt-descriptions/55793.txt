Synopsis: In 'Tube, Train, Tram, and Car; or, Up-to-date locomotion', Arthur H. Beavan takes readers on a thrilling journey through the bustling world of transportation. From the underground tunnels of the London Tube to the sleek trains, trams, and cars that traverse the city streets, Beavan explores the fascinating history, technological advancements, and societal impact of these modes of locomotion. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, he paints a vivid picture of the ever-evolving landscape of transportation, offering a glimpse into the future of travel.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Transportation, Urbanization, Technological Progress
