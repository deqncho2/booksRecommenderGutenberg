Synopsis: In 'The Substance of a Dream' by F. W. Bain, a young artist named Julian embarks on a journey that blurs the lines between reality and imagination. As he delves deeper into his own mind, he discovers a hidden world filled with enchanting landscapes, mysterious characters, and profound revelations. With each step, Julian becomes entangled in a web of dreams and desires, questioning the nature of art, love, and the boundaries of human perception. This captivating tale takes readers on a mesmerizing exploration of the power of dreams and the transformative nature of creativity.

Genres: Fantasy, Magical Realism, Literary Fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, Reality vs. illusion, Self-discovery, Love and desire
