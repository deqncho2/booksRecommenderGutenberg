Synopsis: In 'Hollyhock: A Spirit of Mischief' by Meade, L. T., a mischievous spirit named Hollyhock embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes her from the mystical realm of spirits to the bustling streets of a small town. With her mischievous nature and a heart full of curiosity, Hollyhock finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and ancient magic. As she navigates through unexpected friendships and dangerous encounters, Hollyhock must uncover the truth about her own origins and the role she plays in the balance between the spirit world and the human realm. This enchanting tale is filled with whimsy, mystery, and unexpected twists that will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Magic
