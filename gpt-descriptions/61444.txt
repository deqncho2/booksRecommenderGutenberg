Synopsis: In 'A House Divided Against Itself', the final volume of the gripping trilogy by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), the lives of the characters are thrown into turmoil as long-held secrets and hidden desires come to light. As tensions rise and alliances shift, the once united household becomes a battleground for power, love, and revenge. With unexpected twists and shocking revelations, this concluding installment will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Family dynamics, Betrayal, Redemption, Secrets
