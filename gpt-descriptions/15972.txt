Synopsis: 

In "The Record of a Regiment of the Line: Being a Regimental History of the 1st Battalion Devonshire Regiment during the Boer War 1899-1902," author Jacson, Mainwaring George takes readers on a captivating journey through the trials and triumphs of the 1st Battalion Devonshire Regiment during the Boer War. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the author brings to life the experiences of the soldiers, their camaraderie, and the challenges they faced on the battlefield. From the initial mobilization to the intense battles and the eventual resolution, this regimental history provides a gripping account of the bravery and resilience of the soldiers who fought in this historic conflict.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, War memoir

Themes: Bravery, Resilience, Camaraderie, Conflict, Historical significance
