Synopsis: 
'Yorkshire Ditties, First Series: To Which Is Added The Cream Of Wit And Humour From His Popular Writings' by John Hartley is a delightful collection of witty and humorous poems that capture the essence of Yorkshire life. From charming anecdotes to clever wordplay, Hartley's verses transport readers to the idyllic landscapes and vibrant communities of Yorkshire. This collection also includes a selection of his most popular writings, showcasing his talent for blending wit and humor with a touch of nostalgia. 

Genres: Poetry, Humor, Regional Literature
Themes: Yorkshire life, Wit, Humor, Nostalgia
