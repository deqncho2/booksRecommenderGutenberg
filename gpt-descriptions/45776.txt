Synopsis: In 'The Sky Detectives; Or, How Jack Ralston Got His Man', Jack Ralston, a young and daring aviator, finds himself entangled in a thrilling mystery when a valuable artifact goes missing. With his trusty biplane, Ralston embarks on a high-flying adventure, chasing clues and facing danger at every turn. As he races against time to solve the case, Ralston must rely on his wit, courage, and flying skills to outsmart the cunning criminals and bring them to justice.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Aviation

Themes: Courage, Determination, Crime-solving
