Synopsis: 

In "Uncle Silas: A Tale of Bartram-Haugh" by Joseph Sheridan Le Fanu, the young and innocent Maud Ruthyn finds herself thrust into a world of mystery and danger when she is sent to live with her reclusive and enigmatic Uncle Silas at the eerie Bartram-Haugh. As Maud uncovers dark secrets and encounters a cast of intriguing and suspicious characters, she must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal to protect herself and those she holds dear. With its atmospheric setting and gripping plot, this gothic tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the haunting secrets of Bartram-Haugh.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Family secrets, Female agency, Fear and paranoia
