Synopsis: In the midst of the devastating Yellow Fever Plague in Shreveport, Louisiana, a young woman named Agnes emerges as an unexpected hero. As the disease spreads rapidly, Agnes defies societal norms and risks her own life to care for the sick and dying. With unwavering determination and compassion, she becomes a beacon of hope in a city consumed by fear and despair. But as the epidemic worsens, Agnes finds herself facing unimaginable challenges that test her strength and resilience. Will she be able to overcome the odds and save her community from the grips of this deadly plague?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Medical Thriller

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Resilience, Community, Disease
