Synopsis: 
'Portland, Oregon, A.D. 1999, and other sketches' by Hayes, J. W. takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of interconnected stories set in the vibrant city of Portland in the year 1999. From the eccentric characters that inhabit the city's streets to the unexpected encounters and events that shape their lives, this book offers a unique and thought-provoking exploration of human connection, self-discovery, and the complexities of urban life.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Contemporary Literature

Themes: Human connection, Self-discovery, Urban life
