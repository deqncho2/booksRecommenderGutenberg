Synopsis: In 'The Ego Machine' by Henry Kuttner, a brilliant scientist invents a machine that can transfer a person's consciousness into another body, allowing them to experience life from a completely different perspective. However, as the machine's capabilities become more advanced, the line between reality and illusion begins to blur, leading to unexpected consequences and a thrilling exploration of identity and the nature of existence.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Identity, Reality vs. Illusion, Ethics of Technology
