Synopsis: In 'The Haunted Sentry Box of Porto Rico' by Miller, Lewis, a young soldier stationed in Porto Rico finds himself assigned to a mysterious and eerie sentry box. As he begins his duty, he becomes increasingly aware of strange occurrences and unexplainable phenomena that surround the sentry box. With each passing night, the soldier's fear and curiosity grow, leading him on a chilling journey to uncover the dark secrets hidden within the haunted sentry box.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Curiosity, Supernatural phenomena
