Synopsis: In 'The Lost Parchment: A Detective Story' by Fergus Hume, a renowned detective is called upon to solve a perplexing case involving a missing ancient parchment. As he delves into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected twists that lead him down a path of danger and intrigue. With each clue he uncovers, the detective becomes more entangled in a complex puzzle that could have far-reaching consequences. Will he be able to decipher the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, the pursuit of truth, the power of knowledge
