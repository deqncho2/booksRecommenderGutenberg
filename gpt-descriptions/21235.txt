Synopsis: In 'The Maidens' Lodge; or, None of Self and All of Thee: (In the Reign of Queen Anne)', Emily Sarah Holt takes us back to the captivating era of Queen Anne's reign. The story follows the lives of two young women, Lucy and Dorothy, who find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, romance, and political intrigue. As they navigate the complexities of court life and the challenges of their own personal journeys, they must learn to navigate the treacherous waters of loyalty, sacrifice, and the pursuit of true love. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling characters, this novel will transport readers to a time of grandeur and passion.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Sacrifice, Love, Political Intrigue
