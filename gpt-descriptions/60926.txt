Synopsis: In 'Captains of Harley: A School Story' by Cleaver Hylton, a group of misfit students at Harley High School find themselves unexpectedly thrust into the world of competitive motorcycle racing. As they navigate the challenges of training, rivalries, and personal growth, they discover the true meaning of friendship, determination, and the exhilaration of chasing their dreams. With adrenaline-fueled action and heartfelt moments, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, cheering for the underdogs until the very end.

Genres: Young Adult, Sports, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Self-discovery
