Synopsis: 

In "Scotland's Mark on America" by George Fraser Black, embark on a captivating journey through the lesser-known history of Scottish influence on the United States. From the early Scottish settlers who played pivotal roles in shaping the American landscape to the unsung heroes who left an indelible mark on American culture, this book uncovers the hidden stories that have been overshadowed by more prominent historical narratives. Delve into the lives of Scottish explorers, inventors, politicians, and artists who left their homeland to make their mark across the Atlantic, leaving an enduring legacy that still resonates today. 

Genres: History, Biography, Cultural Studies

Themes: Scottish-American history, Immigration, Cultural exchange
