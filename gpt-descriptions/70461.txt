Synopsis: 
In "An Egyptian Oasis: An account of the oasis of Kharga in the Libyan desert, with special reference to its history, physical geography, and water-supply," H. J. L. Beadnell takes readers on a captivating journey to the mysterious oasis of Kharga. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Beadnell explores the oasis's rich history, its unique physical geography, and the fascinating water-supply system that sustains life in this arid region. From ancient civilizations to modern-day discoveries, this book unveils the secrets hidden within the oasis, leaving readers eager to uncover the wonders that lie within its sandy dunes.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, History

Themes: Exploration, Ancient civilizations, Geography, Water resources
