Synopsis: In 'The King's Threshold; and On Baile's Strand', Yeats takes readers on a captivating journey through two dramatic plays. In 'The King's Threshold', a poet's loyalty is tested as he confronts the harsh realities of power and justice. Meanwhile, 'On Baile's Strand' delves into the complex dynamics of love, honor, and betrayal within a mythical Irish setting. These two enthralling plays explore the depths of human emotions and the consequences of one's choices.

Genres: Drama, Poetry, Irish Literature

Themes: Power and Justice, Loyalty, Love and Betrayal
