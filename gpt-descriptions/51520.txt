Synopsis: In 'The Downfall of the Dervishes; or, The Avenging of Gordon', Ernest Nathaniel Bennett takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous landscapes of Sudan. Set during the late 19th century, the story follows a group of courageous individuals who embark on a perilous mission to seek justice and avenge the death of General Charles Gordon. As they navigate through the heart of enemy territory, they encounter unexpected allies, face deadly challenges, and uncover shocking secrets that could change the course of history. With its gripping narrative and vivid descriptions, this historical adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Justice, Revenge, Loyalty, Sacrifice
