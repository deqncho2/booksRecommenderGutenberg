Synopsis: In "Three Contributions to the Theory of Sex," Freud delves into the mysterious realm of human sexuality, exploring its origins, development, and various manifestations. Through a series of thought-provoking essays, he challenges conventional beliefs and offers groundbreaking insights into the complex nature of human desire. From the exploration of childhood sexuality to the examination of sexual perversions, Freud's work takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden depths of the human psyche.

Genres: Psychology, Non-fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Sexuality, Psychoanalysis, Human Desire
