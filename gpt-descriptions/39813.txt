Synopsis: In 'A Sovereign Remedy' by Flora Annie Webster Steel, a young woman named Leela embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience in the face of adversity. Set in colonial India, Leela's life takes an unexpected turn when she is forced to confront the harsh realities of her society. As she navigates through love, loss, and the pursuit of her dreams, Leela discovers the power of inner strength and the transformative nature of hope.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Identity, Social Injustice, Resilience, Love and Loss
