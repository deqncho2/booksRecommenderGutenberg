Synopsis: In 'Home Occupations for Boys and Girls', Bertha Johnston takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of practical and creative activities for children. From woodworking and gardening to cooking and sewing, this book offers a treasure trove of engaging projects that will keep young minds and hands busy. With clear instructions and delightful illustrations, Johnston inspires children to explore their talents and develop valuable skills while having fun at home. Whether it's making homemade toys or learning how to grow their own vegetables, this book is a delightful resource for children and parents alike.

Genres: Children's literature, Crafts and Hobbies, Education

Themes: Creativity, Skill-building, Self-sufficiency, Practicality
