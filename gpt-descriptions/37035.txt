Synopsis: 
'Journal of Dr. Elias Cornelius, a Revolutionary Surgeon' takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. Dr. Elias Cornelius, a skilled surgeon, finds himself torn between his duty to heal and his desire to fight for freedom. As he navigates the horrors of war, he must confront the ethical dilemmas that arise when medicine and revolution collide. Through his journal entries, readers witness the sacrifices, triumphs, and heart-wrenching moments that shape Dr. Cornelius's life and the lives of those around him.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Medical Drama

Themes: Duty and Sacrifice, Ethical Dilemmas, Courage and Resilience
