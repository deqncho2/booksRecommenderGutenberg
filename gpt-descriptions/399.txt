Synopsis: In 'Cast Upon the Breakers' by Horatio Alger, Jr., a young man named Frank is faced with a sudden change in fortune that leaves him destitute and alone. Forced to navigate the treacherous waters of society, Frank must rely on his wit, determination, and a few unexpected allies to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. As he strives to rebuild his life, Frank discovers the true meaning of resilience and the power of friendship, ultimately leading him on a journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Resilience, Friendship, Redemption
