Synopsis: In 'Daughter of the Night' by Richard S. Shaver, a young woman named Lily discovers a hidden world beneath the surface of her seemingly ordinary life. As she delves deeper into this mysterious realm, she uncovers dark secrets and encounters supernatural beings that challenge her perception of reality. With danger lurking at every turn, Lily must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth about her own identity and the destiny that awaits her.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Supernatural, Perception
