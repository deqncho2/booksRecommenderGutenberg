Synopsis: 
In Tolstoy's "Youth," we follow the journey of a young man named Dmitry, who embarks on a transformative adventure that takes him from the idyllic countryside to the bustling city. As Dmitry navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations, he grapples with the profound questions of life's purpose and the pursuit of true happiness. Through vivid descriptions and introspective musings, Tolstoy paints a captivating portrait of youth's fleeting beauty and the universal quest for meaning.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman, Literary Fiction

Themes: 
Youth, Love, Friendship, Identity, Purpose, Society
