Synopsis: In 'Harriet and the Piper' by Kathleen Thompson Norris, a young woman named Harriet finds herself captivated by the enchanting music of a mysterious piper who appears in her small town. As she becomes entangled in his spellbinding melodies, Harriet must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and the power of music. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, this captivating tale explores the transformative nature of art and the choices we make in pursuit of our passions.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love, Music, Secrets, Transformation
