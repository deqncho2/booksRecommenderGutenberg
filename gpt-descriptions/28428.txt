Synopsis: 

In the sixth edition of 'Manual of Surgery Volume Second: Extremities—Head—Neck,' Miles and Thomson delve into the intricate world of surgical procedures, exploring the complexities of the human body's extremities, head, and neck. This comprehensive guide provides a detailed account of various surgical techniques, from the delicate intricacies of hand surgery to the life-saving procedures performed on the head and neck. With a wealth of knowledge and expertise, the authors navigate through the challenges and advancements in surgical practices, offering invaluable insights into the art and science of surgery.

Genres: Medical, Reference, Non-fiction

Themes: Surgical techniques, Human anatomy, Medical advancements
