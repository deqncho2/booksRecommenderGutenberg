Synopsis: 

'The True Life of Betty Ireland: With Her Birth, Education, and Adventures. Together with Some Account of Her Elder Sister Blanch of Britain. Containing Sundry Very Curious Particulars' is an intriguing tale that follows the extraordinary life of Betty Ireland. From her humble beginnings to her thrilling adventures, this book delves into the captivating journey of a remarkable woman. Alongside Betty's story, the narrative also provides glimpses into the life of her elder sister, Blanch of Britain. Filled with curious and unexpected details, this anonymous work promises to captivate readers with its fascinating characters and enthralling plot.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Identity, Family, Adventure, Coming of Age
