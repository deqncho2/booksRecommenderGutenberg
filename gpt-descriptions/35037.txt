Synopsis: In 'Napoleon's Young Neighbor' by Helen Leah Reed, a young girl finds herself living next door to one of history's most iconic figures, Napoleon Bonaparte. As she navigates the complexities of her own life, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue that could change the course of history. With danger lurking around every corner, she must decide where her loyalties lie and how far she is willing to go to protect those she loves.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Secrets, Historical Events
