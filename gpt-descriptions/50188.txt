Synopsis: In "The Invisible Foe: A Story Adapted from the Play by Walter Hackett," Louise Jordan Miln takes readers on a thrilling journey through the mysterious world of espionage and intrigue. Set against the backdrop of World War I, the story follows a young woman named Evelyn as she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and deception. As she navigates through a treacherous landscape of spies and double agents, Evelyn must rely on her wit and courage to uncover the truth and protect those she holds dear. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Espionage, Deception, Loyalty, Courage
