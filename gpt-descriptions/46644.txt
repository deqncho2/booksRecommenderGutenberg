Synopsis: 

Prepare to embark on a captivating journey through the realms of invention and discovery in this intriguing collection of curious facts and characteristic sketches. Delve into the fascinating world of scientific breakthroughs, ingenious inventions, and the remarkable individuals behind them. From the mysterious origins of everyday objects to the astonishing tales of forgotten inventors, this book unveils a treasure trove of hidden knowledge that will leave you astounded and inspired. With each turn of the page, you will uncover captivating stories that will challenge your perception of the world and ignite your curiosity for the wonders of human ingenuity.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Invention, Discovery, Human Ingenuity
