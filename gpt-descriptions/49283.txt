Synopsis: In 'Edgar the Ready: A Tale of the Third Edward's Reign' by Shervill, W. P., readers are transported to the tumultuous era of King Edward III. The story follows the life of Edgar, a young and ambitious nobleman who finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and treacherous alliances. As Edgar navigates the treacherous court of Edward III, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of the kingdom. With vivid descriptions of medieval England and a cast of captivating characters, this historical fiction novel promises a thrilling journey through a time of power struggles, romance, and the pursuit of personal and political ambitions.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Intrigue

Themes: Power struggles, Love and betrayal, Ambition and destiny
