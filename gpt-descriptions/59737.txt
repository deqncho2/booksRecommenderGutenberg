Synopsis: 

In 'The Common Rocks and Minerals of Missouri' by Keller, W. D., embark on a fascinating journey through the geological wonders of Missouri. From the majestic Ozark Mountains to the mysterious caves and underground formations, this comprehensive guide unveils the hidden treasures that lie beneath the surface of the Show-Me State. With vivid descriptions and stunning photographs, the author takes readers on an exploration of the diverse rocks and minerals that shape Missouri's landscape, revealing their unique characteristics and the stories they hold. Whether you are a seasoned geologist or simply curious about the natural world around you, this book will ignite your sense of wonder and leave you eager to uncover the secrets that lie beneath your feet.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Geology, Natural History, Exploration
