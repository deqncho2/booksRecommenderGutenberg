Synopsis: 
'Home Life on an Ostrich Farm' takes readers on a captivating journey into the unique and fascinating world of an ostrich farm. Through the eyes of the author, Annie Martin, and her mother, Mrs. Martin, we are introduced to the daily challenges, unexpected adventures, and heartwarming moments that come with raising these magnificent birds. From the humorous antics of the ostriches to the bond formed between the Martins and their feathered friends, this memoir offers a delightful and insightful glimpse into a truly extraordinary way of life.

Genres: Memoir, Nature Writing, Animal Studies

Themes: Connection with Nature, Unconventional Lifestyles, Animal-Human Relationships
