Synopsis: 

In 'Trial of the Major War Criminals Before the International Military Tribunal, Nuremberg, 14 November 1945-1 October 1946, Volume 10', the gripping courtroom drama unfolds as the world witnesses the trial of the most notorious figures from Nazi Germany. This volume delves into the intense cross-examinations, shocking revelations, and impassioned arguments that shape the course of justice. As the prosecution and defense clash, the truth behind the atrocities committed during World War II is unveiled, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat until the final verdict is reached.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Legal drama, World War II

Themes: Justice, Accountability, Human rights, Historical memory
