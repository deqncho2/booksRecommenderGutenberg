Synopsis: 
In 'The Rome Express' by Arthur Griffiths, a thrilling journey aboard the famous Rome Express takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious murder occurs on board. As the train hurtles through the picturesque European countryside, a diverse group of passengers find themselves caught up in a web of intrigue, suspicion, and danger. With each passing mile, the tension rises, and everyone becomes a potential suspect. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of the crime is slowly unveiled. Will the truth be uncovered before the train reaches its final destination?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Suspicion, Intrigue, Deception, Investigation
