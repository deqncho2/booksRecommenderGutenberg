Synopsis: 
"The Boy Spy" is a captivating historical memoir that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the secret service operations during the American Civil War. Written by Joseph Orton Kerbey, a soldier who witnessed these events firsthand, the book offers a unique perspective on the war through the eyes of a young spy. Filled with suspense, danger, and unexpected twists, this true account unveils the hidden world of espionage and the bravery of those who risked their lives for their cause.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, War

Themes: Espionage, Bravery, Loyalty, Sacrifice
