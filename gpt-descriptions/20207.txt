Synopsis: 

'Under Wellington's Command: A Tale of the Peninsular War' follows the thrilling adventures of a young British officer, Terence O'Connor, as he joins the British army during the Peninsular War. Terence finds himself in the midst of intense battles, dangerous missions, and encounters with both friends and foes. As he navigates the treacherous landscapes of war-torn Spain and Portugal, Terence must prove his courage, loyalty, and resourcefulness to survive and make a difference in the face of overwhelming odds.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Resourcefulness, Survival
