Synopsis: 
In "Nobody's Man" by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious man with no past and no identity finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger. When he is mistaken for a notorious criminal, he must navigate a treacherous world of spies, double-crosses, and hidden agendas. As he unravels the truth about his own identity, he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game where trust is a luxury he cannot afford. With his life on the line, he must outwit his enemies and uncover the secrets that could either save him or seal his fate.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Espionage, Betrayal
