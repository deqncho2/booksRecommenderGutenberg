Synopsis: 

'Armenia and the Armenians: A List of References in the New York Public Library' is a comprehensive compilation that delves into the rich history, culture, and struggles of Armenia and its people. This meticulously curated collection of references, spanning various mediums, offers a captivating exploration of the Armenian experience. From ancient civilizations to modern-day challenges, the book provides a gateway to understanding the resilience, contributions, and unique identity of the Armenian nation. Whether you are a history enthusiast, a researcher, or simply curious about this fascinating culture, this extensive reference guide promises to be an enlightening and engaging resource.

Genres: Reference, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Armenian history, Armenian culture, Identity, Resilience
