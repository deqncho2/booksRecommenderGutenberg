Synopsis: In 'The Game of Logic' by Lewis Carroll, a renowned mathematician takes readers on a mind-bending journey through the world of logical reasoning. Through a series of intriguing puzzles and thought experiments, Carroll challenges readers to unravel complex logical problems and discover the hidden patterns that lie beneath. As the game unfolds, the boundaries between logic and imagination blur, leading to unexpected twists and revelations. Prepare to be captivated by Carroll's ingenious puzzles and embark on a thrilling intellectual adventure that will test your reasoning skills to the limit.

Genres: Puzzle, Logic, Mathematics

Themes: Logical reasoning, Problem-solving, Imagination
