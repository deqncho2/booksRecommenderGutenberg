Synopsis: 
In "How to Fence," Aaron A. Warford presents a comprehensive guide to the art of fencing and the use of the broadsword, accompanied by detailed instructions in archery. This practical manual covers everything from the basic techniques of fencing to advanced strategies, providing readers with a wealth of knowledge to master these ancient martial arts. With clear explanations and illustrations, Warford takes readers on a journey through the intricacies of swordplay, teaching them the skills necessary to become skilled fencers and archers. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced practitioner, this book is sure to captivate and inspire, offering valuable insights into the world of combat sports.

Genres: Sports, Martial Arts, Instructional

Themes: Skill development, Historical combat techniques, Physical fitness
