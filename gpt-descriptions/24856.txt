Synopsis: 
In 'Odysseus, the Hero of Ithaca', we embark on an epic journey alongside the cunning and courageous Odysseus, as he faces countless trials and tribulations on his quest to return home to Ithaca. From battling mythical creatures to outsmarting gods and goddesses, Odysseus must navigate treacherous waters and overcome formidable obstacles in order to reunite with his beloved wife and son. This thrilling adaptation of the third book of the Primary Schools of Athens, Greece, will transport readers to a world of adventure, heroism, and the enduring power of the human spirit.

Genres: 
Adventure, Mythology, Epic Poetry

Themes: 
Heroism, Perseverance, Homecoming
