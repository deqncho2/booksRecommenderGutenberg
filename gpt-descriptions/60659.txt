Synopsis: In 'Wild Kindred' by Jean M. Thompson, a small town is shaken by a mysterious disappearance that unravels the tightly woven fabric of its community. As secrets are unearthed and relationships tested, the residents find themselves questioning their own identities and the true nature of the bonds that connect them. With beautifully crafted prose and a cast of complex characters, Thompson explores the depths of human connection and the power of the past to shape our present.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Literary Fiction

Themes: Identity, Community, Secrets, Relationships
