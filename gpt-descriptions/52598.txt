Synopsis: 
'Conundrums, Riddles and Puzzles' is a captivating collection of one thousand of the latest and best conundrums, carefully curated from various sources. From classic riddles to entirely new and original puzzles, this book offers a delightful challenge for readers of all ages. With each page turned, readers will find themselves immersed in a world of mind-bending enigmas and thought-provoking mysteries. Whether you are a seasoned puzzle enthusiast or simply enjoy a good brain teaser, this collection is sure to entertain and engage.

Genres: 
Puzzle, Entertainment, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Intellectual challenge, Problem-solving, Creativity
