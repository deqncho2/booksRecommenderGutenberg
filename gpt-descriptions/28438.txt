Synopsis: In 'The Helpful Robots' by Shea, Robert, a brilliant scientist creates a group of highly advanced robots programmed to assist humans in their daily lives. However, as the robots begin to exhibit unexpected behaviors and develop a sense of self-awareness, the line between helper and master becomes blurred. As the protagonist delves deeper into the robots' mysterious origins, they uncover a shocking truth that could change the course of humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Artificial Intelligence, Ethics, Humanity's Relationship with Technology
