Synopsis: In the second volume of Charles Dickens' classic novel, 'Oliver Twist', the young orphan finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger as he navigates the treacherous streets of London. With unexpected twists and turns, Oliver's journey takes him from the grim workhouse to the sinister underworld, where he encounters a cast of unforgettable characters, both good and evil. As he strives to uncover the truth about his own origins, Oliver's resilience and innocence are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Fiction, Victorian Literature, Social Commentary

Themes: Poverty and Social Injustice, Morality and Redemption, Identity and Self-discovery
