Synopsis: In 'Boy: A Sketch' by Marie Corelli, a mysterious young boy with an extraordinary gift captures the attention of a renowned artist. As the artist becomes increasingly fascinated by the boy's abilities, he embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the boy's origins and the source of his remarkable talent. This captivating tale explores the power of art, the complexities of human nature, and the profound impact that one individual can have on the lives of others.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Artistic inspiration, Identity, Fate, Redemption
