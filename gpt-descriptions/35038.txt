Synopsis: In 'The Carleton Case' by Ellery H. Clark, a renowned detective is called upon to solve a perplexing mystery surrounding the disappearance of a wealthy businessman. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects, all while racing against the clock to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Justice
