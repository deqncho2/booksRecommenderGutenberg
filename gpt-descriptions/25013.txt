Synopsis: In 'How to Make a Shoe' by John Parker Headley, a young aspiring shoemaker embarks on a journey of self-discovery and craftsmanship as he learns the intricate art of shoemaking from a mysterious master. As he delves deeper into the world of leather, tools, and techniques, he uncovers hidden secrets and challenges that push him to his limits. With each stitch and every choice, he not only creates a beautiful shoe but also finds himself on a transformative path towards passion, purpose, and the true meaning of craftsmanship.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Craftsmanship, Transformation
