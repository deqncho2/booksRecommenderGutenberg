Synopsis: 

Step into the vibrant and ever-changing city of Calcutta as Massey, Montague takes you on a captivating journey spanning over half a century. Through vivid recollections and personal anecdotes, the author paints a vivid picture of the city's rich history, its diverse culture, and the remarkable individuals who shaped its destiny. From the bustling streets and opulent palaces to the hidden corners and untold stories, this memoir offers a unique perspective on the city that has captured the hearts of many. Prepare to be transported to a world of enchantment, where the past intertwines with the present, and where the spirit of Calcutta comes alive.

Genres: Memoir, History, Travelogue

Themes: Cultural diversity, Historical significance, Personal experiences, City life
