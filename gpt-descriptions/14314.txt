Synopsis: In 'Etiquette' by Emily Post, readers are transported to the glamorous world of high society in early 20th century America. Through the eyes of the charming and witty protagonist, we witness the intricate dance of social interactions, where every gesture and word carries weight. As she navigates the treacherous waters of etiquette, our heroine must learn to balance tradition with her own desires, all while facing the scrutiny of her peers. With a delightful mix of humor, romance, and scandal, 'Etiquette' offers a captivating glimpse into a bygone era where manners were everything.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Social class, Gender roles, Personal identity
