Synopsis: In 'By Scarlet Torch and Blade' by Anthony Euwer, a young woman named Scarlet finds herself thrust into a world of danger and intrigue when she discovers a hidden power within herself. As she navigates a treacherous landscape filled with political conspiracies and ancient secrets, Scarlet must learn to harness her newfound abilities and make choices that will determine the fate of her world. With unexpected allies and formidable enemies at every turn, Scarlet's journey becomes a thrilling adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Power and responsibility, self-discovery, loyalty, overcoming adversity
