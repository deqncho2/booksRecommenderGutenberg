Synopsis: In 'The Room with the Little Door' by Molineux, Roland Burnham, a young woman named Emily discovers a hidden room in her ancestral home that holds a mysterious secret. As she delves deeper into the room's enigmatic history, Emily becomes entangled in a web of family secrets, dark magic, and a haunting presence that threatens her very existence. With each revelation, the stakes rise, and Emily must uncover the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Supernatural Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Dark magic, Haunting presence, Identity, Survival
