Synopsis: Bunny Brown and His Sister Sue are back with another exciting adventure! This time, they decide to create their very own circus in their backyard. With the help of their friends and family, Bunny and Sue transform their ordinary playtime into a spectacular show filled with daring acts, hilarious mishaps, and unexpected surprises. Join them as they navigate the challenges of putting on a circus and discover the true meaning of teamwork and imagination.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Family

Themes: Imagination, Creativity, Teamwork
