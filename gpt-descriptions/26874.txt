Synopsis: In 'Rock of Ages' by Augustus Toplady, a young man embarks on a journey of self-discovery and faith amidst the backdrop of a turbulent world. As he navigates through personal struggles and societal challenges, he finds solace and strength in an unexpected source. With captivating characters and a compelling narrative, this story takes readers on a thought-provoking exploration of life's uncertainties and the power of unwavering belief.

Genres: Fiction, Christian literature, Historical fiction

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Perseverance, Spiritual growth
