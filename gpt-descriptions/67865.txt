Synopsis: In 'The Samovar Girl' by Moore, Frederick Ferdinand, a young woman named Elena embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her mysterious past. Raised in a small village in Russia, Elena's life takes an unexpected turn when she discovers a hidden samovar, a traditional Russian tea urn, that holds clues to her identity. As she delves deeper into her family history, Elena finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and forbidden love. With each revelation, she becomes more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means risking everything she holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Family, Secrets, Love, Betrayal
