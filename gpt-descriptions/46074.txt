Synopsis: In 'Lausanne' by Francis Henry Gribble, a young Englishman finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue while visiting the picturesque city of Lausanne, Switzerland. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the city, he uncovers a hidden world of espionage, forbidden love, and dangerous alliances. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous landscape where nothing is as it seems, and trust becomes a luxury he cannot afford. Will he be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Espionage, Betrayal, Love and Deception
