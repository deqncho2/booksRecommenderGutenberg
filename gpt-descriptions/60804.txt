Synopsis: In 'Daddy Jake the Runaway, and Short Stories Told after Dark' by Joel Chandler Harris, readers are transported to the enchanting world of the American South, where Daddy Jake, a wise old slave, shares captivating tales with the children of the plantation. From thrilling adventures to heartwarming anecdotes, these stories are filled with colorful characters and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As the night unfolds, secrets are revealed, lessons are learned, and the power of storytelling becomes a source of hope and inspiration for all who listen.

Genres: Fiction, Folklore, Short Stories

Themes: The power of storytelling, the importance of tradition and oral history, the resilience of the human spirit
