Synopsis: In Story-land by Elizabeth Harrison takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of storytelling. As the protagonist embarks on a magical adventure, they encounter a host of fascinating characters and explore the power of imagination. With unexpected twists and turns, this tale will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what lies beyond the next storybook. 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Imagination, the Power of Stories, Exploration
