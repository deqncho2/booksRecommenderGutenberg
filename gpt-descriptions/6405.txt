Synopsis: 

'John Keble's Parishes: A History of Hursley and Otterbourne' by Charlotte M. Yonge takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of two English parishes. From the quaint village life to the influential figures who shaped these communities, this book unveils the hidden stories and secrets that have been passed down through generations. As the author delves into the lives of the villagers and the impact of John Keble, a renowned Anglican priest, readers will be enthralled by the intertwining tales of love, faith, and resilience that have shaped these parishes over the years.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Community, Faith, History, Tradition
