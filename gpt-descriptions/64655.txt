Synopsis: In 'Space-Trap at Banya Tor' by W. J. Matthews, a group of intergalactic explorers find themselves stranded on a mysterious planet, trapped in a deadly game of survival. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter strange creatures, tensions rise and alliances are tested. With time running out, they must unravel the secrets of Banya Tor and find a way back home before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Trust, Mystery
