Synopsis: 
The 21st Missouri Regiment Infantry Veteran Volunteers: Historical Memoranda takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of a group of soldiers during the American Civil War. Through vivid and detailed accounts, the book delves into the challenges, triumphs, and sacrifices faced by these brave men as they navigate the horrors of war and strive to uphold their duty. From the front lines to the home front, this historical memoir offers a unique perspective on the war that tore a nation apart.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, War

Themes: Duty and Honor, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Resilience
