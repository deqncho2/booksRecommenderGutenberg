Synopsis: 

In "Venice and Its Story" by Thomas Okey, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting city of Venice. From its humble beginnings as a marshy lagoon to its rise as a powerful maritime republic, this book unveils the rich history, art, and culture that have shaped this iconic city. Discover the secrets of the Venetian canals, the grandeur of St. Mark's Square, and the opulence of the Doge's Palace. As you delve into the pages, you will be transported to a world of gondolas, masked balls, and intricate architecture, all while unraveling the mysteries that lie beneath the surface of this captivating city.

Genres: Historical, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: History, Art, Culture, Architecture
