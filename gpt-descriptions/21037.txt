Synopsis: 
In 'The Cock-House at Fellsgarth' by Reed, Talbot Baines, a group of adventurous schoolboys find themselves embroiled in a mysterious and dangerous secret that threatens their very lives. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they uncover hidden treasures, encounter unexpected allies, and face formidable adversaries. With their courage and resourcefulness tested to the limit, the boys must navigate a treacherous path to unravel the truth and protect what they hold dear.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Loyalty, Discovery
