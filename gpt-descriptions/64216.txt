Synopsis: In 'Tad Coon's Tricks' by Breck, John, a mischievous young boy named Tad Coon discovers a hidden treasure map that leads him on an exhilarating adventure through the mysterious woods surrounding his small town. Along the way, Tad encounters peculiar characters, faces unexpected challenges, and learns valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the power of imagination. As he navigates through a series of clever tricks and daring escapades, Tad's journey becomes a thrilling and heartwarming tale that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Imagination
