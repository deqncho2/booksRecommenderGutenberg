Synopsis: In 'The Virgin in Judgment' by Eden Phillpotts, a small village is shaken by the arrival of a mysterious woman who claims to have the power to see into people's souls and pass judgment on their sins. As the villagers become increasingly intrigued and fearful of her abilities, they are forced to confront their own hidden secrets and desires. With tensions rising and the line between truth and illusion blurring, the fate of the village hangs in the balance.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Morality, Guilt, Redemption, Power of perception
