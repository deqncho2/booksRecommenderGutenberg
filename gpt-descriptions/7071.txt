Synopsis: In Times of Peril: A Tale of India is an enthralling historical adventure set in colonial India. The story follows the courageous journey of a young British officer, Percy Groves, who finds himself caught in the midst of a treacherous rebellion. As he navigates through the dangerous landscapes and encounters various factions vying for power, Percy must rely on his wit, bravery, and the help of unexpected allies to survive. With vivid descriptions of the Indian culture, breathtaking action sequences, and a captivating plot, this novel takes readers on a thrilling ride through a tumultuous period in India's history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Cultural Clash, Survival
