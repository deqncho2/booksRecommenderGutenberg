Synopsis: In "The History of Steam Navigation" by John Kennedy, embark on a captivating journey through time as the author delves into the fascinating evolution of steam-powered ships. From the humble beginnings of steamboats to the grandeur of ocean liners, Kennedy uncovers the triumphs, challenges, and groundbreaking innovations that shaped the course of maritime history. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, this book unveils the remarkable individuals, daring adventures, and technological advancements that propelled steam navigation to new heights. Prepare to be enthralled by tales of daring voyages, fierce competition, and the relentless pursuit of progress that forever changed the way we traverse the world's oceans.

Genres: History, Non-fiction, Maritime

Themes: Technological innovation, Exploration, Competition, Human ingenuity
