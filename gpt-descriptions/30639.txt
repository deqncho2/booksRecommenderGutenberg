Synopsis: In 'Border, Breed Nor Birth' by Mack Reynolds, a young man named Kenneth J. Malone finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and espionage. As an undercover agent, he must navigate a dangerous world where loyalty is tested and identities are constantly questioned. With his life on the line, Malone must uncover the truth and make difficult choices that will shape the future of his nation.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Political Fiction

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Political Manipulation
