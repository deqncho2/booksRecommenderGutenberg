Synopsis: In 'Apparitions and thought-transference: an examination of the evidence for telepathy', Frank Podmore delves into the fascinating world of telepathy, exploring the evidence and theories surrounding this mysterious phenomenon. Through a meticulous examination of apparitions and thought-transference, Podmore presents a compelling case for the existence of telepathic communication. As he delves deeper into the subject, he uncovers astonishing accounts and scientific studies that challenge conventional beliefs. This thought-provoking work invites readers to question the boundaries of human perception and the potential for unseen connections between minds.

Genres: Non-fiction, Psychology, Paranormal

Themes: Telepathy, Human Perception, Scientific Inquiry
