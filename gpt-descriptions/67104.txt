Synopsis: In "The A B C of Relativity," Bertrand Russell takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of relativity, unraveling complex scientific concepts in a clear and accessible manner. From the mind-bending theories of time dilation and space contraction to the mind-expanding implications of the theory of relativity, Russell's engaging explanations and thought-provoking examples will leave readers in awe of the wonders of the universe.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Relativity, Space and Time, Scientific Inquiry
