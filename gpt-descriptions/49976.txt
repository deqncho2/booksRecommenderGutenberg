Synopsis: 

'Escape from the Confederacy' by Benjamin F. Hasson is a gripping historical fiction novel set during the American Civil War. The story follows the journey of a young Union soldier, John, who finds himself trapped behind enemy lines after a fierce battle. Determined to return to his family and escape the clutches of the Confederacy, John embarks on a perilous adventure filled with danger, deception, and unexpected alliances. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters various characters, John's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to outsmart his pursuers and find his way back to freedom?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Bravery, Identity
