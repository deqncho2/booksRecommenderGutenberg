Synopsis: In the small town of Oakdale, a group of misfits find themselves entangled in a thrilling and mysterious adventure. When a gang of criminals invades the town, chaos ensues, and the lives of the townspeople are turned upside down. Among the chaos, a young girl named Bridge, a fugitive named The Oskaloosa Kid, and a circus performer named The Bareback Rider, must band together to uncover the truth behind the Oakdale Affair. As they navigate through danger and deception, they discover hidden secrets that will challenge their perceptions of justice and loyalty.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Crime

Themes: Justice, Loyalty, Identity
