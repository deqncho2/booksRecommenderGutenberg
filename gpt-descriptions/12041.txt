Synopsis: In 'The Shadow of the Cathedral' by Blasco Ibáñez, Vicente, a young artist, finds himself captivated by the grandeur and mystery of the Cathedral of Valencia. As he delves deeper into the secrets hidden within its walls, he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and ambition. Vicente's journey takes him through the vibrant streets of Valencia, where he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own hidden agendas. As he navigates the treacherous world of art and politics, Vicente must confront his own desires and confront the shadows that lurk within the cathedral's hallowed halls.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Art and creativity, Love and passion, Ambition and power, Secrets and betrayal
