Synopsis: In 'Musical Myths and Facts, Volume 2 (of 2)', Carl Engel delves into the fascinating world of music, uncovering hidden truths and debunking popular misconceptions. From the origins of famous compositions to the lives of renowned composers, Engel presents a captivating collection of stories and anecdotes that will leave readers amazed and enlightened. With his extensive knowledge and engaging writing style, Engel invites readers on a journey through the rich history of music, revealing surprising facts and dispelling long-held myths.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, History

Themes: Music history, Composers, Myth debunking
