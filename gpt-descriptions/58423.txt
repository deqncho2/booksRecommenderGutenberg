Synopsis: 
In 'Eight Girls and a Dog' by Carolyn Wells, a group of eight adventurous girls embark on a thrilling summer vacation at a charming seaside cottage. Their days are filled with laughter, mischief, and unexpected surprises, but when a mysterious dog enters their lives, their summer takes an unexpected turn. As they unravel the secrets surrounding the dog, the girls find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of adventure, friendship, and a quest for the truth. With each passing day, their bond grows stronger, and they discover the power of teamwork and determination. Will they be able to solve the mystery and uncover the dog's true identity? Join these eight girls on a heartwarming and captivating journey that will keep you guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Friendship

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Adventure, Mystery
