Synopsis: In "Spain from Within" by Shaw, Rafael, embark on a captivating journey through the heart of Spain, where secrets, passion, and cultural richness intertwine. Follow the protagonist as he delves into the hidden corners of this enchanting country, encountering mysterious characters and uncovering the untold stories that lie beneath the surface. From the vibrant streets of Madrid to the picturesque landscapes of Andalusia, this immersive narrative will transport you to a Spain that is both familiar and yet undiscovered.

Genres: Travel, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cultural identity, Secrets and hidden histories, Exploration of Spain's diverse regions
