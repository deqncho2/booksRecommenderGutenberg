Synopsis: 
'The Life of Joan of Arc, Vol. 1 and 2' by Anatole France is a captivating historical novel that delves into the extraordinary life of Joan of Arc, the legendary French heroine. Set in the 15th century, the story follows Joan's journey from a simple peasant girl to a fearless warrior and ultimately, a martyr. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, France brings to life the trials and triumphs of this iconic figure, exploring the complexities of her character and the turbulent times in which she lived.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Faith, Gender roles, Power dynamics
