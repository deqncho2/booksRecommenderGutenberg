Synopsis: 

In 'Types of Prose Narratives: A Text-Book for the Story Writer', Fansler, Harriott Ely takes readers on an enlightening journey through the various forms of prose narratives. From the captivating world of fairy tales to the gripping realm of mystery and suspense, this text-book delves into the intricacies of storytelling, offering invaluable insights and techniques for aspiring writers. With vivid examples and engaging analysis, Fansler explores the art of crafting compelling characters, building suspenseful plots, and creating immersive settings. Whether you're a seasoned writer or a novice storyteller, this book is sure to inspire and enhance your narrative skills, leaving you eager to embark on your own literary adventures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Writing, Reference

Themes: Storytelling techniques, Character development, Plot construction
