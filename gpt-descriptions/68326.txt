Synopsis: In "History of Electric Light," Schroeder, Henry takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the fascinating evolution of one of humanity's most transformative inventions. From the humble beginnings of flickering candles to the groundbreaking discoveries of inventors like Thomas Edison and Nikola Tesla, this book unveils the untold stories behind the development of electric light. Delving into the triumphs, setbacks, and rivalries that shaped its history, Schroeder's narrative illuminates the profound impact of electric light on society, culture, and the human experience. Prepare to be enlightened by this captivating exploration of a revolutionary invention that forever changed the way we see the world.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Invention and innovation, Technological advancements, Societal impact
