Synopsis: In a dystopian future, a group of individuals find themselves trapped in a seemingly perfect world controlled by an advanced technology known as the Defenders. As they begin to question the nature of their reality, they must navigate a web of deception and uncover the truth behind their existence. With their lives at stake, they must decide whether to conform or fight for their freedom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Control and Manipulation, Rebellion against Authority
