Synopsis: 
In 'Angel Esquire' by Wallace Edgar, a young lawyer named Robert Anstruther finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he takes on a seemingly straightforward case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his own life but also the lives of those he holds dear. With his quick wit and determination, Robert must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to outsmart a cunning adversary. Will he be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Justice, Loyalty
