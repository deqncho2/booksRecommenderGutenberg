Synopsis: In "The Memoirs of Jacques Casanova de Seingalt, 1725-1798. Volume 22: to London," the infamous adventurer and seducer, Jacques Casanova, finds himself in the vibrant city of London. As he navigates the opulent society and indulges in his amorous pursuits, Casanova becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and scandal. With his wit, charm, and insatiable appetite for pleasure, he encounters a cast of fascinating characters, from aristocrats to artists, and experiences the highs and lows of his captivating escapades. This volume offers a captivating glimpse into Casanova's life as he explores the bustling streets of London, leaving readers eager to uncover the secrets and surprises that await him.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and seduction, Society and class, Intrigue and scandal
