Synopsis: In "Time out for Redheads" by Miriam Allen De Ford, a group of redheaded women find themselves transported to a mysterious and enchanting world where their unique hair color holds extraordinary powers. As they navigate this new realm, they must confront unexpected challenges and uncover the truth behind their extraordinary abilities. With danger lurking at every turn, the redheads must band together and embrace their individual strengths to save themselves and the world they now call home.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Friendship, Self-discovery
