Synopsis: Four orphaned siblings, Henry, Jessie, Violet, and Benny, find themselves living in an abandoned boxcar in the woods. Determined to stay together and make a home for themselves, they embark on a thrilling adventure filled with mystery, friendship, and unexpected surprises. As they uncover clues and face challenges, the children's resourcefulness and resilience are put to the test, leading them to discover the true meaning of family and the power of love and determination.

Genres: Children's literature, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Family, Friendship, Resilience, Independence
