Synopsis: In 'The Sun's Babies' by Edith Howes, a mysterious event causes the sun to disappear, plunging the world into darkness. As society struggles to adapt to this new reality, a group of children discover a hidden power within themselves that may hold the key to restoring light to the world. With their newfound abilities, they embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the sun's disappearance and bring hope back to humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Power of imagination, Resilience in the face of adversity, Importance of unity
