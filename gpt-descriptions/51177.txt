Synopsis: In 'Legends of Lancashire' by Peter Landreth, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey to uncover the hidden secrets of her family's ancestral home in the enchanting region of Lancashire. As she delves deeper into the history of the land, she becomes entangled in a web of ancient legends, mystical creatures, and long-lost treasures. With each discovery, Emily's perception of reality is challenged, and she must navigate a world where folklore and reality intertwine. As she unravels the mysteries of Lancashire, Emily's own identity and destiny become intertwined with the legends that have shaped the land for centuries.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family heritage, Mythology, Identity, Adventure
