Synopsis: 
In 'Historic Ghosts and Ghost Hunters', Bruce H. Addington takes readers on a captivating journey through history, exploring the chilling tales of haunted locations and the brave individuals who dared to investigate them. From ancient castles to abandoned asylums, Addington delves into the eerie encounters and supernatural phenomena that have left their mark on these places. With a blend of historical research and spine-tingling storytelling, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of ghosts and the relentless pursuit of those who seek to uncover their mysteries.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Paranormal

Themes: Ghosts, Hauntings, Supernatural, Investigation, History
