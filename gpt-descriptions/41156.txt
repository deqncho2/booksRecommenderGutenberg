Synopsis: 

In the eleventh edition of the renowned Encyclopaedia Britannica, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of Japan. From its rich history and cultural traditions to its breathtaking landscapes and technological advancements, this section of the encyclopedia offers a comprehensive exploration of the Land of the Rising Sun. Delve into the intricate details of Japanese art, literature, religion, and politics, as well as its unique social structure and economic development. Discover the captivating stories of influential figures and pivotal events that have shaped Japan into the nation it is today. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, History

Themes: Japanese culture, History, Art, Religion, Politics, Technology
