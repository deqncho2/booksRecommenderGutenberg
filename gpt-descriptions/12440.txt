Synopsis: In 'D'Ri and I: A Tale of Daring Deeds in the Second War with the British: Being the Memoirs of Colonel Ramon Bell, U.S.A.', author Irving Bacheller takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the tumultuous events of the Second War with the British. The story follows the adventures of Colonel Ramon Bell and his loyal companion, D'Ri, as they navigate the perils of war, engage in daring acts of bravery, and forge unbreakable bonds of friendship. From thrilling battles to heart-wrenching sacrifices, this gripping tale immerses readers in a world of danger, honor, and resilience.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Bravery, Friendship, Loyalty, Sacrifice
