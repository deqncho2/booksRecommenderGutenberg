Synopsis: In 'The Rising Tide' by Margaret Wade Campbell Deland, a small New England town is shaken by the arrival of a mysterious stranger who brings with him a wave of change and upheaval. As the townspeople grapple with their own secrets and desires, they are forced to confront the consequences of their actions and the power of redemption. With vivid characters and a gripping narrative, this novel explores the complexities of human nature and the transformative power of forgiveness.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Secrets, Transformation
