Synopsis: 
'Their Wedding Journey' by William Dean Howells follows the newlywed couple, Kenyon and Ellen, as they embark on a journey through Europe for their honeymoon. However, their journey takes an unexpected turn when they encounter various challenges and cultural differences that test their relationship and force them to confront their own beliefs and expectations. As they navigate through the complexities of love, marriage, and societal norms, Kenyon and Ellen must find a way to strengthen their bond and discover the true meaning of their wedding journey.

Genres: Romance, Travel, Realistic Fiction

Themes: Love and Marriage, Cultural Differences, Self-discovery
