Synopsis: 
"The Analects of Confucius" is a collection of teachings and conversations of the renowned Chinese philosopher, Confucius. Through a series of anecdotes and dialogues, Confucius imparts his wisdom on various aspects of life, including ethics, governance, education, and personal conduct. This timeless work offers profound insights into the principles of Confucianism and provides guidance on how to lead a virtuous and harmonious life.

Genres: Philosophy, Classics, Non-fiction

Themes: Ethics, Education, Governance, Personal Conduct, Virtue
