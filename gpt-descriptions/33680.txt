Synopsis: In 'Highway Pirates; or, The Secret Place at Coverthorne' by Avery, Harold, a group of friends stumble upon a hidden world of adventure and danger when they uncover a secret place along a desolate highway. As they delve deeper into this mysterious realm, they find themselves entangled in a thrilling quest that involves ancient treasures, hidden identities, and a notorious band of pirates. With their lives on the line, the friends must navigate treacherous waters and outsmart their adversaries to uncover the truth and secure their own survival.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Discovery, Courage, Identity
