Synopsis: In 'Windows' by John Galsworthy, a chance encounter between two strangers in a train compartment leads to a captivating story of love, longing, and the complexities of human relationships. As the characters navigate their way through the intricacies of their own lives, they find solace and connection in the windows that offer glimpses into the world beyond. With each window representing a different perspective, the story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, leaving the reader eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the glass.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love, Loneliness, Perception, Human connection
