Synopsis: In 'The Green Mummy' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious mummy is discovered in an ancient Egyptian tomb, setting off a chain of events that lead to a thrilling investigation. As the secrets of the mummy's origins and its connection to a wealthy English family unfold, the reader is taken on a captivating journey filled with suspense, romance, and unexpected twists. With each clue uncovered, the tension builds, leaving the reader eager to unravel the truth behind the enigmatic green mummy.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Ancient Egyptian mythology and culture, Family legacies and inheritance
