Synopsis: In "Once on a Time," A. A. Milne takes readers on a whimsical journey to a kingdom where fairy tales come to life. When a young princess is kidnapped, a brave prince sets out on a quest to rescue her, encountering enchanting creatures and magical adventures along the way. This delightful tale explores the power of imagination, the importance of love and friendship, and the timeless allure of fairy tales.

Genres: Fantasy, Children's Literature, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Love, Friendship, Fairy Tales
