Synopsis: In 'Historical Parallels, vol. 2 of 3)', Arthur Thomas Malkin takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring intriguing connections between past and present. From ancient civilizations to modern societies, Malkin uncovers surprising parallels that shed light on the human experience. As he delves into the annals of history, he reveals how the actions and choices of our ancestors continue to shape our world today. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, this volume offers a thought-provoking exploration of the intricate tapestry of human history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Comparative history, Cultural analysis

Themes: Historical cycles, Human nature, Legacy of the past
