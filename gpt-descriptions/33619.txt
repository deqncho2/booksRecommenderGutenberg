Synopsis: 

In 'Maori and Settler: A Story of The New Zealand War', G. A. Henty takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of the New Zealand War. Set against the backdrop of the stunning New Zealand landscape, this gripping historical novel follows the lives of two young protagonists, a Maori warrior and a British settler, as their paths intertwine amidst the chaos and conflict of war. As they navigate the complexities of loyalty, friendship, and cultural differences, their fates become entwined in a struggle for survival and understanding. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Cultural clash, Loyalty, Friendship, Survival
