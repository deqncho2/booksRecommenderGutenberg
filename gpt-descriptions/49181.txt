Synopsis: In 'The Fool: A Play in Four Acts' by Pollock, Channing, a mysterious and enigmatic fool arrives at the court of a powerful king, bringing with him a series of thought-provoking riddles and challenging the status quo. As the fool's presence disrupts the carefully constructed order of the court, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to a dramatic climax that will leave the audience questioning the nature of power, truth, and the role of the fool in society.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Mystery

Themes: Power dynamics, Truth and deception, Social commentary
