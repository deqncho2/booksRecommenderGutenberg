Synopsis: 
'Stalky & Co.' by Rudyard Kipling is a captivating coming-of-age novel set in a British boarding school. The story follows the mischievous adventures of three cunning and resourceful boys, known as Stalky, M'Turk, and Beetle, as they navigate the challenges of school life and form an unconventional alliance. With their sharp wit and unconventional methods, the trio becomes a force to be reckoned with, challenging the authority of their teachers and outsmarting their peers. As they navigate the complexities of friendship, loyalty, and the pursuit of knowledge, Stalky & Co. offers a thrilling and humorous glimpse into the world of adolescence.

Genres: 
Adventure, Coming-of-age, School

Themes: 
Friendship, Rebellion, Intellectual curiosity
