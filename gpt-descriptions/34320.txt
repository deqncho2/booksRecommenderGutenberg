Synopsis: In 'Project Daedalus' by Thomas Hoover, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Robert Barnes embarks on a groundbreaking project that could change the course of human history. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a web of secrets and conspiracies that threaten not only his work but also his life. With time running out, Dr. Barnes must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to protect his revolutionary creation from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Scientific advancement, Conspiracy, Moral dilemmas
