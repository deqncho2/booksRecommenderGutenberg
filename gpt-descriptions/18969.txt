Synopsis: 
'Platform Monologues' by Tucker, T. G. is a captivating collection of monologues that takes readers on a journey through the lives of various individuals who find themselves at a pivotal moment on a train platform. Each monologue offers a unique perspective, delving into the depths of human emotions, desires, and struggles. From a heartbroken lover seeking closure to a weary traveler contemplating the meaning of life, these monologues provide a glimpse into the inner workings of the human psyche. As the characters share their stories, readers are left pondering the complexities of human existence and the power of connection.

Genres: Drama, Contemporary Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Human connection, Identity, Loneliness, Reflection, Emotional turmoil
