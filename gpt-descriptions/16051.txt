Synopsis: In 'The Voice in the Fog' by MacGrath, Harold, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious voice begins to haunt the city streets. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden identities that threaten to unravel the very fabric of society. With danger lurking at every corner, the detective must race against time to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Power
