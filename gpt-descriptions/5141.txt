Synopsis: Katy Carr is excited to start a new chapter of her life as she heads off to boarding school. However, she soon realizes that school is not all fun and games. From navigating friendships and rivalries to facing unexpected challenges, Katy must learn to adapt and grow in this new environment. Will she be able to find her place and discover the true meaning of friendship?

Genres: Children's literature, Coming-of-age, School story

Themes: Friendship, Growth and self-discovery, Overcoming challenges
