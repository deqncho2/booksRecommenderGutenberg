Synopsis: In "Down the Ravine" by Charles Egbert Craddock, a young woman named Dorinda finds herself caught between two worlds as she navigates the rugged terrain of the Tennessee mountains. When tragedy strikes her family, Dorinda must summon her strength and resilience to face the challenges that lie ahead. As she embarks on a journey filled with danger, love, and self-discovery, Dorinda's determination to protect her loved ones becomes a driving force that will test her in ways she never imagined.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Survival, Family, Resilience
