Synopsis: 
In 'Dorothy Dale's Camping Days' by Margaret Penrose, Dorothy and her friends embark on an exciting camping adventure filled with mystery and unexpected encounters. As they explore the great outdoors, they stumble upon a hidden treasure map that leads them on a thrilling quest. Along the way, they encounter strange characters, face dangerous situations, and uncover secrets that will test their courage and friendship. Will Dorothy and her friends be able to solve the mystery and find the hidden treasure? Join them on this captivating camping journey to find out!

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
