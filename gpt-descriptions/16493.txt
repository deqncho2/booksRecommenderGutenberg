Synopsis: 
"The Man Without a Country" follows the gripping story of a young American army officer who is sentenced to spend the rest of his life aboard naval vessels, never to set foot on American soil again. As he navigates the challenges of his unique predicament, the reader is taken on a thought-provoking journey that explores the power of patriotism, the consequences of one's actions, and the true meaning of belonging.

Genres: Historical fiction, Political fiction, Short story

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Identity, Consequences
