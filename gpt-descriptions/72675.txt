Synopsis: In 'The Amateur Crime' by Anthony Berkeley, a seemingly ordinary man stumbles upon a mysterious crime and becomes entangled in a web of deceit and danger. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he discovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel his own life. With unexpected twists and a relentless pursuit of the truth, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Consequences
