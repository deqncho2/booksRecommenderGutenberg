Synopsis: 
In 'Pilgrimages to the Spas in Pursuit of Health and Recreation', James Johnson takes readers on a captivating journey through various mineral spas, exploring their comparative merits and the ailments they can alleviate or worsen. With a keen eye for detail, Johnson delves into the world of health tourism, uncovering the secrets behind these popular destinations. From the bustling crowds seeking rejuvenation to the tranquil retreats offering solace, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the therapeutic powers of mineral waters.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Travel, Health

Themes: 
Health and wellness, Tourism, Comparative analysis
