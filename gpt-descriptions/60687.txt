Synopsis: 
"The Magician's Own Book, or, the Whole Art of Conjuring" is a comprehensive guide to the world of magic and illusion. Filled with over one thousand experiments, tricks, and puzzles, this book offers a captivating journey into the realm of parlor magic. From optical illusions to chemical reactions, mechanical wonders to magnetic phenomena, and of course, the art of sleight of hand, this book covers it all. Readers will be enthralled by the astonishing tricks, mind-boggling transformations, and celebrated card deceptions presented within its pages. With a focus on modern performers and their most noted tricks, this book is a treasure trove for both aspiring magicians and curious enthusiasts.

Genres: Non-fiction, Magic, Entertainment

Themes: Illusion, Mystery, Skill
