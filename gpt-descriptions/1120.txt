Synopsis: In the midst of political turmoil and conspiracy, the renowned Roman general Julius Caesar finds himself at the center of a power struggle. As his closest allies and friends grapple with their loyalty and ambition, the fate of Rome hangs in the balance. Shakespeare's masterful tragedy explores the consequences of unchecked ambition and the complexities of honor, loyalty, and betrayal.

Genres: Tragedy, Historical Drama

Themes: Ambition, Power, Loyalty, Betrayal
