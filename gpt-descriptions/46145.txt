Synopsis: In 'The Book of Princes and Princesses' by Lang, Mrs., embark on a captivating journey through enchanting tales of royalty from various cultures. From brave princes and clever princesses to magical creatures and treacherous villains, this collection of stories will transport readers to a world filled with adventure, love, and unexpected twists. Discover the secrets of a hidden kingdom, witness the power of true friendship, and unravel the mysteries of ancient legends. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the timeless tales that have been passed down through generations.

Genres: Fairy Tales, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Love, Friendship, Morality
