Synopsis: In Volume 29 of his captivating memoirs, Jacques Casanova takes us on a thrilling journey from the enchanting city of Florence to the vibrant port of Trieste. As he navigates through the intricate web of Italian society, Casanova encounters a cast of fascinating characters, from seductive courtesans to powerful nobles, each with their own secrets and desires. Amidst the backdrop of political intrigue and forbidden love, Casanova's insatiable appetite for adventure and pleasure leads him down a path filled with danger and unexpected twists. Will he be able to outwit his enemies and find the ultimate fulfillment he seeks?

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Class and Power, Deception and Intrigue
