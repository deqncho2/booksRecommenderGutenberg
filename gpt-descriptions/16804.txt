Synopsis: In 'An Eye for an Eye' by Anthony Trollope, a young woman named Lucy Morris finds herself caught in a web of love, betrayal, and revenge. As she navigates the complexities of her relationships with two very different men, Lucy must confront the consequences of her choices and the power of her own desires. Set against the backdrop of Victorian society, this gripping tale explores the lengths people will go to in order to protect their own interests and seek justice.

Genres: Victorian literature, romance, drama

Themes: Love and desire, betrayal, revenge, societal expectations
