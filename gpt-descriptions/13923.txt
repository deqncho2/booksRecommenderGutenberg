Synopsis: 

Step into the world of the White House and discover the secrets of its culinary delights with "The Whitehouse Cookbook (1887)". This comprehensive compendium of recipes, household tips, and etiquette advice offers a fascinating glimpse into the inner workings of the presidential residence. From elegant dinner parties to caring for the sick, this book covers it all, providing a wealth of information for the home. Delve into the pages and uncover the hidden treasures of the White House kitchen, while exploring the art of hosting and maintaining a well-run household.

Genres: Cookbook, Historical Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Culinary traditions, Home management, Etiquette
