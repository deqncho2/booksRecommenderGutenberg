Synopsis: In 'The Dragon's Secret' by Augusta Huiell Seaman, a young girl named Doris stumbles upon a mysterious old house while exploring her new neighborhood. Intrigued by the rumors of hidden treasure, Doris embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets of the house and its enigmatic owner. As she delves deeper into the house's past, Doris discovers a connection to a legendary dragon and a long-lost family legacy. With danger lurking around every corner, Doris must unravel the clues and solve the puzzle before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Treasure hunting, Courage, Friendship
