Synopsis: 
In 'Dave Darrin's First Year at Annapolis', readers are introduced to Dave Darrin and Dan Dalzell, two young men who have just entered the prestigious United States Naval Academy. As they navigate the challenges of their first year as plebe midshipmen, they encounter strict discipline, intense physical training, and the pressures of academic excellence. Along the way, they form lasting friendships, face unexpected obstacles, and learn valuable lessons about honor, loyalty, and perseverance. Will Dave and Dan be able to overcome the trials of their first year and prove themselves worthy of becoming future naval officers?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Military

Themes: Honor, Loyalty, Perseverance, Friendship, Discipline
