Synopsis: 

In 'The Martian' by Glasser, Allen/ Hilliard, A. R. (Alec Rowley), astronaut Mark Watney finds himself stranded alone on Mars after a mission goes awry. With limited supplies and no way to communicate with Earth, Watney must rely on his resourcefulness and ingenuity to survive in the harsh and unforgiving environment of the red planet. As he faces numerous challenges and obstacles, Watney's determination to stay alive and find a way back home becomes a gripping tale of human resilience and the indomitable spirit of exploration.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Survival

Themes: Resilience, Ingenuity, Exploration
