Synopsis: 
In 'Anecdotes of Painters, Engravers, Sculptors and Architects, and Curiosities of Art (Vol. 3 of 3)', Spooner, Shearjashub takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and works of renowned artists, revealing intriguing anecdotes and curiosities that will leave art enthusiasts spellbound. From the eccentricities of famous painters to the untold stories behind iconic sculptures, this volume offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of art and the creative minds that shaped it.

Genres: Biography, Art History, Anecdotes

Themes: Artistic eccentricities, Creative process, Historical context
