Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Hawk-Hollow, a small Pennsylvania town, a centuries-old tradition is about to be challenged. As the annual Hawk Festival approaches, young Ethan discovers a hidden secret that could change everything. With the help of his newfound friends, he embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the hawks that soar above Hawk-Hollow. Along the way, they encounter mysterious clues, ancient legends, and unexpected dangers that test their courage and loyalty. Will they be able to protect the tradition and preserve the magic of Hawk-Hollow?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Tradition, Friendship, Discovery
