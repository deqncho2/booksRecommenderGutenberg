Synopsis: 
The Circular Staircase by Mary Roberts Rinehart is a thrilling mystery that follows Rachel Innes, a middle-aged woman who rents a country house for a peaceful summer getaway. However, her plans are quickly disrupted when a series of strange and sinister events begin to unfold. From unexplained noises in the night to a murder that takes place right under her nose, Rachel finds herself caught in a web of secrets and danger. With the help of her quick-witted maid and a determined detective, she must unravel the mysteries surrounding the house and its inhabitants before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Perseverance
