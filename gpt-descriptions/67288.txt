Synopsis: In 'The Right Thing' by Cummings, Ray, a young woman named Emma finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter that leads her on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's past. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Emma must confront her own fears and make difficult choices that could change her life forever. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and Emma must decide if she is willing to sacrifice everything for what she believes is the right thing to do.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Family secrets, Morality, Personal sacrifice
