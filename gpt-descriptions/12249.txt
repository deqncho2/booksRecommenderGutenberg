Synopsis: In 'Bart Ridgeley: A Story of Northern Ohio' by A. G. Riddle, readers are transported to the picturesque landscapes of Northern Ohio, where they follow the captivating journey of Bart Ridgeley. Born into a humble family, Bart's life takes an unexpected turn when he discovers a hidden secret that unravels a web of mystery and intrigue. As he delves deeper into the enigma, Bart finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of power and deception, where every decision he makes could have far-reaching consequences. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Power and deception, Coming of age
