Synopsis: 
In 'Frost's Laws and By-Laws of American Society', author S. Annie Frost presents a condensed yet comprehensive guide to etiquette in America. This treatise offers plain and reliable directions for proper deportment in every situation one may encounter in life. From social gatherings to business meetings, Frost's work provides invaluable insights into the intricacies of American society and the expectations placed upon individuals. With a focus on practical advice and clear instructions, this treatise is sure to captivate readers seeking to navigate the complexities of American etiquette.

Genres: Non-fiction, Etiquette, Self-help

Themes: Etiquette, Social norms, Cultural expectations
