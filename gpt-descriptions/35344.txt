Synopsis: 

The 'Third Biennial Report of the Oregon State Highway Commission' takes readers on a captivating journey through the development and progress of Oregon's highways from December 1916 to November 1918. This report, compiled by Benson, Simon, Booth, R. A., Nunn, Herbert, and Thompson, W. L., offers a comprehensive overview of the state's transportation infrastructure during a crucial period in history. From the challenges faced during World War I to the innovative solutions implemented to improve road conditions, this report provides a fascinating glimpse into the evolution of Oregon's highways.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Transportation

Themes: Infrastructure development, Progress, World War I impact
