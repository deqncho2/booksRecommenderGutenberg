Synopsis: In "Robert Burns" by Setoun, Gabriel, a young and ambitious poet, embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic life of the legendary Scottish poet, Robert Burns. As Gabriel delves deeper into Burns' past, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and unrequited love. With each revelation, Gabriel's own perception of love, fame, and the pursuit of artistic greatness is challenged, leading him to question the sacrifices one must make for their art.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Artistic Pursuit, Identity and Authenticity
