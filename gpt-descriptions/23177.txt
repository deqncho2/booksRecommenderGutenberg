Synopsis: In 'The Inmate Of The Dungeon: 1894' by Morrow, W. C., a mysterious figure finds himself trapped in a dark and foreboding dungeon, with no recollection of how he got there or who he is. As he navigates the treacherous depths of the dungeon, he encounters strange creatures, uncovers hidden secrets, and begins to question his own sanity. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the enigmatic world of the dungeon, where nothing is as it seems and escape seems impossible.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Memory, Madness
