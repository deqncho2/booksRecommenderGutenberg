Synopsis: In 'The Tiger Lily' by George Manville Fenn, a young girl named Lily finds herself caught in a thrilling adventure when she stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. With the help of her loyal dog, Tiger, Lily embarks on a dangerous journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As she navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters mysterious characters, Lily must summon her courage and resourcefulness to unravel the secrets of the map and uncover the truth behind the treasure. Will Lily and Tiger succeed in their quest, or will they fall victim to the perils that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
