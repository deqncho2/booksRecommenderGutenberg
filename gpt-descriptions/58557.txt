Synopsis:
In the second volume of her autobiography, Miss Cornelia Knight continues to recount her fascinating experiences as the lady companion to Princess Charlotte of Wales. Through extracts from her journals and anecdote books, she offers a captivating glimpse into the inner workings of the royal court and the tumultuous life of the princess. From scandalous affairs to political intrigues, Miss Knight's narrative is filled with captivating anecdotes and personal reflections that shed light on the complexities of life in the early 19th century. As she navigates the challenges of her position and witnesses the highs and lows of Princess Charlotte's life, Miss Knight's story becomes a compelling portrait of a remarkable era.

Genres: Autobiography, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Royal life, Court politics, Personal relationships, Historical events
