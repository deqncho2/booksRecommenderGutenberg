Synopsis: 
'By the Way: Travel Letters Written During Several Journeys Abroad' takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of Agness Greene Foster, a passionate traveler and keen observer of the world. Through a collection of beautifully written letters, Foster shares her experiences, encounters, and reflections from various destinations across the globe. From the bustling streets of Paris to the serene landscapes of Japan, each letter unveils a unique perspective on the people, cultures, and landscapes encountered along the way. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt anecdotes, this book offers a delightful escape into the wonders of travel.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Epistolary

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Personal growth
