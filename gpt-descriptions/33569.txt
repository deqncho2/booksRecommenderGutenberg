Synopsis: In the remote town of Kastle Krags, a series of mysterious events unfold, leaving the residents bewildered and on edge. When a young journalist named Martin arrives to investigate, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, hidden treasures, and ancient curses. As he delves deeper into the town's dark history, Martin discovers that nothing is as it seems, and the truth may be more terrifying than he ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, the power of the past, the thin line between reality and the supernatural
