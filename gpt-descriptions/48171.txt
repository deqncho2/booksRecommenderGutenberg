Synopsis: In "The tragical acts, or comical tragedies of Punch and Judy," the reader is taken on a wild and unpredictable journey through the misadventures of the iconic puppet characters, Punch and Judy. From their hilarious antics to their dark and unexpected turns, this book offers a unique blend of comedy and tragedy that will keep readers hooked until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Tragedy, Puppetry

Themes: Morality, Violence, Absurdity
