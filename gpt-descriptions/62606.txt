Synopsis: In 'A Mirror of the Turf; Or, The Machinery of Horse-Racing Revealed, Showing the Sport of Kings as It Is To-Day', author James Glass Bertram takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of horse-racing. Through a captivating narrative, the book uncovers the intricate workings of this beloved sport, revealing the hidden secrets and scandals that lie beneath its glamorous surface. As readers delve into the pages, they will be immersed in a world of high-stakes gambling, fierce competition, and the relentless pursuit of victory. Bertram's vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the sport of kings.

Genres: Sports, Mystery, Non-fiction

Themes: Gambling, Competition, Scandal, Secrets
