Synopsis: 
In 'Evolution' by Edward Hoare, a groundbreaking scientific discovery sets off a chain of events that challenges everything we thought we knew about the origins of life. When a team of researchers stumbles upon a mysterious artifact buried deep within the Earth, they unwittingly unleash a force that threatens to reshape the very fabric of existence. As they race against time to unravel the secrets hidden within this enigmatic object, they find themselves caught in a web of intrigue, danger, and ethical dilemmas. With each revelation, the boundaries between science, religion, and humanity blur, forcing the characters to confront their own beliefs and the consequences of their actions. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Evolution, Ethics, Identity, Religion, Scientific Discovery
