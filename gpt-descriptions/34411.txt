Synopsis: In "Pipistrellus cinnamomeus Miller 1902 Referred to the Genus Myotis," renowned zoologists E. Raymond Hall and Walter Woelber Dalquest embark on a captivating exploration of a mysterious bat species. As they delve into the intricacies of its classification and behavior, they uncover surprising connections and challenge long-held assumptions. This scientific journey takes readers on a thrilling quest to unravel the secrets of this enigmatic creature, leading to unexpected discoveries that will leave them eager for more.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural history, Zoology

Themes: Taxonomy, Evolution, Scientific discovery
