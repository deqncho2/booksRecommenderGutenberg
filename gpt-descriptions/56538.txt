Synopsis: In "The Naughty Man; or, Sir Thomas Brown: Love, Courtship and Marriage in High Life. A Poetical Satire," Frank Chapman Bliss takes readers on a satirical journey through the world of love, courtship, and marriage in high society. The story revolves around the misadventures of Sir Thomas Brown, a charming yet mischievous character who navigates the complexities of relationships with wit and humor. As the plot unfolds, readers are treated to a delightful exploration of the foibles and follies of the upper class, with unexpected twists and turns that keep them engaged until the very end.

Genres: Satire, Poetry

Themes: Love, Courtship, Marriage, Social Class, Humor
