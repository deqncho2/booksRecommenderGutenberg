Synopsis: In 'The Mercer Boys in the Ghost Patrol', the adventurous Mercer brothers find themselves in the midst of a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon a hidden treasure map. As they embark on a quest to uncover the secrets of the map, they encounter eerie ghostly figures, dangerous traps, and unexpected allies. With their courage and resourcefulness, the Mercer boys must navigate through a series of thrilling escapades to solve the mystery and claim the treasure. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Brotherhood, Courage, Treasure Hunting
