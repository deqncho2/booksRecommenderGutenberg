Synopsis: In 'Grenfell: Knight-Errant of the North', follow the captivating journey of Sir Wilfred Grenfell, a courageous and compassionate doctor who embarks on a mission to bring hope and healing to the remote communities of Newfoundland and Labrador. From treacherous icy landscapes to heartwarming encounters with the locals, Grenfell's determination and selflessness will inspire readers as he faces numerous challenges in his quest to improve the lives of those in need.

Genres: Biography, Adventure, Historical

Themes: Courage, Compassion, Resilience, Social Justice
