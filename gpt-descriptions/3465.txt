Synopsis: 
'Under Two Flags' by Ouida is a captivating novel set in the 19th century, following the life of a young Englishman named Bertie Cecil. Tired of his privileged but suffocating life in England, Bertie abandons his aristocratic roots and joins the French Foreign Legion, seeking adventure, freedom, and a chance to prove himself. As he immerses himself in the harsh realities of military life, Bertie encounters a colorful cast of characters, including a mysterious beauty named Cigarette, who challenges his beliefs and ignites a passionate desire within him. Amidst the backdrop of war, honor, and sacrifice, Bertie must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and self-discovery, ultimately facing a choice that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Freedom, Love and Sacrifice
