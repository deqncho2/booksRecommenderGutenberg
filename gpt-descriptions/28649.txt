Synopsis: 

In 'The Letters of Queen Victoria: A Selection from Her Majesty's Correspondence between the Years 1837 and 1861. Volume 3, 1854-1861', readers are granted a rare glimpse into the personal and political life of Queen Victoria during a crucial period in British history. This volume covers the years 1854 to 1861, a time marked by significant events such as the Crimean War, the Indian Rebellion, and the rise of the British Empire. Through Queen Victoria's intimate letters, we witness her struggles, triumphs, and the profound impact she had on the nation.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction, Epistolary

Themes: Monarchy, Leadership, War, Empire Building, Personal Relationships
