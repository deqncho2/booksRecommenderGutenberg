Synopsis: 

'Brave and True: Short stories for children by G. M. Fenn and Others' is a captivating collection of short stories that will transport young readers into a world of adventure, courage, and imagination. From thrilling tales of daring rescues to heartwarming stories of friendship and loyalty, this book offers a delightful mix of excitement and life lessons. Each story introduces young protagonists who face various challenges and must summon their bravery and integrity to overcome them. With vivid storytelling and relatable characters, 'Brave and True' is sure to captivate young readers and leave them eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature, Short Stories

Themes: Bravery, Friendship, Loyalty, Morality
