Synopsis: In "James VI and the Gowrie Mystery," Andrew Lang delves into a captivating historical event that unfolds in the court of King James VI of Scotland. The story revolves around a mysterious incident involving the young Earl of Gowrie and his brother, which leads to a web of intrigue, conspiracy, and royal secrets. As Lang unravels the enigma, he takes readers on a thrilling journey through the corridors of power, where loyalty, ambition, and betrayal intertwine in a deadly game of cat and mouse.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Biography

Themes: Power and politics, Loyalty and betrayal
