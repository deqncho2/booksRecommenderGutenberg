Synopsis: In 'Wongo and the Wise Old Crow' by Moon, Carl and Moon, Grace, a young boy named Wongo embarks on a magical adventure when he encounters a wise old crow who possesses extraordinary powers. As Wongo learns to navigate the challenges of his journey, he discovers the true meaning of friendship, courage, and the importance of embracing one's unique abilities. With captivating storytelling and enchanting illustrations, this heartwarming tale will transport readers to a world where imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
