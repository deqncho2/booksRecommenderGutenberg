Synopsis: In 'Warning from the Stars' by Ron Cocking, a renowned astrophysicist receives a mysterious message from an extraterrestrial civilization, warning of an impending catastrophe that could wipe out humanity. As he delves deeper into the message's meaning, he uncovers a web of secrets and conspiracies that threaten not only his own life but the fate of the entire planet. With time running out, he must race against the clock to decipher the message and find a way to save humanity from its own destruction.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Extraterrestrial communication, Conspiracy, Survival
