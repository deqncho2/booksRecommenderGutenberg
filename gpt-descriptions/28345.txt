Synopsis: In 'Somehow Good' by William De Morgan, a young man named George escapes a scandalous past and embarks on a journey to reinvent himself. Set in Victorian England, George finds himself entangled in a web of love, secrets, and unexpected twists as he navigates the complexities of society. With a cast of intriguing characters and a captivating plot, this novel explores the power of redemption and the pursuit of happiness in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Love and Betrayal
