Synopsis: In 'Tracked by a Tattoo: A Mystery' by Fergus Hume, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious tattoo becomes the key to solving a series of baffling crimes. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of society, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and danger that threatens to consume him. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Redemption
