Synopsis: 

In 'The Olden Time Series, Vol. 5: Some Strange and Curious Punishments: Gleanings Chiefly from Old Newspapers of Boston and Salem, Massachusetts', author Henry M. Brooks delves into the intriguing world of historical punishments. Through a collection of newspaper clippings from the past, he uncovers a variety of bizarre and unusual methods of discipline that were once employed in the cities of Boston and Salem. From public shaming to creative forms of confinement, these punishments offer a fascinating glimpse into the darker side of society's attempts to maintain order and control. 

Genres: Historical Non-Fiction, Crime, Social History

Themes: Punishment, Justice, Social Control
