Synopsis: In 'The Wilderness Fugitives' by Edward Sylvester Ellis, a group of young adventurers find themselves caught in a treacherous wilderness, pursued by a mysterious enemy. As they navigate through dangerous terrain and face unexpected challenges, they must rely on their wits and courage to survive. With each step, they uncover secrets that could change their lives forever, leading them on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and survival.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Coming of Age
