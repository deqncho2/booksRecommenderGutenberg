Synopsis: 

'Vital Records of the Town of Auburn, (Formerly Ward), Massachusetts, To the end of the year 1850: With the Inscriptions from the Old Burial Grounds' by Franklin P. Rice is a captivating historical record that delves into the rich and mysterious past of Auburn, Massachusetts. This meticulously compiled collection of vital records and burial inscriptions offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives, births, deaths, and marriages of the town's inhabitants during the early 19th century. From the intriguing stories hidden within the old burial grounds to the surprising connections between families, this book unveils a tapestry of forgotten lives and forgotten tales. 

Genres: Historical record, Genealogy, Local history

Themes: History, Genealogy, Community, Life and Death
