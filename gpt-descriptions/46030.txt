Synopsis: 
'Of Walks and Walking Tours: An Attempt to find a Philosophy and a Creed' takes readers on a captivating journey through the author's exploration of the profound connection between walking and the human experience. From the bustling streets of cities to the serene paths of nature, Haultain delves into the philosophical and spiritual aspects of walking, uncovering hidden truths and profound insights along the way. Through vivid descriptions and thought-provoking anecdotes, this book invites readers to embark on a transformative journey of self-discovery and contemplation.

Genres: Philosophy, Travel, Memoir

Themes: The connection between walking and the human experience, Philosophy of nature, Self-discovery and contemplation
