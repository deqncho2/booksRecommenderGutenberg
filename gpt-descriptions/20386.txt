Synopsis: In 'Embroidery and Tapestry Weaving' by Grace Christie, a young artist named Emily discovers a hidden world of creativity and passion when she stumbles upon an ancient tapestry in her grandmother's attic. As she delves deeper into the art of embroidery and tapestry weaving, Emily uncovers a captivating tale of love, loss, and redemption that spans generations. With each stitch, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries that will challenge her artistic abilities and change her life forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Art

Themes: Artistic expression, Family secrets, Personal growth
