Synopsis: 
In 'Tales from the German. Volume I.', Carl Franz van der Velde presents a captivating collection of German folk tales that will transport readers to a world of enchantment and wonder. From magical creatures and mystical forests to brave heroes and treacherous villains, these tales are filled with thrilling adventures and unexpected twists. Each story is beautifully crafted, immersing the reader in a rich tapestry of German folklore and mythology. Whether it's a tale of love and sacrifice or a cautionary fable about the consequences of greed, this collection promises to captivate and entertain readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: 
Love and sacrifice, Greed and its consequences, Heroism, Mythology
