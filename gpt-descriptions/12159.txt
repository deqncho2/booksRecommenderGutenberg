Synopsis: In 'Easy Money: Night Watches, Part 9,' the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of London's criminal world. As the protagonist finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of deceit and betrayal, he must navigate the treacherous streets to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this installment of the 'Easy Money' series will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Crime fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Crime and punishment, Loyalty and betrayal, Survival in a hostile environment
