Synopsis: In 'The Escape of Alice: A Christmas Fantasy' by Vincent Starrett, readers are transported to a whimsical world where Alice, a young girl with an adventurous spirit, embarks on a magical journey during the holiday season. As Alice encounters enchanting creatures and faces unexpected challenges, she must rely on her courage and resourcefulness to find her way back home. This heartwarming tale is filled with captivating twists and turns that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover the outcome of Alice's extraordinary adventure.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Christmas

Themes: Courage, Imagination, Belonging
