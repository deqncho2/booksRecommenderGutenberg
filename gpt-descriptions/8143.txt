Synopsis: In 'The Shadow of the East', a young Englishwoman finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue as she embarks on a journey to the exotic lands of the East. Drawn into a world of secret societies, forbidden love, and political unrest, she must navigate the treacherous landscape of colonial India to uncover the truth and protect those she holds dear. As danger lurks around every corner, she must confront her own fears and discover the strength within her to face the shadows that threaten to consume her.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Colonialism, Identity, Betrayal, Courage
