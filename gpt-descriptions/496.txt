Synopsis: In "The Little Lame Prince," a young prince finds himself trapped in a world of limitations due to a tragic accident. Despite his physical disability, he embarks on a magical journey that leads him to discover his true potential and the power of kindness. With the help of a mysterious fairy godmother, the little prince learns valuable lessons about courage, friendship, and the importance of inner strength. This enchanting tale takes readers on a captivating adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Overcoming adversity, Self-discovery, Kindness, Friendship
