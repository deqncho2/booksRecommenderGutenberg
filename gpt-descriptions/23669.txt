Synopsis: 
In 'Summit' by Reynolds, Mack, a group of elite climbers embarks on a treacherous expedition to conquer the world's highest peak. As they face the unforgiving elements and their own personal demons, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, pushing them to their limits. With each step closer to the summit, the climbers must confront their deepest fears and make life-altering decisions that will test their strength and resilience. Will they reach the top or succumb to the mountain's merciless grip?

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Perseverance, Sacrifice, Redemption
