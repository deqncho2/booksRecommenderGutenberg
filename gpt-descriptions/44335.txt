Synopsis: 
'Curialia Miscellanea, or Anecdotes of Old Times: Regal, Noble, Gentilitial, and Miscellaneous: Including Authentic Anecdotes of the Royal Household, and the Manners and Customs of the Court, at an Early Period of the English History' by Samuel Pegge is a captivating collection of anecdotes and stories from the past. Delving into the regal, noble, and miscellaneous aspects of old times, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the royal household and the customs of the court during the early period of English history. From intriguing tales of the nobility to authentic anecdotes of the royal household, readers will be transported to a bygone era filled with captivating characters and intriguing events.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Anecdotes, Biography

Themes: English history, Royal household, Court customs and manners
