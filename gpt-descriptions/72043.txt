Synopsis: 

In "Engineering reminiscences contributed to 'Power' and 'American machinist'," Charles T. Porter takes us on a captivating journey through his experiences as an engineer, sharing fascinating anecdotes and insights from his illustrious career. From his early days as an apprentice to his groundbreaking inventions and encounters with notable figures, Porter's reminiscences offer a unique glimpse into the world of engineering and its impact on society. With a blend of technical expertise and personal storytelling, this collection of essays is sure to captivate readers and leave them inspired by the wonders of engineering.

Genres: Memoir, Engineering, History

Themes: Innovation, Problem-solving, Career Development
