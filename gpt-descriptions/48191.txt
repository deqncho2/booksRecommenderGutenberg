Synopsis: In "Christina of Denmark, Duchess of Milan and Lorraine, 1522-1590" by Julia Cartwright, the captivating life of Christina of Denmark unfolds. From her royal upbringing to her unexpected journey as a duchess, this biography delves into the fascinating world of a remarkable woman who defied societal norms and left a lasting impact on history. With intrigue, romance, and political maneuvering, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the life of a Renaissance noblewoman.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Power and Politics, Gender Roles, Renaissance Society
