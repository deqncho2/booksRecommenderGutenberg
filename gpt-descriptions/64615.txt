Synopsis:
In 'Honor Bright: A Story of the Days of King Charles', we are transported back to the tumultuous era of King Charles I. Honor Bright, a young and spirited woman, finds herself caught in the midst of political intrigue, forbidden love, and dangerous secrets. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of the English Civil War, Honor must make choices that will test her loyalty, courage, and ultimately, her own sense of honor. With vivid historical detail and captivating characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes:
Loyalty, Courage, Love, Political Intrigue
