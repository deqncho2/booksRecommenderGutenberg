Synopsis: 
In 'Buff: A Collie, and Other Dog-Stories' by Albert Payson Terhune, embark on a heartwarming journey through a collection of captivating tales that revolve around the lives of dogs. From the loyal and intelligent Collie named Buff, to other remarkable canine companions, these stories will take you on an emotional rollercoaster, filled with adventure, loyalty, and the unbreakable bond between humans and their four-legged friends. Each story is a testament to the extraordinary qualities possessed by these remarkable creatures, leaving readers with a renewed appreciation for the love and devotion that dogs bring into our lives.

Genres: 
Fiction, Animal Stories, Short Stories

Themes: 
Loyalty, Friendship, Adventure, Human-Animal Bond
