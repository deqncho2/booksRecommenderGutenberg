Synopsis: In the second volume of "Life and Times of Her Majesty Caroline Matilda," we delve deeper into the captivating life of Queen Caroline Matilda of Denmark and Norway, sister of King George III of England. As her tumultuous journey continues, we witness her struggle to navigate the treacherous waters of court politics, her forbidden love affair that threatens to shatter her world, and the ultimate betrayal that will test her resilience and strength. With vivid historical detail and a compelling narrative, this volume offers a captivating glimpse into the life of a remarkable queen.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Royal Drama

Themes: Power and Politics, Forbidden Love, Betrayal, Resilience
