Synopsis: In 'The Boy Volunteers with the Submarine Fleet' by Kenneth Ward, readers are taken on an exhilarating adventure as young protagonist, Jack, finds himself thrust into the dangerous world of submarines during World War I. As he joins the crew of a submarine, Jack must navigate treacherous waters, face enemy attacks, and confront his own fears. With each mission, Jack's courage and determination are tested, and he learns the true meaning of bravery and sacrifice. Will he be able to prove himself and help his comrades in their fight for victory?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Coming of Age
