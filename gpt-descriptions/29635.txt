Synopsis: In "Dr. Montessori's Own Handbook," Maria Montessori, a renowned educator, shares her groundbreaking approach to education that revolutionized the way children learn. Through captivating anecdotes and practical advice, Montessori unveils her unique method that fosters independence, creativity, and a love for learning in young minds. This handbook serves as a guide for parents and educators alike, offering valuable insights into creating an environment that nurtures the natural curiosity and potential of every child.

Genres: Education, Non-fiction, Psychology

Themes: Child development, Individuality, Self-directed learning, Montessori method
