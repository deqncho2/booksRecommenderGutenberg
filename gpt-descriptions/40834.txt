Synopsis: In 'The Under-Secretary' by William Le Queux, a young and ambitious under-secretary finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and danger. As he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens the stability of the nation, he must navigate through a treacherous world of espionage, betrayal, and hidden agendas. With his life on the line, he must make difficult choices and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Espionage

Themes: Political intrigue, Betrayal, Power struggle
