Synopsis: In 'Hygienic Physiology: with Special Reference to the Use of Alcoholic Drinks and Narcotics', Joel Dorman Steele delves into the fascinating world of human physiology, exploring the effects of alcoholic drinks and narcotics on our bodies. Through a comprehensive examination of the subject, Steele provides valuable insights into the intricate workings of our physical selves and the potential consequences of substance abuse. This thought-provoking book offers a compelling exploration of the human body and the impact of our choices on our overall health and well-being.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Health

Themes: Human physiology, Substance abuse, Health education
