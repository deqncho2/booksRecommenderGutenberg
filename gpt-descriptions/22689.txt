Synopsis: In 'The Confessions of a Caricaturist, Vol. 2', Harry Furniss takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of caricature, revealing the secrets and stories behind his most famous works. From the halls of power to the streets of London, Furniss shares his encounters with influential figures, his humorous observations, and the challenges he faced as a caricaturist. With wit and charm, he unveils the artistry and satire that lie within each stroke of his pen, offering a unique glimpse into the world of a master caricaturist.

Genres: Memoir, Art, Humor

Themes: Artistic expression, Satire, Observational humor
