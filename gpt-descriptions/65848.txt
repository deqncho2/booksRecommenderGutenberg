Synopsis: In "Theodore Savage: A Story of the Past or the Future," the brilliant scientist Theodore Savage finds himself torn between his duty to his country and his own moral compass. Set in a post-World War II world, where the threat of nuclear war looms large, Theodore embarks on a dangerous mission that could change the course of history. As he navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and the pursuit of knowledge, Theodore must confront the ethical dilemmas that arise when science and politics collide.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Morality, Ethics, Nationalism, Love and Sacrifice
