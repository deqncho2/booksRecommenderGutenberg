Synopsis: In the small Scottish town of Hermiston, a young and ambitious judge named Archie Weir finds himself torn between his duty to uphold the law and his forbidden love for a mysterious woman. As he navigates the complexities of his personal and professional life, Archie becomes entangled in a web of secrets, family feuds, and societal expectations. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Scotland, 'Weir of Hermiston: An Unfinished Romance' is a captivating tale of love, justice, and the struggle to find one's true identity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Desire, Duty and Responsibility, Social Expectations, Identity and Self-Discovery
