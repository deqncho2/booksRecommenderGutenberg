Synopsis: In 'The Dare Boys with General Greene' by Cox, Stephen Angus Douglas, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves caught up in the American Revolution. When they stumble upon a secret message from General Nathanael Greene, they embark on a dangerous mission to deliver it to him. Along the way, they face perilous challenges, encounter historical figures, and learn the true meaning of bravery and loyalty. Will the Dare Boys succeed in their mission and help turn the tide of the war?

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Friendship, Courage, Historical events
