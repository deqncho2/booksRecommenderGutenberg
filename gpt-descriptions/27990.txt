Synopsis: In 'Theo: A Sprightly Love Story' by Frances Hodgson Burnett, readers are introduced to Theo, a young woman with a vibrant spirit and a determination to live life on her own terms. Set in the late 19th century, Theo's journey takes her from the bustling streets of New York City to the picturesque countryside of England. Along the way, she encounters unexpected friendships, romantic entanglements, and a series of adventures that test her resilience and challenge societal norms. As Theo navigates the complexities of love and self-discovery, she learns valuable lessons about the power of following one's heart and embracing the beauty of life's unpredictable twists and turns.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Independence, Love, Self-Discovery
