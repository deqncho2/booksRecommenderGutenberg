Synopsis: In 'Teams: A Terran Empire story' by Wilson, Ann, a group of unlikely individuals from different corners of the galaxy are brought together to form a team with a mission that could change the fate of the Terran Empire. As they navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter mysterious beings, and face their own personal demons, they must learn to trust each other and harness their unique abilities to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. With secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances, this thrilling sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Teamwork, Trust, Identity, Redemption
