Synopsis: In 'Get Out of Our Skies!' by Jarvis, E. K., a group of ordinary citizens find themselves caught in a thrilling and mysterious adventure when strange objects begin appearing in the sky. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they uncover a web of secrets and conspiracies that could change the course of humanity forever. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Conspiracy, Exploration of the Unknown, Human Resilience
