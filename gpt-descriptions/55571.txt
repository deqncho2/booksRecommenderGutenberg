Synopsis: In 'Whom God Hath Joined: A Question of Marriage' by Fergus Hume, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she marries a mysterious man. As she delves deeper into her husband's past, she uncovers shocking truths that threaten to unravel their marriage and her own sanity. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning who can be trusted and what lengths one will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Marriage, Deception, Trust, Identity
