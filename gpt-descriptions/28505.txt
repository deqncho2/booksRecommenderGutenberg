Synopsis: In "The Third Degree: A Narrative of Metropolitan Life," Hornblow and Klein take readers on a thrilling journey through the underbelly of the city, where corruption, scandal, and secrets lurk in every corner. As the story unfolds, a young journalist finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger, uncovering shocking truths that could shatter the very foundations of society. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous landscape, where loyalty is tested, and the line between right and wrong becomes increasingly blurred. Will he be able to expose the truth, or will he become just another casualty of the city's dark underbelly?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Corruption, Power, Morality
