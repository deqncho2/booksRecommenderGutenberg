Synopsis: 

Step back in time to 1864 and explore the vast network of post offices in Canada, as documented by the Post Office Department. This comprehensive list unveils the names of the postmasters across the country, providing a fascinating glimpse into the postal system of the era. From bustling cities to remote towns, this compilation offers a unique perspective on the people and places that played a crucial role in connecting communities through mail services. Discover the stories behind these post offices and the individuals who diligently served as postmasters, shaping the communication landscape of Canada during this pivotal period.

Genres: Historical, Reference

Themes: Communication, Community, Historical Documentation
