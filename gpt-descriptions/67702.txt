Synopsis: In 'Crashing Suns' by Hamilton, Edmond, a group of interstellar explorers stumble upon a mysterious planet that defies all known laws of physics. As they delve deeper into its secrets, they uncover a mind-bending truth that could alter the fate of the entire universe. With time running out, they must navigate treacherous alliances and face unimaginable dangers to save humanity from an impending catastrophe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Space Opera, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Time and Space, Existentialism
