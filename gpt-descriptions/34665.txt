Synopsis: 
'Christmas Penny Readings: Original Sketches for the Season' by George Manville Fenn is a collection of heartwarming and humorous sketches that capture the spirit of Christmas. From mischievous children to eccentric relatives, Fenn's sketches bring to life the joy, laughter, and occasional chaos that often accompany the holiday season. Each sketch is filled with delightful characters and unexpected twists that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eagerly awaiting the next surprise. 

Genres: 
Humor, Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: 
Christmas, Family, Joy, Laughter
