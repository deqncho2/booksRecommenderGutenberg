Synopsis: 
In 'O'Flaherty V.C.: A Recruiting Pamphlet' by Bernard Shaw, we are introduced to Private O'Flaherty, a charming and charismatic Irish soldier who is about to be awarded the Victoria Cross for his bravery in battle. However, as the story unfolds, we discover that O'Flaherty's true passion lies not in fighting for his country, but in pursuing his dream of becoming a professional musician. As he navigates the expectations of his family, friends, and the military, O'Flaherty must confront the conflict between duty and personal fulfillment.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Satire

Themes: Patriotism, Identity, Pursuit of Dreams
