Synopsis: 
'Whilst father was fighting' by Eleanora H. Stooke is a gripping historical fiction novel set during World War II. The story follows the lives of two siblings, Lily and Jack, as they navigate the challenges and uncertainties of war-torn England. With their father away fighting on the front lines, Lily and Jack must find strength within themselves and their community to face the hardships and sacrifices that war demands. As they encounter danger, loss, and unexpected friendships, the siblings discover the true meaning of resilience and the power of love in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Family, Resilience, Sacrifice, Friendship, Love, Survival
