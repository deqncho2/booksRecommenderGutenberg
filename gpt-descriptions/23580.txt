Synopsis: In 'The Children's Bible', Kent and Sherman present a captivating retelling of biblical stories that will enchant readers of all ages. Through beautiful illustrations and accessible language, this book brings to life the timeless tales of faith, courage, and redemption. From the creation of the world to the miracles of Jesus, each story is carefully crafted to engage young readers and ignite their imagination. As they journey through the pages, children will discover the power of love, the importance of forgiveness, and the enduring hope found within these sacred stories.

Genres: Children's Literature, Religious Fiction, Picture Book

Themes: Faith, Morality, Redemption, Love, Forgiveness
