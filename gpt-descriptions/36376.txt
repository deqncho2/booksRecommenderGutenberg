Synopsis: In "The Life of Benjamin Franklin: With Many Choice Anecdotes and admirable sayings of this great man never before published by any of his biographers," author M.L. Weems delves into the fascinating life of one of America's most influential figures. From Franklin's humble beginnings as a printer's apprentice to his rise as a renowned scientist, inventor, and statesman, this biography uncovers the lesser-known aspects of his life, including his personal struggles and triumphs. Filled with captivating anecdotes and inspiring quotes, this book offers a fresh perspective on the man behind the legend, leaving readers eager to discover the untold stories of Benjamin Franklin.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Ambition, Self-improvement, Enlightenment, American Revolution
