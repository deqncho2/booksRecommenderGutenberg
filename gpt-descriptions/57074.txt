Synopsis: 
In 'Rambles in Yucatan; or, Notes of Travel Through the Peninsula: Including a Visit to the Remarkable Ruins of Chi-Chen, Kabah, Zayi, and Uxmal', Benjamin Moore Norman takes readers on an enthralling journey through the captivating landscapes and ancient ruins of the Yucatan Peninsula. With vivid descriptions and insightful observations, Norman paints a vivid picture of his exploration, including his encounters with the local people, the awe-inspiring ruins he discovers, and the mysteries that surround them. As he delves deeper into the heart of the peninsula, the author's curiosity and sense of adventure intensify, leading to unexpected discoveries and revelations. This second edition of 'Rambles in Yucatan' offers an expanded perspective and further insights into the rich history and cultural heritage of this fascinating region.

Genres: Travelogue, Archaeology, History

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Ancient Civilizations
