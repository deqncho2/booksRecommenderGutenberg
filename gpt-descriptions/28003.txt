Synopsis: 
In 'Supplement to Punch, or the London Charivari, September 16, 1914: The New Rake's Progress: Cartoons from "Punch" Illustrating the Kaiser's Career, 1888-1914', readers are taken on a visual journey through the career of the Kaiser, from his rise to power in 1888 to the brink of World War I in 1914. Through a collection of witty and satirical cartoons from the renowned publication "Punch," this book offers a unique perspective on the Kaiser's actions, decisions, and impact on the world stage. With each cartoon, the reader is invited to reflect on the political climate, the shifting alliances, and the consequences of the Kaiser's choices. 

Genres: Satire, Political Cartoons, Historical Commentary

Themes: Power and Ambition, International Relations, Satire and Critique
