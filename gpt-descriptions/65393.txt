Synopsis: In "The Phantom Regiment; or, Stories of 'Ours'" by James Grant, a mysterious and elusive group known as the Phantom Regiment emerges, captivating the attention of both friend and foe. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, loyalties are tested, and the true nature of this enigmatic regiment is brought to light. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the Phantom Regiment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity, Honor
