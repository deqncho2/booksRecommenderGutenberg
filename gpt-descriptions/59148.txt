Synopsis: In 'The Cyber and Justice Holmes' by Riley, Frank, a brilliant hacker named Alex Holmes finds himself caught in a web of intrigue when he stumbles upon a dangerous cyber conspiracy. As he delves deeper into the dark corners of the internet, he must navigate a treacherous world of virtual reality, artificial intelligence, and high-stakes espionage. With his life on the line, Holmes must use his exceptional skills to uncover the truth and bring the perpetrators to justice, all while staying one step ahead of those who seek to silence him.

Genres: Techno-thriller, Cyberpunk, Mystery

Themes: Cybersecurity, Ethics in Technology, Surveillance Society
