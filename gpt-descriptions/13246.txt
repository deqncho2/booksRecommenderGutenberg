Synopsis: In "The Conqueror: Being the True and Romantic Story of Alexander Hamilton," Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton weaves a captivating tale that delves into the life of one of America's most influential founding fathers. Set against the backdrop of the Revolutionary War and the birth of a new nation, this historical novel follows the remarkable journey of Alexander Hamilton, from his humble beginnings in the Caribbean to his rise as a key figure in the American Revolution and the formation of the United States. Filled with political intrigue, personal struggles, and passionate relationships, this gripping narrative explores the complexities of Hamilton's character and the indelible impact he left on American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biographical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Patriotism, Love and Relationships, Political Power, Identity
