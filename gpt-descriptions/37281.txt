Synopsis: 
In 'How to Write Music: Musical Orthography', Harris, Clement A. takes readers on an enchanting journey into the world of music composition. Through a captivating blend of historical anecdotes, practical tips, and insightful analysis, Harris unveils the secrets behind creating melodies that resonate with the soul. From the intricacies of musical notation to the art of harmonization, this book serves as a comprehensive guide for aspiring composers and music enthusiasts alike. Prepare to be inspired as Harris unlocks the door to the boundless possibilities of musical expression.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Education

Themes: Music composition, Musical notation, Artistic expression
