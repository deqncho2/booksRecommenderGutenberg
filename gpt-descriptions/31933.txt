Synopsis: In "Landmarks of Scientific Socialism: 'Anti-Duehring'," Friedrich Engels presents a captivating exploration of the fundamental principles of socialism and critiques the influential philosopher Eugen Duehring. Engels delves into the realms of philosophy, economics, and politics, unraveling the flaws in Duehring's theories while offering a comprehensive analysis of the socialist ideology. Through engaging arguments and thought-provoking insights, Engels challenges the prevailing ideas of his time, paving the way for a deeper understanding of the principles that underpin scientific socialism.

Genres: Political philosophy, Social critique, Historical analysis

Themes: Socialism, Critique of capitalism, Historical materialism, Class struggle
