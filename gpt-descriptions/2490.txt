Synopsis:
In the enchanting world of Lamia, John Keats weaves a tale of forbidden love and the consequences of pursuing one's desires. Set in a mythical realm, the story follows the encounter between a mortal man and a captivating creature, blurring the lines between reality and fantasy. As their relationship unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the characters are faced with the ultimate test of their devotion. With lyrical prose and a touch of the supernatural, Keats invites readers on a mesmerizing journey that explores the depths of human longing and the power of illusion.

Genres:
Romantic poetry, Fantasy, Gothic fiction

Themes:
Love and desire, Illusion and reality, Sacrifice and consequences
