Synopsis: In 'Real Folks' by Whitney, A. D. T., a young woman named Ruth experiences a dramatic change in her life when she is forced to leave her comfortable home and move to a small New England town. As she navigates the challenges of her new surroundings, Ruth encounters a cast of colorful characters who challenge her preconceived notions and teach her valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the true meaning of family. With unexpected twists and turns, this heartwarming tale explores the power of resilience and the beauty of finding one's place in the world.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Family Drama

Themes: Identity, Community, Resilience, Friendship
