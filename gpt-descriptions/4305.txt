Synopsis: 

The Unclassed by George Gissing is a captivating novel that follows the life of a young woman named Clara. Born into poverty and facing numerous hardships, Clara is determined to rise above her circumstances and find her place in society. As she navigates the complexities of Victorian England, Clara encounters love, betrayal, and the struggle for social acceptance. With her indomitable spirit and unwavering determination, Clara's journey becomes a compelling exploration of class, identity, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Social Realism, Victorian Literature

Themes: Social class, Identity, Love and relationships, Poverty, Social acceptance
