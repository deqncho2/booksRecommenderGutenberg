Synopsis: In 'My Strangest Case' by Boothby, Guy, the renowned detective Simon Carne finds himself entangled in a perplexing mystery that pushes the boundaries of his investigative skills. When a series of bizarre events unfold, including a stolen artifact and a mysterious disappearance, Carne must navigate a web of secrets and deceit to uncover the truth. As the case takes unexpected twists and turns, Carne's own past comes back to haunt him, adding an intriguing layer of complexity to the investigation. With danger lurking at every corner, Carne must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to solve his strangest case yet.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Redemption, Identity
