Synopsis: In the heart of the Sahara Desert, a captivating tale unfolds as a young Englishwoman finds herself entangled in a web of passion, mystery, and self-discovery. Leaving behind her comfortable life in England, Domini Enfilden embarks on a journey to Algeria, where she encounters the enigmatic Boris Androvsky, a Russian artist haunted by his past. As their paths intertwine amidst the breathtaking backdrop of the Garden of Allah, their lives are forever changed, leading them to question their desires, beliefs, and the true nature of love.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Self-Discovery, Cultural Exploration
