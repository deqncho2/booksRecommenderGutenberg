Synopsis: In 'How the World Travels' by Alice A. Methley, embark on a thrilling journey through time and space as a group of unlikely adventurers discover a hidden secret that could change the course of history. From ancient civilizations to futuristic worlds, they must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter mysterious creatures, and unravel the enigma of the world's most sought-after artifact. As they face danger at every turn, their bonds are tested, and they must rely on their wit, courage, and resourcefulness to survive. Will they unlock the truth and save the world, or will they be consumed by the very forces they seek to conquer?

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Science Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Quest for Knowledge
