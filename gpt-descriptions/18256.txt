Synopsis: In 'Woodside: or, Look, Listen, and Learn,' Hadley Caroline takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Woodside, a small town filled with secrets and mysteries. When a young girl named Lily moves to Woodside, she quickly discovers that the town holds more than meets the eye. As she befriends a group of curious and adventurous children, they embark on a thrilling quest to uncover the truth behind the town's hidden past. With each clue they uncover, they find themselves drawn deeper into a web of intrigue and danger. Will they be able to solve the mysteries of Woodside before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Secrets, Discovery
