Synopsis: In 'The Happy Hunting-Grounds' by Roosevelt, Kermit, a group of adventurous explorers embarks on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of Africa. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter exotic wildlife, they face unexpected challenges that test their courage and resilience. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale takes readers on an unforgettable expedition into the unknown.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Wildlife Conservation
