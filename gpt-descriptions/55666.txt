Synopsis: In "The German Spy in America: The Secret Plotting of German Spies in the United States and the Inside Story of the Sinking of the Lusitania," author John Price Jones takes readers on a thrilling journey into the covert world of German espionage during World War I. Through meticulous research and gripping storytelling, Jones uncovers the hidden plots and intricate web of spies operating within the United States, revealing shocking secrets that lead to the tragic sinking of the Lusitania. As the tension builds, readers will be captivated by the high-stakes game of espionage and the race against time to uncover the truth.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Espionage, War

Themes: Espionage, Betrayal, National Security, War Consequences
