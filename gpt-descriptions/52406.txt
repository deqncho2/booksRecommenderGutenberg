Synopsis: In "Prehistoric man: Researches into the origin of civilization in the Old and the New world," Sir Daniel Wilson takes readers on a captivating journey through time to uncover the mysteries of our ancient ancestors. From the depths of prehistoric caves to the vast landscapes of the New World, Wilson delves into the origins of civilization, exploring the remarkable achievements and advancements of early humans. With meticulous research and vivid storytelling, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the dawn of human civilization, leaving readers eager to uncover the secrets of our past.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Anthropology

Themes: Origins of civilization, Human evolution, Cultural development
