Synopsis: In 'Sara Crewe; Or, What Happened at Miss Minchin's', a young girl named Sara Crewe is sent to a prestigious boarding school in London. With her vivid imagination and kind heart, Sara quickly captures the attention of her classmates and teachers. However, when a sudden turn of events leaves Sara penniless and at the mercy of the strict headmistress, Miss Minchin, she must rely on her resilience and inner strength to navigate the challenges that come her way. This heartwarming tale explores the power of friendship, the importance of staying true to oneself, and the enduring spirit of hope.

Genres: Children's literature, Fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Resilience, Friendship, Imagination, Kindness, Social class
