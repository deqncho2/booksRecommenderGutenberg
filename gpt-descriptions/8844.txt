Synopsis: In 'Weymouth New Testament in Modern Speech, Titus', Richard Francis Weymouth presents a captivating and contemporary retelling of the biblical book of Titus. This powerful narrative follows the journey of Titus, a young leader tasked with establishing order and guiding a community in the midst of chaos and moral decay. As he faces numerous challenges and encounters various characters, Titus must navigate the complexities of faith, leadership, and the pursuit of righteousness. With its engaging storytelling and thought-provoking themes, this modern adaptation of Titus offers readers a fresh perspective on timeless biblical teachings.

Genres: Religious fiction, Biblical retelling, Christian literature

Themes: Faith, Leadership, Morality, Righteousness
