Synopsis: In 'A Warning to the Curious, and Other Ghost Stories', James, M. R. takes readers on a chilling journey through a collection of eerie tales. From a cursed artifact to a haunted coastal town, each story delves into the dark corners of the supernatural, leaving readers on the edge of their seats. As the characters encounter malevolent spirits and ancient curses, they must confront their deepest fears and grapple with the consequences of their actions. With atmospheric settings and spine-tingling suspense, this collection of ghost stories is sure to captivate and haunt readers long after the final page.

Genres: Horror, Gothic fiction, Supernatural fiction

Themes: Fear, the supernatural, the consequences of one's actions
