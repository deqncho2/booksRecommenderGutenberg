Synopsis: 

In "All's not Gold that Glitters;" or, The Young Californian, we follow the journey of a young man named Jack, who sets out to seek his fortune in the gold rush of California. As he navigates the treacherous world of mining camps, saloons, and deceitful characters, Jack's dreams of striking it rich are tested in unexpected ways. With danger lurking at every turn, he must learn to distinguish between true friends and cunning enemies, and discover the true value of wealth and happiness.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Friendship, Morality
