Synopsis: In 'A Dangerous Flirtation; Or, Did Ida May Sin?' by Laura Jean Libbey, the captivating story revolves around Ida May, a young woman who finds herself caught in a web of deceit and temptation. As she navigates the treacherous waters of love and desire, Ida May must confront the consequences of her actions and make difficult choices that could change her life forever. With secrets, scandal, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Suspense

Themes: Love and betrayal, Morality and temptation, Consequences of actions
