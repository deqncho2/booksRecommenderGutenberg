Synopsis: In 'Sophy of Kravonia: A Novel' by Hope, Anthony, a young Englishwoman named Sophy is unexpectedly thrust into a world of political intrigue and danger when she becomes the ruler of a small European kingdom. As she navigates the treacherous waters of power, Sophy must rely on her wit, courage, and the support of a mysterious stranger to protect her throne and the people she has come to care for. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through love, loyalty, and the pursuit of justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Power and Responsibility, Love and Sacrifice, Identity and Self-discovery
