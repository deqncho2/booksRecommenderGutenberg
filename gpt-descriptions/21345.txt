Synopsis: In 'A Wounded Name' by Charles King, readers are transported to a modern-day high school where Ophelia Castellan, a talented and enigmatic teenager, finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and betrayal. As she navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and power, Ophelia's journey takes a dark turn, revealing the haunting truth behind her own name. With echoes of Shakespeare's Hamlet, this gripping novel explores the depths of human emotions and the consequences of unchecked ambition.

Genres: Young Adult, Contemporary, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Revenge, Betrayal, Mental Health
