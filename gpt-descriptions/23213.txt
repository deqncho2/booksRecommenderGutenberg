Synopsis: In "Uncle Wiggily and Old Mother Hubbard: Adventures of the Rabbit Gentleman with the Mother Goose Characters," Howard Roger Garis takes readers on a delightful journey through the whimsical world of Mother Goose. Join Uncle Wiggily, a kind-hearted rabbit gentleman, as he embarks on a series of enchanting adventures alongside beloved characters like Old Mother Hubbard, Jack and Jill, and Little Bo Peep. From solving mysteries to helping friends in need, Uncle Wiggily's escapades are filled with humor, excitement, and valuable life lessons. This charming book is sure to captivate readers of all ages with its timeless tales and lovable characters.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Kindness, Problem-solving, Imagination
