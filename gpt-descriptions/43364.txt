Synopsis:
In 'How Canada Was Won: A Tale of Wolfe and Quebec', F. S. Brereton takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the dramatic events of the Seven Years' War. Set against the backdrop of the rugged Canadian wilderness, this gripping historical adventure follows the exploits of a young British officer, Captain Hugh Pemberton, as he navigates the treacherous landscape and faces formidable enemies. From the thrilling Battle of Quebec to the daring exploits of General James Wolfe, this tale of courage, sacrifice, and determination will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Heroism, Patriotism, Exploration, Conflict
