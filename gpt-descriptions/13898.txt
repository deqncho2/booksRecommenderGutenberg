Synopsis: In 'Don Strong, Patrol Leader' by Heyliger, William, follow the thrilling adventures of Don Strong, a young and courageous patrol leader. As he leads his patrol through the wilderness, they encounter unexpected challenges and dangerous situations that test their skills and teamwork. With each obstacle they face, Don must rely on his quick thinking and resourcefulness to keep his patrol safe. But when a mysterious figure appears, Don's leadership skills are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to guide his patrol through this perilous journey and uncover the truth behind the enigmatic stranger?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Leadership, Teamwork, Courage, Survival
