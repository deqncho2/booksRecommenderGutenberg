Synopsis: In 'A Collection of Seven and Fifty approved Receipts Good against the Plague', Alessio, a renowned doctor, reveals a compilation of secrets to combat the deadly plague. These remedies are specifically designed to benefit the poorer people of various nations. With a total of fifty-seven approved recipes, this book offers a treasure trove of knowledge that could potentially save lives. From ancient herbal remedies to unconventional techniques, readers will be captivated by the innovative solutions presented within these pages.

Genres: Medical, Historical, Self-help

Themes: Disease prevention, Herbal medicine, Empowerment
