Synopsis: 
In 'The Magistrate: A Farce in Three Acts', we are introduced to the respectable and seemingly upright Mr. Posket, a magistrate who prides himself on his moral standing. However, when a series of hilarious misunderstandings and deceptions unfold, Mr. Posket finds himself entangled in a web of lies that threatens to expose his true nature. As the chaos escalates, the audience is taken on a rollercoaster ride of laughter and surprises, as the characters navigate their way through a world of mistaken identities and comical mishaps.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Deception, Identity, Morality
