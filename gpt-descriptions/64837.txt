Synopsis: 
In 'Selections From Josephus', the renowned historian Flavius Josephus takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events of ancient Judea. From the rise of Herod the Great to the destruction of the Second Temple in Jerusalem, Josephus provides a firsthand account of the political intrigues, military conflicts, and religious upheavals that shaped the destiny of his people. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous historical detail, this collection of selections offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of biblical figures, the struggles for power, and the clash of civilizations that defined this pivotal era.

Genres: 
Historical, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Power and Politics, Religion and Faith, War and Conflict
