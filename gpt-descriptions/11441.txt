Synopsis: In 'The Solitary of Juan Fernandez, or the Real Robinson Crusoe', we follow the incredible true story of a man named Alexander Selkirk who finds himself stranded on a remote island in the Pacific Ocean. Cut off from civilization, he must learn to survive in this harsh and unforgiving environment, facing numerous challenges and discovering the depths of his own resilience. As the years pass, Selkirk's isolation takes a toll on his mental and emotional well-being, leading him to question the meaning of life and the importance of human connection. Will he ever be rescued, or is he destined to remain the solitary inhabitant of Juan Fernandez forever?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Survival

Themes: Isolation, Resilience, Human Connection
