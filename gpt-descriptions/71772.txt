Synopsis: 
'Origins of the 'Forty-five: $b and other papers relating to that rising' is a gripping historical account that delves into the events leading up to the Jacobite Rising of 1745. Through a collection of papers, letters, and personal accounts, the book uncovers the hidden motivations, political intrigues, and secret alliances that shaped this pivotal moment in Scottish history. As the narrative unfolds, readers are transported to a time of rebellion, loyalty, and sacrifice, where the fate of nations hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical fiction, Political thriller, Epistolary novel

Themes: Rebellion, Loyalty, Political intrigue
