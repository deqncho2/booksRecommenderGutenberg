Synopsis: 
'One hundred & one Mexican dishes' takes readers on a mouthwatering culinary journey through the vibrant and diverse world of Mexican cuisine. From sizzling street tacos to rich and flavorful mole sauces, this book presents a tantalizing collection of authentic recipes that capture the essence of Mexican cooking. Each dish is accompanied by vivid descriptions and stunning photographs that will transport readers to the bustling markets and bustling kitchens of Mexico. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a novice in the kitchen, this book is sure to inspire you to explore the bold and delicious flavors of Mexico.

Genres: Cookbook, Food & Drink, Travel

Themes: Mexican cuisine, cultural exploration, culinary traditions
