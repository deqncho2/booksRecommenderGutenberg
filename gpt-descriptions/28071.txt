Synopsis: In 'The Red Triangle: Being Some Further Chronicles of Martin Hewitt, Investigator', Arthur Morrison takes readers on a thrilling journey alongside the brilliant detective Martin Hewitt. This collection of interconnected stories delves into a series of perplexing cases that Hewitt must unravel, each one more mysterious and dangerous than the last. From a baffling murder in a locked room to a sinister secret society, Hewitt's keen intellect and unwavering determination are put to the test. As he navigates through the dark underbelly of London, readers will be captivated by the intricate web of clues and the unexpected twists that lie ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Investigation, Intrigue, Crime-solving, Human nature
