Synopsis: In 'Border and Bastille' by George A. Lawrence, a young Englishman finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger as he embarks on a journey to France. Drawn into a world of espionage, secret societies, and political intrigue, he must navigate the treacherous landscape of 19th-century Europe to uncover the truth. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Espionage, Political Intrigue, Identity
