Synopsis: In "She Would Be a Soldier; Or, The Plains of Chippewa" by Noah, M. M., a young woman defies societal expectations and embarks on a daring adventure in the midst of the War of 1812. As she disguises herself as a soldier, she faces numerous challenges and discovers her own strength and resilience. Set against the backdrop of the Chippewa Plains, this captivating tale explores the complexities of gender roles, the power of determination, and the sacrifices made in the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Gender roles, Identity, Courage, Patriotism
