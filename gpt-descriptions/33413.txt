Synopsis: In 'The Story of Books' by Gertrude Burford Rawlings, embark on a captivating journey through the history of books, from ancient scrolls to modern e-books. Delve into the fascinating tales of the individuals who dedicated their lives to preserving and spreading knowledge, and discover the profound impact that books have had on society. From secret libraries to literary revolutions, this book unveils the hidden stories behind the world's most treasured texts, revealing the power and magic that lies within the pages.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Literature

Themes: Knowledge, Preservation, Cultural Impact
