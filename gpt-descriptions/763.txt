Synopsis: In the wild and untamed land of Arizona, a thrilling tale of love, danger, and adventure unfolds. As the Round-Up begins, a young cowboy finds himself caught in a web of treachery and deceit, where loyalty is tested and true love is put to the ultimate test. With the backdrop of the rugged Arizona landscape, this gripping novel takes readers on a journey through the heart-pounding world of the Wild West, where every decision could mean life or death.

Genres: Western, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and betrayal, Loyalty, Survival in the Wild West
