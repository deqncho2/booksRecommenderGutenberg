Synopsis: In 'Deaf and Dumb!: Third Edition' by Elizabeth Sandham, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young woman named Emily finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception. As she navigates a world where appearances can be deceiving, Emily must rely on her wit and determination to uncover the truth. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the mysteries that lie within.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Trust
