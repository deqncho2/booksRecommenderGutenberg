Synopsis: 
In "The "Canary" Murder Case" by S. S. Van Dine, a glamorous opera singer known as "The Canary" is found dead in her luxurious penthouse apartment. As the investigation unfolds, it becomes clear that this is no ordinary murder case. With a complex web of suspects, including jealous lovers, envious rivals, and mysterious acquaintances, the brilliant detective Philo Vance must navigate through a world of high society, deceit, and hidden motives to uncover the truth behind the Canary's untimely demise.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Wealth and privilege, Deception and betrayal, The pursuit of justice
