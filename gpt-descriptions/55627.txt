Synopsis: In the small town of Gettysburg, Pennsylvania, a young girl named Emmeline grows up in the aftermath of the Civil War. As she navigates the challenges of her changing world, she discovers a hidden talent that sets her apart from others. With determination and resilience, Emmeline embarks on a journey of self-discovery, facing societal expectations, personal sacrifices, and unexpected friendships along the way. Set against the backdrop of a divided nation, "Emmeline" is a captivating tale of courage, identity, and the power of embracing one's true self.

Genres: Historical fiction, coming-of-age, drama

Themes: Identity, societal expectations, resilience, friendship, self-discovery
