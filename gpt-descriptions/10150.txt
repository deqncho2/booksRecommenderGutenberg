Synopsis: 
In "Dracula's Guest" by Bram Stoker, a young Englishman finds himself on a mysterious journey through the Carpathian Mountains. As he ventures deeper into the unknown, he encounters eerie landscapes, strange occurrences, and a foreboding sense of danger lurking in the shadows. With each step, the young man becomes entangled in a web of supernatural forces that threaten to consume him. Will he be able to escape the clutches of the unknown, or will he succumb to the darkness that surrounds him?

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, mystery

Themes: Fear of the unknown, the supernatural, the power of evil
