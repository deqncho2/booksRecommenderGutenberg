Synopsis: 
In the small town of Round Anvil Rock, a mysterious newcomer named Lila arrives, captivating the locals with her enigmatic charm. As the townspeople become entangled in Lila's web, secrets are unearthed, passions ignite, and the quiet town is forever changed. With its richly drawn characters and a plot that twists and turns, 'Round Anvil Rock: A Romance' is a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and the power of hidden desires.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Secrets and deception, Small-town dynamics
