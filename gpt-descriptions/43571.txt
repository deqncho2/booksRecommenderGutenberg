Synopsis: In "The Mother of Washington and Her Times" by Sara Agnes Rice Pryor, readers are transported back in time to the fascinating life of Mary Ball Washington, the mother of the first President of the United States, George Washington. From her humble beginnings in colonial Virginia to her influential role in shaping the character of her famous son, this captivating biography delves into the untold story of a remarkable woman who played a pivotal role in American history. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Pryor brings to life the challenges, triumphs, and complexities of Mary Ball Washington's life, offering readers a unique perspective on the founding of a nation.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Motherhood, Influence, American Revolution, Women's History
