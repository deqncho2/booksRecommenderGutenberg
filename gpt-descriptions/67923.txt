Synopsis: In 'Lost Art' by George O. Smith, a renowned art historian stumbles upon a mysterious painting that has been missing for centuries. As he delves deeper into the origins of the artwork, he uncovers a web of secrets and hidden meanings that could change the course of art history forever. With each clue he uncovers, the protagonist finds himself drawn into a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred. As he races against time to solve the puzzle, he must confront his own demons and question the true nature of art itself.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Art, History, Identity, Perception
