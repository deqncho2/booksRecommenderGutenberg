Synopsis: 
Step into the captivating world of illuminated manuscripts in "Illuminated Manuscripts in Classical and Mediaeval Times, Their Art and Their Technique" by J.H. Middleton. Delve into the intricate details and breathtaking beauty of these ancient manuscripts as Middleton takes you on a journey through classical and medieval times. From the vibrant colors to the meticulous craftsmanship, discover the secrets behind the creation of these masterpieces and the stories they tell. Uncover the fascinating techniques employed by skilled scribes and illuminators, and gain a deeper understanding of the historical and cultural significance of these works of art. Prepare to be enchanted by the enchanting world of illuminated manuscripts.

Genres: Art History, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Artistic techniques, Cultural significance, Historical context
