Synopsis: In 'Rhymes of Old Plimouth' by Herbert Randall, readers are transported to the charming town of Plimouth, where they are introduced to a cast of colorful characters and immersed in the rich history and folklore of the region. Through a collection of captivating rhymes, the author weaves together tales of love, adventure, and mystery, taking readers on a journey that spans generations. From the haunting legends of the sea to the heartwarming stories of everyday life, 'Rhymes of Old Plimouth' offers a delightful exploration of a bygone era that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Poetry, Historical Fiction, Folklore

Themes: History, Community, Love, Adventure, Mystery
