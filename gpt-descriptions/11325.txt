Synopsis: 
In 'The Happiest Time of Their Lives', Alice Duer Miller takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two women who find themselves unexpectedly connected. As secrets unravel and pasts collide, the story unfolds with a perfect blend of mystery, romance, and suspense. With each turn of the page, readers will be drawn deeper into the lives of these complex characters, eagerly anticipating the surprising twists and turns that lie ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the power of love and connection
