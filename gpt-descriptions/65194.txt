Synopsis: In 'Jingles' by Medina, Frank J., a struggling musician named Jake stumbles upon a mysterious music box that has the power to bring his jingles to life. As he delves deeper into the box's enchanting abilities, Jake finds himself caught in a web of unexpected consequences and thrilling adventures. With each jingle he creates, the line between reality and fantasy blurs, leading him on a journey that will challenge his perception of what is possible.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: The power of music, the blurred boundaries between reality and imagination, the consequences of one's actions
