Synopsis: In 'Where the Trail Divides' by Will Lillibridge, a young man named Jack Allen finds himself torn between two worlds when he falls in love with a woman from a different culture. As he navigates the challenges of their relationship, Jack must confront his own prejudices and make difficult choices that will shape his future. Set against the backdrop of the American West, this captivating novel explores themes of love, identity, and the power of personal growth.

Genres: Western, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love, Cultural differences, Self-discovery
