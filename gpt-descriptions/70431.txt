Synopsis: In "Between Two Thieves" by Dehan Richard, a mysterious thief with a hidden agenda finds himself caught between two rival criminal organizations. As he navigates the treacherous world of crime, he must outsmart both sides while trying to uncover the truth behind a valuable artifact. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale of deception and betrayal will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Crime fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Redemption
