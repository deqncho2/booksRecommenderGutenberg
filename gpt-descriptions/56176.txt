Synopsis: In 'Guild Court: A London Story' by MacDonald, George, a young artist named Hugh Sutherland finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he moves into a boarding house in London's Guild Court. As he becomes acquainted with the eccentric residents, he discovers a hidden world of secrets, forbidden love, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, Hugh's perception of reality becomes blurred, and he must navigate the treacherous streets of London to uncover the truth before it consumes him.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Identity, Love and Betrayal, Society and Class
