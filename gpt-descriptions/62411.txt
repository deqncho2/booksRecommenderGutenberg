Synopsis: In 'Dick Merriwell's Glory; Or, Friends and Foes' by Standish, Burt L., follow the thrilling adventures of Dick Merriwell, a talented athlete and all-around good guy, as he navigates the treacherous world of college sports. From facing formidable opponents on the field to dealing with jealous rivals off the field, Dick must rely on his wit, skill, and unwavering determination to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to see if Dick can emerge victorious in the face of adversity.

Genres: Sports fiction, Adventure, Young adult

Themes: Friendship, Sportsmanship, Perseverance
