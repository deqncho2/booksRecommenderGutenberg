Synopsis: In 'A Short History of English Liberalism', Blease, W. Lyon takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of English liberalism, exploring its origins, key figures, and defining moments. From the tumultuous times of the English Civil War to the dawn of the 20th century, this concise yet comprehensive account delves into the political, social, and intellectual forces that shaped the liberal tradition in England. Through vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, Blease sheds light on the struggles, triumphs, and enduring ideals that have made English liberalism a powerful force in shaping the nation's history.

Genres: History, Political Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Liberalism, Political Ideologies, Social Change, Intellectual History
