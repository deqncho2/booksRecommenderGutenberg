Synopsis: 
In 'Left at Home: or, The Heart's Resting Place' by Code, Mary L., a young woman named Emily finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is left behind while her family embarks on a grand adventure. As she navigates the challenges of being alone, unexpected friendships and opportunities arise, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of her own dreams. With each passing day, Emily's world expands, and she begins to question the conventional expectations placed upon her. Will she find the courage to forge her own path, or will she succumb to the pressures of society?

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Independence, Self-discovery, Societal expectations
