Synopsis: In the remote wilderness of Canada, a group of hunters embark on a treacherous journey that leads them into the heart of ancient legends and supernatural terror. As they venture deeper into the hauntingly beautiful yet unforgiving landscape, they soon discover that they are not alone. A malevolent force, known as the Wendigo, lurks in the shadows, preying on their fears and testing the limits of their sanity. With each passing night, the hunters find themselves questioning their own humanity and the true nature of the creature they seek. Will they survive the harrowing ordeal, or will they succumb to the insidious power of the Wendigo?

Genres: Horror, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: Man vs. Nature, Madness, The Unknown
