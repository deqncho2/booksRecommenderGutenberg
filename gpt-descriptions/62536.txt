Synopsis: In 'Uttara, the Legend of the Turquoise' by Carlo de Fornaro, a mysterious turquoise stone holds the key to a hidden world of ancient legends and forgotten powers. When a young archaeologist stumbles upon this extraordinary artifact, she embarks on a thrilling journey that takes her across time and space, encountering mythical creatures and facing dangerous adversaries. As she unravels the secrets of the turquoise, she discovers her own hidden potential and the true nature of the world around her.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Mythology, Power of the Past, Self-Discovery
