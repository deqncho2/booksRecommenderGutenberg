Synopsis: 

In the second volume of 'History of the Royal Regiment of Artillery', Francis Duncan delves into the fascinating world of the artillery regiment, uncovering untold stories and shedding light on the pivotal role they played in shaping history. From the early days of cannons and muskets to the advanced weaponry of modern warfare, this comprehensive account takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the triumphs, challenges, and sacrifices of the men and women who served in the regiment. With meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Duncan brings to life the battles, strategies, and innovations that have defined the Royal Regiment of Artillery, making this volume an essential read for history enthusiasts and military buffs alike.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Military strategy, Technological advancements, Heroism, Sacrifice
