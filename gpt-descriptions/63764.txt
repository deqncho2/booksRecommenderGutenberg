Synopsis: In 'Divine Mercy; or, the temporal advantages of the Sabbath' by Bryan, George, a young woman named Emily finds herself torn between her duty to her family and her desire for personal freedom. As she navigates the complexities of societal expectations and her own aspirations, she discovers a hidden secret that could change everything. With themes of love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of happiness, this captivating novel explores the power of faith and the choices we make in the face of adversity.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Duty vs. personal freedom, societal expectations, secrets and revelations, faith and spirituality
