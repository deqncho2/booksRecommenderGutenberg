Synopsis: 
'Lost in the Air' by Roy J. Snell takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the skies. When a group of young aviators embarks on a daring journey, they find themselves facing unexpected challenges and dangers. As they navigate through treacherous weather, encounter mysterious characters, and uncover hidden secrets, their courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
