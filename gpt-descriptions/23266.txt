Synopsis: In 'Janet's Love and Service' by Margaret M. Robertson, a young woman named Janet finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is unexpectedly thrust into a world of wealth and privilege. As she navigates the complexities of love and service, Janet must confront her own insecurities and make difficult choices that will shape her future. With secrets, scandal, and a touch of romance, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Social class, Self-discovery
