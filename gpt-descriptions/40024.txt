Synopsis: In 'Rebecca's Promise', a young woman named Rebecca finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is forced to make a difficult decision that will shape her future. As she navigates through love, loss, and the complexities of family, Rebecca's journey takes her on a path filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will she be able to keep her promise and find happiness, or will the weight of her choices prove too heavy to bear?

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Family dynamics, Self-discovery
