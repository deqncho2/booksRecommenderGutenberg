Synopsis: In 'The New Wonder of the World: Buffalo, the Electric City' by Richmond, A. E., readers are transported to the vibrant city of Buffalo in the late 19th century, where a groundbreaking invention is about to change everything. As the city becomes the first in the world to be illuminated by electric lights, a web of intrigue, ambition, and danger unfolds. Amidst the dazzling glow of progress, a cast of captivating characters navigates their way through a world on the brink of transformation, where secrets and desires intertwine with the flickering lights of the electric city.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Innovation, Power, Ambition, Secrets
