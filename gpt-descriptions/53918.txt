Synopsis: In 'The Woman & the Priest' by Grazia Deledda, a small Sardinian village becomes the backdrop for a forbidden love affair between a young woman and a priest. As their relationship unfolds, the boundaries of faith, desire, and societal expectations are tested, leading to a gripping exploration of the complexities of love and the consequences of breaking the rules.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Religion, Social expectations, Sacrifice
