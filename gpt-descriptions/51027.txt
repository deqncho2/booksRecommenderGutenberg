Synopsis: In the futuristic city of New York, where flying cars and advanced technology are the norm, a mysterious jaywalker disrupts the flow of society. This enigmatic figure, known only as Jaywalker, possesses an uncanny ability to navigate through the bustling streets undetected, leaving authorities baffled. As the city's obsession with Jaywalker grows, a determined detective sets out to uncover the truth behind this elusive figure, leading to a thrilling cat-and-mouse chase that will challenge everything he thought he knew about law and order.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Surveillance society, Individuality vs. conformity, Power dynamics
