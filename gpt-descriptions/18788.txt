Synopsis: In 'Post-Prandial Philosophy' by Allen Grant, a group of friends gather for a delightful dinner party, but little do they know that their conversation will take an unexpected turn. As the evening progresses, the guests engage in a lively debate on various philosophical topics, challenging each other's beliefs and questioning the very nature of reality. With wit, humor, and intellectual depth, this thought-provoking novel explores the complexities of human existence and the power of philosophical inquiry.

Genres: Fiction, Philosophy, Satire

Themes: Existentialism, Perception, Truth, Intellectual Discourse
