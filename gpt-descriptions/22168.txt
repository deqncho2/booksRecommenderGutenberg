Synopsis: In 'The Golden Spears, and Other Fairy Tales' by Edmund Leamy, embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting and whimsical tales. From magical spears that hold unimaginable power to mischievous fairies and brave heroes, these stories will transport readers to a world filled with wonder and adventure. Each tale is a delightful blend of fantasy and folklore, weaving together elements of Irish mythology and traditional storytelling. With unexpected twists and turns, these fairy tales will leave readers spellbound, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies beyond the next magical encounter.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Mythology

Themes: Adventure, Magic, Heroism, Transformation
