Synopsis: In 'The Boy Allies at Jutland; Or, The Greatest Naval Battle of History' by Clair W. Hayes, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure set during World War I. As the British and German navies clash in the epic Battle of Jutland, two young American boys find themselves caught in the midst of the chaos. With their quick thinking and bravery, they must navigate the treacherous waters, outwit enemy forces, and uncover a secret that could change the course of the war. Will they emerge as heroes or become casualties of the greatest naval battle in history?

Genres: Historical fiction, Action, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, War
