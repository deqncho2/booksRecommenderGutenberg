Synopsis: In 'How to be Happy Though Married: Being a Handbook to Marriage', E. J. Hardy presents a captivating guide that explores the intricacies of married life. Through insightful anecdotes, practical advice, and thought-provoking reflections, Hardy delves into the challenges and joys of matrimony, offering readers a fresh perspective on how to cultivate happiness within the bounds of marriage. With a blend of wit, wisdom, and a touch of humor, this handbook provides valuable insights that will resonate with both newlyweds and long-time couples alike.

Genres: Self-help, Relationships, Marriage

Themes: Marriage, Happiness, Communication, Intimacy, Conflict Resolution
