Synopsis: In 'Better Meals for Less Money', Marietta McPherson Greenough takes readers on a culinary journey filled with mouthwatering recipes and clever tips to create delicious meals on a budget. From pantry staples to creative substitutions, Greenough's resourceful approach to cooking will inspire readers to elevate their meals without breaking the bank. With a focus on maximizing flavor and minimizing costs, this book is a must-read for anyone looking to save money while still enjoying tasty and satisfying dishes.

Genres: Cookbook, Personal Finance, Self-help

Themes: Budgeting, Frugal Living, Culinary Creativity
