Synopsis: In 'De Soto, Coronado, Cabrillo: Explorers of the Northern Mystery' by Lavender, David, embark on a thrilling journey through the uncharted territories of North America alongside three legendary explorers. From the treacherous swamps of Florida to the majestic deserts of the Southwest, witness their daring quests for gold, glory, and the unknown. As they encounter indigenous peoples, face harsh landscapes, and navigate political intrigue, the explorers' fates intertwine in unexpected ways, forever shaping the course of history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Ambition, Cultural Encounter
