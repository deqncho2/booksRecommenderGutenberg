Synopsis:
In "Through Shot and Flame: The Adventures and Experiences of J. D. Kestell Chaplain to President Steyn and General Christian De Wet," J. D. Kestell takes us on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous times of the Second Boer War. As the personal chaplain to President Steyn and General Christian De Wet, Kestell witnesses firsthand the horrors of war, the resilience of the Boer people, and the unwavering faith that sustains them in the face of adversity. From daring escapes and covert operations to moments of profound humanity and compassion, this gripping memoir offers a unique perspective on one of the most significant conflicts in South African history.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War

Themes: Resilience, Faith, Humanitarianism, War and Conflict
