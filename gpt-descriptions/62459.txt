Synopsis: In 'Usury; Or, Interest, Premium and Discount', Crittenden, S. H. delves into the controversial world of finance and explores the intricate web of power, greed, and manipulation that surrounds it. Through a captivating narrative, the author takes readers on a journey that exposes the dark underbelly of the financial industry, revealing the hidden forces that shape economies and individuals' lives. As the plot unfolds, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the characters find themselves entangled in a high-stakes game where the consequences are far-reaching. With its gripping storyline and thought-provoking insights, 'Usury; Or, Interest, Premium and Discount' offers a captivating exploration of the complex world of finance and its impact on society.

Genres: Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Finance, Power, Greed, Manipulation, Morality
