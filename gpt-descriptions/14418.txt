Synopsis: 
In 'Thrift' by Samuel Smiles, a young man named Walter embarks on a journey to achieve success and financial stability. Through a series of encounters and experiences, he learns the value of thrift and the importance of making wise financial decisions. As Walter navigates the challenges of life, he discovers that true wealth goes beyond monetary possessions. This captivating tale explores the transformative power of thrift and its impact on personal growth and happiness.

Genres: 
Fiction, Self-help, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Financial responsibility, Personal growth, Happiness
