Synopsis: In 'Those Times and These' by Irvin S. Cobb, readers are transported to a small town in the early 1900s, where the eccentric and colorful characters of the community come to life. Through a series of interconnected stories, Cobb weaves a tapestry of humor, nostalgia, and poignant observations, capturing the essence of a bygone era. From mischievous pranks to heartfelt moments, the author invites readers to laugh, reflect, and appreciate the timeless human experiences that transcend generations.

Genres: Fiction, Humor, Short Stories

Themes: Nostalgia, Community, Human Nature
