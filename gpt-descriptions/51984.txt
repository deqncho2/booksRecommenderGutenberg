Synopsis: In 'Thursday Evening: A Comedy in One Act', the lives of a group of eccentric characters intertwine during a seemingly ordinary Thursday evening. From a bumbling detective to a mysterious stranger, their hilarious encounters and unexpected revelations will keep you guessing until the very end. As the night unfolds, secrets are unveiled, misunderstandings arise, and chaos ensues, all leading to a climactic and uproarious finale. 

Genres: Comedy, Mystery, Farce

Themes: Deception, Miscommunication, Identity
