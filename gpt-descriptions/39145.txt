Synopsis: In 'The Devourers' by Vivanti, Annie, a young woman named Aisha encounters a mysterious stranger who claims to be a shape-shifter. As he weaves a mesmerizing tale of ancient beings and their insatiable hunger, Aisha becomes entangled in a world of dark secrets and forbidden desires. As the boundaries between reality and myth blur, Aisha must confront her own identity and the power that lies within her. This gripping and haunting novel explores the complexities of human nature and the eternal struggle between light and darkness.

Genres: Fantasy, Historical Fiction, Horror

Themes: Identity, Transformation, Desire, Good vs. Evil
