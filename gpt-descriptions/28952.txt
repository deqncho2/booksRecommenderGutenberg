Synopsis: In "Mr. Wicker's Window" by Carley Dawson, a young girl named Ariel discovers a mysterious antique shop and is drawn to a peculiar window display. As she becomes acquainted with the enigmatic Mr. Wicker, she embarks on a journey that intertwines the realms of reality and fantasy, leading her to uncover secrets about her own past and the true nature of the shop. With each visit, Ariel's perception of the world is challenged, and she must navigate a web of magic, love, and self-discovery.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Magic, Identity, Fate, Love, Redemption
