Synopsis: In 'Sentry of the Sky' by Evelyn E. Smith, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Helen Tackitt is faced with a perplexing mystery when a series of unexplained events occur in the skies above Earth. As she delves deeper into her investigation, she uncovers a shocking truth that could have far-reaching consequences for humanity. With time running out, Dr. Tackitt must use her intellect and resourcefulness to unravel the enigma and save the world from an impending catastrophe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Scientific discovery, Human ingenuity, Consequences of knowledge
