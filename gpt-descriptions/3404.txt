Synopsis:
In 'April Hopes' by William Dean Howells, we are introduced to a young couple, Tom and Kitty, who are navigating the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations in late 19th-century America. As they embark on their journey together, they encounter various challenges that test their commitment and force them to confront their own desires and ambitions. With a cast of vibrant characters and a backdrop of changing social norms, 'April Hopes' explores the delicate balance between personal fulfillment and societal obligations, ultimately leaving the reader questioning the true nature of happiness and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres:
Romance, Realism, Social Commentary

Themes:
Love and Marriage, Social Expectations, Personal Fulfillment
