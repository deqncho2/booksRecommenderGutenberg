Synopsis: In "In Times Like These" by Nellie L. McClung, a group of women in a small Canadian town find themselves at the forefront of a battle for women's rights. As they navigate the challenges of societal expectations and gender inequality, they form a bond that will change their lives forever. With wit, humor, and determination, these women challenge the status quo and fight for a better future for themselves and generations to come.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Feminist Literature, Social Commentary

Themes: Women's Rights, Gender Equality, Friendship, Empowerment
