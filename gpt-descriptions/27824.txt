Synopsis: 
In the small town of Waverly, a mysterious and powerful force known as the Juggernaut arrives, bringing with it a series of inexplicable events that disrupt the lives of its inhabitants. As the townspeople grapple with the consequences of the Juggernaut's presence, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the boundaries between reality and the supernatural blur. With each passing day, the Juggernaut's influence grows stronger, leaving the town on the brink of chaos and its residents questioning their own sanity. 

Genres: 
Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological Thriller

Themes: 
Power of belief, Identity, Perception of reality
