Synopsis: In 'Curious, if True: Strange Tales', Elizabeth Gaskell weaves a collection of eerie and mysterious stories that will leave readers captivated and questioning the boundaries of reality. From haunted houses and supernatural encounters to unexplained phenomena and strange coincidences, each tale takes the reader on a thrilling journey into the unknown. As the characters navigate through these strange occurrences, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and grapple with the inexplicable. With Gaskell's masterful storytelling and vivid descriptions, 'Curious, if True: Strange Tales' is a mesmerizing exploration of the supernatural that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural fiction, mystery

Themes: The supernatural, fear and the unknown, the power of storytelling
