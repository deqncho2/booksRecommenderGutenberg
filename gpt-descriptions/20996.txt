Synopsis: In 'An Epoch in History', a mysterious artifact is discovered, setting off a chain of events that will challenge the boundaries of time and space. As a group of unlikely heroes embarks on a perilous journey, they must unravel the secrets of the artifact and confront their own inner demons. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the very fabric of reality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Identity, Redemption
