Synopsis: In "The Shoes of Fortune" by Neil Munro, a mysterious pair of shoes brings unexpected fortune and misfortune to those who possess them. As the shoes change hands, their power becomes increasingly unpredictable, leading to a series of captivating and sometimes humorous events. This thrilling tale explores the consequences of greed, ambition, and the unpredictable nature of fate.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Fate, Fortune, Greed, Ambition
