Synopsis: 
In 'Wee Peter Pug: The Story of a Bit of Mischief and What Came of It' by Ernest A. Aris, readers are introduced to a mischievous little pug named Peter. Despite his small size, Peter's misadventures lead him on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. From encounters with cunning cats to daring escapes, Peter's escapades will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what mischief he will get into next. This heartwarming tale explores the power of friendship, the importance of bravery, and the joy of embracing one's true nature.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Mischief, Friendship, Bravery, Self-discovery
