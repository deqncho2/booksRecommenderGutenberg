Synopsis: In 'Contemporary American History, 1877-1913', Charles A. Beard takes readers on a captivating journey through a transformative period in American history. From the aftermath of the Civil War to the dawn of the Progressive Era, Beard delves into the political, economic, and social changes that shaped the nation during this pivotal time. Through meticulous research and insightful analysis, he explores the rise of industrialization, the struggles of labor movements, the emergence of new political ideologies, and the impact of these developments on American society. With a keen eye for detail and a compelling narrative style, Beard brings to life the key figures and events that defined this era, offering readers a fresh perspective on the complexities of American history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Science

Themes: Industrialization, Labor Movements, Political Ideologies, Social Change, Progressivism
