Synopsis: 
In 'Pride and His Prisoners' by A. L. O. E., a young and ambitious lawyer named Arthur finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and manipulation when he takes on a high-profile case. As he delves deeper into the case, Arthur discovers that the true battle lies not in the courtroom, but within the hearts and minds of those involved. With his own pride at stake, Arthur must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and find redemption.

Genres: Legal thriller, psychological drama, suspense

Themes: Pride, deception, redemption, moral ambiguity
