Synopsis: In 'Atalantis Major' by Daniel Defoe, a young Englishman embarks on a perilous journey to a hidden island, where he encounters a society unlike any he has ever known. As he navigates through political intrigue, forbidden love, and the clash of cultures, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets that could change the course of history. With its captivating narrative and unexpected twists, this novel takes readers on a thrilling adventure that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Political Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Colonialism, Power dynamics, Love and betrayal
