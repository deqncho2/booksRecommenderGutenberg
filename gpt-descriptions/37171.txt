Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Lost Sir Massingberd: A Romance of Real Life', the gripping tale continues as the mysterious disappearance of Sir Massingberd remains unsolved. As the search for answers intensifies, dark secrets and hidden motives come to light, leading to unexpected twists and turns. With the lives of the characters hanging in the balance, the truth behind Sir Massingberd's vanishing act becomes more elusive than ever. Will the truth be uncovered, or will it remain forever lost?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Identity and Disguise
