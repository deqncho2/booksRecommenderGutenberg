Synopsis: 

In 'Recipes Tried and True' by First Presbyterian Church (Marion, Ohio), the Ladies' Aid Society unveils a collection of mouthwatering recipes that have been passed down through generations. From delectable desserts to savory main courses, this cookbook is a treasure trove of culinary delights. But beyond the tantalizing recipes, the book also offers a glimpse into the rich history and close-knit community of the church. As the reader explores the pages, they will discover not only the secrets to creating delicious meals but also the stories and traditions that have shaped the lives of the church members. 

Genres: Cookbook, Memoir, Community

Themes: Culinary traditions, Community bonds, Heritage preservation
