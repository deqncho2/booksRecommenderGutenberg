Synopsis: In 'Black Star's Campaign: A Detective Story' by McCulley Johnston, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of a city plagued by corruption and deceit. When renowned detective, Black Star, is called upon to solve a high-profile murder case, he finds himself entangled in a web of conspiracy that reaches far beyond what he could have ever imagined. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Black Star must navigate a treacherous landscape of treachery, danger, and unexpected alliances, all while trying to uncover the truth and bring justice to a city on the brink of chaos.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Corruption, Betrayal, Justice
