Synopsis: 
In the small town of Florence, Alabama, a young man named Lucius Provine is accused of a heinous crime that shocks the community. As the trial unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of justice is questioned. Set against the backdrop of the post-Civil War South, "Birthright: A Novel" delves into the complexities of race, family, and the pursuit of truth. With its gripping narrative and compelling characters, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Legal Drama

Themes: Racism, Justice, Family, Truth
