Synopsis: In "Dig me no grave" by Robert E. Howard, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, bringing with him a dark secret that threatens to unravel the lives of its inhabitants. As the tension builds and the body count rises, the townspeople must confront their own demons and make a choice between justice and vengeance. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Morality and justice, The darkness within
