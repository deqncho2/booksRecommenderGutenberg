Synopsis: In "The Light Princess" by George MacDonald, a young princess is cursed with a peculiar affliction that causes her to float above the ground. Unable to experience gravity's pull, she lives a carefree and whimsical life, until a prince from a neighboring kingdom enters her world. As their paths intertwine, they embark on a journey filled with enchantment, adventure, and unexpected discoveries. Will the prince be able to break the curse and bring the princess back to reality?

Genres: Fantasy, Fairy Tale, Adventure

Themes: Love and sacrifice, the power of emotions, the search for identity
