Synopsis: In 'The Loom of the Desert' by Strobridge, Idah Meacham, a young woman named Zara finds herself caught between tradition and her own desires when she is forced into an arranged marriage. As she navigates the harsh realities of life in the desert, Zara discovers a hidden talent that could change her fate and the lives of those around her. With secrets, forbidden love, and a quest for independence, 'The Loom of the Desert' weaves a captivating tale of resilience and self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Tradition vs. Individuality, Love and Sacrifice, Female Empowerment
