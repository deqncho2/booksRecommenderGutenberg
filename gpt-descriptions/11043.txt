Synopsis: In 'Midnight' by Octavus Roy Cohen, a mysterious murder takes place in a small Southern town, shrouded in darkness and secrets. As the clock strikes twelve, a web of intrigue and suspicion unfolds, revealing the hidden desires and dark motives of the town's inhabitants. With each passing hour, the tension rises, and the truth becomes more elusive. Will the truth be uncovered before the night is over, or will the secrets remain buried in the shadows?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Southern Gothic

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town dynamics, Moral ambiguity
