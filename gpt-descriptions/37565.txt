Synopsis: 

'Psychical Miscellanea: Being Papers on Psychical Research, Telepathy, Hypnotism, Christian Science, etc.' is a captivating collection of papers written by J. Arthur Hill, delving into the fascinating realms of psychical research, telepathy, hypnotism, and Christian Science. Each paper presents a unique exploration of these enigmatic subjects, offering thought-provoking insights and raising intriguing questions about the nature of the human mind and its potential.

From investigating the mysteries of telepathic communication to examining the power of hypnotism and the controversial practices of Christian Science, Hill's papers take readers on a captivating journey through the uncharted territories of the mind. With a blend of scientific analysis, personal anecdotes, and philosophical musings, this collection offers a comprehensive exploration of various phenomena that challenge our understanding of the world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Psychology, Occult

Themes: Psychical research, Telepathy, Hypnotism, Christian Science, Human consciousness, Mind-body connection
