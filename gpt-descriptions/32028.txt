Synopsis: In "The Battle of Stone River" by Henry Myron Kendall, readers are transported to the heart of the American Civil War, where the fate of a nation hangs in the balance. Amidst the chaos and bloodshed, a diverse cast of characters, both soldiers and civilians, find themselves caught up in the relentless tide of war. As the battle rages on, alliances are tested, loyalties are questioned, and the true cost of freedom becomes painfully clear. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, Kendall brings to life the harrowing events of one of the war's most pivotal battles, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Action/Adventure

Themes: Sacrifice, Loyalty, Survival, The Human Cost of War
