Synopsis: In 'The Alo Man: $b Stories from the Congo', Lamprey and Pratt-Chadwick take readers on a captivating journey through the heart of the Congo, where they encounter the enigmatic figure known as the Alo Man. Through a series of interconnected stories, the authors delve into the mysterious and often dangerous world of the Alo Man, exploring the depths of his powers and the impact he has on the lives of those around him.
