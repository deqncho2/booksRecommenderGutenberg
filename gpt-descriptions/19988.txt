Synopsis: In "Little Maid Marian" by Amy Ella Blanchard, follow the captivating journey of Marian, a young girl who finds herself in a world of adventure and self-discovery. When her father is called away to war, Marian is left in the care of her strict Aunt Matilda. Determined to escape her mundane life, Marian embarks on a series of unexpected escapades, encountering colorful characters and facing thrilling challenges along the way. With her courage and resourcefulness, Marian learns valuable lessons about friendship, loyalty, and the power of imagination. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Independence, Resilience, Imagination
