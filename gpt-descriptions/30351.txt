Synopsis: In 'The Cup of Fury: A Novel of Cities and Shipyards' by Hughes, Rupert, we are transported to the bustling world of cities and shipyards, where the lives of diverse characters intertwine in a gripping tale. From the gritty streets to the towering shipyards, the story follows the journey of individuals who are driven by ambition, love, and the pursuit of justice. As secrets are unveiled and tensions rise, the characters find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger, where their choices will have far-reaching consequences. With vivid descriptions and a captivating narrative, this novel takes readers on a thrilling ride through the heart of urban life and the shipbuilding industry.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Love, Justice, Secrets, Consequences
