Synopsis: 
In the small town of Willow Creek, a mysterious new invention called the Shock Absorber has taken the community by storm. This revolutionary device promises to eliminate all pain and suffering, offering a life free from emotional turmoil. As the townspeople eagerly embrace this seemingly perfect solution, they soon discover that the Shock Absorber comes with a dark and unexpected consequence. A young woman named Lily becomes determined to uncover the truth behind this enigmatic invention, leading her on a thrilling journey that challenges her beliefs and forces her to confront the true nature of human emotions.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Technology and its consequences, Human emotions and vulnerability
