Synopsis: In 'Dave Porter and His Double; Or, The Disappearance of the Basswood Fortune' by Stratemeyer, Edward, Dave Porter finds himself caught up in a mysterious case involving a missing fortune. When a stranger claiming to be his long-lost twin brother appears, Dave's life takes an unexpected turn. As he delves deeper into the disappearance of the Basswood fortune, Dave must navigate a web of secrets, deceit, and danger. With his friends by his side, Dave embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth and reclaim what is rightfully his.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Identity, Family, Friendship, Trust
