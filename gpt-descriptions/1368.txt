Synopsis:
When the World Shook: Being an Account of the Great Adventure of Bastin, Bickley and Arbuthnot is a thrilling and captivating novel by H. Rider Haggard. The story follows three friends, Bastin, Bickley, and Arbuthnot, who embark on a remarkable journey that takes them to uncharted territories and challenges their beliefs about the world. As they encounter strange civilizations, ancient prophecies, and supernatural forces, their lives are forever changed. This gripping tale of adventure, mystery, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Science Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Belief Systems, Fate and Destiny
