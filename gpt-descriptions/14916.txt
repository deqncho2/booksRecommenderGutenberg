Synopsis: 
'Fairy Tales Every Child Should Know' is a captivating collection of timeless stories that will transport readers to enchanting worlds filled with magic, adventure, and wonder. From the beloved tales of Cinderella and Sleeping Beauty to lesser-known gems like The Ugly Duckling and The Little Mermaid, this anthology presents a diverse range of fairy tales that have captivated generations. Each story is filled with memorable characters, unexpected twists, and valuable life lessons that will resonate with readers of all ages. Whether it's a tale of bravery, love, or the power of imagination, these enchanting stories are sure to ignite the imagination and leave readers longing for more.

Genres: Fairy tales, Children's literature, Fantasy

Themes: Magic, Adventure, Love, Bravery, Imagination
