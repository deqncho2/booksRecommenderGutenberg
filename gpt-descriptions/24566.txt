Synopsis: 

In 'Faithfully Yours' by Lou Tabakow, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies when she discovers a mysterious letter hidden in her deceased grandmother's belongings. As she delves deeper into her family's history, Emily uncovers a shocking truth that will challenge everything she thought she knew about her loved ones. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception, where trust is a luxury she can no longer afford. As Emily races against time to uncover the truth, she must confront her own fears and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of her family's legacy.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Family Drama

Themes: Secrets and lies, Family bonds, Betrayal, Identity
