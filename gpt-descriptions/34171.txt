Synopsis: In 'Toppleton's Client; Or, A Spirit in Exile', the eccentric lawyer, Mr. Toppleton, finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving a mysterious client who claims to be a spirit in exile. As Toppleton delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and supernatural occurrences that challenge his rationality and push the boundaries of his belief. With his wit and unconventional methods, Toppleton must navigate through the realms of the living and the dead to solve the perplexing puzzle before him.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Humor

Themes: Belief and skepticism, the power of perception, the boundaries of reality
