Synopsis: In 'The Key to Peace' by Anna Marie Miles, a young woman named Lily discovers an ancient artifact that holds the power to bring harmony to a war-torn world. As she embarks on a perilous journey to unlock its secrets, she must navigate treacherous alliances, face her own inner demons, and confront the true meaning of peace. With unexpected twists and a captivating cast of characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Power of Unity, Self-discovery
