Synopsis: In 'Nobody's Child' by Dejeans, Elizabeth, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies when she discovers a mysterious photograph hidden in her deceased mother's belongings. Determined to uncover the truth about her identity, Emily embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling streets of New York City to the picturesque countryside of France. As she delves deeper into her mother's past, Emily uncovers shocking revelations that challenge everything she thought she knew about her family. With each clue she uncovers, Emily becomes more entangled in a dangerous game of deception, where the truth may be more elusive than she ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Family secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
