Synopsis: 
'Taxidermy and Zoological Collecting: A Complete Handbook for the Amateur Taxidermist, Collector, Osteologist, Museum-Builder, Sportsman, and Traveller' is an extensive guide that takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of taxidermy and zoological collecting. Written by William T. Hornaday and W. J. Holland, this comprehensive handbook provides invaluable insights and practical advice for amateurs and professionals alike. From the art of preserving and mounting animals to the intricacies of bone structure and museum curation, this book covers it all. With vivid descriptions and detailed instructions, readers will discover the secrets behind creating lifelike displays and learn about the adventures of collectors and sportsmen who have traveled the globe in pursuit of rare specimens. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Natural History

Themes: Taxidermy, Zoological collecting, Museum curation, Adventure, Preservation
