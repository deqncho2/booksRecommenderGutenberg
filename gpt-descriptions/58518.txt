Synopsis: In the second volume of the gripping trilogy, 'Kissing the Rod: A Novel', Edmund Yates takes readers on a thrilling journey through the lives of his complex characters. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, the story delves deeper into the intertwined lives of the aristocracy, the working class, and the criminal underworld. As secrets are unveiled and alliances are tested, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and redemption. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel will leave readers eagerly anticipating the final installment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Social class, Morality, Identity
