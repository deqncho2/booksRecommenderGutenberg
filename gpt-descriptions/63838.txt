Synopsis: In "Time Trap" by Frank Belknap Long, a group of scientists embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of time travel. As they delve deeper into the mysteries of the past, they find themselves trapped in a mind-bending web of paradoxes and alternate realities. With each step they take, the boundaries of time blur, and they must confront the consequences of their actions. As they struggle to escape the clutches of time itself, they discover that the true danger may lie within their own minds.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Mystery

Themes: The nature of time, Paradoxes, Consequences of actions
