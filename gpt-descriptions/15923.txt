Synopsis: In "The Boy and the Sunday School: A Manual of Principle and Method for the Work of the Sunday School with Teen Age Boys," John L. Alexander delves into the world of Sunday school education for teenage boys. Through insightful guidance and practical advice, Alexander explores the challenges and opportunities of teaching this unique age group, offering valuable strategies to engage and inspire young minds. This manual serves as a comprehensive resource for Sunday school teachers, providing them with the tools to create a meaningful and impactful learning experience for teenage boys.

Genres: Educational, Religious, Non-fiction

Themes: Sunday school education, Teenage boys, Teaching methods, Spiritual development
