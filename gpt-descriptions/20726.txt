Synopsis: In 'A Slave is a Slave' by H. Beam Piper, a young man finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and power struggles on a distant planet. As he navigates the treacherous world of interstellar trade, he must confront the harsh realities of slavery and the moral dilemmas it presents. With unexpected allies and dangerous enemies, he must decide where his loyalties lie and what he is willing to sacrifice for freedom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Political Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Slavery, Morality, Power Dynamics
