Synopsis: 
In 'Ethel Morton at Chautauqua', Ethel and her friends embark on a thrilling summer adventure at the renowned Chautauqua Institution. As they immerse themselves in the intellectual and cultural atmosphere, they encounter unexpected mysteries and uncover secrets that will test their courage and friendship. With each new discovery, Ethel's determination to solve the puzzles grows, leading her on a captivating journey of self-discovery and personal growth.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Self-discovery
