Synopsis: 
In 'Home-made Toy Motors', Alfred Powell Morgan presents a captivating and practical handbook that unveils the secrets behind building simple yet fully functional electric motors. With detailed instructions and step-by-step guidance, Morgan takes readers on an exciting journey into the world of DIY motors, revealing the fascinating principles and techniques that bring these miniature machines to life. From basic materials to advanced concepts, this handbook offers a wealth of knowledge for both beginners and experienced hobbyists alike. Get ready to embark on a thrilling adventure of creativity and innovation as you discover the joy of constructing your very own toy motors!

Genres: 
Non-fiction, DIY, Science

Themes: 
Creativity, Innovation, Science Education
