Synopsis: 
In 'Rhymes and Jingles' by Mary Mapes Dodge, readers are transported to a world filled with delightful verses and playful rhymes. This collection of poems takes us on a whimsical journey through the imagination, exploring various themes and subjects that will captivate both young and old. From the enchanting tales of fairies and magical creatures to the humorous anecdotes of everyday life, Dodge's words dance off the page, inviting readers to join in the joy of language and rhythm. With each turn of the page, readers will find themselves immersed in a world of laughter, wonder, and the timeless beauty of poetry.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Poetry, Fantasy

Themes: 
Imagination, Playfulness, Everyday Life
