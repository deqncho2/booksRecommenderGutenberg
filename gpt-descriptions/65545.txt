Synopsis: In "The Making of a Man" by James W. Lee, a young man named John embarks on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. As he navigates through various challenges and encounters unexpected mentors, John must confront his past, confront his fears, and ultimately find the strength to become the man he was meant to be. This coming-of-age story explores the complexities of identity, the power of resilience, and the transformative nature of personal growth.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Fiction, Drama

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Personal Growth
