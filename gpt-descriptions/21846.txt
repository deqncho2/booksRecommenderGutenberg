Synopsis: In 'Crowded Out o' Crofield; or, The Boy who made his Way', we follow the journey of a young boy named Ben Bradford, who finds himself in a challenging situation when his family's farm is sold. Forced to leave his beloved home in Crofield, Ben embarks on a remarkable adventure filled with unexpected encounters, trials, and triumphs. As he navigates through various obstacles, Ben's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test, ultimately shaping his character and revealing his true potential.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Resilience, Self-discovery, Overcoming adversity
