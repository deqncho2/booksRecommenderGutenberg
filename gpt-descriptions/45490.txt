Synopsis: 
In 'Stand By: The Story of a Boy's Achievement in Radio' by Hugh McAlister, readers are introduced to a young boy named Jack who discovers a hidden talent for radio broadcasting. As he navigates the challenges of his personal life and the world of radio, Jack's passion and determination lead him on an extraordinary journey. With the support of his friends and mentors, he learns valuable lessons about friendship, perseverance, and the power of following one's dreams. This heartwarming tale takes readers on a captivating adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns, leaving them eagerly anticipating Jack's next move.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Pursuing dreams, Friendship, Overcoming obstacles
