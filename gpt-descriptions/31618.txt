Synopsis: 
'Silver Links' is a captivating collection of salutatory, valedictory, and other addresses delivered at the first five commencements of the female stenographic and typewriting class of the General Society of Mechanics and Tradesmen of the City of New York. Through a series of powerful speeches, this book offers a glimpse into the lives and aspirations of these remarkable women, as they navigate the challenges and triumphs of pursuing a career in a male-dominated field. From heartfelt expressions of gratitude to inspiring calls for equality, 'Silver Links' showcases the resilience and determination of these pioneering individuals.

Genres: Non-fiction, Speeches, Feminism

Themes: Gender equality, Empowerment, Education, Professionalism
