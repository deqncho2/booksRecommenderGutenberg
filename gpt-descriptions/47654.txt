Synopsis: In 'The Monster' by Edgar Saltus, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges from the shadows, captivating the attention of those around him. As his true nature slowly unravels, the reader is drawn into a world of dark secrets, forbidden desires, and the blurred lines between good and evil. With each twist and turn, the story delves deeper into the psyche of the protagonist, leaving the reader questioning their own perceptions of morality and the human condition.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Gothic fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Morality, Forbidden desires, Human nature
