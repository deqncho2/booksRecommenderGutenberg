Synopsis: In 'The Light of Scarthey: A Romance' by Castle, Egerton, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself drawn to the mysterious Scarthey Island, where she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden love, and supernatural occurrences. As she delves deeper into the island's dark history, Evelyn must confront her own fears and uncover the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Gothic fiction, romance, mystery

Themes: Forbidden love, the power of secrets, the supernatural, self-discovery
