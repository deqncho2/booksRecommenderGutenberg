Synopsis: In 'How to Make Friends' by Jim Harmon, a socially awkward teenager named Alex embarks on a journey to discover the secrets of making friends. Faced with the challenges of fitting in and overcoming his own insecurities, Alex navigates through humorous and heartwarming situations that teach him valuable lessons about friendship and self-acceptance. As he learns to step out of his comfort zone, Alex discovers that true friendship can be found in the most unexpected places.

Genres: Young Adult, Coming-of-Age, Comedy

Themes: Friendship, Self-Discovery, Acceptance
