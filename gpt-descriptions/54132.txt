Synopsis: In 'A Loyal Little Red-Coat: A Story of Child-life in New York a Hundred Years Ago', Ruth Ogden takes us on a captivating journey through the streets of New York City in the early 19th century. The story follows the adventures of a young boy named Thomas, who finds himself caught between his loyalty to his British heritage and his growing affection for his American friends. As Thomas navigates the challenges of friendship, family, and identity, readers are transported to a bygone era filled with excitement, danger, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Identity, Cultural Differences
