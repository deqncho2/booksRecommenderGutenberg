Synopsis: In 'The Hand of Providence: As Shown in the History of Nations and Individuals, From the Great Apostasy to the Restoration of the Gospel,' Joseph Harvey Ward takes readers on a captivating journey through history, unveiling the hidden hand of divine intervention in the rise and fall of nations and the lives of individuals. From ancient civilizations to modern times, Ward explores the remarkable events and individuals that shaped the course of humanity, revealing a pattern of providential guidance that transcends time and space. This thought-provoking work will leave readers pondering the intricate tapestry of history and the role of divine influence in shaping our world.

Genres: Historical, Religious, Non-fiction

Themes: Divine Providence, Historical Significance, Faith, Human Agency
