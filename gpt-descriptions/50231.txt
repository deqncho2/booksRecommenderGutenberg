Synopsis: 
"The Romance of Modern Sieges" takes readers on a thrilling journey through the personal adventures, resourcefulness, and daring of both besiegers and the besieged in various parts of the world. From ancient fortresses to modern cities, this book delves into the captivating stories of those involved in the intense struggles of siege warfare. With vivid descriptions and gripping narratives, Edward Gilliat brings to life the triumphs, tragedies, and unexpected twists that unfold during these epic battles.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Adventure

Themes: Courage and resilience in the face of adversity, the strategic and tactical aspects of siege warfare, the human experience in times of conflict
