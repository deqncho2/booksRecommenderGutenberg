Synopsis: In the small Irish town of Chapelizod, a mysterious murder takes place near an old churchyard. As the investigation unfolds, secrets from the past are unearthed, revealing a web of scandal, deceit, and forbidden love. The lives of the town's inhabitants become intertwined in a haunting tale of dark secrets and the consequences of past actions. With its eerie atmosphere and gripping mystery, "The House by the Church-Yard" will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the chilling events that haunt Chapelizod.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Historical fiction

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Forbidden love, Small-town intrigue, Supernatural elements
