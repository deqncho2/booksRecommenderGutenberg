Synopsis: In "Mad Anthony's Scouts; or, The Rangers of Kentucky," Edward Sylvester Ellis takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of Kentucky. Set during the American Revolution, the story follows a group of fearless scouts led by the legendary Mad Anthony Wayne. As they navigate treacherous terrain, encounter hostile Native American tribes, and face the perils of war, the scouts must rely on their cunning, bravery, and unbreakable bond to survive. With each page, the stakes rise higher, and the reader is left on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning to the next chapter to discover the fate of these daring rangers.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Survival, Loyalty
