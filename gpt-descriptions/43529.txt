Synopsis: In 'Secresy; or, Ruin on the Rock' by Eliza Fenwick, a young woman named Emily finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when she is sent to live on a remote island. As she uncovers the mysteries surrounding her new home, Emily must navigate treacherous relationships, hidden agendas, and the consequences of her own actions. With danger lurking around every corner, she must decide who to trust and how far she is willing to go to protect herself and those she cares about.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, Betrayal, Isolation, Identity, Power dynamics
