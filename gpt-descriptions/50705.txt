Synopsis: In 'The de Bercy Affair' by Tracy, Louis, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he is called upon to investigate a high-profile murder case. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the de Bercy family, he uncovers a labyrinth of deceit, hidden motives, and unexpected alliances. With each clue leading him closer to the truth, the detective must navigate a treacherous landscape of power, wealth, and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. As the tension rises and the stakes become higher, the detective must rely on his wit and intuition to solve the puzzle before it consumes him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Power, Betrayal
