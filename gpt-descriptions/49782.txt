Synopsis: In 'The Czar: A tale of the Time of the First Napoleon' by Deborah Alcock, a young Englishwoman finds herself caught in the midst of political intrigue and danger during the tumultuous era of Napoleon Bonaparte. As she navigates the treacherous world of European politics, she becomes entangled with a mysterious Russian nobleman who holds the fate of nations in his hands. With secrets, romance, and high-stakes adventure, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Political intrigue, Love and sacrifice, Power and manipulation
