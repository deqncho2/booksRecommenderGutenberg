Synopsis:
In "Grey Wethers: A Romantic Novel," Victoria Sackville-West takes readers on a captivating journey through the English countryside. The story revolves around a young woman named Emily, who finds herself drawn to the enigmatic charm of Grey Wethers, an ancient stone circle. As she delves deeper into the history and mystery surrounding the stones, Emily becomes entangled in a web of love, secrets, and unexpected revelations. With vivid descriptions and a touch of the supernatural, Sackville-West weaves a tale that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres:
Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Love and passion, the power of ancient places, the search for identity and belonging
