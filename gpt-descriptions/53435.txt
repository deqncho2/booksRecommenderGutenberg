Synopsis: 
'At Minas Basin, and Other Poems' by Theodore H. Rand is a collection of captivating and thought-provoking poems that take readers on a journey through various landscapes, emotions, and experiences. From the enchanting shores of Minas Basin to the depths of the human soul, Rand's words paint vivid pictures and evoke powerful emotions. Each poem offers a unique perspective on life, love, nature, and the human condition, leaving readers with a renewed sense of wonder and a deeper understanding of the world around them.

Genres:
Poetry, Nature, Emotion

Themes:
Nature, Love, Reflection, Human experience
