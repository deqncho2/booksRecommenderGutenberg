Synopsis: In 'Halloween, a Romaunt; with Lays Meditative and Devotional', Coxe takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical and enchanting world of Halloween. Through a series of meditative and devotional lays, the author explores the deeper meaning behind this ancient holiday, delving into the realms of love, loss, and the supernatural. As the night unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the reader is left pondering the true nature of Halloween and its significance in our lives.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Halloween, spirituality, love, loss, the supernatural
