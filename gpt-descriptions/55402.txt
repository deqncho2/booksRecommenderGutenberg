Synopsis: In 'Our Fellows; Or, Skirmishes with the Swamp Dragoons' by Castlemon, Harry, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves embroiled in a thrilling series of encounters with a mysterious group known as the Swamp Dragoons. As they navigate through dangerous swamps and face unexpected challenges, the boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and friendship to unravel the secrets surrounding the enigmatic Swamp Dragoons. With each skirmish, they uncover more about the true nature of their adversaries and the hidden treasures that lie within the swamp. This action-packed tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the ultimate fate of the boys and the Swamp Dragoons.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
