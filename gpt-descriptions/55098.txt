Synopsis: In 'Strong and Steady; Or, Paddle Your Own Canoe' by Horatio Alger, Jr., we follow the journey of a young protagonist who faces numerous challenges and setbacks in his pursuit of success. From humble beginnings to unexpected twists of fate, this gripping tale explores the power of determination, resilience, and self-reliance. As our hero navigates the turbulent waters of life, readers will be captivated by the unexpected turns and inspiring lessons that unfold.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman

Themes: Perseverance, Self-reliance, Social mobility
