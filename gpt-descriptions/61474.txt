Synopsis: In 'Strain' by L. Ron Hubbard, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Henry Mudge discovers a groundbreaking formula that has the potential to change the world. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but also the fate of humanity. With time running out, Dr. Mudge must navigate a treacherous web of conspiracy, betrayal, and danger to protect his discovery and ensure the survival of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific discovery, Conspiracy, Betrayal, Survival
