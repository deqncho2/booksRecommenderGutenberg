Synopsis:
In 'Fear' by A. Mosso, a gripping psychological thriller, a renowned psychiatrist finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse when a mysterious patient with a dark secret seeks his help. As the doctor delves deeper into the patient's troubled mind, he becomes increasingly aware that his own life is at stake. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and illusion blurs, leaving the reader questioning who can be trusted and what is truly lurking in the shadows.

Genres:
Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes:
Fear, Identity, Trust, Mental health
