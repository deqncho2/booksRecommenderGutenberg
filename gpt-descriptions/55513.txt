Synopsis: In "Sea Scouts All: How the 'Olivette' was won," Percy F. Westerman takes readers on an exhilarating adventure on the high seas. When a group of Sea Scouts embark on a sailing expedition, they find themselves facing treacherous waters, unexpected challenges, and a mysterious enemy. As they navigate through dangerous situations, the young scouts must rely on their skills, teamwork, and determination to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. With thrilling twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Resilience
