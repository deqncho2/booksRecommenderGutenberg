Synopsis: 

In 'Aids to Forensic Medicine and Toxicology', author W.G. Aitchison Robertson delves into the fascinating world of forensic science and toxicology, offering a captivating exploration of the methods and techniques used to solve crimes and uncover the truth. From examining crime scenes to analyzing evidence, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through the intricate world of forensic investigation. With gripping case studies and detailed explanations, Robertson sheds light on the crucial role forensic medicine plays in the pursuit of justice. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Crime, Science

Themes: Forensic investigation, Crime solving, Justice
