Synopsis: In the small town of Allenwood, a young woman named Mary Cary finds herself at the center of a scandal that threatens to tear apart the community. As she navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations, Mary's resilience and determination are put to the test. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Virginia countryside, 'The Mettle of the Pasture' is a captivating tale of secrets, redemption, and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Scandal, Social expectations, Redemption, Forgiveness
