Synopsis: In 'With the Persian Expedition', Donohoe takes readers on a thrilling journey through ancient Persia, filled with political intrigue, epic battles, and unexpected alliances. As the protagonist navigates the treacherous landscape, they must confront their own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape the fate of nations. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this historical adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Political Thriller

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
