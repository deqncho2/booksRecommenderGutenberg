Synopsis: 
In "The Country House" by John Galsworthy, a wealthy family gathers at their country estate for a summer retreat, but tensions and secrets simmer beneath the surface. As the characters navigate their complex relationships and grapple with their own desires and ambitions, the idyllic setting becomes a stage for hidden conflicts and unexpected revelations. With a cast of intriguing and flawed characters, this gripping tale explores the complexities of family dynamics and the consequences of unspoken truths.

Genres: Drama, Family Saga, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Family relationships, Class divide, Secrets and lies, Desire and ambition
