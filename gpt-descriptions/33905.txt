Synopsis: In "The Sages and Heroes of the American Revolution," author Judson L. Carroll takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and experiences of the remarkable individuals who played pivotal roles in the American Revolution. From the courageous leaders who spearheaded the fight for independence to the unsung heroes who made sacrifices behind the scenes, this book offers a compelling exploration of the human spirit and the power of unity in the face of adversity. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Carroll brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices of these extraordinary individuals, shedding light on the untold stories that shaped the birth of a nation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, American history

Themes: Courage, Leadership, Patriotism, Sacrifice, Unity
