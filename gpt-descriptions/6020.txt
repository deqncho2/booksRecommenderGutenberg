Synopsis: 
In "Cappy Ricks Retires: But That Doesn't Keep Him from Coming Back Stronger Than Ever," the indomitable Cappy Ricks finds himself facing retirement, but retirement doesn't seem to suit him well. Just when he thinks he can finally relax and enjoy his golden years, a new opportunity arises that pulls him back into the world of business and adventure. With his trademark wit and determination, Cappy Ricks embarks on a new chapter of his life, proving that retirement is just a word and that he is still a force to be reckoned with.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Humor

Themes: Resilience, Aging, Entrepreneurship
