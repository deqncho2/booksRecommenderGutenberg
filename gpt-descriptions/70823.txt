Synopsis: Step into the captivating world of John Singer Sargent, one of the most celebrated artists of his time, as the Grand Central Art Galleries proudly presents a retrospective exhibition of his important works. From the vibrant streets of Paris to the serene landscapes of Italy, immerse yourself in Sargent's masterful brushstrokes and discover the stories behind his iconic portraits. Delve into the artist's life and witness the evolution of his style, as this extraordinary exhibition takes you on a journey through the depths of his artistic genius.

Genres: Art, Biography, Historical

Themes: Portraiture, Impressionism, Society and Culture
