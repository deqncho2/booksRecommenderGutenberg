Synopsis: 
In 'Diplomatic Immunity' by Robert Sheckley, a young diplomat finds himself in a peculiar situation when he is assigned to a distant planet known for its eccentric inhabitants. As he navigates the complex web of intergalactic politics and cultural misunderstandings, he must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to survive. However, he soon discovers that the true challenge lies not in the diplomatic negotiations, but in the unexpected twists and turns that await him at every corner.

Genres: Science Fiction, Satire, Adventure

Themes: Intercultural communication, Political intrigue, Survival
