Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Oscar Wilde' is a comprehensive collection of the literary works of the renowned playwright and poet, Oscar Wilde. This anthology includes a wide range of his works, from plays and essays to novels and poems. Dive into the captivating world of Wilde's wit, satire, and social commentary as you explore his iconic works such as "The Importance of Being Earnest," "The Picture of Dorian Gray," and "Lady Windermere's Fan." This index serves as a gateway to Wilde's brilliant mind and his timeless contributions to literature.

Genres: Drama, Fiction, Poetry, Essays, Satire

Themes: Society and Morality, Art and Aesthetics, Identity and Self-Discovery
