Synopsis: 
In 'The history and poetry of finger-rings' by Edwards, Charles, embark on a captivating journey through time and culture as the author delves into the fascinating world of finger-rings. From ancient civilizations to modern times, this book explores the intricate craftsmanship, symbolic meanings, and poetic allure behind these small but significant adornments. Discover the stories behind famous rings, the secrets they hold, and the emotions they evoke, as Edwards uncovers the hidden narratives woven into these tiny works of art.

Genres: 
History, Art, Poetry

Themes: 
Symbolism, Cultural Significance, Personal Expression
