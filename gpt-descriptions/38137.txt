Synopsis: 
In 'Stories of the Badger State', Reuben Gold Thwaites takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and folklore of Wisconsin. From the early Native American tribes to the European settlers, Thwaites weaves together a collection of intriguing tales that showcase the spirit and resilience of the people who shaped the Badger State. Through stories of adventure, love, and mystery, readers will uncover the hidden gems and untold legends that have shaped Wisconsin's identity. From the shores of Lake Michigan to the rolling hills of the Driftless Area, this collection of stories will transport readers to a time when the land was wild and the possibilities were endless.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Folklore, Short Stories

Themes: History, Identity, Resilience
