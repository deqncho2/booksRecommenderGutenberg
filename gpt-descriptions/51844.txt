Synopsis: In "Someone to watch over me" by Gold, H. L. and Gale, Floyd C., a gripping tale unfolds as a young woman finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. When a series of unsettling events begin to occur, she becomes the target of an unknown adversary, forcing her to confront her deepest fears and rely on her wits to survive. As the tension escalates, secrets are revealed, and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. With each twist and turn, the reader is left guessing until the very end, eager to uncover the truth behind the protagonist's plight.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Trust, Survival
