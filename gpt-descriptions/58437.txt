Synopsis: 

'Colored Troops in the French Army: A Report from the Department of State Relating to the Colored Troops in the French Army and the Number of French Colonial Troops in the Occupied Territory' is a compelling non-fiction work that delves into the often overlooked history of colored troops in the French Army during a specific period. This report, compiled by the United States Department of State, provides a fascinating account of the presence and contributions of these troops, shedding light on their experiences and the challenges they faced. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Military

Themes: Racial discrimination, Military history, Colonialism
