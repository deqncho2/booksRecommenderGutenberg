Synopsis: 
'Eidolon; or, The Course of a Soul; and Other Poems' by Walter Richard Cassels is a captivating collection of poems that takes readers on a profound journey through the depths of the human soul. From exploring the complexities of love and loss to delving into the mysteries of existence and the afterlife, Cassels weaves together a tapestry of emotions and philosophical musings. Each poem is a lyrical masterpiece, inviting readers to reflect on the nature of their own souls and the universal questions that have haunted humanity for centuries.

Genres: Poetry, Philosophy, Spiritual

Themes: Love and Loss, Existentialism, Transcendence, Metaphysics
