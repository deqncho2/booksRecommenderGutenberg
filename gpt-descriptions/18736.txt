Synopsis: 
In 'Illustrated Catalogue of the Collections Obtained from the Indians of New Mexico And Arizona in 1879', James Stevenson takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich cultural heritage of the Native American tribes of the Southwest. Through detailed descriptions and stunning illustrations, Stevenson showcases the diverse artifacts and objects collected during his expedition, shedding light on the customs, traditions, and artistic expressions of these indigenous communities. From intricately woven textiles to intricately carved pottery, this catalogue offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the Native American tribes of New Mexico and Arizona.

Genres: Ethnography, Anthropology, Art History

Themes: Native American culture, Southwest history, Material culture
