Synopsis: In "Christ Legends" by Selma Lagerlöf, readers are transported to a world where miracles and wonders unfold. From a talking Christ child to a mysterious white horse, each story in this collection is a captivating blend of folklore and spirituality. Through these enchanting tales, Lagerlöf explores the power of faith, the resilience of the human spirit, and the enduring presence of miracles in our lives.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: Faith, Miracles, Spirituality, Human Resilience
