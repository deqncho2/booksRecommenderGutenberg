Synopsis: "The Rod in Pickle; or, An Answer to the Appeal of John Church, the Obelisk Preacher" is a captivating narrative that delves into the intriguing circumstances surrounding John Church's departure from Banbury, Oxfordshire. This book presents an authentic account of the charges brought against him, the subsequent meeting of his friends, and the outcome of that gathering. Additionally, it includes Church's compelling letters to the managers of the Banbury Meeting-House, imploring them to keep the cause of his dismissal concealed in London. With its gripping storyline and mysterious undertones, this work promises to captivate readers as they uncover the truth behind Church's departure.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Biography

Themes: Scandal and Intrigue, Religious Controversy, Loyalty and Betrayal
