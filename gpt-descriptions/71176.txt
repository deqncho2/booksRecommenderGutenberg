Synopsis:
In "Ruth Fielding in Alaska: Or, The Girl Miners of Snow Mountain," Ruth Fielding embarks on an exciting adventure to the snowy landscapes of Alaska. Determined to prove her worth as a young woman, Ruth joins a group of girl miners in their quest for gold. As she faces the challenges of the harsh environment and the dangers lurking in the wilderness, Ruth's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. Along the way, she forms deep friendships and discovers the true meaning of courage and perseverance.

Genres:
Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes:
Courage, Perseverance, Friendship, Gender Equality
