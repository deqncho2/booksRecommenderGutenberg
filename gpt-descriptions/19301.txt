Synopsis: 

'Extracts from a Journal of a Voyage of Visitation in the "Hawk," 1859' takes readers on a captivating journey aboard the ship "Hawk" as it embarks on a mysterious voyage in the year 1859. Through the eyes of the journal's author, Edward Feild, we are transported to a world of unknown lands, thrilling encounters, and unexpected discoveries. As the ship sails through uncharted waters, the journal entries unveil a series of intriguing events, encounters with fascinating characters, and encounters with the unknown. With each turn of the page, readers will find themselves immersed in a gripping tale that keeps them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Intrigue
