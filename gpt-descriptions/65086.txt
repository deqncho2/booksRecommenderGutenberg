Synopsis: In 'World of the Mad' by Anderson, Poul, a brilliant scientist named John Thomas is thrust into a mind-bending adventure when he discovers a hidden world within the human mind. As he delves deeper into this mysterious realm, he encounters strange creatures, ancient civilizations, and a power that could reshape reality itself. With his sanity hanging in the balance, John must navigate a treacherous landscape of madness and uncover the truth behind this enigmatic world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Perception of reality, Exploration of the human mind, Sanity vs. madness
