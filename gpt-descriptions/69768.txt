Synopsis: In the aftermath of a tragic bridge collapse in 18th-century Peru, a Franciscan monk becomes obsessed with understanding the interconnected lives of the five people who perished. Through meticulous research and interviews, he uncovers the hidden depths of their stories, revealing the profound impact they had on those around them. As he delves deeper into their lives, he grapples with questions of fate, love, and the meaning of life itself.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Fate and destiny, Love and relationships, The fragility of life, The interconnectedness of humanity
