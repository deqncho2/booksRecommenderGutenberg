Synopsis: In 'A Child of the Glens; or, Elsie's Fortunes' by Edward N. Hoare, readers are transported to the enchanting Scottish Highlands, where Elsie, a young girl with a mysterious past, embarks on a journey that will change her life forever. As she navigates through the rugged landscapes and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Elsie's resilience and determination are put to the test. With secrets waiting to be unraveled and unexpected twists along the way, this captivating tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover Elsie's true destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Fate
