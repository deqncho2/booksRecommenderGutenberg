Synopsis: 
'From Adam's Peak to Elephanta: Sketches in Ceylon and India' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant landscapes and diverse cultures of Ceylon (now Sri Lanka) and India. Edward Carpenter, an intrepid traveler and keen observer, shares his vivid experiences and encounters with the local people, their customs, and the breathtaking natural wonders he encounters along the way. From the awe-inspiring Adam's Peak in Ceylon to the mystical Elephanta Caves in India, Carpenter's sketches paint a vivid picture of the beauty and enchantment of these exotic lands.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Diversity, Natural Beauty
