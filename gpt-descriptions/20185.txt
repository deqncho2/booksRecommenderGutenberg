Synopsis: 
'Eventide: A Series of Tales and Poems' by Afton, Effie is a captivating collection that takes readers on a journey through a tapestry of emotions and experiences. From hauntingly beautiful poems to enchanting short stories, this book explores the depths of human nature, delving into the realms of love, loss, and the mysteries of life. Each tale and poem is a window into a different world, filled with vivid imagery and thought-provoking narratives that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Poetry, Short Stories, Fiction

Themes: Love, Loss, Human Nature, Mystery
