Synopsis: In "The Slavery Question: Speech of Hon. John M. Landrum, of La., Delivered in the House of Representatives, April 27, 1860," John M. Landrum delivers a powerful and thought-provoking speech that addresses the contentious issue of slavery. With eloquence and conviction, Landrum explores the complexities surrounding this deeply divisive topic, shedding light on the perspectives of both proponents and opponents of slavery. As tensions rise and the nation stands on the brink of a defining moment, Landrum's words resonate with urgency and significance, making this speech a crucial contribution to the ongoing debate.

Genres: Historical, Political, Speech

Themes: Slavery, Morality, States' Rights, National Unity
