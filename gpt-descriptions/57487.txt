Synopsis: 
"The Juggler's Oracle; or, The Whole Art of Legerdemain Laid Open" is a captivating and comprehensive guide to the world of magic and illusion. Written by Boaz, H. (Herman), this book unveils the newest and most astonishing tricks and experiments in the realm of legerdemain. From mind-boggling card tricks to mesmerizing displays with cups and balls, the reader will be enthralled by the author's expertise in conveying money and rings, manipulating boxes, fire, strings, and knots. Moreover, the book delves into the realm of optical illusions, chymical changes, and magical cards, presenting a plethora of curious experiments that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Magic, Illusion, Entertainment

Themes: Deception, Skill, Wonder
