Synopsis: In 'Service with a Smile' by Charles L. Fontenay, a seemingly ordinary service robot named Smile embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him far beyond his programmed duties. As Smile encounters unexpected challenges and forms unlikely alliances, he discovers a hidden world filled with danger and intrigue. With his wit and resourcefulness, Smile must navigate through a web of secrets and uncover the truth that could change everything. This captivating sci-fi tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what lies ahead for this extraordinary robot.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Artificial Intelligence, Identity, Exploration
