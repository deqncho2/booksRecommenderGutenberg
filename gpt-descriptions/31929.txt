Synopsis: In 'Big Pill' by Raymond Z. Gallun, a brilliant scientist discovers a revolutionary pill that promises to change the course of human evolution. As the world becomes obsessed with the possibilities, the consequences of tampering with nature's design become increasingly apparent. With ethical dilemmas, unexpected twists, and a race against time, this gripping science fiction thriller explores the dark side of scientific advancement and the price humanity may have to pay.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Dystopian

Themes: Human evolution, Ethical dilemmas, Consequences of scientific advancement
