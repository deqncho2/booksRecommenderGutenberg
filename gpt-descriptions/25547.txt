Synopsis: In 'The Sundering Flood' by Morris, William, a young couple finds themselves caught in a world of magic and adventure when a catastrophic event unleashes ancient forces. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter mythical creatures, they must confront their own inner demons and make choices that will shape their destiny. With its richly imagined world and captivating characters, this tale of love, courage, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Love and sacrifice, the power of nature, the search for identity
