Synopsis: In 'Olivia in India' by Douglas, O., Olivia, a young and adventurous woman, embarks on a journey to India, seeking to discover herself and immerse in the vibrant culture of the country. As she navigates through the bustling streets of Delhi, the serene landscapes of Rajasthan, and the spiritual city of Varanasi, Olivia encounters a series of unexpected events and encounters that challenge her perceptions and ignite her passion for life. From the bustling markets to the tranquil temples, Olivia's journey becomes a transformative experience that will leave readers captivated and yearning for their own adventure in India.

Genres: Travel, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Cultural immersion, Transformation
