Synopsis: 

In "The North Americans of Antiquity: Their origin, migrations, and type of civilization considered," John T. Short takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient history of North America. Delving into the origins and migrations of the continent's early inhabitants, Short uncovers a rich tapestry of civilizations that once thrived across the land. From the enigmatic mound builders to the sophisticated cultures of the Southwest, the author paints a vivid picture of the diverse and complex societies that shaped the North American landscape. With meticulous research and compelling storytelling, Short invites readers to explore the mysteries and wonders of a forgotten past, shedding light on the remarkable achievements of these ancient peoples.

Genres: History, Anthropology, Archaeology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Migration, Cultural diversity
