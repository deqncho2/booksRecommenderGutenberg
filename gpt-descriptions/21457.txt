Synopsis: In "The Cruise of the Mary Rose; Or, Here and There in the Pacific," embark on a thrilling adventure as the crew of the Mary Rose sets sail across the vast Pacific Ocean. Join the young protagonist, Jack Rogers, as he encounters treacherous storms, encounters with native tribes, and unexpected discoveries on remote islands. As the voyage unfolds, secrets are revealed, friendships are tested, and the true nature of courage and resilience is explored. Will Jack and his companions overcome the challenges that lie ahead, or will they be forever lost at sea?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Friendship, Identity
