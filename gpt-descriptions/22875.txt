Synopsis: In 'Circus' by Alan Edward Nourse, a young medical student finds himself entangled in a mysterious world when he stumbles upon a traveling circus that harbors a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic circus, he uncovers a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden knowledge that threatens to consume him. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate the treacherous path between reality and illusion, all while trying to unravel the truth behind the circus and its captivating performers.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Perception, Power
