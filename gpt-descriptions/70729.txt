Synopsis: In 'Vortigern; an historical play', the renowned author W. H. Ireland takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous world of ancient Britain. Set during the Dark Ages, the play follows the rise and fall of the enigmatic King Vortigern, a ruler plagued by political intrigue, betrayal, and a mysterious prophecy. As Vortigern grapples with his own demons and the ever-looming threat of invasion, the play delves into the complex dynamics of power, loyalty, and the consequences of one's actions. With its gripping narrative and rich historical backdrop, 'Vortigern; an historical play' promises to transport readers to a time of legends and leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Power and politics, Betrayal, Fate and prophecy
