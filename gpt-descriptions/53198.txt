Synopsis: 
In 'Penny Allen and the Mystery of the Hidden Treasure' by Jean L. McKechnie, young Penny Allen finds herself embarking on an unexpected adventure when she stumbles upon a mysterious clue that leads her to a hidden treasure. With the help of her loyal friends, Penny must unravel the secrets of the past and navigate through a series of thrilling challenges to uncover the truth. As the stakes rise and danger lurks around every corner, Penny's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. Will she be able to solve the mystery and claim the treasure before it falls into the wrong hands?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
