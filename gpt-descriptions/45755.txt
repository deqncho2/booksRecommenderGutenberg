Synopsis: In a luxurious Austrian resort, a young boy becomes infatuated with a mysterious and charming baron who seems to hold the key to his mother's heart. As their friendship deepens, the boy becomes entangled in a web of secrets, manipulation, and forbidden desires. Set against the backdrop of a society on the brink of collapse, "The Burning Secret" is a gripping tale of obsession, betrayal, and the destructive power of hidden desires.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Forbidden love, Manipulation, Betrayal, Obsession, Social class
