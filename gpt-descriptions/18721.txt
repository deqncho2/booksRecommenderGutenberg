Synopsis: In "The Victim: A Romance of the Real Jefferson Davis," Thomas Dixon, Jr. takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of the infamous Confederate President, Jefferson Davis. Set against the backdrop of the American Civil War, this historical romance delves into the complexities of Davis' character, exploring his personal struggles, political ambitions, and the profound impact he had on the nation. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Dixon paints a vivid portrait of a man torn between duty, love, and the weight of history. As the narrative unfolds, readers are drawn into a world of passion, betrayal, and the relentless pursuit of power, leaving them eager to uncover the untold secrets of one of America's most controversial figures.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Biography

Themes: Politics, Love, Duty, Betrayal, Ambition
