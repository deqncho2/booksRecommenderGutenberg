Synopsis: In 'What The Left Hand Was Doing' by Randall Garrett, a brilliant detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving a missing artifact and a series of mysterious deaths. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel the fabric of society. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of political intrigue and supernatural forces to solve the puzzle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Corruption, Power, Deception
