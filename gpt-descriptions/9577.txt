Synopsis: 
'Anti-Slavery Poems 3.: Part 3 From Volume III of The Works of John Greenleaf Whittier' by John Greenleaf Whittier is a collection of powerful and thought-provoking poems that delve into the dark and harrowing realities of slavery. Through vivid imagery and emotive language, Whittier sheds light on the experiences of enslaved individuals and the moral imperative to fight against this injustice. This volume showcases Whittier's unwavering commitment to the abolitionist cause, as he passionately advocates for freedom and equality. 

Genres: Poetry, Historical, Social Justice

Themes: Slavery, Abolition, Freedom, Equality, Social Justice
