Synopsis: 
'Arrows of the Chace, vol. 1/2: being a collection of scattered letters published chiefly in the daily newspapers 1840-1880' by Ruskin, John is a captivating collection of letters that were published in newspapers over a span of four decades. These letters cover a wide range of topics, from art and literature to social issues and politics. Through his eloquent and thought-provoking writing, Ruskin offers insightful commentary on the world around him, providing readers with a unique perspective on the events and ideas of the time.

Genres: Non-fiction, Epistolary

Themes: Art and aesthetics, Social criticism, Politics, Literature and culture
