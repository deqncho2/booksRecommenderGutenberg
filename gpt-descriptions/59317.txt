Synopsis: 

'Tactics and Duties for Trench Fighting' is a gripping and informative guide written by Bertrand, Georges Etienne and Solbert, Oscar N. This book takes readers deep into the treacherous world of trench warfare, offering invaluable insights into the strategies, techniques, and responsibilities of soldiers on the front lines. Through vivid descriptions and firsthand accounts, the authors explore the challenges faced by soldiers in the trenches, revealing the cunning tactics employed to gain an advantage over the enemy. From the intricacies of trench construction to the intricacies of combat, this book provides a comprehensive and captivating exploration of the realities of trench warfare.

Genres: Military history, Non-fiction, War

Themes: Warfare tactics, Soldier's duties, Survival in extreme conditions
