Synopsis: 
In 'The Art of Theatrical Make-up' by Morton Cavendish, delve into the captivating world of stagecraft and discover the transformative power of make-up. From the ancient Greeks to the modern-day theater, this book takes you on a journey through the evolution of theatrical make-up, revealing the secrets behind the mesmerizing characters that come to life on stage. Explore the intricate techniques used to create stunning illusions, from age and injury to fantastical creatures and mythical beings. With stunning visuals and expert insights, this book is a must-read for anyone fascinated by the magic of the theater.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Performing Arts

Themes: Transformation, Creativity, Illusion
