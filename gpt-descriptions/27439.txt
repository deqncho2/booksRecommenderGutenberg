Synopsis:
'Sundry Accounts' by Cobb, Irvin S. is a collection of humorous and heartwarming short stories that take readers on a delightful journey through the everyday lives of ordinary people. From mischievous children to eccentric neighbors, from small-town gossip to unexpected encounters, each story presents a unique and entertaining glimpse into the human experience. With his signature wit and charm, Cobb captures the essence of human nature and reminds us of the joy and laughter that can be found in the simplest of moments.

Genres:
Humor, Fiction, Short Stories

Themes:
Human nature, Everyday life, Humor
