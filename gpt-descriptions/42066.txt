Synopsis: In "The Young Marooners on the Florida Coast" by F.R. Goulding, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves shipwrecked on a deserted island off the coast of Florida. As they navigate the challenges of survival, they encounter mysterious caves, hidden treasures, and unexpected dangers. With their resourcefulness and determination, the boys must rely on their wits and teamwork to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. Will they be able to find a way back home or will they be forever marooned on the Florida coast?

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Exploration
