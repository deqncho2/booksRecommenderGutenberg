Synopsis: 
'The Voice of the City: Further Stories of the Four Million' by Henry, O. is a collection of short stories that takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling streets of New York City. From the lives of struggling artists to the secrets hidden behind closed doors, each story offers a unique glimpse into the diverse and vibrant characters that make up the city. With unexpected twists and turns, Henry, O. masterfully weaves tales of love, loss, and redemption, leaving readers eager to uncover the next story that awaits them.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Urban Literature

Themes: City life, Human connection, Social inequality
