Synopsis:
In "Betty Wales & Co.: A story for girls," Betty Wales finds herself facing new challenges as she enters her sophomore year at college. With her friends by her side, Betty navigates the complexities of campus life, including academic pressures, social expectations, and the pursuit of personal growth. As she strives to find her place in the world, Betty discovers the power of friendship, the importance of self-expression, and the value of staying true to oneself. This captivating story follows Betty's journey of self-discovery and empowerment, as she learns to embrace her individuality and make a lasting impact on those around her.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Empowerment
