Synopsis: 

In 'Buried Cities: Pompeii, Olympia, Mycenae (Complete)' by Jennie Hall, embark on a captivating journey through time as the author delves into the fascinating histories of three ancient cities: Pompeii, Olympia, and Mycenae. Unearth the secrets of these once-thriving civilizations that were tragically buried by the passage of time. From the grandeur of Pompeii's Roman Empire to the glory of Olympia's Olympic Games and the enigmatic ruins of Mycenae, Hall's vivid descriptions transport readers to these lost worlds, allowing them to witness the rise and fall of these remarkable cities. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Archaeology, Travelogue

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Archaeological discoveries, Human history, Cultural heritage
