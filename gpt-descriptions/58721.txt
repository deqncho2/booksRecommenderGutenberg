Synopsis: In 'Unwelcomed Visitor' by Joseph Samachson, a seemingly ordinary family's life takes a dark turn when an unexpected guest arrives at their doorstep. As the visitor's true intentions become increasingly sinister, the family must confront their deepest fears and secrets to protect themselves and their home. With suspenseful twists and a gripping narrative, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Family dynamics, Secrets and deception, Survival
