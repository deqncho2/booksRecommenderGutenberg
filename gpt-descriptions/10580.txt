Synopsis: In 'The Positive School of Criminology', Enrico Ferri delivers a thought-provoking series of lectures that challenge conventional notions of crime and punishment. Drawing on scientific research and real-life examples, Ferri presents a compelling argument for a new approach to understanding criminal behavior. As he delves into the complexities of human nature and social factors, the reader is taken on a journey that challenges preconceived notions and offers a fresh perspective on the causes and prevention of crime.

Genres: Non-fiction, Criminology, Sociology

Themes: Criminal behavior, Social factors, Scientific approach to crime, Prevention of crime
