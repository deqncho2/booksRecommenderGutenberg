Synopsis: 
'Carolina Chansons: Legends of the Low Country' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich folklore of the Carolina coast. This collection of stories, written by Allen Hervey and DuBose Heyward, weaves together tales of love, tragedy, and the supernatural, all set against the backdrop of the mysterious and haunting Low Country. From ghostly apparitions to forbidden romances, each story unveils a hidden facet of this unique region, leaving readers spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Folklore

Themes: Love and Loss, Southern Culture, Supernatural Elements
