Synopsis: 
'Black Spirits and White: A Book of Ghost Stories' by Ralph Adams Cram is a collection of chilling tales that will send shivers down your spine. From haunted houses to vengeful spirits, this book explores the eerie and supernatural. Each story is crafted with suspense and mystery, keeping readers on the edge of their seats as they delve into the realm of the paranormal. Prepare to be captivated by these haunting tales that will leave you questioning the thin line between the living and the dead.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: Ghosts, Hauntings, Fear, the Unknown
