Synopsis: In 'A Little Miss Nobody; Or, With the Girls of Pinewood Hall', readers are introduced to a young girl named Peggy Raymond who finds herself in a mysterious and challenging situation. As she navigates the ups and downs of her new life at Pinewood Hall, Peggy must uncover the truth about her past and face unexpected obstacles along the way. With the support of her newfound friends, Peggy embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience that will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Resilience
