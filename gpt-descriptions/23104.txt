Synopsis: In 'The Blue Tower' by Evelyn E. Smith, a young woman named Alice finds herself trapped in a mysterious tower with no memory of how she got there. As she explores her surroundings, she uncovers a series of puzzling clues that lead her on a journey of self-discovery and a quest for the truth. With each revelation, Alice's perception of reality becomes increasingly distorted, blurring the lines between fantasy and truth. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the tower, she must confront her own fears and confront the enigmatic forces that hold her captive.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Memory, Perception, Reality vs. Illusion
