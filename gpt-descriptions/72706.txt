Synopsis: 
In 'Transient' by Moore, Ward, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, captivating the residents with his enigmatic presence. As he weaves his way into their lives, secrets are unearthed, and the town's dark underbelly is exposed. With each passing day, the stranger's true intentions become more elusive, leaving the townspeople questioning their own reality and the consequences of their actions. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Consequences
