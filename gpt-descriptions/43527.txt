Synopsis: In 'The Quest: A Romance' by Justus Miles Forman, a young man embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious family secret. As he travels through enchanting landscapes and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, he must confront his own fears and desires. With each step, the protagonist unravels a web of hidden truths that will challenge his perception of reality and lead him to a life-altering revelation.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
