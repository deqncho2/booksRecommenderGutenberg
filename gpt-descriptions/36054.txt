Synopsis: In "The Last of the Vikings" by John Bowling, a young historian named Emma embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind a legendary Viking artifact. As she delves deeper into the mysteries of the past, Emma finds herself entangled in a dangerous web of secrets, betrayal, and ancient Norse mythology. With time running out, she must navigate treacherous waters and face formidable foes to protect the artifact and preserve the legacy of the Vikings.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Heritage and Legacy, Mythology and Folklore, Pursuit of Knowledge
