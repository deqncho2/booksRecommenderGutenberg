Synopsis: In 'Escape Velocity' by Charles L. Fontenay, a brilliant scientist named John Grant embarks on a daring mission to save humanity from an impending catastrophe. As he races against time, Grant must navigate through treacherous obstacles, face unexpected dangers, and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of the entire human race. With its gripping narrative and thought-provoking twists, 'Escape Velocity' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the vastness of space and the depths of human resilience.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Morality, Human Ingenuity
