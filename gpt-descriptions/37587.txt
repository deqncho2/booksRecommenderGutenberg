Synopsis: In "The Katipunan; or, The Rise and Fall of the Filipino Commune," St. Clair takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of the Philippine Revolution. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, the story follows a group of passionate revolutionaries who form the secret society known as the Katipunan. As they navigate the treacherous political landscape and fight for their country's independence, the characters face personal sacrifices, betrayals, and unexpected alliances. With its gripping narrative and rich historical context, this novel offers a compelling exploration of the Filipino struggle for freedom.

Genres: Historical fiction, Political thriller, Adventure

Themes: Revolution, Nationalism, Loyalty, Betrayal
