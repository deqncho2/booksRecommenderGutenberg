Synopsis: In 'Little Rifle; or, The Young Fur Hunters' by Edward Sylvester Ellis, follow the thrilling adventures of a group of young fur hunters as they navigate the treacherous wilderness of the American frontier. Led by the courageous and resourceful Little Rifle, the group faces dangerous encounters with wild animals, hostile Native American tribes, and the harsh realities of survival in the untamed wilderness. As they strive to secure valuable furs and protect their lives, the young fur hunters learn valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the indomitable spirit of the human will.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Courage, Exploration
