Synopsis: In 'The Dream: A Novel', H.G. Wells takes readers on a captivating journey through the dreamscape, blurring the lines between reality and imagination. As the protagonist delves deeper into the realm of dreams, they encounter extraordinary and surreal experiences that challenge their perception of the world. With each dream becoming more vivid and unpredictable, the boundaries of what is possible are pushed to their limits. This thought-provoking novel explores the power of dreams and the profound impact they can have on our waking lives.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Dreams, Reality vs. Imagination, Perception, Identity
