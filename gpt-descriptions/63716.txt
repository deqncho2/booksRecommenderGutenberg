Synopsis: In 'The Time-Techs of Kra' by Sheridan, Max C., a group of brilliant scientists known as the Time-Techs embark on a thrilling adventure through time and space. When their groundbreaking invention, the Time-Travel Device, falls into the wrong hands, they must race against time to prevent catastrophic consequences. As they navigate through different eras and encounter unexpected challenges, the Time-Techs must confront their own fears and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of the universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Time Travel

Themes: Power of technology, Morality and ethics, Consequences of actions
