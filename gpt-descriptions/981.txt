Synopsis: 
In the epic poem 'Beowulf', a fearless warrior named Beowulf embarks on a perilous journey to rid a kingdom of a terrifying monster. As he battles against supernatural creatures and faces his own mortality, Beowulf's heroic deeds and unwavering determination captivate the reader, leading to a climactic showdown that will test his strength and courage. 

Genres: 
Epic, Heroic, Adventure

Themes: 
Heroism, Fate, Good vs. Evil
