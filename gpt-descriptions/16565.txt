Synopsis: In this captivating historical account, Captains Lewis and Clark embark on a daring expedition to explore the uncharted territories of the American West. Facing treacherous landscapes, encounters with Native American tribes, and the constant threat of the unknown, the intrepid explorers push the boundaries of exploration as they journey from the sources of the Missouri River, across the formidable Rocky Mountains, and down the majestic Columbia River to the vast Pacific Ocean. Through vivid descriptions and firsthand narratives, this volume offers a thrilling glimpse into the challenges and triumphs of this groundbreaking expedition.

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, History

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Native American relations, Natural wonders, Perseverance
