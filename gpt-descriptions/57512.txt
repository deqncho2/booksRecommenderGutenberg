Synopsis: In 'John Bull's Womankind (Les Filles de John Bull)', Max O'Rell takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and experiences of women in England. Through witty observations and humorous anecdotes, O'Rell explores the various roles and personalities of English women, shedding light on their unique qualities and quirks. From the sophisticated ladies of high society to the hardworking women of the lower classes, this book offers a delightful and insightful exploration of the diverse womankind found within the borders of John Bull's land.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travelogue, Humor

Themes: Gender roles, Cultural observations
