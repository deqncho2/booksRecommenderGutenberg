Synopsis: 
In "Sarah Bernhardt as I knew her: The Memoirs of Madame Pierre Berton as told to Basil Woon," the reader is taken on an intimate journey through the captivating life of the legendary actress, Sarah Bernhardt. Through the eyes of Madame Pierre Berton, a close friend and confidante, we are granted a rare glimpse into the enigmatic world of one of the greatest stage performers of all time. From her rise to stardom to her tumultuous personal life, this memoir offers a fascinating portrayal of Sarah Bernhardt's talent, charisma, and the profound impact she had on those around her.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Historical

Themes: Fame and Celebrity, Artistic Passion, Friendship and Loyalty
