Synopsis: In the heart of Utah, a small town is about to be turned upside down when three mysterious beavers suddenly appear. As the townspeople try to unravel the secrets behind these unusual creatures, they find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns. With each passing day, the beavers reveal more about themselves, leaving the townspeople questioning everything they thought they knew. Will they be able to uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Discovery, Identity, Community
