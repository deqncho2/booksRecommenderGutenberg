Synopsis: 
In 'The Queen of Hearts' by Collins, Wilkie, a mysterious woman with a dark past arrives in a small English village, captivating the locals with her beauty and enigmatic charm. As she becomes entangled in the lives of the villagers, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the line between love and obsession blurs. With its intricate web of relationships and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity, Love and Obsession
