Synopsis: 

In 'Blacksheep! Blacksheep!' by Nicholson Meredith, we are introduced to the intriguing world of the wealthy and powerful Blacksheep family. The story revolves around the enigmatic protagonist, Amelia Blacksheep, who finds herself caught in a web of family secrets, betrayal, and scandal. As Amelia delves deeper into her family's dark past, she uncovers shocking truths that threaten to unravel everything she thought she knew about her loved ones. With each revelation, Amelia must navigate treacherous waters, where loyalty and trust are constantly tested. 

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Family Saga

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity
