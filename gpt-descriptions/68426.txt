Synopsis: 
In "Two Fares East" by W. C. Tuttle, a young man named Jim is thrust into a thrilling adventure when he unexpectedly finds himself in possession of a mysterious package. As he embarks on a journey across the rugged landscapes of the American West, Jim encounters a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own hidden agendas. With danger lurking at every turn, Jim must navigate treacherous situations and make difficult choices that will test his courage and resourcefulness. As the suspense builds, secrets are revealed, and Jim's life is forever changed.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Survival, Identity
