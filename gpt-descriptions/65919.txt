Synopsis: 

In "History of the Fylde of Lancashire," John Porter takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and often overlooked history of the Fylde region in Lancashire. From its ancient origins to the present day, Porter weaves together a tapestry of fascinating stories, intriguing characters, and significant events that have shaped this unique corner of England. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, he uncovers hidden secrets, uncovers forgotten legends, and sheds light on the remarkable heritage of the Fylde. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, local history, cultural exploration

Themes: Heritage preservation, community identity, historical significance
