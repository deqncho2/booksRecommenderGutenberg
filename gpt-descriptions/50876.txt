Synopsis: In 'Earthbound' by Del Rey, Lester, a young woman named Maya discovers a hidden world beneath the surface of Earth, filled with ancient secrets and powerful beings. As she embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about her own origins, Maya must navigate treacherous alliances, face unimaginable dangers, and confront her own inner demons. With its blend of mystery, adventure, and a touch of romance, 'Earthbound' takes readers on an exhilarating ride through a world that exists right beneath our feet.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Redemption
