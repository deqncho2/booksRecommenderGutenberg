Synopsis: In 'The Treasure Trail: A Romance of the Land of Gold and Sunshine' by Marah Ellis Ryan, a young woman named Dorothy embarks on a thrilling adventure in the rugged and untamed land of California during the Gold Rush era. As she follows a mysterious treasure map, Dorothy encounters danger, romance, and unexpected allies along the way. With each step closer to the treasure, she unravels secrets that will change her life forever. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the hidden treasures of the past.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Pursuit of Wealth, Love and Sacrifice
