Synopsis: 

In 'Canterbury' by Home, Gordon, a small town is shaken by a mysterious event that leaves its residents questioning their reality. As the story unfolds, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the line between truth and illusion becomes increasingly blurred. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the enigmatic world of Canterbury, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Identity, Secrets and Betrayal
