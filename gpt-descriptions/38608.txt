Synopsis: In 'The Girl Scouts at Rocky Ledge; Or, Nora's Real Vacation' by Lilian Garis, a group of adventurous Girl Scouts embark on a thrilling summer vacation at Rocky Ledge. As they explore the beautiful surroundings, they stumble upon a mysterious secret that leads them on an unexpected journey filled with danger and excitement. With their courage and resourcefulness, the Girl Scouts must work together to unravel the truth and protect their newfound discovery. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
