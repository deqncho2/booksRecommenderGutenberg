Synopsis: 
'Ensign Knightley, and Other Stories' is a captivating collection of short stories by A. E. W. Mason that takes readers on a journey through various intriguing and unexpected tales. From the adventures of a young ensign in the British army to the mysterious events surrounding a stolen painting, each story presents a unique and gripping narrative that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. With twists and turns, unexpected revelations, and moments of suspense, this collection offers a delightful blend of mystery, adventure, and intrigue.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Honor, Deception, Redemption
