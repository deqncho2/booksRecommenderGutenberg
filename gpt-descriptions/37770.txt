Synopsis: 

In 'Ecstasy, A Study of Happiness: A Novel' by Louis Couperus, we are introduced to Cecile, a young woman trapped in a loveless marriage. Seeking solace and fulfillment, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery that takes her through the vibrant streets of Paris and the mystical landscapes of India. As Cecile encounters a cast of intriguing characters and experiences a series of transformative events, she begins to question the nature of happiness and the true meaning of ecstasy. 

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Psychological Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Self-Discovery, Pursuit of Happiness
