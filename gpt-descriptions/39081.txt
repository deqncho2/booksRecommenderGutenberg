Synopsis: In 'The Dorrance Domain' by Wells, Carolyn, a young woman named Doris Dorrance unexpectedly inherits a grand estate from a distant relative. As she settles into her new life, Doris discovers that the mansion holds a mysterious secret that has been hidden for generations. With the help of a charming and enigmatic stranger, she embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the Dorrance family's past. As secrets unravel and danger lurks around every corner, Doris must navigate a web of deceit and betrayal to protect her newfound inheritance and the people she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Inheritance, Family secrets, Love and trust, Identity
