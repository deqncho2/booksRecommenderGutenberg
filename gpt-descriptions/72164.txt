Synopsis: In "A Scrap of Paper: The Inner History of German Diplomacy and Her Scheme of World-Wide Conquest," Emile Joseph Dillon takes readers on a captivating journey into the secretive world of German diplomacy. Through meticulous research and insider knowledge, Dillon unveils a web of intrigue, deception, and ambition that reveals Germany's hidden agenda for world domination. As the plot unfolds, readers are drawn into a thrilling narrative that exposes the true nature of German diplomacy and its far-reaching consequences.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political thriller

Themes: Diplomacy, World domination, Deception, Ambition
