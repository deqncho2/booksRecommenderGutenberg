Synopsis: In the secluded town of Dunwich, strange occurrences begin to unfold as a mysterious family harbors a dark secret. As the townspeople become increasingly disturbed by the eerie events, a group of investigators delves into the heart of the mystery, uncovering ancient rituals, forbidden knowledge, and unimaginable horrors lurking in the shadows.

Genres: Horror, Gothic fiction, Supernatural fiction

Themes: Forbidden knowledge, Cosmic horror, Isolation, Madness
