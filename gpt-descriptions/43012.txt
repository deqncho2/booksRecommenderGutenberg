Synopsis: 
'Opium Eating: An Autobiographical Sketch by an Habituate' takes readers on a captivating journey through the dark and mysterious world of opium addiction. Written by an anonymous author, this autobiographical sketch delves into the depths of the protagonist's struggle with opium, revealing the harrowing consequences and the haunting allure of this dangerous substance. As the narrative unfolds, the reader becomes immersed in the protagonist's desperate attempts to break free from the clutches of addiction, leading to unexpected twists and turns that keep them on the edge of their seat.

Genres: Autobiography, Addiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Addiction, Self-Destruction, Redemption
