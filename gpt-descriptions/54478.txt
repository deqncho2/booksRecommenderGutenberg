Synopsis: In 'Harper's Round Table, February 25, 1896', a group of young adventurers embarks on a thrilling journey filled with mystery and danger. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter unexpected obstacles, they must rely on their wit and courage to overcome the perils that lie ahead. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the captivating world of these intrepid explorers, eagerly anticipating what awaits them at every step.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
