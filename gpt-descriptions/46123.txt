Synopsis: In 'Belle Powers' Locket' by Joanna H. Mathews, a mysterious locket holds the key to a young woman's past, present, and future. As Belle Powers embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets behind the locket, she finds herself entangled in a web of family secrets, forbidden love, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, Belle's world is turned upside down, and she must confront her own identity and make choices that will shape her destiny. Will she unlock the truth and find the happiness she seeks, or will the locket's power prove to be too much to handle?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Family Secrets, Love and Sacrifice
