Synopsis: 

In "Sydney to Croydon (Northern Queensland): An Interesting Account of a Journey to the Gulf Country with a Member of Parliament," Saltbush takes readers on a captivating adventure through the rugged and untamed landscapes of Northern Queensland. Joining forces with a Member of Parliament, the author embarks on a journey that unveils the hidden wonders and challenges of the Gulf Country. From encounters with unique wildlife to navigating treacherous terrains, this gripping narrative offers a firsthand account of the awe-inspiring beauty and the harsh realities of the Australian outback.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Australian Outback, Political Engagement
