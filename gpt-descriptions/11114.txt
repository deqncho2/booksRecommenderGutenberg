Synopsis: In 'Debate on Woman Suffrage in the Senate of the United States,' witness a historic clash of ideas as senators passionately argue for and against granting women the right to vote. Delve into the intense debates that took place during the 49th Congress, where powerful voices clashed, and the future of women's suffrage hung in the balance. This compelling account offers a glimpse into the pivotal moments that shaped the suffrage movement and the struggle for gender equality in America.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political discourse, Women's rights

Themes: Gender equality, Political activism, Social progress
