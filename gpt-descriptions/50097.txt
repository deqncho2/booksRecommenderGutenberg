Synopsis: 
In 'Lady Huntworth's Experiment: An original comedy in three acts', the eccentric Lady Huntworth decides to conduct a rather unconventional experiment to prove her theory about the nature of men and women. With the help of her equally eccentric friends, she sets out to challenge societal norms and expectations, leading to a series of hilarious and unexpected situations. As the experiment unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the characters find themselves questioning their own beliefs and desires. 

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Satire

Themes: Gender roles, Social expectations, Love and relationships
