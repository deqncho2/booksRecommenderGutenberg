Synopsis: 

In 'Frank's Campaign; Or, The Farm and the Camp' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Frank Frost, a young and ambitious protagonist who dreams of making a name for himself. When an unexpected opportunity arises, Frank finds himself caught between the allure of city life and the simplicity of his family's farm. As he navigates the challenges of his new surroundings, Frank must confront his own values and priorities, ultimately discovering the true meaning of success and happiness.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Identity, Family, Values
