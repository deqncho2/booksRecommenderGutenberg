Synopsis: In 'Counterweight' by Jerry Sohl, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Paul Cameron invents a groundbreaking device that has the power to change the world. However, as he delves deeper into the mysteries of his creation, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel everything he holds dear. With time running out, Dr. Cameron must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal to protect his invention and those he loves. This gripping tale of science, intrigue, and moral dilemmas will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Betrayal
