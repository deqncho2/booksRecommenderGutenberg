Synopsis: In the twenty-sixth volume of his captivating memoirs, Jacques Casanova de Seingalt takes us on a thrilling journey through the vibrant and mysterious land of Spain. From the bustling streets of Madrid to the enchanting beauty of Andalusia, Casanova's encounters with Spanish nobility, artists, and seductive women paint a vivid picture of a country filled with passion, intrigue, and danger. As he navigates the treacherous world of politics and indulges in his insatiable appetite for love, Casanova's escapades in Spain will leave readers captivated and yearning for more.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and seduction, Travel and exploration, Social and political intrigue
