Synopsis: In 'The Model of a Judge' by Joseph Samachson, a renowned judge finds himself entangled in a perplexing case that challenges his beliefs and forces him to question the very foundation of justice. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that threatens to unravel not only the case but also his own reputation. With time running out, the judge must navigate through a maze of moral dilemmas and make a decision that will have far-reaching consequences. This gripping legal thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Justice, Morality, Deception
