Synopsis: 

'Turgenev in English: A Checklist of Works by and about Him' is a comprehensive guide that explores the life and literary works of Ivan Turgenev, one of Russia's most celebrated authors. This checklist provides a detailed overview of Turgenev's writings, including his novels, short stories, plays, and essays, as well as a compilation of critical works and translations of his works into English. It delves into the impact Turgenev had on the literary world and his influence on subsequent generations of writers. 

Genres: Literary Criticism, Biography, Reference

Themes: Russian Literature, Literary Influence, Translation Studies
