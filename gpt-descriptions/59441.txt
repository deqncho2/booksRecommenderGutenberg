Synopsis: In 'Nacha Regules' by Manuel Gálvez, a young woman named Nacha finds herself caught between tradition and modernity in early 20th century Argentina. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Nacha's journey takes her through a web of secrets and hidden desires. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Gálvez weaves a tale of passion, betrayal, and the struggle for personal freedom.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, drama

Themes: Tradition vs. modernity, societal expectations, love and desire
