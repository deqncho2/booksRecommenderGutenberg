Synopsis: In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 147, August 5th, 1914', a collection of satirical cartoons and humorous articles, the renowned British magazine takes a witty and insightful look at the events leading up to the outbreak of World War I. From political figures to societal norms, this edition of Punch offers a clever commentary on the tensions and absurdities of the time, providing a unique perspective on the eve of a global conflict.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Political Commentary

Themes: War, Politics, Society, Irony
