Synopsis: In 'Watch the Sky' by James H. Schmitz, a young girl named Terry is caught between her loyalty to her family and her growing suspicions about the strange occurrences happening in her small town. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Terry uncovers a secret that could change everything she knows about her world. With the help of her friends, she must navigate a dangerous path to uncover the truth and protect her loved ones.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Family loyalty, Coming of age, Secrets and truth
