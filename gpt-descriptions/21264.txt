Synopsis: In 'The Four-Pools Mystery' by Jean Webster, a group of friends embarks on a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a mysterious secret hidden within the depths of an ancient forest. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they uncover a web of clues, puzzles, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With danger lurking at every turn, the friends must use their wits and teamwork to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Problem-solving
