Synopsis: In "Won in the Ninth," the first installment of The Matty Books series, readers are introduced to a young boy named Matty who possesses an extraordinary talent for baseball. As he joins a local team, Matty's skills are put to the test in a high-stakes game that takes an unexpected turn in the ninth inning. Filled with suspense and excitement, this story will captivate readers as they follow Matty's journey to victory.

Genres: Sports, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Perseverance, Teamwork, Overcoming challenges
