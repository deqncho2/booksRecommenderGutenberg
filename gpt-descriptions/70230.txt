Synopsis: In 'The Ghost Planet' by Murray Leinster, a group of astronauts embarks on a perilous mission to explore a mysterious planet that has been dubbed the "Ghost Planet" due to its eerie and inexplicable nature. As they delve deeper into the planet's secrets, they encounter strange phenomena and encounter a presence that defies all logic. With their lives hanging in the balance, the astronauts must unravel the enigma of the Ghost Planet before it consumes them completely.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Unknown forces, Survival
