Synopsis:
In 'Doing and Daring: A New Zealand Story' by Eleanor Stredder, a young girl named Amelia embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes her across the breathtaking landscapes of New Zealand. Faced with unexpected challenges and surrounded by fascinating characters, Amelia must summon her courage and resourcefulness to navigate through the unknown. As she delves deeper into the heart of the country, Amelia discovers secrets that will change her life forever, and she learns the true meaning of bravery and resilience.

Genres:
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Courage, Exploration, Identity, Resilience
