Synopsis: 
In 'Young Wallingford' by George Randolph Chester, follow the misadventures of a charismatic and ambitious young man named Wallingford as he navigates the world of business and romance in early 20th century America. From get-rich-quick schemes to unexpected encounters with love interests, Wallingford's journey is filled with humor, wit, and unexpected twists that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Love and Relationships
