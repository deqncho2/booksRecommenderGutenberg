Synopsis: In 'The Foot-path Way' by Torrey, Bradford, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure as she sets out to explore the untamed wilderness of New England in the 19th century. Along the footpaths, she encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets and desires. As Mary navigates the rugged terrain and faces unexpected challenges, she learns valuable lessons about love, resilience, and the power of nature. This captivating tale takes readers on a captivating journey through the beauty and dangers of the wilderness, as Mary's path intertwines with the lives of those she meets along the way.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Self-discovery, Nature, Resilience, Love
