Synopsis: In 'The Curious Case of Lady Purbeck: A Scandal of the XVIIth Century' by Thomas Longueville, readers are transported to the intriguing world of 17th-century England. The story revolves around Lady Purbeck, a woman whose scandalous actions and mysterious past captivate the attention of the aristocracy. As her secrets begin to unravel, a web of deceit, betrayal, and forbidden love emerges, leading to shocking consequences that will leave readers questioning the boundaries of loyalty and the power of societal expectations.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Scandal, Identity, Forbidden Love, Betrayal, Societal Expectations
