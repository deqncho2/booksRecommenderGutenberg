Synopsis: 
In 'The Motor Boys on the Atlantic; or, The Mystery of the Lighthouse' by Clarence Young, the adventurous trio of Jack, Mark, and Andy find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey across the Atlantic Ocean. Their mission? To solve the enigmatic puzzle surrounding a mysterious lighthouse that has been causing havoc among sailors. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter suspicious characters, and face unexpected challenges, the Motor Boys must rely on their wit, courage, and mechanical skills to unravel the secrets hidden within the lighthouse. Will they succeed in solving the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
