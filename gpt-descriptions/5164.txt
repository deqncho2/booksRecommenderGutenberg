Synopsis: In 'The Beetle: A Mystery' by Richard Marsh, a mysterious and malevolent creature known as the Beetle terrorizes London, leaving a trail of fear and destruction in its wake. As the city becomes gripped by panic, four individuals find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and supernatural forces. A young woman, a journalist, a politician, and a detective must navigate a treacherous landscape of obsession, revenge, and the battle between good and evil. With each twist and turn, the true nature of the Beetle is slowly revealed, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Thriller

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Revenge, Supernatural forces
