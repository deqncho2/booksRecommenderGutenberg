Synopsis:
'With Fire and Sword' is an enthralling historical novel set in 17th century Poland, filled with epic battles, political intrigue, and passionate romance. The story follows the adventures of a young nobleman, Andrzej Kmicic, as he navigates the treacherous landscape of war and love during the tumultuous times of the Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth. As Andrzej becomes entangled in a web of power struggles and personal vendettas, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of his beloved homeland.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes:
War and Conflict, Loyalty and Betrayal, Love and Sacrifice
