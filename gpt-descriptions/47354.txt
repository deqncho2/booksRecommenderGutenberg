Synopsis: In 'Cricket Songs' by Gale, Norman, a retired cricket player finds himself drawn back into the world of the sport when he stumbles upon a mysterious cricket ball with a hidden secret. As he embarks on a journey to uncover the truth, he must confront his past, face unexpected challenges, and rediscover the power of friendship. This captivating tale weaves together elements of mystery, sports, and personal growth, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to unravel the enigma behind the cricket ball.

Genres: Mystery, Sports, Fiction

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Friendship
