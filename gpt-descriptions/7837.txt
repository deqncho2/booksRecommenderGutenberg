Synopsis: 
In 'The Nest Builder: A Novel', we are introduced to the captivating world of Lady Rosamund, a woman who defies societal expectations and embarks on a journey of self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century England, this enchanting tale follows Lady Rosamund as she navigates love, loss, and the pursuit of her true passions. As she strives to build her own nest, she encounters unexpected challenges and encounters that will test her resilience and determination. With vivid descriptions and a cast of intriguing characters, this novel takes readers on a captivating journey through the complexities of love, family, and the pursuit of personal fulfillment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Women's Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Love and Relationships, Gender Roles, Society and Expectations
