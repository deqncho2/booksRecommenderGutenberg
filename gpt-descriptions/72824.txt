Synopsis: A luxurious blue train becomes the setting for a thrilling mystery when a valuable ruby goes missing and a murder takes place on board. As the passengers and crew are all potential suspects, the investigation takes a twist when a young woman with a mysterious past becomes entangled in the case. With secrets, lies, and unexpected alliances, the truth behind the blue train's mystery slowly unravels, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
