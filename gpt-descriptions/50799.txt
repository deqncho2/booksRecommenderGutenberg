Synopsis: In "The River Motor Boat Boys on the Rio Grande: In Defense of the Rambler," the adventurous River Motor Boat Boys find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery while cruising along the Rio Grande. When their beloved boat, the Rambler, is accused of being involved in smuggling activities, the boys must uncover the truth and clear their boat's name. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter dangerous adversaries, the boys' loyalty and resourcefulness are put to the test. Will they be able to prove the Rambler's innocence and protect their reputation?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Justice
