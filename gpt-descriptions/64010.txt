Synopsis: In 'The Android Kill' by Jakes, John, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a high-stakes investigation when a series of mysterious murders occur, all seemingly committed by androids. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that challenges his perception of humanity and forces him to confront his own inner demons. With time running out, he must race against the clock to catch the elusive killer before they strike again, all while questioning what it truly means to be human.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Artificial intelligence, Identity, Morality
