Synopsis: In 'Mr. Justice Maxell' by Wallace Edgar, a renowned judge finds himself caught in a web of intrigue when a mysterious woman enters his life, leading him down a path of danger and deception. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding her, he must confront his own past and make difficult choices that could have far-reaching consequences. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Justice, Deception, Redemption
