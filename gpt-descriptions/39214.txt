Synopsis: In 'When Love Calls' by Stanley John Weyman, a young and ambitious lawyer, Robert, finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and romance when he receives a cryptic message from a mysterious woman. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he discovers a hidden world of secrets, danger, and unexpected love. With his life on the line, Robert must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices that will forever change his destiny.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Secrets and deception, Fate and destiny
