Synopsis: In 'Brigadier Frederick; and, The Dean's Watch' by Erckmann-Chatrian, a retired brigadier finds himself entangled in a mysterious series of events when he inherits a peculiar pocket watch from his deceased uncle. As he delves deeper into the watch's history, he uncovers a dark secret that connects him to a powerful secret society. Meanwhile, the dean of a prestigious university becomes obsessed with the same watch, leading him down a path of obsession and madness. This gripping tale of intrigue and suspense explores the consequences of power, the allure of forbidden knowledge, and the lengths people will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power, Secrets, Obsession, Knowledge
