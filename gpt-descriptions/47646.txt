Synopsis: 
In "Joan of the Journal" by Helen Diehl Olds, we follow the captivating journey of Joan, a young woman who discovers an old journal hidden in her grandmother's attic. As she delves into the pages, Joan becomes enthralled by the mysterious life of the journal's author, a woman named Eleanor. Determined to uncover the truth behind Eleanor's story, Joan embarks on a quest that takes her on a whirlwind adventure through time, love, and self-discovery. Along the way, she encounters unexpected allies, faces formidable challenges, and unearths secrets that will forever change her perception of herself and her family. 

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: 
Identity, Family, Time, Self-discovery
