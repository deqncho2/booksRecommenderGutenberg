Synopsis: 
In the enchanting city of Paris, a young American woman named Lily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and love. Drawn to the vibrant music scene, she finds herself entangled in a passionate romance with a charismatic French musician. As their relationship deepens, Lily must navigate the complexities of language, culture, and the haunting secrets of her past. Set against the backdrop of picturesque Parisian streets and the intoxicating melodies of French music, 'French Lyrics' is a captivating tale of love, identity, and the power of music to heal and transform.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love, Identity, Cultural exploration, Music
