Synopsis: In 'Ajax, for example' by W. C. Tuttle, a small-town sheriff finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when a notorious outlaw returns to town. As the sheriff races against time to uncover the truth, he must confront his own past and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of his community. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this thrilling Western novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Justice, Moral dilemmas
