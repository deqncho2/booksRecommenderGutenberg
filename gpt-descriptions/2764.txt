Synopsis: In "The Mahatma and the Hare: A Dream Story," Haggard takes readers on a captivating journey through a dreamlike world where a wise Mahatma and a mischievous hare engage in a battle of wits and wisdom. As the story unfolds, the reader is transported to a realm where reality and imagination intertwine, and where the true nature of power and enlightenment is explored. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking encounters, this enchanting tale will leave readers pondering the deeper meaning of life and the choices we make.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Wisdom, Enlightenment, Power, Morality
