Synopsis: In "Saved at Sea: A Lighthouse Story," a young girl named Mabel finds herself in a perilous situation when her family's boat is caught in a violent storm. As the waves crash around them, Mabel's father, the lighthouse keeper, must summon all his courage and skill to guide them to safety. This heart-pounding tale explores the power of hope, resilience, and the unwavering determination to protect those we love.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: Courage, Family, Survival
