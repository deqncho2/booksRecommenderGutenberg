Synopsis: 

In 'The Men on Deck: Master, Mates and Crew, Their Duties and Responsibilities' by Felix Riesenberg, readers are taken on a captivating journey into the world of maritime life. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Riesenberg delves into the intricate workings of a ship, exploring the roles and responsibilities of the master, mates, and crew members. From navigating treacherous waters to managing the ship's operations, the book offers a fascinating glimpse into the challenges and triumphs faced by those who dedicate their lives to the sea. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Maritime, Adventure

Themes: Duty and responsibility, Teamwork, Perseverance
