Synopsis: In 'Swamp Island', a young girl named Mary finds herself stranded on a mysterious island after a boating accident. As she explores the eerie and captivating swamp, she encounters strange creatures and uncovers hidden secrets that will challenge her perception of reality. With the help of newfound friends, Mary must navigate the treacherous swamp and unravel the island's enigmatic past to find her way back home.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Discovery
