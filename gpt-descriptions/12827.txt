Synopsis: In 'The Rising of the Red Man: A Romance of the Louis Riel Rebellion', author John Mackie takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events of the Louis Riel Rebellion. Set against the backdrop of the Canadian prairies in the late 19th century, the story follows the lives of a diverse cast of characters as they navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and the fight for justice. As tensions rise and the rebellion unfolds, the fate of the Red River Settlement hangs in the balance, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Rebellion, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
