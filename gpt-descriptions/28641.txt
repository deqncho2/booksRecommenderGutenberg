Synopsis: In "The History of Dartmouth College" by Smith, Baxter Perry, embark on a captivating journey through the rich and storied past of one of America's oldest and most prestigious institutions of higher learning. From its humble beginnings as a small school in the wilderness to its transformation into a renowned center of intellectual and cultural excellence, this book unveils the fascinating tales of Dartmouth College's founders, faculty, and students. Discover the struggles, triumphs, and pivotal moments that shaped the college's identity and contributed to its enduring legacy. Delve into the vibrant history of Dartmouth College and witness the profound impact it has had on education and society.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Biography

Themes: Education, Legacy, Transformation
