Synopsis: In 'The Girl from the Big Horn Country' by Mary Ellen Chase, a young woman named Ruth embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience in the rugged landscapes of the American West. As she navigates the challenges of love, loss, and personal growth, Ruth's determination and spirit shine through, captivating readers with her captivating story of strength and transformation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Western

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Love and Loss
