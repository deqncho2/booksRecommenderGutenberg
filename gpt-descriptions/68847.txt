Synopsis: In "The Hollow Lens" by Leverage, Henry, a brilliant scientist, stumbles upon a mysterious invention that allows him to see into parallel dimensions. As he delves deeper into this extraordinary discovery, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and danger. With each glimpse through the hollow lens, Henry's perception of reality is shattered, leading him on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind this enigmatic device.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Parallel universes, Perception of reality, Secrets and deception
