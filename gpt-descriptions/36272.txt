Synopsis: 

In 'Some Objections To Socialism: From "The Atheistic Platform", Twelve Lectures', Charles Bradlaugh presents a thought-provoking exploration of the objections to socialism from an atheistic perspective. Through a series of twelve lectures, Bradlaugh delves into the complexities of socialism, challenging conventional beliefs and offering compelling arguments against its implementation. With a keen focus on the intersection of atheism and socialism, this work delves into the philosophical and practical implications of these ideologies, leaving readers questioning their own preconceived notions.

Genres: Philosophy, Political Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Atheism, Socialism, Philosophy of Politics
