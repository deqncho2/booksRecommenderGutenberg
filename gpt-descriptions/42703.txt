Synopsis: In 'Overlooked' by Maurice Baring, a young woman named Alice finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Alice uncovers a series of long-forgotten letters and diaries that reveal a shocking truth about her family's past. With each revelation, Alice becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. As she races against time to uncover the truth, Alice must confront her own fears and make difficult choices that will forever change her life.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity, Courage
