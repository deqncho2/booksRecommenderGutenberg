Synopsis: In "The Lion's Share" by Octave Thanet, a small town is shaken by a mysterious murder that leaves the community in shock. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human greed and ambition is exposed. With a cast of intriguing characters and a gripping plot, this thrilling mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Crime

Themes: Greed, Ambition, Betrayal
