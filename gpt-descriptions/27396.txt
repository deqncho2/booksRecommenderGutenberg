Synopsis: 
'Proud Signild, and Other Ballads' is a collection of captivating and enchanting ballads that transport readers to a world of ancient legends and heroic tales. From the haunting story of a vengeful ghost to the epic saga of a valiant warrior, each ballad weaves together elements of love, betrayal, courage, and sacrifice. As readers delve into these lyrical narratives, they will be swept away by the rich imagery, vivid characters, and unexpected twists that make this collection a true treasure trove of storytelling.

Genres: Poetry, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Love and loss, Heroism, Betrayal, Redemption
