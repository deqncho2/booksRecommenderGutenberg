Synopsis: In "Can Such Things Be?" Ambrose Bierce takes readers on a chilling journey through a collection of eerie and supernatural tales. From haunted houses to mysterious disappearances, Bierce weaves together a series of unsettling stories that will leave readers questioning the boundaries between the natural and the supernatural. As the characters encounter inexplicable phenomena and confront their deepest fears, the line between reality and the unknown becomes increasingly blurred. With each story, Bierce masterfully builds suspense and delivers unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural Fiction

Themes: The supernatural, the unknown, fear, the power of the imagination
