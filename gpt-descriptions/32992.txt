Synopsis: In "The Youngest Girl in the School" by Evelyn Sharp, readers are introduced to a young girl named Lucy who finds herself in a challenging situation as the newest student at a prestigious boarding school. As she navigates the unfamiliar territory of friendships, rivalries, and academic expectations, Lucy's determination and resilience are put to the test. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story explores the complexities of school life and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Coming-of-age, School, Fiction

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Resilience
