Synopsis: 

In 'Organic Gardener's Composting', author Steve Solomon takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of composting, revealing the secrets to creating nutrient-rich soil that will transform any garden into a thriving oasis. With his expert guidance, readers will learn the art of composting, from understanding the science behind it to mastering the techniques that will yield remarkable results. Packed with practical tips, insightful anecdotes, and step-by-step instructions, this book is a must-read for anyone seeking to cultivate a bountiful and sustainable garden.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gardening, Sustainability

Themes: Composting, Organic gardening, Soil health, Sustainability
