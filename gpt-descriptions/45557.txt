Synopsis: In 'Green Eyes' by Roy J. Snell, a young woman named Betty finds herself entangled in a thrilling adventure when she stumbles upon a mysterious secret hidden within a pair of emerald green eyes. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Betty becomes caught in a web of danger, deceit, and unexpected romance. With each twist and turn, she must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices that will ultimately determine her fate. Will Betty uncover the truth behind the green eyes, or will she become another victim of their captivating allure?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Danger and suspense, Love and sacrifice
