Synopsis: 
In 'An Episode under the Terror', Balzac takes us back to the tumultuous period of the French Revolution. The story follows a young man named Godefroid, who finds himself caught up in a web of intrigue and danger as he becomes entangled with a mysterious secret society. As Godefroid delves deeper into the secrets of this clandestine group, he must navigate the treacherous political landscape of the time, where loyalty and betrayal are often indistinguishable. With his life hanging in the balance, Godefroid must make difficult choices that will test his courage and integrity.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Revolution, Loyalty, Betrayal, Courage
