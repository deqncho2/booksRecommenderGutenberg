Synopsis: In 'A Second Coming' by Richard Marsh, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small English village, captivating the locals with his enigmatic presence. As the villagers become increasingly obsessed with the stranger, they soon discover that his arrival is not a mere coincidence. Unraveling the secrets of his past becomes a thrilling journey that challenges their beliefs and forces them to confront their own desires and fears. With unexpected twists and a sense of impending doom, 'A Second Coming' is a gripping tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Fate
