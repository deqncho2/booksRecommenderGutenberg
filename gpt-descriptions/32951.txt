Synopsis: In "The Bull-Run Rout: Scenes Attending the First Clash of Volunteers in the Civil War," author Clement, E. H. takes readers on a gripping journey to the frontlines of the Civil War. Through vivid and intense scenes, the story follows a group of volunteers as they experience the chaos and brutality of their first battle. As the soldiers face the harsh realities of war, their courage and resilience are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and heart-wrenching moments, this captivating narrative captures the essence of the Civil War and the sacrifices made by those who fought in it.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Action/Adventure

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, The Brutality of War, Camaraderie, Coming of Age
