Synopsis: 

In 'Outpost' by Jane G. Austin, a young woman named Miriam embarks on a journey to a remote outpost in the American West, leaving behind her comfortable life in the East. As she adjusts to the harsh conditions and the rugged individuals she encounters, Miriam finds herself caught in a web of secrets and hidden agendas. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate treacherous terrain and make difficult choices that will test her resilience and determination. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Western, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Identity, Betrayal, Courage
