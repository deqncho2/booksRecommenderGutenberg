Synopsis: In 'True Love: A Story of English Domestic Life' by Sarah E. Farro, readers are transported to the charming English countryside, where they follow the lives of the wealthy and influential Pemberton family. As the story unfolds, secrets, scandals, and unexpected twists threaten to unravel the seemingly perfect lives of the characters. Amidst the backdrop of lavish parties, elegant estates, and societal expectations, the novel explores the complexities of love, loyalty, and the pursuit of happiness. With its captivating narrative and richly developed characters, 'True Love' offers a glimpse into the intricate web of relationships and the lengths people will go to protect their deepest desires.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Family dynamics, Social class, Secrets and betrayal
