Synopsis: In 'Our Village in War-time' by Martyn, S. T., readers are transported to a quaint English village during the tumultuous period of World War I. Through the eyes of the author, we witness the impact of war on the lives of the villagers, as they navigate through fear, loss, and resilience. Amidst the chaos, unexpected friendships are formed, secrets are unveiled, and love blossoms in the most unlikely of places. This heartwarming tale captures the essence of community, highlighting the strength and spirit of ordinary people in extraordinary times.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Romance

Themes: Community, Resilience, Love, Loss, Friendship
