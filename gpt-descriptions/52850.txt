Synopsis: In 'Barbers' Manual (Part 1); Text Book on Taxidermy (Part 2)', McConnaughay takes readers on a captivating journey through the intertwined worlds of barbers and taxidermists. Part 1 delves into the lives of barbers, exploring their unique skills, secrets, and the fascinating stories that unfold within their shops. Part 2 then delves into the intricate art of taxidermy, revealing the meticulous techniques and the profound connection between life and death. As the two seemingly unrelated worlds collide, unexpected parallels and hidden truths emerge, leaving readers spellbound and eager to uncover the mysteries that lie within.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Identity, Artistry, Life and Death
