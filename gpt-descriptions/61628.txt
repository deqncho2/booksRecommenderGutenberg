Synopsis: In "Courts, Criminals and the Camorra," Arthur Cheney Train takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of the Italian mafia, known as the Camorra. Set in the early 20th century, the story follows a young American lawyer who finds himself entangled in a web of corruption, violence, and deceit as he navigates the treacherous world of the Italian courts and the dangerous criminals who control them. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the Camorra, he must confront his own moral compass and make difficult choices that could have far-reaching consequences.

Genres: Crime fiction, Legal thriller, Historical fiction

Themes: Corruption, Justice, Morality, Power struggle
