Synopsis:
Ingleside; or, Without Christ and with Him is a gripping novel that follows the lives of two sisters, Mary and Jane, as they navigate the complexities of love, faith, and societal expectations. Set in the picturesque town of Ingleside, the story takes a dramatic turn when a mysterious stranger arrives, challenging the sisters' beliefs and forcing them to confront their deepest desires and fears. As their lives intertwine with those of the townspeople, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the true power of faith is put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and turns, Ingleside; or, Without Christ and with Him is a captivating tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Faith, Love, Redemption, Identity
