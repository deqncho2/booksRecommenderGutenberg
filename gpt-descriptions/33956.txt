Synopsis: In 'True Politeness: A Hand-book of Etiquette for Ladies' by Anonymous, readers are transported to a world where the art of politeness reigns supreme. This captivating guidebook offers a fascinating glimpse into the intricate rules and customs that govern society, providing invaluable advice on how to navigate social interactions with grace and charm. From elegant dinner parties to formal balls, from proper attire to appropriate conversation topics, this book is a treasure trove of wisdom for any lady aspiring to be the epitome of refinement. With its delightful anecdotes and practical tips, 'True Politeness' is a captivating journey into the world of etiquette that will leave readers eager to embrace the elegance of a bygone era.

Genres: Non-fiction, Etiquette, Self-help

Themes: Politeness, Social etiquette, Refinement
