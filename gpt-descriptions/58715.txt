Synopsis: Step back in time and explore the hidden corners of Old London through the mesmerizing drawings of Philip Norman. From the bustling streets of Victorian London to the eerie alleyways of medieval times, Norman's intricate illustrations bring to life the rich history and forgotten stories of this iconic city. As you delve into the pages of this captivating book, you'll uncover the secrets of London's past, encountering eccentric characters, architectural marvels, and the ever-present ghosts of bygone eras. Prepare to be transported to a world where the past and present intertwine, and where the art of drawing becomes a portal to another time.

Genres: Art, History, Travel

Themes: London history, urban exploration, nostalgia
