Synopsis: In 'Historic Tales: The Romance of Reality. Vol. 13 (of 15), King Arthur (1)', Sir Thomas Malory and Charles Morris bring to life the legendary figure of King Arthur and his knights in a captivating tale of chivalry, honor, and adventure. Set in the enchanting realm of Camelot, this volume delves into the origins of Arthur's reign, his noble quest for the mystical sword Excalibur, and the fateful encounters that shape his destiny. As the young king faces treacherous enemies and navigates the complexities of love and loyalty, readers are transported to a world where bravery and magic intertwine, leaving them eager to uncover the next chapter of this timeless legend.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Destiny, Love, Loyalty
