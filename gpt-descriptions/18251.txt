Synopsis: 
'Latin for Beginners' follows the journey of a young protagonist as they embark on a captivating adventure through the ancient world of Latin. From the bustling streets of Rome to the mysterious ruins of Pompeii, the reader is transported to a time of emperors, gladiators, and epic tales. As the protagonist delves deeper into the intricacies of the Latin language, they uncover hidden secrets and unravel a web of ancient mysteries. With each lesson, the reader becomes immersed in the rich history and culture of the Roman Empire, making 'Latin for Beginners' an enthralling and educational experience.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Language Learning

Themes: Exploration, Education, Ancient History
