Synopsis: In 'The Great Secret' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a young man named Richard Viner unexpectedly inherits a vast fortune from a mysterious benefactor. As he delves into the origins of his newfound wealth, Richard becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and international espionage. With the help of a resourceful detective and a beautiful woman, Richard must navigate a treacherous world of secrets and deception to uncover the truth behind his inheritance.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Inheritance, Identity, Betrayal, Intrigue
