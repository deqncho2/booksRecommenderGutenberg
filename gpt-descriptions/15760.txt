Synopsis: In 'The Forest of Swords: A Story of Paris and the Marne', Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of World War I. Set in Paris and the Marne region, the story follows a group of young friends who find themselves caught up in the chaos and danger of war. As they navigate the treacherous landscapes of battlefields and the streets of Paris, they must confront their own fears, forge unlikely alliances, and make sacrifices for the greater good. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Altsheler paints a gripping portrait of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Sacrifice, Coming of Age
