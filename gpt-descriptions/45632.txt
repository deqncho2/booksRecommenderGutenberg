Synopsis: 
In 'Beehive for Bairns, Vol. 2: From 1st Size to 2 Years: With Special Supplement: Bed Jackets', embark on a delightful journey through the world of baby fashion and care. This charming volume takes readers on a comprehensive exploration of clothing and accessories for infants, from their earliest days to their second year. With a special focus on bed jackets, this book offers valuable insights and practical tips for parents and caregivers, ensuring that little ones are both stylish and comfortable. Discover the latest trends, timeless classics, and innovative designs that will make dressing your baby a joyous experience. 

Genres: 
Fashion, Parenting, Lifestyle

Themes: 
Infant care, Baby fashion, Parental guidance
