Synopsis: In "Great-Heart": The Life Story of Theodore Roosevelt, author Daniel Henderson takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the remarkable life of one of America's most iconic presidents. From his childhood struggles with illness to his adventures as a cowboy in the Wild West, and from his rise to political power to his courageous leadership during times of crisis, this captivating biography paints a vivid portrait of Theodore Roosevelt's indomitable spirit and unwavering dedication to public service. With rich historical detail and engaging storytelling, Henderson brings to life the triumphs and challenges that shaped Roosevelt's character and legacy, making this a must-read for history enthusiasts and admirers of extraordinary individuals.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Leadership, Perseverance, Public Service, Adventure
