Synopsis: In 'Mysteries of Washington City, during Several Months of the Session of the 28th Congress', Caleb Atwater takes readers on a captivating journey through the secretive and enigmatic world of Washington D.C. during a pivotal time in American history. As the 28th Congress convenes, Atwater unveils a web of intrigue, scandal, and political maneuvering that lurks beneath the surface of the nation's capital. From clandestine meetings to hidden agendas, the author delves into the mysteries that surround the corridors of power, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth behind the captivating events that unfold.

Genres: Historical fiction, Political thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power and corruption, Secrets and deception, Political intrigue
