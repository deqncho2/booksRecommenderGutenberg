Synopsis: In 'The Danger Mark' by Robert W. Chambers, a young doctor named David Cairn finds himself drawn into a mysterious and dangerous world when he takes up a position at a remote asylum for the mentally ill. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the institution, he uncovers a web of intrigue, forbidden desires, and hidden identities that threaten to consume him. With his own sanity at stake, David must navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and madness to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Sanity vs. Insanity, Identity, Forbidden Desires
