Synopsis: In "Gloves, Past and Present" by Willard M. Smith, a mysterious pair of gloves is discovered in an old attic, leading the protagonist on a captivating journey through time and uncovering the secrets they hold. As the story unfolds, the gloves become a symbol of connection, love, and the power of human touch. With each new era explored, the reader is transported to different worlds, encountering historical figures and witnessing the impact these gloves have had on people's lives. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the enigma surrounding the gloves and their significance.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Time travel, Love, Human connection
