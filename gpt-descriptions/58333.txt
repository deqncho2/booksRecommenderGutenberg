Synopsis: In 'The Fall of Man; Or, The Loves of the Gorillas', Richard Grant White presents a captivating and thought-provoking scientific lecture that delves into the fascinating world of Darwinian theory and sexual selection. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, White explores the intricate relationships and behaviors of gorillas, shedding light on the evolutionary forces that shape their lives. As he weaves together scientific evidence and captivating storytelling, the lecture takes readers on a journey that challenges conventional notions of human nature and offers a fresh perspective on the origins of our own species.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Natural History

Themes: Evolution, Sexual Selection, Animal Behavior, Human Nature
