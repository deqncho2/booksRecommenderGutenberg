Synopsis: 

In 'Motor-car principles; the gasoline automobile', Roger B. Whitman takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the inner workings of the gasoline automobile. From the intricate mechanics of the engine to the art of driving, this comprehensive guide provides a captivating exploration of the motor-car world. With vivid descriptions and insightful explanations, Whitman unveils the secrets behind the power, speed, and efficiency of these remarkable machines. Whether you are a seasoned car enthusiast or simply curious about the marvels of automotive engineering, this book will leave you with a newfound appreciation for the gasoline automobile.

Genres: Non-fiction, Automotive, Technology

Themes: Automotive engineering, Mechanics, Driving
