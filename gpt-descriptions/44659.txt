Synopsis: 

In 'Secret Service: Being the Happenings of a Night in Richmond in the Spring of 1865', the reader is transported to the final days of the American Civil War. The story follows a group of secret service agents as they navigate the dangerous streets of Richmond, Virginia, on a mission of utmost importance. As the night unfolds, they encounter unexpected allies, face treacherous enemies, and find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and suspense. With each passing moment, the stakes rise higher, and the agents must rely on their wit, courage, and resourcefulness to complete their mission and change the course of history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Espionage, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Redemption
