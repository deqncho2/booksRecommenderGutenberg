Synopsis: In "Angel Island" by Inez Haynes Gillmore, a young woman named Doris embarks on a journey to the picturesque Angel Island, a place known for its enchanting beauty and mysterious legends. As she delves deeper into the island's secrets, Doris finds herself entangled in a web of romance, betrayal, and hidden treasures. With each step she takes, the island's past comes alive, revealing a captivating tale that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Love and betrayal, The power of nature
