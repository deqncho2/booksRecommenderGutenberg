Synopsis: In 'Christmas-Tree Land' by Molesworth, Mrs., follow the enchanting journey of a young girl named Griselda as she discovers a magical world hidden within the branches of her Christmas tree. As Griselda explores this extraordinary land, she encounters talking animals, mischievous fairies, and even a mysterious Christmas spirit. With each new encounter, Griselda learns valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the true meaning of Christmas. Will she be able to save Christmas-Tree Land from an impending danger and bring joy back to the holiday season?

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Christmas spirit
