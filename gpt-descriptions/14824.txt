Synopsis: 
'Furnishing the Home of Good Taste: A Brief Sketch of the Period Styles in Interior Decoration with Suggestions as to Their Employment in the Homes of Today' takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of interior decoration. Lucy Abbot Throop provides a delightful exploration of various period styles, offering valuable insights and practical suggestions for incorporating them into modern homes. From the opulence of the Renaissance to the simplicity of the Arts and Crafts movement, Throop's book is a treasure trove of inspiration for anyone seeking to create a home of timeless elegance and good taste.

Genres: Non-fiction, Interior Design, History

Themes: Interior decoration, Period styles, Home design, Aesthetics
