Synopsis: 

In 'The Trade Signs of Essex: A popular account of the origin and meanings of the public houses & other signs', Christy Miller takes readers on a fascinating journey through the history and symbolism of the trade signs found in Essex. From the iconic Red Lion to the enigmatic Three Tuns, Miller uncovers the hidden stories and intriguing origins behind these age-old symbols. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, this book offers a unique glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of Essex and the significance of these signs in the daily lives of its inhabitants.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Symbolism, Local History, Cultural Identity
