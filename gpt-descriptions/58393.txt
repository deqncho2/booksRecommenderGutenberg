Synopsis: In 'The Mirror of Alchimy', Roger Bacon takes readers on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of alchemy. Through a series of intriguing experiments and observations, Bacon explores the secrets of transmutation, the quest for the philosopher's stone, and the pursuit of eternal life. As he delves deeper into the alchemical arts, Bacon uncovers hidden knowledge and uncanny phenomena that challenge the boundaries of science and spirituality. With each revelation, the line between reality and myth becomes increasingly blurred, leaving readers spellbound and eager to uncover the ultimate truth.

Genres: Alchemical fiction, Historical fiction, Mystery

Themes: Alchemy, Transmutation, Quest for knowledge, Mysticism
