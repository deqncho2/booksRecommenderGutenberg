Synopsis: In 'The City of the Discreet' by Pío Baroja, a young man named Andrés is drawn into the mysterious and alluring world of a secret society in the city of Madrid. As he becomes entangled in their clandestine activities, Andrés must navigate a web of intrigue, betrayal, and hidden agendas. With each step deeper into this enigmatic world, he uncovers shocking secrets that challenge his perception of reality and force him to question his own identity. As the city's dark underbelly is revealed, Andrés must decide whether to embrace the power and allure of the discreet or risk losing himself in the process.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Power, Betrayal, Secrets
