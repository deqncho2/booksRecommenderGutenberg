Synopsis: 

Step into the fascinating world of old tavern signs with Fritz August Gottfried Endell's 'Old Tavern Signs: An Excursion in the History of Hospitality.' This captivating book takes readers on a journey through time, exploring the rich history and symbolism behind the signs that adorned taverns of yesteryears. From quaint village inns to bustling city taverns, Endell delves into the stories and legends that surround these iconic symbols, shedding light on their origins and hidden meanings. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, this book offers a unique glimpse into the past, uncovering the secrets and tales that lie behind these intriguing signs.

Genres: History, Art, Cultural Studies

Themes: Hospitality, Symbolism, Folklore
