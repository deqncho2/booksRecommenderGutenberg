Synopsis: 
'Contemporary One-Act Plays' is a captivating collection of thought-provoking and emotionally charged short plays that delve into the complexities of human relationships and the struggles we face in our modern world. From intense dramas to dark comedies, each play offers a unique and compelling exploration of the human condition. Whether it's a poignant examination of love and loss, a satirical critique of societal norms, or a powerful exploration of identity and self-discovery, these one-act plays will leave readers captivated and craving for more.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Satire, Tragedy

Themes: Love and Loss, Identity, Relationships, Society, Self-Discovery
