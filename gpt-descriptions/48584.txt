Synopsis: In 'Big Game Shooting, volume 1 (of 2)', Phillipps-Wolley takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of Africa, where danger lurks at every turn. Join the author as he recounts his thrilling encounters with majestic elephants, fierce lions, and elusive leopards, all while navigating the treacherous terrain of the African savannah. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this book immerses readers in the adrenaline-fueled world of big game hunting, where bravery and skill are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Wildlife conservation, Human-animal interaction
