Synopsis: In 'An Amateur Fireman' by Otis, James, follow the thrilling journey of a young man who unexpectedly finds himself thrust into the dangerous world of firefighting. As he navigates the challenges and perils of his new role, he must confront his own fears and discover the true meaning of bravery. With heart-pounding action and unexpected twists, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Bravery, Overcoming fears, Self-discovery
