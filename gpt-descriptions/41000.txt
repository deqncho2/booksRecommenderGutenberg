Synopsis: In 'Her Majesty's Minister' by William Le Queux, a young and ambitious politician finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and danger when he is appointed as a minister in the British government. As he navigates the treacherous world of power and secrets, he must uncover the truth behind a mysterious conspiracy that threatens not only his career but also the stability of the nation. With his life on the line, he must make difficult choices and face unexpected allies and enemies, all while trying to protect his country and his own reputation.

Genres: Political thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Political corruption, Power struggles, Loyalty, Betrayal
