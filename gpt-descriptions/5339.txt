Synopsis: In "Peter Schlemihl" by Adelbert von Chamisso, the protagonist finds himself in a peculiar predicament after making a fateful deal. Stripped of his shadow, Peter Schlemihl becomes an outcast, facing a world that views him with suspicion and fear. As he embarks on a journey to reclaim what he has lost, he encounters strange characters and experiences both joy and despair. This captivating tale explores the consequences of a single choice and the search for identity in a world that values appearances.

Genres: Fantasy, Gothic fiction, Romanticism

Themes: Identity, Appearance vs. Reality, Alienation, Morality
