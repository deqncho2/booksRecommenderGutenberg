Synopsis: In the gripping novel "Gunsight Pass: How Oil Came to the Cattle Country and Brought a New West" by William MacLeod Raine, a small town in the American West becomes the battleground for a fierce conflict between the traditional cattle industry and the emerging oil boom. As tensions rise and fortunes hang in the balance, a cast of colorful characters must navigate the treacherous landscape of greed, ambition, and love, ultimately shaping the destiny of the region. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling narrative, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through the clash of old and new in the Wild West.

Genres: Western, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Conflict between tradition and progress, the impact of industrialization on rural communities, the pursuit of wealth and power, love and loyalty in the face of adversity.
