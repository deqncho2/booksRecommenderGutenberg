Synopsis: 

In "Women artists in all ages and countries," Elizabeth Fries Ellet takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and works of remarkable women artists from various cultures and time periods. From ancient civilizations to the Renaissance and beyond, Ellet unveils the stories of these talented women who defied societal norms and overcame countless obstacles to pursue their artistic passions. Through vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, the author sheds light on the struggles, triumphs, and enduring legacies of these often overlooked artists. 

Genres: Art history, Biography, Women's studies

Themes: Gender equality, Artistic expression, Cultural diversity
