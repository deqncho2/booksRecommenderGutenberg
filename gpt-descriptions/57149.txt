Synopsis: In "The Camp Fire Girls at Driftwood Heights" by Margaret Love Sanderson, a group of adventurous and spirited young girls embark on a summer camp adventure at the picturesque Driftwood Heights. As they navigate the challenges of camp life, they uncover a hidden mystery that tests their courage and friendship. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the secrets that lie within Driftwood Heights.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
