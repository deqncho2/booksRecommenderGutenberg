Synopsis: In 'Biology versus Theology. The Bible: irreconcilable with Science, Experience, and even its own statements', Julian delves into a thought-provoking exploration of the clash between biology and theology. With meticulous research and compelling arguments, he challenges the traditional beliefs and interpretations of the Bible, revealing how it contradicts scientific evidence, personal experiences, and even its own teachings. This eye-opening journey invites readers to question long-held beliefs and consider the implications of reconciling faith with the advancements of modern science.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Religious Studies

Themes: Science and Religion, Biblical Interpretation, Faith and Reason
