Synopsis: 
'Great leaders: Historic portraits from the great historians' takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the lives and legacies of some of the world's most influential leaders. From ancient rulers to modern visionaries, this book presents a collection of meticulously researched and vividly depicted portraits that bring these great leaders to life. Through the eyes of renowned historians, readers will delve into the triumphs, struggles, and defining moments that shaped these extraordinary individuals. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to different eras and cultures, gaining a deeper understanding of the qualities that make a leader truly great.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Art

Themes: Leadership, Power, Legacy
