Synopsis: In the second volume of his memoirs, Jacques Casanova finds himself in Naples, where he takes on the role of a cleric. Amidst the opulent and scandalous society of the city, Casanova navigates a world of political intrigue, forbidden love affairs, and dangerous alliances. As he immerses himself in the decadent lifestyle of Naples, Casanova's wit, charm, and audacity are put to the test, leading him on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Power and Corruption, Identity and Self-Discovery
