Synopsis: In 'The Other Now' by Murray Leinster, a brilliant scientist invents a device that allows people to glimpse into an alternate reality. However, when he becomes trapped in this other world, he must navigate its dangers and find a way back to his own reality. As he delves deeper into this mysterious parallel universe, he uncovers shocking secrets that could have far-reaching consequences for both worlds. With suspenseful twists and thought-provoking dilemmas, 'The Other Now' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the unknown.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Parallel universes, Identity, Consequences of scientific advancements
