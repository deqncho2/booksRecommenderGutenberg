Synopsis: 
'Ghost Stories of an Antiquary' is a collection of chilling tales that will send shivers down your spine. From haunted houses to ancient curses, this anthology takes readers on a journey through the eerie and supernatural. Each story is filled with suspense and mystery, leaving readers questioning what is real and what lies beyond the realm of the living. Prepare to be captivated by these haunting tales that will leave you questioning the boundaries between the natural and the supernatural.

Genres: Horror, Gothic fiction, Supernatural fiction

Themes: Ghosts, Hauntings, Supernatural phenomena, Mystery, Fear
