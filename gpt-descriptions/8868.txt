Synopsis: 
Botchan (Master Darling) by Natsume Soseki is a captivating coming-of-age novel that follows the misadventures of a young Tokyo graduate who finds himself teaching in a rural school. As Botchan navigates the eccentricities of his colleagues and the unruly students, he becomes entangled in a web of deception, rivalry, and unexpected friendships. With its humorous and satirical tone, this novel takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and the challenges of adapting to a new environment.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Satire

Themes: Identity, Education, Social hierarchy
