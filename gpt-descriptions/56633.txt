Synopsis: In 'The City That Was' by Smith, Stephen, a young journalist named Emma stumbles upon a hidden underground city beneath the bustling streets of New York. As she delves deeper into this mysterious world, she uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel the very fabric of reality. With time running out, Emma must navigate a treacherous labyrinth of secrets, betrayal, and ancient prophecies to save both the underground city and the world above.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden worlds, Betrayal, Prophecy, Reality vs. Illusion
