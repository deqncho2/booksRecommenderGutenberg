Synopsis: 
In "To Be Read at Dusk," Charles Dickens takes readers on a mysterious journey through the streets of London. The story follows a man named Austin, who encounters a stranger with a peculiar request. As Austin agrees to fulfill the stranger's wish, he becomes entangled in a series of eerie events that blur the line between reality and imagination. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the stranger's enigmatic intentions.

Genres: 
Gothic Fiction, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: 
The power of storytelling, the duality of human nature, the blurred boundaries between reality and illusion
