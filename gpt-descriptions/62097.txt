Synopsis: In 'The Last Martian' by Van Houten, Ray, a lone astronaut finds himself stranded on Mars after a catastrophic event leaves him cut off from Earth. As he struggles to survive in the harsh Martian environment, he discovers a mysterious signal that leads him on a journey of self-discovery and the possibility of a greater purpose. With time running out and his sanity hanging by a thread, he must confront the ultimate question: what does it mean to be human?

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Isolation, Survival, Identity, Exploration
