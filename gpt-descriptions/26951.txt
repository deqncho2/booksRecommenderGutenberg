Synopsis: In "Firebrand' Trevison" by Charles Alden Seltzer, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small western town, stirring up trouble and challenging the corrupt local authorities. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the town's fate hangs in the balance, and the stranger must confront his own dark past. With its gripping plot and intriguing characters, this thrilling western tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Western, Action, Mystery

Themes: Justice, Redemption, Corruption
