Synopsis: 
'From the Five Rivers' by Flora Annie Steel is a captivating novel set in colonial India, where the lives of two young women from different backgrounds intertwine against a backdrop of cultural clashes, forbidden love, and the struggle for independence. As they navigate the complexities of their society, their paths converge, leading them on a journey of self-discovery, resilience, and the pursuit of freedom. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel immerses readers in a world of rich traditions, societal expectations, and the enduring power of friendship.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Cultural identity, Love and relationships, Social change, Female empowerment
