Synopsis: In "Cornelli" by Johanna Spyri, a young girl named Cornelli moves to a new town and faces numerous challenges as she tries to fit in. With her unique perspective and unwavering determination, Cornelli navigates through the ups and downs of friendship, family, and societal expectations. As she discovers her own strength and resilience, Cornelli's journey becomes an inspiring tale of self-discovery and the power of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Children's literature, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Resilience, Self-acceptance
