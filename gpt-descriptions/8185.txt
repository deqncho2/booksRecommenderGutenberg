Synopsis: 

In "Fountains in the Sand: Rambles Among the Oases of Tunisia," Douglas, Norman takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting oases of Tunisia. With vivid descriptions and a keen eye for detail, the author explores the hidden corners of this North African country, uncovering the secrets and mysteries that lie within its ancient oases. From the bustling markets of Tunis to the tranquil palm groves of Tozeur, Douglas, Norman's narrative weaves together history, culture, and personal anecdotes, painting a mesmerizing portrait of a land where time seems to stand still. As he encounters fascinating characters and delves into the rich tapestry of Tunisian life, the author invites readers to join him on an unforgettable adventure through the fountains in the sand.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Timelessness
