Synopsis: In 'It's a Small Solar System' by Howard Allan, a group of astronauts embarks on a mission to explore a distant planet, only to find themselves facing unexpected challenges and mysterious phenomena. As they navigate through the vastness of space, they must confront their own fears and grapple with the fragility of human existence. With each twist and turn, the story delves into the depths of the human psyche, exploring themes of isolation, resilience, and the awe-inspiring wonders of the universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Human Nature, Existentialism
