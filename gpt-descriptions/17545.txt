Synopsis:
In 'Princess' by M.G. McClelland, a young woman named Emily finds herself thrust into a world of royalty and intrigue when she discovers a hidden secret about her lineage. As she navigates the opulent halls of the palace, Emily must unravel the mysteries of her past while facing unexpected challenges and dangerous adversaries. With the fate of an entire kingdom hanging in the balance, Emily must embrace her true identity and find the strength within herself to protect those she loves.

Genres:
Romance, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes:
Identity, Love, Power, Betrayal
