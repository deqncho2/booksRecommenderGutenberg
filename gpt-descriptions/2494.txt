Synopsis: 
'For Greater Things: The Story of Saint Stanislaus Kostka' by William T. Kane is a captivating biography that delves into the extraordinary life of Saint Stanislaus Kostka. Set in the 16th century, the story follows Stanislaus, a young Polish nobleman, as he embarks on a spiritual journey that will test his faith, challenge societal norms, and ultimately lead him to become one of the most revered saints in history. From his early struggles with his family's expectations to his encounters with influential figures of the time, Stanislaus's unwavering determination and unwavering devotion to God will inspire readers and leave them in awe of his remarkable journey.

Genres: Biography, Historical, Religious

Themes: Faith, Sacrifice, Spiritual Awakening
