Synopsis: 

"The Female Physician" is a comprehensive medical guide specifically tailored for women, addressing the various diseases and health issues that affect them at different stages of life. Written by John Maubray, this book delves into the diseases and symptoms experienced by virgins, wives, and widows, providing insights into their causes, degrees of danger, and methods of prevention and cure. Additionally, it includes a detailed exploration of new and improved midwifery practices, covering the necessary qualifications of a midwife and offering specific instructions for managing difficult and preternatural births. The book also offers guidance on the diet and regimen for both the mother and child.

Genres: Medical literature, Women's health, Midwifery

Themes: Women's health, Medical knowledge, Maternal care
