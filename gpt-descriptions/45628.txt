Synopsis: In 'A Desperate Game: A Comic Drama in One Act' by Morton, John Maddison, a group of eccentric characters find themselves entangled in a high-stakes game that tests their wit, cunning, and ability to keep secrets. As the game progresses, alliances are formed, secrets are revealed, and unexpected twists and turns keep the audience guessing until the very end. With witty dialogue and hilarious situations, this comic drama explores the lengths people will go to win and the consequences of their actions.

Genres: Comedy, Drama

Themes: Deception, Competition, Human nature
