Synopsis: In 'The Orpheus C. Kerr Papers, Series 2', the witty and satirical Orpheus C. Kerr returns with a new collection of humorous and insightful observations on American society during the Civil War. Through his sharp wit and clever storytelling, Kerr takes readers on a hilarious journey filled with absurd characters, comical situations, and biting social commentary. From political satire to romantic misadventures, this collection offers a delightful and entertaining glimpse into the tumultuous times of the Civil War era.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Historical Fiction

Themes: Social commentary, Political satire, Romantic misadventures
