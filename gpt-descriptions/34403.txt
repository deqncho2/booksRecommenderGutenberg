Synopsis: In 'The Clock Strikes Thirteen' by Mildred A. Wirt, a young girl named Penny Parker finds herself caught up in a mysterious series of events when she stumbles upon a hidden room in an old mansion. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the house, Penny uncovers a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Courage and determination, Coming of age
