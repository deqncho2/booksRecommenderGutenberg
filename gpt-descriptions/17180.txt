Synopsis: In 'The Riddle of the Frozen Flame', a mysterious and seemingly impossible crime takes place, leaving the brilliant detective Hamilton Cleek to unravel the truth. As Cleek delves deeper into the case, he encounters a web of intrigue, deception, and danger that threatens to consume him. With the clock ticking, Cleek must use his extraordinary deductive skills to solve the riddle and bring the culprits to justice before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Detective work, Deception, Intrigue
