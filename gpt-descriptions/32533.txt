Synopsis: In "The Crime of the Century; Or, The Assassination of Dr. Patrick Henry Cronin," author Henry M. Hunt delves into the shocking true story of a prominent Irish-American doctor who becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and murder. Set in late 19th century Chicago, the narrative follows Dr. Cronin as he uncovers a secret society within the Irish community, leading to his untimely demise. As the investigation unfolds, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of the city, filled with political intrigue, secret societies, and a quest for justice.

Genres: True Crime, Historical Non-fiction, Mystery

Themes: Betrayal, Corruption, Identity, Justice
