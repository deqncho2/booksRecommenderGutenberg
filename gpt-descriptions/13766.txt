Synopsis: In 'Cobwebs of Thought' by Arachne, a renowned neuroscientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that challenges the very fabric of human consciousness. As she delves deeper into her research, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between reality and illusion blurs. With her sanity at stake, she must navigate a labyrinth of secrets and hidden agendas, all while questioning the nature of perception and the limits of the human mind.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Science fiction, Mystery

Themes: Perception, Reality vs. Illusion, Mind manipulation, Identity
