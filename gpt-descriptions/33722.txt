Synopsis: In 'Donalblane of Darien' by J. Macdonald Oxley, a young Scottish adventurer named Donalblane sets sail for the uncharted lands of Darien, a mysterious and treacherous place filled with danger and intrigue. As he navigates through the lush jungles and encounters indigenous tribes, Donalblane becomes entangled in a web of political rivalries and forbidden love. With his life hanging in the balance, he must make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of both himself and the land he has come to call home.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
