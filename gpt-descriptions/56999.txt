Synopsis: 
In the remote wilderness of Athabasca, a young man named Jack Chanty finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger. As he embarks on a treacherous journey to uncover the truth behind a series of strange occurrences, Jack must confront his own demons and face the harsh realities of survival in the unforgiving Canadian wilderness. With each step, he unravels a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but the lives of those he holds dear. Will Jack be able to navigate the treacherous landscape and uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Secrets, Redemption
