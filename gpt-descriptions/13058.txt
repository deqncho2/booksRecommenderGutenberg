Synopsis: In 'The Teeth of the Tiger' by Maurice Leblanc, a mysterious and cunning criminal mastermind known as "The Tiger" emerges, leaving a trail of chaos and fear in his wake. As the police struggle to catch him, three brothers with unique skills and backgrounds are recruited by a secret organization to bring down this elusive villain. With their lives on the line, the brothers must navigate a dangerous world of espionage, deception, and high-stakes action to stop The Tiger before he strikes again.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Crime, Justice, Family bonds
