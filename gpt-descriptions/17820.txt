Synopsis: In 'From the Darkness Cometh the Light, or Struggles for Freedom' by Lucy A. Delaney, a young woman named Lucy Ann recounts her extraordinary journey from enslavement to freedom. Through her captivating narrative, she unveils the harrowing experiences she endured, the resilience she discovered within herself, and the profound impact of the Underground Railroad on her life. As Lucy Ann's story unfolds, readers are transported to a world of danger, hope, and the unyielding pursuit of liberty.

Genres: Autobiography, Historical Fiction, African American Literature

Themes: Slavery, Freedom, Resilience, Identity
