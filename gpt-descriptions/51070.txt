Synopsis: 

In 'Raiders of the Sarhad: Being an Account of the Campaign of Arms and Bluff Against the Brigands of the Persian-Baluchi Border during the Great War', R. E. H. Dyer takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous landscapes of the Persian-Baluchi border during World War I. This gripping account follows a group of brave soldiers as they navigate the dangerous terrain, facing not only the physical challenges of the region but also the cunning tactics of the notorious brigands. As the campaign unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of war is laid bare. 

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Survival, Loyalty, Deception
