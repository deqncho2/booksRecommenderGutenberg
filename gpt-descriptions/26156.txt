Synopsis: In 'Hopes and Fears: or, scenes from the life of a spinster', Charlotte M. Yonge takes us on a captivating journey through the life of a spinster named Rachel Curtis. Set in the 19th century, Rachel navigates the challenges and triumphs of her unconventional life, facing societal expectations, personal desires, and the pursuit of happiness. As she grapples with her hopes and fears, Rachel's story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, revealing the complexities of love, friendship, and self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Society and Gender Expectations, Self-Identity and Independence, Love and Relationships
