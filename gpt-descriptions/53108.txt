Synopsis: In 'Confessions of the Czarina', Catherine, Princess Radziwill takes readers on a captivating journey through the opulent and scandalous world of the Russian imperial court. Through her intimate and revealing memoir, she unveils the hidden truths, political intrigues, and personal struggles that shaped the life of the last Russian Empress. From her passionate love affairs to her role in the downfall of the Romanov dynasty, Catherine's confessions offer a mesmerizing glimpse into the life of a woman who wielded immense power and faced unimaginable challenges.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Biography

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Betrayal, Royal Intrigue
