Synopsis: In 'Cottage on the Curve' by Mary Lamers, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn to a charming cottage nestled in a picturesque curve of the countryside. Intrigued by its mysterious past, she embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets hidden within its walls. As she delves deeper into the history of the cottage, Emily discovers a connection that transcends time, leading her to question her own identity and the power of love. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a web of emotions and must make choices that will shape her future.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Love, Secrets, Timelessness
