Synopsis: In "The Mysteries of Free Masonry: Containing All the Degrees of the Order Conferred in a Master's Lodge," William Morgan delves into the secretive world of Freemasonry, revealing the hidden rituals and ceremonies that take place within the walls of a Master's Lodge. As Morgan uncovers the mysteries behind this ancient and enigmatic organization, he finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where loyalty and betrayal go hand in hand. With each degree he uncovers, Morgan's curiosity grows, leading him down a path that will forever change his perception of the world around him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrecy, Loyalty, Betrayal, Power
