Synopsis: In 'The Middle of the Road: A Novel' by Gibbs, Philip, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a series of bizarre events that challenge the residents' beliefs and values. As the townspeople grapple with their own secrets and desires, they are forced to confront the consequences of their choices and the true nature of their community. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of human nature and the complexities of morality.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Morality, Secrets, Small-town dynamics
