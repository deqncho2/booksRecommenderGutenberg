Synopsis: In the small town of Willow Creek, a mysterious stranger arrives with a peculiar talent - the ability to hook watermelons. As the townspeople become captivated by this extraordinary skill, they soon discover that there is more to this stranger than meets the eye. Set in the late 19th century, 'Hooking Watermelons: 1898' takes readers on a journey filled with intrigue, secrets, and unexpected twists that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Deception, Community
