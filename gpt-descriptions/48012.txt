Synopsis: 

In 'Diary of Richard Cocks, Volume 2: Cape-Merchant in the English Factory in Japan, 1615-1622, with Correspondence', we delve into the captivating world of Richard Cocks, an English merchant stationed in Japan during the early 17th century. Through his detailed diary entries and correspondence, we witness the challenges, triumphs, and cultural clashes he encounters while navigating the complex web of trade and diplomacy in a foreign land. From encounters with powerful samurais and shrewd Japanese merchants to his observations on the fascinating customs and traditions of the Japanese people, Cocks provides a unique and intimate perspective on this pivotal period in history. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Memoir

Themes: Cultural exchange, Trade and diplomacy, Exploration and discovery
