Synopsis: 
In 'The Mistress of Shenstone' by Florence L. Barclay, a young woman named Joan Carthew finds herself unexpectedly inheriting a grand estate called Shenstone. As she settles into her new life, she becomes captivated by the mysterious past of the previous mistress of Shenstone, Lady Ingleby. Determined to uncover the truth, Joan embarks on a journey that leads her through a web of secrets, forbidden love, and unexpected revelations. As she delves deeper into Lady Ingleby's story, Joan discovers that her own destiny may be intertwined with that of her enigmatic predecessor.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Secrets and hidden identities, Fate and destiny
