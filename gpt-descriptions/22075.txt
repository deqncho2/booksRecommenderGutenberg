Synopsis: In 'The Life of Duty, v. 2: A year's plain sermons on the Gospels or Epistles', H. J. Wilmot-Buxton takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the teachings of the Gospels and Epistles. With a collection of sermons that delve into the depths of human nature, the author explores the challenges and triumphs of living a life of duty. From the struggles of temptation to the power of forgiveness, this book offers profound insights that will inspire readers to reflect on their own spiritual journey.

Genres: Religious, Sermons, Christian Literature

Themes: Duty, Faith, Temptation, Forgiveness, Spiritual Growth
