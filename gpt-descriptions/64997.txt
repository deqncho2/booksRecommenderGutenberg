Synopsis: In 'The Joss: A Reversion' by Richard Marsh, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young man named Jack discovers an ancient artifact with unimaginable powers. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the Joss, he becomes entangled in a web of danger, deceit, and supernatural forces. With each revelation, Jack's world is turned upside down, and he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear. This thrilling and suspenseful novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the enigma of the Joss.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Power of ancient artifacts, Deception and secrets, Unraveling mysteries
