Synopsis: Bunny Brown and his sister Sue are thrilled when their father surprises them with a Shetland pony named Star. As they embark on exciting adventures with their new pony, Bunny and Sue learn valuable lessons about responsibility, friendship, and the joys of outdoor exploration. From picnics in the countryside to unexpected encounters, the siblings' bond with Star strengthens, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and unforgettable experiences.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Responsibility, Friendship, Outdoor exploration
