Synopsis: 
In 'The Man in Lower Ten' by Mary Roberts Rinehart, a lawyer named Lawrence Blakely finds himself embroiled in a mysterious murder case when he wakes up on a train to find a dead man in the lower berth of his sleeping car. As the investigation unfolds, Blakely becomes entangled in a web of deceit, mistaken identities, and unexpected twists, all while trying to prove his own innocence. With the clock ticking, he must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Identity, Deception, Justice
