Synopsis: In 'King Hacon's Death, and Bran and the Black Dog: Two Ballads', two haunting ballads unfold, each with its own mysterious and captivating story. One tells the tale of a king's demise, shrouded in secrets and treachery, while the other follows a young protagonist's encounter with a menacing black dog that holds a dark secret. As the ballads intertwine, the reader is drawn into a world of suspense, folklore, and the supernatural, where the past and present collide in unexpected ways.

Genres: Poetry, Folklore, Mystery

Themes: Betrayal, Fate, Supernatural, Identity
