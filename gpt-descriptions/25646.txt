Synopsis: In "Child's Health Primer For Primary Classes: With Special Reference to the Effects of Alcoholic Drinks, Stimulants, and Narcotics upon The Human System," Jane Andrews presents a captivating and informative guide that explores the impact of substances on the human body. Through engaging storytelling and relatable examples, Andrews educates young readers about the potential dangers of alcohol, stimulants, and narcotics, emphasizing the importance of making healthy choices. This enlightening primer provides a valuable foundation for children to understand the effects of these substances and empowers them to prioritize their well-being.

Genres: Educational, Children's Literature

Themes: Health education, Substance abuse prevention, Personal responsibility
