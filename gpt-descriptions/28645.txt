Synopsis: In 'Lost in the Future' by Peterson, John Victor, a brilliant scientist invents a time machine that unexpectedly propels him centuries into the future. Stranded in a world vastly different from his own, he must navigate through advanced technology, unfamiliar customs, and a society on the brink of collapse. As he desperately searches for a way back home, he becomes entangled in a dangerous conspiracy that threatens not only his own existence but the fate of humanity itself. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must confront his own beliefs and confront the ethical dilemmas of altering the course of history.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Technology, Identity, Morality
