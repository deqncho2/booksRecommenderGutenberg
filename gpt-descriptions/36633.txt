Synopsis: In 'King Eric and the Outlaws, Vol. 3: or, the Throne, the Church, and the People in the Thirteenth Century,' the captivating tale continues as King Eric and his loyal band of outlaws face their greatest challenges yet. As power struggles intensify between the throne, the church, and the people, Eric must navigate treacherous alliances, uncover hidden secrets, and confront his own inner demons. With the fate of the kingdom hanging in the balance, the stakes have never been higher for Eric and his comrades.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Redemption, Identity
