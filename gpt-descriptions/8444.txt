Synopsis: In 'Cæsar or Nothing' by Pío Baroja, a young man named Cæsar Moncada finds himself torn between his desire for a conventional life and his yearning for something more extraordinary. As he navigates through the vibrant streets of Madrid, he encounters a cast of eccentric characters who challenge his beliefs and push him to question the meaning of success and happiness. Cæsar's journey takes him on a thrilling adventure filled with love, ambition, and self-discovery, ultimately leading him to make a life-altering decision that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Conformity, Individualism
