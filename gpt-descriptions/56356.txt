Synopsis: In 'The Scarlet Bat: A Detective Story' by Fergus Hume, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious figure known as the Scarlet Bat emerges, leaving a trail of cryptic clues behind. As the detective delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden identities that will challenge his wit and intuition. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise, and the detective must race against time to unravel the truth before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Justice
