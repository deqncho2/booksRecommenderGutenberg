Synopsis: In the second volume of "When Valmond Came to Pontiac: The Story of a Lost Napoleon," the captivating tale of Valmond continues as he becomes entangled in a web of love, loyalty, and political intrigue. As his true identity remains shrouded in mystery, Valmond's presence in the small Canadian town of Pontiac sparks both admiration and suspicion among its inhabitants. With his charismatic charm and enigmatic past, Valmond's influence begins to shape the lives of those around him, leading to unexpected consequences and a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Identity, Power, Love and Betrayal
