Synopsis: 

In 'Contribution to Passamaquoddy Folk-Lore', Jesse Walter Fewkes takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich cultural heritage of the Passamaquoddy tribe. Through his meticulous research and firsthand experiences, Fewkes unveils a treasure trove of captivating folk tales, legends, and traditions that have been passed down through generations. From mythical creatures and supernatural encounters to ancient rituals and the significance of nature, this collection of Passamaquoddy folklore offers a fascinating glimpse into a world filled with wonder and enchantment.

Genres: Folklore, Anthropology, Cultural Studies

Themes: Oral Tradition, Mythology, Cultural Identity
