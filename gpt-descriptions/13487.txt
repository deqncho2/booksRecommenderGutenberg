Synopsis: In 'The Ideal Bartender' by Bullock, Tom, embark on a captivating journey through the world of mixology as the author shares his expertise and secrets behind crafting the perfect cocktail. From classic recipes to innovative concoctions, this book serves as a comprehensive guide for both aspiring and seasoned bartenders. With an emphasis on technique, presentation, and the art of hospitality, Bullock's insights will leave readers thirsty for more.

Genres: Non-fiction, Culinary, Lifestyle

Themes: Mixology, Hospitality, Craftsmanship
