Synopsis: 

In 'A Sub and a Submarine: The Story of H.M. Submarine R19 in the Great War', Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a thrilling journey beneath the waves during World War I. The story follows the crew of the R19, a British submarine, as they navigate treacherous waters and face unimaginable dangers. From intense battles with enemy ships to heart-stopping encounters with deadly mines, this gripping tale showcases the bravery and resilience of these submariners in the face of adversity. As the crew fights to protect their vessel and complete their mission, readers will be captivated by the suspense and excitement that unfolds in this gripping wartime adventure.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Bravery, Resilience, Camaraderie, Survival
