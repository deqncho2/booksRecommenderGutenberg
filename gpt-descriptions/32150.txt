Synopsis: In 'Prison of a Billion Years' by Stephen Marlowe, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Jonathan Grant embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that goes horribly wrong, trapping him in a mysterious prison for an unimaginable length of time. As he struggles to understand his predicament and find a way to escape, he uncovers shocking secrets about the nature of time and the true purpose of his confinement. With each passing year, Grant's determination to break free grows stronger, leading him on a mind-bending journey that challenges his sanity and forces him to confront the darkest corners of his own mind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Time, Isolation, Identity, Redemption
