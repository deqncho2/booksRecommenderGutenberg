Synopsis: 
'Overland Red: A Romance of the Moonstone Cañon Trail' follows the thrilling adventures of Red, a rugged and enigmatic cowboy, as he embarks on a treacherous journey through the Moonstone Cañon Trail. Filled with danger, romance, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale takes readers on a wild ride through the untamed American West, where Red's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Courage, Survival, Love, Redemption
