Synopsis: 

'Algonquin Legends of New England' by Charles Godfrey Leland is a captivating collection of Native American folklore that takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the rich cultural heritage of the Algonquin tribes. From enchanting creation myths to thrilling tales of legendary heroes and mystical creatures, this book unveils a world of magic, wisdom, and adventure. Delve into the captivating stories of the Algonquin people and discover the hidden depths of their beliefs, traditions, and values.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Native American Studies

Themes: Cultural heritage, Spirituality, Oral tradition
