Synopsis: In "The Rock of Chickamauga: A Story of the Western Crisis," Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on a gripping journey through the American Civil War. Set against the backdrop of the Battle of Chickamauga, the story follows a young soldier named Harry Kenton as he navigates the chaos and brutality of war. As Harry faces the horrors of combat, he must also confront personal challenges and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With vivid descriptions and intense action, Altsheler paints a vivid picture of the Western Theater of the war and the individuals caught in its tumultuous grip.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Coming of Age
