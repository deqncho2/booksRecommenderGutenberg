Synopsis: 
'A Book of the Cevennes' by S. Baring-Gould takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged and mysterious landscapes of the Cevennes region in France. The story follows a young Englishman who finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, ancient legends, and hidden treasures as he explores the remote villages and untamed wilderness of this enchanting land. With each step, he uncovers the dark history and folklore of the Cevennes, encountering eccentric characters, supernatural occurrences, and unexpected love along the way. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the region, he must confront his own fears and desires, ultimately leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Mythology, Identity, Love and Desire
