Synopsis: 
'Poetical Works of Johnson, Parnell, Gray, and Smollett: With Memoirs, Critical Dissertations, and Explanatory Notes' is a captivating collection of poetry by renowned authors Samuel Johnson, Thomas Parnell, Thomas Gray, and Tobias Smollett. This anthology brings together their most celebrated works, accompanied by insightful memoirs, critical dissertations, and explanatory notes that provide a deeper understanding of the poems. From Johnson's powerful verses to Parnell's lyrical beauty, Gray's melancholic musings, and Smollett's vivid storytelling, this collection offers a diverse range of poetic styles and themes. Whether exploring love, nature, society, or the human condition, these poets captivate readers with their profound insights and masterful use of language.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Criticism, Biography

Themes: Love, Nature, Society, Human Condition
