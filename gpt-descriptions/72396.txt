Synopsis: 
In 'The complete works of John Gower, volume 4: The Latin works', readers are transported to a world of medieval poetry and intellectual exploration. This volume showcases the Latin works of John Gower, a renowned English poet of the 14th century. Through his eloquent verses, Gower delves into various themes, offering insightful reflections on love, morality, and the complexities of human nature. From captivating narratives to thought-provoking allegories, this collection invites readers to immerse themselves in the rich literary tapestry woven by one of the most influential poets of his time.

Genres: Poetry, Medieval Literature, Latin Literature

Themes: Love, Morality, Human Nature
