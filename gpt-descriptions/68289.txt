Synopsis: In 'Billiards' by Broadfoot, William, a seemingly ordinary game of billiards takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious stranger challenges the protagonist to a high-stakes match. As the game progresses, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Redemption, Fate
