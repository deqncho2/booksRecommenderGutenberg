Synopsis: In 'Sweet Clover: Utilization' by Coe, H. S., a small town is forever changed when a mysterious plant called sweet clover appears overnight. As the townspeople begin to discover the plant's unique properties, they find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, the residents must navigate the consequences of their choices and confront the true nature of the sweet clover. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind this extraordinary plant.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Power of nature, Consequences of choices, Secrets and deception
