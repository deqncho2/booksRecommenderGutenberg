Synopsis: 

'Proverb Lore: Many sayings, wise or otherwise, on many subjects, gleaned from many sources' is a captivating collection of timeless wisdom and thought-provoking sayings compiled by F. Edward Hulme. From ancient proverbs to modern adages, this book takes readers on a journey through the rich tapestry of human wisdom, offering insights on various subjects. With each turn of the page, readers will be delighted by the diversity of sources and the profound truths encapsulated in these concise and memorable sayings. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Philosophy

Themes: Wisdom, Human Experience, Cultural Heritage
