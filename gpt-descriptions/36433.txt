Synopsis: 
'Chronicles of the Schonberg-Cotta Family' follows the lives of a German family as they navigate through the tumultuous events of the Reformation in the 16th century. From the perspective of various family members, the story delves into their struggles, triumphs, and unwavering faith in the face of persecution and religious upheaval. As they face personal and societal challenges, the Schonberg-Cotta family's unwavering commitment to their beliefs and each other is put to the test, leading to unexpected revelations and inspiring acts of courage.

Genres: Historical fiction, Christian fiction, Family drama

Themes: Faith, Perseverance, Family bonds, Religious freedom
