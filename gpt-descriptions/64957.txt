Synopsis: In 'The Magnetic Girl' by Richard Marsh, we are transported to the late 19th century where we meet Lulu Hurst, a young girl with an extraordinary power. Lulu possesses the ability to generate a mysterious force that attracts objects towards her, leaving audiences in awe and disbelief. As her fame grows, Lulu finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and manipulation, where her powers become both a blessing and a curse. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, Lulu must navigate a world of deception and danger to uncover the truth about her abilities and ultimately, herself.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Power and Control, Identity and Self-Discovery
