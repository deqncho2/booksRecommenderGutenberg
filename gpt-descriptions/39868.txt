Synopsis: In "Glinda of Oz," Princess Ozma and Dorothy embark on a treacherous journey to the mysterious lands of the Flatheads and the Skeezers. As they face dire perils and encounter strange magic, their only hope lies in the powerful sorcery of Glinda the Good. Will they be able to overcome the dangers and find their way back home?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Magic, Good vs. Evil
