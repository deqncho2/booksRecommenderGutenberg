Synopsis: In "A Friend of Marie-Antoinette (Lady Atkyns)" by Barbey, Frédéric, readers are transported to the opulent court of Versailles, where Lady Atkyns, an Englishwoman, finds herself entangled in a web of intrigue and secrets. As she becomes a confidante of the enigmatic Marie-Antoinette, Lady Atkyns must navigate the treacherous waters of court politics, scandal, and forbidden love. With the French Revolution looming on the horizon, the story unfolds with suspense and passion, revealing the hidden depths of loyalty and betrayal.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Court Intrigue, Friendship, Loyalty, Betrayal
