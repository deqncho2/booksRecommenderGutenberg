Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Sabinsport, secrets run deep and tensions simmer beneath the surface. When a mysterious stranger arrives, the town's residents find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger. As the tide rises, so does the intensity of the unfolding events, leading to unexpected alliances and shocking revelations. With its richly drawn characters and atmospheric setting, 'The Rising of the Tide: The Story of Sabinsport' is a gripping tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Small-town secrets, Intrigue, Betrayal, Redemption
