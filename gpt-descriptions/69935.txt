Synopsis: In 'The Safety First Club' by William Theophilus Nichols, a group of misfit teenagers stumble upon a mysterious club that promises to keep them safe from all harm. As they delve deeper into the club's secrets, they uncover a world of danger, adventure, and unexpected alliances. With their lives on the line, they must navigate a treacherous path filled with thrilling twists and turns, all while questioning the true nature of safety.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Trust, Identity, Risk-taking
