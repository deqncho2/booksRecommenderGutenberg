Synopsis: In 'No Surrender! A Tale of the Rising in La Vendee', George Alfred Henty takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the French Revolution. Set in the picturesque region of La Vendee, the story follows the adventures of a young Englishman, Harry Sandwith, who finds himself caught up in the violent uprising against the revolutionary government. As Harry becomes entangled in the conflict, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, face dangerous enemies, and make difficult choices that will test his loyalty and courage. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Henty paints a vivid picture of the chaos and heroism that unfolded during this lesser-known chapter of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Revolution, Conflict
