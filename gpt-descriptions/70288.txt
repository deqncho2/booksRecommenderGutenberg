Synopsis: In "Conjure Wife" by Fritz Leiber, a professor discovers that his wife practices witchcraft to protect him from the malevolent forces of the world. However, when he convinces her to stop, he soon realizes the terrifying consequences of his actions. As he delves deeper into the dark secrets surrounding him, he must confront his own beliefs and the power of magic that exists in the everyday world.

Genres: Fantasy, Horror, Mystery

Themes: Witchcraft, Power, Gender Roles, Superstition
