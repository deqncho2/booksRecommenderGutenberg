Synopsis: In 'Phantom Wires: A Novel' by Arthur Stringer, a renowned scientist stumbles upon a mysterious phenomenon that defies all known laws of physics. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between reality and illusion blurs. With each discovery, he uncovers a hidden world of secrets and hidden agendas, forcing him to question everything he thought he knew. As the stakes rise, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it consumes him entirely.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Scientific Discovery, Conspiracy
