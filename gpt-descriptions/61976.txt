Synopsis: In "Shadrach" by Nelson S. Bond, a young boy named John befriends an enigmatic old man named Shadrach, who possesses a mysterious power that captivates the entire town. As John delves deeper into Shadrach's world, he uncovers secrets that challenge his beliefs and force him to confront the complexities of life and death. With its blend of magic, friendship, and self-discovery, "Shadrach" takes readers on a thought-provoking journey that will leave them questioning the boundaries of reality.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Mortality
