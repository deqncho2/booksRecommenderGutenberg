Synopsis: 
In 'The Girl Scouts at Home; or, Rosanna's Beautiful Day' by Katherine Keene Galt, follow the adventures of Rosanna and her fellow Girl Scouts as they embark on a day filled with excitement and unexpected challenges. From exploring the great outdoors to solving mysteries, the girls learn valuable lessons about friendship, teamwork, and the importance of embracing new experiences. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the girls' determination and resourcefulness, making this a delightful and heartwarming tale for all ages.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Exploration, Self-discovery
