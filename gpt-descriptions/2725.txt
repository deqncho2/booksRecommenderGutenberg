Synopsis: 
In 'Red Pepper Burns' by Grace S. Richmond, the charismatic and enigmatic Dr. David Burns, known as Red Pepper, captivates the small town of Mercer with his unconventional methods and fiery personality. As he navigates the challenges of his medical practice, he finds himself entangled in a web of love, friendship, and professional ethics. With his quick wit and unyielding determination, Red Pepper must confront his own personal demons while striving to make a difference in the lives of those around him.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Professional ethics, Personal growth
