Synopsis: 
In 'The Little Book of British Quadrupeds' by May, W., embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of British wildlife. This delightful book introduces readers to a variety of fascinating quadrupeds that inhabit the British Isles, from the elusive otter to the nimble red squirrel. With vivid descriptions and charming illustrations, May, W. brings these creatures to life, revealing their unique characteristics, habitats, and behaviors. Whether you're a nature enthusiast or simply curious about the diverse wildlife of Britain, this book is sure to captivate and educate readers of all ages.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Wildlife

Themes: British wildlife, Nature conservation, Animal behavior
