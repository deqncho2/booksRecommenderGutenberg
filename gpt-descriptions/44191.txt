Synopsis: 
In 'Gorillas & Chimpanzees', renowned primatologist Richard Lynch Garner takes readers on an extraordinary journey into the fascinating world of our closest relatives in the animal kingdom. Through captivating anecdotes and scientific insights, Garner unveils the complex social dynamics, remarkable intelligence, and surprising behaviors of gorillas and chimpanzees. From heartwarming tales of familial bonds to astonishing displays of problem-solving skills, this book offers a captivating exploration of these incredible creatures that will leave readers in awe of their similarities to humans.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Animal behavior, Evolution, Conservation
