Synopsis: In 'History of Astronomy' by George Forbes, embark on a captivating journey through the ages as the secrets of the universe are unveiled. From ancient civilizations gazing at the stars in awe to groundbreaking discoveries that revolutionized our understanding of the cosmos, this book delves into the fascinating evolution of astronomy. Prepare to be enthralled by the tales of brilliant minds, their relentless pursuit of knowledge, and the profound impact their findings had on humanity's perception of the universe. As you turn each page, you will witness the birth of new theories, the clash of scientific ideologies, and the triumphs that forever changed our place in the cosmos.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Exploration, Scientific Revolution, Human Curiosity, Intellectual Progress
