Synopsis: 

In "Hunting Dogs: Describes in a Practical Manner the Training, Handling, Treatment, Breeds, Etc., Best Adapted for Night Hunting as Well as Gun Dogs for Daylight Sport" by Oliver Hartley, readers are taken on an exhilarating journey into the world of hunting dogs. This comprehensive guide provides invaluable insights into the training, handling, and treatment of these remarkable animals, with a particular focus on their suitability for night hunting and daylight sport. From the selection of the most suitable breeds to the intricacies of training techniques, Hartley's expertise shines through, offering readers a practical and engaging resource for all their hunting dog needs.

Genres: Non-fiction, Outdoor and Sports, Animal Care

Themes: Hunting, Dog Training, Animal Welfare
