Synopsis: In 'Double-Cross' by Frederik Pohl, a brilliant scientist finds himself caught in a web of deceit and betrayal when his groundbreaking invention attracts the attention of powerful forces. As he navigates a dangerous game of espionage and manipulation, he must uncover the truth and determine who he can trust before it's too late. With unexpected twists and high-stakes action, this thrilling science fiction novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Trust, Technology
