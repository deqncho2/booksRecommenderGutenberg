Synopsis: In 'Death Makes a Mistake' by William P. McGivern, a seemingly routine murder investigation takes a thrilling turn when a detective discovers a shocking mistake made by Death itself. As he delves deeper into the case, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, lies, and supernatural forces that challenge his beliefs and push him to the edge of his sanity. With time running out, he must confront the consequences of Death's error and make a decision that could alter the course of fate forever.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Fate and free will, the nature of death, the power of choice
