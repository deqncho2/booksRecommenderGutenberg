Synopsis: 

In 'Hatchie, the Guardian Slave; or, The Heiress of Bellevue: A Tale of the Mississippi and the South-west', author Warren T. Ashton takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and complex world of the Mississippi and the South-west. The story revolves around Hatchie, a young slave who becomes the guardian of a wealthy heiress named Belle. As they navigate the treacherous waters of the Mississippi River, Hatchie and Belle find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden love, and dangerous alliances. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this tale explores the power dynamics of the antebellum South and the resilience of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Slavery, Forbidden Love, Power Dynamics, Resilience
