Synopsis: In 'Revolt of the Brains' by Stephen Marlowe, a brilliant scientist invents a groundbreaking technology that allows him to tap into the collective intelligence of humanity. However, as he delves deeper into the minds of others, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel the fabric of society. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path of power, manipulation, and moral dilemmas to save the world from the very minds he sought to understand.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Human Nature
