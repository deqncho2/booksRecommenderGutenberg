Synopsis: In "The Passionate Year" by James Hilton, a young and ambitious teacher named Stephen Glennard stumbles upon a collection of love letters written by a famous author. Recognizing their potential value, he decides to exploit the letters for personal gain. However, as he delves deeper into his scheme, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, love, and moral dilemmas that threaten to unravel his carefully constructed life.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Love, Deception
