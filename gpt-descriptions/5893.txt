Synopsis: Two Little Women on a Holiday follows the adventures of two spirited young girls, Ruth and Alice, as they embark on a thrilling summer vacation. Leaving behind the comforts of home, they find themselves in a charming seaside town, where they encounter mysterious strangers, uncover hidden secrets, and stumble upon unexpected friendships. As they navigate through a series of delightful escapades, the girls learn valuable lessons about courage, independence, and the power of imagination. 

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Coming of age
