Synopsis: In 'The King's Achievement' by Robert Hugh Benson, a young man named Richard is faced with a life-altering decision when he discovers a hidden secret about his lineage. As he delves deeper into his family's history, Richard finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue, religious conflict, and personal sacrifice. With the fate of a nation hanging in the balance, Richard must navigate treacherous waters and make a choice that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Power, Faith
