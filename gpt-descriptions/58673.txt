Synopsis: 
In "It Takes a Thief" by Miller, Walter M., a notorious thief named Jack is given an offer he can't refuse: a chance to steal the most valuable artifact in the world. With his unique set of skills, Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him across continents, facing dangerous obstacles and unexpected allies. As he delves deeper into the world of high-stakes theft, Jack begins to question his own motives and the true nature of the artifact he's been tasked to steal. With time running out, he must navigate a web of deception and betrayal to complete the ultimate heist.

Genres: Action, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Morality, Identity
