Synopsis:
In 'Rujub, the Juggler' by G. A. Henty, the reader is transported to the exotic and mysterious world of India during the British Raj. The story follows the adventures of Rujub, a talented juggler who finds himself caught up in a web of intrigue and danger. As he travels through the vibrant landscapes of India, Rujub encounters treacherous enemies, forms unlikely alliances, and uncovers ancient secrets that could change the course of history. With his quick wit, cunning, and extraordinary juggling skills, Rujub becomes a key player in a thrilling game of espionage and survival. Will he be able to outsmart his adversaries and protect those he holds dear?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Espionage, Survival, Cultural clash
