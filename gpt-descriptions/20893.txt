Synopsis: In 'A Guide to Methods and Observation in History: Studies in High School Observation', Davis, Calvin Olin takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of historical research and observation. This insightful guide explores the methods and techniques used by historians to uncover the truth behind the events of the past. From analyzing primary sources to deciphering hidden narratives, Davis provides invaluable tips and strategies for high school students to enhance their historical understanding and critical thinking skills. Through engaging examples and thought-provoking exercises, this book encourages readers to become active participants in the study of history, unlocking the secrets that lie within the annals of time.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, History

Themes: Historical research, Observation, Critical thinking, Education
