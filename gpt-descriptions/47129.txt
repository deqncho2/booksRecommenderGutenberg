Synopsis: 
In "Flying the Atlantic in Sixteen Hours: With a Discussion of Aircraft in Commerce and Transportation," Arthur Whitten Brown, a renowned aviator, takes us on a thrilling journey across the Atlantic Ocean. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding moments, Brown recounts his daring adventure, pushing the limits of aviation and defying the odds. Alongside his captivating narrative, he delves into the fascinating world of aircraft in commerce and transportation, shedding light on the immense impact of aviation on society. This gripping account will leave readers on the edge of their seats, marveling at the courage and innovation that made transatlantic flight a reality.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Aviation

Themes: Exploration, Innovation, Human Achievement
