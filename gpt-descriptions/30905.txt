Synopsis: 
In 'The Boarded-Up House' by Augusta Huiell Seaman, a group of curious teenagers stumble upon an abandoned house with a mysterious past. As they delve deeper into the secrets hidden within its walls, they uncover a series of clues that lead them on a thrilling adventure. With danger lurking around every corner, the teenagers must unravel the truth before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Curiosity, Friendship, Secrets, Danger
