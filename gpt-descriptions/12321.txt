Synopsis: In 'Rudimental Divine Science', Mary Baker Eddy takes readers on a captivating journey of self-discovery and spiritual enlightenment. Through her profound insights and teachings, she unveils the hidden power within each individual to heal, transform, and find true harmony. As the story unfolds, readers are introduced to a world where the boundaries of the physical and spiritual realms blur, and where the pursuit of divine understanding becomes a transformative and life-altering experience. With its thought-provoking narrative and compelling characters, 'Rudimental Divine Science' invites readers to explore the depths of their own spirituality and embrace the limitless possibilities that lie within.

Genres: Spiritual, Self-help, Philosophy

Themes: Self-discovery, Spiritual enlightenment, Healing, Transformation
