Synopsis: In 'First the Blade: A Comedy of Growth' by Dane Clemence, we follow the hilarious and unexpected journey of a young man named Oliver as he navigates the ups and downs of adulthood. From awkward encounters to unexpected friendships, Oliver's life takes a series of comical twists and turns that will keep readers laughing and guessing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Humor
