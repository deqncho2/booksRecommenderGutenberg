Synopsis: 
"The Northmen, Columbus and Cabot, 985-1503" takes readers on a captivating journey through the exploration and discovery of the New World. From the fearless Viking expeditions to the ambitious voyages of Christopher Columbus and John Cabot, this book unveils the remarkable stories of these intrepid explorers who forever changed the course of history. Filled with tales of adventure, courage, and the pursuit of knowledge, this narrative sheds light on the fascinating encounters, triumphs, and challenges faced by these early explorers as they navigated uncharted waters and encountered new civilizations.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Cultural Encounter, Ambition, Legacy
