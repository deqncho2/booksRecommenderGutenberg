Synopsis: 

In 'English Cathedrals Illustrated: Second and Revised Edition', Francis Bond takes readers on a captivating journey through the awe-inspiring beauty and rich history of English cathedrals. With stunning illustrations and detailed descriptions, Bond delves into the architectural marvels, intricate stained glass windows, and majestic spires that have stood the test of time. From the grandeur of Canterbury Cathedral to the ethereal beauty of Salisbury Cathedral, this book offers a visual feast for lovers of history, art, and architecture. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Architecture, History

Themes: English heritage, Religious architecture, Cultural preservation
