Synopsis: In "Head of the Lower School" by Dorothea Moore, a charismatic and unconventional teacher takes on the challenging role of leading a lower school, bringing a fresh perspective to education. As she navigates the complexities of the school system, she inspires both students and colleagues with her innovative teaching methods and unwavering dedication. However, her unorthodox approach also raises eyebrows and invites resistance from those resistant to change. This captivating story explores the power of education, the importance of embracing new ideas, and the impact one individual can have on an entire community.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Education

Themes: Education reform, Innovation, Individuality, Resistance to change
