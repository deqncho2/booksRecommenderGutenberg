Synopsis: 
In 'The Human Machine' by Arnold Bennett, we are introduced to a fascinating character named James Mortimer. Mortimer is an ordinary man with an extraordinary ability - he can control his body and mind to an astonishing degree. As he navigates through the challenges of daily life, Mortimer's unique talent becomes both a blessing and a curse. From his encounters with love and ambition to his struggles with societal expectations, Mortimer's journey is a captivating exploration of the human potential and the power of self-control.

Genres: Fiction, Psychological, Drama

Themes: Human potential, Self-control, Society's expectations
