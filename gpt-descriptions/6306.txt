Synopsis: 
In 'Little Journeys to the Homes of the Great - Volume 06: Little Journeys to the Homes of Eminent Artists', Elbert Hubbard takes readers on a captivating journey into the lives and homes of some of the most renowned artists in history. From the studios of Michelangelo and Rembrandt to the residences of Turner and Whistler, Hubbard provides intimate glimpses into the personal spaces and creative processes of these eminent artists. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, the reader is transported into the world of art, gaining a deeper understanding of the lives and inspirations behind these masterpieces.

Genres: Biography, Art, History

Themes: Artistic inspiration, Creativity, Personal spaces, Art history
