Synopsis: 
"Five-Head" Creek; and Fish Drugging in the Pacific: 1901 by Louis Becke is a thrilling adventure set in the Pacific Islands. The story follows a group of men who embark on a dangerous expedition to a remote creek known as "Five-Head." As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter unexpected challenges, they find themselves entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the true nature of their mission becomes increasingly unclear. Will they uncover the secrets hidden within "Five-Head" Creek, or will they succumb to the perils that await them?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Deception
