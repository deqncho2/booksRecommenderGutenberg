Synopsis: In 'Dave Dashaway, Air Champion; Or, Wizard Work in the Clouds' by Rockwood, Roy, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure alongside young aviator Dave Dashaway. When Dave is given the opportunity to participate in a daring air race, he finds himself facing not only fierce competition but also a mysterious and dangerous enemy. As Dave navigates the skies, he must rely on his wit, courage, and the help of his loyal friends to uncover the truth behind the sinister plot that threatens not only his chances of winning but also the safety of all involved. With high-flying action, unexpected twists, and a race against time, this book will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Perseverance
