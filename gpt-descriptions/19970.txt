Synopsis: In 'Son of Power' by Comfort, Will Levington, a young man named David discovers a hidden power within himself that sets him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and adventure. As he navigates through a world filled with ancient secrets and mystical beings, David must learn to harness his newfound abilities and confront the forces that seek to control him. With danger lurking at every turn, David's choices will determine not only his own fate but also the fate of those he holds dear.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Power and responsibility, Self-discovery, Good vs. evil
