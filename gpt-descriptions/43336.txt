Synopsis: 
"The Pig Brother, and Other Fables and Stories: A Supplementary Reader for the Fourth School Year" by Laura Elizabeth Howe Richards is a collection of enchanting fables and stories that will captivate young readers. From the heartwarming tale of a pig who becomes an unexpected hero to the adventures of a mischievous squirrel, each story is filled with valuable lessons and delightful characters. With its engaging narratives and charming illustrations, this book is sure to spark the imagination and leave readers eager for more.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fables, Short Stories

Themes: Morality, Friendship, Courage, Kindness
