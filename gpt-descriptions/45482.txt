Synopsis: 
"The New Book of Nonsense: Contribution to the Great Central Fair in Aid of the Sanitary Commission" is a delightful collection of whimsical and humorous poems, riddles, and stories. Filled with clever wordplay, absurd situations, and unexpected twists, this anonymous work will transport readers to a world of pure imagination. From mischievous animals to peculiar characters, each page offers a delightful surprise that will leave readers chuckling and pondering. This collection is a true testament to the power of laughter and the joy of embracing the nonsensical.

Genres: Poetry, Humor, Children's Literature

Themes: Wordplay, Absurdity, Imagination
