Synopsis: In "The Substance of Faith Allied with Science (6th Ed.): A Catechism for Parents and Teachers," Sir Oliver Lodge presents a thought-provoking exploration of the intersection between faith and science. Through a series of engaging questions and answers, Lodge delves into the mysteries of the universe, challenging conventional beliefs and inviting readers to embark on a journey of intellectual discovery. With a blend of scientific knowledge and spiritual insight, this book offers a fresh perspective on the age-old debate between reason and faith.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Religion

Themes: Faith and reason, Science and spirituality, Intellectual exploration
