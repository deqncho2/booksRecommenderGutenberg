Synopsis: In the heart of Italy, a small village is plagued by a mysterious creature that emerges only under the cover of darkness. As the villagers live in fear, a renowned detective is called upon to unravel the enigma surrounding this nocturnal menace. With each clue leading him deeper into the secrets of the village, the detective must confront his own demons and navigate a web of deceit, betrayal, and ancient folklore. As the truth slowly unravels, the line between reality and myth becomes blurred, leaving the detective questioning his own sanity and the true nature of the creature of the night.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Identity, Folklore
