Synopsis: In 'The Plague of Lust, Vol. 2 (of 2): Being a History of Venereal Disease in Classical Antiquity' by Julius Rosenbaum, embark on a captivating journey through the ancient world as the author delves into the intriguing history of venereal diseases. From the bustling streets of Rome to the opulent palaces of Greece, this book uncovers the hidden secrets and scandalous tales surrounding the spread of these afflictions. With meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Rosenbaum paints a vivid picture of the societal impact and personal tragedies caused by these diseases, revealing a world where desire and consequence intertwine in unexpected ways.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Medical History, Ancient History

Themes: Sexuality, Disease, Social Stigma, Historical Perspectives
