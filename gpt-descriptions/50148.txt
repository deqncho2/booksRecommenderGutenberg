Synopsis: In 'Looking Forward: A Dream of the United States of the Americas in 1999' by Arthur Bird, a captivating vision of the future unfolds as the author takes us on a journey through a reimagined America. Set in the year 1999, the story follows a diverse group of individuals who find themselves caught up in a web of political intrigue, technological advancements, and personal struggles. As they navigate this new world, they must confront their own beliefs and confront the question of what it truly means to be an American. With its gripping narrative and thought-provoking scenarios, 'Looking Forward' offers a compelling glimpse into a possible future that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Science Fiction, Political Thriller, Speculative Fiction

Themes: Identity, Politics, Technology, Social Change
