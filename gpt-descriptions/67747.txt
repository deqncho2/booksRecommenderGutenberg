Synopsis: In the small town of Yellowbank, a mysterious stranger arrives, stirring up old secrets and hidden desires. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of suspicion and intrigue, they must confront their own pasts and face the consequences of their actions. With a captivating blend of mystery, romance, and suspense, "You Ask Anybody" takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of a seemingly idyllic community.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
