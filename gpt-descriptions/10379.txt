Synopsis: In 'At Love's Cost' by Charles Garvice, a young woman named Muriel finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she falls in love with a mysterious stranger. As their relationship deepens, Muriel must navigate through unexpected twists and turns, risking everything for a chance at true love. With its gripping plot and captivating characters, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Secrets and deception
