Synopsis: In 'A Short View of the Immorality, and Profaneness of the English Stage: Together with the Sense of Antiquity on this Argument', Jeremy Collier delves into the controversial world of English theater, exposing its immorality and profanity. Through a thought-provoking analysis, Collier challenges the prevailing norms of the stage and presents a compelling argument rooted in the wisdom of antiquity. This work offers a fascinating exploration of the moral implications of theatrical productions, inviting readers to question the impact of entertainment on society.

Genres: Non-fiction, Cultural Criticism, Literary Criticism

Themes: Morality, Profanity, Entertainment, Cultural Influence
