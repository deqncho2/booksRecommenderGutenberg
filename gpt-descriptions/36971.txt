Synopsis: In 'The Captured Scout of the Army of the James', we follow the extraordinary life of Sergeant Henry H. Manning, a member of the Twenty-fourth Mass. Regiment during the American Civil War. Manning's story takes a dramatic turn when he is captured by Confederate forces and becomes a scout for the enemy. As he navigates the treacherous world of espionage, Manning's loyalty and courage are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to survive and fulfill his duty to both sides?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Biography

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Espionage
