Synopsis: In "Old Coloured Books" by George Paston, a young woman named Lucy discovers a hidden collection of antique books in her family's attic. As she delves into the enchanting world of these beautifully illustrated volumes, Lucy becomes captivated by the stories they hold and the secrets they reveal. With each book she opens, Lucy embarks on a journey through time, encountering fascinating characters and uncovering unexpected connections to her own life. As she unravels the mysteries of these old coloured books, Lucy's perspective on her family, her past, and her future is forever transformed.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Family secrets, Time travel, Identity, Love of literature
