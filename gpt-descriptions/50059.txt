Synopsis: In 'How to Behave and How to Amuse: A Handy Manual of Etiquette and Parlor Games', George H. Sandison presents a delightful guide that combines the art of proper behavior with the joy of entertaining parlor games. This charming manual offers a treasure trove of advice on social etiquette, from table manners and conversation skills to appropriate dress and hosting etiquette. Alongside these invaluable tips, Sandison provides a collection of amusing parlor games that are sure to enliven any gathering. With its blend of practical advice and entertaining diversions, this book is a must-read for those seeking to navigate social situations with grace and charm.

Genres: Non-fiction, Etiquette, Parlor Games

Themes: Social etiquette, Entertainment, Social gatherings
