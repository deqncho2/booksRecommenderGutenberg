Synopsis: In 'Adventures of Working Men. From the Notebook of a Working Surgeon' by George Manville Fenn, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the lives of hardworking individuals. From the bustling streets of London to the treacherous seas, this collection of stories follows the trials and triumphs of working men from various walks of life. Through the eyes of a dedicated surgeon, we witness the sacrifices, dangers, and unexpected adventures that these men encounter in their pursuit of livelihood. From the heart-pounding rescue missions to the heartwarming tales of camaraderie, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the lives of those who toil to make a living.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Hard work, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Perseverance
