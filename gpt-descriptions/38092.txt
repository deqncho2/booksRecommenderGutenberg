Synopsis: In "The Book of God: In the Light of the Higher Criticism: With Special Reference to Dean Farrar's New Apology," G.W. Foote delves into the controversial topic of biblical criticism, challenging traditional beliefs and shedding light on the inconsistencies found within religious texts. Through a meticulous examination of Dean Farrar's apologetic work, Foote presents a thought-provoking analysis that challenges readers to question long-held beliefs and explore the true origins and nature of religious texts.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Criticism, Theology

Themes: Biblical Criticism, Religious Texts, Historical Accuracy, Faith and Reason
