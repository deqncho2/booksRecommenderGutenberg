Synopsis: In 'The Quickening' by Francis Lynde, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a dark secret that threatens to unravel the fabric of the community. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the townspeople must confront their deepest fears and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of their town. With its gripping suspense and unexpected twists, 'The Quickening' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, Small-town dynamics, Moral dilemmas
