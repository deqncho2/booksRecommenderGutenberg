Synopsis: In "Legends of Longdendale," Thomas C. Middleton weaves a captivating tapestry of tales inspired by the rich folk-lore of the enchanting Longdendale Valley and its surrounding areas. From ancient myths to ghostly apparitions, the stories transport readers to a world where legends come alive, revealing the hidden secrets and mysteries of this mystical land. As the tales unfold, readers will be drawn into a realm of supernatural encounters, heroic quests, and the timeless battle between good and evil. With each story, Middleton masterfully captures the essence of the region's folklore, leaving readers spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Supernatural encounters, Heroism, Good vs. Evil
