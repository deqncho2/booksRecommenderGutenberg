Synopsis: In the small town of Spruce, a mysterious event occurs that alters the lives of its residents forever. As the sky transforms into a mesmerizing line of spruce trees, the townspeople find themselves drawn to its beauty and enigmatic power. Amidst this phenomenon, a young artist named Marshall embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the sky line, encountering unexpected friendships, hidden secrets, and a deeper connection to nature. With each step, Marshall unravels the mysteries of Spruce and discovers the profound impact that art and nature can have on the human spirit.

Genres: Magical realism, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: The power of art, the connection between humans and nature, the search for meaning and truth
