Synopsis: 

In "Drinks of the World," Ashton and Mew take readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of beverages. From ancient traditions to modern mixology, this book explores the rich history, cultural significance, and unique flavors of drinks from every corner of the globe. With vivid descriptions and stunning visuals, the authors delve into the secrets behind iconic cocktails, traditional brews, and exotic concoctions, revealing the stories and techniques that make each drink truly special. Whether you're a seasoned connoisseur or simply curious about the world's libations, this book will leave you thirsty for more.

Genres: Non-fiction, Food and Drink, Travel

Themes: Cultural diversity, History, Culinary traditions
