Synopsis: 
In the midst of a tempestuous storm, a powerful sorcerer named Prospero and his daughter Miranda find themselves stranded on a remote island. As they navigate their new surroundings, they encounter a host of intriguing characters, including a mischievous spirit named Ariel and a deformed creature called Caliban. With his magical abilities, Prospero seeks to manipulate these individuals to exact revenge on those who wronged him in the past. However, as the story unfolds, the boundaries between reality and illusion become blurred, leading to unexpected revelations and profound transformations.

Genres: 
Drama, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: 
Power and Control, Betrayal and Forgiveness, Illusion and Reality
