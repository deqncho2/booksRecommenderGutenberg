Synopsis: In 'Guarding His Goal' by Ralph Henry Barbour, a talented young athlete finds himself torn between his passion for soccer and the expectations of his family. As he navigates the challenges of high school, friendships, and first love, he must make difficult choices that will shape his future. With intense rivalries on the field and personal struggles off the field, this coming-of-age story explores the sacrifices one must make to pursue their dreams.

Genres: Young Adult, Sports, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Pursuing one's passion, Family expectations, Friendship, Sacrifice
