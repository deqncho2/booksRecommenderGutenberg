Synopsis: In 'The Serpent River' by Don Wilcox, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances that have left the community in fear and confusion. As the local sheriff investigates the unsettling events, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel the fabric of reality itself. With each revelation, the tension builds, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, the power of fear, the thin line between reality and the supernatural
