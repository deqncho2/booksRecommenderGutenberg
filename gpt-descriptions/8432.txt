Synopsis: In 'Black Rebellion: Five Slave Revolts', Thomas Wentworth Higginson delves into the gripping and often overlooked history of five significant slave revolts. From the daring rebellion led by Nat Turner to the lesser-known but equally powerful uprisings, Higginson brings to life the stories of these courageous individuals who defied the oppressive system of slavery. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, he explores the motivations, strategies, and ultimate outcomes of these revolts, shedding light on the resilience and determination of those who fought for their freedom.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Slavery, Resistance, Freedom, Social Justice
