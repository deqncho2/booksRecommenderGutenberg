Synopsis: In 'A Queen's Error' by Curties, Henry, a young queen finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and personal dilemmas. As she navigates the treacherous waters of power, she must make difficult choices that could shape the destiny of her kingdom. With secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering who can be trusted and what consequences lie ahead.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Intrigue

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Personal sacrifice
