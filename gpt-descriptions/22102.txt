Synopsis: In 'The Hills of Home' by Alfred Coppel, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets hidden within the ancient hills of a remote village. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the past, he becomes entangled in a web of danger, romance, and intrigue. With each step, he unravels a centuries-old enigma that could change the course of history. Will he unlock the truth before it consumes him?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the power of history and its impact on the present, the pursuit of truth
