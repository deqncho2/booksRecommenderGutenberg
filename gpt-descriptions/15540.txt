Synopsis: 
'Across India; Or, Live Boys in the Far East' follows the thrilling adventures of a group of young boys as they embark on a journey across the vast and diverse landscapes of India. From bustling cities to remote villages, they encounter a myriad of cultures, encounter dangerous situations, and form unexpected friendships along the way. As they navigate through the challenges of the Far East, the boys learn valuable life lessons and discover the true meaning of courage, resilience, and the importance of embracing new experiences.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Cultural diversity, Self-discovery
