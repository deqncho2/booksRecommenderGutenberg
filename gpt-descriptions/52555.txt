Synopsis: In 'Arminell: A Social Romance, Vol. 1', the reader is introduced to the captivating and enigmatic character of Arminell, a young woman who defies societal expectations and norms. Set in the picturesque countryside of Cornwall, this novel follows Arminell's journey as she navigates the complexities of love, family, and her own identity. As secrets unravel and passions ignite, the reader is drawn into a world of intrigue and romance, where the boundaries of social class and convention are tested. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, 'Arminell' offers a captivating exploration of love, self-discovery, and the power of individuality.

Genres: Romance, Social Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Desire, Identity and Self-Discovery, Social Class and Conventions
