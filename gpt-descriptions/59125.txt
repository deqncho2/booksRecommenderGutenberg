Synopsis: In "Aquarium" by Acton, Harold, a young girl named Caitlin discovers a hidden world within the walls of an aquarium that holds secrets and wonders beyond her imagination. As she embarks on a thrilling adventure, Caitlin must navigate through a maze of mysteries, encountering extraordinary creatures and unexpected allies along the way. With each twist and turn, she unravels the truth about the aquarium and herself, leading to a breathtaking climax that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Discovery, Identity, Friendship, Courage
