Synopsis: In 'Vandemark's Folly' by Quick, Herbert, a young and ambitious engineer named Vandemark embarks on a daring project to build a revolutionary machine that could change the world. As he faces numerous challenges and obstacles, Vandemark's determination and ingenuity are put to the test. Along the way, he encounters unexpected allies and adversaries, and must navigate the complex web of politics and personal relationships. With the fate of his invention and his own future hanging in the balance, Vandemark must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Technological Thriller

Themes: Ambition, Innovation, Perseverance, Power Dynamics
