Synopsis: In 'The Struggle for Missouri' by John McElroy, a gripping tale unfolds as the Civil War rages on and the fate of Missouri hangs in the balance. Amidst the chaos and bloodshed, a diverse cast of characters find themselves caught in a web of political intrigue, personal vendettas, and the fight for freedom. As the lines between loyalty and betrayal blur, the struggle for Missouri becomes a battle not only for territory, but for the very soul of a divided nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Political Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity, Freedom, Power
