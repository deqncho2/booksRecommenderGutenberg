Synopsis: In the second volume of "Mortomley's Estate: A Novel," the captivating story continues to unfold, delving deeper into the secrets and mysteries surrounding the Mortomley family and their ancestral estate. As the characters navigate through unexpected twists and turns, long-held secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of the estate's haunting past begins to emerge. With each page, the reader is drawn further into the intricate web of intrigue, unable to resist the urge to uncover the truth behind Mortomley's Estate.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Haunting past, Intrigue, Betrayal
