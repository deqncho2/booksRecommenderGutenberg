Synopsis: In 'The Automobile Girls at Newport; Or, Watching the Summer Parade', the spirited and adventurous Automobile Girls find themselves in the glamorous world of Newport, Rhode Island. As they navigate the social scene and encounter unexpected challenges, they uncover secrets and form unexpected alliances. With their wit and determination, the girls embark on a thrilling journey that will test their friendships and reveal the true nature of those around them.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Social Class
