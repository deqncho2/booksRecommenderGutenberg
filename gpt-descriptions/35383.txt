Synopsis: In 'The Little Missis' by Charlotte Skinner, a young woman named Emily finds herself thrust into a world of wealth and privilege when she marries a wealthy older man. As she navigates the complexities of high society, Emily must grapple with the expectations placed upon her and the sacrifices she must make to maintain her newfound status. However, as she delves deeper into this glamorous world, Emily begins to question the true meaning of happiness and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Social class, Identity, Self-discovery
