Synopsis: In 'Control Group' by Roger D. Aycock, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Emily Morgan finds herself caught in a web of conspiracy and deception when she is recruited to work on a top-secret government project. As she delves deeper into her research, she uncovers shocking truths that challenge her understanding of reality and force her to question who she can trust. With her life on the line, Dr. Morgan must navigate a dangerous game of manipulation and power in order to uncover the truth and protect her discoveries.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Government conspiracy, Scientific ethics, Betrayal, Identity
