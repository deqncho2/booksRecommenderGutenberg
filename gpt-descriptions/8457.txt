Synopsis: 
In 'Frenzied Fiction' by Stephen Leacock, prepare to be whisked away on a wild and hilarious journey through the absurdities of everyday life. From the misadventures of a bumbling detective to the comical mishaps of a hapless inventor, this collection of short stories will have you laughing out loud and eagerly turning the pages. With Leacock's signature wit and clever storytelling, 'Frenzied Fiction' is a delightful escape into a world where the ordinary becomes extraordinary and the mundane becomes uproarious.

Genres: 
Humor, Satire, Short Stories

Themes: 
Absurdity of everyday life, Comedy, Irony
