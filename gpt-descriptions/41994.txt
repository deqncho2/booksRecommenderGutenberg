Synopsis: In 'Money: Thoughts for God's Stewards', Andrew Murray delves into the often overlooked topic of money and its significance in the lives of believers. With thought-provoking insights and practical wisdom, Murray challenges readers to examine their attitudes towards wealth and to align their financial decisions with God's purposes. Through biblical teachings and personal anecdotes, this book offers a fresh perspective on the role of money in the Christian life, encouraging readers to become faithful stewards of God's resources.

Genres: Christian literature, Personal finance, Spiritual growth

Themes: Stewardship, Financial responsibility, Trusting God with resources
