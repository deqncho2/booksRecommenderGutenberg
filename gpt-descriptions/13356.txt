Synopsis: In "The Captain's Toll-Gate" by Frank R. Stockton, a retired sea captain finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a mysterious toll-gate in the middle of nowhere. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the toll-gate, he becomes entangled in a web of danger, romance, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the captain must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to navigate through a world filled with hidden agendas and treacherous characters.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Exploration of hidden worlds, Unexpected alliances, The power of wit and resourcefulness
