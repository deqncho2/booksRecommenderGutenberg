Synopsis: In 'Nelson's Home Comforts: Thirteenth Edition', Mary Hooper takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of domesticity and the secrets that lie within the walls of a seemingly ordinary home. As the protagonist, Nelson, delves into the history of his family's residence, he uncovers a series of hidden rooms, mysterious artifacts, and long-forgotten stories that intertwine with his own life. With each discovery, Nelson becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, leading him to question his own identity and the true nature of the house he calls home. As he unravels the secrets of the past, Nelson must confront the ghosts that haunt both the physical and emotional spaces of his life, ultimately finding solace and understanding in the most unexpected places.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Family, Home, History
