Synopsis: In 'Talks on Talking' by Kleiser Grenville, a renowned linguist and communication expert takes readers on a captivating journey through the art of conversation. With a blend of insightful anecdotes, practical tips, and thought-provoking exercises, this book explores the intricacies of effective communication and the power of words. From mastering the art of listening to decoding non-verbal cues, Grenville provides invaluable guidance on how to navigate conversations with finesse and authenticity. Whether you're seeking to enhance your personal relationships or improve your professional interactions, 'Talks on Talking' offers a wealth of wisdom to help you become a more skilled and engaging communicator.

Genres: Self-help, Communication, Psychology

Themes: Effective communication, Active listening, Non-verbal communication, Interpersonal skills
