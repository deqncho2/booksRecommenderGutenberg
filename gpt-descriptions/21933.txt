Synopsis: In 'Much Darker Days' by Lang, Andrew, a young woman named Emily finds herself trapped in a mysterious town where the sun never shines. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the town, she uncovers a dark and sinister history that threatens her very existence. With the help of a group of unlikely allies, Emily must navigate through a web of deception and danger to uncover the truth and find a way to escape the town's clutches.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Survival, Betrayal
