Synopsis: 
'Verses of a V.A.D.' by Brittain, Vera is a poignant collection of poetry that offers a unique perspective on the experiences of a V.A.D. (Voluntary Aid Detachment) nurse during World War I. Through her heartfelt verses, the author delves into the emotional turmoil, loss, and resilience that she and her fellow nurses faced while tending to wounded soldiers. From the horrors of the battlefield to the tender moments of connection, this collection captures the raw emotions and profound impact of war on those who served.

Genres: Poetry, Memoir, Historical

Themes: War, Loss, Resilience, Human Connection
