Synopsis: 

In "Friction, Lubrication and the Lubricants in Horology," Lewis, W. T. delves into the fascinating world of horology, exploring the intricate relationship between friction, lubrication, and the lubricants used in the field. Through a captivating blend of historical anecdotes, scientific explanations, and practical insights, Lewis takes readers on a journey that unveils the secrets behind the smooth functioning of timepieces. From the ancient mechanisms of early clocks to the cutting-edge technology of modern watches, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the crucial role lubrication plays in horology. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Friction, Lubrication, Horology, Technological advancements, Historical significance
