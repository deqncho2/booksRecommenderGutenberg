Synopsis: 

'Pathfinders of the West: Being the Thrilling Story of the Adventures of the Men Who Discovered the Great Northwest: Radisson, La Vérendrye, Lewis and Clark' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the uncharted territories of the American West. Agnes C. Laut weaves together the captivating tales of four remarkable explorers who braved the unknown, faced unimaginable challenges, and forever changed the course of history. From the daring exploits of Pierre-Esprit Radisson and his quest for furs, to the epic adventures of the La Vérendrye family in their pursuit of a mythical water route to the Pacific, and finally to the legendary expedition of Lewis and Clark, this book immerses readers in the thrilling and dangerous world of early exploration. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Perseverance, Frontier life
