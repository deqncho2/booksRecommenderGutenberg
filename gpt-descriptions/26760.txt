Synopsis: In 'Memories of Bethany' by John R. Macduff, a mysterious letter arrives, plunging the protagonist into a whirlwind of emotions and memories from a long-forgotten past. As they delve into the enigmatic world of Bethany, a small coastal town, they uncover secrets that threaten to unravel their present reality. With each memory, the protagonist becomes entangled in a web of love, loss, and betrayal, leading them to question their own identity and the true nature of the people around them. As the past and present collide, the reader is taken on a captivating journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Memory, Identity, Love and Betrayal
