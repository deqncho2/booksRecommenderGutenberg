Synopsis: In 'Home Fires in France', a young American woman finds herself caught in the midst of World War I as she volunteers to aid the war effort in France. As she navigates the challenges of war-torn Europe, she forms unexpected friendships and discovers her own strength and resilience. This gripping tale explores the impact of war on individuals and the power of human connection in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: War, Friendship, Resilience, Identity
