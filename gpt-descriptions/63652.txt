Synopsis: In 'The Violators' by Binder, Eando, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he is hired to investigate a series of mysterious crimes. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a dark underworld where nothing is as it seems, and the line between right and wrong becomes blurred. With his reputation and life on the line, the detective must navigate a treacherous landscape of corruption and betrayal to uncover the truth before it consumes him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Morality, Corruption
