Synopsis: In the fourth volume of "A History of the Four Georges and of William IV," Justin McCarthy delves into the captivating lives and reigns of the British monarchs from George III to William IV. From political intrigue and scandalous affairs to the struggles for power and the changing social landscape, McCarthy paints a vivid picture of the turbulent times that shaped these influential rulers. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, this volume offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the four Georges and William IV, revealing the complexities and challenges they faced during their reigns.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Monarchy, Politics, Social change
