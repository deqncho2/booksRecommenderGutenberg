Synopsis: 
Step into the fascinating world of Borneo head-hunters, where ancient traditions and captivating folklore intertwine to create a rich tapestry of culture and mystery. In "The home-life of Borneo head-hunters: Its festivals and folk-lore," William Henry Furness takes us on a captivating journey through the daily lives, rituals, and beliefs of these enigmatic people. From their vibrant festivals that pulsate with music and dance to the mesmerizing tales passed down through generations, this book offers a rare glimpse into a world that is both captivating and hauntingly beautiful.

Genres: Anthropology, Cultural Studies, Travelogue

Themes: Cultural traditions, Folklore, Rituals, Identity, Oral storytelling
