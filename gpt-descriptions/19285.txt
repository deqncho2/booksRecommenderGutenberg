Synopsis: In 'The Promulgation of Universal Peace', ʻAbdu'l-Bahá, the son of the founder of the Bahá'í Faith, shares profound insights and teachings on the essential principles of peace, unity, and the spiritual transformation of humanity. Through a series of powerful and thought-provoking speeches delivered during his travels in the early 20th century, ʻAbdu'l-Bahá explores the interconnectedness of all religions, the importance of education, the role of women in society, and the need for global cooperation. With eloquence and wisdom, he offers a vision of a harmonious world where individuals can rise above their differences and work together for the betterment of humanity.

Genres: Spiritual, Philosophy, Non-fiction

Themes: Peace, Unity, Interfaith dialogue, Education, Gender equality
