Synopsis:
In the small village of Markendale, a mysterious house stands shrouded in secrets and whispers. When a young artist named Hugh Grayling arrives to paint the house, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. As he delves deeper into the house's dark history, he uncovers a series of chilling events that threaten to consume him. With each discovery, Hugh's own sanity is tested, and he must confront the malevolent forces that reside within the House of the White Shadows.

Genres:
Mystery, Suspense, Gothic Fiction

Themes:
Secrets and hidden pasts, Psychological suspense, Supernatural elements
