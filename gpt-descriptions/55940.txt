Synopsis: In 'Theosophy and Life's Deeper Problems', Annie Besant delivers four captivating convention lectures in Bombay, exploring the profound mysteries of existence. With thought-provoking insights and a captivating narrative style, Besant delves into the depths of human consciousness, unveiling the hidden truths that lie beneath the surface of everyday life. As she addresses the audience at the fortieth anniversary of the Theosophical Society, December 1915, Besant invites readers on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual enlightenment, challenging conventional beliefs and offering a fresh perspective on life's most profound questions.

Genres: Philosophy, Spiritualism, Non-fiction

Themes: Mysticism, Self-realization, Spiritual awakening, Metaphysics
