Synopsis: 

In "Old Times on the Upper Mississippi: The Recollections of a Steamboat Pilot from 1854 to 1863," George Byron Merrick takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling era of steamboat travel on the Mississippi River. Through vivid and nostalgic recollections, Merrick shares his firsthand experiences as a steamboat pilot during a pivotal period in American history. From treacherous river conditions to encounters with colorful characters, the author paints a vivid picture of the challenges and adventures faced by those who navigated the mighty Mississippi. As the narrative unfolds, readers are transported to a bygone era, filled with excitement, danger, and the ever-present allure of the river.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Adventure

Themes: Nostalgia, Exploration, River Life, American History
