Synopsis: 
In 'Quintus Oakes: A Detective Story' by Charles Ross Jackson, readers are introduced to the enigmatic detective Quintus Oakes, who finds himself entangled in a perplexing case of murder and deception. Set in the early 20th century, Oakes must navigate a web of secrets and lies as he delves into the mysterious death of a prominent socialite. With each clue leading him deeper into a world of intrigue, Oakes must rely on his sharp intellect and keen observation skills to uncover the truth and bring the culprit to justice. As the plot thickens, readers will be captivated by the twists and turns, eagerly following Oakes' every move as he unravels the complex puzzle before him.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Justice
