Synopsis: In 'Our Farm of Four Acres and the Money We Made by It', Coulton, Miss takes us on a captivating journey through her experiences on a small farm. From the challenges of starting from scratch to the unexpected successes that come their way, this memoir offers a delightful and inspiring tale of determination, resilience, and the rewards of hard work. As the seasons change and the farm evolves, the reader is drawn into the joys and struggles of farm life, creating a heartfelt connection to the author's journey.

Genres: Memoir, Non-fiction, Agriculture

Themes: Determination, Resilience, Hard work, Farm life
