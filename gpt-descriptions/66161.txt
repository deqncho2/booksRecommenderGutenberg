Synopsis:
In 'Special Detective (Ashton-Kirk)', the brilliant and enigmatic Ashton-Kirk takes on a perplexing case that has stumped the police. When a wealthy businessman is found dead under mysterious circumstances, Ashton-Kirk is called upon to unravel the intricate web of clues and secrets surrounding the crime. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a world of hidden motives, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With his sharp intellect and unconventional methods, Ashton-Kirk navigates a treacherous path to uncover the truth, all while staying one step ahead of those who would prefer to keep it buried.

Genres:
Mystery, Detective Fiction, Thriller

Themes:
Intelligence and deduction, Crime and justice, Secrets and deception
