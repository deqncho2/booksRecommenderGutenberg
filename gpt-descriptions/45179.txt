Synopsis: 

"The Invention of the Track Circuit" delves into the fascinating history of Dr. William Robinson's groundbreaking creation that revolutionized the world of railways. This captivating narrative explores how his invention of the track circuit paved the way for the development of automatic block signaling and interlocking systems that we rely on today. As the story unfolds, readers will be captivated by the challenges, triumphs, and ingenuity that shaped this pivotal invention, forever changing the landscape of rail transportation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Engineering, Technology

Themes: Innovation, Progress, Transportation advancements
