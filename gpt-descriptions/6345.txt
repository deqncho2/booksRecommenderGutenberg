Synopsis: 
In 'Seven Maids of Far Cathay: Being English Notes From a Chinese Class Book', Ledyard and Mandall take readers on a captivating journey through the lives of seven young Chinese women. Through their unique perspectives, we witness the clash of cultures, the challenges of tradition, and the pursuit of personal freedom. As these maids navigate their way through love, family, and societal expectations, their stories intertwine to create a vivid tapestry of life in Far Cathay.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Cultural Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Cultural identity, Gender roles, Tradition vs. Modernity, Love and relationships
