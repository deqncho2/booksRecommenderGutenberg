Synopsis: In 'Ptomaine Street: The Tale of Warble Petticoat', Wells, Carolyn takes readers on a captivating journey through the eccentric and mysterious Ptomaine Street. Warble Petticoat, a young woman with an extraordinary talent, finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and unexpected encounters as she navigates the peculiar inhabitants of this peculiar street. From peculiar neighbors to hidden treasures, Warble's adventures will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Unconventional communities and neighborhoods
