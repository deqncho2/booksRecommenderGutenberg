Synopsis: In 'Captivity and Escape' by Jean Martin, a gripping tale unfolds as a young woman finds herself trapped in a mysterious and dangerous situation. With each passing day, she must navigate a treacherous web of secrets and deceit, desperately seeking a way to break free. As the tension rises, unexpected alliances are formed, and the true nature of her captors is revealed. Will she be able to outsmart her captors and find a way to escape, or will she remain trapped forever?

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Deception, Resilience
