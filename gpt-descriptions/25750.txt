Synopsis: 

'Colonial Born: A Tale of the Queensland bush' by Scott, G. Firth is a captivating historical fiction novel set in the rugged and untamed landscapes of Queensland, Australia. The story follows the life of a young woman named Emily, who is born and raised in the harsh conditions of the colonial bush. As she navigates the challenges and adversities of her surroundings, Emily's resilience and determination are put to the test. Along her journey, she encounters love, loss, and the complexities of colonial life, ultimately shaping her into a strong and independent individual. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Resilience, Survival, Love, Colonialism
