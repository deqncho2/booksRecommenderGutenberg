Synopsis: In 'P'laski's Tournament: 1891' by Thomas Nelson Page, a small town in Virginia becomes the stage for an extraordinary chess tournament that captivates the entire community. As the tournament unfolds, the players' strategic moves mirror the complex dynamics of the town, revealing hidden secrets, long-held grudges, and unexpected alliances. Amidst the intense battles on the chessboard, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and redemption, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Power dynamics, Secrets and lies, Redemption
