Synopsis: In "What I know of farming," Horace Greeley presents a captivating series of concise and straightforward explanations on the art of practical agriculture, rooted in scientific principles. With a focus on the essentials, Greeley delves into various aspects of farming, offering valuable insights and practical advice to both experienced farmers and those new to the field. From soil management and crop selection to livestock care and farm economics, this book serves as a comprehensive guide to successful farming. Through his engaging writing style and wealth of knowledge, Greeley invites readers to explore the fascinating world of agriculture and discover the secrets to cultivating a thriving farm.

Genres: Non-fiction, Agriculture, Science

Themes: Practicality, Scientific approach, Farming techniques, Agricultural knowledge
