Synopsis: 
In 'The Log of the Empire State' by Geneve L. A. Shaffer, a young sailor named Jack embarks on a thrilling journey aboard the majestic Empire State ship. As he navigates through treacherous waters and encounters mysterious creatures, Jack's courage and determination are put to the test. Along the way, he forms unexpected friendships and uncovers secrets that could change the course of his life forever. This gripping tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Self-discovery
