Synopsis: In 'The Star Beast' by Damon Knight, a young boy named John Thomas Stuart XI finds himself in possession of a peculiar extraterrestrial creature named Lummox. As Lummox grows, so does the chaos it causes, leading to a series of misadventures and misunderstandings that threaten to tear apart the fabric of society. With the help of his resourceful girlfriend, Betty, John must navigate a world filled with bureaucratic red tape, alien politics, and the ever-present danger of interstellar conflict. Will John be able to save Lummox and prevent a catastrophe of cosmic proportions?

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Responsibility, Intergalactic diplomacy
