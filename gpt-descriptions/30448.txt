Synopsis: In 'The Lieutenant-Governor: A Novel' by Carryl, Guy Wetmore, we are transported to the vibrant world of politics and power in a small New England town. The story follows the ambitious and charismatic Lieutenant-Governor, who finds himself entangled in a web of scandal, secrets, and unexpected alliances. As he navigates the treacherous waters of political intrigue, he must confront his own personal demons and make difficult choices that will shape the future of his career and the town he loves. With its gripping plot and complex characters, this novel delves into the dark underbelly of politics, exposing the lengths people will go to protect their interests and the consequences of their actions.

Genres: Political fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal, Ambition and morality
