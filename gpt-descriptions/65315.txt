Synopsis: In "Turner's Water-Colours at Farnley Hall," A. J. Finberg takes us on a captivating journey through the stunning watercolor collection of J.M.W. Turner, one of the greatest landscape painters of all time. As we explore the hidden treasures of Farnley Hall, we are transported to picturesque landscapes, breathtaking seascapes, and enchanting scenes that showcase Turner's mastery of light, color, and atmosphere. Through Finberg's meticulous descriptions and insightful analysis, we gain a deeper understanding of Turner's artistic process and the profound impact his work had on the art world. This book is a visual feast for art enthusiasts and a fascinating exploration of Turner's genius.

Genres: Art History, Non-fiction

Themes: Landscape painting, Romanticism, Artistic technique
