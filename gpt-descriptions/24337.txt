Synopsis: In 'Capitola's Peril: A Sequel to 'The Hidden Hand'' by Emma Dorothy Eliza Nevitte Southworth, the fearless and cunning Capitola faces new challenges as she navigates a world filled with danger, deception, and unexpected twists. As she finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and treachery, Capitola must rely on her wit and resourcefulness to survive. With her indomitable spirit and unwavering determination, she becomes a force to be reckoned with, captivating readers with her daring escapades and captivating charm.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Female empowerment, Resilience, Deception, Survival
