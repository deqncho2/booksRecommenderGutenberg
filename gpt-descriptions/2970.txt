Synopsis: In Volume 20 of his captivating memoirs, Jacques Casanova de Seingalt takes us on a thrilling journey through the vibrant city of Milan. Amidst the opulent palaces and bustling streets, Casanova finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love affairs, and dangerous alliances. As he navigates the treacherous world of high society, Casanova's wit, charm, and audacity are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to outsmart his adversaries and secure his own survival in this captivating tale of passion and deception?

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Power and Politics, Identity and Self-Discovery
