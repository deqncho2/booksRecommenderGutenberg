Synopsis: In "New worlds for old: A plain account of modern socialism," H.G. Wells takes readers on a captivating journey into the realm of modern socialism, exploring the potential for a new and improved society. Through a thought-provoking narrative, Wells delves into the intricacies of socialism, presenting a compelling vision of a world transformed by collective action and social justice. As he navigates the complexities of this alternative future, the author challenges conventional notions of capitalism and advocates for a more equitable and inclusive society. With his characteristic wit and insight, Wells invites readers to imagine a world where the pursuit of individual happiness is harmoniously balanced with the well-being of the entire community.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political philosophy, Social commentary

Themes: Socialism, Utopianism, Equality, Social justice, Collective action
