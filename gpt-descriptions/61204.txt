Synopsis: 
In 'The Recruit' by Walton Bryce, readers are introduced to Alex, a young man with a troubled past who is given a chance to start anew. He is recruited into a secretive organization that trains individuals with exceptional skills for a dangerous mission. As Alex undergoes intense training and faces unexpected challenges, he must navigate a world of deception, loyalty, and betrayal. With his life on the line, Alex must uncover the truth and prove himself worthy of his newfound opportunity.

Genres: Thriller, Action, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Betrayal
