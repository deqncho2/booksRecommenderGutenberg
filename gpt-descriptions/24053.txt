Synopsis: In 'The Little Lame Prince: Rewritten for Young Readers by Margaret Waters', we follow the journey of a young prince who, due to a tragic accident, is left with a physical disability that confines him to a wheelchair. Despite his limitations, the prince embarks on a magical adventure that takes him to a hidden kingdom, where he discovers his true potential and learns valuable lessons about courage, kindness, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Overcoming adversity, Self-discovery, Inner strength, Kindness, Imagination
