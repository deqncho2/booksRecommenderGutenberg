Synopsis: 
In 'Idle Ideas in 1905', Jerome K. Jerome takes readers on a delightful journey through his whimsical and humorous observations of everyday life in the early 20th century. From the peculiarities of train travel to the absurdities of modern technology, Jerome's witty anecdotes and clever insights offer a refreshing perspective on the world around us. With his signature charm and wit, he invites readers to join him in exploring the idiosyncrasies of human behavior and the comical aspects of the mundane. 

Genres: Humor, Satire, Essay collection

Themes: Everyday life, Human behavior, Observational humor
