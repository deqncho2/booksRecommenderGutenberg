Synopsis: 
'Across Coveted Lands; or, a Journey from Flushing (Holland) to Calcutta Overland' takes readers on an extraordinary adventure as Arnold Henry Savage Landor embarks on a daring expedition from Europe to Asia. With vivid descriptions and captivating encounters, Landor's journey unfolds through treacherous terrains, exotic cultures, and unexpected challenges. From the bustling streets of Calcutta to the remote corners of the Himalayas, this gripping travelogue offers a thrilling exploration of the unknown, leaving readers eager to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Perseverance
