Synopsis: In 'Children of the Wild' by Charles G. D. Roberts, a group of young friends embarks on a thrilling adventure deep into the untamed wilderness. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter mysterious creatures, they must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and the bonds of friendship to survive. With each step, they uncover ancient secrets that will change their lives forever, leading them to question the true nature of the wild and their place within it.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Nature, Self-discovery
