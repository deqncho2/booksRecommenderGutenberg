Synopsis: In 'Twelve Stories and a Dream', H.G. Wells takes readers on a captivating journey through twelve imaginative tales, each with its own unique twist. From a man who discovers a mysterious ability to control time to a scientist who invents a machine that can alter reality, these stories will transport you to worlds both familiar and extraordinary. As the characters grapple with their newfound powers and face unexpected consequences, the boundaries between dreams and reality blur, leaving readers questioning the nature of existence itself. Prepare to be enthralled by Wells' masterful storytelling and thought-provoking narratives.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Short Stories

Themes: Time, Reality, Power, Morality
