Synopsis: In the midst of World War I, Sara Lee Kennedy, a young American woman, finds herself in Belgium, where she becomes involved in a secret mission to aid the war effort. As she navigates the dangers and uncertainties of war-torn Europe, Sara Lee discovers her own strength and resilience, and finds love in the most unexpected of places. With a captivating blend of romance, suspense, and adventure, 'The Amazing Interlude' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the chaos of war and the transformative power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Courage in the face of adversity, The impact of war on individuals and society
