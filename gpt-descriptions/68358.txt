Synopsis: In 'Underground Movement' by Lang, Allen Kim, a group of unlikely heroes find themselves caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse as they navigate the treacherous world of underground crime. With secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, they must outsmart their enemies and uncover the truth before it's too late. As the stakes rise, the line between right and wrong becomes blurred, leaving the characters questioning their own morality and the lengths they are willing to go for justice.

Genres: Thriller, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Betrayal, Justice
