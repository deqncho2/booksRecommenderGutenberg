Synopsis: 
In 'The Case and Exceptions: Stories of Counsel and Clients' by Hill, Frederick Trevor, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing legal cases. From high-profile trials to lesser-known conflicts, this book delves into the complex relationships between lawyers and their clients, exploring the moral dilemmas, personal struggles, and unexpected twists that arise within the realm of law. Each story presents a unique and thought-provoking scenario, keeping readers on the edge of their seats as they navigate the intricacies of the legal system and the human condition.

Genres: Legal fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Morality, Justice, Loyalty, Truth, Human nature
