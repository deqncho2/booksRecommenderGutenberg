Synopsis: 
'Christmas in Poetry: Carols and Poems' is a captivating collection that takes readers on a delightful journey through the enchanting world of Christmas. From classic carols to heartfelt poems, this book beautifully captures the essence of the holiday season, evoking feelings of joy, love, and nostalgia. Each page is filled with lyrical verses and vivid imagery, transporting readers to a magical realm where the spirit of Christmas comes alive. Whether you are seeking to relive cherished memories or create new ones, this collection is sure to ignite the festive spirit within you.

Genres: Poetry, Holiday, Anthology

Themes: Christmas spirit, Joy, Love, Nostalgia
