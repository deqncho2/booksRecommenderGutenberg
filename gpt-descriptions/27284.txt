Synopsis: In 'The Talking Horse, and Other Tales' by F. Anstey, readers are transported to a world where animals possess the ability to communicate with humans. From a horse with a sharp wit and a penchant for sarcasm to a mischievous monkey who causes chaos wherever he goes, these enchanting tales explore the humorous and often unexpected interactions between humans and their animal counterparts. As the stories unfold, readers are taken on a delightful journey filled with laughter, clever wordplay, and a touch of whimsy.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Humor

Themes: Animal-human relationships, Communication, Wit and humor
