Synopsis: 

In 'Legend Land, Vol. 3: Being a Further Collection of Some of the Old Tales Told in Those Western Parts of Britain Served by the Great Western Railway', G. Basil Barham takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical and enchanting landscapes of Western Britain. This volume presents a collection of old tales that have been passed down through generations, each one filled with magic, adventure, and the rich folklore of the region. From ancient legends of knights and fairies to tales of haunted castles and hidden treasures, these stories transport readers to a world where the line between reality and myth blurs.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Mythology, Legends, Magic, Adventure, British folklore
