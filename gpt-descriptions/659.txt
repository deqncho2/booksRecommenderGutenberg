Synopsis: In "Paul the Peddler; Or, The Fortunes of a Young Street Merchant," follow the captivating journey of Paul Hoffman, a resourceful and determined young boy who finds himself thrust into the bustling streets of New York City. Facing numerous challenges and setbacks, Paul must navigate the treacherous world of street peddling, encountering both friends and foes along the way. As he strives to overcome adversity and carve out a better life for himself, Paul's resilience and unwavering spirit will inspire readers to believe in the power of determination and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Social mobility, Friendship
