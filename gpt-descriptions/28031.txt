Synopsis: In 'Resurrection' by Robert Shea, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything we know about life and death. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden world of secrets and conspiracies that threaten to unravel the fabric of reality itself. With each revelation, the stakes become higher, and the line between science and the supernatural blurs. This gripping tale of mystery and suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Life and death, Reality vs. perception, Conspiracy
