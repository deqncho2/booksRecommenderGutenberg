Synopsis: 
'Tides: A Book of Poems' by John Drinkwater is a captivating collection of verses that takes readers on a journey through the ebb and flow of life's experiences. From the gentle lapping of waves on a serene shore to the tumultuous crashing of storms, these poems explore the ever-changing nature of existence, delving into the depths of human emotions and the mysteries of the natural world. With each turn of the page, Drinkwater's lyrical words paint vivid images and evoke a range of emotions, leaving readers mesmerized by the power and beauty of his poetic expression.

Genres: Poetry, Nature, Emotion

Themes: Transience, Reflection, Connection with Nature
