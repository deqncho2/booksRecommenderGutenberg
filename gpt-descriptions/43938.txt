Synopsis: In the heart of ancient Rome, a powerful sorceress emerges, wielding dark magic and captivating the city's elite with her mysterious abilities. As she gains influence and control, a young senator's life becomes entangled with hers, leading him down a treacherous path of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of forbidden knowledge. With the fate of Rome hanging in the balance, the senator must navigate a world of political intrigue and supernatural forces to uncover the truth behind the sorceress's power and save his beloved city from destruction.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Power and corruption, Love and sacrifice, The clash of ancient beliefs and modern ideals
