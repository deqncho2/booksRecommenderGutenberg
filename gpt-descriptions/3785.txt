Synopsis: 

In the Reign of Terror: The Adventures of a Westminster Boy is a thrilling historical adventure set during the French Revolution. The story follows Harry Sandwith, a young English boy who finds himself caught up in the chaos and danger of revolutionary France. As he navigates through the treacherous streets of Paris, Harry becomes entangled in a web of espionage, secret missions, and daring escapes. With his quick wit and resourcefulness, he must rely on his courage and cunning to survive in a world where loyalty and betrayal are constantly at odds. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival, Espionage, Revolution
