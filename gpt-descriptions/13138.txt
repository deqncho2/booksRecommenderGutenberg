Synopsis: In 'The Forgotten Threshold: A Journal of Arthur Middleton' by Edward J. O'Brien, readers are transported into the captivating world of Arthur Middleton, a man who embarks on a mysterious journey that blurs the lines between reality and imagination. As Arthur delves deeper into his own mind, he uncovers hidden secrets and encounters enigmatic characters that challenge his perception of the world around him. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn into a mesmerizing narrative that explores the depths of human consciousness and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Perception, Reality vs. Illusion, Self-exploration
