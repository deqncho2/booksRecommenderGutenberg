Synopsis: 
In the thrilling novel 'Tulan' by MacApp, C. C., a young archaeologist named Amelia embarks on a dangerous expedition to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization. As she delves deeper into the mysterious ruins of Tulan, Amelia discovers a hidden world filled with treacherous traps, enigmatic symbols, and a powerful artifact that could change the course of history. With time running out and a ruthless organization hot on her trail, Amelia must decipher the clues left behind by the ancient Tulan people and unlock the truth before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Archaeology, Ancient Civilizations, Pursuit of Knowledge
