Synopsis: 
'Faith and Duty: Sermons on Free Texts, with Reference to the Church-Year' by L. B. Buchheimer is a collection of thought-provoking sermons that explore the intersection of faith and duty. Each sermon delves into a different free text, allowing the author to provide unique insights and perspectives on various aspects of life and spirituality. From examining the challenges of modern society to exploring the depths of human nature, Buchheimer's sermons offer a compelling exploration of faith and the responsibilities that come with it.

Genres: Religious, Sermons, Spirituality

Themes: Faith, Duty, Morality, Spirituality, Human Nature
