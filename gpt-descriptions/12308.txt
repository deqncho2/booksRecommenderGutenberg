Synopsis: In "Winning His Spurs: A Tale of the Crusades," follow the thrilling adventures of young Cuthbert, a brave and determined English lad who finds himself caught up in the tumultuous events of the Crusades. From his humble beginnings as a page in a noble household to his journey to the Holy Land, Cuthbert faces countless challenges, battles, and encounters with both friends and foes. As he strives to prove his worth and loyalty, Cuthbert's courage and skill are put to the ultimate test in this gripping tale of honor, chivalry, and the pursuit of glory.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Honor, Chivalry, Coming of Age
