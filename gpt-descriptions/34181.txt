Synopsis: 
'Irene Iddesleigh' by Ros, Amanda McKittrick is a captivating and unconventional novel that follows the life of the titular character, Irene Iddesleigh. Born into a wealthy family, Irene's life takes unexpected turns as she navigates love, betrayal, and societal expectations. With a unique writing style and unexpected plot twists, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within Irene's world.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Gothic Fiction

Themes: 
Love and Betrayal, Social Expectations, Wealth and Privilege
