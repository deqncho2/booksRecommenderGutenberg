Synopsis: 

Step back in time to the glamorous and scandalous court of Old Whitehall, where power, intrigue, and romance intertwine. In 'Court Beauties of Old Whitehall: Historiettes of the Restoration', author W. R. H. Trowbridge invites readers into the captivating world of the Restoration era, where the lives of the court beauties unfold in a series of captivating historiettes. From the dazzling Lady Castlemaine to the enigmatic Nell Gwyn, these women navigate the treacherous waters of court politics, using their beauty and wit to captivate kings and shape the destiny of a nation. As secrets are revealed and alliances are forged, readers will be enthralled by the scandalous affairs, fierce rivalries, and unexpected twists that define the lives of these court beauties.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Betrayal, Ambition and Intrigue
