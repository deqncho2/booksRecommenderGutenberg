Synopsis: In "A Brace of Boys: 1867, From "Little Brother"" by Ludlow, Fitz Hugh, readers are transported to the vibrant streets of 19th century New York City, where two young brothers embark on a thrilling adventure. As they navigate through the bustling city, encountering a cast of colorful characters and facing unexpected challenges, the boys' bond is tested and their resilience shines through. This captivating tale of brotherhood, courage, and the pursuit of dreams will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Brotherhood, Resilience, Pursuit of dreams
