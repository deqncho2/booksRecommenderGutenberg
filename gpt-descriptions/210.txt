Synopsis: In 'An International Episode' by Henry James, two young American women find themselves in the midst of a transatlantic adventure as they navigate the complexities of love, cultural differences, and societal expectations. Set against the backdrop of London and Paris, this captivating tale follows their encounters with a charming Englishman and a dashing Frenchman, leading to a series of unexpected twists and turns. As the characters grapple with their own desires and the clash of cultures, the story unfolds with wit, intrigue, and a touch of romance.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Cultural differences, Love and relationships, Societal expectations
