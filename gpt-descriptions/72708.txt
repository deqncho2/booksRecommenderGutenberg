Synopsis: 

In "Paddle and Portage, from Moosehead Lake to Aroostook River, Maine," Thomas Sedgwick Steele takes readers on an exhilarating adventure through the untamed wilderness of Maine. Join him as he embarks on a daring journey, navigating treacherous rapids, encountering majestic wildlife, and facing the challenges of the rugged terrain. From the tranquil beauty of Moosehead Lake to the mighty Aroostook River, Steele's vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling will transport readers to a world of awe-inspiring natural wonders and heart-pounding escapades.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Survival
