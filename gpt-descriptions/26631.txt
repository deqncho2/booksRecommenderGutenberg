Synopsis: In 'A Virginia Scout' by Hugh Pendexter, a young scout named Jack embarks on a perilous journey through the untamed wilderness of Virginia during the American Revolution. As he navigates treacherous terrain and encounters both friend and foe, Jack's loyalty and courage are put to the ultimate test. With danger lurking at every turn, he must rely on his wit and survival skills to fulfill his mission and protect those he holds dear. This thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of Jack and the outcome of the war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Survival
