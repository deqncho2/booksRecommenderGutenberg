Synopsis: 
'Poems from Eastern Sources: The Steadfast Prince; and Other Poems' by Richard Chenevix Trench is a captivating collection of poems that takes readers on a journey through the enchanting world of the East. From tales of courageous princes to mesmerizing landscapes, this collection offers a glimpse into the rich tapestry of Eastern culture and folklore. Each poem is a lyrical masterpiece, filled with vivid imagery and profound emotions, leaving readers spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Poetry, Folklore, Eastern Literature

Themes: Courage, Love, Nature, Eastern Culture
