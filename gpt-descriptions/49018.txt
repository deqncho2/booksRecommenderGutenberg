Synopsis: In "The Witness of the Stars," E.W. Bullinger takes readers on a captivating journey through the celestial heavens, unveiling a hidden message that has been encoded in the stars since ancient times. Drawing from astronomy, mythology, and biblical references, Bullinger explores the profound significance of each constellation, revealing a remarkable narrative that transcends time and culture. As readers delve into the mysteries of the zodiac, they will encounter fascinating connections between the stars and the biblical narrative, leading to a deeper understanding of God's divine plan for humanity.

Genres: Astronomy, Mythology, Biblical Studies

Themes: Symbolism, Divine Revelation, Cosmic Order
