Synopsis: In 'Special Method in the Reading of Complete English Classics: In the Grades of the Common School', Charles A. McMurry presents a unique approach to teaching English literature in elementary schools. Through his innovative methods, McMurry aims to engage young minds in the captivating world of classic English literature, fostering a love for reading and a deeper understanding of the texts. This comprehensive guide offers practical strategies and lesson plans for educators to effectively introduce students to renowned works of literature, encouraging critical thinking and analytical skills. With McMurry's guidance, teachers can inspire a lifelong appreciation for the English classics in their students, making the learning experience both enjoyable and enriching.

Genres: Education, Literary Criticism

Themes: Teaching methods, Literature appreciation, Critical thinking
