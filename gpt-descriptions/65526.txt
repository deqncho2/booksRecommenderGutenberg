Synopsis: In 'Bring Back My Brain!' by Dwight V. Swain, a renowned neuroscientist wakes up one morning to find his brain missing. As he embarks on a thrilling journey to recover his stolen brain, he must navigate a world filled with eccentric characters, mind-bending puzzles, and unexpected twists. With time running out, he must unravel the mystery behind his brain's disappearance before his own identity is lost forever.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Memory, Scientific Ethics
