Synopsis: 
'Lords of Creation: Woman Suffrage Drama in Three Acts' by Ella Cheever Thayer is a captivating play that delves into the struggle for women's suffrage in the early 20th century. Set against the backdrop of a small New England town, the story follows a group of determined suffragettes as they face opposition, navigate personal relationships, and fight for their right to vote. As tensions rise and alliances are tested, the characters must confront their own beliefs and the societal norms that seek to suppress their voices. With its compelling narrative and thought-provoking themes, this play offers a powerful exploration of the fight for gender equality and the strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Feminist Literature
Themes: Women's Suffrage, Gender Equality, Activism, Personal Freedom
