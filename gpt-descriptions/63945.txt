Synopsis: In 'Poison Planet' by William Oberfield, a team of scientists embarks on a perilous mission to a distant planet, only to discover a shocking truth that threatens not only their lives but the fate of humanity itself. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter deadly creatures, they must unravel the mysteries of this toxic world before it's too late. With heart-pounding action and unexpected twists, 'Poison Planet' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Environmental degradation, Survival, Exploration
