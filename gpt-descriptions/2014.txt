Synopsis: 
In the bustling streets of London, a mysterious lodger takes up residence in a boarding house owned by the Bunting family. With his peculiar habits and secretive demeanor, the lodger soon becomes the center of attention and suspicion. As the city is gripped by a series of gruesome murders, the Bunting family begins to question whether their new lodger may be more than he appears. Tensions rise, secrets unravel, and the line between innocence and guilt becomes blurred in this gripping tale of suspense and intrigue.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Suspicion, Morality
