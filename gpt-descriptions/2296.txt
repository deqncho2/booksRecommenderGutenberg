Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Norway, a prominent businessman named Karsten Bernick is hailed as a pillar of society. However, beneath the surface of his seemingly perfect life, lies a web of secrets and deceit. As the truth begins to unravel, Karsten must confront the consequences of his actions and face the moral dilemmas that threaten to destroy his reputation and the lives of those around him.

Genres: Drama, Realism, Social Criticism

Themes: Hypocrisy, Morality, Social Responsibility, Identity
