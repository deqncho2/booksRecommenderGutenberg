Synopsis: In 'The Radio Beasts' by Ralph Milne Farley, a brilliant scientist invents a revolutionary device that can transmit thoughts through radio waves. However, when his invention falls into the wrong hands, it unleashes a terrifying force that threatens to consume humanity. As chaos ensues, a group of unlikely heroes must race against time to stop the radio beasts before they bring about the end of civilization as we know it.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Technology gone awry, the power of communication, the resilience of humanity
