Synopsis: 

In "Life and Military Career of Major-General William Tecumseh Sherman," author P. C. Headley delves into the captivating life of one of the most influential figures in American military history. From Sherman's early years as a young cadet to his rise through the ranks during the Civil War, this biography offers a compelling exploration of the man behind the legendary military strategist. With vivid descriptions of Sherman's military campaigns and personal anecdotes, Headley paints a vivid portrait of a complex and enigmatic leader. 

Genres: Biography, History, Military

Themes: Leadership, Strategy, American Civil War
