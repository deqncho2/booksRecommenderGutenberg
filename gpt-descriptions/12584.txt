Synopsis: In 'Phebe, Her Profession: A Sequel to Teddy: Her Book', we follow the captivating journey of Phebe, a young woman who finds herself navigating the complexities of life and love in the bustling city of New York. As she strives to establish herself in her chosen profession, Phebe encounters unexpected challenges, heartwarming friendships, and a romance that will test her resilience. With a cast of vibrant characters and a plot filled with twists and turns, this sequel to 'Teddy: Her Book' promises an enthralling tale of self-discovery, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Self-discovery, Love and Relationships
