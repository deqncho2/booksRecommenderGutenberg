Synopsis: In "A Lost Chapter in the History of the Steamboat," John H. B. Latrobe uncovers a forgotten tale that sheds light on the early days of steamboat travel. Through a series of intriguing events and encounters, Latrobe takes readers on a captivating journey filled with mystery, adventure, and unexpected discoveries. As he delves into the past, Latrobe uncovers a hidden narrative that challenges conventional wisdom and reveals a fascinating chapter in the history of transportation.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Innovation, Historical discovery
