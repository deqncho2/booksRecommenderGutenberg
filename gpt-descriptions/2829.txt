Synopsis:
In "Fanny and the Servant Problem," Fanny, a young and independent woman, finds herself in a peculiar situation when she hires a new servant who turns out to be quite different from what she expected. As Fanny navigates the challenges and surprises that arise from her unconventional choice, she discovers a world of hidden secrets and unexpected connections. With humor and wit, Jerome K. Jerome takes readers on a delightful journey filled with twists and turns that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres:
Comedy, Mystery, Fiction

Themes:
Identity, Social class, Relationships
