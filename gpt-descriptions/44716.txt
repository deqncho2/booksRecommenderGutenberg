Synopsis: In 'Baseball Joe, Captain of the Team; or, Bitter Struggles on the Diamond' by Chadwick, Lester, follow the thrilling journey of Joe Matson as he takes on the role of captain for his baseball team. Faced with intense competition, personal challenges, and unexpected setbacks, Joe must navigate the high-stakes world of baseball while striving to lead his team to victory. With gripping moments on and off the field, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover if Joe can overcome the bitter struggles that await him.

Genres: Sports fiction, Young adult fiction

Themes: Leadership, Perseverance, Teamwork
