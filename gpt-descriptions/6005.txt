Synopsis: In 'Celibates' by George Moore, we are introduced to a group of individuals who have chosen to live a life of celibacy, each with their own unique reasons and struggles. From a passionate artist grappling with his desires, to a devout nun questioning her faith, and a young woman navigating societal expectations, their paths intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to profound revelations and unexpected consequences. As secrets are unveiled and desires are confronted, 'Celibates' explores the complexities of human relationships and the power of personal choice.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Celibacy, Desire, Identity, Religion, Society
