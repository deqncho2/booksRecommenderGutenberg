Synopsis: 
'Village Folk-Tales of Ceylon, Volume 1' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of traditional Sri Lankan folk tales. From mystical creatures and magical encounters to tales of love, bravery, and wisdom, this collection presents a rich tapestry of stories passed down through generations. Each narrative transports readers to a different village, where they will meet fascinating characters and experience the wonders of Ceylon's folklore. With its vivid imagery and captivating storytelling, this volume offers a delightful exploration of the cultural heritage and imagination of the island.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural

Themes: Tradition, Magic, Morality, Cultural Identity
