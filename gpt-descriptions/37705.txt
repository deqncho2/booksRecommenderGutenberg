Synopsis: In 'Popery! As It Was and as It Is. Also, Auricular Confession; And Popish Nunneries', William Hogan delves into the secretive world of Catholicism, exposing the hidden practices and beliefs of the Catholic Church. Through his personal experiences as a former priest, Hogan reveals shocking revelations about the inner workings of the Church, including the controversial practice of auricular confession and the existence of popish nunneries. As he uncovers the truth behind these institutions, Hogan's account becomes a gripping exposé that will leave readers questioning the power and influence of the Catholic Church.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Critique, Exposé

Themes: Religion, Power, Secrecy, Corruption
