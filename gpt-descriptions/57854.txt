Synopsis: In "What Norman Saw in the West," young Norman embarks on a thrilling adventure as he travels to the wild and untamed American West. From encountering cowboys and Native Americans to witnessing breathtaking landscapes, Norman's journey is filled with danger, excitement, and unexpected friendships. As he navigates through this unfamiliar world, Norman's perception of the West and his own identity undergoes a profound transformation.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Cultural Encounter
