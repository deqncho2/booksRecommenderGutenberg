Synopsis: 
In 'The World English Bible (WEB): Zechariah' by Anonymous, a prophet named Zechariah is called upon to deliver messages from God to the people of Israel. As he navigates through visions and prophecies, Zechariah uncovers a hidden truth that will have profound implications for the future of his nation. With its captivating narrative and thought-provoking revelations, this book takes readers on a journey of faith, hope, and redemption.

Genres: Religious fiction, Biblical literature, Prophecy

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Divine guidance
