Synopsis: In "Wakulla: a story of adventure in Florida" by Munroe, Kirk, a young man named Ralph embarks on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of Florida. As he navigates treacherous swamps, encounters dangerous wildlife, and faces unexpected challenges, Ralph's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. Along the way, he forms unlikely alliances and discovers the secrets of the mysterious Wakulla region. This gripping tale of adventure will transport readers to a world of excitement and exploration.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Survival, Exploration
