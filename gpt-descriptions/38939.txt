Synopsis: In "The Little Colonel at Boarding-School," the spirited and lovable protagonist, Lloyd Sherman, embarks on a new adventure as she enters boarding school. Set in the late 19th century, this heartwarming tale follows Lloyd as she navigates the challenges of making new friends, facing unexpected obstacles, and discovering the true meaning of loyalty and courage. With her trademark determination and infectious spirit, Lloyd's journey at boarding school is filled with laughter, tears, and unforgettable life lessons.

Genres: Children's literature, Coming-of-age, Historical fiction

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Self-discovery
