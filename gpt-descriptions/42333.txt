Synopsis: In the small town of Cleverdale, a mysterious machine arrives, captivating the townspeople with its enigmatic purpose. As the machine's wheels turn, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the residents of Cleverdale find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and deception. With each revelation, the true nature of the machine becomes more elusive, leaving the townspeople questioning their own beliefs and the very fabric of their American lives.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town dynamics, Identity and self-discovery
