Synopsis: In 'Mate in Two Moves' by Winston K. Marks, a renowned chess grandmaster finds himself entangled in a high-stakes game of wits and strategy when he receives a mysterious invitation to a secluded island. As he delves deeper into the mind-bending puzzles and cryptic clues, he realizes that the game he thought he knew so well may hold secrets far beyond his imagination. With time running out, he must unravel the enigma before it's too late, all while questioning his own sanity and the true nature of the game itself.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Chess, Mind Games, Psychological Manipulation
