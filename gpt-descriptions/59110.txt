Synopsis: 
'Debs: His Life, Writings and Speeches, with a Department of Appreciations' is a captivating biography that delves into the extraordinary life of Eugene V. Debs, a prominent American labor leader and socialist. This comprehensive collection showcases Debs' personal writings, powerful speeches, and provides a unique perspective on his impact on the labor movement and society as a whole. From his early struggles to his rise as a charismatic leader, readers will be enthralled by Debs' unwavering dedication to social justice and his unwavering belief in the power of the working class. 

Genres: Biography, History, Political Science

Themes: Socialism, Labor Movement, Activism, Social Justice
