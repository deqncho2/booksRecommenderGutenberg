Synopsis: 

In "Practical Hand Book of Gas, Oil and Steam Engines," John B. Rathbun presents a comprehensive guide that delves into the construction, operation, and repair of various types of engines. From stationary and marine engines to farm, traction, automobile, and locomotive engines, this practical handbook covers it all. Rathbun provides detailed explanations of the different parts of engines and explores the use of different kinds of fuel, including gas burners and oil burners. With a focus on simplicity and practicality, this book is an invaluable resource for anyone interested in understanding and working with engines.

Genres: Non-fiction, Engineering, Technology

Themes: Engine construction, Engine operation, Engine repair, Fuel types and usage
