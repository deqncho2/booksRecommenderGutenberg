Synopsis: 
In "The House of Atreus; Being the Agamemnon, the Libation bearers, and the Furies," Aeschylus weaves a gripping tale of power, betrayal, and vengeance. Set in ancient Greece, the story follows the cursed House of Atreus as they navigate the aftermath of the Trojan War. As Agamemnon returns home victorious, he is met with a web of deceit and a haunting past that threatens to tear his family apart. With the weight of their ancestors' sins upon them, the characters must confront their own desires for justice and the consequences that come with it.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Revenge, Justice, Fate, Family, Power
