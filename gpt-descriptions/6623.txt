Synopsis: 
"The Coming of the Princess, and Other Poems" by Kate Seymour MacLean is a captivating collection of poems that takes readers on a journey through various emotions and experiences. From the enchanting arrival of a princess to the exploration of love, loss, and the beauty of nature, these poems offer a glimpse into the depths of human emotions and the power of imagination. Each poem is a unique and thought-provoking piece that will leave readers longing for more.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Love, Nature, Imagination, Loss, Beauty
