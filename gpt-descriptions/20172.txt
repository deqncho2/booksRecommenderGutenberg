Synopsis: 
"The Flutter of the Goldleaf, and Other Plays" is a captivating collection of plays written by Olive Tilford Dargan and Frederick Peterson. Each play within this anthology offers a unique and thought-provoking exploration of human emotions, relationships, and societal issues. From the complexities of love and desire to the struggles of social injustice and personal growth, these plays take readers on a journey through various captivating narratives. Whether it's the tale of a forbidden romance or the examination of the human psyche, this collection promises to engage and enthrall readers with its rich storytelling and compelling characters.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Injustice, Personal Growth
