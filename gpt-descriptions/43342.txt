Synopsis: 

'Round Cape Horn: Voyage of the Passenger-Ship James W. Paige, from Maine to California in the Year 1852' takes readers on an exhilarating journey across treacherous seas and uncharted territories. Join the crew and passengers aboard the James W. Paige as they navigate the dangerous waters around Cape Horn, facing storms, shipwrecks, and encounters with pirates. Experience the hardships and triumphs of a group of individuals seeking a new life in the gold rush era of California. This gripping tale of adventure and survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies beyond the horizon.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Maritime

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Human Resilience
