Synopsis: In 'The Young Alaskans on the Missouri' by Hough, Emerson, four adventurous friends find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey down the mighty Missouri River. As they navigate treacherous rapids, encounter dangerous wildlife, and face unexpected challenges, their bond is tested and their courage is pushed to the limit. With the vast wilderness of Alaska as their backdrop, the young Alaskans must rely on their resourcefulness and determination to survive and achieve their ultimate goal. 

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Action

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Exploration
