Synopsis: 
In "Butterflies Worth Knowing" by Clarence Moores Weed, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of butterflies. From the vibrant colors of the Monarch to the delicate beauty of the Swallowtail, this book unveils the secrets and wonders of these fascinating creatures. As you delve into the pages, you'll discover the intricate details of their life cycles, their remarkable adaptations, and the extraordinary places they call home. With stunning illustrations and insightful anecdotes, Weed's exploration of butterflies will leave you in awe of their extraordinary existence.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Nature, Science

Themes: 
Beauty of nature, Biodiversity, Life cycles
