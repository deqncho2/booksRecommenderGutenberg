Synopsis: In 'Plain Living: A Bush Idyll' by Rolf Boldrewood, a young man named Jack embarks on a journey to the Australian bush, seeking adventure and a fresh start. As he immerses himself in the rugged landscape and encounters a cast of colorful characters, Jack's life takes an unexpected turn. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this novel explores the challenges and triumphs of life in the untamed wilderness, where love, loyalty, and the pursuit of dreams intertwine.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Exploration of the Australian bush, Coming of age, Love and relationships, Pursuit of dreams
