Synopsis: In 'Happiness and Marriage' by Elizabeth Towne, a young woman named Emma finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires when it comes to love and marriage. As she navigates the complexities of relationships and the pursuit of happiness, Emma must confront the question of whether true fulfillment lies in conforming to tradition or forging her own path.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Love, Individuality, Societal Expectations
