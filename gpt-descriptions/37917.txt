Synopsis: In 'Across the Stream' by E. F. Benson, a young woman named Daphne finds herself inexplicably drawn to a mysterious house across the stream from her own. As she becomes more entangled in the secrets and dark history of the house, Daphne must confront her own desires and fears, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, the power of attraction and obsession, the blurred line between reality and imagination
