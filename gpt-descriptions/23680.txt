Synopsis: In 'Sex: Avoided subjects Discussed in Plain English', Stanton Henry fearlessly delves into the taboo topics surrounding human sexuality, offering a refreshingly candid and accessible exploration. With a blend of humor, research, and personal anecdotes, Henry tackles subjects that are often shrouded in secrecy, challenging societal norms and encouraging readers to embrace a more open and honest dialogue about sex. From the complexities of desire and consent to the impact of pornography and the importance of sexual education, this thought-provoking book invites readers to confront their own beliefs and assumptions, ultimately empowering them to navigate the intricacies of human sexuality with confidence and understanding.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Psychology

Themes: Sexuality, Taboos, Communication, Education
