Synopsis: In 'The Radio Boys at Mountain Pass; Or, The Midnight Call for Assistance' by Chapman, Allen, a group of adventurous young radio enthusiasts find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery when they receive a mysterious distress signal from a remote mountain pass. Determined to uncover the truth, the Radio Boys embark on a dangerous journey filled with unexpected twists and turns, as they race against time to save lives and unravel the secrets hidden within the treacherous mountain terrain.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Problem-solving
