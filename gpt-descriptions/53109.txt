Synopsis: In 'The Witch' by Mary Johnston, a young woman named Judith is accused of witchcraft in 17th century Virginia. As she fights to prove her innocence, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and forbidden love. With the threat of execution looming, Judith must navigate a treacherous world where superstition and fear reign, and uncover the truth that could save her life.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Witchcraft, Innocence, Betrayal, Forbidden Love, Justice
