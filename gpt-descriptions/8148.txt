Synopsis: 
In 'Youth and Egolatry' by Pío Baroja, we follow the life of Andrés Hurtado, a young man who is determined to escape the confines of his small town and make a name for himself in the world. Fueled by his ambition and desire for recognition, Andrés embarks on a journey filled with adventure, love, and self-discovery. As he navigates through various social circles and encounters a colorful cast of characters, Andrés grapples with the complexities of youth, the pursuit of success, and the sacrifices one must make to achieve their dreams.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Adventure, Romance

Themes: 
Ambition, Identity, Self-discovery
