Synopsis: In 'Storm Cloud on Deka' by Smith, E. E., a small, remote planet becomes the battleground for an epic conflict between two powerful alien races. As the inhabitants of Deka find themselves caught in the crossfire, a young and determined scientist must uncover the secrets of an ancient artifact that holds the key to their survival. With the fate of their world hanging in the balance, the protagonist embarks on a thrilling journey filled with danger, discovery, and unexpected alliances.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Space Opera

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Interstellar Conflict, Ancient Mysteries
