Synopsis: 
'Chaucerian and Other Pieces: Being a Supplement to the Complete Works of Geoffrey Chaucer' takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of lesser-known works by the renowned poet Geoffrey Chaucer. This compilation includes a variety of intriguing pieces, each offering a unique glimpse into Chaucer's literary genius. From humorous tales to thought-provoking allegories, these writings showcase Chaucer's versatility and mastery of storytelling. Delve into this treasure trove of hidden gems and discover a new side to the beloved poet.

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Satire

Themes: Human nature, Social commentary, Morality
