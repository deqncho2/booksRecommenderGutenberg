Synopsis: In 'Jill: A Flower Girl' by L. T. Meade, a young girl named Jill finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her grandmother's garden. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Jill must navigate through unexpected twists and turns, all while discovering the power of friendship and the importance of trust. With each step she takes, Jill uncovers secrets that will change her life forever, leading her on a thrilling adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Trust, Discovery
