Synopsis: In 'A Terrible Secret: A Novel' by Fleming, May Agnes, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and deceit when she discovers a shocking secret about her family. As she delves deeper into the dark secrets of her past, Evelyn must navigate through a dangerous world filled with betrayal, love, and unexpected twists. With each revelation, the stakes become higher, and Evelyn's life is forever changed. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Love and relationships
