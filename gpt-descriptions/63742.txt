Synopsis: In 'Sixty-Year Extension' by Alan Edward Nourse, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of human existence. As he struggles to navigate the ethical implications of his invention, he finds himself entangled in a web of corporate greed, government control, and personal sacrifice. With time running out, he must make a choice that will determine the fate of humanity. This gripping tale explores the boundaries of scientific progress and the moral dilemmas it presents.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Dystopian

Themes: Ethics, Power, Human Nature
