Synopsis: In 'Curlie Carson Listens In' by Roy J. Snell, young Curlie Carson stumbles upon a mysterious radio message that leads him on an unexpected adventure. As he delves deeper into the secret world of espionage, Curlie must use his wit and resourcefulness to navigate dangerous situations and uncover the truth. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise, and Curlie's courage is put to the test. Will he be able to outsmart the enemy and save the day?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Espionage, Courage, Resourcefulness
