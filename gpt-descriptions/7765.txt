Synopsis: In the gripping play "Night Must Fall: a Play in Three Acts" by Emlyn Williams, a charming and enigmatic young man named Dan arrives at a remote country house, bringing with him an air of mystery and intrigue. As the story unfolds, the inhabitants of the house become increasingly captivated by Dan's charisma, unaware of the dark secrets he hides beneath his charming facade. Tensions rise, suspicions grow, and the audience is left on the edge of their seats, eagerly awaiting the shocking truth that lies at the heart of this thrilling tale.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Manipulation, Morality
