Synopsis: In 'The Cosmic Snare' by Stephen Marlowe, a renowned astrophysicist stumbles upon a mysterious cosmic phenomenon that threatens to unravel the fabric of the universe. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and mind-bending discoveries. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous journey across space and time to unlock the secrets of the cosmic snare before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Existentialism, the nature of reality, the power of knowledge
