Synopsis: In "Common Sense for Housemaids" by Ann Fraser Tytler, a young housemaid named Mary finds herself caught in a web of secrets and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden diary. As she delves deeper into its pages, Mary uncovers shocking revelations that challenge her perception of the world she thought she knew. With danger lurking around every corner, Mary must navigate a treacherous path to protect herself and those she holds dear. This gripping tale of mystery and suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery
