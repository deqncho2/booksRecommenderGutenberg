Synopsis: In 'The Rider in Khaki: A Novel' by Gould, Nat, a mysterious figure clad in khaki emerges in a small town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic presence. As the townspeople become increasingly intrigued by the rider's unconventional lifestyle and his uncanny ability to connect with horses, they are drawn into a web of secrets and hidden pasts. With each encounter, the rider's true identity becomes more elusive, leaving the townsfolk questioning their own perceptions of reality. As tensions rise and the truth slowly unravels, the community must confront their own fears and desires, ultimately leading to a shocking revelation that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Mystery, Western, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Perception, Secrets, Redemption
