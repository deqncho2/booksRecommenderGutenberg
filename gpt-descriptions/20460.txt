Synopsis: In 'The Citizen-Soldier: or, Memoirs of a Volunteer' by John Beatty, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the eyes of a volunteer soldier. Set during the American Civil War, the story follows the protagonist as he navigates the harsh realities of war, forming deep bonds with his fellow soldiers and experiencing the transformative power of camaraderie. From the chaos of the battlefield to the quiet moments of reflection, this memoir offers a poignant and gripping account of one man's journey through the crucible of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Literature, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Identity, Coming of Age
