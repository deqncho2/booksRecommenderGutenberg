Synopsis: In 'Change Signals: A Story of the New Football' by Ralph Henry Barbour, a young football player finds himself caught in the midst of a revolution in the sport. As the game evolves and new strategies emerge, he must navigate the changing dynamics of the team, the pressures of success, and the unexpected challenges that arise. With friendships tested and rivalries intensified, the protagonist must adapt to the shifting landscape of football and discover what truly matters on and off the field.

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Adaptation, Friendship, Competition, Identity
