Synopsis: In 'Gudrid the Fair: A Tale of the Discovery of America', Maurice Hewlett takes readers on a captivating journey through the Viking Age, following the remarkable life of Gudrid, a young woman with a spirit as fierce as the seas she sails. From her humble beginnings in Iceland to her adventures across the Atlantic, Gudrid's story is one of courage, love, and the pursuit of new horizons. As she encounters both friend and foe, Gudrid's determination to carve her own path in a world dominated by men becomes an inspiration to all who cross her path.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Gender roles, Exploration, Resilience
