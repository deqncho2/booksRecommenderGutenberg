Synopsis: 
In 'General Bounce; Or, The Lady and the Locusts' by Whyte-Melville, G. J., readers are transported to the vibrant world of horse racing, where the charismatic General Bounce takes center stage. This thrilling tale follows the General's adventures as he navigates the treacherous world of gambling, love, and high society. As the Lady and the Locusts weave their way into the General's life, readers are left on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the twists and turns that lie ahead.

Genres: 
Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Gambling, Love, Society
