Synopsis: 
In 'Drowsy' by Mitchell, John Ames, a young woman named Lily finds herself trapped in a mysterious and haunting dream world. As she navigates through this surreal realm, she encounters enigmatic characters and uncovers dark secrets that threaten to consume her. With each passing moment, Lily's grip on reality becomes more tenuous, blurring the lines between dreams and waking life. As she fights to escape this nightmarish dimension, she must confront her deepest fears and unravel the truth behind her entrapment.

Genres: 
Psychological thriller, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: 
Reality vs. illusion, Identity, Fear
