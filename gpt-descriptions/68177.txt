Synopsis: 
'Among unknown Eskimo: An account of twelve years intimate relations with the primitive Eskimo of ice-bound Baffin Land, with a description of their ways of living, hunting customs & beliefs' takes readers on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of the Eskimo people living in the remote Baffin Land. Julian W. Bilby shares his extraordinary experiences and deep connections with these primitive Eskimo, providing a vivid description of their unique way of life, their hunting customs, and their fascinating beliefs. Through his immersive storytelling, Bilby offers readers a rare glimpse into a culture that remains largely unknown to the outside world.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Anthropology, Adventure

Themes: 
Cultural exploration, Human connection, Survival in extreme environments
