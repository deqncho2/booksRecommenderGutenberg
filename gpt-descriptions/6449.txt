Synopsis: In 'Little Journeys to the Homes of the Great - Volume 09: Little Journeys to the Homes of Great Reformers', Elbert Hubbard takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and homes of influential reformers. From the humble abode of Martin Luther to the serene sanctuary of John Knox, Hubbard delves into the personal spaces that shaped the lives of these great individuals. With vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, the author paints a vivid picture of the environments that nurtured the ideas and convictions of these reformers, offering readers a unique glimpse into their world.

Genres: Biography, History, Travelogue

Themes: Reform, Influence, Personal Spaces
