Synopsis: 

'U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1975 January - June' by Library of Congress. Copyright Office is a captivating compilation that delves into the world of intellectual property and creative works. This meticulously curated collection showcases the copyright renewals of various artistic endeavors, offering a fascinating glimpse into the creative landscape of the mid-1970s. From literature to music, from film to photography, this book unveils a treasure trove of renewed copyrights, hinting at the diverse and vibrant artistic expressions of the time.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Intellectual property, Creative expression, Artistic diversity
