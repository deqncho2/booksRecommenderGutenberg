Synopsis: 

In 'Ancient Armour and Weapons in Europe: From the Iron Period of the Northern Nations to the End of the Thirteenth Century' by John Hewitt, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the evolution of armor and weaponry in Europe. From the fierce battles of the Iron Period to the medieval era, this book delves into the intricate details of the various types of armor and weapons used by warriors throughout history. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Hewitt brings to life the craftsmanship and ingenuity behind these ancient artifacts, shedding light on the warriors who wore them and the battles they fought. 

Genres: History, Art, Military

Themes: Evolution of weaponry, Historical warfare, Artistic craftsmanship
