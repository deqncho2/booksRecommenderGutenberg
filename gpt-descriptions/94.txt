Synopsis: 
Alexander's Bridge is a captivating novel that follows the life of Bartley Alexander, a renowned engineer who finds himself torn between duty and desire. As he navigates the complexities of his personal and professional life, Bartley is forced to confront the consequences of his choices and the fragility of human relationships. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century America, this gripping tale explores the depths of passion, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Ambition and success, Moral dilemmas
