Synopsis: In 'Our Little Porto Rican Cousin', readers are transported to the vibrant island of Puerto Rico, where they meet young Juanita and follow her adventures as she navigates the unique culture and traditions of her homeland. From exploring the bustling streets of San Juan to experiencing the excitement of a traditional fiesta, Juanita's journey offers a captivating glimpse into the rich tapestry of Puerto Rican life. As she encounters new friends and faces unexpected challenges, Juanita's resilience and determination shine through, making for a heartwarming and enlightening tale.

Genres: Children's literature, Cultural fiction, Adventure

Themes: Cultural identity, Family bonds, Resilience, Exploration of traditions
