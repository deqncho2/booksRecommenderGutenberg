Synopsis: 
In 'The Commune of London, and other studies', John Horace Round takes us on a captivating journey through the fascinating history of London. From the tumultuous events of the Commune of London to the lesser-known stories of the city's past, Round weaves together a tapestry of intrigue, rebellion, and resilience. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, he uncovers the hidden secrets and untold tales that have shaped the city we know today. Prepare to be enthralled as Round delves into the lives of both ordinary citizens and influential figures, shedding light on the complex dynamics that have shaped London's history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Social history

Themes: Rebellion, Urban history, Power dynamics
