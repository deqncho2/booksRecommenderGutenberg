Synopsis: In 'The Girls of Central High on the Stage; Or, The Play That Took The Prize', the talented girls of Central High School are thrilled to be chosen to perform in a prestigious play competition. As they prepare for their big moment, they face unexpected challenges, rivalries, and personal struggles that test their friendships and determination. With their dreams on the line, the girls must come together and find the strength to shine on stage and prove that they are worthy of the prize.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Ambition, Overcoming Challenges
