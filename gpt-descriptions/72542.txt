Synopsis: 
'Behind the Scenes in Peking: Being Experiences during the Siege of the Legations' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events of the siege of the Legations in Peking. Mary Hooker, an eyewitness to the siege, provides a gripping account of the harrowing experiences, the bravery, and the resilience of those trapped within the Legations. As the chaos unfolds, Hooker's narrative offers a unique perspective on the behind-the-scenes struggles, the diplomatic negotiations, and the extraordinary acts of heroism that unfolded during this historic event.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, War

Themes: Survival, Bravery, Diplomacy, Human resilience
