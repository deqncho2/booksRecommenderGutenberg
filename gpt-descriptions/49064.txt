Synopsis: 
In 'Inventing for Boys' by Collins, A. Frederick, young inventor Archie Frederick takes readers on an exhilarating journey through his imaginative mind. Filled with captivating stories and practical advice, this book unveils a world of endless possibilities, where creativity and innovation know no bounds. From building extraordinary gadgets to solving everyday problems, Archie's passion for invention inspires young readers to embrace their own ingenuity and explore the thrilling world of inventing.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Non-fiction, Self-help

Themes: 
Creativity, Innovation, Problem-solving
