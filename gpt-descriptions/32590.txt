Synopsis: In 'The Old Martians' by Phillips, Rog, a group of astronauts embarks on a daring mission to explore the mysterious red planet. However, upon arrival, they discover a shocking secret that challenges everything they thought they knew about Mars. As they delve deeper into the planet's secrets, they must confront their own fears and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of humanity. This gripping sci-fi thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of human exploration and the consequences of our actions.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Morality, Consequences
