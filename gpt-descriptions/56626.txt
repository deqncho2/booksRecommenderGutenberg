Synopsis: In 'Johnny Nelson: How a one-time pupil of Hopalong Cassidy of the famous Bar-20 ranch in the Pecos Valley performed an act of knight-errantry and what came of it' by Clarence Edward Mulford, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure with Johnny Nelson, a former student of the legendary Hopalong Cassidy. When Johnny finds himself in a dangerous situation, he must summon his courage and skills to perform an act of chivalry that sets off a chain of events with unforeseen consequences. This action-packed tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome of Johnny's heroic act.

Genres: Western, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Chivalry
