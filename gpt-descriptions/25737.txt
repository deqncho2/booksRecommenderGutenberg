Synopsis: In 'Concordance: A Terran Empire concordance' by Wilson, Ann, readers are transported to a future where the Terran Empire reigns supreme. As tensions rise between the empire and its colonies, a young linguist named Emma discovers a hidden message that could change the course of history. With the fate of the empire hanging in the balance, Emma embarks on a thrilling journey to decipher the cryptic message and uncover the truth behind the empire's secrets. Filled with intrigue, political maneuvering, and unexpected alliances, this gripping sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power struggles, Language and communication, Secrets and hidden truths
