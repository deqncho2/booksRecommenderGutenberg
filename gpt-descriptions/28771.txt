Synopsis: 
'Child Maidelvold, and Other Ballads' is a captivating collection of haunting ballads that transport readers to a world of enchantment and mystery. From the tragic tale of a young girl's forbidden love to the haunting melody of a lost soul seeking redemption, each ballad weaves a spellbinding narrative that will leave readers spellbound. As the stories unfold, secrets are revealed, destinies are tested, and the power of love and sacrifice is explored in all its forms. Prepare to be transported to a realm where the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, and where the echoes of these timeless ballads will linger long after the final page is turned.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: Love, Sacrifice, Fate, Redemption
