Synopsis:
In the gripping novel 'Simon' by J. Storer Clouston, a mysterious and enigmatic character named Simon enters the lives of the residents of a small English village. With his uncanny ability to predict the future and his unsettling presence, Simon becomes both a source of fascination and fear for the villagers. As they try to unravel the secrets behind Simon's powers, they are drawn into a web of intrigue and danger that will test their beliefs and challenge their understanding of reality. With each twist and turn, the true nature of Simon's intentions becomes increasingly unclear, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat until the final revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fate and destiny, Perception of reality, Power and manipulation
