Synopsis: In 'The Pool of Stars' by Cornelia Meigs, a group of adventurous young friends stumble upon a hidden treasure map that leads them on a thrilling journey through mysterious caves and dangerous encounters. As they navigate through the unknown, they must rely on their wit, courage, and friendship to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. With each step closer to the pool of stars, they uncover secrets that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Self-discovery
