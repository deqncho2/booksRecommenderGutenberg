Synopsis: In 'Number 70, Berlin: A Story of Britain's Peril', Le Queux takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dangerous streets of Berlin. As the protagonist finds himself entangled in a web of espionage and intrigue, he must navigate the treacherous world of international politics to uncover a sinister plot that threatens Britain's security. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Espionage, Mystery

Themes: International intrigue, National security, Betrayal
