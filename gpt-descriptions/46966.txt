Synopsis: In "Adam Hepburn's Vow: A Tale of Kirk and Covenant" by Annie S. Swan, readers are transported to 17th century Scotland, where the protagonist, Adam Hepburn, finds himself torn between his loyalty to the Kirk (the Scottish church) and his commitment to the Covenant (a religious and political movement). As he navigates the treacherous landscape of religious persecution and political unrest, Adam's unwavering determination to uphold his vow leads him on a journey filled with danger, love, and sacrifice. Will he be able to reconcile his conflicting loyalties and find peace in a world torn apart by religious strife?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Faith, Persecution, Love, Sacrifice
