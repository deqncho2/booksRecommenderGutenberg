Synopsis: 
Toby Tyler, a young orphan, finds himself longing for adventure and a sense of belonging. When he joins a traveling circus, he embarks on a thrilling journey filled with colorful characters, daring acts, and unexpected challenges. As Toby navigates the ups and downs of circus life, he discovers the true meaning of family, friendship, and self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Children's literature

Themes: Adventure, Friendship, Belonging, Self-discovery
