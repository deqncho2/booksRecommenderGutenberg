Synopsis: In 'The Belgians to the Front' by Fiske, James, a group of ordinary Belgian citizens find themselves thrust into the chaos of World War I. As they navigate the horrors of war, they must confront their own fears, forge unlikely alliances, and discover the true strength of the human spirit. With gripping storytelling and vivid descriptions, this novel takes readers on a heart-pounding journey through the trenches of war, where bravery and sacrifice become the ultimate test of character.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Sacrifice, Humanity
