Synopsis: In 'The Judgement of Valhalla' by Frankau, Gilbert, a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the key to a long-lost Viking legend. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and a race against time to uncover the truth before it falls into the wrong hands. With each revelation, the line between myth and reality blurs, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Mythology, Identity, Betrayal
