Synopsis: In "Some Say"; Neighbours in Cyrus, a small town in the heart of the American Midwest, secrets and mysteries intertwine as the lives of its residents become unexpectedly entangled. From the enigmatic newcomer who stirs up curiosity to the long-buried secrets that threaten to unravel the community, this captivating tale explores the complexities of human relationships and the power of the past to shape the present. As the bonds between neighbours are tested and unexpected alliances are formed, the town of Cyrus becomes a microcosm of the universal struggle for connection and understanding.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Community and belonging, Redemption and forgiveness
