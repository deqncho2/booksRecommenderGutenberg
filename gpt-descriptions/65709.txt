Synopsis: In 'The House of the Secret (La maison des hommes vivants)' by Farrère, Claude, a young man named André finds himself drawn to a mysterious house that holds a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the house's history, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where nothing is as it seems. With each discovery, André's life is forever changed, and he must confront the truth that lies within the walls of the house.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
