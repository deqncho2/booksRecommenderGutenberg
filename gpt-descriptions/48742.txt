Synopsis: 

In 'The Missionary: An Indian Tale; vol. I' by Morgan, Lady (Sydney), readers are transported to the vibrant and exotic world of colonial India. The story follows the journey of a young and spirited Englishwoman named Olivia, who embarks on a life-changing adventure as a missionary in the heart of India. As Olivia immerses herself in the local culture and forms unexpected connections with the people she encounters, she finds herself torn between her duty as a missionary and her growing empathy for the indigenous population. Amidst the backdrop of political unrest and cultural clashes, Olivia's beliefs and convictions are put to the test, leading her on a path of self-discovery and transformation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Cultural identity, Colonialism, Religion, Personal growth
