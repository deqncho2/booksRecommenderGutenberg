Synopsis: In 'The Corner House Girls on Palm Island' by Grace Brooks Hill, a group of adventurous young girls embark on a thrilling journey to Palm Island. As they explore the island's hidden secrets and encounter unexpected challenges, they must rely on their wit and courage to overcome obstacles and solve a mysterious puzzle. With each twist and turn, the girls uncover the island's rich history and forge lifelong friendships. Will they unravel the island's secrets before it's too late? Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Friendship. Themes: Exploration, Courage, Friendship.
