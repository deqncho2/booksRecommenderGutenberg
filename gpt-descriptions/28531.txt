Synopsis: In 'The Banner Boy Scouts Snowbound; or, A Tour on Skates and Iceboats' by George A. Warren, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts find themselves in an unexpected winter adventure. When a sudden snowstorm traps them in a remote cabin, they must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to survive. As they navigate the frozen landscape on skates and iceboats, they encounter thrilling challenges and unexpected discoveries. Will they be able to overcome the obstacles and make it back home safely?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Action

Themes: Survival, Teamwork, Resourcefulness
