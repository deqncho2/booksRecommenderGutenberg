Synopsis: 

In 'U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1976 July - December', the Library of Congress Copyright Office takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of intellectual property. This compilation unveils a treasure trove of renewed copyrights, revealing the creative endeavors and cultural milestones that shaped the latter half of 1976. From literature to music, film to art, this collection offers a fascinating glimpse into the diverse and ever-evolving landscape of American creativity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Intellectual property, Creativity, Cultural heritage
