Synopsis: In 'The Oxford Circus: A Novel of Oxford and Youth', Miles and Hamish take readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant streets of Oxford, where the lives of a group of young individuals intertwine in unexpected ways. As they navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and self-discovery, secrets are unveiled, dreams are shattered, and the true essence of youth is explored. With its richly developed characters and evocative setting, this novel offers a poignant exploration of the transformative power of youth and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Contemporary Fiction, Romance

Themes: Youth, Identity, Friendship, Love, Ambition
