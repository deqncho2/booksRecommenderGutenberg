Synopsis: In 'Le Petit Nord: or, Annals of a Labrador Harbour', Anne Grenfell and Katie Spalding take us on a captivating journey to a remote Labrador harbour. Through vivid storytelling, they introduce us to the resilient inhabitants of this harsh yet beautiful landscape, sharing their triumphs, struggles, and the profound impact they have on one another. As we delve into the lives of these remarkable individuals, we witness the power of community, the resilience of the human spirit, and the transformative nature of compassion. This poignant and inspiring tale will transport readers to a world they won't soon forget.

Genres: Memoir, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Community, Resilience, Compassion
