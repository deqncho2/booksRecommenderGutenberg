Synopsis: 
In 'Citizen Bird: Scenes from Bird-Life in Plain English for Beginners', authors Mabel Osgood Wright and Elliott Coues take readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of birds. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, they introduce readers to various bird species, their behaviors, habitats, and the challenges they face. From the majestic eagle to the tiny hummingbird, the authors provide a delightful and accessible exploration of these feathered creatures, making it a perfect read for bird enthusiasts and beginners alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Science

Themes: Wildlife appreciation, Environmental conservation
