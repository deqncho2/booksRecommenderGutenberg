Synopsis: In 'A Likely Story' by William De Morgan, a young man named Dick is unexpectedly thrust into a world of adventure and mystery when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. With the help of a quirky group of companions, including a talking parrot and a mischievous monkey, Dick embarks on a thrilling journey filled with danger, deception, and unexpected twists. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter eccentric characters, the true nature of the treasure and its significance gradually unfolds, leading to a climactic showdown that will test their courage and loyalty. Will Dick and his companions be able to outwit their adversaries and claim the treasure, or will they fall victim to the perils that lie in wait?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Treasure hunting, Friendship, Loyalty
