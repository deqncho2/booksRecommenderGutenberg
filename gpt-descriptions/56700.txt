Synopsis: 

"Mormon" Doctrine Plain and Simple; Or, Leaves from the Tree of Life takes readers on a captivating journey through the fundamental teachings and beliefs of the Mormon faith. Written by Charles W. Penrose, this enlightening work provides a comprehensive and accessible exploration of the principles that form the bedrock of the Mormon religion. Through engaging anecdotes, thought-provoking insights, and a clear and concise writing style, Penrose invites readers to delve into the depths of Mormon doctrine, shedding light on its core tenets and shedding misconceptions along the way. 

Genres: Religious, Non-fiction

Themes: Mormonism, Faith, Salvation, Religious Doctrine
