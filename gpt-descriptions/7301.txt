Synopsis: In "Nathaniel Hawthorne" by George Edward Woodberry, delve into the life and mind of one of America's most enigmatic and influential writers. This captivating biography takes readers on a journey through Hawthorne's formative years, his struggles with personal demons, and his remarkable literary achievements. From his haunting tales of sin and guilt to his exploration of the human psyche, Woodberry's insightful narrative unveils the complexities of Hawthorne's life and art, leaving readers eager to discover the secrets behind his timeless works.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Sin and guilt, Human psychology, Literary exploration
