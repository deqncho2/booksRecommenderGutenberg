Synopsis: 

'Our Show' is a humorous and entertaining account of the International Exposition held in honor of the Centennial Anniversary of American Independence. From the inception to completion of the event, the book takes readers on a delightful journey through the description of buildings, biographies of managers, receptions of foreign dignitaries, opening ceremonies, a captivating poem, an inspiring oration, and an amusing survey of all departments. Filled with incidents and anecdotes, this book offers a unique perspective on the grand exhibition that celebrated America's 100 years of independence.

Genres: Humor, History, Non-fiction

Themes: American Independence, International Expositions, Cultural Celebrations
