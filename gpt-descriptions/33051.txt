Synopsis: In "Momotaro; or, Little Peachling: Japanese Fairy Tale Series No. 1," we are transported to ancient Japan where a childless couple discovers a miraculous peach floating down the river. To their astonishment, a little boy emerges from the fruit, and they name him Momotaro. As Momotaro grows up, he embarks on a perilous journey to confront a band of menacing ogres who have been terrorizing the land. With the help of his loyal animal companions, Momotaro faces numerous challenges and discovers the true power of courage, friendship, and determination.

Genres: Fairy Tale, Adventure, Folklore

Themes: Bravery, Friendship, Good vs. Evil
