Synopsis: In the small town of Simsbury, Ma Pettengill is a force to be reckoned with. Widowed and fiercely independent, she runs her own boarding house and takes on the role of the town's unofficial mayor. With her sharp wit and no-nonsense attitude, Ma navigates the ups and downs of small-town life, all while dealing with a colorful cast of characters who come through her doors. From love affairs to secret schemes, Ma's world is never dull, and she always manages to come out on top.

Genres: Fiction, Humor, Drama

Themes: Independence, Community, Resilience
