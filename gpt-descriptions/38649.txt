Synopsis: 
'Early Days in North Queensland' by Edward Palmer takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of North Queensland in the late 19th century. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Palmer recounts his experiences as a pioneer in this rugged and remote region, where danger lurked at every turn. From encounters with indigenous tribes to treacherous encounters with wildlife, Palmer's memoir offers a thrilling and immersive glimpse into the challenges and triumphs of life in the Australian frontier.

Genres: Memoir, Adventure, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural encounters
