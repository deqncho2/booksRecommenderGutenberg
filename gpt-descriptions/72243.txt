Synopsis: 

In the quaint Scottish village of Blinkbonny, a captivating tale unfolds between the years 1841 and 1851. Strathesk, the author, takes us on a journey through the lives of the villagers, delving into their joys, sorrows, and the intricate web of relationships that bind them together. From the charming Bell o' the Manse to the colorful characters that populate the village, this book offers a glimpse into the rich tapestry of Scottish village life during a transformative decade. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Community, Love and relationships, Social change
