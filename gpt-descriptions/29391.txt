Synopsis: In 'Blue-grass and Broadway' by Maria Thompson Daviess, a young woman from a small Kentucky town finds herself thrust into the glamorous world of Broadway when she unexpectedly inherits a fortune. As she navigates the dazzling lights and fast-paced lifestyle of New York City, she must confront the challenges of love, ambition, and staying true to her roots. Will she find her place in the bustling city or will she long for the simplicity of her blue-grass home?

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Pursuit of Dreams, Cultural Clash
