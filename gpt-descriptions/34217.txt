Synopsis: 

"The Life and Public Services of James A. Garfield" is a captivating biography that delves into the remarkable journey of James A. Garfield, the twentieth President of the United States. Emma Elizabeth Brown provides readers with a comprehensive account of Garfield's eventful administration, his tragic assassination, and the gripping details of his last hours and death. Through notable extracts from his speeches and letters, readers gain insight into the brilliant mind and eloquent words of this influential leader. 

Genres: Biography, History, Politics

Themes: Leadership, Tragedy, Legacy
