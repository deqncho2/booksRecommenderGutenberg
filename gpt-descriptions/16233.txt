Synopsis: In 'An Essay towards Fixing the True Standards of Wit, Humour, Railery, Satire, and Ridicule', Morris Corbyn takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the intricacies of wit and humor. Through a series of insightful observations and analyses, Corbyn explores the delicate balance between satire and ridicule, challenging conventional notions and offering a fresh perspective on the art of comedy. With wit and intelligence, the author delves into the essence of humor, unraveling its various forms and shedding light on its true standards.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, Essay

Themes: Wit, Humor, Satire, Ridicule, Artistic Standards
