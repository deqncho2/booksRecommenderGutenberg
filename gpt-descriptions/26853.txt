Synopsis:
Vice Versa; or, A Lesson to Fathers by Anstey, F. is a delightful and humorous tale that follows the misadventures of a father and son who magically switch bodies. When the strict and workaholic Mr. Bultitude suddenly finds himself trapped in the body of his mischievous and carefree son, Dick, chaos ensues. As they navigate their new roles, both father and son learn valuable lessons about the importance of understanding, empathy, and the true meaning of family. With hilarious situations and unexpected twists, this novel takes readers on a whimsical journey that will keep them eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: 
Satire, Comedy, Fantasy

Themes:
Family dynamics, Role reversal, Personal growth
