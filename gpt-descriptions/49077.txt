Synopsis: 
In 'The Art of Horse-Shoeing: A Manual for Farriers', author William Hunting takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of horse-shoeing, revealing the intricate techniques and skills required to master this ancient craft. Through vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, Hunting explores the artistry and science behind the proper care and shoeing of horses, offering invaluable insights and practical advice for both novice and experienced farriers. From the selection of materials to the correct positioning of the shoe, this manual delves into every aspect of the process, providing a comprehensive guide that will leave readers enthralled by the beauty and precision of this age-old practice.

Genres: Non-fiction, How-to, Reference

Themes: Craftsmanship, Animal Care, Tradition
