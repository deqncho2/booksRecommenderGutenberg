Synopsis: 
'Love-Songs of Childhood' by Field, Eugene is a collection of enchanting poems that transport readers back to the innocence and wonder of childhood. Through vivid imagery and lyrical verses, the author explores the joys, dreams, and tender emotions experienced during this magical phase of life. From the whimsical adventures of imaginary friends to the bittersweet nostalgia of growing up, these love-songs capture the essence of childhood in all its beauty and complexity.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: Childhood, Innocence, Imagination, Nostalgia
