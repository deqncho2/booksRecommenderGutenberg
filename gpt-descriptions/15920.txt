Synopsis: 
'Outward Bound Or, Young America Afloat: A Story of Travel and Adventure' follows the thrilling journey of a group of young friends who embark on a grand sailing adventure. As they set sail on a magnificent yacht, they encounter unexpected challenges and face the test of their courage and resourcefulness. From treacherous storms to encounters with pirates, the young adventurers must rely on their wits and teamwork to navigate through perilous waters. This captivating tale of travel and adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies ahead for these brave young souls.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resilience, Coming of Age
