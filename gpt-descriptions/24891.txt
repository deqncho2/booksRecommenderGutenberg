Synopsis: 
In "Stray Leaves from an Arctic Journal: Or, Eighteen Months in the Polar Regions, in Search of Sir John Franklin's Expedition, in the Years 1850-51," Sherard Osborn takes readers on a thrilling and perilous journey to the Arctic in search of the lost expedition of Sir John Franklin. Through vivid descriptions and harrowing encounters with the treacherous Arctic environment, Osborn provides a firsthand account of the challenges faced by explorers in their quest for discovery. As the expedition unfolds, unexpected discoveries and encounters with indigenous peoples add layers of intrigue and mystery to the narrative. Will Osborn and his team uncover the fate of Franklin's expedition, or will they succumb to the unforgiving Arctic wilderness?

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Exploration

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Survival in extreme conditions, Cultural encounters
