Synopsis: 
In 'The Poetaster' by Ben Jonson, a young aspiring poet finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and rivalry as he seeks to make a name for himself in the literary world of ancient Rome. As he navigates the treacherous waters of jealousy, manipulation, and artistic integrity, he must confront the consequences of his actions and decide what truly matters to him. With witty dialogue, unexpected twists, and a cast of colorful characters, 'The Poetaster' is a captivating tale that explores the complexities of ambition and the power of words.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Artistic Integrity, Jealousy, Manipulation
