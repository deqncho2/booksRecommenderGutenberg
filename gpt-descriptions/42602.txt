Synopsis: In "The Steam Engine Explained and Illustrated," Dionysius Lardner delves into the fascinating world of steam engines, providing a comprehensive account of their invention, progressive improvement, and various applications in navigation and railways. This seventh edition also includes a memoir of James Watt, the renowned Scottish inventor and engineer. Through detailed explanations and illustrations, Lardner offers readers a captivating exploration of the steam engine's mechanics, its impact on transportation, and the remarkable achievements of Watt. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Engineering, History

Themes: Invention, Technological Progress, Transportation
