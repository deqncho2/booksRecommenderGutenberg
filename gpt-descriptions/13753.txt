Synopsis: 
In "Dorothy Dainty's Gay Times" by Amy Brooks, follow the delightful adventures of young Dorothy Dainty as she embarks on a series of whimsical escapades. From attending a grand ball to exploring a mysterious island, Dorothy's life is filled with excitement, laughter, and unexpected surprises. With her infectious spirit and boundless curiosity, Dorothy's gay times are sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Adventure, Friendship, Imagination
