Synopsis: In 'The Voyage of The First Hessian Army from Portsmouth to New York, 1776', Pfister and Seume take us on a captivating journey across the Atlantic during the American Revolutionary War. Through vivid descriptions and personal accounts, we witness the trials and tribulations faced by the Hessian soldiers as they embark on a perilous voyage to join the British forces in America. From treacherous storms to encounters with enemy ships, this gripping narrative explores the challenges and sacrifices made by these soldiers in their quest for victory.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War literature, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Survival, Exploration
