Synopsis: In 'Mountain Moggy: The Stoning of the Witch' by William Henry Giles Kingston, a small village is plagued by fear and superstition when a mysterious woman arrives, accused of being a witch. As tensions rise and the villagers turn against her, a young boy named Moggy finds himself caught in the middle, torn between loyalty to his community and his growing friendship with the accused. With secrets and betrayal lurking in every corner, Moggy must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and save the woman from a horrifying fate.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Fear and superstition, Loyalty and betrayal, Friendship and compassion
