Synopsis: In 'The Princess Aline' by Davis, Richard Harding, a young American journalist finds himself captivated by the enigmatic Princess Aline of Hohenwald. As he delves deeper into her world, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the pursuit of personal freedom. With the fate of a nation hanging in the balance, the journalist must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the truth and protect the woman who has stolen his heart.

Genres: Romance, Political Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Identity and Freedom, Power and Politics
