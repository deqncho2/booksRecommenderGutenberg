Synopsis: In the gripping tragedy of 'Othello' by William Shakespeare, a respected military general finds himself entangled in a web of jealousy, manipulation, and deceit. As Othello's world unravels, the play explores the destructive power of suspicion and the fragility of trust, leading to a heart-wrenching climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Jealousy, Betrayal, Racism, Manipulation, Trust
