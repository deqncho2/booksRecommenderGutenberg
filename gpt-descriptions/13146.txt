Synopsis: In 'The Beauty and the Bolshevist' by Alice Duer Miller, a captivating tale unfolds as a young American woman finds herself entangled in a whirlwind romance with a mysterious Russian revolutionary. Set against the backdrop of the tumultuous Russian Revolution, their love story is filled with passion, danger, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As their worlds collide, the protagonist must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and sacrifice, ultimately questioning her own beliefs and values. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Political Ideologies, Identity and Belonging
