Synopsis: In 'The Four Feathers' by A.E.W. Mason, a young British officer named Harry Feversham finds himself facing a life-altering decision when he receives four white feathers, a symbol of cowardice. Determined to prove his bravery, Harry embarks on a journey that takes him to the heart of danger and tests his loyalty, honor, and resilience. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters unexpected allies and enemies, Harry's true character is revealed, leading to a gripping and suspenseful climax.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Bravery, Loyalty, Identity, Redemption
