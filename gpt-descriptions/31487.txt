Synopsis: 
'Boy Scouts on the Great Divide; Or, The Ending of the Trail' follows a group of adventurous Boy Scouts as they embark on a thrilling journey through the rugged and untamed wilderness of the Great Divide. Led by their resourceful and courageous scoutmaster, the boys face numerous challenges and dangers, testing their skills, teamwork, and resilience. As they navigate treacherous terrain, encounter wild animals, and confront unexpected obstacles, the Scouts learn valuable lessons about friendship, perseverance, and the true meaning of bravery. Will they successfully conquer the Great Divide and complete their trail, or will the forces of nature prove too formidable?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival, Nature, Self-discovery
