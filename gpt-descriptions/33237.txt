Synopsis: In 'The Call of the World; or, Every Man's Supreme Opportunity' by William E. Doughty, a young man named John embarks on a journey that takes him across continents and through various life-changing experiences. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene landscapes of distant lands, John encounters a diverse cast of characters who challenge his beliefs and push him to question his purpose in life. As he navigates through love, loss, and self-discovery, John must ultimately confront the call of the world and make a decision that will shape his future.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Purpose in life, Cultural exploration
