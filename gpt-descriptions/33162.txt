Synopsis: In 'Joseph in the Snow, and The Clockmaker. In Three Volumes. Vol. I.', Berthold Auerbach weaves a captivating tale that follows the life of Joseph, a young man who finds himself in a mysterious and enchanting snowy landscape. As Joseph navigates through this unfamiliar world, he encounters a clockmaker who holds the key to unlocking the secrets of his past and the destiny that awaits him. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn deeper into a mesmerizing journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Fate, Redemption
