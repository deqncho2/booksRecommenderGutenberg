Synopsis: In 'All But Lost: A Novel. Vol. 1 of 3' by G. A. Henty, readers are transported to the tumultuous times of the English Civil War. The story follows the adventures of a young royalist, Vincent Wingfield, as he navigates the treacherous landscape of war, betrayal, and love. From daring escapes to secret missions, Vincent's loyalty to the crown is tested at every turn. As he fights for his beliefs and strives to protect those he holds dear, Vincent's journey becomes a thrilling tale of courage and resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Courage, Love, War
