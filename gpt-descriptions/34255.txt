Synopsis: In 'Comrades' by Phelps, Elizabeth Stuart, two childhood friends, Margaret and Mary, find themselves on opposite sides of a heated social and political conflict. As they navigate their diverging paths, their loyalty and friendship are put to the ultimate test. Set against the backdrop of a tumultuous era, this gripping tale explores the complexities of friendship, loyalty, and the power of personal convictions.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Social and political conflict
