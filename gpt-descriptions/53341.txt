Synopsis: In 'The Dorrington Deed-Box' by Arthur Morrison, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the criminal underworld of Victorian London. The story follows the cunning and enigmatic detective, Horace Dorrington, as he navigates a web of deceit, forgery, and murder. When a mysterious deed-box containing valuable documents goes missing, Dorrington is hired to recover it. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a complex network of criminals and a shocking conspiracy that threatens to unravel society itself. With his sharp intellect and unorthodox methods, Dorrington must outwit both the criminals and the law to bring justice to those who deserve it.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Greed, Justice
