Synopsis: In "Susan Clegg and Her Neighbors' Affairs" by Anne Warner, we are introduced to the charming and witty Susan Clegg, a middle-aged woman living in a small New England town. Susan's peaceful life takes an unexpected turn when she becomes entangled in the scandalous affairs of her neighbors. As she navigates through the secrets, lies, and unexpected twists, Susan's sharp observations and quick thinking make her an unlikely detective in this delightful tale of small-town intrigue.

Genres: Mystery, Humor, Fiction

Themes: Small-town gossip, Intrigue, Friendship
