Synopsis: In 'Etiquette' by Agnes H. Morton, a young woman named Emily finds herself thrust into high society, where she must navigate the intricate rules and expectations of proper behavior. As she strives to fit in and make a good impression, Emily discovers that the world of etiquette is not as straightforward as it seems. With the help of a mysterious mentor, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, challenging societal norms and questioning the true meaning of etiquette. Will Emily find her place in this world, or will she forge her own path?

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Social expectations, Identity, Individuality
