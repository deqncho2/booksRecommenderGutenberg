Synopsis: 
In 'Ethel Morton's Holidays', Ethel and her family embark on a series of exciting adventures during their summer vacation. From exploring a mysterious island to participating in a thrilling treasure hunt, Ethel discovers the joy of new friendships and the importance of embracing unexpected opportunities. As she navigates through unexpected challenges and delightful surprises, Ethel learns valuable life lessons that will shape her future.

Genres: 
Children's literature, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Friendship, Exploration, Self-discovery
