Synopsis: 
Tom Brown's School Days is a captivating coming-of-age novel that follows the adventures of young Tom Brown as he navigates the challenges and triumphs of life at Rugby School. From his initial struggles with bullying and the strict school rules to his unexpected friendships and thrilling escapades, Tom's journey is filled with excitement, growth, and valuable life lessons. As he learns to navigate the complexities of school life, Tom's character is tested, and he must find the strength to stand up for what he believes in. With its vivid portrayal of school life in the 19th century, this novel offers a compelling and timeless tale of resilience, friendship, and the pursuit of personal integrity.

Genres: Bildungsroman, Historical Fiction

Themes: Coming-of-age, Friendship, Morality, Resilience
