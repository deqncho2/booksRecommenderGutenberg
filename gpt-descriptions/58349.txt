Synopsis: 

In 'History of Geography', Howarth and Keltie take readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the evolution of geography as a discipline and its profound impact on our understanding of the world. From ancient civilizations to modern explorations, this book delves into the fascinating stories of the individuals who shaped the field, their groundbreaking discoveries, and the challenges they faced. Through vivid narratives and insightful analysis, the authors reveal how geography has not only shaped our physical surroundings but also influenced cultural, political, and economic developments throughout history. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Geography

Themes: Evolution of knowledge, Exploration and discovery, Influence on society
