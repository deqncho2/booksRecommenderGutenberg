Synopsis: In 'Harley Greenoak's Charge' by Mitford, Bertram, a young and ambitious lawyer named Harley Greenoak finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he takes on a seemingly straightforward case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his career but also his life. With time running out, Harley must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Legal Drama

Themes: Justice and morality, Betrayal and loyalty
