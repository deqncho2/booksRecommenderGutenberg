Synopsis: In 'Fighting the Whales' by R. M. Ballantyne, a young sailor named Jack ventures into the treacherous world of whaling, where he must face the immense power and danger of these majestic creatures. As he battles the elements and his own fears, Jack discovers the true nature of courage and the price one must pay for survival in the unforgiving depths of the ocean.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Survival, Man vs. Nature
