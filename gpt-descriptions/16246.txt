Synopsis: In "A Very Pleasaunt & Fruitful Diologe Called the Epicure," Erasmus takes us on a captivating journey through a thought-provoking conversation between two friends, Epicure and Stoic. As they engage in a lively debate, they explore the nature of happiness, the pursuit of pleasure, and the meaning of a virtuous life. Through their witty and insightful dialogue, Erasmus challenges conventional wisdom and invites readers to question their own beliefs and values. This engaging work offers a unique perspective on philosophy and human nature, leaving readers eager to delve deeper into the profound ideas it presents.

Genres: Philosophy, Dialogue, Satire

Themes: Happiness, Pleasure, Virtue, Morality, Human Nature
