Synopsis: In "Adventures in American Bookshops, Antique Stores and Auction Rooms," Bruno, Guido takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden treasures and unexpected encounters found within the world of American bookshops, antique stores, and auction rooms. From dusty shelves filled with forgotten classics to the thrill of bidding on rare artifacts, this delightful memoir explores the author's passion for collecting and the fascinating stories behind each unique find. With humor and curiosity, Bruno, Guido invites readers to join him on a quest that unveils the enchanting world of literary and historical treasures.

Genres: Memoir, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Collecting, History, Serendipity, Curiosity
