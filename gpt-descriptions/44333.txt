Synopsis: 
In 'Hesperothen; Notes from the West, Vol. 2 (of 2): A Record of a Ramble in the United States and Canada in the Spring and Summer of 1881', Sir William Howard Russell takes readers on an enthralling journey through the United States and Canada during the spring and summer of 1881. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Russell paints a vivid picture of the landscapes, people, and cultures he encounters, providing a unique perspective on the North American continent. From bustling cities to breathtaking natural wonders, this travelogue offers a fascinating glimpse into a bygone era and the wonders of the New World.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Exchange, Natural Beauty
