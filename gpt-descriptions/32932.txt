Synopsis: In 'Seven Frozen Sailors' by George Manville Fenn, a group of sailors find themselves stranded in the Arctic after their ship becomes trapped in ice. As they struggle to survive in the harsh conditions, they uncover a mysterious secret that threatens their very existence. With danger lurking at every turn, the sailors must rely on their wits and courage to unravel the truth and find a way back home.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Survival

Themes: Perseverance, Survival, Discovery
