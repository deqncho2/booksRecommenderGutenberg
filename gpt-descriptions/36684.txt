Synopsis: In "Molly Brown's Freshman Days" by Nell Speed, follow the spirited and determined Molly Brown as she embarks on her first year of college. From navigating new friendships and academic challenges to discovering her own identity, Molly's journey is filled with laughter, heartache, and unexpected twists. As she learns to navigate the complexities of college life, Molly's resilience and zest for adventure shine through, making for an unforgettable coming-of-age tale.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Resilience, Self-discovery
