Synopsis: In 'Pearl and Periwinkle' by Anna Graetz, a chance encounter between two strangers, Pearl and Periwinkle, sets off a chain of events that will challenge their beliefs, push them to their limits, and ultimately change their lives forever. As they navigate through a world filled with secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances, Pearl and Periwinkle must confront their own pasts and confront the truth about who they really are. With each twist and turn, the bond between them grows stronger, leading them on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Secrets
