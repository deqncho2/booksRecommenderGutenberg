Synopsis: In Spite of All: A Novel by Edna Lyall is a captivating story that follows the life of a young woman named Hester. Set in Victorian England, Hester faces numerous challenges and hardships as she navigates through a society that is often unforgiving and restrictive. From her humble beginnings to her pursuit of education and independence, Hester's journey is filled with love, loss, and the constant struggle to defy societal expectations. As she fights against the odds, Hester's determination and resilience shine through, making her an inspiring and unforgettable protagonist.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Gender roles, Social class, Individualism, Resilience
