Synopsis: In the small village of Blangy, a young and ambitious rector named Adrien finds himself entangled in a web of love, power, and secrets. As he navigates the complexities of his position, he becomes the center of attention for the village's inhabitants, each with their own hidden desires and agendas. Adrien's journey takes him through a series of unexpected events that challenge his faith, test his character, and force him to confront the dark underbelly of human nature.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Power and corruption, Love and desire, Morality and ethics
