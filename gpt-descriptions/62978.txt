Synopsis: 
In 'Neighbourhood: A year's life in and about an English village', Edwardes Tickner takes us on a captivating journey through the seasons in a quaint English village. From the lively village fete to the mysterious occurrences in the old manor house, the author introduces us to a cast of charming and eccentric characters who bring the village to life. As the year unfolds, secrets are revealed, friendships are tested, and unexpected events change the course of the villagers' lives. With its vivid descriptions and delightful anecdotes, this book offers a heartwarming and immersive exploration of the joys and challenges of small-town life.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Contemporary

Themes: Community, Tradition, Change, Secrets
