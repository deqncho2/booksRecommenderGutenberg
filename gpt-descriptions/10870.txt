Synopsis: In 'Are You a Bromide?: The Sulphitic Theory Expounded and Exemplified According to the Most Recent Researches into the Psychology of Boredom, Including Many Well-Known Bromidioms Now in Use', Burgess, Gelett takes readers on a fascinating journey into the depths of boredom and its psychological implications. Through a series of captivating anecdotes and insightful analysis, the author explores the concept of being a "bromide" and the various ways in which boredom manifests in our lives. This thought-provoking work will challenge readers to reconsider their own experiences of monotony and discover the hidden depths within the seemingly mundane.

Genres: Psychology, Self-help, Satire

Themes: Boredom, Psychology of monotony, Self-reflection
