Synopsis: In "Salve Venetia, gleanings from Venetian history; vol. I," F. Marion Crawford takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and mysterious history of Venice. From the city's legendary origins to its glorious Renaissance period, this volume offers a collection of intriguing tales and anecdotes that bring the past to life. Delve into the secrets of the Venetian Republic, explore the hidden corners of the city, and uncover the captivating stories of its illustrious inhabitants. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Crawford paints a vivid picture of Venice's vibrant past, leaving readers eager to discover more.

Genres: Historical fiction, Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: History, Power and politics, Cultural heritage
