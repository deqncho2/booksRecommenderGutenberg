Synopsis: In 'The Book of Town & Window Gardening', Frances A. Bardswell takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of urban gardening. Through vivid descriptions and practical advice, Bardswell explores the art of transforming small spaces into lush and vibrant gardens. From window boxes to rooftop terraces, she reveals the secrets to creating stunning displays of flowers, herbs, and even vegetables. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will discover the joys and challenges of gardening in an urban environment, and be inspired to bring the beauty of nature into their own homes.

Genres: Gardening, Non-fiction

Themes: Urban gardening, Small space gardening, Nature in urban environments
