Synopsis: 
'Prince Ricardo of Pantouflia: Being the Adventures of Prince Prigio's Son' follows the thrilling journey of Prince Ricardo, the son of the beloved Prince Prigio. Set in the enchanting kingdom of Pantouflia, this captivating tale takes readers on a whirlwind adventure filled with magic, danger, and unexpected twists. As Prince Ricardo embarks on a quest to prove his worth and fulfill his destiny, he encounters mysterious creatures, encounters treacherous villains, and discovers the true meaning of courage and loyalty. Will Prince Ricardo overcome the challenges that lie ahead and become the hero Pantouflia needs? 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Fairy Tale

Themes: Heroism, Destiny, Family, Magic
