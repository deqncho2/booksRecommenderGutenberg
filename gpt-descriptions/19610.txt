Synopsis: 
In 'Selections from Previous Works: With Remarks on Romanes' Mental Evolution in Animals, and a Psalm of Montreal', Samuel Butler takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through a collection of his previous works. With a keen eye for observation and a touch of wit, Butler explores the fascinating concept of mental evolution in animals, challenging conventional beliefs and shedding light on the intricate workings of the animal mind. Alongside these insightful selections, Butler presents his unique and poetic 'Psalm of Montreal', a captivating ode to the vibrant city. This compilation offers a captivating blend of intellectual exploration and artistic expression, leaving readers pondering the mysteries of the animal kingdom and the beauty of human creativity.

Genres: Essay, Poetry, Philosophy
Themes: Mental evolution, Animal intelligence, Human creativity, Observational analysis
