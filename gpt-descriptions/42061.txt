Synopsis: 

In "The American Egypt: A Record of Travel in Yucatan," Arnold Channing and Frederick J. Tabor Frost take readers on an enthralling journey through the mysterious and captivating landscapes of Yucatan. As they explore the ancient ruins and delve into the rich history of the Mayan civilization, the authors encounter unexpected challenges and uncover secrets that have remained hidden for centuries. With vivid descriptions and a keen eye for detail, this travelogue offers a fascinating glimpse into a world shrouded in myth and legend.

Genres: Travelogue, History, Archaeology

Themes: Exploration, Ancient Civilizations, Cultural Heritage
