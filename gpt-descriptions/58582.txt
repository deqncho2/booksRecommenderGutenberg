Synopsis: In 'St. Martin's Eve: A Novel', the lives of two young women, Lucy and Helen, become intertwined when they both find themselves in a mysterious and enchanting old house. As they uncover the secrets hidden within its walls, they are drawn into a world of romance, intrigue, and supernatural occurrences. With each passing day, the house reveals more about its past and the dark forces that still linger. As Lucy and Helen navigate through love, betrayal, and the unknown, they must confront their own fears and make choices that will shape their destinies forever.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Supernatural elements, Love and betrayal
