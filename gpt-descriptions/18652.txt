Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Henrietta Hen' by Bailey, Arthur Scott, readers are introduced to Henrietta, a curious and adventurous hen who embarks on a thrilling journey. As she explores the world beyond her cozy farm, Henrietta encounters various challenges and unexpected friendships. With each new experience, she learns valuable lessons about courage, resilience, and the importance of embracing one's true self. This heartwarming tale will captivate readers as they follow Henrietta's remarkable journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Courage, Self-discovery, Friendship
