Synopsis: In the Transactions of the American Society of Civil Engineers, Vol. LXVIII, Sept. 1910, E. B. Temple presents an intriguing paper titled "The New York Tunnel Extension of the Pennsylvania Railroad; Meadows Division and Harrison Transfer Yard." This paper delves into the fascinating world of civil engineering, exploring the ambitious project of extending the Pennsylvania Railroad's tunnel in New York. Temple provides detailed insights into the challenges faced during the construction of the Meadows Division and the Harrison Transfer Yard, captivating readers with the complexities and innovations involved in this monumental undertaking.

Genres: Technical, Engineering, Historical

Themes: Infrastructure development, Engineering challenges, Urban transportation
