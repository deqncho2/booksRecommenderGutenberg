Synopsis: In "It Happened in Egypt," a young Englishwoman finds herself caught up in a thrilling adventure when she travels to Egypt to visit her archaeologist father. As she becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue, she must navigate the treacherous world of ancient artifacts, secret societies, and a forbidden love that could change her life forever. With the backdrop of the exotic Egyptian landscape, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets hidden within the sands of Egypt.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Ancient History, Forbidden Love
