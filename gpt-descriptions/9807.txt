Synopsis: In the mysterious and atmospheric Scarhaven Keep, a renowned surgeon finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and danger. As he delves deeper into the dark history of the keep and its inhabitants, he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens to destroy lives and reputations. With his own life at stake, he must navigate through a labyrinth of deception and treachery to uncover the truth and bring justice to Scarhaven Keep.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Betrayal and trust, Revenge and justice
