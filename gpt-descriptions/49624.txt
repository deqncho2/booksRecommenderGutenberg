Synopsis: In 'Ballad of the Lost Hare' by Sidney, Margaret, a young girl named Lily discovers a mysterious hare in her backyard. As she embarks on a captivating adventure to find the hare's true home, Lily uncovers secrets about her own family and the power of love and friendship. With each step, she unravels a tale that intertwines the past and present, leading her to unexpected places and encounters that will forever change her life.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Family, Friendship, Identity, Discovery
