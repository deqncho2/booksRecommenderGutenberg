Synopsis: In the small town of Superior, something extraordinary is about to happen. One day, without any warning, the entire town takes off into the sky, leaving behind a bewildered population and a world in shock. As the town floats through the atmosphere, its inhabitants must navigate the challenges of living in a floating community, while also grappling with the mysteries of their sudden departure. With humor and imagination, 'And Then the Town Took Off' takes readers on a whimsical journey that explores the human spirit and the power of community.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Humor

Themes: Adventure, Community, Identity
