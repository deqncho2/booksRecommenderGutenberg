Synopsis: In the small town of Onzar, a series of mysterious and sinister events begin to unfold, shrouding the community in fear and darkness. As the townspeople grapple with the unexplainable occurrences, a group of unlikely heroes emerges, determined to uncover the truth behind the evil that has taken hold of their once peaceful town. With each step they take, they delve deeper into a web of secrets, ancient curses, and supernatural forces that threaten to consume them all. As the line between reality and nightmare blurs, they must confront their own fears and confront the malevolent presence that lurks in the shadows of Onzar.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Fear, Secrets and Betrayal
