Synopsis: In the small town of Simsbury, Average Jones is an ordinary man with an extraordinary talent - he can solve any mystery. When a series of baffling crimes plague the town, Average is called upon to use his unique skills to uncover the truth. As he delves deeper into the mysteries, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With his sharp wit and keen observation, Average Jones becomes a captivating detective, unraveling the truth behind the perplexing crimes that have the town in a frenzy.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Hidden Truths, Unraveling Mysteries
