Synopsis: In "Brotherly Love: Shewing That as Merely Human It May Not Always Be Depended Upon," Kelly and Sherwood delve into the complex dynamics of sibling relationships, exploring the depths of love, loyalty, and betrayal. Set in a time when family ties were paramount, this gripping tale follows the lives of two brothers who find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and deceit. As their bond is tested, the story takes unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers questioning the true nature of brotherly love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Family, Loyalty, Betrayal, Secrets
