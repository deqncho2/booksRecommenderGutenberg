Synopsis: In 'Friendless Felicia: Or, a little city sparrow' by Eleanora H. Stooke, follow the captivating journey of Felicia, a young sparrow who finds herself alone in the bustling city. As she navigates the challenges of survival and searches for companionship, Felicia's determination and resilience are put to the test. Will she find her place in this unfamiliar world, or will she forever remain friendless?

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Belonging
