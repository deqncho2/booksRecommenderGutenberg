Synopsis: 
"The Black Border: Gullah Stories of the Carolina Coast" takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and vibrant culture of the Gullah people. Set against the backdrop of the Carolina coast, this collection of stories weaves together tales of love, loss, resilience, and the enduring power of community. From mystical encounters with spirits to the struggles of everyday life, each story offers a glimpse into the unique world of the Gullah people and their deep connection to their ancestral roots. With a glossary to help readers navigate the Gullah dialect, this book immerses readers in a world filled with captivating characters and unforgettable stories.

Genres: Fiction, Cultural, Short Stories

Themes: Cultural heritage, Community, Spirituality, Resilience
