Synopsis: 

In 'Tracks and Tracking' by Josef Brunner, a renowned tracker and wilderness expert, embark on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness as he shares his captivating experiences and invaluable knowledge of tracking elusive animals. From the vast African savannah to the dense forests of North America, Brunner's vivid storytelling takes readers on a gripping adventure, revealing the secrets of animal behavior and the art of tracking. As he unravels the mysteries of the wild, readers will be enthralled by the challenges faced by both predator and prey, and the delicate balance that exists in nature's intricate web.

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, Nature

Themes: Wildlife conservation, Survival skills, Animal behavior, Connection with nature
