Synopsis: 
In 'Huntley: A Mason Family Country House' by Tony P. Wrenn, readers are transported to the enchanting world of Huntley, a magnificent country house nestled in the heart of the English countryside. As the story unfolds, secrets from the past begin to resurface, threatening to unravel the idyllic facade of this grand estate. With a cast of intriguing characters and a captivating mystery at its core, this novel takes readers on a journey of discovery, love, and betrayal that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Betrayal, Love and loss
