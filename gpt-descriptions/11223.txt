Synopsis: In "Big Timber: A Story of the Northwest," Bertrand W. Sinclair takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rugged landscapes of the Pacific Northwest. Set against the backdrop of the timber industry, the story follows the life of a young man named David Carrigan, who finds himself caught in a web of love, betrayal, and danger. As he navigates the treacherous world of logging camps and the cutthroat business of timber, David must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will shape his future. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Sinclair paints a vivid picture of the untamed wilderness and the human spirit's resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Survival, Ambition, Love and Betrayal
