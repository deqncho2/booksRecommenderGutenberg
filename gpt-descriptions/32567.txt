Synopsis: In 'Forging the Blades: A Tale of the Zulu Rebellion', Bertram Mitford takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of the Zulu Kingdom during a time of rebellion and unrest. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate the treacherous landscape of war, honor, and sacrifice. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Mitford paints a vivid picture of a tumultuous era that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Conflict, Survival
