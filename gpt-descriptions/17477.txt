Synopsis: In 'The Trail Horde' by Charles Alden Seltzer, a mysterious and dangerous horde of outlaws descends upon a small western town, leaving destruction and chaos in their wake. As the townspeople struggle to defend themselves, a lone cowboy with a dark past emerges as their only hope. With thrilling gunfights, unexpected alliances, and a relentless pursuit of justice, this gripping tale takes readers on a wild ride through the untamed frontier.

Genres: Western, Action, Adventure

Themes: Justice, Redemption, Survival
