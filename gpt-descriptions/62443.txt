Synopsis: In 'Destination—Death' by Wilbur S. Peacock, a group of strangers find themselves aboard a luxurious cruise ship, unaware that their journey is about to take a sinister turn. As the ship sails towards its destination, a series of mysterious events unfold, leaving the passengers questioning their own sanity and the true nature of their fellow travelers. With danger lurking around every corner, they must unravel the secrets of the ship before it reaches its final destination—death.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Paranoia, Survival
