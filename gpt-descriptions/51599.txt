Synopsis: In 'What We Saw at Madame World's Fair: Being a Series of Letters from the Twins at the Panama-Pacific International Exposition to Their Cousins at Home', the adventurous twins embark on a thrilling journey at the Panama-Pacific International Exposition. Through a series of captivating letters, they share their experiences and discoveries with their cousins back home, uncovering secrets and encountering unexpected twists along the way. As they navigate the wonders of the fair, the twins find themselves entangled in a web of mystery that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Family, Secrets
