Synopsis: In 'From out the Vasty Deep' by Marie Belloc Lowndes, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious and haunting presence that has taken hold of her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the dark secrets of the past, Mary finds herself entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and unexplained phenomena. With each revelation, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leaving Mary questioning her own sanity and the true nature of the malevolent force that lurks within the depths of the house.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Haunting, Family secrets, Sanity vs. madness
