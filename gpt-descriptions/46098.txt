Synopsis: In the Name of Liberty: A Story of the Terror takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous era of the French Revolution. Amidst the chaos and violence, we follow the lives of two individuals from vastly different backgrounds, whose paths intertwine in unexpected ways. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of political intrigue and personal sacrifice, they must grapple with questions of loyalty, justice, and the true meaning of liberty. With its vivid historical backdrop and compelling characters, this novel offers a captivating exploration of the human spirit in times of revolution and upheaval.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Drama

Themes: Revolution, Loyalty, Justice, Sacrifice
