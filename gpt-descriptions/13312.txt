Synopsis: In 'The Light That Lures' by Percy James Brebner, a young artist named Emily finds herself captivated by a mysterious lighthouse that holds a secret from her past. As she delves deeper into the lighthouse's history, she becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and hidden treasures. With each step closer to the truth, Emily must confront her own fears and desires, ultimately discovering that the light that lures can also lead to darkness.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, the power of art and creativity, the duality of light and darkness
