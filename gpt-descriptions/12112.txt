Synopsis: 
In 'The Boy Scouts of the Eagle Patrol' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of young scouts find themselves embarking on an extraordinary adventure that will test their courage, teamwork, and survival skills. As they navigate through treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected challenges, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness and determination to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. Along the way, they learn valuable lessons about friendship, loyalty, and the true meaning of bravery. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival, Teamwork
