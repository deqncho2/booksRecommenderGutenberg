Synopsis: In 'The Iron Boys on the Ore Boats; or, Roughing It on the Great Lakes' by James R. Mears, readers are taken on an exciting adventure as three young boys embark on a journey working on the massive ore boats that traverse the Great Lakes. From facing treacherous storms to encountering mysterious characters, the boys find themselves in thrilling and dangerous situations. As they navigate the challenges of life on the boats, they also uncover secrets that could change their lives forever. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies ahead for the Iron Boys.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
