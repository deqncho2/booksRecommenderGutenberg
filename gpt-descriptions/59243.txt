Synopsis: In 'The Pacifists' by Charles E. Fritch, a small town is suddenly plagued by a series of mysterious and brutal murders. As the body count rises, the residents become increasingly terrified, but one man stands out from the rest. John, a pacifist with a dark past, is determined to uncover the truth behind the killings and put an end to the violence. With the clock ticking and the town on the brink of chaos, John must confront his own demons and make a choice that could change everything.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Pacifism, Redemption, Morality
