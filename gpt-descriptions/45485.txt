Synopsis: In 'The Sky Trail' by Graham M. Dean, a young adventurer named Alex embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization hidden high in the mountains. As he navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters mysterious creatures, Alex must rely on his wit and courage to unravel the enigmatic clues left behind by the ancient civilization. With each step closer to the truth, he discovers that the fate of the world may rest in his hands. Will Alex be able to unlock the secrets of the Sky Trail before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Legacy, Destiny
