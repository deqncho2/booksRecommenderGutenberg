Synopsis: In the whimsical world of Pudibundia, where politeness is taken to extreme levels, a peculiar group of individuals navigate a society governed by strict etiquette and manners. As they strive to maintain their polite facade, they find themselves entangled in a series of hilarious and absurd situations that challenge their notions of propriety. From tea parties gone awry to elaborate greetings that go on for hours, the polite people of Pudibundia must confront the consequences of their excessive politeness and discover the true meaning of civility.

Genres: Satire, Fantasy, Humor

Themes: Politeness, Social norms, Individuality
