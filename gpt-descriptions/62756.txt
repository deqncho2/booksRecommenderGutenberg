Synopsis: In 'Margaret and Her Friends', join Margaret Fuller and her intellectual companions as they engage in ten captivating conversations about Greek mythology and its artistic expressions. Set in the house of the Reverend George Ripley in Boston, these discussions delve into the rich tapestry of Greek myths and their relevance to the world of art. As the conversations unfold, the reader is transported to a world of ancient gods and heroes, exploring the profound impact of Greek mythology on human creativity and imagination.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Literary Criticism

Themes: Greek mythology, Artistic expression, Intellectual discourse, Creativity, Imagination
