Synopsis: 
In "Tom Slade on Mystery Trail" by Percy Keese Fitzhugh, Tom Slade finds himself embarking on a thrilling adventure as he sets out to solve a perplexing mystery. When a valuable artifact goes missing, Tom's keen observation skills and determination lead him on a trail filled with unexpected twists and turns. Along the way, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, uncovers hidden secrets, and faces dangerous situations that test his courage and resourcefulness. As Tom delves deeper into the mystery, he must rely on his wit and intuition to unravel the truth and bring justice to those involved.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Detective work, Problem-solving, Bravery, Friendship
