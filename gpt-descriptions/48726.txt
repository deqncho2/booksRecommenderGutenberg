Synopsis: In the captivating novel "Diane of Ville Marie: A Romance of French Canada," Blanche Lucile Macdonnell takes readers on a journey to 17th-century Montreal, where the young and spirited Diane finds herself torn between duty and desire. As she navigates the challenges of colonial life, Diane's path intertwines with that of a mysterious stranger, leading her on a thrilling adventure filled with love, betrayal, and the pursuit of freedom. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant and evolving New France, this tale of passion and resilience will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Identity and Independence, Colonial Life and Society
