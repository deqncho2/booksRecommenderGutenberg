Synopsis: In 'The Girl from Infinite Smallness' by Cummings, Ray, a young woman named Lily discovers a hidden world beyond her imagination when she stumbles upon a mysterious book in her grandmother's attic. As she delves deeper into the secrets of this enchanted realm, Lily must navigate a treacherous journey filled with unexpected allies, dangerous adversaries, and a destiny she never could have anticipated. With each step she takes, Lily uncovers the truth about her own identity and the power she possesses, leading her to question the boundaries of reality and the infinite possibilities that lie within the smallest of things.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Perception of Reality
