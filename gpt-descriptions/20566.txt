Synopsis: In "Rogers-isms, the Cowboy Philosopher on the Peace Conference" by Will Rogers, the beloved cowboy philosopher takes on the weighty topic of a peace conference with his signature wit and wisdom. As he navigates the complexities of international diplomacy, Rogers offers a unique perspective that challenges conventional thinking and sheds light on the absurdities of politics. With his trademark humor and insightful observations, he brings a refreshing and thought-provoking take on the pursuit of peace. 

Genres: Humor, Satire, Political Commentary

Themes: Diplomacy, International Relations, Political Absurdities
