Synopsis: 
In "Tom Slade at Temple Camp," Tom Slade finds himself embarking on a thrilling adventure as he joins his fellow scouts at Temple Camp. From navigating treacherous waters to solving mysterious puzzles, Tom's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. As he forms new friendships and faces unexpected challenges, Tom learns valuable lessons about teamwork, courage, and the true meaning of leadership. Will Tom be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way and prove himself as a worthy scout?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Leadership, Teamwork
