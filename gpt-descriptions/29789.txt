Synopsis: In 'Poppa Needs Shorts' by Richmond, Leigh and Richmond, Walt, a seemingly ordinary family vacation takes an unexpected turn when a mischievous monkey steals the father's shorts. As the family embarks on a hilarious and adventurous journey to retrieve the shorts, they encounter a series of comical mishaps and eccentric characters. With each step, they learn valuable lessons about family, resilience, and the importance of embracing the unexpected.

Genres: Humor, Adventure, Family

Themes: Family bonds, Resilience, Embracing the unexpected
