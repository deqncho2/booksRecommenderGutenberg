Synopsis: In 'Torwood's Trust', the first volume of a captivating trilogy, author Evelyn Everett-Green introduces readers to the mysterious Torwood family. As the story unfolds, secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances emerge, all centered around the enigmatic Torwood estate. With a cast of intriguing characters and a web of hidden motives, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through love, betrayal, and the pursuit of truth.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Trust and betrayal, Love and relationships
