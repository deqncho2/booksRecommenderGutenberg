Synopsis: In 'The Story of Silk' by Sara Ware Bassett, a young woman named Ruth embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling streets of New York City to the enchanting landscapes of China. Drawn into the captivating world of silk production, Ruth becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal. As she unravels the mysteries surrounding this ancient and luxurious fabric, Ruth discovers that the true power of silk lies not only in its beauty but also in its ability to transform lives.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Exploration of cultural exchange, Pursuit of passion and dreams, Uncovering hidden truths
