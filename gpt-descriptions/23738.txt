Synopsis: 
In 'The Thing from the Lake', a young couple moves into a charming old house by a lake, unaware of the mysterious presence that resides within its depths. As they uncover the house's dark secrets, they find themselves entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and unexplained phenomena. With each passing day, the couple becomes more desperate to uncover the truth behind the thing from the lake, before it consumes them entirely.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, the power of the unknown, the thin line between reality and the supernatural
