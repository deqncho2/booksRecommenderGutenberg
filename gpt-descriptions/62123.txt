Synopsis: In "The Sworn Brothers: A Tale of the Early Days of Iceland," Gunnarsson takes readers on a captivating journey to the rugged landscapes of early Iceland. The story follows two young men, Thorgeir and Thormod, who forge a deep bond as sworn brothers amidst the harsh realities of their world. As they navigate the treacherous paths of love, honor, and loyalty, their lives become intertwined with the fate of their homeland. With vivid descriptions and rich historical detail, Gunnarsson paints a vivid picture of a bygone era, where the strength of friendship and the pursuit of justice collide in a gripping tale.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Honor, Loyalty, Love, Justice
