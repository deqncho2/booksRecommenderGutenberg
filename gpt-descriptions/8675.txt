Synopsis: 
'Welsh Fairy-Tales and Other Stories' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Welsh folklore. From mischievous fairies to heroic knights, this collection of tales weaves together magical adventures that will transport readers to a realm filled with wonder and mystery. Each story is filled with vivid imagery and memorable characters, making it impossible to resist getting lost in the pages of this timeless collection.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: 
Magic, Heroism, Welsh Culture
