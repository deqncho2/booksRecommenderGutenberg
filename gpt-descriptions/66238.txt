Synopsis: In 'The Collected Works of Henrik Ibsen, Vol. 03 (of 11)', Ibsen takes us on a captivating journey through a series of interconnected plays that explore the complexities of human relationships and the consequences of societal expectations. From the struggles of a young woman trapped in a loveless marriage to the clash between tradition and individual freedom, this volume delves into the depths of the human psyche, challenging our perceptions and leaving us yearning for more.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Realism

Themes: Marriage and its constraints, Gender roles and expectations, Individual freedom and societal norms
