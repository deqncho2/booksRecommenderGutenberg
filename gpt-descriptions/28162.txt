Synopsis: 
In 'The Invader: A Novel', Margaret L. Woods weaves a gripping tale of mystery and suspense that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Set in a small coastal town, the story follows the arrival of a mysterious stranger who disrupts the peaceful lives of the townspeople. As tensions rise and secrets are unveiled, the protagonist finds themselves entangled in a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn deeper into the enigma surrounding the invader, eagerly anticipating the shocking revelations that lie ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Betrayal, Small-town dynamics
