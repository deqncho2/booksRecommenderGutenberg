Synopsis: In 'The Lost Giant, and Other American Indian Tales Retold', Violet Moore Higgins takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting American Indian tales. From the mysterious disappearance of a giant to the adventures of brave warriors and cunning animals, these retellings bring to life the rich folklore and traditions of Native American cultures. With vivid storytelling and vibrant illustrations, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the magical world of American Indian folklore.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Children's Literature

Themes: Cultural heritage, Nature and animals, Heroism, Wisdom
