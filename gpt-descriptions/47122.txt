Synopsis: In 'Agnes Strickland's Queens of England, Vol. 2. (of 3): Abridged and Fully Illustrated', the captivating stories of the remarkable queens who shaped England's history continue. From the fierce and determined Queen Eleanor of Aquitaine to the enigmatic and tragic Queen Anne Boleyn, this volume delves into the lives of these influential women who held immense power and faced unimaginable challenges. With vivid illustrations bringing their stories to life, readers will be transported to a world of royal intrigue, love, and betrayal.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and politics, Gender roles and expectations, Love and relationships
