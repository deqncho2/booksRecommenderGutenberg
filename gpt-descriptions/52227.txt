Synopsis: 
'Piece Goods Manual: Fabrics described; textile, knit goods, weaving terms, etc., explained; with notes on the classification of samples' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the world of fabrics, providing detailed descriptions and explanations of various textile, knit goods, and weaving terms. The manual also includes valuable insights on the classification of fabric samples. Written by A. E. Blanco, this book is a must-read for anyone interested in understanding the intricacies of fabrics and expanding their knowledge in the field.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Textiles, Fabric Classification, Weaving Terminology
