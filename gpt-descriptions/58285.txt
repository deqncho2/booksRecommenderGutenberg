Synopsis: In 'Girls of '64', follow the lives of four young women as they navigate the tumultuous decade of the 1960s. From the vibrant streets of New York City to the battlefields of Vietnam, these girls will face love, loss, and the ever-changing social and political landscape of the era. As they strive to find their place in a world filled with uncertainty, their friendships will be tested, secrets will be revealed, and their lives will be forever changed.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Drama

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Social Change, Love and Loss
