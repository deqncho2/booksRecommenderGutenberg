Synopsis: 

In 'Civilization: Tales of the Orient' by Ellen N. La Motte, readers are transported to the vibrant and mysterious world of the Orient. Through a collection of captivating tales, the author weaves together a tapestry of exotic landscapes, intriguing characters, and thought-provoking encounters. From the bustling streets of ancient cities to the serene beauty of nature, each story offers a unique glimpse into the rich tapestry of Eastern culture. As readers delve into the pages, they will be enthralled by the vivid descriptions, unexpected twists, and profound insights that unfold. 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Cultural Fiction

Themes: Exploration of Eastern culture, Human connection, Identity, Transformation
