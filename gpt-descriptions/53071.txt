Synopsis: In "Mark the Match Boy; or, Richard Hunter's Ward" by Horatio Alger, Jr., a young orphan named Mark finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he becomes the ward of the wealthy Richard Hunter. As Mark navigates the challenges of his new life, he uncovers secrets that could change everything. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the truth behind Mark's past and his connection to Richard Hunter.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Orphanhood, Social class, Identity, Redemption
