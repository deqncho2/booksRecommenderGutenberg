Synopsis: In 'God Hath Spoken' by Dark, Harris J., a mysterious ancient artifact is discovered, setting off a thrilling race against time as a group of unlikely heroes must decipher its secrets and prevent a catastrophic event. As they delve deeper into the enigmatic past, they uncover a web of conspiracy and danger that spans centuries, leading them to question their own beliefs and the very nature of faith. With each revelation, the stakes grow higher, and the line between good and evil becomes increasingly blurred. Will they be able to unlock the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Faith and belief, Conspiracy, Redemption, Moral ambiguity
