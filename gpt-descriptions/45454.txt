Synopsis: In "Gun running for Casement in the Easter rebellion, 1916" by Spindler, Karl, a young and daring protagonist finds himself entangled in a dangerous mission to smuggle weapons for a prominent figure in the Easter Rebellion of 1916. As he navigates the treacherous world of gunrunning, he must face unexpected challenges, betrayal, and the looming threat of discovery. With each step, the stakes rise higher, and the fate of a nation hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Betrayal, Courage, Sacrifice
