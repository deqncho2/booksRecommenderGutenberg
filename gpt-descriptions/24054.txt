Synopsis: 
In 'Dead Giveaway' by Randall Garrett, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a seemingly impossible murder occurs within a locked room. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that challenge his deductive skills and put his own life at risk. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects, all while racing against the clock to solve the baffling mystery before another life is lost.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Deduction
