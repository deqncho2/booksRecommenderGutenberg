Synopsis: 

'Chinese Folk-Lore Tales' by Macgowan, J. is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that transport readers into the rich tapestry of Chinese culture. From mythical creatures and legendary heroes to supernatural encounters and moral lessons, this anthology offers a mesmerizing glimpse into the world of Chinese folklore. Each tale is filled with vivid imagery, intricate plot twists, and unexpected revelations, keeping readers on the edge of their seats. Whether it's a tale of love and sacrifice or a fable about the consequences of greed, these stories are sure to ignite the imagination and leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural

Themes: Mythology, Morality, Tradition
