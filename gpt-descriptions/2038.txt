Synopsis: 
The Lock and Key Library: Classic Mystery and Detective Stories: Modern English is a captivating collection of thrilling mystery and detective stories. From the chilling tales of Sherlock Holmes to the mind-bending puzzles of Edgar Allan Poe, this anthology brings together some of the most iconic and gripping stories in the genre. Each story is carefully selected to keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they unravel the mysteries and follow the clues alongside the brilliant detectives. With twists and turns at every corner, this collection promises to keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Detective, Thriller

Themes: Crime, Investigation, Suspense
