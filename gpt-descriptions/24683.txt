Synopsis: Nan Sherwood, a spirited and adventurous young woman, finds herself embarking on a thrilling journey to Palm Beach. As she explores the vibrant orange groves and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Nan becomes entangled in a series of strange and unexpected adventures. From uncovering hidden secrets to navigating the complexities of romance, Nan's time in Palm Beach is filled with excitement and mystery. Will she be able to unravel the enigmas that surround her and find her way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Independence, Love and Relationships
