Synopsis: 
In "Sunny Boy in the Big City" by Ramy Allison White, we follow the captivating journey of Sunny, a young boy with an extraordinary ability to see the world in a unique way. When Sunny's family moves from a small town to the bustling metropolis, he finds himself navigating the complexities of city life, encountering colorful characters and unexpected adventures along the way. As Sunny explores the city's hidden corners and learns to embrace his own individuality, he discovers that even in the chaos of the big city, there is beauty and magic to be found.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Individuality, Exploration, Finding Beauty in the Ordinary
