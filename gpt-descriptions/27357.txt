Synopsis: In "The Landing of the Pilgrims" by Carlton, Henry Fisk, embark on a captivating journey back in time to the momentous arrival of the Pilgrims in the New World. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the author brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices of these brave pioneers as they establish a new society in a foreign land. From the treacherous voyage across the Atlantic to the challenges they face upon arrival, this gripping narrative immerses readers in the hardships and hopes of the Pilgrims, painting a vivid picture of their indomitable spirit and unwavering determination.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Cultural Clash, Faith
