Synopsis: In the remote and mysterious landscape of Exmoor, a captivating tale unfolds as the enigmatic figure of Katerfelto arrives. With his mesmerizing magic and uncanny ability to communicate with animals, Katerfelto becomes a source of fascination and intrigue for the locals. As his presence stirs up both admiration and suspicion, the story delves into the secrets and hidden desires of the community, ultimately leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Gothic

Themes: Magic and illusion, Secrets and deception, Community dynamics
