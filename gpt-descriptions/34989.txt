Synopsis: In "An Examination of the Testimony of the Four Evangelists, by the Rules of Evidence Administered in Courts of Justice: With an Account of the Trial of Jesus," Simon Greenleaf presents a thought-provoking analysis of the testimonies provided by the four evangelists in the Bible. Drawing upon his expertise in legal matters, Greenleaf applies the rules of evidence used in courts of justice to scrutinize the credibility and reliability of the accounts. Through a meticulous examination, he delves into the trial of Jesus, shedding light on the historical and legal aspects surrounding this pivotal event. This compelling work challenges readers to consider the testimonies from a fresh perspective, inviting them to explore the truth behind one of the most significant trials in history.

Genres: Non-fiction, Legal Analysis, Religious Studies

Themes: Historical accuracy, Legal examination, Religious testimony, Truth-seeking
