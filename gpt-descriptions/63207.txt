Synopsis: 
In 'The Boy Electrician' by Alfred Powell Morgan, a young boy named Tom discovers his passion for electricity and embarks on a thrilling journey of exploration and invention. As he delves deeper into the world of circuits, wires, and currents, Tom's ingenuity and curiosity lead him to create remarkable electrical devices that astonish those around him. However, his newfound talent also attracts the attention of a mysterious figure who seeks to exploit Tom's abilities for their own nefarious purposes. With danger lurking around every corner, Tom must use his skills and resourcefulness to outsmart his adversaries and protect his inventions. Will he be able to harness the power of electricity for good and overcome the challenges that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Curiosity, Ingenuity, Power of Knowledge
