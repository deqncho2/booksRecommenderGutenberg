Synopsis: 

'Legends of Fire Island Beach and the South Side' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and enchanting tales of Fire Island Beach and the South Side of Long Island. From the mysterious legends of buried treasure to the haunting stories of shipwrecks and ghostly apparitions, this book unveils the hidden secrets and folklore that have shaped the region for centuries. As readers delve into the pages, they will be transported to a world of adventure, intrigue, and wonder, where the past intertwines with the present, and the line between reality and myth becomes blurred. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Folklore

Themes: Legends and folklore, History and heritage, Mystery and intrigue
