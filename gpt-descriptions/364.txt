Synopsis: In 'The Mad King' by Edgar Rice Burroughs, a young American soldier finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and mistaken identity in the fictional European kingdom of Lutha. As he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power, he must navigate through treacherous alliances, secret societies, and a love triangle that could cost him everything. With twists and turns at every corner, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Power struggles, Love and loyalty
