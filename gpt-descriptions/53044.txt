Synopsis: In the small village of Little Primpton, a mysterious barber named Devlin arrives, bringing with him a peculiar charm and an uncanny ability to transform the lives of his customers. As the townspeople flock to his shop, they soon discover that Devlin's haircuts have a magical effect, altering not only their appearance but also their destinies. However, as the transformations become more extraordinary, the villagers begin to question the true nature of Devlin's powers and the price they must pay for their newfound beauty. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, Devlin the Barber weaves a captivating tale of enchantment, desire, and the consequences of vanity.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Magical Realism

Themes: Transformation, Beauty, Identity, Power
