Synopsis: In 'Christmas Speakin' at Skaggs's Skule' by Marie Irish, the students of Skaggs's Skule are preparing for their annual Christmas program. As they rehearse their lines and practice their songs, unexpected events and hilarious mishaps ensue, making for a memorable and entertaining holiday season. With a cast of charming characters and plenty of laughter, this heartwarming tale captures the joy and spirit of Christmas in a small town.

Genres: Humor, Fiction, Holiday

Themes: Community, Friendship, Celebration
