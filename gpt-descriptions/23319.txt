Synopsis: 

In "Mechanical Drawing Self-Taught," Joshua Rose presents a comprehensive guide to mastering the art of mechanical drawing. This self-teaching manual covers everything from selecting and preparing drawing instruments to practical instruction in mechanical drawing. With examples in simple geometry and elementary mechanism, readers will learn about screw threads, gear wheels, mechanical motions, engines, and boilers. Rose's clear and concise instructions make this book an invaluable resource for anyone interested in honing their mechanical drawing skills.

Genres: Educational, Reference, Technical

Themes: Skill development, Engineering, Mechanical design
