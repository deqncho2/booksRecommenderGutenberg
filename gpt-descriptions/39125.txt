Synopsis: In 'Maggie's Wish' by Marilyn D. Anderson, a young woman named Maggie finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies when she stumbles upon an old photograph that leads her on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's past. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Maggie must confront her own fears and make difficult choices that could change her life forever. With each revelation, the stakes become higher, and Maggie's determination to uncover the truth becomes unwavering. Will she be able to unravel the secrets that have haunted her family for generations?

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Family

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Family Legacy
