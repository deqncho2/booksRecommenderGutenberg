Synopsis: In 'Who Ate the Pink Sweetmeat? And Other Christmas Stories', a collection of captivating tales by various authors, readers are transported to the enchanting world of Christmas. From mysterious disappearances to heartwarming reunions, these stories explore the magic and wonder of the holiday season. With each turn of the page, readers will be left guessing who ate the pink sweetmeat and eagerly anticipating the resolution of each tale. 

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Holiday

Themes: Christmas spirit, Family, Mystery, Redemption
