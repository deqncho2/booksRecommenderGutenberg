Synopsis: 
In 'Snagged and Sunk; Or, The Adventures of a Canvas Canoe' by Castlemon, Harry, a group of adventurous friends embark on a thrilling canoe trip down a treacherous river. As they navigate through dangerous rapids, encounter unexpected obstacles, and face the perils of the wilderness, their bond is tested and their courage is pushed to the limit. With each twist and turn, the friends must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to survive the challenges that lie ahead. Will they make it to their destination unscathed, or will they be snagged and sunk by the unforgiving forces of nature?

Genres: Adventure, Action, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Resilience
