Synopsis: 
'Pariah' by Stephen Marlowe is a gripping and suspenseful thriller that follows the life of a man named Simon Jakes, who finds himself caught in a web of deceit and danger after stumbling upon a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Simon becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where his own life is at stake. With each twist and turn, the tension escalates, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic Pariah.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Betrayal
