Synopsis: In 'The Galleon's Gold; or, Frank Reade, Jr.'s Deep Sea Search,' the brilliant inventor Frank Reade, Jr. embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the lost treasure of a Spanish galleon. Equipped with his remarkable deep-sea diving vessel, Frank and his loyal crew face treacherous waters, dangerous creatures, and unexpected challenges as they delve into the depths of the ocean. With each twist and turn, the search for the galleon's gold becomes a race against time, testing Frank's ingenuity and courage to the limit.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Invention, Treasure Hunting
