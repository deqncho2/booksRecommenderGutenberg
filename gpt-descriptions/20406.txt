Synopsis: In 'Epic and Romance: Essays on Medieval Literature', W. P. Ker takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of medieval literature. With insightful analysis and engaging storytelling, Ker explores the fascinating themes and motifs found in these timeless tales. From the heroic epics of Beowulf and Arthurian legends to the romantic narratives of Tristan and Isolde, this collection of essays offers a rich exploration of the medieval literary landscape. Delve into the captivating world of knights, damsels, and quests as Ker unravels the intricate tapestry of medieval literature.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Medieval Literature, Essays

Themes: Heroism, Romance, Chivalry, Quests, Love and Honor
