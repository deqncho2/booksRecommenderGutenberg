Synopsis: In 'A Manual of Conchology', Thomas Wyatt presents a comprehensive guide to the study of shells, following the system established by Lamarck and incorporating the latest advancements by De Blainville. This meticulously arranged manual is designed to captivate students and enthusiasts alike, offering a wealth of exemplified knowledge on the fascinating world of conchology.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Reference

Themes: Natural history, Classification, Scientific methodology
