Synopsis: 
'Story-Telling Ballads' is a captivating collection of selected and arranged ballads that are perfect for both story-telling and reading aloud. This book is specifically designed to engage the imagination of boys and girls, providing them with thrilling tales that will transport them to different worlds and times. From epic adventures to haunting love stories, these ballads will captivate readers of all ages and leave them eagerly turning the pages to discover what happens next.

Genres: 
Folklore, Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: 
Adventure, Love, Heroism, Morality
