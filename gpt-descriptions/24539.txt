Synopsis: 

In the midst of the American Civil War, a daring expedition takes place in December 1862, as a group of soldiers embarks on a treacherous journey through the towns of Kinston, Whitehall, and Goldsboro in North Carolina. Led by a courageous and resourceful commander, the expedition faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected obstacles along the way. As they navigate through hostile territory, the soldiers must rely on their wits, bravery, and camaraderie to accomplish their mission and overcome the odds stacked against them.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Brotherhood, Survival
