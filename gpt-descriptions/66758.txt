Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 139, May 8, 1915: The Pressing Peril', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue when he is hired to investigate a mysterious disappearance. As he delves deeper into the case, Carter uncovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his own life but also the safety of an entire city. With time running out, he must use all his wit and cunning to outsmart a cunning adversary and bring justice to those in peril.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Detective work, Danger, Intrigue
