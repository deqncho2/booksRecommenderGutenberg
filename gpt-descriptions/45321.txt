Synopsis: 
'Serbian Folk-lore' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Serbian mythology and folklore. From ancient legends of mythical creatures to enchanting tales of love and betrayal, this collection offers a glimpse into a world where reality and fantasy intertwine. Delve into the mysterious realms of witches, vampires, and shape-shifters, as well as the extraordinary adventures of heroes and heroines who navigate the supernatural forces that shape their lives. With each story, the reader is transported to a realm where the boundaries between the natural and the supernatural blur, leaving them spellbound and eager for more.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Fantasy

Themes: Supernatural beings, Love and betrayal, Heroism, Transformation
