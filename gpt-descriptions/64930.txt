Synopsis: In 'The Goddess: A Demon' by Richard Marsh, a mysterious and captivating woman named Lilith enters the lives of two brothers, Edmund and Richard. As they become entangled in her web of seduction and manipulation, they soon discover that Lilith is not what she appears to be. With her dark powers and hidden agenda, Lilith threatens to destroy everything the brothers hold dear, forcing them to confront their deepest fears and desires. As the lines between good and evil blur, Edmund and Richard must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth about Lilith and ultimately save themselves from her clutches.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: Seduction, Manipulation, Good vs. Evil
