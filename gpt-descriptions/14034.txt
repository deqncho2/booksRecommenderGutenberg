Synopsis: In 'King Alfred's Viking: A Story of the First English Fleet', Charles W. Whistler takes readers on a thrilling journey back to the 9th century, where the legendary King Alfred the Great faces a formidable Viking invasion. Amidst the chaos and uncertainty, a young Englishman named Edmund finds himself caught in the middle of the conflict, torn between his loyalty to his people and his unexpected friendship with a Viking warrior. As the tides of war shift and alliances are tested, Edmund must navigate treacherous waters to protect his loved ones and determine where his true allegiance lies.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Identity, Conflict
