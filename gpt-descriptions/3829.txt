Synopsis: 
'Love Among the Chickens' follows the misadventures of a young writer who finds himself entangled in a hilarious series of events when he decides to start a chicken farm on a remote island. As he navigates the challenges of poultry farming, he also becomes embroiled in a love triangle and encounters eccentric characters who add to the chaos. With witty dialogue and comical situations, this novel is a delightful romp that will keep readers laughing until the very end.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Romantic Comedy

Themes: Love and relationships, Pursuit of dreams, Social satire
