Synopsis: In 'A Letter to the Right Honourable the Earl of Derby: on the cruelty and injustice of opening the Crystal Palace on the Sabbath,' Benjamin Parsons passionately addresses the Earl of Derby, expressing his concerns about the decision to open the Crystal Palace on Sundays. Parsons delves into the moral implications and societal consequences of this action, urging the Earl to reconsider. Through a thought-provoking and persuasive argument, Parsons sheds light on the potential cruelty and injustice that may arise from disregarding the sanctity of the Sabbath.

Genres: Non-fiction, Essay, Social Commentary

Themes: Morality, Religion, Social Justice
