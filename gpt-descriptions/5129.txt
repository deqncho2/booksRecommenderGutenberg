Synopsis: In 'The Prodigal Judge' by Kester, Vaughan, a small town is shaken by a mysterious murder that threatens to expose long-buried secrets. As the investigation unfolds, a young lawyer finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, corruption, and forbidden love. With the town's dark past resurfacing, he must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the truth and bring justice to those who deserve it.

Genres: Mystery, Legal Thriller, Romance

Themes: Justice and Morality, Betrayal, Redemption
