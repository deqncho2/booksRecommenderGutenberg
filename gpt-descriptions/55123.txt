Synopsis: In "Perkins, the Fakeer: A Travesty on Reincarnation," follow the eccentric and enigmatic character of Perkins as he embarks on a series of extraordinary adventures. From the perplexing case of "When Reginald was Caroline" to the mysterious encounter of "How Chopin came to Remsen" and the comical chaos surrounding "Clarissa's troublesome baby," Perkins' unique abilities and unconventional methods will leave readers captivated and intrigued. With unexpected twists and turns, this book takes readers on a whimsical journey through the realms of reincarnation and the unexplained.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Comedy

Themes: Reincarnation, Identity, Fate, Transformation
