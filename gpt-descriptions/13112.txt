Synopsis: 

'Speeches from the Dock; Or, Protests of Irish Patriotism' is a collection of powerful speeches delivered by prominent Irish figures who were convicted for their involvement in acts of rebellion and patriotism. From Theobald Wolfe Tone to Captain Mackay, these speeches provide a captivating insight into the minds and hearts of these individuals as they passionately defend their actions and beliefs. Each speech is a testament to the unwavering spirit and resilience of the Irish people in their fight for freedom and justice.

Genres: Historical, Political, Speeches

Themes: Patriotism, Rebellion, Freedom, Justice
