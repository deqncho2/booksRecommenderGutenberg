Synopsis: In "Education of a Martian" by Joseph Shallit, a young Martian named Zog finds himself stranded on Earth after his spaceship crash-lands. With no way to return home, Zog must navigate the complexities of human society and adapt to life on a new planet. As he attends school and interacts with his human classmates, Zog's unique perspective challenges the norms and assumptions of Earthlings, leading to both humorous and thought-provoking situations. Through his experiences, Zog learns about friendship, acceptance, and the true meaning of being human.

Genres: Science Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Humor

Themes: Identity, Cultural Differences, Friendship, Acceptance
