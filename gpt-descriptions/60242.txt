Synopsis: In this thrilling sequel to "From the Earth to the Moon," Jules Verne takes us on an extraordinary adventure as a group of intrepid explorers set out to purchase the North Pole. As they face treacherous obstacles and encounter unexpected allies, the fate of the world hangs in the balance. With Verne's signature blend of scientific imagination and gripping storytelling, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the outcome of this audacious mission.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Scientific Progress, Human Ambition
