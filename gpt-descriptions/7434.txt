Synopsis: 
In 'The Adventures of Joel Pepper', follow the heartwarming journey of young Joel Pepper as he navigates the challenges and joys of his humble life. From his mischievous escapades with his siblings to unexpected encounters with strangers, Joel's world is filled with both excitement and lessons to be learned. As he faces adversity and discovers the power of love and friendship, Joel's determination and resilience shine through, making this a captivating tale that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: 
Children's literature, Adventure, Family

Themes: 
Resilience, Love and friendship, Coming of age
