Synopsis: In 'Notes on Old Peterborough' by Andrew Percival, a mysterious diary is discovered, shedding light on the forgotten history of a small town. As the reader delves into the secrets and intrigues of the past, they are taken on a captivating journey through time, uncovering hidden treasures and long-lost tales. With each turn of the page, the diary reveals a rich tapestry of characters and events that will leave the reader yearning for more.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: History, Secrets, Discovery
