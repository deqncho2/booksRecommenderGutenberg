Synopsis: 
'Dreams and Images: An Anthology of Catholic Poets' takes readers on a captivating journey through the minds and hearts of Catholic poets from various backgrounds and time periods. This anthology showcases a rich tapestry of emotions, beliefs, and experiences, as each poet delves into the depths of their faith and explores the complexities of the human spirit. From profound reflections on divine love to poignant meditations on the struggles of life, these poems offer a glimpse into the profound beauty and mystery of Catholicism. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to a world where words become prayers and images become windows into the divine.

Genres: Poetry, Religious Literature, Anthology
Themes: Faith, Spirituality, Love, Redemption, Human Experience
