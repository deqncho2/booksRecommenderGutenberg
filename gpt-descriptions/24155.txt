Synopsis: In 'Lady Bountiful' by George A. Birmingham, a spirited young woman named Lady Bountiful finds herself thrust into a world of political intrigue and danger when she inherits her late father's estate. As she navigates the treacherous waters of high society, Lady Bountiful must uncover the truth behind her father's mysterious death while also grappling with her own desires and ambitions. With unexpected allies and formidable enemies at every turn, she must rely on her wit and determination to unravel the secrets that threaten to destroy everything she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Power and Politics, Identity and Self-discovery, Love and Betrayal
