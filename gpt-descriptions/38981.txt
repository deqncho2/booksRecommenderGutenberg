Synopsis: In 'The Chase of the Golden Plate' by Jacques Futrelle, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a thrilling race against time to recover a priceless artifact. When a mysterious thief steals the legendary Golden Plate, the detective must navigate a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected twists to track down the culprit and retrieve the precious treasure. As the chase intensifies, the detective's skills and wit are put to the ultimate test, leading to a heart-pounding climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Crime, Pursuit of Justice, Deception
