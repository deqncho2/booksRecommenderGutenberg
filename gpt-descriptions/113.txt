Synopsis: 
When young Mary Lennox is sent to live with her uncle in a gloomy manor on the Yorkshire moors, she discovers a hidden garden that holds the key to unlocking the mysteries of her own past. As Mary tends to the neglected garden, she not only transforms the once desolate space but also begins to heal the hearts of those around her. With the help of newfound friends, Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery, friendship, and the power of nature's rejuvenating touch.

Genres: 
Children's literature, Fiction, Classic

Themes: 
The healing power of nature, the importance of friendship, personal growth and transformation
