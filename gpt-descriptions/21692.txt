Synopsis: In 'The Pirate City: An Algerine Tale', follow the thrilling adventures of young Jack Rogers as he finds himself captured by pirates and taken to the notorious pirate city of Algiers. Amidst the dangers and treachery of the pirate world, Jack must navigate a web of deceit, forge unlikely alliances, and uncover the secrets of the pirate city. Will he be able to escape the clutches of the ruthless pirates and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Betrayal, Identity
