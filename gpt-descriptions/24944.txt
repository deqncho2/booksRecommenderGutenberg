Synopsis: 

In "Mushrooms: how to grow them," William Falconer takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of mushroom cultivation. With a blend of practical advice and captivating anecdotes, Falconer unveils the secrets behind successfully growing mushrooms for both profit and pleasure. From selecting the ideal growing medium to understanding the intricacies of mushroom reproduction, this treatise provides a comprehensive guide for aspiring mushroom cultivators. As readers delve into the pages, they will discover the surprising versatility of mushrooms and the endless possibilities they offer. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or a curious beginner, Falconer's expertise and passion for mushrooms will leave you inspired to embark on your own mushroom-growing adventure.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gardening, How-to

Themes: Mushroom cultivation, Profitable gardening, Natural resource utilization
