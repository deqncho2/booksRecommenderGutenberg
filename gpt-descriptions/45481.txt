Synopsis: In "What Is Christian Science?" Mangasarian delves into the controversial teachings of Christian Science, challenging its claims and shedding light on its origins and practices. Through a critical examination of its principles and doctrines, the author invites readers to question the validity of this influential religious movement. With thought-provoking arguments and insightful analysis, Mangasarian explores the impact of Christian Science on individuals and society, ultimately raising important questions about faith, healing, and the nature of reality.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Criticism, Philosophy

Themes: Religion, Faith, Healing, Skepticism, Reality
