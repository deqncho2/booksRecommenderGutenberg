Synopsis: 
In 'Amateur Fish Culture' by Charles Edward Walker, a seemingly ordinary man stumbles upon a hidden world of fish breeding that will change his life forever. As he delves deeper into this captivating hobby, he discovers a community of passionate enthusiasts, secret techniques, and unexpected challenges. With each new discovery, he becomes more entangled in the intricate web of fish culture, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and obsession. Will he be able to navigate the treacherous waters of this clandestine world, or will he be consumed by his newfound passion?

Genres: 
Drama, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: 
Obsession, Self-discovery, Community
