Synopsis: 

'How It Flies; or, The Conquest of the Air: The Story of Man's Endeavors to Fly and of the Inventions by Which He Has Succeeded' takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of human flight. From the earliest dreams of soaring through the skies to the groundbreaking inventions that made it possible, this book explores the relentless pursuit of flight and the remarkable individuals who made it a reality. Filled with fascinating anecdotes, gripping accounts of daring adventures, and detailed explanations of the technological advancements, this book offers a comprehensive and thrilling exploration of mankind's conquest of the air.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Human ingenuity, Exploration, Innovation, Persistence
