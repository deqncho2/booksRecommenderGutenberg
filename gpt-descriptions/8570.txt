Synopsis: In 'The Philistines' by Bates, Arlo, a small coastal town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances that leave the community in a state of fear and suspicion. As the townspeople grapple with their own secrets and hidden desires, a young woman named Lily becomes determined to uncover the truth behind the unsettling events. With each revelation, the boundaries between reality and the supernatural blur, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of evil and the power of redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and deception, Fear and paranoia, Redemption and forgiveness
