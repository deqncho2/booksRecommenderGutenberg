Synopsis: In 'Clubfoot the Avenger: Being some further adventures of Desmond Oakwood, of the Secret Service', Desmond Oakwood, a skilled agent of the Secret Service, finds himself entangled in a web of international intrigue and danger. As he navigates through a world of spies, double-crosses, and hidden agendas, Oakwood must rely on his wit, resourcefulness, and his unique physical condition - a clubfoot - to outsmart his enemies and bring justice to those who deserve it. With thrilling action sequences and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Espionage, Justice, Overcoming adversity
