Synopsis: 
In 'Together' by Douglas, Norman, a chance encounter between two strangers on a train sets off a chain of events that will challenge their beliefs, push the boundaries of their comfort zones, and ultimately change their lives forever. As they embark on a journey filled with unexpected twists and turns, they discover the power of human connection and the transformative nature of shared experiences. With each passing moment, their lives become intertwined in ways they never could have imagined, leading them to question their own identities and the true meaning of love and friendship. 

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Human connection, Identity, Love, Friendship
