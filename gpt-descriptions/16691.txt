Synopsis: 
In the bustling streets of New York City's Fifth Avenue, a young and ambitious architect finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. As he delves deeper into the secrets hidden behind the luxurious facades of the city's elite, he uncovers a shocking truth that could shatter his world. With danger lurking around every corner, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect those he loves and expose the dark underbelly of high society.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Wealth and power, Deception and betrayal, Identity and self-discovery
