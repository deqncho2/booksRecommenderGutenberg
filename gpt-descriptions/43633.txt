Synopsis: In "The Royal Life Guard; or, the flight of the royal family," Alexandre Dumas takes us on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous times of the French Revolution. Set against the backdrop of the suppression of the monarchy, this historical romance follows the perilous escape of the royal family as they navigate treacherous paths and encounter unexpected allies and enemies. With danger lurking at every turn, the fate of the monarchy hangs in the balance, and the reader is left on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to discover the outcome.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Survival, Love, Political upheaval
