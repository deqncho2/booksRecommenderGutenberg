Synopsis: In 'The Mentor: Two Early German Painters, Dürer and Holbein', Frank Jewett Mather takes us on a captivating journey through the lives and works of two influential artists from the Renaissance era. Volume 1, Number 48 of this serial publication delves into the fascinating stories of Albrecht Dürer and Hans Holbein, shedding light on their artistic development, struggles, and triumphs. From Dürer's groundbreaking techniques to Holbein's captivating portraits, this volume offers a glimpse into the world of these master painters, their unique styles, and the impact they had on the art world.

Genres: Art History, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Renaissance art, artistic development, influence and legacy
