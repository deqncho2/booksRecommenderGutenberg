Synopsis: In "The Super Race: An American Problem" by Nearing, Scott, a gripping exploration of the dark underbelly of American society unfolds. The story follows a diverse group of individuals who find themselves entangled in a web of corruption, power struggles, and moral dilemmas. As they navigate through the treacherous landscape of greed and ambition, their lives become intertwined in unexpected ways, leading to shocking revelations and heart-wrenching consequences. With its intricate plot and complex characters, this thought-provoking novel delves into the depths of human nature, challenging readers to question the true cost of success and the lengths some will go to achieve it.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Corruption, Power, Morality, Ambition
