Synopsis: In 'The Golden Bough: A Study of Magic and Religion', James George Frazer takes readers on a captivating journey through the realms of ancient rituals, folklore, and belief systems. From the mystical practices of tribal societies to the enigmatic rites of ancient civilizations, Frazer explores the fascinating connections between magic, religion, and the human psyche. Delving into the realms of myth and superstition, this thought-provoking work unveils the intricate tapestry of human beliefs and the profound impact they have had on shaping our history and culture.

Genres: Anthropology, Mythology, History

Themes: Magic, Religion, Rituals, Belief Systems, Cultural Evolution
