Synopsis: In "Geoffrey de Mandeville: A study of the Anarchy," John Horace Round delves into the captivating life of Geoffrey de Mandeville, a prominent figure during the tumultuous period known as the Anarchy. Set in medieval England, this meticulously researched biography uncovers the enigmatic character of Mandeville, exploring his rise to power, his complex relationships with the monarchy, and his controversial actions that shaped the course of history. As Round unravels the mysteries surrounding Mandeville's life, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through political intrigue, power struggles, and the chaotic landscape of medieval England.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Power and ambition, Loyalty and betrayal, Political turmoil
