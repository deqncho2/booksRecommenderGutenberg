Synopsis: 

In 'With Wolfe in Canada: The Winning of a Continent', G. A. Henty takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of North America during the 18th century. The story follows the adventures of a young Englishman named Ned, who finds himself caught up in the midst of the fierce conflict between the British and the French for control over the vast Canadian territories. As Ned joins the British forces led by the daring General Wolfe, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, face formidable enemies, and confront his own fears and doubts. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this historical novel immerses readers in the epic struggle that shaped the destiny of a continent.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Exploration, Conflict, Identity
