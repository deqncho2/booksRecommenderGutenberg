Synopsis: 
'Pushing to the Front' by Orison Swett Marden is a motivational and self-help book that takes readers on a journey of personal growth and success. Through a collection of inspiring stories and practical advice, Marden explores the power of perseverance, determination, and positive thinking. From overcoming obstacles to achieving one's goals, this book serves as a guide to push oneself to the forefront of life and unlock one's full potential.

Genres: Self-help, Motivational, Personal Development

Themes: Perseverance, Determination, Positive Thinking, Success
