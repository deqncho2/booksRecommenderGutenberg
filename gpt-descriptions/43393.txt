Synopsis: In 'At the Councillor's; or, A Nameless History' by Marlitt, E., a young woman named Hulda finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love when she becomes the governess to the children of a wealthy councillor. As she navigates the complexities of the household, Hulda discovers shocking truths that threaten to unravel the lives of those around her. With her own past haunting her, Hulda must make difficult choices that will determine her future and the fate of those she cares for.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Forbidden love, Secrets and betrayal, Social class and hierarchy
