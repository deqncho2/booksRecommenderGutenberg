Synopsis: In 'The Adhesive Postage Stamp' by Chalmers, Patrick, a mysterious postage stamp sets off a chain of events that leads a young woman on a thrilling journey through time and space. As she unravels the secrets behind the stamp, she discovers hidden connections to her own past and the fate of the world. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Identity, Fate
