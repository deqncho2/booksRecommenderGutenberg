Synopsis: In "Recollections of the Civil War: With the Leaders at Washington and in the Field in the Sixties," Charles A. Dana takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous era of the American Civil War. Through his firsthand experiences and encounters with influential figures, Dana provides a unique perspective on the war, shedding light on the inner workings of the Union government and the strategies employed on the battlefield. From the halls of power in Washington to the front lines of major battles, Dana's recollections offer a gripping account of the war that tore a nation apart.

Genres: Memoir, History, Military

Themes: American Civil War, Leadership, Politics, War Strategy
