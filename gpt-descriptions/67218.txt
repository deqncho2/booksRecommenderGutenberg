Synopsis: In 'The Conscript Mother' by Robert Herrick, a mother's love is put to the ultimate test when her son is conscripted into the military during World War I. As she navigates the challenges of war and the sacrifices it demands, she must confront her own fears and find strength in the face of adversity. This poignant and gripping tale explores the profound bond between a mother and her child, and the resilience of the human spirit in times of turmoil.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Motherhood, Sacrifice, Love, Resilience
