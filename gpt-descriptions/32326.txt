Synopsis: 
In 'Tales of Troy and Greece' by Andrew Lang, embark on a captivating journey through the ancient world of gods, heroes, and epic battles. From the legendary city of Troy to the mythical adventures of Hercules, this collection of stories brings to life the timeless tales of Greek mythology. Discover the heroic deeds of Achilles, the cunning strategies of Odysseus, and the tragic love story of Helen and Paris. With vivid storytelling and rich imagery, Lang transports readers to a world filled with gods and goddesses, mythical creatures, and larger-than-life heroes. Prepare to be enthralled by the epic tales that have captivated audiences for centuries.

Genres: Mythology, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Love and Betrayal, Gods and Mortals
