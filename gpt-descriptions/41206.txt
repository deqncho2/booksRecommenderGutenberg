Synopsis: 
In 'Batting to Win: A Story of College Baseball' by Chadwick, Lester, we follow the journey of a talented college baseball player named Jake as he navigates the challenges of the game, the pressures of competition, and the pursuit of his dreams. As Jake faces both personal and team obstacles, he must learn to overcome adversity, make difficult choices, and discover the true meaning of success. With gripping game sequences, heartfelt relationships, and unexpected twists, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, rooting for Jake and his team until the very last pitch.

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Perseverance, Teamwork, Identity, Ambition
