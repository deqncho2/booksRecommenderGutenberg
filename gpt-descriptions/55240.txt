Synopsis: In 'More Italian Yesterdays', Mrs. Hugh Fraser takes us on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Italy. With her vivid descriptions and charming anecdotes, she invites readers to immerse themselves in the rich history, art, and culture of this enchanting country. From the bustling streets of Rome to the serene beauty of Tuscany, Mrs. Fraser's personal experiences and encounters with locals provide a unique perspective on Italy's past and present. Through her eyes, we discover hidden gems, indulge in delectable cuisine, and unravel the mysteries of Italy's captivating past. 

Genres: Travel, Memoir, Non-fiction

Themes: Cultural exploration, History, Art appreciation
