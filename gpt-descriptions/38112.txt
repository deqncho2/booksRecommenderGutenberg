Synopsis: 
'Mighty Mikko: A Book of Finnish Fairy Tales and Folk Tales' by Fillmore Parker is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that transport readers to the magical world of Finnish folklore. From brave heroes and cunning tricksters to mythical creatures and mystical quests, this book is filled with captivating tales that will leave readers spellbound. Discover the adventures of Mighty Mikko and his extraordinary feats, as well as the timeless wisdom and lessons hidden within these captivating narratives. 

Genres: 
Fairy tales, Folk tales, Fantasy

Themes: 
Bravery, Wisdom, Mythology
