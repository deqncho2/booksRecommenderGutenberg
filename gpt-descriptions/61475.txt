Synopsis: 

In "Byzantine Constantinople, the walls of the city and adjoining historical sites" by Van Millingen, Alexander, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the ancient city of Constantinople. From the majestic walls that protected the city to the fascinating historical sites that surround it, this book unveils the secrets and grandeur of Byzantine Constantinople. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the author brings to life the rich history, architectural marvels, and cultural significance of this remarkable city. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Architecture

Themes: Byzantine history, Architecture and urban planning, Cultural heritage
