Synopsis: In 'The Color of a Great City' by Theodore Dreiser, readers are transported to the bustling streets of New York City, where they follow the lives of several individuals from different walks of life. From the ambitious young artist struggling to make a name for himself, to the wealthy socialite trapped in a loveless marriage, and the immigrant family striving for a better future, their paths intertwine in unexpected ways, revealing the hidden desires, sacrifices, and secrets that lie beneath the surface of the city that never sleeps. As they navigate the challenges and opportunities that the city presents, their lives are forever changed, leaving readers captivated by the vibrant tapestry of human experiences.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Urban Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Love, Social class, Identity, Dreams and aspirations
