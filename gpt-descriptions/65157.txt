Synopsis: In 'The Way of Martha and the Way of Mary' by Stephen Graham, two women from different walks of life find themselves on a transformative journey that challenges their beliefs and forces them to confront their deepest desires. As Martha and Mary navigate through unexpected twists and turns, they discover the power of self-discovery, the complexities of human relationships, and the importance of embracing one's true calling. This captivating tale will keep readers hooked as they unravel the mysteries that lie within the hearts of these two remarkable women.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Relationships, Personal growth
