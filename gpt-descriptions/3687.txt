Synopsis: In the thrilling adventure novel "The Ruby of Kishmoor" by Howard Pyle, a young nobleman named Sir Richard of the Lea finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. When he stumbles upon a hidden treasure, the legendary Ruby of Kishmoor, Sir Richard becomes the target of a nefarious plot that spans across land and sea. With his loyal companions by his side, he must navigate treacherous waters, outsmart cunning adversaries, and uncover the truth behind the ruby's power. As the stakes rise and the danger intensifies, Sir Richard's courage and wit are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Treasure hunting, Loyalty, Betrayal, Heroism
