Synopsis: 

'Royalty in All Ages: The Amusements, Eccentricities, Accomplishments, Superstitions and Frolics of the Kings and Queens of Europe' takes readers on a captivating journey through the intriguing lives of European monarchs. From the opulent courts of ancient times to the modern-day royal families, this book delves into the hidden world of royalty, revealing their amusements, eccentricities, accomplishments, superstitions, and frolics. With fascinating anecdotes and historical accounts, the author paints a vivid picture of the extravagant lifestyles, scandalous affairs, and peculiar habits of kings and queens throughout the ages. 

Genres: History, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Royalty, Power, Intrigue, Human Nature
