Synopsis: In 'Wanderfoot (The Dream Ship)' by Cynthia Stockley, a young girl named Lucy discovers a hidden world beyond her wildest dreams when she stumbles upon a mysterious ship that sails through the skies. As she embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with magical creatures and enchanting landscapes, Lucy must uncover the secrets of the Dream Ship and find her own place in this extraordinary realm.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Discovery, Imagination, Identity
