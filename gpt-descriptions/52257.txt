Synopsis: In "When Sarah Saved the Day" by Singmaster, Elsie, a young girl named Sarah finds herself in a thrilling adventure when she stumbles upon a mysterious artifact. As she unravels its secrets, Sarah must use her wit and courage to overcome challenges and save the day. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what happens next.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Courage, Problem-solving, Friendship
