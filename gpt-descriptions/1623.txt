Synopsis: 
The New Magdalen by Wilkie Collins is a captivating novel that follows the lives of two women, Mercy Merrick and Grace Roseberry, who are both entangled in a web of secrets and deception. When Mercy, a former nurse, crosses paths with Grace, a mysterious and enigmatic woman, their lives become intertwined in unexpected ways. As their stories unfold, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with love, betrayal, and the pursuit of redemption. Will Mercy and Grace be able to escape their pasts and find solace in a world that is quick to judge? 

Genres: 
Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Identity, Redemption, Society's Judgment
