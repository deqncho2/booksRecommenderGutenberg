Synopsis: 

In 'In the School-Room: Chapters in the Philosophy of Education', John S. Hart takes readers on a thought-provoking journey into the world of education. Through a series of insightful chapters, Hart explores the fundamental principles and philosophies that shape the modern school system. From the role of teachers and students to the importance of curriculum and discipline, this book delves into the complexities of education and challenges readers to question their own beliefs and assumptions about learning. With engaging anecdotes and thought-provoking arguments, Hart offers a fresh perspective on the purpose and potential of education, leaving readers inspired to reimagine the possibilities within the classroom.

Genres: Education, Philosophy

Themes: Philosophy of education, Teaching methods, Curriculum development, Student-teacher dynamics
