Synopsis: 
Step into the captivating world of Charles Dana Gibson, a masterful artist whose drawings effortlessly capture the essence of American society in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. From elegant socialites to independent and spirited Gibson Girls, his illustrations provide a fascinating glimpse into the evolving roles of women and the changing dynamics of the era. With a keen eye for detail and a touch of humor, Gibson's drawings transport readers to a bygone era, where they can witness the charm, wit, and complexities of everyday life through his art. 

Genres: Art, Illustration, Social Commentary

Themes: Gender roles, Social class, Cultural shifts
