Synopsis: In 'The Lives of the Twelve Caesars, Volume 03: Tiberius' by Suetonius, delve into the intriguing life and reign of Tiberius, the second Roman Emperor. From his complex relationships with his mother and stepfather to his controversial rule, this book uncovers the secrets, scandals, and political machinations that shaped Tiberius' reign. Discover the dark side of power as you explore the depths of his character and the impact of his decisions on the Roman Empire.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and corruption, Family dynamics, Political intrigue
