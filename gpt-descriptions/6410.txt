Synopsis: 

'Once Aboard the Lugger-- The History of George and his Mary' by A. S. M. Hutchinson is a captivating tale that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the lives of George and Mary. Set against the backdrop of a small coastal village, this story follows the couple as they navigate the complexities of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of happiness. As secrets unravel and unexpected events unfold, George and Mary find themselves facing unforeseen challenges that test the strength of their relationship. With vivid descriptions and a cast of intriguing characters, this novel promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, mystery

Themes: Love and relationships, secrets and betrayal, resilience and personal growth
