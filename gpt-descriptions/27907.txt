Synopsis: In "Hunting the Skipper: The Cruise of the "Seafowl" Sloop," George Manville Fenn takes readers on a thrilling adventure across the high seas. When Captain Dingley and his crew set sail on the "Seafowl" sloop, they embark on a dangerous mission to track down a notorious skipper who has been wreaking havoc on the shipping lanes. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter unexpected challenges, the crew must rely on their wits and courage to outsmart their elusive target. With its gripping plot and vivid descriptions, this nautical tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Pursuit of justice, Perseverance, Camaraderie
