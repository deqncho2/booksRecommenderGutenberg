Synopsis: In 'Alvira: The Heroine of Vesuvius' by A.J. O'Reilly, readers are transported to the ancient city of Pompeii, just days before the catastrophic eruption of Mount Vesuvius. Amidst the bustling streets and lavish villas, we meet Alvira, a young woman with a mysterious past and an extraordinary gift. As the volcano's rumblings grow louder, Alvira finds herself caught in a web of danger and intrigue, where her courage and determination will be put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to save herself and those she loves before it's too late?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Sacrifice, Resilience
