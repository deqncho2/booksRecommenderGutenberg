Synopsis: In 'My Lady of Doubt' by Parrish, Randall, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself caught in a web of mystery and deception when she becomes entangled with a charming and enigmatic stranger. As she delves deeper into his world, she must confront her own doubts and fears, questioning everything she thought she knew about love and trust. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving Evelyn to wonder if she can ever truly find the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Trust, Perception
