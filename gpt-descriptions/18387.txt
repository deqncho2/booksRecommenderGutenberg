Synopsis: In 'The Days of Bruce: A Story from Scottish History. Vol. 1', Grace Aguilar takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of Robert the Bruce, the legendary Scottish king. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue, battles, and personal sacrifices, this historical novel follows the life of Bruce as he navigates the complexities of power, loyalty, and love. As the fate of Scotland hangs in the balance, Bruce's determination and resilience are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to unite his people and secure their freedom? Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance. Themes: Power and Leadership, Loyalty, Love and Sacrifice.
