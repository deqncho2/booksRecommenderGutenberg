Synopsis: In the small town of Maplewood, young Tommy is known as the worst boy in town. Constantly getting into mischief and causing trouble, he seems to have no redeeming qualities. However, when a mysterious stranger arrives in town, Tommy's life takes an unexpected turn. As he navigates through a series of unexpected events, Tommy begins to discover hidden talents and a newfound sense of purpose. Will he be able to prove that he is more than just the worst boy in town?

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Redemption, Self-discovery, Friendship
