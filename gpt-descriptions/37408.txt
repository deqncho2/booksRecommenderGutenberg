Synopsis: In "The Rising Tide of Color Against White World-Supremacy," Lothrop Stoddard explores the global shift in power dynamics and the potential consequences for the white race. Through a thought-provoking analysis of historical events and demographic trends, Stoddard presents a compelling argument about the rising influence of non-white populations and its implications for the future. This book delves into the complexities of race, politics, and the clash of civilizations, offering a unique perspective on a topic that continues to shape our world today.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Sociology

Themes: Race relations, Global power dynamics, Demographic shifts
