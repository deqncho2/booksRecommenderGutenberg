Synopsis: In 'The Mentor: Russian Music, Vol. 4, Num. 18, Serial No. 118, November 1, 1916' by Henry T. Finck, readers are transported to the vibrant world of Russian music during the early 20th century. Through captivating storytelling and insightful analysis, Finck delves into the lives and works of renowned Russian composers, shedding light on their unique artistic journeys and the profound impact they had on the world of music. From the passionate melodies of Tchaikovsky to the innovative compositions of Rimsky-Korsakov, this edition of The Mentor offers a fascinating exploration of the rich tapestry of Russian musical heritage.

Genres: Music history, Biographical, Cultural

Themes: Russian music, Artistic expression, Influence of composers
