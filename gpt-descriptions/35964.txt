Synopsis: 
In "The Go Ahead Boys and the Mysterious Old House" by Kay, Ross, three adventurous boys stumble upon an abandoned house that holds a dark secret. As they delve deeper into the mysteries surrounding the old house, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling and dangerous adventure. With each clue they uncover, the boys become more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means risking their own safety. Will they be able to solve the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Curiosity, Secrets
