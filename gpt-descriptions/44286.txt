Synopsis: In "If Youth but Knew!" by Castle, Egerton and Castle, Agnes, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she becomes entangled with a mysterious stranger. As she delves deeper into his world, she discovers shocking truths that challenge her perception of love, trust, and the power of youth. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Love, Trust, Coming of Age
