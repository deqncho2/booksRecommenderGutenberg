Synopsis: In 'The Book of Camp-Lore and Woodcraft', Daniel Carter Beard takes readers on an adventurous journey into the world of outdoor survival and wilderness skills. Filled with practical tips, captivating stories, and detailed illustrations, this book serves as a comprehensive guide for anyone seeking to master the art of camp-life and woodcraft. From building shelters and starting fires to tracking animals and navigating through the wilderness, Beard's expertise and enthusiasm shine through every page, inspiring readers to embrace the wonders of nature and develop their own outdoor skills.

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, Survival

Themes: Wilderness skills, Outdoor education, Nature appreciation
