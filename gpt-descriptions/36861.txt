Synopsis: In 'The Green Casket, and other stories' by Molesworth, Mrs., readers are transported to a world where mysterious events unfold, secrets are revealed, and unexpected twists keep them on the edge of their seats. From a peculiar green casket that holds a haunting secret to a young girl's encounter with a mischievous ghost, each story in this collection is filled with suspense, intrigue, and a touch of the supernatural. As readers delve into the pages of this captivating book, they will be drawn into a realm where the ordinary becomes extraordinary, and where the line between reality and fantasy blurs.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, the power of imagination, the thin veil between the seen and unseen.
