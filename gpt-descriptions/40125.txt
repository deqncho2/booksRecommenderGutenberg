Synopsis: In 'The Railroad Problem' by Edward Hungerford, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious event occurs at the local railroad station. As the townspeople grapple with the consequences, they uncover dark secrets and unexpected alliances, leading to a thrilling journey of suspense and intrigue. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning who can be trusted and what lies beneath the surface of this seemingly ordinary town.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Small-town secrets, Trust and betrayal
