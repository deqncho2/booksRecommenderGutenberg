Synopsis: In 'Sir Gibbie' by George MacDonald, follow the extraordinary journey of a young boy named Gibbie, who despite his challenging circumstances, possesses a heart full of compassion and a spirit that refuses to be broken. Set in the rugged landscapes of Scotland, this heartwarming tale takes readers on a transformative adventure as Gibbie encounters various characters and navigates the complexities of life, ultimately teaching us the power of love, forgiveness, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Compassion, Resilience, Redemption, Forgiveness
