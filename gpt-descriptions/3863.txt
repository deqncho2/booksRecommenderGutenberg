Synopsis:
In the fourth volume of "Memoirs of Louis XIV and His Court and of the Regency," Saint-Simon, the duc de Saint-Simon, continues his captivating account of the French court during the reign of Louis XIV and the subsequent regency. This volume delves into the intricate power struggles, political maneuverings, and scandalous affairs that shaped the court's dynamics. From the rise of the Duke of Orleans to the intriguing secrets of the royal mistresses, Saint-Simon's memoirs offer a fascinating glimpse into the opulent and often treacherous world of the French monarchy.

Genres:
Memoir, Historical, Biography

Themes:
Power and politics, Court intrigue, Royal scandals
