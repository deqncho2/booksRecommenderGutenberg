Synopsis: In 'The Pools of Silence' by H. De Vere Stacpoole, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey to a remote island in the Pacific Ocean. As she explores the lush landscapes and encounters the enigmatic inhabitants, Mary becomes entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love. With each passing day, the island's mysteries deepen, and Mary finds herself torn between her desire for adventure and the consequences of uncovering the truth. This captivating tale of romance, intrigue, and self-discovery will transport readers to a world where silence holds the key to unlocking the greatest mysteries of the heart.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Forbidden love, Self-discovery, Secrets and mysteries
