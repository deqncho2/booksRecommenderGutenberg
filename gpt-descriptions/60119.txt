Synopsis: In "Isis very much unveiled, being the story of the great Mahatma hoax," Garrett, Fydell Edmund takes readers on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of spiritualism and deception. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows a renowned investigator as he delves into the enigmatic case of the Mahatma, a supposed spiritual leader with extraordinary powers. As the investigator unravels the layers of this elaborate hoax, he uncovers a web of secrets, manipulation, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each revelation, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Spiritualism, Truth vs. Illusion
