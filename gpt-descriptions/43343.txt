Synopsis: In 'The Vegetable Lamb of Tartary: A Curious Fable of the Cotton Plant', author Henry Lee takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious world of the cotton plant. This unique fable explores the origins and history of cotton, while also delving into the intriguing concept of a mythical creature known as the Vegetable Lamb. As readers follow the adventures of the Vegetable Lamb, they will uncover fascinating insights into the cotton trade and its impact on society. With its blend of folklore, history, and botanical exploration, this book offers a captivating and thought-provoking exploration of the cotton plant and its significance.

Genres: Historical fiction, folklore, botanical exploration

Themes: Origins and history, mythology and folklore, societal impact
