Synopsis:
In 'Dick's Desertion: A Boy's Adventures in Canadian Forests: A Tale of the Early Settlement of Ontario', Marjorie L. C. Pickthall takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed Canadian wilderness. The story follows young Dick as he navigates the challenges of survival, encounters mysterious characters, and uncovers hidden secrets in the vast forests of Ontario. As Dick's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test, readers will be captivated by the thrilling adventures that unfold in this captivating tale.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Wilderness survival, Exploration, Friendship, Self-discovery
