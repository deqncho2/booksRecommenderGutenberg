Synopsis: In 'Turn About Eleanor' by Ethel M. Kelley, a young woman named Eleanor finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits a mysterious estate from a distant relative. As she delves into the history of the estate, Eleanor uncovers a hidden world filled with dark family secrets, forbidden love, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, Eleanor's life becomes increasingly entangled in a dangerous game where trust is a luxury she cannot afford. Will she be able to navigate the treacherous path laid before her, or will she become another victim of the estate's haunting past?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Love and sacrifice
