Synopsis: In "Ten years after: a reminder" by Philip Gibbs, a mysterious event unfolds, ten years after a life-altering incident that left the world in chaos. As the protagonist navigates through a dystopian society, they uncover shocking secrets and encounter unexpected allies and enemies. With time running out, they must confront their past and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of humanity. This gripping tale of survival, redemption, and the power of memory will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Memory, Identity, Survival, Redemption
