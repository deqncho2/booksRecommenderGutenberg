Synopsis: In "The Circus Comes to Town" by Mitchell, Lebbeus, a small, ordinary town is forever transformed when a mysterious circus arrives, bringing with it a sense of wonder and magic. As the townspeople become enthralled by the circus's enchanting performances, they soon discover that there is more to this spectacle than meets the eye. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, the arrival of the circus sets off a chain of events that will challenge the town's perceptions and change their lives forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Transformation, Perception, Mystery, Magic
