Synopsis: In the city of Vienna, corruption and immorality run rampant, prompting the Duke to temporarily leave and appoint a strict deputy, Angelo, to restore order. However, when Angelo's power goes to his head, he begins to enforce the law with an iron fist, leading to a series of moral dilemmas and unexpected twists. As the lives of the citizens hang in the balance, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and justice is put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Tragicomedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Morality, Justice, Power, Deception, Mercy
