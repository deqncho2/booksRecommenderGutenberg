Synopsis: In 'The Village' by Ivan Alekseevich Bunin, a young man returns to his childhood village after many years of living in the city. As he reconnects with his family and old friends, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, desires, and forbidden love. The idyllic rural setting hides a multitude of complex emotions and hidden truths, leading to a series of unexpected events that will forever change the lives of those involved.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Rural life, Family dynamics, Forbidden love, Secrets and deception
