Synopsis: 

'Grand Moving Diorama of Hindostan: Displaying the Scenery of the Hoogly, the Bhagirathi, and the Ganges, from Fort William, Bengal, to Gangoutri, in the Himalaya' takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes of India. Fanny Parkes, the author, invites us to witness the breathtaking beauty of the Hoogly, Bhagirathi, and Ganges rivers as she travels from Fort William in Bengal to Gangoutri in the Himalaya. Through vivid descriptions and stunning imagery, Parkes paints a vivid picture of the awe-inspiring scenery she encounters along the way.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Nature's Majesty, Cultural Diversity
