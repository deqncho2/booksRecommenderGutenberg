Synopsis: In 'The suppressed Gospels and Epistles of the original New Testament of Jesus the Christ, Volume 3, Infancy of Jesus Christ' by William Wake, delve into the lesser-known stories of Jesus' early years. Uncover the mysteries surrounding his childhood, from miraculous events to encounters with supernatural beings. This volume sheds light on the extraordinary events that shaped the life of Jesus before his ministry, offering a fresh perspective on the beloved figure of Christianity.

Genres: Religious literature, Historical fiction, Spiritual

Themes: Divine intervention, Miracles, Spiritual growth
