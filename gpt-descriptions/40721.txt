Synopsis: In 'Dan Carter and the Cub Honor' by Mildred A. Wirt, a thrilling adventure unfolds as Dan Carter, a young and determined detective, finds himself entangled in a mysterious case involving a missing treasure and a secret society. With his quick wit and resourcefulness, Dan must navigate through a web of clues and danger to uncover the truth and restore honor. As the stakes rise, Dan's courage and determination are put to the test, leading to a heart-pounding climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Honor, Friendship, Perseverance
