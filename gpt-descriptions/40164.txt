Synopsis: 

Step into the captivating world of opera as you embark on a journey through time with "History of the Opera from its Origin in Italy to the present Time: With Anecdotes of the Most Celebrated Composers and Vocalists of Europe" by H. Sutherland Edwards. Delve into the origins of this enchanting art form and discover the fascinating anecdotes surrounding the most renowned composers and vocalists of Europe. From the birthplace of opera in Italy to its evolution and influence across the continent, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the rich history and captivating stories that have shaped the opera as we know it today.

Genres: Historical, Music, Biography

Themes: Opera, Music history, Cultural influence
