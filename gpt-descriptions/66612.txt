Synopsis: In a world gone mad, where chaos reigns and sanity is a rare commodity, a mysterious figure emerges. Wanted: One Sane Man follows the journey of a lone individual who possesses an uncanny ability to see through the madness and bring order to the chaos. As he navigates through a dystopian landscape filled with eccentric characters and bizarre situations, he becomes the target of both those who fear his sanity and those who seek to exploit it. With his wit, intelligence, and unwavering determination, he must unravel the secrets of this deranged world and find his place in it, all while trying to preserve his own sanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Satire

Themes: Sanity vs. Insanity, Individuality, Society's Conformity
