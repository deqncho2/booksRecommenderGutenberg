Synopsis: In "Utopia? Never!" by Thomas M. Disch, a seemingly ordinary man named John finds himself thrust into a bizarre and unsettling world where everything appears to be perfect. As he navigates this seemingly utopian society, John begins to question the true nature of perfection and the cost it exacts on individuality and freedom. With each revelation, he becomes increasingly determined to uncover the dark secrets lurking beneath the surface of this seemingly flawless existence.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Utopia vs. Dystopia, Individuality, Freedom, Conformity, Human Nature
