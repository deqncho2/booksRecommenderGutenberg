Synopsis:
In 'Timothy Crump's Ward: A Story of American Life' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Timothy Crump, a hardworking and kind-hearted young man who finds himself in a challenging situation. When Timothy's wealthy uncle unexpectedly passes away, he becomes the guardian of his uncle's young ward, Grace. As Timothy takes on the responsibility of caring for Grace, he faces numerous obstacles and encounters individuals who are determined to undermine his efforts. With determination and resilience, Timothy must navigate the complexities of American society and prove that he is worthy of the trust placed upon him. Will Timothy be able to overcome the challenges and provide a better life for Grace?

Genres: 
- Coming-of-age
- Adventure
- Social commentary

Themes:
- Hard work and perseverance
- Social class and inequality
- Morality and integrity
