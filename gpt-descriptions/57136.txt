Synopsis: In "Science from an Easy Chair," Sir Edwin Ray Lankester takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders of science, exploring various fascinating topics from the comfort of his easy chair. From the mysteries of the natural world to the intricacies of human anatomy, Lankester's engaging and accessible writing style brings complex scientific concepts to life, making them accessible to readers of all backgrounds. With a blend of personal anecdotes, historical references, and thought-provoking insights, this book offers a unique perspective on the marvels of the scientific world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Popular Science

Themes: Wonder of the natural world, Scientific exploration, Accessibility of science
