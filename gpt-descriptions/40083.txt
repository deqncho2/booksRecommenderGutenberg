Synopsis: In 'Mollie's Prince: A Novel' by Carey, Rosa Nouchette, we are introduced to Mollie, a young woman with dreams of finding her own Prince Charming. Set in the late 19th century, Mollie's journey takes her through the ups and downs of love, society's expectations, and the pursuit of true happiness. As she navigates the complexities of relationships and the challenges of her time, Mollie's Prince offers a captivating tale of romance, self-discovery, and the power of following one's heart.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Relationships, Society's Expectations, Self-Discovery
