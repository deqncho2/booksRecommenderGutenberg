Synopsis: In 'The Rambler Club's aeroplane', a group of adventurous friends stumble upon a mysterious aircraft hidden deep in the woods. As they embark on a thrilling journey to uncover its secrets, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger and intrigue. With each discovery, their lives are forever changed, and they must confront their deepest fears to unravel the truth behind the enigmatic aeroplane.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Courage, Secrets
