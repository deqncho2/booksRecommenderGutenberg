Synopsis: In 'Italian Yesterdays, vol. 2', Mrs. Hugh Fraser takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and enchanting landscapes of Italy. Through her vivid storytelling, she unveils hidden tales of ancient ruins, picturesque villages, and legendary figures that have shaped the country's past. From the bustling streets of Rome to the serene beauty of Tuscany, this volume immerses readers in the vibrant tapestry of Italian culture, art, and traditions. With each chapter, Mrs. Fraser unveils a new layer of Italy's captivating past, leaving readers yearning to explore the country's timeless wonders.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Italian history, Cultural heritage, Exploration and discovery
