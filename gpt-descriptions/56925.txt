Synopsis: In 'Deadlock: Pilgrimage, Volume 6', the gripping conclusion to Dorothy M. Richardson's acclaimed series, the protagonist, Miriam Henderson, embarks on a transformative journey of self-discovery. As she navigates the complexities of her relationships and confronts the societal expectations placed upon her, Miriam finds herself at a crossroads, torn between duty and desire. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century England, this compelling narrative explores the depths of human emotions and the pursuit of personal freedom.

Genres: Literary fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical fiction

Themes: Identity, Gender roles, Independence, Social conventions
