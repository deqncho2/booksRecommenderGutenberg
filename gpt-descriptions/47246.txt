Synopsis: In 'A Deal with the Devil' by Eden Phillpotts, a young man finds himself entangled in a mysterious pact that leads him down a treacherous path. As he delves deeper into the dark secrets of his family's past, he must confront the consequences of his choices and grapple with the true nature of evil. With each twist and turn, the protagonist's sanity and soul hang in the balance, making this a gripping tale of suspense and moral dilemmas.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Redemption, Family Secrets
