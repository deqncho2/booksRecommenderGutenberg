Synopsis: In 'John Dough and the Cherub', follow the extraordinary adventures of a living gingerbread man named John Dough and his loyal companion, Chick the Cherub. From escaping the clutches of a wicked queen to encountering fantastical creatures in a hidden kingdom, John Dough's journey is filled with danger, magic, and unexpected friendships. As he strives to find his place in a world that sees him as nothing more than a tasty treat, John Dough's resilience and bravery will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Courage
