Synopsis: 
'Tales from Gorky' is a captivating collection of short stories by the renowned Russian author, Maksim Gorky. Each tale delves into the lives of ordinary people, exploring their struggles, dreams, and the complexities of human nature. From the bustling streets of St. Petersburg to the remote villages of rural Russia, Gorky's vivid storytelling transports readers to a world filled with raw emotions, unexpected twists, and profound insights. Through his masterful prose, Gorky paints a vivid portrait of the human condition, revealing the beauty and tragedy that coexist in our everyday lives.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Literary Fiction

Themes: Human nature, Social inequality, Dreams and aspirations, Resilience, Realism
