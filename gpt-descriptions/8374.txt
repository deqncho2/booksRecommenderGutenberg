Synopsis:
'Alton Locke, Tailor and Poet: An Autobiography' follows the life of Alton Locke, a young tailor and aspiring poet living in early 19th-century London. As Alton navigates the challenges of his working-class background, he becomes increasingly disillusioned with the social and political injustices of the time. Through his encounters with various characters, including fellow poets, politicians, and social reformers, Alton embarks on a journey of self-discovery and rebellion against the oppressive systems that confine him. This gripping autobiography delves into the struggles of a passionate artist striving for recognition and justice in a society that often overlooks the voices of the marginalized.

Genres:
Autobiographical novel, Social commentary, Historical fiction

Themes:
Social inequality, Political activism, Artistic expression, Class struggle
