Synopsis: 
'For Every Music Lover: A Series of Practical Essays on Music' by Moore, Aubertine Woodward is a captivating collection of essays that delves into the world of music, offering practical insights and thought-provoking perspectives. From exploring the power of music to its influence on emotions and society, this book takes readers on a journey through the various aspects of music, providing a deeper understanding and appreciation for this art form.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Essays

Themes: The power of music, Emotional impact of music, Societal influence of music
