Synopsis: In 'Brothers in Arms' by E. Alexander Powell, a gripping tale unfolds as two brothers find themselves on opposite sides of a conflict that threatens to tear their family apart. Set against the backdrop of World War II, this heart-wrenching story explores the complexities of loyalty, sacrifice, and the enduring bond between siblings. As the war rages on, the brothers must navigate their own personal battles while grappling with the moral dilemmas that arise in times of war. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, 'Brothers in Arms' takes readers on an emotional journey that will leave them questioning the true meaning of brotherhood.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Sacrifice, Family, Morality, Conflict
