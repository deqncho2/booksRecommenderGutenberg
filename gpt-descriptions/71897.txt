Synopsis: 
In 'History for ready reference, Volume 6: Recent history - 1894-5 to 1901 A-Z', J.N. Larned takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous years of the late 19th and early 20th centuries. From political upheavals to technological advancements, this volume provides a comprehensive and engaging account of the significant events and influential figures that shaped this era. With meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Larned offers readers a unique opportunity to delve into the past and gain a deeper understanding of the world during this transformative period.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, Reference

Themes: 
History, Politics, Technological advancements
