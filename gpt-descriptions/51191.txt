Synopsis: 
'Nooks and Corners of Lancashire and Cheshire: A Wayfarer's Notes in the Palatine Counties, Historical, Legendary, Genealogical, and Descriptive' takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden gems and lesser-known aspects of Lancashire and Cheshire. James Croston, an avid traveler and historian, delves into the rich history, enchanting legends, intricate genealogy, and picturesque landscapes of these two counties. From ancient castles and mysterious ruins to quaint villages and bustling towns, this book uncovers the fascinating stories and hidden treasures that lie within the nooks and corners of Lancashire and Cheshire.

Genres: Travel, History, Genealogy, Non-fiction

Themes: Local history, Legends and folklore, Heritage and culture, Exploration and discovery
