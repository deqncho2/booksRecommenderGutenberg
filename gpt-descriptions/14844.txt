Synopsis: 
In 'The Taming of Red Butte Western' by Francis Lynde, a young and ambitious railroad engineer named Larry Red King finds himself caught in a dangerous web of deceit and treachery as he tries to complete the construction of a crucial railway line. With his determination and resourcefulness, Larry must navigate through a series of unexpected challenges, including a mysterious murder, a cunning rival, and a beautiful woman who may hold the key to his success or downfall. As the stakes rise and tensions mount, Larry must rely on his wits and courage to tame not only the wild terrain of Red Butte Western but also the untamed forces that threaten to derail his dreams.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Love and Betrayal
