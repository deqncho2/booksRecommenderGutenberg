Synopsis: 

In 'The zoology of the voyage of H.M.S. Beagle [vol. 2 of 5]: Mammalia', George Robert Waterhouse takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse world of mammals discovered during the famous voyage of the H.M.S. Beagle. From the enchanting marsupials of Australia to the majestic big cats of South America, this volume delves into the fascinating lives and behaviors of these extraordinary creatures. With vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, Waterhouse brings to life the incredible diversity of mammalian species encountered during this historic expedition.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Zoology

Themes: Exploration, Biodiversity, Animal Behavior
