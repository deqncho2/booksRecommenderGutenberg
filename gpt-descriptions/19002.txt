Synopsis: 
In "Alice's Adventures Under Ground: Being a facsimile of the original Ms. book afterwards developed into 'Alice's Adventures in Wonderland'," Lewis Carroll takes readers on a whimsical journey through a fantastical world. Follow Alice as she falls down a rabbit hole and encounters peculiar characters, engaging in nonsensical conversations and encountering bizarre situations. This facsimile of Carroll's original manuscript provides a unique glimpse into the genesis of the beloved classic, "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland."

Genres: 
Fantasy, Children's Literature, Adventure

Themes: 
Imagination, Absurdity, Identity
