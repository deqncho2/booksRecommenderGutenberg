Synopsis: In 'Monograms & Ciphers' by Carlton Studio/ Turbayne, A. A. (Albert Angus), a mysterious collection of monograms and ciphers is discovered, leading the reader on a captivating journey through the intricate world of secret codes and hidden messages. As the protagonist delves deeper into the enigmatic symbols, they uncover a web of intrigue and danger that spans across time and continents. With each deciphered clue, the stakes rise, and the race to unlock the ultimate secret becomes a thrilling battle of wits. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the cryptic monograms and ciphers.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cryptography, Secrets, Intrigue
