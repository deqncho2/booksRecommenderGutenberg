Synopsis: In "1900; or, The last President," Lockwood Ingersoll takes readers on a thrilling journey into a world where political chaos and societal upheaval collide. Set in the year 1900, the story follows the unexpected events that unfold after the election of a controversial president. As the nation grapples with uncertainty and unrest, a group of individuals find themselves at the center of a mysterious and potentially cataclysmic situation. With secrets, conspiracies, and a sense of impending doom, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Political thriller, dystopian fiction, mystery

Themes: Political turmoil, societal collapse, conspiracy, power dynamics
