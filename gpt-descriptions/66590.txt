Synopsis: In 'Problem Planet' by R. R. Winterbotham, a group of scientists embarks on a daring mission to a distant planet, only to discover that it holds a dark secret that threatens not only their lives but the fate of the entire universe. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter mysterious beings, they must unravel the enigma of this problem planet before it's too late. With heart-pounding action and mind-bending twists, this gripping sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Consequences of Scientific Discovery
