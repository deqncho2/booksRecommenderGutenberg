Synopsis: In 'The Furious Rose' by Evans Dean, a young woman named Rose finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter that leads her on a thrilling journey. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Rose discovers a hidden world filled with danger, romance, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, she must navigate treacherous waters and make choices that will determine not only her own fate but also the fate of those she holds dear. Will Rose uncover the truth before it's too late, or will she become entangled in a web of lies that could destroy everything she loves?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and sacrifice
