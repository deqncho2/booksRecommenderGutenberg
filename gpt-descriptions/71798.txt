Synopsis: Delight and Power in Speech is a comprehensive guide that offers a fresh and practical approach to enhancing both silent and oral reading, as well as private and public speaking. Written by James, George Wharton and Nattkemper, Leonard G., this book presents a new method that aims to captivate and engage readers while improving their efficiency in communication. It includes a diverse collection of carefully selected prose and poetry, along with insightful chapters on memory cultivation and after-dinner speaking. Delve into this universal dramatic reader and discover the secrets to mastering the art of speech.

Genres: Self-help, Education, Communication

Themes: Public speaking, Reading skills, Memory cultivation, Communication efficiency
