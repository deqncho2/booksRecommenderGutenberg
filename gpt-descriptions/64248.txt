Synopsis: In the midst of World War I, a group of British soldiers find themselves stationed in France, grappling with the harsh realities of war and the complexities of human relationships. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of the war-torn country, their lives become intertwined with a mysterious woman who challenges their beliefs and forces them to confront their own desires and fears. Through vivid prose and intricate character development, Ford Madox Ford paints a haunting portrait of love, loyalty, and the profound impact of war on the human psyche.

Genres: Historical fiction, war fiction, literary fiction

Themes: Love and desire, the effects of war, loyalty and betrayal, the human condition
