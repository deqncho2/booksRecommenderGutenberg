Synopsis: In 'Lord Alistair's Rebellion' by Upward, Allen, a young nobleman finds himself torn between duty and desire as he becomes entangled in a dangerous plot against the crown. With political intrigue, forbidden romance, and a secret society lurking in the shadows, Lord Alistair must navigate treacherous waters to protect his family and uncover the truth. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the fate of the kingdom hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Duty and honor, Betrayal, Power struggles, Forbidden love
