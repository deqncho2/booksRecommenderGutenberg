Synopsis: In 'The Search' by Grace Livingston Hill, a young woman named Lois is faced with a sudden and unexpected tragedy that turns her world upside down. As she embarks on a journey to find answers and seek solace, she encounters a mysterious stranger who offers her a glimmer of hope. Together, they navigate through the challenges of loss, love, and faith, leading Lois to discover the true meaning of redemption and the power of second chances.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Inspirational

Themes: Grief and healing, Redemption, Faith and spirituality
