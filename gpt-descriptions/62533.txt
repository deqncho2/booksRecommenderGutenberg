Synopsis: In 'The Saint of the Speedway' by Cullum, Ridgwell, a young and talented race car driver finds himself entangled in a web of danger and deceit when he becomes the target of a mysterious criminal organization. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he must navigate treacherous tracks, unexpected alliances, and a love that could either save him or destroy him. With adrenaline-pumping action and heart-stopping suspense, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last lap.

Genres: Action, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Danger and Deceit, Pursuit of Truth, Love and Betrayal
