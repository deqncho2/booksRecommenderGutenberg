Synopsis: In 'Children of the Bush' by Henry Lawson, a group of children find themselves lost in the vast Australian bush, facing the harsh realities of survival and the unexpected challenges that come their way. As they navigate through the unforgiving landscape, they form an unbreakable bond and discover the strength within themselves that they never knew existed. This gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey of resilience, friendship, and the indomitable spirit of youth.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Australian Literature

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Resilience
