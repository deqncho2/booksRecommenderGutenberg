Synopsis: In 'The Outcasts' by William Alexander Fraser, a group of misfit teenagers find themselves thrown together in a remote boarding school, where they must navigate the challenges of friendship, secrets, and the supernatural. As they uncover the mysteries surrounding the school and their own hidden abilities, they realize they are not just outcasts, but part of something much bigger. With danger lurking around every corner, they must band together to uncover the truth and save themselves. 

Genres: Young Adult, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Acceptance, Supernatural Abilities
