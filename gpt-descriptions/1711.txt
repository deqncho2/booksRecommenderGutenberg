Synopsis: 

In 'Child of Storm' by H. Rider Haggard, a young Englishman named Leonard Outram finds himself embroiled in a dangerous and thrilling adventure in the heart of Africa. As he navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters mysterious tribes, Leonard becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love. With his life hanging in the balance, he must uncover the truth behind a hidden secret that could change the course of history. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Colonialism, Love and Sacrifice
