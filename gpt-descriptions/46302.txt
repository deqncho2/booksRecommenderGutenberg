Synopsis: In 'Riding and Driving' by Collier, Price/ Anderson, Edward L., a chance encounter between two strangers leads to an unexpected journey filled with adventure, danger, and self-discovery. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and face unforeseen obstacles, their lives intertwine in ways they could never have imagined. With each passing mile, they uncover secrets that challenge their beliefs and force them to confront their deepest fears. Will they find the courage to embrace the unknown and forge a new path, or will they be forever trapped in the shadows of their past?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Redemption, Overcoming adversity
