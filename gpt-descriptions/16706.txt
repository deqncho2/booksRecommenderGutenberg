Synopsis: In 'A Sixth-Century Fragment of the Letters of Pliny the Younger', Rand and Lowe delve into the fascinating world of ancient manuscripts, uncovering the secrets of six leaves from an uncial manuscript preserved in the Pierpont Morgan Library in New York. Through meticulous analysis and historical context, they shed light on the life and writings of Pliny the Younger, a prominent figure in ancient Rome. This captivating study offers a glimpse into the past, revealing the significance of these fragments and their contribution to our understanding of Pliny's letters.

Genres: Historical research, Manuscript studies, Ancient literature

Themes: Ancient Rome, Epistolary writing, Manuscript preservation
