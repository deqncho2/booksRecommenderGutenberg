Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Fourteenth, or, the Buckinghamshire Regiment of Foot: Containing an Account of the Formation of the Regiment in 1685, and of Its Subsequent Services to 1845', Richard Cannon delves into the captivating history of the Fourteenth Regiment of Foot, also known as the Buckinghamshire Regiment. From its humble beginnings in 1685 to its remarkable achievements up until 1845, this book unveils the untold stories of bravery, sacrifice, and resilience that shaped the regiment's legacy. Cannon's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life the battles, campaigns, and pivotal moments that defined the regiment, making this a must-read for history enthusiasts and military buffs alike.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Resilience, Military Strategy
