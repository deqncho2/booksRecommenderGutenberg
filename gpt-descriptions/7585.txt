Synopsis: 

'Widger's Quotes and Images from A Woodland Queen by André Theuriet: The French Immortals: Quotes and Images' is a captivating collection that offers a glimpse into the enchanting world of André Theuriet's novel, 'A Woodland Queen'. Through carefully selected quotes and accompanying images, this book transports readers to a mesmerizing woodland setting, where they will encounter a cast of intriguing characters and witness the unfolding of a captivating story. 

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Nature, Love, Intrigue
