Synopsis: In 'Library Notes' by Addison Peale Russell, a mysterious librarian discovers a hidden collection of notes left by previous patrons, each containing a secret that could change their lives forever. As she delves deeper into the enigmatic world of these notes, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected connections. With each note she uncovers, the librarian finds herself drawn into a thrilling adventure that challenges her perception of reality and forces her to confront her own past. As the secrets of the library unfold, she must race against time to unravel the truth before it consumes her. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Identity, Fate, Redemption
