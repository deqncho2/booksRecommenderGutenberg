Synopsis: In 'A Man's Woman' by Frank Norris, we are introduced to Vandover, a young and ambitious artist living in San Francisco during the late 19th century. As he navigates the bohemian lifestyle of the city, Vandover becomes infatuated with a captivating woman named Flossie. Their passionate and tumultuous relationship takes unexpected turns, leading Vandover down a path of self-discovery and moral dilemmas. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society, this gripping tale explores the complexities of love, desire, and the pursuit of personal fulfillment.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Morality and ethics, Self-discovery
