Synopsis: In 'A World of Girls: The Story of a School' by L. T. Meade, readers are transported to the vibrant and diverse world of Miss Blackburn's School for Girls. Follow the lives of a group of spirited young women as they navigate the challenges of friendship, love, and personal growth. From secret societies to unexpected romances, this captivating tale explores the bonds that form among these girls and the transformative power of education.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Romance

Themes: Friendship, Education, Self-discovery
