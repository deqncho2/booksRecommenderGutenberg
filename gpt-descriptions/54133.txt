Synopsis: In 'A Little Queen of Hearts: An International Story' by Ruth Ogden, follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Alice as she embarks on a thrilling adventure across different countries. From the bustling streets of New York City to the enchanting landscapes of Europe, Alice encounters a cast of intriguing characters and faces unexpected challenges. As she navigates through unfamiliar territories, Alice discovers the power of friendship, the importance of embracing diversity, and the resilience of the human spirit. This heartwarming tale will transport readers to a world filled with wonder, where the bonds of love and courage transcend borders.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Diversity, Resilience
