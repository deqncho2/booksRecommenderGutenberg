Synopsis: In "Rajah Brooke: The Englishman as Ruler of an Eastern State," Spenser St. John takes us on a captivating journey through the life of Sir James Brooke, an Englishman who becomes the ruler of an eastern state. From his adventurous exploits in the jungles of Borneo to his struggles in navigating the complexities of power and authority, this remarkable true story unveils the extraordinary life of a man who defied conventions and left an indelible mark on history.

Genres: Biography, Adventure, History

Themes: Colonialism, Power and Authority, Cultural Exchange
