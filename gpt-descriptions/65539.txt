Synopsis: 
'Boy Scouts in the White Mountains: The Story of a Long Hike' follows a group of adventurous Boy Scouts as they embark on a challenging journey through the stunning White Mountains. Led by their experienced scoutmaster, the boys face numerous obstacles and tests of their skills and determination. As they navigate treacherous terrains, encounter unexpected wildlife, and confront their own fears, the boys learn valuable lessons about teamwork, resilience, and the power of nature. This thrilling tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Outdoor

Themes: Teamwork, Resilience, Nature's Power
