Synopsis: 
In "Ran Away to Sea" by Mayne Reid, a young boy named Harry is captivated by the tales of adventure and freedom that sailors bring to his small coastal village. Yearning for a life beyond the mundane, he decides to run away and join a ship's crew. As Harry embarks on his maritime journey, he encounters thrilling escapades, dangerous encounters, and unexpected friendships. From battling treacherous storms to exploring exotic lands, Harry's courage and determination are put to the test. Will he find the excitement and purpose he seeks, or will the harsh realities of life at sea prove too much for him?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Independence, Self-discovery
