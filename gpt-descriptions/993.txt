Synopsis: In "Malbone: An Oldport Romance," Thomas Wentworth Higginson takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Oldport, a coastal town filled with love, passion, and secrets. The story revolves around Malbone, a talented artist torn between his artistic pursuits and his heart's desires. As he navigates the complexities of love and the constraints of society, Malbone finds himself entangled in a web of emotions, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Set against the backdrop of picturesque landscapes and the vibrant social scene of Oldport, this novel explores the depths of human emotions and the choices we make in the pursuit of love and happiness.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Artistic Pursuits, Social Constraints
