Synopsis: 

In 'A History of Nursery Rhymes' by Green, Percy B., embark on a fascinating journey through time as the author unravels the origins and evolution of beloved nursery rhymes. From the enchanting tales of Mother Goose to the mysterious origins of popular rhymes like "Humpty Dumpty" and "Jack and Jill," this captivating book delves into the rich history and cultural significance of these timeless verses. As Green uncovers surprising connections and hidden meanings behind these seemingly simple rhymes, readers will be transported to a world of whimsy and wonder, where nursery rhymes hold secrets waiting to be discovered.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Literature

Themes: Folklore, Cultural Significance, Language and Communication
