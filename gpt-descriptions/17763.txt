Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of the Hasty Arrow' by Anna Katharine Green, a seemingly perfect society is shattered when a young woman is found dead with an arrow through her heart. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the list of suspects grows longer. With twists and turns at every corner, the truth becomes increasingly elusive, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking conclusion.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice, Society's Facade
