Synopsis: 

In 'Report on the Migration of Birds in the Spring and Autumn of 1882. Fourth Report', Cordeaux, Harvie-Brown, Barrington, and More delve into the fascinating world of bird migration during the year 1882. Through meticulous observations and detailed analysis, they uncover remarkable patterns and behaviors exhibited by various bird species during their journeys. From the awe-inspiring spectacle of thousands of birds taking flight to the challenges they face along the way, this report offers a captivating exploration of the natural phenomenon that is bird migration.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Ornithology

Themes: Migration, Animal Behavior, Conservation
