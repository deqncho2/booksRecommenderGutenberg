Synopsis: In 'Peter Cotterell's Treasure' by Rupert Sargent Holland, a young man named Peter Cotterell embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover a hidden treasure. Drawn into a world of mystery and danger, Peter must decipher cryptic clues, navigate treacherous landscapes, and outsmart cunning adversaries. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the past, Peter discovers that the treasure holds not only immense wealth but also a profound connection to his own identity. With time running out, he must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to unravel the truth and claim his rightful inheritance.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Legacy, Pursuit of Wealth
