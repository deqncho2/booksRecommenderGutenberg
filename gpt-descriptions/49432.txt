Synopsis: In 'The Myth of the "Manuscript Found," or the Absurdities of the "Spaulding Story,"' the author delves into the fascinating world of historical controversies surrounding the origins of the Book of Mormon. Through meticulous research and compelling arguments, the book challenges the popular belief that the Book of Mormon was plagiarized from an unpublished manuscript by Solomon Spaulding. As the author unravels the web of misconceptions and absurdities surrounding this theory, readers are taken on a thought-provoking journey that sheds light on the true origins of the Book of Mormon.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Religious

Themes: Historical accuracy, Religious faith, Origins of religious texts
