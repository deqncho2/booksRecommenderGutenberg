Synopsis: In 'They Call Me Carpenter: A Tale of the Second Coming', Sinclair, Upton takes readers on a thought-provoking journey as a mysterious man named Carpenter arrives in a small California town. As Carpenter interacts with the townspeople, his unconventional teachings and miraculous acts challenge their beliefs and ignite a wave of controversy. Through the eyes of a skeptical journalist, the story explores the impact of Carpenter's presence on faith, society, and the human condition.

Genres: Fiction, Religious Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: Religion, Faith, Skepticism, Social Change
