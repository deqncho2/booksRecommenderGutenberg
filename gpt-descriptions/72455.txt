Synopsis: In "The Oak Staircase: A Narrative of the Times of James II," authors Catherine and Mary Lee take readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous era of King James II. The story revolves around a mysterious oak staircase that holds secrets and hidden passages, becoming a symbol of intrigue and danger. As the characters navigate the treacherous political landscape and face personal challenges, they uncover shocking revelations that could change the course of history. With its blend of historical fiction and suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Political intrigue, Secrets and hidden passages, Personal challenges and growth
