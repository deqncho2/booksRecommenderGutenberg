Synopsis: In the wild and rugged landscape of Exmoor, a forbidden love story unfolds between John Ridd, a young farmer, and Lorna Doone, a beautiful and mysterious woman. Set in the 17th century, amidst the backdrop of political turmoil and lawlessness, their love is tested by feuds, betrayals, and the violent Doone clan. As John becomes entangled in a web of secrets and revenge, he must navigate the treacherous path between loyalty and desire, risking everything for a chance at true love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Forbidden Love, Loyalty, Revenge, Social Class
