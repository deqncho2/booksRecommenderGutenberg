Synopsis: In 'Elkan Lubliner, American' by Glass, Montague, follow the captivating journey of Elkan Lubliner, a young immigrant who embarks on a quest for the American dream. From his humble beginnings in Eastern Europe to his arrival in the bustling streets of New York City, Elkan's story is one of resilience, ambition, and the pursuit of identity. As he navigates the challenges of assimilation, love, and success, Elkan's experiences shed light on the complexities of the immigrant experience and the universal desire for belonging.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Immigration

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Assimilation, Belonging
