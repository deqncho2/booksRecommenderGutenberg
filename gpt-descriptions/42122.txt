Synopsis: In 'Atchoo! Sneezes from a Hilarious Vaudevillian' by Niblo, George, follow the uproarious adventures of a vaudevillian performer whose uncontrollable sneezes lead to unexpected and hilarious consequences. From the stage to the streets, this comedic tale takes readers on a wild ride filled with mishaps, misunderstandings, and plenty of laughter. Will our sneezing protagonist find a way to overcome this peculiar predicament? Find out in this side-splitting story that will leave you reaching for the tissues, but not for the reasons you might think!

Genres: Comedy, Humor, Fiction

Themes: Overcoming challenges, The power of laughter, Embracing uniqueness
