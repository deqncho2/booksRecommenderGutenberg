Synopsis: 
In 'The Cornwall Coast' by Arthur L. Salmon, a mysterious disappearance sets off a chain of events that will unravel the secrets hidden within the rugged cliffs and picturesque villages of Cornwall. As the protagonist delves deeper into the enigma, they uncover a web of deceit, betrayal, and long-buried family secrets that threaten to tear apart the idyllic coastal community. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn into a captivating tale of suspense and intrigue, where the stunning beauty of the Cornwall Coast hides a dark and haunting past.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Family, Coastal Life
