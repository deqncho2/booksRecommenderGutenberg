Synopsis: 

'Club Life of London, Vol. 2' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant social scene of London during the 17th, 18th, and 19th centuries. In this second volume, author John Timbs delves deeper into the intriguing world of clubs, coffee-houses, and taverns, providing a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the city's elite and the unique establishments they frequented. Through a collection of anecdotes and historical accounts, Timbs paints a vivid picture of the bustling metropolis and the colorful characters that inhabited it. From the secret societies of the 17th century to the opulent clubs of the 19th century, this book offers a captivating exploration of London's social history.

Genres: History, Social Commentary, Biography

Themes: Social life and customs, Urban culture, Elite societies, Historical anecdotes
