Synopsis: 
'Pathfinding on Plain and Prairie: Stirring Scenes of Life in the Canadian North-west' by McDougall, John takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of the Canadian North-west. Through vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, the author shares his experiences and encounters with the indigenous people, wildlife, and the challenges of survival in this rugged and awe-inspiring landscape. From thrilling adventures to heartwarming moments of human connection, this book offers a unique glimpse into the untamed beauty and harsh realities of the Canadian North-west.

Genres: Adventure, Memoir, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Indigenous culture, Survival
