Synopsis: In "Mungo Park and the Niger" by Joseph Thomson, embark on a thrilling adventure through the uncharted territories of Africa alongside the intrepid explorer, Mungo Park. As he sets out to unravel the mysteries of the Niger River, Park encounters treacherous landscapes, encounters with indigenous tribes, and the constant threat of danger lurking in the unknown. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding encounters, this captivating narrative will transport readers to a world of exploration and discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Cultural Encounter
