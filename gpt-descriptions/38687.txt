Synopsis: 
In 'Zoological Mythology; or, The Legends of Animals, Volume 1', De Gubernatis takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of animal legends and myths from various cultures around the world. From the cunning foxes of Japan to the majestic lions of Africa, this volume unravels the fascinating stories and symbolic meanings behind these creatures. Delving into the realms of folklore, literature, and ancient beliefs, De Gubernatis weaves together a collection of enchanting tales that will leave readers spellbound and eager to explore the second volume.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Cultural Studies

Themes: Animal symbolism, Cultural diversity, Mythological creatures
