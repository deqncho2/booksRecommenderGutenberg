Synopsis: In 'Shipping Clerk' by Joseph Samachson, a seemingly ordinary shipping clerk finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret within the company he works for. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, risking everything to uncover the truth. With his life on the line, the shipping clerk must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to outsmart those who will stop at nothing to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Intrigue, Betrayal, Deception
