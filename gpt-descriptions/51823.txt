Synopsis: In 'The Back of Our Heads' by Barr, Stephen, a group of childhood friends reunite after years of separation to confront a shared secret from their past. As they delve into the mystery, they uncover dark truths that threaten to unravel their lives and test the limits of their loyalty. Told through multiple perspectives, this gripping tale explores the complexities of friendship, the weight of guilt, and the lengths people will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Guilt, Secrets, Loyalty
