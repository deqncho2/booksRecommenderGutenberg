Synopsis: In 'The Ocean Wireless Boys and the Lost Liner' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of young radio operators find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure when they intercept a mysterious distress signal from a lost ocean liner. As they set out to uncover the truth behind the signal, they encounter treacherous waters, hidden secrets, and unexpected allies, all while racing against time to save the passengers and crew of the ill-fated ship. With danger lurking at every turn, the Ocean Wireless Boys must rely on their wit, courage, and technological skills to unravel the enigma and bring the lost liner back to safety.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Survival
