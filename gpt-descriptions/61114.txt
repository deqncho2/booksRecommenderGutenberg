Synopsis: 
In 'Westy Martin in the Yellowstone' by Percy Keese Fitzhugh, young Westy Martin embarks on an exciting adventure in the breathtaking Yellowstone National Park. Joined by his loyal friends, Westy finds himself facing unexpected challenges and encountering mysterious characters as they explore the wonders of the park. From thrilling encounters with wild animals to unraveling a puzzling mystery, Westy's journey in the Yellowstone promises to be a captivating and unforgettable experience.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Nature, Courage
