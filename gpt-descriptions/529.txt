Synopsis: In "Knights of Art: Stories of the Italian Painters," Amy Steedman takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and works of renowned Italian painters. From the masterpieces of Leonardo da Vinci to the vibrant frescoes of Giotto, this book unveils the fascinating stories behind these artistic geniuses. Steedman's vivid storytelling transports readers to the bustling streets of Renaissance Italy, where passion, rivalry, and creativity intertwine. Through her engaging narratives, readers will discover the struggles, triumphs, and secrets that shaped the art world of this extraordinary era.

Genres: Biography, Art History, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic genius, Renaissance culture, Creative inspiration, Historical context
