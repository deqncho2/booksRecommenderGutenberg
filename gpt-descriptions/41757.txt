Synopsis: In 'Roger the Bold: A Tale of the Conquest of Mexico', follow the thrilling adventures of Roger, a young Englishman who finds himself caught up in the epic conquest of Mexico led by the famous Spanish conquistador, Hernán Cortés. As Roger navigates the treacherous landscapes and encounters the vibrant Aztec civilization, he must face dangerous challenges, forge unlikely alliances, and confront his own inner demons. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this historical adventure will transport readers to a time of exploration, conquest, and the clash of civilizations.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Conquest, Cultural Clash, Personal Growth
