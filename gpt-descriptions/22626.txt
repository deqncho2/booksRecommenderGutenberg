Synopsis: In this captivating ode, Charles Sprague takes us back to the historic city of Boston during its centennial celebration. Through his eloquent words, he transports us to a time of great significance, where the spirit of the city and its inhabitants are celebrated. Sprague's ode beautifully captures the essence of Boston's settlement, painting a vivid picture of its growth, achievements, and the indomitable spirit of its people. This work is a tribute to the city's rich history and the resilience of its residents, leaving readers with a sense of awe and admiration for Boston's remarkable journey.

Genres: Poetry, Historical

Themes: History, Patriotism, Community
