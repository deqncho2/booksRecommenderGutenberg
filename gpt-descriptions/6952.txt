Synopsis: In 'By Pike and Dyke: a Tale of the Rise of the Dutch Republic', George Alfred Henty takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Dutch War of Independence. Set in the 16th century, the story follows the adventures of Ned Martin, an English lad who finds himself caught up in the struggle for freedom against Spanish rule. From daring sea battles to secret missions and political intrigue, Ned's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test as he becomes an integral part of the Dutch resistance. Will he be able to help the Dutch Republic rise against all odds?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Freedom, Loyalty
