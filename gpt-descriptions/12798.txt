Synopsis: 
'By Rock and Pool on an Austral Shore, and Other Stories' is a captivating collection of short stories by Louis Becke that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the exotic landscapes of the South Pacific. From the treacherous waters of the Pacific Ocean to the lush jungles of remote islands, each story unveils a unique tale of adventure, love, and survival. Whether it's a gripping account of a shipwreck survivor or a forbidden romance amidst tribal conflicts, these stories are bound to keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies beyond the next shore.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural Clash
