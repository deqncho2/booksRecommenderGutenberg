Synopsis: 
'Vera' by Elizabeth von Arnim is a captivating novel that follows the life of Lucy Entwhistle, a young woman who finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries when she agrees to marry the enigmatic and alluring Everard Wemyss. As Lucy delves deeper into the secrets of her new husband's past, she discovers shocking truths that challenge her perception of love, trust, and the complexities of human nature. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque English countryside, 'Vera' is a gripping tale of passion, betrayal, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and deception, Identity and self-discovery, Secrets and their consequences
