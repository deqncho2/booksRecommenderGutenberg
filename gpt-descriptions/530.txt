Synopsis: 
In 'Driven from Home; Or, Carl Crawford's Experience' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Carl Crawford, a young boy who is suddenly forced to leave his comfortable home and embark on a journey filled with unexpected challenges and life-altering experiences. As Carl faces numerous obstacles and encounters a diverse cast of characters, he must rely on his determination, resourcefulness, and resilience to navigate through the trials that come his way. This captivating tale takes readers on a thrilling adventure, filled with twists and turns, as Carl strives to find his place in the world and discover the true meaning of home.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Resilience, Self-discovery, Overcoming adversity
