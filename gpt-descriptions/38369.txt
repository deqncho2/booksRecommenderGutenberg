Synopsis: 
"A Journal of Two Campaigns of the Fourth Regiment of U.S. Infantry" takes readers on a thrilling journey through the uncharted territories of Michigan and Indiana in the early 19th century. Through the eyes of Col. John P. Boyd and Lt. Col. James Miller, the reader witnesses the challenges, triumphs, and dangers faced by the soldiers as they navigate unfamiliar landscapes, encounter Native American tribes, and engage in intense battles. This gripping journal offers a firsthand account of the bravery, camaraderie, and sacrifices made by these soldiers in their quest to secure the territories for the United States.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Adventure

Themes: Exploration and conquest, Native American relations, Military strategy and tactics, Patriotism
