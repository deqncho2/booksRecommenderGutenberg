Synopsis: In "Abraham Lincoln" by Godfrey Rathbone Benson, Baron Charnwood, delve into the captivating life of one of America's most iconic figures. This meticulously researched biography takes readers on a journey through Lincoln's humble beginnings, his rise to political prominence, and his pivotal role in shaping the nation during one of its most tumultuous periods. From his unwavering commitment to preserving the Union to his profound impact on the abolition of slavery, this book offers a comprehensive and intimate portrait of a man who became a symbol of hope and resilience for generations to come.

Genres: Biography, History, Politics

Themes: Leadership, Equality, Civil Rights, American History
