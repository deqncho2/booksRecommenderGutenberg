Synopsis: 

In 'Traditions of Lancashire, Volume 1', Roby, John takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and history of Lancashire, England. From ancient legends and ghostly tales to local customs and superstitions, this collection of stories offers a fascinating glimpse into the traditions that have shaped the region for centuries. As readers delve into the pages, they will encounter mysterious specters haunting old manor houses, brave heroes battling supernatural forces, and ordinary people caught in extraordinary circumstances. With each story, Roby weaves a tapestry of Lancashire's past, blending fact and fiction to create a captivating narrative that will leave readers eager for more.

Genres: Historical fiction, Folklore, Supernatural

Themes: Tradition, History, Supernatural folklore
