Synopsis: 
In 'A Thief in the Night: A Book of Raffles' Adventures', follow the thrilling escapades of A.J. Raffles, a charming and enigmatic gentleman thief. With his impeccable wit and audacious schemes, Raffles embarks on a series of daring heists that keep the reader on the edge of their seat. From high society parties to hidden underground lairs, Raffles navigates a dangerous world of crime and deception, always managing to stay one step ahead of the law. But as the stakes rise and the risks become greater, Raffles finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue that could jeopardize everything he holds dear. Will he be able to outsmart his pursuers and continue his life of thrilling adventure, or will his luck finally run out?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Morality, Identity, Risk-taking
