Synopsis: In 'The Way of the Gods' by John Luther Long, a young American woman finds herself immersed in the captivating world of ancient Japanese mythology. Drawn to the enigmatic allure of the country, she embarks on a journey that intertwines her fate with that of the gods themselves. As she delves deeper into the secrets of this mystical realm, she must navigate a treacherous path filled with love, betrayal, and the ultimate quest for self-discovery.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Mythology

Themes: Cultural exploration, Fate and destiny, Identity and self-discovery
