Synopsis: In "The Outdoor Girls Around the Campfire; or, The Old Maid of the Mountains" by Laura Lee Hope, the adventurous Outdoor Girls embark on a thrilling camping trip in the picturesque mountains. As they gather around the campfire, they encounter a mysterious old maid who holds a secret that could change their lives forever. With their curiosity piqued, the girls find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, leading them on a captivating journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Secrets, Courage
