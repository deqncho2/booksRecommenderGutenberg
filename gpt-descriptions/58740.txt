Synopsis: In "Complete Life of William McKinley and Story of His Assassination," Everett, Marshall presents an authentic and official memorial edition that delves into the remarkable life of the immortal statesman, soldier, orator, and patriot, William McKinley. This comprehensive account covers every significant incident in McKinley's career, from his rise to political prominence to his tragic assassination. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the author paints a captivating portrait of a man who left an indelible mark on American history.

Genres: Biography, History, Political Non-fiction

Themes: Leadership, Patriotism, Tragedy
