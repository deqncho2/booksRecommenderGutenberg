Synopsis: In 'The Honorable Senator Sage-Brush' by Lynde, Francis, a charismatic and enigmatic senator finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal vendettas. As he navigates the treacherous world of Washington D.C., he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that could shape the future of the nation. With a cast of colorful characters and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of politics and the lengths one man will go to protect his ideals.

Genres: Political thriller, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal, Redemption
