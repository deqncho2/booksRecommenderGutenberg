Synopsis: In 'Stories of Invention, Told by Inventors and their Friends' by Edward Everett Hale, embark on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of inventors and their groundbreaking creations. From the invention of the telephone to the creation of the light bulb, this collection of stories unveils the trials, triumphs, and unexpected twists that shaped some of history's most revolutionary inventions. Through firsthand accounts and anecdotes from inventors and their friends, readers will be enthralled by the ingenuity, perseverance, and sheer brilliance that led to these remarkable innovations. Prepare to be inspired by the untold stories behind the inventions that have shaped our modern world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, History

Themes: Invention, Innovation, Perseverance, Ingenuity
