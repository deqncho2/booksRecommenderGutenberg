Synopsis: 

In 'Men's Sewed Straw Hats: Report of the United States Tariff Commission to the President of the United States (1926)', the United States Tariff Commission delves into the fascinating world of men's sewed straw hats. This comprehensive report explores the intricate details of the manufacturing process, the economic impact of imports, and the challenges faced by American hat makers. With meticulous research and insightful analysis, the Commission presents a compelling case for the protection of domestic hat production. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Economic analysis, Government report

Themes: Trade protectionism, Economic impact, Manufacturing industry
