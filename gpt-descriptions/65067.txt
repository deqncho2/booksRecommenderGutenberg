Synopsis: In 'It's Raining Frogs!' by Stephen Marlowe, a small town is thrown into chaos when an inexplicable phenomenon occurs: frogs start falling from the sky. As the townspeople grapple with this bizarre occurrence, they are forced to confront their own fears, desires, and secrets. Amidst the chaos, a group of unlikely allies forms, determined to uncover the truth behind the raining frogs and restore order to their town. With a blend of mystery, humor, and unexpected twists, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Drama

Themes: Small-town dynamics, Human nature, Uncovering the truth
