Synopsis: In 'Our Admirable Betty: A Romance' by Farnol, Jeffery, we are introduced to the captivating and enigmatic character of Betty, a young woman with a mysterious past. Set in the backdrop of 18th-century England, the story follows Betty's journey as she navigates through a web of secrets, love, and danger. As her true identity slowly unravels, Betty finds herself caught in a whirlwind of unexpected events, testing her resilience and challenging her notions of trust and loyalty. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot filled with twists and turns, 'Our Admirable Betty' promises an enthralling tale of adventure, romance, and self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Love and Betrayal
