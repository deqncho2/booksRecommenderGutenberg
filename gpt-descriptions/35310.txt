Synopsis: 

In 'The Childrens' Story of the War, Volume 1', author Edward Parrott takes young readers on an engaging journey through the early stages of World War I. From the outbreak of the war to the momentous landing of the British Army in France, this captivating volume provides a vivid account of the events that shaped the conflict. Through the eyes of children, the story unfolds, offering a unique perspective on the war's impact on families, communities, and nations. As the war escalates, the young protagonists navigate through fear, uncertainty, and the challenges of a world at war, discovering their own resilience and courage along the way. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: War, Resilience, Courage, Family, Community
