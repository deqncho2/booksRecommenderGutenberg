Synopsis: In 'The Mormon Prophet' by Lily Dougall, a young woman named Emma finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she becomes entangled with a charismatic religious leader. As she delves deeper into the world of the Mormon faith, Emma must navigate the complexities of love, faith, and personal identity, all while questioning the true nature of the prophet she has come to admire. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale explores the power of belief and the lengths one will go to protect their own truth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Religion, Identity, Deception
