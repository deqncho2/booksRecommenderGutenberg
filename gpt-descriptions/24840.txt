Synopsis: In 'The Re-echo Club' by Wells, Carolyn, a group of friends forms a secret society dedicated to solving mysteries and uncovering hidden truths. When a perplexing case involving a missing artifact arises, they embark on a thrilling adventure that takes them from the bustling streets of New York City to the mysterious depths of an ancient Egyptian tomb. As they unravel the clues and face unexpected dangers, the members of the Re-echo Club must rely on their wit, courage, and unwavering friendship to solve the puzzle and protect the secrets that lie within. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Secrets and Hidden Knowledge
