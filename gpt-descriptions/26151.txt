Synopsis: In 'The Tapestry Book' by Helen Churchill Candee, a mysterious tapestry holds the key to a hidden world of secrets and adventure. When a young woman stumbles upon this ancient artifact, she is drawn into a captivating journey that spans across time and continents. As she unravels the threads of the tapestry, she discovers a web of intrigue, romance, and danger that will challenge her beliefs and change her life forever. With each stitch, the tapestry reveals a new chapter in a story that intertwines past and present, weaving together the lives of its characters in unexpected ways. As the young woman delves deeper into the tapestry's mysteries, she must confront her own fears and confront the truth that lies within the threads.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Secrets, Adventure, Identity, Fate
