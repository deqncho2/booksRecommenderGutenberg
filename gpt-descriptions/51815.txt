Synopsis: In "Henry Smeaton: A Jacobite Story of the Reign of George the First," James, G. P. R. takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous era of the Jacobite rebellion during the reign of George the First. The story follows Henry Smeaton, a young and ambitious man caught between his loyalty to the crown and his sympathy for the Jacobite cause. As he navigates treacherous political landscapes and dangerous encounters, Henry's loyalties are tested, and he must make difficult choices that could change the course of history. With its gripping narrative and rich historical backdrop, this novel promises to captivate readers and keep them on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Political Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Political Intrigue, Identity, Rebellion
