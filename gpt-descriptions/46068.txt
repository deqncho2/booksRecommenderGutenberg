Synopsis:
'Abbotsford' by W.S. Crockett is a captivating tale set in the enchanting Scottish countryside. The story follows the life of a young man named Walter Scott, who finds himself drawn to the mysterious and hauntingly beautiful Abbotsford estate. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the estate, Walter uncovers a hidden world of ancient legends, forbidden love, and dark family secrets. With each revelation, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that threatens to unravel everything he holds dear. 'Abbotsford' is a gripping and atmospheric novel that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Scottish folklore
