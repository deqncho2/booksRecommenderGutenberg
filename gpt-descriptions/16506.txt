Synopsis: In 'Epistle to a Friend Concerning Poetry' and 'Essay on Heroic Poetry', Samuel Wesley explores the power and significance of poetry, delving into its various forms and discussing its impact on society. Through thoughtful analysis and engaging anecdotes, Wesley offers a unique perspective on the art of poetry and its ability to inspire, educate, and entertain.

Genres: Literary criticism, essay, poetry

Themes: The power of poetry, the role of poetry in society, the different forms of poetry, the impact of poetry on individuals and communities.
