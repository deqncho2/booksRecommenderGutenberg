Synopsis: 
'Via Crucis: A Romance of the Second Crusade' by F. Marion Crawford is a captivating historical novel set during the tumultuous era of the Second Crusade. The story follows the lives of two individuals from vastly different backgrounds, whose paths intertwine amidst the chaos and violence of the Holy Land. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of war, love, and religious fervor, they are forced to confront their own beliefs, loyalties, and desires. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the trials and tribulations of the Crusades.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Faith and Religion, Identity and Belonging
