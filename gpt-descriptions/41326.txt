Synopsis: 
In 'The Girls of St. Wode's' by L. T. Meade, a group of spirited and independent young women find themselves at the prestigious St. Wode's boarding school. As they navigate the challenges of academic pressure, strict rules, and the complexities of friendship, they uncover a hidden secret that threatens to disrupt their lives. With courage and determination, these girls must band together to protect their futures and the bonds they have formed. 

Genres: 
Mystery, Coming-of-age, School Life

Themes: 
Friendship, Identity, Resilience
