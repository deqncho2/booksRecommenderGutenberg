Synopsis: 
In 'The Itinerary of Archbishop Baldwin Through Wales', Giraldus Cambrensis takes us on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of medieval Wales. As Archbishop Baldwin travels across the country, encountering both the beauty of nature and the challenges of the Welsh people, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and mystery. From ancient castles to sacred sites, from noble knights to cunning outlaws, this gripping account of his travels is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Will the Archbishop succeed in his mission, or will he be forever changed by the secrets he uncovers along the way?

Genres: Historical fiction, travelogue, adventure

Themes: Exploration, cultural clash, the power of nature, the search for truth
