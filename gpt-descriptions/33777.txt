Synopsis: 
In the classic novel "Tom Brown at Rugby," young Tom Brown embarks on a thrilling journey as he enters the prestigious Rugby School. From facing bullies to forming lifelong friendships, Tom navigates the challenges of school life while discovering the true meaning of honor, courage, and loyalty. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century England, this coming-of-age tale captures the essence of adventure, camaraderie, and the pursuit of personal growth.

Genres: 
Adventure, Bildungsroman, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Friendship, Honor, Courage, Loyalty
