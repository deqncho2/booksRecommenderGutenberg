Synopsis: 
In 'The Voice on the Wire' by Ball, Eustace Hale, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when he receives a mysterious phone call from an unknown woman. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he discovers a web of secrets, deceit, and danger that threatens to unravel his own sanity. With each cryptic message, the detective becomes more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means confronting his own demons and risking everything he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Psychological manipulation
