Synopsis: 
'The Lyon in Mourning, Vol. 1: or a collection of speeches, letters, journals, etc. relative to the affairs of Prince Charles Edward Stuart' is a captivating historical collection that delves into the tumultuous events surrounding Prince Charles Edward Stuart, also known as Bonnie Prince Charlie. Through a compilation of speeches, letters, and journals, author Robert Forbes offers a unique perspective on the affairs and struggles faced by the prince during his quest for the British throne. This volume provides a fascinating insight into the political intrigue, personal sacrifices, and emotional turmoil experienced by those involved in the Jacobite Rising of 1745.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Political literature
Themes: Political intrigue, Loyalty and betrayal, Personal sacrifice, Historical events
