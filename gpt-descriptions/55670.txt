Synopsis: In "Men I'm Not Married To; Women I'm Not Married To," Adams and Parker take readers on a captivating journey through a collection of witty and insightful essays. With their unique perspectives, they explore the complexities of relationships, the joys and challenges of singlehood, and the humorous encounters with potential partners that never quite lead to the altar. Through their sharp observations and clever anecdotes, Adams and Parker offer a delightful exploration of the human heart and the ever-elusive search for love.

Genres: Essay, Humor, Non-fiction

Themes: Relationships, Singlehood, Love, Humor
