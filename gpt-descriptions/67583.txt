Synopsis: 
'The Jazz Singer' by Raphaelson, Samson is a captivating story that follows the life of a young man named Jakie Rabinowitz, who dreams of becoming a jazz singer. However, his aspirations clash with the expectations of his traditional Jewish family, who want him to follow in his father's footsteps as a cantor. As Jakie navigates the challenges of pursuing his passion while staying true to his roots, he finds himself torn between his love for music and his loyalty to his family. This gripping tale explores the complexities of identity, tradition, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Drama, Music, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Tradition, Family, Pursuit of Dreams
