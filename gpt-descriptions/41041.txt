Synopsis: In 'The Cumberland Road' by Hulbert, Archer Butler, a young man embarks on a journey along the historic Cumberland Road, a path that stretches across the young United States. As he travels, he encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own stories and secrets. From the bustling cities to the untamed wilderness, the road becomes a backdrop for adventure, romance, and unexpected twists. With every step, our protagonist unravels the mysteries of the road and discovers the true meaning of freedom and self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Freedom, Love, Secrets
