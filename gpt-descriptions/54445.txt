Synopsis: In 'The Hospital Murders' by Davis Means, a renowned surgeon finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when a series of unexplained deaths occur within the walls of his prestigious hospital. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten not only his career but also his own life. With time running out, he must race against the clock to expose the truth before he becomes the next victim.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Medical malpractice, Corruption, Betrayal
