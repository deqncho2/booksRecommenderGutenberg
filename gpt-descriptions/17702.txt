Synopsis:
In the second volume of 'The Tales of the Heptameron', Marguerite, Queen of Navarre, continues to captivate readers with her collection of seventy-two intriguing and scandalous stories. Set in the French court of the 16th century, this collection follows a group of noblewomen and men who gather to share tales of love, betrayal, and deception. From forbidden romances to cunning schemes, each story delves into the complexities of human desires and the consequences that arise from pursuing them. As the characters navigate the treacherous waters of courtly life, they reveal the dark underbelly of power and the fragility of trust. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling narratives, 'The Tales of the Heptameron, Vol. 2' promises to transport readers to a world of intrigue and passion.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Short stories
Themes: Love and desire, Betrayal, Power dynamics, Courtly life
