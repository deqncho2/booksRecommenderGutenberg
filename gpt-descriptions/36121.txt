Synopsis: In the small town of Frostwood, a mysterious stranger arrives during the coldest winter in decades. As the townspeople struggle to survive the harsh conditions, they soon discover that this stranger possesses an extraordinary ability to manipulate snow and ice. But with this power comes a dark secret that threatens to engulf the entire town in a deadly blizzard. As tensions rise and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred, the residents of Frostwood must confront their deepest fears and make unimaginable sacrifices to save themselves from the chilling fate that awaits them.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Power and its consequences, Sacrifice, Survival
