Synopsis: In 'The Perjur'd Husband; or, The Adventures of Venice. A Tragedy' by Susanna Centlivre, the reader is transported to the captivating city of Venice, where a web of deceit, betrayal, and forbidden love unfolds. The story follows the life of a husband who finds himself entangled in a series of dangerous adventures, leading him down a treacherous path that threatens to destroy his marriage and reputation. As secrets are revealed and loyalties are tested, the reader is left on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of the characters.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Love, Loyalty
