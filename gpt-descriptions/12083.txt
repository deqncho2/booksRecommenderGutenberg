Synopsis:
'Eric; Or, Little by Little' by Farrar, F. W. is a gripping coming-of-age novel that follows the life of Eric Williams, a young boy from a respectable family who finds himself caught in a downward spiral of bad influences and moral dilemmas. As Eric navigates the challenges of adolescence, he is faced with temptations that test his character and lead him down a dangerous path. Will Eric be able to resist the allure of vice and find redemption, or will he succumb to the pressures of his peers and the darker side of his own nature?

Genres:
Coming-of-age, Drama, Moral Fiction

Themes:
Moral choices, Peer pressure, Redemption, Consequences of actions
