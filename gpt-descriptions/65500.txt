Synopsis: 
In 'Essentials of Music Theory: Elementary', author Carl E. Gardner takes readers on a captivating journey through the fundamentals of music theory. With a clear and concise writing style, Gardner introduces readers to the essential concepts and principles that form the foundation of music. From understanding notation and rhythm to exploring scales and chords, this book provides a comprehensive and engaging exploration of the elementary aspects of music theory. Through practical examples and exercises, readers will gain a deeper understanding of how music works and develop the skills necessary to analyze and appreciate music on a whole new level.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Music Education, Reference

Themes: 
Music theory, Musical analysis, Skill development
