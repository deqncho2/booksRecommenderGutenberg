Synopsis: In 'Public School Domestic Science' by Adelaide Hoodless, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery as she navigates the challenges of domestic life in a public school setting. With determination and resilience, she strives to master the art of homemaking while facing unexpected obstacles and societal expectations. As Emily's skills and confidence grow, she becomes an inspiration to her peers and a catalyst for change in the realm of domestic education. This captivating tale explores the transformative power of education and the importance of embracing one's passions, even in the face of adversity.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Education, Gender roles, Self-discovery
