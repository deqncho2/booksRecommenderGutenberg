Synopsis:
"The Early Life of Mark Rutherford" is a captivating coming-of-age novel that follows the life of Mark Rutherford, a young man struggling to find his place in the world during the Victorian era. From his humble beginnings in a small English village to his journey through various educational institutions, Mark grapples with his identity, religious beliefs, and the societal expectations placed upon him. As he navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and personal growth, Mark's introspective nature and unwavering determination lead him on a path of self-discovery and transformation. 

Genres:
Fiction, Bildungsroman, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Identity, Religion, Society, Love, Friendship, Personal Growth
