Synopsis: In 'The Green Hand: Adventures of a Naval Lieutenant' by Cupples, George, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside a young naval lieutenant as he embarks on a series of daring adventures at sea. From battling treacherous storms to encountering dangerous pirates, the lieutenant's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. As he navigates the unpredictable waters, he also grapples with personal growth, camaraderie, and the pursuit of honor. This gripping tale of naval life is filled with action, suspense, and unexpected twists that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Naval Fiction

Themes: Courage, Honor, Personal Growth, Camaraderie
