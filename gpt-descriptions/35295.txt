Synopsis: 
In 'The Maroon' by Reid, Mayne, a young sailor finds himself stranded on a remote island after a shipwreck. As he struggles to survive in this unfamiliar and hostile environment, he encounters a mysterious figure who has been living on the island for years. Together, they embark on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, discovery, and unexpected alliances. As they navigate the treacherous landscape, they uncover secrets that will challenge their beliefs and change their lives forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Survival

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Identity, Exploration
