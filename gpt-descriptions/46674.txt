Synopsis: 
In "Stover at Yale" by Owen Johnson, we follow the journey of Dink Stover, a young man from a small town who enters the prestigious Yale University. As he navigates the complexities of college life, Stover encounters a colorful cast of characters, faces academic challenges, and becomes entangled in the intense rivalries and traditions of the Ivy League institution. With his wit, determination, and a touch of rebellion, Stover strives to find his place in this new world, all while discovering the true meaning of friendship, loyalty, and personal growth.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Satire

Themes: Identity, Social class, Tradition, Friendship, Personal growth
