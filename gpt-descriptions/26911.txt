Synopsis: In 'Crooked Trails and Straight' by Raine, William MacLeod, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Dry Bottom, stirring up trouble and challenging the status quo. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With danger lurking around every corner, the residents of Dry Bottom must navigate crooked trails and straight paths to uncover the truth and protect what they hold dear.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Justice, Redemption, Loyalty, Deception
