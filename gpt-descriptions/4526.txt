Synopsis: In 'Born in Exile' by George Gissing, we follow the life of Godwin Peak, a talented writer who finds himself caught between his passion for literature and the harsh realities of Victorian society. As he navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Godwin's journey takes him from the bustling streets of London to the idyllic countryside, where he must confront the consequences of his choices. With its richly drawn characters and thought-provoking exploration of art, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness, 'Born in Exile' is a captivating tale that delves into the complexities of human nature.

Genres: Fiction, Victorian Literature, Social Commentary

Themes: Art and Society, Love and Relationships, Ambition and Identity
