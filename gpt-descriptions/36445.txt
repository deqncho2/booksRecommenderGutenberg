Synopsis: In 'Linda Lee, Incorporated: A Novel' by Vance, Louis Joseph, we are introduced to Linda Lee, a young woman with a mysterious past who finds herself at the center of a high-stakes corporate conspiracy. As Linda delves deeper into the secrets of her own identity, she becomes entangled in a web of deceit, power struggles, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, Linda must navigate a dangerous world where nothing is as it seems, and where her own survival may depend on uncovering the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Power
