Synopsis: In 'More Minor Horrors', Sir Arthur Everett Shipley takes readers on a thrilling journey through a collection of eerie and unsettling tales. From haunted houses to mysterious disappearances, each story is crafted to send shivers down your spine and keep you on the edge of your seat. Prepare to be captivated by Shipley's masterful storytelling as he delves into the darker side of human nature and explores the unknown.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Fear, Supernatural, Human Nature
