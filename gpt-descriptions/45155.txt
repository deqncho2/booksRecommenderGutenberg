Synopsis: In 'The Overall Boys in Switzerland' by Grover, Eulalie Osgood, join the adventurous Overall boys as they embark on a thrilling journey through the picturesque landscapes of Switzerland. As they explore the majestic mountains, charming villages, and sparkling lakes, the boys find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected events that test their courage, resourcefulness, and brotherly bond. From encountering mysterious strangers to unraveling ancient secrets, the Overall boys' summer vacation becomes an unforgettable adventure filled with excitement, danger, and heartwarming moments.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Brotherhood, Exploration, Resilience
