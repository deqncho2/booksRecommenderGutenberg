Synopsis: 

'Widger's Quotes and Images from Serge Panine by George Ohnet: The French Immortals: Quotes and Images' is a captivating collection that offers a glimpse into the world of George Ohnet's renowned novel, 'Serge Panine.' Through carefully selected quotes and accompanying images, this book provides a tantalizing taste of the story's essence, without revealing its full plot. 

Genres: Literary Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Social Class, Ambition, Family Dynamics
