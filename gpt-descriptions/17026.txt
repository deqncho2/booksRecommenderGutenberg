Synopsis: In "Craphound" by Cory Doctorow, a young alien named Zara finds herself caught between her duty to her home planet and her growing fascination with Earth's culture. As she navigates the complexities of intergalactic trade and the allure of human artifacts, Zara discovers a hidden world of collectors, thieves, and unexpected connections. With her unique perspective and a touch of humor, Zara's journey explores the boundaries of identity, the power of nostalgia, and the universal desire for connection.

Genres: Science Fiction, Comedy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Cultural Exchange, Nostalgia, Connection
