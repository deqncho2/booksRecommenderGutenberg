Synopsis: In 'With These Hands' by C. M. Kornbluth, a young man named Danny is thrust into a world of power and corruption when he becomes an apprentice to a wealthy and influential businessman. As Danny navigates this new world, he discovers the dark secrets and moral dilemmas that come with his newfound position. With his hands tied, he must decide whether to succumb to the allure of wealth and privilege or fight for justice and integrity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Political Thriller

Themes: Power and Corruption, Morality and Ethics, Social Inequality
