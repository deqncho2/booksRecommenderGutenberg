Synopsis: In 'The Trail of the Sandhill Stag' by Ernest Thompson Seton, a young boy named Sandy embarks on a thrilling adventure in the wilderness. When he encounters a majestic sandhill stag, Sandy becomes determined to track and observe the elusive creature. As he follows the stag's trail, Sandy faces numerous challenges and learns valuable lessons about nature, perseverance, and the interconnectedness of all living beings.

Genres: Adventure, Wildlife Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Nature, Conservation, Exploration, Resilience
