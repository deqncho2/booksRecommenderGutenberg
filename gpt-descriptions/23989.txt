Synopsis: In "Caleb in the Country" by Abbott, Jacob, a young boy named Caleb embarks on a journey that takes him from the bustling city to the serene countryside. As he explores the wonders of nature and encounters new friends, Caleb's perspective on life begins to shift. However, unexpected challenges arise, testing his resilience and determination. Will Caleb find the strength to overcome these obstacles and discover the true meaning of happiness?

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Friendship, Self-discovery
