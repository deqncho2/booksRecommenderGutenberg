Synopsis: 

'Geographic Variation in the Pocket Gopher, Cratogeomys castanops, in Coahuila, México' explores the fascinating world of pocket gophers and their geographic variations in the region of Coahuila, México. The study delves into the unique characteristics and adaptations of these small mammals, shedding light on their distribution patterns and the factors that contribute to their diversity. Through meticulous research and analysis, the authors uncover intriguing insights into the pocket gopher population, providing a comprehensive understanding of their ecological significance and evolutionary processes.

Genres: Scientific research, Zoology, Ecology

Themes: Biodiversity, Adaptation, Evolution
