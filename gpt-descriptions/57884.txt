Synopsis: In 'The Little Moment of Happiness' by Clarence Budington Kelland, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter. As she delves deeper into the truth, she must confront her own past and make difficult choices that could change her life forever. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this novel explores the power of forgiveness, the complexities of human relationships, and the pursuit of true happiness.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Secrets and lies, Forgiveness, Self-discovery, Love and relationships
