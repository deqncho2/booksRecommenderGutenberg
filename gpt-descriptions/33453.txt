Synopsis: 
In 'A Traitor's Wooing' by Hill, Headon, a young woman finds herself torn between love and loyalty when she becomes entangled with a mysterious and dangerous man. As secrets unravel and the stakes rise, she must navigate a treacherous path filled with deception, betrayal, and unexpected twists. With each page, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of intrigue and forbidden desire, wondering who can be trusted and what sacrifices will be made in the name of love.

Genres: Romance, Suspense, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and loyalty, Deception and betrayal, Forbidden desire
