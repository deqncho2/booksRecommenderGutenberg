Synopsis: In 'A Court of Inquiry' by Grace S. Richmond, a small town is thrown into turmoil when a mysterious crime takes place, leaving the community divided and suspicious of one another. As the court proceedings unfold, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the truth becomes a matter of life and death. With gripping suspense and unexpected twists, this captivating novel explores the complexities of human nature and the lengths people will go to protect their own interests.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Drama

Themes: Justice, Betrayal, Community
