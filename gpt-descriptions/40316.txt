Synopsis: In 'At the Age of Eve' by Kate Trimble Sharber, a young woman named Eve finds herself thrust into a world where the line between reality and fantasy blurs. As she navigates through a series of mysterious events and encounters, Eve must confront her own identity and the secrets that lie within her. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a web of ancient prophecies and hidden powers, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and adventure.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Power, Self-discovery
