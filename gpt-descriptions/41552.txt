Synopsis: In 'The Weird Sisters: A Romance. Volume 1 (of 3)', Dowling takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical world of three sisters who possess extraordinary powers. Set in a small village, the sisters find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden love, and ancient prophecies. As they navigate their individual paths, their destinies become intertwined, leading them to uncover the truth about their heritage and the immense power they hold. With a blend of enchantment, suspense, and romance, this first volume sets the stage for an epic tale of sisterhood, self-discovery, and the battle between light and darkness.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Sisterhood, Destiny, Power, Secrets
