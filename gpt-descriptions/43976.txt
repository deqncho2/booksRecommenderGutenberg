Synopsis: 

In 'Ancient Plants: Being a Simple Account of the past Vegetation of the Earth and of the Recent Important Discoveries Made in This Realm of Nature', Marie Carmichael Stopes takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the fascinating world of ancient plants. From the towering ferns of the Carboniferous period to the delicate flowers of the Cretaceous, Stopes unveils the secrets of these prehistoric botanical wonders. Along the way, she shares the remarkable discoveries that have shed light on the evolution and diversity of plant life throughout history. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Natural History

Themes: Evolution, Paleobotany, Prehistoric Life
