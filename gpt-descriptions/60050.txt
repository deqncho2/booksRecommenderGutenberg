Synopsis: In 'The Book of Herbs' by Rosalind Northcote, a young woman named Lily discovers an ancient book filled with secrets about the magical properties of herbs. As she delves deeper into the world of herbalism, she uncovers a hidden society of herbalists who possess extraordinary abilities. With danger lurking at every turn, Lily must navigate a treacherous path to protect the book and unlock her own potential. This enchanting tale of mystery and self-discovery will transport readers into a world where herbs hold the power to change lives.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Self-discovery, Secrets, Nature
