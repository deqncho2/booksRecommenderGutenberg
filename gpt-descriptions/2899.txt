Synopsis: In 'The Agrarian Crusade: A Chronicle of the Farmer in Politics', Solon J. Buck delves into the captivating world of American farmers and their struggle for political power. Through a meticulous exploration of historical events and personal narratives, Buck uncovers the untold story of how farmers fought against economic oppression and sought to shape the course of American politics. From the rise of the Populist movement to the battles for agricultural reform, this chronicle offers a gripping account of the farmer's relentless crusade for justice and equality.

Genres: History, Political Science, Biography

Themes: Agricultural reform, Economic oppression, Political activism
