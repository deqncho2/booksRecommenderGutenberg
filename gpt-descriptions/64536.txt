Synopsis: 

'English Folk-Song and Dance' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of traditional English music and dance. Kidson and Neal delve into the heart of this vibrant cultural heritage, exploring the origins, evolution, and significance of folk songs and dances that have been passed down through generations. From haunting ballads to lively jigs, this book uncovers the hidden stories and melodies that have shaped English folk traditions. 

Genres: Music, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Tradition, Identity, Oral History
