Synopsis: In 'Rainbow's End' by Rex Beach, a young man named John Cardigan inherits his father's lumber empire and finds himself facing numerous challenges and adversaries. As he navigates the treacherous world of business, he must also confront personal tragedies and betrayals that threaten to unravel everything he holds dear. With determination and resilience, John must find a way to protect his legacy and discover the true meaning of success.

Genres: Drama, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Family legacy, Business struggles, Personal resilience
