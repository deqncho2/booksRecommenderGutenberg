Synopsis:
In the small Russian town of Melitopol, a charismatic and enigmatic young man named Sanine arrives, captivating everyone he meets with his unconventional ideas and rebellious nature. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the local residents, including the wealthy and influential Karsavina family, Sanine's presence begins to expose the hidden desires, passions, and moral conflicts of those around him. Through his interactions, Sanine challenges societal norms and conventions, leading to a series of unexpected and dramatic events that will forever change the lives of those involved.

Genres:
Literary fiction, Psychological drama, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Rebellion, Individualism, Morality, Desire, Social class
