Synopsis: 
'Ballads, Founded on Anecdotes Relating to Animals' by William Hayley is a captivating collection of poetic ballads that brings to life the fascinating and often overlooked stories of animals. From the heroic deeds of a loyal dog to the mischievous adventures of a clever monkey, each ballad presents a unique and heartwarming tale that will leave readers both enchanted and moved. With vivid imagery and lyrical storytelling, Hayley explores the deep connections between humans and animals, reminding us of the profound impact they can have on our lives. 

Genres: Poetry, Animal Fiction, Anthology

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Nature, Human-Animal Bond
