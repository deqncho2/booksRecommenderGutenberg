Synopsis: In 'Our Foreigners: A Chronicle of Americans in the Making', Samuel Peter Orth takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of immigrants in America. Through a series of compelling stories, Orth explores the challenges, triumphs, and transformations experienced by these individuals as they navigate their way in a new land. From the bustling streets of New York City to the vast landscapes of the Midwest, this chronicle delves into the diverse backgrounds, dreams, and aspirations of these foreigners, shedding light on the rich tapestry of American society.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Sociology

Themes: Immigration, Identity, Assimilation, Cultural diversity
