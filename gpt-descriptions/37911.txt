Synopsis: In "The Motor Girls at Lookout Beach; Or, In Quest of the Runaways" by Margaret Penrose, the adventurous and resourceful Motor Girls find themselves embarking on a thrilling quest to locate a group of missing children. As they navigate the sandy shores of Lookout Beach, they encounter unexpected challenges and uncover a mysterious secret that could change everything. With their determination and quick thinking, the Motor Girls must race against time to bring the runaways back home safely.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
