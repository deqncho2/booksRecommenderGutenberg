Synopsis: The Project Gutenberg Collection of Works by Freethinkers is a vast compilation of writings by 90 authors, spanning 157 volumes. This collection includes both online and offline indexes, making it a comprehensive resource for readers interested in exploring the thoughts and ideas of these freethinkers. From philosophical musings to critical analyses of religion and society, this collection offers a diverse range of perspectives that challenge conventional beliefs and encourage independent thought.

Genres: Philosophy, Non-fiction, Literature

Themes: Freethought, Religion, Society, Philosophy, Critical Thinking
