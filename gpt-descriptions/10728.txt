Synopsis: In "Christie, the King's Servant: A Sequel to "Christie's Old Organ," we follow the journey of Christie, a young boy who has overcome great adversity and is now serving as a page in the royal court. As he navigates the challenges and intrigues of palace life, Christie's faith and integrity are put to the test. With the help of his old organ and the lessons he learned from it, Christie must find the strength to stay true to himself and make a difference in the lives of those around him.

Genres: Christian fiction, Historical fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Faith, Integrity, Redemption, Overcoming adversity
