Synopsis: In "Marjorie's New Friend" by Carolyn Wells, Marjorie finds herself captivated by a mysterious and charming stranger who enters her life unexpectedly. As their friendship blossoms, Marjorie is drawn into a world of intrigue and adventure, where secrets and hidden agendas lurk beneath the surface. With each passing day, Marjorie becomes more entangled in a web of deception, unsure of who she can trust. As she unravels the truth, Marjorie must confront her own fears and make difficult choices that will ultimately determine her fate.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Romance

Themes: Trust, Deception, Friendship, Identity
