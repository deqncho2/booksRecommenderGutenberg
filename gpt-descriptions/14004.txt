Synopsis: 
"The Every-day Life of Abraham Lincoln: A Narrative And Descriptive Biography With Pen-Pictures And Personal Recollections By Those Who Knew Him" offers an intimate and captivating glimpse into the life of one of America's most iconic figures. Through the eyes of those who were closest to him, this biography paints a vivid picture of Abraham Lincoln's everyday experiences, revealing the man behind the legend. From his humble beginnings to his rise to the presidency, this narrative takes readers on a journey through Lincoln's personal and professional life, exploring the challenges he faced and the impact he made on the nation. Filled with fascinating anecdotes and personal recollections, this biography offers a unique perspective on the extraordinary life of Abraham Lincoln.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Leadership, Perseverance, Legacy, American History
