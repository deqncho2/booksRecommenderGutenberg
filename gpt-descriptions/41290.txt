Synopsis: 

'Arbuthnotiana: The Story of the St. Alb-ns Ghost (1712) A Catalogue of Dr. Arbuthnot's Library (1779)' is a captivating literary work that intertwines two intriguing narratives. The first part, 'The Story of the St. Alb-ns Ghost,' delves into the mysterious and chilling events surrounding a haunting in St. Albans. As the ghostly encounters escalate, the townspeople are left questioning the nature of the supernatural and the secrets that lie hidden within their community.

The second part, 'A Catalogue of Dr. Arbuthnot's Library,' takes readers on a fascinating journey through the personal library of Dr. Arbuthnot, a renowned figure of the time. This catalogue provides a glimpse into the intellectual world of the era, showcasing a diverse collection of books that reflect the interests and passions of a brilliant mind.

Genres: Mystery, Ghost story, Catalogue

Themes: Supernatural, Secrets, Intellectual curiosity
