Synopsis: In 'Courts and Criminals' by Arthur Cheney Train, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the intricate world of the legal system and the minds of cunning criminals. This gripping narrative follows the brilliant lawyer Ephraim Tutt as he navigates through a series of captivating court cases, each presenting its own unique challenges and surprises. From high-stakes murder trials to perplexing thefts, Tutt's sharp wit and unconventional methods make him a force to be reckoned with in the courtroom. As the tension rises and the truth becomes increasingly elusive, readers will find themselves on the edge of their seats, eagerly awaiting the next twist in this enthralling legal thriller.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Crime fiction

Themes: Justice and injustice, Legal ethics, Human nature
