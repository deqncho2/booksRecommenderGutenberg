Synopsis: In 'Comedy of Marriage and Other Tales', Guy de Maupassant takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of human relationships. From the humorous misadventures of newlyweds to the unexpected twists and turns of love affairs, this collection of tales explores the joys, challenges, and complexities of marriage and romance. With his signature wit and keen observation, Maupassant delves into the foibles and follies of his characters, revealing the universal truths that lie beneath the surface of their seemingly ordinary lives.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Comedy

Themes: Marriage, Love, Relationships, Human Nature
