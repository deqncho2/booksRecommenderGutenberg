Synopsis: In 'The Heart of Hyacinth' by Watanna, Onoto, a young woman named Hyacinth finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is forced into an arranged marriage. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, she discovers a hidden world of passion, secrets, and forbidden love. With each twist and turn, Hyacinth must confront her own desires and make choices that will shape her destiny. Will she follow her heart or succumb to societal expectations?

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Duty and Society, Identity and Self-Discovery
