Synopsis: 
In "Modern bookbindings: Their design and decoration," Sarah Treverbian Prideaux takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of bookbinding, exploring the artistry and craftsmanship behind these exquisite creations. From the intricately designed covers to the meticulous techniques employed, Prideaux delves into the history, styles, and innovations that have shaped modern bookbindings. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, she unveils the secrets of this often-overlooked art form, revealing the passion and dedication of the binders who transform books into works of art. Whether you are a book lover, an art enthusiast, or simply curious about the beauty that lies within the covers of a book, this enchanting exploration will leave you with a newfound appreciation for the art of bookbinding.

Genres: Art, Crafts, Non-fiction

Themes: Bookbinding, Artistry, Craftsmanship, History
