Synopsis: In "The Magic of Oz," join Dorothy and her friends as they embark on a thrilling adventure to save the Land of Oz from an evil sorceress. With the help of magical creatures and enchanting spells, they must overcome obstacles and outsmart their enemies to restore peace and harmony to their beloved land. Will they succeed in their quest or will the sorceress's dark powers prevail?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Good vs. Evil
