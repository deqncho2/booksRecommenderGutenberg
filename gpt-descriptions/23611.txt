Synopsis: In 'Why They Married' by James Montgomery Flagg, a young woman named Alice finds herself caught in a whirlwind of unexpected events after a chance encounter with a mysterious stranger. As their lives become intertwined, Alice is faced with a series of choices that will challenge her beliefs and force her to confront the true nature of love and marriage. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating tale explores the complexities of relationships and the power of destiny.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Love, Fate, Identity
