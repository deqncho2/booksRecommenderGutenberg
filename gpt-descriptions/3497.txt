Synopsis: In "The Swiss Twins" by Lucy Fitch Perkins, follow the captivating journey of two Swiss siblings, Leneli and Seppi, as they embark on a series of adventures that take them from their picturesque Alpine village to bustling cities and beyond. From encountering new cultures and making unexpected friends to facing challenges and discovering their own strengths, the twins' experiences will transport readers to a world filled with excitement, discovery, and heartwarming moments.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cultural exploration, Friendship, Resilience, Self-discovery
