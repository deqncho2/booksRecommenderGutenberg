Synopsis: In 'The Radio Girls at Forest Lodge; or, The Strange Hut in the Swamp' by Margaret Penrose, a group of adventurous young women find themselves embroiled in a mysterious and thrilling adventure. When they stumble upon a hidden hut in the swamp, they uncover a secret that could change their lives forever. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they must use their wit, courage, and radio skills to unravel the truth and face unexpected dangers. With each twist and turn, the Radio Girls prove that they are not just ordinary young women, but capable heroines ready to take on any challenge.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Female Empowerment
