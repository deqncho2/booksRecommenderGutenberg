Synopsis: In 'Out Around Rigel' by Robert H. Wilson, a group of astronauts embarks on a perilous journey to the distant star system of Rigel. As they navigate through uncharted territories and encounter strange phenomena, they must confront their own fears and the mysteries of the universe. With their lives hanging in the balance, the crew must find the strength to overcome the unknown and make it back home.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human Nature
