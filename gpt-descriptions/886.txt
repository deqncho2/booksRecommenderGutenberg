Synopsis: 
'Letters from the Cape' is a captivating collection of letters written by Lucie, Lady Duff Gordon during her time in South Africa. Through her vivid and intimate correspondence, she takes the reader on a remarkable journey filled with adventure, love, and unexpected challenges. From her encounters with fascinating locals to her observations of the breathtaking landscapes, Lady Duff Gordon's letters offer a unique glimpse into a world that is both enchanting and unpredictable. As the pages unfold, readers will find themselves immersed in her captivating storytelling, eagerly awaiting each new letter and the surprises it holds.

Genres: 
Biography, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: 
Exploration, Cultural Exchange, Resilience
