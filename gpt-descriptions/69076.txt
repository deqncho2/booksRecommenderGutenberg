Synopsis: In 'The Roly-Poly Book' by Laura Rountree Smith, a young girl named Lily discovers a mysterious book hidden in her grandmother's attic. Little does she know that this seemingly ordinary book holds the power to transport her into a whimsical world filled with enchanting creatures and magical adventures. As Lily embarks on a journey to unravel the secrets of the Roly-Poly book, she must confront her fears, make new friends, and ultimately learn the true meaning of courage. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the delightful surprises and imaginative twists that await Lily in this extraordinary tale.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Bravery
