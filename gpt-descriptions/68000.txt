Synopsis: In 'Calling the Empress' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist discovers a way to communicate with beings from another dimension. As he delves deeper into this extraordinary connection, he uncovers a shocking secret that could change the course of humanity forever. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path of intrigue, danger, and moral dilemmas. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of science and the consequences of our actions.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of alternate dimensions, Ethics of scientific discovery, Consequences of human curiosity
