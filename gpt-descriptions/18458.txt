Synopsis: In "Star Born" by Andre Norton, a young man named Raf Kurbi embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient alien civilization. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters strange creatures, Raf must confront his own fears and discover his true destiny. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, Raf's quest becomes a thrilling adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Survival
