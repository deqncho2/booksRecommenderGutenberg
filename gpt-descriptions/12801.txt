Synopsis: In 'Abraham Lincoln, Volume II', John T. Morse, Jr. delves into the life and presidency of one of America's most iconic leaders. This captivating volume explores Lincoln's struggles and triumphs during the tumultuous years of the Civil War, shedding light on his political genius, personal growth, and unwavering commitment to preserving the Union. From the Emancipation Proclamation to the Gettysburg Address, Morse paints a vivid portrait of Lincoln's leadership and the profound impact he had on the nation.

Genres: Biography, History, Politics

Themes: Leadership, Civil War, Emancipation, National Unity
