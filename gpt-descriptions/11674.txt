Synopsis: In 'The Torrent (Entre Naranjos)' by Blasco Ibáñez, Vicente, a young man named Gabriel returns to his hometown in Valencia, Spain, after studying in Paris. He becomes captivated by the beauty of the orange groves and the vibrant culture of the region. However, his infatuation with a mysterious woman named Aurora leads him down a path of passion, jealousy, and ultimately, self-discovery. As Gabriel navigates the complexities of love and societal expectations, he must confront his own desires and the consequences of his actions.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, self-discovery
