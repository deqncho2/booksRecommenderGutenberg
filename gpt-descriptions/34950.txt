Synopsis:
In 'Rowing' by R. C. Lehmann, a young woman named Sylvia finds herself caught between the expectations of society and her own desires. When she discovers the exhilarating world of rowing, she becomes determined to break free from the constraints of her conventional life. As Sylvia navigates the challenges of pursuing her passion, she must confront the complexities of love, friendship, and self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque River Thames, this captivating novel explores the transformative power of following one's dreams.

Genres:
Fiction, Coming-of-age, Sports

Themes:
Self-discovery, Breaking societal norms, Pursuing passion
