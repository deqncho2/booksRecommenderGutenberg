Synopsis: In "It Was Marlowe: A Story of the Secret of Three Centuries" by Wilbur Gleason Zeigler, a mysterious manuscript is discovered, revealing a shocking secret that has been hidden for centuries. As the protagonist delves deeper into the enigma, they uncover a web of intrigue, deception, and historical significance that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With twists and turns at every corner, this thrilling tale explores the life of a renowned playwright and the secrets he left behind.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Historical conspiracy, Literary intrigue
