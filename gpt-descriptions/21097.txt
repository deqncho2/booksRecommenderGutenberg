Synopsis: In 'Tom Finch's Monkey: and How he Dined with the Admiral' by John C. Hutcheson, a mischievous monkey named Tom Finch finds himself embarking on a thrilling adventure that leads him to an unexpected encounter with a high-ranking naval officer. As Tom Finch navigates through various escapades and challenges, readers will be captivated by the unpredictable twists and turns that unfold, keeping them on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Exploration
