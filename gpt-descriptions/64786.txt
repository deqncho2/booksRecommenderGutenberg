Synopsis: 

"The Kashf al-mahjúb: The oldest Persian treatise on Súfiism" by Hujviri, ‘Ali ibn ‘Usman is a captivating exploration of the mystical world of Súfiism. Through a series of enlightening anecdotes and profound teachings, the author takes readers on a transformative journey into the depths of the Súfi tradition. As the secrets of the spiritual path are unveiled, readers are invited to delve into the mysteries of divine love, self-realization, and the pursuit of inner peace. With its rich historical context and profound insights, this ancient Persian treatise offers a profound understanding of the Súfi way of life.

Genres: Religious literature, Philosophy, Mysticism

Themes: Súfiism, Divine love, Self-realization, Inner peace
