Synopsis: 
In 'Winning His Way' by Charles Carleton Coffin, a young man named Harry is faced with a life-altering decision that will test his character and determination. As he navigates through unexpected challenges and encounters intriguing individuals, Harry's journey becomes a thrilling adventure filled with suspense and unexpected twists. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles in his path and ultimately find his way to victory?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Perseverance, Morality, Self-discovery
