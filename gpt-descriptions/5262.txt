Synopsis: 
Curly and Floppy Twistytail (The Funny Piggie Boys) is a delightful children's book that follows the misadventures of two mischievous piglets, Curly and Floppy. From getting into trouble at school to exploring the enchanted forest, these lovable piggies embark on a series of hilarious escapades that will keep young readers hooked from beginning to end. With their boundless energy and infectious laughter, Curly and Floppy Twistytail are sure to bring joy and laughter to children of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Humor, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Problem-solving
