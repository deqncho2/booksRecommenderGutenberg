Synopsis: In this captivating biographical sketch, George Parsons Lathrop delves into the life of the renowned American author, Nathaniel Hawthorne. From his early years in Salem to his literary success and personal struggles, Lathrop paints a vivid portrait of Hawthorne's life, revealing the inspirations behind his haunting tales and enigmatic characters. Through anecdotes and insights, Lathrop explores the complexities of Hawthorne's personality and the profound impact his writing had on American literature. This biographical sketch offers a fascinating glimpse into the life of one of America's most celebrated authors.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Life and influences of Nathaniel Hawthorne, American literature, Literary analysis
