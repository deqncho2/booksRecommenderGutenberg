Synopsis: 
In 'Pilgrim Sorrow: A Cycle of Tales' by Sylva Carmen, embark on a mesmerizing journey through a collection of interconnected stories that weave together the lives of diverse characters from different time periods and cultures. From a young girl in ancient Egypt to a troubled artist in modern-day New York City, each tale explores the universal themes of love, loss, redemption, and the search for meaning. As the narratives unfold, secrets are revealed, destinies are intertwined, and the power of storytelling itself becomes a transformative force. Prepare to be captivated by this enchanting tapestry of tales that will leave you pondering the profound connections that bind us all.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Magical Realism

Themes: Love, Loss, Redemption, Identity, Fate, the Power of Storytelling
