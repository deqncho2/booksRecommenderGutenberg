Synopsis: 
In "On the track of Ulysses; Together with an excursion in quest of the so-called Venus of Melos: Two studies in archaeology, made during a cruise among the Greek islands," William James Stillman takes readers on a captivating journey through the Greek islands, following in the footsteps of the legendary Ulysses. Along the way, he embarks on an exciting quest to uncover the truth behind the famous Venus of Melos. As Stillman delves into the rich history and mythology of Greece, he encounters ancient ruins, explores hidden caves, and unravels the mysteries of the past. This enthralling account of his archaeological adventures will transport readers to the enchanting world of ancient Greece, where myth and reality intertwine.

Genres: Travelogue, Archaeology, Mythology
Themes: Exploration, History, Mythology
