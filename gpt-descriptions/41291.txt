Synopsis: In 'New Homes for Old' by Sophonisba Preston Breckinridge, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey to transform the lives of impoverished families in a small rural community. As she faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected allies, Mary's determination to bring about positive change is put to the test. With secrets, romance, and a quest for social justice, this captivating novel explores the power of community and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Social Justice

Themes: Social reform, Community, Resilience
