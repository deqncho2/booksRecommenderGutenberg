Synopsis: 
'Behind the Screen' takes readers on a captivating journey through the glamorous and cutthroat world of Hollywood during its golden age. Samuel Goldwyn, one of the most influential film producers of his time, shares his personal experiences and behind-the-scenes stories that shed light on the magic and madness of the movie industry. From the rise of silent films to the advent of talkies, Goldwyn's narrative unveils the secrets, scandals, and triumphs that shaped the silver screen. As he navigates the treacherous waters of fame and fortune, readers are treated to a captivating tale that explores the price of success and the sacrifices made in pursuit of a dream.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Film History

Themes: Ambition, Success, Hollywood, Show Business, Perseverance
