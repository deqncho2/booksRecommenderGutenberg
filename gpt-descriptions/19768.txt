Synopsis: In 'Professor Royce's Libel: A Public Appeal for Redress to the Corporation and Overseers of Harvard University', Abbot, Francis Ellingwood takes us on a gripping journey through the scandalous world of academia. When renowned professor Royce finds himself at the center of a libelous accusation, he must fight to clear his name and expose the truth. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the very foundations of Harvard University are shaken. Will justice prevail, or will the powerful forces at play silence the truth forever?

Genres: Mystery, Legal Drama, Academic Fiction

Themes: Truth and Justice, Power and Corruption, Academic Integrity
