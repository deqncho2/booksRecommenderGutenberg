Synopsis: 

'Under the White Ensign: A Naval Story of the Great War' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous waters of World War I. Set against the backdrop of the vast and treacherous oceans, this gripping naval story follows the adventures of a young sailor named Jack, who finds himself thrust into the heart of the conflict. As he navigates the perils of war, Jack must confront not only the enemy ships that lurk beneath the waves, but also the internal struggles and sacrifices that come with serving under the white ensign. With danger at every turn and the fate of nations hanging in the balance, Jack's courage and determination will be put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Bravery, Sacrifice
