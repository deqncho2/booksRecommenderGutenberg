Synopsis: In "Medusa's Coil," a young couple moves to a small town in the American South, only to discover a dark and sinister secret lurking beneath the surface. As they delve deeper into the town's history, they uncover a chilling tale of forbidden love, ancient curses, and a mysterious woman with a deadly power. With each revelation, their own lives become entangled in a web of horror and madness, leading them to question their own sanity and the true nature of evil.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Forbidden love, Curses, Madness, Power of the supernatural
