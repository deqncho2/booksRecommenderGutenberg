Synopsis: In "An Apology for Idlers, and Other Essays," Robert Louis Stevenson takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking and witty essays. From exploring the joys of laziness to delving into the art of conversation, Stevenson's words offer a refreshing perspective on the value of leisure and the importance of embracing the simple pleasures in life. With his signature charm and eloquence, the author invites readers to question societal norms and reevaluate their own priorities, ultimately inspiring a newfound appreciation for the art of idleness.

Genres: Essay, Non-fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Leisure, Work-life balance, Individualism, Societal expectations
