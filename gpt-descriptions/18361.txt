Synopsis: In 'Operation: Outer Space' by Murray Leinster, a team of scientists and astronauts embark on a daring mission to explore the mysteries of the universe beyond Earth. As they venture into the vastness of space, they encounter unexpected dangers and encounter a threat that could have catastrophic consequences for humanity. With time running out, they must use their ingenuity and courage to overcome the challenges they face and ensure the survival of the human race.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Space exploration, Human ingenuity, Survival
