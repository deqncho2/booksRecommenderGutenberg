Synopsis: In 'The Tin Soldier' by Bailey, Temple, a young boy discovers a mysterious tin soldier hidden away in his grandfather's attic. Little does he know that this seemingly ordinary toy holds a secret that will transport him to a world filled with adventure, danger, and unexpected friendships. As he embarks on a thrilling journey, the boy must navigate through a series of challenges and confront his deepest fears. Will he find the courage to face the unknown and uncover the truth behind the tin soldier's origins?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Imagination
