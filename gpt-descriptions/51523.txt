Synopsis: 
'With the Scottish Regiments at the Front' takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of Scottish soldiers during World War I. Vivian, Evelyn Charles, a war correspondent, provides a vivid and intimate account of the soldiers' daily lives, their bravery, and the challenges they face on the front lines. From the trenches of France to the battlefields of Belgium, this book offers a unique perspective on the war, highlighting the camaraderie, resilience, and sacrifices of the Scottish regiments.

Genres: 
War memoir, Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: 
Bravery, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Resilience
