Synopsis: In 'Opinions' by Claude C. Washburn, a renowned journalist finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger as he investigates a controversial political figure. As he delves deeper into the story, he uncovers shocking secrets that could change the course of history. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of deception and manipulation to expose the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Political Fiction

Themes: Truth and deception, Power and corruption
