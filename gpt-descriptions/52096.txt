Synopsis: 
'Plays: Being: An unhistorical pastoral: A romantic farce: Bruce, a chronicle play: Smith, a tragic farce: and Scaramouch in Naxos, a pantomime' is a collection of five plays that take the reader on a journey through various genres and themes. From an unhistorical pastoral to a romantic farce, a chronicle play, a tragic farce, and a pantomime set in Naxos, each play offers a unique and captivating story. These plays are filled with unexpected twists, witty dialogue, and memorable characters that will keep readers engaged from beginning to end.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Romance, Historical Fiction, Pantomime

Themes: Love and romance, Identity and self-discovery, Satire and social commentary, Tragedy and loss, The power of performance and artistry
