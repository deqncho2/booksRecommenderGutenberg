Synopsis: In 'Back to Julie' by Richard Wilson, a mysterious letter leads a young woman on a journey to uncover the truth about her past. As she delves deeper into her family's history, she discovers long-held secrets and unexpected connections that challenge everything she thought she knew. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and redemption, ultimately leading her to make a life-altering decision. This gripping tale of self-discovery and the power of forgiveness will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Redemption
