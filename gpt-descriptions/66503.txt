Synopsis: In 'With Sword and Crucifix: Being an Account of the Strange Adventures of Count Louis Sancerre, Companion of Sieur LaSalle, on the Lower Mississippi, in the Year of Grace 1682' by Van Zile, Edward S., readers are transported to the untamed wilderness of the Lower Mississippi in the 17th century. Count Louis Sancerre, a nobleman from France, embarks on a perilous journey alongside Sieur LaSalle, encountering treacherous landscapes, indigenous tribes, and mysterious forces that test their courage and faith. As they navigate the uncharted territories, the count's loyalty and resolve are put to the ultimate test, leading to unexpected alliances and life-altering discoveries.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Loyalty, Faith, Cultural Encounter
