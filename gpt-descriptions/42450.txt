Synopsis: 
'Housekeeping in Old Virginia' takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of managing a household in the historical setting of Virginia. Filled with practical advice, fascinating anecdotes, and timeless wisdom, this book offers a unique glimpse into the world of domesticity in the past. From preserving food to organizing social events, from maintaining a beautiful home to managing the complexities of relationships, 'Housekeeping in Old Virginia' provides a rich tapestry of knowledge and insights that will captivate both history enthusiasts and those seeking inspiration for modern-day homemaking.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Domesticity

Themes: Domestic management, Historical preservation, Gender roles
