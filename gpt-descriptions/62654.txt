Synopsis: 
In "The Greycliff Girls in Camp" by Harriet Pyne Grove, a group of adventurous young girls embark on a thrilling summer camp experience at Greycliff Lodge. As they navigate the challenges of camp life, they uncover a mysterious secret that leads them on a captivating journey filled with friendship, courage, and unexpected discoveries. With each passing day, the girls find themselves drawn deeper into a world of intrigue and excitement, testing their bonds and pushing them to their limits. Will they be able to unravel the enigma that surrounds Greycliff Lodge before it's too late? Join the Greycliff Girls on this unforgettable adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
