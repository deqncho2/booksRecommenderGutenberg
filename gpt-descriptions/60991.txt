Synopsis: In 'The Connoisseur' by Banta, Frank, a mysterious art collector embarks on a quest to acquire a legendary masterpiece that has eluded the world for centuries. As he delves deeper into the art world's secrets, he becomes entangled in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each step closer to his goal, the stakes rise higher, and the line between obsession and passion blurs. Will he succeed in his pursuit, or will the elusive masterpiece forever remain out of reach?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Art

Themes: Obsession, Deception, Power
