Synopsis: In "Life and Matter: A Criticism of Professor Haeckel's "Riddle of the Universe," Sir Oliver Lodge presents a thought-provoking critique of one of the most influential scientific theories of his time. With meticulous analysis and compelling arguments, Lodge challenges the widely accepted ideas of renowned biologist Ernst Haeckel, delving into the intricate relationship between life and matter. As he navigates through the complexities of scientific thought, Lodge invites readers on a captivating intellectual journey that questions the very essence of existence.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Philosophy

Themes: Critique of scientific theories, Relationship between life and matter, Existential inquiry
