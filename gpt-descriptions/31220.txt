Synopsis: In 'A New Subspecies of Bat (Myotis velifer) from Southeastern California and Arizona', Terry A. Vaughan takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of bats, unveiling the discovery of a previously unknown subspecies. Set in the diverse landscapes of Southeastern California and Arizona, this scientific exploration delves into the intricacies of bat taxonomy and the fascinating process of identifying a new species. As Vaughan unravels the mysteries surrounding this unique bat, readers are immersed in the excitement of scientific discovery and the importance of preserving biodiversity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Biodiversity, Scientific Discovery, Conservation
