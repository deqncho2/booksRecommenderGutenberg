Synopsis: In 'Haney's Art of Training Animals', author W. H. Burroughs presents a comprehensive guide for both amateur and professional trainers, offering full instructions on breaking, taming, and teaching various animals. From an improved method of horse breaking to the management of farm animals, training sporting dogs, serpent charming, and even the care and tuition of talking, singing, and performing birds, this book covers it all. With detailed instructions for teaching circus tricks and many other wonderful feats, readers will be captivated by the fascinating world of animal training.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Training, How-to Guide

Themes: Animal behavior, Training techniques, Communication with animals
