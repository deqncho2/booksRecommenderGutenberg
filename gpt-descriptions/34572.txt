Synopsis: In 'The Awakening of the Soul' by Ibn Tufayl, we are introduced to a young boy named Hayy who grows up alone on a deserted island. With no human contact, he is left to his own devices and must rely on his innate curiosity and intellect to navigate the world around him. As he explores the island and observes the natural world, Hayy begins to question the meaning of life and the existence of a higher power. His journey of self-discovery takes him on a profound spiritual awakening, leading him to contemplate the nature of the soul and its connection to the universe.

Genres: Philosophical fiction, allegory, spiritual literature

Themes: Self-discovery, the search for meaning, the nature of the soul, spirituality, the relationship between humanity and nature
