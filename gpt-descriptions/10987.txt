Synopsis: In 'The Extraordinary Adventures of Poor Little Bewildered Henry, Who was shut up in an Old Abbey for Three Weeks: A Story Founded on Fact', we follow the gripping tale of young Henry, who finds himself trapped in an ancient abbey for an extended period. As he navigates the eerie halls and uncovers the secrets hidden within, Henry's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the test. With each passing day, the mystery deepens, and the reader is left on the edge of their seat, eagerly anticipating the shocking revelations that lie ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Isolation, Secrets and Intrigue
