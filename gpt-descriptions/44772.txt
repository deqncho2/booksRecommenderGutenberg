Synopsis: In "The Golden Bough: A Study in Magic and Religion," James George Frazer takes readers on a captivating journey through the realms of ancient beliefs and rituals. This third edition, the final volume of twelve, delves into the fascinating world of magic and religion, exploring the interconnectedness of various cultures and their practices. From the mystical rites of ancient priests to the enchanting folklore of distant lands, Frazer's meticulous research and engaging storytelling unravel the mysteries of human spirituality. As readers delve deeper into this vast tapestry of beliefs, they are bound to be enthralled by the intricate web of connections that span across time and continents.

Genres: Anthropology, History, Mythology

Themes: Magic, Religion, Cultural Beliefs, Rituals, Human Spirituality
