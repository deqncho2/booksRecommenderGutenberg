Synopsis: In 'The Brighton Boys in the Trenches' by James R. Driscoll, a group of adventurous young men from Brighton find themselves thrust into the chaos and danger of World War I. As they navigate the treacherous trenches and face the horrors of war, they form unbreakable bonds of friendship and discover the true meaning of courage. With each passing day, they are tested in ways they never could have imagined, and their resilience and determination become their greatest weapons. Will they survive the brutal realities of the battlefield and make it back home?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival
