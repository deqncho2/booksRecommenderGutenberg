Synopsis: In "What the Wind Did" by Amy Le Feuvre, a young girl named Amy embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience after a life-altering event. As she navigates through the challenges and uncertainties of her new reality, Amy finds solace and strength in the power of nature and the mysterious whispers of the wind. With the wind as her guide, she learns valuable lessons about love, forgiveness, and the importance of embracing change. This captivating tale takes readers on an enchanting adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns, leaving them eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within the wind's whispers.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Resilience, Self-discovery, Nature's influence, Love, Forgiveness
