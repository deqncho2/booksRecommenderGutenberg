Synopsis: 
'Etiquette for Little Folks' is a charming and instructive guidebook that takes young readers on a delightful journey through the world of proper behavior. Written by Mrs. Henry S. Mackarness, this book offers a collection of engaging stories and practical advice, teaching children the importance of good manners and polite conduct in various social situations. From table manners to conversation etiquette, this book provides valuable lessons that will help young readers navigate the complexities of social interactions with grace and confidence.

Genres: Children's Literature, Etiquette, Education

Themes: Manners, Politeness, Social Skills, Education
