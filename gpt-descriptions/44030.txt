Synopsis: In 'Our Little Danish Cousin' by Luna May Ennis, readers are transported to the charming world of Denmark through the eyes of a young Danish girl named Ingeborg. As Ingeborg navigates her daily life, readers are introduced to the rich culture, traditions, and landscapes of Denmark. From exploring the bustling streets of Copenhagen to experiencing the beauty of the Danish countryside, Ingeborg's adventures offer a delightful glimpse into the Danish way of life. Along the way, she encounters new friendships, faces challenges, and learns valuable lessons about family, loyalty, and the importance of embracing one's heritage.

Genres: Children's Literature, Cultural Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Cultural identity, Family, Friendship, Adventure
