Synopsis: In 'The Lever: A Novel' by William Dana Orcutt, a mysterious lever is discovered in an old, forgotten attic. As the lever is pulled, it sets off a chain of events that transcends time and space, leading the protagonist on a thrilling journey through different dimensions and alternate realities. With each pull of the lever, the protagonist must confront their deepest fears and make life-altering choices. As the line between reality and imagination blurs, the true power of the lever is revealed, leaving the reader questioning the nature of existence itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Reality vs. Imagination, Choice and Consequence, Exploration of Alternate Realities
