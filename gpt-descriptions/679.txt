Synopsis: 
'Poems' by Frances Ellen Watkins Harper is a collection of powerful and thought-provoking verses that delve into the depths of human emotions and experiences. From exploring the complexities of love and loss to shedding light on the struggles of marginalized communities, Harper's poetry captivates readers with its lyrical beauty and profound insights. Through her words, she paints vivid pictures of life's joys and sorrows, inviting readers to reflect on their own journeys and find solace in the power of poetry.

Genres: Poetry, African American literature, Social commentary

Themes: Love, Loss, Identity, Social justice, Empowerment
