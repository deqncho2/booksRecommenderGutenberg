Synopsis: In the eighth volume of his famous diary, Samuel Pepys takes us on a captivating journey through the final months of 1660. As he navigates the bustling streets of London, Pepys encounters a myriad of intriguing characters and finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, scandalous affairs, and personal triumphs. From the grandeur of the royal court to the seedy underbelly of the city, Pepys provides a vivid and intimate account of the events that shape his world, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets and surprises that lie within.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Diary

Themes: Politics, Society, Personal Growth, Intrigue
