Synopsis: In 'The Game and the Candle' by Eleanor M. Ingram, a mysterious game of chance and a seemingly ordinary candle intertwine the lives of two strangers, leading them on a thrilling journey of danger, romance, and unexpected twists. As they navigate through a web of secrets and deceit, they must unravel the truth behind the game and the candle before it's too late. Will they be able to trust each other and find a way out, or will they become pawns in a much larger game?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Trust, Deception, Fate
