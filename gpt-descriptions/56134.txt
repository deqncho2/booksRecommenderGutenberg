Synopsis: 
'Girls and Athletics' is a comprehensive guide that explores the exciting world of sports and physical activities for girls in schools, colleges, and women's clubs. From archery to water polo, this book provides a brief summary of each game, including its rules and method of administration. Whether it's the grace of gymnastics, the intensity of ice hockey, or the teamwork in volley ball, readers will discover a wide range of sports that empower and inspire girls to stay active and competitive. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Education

Themes: Female empowerment, Physical fitness, Teamwork, Sportsmanship
