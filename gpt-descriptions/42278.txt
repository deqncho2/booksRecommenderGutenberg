Synopsis: In 'Toy-Making at Home: How to Make a Hundred Toys from Odds and Ends', Adams, Morley takes readers on a delightful journey into the world of toy-making. With step-by-step instructions and creative ideas, this book offers a treasure trove of inspiration for crafting unique toys using everyday materials. From simple paper crafts to intricate wooden creations, the author's passion for toy-making shines through, making this a must-read for anyone seeking to unleash their inner child and bring joy to others through handmade toys.

Genres: Crafts and Hobbies, DIY, Children's Nonfiction

Themes: Creativity, Resourcefulness, Joy of Handmade Toys
