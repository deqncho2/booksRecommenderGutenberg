Synopsis: In 'No Quarter!' by Reid, Mayne, a young and ambitious pirate captain finds himself caught in a treacherous game of power and betrayal on the high seas. As he navigates through dangerous waters and encounters formidable adversaries, he must make difficult choices that will determine his fate and the fate of his crew. With thrilling action, unexpected twists, and a cast of intriguing characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Action

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Morality
