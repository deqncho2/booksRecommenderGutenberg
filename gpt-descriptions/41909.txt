Synopsis: In 'The Crimson Thread: An Adventure Story for Girls', follow the thrilling journey of young Mary as she embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets of a mysterious crimson thread. With danger lurking at every turn, Mary must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter enigmatic characters, and solve perplexing riddles to unravel the truth behind the crimson thread's power. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Mary discovers the strength within herself and the importance of friendship and perseverance. Will she be able to unlock the secrets before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Perseverance
