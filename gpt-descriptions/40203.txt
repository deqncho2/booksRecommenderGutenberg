Synopsis: 
In 'Arsène Lupin versus Herlock Sholmes', the infamous gentleman thief, Arsène Lupin, finds himself in a thrilling battle of wits against the renowned detective, Herlock Sholmes. When Lupin steals a valuable object, Sholmes is determined to catch him, leading to a captivating game of cat and mouse across Europe. As the two masterminds match wits and employ their unique methods, the stakes rise higher, and the lines between hero and villain become blurred. With unexpected twists and clever strategies, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Deception, Intelligence, Morality
