Synopsis: In 'Bertha, Our Little German Cousin', we follow the adventures of young Bertha as she navigates life in Germany. From exploring the beautiful countryside to experiencing the excitement of a traditional German festival, Bertha's journey is filled with cultural discoveries and heartwarming moments. As she learns about her heritage and forms new friendships, Bertha's perspective on the world expands, leaving readers captivated by her charming and endearing personality.

Genres: Children's Literature, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cultural exploration, Friendship, Heritage
