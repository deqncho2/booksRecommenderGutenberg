Synopsis: In 'The Christiana Riot and the Treason Trials of 1851: An Historical Sketch', author W. U. Hensel delves into a gripping and little-known event in American history. Set in the tense backdrop of pre-Civil War America, this book uncovers the dramatic events surrounding the Christiana Riot and the subsequent treason trials that captivated the nation. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Hensel brings to life the clash between fugitive slaves and their pursuers, shedding light on the complex dynamics of race, justice, and freedom in 19th-century America.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, True crime, Legal history

Themes: Slavery and abolition, Racial tensions, Legal and social justice
