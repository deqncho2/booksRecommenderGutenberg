Synopsis: 
'Adventures of a Sixpence in Guernsey by A Native' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the picturesque island of Guernsey. Narrated by an anonymous native, the story follows the escapades of a sixpence coin as it passes through the hands of various characters, each with their own unique story to tell. From smugglers to artists, lovers to fighters, the sixpence becomes a witness to the island's rich history and vibrant culture. As the coin changes hands, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and lives are forever altered. With its vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the hidden depths of Guernsey's past and present.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Fate, Community
