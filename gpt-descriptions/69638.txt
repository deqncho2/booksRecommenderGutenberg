Synopsis: In 'Nineteen hundred? A forecast and a story' by Marianne Farningham, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as the protagonist finds herself caught between the realms of reality and the supernatural. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the past, she uncovers a hidden world that challenges her perception of time and existence. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn into a web of intrigue and suspense, wondering what lies beyond the boundaries of our known reality.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Historical Fiction

Themes: Time, Perception, Identity, Secrets
