Synopsis: 
The Aeneid of Virgil is an epic poem that follows the journey of Aeneas, a Trojan hero, as he navigates through war, love, and destiny. After the fall of Troy, Aeneas embarks on a perilous voyage across the Mediterranean, facing numerous challenges and encountering both mortal and divine beings. Along the way, he must confront his own inner struggles and make difficult choices that will shape the future of his people. Filled with vivid imagery, intense battles, and profound emotions, this epic tale explores the themes of fate, duty, honor, and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: Epic poetry, Mythology, Historical fiction

Themes: Fate and destiny, Heroism, Love and passion, Loyalty and duty, War and its consequences
