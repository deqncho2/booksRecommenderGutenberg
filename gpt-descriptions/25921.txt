Synopsis: In 'The Long Trick' by Bartimeus, a master illusionist named Victor embarks on a daring heist that will test his skills and challenge his moral compass. As he assembles a team of misfits and experts, they must navigate a web of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and illusion becomes blurred, leaving readers questioning who can be trusted and what is truly real.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
