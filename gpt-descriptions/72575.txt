Synopsis: In 'A Moment of Madness, and Other Stories', Florence Marryat takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing tales. From a mysterious encounter on a train to a haunting ghost story set in the Scottish Highlands, each story presents a unique and unexpected twist. Delve into the lives of diverse characters as they navigate love, loss, and the complexities of human nature. With Marryat's signature blend of suspense and emotion, this volume will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, craving more of her captivating storytelling.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Love and relationships, Mystery and intrigue, Supernatural elements
