Synopsis: In 'Autres Temps...: 1916' by Edith Wharton, we are transported to the elegant world of New York society during World War I. The story follows the life of Fanny Frisbee, a woman who finds herself at a crossroads as she navigates the changing social landscape and grapples with the choices she has made. As Fanny reflects on her past and contemplates her future, she must confront the consequences of her actions and the sacrifices she has made for love and societal expectations. With its richly drawn characters and evocative setting, 'Autres Temps...: 1916' explores themes of love, duty, and the complexities of personal fulfillment.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Society and class, Personal identity and fulfillment
