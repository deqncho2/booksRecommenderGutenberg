Synopsis: In "Old-Fashioned Fairy Tales," Juliana Horatia Ewing takes readers on a delightful journey through a collection of enchanting and whimsical tales. From mischievous fairies to talking animals, each story is filled with magic and wonder. These old-fashioned fairy tales transport readers to a world where anything is possible, where bravery and kindness prevail, and where dreams come true. 

Genres: Fantasy, Children's Literature, Fairy Tales

Themes: Magic, Morality, Imagination
