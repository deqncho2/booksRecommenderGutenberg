Synopsis: 

In 'Return-Loads Bureaus to Save Waste in Transportation', the United States Council of National Defense's Highways Transport Committee presents a groundbreaking solution to the inefficiencies and waste in transportation. This informative work explores the concept of return-loads bureaus, which aim to optimize the use of transportation resources by matching empty return trips with available loads. By delving into the intricacies of this innovative system, the book sheds light on how it can revolutionize the transportation industry and contribute to a more sustainable future.

Genres: Non-fiction, Transportation, Logistics

Themes: Efficiency, Waste reduction, Sustainability
