Synopsis: 

In 'Common Objects of the Microscope', author J. G. Wood takes readers on a captivating journey into the hidden world that exists right before our eyes. Through the lens of a microscope, Wood unveils the astonishing beauty and complexity of everyday objects that often go unnoticed. From the delicate intricacies of a butterfly's wing to the mesmerizing patterns found in a single grain of sand, this book reveals a universe of wonders that will leave readers in awe of the microscopic realm. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Exploration, Beauty in the Small, Scientific Curiosity
