Synopsis: In "History of circumcision from the earliest times to the present," Remondino delves into the intriguing and controversial topic of circumcision, exploring its moral and physical reasons for performance. He also delves into related subjects such as eunuchism and hermaphrodism, providing a comprehensive understanding of the different operations practiced upon the prepuce. This thought-provoking work takes readers on a journey through time, shedding light on the historical significance and evolution of circumcision.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Medical

Themes: Cultural practices, Medical history, Gender and sexuality
