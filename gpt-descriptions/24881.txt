Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Grumpy Weasel: Sleepy-Time Tales' by Bailey, Arthur Scott, follow the misadventures of Grumpy Weasel, a grumpy and irritable creature who just can't seem to find a good night's sleep. From encountering peculiar dreams to facing unexpected challenges, Grumpy Weasel's journey takes readers on a whimsical and humorous exploration of the importance of rest and the power of a positive attitude. Will Grumpy Weasel finally discover the secret to a peaceful slumber?

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Humor

Themes: Sleep, Attitude, Resilience
