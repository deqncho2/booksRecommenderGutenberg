Synopsis: In 'Two, by Tricks: A Novel' by Edmund Yates, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as two strangers, both harboring secrets, find themselves unexpectedly entangled in a web of deceit and desire. As their lives become increasingly intertwined, they must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, where nothing is as it seems. With each page, the reader is drawn deeper into a world of intrigue, where the truth is elusive and trust is a dangerous game.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Betrayal
