Synopsis: 
In 'The Range Boss' by Charles Alden Seltzer, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Dry Bottom, stirring up trouble and challenging the authority of the local ranchers. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the range boss must navigate a treacherous landscape of greed, betrayal, and revenge. With his quick wit and sharpshooting skills, he becomes the town's only hope for survival. But will he be able to outsmart his enemies and restore peace to Dry Bottom?

Genres: Western, Action, Adventure

Themes: Justice, Loyalty, Redemption
