Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a young and adventurous girl, finds herself embarking on a thrilling journey along the St. Lawrence River. As she explores the picturesque Thousand Islands, she encounters a mysterious old man who holds secrets that could change her life forever. With her determination and curiosity, Ruth sets out to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic figure and the hidden treasures of the islands. This captivating tale of mystery and discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Ruth's quest for answers.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, Self-discovery
