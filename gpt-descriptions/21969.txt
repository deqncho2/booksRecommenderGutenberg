Synopsis: In 'The Outcry' by Henry James, a valuable work of art becomes the center of a fierce battle between an English aristocrat and an American millionaire. As the two men engage in a high-stakes bidding war, their clash of cultures and personal ambitions leads to unexpected consequences. Set against the backdrop of the glamorous art world, this novel explores the complexities of wealth, power, and the pursuit of beauty.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Satire

Themes: Class conflict, Materialism, Cultural differences
