Synopsis: 
'Ivory, Apes and Peacocks' by James Huneker is a captivating collection of essays that takes readers on a journey through the realms of art, music, literature, and philosophy. With his sharp wit and insightful observations, Huneker delves into the lives and works of various artists, composers, and writers, exploring their genius, eccentricities, and the impact they have had on the world. From the passionate melodies of Chopin to the enigmatic paintings of Whistler, Huneker's essays offer a rich tapestry of cultural exploration that will leave readers inspired and enlightened.

Genres: 
Essay, Biography, Criticism

Themes: 
Artistic genius, Cultural exploration
