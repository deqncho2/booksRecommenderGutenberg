Synopsis: 
'Uncanny Stories' by Sinclair, May is a collection of eerie and unsettling tales that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. From haunted houses to mysterious disappearances, each story delves into the realm of the supernatural, exploring the dark corners of the human psyche. As the characters navigate through strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and grapple with the unknown. With unexpected twists and spine-chilling moments, 'Uncanny Stories' is a captivating journey into the realm of the uncanny.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, the supernatural, the human psyche
