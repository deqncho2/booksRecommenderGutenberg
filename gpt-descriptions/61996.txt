Synopsis: 

In 'Throttled! The Detection of the German and Anarchist Bomb Plotters', author Thomas J. Tunney takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of espionage and terrorism in early 20th century America. Set against the backdrop of World War I, this gripping true story follows the relentless pursuit of German and anarchist bomb plotters who are determined to wreak havoc on American soil. As the tension mounts and the stakes rise, readers will be captivated by the intricate web of deception, the race against time, and the courageous individuals who risk everything to protect their country.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, True Crime, Espionage

Themes: Espionage, Terrorism, Patriotism, National Security
