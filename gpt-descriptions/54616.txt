Synopsis: 

In 'The Mythology of the British Islands: An Introduction to Celtic Myth, Legend, Poetry, and Romance', Charles Squire takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and enchanting world of Celtic mythology. From the ancient tales of gods and goddesses to the heroic legends of warriors and kings, this book delves into the fascinating stories that have shaped the British Isles.

With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, Squire explores the origins and significance of Celtic myths, revealing their enduring influence on literature, art, and culture. From the mystical realms of the Otherworld to the epic battles between heroes and monsters, readers will be transported to a realm where magic and reality intertwine.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Literary Criticism
Themes: Celtic mythology, Legends, Cultural heritage
