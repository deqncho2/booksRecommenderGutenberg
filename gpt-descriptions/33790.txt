Synopsis: In 'Delayed Action' by Charles V. De Vet, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Richard Seaton invents a revolutionary device that can manipulate time. However, when a mysterious organization discovers his creation, they will stop at nothing to possess it. As Dr. Seaton races against time to protect his invention, he finds himself caught in a thrilling battle of wits and danger, where the fate of the world hangs in the balance.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Time manipulation, Scientific discovery, Pursuit of power
