Synopsis: In 'Mermaid' by Grant M. Overton, a mysterious and enchanting woman named Marina arrives in a small coastal town, captivating the locals with her ethereal beauty and mesmerizing voice. As the townspeople become infatuated with Marina, they soon discover that her presence brings both joy and tragedy, stirring up long-buried secrets and igniting forbidden desires. With the line between reality and fantasy blurring, the townspeople must confront their deepest fears and desires, ultimately leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Beauty and allure, Secrets and forbidden desires, Illusion versus reality
