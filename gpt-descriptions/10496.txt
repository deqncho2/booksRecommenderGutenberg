Synopsis: In 'Red Masquerade: Being the Story of the Lone Wolf's Daughter' by Vance, Louis Joseph, follow the thrilling journey of a young woman named Thalia, who discovers her true identity as the daughter of a notorious thief known as the Lone Wolf. As she delves into her father's mysterious past, Thalia finds herself entangled in a dangerous game of secrets, betrayal, and hidden treasures. With her life on the line, she must navigate a treacherous world of deception and intrigue, all while trying to uncover the truth about her father and her own destiny.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Family, Betrayal, Redemption
