Synopsis: 

In 'Live to be Useful: or, The Story of Annie Lee and her Irish Nurse' by Anonymous, readers are transported to the enchanting world of Annie Lee, a young girl who finds herself in the care of a remarkable Irish nurse. As their bond deepens, Annie Lee embarks on a journey of self-discovery, navigating the challenges of life with the guidance and wisdom of her devoted nurse. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, this heartwarming tale explores the power of friendship, resilience, and the transformative impact of love.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Love, Self-Discovery
