Synopsis: In 'The Second Deluge' by Garrett Putman Serviss, a catastrophic event threatens to wipe out humanity once again. As the world faces the imminent danger of a second global flood, a group of scientists and adventurers embark on a daring mission to save mankind. With suspenseful twists and unexpected discoveries, this thrilling tale takes readers on a gripping journey through a world on the brink of destruction.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Catastrophe, Survival, Human Ingenuity
