Synopsis: 

In "Standish of Standish: A Story of the Pilgrims," Jane G. Austin takes readers on a captivating journey back to the early days of the Pilgrims' settlement in Plymouth, Massachusetts. The story revolves around the life of Myles Standish, a courageous and enigmatic figure who played a crucial role in the survival of the Pilgrims in the New World. As the narrative unfolds, readers are immersed in the challenges and triumphs of the Pilgrims' quest for religious freedom, their encounters with Native Americans, and the complexities of their relationships within the community. Through the eyes of Standish, Austin weaves a tale of love, loyalty, and sacrifice, showcasing the indomitable spirit of those who sought a new beginning in a foreign land.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Courage, Faith, Community, Cultural Encounter
