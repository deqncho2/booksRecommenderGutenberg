Synopsis: In 'The Spell of Scotland' by Keith Clark, a young American woman embarks on a journey to Scotland, seeking solace and a fresh start after a devastating loss. As she immerses herself in the enchanting landscapes and rich history of the country, she discovers a hidden world of ancient legends, mystical rituals, and a love that transcends time. With each step she takes, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and supernatural forces that threaten to unravel her newfound happiness. Will she be able to break the spell that binds her to Scotland, or will she be forever captivated by its magic?

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Love and loss, Identity and self-discovery, Supernatural elements
