Synopsis: In 'Turning Point' by Alfred Coppel, a brilliant scientist finds himself caught in a web of international intrigue and personal danger when he stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery. As he races against time to protect his invention, he must navigate a treacherous world of spies, double agents, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the stakes grow higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. Will he be able to outsmart his enemies and safeguard his invention, or will he become a pawn in a deadly game of power? 

Genres: Thriller, Espionage, Science Fiction

Themes: Betrayal, Technology, Survival
