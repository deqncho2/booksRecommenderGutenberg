Synopsis: In 'A Little House in War Time', the Castle siblings, Egerton and Agnes, transport readers to a quaint countryside cottage during the tumultuous period of war. As the world outside is consumed by chaos, the siblings find solace and strength within the walls of their little house. Through their unique perspectives, they navigate the challenges of wartime, discovering the power of resilience, love, and the indomitable human spirit. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Resilience, Love, Home, Survival, Human Spirit
