Synopsis: In 'The Story of Garfield: Farm-boy, Soldier, and President' by William G. Rutherford, embark on a captivating journey through the remarkable life of James A. Garfield. From his humble beginnings as a farm-boy to his heroic service as a soldier during the Civil War, and ultimately his rise to become the 20th President of the United States, this biography unveils the extraordinary achievements and challenges faced by one of America's lesser-known leaders. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Rutherford brings to life the untold story of Garfield, shedding light on his unwavering determination, his unwavering commitment to justice, and the enduring legacy he left behind.

Genres: Biography, History, Politics

Themes: Perseverance, Leadership, American History
