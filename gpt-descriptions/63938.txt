Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the fascinating world of medicine in the Middle Ages through the eyes of Dr. Edmond Dupouy. In this captivating collection of extracts from his renowned work "Le Moyen Age Medical," Dr. Dupouy takes us on a journey through the medical practices, beliefs, and discoveries of this tumultuous era. From the use of herbs and potions to the mysterious art of bloodletting, the author unveils the secrets of medieval medicine, shedding light on the methods and theories that shaped the lives of both healers and patients. With vivid descriptions and intriguing anecdotes, this book offers a unique glimpse into a time when medicine was as much a science as it was a mystical art.

Genres: History, Medicine, Non-fiction

Themes: Medicine and science in the Middle Ages, Medical practices and beliefs, Historical perspectives on healthcare
