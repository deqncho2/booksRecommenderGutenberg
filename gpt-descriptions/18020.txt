Synopsis: In 'The Wits and Beaux of Society. Volume 1', the authors take us on a captivating journey through the glamorous and scandalous world of high society in the 18th century. Through a series of witty and satirical sketches, they introduce us to a colorful cast of characters, from charming rakes and fashionable ladies to cunning social climbers and eccentric artists. As we delve into their lives and witness their triumphs and misadventures, we are immersed in a world of extravagant parties, secret affairs, and intricate social hierarchies. With sharp observations and clever humor, the authors provide a fascinating glimpse into the complexities and contradictions of the elite society of their time.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Social class, Morality, Hypocrisy, Love and relationships
