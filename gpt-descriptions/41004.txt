Synopsis: In 'An Eye for an Eye' by William Le Queux, a mysterious and enigmatic stranger arrives in a small English village, bringing with him a dark secret from the past. As the villagers become entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, they must confront their own demons and make difficult choices that will test their loyalty and morality. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of justice and revenge.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Revenge, Morality, Loyalty
