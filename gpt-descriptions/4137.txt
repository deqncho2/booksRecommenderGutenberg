Synopsis: In the nineteenth volume of his famous diary, Samuel Pepys takes us on a captivating journey through the months of November and December 1662. As a high-ranking naval administrator, Pepys encounters a myriad of intriguing characters and experiences, from attending lavish parties and witnessing scandalous affairs to navigating the treacherous waters of political intrigue. Amidst the chaos, Pepys also grapples with personal challenges, including his health and the complexities of his relationships. With his signature wit and candidness, Pepys offers a fascinating glimpse into the vibrant and tumultuous world of 17th-century England.

Genres: Diary, Historical Fiction, Memoir

Themes: Social life, Politics, Personal struggles, Relationships
