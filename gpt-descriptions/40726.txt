Synopsis: In "Through Welsh Doorways" by Jeannette Augustus Marks, a young woman embarks on a journey to Wales, where she discovers a hidden world filled with enchantment and mystery. As she delves deeper into the secrets of this magical land, she uncovers a connection to her own past that she never could have imagined. With each doorway she passes through, she is transported to a different time and place, encountering fascinating characters and experiencing extraordinary adventures. This captivating tale will transport readers to a realm where reality and fantasy intertwine, leaving them eager to unlock the secrets that lie beyond the Welsh doorways.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Time Travel, Mythology, Self-discovery
