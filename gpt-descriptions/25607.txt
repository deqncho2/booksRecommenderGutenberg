Synopsis: In 'Witness to the Deed' by George Manville Fenn, a young artist named Frank is drawn into a web of mystery and intrigue when he witnesses a shocking crime. As he becomes entangled in the dangerous secrets of those involved, Frank must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect himself from the consequences. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Crime, Betrayal, Secrets, Justice
