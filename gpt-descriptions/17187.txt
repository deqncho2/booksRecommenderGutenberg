Synopsis: In 'Three Plays: Lawing and Jawing; Forty Yards; Woofing', Zora Neale Hurston takes readers on a captivating journey through three distinct plays. In "Lawing and Jawing," two friends engage in a hilarious battle of wits and words. "Forty Yards" explores the complexities of love and relationships through a unique lens. Lastly, "Woofing" delves into the world of gossip and its consequences in a small community. Each play offers a vibrant and thought-provoking exploration of human nature and the dynamics of interpersonal relationships.

Genres: Drama, Comedy

Themes: Friendship, Love, Communication, Gossip
