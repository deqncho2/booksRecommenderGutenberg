Synopsis: In 'Told by the Colonel' by Alden, W. L., readers are transported to the intriguing world of a retired military officer known as the Colonel. Through a series of captivating anecdotes and personal recollections, the Colonel shares his experiences and encounters with a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets and hidden agendas. As the Colonel's tales unfold, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of mystery, suspense, and unexpected twists, leaving them eager to uncover the truth behind the Colonel's enigmatic past.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery, Power and manipulation
