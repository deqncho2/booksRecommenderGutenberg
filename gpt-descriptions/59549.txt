Synopsis: In 'Signs & Wonders' by J. D. Beresford, a mysterious phenomenon begins to sweep across the world, causing inexplicable and awe-inspiring events to occur. As society grapples with the implications of these extraordinary occurrences, a young scientist finds himself at the center of a thrilling investigation that delves into the realms of science, spirituality, and the unknown. With each revelation, the boundaries of reality are pushed, leaving readers captivated by the enigmatic forces at play.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Supernatural phenomena, Existentialism, Human curiosity, The nature of reality
