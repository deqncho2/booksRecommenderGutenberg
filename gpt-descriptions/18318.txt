Synopsis: In the midst of the Civil War, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey of love, sacrifice, and resilience in the heart of Kentucky. As she navigates the complexities of war-torn society, Mary finds herself torn between loyalty to her family and her own desires. With the fate of her loved ones hanging in the balance, she must make difficult choices that will shape her future and the lives of those around her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Loyalty and family, Resilience in the face of adversity
