Synopsis: In 'Sex-linked Inheritance in Drosophila', renowned geneticists Morgan and Bridges delve into the fascinating world of fruit flies to uncover groundbreaking discoveries about inheritance patterns. As they meticulously observe the intricate interactions between genes and chromosomes, unexpected revelations emerge, challenging long-held beliefs and revolutionizing the field of genetics. With each experiment, the authors unravel the mysteries of sex-linked traits, shedding light on the complexities of inheritance and paving the way for future scientific breakthroughs.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Genetics

Themes: Inheritance, Genetics, Sex-linked traits
