Synopsis: In 'The Relentless City' by E. F. Benson, a young artist named Richard is drawn into the vibrant and scandalous world of London's bohemian society. As he navigates the city's artistic circles, he becomes entangled in a web of love, ambition, and betrayal. With each encounter, Richard's perception of the city and its inhabitants is challenged, leading him to question his own desires and the true nature of success.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Artistic expression, Society and class, Love and relationships
