Synopsis: In 'Pharaoh's Broker: Being the Very Remarkable Experiences in Another World of Isidor Werner', Isidor Werner, a young and ambitious stockbroker, finds himself transported to ancient Egypt after a mysterious encounter. As he navigates this unfamiliar world, Isidor becomes entangled in the political intrigues of the pharaoh's court, where power, betrayal, and forbidden love lurk at every corner. With his modern knowledge and cunning, Isidor must navigate treacherous waters to survive and find a way back home.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Power and politics, Love and betrayal
