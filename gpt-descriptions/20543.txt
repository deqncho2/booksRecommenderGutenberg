Synopsis: In "Edward FitzGerald and "Posh": "Herring Merchants," author James Blyth takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Edward FitzGerald, a man whose seemingly ordinary existence takes an unexpected turn when he becomes entangled in a world of herring merchants. As FitzGerald navigates the complexities of this newfound world, he discovers hidden secrets, unexpected alliances, and a web of intrigue that threatens to unravel his very existence. With vivid storytelling and a cast of intriguing characters, Blyth weaves a tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the herring merchants.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Intrigue
