Synopsis: 
'Tales from Shakespeare' is a captivating collection of retellings of some of William Shakespeare's most beloved plays. Written by siblings Charles and Mary Lamb, this book takes readers on a journey through the enchanting worlds of Shakespeare's timeless stories. From the tragic love story of Romeo and Juliet to the magical adventures of A Midsummer Night's Dream, the Lambs bring these iconic plays to life in a way that is accessible and engaging for readers of all ages. With their unique storytelling style, the Lambs capture the essence of Shakespeare's works, making them accessible to those who may find the original texts daunting. 

Genres: 
Classic Literature, Retellings, Drama

Themes: 
Love and Romance, Tragedy, Comedy, Magic and Fantasy
