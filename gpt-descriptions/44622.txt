Synopsis: In the Days of Giants: A Book of Norse Tales takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Norse mythology. From the creation of the world to the epic battles of gods and giants, this collection of tales introduces us to a cast of fascinating characters, including Thor, Loki, Odin, and Freya. With vivid storytelling and rich imagery, Abbie Farwell Brown brings these ancient myths to life, immersing readers in a world of adventure, magic, and heroism. Whether it's the thrilling exploits of the gods or the cunning tricks of the mischievous Loki, this book is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Mythology, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Power of Nature, Trickery
