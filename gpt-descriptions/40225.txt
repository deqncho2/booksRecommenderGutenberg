Synopsis: In "America Discovered by the Welsh in 1170 A.D." by Bowen, Benjamin Franklin, a hidden historical secret is unveiled as the author delves into the intriguing possibility that America was actually discovered by the Welsh long before Columbus. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, the book uncovers evidence that challenges conventional beliefs about the discovery of the New World. As the narrative unfolds, readers are taken on a thrilling journey of exploration, mystery, and the quest for truth.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Historical revisionism
