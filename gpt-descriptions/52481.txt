Synopsis: In "Life of St. Rita of Cascia, O.S.A.: from the Italian" by Connolly, Richard, readers are transported to 14th-century Italy to witness the extraordinary life of St. Rita. Born into a world of violence and turmoil, Rita's journey takes her from a troubled marriage to a life of devotion and miracles. As she navigates through personal tragedy and societal challenges, her unwavering faith and determination inspire those around her. This captivating biography delves into the trials and triumphs of a woman who becomes a symbol of hope and resilience.

Genres: Biography, Historical, Religious

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Perseverance, Miracles
