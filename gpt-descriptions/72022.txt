Synopsis: In "The Unpretenders" by Ruth Cranston, a group of seemingly ordinary individuals find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and hidden identities. As they navigate through a series of unexpected events, their true selves are gradually revealed, challenging their perceptions of reality and forcing them to confront their deepest desires and fears. With each twist and turn, the characters must grapple with the question of authenticity and the consequences of living a life built on pretense. As the story unfolds, the reader is drawn into a captivating tale of self-discovery and the power of embracing one's true identity.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Authenticity, Self-discovery
