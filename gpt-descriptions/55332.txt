Synopsis: 

In "The Republic of Ragusa: An Episode of the Turkish Conquest" by Luigi Villari, readers are transported to the captivating world of 15th-century Ragusa, a small but prosperous republic located on the Adriatic coast. As the Ottoman Empire's power grows, the city-state finds itself caught in the crosshairs of the Turkish conquest. Amidst political intrigue, shifting alliances, and the looming threat of invasion, the citizens of Ragusa must navigate treacherous waters to protect their beloved republic and preserve their way of life. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Political thriller, Adventure

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Survival, Cultural identity
