Synopsis: In the chilling Gothic novel, "The Wyvern Mystery," a young woman named Alice Maybell finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and dark family history when she marries the enigmatic Charles Fairfield. As Alice delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding her new husband and his ancestral home, Wyvern, she uncovers a haunting past that threatens to consume her. With eerie occurrences, hidden identities, and a sense of impending doom, this atmospheric tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of love, trust, and the power of the past.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Love and betrayal, Supernatural elements, Family history and legacy
