Synopsis: In "The Temptation of St. Antony; Or, A Revelation of the Soul," Gustave Flaubert takes us on a mesmerizing journey through the tormented mind of St. Antony, a devout monk plagued by vivid hallucinations and relentless temptations. As he battles with his inner demons, St. Antony is confronted with a series of surreal and fantastical visions that challenge his faith and sanity. From encounters with mythical creatures to seductive temptresses, Flaubert weaves a tapestry of psychological turmoil and spiritual awakening that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Religious fiction, Surrealism

Themes: Faith and doubt, Temptation and desire, Madness and sanity, Spiritual awakening
