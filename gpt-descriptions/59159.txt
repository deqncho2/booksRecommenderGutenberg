Synopsis: In 'Josiah's Secret: A Play' by Holley Marietta, a small town is shaken when a mysterious secret is uncovered, threatening to unravel the lives of its residents. As the truth slowly emerges, alliances are tested, friendships are strained, and the community must come to terms with the consequences of their actions. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this play explores the power of secrets and the lengths people will go to protect them.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Community, Consequences
