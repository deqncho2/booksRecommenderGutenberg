Synopsis: In 'The Catalpa Expedition', Zephaniah Walter Pease takes readers on a thrilling journey to the late 19th century, where a daring plan is hatched to rescue six Irish political prisoners from a remote Australian penal colony. With a team of determined men, a disguised American whaling ship named Catalpa sets sail on a perilous mission, facing treacherous seas, cunning adversaries, and the constant threat of discovery. As the tension builds, the fate of these prisoners and the success of their audacious escape become intertwined, creating a gripping tale of courage, resilience, and the power of human determination.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, True Crime

Themes: Freedom and Liberation, Loyalty and Sacrifice, Justice and Injustice
