Synopsis: 
In "Mark Tidd: His Adventures and Strategies" by Clarence Budington Kelland, readers are introduced to the clever and resourceful Mark Tidd, a young boy with an insatiable curiosity and a knack for finding himself in thrilling adventures. When Mark and his friends stumble upon a mysterious clue, they embark on a quest that takes them through hidden tunnels, secret codes, and unexpected encounters. As they navigate through the challenges, Mark's strategic thinking and determination are put to the test, leading to a series of exhilarating escapades that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Problem-solving, Courage
