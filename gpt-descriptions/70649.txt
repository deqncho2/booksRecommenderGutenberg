Synopsis: 
Step into the world of A. Augustus Healy, a prominent art collector from Brooklyn, as his private collection of valuable paintings and watercolors is about to be unveiled to the public. This catalogue showcases the stunning works of the modern Dutch school, offering a glimpse into the beauty and innovation of this artistic movement. With the promise of an unrestricted public sale, art enthusiasts and collectors alike eagerly anticipate the opportunity to acquire these remarkable pieces. Don't miss the chance to immerse yourself in the vibrant world of Dutch art and witness the extraordinary talent of A. Augustus Healy's collection.

Genres: Art, Auction, Catalogue

Themes: Dutch art, Modernism, Collecting
