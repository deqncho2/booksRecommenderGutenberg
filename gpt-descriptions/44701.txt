Synopsis: 

In 'British Regiments at the Front, The Story of Their Battle Honours', author Reginald Hodder takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of British regiments in battle. From the iconic battles of Waterloo and Trafalgar to the lesser-known conflicts, this book unveils the untold stories of bravery, sacrifice, and triumph that have shaped the regiments' battle honors. Through vivid descriptions and firsthand accounts, Hodder brings to life the harrowing experiences of soldiers on the front lines, highlighting the indomitable spirit and unwavering dedication of these regiments. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Military Tradition, Heroism
