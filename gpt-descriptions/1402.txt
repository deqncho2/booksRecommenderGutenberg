Synopsis: In "Where the Blue Begins" by Christopher Morley, a young man named Gissing embarks on a whimsical adventure that takes him to a fantastical world where dogs can talk and have their own society. As Gissing navigates this canine civilization, he encounters a variety of colorful characters and experiences unexpected challenges. With wit and charm, Morley explores the boundaries between reality and imagination, inviting readers to question the nature of identity and the power of imagination.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Satire

Themes: Imagination, Identity, Society
