Synopsis: In 'The Bird Hospital' by Caroline Crowninshield Bascom, a young veterinarian named Lily finds herself drawn into a mysterious world when she takes a job at a renowned bird hospital. As she navigates the complexities of avian medicine, Lily uncovers a hidden network of bird enthusiasts, each with their own secrets and obsessions. With the help of a quirky staff and a charming bird whisperer, Lily must unravel the truth behind a series of puzzling bird ailments and confront her own fears and insecurities. As she delves deeper into this captivating world, Lily discovers that the birds she cares for are not the only ones in need of healing.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Identity, Healing, Connection, Obsession
