Synopsis: In "The Last Class" by Richard Banks, a mysterious new teacher arrives at a small town high school, captivating the students with his unconventional teaching methods. As the class delves into thought-provoking discussions and engages in unexpected activities, the students begin to question their own beliefs and the purpose of education. With each passing day, the boundaries between teacher and student blur, leading to a surprising and transformative climax that will leave readers pondering the true meaning of learning and personal growth.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Education, Identity, Transformation
