Synopsis: In 'The Moving Picture Girls in War Plays: Or, The Sham Battles at Oak Farm', the adventurous and talented Moving Picture Girls find themselves caught up in the excitement of war plays being filmed at Oak Farm. As they immerse themselves in the world of battle reenactments, they soon discover that the line between reality and fiction becomes blurred, leading them on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will they be able to navigate the challenges and uncover the truth behind the sham battles?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Filmmaking, Friendship, Perception of Reality
