Synopsis: In "Seven Daughters" by Amanda M. Douglas, a wealthy and influential family finds their lives turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives in their small town. As the stranger's true identity is slowly revealed, secrets from the past are unearthed, leading to unexpected alliances and heart-wrenching betrayals. Set against the backdrop of the Gilded Age, this captivating tale explores the complexities of family dynamics, the power of love, and the lengths one will go to protect their own.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family, Identity, Betrayal, Love, Secrets
