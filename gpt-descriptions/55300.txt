Synopsis: In "Society as I Have Found It," McAllister Ward takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate web of high society in the late 19th century. Through the eyes of the charismatic protagonist, we are introduced to a world of opulence, scandal, and hidden secrets. As Ward navigates the treacherous waters of the upper class, he uncovers shocking truths that threaten to unravel the very fabric of society. With each revelation, the reader is drawn deeper into a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and the lengths people will go to protect their social standing.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Social class, Deception, Morality, Power dynamics
