Synopsis: In "The Motor Girls on a Tour" by Margaret Penrose, a group of adventurous young women embark on an exhilarating journey across the country in their trusty automobiles. As they navigate through challenging terrains, encounter unexpected obstacles, and make new friends along the way, the Motor Girls find themselves caught up in a thrilling mystery that tests their courage and resourcefulness. With their determination and quick thinking, they must unravel the secrets that lie hidden in the places they visit, all while enjoying the freedom and excitement of the open road.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Exploration
