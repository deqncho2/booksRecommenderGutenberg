Synopsis: In 'Under the Storm' by Charlotte M. Yonge, a young woman named Ethel May is faced with a series of unexpected challenges that test her resilience and determination. As she navigates through the stormy waters of life, Ethel May must confront her own fears and confront the secrets that threaten to tear her world apart. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth that lies beneath the storm.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Resilience, Secrets, Self-discovery
