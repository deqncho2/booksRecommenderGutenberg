Synopsis: 
In "Men I'm Not Married To," Dorothy Parker takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of witty and insightful short stories. From charming encounters to bittersweet reflections, Parker explores the complexities of relationships and the various men who have left an indelible mark on her life. With her signature sharp humor and keen observations, she delves into the intricacies of love, desire, and the ever-elusive pursuit of happiness. Each story offers a glimpse into the lives of these men, leaving readers yearning for more of Parker's brilliant storytelling.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Humor

Themes: Love, Relationships, Feminism, Human Nature
