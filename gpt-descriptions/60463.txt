Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's Diamond Foes; Or, Straight Over the Plate' by Standish, Burt L., Frank Merriwell, the talented and charismatic athlete, finds himself facing a formidable challenge on the baseball field. As he leads his team against a group of skilled opponents, Frank must rely on his wit, determination, and unwavering sportsmanship to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way. With high stakes and intense competition, this thrilling story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Frank's journey to victory.

Genres: Sports, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Sportsmanship, Perseverance, Teamwork
