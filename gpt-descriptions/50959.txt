Synopsis:
In 'End as a World' by Wallace, F. L., a small town is suddenly plagued by a series of inexplicable and terrifying events. As the residents struggle to make sense of the chaos, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and darkest secrets. With each passing day, the situation becomes increasingly dire, and the line between reality and nightmare begins to blur. As the town teeters on the brink of destruction, a group of unlikely heroes must band together to uncover the truth and save themselves from the impending doom that threatens to consume them all.

Genres:
Horror, Mystery, Thriller

Themes:
Fear, Secrets, Survival
