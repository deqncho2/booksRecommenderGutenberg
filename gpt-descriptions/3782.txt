Synopsis: In 'Huntingtower' by John Buchan, a retired grocer named Dickson McCunn finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a group of mysterious strangers seeking refuge in an old Scottish castle. As he becomes entangled in their dangerous predicament, McCunn must summon his courage and resourcefulness to outwit a cunning enemy and protect those he has come to care for. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Redemption
