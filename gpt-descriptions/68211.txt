Synopsis: In 'Gold and Glory; or, Wild Ways of Other Days, a Tale of Early American Discovery' by Grace Stebbing, embark on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of early America. Follow the adventures of a courageous explorer as he navigates treacherous landscapes, encounters indigenous tribes, and uncovers hidden treasures. With danger lurking at every turn, the protagonist must rely on wit, resilience, and a burning desire for gold and glory to survive. This captivating tale of discovery and adventure will transport readers to a time when the American frontier was a vast and mysterious land waiting to be explored.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Exploration and Discovery, Ambition and Desire for Wealth, Survival and Resilience
