Synopsis: 
'An account of some kjoekkenmoeddings, or shell-heaps, in Maine and Massachusetts' takes readers on a fascinating journey into the ancient past, exploring the mysterious shell-heaps found in coastal regions. Through meticulous research and vivid descriptions, Wyman, Jeffries uncovers the secrets hidden within these archaeological sites, shedding light on the lives and customs of the people who once inhabited them. From the discovery of unique artifacts to the analysis of ancient food sources, this account offers a captivating glimpse into a forgotten world.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Archaeology, History

Themes: 
Ancient civilizations, Archaeological exploration, Cultural anthropology
