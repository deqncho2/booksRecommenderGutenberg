Synopsis: In 'The Beggar's Purse: A Fairy Tale of Familiar Finance' by Samuel Hopkins Adams, a young woman named Alice discovers a mysterious purse that has the power to grant her every wish. As she navigates the temptations and consequences of her newfound wealth, Alice must learn the true value of money and the importance of personal responsibility. With whimsical characters and unexpected twists, this enchanting fairy tale explores the complexities of finance and the choices we make in pursuit of our desires.

Genres: Fairy Tale, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Wealth and its consequences, Personal responsibility, Temptation and desire
