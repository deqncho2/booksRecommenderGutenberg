Synopsis: 
Anna Karenina, a beautiful and passionate woman, finds herself torn between her loveless marriage and a forbidden affair that threatens to unravel her entire world. Set in 19th-century Russia, this epic novel explores the complexities of love, desire, and societal expectations, as Anna navigates the treacherous waters of her own heart. As her choices lead to devastating consequences, the reader is left questioning the nature of love and the sacrifices one is willing to make in its pursuit.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Morality and Society, Individual Freedom
