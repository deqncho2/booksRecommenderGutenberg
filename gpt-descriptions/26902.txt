Synopsis: In "The Rover Boys in Alaska; or, Lost in the Fields of Ice" by Edward Stratemeyer, the adventurous Rover boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey to the icy landscapes of Alaska. As they set out to explore the untamed wilderness, they encounter treacherous obstacles and unexpected dangers that test their courage and resourcefulness. With their lives hanging in the balance, the Rover boys must rely on their wits and brotherly bond to navigate through the frozen fields of ice and ultimately find their way back home.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Brotherhood
