Synopsis: In 'The Touch of Abner' by Cody, H. A., a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, possessing an extraordinary ability that captivates the townspeople. As the stranger's power begins to affect the lives of those around him, the community becomes divided, leading to unexpected consequences and a thrilling climax. This gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature and the power of influence, leaving readers questioning the boundaries of morality and the true nature of heroism.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Power and influence, Morality, Heroism
