Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of the Green Ray' by William Le Queux, a young woman named Phyllis Benton becomes entangled in a perplexing and dangerous adventure after witnessing a strange phenomenon known as the Green Ray. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she finds herself pursued by shadowy figures and embarks on a thrilling journey across Europe to uncover the truth behind this enigmatic occurrence. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Intrigue, Pursuit of Truth
