Synopsis: In 'A Guide for the Dissection of the Dogfish (Squalus Acanthias)', Griffin, Lawrence Edmonds takes readers on a captivating journey into the intricate anatomy of the dogfish shark. Through detailed descriptions and illustrations, the author provides a comprehensive guide for dissecting this fascinating creature, revealing its unique features and physiological functions. As readers delve into the dissection process, they uncover the secrets of the dogfish's skeletal, muscular, circulatory, and digestive systems, gaining a deeper understanding of its remarkable adaptation to its marine environment. This guide not only serves as a valuable resource for students and researchers but also offers a captivating exploration of the wonders of marine life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Education

Themes: Anatomy, Marine Biology, Scientific Exploration
