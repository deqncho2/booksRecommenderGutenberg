Synopsis: In the thrilling conclusion to 'The Cruise of the Midge', Captain Weatherall and his crew find themselves embroiled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse as they navigate treacherous waters and encounter a mysterious enemy. With their lives hanging in the balance, they must rely on their wits and courage to survive the perils that lie ahead. As secrets are revealed and alliances tested, the fate of the Midge and her crew hangs in the balance, making for a gripping and suspenseful adventure on the high seas.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Loyalty, Perseverance
