Synopsis: In 'The Young Trailers: A Story of Early Kentucky', follow the thrilling adventures of a group of young pioneers as they navigate the untamed wilderness of Kentucky in the late 18th century. Amidst the dangers of Indian attacks, treacherous terrain, and the constant struggle for survival, these brave individuals must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering determination to forge a new life in the frontier. As they face countless challenges and form unlikely alliances, the young trailblazers discover the true meaning of friendship, loyalty, and the indomitable spirit of the American pioneers.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration and Discovery, Survival, Friendship, Loyalty
