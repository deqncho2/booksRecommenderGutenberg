Synopsis: In 'The Intrusions of Peggy' by Hope, Anthony, a young woman named Peggy finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Peggy becomes the target of a dangerous adversary who will stop at nothing to protect the truth from being revealed. With her life on the line, Peggy must navigate a treacherous path filled with deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, all while trying to uncover the dark secrets that have haunted her family for generations.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Betrayal, Survival
