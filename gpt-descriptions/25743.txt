Synopsis: In 'Fearful Symmetry: A Terran Empire novel' by Wilson, Ann, a young scientist named Emma discovers a hidden message in an ancient artifact that could change the course of the Terran Empire forever. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets, betrayal, and a race against time to save her world from impending doom. With unexpected allies and formidable enemies, Emma must navigate a treacherous path where nothing is as it seems, and the fate of the entire empire hangs in the balance.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Power and corruption, Identity and self-discovery, The consequences of knowledge
