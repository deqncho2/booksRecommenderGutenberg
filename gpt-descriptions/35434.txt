Synopsis: 
'Above the Snow Line: Mountaineering Sketches Between 1870 and 1880' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the breathtaking world of mountaineering in the late 19th century. Written by C. T. Dent, an experienced mountaineer himself, this collection of sketches offers a captivating glimpse into the challenges, triumphs, and awe-inspiring beauty of scaling some of the world's most treacherous peaks. From daring ascents to unexpected encounters, each sketch paints a vivid picture of the exhilarating and often perilous world of mountaineering.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Travel

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Nature's Majesty
