Synopsis: 
In 'A Book of the West. Volume 1: Devon: Being an introduction to Devon and Cornwall', Sabine Baring-Gould takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of Devon and Cornwall. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Baring-Gould explores the rich history, folklore, and natural beauty of these regions, uncovering hidden tales and legends that have shaped their identity. From ancient ruins to picturesque villages, from rugged coastlines to mystical moors, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the unique charm and allure of Devon and Cornwall.

Genres: Travel, History, Folklore

Themes: Regional identity, Cultural heritage, Natural beauty
