Synopsis: In the ancient city of Numantia, a small but fiercely determined group of warriors stands against the mighty Roman Empire. As the Romans lay siege to the city, the inhabitants of Numantia face a choice between surrender and annihilation. Amidst the chaos and despair, love, honor, and the indomitable spirit of resistance emerge, shaping the destiny of the city and its people.

Genres: Historical fiction, tragedy, war drama

Themes: Resistance, honor, love, the clash of civilizations
