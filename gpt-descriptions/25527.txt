Synopsis: 

Embark on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Australia, as the Department of External Affairs unveils the hidden treasures of 'Australia, The Dairy Country'. This enthralling book takes readers on a mesmerizing exploration of the country's rich dairy industry, revealing the untold stories of hardworking farmers, the secrets behind their world-renowned products, and the challenges they face in preserving their heritage. From the lush green pastures to the bustling dairy farms, this immersive narrative paints a vivid portrait of a nation deeply rooted in its agricultural traditions. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, Agriculture

Themes: Agriculture, Sustainability, Cultural Heritage
