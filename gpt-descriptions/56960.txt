Synopsis: In 'The Midnight Guest: A Detective Story', a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious guest arrives at a secluded mansion in the dead of night. As the detective delves deeper into the secrets of the mansion's inhabitants, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden motives. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and red herrings to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Investigation
