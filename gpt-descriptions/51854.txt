Synopsis: In 'The Rat Race' by Franklin Jay, a young and ambitious executive finds himself caught in a cutthroat corporate world where success comes at a high price. As he navigates through office politics, backstabbing colleagues, and relentless competition, he must question his own values and priorities. With his career and personal life hanging in the balance, he must decide how far he is willing to go to achieve his dreams.

Genres: Drama, Thriller, Satire

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Workplace dynamics
