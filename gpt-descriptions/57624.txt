Synopsis: In Savage Africa: Or, The adventures of Frank Baldwin from the Gold Coast to Zanzibar takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed landscapes of Africa. Frank Baldwin, a courageous explorer, embarks on a perilous expedition from the Gold Coast to Zanzibar, encountering treacherous terrain, wild animals, and hostile tribes along the way. As he navigates through the heart of Africa, Frank must rely on his wit, resilience, and the help of unexpected allies to survive the dangers that lie in wait. This gripping tale of adventure and survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies ahead for Frank Baldwin.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural Encounter
