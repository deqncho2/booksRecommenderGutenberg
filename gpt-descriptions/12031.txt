Synopsis: 
'Collected Poems 1901-1918 in Two Volumes. Volume I.' by Walter De la Mare takes readers on a captivating journey through the early 20th century. This collection of poems, carefully curated by the author, delves into the depths of human emotions, exploring themes of love, loss, nature, and the mysteries of the human psyche. With each poem, De la Mare weaves a tapestry of vivid imagery and lyrical language, inviting readers to reflect on the beauty and complexity of life. From hauntingly melancholic verses to whimsical and enchanting tales, this volume offers a glimpse into the poetic brilliance of one of the most celebrated writers of his time.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Fiction

Themes: Love, Loss, Nature, Human Psyche
