Synopsis: In "The Man Who Wouldn't Sign Up" by Tom Purdom, a young man finds himself torn between his duty to his country and his personal beliefs. As the world around him is consumed by war, he becomes a symbol of resistance, refusing to conform to societal expectations. With his refusal to enlist, he becomes an outcast, facing the consequences of his choices. This gripping tale explores the complexities of patriotism, individualism, and the power of standing up for what one believes in.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Drama

Themes: Patriotism, Individualism, Conformity, Morality
