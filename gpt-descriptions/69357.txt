Synopsis: In 'Forgotten World' by Edmond Hamilton, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous expedition to a remote and mysterious island, where he uncovers ancient secrets that challenge everything he thought he knew about the world. As he delves deeper into the forgotten civilization, he becomes entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and a race against time to protect the knowledge that could change humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Ancient Civilizations, Knowledge and Power
