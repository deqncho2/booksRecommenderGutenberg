Synopsis: 
When Matthew Cuthbert decides to adopt a boy to help him and his sister Marilla on their farm, he is in for a surprise. Instead of a boy, he is sent a spirited and imaginative red-haired girl named Anne Shirley. Despite the initial mix-up, Anne quickly finds her place in the hearts of the Cuthberts and the small town of Avonlea. Follow Anne's journey as she navigates the challenges and joys of growing up, forming friendships, and discovering her own unique identity in the picturesque setting of Prince Edward Island.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Fiction, Classic

Themes: 
Identity, Friendship, Imagination, Acceptance
