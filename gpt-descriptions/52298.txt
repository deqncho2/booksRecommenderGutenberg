Synopsis: In 'Budge & Toddie; Or, Helen's Babies at Play' by John Habberton, two mischievous toddlers, Budge and Toddie, embark on a series of hilarious adventures that keep their caretakers on their toes. From causing chaos at a picnic to wreaking havoc in a toy store, these pint-sized troublemakers bring laughter and mayhem wherever they go. As their antics escalate, the adults struggle to maintain control, leading to a delightful and unpredictable tale of childhood mischief.

Genres: Humor, Children's Literature

Themes: Childhood innocence, Parenting challenges
