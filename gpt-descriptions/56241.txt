Synopsis: 
In 'The Indian Bangle' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young woman named Helen is gifted an exquisite Indian bangle. Little does she know that this seemingly innocent piece of jewelry holds a dark secret that will lead her on a thrilling journey of love, betrayal, and redemption. As Helen delves deeper into the bangle's history, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Helen must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Love and betrayal, Redemption and self-discovery
