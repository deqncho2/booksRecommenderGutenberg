Synopsis: 

In "Historical record of the Eighty-Sixth, or the Royal County Down Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes us on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment from 1793 to 1842. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, Cannon delves into the triumphs and challenges faced by the soldiers, offering a fascinating glimpse into the history of this esteemed military unit.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Bravery, Military strategy
