Synopsis: In 'Experiments on Animals' by Stephen Paget, a renowned scientist embarks on a groundbreaking research project that pushes the boundaries of ethical experimentation. As he delves deeper into his work, he becomes entangled in a web of moral dilemmas and personal sacrifices, forcing him to question the very nature of scientific progress. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking revelations, this gripping tale explores the fine line between scientific advancement and the ethical treatment of animals.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Ethics, Scientific Responsibility, Moral Dilemmas
