Synopsis: In "The Jacquard Machine Analyzed and Explained," E. A. Posselt delves into the intricacies of the revolutionary Jacquard machine, unraveling its secrets and providing practical guidance for aspiring jacquard designers. With an appendix on the preparation of jacquard cards and insightful hints, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the machine's inner workings and its potential for artistic expression. Whether you are a curious reader or a budding designer, this captivating guide will unveil the fascinating world of jacquard weaving.

Genres: Non-fiction, Technology, Art

Themes: Textile industry, Innovation, Design
