Synopsis: In "The Cockaynes in Paris; Or, 'Gone abroad'," the eccentric Cockayne family embarks on a hilarious adventure in the vibrant city of Paris. As they navigate the French capital's cultural wonders and peculiarities, their comical misadventures and encounters with colorful characters will keep readers entertained and laughing throughout. From mistaken identities to language barriers, the Cockaynes' escapades in Paris are a delightful blend of humor, satire, and cultural observations.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Travelogue

Themes: Cultural differences, Identity, Language barriers
