Synopsis: 
'The Heart of Mid-Lothian, Complete' by Walter Scott is a captivating historical novel set in 18th-century Scotland. The story follows the life of Jeanie Deans, a strong-willed and virtuous young woman who embarks on a perilous journey to save her sister from a wrongful conviction. As Jeanie faces numerous challenges and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, including the notorious criminal George Robertson, the novel delves into themes of justice, sacrifice, and the power of love. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling narrative, 'The Heart of Mid-Lothian' is a gripping tale that will keep readers enthralled until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Justice, Sacrifice, Love
