Synopsis: 
'Modern Prose And Poetry; For Secondary Schools: Edited With Notes, Study Helps, And Reading Lists' is a captivating anthology that brings together a diverse collection of contemporary prose and poetry. From thought-provoking short stories to poignant poems, this book offers a rich tapestry of literary works that will engage and inspire readers. With comprehensive notes, study aids, and reading lists, it serves as an invaluable resource for secondary school students, providing them with the tools to delve deeper into the world of literature and develop a deeper appreciation for the written word.

Genres: Anthology, Education, Literature

Themes: Diversity, Exploration of Human Experience, Literary Analysis
