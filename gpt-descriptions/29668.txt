Synopsis: In 'The Flockmaster of Poison Creek', a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Poison Creek, bringing with him a flock of peculiar sheep. As the flockmaster's presence disrupts the quiet lives of the townspeople, tensions rise and secrets are revealed. With the fate of the town hanging in the balance, the residents must confront their own fears and desires, leading to unexpected consequences that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Community
