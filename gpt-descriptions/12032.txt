Synopsis: 
'Collected Poems 1901-1918 in Two Volumes. Volume II.' by Walter De la Mare is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through the early 20th century. This volume delves into the depths of human emotions, exploring themes of love, loss, nature, and the mysteries of the human psyche. With his lyrical and evocative language, De la Mare paints vivid pictures that transport readers to ethereal landscapes and delve into the complexities of the human experience. From hauntingly beautiful verses to thought-provoking reflections, this collection is a testament to De la Mare's mastery of the poetic craft.

Genres: Poetry, Romanticism, Symbolism
Themes: Love, Loss, Nature, Human Psyche
