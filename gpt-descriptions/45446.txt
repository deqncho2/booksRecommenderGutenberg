Synopsis: In 'Makers of Electricity', James J. Walsh and Brother Potamian take readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the brilliant minds behind the invention of electricity. From the early pioneers to the modern-day innovators, this book unveils the untold stories of these extraordinary individuals and their groundbreaking discoveries. As the pages turn, readers will be enthralled by the struggles, triumphs, and sheer brilliance of these inventors, shedding light on the incredible impact they have had on the world we live in today.

Genres: Biography, History, Science

Themes: Innovation, Scientific Discovery, Human Ingenuity
