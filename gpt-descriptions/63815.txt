Synopsis: 

In 'Frank Merriwell on the Road; Or, The All-Star Combination' by Standish, Burt L., Frank Merriwell, the beloved college athlete, finds himself embarking on a thrilling journey that takes him far from the familiar grounds of Yale University. Joined by a group of talented friends, Frank becomes part of an extraordinary team known as the All-Star Combination. As they travel across the country, facing various challenges and adversaries, Frank must rely on his exceptional skills, intelligence, and unwavering determination to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. With each new adventure, Frank and his companions discover the true meaning of teamwork, loyalty, and the importance of never giving up.

Genres: Adventure, Sports, Young Adult

Themes: Teamwork, Friendship, Perseverance
