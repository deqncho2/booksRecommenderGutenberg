Synopsis: In 'The First Easter' by James Hartwell Willard, a young girl named Lily embarks on a magical adventure that takes her back in time to witness the very first Easter. As she navigates through ancient Jerusalem, she encounters biblical figures and experiences the profound events leading up to the resurrection of Jesus Christ. With each encounter, Lily's faith is tested and strengthened, ultimately leading her to a life-changing revelation.

Genres: Christian fiction, historical fiction, time travel

Themes: Faith, redemption, the power of belief, the significance of Easter
