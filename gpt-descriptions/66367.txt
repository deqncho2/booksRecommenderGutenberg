Synopsis: In the picturesque village of Oakford, a young woman named Jenny leads a seemingly idyllic life. But beneath the surface of this charming community lies a web of secrets, scandals, and forbidden love. As Jenny navigates the complexities of village life, she becomes entangled in a series of unexpected events that will challenge her beliefs and force her to confront the truth about herself and those around her. With its captivating characters and intriguing plot twists, 'Jenny: A Village Idyl' is a compelling tale that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Small-town life, Secrets and lies, Love and betrayal
