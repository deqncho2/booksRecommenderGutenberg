Synopsis: In 'The Daughters of a Genius', Mrs. Vaizey introduces us to the charming and unconventional Fairfax family. The story revolves around the three daughters of the brilliant but absent-minded Professor Fairfax, who find themselves navigating the challenges of love, society, and personal growth. As they encounter various suitors, unexpected friendships, and societal expectations, the sisters must balance their own desires with the expectations placed upon them. With wit, humor, and a touch of romance, this delightful novel takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and relationships, Family dynamics, Individuality and societal expectations
