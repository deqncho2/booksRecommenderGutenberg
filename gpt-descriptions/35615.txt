Synopsis: In 'The Treasure of the Tigris: A Tale of Mesopotamia' by A. F. Mockler-Ferryman, a thrilling adventure unfolds in the ancient land of Mesopotamia. When a young archaeologist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact, he becomes entangled in a dangerous quest to uncover the legendary Treasure of the Tigris. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the past, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, outsmart cunning adversaries, and decipher cryptic clues. With each step closer to the treasure, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. Will he succeed in his quest, or will the ancient power of the Tigris remain forever hidden?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Ancient Civilizations, Greed, Betrayal
