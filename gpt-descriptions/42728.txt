Synopsis: 
In 'Forbidden Cargoes' by Roy J. Snell, a young and adventurous journalist named Jerry is on a mission to uncover the truth behind a mysterious ship that has been smuggling illegal goods into the country. As he delves deeper into the dangerous world of smuggling, Jerry finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With his life on the line, he must navigate treacherous waters and outsmart cunning criminals to expose the truth and bring justice to those involved.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Crime and justice, Deception and betrayal, Courage and determination
