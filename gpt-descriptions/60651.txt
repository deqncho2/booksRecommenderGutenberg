Synopsis: 
In the small town of Willowbrook, Dr. Wainwright is known for his exceptional medical skills and compassionate care. When a mysterious patient arrives at his clinic, the doctor's life takes an unexpected turn. As he delves deeper into the patient's enigmatic past, Dr. Wainwright uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that threatens to unravel not only his patient's life but also his own. With each revelation, the doctor finds himself questioning his own ethics and the true nature of his profession. As the lines between right and wrong blur, Dr. Wainwright must confront the consequences of his actions and make a choice that will forever change his life.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Morality, Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
