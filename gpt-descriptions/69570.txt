Synopsis: In "Friends and Cousins" by Abbie Farwell Brown, two young girls, Anne and Dorothy, find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of adventures and misadventures during a summer spent with their extended family. From exploring hidden passageways in an old mansion to unraveling family secrets, the girls navigate the complexities of friendship, family, and the power of imagination. As they uncover the mysteries of their ancestors, Anne and Dorothy learn valuable lessons about loyalty, forgiveness, and the enduring bonds of kinship.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Family, Imagination, Discovery
