Synopsis: In "The Rose of Paradise," Captain John Mackra recounts his thrilling encounters with the notorious pirate Edward England in the year 1720. Set off the Island of Juanna in the Mozambique Channel, this gripping tale follows Mackra as he navigates treacherous waters, battles with England's crew, and faces unexpected challenges. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this first-hand account offers a captivating glimpse into the dangerous world of piracy during the Golden Age. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Pirates, Survival, Loyalty, Morality
