Synopsis: In 'The Death From Orion' by Matthews, W. J., a renowned astrophysicist makes a shocking discovery that threatens the very fabric of reality. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger, where the fate of humanity hangs in the balance. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic Death From Orion.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Existentialism, Conspiracy, Human Potential
