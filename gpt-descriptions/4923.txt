Synopsis: In 'King Midas: a Romance' by Sinclair, Upton, a wealthy and powerful man finds himself trapped in a life of material excess and unfulfilled desires. Seeking a way to break free from his golden prison, he embarks on a journey that will challenge his beliefs and force him to confront the consequences of his insatiable greed. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of his own existence, he discovers that true wealth lies not in possessions, but in the intangible treasures of love, compassion, and self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Allegory

Themes: Greed, Materialism, Self-discovery, Redemption
