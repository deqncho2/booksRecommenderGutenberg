Synopsis: In 'English Grammar in Familiar Lectures', Samuel Kirkham presents a comprehensive guide to English grammar, accompanied by a compendium of exercises and a new systematic order of parsing. The book also introduces a fresh system of punctuation, exercises in false syntax, and a system of philosophical grammar. Designed for both schools and private learners, this work aims to provide a clear and accessible understanding of English grammar through engaging lectures and practical exercises.

Genres: Educational, Reference, Language Learning

Themes: English grammar, Language instruction, Learning and education
