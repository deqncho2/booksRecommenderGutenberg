Synopsis: 
'The Defeat of Youth, and Other Poems' by Aldous Huxley is a captivating collection of poems that delves into the complexities of human existence and the struggles faced by the younger generation. Through vivid imagery and thought-provoking verses, Huxley explores themes of love, loss, disillusionment, and the search for meaning in a rapidly changing world. From the depths of despair to moments of profound beauty, these poems offer a poignant reflection on the human condition and the challenges of navigating the tumultuous journey of youth.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Fiction

Themes: Youth, Love, Loss, Disillusionment, Existentialism
