Synopsis: In "What Have the Greeks Done for Modern Civilisation?" J.P. Mahaffy takes readers on a captivating journey through the profound influence of ancient Greek culture on our modern world. From philosophy and democracy to art and literature, Mahaffy explores the enduring legacy of the Greeks and their contributions that continue to shape our society today. Through engaging anecdotes and insightful analysis, this book unveils the remarkable impact of the Greeks on various aspects of modern civilization, leaving readers with a newfound appreciation for the rich heritage we owe to this ancient civilization.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Cultural heritage, Intellectual legacy, Influence of ancient civilizations
