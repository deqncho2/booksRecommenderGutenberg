Synopsis: 
'Legends That Every Child Should Know' is a captivating collection of timeless legends from various cultures and eras, carefully selected and retold by Hamilton Wright Mabie. From the heroic tales of King Arthur and Robin Hood to the enchanting stories of Greek mythology and Arabian Nights, this book offers a delightful journey into the world of legends and folklore. Each story is filled with adventure, magic, and moral lessons, captivating young readers and igniting their imagination. Whether it's the epic battles of gods and heroes or the cunning tricks of legendary figures, these legends are sure to entertain and inspire children of all ages.

Genres: 
Folklore, Mythology, Children's Literature

Themes: 
Heroism, Adventure, Morality, Magic, Cultural Diversity
