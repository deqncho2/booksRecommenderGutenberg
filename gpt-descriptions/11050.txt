Synopsis: In the small Italian village of Taquisara, a young American artist named Maurice meets a captivating woman named Corona. As their paths intertwine, Maurice becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and mystery. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Italian countryside, Taquisara explores the complexities of human relationships and the consequences of one's choices.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love and passion, Betrayal, Identity and self-discovery
