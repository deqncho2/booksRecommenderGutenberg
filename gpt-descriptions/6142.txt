Synopsis: In 'A Girl of the People', we follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Mabel, who finds herself torn between the expectations of her social class and her desire for independence. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society, Mabel must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and self-discovery, as she strives to find her place in a world that seems determined to define it for her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Social class, Gender roles, Identity, Self-determination
