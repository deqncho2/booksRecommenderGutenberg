Synopsis: In 'With Edged Tools' by Henry Seton Merriman, a young Englishman finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and danger while on a journey through Eastern Europe. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters mysterious characters, he must confront his own beliefs and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Political Thriller

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Betrayal, Moral Dilemmas
