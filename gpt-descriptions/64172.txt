Synopsis: In 'Scrambled World' by Basil Wells, a young woman named Alice discovers a hidden portal that leads her to a parallel universe where everything is turned upside down. As she navigates this topsy-turvy world, she encounters bizarre creatures, mind-bending landscapes, and a mysterious figure who holds the key to her return. With each twist and turn, Alice must unravel the secrets of this scrambled world and find her way back home.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Parallel universes, Identity, Exploration
