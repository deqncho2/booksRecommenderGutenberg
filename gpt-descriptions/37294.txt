Synopsis: In 'The Red Cross Barge' by Marie Belloc Lowndes, a mysterious barge appears on the Thames, catching the attention of the locals. As the story unfolds, secrets, love affairs, and unexpected connections are revealed, all centered around the enigmatic barge and its inhabitants. With a blend of suspense and romance, this captivating tale takes readers on a journey filled with intrigue and unexpected twists.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Love and relationships, Intrigue and suspense
