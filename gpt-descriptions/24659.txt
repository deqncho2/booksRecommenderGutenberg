Synopsis:
In 'Hortense: Makers of History Series', John S. C. Abbott takes us on a captivating journey through the life of Hortense de Beauharnais, stepdaughter of Napoleon Bonaparte. From her tumultuous childhood to her rise as a prominent figure in French society, Hortense's story is one of resilience, love, and political intrigue. As she navigates the complexities of her relationships and the ever-changing political landscape, Hortense's strength and determination shine through, making her a truly remarkable historical figure.

Genres:
Historical fiction, Biography, Romance

Themes:
Resilience, Love, Political intrigue
