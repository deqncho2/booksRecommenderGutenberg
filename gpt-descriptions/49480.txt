Synopsis: In 'Poison: A Farce' by George M. Baker, a seemingly ordinary dinner party takes a hilariously unexpected turn when a case of mistaken identity leads to a series of comical misunderstandings and outrageous situations. As the guests navigate their way through a web of confusion, secrets, and hidden agendas, the line between truth and deception becomes increasingly blurred. With witty dialogue, eccentric characters, and a fast-paced plot, this farcical tale will keep readers guessing and laughing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Mystery

Themes: Mistaken identity, Deception, Secrets, Humor
