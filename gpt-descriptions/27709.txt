Synopsis: In 'Five O'Clock Tea: Farce' by William Dean Howells, a seemingly innocent tea party turns into a hilarious and chaotic affair when unexpected guests arrive, leading to a series of misunderstandings, mistaken identities, and uproarious situations. As the characters navigate through the comical chaos, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of human behavior is exposed. This delightful farce will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly anticipating the next twist and turn.

Genres: Farce, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Social conventions, Deception, Human folly
