Synopsis: In 'Anguish Languish' by Chace, Howard L., embark on a hilarious linguistic adventure where familiar phrases are hilariously twisted and turned into nonsensical yet strangely comprehensible tales. Follow the misadventures of characters like Little Red Riding Hood, Jack and the Beanstalk, and more, as they navigate a world where words take on a whole new meaning. With clever wordplay and absurd situations, this book will leave you laughing and scratching your head in equal measure.

Genres: Humor, Language, Fiction

Themes: Wordplay, Miscommunication
