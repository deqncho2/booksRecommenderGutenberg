Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Jules Verne' is a captivating collection of works by the renowned author Jules Verne. This compilation includes some of Verne's most beloved and imaginative stories, taking readers on thrilling adventures to the depths of the ocean, the heights of the sky, and even to the center of the Earth. From the mysterious island of Captain Nemo to the extraordinary journey around the world in 80 days, this index offers a glimpse into Verne's extraordinary imagination and his ability to transport readers to fantastical worlds.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Technology, Human Ingenuity
