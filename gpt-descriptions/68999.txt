Synopsis: In 'The Kopje Farm' by William Johnston, a young couple, Sarah and David, embark on a journey to start a new life on a remote farm in Africa. As they settle into their new surroundings, they encounter unexpected challenges and mysteries that test their resilience and love for each other. With the backdrop of the breathtaking African landscape, the couple must navigate through personal struggles, cultural differences, and the haunting secrets of the farm. As they uncover the truth, they are forced to confront their own fears and make difficult choices that will shape their future.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Cultural exploration, Secrets and revelations
