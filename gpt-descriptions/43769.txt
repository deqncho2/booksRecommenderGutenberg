Synopsis: In 'A Little Girl in Old Pittsburgh' by Amanda M. Douglas, readers are transported to the bustling city of Pittsburgh in the late 19th century, where they follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Ruth. As Ruth navigates the challenges of growing up in a rapidly changing industrial city, she encounters unexpected friendships, family secrets, and the transformative power of love. With vivid descriptions of the city's landmarks and the struggles faced by its inhabitants, this heartwarming tale offers a glimpse into the past and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: Growth and Transformation, Friendship, Secrets and Discovery
