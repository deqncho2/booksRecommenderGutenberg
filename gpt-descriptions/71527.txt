Synopsis: In "14000 miles, a carriage and two women" by Frances S. Howe, a daring adventure unfolds as two women embark on a journey that spans continents and challenges societal norms. Set in the late 19th century, this captivating tale follows the lives of two courageous women who defy expectations and set out on a remarkable expedition. As they traverse through diverse landscapes, encounter unexpected obstacles, and form an unbreakable bond, their journey becomes a testament to the power of friendship, resilience, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Women's Fiction

Themes: Female Empowerment, Friendship, Breaking Barriers, Self-Discovery
