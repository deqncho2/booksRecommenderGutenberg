Synopsis: In "A Christmas Garland," Sir Max Beerbohm takes readers on a delightful journey through the festive season, exploring the various traditions, characters, and customs associated with Christmas. From the bustling streets of London to the cozy countryside, Beerbohm's witty observations and charming anecdotes capture the essence of this beloved holiday, offering a unique and humorous perspective on the joys and absurdities of Christmas.

Genres: Fiction, Humor, Satire

Themes: Christmas traditions, Holiday spirit, Social commentary
