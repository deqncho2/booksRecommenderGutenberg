Synopsis: In 'The Robber Baron of Bedford Castle', a mysterious figure emerges from the shadows of Bedford Castle, wreaking havoc on the lives of its inhabitants. As the townspeople become increasingly fearful, a young and determined detective sets out to uncover the truth behind the robber baron's identity and motives. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Power and corruption, Justice and morality, Identity and deception
