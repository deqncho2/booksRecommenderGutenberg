Synopsis: 
In 'How to Succeed as an Inventor', Smith, Goodwin Brooke takes readers on an exciting journey into the world of invention, revealing the boundless possibilities and the pitfalls to avoid. This comprehensive guide explores the process of perfecting and developing new ideas, ultimately transforming them into lucrative ventures. Filled with practical advice and inspiring anecdotes, this book is a must-read for aspiring inventors seeking to navigate the complex landscape of innovation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Business

Themes: Invention, Entrepreneurship, Success
