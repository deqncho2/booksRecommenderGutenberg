Synopsis: In 'Spadacrene Anglica: The English Spa Fountain', Edmund Deane takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of medicinal springs and the healing powers they possess. Set in 17th-century England, the story follows a young protagonist who embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets of a renowned spa fountain. As the protagonist delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the fountain, they encounter a cast of intriguing characters and face unexpected challenges. With vivid descriptions and a touch of mystery, Deane's narrative explores the transformative power of nature and the pursuit of knowledge.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Healing powers of nature, Quest for knowledge
