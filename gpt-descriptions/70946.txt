Synopsis: 

In "Constantinople Old and New" by Dwight, H. G., embark on a captivating journey through the vibrant city of Constantinople, where ancient history intertwines with modern marvels. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, the author paints a vivid picture of the city's rich cultural heritage, architectural wonders, and the bustling energy of its inhabitants. From the majestic Hagia Sophia to the bustling Grand Bazaar, readers will be transported to a world where East meets West, and tradition merges with progress. As the narrative unfolds, unexpected encounters and hidden secrets add an element of mystery and intrigue, keeping readers eagerly turning the pages. 

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Cultural heritage, Architecture, East meets West, Tradition and progress
