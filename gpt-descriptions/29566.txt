Synopsis: In 'The Centurion's Story' by David James Burrell, a seasoned Roman centurion finds himself torn between duty and compassion when he is assigned to oversee the crucifixion of a man named Jesus. As he witnesses the events surrounding Jesus' death, the centurion's beliefs and loyalties are challenged, leading him on a transformative journey that will forever change his life.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Religious Fiction, Drama

Themes: Faith and Doubt, Redemption, Moral Dilemmas
