Synopsis: In 'The House from Nowhere' by Arthur G. Stangland, a young couple stumbles upon an abandoned house in the middle of nowhere. Intrigued by its mysterious presence, they decide to explore its secrets, only to find themselves trapped in a web of inexplicable events and supernatural occurrences. As they delve deeper into the house's dark history, they uncover a haunting truth that threatens to consume them. With each twist and turn, the couple must confront their deepest fears and fight to escape the clutches of the house's malevolent power.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Haunting, Fear, Secrets, Supernatural forces
