Synopsis: In 'Cue for Quiet' by T. L. Sherred, a brilliant scientist invents a device that allows people to hear the thoughts of others, unraveling the mysteries of the human mind. However, as the technology spreads, it becomes clear that not all thoughts are meant to be heard, leading to unforeseen consequences and a race against time to restore peace and privacy. This gripping science fiction novel explores the ethical implications of mind-reading technology and the delicate balance between knowledge and privacy.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Privacy, Human Nature
