Synopsis: In 'Youngling: A Terran Empire story' by Wilson, Ann, follow the thrilling journey of a young orphan named Alex as he navigates the treacherous world of the Terran Empire. With his extraordinary abilities and a mysterious past, Alex finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue, dangerous alliances, and a secret that could change the fate of the empire forever. As he uncovers the truth about his origins, Alex must confront his own identity and make choices that will shape the destiny of not only himself but also the entire empire.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Loyalty
