Synopsis: 

'Alchemy: Ancient and Modern' delves into the fascinating world of alchemy, exploring its doctrines and their connections to mysticism and recent discoveries in physical science. The book provides a brief account of the lives and teachings of the most renowned alchemists, shedding light on their enigmatic practices and beliefs. From the ancient alchemists who sought to transmute base metals into gold, to the modern alchemists who delve into the realms of consciousness and spirituality, this work offers a captivating exploration of the history and evolution of alchemy.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Occult

Themes: Alchemy, Mysticism, Science, Spiritual Transformation
