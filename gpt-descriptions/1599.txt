Synopsis: In 'Cinderella; Or, The Little Glass Slipper, and Other Stories', we are transported into a world of enchantment and wonder. This collection of timeless tales introduces us to the captivating story of Cinderella, a young girl whose life takes an unexpected turn when she receives a magical gift. As we follow her journey, we encounter other intriguing characters and their extraordinary adventures. From talking animals to mystical transformations, these stories are filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. Prepare to be swept away by the magic and charm of these classic tales!

Genres: Fairy tale, Fantasy, Children's literature

Themes: Transformation, Love and kindness, Overcoming adversity
