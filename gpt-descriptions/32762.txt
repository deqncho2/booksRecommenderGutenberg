Synopsis: In "Michelangelo" by Romain Rolland, delve into the captivating life of the renowned Italian artist, Michelangelo Buonarroti. From his humble beginnings as a young sculptor to his grand masterpieces that shaped the Renaissance era, this biographical novel takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the artist's triumphs, struggles, and the profound impact he left on the world. With vivid descriptions and intimate insights, Rolland paints a vivid portrait of Michelangelo's artistic genius, his complex relationships, and the relentless pursuit of his artistic vision. Prepare to be enthralled by the life of one of history's greatest artists.

Genres: Biographical Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Artistic passion, Renaissance, Human struggle and triumph
