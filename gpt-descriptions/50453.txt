Synopsis:
In 'The Pest' by W. Teignmouth Shore, a small English village is plagued by a mysterious and malevolent force that threatens the lives of its inhabitants. As the tension rises and the danger escalates, the villagers must confront their deepest fears and unravel the dark secrets that lie hidden within their community. With each page, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of suspense and intrigue, as they try to uncover the truth behind the haunting presence that haunts the village.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes:
Fear, Secrets, Community
