Synopsis: 
In 'Hilda Lessways' by Arnold Bennett, we follow the captivating journey of Hilda, a young woman with a strong will and an insatiable desire for independence. Set in the early 20th century, Hilda navigates the challenges of love, societal expectations, and her own personal growth. As she encounters various characters and experiences, Hilda's determination to forge her own path becomes both inspiring and thought-provoking. With unexpected twists and turns, this novel explores the complexities of self-discovery and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: 
Drama, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Independence, Love, Societal Expectations, Personal Growth
