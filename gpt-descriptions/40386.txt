Synopsis: In 'Wandering Ghosts' by F. Marion Crawford, a young man named Paul Griggs finds himself drawn into a mysterious and haunting world when he moves into an old house in Rome. As he delves deeper into the history of the house and its former inhabitants, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, supernatural occurrences, and the restless spirits that wander the halls. With each encounter, Paul's curiosity grows, leading him on a chilling journey that will test his courage and sanity.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, mystery

Themes: Hauntings, the power of the past, the thin line between reality and the supernatural
