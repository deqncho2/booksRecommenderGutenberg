Synopsis: In 'Gentlemen of the Jury: A Farce', chaos ensues when a group of eccentric jurors are tasked with deciding the fate of a man accused of a heinous crime. As the trial unfolds, the jurors' outrageous personalities and comical antics take center stage, turning the courtroom into a hilarious battleground of wit and absurdity. With each juror bringing their own unique quirks and biases to the table, the outcome of the trial becomes uncertain, leading to unexpected twists and uproarious moments that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Farce, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Justice, Prejudice, Absurdity, Human nature
