Synopsis: In 'The Red Lily - Volume 01' by Anatole France, a young woman named Sabine falls in love with a mysterious man who enters her life unexpectedly. As their passionate affair unfolds, Sabine finds herself torn between her desire for love and the constraints of society. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Paris, this captivating novel explores the complexities of love, desire, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Society and Conformity, Personal Freedom
