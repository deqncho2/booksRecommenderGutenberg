Synopsis: 
'Animal Life of the British Isles: A Pocket Guide to the Mammals, Reptiles and Batrachians of Wayside and Woodland' by Step, Edward is a captivating and informative guide that takes readers on a journey through the diverse wildlife found in the British Isles. From the elusive mammals that roam the countryside to the fascinating reptiles and batrachians that inhabit the woodlands, this pocket guide provides a comprehensive overview of the animal life in these regions. Filled with stunning illustrations and detailed descriptions, Step's guide is a must-have for nature enthusiasts and anyone curious about the incredible creatures that call the British Isles home.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Guide, Wildlife

Themes: Biodiversity, Conservation, Natural History
