Synopsis: In 'The Haunted Man and the Ghost's Bargain', a tormented professor makes a pact with a ghostly figure that promises to rid him of his painful memories. However, as the professor's past is erased, he soon realizes the devastating consequences of his decision. This haunting tale explores the depths of human emotions and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, psychological thriller

Themes: Regret, redemption, the nature of memory, the consequences of one's actions
