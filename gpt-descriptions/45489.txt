Synopsis: 
In 'Stories of Old Greece and Rome', Emilie K. Baker takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient myths and legends of Greece and Rome. From the heroic adventures of Hercules to the tragic love story of Pyramus and Thisbe, this collection of tales brings to life the rich tapestry of gods, goddesses, and mortals that populated the ancient world. With vivid storytelling and colorful descriptions, Baker transports readers to a time of gods and goddesses, heroes and villains, and epic battles that shaped the foundations of Western civilization. Whether it's the cunning Odysseus outsmarting his enemies or the beautiful Aphrodite causing mischief, each story is filled with excitement, drama, and timeless lessons that still resonate today. 

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Historical Fiction

Themes: Heroism, Love and Betrayal, Fate and Destiny, Gods and Mortals
