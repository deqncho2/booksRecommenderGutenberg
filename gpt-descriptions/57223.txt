Synopsis: In 'Airplane Boys Discover the Secrets of Cuzco' by Craine, E. J., two adventurous brothers embark on a thrilling journey to the ancient city of Cuzco in Peru. As they explore the mysterious ruins and encounter unexpected challenges, they uncover hidden secrets that could change their lives forever. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must rely on their wit, courage, and the help of newfound friends to unravel the enigma of Cuzco.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Cultural Heritage
