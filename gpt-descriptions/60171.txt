Synopsis: 
'Vahram's Chronicle of the Armenian Kingdom in Cilicia, during the Time of the Crusades' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous era of the Crusades, as seen through the eyes of Vahram, a nobleman in the Armenian Kingdom of Cilicia. As the Crusaders clash with the Muslim forces, Vahram finds himself caught in the crossfire, torn between loyalty to his kingdom and his own personal quest for justice. With vivid descriptions of battles, political intrigue, and personal struggles, this chronicle offers a unique perspective on a lesser-known chapter of history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Justice, Identity, Conflict
