Synopsis: In 'Elves and Heroes' by Donald A. Mackenzie, embark on a captivating journey through ancient folklore and mythology as the author delves into the enchanting realms of elves and heroes. From the mystical lands of Celtic mythology to the epic tales of Norse sagas, this book unveils the hidden secrets and extraordinary adventures of these legendary beings. Discover the untold stories of valiant heroes, cunning tricksters, and ethereal elves, as Mackenzie weaves together a tapestry of enchantment and wonder.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Magic, Mythical Creatures, Legends
