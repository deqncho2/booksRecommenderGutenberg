Synopsis: In 'A Mummer's Wife' by Moore, George, we are introduced to Kate Ede, a young woman who finds herself trapped in a loveless marriage. As she navigates the gritty streets of London, she becomes entangled in a web of desire, betrayal, and scandal. With vivid descriptions and complex characters, this novel explores the dark underbelly of society and the consequences of pursuing forbidden passions.

Genres: Realistic fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and desire, Marriage and infidelity, Social class and morality
