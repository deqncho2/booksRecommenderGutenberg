Synopsis: In 'Hostage of Tomorrow' by Abernathy, Robert, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Emily Parker finds herself trapped in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse when her groundbreaking invention falls into the wrong hands. As she races against time to save her creation and protect the world from its potential misuse, Emily must navigate a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred, leaving Emily questioning who she can trust and what she is willing to sacrifice to ensure a better future.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Technology and its consequences, Trust and betrayal, Moral dilemmas
