Synopsis: In 'The Heart's Country' by Mary Heaton Vorse, a young woman named Ruth embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience in the face of adversity. Set against the backdrop of the early 20th century, Ruth's path intertwines with the struggles of the working class, the fight for women's rights, and the complexities of love and loyalty. As she navigates through the challenges of her time, Ruth's determination and courage lead her to unexpected places, where she finds her true purpose and the strength to follow her heart.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Social Commentary

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Social Justice, Love and Loyalty
