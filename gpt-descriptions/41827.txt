Synopsis: In "Dick Merriwell Abroad; Or, The Ban of the Terrible Ten" by Standish, Burt L., Dick Merriwell finds himself embarking on a thrilling adventure overseas. As he travels to foreign lands, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of mystery and intrigue. With his quick wit and athletic prowess, Dick must navigate treacherous situations and face formidable adversaries. Will he be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and unravel the secrets that threaten his very existence?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
