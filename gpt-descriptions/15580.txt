Synopsis: In the wild and lawless land of Pecos County, Texas, a group of ruthless rustlers terrorizes the local ranchers, leaving a trail of stolen cattle and bloodshed in their wake. When Jim Wales, a young cowboy with a mysterious past, arrives in town, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse with the rustlers. As Jim fights to protect the innocent and bring justice to the lawless land, he must confront his own demons and make a choice that could change his life forever.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Action

Themes: Justice, Redemption, Identity
