Synopsis: In 'The Book of Gud' by Hastings, Milo and Hersey, Harold, a mysterious ancient manuscript is discovered, containing a collection of enigmatic stories and riddles that challenge the reader's perception of reality. As the protagonist delves deeper into the book's secrets, they are drawn into a world where imagination and truth intertwine, leading to a mind-bending journey of self-discovery and the exploration of the human condition.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Perception, Reality, Identity, Existentialism
