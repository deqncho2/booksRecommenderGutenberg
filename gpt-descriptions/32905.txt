Synopsis: In 'Mask of Death' by Paul Ernst, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is called to investigate a series of bizarre murders. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a sinister plot that reaches far beyond what he could have imagined. With each clue leading him closer to the truth, he must navigate a treacherous world of deception and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. As the body count rises, the detective must race against time to unmask the killer before he becomes the next victim.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Danger, Investigation
