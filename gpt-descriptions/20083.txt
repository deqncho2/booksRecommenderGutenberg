Synopsis: 
In 'A Boswell of Baghdad; With Diversions', Lucas, E. V. takes readers on a captivating journey through the streets of Baghdad, offering a unique perspective on the city's culture, people, and customs. Through a series of delightful anecdotes and observations, the author introduces us to a colorful cast of characters, from beggars to aristocrats, and shares his encounters with the unexpected and the extraordinary. With his signature wit and charm, Lucas paints a vivid picture of life in Baghdad, transporting readers to a world filled with humor, intrigue, and a touch of the exotic.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Humor

Themes: Cultural exploration, Human connection, Adventure
