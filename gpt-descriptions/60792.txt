Synopsis: In 'Adam & Eve & Pinch Me' by A. E. Coppard, a seemingly ordinary woman named Eve finds herself caught in a web of mysterious events that blur the lines between reality and fantasy. As she navigates through a series of strange encounters and uncanny coincidences, Eve becomes entangled in a world where dreams and desires intertwine, leading her to question her own sanity and the nature of her existence. With each twist and turn, the boundaries of her reality are pushed to the limit, leaving readers captivated by the enigmatic journey of Eve and the secrets she unravels.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Fiction, Magical Realism

Themes: Identity, Perception, Reality vs. Illusion
