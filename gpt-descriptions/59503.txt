Synopsis: In 'Home Manufacture and Use of Unfermented Grape Juice', George C. Husmann takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of grape juice production. From the careful selection of grapes to the meticulous process of extraction, Husmann provides a comprehensive guide for creating delicious, unfermented grape juice right at home. With detailed instructions and insightful tips, this book offers a unique opportunity to explore the art of grape juice making and discover the countless possibilities it holds.

Genres: Non-fiction, Food and Drink, DIY

Themes: Home production, Culinary arts, Self-sufficiency
