Synopsis: In 'First Lensman', the first installment of the epic Lensman series, a young officer named Virgil Samms is chosen to join an elite group of interstellar peacekeepers known as the Lensmen. Equipped with powerful lenses that grant them extraordinary abilities, Samms and his fellow Lensmen must confront a mysterious and ancient evil that threatens the entire galaxy. As they embark on a perilous journey across the cosmos, they uncover secrets that will forever change the course of humanity's destiny.

Genres: Science Fiction, Space Opera, Adventure

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Exploration of the Universe, Evolution of Humanity
