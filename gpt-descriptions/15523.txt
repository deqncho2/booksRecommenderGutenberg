Synopsis: 
In 'Getting Together' by Ian Hay, a group of friends reunite after many years to reminisce about their university days. As they share stories and catch up on each other's lives, old rivalries and secrets resurface, leading to unexpected twists and turns. With witty dialogue and charming characters, this delightful novel takes readers on a nostalgic journey filled with laughter, romance, and a touch of mystery.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Friendship, Nostalgia, Secrets
