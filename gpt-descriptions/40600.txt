Synopsis: In 'Over the Border: A Novel' by Whitaker Herman, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change her life forever. As she embarks on a thrilling journey across borders, she must navigate through treacherous landscapes, encounter enigmatic characters, and confront her own fears. With each step closer to the truth, Emily discovers that the line between right and wrong becomes increasingly blurred, forcing her to question her own morality and the lengths she is willing to go to protect what she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Morality, Secrets, Courage
