Synopsis: In "I'll Leave It To You," a young woman named Pamela is faced with a difficult decision that could shape her future. As she navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations, Pamela finds herself caught in a web of humorous misunderstandings and unexpected encounters. With a cast of eccentric characters and witty dialogue, this light comedy takes the reader on a delightful journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Coming of age, Identity, Society's expectations
