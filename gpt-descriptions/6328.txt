Synopsis: 
The Junior Classics, Volume 5: Stories that never grow old is a captivating collection of timeless tales that will transport readers to enchanting worlds filled with adventure, magic, and wonder. From the beloved stories of Hans Christian Andersen and the Brothers Grimm to classic fables and myths, this anthology brings together a diverse range of narratives that have captivated generations. Discover the courage of a young girl facing a fearsome dragon, the cunning of a clever fox outsmarting his adversaries, and the power of love and friendship that can overcome any obstacle. Each story is a treasure trove of imagination and inspiration, reminding us why these tales have endured through the ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fairy Tales, Folklore

Themes: Adventure, Courage, Friendship, Love, Morality
