Synopsis: In 'Oxford Days; or, How Ross Got His Degree', follow the captivating journey of Ross as he navigates the prestigious halls of Oxford University. From the eccentric professors to the demanding academic challenges, Ross must overcome numerous obstacles to achieve his ultimate goal. Along the way, he encounters unexpected friendships, romantic entanglements, and a series of comical mishaps that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. Will Ross be able to conquer the trials of university life and obtain his coveted degree?

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Education, Friendship, Personal growth
