Synopsis: In 'Project Hush' by William Tenn, a brilliant scientist is recruited by a secretive government agency to work on a top-secret project. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a shocking truth that could change the course of humanity forever. With danger lurking at every corner, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal to protect his discovery and ensure the survival of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Government secrecy, Scientific ethics, Human survival
