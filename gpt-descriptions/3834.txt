Synopsis: In "Hassan: The Story of Hassan of Bagdad, and How He Came to Make the Golden Journey to Samarkand," we are transported to the enchanting world of ancient Bagdad. The play follows the captivating journey of Hassan, a young poet, as he embarks on a quest to the fabled city of Samarkand. Along the way, he encounters a myriad of colorful characters, faces dangerous trials, and experiences profound moments of love, loss, and self-discovery. As Hassan's journey unfolds, the audience is immersed in a mesmerizing tale that explores the power of dreams, the pursuit of destiny, and the eternal longing for something greater.

Genres: Drama, Adventure, Poetry

Themes: Quest for meaning, Love and loss, Fate and destiny
