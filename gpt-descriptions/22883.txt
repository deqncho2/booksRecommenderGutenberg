Synopsis: In "Doctor Luttrell's First Patient" by Rosa Nouchette Carey, a young and ambitious doctor finds himself faced with a mysterious patient who challenges his medical knowledge and tests his compassion. As he delves deeper into the patient's case, he uncovers a web of secrets and unexpected connections that will change his life forever. With each twist and turn, the doctor must navigate the delicate balance between professional duty and personal involvement, ultimately questioning the very nature of his chosen profession.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Medical ethics, Personal growth, Love and sacrifice
