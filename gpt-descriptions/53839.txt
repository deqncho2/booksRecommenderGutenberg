Synopsis: In 'Tragic Romances: Re-issue of the Shorter Stories of Fiona Macleod; Rearranged, with Additional Tales' by William Sharp, readers are transported to a world of enchantment and heartbreak. This collection of shorter stories, beautifully rearranged and enhanced with additional tales, delves into the depths of human emotions and explores the complexities of love, loss, and destiny. From ethereal landscapes to haunting characters, each story weaves a spellbinding narrative that will leave readers captivated until the very last page.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Love, Tragedy, Fate, Mythology
