Synopsis: In 'The Lady Doc' by Caroline Lockhart, a young and ambitious doctor named Dr. Mary MacLane arrives in a small western town in the early 1900s, determined to prove herself in a male-dominated profession. As she faces numerous challenges and prejudices, she finds unexpected allies and uncovers a web of secrets that could change the course of her life forever. With her determination and resilience, Dr. MacLane navigates the complexities of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of her dreams in a world that constantly underestimates her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Western, Romance

Themes: Gender equality, Ambition, Identity, Overcoming adversity
