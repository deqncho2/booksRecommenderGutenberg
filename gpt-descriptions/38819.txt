Synopsis: 

In 'Behind the Mirrors: The Psychology of Disintegration at Washington', author Clinton W. Gilbert delves into the dark underbelly of Washington politics, exposing the intricate web of power, manipulation, and psychological warfare that lies behind the polished facade. As a renowned psychologist, Gilbert uncovers the hidden motivations and psychological tactics employed by those in power, revealing shocking secrets that threaten to shatter the very foundations of the nation's capital. With a gripping narrative and a cast of complex characters, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through the twisted corridors of power, where nothing is as it seems and everyone has something to hide.

Genres: Political thriller, Psychological suspense, Mystery

Themes: Power dynamics, Psychological manipulation, Corruption, Deception
