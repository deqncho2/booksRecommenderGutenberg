Synopsis: Step back in time and embark on a thrilling journey through the fascinating world of stage-coaches and mail delivery in the bygone era. In this captivating volume, Charles G. Harper takes readers on a vivid and picturesque exploration of the coaching age, delving into the history, anecdotes, and adventures of these iconic modes of transportation. From the bustling streets of London to the remote corners of the countryside, experience the excitement, danger, and romance that accompanied the stage-coach and mail services of yore. Discover the stories of the brave drivers, the daring highwaymen, and the curious passengers who traversed the ever-changing landscapes of 18th and 19th century England. With rich descriptions and captivating illustrations, this book brings to life a forgotten era and invites readers to immerse themselves in the thrilling world of stage-coaches and mail delivery.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Travel, Adventure

Themes: Transportation, History, Exploration, Society
