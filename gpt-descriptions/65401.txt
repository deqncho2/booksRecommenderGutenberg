Synopsis: In 'A Perfect Fool: A Novel' by Florence Warden, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and deception when she becomes the target of a cunning and manipulative con artist. As she navigates through a world of secrets and hidden identities, Evelyn must rely on her wit and resilience to uncover the truth and protect herself from danger. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the motives and loyalties of every character.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Trust
