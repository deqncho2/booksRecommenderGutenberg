Synopsis: 

In the enchanting world of Devonshire, a small coastal town in England, secrets lie hidden beneath the picturesque facade. When a mysterious newcomer arrives, the lives of the townspeople are forever changed. As the past intertwines with the present, a web of intrigue, romance, and betrayal begins to unravel, revealing shocking truths that threaten to tear the community apart. With each page, the reader is drawn deeper into the captivating world of Devonshire, where nothing is as it seems and every character has something to hide.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and betrayal, Small-town dynamics
