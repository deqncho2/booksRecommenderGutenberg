Synopsis: In 'The Runaway Equator, and the Strange Adventures of a Little Boy in Pursuit of It' by Bell, Lilian, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside a young boy who embarks on a quest to find the runaway equator. As he travels through enchanting lands and encounters peculiar characters, the boy's determination and courage are put to the test. With each twist and turn, the story unfolds into a captivating adventure that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what lies ahead for the young protagonist.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Children's Literature

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Imagination
