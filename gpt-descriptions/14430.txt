Synopsis: In "Purcell" by John F. Runciman, a renowned composer finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a hidden manuscript that could change the course of music history. As he delves deeper into the secrets of this enigmatic score, he becomes embroiled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where his life and the fate of his beloved music hang in the balance. With twists and turns at every corner, "Purcell" is a thrilling journey through the world of classical music, where passion, betrayal, and the pursuit of artistic perfection collide.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Music

Themes: Artistic obsession, Betrayal, Historical secrets
