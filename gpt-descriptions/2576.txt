Synopsis: 
'Alps and Sanctuaries of Piedmont and the Canton Ticino' takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Piedmont and the Canton Ticino. Samuel Butler, an English author and artist, shares his experiences and encounters as he explores the stunning alpine regions, uncovering hidden sanctuaries and encountering the unique cultures and traditions of the local people. From breathtaking mountain vistas to ancient religious sites, Butler's vivid descriptions and insightful observations paint a vivid picture of these enchanting regions, leaving readers yearning to embark on their own adventure.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Nature and landscape, Cultural exploration, Spiritual and religious experiences
