Synopsis: In 'Transfer Point' by Anthony Boucher, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and deception when he is called to investigate a series of bizarre disappearances at a remote train station. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel the fabric of reality itself. With time running out, he must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and confront his own demons to solve the enigma of the Transfer Point.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Perception, Reality vs. Illusion
