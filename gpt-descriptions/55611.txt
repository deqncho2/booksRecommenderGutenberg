Synopsis: In 'Karma: A Re-incarnation Play: In Prologue, Epilogue & Three Acts', Blackwood and Pearn take readers on a captivating journey through the realms of karma and re-incarnation. The story follows a diverse cast of characters as they navigate the complexities of their past lives and the consequences that unfold in their present existence. As the play unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the characters are forced to confront the karmic debts they owe. With each act, the stakes rise higher, leading to a climactic finale that will leave readers questioning the nature of fate and the power of redemption.

Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Spiritual

Themes: Karma, Re-incarnation, Fate, Redemption, Secrets
