Synopsis: 

In "Elements of Debating: A Manual for Use in High Schools and Academies," author Leverett S. Lyon presents a captivating guide that unveils the art of persuasive argumentation. This comprehensive manual takes readers on a journey through the intricacies of debating, offering invaluable insights and practical techniques to master the art of rhetoric. From constructing compelling arguments to countering opponents' claims, Lyon's manual equips aspiring debaters with the tools necessary to excel in the world of competitive discourse. With engaging examples and thought-provoking exercises, this book is sure to ignite a passion for debate and empower readers to articulate their ideas with eloquence and conviction.

Genres: Educational, Non-fiction

Themes: Persuasion, Critical Thinking, Public Speaking
