Synopsis: In 'None Other Gods' by Robert Hugh Benson, a young man named Percy Franklin embarks on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. Drawn into the world of the occult, he encounters mysterious forces that challenge his beliefs and test his faith. As Percy delves deeper into the realm of the supernatural, he must confront the ultimate question of whether there are indeed none other gods. This gripping tale explores the boundaries between good and evil, the power of faith, and the search for truth in a world filled with deception and temptation.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Religious Fiction

Themes: Faith, Spiritual Awakening, Good vs. Evil
