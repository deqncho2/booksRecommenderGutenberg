Synopsis: In 'The Case of the Golden Bullet' by Auguste Groner, a renowned detective is faced with a perplexing murder mystery when a wealthy businessman is found dead in his luxurious mansion. The investigation takes a thrilling turn when the only clue left behind is a golden bullet. As the detective delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives, leading him to question everyone's innocence. With time running out, he must unravel the truth before another life is taken. 

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Justice
