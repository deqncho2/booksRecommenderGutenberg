Synopsis: In 'To Remember Charlie By' by Roger D. Aycock, a small town is shaken by the mysterious disappearance of a young boy named Charlie. As the community rallies together to search for him, secrets and hidden truths begin to unravel, revealing a web of deceit and betrayal. With each passing day, the urgency to find Charlie intensifies, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Loss, Community, Deception, Redemption
