Synopsis: 

In 'Another Earth', a young woman named Rhoda Williams is haunted by a tragic event that changes the course of her life forever. As a second Earth is discovered in the sky, Rhoda becomes obsessed with the possibility of redemption and a chance to make amends. She embarks on a journey that blurs the boundaries between reality and dreams, leading her to confront her past and discover the true nature of her existence.

Genres: Science Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Parallel Universes, Identity, Guilt
