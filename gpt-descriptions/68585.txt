Synopsis: 
'Puppies and kittens, and other stories' by Cadby, Carine is a collection of heartwarming and whimsical tales that will transport readers to a world filled with adorable animals and magical moments. From mischievous puppies getting into all sorts of trouble to curious kittens embarking on unexpected adventures, each story is a delightful exploration of the joys and challenges of animal companionship. With enchanting illustrations and captivating storytelling, this book is sure to captivate readers of all ages and leave them with a warm and fuzzy feeling.

Genres: Children's Literature, Animal Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Playfulness, Love for Animals
