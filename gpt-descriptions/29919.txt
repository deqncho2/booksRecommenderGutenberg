Synopsis: 
In the November 1930 issue of 'Astounding Stories of Super-Science,' prepare to be transported to a world where scientific wonders and extraordinary adventures await. From the depths of the ocean to the far reaches of outer space, this collection of thrilling tales will captivate your imagination. Encounter a race of superhumans with incredible powers, witness a battle against an alien invasion, and delve into the mysteries of time travel. Brace yourself for a rollercoaster ride of suspense, action, and mind-bending concepts that will leave you eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Superhuman abilities, Extraterrestrial encounters, Time travel, Exploration of the unknown
