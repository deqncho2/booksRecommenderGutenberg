Synopsis: 

The 2004 CIA World Factbook is a captivating and eye-opening reference book that takes readers on a global journey, providing a comprehensive overview of the countries and territories around the world. From the bustling cities of Asia to the vast landscapes of Africa, this Factbook offers a wealth of information on each nation's geography, population, government, economy, and much more. Delve into the intricate details of each country's history, culture, and unique characteristics, as well as their relationships with other nations. With its wealth of data and intriguing insights, this Factbook is a must-read for anyone seeking to expand their knowledge of the world.

Genres: Reference, Non-fiction

Themes: Geography, Politics, Culture, International Relations
