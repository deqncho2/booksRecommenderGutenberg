Synopsis: 
'Jungle and Stream; Or, The Adventures of Two Boys in Siam' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the lush landscapes of Siam, as two young boys embark on a thrilling adventure. Filled with mystery, danger, and unexpected encounters, this captivating tale follows their quest to uncover hidden treasures and unravel the secrets of the jungle. As they navigate through treacherous terrain and encounter fascinating wildlife, the boys learn valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the power of perseverance. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Survival
