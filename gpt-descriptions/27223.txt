Synopsis: In 'Eyebright: A Story' by Susan Coolidge, we follow the journey of a young girl named Eyebright as she navigates the challenges of growing up in a small New England town. With her vivid imagination and curious nature, Eyebright finds herself entangled in a series of unexpected adventures, discovering the true meaning of friendship, family, and the power of self-belief. As she encounters a cast of colorful characters and explores the enchanting landscapes of her surroundings, Eyebright's journey becomes a heartwarming tale of self-discovery and resilience.

Genres: Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Self-discovery
