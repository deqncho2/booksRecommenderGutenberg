Synopsis:
In "Lady Anna" by Anthony Trollope, a young woman finds herself torn between love and duty when she becomes embroiled in a complex legal battle. Lady Anna, a beautiful and spirited heiress, is caught in a web of conflicting interests as she navigates the expectations of her aristocratic family and her own desires. With the fate of her inheritance hanging in the balance, Lady Anna must make a choice that will not only determine her future but also challenge the rigid social norms of Victorian England.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Love and duty, Social class, Women's agency
