Synopsis:
'Dorothy Vernon of Haddon Hall' by Major Charles is a captivating historical romance set in 16th century England. The story follows the spirited and independent Dorothy Vernon, who finds herself torn between duty and love when she meets the dashing John Manners. As their forbidden romance unfolds against the backdrop of Haddon Hall, a grand and mysterious estate, Dorothy must navigate treacherous family secrets and societal expectations. Will she choose to follow her heart or succumb to the pressures of her noble lineage?

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes:
Love and Duty, Family Secrets, Social Expectations
