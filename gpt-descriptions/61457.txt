Synopsis: 
In 'Charley's Log: A Story of Schoolboy Life' by Leslie, Emma, we follow the adventures of Charley, a young schoolboy who finds himself caught up in a series of unexpected events. From navigating the challenges of friendship and rivalry to uncovering hidden secrets, Charley's journey takes him on a thrilling ride that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding his school, Charley discovers that sometimes the most unexpected friendships can lead to the greatest discoveries.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Rivalry, Discovery
