Synopsis: In 'A Thief in the Night: Further adventures of A. J. Raffles, Cricketer and Cracksman', the notorious gentleman thief A. J. Raffles returns for more thrilling escapades that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. This time, Raffles finds himself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue as he navigates the treacherous world of high society, outwitting both the police and his criminal rivals. With his cunning wit and audacious charm, Raffles must pull off his most daring heists yet, all while maintaining his double life as a respected cricketer. As the stakes rise and the tension mounts, readers will be captivated by the exhilarating twists and turns that await them in this gripping tale.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Deception, Morality, Class divide
