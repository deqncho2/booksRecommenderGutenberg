Synopsis: 
'Queer Luck: Poker Stories from the New York Sun' by Curtis, David A. is a captivating collection of poker stories that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. From high-stakes tournaments to underground gambling dens, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through the world of poker in New York City. Each story introduces a unique cast of characters, each with their own motivations and secrets, as they navigate the unpredictable world of poker. With unexpected twists and turns, 'Queer Luck' explores the highs and lows of the game, revealing the true nature of luck and skill in the world of poker.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Gambling, Luck, Deception, Human nature
