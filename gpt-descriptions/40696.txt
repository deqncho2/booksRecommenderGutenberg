Synopsis: In the remote and desolate Antarctic, a group of scientists stumble upon a mysterious and ancient creature buried deep in the ice. As they attempt to thaw it out, they soon realize that this creature is unlike anything they have ever encountered before. With paranoia and suspicion running high, the scientists must confront the terrifying possibility that one of them may not be who they appear to be. As tensions rise and trust crumbles, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and fight for their survival against an unimaginable threat.

Genres: Science Fiction, Horror, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Paranoia, Survival
