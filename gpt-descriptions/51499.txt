Synopsis: 
In the thrilling novel 'Blueblood' by Jim Harmon, a young detective finds himself entangled in a web of corruption and deceit when he is assigned to investigate a high-profile murder case. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of the city's elite, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel everything he thought he knew. With his own life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path of power, privilege, and betrayal to bring the truth to light.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Corruption, Deception, Power
