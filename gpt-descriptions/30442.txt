Synopsis: 
'Letters from My Windmill' is a collection of charming and humorous stories set in the picturesque countryside of Provence, France. Through a series of letters, the author, Alphonse Daudet, shares his delightful encounters with eccentric characters, explores the beauty of nature, and delves into the idiosyncrasies of rural life. From mischievous millers to loquacious donkeys, each tale offers a glimpse into the heartwarming and sometimes absurd moments that unfold in this quaint corner of the world.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Humor

Themes: Rural life, Human nature, Nature's beauty
