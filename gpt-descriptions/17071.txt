Synopsis: 

'Folk-Lore and Legends: Scotland' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Scottish folklore and legends. From ancient myths and mystical creatures to heroic tales and haunting ghost stories, this collection unveils the hidden depths of Scotland's cultural heritage. Delve into the enchanting world of selkies, kelpies, and fairies, and discover the secrets of ancient standing stones and haunted castles. With each page, the reader is transported to a land of mystery and wonder, where the past intertwines with the present, and where the power of storytelling reigns supreme.

Genres: Folklore, Legends, Mythology

Themes: Scottish culture, Supernatural beings, Oral tradition
