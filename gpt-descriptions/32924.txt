Synopsis: In 'The New Mistress: A Tale' by George Manville Fenn, a young woman named Hilda finds herself caught in a web of secrets and intrigue when she becomes the new mistress of a grand estate. As she navigates the complexities of her new role, Hilda uncovers dark family secrets and must confront the consequences of her choices. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, Love and sacrifice
