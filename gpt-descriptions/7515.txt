Synopsis: In 'The Glory of the Trenches' by Dawson, Coningsby, a young soldier finds himself thrust into the chaos and brutality of World War I. As he navigates the horrors of the trenches, he forms unexpected friendships, faces unimaginable challenges, and discovers the true meaning of courage and sacrifice. With vivid and gripping storytelling, this novel takes readers on an emotional journey through the devastating realities of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War, Friendship, Courage, Sacrifice
