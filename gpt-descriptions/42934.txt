Synopsis: Polly's Southern Cruise takes readers on a thrilling adventure as Polly and her friends embark on a cruise along the picturesque southern coast. However, their peaceful journey soon turns into a whirlwind of mystery and danger when they stumble upon a hidden treasure map. As they navigate through treacherous waters and encounter unexpected obstacles, Polly and her friends must rely on their wit and courage to unravel the secrets of the map and outsmart a group of cunning treasure hunters.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
