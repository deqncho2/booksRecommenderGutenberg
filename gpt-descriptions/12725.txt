Synopsis: In 'The Vale of Cedars; Or, The Martyr' by Grace Aguilar, a young woman named Eva is forced to hide her Jewish identity in order to survive in a society that discriminates against her people. As she navigates the challenges of concealing her true heritage, Eva finds herself torn between love and duty, and must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century England, this captivating tale explores the power of faith, the struggle for identity, and the enduring strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Identity, Discrimination, Faith, Love, Sacrifice
