Synopsis: In 'Judith, a Play in Three Acts; Founded on the Apocryphal Book of Judith', Arnold Bennett brings to life the captivating story of Judith, a courageous and enigmatic woman who takes matters into her own hands to save her people. Set in a time of war and political turmoil, the play follows Judith's journey as she navigates treacherous alliances, deceit, and personal sacrifice. As the plot unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the true nature of power and heroism is explored in this gripping tale of bravery and redemption.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Tragedy

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Power, Deception
