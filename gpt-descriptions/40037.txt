Synopsis: 

'Fort Duquesne and Fort Pitt; Early Names of Pittsburgh Streets' takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, exploring the origins and significance of the city's street names. From the early days of Fort Duquesne to the establishment of Fort Pitt, this book delves into the rich historical tapestry of Pittsburgh's past, shedding light on the stories and individuals that shaped the city's development. With fascinating anecdotes and meticulous research, the Daughters of the American Revolution Pittsburgh Chapter uncovers the hidden meanings and forgotten tales behind the names of Pittsburgh's streets, offering readers a unique perspective on the city's heritage.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Local History

Themes: History, Heritage, Urban Development
