Synopsis: 
'Four Months Among the Gold-Finders in Alta California: Being the Diary of an Expedition From San Francisco to the Gold Districts' follows the captivating journey of an expedition from San Francisco to the gold districts of Alta California. Through the eyes of the author, Henry Vizetelly, readers are transported to a time of great excitement and adventure as they witness the challenges, dangers, and triumphs faced by the gold-finders. As the expedition delves deeper into the gold districts, the diary entries provide a vivid and immersive experience, leaving readers eager to discover the outcome of their quest.

Genres: Historical, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Pursuit of Wealth, Survival
