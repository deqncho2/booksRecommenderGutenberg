Synopsis: In 'Mass' George: A Boy's Adventures in the Old Savannah', young George finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. With the help of his friends, he embarks on a journey through the old Savannah, encountering dangerous obstacles and unexpected allies along the way. As George delves deeper into the mystery, he discovers secrets that will change his life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery, Treasure Hunting
