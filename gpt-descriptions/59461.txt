Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Jack London' is a comprehensive collection of the works of the renowned author Jack London. This compilation includes a wide range of his writings, from adventure stories set in the wild landscapes of Alaska and the Klondike, to thought-provoking social commentaries and tales of survival. From the gripping adventures of his beloved canine characters to the exploration of human nature and the struggle for survival, this index offers a captivating glimpse into the diverse literary world of Jack London.

Genres: Adventure, Fiction, Short Stories, Social Commentary

Themes: Survival, Nature, Human Nature, Social Inequality
