Synopsis: 
In "The Hunter's Lodge Case" by Agatha Christie, a group of friends gather at a remote hunting lodge for a weekend getaway. However, their peaceful retreat takes a sinister turn when a murder occurs, leaving everyone in shock and suspicion. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, alibis are questioned, and the true nature of each character is brought to light. With a web of deceit and a limited pool of suspects, it becomes a race against time to uncover the identity of the killer before they strike again.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Trust, Justice
