Synopsis: In "An Essay Towards Regulating the Trade, and Employing the Poor of This Kingdom: Whereunto is Added, an Essay Towards Paying Off the Publick Debts," John Cary delves into the pressing issues of trade regulation, poverty, and public debt in the kingdom. Through a thought-provoking analysis, Cary proposes innovative solutions to these societal challenges, offering a fresh perspective on how to improve the economic and social conditions of the nation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Economics, Political Science

Themes: Trade regulation, Poverty alleviation, Public debt management
