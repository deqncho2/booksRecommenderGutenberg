Synopsis: In the small town of Strawberry Acres, secrets run deep and tensions simmer beneath the surface. When a mysterious newcomer arrives, the lives of the townspeople are thrown into disarray, and long-held secrets threaten to unravel. As the truth slowly emerges, the residents of Strawberry Acres must confront their pasts and make difficult choices that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, Small-town dynamics, Redemption and forgiveness
