Synopsis: 
'Abbreviations and Signs: A Primer of Information about Abbreviations and Signs, with Classified Lists of Those in Most Common Use' by Frederick W. Hamilton is a fascinating exploration of the world of abbreviations and signs. This comprehensive guide takes readers on a journey through the vast array of abbreviations and signs used in various fields, providing valuable insights into their meanings and usage. From medical abbreviations to mathematical symbols, from shorthand to traffic signs, this primer covers it all. With its engaging examples and practical explanations, Hamilton's work is sure to captivate anyone curious about the language of symbols and abbreviations.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Language and communication, Symbolism, Information literacy
