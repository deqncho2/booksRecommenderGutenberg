Synopsis: 
In "What Every Woman Knows," a young woman named Maggie Wylie possesses an extraordinary talent for shaping the lives of those around her. Despite her lack of formal education, Maggie's wit and intelligence enable her to navigate the complexities of society and influence the actions of powerful men. As she becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and romantic entanglements, Maggie's true strength lies in her ability to challenge societal expectations and redefine the role of women in a male-dominated world.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Gender roles, Power dynamics, Love and relationships
