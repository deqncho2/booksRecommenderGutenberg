Synopsis: In 'Manifesto of the Communist Party', Engels and Marx present a powerful critique of capitalism and its inherent contradictions. Through a thought-provoking analysis of historical class struggles, they unveil a vision for a society free from exploitation and inequality. This groundbreaking work challenges conventional notions of economic systems and offers a compelling call to action for a more just and equitable world.

Genres: Political philosophy, Social criticism, Historical analysis

Themes: Capitalism, Class struggle, Socialism, Revolution, Inequality
