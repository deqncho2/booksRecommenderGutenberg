Synopsis: In 'A Cry in the Wilderness' by Mary E. Waller, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in the remote wilderness. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she must confront her own fears and navigate treacherous paths, all while trying to uncover the truth that could change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Survival and resilience
