Synopsis: 
In 'The Turn of the Road: A Play in Two Scenes and an Epilogue' by Mayne, Rutherford, a group of strangers find themselves stranded in a remote inn during a stormy night. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, they must confront their pasts and make choices that will determine their futures. With unexpected twists and a haunting atmosphere, this gripping play explores the depths of human nature and the consequences of our actions.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Guilt, Fate, Moral Dilemmas
