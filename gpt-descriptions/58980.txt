Synopsis: In 'A Witch in Time' by Herb Williams, a young woman named Emma discovers an ancient spellbook that allows her to travel through time. As she explores different eras, she encounters a mysterious coven of witches and uncovers a dark secret that threatens her very existence. With each leap through time, Emma must navigate treacherous landscapes, face dangerous adversaries, and unravel the truth behind her own powers. As the lines between past and present blur, Emma must confront her own destiny and make a choice that will change the course of history forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Time Travel, Mystery

Themes: Power of Choice, Identity, Fate vs. Free Will
