Synopsis: In 'Concerning Justice', Lucilius A. Emery takes readers on a gripping journey through the intricate world of law and morality. When a renowned defense attorney is faced with a perplexing case that challenges his own beliefs, he must navigate the blurred lines between justice and truth. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers shocking secrets that could shatter the very foundation of the legal system. With each twist and turn, the protagonist finds himself questioning his own principles and the true nature of justice.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Morality, Ethics, Truth, Corruption
