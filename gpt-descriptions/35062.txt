Synopsis: In the small town of Pineville, a mysterious series of events unfolds when a group of woodpeckers suddenly begins wreaking havoc on the community. As the townspeople struggle to understand the reason behind the woodpeckers' unusual behavior, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden connections that will change their lives forever. With each chapter revealing a new twist, 'The Woodpeckers' keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind this enigmatic phenomenon.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden connections, Small-town dynamics, Nature's influence on human lives
