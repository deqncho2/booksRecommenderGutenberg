Synopsis: 

In 'Frye's Practical Candy Maker: Comprising Practical Receipts for the Manufacture of Fine "Hand-Made" Candies', George V. Frye takes readers on a delectable journey into the world of candy making. With a wealth of practical recipes and techniques, this book unveils the secrets behind creating exquisite, hand-crafted candies that are sure to delight both the eyes and the taste buds. From classic favorites to innovative creations, Frye's expertise and passion for candy making shine through, making this a must-have guide for aspiring confectioners and candy enthusiasts alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Cookbook, Culinary

Themes: Candy making, Confectionery, Artistry
