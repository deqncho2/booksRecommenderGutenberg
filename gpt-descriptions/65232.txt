Synopsis: In 'The Shadow-Eater' by Benjamin De Casseres, a mysterious figure known as the Shadow-Eater emerges, possessing the ability to consume the shadows of individuals. As the Shadow-Eater's powers grow, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, navigating a world where shadows hold secrets and darkness conceals hidden truths. With each shadow devoured, the Shadow-Eater uncovers a deeper understanding of the human psyche, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and transformation.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Perception, Power, Existentialism
