Synopsis: In 'Subversive' by Reynolds, Mack, a brilliant hacker named Alex finds herself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger when she stumbles upon a classified government project. As she delves deeper into the secrets she has uncovered, she becomes entangled with a mysterious group of rebels who are determined to expose the truth. With her skills pushed to the limit and her life on the line, Alex must decide where her loyalties lie and how far she is willing to go to fight against a corrupt system.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Cyberpunk

Themes: Government conspiracy, Rebellion, Ethical dilemmas, Technological advancements
