Synopsis: In "Hope Mills; Or, Between Friend and Sweetheart" by Amanda M. Douglas, a young woman named Hope finds herself torn between her close friendship with a kind-hearted doctor and her growing feelings for a charming newcomer to their small town. As secrets are revealed and emotions run high, Hope must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and the choices that will shape her future. Will she follow her heart or stay true to her friendship?

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and friendship, loyalty, self-discovery
