Synopsis: In 'Zina: the Slave Girl; or, Which the Traitor?: A Drama in Four Acts', the reader is transported to the tumultuous world of 19th-century America, where the lives of Zina, a courageous slave girl, and a mysterious traitor become intertwined in a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and redemption. As Zina fights for her freedom and searches for her true identity, she must navigate treacherous paths and face unexpected challenges that will test her resilience and determination. With each act, the tension rises, secrets are unveiled, and the characters' fates hang in the balance, leaving the reader captivated until the final curtain falls.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Slavery, Identity, Betrayal, Freedom
