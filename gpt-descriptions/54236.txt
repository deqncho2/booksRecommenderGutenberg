Synopsis: In 'The Intruder' by Gabriele D'Annunzio, a mysterious and captivating stranger enters the lives of a wealthy Italian family, bringing with him an air of danger and seduction. As he weaves his way into their lives, the family becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and forbidden desires. With each passing moment, the tension rises, and the line between love and obsession blurs. Will the family succumb to the allure of the intruder, or will they uncover the truth behind his enigmatic presence?

Genres: Psychological thriller, Gothic fiction, Romance

Themes: Forbidden love, Seduction, Betrayal, Obsession
