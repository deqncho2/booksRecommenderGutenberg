Synopsis: 
In "Covered With Mud and Glory: A Machine Gun Company in Action ("Ma Mitrailleuse")" by Lafond, Georges, readers are transported to the front lines of World War I, where a machine gun company fights for survival amidst the chaos and devastation of the battlefield. Through the eyes of the protagonist, we witness the relentless horrors of war, the bonds formed between comrades, and the indomitable spirit that keeps them going. As the company faces unimaginable challenges and confronts their own mortality, they must find the strength to carry on and fulfill their duty.

Genres: War fiction, Historical fiction, Action

Themes: The brutality of war, camaraderie and brotherhood, resilience and determination
