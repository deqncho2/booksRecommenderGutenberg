Synopsis: In 'A Mortal Antipathy' by Oliver Wendell Holmes, a renowned physician finds himself inexplicably repulsed by a particular patient, a young woman who suffers from a mysterious illness. As he delves deeper into her case, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, uncovering secrets that challenge his beliefs and force him to confront his own mortality. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this novel explores the complexities of human nature and the power of empathy.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Medical Fiction

Themes: Human psychology, Empathy, Morality, Mortality
