Synopsis: In "The Circus Boys Across the Continent," follow the thrilling adventures of Phil Forrest and his friends as they embark on a journey that takes them from the excitement of the circus to the vast landscapes of America. As they travel across the continent, they encounter unexpected challenges, face dangerous situations, and form unbreakable bonds. With their determination and courage, the young circus boys strive to overcome obstacles and prove their worth, all while discovering the true meaning of friendship and loyalty.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Perseverance, Self-discovery
