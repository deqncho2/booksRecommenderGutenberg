Synopsis: 
In 'His Royal Nibs' by Watanna, Onoto, a young American woman finds herself entangled in a whirlwind romance with a mysterious and captivating foreign prince. As their love blossoms, she is thrust into a world of opulence, intrigue, and danger, where she must navigate the treacherous waters of palace politics and societal expectations. With secrets lurking around every corner, she must decide whether to follow her heart or succumb to the pressures of duty and tradition. Will their love conquer all, or will the forces working against them tear them apart?

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Cultural clash, Power and duty
