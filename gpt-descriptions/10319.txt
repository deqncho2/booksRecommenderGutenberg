Synopsis: 
In 'Dave Darrin's Third Year at Annapolis; Or, Leaders of the Second Class Midshipmen', Dave Darrin returns to the United States Naval Academy for his third year, facing new challenges and responsibilities as a leader among his fellow midshipmen. As he navigates the demanding academic and military training, Dave finds himself caught up in a series of unexpected events that test his character, loyalty, and determination. With his friends by his side, Dave must confront personal and professional obstacles that will shape his future as a naval officer.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Military

Themes: Leadership, Friendship, Honor, Perseverance
