Synopsis: In "Afloat on the Ohio: An Historical Pilgrimage of a Thousand Miles in a Skiff, from Redstone to Cairo," Reuben Gold Thwaites takes readers on an extraordinary journey along the Ohio River. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Thwaites recounts his adventurous voyage, encountering fascinating characters, exploring historical landmarks, and immersing himself in the rich tapestry of the region's past. From the bustling cities to the serene countryside, this travelogue offers a unique perspective on the diverse landscapes and cultural heritage of the Ohio River Valley.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, History, Cultural Heritage
