Synopsis: In 'Playing Santa Claus, and Other Christmas Tales', Sarah P. Doughty weaves together a collection of heartwarming and enchanting stories that capture the true spirit of Christmas. From unexpected encounters with Santa Claus to magical snowstorms, these tales transport readers to a world filled with wonder and joy. Each story is a delightful reminder of the power of love, kindness, and the magic that can be found during the holiday season.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Christmas

Themes: Love, Kindness, Magic, Holiday Spirit
