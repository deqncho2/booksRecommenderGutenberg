Synopsis: In "Reminiscences of a Confederate Soldier of Co. C, 2nd Va. Cavalry," Rufus H. Peck takes readers on a captivating journey through his experiences as a soldier during the American Civil War. From the adrenaline-fueled battles to the camaraderie among comrades, Peck's vivid recollections provide a unique perspective on the war that tore a nation apart. Through his personal anecdotes and reflections, readers will be transported to the front lines, witnessing the harsh realities of war and the indomitable spirit of those who fought.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: War and its impact on individuals and society, Loyalty and camaraderie in the face of adversity, The human experience in times of conflict
