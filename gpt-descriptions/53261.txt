Synopsis: In 'We Moderns: Enigmas and Guesses' by Edwin Muir, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the complexities of modern life. Through a series of thought-provoking essays, Muir explores the enigmatic nature of our existence, delving into the mysteries of art, literature, philosophy, and human relationships. With his insightful observations and eloquent prose, Muir challenges readers to question the world around them and to seek meaning in the midst of uncertainty. This collection of essays offers a profound exploration of the human condition and the ever-evolving landscape of modernity.

Genres: Essay, Non-fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Modernity, Existentialism, Artistic Expression, Human Nature
