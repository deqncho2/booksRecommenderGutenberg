Synopsis: In 'Blue Jackets: The Log of the Teaser' by George Manville Fenn, a group of young sailors find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure aboard the Teaser, a mysterious ship with a dark past. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter dangerous enemies, the crew must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and camaraderie to survive. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, the Teaser becomes a vessel of both excitement and peril, leading the young sailors on a journey they will never forget.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
