Synopsis: In "Kenneth McAlpine: A Tale of Mountain, Moorland and Sea," Gordon Stables takes readers on an exhilarating adventure through the rugged landscapes of Scotland. Follow the young and daring Kenneth McAlpine as he navigates treacherous mountains, vast moorlands, and unpredictable seas, encountering both natural wonders and formidable challenges along the way. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this captivating tale will transport readers to a world of breathtaking beauty and thrilling escapades.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Resilience, Nature's Majesty
