Synopsis: 

In 'The Masculine Cross: A History of Ancient and Modern Crosses and Their Connection with the Mysteries of Sex Worship; Also an Account of the Kindred Phases of Phallic Faiths and Practices', the anonymous author delves into the intriguing and often taboo subject of sex worship and its connection to the symbolism of crosses throughout history. This captivating exploration takes readers on a journey through ancient and modern cultures, uncovering the hidden meanings behind various cross symbols and their ties to the mysteries of human sexuality. From the phallic faiths of ancient civilizations to the practices of modern societies, this book offers a thought-provoking examination of the intersection between religion, sexuality, and symbolism.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Religious Studies

Themes: Sexuality, Religion, Symbolism, Cultural History
