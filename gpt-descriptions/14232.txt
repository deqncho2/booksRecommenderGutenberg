Synopsis: 
'A Celtic Psaltery: Being Mainly Renderings in English Verse from Irish & Welsh Poetry' by Alfred Perceval Graves is a captivating collection of English verse translations from ancient Irish and Welsh poetry. This anthology takes readers on a journey through the rich and mystical world of Celtic literature, offering a glimpse into the beauty and power of these ancient cultures. From epic tales of heroes and warriors to enchanting love songs and spiritual hymns, Graves skillfully brings these timeless verses to life, preserving their essence while making them accessible to a wider audience. Immerse yourself in the enchanting melodies and evocative imagery of Celtic poetry, and discover the profound wisdom and enduring themes that continue to resonate across centuries.

Genres: Poetry, Translation, Celtic Literature
Themes: Celtic mythology, Love and romance, Heroism, Spirituality, Nature
