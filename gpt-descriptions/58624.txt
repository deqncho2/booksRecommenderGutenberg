Synopsis: 
In "Sailor and beachcomber: Confessions of a life at sea, in Australia, and amid the islands of the Pacific," Arnold Safroni-Middleton takes readers on an exhilarating journey through his adventurous life as a sailor and beachcomber. From his early days as a young sailor in Australia to his captivating experiences amidst the enchanting islands of the Pacific, Safroni-Middleton's memoir is filled with tales of danger, discovery, and the irresistible allure of the sea. With vivid descriptions and a captivating narrative, this book offers a unique glimpse into a life lived on the edge of the world, where every day brings new challenges and unexpected encounters.

Genres: Memoir, Adventure, Travel

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Nature, Cultural encounters
