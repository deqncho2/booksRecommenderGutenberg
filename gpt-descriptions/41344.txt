Synopsis: 

'Wisconsin in Story and Song: Selections from the Prose and Poetry of Badger State Writers' takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Wisconsin. This anthology showcases the literary talents of Badger State writers, offering a collection of stories and poems that beautifully capture the essence of Wisconsin's people, history, and natural beauty. From the bustling cities to the tranquil countryside, readers will be immersed in the unique experiences and perspectives of Wisconsin's residents, as they navigate love, loss, adventure, and the complexities of life. Through vivid imagery and heartfelt storytelling, this anthology paints a vivid portrait of Wisconsin, inviting readers to explore the state's vibrant tapestry of voices and narratives.

Genres: Anthology, Fiction, Poetry

Themes: Wisconsin culture, Nature, Identity, Community, Love and Loss
