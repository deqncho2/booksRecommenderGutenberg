Synopsis: 
"The Iliads of Homer: Translated according to the Greek" takes readers on an epic journey to the heart of the Trojan War, where heroes clash, gods meddle, and destinies are forged. This timeless masterpiece follows the wrath of Achilles, the greatest warrior of his time, as he battles against the Trojans and faces the consequences of his actions. Filled with intense battles, intricate alliances, and profound human emotions, this translation captures the essence of Homer's original work, transporting readers to a world of honor, glory, and the complexities of war.

Genres: Epic Poetry, Historical Fiction, Mythology

Themes: Honor and Glory, Fate and Destiny, The Power of the Gods, The Human Condition
