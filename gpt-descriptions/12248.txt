Synopsis: In 'The King's Cup-Bearer' by Walton, O. F., Mrs., a young woman finds herself thrust into a world of intrigue and danger when she becomes the cup-bearer to the king. As she navigates the treacherous court, she uncovers dark secrets and must make difficult choices that could change the course of her life and the kingdom. With unexpected twists and a captivating romance, this story will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
