Synopsis: 
"Green Balls: The Adventures of a Night-Bomber" takes readers on a thrilling journey through the harrowing experiences of a night-bomber during World War II. As the protagonist navigates the treacherous skies, facing enemy fire and witnessing the devastating consequences of war, the story unfolds with gripping intensity. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this book offers a unique perspective on the courage and resilience of those who fought in the air.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Sacrifice, The human cost of war
