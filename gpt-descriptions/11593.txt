Synopsis: In 'The Purchase Price; Or, The Cause of Compromise' by Hough, Emerson, a young woman finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is forced to make a difficult decision that could change her life forever. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society, this gripping tale explores the complexities of love, sacrifice, and the consequences of compromising one's principles.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Sacrifice, Consequences of Compromise
