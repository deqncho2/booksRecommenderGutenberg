Synopsis: 
In "Lady Rose's Daughter" by Ward, Humphry, Mrs., the story revolves around the life of Lady Rose's daughter, who finds herself torn between duty and desire. Set in the late 19th century, the young protagonist must navigate the complexities of society, love, and family secrets. As she grapples with her own identity and the expectations placed upon her, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery that will challenge her beliefs and test her loyalties. With a backdrop of opulent settings and intricate relationships, this captivating novel explores the timeless themes of love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama
Themes: Identity, Duty, Family Secrets, Society's Expectations
