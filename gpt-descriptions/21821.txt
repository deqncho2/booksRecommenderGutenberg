Synopsis: In 'The Mark of Cain' by Andrew Lang, a mysterious artifact with a dark history resurfaces, setting off a chain of events that will test the limits of human morality and challenge the boundaries of science. As a group of unlikely allies embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind the mark, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and ancient curses. With each step closer to the truth, they must confront their own demons and make impossible choices that will forever change their lives. Will they be able to break the curse, or will they succumb to the mark's sinister power?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Morality, Betrayal, Redemption, Ancient Curses
