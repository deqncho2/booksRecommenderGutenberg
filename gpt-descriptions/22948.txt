Synopsis: In the small town of Oakdale, two talented high school pitchers, Jake and Ryan, find themselves in a fierce competition for the starting spot on the baseball team. As their rivalry intensifies, they must navigate the pressures of friendship, family expectations, and personal growth. With their dreams on the line, the two pitchers must learn to balance their competitive drive with the true meaning of teamwork and sportsmanship.

Genres: Sports, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Competition, Friendship, Family, Personal growth, Sportsmanship
