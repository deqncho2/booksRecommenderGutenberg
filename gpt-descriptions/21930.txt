Synopsis:
In the small coastal town of Salthaven, a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a dark secret that threatens to unravel the lives of the townspeople. As the stranger's presence becomes increasingly unsettling, the residents of Salthaven find themselves caught in a web of intrigue, deception, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, the tension builds, and the true nature of the stranger's intentions becomes more and more elusive. As the town teeters on the brink of chaos, the residents must confront their own fears and desires, ultimately leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of trust and the depths of human nature.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town dynamics, Trust and betrayal
