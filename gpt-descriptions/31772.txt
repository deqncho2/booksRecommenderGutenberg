Synopsis: In 'Si Klegg, Book 2: Thru the Stone River Campaign and in Winter Quarters at Murfreesboro' by John McElroy, we follow the humorous and adventurous journey of Si Klegg, a young soldier in the Union Army during the American Civil War. As Si and his comrades face the challenges of the Stone River Campaign, they navigate through the perils of battle, encounter unexpected allies, and find themselves in the midst of thrilling escapades. From the frontlines to the winter quarters at Murfreesboro, Si's experiences provide a captivating glimpse into the life of a soldier during this tumultuous period in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Humor

Themes: Coming of Age, Friendship, Survival, Patriotism
