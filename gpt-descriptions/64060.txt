Synopsis: 

'Advance Australasia: A Day-to-Day Record of a Recent Visit to Australasia: Second Edition' takes readers on an enthralling journey through the captivating landscapes and vibrant cultures of Australasia. Written by Frank Thomas Bullen, this travelogue offers a unique perspective as the author recounts his personal experiences and encounters during his visit to this fascinating region. From the bustling cities to the remote wilderness, Bullen's vivid descriptions and engaging narrative transport readers to the heart of Australasia, providing a captivating glimpse into its diverse people, breathtaking scenery, and rich history.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Natural beauty
