Synopsis: 
In 'A Year with the Birds: Third Edition, Enlarged', W. Warde Fowler takes readers on an enchanting journey through the world of birds. From the bustling city streets to the serene countryside, Fowler's vivid descriptions and keen observations bring these feathered creatures to life. With each passing month, the author unveils the fascinating behaviors, intricate mating rituals, and captivating songs of various bird species. As the seasons change, so do the landscapes and the birds that inhabit them, providing readers with a rich tapestry of avian wonders. This expanded edition includes new chapters and illustrations, making it an essential companion for both bird enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Ornithology

Themes: Nature appreciation, Wildlife observation, Environmental conservation
