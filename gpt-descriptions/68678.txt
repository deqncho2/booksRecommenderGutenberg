Synopsis: In 'The Sporting Chance', Alice and Claude Askew take readers on a thrilling journey through the world of high-stakes gambling and competitive sports. Set in the early 20th century, the story follows a group of ambitious athletes who find themselves entangled in a dangerous game where the stakes are higher than they could have ever imagined. As the tension rises and secrets are revealed, the characters must navigate a treacherous landscape of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the reader is left on the edge of their seat, eagerly anticipating the outcome of this gripping tale.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Betrayal, Competition
