Synopsis: 
In "Tenting To-night: A Chronicle of Sport and Adventure in Glacier Park and the Cascade Mountains," Mary Roberts Rinehart takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the breathtaking landscapes of Glacier Park and the Cascade Mountains. Join a group of adventurous travelers as they embark on a thrilling camping expedition filled with unexpected encounters, daring escapades, and the beauty of the great outdoors. As they navigate through treacherous terrains and face various challenges, the group forms unbreakable bonds and discovers the true meaning of courage and resilience. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Rinehart paints a vivid picture of the wilderness and the human spirit's indomitable will to conquer it.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Nature, Survival
