Synopsis: In 'The Book of Friendly Giants' by Fuller, Eunice, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden world filled with extraordinary giants who possess magical abilities. As Lily befriends these gentle giants, she embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes her to breathtaking landscapes and introduces her to the wonders of their ancient culture. However, as she delves deeper into their world, Lily uncovers a dark secret that threatens the harmony between humans and giants. With the fate of both worlds hanging in the balance, Lily must summon her courage and embark on a quest to restore peace and understanding.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Acceptance, Preservation of Nature
