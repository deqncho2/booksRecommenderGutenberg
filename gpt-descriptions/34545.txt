Synopsis: In 'Dr. Rumsey's Patient: A Very Strange Story', a renowned psychiatrist, Dr. Rumsey, takes on a new patient with a mysterious condition that defies all medical explanation. As he delves deeper into the patient's mind, he uncovers a web of secrets and dark forces that threaten to consume them both. With each session, the boundaries between reality and madness blur, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of the human mind.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Horror

Themes: Sanity vs. insanity, the power of the subconscious mind, the limits of science and reason
