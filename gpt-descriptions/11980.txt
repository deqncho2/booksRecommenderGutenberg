Synopsis: 
In 'A Tramp's Sketches', Graham, Stephen takes us on a captivating journey through the eyes of a wandering tramp, exploring the hidden corners of society and the human condition. Through a series of vivid and thought-provoking sketches, the reader is transported to various landscapes and encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique story to tell. From the bustling streets of London to the tranquil countryside, the tramp's observations offer a poignant and often humorous reflection on the complexities of life and the search for meaning in an ever-changing world.

Genres: 
Travelogue, Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: 
Existentialism, Social inequality, Human connection
