Synopsis: In 'Riddle of the Storm: A Mystery Story for Boys', a group of adventurous boys find themselves caught in a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon a hidden treasure map during a summer camping trip. As they embark on a dangerous journey to uncover the secrets of the map, they encounter unexpected challenges, cryptic clues, and a menacing storm that threatens to derail their quest. With time running out, the boys must rely on their wits, teamwork, and bravery to solve the riddle and unravel the mystery before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Treasure Hunting
