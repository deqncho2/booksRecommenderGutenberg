Synopsis: In '7 to 12: A Detective Story' by Anna Katharine Green, a renowned detective is faced with a perplexing case when a group of strangers find themselves trapped in a luxurious mansion. As the clock ticks, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and the detective must unravel a web of deceit to uncover the truth before time runs out. With unexpected twists and a race against time, this gripping detective story will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Time, Intrigue
