Synopsis: 

'Chronicles and Characters of the Stock Exchange' takes readers on a captivating journey into the heart of the financial world, as seen through the eyes of John Francis, an esteemed member of the Bank of England. Through a series of intriguing anecdotes and vivid descriptions, Francis unveils the hidden stories and colorful personalities that shape the stock exchange. From the exhilarating highs to the devastating lows, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the inner workings of one of the most influential institutions in the world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Finance, Biography

Themes: Financial markets, Human psychology, Power and influence
