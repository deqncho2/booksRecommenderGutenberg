Synopsis: In 'Battle Out of Time' by Dwight V. Swain, a brilliant scientist invents a time machine that inadvertently sends him hurtling back to a pivotal moment in history. Stranded in a world torn apart by war, he must navigate treacherous alliances, face unexpected dangers, and find a way to return home before altering the course of history forever. As he battles against time itself, the protagonist discovers shocking secrets and grapples with the moral implications of his actions. This thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of both the protagonist and the world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, War, Morality, Discovery
