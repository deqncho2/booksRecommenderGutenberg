Synopsis: In 'American Historical and Literary Curiosities, Part 07', Smith and Watson take readers on a captivating journey through lesser-known stories and intriguing anecdotes from American history and literature. From the mysterious disappearance of a famous author to the surprising origins of a beloved American holiday, this collection of curiosities will leave readers both entertained and enlightened. Delve into the hidden corners of American history and literature, where unexpected tales and fascinating characters await.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Literature

Themes: American history, American literature, Curiosities, Hidden stories
