Synopsis: In the enchanting world of the Blue Ridge Mountains, a young woman named Judith embarks on a journey of self-discovery and forbidden love. As she navigates the complexities of her relationships and confronts the secrets of her past, Judith finds herself torn between duty and desire, and must make choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque landscape, 'The Purple Heights' is a captivating tale of passion, mystery, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Love and desire, Identity and self-discovery, Secrets and betrayal
