Synopsis: 
Step into the opulent world of the Vanderbilt Mansion National Historic Site in New York, as author Charles W. Snell takes you on a captivating journey through its grand halls and lush gardens. From the lavish parties to the hidden secrets, this book unveils the captivating history of one of America's most iconic estates. As you explore the intricate details of the mansion's architecture and the stories of the Vanderbilt family, you'll be transported to a bygone era of wealth, power, and intrigue.

Genres: 
Historical Non-Fiction, Architecture, Biography

Themes: 
Wealth and Power, Gilded Age, American History
