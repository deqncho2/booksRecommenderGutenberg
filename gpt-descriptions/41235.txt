Synopsis: 
In 'The Sunset Trail' by Alfred Henry Lewis, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small western town, stirring up trouble and leaving a trail of unanswered questions in his wake. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and revenge, they must confront their own dark secrets and make choices that will determine their fate. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, 'The Sunset Trail' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the Wild West, where danger lurks at every turn and the line between justice and vengeance becomes blurred.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Justice, Revenge, Morality
