Synopsis: In the final volume of 'Memoirs of the Dukes of Urbino', James Dennistoun takes us on a captivating journey through the rich history of Italy from 1440 to 1630. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Dennistoun explores the fascinating world of the dukes of Urbino, shedding light on their arms, arts, and literature. From the grandeur of Renaissance Italy to the political intrigues and cultural achievements of the time, this volume offers a captivating glimpse into a bygone era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Art history

Themes: Renaissance Italy, Italian nobility, Cultural and artistic patronage, Political power and intrigue
