Synopsis: In 'The Messenger of the Black Prince' by Thomas A. H. Mawhinney, a young messenger finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger as he becomes the personal courier for the notorious Black Prince. As he embarks on a perilous journey across medieval Europe, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter mysterious characters, and uncover dark secrets that could change the course of history. With each message he delivers, the messenger becomes entangled in a complex plot that threatens not only his own life but also the fate of nations. This thrilling historical adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the truth behind the Black Prince's intentions.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Power, Identity
