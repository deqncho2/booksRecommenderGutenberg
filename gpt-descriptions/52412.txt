Synopsis: In "Abraham Lincoln and the Abolition of Slavery in the United States" by Leland, Charles Godfrey, delve into the captivating story of one of America's most iconic figures and his pivotal role in ending the institution of slavery. This insightful biography takes readers on a journey through Lincoln's life, from his humble beginnings to his presidency, as he navigates the complex political landscape of the time. Discover the challenges he faced, the decisions he made, and the profound impact he had on the nation's history.

Genres: Biography, History, Politics

Themes: Leadership, Equality, Social Justice
