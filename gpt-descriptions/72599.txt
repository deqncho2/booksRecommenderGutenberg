Synopsis: In 'The art of music, Vol. 04 (of 14) : Music in America', embark on a captivating journey through the rich and diverse musical landscape of America. From the soulful rhythms of jazz to the powerful melodies of classical compositions, this volume explores the vibrant tapestry of music that has shaped the American cultural identity. Discover the stories behind iconic American musicians and composers, and delve into the unique musical traditions that have emerged from different regions of the country. With fascinating anecdotes and insightful analysis, this volume celebrates the profound impact of music on American society and its enduring legacy.

Genres: Music History, Cultural Studies, Non-fiction

Themes: American Identity, Musical Traditions, Cultural Influence
