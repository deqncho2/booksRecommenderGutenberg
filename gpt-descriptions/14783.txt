Synopsis: In the ancient city of Rome, a group of twelve individuals is tasked with creating a set of laws that will shape the destiny of an empire. As they delve into the complexities of justice, power, and societal norms, they must confront their own personal demons and navigate the treacherous waters of politics and corruption. With the fate of Rome hanging in the balance, the Twelve Tables must find a way to balance the scales of justice and ensure a fair and just society for all.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Legal Drama, Political Thriller

Themes: Justice, Power, Corruption, Morality, Society
