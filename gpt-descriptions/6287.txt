Synopsis: In 'You Never Know Your Luck; being the story of a matrimonial deserter. Volume 3.', we follow the captivating journey of a matrimonial deserter who finds himself entangled in a web of unexpected circumstances. As he navigates through a world filled with secrets, betrayals, and hidden identities, our protagonist must confront the consequences of his choices and the unpredictable nature of fate. With each twist and turn, the story unravels the complexities of love, loyalty, and the pursuit of happiness, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Relationships, Identity and Self-Discovery, Consequences of Choices
