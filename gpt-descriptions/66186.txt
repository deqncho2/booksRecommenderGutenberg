Synopsis: In 'The Collected Works of Henrik Ibsen, Vol. 02 (of 11)', Henrik Ibsen presents a collection of his captivating plays that delve into the complexities of human relationships and societal norms. From the intense exploration of family dynamics in "Ghosts" to the examination of the consequences of societal expectations in "A Doll's House," Ibsen's masterful storytelling and thought-provoking characters will keep readers engrossed in the intricate web of emotions and secrets that unfold within each play.

Genres: Drama, Realism, Tragedy

Themes: Family, Gender roles, Social expectations, Morality
