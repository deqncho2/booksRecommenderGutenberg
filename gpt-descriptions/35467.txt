Synopsis: In 'The Tenants of Malory, Volume 1' by Joseph Sheridan Le Fanu, a young woman named Alice Maybell finds herself drawn into a mysterious and haunting world when she becomes the governess at the remote and eerie Malory Manor. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the house and its inhabitants, Alice uncovers a web of dark family history, forbidden love, and supernatural occurrences that threaten to consume her. With each passing day, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leaving Alice questioning her own sanity and safety. Will she be able to unravel the mysteries of Malory Manor before it's too late?

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Madness, Supernatural occurrences
