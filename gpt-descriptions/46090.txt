Synopsis: In 'Lancashire Sketches: Third Edition', Edwin Waugh takes us on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes and vibrant communities of Lancashire. Through his vivid descriptions and keen observations, Waugh brings to life the unique charm and character of this region, from its bustling industrial towns to its serene countryside. From encounters with colorful locals to tales of folklore and tradition, this collection of sketches offers a delightful glimpse into the heart and soul of Lancashire.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, Regional Literature

Themes: Regional identity, Community, Cultural heritage
