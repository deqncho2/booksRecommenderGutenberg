Synopsis: In 'Tiger by the Tail' by Anderson, Poul, a group of interstellar explorers stumble upon a mysterious planet that holds the key to unimaginable power. As they delve deeper into the planet's secrets, they find themselves caught in a dangerous game of survival, facing treacherous creatures and unforeseen challenges. With time running out, they must navigate the perilous landscape and unravel the planet's enigmatic past before it consumes them all.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Power, Survival
