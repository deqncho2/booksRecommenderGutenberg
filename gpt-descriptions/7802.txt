Synopsis: 
In 'Seven O'Clock Stories' by Robert Gordon Anderson, readers are transported to a world where imagination knows no bounds. This collection of enchanting tales takes place at the magical hour of seven o'clock, when extraordinary things happen. From a mischievous cat who leads a secret double life to a young boy who discovers a hidden treasure map, each story is filled with wonder, adventure, and unexpected twists. As the clock strikes seven, prepare to embark on a journey that will ignite your imagination and leave you eagerly awaiting the next tale.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Discovery, Mystery
