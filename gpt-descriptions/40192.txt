Synopsis: In the second volume of "A Student's History of England," Samuel Rawson Gardiner takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period from 1509 to 1689. From the reign of Henry VIII to the Glorious Revolution, this comprehensive history delves into the political, religious, and social upheavals that shaped England during this transformative era. Gardiner's vivid storytelling and meticulous research bring to life the fascinating characters and events that defined this pivotal period in English history, making it an essential read for anyone seeking a deeper understanding of the country's past.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Political upheaval, Religious conflict, Social change
