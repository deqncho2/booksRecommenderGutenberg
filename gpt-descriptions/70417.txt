Synopsis: 

In the second edition of 'Tinting and Toning of Eastman Positive Motion Picture Film,' Eastman Kodak Company takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of film tinting and toning. This revised edition delves into the intricate techniques and processes used to enhance the visual experience of motion pictures, offering a comprehensive guide for both professionals and enthusiasts alike. From the vibrant hues of tinting to the subtle shades of toning, this book unveils the secrets behind transforming black and white films into mesmerizing works of art. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Film-making, Technical guide

Themes: Film restoration, Visual aesthetics, Cinematic storytelling
