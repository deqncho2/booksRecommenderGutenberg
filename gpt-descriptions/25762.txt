Synopsis: 
When Billie Bradley and her friends stumble upon a mysterious wreck on Lighthouse Island, they find themselves embroiled in a thrilling adventure filled with secrets, danger, and unexpected twists. As they delve deeper into the enigma surrounding the wreck, Billie and her companions must use their wits and bravery to uncover the truth before it's too late. With each clue they uncover, the mystery only deepens, leading them on a suspenseful journey that will test their courage and friendship.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
