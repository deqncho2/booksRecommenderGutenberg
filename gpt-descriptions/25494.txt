Synopsis: 
In the thrilling adventure novel 'The Young Alaskans' by Hough, Emerson, four young friends embark on a daring journey through the rugged wilderness of Alaska. Determined to prove their mettle and test their survival skills, they face treacherous landscapes, encounter wild animals, and navigate dangerous waters. As they navigate the untamed Alaskan frontier, they discover hidden secrets and face unexpected challenges that will push them to their limits. Will their friendship and resourcefulness be enough to overcome the perils that await them?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Exploration
