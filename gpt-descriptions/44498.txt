Synopsis: In 'The Wreck of the Grosvenor, Volume 2 of 3: An account of the mutiny of the crew and the loss of the ship when trying to make the Bermudas', the gripping tale continues as the crew of the ill-fated ship faces a mutiny and the devastating loss of their vessel while attempting to reach the Bermudas. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey of survival and betrayal, where the fate of the crew hangs in the balance.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Maritime

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Loyalty
