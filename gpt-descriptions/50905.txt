Synopsis: In 'Yesterday House' by Leiber, Fritz, a struggling writer named Tom encounters a mysterious old house that seems to exist outside of time. As he explores its rooms, he discovers that each one represents a different era in history, complete with authentic details and inhabitants. Tom becomes enthralled by the house's ability to transport him to different periods, but soon realizes that there may be a dark secret lurking within its walls. As he delves deeper into the house's mysteries, Tom must confront his own desires and fears, leading to a thrilling and unexpected conclusion.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Time travel, Identity, Perception of reality
