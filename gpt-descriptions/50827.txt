Synopsis: In 'Orphans of the Void' by Michael Shaara, a group of young astronauts find themselves stranded on a mysterious planet after their spaceship crashes. As they struggle to survive in this unfamiliar and dangerous environment, they uncover startling secrets about the planet and its inhabitants. With their lives hanging in the balance, the orphans of the void must navigate treacherous terrain, face unexpected challenges, and confront their own fears in order to find a way back home.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Identity, Friendship
