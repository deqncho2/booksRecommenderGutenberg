Synopsis: 
In 'More Bab Ballads', Gilbert, W. S. presents a delightful collection of humorous and satirical poems that will leave readers chuckling and pondering. From absurd tales of love and courtship to witty commentaries on societal norms, Gilbert's clever wordplay and sharp wit shine through each verse. Whether it's a comical exploration of the perils of marriage or a whimsical examination of human folly, these ballads offer a delightful escape into a world of laughter and reflection.

Genres: 
Humor, Satire, Poetry

Themes: 
Love and courtship, Society and its conventions, Human folly
