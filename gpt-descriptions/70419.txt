Synopsis: In "History of Christian Names," Charlotte M. Yonge takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins and meanings of various Christian names. From biblical figures to saints and martyrs, Yonge explores the rich history and cultural significance behind these names, shedding light on the traditions and beliefs that have shaped them over centuries. With fascinating anecdotes and historical context, this book offers a delightful exploration of the stories and symbolism behind the names we bear.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Reference

Themes: Naming traditions, Cultural significance, Religious symbolism
