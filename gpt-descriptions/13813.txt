Synopsis:
In 'The Common Law' by Robert W. Chambers, a young artist named John Sargent is captivated by the enigmatic beauty of a woman he encounters in Paris. As their paths continue to cross, their lives become intertwined in a complex web of love, desire, and betrayal. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant art scene in late 19th-century France, this gripping tale explores the boundaries of passion and the consequences of following one's heart.

Genres:
Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Love and desire, Betrayal, Art and creativity
