Synopsis: In 'Horrors and Atrocities of the Great War: Including the Tragic Destruction of the Lusitania' by Marshall, Logan, readers are taken on a gripping journey through the harrowing events of World War I. From the trenches of the Western Front to the devastating sinking of the Lusitania, this book unveils the shocking horrors and atrocities that unfolded during this tumultuous period in history. Through vivid descriptions and personal accounts, the author paints a haunting picture of the human cost of war, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the untold stories of bravery, tragedy, and resilience.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, War Literature, True Crime

Themes: War, Human Tragedy, Resilience, Historical Events
