Synopsis: In 'A Little World' by George Manville Fenn, a young boy named Dick is thrust into a thrilling adventure when he discovers a hidden world within his own home. As he explores this mysterious realm, he encounters strange creatures, uncovers ancient secrets, and faces dangerous challenges. With the help of newfound friends, Dick must navigate this fantastical world and unravel its enigmatic past. Filled with suspense, wonder, and unexpected twists, 'A Little World' takes readers on a captivating journey that will leave them eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Discovery
