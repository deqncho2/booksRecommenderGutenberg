Synopsis: 
In the small town of Willow Creek, Daisy Burns leads a seemingly ordinary life as a high school student. However, everything changes when she discovers a hidden family secret that unlocks a world of magic and mystery. As Daisy delves deeper into her newfound powers, she must navigate the dangerous realm of supernatural creatures, uncover dark secrets, and confront her own inner demons. With the help of a group of unlikely allies, Daisy embarks on a thrilling adventure that will test her courage, loyalty, and determination. Will she be able to embrace her destiny and save her loved ones from the looming darkness?

Genres: Fantasy, Young Adult, Mystery

Themes: Coming of age, Identity, Power and responsibility
