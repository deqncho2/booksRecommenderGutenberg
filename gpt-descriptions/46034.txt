Synopsis: 

In 'Gibraltar' by Henry M. Field, a gripping tale unfolds as a young American traveler finds himself embroiled in a mysterious and dangerous adventure while visiting the iconic Rock of Gibraltar. As he delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the ancient fortress, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, espionage, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices that will determine not only his own fate but also the fate of those around him. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Intrigue, Espionage, Identity, Loyalty
