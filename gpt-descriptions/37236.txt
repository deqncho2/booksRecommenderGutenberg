Synopsis: In 'The Tigress' by Warner, Anne, a mysterious woman named Isabella arrives in a small coastal town, captivating the locals with her enigmatic charm and striking beauty. As the townspeople become increasingly infatuated with her, they soon discover that Isabella is not who she appears to be. Secrets, lies, and hidden desires unravel, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Obsession, Betrayal
