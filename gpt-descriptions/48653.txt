Synopsis: In 'The Khaki Boys at the Front; or, Shoulder to Shoulder in the Trenches' by Josephine Chase, readers are transported to the harrowing battlefields of World War I. Follow the gripping journey of three young American soldiers as they navigate the horrors of war, forge unbreakable bonds, and discover the true meaning of bravery and sacrifice. As they face unimaginable challenges and witness the devastating consequences of war, the Khaki Boys must find strength within themselves and each other to survive the trenches and make it back home.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Sacrifice, Survival
