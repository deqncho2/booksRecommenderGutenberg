Synopsis: In 'Selina: Her Hopeful Efforts and Her Livelier Failures' by Martin, George Madden, follow the captivating journey of Selina, a young woman determined to overcome the challenges of her time. From her humble beginnings in a small town to her pursuit of education and love, Selina's story is filled with both triumphs and setbacks. As she navigates societal expectations and personal ambitions, readers will be drawn into a world of hope, resilience, and the complexities of human relationships.

Genres: Historical fiction, coming-of-age, romance

Themes: Ambition, societal expectations, resilience, love, personal growth
