Synopsis: In the enchanting kingdom of Graustark, a young American named Grenfall Lorry finds himself embroiled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and high-stakes adventure. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of this foreign land, Grenfall must choose between duty and desire, loyalty and self-preservation. With the fate of Graustark hanging in the balance, will he be able to unravel the secrets that threaten to tear the kingdom apart?

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Political Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Loyalty and betrayal, Power and politics
