Synopsis: 

In 'Field Mice as Farm and Orchard Pests', David E. Lantz delves into the fascinating world of these seemingly harmless creatures and uncovers the hidden dangers they pose to farms and orchards. Through meticulous research and captivating anecdotes, Lantz explores the intricate relationship between field mice and agricultural landscapes, revealing the surprising extent of their impact on crop yields and the livelihoods of farmers. As he uncovers the cunning strategies employed by these tiny rodents, readers will be captivated by the battle between man and mouse, and the ingenious methods employed to protect valuable crops. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Agriculture, Environmental Science

Themes: Pest control, Agriculture, Ecological balance
