Synopsis: In 'What Necessity Knows' by L. Dougall, a young woman named Lily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience as she navigates the complexities of love, loss, and the pursuit of her dreams. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, Lily's story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, as she grapples with societal expectations and the limitations placed upon her gender. As she faces numerous challenges and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Lily must confront her own desires and confront the question of what truly matters in life.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Gender roles, Self-discovery, Love and loss
