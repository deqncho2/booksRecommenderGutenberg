Synopsis: In 'The Scape-Goat: A Farce in One Act', chaos ensues when a case of mistaken identity leads to a series of hilarious and absurd situations. As the protagonist finds himself caught in a web of misunderstandings, he must navigate through a world of eccentric characters and comical mishaps, all while desperately trying to clear his name. This fast-paced farce will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly anticipating the next twist and turn.

Genres: Farce, Comedy

Themes: Mistaken identity, Absurdity, Social satire
