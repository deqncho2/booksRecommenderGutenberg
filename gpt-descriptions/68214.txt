Synopsis: In "The Old Paths, or the Talmud Tested by Scripture," Alexander McCaul delves into a thought-provoking comparison between modern Judaism and the religion of Moses and the prophets. Through a meticulous examination of principles and doctrines, McCaul challenges the reader to question the evolution of Judaism and its alignment with its ancient roots. With a focus on scriptural analysis, this work offers a fascinating exploration of the tensions between tradition and interpretation.

Genres: Religious Studies, Comparative Religion, Historical Analysis

Themes: Tradition vs. Interpretation, Scriptural Analysis, Evolution of Religious Beliefs
