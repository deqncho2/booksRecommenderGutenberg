Synopsis: In 'Account of a voyage of discovery to the west coast of Corea, and the great Loo-Choo Island', Basil Hall takes readers on a captivating journey to the mysterious and unexplored lands of Corea and Loo-Choo Island. Through vivid descriptions and thrilling encounters, Hall recounts his adventures, encounters with the local people, and the fascinating customs and traditions he discovers along the way. As he navigates through treacherous waters and encounters unexpected challenges, readers will be enthralled by the sense of discovery and the unknown that permeates every page.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Exploration of unknown lands, Cultural encounters, Survival in unfamiliar environments
