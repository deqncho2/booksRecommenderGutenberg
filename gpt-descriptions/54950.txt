Synopsis: In 'The Vermilion Pencil: A Romance of China' by Lea, Homer, a young artist named Mei embarks on a journey that intertwines love, art, and the rich tapestry of Chinese culture. Set against the backdrop of ancient China, Mei's life takes an unexpected turn when she encounters a mysterious vermilion pencil that holds a secret power. As she delves deeper into the enigmatic world of the pencil, Mei discovers a hidden realm where her artistic creations come to life, blurring the lines between reality and imagination. With each stroke of the vermilion pencil, Mei's artistic abilities soar, but she soon realizes that this newfound power comes with a price. As she navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of artistic perfection, Mei must confront the consequences of her choices and decide what truly matters to her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Artistic expression, Love and sacrifice, Cultural identity
