Synopsis: In "Morgan's Men," Henry Lane Stone takes us on a thrilling journey through the American Civil War, recounting his personal experiences as a member of the legendary Confederate cavalry unit led by General John Hunt Morgan. From daring raids and intense battles to unexpected friendships and heart-wrenching losses, Stone's vivid narrative immerses us in the harrowing realities of war and the indomitable spirit of those who fought on both sides.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Memoir

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, The Human Cost of War
