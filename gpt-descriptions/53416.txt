Synopsis: In 'Only a Girl's Love' by Charles Garvice, a young woman named Muriel finds herself caught between the expectations of society and the desires of her heart. As she navigates the complexities of love and loyalty, Muriel must make difficult choices that will shape her future. With captivating twists and turns, this novel explores the power of love and the strength of a woman's determination.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, societal expectations, personal growth
