Synopsis: In "Miles Wallingford: Sequel to 'Afloat and Ashore'," James Fenimore Cooper takes readers on a thrilling maritime adventure as Miles Wallingford, a young sailor, navigates the treacherous waters of love, friendship, and betrayal. Set against the backdrop of the early 19th century, Miles finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue that will test his loyalty and resilience. As he sails across the vast oceans, encountering pirates, storms, and unexpected allies, Miles must confront his own past and make difficult choices that will shape his future. Will he find redemption and true love amidst the perils of the sea?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Redemption, Love, Coming of Age
