Synopsis: 

'North Cornwall Fairies and Legends' by Tregarthen, Enys is a captivating collection of enchanting tales that transport readers into the mystical world of North Cornwall. From mischievous fairies to ancient legends, this book unveils the hidden secrets and magical creatures that reside in the picturesque landscapes of Cornwall. Each story is filled with wonder, adventure, and a touch of mystery, leaving readers spellbound and eager to uncover the next mythical tale.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Short Stories

Themes: Magic, Mythology, Nature, Adventure
