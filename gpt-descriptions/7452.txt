Synopsis: 
'Autobiography of a Yogi' is a captivating memoir that takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the life of Paramahansa Yogananda, a renowned spiritual leader and yogi. From his early years in India to his encounters with saints and sages, Yogananda shares his profound experiences and spiritual insights, offering a glimpse into the mystical world of yoga and meditation. With vivid storytelling and a blend of adventure, wisdom, and humor, this autobiography invites readers to explore the depths of human potential and the transformative power of spirituality.

Genres: 
Biography, Spiritual, Memoir

Themes: 
Spirituality, Self-realization, Mysticism, Yoga, Meditation
