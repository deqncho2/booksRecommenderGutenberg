Synopsis: 

In 'Banking' by William Amasa Scott, a young and ambitious banker named John finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, power struggles, and financial intrigue. As he navigates the cutthroat world of banking, John must confront his own moral compass and make difficult choices that could either lead to his success or downfall. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of the banking industry and the lengths people will go to protect their interests.

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Mystery

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Power, Deception
