Synopsis: In "The Sacred Formulas of the Cherokees," James Mooney delves into the rich cultural heritage of the Cherokee people, uncovering their sacred rituals and ancient formulas. Through meticulous research and firsthand accounts, Mooney provides a fascinating glimpse into the spiritual practices and beliefs of this Native American tribe. From healing ceremonies to divination rituals, this comprehensive study sheds light on the profound connection between the Cherokees and the natural world around them. As readers delve into the pages of this captivating work, they will be transported to a time and place where ancient traditions and spiritual wisdom intertwine.

Genres: Ethnography, Anthropology, Native American Studies

Themes: Native American spirituality, Cultural heritage, Rituals and ceremonies
