Synopsis: In "How to paint permanent pictures" by Maximilian Toch, a struggling artist stumbles upon a mysterious technique that allows him to create paintings with an extraordinary quality. As he delves deeper into this newfound ability, he discovers that his art has the power to transcend reality and touch the souls of those who view it. However, with great power comes great responsibility, and he soon finds himself facing a moral dilemma that could change the course of his artistic journey forever.

Genres: Fiction, Magical Realism, Drama

Themes: Artistic expression, the power of creativity, morality and ethics
