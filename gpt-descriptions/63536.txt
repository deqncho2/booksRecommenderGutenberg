Synopsis: Advisory Ben: A Story follows the intriguing journey of Ben, a young man with a unique ability to offer advice to those in need. As he navigates through various encounters and challenges, Ben's wisdom and guidance become sought after by individuals from all walks of life. From troubled relationships to career dilemmas, Ben's insights have the power to transform lives. However, as his popularity grows, Ben must confront the consequences of his extraordinary gift and the impact it has on his own personal journey.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Wisdom, Personal growth, Human connection
