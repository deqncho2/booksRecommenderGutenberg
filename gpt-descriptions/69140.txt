Synopsis: In 'Remember me, Kama!' by Kubilius, Walter, a young woman named Kama embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure after receiving a mysterious letter from her estranged father. As she delves into her family's past, Kama uncovers secrets that will challenge her perception of love, loyalty, and the power of forgiveness. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that spans generations, leading her to question her own identity and the choices she has made. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant and exotic world, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Family, Identity, Secrets, Forgiveness
