Synopsis: In 'Mystery Wings: A Mystery Story for Boys', a group of adventurous boys stumble upon a hidden secret while exploring an old abandoned airfield. As they uncover clues and encounter unexpected dangers, they find themselves entangled in a thrilling mystery that involves mysterious aircraft, hidden treasures, and a race against time. With their courage and resourcefulness, the boys must unravel the enigma and solve the puzzle before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
