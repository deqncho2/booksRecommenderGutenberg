Synopsis: 
In 'Fair and Warmer' by Von Wald, E. G., a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious events that leave the residents questioning their own sanity. As the occurrences become increasingly bizarre, a group of unlikely allies must unravel the truth behind the strange happenings before it's too late. With a blend of suspense, humor, and a touch of the supernatural, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Community, Friendship
