Synopsis: 
In 'My Life in China and America', Yung Wing takes us on a captivating journey through his extraordinary life, spanning two vastly different cultures. From his humble beginnings in China to his groundbreaking education in America, Yung Wing's memoir is a compelling tale of resilience, ambition, and the pursuit of the American Dream. As he navigates the complexities of identity, family, and societal expectations, Yung Wing's experiences offer a unique perspective on the challenges and triumphs of assimilation and cultural integration.

Genres: Autobiography, Memoir, Cultural History

Themes: Identity, Assimilation, Cultural Exchange, Education, Family
