Synopsis: In 'The Ear in the Wall' by Arthur B. Reeve, a brilliant detective and his loyal sidekick find themselves entangled in a perplexing case when they stumble upon a mysterious ear hidden within the walls of a grand mansion. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the house, they uncover a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden identities that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each twist and turn, the duo must race against time to solve the puzzle before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Intrigue
