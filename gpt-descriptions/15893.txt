Synopsis: In 'The Lighted Way' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious and enigmatic woman enters the life of a successful lawyer, leading him down a path of danger, intrigue, and unexpected romance. As he becomes entangled in her web of secrets, he must navigate a treacherous world of espionage and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. With his reputation and life on the line, he must uncover the truth before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Deception, Trust, Redemption
