Synopsis: In the short story "Zero Hour" by Ray Bradbury, a group of children become obsessed with a new game called "Invasion," which involves preparing for an imminent alien invasion. As the game becomes increasingly intense, the children's innocent play takes a chilling turn, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers questioning the power of imagination and the blurred lines between reality and fantasy.

Genres: Science Fiction, Horror, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Imagination, Childhood, Reality vs. Fantasy
