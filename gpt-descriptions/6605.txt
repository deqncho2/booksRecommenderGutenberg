Synopsis: In 'Unconscious Memory' by Samuel Butler, a young man embarks on a journey of self-discovery as he delves into the depths of his own mind. Through a series of vivid and haunting dreams, he uncovers hidden memories and forgotten experiences that challenge his perception of reality. As he navigates the blurred lines between past and present, the protagonist must confront the power of the unconscious mind and its profound impact on his life. This thought-provoking exploration of memory and identity will captivate readers, leaving them questioning the boundaries of their own consciousness.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Mystery, Surrealism

Themes: Memory, Identity, Perception, Dreams
