Synopsis: In the gripping thriller 'The Untouchable' by Stephen A. Kallis, a brilliant hacker named Alex finds himself caught in a deadly game of cat and mouse when he inadvertently uncovers a dangerous secret. As he races against time to protect himself and those he loves, he must navigate a treacherous web of deception, corruption, and betrayal. With his life on the line, Alex must use all his skills and cunning to outsmart his enemies and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Technology, Conspiracy, Survival
