Synopsis: 

'Whales, Dolphins, and Porpoises of the Western North Atlantic: A Guide to Their Identification' is an informative and captivating guide that takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of these magnificent marine creatures. Written by Caldwell, David Keller, Leatherwood, Stephen, and Winn, Howard Elliott, this comprehensive book provides a wealth of knowledge about the various species found in the Western North Atlantic, offering detailed descriptions, stunning photographs, and helpful identification tips. From the majestic humpback whales to the playful dolphins and elusive porpoises, this guide is a must-have for anyone interested in marine life and conservation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Marine Biology

Themes: Marine conservation, Biodiversity, Wildlife identification
