Synopsis: In 'The Real Tsaritsa' by Den, Lili, a young woman named Anastasia finds herself thrust into the opulent world of the Russian royal court. As she navigates the treacherous waters of palace intrigue and power struggles, Anastasia must uncover the truth about her own identity and the secrets that threaten to tear her world apart. With danger lurking at every turn, she must decide whom to trust and how far she is willing to go to protect her loved ones. This gripping tale of love, betrayal, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Loyalty, Deception
