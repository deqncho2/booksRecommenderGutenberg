Synopsis: 
In 'The Kitáb-i-Íqán', Bahá'u'lláh presents a profound exploration of the nature of religion, unveiling the hidden truths and mysteries that lie at the heart of human existence. Through a captivating dialogue between the Prophet Muhammad and a learned scholar, the reader is taken on a transformative journey of spiritual discovery, challenging conventional beliefs and shedding light on the unity of all religions. As the narrative unfolds, Bahá'u'lláh's words resonate with timeless wisdom, inviting readers to question their own understanding of faith and embark on a path of enlightenment.

Genres: Religious literature, Philosophy, Spiritual teachings

Themes: Unity of religions, Spiritual enlightenment, Divine revelation, Religious truth
