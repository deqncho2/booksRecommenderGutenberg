Synopsis: In Fulton's "Steam Battery": Blockship and Catamaran, Howard Irving Chapelle takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of naval engineering and innovation. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Chapelle explores the fascinating story of Robert Fulton's revolutionary steam-powered warship designs. From the challenges faced in constructing these groundbreaking vessels to the dramatic impact they had on naval warfare, this book offers a thrilling account of Fulton's visionary creations. Delve into the pages of this historical narrative to uncover the secrets behind Fulton's "Steam Battery" and its significant contributions to naval technology.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Maritime History, Engineering

Themes: Innovation, Naval Warfare, Technological Advancements
