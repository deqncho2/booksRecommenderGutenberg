Synopsis: In 'The Billiard Room Mystery' by Flynn, Brian, a renowned detective is called to solve a perplexing murder that takes place in an opulent mansion during a high-stakes billiards tournament. As the detective delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives among the eccentric cast of characters. With time running out and the tension rising, the detective must unravel the truth before another life is lost.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Intrigue
