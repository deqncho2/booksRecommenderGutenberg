Synopsis: 

In 'Irish Historical Allusions, Curious Customs and Superstitions, County of Kerry, Corkaguiny', author Patrick M. Foley takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history, intriguing customs, and enchanting superstitions of the County of Kerry in Ireland. From ancient legends and folklore to the unique traditions and beliefs of the local people, this book unveils a world of captivating stories and curious practices that have shaped the cultural fabric of the region. Delving into the depths of Kerry's past, Foley uncovers hidden gems of historical allusions, shedding light on the events and figures that have left an indelible mark on the county. With a blend of meticulous research and engaging storytelling, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the captivating world of Kerry's history, customs, and superstitions.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Folklore

Themes: Irish culture, Local traditions, Historical events, Superstitions
