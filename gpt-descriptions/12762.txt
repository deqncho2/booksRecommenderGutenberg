Synopsis: In "The Story of a Piece of Coal: What It Is, Whence It Comes, and Whither It Goes," Edward A. Martin takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of coal. Through vivid storytelling and insightful explanations, Martin explores the origins, formation, and remarkable journey of a single piece of coal, revealing the hidden secrets and incredible transformations that occur over millions of years. From ancient forests to bustling cities, from the depths of the Earth to the heights of human innovation, this book unveils the extraordinary impact that coal has had on our planet and our civilization. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Geology, Energy, Industrialization
