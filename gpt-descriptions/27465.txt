Synopsis: 
In 'Birds in the Calendar' by Frederick G. Aflalo, a mysterious calendar arrives at the doorstep of a young couple, bringing with it a series of unexpected events. As they delve into the calendar's enigmatic origins, they uncover a hidden world filled with secrets, adventure, and a connection to the avian realm that they never could have imagined. With each passing month, the couple becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, leading them on a thrilling journey that will test their courage and challenge their perceptions of reality.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Fate and destiny, the power of nature, the exploration of hidden realms
