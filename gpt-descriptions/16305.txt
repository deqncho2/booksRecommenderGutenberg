Synopsis: In 'Crayon and Character: Truth Made Clear Through Eye and Ear: Or, Ten-Minute Talks with Colored Chalks', author B. J. Griswold takes readers on a captivating journey through a series of ten-minute talks that use colored chalks to reveal profound truths about life. Each talk is a unique blend of art and storytelling, as the author skillfully weaves together vivid illustrations and engaging narratives to convey important life lessons. From exploring the power of imagination to delving into the complexities of human character, this book offers a refreshing and enlightening perspective on the world around us.

Genres: Self-help, Inspirational, Art

Themes: Personal growth, Character development, Creativity, Perception
