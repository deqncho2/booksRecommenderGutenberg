Synopsis: In 'Hints on the Use and Handling of Firearms Generally, and the Revolver in Particular', Curling, H. Onslow provides a comprehensive guide to mastering the art of firearms, with a particular focus on the revolver. From the basics of loading and aiming to advanced techniques for accuracy and speed, this book offers invaluable advice for both novice and experienced shooters. With vivid descriptions and practical tips, Curling explores the intricacies of handling firearms, ensuring readers are captivated by the fascinating world of marksmanship.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Reference

Themes: Firearms, Shooting techniques, Safety
