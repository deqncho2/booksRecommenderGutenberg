Synopsis: In 'The Green Goddess', a group of passengers find themselves stranded in the remote jungles of India after their plane crashes. As they struggle to survive, they encounter a mysterious temple and its enigmatic high priestess, the Green Goddess. With tensions rising and secrets unraveling, the passengers must navigate a treacherous web of love, betrayal, and sacrifice in order to escape the clutches of the jungle and the allure of the Green Goddess.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Survival, Forbidden Love, Spiritualism
