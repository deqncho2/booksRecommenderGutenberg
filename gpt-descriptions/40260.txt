Synopsis: In "The Last Days of Tolstoy," Chertkov takes us on a gripping journey into the final chapter of one of the greatest literary minds in history. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century Russia, this captivating narrative explores the tumultuous relationship between Leo Tolstoy and his devoted disciple, Chertkov. As Tolstoy grapples with his own mortality and the weight of his literary legacy, Chertkov finds himself torn between loyalty and the pursuit of his own ideals. With political intrigue, personal conflicts, and a race against time, this poignant tale delves into the complexities of friendship, betrayal, and the enduring power of literature.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biographical Fiction, Literary Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Betrayal, Mortality, Legacy, Literature
