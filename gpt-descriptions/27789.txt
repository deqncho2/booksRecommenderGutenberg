Synopsis: In 'A Royal Prisoner', a thrilling tale of political intrigue and suspense, a young prince finds himself imprisoned in a remote castle, stripped of his title and forced to live a life of anonymity. As he navigates the treacherous world of courtly conspiracies and power struggles, he must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to survive and reclaim his rightful place on the throne. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power and corruption, Identity and self-discovery, Loyalty and betrayal
