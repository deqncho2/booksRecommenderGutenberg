Synopsis: In 'Advice on Fox-Hunting', Willoughby de Broke, Henry Verney, baron, takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the world of fox-hunting, sharing invaluable insights and tips for both novice and experienced hunters. From the thrill of the chase to the intricacies of the hunt, this guide offers a captivating exploration of the sport, filled with anecdotes, advice, and a deep appreciation for the beauty of the countryside. Whether you are a passionate hunter or simply curious about this age-old tradition, this book promises to be an engaging and informative read.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Outdoor Recreation

Themes: Tradition, Nature, Skill
