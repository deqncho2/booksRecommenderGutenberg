Synopsis: In "Bokwala: The Story of a Congo Victim," we are transported to the heart of the Democratic Republic of Congo, where we follow the gripping journey of Bokwala, a young Congolese woman whose life is forever altered by the brutal conflict that ravages her homeland. As she navigates through the chaos and violence, Bokwala's resilience and determination become her greatest weapons in the face of unimaginable adversity. This powerful narrative sheds light on the untold stories of the Congolese people, their unwavering spirit, and the indomitable strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Resilience, Survival, Identity, Social Injustice
