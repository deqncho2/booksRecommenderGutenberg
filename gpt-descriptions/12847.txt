Synopsis: In 'Havelok the Dane: A Legend of Old Grimsby and Lincoln', Charles W. Whistler weaves a captivating tale set in the medieval era. The story follows Havelok, a young prince with a mysterious past, as he embarks on a journey filled with adventure, romance, and treachery. As Havelok navigates the complexities of his newfound identity, he must confront his destiny and face the challenges that lie ahead. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling characters, this legend unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Destiny, Betrayal, Love
