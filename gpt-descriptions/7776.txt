Synopsis: In the remote and rugged mountains of Kentucky, a young man named Chadwick Buford finds himself torn between his love for a beautiful mountain girl and his duty to his family. As he navigates the challenges of the Appalachian wilderness, Chadwick must confront his own desires and the expectations of his community, leading to a gripping tale of love, loyalty, and self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Tradition and Modernity, Nature and Wilderness
