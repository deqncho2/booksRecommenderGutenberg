Synopsis: In 'The Child's Pictorial History of England: From the Earliest Period to the Present Time', Miss Corner takes young readers on an enchanting journey through the captivating history of England. Through vivid illustrations and engaging storytelling, this book brings to life the remarkable events, influential figures, and significant moments that have shaped the nation. From the ancient Romans to the Tudors, the Industrial Revolution to the World Wars, and beyond, readers will discover the rich tapestry of England's past and gain a deeper understanding of its cultural heritage. This delightful and educational book is a perfect introduction to history for young minds.

Genres: Children's Literature, History, Non-fiction

Themes: History, Cultural Heritage, Education
