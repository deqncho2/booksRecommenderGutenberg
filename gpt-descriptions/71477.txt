Synopsis: In "Brave Bessie Westland: A Story of Quaker Persecution," we follow the courageous journey of Bessie Westland, a young Quaker woman living in a time of religious intolerance and persecution. Set in the 17th century, Bessie's unwavering faith and determination lead her on a path filled with danger, sacrifice, and unexpected alliances. As she navigates through a society that fears and condemns her beliefs, Bessie's resilience and bravery are put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to overcome the challenges and stand up for her faith, or will she succumb to the pressures of conformity?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Religious Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Religious persecution, Faith and resilience, Courage and sacrifice
