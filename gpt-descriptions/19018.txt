Synopsis: In the small Norwegian town of Ostrat, Lady Inger, a powerful and enigmatic figure, finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and personal dilemmas. As tensions rise between the Danish king and the Norwegian rebels, Lady Inger must navigate the treacherous waters of loyalty, betrayal, and the pursuit of power. With her family's fate hanging in the balance, she must make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of her loved ones and her nation.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Political Thriller

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Family and Duty
