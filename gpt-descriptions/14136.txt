Synopsis: In "The Outdoor Girls at the Hostess House; Or, Doing Their Best for the Soldiers" by Laura Lee Hope, the adventurous Outdoor Girls find themselves at a military camp, where they are determined to make a difference in the lives of the soldiers. As they navigate the challenges of war and the complexities of relationships, the girls discover the true meaning of friendship, loyalty, and sacrifice.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Patriotism, Service, Coming of Age
