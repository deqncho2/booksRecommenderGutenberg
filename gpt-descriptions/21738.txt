Synopsis:
In 'Hunted and Harried' by R. M. Ballantyne, a young boy named Hugh Proctor finds himself caught in a thrilling adventure when he becomes entangled with a notorious gang of smugglers. As he tries to outwit his pursuers and uncover their secrets, Hugh must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to survive in a world filled with danger and treachery. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leading to a heart-pounding climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres:
Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes:
Courage, Survival, Loyalty, Deception
