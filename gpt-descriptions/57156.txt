Synopsis: In "The Scarecrow; or The Glass of Truth: A Tragedy of the Ludicrous," MacKaye takes readers on a whimsical journey through a small New England town where a mysterious scarecrow comes to life. As the scarecrow interacts with the townspeople, their deepest secrets and desires are revealed, leading to unexpected consequences. This tragicomic tale explores the power of truth, the masks we wear, and the consequences of our actions.

Genres: Tragicomedy, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Truth and deception, Identity and self-discovery, Consequences of actions
