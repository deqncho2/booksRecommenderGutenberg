Synopsis: In 'Pickle the Spy; Or, the Incognito of Prince Charles' by Lang, Andrew, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure set in the 18th century. The story follows the enigmatic character of Pickle, a spy who finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and espionage. As Pickle navigates through dangerous encounters and secret missions, he must maintain his disguise and protect his true identity at all costs. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale keeps readers on the edge of their seats, wondering who can be trusted and what secrets lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Espionage, Identity, Loyalty, Deception
