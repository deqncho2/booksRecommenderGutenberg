Synopsis: 

In "First Across the Continent: The Story of the Exploring Expedition of Lewis and Clark in 1804-5-6" by Noah Brooks, embark on a thrilling adventure through the uncharted American wilderness. Join Meriwether Lewis and William Clark as they lead a courageous team of explorers on a daring expedition to discover a route to the Pacific Ocean. Encounter breathtaking landscapes, encounter Native American tribes, and face the challenges of survival in this gripping tale of exploration and discovery.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Courage, Native American culture
