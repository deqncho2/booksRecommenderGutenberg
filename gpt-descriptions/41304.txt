Synopsis: In 'The Mother of Parliaments' by Harry Graham, a young journalist stumbles upon a hidden secret within the hallowed halls of the British Parliament. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he uncovers a web of corruption, power struggles, and personal vendettas that threaten to shake the very foundations of democracy. With his career and life on the line, he must navigate the treacherous world of politics and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Political Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Power, Betrayal, Truth
