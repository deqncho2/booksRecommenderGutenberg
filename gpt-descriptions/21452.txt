Synopsis: In 'Ernest Bracebridge: School Days' by William Henry Giles Kingston, follow the captivating journey of young Ernest Bracebridge as he navigates the challenges and adventures of his school days. From forming lasting friendships to facing unexpected trials, Ernest's experiences will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what lies ahead for this determined and spirited protagonist.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Growth, Education
