Synopsis: In the enchanting town of Zodiac, where the stars come alive, two young siblings, Amos and Ann, embark on a whimsical adventure filled with magical rhymes. As they explore the town's hidden secrets and encounter peculiar characters, they uncover a mysterious connection between the stars and their own destinies. With each rhyme they discover, Amos and Ann's bond grows stronger, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and the power of imagination. 

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Friendship, Self-discovery
