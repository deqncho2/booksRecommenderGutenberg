Synopsis: In 'A Prince of Good Fellows' by Robert Barr, a charming and enigmatic young man named Jack is thrust into the bustling streets of London, where he quickly becomes the center of attention. With his wit, intelligence, and knack for getting into trouble, Jack captivates the hearts of both high society and the working class. As he navigates the city's social circles and encounters a cast of colorful characters, Jack's true identity and motives remain a mystery. With each twist and turn, the reader is left wondering: Who is this Prince of Good Fellows?

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Deception, Social Class
