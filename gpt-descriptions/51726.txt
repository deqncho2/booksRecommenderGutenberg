Synopsis: In "Wall of Crystal, Eye of Night" by Algis Budrys, a renowned scientist embarks on a perilous journey to unravel the mysteries of an enigmatic alien artifact that has appeared on Earth. As he delves deeper into its secrets, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between reality and illusion blurs. With time running out, he must confront his own fears and confront the ultimate truth hidden within the wall of crystal.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the nature of reality, the limits of human perception
