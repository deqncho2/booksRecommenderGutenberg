Synopsis: In 'St. George and St. Michael, Volume 1' by George MacDonald, two young men find themselves on opposite sides of a brewing conflict during the English Civil War. As they navigate the complexities of war, love, and loyalty, their paths intertwine with a cast of captivating characters, leading to unexpected alliances and heart-wrenching choices. Set against the backdrop of a divided nation, this enthralling tale explores the power of friendship, the clash of ideologies, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Ideological conflict, Resilience
