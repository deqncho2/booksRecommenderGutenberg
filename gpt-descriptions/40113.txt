Synopsis: 
In 'The Flags of the World: Their History, Blazonry, and Associations', F. Edward Hulme takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of flags. From the iconic flags of nations to the lesser-known flags of organizations and societies, Hulme delves into the rich history, symbolism, and stories behind each flag. With vivid descriptions and beautiful illustrations, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the flags that have shaped our world, revealing the hidden meanings and cultural significance behind these colorful emblems.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Reference

Themes: Symbolism, Identity, Cultural Heritage
