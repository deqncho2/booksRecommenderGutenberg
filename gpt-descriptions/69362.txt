Synopsis: In "Mendel's Principles of Heredity: A Defence," Gregor Mendel and William Bateson embark on a captivating journey to unravel the mysteries of inheritance. Through meticulous experiments and groundbreaking observations, they challenge the prevailing beliefs of their time and present a revolutionary theory that will forever change our understanding of genetics. As they navigate the complexities of pea plants and delve into the intricacies of hereditary traits, Mendel and Bateson's work takes them on a thrilling scientific adventure that will leave readers in awe of the power and beauty of nature's secrets.

Genres: Science, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Genetics, Heredity, Scientific Revolution
