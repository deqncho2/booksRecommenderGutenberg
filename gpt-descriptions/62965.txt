Synopsis: 
In "Eugenie, Empress of the French" by Clara Tschudi, readers are transported to the captivating world of Eugenie de Montijo, a woman who defied societal expectations and rose to become the Empress of France. Set against the backdrop of the opulent Second French Empire, this historical novel follows Eugenie's journey from a young Spanish noblewoman to the influential and enigmatic figure she became. With secrets, scandals, and political intrigue, the story delves into the complexities of power, love, and the sacrifices one must make to maintain their position in a tumultuous world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Biography

Themes: Power, Love, Sacrifice
