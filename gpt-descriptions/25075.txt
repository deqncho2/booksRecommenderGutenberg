Synopsis: In "A Letter from Major Robert Carmichael-Smyth to His Friend, the Author of 'The Clockmaker'," Major Carmichael-Smyth recounts a series of extraordinary events that unfolded during his time in a remote village. As he delves into the mysterious disappearance of a local resident, he uncovers a web of secrets and uncanny occurrences that challenge his understanding of reality. Through his captivating narrative, the major invites his friend, the author of 'The Clockmaker,' to explore the enigmatic world he has stumbled upon, leaving the reader eager to uncover the truth behind the village's dark secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Mystery, Perception of Reality, Secrets
