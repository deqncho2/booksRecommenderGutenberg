Synopsis: In 'Captain of the Crew' by Ralph Henry Barbour, a talented young athlete named Jack is faced with a difficult decision that could change the course of his life. As he navigates the challenges of school, friendships, and his passion for rowing, Jack must confront his own fears and insecurities. With the support of his teammates and the guidance of a wise coach, Jack learns valuable lessons about determination, loyalty, and the true meaning of leadership.

Genres: Young Adult, Sports, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Self-discovery
