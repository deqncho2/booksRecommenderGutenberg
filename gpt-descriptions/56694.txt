Synopsis: In 'Five Little Bush Girls' by Ryan, E. Lee, a seemingly ordinary family vacation takes a dark turn when five sisters stumble upon a hidden secret in the woods. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they uncover a web of lies, betrayal, and danger that threatens to tear their family apart. With each twist and turn, the girls must rely on their wit, courage, and sisterly bond to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: Family bonds, Secrets and betrayal, Coming of age
