Synopsis: 
In 'Midnight Jack, or The road-agent' by T.C. Harbaugh, a mysterious and enigmatic figure known as Midnight Jack emerges from the shadows of the Wild West. With his uncanny ability to outwit and outmaneuver his adversaries, he becomes a legendary road-agent feared by all. As the story unfolds, secrets from Jack's past are revealed, shedding light on his motivations and the darkness that drives him. With thrilling action, unexpected twists, and a captivating protagonist, this tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the lawless frontier.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Justice
