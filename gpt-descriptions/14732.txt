Synopsis: 
In 'The Adventures of Unc' Billy Possum', follow the mischievous and daring escapades of a clever possum named Unc' Billy. When Unc' Billy decides to venture beyond his cozy home in the Green Forest, he finds himself in a series of thrilling and unexpected situations. From encounters with cunning predators to unexpected friendships, Unc' Billy's adventures will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what will happen next.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
