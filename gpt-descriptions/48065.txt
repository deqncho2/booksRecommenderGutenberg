Synopsis: 

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the vibrant and scandalous world of 18th-century London. In this captivating volume, James Peller Malcolm takes us on a journey through the bustling streets, revealing the hidden secrets, peculiar customs, and extravagant lifestyles of the city's inhabitants. From the opulent balls and masquerades to the squalid slums and notorious prisons, Malcolm's anecdotes paint a vivid picture of a society teeming with both glamour and depravity. Delve into the fascinating stories of London's charities, the eccentricities of fashion, and the peculiar amusements that captivated the city's residents. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Social History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Society and Class, Morality and Decadence, Urban Life and Culture
