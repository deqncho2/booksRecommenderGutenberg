Synopsis: In the remote and mysterious village of Vezelay, a young artist named Richard inherits an ancient and enigmatic painting that holds a captivating secret. As he delves deeper into the painting's origins, Richard finds himself drawn into a world where reality and imagination intertwine, blurring the boundaries between the human and the supernatural. With each step he takes, Richard uncovers a hidden realm filled with ancient myths, mystical creatures, and a dark power that threatens to consume him. As he navigates through this surreal landscape, Richard must confront his own fears and desires, ultimately discovering the true nature of art and the transformative power it holds.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Art and creativity, the blurred line between reality and fantasy, the power of imagination, the search for identity and purpose
