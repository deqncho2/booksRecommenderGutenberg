Synopsis: In the untamed wilderness of early America, a young Native American warrior must navigate the treacherous waters of love, loyalty, and honor as his tribe faces the encroachment of European settlers. As tensions rise and old traditions clash with new ways of life, he finds himself torn between his duty to his people and his forbidden love for a young settler woman. With danger lurking at every turn, he must make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of his tribe and the future of their land.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Cultural clash, Love and sacrifice, Nature and wilderness, Identity and heritage
