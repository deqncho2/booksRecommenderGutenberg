Synopsis: In the small town of Pineville, a legendary creature known as the Wampus has been terrorizing the locals for generations. When a group of friends stumble upon evidence of its existence, they embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the myth. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they soon realize that the Wampus is not the only secret lurking in the shadows of Pineville. With danger lurking at every turn, the friends must confront their fears and unravel the dark secrets that have haunted their town for years.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Legends and folklore, Friendship, Overcoming fears
