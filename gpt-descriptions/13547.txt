Synopsis: 

In the picturesque village of Valpré, nestled along the rugged coast of Brittany, a mysterious tragedy unfolds. The lives of two families, the Delavals and the St. Pierres, become intricately entwined as secrets from the past resurface, threatening to shatter their idyllic existence. As the story unfolds, the reader is taken on a journey through love, betrayal, and redemption, all set against the backdrop of the breathtaking Valpré rocks. 

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Redemption, Resilience
