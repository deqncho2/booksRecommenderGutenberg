Synopsis: 

'Venice' by Dorothy Menpes takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting city of Venice. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, the book explores the hidden corners, bustling canals, and timeless beauty of this iconic Italian city. As the protagonist immerses themselves in the vibrant culture and rich history of Venice, they encounter intriguing characters and experience the city's unique charm. From gondola rides along the Grand Canal to exploring the labyrinthine streets, 'Venice' offers a mesmerizing glimpse into the heart and soul of this captivating city.

Genres: Travelogue, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: Beauty of Venice, Cultural exploration, Historical significance
