Synopsis: In the picturesque countryside of New England, a chance encounter between a successful businessman and a woman from his past sets off a chain of events that will challenge their beliefs, ignite long-buried desires, and force them to confront the complexities of love and loyalty. As the autumn leaves fall, secrets unravel, and lives are forever changed in this captivating tale of lost connections and second chances.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love, Redemption, Regret, Identity, Class Struggles
