Synopsis:
In 'The Stolen Brain; Or, A Wonderful Crime' by Carter, Nicholas (House name), a renowned neuroscientist wakes up one morning to find his most prized possession missing - his own brain. As he embarks on a thrilling journey to recover his stolen brain, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and scientific intrigue that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the limits of scientific advancement and the dark side of human ambition.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes:
Identity, Morality, Scientific Ethics
