Synopsis: In 'The Enemy' by Richard Wilson, a small town is plagued by a mysterious force that turns its residents against each other. As friendships crumble and trust fades away, a group of unlikely allies must uncover the truth behind this malevolent presence before it consumes them all. With suspenseful twists and heart-pounding moments, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Horror, Mystery

Themes: Betrayal, Survival, Paranoia
