Synopsis: In 'Watch—Work—Wait: Or, The Orphan's Victory' by Sarah A. Myers, a young orphan named Emily finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her new home. As she unravels the truth, Emily must navigate treacherous paths, face unexpected allies and enemies, and ultimately discover her own strength and resilience. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into Emily's world, eagerly anticipating the next revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perseverance, Family
