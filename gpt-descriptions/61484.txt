Synopsis: In 'Nicolette: a tale of old Provence', the reader is transported to the enchanting world of medieval Provence, where the spirited Nicolette, a young troubadour's daughter, embarks on a captivating journey filled with love, adventure, and the pursuit of her dreams. As she navigates the complexities of courtly life and the constraints imposed on women, Nicolette's indomitable spirit and unwavering determination shine through, leading her on a path of self-discovery and unexpected twists. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant and richly detailed Provencal landscape, this tale weaves together romance, mystery, and the power of music, captivating readers with its lyrical prose and unforgettable characters.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Gender Roles and Expectations, Pursuit of Dreams
