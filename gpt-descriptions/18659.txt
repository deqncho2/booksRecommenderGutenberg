Synopsis: In 'The Seventh Manchesters: July 1916 to March 1919', author S. J. Wilson takes readers on a gripping journey through the experiences of the Seventh Battalion of the Manchester Regiment during World War I. From the intense battles of the Somme to the harrowing conditions of the trenches, this historical account delves into the lives of the soldiers, their camaraderie, and the sacrifices they made for their country. As the battalion faces unimaginable challenges and confronts the horrors of war, the story unfolds with a mix of heart-wrenching moments and unexpected triumphs, leaving readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Military History

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Resilience, Loss
