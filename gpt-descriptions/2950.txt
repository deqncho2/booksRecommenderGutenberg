Synopsis: In 'The Midnight Queen' by Fleming, May Agnes, a young woman named Florence is thrust into a world of mystery and magic when she becomes entangled with a secretive society of sorcerers. As she navigates the dangerous realm of sorcery, Florence must uncover the truth about her own hidden powers and the dark forces that threaten to destroy everything she holds dear. With a captivating blend of romance, intrigue, and enchantment, 'The Midnight Queen' takes readers on a thrilling journey through a world where magic and danger collide.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Power of love, Identity and self-discovery, Good vs. evil
