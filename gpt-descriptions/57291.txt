Synopsis: 

In 'Keys to Fungi on Dung', Richardson and Watling take readers on a fascinating journey into the hidden world of fungi thriving on animal dung. This comprehensive guide provides a unique insight into the diverse and often overlooked species that inhabit this peculiar habitat. From the vibrant colors and intricate shapes to the vital ecological roles they play, the authors shed light on the captivating world of dung fungi. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Science

Themes: Biodiversity, Ecology, Microorganisms
