Synopsis: 
'As Time Glides On: The Months in Picture and Poem' takes readers on a captivating journey through the changing seasons, exploring the beauty and essence of each month through a combination of stunning illustrations and evocative poetry. From the vibrant colors of spring to the cozy warmth of winter, this book invites readers to immerse themselves in the ever-shifting tapestry of nature's rhythms. As the pages turn, readers will discover a delightful blend of imagery and words that capture the essence of each month, leaving them longing for the next chapter of the year.

Genres: Poetry, Art, Nature

Themes: Seasons, Time, Beauty of Nature
