Synopsis: In 'Slave Planet' by Laurence M. Janifer, a young man named Mark lands on a seemingly idyllic planet, only to discover a dark secret lurking beneath its surface. As he delves deeper into the planet's society, Mark becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between freedom and slavery blurs. With unexpected allies and formidable adversaries, Mark must navigate a treacherous world to uncover the truth and fight for his own liberation.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Oppression, Freedom, Identity
