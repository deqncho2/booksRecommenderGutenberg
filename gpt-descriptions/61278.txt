Synopsis: 
In 'Too Many Eggs' by Neville Kris, a small town is thrown into chaos when an unexpected event leads to an abundance of eggs. As the townspeople struggle to cope with the overwhelming surplus, they find themselves caught up in a series of hilarious and heartwarming misadventures. From egg-themed competitions to creative recipes, the community must come together to find a solution before the situation cracks them all. 

Genres: Comedy, Fiction, Humor

Themes: Community, Resourcefulness, Adaptation
