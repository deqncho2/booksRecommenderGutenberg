Synopsis: 
In 'Selected Poems of Oscar Wilde', readers are transported into the enchanting world of Oscar Wilde's poetic genius. This collection showcases the breadth and depth of Wilde's lyrical prowess, exploring themes of love, beauty, society, and the human condition. From poignant sonnets to witty and satirical verses, Wilde's words captivate and provoke thought, inviting readers to delve into the complexities of life and the power of language. With each poem, Wilde unveils a new facet of his literary brilliance, leaving readers spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Poetry, Classic Literature

Themes: Love, Beauty, Society, Human Condition
