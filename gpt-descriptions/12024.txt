Synopsis: In "Bred in the Bone; Or, Like Father, Like Son: A Novel," secrets, family legacies, and unexpected connections intertwine to create a gripping tale. The story follows the lives of two men, one a successful artist and the other a struggling writer, who are brought together by a mysterious inheritance. As they delve into their shared past, they uncover a web of deceit, love, and betrayal that spans generations. With each revelation, the characters are forced to confront their own identities and question the true nature of their relationships. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this captivating novel explores the complexities of family, the power of art, and the enduring bonds that tie us together.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Identity, Inheritance, Artistic Expression, Family Secrets
