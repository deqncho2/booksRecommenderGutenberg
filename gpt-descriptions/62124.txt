Synopsis: In "Sanitation of Mofussil Bazaars" by Disney, G. W., a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, bringing with him a peculiar contraption that promises to revolutionize the way the local bazaars are maintained. As the townspeople become increasingly intrigued by the stranger's invention, they soon discover that there is more to this device than meets the eye. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger, as they uncover the truth behind the sanitation of their beloved bazaars.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Innovation, Deception, Community
