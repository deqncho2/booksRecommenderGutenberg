Synopsis: In 'The Frame Up' by Davis, Richard Harding, a renowned artist finds himself caught in a web of deception and intrigue when he becomes the prime suspect in a high-profile art theft. As he races against time to clear his name, he uncovers a sinister plot that goes far beyond the stolen masterpiece. With unexpected twists and a captivating cast of characters, this thrilling mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Art, Deception, Identity
