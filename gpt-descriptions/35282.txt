Synopsis: 
'Fräulein Schmidt and Mr. Anstruther' by Von Arnim, Elizabeth is a captivating and poignant tale of an unexpected connection between two individuals from vastly different backgrounds. Set in the early 20th century, the story follows Fräulein Schmidt, a young German governess, and Mr. Anstruther, an English gentleman. Through a series of heartfelt letters, they embark on a journey of self-discovery, exploring themes of love, longing, and the complexities of human relationships. As their correspondence deepens, secrets are revealed, and their lives become intertwined in ways they never could have imagined.

Genres: 
Epistolary fiction, Romance, Historical fiction

Themes: 
Love and longing, Cross-cultural connections, Self-discovery
