Synopsis: In 'The Quest of Happy Hearts' by Kathleen Hay, a group of unlikely friends embarks on a whimsical adventure to find the key to true happiness. As they journey through enchanted lands and encounter mystical creatures, they discover that the secret lies within themselves. With each challenge they face, they learn valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the power of self-discovery. Will they be able to unlock the key to happiness before it's too late?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Friendship

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Friendship
