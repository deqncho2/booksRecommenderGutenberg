Synopsis: In 'Mr. Jervis, Vol. 2 (of 3)' by B. M. Croker, the captivating story continues as the enigmatic Mr. Jervis finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue. As his past begins to catch up with him, he must navigate through a world of deception and danger, where nothing is as it seems. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling volume will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Romance

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
