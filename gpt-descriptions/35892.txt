Synopsis: In 'Feats on the Fiord' by Harriet Martineau, a young woman named Hilda embarks on a journey to a remote village in Norway, where she encounters a series of unexpected challenges and adventures. As she navigates the treacherous landscapes and interacts with the locals, Hilda's courage and determination are put to the test. With each feat she accomplishes, Hilda's character is shaped and transformed, leading her to discover the true meaning of resilience and self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Self-Discovery
