Synopsis: In 'Commercialism and Journalism' by Holt, Hamilton, a seasoned journalist finds himself caught in a web of corporate interests and ethical dilemmas as he uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to shake the foundations of the media industry. As he navigates the treacherous waters of commercialism, he must decide whether to prioritize his career or uphold the principles of honest reporting. With high-stakes investigations, unexpected alliances, and a race against time, this gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of journalism and the price one must pay for the truth.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Commercialization of media, Ethics in journalism, Corruption, Truth and integrity
