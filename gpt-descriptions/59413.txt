Synopsis: In 'The Wooing of Wistaria' by Watanna, Onoto, a young American woman named Wistaria is swept away to Japan, where she finds herself caught between two suitors from different cultures. As she navigates the complexities of love, tradition, and societal expectations, Wistaria must make a choice that will shape her future and challenge the boundaries of her own identity.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Love and Desire, Cultural Clashes, Identity and Self-Discovery
