Synopsis: In 'The Sporting Dictionary, and Rural Repository, Volume 1', author William Taplin takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of sports and rural life. This comprehensive volume provides a wealth of information on various subjects related to the sports of the field, offering a fascinating glimpse into the practices, techniques, and traditions of the time. From hunting and fishing to horse racing and falconry, Taplin's work covers a wide range of topics, providing both practical advice and entertaining anecdotes. With its engaging narrative and extensive knowledge, this book is sure to captivate readers interested in the sporting pursuits of the past.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Reference

Themes: Rural life, Sportsmanship, Tradition
