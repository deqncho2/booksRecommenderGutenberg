Synopsis: 
In 'With a Vengeance' by J. B. Woodley, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and revenge when a series of mysterious murders rock the city. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel everything he thought he knew. With time running out and the body count rising, he must navigate a treacherous path of lies and betrayal to bring the killer to justice. Filled with unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, 'With a Vengeance' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Revenge, Deception, Justice
