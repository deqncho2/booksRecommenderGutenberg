Synopsis: 
In 'On the Yukon Trail: Radio-Phone Boys Series, #2' by Roy J. Snell, the thrilling adventures of Jack and Joe, the Radio-Phone Boys, continue as they embark on a dangerous journey through the treacherous Yukon Trail. With their trusty radio-phone, the boys find themselves facing perilous obstacles, mysterious strangers, and a race against time to uncover a hidden secret. As they navigate the frozen wilderness, their courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will they be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and solve the enigma that awaits them?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Exploration
