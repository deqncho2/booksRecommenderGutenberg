Synopsis:
In "Backlash" by Winston K. Marks, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he is hired to investigate a high-profile murder case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a shocking conspiracy that reaches the highest levels of power. With his own life on the line, the detective must navigate a treacherous landscape of corruption and betrayal to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes:
Corruption, Betrayal, Power
