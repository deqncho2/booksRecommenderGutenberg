Synopsis: 
In 'My Little Sister' by Elizabeth Robins, a seemingly ordinary family is torn apart when a mysterious secret about the youngest daughter is revealed. As the truth unfolds, the family is forced to confront their own fears and insecurities, leading to unexpected consequences that will forever change their lives. With a gripping narrative and complex characters, this suspenseful novel explores the depths of family bonds and the lengths we will go to protect the ones we love.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Family Drama

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Identity and Self-discovery, Family Dynamics
