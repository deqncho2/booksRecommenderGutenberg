Synopsis: In 'Serapis - Volume 01' by Georg Ebers, the reader is transported to ancient Egypt, where the young Egyptian priest, Serapion, finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and religious upheaval. As Serapion navigates the treacherous world of the Egyptian court, he becomes embroiled in a dangerous quest to uncover the truth behind a mysterious death, all while grappling with his own personal demons. With vivid descriptions of ancient Egyptian rituals, captivating characters, and a gripping plot, 'Serapis - Volume 01' takes readers on a thrilling journey through a fascinating era in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Power and Politics, Religion and Belief, Love and Betrayal
