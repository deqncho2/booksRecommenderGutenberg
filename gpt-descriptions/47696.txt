Synopsis: In "Barry Blake of the Flying Fortress" by Du Bois, Gaylord, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside Barry Blake, a young pilot who finds himself in the midst of World War II. As he joins the crew of the Flying Fortress, a powerful bomber aircraft, Barry must navigate the treacherous skies and face the challenges of war. With danger lurking at every turn, Barry's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the odds and emerge victorious?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Heroism, Sacrifice
