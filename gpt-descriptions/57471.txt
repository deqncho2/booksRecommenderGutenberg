Synopsis: 

In 'A Settler's 35 Years' Experience in Victoria, Australia: And how £6 8s. became £8,000', Edward Hulme takes readers on a captivating journey through his personal experiences as a settler in Victoria, Australia. From humble beginnings with a meager £6 8s., Hulme's story unfolds into an astonishing tale of transformation and success, as he reveals how his initial investment grew into an impressive £8,000.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration and settlement, Financial success and growth, Personal resilience and determination
