Synopsis: 
"The Laws of Euchre: As adopted by the Somerset Club of Boston, March 1, 1888" is a fascinating guidebook that delves into the intricacies of the popular card game, Euchre. Written by Dwight, James and Leeds, Herbert Corey, this comprehensive manual provides a detailed exploration of the rules and strategies of Euchre, offering valuable insights and tips for both beginners and experienced players alike. With its historical context and practical advice, this book is a must-read for anyone looking to master the art of Euchre.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gaming, Reference

Themes: Card games, Strategy, Skill development
