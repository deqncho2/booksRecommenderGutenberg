Synopsis: In "Hervey Willetts" by Percy Keese Fitzhugh, a young boy named Hervey finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a mysterious secret hidden within his family's ancestral home. As he delves deeper into the enigma, Hervey must navigate treacherous obstacles, solve perplexing riddles, and confront his own fears. With the help of newfound friends, he embarks on a quest that will test his courage and determination, leading him to uncover the truth behind his family's past and the power that lies within him.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family legacy, Self-discovery, Courage
