Synopsis: 

'Defenders of Democracy: Contributions from representative men and women of letters and other arts from our allies and our own country' is a captivating anthology edited by the Gift Book Committee of the Militia of Mercy. This collection brings together a diverse range of voices, showcasing the artistic and intellectual contributions of both allies and fellow countrymen. From thought-provoking essays to poignant poetry, this anthology explores the power of literature and the arts in promoting and defending democracy. 

Genres: Anthology, Non-fiction, Literature

Themes: Democracy, Artistic Expression, Patriotism
