Synopsis: 

'Over Periscope Pond: Letters from Two American Girls in Paris October 1916-January 1918' is a captivating collection of letters exchanged between Marjorie Crocker and Esther Sayles, two young American girls living in Paris during the tumultuous years of World War I. Through their heartfelt correspondence, the reader is transported to the vibrant streets of Paris, where Marjorie and Esther navigate the challenges of war, love, and self-discovery. As they immerse themselves in the rich culture and artistic scene of the city, their letters reveal a deep bond and a shared desire to make the most of their time in the City of Light. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Epistolary Novel, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Adventure, Love, Cultural Exploration, Resilience
