Synopsis: In 'The Eye of Dread' by Erskine, Payne, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious family curse that has haunted her ancestors for generations. As she delves deeper into the dark secrets of her lineage, Mary finds herself entangled in a web of forbidden love, supernatural occurrences, and a chilling sense of impending doom. With each revelation, the tension builds, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to discover the ultimate fate of Mary and her family.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family curses, Forbidden love, Supernatural elements, Fate and destiny
