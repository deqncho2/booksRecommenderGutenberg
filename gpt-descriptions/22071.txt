Synopsis: 
In "Marjorie Dean, College Sophomore" by Josephine Chase, we follow the captivating journey of Marjorie Dean as she enters her second year of college. Filled with new friendships, unexpected challenges, and a blossoming romance, Marjorie's sophomore year proves to be a transformative period in her life. As she navigates the complexities of academia, social dynamics, and personal growth, Marjorie must confront her own insecurities and discover the true meaning of loyalty and perseverance. With each twist and turn, readers will be captivated by Marjorie's determination to overcome obstacles and find her place in the world.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Romance, Drama

Themes: 
Identity, Friendship, Self-discovery
