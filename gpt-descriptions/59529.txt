Synopsis: In the final installment of the thrilling adventure series, 'All But Lost: A Novel. Vol. 3 of 3', the courageous protagonist finds himself facing his most dangerous mission yet. Set against the backdrop of the Napoleonic Wars, our hero must navigate treacherous waters, outwit cunning enemies, and overcome personal challenges to fulfill his duty and protect his loved ones. As the stakes rise and the tension mounts, readers will be captivated by the gripping twists and turns that will keep them on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival, Coming of Age
