Synopsis: In 'M. P.'s in Session: From Mr. Punch's Parliamentary Portrait Gallery', Harry Furniss takes readers on a satirical journey through the world of British politics. Through his witty and clever illustrations, Furniss introduces us to a colorful cast of politicians, each with their own quirks and flaws. From the pompous to the bumbling, the cunning to the clueless, these caricatures provide a humorous and insightful commentary on the state of parliamentary affairs. As we delve into the pages of this book, we witness the absurdity and absurdities of political life, leaving us both entertained and contemplative.

Genres: Satire, Political Cartoon, Humor

Themes: Politics, Satire, Social Commentary
