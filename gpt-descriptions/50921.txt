Synopsis: 
In '$1,000 a Plate' by McKenty, Jack, a renowned chef finds himself entangled in a high-stakes culinary competition that pushes the boundaries of taste, creativity, and morality. As he navigates through the cutthroat world of elite gastronomy, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that could either make or break his career. With each dish he creates, the tension rises, and the reader is left wondering if he will be able to maintain his integrity or succumb to the pressures of the culinary world.

Genres: 
Thriller, Mystery, Culinary Fiction

Themes: 
Ambition, Morality, Artistic Expression
