Synopsis: 
In 'Don, a Runaway Dog: His Many Adventures' by Richard Barnum, follow the thrilling journey of Don, a lovable and mischievous dog who escapes from his home and embarks on a series of extraordinary adventures. From dodging danger in the bustling city streets to finding unexpected friendship in the most unlikely places, Don's escapades will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As Don navigates through various challenges and encounters, he learns valuable lessons about loyalty, bravery, and the true meaning of home. Will Don ever find his way back or will he forever be a runaway dog?

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Homecoming
