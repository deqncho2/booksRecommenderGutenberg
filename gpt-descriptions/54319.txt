Synopsis: 

'Liverpool: A Sketch Book' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the vibrant city of Liverpool. Through the intricate and detailed sketches of Samuel J. M. Brown, the book showcases the city's iconic landmarks, bustling streets, and hidden corners, capturing the essence and spirit of Liverpool in a unique and artistic way. From the majestic architecture of the Liver Building to the lively atmosphere of the Albert Dock, each sketch tells a story and invites readers to explore the rich history and culture of this remarkable city. 

Genres: Art, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: City life, Architecture, Cultural heritage
