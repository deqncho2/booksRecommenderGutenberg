Synopsis: In 'The Millionaire Mystery' by Fergus Hume, a wealthy businessman is found dead in his opulent mansion, and the circumstances surrounding his death are shrouded in mystery. As the investigation unfolds, a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances emerges, leading the reader on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of high society. With each twist and turn, the truth becomes more elusive, and the stakes higher than ever before. Will the detective be able to unravel the millionaire's secrets before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Wealth and power, Deception and betrayal, Justice and morality
