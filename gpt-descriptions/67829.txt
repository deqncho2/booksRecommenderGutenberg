Synopsis: In 'A Floating City, and The Blockade Runners' by Jules Verne, readers are taken on a thrilling journey aboard a luxurious transatlantic steamship, where a diverse group of passengers find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected events. As the ship navigates treacherous waters and encounters various challenges, the passengers must confront their own desires, secrets, and ambitions. Meanwhile, in 'The Blockade Runners,' the story shifts to the high-stakes world of smuggling during the American Civil War, as a daring captain and his crew risk their lives to transport vital supplies past enemy lines. With gripping suspense and vivid descriptions, Verne masterfully weaves together these two tales of adventure and intrigue.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Maritime Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Human Nature, Morality, Survival
