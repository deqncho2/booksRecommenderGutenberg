Synopsis:
'The Orlando Innamorato' by Boiardo, Matteo Maria is an epic romance that follows the adventures of the brave knight Orlando as he embarks on a quest to win the heart of his beloved Angelica. Along the way, Orlando encounters a multitude of fantastical creatures, engages in thrilling battles, and faces numerous challenges that test his courage and loyalty. As the story unfolds, the reader is transported to a world filled with chivalry, magic, and unrequited love, where the fate of kingdoms and the destiny of heroes hang in the balance.

Genres:
Romance, Epic, Adventure

Themes:
Love and Desire, Heroism, Fate and Destiny
