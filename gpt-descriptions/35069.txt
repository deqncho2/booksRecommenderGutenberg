Synopsis: In 'The Sign of Flame' by Werner, E., a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds a mysterious power. As she delves deeper into its origins, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and hidden agendas. With time running out, she must decipher the sign of flame before it falls into the wrong hands, risking the fate of humanity itself.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Power of ancient artifacts, Uncovering hidden truths, Race against time
