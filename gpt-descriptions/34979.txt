Synopsis: 

In 'Twentieth Century Socialism: What It Is Not; What It Is: How It May Come', Kelly, Edmond takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the complexities of socialism in the modern era. Through a combination of historical analysis and forward-thinking ideas, the author challenges conventional notions of socialism and offers a fresh perspective on its potential future. With a captivating narrative and insightful arguments, this book explores the misconceptions surrounding socialism and presents a compelling vision for its realization.

Genres: Political theory, Non-fiction, Social commentary

Themes: Socialism, Political ideology, Economic systems, Social justice
