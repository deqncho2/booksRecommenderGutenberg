Synopsis: In 'The Three Eyes' by Maurice Leblanc, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is called upon to solve a perplexing case involving a secret society and a hidden treasure. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he discovers a world filled with hidden identities, unexpected alliances, and a race against time to uncover the truth. With each twist and turn, the detective must rely on his wit, intuition, and the help of an enigmatic woman to unravel the secrets that lie behind the three enigmatic eyes. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Treasure hunting, Intrigue and suspense
