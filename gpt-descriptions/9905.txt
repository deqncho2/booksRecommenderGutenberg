Synopsis: In 'A Deal in Wheat and Other Stories of the New and Old West', Frank Norris takes readers on a captivating journey through the landscapes of the American West. From the bustling wheat fields of California to the rugged frontier towns, these stories delve into the lives of farmers, ranchers, and pioneers, exploring the triumphs and struggles they face in their pursuit of success and survival. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Norris paints a vivid picture of the harsh realities and moral dilemmas that shape the lives of those living in the West.

Genres: Western fiction, Historical fiction, Realism

Themes: The pursuit of wealth, the impact of industrialization, the struggle for survival, the clash between nature and civilization, the moral complexities of human nature
