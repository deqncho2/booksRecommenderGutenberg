Synopsis: 
In 'The Wild North Land: The Story of a Winter Journey with Dogs across Northern North America', Sir William Francis Butler takes readers on an exhilarating adventure through the untamed wilderness of Northern North America. Join him as he embarks on a daring winter journey, braving treacherous landscapes, harsh weather conditions, and encounters with indigenous peoples. With a team of loyal sled dogs by his side, Butler's gripping narrative will transport you to a world of breathtaking beauty and heart-pounding danger. 

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural encounters
