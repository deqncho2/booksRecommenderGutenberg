Synopsis: 

'Ride A Cock-Horse To Banbury Cross & A Farmer Went Trotting Upon His Grey Mare: R. Caldecott's Picture Books' takes readers on a delightful journey through two classic nursery rhymes brought to life by the renowned illustrator Randolph Caldecott. In the first story, children will join a merry group of characters as they embark on a whimsical adventure to Banbury Cross. The second story follows a farmer and his trusty grey mare as they encounter various obstacles along their trotting journey. With Caldecott's enchanting illustrations, these timeless tales are sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book

Themes: Adventure, Imagination, Traditional Nursery Rhymes
