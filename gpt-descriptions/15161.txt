Synopsis: In 'Great Fortunes, and How They Were Made', James Dabney McCabe takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating stories of individuals who achieved immense wealth. From rags to riches tales to innovative business strategies, this book unveils the secrets behind the creation of great fortunes. Discover the extraordinary lives and extraordinary circumstances that led to the accumulation of vast wealth, leaving readers inspired and in awe of the human potential for success.

Genres: Biography, Business, History

Themes: Success, Entrepreneurship, Wealth Creation
