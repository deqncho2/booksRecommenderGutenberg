Synopsis: 
In 'Some One Like You' by James W. Foley, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, capturing the attention and curiosity of the locals. As the townspeople become entangled in the stranger's enigmatic past, secrets are unveiled, relationships are tested, and lives are forever changed. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Secrets, Small-town dynamics
