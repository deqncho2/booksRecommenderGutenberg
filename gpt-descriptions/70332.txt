Synopsis: 
In "The declaration and confession of Robert Watt," the reader is taken on a gripping journey through the mind of the notorious Robert Watt, just hours before his execution for high treason. As Watt reflects on his life and the choices that led him to this fateful moment, he unveils a shocking truth that will leave readers questioning their own beliefs and the nature of justice. With each word, Watt's confession becomes a haunting testament to the complexities of loyalty, betrayal, and the lengths one will go to fight for what they believe in.

Genres: 
Historical fiction, Memoir, Crime

Themes: 
Loyalty, Betrayal, Justice, Rebellion
