Synopsis: In this captivating and whimsical tale, a hen takes center stage as she recounts her extraordinary adventures to her family of chickens. From daring escapades to unexpected encounters, the hen's narrative is filled with surprises, humor, and a touch of mystery. As she shares her unique perspective on the world, readers will be drawn into a delightful journey that explores the wonders of life through the eyes of a courageous and insightful hen.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Courage, Perspective, Family bonds
