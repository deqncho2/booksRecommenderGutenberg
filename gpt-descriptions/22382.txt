Synopsis: 
The Iliad, an epic poem by Homer, takes readers on a thrilling journey through the final year of the Trojan War. Filled with larger-than-life heroes, intense battles, and divine interventions, this ancient masterpiece explores the consequences of pride, honor, and the devastating impact of war on both individuals and societies. As the Greeks and Trojans clash on the battlefield, the poem delves into the complex emotions, personal sacrifices, and moral dilemmas faced by its diverse cast of characters. Through vivid storytelling and poetic language, The Iliad captivates readers with its timeless exploration of the human condition and the eternal struggle between fate and free will.

Genres: Epic poetry, Historical fiction, Mythology

Themes: Pride, Honor, War, Fate vs. Free will, Heroism, Mortality
