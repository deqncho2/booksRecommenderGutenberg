Synopsis:
'With Wellington in Spain: A Story of the Peninsula' follows the adventures of a young British officer, Charles, who finds himself thrust into the heart of the Peninsular War. As he joins the ranks of the famous Duke of Wellington's army, Charles experiences the brutal realities of war, the camaraderie among soldiers, and the complexities of the political landscape. From the intense battles to the personal sacrifices made by individuals, the story takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous events of the war. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this historical novel immerses readers in the harrowing and heroic world of the Peninsular War.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: War and its impact on individuals and society, Loyalty and camaraderie, Courage and sacrifice
