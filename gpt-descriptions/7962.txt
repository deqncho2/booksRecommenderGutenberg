Synopsis: 
"Over the Top" is a gripping memoir written by Arthur Guy Empey, an American soldier who volunteered to fight in World War I. Through vivid and harrowing accounts, Empey takes readers on a journey through the trenches, sharing his experiences of the brutal realities of war, the camaraderie among soldiers, and the indomitable spirit that kept them going. Alongside the memoir, the book also includes "Tommy's Dictionary of the Trenches," a unique and humorous collection of slang and jargon used by soldiers during the war. 

Genres: Memoir, War, Historical

Themes: War experiences, Camaraderie, Survival
