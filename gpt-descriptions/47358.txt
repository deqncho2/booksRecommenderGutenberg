Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts Afoot in France; or, With the Red Cross Corps at the Marne', a group of adventurous Boy Scouts find themselves in the midst of World War I, serving with the Red Cross Corps at the Marne. As they navigate the dangers and challenges of war-torn France, they discover the true meaning of bravery, sacrifice, and the power of friendship. Will these young scouts be able to make a difference in the face of adversity?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Friendship, Coming of Age
