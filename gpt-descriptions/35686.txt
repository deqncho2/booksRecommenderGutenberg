Synopsis: In "Mississippi Outlaws and the Detectives: Don Pedro and the Detectives; Poisoner and the Detectives," Pinkerton, Allan takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dangerous world of outlaws and the relentless pursuit of justice. This gripping collection of stories follows the adventures of Don Pedro and the detectives as they navigate the treacherous landscapes of Mississippi, facing cunning criminals and solving perplexing mysteries. From high-stakes heists to deadly poisonings, each tale is filled with suspense, unexpected twists, and the unwavering determination of the detectives to bring the outlaws to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Justice, Pursuit of Truth, Morality
