Synopsis: 
'Motley, and other poems' by Walter De la Mare is a captivating collection of poems that takes readers on a journey through the realms of imagination and the mysteries of the human experience. From enchanting landscapes to haunting encounters, these poems explore the depths of emotions and the beauty of the natural world. With each verse, De la Mare weaves a tapestry of words that will leave readers spellbound and longing for more.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Imagination, Nature, Human Experience
