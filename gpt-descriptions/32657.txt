Synopsis: In the near future, a mysterious phenomenon known as "spillthrough" begins to occur, causing people to suddenly vanish without a trace. As the world grapples with this unexplained occurrence, a group of individuals find themselves drawn together in their search for answers. As they delve deeper into the secrets of spillthrough, they uncover a shocking truth that will challenge their understanding of reality and the nature of existence itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Identity, Existentialism
