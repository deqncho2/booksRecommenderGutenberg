Synopsis: In 'Peeps at Many Lands: Norway', Mockler-Ferryman takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Norway. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, the author introduces us to the fascinating traditions, folklore, and natural wonders of this Nordic country. From the majestic fjords to the vibrant cities, readers will be immersed in the beauty and charm of Norway, while also gaining insights into its history and way of life. This delightful travelogue is sure to leave readers yearning to explore the wonders of Norway for themselves.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Cultural heritage, Natural beauty, Exploration
