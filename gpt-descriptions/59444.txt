Synopsis: In "William the Conqueror and the Rule of the Normans," F. M. Stenton delves into the captivating story of William the Conqueror and his Norman dynasty. From his audacious invasion of England to his establishment of a new ruling class, this book explores the triumphs and challenges faced by William and his descendants. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Stenton brings to life the epic tale of one of history's most influential rulers.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Power and conquest, Legacy and succession
