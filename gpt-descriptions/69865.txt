Synopsis: In "North by Night" by Peter Burchard, we follow the gripping journey of a young girl named Liz who finds herself caught in the dangerous world of the Underground Railroad. Faced with the harsh realities of slavery and the constant threat of discovery, Liz must navigate a treacherous path to freedom, relying on her courage, resourcefulness, and the help of unexpected allies. As she embarks on this perilous adventure, Liz discovers the true meaning of bravery and the power of hope.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Freedom, Courage, Resilience, Friendship
