Synopsis: 
In "How to know the ferns: A guide to the names, haunts and habitats of our common ferns," Frances Theodora Parsons takes readers on an enchanting journey through the world of ferns. With vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, Parsons introduces us to the diverse species of ferns, revealing their unique names, haunts, and habitats. From the delicate Maidenhair Fern to the majestic Royal Fern, this guide provides a comprehensive understanding of these fascinating plants. Whether you are a seasoned botanist or a curious nature lover, this book will captivate your imagination and deepen your appreciation for the beauty and intricacy of ferns.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Botany
Themes: Plant identification, Nature exploration, Environmental appreciation
