Synopsis: In "A Short History of Newnham College, Cambridge," Alice Gardner takes readers on a captivating journey through the establishment and growth of one of the most prestigious women's colleges in Cambridge. From its humble beginnings as a small house to its transformation into a thriving institution, Gardner delves into the lives of the pioneering women who fought for their right to education and paved the way for future generations. Through anecdotes, historical accounts, and personal stories, the author paints a vivid picture of the challenges faced and triumphs achieved by the women of Newnham College, offering a fascinating glimpse into the evolution of women's education in the late 19th and early 20th centuries.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Women's education, Gender equality, Social progress
