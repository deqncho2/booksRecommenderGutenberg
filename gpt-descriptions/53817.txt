Synopsis: In 'The Goddess of Reason: A Drama in Five Acts' by Mary Johnston, a captivating tale unfolds as the protagonist, a young woman named Celeste, finds herself caught in the midst of the French Revolution. As chaos and violence engulf the nation, Celeste becomes entangled in a dangerous web of political intrigue, love, and betrayal. With her life hanging in the balance, she must navigate the treacherous waters of revolution while grappling with her own personal desires and the pursuit of justice. Will Celeste be able to survive the tumultuous times and find her own path amidst the chaos?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Revolution, Love, Betrayal, Justice
