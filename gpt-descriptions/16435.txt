Synopsis: 
The Atlantic Book of Modern Plays is a captivating collection of thought-provoking and emotionally charged plays that will transport readers into the depths of human experiences. From the gripping tale of a family torn apart by secrets to the haunting exploration of love and loss, each play in this anthology offers a unique and powerful narrative that will leave readers captivated and eager for more. With its diverse range of stories and compelling characters, this collection is sure to ignite the imagination and spark conversations about the complexities of the human condition.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Comedy, Absurdist

Themes: Family dynamics, Love and loss, Identity, Secrets and revelations, Social commentary
