Synopsis: 
'Going Some' by Rex Beach is a thrilling and suspenseful novel that follows the adventures of a young man named Larry, who finds himself caught up in a dangerous game of high-stakes gambling and deceit. As Larry navigates the treacherous world of underground gambling dens and shady characters, he must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to survive. With unexpected twists and turns, 'Going Some' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the game.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Deception, Survival
