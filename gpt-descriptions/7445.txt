Synopsis: In "Ars Recte Vivendi; Being Essays Contributed to 'The Easy Chair'," George William Curtis takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking essays. From exploring the complexities of human nature to delving into the nuances of society, Curtis offers insightful observations and reflections that will leave readers pondering the intricacies of life. With his eloquent prose and keen eye for detail, he invites readers to join him in contemplating the art of living well. 

Genres: Essay, Non-fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Human nature, Society, Reflection, Life
