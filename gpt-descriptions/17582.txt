Synopsis: 
'Round-about Rambles in Lands of Fact and Fancy' takes readers on a captivating journey through various lands, both real and imagined. Frank R. Stockton weaves together a collection of enchanting tales that transport readers to different corners of the world, from the bustling streets of New York City to the mystical realms of fantasy. With each turn of the page, readers will encounter a delightful mix of adventure, humor, and unexpected twists. From encounters with eccentric characters to encounters with mythical creatures, this book promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning pages to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Imagination, Unexpected Encounters
