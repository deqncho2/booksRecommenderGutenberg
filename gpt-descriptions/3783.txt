Synopsis: In 'Mother' by Maksim Gorky, we follow the journey of a strong-willed and resilient woman named Pelageya Nilovna, who finds herself caught in the midst of a turbulent and oppressive society. As she navigates through poverty, injustice, and the struggles of motherhood, Pelageya's determination to protect her family and fight for a better future becomes a powerful force. This gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature, the strength of maternal love, and the indomitable spirit of the human soul.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Social Realism

Themes: Motherhood, Poverty, Social Injustice, Resilience, Determination
