Synopsis: In 'The Bomb-Makers', we are introduced to Theodore Drost, an enigmatic enemy alien residing in London, and his daughter Ella. As the story unfolds, we delve into the craft and cunning of Theodore, a master bomb-maker, and the secrets he holds. With certain revelations about Ella, the plot takes unexpected turns, keeping readers on the edge of their seats. This thrilling tale explores the dangerous world of espionage and the lengths one will go to protect their loved ones.

Genres: Thriller, Espionage, Mystery

Themes: Secrets and deception, Family bonds, Loyalty and betrayal
