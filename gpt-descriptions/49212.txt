Synopsis: In 'The Warfare of Science', Andrew Dickson White takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of science and its tumultuous relationship with religion and superstition. From the ancient world to the modern era, White explores the clashes, controversies, and triumphs that have shaped the progress of scientific knowledge. Through engaging anecdotes and meticulous research, he unveils the fascinating stories of scientists who challenged prevailing beliefs and faced persecution for their groundbreaking discoveries. This thought-provoking work sheds light on the ongoing battle between reason and dogma, offering a compelling exploration of the clash between science and religion throughout history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Conflict between science and religion, Evolution of scientific knowledge, Persecution of scientists, Progress and innovation
