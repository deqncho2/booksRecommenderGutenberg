Synopsis: In 'The Death of Captain Wells', a small town is shaken by the mysterious death of a beloved local hero. As the community mourns, a determined detective begins to uncover a web of secrets and lies that lead to shocking revelations. With each clue, the detective gets closer to the truth, but also finds themselves in increasing danger. This gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the truth behind Captain Wells' death.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Justice
