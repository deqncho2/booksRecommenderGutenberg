Synopsis: In 'The Fishguard Invasion by the French in 1797' by Margaret Ellen James, a small coastal town in Wales becomes the unexpected battleground for a daring invasion by the French. As the townspeople are thrust into a world of chaos and uncertainty, they must navigate the treacherous waters of war, betrayal, and survival. With lives hanging in the balance, the fate of Fishguard rests on the shoulders of a few courageous individuals who must rise above their fears and fight for their homeland.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Resilience, Sacrifice
