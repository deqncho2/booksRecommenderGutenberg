Synopsis: In 'The Grey Friars in Oxford', a mysterious discovery in the heart of Oxford's historic Greyfriars College sets off a chain of events that will challenge the boundaries of faith, history, and the human mind. As a group of scholars and archaeologists delve into the secrets hidden within the college's ancient walls, they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and the supernatural. With each revelation, the line between reality and myth becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Faith and belief, History and its impact on the present, the power of secrets and hidden knowledge
