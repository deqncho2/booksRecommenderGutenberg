Synopsis: In 'A Quarter-Back's Pluck: A Story of College Football' by Chadwick, Lester, we follow the journey of a talented young quarterback named Tom, who faces numerous challenges and obstacles on his path to success. From the intense rivalries on the field to the personal struggles he must overcome, Tom's determination and resilience are put to the test. As he navigates the pressures of college football, friendships, and love, readers will be captivated by the gripping narrative that explores the sacrifices and triumphs of a young athlete striving for greatness.

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Perseverance, Teamwork, Identity, Ambition
