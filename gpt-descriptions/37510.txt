Synopsis: 
'By Trench and Trail in Song and Story' by Mackay, Angus is a captivating collection of tales and poems that takes readers on a journey through the trenches and trails of war and adventure. From the battlefields of World War I to the untamed wilderness, this book weaves together stories of courage, love, and the indomitable human spirit. With vivid imagery and heartfelt emotions, Mackay's words transport readers to different landscapes and eras, immersing them in the triumphs and tribulations of the characters. Whether it's a soldier's struggle for survival or a traveler's quest for self-discovery, each narrative in this collection is a testament to the power of storytelling and the resilience of the human soul.

Genres: Poetry, Short Stories, Historical Fiction
Themes: War, Adventure, Love, Courage, Resilience
