Synopsis: In 'The Bible, Douay-Rheims, Book 15: 1 Esdras: The Challoner Revision', readers are transported to a time of political intrigue and spiritual awakening. As the Persian king, Darius, seeks to solidify his reign, a young scribe named Esdras finds himself caught in a web of power struggles and divine revelations. With the fate of a nation hanging in the balance, Esdras embarks on a journey that will test his faith, challenge his understanding of justice, and uncover hidden truths that could change everything. 

Genres: Historical fiction, religious literature, epic

Themes: Faith and doubt, political power and corruption, divine intervention, the search for truth
