Synopsis: In 'Miss Million's Maid: A Romance of Love and Fortune' by Berta Ruck, a young woman named Joan is unexpectedly thrust into a world of wealth and luxury when she becomes the personal maid to the enigmatic Miss Million. As Joan navigates the complexities of her new position, she finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and unexpected romance. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating tale explores the transformative power of love and the pursuit of happiness amidst the trappings of fortune.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love, Wealth, Identity, Social class
