Synopsis: In 'Sound of Terror' by Don Berry, a small town is plagued by a mysterious and chilling sound that haunts its residents, driving them to the brink of madness. As the eerie noise intensifies, a group of unlikely allies must uncover the truth behind the sound and confront their deepest fears before it consumes them all. With heart-pounding suspense and unexpected twists, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Horror

Themes: Fear, Madness, Community
