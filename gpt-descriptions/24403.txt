Synopsis: In "The Young Lord, and Other Tales; to which is added Victorine Durocher," Mary Martha Sherwood and Newton Crosland present a collection of captivating tales that will transport readers to different worlds and times. From the story of a young lord who embarks on a perilous journey to the intriguing tale of Victorine Durocher, these narratives are filled with mystery, adventure, and unexpected twists. Each story delves into the complexities of human nature, exploring themes of love, sacrifice, redemption, and the pursuit of truth. 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love, Sacrifice, Redemption, Truth
