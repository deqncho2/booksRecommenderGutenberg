Synopsis: In 'Archangel: The American War with Russia', John Cudahy takes readers on a thrilling journey into an alternate history where the United States finds itself embroiled in a devastating conflict with Russia. As tensions escalate and the world teeters on the brink of destruction, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate treacherous political landscapes, engage in heart-pounding battles, and uncover hidden secrets that could change the course of the war. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this book will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Alternate history, military fiction, political thriller

Themes: War, diplomacy, loyalty, sacrifice
