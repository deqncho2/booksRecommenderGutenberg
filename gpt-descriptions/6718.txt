Synopsis: In 'Cap'n Dan's Daughter' by Joseph Crosby Lincoln, a young woman named Ruth inherits her father's fishing boat and takes on the challenge of running the business in a male-dominated industry. As she navigates the treacherous waters of the fishing trade, Ruth faces numerous obstacles and must prove herself capable of leading the crew and preserving her father's legacy. Along the way, she encounters unexpected romance and learns valuable lessons about determination, resilience, and the power of family bonds.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Gender roles, Family legacy, Resilience, Love and relationships
