Synopsis: In 'The Count of the Saxon Shore; or The Villa in Vectis', we are transported to the waning days of Roman rule in Britain. The story follows the life of a young Roman nobleman, Lucius, who finds himself torn between his loyalty to Rome and his growing fascination with the mysterious Saxon invaders. As the Romans prepare to abandon Britain, Lucius becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the clash of cultures. With the fate of his family and the future of Britain hanging in the balance, Lucius must navigate treacherous waters and make difficult choices that will shape the course of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Cultural Clash, Political Intrigue
