Synopsis: In 'Dick Kent at Half-Way House' by M. M. Oblinger, a young boy named Dick Kent finds himself in a mysterious and enchanting world when he stumbles upon an old, abandoned house. As he explores the house, he uncovers secrets and encounters strange beings that challenge his perception of reality. With each discovery, Dick is drawn deeper into a captivating adventure that will test his courage and determination. Will he be able to unravel the mysteries of Half-Way House and find his way back home?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Courage
