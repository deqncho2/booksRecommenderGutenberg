Synopsis: In the gripping novel 'Scandal', secrets and lies threaten to unravel the lives of a group of influential individuals in the heart of a bustling city. As a scandalous event rocks their world, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, power struggles, and forbidden desires. With each chapter, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the scandal that could shatter reputations and change lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Betrayal, Power, Morality, Redemption
