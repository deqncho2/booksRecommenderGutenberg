Synopsis: In 'A Project for Flying: In Earnest at Last!' by Hardley, Robert, a brilliant yet eccentric inventor embarks on a daring quest to achieve the impossible: human flight. Filled with humor, adventure, and unexpected twists, this captivating story follows the inventor's relentless pursuit of his dream, leading to a series of extraordinary events that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Comedy

Themes: Ambition, Innovation, Perseverance
