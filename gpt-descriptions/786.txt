Synopsis: In the industrial town of Coketown, where facts and rationality reign supreme, a cast of intriguing characters navigate the harsh realities of life. Thomas Gradgrind, a strict and utilitarian schoolmaster, raises his children on the principles of logic and facts, while the enigmatic circus performer, Sleary, brings a touch of whimsy to the town. As the lives of these characters intertwine, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of humanity is explored in this captivating tale of love, betrayal, and redemption.

Genres: Fiction, Social Criticism, Bildungsroman

Themes: Utilitarianism, Education, Industrialization, Class Struggle, Redemption
