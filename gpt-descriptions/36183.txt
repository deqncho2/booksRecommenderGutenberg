Synopsis: In this captivating biography, Josephine Pollard presents the remarkable life of George Washington, the first President of the United States, using only words of one syllable. From his humble beginnings to his pivotal role in the American Revolution and his leadership in shaping a new nation, readers will be enthralled by the extraordinary journey of this iconic figure. With simplicity and clarity, Pollard brings to life the triumphs, challenges, and sacrifices of George Washington, offering a unique perspective on his legacy and the enduring impact he had on American history.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Leadership, Patriotism, American Revolution, Legacy
