Synopsis: In 'The Stoker's Catechism' by Connor, W. J., a young stoker named Thomas finds himself thrust into a world of dark secrets and hidden truths when he uncovers a mysterious book in the depths of the ship he works on. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic text, Thomas becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and deception, where the line between reality and fantasy blurs. With his life on the line, Thomas must navigate treacherous waters and confront the shadows that lurk within the ship, all while trying to unravel the truth behind the book's origins and its connection to his own past.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Power and deception, Reality vs. fantasy
