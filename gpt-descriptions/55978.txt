Synopsis: 
'A History of Troop A Cavalry, Connecticut National Guard and Its Service in the Great War as Co. D, 102d Machine Gun Battalion' is a captivating historical account that delves into the experiences of Troop A Cavalry, a group of brave soldiers from Connecticut who served in World War I as part of the 102d Machine Gun Battalion. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the book explores the trials and triumphs of these soldiers as they navigate the horrors of war, forge unbreakable bonds, and face the ultimate test of their courage and loyalty. From the trenches of Europe to the heart-wrenching moments on the battlefield, this gripping narrative sheds light on the untold stories of these unsung heroes and their significant contributions to the war effort.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Resilience
