Synopsis: In the picturesque town of Sorrento, a young and mysterious girl named Agnes finds herself caught between the worlds of privilege and poverty. As she navigates the complexities of her identity and the challenges of her circumstances, Agnes must confront the secrets and betrayals that threaten to unravel her life. With themes of love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of truth, 'Agnes of Sorrento' is a captivating tale that explores the power of resilience and the enduring strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Identity, Social class, Secrets and betrayal, Resilience
