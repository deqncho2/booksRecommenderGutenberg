Synopsis: In 'Loyola and the Educational System of the Jesuits', Thomas Hughes delves into the fascinating life of Ignatius of Loyola, the founder of the Society of Jesus, and explores the revolutionary educational system he established. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Hughes uncovers the challenges and triumphs faced by Loyola as he shaped the minds of countless students and transformed the landscape of education. From Loyola's early struggles to his profound impact on the world, this book offers a captivating journey into the life of a visionary leader and the enduring legacy of the Jesuit educational system.

Genres: Biography, History, Education

Themes: Leadership, Education, Religious Institutions
