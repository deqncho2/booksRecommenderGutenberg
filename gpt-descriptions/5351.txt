Synopsis: 
In 'If I Were King' by Justin H. McCarthy, a young and ambitious poet finds himself unexpectedly thrust into a position of power and authority. As he navigates the treacherous world of politics and court intrigue, he must use his wit and charm to outmaneuver his enemies and protect his newfound kingdom. With his poetic sensibilities and unconventional approach to ruling, he brings about a series of unexpected changes that challenge the status quo and captivate the hearts of his subjects. Will his reign be marked by triumph or tragedy?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Romance

Themes: Power and Leadership, Love and Sacrifice, Artistic Expression
