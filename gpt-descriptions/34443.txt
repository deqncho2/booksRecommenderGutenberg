Synopsis: In "The Camp Fire Girls at the Seashore; Or, Bessie King's Happiness" by Jane L. Stewart, Bessie King embarks on a thrilling adventure with her Camp Fire Girls group at the picturesque seashore. As they explore the sandy beaches, encounter mysterious strangers, and uncover hidden secrets, Bessie's life takes an unexpected turn. With the ocean as their backdrop, the Camp Fire Girls learn valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the true meaning of happiness.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
