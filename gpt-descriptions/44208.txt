Synopsis: In 'The Hive' by Comfort, Will Levington, a mysterious and exclusive social club known as The Hive holds the power to grant its members their deepest desires. As a young woman named Cassie is initiated into this secretive world, she becomes entangled in a web of seduction, manipulation, and dangerous secrets. With each step deeper into The Hive, Cassie must navigate the blurred lines between pleasure and power, trust and betrayal, as she discovers the true nature of this enigmatic society.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Desire, Power, Deception, Identity
