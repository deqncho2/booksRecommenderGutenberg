Synopsis: 

In 'Faithful Margaret: A Novel', we are introduced to Margaret, a young woman who finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception. Set in the Victorian era, this gripping tale takes us on a journey through the dark underbelly of society, where appearances can be deceiving and trust is a luxury few can afford. As Margaret navigates through a world filled with treachery and unexpected alliances, she must confront her own inner demons and make choices that will determine her fate. With its intricate plot twists and complex characters, 'Faithful Margaret' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity, Morality
