Synopsis: In 'The Adventures of Seumas Beg; The Rocky Road to Dublin', follow the captivating journey of Seumas Beg, a young Irishman, as he embarks on a series of thrilling and unexpected adventures. From encounters with eccentric characters to dangerous escapades across the Irish countryside, Seumas's quest takes him on a rollercoaster ride filled with humor, danger, and self-discovery. As he navigates through the challenges and surprises that come his way, Seumas learns valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the true meaning of home.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Belonging, Resilience
