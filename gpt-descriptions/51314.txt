Synopsis: 
In "In Bad Company, and other stories" by Rolf Boldrewood, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through a collection of captivating tales. From the treacherous Australian outback to the bustling streets of London, this book presents a diverse range of stories that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Whether it's a tale of revenge, a daring escape, or a mysterious encounter, each story is filled with suspense, unexpected twists, and unforgettable characters. Prepare to be transported to different worlds and experience the thrill of adventure in this enthralling collection.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Revenge, Survival, Redemption
