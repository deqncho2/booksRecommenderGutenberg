Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Second, or Queen's Royal Regiment of Foot', Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of one of the oldest regiments in the British Army. From its establishment in 1661 to the year 1837, Cannon delves into the regiment's rich history, chronicling its triumphs, challenges, and the brave men who served within its ranks. With meticulous attention to detail and a compelling narrative style, this historical record offers a fascinating glimpse into the regiment's role in various conflicts and campaigns, shedding light on the experiences and sacrifices of those who fought for their country.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Military service, Regimental history, British Army, Warfare, Patriotism
