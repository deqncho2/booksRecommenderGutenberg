Synopsis: 
In "Heortology: A history of the Christian festivals from their origin to the present day," Kellner takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the rich tapestry of Christian celebrations. From the early origins of these festivals to their evolution and significance in modern times, this book delves into the fascinating history behind the traditions that have shaped the Christian faith. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Kellner uncovers the hidden stories, cultural influences, and religious developments that have shaped these festivals throughout the centuries. From Christmas and Easter to lesser-known celebrations, this comprehensive exploration offers a fresh perspective on the Christian calendar, shedding light on the deep-rooted traditions that continue to inspire and unite believers worldwide.

Genres: History, Religion, Cultural Studies

Themes: Christian traditions, Religious history, Cultural significance
