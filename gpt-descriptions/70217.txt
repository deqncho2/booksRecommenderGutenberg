Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Bruggil, a young woman named Elara finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she becomes the unexpected bride of the enigmatic Lord Bruggil. As she navigates the treacherous halls of his ancient castle, Elara discovers dark secrets and hidden powers that threaten to unravel her world. With her life hanging in the balance, she must uncover the truth behind Bruggil's intentions and find the strength within herself to face the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Power and deception, Self-discovery
