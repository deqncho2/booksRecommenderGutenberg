Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Snowball Lamb' by Bailey, Arthur Scott, a young lamb named Snowball embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him far from his cozy farm. Along the way, he encounters a cast of colorful characters and faces numerous challenges that test his courage and determination. As Snowball navigates through unknown territories, he learns valuable lessons about friendship, loyalty, and the power of self-belief. Will Snowball find his way back home, or will he discover a new place to call his own?

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
