Synopsis: In 'Double Standard' by Alfred Coppel, a renowned journalist finds himself entangled in a web of international intrigue when he stumbles upon a shocking secret that threatens to shake the foundations of global politics. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he must navigate a dangerous world of espionage, betrayal, and hidden agendas, where nothing is as it seems. With his life on the line, he must decide how far he is willing to go to expose the double standard that lies at the heart of power.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Espionage

Themes: Political corruption, Truth and deception
