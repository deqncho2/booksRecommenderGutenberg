Synopsis: In 'Conquest Over Time' by Michael Shaara, a brilliant scientist invents a groundbreaking technology that allows him to travel through time. As he embarks on a thrilling journey through different eras, he encounters historical figures, witnesses pivotal moments, and faces unexpected challenges. With each leap, he must grapple with the consequences of altering the past and the ethical dilemmas that arise. This captivating tale of time travel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the nature of destiny and the power of human choices.

Genres: Science Fiction, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Ethics, Historical events, Human agency
