Synopsis:
In "Founding Father" by Jesse F. Bone, a young historian stumbles upon a hidden diary that reveals a shocking secret about one of America's most revered founding fathers. As he delves deeper into the mysterious past, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of conspiracy and betrayal. With each revelation, the young historian's life is put at risk, forcing him to question everything he thought he knew about history and the true nature of power.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes:
Secrets and hidden truths, the manipulation of history, the pursuit of power
