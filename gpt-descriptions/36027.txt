Synopsis: In 'Silent Struggles', Ann S. Stephens takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of three women who are bound by secrets and haunted by their pasts. As their paths intertwine, they must confront their inner demons and find the strength to overcome the challenges that threaten to consume them. With each page, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the silent struggles these women face.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Redemption and Forgiveness, Female Empowerment
