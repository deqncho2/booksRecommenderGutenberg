Synopsis: In "Old Spookses' Pass, Malcolm's Katie, and other poems," Isabella Valancy Crawford takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of poems that explore the beauty and complexity of life. From tales of love and loss to reflections on nature and the human spirit, each poem offers a unique perspective and evokes a range of emotions. With vivid imagery and lyrical language, Crawford's words transport readers to different worlds and invite them to ponder the mysteries of existence.

Genres: Poetry, Romanticism, Canadian Literature

Themes: Love, Nature, Spirituality, Identity, Transcendence
