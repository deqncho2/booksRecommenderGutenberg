Synopsis: In "A Journalist's Note-Book" by Frank Frankfort Moore, a seasoned journalist embarks on a thrilling journey through the world of news reporting, uncovering hidden truths and exposing the dark underbelly of society. From scandalous affairs to political corruption, he delves deep into the lives of the powerful and influential, risking his own safety to bring the truth to light. As he navigates the treacherous waters of journalism, he must confront his own moral compass and make difficult choices that could change the course of his career forever.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Journalism

Themes: Truth and deception, Power and corruption
