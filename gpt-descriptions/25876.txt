Synopsis: In the small Scottish town of Barbie, a seemingly idyllic community is disrupted when a new family moves into the house with the green shutters. As the story unfolds, the dark secrets and hidden desires of the townspeople are gradually revealed, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of human nature and the consequences of societal expectations.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Gothic fiction, Tragedy

Themes: Hypocrisy, societal pressure, the destructive power of secrets, the human capacity for evil
