Synopsis: In 'A Vision of Venus; Or, A Midsummer-Night's Nightmare' by Pleon, Harry, a young artist named Oliver finds himself captivated by a mysterious woman who appears in his dreams. As he delves deeper into the realm of his subconscious, Oliver becomes entangled in a web of love, desire, and surreal encounters. With each passing night, the line between reality and fantasy blurs, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and forbidden passion.

Genres: Romance, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Dreams and reality, Love and desire, Identity and self-discovery
