Synopsis: In 'The Camp Fire Girls' Careers' by Margaret Vandercook, a group of adventurous and determined young women embark on a journey to pursue their dreams and forge their own paths in the world. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene landscapes of the countryside, they face challenges, make new friends, and discover their true passions. As they navigate through various careers and unexpected twists of fate, the Camp Fire Girls prove that with courage, resilience, and the support of their sisterhood, they can overcome any obstacle and achieve their goals.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Empowerment, Self-discovery, Female camaraderie
