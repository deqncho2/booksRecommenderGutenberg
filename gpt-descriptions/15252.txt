Synopsis: 
'Victorian Short Stories: Stories of Successful Marriages' is a captivating collection of tales set in the Victorian era, where love and matrimony take center stage. Each story delves into the lives of different couples, exploring the complexities and triumphs of their relationships. From unexpected encounters to societal pressures, these narratives offer a glimpse into the challenges faced by couples in a time of strict social norms. With richly developed characters and intricate plotlines, this anthology paints a vivid picture of the joys and struggles that come with finding and maintaining a successful marriage.

Genres: 
Romance, Historical Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: 
Love, Marriage, Social Expectations, Gender Roles
