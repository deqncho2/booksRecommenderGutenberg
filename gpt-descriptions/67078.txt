Synopsis: In 'The Lady from Long Acre' by Victor Bridges, a mysterious woman arrives in the bustling streets of London's Long Acre district, capturing the attention of all who encounter her. As her true identity remains shrouded in secrecy, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, drawing the reader into a thrilling tale of suspense and unexpected twists. With each page, the enigmatic lady's past slowly unravels, revealing a captivating story that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Secrets
