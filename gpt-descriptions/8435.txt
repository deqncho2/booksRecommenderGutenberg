Synopsis: 

In 'The Sturdy Oak: A composite Novel of American Politics by fourteen American authors', readers are taken on a captivating journey through the intricate world of American politics. Set against the backdrop of a tumultuous election season, this composite novel weaves together the stories of various characters, each representing a different facet of the political landscape. From ambitious politicians and cunning strategists to passionate activists and disillusioned voters, the narrative delves into the personal and professional lives of these individuals, revealing the sacrifices, scandals, and triumphs that shape their destinies. As the stakes rise and tensions escalate, the reader becomes engrossed in a web of power struggles, moral dilemmas, and unexpected alliances. 

Genres: Political fiction, Drama, Contemporary fiction

Themes: Power and corruption, Idealism and compromise, Loyalty and betrayal, Public perception and private lives
