Synopsis: 

In "Trial of the major war criminals before the International Military Tribunal, Nuremberg, 14 November 1945-1 October 1946, volume 17," readers are transported to the historic courtroom where the most notorious figures of the Nazi regime face justice for their heinous crimes during World War II. This gripping volume delves into the intense legal battles, shocking revelations, and emotional testimonies that unfold as the prosecution seeks to hold these major war criminals accountable for their actions. As the trial progresses, the reader becomes immersed in the complex web of evidence, witness accounts, and legal arguments, witnessing the pursuit of justice on an unprecedented scale.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Legal drama, World War II history

Themes: Justice and accountability, War crimes, Human rights, Historical memory
