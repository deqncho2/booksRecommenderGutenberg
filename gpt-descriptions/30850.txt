Synopsis: 

'Philippine Mats: Philippine Craftsman Reprint Series No. 1' takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich cultural heritage of the Philippines through the art of mat weaving. This beautifully illustrated book showcases the intricate craftsmanship and traditional techniques employed by Filipino artisans in creating these exquisite mats. From the vibrant colors to the intricate patterns, each mat tells a unique story of the region it originates from, reflecting the diverse cultural tapestry of the Philippines. 

Genres: Art, Cultural Heritage, Non-fiction

Themes: Filipino craftsmanship, Cultural preservation, Traditional arts and crafts
