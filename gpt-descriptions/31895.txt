Synopsis:
In "In and Out of Rebel Prisons" by A. (Alonzo) Cooper, the reader is taken on a gripping journey through the author's personal experiences as a prisoner during the American Civil War. From the moment of capture to the harrowing conditions of confinement, Cooper's vivid and detailed account provides a unique perspective on the challenges faced by prisoners of war. With each page, the reader is immersed in the struggle for survival, the bonds formed between fellow prisoners, and the relentless pursuit of freedom. Will Cooper be able to escape the clutches of the enemy and find his way back home?

Genres:
Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War

Themes:
Survival, Camaraderie, Freedom
