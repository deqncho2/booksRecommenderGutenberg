Synopsis: In 'Frank Reade and His Steam Horse' by Luis Senarens, readers are introduced to the brilliant inventor Frank Reade and his incredible creation, the steam horse. This thrilling adventure takes us on a journey through uncharted territories, as Frank and his steam horse encounter dangerous obstacles, mysterious villains, and unexpected allies. With its fast-paced action and imaginative technology, this story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to see what daring escapades await Frank and his remarkable invention.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Steampunk

Themes: Invention and innovation, Exploration and discovery
