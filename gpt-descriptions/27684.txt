Synopsis: 
In 'Anthony Lyveden' by Yates, Dornford, a mysterious and enigmatic young man named Anthony Lyveden suddenly appears in the lives of the wealthy and influential Chaytor family. As he becomes entangled in their affairs, secrets from the past are unearthed, leading to unexpected consequences and a thrilling journey of self-discovery. With his unconventional charm and hidden agenda, Anthony Lyveden captivates those around him, leaving readers questioning his true intentions and the impact he will have on the Chaytors' lives.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Identity, Deception, Redemption
