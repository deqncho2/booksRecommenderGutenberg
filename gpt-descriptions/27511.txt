Synopsis: In 'The Free Range' by Sullivan, Francis William, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small, secluded town, bringing with him a series of inexplicable events that disrupt the peaceful lives of its inhabitants. As the townspeople grapple with the stranger's presence, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and desires, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of reality itself.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Consequences of Actions
