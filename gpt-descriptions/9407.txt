Synopsis: In "The Little Colonel," a spirited young girl named Lloyd Sherman finds herself in the midst of a family feud that spans generations. As she navigates the challenges of growing up, she discovers the power of friendship, the importance of forgiveness, and the beauty of embracing diversity. Set against the backdrop of the post-Civil War era, this heartwarming tale takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and the triumph of love and unity.

Genres: Historical fiction, coming-of-age, family drama

Themes: Friendship, forgiveness, family, unity, diversity
