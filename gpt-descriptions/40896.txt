Synopsis: In 'Elementary Color' by Bradley Milton, a renowned artist mysteriously disappears, leaving behind a trail of cryptic clues that lead his estranged daughter on a thrilling journey through the world of art, secrets, and hidden truths. As she delves deeper into her father's enigmatic past, she uncovers a web of deception and betrayal that threatens to shatter her perception of reality. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game where art becomes a weapon and colors hold the key to unlocking the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Art

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Artistic expression
