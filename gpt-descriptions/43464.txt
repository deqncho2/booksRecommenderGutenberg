Synopsis: In "Lay Help the Church's Present Need," William Baird, the Vicar of Homerton, Middlesex, presents a thought-provoking paper that delves into the urgent needs of the church. Through his insightful analysis, Baird explores the challenges faced by the church and proposes innovative solutions that could potentially revolutionize the way the church operates. This paper, read at St. Mary's Schools in West Brompton, offers a fresh perspective on the role of laypeople in addressing the church's current needs. 

Genres: Religious, Non-fiction, Academic

Themes: Church reform, Lay involvement, Religious leadership
