Synopsis: In the first volume of "Kalevala: The Epic Poem of Finland," we are transported to the ancient land of Finland, where mythical heroes and mystical creatures dwell. This epic poem takes us on a captivating journey through the creation of the world, the adventures of mighty warriors, and the power struggles between gods and mortals. From the enchanting melodies of the magical kantele to the battles fought with valor and cunning, the first volume of Kalevala immerses readers in a rich tapestry of Finnish folklore and mythology.

Genres: Epic poetry, Mythology, Folklore

Themes: Creation, Heroism, Power dynamics, Nature, Music
