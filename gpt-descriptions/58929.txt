Synopsis: In 'A Strange World: A Novel. Volume 2 (of 3)' by Braddon, M. E., the captivating story continues as the protagonist finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception. As she delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding her, she uncovers shocking revelations that challenge her perception of reality. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate through a strange world filled with unexpected twists and turns, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Deception, Identity, Perception
