Synopsis: In "Where the Pavement Ends" by John Russell, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances that leave the community in a state of fear and uncertainty. As the townspeople struggle to uncover the truth behind these unsettling events, they are forced to confront their own darkest secrets and face the consequences of their actions. With each twist and turn, the story delves deeper into the darkness that lurks beneath the surface of this seemingly idyllic town, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking climax.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Small-town dynamics, Fear and paranoia
