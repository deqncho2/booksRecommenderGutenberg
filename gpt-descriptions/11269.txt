Synopsis: In 'Virgie's Inheritance' by Sheldon, Georgie, Mrs., a young woman named Virgie unexpectedly inherits a vast fortune from a mysterious benefactor. As she navigates her newfound wealth, Virgie becomes entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and danger. With her life on the line, she must uncover the truth behind her inheritance and confront the dark forces that threaten to destroy everything she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Betrayal, Identity
