Synopsis: The Legislative Manual, of the State of Colorado is a comprehensive guide that delves into the rich history of Colorado, providing insights into the annals of the legislature, customs, precedents, and forms. It also includes the rules of parliamentary practice and the constitutions of both the United States and the State of Colorado. Additionally, it offers a chronological table of American history, lists and tables for reference, biographies, and more. This manual serves as an invaluable resource for anyone interested in understanding the legislative and historical aspects of Colorado.

Genres: Reference, History, Politics

Themes: Legislative history, Parliamentary practice, Constitutional law, American history
