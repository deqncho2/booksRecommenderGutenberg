Synopsis: In "The Ranch Girls at Boarding School" by Margaret Vandercook, four spirited sisters from a ranch in the American West find themselves in a completely new and unfamiliar environment when they are sent to a prestigious boarding school in the East. As they navigate the challenges of fitting in, making friends, and adapting to the strict rules and expectations of their new school, the girls discover hidden talents, form unexpected alliances, and learn valuable life lessons. With their unique personalities and determination, the Ranch Girls bring their own brand of excitement and adventure to the boarding school, leaving an indelible mark on everyone they encounter.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Resilience, Adaptation
