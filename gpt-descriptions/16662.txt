Synopsis: In 'Bad Hugh' by Mary Jane Holmes, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger named Hugh arrives. With his dark past and enigmatic charm, Hugh captivates the townspeople, but soon secrets begin to unravel, leading to unexpected consequences. As the truth unfolds, the residents find themselves questioning their own morals and the true nature of forgiveness. This gripping tale of love, betrayal, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Secrets and lies, Redemption, Morality
