Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts of the Field Hospital' by Robert Shaler, a group of young boys find themselves thrust into the chaos of war as they join a field hospital during World War I. As they navigate the horrors of the battlefield and the challenges of providing medical aid, they must confront their own fears and discover the true meaning of courage. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and unwavering determination to save lives and make a difference. Will they be able to rise above the brutality of war and prove themselves as true heroes?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Coming of Age
