Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Thomas Wentworth Higginson' by Thomas Wentworth Higginson is a comprehensive collection of the works of this influential American author and abolitionist. From essays and biographies to poetry and historical accounts, this index provides a glimpse into Higginson's diverse literary contributions. Whether exploring the lives of notable figures or delving into the complexities of social issues, Higginson's writings offer a captivating perspective on the events and ideas that shaped his era.

Genres: Essays, Biographies, Poetry, History

Themes: Social justice, Civil rights, American history, Literary criticism
