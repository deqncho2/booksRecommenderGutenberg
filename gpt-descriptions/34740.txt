Synopsis: 
In "Wayside and Woodland Trees: A pocket guide to the British sylva," Step, Edward takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of British trees. From the towering oak to the delicate birch, this pocket guide unveils the secrets and wonders of each tree species, providing fascinating insights into their characteristics, folklore, and historical significance. With vivid descriptions and beautiful illustrations, Step, Edward invites readers to explore the hidden treasures of the British sylva, igniting a sense of wonder and appreciation for the natural world that surrounds us.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Reference

Themes: Nature appreciation, Botany, British flora
