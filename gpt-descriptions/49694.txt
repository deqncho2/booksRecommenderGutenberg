Synopsis: In 'The Duke in the Suburbs' by Wallace Edgar, a mysterious and enigmatic Duke finds himself in an unexpected situation when he is forced to take up residence in a suburban neighborhood. As he navigates the unfamiliar territory, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With his wit and charm, the Duke must unravel the truth behind the seemingly ordinary facade of suburban life, all while trying to maintain his own hidden agenda.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Identity, Deception, Social class
