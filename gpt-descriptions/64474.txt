Synopsis: In "Planet in Reverse" by Guth, Henry, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything we know about the universe. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden truth that could have catastrophic consequences for humanity. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of secrets and betrayal to save the world from impending doom.

Genres: Science fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific exploration, Consequences of knowledge, Betrayal
