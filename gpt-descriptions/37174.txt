Synopsis: In the final installment of "In a Glass Darkly," Le Fanu takes readers on a chilling journey through a series of supernatural encounters. From a haunted house to a mysterious portrait, each story delves deeper into the dark corners of the human psyche, leaving readers questioning the boundaries between reality and the supernatural. As the characters grapple with their own fears and desires, they are forced to confront the terrifying consequences of their actions. With each tale more unsettling than the last, "In a Glass Darkly, v. 3/3" will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, mystery

Themes: The supernatural, psychological exploration, morality
