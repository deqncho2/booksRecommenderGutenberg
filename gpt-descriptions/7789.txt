Synopsis: In "Memoirs of My Dead Life" by Moore, George, a young man named Maurice finds himself trapped in a monotonous existence, yearning for something more. When he stumbles upon a mysterious book that promises to reveal the secrets of the afterlife, Maurice embarks on a journey that blurs the lines between reality and fantasy. As he delves deeper into the pages, he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and the supernatural, ultimately questioning the very nature of life and death.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, psychological thriller

Themes: Existentialism, the search for meaning, the boundaries of reality, love and desire
