Synopsis: 
'A Manual of the Art of Bookbinding' is a comprehensive guide that unveils the secrets of the ancient craft of bookbinding. Written by James B. Nicholson, this book provides detailed instructions on various aspects of bookbinding, including forwarding, gilding, finishing, and the art of marbling book-edges and paper. Whether you are a novice or an experienced book lover, this manual will captivate your imagination and inspire you to explore the artistry and techniques behind creating beautiful and enduring books.

Genres: Non-fiction, Craft, Art

Themes: Bookbinding, Artistry, Craftsmanship, Preservation
