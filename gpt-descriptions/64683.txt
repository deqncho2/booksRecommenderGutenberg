Synopsis: In 'The Kingdom of Promise and Prophecy' by Whiteside, Robertson L., a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the key to a long-lost kingdom. As she delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the artifact, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, prophecies, and power struggles that span across time. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate treacherous alliances and decipher cryptic clues to unlock the truth about the kingdom's past and its potential future.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Destiny, Power, Betrayal, Redemption
