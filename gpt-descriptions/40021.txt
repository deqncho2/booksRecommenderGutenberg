Synopsis: In 'The History of Chivalry; Or, Knighthood and Its Times, Volume 1', Charles Mills takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of knights and chivalry. From the legendary tales of King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table to the noble codes of conduct and the epic battles fought on horseback, this volume delves into the rich history and traditions of knighthood. Mills explores the origins of chivalry, the rise of the knightly class, and the impact of chivalric ideals on medieval society. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the knights' heroic deeds, their romantic pursuits, and the complex social structures they inhabited. 

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Chivalry, Knighthood, Medieval Society, Codes of Conduct, Heroism
