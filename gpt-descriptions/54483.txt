Synopsis:
In 'The Rapin' by H. De Vere Stacpoole, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Mary becomes entangled in a thrilling adventure that takes her from the tranquil English countryside to the vibrant streets of Paris. With each step, she uncovers shocking revelations and encounters unexpected allies and adversaries. As the truth unravels, Mary must summon her courage and wit to navigate a treacherous path and protect those she holds dear.

Genres:
Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Family secrets, Courage, Betrayal, Love
