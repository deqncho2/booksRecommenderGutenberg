Synopsis: In 'The Mannion Court-Martial' by Randall Garrett, a decorated military officer finds himself facing a court-martial for a crime he claims he did not commit. As the trial unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of justice is put to the ultimate test. With gripping courtroom drama and unexpected twists, this thrilling novel explores the lengths one man will go to prove his innocence and the price he may have to pay. 

Genres: Mystery, Legal Thriller, Military Fiction

Themes: Justice, Honor, Betrayal, Truth
