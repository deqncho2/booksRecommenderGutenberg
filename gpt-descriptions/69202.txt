Synopsis: In 'The Cost of Wings, and Other Stories' by Richard Dehan, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking tales. From the haunting secrets of a forgotten mansion to the unexpected consequences of a forbidden love affair, each story explores the complexities of human emotions and the choices we make. With vivid storytelling and unexpected twists, this book delves into the depths of the human psyche, leaving readers questioning the true cost of their own desires.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love and loss, Secrets and consequences, Human nature and choices
