Synopsis: In 'Cato, the creeper; or, The demon of Dead-Man's Forest' by Frederick H. Dewey, a small town is plagued by a mysterious and malevolent presence lurking in the depths of Dead-Man's Forest. As the townspeople become increasingly terrified, a group of unlikely heroes must band together to uncover the truth behind the demon and put an end to its reign of terror. With suspenseful twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Fear, Courage, Supernatural
