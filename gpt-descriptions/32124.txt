Synopsis: In 'To Choke an Ocean' by Jesse F. Bone, a small coastal town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances that leave the community in a state of fear and suspicion. As the residents grapple with their own secrets and hidden desires, a young journalist becomes determined to uncover the truth behind the unsettling events. With each revelation, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the depths of human nature.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and deception, the power of fear, the thin line between reality and the supernatural
