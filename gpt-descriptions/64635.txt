Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's First Job; Or, At the Foot of the Ladder' by Standish, Burt L., we follow the adventures of Frank Merriwell as he embarks on his first job after leaving college. Frank finds himself in a challenging position, facing various obstacles and unexpected situations that test his character and determination. As he navigates the complexities of the working world, Frank must rely on his intelligence, athleticism, and moral compass to overcome the hurdles that come his way. With a mix of thrilling encounters, unexpected friendships, and personal growth, this story takes readers on a captivating journey through the early stages of Frank Merriwell's career.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Sports

Themes: Perseverance, Integrity, Friendship, Ambition
