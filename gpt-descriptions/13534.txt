Synopsis: In 'Past and Present', Carlyle takes readers on a captivating journey through time, intertwining the lives of individuals from different eras. From the struggles of medieval serfs to the challenges faced by the emerging industrial society, Carlyle explores the impact of history on the present and the potential for change. Through vivid storytelling and thought-provoking analysis, this work delves into the complexities of human progress, societal transformation, and the eternal quest for justice and equality.

Genres: Historical fiction, Social commentary, Philosophical literature

Themes: History and its influence, Social inequality, Progress and change, Justice and equality
