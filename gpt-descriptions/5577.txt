Synopsis: In 'A Word, Only a Word', a chance encounter between two strangers leads to a whirlwind romance that is both passionate and forbidden. As their lives become entangled, they must navigate societal expectations, personal sacrifices, and the power of love. With secrets lurking in the shadows, this captivating tale explores the complexities of relationships and the lengths one will go to protect their heart.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, societal expectations, sacrifice, secrets
