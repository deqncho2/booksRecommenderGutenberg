Synopsis:
'Funny Stories Told by the Soldiers: Pranks, Jokes and Laughable Affairs of Our Boys and Their Allies in the Great War' takes readers on a hilarious journey through the lighter side of war. Carleton B. Case presents a collection of uproarious anecdotes and humorous tales shared by soldiers during the Great War. From clever pranks to witty jokes, this book offers a unique perspective on the camaraderie and resilience of soldiers in the face of adversity. With each page, readers will find themselves laughing out loud at the absurd and comical situations that unfolded amidst the chaos of war.

Genres:
Humor, Military History, Anthology

Themes:
Camaraderie, Resilience, Humor in Adversity
