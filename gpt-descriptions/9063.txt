Synopsis: 
'The Penance of Magdalena and Other Tales of the California Missions' is a captivating collection of short stories that takes readers on a journey through the intriguing and often mysterious world of the California Missions. From the haunting tale of a young woman seeking redemption to the enigmatic secrets hidden within the mission walls, each story offers a unique glimpse into the lives of those who lived and worked in these historic sites. As the characters navigate love, loss, and the complexities of faith, their stories intertwine to create a rich tapestry of human experiences that will leave readers both enthralled and moved.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Short Stories

Themes: Redemption, Faith, Love, Secrets, Human Experience
