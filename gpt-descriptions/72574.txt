Synopsis: In "A Moment of Madness, and Other Stories," Florence Marryat takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing tales. From a mysterious encounter on a train to a haunting ghost story set in the Scottish Highlands, each story presents a unique and gripping narrative. With unexpected twists and turns, Marryat's vivid storytelling keeps readers on the edge of their seats, exploring the depths of human emotions and the complexities of the human psyche. This volume is the first of three, promising an enthralling reading experience that will leave readers eagerly anticipating the next installment.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Intrigue, Psychological exploration
