Synopsis: In 'The Gilded Chair: A Novel' by Melville Davisson Post, a young lawyer named Randolph Mason finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving a mysterious chair made entirely of gold. As he delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the chair, Mason uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that threaten to unravel his own life. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Mason must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth behind the gilded chair.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Legal Fiction

Themes: Greed, Power, Deception, Justice
