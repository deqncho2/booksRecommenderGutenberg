Synopsis: 
In "Ella, a little schoolgirl of the sixties," readers are transported back to the vibrant era of the 1960s through the eyes of Ella, a curious and spirited young girl. As Ella navigates the challenges and joys of school, friendships, and family, she discovers the power of love, resilience, and the importance of standing up for what is right. Set against the backdrop of significant historical events, this heartwarming tale captures the essence of a transformative decade and the timeless lessons it holds for both children and adults.

Genres: Children's literature, Historical fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Family, Resilience, Social change, Identity
