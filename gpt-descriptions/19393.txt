Synopsis: 
'Plays of Near & Far' by Lord Dunsany is a captivating collection of short plays that transport readers to both familiar and fantastical realms. From the enchanting forests of ancient Greece to the bustling streets of modern-day London, these plays explore the depths of human nature, the power of imagination, and the interplay between reality and fantasy. Each play presents a unique and thought-provoking story, filled with vivid characters and unexpected twists. Whether it's a tale of love and sacrifice or a journey through the realms of dreams, 'Plays of Near & Far' promises to captivate readers with its imaginative storytelling and richly crafted worlds.

Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Human nature, Imagination, Reality vs. Fantasy
