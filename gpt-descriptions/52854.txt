Synopsis: In 'Knights in Armour' by Edward S. Woods, a mysterious artifact is discovered, leading a group of unlikely heroes on a thrilling adventure through time and space. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter formidable enemies, they must uncover the truth behind the artifact's power and its connection to an ancient order of knights. With danger lurking at every turn, the fate of the world hangs in the balance.

Genres: Action, Adventure, Science Fiction

Themes: Time travel, Heroism, Mystery
