Synopsis: In a future world where population control is enforced through a strict one-in, one-out policy, a man finds himself faced with a heart-wrenching decision that challenges the very essence of humanity. As he grapples with the consequences of his choice, he is forced to confront the true meaning of life and the value of individuality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction

Themes: Population Control, Ethics, Individuality
