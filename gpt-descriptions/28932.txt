Synopsis: 
In 'Eskimo Folk-Tales', embark on a captivating journey through the rich oral tradition of the Eskimo people. This collection of enchanting tales takes readers deep into the frozen landscapes of the Arctic, where mythical creatures, brave hunters, and cunning tricksters come to life. From epic adventures to heartwarming fables, these stories offer a glimpse into the cultural heritage and spiritual beliefs of the Eskimo community. Prepare to be transported to a world where ice and snow hold secrets, and where the power of storytelling weaves a tapestry of wonder and wisdom.

Genres: 
Folklore, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: 
Cultural heritage, Spirituality, Wisdom
