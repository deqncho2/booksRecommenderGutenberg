Synopsis: 
'A Peep Behind the Scenes' by Mrs. O. F. Walton takes readers on a captivating journey into the hidden world behind the curtains of a traveling circus. Through the eyes of a young girl named Rosalie, we are introduced to the enchanting yet mysterious life of the performers, witnessing the struggles, secrets, and unexpected friendships that unfold behind the scenes. As Rosalie navigates the challenges of her own life, she discovers the power of faith, love, and the importance of staying true to oneself. This heartwarming tale will leave readers eager to uncover the hidden truths that lie beneath the surface of the circus world.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Christian Fiction, Drama

Themes: 
Faith, Friendship, Identity, Redemption
