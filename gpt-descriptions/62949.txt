Synopsis: In 'Good Night (Buenas Noches)' by Eleanor Gates, a young woman finds herself caught between two worlds as she navigates the complexities of love, family, and cultural identity. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant and diverse community, this captivating story explores the power of self-discovery and the bonds that transcend language and borders.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Romance, Multicultural

Themes: Identity, Love, Family, Cultural diversity
