Synopsis: 
"Noted Speeches of Abraham Lincoln, Including the Lincoln-Douglas Debate" is a captivating collection of powerful speeches delivered by the iconic American president, Abraham Lincoln. From his early political career to his presidency, this book showcases Lincoln's eloquence and profound insights on various issues that shaped the nation. The highlight of the collection is the intense and historic Lincoln-Douglas Debate, where Lincoln's oratory skills and political acumen are on full display. Through these speeches, readers will gain a deeper understanding of Lincoln's leadership, his commitment to equality, and his unwavering dedication to preserving the Union.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Science

Themes: Leadership, Equality, National Unity
