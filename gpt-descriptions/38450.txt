Synopsis: In "The Six River Motor Boat Boys on the St. Lawrence; Or, The Lost Channel" by Harry Gordon, a group of adventurous boys embark on a thrilling journey along the majestic St. Lawrence River. As they navigate through treacherous waters, encounter mysterious islands, and face unexpected challenges, the boys find themselves caught up in a captivating quest to uncover the secrets of a lost channel. With danger lurking at every turn, their courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will they be able to solve the riddle of the lost channel and emerge victorious from their daring expedition?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Perseverance
