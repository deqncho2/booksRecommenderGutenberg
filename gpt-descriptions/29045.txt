Synopsis: 

'From the St. Lawrence to the Yser with the 1st Canadian brigade' takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of the 1st Canadian Brigade during World War I. Written by Frederic C. Curry, this captivating account follows the brigade's courageous soldiers as they navigate the treacherous battlefields from the St. Lawrence River in Canada to the Yser River in Belgium. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Curry paints a vivid picture of the bravery, sacrifice, and resilience displayed by these Canadian heroes in the face of unimaginable adversity.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War memoir

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Resilience, Canadian military history
