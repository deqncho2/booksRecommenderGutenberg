Synopsis: In 'Zeta Exchange: A Terran Empire story' by Wilson, Ann, readers are transported to a distant future where the Terran Empire reigns supreme. As tensions rise between the empire and the rebellious Zeta colony, a young engineer named Alex finds herself caught in the middle of a dangerous conspiracy. With her life on the line, she must navigate a treacherous web of secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances. As the fate of the empire hangs in the balance, Alex's choices will have far-reaching consequences that could change the course of history.

Genres: Science Fiction, Action, Thriller

Themes: Power struggles, Rebellion, Loyalty, Betrayal
