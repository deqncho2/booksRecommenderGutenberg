Synopsis: In 'The Ways of the Planets' by Martin, Martha Evans, a young astronomer stumbles upon a mysterious phenomenon that challenges everything she thought she knew about the universe. As she delves deeper into her research, she uncovers a hidden connection between the planets and the human experience, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and cosmic revelation. With each planet holding its own secrets and surprises, the protagonist must navigate the complexities of science, love, and the unknown to unlock the ultimate truth.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Exploration of the universe, Identity and self-discovery, Interconnectedness of the cosmos
