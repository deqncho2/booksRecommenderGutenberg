Synopsis: In 'The Sea Shore' by William S. Furneaux, a mysterious discovery washes ashore, captivating the attention of a small coastal town. As the townspeople become entangled in the secrets and intrigue surrounding this enigmatic object, they are forced to confront their own desires, fears, and the consequences of their actions. With each passing day, the tension rises, and the line between reality and imagination blurs, leading to unexpected and thrilling consequences.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, the power of the unknown, the human psyche, the consequences of actions
