Synopsis: 
In 'Heritage' by V. Sackville-West, the reader is transported to the enchanting world of Priorsford, a grand ancestral home nestled in the English countryside. As the story unfolds, we follow the lives of the Priors, a family deeply rooted in tradition and history. However, when a mysterious stranger arrives, secrets long buried begin to resurface, threatening to unravel the very fabric of their heritage. With its captivating characters and a plot filled with intrigue and suspense, 'Heritage' takes readers on a journey of discovery, exploring the complexities of family, identity, and the enduring power of the past.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Family, Tradition, Identity, Secrets, History
