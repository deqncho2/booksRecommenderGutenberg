Synopsis: In 'Magic and Religion' by Lang, Andrew, embark on a captivating exploration of the fascinating relationship between magic and religion throughout history. From ancient civilizations to modern societies, this thought-provoking book delves into the mysterious realms of belief, ritual, and the supernatural. Prepare to be enthralled as Lang uncovers the hidden connections and uncanny parallels between these two powerful forces that have shaped human culture and spirituality for centuries.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Anthropology

Themes: Belief systems, Rituals, Supernatural, Cultural influences
