Synopsis: 

'Food Guide for War Service at Home' is a comprehensive guidebook prepared under the direction of the United States Food Administration, in collaboration with the United States Department of Agriculture and the Bureau of Education. With a preface by Herbert Hoover, this book provides essential information and practical advice on how to make the most of available food resources during times of war. It offers valuable insights into nutrition, meal planning, food preservation, and creative cooking techniques, all aimed at supporting the war effort and ensuring the well-being of families on the home front.

Genres: Non-fiction, Cookbook, Historical

Themes: Food conservation, Nutrition, Homefront support, War effort
