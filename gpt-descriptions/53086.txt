Synopsis: In "The Inventor. Introducing the Wishing Box. A Christmas Play" by Elsie Duncan Yale, a brilliant inventor creates a magical box that grants wishes. As the town prepares for Christmas, the inventor's creation brings unexpected joy, but also challenges the community to reflect on the true meaning of their desires. With a touch of enchantment and a dash of holiday spirit, this heartwarming play explores the power of wishes and the importance of selflessness during the festive season.

Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Christmas

Themes: Magic, Community, Selflessness, Christmas Spirit
