Synopsis: In 'The London Pulpit' by J. Ewing Ritchie, a mysterious and charismatic preacher captivates the city of London with his powerful sermons. As his influence grows, the preacher's true intentions become increasingly enigmatic, leaving the congregation and the city questioning his motives. Set against the backdrop of Victorian London, this gripping tale explores the complexities of faith, deception, and the power of persuasion.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Religion, Manipulation, Identity
