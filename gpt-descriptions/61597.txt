Synopsis: 

In 'Eleanor Ormerod, LL. D., Economic Entomologist: Autobiography and Correspondence', Eleanor A. Ormerod takes us on a captivating journey through her life as a pioneering female scientist in the field of entomology. Through her autobiography and correspondence, we are introduced to Ormerod's relentless pursuit of knowledge, her groundbreaking research, and her unwavering dedication to protecting crops from destructive insects. As we delve into her personal and professional life, we witness the challenges she faced as a woman in a male-dominated field, the triumphs she achieved through her scientific discoveries, and the profound impact she had on the world of entomology. 

Genres: Biography, Science, History

Themes: Female empowerment, Scientific exploration, Persistence and resilience
