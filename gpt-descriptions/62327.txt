Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the rich history and captivating stories of the town and soke of Horncastle in the county of Lincoln. In this meticulously researched and beautifully written book, George Weir takes readers on a journey through time, uncovering the secrets and hidden gems of this fascinating region. From the ancient Roman settlement to the bustling market town of the 19th century, Weir's vivid descriptions and engaging narratives bring the past to life. Discover the tales of notable figures, the architectural wonders, and the everyday lives of the people who shaped Horncastle and its surrounding areas. 

Genres: Historical, Descriptive, Local History

Themes: History, Architecture, Local Culture
