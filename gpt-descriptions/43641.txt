Synopsis: 

'Following the Flag, from August 1861 to November 1862, with the Army of the Potomac' by Charles Carleton Coffin takes readers on a gripping journey through the American Civil War. Through vivid and detailed accounts, Coffin provides an intimate look into the experiences of soldiers and civilians alike, as they navigate the challenges and horrors of war. From the initial excitement and patriotism that led men to enlist, to the harsh realities of battle and the toll it takes on both body and spirit, this book offers a compelling narrative that captures the essence of the era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War literature

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Human resilience, The impact of war on individuals and society
