Synopsis: 
In 'Flash Evans and the Darkroom Mystery' by Bell, Frank, a young and talented photographer named Flash Evans stumbles upon a mysterious photograph that leads him into a world of secrets and danger. As he delves deeper into the darkroom mystery, Flash must use his keen eye and quick thinking to uncover the truth before it's too late. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the enigma alongside Flash.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, the power of observation and perception
