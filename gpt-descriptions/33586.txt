Synopsis: 

In "Drugging a Nation: The Story of China and the Opium Curse" by Samuel Merwin, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the dark history of China's struggle with opium addiction. Set against the backdrop of the 19th century, this gripping narrative explores the devastating consequences of the opium trade on the Chinese people and their society. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Merwin unveils the intricate web of political intrigue, international power struggles, and personal tragedies that shaped this harrowing chapter in China's history. As the story unfolds, readers will be drawn into a world of forbidden desires, moral dilemmas, and the relentless pursuit of power. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political thriller, Cultural exploration

Themes: Addiction, Colonialism, Power dynamics, Cultural clash
