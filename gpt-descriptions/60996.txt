Synopsis: In "The Soul of Abraham Lincoln," William E. Barton delves into the depths of the iconic president's character, exploring the spiritual journey that shaped his life and leadership. Through meticulous research and insightful analysis, Barton uncovers the profound influence of Lincoln's faith, his struggles with doubt, and the profound impact of his moral convictions on his decision-making. This captivating biography offers a unique perspective on one of history's most revered figures, shedding light on the inner workings of Lincoln's soul and the enduring legacy he left behind.

Genres: Biography, History, Religion

Themes: Faith and doubt, Moral convictions, Leadership
