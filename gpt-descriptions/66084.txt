Synopsis: In "Sir Gawain and the Green Knight: A Middle-English Arthurian Romance Retold in Modern Prose," Jessie L. Weston brings to life the captivating tale of Sir Gawain, a knight of King Arthur's Round Table, and his encounter with a mysterious green knight. Set in the enchanting world of Arthurian legend, this retelling follows Sir Gawain as he embarks on a perilous journey filled with tests of courage, honor, and loyalty. As the green knight challenges him to a deadly game, Sir Gawain must navigate a treacherous path, facing both external threats and his own inner struggles. With its rich imagery and timeless themes, this modern prose adaptation offers a fresh perspective on a classic medieval romance.

Genres: Arthurian literature, Medieval romance, Historical fiction

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Loyalty, Temptation, The nature of heroism
