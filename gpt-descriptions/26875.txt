Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scout Fire Fighters; Or Jack Danby's Bravest Deed' by Robert Maitland, young Jack Danby finds himself in the midst of a dangerous fire that threatens his town. As a Boy Scout, Jack must use his training and bravery to help save lives and protect his community. With flames raging and lives at stake, Jack's courage is put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the challenges and prove himself as a true hero?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Action

Themes: Bravery, Community, Heroism
