Synopsis: In "The Boy who sailed with Blake" by William Henry Giles Kingston, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure alongside young Ned, who finds himself embarking on a perilous journey with the legendary English naval commander, Admiral Blake. As they sail the high seas, facing treacherous storms, encounters with pirates, and the mysteries of uncharted territories, Ned's courage and determination are put to the test. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this tale of bravery and discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Friendship, Coming of Age
