Synopsis: 

'Rivers of Great Britain. The Thames, from Source to Sea.: Descriptive, Historical, Pictorial' takes readers on an enchanting journey along the iconic River Thames, uncovering its hidden treasures and rich history. From its humble beginnings in the picturesque Cotswolds to its grand finale at the bustling city of London, this book paints a vivid and captivating portrait of one of Britain's most beloved waterways. Through descriptive prose and stunning pictorial illustrations, the reader is transported to the banks of the Thames, immersing themselves in the beauty and significance of this majestic river.

Genres: Travel, History, Photography

Themes: Exploration, Heritage, Natural Beauty
