Synopsis: "Milch Cows and Dairy Farming" is a comprehensive guide that delves into the world of dairy farming, covering topics such as breeds, breeding, and management of dairy stock, as well as the selection of milch cows. It provides a detailed explanation of Guenon's method and explores the cultivation of forage plants, milk production, butter making, and cheese production. The book incorporates the latest advancements in the field and is specifically tailored to farming practices in the United States and British Provinces. Additionally, it includes a treatise on the dairy husbandry of Holland and Horsfall's system of dairy management.

Genres: Non-fiction, Agriculture, Farming

Themes: Dairy farming, Livestock management, Agricultural practices
