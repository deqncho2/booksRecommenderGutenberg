Synopsis: In the bohemian streets of 19th-century Paris, a group of artists and musicians become captivated by the enigmatic Trilby, a young woman with a mesmerizing voice. As her talent is exploited by those around her, Trilby's life takes a dramatic turn, leading to a haunting tale of love, obsession, and the power of art.

Genres: Gothic fiction, romance, psychological thriller

Themes: Identity, manipulation, the pursuit of artistic perfection
