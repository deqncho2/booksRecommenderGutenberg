Synopsis: 

'Answers to Prayer, from George Müller's Narratives' is a captivating collection of true stories that will leave readers in awe of the power of faith and prayer. George Müller, a renowned Christian evangelist and philanthropist, shares his personal experiences and encounters with divine intervention, showcasing the miraculous ways in which God answered his prayers. From the provision of daily needs to the transformation of lives, Müller's narratives offer a glimpse into a world where faith moves mountains and miracles become a reality. This inspiring book will leave readers inspired and encouraged to deepen their own prayer lives, as they witness the extraordinary ways in which God works in the lives of His people.

Genres: Non-fiction, Christian literature, Biography

Themes: Faith, Prayer, Divine intervention, Miracles
