Synopsis: In 'The Boy Spies with the Regulators', young readers are transported to the tumultuous times of the American Revolution, specifically the Carolina Patriots' struggle to liberate their state from British control. Join the courageous and resourceful boy spies as they embark on a thrilling adventure, using their wit and bravery to assist the Patriots in their fight for freedom. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must navigate treacherous paths, uncover secret codes, and outsmart the enemy to aid their fellow countrymen. This captivating historical tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the fate of the Carolina Patriots.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Loyalty, Freedom
