Synopsis: 
In 'He Knew He Was Right' by Anthony Trollope, a seemingly perfect marriage is torn apart by jealousy and suspicion. Louis Trevelyan becomes convinced that his wife, Emily, is having an affair, leading him down a path of obsession and irrational behavior. As their relationship deteriorates, both Louis and Emily find themselves trapped in a web of misunderstandings and stubbornness, with devastating consequences for their family and friends. This gripping novel explores the destructive power of jealousy and the complexities of human relationships, leaving readers questioning the limits of love and trust.

Genres: 
Psychological fiction, Domestic fiction, Victorian literature

Themes: 
Jealousy, Trust, Marriage, Miscommunication
