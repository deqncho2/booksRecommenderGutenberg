Synopsis: 
'Noteworthy Records of Bats From Nicaragua, with a Checklist of the Chiropteran Fauna of the Country' takes readers on an intriguing journey into the world of bats in Nicaragua. Jones, Smith, and Turner present a comprehensive checklist of the chiropteran fauna, highlighting the remarkable and unique species found in this Central American country. From rare sightings to fascinating behaviors, this book offers a captivating exploration of the diverse bat population in Nicaragua.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Zoology

Themes: Biodiversity, Conservation, Wildlife Research
