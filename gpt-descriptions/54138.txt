Synopsis: 
In 'Camp Cookery. How to Live in Camp', Maria Parloa takes readers on an adventurous journey into the world of outdoor cooking and living. With her expert guidance, she unveils the secrets of preparing delicious meals and maintaining a comfortable campsite, making every camping experience a memorable one. From mouthwatering recipes to practical tips on equipment and organization, Parloa's comprehensive guide is a must-have for both novice and seasoned campers. Get ready to embark on a culinary adventure in the great outdoors!

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Cooking, Outdoor Living

Themes: 
Camping, Cooking, Self-sufficiency
