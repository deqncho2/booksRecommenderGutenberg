Synopsis: In 'Destiny times three' by Leiber, Fritz, a mysterious artifact grants three individuals the power to manipulate time. As they navigate the consequences of their newfound abilities, they must confront their own desires, face moral dilemmas, and grapple with the unpredictable nature of fate. With each character's unique perspective, the story weaves a complex web of choices and consequences, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Time manipulation, Morality, Fate
