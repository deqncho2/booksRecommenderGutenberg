Synopsis: In "The Lonely House" by Marie Belloc Lowndes, a young woman named Mary moves into a secluded house in the countryside, hoping to find solace and escape from her troubled past. However, as she settles into her new home, she begins to experience strange occurrences that make her question her sanity. Is there something sinister lurking within the walls of the lonely house, or is Mary's mind playing tricks on her? As she delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding her new dwelling, Mary must confront her fears and uncover the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Isolation, Sanity vs. Madness, Secrets and Hidden Past
