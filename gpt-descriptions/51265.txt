Synopsis: In 'The Last of the Mortimers: A Story in Two Voices', Mrs. Oliphant weaves a captivating tale of mystery and family secrets. Set in the picturesque English countryside, the story follows two distinct voices, each with their own perspective and secrets to unveil. As the narrative unfolds, the reader is drawn into a web of intrigue, as the characters navigate love, betrayal, and the consequences of their actions. With its richly drawn characters and atmospheric setting, this novel will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Love and Relationships, Family Legacy
