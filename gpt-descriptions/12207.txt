Synopsis: In 'Establishing Relations: Odd Craft, Part 7', the protagonist finds himself entangled in a peculiar situation when he is mistaken for someone else. As he tries to navigate this case of mistaken identity, he becomes embroiled in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must unravel the truth before it's too late. Will he be able to establish his true identity and escape unscathed?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Trust
