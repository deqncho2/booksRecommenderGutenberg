Synopsis: In 'Branded' by Lynde Francis, a young woman named Emma finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious symbol that leads her on a dangerous journey. As she delves deeper into the truth behind the symbol, Emma must confront her own past and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of those she loves. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, 'Branded' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
