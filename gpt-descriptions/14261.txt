Synopsis: In 'Alton of Somasco: A Romance of the Great Northwest', author Harold Bindloss takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rugged landscapes of the Canadian wilderness. The story follows Alton, a young man with a mysterious past, as he navigates the challenges of life in the untamed Northwest. As Alton becomes entangled in a web of danger, adventure, and romance, he must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape his future. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Bindloss paints a vivid picture of the harsh yet beautiful wilderness, while keeping readers on the edge of their seats with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Western

Themes: Wilderness survival, Identity, Love and sacrifice
