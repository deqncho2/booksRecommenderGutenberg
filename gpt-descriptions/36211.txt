Synopsis: 
In 'Men, Women and Guns' by H. C. McNeile, a thrilling tale unfolds as Captain Hugh Drummond finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of espionage and intrigue. When a mysterious woman seeks his help in recovering stolen plans for a deadly weapon, Drummond is thrust into a world of international espionage, where he must navigate a treacherous landscape of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As the stakes rise and the danger escalates, Drummond must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to outsmart his adversaries and save the day.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Espionage, Betrayal, Heroism
