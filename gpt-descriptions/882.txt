Synopsis: 

In 'Sketches by Boz, Illustrative of Every-Day Life and Every-Day People', Charles Dickens takes us on a captivating journey through the bustling streets of 19th-century London. Through a series of vivid and humorous sketches, Dickens introduces us to a colorful cast of characters, from the eccentric to the ordinary, each with their own quirks and stories to tell. From the misadventures of a bumbling actor to the trials and tribulations of a street vendor, these sketches offer a delightful glimpse into the lives of everyday people and the vibrant tapestry of city life.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Social inequality, Human nature, Urban life
