Synopsis: In 'The Belfry' by Sinclair, May, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn to an old abandoned church with a mysterious belfry. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the belfry, she uncovers a haunting tale of love, loss, and redemption that transcends time. With each discovery, Emily becomes entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences that challenge her perception of reality and force her to confront her own inner demons. As she navigates the eerie corridors of the belfry, Emily must unravel the truth before it consumes her completely.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Love and Loss, Redemption, Perception of Reality
