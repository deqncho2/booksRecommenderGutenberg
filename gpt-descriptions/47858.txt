Synopsis: In 'The Islets of the Channel' by Walter Cooper Dendy, a young woman named Alice embarks on a journey to the enchanting Channel Islands, where she discovers a hidden world filled with mystery, romance, and danger. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the islets, Alice finds herself caught in a web of intrigue that challenges her perception of reality and tests her courage. With each twist and turn, she uncovers the dark history of the islands and the sinister forces that lurk beneath their picturesque surface. Will Alice be able to unravel the truth before it consumes her?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Perception versus reality, Courage and self-discovery
