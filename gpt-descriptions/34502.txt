Synopsis: 

'Charles Lyell and Modern Geology' by T. G. Bonney takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and groundbreaking work of Charles Lyell, a renowned geologist of the 19th century. From his early explorations to his revolutionary theories, this book delves into the fascinating world of geology and the impact Lyell had on shaping our understanding of the Earth's history. As readers follow Lyell's adventures and discoveries, they will be enthralled by the scientific intrigue and the profound implications his work had on the scientific community.

Genres: Biography, Science, History

Themes: Scientific discovery, Evolution of geological theories, Impact of individual scientists
