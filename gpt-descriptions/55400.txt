Synopsis: In 'Common Cause: A Novel of the War in America' by Samuel Hopkins Adams, a young journalist finds himself caught in the midst of the American Civil War, torn between his duty to report the truth and his personal convictions. As he navigates the chaos and violence of the war-torn nation, he uncovers a web of corruption and deceit that threatens to tear the country apart. With danger lurking at every turn, he must make difficult choices that will not only shape his own destiny but also the fate of a divided nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Political Thriller

Themes: Loyalty, Morality, Corruption, Patriotism, Identity
