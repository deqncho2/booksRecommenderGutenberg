Synopsis: 

In 'How to Make Candy: A Complete Hand Book for Making All Kinds of Candy, Ice Cream, Syrups, Essences, Etc., Etc.', an anonymous author takes readers on a delightful journey into the world of confectionery. This comprehensive guide provides step-by-step instructions, secret recipes, and expert tips for creating a wide variety of mouthwatering candies, ice creams, syrups, and more. From classic favorites to innovative creations, this book is a treasure trove of sweet indulgence that will inspire both novice and experienced candy makers alike. 

Genres: Cookbook, Non-fiction

Themes: Culinary arts, Creativity, Sweet indulgence
