Synopsis:
In "The Battle of Tsu-shima: between the Japanese and Russian fleets, fought on 27th May 1905," Semenov, V. I. takes us on a thrilling journey into one of the most significant naval battles in history. Set against the backdrop of the Russo-Japanese War, this gripping account unveils the intense clash between two powerful fleets, as they engage in a battle that will shape the course of history. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Semenov, V. I. brings to life the bravery, strategy, and sacrifice of the men who fought on both sides, creating a narrative that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Naval warfare

Themes: War and conflict, Strategy and tactics, Heroism and sacrifice
