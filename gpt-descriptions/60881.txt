Synopsis: In 'The Last Trespasser' by Jim Harmon, a mysterious figure emerges from the shadows, trespassing on forbidden grounds and leaving a trail of enigmatic clues behind. As a determined detective races against time to unravel the truth, a web of secrets, deception, and unexpected alliances begins to unravel, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax. This gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of trust and the lengths one will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Trust
