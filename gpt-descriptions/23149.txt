Synopsis: 
In 'In the Control Tower' by Mohler, Will, a young air traffic controller named Alex finds himself caught in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse when a mysterious hacker begins targeting the control tower. As the hacker's attacks escalate, Alex must race against time to uncover their identity and stop them before disaster strikes. With the lives of countless passengers hanging in the balance, Alex must navigate a web of deception and betrayal, all while trying to maintain control in the chaos of the control tower.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Technology, Trust, Power
