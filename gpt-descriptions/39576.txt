Synopsis: In 'The Radio Detectives' by A. Hyatt Verrill, a group of amateur radio enthusiasts stumble upon a mysterious message that leads them on a thrilling adventure. As they delve deeper into the cryptic clues, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger, espionage, and secret codes. With their radio skills and quick thinking, they must race against time to uncover the truth and outsmart the cunning criminals who will stop at nothing to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Amateur sleuths, Technology and communication, Pursuit of truth
