Synopsis: In 'The First One' by Kastle, Herbert D., a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of human history. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but the fate of humanity. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of deception and betrayal to protect his discovery and those he holds dear. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Scientific advancement, Betrayal, Morality
