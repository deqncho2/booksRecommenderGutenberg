Synopsis: In 'Why Frau Frohmann Raised Her Prices, and Other Stories', Anthony Trollope presents a collection of captivating tales that delve into the lives of various characters. From a determined widow who takes a bold step to secure her future, to a young woman who finds herself caught between duty and love, each story offers a unique glimpse into the complexities of human nature. With Trollope's signature wit and insightful observations, these stories explore themes of ambition, love, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Ambition, Love, Happiness
