Synopsis: In "Personal Recollections of Chickamauga," James R. Carnahan takes readers on a gripping journey through one of the bloodiest battles of the American Civil War. Through his vivid and personal accounts, Carnahan provides a unique perspective on the chaos, heroism, and devastation that unfolded on the battlefield of Chickamauga. As he recounts his experiences as a soldier, the reader is transported to the front lines, feeling the intensity of the conflict and the profound impact it had on those involved. This powerful narrative offers a firsthand glimpse into the horrors of war and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Military history

Themes: War and its consequences, Heroism, Human resilience
