Synopsis: In "Four Max Carrados Detective Stories," the enigmatic and resourceful Max Carrados takes on four intriguing cases that will challenge his deductive skills and push the boundaries of his perception. From a baffling art theft to a mysterious disappearance, Carrados navigates a world of secrets and deception, using his unique abilities to unravel the truth and bring justice to those in need. With each story, the stakes rise higher, and Carrados must rely on his keen senses and sharp intellect to solve the puzzles that lie before him.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Perception, Deception, Justice
