Synopsis: 

In 'A History of Dentistry from the most Ancient Times until the end of the Eighteenth Century' by Vincenzo Guerini, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the evolution of dentistry, exploring its origins in ancient civilizations and tracing its development up until the eighteenth century. From the ancient Egyptians' use of dental prosthetics to the innovative techniques of the Renaissance period, this comprehensive account sheds light on the fascinating and often overlooked history of dentistry. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Guerini uncovers the struggles, breakthroughs, and triumphs that have shaped the field of dentistry throughout the ages.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Medicine

Themes: Evolution of medical practices, Cultural influences on dentistry, Technological advancements in dentistry
