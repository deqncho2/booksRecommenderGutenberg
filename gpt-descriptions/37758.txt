Synopsis: 

'Atlantic Classics' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the vast and mysterious Atlantic Ocean. Set in the early 1900s, the story follows a group of adventurers who embark on a perilous expedition to uncover the secrets hidden beneath the ocean's depths. As they encounter treacherous storms, encounter strange creatures, and face unexpected challenges, the characters must rely on their wit, courage, and teamwork to survive. With each twist and turn, the plot unravels a captivating tale of exploration, danger, and the unyielding human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human Resilience
