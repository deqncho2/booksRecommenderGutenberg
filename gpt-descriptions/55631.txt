Synopsis: 

In 'History of Chemistry, Volume 2 (of 2): From 1850 to 1910', Thorpe takes readers on a captivating journey through the transformative period of chemistry during the late 19th and early 20th centuries. From groundbreaking discoveries to fierce rivalries, this volume delves into the lives and work of the brilliant minds who shaped the field of chemistry during this pivotal era. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Thorpe uncovers the secrets behind the development of new theories, the birth of modern chemical industries, and the profound impact of chemistry on society. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Scientific progress, Intellectual rivalries, Societal impact
