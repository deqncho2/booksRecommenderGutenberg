Synopsis: In 'Adaptation' by Reynolds Mack, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Amelia Grant makes a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the course of human evolution. However, her invention attracts the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess it. As Dr. Grant finds herself caught in a dangerous web of intrigue and betrayal, she must navigate a treacherous world where nothing is as it seems. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, she must adapt to survive and protect her groundbreaking creation.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Evolution, Technology, Betrayal, Survival
