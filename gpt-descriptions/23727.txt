Synopsis:
The Lost Girl follows Alvina Houghton, a young woman living in a small English town, who feels trapped by the expectations of her family and society. Seeking adventure and freedom, she embarks on a journey that takes her to various European cities, where she encounters a cast of intriguing characters and experiences both love and heartbreak. As Alvina navigates the complexities of her relationships and grapples with her own desires, she must ultimately confront the question of what it truly means to be lost and found.

Genres:
Literary fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes:
Identity, Freedom, Love and relationships
