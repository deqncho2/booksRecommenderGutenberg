Synopsis: In 'The Mayflower, January, 1905', a mysterious letter sets off a chain of events that leads a young woman on a captivating journey to uncover the truth about her family's past. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within her ancestral home, she discovers a web of deceit, love, and betrayal that spans generations. With each revelation, she becomes more entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the stakes are higher than she could have ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Betrayal, Love and loss
