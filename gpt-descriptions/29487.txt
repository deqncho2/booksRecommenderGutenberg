Synopsis: In 'Forever' by Robert Sheckley, a man wakes up to find himself in a strange and seemingly perfect world, where all his desires are instantly fulfilled. However, as he delves deeper into this seemingly idyllic existence, he begins to question the true nature of his reality and the price he must pay for eternal happiness. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking scenarios, 'Forever' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the boundaries of perception and the pursuit of true fulfillment.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Human Desire and Satisfaction, Existentialism
