Synopsis: 

In the secluded and mysterious Minnewaska Mountain Houses, a group of strangers find themselves drawn together by a shared secret. As they navigate the grand estate's hidden corridors and lush gardens, they uncover a web of intrigue and deception that threatens to unravel their lives. With each passing day, the tension mounts, and the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred. As the truth slowly unravels, the characters must confront their own pasts and face the consequences of their actions. 

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and lies, Identity, Perception vs. reality
