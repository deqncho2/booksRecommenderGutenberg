Synopsis: In 'On the Portraits of English Authors on Gardening,' Samuel Felton delves into the fascinating world of gardening through the lens of English authors. With the second edition of this book, Felton adds considerable additions to his biographical notices, providing readers with a deeper understanding of the authors and their contributions to the art of gardening. From the enchanting descriptions of lush gardens to the insightful anecdotes about the authors' lives, this book offers a captivating exploration of the intersection between literature and horticulture.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Gardening

Themes: Literature and gardening, Biographical exploration, Artistic inspiration
