Synopsis: In 'Hospital Sketches' by Louisa May Alcott, follow the captivating journey of a young woman who volunteers as a nurse during the American Civil War. Through her vivid and heartfelt sketches, she provides a unique and intimate glimpse into the lives of the soldiers she cares for, the challenges she faces, and the resilience she discovers within herself. As she navigates the horrors of war and the complexities of human nature, her experiences will leave readers both moved and inspired.

Genres: Historical fiction, Memoir, War literature

Themes: Compassion, Resilience, Sacrifice, Gender roles, Human connection
