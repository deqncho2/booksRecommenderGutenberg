Synopsis: In 'A Song-Bird' by Eleanora H. Stooke, a young woman named Lily finds herself drawn into a mysterious world of music and secrets when she discovers an enchanted songbird. As she delves deeper into the bird's origins and the power it possesses, Lily must navigate a dangerous path filled with hidden motives and unexpected alliances. With her own voice as her greatest weapon, Lily must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Music, Power, Identity, Secrets
