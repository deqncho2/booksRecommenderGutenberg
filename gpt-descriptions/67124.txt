Synopsis: In 'Father Thames' by Walter Higgins, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds along the banks of the iconic River Thames. As the protagonist embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind a series of unexplained events, they become entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and ancient folklore. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a world where the line between reality and myth blurs, and where the river itself holds the key to unlocking the mysteries that lie hidden beneath its surface.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Mythology, Secrets and Hidden Truths, the Power of Nature
