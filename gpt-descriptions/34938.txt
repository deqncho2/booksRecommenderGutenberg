Synopsis: In 'Education: How Old The New', James J. Walsh takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of education, exploring the evolution of teaching methods and the impact of technological advancements. From ancient civilizations to modern times, Walsh delves into the fascinating stories of influential educators and their innovative approaches to learning. This thought-provoking book challenges conventional notions of education and offers insights into how we can shape the future of learning.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, History

Themes: Evolution of education, Teaching methods, Technological advancements
