Synopsis: In 'The Doom of the Griffiths' by Elizabeth Gaskell, a young girl named Mary Griffiths finds herself caught in a web of family secrets and tragic events. As she grows up, Mary becomes increasingly aware of the dark history that haunts her family, and the devastating consequences it has on their lives. With a sense of impending doom, Mary must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and the weight of her family's past, all while trying to forge her own path in a world filled with uncertainty.

Genres: Gothic fiction, family drama, historical fiction

Themes: Family secrets, tragedy, love and loyalty, the burden of the past
