Synopsis: In a distant future where interstellar travel is the norm, Lucas Trask, a charismatic and ambitious space pirate, embarks on a quest for revenge against those who destroyed his home planet. As he builds a formidable fleet and becomes a feared Space Viking, he must navigate treacherous political alliances, uncover ancient secrets, and confront the moral dilemmas of power and justice. With epic battles, interplanetary romance, and a gripping exploration of the human spirit, 'Space Viking' takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the cosmos.

Genres: Science Fiction, Space Opera, Adventure

Themes: Revenge, Power, Morality, Love, Exploration
