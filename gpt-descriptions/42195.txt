Synopsis: 
In 'Seventy Years on the Frontier', Majors, Alexander takes us on a captivating journey through his remarkable life as a frontiersman. From his early days as a young adventurer to his experiences as a trailblazer and pioneer, Majors shares gripping tales of danger, survival, and the indomitable spirit of the American frontier. With vivid descriptions and thrilling anecdotes, he paints a vivid picture of the challenges and triumphs that shaped his extraordinary existence. Prepare to be transported to a time of untamed wilderness, where courage and resilience were the keys to survival.

Genres: Memoir, Adventure, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Frontier Spirit
