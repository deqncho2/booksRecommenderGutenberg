Synopsis: In "The Acadian Exiles: a Chronicle of the Land of Evangeline," Arthur G. Doughty takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous history of the Acadian people. Set against the backdrop of the beautiful and enchanting Land of Evangeline, this chronicle follows the lives of the Acadian exiles as they face heart-wrenching challenges, endure unimaginable hardships, and strive to preserve their culture and identity. From the tragic events leading to their expulsion to their relentless pursuit of a place to call home, this gripping narrative sheds light on the resilience, courage, and unwavering spirit of the Acadian people.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Adventure

Themes: Resilience, Identity, Cultural preservation, Homecoming
