Synopsis: In 'The Prince and the Page: A Story of the Last Crusade', a young prince and a humble page find their lives intertwined as they embark on a perilous journey during the tumultuous times of the Last Crusade. As they face treacherous enemies, unexpected alliances, and the test of their own courage, they discover the true meaning of loyalty, honor, and sacrifice. Will they be able to fulfill their destinies and bring about a lasting peace?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Courage, Destiny, Peace
