Synopsis: In the small town of Emu Plains, a mysterious set of twins arrives, turning the community upside down. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, the twins find themselves at the center of a web of deception and danger. With their unique abilities and a dark past, they must navigate through a world filled with hidden agendas and unexpected alliances. Will they be able to uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Family, Betrayal, Power
