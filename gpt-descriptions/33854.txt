Synopsis: In 'Ask a Foolish Question' by Robert Sheckley, a curious young man stumbles upon a peculiar shop that claims to have the answers to any question. Intrigued, he decides to test the shop's credibility by asking a seemingly foolish question. Little does he know that this innocent inquiry will lead him on a mind-bending journey through a world where reality and perception intertwine, challenging his beliefs and forcing him to question the nature of truth itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Existentialism, The Power of Questions
