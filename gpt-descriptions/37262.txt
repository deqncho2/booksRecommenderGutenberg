Synopsis: In 'The Shadow of the Past' by F. E. Mills Young, a young woman named Alice finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries when she inherits an old family estate. As she delves into the history of the house, she uncovers dark secrets that have been hidden for generations. With each revelation, Alice becomes more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means putting her own life at risk. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Inheritance, Identity, Betrayal
