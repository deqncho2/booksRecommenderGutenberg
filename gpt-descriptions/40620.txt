Synopsis: In 'Hilda's Mascot: A Tale of "Maryland, My Maryland"', Hilda, a young girl with a deep love for her home state of Maryland, embarks on a journey to uncover the true meaning behind the state song. Along the way, she encounters unexpected challenges and forms unlikely friendships that will forever change her perspective on her beloved Maryland. This heartwarming tale explores the power of music, the importance of heritage, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Identity, Friendship, Resilience
