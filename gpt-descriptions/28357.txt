Synopsis: 

'By Conduct and Courage: A Story of the Days of Nelson' by G. A. Henty is an enthralling historical adventure set during the time of Admiral Horatio Nelson. The story follows the journey of Ned Hearne, a young Englishman who finds himself caught up in the tumultuous events of the Napoleonic Wars. From the treacherous seas to the battlefields of Europe, Ned must navigate the perils of war, face personal challenges, and prove his mettle in the face of adversity. As he rises through the ranks, Ned's loyalty, bravery, and unwavering determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he emerge as a hero or succumb to the dangers that surround him?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Honor, Coming of Age
