Synopsis: In 'Reviews' by Oscar Wilde, a renowned playwright finds himself at the center of a scandalous controversy when his latest work receives scathing reviews from critics. As he navigates the treacherous world of literary criticism, he discovers the power of words and the lengths people will go to protect their reputations. With wit and charm, Wilde delves into the complexities of art, society, and the blurred lines between truth and perception.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Drama

Themes: Artistic integrity, Social hypocrisy, Perception vs reality
