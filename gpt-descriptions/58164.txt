Synopsis: In 'Doodles, the Sunshine Boy' by Emma C. Dowd, readers are introduced to Doodles, a young boy with an extraordinary ability to bring joy and happiness to everyone he meets. Despite facing numerous challenges in his own life, Doodles uses his infectious positivity to inspire those around him and make a lasting impact on their lives. As the story unfolds, readers are taken on a heartwarming journey filled with unexpected twists and turns, as Doodles' unique gift continues to touch the lives of those he encounters.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Inspirational, Family

Themes: Positivity, Resilience, Impact of Kindness
