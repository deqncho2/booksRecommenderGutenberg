Synopsis: In 'The Princess Athura: A romance of Iran', Samuel W. Odell takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of ancient Persia. The story revolves around the beautiful and courageous Princess Athura, who finds herself torn between duty and desire when she falls in love with a mysterious stranger. As their forbidden love blossoms, they must navigate treacherous political intrigues, cultural clashes, and personal sacrifices. With vivid descriptions and rich historical details, Odell weaves a tale of passion, betrayal, and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Forbidden love, Duty and sacrifice, Cultural clashes
