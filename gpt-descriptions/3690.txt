Synopsis: In 'Floor Games; a companion volume to "Little Wars"', H.G. Wells invites readers into a world of imagination and creativity as he explores the joy and excitement of playing with miniature figures on the floor. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Wells takes us on a journey where ordinary household objects transform into fantastical landscapes and epic battles unfold. From the thrill of constructing intricate forts to the strategic maneuvers of miniature armies, this companion volume to "Little Wars" offers a delightful and inspiring exploration of the power of play.

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Games

Themes: Imagination, Creativity, Play, Childhood, Education
