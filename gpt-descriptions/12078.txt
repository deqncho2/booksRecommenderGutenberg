Synopsis: 
'Ireland, Historic and Picturesque' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and breathtaking landscapes of Ireland. From ancient ruins to vibrant cities, the author, Charles Johnston, paints a vivid picture of the country's past and present, immersing readers in its unique charm and beauty. Through his engaging storytelling and descriptive prose, Johnston invites readers to explore the hidden gems and iconic landmarks that make Ireland a truly enchanting destination. Whether it's the mystical allure of ancient Celtic sites or the bustling streets of Dublin, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the heart and soul of Ireland.

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Irish culture, Landscape and nature, Historical landmarks
