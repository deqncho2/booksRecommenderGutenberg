Synopsis: In 'Thoughts on a Revelation' by Jerram, S. J., a renowned philosopher embarks on a journey of self-discovery after a life-altering revelation. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of existence, he grapples with profound questions about the nature of reality, the limits of human knowledge, and the meaning of life itself. Through a series of thought-provoking encounters and philosophical musings, the protagonist's worldview is challenged, leading him to confront his deepest fears and confront the ultimate truth. This captivating narrative explores the intricate interplay between reason and faith, inviting readers to ponder the profound mysteries that lie at the heart of our existence.

Genres: Philosophical fiction, Mystery, Spiritual

Themes: Existentialism, Metaphysics, Faith and Reason
