Synopsis: In 'The Laboratorians' by Edward Peattie, a group of brilliant scientists find themselves entangled in a web of secrecy and danger as they uncover a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. As they navigate through treacherous alliances and unexpected betrayals, they must race against time to protect their invention from falling into the wrong hands. With its gripping plot and complex characters, this thrilling scientific mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Scientific discovery, Ethics, Betrayal
