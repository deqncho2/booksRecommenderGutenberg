Synopsis: 
In 'Memorials of old Durham', the reader is transported back in time to the enchanting city of Durham, where ancient tales and forgotten legends come to life. Through vivid descriptions and captivating narratives, the author unveils the hidden secrets and untold stories that lie within the city's historic landmarks, from the majestic cathedral to the winding riverbanks. As the reader delves deeper into the past, they uncover a world of mystery, intrigue, and the enduring spirit of a community that has withstood the test of time. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Travelogue

Themes: History, Heritage, Community, Legends
