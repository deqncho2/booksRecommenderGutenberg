Synopsis: In "A Letter to American Workingmen, from the Socialist Soviet Republic of Russia," Vladimir Lenin addresses the American working class, offering a unique perspective on the struggles and aspirations of the Russian Revolution. Through a passionate and persuasive letter, Lenin seeks to forge a connection between the American and Russian workers, urging them to unite in their fight against capitalism and imperialism. With powerful rhetoric and insightful analysis, he highlights the potential for a global revolution that could reshape the world order. 

Genres: Political literature, Historical document, Persuasive writing

Themes: Revolution, Socialism, International solidarity, Class struggle
