Synopsis: In 'The Pearl of Peace; or, The Little Peacemaker' by Leslie, Madeline, a young girl named Lily discovers a mysterious pearl that possesses a unique power. As she embarks on a journey to understand its true nature, Lily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and danger. With the fate of her world hanging in the balance, she must navigate treacherous waters and make difficult choices to protect the precious pearl and bring peace to her land.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Power of innocence, Quest for peace, Sacrifice
