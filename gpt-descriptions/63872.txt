Synopsis: In 'The Beast-Jewel of Mars' by Leigh Brackett, a daring adventurer finds himself entangled in a treacherous quest on the mysterious planet of Mars. As he navigates through dangerous landscapes and encounters enigmatic beings, he becomes obsessed with a legendary artifact known as the Beast-Jewel. With the fate of Mars hanging in the balance, he must confront his own desires and face the ultimate test of his courage.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Power
