Synopsis: 

In 'Breakaway' by Gimble Stanley, a young prodigy named Alex finds himself trapped in a world where conformity is the norm. Fueled by his passion for music and a burning desire for freedom, he embarks on a thrilling journey to break free from the chains that bind him. As he navigates through unexpected challenges and encounters a group of like-minded individuals, Alex discovers the true power of self-expression and the importance of staying true to oneself. With every note he plays, he inspires others to embrace their own uniqueness and join him in a rebellion against the status quo. 

Genres: Young Adult, Dystopian, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Individuality, Self-expression, Rebellion, Identity
