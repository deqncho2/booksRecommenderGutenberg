Synopsis: 

In 'Fifteen Months in Dixie; Or, My Personal Experience in Rebel Prisons', William W. Day takes us on a harrowing journey through his time as a prisoner of war during the American Civil War. From the moment of his capture to his eventual release, Day recounts the unimaginable hardships, the camaraderie among fellow prisoners, and the constant struggle for survival in the face of adversity. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt anecdotes, this memoir offers a gripping firsthand account of the brutal realities of life in Confederate prisons.

Genres: Memoir, History, War

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Comradeship, Human Spirit
