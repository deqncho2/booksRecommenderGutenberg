Synopsis: In 'Angela's Business' by Henry Sydnor Harrison, a young woman named Angela embarks on a daring venture that challenges societal norms and tests her resilience. As she navigates the world of business in early 20th century America, Angela faces unexpected obstacles and discovers the power of determination and self-belief. With her unwavering spirit, she strives to carve her own path in a male-dominated industry, leaving readers captivated by her journey of ambition and courage.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Women's Fiction

Themes: Gender Equality, Entrepreneurship, Perseverance
