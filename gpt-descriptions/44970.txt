Synopsis: In 'The Recollections of a Drummer-Boy' by Henry Martyn Kieffer, follow the captivating journey of a young boy who finds himself thrust into the chaos of war. From the innocence of his small town to the brutality of the battlefield, he must navigate the trials and tribulations of being a drummer-boy during the Civil War. Through his eyes, experience the camaraderie, the fear, and the resilience of those who fought for their beliefs. This poignant and gripping tale will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of this courageous young protagonist.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Innocence, Loss, Resilience
