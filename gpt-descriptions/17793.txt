Synopsis: In 'The Debtor: A Novel', a young woman named Mary finds herself in a dire financial situation after her father's sudden death. Desperate to support herself and her family, she takes a job as a governess in a wealthy household. However, as she becomes entangled in the lives of the family members, Mary discovers dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel her own life. With her resilience and determination, she must navigate the treacherous waters of deceit and betrayal to secure her future.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Financial struggle, Social class, Family dynamics, Deception
