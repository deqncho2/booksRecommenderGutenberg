Synopsis: In 'The Geisha Memory' by Winston K. Marks, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he is hired to investigate the disappearance of a geisha. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a dark underworld filled with secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With danger lurking at every turn, the detective must navigate the treacherous streets of Kyoto to unravel the truth and bring justice to those who deserve it.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Cultural Identity, Justice and Redemption
