Synopsis: In 'The Trial of an Ox, for Killing a Man', the reader is transported to a fascinating world where animals have their own court system. When an ox is accused of a shocking crime, the trial unfolds with gripping intensity as witnesses take the stand and the truth slowly emerges. As the case progresses, unexpected twists and turns keep the reader on the edge of their seat, questioning the nature of justice and the complexities of morality. 

Genres: Legal fiction, Satire, Fantasy

Themes: Justice, Morality, Anthropomorphism
