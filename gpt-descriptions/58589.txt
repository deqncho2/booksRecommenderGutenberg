Synopsis:
'The Boys' and Girls' Pliny: Being parts of Pliny's "Natural History" edited for boys and girls, with an Introduction' is a captivating collection of excerpts from Pliny the Elder's renowned work, "Natural History." This edited version is specifically tailored for young readers, providing them with a fascinating glimpse into the natural world as observed by one of the greatest ancient scholars. From descriptions of exotic animals and plants to accounts of ancient civilizations and their technological advancements, this book offers a captivating journey through the wonders of the natural world. With an engaging introduction that sets the stage for exploration, 'The Boys' and Girls' Pliny' is sure to ignite the curiosity of young readers and leave them eager to learn more about the world around them.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Educational

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, Ancient Knowledge
