Synopsis: 
In 'Bennie Ben Cree: Being the Story of His Adventure to Southward in the Year '62', readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside the protagonist, Bennie Ben Cree. Set in the year 1862, the story follows Bennie as he embarks on a daring adventure to the south. Faced with numerous challenges and unexpected encounters, Bennie's journey becomes a test of his courage, resilience, and determination. As he navigates through unfamiliar territories and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Bennie's story unfolds with gripping suspense and unexpected twists. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Identity, Friendship
