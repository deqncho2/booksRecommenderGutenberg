Synopsis: In 'Rodney, the Overseer' by Castlemon, Harry, a young boy named Rodney finds himself thrust into a world of adventure and responsibility when he becomes the overseer of a large plantation. As he navigates the challenges of managing the plantation and dealing with the complex dynamics of the people around him, Rodney discovers hidden secrets and uncovers a plot that could change everything. With danger lurking at every turn, Rodney must rely on his wit, courage, and newfound leadership skills to protect those he cares about and ensure justice is served.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Coming of age, Responsibility, Courage, Justice
