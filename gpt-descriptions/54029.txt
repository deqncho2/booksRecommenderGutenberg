Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Twenty-first Regiment, or the Royal North British Fusiliers', Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this esteemed regiment from 1678 to 1849. From its humble beginnings to its involvement in significant historical events, Cannon's account offers a fascinating insight into the trials, triumphs, and sacrifices of the Royal North British Fusiliers. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military service, Regimental history, Historical events
