Synopsis: In 'The Crescent Moon' by Francis Brett Young, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey to a remote village in the English countryside, seeking solace and a fresh start. However, she soon discovers that the village holds dark secrets and mysterious occurrences that challenge her perception of reality. As Mary delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the village, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden love, leading her to question her own sanity and the true nature of the world around her.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Perception, Secrets, Forbidden Love
