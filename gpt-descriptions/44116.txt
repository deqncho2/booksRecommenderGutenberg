Synopsis: 
'A History of the Ninth Regiment, Illinois Volunteer Infantry' by Marion Morrison is a captivating account of the experiences and triumphs of the soldiers who served in the Ninth Regiment during the American Civil War. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Morrison delves into the battles, hardships, and camaraderie that defined the regiment's journey. From the initial enlistment to the final victory, this historical narrative offers a compelling glimpse into the lives of these brave men and the impact they had on the war.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: 
War and its consequences, Sacrifice and bravery, Brotherhood and camaraderie
