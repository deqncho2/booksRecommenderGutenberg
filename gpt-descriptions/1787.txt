Synopsis: 
In the wake of his father's sudden death, Prince Hamlet returns to Denmark to find his world turned upside down. Haunted by his father's ghost and consumed by thoughts of revenge, Hamlet embarks on a journey of self-discovery and moral dilemma. As he navigates through a web of deceit, betrayal, and madness, Hamlet must confront the ultimate question: to act or not to act? With its intricate plot, complex characters, and timeless soliloquies, Shakespeare's 'Hamlet' is a gripping tale of love, loyalty, and the human condition.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Revenge, Madness, Moral Dilemma, Existentialism
