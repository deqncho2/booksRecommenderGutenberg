Synopsis: 

Embark on a captivating journey through time and discover the hidden secrets of the Isle of Wight in 'The Geological Story of the Isle of Wight' by J. Cecil Hughes. This enthralling work takes readers on an exploration of the island's geological history, unveiling a world of ancient landscapes, prehistoric creatures, and dramatic geological events. From the formation of the island millions of years ago to the present day, Hughes skillfully weaves together scientific knowledge and captivating storytelling to paint a vivid picture of the Isle of Wight's geological wonders. Prepare to be amazed by the island's rich fossil record, breathtaking cliffs, and the fascinating forces that have shaped this unique corner of the world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Geology

Themes: Geological history, Fossils, Natural landscapes, Prehistoric life
