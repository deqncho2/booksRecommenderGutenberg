Synopsis: In 'The Radio Boys with the Revenue Guards' by Gerald Breckenridge, a group of adventurous young radio enthusiasts find themselves caught up in a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon a secret operation involving smugglers and revenue guards. As they delve deeper into the dangerous world of illegal activities, the boys must use their wit, courage, and radio skills to outsmart the criminals and bring them to justice. With suspenseful twists and turns, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following the Radio Boys' quest to uncover the truth.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Crime and Justice
