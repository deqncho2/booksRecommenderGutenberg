Synopsis: In "A Bullet for Cinderella," a man named Tony is forced to confront his past when he receives a mysterious letter that threatens to expose a long-buried secret. As he delves into his own history, Tony becomes entangled in a dangerous web of deceit, betrayal, and redemption. With his life hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he loves.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Betrayal
