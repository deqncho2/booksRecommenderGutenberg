Synopsis: In 'Checking the Waste: A Study in Conservation', Mary Huston Gregory takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of waste management and conservation. Through her meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Gregory explores the hidden world of waste, uncovering surprising truths and shedding light on the urgent need for sustainable practices. From the bustling streets of cities to the vast landscapes of nature, this thought-provoking study challenges readers to reconsider their own role in the cycle of waste and inspires them to take action for a greener future.

Genres: Non-fiction, Environmental Science, Conservation

Themes: Waste management, Sustainability, Environmental consciousness
