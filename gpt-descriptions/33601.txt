Synopsis: In 'The Master's Violin' by Reed, Myrtle, a young violinist named Pauline discovers a mysterious violin hidden in her attic. As she begins to play it, she is transported to a different time and place, where she encounters a talented musician who becomes her mentor. Together, they embark on a musical journey that will challenge Pauline's skills and reveal the true power of music. But as Pauline delves deeper into the secrets of the violin, she must confront her own fears and make a difficult choice that will change her life forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Music, Time Travel, Self-Discovery
