Synopsis: 

'Daily Thoughts: selected from the writings of Charles Kingsley by his wife' offers a captivating collection of insightful and thought-provoking musings from the renowned author Charles Kingsley. Through a compilation of his wife's careful selection, readers are invited into the mind of this influential figure, exploring his profound reflections on life, nature, spirituality, and the human condition. With each passing page, Kingsley's words resonate deeply, inspiring readers to contemplate their own existence and find solace in the beauty and complexity of the world around them.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Inspirational

Themes: Life, Nature, Spirituality, Reflection, Human Condition
