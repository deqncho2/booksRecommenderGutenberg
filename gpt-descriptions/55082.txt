Synopsis: 

In 'Everybody's Book of Luck' by Anonymous, a mysterious and enchanting guidebook is discovered, promising to unlock the secrets of luck and fortune. As readers delve into its pages, they are taken on a captivating journey through ancient rituals, superstitions, and tales of extraordinary luck. From lucky charms to lucky numbers, from lucky symbols to lucky rituals, this book explores the fascinating world of luck in all its forms. As readers explore the book's wisdom, they begin to question the role of luck in their own lives and ponder whether they can harness its power to shape their destinies. 

Genres: Self-help, Mystery, Folklore

Themes: Luck, Destiny, Superstition, Personal Growth
