Synopsis: In 'Jane Austen's sailor brothers: Being the adventures of Sir Francis Austen, G.C.B., Admiral of the Fleet and Rear-Admiral Charles Austen', authors Edith C. Hubback and J. H. Hubback delve into the captivating lives of Jane Austen's real-life sailor brothers. Set against the backdrop of the Napoleonic Wars, this enthralling biography takes readers on a thrilling journey through the naval exploits of Sir Francis Austen and Rear-Admiral Charles Austen. From daring sea battles to encounters with pirates, the brothers' adventures are filled with danger, romance, and unexpected twists. Through their extraordinary lives, the authors shed light on the influence of the Austen family's naval connections on Jane Austen's own writing, offering a fascinating glimpse into the world that inspired her beloved novels.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction, Naval History

Themes: Family ties, Naval warfare, Influence on literature
