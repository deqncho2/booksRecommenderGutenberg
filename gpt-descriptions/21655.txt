Synopsis: In 'The works of Guy de Maupassant, Vol. 5: Une Vie and Other Stories', readers are transported to the captivating world of 19th-century France. This collection of short stories, including the poignant novella 'Une Vie', delves into the lives of various characters, exploring their desires, struggles, and the consequences of their choices. From tales of love and betrayal to stories of societal expectations and the human condition, Maupassant's masterful storytelling paints a vivid picture of the complexities of life during this era.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Literary Fiction

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Society and Class, Human Nature, Consequences of Choices
