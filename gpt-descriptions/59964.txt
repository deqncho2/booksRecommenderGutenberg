Synopsis: In 'The Open Window: Tales of the Months', Mabel Osgood Wright takes readers on a captivating journey through the changing seasons. Each month brings a new tale, filled with mystery, adventure, and unexpected twists. From the enchanting secrets of a hidden garden to the haunting echoes of a forgotten past, these stories will transport readers to a world where the ordinary becomes extraordinary. As the open window serves as a gateway to these captivating tales, readers will be left eagerly anticipating what lies beyond each turn of the page.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Mystery

Themes: Nature, Seasons, Imagination, Secrets
