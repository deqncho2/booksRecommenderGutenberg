Synopsis: In 'The Banner Boy Scouts; or, The Struggle for Leadership' by George A. Warren, a group of young scouts embarks on an exciting adventure filled with challenges and unexpected twists. As they strive to prove their worth and demonstrate their leadership skills, the boys find themselves facing a series of tests that push them to their limits. With friendship, loyalty, and determination at the forefront, this thrilling tale follows the Banner Boy Scouts as they navigate through thrilling escapades and learn valuable life lessons along the way.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Leadership, Friendship, Perseverance
