Synopsis: In 'Walls of Acid' by Hasse, Henry, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Amelia Collins embarks on a dangerous mission to uncover the truth behind a mysterious phenomenon that threatens to engulf the world. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she must confront her own past and make unimaginable sacrifices to save humanity from an impending catastrophe. With heart-pounding suspense and mind-bending twists, this gripping sci-fi thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific exploration, Sacrifice, Redemption
