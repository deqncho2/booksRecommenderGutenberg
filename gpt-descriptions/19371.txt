Synopsis: In 'The Forfeit' by Cullum Ridgwell, a mysterious invitation leads a group of strangers to a secluded mansion where they are forced to participate in a deadly game. As the night unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and the true nature of the game becomes increasingly sinister. With their lives on the line, the characters must navigate a treacherous maze of deception and betrayal, all while trying to uncover the identity of their enigmatic host. Will they be able to outwit their captor and escape with their lives, or will they become pawns in a twisted game of fate?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Survival
