Synopsis: In 'The Happy Family' by B. M. Bower, a young woman named Joan embarks on a journey to find her long-lost family, only to stumble upon a mysterious and captivating cowboy named Jim. As their paths intertwine, Joan discovers secrets about her past that will challenge her perception of family and love. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the happy family.

Genres: Western, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Family, Identity, Love, Secrets
