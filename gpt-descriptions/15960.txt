Synopsis: In 'Literary Character of Men of Genius: Drawn from Their Own Feelings and Confessions', Disraeli delves into the inner world of renowned literary figures, exploring their deepest emotions and personal experiences. Through a collection of intimate revelations, the reader is invited to witness the complex and often tumultuous lives of these brilliant minds. From the struggles of love and ambition to the depths of despair and the pursuit of artistic excellence, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the souls of men who have shaped the literary landscape.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Psychology

Themes: Creativity, Passion, Human Nature
