Synopsis: 
The German Classics of the Nineteenth and Twentieth Centuries, Volume 07: Masterpieces of German Literature Translated into English. in Twenty Volumes is a captivating anthology that brings together a collection of remarkable German literary works from the 19th and 20th centuries. This volume, the seventh in the series, presents a diverse range of masterpieces that have been beautifully translated into English, allowing readers to immerse themselves in the rich and profound world of German literature. From gripping tales of love and adventure to thought-provoking philosophical explorations, this anthology offers a glimpse into the depth and brilliance of German literary tradition. 

Genres: Anthology, Fiction, Literature
Themes: Love, Adventure, Philosophy, German culture and society
