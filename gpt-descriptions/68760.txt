Synopsis: 
In 'Betrothed for a day: Or, Queenie Trevalyn's love test', Queenie Trevalyn, a young and spirited woman, finds herself caught in a web of love and deception. When she agrees to a betrothal for just one day, she embarks on a journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As she navigates through the challenges of love and loyalty, Queenie must confront her own desires and make difficult choices that will shape her future. Will she find true love or be forever bound by duty?

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Identity and self-discovery, Duty and obligation
