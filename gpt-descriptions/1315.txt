Synopsis: 
In 'Autobiography and Selected Essays', Thomas Henry Huxley takes us on a captivating journey through his life, sharing his personal experiences, reflections, and profound insights. From his early struggles and triumphs to his encounters with influential figures of his time, Huxley's autobiography offers a fascinating glimpse into the life of a brilliant scientist and thinker. Alongside his autobiography, this collection also includes a selection of his thought-provoking essays, covering a wide range of topics such as education, evolution, and the nature of knowledge. With his eloquent prose and sharp intellect, Huxley invites readers to explore the depths of his mind and engage in stimulating intellectual discourse.

Genres: Autobiography, Essays, Non-fiction

Themes: Personal growth, Scientific inquiry, Intellectual exploration
