Synopsis: In Greek Waters: A Story of the Grecian War of Independence follows the thrilling adventures of young Frank Norris, an English lad who finds himself caught up in the tumultuous events of the Greek War of Independence in the early 19th century. As he joins the Greek rebels in their fight against the Ottoman Empire, Frank must navigate treacherous waters, face dangerous enemies, and forge unlikely alliances. With vivid descriptions of naval battles, daring escapes, and the rich historical backdrop of Greece, this gripping tale will transport readers to a time of bravery, honor, and the struggle for freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Patriotism, Freedom, Loyalty
