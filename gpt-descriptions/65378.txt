Synopsis: In 'Reality Unlimited' by Robert Silverberg, a brilliant scientist invents a groundbreaking technology that allows people to experience any reality they desire. As the world becomes addicted to this new form of escapism, the line between fantasy and reality begins to blur, leading to unforeseen consequences. Amidst the chaos, a group of rebels emerges, determined to expose the dangers of this addictive technology and restore the balance between the real and the virtual.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Dystopian

Themes: Virtual reality, Addiction, Ethics, Identity
