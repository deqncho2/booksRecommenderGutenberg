Synopsis: 
'Letters of the Motor Girl' is a captivating coming-of-age novel that follows the journey of a young woman named Ethellyn Gardner. Through a series of heartfelt letters, Ethellyn shares her experiences as she embarks on a thrilling adventure, defying societal norms and embracing her passion for automobiles. From her encounters with fascinating characters to the challenges she faces in a male-dominated world, Ethellyn's letters paint a vivid picture of her determination, resilience, and the transformative power of pursuing one's dreams.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Epistolary

Themes: 
Gender roles, Self-discovery, Pursuit of passion
