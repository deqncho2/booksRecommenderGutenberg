Synopsis: In 'Santal Folk Tales' by Campbell, A., readers are transported to the enchanting world of Santal folklore, where mythical creatures, magical beings, and ordinary people coexist in extraordinary tales. From the mischievous antics of clever animals to the heartwarming stories of love and sacrifice, these folk tales offer a captivating glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of the Santal people. As readers delve into the pages, they will be captivated by the vivid imagery, the timeless wisdom, and the universal truths that lie within these captivating narratives.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural

Themes: Tradition and Heritage, Morality and Ethics, Power of Storytelling
