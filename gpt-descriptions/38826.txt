Synopsis: In 'Bolanyo' by Read, Opie Percival, a young boy named Bolanyo embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him through the vibrant streets of Accra, Ghana. As he navigates the challenges of growing up in a bustling city, Bolanyo discovers a hidden world filled with secrets, danger, and unexpected friendships. With each twist and turn, Bolanyo's courage and determination are put to the test, leading him to uncover the truth about his own identity and the power of self-belief. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Self-discovery
