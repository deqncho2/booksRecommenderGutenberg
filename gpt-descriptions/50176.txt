Synopsis: In 'The Pride of Eve' by Warwick Deeping, a young woman named Eve finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a passionate love affair. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, Eve must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations, leading to a gripping tale of sacrifice and self-discovery.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Duty and Responsibility, Societal Expectations
