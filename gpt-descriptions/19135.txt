Synopsis: In "The Southerner: A Romance of the Real Lincoln," Thomas Dixon Jr. weaves a captivating tale set against the backdrop of the American Civil War. The story follows a young Southern man named John Graham, who finds himself torn between his loyalty to his family and his growing admiration for Abraham Lincoln. As John navigates the complexities of love, honor, and duty, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal sacrifice. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Dixon explores the profound impact of war on individuals and the enduring power of human resilience.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Love, War, Political Ideals
