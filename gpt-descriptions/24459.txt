Synopsis: In "The Lost Princess of Oz," the magical land of Oz faces a new threat as Princess Ozma mysteriously disappears. With the help of Dorothy, the Scarecrow, the Tin Woodman, and other beloved characters, a daring rescue mission is launched to find the lost princess and restore peace to the enchanting realm of Oz. As they journey through treacherous lands and encounter peculiar creatures, the group must rely on their wit, courage, and friendship to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Loyalty, Magic
