Synopsis: 
'Among the Farmyard People' takes readers on a delightful journey into the lives of various animals living on a farm. From the mischievous kittens to the wise old rooster, each character has a unique story to tell. Through their adventures and interactions, the reader gains a deeper understanding of the animal kingdom and the lessons they can teach us about life.

Genres: Children's literature, Animal fiction, Short stories

Themes: Animal behavior, Friendship, Life lessons
