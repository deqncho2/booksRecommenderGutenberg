Synopsis: 
In 'The Call of the Canyon' by Zane Grey, a wealthy New York socialite named Carley Burch finds herself at a crossroads in life after a devastating personal loss. Seeking solace and a fresh start, she embarks on a journey to the rugged beauty of Arizona's Oak Creek Canyon. There, amidst the breathtaking landscapes and the vibrant community of ranchers and outcasts, Carley discovers a world vastly different from her own. As she becomes entangled in the lives of those around her, Carley must confront her own prejudices and desires, ultimately facing a choice that could change her life forever.

Genres: Western, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Self-discovery, Love and relationships, Nature and wilderness
