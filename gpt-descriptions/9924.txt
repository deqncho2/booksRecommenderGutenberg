Synopsis: 
In the small village of St. Gurlott, a mysterious and captivating young woman named Viviette arrives, capturing the attention of all who encounter her. As she becomes entangled in the lives of the villagers, secrets from the past begin to unravel, leading to unexpected consequences. With its atmospheric setting and intriguing characters, 'Viviette' takes readers on a suspenseful journey filled with love, betrayal, and the power of hidden desires.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, Forbidden love, Identity and self-discovery
