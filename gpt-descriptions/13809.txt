Synopsis: 
In 'Mountain Idylls, and Other Poems' by King, Alfred Castner, readers are transported to the breathtaking landscapes of the mountains, where nature's beauty intertwines with the human experience. Through vivid imagery and lyrical verses, the poet explores the profound connections between man and his surroundings, delving into themes of love, longing, and the eternal quest for meaning. From the serenity of mountain lakes to the rugged peaks that touch the sky, this collection of poems invites readers to embark on a poetic journey that will leave them captivated and inspired.

Genres: Poetry, Nature Writing, Romanticism

Themes: Nature, Love, Longing, Human Connection, Transcendence
