Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 148, July 10, 1915; The Mark of Cain; or, Nick Carter's Air-line Case', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving a mysterious mark and a high-stakes air-line conspiracy. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Carter must navigate a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected twists to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Conspiracy, Justice
