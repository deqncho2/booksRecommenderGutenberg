Synopsis: In 'Ada, the Betrayed; Or, The Murder at the Old Smithy. A Romance of Passion', the reader is transported to a small village where secrets, betrayal, and murder lurk in the shadows. Ada, a young and innocent woman, finds herself entangled in a web of deceit and forbidden desires. As she uncovers the truth behind the murder at the old smithy, Ada's world is turned upside down, and she must navigate treacherous paths to protect her own life and the ones she holds dear. With its gripping plot and complex characters, this thrilling romance will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Betrayal, Forbidden Love, Secrets, Justice
