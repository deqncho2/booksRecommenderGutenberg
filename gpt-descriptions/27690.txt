Synopsis: 
Nobody's Girl: (En Famille) follows the journey of Perrine, a young girl who finds herself alone in the world after her father's death. Determined to survive and reunite with her mother, Perrine embarks on a remarkable adventure, facing numerous challenges and meeting unforgettable characters along the way. From the bustling streets of Paris to the picturesque countryside, Perrine's resilience and unwavering spirit shine through as she navigates the complexities of life and discovers the true meaning of family.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-Age, Drama

Themes: Resilience, Family, Self-discovery
