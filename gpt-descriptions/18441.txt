Synopsis: In 'Bright-Wits, Prince of Mogadore' by Flood, L. L. (Leopold L.) and Laughlin, Burren, embark on a thrilling adventure through the mystical land of Mogadore, where a young prince named Bright-Wits discovers his extraordinary powers. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters mythical creatures, Bright-Wits must uncover the truth about his past and fulfill his destiny to save his kingdom from impending doom. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Coming of age, Destiny, Power and responsibility
