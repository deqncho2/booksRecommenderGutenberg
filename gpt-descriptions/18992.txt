Synopsis: 
'Current Superstitions: Collected from the Oral Tradition of English Speaking Folk' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of superstitions that have been passed down through generations. From the peculiar to the eerie, this collection explores the fascinating beliefs and rituals that have shaped the lives of English-speaking communities. Delving into the origins and significance of these superstitions, the book offers a glimpse into the mysterious world of folklore and the power it holds over our lives.

Genres: Folklore, Cultural Studies, Anthropology

Themes: Tradition, Belief Systems, Cultural Heritage
