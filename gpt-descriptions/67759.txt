Synopsis: In the depths of the mysterious Maine forests, a young trapper named Ethan embarks on a perilous journey to confront the malevolent demons that haunt the region. Armed with ancient knowledge and a fierce determination, Ethan must navigate treacherous terrain, face unimaginable dangers, and uncover the truth behind the demon infestation. As he delves deeper into the dark secrets of Umbagog, Ethan discovers that the line between good and evil is not as clear-cut as it seems, and that the true demons may not be the ones he initially set out to trap.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Redemption, Courage, Mystery
