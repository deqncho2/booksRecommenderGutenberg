Synopsis: 
In 'The Great God Success: A Novel' by Phillips, David Graham, we are introduced to the ambitious and enigmatic character, John Burkett Ryder, who rises from humble beginnings to become one of the most powerful and influential figures in New York City. As Ryder navigates the cutthroat world of finance and politics, he becomes entangled in a web of scandal, power struggles, and personal sacrifices. With his insatiable hunger for success, Ryder must confront the consequences of his actions and the true cost of achieving his dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Power, Morality, Corruption
