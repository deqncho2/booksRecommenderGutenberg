Synopsis: In 'The Price of Coal: A Play' by Harold Brighouse, the story revolves around the lives of coal miners and their families in a small mining town. As tensions rise between the workers and the mine owners, a young miner finds himself caught in the middle of a struggle for justice and equality. With a backdrop of love, sacrifice, and the fight for workers' rights, this gripping play explores the harsh realities of the coal mining industry and the price one must pay for standing up against oppression.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Social justice, Labor rights, Sacrifice
