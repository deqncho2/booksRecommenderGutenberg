Synopsis: Brenda's cousin, Helen, embarks on a thrilling adventure at Radcliffe College, where she encounters unexpected challenges and forms lifelong friendships. As Helen navigates the complexities of academia, she discovers her true passions and learns valuable life lessons that will shape her future. With determination and resilience, Helen's journey at Radcliffe becomes a transformative experience that will inspire readers of all ages.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Young Adult, Fiction

Themes: Education, Self-discovery, Friendship, Resilience
