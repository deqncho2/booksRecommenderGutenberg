Synopsis: In 'The Story of Coventry' by Mary Dormer Harris, a young woman named Alice finds herself caught in the midst of a centuries-old mystery when she stumbles upon a hidden diary in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the secrets of Coventry, she uncovers a web of intrigue, betrayal, and forbidden love that spans generations. With each revelation, Alice becomes more entangled in the past, and she must navigate the treacherous waters of her own family history to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Identity, History and heritage
