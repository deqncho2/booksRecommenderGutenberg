Synopsis: In 'Forgotten Tales of Long Ago', Lucas E. V. takes readers on a captivating journey through time, unearthing enchanting stories that have been lost in the annals of history. From mythical creatures and magical realms to heroic quests and forbidden love, this collection of forgotten tales will transport readers to a world of wonder and imagination. As each story unfolds, secrets are revealed, mysteries are unraveled, and the power of storytelling is celebrated. Prepare to be captivated by these long-lost tales that have been waiting to be rediscovered.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mythology

Themes: Imagination, Legacy, the Power of Storytelling
