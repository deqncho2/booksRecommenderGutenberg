Synopsis: In "A Garland for Girls" by Louisa May Alcott, follow the captivating journey of a group of young women as they navigate the challenges and joys of life. From unexpected friendships to personal growth, each girl discovers her own unique path towards happiness and fulfillment. Through heartwarming tales and inspiring adventures, this collection of stories will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to see what lies ahead for these remarkable girls.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Short Stories

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Self-discovery, Empowerment
