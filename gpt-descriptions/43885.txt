Synopsis: In 'Alila, Our Little Philippine Cousin', readers are transported to the beautiful islands of the Philippines, where they meet Alila, a young girl with a heart full of curiosity and a spirit of adventure. Through Alila's eyes, we explore the vibrant culture, traditions, and natural wonders of her homeland, from bustling markets to breathtaking landscapes. As Alila navigates the challenges and joys of everyday life, readers will be captivated by her resilience, kindness, and unwavering love for her family and community.

Genres: Children's Literature, Cultural Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Cultural diversity, Family bonds, Exploration and discovery
