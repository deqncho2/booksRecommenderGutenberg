Synopsis: In 'The Haunted Room: A Tale' by A. L. O. E., a young girl named Mabel finds herself drawn to a mysterious room in her family's ancestral home. Despite warnings and rumors of a haunting, Mabel's curiosity leads her to uncover dark secrets and encounter supernatural occurrences within the room's walls. As she delves deeper into the room's history, Mabel must confront her fears and unravel the truth behind the haunting, all while risking her own sanity and safety.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Curiosity, Secrets, Haunting, Fear, Sanity
