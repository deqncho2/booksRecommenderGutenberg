Synopsis: 
Step back in time and embark on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of carriages and coaches in Ralph Straus' "Carriages & Coaches: Their History & Their Evolution." Delve into the rich history of these elegant modes of transportation, from their humble beginnings to their opulent transformations. Uncover the secrets behind the intricate craftsmanship and innovative designs that made carriages and coaches a symbol of status and luxury. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Straus brings to life the stories of the people who rode in these magnificent vehicles, from royalty to everyday travelers. Discover the hidden tales of romance, adventure, and intrigue that unfolded within the confines of these rolling works of art. Whether you are a history enthusiast, a lover of vintage transportation, or simply curious about the bygone era of carriages and coaches, this book is sure to captivate your imagination and transport you to a world of elegance and grandeur.

Genres: History, Transportation, Art

Themes: Evolution of technology, Social status, Nostalgia
