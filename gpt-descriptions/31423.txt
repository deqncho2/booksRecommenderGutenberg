Synopsis: 

'In-Door Gardening for Every Week in the Year' is a comprehensive guide written by William Keane, a skilled gardener, that unveils the secrets to successful plant cultivation in various indoor settings. From greenhouses and conservatories to stoves, pits, orchids, and forcing-houses, Keane shares his expertise on nurturing plants throughout the year. This invaluable resource provides readers with practical advice, tips, and techniques to ensure the health and vitality of their indoor gardens. Whether you are a seasoned gardener or a beginner, this book is sure to captivate your interest and inspire you to create your own thriving indoor oasis.

Genres: Gardening, Non-fiction

Themes: Indoor gardening, Plant cultivation, Horticulture
