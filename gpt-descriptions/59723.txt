Synopsis: In "History of the Jesuits: Their origin, progress, doctrines, and designs," author G.B. Nicolini delves into the captivating story of the Jesuit order, exploring their mysterious origins, their remarkable growth and influence, and the controversial doctrines and designs that have surrounded them throughout history. From their humble beginnings to their involvement in political intrigues and their impact on education and missionary work, this book unveils the fascinating journey of the Jesuits and their enduring legacy. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Religious studies

Themes: Power and influence, Religious devotion, Education and scholarship, Political intrigue
