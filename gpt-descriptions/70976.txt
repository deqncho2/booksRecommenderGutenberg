Synopsis: In 'The Radiant City: An Allegory', author Evelyn R. Garratt takes readers on a captivating journey through a mysterious and enchanting city. As the protagonist navigates the city's labyrinthine streets, they encounter a series of intriguing characters and uncover hidden secrets that challenge their perception of reality. With each twist and turn, the city's true nature is gradually revealed, leaving readers questioning the boundaries between dreams and reality, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Allegory

Themes: Perception, Reality vs. Illusion, Power of Imagination
