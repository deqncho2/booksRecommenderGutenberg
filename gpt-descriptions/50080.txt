Synopsis: In 'Motor Matt Makes Good; or, Another Victory For the Motor Boys' by Stanley R. Matthews, readers are taken on an exhilarating journey alongside the adventurous Motor Boys. When a mysterious crime wave hits the city, Motor Matt and his friends find themselves caught up in a dangerous web of intrigue and deception. With their trusty motor vehicles as their only allies, the Motor Boys must use their wits and skills to unravel the truth and bring the culprits to justice. Packed with thrilling chase scenes, unexpected twists, and a dash of romance, this action-packed tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Justice
