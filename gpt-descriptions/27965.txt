Synopsis:
In the small town of Mersham, a mysterious murder takes place, leaving the local police baffled. As the investigation unfolds, it becomes clear that the victim's family, the Chestermarkes, hold a dark secret that could be the key to solving the crime. With a web of deceit, hidden motives, and unexpected twists, 'The Chestermarke Instinct' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the murder.

Genres:
Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes:
Family secrets, Deception, Betrayal
