Synopsis: In the ballad 'Hafbur and Signe', two star-crossed lovers find themselves entangled in a web of forbidden love, treachery, and sacrifice. Hafbur, a valiant warrior, and Signe, a noblewoman, must navigate the complexities of their society's expectations and the consequences of their choices. As their love story unfolds, they face numerous challenges that test their loyalty, courage, and resilience. Will their love conquer all, or will fate tear them apart?

Genres: Ballad, Romance, Tragedy

Themes: Forbidden love, Loyalty, Sacrifice
