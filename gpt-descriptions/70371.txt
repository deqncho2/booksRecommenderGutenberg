Synopsis: In the charming French town of Clamecy, the boisterous and irreverent Colas Breugnon, a master woodcarver, leads a life filled with laughter, love, and a rebellious spirit. Set against the backdrop of the late 18th century, this delightful novel takes us on a journey through Colas' mischievous adventures, his encounters with eccentric characters, and his unwavering dedication to his craft. From his humorous escapades to his passionate pursuit of freedom, Colas Breugnon embodies the essence of a free-spirited artist who refuses to conform to societal norms.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Artistic expression, Individualism, Love and relationships, Social rebellion
