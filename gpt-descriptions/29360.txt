Synopsis: In 'The Bad Family & Other Stories' by Eliza Fenwick, readers are introduced to a collection of captivating and unconventional tales that explore the complexities of human nature. From a family with a peculiar penchant for mischief to a young woman's journey of self-discovery, each story presents a unique and thought-provoking narrative. With unexpected twists and turns, these tales delve into the depths of human emotions, challenging societal norms and shedding light on the intricacies of relationships. Prepare to be enthralled by Fenwick's masterful storytelling as she takes readers on a journey through the extraordinary lives of her characters.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Literary Fiction

Themes: Human nature, Family dynamics, Identity, Morality, Social norms
