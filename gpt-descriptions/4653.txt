Synopsis: In 'God's Good Man: A Simple Love Story' by Corelli, Marie, a young man named David is torn between his love for a woman and his devotion to his religious calling. As he navigates the complexities of his heart and the challenges of his faith, David's journey takes unexpected turns that test his resolve and force him to confront the true nature of love and sacrifice.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Spiritual

Themes: Love and duty, Faith and doubt, Sacrifice and redemption
