Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Triona' by William John Locke, a young woman named Triona finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she is unexpectedly thrust into a world of wealth and privilege. As she navigates the treacherous waters of high society, Triona must unravel the mysteries of her own past and confront the choices that will determine her future. With its captivating characters and intricate plot twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Social class, Betrayal
