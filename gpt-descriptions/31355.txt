Synopsis: 
In 'To Each His Star' by Bryce Walton, a renowned astronomer stumbles upon a mysterious celestial phenomenon that defies all scientific explanation. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the boundaries between reality and the unknown blur. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the power of knowledge, the fragility of existence
