Synopsis: 
'Fairy Tales from Spain' by Muñoz Escámez, José takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Spanish folklore. From mischievous goblins to noble knights, this collection of fairy tales weaves together magical adventures that will transport readers to a realm filled with wonder and imagination. Each tale is filled with unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers on the edge of their seats as they delve into the rich tapestry of Spanish storytelling. 

Genres: 
Fairy tale, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: 
Magic, Adventure, Morality, Heroism
