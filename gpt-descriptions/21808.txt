Synopsis: In 'The House: An Episode in the Lives of Reuben Baker, Astronomer, and of His Wife, Alice' by Field, Eugene, readers are transported into the lives of Reuben Baker, a dedicated astronomer, and his wife, Alice. Set in a quaint house nestled in the countryside, the story unfolds as the couple experiences a series of mysterious and unexplained events that challenge their perception of reality. As they navigate through strange occurrences and encounter peculiar characters, Reuben and Alice find themselves questioning their own sanity and the true nature of the world around them. This gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with suspense, intrigue, and a touch of the supernatural.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Supernatural

Themes: Perception of reality, Sanity, Mystery
