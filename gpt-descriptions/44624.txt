Synopsis: 
In 'Sweden' by Nilsson, Victor Alfred, a young journalist named Erik embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious disappearance in the heart of Sweden. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic case, Erik finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, political intrigue, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and Erik must navigate a treacherous landscape where trust is scarce and danger lurks at every turn. As he races against time to solve the puzzle, Erik discovers that the truth he seeks may have far-reaching consequences that could change the course of his own life and the fate of an entire nation.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Political Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Political corruption, Personal sacrifice
