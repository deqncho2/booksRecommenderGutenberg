Synopsis: In 'G-r-r-r...!', a gripping psychological thriller by Robert Donald Locke, a renowned psychiatrist finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse when a mysterious patient with a dark secret enters his life. As the boundaries between reality and illusion blur, the psychiatrist must confront his own demons and unravel the truth before it's too late. With unexpected twists and a relentless sense of suspense, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Sanity vs. Madness, Deception
