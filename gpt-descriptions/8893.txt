Synopsis: 
Prepare to be captivated by the dark and mysterious world of Edgar Allan Poe in "Selections from Poe." This collection of his most haunting and chilling tales will transport you to the depths of the human psyche, where madness, obsession, and the supernatural intertwine. From the eerie atmosphere of "The Fall of the House of Usher" to the psychological torment of "The Tell-Tale Heart," each story will leave you on the edge of your seat, questioning the boundaries of reality and the depths of human depravity. With his masterful storytelling and unparalleled ability to evoke fear and suspense, Poe's works will leave an indelible mark on your imagination.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Horror, Mystery

Themes: Madness, Death, Isolation, Guilt, Supernatural
