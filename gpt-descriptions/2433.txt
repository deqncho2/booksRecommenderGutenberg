Synopsis:
In 'Donal Grant' by George MacDonald, a young man named Donal Grant finds himself in a small Scottish town, where he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. As he navigates through the town's secrets and encounters a cast of eccentric characters, Donal's own past and identity are called into question. With the help of a wise old woman and a mischievous young girl, Donal embarks on a journey of self-discovery that will challenge his beliefs and change his life forever.

Genres:
Fantasy, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Identity, Redemption, Faith
