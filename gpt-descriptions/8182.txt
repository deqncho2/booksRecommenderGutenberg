Synopsis: In 'The Ghost of Guir House' by Charles Willing Beale, a young man named Malcolm inherits an old mansion with a dark history. As he settles into his new home, he begins to experience strange and unexplainable occurrences that lead him to believe that the house is haunted. Determined to uncover the truth, Malcolm delves into the secrets of Guir House, uncovering a chilling mystery that will test his courage and sanity.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Haunting, Secrets and Betrayal, Psychological Suspense
