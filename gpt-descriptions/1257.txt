Synopsis: In 17th century France, a young and ambitious swordsman named d'Artagnan sets off to join the prestigious Musketeers of the Guard. Alongside his new comrades Athos, Porthos, and Aramis, they become embroiled in a web of political intrigue, secret affairs, and dangerous duels. As they navigate the treacherous world of courtly life, their loyalty and friendship are tested, and they must fight to protect their honor and the honor of their queen.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Honor, Betrayal, Love, Political Intrigue
