Synopsis: In 'The Impostor: A Tale of Old Annapolis' by John Reed Scott, a young man named Philip Kent arrives in the vibrant city of Annapolis, seeking to fulfill his dreams of becoming a naval officer. However, he soon finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and deception, as he becomes the target of a mysterious impostor who threatens to unravel his aspirations and jeopardize his very identity. As Philip navigates the treacherous waters of Annapolis society, he must uncover the truth behind the impostor's motives and protect his future from being stolen away.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Deception, Ambition, Society
