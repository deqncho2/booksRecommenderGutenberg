Synopsis: 

In 'Half Hours in Bible Lands, Volume 2: Patriarchs, Kings, and Kingdoms', author P. C. Headley takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient lands of the Bible. From the stories of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob to the rise and fall of powerful kings and kingdoms, this volume delves into the rich history and profound lessons found within the pages of the Old Testament. With vivid descriptions and engaging narratives, Headley brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and complexities of the biblical figures and the world they inhabited. 

Genres: Historical, Religious, Non-fiction

Themes: Faith and obedience, Divine providence, Leadership and governance
