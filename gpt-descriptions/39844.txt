Synopsis: In 'The Ascent of Man' by Blind, Mathilde, a brilliant scientist embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that challenges the very essence of humanity. As she delves deeper into her research, she uncovers a hidden world of secrets and mysteries that could change the course of human evolution forever. With each revelation, the line between science and morality becomes increasingly blurred, forcing her to confront the ethical implications of her discoveries. As the stakes rise, she must navigate a treacherous path filled with danger and betrayal, all while grappling with the fundamental question of what it truly means to be human.

Genres: Science fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Human evolution, Morality, Ethical dilemmas
