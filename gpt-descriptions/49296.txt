Synopsis: In 'Retained for the Defence: A Farce, in One Act', chaos ensues when a bumbling lawyer is tasked with defending a client in a high-stakes court case. As the lawyer's incompetence and absurd antics unfold, the courtroom becomes a stage for hilarious misunderstandings, mistaken identities, and outrageous situations. With the fate of the client hanging in the balance, will the lawyer's unconventional methods lead to a surprising victory or an utter disaster?

Genres: Farce, Comedy, Theatre

Themes: Legal incompetence, Absurdity, Mistaken identity
