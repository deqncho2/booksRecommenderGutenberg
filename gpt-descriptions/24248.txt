Synopsis: In 'The Girl Scouts' Good Turn' by Lavell, Edith, a group of Girl Scouts embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes them deep into the heart of a mysterious forest. As they navigate through unexpected challenges and encounter strange creatures, the girls must rely on their courage, teamwork, and resourcefulness to overcome the obstacles in their path. With each twist and turn, they uncover secrets that will change their lives forever, leading them to question the true meaning of friendship and the power of kindness. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Teamwork, Self-discovery
