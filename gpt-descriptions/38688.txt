Synopsis: In 'Zoological Mythology; or, The Legends of Animals, Volume 2', De Gubernatis delves into the captivating world of animal legends and folklore from various cultures around the globe. From the cunning foxes of Japan to the majestic lions of Africa, this volume explores the rich tapestry of mythical creatures and their significance in human imagination. Through a collection of enchanting tales and insightful analysis, De Gubernatis invites readers on a mesmerizing journey that unveils the hidden meanings and symbolism behind these captivating animal myths.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Cultural Studies

Themes: Animal symbolism, Cultural diversity, Human-animal relationships
