Synopsis: In "Sarah's First Start in Life" by Adelaide M. G. Campbell, a young woman named Sarah embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience. Set in the late 19th century, Sarah faces numerous challenges and setbacks as she navigates the complexities of society and strives to find her place in the world. With determination and a strong spirit, she learns valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the power of perseverance. This captivating tale takes readers on a heartfelt adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Society's Expectations
