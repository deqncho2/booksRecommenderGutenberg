Synopsis: In 'Death's Wisher' by Jim Wannamaker, a mysterious figure known as the Wisher emerges, possessing the ability to grant people's deepest desires. However, there is a catch - every wish granted comes with a deadly consequence. As the Wisher's power spreads, a group of individuals must navigate the dangerous consequences of their own desires and uncover the truth behind the Wisher's origins. With each wish, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful battle against fate itself.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Desire and its consequences, Fate and free will, Morality and ethics
