Synopsis: 
'With Our Soldiers in France' takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of soldiers on the front lines during World War I. Through vivid and poignant storytelling, Eddy Sherwood offers a firsthand account of the bravery, camaraderie, and sacrifices made by these courageous men. From the trenches to the battlefields, this book provides a unique perspective on the realities of war, capturing both the horrors and the moments of humanity that emerge amidst the chaos. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be captivated by the resilience and determination of these soldiers, as they navigate the challenges of war and strive to find hope amidst the darkness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Memoir

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Humanity, Resilience
