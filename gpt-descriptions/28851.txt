Synopsis: In "The Faith Healer: A Play in Three Acts," the enigmatic and charismatic faith healer, Francis Hardy, takes center stage as he recounts his extraordinary life and the miracles he claims to have performed. Through a series of monologues, Francis, his wife Grace, and his manager Teddy share their perspectives on faith, hope, and the power of belief. As the play unfolds, the audience is left to question the nature of truth and the limits of faith.

Genres: Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Faith and Doubt, Redemption, the Human Condition
