Synopsis: 
In 'Texas Fossils: An Amateur Collector's Handbook', author William Henry Matthews takes readers on an exciting journey through the fascinating world of fossil hunting in Texas. From the ancient marine creatures that once roamed the state's prehistoric seas to the remnants of dinosaurs that once roamed its land, this handbook provides a comprehensive guide for amateur collectors looking to uncover the secrets of Texas' rich fossil record. With detailed descriptions, helpful tips, and stunning photographs, Matthews invites readers to join him in the thrill of discovery as he shares his knowledge and passion for fossils found in the Lone Star State.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Fossil hunting, Paleontology, Texas history, Amateur science
