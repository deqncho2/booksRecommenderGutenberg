Synopsis: In 'The Magic Curtain: A Mystery Story for Girls', a group of adventurous girls stumble upon a hidden secret while exploring an old theater. As they unravel the mystery behind the magic curtain, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns. With their friendship and courage put to the test, the girls must solve the puzzle before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
