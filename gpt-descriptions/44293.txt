Synopsis: Tom Fairfield, a young and adventurous boy, finds himself embarking on a thrilling summer camp adventure. Little does he know that this seemingly ordinary camp holds a mysterious secret tied to an old mill. As Tom and his friends uncover clues and face unexpected challenges, they must rely on their wit and courage to unravel the enigma and protect what lies within the old mill. With danger lurking around every corner, Tom Fairfield in Camp is a captivating tale of friendship, discovery, and the pursuit of truth.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Secrets
