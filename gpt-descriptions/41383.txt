Synopsis: 
'Punch and Judy, with Instructions How to Manage the Little Wooden Actors' is a comprehensive guidebook written by Thomas A. M. Ward that not only provides step-by-step instructions on how to work the puppets, but also includes new and easy dialogues for beginners. This book is specifically designed for individuals interested in learning the art of puppetry for various occasions such as Sunday schools, private parties, festivals, and parlor entertainments. 

Genres: 
Instructional, Puppetry, Entertainment

Themes: 
Art of puppetry, Performance, Education
