Synopsis:
Ptolemy's Tetrabiblos is a captivating and enlightening exploration of the influence of the stars on human life. Divided into four books, this ancient masterpiece delves into the intricate connections between celestial bodies and earthly affairs. With a preface, explanatory notes, and an appendix containing extracts from Ptolemy's Almagest and Centiloquy, as well as a fascinating insight into Mr. Ranger's zodiacal planisphere, this work offers a comprehensive understanding of astrology and its practical applications. Prepare to be enthralled by Ptolemy's profound observations and his ability to unravel the mysteries of the cosmos.

Genres:
Astrology, Philosophy, Science

Themes:
Celestial influence, Fate and destiny, Human nature
