Synopsis: 
"Through the first Antarctic night, 1898-1899" takes readers on a thrilling and perilous journey aboard the "Belgica," a ship venturing into uncharted territories near the South Pole. Led by the intrepid explorer Frederick Albert Cook, the crew faces treacherous conditions, encounters with unknown lands, and the challenges of surviving the unforgiving Antarctic winter. As they navigate through the darkness and isolation, the crew's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. This gripping narrative offers a captivating account of a historic voyage that pushes the boundaries of human exploration.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human resilience
