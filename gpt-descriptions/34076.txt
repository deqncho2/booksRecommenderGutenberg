Synopsis: 
'Fox Trapping: A Book of Instruction Telling How to Trap, Snare, Poison and Shoot: A Valuable Book for Trappers' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the art of fox trapping. Written by A. R. Harding, this instructional book provides detailed techniques and strategies for trapping, snaring, poisoning, and shooting foxes. Whether you are a seasoned trapper or a beginner, this valuable resource offers practical advice and insights to help you successfully catch these cunning creatures. From setting traps to understanding fox behavior, Harding's expertise shines through, making this book an essential tool for anyone interested in the world of trapping.

Genres: Non-fiction, Outdoor and Nature, Hunting

Themes: Trapping techniques, Wildlife management, Hunting strategies
