Synopsis: In 'The Rival Heirs; being the Third and Last Chronicle of Aescendune', the gripping conclusion to the Aescendune trilogy, the once peaceful village of Aescendune is once again thrown into turmoil as rival heirs emerge to claim their birthright. As the power struggle intensifies, long-held secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the fate of Aescendune hangs in the balance. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling characters, this final installment is a tale of love, betrayal, and redemption that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Power and inheritance, Loyalty and betrayal, Redemption and forgiveness
