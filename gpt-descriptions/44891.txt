Synopsis: 
'The Works of the Right Honourable John, Earl of Rochester: Consisting of Satires, Songs, Translations, and other Occasional Poems' is a collection of captivating and provocative writings by John Wilmot, Earl of Rochester. This anthology showcases a wide range of his literary talents, including satires that mercilessly critique society, heartfelt songs that explore the depths of human emotions, and translations that bring new life to classic works. With each poem, Rochester delves into the complexities of love, desire, power, and the human condition, leaving readers both enthralled and contemplative.

Genres: 
Satire, Poetry, Translation

Themes: 
Love, Desire, Society, Power, Human Condition
