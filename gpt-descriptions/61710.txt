Synopsis: 
"The Boy's Froissart" is a captivating collection of chronicles written by Sir John Froissart, recounting thrilling adventures, epic battles, and fascinating customs in England, France, Spain, and beyond. Through vivid storytelling, Froissart takes readers on a journey through the medieval world, filled with knights, chivalry, political intrigue, and the clash of empires. From the Hundred Years' War to the Black Death, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the tumultuous events that shaped Europe during the 14th and 15th centuries.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Chivalry, War and Conflict, Cultural Exchange
