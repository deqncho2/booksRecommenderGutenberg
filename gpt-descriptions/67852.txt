Synopsis: 
In 'Model Aeroplanes and Their Engines: A Practical Book for Beginners', author George Anthony Cavanagh takes readers on an exhilarating journey into the world of model aeroplanes. With detailed instructions and insightful tips, this book serves as the ultimate guide for those looking to delve into the fascinating hobby of building and flying model aeroplanes. From selecting the right materials to mastering the art of engine assembly, Cavanagh's expertise shines through, making this book an indispensable resource for beginners and enthusiasts alike. Prepare to be captivated by the thrill of flight and the joy of creating something truly remarkable.

Genres: Non-fiction, Hobby, How-to

Themes: Aviation, Engineering, Creativity
