Synopsis: In 'Early Man Projectile Points in the Southwest', Kenneth Honea takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient world of the Southwest, exploring the fascinating history and significance of projectile points. From the earliest known examples to the intricate designs of later periods, Honea delves into the craftsmanship, cultural context, and technological advancements that shaped these essential tools. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this book unveils the secrets of these ancient artifacts, shedding light on the lives and ingenuity of early humans in the Southwest.

Genres: Archaeology, Anthropology, History

Themes: Prehistoric technology, Cultural evolution, Material culture
