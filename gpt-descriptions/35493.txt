Synopsis: In the remote wilderness of the Canadian Rockies, a young boy named Ken discovers a mysterious black fawn, a rare and beautiful creature unlike anything he has ever seen before. As he forms an unlikely bond with the fawn, Ken embarks on a thrilling adventure to protect it from those who seek to exploit its uniqueness. With danger lurking at every turn, Ken must rely on his courage and resourcefulness to ensure the black fawn's safety and freedom.

Genres: Adventure, Wildlife, Coming-of-age

Themes: Conservation, Friendship, Courage
