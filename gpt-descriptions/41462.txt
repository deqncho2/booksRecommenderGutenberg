Synopsis: 
In 'Behind the Throne' by William Le Queux, a gripping tale of political intrigue and espionage unfolds as a young journalist stumbles upon a secret that could shake the foundations of the British monarchy. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of deception, where loyalties are tested and the truth becomes a matter of life and death. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous landscape of power, corruption, and hidden agendas, all while trying to protect those he holds dear. Will he uncover the truth behind the throne, or will he become just another pawn in a deadly game?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Political intrigue, Loyalty, Deception
