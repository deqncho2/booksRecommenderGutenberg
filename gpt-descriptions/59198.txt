Synopsis:
In 'The Seven Conundrums' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of perplexing mysteries. When a wealthy businessman is found dead under suspicious circumstances, the detective is called upon to unravel the truth. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a series of intricate puzzles, each more baffling than the last. With each conundrum, the detective's skills are put to the test, leading him on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As the stakes rise, he must race against time to solve the enigmatic puzzles before they claim more victims.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Intrigue, Puzzle-solving, Crime-solving
