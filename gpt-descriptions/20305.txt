Synopsis: In 'Marion's Faith' by Charles King, a young woman named Marion finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter that leads her on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's past. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Marion must confront her own beliefs and make difficult choices that will test her faith and resilience. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Family secrets, Faith and belief, Identity
