Synopsis: Marcy, a fearless and cunning young woman, finds herself caught in the midst of a dangerous game of espionage and adventure as she becomes a blockade runner during the Civil War. With her quick thinking and resourcefulness, Marcy navigates treacherous waters, evades capture, and outwits her enemies in a thrilling race against time. As she risks everything to fulfill her mission, Marcy discovers the true meaning of loyalty, bravery, and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Espionage

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival, Sacrifice
