Synopsis: 

'A Slave Girl's Story: Being an Autobiography of Kate Drumgoold' is a captivating memoir that takes readers on a remarkable journey through the life of Kate Drumgoold, a former slave. From her early years on a plantation to her eventual escape to freedom, Kate's story is one of resilience, courage, and the pursuit of a better life. Through her vivid and heartfelt narrative, she sheds light on the harsh realities of slavery, the challenges faced by African Americans during Reconstruction, and the power of education in overcoming adversity. As readers delve into Kate's captivating tale, they will be inspired by her strength and determination to forge her own path in a world that sought to confine her.

Genres: Autobiography, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Slavery, Freedom, Resilience, Education
