Synopsis: 
In 'The Wireless Officer' by Percy F. Westerman, a young wireless operator finds himself caught in the midst of a thrilling adventure when his ship is attacked by a mysterious enemy vessel. As he fights to survive and protect his crewmates, he uncovers a dangerous secret that could change the course of history. With high-stakes action, unexpected twists, and a race against time, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Technology, Intrigue
