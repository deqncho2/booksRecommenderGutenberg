Synopsis: In 'Tales of the Road' by Charles N. Crewdson, embark on a thrilling journey through the winding roads of America, where a mysterious hitchhiker encounters a series of captivating characters and uncovers hidden secrets along the way. As the hitchhiker's adventures unfold, the reader is taken on a rollercoaster ride of suspense, intrigue, and unexpected twists that will keep them on the edge of their seat until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Road Trip

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Fate
