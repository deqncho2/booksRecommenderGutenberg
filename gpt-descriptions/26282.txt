Synopsis: In "The Witch of Salem; or, Credulity Run Mad," John R. Musick takes readers on a thrilling journey to the infamous Salem witch trials. Set in the 17th century, this gripping tale follows the life of a young woman named Mercy, who finds herself caught in the midst of the hysteria and paranoia that grips the town. As accusations fly and the line between truth and deception blurs, Mercy must navigate a treacherous path to protect herself and those she holds dear. With its rich historical backdrop and a cast of intriguing characters, this novel delves into the dark depths of human nature and the consequences of unchecked fear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Witchcraft, Hysteria, Betrayal, Justice
