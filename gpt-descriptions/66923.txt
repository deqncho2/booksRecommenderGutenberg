Synopsis: 
'West African Folk-Tales' is a captivating collection of traditional stories that transport readers to the vibrant and enchanting world of West Africa. From cunning animals outsmarting humans to mythical creatures embarking on epic quests, these tales are filled with adventure, wisdom, and a touch of magic. Each story offers a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of West Africa, showcasing the values, beliefs, and traditions passed down through generations. With its vivid storytelling and colorful characters, this collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages and leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Wisdom, Morality, Cultural Heritage
