Synopsis: In "Information please" by Stanley Whiteside, a mysterious phone call sets off a chain of events that plunges a young woman into a world of secrets and danger. As she delves deeper into the enigmatic caller's cryptic messages, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that threatens to unravel her own life. With each clue she uncovers, the stakes rise higher, leading her on a thrilling journey of discovery and self-preservation. Will she be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Intrigue, Self-discovery
