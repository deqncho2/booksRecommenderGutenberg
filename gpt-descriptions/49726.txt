Synopsis: In 'The Works of Voltaire, Vol. IV of XLIII.: Romances, Vol. III of III, and A Treatise on Toleration,' Voltaire takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of his most intriguing romances and a thought-provoking treatise on the importance of religious tolerance. From tales of forbidden love and political intrigue to philosophical discussions on the nature of freedom and the dangers of fanaticism, this volume offers a rich tapestry of Voltaire's wit, wisdom, and literary prowess.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Philosophy

Themes: Love and Desire, Political Intrigue, Religious Tolerance, Freedom of Thought
