Synopsis: 'The Language of Flowers: The Floral Offering' by Henrietta Dumont is a captivating exploration of the symbolism and poetry behind flowers. This enchanting book delves into the hidden meanings and messages conveyed through different types of flowers, revealing the intricate language of flowers and their significance in human relationships. Through beautiful illustrations and poetic descriptions, Dumont invites readers to discover the profound emotions and sentiments that can be expressed through the simple act of giving flowers. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Poetry

Themes: Symbolism, Communication, Relationships
