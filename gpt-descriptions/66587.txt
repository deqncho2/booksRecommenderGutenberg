Synopsis: In 'Cliquot: A Racing Story of Ideal Beauty' by Ferguson, Kate Lee, follow the captivating journey of a spirited young filly named Cliquot as she defies all odds to become a champion racehorse. From her humble beginnings on a small farm to the glamorous world of horse racing, Cliquot's determination and unwavering spirit will inspire readers as they witness her rise to greatness. With thrilling races, unexpected twists, and a cast of unforgettable characters, this heartwarming tale explores the power of dreams, the bonds of friendship, and the beauty found in chasing one's passion.

Genres: Fiction, Sports, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Pursuit of Dreams
