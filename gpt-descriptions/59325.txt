Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Marguerite, Queen of Navarre' is a collection of works by Marguerite, Queen of Navarre, a prominent figure in French literature during the Renaissance. This compilation includes a variety of her writings, ranging from poetry and plays to novels and religious works. Dive into the captivating world of Marguerite, Queen of Navarre, and explore her diverse literary talents through this comprehensive index.

Genres: 
Poetry, Plays, Novels, Religious Works

Themes: 
Love, Power, Religion, Feminism
