Synopsis: In 'The Wailing Asteroid' by Murray Leinster, a mysterious asteroid appears in the sky, emitting an eerie wailing sound that drives people to madness. As chaos ensues, a group of scientists and government officials must race against time to uncover the truth behind the asteroid's origins and find a way to save humanity from its devastating effects. With suspenseful twists and unexpected discoveries, this thrilling science fiction novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Exploration of the Unknown, Human Resilience
