Synopsis: In 'Out of the Triangle: A Story of the Far East', Mary E. Bamford takes readers on a captivating journey through the exotic landscapes and vibrant cultures of the Far East. The story follows a young protagonist who finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue, as she navigates through the complexities of love, betrayal, and self-discovery. From the bustling streets of Shanghai to the serene temples of Kyoto, the narrative unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers on the edge of their seats. As the protagonist delves deeper into the secrets of the Far East, she must confront her own fears and confront the harsh realities of the world around her. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Cultural exploration, Identity, Betrayal, Self-discovery
