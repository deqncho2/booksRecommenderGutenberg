Synopsis: Masks, with Jim's beast, Tides, Among the lions, The reason, The house: one act plays of contemporary life is a collection of thought-provoking and emotionally charged one-act plays by George Middleton. Each play delves into the complexities of human relationships and the masks we wear in our daily lives. From the haunting exploration of a man's inner struggles to the tense dynamics within a family, these plays offer a glimpse into the hidden depths of contemporary life. With captivating dialogue and powerful storytelling, Middleton invites readers to question the facades we present to the world and the true nature of our existence.

Genres: Drama, Contemporary Fiction, Theatre

Themes: Identity, Deception, Family Dynamics, Inner Struggles, Human Relationships
