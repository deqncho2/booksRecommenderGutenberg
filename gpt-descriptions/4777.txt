Synopsis: In 'Strong as Death' by Guy de Maupassant, a passionate and forbidden love affair unfolds between a talented artist and a beautiful young woman. As their relationship intensifies, they find themselves entangled in a web of desire, jealousy, and obsession. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Paris, this gripping tale explores the depths of human emotions and the consequences of pursuing love at any cost.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Passion, Obsession, Jealousy
