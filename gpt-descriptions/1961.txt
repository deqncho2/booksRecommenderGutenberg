Synopsis: 
In "Books and Bookmen," Lang, Andrew takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of literature, exploring the lives of famous authors, the art of book collecting, and the enchanting stories behind beloved books. From the mysterious origins of ancient manuscripts to the eccentricities of bibliophiles, Lang delves into the fascinating realm of books and the people who cherish them. With wit, humor, and a deep love for literature, this collection of essays offers a delightful exploration of the enchanting world of books and the passionate individuals who dedicate their lives to them.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, Essays

Themes: Love for literature, Book collecting, History of books, Authorship, Bibliophilia
