Synopsis: 

'Behind the Scenes in a Hotel' takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden world of the hotel industry. Through meticulous research and firsthand accounts, the Consumers' League of New York City unveils the secrets and challenges faced by hotel workers, shedding light on the often overlooked aspects of this bustling industry. From the bustling front desk to the unseen corners of housekeeping, this eye-opening exploration reveals the untold stories of those who work tirelessly to ensure our comfort and satisfaction during our hotel stays.

Genres: Non-fiction, investigative journalism, social commentary

Themes: Labor rights, inequality, working conditions, consumer awareness
