Synopsis: In "Down among men" by Will Levington Comfort, a young man named John finds himself caught between the allure of the city and the simplicity of rural life. As he navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and ambition, John is faced with difficult choices that will shape his future. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century America, this captivating novel explores the clash between urban and rural lifestyles, and the profound impact it has on one man's journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Love and Relationships, Urban vs Rural Life
