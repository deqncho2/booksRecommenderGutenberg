Synopsis: In the final volume of the captivating Morley Ashton series, the protagonist finds himself embarking on a perilous journey across the treacherous seas. As he navigates through storms, encounters dangerous pirates, and faces unexpected challenges, Morley's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. With his life hanging in the balance, will he be able to overcome the odds and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Maritime

Themes: Survival, Perseverance, Exploration
