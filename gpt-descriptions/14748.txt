Synopsis: In 'For the Faith: A Story of the Young Pioneers of Reformation in Oxford' by Evelyn Everett-Green, follow the captivating journey of a group of young students in the historic city of Oxford during the tumultuous times of the Reformation. As they navigate the challenges of their faith, friendships, and the pursuit of knowledge, they find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger. With secrets to uncover and decisions to make, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, testing their loyalty and courage. Will they stand firm in their beliefs or succumb to the pressures of the time? This gripping tale of faith, friendship, and the pursuit of truth will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Christian Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Faith, Friendship, Reformation, Loyalty, Courage
