Synopsis: 
In the small coastal town of Seabrook, a mysterious artist named Amelia arrives and begins painting stunningly lifelike portraits of birds. As the townspeople become captivated by her artwork, they soon discover that these paintings hold a secret power that goes beyond their beauty. As the community delves deeper into the enigma surrounding Amelia and her work, they find themselves questioning their own perceptions of reality and the true nature of art. With each portrait, a new layer of intrigue is unveiled, leading to unexpected connections and revelations that will forever change the lives of those involved.

Genres: Mystery, Magical Realism, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Perception, Identity, Artistic Expression, Community, Secrets
