Synopsis: In 'The Queen Who Flew: A Fairy Tale' by Ford, Ford Madox, we are transported to a whimsical world where a queen discovers a hidden power within herself that allows her to soar through the skies. As she embarks on a thrilling journey, she encounters magical creatures, faces unexpected challenges, and learns valuable lessons about courage, self-discovery, and the true meaning of freedom. This enchanting tale will captivate readers with its imaginative storytelling and leave them yearning for their own wings to take flight.

Genres: Fantasy, Fairy Tale, Adventure

Themes: Self-discovery, Courage, Freedom
