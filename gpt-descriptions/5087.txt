Synopsis: In 'The Treasure-Train' by Arthur B. Reeve, a thrilling tale unfolds as a mysterious train carrying a priceless cargo disappears without a trace. Enter Craig Kennedy, a brilliant detective, and his trusty sidekick, Walter Jameson, as they embark on a high-stakes adventure to uncover the truth behind the vanishing treasure. As they delve deeper into the case, they encounter a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Treasure hunting, Detective work, Intrigue
