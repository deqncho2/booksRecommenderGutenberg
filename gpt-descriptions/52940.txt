Synopsis: In the second volume of "Life of John Knox," Thomas M'Crie delves deeper into the captivating history of the Reformation in Scotland. This edition provides a rich tapestry of illustrations that bring to life the struggles, triumphs, and complexities of this pivotal period. From the fiery sermons of John Knox to the political intrigues that shaped the fate of the nation, M'Crie's meticulous research and vivid storytelling paint a vivid picture of a nation in the throes of religious and political transformation. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the courage and conviction of those who fought for their beliefs, and the profound impact their actions had on the course of Scottish history.

Genres: Biography, History, Religious Studies

Themes: Reformation, Religious Freedom, Political Intrigue, Courage, Conviction
