Synopsis: 
'Original Short Stories, Complete, Volumes 1-13: An Index to All Stories' by Guy de Maupassant is a captivating collection of short stories that will transport readers to various settings and introduce them to a diverse cast of characters. From tales of love and betrayal to stories of mystery and suspense, each narrative is crafted with Maupassant's signature style, immersing readers in vivid imagery and thought-provoking situations. This comprehensive index serves as a gateway to Maupassant's literary world, enticing readers to explore the depths of human emotions and the complexities of the human condition.

Genres: 
Literary Fiction, Short Stories, Classic Literature

Themes: 
Love and Betrayal, Human Nature, Social Critique
