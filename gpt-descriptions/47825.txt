Synopsis: In 'Darkness and Dawn; Or, Scenes in the Days of Nero. An Historic Tale', Farrar takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous reign of Emperor Nero in ancient Rome. The story follows the lives of two young protagonists, Marcus and Helius, as they navigate the treacherous political landscape, face personal trials, and strive to find love and redemption amidst the darkness that engulfs their world. As the city burns and the empire crumbles, the characters must confront their deepest fears and make choices that will shape their destinies. This gripping tale of love, betrayal, and resilience offers a vivid portrayal of a pivotal period in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and Corruption, Redemption, Love and Sacrifice
