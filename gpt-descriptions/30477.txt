Synopsis: In 'The Sign of Silence' by William Le Queux, a mysterious murder takes place in a quiet English village, leaving the residents in shock and fear. As the investigation unfolds, a web of secrets and hidden motives is revealed, leading the protagonist on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Intrigue
