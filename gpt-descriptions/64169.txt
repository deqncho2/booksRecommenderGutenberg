Synopsis: In "Tom Newcombe; Or, the Boy of Bad Habits" by Castlemon, Harry, we follow the adventures of Tom, a young boy with a penchant for mischief and trouble. From his misadventures at school to his encounters with dangerous situations, Tom's journey is filled with excitement and unexpected twists. As he navigates through his bad habits and learns valuable life lessons, readers will be captivated by the thrilling escapades and the growth of this unforgettable character.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Juvenile Fiction

Themes: Redemption, Self-discovery, Consequences of actions
