Synopsis: In 'The Corner House Girls' Odd Find: Where they made it, and What the Strange Discovery led to' by Grace Brooks Hill, a group of adventurous young girls stumble upon a mysterious object hidden in the attic of their beloved corner house. As they embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the origins and purpose of this strange find, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and unexpected twists. With each clue they uncover, the girls are drawn deeper into a captivating mystery that will test their courage and friendship. Will they be able to solve the puzzle before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Discovery
