Synopsis: In 'One-Shot' by James Blish, a renowned scientist discovers a groundbreaking invention that has the potential to change the course of human history. However, as the world becomes aware of this revolutionary technology, it becomes clear that not everyone has the best intentions for its use. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, the scientist must navigate a treacherous landscape of power, greed, and moral dilemmas to protect his creation.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Human Nature
