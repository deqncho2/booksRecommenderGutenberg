Synopsis: In 'The Red Planet' by William John Locke, a young Englishman named Anthony Trent finds himself entangled in a thrilling adventure when he unexpectedly inherits a mysterious estate on the remote island of San Pietro. As he delves into the secrets of the island, Trent becomes captivated by the enigmatic beauty of a local woman and discovers a hidden world filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected twists. With the fate of the island hanging in the balance, Trent must navigate treacherous alliances and confront his own past to uncover the truth and save the people he has come to care for.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Love and Sacrifice, Power and Corruption
