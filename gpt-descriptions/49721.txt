Synopsis: 
'Poems' by George Santayana is a collection of beautifully crafted verses that explore the depths of human emotions, the wonders of nature, and the complexities of existence. From poignant reflections on love and loss to contemplations on the fleeting nature of time, these poems take readers on a lyrical journey through the profound and the mundane. With each line, Santayana invites readers to delve into the depths of their own souls and ponder the mysteries of life.

Genres: Poetry, Philosophy

Themes: Love, Nature, Time, Existence
