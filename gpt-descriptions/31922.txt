Synopsis: In 'Felony' by Causey, James, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and corruption when he is assigned to investigate a high-profile murder case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel the very fabric of society. With his own life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to bring the truth to light.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Corruption, Deception, Justice
