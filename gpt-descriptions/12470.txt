Synopsis: In 'A Perilous Secret' by Charles Reade, a young woman named Emily finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she discovers a hidden secret that could change her life forever. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she becomes the target of those who will stop at nothing to keep the truth buried. With her life on the line, Emily must navigate a treacherous path of deception and betrayal, unsure of who she can trust. Will she uncover the truth and survive the perilous secret?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Betrayal, Survival
