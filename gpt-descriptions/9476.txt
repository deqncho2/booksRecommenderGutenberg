Synopsis: 

In 'Ridgeway: An Historical Romance of the Fenian Invasion of Canada', Dubh Scian takes readers on a thrilling journey through a little-known chapter of Canadian history. Set during the Fenian Invasion of Canada in 1866, the story follows the lives of two young lovers, Liam and Fiona, who find themselves caught up in the chaos and danger of the conflict. As they navigate the treacherous landscape of war, their love is tested, friendships are forged, and sacrifices are made. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, the novel explores the complexities of loyalty, patriotism, and the resilience of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty, Patriotism, Resilience
