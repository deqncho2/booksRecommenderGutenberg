Synopsis: 
In 'Some Persons Unknown' by E. W. Hornung, a mysterious murder takes place in a quiet English village, leaving the locals baffled and fearful. As the investigation unfolds, a web of secrets, hidden identities, and unexpected alliances emerges, leading to a thrilling game of cat and mouse between the cunning detective and an elusive killer. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the dark underbelly of this seemingly idyllic community, where nothing is as it seems and everyone is a suspect.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Betrayal, Small-town secrets
