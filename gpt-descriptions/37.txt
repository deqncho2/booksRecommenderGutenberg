Synopsis: 

In "The 1990 United States Census [2nd]," the United States Bureau of the Census takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of one of the most significant population surveys in American history. With meticulous attention to detail, the Bureau unveils the challenges, triumphs, and surprising discoveries that unfolded during this groundbreaking census. From the innovative methodologies employed to the unexpected demographic shifts observed, this gripping account sheds light on the untold stories behind the numbers, revealing a nation in constant flux and transformation.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Social Sciences

Themes: Demographics, Population Trends, Social Change
