Synopsis: In 'The Human Aura: Astral Colors and Thought Forms', William Walker Atkinson delves into the fascinating world of the human aura, exploring the vibrant astral colors that surround us and the powerful thought forms we create. Through a combination of scientific research and metaphysical insights, Atkinson unveils the hidden mysteries of the aura, revealing its profound influence on our physical, mental, and spiritual well-being. As he unravels the secrets of this ethereal energy field, the reader is taken on a captivating journey of self-discovery and enlightenment, discovering the incredible potential that lies within each individual.

Genres: Metaphysics, Spiritualism, Self-help

Themes: Aura, Energy, Consciousness, Personal Growth
