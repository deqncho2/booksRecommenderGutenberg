Synopsis: In 'My Lords of Strogue, Vol. 3 (of 3): A Chronicle of Ireland, from the Convention to the Union' by Wingfield, Lewis, the captivating conclusion to the series takes readers on a tumultuous journey through the political landscape of Ireland. As the Convention debates the future of the nation, the Lords of Strogue find themselves caught in a web of intrigue, power struggles, and personal sacrifices. Amidst the backdrop of historical events, secrets are unveiled, loyalties are tested, and the fate of Ireland hangs in the balance. Will the Lords of Strogue be able to navigate the treacherous waters of politics and secure a prosperous future for their beloved country?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Romance

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, National Identity
