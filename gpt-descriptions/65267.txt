Synopsis: 

'Rebels and Reformers: Biographies for Young People' is a captivating collection of biographies written by Arthur Ponsonby, Baron Ponsonby, and Dorothea Ponsonby. This book introduces young readers to the lives of remarkable individuals who have challenged the status quo and fought for change. From political activists to social reformers, each biography offers a glimpse into the struggles and triumphs of these inspiring figures. Through engaging storytelling and vivid descriptions, the authors bring to life the stories of rebels and reformers who have left an indelible mark on history.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Activism, Social Change, Courage, Empowerment
