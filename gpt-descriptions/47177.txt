Synopsis: In 'The Sunbonnet Babies in Italy' by Grover, Eulalie Osgood, join the adorable Sunbonnet Babies on a delightful adventure as they explore the enchanting country of Italy. From the bustling streets of Rome to the picturesque canals of Venice, the babies immerse themselves in the rich culture, delicious food, and breathtaking sights of this captivating land. Along the way, they encounter friendly locals, encounter famous landmarks, and even stumble upon a hidden treasure. With charming illustrations and a heartwarming storyline, this book is sure to transport readers of all ages to the magical world of Italy.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book

Themes: Travel and Exploration, Cultural Appreciation
