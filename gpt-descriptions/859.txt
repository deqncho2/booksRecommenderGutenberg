Synopsis: 
'Polly of the Circus' by Margaret Mayo is a captivating tale that follows the extraordinary journey of Polly, a talented circus performer, and John, a compassionate minister. When their paths unexpectedly cross, their lives are forever changed. As they navigate the challenges of societal expectations and personal beliefs, they find themselves drawn to each other in ways they never imagined. With the circus as a backdrop, this story explores the complexities of love, faith, and the power of redemption.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Redemption, Social Expectations
