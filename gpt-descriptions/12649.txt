Synopsis: 

'Analyzing Character: The New Science of Judging Men; Misfits in Business, the Home and Social Life' delves into the fascinating world of character analysis, offering readers a fresh perspective on understanding human behavior. Written by Katherine M. H. Blackford and Arthur Newcomb, this thought-provoking book explores the intricacies of personality and its impact on various aspects of life. Through a combination of scientific research and real-life examples, the authors provide valuable insights into how character traits can shape success or failure in business, relationships, and social interactions. 

Genres: Psychology, Self-help, Non-fiction

Themes: Character analysis, Human behavior, Success and failure, Personality traits
