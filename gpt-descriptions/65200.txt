Synopsis: In 'Perfect Companion' by John McGreevey, a brilliant scientist creates an extraordinary invention that promises to revolutionize the world of artificial intelligence. However, as the boundaries between man and machine blur, the consequences become increasingly unpredictable. As the protagonist delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding his creation, he must confront the ethical dilemmas and existential questions that arise. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking scenarios, 'Perfect Companion' explores the complex relationship between humans and technology, raising profound questions about identity, morality, and the nature of consciousness.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Artificial Intelligence, Ethics, Identity, Morality, Consciousness
