Synopsis:
In 'Frank Merriwell's Endurance; or, A Square Shooter' by Standish, Burt L., we follow the thrilling adventures of Frank Merriwell, a young and talented athlete who finds himself facing a series of unexpected challenges. As Frank navigates through a web of deceit, betrayal, and danger, he must rely on his endurance and unwavering determination to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way. With his reputation and future on the line, Frank's journey takes him to the edge of his physical and mental limits, testing his character and forcing him to make difficult choices. Will Frank's endurance be enough to triumph over the forces working against him?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Sports

Themes: Perseverance, Integrity, Betrayal
