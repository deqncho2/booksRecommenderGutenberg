Synopsis: In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 147, August 19th, 1914', readers are transported to the tumultuous time of World War I, where the satirical magazine Punch provides a unique perspective on the events unfolding in London. Through witty cartoons, humorous articles, and clever commentary, this volume captures the spirit of the era and offers a lighthearted yet thought-provoking take on the war and its impact on society.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Political Commentary

Themes: War, Society, Patriotism
