Synopsis: 
'A Chronological Table of the Catholic Primates of Ireland: With the Years in Which They Succeeded to the Metropolitan Sees of Armagh, Dublin, Cashell and Tuam' by John Murphy is a meticulously researched and comprehensive guide that traces the succession of Catholic primates in Ireland. From the earliest recorded primates to the present day, this book provides a detailed account of the individuals who held these influential positions and the years in which they succeeded to the metropolitan sees of Armagh, Dublin, Cashell, and Tuam. 

Genres: Historical, Reference

Themes: Catholic Church history, Ecclesiastical hierarchy, Irish religious heritage
