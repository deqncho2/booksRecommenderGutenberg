Synopsis: 
In 'The Fool Errant: Being the Memoirs of Francis-Anthony Strelley, Esq., Citizen of Lucca', we follow the captivating journey of Francis-Anthony Strelley, a young man with a thirst for adventure and a penchant for getting into trouble. From his humble beginnings in Lucca, Italy, to his encounters with eccentric characters and his escapades across Europe, Strelley's memoirs offer a delightful blend of humor, romance, and unexpected twists. As he navigates through various misadventures and personal growth, readers will be drawn into a world of wit, charm, and the unpredictable nature of life.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Comedy

Themes: Coming of Age, Identity, Love and Relationships, Self-Discovery
