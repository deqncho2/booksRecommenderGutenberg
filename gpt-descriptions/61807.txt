Synopsis: In 'Psychomancy: Spirit-Rappings and Table-Tippings Exposed', Charles Grafton Page delves into the mysterious world of spiritualism, uncovering the truth behind the popular practices of spirit-rappings and table-tippings. With a keen eye for detail and a skeptical mind, Page investigates the methods used by mediums and exposes the tricks and deceptions behind these supernatural phenomena. As he unravels the secrets of psychomancy, the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the realm of the occult, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Investigative

Themes: Skepticism, Deception, Supernatural phenomena
