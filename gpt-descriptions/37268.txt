Synopsis: In 'Hot Corn: Life Scenes in New York Illustrated', Robinson, Solon takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling streets of New York City. Through a series of vividly depicted scenes, the author introduces us to a diverse cast of characters, each with their own unique stories and struggles. From the vibrant markets to the dimly lit alleyways, the narrative unfolds with a mix of humor, heartbreak, and unexpected twists. As we delve deeper into the lives of these individuals, we witness the harsh realities of urban life and the resilience of the human spirit. 'Hot Corn' offers a captivating glimpse into the vibrant tapestry of New York City, where dreams are pursued, fortunes are made and lost, and the pursuit of happiness takes on many forms.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Urban Fiction

Themes: Urban life, Human resilience, Pursuit of happiness
