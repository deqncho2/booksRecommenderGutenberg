Synopsis: 
In 'Ruby Roland, the Girl Spy; or, Simon Kenton's Protege' by Frederick Whittaker, readers are introduced to the captivating world of espionage and adventure. Set in the backdrop of the American frontier, the story follows Ruby Roland, a young and fearless girl who becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and intrigue. As she navigates through treacherous terrain and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Ruby's skills as a spy are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Espionage, Courage, Identity
