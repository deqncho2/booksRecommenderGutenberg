Synopsis: 
In "Falling in Love; With Other Essays on More Exact Branches of Science," Allen Grant takes readers on a captivating journey through the realms of science and human emotions. Through a series of thought-provoking essays, he explores the intricate connections between love, attraction, and the laws of nature. From the chemistry of romance to the physics of desire, Allen delves into the scientific explanations behind the mysteries of the heart. With a blend of scientific rigor and poetic prose, he uncovers the hidden forces that shape our relationships and offers a fresh perspective on the age-old question of what it means to fall in love.

Genres: Science, Philosophy, Essay Collection

Themes: Love, Science, Human Emotions, Relationships, Nature
