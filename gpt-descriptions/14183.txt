Synopsis: In 'There is No Harm in Dancing' by Penn, W. E., a young woman named Lily finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires when she discovers a hidden passion for dance. As she navigates the challenges of pursuing her dreams, Lily must confront the judgments of others and confront her own fears. With the support of unexpected allies, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment that will change her life forever.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Romance, Drama

Themes: Self-discovery, Pursuing passions, Overcoming societal expectations
