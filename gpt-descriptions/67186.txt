Synopsis: In "The Rider of the Mohave: A Western Story" by James Fellom, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Mohave, bringing with him a dark past and a hidden agenda. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the town's residents find themselves caught in a web of danger and deception. With thrilling gunfights, unexpected alliances, and a relentless pursuit of justice, this gripping Western tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Action, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Justice
