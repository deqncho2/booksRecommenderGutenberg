Synopsis: In 'Dorothy Dale's School Rivals' by Margaret Penrose, Dorothy finds herself caught in a web of mystery and rivalry as she enters a new school. With her quick wit and determination, she must navigate through a series of unexpected events and uncover the truth behind her school rivals. As friendships are tested and secrets are revealed, Dorothy's resilience and courage are put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to overcome the challenges and emerge victorious?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Competition, Resilience
