Synopsis: In 'Bermuda Houses' by John S. Humphreys, a young couple embarks on a journey to the enchanting island of Bermuda, seeking solace and a fresh start. As they settle into their new home, they soon discover that their quaint house holds a mysterious secret, one that will challenge their beliefs and push the boundaries of their reality. With each passing day, the couple becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, uncovering hidden truths about the island's history and the house's previous occupants. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they must confront their own fears and confront the haunting presence that lurks within the walls of their Bermuda house.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of belief, the thin line between reality and the supernatural
