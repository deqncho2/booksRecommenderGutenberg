Synopsis: 
In "Unlucky: A Fragment of a Girl's Life" by Caroline Austin, readers are introduced to a young girl named Lily who seems to be plagued by a series of unfortunate events. From a mysterious accident to a string of misfortunes, Lily's life takes a dark and unexpected turn. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the reader is left questioning the true nature of luck and the power of fate. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Coming-of-age

Themes: Fate, Luck, Secrets, Resilience
