Synopsis: In the Heart of a Fool takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a young African American man named Sandy. Born into poverty and facing numerous challenges, Sandy's determination and resilience lead him on a path of self-discovery and personal growth. From his humble beginnings in the rural South to his encounters with love, loss, and the complexities of race in America, Sandy's story is a powerful exploration of identity, social injustice, and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Identity, Racism, Social Injustice, Resilience
