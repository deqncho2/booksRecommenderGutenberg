Synopsis: In "Frederick the Great and His Court," Luise Mühlbach takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous and opulent court of Frederick the Great, the renowned King of Prussia. Amidst the backdrop of political intrigue, power struggles, and lavish festivities, Mühlbach weaves a tale of love, loyalty, and sacrifice that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. As the king navigates the treacherous waters of European politics, his courtiers and confidantes find themselves entangled in their own personal dramas, testing their loyalties and challenging their beliefs. From the grand halls of the palace to the battlefields of war, this historical novel offers a vivid and immersive portrayal of one of Europe's most fascinating periods.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Love and Sacrifice
