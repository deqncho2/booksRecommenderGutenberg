Synopsis: 
'Colors of Life: Poems and Songs and Sonnets' by Eastman, Max is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through the vibrant tapestry of human emotions and experiences. From the depths of sorrow to the heights of joy, these verses explore the complexities of life, love, and the human spirit. With lyrical beauty and profound insights, Eastman weaves together a tapestry of words that will resonate with readers and leave them contemplating the myriad of colors that make up the human experience.

Genres: Poetry, Songwriting, Sonnets

Themes: Life, Love, Emotions, Human Experience
