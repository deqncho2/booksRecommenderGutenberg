Synopsis: In 'Aunt Jane's Nieces at Work', three young and spirited nieces find themselves embarking on a new adventure as they enter the world of business. Facing challenges and unexpected twists, they must navigate the complexities of the corporate world while staying true to their values and maintaining their close bond. As they strive for success, they discover the power of determination, friendship, and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Female Empowerment, Family Bonds
