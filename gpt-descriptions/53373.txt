Synopsis: 
'A Manual of Historic Ornament' by Richard Glazier is a comprehensive guide that explores the evolution, tradition, and development of architecture and other applied arts. Designed specifically for students and craftsmen, this book delves into the intricate details of various ornamental styles throughout history, providing valuable insights and inspiration for those interested in the field. From ancient civilizations to modern times, Glazier takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of ornamentation, showcasing the rich cultural heritage and artistic achievements of different periods and regions.

Genres: 
Art History, Architecture, Design

Themes: 
Evolution of Art, Cultural Heritage, Craftsmanship
