Synopsis: 
'The Sleeping Beauty and other fairy tales from the Old French' is a captivating collection of enchanting tales that transport readers to a world of magic, romance, and adventure. From the timeless story of Sleeping Beauty to lesser-known tales of brave knights and cunning fairies, this book offers a delightful journey through the rich tapestry of Old French folklore. Each story is filled with captivating characters, unexpected twists, and moral lessons that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Classic Literature

Themes: Love and Romance, Good vs. Evil, Fate and Destiny
