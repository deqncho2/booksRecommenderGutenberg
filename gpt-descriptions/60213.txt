Synopsis: 
'Notre Dame de Paris: A Short History & Description of the Cathedral, With Some Account of the Churches Which Preceded It' by Hiatt, Charles takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and architectural marvels of the iconic Notre Dame Cathedral. From its humble beginnings to its grandeur, the book delves into the intricate details of the cathedral's construction, exploring the churches that came before it. With vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, Hiatt paints a vivid picture of the cathedral's significance and the cultural impact it has had over the centuries.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Architecture

Themes: Cultural heritage, Religious symbolism, Architectural significance
