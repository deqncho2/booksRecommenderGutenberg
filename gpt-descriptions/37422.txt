Synopsis: In 'The Life and Death of Doctor Faustus Made into a Farce', Marlowe and Mountfort take the classic tale of Doctor Faustus and transform it into a hilarious and satirical farce. The story follows the ambitious and cunning Doctor Faustus as he makes a pact with the devil, but instead of the usual tragic consequences, the authors present a comical twist that will keep readers laughing and guessing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Farce

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Supernatural, Deception
