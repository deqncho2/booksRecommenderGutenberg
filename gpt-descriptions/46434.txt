Synopsis: In 'Our Part in the Great War' by Arthur Gleason, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of World War I, where the lives of two young Americans are forever changed. As they navigate the horrors of war and the complexities of love, their paths intertwine with historical events that will shape the course of their lives. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this gripping tale explores the sacrifices, resilience, and unexpected bonds forged in the crucible of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Romance

Themes: Sacrifice, Resilience, Love and Loss, Friendship, Historical Events
