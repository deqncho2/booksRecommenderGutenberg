Synopsis: In 'Venusian Invader' by Larry Sternig, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious visitor from Venus arrives. As the townspeople grapple with the implications of this extraterrestrial presence, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and desires. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, 'Venusian Invader' takes readers on a thrilling journey that explores the complexities of human nature and the mysteries of the universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Alien encounters, Fear and curiosity, Human nature, Existentialism
