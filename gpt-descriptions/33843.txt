Synopsis: In 'The Natives' by Katherine MacLean, a group of scientists embarks on a groundbreaking mission to study an alien civilization on a distant planet. As they immerse themselves in the natives' culture, they uncover astonishing secrets that challenge their understanding of humanity and the universe. With each discovery, the scientists find themselves questioning their own identities and the very nature of what it means to be human. As tensions rise and the line between observer and participant blurs, they must confront the consequences of their actions and make choices that will forever alter their lives.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of alien cultures, Identity, Ethics
