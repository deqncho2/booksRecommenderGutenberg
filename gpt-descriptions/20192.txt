Synopsis:
In "Orrain: A Romance" by Levett Yeats, S. (Sidney), readers are transported to a captivating world where love, mystery, and adventure intertwine. The story follows the enigmatic Orrain, a young woman with a mysterious past and an extraordinary gift. As she navigates through a society filled with secrets and hidden agendas, Orrain finds herself entangled in a web of forbidden love and dangerous alliances. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into Orrain's world, where nothing is as it seems and the line between reality and fantasy becomes blurred. As the story unfolds, the reader is left questioning the true nature of love, the power of destiny, and the lengths one will go to protect those they hold dear.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Love, Identity, Destiny, Secrets
