Synopsis: In 'The Image and the Likeness' by John Scott Campbell, a renowned artist discovers a mysterious painting that seems to possess an uncanny power to bring its subjects to life. As he delves deeper into the origins of the artwork, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and dark forces that threaten to consume him and those around him. With each stroke of his brush, the line between reality and imagination blurs, leading to a thrilling and unexpected climax.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Artistic obsession, the power of creation, the thin line between reality and illusion
