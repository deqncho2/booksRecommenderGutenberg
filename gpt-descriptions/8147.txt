Synopsis: Two adventurous British ex-soldiers, Daniel Dravot and Peachey Carnehan, set out on a daring quest to become kings in a remote and mysterious land. With their cunning and charisma, they manage to gain the trust and admiration of the locals, but their newfound power comes at a cost they never anticipated. As their grand ambitions unravel, the true nature of their characters is put to the ultimate test, leading to a gripping and unexpected climax.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Power, Colonialism, Identity
