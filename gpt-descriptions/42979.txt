Synopsis: In 'Pomo Bear Doctors' by S. A. Barrett, a young anthropologist named Emma embarks on a journey to a remote village in Northern California, where she encounters a mysterious tribe known as the Pomo. As she delves deeper into their culture and traditions, Emma discovers a hidden world of ancient healing practices and shamanic rituals involving bears. With each encounter, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and supernatural phenomena that challenge her understanding of reality. As Emma's curiosity grows, she must navigate the delicate balance between preserving the Pomo's sacred knowledge and the consequences of revealing their secrets to the outside world.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Cultural exploration, Indigenous spirituality, the clash between tradition and modernity
