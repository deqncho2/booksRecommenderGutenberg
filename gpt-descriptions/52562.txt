Synopsis: In 'The Unbidden Guest' by E. W. Hornung, a mysterious and unexpected visitor arrives at the doorstep of a wealthy family, disrupting their seemingly perfect lives. As the guest's true intentions remain shrouded in secrecy, tensions rise and dark secrets are unearthed, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful tale of deception, betrayal, and unexpected consequences. With each passing page, the reader is drawn deeper into the web of intrigue, questioning the motives of every character and eagerly anticipating the shocking revelations that lie ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Secrets, Consequences
