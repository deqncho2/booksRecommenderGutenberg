Synopsis: In 'Love Instigated: The Story of a Carved Ivory Umbrella Handle' by Sherley Douglass, a mysterious and intricately carved ivory umbrella handle becomes the catalyst for a captivating tale of love, passion, and hidden secrets. As the handle changes hands over the years, it weaves together the lives of its owners, revealing a web of interconnected stories that span generations and continents. From the bustling streets of Paris to the exotic landscapes of Africa, this enchanting novel takes readers on a journey filled with romance, intrigue, and the power of human connection.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love, Fate, Identity, Art
