Synopsis: In 'A Little Girl in Old St. Louis' by Amanda M. Douglas, readers are transported to the vibrant city of St. Louis in the late 19th century, where they follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Edna. Through her eyes, we witness the bustling streets, the grandeur of the World's Fair, and the challenges faced by a spirited girl growing up in a rapidly changing world. As Edna navigates the complexities of family, friendship, and societal expectations, she discovers the power of resilience and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Social Change
