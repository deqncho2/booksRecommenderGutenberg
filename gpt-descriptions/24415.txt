Synopsis: In 'Proud and Lazy: A Story for Little Folks' by Optic, Oliver, we follow the adventures of two young siblings, Lucy and Harry, as they navigate the challenges of pride and laziness. When their parents go away for the weekend, Lucy and Harry find themselves in charge of the household chores. However, their differing attitudes towards responsibility lead to unexpected consequences and valuable life lessons. As they learn the importance of humility and hard work, the siblings embark on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Moral Tale

Themes: Pride, Laziness, Responsibility, Humility, Personal Growth
