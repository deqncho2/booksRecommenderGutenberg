Synopsis: In "Meridiana: The Adventures of Three Englishmen and Three Russians: In South Africa" by Jules Verne, a group of six men find themselves stranded in the vast and treacherous landscapes of South Africa. As they navigate through dangerous encounters with wild animals, hostile tribes, and the unforgiving elements, they must rely on their wit, courage, and teamwork to survive. Along the way, they uncover hidden treasures, encounter mysterious phenomena, and face moral dilemmas that test their character. This thrilling adventure takes readers on a captivating journey filled with suspense, danger, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Cultural Differences
