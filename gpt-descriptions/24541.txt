Synopsis: 

'Between the Lines: Secret Service Stories Told Fifty Years After' takes readers on a thrilling journey into the covert world of the Secret Service through the eyes of Henry Bascom Smith, a former agent. In this captivating memoir, Smith recounts his experiences and encounters with danger, intrigue, and high-stakes missions during his time in the service. From protecting world leaders to infiltrating criminal organizations, Smith's stories provide a rare glimpse into the hidden world of those who dedicate their lives to safeguarding the nation's secrets. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be drawn into a web of suspense and mystery, as Smith's tales reveal the hidden truths that lie between the lines of official reports and public knowledge.

Genres: Memoir, Thriller, Non-fiction

Themes: Espionage, Duty and Sacrifice, Loyalty, National Security
