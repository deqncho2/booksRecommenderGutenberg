Synopsis: 
In 'Pawned' by Frank L. Packard, a down-on-his-luck detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a pawnshop robbery gone wrong. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a sinister plot that reaches far beyond the stolen goods, leading him on a thrilling chase through the seedy underbelly of the city. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous game of cat and mouse, where nothing is as it seems and trust is a luxury he can't afford.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Deception
