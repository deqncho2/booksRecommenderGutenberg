Synopsis: In "The People of Palestine: An enlarged edition of "The Peasantry of Palestine, Life, Manners and Customs of the Village," Elihu Grant takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and complex world of Palestine. Through vivid descriptions and intimate encounters, Grant delves into the lives of the people, their traditions, and the challenges they face. From the bustling markets of Jerusalem to the serene olive groves of the countryside, this enlarged edition offers a rich tapestry of stories and insights that will leave readers captivated and enlightened.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travelogue, Cultural Studies

Themes: Cultural identity, Tradition, Socio-economic challenges
