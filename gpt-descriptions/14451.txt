Synopsis: 
In "African Camp Fires," Stewart Edward White takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of Africa. Through a series of thrilling and heartwarming tales, White shares his experiences of living among the native tribes, encountering dangerous wildlife, and navigating the challenges of the African landscape. From heart-stopping encounters with lions and elephants to heartwarming interactions with the local people, this collection of stories paints a vivid picture of the beauty, danger, and wonder of Africa.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Exchange, Wildlife Conservation
