Synopsis: 

'Portraits of Dr. William Harvey' takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and achievements of one of the most influential figures in the history of medicine. This collection of portraits, curated by the Royal Society of Medicine, offers a unique glimpse into the mind of a brilliant physician who revolutionized our understanding of the human circulatory system. From his groundbreaking discoveries to his struggles against societal norms, this book unveils the untold stories behind Dr. William Harvey's remarkable legacy.

Genres: Biography, History, Medicine

Themes: Scientific discovery, Medical revolution, Societal norms
