Synopsis: In volume 07 of "The Historians' History of the World," delve into the captivating and tumultuous era of the later Roman Empire. Witness the rise and fall of emperors, the struggles for power, and the intricate web of political intrigue that shaped this pivotal period. From the grandeur of Rome to the distant frontiers, this volume unveils the complex dynamics that ultimately led to the empire's decline. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, explore the fascinating world of the later Roman Empire and its lasting impact on history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Power struggles, Political intrigue, Decline and fall of empires, Legacy of the Roman Empire
