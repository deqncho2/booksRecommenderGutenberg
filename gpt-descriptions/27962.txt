Synopsis: In 'One Snowy Night: Long ago at Oxford' by Emily Sarah Holt, a young woman named Lucy finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret at Oxford University. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Lucy uncovers a centuries-old conspiracy that threatens to shake the very foundations of the prestigious institution. With the help of an unlikely ally, she must navigate the treacherous world of academia and unravel the truth before it's too late. This thrilling tale of secrets and betrayal will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Academic Intrigue
