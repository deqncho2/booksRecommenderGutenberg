Synopsis: In "Fables for the Frivolous," Guy Wetmore Carryl presents a collection of witty and humorous fables that cleverly satirize various aspects of society. From talking animals to absurd situations, these fables explore the follies and foibles of human nature in a lighthearted and entertaining manner. Each fable offers a unique moral lesson, often with a twist, leaving readers both amused and contemplative.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Fiction

Themes: Human nature, Society, Morality
