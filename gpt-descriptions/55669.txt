Synopsis: In 'The Brighton Boys at St. Mihiel' by James R. Driscoll, a group of young American soldiers find themselves thrust into the heart of World War I as they join the fight in the historic Battle of St. Mihiel. As they navigate the treacherous trenches and face the horrors of war, the Brighton Boys must rely on their courage, camaraderie, and resourcefulness to survive and fulfill their duty. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys' determination and resilience are put to the ultimate test in this gripping tale of bravery and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Sacrifice, Coming of Age
