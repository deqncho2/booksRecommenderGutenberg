Synopsis: In 'The White Gauntlet' by Reid, Mayne, a young man finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he inherits a family estate with a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the history of the property, he uncovers a series of chilling events that threaten his very existence. With the help of a courageous young woman, he must navigate treacherous paths and face formidable adversaries to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Courage, Survival
