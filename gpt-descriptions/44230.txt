Synopsis: 

In 'On Yachts and Yacht Handling' by Thomas Fleming Day, embark on an exhilarating journey into the world of yachting, where the author shares his extensive knowledge and experiences with passion and expertise. From the basics of yacht handling to advanced techniques, this book serves as a comprehensive guide for both novice and seasoned sailors. With vivid descriptions and practical advice, Day takes readers on a captivating voyage, exploring the intricacies of yacht navigation, sail trimming, and maneuvering in various weather conditions. As the author delves into the art of yacht handling, readers will find themselves immersed in a thrilling narrative that combines technical expertise with the sheer joy of sailing.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Adventure

Themes: Yachting, Seamanship, Skill Development
