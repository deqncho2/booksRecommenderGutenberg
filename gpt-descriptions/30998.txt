Synopsis: In 'A New Bat (Genus Pipistrellus) from Northeastern Mexico', Baker takes us on a captivating journey to the remote corners of Mexico, where he stumbles upon a remarkable discovery. As he unravels the mystery surrounding a new bat species, the narrative unfolds with suspense and intrigue, leaving readers on the edge of their seats. With vivid descriptions of the Mexican landscape and the fascinating world of bats, Baker's narrative is a thrilling exploration of the unknown.

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, Natural History

Themes: Discovery, Biodiversity, Exploration
