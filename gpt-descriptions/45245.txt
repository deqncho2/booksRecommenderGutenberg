Synopsis: In 'The Long Patrol: A Tale of the Mounted Police', author Cody, H. A. takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the rugged Canadian wilderness. Set in the late 19th century, this gripping adventure follows a group of courageous Mounties as they navigate treacherous terrain, face dangerous outlaws, and strive to uphold justice in the untamed frontier. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this tale of honor, loyalty, and sacrifice will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Courage, Duty, Wilderness Survival
