Synopsis: In "Augusta Tabor: Her Side of the Scandal," Bancroft, Caroline delves into the captivating life of Augusta Tabor, a woman who defied societal norms and became a prominent figure in the tumultuous mining era of the American West. From her humble beginnings to her rise as a wealthy and influential woman, Augusta's story is one of ambition, love, and scandal. As secrets unravel and alliances shift, readers will be drawn into a world of power struggles, forbidden desires, and the indomitable spirit of a woman determined to carve her own path.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biographical Fiction, Western

Themes: Gender Roles, Social Status, Love and Betrayal, Ambition, Resilience
