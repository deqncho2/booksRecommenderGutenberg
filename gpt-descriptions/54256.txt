Synopsis: In 'Frank Reade Jr.'s Air Wonder, The "Kite"; Or, A Six Weeks' Flight Over the Andes', readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as Frank Reade Jr. embarks on a daring expedition to explore the majestic Andes Mountains. Equipped with his incredible invention, the "Kite," a remarkable airship, Frank and his companions soar through the skies, encountering breathtaking landscapes, treacherous weather conditions, and unexpected challenges. As they navigate through the awe-inspiring beauty and perilous terrain of the Andes, the team must rely on their ingenuity, courage, and resourcefulness to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. Will they successfully complete their mission and uncover the secrets hidden within the mountains? Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Steampunk Themes: Exploration, Invention, Perseverance
