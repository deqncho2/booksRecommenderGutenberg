Synopsis: In 'Square and Compasses; Or, Building the House' by Optic, Oliver, a young man named Frank enters the world of Freemasonry, a secret society that holds ancient rituals and traditions. As Frank progresses through the ranks, he uncovers hidden truths and faces unexpected challenges that test his loyalty and integrity. With each step, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue, leading him to question his own beliefs and the true purpose of the organization. This captivating tale takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with secrets, symbolism, and self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden knowledge, Loyalty and betrayal, Self-discovery
