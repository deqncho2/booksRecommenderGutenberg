Synopsis: In 'Wau-nan-gee; Or, the Massacre at Chicago: A Romance of the American Revolution', Major Richardson takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of the American frontier during the Revolutionary War. The story follows the adventures of Captain Walter Colburn, a brave and resourceful soldier, as he navigates treacherous landscapes, encounters Native American tribes, and faces the horrors of war. Amidst the chaos and violence, a forbidden love blossoms, adding an element of passion and intrigue to the narrative. As the plot unfolds, readers are kept on the edge of their seats, wondering what fate has in store for Captain Colburn and the people he holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: War and Conflict, Love and Forbidden Desire, Survival and Resilience
