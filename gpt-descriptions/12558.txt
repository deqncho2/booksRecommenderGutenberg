Synopsis: In 'Snarleyyow, or, the Dog Fiend' by Frederick Marryat, a mischievous and cunning dog named Snarleyyow wreaks havoc on a British naval ship during the Napoleonic Wars. As the crew battles their enemies at sea, they must also contend with the unpredictable and dangerous antics of this canine troublemaker. With a mix of humor, adventure, and suspense, this tale takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Comedy

Themes: Loyalty, Survival, Man vs. Nature
