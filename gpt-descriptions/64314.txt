Synopsis: 

In 'Beyond the X Ecliptic' by Holden, Fox B., a brilliant astrophysicist named Dr. Amelia Collins discovers a mysterious anomaly in the far reaches of space that defies all known laws of physics. As she embarks on a perilous journey to unravel the secrets of this enigma, she finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, where the fate of humanity hangs in the balance. With unexpected allies and formidable adversaries, Dr. Collins must navigate through treacherous cosmic landscapes and confront her own inner demons to uncover the truth that lies beyond the X ecliptic.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the power of knowledge, the fragility of existence
