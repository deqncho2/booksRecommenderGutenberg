Synopsis: 

'China and Pottery Marks' takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate world of antique ceramics, unveiling the secrets behind the mysterious marks found on these delicate treasures. From ancient dynasties to modern-day collectors, this book delves into the fascinating history, craftsmanship, and cultural significance of Chinese pottery. As the protagonist unravels the enigmatic symbols and hidden stories behind each mark, they embark on a quest that intertwines art, history, and a touch of mystery. With each chapter, the reader is transported to different eras, encountering renowned artisans, uncovering long-lost techniques, and unearthing the untold tales behind these exquisite pieces. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Art

Themes: Artistic craftsmanship, Cultural heritage, Historical exploration
