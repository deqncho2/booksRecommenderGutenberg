Synopsis: In 'History of the English People, Volume VIII: Modern England, 1760-1815', John Richard Green takes readers on a captivating journey through a transformative period in English history. From the rise of industrialization to the tumultuous events of the French Revolution and the Napoleonic Wars, Green delves into the political, social, and cultural changes that shaped the nation during this era. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, he explores the lives of both ordinary people and influential figures, shedding light on the struggles, triumphs, and complexities of this pivotal time in English history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Industrialization, Revolution, War, Social change, Political upheaval
