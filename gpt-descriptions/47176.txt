Synopsis: 
'Drawn at a Venture: A Collection of Drawings' by Fougasse is a captivating compilation of illustrations that takes readers on a whimsical journey through various scenes and scenarios. From humorous depictions of everyday life to clever satirical sketches, this collection showcases the artist's keen eye for detail and wit. Each drawing tells a unique story, inviting readers to interpret and engage with the images in their own imaginative way. Whether it's a comical take on social norms or a thought-provoking commentary on human nature, Fougasse's drawings are sure to captivate and entertain readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Art, Humor, Satire

Themes: 
Everyday life, Social commentary, Wit
