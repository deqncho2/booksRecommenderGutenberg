Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, a mysterious Starbucks opens its doors, promising more than just coffee. As the townspeople become enchanted by the magical atmosphere and the baristas' uncanny ability to fulfill their deepest desires, they soon discover that every wish comes with a price. With secrets lurking behind every latte and a brewing darkness threatening to consume them all, the residents of Willowbrook must confront their own desires and make a choice between their dreams and the reality they once knew.

Genres: Magical realism, fantasy, mystery

Themes: Desire and temptation, the cost of fulfilling one's dreams, the power of choice
