Synopsis: In 'A Young Folks' History of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints', Nephi Anderson takes readers on a captivating journey through the early history of the Church. From the humble beginnings of Joseph Smith to the challenges faced by the early Saints, this book provides a fascinating account of the faith, perseverance, and sacrifices that shaped the Church. Through the eyes of young protagonists, readers will witness the struggles, triumphs, and miracles that defined the early years of the Church, making this a compelling and inspiring read.

Genres: Historical fiction, Religious fiction, Young adult fiction

Themes: Faith, Perseverance, Sacrifice, Miracles
