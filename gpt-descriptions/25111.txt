Synopsis: In "Over the Fireside with Silent Friends" by Richard King, a mysterious group of friends gathers around a cozy fireside, sharing their untold stories and secrets. As the night unfolds, the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the lives of these enigmatic characters, each with their own hidden pasts and desires. With each tale, the line between reality and imagination blurs, leaving the reader questioning what is true and what is merely a figment of the characters' imagination. This mesmerizing collection of stories will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly awaiting the next revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Identity, Perception, Reality vs. Illusion
