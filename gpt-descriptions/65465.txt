Synopsis: In "The Professor's House," a renowned professor finds himself at a crossroads in his life as he grapples with the pressures of academia, the complexities of family dynamics, and the allure of a mysterious ancient civilization. As he navigates through his own introspection and the challenges of his personal relationships, he is forced to confront the choices he has made and the path he wants to take. Will he find solace in the past or embrace the possibilities of the future?

Genres: Fiction, Literary Fiction, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Regret, Ambition, Family Dynamics, Nostalgia
