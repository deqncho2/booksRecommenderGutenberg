Synopsis: 
In 'Diabetes: Its Cause and Its Treatment With Insulin', Russell M. Wilder delves into the fascinating world of diabetes, unraveling its mysterious cause and exploring the groundbreaking treatment with insulin. Through meticulous research and compelling anecdotes, Wilder takes readers on a journey to understand the origins of this complex disease and the revolutionary discovery that changed the lives of millions. With a blend of scientific expertise and captivating storytelling, this book offers a captivating exploration of diabetes and its remarkable treatment.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Science

Themes: Disease, Medical breakthrough, Scientific discovery
