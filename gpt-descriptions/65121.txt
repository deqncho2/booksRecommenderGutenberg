Synopsis: In "Hindu Magic: An Expose of the Tricks of the Yogis and Fakirs of India," Hereward Carrington takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical world of Indian magic. Through his firsthand experiences and encounters with yogis and fakirs, Carrington unveils the secrets behind their seemingly supernatural abilities. From levitation and fire-walking to mind reading and snake charming, this book delves into the astonishing tricks and illusions employed by these mystics. As Carrington explores the line between reality and illusion, readers will be enthralled by the mind-bending feats and the cultural significance of Hindu magic.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Travelogue

Themes: Magic and illusion, Mysticism, Cultural exploration
