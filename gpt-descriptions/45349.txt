Synopsis: 

In 'Remarkable Rogues: The Careers of Some Notable Criminals of Europe and America; Second Edition', Charles Kingston delves into the captivating lives of infamous criminals from both sides of the Atlantic. From audacious bank robbers to cunning con artists, this book uncovers the thrilling tales of these remarkable rogues. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Kingston takes readers on a journey through the dark underbelly of society, exploring the motivations, methods, and ultimate fates of these notorious individuals. Prepare to be enthralled by the audacity and ingenuity of these criminals as their gripping stories unfold.

Genres: True Crime, Biography, History

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Society and Morality, Human Nature
