Synopsis: In 'Critical Difference' by Murray Leinster, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dangerous secret that puts his life and the fate of the world at risk. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of intrigue and deception to protect his discovery and ensure its potential is used for the greater good. This thrilling science fiction novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the ethical implications of scientific advancements and the lengths some will go to control them.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific ethics, Power and control, Human progress
