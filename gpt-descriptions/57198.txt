Synopsis: In 'Regeneration' by Thomas Hunt Morgan, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges the very essence of life itself. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden world of secrets and ethical dilemmas that threaten to unravel everything he thought he knew. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path of moral ambiguity and personal sacrifice. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of science and the consequences of playing god.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Morality, Scientific Responsibility
