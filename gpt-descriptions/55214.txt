Synopsis: In 'The Village of Hide and Seek' by Wilson, Bingham Thoburn, a group of children stumble upon a mysterious village hidden deep in the forest. As they explore the village, they soon realize that there is more to it than meets the eye. With each game of hide and seek they play, they uncover secrets and unravel a thrilling mystery that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Discovery, Secrets, Courage
