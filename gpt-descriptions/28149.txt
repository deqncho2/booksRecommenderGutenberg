Synopsis: In 'Her Ladyship's Elephant' by David Dwight Wells, a mysterious and enchanting elephant arrives at the doorstep of Lady Amelia, turning her quiet life upside down. As Lady Amelia embarks on a whimsical adventure to uncover the origins of her unexpected guest, she discovers secrets, encounters eccentric characters, and finds herself questioning the boundaries of love and loyalty. This delightful tale weaves together elements of mystery, romance, and fantasy, captivating readers with its charm and wit.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Adventure, Identity, Love and Loyalty
