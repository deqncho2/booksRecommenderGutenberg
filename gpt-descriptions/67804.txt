Synopsis: In "The First of the English: A Novel" by Gunter, Archibald Clavering, a young Englishman finds himself caught in the midst of a power struggle between rival factions in medieval England. As he navigates treacherous alliances and dangerous secrets, he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and political intrigue. With the fate of the kingdom hanging in the balance, he must make difficult choices that will shape the course of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Love and Betrayal
