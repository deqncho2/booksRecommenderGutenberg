Synopsis:
In 'Vanishing Point' by Beck, C. C., a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a series of bizarre disappearances occur in a small, seemingly idyllic town. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that challenge his own perceptions of reality. With time running out and the stakes growing higher, the detective must navigate a treacherous maze of clues and confront his own demons to solve the mystery before it consumes him.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes:
Perception vs. Reality, Deception, Identity
