Synopsis: In 'My Queen: A Weekly Journal for Young Women. Issue 4, October 20, 1900: Marion Marlowe's Noble Work; or, The Tragedy at the Hospital' by Sheldon, Lurana, readers are introduced to Marion Marlowe, a young woman dedicated to making a difference in the world. As she embarks on her noble work at a hospital, a tragic event unfolds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the outcome.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Dedication to service, Tragedy, Women's empowerment
