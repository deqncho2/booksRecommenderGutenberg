Synopsis: In "Homer and His Age" by Lang, Andrew, embark on a captivating journey through the ancient world of Homer, the legendary poet behind the epic tales of the Iliad and the Odyssey. Delve into the mysteries surrounding Homer's life, his works, and the historical context in which he lived. Uncover the enigmatic origins of these timeless masterpieces and explore the enduring impact they have had on literature and culture throughout the ages. Lang's meticulous research and engaging storytelling will transport you to a world of heroes, gods, and epic adventures, leaving you with a deeper appreciation for the genius of Homer.

Genres: Literary Criticism, History, Mythology

Themes: Ancient Greek literature, Epic poetry, Oral tradition, Historical context
