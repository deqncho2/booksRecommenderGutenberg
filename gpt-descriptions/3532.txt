Synopsis: In "My Discovery of England," Stephen Leacock takes readers on a delightful journey through the eccentricities and idiosyncrasies of English society. Through his witty observations and humorous anecdotes, Leacock unveils the peculiarities of the English people, their customs, and their way of life. From encounters with peculiar characters to unexpected adventures, this travelogue offers a unique perspective on England that is both entertaining and enlightening.

Genres: Travelogue, Humor, Satire

Themes: Cultural differences, National identity, Social commentary
