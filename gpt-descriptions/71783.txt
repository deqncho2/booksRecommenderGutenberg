Synopsis: In "Sunshine and Shadow, or, Paul Burton's Surprise: A Romance of the American Revolution," Julia A. Moore takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. The story follows the life of Paul Burton, a young man whose world is turned upside down when he becomes entangled in the fight for independence. As Paul navigates the treacherous paths of love, loyalty, and betrayal, he discovers shocking secrets that challenge his beliefs and reshape his destiny. Filled with unexpected twists and heart-wrenching moments, this historical romance will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth and witness the triumphs and tragedies of Paul Burton's life.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
