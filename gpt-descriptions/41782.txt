Synopsis: In 'The Moths of the British Isles, First Series: Comprising the Families Sphingidæ to Noctuidæ', Richard South takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of moths found in the British Isles. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, South explores the diverse species of moths, their habitats, and their intriguing behaviors. From the mysterious Sphingidæ to the mesmerizing Noctuidæ, this comprehensive guide offers a fascinating glimpse into the hidden lives of these nocturnal creatures. Whether you are a seasoned lepidopterist or simply curious about the wonders of nature, this book is sure to ignite your curiosity and appreciation for the beauty of moths.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Field Guide

Themes: Biodiversity, Nature Exploration, Insect Behavior
