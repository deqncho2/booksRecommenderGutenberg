Synopsis: 

The 2009 CIA World Factbook is a captivating and eye-opening reference guide that takes readers on a global journey, providing a comprehensive overview of the countries and territories around the world. From the bustling cities of Asia to the vast landscapes of Africa, this Factbook offers a wealth of information on each nation's geography, population, government, economy, and much more. Delve into the intricate details of each country, uncovering fascinating facts and statistics that shed light on the diverse cultures and societies that make up our world. 

Genres: Reference, Non-fiction, Geography

Themes: Global diversity, Cultural exploration, International relations
