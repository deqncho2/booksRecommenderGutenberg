Synopsis: In "Bertha Weisser's Wish: A Christmas Story," author Mary L. Bissell takes readers on a heartwarming journey through the life of Bertha Weisser, a young girl with a special wish during the holiday season. As Bertha's wish unfolds, unexpected events and encounters lead her to discover the true meaning of Christmas and the power of kindness. This enchanting tale captures the essence of the holiday spirit and reminds us of the magic that can be found in the simplest of gestures.

Genres: Fiction, Christmas, Family

Themes: Hope, Generosity, Belief, Redemption
