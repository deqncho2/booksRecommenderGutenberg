Synopsis: In 'Star of India' by Alice Perrin, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young woman named Muriel embarks on a journey to India. Drawn by the allure of adventure and the promise of a new life, she finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love. As she delves deeper into the enigmatic world of the Indian aristocracy, Muriel must navigate treacherous waters and make choices that will shape her destiny. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, 'Star of India' takes readers on a thrilling and emotional ride through the vibrant landscapes of India.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Cultural exploration, Identity
