Synopsis: 
In "True Irish Ghost Stories," prepare to be captivated by a collection of spine-chilling tales that will transport you to the misty landscapes of Ireland. From haunted castles to eerie encounters with restless spirits, this book delves into the rich folklore and supernatural occurrences that have long fascinated the Irish people. Each story is meticulously recounted, drawing you into a world where the line between the living and the dead becomes blurred. Brace yourself for a haunting journey through the realms of the paranormal, where the inexplicable and the terrifying intertwine.

Genres: 
Horror, Supernatural, Folklore

Themes: 
Ghosts, Hauntings, Irish folklore, Supernatural encounters
