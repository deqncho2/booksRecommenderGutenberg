Synopsis: 
'A Selection from the Poems of William Morris' is a captivating collection of poetry by the renowned writer William Morris. This anthology takes readers on a journey through Morris's imaginative and evocative verses, exploring themes of love, nature, social justice, and the beauty of the medieval world. From enchanting ballads to thought-provoking narratives, these poems transport readers to different realms and inspire them to reflect on the power of art and the human spirit.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Love, Nature, Social Justice, Medievalism
