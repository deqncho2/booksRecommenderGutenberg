Synopsis: In 'The Trader's Wife: 1901' by Louis Becke, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught between two worlds when she marries a trader and moves to a remote island in the Pacific. As she navigates the challenges of her new life, Mary becomes entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden desires, and dangerous encounters. With the arrival of a mysterious stranger, her world is turned upside down, forcing her to confront the true nature of love, loyalty, and survival.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Cultural clash, Forbidden love, Survival
