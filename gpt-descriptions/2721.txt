Synopsis: 
In the remote land of Iceland, a young warrior named Eric Brighteyes finds himself entangled in a dangerous love triangle that threatens to tear apart his world. As he battles against treacherous enemies and faces the wrath of the gods, Eric must navigate a treacherous journey filled with adventure, honor, and sacrifice. With his unwavering courage and unyielding determination, Eric Brighteyes becomes a legendary figure whose fate will forever be etched in the annals of history.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Honor and Loyalty, Fate and Destiny
