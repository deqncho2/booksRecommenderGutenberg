Synopsis: In 'The Sunlit Hours' by Emile Verhaeren, a mysterious woman arrives in a small coastal town, captivating the locals with her enigmatic presence. As the townspeople become increasingly infatuated with her, they are drawn into a web of secrets and desires that threaten to unravel their lives. With its atmospheric setting and compelling characters, this novel explores the depths of human nature and the power of obsession.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological thriller, Literary fiction

Themes: Secrets, Desire, Obsession, Human nature
