Synopsis: 
In 'The World English Bible (WEB): Ruth' by Anonymous, a young widow named Ruth finds herself at a crossroads after the death of her husband. Determined to support her mother-in-law, Naomi, she embarks on a journey to a foreign land, where she encounters unexpected kindness and a chance at a new beginning. As Ruth navigates the challenges of her new life, she discovers the power of loyalty, love, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: 
Biblical fiction, Historical fiction, Romance

Themes: 
Loyalty, Love, Resilience
