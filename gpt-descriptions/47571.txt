Synopsis: In 'Neæra: A Tale of Ancient Rome' by John W. Graham, readers are transported to the vibrant and treacherous world of ancient Rome. The story follows the life of Neæra, a young and captivating slave girl who finds herself entangled in a web of power, love, and betrayal. As Neæra navigates the opulent and dangerous society of Rome, she must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and challenge the very foundations of the empire. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of ancient Rome.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Power and Corruption, Love and Sacrifice, Social Hierarchy
