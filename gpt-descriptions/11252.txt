Synopsis: 
In "Martin Hewitt, Investigator" by Arthur Morrison, readers are introduced to the brilliant detective Martin Hewitt, who takes on a series of perplexing cases that will keep them on the edge of their seats. From a mysterious disappearance to a baffling murder, Hewitt's keen observation skills and astute deductions lead him down a labyrinthine path, uncovering secrets and unmasking culprits in the most unexpected ways. As the tension builds and the stakes rise, readers will be captivated by Hewitt's relentless pursuit of the truth and his unwavering commitment to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Crime Fiction

Themes: Deduction, Crime-solving, Justice
