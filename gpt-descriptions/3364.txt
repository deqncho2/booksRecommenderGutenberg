Synopsis: In the small town of Equity, Dr. Breen is a respected physician known for his dedication to his patients. However, his practice takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious young woman arrives seeking his help. As Dr. Breen delves into her case, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, scandal, and moral dilemmas that threaten to unravel his own life and reputation. With each new revelation, the boundaries between right and wrong blur, forcing Dr. Breen to confront the complexities of human nature and the consequences of his own actions.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Morality, Ethics, Consequences of Actions, Human Nature
