Synopsis: In 'The Martians and the Coys' by Reynolds, Mack, a group of Martians crash-lands on Earth and finds themselves in the middle of a long-standing feud between two rival families, the Coys and the McCoys. As the Martians try to navigate this strange new world, they inadvertently become entangled in the hilarious and chaotic antics of the feuding families. With misunderstandings, pranks, and unexpected alliances, this interplanetary clash of cultures takes on a whole new level of absurdity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Cultural misunderstandings, Family feuds, Interplanetary relations
