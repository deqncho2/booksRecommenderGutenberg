Synopsis: In 'The Y. M. C. A. boys on Bass Island: or, The mystery of Russabaga camp' by Henderley Brooks, a group of adventurous boys embark on a thrilling summer camp experience on Bass Island. However, their excitement quickly turns into a perplexing mystery when they stumble upon a hidden secret that could change everything. As they delve deeper into the enigma, the boys must use their wit, teamwork, and determination to unravel the truth and solve the mystery of Russabaga camp.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Problem-solving
