Synopsis: In 'The Book of Antelopes, Vol. 2 (of 4)', Sclater and Thomas take readers on a captivating journey through the diverse world of antelopes. From the majestic eland to the elusive dik-dik, this volume delves into the fascinating lives, behaviors, and habitats of these graceful creatures. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, the authors provide a comprehensive exploration of antelopes, shedding light on their evolutionary history and the challenges they face in the wild. As readers delve into this captivating volume, they will be transported to the enchanting realm of antelopes, discovering the wonders of these remarkable animals.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Zoology

Themes: Biodiversity, Animal Behavior, Conservation
