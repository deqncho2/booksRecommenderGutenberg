Synopsis: 
In 'Jack Ranger's Gun Club; Or, From Schoolroom to Camp and Trail', young Jack Ranger embarks on an exhilarating adventure that takes him from the confines of his schoolroom to the rugged wilderness. Alongside his loyal friends, Jack forms a gun club and sets out to explore the great outdoors, encountering thrilling challenges and unexpected dangers. As they navigate through uncharted territories, the boys learn valuable lessons about teamwork, courage, and the importance of self-reliance. Will Jack and his companions conquer the trials that lie ahead and emerge as true wilderness explorers?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Self-discovery
