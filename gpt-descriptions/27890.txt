Synopsis: In 'The Merriweather Girls in Quest of Treasure' by Lizette M. Edholm, four adventurous sisters embark on a thrilling journey to uncover a hidden treasure. When the girls stumble upon an old map, they are determined to solve the mystery it holds and follow the clues that lead them to an exciting and dangerous quest. Along the way, they encounter unexpected challenges, make new friends, and learn valuable lessons about teamwork, perseverance, and the true meaning of treasure. Will the Merriweather girls be able to unravel the secrets of the map and find the long-lost treasure?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Treasure hunting, Sisterhood, Friendship, Perseverance
