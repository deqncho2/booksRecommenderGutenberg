Synopsis: In 'Look Back on Happiness', we follow the life of Ove Rolandsen, a dreamer and a misfit in a small Norwegian town. As he navigates through his mundane job as a telegraph operator, Ove's vivid imagination and unconventional behavior lead him on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected encounters. Through his interactions with the townspeople, Ove's unique perspective challenges societal norms and raises questions about the nature of happiness and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Psychological, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Individuality, Imagination, Social Conformity, Happiness
