Synopsis: In 'The Boarding School: Familiar conversations between a governess and her pupils', readers are transported to a prestigious boarding school where a young governess engages in captivating and enlightening conversations with her pupils. Through these conversations, the governess imparts valuable lessons on various subjects, from history and literature to etiquette and morality. As the girls navigate their way through the challenges of adolescence, they learn not only from their governess but also from each other, forming deep bonds of friendship and sisterhood. This delightful and instructive book offers a glimpse into the lives of young ladies in a bygone era, filled with wit, wisdom, and the joys of learning.

Genres: Fiction, Historical, Educational

Themes: Education, Friendship, Coming of Age
