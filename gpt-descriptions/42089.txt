Synopsis: In 'Abolitionism Exposed!', author W. W. Sleigh presents a thought-provoking examination of the principles of abolitionism, challenging the widely accepted narrative surrounding the movement. Through a compelling analysis, Sleigh argues that abolitionism may not be as beneficial to the slaves themselves, the nation, or even in accordance with the express commands of God. This thought-provoking work delves into the complexities of the abolitionist movement, shedding light on its potential unintended consequences and raising important questions about its true impact.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Commentary

Themes: Abolitionism, Slavery, Morality, Religion, Social Justice
