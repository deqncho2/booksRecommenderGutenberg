Synopsis:
'Four Years' by W.B. Yeats is a captivating collection of poetry that delves into the complexities of love, loss, and the passage of time. Through vivid imagery and lyrical language, Yeats explores the depths of human emotions and the transformative power of personal experiences. Each poem in this collection offers a unique perspective on the fleeting nature of life and the profound impact that relationships can have on our existence. From passionate love affairs to moments of introspection and self-discovery, 'Four Years' takes readers on a poignant journey through the highs and lows of the human heart.

Genres:
Poetry, Romanticism, Symbolism

Themes:
Love, Loss, Time, Transformation
