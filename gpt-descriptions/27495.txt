Synopsis: In 'The Girls of St. Olave's' by Mabel Mackintosh, a group of spirited young women embark on a journey of self-discovery and friendship at the prestigious St. Olave's boarding school. As they navigate the challenges of adolescence, they uncover hidden secrets, face unexpected obstacles, and form unbreakable bonds that will shape their futures. With its captivating characters and intriguing plot twists, this coming-of-age tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to uncover the mysteries that lie within the hallowed halls of St. Olave's.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Boarding School, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Secrets, Coming-of-Age
