Synopsis: 
In 'The Last of the De Mullins: A Play Without a Preface', the De Mullin family is on the brink of financial ruin, and their only hope lies in the hands of their eccentric and mysterious relative, Mrs. De Mullins. As the family gathers to meet her, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and unexpected twists unfold, leaving the audience captivated until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Family dynamics, Wealth and social status, Identity and self-discovery
