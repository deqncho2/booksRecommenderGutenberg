Synopsis: In 'A Prize for Edie' by Jesse F. Bone, Edie, a young and ambitious artist, finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she wins a prestigious art prize. As she delves deeper into the world of art and its secrets, Edie must navigate through unexpected twists and turns, questioning her own talent and the true nature of the prize she has won. With each revelation, Edie's determination to uncover the truth grows stronger, leading her on a thrilling journey that will challenge her perceptions and change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Artistic ambition, Identity, Deception, Truth
