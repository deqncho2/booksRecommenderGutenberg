Synopsis: 
'Notes of an Itinerant Policeman' follows the gripping and unpredictable journey of Josiah Flynt, a former tramp turned undercover detective, as he infiltrates the criminal underworld of late 19th-century America. From the dangerous streets of New York City to the lawless territories of the Wild West, Flynt's encounters with notorious criminals and his relentless pursuit of justice make for a thrilling and thought-provoking narrative. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of society, Flynt's own moral compass is tested, blurring the lines between right and wrong, and forcing him to question the very nature of law and order.

Genres: Crime fiction, Historical fiction, Adventure

Themes: Justice and morality, Identity and transformation, Society and its outcasts
