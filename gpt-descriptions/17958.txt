Synopsis: In 'Warlord of Kor' by Terry Carr, a man named John Earthman finds himself transported to the distant planet of Kor, where he is mistaken for a legendary warlord. As he navigates the treacherous political landscape and battles against powerful enemies, John must embrace his newfound role and uncover the truth about his own identity. With unexpected twists and a thrilling adventure, this science fiction novel explores the complexities of power, identity, and the consequences of one's actions.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Power, Identity, Consequences
