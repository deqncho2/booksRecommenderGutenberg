Synopsis: In 'The Auto Boys' Mystery' by James A. Braden, a group of adventurous friends stumble upon a hidden secret while exploring an abandoned auto repair shop. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they uncover clues that lead them on a thrilling journey filled with danger, suspense, and unexpected twists. With their quick thinking and resourcefulness, the Auto Boys must unravel the truth before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Problem-solving
