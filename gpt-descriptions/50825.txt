Synopsis: 

'Newark's Last Fifteen Years, 1904-1919. The Story in Outline' takes readers on a captivating journey through the transformative period in Newark's history. From the bustling streets to the political upheavals, this book offers a unique perspective on the city's evolution during a crucial era. Through a series of carefully curated events and anecdotes, the reader is transported to a time of great change, where the past collides with the future, and the fate of Newark hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical Non-Fiction, Local History

Themes: Urban Development, Social Change, Political Turmoil
