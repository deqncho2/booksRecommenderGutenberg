Synopsis: 
The Cattle-Raid of Cualnge (Tain Bo Cualnge) is an ancient Irish prose-epic that takes readers on a thrilling journey through a land of heroes, warriors, and mythical creatures. Set in a time of great turmoil, the story revolves around the legendary cattle raid of Queen Medb of Connacht, who seeks to acquire the prized bull of Ulster. As the epic unfolds, readers are immersed in a world of fierce battles, treacherous alliances, and the indomitable spirit of the Irish people. With its rich tapestry of characters and captivating storytelling, this epic tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Epic, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Honor, Loyalty, Power, Fate
