Synopsis: 
'Chaldea: From the Earliest Times to the Rise of Assyria' takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient civilization of Chaldea, exploring its rich history, myths, and legends. From the mysterious origins of the Chaldeans to their rise as a powerful empire, Zénaïde A. Ragozin delves into the fascinating world of this ancient civilization, uncovering its secrets and shedding light on its significant contributions to human history. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Ragozin paints a vivid picture of the Chaldean society, its customs, and its interactions with neighboring civilizations. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the tales of legendary kings, the enigmatic practices of divination, and the profound influence of Chaldean culture on subsequent civilizations. 

Genres: History, Mythology, Archaeology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Power and empire, Cultural influence
