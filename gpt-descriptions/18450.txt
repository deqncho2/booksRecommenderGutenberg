Synopsis: 
'Hawaiian Folk Tales: A Collection of Native Legends' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and mythology of Hawaii. From ancient gods and goddesses to mischievous spirits and legendary creatures, this collection presents a diverse array of enchanting tales that have been passed down through generations. Each story offers a glimpse into the vibrant culture and traditions of the Hawaiian people, weaving together elements of adventure, romance, and the supernatural. Whether it's the epic battles between powerful deities or the heartwarming tales of love and loyalty, these legends transport readers to a world where the boundaries between the natural and the supernatural blur, leaving them spellbound until the very last page.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Fantasy

Themes: Cultural heritage, Supernatural beings, Love and loyalty
