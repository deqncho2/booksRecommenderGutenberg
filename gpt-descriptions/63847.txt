Synopsis: In 'Garden of Evil' by Margaret St. Clair, a young woman named Laura finds herself drawn to a mysterious garden hidden within the walls of an old mansion. As she explores the enchanting flora, she becomes entangled in a web of dark secrets and supernatural forces that threaten to consume her. With each step deeper into the garden, Laura must confront her own desires and face the consequences of her choices. Will she be able to escape the clutches of the garden's evil, or will she become its next victim?

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, mystery

Themes: Temptation, the power of nature, the duality of human nature
