Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Fifth, or Princess Charlotte of Wales's Regiment of Dragoon Guards," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment. From its inception in 1685 to its exploits up until 1838, Cannon delves into the regiment's rich history, chronicling its triumphs, challenges, and the remarkable individuals who shaped its legacy. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of the Fifth Dragoon Guards, revealing the untold stories and heroic deeds that have made them a force to be reckoned with.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military valor, Regimental history, Historical significance
