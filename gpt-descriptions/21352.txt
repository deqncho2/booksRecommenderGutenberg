Synopsis: 

'Fragments of Two Centuries: Glimpses of Country Life when George III. was King' takes readers on a captivating journey through time, offering glimpses into the lives of people living in the countryside during the reign of King George III. Through a collection of vivid and engaging stories, author Alfred Kingston paints a rich tapestry of rural life, filled with both joyous and challenging moments. From the bustling village fairs to the quiet solitude of the countryside, this book immerses readers in a bygone era, where the beauty of nature and the resilience of the human spirit shine through.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Rural Life, Historical Events, Human Resilience
