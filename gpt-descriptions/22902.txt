Synopsis: In 'The Liberty Boys Running the Blockade; or, Getting Out of New York' by Moore, Harry, the Liberty Boys find themselves trapped in New York City during the American Revolution. With the British tightening their grip on the city, the young patriots must devise a daring plan to escape the blockade and continue their fight for freedom. As they navigate treacherous waters and face unexpected challenges, the Liberty Boys' courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will they succeed in their mission and outsmart the enemy, or will they be captured and silenced forever?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Bravery, Survival
