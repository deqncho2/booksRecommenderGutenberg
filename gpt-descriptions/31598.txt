Synopsis: In 'The Egyptian Cat Mystery: A Rick Brant Science-Adventure Story', Rick Brant and his friend Scotty embark on a thrilling journey to Egypt, where they find themselves entangled in a mysterious plot involving ancient artifacts, secret societies, and a mischievous cat. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they must use their scientific knowledge and quick thinking to unravel the truth and protect the treasures of the past.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Ancient history, Archaeology, Friendship, Problem-solving
