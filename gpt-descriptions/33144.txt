Synopsis: 
In 'The Oriental Rug: A Monograph on Eastern Rugs and Carpets, Saddle-Bags, Mats & Pillows, with a Consideration of Kinds and Classes, Types, Borders, Figures, Dyes, Symbols, etc. Together with Some Practical Advice to Collectors,' William De Lancey Ellwanger takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of Eastern rugs and carpets. With meticulous attention to detail, the author explores the various types, designs, and symbols found in these exquisite works of art. From the vibrant dyes to the intricate borders and figures, Ellwanger's monograph provides a comprehensive understanding of the craftsmanship and cultural significance behind each rug. Moreover, he offers practical advice to collectors, making this book an invaluable resource for both enthusiasts and connoisseurs alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Collecting

Themes: Eastern culture, Craftsmanship, Symbolism, Collecting
