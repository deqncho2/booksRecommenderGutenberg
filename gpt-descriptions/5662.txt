Synopsis: In 'The Potato Child & Others' by Lucia Prudence Hall Woodbury, prepare to be captivated by a collection of enchanting and mysterious tales. From a peculiar child made entirely of potatoes to a haunting encounter with a ghostly figure, each story will transport you to a world where the ordinary becomes extraordinary. With unexpected twists and turns, these tales will leave you questioning reality and embracing the power of imagination. 

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Imagination, Transformation, Mystery
