Synopsis: 

Join Captain David Sutherland on an exhilarating journey as he takes you on a tour up the straits, from Gibraltar to Constantinople. Set against the backdrop of the intense war between the Austrians, Russians, and the Turks, this captivating narrative explores the leading events that shaped the conflict up until the year 1789. From the stunning landscapes and vibrant cultures encountered along the way to the strategic maneuvers and political intrigues that unfold, Sutherland's account offers a thrilling and insightful perspective on this tumultuous period in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, travelogue, war narrative

Themes: War and conflict, cultural exploration, political intrigue
