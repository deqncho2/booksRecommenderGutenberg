Synopsis: In "Monsieur Beaucaire" by Booth Tarkington, a dashing French nobleman finds himself in a precarious situation when he is mistaken for a barber. As he navigates the complexities of his new identity, he becomes entangled in a web of romance, intrigue, and mistaken identities. With wit and charm, Monsieur Beaucaire must use his cunning to navigate the treacherous waters of high society and prove that true love can conquer all.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Identity, Love, Social Class
