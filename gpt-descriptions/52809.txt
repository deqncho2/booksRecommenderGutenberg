Synopsis: In 'The Banner Boy Scouts Mystery' by George A. Warren, a group of adventurous boy scouts find themselves embroiled in a perplexing mystery during their camping trip. As they stumble upon a hidden cave and a series of cryptic clues, the young scouts must use their wit and resourcefulness to unravel the secrets that lie within. With danger lurking around every corner, the boys must work together to solve the mystery before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
