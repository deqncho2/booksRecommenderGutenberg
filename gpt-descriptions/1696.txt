Synopsis: In 'The Club of Queer Trades', a group of eccentric individuals form a secret society where each member must have a unique and unconventional profession. Enter the world of a deaf man who is a professional eavesdropper, a man who makes a living by predicting the future, and a man who specializes in creating disguises. As the stories unfold, the reader is taken on a series of mysterious and humorous adventures, where the unexpected becomes the norm.

Genres: Mystery, Humor, Adventure

Themes: Eccentricity, Unconventional Professions, Mystery
