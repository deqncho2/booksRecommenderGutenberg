Synopsis: In 'Every Man for Himself' by Duncan, Norman, we are transported to the luxurious and opulent world of the Titanic, just days before its tragic demise. Through the eyes of a young American artist, we witness the intertwining lives of the ship's passengers, from the wealthy elite to the struggling immigrants. As the ship sails towards its fateful encounter with an iceberg, secrets are unveiled, love blossoms, and the true nature of humanity is put to the ultimate test. Will anyone be able to escape the impending disaster unscathed?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Class divisions, Love and sacrifice, Human resilience
