Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Two Bad Mice', two mischievous mice named Tom Thumb and Hunca Munca embark on a thrilling adventure in a dollhouse. However, their plans take an unexpected turn, leading to a series of hilarious and heartwarming events. Will these two bad mice learn their lesson or continue their mischievous ways?

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Mischief, Consequences, Redemption
