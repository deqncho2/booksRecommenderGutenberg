Synopsis: In 'A Sagebrush Cinderella' by Max Brand, a young woman named Mary Jane is trapped in a life of hardship and servitude on a remote ranch. However, when a mysterious stranger arrives, her world is turned upside down. As Mary Jane navigates the treacherous landscape of love, betrayal, and redemption, she discovers hidden strengths within herself that she never knew existed. Will she find her happily ever after, or will her past continue to haunt her?

Genres: Western, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Overcoming adversity, self-discovery, the power of love
