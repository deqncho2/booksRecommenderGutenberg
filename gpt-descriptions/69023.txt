Synopsis: In "The passing of the great Queen: A tribute to the noble life of Victoria Regina," Corelli, Marie takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and legacy of the iconic Queen Victoria. Through a blend of historical facts and imaginative storytelling, the author delves into the Queen's personal struggles, political triumphs, and the profound impact she had on the British Empire. As the narrative unfolds, readers are transported to a bygone era, witnessing the Queen's rise to power, her enduring love for Prince Albert, and the challenges she faced as a female monarch in a male-dominated world. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt tributes, Corelli paints a vivid portrait of a remarkable woman who left an indelible mark on history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Tribute

Themes: Power and leadership, Love and loss, Gender roles, Legacy and remembrance
