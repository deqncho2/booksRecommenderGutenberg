Synopsis: In 'The Claim Jumpers: A Romance' by Stewart Edward White, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a dangerous web of deceit and greed when she inherits a valuable gold mine in the rugged wilderness of the American West. As she struggles to protect her newfound wealth, Mary must navigate treacherous alliances, unexpected romance, and the constant threat of claim jumpers who will stop at nothing to steal her fortune. With the stakes higher than ever, Mary must rely on her wit, courage, and the help of a mysterious stranger to outsmart her enemies and secure her rightful claim.

Genres: Western, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Greed, Deception, Love and Sacrifice
