Synopsis: 

'Widger's Quotes and Images from Conscience by Hector Malot: The French Immortals: Quotes and Images' by Hector Malot is a captivating collection of quotes and images that delve into the depths of human conscience. This unique compilation offers a thought-provoking exploration of the moral dilemmas, inner conflicts, and ethical choices faced by the characters in Malot's renowned novel, 'Conscience'. Through carefully selected quotes and accompanying images, the reader is invited to reflect upon the complexities of human nature and the consequences of our actions. 

Genres: Literary Fiction, Philosophy, Inspirational

Themes: Conscience, Morality, Ethical Dilemmas, Human Nature
