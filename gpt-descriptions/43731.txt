Synopsis: In 'Bud: A Novel' by Neil Munro, readers are transported to the rugged and captivating landscapes of Scotland. Set in the early 20th century, the story follows the life of Bud, a young boy with an insatiable curiosity and a deep connection to nature. As Bud navigates the challenges of growing up in a small coastal village, he encounters a cast of colorful characters, each with their own secrets and desires. Through Bud's eyes, we witness the beauty and brutality of life, the power of friendship, and the resilience of the human spirit. This poignant coming-of-age tale is filled with adventure, mystery, and a touch of magic, making it a captivating read for all ages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Nature, Friendship, Resilience
