Synopsis: 

'Blacker's Art of Fly Making, &c.: Comprising Angling, & Dyeing of Colours, with Engravings of Salmon & Trout Flies' by Blacker, W. (William) is a comprehensive guide that delves into the intricate world of fly making and angling. This book takes readers on a journey through the art of creating flies for fishing, exploring the techniques and materials used, as well as the process of dyeing colors to achieve the perfect lure. With detailed engravings of salmon and trout flies, this work offers a visual feast for fishing enthusiasts and aspiring fly makers alike. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Crafts

Themes: Fly fishing, Artistry, Nature
