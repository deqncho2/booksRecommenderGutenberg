Synopsis: In the midst of the tumultuous crisis of 1914, the New York Stock Exchange becomes the epicenter of a gripping tale that intertwines the lives of ambitious traders, powerful financiers, and ordinary investors. As the world teeters on the brink of war, the fate of nations and fortunes hang in the balance, and the NYSE becomes a battleground where greed, ambition, and desperation collide. Amidst the chaos, a young trader finds himself caught in a web of deceit and betrayal, forced to navigate the treacherous waters of Wall Street to protect his livelihood and uncover the truth behind a mysterious conspiracy.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Financial crisis, Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal
