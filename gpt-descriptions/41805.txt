Synopsis: 

In "History of the Catholic Church in Paterson, N.J.: with an Account of the Celebration of the Fiftieth Anniversary of the Establishment of St. John's Church," Charles A. Shriner takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of the Catholic Church in Paterson, New Jersey. From its humble beginnings to the grand celebration of St. John's Church's fiftieth anniversary, this book unveils the struggles, triumphs, and enduring faith of the Catholic community in this vibrant city. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Religious History

Themes: Faith and Devotion, Community Building, Historical Significance
