Synopsis:
In 'Delaware Tom; or, The Traitor Guide' by Badger, Jos. E., readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the American Revolution. The story follows the enigmatic Delaware Tom, a skilled tracker and guide, as he navigates the treacherous landscape of war-torn America. Tom finds himself torn between loyalty to his country and the allure of personal gain, leading him down a path filled with danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As the stakes rise and the lines between friend and foe blur, Delaware Tom must make choices that will shape the course of history.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes:
Loyalty, Betrayal, Morality, Identity
