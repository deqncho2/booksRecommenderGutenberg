Synopsis: 
In "In Accordance with the Evidence" by Oliver Onions, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a young woman is accused of a heinous crime. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives that challenge his own beliefs and force him to question the nature of truth and justice. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of evidence, all while battling his own personal demons. Will he be able to uncover the truth and bring the real culprit to justice, or will the weight of the evidence lead him astray?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Truth and deception, Justice and morality, Psychological exploration
