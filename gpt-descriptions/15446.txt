Synopsis: 
'Vocal Mastery: Talks with Master Singers and Teachers, Comprising Interviews with Caruso, Farrar, Maurel, Lehmann, and Others' takes readers on an intimate journey into the world of renowned singers and teachers. Through a series of captivating interviews, author Harriette Brower delves into the secrets, techniques, and experiences of some of the greatest vocalists of the time, including Caruso, Farrar, Maurel, Lehmann, and more. From their early struggles to their triumphant performances, this book offers a rare glimpse into the lives and minds of these masterful artists, providing invaluable insights for aspiring singers and music enthusiasts alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Music

Themes: Vocal technique, Artistry, Musical education, Success and perseverance
