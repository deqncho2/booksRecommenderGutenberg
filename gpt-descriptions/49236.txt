Synopsis: 

In "Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart," Herbert F. Peyser takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and genius of one of history's greatest composers. From Mozart's prodigious childhood to his meteoric rise in the musical world, Peyser delves into the complexities of his character, his relationships, and the profound impact his music had on society. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, this biography offers a fascinating exploration of the man behind the music, revealing the triumphs, struggles, and untold stories that shaped Mozart's extraordinary legacy.

Genres: Biography, Music, History

Themes: Genius and creativity, Passion for music, Society and cultural impact
