Synopsis: In 'The Golden Circle: A Mystery Story for Girls' by Roy J. Snell, a group of adventurous girls embarks on a thrilling journey to solve a mysterious puzzle left behind by a famous explorer. As they follow the clues, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, facing unexpected twists and turns that test their courage and friendship. With each step closer to unraveling the secret of the Golden Circle, the girls uncover hidden treasures and uncover a shocking truth that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Secrets
