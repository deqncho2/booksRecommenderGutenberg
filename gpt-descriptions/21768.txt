Synopsis: In "A Desert Drama: Being the Tragedy of the 'Korosko'," Arthur Conan Doyle takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous Egyptian desert. When a group of European tourists find themselves stranded in the midst of a dangerous conflict, they must rely on their wits and courage to survive. As tensions rise and unexpected alliances form, the true nature of humanity is put to the test in this gripping tale of adventure and survival.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Human nature, Cultural clashes
