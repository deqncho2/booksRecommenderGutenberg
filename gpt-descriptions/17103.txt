Synopsis: In 'The Double Life Of Mr. Alfred Burton', we are introduced to a seemingly ordinary man named Alfred Burton, who leads a quiet and uneventful life. However, beneath his unassuming exterior lies a secret that could shatter his entire existence. As the story unfolds, we are taken on a thrilling journey of deception, mystery, and unexpected twists, as Alfred's double life begins to unravel, leaving us questioning the true nature of identity and the consequences of hidden truths.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Secrets
