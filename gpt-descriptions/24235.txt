Synopsis:
In "Mary Ware's Promised Land," Mary Ware finds herself facing a new chapter in her life as she leaves her beloved home in the country to pursue her dreams in the bustling city of Chicago. With her determination and unwavering spirit, Mary navigates the challenges of city life, making new friends, facing unexpected obstacles, and discovering the true meaning of home. As she strives to find her place in the world, Mary's journey becomes a heartwarming tale of self-discovery, resilience, and the power of friendship.

Genres:
Children's literature, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes:
Self-discovery, Resilience, Friendship
