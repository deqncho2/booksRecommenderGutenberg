Synopsis: 

'Walladmor, Vol. 1 (of 2): "Freely Translated into German from the English of Sir Walter Scott." And Now Freely Translated from the German into English.' by Alexis, Willibald is a captivating historical novel that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous times of the Napoleonic Wars. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue, romance, and adventure, the story follows the enigmatic Walladmor, a mysterious figure whose actions have far-reaching consequences. As the narrative unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and loyalties are questioned, all while the fate of nations hangs in the balance. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Political Intrigue, Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity
