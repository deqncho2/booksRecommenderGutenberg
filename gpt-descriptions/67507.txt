Synopsis: In 'Cheating the Junk-Pile: The Purchase and Maintenance of Household Equipments', Ethel R. Peyser takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of household equipment. From the art of finding hidden gems in junkyards to the secrets of maintaining and repairing these treasures, Peyser's insightful guide offers a unique perspective on the often overlooked world of household appliances. With a blend of practical advice and fascinating anecdotes, this book is a must-read for anyone seeking to transform their home with budget-friendly and sustainable solutions.

Genres: Non-fiction, Home Improvement, Lifestyle

Themes: Thriftiness, Sustainability, DIY
