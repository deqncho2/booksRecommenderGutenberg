Synopsis: In 'Daemonologie,' King James I delves into the mysterious world of witchcraft and the supernatural, exploring the dark forces that threaten society. Through a series of captivating dialogues and insightful observations, he investigates the nature of demons, witches, and their malevolent practices. As he uncovers the hidden truths behind these occult phenomena, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey that challenges their beliefs and leaves them questioning the boundaries between good and evil.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Supernatural

Themes: Witchcraft, Demonology, Religion, Power, Morality
