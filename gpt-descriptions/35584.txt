Synopsis: 
'Aphorisms and Reflections from the works of T. H. Huxley' is a captivating collection of thought-provoking insights and philosophical musings by the renowned scientist and philosopher, Thomas Henry Huxley. This compilation delves into a wide range of topics, from the nature of knowledge and the pursuit of truth, to the complexities of human existence and the mysteries of the natural world. Through concise and profound aphorisms, Huxley challenges readers to question their beliefs, explore the boundaries of science and religion, and contemplate the meaning of life itself. This collection serves as a captivating window into the brilliant mind of one of the most influential thinkers of the 19th century.

Genres: Philosophy, Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Knowledge, Truth, Human Existence, Science and Religion
