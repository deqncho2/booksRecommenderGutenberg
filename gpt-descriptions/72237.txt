Synopsis: In 'Peck's Bad Boy in an airship', young mischievous Peck finds himself embarking on an extraordinary adventure when he stumbles upon an airship. With his usual knack for trouble, Peck's antics take him to new heights as he explores the skies and encounters unexpected challenges. Will Peck's mischievous nature lead to disaster or will he find a way to save the day?

Genres: Adventure, Humor, Children's Literature

Themes: Mischief, Exploration, Imagination
