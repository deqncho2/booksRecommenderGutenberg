Synopsis: 

In "Mine Pumping in Agricola's Time and Later," Multhauf takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of mine pumping, exploring the technological advancements and challenges faced by miners during Agricola's era and beyond. From the ingenious methods employed by ancient miners to the innovative machinery developed in later centuries, this book unveils the fascinating world of mine pumping and its impact on the mining industry. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Multhauf sheds light on the struggles, triumphs, and ingenuity of those who sought to keep mines dry and operational. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Engineering, Technology

Themes: Technological advancements, Mining industry, Historical innovation
