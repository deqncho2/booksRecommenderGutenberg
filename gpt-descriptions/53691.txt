Synopsis: In "Men We Meet in the Field; or, The Bullshire Hounds" by A. G. Bagot, a group of diverse and eccentric characters find themselves entangled in the world of fox hunting in the English countryside. From the enigmatic and enigmatic Lord Ballybun to the charming and audacious Captain Dashwood, each character brings their own unique personality and secrets to the hunt. As the story unfolds, unexpected alliances are formed, rivalries ignite, and hidden desires come to the surface. With wit, humor, and a touch of scandal, this captivating tale takes readers on a thrilling ride through the world of fox hunting, where the true nature of these men is revealed.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Masculinity, Social class, Secrets
