Synopsis: 

'Korean Fairy Tales' by Griffis, William Elliot is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that transport readers into the magical world of Korean folklore. From mischievous goblins and cunning animals to brave heroes and beautiful princesses, these tales are filled with adventure, humor, and valuable life lessons. Each story unveils a unique aspect of Korean culture, offering a glimpse into the rich heritage and traditions of this fascinating country. With its vivid imagery and captivating storytelling, 'Korean Fairy Tales' is sure to captivate readers of all ages and leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Fairy tales, Folklore, Children's literature

Themes: Adventure, Morality, Cultural heritage
