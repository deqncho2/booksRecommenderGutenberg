Synopsis: 
In 'Midnight Sunbeams; or, Bits of Travel Through the Land of the Norseman', Edwin Coolidge Kimball takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of Norway. From the majestic fjords to the vibrant cities, Kimball's vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes paint a vivid picture of this fascinating country. As he explores the rich history, folklore, and cultural traditions of the Norseman, the author encounters unexpected adventures and encounters with locals that provide a unique perspective on the land and its people. With each page, readers will be transported to the breathtaking beauty of Norway, immersing themselves in its captivating allure.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Identity, Natural Beauty
