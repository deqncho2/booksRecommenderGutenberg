Synopsis: 
In 'Old Lady Mary: A Story of the Seen and the Unseen', we are introduced to the enigmatic character of Lady Mary, a woman who possesses an uncanny ability to see beyond the ordinary. As the story unfolds, we are drawn into a world where the boundaries between the seen and the unseen blur, and where Lady Mary's unique perspective challenges our understanding of reality. With a cast of intriguing characters and a series of mysterious events, this captivating tale takes us on a journey that explores the power of perception and the hidden depths of the human experience.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Perception, Reality, Mystery, Spirituality
