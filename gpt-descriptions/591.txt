Synopsis: 
'Flame and Shadow' by Sara Teasdale is a captivating collection of poetry that explores the depths of human emotions and the complexities of love, loss, and longing. Through her lyrical and evocative verses, Teasdale takes readers on a journey of self-discovery, delving into the beauty and pain of existence. With each poem, she weaves a tapestry of emotions, painting vivid images that resonate with the reader's own experiences. From the fiery passion of love to the haunting shadows of sorrow, 'Flame and Shadow' is a poetic exploration of the human soul.

Genres: Poetry, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love, Loss, Longing, Self-discovery
