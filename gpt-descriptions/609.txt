Synopsis: In "America, Through the Spectacles of an Oriental Diplomat," Wu, Tingfang takes us on a captivating journey through the eyes of an Oriental diplomat exploring the vast and diverse landscapes of America. From bustling cities to serene countryside, he encounters the unique customs, traditions, and people that shape the American nation. Through his keen observations and insightful anecdotes, Wu, Tingfang offers a fresh perspective on the American way of life, shedding light on both the strengths and challenges of this great nation.

Genres: Travelogue, Cultural Commentary, Memoir

Themes: Cross-cultural exploration, Identity, Nationalism
