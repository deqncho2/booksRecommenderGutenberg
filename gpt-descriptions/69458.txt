Synopsis: 
Uncle Wiggily and Mother Goose is a delightful collection of fifty-two stories, each one perfect for every week of the year. Join Uncle Wiggily, a lovable rabbit with a knack for adventure, as he embarks on whimsical journeys alongside the beloved characters from Mother Goose's nursery rhymes. From encounters with Humpty Dumpty to helping Little Bo Peep find her lost sheep, Uncle Wiggily's escapades are filled with laughter, excitement, and valuable life lessons. This heartwarming collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages and bring joy to storytime throughout the year.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Imagination, Problem-solving
