Synopsis: 
'Wit, Character, Folklore & Customs of the North Riding of Yorkshire: With a Glossary of over 4,000 Words and Idioms Now in Use' by Richard Blakeborough is a captivating exploration of the rich cultural heritage and unique traditions of the North Riding of Yorkshire. Through a collection of anecdotes, folklore, and customs, the author paints a vivid picture of the region's distinctive wit and character. From humorous tales to insightful observations, this book offers a delightful journey into the heart of Yorkshire's cultural tapestry, providing readers with a deeper understanding of its people and their way of life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Cultural Studies, Folklore

Themes: Yorkshire culture, Wit and humor, Regional customs and traditions
