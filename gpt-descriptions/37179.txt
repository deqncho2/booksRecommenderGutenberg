Synopsis: In 'Limbo, and Other Essays; To which is now added Ariadne in Mantua', Lee, Vernon takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking essays and a mesmerizing novella. From exploring the depths of human consciousness to delving into the complexities of love and loss, Lee's writing offers a unique blend of introspection and storytelling. In 'Ariadne in Mantua', readers are transported to a world where myth and reality intertwine, as a young woman navigates the labyrinth of her own emotions. With rich prose and profound insights, this collection invites readers to ponder the mysteries of life and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Essay, Novella, Literary Fiction

Themes: Consciousness, Love, Loss, Mythology, Self-discovery
