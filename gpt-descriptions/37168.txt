Synopsis: 
In the small, secluded village of Norston's Rest, a mysterious secret lies buried beneath the surface. When a young woman named Emily arrives to take up a teaching position, she quickly becomes entangled in the town's dark history and the enigmatic figure of Mr. Norston. As Emily delves deeper into the secrets of Norston's Rest, she uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and supernatural occurrences that threaten to consume her. With each revelation, Emily's life is put at risk, and she must navigate the treacherous path between truth and danger before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, the power of the supernatural, the struggle between good and evil
