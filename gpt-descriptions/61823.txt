Synopsis: In "The Old-Time Spelling School; In Three Parts" by John G. Ellenbecker, readers are transported back to a bygone era where a small community gathers for a traditional spelling bee. As the participants compete, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and unexpected alliances are formed. With each round, the stakes become higher, and the outcome of the spelling bee takes on a deeper significance than anyone could have anticipated. This captivating story explores the power of words, the complexities of human relationships, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Community, Competition, Secrets, Redemption
