Synopsis: In 'The Radio Girls on Station Island: The wireless from the steam yacht', the Radio Girls find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a mysterious message while working at their radio station on Station Island. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they uncover a web of secrets and danger that takes them from the tranquil island to the high seas aboard a steam yacht. With their quick wit and determination, the Radio Girls must navigate treacherous waters and outsmart cunning adversaries to solve the puzzle and protect their newfound knowledge.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Female empowerment, Friendship, Pursuit of knowledge
