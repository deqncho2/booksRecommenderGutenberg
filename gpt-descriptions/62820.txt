Synopsis: In 'The Fire Within' by Patricia Wentworth, a mysterious fire sets off a chain of events that unravels the secrets of a seemingly ordinary English village. As the flames ignite hidden desires and long-buried truths, a web of deceit and betrayal emerges, leaving the residents questioning their own innocence. With each page, the tension builds, and the reader is drawn deeper into the gripping mystery, desperate to uncover the truth before it consumes them all.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Small-town intrigue
