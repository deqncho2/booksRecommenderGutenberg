Synopsis: In "Trouble Near the Sun" by Alan J. Ramm, a brilliant scientist embarks on a dangerous mission to uncover the truth behind a mysterious phenomenon that threatens the very existence of humanity. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a web of secrets, betrayal, and a race against time to save the world from impending doom. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this thrilling sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Consequences of scientific advancements, Sacrifice for the greater good
