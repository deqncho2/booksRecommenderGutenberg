Synopsis: In 'The Amateur Army' by Patrick MacGill, a group of unlikely heroes find themselves thrust into the chaos of war. As they navigate the treacherous trenches and face the horrors of battle, they must learn to rely on each other and their own inner strength. With humor, heart, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the resilience of the human spirit and the power of camaraderie in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
