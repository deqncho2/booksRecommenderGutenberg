Synopsis: 

In 'The Story of the Zulu Campaign', Ashe, Waller/ Edgell, Edmund Verney Wyatt takes readers on a thrilling journey through the intense and dramatic events of the Zulu War. Set in the late 19th century, this gripping historical account follows the British forces as they face the formidable Zulu warriors in a battle for survival and dominance. With vivid descriptions and meticulous attention to detail, the authors bring to life the bravery, strategies, and sacrifices made by both sides in this epic clash of cultures.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Colonialism, Cultural clash, Bravery, Survival
