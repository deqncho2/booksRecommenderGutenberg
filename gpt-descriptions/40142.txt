Synopsis: In 'Miss Stuart's Legacy' by Flora Annie Steel, a young woman named Miss Stuart unexpectedly inherits a mysterious legacy that takes her on a journey of self-discovery and adventure. As she delves into the secrets of her inheritance, Miss Stuart finds herself entangled in a web of intrigue, romance, and danger. With each clue she uncovers, she becomes more determined to uncover the truth behind her legacy, even if it means risking everything she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Inheritance, Self-discovery, Secrets
