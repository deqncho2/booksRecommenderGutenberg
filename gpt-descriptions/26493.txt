Synopsis: In 'The Life of Gordon, Volume II' by Boulger, Demetrius Charles, the captivating story of General Charles Gordon continues as he embarks on a new and perilous journey. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Sudan, this gripping historical biography delves into Gordon's relentless pursuit of justice and his unwavering commitment to his principles. As he faces treacherous landscapes, political intrigue, and personal challenges, Gordon's indomitable spirit shines through, captivating readers with his extraordinary courage and determination. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way and fulfill his mission? Find out in this enthralling second volume of Gordon's remarkable life.

Genres: Historical Biography, Adventure, Political Thriller

Themes: Heroism, Duty, Sacrifice, Colonialism, Religious Conflict
