Synopsis: In 'The Mississippi Bubble: How the Star of Good Fortune Rose and Set and Rose Again, by a Woman's Grace, for One John Law of Lauriston' by Hough, Emerson, readers are transported to 18th century France, where a man named John Law rises from obscurity to become one of the most influential figures in the financial world. As Law's fortunes soar, he becomes entangled in a web of power, greed, and love, leading to a dramatic rise and fall that captivates the reader until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Financial Thriller, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Wealth and Power, Love and Betrayal
