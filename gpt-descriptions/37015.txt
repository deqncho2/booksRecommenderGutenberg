Synopsis: In the small town of Corners, Carolyn leads a seemingly ordinary life as a young woman. However, her world is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a secret that will challenge everything she thought she knew. As Carolyn delves deeper into the secrets of her past, she must navigate love, betrayal, and the dark underbelly of her seemingly idyllic town. With each twist and turn, Carolyn's courage and resilience are put to the test, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the power of love and forgiveness, the search for truth and self-discovery
