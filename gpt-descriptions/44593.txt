Synopsis: 
The Oxford Book of Ballads is a captivating collection of traditional ballads that takes readers on a journey through the rich tapestry of folk storytelling. From haunting tales of love and betrayal to epic adventures and supernatural encounters, this anthology brings together a diverse range of ballads that have been passed down through generations. Each ballad is a window into a different world, filled with vivid characters, gripping narratives, and timeless themes that continue to resonate with readers today. Whether it's the tragic fate of star-crossed lovers or the triumph of justice against all odds, these ballads offer a glimpse into the human experience and the power of storytelling.

Genres: 
Folklore, Poetry, Anthology

Themes: 
Love and Betrayal, Adventure, Supernatural, Justice, Human Experience
