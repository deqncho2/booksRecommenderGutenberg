Synopsis: 
In 'Aunt Jane's Nieces Abroad', three young and spirited nieces embark on a thrilling journey across Europe with their wealthy Aunt Jane. As they explore the enchanting cities and encounter unexpected adventures, the girls find themselves caught up in a web of mystery and intrigue. From the picturesque landscapes of Switzerland to the bustling streets of Paris, their travels lead them to uncover secrets that will test their courage and loyalty. With each new destination, the girls discover the importance of friendship, independence, and the power of embracing new experiences.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Exploration
