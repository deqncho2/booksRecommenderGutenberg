Synopsis: In 'Be Young Again!' by Leinster, Murray, a brilliant scientist discovers a groundbreaking formula that has the power to reverse the aging process. As he tests his creation on himself, he is transported back to his youth, reliving the joys and challenges of his early years. However, he soon realizes that being young again comes with unexpected consequences and difficult choices. This thrilling and thought-provoking tale explores the timeless desire to turn back the hands of time and the profound impact it can have on one's life.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Adventure

Themes: Aging, Regret, Second Chances
