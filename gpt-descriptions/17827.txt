Synopsis: In "The Story of Mattie J. Jackson: Her Parentage—Experience of Eighteen years in Slavery—Incidents during the War—Her Escape from Slavery," we follow the remarkable journey of Mattie J. Jackson, a young woman who endures the hardships of slavery and navigates the challenges of the Civil War. Through her eyes, we witness the resilience and determination of a courageous individual who defies the odds to secure her freedom. This gripping narrative unveils the untold stories of those who fought for their liberty, leaving readers captivated by Mattie's extraordinary tale.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Memoir

Themes: Slavery, Freedom, Resilience, Identity
