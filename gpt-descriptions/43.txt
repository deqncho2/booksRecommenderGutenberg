Synopsis: In the dark and mysterious streets of Victorian London, a respected and well-known doctor named Henry Jekyll becomes entangled in a chilling and perplexing case. As the story unfolds, a sinister alter ego named Edward Hyde emerges, wreaking havoc and terrorizing the city. Driven by a desire to understand the duality of human nature, Dr. Jekyll embarks on a dangerous experiment that unleashes a chain of events with devastating consequences. This gripping tale explores the depths of human morality, the struggle between good and evil, and the consequences of suppressing one's true nature.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, mystery

Themes: Duality of human nature, morality, identity, repression
