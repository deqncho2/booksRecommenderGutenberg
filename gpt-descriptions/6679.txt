Synopsis: In the small town of Dunbury, a mysterious old stone house holds secrets that have been buried for generations. When a young woman named Hester moves into the house, she becomes entangled in a web of family history, forbidden love, and long-buried secrets. As she delves deeper into the house's past, Hester uncovers a haunting tale that will challenge her beliefs and change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Redemption, Identity
