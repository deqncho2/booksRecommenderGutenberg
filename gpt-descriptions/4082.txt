Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Santa Ysabel, a mysterious barrier suddenly appears, cutting off the town from the rest of the world. As the residents struggle to understand the nature of this inexplicable phenomenon, tensions rise and secrets are unearthed, revealing the true nature of the barrier and its impact on the town's inhabitants. With its gripping suspense and intriguing characters, 'The Barrier' takes readers on a thrilling journey of discovery and survival.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Isolation, Secrets, Survival
