Synopsis: 
"The Basket of Flowers" by Christoph von Schmid is a heartwarming tale that follows the life of a young girl named Mary, who lives a simple and contented life with her father, the village gardener. However, when a series of unfortunate events befall her family, Mary's faith and resilience are put to the test. With the help of her father's teachings and the power of forgiveness, Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery and redemption that will touch the hearts of readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Christian Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Faith, Forgiveness, Redemption
