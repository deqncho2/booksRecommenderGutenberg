Synopsis: In 'The Pillar of Light' by Tracy, Louis, a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds a mysterious power. As she delves deeper into its origins, she becomes entangled in a dangerous race against time, facing dark forces that will stop at nothing to possess the pillar of light. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, she must uncover the truth hidden within the artifact before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Power of ancient artifacts, Good vs. evil, Quest for knowledge
