Synopsis: In "Brother Bosch", an Airman's Escape from Germany, Gerald Featherstone Knight takes readers on a thrilling journey through his harrowing escape from a German prisoner-of-war camp during World War II. As an RAF pilot, Knight finds himself captured and imprisoned, but his determination to regain his freedom leads him to embark on a daring and dangerous escape plan. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding moments, Knight's story of survival and resilience will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover if he can outsmart his captors and make it back home.

Genres: Memoir, War, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Courage, Escape
