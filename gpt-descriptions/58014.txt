Synopsis: In 'The Nature of a Gospel Church: A Confession of Evangelical Principles; and the Members' Covenant. Intended for the Use of the Church of Christ.', J. Church delves into the essence of a Gospel Church, exploring its principles and the covenant that binds its members. Through a thought-provoking examination, Church offers insights into the nature of a church and its role in the lives of believers. This work provides a valuable resource for those seeking a deeper understanding of the Church's purpose and the commitment required to be a part of it.

Genres: Religious, Theology, Christian Literature

Themes: Church community, Evangelical principles, Covenant, Spiritual commitment
