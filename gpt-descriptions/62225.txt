Synopsis: 

In 'Battlefield Church, Salop: an historical and descriptive sketch', author W. G. D. Fletcher takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the rich history and intriguing stories surrounding the Battlefield Church in Shrewsbury. From the famous battle that took place in its vicinity to the foundation of the college or chantry, Fletcher weaves together a fascinating narrative that brings this historical site to life. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Architecture, Local history

Themes: Battle and conflict, Religious institutions, Local heritage
