Synopsis: 
In 'That Affair Next Door', a seemingly ordinary woman named Miss Butterworth finds herself entangled in a perplexing mystery when a shocking crime occurs right next door. As she delves deeper into the investigation, Miss Butterworth uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that will keep readers guessing until the very end. With her keen observation skills and sharp intellect, she must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects to uncover the truth. 

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Intrigue, Deception, Perception, Justice
