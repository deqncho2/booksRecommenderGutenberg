Synopsis: In 'A Young Man in a Hurry, and Other Short Stories', Robert W. Chambers takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing tales. From a young man's desperate race against time to a mysterious encounter in a forgotten garden, each story presents a unique and gripping narrative that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. With unexpected twists and turns, these short stories explore the depths of human emotions and the consequences of our choices. Prepare to be enthralled by the suspense and mystery that awaits within these pages.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Time, Choices, Consequences
