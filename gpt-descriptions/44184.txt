Synopsis: 
In 'On the Seaboard: A Novel of the Baltic Islands' by August Strindberg, the reader is transported to the captivating world of the Baltic Islands. The story follows a group of diverse characters who find themselves entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and mystery. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, the idyllic setting becomes a backdrop for intense emotions and unexpected twists. Strindberg's vivid descriptions and complex character dynamics make this novel a compelling exploration of human nature and the power of the sea.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Love and relationships, Betrayal, Secrets, Nature and the elements
