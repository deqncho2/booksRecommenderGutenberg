Synopsis: In the small town of Pineville, a young man named David comes face to face with the challenges and triumphs of life in the North Country during the time of Silas Wright. As he navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and ambition, David finds himself drawn to the mysterious and captivating figure of Silas Wright, a prominent politician and advocate for the common people. Through his encounters with Wright and the people of Pineville, David learns valuable lessons about the power of perseverance, the importance of community, and the enduring light that can be found even in the darkest of times.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Political Drama

Themes: Ambition, Love and Relationships, Community, Social Justice
