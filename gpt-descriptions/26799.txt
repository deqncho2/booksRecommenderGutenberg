Synopsis: 

In 'Narrative of the Captivity of William Biggs among the Kickapoo Indians in Illinois in 1788', William Biggs takes us on a gripping journey through his harrowing experience of being held captive by the Kickapoo Indians. From the moment of his capture to his eventual escape, Biggs recounts the challenges he faced, the cultural clashes he encountered, and the unexpected friendships he formed during his time among the tribe. This captivating narrative offers a unique perspective on the complexities of survival, identity, and the power of human resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Captivity, Cultural clash, Survival, Identity, Resilience
