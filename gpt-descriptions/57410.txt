Synopsis: 

Step back in time to the year 1890 and explore the fascinating world of medicine through the pages of J. & A. Churchill's General Catalogue. This selection showcases a wide range of recent works published by the esteemed Churchill brothers, offering a glimpse into the art and science of medicine during this era. From groundbreaking discoveries to innovative techniques, this catalogue presents a treasure trove of knowledge that will captivate both medical professionals and history enthusiasts alike. Delve into the pages and embark on a journey through the past, where the pursuit of medical advancement knows no bounds.

Genres: Medical literature, Historical reference

Themes: Medicine, Science, History
