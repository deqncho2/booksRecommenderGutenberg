Synopsis: In "Peter of New Amsterdam: A Story of Old New York," readers are transported to the bustling streets of 17th-century New Amsterdam, where they follow the adventures of young Peter Stuyvesant. As Peter navigates the challenges of his new home, he encounters a mysterious girl, uncovers hidden secrets, and becomes entangled in a dangerous plot that could change the fate of the city forever. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this historical fiction novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rich history of New York City.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Courage, Loyalty
