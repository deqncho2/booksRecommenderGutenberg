Synopsis: In 'A Virginia Cousin, & Bar Harbor Tales' by Harrison, Burton, Mrs., readers are transported to the charming world of Virginia and the picturesque landscapes of Bar Harbor. Through a series of captivating tales, the author weaves together stories of love, family, and the complexities of social dynamics. From the vibrant streets of Virginia to the breathtaking shores of Bar Harbor, this collection of stories introduces a cast of intriguing characters whose lives intertwine in unexpected ways. With its rich descriptions and compelling narratives, this book promises to take readers on a delightful journey filled with romance, mystery, and the timeless beauty of these two enchanting settings.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love, Family, Social dynamics
