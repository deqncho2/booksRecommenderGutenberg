Synopsis: In 'They Shall Not Pass' by Frank H. Simonds, a gripping tale unfolds as a young soldier finds himself thrust into the chaos and horrors of World War I. As he navigates the treacherous trenches and faces the relentless onslaught of enemy forces, he must grapple with the true meaning of courage, sacrifice, and the indomitable human spirit. With vivid descriptions and heart-wrenching moments, this novel takes readers on an unforgettable journey through the harrowing landscapes of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Humanity, Survival
