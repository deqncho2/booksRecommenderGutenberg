Synopsis: In the fifth volume of "The Life of George Washington," author John Marshall delves into the pivotal role that Washington played as the Commander in Chief of the American forces during the War of Independence. This captivating biography explores the challenges and triumphs that Washington faced as he led his country to establish its independence. From his strategic military decisions to his unwavering determination, readers will be enthralled by the remarkable journey of this iconic figure. As the first President of the United States, Washington's leadership and legacy continue to shape the nation to this day.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Leadership, Independence, Legacy
