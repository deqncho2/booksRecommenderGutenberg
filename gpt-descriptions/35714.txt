Synopsis: In 'How She Felt in Her First Corset, and Other Poems' by Matthew W. Alderson, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking and emotionally charged poems. From exploring the complexities of love and heartbreak to delving into the depths of self-discovery and empowerment, each poem offers a unique perspective on the human experience. With vivid imagery and lyrical language, Alderson's words evoke a range of emotions, leaving readers captivated and eager to uncover the next poetic gem.

Genres: Poetry, Contemporary Literature

Themes: Love, Heartbreak, Self-discovery, Empowerment
