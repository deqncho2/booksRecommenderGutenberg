Synopsis: 

In 'Idealia, a Utopian Dream; or, Resthaven', Harriet Alfarata Chapman takes readers on a captivating journey to a seemingly perfect world called Idealia. As the protagonist, Clara, embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets of this utopian society, she discovers that not everything is as idyllic as it appears. With unexpected twists and turns, the story delves into the depths of human nature, challenging the reader to question the true meaning of perfection and the sacrifices required to achieve it.

Genres: Utopian fiction, Dystopian fiction, Mystery

Themes: Utopia vs. reality, Human nature, Sacrifice
