Synopsis: In "The Africanders: A Century of Dutch-English Feud in South Africa," Hooker and Le Roy delve into the captivating history of the intense rivalry between the Dutch and English settlers in South Africa. From the early days of colonization to the tumultuous events that shaped the nation, this book uncovers the deep-rooted animosity and power struggles that defined the relationship between these two influential groups. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors bring to life the dramatic conflicts, political maneuverings, and cultural clashes that shaped the destiny of a nation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political history, Cultural studies

Themes: Colonialism, National identity, Ethnic tensions, Power struggles
