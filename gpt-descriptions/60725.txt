Synopsis: In 'Not Snow Nor Rain' by Miriam Allen De Ford, a young woman named Jane finds herself in a peculiar situation when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter addressed to the postmaster of a small town. Intrigued by its contents, Jane embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic message, leading her into a world of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Jane discovers that the power of communication can transcend time and space, ultimately challenging her perception of reality.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Communication, Secrets, Perception
