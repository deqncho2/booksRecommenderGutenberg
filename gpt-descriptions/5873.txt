Synopsis: 
In 'Farewell' by Honoré de Balzac, a young man named Horace de Beauséant finds himself caught between his duty to his family and his desire for personal freedom. As he navigates the complexities of society and love, Horace must make difficult choices that will shape his future. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century France, this captivating tale explores the conflicts between tradition and individuality, duty and passion, and the consequences of one's decisions.

Genres: 
Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: 
Society and Class, Love and Relationships, Personal Freedom
