Synopsis: In the bustling city of London, a young woman named Mary finds herself drawn to the mysterious world of the wharf by the docks. As she becomes entangled in the lives of the dockworkers and their families, she uncovers secrets and scandals that threaten to unravel the very fabric of their community. With her determination and resilience, Mary must navigate the treacherous waters of love, loyalty, and betrayal, all while trying to find her own place in this gritty and captivating world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Social class, Identity, Secrets and betrayal
