Synopsis: 

In 'Hindu Gods And Heroes: Studies in the History of the Religion of India', Lionel D. Barnett takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Hindu mythology and legends. Delving into the fascinating world of gods, goddesses, and heroic figures, Barnett explores the origins, symbolism, and significance of these divine beings in the religious and cultural landscape of India. From the awe-inspiring tales of creation to the epic battles between good and evil, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the Hindu pantheon and the heroic figures that have shaped the religion for centuries.

Genres: Mythology, Religious Studies, History

Themes: Hindu mythology, religious symbolism, cultural significance, heroism, divine beings
