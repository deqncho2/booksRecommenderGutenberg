Synopsis: 
'Byeways in Palestine' by James Finn takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich history of Palestine. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Finn explores the hidden corners and lesser-known paths of this ancient land, uncovering its secrets and shedding light on its diverse culture. From bustling markets to sacred sites, from encounters with locals to reflections on the region's complex political situation, Finn's narrative offers a unique perspective on Palestine, inviting readers to discover the beauty and complexity of this remarkable place.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, History

Themes: Cultural exploration, Identity, Political landscape
