Synopsis: In "My Escape from Donington Hall, Preceded by an Account of the Siege of Kiao-Chow in 1915," Gunther Plüschow takes us on a thrilling journey through his daring escape from a notorious prison camp. But before that, he recounts his harrowing experiences during the intense Siege of Kiao-Chow in 1915, where he faced unimaginable challenges and witnessed the true nature of war. Plüschow's gripping narrative will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover how he manages to break free from captivity and what awaits him on the other side.

Genres: Memoir, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Survival, Resilience, Human spirit
