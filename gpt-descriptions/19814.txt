Synopsis: 

In 'Three years in France with the Guns: Being Episodes in the life of a Field Battery', C. A. Rose takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of a field battery during World War I. Through vivid and detailed storytelling, Rose paints a vivid picture of the challenges, camaraderie, and sacrifices faced by the soldiers on the front lines. From the trenches to the battlefields, the author provides a unique perspective on the war, highlighting the courage and resilience of those involved. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Memoir

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Resilience
