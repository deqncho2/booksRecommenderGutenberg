Synopsis: In 'Dan Carter and the Great Carved Face' by Mildred A. Wirt, a young boy named Dan embarks on a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a mysterious carved face hidden deep in the woods. As Dan delves into the secrets surrounding the ancient artifact, he finds himself caught up in a web of danger and intrigue. With the help of his friends, Dan must unravel the enigma of the Great Carved Face before it falls into the wrong hands. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the ancient relic.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Ancient Mysteries
