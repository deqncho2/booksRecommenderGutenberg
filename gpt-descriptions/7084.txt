Synopsis: In 'A Girl Among the Anarchists' by Meredith, Isabel, a young woman finds herself drawn into the captivating world of anarchists in late 19th century London. As she becomes entangled in their radical ideas and dangerous activities, Isabel must navigate a treacherous path between loyalty and self-discovery. With secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances, this gripping tale explores the blurred lines between idealism and reality, challenging the reader to question their own beliefs and the lengths they would go to fight for what they believe in.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Anarchy, Rebellion, Identity, Morality
