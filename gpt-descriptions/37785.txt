Synopsis: In "Cleopatra's Needle: A History of the London Obelisk, with an Exposition of the Hieroglyphics," James King takes readers on a captivating journey through time, unraveling the mysteries surrounding the ancient Egyptian obelisk that now stands proudly in London. From its origins in the sands of Egypt to its perilous journey across the seas, King delves into the fascinating history of this iconic monument. Along the way, he explores the enigmatic hieroglyphics adorning its surface, offering readers a glimpse into the ancient world and the secrets it holds. Prepare to be enthralled by the tales of adventure, intrigue, and discovery that surround Cleopatra's Needle.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Archaeology, Travel

Themes: Ancient Egypt, Monuments and Landmarks, Cultural Heritage
