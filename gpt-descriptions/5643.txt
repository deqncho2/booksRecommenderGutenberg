Synopsis: 
In 'Ranson's Folly' by Richard Harding Davis, a wealthy and adventurous young man named Ranson finds himself caught up in a thrilling and dangerous adventure while on a trip to South America. As he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and mystery, Ranson must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter fascinating characters, and face unexpected challenges that test his courage and resourcefulness. With his life on the line, Ranson must rely on his wits and determination to survive and unravel the secrets that lie hidden in the heart of the jungle.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Courage, Intrigue
