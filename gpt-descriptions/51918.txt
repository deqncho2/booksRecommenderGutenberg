Synopsis: In 'Pole Baker: A Novel' by Will N. Harben, readers are transported to the small town of Darley, where the enigmatic Pole Baker arrives with a mysterious past. As the townspeople become captivated by his charm and charisma, they soon discover that Pole's presence will unearth long-buried secrets and ignite a series of unexpected events. With its richly drawn characters and a plot filled with twists and turns, this novel delves into the complexities of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Southern Fiction

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Redemption, Small-town dynamics
