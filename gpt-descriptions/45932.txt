Synopsis: In 'The Gasoline Motor' by Harold Whiting Slauson, a brilliant engineer embarks on a thrilling journey to revolutionize transportation with his groundbreaking invention. As he faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected allies and adversaries, the reader is taken on a captivating ride filled with suspense, innovation, and the pursuit of greatness.

Genres: Adventure, Technological Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Innovation, Ambition, Perseverance
