Synopsis: In 'The Russian Ballet' by A. E. Johnson, a young American dancer finds herself immersed in the captivating world of the renowned Russian Ballet. As she navigates the intense training, fierce competition, and complex relationships within the company, she discovers a hidden secret that threatens to unravel everything she has worked so hard for. With passion, ambition, and betrayal intertwining, this gripping tale takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the dazzling world of ballet.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Identity, Artistic Pursuit
