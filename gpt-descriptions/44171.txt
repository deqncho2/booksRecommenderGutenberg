Synopsis: In "The Postal System of the United States and the New York General Post Office," Thomas C. Jefferies takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate workings of the American postal system, with a particular focus on the bustling hub of the New York General Post Office. From the bustling mailrooms to the dedicated postal workers, Jefferies unveils the hidden world behind the delivery of letters and packages, shedding light on the challenges, triumphs, and secrets that lie within. As he delves into the history, operations, and innovations of the postal system, readers will be enthralled by the fascinating stories and anecdotes that bring this vital institution to life.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Memoir

Themes: Communication, Infrastructure, Public Service
