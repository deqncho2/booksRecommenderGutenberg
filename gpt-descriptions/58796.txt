Synopsis: 
In 'Through the Wheat' by Thomas Boyd, readers are transported to the harrowing battlefields of World War I, where they follow the journey of a young American soldier named Private Tom Benton. As Benton navigates the brutal realities of war, he grapples with the complexities of loyalty, camaraderie, and the true meaning of sacrifice. Through vivid and haunting prose, Boyd paints a poignant portrait of the human spirit amidst the chaos and devastation of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Loyalty, Camaraderie, Human Spirit
