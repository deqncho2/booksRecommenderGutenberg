Synopsis: In 'Call Him Nemesis' by Donald E. Westlake, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of danger and deception when he is mistaken for a notorious criminal mastermind. As he navigates through a series of thrilling and unexpected events, he must unravel the truth and outsmart those who are determined to bring him down. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Identity, Deception, Redemption
