Synopsis: 
'Tolstoi for the young: Select tales from Tolstoi' is a captivating collection of short stories written by the renowned author Leo Tolstoy. This book takes readers on a journey through various tales that explore the complexities of human nature, the struggles of morality, and the pursuit of truth. From heart-wrenching love stories to thought-provoking moral dilemmas, each story in this collection offers a unique perspective on life and the human condition. With Tolstoy's masterful storytelling and profound insights, readers will be enthralled by the timeless wisdom and universal themes that permeate these captivating tales.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Classic Literature

Themes: Human nature, Morality, Truth, Love, Life's complexities
