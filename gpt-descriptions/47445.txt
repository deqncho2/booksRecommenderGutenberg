Synopsis: 
In "Twenty Years a Detective in the Wickedest City in the World," Clifton R. Wooldridge takes us on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of a notorious city. As a seasoned detective, Wooldridge shares his captivating experiences, from chasing notorious criminals to unraveling complex mysteries. With each chapter, the reader is immersed in a world of crime, corruption, and danger, where the line between good and evil becomes blurred. From high-stakes investigations to heart-pounding encounters, this gripping memoir offers a unique perspective on the relentless pursuit of justice in the wickedest city in the world.

Genres: Memoir, True Crime, Mystery

Themes: Crime and Justice, Corruption, Morality, Perseverance
