Synopsis: In "The Reason Why: A Careful Collection of Many Hundreds of Reasons for Things Which, Though Generally Believed, Are Imperfectly Understood," Robert Kemp Philp delves into the mysteries and misconceptions that surround various aspects of life. From the origins of common sayings to the hidden meanings behind everyday occurrences, this thought-provoking collection offers a fascinating exploration of the unknown. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Philp uncovers the reasons behind these enigmas, shedding light on the often misunderstood aspects of our world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Trivia

Themes: Curiosity, Knowledge, Perception
