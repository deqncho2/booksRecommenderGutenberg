Synopsis: In 'The Silent Barrier' by Tracy, Louis, a young woman named Marion finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret that threatens to unravel her world. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Marion must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, all while trying to protect her own life. With each step she takes, the stakes grow higher, and Marion must summon her courage and intelligence to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Betrayal, Survival
