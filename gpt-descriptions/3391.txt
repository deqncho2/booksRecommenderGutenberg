Synopsis: In "A Belated Guest," a chance encounter between two old friends leads to a surprising and mysterious turn of events. As they reconnect and reminisce, a hidden secret from the past resurfaces, forcing them to confront the consequences of their actions. With a touch of suspense and intrigue, this story explores the complexities of friendship and the power of long-buried secrets.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Regret, Redemption, Secrets
