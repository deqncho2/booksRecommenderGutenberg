Synopsis: In 'Cry Chaos!' by Dwight V. Swain, a retired CIA operative finds himself entangled in a web of international intrigue when he is unexpectedly drawn into a high-stakes game involving a stolen artifact and a dangerous secret society. As he races against time to unravel the truth, he must navigate a treacherous world of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must confront his own demons and question his loyalties, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Action

Themes: Espionage, Conspiracy, Redemption
