Synopsis: In 'I Go A-Marketing' by Henrietta Sowle, follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Mary as she embarks on a daring adventure to make her dreams come true. Faced with unexpected challenges and unexpected allies, Mary's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test as she navigates the bustling world of marketing. With each step she takes, secrets are unveiled, relationships are formed, and her life takes an unexpected turn. Will Mary succeed in her quest for success and happiness, or will she be forced to confront the harsh realities of the world she has entered?

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Self-discovery, Love, Resilience
