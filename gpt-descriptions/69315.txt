Synopsis: In 'In search of fortune: A tale of the old land and the new' by Gordon Stables, we follow the captivating journey of a young protagonist who embarks on a daring adventure from the familiar comforts of his homeland to the uncharted territories of a new land. Filled with unexpected twists and turns, this enthralling tale explores the challenges, triumphs, and sacrifices that come with the pursuit of fortune and the pursuit of one's dreams. As our protagonist navigates through unfamiliar landscapes and encounters a diverse cast of characters, the story delves into themes of ambition, resilience, and the transformative power of self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Ambition, Resilience, Self-Discovery
