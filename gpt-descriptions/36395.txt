Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a young and determined girl, finds herself in the midst of World War I as she joins the Red Cross to do her part for Uncle Sam. As she navigates the challenges of war, Ruth's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. From aiding wounded soldiers to unraveling mysterious secrets, Ruth's journey in the Red Cross takes her on a thrilling and heartwarming adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Service, Coming of Age
