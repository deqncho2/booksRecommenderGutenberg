Synopsis: In 'Friendship Village' by Gale, Zona, a group of friends find themselves in a picturesque village where secrets, mysteries, and unexpected connections unravel. As they navigate through the enchanting streets and encounter intriguing characters, they soon realize that Friendship Village holds more than meets the eye. With each twist and turn, the bonds of friendship are tested, and the true nature of the village is revealed, leaving the friends questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Fiction, Contemporary

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Identity, Community
