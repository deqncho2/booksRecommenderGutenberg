Synopsis: In 'The Missing Disclaimer' by Samuel J. Sackett, a renowned lawyer finds himself entangled in a high-stakes legal case when a crucial disclaimer mysteriously disappears. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of deceit, corruption, and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel not only his career but also his life. With time running out, he must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and confront his own demons to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Corruption, Redemption
