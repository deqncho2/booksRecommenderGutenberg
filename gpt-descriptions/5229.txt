Synopsis: 
In "Felix O'Day" by Francis Hopkinson Smith, a young and ambitious artist named Felix O'Day finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a hidden secret. As he delves deeper into the enigma, Felix must navigate through a world of art, love, and deception, where nothing is as it seems. With his determination and artistic talent, Felix embarks on a thrilling journey that will test his courage and reveal the true nature of those around him.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Artistic passion, Deception, Identity
