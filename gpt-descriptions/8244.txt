Synopsis: 

In 'The World English Bible (WEB): Esther' by Anonymous, readers are transported to the ancient Persian Empire, where a young Jewish woman named Esther finds herself thrust into a position of power and influence. As she navigates the treacherous world of palace politics and intrigue, Esther must make difficult choices that will not only determine her own fate but also the fate of her people. With danger lurking at every corner and secrets threatening to unravel, Esther's courage and wit are put to the ultimate test. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biblical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Power and Influence, Identity and Belonging, Courage and Sacrifice
