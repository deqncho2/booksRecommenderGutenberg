Synopsis: 

In 'Modern Greek Folklore and Ancient Greek Religion: A Study in Survivals', J.C. Lawson delves into the fascinating world of Greek folklore and its connection to the ancient Greek religion. Through a meticulous exploration of customs, rituals, and beliefs, Lawson uncovers the remarkable survival of ancient Greek religious practices in the modern Greek culture. From the mysterious rituals of the Greek Orthodox Church to the enduring traditions of rural communities, this book offers a captivating journey into the rich tapestry of Greek folklore and its deep-rooted ties to the ancient past.

Genres: Non-fiction, Folklore, Religious Studies

Themes: Cultural continuity, Religious syncretism, Folk traditions
