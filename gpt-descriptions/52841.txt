Synopsis: In 'Index to Library Reports', Moody, Katharine Twining takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden world of library reports. This unique and intriguing index unveils a treasure trove of information, revealing the untold stories and fascinating insights buried within the reports. From the mundane to the extraordinary, Moody's meticulous indexing brings to light the secrets, scandals, and surprises that lie within the pages of these seemingly ordinary documents. As readers delve into this captivating index, they will uncover a world of hidden knowledge and unexpected revelations that will leave them eager to explore the depths of library reports themselves.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, History

Themes: Information retrieval, Archival research, Hidden narratives
