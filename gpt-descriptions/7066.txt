Synopsis: 

'Under the Prophet in Utah; the National Menace of a Political Priestcraft' takes readers on a gripping journey into the heart of Utah, where a powerful religious leader holds sway over the lives of his followers. As the story unfolds, the authors delve into the dark underbelly of this religious community, exposing the hidden dangers and political machinations that threaten not only the residents of Utah but also the entire nation. With vivid descriptions and shocking revelations, the book uncovers a web of deceit, corruption, and manipulation that will leave readers questioning the true nature of power and faith.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political thriller, Investigative journalism

Themes: Religious extremism, Abuse of power, Political corruption, Freedom of religion, Separation of church and state
