Synopsis: In 'Heriot's Choice: A Tale' by Carey, Rosa Nouchette, a young woman named Heriot finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is faced with a life-altering decision. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Heriot must confront her own desires and make a choice that will shape her future. With secrets, unexpected twists, and a cast of intriguing characters, this captivating tale explores the power of choice and the consequences that come with it.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Duty and responsibility, Self-discovery
