Synopsis: 
In the bustling streets of 17th century London, a young prodigal son finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, love, and redemption. As he navigates the treacherous world of the city's underworld, he must confront his own demons and make choices that will shape his destiny. With vivid descriptions of the city's vibrant atmosphere and a cast of intriguing characters, 'The London Prodigal' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of London society.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Identity, Love, Morality
