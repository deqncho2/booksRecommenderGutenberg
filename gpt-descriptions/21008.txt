Synopsis: In 'The Boy With the U. S. Fisheries' by Rolt-Wheeler, Francis, follow the thrilling journey of young Tom as he embarks on an adventure with the U.S. Fisheries. Tom's life takes an unexpected turn when he joins a team of dedicated scientists and fishermen, exploring the vast oceans and encountering dangerous sea creatures. As he learns the secrets of marine life and the importance of conservation, Tom's courage and determination are put to the test. Will he be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and make a difference in the world of fisheries?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Environmental

Themes: Conservation, Exploration, Coming of Age
