Synopsis: 
Nobody's Boy: Sans Famille is a captivating coming-of-age tale that follows the extraordinary journey of a young boy named Rémi. Abandoned as a baby and raised by a kind-hearted musician, Rémi's life takes an unexpected turn when he is sold to a traveling artist. As he embarks on a series of adventures across France, Rémi encounters a colorful cast of characters, faces numerous challenges, and discovers the true meaning of family. With his unwavering spirit and resilience, Rémi's quest for identity and belonging will keep readers enthralled until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Bildungsroman, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Belonging, Resilience, Friendship
