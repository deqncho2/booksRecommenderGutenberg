Synopsis: In 'The Dragon and the Raven; Or, The Days of King Alfred' by G. A. Henty, embark on an epic adventure set in the tumultuous times of King Alfred the Great. Follow the courageous young protagonist, Edmund, as he joins the ranks of the Saxon army and faces the fierce Viking invaders. Amidst battles, sieges, and treacherous plots, Edmund must prove his loyalty and bravery to protect his homeland from the clutches of the ruthless Vikings. Will he emerge victorious and help shape the destiny of England?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Patriotism, Conflict
