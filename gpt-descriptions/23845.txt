Synopsis: In 'Talents, Incorporated' by Murray Leinster, a mysterious organization offers people with extraordinary abilities the chance to use their talents for profit. When a struggling musician, a brilliant mathematician, and a gifted artist join forces with Talents, Incorporated, they soon discover that their unique skills can lead them down a dangerous path. As they navigate a world of corporate espionage and international intrigue, they must decide how far they are willing to go to protect their talents and the secrets they hold. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power of human potential, Ethics of using talents, Consequences of exploiting abilities
