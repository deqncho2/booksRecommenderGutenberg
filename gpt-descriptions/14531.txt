Synopsis: In 'The Singing Man: A Book of Songs and Shadows' by Josephine Preston Peabody, a mysterious and captivating figure known as the Singing Man arrives in a small village, bringing with him a collection of enchanting songs. As the villagers become entranced by his music, they are drawn into a world of shadows and secrets, where the power of music holds both beauty and danger. Through a series of interconnected stories, the Singing Man's songs weave a spellbinding tapestry of love, loss, and the transformative power of art.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Folklore

Themes: Music, Transformation, Secrets, Love, Loss
