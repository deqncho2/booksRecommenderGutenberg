Synopsis: 
In 'The Poetical Works of John Dryden, Volume 1: With Life, Critical Dissertation, and Explanatory Notes', readers are invited into the captivating world of one of the most influential poets of the Restoration period. This volume showcases a collection of Dryden's remarkable poems, including his renowned satires, elegies, and translations. From his witty and sharp social commentaries to his heartfelt expressions of love and loss, each poem offers a unique glimpse into the depth and versatility of Dryden's poetic genius. Accompanied by a comprehensive critical dissertation and enlightening explanatory notes, this volume provides a rich exploration of Dryden's life and works, making it an essential addition to any poetry lover's library.

Genres: Poetry, Literary Criticism, Biography
Themes: Satire, Love, Loss, Social Commentary
