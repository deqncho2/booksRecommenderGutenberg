Synopsis: In 'New England and the Bavarian Illuminati', author Vernon Stauffer delves into the mysterious world of secret societies and their influence on the early history of the United States. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Stauffer uncovers a hidden web of intrigue, connecting the enigmatic Bavarian Illuminati to the birth of New England and its impact on the American Revolution. As the plot unfolds, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through clandestine meetings, coded messages, and the pursuit of power. This thought-provoking exploration sheds light on the untold connections between the Old World and the New, leaving readers questioning the true forces that shaped the course of history.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Conspiracy, Mystery

Themes: Secret societies, Power and influence, Hidden history
