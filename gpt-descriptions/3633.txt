Synopsis: In 'Jezebel's Daughter' by Wilkie Collins, a young woman named Natalie Graybrooke finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and deceit when she inherits a peculiar gift from her deceased stepmother. As she delves deeper into the secrets of her family's past, Natalie becomes the target of a sinister plot that threatens to destroy her happiness and reputation. With the help of a trusted friend, she must navigate through a world of hidden identities, unexpected alliances, and dangerous secrets to uncover the truth and protect her future.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Family secrets, Revenge
