Synopsis: 
In the small country town of Sancerre, a web of jealousy, secrets, and hidden desires begins to unravel, exposing the dark underbelly of its seemingly idyllic facade. As the lives of its inhabitants intertwine, a series of unexpected events and shocking revelations threaten to tear apart the fragile harmony they have carefully constructed. From forbidden love affairs to bitter rivalries, 'The Jealousies of a Country Town' delves into the complex dynamics of human relationships and the destructive power of envy.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Jealousy, Secrets, Desire, Betrayal, Small-town dynamics
