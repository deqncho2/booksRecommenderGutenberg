Synopsis: In 'The Prince and the Pauper, Part 7' by Mark Twain, the lives of the prince and the pauper continue to intertwine as they face unexpected challenges and discover the true meaning of identity. As they navigate through a world of deception and danger, their paths cross with influential figures who hold the power to change their destinies forever. With their lives hanging in the balance, the prince and the pauper must rely on their wits and courage to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Social class, Friendship, Power
