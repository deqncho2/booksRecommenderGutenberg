Synopsis: In 'The Venetian School of Painting' by Phillipps, Evelyn March, embark on a captivating journey through the vibrant and enchanting world of Venetian art. Delve into the lives and works of renowned painters such as Titian, Tintoretto, and Veronese, as their masterpieces come to life on the pages. Uncover the secrets behind their techniques, the influence of the city's unique atmosphere, and the fascinating stories that shaped their artistic careers. From the opulent palaces to the bustling canals, this book offers a mesmerizing exploration of the Venetian School of Painting that will leave readers captivated and inspired.

Genres: Art History, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Renaissance art, Venetian culture, artistic techniques, artistic inspiration
