Synopsis: In "History of Phoenicia," George Rawlinson takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient civilization of Phoenicia, a maritime empire that thrived for centuries. From their legendary seafaring prowess to their influential trade networks, Rawlinson unveils the fascinating history of this enigmatic civilization. Exploring their cultural achievements, religious practices, and political intrigues, the author paints a vivid picture of a civilization that left an indelible mark on the ancient world. As readers delve into the pages of this historical masterpiece, they will uncover the secrets of Phoenicia's rise and fall, encountering tales of conquest, exploration, and cultural exchange along the way.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Maritime history, Trade and commerce, Cultural exchange
