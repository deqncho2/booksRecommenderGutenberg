Synopsis: In 'Songs for a Little House' by Christopher Morley, a young woman named Alice embarks on a journey of self-discovery and adventure as she moves into a charming little house in the countryside. As she settles into her new home, Alice encounters a cast of eccentric characters who each have their own unique stories and secrets. Through music, poetry, and unexpected friendships, Alice learns valuable lessons about love, loss, and the power of embracing the present moment. This heartwarming tale will transport readers to a world filled with enchantment and the beauty of everyday life.

Genres: Fiction, Contemporary, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Love, Embracing the present moment
