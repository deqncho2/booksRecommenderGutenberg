Synopsis: 
In 'The Logic of Chance, 3rd edition', John Venn delves into the fascinating world of probability, exploring its foundations and its wide-ranging applications in various fields. With a focus on the logical aspects of probability theory, Venn takes readers on a journey through the intricate connections between probability, logic, and the realms of moral and social science, as well as statistics. Through thought-provoking analysis and insightful examples, Venn sheds light on the profound implications of probability theory and its role in understanding the uncertainties of our world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Mathematics, Philosophy

Themes: Probability theory, Logic, Applications in social science and statistics
