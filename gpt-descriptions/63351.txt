Synopsis: In "Robert Fulton and the Submarine" by William Barclay Parsons, embark on a thrilling journey through the life of the brilliant inventor, Robert Fulton. From his humble beginnings to his groundbreaking achievements, this captivating biography delves into Fulton's relentless pursuit of innovation and his quest to conquer the depths of the ocean. As Fulton's determination and ingenuity are put to the test, readers will be captivated by the suspenseful twists and turns that shape his remarkable legacy.

Genres: Biography, History, Adventure

Themes: Innovation, Perseverance, Exploration
