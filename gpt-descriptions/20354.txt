Synopsis: In 'Jack of Both Sides: The Story of a School War' by Florence Coombe, a seemingly ordinary school becomes the battleground for an intense war between two rival groups. As tensions rise and alliances shift, Jack finds himself caught in the middle, forced to navigate a treacherous landscape of friendships, loyalties, and secrets. With each side determined to claim victory, Jack must decide where his true allegiance lies and what he is willing to sacrifice to bring peace to his school.

Genres: Young Adult, Coming-of-Age, Drama

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Identity, Conflict
