Synopsis: 
In the wild and rugged West, Alec Lloyd finds himself caught in a web of danger and adventure as he takes on the role of a cowpuncher. From thrilling cattle drives to unexpected encounters with outlaws, Alec must navigate the treacherous landscape of the frontier while facing personal challenges that test his courage and resilience. As he forms unlikely alliances and discovers hidden truths, Alec's journey becomes a gripping tale of survival and self-discovery in the untamed American West.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Survival, Self-discovery
