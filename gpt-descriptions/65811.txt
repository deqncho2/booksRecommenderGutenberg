Synopsis: In 'The Phone Booth Mystery' by John Ironside, a seemingly ordinary phone booth becomes the center of a perplexing and suspenseful tale. When a young woman receives a mysterious phone call that leads to a series of unexpected events, she finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and danger. As she delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the phone booth, she uncovers shocking revelations that challenge her perception of reality. With each twist and turn, the tension escalates, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the truth behind the phone booth mystery.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Perception vs. reality
