Synopsis: In "How the Flag Became Old Glory," Mrs. Emma Look Scott takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of the American flag. From its humble beginnings to its transformation into a symbol of national pride, this book explores the fascinating stories and individuals that shaped the flag's significance. Through vivid storytelling and historical anecdotes, Scott unveils the flag's evolution, revealing the deep emotions and sacrifices associated with it. This engaging narrative will leave readers with a newfound appreciation for the iconic symbol that is Old Glory.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, National identity, Symbolism
