Synopsis: In 'The Exclusives (vol. 2 of 3)' by Charlotte Campbell, Lady Bury, secrets and scandals continue to unravel as the elite world of high society is rocked by a shocking revelation. As the lives of the privileged few intertwine, friendships are tested, alliances are formed, and loyalties are questioned. With each page turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of deceit, ambition, and desire, where nothing is as it seems and everyone has something to hide.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Betrayal, Power, Social Class, Identity
