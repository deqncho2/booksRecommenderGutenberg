Synopsis: In 'The Silver Ring Mystery' by Helen Wells, a group of young nurses find themselves caught up in a puzzling and dangerous mystery when a patient mysteriously disappears from the hospital. As they dig deeper into the case, they uncover a web of secrets, hidden identities, and unexpected connections that will keep readers guessing until the very end. With suspenseful twists and turns, this thrilling mystery will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Trust, Deception, Identity
