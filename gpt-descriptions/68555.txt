Synopsis: 
In the eerie darkness of the night, two telegraph operators find themselves entangled in a series of inexplicable and chilling messages that defy all logic and reason. As they struggle to make sense of the mysterious transmissions, they uncover a haunting secret that threatens to consume them both. With each passing hour, the tension mounts, and the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leaving the operators questioning their own sanity and the very nature of the world they inhabit.

Genres: 
Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: 
Isolation, Fear of the Unknown, Sanity vs. Madness
