Synopsis: In "Madame X: a story of mother-love," a mysterious woman with a hidden past finds herself torn between her love for her son and the secrets she must keep to protect him. As her past threatens to unravel, she must navigate a treacherous world of deception and sacrifice to ensure her son's happiness. This gripping tale explores the depths of a mother's love and the lengths she will go to protect her child.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Motherhood, Sacrifice, Redemption
