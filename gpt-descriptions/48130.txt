Synopsis: 
In 'French Idioms and Proverbs: A Companion to Deshumbert's "Dictionary of Difficulties"', V. Payen-Payne takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of French language and culture. This companion book to Deshumbert's renowned dictionary delves into the fascinating world of idioms and proverbs, offering a unique insight into the nuances and hidden meanings behind these linguistic gems. With vivid examples and engaging explanations, Payen-Payne unravels the mysteries of French expressions, inviting readers to explore the depths of the language and gain a deeper understanding of its cultural context. Whether you are a language enthusiast, a student, or simply curious about the intricacies of French, this book is sure to captivate and enlighten.

Genres: Language, Reference, Cultural Studies
Themes: French language and culture, Idioms and proverbs, Linguistics
