Synopsis: In 'The French and British at Three Rivers', the Public Library of Fort Wayne and Allen County takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical events that unfolded at the strategic location of Three Rivers. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, this book unveils the intense rivalry between the French and British forces, the cunning strategies employed, and the pivotal battles that shaped the destiny of North America. As the tension escalates and alliances are tested, readers will be enthralled by the gripping narrative that brings this pivotal moment in history to life.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Colonial history

Themes: Rivalry, Strategy, Power struggle, Alliance, Colonial expansion
