Synopsis: In 'Rounding up the Raider: A Naval Story of the Great War', Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous waters of the Great War. As the conflict rages on, a young naval officer finds himself at the center of a dangerous mission to track down and capture a notorious raider wreaking havoc on the high seas. With tension mounting and lives hanging in the balance, the protagonist must navigate through enemy territory, facing unexpected challenges and uncovering shocking secrets along the way. Will he succeed in his mission and bring the raider to justice, or will the perils of war prove too great?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Survival
