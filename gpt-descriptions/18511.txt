Synopsis: 

In 'Historic Tales: The Romance of Reality. Vol. 04 (of 15), English', Charles Morris takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of English history. From the legendary King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table to the tumultuous reign of Henry VIII, this volume delves into the fascinating stories and events that have shaped the English nation. With vivid descriptions and engaging narratives, Morris brings to life the triumphs and tragedies of England's past, transporting readers to a world of chivalry, political intrigue, and epic battles. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Heroism and Honor, Love and Betrayal
