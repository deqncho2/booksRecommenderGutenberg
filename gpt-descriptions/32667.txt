Synopsis: In 'The Holes and John Smith' by Ludwig, Edward W., a young boy named John Smith discovers a mysterious set of holes in his backyard that seem to hold a secret power. As he delves deeper into the holes, John uncovers a hidden world filled with adventure, danger, and unexpected friendships. With each hole leading to a different time and place, John must navigate through various challenges and make difficult choices that will ultimately shape his destiny. This captivating tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through time and space, as John Smith unravels the mysteries of the holes and discovers the true power within himself.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Discovery, Friendship, Self-discovery
