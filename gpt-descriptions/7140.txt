Synopsis: In 'The Great Conspiracy, Complete' by John Alexander Logan, a gripping tale unfolds as a young lawyer finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, corruption, and political intrigue. Set against the backdrop of post-Civil War America, this thrilling novel follows the protagonist's relentless pursuit of truth, leading him down a dangerous path where loyalties are tested and secrets are unveiled. As he delves deeper into the conspiracy, he discovers that the stakes are higher than he ever imagined, and the consequences of his actions could change the course of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Political Thriller

Themes: Power and Corruption, Loyalty and Betrayal, Justice and Redemption
