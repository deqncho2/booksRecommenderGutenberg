Synopsis: 

'Fortunes and Dreams: A practical manual of fortune telling, divination and the interpretation of dreams, signs and omens' by Cielo Astra is a captivating guide that delves into the mystical world of fortune telling and dream interpretation. This comprehensive manual offers a wealth of knowledge and techniques to unlock the secrets of the future and decipher the hidden messages within our dreams. From tarot card readings to palmistry, astrology to tea leaf reading, the author explores a wide range of divination practices, providing step-by-step instructions and insightful explanations. With fascinating anecdotes and real-life examples, 'Fortunes and Dreams' takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and enlightenment, revealing the profound connections between the spiritual realm and our everyday lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Self-help

Themes: Divination, Dreams, Personal growth, Spirituality
