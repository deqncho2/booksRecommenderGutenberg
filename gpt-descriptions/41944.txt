Synopsis: 
In 'On Patrol' by John Graham Bower, a young police officer finds himself thrust into a dangerous world of crime and corruption when he is assigned to a notorious neighborhood. As he navigates the treacherous streets, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will test his loyalty and integrity. With each patrol, the stakes get higher, and he must decide how far he is willing to go to protect his community and himself.

Genres: Crime fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Corruption, Morality, Loyalty, Redemption
