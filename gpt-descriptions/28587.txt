Synopsis:
The Roman History of Ammianus Marcellinus takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous reigns of the Emperors Constantius, Julian, Jovianus, Valentinian, and Valens. Through the eyes of Ammianus Marcellinus, a Roman soldier and historian, we witness the rise and fall of empires, political intrigue, military campaigns, and the clash of cultures. From the grandeur of Rome to the distant frontiers, this epic historical account immerses readers in the gripping events that shaped the Roman Empire during this pivotal period.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Biography, Military History

Themes:
Power and Politics, War and Conquest, Cultural Exchange and Conflict
