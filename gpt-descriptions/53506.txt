Synopsis: 
'A Collection of Kachári Folk-Tales and Rhymes' by Anderson, J. D. is a captivating anthology that delves into the rich cultural heritage of the Kachári people. Through a series of enchanting folk-tales and rhythmic rhymes, the reader is transported to a world filled with mythical creatures, brave heroes, and timeless wisdom. From the origins of the Kachári people to their struggles and triumphs, this collection offers a mesmerizing glimpse into their folklore and traditions. Each story and rhyme is beautifully woven with vivid imagery and lyrical language, making it a delightful read for both children and adults alike.

Genres: Folklore, Poetry, Cultural Studies

Themes: Cultural heritage, Mythology, Wisdom, Tradition
