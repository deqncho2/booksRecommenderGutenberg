Synopsis: In 'The Stickit Minister's Wooing, and Other Galloway Stories', S.R. Crockett takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged landscapes of Galloway, Scotland. This collection of stories introduces us to a colorful cast of characters, including a determined minister, a mysterious stranger, and a young woman torn between duty and love. As their lives intertwine, secrets are revealed, loyalties are tested, and unexpected alliances are formed. With its richly descriptive prose and compelling narratives, this book offers a glimpse into the complexities of human nature and the power of love and redemption.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Duty and Sacrifice, Redemption, Love and Relationships
