Synopsis: 
In the thirteenth edition of the 'Popular Official Guide to the New York Zoological Park', William T. Hornaday takes readers on an immersive journey through the captivating world of the renowned zoo. From the majestic lions to the playful monkeys, Hornaday's vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations bring the animals to life, making this guide a must-read for both animal enthusiasts and curious visitors. As readers explore the park's various exhibits, they will uncover fascinating facts about the diverse range of species and gain a deeper understanding of the importance of conservation efforts. Whether you are planning a visit or simply want to delve into the wonders of the animal kingdom, this guide is an invaluable resource that will leave you in awe of the natural world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, Natural History

Themes: Animal conservation, Education, Wildlife preservation
