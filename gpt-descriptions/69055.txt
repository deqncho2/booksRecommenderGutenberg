Synopsis: In 'John de Lancaster: a novel; vol. I.', Richard Cumberland takes us on a captivating journey through the life of the eponymous protagonist. John de Lancaster, a young and ambitious man, finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and political intrigue. As he navigates the treacherous waters of 18th-century England, John must confront his own desires and ambitions while facing the consequences of his actions. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Cumberland weaves a tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what fate has in store for John de Lancaster.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Political intrigue

Themes: Ambition, Love and betrayal, Consequences of actions
