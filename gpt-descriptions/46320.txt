Synopsis: In 'Wulnoth the Wanderer: A Story of King Alfred of England', follow the thrilling adventures of Wulnoth, a young warrior who finds himself caught in the midst of a power struggle for the throne of England. As he journeys through treacherous landscapes and encounters formidable foes, Wulnoth must navigate the complexities of loyalty, honor, and sacrifice. Set against the backdrop of King Alfred's reign, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of Wulnoth and the destiny of England.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Epic

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Power, Identity
