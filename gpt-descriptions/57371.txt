Synopsis: In 'Fenris, the Wolf: A Tragedy' by MacKaye, Percy, a dark and haunting tale unfolds as the ancient Norse myth of Fenrir the wolf is brought to life on the stage. Set against a backdrop of gods and mortals, the play delves into the complex relationships between power, destiny, and the consequences of one's actions. As the story unfolds, the audience is drawn into a world of betrayal, sacrifice, and the eternal struggle between good and evil. With its gripping narrative and richly developed characters, 'Fenris, the Wolf: A Tragedy' takes readers on a captivating journey through the depths of human nature and the forces that shape our destinies.

Genres: Drama, Mythology, Tragedy

Themes: Power, Destiny, Betrayal, Sacrifice, Good vs. Evil
