Synopsis: In the Great Steep's Garden takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of a young girl named Steep, who discovers a hidden garden filled with extraordinary secrets. As she explores the garden's mysteries, Steep encounters a cast of intriguing characters and embarks on a quest that will test her courage and resilience. With vivid descriptions and lyrical prose, Elizabeth Madox Roberts weaves a tale that will leave readers spellbound, eager to uncover the secrets of the Great Steep's Garden.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Discovery, Imagination, Resilience, Friendship
