Synopsis: In 'Notes of a Gold Digger, and Gold Diggers' Guide', James Bonwick takes us on a captivating journey through the world of gold digging, revealing the secrets, strategies, and scandals of those who seek fortune through cunning and manipulation. Through a series of intriguing anecdotes and personal experiences, Bonwick explores the dark underbelly of society, where desire for wealth and power reigns supreme. From the glittering ballrooms of high society to the seedy back alleys of the city, this book uncovers the hidden truths behind the art of gold digging, leaving readers both fascinated and appalled by the lengths some will go to achieve their ambitions.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Social Commentary

Themes: Wealth and Power, Manipulation, Morality, Social Class
