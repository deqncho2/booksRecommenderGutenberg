Synopsis: In 'The Texan Scouts: A Story of the Alamo and Goliad', Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on a thrilling journey through the Texas Revolution. Set against the backdrop of the Alamo and Goliad, this gripping tale follows a group of Texan scouts as they navigate the treacherous terrain, face formidable enemies, and make sacrifices for the cause of freedom. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Altsheler brings to life the bravery and resilience of the Texan fighters, capturing the spirit of a pivotal moment in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Patriotism, Freedom
