Synopsis: 

In 'New Zealand Moths and Butterflies (Macro-Lepidoptera)', renowned entomologist George Vernon Hudson takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of these delicate creatures. From the vibrant colors of the butterflies to the intricate patterns of the moths, Hudson's meticulous observations and stunning illustrations bring these fascinating insects to life. As he explores the diverse habitats and behaviors of these winged wonders, readers will be transported to the breathtaking landscapes of New Zealand, discovering the hidden secrets and remarkable adaptations of its native lepidopteran species. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Science

Themes: Biodiversity, Conservation, Natural Beauty
