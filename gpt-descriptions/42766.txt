Synopsis: 

In 'Report of the Committee Appointed to Investigate the Railroad Riots in July, 1877', the Pennsylvania General Assembly delves into the tumultuous events that unfolded during the railroad riots of July 1877. This gripping report uncovers the underlying causes, the violent clashes, and the aftermath of one of the most significant labor uprisings in American history. Through meticulous investigation and testimonies, the committee sheds light on the complex dynamics between workers, corporations, and the government, revealing a society on the brink of social and economic transformation.

Genres: Historical, Political, Investigative

Themes: Labor rights, Social inequality, Government response to civil unrest
