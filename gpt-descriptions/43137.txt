Synopsis: 
In 'The Girl and Her Fortune' by L. T. Meade, a young woman named Alice finds herself unexpectedly inheriting a vast fortune from a distant relative. As she navigates her newfound wealth and the challenges that come with it, Alice discovers that her fortune holds secrets and mysteries that she never could have imagined. With the help of a charming and enigmatic stranger, she embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind her inheritance, leading her to unexpected places and encounters that will change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Self-discovery
