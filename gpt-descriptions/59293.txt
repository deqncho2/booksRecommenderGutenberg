Synopsis: 
In 'My Spanish Year' by Whishaw, Ellen M., we follow the captivating journey of Sarah, a young woman who embarks on a life-changing adventure in Spain. Leaving behind her familiar surroundings, she immerses herself in the vibrant culture, language, and traditions of her new home. As Sarah navigates the challenges of adapting to a foreign country, she discovers unexpected friendships, uncovers hidden secrets, and experiences the transformative power of love. With each passing day, Sarah's Spanish year becomes a transformative odyssey that will forever shape her perspective on life.

Genres: 
Contemporary Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Travel

Themes: 
Cultural immersion, Self-discovery, Friendship, Love, Identity
