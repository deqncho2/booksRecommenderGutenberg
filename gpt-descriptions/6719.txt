Synopsis: In 'The Earth Trembled' by Edward Payson Roe, a small town is shaken to its core when a mysterious event rocks the community, leaving its residents questioning their faith, relationships, and the very fabric of their existence. As the tremors continue, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the town's inhabitants must confront their deepest fears and desires. With each passing tremor, the tension escalates, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Suspense

Themes: Faith and doubt, Secrets and revelations, Community and relationships
