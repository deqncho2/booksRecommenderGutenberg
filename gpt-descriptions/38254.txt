Synopsis: 
In 'Bart Keene's Hunting Days; or, The Darewell Chums in a Winter Camp' by Chapman, Allen, a group of adventurous friends embark on a thrilling winter camping trip. As they navigate through the snowy wilderness, they encounter unexpected challenges and face the test of their survival skills. With danger lurking in the shadows, the Darewell Chums must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to overcome the obstacles that come their way. Will they emerge victorious from this icy adventure?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Outdoor

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Adventure
