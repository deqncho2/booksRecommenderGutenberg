Synopsis: 

In 'The Great Events by Famous Historians, Volume 08: The Later Renaissance: from Gutenberg to the Reformation', readers are transported to a pivotal period in history where the world was on the cusp of transformation. This volume delves into the fascinating era that witnessed the birth of the printing press, the exploration of new lands, and the profound impact of the Reformation. From the groundbreaking works of Gutenberg to the revolutionary ideas of Martin Luther, this book unveils the intricate tapestry of events that shaped the course of Western civilization. 

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Renaissance, Printing Revolution, Exploration, Reformation
