Synopsis: 

In 'A report on the feasibility and advisability of some policy to inaugurate a system of rifle practice throughout the public schools of the country', Critchfield, Ammon B., National Board for the Promotion of Rifle Practice, and Wingate, George Wood present a thought-provoking examination of a controversial proposal. This report delves into the potential benefits and drawbacks of implementing a system of rifle practice in public schools, exploring the impact it could have on students, society, and the nation as a whole. Through a comprehensive analysis, the authors shed light on the complexities surrounding this policy, leaving readers with much to ponder.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, Policy Analysis

Themes: Gun control, Education reform, Youth development
