Synopsis: 
In 'The Killer' by Oliver, J. T., a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a twisted game of cat and mouse with a cunning serial killer. As the body count rises and the clues become increasingly cryptic, the detective must delve into the darkest corners of his own psyche to outsmart the elusive murderer. With each step closer to the truth, the detective's own sanity is pushed to the brink, blurring the line between hunter and hunted. This gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the nature of good and evil until the final shocking revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Serial killers, Psychological manipulation, Moral ambiguity
