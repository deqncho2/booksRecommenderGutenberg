Synopsis: In "Mars is My Destination" by Frank Belknap Long, we follow the captivating journey of a man named Kenton Stanfield who is determined to leave Earth behind and make his way to the mysterious and alluring planet of Mars. Faced with numerous obstacles and dangers, Kenton's quest for a new life on Mars becomes a thrilling adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Human ambition, Survival
