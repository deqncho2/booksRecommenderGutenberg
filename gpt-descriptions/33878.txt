Synopsis: In 'At the Ghost Hour. The House of the Unbelieving Thomas' by Paul Heyse, a young man named Thomas finds himself drawn to a mysterious house that is said to be haunted. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the house, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and supernatural occurrences that challenge his beliefs and sanity. With each passing hour, Thomas becomes entangled in a haunting tale that blurs the line between reality and the supernatural, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Belief vs. skepticism, the power of the supernatural, the fragility of the human mind
