Synopsis: In 'Peeps at Many Lands: Canada', join the adventurous journey of a group of young explorers as they embark on a thrilling expedition across the vast and diverse landscapes of Canada. From the bustling city of Toronto to the breathtaking Rocky Mountains, and from the charming coastal towns of Nova Scotia to the remote wilderness of the Yukon, the young explorers encounter fascinating cultures, encounter wildlife, and uncover the rich history of this captivating country. As they navigate through the stunning landscapes and encounter unexpected challenges, the young explorers learn valuable lessons about friendship, resilience, and the importance of embracing new experiences.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Children's Literature

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Diversity, Friendship, Resilience
