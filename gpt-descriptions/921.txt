Synopsis: In 'De Profundis', Oscar Wilde takes readers on a deeply introspective journey as he reflects on his life, his art, and his imprisonment. Through a series of heartfelt letters, Wilde explores themes of love, suffering, and redemption, offering a profound and poignant exploration of the human condition.

Genres: Autobiography, Epistolary, Literary Nonfiction

Themes: Love, Suffering, Redemption
