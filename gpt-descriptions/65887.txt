Synopsis: 
In 'A Living Lie' by Paul Bourget, a young and talented artist named André Mariolle finds himself caught in a web of deception and desire when he becomes infatuated with a captivating woman named Madame de Burne. As André's obsession grows, he becomes entangled in a complex game of love, betrayal, and self-discovery, where appearances can be deceiving and the truth is often elusive. Set against the backdrop of Parisian society, this gripping tale explores the depths of human emotions and the consequences of living a life built on lies.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Obsession, Identity, Love and Betrayal
