Synopsis: 
In 'Beadle's Dime Book of Practical Etiquette for Ladies and Gentlemen', an anonymous author presents a comprehensive guide to navigating the intricacies of society with grace and elegance. Filled with practical advice and insightful observations, this book offers a glimpse into the world of true gentility and good-breeding. From proper introductions and conversation etiquette to dress codes and social customs, the author leaves no stone unturned in their quest to educate readers on the usages and observances of society. Whether you are a lady or a gentleman, this indispensable guide will help you navigate social situations with confidence and poise.

Genres: Self-help, Etiquette, Reference

Themes: Social etiquette, Manners, Social customs
