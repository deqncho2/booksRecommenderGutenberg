Synopsis: In "Mother's Knitter: Containing some patterns of things for little children," Elvina Mary Corbould takes readers on a delightful journey through the world of knitting for children. From adorable baby booties to cozy blankets, this book is filled with charming patterns that will captivate both experienced knitters and beginners alike. As readers explore the pages, they will discover the joy of creating handmade treasures for the little ones in their lives. With detailed instructions and beautiful illustrations, Corbould's book is a must-have for anyone looking to add a touch of warmth and love to their knitting projects.

Genres: Craft, Non-fiction

Themes: Knitting, Creativity, Motherhood
