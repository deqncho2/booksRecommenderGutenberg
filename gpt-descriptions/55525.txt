Synopsis: In 'Trains at Work' by Mary Elting, embark on an exciting journey through the world of trains as they tirelessly work to transport goods and people across vast landscapes. From bustling train stations to remote tracks, this captivating book takes readers on a thrilling ride, showcasing the various types of trains and the important roles they play in our daily lives. With vivid illustrations and engaging descriptions, 'Trains at Work' offers a fascinating glimpse into the fascinating world of locomotives, leaving readers eager to learn more about these incredible machines.

Genres: Children's Literature, Non-fiction

Themes: Transportation, Engineering, Exploration
