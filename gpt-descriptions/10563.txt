Synopsis: In 'Good Intentions: Ship's Company, Part 3,' the reader is taken on a thrilling journey aboard the Good Intentions ship, where unexpected events and mysterious characters unfold. As the crew navigates treacherous waters, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and deceit. With danger lurking at every turn, the crew must rely on their wits and courage to uncover the truth and ensure their survival. Will their good intentions be enough to overcome the challenges they face?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Survival, Loyalty
