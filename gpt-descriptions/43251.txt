Synopsis: In 'Yellow Thunder, Our Little Indian Cousin', readers are transported to the fascinating world of a young Native American boy named Yellow Thunder. Through his eyes, we witness the daily life, traditions, and adventures of his tribe, the Sioux. From hunting expeditions to tribal ceremonies, Yellow Thunder's story is filled with excitement, friendship, and a deep connection to nature. As he navigates the challenges of growing up in a changing world, readers will be captivated by his resilience and the rich cultural heritage he represents.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: Native American culture, Coming of age, Nature and spirituality
