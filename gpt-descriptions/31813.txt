Synopsis: In 'Checkers: A Hard-luck Story' by Blossom, Henry, we follow the journey of Checkers, a lovable and determined dog who faces numerous challenges in his quest for a forever home. From heartwarming encounters to heart-wrenching setbacks, Checkers' resilience and unwavering spirit will captivate readers as they root for him to find the love and happiness he deserves. This heartwarming tale is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Animal Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Perseverance, Hope, Friendship, Belonging
