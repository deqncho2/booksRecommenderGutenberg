Synopsis: In 'Prejudices, fourth series', H. L. Mencken takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through his sharp and unapologetic observations of American society. With his signature wit and biting humor, Mencken delves into various topics, from politics and religion to literature and culture, challenging conventional wisdom and exposing the flaws and contradictions of the world around us. Through his incisive essays, he invites readers to question their own beliefs and prejudices, offering a refreshing and often controversial perspective on the complexities of human nature and the society we inhabit.

Genres: Essay collection, Satire, Social commentary

Themes: Prejudice, Society, Culture, Politics, Religion, Literature
