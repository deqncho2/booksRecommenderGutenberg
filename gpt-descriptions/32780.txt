Synopsis: In "Asteroid of Fear" by Raymond Z. Gallun, a group of scientists embarks on a perilous mission to investigate a mysterious asteroid hurtling towards Earth. As they venture into the unknown, they encounter unimaginable terrors and face the ultimate test of their courage and resilience. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, the scientists must confront their deepest fears and make unimaginable sacrifices to save the world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Human Resilience
