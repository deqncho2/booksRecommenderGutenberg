Synopsis: In 'Aeroplanes' by Zerbe, James Slough, a young engineer named Ethan finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret within the aviation industry. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. With his life on the line, Ethan must navigate a treacherous path filled with deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the elusive Aeroplanes project.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden agendas, Technological advancements and their consequences
