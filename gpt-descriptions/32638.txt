Synopsis: 
In the Dark by Clark, Dale is a gripping psychological thriller that follows the life of a young woman named Emma, who wakes up one morning to find herself trapped in a pitch-black room with no memory of how she got there. As she desperately tries to piece together the events leading up to her confinement, Emma discovers shocking secrets about her past and the people around her. With time running out and her sanity hanging by a thread, she must unravel the truth before it consumes her completely.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Memory loss, Identity, Deception, Survival
