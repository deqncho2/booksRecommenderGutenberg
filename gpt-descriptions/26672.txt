Synopsis: 
'Bookbinding, and the Care of Books: A Handbook for Amateurs, Bookbinders & Librarians' by Cockerell, Douglas is a comprehensive guide that takes readers on a journey into the art of bookbinding and the preservation of books. From the basics of materials and tools to advanced techniques, this handbook provides invaluable knowledge for both amateurs and professionals in the field. With detailed instructions and practical tips, readers will discover the secrets behind creating beautiful and durable bookbindings, as well as learn how to properly care for and maintain their cherished books. Whether you are a book lover, a bookbinder, or a librarian, this book is an essential resource that will deepen your appreciation for the craftsmanship and artistry of bookbinding.

Genres: Non-fiction, Crafts & Hobbies, Reference

Themes: Bookbinding, Preservation, Craftsmanship, Knowledge
