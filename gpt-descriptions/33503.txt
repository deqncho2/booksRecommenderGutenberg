Synopsis: In "The Sweating Sickness: A boke or counseill against the disease commonly called the sweate or sweatyng sicknesse," John Caius delves into the mysterious and deadly Sweating Sickness that plagued England during the 16th century. Through meticulous research and personal experiences, Caius uncovers the origins, symptoms, and potential cures for this enigmatic disease. As he navigates through the panic and devastation caused by the Sweating Sickness, Caius offers valuable advice and precautions to prevent its spread, ultimately aiming to protect the population from this terrifying epidemic.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, medical literature, epidemiology

Themes: Disease outbreak, public health, historical medicine
