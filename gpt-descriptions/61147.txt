Synopsis: In 'The Day of Glory' by Dorothy Canfield Fisher, a young woman named Mary is faced with a life-altering decision that challenges her beliefs and forces her to confront the complexities of love, duty, and sacrifice. Set against the backdrop of World War I, Mary's journey takes her from the tranquil countryside of Vermont to the war-torn landscapes of France, where she must navigate the intricacies of loyalty and personal growth. As she grapples with the consequences of her choices, Mary discovers the true meaning of courage and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Duty and Responsibility, Personal Growth
