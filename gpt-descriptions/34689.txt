Synopsis: In 'The Wishing Well' by Mildred A. Wirt, a young girl named Peggy discovers a mysterious well hidden deep within the woods near her home. Intrigued by its magical reputation, she decides to make a wish, unaware of the consequences it may bring. As Peggy's wish starts to come true, she finds herself caught in a whirlwind of unexpected events and must navigate through challenges and secrets to uncover the truth behind the well's powers. With each wish, Peggy's life becomes more entangled in a web of enchantment and danger, leading her on a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and resilience.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Friendship, Self-discovery
