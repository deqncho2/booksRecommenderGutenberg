Synopsis: In 'The Social Secretary' by David Graham Phillips, a young woman named Miss Madison is thrust into the glamorous and cutthroat world of high society as a social secretary. As she navigates the intricate web of power, wealth, and scandal, she must learn to balance her own ambitions with the demands of her influential employers. With secrets lurking behind every elegant facade, Miss Madison finds herself entangled in a dangerous game where loyalty and betrayal are constantly at odds. Will she be able to maintain her integrity and survive in this treacherous world?

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Social hierarchy, Ambition, Betrayal, Identity
