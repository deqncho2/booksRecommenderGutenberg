Synopsis: In the heart of Washington D.C., a young economist stumbles upon a dark secret hidden within the walls of the Federal Reserve. As he delves deeper into the mysterious workings of the institution, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy, power struggles, and unimaginable consequences. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path to expose the truth and protect the nation from an insidious monster lurking in the shadows.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Government corruption, Financial manipulation, Personal sacrifice
