Synopsis: In "A Yankee Girl at Shiloh" by Alice Turner Curtis, follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Sylvia as she finds herself caught in the midst of the American Civil War. When her father is called to serve in the Union Army, Sylvia's life takes an unexpected turn as she embarks on a perilous adventure to reunite with her family. Along the way, she encounters both friends and foes, facing the harsh realities of war and discovering her own strength and resilience. Set against the backdrop of the historic Battle of Shiloh, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover Sylvia's fate.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War, Family, Courage, Resilience
