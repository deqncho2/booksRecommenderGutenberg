Synopsis: In "The Night the Mountain Fell: The Story of the Montana-Yellowstone Earthquake," author Edmund Christopherson takes readers on a gripping journey through one of the most devastating natural disasters in American history. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Montana wilderness, this true story unfolds with heart-stopping intensity as a powerful earthquake strikes unexpectedly, leaving a trail of destruction in its wake. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Christopherson brings to life the harrowing experiences of the survivors, the heroic efforts of rescue teams, and the profound impact of this catastrophic event on the local community. As the tale unfolds, readers will be captivated by the resilience and courage displayed in the face of unimaginable adversity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Natural Disaster

Themes: Resilience, Community, Survival, Natural Disasters
