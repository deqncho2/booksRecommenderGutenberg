Synopsis: In 'Treasure and Trouble Therewith: A Tale of California' by Geraldine Bonner, a young woman named Mary is thrust into a thrilling adventure when she inherits a mysterious treasure map. Determined to uncover the secrets it holds, Mary embarks on a perilous journey through the rugged landscapes of California, encountering danger, romance, and unexpected allies along the way. As she delves deeper into the treasure hunt, Mary must navigate treacherous waters and face the consequences of her choices, all while discovering her own strength and resilience.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Perseverance
