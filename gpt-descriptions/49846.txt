Synopsis: In 'Florizel's Folly' by John Ashton, a young and ambitious architect named Florizel embarks on a daring project that pushes the boundaries of imagination and challenges societal norms. As he delves deeper into his creation, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal that threatens to unravel everything he has worked for. With each twist and turn, Florizel must navigate the treacherous waters of ambition and passion, ultimately questioning the true cost of his dreams.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Love, Betrayal, Society's Expectations
