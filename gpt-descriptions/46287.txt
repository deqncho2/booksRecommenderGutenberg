Synopsis: 
'The Book of Vagabonds and Beggars, with a Vocabulary of Their Language' takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden world of vagabonds and beggars. This intriguing book delves into the lives, customs, and secret language of these marginalized individuals, offering a unique glimpse into their daily struggles and extraordinary resilience. As readers explore the pages of this enlightening work, they will encounter a rich tapestry of characters, each with their own captivating stories and unexpected twists. From the bustling streets of London to the remote corners of the countryside, this book paints a vivid portrait of a society often overlooked and misunderstood. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Linguistics

Themes: Marginalization, Survival, Language and Communication
