Synopsis: In 'Fred Fenton Marathon Runner: The Great Race at Riverport School' by Chapman, Allen, follow the thrilling journey of Fred Fenton as he prepares for the biggest race of his life at Riverport School. As Fred faces challenges, unexpected friendships, and intense competition, readers will be captivated by his determination and perseverance. Will Fred be able to overcome the obstacles and achieve his dreams?

Genres: Children's Literature, Sports Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Competition
