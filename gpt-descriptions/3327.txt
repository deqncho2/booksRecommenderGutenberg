Synopsis: 
In 'Bulfinch's Mythology: The Age of Fable', Thomas Bulfinch takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of ancient myths and legends. From the heroic deeds of Greek gods and goddesses to the epic tales of Norse mythology, this book unveils a tapestry of timeless stories that have shaped cultures and inspired generations. With vivid descriptions and engaging narratives, Bulfinch brings to life the adventures of mythical heroes and the whimsical realms they inhabit. From the creation of the universe to the fates of mortals and immortals alike, this collection of myths is a treasure trove of wonder and imagination.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Classics

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Creation, Gods and Goddesses
