Synopsis: In 'The Paladins of Edwin the Great' by Clements R. Markham, a captivating tale unfolds as a group of valiant knights embark on a perilous quest to protect their kingdom from an ancient evil. As they face treacherous landscapes, formidable foes, and unexpected alliances, the fate of their realm hangs in the balance. With secrets to uncover and sacrifices to be made, the paladins must summon their courage and loyalty to fulfill their sworn duty. Will they triumph against all odds, or will darkness prevail?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Good vs. Evil, Sacrifice
