Synopsis: In 'True Stories of Girl Heroines' by Everett-Green, Evelyn, embark on a captivating journey through the lives of remarkable young women who defy societal expectations and overcome extraordinary challenges. From courageous adventurers to brilliant inventors, these inspiring stories will transport readers to different times and places, showcasing the indomitable spirit and resilience of girl heroines. Each tale is filled with suspense, heartwarming moments, and unexpected twists, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover the incredible feats accomplished by these extraordinary girls.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Empowerment, Breaking Gender Stereotypes
