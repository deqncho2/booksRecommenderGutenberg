Synopsis: In 'Missing Link' by Herbert, Frank, a renowned scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything we know about human evolution. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger, forcing him to question the very nature of humanity itself. With thrilling twists and thought-provoking revelations, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Evolution, Identity, Conspiracy
