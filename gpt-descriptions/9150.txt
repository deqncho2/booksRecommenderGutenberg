Synopsis: 
In 'Dick Sands, the Boy Captain' by Jules Verne, a young boy named Dick Sands finds himself thrust into a thrilling adventure when his ship is wrecked in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean. Stranded on a deserted island, Dick must navigate treacherous waters, encounter dangerous animals, and face unexpected challenges as he strives to survive and find his way back home. Along the way, he forms unlikely alliances and discovers hidden secrets that will test his courage and determination. Will Dick Sands prove himself as a true captain and overcome the perils that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Friendship, Exploration
