Synopsis: 
Step into the world of George Eliot, one of the most celebrated authors of the 19th century, through the pages of this captivating catalogue. Delve into a treasure trove of relics, manuscripts, prints, paintings, photographs, and books that offer a unique glimpse into the life and works of this literary genius. From the personal artifacts that shaped her writing to the artistic interpretations of her characters, this collection is a testament to the enduring legacy of George Eliot. Discover the secrets behind her masterpieces and unravel the mysteries that surrounded her life, as you embark on a journey through the captivating world of this remarkable author.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Literature, Victorian Era, Feminism, Identity, Artistic Expression
