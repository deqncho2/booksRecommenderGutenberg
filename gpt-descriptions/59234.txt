Synopsis: 

In 'Obstetrical Nursing: A Text-Book on the Nursing Care of the Expectant Mother, the Woman in Labor, the Young Mother and Her Baby', author Carolyn Conant Van Blarcom takes readers on an enlightening journey into the world of obstetrical nursing. Through a comprehensive exploration of the nursing care provided to expectant mothers, women in labor, and young mothers and their babies, this text-book offers a wealth of knowledge and practical guidance for nurses in this specialized field. From prenatal care to postpartum support, the author delves into the intricacies of obstetrical nursing, providing valuable insights and strategies for ensuring the well-being of both mother and child. With a focus on evidence-based practices and compassionate care, this book serves as an indispensable resource for nurses seeking to enhance their skills and provide exceptional care to their patients.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Nursing

Themes: Obstetrical nursing, Prenatal care, Labor and delivery, Postpartum care, Maternal and infant health
