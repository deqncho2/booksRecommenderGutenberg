Synopsis: In "Mary S. Peake: The Colored Teacher at Fortress Monroe," Lewis C. Lockwood delves into the remarkable life of Mary S. Peake, an influential figure in American history. Set against the backdrop of the Civil War, this captivating biography explores Peake's journey as she defies societal norms and becomes a beacon of hope for her community. From her humble beginnings to her pivotal role in education, Peake's story is one of resilience, determination, and the power of education to transform lives. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be drawn into the inspiring tale of a woman who overcame adversity and left an indelible mark on the fight for equality.

Genres: Biography, History, Civil Rights

Themes: Resilience, Education, Equality
