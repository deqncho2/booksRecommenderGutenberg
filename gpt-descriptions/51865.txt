Synopsis: In 'The Lost Fruits of Waterloo' by John Spencer Bassett, a mysterious discovery in an old attic leads a young historian on a captivating journey through time. As he delves into the secrets of the past, he uncovers a hidden world of forgotten treasures, lost love, and the untold stories of those who fought in the Battle of Waterloo. With each revelation, the historian becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that challenges his understanding of history and his own identity. This gripping tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: History, Identity, Secrets, Love, Redemption
