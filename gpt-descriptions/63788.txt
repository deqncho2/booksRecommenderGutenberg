Synopsis: 

In "Plants Poisonous to Live Stock" by Harold Cecil Long, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the world of toxic plants and their impact on livestock. From the mysterious allure of deadly nightshade to the unsuspecting dangers of common pasture plants, this book unveils the hidden threats that can lurk within a seemingly harmless field. As the author delves into the fascinating history, symptoms, and prevention methods surrounding these poisonous plants, readers will find themselves engrossed in a wealth of knowledge that is both enlightening and essential for anyone involved in animal husbandry. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Agriculture, Veterinary Medicine

Themes: Animal health, Plant toxicity, Livestock management
