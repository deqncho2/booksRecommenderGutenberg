Synopsis: In 'Once to Every Man' by Larry Evans, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic charm and uncanny abilities. As the townspeople become entangled in his web, they soon discover that his presence brings both blessings and curses, forcing them to confront their deepest desires and darkest secrets. With each passing day, the stranger's true intentions become more elusive, leaving the townsfolk questioning their own sanity and the nature of reality itself. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Morality, Power, Perception
