Synopsis: In 'The Impossibles' by Garrett, Randall/ Janifer, Laurence M., a group of ordinary people with extraordinary abilities are recruited by a secret organization to protect the world from supernatural threats. As they navigate through dangerous missions and unravel the mysteries surrounding their own powers, they must learn to trust each other and harness their unique gifts to save humanity from impending doom. With a blend of action, suspense, and a touch of humor, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Superhuman abilities, Teamwork, Identity
