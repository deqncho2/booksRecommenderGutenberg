Synopsis: In the small mining town of Montsou, a young coal miner named Étienne Lantier arrives seeking work. As he becomes acquainted with the harsh conditions and struggles faced by the miners and their families, he becomes increasingly drawn to the idea of organizing a strike to demand better wages and improved working conditions. Amidst the backdrop of poverty, exploitation, and social inequality, Étienne's determination to bring about change ignites a powerful movement that will test the limits of both the workers and the ruling class.

Genres: Historical fiction, social realism, naturalism

Themes: Social injustice, class struggle, labor rights, poverty, industrialization
