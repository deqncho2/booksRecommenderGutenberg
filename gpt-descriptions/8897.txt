Synopsis:
In the bustling city of Prague, Nina Balatka, a young Jewish woman, finds herself torn between her love for a Christian man and the expectations of her community. As she navigates the complexities of her relationships and the prejudices of society, Nina must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the traditions that bind her. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Europe, this captivating novel explores themes of love, identity, and the struggle for acceptance in a world divided by religion and social class.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Cultural Fiction

Themes:
Love and Relationships, Identity, Prejudice and Discrimination
