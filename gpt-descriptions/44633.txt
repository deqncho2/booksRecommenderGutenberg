Synopsis: 
In 'Held to Answer: A Novel' by MacFarlane, Peter Clark, a small-town lawyer named John Harper finds himself caught in a web of deceit and corruption when he takes on a seemingly straightforward case. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel the fabric of his community. With his reputation and even his life on the line, Harper must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and bring justice to those who have been wronged.

Genres: Mystery, Legal Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Justice, Betrayal
