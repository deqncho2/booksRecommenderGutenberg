Synopsis: In "Molly Bawn" by Duchess, a young and vivacious woman named Molly finds herself caught in a web of love, scandal, and societal expectations. When she catches the eye of a wealthy and influential man, Molly's life takes an unexpected turn as she navigates the complexities of romance, loyalty, and personal identity. As secrets unravel and hearts are tested, Molly must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Victorian Literature

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Expectations, Personal Identity
