Synopsis: In 'Second Childhood' by Clifford D. Simak, a mysterious phenomenon begins to affect the elderly population, causing them to revert back to their childhood selves. As the world grapples with this strange occurrence, a group of scientists and researchers embark on a journey to uncover the truth behind this bizarre transformation. Along the way, they encounter unexpected challenges and revelations that force them to question the nature of aging, memory, and the essence of humanity itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Speculative Fiction

Themes: Aging, Memory, Identity, Human Nature
