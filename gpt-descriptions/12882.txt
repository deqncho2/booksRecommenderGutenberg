Synopsis: In 'Minstrelsy of the Scottish Border, Volume 2', Walter Scott takes us on a captivating journey through the enchanting Southern Counties of Scotland. This collection of historical and romantic ballads, infused with local tradition, offers a glimpse into the rich tapestry of Scottish folklore. From tales of heroic battles to stories of forbidden love, Scott weaves together a tapestry of emotions and adventures that will transport readers to a bygone era. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Poetry

Themes: Scottish folklore, Love and passion, Heroism, Tradition and heritage
