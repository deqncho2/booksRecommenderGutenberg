Synopsis: 
"The Grip cartoons: vols. I & II, May 1873 to May 1874" by Bengough, J. W. (John Wilson) is a captivating collection of political cartoons that takes readers on a satirical journey through the tumultuous years of 1873 to 1874. With wit and humor, Bengough's illustrations provide a sharp commentary on the social and political issues of the time, offering a unique perspective on the events that shaped the era. From the scandals of the day to the struggles of the working class, this collection offers a thought-provoking and entertaining glimpse into the past.

Genres: Political satire, Historical commentary, Graphic novel

Themes: Politics, Social issues, Satire
