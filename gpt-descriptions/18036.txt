Synopsis:
'Uppingham by the Sea: A Narrative of the Year at Borth' follows the adventures of a group of young boys who are sent to a seaside town for a year of education and exploration. As they navigate the challenges and joys of their new environment, they encounter eccentric characters, uncover hidden secrets, and embark on thrilling escapades. From mysterious shipwrecks to daring rescues, this captivating narrative takes readers on a journey filled with excitement, friendship, and unexpected discoveries.

Genres:
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Friendship, Exploration, Resilience
