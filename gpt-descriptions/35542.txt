Synopsis: In 'House Rats and Mice' by David E. Lantz, a seemingly ordinary suburban neighborhood becomes the backdrop for a thrilling tale of mystery and suspense. When a family moves into their new home, they quickly discover that they are not alone. Strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena lead them on a journey to uncover the truth behind their house's dark secrets. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger and deception, where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, the family must confront their deepest fears and fight to protect their loved ones from the sinister forces lurking within their own walls.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Supernatural elements, Family bonds and protection
