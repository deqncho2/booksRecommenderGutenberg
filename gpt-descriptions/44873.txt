Synopsis: In 'Third Warning: A Mystery Story for Girls', a group of adventurous young girls stumble upon a mysterious warning that leads them on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth. As they delve deeper into the secrets surrounding the warning, they find themselves facing unexpected dangers and uncovering hidden connections that could change their lives forever. With each clue they unravel, the girls become more determined to solve the mystery, but they soon realize that they may be in over their heads. Will they be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
