Synopsis: 

In "The Outline of History: Being a Plain History of Life and Mankind," H.G. Wells takes readers on an extraordinary journey through time, exploring the origins of life, the rise and fall of civilizations, and the remarkable achievements of humanity. From the earliest days of human existence to the modern era, Wells presents a comprehensive and engaging account of the world's history, filled with fascinating anecdotes, thought-provoking insights, and captivating storytelling.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Science fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Evolution, Progress, Human nature, Societal development
