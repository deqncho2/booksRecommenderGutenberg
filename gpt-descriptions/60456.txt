Synopsis: 

In 'From Office Boy to Reporter; Or, The First Step in Journalism' by Howard Roger Garis, we follow the journey of a young office boy named Jack, who dreams of becoming a reporter. Jack's determination and resourcefulness lead him to unexpected opportunities, as he navigates the bustling world of journalism, uncovering secrets and chasing stories that could change his life forever. From the bustling newsroom to the gritty streets of the city, Jack's transformation from an ordinary office boy to a tenacious reporter is filled with thrilling adventures and unexpected twists.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Pursuit of Dreams
