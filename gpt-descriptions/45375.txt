Synopsis: 
'Plays by August Strindberg, First Series' is a collection of five captivating plays that delve into the complexities of human relationships and the struggles of the human psyche. From the intense and passionate conflicts between lovers to the power dynamics within families, Strindberg's plays explore the depths of human emotions and the consequences of societal norms. Each play presents a unique and thought-provoking narrative, filled with tension, suspense, and unexpected twists. As the characters navigate through love, desire, jealousy, and betrayal, the audience is taken on a rollercoaster ride of emotions, questioning the very nature of human existence.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Naturalism

Themes: Love and desire, Power dynamics, Social norms and expectations
