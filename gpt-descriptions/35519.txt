Synopsis: In "Joscelyn Cheshire: A Story of Revolutionary Days in the Carolinas," Sara Beaumont Kennedy takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. Set in the Carolinas, this historical novel follows the life of Joscelyn Cheshire, a young woman whose world is turned upside down by the war. As she navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and sacrifice, Joscelyn finds herself caught between her family's loyalties and her own desires. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Kennedy weaves a tale of courage, resilience, and the indomitable spirit of those who fought for freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Patriotism, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
