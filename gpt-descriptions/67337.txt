Synopsis: In 'The Two Dianas, Volume 1', Meurice takes readers on a thrilling journey through 16th-century France, where two women named Diana find their lives intertwined in unexpected ways. As secrets, betrayals, and political intrigue unfold, the reader is drawn into a captivating tale of love, power, and the lengths one will go to protect what they hold dear. With vivid descriptions and a cast of intriguing characters, this historical novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the mysteries that lie within.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Identity and Deception, Power and Politics
