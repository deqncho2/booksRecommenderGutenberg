Synopsis: 
In the heart of the Australian outback, a notorious outlaw known as Stingaree emerges, captivating the imagination of the public with his daring escapades and charming demeanor. With his cunning disguises and audacious heists, Stingaree becomes a legend, leaving the authorities baffled and the people enthralled. But when he crosses paths with a beautiful opera singer named Hermione, his life takes an unexpected turn. As their lives become intertwined, Stingaree finds himself torn between his criminal pursuits and a love that could change everything.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Love and Sacrifice, Society's fascination with outlaws
