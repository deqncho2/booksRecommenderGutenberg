Synopsis: 
In "Say "Hello" for Me" by Frank W. Coggins, a chance encounter between two strangers on a train sets off a chain of events that will forever change their lives. As they navigate the complexities of love, loss, and the power of human connection, they are forced to confront their pasts and make difficult choices about their futures. With its captivating characters and unexpected twists, this heartfelt novel explores the profound impact that one person can have on another, and the enduring power of hope and forgiveness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love, Loss, Redemption, Second Chances
