Synopsis: In 'Happy House' by Jane Abbott, a seemingly perfect suburban neighborhood hides dark secrets behind closed doors. When a new family moves in, they quickly discover that the idyllic facade of Happy House is shattered by jealousy, betrayal, and a web of lies. As tensions rise and secrets unravel, the newcomers find themselves entangled in a dangerous game where trust is a luxury they can't afford. With each twist and turn, the truth becomes more elusive, leaving them questioning who they can truly trust in this gripping tale of suburban suspense.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Secrets, Trust
