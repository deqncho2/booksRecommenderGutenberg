Synopsis: In 'Curious Epitaphs', a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon a mysterious graveyard filled with peculiar tombstones that hold cryptic messages. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic history of these graves, he uncovers a web of secrets and hidden connections that span across centuries. With each epitaph he unravels, the archaeologist becomes entangled in a thrilling journey of discovery, danger, and unexpected revelations.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of words and symbols, the interconnectedness of past and present
