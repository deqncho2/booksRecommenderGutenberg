Synopsis: In 'Defenseless America' by Maxim Hudson, a small town is suddenly thrust into chaos when a mysterious threat looms over the nation. As the residents struggle to protect their loved ones and maintain their way of life, they must confront their deepest fears and make unimaginable sacrifices. With suspenseful twists and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Action

Themes: Survival, Sacrifice, Fear
