Synopsis: In "The White Queen of Okoyong: A True Story of Adventure, Heroism and Faith," author W. P. Livingstone takes us on a captivating journey to the heart of Africa. Set in the late 19th century, this remarkable true story follows the life of Mary Mitchell Slessor, a courageous Scottish missionary who defied societal norms and ventured into the dangerous and mysterious Okoyong region. Facing unimaginable challenges and encountering a world vastly different from her own, Mary's unwavering determination and unwavering faith lead her to become a beacon of hope and a symbol of resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Faith, Cultural Exploration, Gender Roles
