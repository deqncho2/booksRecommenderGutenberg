Synopsis: In 'Tioba, and Other Tales' by Arthur Colton, readers are transported to a world where ordinary lives intersect with extraordinary events. From the mysterious disappearance of a young girl to a haunting encounter with a ghostly figure, these captivating tales explore the depths of human emotions and the power of the unknown. As the characters navigate through love, loss, and the supernatural, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and question the boundaries of reality. With each story offering a unique twist and unexpected revelations, 'Tioba, and Other Tales' will leave readers spellbound until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Fate, Perception, Loss, Redemption
