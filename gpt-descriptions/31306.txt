Synopsis: In 'Bride of the Dark One' by Florence Verbell Brown, a young woman named Elara finds herself caught in a forbidden love affair with a mysterious and dangerous being known as the Dark One. As their relationship deepens, Elara must navigate a treacherous world of secrets, magic, and ancient prophecies that threaten to tear them apart. With her life hanging in the balance, Elara must decide how far she is willing to go for love and whether she can trust the Dark One with her heart.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Paranormal

Themes: Forbidden love, Sacrifice, Destiny
