Synopsis: In "Yankee Boys in Japan; Or, The Young Merchants of Yokohama," follow the thrilling adventures of a group of young American merchants who find themselves in the bustling city of Yokohama during the late 19th century. As they navigate the unfamiliar culture and customs of Japan, they become entangled in a web of intrigue and danger that tests their courage and resourcefulness. From encounters with samurais to uncovering hidden secrets, this captivating tale will transport readers to a world of mystery and excitement.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Cultural exploration, Friendship, Bravery
