Synopsis: 

Join the thrilling adventure of the Dominion Government Expedition as they embark on a daring journey to Hudson Bay and the Arctic Islands aboard the D.G.S. Neptune in 1903-1904. Led by the intrepid explorer Albert Peter Low, this gripping account takes readers through treacherous icy waters, encounters with indigenous communities, and the exploration of uncharted territories. As the crew faces the harsh realities of the Arctic, they must navigate through dangerous conditions, unforeseen challenges, and the constant battle against nature's unforgiving forces. Will they succeed in their mission and uncover the secrets hidden within the Arctic wilderness?

Genres: Adventure, Exploration, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration and Discovery, Survival, Indigenous Cultures
