Synopsis: In 'Under the Country Sky' by Grace S. Richmond, a young woman named Ruth finds herself torn between the expectations of her family and her own desires for independence. When she meets a charming stranger who introduces her to a world beyond her small town, Ruth must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and self-discovery. As she grapples with the choices before her, Ruth learns that sometimes the path to happiness lies in unexpected places.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Independence, Love, Family dynamics, Self-discovery
