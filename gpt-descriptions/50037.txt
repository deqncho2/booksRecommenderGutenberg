Synopsis:
In 'Wyllard's Weird: A Novel' by M. E. Braddon, a young woman named Ellinor is drawn into a mysterious and haunting family secret when she becomes the governess to the Wyllard family. As she delves deeper into the family's history, she uncovers dark secrets, hidden identities, and a web of deceit that threatens to consume her. With its eerie atmosphere and gripping plot, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind Wyllard's weird.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Deception, Psychological manipulation
