Synopsis: 
In 'Call Mr. Fortune' by H. C. Bailey, the enigmatic and unconventional detective, Reggie Fortune, finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is called upon to solve a perplexing case involving a missing person. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Reggie must navigate through a labyrinth of secrets, deceit, and unexpected twists, all while employing his sharp wit and unorthodox methods to uncover the truth. With each clue leading him closer to the heart of the mystery, Reggie must race against time to unravel the complex puzzle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Intrigue, Deception, Unconventional Investigation
