Synopsis: In the heart of New York City, a Russian scientist seeks revenge against those who wronged him, using a deadly weapon that could bring chaos and destruction. As the city trembles under the threat of the unknown, a brave detective and a beautiful young woman find themselves entangled in a web of danger, espionage, and romance. With time running out, they must unravel the mystery behind the drums of jeopardy before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Revenge, Espionage, Love and Sacrifice
