Synopsis: 
In 'Maw's Vacation: The Story of a Human Being in the Yellowstone', follow the captivating journey of Maw, a curious and adventurous individual, as they embark on a vacation in the mesmerizing Yellowstone National Park. As Maw immerses themselves in the breathtaking landscapes and encounters the park's diverse wildlife, they find themselves on a transformative and introspective adventure. With each encounter and experience, Maw's perception of the world and their place in it is forever altered. 

Genres: Adventure, Nature Writing, Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Connection with Nature
