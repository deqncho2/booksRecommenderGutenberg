Synopsis: 
'Folk-Speech of Cumberland and Some Districts Adjacent: Being Short Stories and Rhymes in the Dialects of the West Border Counties' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and vibrant dialects of the West Border Counties. Through a collection of short stories and rhymes, author Alexander Craig Gibson immerses readers in the unique language and culture of the region, offering a delightful glimpse into the lives and experiences of its inhabitants. From humorous anecdotes to heartfelt tales, this book showcases the beauty and charm of the local folk-speech, providing a fascinating exploration of the West Border Counties' linguistic heritage.

Genres: 
Folklore, Linguistics, Short Stories

Themes: 
Regional identity, Cultural preservation, Oral traditions
