Synopsis: 
"The Unbearable Bassington" is a captivating satirical novella that follows the misadventures of Comus Bassington, a charming and mischievous young boy with a knack for causing trouble. From his unconventional upbringing to his rebellious exploits at school, Comus's antics and wit will keep readers hooked as they witness his transformation from a precocious child to a cunning young man. With a cast of eccentric characters and Saki's signature biting humor, this story explores the complexities of family dynamics, societal expectations, and the consequences of unchecked indulgence.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family relationships, Rebellion, Social expectations
