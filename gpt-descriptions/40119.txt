Synopsis: In 'Curiosities of Light and Sight' by Bidwell Shelford, a renowned scientist embarks on a captivating journey to unravel the mysteries of light and sight. As he delves into the depths of scientific exploration, he encounters unexpected phenomena and uncovers astonishing secrets that challenge his understanding of the world. With each discovery, the boundaries between science and the supernatural blur, leading him to question the very nature of reality. This enthralling tale takes readers on a thought-provoking adventure that will leave them questioning the limits of human perception.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Perception, Reality, Scientific Exploration
