Synopsis: 
In 'Lady Jim of Curzon Street: A Novel' by Fergus Hume, readers are introduced to the enigmatic Lady Jim, a captivating and mysterious woman who resides on Curzon Street. As the story unfolds, Lady Jim finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances. With her wit, charm, and cunning, she navigates the treacherous waters of high society, where appearances can be deceiving and loyalty is a rare commodity. As the plot thickens, readers are taken on a thrilling journey filled with intrigue, romance, and unexpected twists that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Power, Society, Identity
