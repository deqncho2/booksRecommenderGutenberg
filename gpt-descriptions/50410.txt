Synopsis: In 'With the Rank and File' by Thomas J. Ford, a young journalist finds himself embedded with a group of passionate activists fighting for social justice. As he delves deeper into their world, he becomes torn between his duty as a reporter and his growing empathy for their cause. With secrets to uncover and personal convictions to confront, he must navigate a complex web of loyalty, truth, and the power of collective action.

Genres: Political thriller, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Social justice, Activism, Ethics, Identity
