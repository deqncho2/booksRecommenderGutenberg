Synopsis: 
In 'Writing for Vaudeville' by Page, Brett, a struggling playwright named Jack finds himself caught in the whirlwind world of vaudeville, a popular form of entertainment in the early 20th century. As he navigates the challenges of creating captivating acts for a demanding audience, Jack encounters a colorful cast of characters, including eccentric performers, conniving producers, and a mysterious love interest. With his dreams on the line, Jack must learn to balance his artistic integrity with the commercial demands of the vaudeville stage, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Pursuit of artistic passion, The clash between art and commerce, Love and ambition
