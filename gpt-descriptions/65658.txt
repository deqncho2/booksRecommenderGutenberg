Synopsis: 

In 'The Art of Music. Vol. 01 (of 14), A Narrative History of Music. Book 1, The Pre-classic Periods: A Comprehensive Library of Information for Music Lovers and Musicians', embark on a captivating journey through the origins and evolution of music. This first volume delves into the enchanting pre-classic periods, unearthing the rich tapestry of musical traditions that laid the foundation for the music we know today. From ancient civilizations to the Renaissance, this book uncovers the fascinating stories behind the melodies, instruments, and composers that shaped the course of music history.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music History, Reference

Themes: Evolution of Music, Cultural Significance, Musical Traditions
