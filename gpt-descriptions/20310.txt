Synopsis: 

In 'Hammersmith, Fulham and Putney', Geikie and Mitton take readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and hidden gems of these three vibrant districts in London. From the bustling streets of Hammersmith to the tranquil beauty of Putney, the authors delve into the fascinating stories behind the iconic landmarks, famous residents, and lesser-known corners of these neighborhoods. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, they bring to life the unique character and charm of each area, revealing a tapestry of historical events, cultural influences, and architectural wonders that have shaped these districts over the centuries. 

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Local history, Urban exploration, Architectural heritage
