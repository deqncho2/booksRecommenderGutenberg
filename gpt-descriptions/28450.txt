Synopsis: In 'Stopover' by William Gerken, a chance encounter at an airport leads to a mysterious and thrilling journey that blurs the lines between reality and imagination. As the protagonist delves deeper into the enigmatic world of his newfound companion, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden agendas. With each stopover, the stakes rise, and the truth becomes more elusive. Will he uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Deception
