Synopsis: In 'Hal Kenyon Disappears', a renowned adventurer mysteriously vanishes during an expedition in the Amazon rainforest. As his friends and family search for answers, they uncover a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. With time running out, they must navigate treacherous terrain and face their own demons to unravel the truth behind Hal's disappearance.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Loyalty, Betrayal, Survival
