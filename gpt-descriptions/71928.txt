Synopsis: 

In 'The gunsmith's manual: A complete handbook for the American gunsmith', authors Harrison and Stelle present a comprehensive guide that delves into the intricate world of gunsmithing. This practical handbook covers all aspects of the trade, providing invaluable knowledge and techniques for both novice and experienced gunsmiths. From the basics of firearm design and function to advanced customization and repair, this manual offers step-by-step instructions, accompanied by detailed illustrations, to help readers master the art of gunsmithing. Whether you are interested in historical firearms or modern weaponry, this book is a must-have resource for anyone passionate about the craft.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Crafts and Hobbies

Themes: Gunsmithing, Firearms, Craftsmanship, Technical Skills
