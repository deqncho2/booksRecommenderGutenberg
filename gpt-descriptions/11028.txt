Synopsis: 

'Philippine Folk-Tales' is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that transport readers to the rich and vibrant world of Philippine folklore. From mythical creatures and magical encounters to tales of love, bravery, and trickery, this anthology offers a delightful exploration of the country's cultural heritage. Each story is filled with vivid imagery and captivating characters, taking readers on a journey through the mystical landscapes of the Philippines. 

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Cultural heritage, Mythology, Magic, Love, Bravery, Trickery
