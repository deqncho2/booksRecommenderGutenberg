Synopsis: In the small town of Blackwood, a mysterious flower blooms every 100 years, bringing both beauty and terror. When a young botanist named Emily discovers the existence of this rare Hellflower, she becomes entangled in a web of dark secrets and supernatural forces. As the flower's power grows stronger, Emily must uncover the truth behind its origins and find a way to stop its malevolent influence before it consumes the entire town.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: The duality of beauty and danger, the consequences of meddling with the unknown, the power of nature and its connection to the supernatural.
