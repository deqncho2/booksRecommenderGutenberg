Synopsis: In 'The Saddle Boys of the Rockies; Or, Lost on Thunder Mountain', three adventurous young men embark on a thrilling journey through the rugged landscapes of the Rockies. As they navigate treacherous trails and encounter unexpected dangers, they find themselves facing a mysterious and perilous situation on Thunder Mountain. With their courage and resourcefulness put to the test, the Saddle Boys must rely on their wits and teamwork to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival
