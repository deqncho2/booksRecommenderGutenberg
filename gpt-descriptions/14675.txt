Synopsis: In 'Secret Enemies of True Republicanism', author Smolnikar delves into the inner life of man and the spirit world, uncovering the most important developments that could potentially abolish revolutions and wars, and establish permanent peace on earth. With a plan for redeeming nations from oppressive speculations and introducing a new era of harmony, truth, and righteousness, this book offers a captivating exploration of the promised new era that could transform the entire globe.

Genres: Philosophy, Political Science, Spirituality

Themes: Peace, Revolution, Redemption, Harmony, Truth, Righteousness
