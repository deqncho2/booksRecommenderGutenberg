Synopsis: In "Watch and Ward" by Henry James, a young man named Roger Lawrence finds himself unexpectedly responsible for the care and upbringing of a young girl named Nora. As he navigates the challenges of raising her, he becomes increasingly drawn to her, leading to a complex and forbidden relationship. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century New England, this gripping tale explores the boundaries of love, duty, and societal expectations.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Forbidden love, Duty and responsibility, Social expectations
