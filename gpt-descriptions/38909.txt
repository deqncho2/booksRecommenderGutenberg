Synopsis: 
In "Andromache: A Play in Three Acts," Murray Gilbert takes us on a captivating journey through the aftermath of the Trojan War. Set in ancient Greece, the play follows the tragic fate of Andromache, the widow of the fallen Trojan hero Hector. As she navigates the treacherous world of power struggles, political intrigue, and forbidden love, Andromache must confront her own inner demons and make heart-wrenching choices that will shape her destiny. With its gripping plot twists and complex characters, this play delves into the depths of human emotions and the consequences of war, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the final act.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Love and loss, Power and politics, Morality and ethics
