Synopsis: In 'Bobbie, General Manager: A Novel' by Olive Higgins Prouty, we follow the journey of Bobbie, a young woman who defies societal expectations and becomes the first female general manager of a prestigious hotel. As she navigates the challenges of a male-dominated industry, Bobbie must confront her own insecurities and prove her worth in a world that constantly underestimates her. With determination and resilience, she strives to break barriers and shatter glass ceilings, all while discovering the true meaning of success and finding love in unexpected places.

Genres: Fiction, Women's Literature, Historical Fiction

Themes: Gender equality, ambition, self-discovery, societal expectations
