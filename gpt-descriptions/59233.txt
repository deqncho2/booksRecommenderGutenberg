Synopsis: In "Scarabs: An Introduction to the Study of Egyptian Seals and Signet Rings," Percy E. Newberry takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of ancient Egypt's seals and signet rings. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Newberry unveils the secrets behind these intricate artifacts, shedding light on their historical significance and the stories they tell. From the symbolism of scarabs to the intricate craftsmanship of signet rings, this book offers a captivating exploration of ancient Egyptian culture and its enduring legacy.

Genres: Non-fiction, Archaeology, History

Themes: Ancient Egypt, Symbolism, Art and Craftsmanship
