Synopsis: In 'The Moneychangers' by Sinclair, Upton, the lives of several individuals intertwine within the high-stakes world of finance. As the story unfolds, we witness the power struggles, corruption, and personal sacrifices that come with the pursuit of wealth and success. From the boardrooms of Wall Street to the back alleys of Chicago, this gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of the banking industry and the lengths people will go to protect their fortunes.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Greed, Morality, Power, Corruption
