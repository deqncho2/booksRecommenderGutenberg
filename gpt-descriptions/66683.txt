Synopsis: In "The Story of the Battle Hymn of the Republic," Florence Howe Hall takes readers on a captivating journey through the creation and impact of one of America's most iconic patriotic songs. From the song's humble beginnings during the Civil War to its enduring significance in shaping the nation's history, Hall delves into the fascinating story behind the Battle Hymn of the Republic. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, she explores the song's origins, its powerful message, and the individuals who played a pivotal role in its creation. This captivating narrative sheds light on the song's enduring legacy and its profound impact on American culture.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Music

Themes: Patriotism, Civil War, American identity, Social justice
