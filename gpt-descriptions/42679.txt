Synopsis:
In 'Fires - Book 3: The Hare, and Other Tales' by Gibson, Wilfrid Wilson, readers are transported to a world where enchanting tales unfold, each more captivating than the last. From the mysterious adventures of a cunning hare to the haunting encounters with supernatural beings, this collection of stories will leave readers spellbound. As the characters navigate through the realms of fantasy and reality, they grapple with themes of fate, transformation, and the power of storytelling itself. Prepare to be enthralled by the magical tapestry woven by Gibson's vivid imagination.

Genres: Fantasy, Magical Realism, Short Stories

Themes: Fate, Transformation, Power of Storytelling
