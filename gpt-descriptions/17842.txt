Synopsis: 
In the small coastal village of Troy, a mysterious rock stands as a haunting reminder of a dark secret buried in the past. When a young man named John Treharne arrives in Troy, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger surrounding the enigmatic Dead Man's Rock. As he delves deeper into the village's history, John uncovers a series of chilling events that threaten to unravel the fabric of his own existence. With each revelation, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leading John on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind Dead Man's Rock.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of the past, the thin line between reality and the supernatural
