Synopsis: In 'Wanderers of the Wolf-Moon' by Bond, Nelson S., a group of unlikely companions embarks on a perilous journey through a mystical realm filled with ancient secrets and dangerous creatures. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter enigmatic beings, they must confront their own inner demons and forge unbreakable bonds of friendship. With each step, they uncover the truth about their own identities and the destiny that awaits them under the watchful gaze of the Wolf-Moon.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Destiny
