Synopsis: In 'Lysis' by Plato, a young boy named Lysis becomes the center of attention as Socrates and his friends engage in a thought-provoking dialogue about the nature of friendship. As they explore the complexities of this fundamental human relationship, they delve into questions of love, knowledge, and the pursuit of wisdom. With Socrates as their guide, the characters embark on a philosophical journey that challenges their preconceived notions and invites readers to reflect on the true essence of friendship.

Genres: Philosophy, Dialogue, Classical Literature

Themes: Friendship, Love, Knowledge, Wisdom
