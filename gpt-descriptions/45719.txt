Synopsis: In the quaint English village of Arundel, a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a sense of intrigue and danger. As the villagers become entangled in his web of secrets, their lives are forever changed. With its captivating blend of suspense, romance, and unexpected twists, 'Arundel' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the hidden depths of human nature.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town dynamics, Unpredictability of human behavior
