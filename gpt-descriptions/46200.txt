Synopsis: In 'Plowing On Sunday' by Sterling North, a young boy named Sterling embarks on a thrilling adventure when he discovers a hidden treasure map in his grandfather's attic. With the help of his friends, Sterling sets off on a quest to find the buried treasure, encountering unexpected challenges and uncovering long-held family secrets along the way. As they navigate through dangerous terrain and face their fears, the group learns valuable lessons about friendship, perseverance, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Self-discovery
