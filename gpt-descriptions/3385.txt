Synopsis: In "Last Days in a Dutch Hotel," William Dean Howells takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of a diverse group of guests staying at a hotel in the Netherlands. From an American couple seeking adventure to a mysterious Englishman with a hidden past, the hotel becomes a microcosm of human emotions, desires, and secrets. As the guests navigate their personal struggles and relationships, the hotel's atmosphere becomes increasingly charged with tension and intrigue. Howells masterfully weaves together their stories, creating a rich tapestry of human experiences that will keep readers engrossed until the very last page.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Human nature, Cultural exploration, Secrets and hidden identities
