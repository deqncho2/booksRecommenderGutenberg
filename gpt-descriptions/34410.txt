Synopsis: 
In 'The Treasure of the Isle of Mist' by Tarn, W. W., a young archaeologist named Amelia embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets of a mysterious island. As she delves deeper into the island's history, Amelia encounters treacherous obstacles, ancient riddles, and unexpected allies. With danger lurking at every turn, she must race against time to unlock the island's hidden treasure before it falls into the wrong hands. This captivating tale of exploration and discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Treasure Hunting, Ancient History
