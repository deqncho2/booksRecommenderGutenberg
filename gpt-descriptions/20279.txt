Synopsis: In 'The Veterinarian' by Charles James Korinek, a small-town veterinarian finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when a series of strange animal deaths occur in his community. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers dark secrets that threaten not only the animals he cares for but also his own life. With the help of an enigmatic woman and his own determination, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, the bond between humans and animals, the pursuit of justice
