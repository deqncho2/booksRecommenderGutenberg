Synopsis: In "Tried for Her Life: A Sequel to 'Cruel As the Grave'", the captivating story continues as the courageous and resilient heroine faces a new set of challenges that threaten to unravel her life once again. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping sequel delves deeper into the secrets and mysteries surrounding the protagonist, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Resilience, Betrayal, Redemption
