Synopsis: In 'The Jessamy Bride' by Frank Frankfort Moore, a young woman named Jessamy finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she agrees to marry a mysterious stranger. As she delves deeper into her new husband's past, she uncovers a dark and dangerous world that threatens to consume her. With her life hanging in the balance, Jessamy must navigate treacherous waters and make difficult choices to protect herself and those she loves.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and sacrifice, Identity and self-discovery
