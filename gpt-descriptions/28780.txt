Synopsis: In 'Peter the Brazen: A Mystery Story of Modern China', George F. Worts takes readers on a thrilling journey through the bustling streets of Shanghai. When a prominent American businessman is found dead under mysterious circumstances, Peter Moore, an intrepid journalist, becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. As he delves deeper into the case, Moore uncovers a complex network of corruption, espionage, and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. With his life on the line, Moore must navigate the treacherous underworld of Shanghai and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Corruption, Espionage, Betrayal, Cultural clash
