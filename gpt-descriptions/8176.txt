Synopsis: 
In 'Death at the Excelsior, and Other Stories', P. G. Wodehouse presents a collection of captivating tales that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. From mysterious deaths at a luxurious hotel to comical misunderstandings and romantic entanglements, this anthology showcases Wodehouse's signature wit and charm. Follow a cast of eccentric characters as they navigate the intricacies of love, friendship, and the occasional murder. With each story offering a delightful blend of humor, suspense, and unexpected twists, 'Death at the Excelsior, and Other Stories' is a must-read for fans of classic mystery and comedy.

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Short Stories

Themes: Love and romance, Friendship, Intrigue, Humor
