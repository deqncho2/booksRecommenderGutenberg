Synopsis: In "The Loss of the Kent, East Indiaman, in the Bay of Biscay: Narrated in a Letter to a Friend," Duncan McGregor takes readers on a gripping journey through the treacherous waters of the Bay of Biscay. Through a heartfelt letter, McGregor recounts the harrowing events that unfolded aboard the ill-fated ship, the Kent. As the story unfolds, readers are drawn into a tale of danger, survival, and the indomitable human spirit. Will the crew and passengers of the Kent be able to overcome the perils that await them in the unforgiving bay?

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Maritime

Themes: Perseverance, Survival, Human resilience, Man vs. Nature
