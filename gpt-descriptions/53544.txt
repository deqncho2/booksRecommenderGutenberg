Synopsis: 

'George at the Wheel; Or, Life in the Pilot-House' by Castlemon, Harry is an exciting adventure novel that follows the thrilling journey of young George as he embarks on a life-changing voyage aboard a steamboat. As George navigates the treacherous waters of the Mississippi River, he encounters a series of unexpected challenges and dangers that test his courage and determination. From navigating treacherous rapids to facing off against ruthless river pirates, George's journey is filled with heart-pounding moments and unexpected twists. Will he be able to overcome these obstacles and prove himself worthy of the pilot-house?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Determination, Coming of Age
