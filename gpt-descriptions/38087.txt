Synopsis: 
In 'The Boy Ranchers of Puget Sound' by Harold Bindloss, three adventurous brothers find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon a hidden treasure map while exploring their family's ranch. As they set out on a daring quest to uncover the secrets of the map, they encounter dangerous obstacles, encounter unexpected allies, and face the perils of the rugged Puget Sound landscape. With their courage and resourcefulness tested to the limit, the brothers must navigate through treacherous waters and outsmart cunning adversaries to solve the mystery and claim the hidden treasure.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Brotherhood, Exploration, Treasure Hunting
