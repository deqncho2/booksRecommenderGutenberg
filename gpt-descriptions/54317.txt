Synopsis: 

'Isle of Wight: A Sketch-Book' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the picturesque landscapes and charming towns of the Isle of Wight. Through the eyes of the talented artist Dorothy Woollard, this sketchbook offers a unique perspective on the island's beauty, capturing its idyllic beaches, rolling hills, and historic landmarks. From bustling seaside resorts to tranquil countryside retreats, each sketch is a delightful invitation to explore the island's hidden gems and discover its rich history and culture. 

Genres: Travel, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: Beauty of nature, Cultural exploration, Artistic expression
