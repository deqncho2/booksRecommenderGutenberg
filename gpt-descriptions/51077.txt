Synopsis: In 'The Amateur Diplomat: A Novel', follow the captivating journey of a seemingly ordinary man who unexpectedly finds himself thrust into the world of international diplomacy. As he navigates the treacherous waters of political intrigue and power struggles, our protagonist must rely on his wit, resourcefulness, and a touch of luck to survive. With twists and turns at every corner, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering how an amateur can possibly succeed in such a high-stakes game.

Genres: Political thriller, suspense, adventure

Themes: Diplomacy, power dynamics, personal growth
