Synopsis: 
In "Confessions of an Opera Singer" by Kathleen Howard, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the glamorous and tumultuous world of opera. The story follows the life of a talented and ambitious opera singer, as she navigates the challenges of love, fame, and personal sacrifices. From the grand stages of renowned opera houses to the intimate moments behind the scenes, the protagonist's passion for her art and the sacrifices she makes to pursue her dreams will keep readers enthralled until the final note.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Music

Themes: Pursuit of passion, Sacrifice, Love and relationships
