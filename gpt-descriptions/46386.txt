Synopsis: In 'The Castaways of Pete's Patch: (A Sequel to The Adopting of Rosa Marie)' by Rankin, Carroll Watson, the story continues with the lovable characters from 'The Adopting of Rosa Marie' as they find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure. When a storm hits Pete's Patch, a small coastal town, a group of friends become stranded on a mysterious island. As they navigate the challenges of survival, they uncover hidden secrets and encounter unexpected allies. With danger lurking around every corner, the castaways must rely on their resourcefulness and friendship to find a way back home.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Discovery
