Synopsis: 
In 'The Motor-Bus in War: Being the Impressions of an A.S.C. Officer during Two and a Half Years at the Front', A.M. Beatson takes us on a captivating journey through the chaos and camaraderie of war. Through his eyes, we witness the trials and triumphs of an A.S.C. (Army Service Corps) officer, as he navigates the treacherous battlefields of World War I. From the relentless dangers of the front lines to the unexpected moments of humanity amidst the chaos, Beatson's vivid and poignant account offers a unique perspective on the war that forever changed the world.

Genres: Memoir, War, History

Themes: Courage, Camaraderie, Human resilience, The impact of war
