Synopsis: In "Kilgorman: A Story of Ireland in 1798" by Reed, Talbot Baines, we are transported to the tumultuous era of late 18th-century Ireland. The story follows the lives of two young men, one a Protestant and the other a Catholic, as they navigate the treacherous political landscape and their own personal struggles. As rebellion brews and loyalties are tested, their paths intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and the fight for freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Romance

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Revolution, Love and Sacrifice
