Synopsis: In 'The Boy Patriot' by Edward Sylvester Ellis, follow the thrilling journey of a young boy named Jack as he becomes entangled in a dangerous mission during the American Revolution. With his quick wit and bravery, Jack finds himself in the midst of espionage, secret codes, and perilous encounters. As he navigates through treacherous situations, Jack's loyalty to his country and his determination to make a difference are put to the ultimate test. Will he succeed in his mission and become a true patriot?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Loyalty
