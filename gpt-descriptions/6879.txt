Synopsis:
In "The Gold Bat," a prestigious English boarding school is thrown into chaos when a valuable gold cricket bat goes missing. As the students and faculty scramble to solve the mystery, friendships are tested, rivalries intensify, and unexpected alliances are formed. With witty dialogue, hilarious mishaps, and a cast of eccentric characters, this uproarious novel takes readers on a delightful journey through the world of schoolboy cricket and the lengths people will go to win.

Genres:
Comedy, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Friendship, Competition, Loyalty
