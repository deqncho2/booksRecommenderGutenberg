Synopsis: In "Things as they are: Mission work in Southern India," Amy Carmichael takes readers on a captivating journey through her experiences as a missionary in India. Through vivid storytelling and heartfelt reflections, she unveils the challenges, triumphs, and complexities of her mission work, offering a unique glimpse into the lives of the people she served. From encounters with local customs and traditions to the deep spiritual struggles faced by both missionaries and converts, Carmichael's narrative paints a rich and compelling portrait of the realities of mission work in Southern India.

Genres: Memoir, Non-fiction, Christian Literature

Themes: Missionary work, Cultural immersion, Spiritual struggles, Cross-cultural communication
