Synopsis: In the second volume of the captivating novel series, 'Dulcie Carlyon,' the eponymous protagonist finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception. As Dulcie navigates the treacherous waters of high society, she uncovers shocking revelations about her own past and the true nature of those around her. With her resilience and determination tested to the limit, Dulcie must make difficult choices that will shape her future and the lives of those she holds dear. As the plot thickens and the stakes rise, readers will be irresistibly drawn into Dulcie's world of intrigue, romance, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Love and Sacrifice
