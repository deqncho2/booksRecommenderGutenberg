Synopsis:
'With the Naval Brigade in Natal, 1899-1900: Journal of Active Service' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed landscapes of Natal during the late 19th century. Written by Charles Richard Newdigate Burne, this gripping journal recounts his experiences as part of the Naval Brigade during a tumultuous period of conflict. From daring battles to unexpected alliances, Burne's vivid descriptions and personal insights provide a captivating account of the challenges and triumphs faced by the brigade. As the story unfolds, readers will find themselves immersed in the heart-pounding action and the camaraderie that develops amidst the chaos of war.

Genres:
Historical Non-fiction, Military Memoir, Adventure

Themes:
War and Conflict, Camaraderie, Survival
