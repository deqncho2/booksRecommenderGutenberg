Synopsis: 
In 'Rhymes of a child's world: a book of verse for children', Miriam Clark Potter takes young readers on a delightful journey through the magical world of childhood. Through a collection of charming and whimsical poems, Potter captures the innocence, imagination, and wonder that define a child's perspective. From playful animals to imaginary adventures, each verse is filled with vivid imagery and lyrical language that will captivate both children and adults alike. With its delightful rhymes and relatable themes, this book is sure to become a cherished favorite for young readers.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: Childhood, Imagination, Innocence
