Synopsis: In 'The History of the Hen Fever. A Humorous Record', George Pickering Burnham takes readers on a delightful journey through the fascinating world of hen enthusiasts. From the eccentric characters who obsess over their feathered friends to the unexpected challenges they face, this humorous record offers a unique perspective on the captivating phenomenon known as the "Hen Fever." With wit and charm, Burnham explores the highs and lows of this peculiar obsession, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Non-fiction

Themes: Obsession, Eccentricity, Community
