Synopsis: 
'Under Lock and Key: A Story. Volume 1 (of 3)' follows the gripping tale of a young woman named Emily, who finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she inherits a mysterious key from her late grandmother. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the key, Emily discovers a hidden world filled with dark secrets, hidden treasures, and unexpected alliances. With each step she takes, Emily uncovers more about her family's past and the true power of the key, leading her on a thrilling journey that will test her courage and determination.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Family secrets, Hidden treasures, Courage and determination
