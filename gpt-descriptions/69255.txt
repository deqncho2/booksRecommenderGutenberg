Synopsis: In "Of One Blood: or, The Hidden Self" by Pauline E. Hopkins, a young man named Reuel Briggs embarks on a journey of self-discovery that takes him from the bustling streets of Boston to the mysterious land of Ethiopia. As he delves deeper into his ancestral roots, Reuel uncovers a hidden world filled with ancient secrets, forbidden love, and a powerful destiny that could change his life forever. With each revelation, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, forcing him to confront his own identity and the true nature of humanity.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Ancestry, Forbidden love, Destiny, Cultural exploration
