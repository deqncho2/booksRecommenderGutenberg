Synopsis: 
In 'All for Love; Or, The World Well Lost: A Tragedy' by John Dryden, the reader is transported to ancient Egypt, where the passionate and forbidden love between Antony and Cleopatra unfolds. As political turmoil threatens their relationship, they must navigate the treacherous waters of power, loyalty, and sacrifice. With poetic language and intense emotions, this tragic tale explores the depths of human desire and the consequences of choosing love over duty.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and passion, Power and politics, Loyalty and betrayal
