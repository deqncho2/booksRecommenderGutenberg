Synopsis: 

'Prisons Over Seas: Deportation and Colonization; British and American Prisons of To-day' by Griffiths, Arthur takes readers on a captivating exploration of the hidden world of prisons and deportation. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the author delves into the dark underbelly of the British and American prison systems, revealing shocking truths about the practice of deportation and its connection to colonization. From the harrowing tales of prisoners to the intricate web of political and economic interests, this book uncovers the complex dynamics that shape the modern prison landscape. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Social Justice

Themes: Deportation, Colonization, Prison System, Social Injustice
