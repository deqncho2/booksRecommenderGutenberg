Synopsis: In 'A Rough Diamond: A Comic Drama in One Act' by Buckstone, John Baldwin, we are introduced to a peculiar character named Mr. Snobson, who finds himself in a series of comical situations as he tries to navigate the complexities of high society. From mistaken identities to hilarious misunderstandings, this one-act play takes the reader on a delightful journey filled with laughter and unexpected twists. 

Genres: Comedy, Drama

Themes: Social class, Identity, Humor
