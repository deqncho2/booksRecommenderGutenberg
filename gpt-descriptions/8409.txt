Synopsis: 
'Love-Letters Between a Nobleman and His Sister' by Aphra Behn is a captivating epistolary novel that delves into the forbidden and scandalous world of secret love affairs. The story unfolds through a series of passionate letters exchanged between a nobleman and his sister, revealing a web of deceit, desire, and betrayal. As the characters navigate the complexities of their relationships, they are forced to confront societal norms and the consequences of their actions. With each letter, the reader is drawn deeper into a world of intrigue and passion, eagerly anticipating the next twist in this captivating tale.

Genres: 
Epistolary fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: 
Forbidden love, Betrayal, Gender roles, Social expectations
