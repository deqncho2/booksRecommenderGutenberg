Synopsis: 
'For Love and Life; vol. 1 of 2' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret) is a captivating tale that follows the lives of two young women, Lucy and Edith, as they navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations in Victorian England. Lucy, a spirited and independent woman, finds herself torn between her duty to her family and her desire for personal fulfillment. Meanwhile, Edith, a sheltered and naive girl, embarks on a journey of self-discovery and learns the harsh realities of the world. As their paths intertwine, both women must confront the challenges and sacrifices that come with pursuing their dreams and finding true happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Women's roles in society, Personal growth and self-discovery
