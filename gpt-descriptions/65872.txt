Synopsis: 

In 'Chronicles of Pharmacy, Vol. 1 (of 2)', author A. C. Wootton takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of pharmacy. Set in a bustling city, the story follows the life of a young pharmacist named Ethan, who finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. As he delves deeper into his profession, Ethan uncovers hidden secrets, encounters unexpected allies, and faces dangerous adversaries. With each discovery, he becomes more determined to uncover the truth behind a series of puzzling events that threaten not only his own life but also the lives of those around him. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, the power of knowledge, the pursuit of truth
