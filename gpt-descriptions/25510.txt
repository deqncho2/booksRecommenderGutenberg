Synopsis:
Betty Vivian is a spirited and determined young girl who finds herself enrolled in the prestigious Haddo Court School. As she navigates the challenges of fitting in and excelling academically, Betty discovers a hidden secret that threatens to unravel the very fabric of the school. With her newfound friends, she embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth and protect the legacy of Haddo Court School.

Genres:
Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Secrets
