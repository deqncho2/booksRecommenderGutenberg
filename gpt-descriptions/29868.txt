Synopsis: In 'Love and Lucy' by Maurice Hewlett, a young woman named Lucy finds herself caught in a web of love, desire, and betrayal in the picturesque setting of medieval France. As she navigates through the complexities of courtly life, Lucy must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and challenge her notions of love and loyalty. With captivating characters and unexpected twists, this tale of passion and intrigue will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
