Synopsis: 
'Practical Mechanics for Boys' is an engaging and informative guide that takes young readers on a thrilling journey through the world of mechanics. Written by James Slough Zerbe, this book is filled with practical experiments, step-by-step instructions, and fascinating anecdotes that will captivate the minds of aspiring engineers and inventors. From constructing simple machines to understanding the principles of electricity and magnetism, this book provides a hands-on approach to learning the fundamentals of mechanics. With its emphasis on creativity, problem-solving, and the joy of discovery, 'Practical Mechanics for Boys' is sure to inspire young minds to explore the wonders of the mechanical world.

Genres: Educational, Non-fiction, How-to

Themes: Engineering, Invention, Problem-solving
