Synopsis: 

In "Menasseh ben Israel's Mission to Oliver Cromwell," we are transported back to the 17th century, where a determined Jewish scholar named Menasseh ben Israel embarks on a daring mission to secure the re-admission of Jews into England. Through a series of thought-provoking pamphlets, Menasseh engages with the powerful figure of Oliver Cromwell, advocating for the rights and acceptance of his fellow Jews. As the story unfolds, we witness the intricate negotiations, political intrigues, and intellectual debates that shape this pivotal moment in history. Will Menasseh's mission succeed, or will he face insurmountable obstacles in his quest for religious freedom?

Genres: Historical fiction, Political drama, Religious literature

Themes: Religious tolerance, Identity and belonging, Diplomacy and negotiation
