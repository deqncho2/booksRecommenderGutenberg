Synopsis: In 'Cherry & Violet: A Tale of the Great Plague' by Manning, Anne, two young girls find themselves caught in the midst of the devastating Great Plague that ravaged London in the 17th century. Cherry, a spirited and resourceful girl, and Violet, a gentle and imaginative soul, form an unlikely friendship as they navigate the chaos and fear that surrounds them. Together, they must face unimaginable challenges, make difficult choices, and find strength in each other to survive the darkest of times.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Survival, Loss, Hope
