Synopsis: 
In 'Bully and Bawly No-Tail (the Jumping Frogs)' by Howard Roger Garis, readers are introduced to two adventurous and mischievous frog brothers, Bully and Bawly. Living in a small pond, the brothers embark on a series of thrilling escapades, encountering various challenges and making new friends along the way. From daring jumps to unexpected encounters, their journey is filled with excitement and surprises that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Overcoming Challenges
