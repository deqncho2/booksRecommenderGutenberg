Synopsis: In "The Sand-Hills of Jutland," Hans Christian Andersen takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of Denmark. The story follows a young boy named Niels, who embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with mystery, magic, and unexpected encounters. As Niels explores the sand dunes of Jutland, he discovers hidden treasures, encounters mythical creatures, and learns valuable life lessons along the way. This enchanting tale will transport readers to a world of wonder and imagination, leaving them eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the sand-hills of Jutland.

Genres: Fairy tale, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Imagination, Life lessons
