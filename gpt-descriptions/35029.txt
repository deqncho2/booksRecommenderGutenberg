Synopsis: In 'Half-Past Bedtime' by H. H. Bashford, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a wealthy socialite mysteriously disappears from her lavish mansion. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances, leading him to question the true nature of those closest to him. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate a treacherous world of high society, where appearances can be deceiving and danger lurks behind every opulent facade.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Class divide
