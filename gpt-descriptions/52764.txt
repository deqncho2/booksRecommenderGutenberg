Synopsis: 
'Under a Veil: A Comedietta in One Act' follows the hilarious misadventures of a group of characters as they navigate a series of misunderstandings and mistaken identities. Set in a charming country house, the story unfolds with witty dialogue, clever disguises, and unexpected revelations. As the characters' secrets are gradually unveiled, the audience is taken on a delightful journey filled with laughter and surprises.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Deception, Identity, Love, Humor
