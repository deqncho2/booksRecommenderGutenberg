Synopsis: 
'A Guide to the Mount's Bay and the Land's End' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of West Cornwall. Written by John Ayrton Paris, this comprehensive guide explores the region's topography, botany, agriculture, fisheries, antiquities, mining, mineralogy, and geology. From the rugged cliffs of Land's End to the picturesque Mount's Bay, Paris delves into the rich history and natural wonders of this remarkable corner of England. With vivid descriptions and insightful observations, the guide offers a fascinating exploration of the region's diverse features, providing readers with a deeper understanding and appreciation of West Cornwall's unique beauty.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, Natural History

Themes: Exploration, Nature, History, Cultural Heritage
