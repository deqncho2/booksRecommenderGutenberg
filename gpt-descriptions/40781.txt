Synopsis: 
"The Art of Cross-Examination: With the Cross-Examinations of Important Witnesses in Some Celebrated Cases" by Francis L. Wellman is a captivating exploration of the art of cross-examination through the examination of key witnesses in famous legal cases. This book delves into the strategies, techniques, and psychology behind effective cross-examination, providing readers with a fascinating insight into the world of courtroom drama. From dissecting witness testimonies to unraveling hidden truths, Wellman's work offers a thrilling journey into the intricate world of legal battles.

Genres: Non-fiction, Law, Legal Thriller

Themes: Cross-examination, Legal strategy, Psychology of witnesses, Truth and deception
