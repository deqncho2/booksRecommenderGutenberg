Synopsis: 

'Lightning Rod Conference: Report of the Delegates from the Following Societies, Viz.: Meteorological Society, Royal Institute of British Architects, Society of Telegraph Engineers and of Electricians, Physical Society. With a Code of Rules for the Erection of Lightning Conductors; and Various Appendices' takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of lightning rods and their significance. This report compiles the findings and recommendations of delegates from prestigious societies, each bringing their unique expertise to the table. As the delegates delve into the intricacies of lightning conductors, their discussions uncover surprising revelations and innovative solutions. With a wealth of appendices providing additional insights, this report is an essential read for anyone interested in the fascinating realm of lightning protection.

Genres: Non-fiction, Scientific report, Technical manual

Themes: Lightning protection, Scientific collaboration, Technological advancements
