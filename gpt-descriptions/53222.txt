Synopsis: In 'Royal Railways with Uniform Rates', Arnold Whately C. presents a groundbreaking proposal that suggests the amalgamation of Railways with the General Post Office, along with the adoption of uniform fares and rates for any distance. This proposal aims to revolutionize the transportation system and bring about greater efficiency and convenience for the public. As the plot unfolds, the author explores the potential benefits and challenges of this ambitious plan, captivating readers with its innovative ideas and thought-provoking arguments.

Genres: Non-fiction, Economics, Transportation

Themes: Transportation reform, Efficiency, Public convenience
