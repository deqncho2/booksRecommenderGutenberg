Synopsis: In 'The Sound of Silence' by Barbara Constant, a renowned pianist mysteriously loses her ability to hear music, plunging her into a world of silence. As she embarks on a journey to rediscover her passion, she encounters a charismatic violinist who holds the key to unlocking her musical soul. Together, they navigate the complexities of love, loss, and the power of sound, leading to a breathtaking climax that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Music, Identity, Redemption
