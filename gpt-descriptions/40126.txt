Synopsis: In "The Cock and Anchor" by Joseph Sheridan Le Fanu, a young woman named Mary Dermody finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she inherits a curious establishment known as The Cock and Anchor Inn. As Mary delves deeper into the secrets of the inn, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. With her life hanging in the balance, Mary must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and secure her future.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Inheritance, Deception, Betrayal, Identity
