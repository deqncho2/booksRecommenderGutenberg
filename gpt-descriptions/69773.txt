Synopsis: In 'The Long Patrol' by Albert M. Treynor, a group of elite soldiers embarks on a dangerous mission deep behind enemy lines. As they navigate treacherous terrain and face relentless adversaries, they must rely on their training, camaraderie, and sheer determination to survive. With each step, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. This gripping tale of courage, sacrifice, and the unbreakable bonds of brotherhood will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Action, Thriller, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival
