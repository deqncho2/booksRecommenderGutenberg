Synopsis: 
In 'Young Folks' Treasury, Volume 2: Myths and Legendary Heroes', embark on a captivating journey through the realms of ancient myths and legendary heroes. From the epic tales of Greek gods and goddesses to the heroic adventures of King Arthur and his Knights of the Round Table, this volume brings to life the timeless stories that have captivated generations. Discover the courage, cunning, and sacrifice of legendary figures such as Hercules, Perseus, and Beowulf as they face formidable challenges and embark on extraordinary quests. With vivid storytelling and enchanting illustrations, this collection will transport readers to a world of mythical creatures, heroic deeds, and the enduring power of legends.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Adventure
Themes: Heroism, Fate and Destiny, Moral Lessons
