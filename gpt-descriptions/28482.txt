Synopsis: 
In 'Sawtooth Ranch' by B. M. Bower, a young woman named Joan embarks on a journey to the rugged Sawtooth Mountains, seeking solace and a fresh start. However, she soon finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger as she uncovers dark secrets about the ranch and its inhabitants. With her determination and resilience, Joan must navigate treacherous paths and confront her own past to uncover the truth and protect those she cares about.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Resilience
