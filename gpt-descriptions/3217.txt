Synopsis: In 'Mr. Honey's Small Business Dictionary (English-German)', author Winfried Honig takes readers on a captivating linguistic journey through the world of small business. This unique dictionary not only provides translations of essential business terms from English to German, but also offers insightful explanations and examples that shed light on the intricacies of the business world. From marketing strategies to financial concepts, readers will find themselves immersed in a wealth of knowledge that transcends mere language translation. With its comprehensive coverage and engaging style, 'Mr. Honey's Small Business Dictionary' is an indispensable resource for entrepreneurs, students, and professionals alike.

Genres: Reference, Business, Language Learning

Themes: Small business, Entrepreneurship, Language acquisition
