Synopsis: In 'The Duke of Brittany' by Jeanrenaud, Henriette, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love in the opulent court of 15th century France. As she navigates the treacherous waters of power and passion, Henriette must make choices that will shape her destiny and the fate of those she holds dear. With its rich historical backdrop and captivating characters, this tale of love, betrayal, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Power struggles, Forbidden love, Personal sacrifice, Loyalty
