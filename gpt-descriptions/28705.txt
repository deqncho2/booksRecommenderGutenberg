Synopsis: In 'The God in the Box' by Wright, Sewell Peaslee, a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds a mysterious power. As he delves deeper into its origins, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and danger, where the line between myth and reality blurs. With each revelation, he must confront his own beliefs and face the consequences of tampering with forces beyond his control.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Power of belief, Exploration of ancient civilizations, Consequences of curiosity
