Synopsis: 
In "Patricia" by Caroline Emilia Jacobs, a young woman named Patricia finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter that leads her on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's past. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Patricia becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where nothing is as it seems and trust is a luxury she cannot afford. With each revelation, Patricia's world is turned upside down, forcing her to question her own identity and confront the dark forces that threaten to destroy everything she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Deception, Trust
