Synopsis: 
In 'Stories from Tagore', Rabindranath Tagore takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales that explore the depths of human emotions and the complexities of relationships. From poignant love stories to thought-provoking moral dilemmas, each story is a window into the rich tapestry of life, beautifully woven with Tagore's lyrical prose. As readers delve into the lives of diverse characters, they will be transported to a world where love, longing, sacrifice, and redemption intertwine, leaving them spellbound till the very last page.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Literary Fiction

Themes: Love, Relationships, Morality, Identity, Redemption
