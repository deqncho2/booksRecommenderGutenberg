Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Port Haven, a mysterious stranger arrives at the harbor, setting off a chain of events that will unravel the secrets hidden within the community. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of deception, love, and betrayal, they must confront their own pasts and make choices that will forever change their lives. With its atmospheric setting and a cast of intriguing characters, 'The Harbor' takes readers on a gripping journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Secrets and lies, Redemption, Small-town dynamics
