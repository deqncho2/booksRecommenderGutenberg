Synopsis: In 'Darkness and Daylight: A Novel' by Mary Jane Holmes, a young woman named Fanny Raynor finds herself caught between two worlds - one of darkness and one of daylight. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Fanny must confront the secrets that threaten to unravel her world. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel explores the power of resilience and the choices we make in the face of adversity.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Love and betrayal, societal expectations, resilience
