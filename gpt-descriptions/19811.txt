Synopsis: 
'A Book of English Prose: Part II, Arranged for Secondary and High Schools' by Lubbock, Percy is a captivating collection of carefully selected prose pieces that will transport readers on a literary journey through various time periods and perspectives. From thought-provoking essays to gripping narratives, this anthology offers a diverse range of English prose that will engage and inspire readers of all ages. Each piece has been thoughtfully chosen to provide a glimpse into the rich tapestry of English literature, showcasing the power of language and the art of storytelling. Whether exploring the depths of human emotions or delving into societal issues, this collection invites readers to immerse themselves in the beauty and complexity of the English language.

Genres: Anthology, Literature, Education

Themes: Language and communication, Human emotions, Society and culture
