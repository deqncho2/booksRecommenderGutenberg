Synopsis: In the enchanting town of old St. Augustine, secrets lie hidden beneath its charming facade. As a young historian delves into the forgotten past, she uncovers a series of mysterious events that have shaped the town's history. From hidden treasures to long-lost love stories, she unravels the untold tales that have been kept hidden for centuries. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, leading her on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth and preserve the town's legacy.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of storytelling, the preservation of heritage
