Synopsis: In 'Victor Serenus: A Story of the Pauline Era', we are transported back to the tumultuous time of the Pauline era, where the protagonist, Victor Serenus, finds himself caught between his loyalty to the Roman Empire and his growing fascination with the teachings of the apostle Paul. As Victor navigates the treacherous political landscape and grapples with his own inner conflicts, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, love, and spiritual awakening. With vivid historical detail and compelling characters, this captivating tale explores the clash between power and faith, and the transformative power of belief.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Religious Fiction, Political Drama

Themes: Faith and Doubt, Loyalty and Betrayal, Power and Corruption
