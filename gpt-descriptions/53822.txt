Synopsis: 

'Footprints of Abraham Lincoln: Presenting many interesting facts, reminiscences and illustrations never before published' takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and legacy of one of America's most iconic figures. Written by J. T. Hobson, this book unveils a treasure trove of untold stories, personal anecdotes, and rare illustrations that shed new light on the enigmatic Abraham Lincoln. From his humble beginnings to his rise as the 16th President of the United States, this book delves into the lesser-known aspects of Lincoln's life, offering a fresh perspective on his character, struggles, and triumphs. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the intriguing facts and intimate glimpses into the world of this remarkable leader.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Leadership, Legacy, Historical Significance
