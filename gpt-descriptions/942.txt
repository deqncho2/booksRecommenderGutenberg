Synopsis: In the heart of the lush tropical forest, a mysterious and enchanting love story unfolds. Abel, a young man seeking solace from his troubled past, finds himself drawn to the ethereal beauty of Rima, a wild and captivating creature who lives harmoniously with nature. As their bond deepens, Abel becomes entangled in a world of secrets and ancient legends, where love and danger intertwine in ways he could never have imagined.

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Love and connection with nature, the clash between civilization and the natural world, the search for identity and purpose
