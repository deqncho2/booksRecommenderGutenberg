Synopsis: In 'The Black Tor: A Tale of the Reign of James the First' by George Manville Fenn, a young nobleman finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he inherits a remote estate known as the Black Tor. As he delves into the secrets of the tor, he uncovers a dark history and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, including a beautiful but enigmatic woman and a sinister figure who will stop at nothing to possess the tor's hidden treasures. With his life on the line, the young nobleman must navigate treacherous paths and face unexpected challenges to unravel the tor's secrets and protect his inheritance.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets and Betrayal, Courage and Perseverance
