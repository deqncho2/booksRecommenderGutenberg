Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Sixteenth, or, the Bedfordshire Regiment of Foot: Containing an Account of the Formation of the Regiment in 1688, and of Its Subsequent Services to 1848', Richard Cannon delves into the captivating history of the Bedfordshire Regiment of Foot. From its humble beginnings in 1688 to its remarkable achievements up until 1848, this book unveils the untold stories of bravery, sacrifice, and resilience that shaped the regiment's legacy. Cannon's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life the battles, campaigns, and pivotal moments that defined the regiment's journey. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Resilience, Military Strategy, Historical Legacy
