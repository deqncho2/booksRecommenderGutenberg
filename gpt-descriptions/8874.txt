Synopsis: 
'Queechy' by Susan Warner is a captivating novel that follows the life of Fleda Vetch, a young orphan who finds herself in a world of wealth and privilege. As she navigates the complexities of her new surroundings, Fleda must confront the challenges of love, loyalty, and self-discovery. With unexpected twists and turns, this story takes readers on a journey of personal growth and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and relationships, Social class, Identity and self-discovery
