Synopsis: 
'Contact, and Other Stories' by Frances Noyes Hart is a captivating collection of short stories that will transport readers into a world of mystery, suspense, and unexpected twists. From a chilling encounter with a ghostly presence to a perplexing disappearance, each story presents a unique and gripping narrative that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. As the characters navigate through eerie situations and unexplained phenomena, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and question the boundaries of reality. With its masterful storytelling and atmospheric settings, 'Contact, and Other Stories' is a must-read for fans of thrilling and thought-provoking tales.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Reality, Perception
