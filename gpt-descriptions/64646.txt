Synopsis: In 'Goma's Follicles' by De Courcy, Dorothy/ De Courcy, John, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the mysterious world of Goma, a young woman with a unique ability to communicate with her hair follicles. As Goma discovers the hidden powers of her follicles, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and intrigue. With the help of a quirky group of allies, Goma must navigate through treacherous obstacles and uncover the truth about her extraordinary gift. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the mysteries that lie within Goma's follicles.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Power, Self-discovery
