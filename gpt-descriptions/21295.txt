Synopsis: In 'Cormorant Crag: A Tale of the Smuggling Days' by George Manville Fenn, a young man named Dick Varley finds himself entangled in a dangerous world of smuggling along the rugged coast of Cornwall. Drawn into a web of mystery and intrigue, Dick must navigate treacherous cliffs, secret caves, and a network of cunning smugglers to uncover the truth. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of the smuggling trade, Dick's courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to outwit his adversaries and bring justice to Cormorant Crag?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Smuggling, Courage, Betrayal, Justice
