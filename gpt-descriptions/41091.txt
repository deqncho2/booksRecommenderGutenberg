Synopsis: In 'The Price of Power: Being Chapters from the Secret History of the Imperial Court of Russia', Le Queux takes readers on a thrilling journey through the hidden corridors of power in the Russian Imperial Court. As a young British diplomat finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden romances, and dangerous secrets, he must navigate the treacherous world of the Russian elite to uncover the truth. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the price of power becomes increasingly evident.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Political intrigue, Power struggles, Forbidden love, Secrets and deception
