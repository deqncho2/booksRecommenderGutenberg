Synopsis: In "Roads of Destiny" by O. Henry, a mysterious stranger finds himself at a crossroads, where he is given the power to choose between three different paths that will determine his destiny. Each road leads him to a different time, place, and set of circumstances, presenting him with unexpected challenges and opportunities. As he navigates through these divergent paths, the stranger must confront the consequences of his choices and ultimately discover the true meaning of fate and free will.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Mystery

Themes: Fate vs. Free Will, Choices and Consequences, Serendipity
