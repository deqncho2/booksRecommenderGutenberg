Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts for Home Protection' by Robert Shaler, a group of resourceful and courageous Boy Scouts find themselves facing unexpected dangers and mysteries in their own neighborhood. As they navigate through thrilling adventures, they uncover secrets that could change their lives forever. With their skills and determination, these young scouts prove that they are not just prepared for the wilderness, but also for the challenges that lie closer to home.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Community, Self-discovery
