Synopsis: 
In 'Phineas Finn: The Irish Member' by Anthony Trollope, we follow the journey of Phineas Finn, a young and ambitious Irishman who enters the world of British politics. As he navigates the treacherous waters of Parliament, Phineas encounters love, friendship, and betrayal, all while grappling with his own principles and ambitions. With a cast of intriguing characters and a backdrop of political intrigue, this novel takes readers on a captivating journey through the complexities of power and personal growth.

Genres: 
Political fiction, Historical fiction, Romance

Themes: 
Ambition, Loyalty, Love, Identity, Social class
