Synopsis: In "The great probability of a North West Passage: deduced from observations on the letter of Admiral de Fonte," the author delves into the intriguing account of Admiral de Fonte's letter, which claims the discovery of a navigable passage through the North West. As the author investigates the authenticity of the letter and its implications, a captivating exploration of uncharted territories, hidden secrets, and the pursuit of knowledge unfolds. With each revelation, the reader is drawn deeper into the mystery surrounding the North West Passage and the potential impact it could have on the world.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Deception, Quest for knowledge
