Synopsis: In 'Galatea' by Miguel de Cervantes Saavedra, a young shepherd named Elicio embarks on a journey to find his lost love, Galatea. Along the way, he encounters a series of captivating adventures, including encounters with mythical creatures and encounters with other individuals who are also searching for their own lost loves. As Elicio's quest unfolds, the boundaries between reality and fantasy become blurred, leading to unexpected twists and turns that keep the reader guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Love and longing, the power of imagination, the search for identity
