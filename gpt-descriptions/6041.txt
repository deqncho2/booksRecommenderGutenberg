Synopsis: 

'Stories by English Authors: The Sea' is a captivating collection of short stories that will transport readers to the vast and unpredictable world of the sea. From tales of adventure and survival to haunting encounters with the unknown, this anthology explores the deep and mysterious allure of the ocean. Dive into the pages and embark on thrilling journeys that will leave you breathless and yearning for more.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Human Nature
