Synopsis: In 'Why We Love Music', Carl E. Seashore takes readers on a captivating journey into the depths of our emotional connection with music. Through a combination of scientific research, personal anecdotes, and historical examples, Seashore explores the profound impact that music has on our lives, delving into the mysteries of why certain melodies can evoke intense emotions and transport us to different worlds. From the power of rhythm to the influence of cultural backgrounds, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the multifaceted reasons behind our love for music.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Psychology

Themes: Emotional connection, Cultural influence, Psychological impact
