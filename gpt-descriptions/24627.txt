Synopsis: In "Booker T. Washington, Builder of a Civilization," Scott and Stowe delve into the extraordinary life of one of America's most influential figures. From his humble beginnings as a slave to becoming a renowned educator and civil rights leader, Booker T. Washington's journey is a testament to the power of determination and perseverance. This captivating biography explores the challenges he faced, the impact he made on society, and the legacy he left behind. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors bring to life the inspiring story of a man who dedicated his life to building a better future for African Americans.

Genres: Biography, History, Education

Themes: Perseverance, Education, Civil Rights, Leadership
