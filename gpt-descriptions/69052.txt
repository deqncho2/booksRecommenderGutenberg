Synopsis: In 'The Ward of Tecumseh' by Marriott, Crittenden, a young orphan named Mary finds herself caught in the midst of the War of 1812. Placed under the care of the legendary Shawnee leader Tecumseh, Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience as she navigates the complexities of war, love, and her own identity. As the conflict intensifies, Mary must confront her deepest fears and make choices that will shape her destiny. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, War, Resilience, Love
