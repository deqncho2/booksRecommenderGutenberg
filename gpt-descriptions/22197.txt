Synopsis: In 'The Goody-Naughty Book' by Sarah Cory Rippey, embark on a whimsical journey through the pages of a mischievous book that has a mind of its own. As the book's characters come to life, they challenge the reader's expectations and blur the lines between good and naughty. With delightful surprises and unexpected twists, this enchanting tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Morality, Self-discovery
