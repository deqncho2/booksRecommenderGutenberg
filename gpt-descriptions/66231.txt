Synopsis: In 'Wolf-Cap; or, The Night-Hawks of the Fire-Lands: A Tale of the Bloody Fort' by T.C. Harbaugh, a young frontiersman named Wolf-Cap finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous conflict between settlers and Native American tribes in the Fire-Lands. As he navigates the treacherous wilderness and encounters a secret society known as the Night-Hawks, Wolf-Cap must uncover the truth behind the Bloody Fort and confront his own inner demons. This thrilling tale of adventure and mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they follow Wolf-Cap's journey through a land filled with danger and intrigue.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Conflict and reconciliation, Identity and self-discovery, Exploration of frontier life
