Synopsis: In "Pictorial history of the war for the Union, volume 1," Ann S. Stephens presents a captivating and comprehensive account of the American Civil War. From the very beginning to its dramatic conclusion, this book takes readers on a visual journey through the war's most significant events. Accompanied by a detailed chronological analysis, Stephens provides a reliable and engaging narrative that brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices of both the Union and Confederate forces. With vivid illustrations and meticulous research, this volume offers a unique perspective on one of the most pivotal periods in American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Pictorial history

Themes: American Civil War, Military history, Historical analysis
