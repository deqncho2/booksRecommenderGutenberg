Synopsis: In 'Lord of the World', a gripping dystopian novel set in the early 21st century, the world is on the brink of a global crisis. As political tensions rise and religious beliefs clash, a charismatic leader emerges, captivating the masses with his promises of peace and unity. Amidst this backdrop, a young Catholic priest, a disillusioned politician, and a brave woman find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger, as they navigate a world on the verge of collapse. With its thought-provoking exploration of faith, politics, and the human condition, 'Lord of the World' is a thrilling and haunting tale that will leave readers questioning the future of humanity.

Genres: Dystopian fiction, Political thriller, Religious fiction

Themes: Religion and spirituality, Politics and power, Faith and doubt, Human nature and morality
