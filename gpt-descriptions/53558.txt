Synopsis: In 'The Duchess of Rosemary Lane: A Novel', follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Rosemary as she navigates the bustling streets of London in the early 20th century. From her humble beginnings in the slums to her unexpected rise in society, Rosemary's life takes a dramatic turn when she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal. As she strives to find her place in a world filled with both cruelty and kindness, Rosemary's resilience and determination will captivate readers, leaving them eagerly turning the pages to uncover the mysteries that lie ahead.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Social class, Identity, Love and betrayal
