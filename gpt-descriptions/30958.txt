Synopsis: 
In 'Jack Winters' Campmates' by Mark Overton, a group of friends embark on a thrilling camping trip that takes an unexpected turn. As they navigate through the wilderness, they encounter mysterious occurrences and face unforeseen challenges that test their friendship and survival skills. With each passing day, the campmates uncover secrets that bind them together, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Secrets
