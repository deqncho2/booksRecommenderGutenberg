Synopsis: In 'Count Hannibal: A Romance of the Court of France', Stanley John Weyman takes us on a thrilling journey through the opulent and treacherous world of the French court. Set in the 16th century, the story follows the enigmatic Count Hannibal as he navigates the dangerous political landscape, where loyalty and betrayal go hand in hand. With his cunning mind and mysterious past, Count Hannibal becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, romance, and deadly secrets that could change the fate of France forever. As the tension rises and the stakes become higher, readers will be captivated by the Count's audacious schemes and the unexpected twists that lie ahead.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Intrigue, Loyalty, Betrayal, Power
