Synopsis: 
In "Daisy: the autobiography of a cat," we are taken on a captivating journey through the eyes of Daisy, a curious and independent feline. From her humble beginnings as a stray kitten to her adventures in the bustling city, Daisy's story is filled with heartwarming moments, unexpected friendships, and a few surprises along the way. As she navigates the complexities of human relationships and the challenges of survival, Daisy's unique perspective offers a fresh and enchanting take on the world we think we know.

Genres: Autobiography, Fiction, Animal Literature

Themes: Independence, Friendship, Survival, Human-Animal Connection
