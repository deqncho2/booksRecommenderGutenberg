Synopsis: In 'Traitor and True: A Romance' by Bloundelle-Burton, John, a young woman finds herself torn between loyalty and love in the midst of political intrigue and danger. Set against the backdrop of a turbulent era, she must navigate treacherous alliances and make difficult choices that will shape her destiny. As secrets unravel and loyalties are tested, the line between friend and foe becomes blurred, leading to a captivating tale of romance, betrayal, and the pursuit of truth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Loyalty, Love, Betrayal, Identity, Power
