Synopsis: In 'The Jealousy of le Barbouillé', Molière takes us on a hilarious journey through the absurdities of jealousy. The story revolves around a man named le Barbouillé, whose unfounded suspicions lead him to question the fidelity of his wife. As his jealousy escalates, chaos ensues, with mistaken identities, comical misunderstandings, and a series of outrageous events that will keep readers laughing until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Farce

Themes: Jealousy, Trust, Deception
