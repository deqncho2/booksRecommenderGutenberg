Synopsis: 

In "Lives of the Engineers: The Locomotive. George and Robert Stephenson," Samuel Smiles takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two remarkable individuals who revolutionized the world of engineering. This enthralling biography delves into the trials and triumphs of George and Robert Stephenson, exploring their relentless pursuit of innovation and their unwavering dedication to the development of the locomotive. From their humble beginnings to their groundbreaking achievements, Smiles paints a vivid portrait of these visionary engineers, showcasing their indomitable spirit and the profound impact they had on the industrial revolution.

Genres: Biography, History, Engineering

Themes: Innovation, Perseverance, Industrial Revolution
