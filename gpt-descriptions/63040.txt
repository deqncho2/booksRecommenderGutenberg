Synopsis: 

"A Minor War History Compiled from a Soldier Boy's Letters to 'the Girl I Left Behind Me': 1861-1864" is a captivating collection of letters written by a young soldier during the American Civil War. Through these heartfelt correspondences, the reader is transported to the frontlines, experiencing the trials and triumphs of war through the eyes of a courageous and devoted soldier. As the letters unfold, secrets are revealed, friendships are forged, and love is tested, painting a vivid picture of the realities faced by those who fought in this historic conflict.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Epistolary Novel, War Literature

Themes: Love and Longing, Sacrifice and Bravery, Friendship and Loyalty
