Synopsis: In 'The Pony Rider Boys in Montana; Or, The Mystery of the Old Custer Trail', the adventurous group of young riders embarks on a thrilling journey through the rugged landscapes of Montana. As they follow the trail of the legendary General Custer, they stumble upon a perplexing mystery that leads them into uncharted territory. With danger lurking at every turn, the Pony Rider Boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to unravel the secrets of the Old Custer Trail.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Courage, History
