Synopsis: In 'The Red Horizon' by Patrick MacGill, a young Irishman named Dermot O'Donovan finds himself caught in the midst of the tumultuous events of World War I. Leaving behind his peaceful life in Ireland, Dermot embarks on a journey that takes him from the trenches of the Western Front to the desolate landscapes of Gallipoli. As he witnesses the horrors of war and forms unlikely friendships, Dermot's resilience and determination are tested to their limits. Will he be able to survive the chaos and find his way back home?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: The human cost of war, resilience in the face of adversity, the bonds of friendship
