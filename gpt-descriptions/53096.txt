Synopsis: In 'Self-Doomed: A Novel' by B. L. Farjeon, a mysterious and enigmatic stranger arrives in a small English village, captivating the residents with his charm and charisma. As the villagers become increasingly infatuated with him, they soon discover that his presence brings about a series of inexplicable and tragic events. With each passing day, the stranger's true nature becomes more apparent, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Deception, Manipulation, Consequences of Actions
