Synopsis: In 'Neighbours on the Green' by Mrs. Oliphant, a seemingly idyllic neighborhood is disrupted when a mysterious newcomer moves in, bringing with them a dark secret that threatens to unravel the lives of the residents. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the once peaceful community is forced to confront their own hidden desires and the consequences of their actions. With its gripping plot and complex characters, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the facade of suburban bliss.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Domestic Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Community dynamics, Morality and ethics
