Synopsis: 
'Theft: A Play In Four Acts' by Jack London is a gripping and suspenseful drama that follows the lives of two brothers, Joe and Andy, who find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and stolen identities. As the plot unfolds, the brothers are forced to confront their own moral compasses and make difficult choices that will forever alter their lives. With unexpected twists and turns, 'Theft' explores the depths of human nature and the consequences of our actions.

Genres: Drama, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Morality, Betrayal, Consequences
