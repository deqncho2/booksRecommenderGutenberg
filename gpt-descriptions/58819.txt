Synopsis: In 'The Choir School of St. Bede's' by Frederick Harrison, a talented young boy named Thomas finds himself thrust into the prestigious world of St. Bede's, a renowned choir school. As he navigates the challenges of rigorous training, intense competition, and the complexities of friendship, Thomas discovers that the pursuit of excellence comes at a price. With secrets lurking behind the grandeur of the school, Thomas must uncover the truth while striving to protect his dreams and the bonds he forms along the way.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Friendship, Identity, Secrets
