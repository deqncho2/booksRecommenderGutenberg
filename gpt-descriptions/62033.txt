Synopsis: In 'Blood Will Tell: The Strange Story of a Son of Ham' by Davenport, Benjamin Rush, a young man named Ham embarks on a journey of self-discovery that takes him through the dark underbelly of society. As he navigates a world filled with secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, Ham's true identity becomes a matter of life and death. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of intrigue and mystery, wondering if Ham will ever uncover the truth about his past and find redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Betrayal, Secrets
