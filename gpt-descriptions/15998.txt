Synopsis: 

'Alfred Russel Wallace: Letters and Reminiscences, Vol. 2' takes readers on an intimate journey through the life and mind of the renowned naturalist and explorer, Alfred Russel Wallace. This captivating collection of letters and reminiscences, compiled by James Marchant, offers a unique glimpse into Wallace's personal experiences, scientific discoveries, and his interactions with some of the greatest minds of his time. From his adventures in the Amazon rainforest to his groundbreaking theory of evolution, this volume delves into the fascinating life of a man whose contributions to science were overshadowed by his contemporary, Charles Darwin. 

Genres: Biography, Science, History

Themes: Exploration, Evolution, Scientific Discovery
