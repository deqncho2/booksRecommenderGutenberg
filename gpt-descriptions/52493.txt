Synopsis: In 'Susan Gets Ready for Church: A Monologue' by MacKenzie, Edna I., readers are taken on a captivating journey through the mind of Susan as she prepares for a significant event. Through her introspective monologue, we witness Susan's inner struggles, desires, and the complexities of her relationships. As she meticulously gets ready for church, a series of unexpected revelations and emotions unfold, leaving readers intrigued and eager to uncover the secrets that lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Drama, Psychological Fiction, Monologue

Themes: Identity, Religion, Self-reflection, Relationships
