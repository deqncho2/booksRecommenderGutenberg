Synopsis: In 'Mr. Meek Plays Polo' by Clifford D. Simak, a seemingly ordinary man named Mr. Meek unexpectedly finds himself thrust into the world of polo, a sport he knows nothing about. As he navigates the challenges and complexities of the game, Mr. Meek's journey becomes a captivating exploration of determination, personal growth, and the unexpected paths life can take.

Genres: Science Fiction, Sports, Adventure

Themes: Personal transformation, Perseverance, Embracing the unknown
