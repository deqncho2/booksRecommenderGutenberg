Synopsis: In 'Ride Proud, Rebel!' by Andre Norton, we follow the gripping journey of Drew Rennie, a young Confederate soldier who finds himself caught between loyalty to his cause and the pursuit of his own freedom. As the war rages on, Drew embarks on a dangerous mission that will test his courage, resilience, and determination to survive. With unexpected alliances and heart-pounding adventures, this captivating tale explores the complexities of war and the indomitable spirit of a young man fighting for his beliefs.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Freedom, Identity, Survival
