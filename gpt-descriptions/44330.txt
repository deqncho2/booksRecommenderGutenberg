Synopsis: In 'Valerius. A Roman Story' by J. G. Lockhart, readers are transported to ancient Rome, where they follow the journey of Valerius, a young and ambitious Roman soldier. As Valerius rises through the ranks of the Roman army, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and dangerous alliances. With vivid descriptions of the grandeur and brutality of ancient Rome, this captivating tale explores the lengths one man will go to protect his honor and achieve his dreams.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Honor, Love, Betrayal
