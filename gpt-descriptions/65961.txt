Synopsis: In 'When Oscar Went Wild' by W. C. Tuttle, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger named Oscar arrives with a wild animal in tow. As the townspeople try to unravel the secrets behind Oscar and his unusual companion, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling adventure filled with danger, suspense, and unexpected twists. With each passing day, the line between reality and fantasy becomes blurred, leaving everyone wondering just how wild things can get.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Perception, Exploration
