Synopsis: 

'A Guide for the Study of Animals' is a captivating and informative book that takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of animals. Written by Lucas, Frederic Colby, Shinn, Harold Brough, Smallwood, Mabel Elizabeth, and Whitney, Worrallo, this comprehensive guide provides a wealth of knowledge about various animal species, their behaviors, habitats, and interactions with humans. From the smallest insects to the largest mammals, the authors delve into the intricate details of animal life, offering captivating anecdotes and scientific insights that will leave readers in awe of the natural world. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Animal behavior, Biodiversity, Environmental conservation
