Synopsis: In "Pig-Headed" Sailor Men, Louis Becke takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous world of sailors in the early 1900s. The story follows the adventures of James Shervinton, a young man who finds himself entangled in the dangerous lives of sailors and their reckless behavior. From shipwrecks to mutinies, Shervinton must navigate the unpredictable seas and the even more unpredictable personalities of his fellow sailors. As the story unfolds, readers are drawn into a world of high-stakes adventure, where loyalty and survival are constantly tested.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Survival, Recklessness
