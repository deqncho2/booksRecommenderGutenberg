Synopsis: In 'The Zeit-Geist' by L. Dougall, a mysterious artifact is discovered, unleashing a chain of events that intertwines the lives of three individuals from different time periods. As they navigate through the complexities of love, loss, and the pursuit of truth, they are forced to confront their own demons and make choices that will shape their destinies. With elements of mystery, romance, and time travel, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Time Travel

Themes: Love, Loss, Fate, Redemption
