Synopsis: In the mystical land of Pegana, the gods reign supreme, each with their own unique powers and domains. From the creation of the world to the whimsical tales of their interactions, 'The Gods of Pegana' takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through a realm where gods shape the very fabric of existence. As the gods' stories unfold, a deeper understanding of their nature and the intricate balance they maintain is revealed, leading to profound questions about the nature of divinity and the power of belief.

Genres: Fantasy, Mythology, Short Stories

Themes: The nature of gods and divinity, the power of belief, the creation and order of the universe, the interplay between mortals and immortals.
