Synopsis: In "The Bolsheviki and World Peace," Leon Trotsky presents a compelling analysis of the Bolshevik revolution and its impact on the global stage. Through a combination of historical context and political insight, Trotsky delves into the intricate web of international relations, exploring the challenges faced by the newly formed Soviet government in their pursuit of world peace. With a keen eye for detail and a thought-provoking narrative, Trotsky offers a unique perspective on the role of the Bolsheviki in shaping the future of global politics.

Genres: Political analysis, historical non-fiction

Themes: Revolution, international relations, peacebuilding
