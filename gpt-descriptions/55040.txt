Synopsis: In "The Nursery "Alice"" by Lewis Carroll, follow Alice's whimsical adventures as she navigates a world filled with talking animals, peculiar characters, and enchanting riddles. This delightful adaptation of Carroll's classic tale is specially designed for young readers, with simplified language and captivating illustrations that bring the story to life. Join Alice on her imaginative journey through Wonderland, where nothing is quite as it seems, and every page is filled with wonder and curiosity.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Curiosity, Absurdity
