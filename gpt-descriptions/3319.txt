Synopsis: 
In "Letters to Dead Authors" by Andrew Lang, readers are taken on a captivating journey through time and literature as the author pens heartfelt letters to deceased literary figures. From Shakespeare to Dickens, Lang's letters are filled with admiration, curiosity, and a desire to understand the minds behind these timeless works. As he delves into the lives and works of these authors, Lang's letters become a fascinating exploration of the power of literature and its ability to transcend time and connect souls across generations.

Genres: Literary criticism, Epistolary, Biography

Themes: Literature, Timelessness, Connection, Inspiration
