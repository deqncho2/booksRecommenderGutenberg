Synopsis: In 'The Manoeuvring Mother (vol. 2 of 3)' by Charlotte Campbell, Lady Bury, the captivating story continues as Lady Emily navigates the treacherous waters of high society, facing scandal, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As she strives to secure her family's future, Lady Emily must outwit her adversaries and make difficult choices that could forever alter her life. With secrets lurking around every corner, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social climbing, Family dynamics, Deception, Power struggles
