Synopsis: In 'The Idol of the Blind: A Novel' by Tom Gallon, a mysterious artifact with the power to grant unimaginable abilities falls into the hands of a young woman named Amelia. As she navigates a world filled with danger and deception, Amelia must decide whether to use the idol's power for good or succumb to its dark allure. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this thrilling novel explores the consequences of power and the choices we make when faced with temptation.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Power and its consequences, Morality and temptation
