Synopsis: In 'A Square Deal', Theodore Roosevelt takes readers on a captivating journey through his experiences as the 26th President of the United States. From his early days as a rancher in the Wild West to his tireless efforts in conservation and trust-busting, Roosevelt's memoir offers a thrilling account of his determination to create a fair and just society. With vivid anecdotes and a passionate voice, he shares the challenges he faced and the victories he achieved, providing a unique perspective on the tumultuous era of American history in which he served.

Genres: Autobiography, History, Political Memoir

Themes: Leadership, Social Justice, Conservation, American History
