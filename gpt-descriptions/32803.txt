Synopsis: 
'Letters from a Father to His Son Entering College' is a collection of heartfelt and thought-provoking letters written by Charles Franklin Thwing. Through these letters, Thwing offers invaluable advice, guidance, and wisdom to his son as he embarks on his college journey. Covering a wide range of topics, from academics and friendships to personal growth and character development, Thwing's letters provide a unique perspective on the challenges and opportunities that await his son in college. With a blend of warmth, wit, and profound insights, this collection of letters is a must-read for anyone entering college or seeking guidance in navigating life's transitions.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Parenting

Themes: Education, Personal growth, Family, Mentorship
