Synopsis: In 'The Minute Boys of the Mohawk Valley' by James Otis, a group of young boys find themselves caught up in the midst of the American Revolution. As tensions rise and battles loom, these brave boys must navigate the dangers of war, forge unlikely alliances, and discover the true meaning of loyalty and sacrifice. With their courage and determination, they become an integral part of the fight for freedom, leaving a lasting impact on the history of their nation.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Friendship, Courage, Coming of Age
