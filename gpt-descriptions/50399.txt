Synopsis:
In 'Our Eternity' by Maeterlinck, Maurice, a young couple embarks on a journey to uncover the mysteries of life and death. As they delve into the realms of the supernatural, they encounter ethereal beings and explore the boundaries of human existence. Their quest for understanding takes them on a captivating and thought-provoking adventure that challenges their perceptions of reality and immortality.

Genres:
Fantasy, Mystery, Drama

Themes:
Life and death, Existentialism, Spirituality
