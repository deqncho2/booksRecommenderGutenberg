Synopsis: In "The Coming of Evolution: The Story of a Great Revolution in Science," John W. Judd takes readers on a captivating journey through the groundbreaking discoveries and controversies that shaped the field of evolutionary biology. From the early debates surrounding Charles Darwin's theory of natural selection to the modern advancements in genetics and paleontology, this book delves into the fascinating world of evolutionary science. Through vivid storytelling and engaging anecdotes, Judd explores the profound impact of evolution on our understanding of life on Earth, challenging long-held beliefs and igniting a revolution in scientific thought.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Evolution, Scientific Revolution, Controversy, Paradigm Shift, Natural Selection
