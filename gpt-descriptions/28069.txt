Synopsis: In 'Alice in Blunderland: An Iridescent Dream' by John Kendrick Bangs, readers are transported to a whimsical and enchanting world where Alice embarks on a surreal journey filled with peculiar characters and mind-bending adventures. As she navigates through this topsy-turvy realm, Alice encounters absurd situations and encounters that challenge her perception of reality. With each twist and turn, the line between dreams and reality becomes increasingly blurred, leaving readers captivated by the unpredictable and imaginative narrative.

Genres: Fantasy, Satire, Adventure

Themes: Perception of reality, Absurdity, Imagination
