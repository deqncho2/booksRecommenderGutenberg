Synopsis: In 'The Gaming Table: Its Votaries and Victims. Volume 2', Andrew Steinmetz delves deeper into the captivating world of gambling, revealing the stories of both those who are drawn to the gaming table and those who fall victim to its allure. From the opulent casinos of Europe to the seedy underground gambling dens of America, this volume explores the highs and lows of the gambling lifestyle, painting a vivid picture of the characters and circumstances that surround this addictive pastime. With tales of fortune and ruin, passion and deceit, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the captivating and dangerous world of gambling.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gambling, History

Themes: Addiction, Risk, Human nature, Society
