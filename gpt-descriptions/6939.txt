Synopsis: In 'Old Mortality, Volume 1' by Walter Scott, a young man named Henry Morton finds himself caught in the midst of political and religious turmoil in 17th century Scotland. As he navigates the dangerous landscape of divided loyalties and secret societies, Henry becomes entangled with a mysterious woman who holds the key to his family's past. With his life hanging in the balance, Henry must uncover the truth and make choices that will shape his destiny.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Political and Religious Conflict
