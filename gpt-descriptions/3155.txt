Synopsis:
In 'She', a young man named Leo Vincey embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about his ancestry and the mysterious woman who holds the key to his destiny. As he ventures into the heart of Africa, Leo encounters a hidden civilization ruled by a captivating and enigmatic queen known as She-Who-Must-Be-Obeyed. With danger lurking at every turn, Leo must navigate treacherous landscapes and face his deepest fears in order to unlock the secrets of his past and the extraordinary power that lies within him.

Genres:
Adventure, Fantasy, Romance

Themes:
Love and obsession, Immortality, Fate and destiny
