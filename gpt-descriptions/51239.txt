Synopsis: In 'The Modern Athens: A dissection and demonstration of men and things in the Scotch Capital,' Robert Mudie takes us on a captivating journey through the vibrant city of Edinburgh. Through his keen observations and witty anecdotes, Mudie unveils the essence of this Scottish metropolis, delving into its history, architecture, culture, and the colorful characters that inhabit its streets. From the grandeur of the Royal Mile to the hidden corners of the Old Town, Mudie's exploration of Edinburgh is a delightful and enlightening adventure that will leave readers yearning to experience the city for themselves.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, History

Themes: City exploration, Scottish culture, Architectural heritage
