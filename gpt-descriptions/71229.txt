Synopsis: In 'John's Lily' by Eleanor C. Price, a young woman named Lily finds herself entangled in a mysterious web of secrets and danger when she crosses paths with the enigmatic John. As their lives become increasingly intertwined, Lily must navigate a treacherous world of deceit and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Lily must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Trust, Identity, Deception
