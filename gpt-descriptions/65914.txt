Synopsis: In 'Shaming the Speed Limit' by Standish, Burt L., a small town is plagued by reckless drivers who constantly break the speed limit. Frustrated by the lack of enforcement, a group of citizens takes matters into their own hands, devising a unique and controversial method to shame those who disregard the rules of the road. As tensions rise and the town becomes divided, the consequences of their actions become increasingly unpredictable, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers questioning the limits of justice and the power of public shaming.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Vigilantism, Justice, Morality
