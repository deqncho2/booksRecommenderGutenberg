Synopsis:
In the remote wilderness of Canada, a young man named Mordaunt finds himself embarking on a thrilling adventure that will test his courage and resilience. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters dangerous wildlife, Mordaunt's determination to uncover the secrets of Silver Lake becomes an obsession. Along the way, he forms unexpected alliances and faces unexpected challenges that will push him to his limits. With each step closer to the truth, Mordaunt unravels a mystery that will change his life forever.

Genres:
Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Exploration, Survival, Identity, Nature
