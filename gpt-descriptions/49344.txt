Synopsis: In "The Queen's Favourite: A Story of the Restoration," secrets, power struggles, and forbidden love intertwine within the opulent court of King Charles II. As the young and captivating Queen Catherine finds herself drawn to a mysterious courtier, she must navigate the treacherous waters of loyalty, betrayal, and political intrigue. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant Restoration era, this captivating tale explores the complexities of love, ambition, and the lengths one will go to protect their heart's desires.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Power, Love, Betrayal, Loyalty, Ambition
