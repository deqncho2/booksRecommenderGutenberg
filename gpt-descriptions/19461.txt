Synopsis: 
'Tales of Wonder Every Child Should Know' is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that will transport readers to magical realms and ignite their imagination. From mythical creatures to extraordinary adventures, this book presents a diverse range of tales that will captivate both young and old. Each story is filled with wonder, mystery, and valuable life lessons, making it a must-read for children and adults alike. Get ready to embark on a journey through fantastical worlds and discover the power of imagination.

Genres: Fantasy, Fairy Tales, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Morality, Life Lessons
