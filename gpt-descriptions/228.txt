Synopsis:
The Aeneid by Virgil is an epic poem that follows the journey of Aeneas, a Trojan hero, as he escapes the ruins of Troy and embarks on a perilous adventure to fulfill his destiny. Guided by the gods, Aeneas faces numerous challenges, encounters mythical creatures, and engages in epic battles, all while striving to establish a new homeland for his people. Along the way, he encounters love, loss, and the complexities of fate, ultimately shaping the destiny of Rome itself.

Genres:
Epic, Mythology, Adventure

Themes:
Fate and Destiny, Heroism, Love and Loss, The Power of the Gods
