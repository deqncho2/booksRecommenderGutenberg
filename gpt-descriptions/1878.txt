Synopsis: In 'A Millionaire of Yesterday', a mysterious and enigmatic millionaire suddenly appears in a small English village, capturing the attention and curiosity of the locals. As his past is slowly unraveled, secrets, scandals, and unexpected connections come to light, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful journey that explores the complexities of wealth, power, and the true nature of identity.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Wealth and power, Identity and self-discovery
