Synopsis: In 'Once Upon A Time' by Davis, Richard Harding, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, captivating the residents with his enchanting stories. As the townspeople become enthralled by his tales, they soon discover that his stories hold a deeper meaning, intertwining their lives in unexpected ways. With each story, the stranger reveals a hidden truth about the human condition, forcing the townspeople to confront their own fears, desires, and secrets. As the line between reality and fiction blurs, the residents must navigate the consequences of their actions and the power of storytelling itself.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Truth and deception, the power of storytelling, self-discovery, the human condition
