Synopsis: In 'Indian Palmistry', Mrs. Dale takes readers on a captivating journey into the ancient art of palmistry, exploring the intricate lines and markings on our hands that hold the secrets to our past, present, and future. With a blend of historical anecdotes, personal experiences, and practical guidance, this book unveils the fascinating world of palm reading, revealing how it can provide insights into love, career, health, and more. As readers delve into the pages, they will discover the power of this mystical practice and how it can unlock hidden truths about themselves and those around them.

Genres: Non-fiction, Spirituality, Self-help

Themes: Palmistry, Divination, Self-discovery
