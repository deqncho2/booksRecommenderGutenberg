Synopsis: In 'Terry: A Tale of the Hill People' by Thomson, Charles Goff, a young boy named Terry finds himself caught between two worlds when his family moves from the bustling city to a remote hillside community. As Terry navigates the challenges of adapting to his new surroundings, he discovers a hidden world filled with ancient traditions, mysterious legends, and a deep connection to nature. With the help of newfound friends, Terry embarks on a journey of self-discovery, where he must confront his fears, unravel family secrets, and ultimately find his place among the Hill People.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Belonging, Tradition, Nature
