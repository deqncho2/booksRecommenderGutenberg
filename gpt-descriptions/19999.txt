Synopsis: In 'The Drummer Boy' by J. T. Trowbridge, a young boy named Joe is faced with the challenges of war and the harsh realities of life during the Civil War. As he joins the Union Army as a drummer boy, Joe must navigate the dangers of battle, the loss of loved ones, and the bonds formed with his fellow soldiers. Through his journey, Joe learns the true meaning of bravery, sacrifice, and the power of music.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Friendship, Music
