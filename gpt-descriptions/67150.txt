Synopsis: In "Bobbie", a Story of the Confederacy, Kate Langley Bosher takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of the American Civil War. Set in the heart of the Confederacy, the story follows the life of Bobbie, a spirited young woman who finds herself caught between her loyalty to her family and her desire for independence. As the war rages on, Bobbie's strength and resilience are put to the test, leading her on a path of self-discovery, love, and sacrifice. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Bosher paints a vivid picture of the era, immersing readers in the struggles and triumphs of the Confederacy.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Independence, Love, Sacrifice
