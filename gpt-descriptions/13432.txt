Synopsis: In 'Miss Bretherton' by Ward, Humphry, Mrs., a young woman named Miss Bretherton finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love and marriage, she must confront the consequences of her choices and the limitations imposed upon her by society. With secrets, scandals, and unexpected twists, this captivating novel explores the challenges faced by women in the Victorian era and the power of personal agency.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Marriage, Gender Roles, Social Expectations
