Synopsis: In the tenth volume of "The Last of the Barons," the story takes an unexpected turn as the fate of the protagonist, Warwick the Kingmaker, hangs in the balance. As political alliances shift and treachery lurks in every corner, Warwick finds himself facing a formidable adversary who threatens to unravel all his plans. With the kingdom on the brink of chaos, Warwick must summon all his strength and cunning to navigate the treacherous waters of power and secure his legacy. Will he emerge victorious or succumb to the forces working against him?

Genres: Historical fiction, Political drama, Romance

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Betrayal, Ambition
