Synopsis: 
In "Buffalo Bill's Boy Bugler; Or, The Last of the Indian Ring," young Tom Tracy finds himself caught in a thrilling adventure as he joins Buffalo Bill's Wild West Show. As he travels across the American frontier, Tom encounters dangerous outlaws, treacherous landscapes, and a mysterious Indian ring that threatens the safety of the show and its performers. With his courage and resourcefulness, Tom must navigate through a series of perilous situations, all while uncovering the secrets behind the Indian ring. Will he be able to save the show and its people from the clutches of the ring? This action-packed tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of Buffalo Bill's Boy Bugler.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Historical Fiction
Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
