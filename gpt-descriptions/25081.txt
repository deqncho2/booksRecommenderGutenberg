Synopsis: 
In 'The Cricket' by Marjorie Benton Cooke, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught between two worlds - the bustling city of New York and the tranquil countryside of Vermont. When she inherits a dilapidated farmhouse, Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery, encountering unexpected friendships, romantic entanglements, and a mysterious cricket that seems to hold the key to her future. As Mary navigates the complexities of love, family, and personal growth, she must ultimately decide where her heart truly belongs.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Love, Finding one's place in the world
