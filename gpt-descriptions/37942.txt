Synopsis: In 'A Tale of the Kloster: A Romance of the German Mystics of the Cocalico', Jabez, Brother takes us on a captivating journey to the mystical world of the Cocalico Valley in Germany. Set in the 18th century, the story follows the lives of a group of German mystics who reside in a secluded monastery. As the plot unfolds, secrets are revealed, forbidden love blossoms, and the characters are faced with profound choices that will test their faith and loyalty. With its rich historical backdrop and intriguing characters, this novel promises to transport readers to a world of mystery, passion, and spiritual awakening.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Forbidden love, Secrets and revelations
