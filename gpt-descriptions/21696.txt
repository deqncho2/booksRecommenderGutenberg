Synopsis:
In 'Red Rooney: The Last of the Crew' by R. M. Ballantyne, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure set in the world of pirates and treasure hunting. The story follows the courageous and resourceful Red Rooney, a young sailor who finds himself caught up in a dangerous quest for hidden riches. As he navigates treacherous waters, encounters formidable foes, and uncovers long-lost secrets, Red Rooney must rely on his wit and bravery to survive. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Survival, Treasure Hunting
