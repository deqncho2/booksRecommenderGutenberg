Synopsis: In 'Countdown' by Julian F. Grow, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Amelia Brooks discovers a mysterious countdown timer embedded in her own body. With each passing day, the timer ticks closer to zero, leaving her with a sense of impending doom. As she races against time to unravel the origins and purpose of the countdown, Amelia finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, she must confront her own past and make unimaginable choices to save herself and those she loves.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Time, Identity, Sacrifice, Redemption
