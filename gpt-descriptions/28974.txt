Synopsis: 
In 'The Manor House School' by Angela Brazil, a young girl named Marjorie finds herself thrust into a new world when she is sent to a prestigious boarding school. As she navigates the challenges of making friends, facing strict rules, and discovering hidden secrets, Marjorie's determination and resilience are put to the test. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and the power of friendship.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, School Story

Themes: 
Friendship, Independence, Resilience, Self-Discovery
