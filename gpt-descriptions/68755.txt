Synopsis: In 'Your Pay Envelope' by John Richard Meader, a young factory worker named Joe finds himself caught in the midst of a labor dispute that threatens to upend his life. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, Joe must navigate the complexities of power, justice, and the pursuit of a better future. With unexpected twists and a compelling cast of characters, this gripping tale explores the struggles and sacrifices of the working class in a changing world.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes: Labor rights, Social inequality, Personal growth
