Synopsis: 

In "Sahara" by Angus Buchanan, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a thrilling expedition deep into the heart of the Sahara Desert. Driven by a mysterious artifact and fueled by the desire to uncover ancient secrets, he finds himself entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. As he delves deeper into the unforgiving desert, he must confront not only the harsh elements but also his own past, leading to a breathtaking climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Redemption
