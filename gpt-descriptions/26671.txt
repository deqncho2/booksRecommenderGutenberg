Synopsis: In 'The Boy Crusaders: A Story of the Days of Louis IX,' young brothers, Hugh and Roger, embark on a thrilling adventure during the time of King Louis IX's reign. As the brothers join the crusade to the Holy Land, they encounter perilous challenges, unexpected friendships, and discover the true meaning of bravery and loyalty. Set against the backdrop of medieval Europe, this captivating tale follows the boys' journey as they navigate the complexities of war, faith, and the pursuit of their noble cause.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Faith, Honor
