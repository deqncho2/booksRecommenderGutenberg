Synopsis: 
In 'Romanesque Art in Southern Manche: Album', Marie Lebert takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the stunning Romanesque art found in the southern region of Manche. Through a collection of beautifully curated photographs, Lebert explores the intricate details and rich symbolism of the region's churches, sculptures, and architectural marvels. From the awe-inspiring stained glass windows to the meticulously carved stone reliefs, this album offers a glimpse into the artistic mastery of the Romanesque period. With insightful commentary accompanying each image, readers will gain a deeper understanding of the historical and cultural significance of these remarkable works of art.

Genres: Art, History, Photography

Themes: Romanesque architecture, Religious symbolism, Cultural heritage
