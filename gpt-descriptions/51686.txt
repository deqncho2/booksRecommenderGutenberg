Synopsis: 
In 'Marjorie Dean, Post-Graduate' by Josephine Chase, Marjorie Dean embarks on a new chapter of her life after graduating from college. As she navigates the challenges of adulthood, Marjorie finds herself facing unexpected opportunities, personal growth, and the complexities of love. With her determination and resilience, she strives to make a difference in the world while staying true to herself.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Romance, Drama

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Ambition, Love and Relationships
