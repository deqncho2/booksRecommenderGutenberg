Synopsis: In 'Young Folks' Bible in Words of Easy Reading: The Sweet Stories of God's Word in the Language of Childhood', Josephine Pollard presents a collection of captivating and accessible retellings of the Bible's most beloved stories. From the creation of the world to the adventures of Moses and the miracles of Jesus, this book brings the timeless tales of God's Word to life in a way that young readers can easily understand and enjoy. With its engaging storytelling and vibrant illustrations, 'Young Folks' Bible' is sure to captivate the hearts and minds of children, fostering a love for the Bible and its teachings.

Genres: Children's Literature, Religious Literature

Themes: Faith, Morality, Biblical Stories
