Synopsis: In 'Dotty Dimple At Home' by Sophie May, readers are introduced to the delightful and mischievous Dotty Dimple as she navigates the ups and downs of life at home. From unexpected visitors to secret adventures, Dotty's lively spirit and curious nature lead her on a series of charming escapades that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Family, Friendship, Imagination, Curiosity
