Synopsis: In 'My Escape from Germany', Keith, Eric A. takes readers on a thrilling journey through his harrowing escape from Nazi Germany during World War II. Faced with danger at every turn, Keith's gripping account of survival and resilience will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters unexpected allies. This captivating memoir offers a unique perspective on the war and sheds light on the indomitable human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, War

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Courage, Human Spirit
