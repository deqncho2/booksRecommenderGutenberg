Synopsis: Othello, the Moor of Venice, is a gripping tragedy that explores the destructive power of jealousy and manipulation. Set in 16th-century Venice, the play follows the noble and respected Othello, a Moorish general, as he navigates the treacherous world of politics, love, and betrayal. When his ensign, Iago, harboring a deep-seated resentment, sets out to destroy Othello's life, the consequences are devastating. As the web of deceit tightens, friendships are tested, love is corrupted, and lives are shattered. Will Othello succumb to the poison of jealousy, or will he uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Jealousy, Manipulation, Betrayal, Racism, Love, Power
