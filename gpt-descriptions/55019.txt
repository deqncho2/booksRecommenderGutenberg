Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Fifteenth, or, the Yorkshire East Riding, Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment. From its origins in 1685 to its remarkable achievements up until 1848, Cannon delves into the regiment's rich history, shedding light on its triumphs, challenges, and the brave individuals who served within its ranks. This meticulously researched account offers a fascinating glimpse into the regiment's role in various conflicts and campaigns, providing a comprehensive understanding of its impact on the Yorkshire East Riding region and beyond.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military service, Regimental history, Regional impact
