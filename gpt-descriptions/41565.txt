Synopsis: In 'Consignment' by Alan Edward Nourse, a young doctor finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a secret organization that deals in a highly unusual and forbidden commodity. As he delves deeper into this underground world, he must navigate treacherous alliances, face moral dilemmas, and confront the consequences of his own actions. With each step, the doctor's life becomes increasingly entangled in a thrilling and unpredictable journey that will test his courage and challenge his beliefs.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Morality, Ethics, Consequences of Actions
