Synopsis: In 'The Corner House Girls Solve a Mystery: What It Was, Where It Was, and Who Found It' by Grace Brooks Hill, a group of adventurous young girls stumble upon a perplexing mystery that takes them on a thrilling journey. As they unravel clues and follow leads, they find themselves caught up in a web of secrets and unexpected twists. With their determination and resourcefulness, the Corner House Girls are determined to solve the mystery and bring justice to those involved. Will they be able to uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Problem-solving
