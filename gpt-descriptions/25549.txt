Synopsis: 
'A Chosen Few: Short Stories' by Frank R. Stockton is a captivating collection of tales that will transport readers to different worlds and unexpected situations. From a mysterious island inhabited by peculiar creatures to a man who discovers a hidden talent for time travel, each story presents a unique and thought-provoking narrative. As the characters navigate through extraordinary circumstances, they are faced with moral dilemmas, unexpected twists, and the consequences of their choices. With each turn of the page, readers will be drawn deeper into the imaginative and unpredictable worlds created by Stockton.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: 
Moral dilemmas, Consequences of choices, Unexpected twists
