Synopsis: 
"Short Sixes: Stories to be Read While the Candle Burns" is a collection of captivating short stories that will keep readers enthralled until the very last page. From tales of love and betrayal to stories of mystery and adventure, each narrative is carefully crafted to transport readers to different worlds and evoke a range of emotions. With unexpected twists and turns, these stories are perfect for those seeking a quick escape into the realm of imagination.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Mystery and Intrigue, Human Nature
