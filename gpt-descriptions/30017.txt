Synopsis: In 'My Father's Dragon', a young boy named Elmer Elevator embarks on a daring adventure to rescue a baby dragon held captive on a mysterious island. With his wit, courage, and a few unexpected allies, Elmer must navigate treacherous landscapes and outsmart cunning creatures to fulfill his mission. This enchanting tale of bravery and friendship will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Imagination
