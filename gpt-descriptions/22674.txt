Synopsis:
In "Boy Scouts on Hudson Bay; Or, The Disappearing Fleet" by G. Harvey Ralphson, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts embark on a thrilling journey to Hudson Bay. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter unexpected challenges, they find themselves caught up in a mysterious disappearance that threatens their very survival. With their resourcefulness and determination, the Boy Scouts must unravel the secrets of the disappearing fleet and overcome the dangers that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Teamwork, Survival
