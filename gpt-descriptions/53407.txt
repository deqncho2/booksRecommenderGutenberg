Synopsis: In 'Dave Porter's Return to School; Or, Winning the Medal of Honor' by Edward Stratemeyer, we follow the thrilling journey of Dave Porter as he returns to school after a summer filled with adventures. However, little does he know that his return will be anything but ordinary. As Dave faces new challenges, unexpected friendships, and a mysterious secret, he must summon all his courage and determination to overcome the obstacles that lie ahead. With each twist and turn, Dave's character is tested, and he must prove himself worthy of the prestigious Medal of Honor. Will Dave be able to rise to the occasion and emerge victorious?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Perseverance
