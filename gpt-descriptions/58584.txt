Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Frédéric Bastiat' is a comprehensive collection of the works of renowned French economist and writer, Frédéric Bastiat. This compilation includes a wide range of his influential writings, covering topics such as economics, politics, and philosophy. From his famous treatise on the concept of "The Law" to his insightful essays on free trade and protectionism, Bastiat's works provide thought-provoking perspectives on the role of government, individual liberty, and the principles of a free society. This index serves as a valuable resource for anyone interested in exploring the intellectual legacy of one of the most influential thinkers of the 19th century.

Genres: Economics, Political Philosophy, Essays

Themes: Free trade, Individual liberty, Role of government
