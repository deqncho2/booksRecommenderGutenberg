Synopsis: In 'Electricity for the 4-H Scientist', Wilson, Eric B. takes young readers on an electrifying journey through the fascinating world of electricity. Through engaging experiments and hands-on activities, this book introduces readers to the wonders of electrical circuits, magnetism, and the power of electricity. From building their own simple circuits to understanding the principles behind electric motors, readers will be captivated by the endless possibilities that electricity offers. With clear explanations and step-by-step instructions, this book is a perfect guide for young scientists eager to explore the mysteries of electricity.

Genres: Science, Education, Children's

Themes: Electricity, STEM education, Hands-on learning
