Synopsis: 
'Extracts from the Diary of William Bray' is a captivating collection of diary entries that offers a glimpse into the life and mind of William Bray, a mysterious and enigmatic individual. As the reader delves into the pages of Bray's diary, they are drawn into a world filled with secrets, intrigue, and unexpected twists. Each entry unveils a new layer of Bray's complex personality, leaving the reader eager to uncover the truth behind his enigmatic existence.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Secrets, Psychological Exploration
