Synopsis: 

In 'Mental Philosophy: Including the Intellect, Sensibilities, and Will', Joseph Haven delves into the intricate workings of the human mind, exploring the depths of the intellect, sensibilities, and will. Through a captivating blend of philosophical inquiry and psychological analysis, Haven takes readers on a thought-provoking journey, unraveling the mysteries of human consciousness and the complexities of our inner world. From the nature of perception to the intricacies of decision-making, this book offers a profound exploration of the human mind and its profound impact on our lives.

Genres: Philosophy, Psychology, Self-help

Themes: Consciousness, Perception, Decision-making, Self-awareness
