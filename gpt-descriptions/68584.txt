Synopsis: 
In "Lost in the Backwoods" by Edith C. Kenyon, a young woman named Emily finds herself stranded in the vast and treacherous wilderness after a tragic accident separates her from her family. With only her wits and determination to rely on, she must navigate through the unforgiving terrain, facing unexpected dangers and encountering mysterious characters along the way. As Emily fights for survival, she discovers inner strength she never knew she possessed and learns profound lessons about resilience, self-discovery, and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Resilience, Self-discovery, Survival, Nature
