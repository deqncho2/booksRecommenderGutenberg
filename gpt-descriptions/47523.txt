Synopsis: In "The Bishop's Apron: A study in the origins of a great family," W. Somerset Maugham takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the Marsham family. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows the ambitious and enigmatic Oliver Marsham as he navigates the complexities of love, family secrets, and societal expectations. As the layers of the Marsham family history are gradually peeled back, readers are drawn into a web of intrigue and scandal that spans generations. With Maugham's signature wit and keen observations, this novel explores the choices we make and the consequences they have on our lives and those around us.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Family saga

Themes: Ambition, Love and relationships, Family secrets, Social expectations
