Synopsis: In 'A Study of Recent Earthquakes', Davison, Charles takes readers on a thrilling journey through the fascinating world of seismic activity. From the bustling cities to the remote corners of the globe, this gripping account delves into the science behind earthquakes, exploring their causes, effects, and the potential for future devastation. As the author uncovers shocking discoveries and shares personal experiences, readers will be captivated by the raw power of these natural disasters and the profound impact they have on our planet and its inhabitants.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Natural Disasters

Themes: Earthquakes, Geology, Environmental Science, Human Resilience
