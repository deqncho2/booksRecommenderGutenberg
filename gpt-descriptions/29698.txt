Synopsis: In 'Lighter Than You Think' by Bond, Nelson S., a brilliant scientist discovers a groundbreaking invention that has the power to change the world. However, as the technology falls into the wrong hands, it becomes a dangerous weapon that threatens humanity's existence. With time running out, a group of unlikely heroes must come together to stop the impending catastrophe and protect the future of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Action

Themes: Technology, Power, Responsibility
