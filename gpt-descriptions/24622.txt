Synopsis: 
In 'The Pianolist: A Guide for Pianola Players', Gustav Kobbé takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of the pianola, an instrument that brings music to life with the touch of a button. Through vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, Kobbé explores the history, mechanics, and artistry of this remarkable invention. From the early days of player pianos to the intricacies of modern reproducing pianos, he delves into the technical aspects while also sharing the stories of renowned pianola virtuosos. With a blend of technical expertise and passion for music, Kobbé's guide offers a fascinating exploration of the pianola's role in shaping the musical landscape.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, History

Themes: Music appreciation, Technological advancements, Artistry
