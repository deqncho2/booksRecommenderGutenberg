Synopsis: 
'From Pekin to Calais by Land' takes readers on an extraordinary journey across the vast landscapes of Asia and Europe. Join the intrepid adventurer, Harry De Windt, as he embarks on a daring expedition, facing treacherous terrains, encounters with fascinating cultures, and unexpected twists and turns along the way. From the bustling streets of Pekin to the historic city of Calais, this gripping travelogue will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Perseverance
