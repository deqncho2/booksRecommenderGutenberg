Synopsis: In 'Worrying Won't Win' by Glass, Montague, a young woman named Lily finds herself trapped in a cycle of anxiety and self-doubt. As she navigates the challenges of her personal and professional life, she discovers a mysterious book that holds the key to overcoming her worries. With the help of unexpected allies, Lily embarks on a transformative journey that will test her resilience and teach her the power of embracing uncertainty.

Genres: Fiction, Self-help, Psychological

Themes: Anxiety, Self-discovery, Resilience, Transformation
