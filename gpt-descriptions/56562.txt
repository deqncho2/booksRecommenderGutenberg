Synopsis: 
In 'Kidnapped (Illustrated): Being Memoirs of the Adventures of David Balfour in the Year 1751', young David Balfour's life takes an unexpected turn when he is kidnapped and thrown into a world of danger and intrigue. Set in 18th-century Scotland, this thrilling tale follows David's quest for justice and his fight for survival as he navigates treacherous landscapes, encounters mysterious characters, and uncovers shocking secrets. With stunning illustrations that bring the story to life, this gripping adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Justice, Survival
