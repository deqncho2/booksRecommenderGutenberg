Synopsis: In "The Steel Flea," a talented craftsman is challenged to create a miniature mechanical flea that can perform incredible feats. As the story unfolds, the flea becomes the center of attention, captivating both the high society and the common people. However, the true power of the steel flea lies in its ability to reveal the true nature of those who possess it. With humor and wit, Leskov explores the impact of this tiny creation on the lives of those who encounter it.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Historical

Themes: Artistry and craftsmanship, societal obsession, human nature
