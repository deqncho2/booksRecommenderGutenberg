Synopsis: In "The Reckoning: A Play in One Act" by Percival Wilde, a group of strangers find themselves trapped in a mysterious room, forced to confront their past actions and face the consequences of their choices. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the characters must grapple with guilt, redemption, and the true nature of justice. With each passing moment, the stakes become higher, leading to a gripping climax that will leave the audience questioning their own moral compass.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Guilt, Redemption, Justice, Morality
