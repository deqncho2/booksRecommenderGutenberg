Synopsis: In 'Clutch of Morpheus' by Larry Sternig, a renowned sleep researcher finds himself caught in a web of intrigue when he stumbles upon a mysterious phenomenon that blurs the line between dreams and reality. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game where the boundaries of the mind are tested, and the consequences of his discoveries could be more than he ever imagined.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Science fiction, Mystery

Themes: Perception vs. reality, the power of dreams, the fragility of the human mind
