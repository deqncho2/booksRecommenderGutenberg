Synopsis: In the small village of Granpere, a young woman named Marie falls in love with a wealthy Englishman, Harry. However, their relationship is threatened by the disapproval of Marie's father and the interference of a cunning rival suitor. As Marie and Harry navigate the challenges of their forbidden love, they must confront the complexities of loyalty, societal expectations, and the power of true love.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Social class and prejudice, Family dynamics
