Synopsis: Pinnock's improved edition of Dr. Goldsmith's History of Rome, written by Oliver Goldsmith and edited by Wm. C. Taylor, is a comprehensive and enhanced version of the original historical work. This edition includes an introduction to the study of Roman history, providing readers with valuable information on the manners, institutions, and antiquities of the Romans. Throughout the book, numerous biographical and historical notes are added, enriching the narrative and offering a deeper understanding of the events and characters. Additionally, each section concludes with examination questions, encouraging readers to engage with the material and test their knowledge. 

Genres: History, Education, Reference

Themes: Roman history, Manners and institutions, Antiquities, Biographical exploration, Historical analysis, Education and examination.
