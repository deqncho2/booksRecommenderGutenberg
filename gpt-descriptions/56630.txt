Synopsis:
In 'Rome', Fowler, W. Warde takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient city that was once the heart of the Roman Empire. From the grandeur of the Colosseum to the intricate politics of the Senate, this book delves into the rich history and vibrant culture of Rome. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, the author brings to life the fascinating characters and events that shaped this iconic city. From the rise and fall of emperors to the daily lives of ordinary citizens, 'Rome' offers a captivating exploration of one of the greatest civilizations in history.

Genres:
Historical Non-fiction, Travelogue, Biography

Themes:
Power and Politics, Cultural Identity, Architecture and Engineering, Daily Life in Ancient Rome
