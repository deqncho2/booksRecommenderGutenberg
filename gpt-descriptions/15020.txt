Synopsis: 

Step back in time to the picturesque countryside of Normandy in the late 19th century, as 'The Brochure Series of Architectural Illustration, Vol. 01, No. 11, November, 1895: The Country Houses of Normandy' takes you on a visual journey through the charming and elegant country houses that dot the landscape. Immerse yourself in the intricate architectural details, lush gardens, and idyllic settings that make these homes truly enchanting. From quaint cottages to grand manors, this volume showcases the diverse range of architectural styles and captures the essence of the Normandy countryside. 

Genres: Architecture, Art, Travelogue

Themes: Historical preservation, Cultural heritage, Architectural diversity
