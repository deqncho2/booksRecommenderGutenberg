Synopsis: In 'My First Battle: A Sergeant's Story' by Mickiewicz, Adam, readers are taken on a gripping journey through the eyes of a young sergeant as he experiences the chaos and brutality of war for the first time. From the adrenaline-fueled battles to the heart-wrenching moments of loss and camaraderie, this poignant narrative explores the harrowing realities of combat and the indomitable spirit of those who fight.

Genres: War fiction, Historical fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Innocence, Survival
