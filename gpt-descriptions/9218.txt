Synopsis: In "Footprints on the Sea-Shore," Hawthorne weaves a mysterious tale of a young man who discovers a set of footprints leading from the sea to his home, but no sign of the person who made them. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a haunting secret that will challenge his perception of reality and the boundaries of the human experience.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Perception, Supernatural, Isolation
