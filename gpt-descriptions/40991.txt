Synopsis: In 'The Butterfly Kiss' by Arthur Dekker Savage, a mysterious and enchanting woman enters the life of a struggling artist, igniting a passionate and dangerous affair that will challenge his perception of reality. As their relationship intensifies, the artist becomes entangled in a web of secrets and deception, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and forbidden desires. With each encounter, the boundaries between love and obsession blur, leaving the reader captivated by the unpredictable twists and turns of this seductive tale.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Love and obsession, Identity and self-discovery, Deception and secrets
