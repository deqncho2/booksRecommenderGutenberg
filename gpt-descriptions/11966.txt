Synopsis: In 'The Village Sunday School, with brief sketches of three of its scholars' by John C. Symons, readers are introduced to a quaint village and its Sunday school, where three young scholars stand out amidst their peers. The first scholar, Emily, is a bright and curious girl who possesses a deep love for learning. The second scholar, Thomas, is a mischievous boy with a knack for storytelling and a hidden vulnerability. Lastly, there is Sarah, a shy and reserved girl who harbors a secret talent that could change her life. As the story unfolds, these three scholars navigate the challenges of their village and the Sunday school, each facing their own unique trials and triumphs. 

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Inspirational

Themes: Education, Friendship, Self-discovery
