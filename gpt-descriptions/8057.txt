Synopsis: In the Book of Philemon, a heartfelt letter is written by the apostle Paul to a man named Philemon, urging him to forgive and accept his runaway slave, Onesimus. As Paul navigates the delicate balance between justice and mercy, the letter explores themes of redemption, forgiveness, and the transformative power of love.

Genres: Epistle, Religious Literature

Themes: Redemption, Forgiveness, Love
