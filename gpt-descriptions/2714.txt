Synopsis: In 'Long Live the King!' by Mary Roberts Rinehart, a mysterious murder takes place in a small European kingdom, plunging the royal family into chaos. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of power is exposed. With twists and turns at every corner, this thrilling mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Power, Loyalty, Deception
