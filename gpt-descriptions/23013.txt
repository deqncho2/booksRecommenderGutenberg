Synopsis: In "George Washington's" Last Duel: 1891, Thomas Nelson Page takes readers on a thrilling journey through the post-Civil War South, where a young lawyer finds himself entangled in a deadly duel that echoes the legacy of America's first president. As the tension rises and secrets are unveiled, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous web of honor, loyalty, and justice, ultimately questioning the true meaning of heroism and the cost of preserving one's reputation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Honor, Legacy, Justice, Identity
