Synopsis: In 'The Queen's Advocate' by Arthur W. Marchmont, a young lawyer finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he is appointed as the advocate for the Queen. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to shake the very foundations of the monarchy. With his reputation and life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth and protect the Queen. Will he succeed or become a pawn in a dangerous game?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal
