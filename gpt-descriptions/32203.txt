Synopsis: In 'The Land of Long Ago' by Eliza Calvert Hall, a young girl named Mary embarks on a magical journey to a hidden world filled with enchantment and wonder. As she explores this extraordinary realm, she encounters mythical creatures, uncovers ancient secrets, and discovers her own inner strength. With each step she takes, Mary's perception of reality is challenged, leading her to question the boundaries between imagination and truth. This captivating tale will transport readers to a realm where dreams come alive and the power of belief knows no bounds.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Imagination, Self-discovery, Belief
