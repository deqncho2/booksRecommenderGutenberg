Synopsis: In the dark and mysterious streets of Victorian London, a secret society known as The Oversight safeguards the balance between the natural and supernatural worlds. Led by a group of enigmatic individuals with extraordinary abilities, they protect humanity from the dangerous and malevolent forces that lurk in the shadows. But when a series of gruesome murders threatens to unravel the delicate equilibrium, The Oversight must delve into the depths of their own secrets and confront a powerful enemy that could bring about the end of their order.

Genres: Fantasy, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Power and Responsibility, Secrets and Betrayal
