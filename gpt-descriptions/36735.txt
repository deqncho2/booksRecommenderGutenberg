Synopsis: In this captivating volume of the Encyclopaedia Britannica, the reader is taken on a journey through the Evangelical Church Conference to the life and contributions of Sir William Fairbairn. From the fervent discussions and debates of the conference to the remarkable achievements of Fairbairn, this volume offers a fascinating exploration of religious movements and the remarkable individuals who shaped them.

Genres: Historical, Biographical, Religious

Themes: Religious movements, Church conferences, Individual contributions
