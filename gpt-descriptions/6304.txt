Synopsis: 
'Without Prejudice' by Zangwill, Israel is a gripping novel that follows the life of a young lawyer named Leonard Outram. When Outram is accused of a crime he did not commit, he finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden motives. As he fights to clear his name, Outram must navigate through a labyrinth of legal complexities, personal relationships, and societal prejudices. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale explores the depths of human nature and the power of justice.

Genres: Mystery, Legal Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Prejudice, Justice, Betrayal, Identity
