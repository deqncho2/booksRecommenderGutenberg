Synopsis: In 'Combat' by Reynolds, Mack, a highly skilled and enigmatic soldier finds himself thrust into a dangerous mission that will test his abilities and challenge his beliefs. As he navigates through a treacherous landscape filled with political intrigue and deadly adversaries, he must confront his own past and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of not only himself, but also those he holds dear. With pulse-pounding action and unexpected twists, 'Combat' takes readers on a thrilling journey of survival, sacrifice, and the true nature of heroism.

Genres: Action, Thriller, Military Fiction

Themes: Duty and Honor, Redemption, Moral Ambiguity
