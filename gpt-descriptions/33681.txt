Synopsis: 
'Poems of the Great War: Published on the Behalf of the Prince of Wales's National Relief Fund' is a poignant collection of poems that captures the raw emotions and experiences of soldiers during World War I. From the trenches to the battlefields, these poems offer a glimpse into the harrowing realities of war, exploring themes of sacrifice, loss, camaraderie, and the indomitable human spirit. Through powerful imagery and evocative language, this anthology pays tribute to the bravery and resilience of those who fought and suffered during one of the most devastating conflicts in history.

Genres: Poetry, Historical, War

Themes: Sacrifice, Loss, Camaraderie, Resilience
