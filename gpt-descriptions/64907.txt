Synopsis: 
In 'The Other Man' by Wallace Edgar, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is hired to investigate a seemingly ordinary case of a missing person. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a series of shocking secrets and unexpected connections that lead him down a path of deception and betrayal. With his own life at stake, the detective must navigate a treacherous world of hidden identities and sinister motives to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
