Synopsis: In 'Doing My Bit For Ireland', Margaret Skinnider takes us on a captivating journey through her experiences as a female revolutionary during the Irish War of Independence. From her daring escapades as a member of the Irish Citizen Army to her involvement in pivotal events, Skinnider's memoir offers a unique perspective on the fight for Irish independence. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt reflections, she paints a vivid picture of the sacrifices, challenges, and triumphs that shaped her role in this historic struggle.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Revolution, Patriotism, Gender Roles, Sacrifice
