Synopsis: 
In Henry IV, Part 1, Shakespeare takes us into the tumultuous world of medieval England, where political intrigue, rebellion, and honor collide. The play follows the journey of Prince Hal, the wayward son of King Henry IV, as he navigates the treacherous path to kingship. Alongside his mischievous companion, Falstaff, Hal finds himself torn between his wild youth and the weight of his royal destiny. As rebellion brews and battles loom, Hal must confront his own identity and make choices that will shape the future of the kingdom.

Genres: Historical drama, Tragedy, Comedy

Themes: Power and kingship, Honor and loyalty, Identity and self-discovery
