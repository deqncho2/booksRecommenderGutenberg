Synopsis: In 'The Cauliflower' by A. A. Crozier, a mysterious and enigmatic vegetable takes center stage, captivating the attention of a small town. As the townspeople become increasingly obsessed with the cauliflower, strange events begin to unfold, blurring the lines between reality and imagination. With its intriguing characters and unexpected twists, this novel explores the power of obsession and the thin veil between the ordinary and the extraordinary.

Genres: Mystery, Magical Realism, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Obsession, Perception, Reality vs. Illusion
