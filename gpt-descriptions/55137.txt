Synopsis: In 'The Mystery Queen' by Fergus Hume, a renowned detective is faced with a perplexing case when a wealthy woman is found dead under mysterious circumstances. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden identities that will keep readers guessing until the very end. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping mystery will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Secrets
