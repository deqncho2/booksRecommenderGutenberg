Synopsis: 

'The Chronicles of Crime or The New Newgate Calendar' is a captivating collection of memoirs and anecdotes that delves into the lives of notorious characters who have committed heinous crimes in Great Britain. From the earliest period to 1841, this book uncovers the dark underbelly of society, revealing the shocking and often unbelievable stories of individuals who have outraged the laws of the land. With each chapter, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the criminal underworld, encountering a diverse range of criminals and their nefarious deeds. From cunning thieves to cold-blooded murderers, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of those who have chosen a path of crime and the consequences they face.

Genres: True Crime, Memoir, Anecdotes

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Morality, Society and Deviance
