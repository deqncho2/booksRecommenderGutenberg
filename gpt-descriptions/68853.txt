Synopsis: In 'Pastiche and Prejudice' by Arthur Bingham Walkley, a talented young artist named Emma finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she is commissioned to restore a mysterious painting. As she delves deeper into the world of art forgery and hidden identities, Emma must navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and artistic ambition. With each brushstroke, she uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to shatter her perception of reality. Will Emma be able to untangle the threads of deceit before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Art forgery, Identity, Deception
