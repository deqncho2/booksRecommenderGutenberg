Synopsis: In 'The Bible Object Book', author C.H. Woolston presents a collection of unique object lessons that bring the stories and teachings of the Bible to life. Written in plain English and using common words, this book offers a fresh perspective on familiar biblical narratives, making them accessible and engaging for readers of all ages. From everyday objects to unexpected symbols, Woolston's object lessons provide thought-provoking insights that will captivate and inspire readers on their spiritual journey.

Genres: Religious, Educational, Inspirational

Themes: Biblical teachings, Symbolism, Spiritual enlightenment
