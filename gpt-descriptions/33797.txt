Synopsis: 
Sinister Street, vol. 1 by MacKenzie, Compton is a captivating coming-of-age novel that follows the life of Michael Fane, a young boy growing up in Edwardian England. From his early childhood in a small village to his experiences at a prestigious boarding school, Michael navigates the complexities of family, friendship, and love. As he encounters various characters and faces personal challenges, the reader is drawn into a world of secrets, desires, and the pursuit of self-discovery. With vivid descriptions and a compelling narrative, Sinister Street, vol. 1 takes readers on a journey through the formative years of a young man's life, filled with both triumphs and tribulations.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Bildungsroman

Themes: Identity, Growth, Relationships, Society, Self-discovery
