Synopsis: 
"The attitudes of animals in motion, illustrated with the zoopraxiscope" by Eadweard Muybridge takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the world of animals in motion. Through the use of the zoopraxiscope, a device that captures and displays sequential photographs, Muybridge presents a stunning collection of images showcasing the various movements and postures of animals. From galloping horses to soaring birds, the book offers a mesmerizing exploration of the beauty and grace found in the natural world.

Genres: 
Art, Photography, Natural History

Themes: 
Motion, Animal Behavior, Aesthetics
