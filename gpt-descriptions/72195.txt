Synopsis: 
In "A Woman's Wanderings and Trials during the Anglo-Boer War," Jacoba Elizabeth De la Rey takes us on a captivating journey through her experiences as a woman during the tumultuous Anglo-Boer War. From the chaos of battlefields to the struggles of survival, she provides a unique perspective on the war, shedding light on the untold stories of women who faced immense challenges and hardships. With her unwavering determination and resilience, Jacoba Elizabeth's narrative offers a gripping account of courage, sacrifice, and the indomitable spirit of women in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, War

Themes: Women's experiences, Resilience, Survival, War atrocities
