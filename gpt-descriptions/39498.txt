Synopsis: In 'Mariquita: A Novel' by Ayscough, John, we are transported to the vibrant streets of 19th-century Seville, Spain. The story revolves around the enigmatic Mariquita, a young woman with a mysterious past and an irresistible allure. As she navigates the complexities of love, betrayal, and societal expectations, Mariquita becomes entangled in a web of secrets that threaten to unravel her world. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through passion, intrigue, and the pursuit of identity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Love and Betrayal, Secrets and Deception
