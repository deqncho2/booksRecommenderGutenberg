Synopsis: 
In 'The Pyrotechnist's Treasury; Or, Complete Art of Making Fireworks', Thomas Kentish takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of pyrotechnics. From the mesmerizing colors of exploding rockets to the intricate designs of sparkling fountains, this comprehensive guide unveils the secrets behind creating breathtaking fireworks displays. With detailed instructions and illustrations, Kentish reveals the artistry and science behind each pyrotechnic masterpiece, while also delving into the history and cultural significance of fireworks. As readers delve into the pages of this treasury, they will be inspired to ignite their own creativity and explore the boundless possibilities of pyrotechnics.

Genres: Non-fiction, How-to, History

Themes: Artistry, Science, Creativity, Cultural Significance
