Synopsis: In 'Mammy Tittleback and Her Family: A True Story of Seventeen Cats', Helen Hunt Jackson takes us on a heartwarming journey into the lives of a remarkable feline family. Set in a small New England town, this charming tale follows the adventures of Mammy Tittleback, a wise and loving cat, and her seventeen adorable kittens. From their mischievous escapades to their heartwarming interactions with the townspeople, this true story will captivate readers of all ages. As the Tittleback family navigates the challenges of life, they teach us valuable lessons about love, loyalty, and the enduring bond between humans and animals.

Genres: Children's Literature, Animal Fiction, Memoir

Themes: Love, Family, Loyalty, Animal-Human Connection
