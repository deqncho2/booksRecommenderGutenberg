Synopsis: In 'Miss Theodosia's Heartstrings' by Annie Hamilton Donnell, a young woman named Theodosia finds herself torn between duty and desire when she meets a charming stranger who awakens her long-dormant passions. As she navigates the complexities of love and societal expectations, Theodosia must confront her own fears and make a choice that could change her life forever.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Desire, Duty and Responsibility, Self-Discovery
