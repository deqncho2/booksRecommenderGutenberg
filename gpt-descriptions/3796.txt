Synopsis:
In the final installment of the beloved Anne of Green Gables series, we are introduced to Rilla Blythe, the youngest daughter of Anne Shirley. Set during World War I, Rilla of Ingleside follows Rilla's journey from carefree adolescence to a young woman facing the challenges and heartbreaks of war. As her brothers and friends enlist to fight, Rilla finds herself thrust into a world of uncertainty and sacrifice. Through her experiences, she discovers strength, resilience, and the power of love in the face of adversity.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, War

Themes:
Love, Family, Patriotism, Loss, Resilience
