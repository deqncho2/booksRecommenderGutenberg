Synopsis: In "Betty at St. Benedick's: A School Story for Girls" by Ethel Talbot, readers are introduced to Betty, a spirited and determined young girl who finds herself enrolled in the prestigious St. Benedick's School for Girls. As Betty navigates the challenges of fitting in and making friends, she discovers a hidden secret within the school's walls that could change everything. With her newfound friends, Betty embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth, all while facing unexpected obstacles and learning valuable life lessons along the way.

Genres: Children's Literature, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Courage, Discovery
