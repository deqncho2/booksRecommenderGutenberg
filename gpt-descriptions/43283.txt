Synopsis: 
"The Correspondence of Madame, Princess Palatine, Mother of the Regent; of Marie-Adélaïde de Savoie, Duchesse de Bourgogne; and of Madame de Maintenon, in Relation to Saint-Cyr" is a captivating collection of letters that offers a unique glimpse into the lives of three influential women in 17th-century France. Through their correspondence, we witness the complex relationships, political intrigues, and personal struggles that shaped their lives and the establishment of the prestigious Saint-Cyr school. As their letters unfold, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and the power dynamics of the French court are laid bare. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Epistolary novel, Biography

Themes: Power and politics, Female empowerment, Education and enlightenment
