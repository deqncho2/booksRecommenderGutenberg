Synopsis: 
'Book-plates of To-day' takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of bookplates, exploring their history, design, and significance in the modern era. From intricate illustrations to personalized symbols, this book unveils the fascinating stories behind these miniature works of art. As the pages turn, readers will discover the diverse range of bookplates and the individuals who commission them, revealing a hidden world of creativity and personal expression. With stunning visuals and insightful commentary, 'Book-plates of To-day' offers a captivating exploration of this unique art form that will leave readers inspired and eager to delve deeper into the world of bookplates.

Genres: Art, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Personal expression, Creativity, Symbolism
