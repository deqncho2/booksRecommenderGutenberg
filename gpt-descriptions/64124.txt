Synopsis: 
In "Santa Claus' Sweetheart" by Imogen Clark, a young woman named Holly is unexpectedly thrown into the world of Christmas when she takes on the role of Santa Claus' personal assistant. As she navigates the chaos of the North Pole and the demands of her new job, Holly finds herself drawn to the enigmatic and charming Santa Claus. But as secrets from Santa's past begin to unravel, Holly must decide if she can trust her heart and believe in the magic of Christmas.

Genres: Romance, Holiday, Women's Fiction

Themes: Love, Trust, Redemption, Belief
