Synopsis: 
In the small town of Top Hill, Penny discovers a hidden trail that leads her to a mysterious world filled with enchantment and danger. As she embarks on a thrilling adventure, she uncovers secrets about her own family and the history of Top Hill that will change everything she thought she knew. With the help of new friends and a touch of magic, Penny must navigate through treacherous obstacles and face her fears to save her loved ones and the town she calls home.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: 
Discovery, Family, Courage, Friendship
