Synopsis: In 'Grist' by Murray Leinster, a brilliant scientist invents a machine that can manipulate time, allowing him to witness the past and potentially change the future. However, when he discovers a shocking secret from history, he must navigate the ethical implications of altering the course of events. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of time travel, he finds himself caught in a thrilling race against time to prevent a catastrophic event. With unexpected twists and a thought-provoking exploration of the consequences of tampering with history, 'Grist' will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Thriller

Themes: Ethics, Consequences of Actions, Historical Significance
