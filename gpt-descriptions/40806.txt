Synopsis: In 'Little Jeanne of France' by Madeline Brandeis, readers are transported to the enchanting world of a young French girl named Jeanne. Living in a picturesque village, Jeanne embarks on a remarkable adventure that takes her through the vibrant streets of Paris and beyond. Along the way, she encounters fascinating characters, uncovers hidden secrets, and discovers the power of friendship and courage. As Jeanne navigates the challenges and wonders of her journey, readers will be captivated by the charm and beauty of France, and the indomitable spirit of its young heroine.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Cultural Identity
