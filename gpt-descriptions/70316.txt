Synopsis: 
In "Thamyris; or, Is there a future for poetry?" we follow the journey of Thamyris, a young poet who embarks on a quest to discover the true essence and relevance of poetry in a rapidly changing world. As he encounters various characters and explores different artistic movements, Thamyris grapples with the question of whether poetry can still hold its power and significance in the face of modernity. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking encounters, this novel takes readers on a captivating exploration of the timeless art form of poetry.

Genres: Fiction, Literary Fiction, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, the role of poetry in society, the impact of modernity on traditional art forms
