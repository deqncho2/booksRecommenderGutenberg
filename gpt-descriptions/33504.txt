Synopsis: 

In 'Opticks: or, A treatise of the reflections, refractions, inflections and colours of light', Isaac Newton takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of light and its mesmerizing properties. Through a series of experiments and observations, Newton unveils the secrets behind the behavior of light, exploring its reflections, refractions, inflections, and the stunning array of colors it produces. As he delves deeper into the subject, Newton's discoveries challenge conventional wisdom and pave the way for groundbreaking scientific advancements.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Light, Physics, Scientific discovery
