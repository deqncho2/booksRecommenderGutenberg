Synopsis: In 'St. John's College, Cambridge' by Robert Forsyth Scott, a mysterious discovery within the ancient walls of the prestigious college sets off a chain of events that will challenge the very foundations of academia. As secrets unravel and hidden histories come to light, a young scholar finds himself caught in a web of intrigue, where the pursuit of knowledge becomes a dangerous game of survival. With its rich historical backdrop and a cast of enigmatic characters, this gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the hallowed halls of one of the world's most renowned educational institutions.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the pursuit of knowledge, the clash between tradition and progress
