Synopsis: 
In 'The Arctic Prairies: a Canoe-Journey of 2,000 Miles in Search of the Caribou', Ernest Thompson Seton takes readers on an extraordinary adventure through the vast and untamed Arctic wilderness. Join him as he embarks on a daring canoe journey spanning 2,000 miles, in pursuit of the elusive caribou. Seton's vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling transport readers to the remote region north of Aylemer Lake, where they will encounter breathtaking landscapes, encounter fascinating wildlife, and witness the challenges and triumphs of survival in one of the harshest environments on Earth.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Natural History

Themes: Exploration, Wildlife, Survival, Human-Nature Relationship
