Synopsis: 

'Endless Amusement' is a captivating collection of nearly 400 entertaining experiments that delve into various branches of science, including acoustics, electricity, magnetism, arithmetic, hydraulics, mechanics, chemistry, hydrostatics, optics, and more. This intriguing compilation also explores the wonders of the air-pump and reveals all the popular tricks and changes of the cards. As if that wasn't enough, the book even includes a complete system of pyrotechny, teaching the art of making fireworks. 

Genres: Science, Experiments, Entertainment

Themes: Exploration of science, Amusement and entertainment
