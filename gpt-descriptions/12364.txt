Synopsis: In 'The United States in the Light of Prophecy: Or, an Exposition of Rev. 13:11-17', Uriah Smith delves into a thought-provoking exploration of biblical prophecy and its connection to the United States. With meticulous analysis and compelling arguments, Smith uncovers a hidden narrative that sheds light on the nation's role in the grand scheme of history. As he unravels the mysteries of Revelation 13:11-17, readers will be captivated by the revelations and implications that emerge, challenging their understanding of the United States and its place in the world.

Genres: Religious, Non-fiction, Prophecy

Themes: Biblical prophecy, United States history, End times, Religious interpretation
