Synopsis:
In "Twenty Centuries of Paris," Mabell S. C. Smith takes readers on an enchanting journey through the rich history of the captivating city of Paris. From its humble beginnings as a Roman settlement to its transformation into a bustling metropolis, Smith delves into the fascinating stories, legends, and secrets that have shaped Paris over the course of twenty centuries. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, she brings to life the iconic landmarks, the vibrant culture, and the resilient spirit of the city, painting a vivid picture of Paris throughout the ages. 

Genres:
Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Cultural exploration

Themes:
History, Architecture, Art, Culture, Transformation
