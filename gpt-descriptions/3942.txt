Synopsis: 
In 'The Confession of a Child of the Century', we follow the tumultuous journey of Octave, a young man who finds himself lost in the aftermath of a devastating heartbreak. Set in 19th century France, Octave embarks on a passionate and reckless exploration of love, desire, and the complexities of human relationships. As he navigates through the bohemian world of Parisian society, Octave encounters a cast of intriguing characters who challenge his beliefs and force him to confront his own inner demons. With its raw emotions and introspective narrative, this novel delves into the depths of the human soul, exploring the transformative power of love and the pursuit of personal freedom.

Genres: 
Romantic novel, Bildungsroman, Historical fiction

Themes: 
Love, heartbreak, self-discovery, societal norms, personal freedom
