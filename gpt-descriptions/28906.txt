Synopsis: In the small, secluded town of Timber, a mysterious newcomer arrives, bringing with him a dark secret that threatens to unravel the peaceful existence of the townspeople. As the secrets of Timber Town slowly come to light, the residents find themselves caught in a web of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, the tension rises, and the true nature of the newcomer's intentions becomes increasingly unclear. Will the townspeople be able to uncover the truth before it's too late, or will Timber Town be forever changed by the arrival of this enigmatic stranger?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Small-town dynamics
