Synopsis: In 'Composition-Rhetoric', Brooks and Hubbard take readers on a captivating journey through the art of writing and persuasion. With a unique blend of practical advice and thought-provoking examples, they explore the power of language and its ability to shape our thoughts and influence others. From dissecting the techniques of great orators to unraveling the secrets of effective storytelling, this book is a treasure trove of insights that will inspire both aspiring writers and seasoned wordsmiths alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Writing, Language

Themes: Persuasion, Communication, Rhetoric
