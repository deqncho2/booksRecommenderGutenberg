Synopsis: 

In the final volume of the captivating series "Women of America: Woman: In all ages and in all countries," author John Ruse Larus delves into the remarkable stories of women who have shaped the history of America. From the pioneers who braved the untamed wilderness to the suffragettes who fought for women's rights, this volume uncovers the untold tales of courage, resilience, and determination that have defined the female experience in America. Through vivid narratives and insightful analysis, Larus paints a vivid portrait of the diverse and extraordinary women who have left an indelible mark on the nation's history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Women's Studies

Themes: Women's empowerment, Gender equality, Historical significance, Social progress
