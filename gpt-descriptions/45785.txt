Synopsis: 

In the July 1918 edition of the New York Times Current History, delve into the gripping accounts and analysis of the ongoing European War. This volume, Part 2, No. 1, takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events and critical turning points of the war. From the frontlines to the political corridors, this edition offers a comprehensive and insightful perspective on the conflict that has engulfed Europe. With vivid descriptions and firsthand reports, this edition of the New York Times Current History brings to life the intensity, sacrifices, and complexities of the European War.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War journalism, Current affairs

Themes: War and conflict, Politics and diplomacy, Global impact
