Synopsis: In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 146, April 8, 1914', readers are transported to the vibrant world of early 20th-century London through a collection of satirical cartoons, humorous articles, and witty commentary. From political figures to societal norms, this volume offers a hilarious and thought-provoking exploration of the absurdities of the time. Whether it's poking fun at the upper class or shedding light on the quirks of everyday life, 'Punch' delivers a delightful and entertaining snapshot of the era.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Periodical

Themes: Social commentary, Political satire, British society, Everyday absurdities
