Synopsis: 
In 'The Little Clay Cart', we are transported to the vibrant city of Ujjayini, where a chance encounter between a charming young man and a beautiful courtesan sets off a chain of events that will test their love and loyalty. As their paths intertwine with those of a cunning merchant, a powerful king, and a clever washerwoman, the story takes unexpected twists and turns, filled with romance, humor, and suspense. Will love conquer all, or will the characters be swept away by the whims of fate?

Genres: Drama, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Love and desire, social class and hierarchy, fate and destiny
