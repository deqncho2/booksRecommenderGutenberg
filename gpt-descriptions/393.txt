Synopsis: 
In 'The Blue Lagoon: A Romance' by H. De Vere Stacpoole, two young cousins, Richard and Emmeline, find themselves stranded on a deserted island after a shipwreck. As they navigate their new surroundings, they must learn to survive and adapt to the challenges of their isolated paradise. As they grow older, their bond deepens, and they discover the complexities of love and desire in a world untouched by society's rules. But as they explore the mysteries of the island, they also face unexpected dangers that threaten their idyllic existence. Will their love be enough to overcome the obstacles they encounter, or will their paradise be lost forever?

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love, Survival, Innocence, Nature
