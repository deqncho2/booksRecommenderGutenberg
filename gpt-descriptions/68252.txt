Synopsis: 
In "The Life Story of a Squirrel" by T. C. Bridges, embark on an extraordinary journey through the eyes of a small squirrel named Nutmeg. From the bustling city streets to the tranquil forests, Nutmeg's life takes unexpected turns as he navigates the challenges of survival, forms unlikely friendships, and discovers the true meaning of home. This heartwarming tale will captivate readers as they witness Nutmeg's resilience, courage, and unwavering determination to overcome the obstacles that come his way.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: 
Resilience, Friendship, Finding Home
