Synopsis: In the secluded world of Greycliff Academy, a prestigious boarding school for girls, secrets and mysteries abound. When new student Emily arrives, she quickly becomes entangled in a web of friendships, rivalries, and hidden agendas. As she navigates the complex social dynamics of the school, Emily discovers that Greycliff holds dark secrets that could shatter the lives of everyone involved. With danger lurking around every corner, Emily must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Betrayal, Secrets, Coming of Age
