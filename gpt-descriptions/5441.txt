Synopsis: In 'Uarda: a Romance of Ancient Egypt - Volume 03', the captivating story continues as the lives of the characters intertwine amidst the political turmoil and religious conflicts of ancient Egypt. As the young protagonist, Uarda, navigates her way through love, betrayal, and the pursuit of truth, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets that could change the course of history. With vivid descriptions of the majestic Nile, the opulent palaces, and the mystical temples, this volume takes readers on a thrilling journey through the ancient world, where passion and treachery lurk at every corner.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Political Intrigue, Ancient Egyptian Culture and Religion
