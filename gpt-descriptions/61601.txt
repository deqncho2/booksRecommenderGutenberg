Synopsis: In the gripping and shocking "Account of the Terrific and Fatal Riot at the New-York Astor Place Opera House on the Night of May 10th, 1849," the anonymous author delves into the intense rivalry between two renowned actors, Forrest and Macready, and the tragic events that unfolded as a result. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant New York theater scene, this account uncovers the deep-seated animosity and bitter quarrels that led to a horrifying riot, where an enraged mob clashed with the authorities and military. With more than fifty citizens meeting a gruesome fate, the narrative provides full and authentic particulars of this mournful and unexpected tragedy.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, True crime, Drama

Themes: Rivalry, Celebrity culture, Violence, Power dynamics
