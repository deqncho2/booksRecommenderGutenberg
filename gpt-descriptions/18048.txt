Synopsis: 

In "Football Days: Memories of the Game and of the Men behind the Ball," William H. Edwards takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of football, delving into the rich history and the remarkable individuals who have shaped the sport. From legendary players to influential coaches, Edwards paints a vivid picture of the game's evolution and the profound impact it has had on American culture. Through a series of captivating anecdotes and personal recollections, the author offers a unique perspective on the game, revealing the passion, dedication, and camaraderie that lie at its core. 

Genres: Sports, Memoir, History

Themes: Football culture, Legacy, Personal growth
