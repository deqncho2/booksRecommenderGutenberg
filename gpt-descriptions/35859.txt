Synopsis: 
In 'Aunt Jane's Nieces on the Ranch', three young girls find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure as they leave the comforts of their city life behind and head to a ranch in the Wild West. As they navigate the challenges of ranch life, they encounter mysterious strangers, uncover hidden secrets, and even find themselves caught up in a dangerous situation. With their courage and determination, the girls must rely on each other to overcome the obstacles they face and prove that they are more than capable of handling life on the ranch.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Independence, Friendship, Courage
