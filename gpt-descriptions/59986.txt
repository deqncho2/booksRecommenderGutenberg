Synopsis: In 'The Dreadnought Boys' World Cruise' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of adventurous young men embark on a thrilling journey around the globe aboard the mighty battleship, Dreadnought. As they encounter treacherous seas, mysterious islands, and unexpected dangers, the boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and camaraderie to overcome the perils that lie ahead. With each new destination, they uncover secrets and face challenges that test their mettle, leading to a grand finale that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Bravery
