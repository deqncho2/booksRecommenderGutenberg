Synopsis: In 'Oliver's Bride; A true Story' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman named Oliver finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she agrees to marry a mysterious stranger. As she delves deeper into her husband's past, Oliver uncovers a dark and haunting truth that threatens to unravel their marriage and her own sanity. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning who can be trusted and what lies beneath the surface of this seemingly idyllic relationship.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery, Love and betrayal
