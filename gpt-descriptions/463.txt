Synopsis: 
"The Red Badge of Courage: An Episode of the American Civil War" by Stephen Crane is a gripping and intense novel that follows the journey of a young soldier named Henry Fleming. Set against the backdrop of the American Civil War, the story delves into the psychological and emotional turmoil experienced by Henry as he grapples with fear, doubt, and the desire for glory on the battlefield. As Henry faces the harsh realities of war, he undergoes a profound transformation, questioning his own courage and ultimately seeking redemption. 

Genres: 
War fiction, Historical fiction, Psychological fiction

Themes: 
Courage, Fear, Identity, Heroism, Redemption
