Synopsis: 
In "The Way Back" by Sam Moskowitz, a young woman named Emma finds herself trapped in a mysterious and dangerous world after a tragic accident. As she navigates through this unfamiliar realm, she encounters a group of unlikely allies who help her uncover the truth about her past and the secrets that bind them all together. With each step closer to the truth, Emma must confront her own fears and make difficult choices that will determine not only her fate but also the fate of those she cares about.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Friendship, Overcoming Adversity
