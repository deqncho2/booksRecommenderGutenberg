Synopsis: In 'The Little Gingerbread Man' by George Haven Putnam, a seemingly ordinary gingerbread man embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him through various enchanting settings. As he encounters a host of colorful characters, both friendly and treacherous, the little gingerbread man must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to navigate the challenges that lie ahead. Will he be able to outsmart those who seek to devour him, or will he become a tasty treat before his journey is complete?

Genres: Children's Literature, Fairy Tale, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Resourcefulness, Overcoming Obstacles
