Synopsis: 
In 'Little Mitchell: The Story of a Mountain Squirrel' by Margaret Warner Morley, follow the captivating journey of a young squirrel named Mitchell as he navigates the challenges and wonders of his mountain home. From daring adventures to unexpected friendships, Mitchell's determination and resilience will captivate readers of all ages. As Mitchell encounters various creatures and explores the beauty of nature, he learns valuable lessons about courage, loyalty, and the importance of embracing one's true self.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Self-discovery
