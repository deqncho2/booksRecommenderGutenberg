Synopsis: 
In "Proceedings of a board of general officers held by order of His Excellency Gen. Washington," the fate of Major John André, adjutant general of the British Army, hangs in the balance. Set during the American Revolutionary War, this gripping account unveils the intense deliberations and moral dilemmas faced by the board as they weigh the consequences of André's actions. With high stakes and conflicting loyalties, the proceedings delve into the complexities of war, honor, and justice, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical fiction, Military drama, Legal thriller

Themes: Loyalty, Morality, Justice, Consequences of war
