Synopsis: In 'Stories of Siegfried, Told to the Children', Mary Macgregor weaves a captivating tapestry of adventure, heroism, and Norse mythology. Follow the young hero Siegfried as he embarks on a journey filled with dragons, enchanted swords, and epic battles. From his humble beginnings to his rise as a legendary figure, this collection of tales will transport readers to a world of bravery and destiny.

Genres: Fantasy, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Mythological Creatures
