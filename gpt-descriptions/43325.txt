Synopsis: In 'Her Benny: A Story of Street Life', Silas K. Hocking takes readers on a captivating journey through the gritty streets of Liverpool in the 1800s. The story revolves around Benny, a young boy who navigates the harsh realities of poverty and survival alongside his sister, Nell. As they face numerous challenges and encounter a cast of colorful characters, Benny's unwavering determination and the power of love shine through, offering hope in the midst of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: Poverty, Resilience, Love, Friendship
