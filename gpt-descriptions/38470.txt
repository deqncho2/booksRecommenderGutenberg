Synopsis: 
In "Lord John in New York," Lord John finds himself embroiled in a thrilling mystery while visiting the bustling city. When a prominent figure from his past is found dead under suspicious circumstances, Lord John is determined to uncover the truth, even if it means delving into the dangerous underworld of New York. As he navigates the treacherous streets and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Lord John must unravel a web of secrets and deceit that could have far-reaching consequences. With his reputation and life on the line, Lord John must use all his wit and resourcefulness to solve the mystery before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Betrayal, Identity, Justice
