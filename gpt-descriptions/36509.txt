Synopsis: In 'The Higher Court' by Mary Stewart Daggett, a young lawyer named Sarah finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when she takes on a high-profile murder case. As she delves deeper into the case, Sarah uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel not only her career but also her personal life. With her reputation on the line, she must navigate the treacherous waters of the legal system and confront her own demons to uncover the truth and seek justice. 

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Justice, Betrayal, Redemption
