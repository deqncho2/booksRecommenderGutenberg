Synopsis: In 'Tom Fairfield's Pluck and Luck; Or, Working to Clear His Name' by Chapman, Allen, Tom Fairfield finds himself in a precarious situation when he is wrongfully accused of a crime he did not commit. Determined to prove his innocence, Tom embarks on a thrilling journey filled with danger, unexpected allies, and surprising twists. As he works tirelessly to clear his name, Tom's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test, leading him to uncover a web of deceit and uncover the truth behind the crime. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Justice, Perseverance, Friendship
