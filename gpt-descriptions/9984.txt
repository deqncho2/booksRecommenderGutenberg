Synopsis: In Volume 2 of 'The poetical works of George MacDonald', readers will embark on a captivating journey through the imaginative and thought-provoking poetry of one of the most beloved Victorian authors. This collection showcases MacDonald's ability to weave together enchanting tales, explore the depths of human emotions, and delve into the mysteries of life and faith. From poignant reflections on love and loss to whimsical narratives that transport readers to fantastical realms, MacDonald's poetry will captivate and inspire. Whether exploring the beauty of nature or delving into the complexities of the human soul, this volume is a testament to MacDonald's profound insight and lyrical prowess.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Spiritual

Themes: Love, Loss, Nature, Faith, Imagination
