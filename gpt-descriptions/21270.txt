Synopsis: In 'Five Hundred Dollars; or, Jacob Marlowe's Secret' by Horatio Alger, Jr., a young man named Jacob Marlowe finds himself in possession of a significant sum of money. However, this windfall comes with a catch - he must keep it a secret. As Jacob navigates the challenges of concealing his newfound wealth, he encounters unexpected friendships, dangerous adversaries, and life-altering choices. Will Jacob be able to protect his secret and use the money wisely, or will the truth unravel his carefully constructed facade?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Wealth and its consequences, Identity and self-discovery, Morality and integrity
