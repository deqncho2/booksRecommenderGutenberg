Synopsis: 
'The Surprising Adventures of Bampfylde Moore Carew, King of the Beggars' is a captivating and thrilling tale that follows the extraordinary life of its eponymous protagonist. From his humble beginnings to his rise as the King of the Beggars, Bampfylde Moore Carew embarks on a series of surprising adventures that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Along the way, readers will be introduced to a fascinating dictionary of the Cant language, adding an intriguing layer to the narrative. Filled with entertaining and unexpected twists, this book is a must-read for those seeking an exhilarating journey through the life of an extraordinary man.

Genres: Adventure, Biography, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Survival, Social Hierarchy
