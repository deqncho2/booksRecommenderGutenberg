Synopsis: In 'Witch Winnie: The Story of a "King's Daughter"', follow the enchanting journey of Winnie, a young girl with a mysterious past and extraordinary powers. Raised by a wise old woman in a secluded cottage, Winnie's life takes a thrilling turn when she discovers her true identity and embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets of her lineage. With magical creatures, hidden realms, and a destiny waiting to be fulfilled, Witch Winnie is a captivating tale of self-discovery and the power of embracing one's true nature.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Magic, Destiny
