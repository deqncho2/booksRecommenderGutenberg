Synopsis: In 'Our Holidays: Their Meaning and Spirit; retold from St. Nicholas', embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of holidays as seen through the eyes of St. Nicholas. Delve into the origins and significance of various holidays, from Christmas to Easter, as St. Nicholas weaves together heartwarming tales and insightful anecdotes. Discover the hidden depths and timeless traditions that make these holidays so cherished, while uncovering the true spirit that unites us all during these special times.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Historical

Themes: Tradition, Unity, Celebration, Spirituality
