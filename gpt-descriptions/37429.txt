Synopsis: Polly and Her Friends Abroad follows the adventures of Polly and her group of friends as they embark on an exciting journey to a foreign land. From navigating unfamiliar cultures to encountering unexpected challenges, Polly and her friends find themselves in a whirlwind of thrilling escapades. As they explore new landscapes and forge new friendships, they discover the true meaning of adventure and the importance of embracing the unknown. Will Polly and her friends be able to overcome the obstacles they face and make the most of their time abroad?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Friendship, Self-discovery
