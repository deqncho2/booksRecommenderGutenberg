Synopsis: In "Navy Boys to the Rescue," a thrilling adventure unfolds as a group of courageous navy boys respond to a distress call over the wireless. As they embark on a daring mission, they face treacherous waters, unexpected obstacles, and a race against time to save lives. With their unwavering determination and camaraderie, these young heroes prove that bravery knows no bounds.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Military

Themes: Courage, Teamwork, Heroism
