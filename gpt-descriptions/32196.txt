Synopsis: In "Crécy" by Hilaire Belloc, the reader is transported to the year 1346, amidst the chaos of the Hundred Years' War. Through the eyes of a young English archer, we witness the brutal and awe-inspiring Battle of Crécy, a pivotal moment in history. As the protagonist navigates the horrors of war, the narrative delves into the complexities of loyalty, courage, and the human spirit, painting a vivid picture of the medieval battlefield.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Human spirit
