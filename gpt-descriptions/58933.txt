Synopsis: 
'Early Voyages to Terra Australis, Now Called Australia: A Collection of Documents, and Extracts from Early Manuscript Maps, Illustrative of the History of Discovery on the Coasts of That Vast Island, from the Beginning of the Sixteenth Century to the Time of Captain Cook' is a captivating historical collection that delves into the early voyages and exploration of Australia. Major Richard Henry presents a compilation of documents and extracts from manuscript maps, shedding light on the fascinating history of discovery along the coast of this vast island. From the early sixteenth century to the time of Captain Cook, readers will embark on a journey through time, uncovering the stories of courageous explorers and their encounters with the unknown.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Exploration, Maritime history

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Historical navigation, Indigenous cultures, Colonialism
