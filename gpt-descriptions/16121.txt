Synopsis: 
'Brothers of Pity and Other Tales of Beasts and Men' is a captivating collection of short stories by Juliana Horatia Ewing. Each tale takes the reader on a journey into a world where animals and humans coexist, exploring the intricate relationships and emotions that bind them together. From a courageous dog who risks everything to save his master, to a mischievous monkey who brings chaos to a household, these stories are filled with heartwarming moments, unexpected twists, and thought-provoking insights into the human condition. Through her vivid storytelling, Ewing invites readers to reflect on the complex connections between humans and animals, and the profound impact they can have on each other's lives.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Animal Fiction

Themes: Human-animal relationships, Loyalty, Compassion, Morality
