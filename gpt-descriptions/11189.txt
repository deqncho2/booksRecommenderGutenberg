Synopsis: In 'The Madness of Mr. Lister: Captains All, Book 9,' W. W. Jacobs takes readers on a thrilling journey through the mind of Mr. Lister, a seemingly ordinary man whose sanity begins to unravel. As his obsession with a mysterious object intensifies, Mr. Lister's actions become increasingly erratic, leading to a series of unexpected and dangerous encounters. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and madness blurs, leaving readers questioning what is truly happening and what is merely a product of Mr. Lister's deteriorating mental state.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Madness, Obsession, Reality vs. Illusion
