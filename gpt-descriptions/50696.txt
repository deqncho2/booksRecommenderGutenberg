Synopsis: 

In 'Railway Construction' by Mills, William Hemingway, a young engineer finds himself thrust into the challenging world of railway construction in the late 19th century. As he navigates the treacherous terrain, battles against time and nature, and faces unexpected obstacles, he must summon all his skills and determination to complete the monumental project. With danger lurking at every turn and the fate of an entire community hanging in the balance, the young engineer's journey becomes a thrilling and suspenseful race against the clock.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Perseverance, Ambition, Man vs. Nature
