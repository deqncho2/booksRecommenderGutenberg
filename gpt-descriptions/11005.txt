Synopsis: In 'O+F' by John Moncure Wetterau, a mysterious and enigmatic character named O+F arrives in a small town, captivating the residents with his charm and charisma. As the townspeople become increasingly infatuated with O+F, they begin to question their own desires and the consequences of their actions. With secrets and hidden agendas lurking beneath the surface, the arrival of O+F sets off a chain of events that will forever change the lives of those involved.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Manipulation, Morality
