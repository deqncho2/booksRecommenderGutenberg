Synopsis: 
In the small village of Dunstanborough, a mysterious stranger arrives at the local inn, captivating the attention of the townspeople. As the stranger's presence begins to unravel the secrets and desires of the villagers, a web of love, betrayal, and unexpected alliances is woven. Set against the backdrop of a quaint English countryside, 'The Affair at the Inn' takes readers on a thrilling journey of intrigue and passion, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and desire, Small-town dynamics
