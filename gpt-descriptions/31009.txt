Synopsis: 
In 'If at First You Don't...' by Brudy, John, a young artist named Alex finds himself trapped in a never-ending loop, reliving the same day over and over again. As he tries to break free from this mysterious cycle, he discovers that his actions have far-reaching consequences, not only for himself but for the people around him. With each repetition, Alex uncovers hidden truths and must confront his deepest fears in order to find a way out. This gripping tale of self-discovery and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the nature of reality and the power of second chances.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Time loops, Identity, Redemption
