Synopsis: In 'A War-Time Wooing: A Story' by Charles King, a captivating tale unfolds amidst the chaos of war. As the protagonist finds himself torn between duty and love, he embarks on a passionate journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. With danger lurking at every corner, the reader is left wondering if love can truly conquer all.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty, Conflict and Resolution
