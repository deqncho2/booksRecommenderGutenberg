Synopsis: 
In 'Around Old Bethany: A Story of the Adventures of Robert and Mary Davis' by R. L. Berry, readers are transported to the charming town of Old Bethany, where siblings Robert and Mary Davis embark on a series of thrilling and mysterious adventures. As they explore the town's hidden secrets and encounter a cast of intriguing characters, the siblings find themselves entangled in a web of danger and intrigue. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Robert and Mary must rely on their wit and courage to navigate the treacherous path ahead. Will they uncover the truth behind the town's mysteries, or will they become victims of the very secrets they seek to unravel?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family bonds, Courage, Discovery, Secrets
