Synopsis: In 'Our Little Polish Cousin', we follow the captivating journey of a young Polish girl named Jadwiga, as she navigates the rich traditions and cultural heritage of her homeland. From the bustling streets of Warsaw to the serene countryside, Jadwiga's adventures offer a glimpse into the vibrant Polish way of life, filled with colorful festivals, folk dances, and heartwarming family traditions. As she encounters new experiences and forms lasting friendships, Jadwiga's story becomes a delightful exploration of Polish customs and values, leaving readers enchanted by the beauty and resilience of this little Polish cousin.

Genres: Children's Literature, Cultural Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cultural identity, Family traditions, Friendship, Heritage preservation
