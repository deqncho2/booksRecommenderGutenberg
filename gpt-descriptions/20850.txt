Synopsis: 
In "Prince Prigio" by Andrew Lang, readers are introduced to a young prince who possesses a rather peculiar gift - he is able to see through people's disguises and uncover their true nature. However, this extraordinary ability leads him on a thrilling adventure filled with enchantment, danger, and unexpected twists. As Prince Prigio navigates through a world of magical creatures, treacherous villains, and mysterious quests, he must rely on his wit and courage to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. Will his unique talent be enough to save the day and bring about a happy ending?

Genres: Fairy tale, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Deception, Courage, Good vs. Evil
