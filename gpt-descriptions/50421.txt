Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Third, Or the King's Own Regiment of Light Dragoons," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment from 1685 to 1846. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, Cannon delves into the fascinating history of this regiment, shedding light on the trials, triumphs, and sacrifices of the soldiers who served under its banner. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this historical record offers a compelling narrative that will leave readers eager to uncover the untold stories and remarkable events that shaped the regiment's legacy.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Military service, Regimental history, Historical legacy
