Synopsis: 
Miss Mackenzie, a middle-aged spinster, unexpectedly inherits a fortune and finds herself thrust into a world of newfound wealth and social status. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, Miss Mackenzie must grapple with the choices she must make, including matters of the heart and the pursuit of happiness. Will she find true love and fulfillment, or will the pressures of society and her own insecurities hinder her journey?

Genres: 
Victorian Literature, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: 
Wealth and Social Status, Love and Relationships, Self-discovery
