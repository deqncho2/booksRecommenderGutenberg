Synopsis: In 'Our National Defense: The Patriotism of Peace', Maxwell, George Hebard takes readers on a captivating exploration of a nation's strength lying not in war, but in the pursuit of peace. Through thought-provoking anecdotes and historical analysis, the author challenges conventional notions of defense and patriotism, offering a fresh perspective on how a country can truly protect its citizens. As the narrative unfolds, readers are invited to question the status quo and consider alternative approaches to national security, ultimately inspiring a deeper understanding of the true essence of patriotism.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political Science, History

Themes: National defense, Patriotism, Peace, Alternative approaches to security
