Synopsis: In 'Cleo The Magnificent; Or, The Muse of the Real: A Novel' by Zangwill, Louis, we are introduced to Cleo, a captivating and enigmatic woman who possesses an extraordinary ability to bring art to life. As she navigates the vibrant world of bohemian London, Cleo's presence sparks a series of transformative events that blur the lines between reality and imagination. With a cast of eccentric characters and a touch of magical realism, this novel takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the power of creativity and the complexities of human connection.

Genres: Magical realism, historical fiction, literary fiction

Themes: Art and creativity, the blurred boundaries between reality and fantasy, the transformative power of love and passion
