Synopsis: 
In 'Debris: Selections from Poems' by Madge Morris Wagner, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the depths of human emotions and experiences. This collection of poems delves into the complexities of love, loss, and the fragility of life, offering a raw and introspective exploration of the human condition. From the hauntingly beautiful imagery to the poignant reflections on existence, Wagner's words will resonate deeply with readers, leaving them pondering the profound mysteries of life and the power of the written word.

Genres: Poetry, Contemporary Literature

Themes: Love, Loss, Human Emotions, Existentialism
