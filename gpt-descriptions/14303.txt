Synopsis: In 'Queed: A Novel' by Henry Sydnor Harrison, a young and ambitious newspaper editor named John Queed finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal growth. As he navigates the cutthroat world of journalism, Queed encounters a cast of colorful characters who challenge his beliefs and force him to confront his own limitations. With wit, charm, and a touch of romance, this captivating novel takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of success in the face of adversity.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Ambition, Personal Growth, Political Intrigue
