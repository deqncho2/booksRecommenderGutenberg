Synopsis: In the gripping novel 'Vivisection' by Albert Leffingwell, a renowned scientist finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and ethical dilemmas. Driven by his insatiable curiosity, he embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that pushes the boundaries of science and morality. As the consequences of his actions unfold, he must confront the dark side of his own ambition and grapple with the profound implications of his discoveries. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn deeper into a suspenseful tale that explores the fine line between progress and hubris, and the haunting consequences of playing god.

Genres: Science fiction, Thriller, Psychological drama

Themes: Ethics, Morality, Ambition, Consequences
