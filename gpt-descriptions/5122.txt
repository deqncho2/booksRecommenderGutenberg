Synopsis: In the remote mountains of Kentucky, two families find themselves entangled in a bitter feud that has spanned generations. When a young engineer from the outside world arrives, he becomes caught in the middle of this intense conflict, torn between loyalty and love. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the fate of these families hangs in the balance, and the power of the lonesome pine holds the key to their future.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Family feuds, Love and loyalty, Conflict and reconciliation
