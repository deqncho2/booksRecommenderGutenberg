Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, strange occurrences begin to unfold on the night of Hoggy Darn, an annual festival that celebrates the town's unique folklore. As the night progresses, secrets are revealed, tensions rise, and the line between reality and myth becomes blurred. With a cast of eccentric characters and a mysterious atmosphere, 'The Night of Hoggy Darn' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of a seemingly idyllic community.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Folklore, Secrets, Perception of Reality
