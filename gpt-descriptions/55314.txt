Synopsis: 

In "The English Housekeeper: Or, Manual of Domestic Management," Anne Cobbett provides young ladies with a comprehensive guide to successfully managing their own households. Filled with practical advice and instructions, this manual covers every aspect of domestic affairs, from the store-room to the kitchen, the larder to the cellar, and even the dairy. With an emphasis on the conduct of household affairs, this book equips young women with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the role of a housekeeper.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Domestic literature

Themes: Domestic management, Household affairs, Women's empowerment
