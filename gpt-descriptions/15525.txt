Synopsis: In 'Miriam's Schooling and Other Papers' by William Hale White, readers are introduced to Miriam, a young woman who embarks on a journey of self-discovery and intellectual growth. Through a series of papers, we witness Miriam's experiences as she navigates the challenges of education, societal expectations, and personal relationships. As she grapples with her own identity and aspirations, Miriam's story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers captivated by her determination and resilience.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman

Themes: Education, Identity, Self-discovery, Society, Relationships
