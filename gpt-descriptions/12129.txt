Synopsis: In 'A Mixed Proposal: The Lady of the Barge and Others, Part 9,' W.W. Jacobs weaves together a collection of captivating short stories that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. From mysterious barges to unexpected proposals, each tale is filled with suspense, humor, and unexpected twists. As the characters navigate through love, deception, and the complexities of human nature, readers will be drawn into a world where nothing is as it seems. Prepare to be enthralled by this enthralling collection of stories that will leave you wanting more.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Humor

Themes: Love, Deception, Human nature
