Synopsis: In the small village of Orcival, a shocking crime takes place that leaves the community in a state of fear and suspicion. A wealthy and respected family is brutally murdered, and all evidence points to a mysterious stranger who has recently arrived in town. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of the human psyche is explored. With twists and turns at every corner, 'The Mystery of Orcival' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind this perplexing crime.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice, Human nature
