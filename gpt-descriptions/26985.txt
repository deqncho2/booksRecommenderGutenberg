Synopsis: In the enchanting tale of 'Killykinick', a young girl named Eileen embarks on a thrilling adventure when she discovers a hidden world filled with magical creatures. As she befriends a mischievous leprechaun and encounters other fantastical beings, Eileen must navigate the challenges of this extraordinary realm while uncovering the secrets of her own past. With danger lurking at every turn, Eileen's courage and determination are put to the test as she strives to protect her newfound friends and unlock the mysteries of Killykinick.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Identity, Magic
