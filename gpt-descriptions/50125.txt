Synopsis: In "The Court of Philip IV.: Spain in Decadence," Hume takes us on a captivating journey through the opulent and scandalous court of King Philip IV of Spain. Set against the backdrop of a declining empire, this historical narrative delves into the intricate web of power, politics, and passion that defined the Spanish court during the 17th century. From the lavish festivities to the secret affairs, Hume paints a vivid picture of a society on the brink of collapse, where loyalty and betrayal intertwine in unexpected ways. As the courtiers navigate the treacherous waters of courtly life, their personal ambitions and desires clash with the fate of an entire nation. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Romance

Themes: Power and Corruption, Love and Betrayal, Decline of Empires
