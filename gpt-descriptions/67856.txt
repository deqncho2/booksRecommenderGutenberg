Synopsis: In 'Eris' by Robert W. Chambers, a young artist named Philip embarks on a journey to the mysterious island of Eris, where he encounters a captivating woman who seems to possess an otherworldly beauty. As Philip becomes entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden desires, he must navigate the blurred lines between reality and fantasy, ultimately questioning the nature of love, art, and the human soul.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Beauty and illusion, the power of art, the search for meaning and identity
