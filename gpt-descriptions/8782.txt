Synopsis: In 'The Divine Comedy by Dante, Illustrated, Hell, Volume 04', Dante continues his journey through the depths of Hell, encountering a multitude of sinners and witnessing their punishments. As he descends further into the infernal realms, he confronts the consequences of greed, violence, and fraud, all while grappling with his own moral dilemmas. With vivid illustrations bringing the haunting imagery to life, this volume delves deeper into the dark and captivating world of Dante's Hell.

Genres: Epic Poetry, Allegory, Fantasy

Themes: Sin and Punishment, Redemption, Moral Choices
