Synopsis: In 'The Slaves of Society: A Comedy in Covers' by Upward, Allen, we are introduced to a group of eccentric characters who find themselves entangled in a web of social expectations and societal norms. As they navigate through the absurdities of their upper-class lives, their true desires and passions begin to emerge, leading to unexpected consequences and hilarious situations. This witty and satirical comedy takes a sharp look at the constraints of society and the lengths people will go to break free from its grip.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Social conformity, Individuality, Hypocrisy, Freedom
