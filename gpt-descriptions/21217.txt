Synopsis: In 'The One Moss-Rose' by Philip Bennett Power, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young woman named Rose finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love. Set in the enchanting backdrop of Victorian England, Rose's journey takes her through a labyrinth of hidden desires, deceit, and unexpected alliances. As she navigates through the complexities of her own identity, Rose discovers that the power of love can both heal and destroy. With each turn of the page, readers will be drawn deeper into the intricate tapestry of this mesmerizing story.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Forbidden Love, Deception, Redemption
