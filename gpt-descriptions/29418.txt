Synopsis: In "The Man from Time" by Frank Belknap Long, a renowned scientist accidentally discovers a way to travel through time. As he embarks on a thrilling journey through different eras, he encounters ancient civilizations, futuristic societies, and even encounters his own past and future selves. However, the consequences of his time-traveling adventures become increasingly perilous, leading him to question the very fabric of reality and the nature of his own existence.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Adventure

Themes: Exploration of time and its implications, the nature of identity and self, the consequences of tampering with the fabric of reality
