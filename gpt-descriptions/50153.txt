Synopsis: 
"Wanted, a Young Lady" is a hilarious farce that takes place in one act. The story revolves around a young man who finds himself in a desperate situation and decides to place an advertisement in the newspaper, seeking a young lady to help him out. As the potential candidates arrive, chaos ensues, leading to a series of comical misunderstandings and unexpected twists. This fast-paced and witty play will keep readers eagerly turning the pages to see how the protagonist's predicament unfolds.

Genres: Farce, Comedy

Themes: Love and romance, Miscommunication and mistaken identity
