Synopsis: In 'The Secret Service, the Field, the Dungeon, and the Escape', Albert D. Richardson takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dangerous world of espionage and captivity during the American Civil War. This gripping true story follows the author's harrowing experiences as a Union spy, his capture by Confederate forces, and his daring escape from a notorious prison. Filled with suspense, intrigue, and heart-pounding action, Richardson's account offers a unique perspective on the tumultuous events of the era, showcasing the bravery and resilience of those who risked everything for their cause.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Espionage, Captivity, Courage, Survival
