Synopsis: 
'Spectra: A Book of Poetic Experiments' takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through a collection of innovative and thought-provoking poems. Arthur Davison Ficke and Witter Bynner collaborate to create a captivating exploration of language, form, and emotion. Each poem is a unique experiment, pushing the boundaries of traditional poetry and inviting readers to experience a new realm of artistic expression. From vivid imagery to unexpected wordplay, 'Spectra' challenges readers to question the limitations of language and discover the power of poetic experimentation.

Genres: Poetry, Experimental Literature

Themes: Language and its limitations, Artistic expression, Innovation
