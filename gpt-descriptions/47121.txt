Synopsis: 

Agnes Strickland's Queens of England, Vol. 1. (of 3): Abridged and Fully Illustrated takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the remarkable queens who shaped England's history. From the legendary Queen Boadicea to the influential Queen Matilda, this abridged volume delves into the trials, triumphs, and secrets of these powerful women. With vivid illustrations bringing their stories to life, readers will be enthralled by the tales of love, betrayal, and political intrigue that unfolded within the royal courts. 

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and Politics, Gender and Society, Royal Intrigue
