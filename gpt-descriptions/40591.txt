Synopsis: In "Alexander Hamilton" by Charles A. Conant, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of America's most influential founding fathers. From his humble beginnings in the Caribbean to his rise as a key figure in the American Revolution and the formation of the United States, Hamilton's story is one of ambition, resilience, and political genius. Through Conant's meticulous research and engaging narrative, readers will discover the untold details of Hamilton's personal and professional life, his complex relationships, and his enduring legacy that still shapes the nation today.

Genres: Biography, History, Politics

Themes: Ambition, Resilience, Political Genius
