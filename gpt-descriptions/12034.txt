Synopsis: 

In "Knights of Malta, 1523-1798" by Reuben Cohen, embark on a captivating journey through the tumultuous history of the Knights of Malta. From their humble beginnings as a small group of warrior monks to their rise as a powerful force in the Mediterranean, this book unveils the secrets, triumphs, and tragedies that shaped their destiny. As the Knights face relentless attacks from the Ottoman Empire and navigate treacherous political landscapes, their unwavering dedication to their mission and the preservation of their order is put to the ultimate test. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Cohen brings to life the epic tale of the Knights of Malta, a story of honor, sacrifice, and the enduring legacy of a legendary brotherhood.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Chivalry, Religious devotion, Conflict and warfare, Legacy and heritage
