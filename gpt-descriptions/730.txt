Synopsis: In the dark and gritty streets of 19th-century London, a young orphan named Oliver Twist embarks on a journey that will test his resilience and challenge the corrupt society he finds himself in. From the harsh conditions of the workhouse to the treacherous world of pickpocketing, Oliver's quest for love, belonging, and justice takes him through a series of unexpected twists and turns. Along the way, he encounters a cast of unforgettable characters, both good and evil, who shape his destiny and reveal the true nature of humanity.

Genres: Victorian Literature, Social Criticism, Bildungsroman

Themes: Poverty and Social Injustice, Morality and Redemption, Identity and Belonging
