Synopsis: 

'Lettres d'un Innocent: The Letters of Captain Dreyfus to His Wife' is a captivating collection of letters written by Alfred Dreyfus, a French army officer wrongfully accused of treason in the late 19th century. As Dreyfus endures the hardships of imprisonment on Devil's Island, he pours his heart out to his beloved wife, sharing his innermost thoughts, fears, and hopes. Through these poignant letters, we witness the strength of their love, the resilience of the human spirit, and the pursuit of justice in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Epistolary Novel

Themes: Love and devotion, Injustice and persecution, Resilience and hope
