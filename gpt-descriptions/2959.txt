Synopsis: In "The Memoirs of Jacques Casanova de Seingalt, 1725-1798. Volume 09: the False Nun," Casanova finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and deception as he encounters a captivating woman who claims to be a nun. Drawn into her world, Casanova must navigate a dangerous game of secrets and hidden identities, where nothing is as it seems. As he delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the false nun, he uncovers a shocking truth that could change his life forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Deception, Forbidden Love
