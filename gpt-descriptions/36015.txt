Synopsis: 
'Little Miss Peggy: Only a Nursery Story' by Molesworth, Mrs. is a delightful tale that follows the adventures of a young girl named Peggy. From her imaginative playtime to her encounters with various animals, Peggy's world is filled with wonder and excitement. As she navigates through the ups and downs of childhood, readers will be captivated by her innocence and charm. This heartwarming story is sure to bring a smile to the faces of both young and old alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Innocence, Childhood
