Synopsis: In "Robert Louis Stevenson: A Record, an Estimate, and a Memorial," Alexander H. Japp delves into the life and works of the renowned Scottish author, Robert Louis Stevenson. Through a captivating exploration of Stevenson's personal experiences, literary achievements, and the impact he left on the world, Japp paints a vivid portrait of this enigmatic figure. From Stevenson's adventurous travels to his struggles with illness, Japp uncovers the fascinating layers of the author's life, offering readers a deeper understanding of his genius and the enduring legacy he left behind.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Memoir

Themes: Adventure, Illness, Literary Legacy
