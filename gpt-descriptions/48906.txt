Synopsis: 

In "Schools of To-morrow," John Dewey and Evelyn Dewey take readers on a captivating journey into the future of education. Through their visionary exploration, they challenge traditional notions of schooling and present a compelling vision of what schools could become. With thought-provoking insights and practical examples, the authors delve into the importance of experiential learning, the integration of technology, and the cultivation of critical thinking skills. As they paint a vivid picture of the transformative potential of education, readers are left inspired and eager to reimagine the possibilities for schools in the years to come.

Genres: Education, Philosophy, Non-fiction

Themes: Experiential learning, Educational reform, Critical thinking, Technology integration
