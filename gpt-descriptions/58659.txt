Synopsis: In 'Resurrection Seven' by Stephen Marlowe, a renowned scientist discovers a groundbreaking technology that can bring the dead back to life. As he delves deeper into this mysterious power, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel the fabric of existence itself. With the line between life and death blurred, he must navigate a treacherous path of moral dilemmas, scientific ethics, and personal redemption. This gripping tale explores the consequences of playing god and the limits of human knowledge.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Life and death, Morality, Scientific ethics, Redemption
