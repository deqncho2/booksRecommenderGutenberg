Synopsis: In Wagner's epic opera, Tristan and Isolda, two star-crossed lovers find themselves entangled in a passionate and forbidden affair that defies societal norms. As their love intensifies, they are faced with a series of challenges and obstacles that push the boundaries of their emotions and loyalty. With its soaring melodies and dramatic intensity, this opera takes the audience on a journey of love, desire, and sacrifice that will leave them captivated until the final note.

Genres: Opera, Romantic Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Forbidden love, Passion, Fate, Betrayal, Sacrifice
