Synopsis: In "Lost on the Orinoco; or, American boys in Venezuela" by Edward Stratemeyer, readers are taken on an exhilarating adventure through the untamed wilderness of Venezuela. When a group of American boys find themselves stranded in this unfamiliar land, they must rely on their resourcefulness, courage, and friendship to navigate the treacherous Orinoco River and find their way back home. As they encounter dangerous animals, encounter hostile tribes, and face the perils of the jungle, the boys discover the true meaning of resilience and the power of unity. Will they conquer the challenges that lie ahead and make it back to safety?

Genres: Adventure, Action, Young Adult

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Exploration
