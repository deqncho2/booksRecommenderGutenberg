Synopsis: 
'Legal Lore: Curiosities of Law and Lawyers' takes readers on a captivating journey through the intriguing world of law, filled with bizarre cases, eccentric lawyers, and unexpected legal loopholes. From the peculiar story of a man who sued himself to the enigmatic disappearance of a renowned attorney, this book delves into the fascinating and often humorous side of the legal profession. With each page, readers will be enthralled by the peculiarities and complexities of the law, leaving them both entertained and enlightened.

Genres: Non-fiction, Legal, Humor

Themes: Legal curiosities, Eccentricity in law, Unusual cases
