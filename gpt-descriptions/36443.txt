Synopsis: In 'King of the Air; Or, To Morocco on an Aeroplane' by Herbert Strang, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as they follow the daring exploits of a young aviator named Rex. When Rex's father mysteriously disappears during a flight to Morocco, Rex is determined to uncover the truth and rescue his father. With the help of his loyal friends, Rex embarks on a perilous journey filled with unexpected twists and turns, facing danger at every corner. As he navigates the skies and unravels the mystery, Rex discovers secrets that will change his life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Family, Perseverance
