Synopsis: In 'My Brilliant Career' by Franklin, Miles, we follow the captivating journey of Sybylla, a headstrong and fiercely independent young woman growing up in rural Australia in the late 19th century. Faced with societal expectations and the limitations imposed on women of her time, Sybylla embarks on a quest to find her true purpose and navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and self-discovery. As she grapples with her desires and dreams, Sybylla's unconventional spirit and sharp wit lead her on a path that challenges the norms of her era, ultimately shaping her brilliant and unconventional future.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Feminist Literature

Themes: Gender roles and expectations, Individualism, Pursuit of dreams, Social constraints, Love and relationships
