Synopsis: 
'Stories to Read or Tell from Fairy Tales and Folklore' is a captivating collection of enchanting tales that will transport readers to magical realms and introduce them to extraordinary characters. From brave knights and cunning witches to talking animals and mythical creatures, each story is a delightful blend of adventure, mystery, and wonder. Whether it's a quest to break a curse, a test of true love, or a lesson in bravery, these timeless tales will captivate readers of all ages and leave them longing for more.

Genres: Fairy Tale, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Adventure, Magic, Morality, Love, Bravery
