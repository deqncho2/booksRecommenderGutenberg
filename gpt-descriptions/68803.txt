Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 123, January 16, 1915: Half a million ransom; or, Nick Carter and the needy nine,' renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a high-stakes kidnapping case. When a wealthy industrialist's daughter is abducted, the ransom demand reaches an astonishing half a million dollars. As Nick Carter races against time to rescue the young girl, he uncovers a web of deceit, desperation, and unexpected alliances. With his sharp intellect and relentless determination, Carter must navigate through a dangerous underworld to bring the kidnappers to justice and save the innocent victim.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Kidnapping, Wealth and Power, Justice
