Synopsis: In 'Planet of the Gods' by Robert Moore Williams, a group of astronauts crash-land on a mysterious planet that seems to be inhabited by ancient gods. As they explore the planet, they uncover a civilization that defies all logic and challenges their understanding of reality. With each step, they are drawn deeper into a world where the line between myth and truth becomes blurred, and the fate of humanity hangs in the balance.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Clash of civilizations, Perception of reality
