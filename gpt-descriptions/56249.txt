Synopsis: In 'The Problem of Manflight' by James Means, a brilliant inventor embarks on a daring quest to solve the age-old mystery of human flight. Filled with intrigue, suspense, and unexpected twists, this captivating story takes readers on a thrilling journey through the realms of science and imagination. As the protagonist faces numerous challenges and encounters a cast of fascinating characters, the reader is left wondering if the dream of manflight will finally become a reality.

Genres: Science fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Ambition, Innovation, Perseverance
