Synopsis: In 'Cultural Exchange' by Keith Laumer, a group of interstellar diplomats embark on a mission to establish peaceful relations with an alien civilization. However, they soon find themselves caught in a web of political intrigue, dangerous secrets, and unexpected alliances. As they navigate the complexities of this new world, they must confront their own biases and preconceptions, challenging the very notion of what it means to understand and coexist with a different culture.

Genres: Science Fiction, Political Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Intercultural Communication, Diplomacy, Prejudice, Power Dynamics
