Synopsis: 
'Songs of Labor, and Other Poems' by Morris Rosenfeld is a collection of powerful and evocative poems that delve into the struggles and triumphs of the working class. Through vivid imagery and heartfelt verses, Rosenfeld captures the essence of the labor movement, the hardships faced by workers, and the resilience of the human spirit. From the sweat-soaked factories to the bustling streets, these poems paint a poignant picture of the laborers' daily lives, their dreams, and their fight for justice. 

Genres: Poetry, Social Commentary, Historical

Themes: Labor, Social Justice, Human Resilience
