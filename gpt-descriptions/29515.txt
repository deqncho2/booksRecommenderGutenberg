Synopsis: In 'The Battle of the Bays' by Seaman, Owen, a small coastal town finds itself torn apart by a bitter rivalry between two prominent families, the Baylors and the Bayfields. As tensions escalate, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the battle for power and control reaches a boiling point. With lives hanging in the balance, the town must confront its dark past and make choices that will shape its future forever.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Family feuds, Power struggles, Small-town dynamics
