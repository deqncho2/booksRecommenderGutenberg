Synopsis: 
'One-Act Plays by Modern Authors' is a captivating collection of short plays that will transport readers into a world of diverse characters and thought-provoking situations. From the depths of human emotions to the complexities of relationships, each play offers a unique and compelling story that will leave readers wanting more. Whether it's a tale of love and loss, a humorous exploration of societal norms, or a poignant reflection on the human condition, these one-act plays by modern authors are sure to captivate and engage readers from start to finish.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Tragedy, Absurdism, Satire

Themes: Love and relationships, Identity and self-discovery, Social commentary, Human nature and existence
