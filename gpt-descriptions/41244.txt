Synopsis: 

Join author Franklin Matthews on an exhilarating journey as he takes you aboard the sixteen battleships of the United States Atlantic Fleet. Set sail from Hampton Roads to the Golden Gate, experiencing the awe-inspiring power and grandeur of these mighty warships. From the tumultuous seas to the breathtaking landscapes, witness the trials and triumphs of the fleet as they navigate through treacherous waters and face unexpected challenges. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Matthews brings to life the epic voyage of the Battle Fleet, offering a unique glimpse into the world of naval warfare and the indomitable spirit of the sailors who serve.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Military

Themes: Naval warfare, Exploration, Patriotism
