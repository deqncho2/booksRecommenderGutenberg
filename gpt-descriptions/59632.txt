Synopsis: 
In the September 26, 1882 edition of Harper's Young People, readers are transported to a captivating world filled with adventure, mystery, and heartwarming tales. This illustrated weekly publication takes young readers on a thrilling journey through various stories, including a thrilling account of a daring rescue at sea, a clever and mischievous monkey's escapades, and a heartwarming tale of friendship between a boy and his loyal dog. With vivid illustrations and engaging narratives, Harper's Young People offers a delightful reading experience that will leave readers eagerly anticipating the next edition.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Loyalty
