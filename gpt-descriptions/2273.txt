Synopsis: In 'Tom Swift and His Motor-Boat; Or, The Rivals of Lake Carlopa' by Appleton, Victor, young inventor Tom Swift finds himself in a thrilling competition on Lake Carlopa. When a rival challenges Tom to a race, he must use his ingenuity and resourcefulness to overcome obstacles and emerge victorious. As Tom navigates the treacherous waters, he encounters unexpected dangers and faces off against cunning adversaries. With his trusty motor-boat, Tom Swift embarks on an exhilarating adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Invention and innovation, Competition and rivalry
