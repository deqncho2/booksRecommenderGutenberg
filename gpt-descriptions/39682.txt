Synopsis: In "The Idiot at Home" by John Kendrick Bangs, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself in a series of hilarious and absurd situations within the comfort of his own home. From encountering eccentric visitors to navigating peculiar household mishaps, the protagonist's life takes an unexpected turn, leading to a delightful and unpredictable adventure. As the story unfolds, readers will be captivated by the humorous escapades and the protagonist's ability to navigate through the chaos with wit and charm.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Absurdity of everyday life, Unexpected adventures
