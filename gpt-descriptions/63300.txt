Synopsis: 

In 'Iamblichus' Life of Pythagoras, or Pythagoric Life', the reader is taken on a captivating journey into the life and teachings of the renowned philosopher and mathematician, Pythagoras. Through a combination of historical accounts, fragments of ethical writings, and a collection of Pythagoric sentences, Iamblichus provides a comprehensive and insightful exploration of Pythagoras' life, his disciples, and the profound wisdom that shaped their philosophical school.

Genres: Biography, Philosophy, Ancient Literature

Themes: Philosophy, Ethics, Mysticism, Mathematics, Spiritual Enlightenment
