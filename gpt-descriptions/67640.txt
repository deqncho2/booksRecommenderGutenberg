Synopsis: 
"The Tuberculosis Nurse: Her Function and Her Qualifications: A Handbook for Practical Workers in the Tuberculosis Campaign" is a comprehensive guide written by Ellen N. La Motte, providing invaluable insights into the role and expertise of tuberculosis nurses. Through a combination of personal experiences, practical advice, and medical knowledge, La Motte offers a compelling exploration of the challenges faced by these dedicated healthcare professionals. From the intricacies of patient care to the importance of public education, this handbook serves as an enlightening resource for anyone involved in the fight against tuberculosis.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Educational

Themes: Healthcare, Tuberculosis, Nursing, Public Health
