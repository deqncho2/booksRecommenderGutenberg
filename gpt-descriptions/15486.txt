Synopsis: In 'Edna's Sacrifice and Other Stories' by Frances Henshaw Baden, delve into a collection of captivating tales that explore the complexities of human emotions and the choices we make. From the heart-wrenching sacrifice of a young woman named Edna, to the unexpected consequences of a forbidden love affair, each story takes readers on a journey of self-discovery, resilience, and the power of love. With richly developed characters and thought-provoking narratives, this collection will leave readers pondering the depths of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Sacrifice, Love, Consequences, Resilience, Self-discovery
