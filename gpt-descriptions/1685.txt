Synopsis: In 'The Mystery of the Yellow Room' by Gaston Leroux, a young woman is found brutally attacked in a locked room, with no sign of the assailant. As the investigation unfolds, a brilliant detective and his loyal sidekick delve into a web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives, determined to unravel the truth behind this perplexing crime. With each twist and turn, the reader is kept on the edge of their seat, trying to piece together the puzzle before the shocking reveal.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Perception, Deduction
