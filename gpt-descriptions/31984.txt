Synopsis: 
In 'A Life Sentence: A Novel' by Sergeant, Adeline, we are introduced to Emma, a young woman who finds herself trapped in a seemingly perfect life that slowly unravels into a nightmare. As she navigates through a web of secrets, lies, and betrayal, Emma must confront her own past and make difficult choices that will determine her future. With each turn of the page, the tension builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Emma's life sentence.

Genres: Psychological thriller, suspense, mystery

Themes: Identity, deception, redemption
