Synopsis: In 'The Motor Boys Afloat; or, The Stirring Cruise of the Dartaway', the adventurous trio of the Motor Boys embark on a thrilling journey aboard their trusty motorboat, the Dartaway. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter mysterious characters, and face unexpected challenges, the boys find themselves caught up in a series of exhilarating and dangerous escapades. With their quick thinking and resourcefulness, they must rely on their wits and their beloved motorboat to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. Will the Motor Boys be able to navigate the perils of the open sea and emerge victorious from their daring adventure?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resourcefulness
