Synopsis: In 'The Betrayal' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a young man finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and treachery when he becomes the target of a mysterious organization. As he delves deeper into the dangerous world of espionage, he must navigate a complex network of double-crosses and unexpected alliances to uncover the truth. With his life hanging in the balance, he must decipher who he can trust and who is out to betray him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Trust
