Synopsis: In 'The Ivory Tower' by Henry James, a young American sculptor finds himself entangled in a web of love, ambition, and societal expectations as he navigates the vibrant art scene of 19th-century Rome. As he grapples with his own artistic vision and the complexities of relationships, he becomes a witness to the clash between old-world traditions and the emerging modern era. With a cast of intriguing characters and a backdrop of artistic fervor, this novel explores the delicate balance between passion and duty, and the sacrifices one must make to pursue their dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Artistic expression, Love and desire, Social expectations, Cultural clashes
