Synopsis: In "Black Bartlemy's Treasure" by Farnol, Jeffery, the reader is taken on a thrilling adventure set in the 17th century. The story follows the young and courageous Martin Conisby, who seeks revenge against the treacherous pirate, Black Bartlemy. As Martin embarks on a perilous journey across the high seas, he encounters dangerous foes, unexpected allies, and the allure of hidden treasure. With swashbuckling action, suspenseful twists, and a touch of romance, this tale of vengeance and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Revenge, Betrayal, Redemption, Treasure
