Synopsis: In 'An Easter Disciple: The Chronicle of Quintus, the Roman Knight', readers are transported to ancient Rome, where they follow the captivating journey of Quintus, a Roman knight, during the tumultuous time of Jesus' crucifixion and resurrection. As Quintus finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and religious fervor, he is faced with a series of moral dilemmas that challenge his loyalty, faith, and ultimately, his destiny. With vivid descriptions of Roman society, the author skillfully weaves together historical events and fictional elements, creating a gripping tale of love, sacrifice, and redemption.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Religious Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Faith and Doubt, Loyalty and Betrayal, Redemption and Forgiveness
