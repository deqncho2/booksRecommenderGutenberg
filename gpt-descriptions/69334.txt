Synopsis: 
In 'High-class cookery made easy', Mrs. Hart takes readers on a culinary journey filled with exquisite flavors and elegant dishes. With her expert guidance, even the most novice cooks can create gourmet meals that will impress even the most discerning palates. From delicate appetizers to decadent desserts, this book is a treasure trove of sophisticated recipes that are surprisingly simple to prepare. Mrs. Hart's passion for high-class cuisine shines through as she shares her secrets for achieving culinary excellence without the need for extensive training or expensive ingredients. Get ready to elevate your cooking skills and indulge in a world of refined flavors with 'High-class cookery made easy'.

Genres: Cookbook, Culinary

Themes: Gourmet cooking, Culinary expertise, Simplicity in high-class cuisine
