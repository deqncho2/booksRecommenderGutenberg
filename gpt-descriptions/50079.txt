Synopsis: In 'A Book on Vegetable Dyes', Ethel Mairet takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of natural dyes. Through her meticulous research and personal experiences, she unveils the hidden secrets of vibrant colors derived from plants, offering a wealth of knowledge and practical guidance. From the ancient art of dyeing to the modern revival of natural dyeing techniques, Mairet's book is a treasure trove of inspiration for artists, crafters, and anyone seeking a deeper connection with nature's palette.

Genres: Non-fiction, Craft, Art

Themes: Nature, Sustainability, Creativity
