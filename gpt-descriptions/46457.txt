Synopsis: In the lawless and treacherous Wild West, a mysterious gunslinger known as White Wolf emerges as a force to be reckoned with. When a ruthless gang of outlaws terrorizes a small town, White Wolf takes it upon himself to bring justice to the lawless land. As he navigates through a web of deceit, betrayal, and danger, White Wolf's true identity and past are slowly revealed, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the final showdown.

Genres: Western, Action, Mystery

Themes: Justice, Redemption, Identity
