Synopsis: In 'Mixed Pickles' by Raymond, Evelyn, a young woman named Lily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter hidden in her deceased grandmother's belongings. As she delves deeper into her family's past, she uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to unravel everything she thought she knew. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, Lily embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the hidden secrets of her family's history, leading her to question her own identity and the true meaning of love and loyalty.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and loyalty
