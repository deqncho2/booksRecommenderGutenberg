Synopsis: In 'Life and Death' by A. Dastre, a mysterious event shatters the peaceful existence of a small town, leaving its inhabitants questioning the very nature of life and death. As the community grapples with the aftermath, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the boundaries between the living and the dead blur. With a gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this thought-provoking novel explores the profound mysteries of existence and the fragile line between the two realms.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Life and Death, Identity, Secrets, Perception, Reality
