Synopsis: In 'He' by Lang, Andrew/ Pollock, Walter Herries, a young man embarks on a perilous journey to a remote land in search of a legendary figure known as "She-Who-Must-Be-Obeyed." As he delves deeper into this mysterious world, he becomes entangled in a web of love, power, and immortality. With danger lurking at every turn, he must confront his own desires and face the ultimate test of his character. This captivating tale explores the depths of human nature and the pursuit of eternal life.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Love and desire, Power and immortality, Self-discovery
