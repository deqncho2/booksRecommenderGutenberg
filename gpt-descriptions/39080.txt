Synopsis: In 'My Mother's Gold Ring: Founded on Fact', Lucius M. Sargent weaves a captivating tale that revolves around a mysterious gold ring and the secrets it holds. Set in a small town, the story follows a young woman named Emily who inherits the ring from her late mother. As Emily delves into the ring's history, she uncovers a web of family secrets, hidden treasures, and unexpected connections that span generations. With each revelation, Emily's world is turned upside down, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and redemption. This gripping novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic gold ring.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Family secrets, Inheritance, Redemption, Self-discovery
