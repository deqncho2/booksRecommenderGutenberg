Synopsis: 

"A Journal of the Expedition to Carthagena, with Notes: In Answer to a Late Pamphlet Entitled, An account of the Expedition to Carthagena" takes readers on a thrilling journey to the vibrant city of Carthagena. Through the eyes of the author, we witness the challenges, triumphs, and unexpected twists encountered during this daring expedition. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human resilience is explored. This gripping account will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the outcome of this extraordinary adventure.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Warfare, Resilience, Betrayal
