Synopsis: In "Geoffrey Strong" by Laura Elizabeth Howe Richards, a young boy named Geoffrey discovers a hidden talent that sets him apart from his peers. As he navigates the challenges of growing up, Geoffrey's unique ability becomes both a blessing and a burden, leading him on a journey of self-discovery and acceptance. With the support of his family and friends, Geoffrey learns to embrace his individuality and find his place in the world.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Fiction, Children's literature

Themes: Individuality, Self-discovery, Acceptance
