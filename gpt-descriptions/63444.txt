Synopsis: In the final volume of "The Life and Times of Queen Victoria," author Robert Wilson delves into the later years of Queen Victoria's reign, exploring the challenges she faced as a ruler and as a woman. From political turmoil to personal tragedies, this captivating biography unveils the untold stories of Victoria's life, shedding light on her resilience and determination in the face of adversity. As the Queen navigates through the complexities of her role, readers are taken on a journey that reveals the true essence of one of history's most iconic figures.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Leadership, Resilience, Gender roles, Monarchy
