Synopsis: 
Step into the world of the theater with 'The Exhibition Drama: Comprising Drama, Comedy, and Farce, Together with Dramatic and Musical Entertainments' by George M. Baker. This captivating collection takes readers on a journey through various dramatic and comedic performances, filled with unexpected twists and turns. From heart-wrenching dramas to side-splitting farces, this anthology offers a diverse range of stories that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each new play, Baker masterfully explores the complexities of human emotions, relationships, and the power of performance. Get ready to be enthralled by the magic of the stage!

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Farce, Musical Entertainment

Themes: Human emotions, Relationships, Power of performance
