Synopsis: In 'Our Little Spanish Cousin' by Mary F. Nixon-Roulet, readers are transported to the vibrant and colorful world of Spain through the eyes of a young Spanish girl named Dolores. As Dolores takes us on a journey through her daily life, we discover the rich traditions, customs, and festivities of her country. From the bustling streets of Madrid to the picturesque countryside, Dolores introduces us to her family, friends, and the enchanting beauty of Spain. Through her adventures, we learn about Spanish cuisine, flamenco dancing, bullfighting, and the importance of family and community. This delightful tale offers a captivating glimpse into the life of a young Spanish girl and the unique culture that surrounds her.

Genres: Children's Literature, Cultural Fiction

Themes: Cultural diversity, Family and community, Traditions and customs
