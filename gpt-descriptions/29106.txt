Synopsis: In 'A Bride of the Plains' by Emmuska Orczy, Baroness Orczy, a young woman finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is forced into an arranged marriage with a wealthy landowner in the American West. As she navigates the challenges of her new life on the vast plains, she discovers secrets and hidden motives that threaten to unravel her world. With danger lurking at every turn, she must summon her strength and courage to protect her newfound love and secure her own happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Duty vs. Desire, Love and Sacrifice, Secrets and Betrayal
