Synopsis: In "Master and Man" by Leo Tolstoy, a wealthy landowner and his loyal servant embark on a treacherous journey through a snowstorm, facing life-threatening challenges along the way. As they struggle to survive, their relationship evolves, revealing the true nature of their characters and the depths of human resilience. This gripping tale explores the complexities of human nature and the transformative power of selflessness.

Genres: Fiction, Novella, Adventure

Themes: Human nature, Resilience, Selflessness
