Synopsis: In "Reminiscences of a Private," Frank M. Mixson takes readers on a captivating journey through his experiences as a soldier in Company "E" 1st S. C. Vols. (Hagood's). From the chaos of battlefields to the camaraderie among fellow soldiers, Mixson's vivid recollections provide a unique perspective on the American Civil War. Through his personal anecdotes and reflections, the reader gains insight into the hardships, triumphs, and sacrifices of those who fought in this historic conflict.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, Brotherhood and camaraderie in the face of adversity, The human experience in times of conflict
