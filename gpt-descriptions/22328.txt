Synopsis: In 'Oh, You Tex!' by Raine, William MacLeod, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Texville, Texas, stirring up trouble and challenging the local law enforcement. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances. With its gripping plot and colorful characters, this thrilling Western tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final showdown.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Action

Themes: Justice, Betrayal, Redemption
