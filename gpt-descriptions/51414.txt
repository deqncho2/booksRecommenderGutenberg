Synopsis: In '...So They Baked a Cake' by Winston K. Marks, a seemingly ordinary baking competition takes an unexpected turn when the contestants discover a mysterious ingredient that has the power to change their lives forever. As the competition heats up, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and the true nature of the cake becomes a matter of life and death. With each slice, the stakes get higher, and the contestants must navigate a world where nothing is as it seems. Will they be able to handle the truth that lies beneath the frosting? 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Power
