Synopsis: In 'There Are Crimes and Crimes' by August Strindberg, a mysterious murder takes place in a small town, leaving the community in shock and suspicion. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human behavior is exposed. With a web of deceit and hidden motives, this gripping tale keeps readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of morality and justice.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Crime and punishment, Morality, Human nature
