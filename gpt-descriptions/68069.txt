Synopsis: In "The Principal Girl" by J.C. Snaith, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she takes on the role of a principal girl in a local theater production. As she delves deeper into the world of the theater, Mary uncovers secrets that could have far-reaching consequences, and she must navigate a complex web of relationships and hidden agendas to protect herself and those she cares about. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, the power of performance, love and loyalty
