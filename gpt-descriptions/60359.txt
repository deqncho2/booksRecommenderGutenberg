Synopsis: 
In 'Oregon, the Picturesque: A Book of Rambles in the Oregon Country and in the Wilds of Northern California', Thomas Dowler Murphy takes readers on an enchanting journey through the breathtaking landscapes of Oregon and Northern California. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Murphy explores the untamed beauty of the region, from towering mountains and lush forests to roaring waterfalls and serene lakes. Along the way, he encounters fascinating wildlife, encounters with Native American tribes, and uncovers the rich history and folklore of the area. This travelogue is a delightful invitation to explore the wonders of the Pacific Northwest and discover the hidden treasures of the Oregon Country and Northern California.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, Adventure

Themes: Nature exploration, Cultural encounters, History and folklore
