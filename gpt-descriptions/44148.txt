Synopsis: In 'The Confessions of a Daddy' by Butler, Ellis Parker, a seemingly ordinary father finds himself caught in a web of secrets and unexpected events that threaten to unravel his picture-perfect family life. As he navigates through the challenges of fatherhood, he is forced to confront his own past and make difficult choices that will shape the future of his loved ones. With a blend of humor, suspense, and heartfelt moments, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Family Drama

Themes: Parenthood, Identity, Secrets, Redemption
