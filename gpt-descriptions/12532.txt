Synopsis: In 'The Shades of the Wilderness: A Story of Lee's Great Stand', Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous American Civil War. Set against the backdrop of General Robert E. Lee's legendary stand, the story follows a young soldier named Harry Kenton as he navigates the horrors of war, battles with his own conscience, and forms unlikely alliances. As the war rages on, Harry finds himself caught between duty and personal beliefs, facing the ultimate test of loyalty and honor. With vivid descriptions and intense action, Altsheler paints a vivid picture of the battlefield and the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Morality, Conflict, Sacrifice
