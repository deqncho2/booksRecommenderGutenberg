Synopsis: In 'The Believing Years' by Edmund Lester Pearson, a young woman named Alice embarks on a journey of self-discovery and faith in a world filled with doubt and skepticism. As she navigates through the challenges of life, Alice encounters a mysterious group of believers who challenge her perceptions and force her to question everything she thought she knew. With each encounter, Alice's beliefs are tested, leading her on a path towards enlightenment and a deeper understanding of the power of belief.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Spiritual

Themes: Faith, Doubt, Self-discovery
