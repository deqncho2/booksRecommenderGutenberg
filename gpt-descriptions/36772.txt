Synopsis: In 'The Hearts of Men' by H. Fielding, a group of strangers find themselves stranded in a remote cabin during a snowstorm. As they wait for help to arrive, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to unexpected alliances and betrayals. With each passing hour, the true nature of the hearts of men is exposed, leaving the characters questioning their own morality and the lengths they are willing to go to survive.

Genres: Psychological thriller, suspense, mystery

Themes: Morality, survival, human nature
