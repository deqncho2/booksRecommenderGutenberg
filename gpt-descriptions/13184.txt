Synopsis: 
'Narrative and Lyric Poems (first series) for use in the Lower School' is a captivating collection of poems that will transport readers into a world of imagination and emotion. From tales of adventure and mystery to heartfelt expressions of love and loss, this anthology offers a diverse range of narratives and lyrical verses that will leave readers spellbound. Each poem is carefully crafted to engage young minds and ignite their love for poetry, making it an ideal introduction to the beauty and power of language.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: Adventure, Mystery, Love, Loss, Imagination
