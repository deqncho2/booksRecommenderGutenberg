Synopsis: 
In 'What's Bred in the Bone' by Allen Grant, a renowned artist named Frank Armour mysteriously disappears, leaving behind a cryptic message that hints at a hidden secret. As his friends and family search for answers, they uncover a web of intrigue, deceit, and long-buried family secrets that span generations. The story takes us on a thrilling journey through the art world, exploring the depths of human ambition, the power of art, and the lengths people will go to protect their legacies.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Ambition, Art and creativity, Legacy
