Synopsis: 
In 'The Westminster Alice' by Saki, the reader is transported to a whimsical world where political satire meets Lewis Carroll's beloved characters. As the story unfolds, we follow the misadventures of a young girl named Alice as she navigates the treacherous landscape of British politics. From encounters with eccentric politicians to absurd parliamentary debates, this satirical tale offers a hilarious and thought-provoking commentary on the absurdities of the political system. With wit and clever wordplay, Saki invites readers to question the logic and motives behind the actions of those in power, all while keeping them entertained with his unique blend of humor and social critique.

Genres: Satire, Political Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Political satire, Absurdity of politics, Critique of power
