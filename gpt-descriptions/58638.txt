Synopsis: In 'Rogues' Haven' by Bridges, Roy, a group of misfits find themselves drawn together in a hidden haven for outcasts. As they navigate the treacherous world of crime and deception, they must confront their own pasts and make choices that will determine their future. With unexpected alliances and thrilling twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Crime fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Loyalty
