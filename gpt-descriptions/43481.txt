Synopsis: In 'Tobacco and Alcohol: I. It Does Pay to Smoke. II. The Coming Man Will Drink Wine.', Fiske, John explores the controversial topics of tobacco and alcohol consumption. Through a thought-provoking analysis, the author challenges conventional beliefs and sheds light on the potential benefits and consequences of these vices. With compelling arguments and insightful observations, Fiske delves into the societal, economic, and personal implications of smoking and drinking, leaving readers questioning their own perceptions and assumptions.

Genres: Non-fiction, Social commentary, Cultural analysis

Themes: Vice and virtue, Societal norms, Personal choice, Health and well-being
