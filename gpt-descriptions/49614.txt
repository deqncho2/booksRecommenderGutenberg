Synopsis: In 'The Collected Works in Verse and Prose of William Butler Yeats, Vol. 7 (of 8): The Secret Rose. Rosa Alchemica. The Tables of the Law. The Adoration of the Magi. John Sherman and Dhoya', William Butler Yeats presents a collection of captivating tales that delve into the realms of mysticism, alchemy, and ancient legends. From the enigmatic search for a hidden rose to the pursuit of spiritual enlightenment, these stories transport readers to a world where secrets are unveiled and the boundaries between reality and imagination blur.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Poetry

Themes: Mysticism, Alchemy, Spirituality, Quest for Knowledge
