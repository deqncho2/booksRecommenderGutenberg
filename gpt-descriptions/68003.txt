Synopsis: In 'The Long Way' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist embarks on a perilous journey across the galaxy to uncover the truth behind a mysterious phenomenon that threatens the very fabric of space and time. As he navigates through treacherous alien worlds and encounters enigmatic beings, he must confront his own inner demons and make unimaginable sacrifices to save humanity from impending doom. This gripping science fiction adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the limits of human knowledge and the price of redemption.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Sacrifice, Redemption
