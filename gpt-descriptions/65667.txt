Synopsis: In 'The Wolf Queen; or, The Giant Hermit of the Scioto', a young woman named Rosalind finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she discovers a hidden secret about her ancestry. As she embarks on a journey to uncover the truth, she encounters a cast of intriguing characters, including a mysterious hermit and a pack of wolves. With each step, Rosalind's courage and determination are tested, leading her to confront her own inner strength and the power of her lineage. This captivating tale of adventure and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Family, Courage, Secrets
