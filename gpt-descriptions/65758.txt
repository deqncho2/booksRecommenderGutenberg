Synopsis:
In 'Jill, Vol. 2 (of 2)', the captivating sequel to the first volume, we continue to follow the spirited and independent Jill as she navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations. Set in the enchanting backdrop of 19th-century England, Jill's journey takes unexpected turns as she grapples with her own desires and the pressures of society. With secrets, scandals, and a cast of intriguing characters, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of our beloved heroine.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Desire, Society and Expectations, Independence and Self-Discovery
