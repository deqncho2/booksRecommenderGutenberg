Synopsis: 

'Foods that will win the war and how to cook them' takes readers back to the year 1918, during the height of World War I. In this captivating book, authors Alberta M. Goudiss and C. Houston Goudiss reveal a treasure trove of culinary secrets that were used to sustain and nourish soldiers on the front lines. From ingenious recipes to practical tips, this guide offers a fascinating glimpse into the power of food during times of conflict.

Genres: Historical, Cookbook, War

Themes: Food as a weapon, Resourcefulness in times of scarcity
