Synopsis: In 'A Bill of Divorcement: A Play in Three Acts', a family is torn apart when a man returns home after spending fifteen years in a mental institution. As secrets are revealed and emotions run high, the characters must confront their past and make difficult choices that will shape their future. This gripping play explores the complexities of love, loyalty, and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Drama, Family, Psychological

Themes: Mental illness, Family dynamics, Redemption, Sacrifice
