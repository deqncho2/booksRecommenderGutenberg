Synopsis: 

In 'The Canadian Girl at Work: A Book of Vocational Guidance', Marjory MacMurchy, Lady Willison takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of work, offering invaluable guidance to young Canadian women seeking their place in society. Through a series of engaging stories and practical advice, Lady Willison explores various professions and industries, shedding light on the challenges and triumphs that await these ambitious women. From the bustling offices of the city to the serene landscapes of rural Canada, this book paints a vivid picture of the diverse opportunities available to the modern Canadian girl. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Career guidance

Themes: Women's empowerment, Career exploration, Work-life balance
