Synopsis:
In 'Harold: the Last of the Saxon Kings - Volume 12', the gripping conclusion to the historical novel series, Edward Bulwer Lytton takes readers on a thrilling journey through the final days of Harold, the last Saxon king of England. As Harold faces the imminent invasion of William the Conqueror and the Battle of Hastings, he must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape the destiny of a nation. With vivid descriptions of medieval England and its political intrigues, this volume is a captivating tale of power, love, and sacrifice that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance
Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Fate, Love and Sacrifice
