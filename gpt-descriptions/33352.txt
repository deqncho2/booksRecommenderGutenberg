Synopsis: In 'Little Oskaloo; or, The White Whirlwind' by T.C. Harbaugh, a young boy named Oskaloo embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him to unexpected places and introduces him to extraordinary characters. As he navigates through a world filled with mystery and danger, Oskaloo discovers hidden powers within himself that could change everything. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Courage, Friendship
