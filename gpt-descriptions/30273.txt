Synopsis: In "Tom and Maggie Tulliver" by George Eliot, we are introduced to the lives of two siblings who navigate the challenges of growing up in a small English town. Tom, the older brother, is determined to uphold the family's reputation and secure their future, while Maggie, the spirited and imaginative younger sister, yearns for acceptance and freedom. As they face societal expectations, family conflicts, and the complexities of love, their bond is tested, leading them on separate paths that will ultimately shape their destinies. This poignant tale explores the complexities of family dynamics, societal pressures, and the pursuit of personal identity.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Family Drama

Themes: Family relationships, Identity, Social expectations, Love and acceptance
