Synopsis: In 'The Spectacle Man: A Story of the Missing Bridge' by Leonard, Mary Finley, a small town is thrown into chaos when their beloved bridge mysteriously disappears overnight. As the townspeople scramble to find answers, they encounter a peculiar man known as the Spectacle Man, who claims to have the power to see what others cannot. With his enigmatic presence and uncanny abilities, the Spectacle Man becomes the key to unraveling the mystery behind the missing bridge. As the truth unfolds, the townspeople are forced to confront their own fears and secrets, leading to unexpected revelations and a journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Community, Identity
