Synopsis: 

In 'British Butterflies' by Stewart, Alexander Morrison, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of butterflies. This beautifully illustrated book takes readers on a mesmerizing exploration of the diverse species that grace the British Isles. From the vibrant colors of the Red Admiral to the delicate patterns of the Small Tortoiseshell, each butterfly unveils its own unique story. As the author delves into the fascinating life cycles, habitats, and behaviors of these winged wonders, readers will be transported to a world of natural beauty and wonder. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Science

Themes: Biodiversity, Conservation, Natural History
