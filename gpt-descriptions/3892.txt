Synopsis: 
In 'Memoirs of the Court of St. Cloud', a gentleman in Paris unveils a captivating world of secrets and scandals through a series of secret letters to a nobleman in London. As the reader delves into the inner workings of the French court, they are introduced to a cast of intriguing characters and witness the power struggles, romances, and betrayals that shape the lives of those in high society. With each letter, the gentleman uncovers shocking revelations that will keep the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the truth behind the glamorous facade of the Court of St. Cloud.

Genres: Historical fiction, Epistolary novel, Political intrigue

Themes: Power and corruption, Love and betrayal, Secrets and scandal
