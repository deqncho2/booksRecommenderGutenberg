Synopsis: In 'The Motor Boys Over the Ocean; Or, A Marvelous Rescue in Mid-Air' by Clarence Young, the adventurous Motor Boys find themselves embarking on a daring journey across the vast ocean. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter unexpected challenges, their skills and courage are put to the ultimate test. With danger lurking at every turn, the Motor Boys must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. Will they be able to complete their mission and execute a remarkable rescue in mid-air?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Perseverance
