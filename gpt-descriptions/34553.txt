Synopsis: In 'Over the Plum Pudding' by John Kendrick Bangs, a seemingly ordinary Christmas gathering takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious guest arrives with a peculiar gift. As the night unfolds, the guests find themselves embarking on a whimsical and enchanting adventure that challenges their beliefs and perceptions. With humor and imagination, Bangs weaves a tale that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Comedy, Mystery

Themes: Magic, Perception, Christmas spirit
