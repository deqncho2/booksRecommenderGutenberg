Synopsis: In 'Prowling about Panama', George A. Miller takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the vibrant landscapes and bustling cities of Panama. As the author immerses himself in the local culture, he encounters a series of unexpected adventures and encounters that range from humorous to heart-stopping. From navigating treacherous jungles to uncovering hidden treasures, Miller's escapades in Panama will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies around the next corner.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Serendipitous encounters
