Synopsis:
In 'The Rubicon' by E. F. Benson, a seemingly idyllic English village is disrupted by the arrival of a mysterious stranger. As the villagers become entangled in his web of secrets and deceit, tensions rise and long-held grudges resurface. With each passing day, the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the true nature of human nature.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes:
Deception, Betrayal, Small-town Secrets
