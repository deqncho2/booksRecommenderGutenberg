Synopsis: 
"The Diary of Delia: Being a Veracious Chronicle of the Kitchen, with Some Side-Lights on the Parlour" is a captivating novel that takes readers on a journey through the life of Delia, a young woman working in a bustling kitchen. Through her diary entries, we witness the daily challenges, triumphs, and secrets that unfold within the walls of the kitchen, while also catching glimpses of the intriguing events happening in the adjacent parlour. Delia's observations and experiences shed light on the complex dynamics between the kitchen staff and the upper-class residents they serve, revealing a world filled with passion, ambition, and hidden desires.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance
Themes: Social class, Ambition, Secrets, Forbidden love
