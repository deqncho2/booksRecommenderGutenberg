Synopsis: 

In 'The American Reformed Cattle Doctor', George H. Dadd presents a comprehensive guide to preserving the health and curing diseases of various farm animals, including oxen, cows, sheep, and swine. Filled with a wealth of original recipes and valuable information on farm and dairy management, this book serves as an indispensable resource for farmers and animal enthusiasts alike. From diagnosing common ailments to providing practical solutions, Dadd's expertise and insights offer a holistic approach to animal care, ensuring the well-being of livestock and the success of agricultural endeavors.

Genres: Non-fiction, Veterinary Medicine, Farming

Themes: Animal health and care, Farm management, Livestock diseases and remedies
