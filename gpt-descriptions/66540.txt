Synopsis: In 'Cosmic Saboteur' by Robinson, Frank M., a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a shocking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and prevent a catastrophic event from unfolding.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific discovery, Conspiracy, Morality, Survival
