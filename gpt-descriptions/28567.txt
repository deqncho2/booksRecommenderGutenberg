Synopsis: 
In 'Godfrey Marten, Undergraduate' by Charles Turley, we follow the misadventures of the eponymous protagonist as he navigates the challenges of university life. Godfrey, a charming but somewhat hapless undergraduate, finds himself entangled in a series of hilarious and often absurd situations, from academic mishaps to romantic entanglements. As he stumbles through his studies, Godfrey's escapades provide a humorous and entertaining glimpse into the world of university education in the early 20th century.

Genres: 
Comedy, Coming-of-age, Satire

Themes: 
Education, Identity, Social class
