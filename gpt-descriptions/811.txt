Synopsis: In 'The Tragical History of Doctor Faustus', we are introduced to a brilliant scholar who becomes disillusioned with the limitations of human knowledge. Desperate for power and eternal knowledge, he makes a dangerous pact with the devil, unleashing a series of thrilling and morally complex events that will test the boundaries of his own soul. As Faustus delves deeper into the dark arts, he must confront the consequences of his choices and grapple with the ultimate price of his ambition.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama, Supernatural

Themes: Ambition, Knowledge, Morality, Power, Redemption
