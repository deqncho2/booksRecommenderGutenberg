Synopsis: In a dystopian future where reality is a manufactured illusion, a group of rebels known as "The Chasers" embark on a dangerous mission to uncover the truth behind the artificial world they inhabit. As they navigate through a maze of deceit and danger, they must confront their own identities and question the nature of reality itself. With each step, they inch closer to a shocking revelation that could change everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Identity, Rebellion
