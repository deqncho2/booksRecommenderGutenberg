Synopsis: 

"The Law of the Sea: A manual of the principles of admiralty law for students, mariners, and ship operators" is a comprehensive guide that delves into the intricate world of admiralty law. Written by Brinton, J. Y., Canfield, George L., and Dalzell, George W., this manual serves as an indispensable resource for anyone involved in maritime affairs. From students seeking a solid foundation in admiralty law to mariners and ship operators navigating the complex legal waters, this book offers a clear and concise understanding of the principles that govern the sea.

Genres: Legal, Reference, Maritime

Themes: Admiralty law, Maritime regulations, Legal principles
