Synopsis: In 'Rags: (The Story Of A Dog)' by Karen Niemann, follow the incredible journey of Rags, a lovable and determined dog who finds himself in the midst of a heartwarming adventure. From the bustling streets of New York City to the tranquil countryside, Rags encounters a diverse cast of characters who teach him valuable lessons about loyalty, friendship, and the true meaning of home. As Rags navigates through various challenges and triumphs, readers will be captivated by his unwavering spirit and unwavering determination to find his place in the world.

Genres: Children's Literature, Animal Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Finding Home
