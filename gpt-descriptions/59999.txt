Synopsis: 

'Argentina and Her People of To-day' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and diverse culture of Argentina. Nevin O. Winter provides a comprehensive account of the customs, characteristics, amusements, history, and advancement of the Argentinians, while also exploring the development and resources of their country. From the bustling streets of Buenos Aires to the vast landscapes of the Pampas, Winter paints a vivid picture of Argentina and its people, offering fascinating insights into their traditions, achievements, and way of life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, Cultural Studies

Themes: Cultural diversity, National identity, Historical development, Socio-economic progress
