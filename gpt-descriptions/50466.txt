Synopsis: 
In 'Animal Chums: True Tales about Four-footed Friends' by McIntosh, Jean, embark on a heartwarming journey through a collection of captivating stories that celebrate the extraordinary bond between humans and animals. From mischievous cats to loyal dogs, and even unexpected encounters with wild creatures, these true tales will leave readers both amazed and touched by the incredible connections formed between humans and their four-legged companions. Prepare to be enchanted by the remarkable adventures and heartwarming moments that unfold in this delightful book.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Stories, Memoir

Themes: Animal-human relationships, Loyalty, Adventure, Connection
