Synopsis: In "The Alley Cat's Kitten" by Caroline Fuller, a street-smart alley cat named Whiskers finds herself unexpectedly caring for a tiny, abandoned kitten. As Whiskers navigates the challenges of motherhood and protects her newfound family from the dangers of the city, she discovers the true meaning of love and sacrifice. With heartwarming moments and unexpected twists, this tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, rooting for Whiskers and her adorable kitten.

Genres: Children's Literature, Animal Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Motherhood, Love, Survival
