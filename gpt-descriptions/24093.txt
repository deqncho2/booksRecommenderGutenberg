Synopsis: In 'The Root of Evil' by Dixon, Thomas, Jr., a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious and gruesome murders that leave the community in a state of fear and suspicion. As the body count rises, a determined detective must unravel the dark secrets hidden within the town's history to uncover the truth behind the killings. With each twist and turn, the detective finds himself drawn deeper into a web of deceit, betrayal, and a sinister evil that threatens to consume them all.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Secrets and lies, the darkness within, the power of redemption
