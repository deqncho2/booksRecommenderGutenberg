Synopsis: 

In 'Great Artists, Vol 1.: Raphael, Rubens, Murillo, and Durer' by Jennie Ellis Keysor, embark on a captivating journey through the lives and works of four legendary artists who left an indelible mark on the world of art. From the exquisite beauty of Raphael's Renaissance masterpieces to the vibrant and dynamic paintings of Rubens, the tender and emotive art of Murillo, and the intricate engravings of Durer, this volume offers a fascinating exploration of their artistic genius. Delve into the personal stories, inspirations, and techniques that shaped these artists' careers, and discover the profound impact they had on the art world and beyond.

Genres: Art History, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic genius, Renaissance, Baroque, Individuality, Inspiration
