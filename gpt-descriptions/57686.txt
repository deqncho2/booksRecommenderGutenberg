Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Robert Barr' is a collection of works by the renowned author Robert Barr. This compilation includes a diverse range of stories that will captivate readers with their intriguing plots, memorable characters, and unexpected twists. From thrilling mysteries to historical adventures, Barr's writing takes readers on a journey through different time periods and settings, keeping them engaged from start to finish. Whether it's a tale of suspense, romance, or humor, this collection offers a delightful reading experience that showcases the versatility and talent of Robert Barr.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance, Humor
