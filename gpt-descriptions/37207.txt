Synopsis: In 'Winona of the Camp Fire' by Margaret Widdemer, a young girl named Winona finds herself in the midst of a thrilling adventure when she joins the Camp Fire Girls. As she navigates the challenges of camp life, she discovers hidden secrets, forms deep friendships, and uncovers a mystery that will test her courage and determination. With each twist and turn, Winona's journey becomes a captivating tale of self-discovery and resilience.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
