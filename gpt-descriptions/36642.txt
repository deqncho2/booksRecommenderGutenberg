Synopsis: In "Eli's Children: The Chronicles of an Unhappy Family" by George Manville Fenn, readers are introduced to the tumultuous lives of the Eli family. Set in the backdrop of Victorian England, this gripping tale follows the lives of the four Eli siblings as they navigate through a web of secrets, betrayals, and hidden desires. As the family's dark past resurfaces, the siblings find themselves entangled in a dangerous game of survival, where their loyalty and love for one another are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel explores the complexities of family dynamics and the lengths one would go to protect their own.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Family Drama

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Loyalty, Survival
