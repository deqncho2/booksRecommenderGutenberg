Synopsis: In Henrik Ibsen's "Hedda Gabler," we are introduced to the enigmatic and complex character of Hedda Gabler, a newly married woman trapped in a stifling and conventional society. As the story unfolds, we witness Hedda's struggle for power, her manipulation of those around her, and her desperate search for freedom. With a series of unexpected twists and turns, "Hedda Gabler" explores the dark depths of human desires and the consequences of societal constraints.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Power and control, Gender roles and expectations, Individual freedom, Desperation and manipulation
