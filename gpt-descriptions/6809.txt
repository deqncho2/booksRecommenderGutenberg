Synopsis: In 'The Doctor's Daughter' by Vera, a renowned doctor finds herself facing a mysterious and life-altering event when she discovers a long-lost letter from her deceased father. As she delves into her family's past, she uncovers shocking secrets that challenge her perception of her own identity and force her to confront the complexities of love, loss, and the power of forgiveness. This gripping tale takes readers on a captivating journey of self-discovery and the enduring bonds of family.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Family Saga

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Family, Love, Forgiveness
