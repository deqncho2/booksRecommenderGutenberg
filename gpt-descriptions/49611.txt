Synopsis: In this fourth volume of William Butler Yeats' collected works, readers are treated to a captivating blend of verse and prose. From the enchanting play "Cathleen ni Houlihan" to the mystical tale of "The Golden Helmet," and the exploration of the Irish Dramatic Movement, this collection offers a rich tapestry of Irish literature. Delve into the themes of national identity, sacrifice, and the power of art as Yeats masterfully weaves together stories that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Poetry, Drama, Prose

Themes: National identity, Sacrifice, Power of art
