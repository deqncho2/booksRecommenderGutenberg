Synopsis: In '4-1/2B, Eros' by Malcolm Jameson, a mysterious and alluring woman moves into an apartment building, captivating the attention of its residents. As the tenants become entangled in her enigmatic presence, their lives take unexpected turns, leading to a series of intriguing and suspenseful events. With each passing day, the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, leaving the reader questioning the true nature of desire and the power it holds over human lives.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological thriller, Suspense

Themes: Desire, Identity, Perception, Human nature
