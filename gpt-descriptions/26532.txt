Synopsis: In 'Over the Line' by Harold M. Sherman, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he is assigned to investigate a high-profile murder case. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he uncovers a sinister plot that reaches far beyond the crime scene, forcing him to confront his own demons and question the very nature of justice. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Justice, Deception, Redemption
