Synopsis: In 'The Brighton Boys at Chateau-Thierry' by James R. Driscoll, a group of young American soldiers find themselves thrust into the heart of World War I as they are sent to the front lines in Chateau-Thierry, France. As they navigate the chaos and danger of war, they form unbreakable bonds of friendship and face unimaginable challenges that will test their courage and resilience. With their lives on the line, the Brighton Boys must find the strength to fight for their country and each other.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Sacrifice, Coming of Age
