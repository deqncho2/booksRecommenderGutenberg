Synopsis: 
In 'The Cruise of the Make-Believes' by Tom Gallon, a group of strangers find themselves aboard a mysterious ship that seems to defy reality. As they embark on a journey filled with unexpected twists and turns, they must confront their deepest fears and desires. Each passenger has a secret, and as the voyage progresses, their true identities and motivations are slowly revealed. With danger lurking around every corner, the line between fantasy and reality becomes blurred, leaving the reader questioning what is truly possible.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Deception, Perception of Reality
