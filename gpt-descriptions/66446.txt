Synopsis: 
When a wealthy American heiress is found dead on the Plymouth Express, it sets off a chain of events that will leave readers guessing until the very end. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, motives are questioned, and the true identity of the killer remains elusive. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot full of twists and turns, 'The Plymouth Express Affair' is a gripping mystery that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Greed, Betrayal, Justice
