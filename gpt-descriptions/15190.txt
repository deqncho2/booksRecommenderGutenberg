Synopsis: 
'With the Harmony to Labrador: Notes of a visit to the Moravian mission stations on the north-east coast of Labrador' takes readers on a captivating journey to the remote and rugged region of Labrador. Benjamin La Trobe, an adventurous traveler, shares his firsthand experiences and encounters with the Moravian missionaries who have dedicated their lives to spreading the gospel in this challenging environment. From treacherous sea voyages to heartwarming interactions with the indigenous Inuit people, La Trobe's narrative paints a vivid picture of the beauty, hardships, and resilience found in this untamed land.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Faith, Cultural Exchange
