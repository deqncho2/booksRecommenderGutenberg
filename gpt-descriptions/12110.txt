Synopsis: 
In 'The Scornful Lady', a young and witty woman named Lady Brute finds herself trapped in a loveless marriage. Determined to assert her independence and challenge societal norms, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and rebellion. As she navigates through a web of deceit, secret affairs, and unexpected alliances, Lady Brute's sharp tongue and cunning wit become her most powerful weapons. Will she find true love and happiness, or will her defiance lead to her downfall?

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and marriage, Gender roles and societal expectations, Deception and manipulation
