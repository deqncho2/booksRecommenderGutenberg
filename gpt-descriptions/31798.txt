Synopsis: In 'Games' by Katherine MacLean, a group of scientists embark on a groundbreaking experiment that blurs the line between reality and illusion. As they delve deeper into the game, they discover that the consequences are far more dangerous than they could have ever imagined. With their lives at stake, they must navigate a treacherous maze of mind-bending challenges and uncover the truth behind the game before it consumes them completely.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Morality, Human Nature
