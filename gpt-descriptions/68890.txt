Synopsis: 

In 'Dogs of all nations: a complete work, profusely illustrated, bearing on the world's different varieties of the dog, grouped under their several nationalities, with descriptive matter explaining the characteristics and utility of each,' Walter Esplin Mason takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse world of dogs. This comprehensive and visually stunning book explores the various breeds of dogs from different countries, shedding light on their unique characteristics and practical uses. From the loyal German Shepherds of Germany to the elegant Afghan Hounds of Afghanistan, Mason delves into the fascinating history, traits, and roles of each breed. With an abundance of illustrations, this work offers a captivating glimpse into the rich tapestry of dogs from around the globe.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal reference, Illustrated book

Themes: Cultural diversity, Animal companionship, Breed characteristics
