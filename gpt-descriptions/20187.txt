Synopsis: On Christmas Day in the Morning is a heartwarming tale that follows a young boy named Rob as he embarks on a special mission to surprise his father on Christmas morning. Filled with anticipation and determination, Rob sets out to show his love and gratitude in a truly unforgettable way. This touching story captures the essence of the holiday spirit and reminds us of the power of selflessness and the joy of giving.

Genres: Fiction, Holiday, Family

Themes: Love, Gratitude, Generosity
