Synopsis: In 'Kitty Alone: A Story of Three Fires', the final volume of the trilogy, the captivating tale of Kitty Alone reaches its climactic conclusion. Set against the backdrop of the tumultuous times of the English Civil War, this gripping historical novel follows the resilient and resourceful Kitty as she navigates through the chaos and danger that surrounds her. As fires rage and loyalties are tested, Kitty's strength and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and find her place in a world torn apart by conflict?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Resilience, Survival, Loyalty, Conflict
