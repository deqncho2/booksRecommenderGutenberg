Synopsis: In 'Gratitude' by Norton, Roy, a renowned scientist finds himself facing a life-altering decision when he is diagnosed with a terminal illness. As he navigates through the complexities of his personal and professional life, he embarks on a journey of self-discovery, exploring the depths of human connection, the fragility of existence, and the power of gratitude. This poignant and thought-provoking novel delves into the profound questions of what truly matters in life and the transformative nature of embracing gratitude.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Philosophy

Themes: Mortality, Relationships, Self-reflection, Gratitude
