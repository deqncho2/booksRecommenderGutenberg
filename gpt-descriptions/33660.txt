Synopsis: In the small town of Madison, strange occurrences begin to unfold when a mysterious meteor shower blankets the sky. As the townspeople try to make sense of the bizarre events, they soon realize that the stardust from the meteor shower is causing extraordinary changes in the world around them. With the fate of their town hanging in the balance, a group of unlikely heroes must uncover the truth behind the stardust and find a way to save their community from impending disaster.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Catastrophe, Survival, Exploration
