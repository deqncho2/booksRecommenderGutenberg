Synopsis: 
In 'Dave Porter At Bear Camp; Or, The Wild Man of Mirror Lake' by Stratemeyer, Edward, Dave Porter finds himself embarking on a thrilling adventure in the wilderness. When a mysterious wild man is spotted near Mirror Lake, Dave and his friends set out to uncover the truth behind the legend. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected dangers, Dave's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. Will they be able to solve the mystery and survive the untamed wilderness?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
