Synopsis: 
'An American Religious Movement: A Brief History of the Disciples of Christ' by Garrison, Winfred Ernest is a captivating exploration of the origins and development of the Disciples of Christ, a prominent religious movement in America. From its humble beginnings in the early 19th century to its growth and influence in the modern era, this book delves into the key figures, events, and ideologies that shaped the movement. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Garrison unveils the struggles, triumphs, and controversies that have defined the Disciples of Christ, offering readers a fascinating glimpse into the evolution of this influential religious group.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Religion

Themes: Religious movements, American history, Faith and spirituality
