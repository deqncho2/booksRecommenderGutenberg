Synopsis: 
In 'Boyville: A History of Fifteen Years' Work Among Newsboys', John E. Gunckel takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of newsboys in the early 20th century. Through vivid storytelling and personal anecdotes, Gunckel paints a vivid picture of the struggles, triumphs, and resilience of these young boys who navigated the bustling streets of Boyville. From their daily battles for survival to the formation of a unique community, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a forgotten era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, memoir, social commentary

Themes: Poverty and survival, community and camaraderie, the power of education and mentorship
