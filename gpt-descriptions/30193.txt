Synopsis: In 'East of the Shadows' by Barclay, Hubert, Mrs., a young woman named Amelia finds herself drawn into a mysterious world of secrets and hidden truths when she inherits an old family estate. As she delves deeper into the history of the house, Amelia uncovers a dark and haunting past that threatens to consume her. With the help of a enigmatic stranger, she must navigate through a web of deceit and danger to uncover the truth and save herself from the shadows that lurk within.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Redemption, the power of the past
