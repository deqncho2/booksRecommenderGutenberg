Synopsis: 
'The Shih King, or, Book of Poetry: From the Sacred Books of the East Volume 3' by Legge, James is a captivating collection of ancient Chinese poems that offers a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of China. This anthology presents a diverse range of verses, from lyrical odes to heartfelt elegies, exploring themes of love, nature, and the human experience. Through its evocative imagery and profound emotions, the book transports readers to a bygone era, immersing them in the beauty and wisdom of ancient Chinese literature.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology, Cultural

Themes: Love, Nature, Human experience
