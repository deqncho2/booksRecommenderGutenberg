Synopsis: In 'Flames of the Storm' by W. C. Tuttle, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Thunder Basin, bringing with him a dark secret and a trail of violence. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal, they must confront their own demons and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of their community. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Justice, Moral ambiguity
