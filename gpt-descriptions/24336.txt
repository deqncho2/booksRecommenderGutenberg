Synopsis: In 'Little People: An Alphabet', T. W. H. Crosland takes readers on a whimsical journey through the alphabet, introducing a delightful cast of characters along the way. From the adventurous antics of Arthur and Alice to the mischievous mischief of Molly and Max, each letter brings forth a new and enchanting tale. With charming illustrations and clever wordplay, this book is sure to captivate both young and old, inviting them to explore the magical world of the little people.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Picture Book

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Adventure
