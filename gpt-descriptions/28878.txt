Synopsis: 
In 'Four Little Blossoms at Oak Hill School' by Mabel C. Hawley, the four adventurous Blossom children embark on a new chapter of their lives as they start attending Oak Hill School. From making new friends to facing unexpected challenges, the siblings navigate through the ups and downs of school life, all while discovering the importance of teamwork, perseverance, and the power of friendship. With each passing day, the Blossom children learn valuable life lessons that will shape their futures and create lasting memories.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Education, Growth, Resilience
