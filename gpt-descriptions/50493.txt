Synopsis: In 'The Black Eagle; or, Ticonderoga' by James, G. P. R., a young British officer finds himself embroiled in a dangerous mission during the French and Indian War. As he navigates treacherous landscapes and faces formidable enemies, he must uncover a hidden secret that could change the course of the war. With thrilling battles, unexpected alliances, and a race against time, this gripping historical adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Courage, Honor
