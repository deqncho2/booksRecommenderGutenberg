Synopsis: In 'The Crochet Book, Fourth Series' by Riego de la Branchardière, Eléonore, a young woman with a passion for crochet, embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling streets of Paris to the enchanting countryside of Provence. As she delves deeper into the world of crochet, Eléonore discovers a hidden network of artisans, each with their own unique stories and techniques. Along the way, she encounters unexpected friendships, unravels family secrets, and finds inspiration in the most unlikely places. With every stitch, Eléonore weaves together a tapestry of love, creativity, and self-discovery that will leave readers captivated until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Craft and Hobby

Themes: Artistry, Identity, Connection
