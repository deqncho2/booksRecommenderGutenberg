Synopsis: In 'The Rival Pitchers: A Story of College Baseball' by Chadwick, Lester, two talented pitchers find themselves on opposing teams, igniting a fierce rivalry that goes beyond the baseball diamond. As they battle for supremacy, their personal lives become entangled, leading to unexpected consequences that will test their loyalty and determination. With high-stakes games, intense competition, and a dash of romance, this gripping tale explores the bonds of friendship, the pursuit of excellence, and the sacrifices one must make to achieve their dreams.

Genres: Sports Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Drama

Themes: Rivalry, Friendship, Ambition, Sacrifice
