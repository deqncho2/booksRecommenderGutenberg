Synopsis: In 'Lectures on the constitution and laws of England: With a commentary on Magna Charta, and illustrations of many of the English statutes', Francis Stoughton Sullivan takes readers on an enlightening journey through the intricate web of England's legal system. Through a series of engaging lectures, Sullivan explores the historical context, principles, and evolution of the English constitution and laws, shedding light on the significance of Magna Charta and various statutes. With insightful commentary and vivid illustrations, this work offers a captivating exploration of the foundations of English law.

Genres: Legal literature, Historical non-fiction, Educational

Themes: English legal history, Constitutional law, Magna Charta, Statutes
