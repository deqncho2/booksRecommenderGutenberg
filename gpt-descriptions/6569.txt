Synopsis: In 'Bessie Bradford's Prize' by Joanna H. Mathews, a young and ambitious artist named Bessie Bradford finds herself caught between her passion for art and the expectations of her traditional family. When an unexpected opportunity arises, Bessie must make a difficult decision that could change the course of her life forever. As she navigates the challenges of pursuing her dreams, Bessie discovers the true meaning of success and the power of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Pursuit of passion, Family expectations, Self-discovery
