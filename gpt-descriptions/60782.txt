Synopsis: 
Beside the Fire: A collection of Irish Gaelic folk stories takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Irish folklore. From mischievous fairies to heroic warriors, this collection brings to life the rich tapestry of Irish culture and mythology. Each story is filled with vivid imagery and unforgettable characters, transporting readers to a realm where ancient legends and everyday life intertwine. Whether it's a tale of love and loss or a battle against supernatural forces, these stories are sure to captivate and leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Irish culture, Mythical creatures, Heroism, Love and loss
