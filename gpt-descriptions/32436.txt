Synopsis: In 'Duel on Syrtis' by Poul Anderson, a skilled and enigmatic pilot finds himself caught in a deadly game of cat and mouse on the desolate and treacherous Martian landscape. As he navigates through a web of political intrigue and personal vendettas, he must rely on his cunning and resourcefulness to survive. With each twist and turn, the stakes escalate, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Revenge
