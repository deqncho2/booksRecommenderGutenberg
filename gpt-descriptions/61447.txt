Synopsis: In 'Wrecked in Port: A Novel' by Edmund Yates, a young sailor finds himself stranded in a mysterious port town after a devastating shipwreck. As he navigates the unfamiliar surroundings, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With danger lurking around every corner, he must uncover the truth behind the town's dark past and find a way to escape before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Redemption
