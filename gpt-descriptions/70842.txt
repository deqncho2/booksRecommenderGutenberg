Synopsis: 

The Champlain tercentenary: Final report of the New York Lake Champlain tercentenary commission is a comprehensive account of the celebrations and events organized by the commission to commemorate the 300th anniversary of Samuel de Champlain's exploration of Lake Champlain. This report provides a detailed overview of the various activities, exhibitions, and historical reenactments that took place during the tercentenary, shedding light on the rich history and cultural significance of the region. It also includes insightful analysis and recommendations for future preservation and promotion of Lake Champlain's heritage.

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction, Report

Themes: History, Cultural heritage, Commemoration, Preservation
