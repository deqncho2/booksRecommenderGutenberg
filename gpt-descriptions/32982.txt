Synopsis: 
In 'Student's Hand-book of Mushrooms of America, Edible and Poisonous', Thomas Taylor takes readers on an intriguing journey through the fascinating world of mushrooms. This comprehensive guide provides a wealth of information on identifying and distinguishing between edible and poisonous mushrooms found across America. With vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, Taylor's handbook becomes an essential companion for both amateur mycologists and nature enthusiasts. From the enchanting allure of edible mushrooms to the dangers lurking within poisonous varieties, this book offers a captivating exploration of the diverse and often mysterious fungi that inhabit our forests and fields.

Genres: Non-fiction, Field Guide, Nature

Themes: Mushroom identification, Edible vs. poisonous, Nature exploration
