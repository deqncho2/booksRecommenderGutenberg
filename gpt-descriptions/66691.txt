Synopsis: In the midst of World War II, a group of American soldiers find themselves stranded on a remote island in the Aleutian archipelago. As they battle the harsh elements and face the constant threat of a deadly williwaw, a violent storm unique to the region, tensions rise among the men. With their survival hanging in the balance, they must confront their deepest fears and grapple with the moral complexities of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Camaraderie, Moral Dilemmas
