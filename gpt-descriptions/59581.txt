Synopsis: 
In 'Brain Teaser' by Tom Godwin, a brilliant neuroscientist named Dr. Emily Parker finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of human evolution. As she delves deeper into her research, she becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where her own mind becomes the ultimate battleground. With her sanity hanging in the balance, Dr. Parker must navigate a treacherous path of deception and betrayal, all while trying to unlock the secrets of the human brain.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Human potential, Ethical dilemmas, Psychological warfare
