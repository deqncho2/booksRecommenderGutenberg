Synopsis: Join Woodes Rogers on an exhilarating adventure as he embarks on a daring voyage around the world. From treacherous encounters with pirates to breathtaking encounters with exotic lands and cultures, this gripping tale will keep you on the edge of your seat. As Rogers navigates the vast oceans, he must confront his own fears and test the limits of his courage. Will he emerge victorious or succumb to the perils that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Bravery
