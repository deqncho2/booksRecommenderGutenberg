Synopsis: In "The Worshipper of the Image" by Richard Le Gallienne, a young artist becomes infatuated with a mysterious woman who seems to embody the beauty and perfection he seeks to capture in his art. As he delves deeper into his obsession, he discovers a dark secret that threatens to consume him. This haunting tale explores the blurred lines between reality and illusion, the power of art to both inspire and destroy, and the dangerous allure of unattainable beauty.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, romance

Themes: Obsession, beauty, art, illusion, the destructive nature of desire
