Synopsis: In "Cholera and the Water Supply in the South Districts of London in 1854," John Snow delves into a gripping investigation that unravels the mysterious outbreak of cholera in London. As the deadly disease spreads rapidly, Snow embarks on a quest to uncover the source of the contamination, challenging prevailing beliefs and risking his own reputation in the process. With meticulous research and a relentless pursuit of truth, Snow's findings could hold the key to saving countless lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical thriller, Historical investigation

Themes: Disease outbreak, Scientific inquiry, Social reform
