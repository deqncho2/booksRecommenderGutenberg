Synopsis: In 'Flight From Time' by Alfred Coppel, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Richard Arlen invents a groundbreaking device that allows him to travel through time. However, when he becomes trapped in a time loop, he must navigate through different eras and face unexpected challenges to find a way back to his own time. As he encounters historical figures and witnesses pivotal moments in history, Dr. Arlen's journey becomes a thrilling race against time itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Adventure

Themes: Exploration of time, Consequences of altering history, Human resilience
