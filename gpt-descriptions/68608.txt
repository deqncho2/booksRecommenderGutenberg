Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Don Sebastian: or, The House of the Braganza', Anna Maria Porter continues the enthralling historical romance that captivated readers in the first installment. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue and forbidden love, this tale follows the lives of the noble Braganza family as they navigate the treacherous waters of power and passion. As secrets unravel and loyalties are tested, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of deception and desire that threatens to tear their world apart. Will love conquer all, or will the weight of duty and ambition prove too great?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Intrigue

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Power and Ambition, Loyalty and Betrayal
