Synopsis: 
Gregor Samsa, a hardworking salesman, wakes up one morning to find himself transformed into a gigantic insect-like creature. As he struggles to come to terms with his new form, Gregor's life takes a dramatic turn, affecting not only him but also his family. Through this bizarre and unsettling transformation, Kafka explores the themes of alienation, identity, and the absurdity of human existence.

Genres: 
Absurdist fiction, existentialism, psychological fiction

Themes: 
Alienation, identity, existential crisis, family dynamics
