Synopsis: 
'Under the Witches' Moon: A Romantic Tale of Mediaeval Rome' by Gallizier, Nathan is a captivating historical romance set in the enchanting city of Rome during the Middle Ages. The story follows the journey of Isabella, a young and spirited woman who finds herself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and dark magic. As she navigates the treacherous streets of Rome, Isabella discovers a hidden world of witches and sorcery, where her heart's desires and her very life are at stake. With danger lurking at every corner, Isabella must choose between the love she craves and the secrets she uncovers, all under the bewitching glow of the moon.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Love and Desire, Betrayal, Magic and Witchcraft
