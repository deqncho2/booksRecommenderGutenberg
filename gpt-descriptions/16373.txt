Synopsis: In 'Mrs. Red Pepper' by Grace S. Richmond, a charming and mysterious woman named Mrs. Red Pepper arrives in a small town, capturing the attention and curiosity of the locals. As she becomes entangled in the lives of the townspeople, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and unexpected alliances are formed. With her wit, intelligence, and a touch of mischief, Mrs. Red Pepper brings about a series of events that will forever change the lives of those around her.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Secrets and deception, Community and belonging
