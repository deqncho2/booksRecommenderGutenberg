Synopsis: In 'History Repeats' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a shocking discovery that challenges everything we know about the past. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of time, he uncovers a dangerous secret that could alter the course of history forever. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Time travel, Consequences of knowledge
