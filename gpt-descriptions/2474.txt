Synopsis: 
In "The Circus Boys on the Flying Rings; Or, Making the Start in the Sawdust Life," we follow the thrilling journey of Phil Forrest, a young and ambitious circus performer. As Phil joins the circus, he quickly discovers that life under the big top is not all glamour and excitement. Facing challenges, rivalries, and unexpected dangers, Phil must prove his worth and overcome his fears to achieve his dreams. With breathtaking acrobatics, heart-stopping stunts, and a cast of colorful characters, this captivating tale takes readers on a wild ride through the captivating world of the circus.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Overcoming fears, Friendship
