Synopsis: In 'A Moment of Madness, and Other Stories', Florence Marryat takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing tales. From a mysterious encounter on a train to a haunting ghost story, each narrative presents a unique and unexpected twist. Delve into the depths of human emotions, explore the complexities of relationships, and experience the thrill of the unknown in this enthralling volume.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Love and betrayal, Identity and self-discovery, The power of the supernatural
