Synopsis: In 'The Abandoned Farmers: His Humorous Account of a Retreat from the City to the Farm', Irvin S. Cobb takes readers on a delightful journey as he recounts his comical experiences of leaving the bustling city life behind and embarking on a new adventure as a farmer. From his initial struggles with the land and the animals to his encounters with eccentric neighbors and unexpected mishaps, Cobb's witty and entertaining anecdotes will keep readers laughing and engaged throughout.

Genres: Humor, Memoir, Non-fiction

Themes: Rural life, Adaptation to change, Humor in everyday situations
