Synopsis: In 'Peeps at People' by Holliday, Robert Cortes, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the lives of various individuals, each with their own unique stories and secrets. From the bustling streets of London to the tranquil countryside, this collection of interconnected tales offers a glimpse into the joys, sorrows, and complexities of human existence. As the lives of these diverse characters intertwine, unexpected connections and revelations emerge, leaving readers eager to uncover the hidden truths that lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Drama

Themes: Human nature, Relationships, Identity, Secrets
