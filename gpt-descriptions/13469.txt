Synopsis: 
In 'A History of Pantomime' by Broadbent, R. J., embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of pantomime, where laughter, music, and spectacle intertwine to create a magical theatrical experience. From its humble origins in ancient Greece to its extravagant productions in modern times, this book delves into the rich history and evolution of pantomime, exploring the colorful characters, hilarious slapstick comedy, and heartwarming stories that have captivated audiences for centuries. Discover the secrets behind the iconic pantomime traditions, the dazzling costumes, and the enduring appeal of this beloved form of entertainment. 

Genres: Theatre, History, Performing Arts

Themes: Tradition, Comedy, Theatricality
