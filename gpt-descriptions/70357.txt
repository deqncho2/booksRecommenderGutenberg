Synopsis: In 'The Jumano Indians' by Frederick Webb Hodge, embark on a captivating journey through the rich history and culture of the Jumano Indians. Delve into their fascinating traditions, mysterious rituals, and their interactions with other indigenous tribes and European explorers. Uncover the secrets of their ancient civilization and witness the resilience and strength of a people determined to preserve their heritage. This meticulously researched account offers a compelling exploration of a lesser-known Native American tribe, shedding light on their unique way of life and their enduring legacy.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Anthropology

Themes: Native American culture, Indigenous history, Cultural preservation
