Synopsis: 
In 'Married Life: A Comedy, in Three Acts', the audience is introduced to a charming and witty couple, Mr. and Mrs. Somerton, who seem to have the perfect marriage. However, as the story unfolds, their seemingly idyllic relationship is put to the test when a series of unexpected events and miscommunications lead to hilarious misunderstandings and uproarious situations. With a cast of eccentric characters and clever dialogue, this comedy explores the complexities of married life and the humorous challenges that come with it.

Genres: Comedy, Theatre, Romantic Comedy

Themes: Marriage, Communication, Humor
