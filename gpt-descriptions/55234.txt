Synopsis: In "The Life of La Fayette, the Knight of Liberty in Two Worlds and Two Centuries," Lydia Hoyt Farmer takes readers on a captivating journey through the extraordinary life of the renowned Marquis de La Fayette. From his early days as a young French nobleman to his pivotal role in the American Revolution and his unwavering commitment to the ideals of liberty, this biography delves into the fascinating experiences and adventures of a man who became a symbol of freedom in both Europe and America. Farmer's vivid storytelling and meticulous research bring to life the trials, triumphs, and enduring legacy of this remarkable historical figure.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Liberty, Revolution, Heroism, Legacy
