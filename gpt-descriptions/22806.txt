Synopsis: In 'The Bronze Hand: 1897' by Anna Katharine Green, a mysterious bronze hand becomes the center of a perplexing murder case. When a wealthy man is found dead in his study, his hand clutching the bronze artifact, detective Ebenezer Gryce is called upon to unravel the secrets behind this puzzling crime. As Gryce delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of deceit, hidden motives, and unexpected connections that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Intrigue
