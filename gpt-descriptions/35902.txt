Synopsis: In 'Final Proof; Or, The Value of Evidence' by Ottolengui, Rodrigues, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a wealthy man is found dead under suspicious circumstances. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of deceit, secrets, and unexpected alliances. With each piece of evidence, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of lies to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice. This thrilling mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the complex puzzle.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Truth and deception, the power of evidence, the complexity of human nature
