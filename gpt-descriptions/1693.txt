Synopsis: In 'Dangerous Days' by Mary Roberts Rinehart, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she becomes entangled with a notorious criminal. As she navigates through a series of unexpected events and encounters, she must rely on her wit and courage to uncover the truth and protect herself from the lurking threats that surround her. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the secrets hidden within the pages of this thrilling tale.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Crime and Justice, Deception and Betrayal
