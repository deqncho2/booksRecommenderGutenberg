Synopsis: In 'Crime and Corruption' by Vaknin, Samuel, a gripping tale unfolds as a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, power, and treachery. Set in a city plagued by crime and corruption, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of secrets and lies to uncover the truth. As the stakes rise and the danger escalates, the detective's own moral compass is put to the test, blurring the lines between justice and revenge. With unexpected twists and a cast of intriguing characters, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Corruption, Power, Morality
