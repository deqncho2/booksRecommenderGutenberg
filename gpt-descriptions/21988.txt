Synopsis: In 'Breaking Point' by James E. Gunn, a renowned psychologist is tasked with unraveling the mysterious and disturbing behavior of a group of individuals who have reached their breaking point. As he delves deeper into their minds, he uncovers a web of secrets and hidden desires that threaten to push him to his own breaking point. With each revelation, the line between sanity and madness becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a gripping and suspenseful climax.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Human psychology, Mental breakdown, Hidden desires
