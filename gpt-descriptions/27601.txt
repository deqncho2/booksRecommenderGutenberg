Synopsis: In 'Hawtrey's Deputy' by Bindloss, Harold, a young and ambitious man named Ralph Hawtrey finds himself unexpectedly thrust into a position of power and responsibility. As he navigates the treacherous world of the Canadian wilderness, he must confront dangerous outlaws, face his own inner demons, and prove himself worthy of the trust placed upon him. With his determination and resourcefulness, Ralph must rise to the challenge and become the deputy he never thought he could be.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Historical Fiction

Themes: Coming of age, Courage, Redemption
