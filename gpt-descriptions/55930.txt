Synopsis: 
In 'Section-Cutting: A Practical Guide to the Preparation and Mounting of Sections for the Microscope, Special Prominence Being given to the Subject of Animal Sections', Sylvester Marsh takes readers on a fascinating journey into the intricate world of section-cutting for microscopic analysis. With meticulous attention to detail, Marsh unveils the secrets behind the preparation and mounting of sections, focusing particularly on animal specimens. From the delicate techniques required to obtain precise slices to the art of preserving and displaying these sections, this practical guide offers invaluable insights for both beginners and experienced microscopists. As readers delve into the pages, they will discover a wealth of knowledge that will enhance their understanding of the microscopic world and enable them to unlock new discoveries.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Microscopy, Scientific Techniques, Animal Anatomy
