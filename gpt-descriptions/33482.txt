Synopsis: In 'The Furnace' by Rose Macaulay, a young woman named Louise is drawn into a mysterious world of secrets and intrigue when she inherits a dilapidated mansion in the English countryside. As she delves deeper into the history of the house, she uncovers a hidden chamber that holds a dark and dangerous secret. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, Louise must navigate a treacherous path filled with deception and betrayal, all while trying to unravel the truth behind the mansion's haunting past.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the power of the past, the blurred lines between reality and illusion
