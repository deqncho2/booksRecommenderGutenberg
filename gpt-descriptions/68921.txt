Synopsis: In "The Making of a Man" by Flatt, W. D., we follow the journey of a young man named James as he navigates the challenges and triumphs of coming of age in a small town. From his early years filled with innocence and curiosity to the complexities of adulthood, James must confront his own fears, make difficult choices, and discover the true meaning of manhood. As he encounters love, loss, and self-discovery, James's transformation becomes a captivating exploration of the human spirit and the power of personal growth.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Bildungsroman

Themes: Identity, Growth, Self-discovery, Love, Loss
