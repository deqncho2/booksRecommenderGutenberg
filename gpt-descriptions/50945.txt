Synopsis: 

In 'Point of Departure' by Shelton Vaughan, a young woman named Lily finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden artifact that holds the key to a long-lost secret. As she embarks on a thrilling journey across continents, she must navigate treacherous alliances and face unexpected adversaries who will stop at nothing to possess the artifact. With time running out, Lily must unravel the truth before it consumes her and those she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Secrets and hidden knowledge, Betrayal and trust, Self-discovery
