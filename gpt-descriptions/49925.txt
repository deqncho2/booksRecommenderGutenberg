Synopsis: 

In "In Defense of the Flag: A true war story," author David W. Stafford takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous times of the great rebellion. Through vivid pen pictures, he paints scenes and incidents that capture the essence of this historic period. The narrative delves into the thrilling experiences of the protagonist during his escape from southern prisons, offering a firsthand account of the dangers and challenges faced. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, War literature

Themes: Patriotism, Survival, Resilience
