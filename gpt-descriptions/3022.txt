Synopsis: In the remote mountains of Kentucky, a long-standing feud between two families reaches a boiling point when a young woman is caught in the crossfire. As tensions rise and blood is shed, a relentless pursuit for justice ensues, leading to unexpected alliances and devastating consequences. Set against the backdrop of the rugged Cumberland region, this gripping tale explores the depths of revenge, loyalty, and the power of family bonds.

Genres: Western, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Feuds, Revenge, Family, Justice
