Synopsis: In 'The Seaboard Parish Volume 2' by George MacDonald, the captivating sequel to the first volume, we return to the idyllic coastal village of Portlossie. As the lives of the villagers intertwine, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and unexpected events unfold. Amidst the beauty of the Scottish landscape, the characters navigate through love, loss, and the complexities of human nature. With MacDonald's signature blend of vivid storytelling and profound insights, this volume takes readers on a journey that will leave them eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what lies ahead for the inhabitants of Portlossie.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and relationships, Secrets and revelations, Redemption, Community dynamics
