Synopsis: 

In 'A Short History of Germany' by Mary Platt Parmele, embark on a captivating journey through the tumultuous and fascinating history of Germany. From the ancient tribes that inhabited the land to the rise and fall of empires, this concise yet comprehensive account unveils the secrets and struggles that have shaped the nation. Discover the legendary figures who left an indelible mark on Germany, and witness the pivotal moments that defined its destiny. From the Holy Roman Empire to the devastating World Wars, this gripping narrative explores the triumphs and tragedies that have made Germany one of the most influential countries in the world.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: National identity, Power and politics, Cultural evolution
