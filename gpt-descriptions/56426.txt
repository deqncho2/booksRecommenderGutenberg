Synopsis: In 'The Mummy! A Tale of the Twenty-Second Century', a young man named Edmund is transported to a future world where he encounters a mysterious mummy named Cheops. As Edmund navigates this futuristic society, he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the quest for immortality. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, Edmund must uncover the secrets of the mummy and confront his own destiny.

Genres: Science Fiction, Gothic Fiction, Romance

Themes: Time travel, societal change, love and desire, the pursuit of eternal life
