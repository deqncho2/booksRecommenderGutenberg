Synopsis: 
"The Poems and Fragments of Catullus: Translated in the Metres of the Original" is a captivating collection of poetry by the renowned Roman poet Catullus, skillfully translated into the original meters. This anthology takes readers on a journey through the passionate and often scandalous world of Catullus, exploring themes of love, desire, friendship, and the complexities of human emotions. With its lyrical beauty and raw honesty, this collection offers a glimpse into the life and mind of one of the greatest poets of ancient Rome.

Genres: Poetry, Classical Literature, Translation

Themes: Love, Desire, Friendship, Human Emotions
