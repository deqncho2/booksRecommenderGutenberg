Synopsis: 
In "Cynthia's Chauffeur" by Tracy, Louis, a chance encounter between a wealthy socialite and a mysterious chauffeur sets off a chain of events that will challenge their beliefs, ignite forbidden desires, and lead them down a dangerous path. As secrets unravel and hidden agendas come to light, Cynthia and her chauffeur find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, passion, and betrayal. With each twist and turn, the boundaries of trust and loyalty are tested, leaving them questioning everything they thought they knew about love and power.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Forbidden love, Deception, Power dynamics
