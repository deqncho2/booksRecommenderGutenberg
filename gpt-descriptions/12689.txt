Synopsis: 
In 'The High School Freshmen; or, Dick & Co.'s First Year Pranks and Sports', follow the mischievous adventures of Dick Prescott and his friends as they navigate their first year of high school. From clever pranks to thrilling sports competitions, this group of freshmen is determined to make their mark. But as they push the boundaries of fun and friendship, they soon find themselves facing unexpected challenges that test their loyalty and resilience. Will they be able to overcome these obstacles and emerge victorious? Find out in this exciting tale of friendship, rivalry, and the pursuit of adventure.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Resilience
