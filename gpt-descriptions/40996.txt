Synopsis: In 'In White Raiment' by William Le Queux, a young woman named Muriel Chetwynd finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she inherits a grand estate from a distant relative. As she delves into the secrets of the mansion, she uncovers a hidden world of espionage, betrayal, and forbidden love. With her life at stake, Muriel must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit and treachery, unsure of who to trust. Will she uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, Espionage, Love and trust
