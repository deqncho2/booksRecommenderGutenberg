Synopsis:
In the second volume of Les Misérables, titled "Cosette," Victor Hugo delves into the life of the young girl who has captured the hearts of readers since the beginning. As the story unfolds, we witness the transformation of Cosette from a mistreated child to a blossoming young woman. Amidst the backdrop of post-revolutionary France, her journey intertwines with the lives of various characters, each with their own struggles and desires. From the bustling streets of Paris to the tranquil countryside, Cosette's path is filled with love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of redemption. This volume of Les Misérables offers a captivating exploration of the human spirit and the power of hope.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love, Redemption, Social injustice, Sacrifice
