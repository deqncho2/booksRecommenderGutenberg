Synopsis: 
'The Complete Cynic: Being Bunches of Wisdom Culled from the Calendars of Oliver Herford, Ethel Watts Mumford, Addison Mizner' is a collection of witty and thought-provoking quotes and aphorisms from three renowned authors. This compilation offers a delightful exploration of the human condition, revealing the absurdities and contradictions of life through clever observations and biting humor. From love and friendship to society and politics, these cynically humorous insights will entertain and challenge readers to question the world around them.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Non-fiction

Themes: Human nature, Society, Irony
