Synopsis: In 'Frenzied Finance, Vol. 1: The Crime of Amalgamated', Thomas William Lawson takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of Wall Street in the late 19th century. The story follows the ambitious and cunning protagonist, Amalgamated, as he rises to power in the world of high finance, using every means necessary to achieve his goals. As the plot unfolds, secrets, scandals, and shocking revelations emerge, exposing the corrupt practices and manipulation that plagued the era. With its intricate web of deceit, power struggles, and financial intrigue, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Greed, Corruption, Power, Deception
