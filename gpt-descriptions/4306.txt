Synopsis: In 'Veranilda' by George Gissing, a young nobleman named Basil embarks on a perilous journey across Italy in search of his beloved Veranilda, who has been taken captive. As he navigates through a world of political intrigue, religious conflict, and personal sacrifice, Basil's determination is tested, and he must confront his own inner demons. Set against the backdrop of the Gothic era, this captivating tale explores the lengths one will go to for love and the transformative power of faith.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and devotion, Political and religious turmoil, Personal growth and redemption
