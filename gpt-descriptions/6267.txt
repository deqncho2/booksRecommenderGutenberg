Synopsis: In 'The Weavers: a tale of England and Egypt of fifty years ago - Complete', Gilbert Parker takes readers on a captivating journey through the intertwining lives of two families, one in England and the other in Egypt, during a tumultuous period fifty years ago. As the story unfolds, secrets, love, and betrayal come to light, revealing the complex web of relationships that connect these two distant lands. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Parker paints a vivid picture of the contrasting worlds of England and Egypt, and the challenges faced by those caught between tradition and progress.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Cultural clash, Family dynamics, Love and betrayal, Tradition vs. modernity
