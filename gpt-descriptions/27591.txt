Synopsis: In 'The Birthright' by Joseph Hocking, a young man named David embarks on a journey that takes him from the bustling streets of London to the rugged landscapes of Cornwall. Drawn by a mysterious inheritance, David finds himself entangled in a web of family secrets, hidden treasures, and a love that defies all odds. As he delves deeper into his birthright, David must confront his own identity and make choices that will shape his future. With each twist and turn, the story unravels a captivating tale of adventure, romance, and the power of destiny.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Fate
