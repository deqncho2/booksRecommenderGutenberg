Synopsis: In 'Blood Royal: A Novel' by Allen Grant, a gripping tale of mystery and intrigue unfolds as a young woman finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception within the royal court. As she delves deeper into the hidden history of her family, she uncovers shocking truths that threaten to unravel the very fabric of the monarchy. With danger lurking at every turn, she must navigate a treacherous path to protect her loved ones and preserve the legacy of her bloodline.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Royalty, Family Secrets, Betrayal, Power Struggles
