Synopsis: In 'Boating' by Walter Bradford Woodgate, a group of friends embarks on a seemingly innocent boating trip that quickly turns into a thrilling and suspenseful adventure. As they navigate through treacherous waters and encounter unexpected challenges, the characters are forced to confront their deepest fears and make life-altering decisions. With each twist and turn, the story keeps readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what will happen next and how the characters will survive the perils that await them.

Genres: Adventure, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Facing fears
