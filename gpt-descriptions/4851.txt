Synopsis: In 'History of the United Netherlands, 1587a', John Lothrop Motley takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events that shaped the United Netherlands during the late 16th century. From political intrigue and religious conflicts to epic battles and diplomatic maneuvering, this gripping historical account unveils the struggles and triumphs of a nation fighting for its independence. Motley's vivid storytelling and meticulous research bring to life the key figures and events that shaped this pivotal period in European history, leaving readers enthralled by the bravery, cunning, and resilience of the Dutch people.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Independence and self-determination, Religious conflict, Diplomacy and international relations
