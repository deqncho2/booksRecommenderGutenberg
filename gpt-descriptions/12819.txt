Synopsis: 

In 'Dick Prescott's Second Year at West Point: Or, Finding the Glory of the Soldier's Life', we follow the thrilling journey of Dick Prescott as he continues his education and training at the prestigious West Point Military Academy. As he faces new challenges, forms lasting friendships, and navigates the complexities of military life, Dick's determination and courage are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover what lies ahead for Dick Prescott.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-Age, Military Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Honor, Duty
