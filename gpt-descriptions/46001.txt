Synopsis: In 'A Parody on Iolanthe' by Dalziel, a whimsical and hilarious twist is given to the classic Gilbert and Sullivan operetta. Set in a fantastical world of fairies, mortals, and political satire, the story follows the misadventures of a group of bumbling politicians who find themselves entangled in a magical realm. As they navigate the absurdities of love, power, and the clash between the supernatural and the mundane, the characters must confront their own flaws and prejudices. With witty dialogue, clever wordplay, and a delightful blend of fantasy and farce, this parody offers a fresh and entertaining take on a beloved theatrical masterpiece.

Genres: Parody, Comedy, Fantasy

Themes: Politics, Love, Power, Prejudice
