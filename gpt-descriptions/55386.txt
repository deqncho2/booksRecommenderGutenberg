Synopsis: In 'The Girl Scouts on the Ranch' by Lavell, Edith, a group of adventurous Girl Scouts embark on a thrilling journey to a ranch, where they encounter unexpected challenges and forge lifelong friendships. As they navigate the rugged terrain and face various obstacles, the girls learn valuable lessons about teamwork, resilience, and the power of self-discovery. With each new adventure, they uncover hidden strengths within themselves and discover the true meaning of sisterhood. Will they be able to overcome the obstacles and make their time on the ranch an unforgettable experience?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Teamwork, Resilience, Self-discovery, Sisterhood
