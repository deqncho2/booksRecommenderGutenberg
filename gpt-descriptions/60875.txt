Synopsis: In 'The Straits Impregnable' by Sydney Loch, a young journalist finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and danger as he investigates a mysterious disappearance in the treacherous waters of the Straits of Gibraltar. With each clue he uncovers, he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where nothing is as it seems and the truth may cost him everything.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Courage
