Synopsis: 

'A Select Collection of Old English Plays: Originally Published by Robert Dodsley in the year 1744' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Old English drama. This collection features a diverse range of plays, each offering a unique glimpse into the vibrant theatrical landscape of the past. From tragic tales of love and betrayal to uproarious comedies filled with wit and mischief, these plays transport readers to a bygone era, immersing them in the timeless beauty of the English stage. 

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Tragedy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Morality, Social Class, Human Nature
