Synopsis: In 'Rogers' Rock, Lake George, March 13, 1758: A Battle Fought on Snow Shoes', Mary Cochrane Rogers takes us on a thrilling journey through a little-known battle that took place during the French and Indian War. Set against the backdrop of the snowy wilderness, this gripping account follows the courageous soldiers who fought on snowshoes, navigating treacherous terrain and facing unexpected challenges. As the battle unfolds, alliances are tested, loyalties are questioned, and the true nature of heroism is revealed. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Rogers brings this forgotten chapter of history to life, immersing readers in the heart-pounding action and the indomitable spirit of those who fought.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival
