Synopsis: In 'The Boy Allies with Pershing in France; Or, Over the Top at Chateau Thierry', author Clair W. Hayes takes readers on an exhilarating journey alongside two young American boys who find themselves thrust into the heart of World War I. As they join General Pershing's forces in France, they must navigate the treacherous battlefields, face the horrors of war, and prove their bravery in the face of adversity. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must rely on their wit, courage, and unwavering friendship to survive and make a difference in the war-torn landscape.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Coming of Age, Patriotism
