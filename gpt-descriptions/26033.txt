Synopsis: In 'How I Know God Answers Prayer: The Personal Testimony of One Life-Time', Rosalind Goforth takes readers on a captivating journey through her extraordinary life, filled with trials, triumphs, and unwavering faith. From her early years as a missionary in China to the heart-wrenching challenges she faced, Goforth's powerful testimony reveals the miraculous ways in which God answers prayers and transforms lives. With heartfelt anecdotes and profound insights, this book offers a compelling exploration of the profound connection between prayer and divine intervention.

Genres: Autobiography, Christian Literature, Inspirational

Themes: Faith, Prayer, Divine Intervention, Testimony
