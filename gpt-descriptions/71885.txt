Synopsis: In 'Elements of Metaphysics', A. E. Taylor takes readers on a captivating journey through the fundamental questions of existence and reality. With a blend of philosophical insight and intellectual curiosity, Taylor explores the nature of being, the relationship between mind and matter, and the mysteries of time and space. Through thought-provoking arguments and engaging examples, he challenges readers to question their assumptions and delve into the depths of metaphysical inquiry. As the pages unfold, Taylor's exploration of these profound concepts unveils a world of wonder and invites readers to ponder the very essence of their own existence.

Genres: Philosophy, Metaphysics

Themes: Existence, Reality, Mind and Matter, Time and Space
