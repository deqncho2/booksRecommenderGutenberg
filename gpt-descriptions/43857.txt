Synopsis: 
Discover a hidden treasure trove of knowledge in the 'Catalogue of books on philately in the Public Library of the city of Boston'. Delve into the fascinating world of stamp collecting as the Boston Philatelic Society and Boston Public Library unveil an extensive collection of books on this captivating hobby. From the origins of postage stamps to the artistry behind their designs, this catalogue offers a comprehensive guide for both beginners and seasoned collectors. Explore the pages and unlock the secrets of philately, as you embark on a journey that will ignite your curiosity and deepen your appreciation for this timeless pastime.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Hobbies

Themes: Philately, Stamp collecting, History of postage, Artistry in stamp design
