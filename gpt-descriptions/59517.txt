Synopsis: In 'Sink or Swim; or, Harry Raymond's Resolve' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Harry Raymond, a young man who finds himself facing numerous challenges and setbacks. Determined to overcome his circumstances, Harry embarks on a journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As he navigates through the trials of life, Harry's resilience and determination are put to the test, leading him to discover the true meaning of perseverance and the power of self-belief. Will Harry be able to rise above his circumstances and achieve his dreams, or will he be swallowed by the currents of adversity?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Self-belief, Overcoming adversity
