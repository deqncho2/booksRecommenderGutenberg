Synopsis: In 'The Sun Maid: A Story of Fort Dearborn', Raymond Evelyn takes readers on a captivating journey to the early 19th century, where the lives of two young women, one Native American and one settler, become intertwined amidst the backdrop of the historic Fort Dearborn. As tensions rise between the Native tribes and the settlers, the Sun Maid and her newfound friend must navigate the complexities of loyalty, love, and survival in a world on the brink of change.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Cultural clash, Friendship, Identity, Resilience
