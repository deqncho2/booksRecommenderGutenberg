Synopsis: 
In 'Publisher's Advertising (1872)', a young and ambitious publisher finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a cryptic advertisement in a local newspaper. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic world of advertising, he uncovers a hidden network of secrets, rivalries, and unexpected alliances. With each clue he unravels, the publisher becomes more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means risking everything he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Power dynamics
