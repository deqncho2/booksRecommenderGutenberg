Synopsis: In "Tom Swift and his great oil gusher: $b or, The treasure of Goby Farm" by Appleton, Victor, young inventor Tom Swift finds himself embarking on a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure while searching for oil. As he races against time to uncover the secrets of Goby Farm, Tom must navigate treacherous obstacles and outsmart cunning adversaries. With his ingenious inventions and unwavering determination, Tom Swift sets out to unlock the mysteries that lie beneath the surface, leading to an exhilarating climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Invention and innovation, Exploration and discovery
