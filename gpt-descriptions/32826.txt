Synopsis: In 'Fairfax and His Pride: A Novel' by Marie Van Vorst, readers are transported to the glamorous world of New York City's high society in the early 20th century. The story revolves around the enigmatic and charismatic protagonist, Fairfax, whose pride and ambition drive him to navigate the intricate web of social expectations, love affairs, and personal sacrifices. As Fairfax rises to prominence, he must confront the consequences of his choices and the true nature of his pride. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this novel explores the complexities of human desires and the pursuit of happiness in a world defined by wealth and status.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Pride, Social Expectations, Love, Sacrifice
