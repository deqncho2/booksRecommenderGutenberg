Synopsis: In 'The Crime Against Europe: A Possible Outcome of the War of 1914', Casement, Roger presents a gripping account of a potential consequence of the devastating war that engulfed Europe. Through a combination of historical analysis and personal experiences, Casement delves into the dark underbelly of war, revealing a shocking crime that threatens to tear the continent apart. As the narrative unfolds, the reader is drawn into a web of intrigue and suspense, as Casement uncovers the truth behind this heinous act. With each page, the tension mounts, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to discover the outcome of this gripping tale.

Genres: Historical fiction, Thriller, Political commentary

Themes: War, Crime, Betrayal, Consequences of actions
