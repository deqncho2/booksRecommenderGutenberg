Synopsis: 

From Makin to Bougainville: Marine Raiders in the Pacific War takes readers on a gripping journey through the intense and harrowing experiences of the Marine Raiders during World War II. Author Jon T. Hoffman delves into the untold stories of these elite warriors as they fought against the Japanese forces in the Pacific theater. From their daring amphibious assaults to their relentless guerrilla warfare tactics, the Marine Raiders' bravery and resilience are vividly portrayed in this captivating narrative. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History, War Memoir

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Resilience, Warfare Strategies
