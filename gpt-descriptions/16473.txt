Synopsis: In 'Queen Hildegarde', Laura Elizabeth Howe Richards takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a young girl named Hildegarde, who unexpectedly finds herself thrust into a world of royalty and intrigue. As Hildegarde navigates the challenges of her new role, she discovers hidden secrets, forms unlikely friendships, and learns the true meaning of courage and loyalty. With its delightful blend of adventure, mystery, and heartwarming moments, 'Queen Hildegarde' is a captivating tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Coming of Age, Identity, Friendship, Courage
