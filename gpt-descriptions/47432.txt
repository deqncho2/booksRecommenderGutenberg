Synopsis: In 'Babylon, Volume 2' by Allen Grant, the captivating story continues as the ambitious protagonist, Felix Babylon, finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the ancient city, he uncovers hidden treasures, encounters enigmatic characters, and faces unforeseen challenges that test his wit and courage. With each step closer to unraveling the truth, Babylon's journey becomes more perilous, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Exploration, Secrets and Intrigue
