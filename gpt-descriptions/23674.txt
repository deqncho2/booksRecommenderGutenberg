Synopsis: In "Swept Out to Sea; Or, Clint Webb Among the Whalers," young Clint Webb finds himself embarking on a thrilling adventure aboard a whaling ship. As he navigates the treacherous waters and encounters the dangers of the deep, Clint must prove his mettle and face unexpected challenges. With vivid descriptions of life at sea and the camaraderie among the crew, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies beyond the horizon.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Survival, Exploration
