Synopsis: In 'The Maid of Honour: A Tale of the Dark Days of France', the reader is transported to the tumultuous era of the French Revolution. Amidst the chaos and violence, we follow the journey of a young woman named Marie, who finds herself caught between loyalty to her family and her own desires. As Marie navigates the treacherous political landscape, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayals, and forbidden love. With the fate of her loved ones hanging in the balance, Marie must summon the strength to make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and the future of France.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Love, Sacrifice, Political Turmoil
