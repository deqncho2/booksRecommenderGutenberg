Synopsis: 

In 'Essentials in Church History', Joseph Fielding Smith takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, from its humble beginnings with the birth of Joseph Smith to the present time in 1922. With introductory chapters that explore the antiquity of the gospel and the concept of the "falling away," Smith provides a comprehensive account of the Church's development, struggles, and triumphs.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Religious

Themes: Religious restoration, Divine guidance, Persecution and resilience, Growth and expansion
