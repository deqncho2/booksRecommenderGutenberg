Synopsis: In 'Creation or Evolution? A Philosophical Inquiry', George Ticknor Curtis takes readers on a thought-provoking journey that explores the age-old debate between creationism and evolution. Through a philosophical lens, Curtis delves into the fundamental questions surrounding the origins of life, the nature of existence, and the role of science and religion in shaping our understanding of the world. As he navigates through various arguments and counterarguments, the author challenges readers to critically examine their own beliefs and consider the implications of each perspective. With a balanced and insightful approach, 'Creation or Evolution? A Philosophical Inquiry' offers a captivating exploration of one of humanity's most enduring debates.

Genres: Philosophy, Science, Religion

Themes: Origins of life, Science vs. Religion, Nature of existence, Epistemology
