Synopsis: In "Indian Conjuring" by Branson, L. H., a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds a mysterious power. As he delves deeper into its origins, he becomes entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and dark secrets. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and illusion blurs, leaving him questioning his own sanity. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he must confront his deepest fears and confront the ancient forces that threaten to consume him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Ancient Mysteries, Psychological Suspense, Supernatural Powers
