Synopsis: In 'A Coffin for Jacob' by Edward W. Ludwig, a small town is shaken by the mysterious death of a beloved resident, Jacob. As the community mourns, a determined detective begins to uncover a web of secrets and lies that lead to unexpected revelations. With each clue, the detective delves deeper into the dark underbelly of the town, where nothing is as it seems. As the truth slowly unravels, the detective must confront the shocking reality that even in the most idyllic places, darkness can lurk beneath the surface.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town intrigue, Uncovering the truth
