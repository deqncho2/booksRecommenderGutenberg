Synopsis: 
In 'His Honour, and a Lady' by Sara Jeannette Duncan, a young woman named Lady Jane finds herself caught in a web of scandal and intrigue when she becomes entangled with a prominent judge. As their relationship deepens, Lady Jane must navigate the complexities of love, power, and societal expectations, all while trying to protect her own reputation. Set against the backdrop of late 19th-century England, this captivating novel explores the consequences of forbidden love and the lengths one will go to preserve their honour.

Genres: 
Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: 
Forbidden love, Reputation, Power dynamics
