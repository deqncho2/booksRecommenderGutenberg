Synopsis: 

In 'In Case of Fire' by Randall Garrett, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious fire breaks out in a wealthy industrialist's mansion. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that threaten to consume him. With time running out and the flames of danger closing in, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects to uncover the truth before everything goes up in smoke.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Justice
