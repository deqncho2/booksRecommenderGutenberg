Synopsis: In 'The Ebb-Tide: A Trio And Quartette', a group of desperate men find themselves stranded on a remote island in the South Pacific. As they struggle to survive, they become entangled in a web of greed, betrayal, and moral dilemmas. With their pasts haunting them and their futures uncertain, the characters must navigate treacherous waters both literally and metaphorically. Will they succumb to their darkest instincts or find redemption in the face of adversity?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Greed, Morality, Redemption, Survival
