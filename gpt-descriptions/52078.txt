Synopsis: In 'The Captain of the Guard' by James Grant, a seasoned military officer finds himself torn between duty and loyalty when he is assigned to protect a controversial political figure. As he delves deeper into the dangerous world of politics, he must navigate treacherous alliances, uncover hidden agendas, and confront his own moral compass. With high-stakes action, political intrigue, and complex characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Military Drama

Themes: Duty vs. Loyalty, Power and Corruption, Moral Dilemmas
