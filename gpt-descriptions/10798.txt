Synopsis: 
'Paths of Glory: Impressions of War Written at and Near the Front' by Irvin S. Cobb takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of soldiers on the front lines during World War I. Through vivid and poignant storytelling, Cobb provides a firsthand account of the brutal realities of war, capturing the camaraderie, courage, and sacrifice of those who fought. From the trenches to the battlefields, this powerful narrative offers a unique perspective on the human spirit in the face of unimaginable adversity.

Genres: War memoir, Historical non-fiction

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Human resilience
