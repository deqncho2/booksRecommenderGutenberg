Synopsis: In 'Lightning, Thunder and Lightning Conductors' by Gerald Molloy, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious lightning strikes that seem to defy all scientific explanation. As the townspeople grapple with the unexplained phenomena, a young scientist named Emma embarks on a quest to uncover the truth behind the lightning and protect her community from its destructive power. Along the way, she encounters a cast of eccentric characters, uncovers long-held secrets, and delves into the realms of science and mythology. With each new discovery, Emma finds herself drawn deeper into a world where reality and the supernatural intertwine, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Science Fiction

Themes: Science vs. Superstition, Power of Nature, Quest for Knowledge
