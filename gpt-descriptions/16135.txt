Synopsis: In 'Safe Marriage: A Return to Sanity', Ettie Annie Rout takes readers on a captivating journey through the complexities of modern relationships. With a unique blend of wit, wisdom, and practical advice, Rout explores the challenges faced by couples in an ever-changing world. From communication breakdowns to societal pressures, she delves into the secrets of maintaining a strong and fulfilling marriage. As readers navigate the pages, they will discover unexpected twists and turns that challenge conventional wisdom, leaving them eager to uncover the secrets to a truly safe and harmonious union.

Genres: Self-help, Relationships, Non-fiction

Themes: Marriage, Communication, Intimacy, Societal Expectations
