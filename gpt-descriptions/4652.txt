Synopsis: 
'Escape, and Other Essays' by Arthur Christopher Benson is a captivating collection of thought-provoking essays that explore the concept of escape in various forms. From the allure of travel to the power of imagination, Benson delves into the human desire to break free from the constraints of everyday life. Through his eloquent prose, he takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and invites them to contemplate the different ways in which we seek liberation. Whether it be through literature, nature, or the pursuit of dreams, Benson's essays offer a profound reflection on the universal longing for escape.

Genres: 
Essay, Non-fiction, Philosophy

Themes: 
Escape, Freedom, Imagination, Self-discovery
