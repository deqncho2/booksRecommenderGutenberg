Synopsis: In 'Henry IV, Makers of History' by John S. C. Abbott, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of England's most intriguing monarchs. From his tumultuous rise to power to his struggles to maintain control, this gripping historical account delves into the complex world of politics, power, and personal ambition. As Henry IV navigates treacherous alliances, faces rebellions, and battles his own inner demons, readers will be enthralled by the captivating narrative that brings this fascinating period of history to life.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and politics, Ambition, Loyalty, Leadership
