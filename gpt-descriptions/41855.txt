Synopsis: In the midst of World War I, a young American woman finds herself caught up in the chaos and danger of the conflict as she travels through war-torn France. As she witnesses the devastation and meets individuals from all walks of life, she is forced to confront her own beliefs and desires, ultimately discovering the true meaning of courage and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical fiction, war fiction, romance

Themes: War and its impact on individuals and society, the search for identity and purpose, the power of love and human connection
