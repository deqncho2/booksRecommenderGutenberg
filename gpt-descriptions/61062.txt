Synopsis: 

"The Great Revival of the Eighteenth Century: With a supplemental chapter on the revival in America" by Edwin Paxton Hood takes readers on a captivating journey through one of the most transformative periods in history. This enlightening work delves into the profound religious revival that swept across Europe and America during the eighteenth century, exploring its origins, impact, and the remarkable individuals who played pivotal roles in this spiritual awakening. With a supplemental chapter dedicated to the revival in America, Hood provides a comprehensive account of this extraordinary movement that forever changed the course of Christianity.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Religious studies, Biography

Themes: Religious revival, Spiritual awakening, Historical transformation
