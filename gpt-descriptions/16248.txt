Synopsis: 
'The Opera: A Sketch of the Development of Opera. With full Descriptions of all Works in the Modern Repertory.' takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of opera, providing detailed descriptions of all the significant works in the modern repertory. From the early origins of opera to the grand productions of the present day, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the art form's development, highlighting the key composers, librettists, and performers who have shaped its history. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, it delves into the enchanting world of opera, offering a glimpse into the magic and drama that unfolds on stage.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, History

Themes: Evolution of art, Cultural significance, Impact of composers and performers
