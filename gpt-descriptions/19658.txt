Synopsis: In 'The Judgment of Eve' by Sinclair, May, a young woman named Eve finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies when she becomes entangled with a mysterious stranger. As Eve delves deeper into the stranger's world, she uncovers shocking truths that challenge her perception of reality and force her to confront her own dark past. With danger lurking at every turn, Eve must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and ultimately face the judgment that awaits her.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Redemption
