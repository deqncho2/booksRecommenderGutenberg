Synopsis: In 'Technology and Books for All' by Marie Lebert, embark on a captivating journey that explores the fascinating intersection between technology and literature. Delve into the world of e-books, digital libraries, and the ever-evolving landscape of reading in the digital age. Discover how technology has revolutionized the way we consume and interact with books, while also uncovering the enduring power of traditional printed works. With insightful anecdotes and thought-provoking analysis, Lebert invites readers to ponder the future of reading and the profound impact of technology on our literary experiences.

Genres: Non-fiction, Technology, Literature

Themes: Digital revolution, Reading habits, Technological advancements
