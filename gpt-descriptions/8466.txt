Synopsis: 
'Musa Pedestris - Three Centuries of Canting Songs and Slang Rhymes [1536 - 1896]' by John Stephen Farmer is a captivating exploration of the secret language of the streets. This comprehensive collection of canting songs and slang rhymes takes readers on a journey through three centuries, revealing the hidden world of thieves, beggars, and other colorful characters. From the Elizabethan era to the Victorian era, this book unveils the rich tapestry of language used by those on the fringes of society, offering a fascinating glimpse into their lives and subcultures. 

Genres: 
Historical, Linguistics, Cultural Studies

Themes: 
Language and Communication, Social Marginalization, Subcultures
