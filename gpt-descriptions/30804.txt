Synopsis: In "Applied Design for Printers: A Handbook of the Principles of Arrangement, with Brief Comment on the Periods of Design Which Have Most Strongly Influenced Printing," Harry Lawrence Gage takes readers on a captivating journey through the art of design in printing. From the intricate details of layout and composition to the historical influences that have shaped the field, Gage's handbook offers a comprehensive guide for printers seeking to create visually stunning and impactful works. With insightful commentary and practical advice, this book is a must-read for anyone interested in the artistry and craftsmanship of print design.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Design

Themes: Design principles, Historical influences, Visual communication
