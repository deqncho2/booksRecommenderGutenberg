Synopsis: 

'Old and New Favorites: Crochet Designs' by Spool Cotton Co. is a captivating collection of crochet patterns that have become nation-wide favorites. This book features a variety of designs for tablecloths, bedspreads, luncheon sets, chair sets, doilies, and more. Whether you are a seasoned crocheter or a beginner, this book offers a range of patterns that will inspire and delight. From timeless classics to modern creations, 'Old and New Favorites' is a treasure trove of crochet designs that have been repeated by request across the country.

Genres: Crafts, Hobbies, Non-fiction

Themes: Crochet, Home Decor, Handicrafts
