Synopsis: In "English as she is spoke; or, a jest in sober earnest," Carolino and Fonseca present a hilariously misguided English phrasebook that is sure to leave readers in stitches. This unconventional guide to the English language is filled with comically incorrect translations and absurd phrases, resulting in a delightful and entertaining read. As readers navigate through the book's unintentional humor, they will encounter a plethora of linguistic mishaps that will keep them laughing from start to finish.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Linguistics

Themes: Language and communication, Cultural misunderstandings
