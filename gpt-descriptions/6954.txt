Synopsis: In the small town of Aikenside, a mysterious newcomer named Dr. Alan Grant arrives, captivating the townspeople with his enigmatic charm and unconventional methods. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the locals, secrets begin to unravel, and the true nature of Dr. Grant's intentions becomes increasingly unclear. With each twist and turn, the residents of Aikenside find themselves questioning their own beliefs and the limits of trust, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Trust, Identity, Morality
