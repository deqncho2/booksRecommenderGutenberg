Synopsis: In "Alida; or, Miscellaneous Sketches of Incidents During the Late American War.: Founded on Fact," Amelia Stratton Comfield takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of the American War. Through a series of intriguing sketches, the author introduces us to Alida, a young woman whose life is forever changed by the events of the war. From the bustling streets of New York City to the battlefields of the South, Alida's experiences offer a unique perspective on the human cost of war and the resilience of the human spirit. As the narrative unfolds, readers are drawn into a world of love, loss, and the indomitable will to survive.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Romance

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, Love and resilience in the face of adversity
