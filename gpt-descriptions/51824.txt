Synopsis: In 'World in a Bottle' by Lang, Allen Kim, a brilliant scientist creates a miniature world contained within a glass bottle. As the inhabitants of this tiny world face their own struggles and triumphs, the scientist becomes increasingly obsessed with their existence. However, when unforeseen consequences arise, he must confront the ethical implications of playing god. This thought-provoking tale explores the boundaries of science, morality, and the power of creation.

Genres: Science Fiction, Speculative Fiction, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Ethics, Creation, Morality, Power
