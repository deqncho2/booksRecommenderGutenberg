Synopsis: In 'Ragnarok: The Age of Fire and Gravel', Donnelly takes readers on a captivating journey through ancient myths, geological mysteries, and cataclysmic events that have shaped our planet. Blending scientific research with imaginative storytelling, he explores the possibility of a forgotten catastrophe that once reshaped the Earth's surface. As he delves into the evidence and theories surrounding this cataclysm, Donnelly uncovers a hidden history that challenges our understanding of the world and its future.

Genres: Science Fiction, Historical Fiction, Mythology

Themes: Catastrophe, Ancient Myths, Geological Mysteries, Human History, Scientific Inquiry
