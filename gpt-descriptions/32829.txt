Synopsis: 
Step back in time to the vibrant city of Norwich in 1811, as Christopher Berry takes you on a captivating journey through its rich history and bustling streets. From the grandeur of its medieval cathedral to the lively markets and thriving industries, this concise history and directory offers a fascinating glimpse into the daily lives of the city's inhabitants. Discover the influential figures, the hidden corners, and the remarkable stories that shaped Norwich into the vibrant city it is today.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Local History, Directory

Themes: Urban Development, Social History, Cultural Heritage
