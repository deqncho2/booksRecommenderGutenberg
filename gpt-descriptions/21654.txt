Synopsis: In and Around Berlin takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and tumultuous city of Berlin, as seen through the eyes of the enigmatic protagonist, Minerva Brace. From the bustling streets to the hidden corners, Minerva's encounters with a diverse cast of characters and her exploration of the city's rich history and culture intertwine to create a tapestry of intrigue, mystery, and self-discovery. As Minerva delves deeper into the secrets of Berlin, she finds herself entangled in a web of personal and political complexities that will challenge her perceptions and force her to confront her own past.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Memory, History, Personal Growth
