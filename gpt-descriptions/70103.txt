Synopsis:
Set during the tumultuous period of the Vendean War, "Chantemerle: A Romance of the Vendean War" takes readers on a captivating journey through love, loyalty, and the resilience of the human spirit. As the conflict rages on, a young woman named Chantemerle finds herself torn between her duty to her family and her growing affection for a dashing soldier. Amidst the chaos and danger, Chantemerle must navigate treacherous alliances, heartbreaking betrayals, and the ever-present threat of violence. Will she be able to protect her loved ones and find happiness amidst the chaos of war?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty, Resilience, Conflict and Betrayal
