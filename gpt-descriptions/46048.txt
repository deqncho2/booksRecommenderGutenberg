Synopsis: In 'The Turned-About Girls' by Dix, Beulah Marie, two young girls find themselves in a peculiar situation that turns their lives upside down. As they navigate through unexpected challenges and embark on a thrilling adventure, they discover hidden secrets and uncover the truth about their own identities. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the mysteries that lie within.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Self-discovery
