Synopsis: In "My Lady Peggy Goes to Town," the charming and vivacious Lady Peggy finds herself in the bustling city of New York, leaving behind her sheltered life in the countryside. As she navigates the glamorous social scene, she encounters a whirlwind of new experiences, unexpected friendships, and romantic entanglements. With her wit and charm, Lady Peggy must navigate the complexities of high society while staying true to herself and her values.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Comedy

Themes: Social class, Identity, Love and relationships
