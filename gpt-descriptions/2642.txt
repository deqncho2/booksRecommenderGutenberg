Synopsis: 
In 'Back Home' by Eugene Wood, a young woman named Emily returns to her childhood home after a decade of being away. As she navigates the familiar streets and encounters old friends, Emily is forced to confront the ghosts of her past and the secrets that have haunted her for years. With each step closer to the truth, she unravels a web of deception and betrayal that threatens to shatter her world once again. As the tension builds, Emily must decide whether to embrace the darkness or find the strength to forge a new path forward.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, Coming to terms with the past, Identity and self-discovery
