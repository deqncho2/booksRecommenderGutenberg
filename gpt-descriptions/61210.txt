Synopsis: 
In 'This Way to Christmas' by Ruth Sawyer, a young girl named Carol embarks on a magical journey through the snowy woods on Christmas Eve. Along the way, she encounters a cast of enchanting characters who help her discover the true meaning of Christmas. With each encounter, Carol's heart is filled with joy and wonder, as she learns valuable lessons about love, kindness, and the importance of family. Will Carol find her way back home in time for Christmas morning?

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Fantasy, Holiday

Themes: 
Magic and Wonder, Family and Togetherness, Kindness and Generosity
