Synopsis: 

'Fort Sumter: Anvil of War: Fort Sumter National Monument, South Carolina' by United States. National Park Service takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and significance of Fort Sumter. This iconic fort, located in South Carolina, witnessed the opening shots of the American Civil War and became a symbol of the nation's struggle for unity. Through vivid descriptions and historical accounts, the book explores the events leading up to the fateful battle at Fort Sumter, the intense conflict that unfolded, and the lasting impact it had on the course of American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, American history

Themes: Conflict and division, National identity, Historical preservation
