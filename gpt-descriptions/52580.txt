Synopsis: In 'Pearls & Parasites' by A. E. Shipley, a captivating tale unfolds as a young archaeologist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to an ancient secret. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she finds herself entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each step closer to the truth, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. Will she unravel the secret before it consumes her?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden knowledge, Betrayal and trust, Archaeology and ancient civilizations
