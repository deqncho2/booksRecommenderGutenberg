Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 138 May 1, 1915; The Traitors of the Tropics; or, Nick Carter's Royal Flush', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a treacherous web of deceit and danger as he investigates a mysterious group of traitors lurking in the tropical paradise. With his quick wit and unparalleled skills, Carter must navigate through a thrilling game of cat and mouse, where the stakes are high and the consequences deadly. Will he be able to uncover the truth and bring the traitors to justice before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Betrayal, Deception, Justice
