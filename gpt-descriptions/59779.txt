Synopsis: 

Step into the rich and tumultuous history of the Royal Irish Regiment as you embark on a captivating journey spanning over three centuries. From the regiment's inception in 1684 to its remarkable exploits in 1902, George Le Mesurier Gretton masterfully chronicles the campaigns and events that shaped this renowned military unit. Delve into the gripping tales of battles fought on distant shores, the bravery of soldiers in the face of adversity, and the enduring legacy of the Royal Irish Regiment. 

Genres: 
Historical Non-fiction, Military History, Biography

Themes: 
War and Conflict, National Identity, Courage and Resilience
