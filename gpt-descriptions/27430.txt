Synopsis: In "The Critical Period of American History," John Fiske takes readers on a captivating journey through a pivotal era in American history. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Fiske explores the challenges and triumphs that shaped the nation during its formative years. From the struggles of the Continental Congress to the creation of the Constitution, Fiske unveils the untold stories and lesser-known figures that played a crucial role in shaping the United States. As the nation teeters on the brink of collapse, readers will be enthralled by the dramatic events and political intrigues that unfold, leaving them eager to discover how America emerged from this critical period.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: American Revolution, Early American Politics, Nation Building
