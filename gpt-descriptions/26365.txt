Synopsis: 

'Intestinal Ills: Chronic Constipation, Indigestion, Autogenetic Poisons, Diarrhea, Piles, Etc. Also Auto-Infection, Auto-Intoxication, Anemia, Emaciation, Etc. Due to Proctitis and Colitis' delves into the intricate world of digestive health, exploring the various ailments that plague the human intestines. Alcinous B. Jamison takes readers on a journey through the complexities of chronic constipation, indigestion, autogenetic poisons, diarrhea, piles, and more. With a focus on proctitis and colitis, the book uncovers the hidden dangers of auto-infection, auto-intoxication, anemia, and emaciation. 

Genres: Medical literature, Health and wellness, Self-help

Themes: Digestive health, Disease prevention, Self-care, Medical treatments
