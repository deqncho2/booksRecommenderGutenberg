Synopsis: In "The Figure in the Carpet" by Henry James, a young literary critic becomes obsessed with uncovering the hidden meaning behind a renowned author's work. As he delves deeper into the author's mysterious intentions, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, leading him on a quest to discover the elusive "figure in the carpet." This gripping tale explores the blurred lines between reality and fiction, the pursuit of artistic truth, and the power of interpretation.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Fiction, Literary Fiction

Themes: Obsession, Artistic Interpretation, Truth and Deception
