Synopsis: In 'Disturbing Sun' by Richardson, Robert S., a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious events that seem to be connected to the appearance of a strange, glowing sun. As the townspeople grapple with their fears and suspicions, a young woman named Emma becomes determined to uncover the truth behind the disturbing phenomenon. With each revelation, Emma finds herself drawn deeper into a web of secrets and dark forces that threaten to consume her and the entire town. As the tension escalates, the line between reality and nightmare becomes blurred, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the ultimate truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Secrets, Supernatural forces
