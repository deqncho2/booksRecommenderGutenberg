Synopsis: 
In "Stephen A. Douglas: A Study in American Politics," Johnson Allen delves into the captivating life and political career of one of America's most influential figures. From his humble beginnings in Vermont to his rise as a prominent senator, Douglas's journey is a testament to his unwavering determination and political prowess. Allen skillfully explores the complex relationships and fierce debates that shaped Douglas's career, including his famous rivalry with Abraham Lincoln. As the narrative unfolds, readers are taken on a gripping journey through the tumultuous era of American politics, witnessing firsthand the profound impact Douglas had on the nation's history.

Genres: Biography, History, Political Science

Themes: Ambition, Power, Political Ideology, Leadership
