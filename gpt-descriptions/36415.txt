Synopsis: In 'Great Lent: A School of Repentance. Its Meaning for Orthodox Christians', Alexander Schmemann takes readers on a profound journey into the heart of the Orthodox Christian tradition during the season of Great Lent. With captivating insights and thought-provoking reflections, Schmemann explores the transformative power of repentance and the significance of this sacred period in the lives of Orthodox believers. Through engaging anecdotes and spiritual wisdom, he invites readers to delve into the depths of their souls and discover the true meaning of repentance, leading to a renewed understanding of their faith and a path towards spiritual growth.

Genres: Religious, Spiritual, Non-fiction

Themes: Repentance, Orthodox Christianity, Spiritual Growth
