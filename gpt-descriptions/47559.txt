Synopsis: 
"The Prude's Progress: A Comedy in Three Acts" follows the hilarious misadventures of a young woman named Prudence, who is known for her strict adherence to propriety and moral values. When Prudence finds herself in a series of compromising situations, she must navigate the complexities of love, scandal, and societal expectations. As she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, Prudence's prudishness is put to the test, leading to uproarious and unexpected consequences.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Farce

Themes: Morality, Social expectations, Self-discovery
