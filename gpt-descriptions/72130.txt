Synopsis: In 'Orphan Dinah' by Eden Phillpotts, a young girl named Dinah finds herself alone in the world after the sudden death of her parents. Sent to live with her strict and mysterious Aunt Rachel, Dinah soon discovers a hidden family secret that will change her life forever. As she unravels the truth behind her parents' untimely demise, Dinah embarks on a journey of self-discovery, facing unexpected challenges and encountering unlikely allies along the way. With danger lurking in the shadows, Dinah must summon her courage and resilience to uncover the truth and find her place in the world.

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Resilience
