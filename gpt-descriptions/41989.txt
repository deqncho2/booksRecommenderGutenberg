Synopsis: In 'The Starling: A Scottish Story' by Macleod, Norman, a young woman named Fiona finds herself drawn to a mysterious starling that appears in her garden. As she forms a unique bond with the bird, Fiona embarks on a journey of self-discovery, uncovering hidden secrets and confronting her own fears. Set against the backdrop of the enchanting Scottish countryside, this captivating tale explores the power of nature, the complexities of human relationships, and the transformative nature of love.

Genres: Fiction, Magical Realism, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Nature, Identity, Love, Self-Discovery
