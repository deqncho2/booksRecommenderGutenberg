Synopsis: 
In "Trees Every Child Should Know: Easy Tree Studies for All Seasons of the Year" by Julia Ellen Rogers, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of trees. This delightful book introduces various tree species and explores their unique characteristics, from the majestic oak to the delicate cherry blossom. With vivid descriptions and beautiful illustrations, Rogers brings these trees to life, revealing their importance in our ecosystem and their fascinating stories. Whether it's the vibrant colors of autumn or the blossoming of spring, this book is a celebration of nature's wonders that will leave readers with a newfound appreciation for the trees that surround us.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Education

Themes: Nature appreciation, Environmental conservation, Education
