Synopsis: In "The Criticism of the Fourth Gospel," Sanday presents a thought-provoking series of lectures that challenge the traditional understanding of the Gospel of John. With meticulous analysis and scholarly expertise, he delves into the historical context, authorship, and purpose of the text, unraveling its complexities and shedding new light on its interpretation. As he navigates through the intricacies of biblical criticism, Sanday invites readers on a captivating intellectual journey that will challenge their preconceived notions and deepen their understanding of this influential gospel.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biblical criticism, Theology

Themes: Biblical interpretation, Historical context, Authorship, Gospel of John
