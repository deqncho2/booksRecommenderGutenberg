Synopsis: In the grip of the Mullah: A tale of adventure in Somaliland follows the thrilling journey of a young British officer, Lieutenant Brereton, who finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous rebellion led by a powerful Mullah in the desolate lands of Somaliland. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes, encounters fierce tribes, and faces the wrath of the Mullah's followers, Brereton must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to survive and bring an end to the uprising. This gripping tale of adventure and survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of Lieutenant Brereton and the outcome of the rebellion.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Survival, Exploration, Colonialism
