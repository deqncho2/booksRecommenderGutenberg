Synopsis: 
'Among the Pines; or, South in Secession Time' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of the American Civil War. Set in the deep South, the story follows the lives of a diverse group of individuals who find themselves intertwined amidst the chaos and uncertainty of secession. As the war rages on, friendships are tested, loyalties are questioned, and love blossoms in unexpected places. With vivid descriptions of the Southern landscape and the emotional turmoil of its characters, this gripping tale explores the profound impact of war on both individuals and society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Love, Identity, Conflict
