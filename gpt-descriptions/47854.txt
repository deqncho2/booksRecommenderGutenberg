Synopsis: In 'Greenacre Girls' by Izola L. Forrester, a group of young women find themselves thrown together in a small, secluded boarding school. As they navigate the challenges of adolescence and form deep bonds of friendship, they uncover hidden secrets and face unexpected obstacles that will test their resilience and loyalty. With each girl harboring her own dreams and aspirations, they must learn to navigate the complexities of love, loss, and self-discovery in a world that often feels confining. 'Greenacre Girls' is a captivating coming-of-age tale that explores the power of friendship and the indomitable spirit of young women.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Resilience, Secrets, Adolescence
