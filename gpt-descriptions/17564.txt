Synopsis: In 'By the Light of the Soul: A Novel' by Mary Eleanor Wilkins Freeman, a young woman named Ruth inherits a mysterious family heirloom that holds a powerful secret. As she delves into her family's history, she uncovers a hidden world of supernatural forces and dark secrets that threaten to consume her. With the help of a charismatic stranger, Ruth must navigate a treacherous path to unlock the truth and protect her loved ones from the malevolent forces at play. This gripping tale of mystery and the supernatural will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Gothic

Themes: Family secrets, Supernatural forces, Identity
