Synopsis: In the dark and violent world of ancient Rome, a renowned general returns home from war, only to find himself entangled in a web of revenge, betrayal, and unspeakable horrors. As the body count rises and the boundaries of morality blur, Titus Andronicus becomes a chilling exploration of the depths of human depravity and the consequences of unchecked vengeance.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama, Revenge

Themes: Revenge, Violence, Power, Justice, Madness
