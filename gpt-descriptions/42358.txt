Synopsis: In 'Frank at Don Carlos' Rancho' by Castlemon, Harry, readers are taken on an exciting journey as Frank, a young adventurer, finds himself in the heart of the Wild West. When Frank arrives at Don Carlos' Rancho, he is thrust into a world of danger, mystery, and unexpected friendships. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters various challenges, Frank's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. With thrilling escapades and unexpected twists, 'Frank at Don Carlos' Rancho' is a captivating tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
