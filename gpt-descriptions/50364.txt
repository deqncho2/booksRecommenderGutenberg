Synopsis: 
"The Roman Wall" is a captivating historical and topographical account that takes readers on a journey along the barrier of the lower isthmus, stretching from the Tyne to the Solway. Through numerous personal surveys, the author, J. Collingwood Bruce, unveils the fascinating details of this ancient Roman wall, shedding light on its construction, purpose, and the lives of those who lived and guarded it. As readers delve into the pages of this descriptive narrative, they will be transported back in time, exploring the rich history and captivating landscapes that surround this remarkable structure.

Genres: 
Historical, Topographical, Descriptive

Themes: 
Roman history, Ancient architecture, Cultural heritage
