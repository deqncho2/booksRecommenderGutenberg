Synopsis:
'Ralph Clavering; Or, We Must Try Before We Can Do' by William Henry Giles Kingston is an enthralling adventure novel set in the 19th century. The story follows the young and ambitious Ralph Clavering as he embarks on a journey filled with danger, discovery, and personal growth. From facing treacherous seas to encountering mysterious lands, Ralph's determination and resilience are put to the test. As he navigates through various challenges, he learns valuable lessons about courage, friendship, and the importance of perseverance. Will Ralph's unwavering spirit lead him to triumph, or will he be consumed by the perils that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Friendship
