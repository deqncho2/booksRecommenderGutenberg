Synopsis: 

In 'Men of the Bible; Some Lesser-Known Characters', a team of renowned authors delves into the lives of intriguing figures from the Bible who often go unnoticed. From the lesser-known characters like Jephthah, Mephibosheth, and Onesimus, to the more familiar ones like Barnabas and Silas, this book uncovers the untold stories and hidden depths of these men. Through vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, the authors bring these characters to life, shedding light on their struggles, triumphs, and the profound impact they had on biblical history. 

Genres: Religious literature, Biblical studies, Biography

Themes: Faith and redemption, Overcoming adversity, Unseen heroes
