Synopsis:
In 'The Son of Monte-Cristo, Volume I' by Jules Lermina, a young man named Roland de Montrevel finds himself caught in a web of mystery and revenge. After discovering a hidden treasure, Roland's life takes a dramatic turn as he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and deception. As he navigates through a world of secret societies, political intrigue, and forbidden love, Roland must uncover the truth about his heritage and embrace his destiny as the son of the legendary Count of Monte-Cristo. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within the shadows.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Revenge, Identity, Betrayal, Love, Justice
