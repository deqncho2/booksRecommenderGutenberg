Synopsis: 

In 'English Surnames: Their Sources and Significations', Charles Wareing Endell Bardsley takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of English surnames. Delving into the origins and meanings behind these names, Bardsley uncovers a treasure trove of historical anecdotes, linguistic insights, and surprising connections. From the noble Norman conquerors to the humble tradesmen, each surname carries a story waiting to be unraveled. With wit and erudition, Bardsley guides readers through the intricate web of etymology, shedding light on the social, cultural, and geographical influences that have shaped English surnames over the centuries. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Linguistics

Themes: Etymology, Cultural History, Language Evolution
