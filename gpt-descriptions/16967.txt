Synopsis: In the 'English-Esperanto Dictionary', Hayes and O'Connor present a comprehensive and user-friendly guide to the fascinating language of Esperanto. This dictionary not only provides translations from English to Esperanto and vice versa, but also includes detailed explanations of grammar, pronunciation, and usage. With its extensive vocabulary and helpful examples, this dictionary is an essential tool for anyone interested in learning or improving their proficiency in Esperanto.

Genres: Language Learning, Reference

Themes: Language Acquisition, Linguistics
