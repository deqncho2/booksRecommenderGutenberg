Synopsis: In 'Mona; Or, The Secret of a Royal Mirror' by Sheldon, Georgie, Mrs., a young woman named Mona finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when she discovers a hidden secret within a royal mirror. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Mona's life takes an unexpected turn, leading her on a thrilling journey filled with danger, romance, and the pursuit of truth. With each revelation, Mona must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices that will ultimately determine her fate. 

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Love and sacrifice, Power and deception
