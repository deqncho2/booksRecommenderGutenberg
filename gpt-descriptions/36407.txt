Synopsis: 

'A Mediaeval Mystic: A Short Account of the Life and Writings of Blessed John Ruysbroeck, Canon Regular of Groenendael A.D. 1293-1381' by Scully, Vincent takes readers on a captivating journey into the life and writings of Blessed John Ruysbroeck, a renowned mediaeval mystic. This short account delves into the fascinating experiences and profound insights of Ruysbroeck, offering a glimpse into the mystical world he inhabited. From his early years as a canon regular to his later spiritual awakening, the book explores the transformative power of Ruysbroeck's writings and the impact they had on his contemporaries and future generations. 

Genres: Biography, Spirituality, History

Themes: Mysticism, Spiritual Awakening, Religious Devotion
