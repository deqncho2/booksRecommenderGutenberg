Synopsis: In 'The Keepers of the King's Peace' by Wallace, Edgar, a renowned detective is called upon to solve a perplexing case involving a stolen artifact that holds immense power. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, betrayal, and danger that threatens the stability of the kingdom. With time running out, the detective must navigate through a maze of suspects and unexpected twists to restore peace and protect the monarchy.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal, Justice and redemption
