Synopsis: 

'American Antiquities. Auction Catalogue, January 8, 1898' by William B. Norman takes readers on a captivating journey through a meticulously curated collection of historical artifacts. As the auction catalogue unveils a wide array of intriguing items, from Native American relics to colonial-era treasures, readers are transported to a time when America was still discovering its identity. Each item holds a story, a piece of the past waiting to be discovered and appreciated by bidders and history enthusiasts alike. With vivid descriptions and historical context, this catalogue offers a glimpse into the rich tapestry of American history, inviting readers to explore the significance and beauty of these antiquities.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Art Catalogue, Americana

Themes: American history, cultural heritage, preservation of artifacts
