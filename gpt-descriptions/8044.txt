Synopsis: 

In the Book of Acts, the story continues from the Gospel of Luke as the early Christian movement gains momentum and spreads across the ancient world. This captivating narrative follows the journeys of the apostles, particularly Peter and Paul, as they face persecution, perform miracles, and establish new Christian communities. From the dramatic conversion of Saul to the tumultuous events in Jerusalem and the eventual expansion of the faith to Rome, Acts is a thrilling account of faith, courage, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Genres: Religious literature, Historical narrative, Biography

Themes: Faith and belief, Persecution and resilience, Divine intervention, Community building
