Synopsis: In 'Forgers and Forgeries', Constable, W. G. takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of art forgery. Set in the bustling art scene of early 20th century London, the story follows a renowned art dealer who becomes entangled in a web of deception and deceit when he discovers a series of masterful forgeries. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he must navigate a dangerous underworld of criminals and con artists, all while trying to protect his reputation and uncover the truth behind the forgeries. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Art, Deception, Identity
