Synopsis: In "Frank Reade Jr. and His Engine of the Clouds: Or, Chased Around the World in the Sky," follow the thrilling adventures of Frank Reade Jr. as he takes to the skies in his incredible flying machine. From daring escapes to encounters with dangerous foes, Frank Reade Jr. embarks on a whirlwind journey around the world, pushing the limits of technology and exploring the unknown. With each new destination, the stakes rise higher, and the suspense keeps readers on the edge of their seats. Will Frank Reade Jr. and his remarkable invention be able to overcome the challenges they face? Find out in this exhilarating tale of sky-high adventure.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Steampunk

Themes: Exploration, Technological Innovation, Perseverance
