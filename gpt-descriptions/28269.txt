Synopsis: 

'Art in Needlework: A Book about Embroidery' is a captivating exploration of the art of embroidery, written by Mary Buckle and Lewis F. Day. This enchanting book takes readers on a journey through the intricate world of needlework, revealing the beauty and creativity that can be achieved through this timeless craft. From delicate stitches to vibrant colors, the authors delve into the various techniques and styles of embroidery, showcasing stunning examples and providing practical guidance for both beginners and experienced embroiderers. With its rich illustrations and insightful commentary, 'Art in Needlework' is sure to inspire and captivate anyone with an appreciation for the artistry of needlework.

Genres: Art, Crafts, Non-fiction

Themes: Creativity, Beauty, Craftsmanship
