Synopsis: 
In 'Lady Merton, Colonist' by Mrs. Humphry Ward, readers are transported to the vibrant world of colonial Africa. The story follows Lady Merton, a strong-willed and adventurous woman who defies societal expectations to embark on a journey of self-discovery and resilience. As she navigates the challenges of colonial life, Lady Merton finds herself torn between duty, love, and her own desires. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel explores the complexities of identity, the clash of cultures, and the pursuit of personal freedom in a rapidly changing world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Colonialism, Love and Duty, Women's Empowerment
