Synopsis: In "The Sleeper is a Rebel" by Bryce Walton, a seemingly ordinary man discovers a hidden power within himself that could change the course of history. As he grapples with his newfound abilities, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of espionage and rebellion, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of secrets and betrayal to uncover the truth and determine his own destiny.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power and Responsibility, Identity and Self-Discovery, Espionage and Intrigue
