Synopsis: 
'Bygone Church Life in Scotland' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of Scottish churches, uncovering forgotten tales and intriguing anecdotes that shed light on the vibrant religious life of the past. From ancient cathedrals to humble parish churches, this book delves into the fascinating stories behind these sacred spaces, revealing the struggles, triumphs, and mysteries that have shaped Scotland's religious heritage. With vivid descriptions and captivating narratives, the author paints a vivid picture of the bygone church life, transporting readers to a time when faith and community were deeply intertwined.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Travel, Cultural History

Themes: Religious heritage, Community, Scottish history
