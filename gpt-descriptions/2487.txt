Synopsis: In 'Cross Roads' by Margaret E. Sangster, a young woman named Alice finds herself at a pivotal moment in her life when she unexpectedly inherits a charming bed and breakfast in a small coastal town. As she navigates the challenges of running the inn and getting to know the eclectic group of guests, Alice discovers that the crossroads she faces extend far beyond the physical boundaries of the property. With each new encounter, she is forced to confront her own fears, desires, and the choices that will shape her future. As the secrets of the inn and its inhabitants unravel, Alice must decide which path to take, ultimately leading her to unexpected revelations and a deeper understanding of herself.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Choices, Secrets, Transformation
