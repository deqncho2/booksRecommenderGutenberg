Synopsis: In 'Raftmates: A Story of the Great River' by Munroe, Kirk, two unlikely companions find themselves on a thrilling adventure down the treacherous Great River. As they navigate dangerous rapids, encounter mysterious creatures, and face unexpected challenges, their bond grows stronger. With each twist and turn, they discover the true meaning of friendship and the power of resilience. Will they conquer the perils of the river and find their way home? Genres: Adventure, Friendship, Coming-of-age. Themes: Survival, Exploration, Camaraderie.
