Synopsis: In 'His Glorious Appearing: An Exposition of Matthew Twenty-Four', James White takes readers on a captivating journey through the book of Matthew, specifically focusing on chapter twenty-four. With a blend of biblical scholarship and engaging storytelling, White delves into the prophetic words of Jesus, exploring the signs of the end times and the glorious appearing of Christ. As he unravels the mysteries of this pivotal chapter, readers will be captivated by the profound insights and thought-provoking interpretations that White presents.

Genres: Christian non-fiction, Biblical studies, Eschatology

Themes: End times, Second coming of Christ, Biblical prophecy, Interpretation of scripture
