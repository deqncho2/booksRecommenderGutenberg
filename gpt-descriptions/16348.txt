Synopsis: 
In the small town of East Amwell, a mysterious carnival arrives overnight, bringing with it a sense of enchantment and wonder. As the townspeople become captivated by the carnival's attractions, they soon discover that there is more to Dreamland than meets the eye. Amidst the thrilling rides and dazzling performances, secrets begin to unravel, and the line between reality and fantasy becomes blurred. With each passing day, the residents of East Amwell find themselves drawn deeper into the spellbinding world of Dreamland, where dreams and nightmares intertwine, and nothing is as it seems.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: 
Illusion vs. Reality, the Power of Dreams, the Dark Side of Desires
