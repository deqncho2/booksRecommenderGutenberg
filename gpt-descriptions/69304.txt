Synopsis: In "Mozart" by Prout, Ebenezer, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of the greatest musical prodigies in history. This biographical work delves into the extraordinary talent, tumultuous relationships, and the relentless pursuit of artistic perfection that defined the life of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart. From his early years as a child prodigy to his rise as a renowned composer, witness the triumphs and tribulations that shaped Mozart's genius and left an indelible mark on the world of classical music.

Genres: Biography, Music, Historical

Themes: Genius and creativity, Passion and obsession, Artistic expression, Personal relationships
