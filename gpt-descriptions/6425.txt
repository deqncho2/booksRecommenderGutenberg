Synopsis: In 'Flowing Gold' by Rex Beach, a young geologist named John Storm embarks on a thrilling adventure in the dangerous oil fields of Texas. As he navigates the treacherous world of oil drilling, Storm finds himself entangled in a web of greed, corruption, and romance. With his determination and expertise, he must overcome numerous obstacles to uncover the truth behind a mysterious oil well and protect those he cares about. This gripping tale of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of wealth will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Greed, Corruption, Determination
