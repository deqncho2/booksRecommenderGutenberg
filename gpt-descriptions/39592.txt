Synopsis: Princess Mary's Gift Book is a captivating collection of stories, poems, and illustrations, all compiled for a noble cause. With all profits going to the Queen's "Work for Women" Fund, which supports The National Relief Fund, this book is a heartfelt tribute to the resilience and strength of women. From tales of adventure and romance to thought-provoking poetry, this anthology promises to transport readers to enchanting worlds and leave them inspired. With contributions from renowned authors and artists, Princess Mary's Gift Book is a treasure trove of literary gems that will warm hearts and make a difference.

Genres: Anthology, Fiction, Poetry, Art

Themes: Charity, Women's Empowerment, Compassion, Creativity
