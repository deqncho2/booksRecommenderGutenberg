Synopsis: In 'Erema; Or, My Father's Sin', the reader is transported to the rugged and enchanting landscapes of America's Pacific Northwest. Erema, a young and spirited Englishwoman, finds herself embroiled in a mysterious family secret that takes her on a journey of self-discovery and redemption. As she navigates the treacherous paths of love, betrayal, and forgiveness, Erema's resilience and determination are tested to their limits. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this novel explores the depths of human nature and the power of redemption.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Redemption
