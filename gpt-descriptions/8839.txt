Synopsis: In 'Weymouth New Testament in Modern Speech, Colossians', Richard Francis Weymouth presents a fresh and contemporary translation of the biblical book of Colossians. This powerful epistle, written by the apostle Paul, addresses the early Christian community in Colossae, offering guidance, encouragement, and profound insights into the nature of Christ and the Christian faith. Weymouth's translation brings the timeless message of Colossians to life, captivating readers with its clarity and relevance.

Genres: Religious literature, Biblical translation, Christian non-fiction

Themes: The supremacy of Christ, the importance of faith, the unity of believers, spiritual growth and maturity, the dangers of false teachings
