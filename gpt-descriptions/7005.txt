Synopsis:
In the small coastal town of Fairport, Scotland, a mysterious antiquary named Jonathan Oldbuck becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue. As he delves into the history of the region, he uncovers ancient relics, hidden treasures, and long-forgotten legends. Alongside his young friend Lovel, Oldbuck finds himself caught up in a series of unexpected events that test his wit and courage. From encounters with smugglers to encounters with the supernatural, the antiquary's quest for knowledge takes him on a thrilling journey through the past and present, where the line between reality and myth becomes blurred.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes:
History and its impact on the present, the power of knowledge and curiosity, the clash between tradition and progress
