Synopsis: In 'My Religion' by Leo Tolstoy, a renowned Russian author, the protagonist embarks on a profound spiritual journey that challenges his beliefs and forces him to question the very essence of life. As he grapples with existential questions and confronts the hypocrisy of organized religion, he seeks to find a deeper, more authentic connection with the divine. Through introspection and self-discovery, he strives to reconcile his own moral compass with the teachings of the church, ultimately leading to a transformative revelation that will leave readers contemplating their own faith and the meaning of existence.

Genres: Philosophical fiction, Spiritual literature

Themes: Religion, Spirituality, Morality, Existentialism
