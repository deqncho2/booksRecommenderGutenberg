Synopsis: In 'The Best Made Plans' by Cole, Everett B., a brilliant architect finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he is hired to design a luxurious mansion for a mysterious client. As he delves deeper into the project, he uncovers dark secrets that threaten not only his career but also his life. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous path of betrayal and manipulation to uncover the truth and save himself from the clutches of a powerful enemy.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Power
