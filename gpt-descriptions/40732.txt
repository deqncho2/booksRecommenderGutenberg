Synopsis: In "Liverpool a few years since: by an old stager," the reader is transported to the vibrant city of Liverpool, where they follow the experiences of an intriguing and seasoned individual. Through their eyes, we witness the bustling streets, the lively characters, and the hidden secrets that lie within this thriving metropolis. As the story unfolds, the reader becomes immersed in a world of mystery, adventure, and unexpected twists, all set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society. With each turn of the page, the reader is left wondering what lies around the next corner and eagerly anticipating the revelations that await them.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Urban life, Transformation, Secrets
