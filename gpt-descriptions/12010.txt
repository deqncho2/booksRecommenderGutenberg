Synopsis: In 'The Fourth-Dimensional Reaches of the Exposition: San Francisco, 1915', Cora Lenore Williams takes readers on a captivating journey through the groundbreaking Panama-Pacific International Exposition. As the protagonist immerses herself in the wonders of this grand event, she discovers a hidden world that transcends time and space, revealing the true power of human imagination. With each turn of the page, the reader is transported to a realm where reality and fantasy intertwine, leaving them spellbound by the mysteries that unfold.

Genres: Historical fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the human imagination, Blurring of reality and fantasy, The transformative power of art and culture
