Synopsis: In 'The Christmas Child' by Hesba Stretton, a young orphan named Lizzie embarks on a journey that will change her life forever. When she is taken in by a kind-hearted family, Lizzie discovers the true meaning of love, forgiveness, and the power of hope. As Christmas approaches, secrets from the past unravel, leading Lizzie on a path of self-discovery and transformation. This heartwarming tale explores the bonds of family, the importance of second chances, and the magic of the holiday season.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Christmas Literature

Themes: Family, Redemption, Forgiveness, Hope, Self-discovery
