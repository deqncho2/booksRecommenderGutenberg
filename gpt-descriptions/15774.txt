Synopsis: In 'Ishmael; Or, In the Depths', the young and spirited Ishmael Worth finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she is mistaken for a missing heiress. As she navigates through a world of deception, secret societies, and hidden identities, Ishmael must rely on her wit and courage to uncover the truth and protect her own life. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Courage, Society
