Synopsis: In 'A Minion of the Moon: A Romance of the King's Highway', a young man named Richard is drawn into a mysterious world of secrets and intrigue when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. As he embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth, he finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and ancient legends that will test his courage and determination. With each step closer to the treasure, Richard must navigate treacherous paths and face unexpected challenges that will forever change his life.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Quest for truth, Love and betrayal, Courage and resilience
