Synopsis: 

In 'The Raisin Industry: A practical treatise on the raisin grapes, their history, culture and curing', Gustavus A. Eisen takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of raisin grapes. This comprehensive guide delves into the rich history, cultivation techniques, and intricate process of curing these delectable fruits. From the origins of raisin grapes to the modern methods employed in their production, Eisen's expertise shines through as he explores every aspect of this beloved industry. Whether you are a curious enthusiast or a seasoned professional, this book offers valuable insights and practical knowledge that will leave you with a newfound appreciation for the raisin industry.

Genres: Non-fiction, Agriculture, Culinary

Themes: History, Cultivation, Food production
