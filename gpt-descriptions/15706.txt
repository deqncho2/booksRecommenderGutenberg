Synopsis: In 'Winchester' by Heath, Sidney, a renowned architect is summoned to the mysterious Winchester Manor, a sprawling mansion with a dark and haunted past. As he delves into the secrets of the house, he becomes entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and unexplained phenomena. With each step closer to uncovering the truth, he finds himself questioning his own sanity and the boundaries between the living and the dead. As the chilling secrets of Winchester Manor are revealed, he must confront his own demons and fight to survive the malevolent forces that lurk within its walls.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Hauntings, Madness, Legacy, Redemption
