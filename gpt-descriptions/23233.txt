Synopsis: In 'Posthumous Works of the Author of A Vindication of the Rights of Woman,' Mary Wollstonecraft presents a collection of thought-provoking essays and stories that delve into the complexities of human nature, society, and the pursuit of happiness. From exploring the power dynamics within relationships to challenging societal norms, Wollstonecraft's posthumous works offer a captivating glimpse into her brilliant mind and unwavering commitment to advocating for equality and justice.

Genres: Non-fiction, Feminist literature, Political philosophy

Themes: Gender equality, Individual freedom, Social reform, Human nature
