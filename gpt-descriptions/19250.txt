Synopsis: In 'Abdu'l-Bahá in London, ʻAbdu'l-Bahá, the son of the founder of the Bahá'í Faith, embarks on a transformative journey to London in the early 20th century. Through his encounters with influential figures, captivating speeches, and profound interactions with people from all walks of life, he challenges societal norms and inspires a movement of unity and spiritual awakening. As he navigates the complexities of a rapidly changing world, 'Abdu'l-Bahá's wisdom and compassion shine, leaving an indelible mark on those who have the privilege of crossing his path.

Genres: Spiritual, Biography, Historical

Themes: Unity, Social Justice, Spiritual Enlightenment
