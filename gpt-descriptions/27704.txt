Synopsis:
In the second volume of "Memoirs of the Court and Cabinets of George the Third," the Duke of Buckingham and Chandos presents a captivating account of the inner workings of the British monarchy during the reign of King George III. Drawing from original family documents, the author delves into the intrigues, scandals, and power struggles that shaped the court and cabinets of this pivotal era. From the political maneuverings of influential figures to the personal dramas of the royal family, this volume offers a fascinating glimpse into the hidden world of the British monarchy.

Genres: Biography, History, Political Non-fiction

Themes: Power and politics, Royal family dynamics, Historical events and their impact
