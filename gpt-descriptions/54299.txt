Synopsis: 

Step into the world of artistic creations from the 1960s with 'U.S. Copyright Renewals: Artwork 1960-1964: Catalog of Copyright Entries' by Library of Congress. Copyright Office. This captivating catalog takes readers on a visual journey through a diverse range of artwork that was copyrighted during this pivotal period. From vibrant paintings to intricate sculptures, each page unveils a new masterpiece that will leave you in awe of the creativity and talent of these artists. Discover the hidden stories behind these captivating works and immerse yourself in the artistic spirit of the 1960s.

Genres: Art, Reference

Themes: Creativity, Cultural expression, Historical significance
