Synopsis: In "Annie Kilburn: a Novel" by William Dean Howells, we are introduced to Annie Kilburn, a young woman who finds herself torn between her family's traditional values and her own desire for independence. Set in the late 19th century, Annie's journey takes her from a small New England town to the bustling city of Boston, where she encounters a diverse cast of characters who challenge her beliefs and push her to question societal norms. As Annie navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and self-discovery, she must ultimately decide whether to conform to the expectations placed upon her or forge her own path.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Gender Roles, Social Expectations, Individualism
