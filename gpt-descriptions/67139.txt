Synopsis: In 'Shepherds for Science' by W. C. Tuttle, a group of scientists embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that pushes the boundaries of human knowledge and ethics. As they delve deeper into their research, they uncover shocking secrets that challenge their beliefs and force them to question the very nature of humanity. With high stakes and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the fine line between scientific progress and moral responsibility.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Human Nature, Scientific Progress
