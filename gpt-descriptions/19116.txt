Synopsis: In 'How to sing [Meine Gesangskunst]' by Lilli Lehmann, the renowned opera singer shares her invaluable insights and techniques on the art of singing. Through personal anecdotes and practical advice, Lehmann takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and vocal mastery. From breath control to interpretation, she delves into the intricacies of vocal technique, offering guidance to aspiring singers and music enthusiasts alike. With passion and expertise, Lehmann's book serves as a comprehensive guide to unlocking the full potential of one's voice.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, Self-help

Themes: Vocal technique, Artistic expression, Personal growth
