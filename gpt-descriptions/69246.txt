Synopsis: In "Broken Music" by Phyllis Bottome, a renowned pianist finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she agrees to give a private concert for a reclusive millionaire. As she delves deeper into the secrets of her host's past, she must confront her own haunted memories and navigate a treacherous path where music becomes a powerful weapon. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final note.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, the power of music, the complexity of human emotions
