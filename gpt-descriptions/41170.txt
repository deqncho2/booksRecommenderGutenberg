Synopsis: 
'Great Ghost Stories' is a collection of chilling tales that will send shivers down your spine and leave you questioning the boundaries between the living and the dead. From haunted houses to vengeful spirits, this anthology explores the darkest corners of the supernatural world, captivating readers with its eerie atmosphere and spine-tingling suspense. Each story is carefully crafted to keep you on the edge of your seat, as you delve into the unknown and encounter the unexplainable. Prepare to be haunted by the lingering presence of these unforgettable ghostly tales.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: Ghostly encounters, Hauntings, Fear of the unknown
