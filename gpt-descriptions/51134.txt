Synopsis: 
'A Book of Dartmoor: Second Edition' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged and mysterious landscapes of Dartmoor, a region in southwestern England. Written by S. Baring-Gould, this book offers a collection of fascinating stories, legends, and historical accounts that bring the enchanting moorland to life. From tales of ancient burial sites and ghostly apparitions to accounts of daring escapes and local customs, each chapter unveils a new facet of Dartmoor's rich heritage. With vivid descriptions and a keen eye for detail, Baring-Gould paints a vivid picture of this unique and hauntingly beautiful place, leaving readers eager to explore its secrets for themselves.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, History, Folklore

Themes: Mystery, History, Folklore, Landscape, Local customs
