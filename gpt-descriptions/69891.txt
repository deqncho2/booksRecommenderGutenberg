Synopsis:
'Les liaisons dangereuses, volume 1' is a captivating epistolary novel that delves into the scandalous lives of the French aristocracy in the 18th century. Through a series of letters exchanged between various characters, the reader is drawn into a web of deceit, manipulation, and seduction. The story revolves around the Marquise de Merteuil and the Vicomte de Valmont, two former lovers who engage in a dangerous game of seducing and corrupting others for their own amusement. As their intricate plans unfold, the consequences become increasingly dire, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep the reader on the edge of their seat.

Genres:
Epistolary fiction, Historical fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes:
Love and desire, Power and manipulation, Morality and ethics
