Synopsis: In 'Mohave Pottery', Kroeber and Harner take readers on a captivating journey into the world of the Mohave people, exploring the artistry and cultural significance of their pottery. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, the authors delve into the techniques, symbolism, and historical context behind these exquisite creations, shedding light on the Mohave's deep connection to their land and heritage. From the intricate designs to the spiritual beliefs woven into each piece, 'Mohave Pottery' offers a fascinating glimpse into a rich artistic tradition that has endured for centuries.

Genres: Anthropology, Art, History

Themes: Cultural heritage, Artistic expression, Indigenous traditions
