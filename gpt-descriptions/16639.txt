Synopsis: 
"The Fotygraft Album: Shown to the New Neighbor by Rebecca Sparks Peters Aged Eleven" is a captivating and mysterious tale that follows the adventures of young Rebecca as she unveils a hidden album filled with enigmatic photographs. As she delves deeper into the secrets behind the pictures, Rebecca discovers a world of intrigue, family history, and unexpected connections that will forever change her perspective on life.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Discovery
