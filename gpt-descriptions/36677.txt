Synopsis: In 'Sea Monsters Unmasked, and Sea Fables Explained', Lee, Henry takes readers on a captivating journey through the depths of the ocean, unraveling the mysteries behind legendary sea creatures and uncovering the truth behind ancient maritime myths. From the terrifying Kraken to the enchanting mermaids, Lee delves into the origins, folklore, and scientific explanations of these captivating beings, challenging our perceptions and leaving us in awe of the wonders that lie beneath the waves.

Genres: Non-fiction, Mythology, Natural History

Themes: Exploration, Mythology, Curiosity, Wonder
