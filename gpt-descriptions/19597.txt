Synopsis: In 'National Character: A Thanksgiving Discourse', Burt explores the essence of national identity and its impact on society. Through thought-provoking insights and historical references, he delves into the complexities of what defines a nation and how it shapes its people. This discourse challenges readers to reflect on their own national character and its significance in the broader context of humanity.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Philosophical

Themes: National identity, Society, Human nature
