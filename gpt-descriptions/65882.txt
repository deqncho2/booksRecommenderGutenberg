Synopsis: In "The Battle of Dorking," a gripping and thought-provoking novella, Chesney takes us on a thrilling journey through an alternate history where Britain faces a devastating invasion. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows a retired soldier who witnesses the shocking aftermath of a successful foreign invasion on British soil. As the nation grapples with the consequences of its unpreparedness, the protagonist reflects on the past and contemplates the future of a once mighty empire. With vivid descriptions and a sense of urgency, Chesney paints a haunting picture of a nation on the brink of collapse.

Genres: Alternate history, military fiction, speculative fiction

Themes: National defense, patriotism, the decline of empires, the consequences of unpreparedness
