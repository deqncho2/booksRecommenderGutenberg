Synopsis:
In 'True Riches; Or, Wealth Without Wings' by Arthur, T. S., we follow the journey of a young man named Frank, who is determined to make his fortune in the bustling city. However, as he climbs the ladder of success, he begins to question the true meaning of wealth and happiness. Along the way, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own perspective on what it means to be truly rich. As Frank navigates the temptations and pitfalls of his newfound wealth, he must ultimately decide what he values most in life.

Genres:
Drama, Fiction, Moral Tale

Themes:
Wealth and materialism, Happiness and fulfillment, Morality and values
