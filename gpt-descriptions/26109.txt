Synopsis: In 'Hail to the Chief' by Randall Garrett, a gripping political thriller unfolds as a cunning and enigmatic assassin targets the President of the United States. As the nation's leader faces imminent danger, a relentless Secret Service agent races against time to uncover the truth behind the assassination plot, navigating a treacherous web of conspiracy and betrayal. With the fate of the country hanging in the balance, the stakes have never been higher.

Genres: Political thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal
