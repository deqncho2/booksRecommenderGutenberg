Synopsis: In "The White Old Maid," Hawthorne weaves a haunting tale of a mysterious woman who has lived a solitary life for decades, shrouded in secrets and whispers. As her past begins to unravel, the reader is drawn into a web of intrigue and suspense, wondering what dark truths lie hidden beneath her enigmatic facade. With each revelation, the story takes unexpected turns, leaving the reader captivated and eager to uncover the ultimate truth behind the white old maid's enigmatic existence.

Genres: Gothic fiction, mystery, psychological thriller

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, the power of appearances, the weight of guilt and regret
