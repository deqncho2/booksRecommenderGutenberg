Synopsis: 
Step into a world of enchantment and imagination as Aria, Mrs. (Eliza Davis) takes you on a captivating journey through the realm of costumes. From the whimsical to the historical, and the theatrical to the fantastical, this book unveils the secrets behind the creation of extraordinary attire. Delve into the rich tapestry of costume design, exploring the intricate details, inspirations, and techniques that bring characters to life on stage and screen. With stunning visuals and insightful anecdotes, 'Costume: Fanciful, Historical and Theatrical' is a treasure trove for anyone fascinated by the transformative power of clothing.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Fashion

Themes: Creativity, Historical representation, Theatricality
