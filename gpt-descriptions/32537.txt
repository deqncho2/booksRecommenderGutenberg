Synopsis: In 'The Newsboy Partners; Or, Who Was Dick Box?' by Frank V. Webster, readers are introduced to a group of young newsboys who stumble upon a mysterious box left behind by a fellow newsboy named Dick. As they embark on a quest to uncover the truth about Dick's disappearance, they find themselves entangled in a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns. Along the way, they encounter suspicious characters, secret codes, and hidden treasures, all while trying to solve the mystery of who Dick Box really was.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Discovery
