Synopsis: In "Cambridge Sketches," Frank Preston Stearns takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and intellectual world of Cambridge, Massachusetts. Through a series of vivid and insightful sketches, Stearns introduces us to a diverse cast of characters, from renowned professors and ambitious students to eccentric locals and passionate activists. As we delve into the heart of this historic city, we witness the clash of ideas, the pursuit of knowledge, and the ever-present tension between tradition and progress. With wit and charm, Stearns paints a captivating portrait of Cambridge, offering a glimpse into the lives and minds of those who call it home.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, History

Themes: Intellectualism, Education, Community, Tradition vs. Progress
