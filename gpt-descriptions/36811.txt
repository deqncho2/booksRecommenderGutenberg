Synopsis: In 'The Hour Will Come: A Tale of an Alpine Cloister', Wilhelmine von Hillern takes readers on a captivating journey to an isolated Alpine cloister. Set in the 17th century, the story follows the lives of two young women, Agnes and Hedwig, who find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden love, and the pursuit of personal freedom. As they navigate the strict rules and traditions of the cloister, their paths intersect with a mysterious stranger who holds the key to their destinies. With vivid descriptions of the breathtaking Alpine landscape and a cast of intriguing characters, this tale of love, sacrifice, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Forbidden love, Personal freedom, Secrets and deception
