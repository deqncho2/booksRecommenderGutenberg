Synopsis: In "The Motor Boys Across the Plains; or, The Hermit of Lost Lake," the adventurous Motor Boys embark on a thrilling journey across the vast plains, encountering mysterious hermits, hidden treasures, and unexpected dangers. As they navigate through the rugged wilderness, their courage and resourcefulness are put to the test, leading to a series of heart-pounding escapades that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
