Synopsis: In "The Intelligence Office," a young man stumbles upon a peculiar establishment in a small New England town. Intrigued by the enigmatic owner and the mysterious activities taking place within, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden agendas. As he delves deeper into the intelligence office's operations, he discovers that nothing is as it seems, and the consequences of his curiosity may be more than he bargained for.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Curiosity, Deception, Power dynamics, Identity
