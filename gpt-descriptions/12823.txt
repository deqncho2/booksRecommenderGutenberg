Synopsis: In 'Joe's Luck; Or, Always Wide Awake' by Horatio Alger, Jr., follow the thrilling journey of Joe, a young boy with an indomitable spirit and an unwavering determination to overcome adversity. From his humble beginnings, Joe faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected opportunities that test his resilience and resourcefulness. As he navigates through a world filled with both kind-hearted individuals and cunning adversaries, Joe's luck takes unexpected turns, leading him on a path towards self-discovery and success. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly rooting for Joe as he strives to make his own luck in a world full of possibilities.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Rags-to-riches

Themes: Perseverance, Self-reliance, Social mobility
