Synopsis: In 'A Fleece of Gold; Five Lessons from the Fable of Jason and the Golden Fleece', Charles Stewart Given takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient myth of Jason and the Golden Fleece. Drawing upon this timeless tale, Given weaves together a thought-provoking narrative that explores the complexities of human ambition, the power of perseverance, and the transformative nature of self-discovery. Through five insightful lessons, readers are invited to reflect on their own quests for success and fulfillment, as they uncover the hidden wisdom within this legendary fable.

Genres: Mythology, Self-help, Philosophy

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Self-discovery
