Synopsis: 
'Four Afoot: Being the Adventures of the Big Four on the Highway' follows the thrilling escapades of four young friends who embark on a daring journey across the country. As they traverse the open road, they encounter a series of unexpected challenges and encounters that test their courage, resourcefulness, and loyalty to one another. From encounters with dangerous criminals to unexpected acts of heroism, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to see what happens next.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Adventure, Loyalty
