Synopsis: In 'The Great Taxicab Robbery: A True Detective Story', author James H. Collins takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of a city plagued by a series of audacious taxicab robberies. As the crimes escalate, a determined detective races against time to unravel the mystery behind the robberies and bring the culprits to justice. Filled with unexpected twists and turns, this gripping true crime tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: True Crime, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Crime and Justice, Investigation, Criminal Psychology
