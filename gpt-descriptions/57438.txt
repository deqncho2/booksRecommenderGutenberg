Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, a mysterious pair of gloves appears, possessing an uncanny ability to grant the wearer extraordinary powers. As the townspeople become enthralled by the gloves' allure, they soon discover that their newfound abilities come at a great cost. With the line between good and evil blurred, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate the dangerous consequences of their desires, leading to a thrilling battle for control over the gloves and the fate of their town.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Power and its consequences, Morality and ethics, Sacrifice and redemption
