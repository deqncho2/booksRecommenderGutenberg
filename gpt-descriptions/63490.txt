Synopsis: In 'The Altar of Freedom' by Mary Roberts Rinehart, a young woman finds herself caught in the midst of the American Civil War, torn between her loyalty to her family and her desire for personal freedom. As she navigates the dangers and uncertainties of war, she discovers the true meaning of sacrifice and the power of love. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this historical novel takes readers on a captivating journey through one of the most tumultuous periods in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Loyalty, Freedom, Sacrifice
