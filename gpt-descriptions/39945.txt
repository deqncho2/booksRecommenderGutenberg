Synopsis: 
'Peeps at People: Being Certain Papers from the Writings of Anne Warrington Witherup' takes readers on a delightful journey through the witty and observant writings of Anne Warrington Witherup. Through her keen observations and humorous anecdotes, Witherup offers a unique perspective on the quirks and idiosyncrasies of the people she encounters in her daily life. From eccentric neighbors to pompous socialites, each character comes to life through Witherup's sharp wit and astute observations. This collection of papers provides a charming and entertaining glimpse into the world of Anne Warrington Witherup and the fascinating people she encounters along the way.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Social observation, Human nature, Satire
