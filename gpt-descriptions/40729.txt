Synopsis: "Old Scrooge" is a captivating dramatization of Charles Dickens' beloved Christmas story, "A Christmas Carol," presented in five staves. The story follows the miserly Ebenezer Scrooge as he is visited by three spirits on Christmas Eve, who take him on a transformative journey through his past, present, and future. As Scrooge confronts his own greed and selfishness, he is forced to reevaluate his life choices and rediscover the true meaning of Christmas.

Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Holiday

Themes: Redemption, Generosity, Transformation, the Spirit of Christmas
