Synopsis: 
In "Twenty Years on Horseback; or, Itinerating in West Virginia," W. M. Weekley takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the rugged landscapes and untamed wilderness of West Virginia. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, the author recounts his thrilling adventures as he traverses the state on horseback, encountering fascinating characters, facing unexpected challenges, and discovering the hidden gems of this enchanting region. From breathtaking mountain ranges to treacherous river crossings, from encounters with wild animals to heartwarming interactions with local communities, this memoir offers a captivating glimpse into the untamed beauty and rich history of West Virginia.

Genres: Memoir, Travelogue, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Resilience, Cultural encounters
