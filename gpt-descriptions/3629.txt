Synopsis: 
'The Titan' by Theodore Dreiser is a gripping novel that follows the life of Frank Cowperwood, a charismatic and ambitious businessman in late 19th century America. From his humble beginnings as a young clerk, Cowperwood rises to become a powerful figure in the financial world, navigating through love affairs, political corruption, and financial crises. As he strives for success and wealth, Cowperwood's relentless pursuit of power leads him down a path filled with moral dilemmas and personal sacrifices. Will he be able to maintain his position as the titan of industry, or will his insatiable ambition ultimately be his downfall?

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Drama, Business

Themes: 
Ambition, Power, Morality, Corruption
