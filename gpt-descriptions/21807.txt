Synopsis: In 'The Holy Cross and Other Tales' by Field, Eugene, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting and thought-provoking tales. From the heartwarming story of a young boy's encounter with a mysterious cross to the whimsical adventures of mischievous fairies, each tale is filled with wonder, magic, and unexpected twists. As the pages turn, readers will be transported to a world where imagination knows no bounds and where the power of love, faith, and redemption can overcome any obstacle. 

Genres: Fantasy, Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Imagination, Love, Magic
