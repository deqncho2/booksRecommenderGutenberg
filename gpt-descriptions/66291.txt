Synopsis: In 'The Plagiarist From Rigel IV' by Hunter, Evan, a renowned intergalactic detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious alien from Rigel IV is accused of plagiarism. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that stretches across the galaxy, challenging his own beliefs and forcing him to confront the true nature of creativity and originality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Ethics, Intellectual Property
