Synopsis: In 'The Nervous Housewife' by Abraham Myerson, a seemingly ordinary housewife finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden secret that threatens to unravel her entire existence. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she must confront her own fears and insecurities, ultimately leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and empowerment. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Empowerment
