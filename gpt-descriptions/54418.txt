Synopsis: In 'Mr. Wycherly's Wards' by L. Allen Harker, a mysterious and reclusive man becomes the guardian of two young girls, thrusting them into a world of secrets, hidden pasts, and unexpected adventures. As the girls uncover the truth about their guardian and his peculiar household, they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue that will test their courage and loyalty. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family, Identity, Trust, Secrets
