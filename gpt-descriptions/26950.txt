Synopsis: 
In 'Humorous Ghost Stories' by Dorothy Scarborough, prepare to be entertained and spooked in equal measure as you delve into a collection of delightful and witty tales. From mischievous specters to comical encounters with the supernatural, these stories will have you laughing out loud while keeping you on the edge of your seat. Join the author as she weaves together a tapestry of humor, mystery, and the paranormal, creating a unique reading experience that will leave you wanting more.

Genres: 
Humor, Ghost Stories, Mystery

Themes: 
Supernatural encounters, Comedy, Mystery
