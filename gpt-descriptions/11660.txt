Synopsis:
In 'Ethel Morton's Enterprise', Ethel Morton and her friends embark on an exciting adventure that takes them far from their familiar surroundings. As they navigate through unexpected challenges and encounters, Ethel's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. With a mix of humor, mystery, and heartwarming moments, this captivating story will keep readers eagerly turning the pages to see how Ethel's enterprise unfolds.

Genres:
Children's literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes:
Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
