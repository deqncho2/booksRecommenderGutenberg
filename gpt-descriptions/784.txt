Synopsis: 
'Boyhood in Norway: Stories of Boy-Life in the Land of the Midnight Sun' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of Norway, as seen through the eyes of a young boy. From thrilling adventures in the wilderness to heartwarming encounters with local traditions and folklore, this collection of stories offers a vivid portrayal of boyhood in a land where the sun never sets during summer. As the protagonist navigates the challenges and joys of growing up, readers are transported to a world filled with wonder, discovery, and the timeless bonds of friendship.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Cultural

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Identity, Friendship
