Synopsis:
In "Peggy Stewart at School" by Gabrielle E. Jackson, readers are introduced to Peggy, a spirited and determined young girl who embarks on a new adventure at school. As she navigates the challenges of fitting in and making friends, Peggy's curiosity and resilience lead her on a path of self-discovery and unexpected discoveries. With each new day, Peggy's experiences at school become more exciting and unpredictable, leaving readers eager to uncover what lies ahead for this spirited protagonist.

Genres:
Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes:
Friendship, Self-Discovery, Resilience
