Synopsis: 
Patty in the City follows the adventures of Patty Fairfield, a young girl from a small town who finds herself in the bustling city of New York. As she navigates the unfamiliar streets and encounters a cast of colorful characters, Patty's journey becomes a delightful exploration of friendship, independence, and self-discovery. From unexpected encounters to heartwarming moments, Patty's experiences in the city will captivate readers and leave them eager to join her on her next adventure.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Self-discovery
