Synopsis: In 'The Haunting of Low Fennel' by Rohmer, Sax, a young woman named Emily moves to the secluded town of Low Fennel to escape her troubled past. However, she soon discovers that the town holds dark secrets and is plagued by a malevolent force that haunts its residents. As Emily delves deeper into the mysteries of Low Fennel, she must confront her own fears and unravel the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Confronting fears, Supernatural forces
