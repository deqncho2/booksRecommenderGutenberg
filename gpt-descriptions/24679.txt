Synopsis: In 'Lilith: The Legend of the First Woman', Ada Langworthy Collier delves into the ancient myth of Lilith, the first woman created before Eve. Set in a world where darkness and light intertwine, Lilith embarks on a captivating journey filled with forbidden knowledge, mystical creatures, and a battle for her own identity. As she navigates through the realms of love, power, and self-discovery, Lilith's choices will shape the destiny of humanity itself.

Genres: Fantasy, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Feminism, Good vs. Evil
