Synopsis: In "Midst the Wild Carpathians," a young noblewoman named Gálffi Ágnes finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and political intrigue. Set against the backdrop of the stunning Carpathian Mountains, Ágnes must navigate the complexities of her heart and the treacherous world around her, where secrets and hidden agendas threaten to shatter her world. As she grapples with her own desires and the expectations of society, Ágnes must make choices that will shape her destiny and the lives of those around her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and passion, Duty and sacrifice, Betrayal and loyalty, Social expectations and constraints
