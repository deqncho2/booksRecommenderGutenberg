Synopsis: 
In 'Legends of Old Testament characters, from the Talmud and other sources', S. Baring-Gould delves into the captivating world of ancient legends surrounding well-known figures from the Old Testament. From Adam and Eve to Moses and King David, this book uncovers fascinating tales that have been passed down through generations, shedding new light on these iconic biblical characters. With rich storytelling and meticulous research, Baring-Gould brings to life the extraordinary adventures, trials, and triumphs of these legendary figures, offering a fresh perspective on their significance and impact. Prepare to be enthralled by the untold stories that lie beneath the surface of the familiar biblical narratives.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Mythology, Religious Studies

Themes: 
Myth and Legend, Human Nature, Faith and Belief
