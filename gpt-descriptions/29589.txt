Synopsis: In "Rodney, the Ranger, with Daniel Morgan on Trail and Battlefield" by John V. Lane, embark on an exhilarating journey through the untamed American frontier alongside the fearless Rodney, a skilled ranger, and the legendary Daniel Morgan. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and face formidable foes, their bond is tested, and their courage is pushed to the limits. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will transport readers to a time of danger, honor, and the pursuit of justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Survival, Justice
