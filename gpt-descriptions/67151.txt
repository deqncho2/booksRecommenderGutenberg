Synopsis: In 'The Proofs of Christ's Resurrection; from a Lawyer's Standpoint', Charles R. Morrison presents a compelling and thought-provoking examination of the evidence surrounding the resurrection of Jesus Christ. Drawing upon his legal expertise, Morrison meticulously dissects the historical accounts, eyewitness testimonies, and various theories, presenting a compelling case that challenges both skeptics and believers alike. As he delves into the intricacies of the trial, Morrison skillfully weaves together a narrative that will leave readers questioning their own beliefs and pondering the profound implications of this pivotal event in human history.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious, Historical

Themes: Faith, Evidence, Historical Accuracy
