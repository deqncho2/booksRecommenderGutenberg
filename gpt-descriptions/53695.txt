Synopsis: In "Among the River Pirates: A Skippy Dare Mystery Story," young Skippy Dare finds himself embroiled in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a gang of dangerous river pirates. With his quick thinking and resourcefulness, Skippy must navigate treacherous waters, outsmart the cunning criminals, and uncover the truth behind their nefarious activities. As the mystery deepens, Skippy's courage and determination are put to the test, leading to a heart-pounding climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Resourcefulness, Friendship, Crime
