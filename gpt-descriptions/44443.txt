Synopsis: 

'Military schools and courses of instruction in the science and art of war' takes readers on a captivating journey through the military education systems of nine influential countries. From France to the United States, the book delves into the inner workings of these nations' military schools, providing a comprehensive overview of their training methods, curriculum, and strategies. Drawing from recent official reports and documents, this revised edition offers a unique glimpse into the evolution of military education across Europe and North America. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Military History, Education

Themes: Military training, National defense, Comparative analysis
