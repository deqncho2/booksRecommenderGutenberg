Synopsis: 
In 'The Boy's Playbook of Science', John Henry Pepper takes young readers on an exciting journey through the world of scientific experiments. Filled with captivating illustrations and step-by-step instructions, this book introduces children to the wonders of chemistry and natural philosophy. From creating colorful chemical reactions to understanding the principles of physics, readers will be inspired to explore and discover the secrets of the scientific world. With its engaging approach, 'The Boy's Playbook of Science' is sure to ignite a lifelong passion for learning and experimentation.

Genres: Educational, Science, Children's Literature

Themes: Curiosity, Exploration, Learning
