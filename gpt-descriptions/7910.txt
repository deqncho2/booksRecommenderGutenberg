Synopsis: In 'American Historical and Literary Curiosities, Part 10', Smith and Watson delve into the fascinating and lesser-known aspects of American history and literature. From mysterious disappearances to hidden treasures, this collection of curiosities will captivate readers with its intriguing tales. Uncover the secrets behind famous landmarks, explore the lives of forgotten heroes, and unravel the enigmatic events that have shaped America's past. With each page, readers will be transported to a world of historical and literary wonders, leaving them eager to uncover more hidden gems.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Literature

Themes: American history, Literary exploration, Curiosity, Hidden stories
