Synopsis: In 'The Rotifers' by Abernathy, Robert, a small town is plagued by a mysterious phenomenon that threatens to unravel the fabric of reality. As the townspeople grapple with the bizarre occurrences, a group of unlikely heroes emerges, determined to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic rotifers. With each revelation, they delve deeper into a world of secrets, danger, and the unknown, where nothing is as it seems. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the nature of reality itself.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Conspiracy, Identity
