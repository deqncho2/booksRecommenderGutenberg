Synopsis: In 'Saturday's Child' by Kathleen Thompson Norris, a young woman named Marcia is faced with a life-altering decision that will challenge her beliefs, test her resilience, and force her to confront the complexities of love and duty. As she navigates through the trials and tribulations of early 20th-century society, Marcia must find the strength to follow her heart and forge her own path, even if it means defying societal expectations and risking everything she holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Gender Roles and Expectations, Self-Discovery
