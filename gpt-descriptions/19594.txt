Synopsis: In 'The Eugenic Marriage, Volume 1', author W. Grant Hague delves into the fascinating world of eugenics, a new science that promises to revolutionize the way we live and create better offspring. Through a personal guide, Hague explores the principles and practices of eugenics, offering readers a glimpse into a future where individuals can actively shape the genetic makeup of their children. With thought-provoking insights and practical advice, this volume sets the stage for a captivating exploration of the potential of eugenics.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Self-help

Themes: Eugenics, Genetics, Personal improvement, Future society
