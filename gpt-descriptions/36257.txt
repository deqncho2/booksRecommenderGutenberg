Synopsis: In 'The Fatal Falsehood: A Tragedy. In Five Acts' by Hannah More, a web of deceit and mistaken identities unfolds, leading to devastating consequences for the characters involved. As secrets are revealed and truths are uncovered, the boundaries between right and wrong become blurred, leaving the reader questioning the nature of truth itself. This gripping tragedy explores the power of deception and the devastating impact it can have on individuals and society as a whole.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Deception, Identity, Truth
