Synopsis: In 'Car Pool' by Rosel George Brown, a seemingly ordinary carpool turns into a thrilling journey when the passengers discover a mysterious package hidden in the trunk. As they delve deeper into the secrets surrounding the package, they find themselves entangled in a dangerous web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each passing mile, tensions rise, and the true nature of their mission becomes increasingly unclear. Will they be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Trust, Deception, Loyalty
