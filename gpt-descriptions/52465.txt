Synopsis: In "The faithful servant: A sermon, preached in St. Peter's, Southborough, on occasion of the death of the Rev. Stephen Langston" by Edward Hoare, a captivating sermon unfolds, revealing the remarkable life and unwavering dedication of the late Reverend Stephen Langston. As the congregation gathers to mourn his passing, they are taken on a journey through Langston's selfless acts, his unyielding faith, and the profound impact he had on the lives of those around him. Through poignant anecdotes and heartfelt reflections, the sermon explores the depths of Langston's character, leaving the audience inspired and challenged to live a life of true devotion.

Genres: Sermon, Biography

Themes: Faith, Service, Legacy
