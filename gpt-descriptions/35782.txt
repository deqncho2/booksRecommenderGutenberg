Synopsis: In 'The Second String' by Nat Gould, a talented jockey named Jack is faced with a life-altering decision when he is unexpectedly offered the opportunity to ride a renowned racehorse. As he grapples with the weight of his choice, Jack must navigate the treacherous world of horse racing, filled with rivalries, secrets, and unexpected alliances. With high stakes and thrilling races, this gripping tale explores the sacrifices one must make to pursue their dreams and the unpredictable nature of fate.

Genres: Sports fiction, Drama, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Loyalty, Fate
