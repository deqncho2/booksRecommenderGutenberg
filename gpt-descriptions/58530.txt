Synopsis: 

In 'English Wayfaring Life in the Middle Ages (XIVth Century)', J.J. Jusserand takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling and vibrant world of medieval England. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Jusserand paints a vivid picture of the daily lives, customs, and adventures of the English people during the 14th century. From the bustling streets of London to the tranquil countryside, readers will be transported to a time of knights, pilgrims, and merchants, experiencing the sights, sounds, and smells of a bygone era. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue

Themes: Medieval life, Travel and exploration, Social customs and traditions
