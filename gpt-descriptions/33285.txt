Synopsis: In "Her Royal Highness Woman" by O'Rell, Max, a young American man finds himself in the midst of a whirlwind adventure when he unexpectedly becomes entangled with a mysterious and captivating European woman. As their paths intertwine, he is thrust into a world of royalty, intrigue, and forbidden love, where he must navigate the complexities of social class and cultural differences. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the power dynamics between men and women, challenging societal norms and expectations along the way.

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and desire, Social class and hierarchy, Cultural differences
