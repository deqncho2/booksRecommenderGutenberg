Synopsis: 

In 'Witnesses to Truth' by Edward Hoare, a renowned investigative journalist, a shocking murder takes place in a small coastal town, sending ripples of fear and suspicion throughout the community. As the police struggle to uncover the truth, a group of unlikely witnesses emerges, each with their own secrets and motives. As the plot thickens, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey of twists and turns, where nothing is as it seems and everyone is a potential suspect. With each witness providing a unique perspective on the events, the truth becomes increasingly elusive, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat until the final revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Truth and deception, the power of perception, the complexity of human nature
