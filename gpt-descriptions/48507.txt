Synopsis: 
In 'Devonshire Characters and Strange Events', Baring-Gould takes us on a captivating journey through the eccentricities and peculiarities of the people and events that have shaped the history of Devonshire. From tales of ghostly apparitions and mysterious disappearances to encounters with legendary creatures and encounters with the supernatural, this collection of stories will leave readers spellbound. With his vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Baring-Gould brings to life the rich tapestry of Devonshire's past, blending history, folklore, and the unexplained in a truly mesmerizing way.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, History, Folklore

Themes: 
Mystery, Supernatural, Local Legends, History
