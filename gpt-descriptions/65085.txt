Synopsis: In a dystopian future where society is divided by a mysterious barrier, a young woman named Maya embarks on a dangerous journey to uncover the truth behind its existence. As she navigates through a world filled with secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, Maya discovers that the barrier holds more than just physical boundaries. With her life hanging in the balance, she must confront her own fears and make choices that will determine the fate of her world.

Genres: Dystopian, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Resistance, Sacrifice
