Synopsis: In 'Snowflakes' by Karn, Esther Nelson, a young woman named Lily finds herself drawn into a mysterious world of enchantment when she discovers a hidden snow globe in her grandmother's attic. As she becomes more entangled in the secrets of the snow globe, Lily embarks on a captivating journey that blurs the lines between reality and fantasy. With each shake of the globe, she uncovers a new and magical snowflake, each holding a unique story and a connection to her own past. As Lily delves deeper into the snow globe's enchantment, she must confront her fears, make difficult choices, and ultimately discover the true power of love and self-discovery.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Magic, Family, Identity, Self-discovery
