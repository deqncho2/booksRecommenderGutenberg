Synopsis: In "Good-Morning, Rosamond!" by Constance Lindsay Skinner, a young woman named Rosamond finds herself caught between tradition and her own desires as she navigates the complexities of love and societal expectations. Set in the early 20th century, Rosamond's journey takes her from the bustling streets of New York City to the tranquil countryside of England, where she must confront her own fears and make difficult choices that will shape her future. As she grapples with the pressures of family, society, and her own heart, Rosamond discovers the power of self-discovery and the courage to follow her own path.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Desire, Tradition and Modernity, Self-Discovery
