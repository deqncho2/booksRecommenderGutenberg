Synopsis: In Volume 08 of "The Works of Samuel Johnson, LL.D.", the renowned author delves into the lives of various poets, providing captivating biographical accounts that shed light on their works and personalities. From the celebrated John Milton to the lesser-known Richard Savage, Johnson's meticulous research and insightful analysis offer a fascinating glimpse into the lives and minds of these literary figures. With his characteristic wit and erudition, Johnson explores the triumphs and tribulations of these poets, revealing the complexities of their creative journeys and the impact they had on the world of literature.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Life and works of poets, Literary analysis, Influence of poets on literature
