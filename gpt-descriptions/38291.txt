Synopsis: In 'A Witch of the Hills, v. 1 [of 2]' by Florence Warden, a young woman named Maud is drawn into a mysterious world of witchcraft and dark secrets when she moves to a secluded village in the hills. As she uncovers the truth about her own heritage and the ancient powers that lie dormant within her, Maud must navigate a treacherous path filled with danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of both the village and her own destiny hanging in the balance, Maud must embrace her inner strength and confront the forces that threaten to consume her.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Supernatural

Themes: Witchcraft, Heritage, Power, Identity
