Synopsis: In "My Brother, Theodore Roosevelt," Corinne Roosevelt Robinson offers a captivating and intimate portrait of her larger-than-life brother, Theodore Roosevelt. Through personal anecdotes and heartfelt recollections, Robinson takes readers on a journey through the remarkable life of one of America's most influential presidents. From their childhood adventures to Theodore's rise to political prominence, this memoir provides a unique perspective on the man behind the legend. With a blend of humor, admiration, and deep familial love, Robinson paints a vivid picture of a man who forever left his mark on American history.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, History

Themes: Family bonds, Personal growth, Political leadership, American history
