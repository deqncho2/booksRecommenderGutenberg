Synopsis: 
In "The Gold Bag" by Wells, Carolyn, a mysterious murder takes place during a glamorous society event, leaving everyone baffled. As the investigation unfolds, a clever detective is determined to uncover the truth behind the crime, delving into the lives of the wealthy and uncovering a web of secrets and deceit. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Wealth and Privilege, Intrigue
