Synopsis: 
In 'Sky Island: Being the Further Exciting Adventures of Trot and Cap'n Bill After Their Visit to the Sea Fairies', Trot and Cap'n Bill find themselves embarking on another thrilling journey, this time to a mysterious floating island in the sky. As they explore this enchanting realm, they encounter peculiar creatures, encounter perilous situations, and uncover the island's hidden secrets. With their courage and resourcefulness tested, Trot and Cap'n Bill must rely on their wits to navigate through this fantastical world and find their way back home.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Courage
