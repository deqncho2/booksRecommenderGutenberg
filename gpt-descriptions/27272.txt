Synopsis: Roy Blakeley and his friends embark on an exciting adventure as they take their camp on wheels to explore the great outdoors. From encountering mysterious strangers to facing unexpected challenges, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to navigate through thrilling escapades. With laughter, danger, and a touch of mystery, 'Roy Blakeley's Camp on Wheels' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to join the boys on their unforgettable journey.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Resourcefulness, Exploration
