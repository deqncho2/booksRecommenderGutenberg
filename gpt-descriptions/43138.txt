Synopsis: 
In 'Three Girls from School' by L. T. Meade, three young girls find themselves thrown together in a boarding school, each with their own unique backgrounds and secrets. As they navigate the challenges of school life, they form an unlikely bond and uncover a hidden mystery that will test their friendship and courage. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Identity, Resilience
