Synopsis: In 'Rebel Spurs' by Norton, Andre, a young Confederate soldier named Drew Rennie finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger as he embarks on a mission to deliver a secret message. With his life on the line, Drew must navigate through treacherous territory, encountering unexpected allies and enemies along the way. As he fights for survival and searches for answers, Drew's loyalty and courage are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Survival
