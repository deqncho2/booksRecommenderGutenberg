Synopsis: 
In "Biltmore Oswald: The Diary of a Hapless Recruit," we follow the misadventures of Oswald, a young man who finds himself thrust into the chaotic world of military training during World War I. From his initial confusion and struggles to his unexpected encounters with eccentric characters, Oswald's diary entries provide a humorous and insightful glimpse into the life of a hapless recruit. As he navigates the challenges of discipline, camaraderie, and the absurdities of war, Oswald's journey takes unexpected turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Humor, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Military life, Identity, Absurdity of war
