Synopsis: In the final volume of "An Old Story of My Farming Days," the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the trials and triumphs of the protagonist's farming life. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, the story delves into the complexities of love, loss, and the pursuit of happiness. As the protagonist navigates the challenges of rural life, unexpected events and encounters shape their perspective, leading to profound personal growth and self-discovery. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt storytelling, this volume brings the captivating trilogy to a satisfying conclusion.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Bildungsroman, Rural Literature

Themes: Love and Relationships, Social Change, Personal Growth, Resilience
