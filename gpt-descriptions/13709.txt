Synopsis: In the small town of Wolfville, a colorful cast of characters come alive after dark, revealing their hidden desires, secrets, and ambitions. From the cunning saloon owner to the mysterious stranger, each night brings a new tale of love, betrayal, and redemption. As the moon shines over Wolfville, the town's dark underbelly is exposed, leaving readers captivated by the unpredictable twists and turns that unfold under the cover of night.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Small-town life, Morality, Deception
