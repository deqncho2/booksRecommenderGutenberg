Synopsis: In 'Dick and Larry: Freshmen' by Lynde, Francis, two college freshmen, Dick and Larry, find themselves embarking on a wild and unpredictable journey as they navigate the challenges of their new academic and social lives. From hilarious misadventures to unexpected friendships, the duo's experiences will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what hilarious escapade awaits them next.

Genres: Comedy, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Self-discovery
