Synopsis: 
In 'The Summons of the Lord of Hosts', Bahá'u'lláh, the founder of the Bahá'í Faith, delivers a powerful and transformative message to the rulers and religious leaders of the world. Through a series of letters and proclamations, he challenges the prevailing systems of power and calls for a new era of unity, justice, and spiritual awakening. As the reader delves into the profound words of Bahá'u'lláh, they are invited to reflect on the nature of humanity, the purpose of life, and the potential for global transformation.

Genres: Religious literature, Philosophy, Inspirational

Themes: Unity, Justice, Spiritual Awakening, Global Transformation
