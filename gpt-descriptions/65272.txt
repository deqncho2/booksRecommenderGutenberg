Synopsis: In 'The Hunt Pack' by Samuel Alexander White, a group of strangers find themselves thrust into a deadly game where survival is the ultimate prize. As they navigate through treacherous terrain and face unimaginable challenges, they must rely on their wits and instincts to outsmart their pursuers. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the line between hunter and hunted becomes blurred. Will they be able to uncover the truth behind this twisted game before it's too late?

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Action

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Identity
