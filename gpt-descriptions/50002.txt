Synopsis: In 'The Hand-Made Gentleman: A Tale of the Battles of Peace' by Bacheller, Irving, we are introduced to a mysterious and enigmatic character named Mr. Latham. As the story unfolds, we follow Mr. Latham's journey through various encounters and experiences that challenge his beliefs and force him to confront his past. With each encounter, Mr. Latham's true nature is slowly revealed, leaving the reader captivated and eager to uncover the secrets that lie within his past.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Redemption, the Power of Secrets
