Synopsis: In 'Man Alone' by Don Berry, a solitary man named Wes Steele finds himself caught in a web of danger and deceit after a tragic incident leaves him isolated in the rugged Oregon wilderness. As he struggles to survive against the elements and the relentless pursuit of ruthless men, Wes must confront his own inner demons and discover the true meaning of courage and redemption. With vivid descriptions of the untamed landscape and a gripping narrative, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Isolation, Redemption
