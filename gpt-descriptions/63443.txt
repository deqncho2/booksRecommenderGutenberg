Synopsis: In 'Twenty Unsettled Miles in the Northeast Boundary', Thomas C. Mendenhall takes readers on a captivating journey through a mysterious and disputed region. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Mendenhall explores the untamed wilderness and the historical significance of this enigmatic area. As he delves into the complexities of the boundary dispute, unexpected discoveries and surprising revelations await, making this a compelling read for history enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Exploration

Themes: Boundary disputes, Wilderness exploration, Historical significance
