Synopsis: In 'Gallery of Comicalities; Embracing Humorous Sketches', authors Seymour, Robert, and Cruikshank, George take readers on a delightful journey through a collection of comical and entertaining sketches. From absurd situations to witty observations, this book presents a gallery of humorous characters and scenarios that will leave readers laughing out loud. With the clever illustrations by Cruikshank, Robert, the sketches come to life, adding an extra layer of hilarity to the already amusing tales. Get ready to be entertained by this delightful collection of comicalities that will surely brighten your day.

Genres: Humor, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Absurdity, Wit, Observational humor
