Synopsis: Step into the enchanting world of British wild-flowers with 'Wayside and Woodland Blossoms: A Pocket Guide to British Wild-flowers for the Country Rambler' by Step, Edward. This captivating pocket guide takes readers on a journey through the picturesque countryside, unveiling the hidden beauty of the diverse flora that graces the British landscape. From delicate bluebells to vibrant poppies, each flower is lovingly described, accompanied by stunning illustrations that bring them to life. Whether you are an avid nature enthusiast or simply appreciate the wonders of the natural world, this guide is sure to ignite your curiosity and deepen your appreciation for the wild-flowers that surround us.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Guide, Botany

Themes: Nature appreciation, British flora, Country exploration
