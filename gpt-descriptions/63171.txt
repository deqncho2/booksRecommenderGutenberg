Synopsis:
In 'Stories Pictures Tell. Book 7' by Flora L. Carpenter, prepare to embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales that come to life through vivid illustrations. From mysterious adventures to heartwarming friendships, each story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead. As the pictures tell their own stories, readers are invited to let their imagination soar and interpret the hidden meanings behind each captivating image. Get ready to be transported to a world where pictures truly do speak a thousand words.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes:
Imagination, Interpretation, Friendship
