Synopsis: In 'Torwood's Trust', the second volume of Evelyn Everett-Green's captivating novel series, the story continues to unfold with unexpected twists and turns. Set in the enchanting countryside of Torwood, the narrative follows the lives of a diverse cast of characters, each harboring their own secrets and desires. As the plot thickens, alliances are tested, loyalties are questioned, and the true nature of trust is put to the ultimate test. With its richly developed characters and intricate plot, 'Torwood's Trust' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the mysteries that lie within.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance
Themes: Trust, Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
