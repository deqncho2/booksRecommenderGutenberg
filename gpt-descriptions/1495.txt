Synopsis: In 'The Golf Course Mystery' by Chester K. Steele, a renowned golf course designer finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and danger when a mysterious death occurs on one of his most prestigious courses. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a trail of deceit, betrayal, and hidden motives that threaten to unravel not only his career but also his life. With each clue leading him closer to the truth, he must navigate the treacherous world of high-stakes golf and uncover the shocking secrets that lie beneath the perfectly manicured surface.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Ambition, Betrayal
