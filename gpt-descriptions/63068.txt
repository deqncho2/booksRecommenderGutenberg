Synopsis: In 'The Washington Peace Carillon' by James Marion Shull, a young musician named David embarks on a journey to restore a historic carillon in Washington, D.C. Little does he know that this task will lead him on a captivating adventure filled with mystery, romance, and unexpected discoveries. As David delves deeper into the secrets of the carillon, he uncovers a hidden world that holds the key to peace and harmony. With each chime of the bells, David's understanding of music, history, and the power of unity is transformed, forever altering his perspective on life.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Music, History, Unity, Peace
