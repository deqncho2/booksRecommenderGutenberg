Synopsis: In "Peggy Owen at Yorktown" by Lucy Foster Madison, a young girl named Peggy finds herself caught up in the midst of the American Revolution. As the British army closes in on Yorktown, Peggy's bravery and resourcefulness are put to the test. With danger lurking at every turn, Peggy must navigate the complexities of war, forge unlikely alliances, and make difficult choices that will shape her future. Will she be able to overcome the challenges and play a pivotal role in the fight for independence?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Patriotism, Identity, Sacrifice
