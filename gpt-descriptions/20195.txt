Synopsis: 
'Wood-Block Printing: A Description of the Craft of Woodcutting and Colour Printing Based on the Japanese Practice' takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of woodcutting and colour printing, drawing inspiration from the rich tradition of Japanese artistry. Through detailed descriptions and insightful techniques, the author unveils the secrets behind this ancient craft, offering a glimpse into the meticulous process of creating stunning woodblock prints. From the selection of materials to the intricacies of carving and printing, this book serves as a comprehensive guide for both beginners and seasoned artists alike. With its vivid illustrations and engaging narrative, 'Wood-Block Printing' is sure to ignite a passion for this timeless art form.

Genres: Art, Craft, Non-fiction

Themes: Traditional craftsmanship, Japanese art, Printmaking techniques
