Synopsis: In "William Mackay on T. W. Robertson's Play 'School'," William Mackay offers a captivating analysis of T. W. Robertson's renowned play "School." Mackay delves into the intricate web of relationships and secrets that unfold within the walls of a prestigious boarding school. As he explores the complex characters and their hidden motivations, Mackay skillfully navigates the twists and turns of the plot, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth behind the school's facade. This insightful critique provides a tantalizing glimpse into the captivating world of "School" and its intriguing cast of characters.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Deception, Power dynamics, Identity, Secrets
