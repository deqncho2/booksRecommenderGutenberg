Synopsis: In 'Dante' by Edmund G. Gardner, a young and ambitious artist named Dante embarks on a journey that takes him from the vibrant streets of Florence to the mysterious depths of the supernatural. As he delves into the secrets of his own past and the enigmatic world of art, Dante finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of artistic perfection. With each step he takes, Dante uncovers hidden truths that challenge his perception of reality and force him to confront the darkest corners of his own soul. This gripping tale of passion, mystery, and self-discovery will keep readers captivated until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Artistic pursuit, Love and betrayal, Self-discovery
