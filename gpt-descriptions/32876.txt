Synopsis: In 'Castle of Terror' by Liston, E. J., a group of friends embarks on a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon an ancient castle hidden deep within the woods. As they explore the eerie corridors and secret chambers, they soon realize that the castle holds dark secrets and malevolent forces that will test their courage and friendship to the limit. With each step they take, the tension rises, and they must unravel the castle's mysteries before it consumes them all.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Fear, Friendship, Supernatural forces
