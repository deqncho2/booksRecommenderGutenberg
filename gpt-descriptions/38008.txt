Synopsis: In 'Mated from the Morgue: A Tale of the Second Empire' by John Augustus O'Shea, a mysterious murder in the heart of Victorian London sets off a chain of events that leads a determined detective and a brilliant scientist to uncover a dark secret hidden within the city's underbelly. As they delve deeper into the investigation, they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden love that challenges their beliefs and pushes the boundaries of science and morality. With each step closer to the truth, they must confront their own demons and make choices that could change the course of history.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and obsession, Science and morality, Society and class dynamics
