Synopsis: 

In "Newspaper Reporting and Correspondence: A Manual for Reporters, Correspondents, and Students of Newspaper Writing," Grant Milnor Hyde presents a comprehensive guide that takes readers behind the scenes of the newspaper industry. From the basics of reporting to the intricacies of correspondence, this manual offers invaluable insights and practical advice for aspiring journalists and seasoned professionals alike. Hyde's engaging writing style and real-life examples make this manual an enjoyable and informative read, providing readers with the tools they need to excel in the fast-paced world of newspaper reporting.

Genres: Non-fiction, Journalism, Writing

Themes: Journalism ethics, News gathering techniques, Writing style and structure, Professional development
