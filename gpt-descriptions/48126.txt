Synopsis: 

Embark on a captivating journey through time and space as you explore the ancient landscapes of the Grand Canyon region. In this enthralling book, Edwin D. McKee takes you on a geological adventure, unraveling the mysteries of the awe-inspiring Grand Canyon, Zion, Bryce, Petrified Forest, and Painted Desert. From the towering cliffs to the vibrant hues of the rock formations, McKee's vivid descriptions and detailed analysis bring these remarkable landscapes to life. Discover the fascinating geological processes that shaped these natural wonders and delve into the rich history that lies within their layers. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Geology, Travel

Themes: Natural wonders, Geological processes, History
