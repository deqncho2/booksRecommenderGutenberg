Synopsis: In "The Outcast" by Selma Lagerlöf, a young man named Gösta Berling finds himself exiled from his former life as a minister due to his reckless behavior. Forced to seek refuge in a remote manor, he becomes entangled in the lives of the eccentric inhabitants, each with their own secrets and desires. As Gösta navigates the complex web of relationships, he discovers a newfound sense of purpose and redemption, ultimately leading him on a transformative journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Historical fiction, Gothic literature, Romance

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Love and Desire
