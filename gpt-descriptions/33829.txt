Synopsis: In 'The Nightriders' Feud' by McConnell, Walter Caruth, a small town is plagued by a mysterious group of outlaws known as the Nightriders. As the town's sheriff, John Dawson, sets out to bring justice to the community, he uncovers a web of secrets and betrayal that runs deeper than he could have ever imagined. With the help of a determined young woman named Emily, Dawson must navigate the treacherous landscape of the Nightriders' feud, risking everything to protect the town he loves.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Justice, Betrayal, Redemption
