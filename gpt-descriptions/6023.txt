Synopsis: 
In the small village of Cossington, Catharine Furze, a spirited and independent young woman, finds herself caught between tradition and her own desires. As she navigates the challenges of love, family, and societal expectations, Catharine's determination and resilience shine through. With a cast of colorful characters and a captivating plot, 'Catharine Furze' takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of happiness in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Independence, Love and Relationships, Social Expectations
