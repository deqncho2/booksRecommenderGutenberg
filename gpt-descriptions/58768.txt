Synopsis: 
Join adventurer Fred Burnaby as he embarks on an extraordinary journey through the vast and mysterious landscapes of Asia Minor. Mounted on horseback, Burnaby takes readers on a thrilling expedition, encountering breathtaking landscapes, treacherous terrains, and fascinating encounters with local cultures and customs. From the bustling bazaars of Istanbul to the remote mountain villages, Burnaby's vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes paint a vivid picture of his daring escapades. As he navigates through the heart of Asia Minor, readers will be enthralled by the unexpected twists and turns of his journey, making this travelogue a true page-turner.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Adventure, Human resilience
