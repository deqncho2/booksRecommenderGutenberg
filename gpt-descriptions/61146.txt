Synopsis: In 'Retief of the Red-Tape Mountain' by Keith Laumer, diplomat Jame Retief finds himself in the midst of interstellar bureaucracy as he navigates the treacherous world of alien politics. With his quick wit and unconventional methods, Retief must outmaneuver scheming bureaucrats, unravel complex conspiracies, and prevent intergalactic chaos. As he battles against red tape and corruption, Retief's daring adventures take him to the heart of power, where he must rely on his resourcefulness to save the day.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Satire

Themes: Bureaucracy, Diplomacy, Corruption, Humor
