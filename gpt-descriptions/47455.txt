Synopsis: 
In 'The Fiction Factory: Being the experience of a writer who, for twenty-two years, has kept a story-mill grinding successfully,' William Wallace Cook takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of a prolific writer. Through his own experiences, Cook unveils the secrets and challenges of producing a constant stream of stories for over two decades. From the highs of creative inspiration to the lows of writer's block, he shares the trials and triumphs of a writer's life, offering a fascinating glimpse into the inner workings of the fiction factory.

Genres: Memoir, Writing, Literary Criticism

Themes: Creativity, Perseverance, Artistic Process
