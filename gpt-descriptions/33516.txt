Synopsis: In 'Abandoned' by Jules Verne, a young man finds himself stranded on a deserted island after a shipwreck, left to fend for himself in a harsh and unforgiving environment. As he struggles to survive, he discovers mysterious clues that hint at the island's hidden secrets and a possible way to escape. With each passing day, the protagonist's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test, leading him on a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Survival

Themes: Resilience, Exploration, Discovery
