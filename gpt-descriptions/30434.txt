Synopsis: In 'Occasion ... for Disaster' by Garrett, Randall/ Janifer, Laurence M., a seemingly ordinary day takes a turn for the worse when a series of bizarre and hilarious events unfold. From a malfunctioning time machine to a mischievous alien, the protagonists find themselves caught in a whirlwind of chaos and must navigate through a string of disastrous situations. As they struggle to regain control, they soon realize that their actions have far-reaching consequences, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Comedy, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Consequences of actions, Unexpected circumstances
