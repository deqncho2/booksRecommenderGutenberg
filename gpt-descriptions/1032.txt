Synopsis: In 'The Pupil' by Henry James, a young boy named Pemberton finds himself in a peculiar situation when he becomes the private tutor to an enigmatic and unconventional young boy. As Pemberton delves deeper into his role, he discovers that his pupil's family holds secrets that could change everything. With tensions rising and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the complexities of relationships and the blurred lines between loyalty and betrayal.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Power dynamics, Deception, Identity
