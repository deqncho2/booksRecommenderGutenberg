Synopsis: In 'Cupid of Campion' by Finn, Francis J., a young and charismatic priest finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he is assigned to a small, seemingly idyllic town. As he delves deeper into the secrets of Campion, he uncovers a dark underbelly that threatens to shatter the peace and harmony of the community. With his faith and determination tested, the priest must navigate a treacherous path to bring justice and redemption to those in need, all while grappling with his own inner demons.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Religious Fiction

Themes: Faith and doubt, Morality and ethics, Redemption, Secrets and deception
