Synopsis: 
"The Diary of a Superfluous Man and Other Stories" is a collection of poignant and introspective tales by Ivan Turgenev. From the diary entries of a man who feels out of place in society to the story of a young woman torn between duty and love, these stories explore the complexities of human emotions and the struggles faced by individuals in a changing world. Turgenev's masterful storytelling delves into themes of love, loneliness, societal expectations, and the search for meaning in life.

Genres: 
Fiction, Short Stories, Literary Fiction

Themes: 
Love, Loneliness, Society, Identity, Existentialism
