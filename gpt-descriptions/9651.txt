Synopsis: 
In 'A Child-World' by James Whitcomb Riley, readers are transported to a whimsical and enchanting world where the innocence and imagination of childhood reign supreme. Through a collection of heartwarming and humorous poems, Riley captures the essence of childhood experiences, from the joys of playing outdoors to the wonders of storytelling and the magic of dreams. With vivid imagery and a nostalgic tone, this delightful work invites readers of all ages to relive the cherished moments of their own childhood and rediscover the beauty in the simplest of things.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: Childhood, Imagination, Innocence, Nostalgia
