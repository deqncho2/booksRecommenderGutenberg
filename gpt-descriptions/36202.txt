Synopsis: In "Nan Sherwood on the Mexican Border" by Annie Roe Carr, Nan finds herself embarking on a thrilling adventure as she travels to the Mexican border. Join Nan as she encounters mysterious strangers, uncovers hidden secrets, and becomes entangled in a dangerous plot. With her determination and courage, Nan must navigate through treacherous situations to protect herself and those she cares about. Will she be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
