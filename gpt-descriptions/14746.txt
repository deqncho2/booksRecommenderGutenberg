Synopsis:
"A dialogue or communication of two persons: Deuysyd and set forthe in the late[n] tonge, by the noble and famose clarke. Desiderius Erasmus intituled [the] pylgremage of pure deuotyon. Newly tra[n]slatyd into Englishe" is a thought-provoking and engaging work by Desiderius Erasmus. The book follows a conversation between two individuals, Deuysyd and Set Forthe, as they embark on a pilgrimage of pure devotion. Through their dialogue, they explore various aspects of faith, spirituality, and the pursuit of a virtuous life. This newly translated English version of Erasmus' work offers readers a unique opportunity to delve into the profound insights and reflections of this renowned scholar.

Genres:
Philosophical dialogue, Religious literature

Themes:
Faith, Spirituality, Virtue, Pilgrimage
