Synopsis: In "Occult Science in Medicine," Franz Hartmann delves into the mysterious realm of the occult and its connection to the field of medicine. Through a captivating exploration of ancient wisdom, esoteric knowledge, and hidden forces, Hartmann uncovers the hidden truths that lie beyond the conventional understanding of medical science. As he delves into the realms of astrology, mesmerism, and spiritual healing, the author challenges the reader to question the boundaries of modern medicine and consider the profound impact that unseen forces may have on our well-being. With thought-provoking anecdotes and compelling arguments, "Occult Science in Medicine" invites readers on a journey that will forever change their perception of the healing arts.

Genres: Occult, Medical, Philosophy

Themes: Esoteric knowledge, Alternative medicine, Spiritual healing, Hidden forces
