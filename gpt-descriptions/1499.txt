Synopsis: In 'Beacon Lights of History, Volume 3 part 2: Renaissance and Reformation', Lord John takes readers on a captivating journey through the transformative periods of the Renaissance and Reformation. Delving into the lives of influential figures such as Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, Martin Luther, and Queen Elizabeth I, Lord John explores the profound impact these individuals had on art, science, religion, and politics. From the revival of classical learning to the religious upheaval that reshaped Europe, this volume offers a rich tapestry of historical events and the remarkable individuals who shaped them.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Cultural history

Themes: Renaissance, Reformation, Artistic and intellectual revolution, Religious and political reform
