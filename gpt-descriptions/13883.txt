Synopsis: In 'The Tree of Heaven' by Sinclair, May, a young woman named Lily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience in the face of adversity. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century New York City, Lily navigates the challenges of poverty, discrimination, and societal expectations, all while striving to find her own path and create a better future for herself and her loved ones. As she encounters various characters and experiences, Lily's determination and strength are put to the test, ultimately leading her to uncover the true meaning of family, love, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Drama

Themes: Resilience, Identity, Social Inequality, Family, Love
