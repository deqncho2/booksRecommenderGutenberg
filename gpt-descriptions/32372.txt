Synopsis: 

'Dickens's Children: Ten Drawings' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vivid imagination of Charles Dickens, as seen through the eyes of renowned artist Jessie Willcox Smith. This beautifully illustrated book brings to life ten beloved characters from Dickens's novels, capturing their essence and emotions in stunning detail. From the mischievous Oliver Twist to the resilient Little Nell, each drawing transports readers into the heart of Dickens's literary world, evoking a sense of nostalgia and wonder. 

Genres: Art, Literature, Biography

Themes: Childhood, Resilience, Social Injustice
