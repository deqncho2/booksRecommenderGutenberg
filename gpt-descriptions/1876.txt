Synopsis: In "The Shape of Fear" by Elia Wilkinson Peattie, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious and haunting figure that has been terrorizing her small town. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the past, Mary finds herself confronting her own fears and discovering the power of courage and resilience. With each twist and turn, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Fear, Secrets, Courage, Resilience
