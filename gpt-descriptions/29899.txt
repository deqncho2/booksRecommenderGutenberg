Synopsis: 

'Marriage Enrichment Retreats: Story of a Quaker Project' takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of a unique Quaker project aimed at strengthening marriages. Written by David Robert Mace and Vera Mace, this book delves into the experiences, challenges, and triumphs of couples who participate in these retreats. Through personal stories and insightful observations, the authors explore the transformative power of love, communication, and self-reflection in the context of marriage. As the project unfolds, readers are invited to witness the profound impact these retreats have on the couples' relationships, offering a glimpse into the potential for growth and renewal within the institution of marriage.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Psychology

Themes: Marriage, Communication, Self-reflection, Relationship growth, Renewal
