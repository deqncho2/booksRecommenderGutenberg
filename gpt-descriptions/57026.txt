Synopsis: In "The Last of the Bushrangers: An Account of the Capture of the Kelly Gang," Francis Augustus Hare takes readers on a thrilling journey through the Australian outback as he recounts his pursuit and capture of the notorious Kelly Gang. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, Hare delves into the dangerous world of bushrangers, exploring the motivations and tactics of these infamous outlaws. As the chase intensifies, readers will be captivated by the twists and turns of this gripping true story.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, History

Themes: Crime and punishment, Outlaw culture, Law enforcement
