Synopsis: In 'Poor and Proud; Or, The Fortunes of Katy Redburn: A Story for Young Folks', we follow the journey of Katy Redburn, a young girl who finds herself in challenging circumstances. Despite her poverty, Katy possesses an indomitable spirit and a determination to rise above her circumstances. As she navigates through various trials and tribulations, Katy's resilience and unwavering pride become her guiding forces. This heartwarming tale explores the power of perseverance, the importance of self-worth, and the triumph of the human spirit.

Genres: Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Poverty, Resilience, Self-Worth, Perseverance
