Synopsis: In 'Divine Authority; Or, the Question: Was Joseph Smith Sent of God?' Orson Pratt delves into the intriguing and controversial life of Joseph Smith, the founder of the Mormon religion. Through meticulous research and compelling arguments, Pratt explores the evidence surrounding Smith's divine calling, leaving readers captivated by the mysteries and revelations that unfold.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious, Biography

Themes: Faith, Divine Revelation, Religious Leadership
