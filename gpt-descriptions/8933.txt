Synopsis: 
'Popular Tales from the Norse' is a collection of enchanting and captivating folk tales from the rich mythology of the Norse people. From the adventures of brave warriors and cunning tricksters to the magical encounters with mythical creatures, these tales transport readers to a world of wonder and imagination. Delve into the realms of gods and giants, explore the depths of the sea, and witness the triumph of love and bravery against all odds. Each story is a delightful blend of adventure, humor, and wisdom, leaving readers spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: 
Folklore, Mythology, Fantasy

Themes: 
Heroism, Magic, Fate, Morality
