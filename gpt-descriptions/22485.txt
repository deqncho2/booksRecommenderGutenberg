Synopsis: 

In "Devon, Its Moorlands, Streams and Coasts" by Rosalind Northcote, embark on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Devon. From the rugged moorlands to the meandering streams and breathtaking coasts, this book takes readers on an immersive exploration of the region's natural beauty. Through vivid descriptions and enchanting anecdotes, Northcote paints a vivid picture of Devon's charm, revealing hidden gems and secret spots that will leave readers yearning to experience it for themselves. 

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Nature Writing

Themes: Natural beauty, Exploration, Local culture
