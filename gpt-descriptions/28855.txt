Synopsis: In 'The Girl Scouts at Sea Crest; Or, the Wig Wag Rescue' by Lilian Garis, a group of adventurous Girl Scouts find themselves embarking on a thrilling seaside adventure. When a mysterious message is discovered, the girls must use their wit and resourcefulness to unravel the secrets hidden within Sea Crest. As they navigate through unexpected challenges and encounters, the Girl Scouts learn the importance of teamwork, bravery, and the power of friendship. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Courage, Problem-solving
