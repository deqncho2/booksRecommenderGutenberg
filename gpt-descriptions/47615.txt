Synopsis: 
In "The Saddle Boys at Circle Ranch; Or, In at the Grand Round-Up" by James Carson, Captain, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey at Circle Ranch. As they join the grand round-up, they encounter unexpected challenges and dangers that test their courage and resourcefulness. With their trusty horses and unwavering determination, the Saddle Boys navigate through the rugged terrain, forming unbreakable bonds and learning valuable life lessons along the way.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resilience, Coming of Age
