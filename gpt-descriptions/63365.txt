Synopsis: In 'The Flying Boys to the Rescue' by Edward Sylvester Ellis, a group of adventurous young boys discover a hidden secret that leads them on a thrilling journey filled with danger and excitement. As they navigate through treacherous obstacles and face unexpected challenges, their courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. With their trusty flying machines, the boys embark on a mission to save someone in desperate need, showcasing the power of friendship and the indomitable spirit of youth.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
