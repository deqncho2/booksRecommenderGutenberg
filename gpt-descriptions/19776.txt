Synopsis: In the rugged mountains of Tennessee, a young woman named Nance is caught between two suitors, each with their own secrets and desires. As she navigates the treacherous terrain of love and loyalty, Nance finds herself facing an unexpected and dangerous ordeal that will test her strength and resilience. Set against the backdrop of the breathtaking Appalachian landscape, 'The Ordeal: A Mountain Romance of Tennessee' is a captivating tale of passion, betrayal, and the indomitable spirit of the human heart.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Loyalty and Betrayal, Resilience and Survival
