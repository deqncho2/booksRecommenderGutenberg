Synopsis: In 'An Anarchist Woman' by Hapgood, Hutchins, a young woman named Sophie finds herself caught in the midst of a tumultuous political movement. As she becomes increasingly involved with anarchist ideals, Sophie must navigate a dangerous world of rebellion, love, and betrayal. With her life hanging in the balance, she must decide where her loyalties truly lie.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Romance

Themes: Anarchy, Revolution, Love and Betrayal
