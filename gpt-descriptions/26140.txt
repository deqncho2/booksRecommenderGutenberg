Synopsis: 
In the heart-pounding thriller "Security" by Ernest M. Kenyon, a former Navy SEAL turned security consultant finds himself entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger when he is hired to protect a high-profile client. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his client's life but also the security of the entire nation. With time running out, he must use all his skills and instincts to outsmart the ruthless enemies who will stop at nothing to achieve their nefarious goals.

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Action

Themes: Espionage, Betrayal, National Security
