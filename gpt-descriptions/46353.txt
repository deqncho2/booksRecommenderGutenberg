Synopsis:
In 'The Walls of Constantinople', author B. Granville Baker takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient city of Constantinople, a place steeped in history, mystery, and intrigue. As the story unfolds, we are introduced to a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets and motivations, who find themselves entangled in a web of political power struggles, forbidden love, and the looming threat of invasion. Against the backdrop of the magnificent walls that have protected the city for centuries, the fate of Constantinople hangs in the balance, and the characters must navigate treacherous alliances and personal sacrifices to ensure its survival. With its rich historical detail and gripping narrative, 'The Walls of Constantinople' is a thrilling tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Power struggles, Forbidden love, Loyalty, Survival
