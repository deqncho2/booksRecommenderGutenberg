Synopsis: 

'English Fairy Tales' by Flora Annie Steel is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that will transport readers into a world of magic, adventure, and wonder. From mischievous fairies to brave knights, this book is filled with captivating characters and thrilling tales that will leave readers spellbound. Each story is beautifully woven with vivid imagery and unexpected twists, making it impossible to put down. Whether it's a tale of love, bravery, or the triumph of good over evil, 'English Fairy Tales' is sure to captivate readers of all ages and ignite their imagination.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Bravery, Love, Good vs. Evil
