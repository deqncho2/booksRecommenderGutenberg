Synopsis: 
In "The Evolution of English Lexicography," Sir James Augustus Henry Murray takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating history of English dictionaries. From the humble beginnings of lexicography to the monumental task of compiling the Oxford English Dictionary, Murray unveils the untold stories of the dedicated individuals who dedicated their lives to capturing the ever-evolving English language. Through anecdotes, challenges, and triumphs, this book offers a captivating exploration of the intricate world of lexicography, shedding light on the profound impact dictionaries have had on our understanding of language and culture.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Linguistics

Themes: Language evolution, Lexicography, Historical linguistics
