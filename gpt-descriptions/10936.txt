Synopsis: In 'The Girl Aviators' Motor Butterfly' by Margaret Burnham, a group of adventurous young women take to the skies in their newly acquired motor butterfly, a unique flying machine. As they embark on thrilling aerial escapades, they find themselves caught up in a mysterious series of events that test their courage and ingenuity. With danger lurking at every turn, the girl aviators must rely on their skills and determination to unravel the secrets that surround them.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Female empowerment, Friendship, Courage, Exploration
