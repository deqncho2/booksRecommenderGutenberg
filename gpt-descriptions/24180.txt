Synopsis: 
In 'Alarm Clock' by Cole, Everett B., a seemingly ordinary alarm clock becomes the catalyst for a series of mysterious events that turn a young woman's life upside down. As she delves deeper into the clock's secrets, she uncovers a hidden world filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected connections. With time ticking away, she must navigate through a web of deception and make choices that could alter the course of her own destiny. Prepare to be captivated by this thrilling tale of suspense and discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Fate and destiny
