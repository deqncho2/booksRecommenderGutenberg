Synopsis: In "Molly Brown's Post-Graduate Days," we follow the spirited and ambitious Molly Brown as she navigates the challenges and adventures that come with life after college. From her first job in a bustling city to unexpected encounters with intriguing individuals, Molly's journey is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. As she strives to find her place in the world, Molly learns valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Self-discovery, Ambition, Friendship
