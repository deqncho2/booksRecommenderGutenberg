Synopsis: In "Complete Prose Works: Specimen Days and Collect, November Boughs and Goodbye My Fancy," Walt Whitman takes readers on a captivating journey through his personal experiences, reflections, and musings. From his observations of nature and society to his contemplations on life, love, and the human condition, Whitman's prose offers a profound exploration of the world around him. Through vivid descriptions and introspective narratives, he invites readers to join him in his exploration of the beauty and complexity of existence.

Genres: Autobiography, Memoir, Essay

Themes: Nature, Identity, Spirituality, Love, Democracy
