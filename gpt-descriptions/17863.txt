Synopsis: In "Blackbeard; Or, The Pirate of Roanoke," Benjamin Barker takes readers on a thrilling adventure set in the Golden Age of Piracy. The story follows the notorious pirate Blackbeard as he terrorizes the high seas, seeking treasure and revenge. As Blackbeard's legend grows, a young sailor named Robert Maynard is tasked with bringing him to justice. Filled with swashbuckling action, treacherous betrayals, and unexpected alliances, this tale of piracy and adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Power and Greed, Loyalty and Betrayal, Justice and Revenge
