Synopsis: In 'The Last Look: A Tale of the Spanish Inquisition' by Kingston, William Henry Giles, readers are transported to the dark and treacherous era of the Spanish Inquisition. The story follows the life of a young artist, Diego, who finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and betrayal. As he navigates the dangerous world of religious persecution, Diego's art becomes a powerful tool for both survival and rebellion. With each stroke of his brush, he unveils hidden truths and challenges the oppressive forces that seek to silence him. In a race against time, Diego must make a choice that could cost him everything he holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Religious persecution, Art as a form of resistance, Betrayal and loyalty
