Synopsis: In the High Valley follows the final chapter of Katy Carr's life as she embarks on a new adventure in a picturesque valley. Set against the backdrop of the beautiful Swiss Alps, Katy and her family face unexpected challenges and discover the true meaning of love, friendship, and resilience. As they navigate through the highs and lows of life, they learn valuable lessons that will shape their future and leave readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Family, Love, Resilience, Growth
