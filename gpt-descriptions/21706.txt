Synopsis: In 'Twice Bought' by R. M. Ballantyne, a young boy named Dick Varley finds himself thrust into a world of adventure and danger when he is kidnapped and sold into slavery. Separated from his family and forced to work on a plantation in the West Indies, Dick must navigate treacherous waters, encounter pirates, and face the harsh realities of life as a slave. Determined to regain his freedom and reunite with his loved ones, Dick embarks on a thrilling journey that will test his courage and resilience.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Freedom, Resilience, Family
