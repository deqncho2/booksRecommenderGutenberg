Synopsis: 
In 'The Blue Grass Seminary Girls on the Water: Or, Exciting Adventures on a Summer Cruise Through the Panama Canal', a group of adventurous young women from Kentucky embark on a thrilling summer cruise through the Panama Canal. As they navigate the treacherous waters and encounter unexpected challenges, the girls must rely on their wit, courage, and friendship to overcome the obstacles that come their way. With each twist and turn, they discover the true meaning of resilience and the power of unity. 

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resilience
