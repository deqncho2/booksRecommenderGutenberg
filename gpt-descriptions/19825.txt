Synopsis: In 'Herself: Talks with Women Concerning Themselves', Edith Belle Lowry delves into the lives of women from various walks of life, engaging in intimate conversations that reveal their deepest thoughts, struggles, and triumphs. From artists to activists, mothers to entrepreneurs, each woman shares her unique story, shedding light on the complexities of womanhood and the power of resilience. Through these heartfelt dialogues, Lowry invites readers to explore the multifaceted experiences of women and discover the strength that lies within them.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Women's Studies

Themes: Identity, Empowerment, Resilience, Gender Roles, Intersectionality
