Synopsis: Nan Sherwood's Summer Holidays follows the spirited and adventurous Nan Sherwood as she embarks on a thrilling summer vacation. Leaving behind her beloved Lakeview Hall, Nan finds herself in a new and unfamiliar setting, surrounded by captivating landscapes and intriguing characters. As she navigates through unexpected challenges and uncovers hidden secrets, Nan's summer holidays become a transformative journey of self-discovery and personal growth. With each passing day, Nan's determination and resilience are put to the test, leading her to unexpected friendships and unforgettable experiences. Will Nan's summer holidays be everything she hoped for, or will she face unforeseen obstacles that will change her life forever?

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Coming of Age, Friendship, Exploration, Independence
