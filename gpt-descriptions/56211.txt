Synopsis: 
In "Recollections of Rifleman Harris, (Old 95th.): with anecdotes of his officers and his comrades," Benjamin Harris takes us on a captivating journey through his experiences as a rifleman during the Napoleonic Wars. From the brutal battles of the Peninsular Campaign to the camaraderie and hardships of life in the British Army, Harris shares his unique perspective as an ordinary soldier amidst extraordinary circumstances. Through his vivid recollections and entertaining anecdotes, we gain insight into the courage, resilience, and sacrifices of the men who fought alongside him.

Genres: 
Memoir, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: 
Warfare, Camaraderie, Resilience, Sacrifice
