Synopsis: In 'The Career of Claudia' by Frances Mary Peard, readers are introduced to Claudia, a young woman with a burning desire for independence and success in a society that expects her to conform. As she navigates the challenges of pursuing her dreams, Claudia finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own ambitions. With determination and resilience, she embarks on a journey that will test her strength and redefine her understanding of success. Will Claudia be able to carve her own path and find fulfillment in her career, or will she succumb to the pressures of society? 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Independence, Ambition, Society's Expectations
