Synopsis: In "Nightmare on the Nose" by Smith, Evelyn E., a young woman named Lily discovers a mysterious and unsettling secret hidden within her own sense of smell. As she delves deeper into this enigmatic world, she becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where the line between reality and nightmares blurs. With her sanity at stake, Lily must navigate through a labyrinth of olfactory clues to uncover the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Fear
