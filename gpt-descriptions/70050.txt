Synopsis: 

In the heart-pounding world of horse racing, 'Racehorses in Australia' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the captivating lives of these majestic creatures and the people who dedicate their lives to them. Set against the backdrop of the Australian racing industry, this gripping tale explores the intense competition, the high-stakes gambles, and the unbreakable bond between horse and rider. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the pursuit of victory becomes a matter of life and death. 

Genres: Sports, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Passion, Ambition, Loyalty, Betrayal
