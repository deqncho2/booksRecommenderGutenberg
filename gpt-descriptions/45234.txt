Synopsis: 
'After Prison--What?' by Booth, Maud Ballington takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of individuals who have experienced incarceration and the challenges they face upon reentering society. Through powerful storytelling and insightful analysis, the author delves into the complexities of post-prison life, exploring the struggles, triumphs, and ultimate redemption of these individuals. This thought-provoking narrative sheds light on the importance of second chances and the transformative power of compassion and support.

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Social Justice

Themes: Rehabilitation, Reintegration, Second chances, Redemption
