Synopsis: In 'The Pagans' by Bates, Arlo, a small town is shaken by a series of mysterious events that seem to be connected to an ancient pagan ritual. As the townspeople grapple with their own beliefs and fears, a group of unlikely allies must uncover the truth behind these dark occurrences before it's too late. With secrets, suspense, and a touch of the supernatural, 'The Pagans' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Belief systems, Small-town dynamics, Uncovering the truth
