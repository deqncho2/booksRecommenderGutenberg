Synopsis: In 'The Space Flame' by Alexander M. Phillips, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious phenomenon that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but the fate of the entire universe. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of intrigue and betrayal to unlock the truth and save humanity from impending doom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the power of knowledge, the consequences of scientific discovery
