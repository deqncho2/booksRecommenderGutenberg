Synopsis: In 'Kari the Elephant', Mukerji takes readers on a captivating journey through the lush jungles of India, where a young boy named Kari forms an extraordinary bond with an enchanting elephant. As Kari and the elephant embark on thrilling adventures together, they encounter danger, friendship, and the timeless wisdom of nature. This heartwarming tale explores the power of companionship, the beauty of the natural world, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Nature, Courage, Coming of Age
