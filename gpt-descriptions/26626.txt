Synopsis: In 'Our Little Brown House, A Poem of West Point', Maria L. Stewart takes readers on a captivating journey to the prestigious United States Military Academy. Set during the New Year's Festival at the Cadets' Sabbath-school, the poem explores the lives of the cadets and their experiences within the walls of West Point. Through vivid imagery and heartfelt verses, Stewart delves into the challenges, dreams, and camaraderie that define these young men's lives. As the poem unfolds, readers are invited to witness the unique bond formed within the little brown house and the profound impact it has on the cadets' character and future. 

Genres: Poetry, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Duty, Sacrifice, Pursuit of Excellence
