Synopsis: In 'A Blundering Boy: A Humorous Story' by Munro, Bruce Weston, follow the misadventures of a young boy named Charlie as he navigates through a series of comical mishaps and hilarious misunderstandings. From mistaken identities to outrageous pranks, Charlie's blunders will keep readers laughing and eagerly turning the pages. But amidst the laughter, there may be some unexpected lessons to be learned along the way.

Genres: Humor, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Growth
