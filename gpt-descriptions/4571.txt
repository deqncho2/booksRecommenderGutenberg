Synopsis: In 'Master Sunshine' by Fraser, C. F., Mrs., a mysterious and enigmatic character named Master Sunshine arrives in a small town, bringing with him a radiant energy that captivates the townspeople. As the residents become increasingly drawn to his charm and charisma, they find themselves questioning their own lives and desires. However, as the true nature of Master Sunshine's intentions slowly unravels, the town is faced with a choice that will challenge their beliefs and force them to confront their deepest fears.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Manipulation, Morality, Power
