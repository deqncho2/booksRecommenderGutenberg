Synopsis: In 'The Reverberator' by Henry James, a young American journalist arrives in Paris with a scandalous invention that threatens to expose the secrets of the city's elite. As he delves into the lives of the upper class, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, love affairs, and social politics. With each revelation, the reverberations of his actions grow stronger, leaving readers captivated by the consequences of his meddling.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Society and class, Morality and ethics, Intrigue and gossip
