Synopsis: In 'Harold: the Last of the Saxon Kings - Volume 07', the gripping tale continues as Harold, the valiant Saxon king, faces a series of treacherous challenges that threaten to shatter his kingdom. As he navigates through political intrigue, romantic entanglements, and the ever-looming threat of invasion, Harold must summon all his strength and cunning to protect his people and secure his legacy. With the fate of England hanging in the balance, this volume takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and politics, Loyalty and betrayal, Love and sacrifice
