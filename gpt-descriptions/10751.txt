Synopsis: 
'Oregon, Washington and Alaska; Sights and Scenes for the Tourist' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the breathtaking landscapes and hidden gems of the Pacific Northwest. From the majestic mountains of Oregon to the rugged coastlines of Washington and the untamed wilderness of Alaska, this travel guide offers a captivating exploration of the region's natural wonders, cultural landmarks, and unique experiences. With vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, author E. L. Lomax paints a vivid picture of the region's beauty, inviting readers to embark on their own unforgettable adventure.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Cultural Heritage
