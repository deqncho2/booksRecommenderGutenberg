Synopsis: 
'Going Afoot: A book on walking' takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the art of walking, exploring the profound impact it can have on our physical, mental, and spiritual well-being. Christy, Bayard Henderson, an avid walker and philosopher, delves into the depths of this seemingly simple act, uncovering its hidden wonders and revealing the transformative power it holds. From the bustling streets of the city to the serene trails of nature, this book invites readers to embark on a captivating exploration of the world on foot, discovering the beauty and meaning that can be found in every step.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Travel

Themes: Walking as a form of self-discovery, the connection between walking and creativity, the healing power of nature, the philosophy of movement and mindfulness.
