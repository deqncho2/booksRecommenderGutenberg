Synopsis: 

"For the Temple: A Tale of the Fall of Jerusalem" by G. A. Henty is a gripping historical adventure set during the tumultuous times of the Roman Empire. The story follows the journey of a young Jewish boy named John, who finds himself caught in the midst of the fierce conflict between the Jews and the Romans. As Jerusalem faces the imminent threat of destruction, John must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to protect his people and their sacred temple. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Bravery, Sacrifice, Cultural Identity
