Synopsis: 

In "Four Years a Scout and Spy: 'General Bunker'", readers are taken on an exhilarating journey through the experiences of Corporal Ruggles, one of Lieutenant General Grant's most audacious and triumphant scouts. Set during the American Civil War, this gripping narrative unveils the thrilling adventures and perilous missions undertaken by Ruggles as he serves as a scout and spy for the Federal Army. From daring infiltrations behind enemy lines to gathering crucial intelligence, Ruggles' bravery and resourcefulness are put to the test time and again. As the story unfolds, readers are immersed in a world of danger, intrigue, and the relentless pursuit of victory.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Bravery, Espionage, Loyalty, Sacrifice
