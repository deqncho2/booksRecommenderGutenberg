Synopsis: 

Join the adventurous journey of a group of young explorers as they embark on a thrilling camping trip along the majestic St. Lawrence River. Led by their knowledgeable guide, they follow in the footsteps of the early discoverers, uncovering hidden treasures and encountering unexpected challenges along the way. From breathtaking landscapes to mysterious historical sites, this captivating tale will transport readers to a world of exploration and discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, History, Friendship, Nature
