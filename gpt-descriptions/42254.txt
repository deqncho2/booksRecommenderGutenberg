Synopsis:
In "Beyond the Black River," Conan the Cimmerian finds himself in the untamed wilderness of the Black River, a land teeming with danger and ancient evil. As he becomes embroiled in a deadly conflict between settlers and savage tribes, Conan must navigate treacherous terrain, face formidable foes, and uncover the dark secrets that lie hidden in the heart of the wilderness. With his strength, cunning, and indomitable will, Conan must fight to survive and protect those he holds dear in this thrilling and action-packed adventure.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Adventure, Sword and Sorcery

Themes:
Survival, Conflict, Exploration, Cultural Clash
