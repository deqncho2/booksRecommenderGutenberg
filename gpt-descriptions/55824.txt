Synopsis: 
'From Dan to Beersheba: A Description of the Wonderful Land, with Maps and Engravings and a Prologue by the Author Containing the Latest Explorations and Discoveries' takes readers on an immersive journey through the captivating landscapes of the Holy Land. Written by John Philip Newman, this travelogue combines vivid descriptions, detailed maps, and enchanting engravings to paint a vivid picture of the region. With a prologue that unveils the latest explorations and discoveries, the book offers a fresh perspective on this ancient land, enticing readers to explore its wonders for themselves.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Cultural Heritage
