Synopsis: 
In "Sketches and Studies in Italy and Greece, First Series," John Addington Symonds takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Italy and Greece. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Symonds paints a vivid picture of the cultural, historical, and artistic treasures he encounters during his travels. From the bustling streets of Rome to the serene beauty of the Greek islands, this collection of sketches and studies offers a unique perspective on the rich heritage of these iconic Mediterranean countries.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Art History

Themes: Cultural exploration, Historical significance, Artistic appreciation
