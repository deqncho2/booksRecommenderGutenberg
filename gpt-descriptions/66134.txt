Synopsis: In 'Jabberwock, Beware!' by Richard A. Sternbach, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances, leaving the community in a state of fear and uncertainty. As the townspeople struggle to uncover the truth behind these unsettling events, they soon realize that they are facing a formidable and ancient evil lurking in the shadows. With time running out, a group of unlikely heroes must band together to confront the malevolent force and save their town from its impending doom.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Thriller

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Courage, Community
