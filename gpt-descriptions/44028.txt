Synopsis: 
'The Every Day Book of History and Chronology' is a captivating and comprehensive collection that takes readers on a journey through time, exploring the anniversaries of significant individuals and events from the beginning of the world until the present day. From ancient civilizations to modern revolutions, this book provides a daily dose of historical knowledge, offering intriguing insights into the diverse tapestry of human history. With each turn of the page, readers will uncover fascinating stories, remarkable achievements, and pivotal moments that have shaped our world.

Genres: 
Historical Non-fiction, Reference, Almanac

Themes: 
History, Chronology, Significance of Anniversaries
