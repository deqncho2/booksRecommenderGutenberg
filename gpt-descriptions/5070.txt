Synopsis: In 'The Doctor's Dilemma' by Bernard Shaw, a renowned doctor finds himself torn between his duty to save lives and the moral dilemma of choosing who deserves to be saved. When a talented artist falls ill, the doctor must make a difficult decision that challenges his beliefs and forces him to confront the complexities of human nature. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the doctor's ethical dilemma becomes increasingly entangled. This thought-provoking play delves into the depths of human morality and the consequences of playing God.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Morality, Ethics, Responsibility, Artistic Integrity
