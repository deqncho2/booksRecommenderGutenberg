Synopsis: 

In this intriguing historical document, Canada's Post Office Department presents a comprehensive list of post offices in Canada, along with the names of the postmasters, covering the year 1856. As an added bonus, a supplementary list up to March 1st, 1857, is included, making it a valuable resource for anyone interested in the postal system of that era. Delve into the past and discover the vast network of post offices that connected communities across Canada during this pivotal time in history.

Genres: Historical, Reference

Themes: Communication, Infrastructure, Historical Documentation
