Synopsis: In 'And So They Were Married' by Florence Morse Kingsley, a young woman named Ruth finds herself caught between duty and desire when she is forced into an arranged marriage with a man she barely knows. As Ruth navigates the challenges of her new life, she discovers unexpected friendships, hidden secrets, and a love that defies societal expectations. Will she find the courage to follow her heart and forge her own path, or will she succumb to the pressures of society? This captivating tale explores the complexities of love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of happiness in a world bound by tradition and societal norms.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and duty, societal expectations, self-discovery
