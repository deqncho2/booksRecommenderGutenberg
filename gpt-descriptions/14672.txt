Synopsis: 
In 'The Religion of the Ancient Celts', J.A. MacCulloch delves into the fascinating world of the ancient Celtic people and their religious beliefs. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, MacCulloch uncovers the mysteries surrounding the Celtic gods and goddesses, their rituals, and the profound influence of their religion on Celtic society. From the sacred groves to the enigmatic druids, this book offers a captivating exploration of a long-lost spiritual tradition that continues to intrigue and inspire.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Mythology

Themes: Celtic religion, Mythology, Rituals, Spirituality
