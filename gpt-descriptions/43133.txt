Synopsis: Imogen, a young and spirited girl, finds herself thrust into a world of unexpected challenges and adventures as she turns eighteen. From the bustling streets of London to the enchanting countryside, Imogen encounters a cast of intriguing characters who will shape her journey of self-discovery. With her wit, determination, and a touch of mischief, Imogen navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations, all while staying true to herself. As she embraces the joys and sorrows of adulthood, Imogen's story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Friendship, Society's expectations
