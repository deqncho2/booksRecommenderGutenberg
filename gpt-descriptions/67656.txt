Synopsis: In 'South-West Africa' by William Eveleigh, a young journalist named Sarah embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious disappearance in the remote and treacherous landscapes of South-West Africa. As she delves deeper into the investigation, Sarah becomes entangled in a web of secrets, political intrigue, and personal danger. With each step closer to the truth, she discovers shocking revelations that challenge her beliefs and force her to confront her own past. This gripping tale of suspense and adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Truth and deception, Political intrigue, Personal growth
