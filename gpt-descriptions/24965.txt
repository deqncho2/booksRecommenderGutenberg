Synopsis: In 'The Deadly Daughters' by Winston K. Marks, a small town is shaken by a series of mysterious deaths, all seemingly connected to a group of young women. As the body count rises, a determined detective must unravel the dark secrets that bind these deadly daughters together, before it's too late. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking truth is revealed.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Family secrets, Revenge, Female empowerment
