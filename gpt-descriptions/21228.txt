Synopsis: In "White Lilac; or the Queen of the May" by Amy Walton, a young girl named Lilian finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she discovers a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the past, she uncovers a tale of forbidden love, betrayal, and a long-lost treasure. With the help of her loyal friends, Lilian embarks on a thrilling adventure to unravel the truth and restore justice to her family's name.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Family secrets, Love and betrayal, Friendship, Identity
