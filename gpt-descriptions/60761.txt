Synopsis: In 'The Good Seed' by Reynolds Mack, a small town is shaken by a mysterious event that leaves the community divided and questioning their beliefs. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, a young woman named Lily finds herself at the center of it all, forced to confront her own past and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of her town. With its gripping plot and complex characters, 'The Good Seed' explores the power of forgiveness, the consequences of secrets, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Forgiveness, Secrets, Redemption, Community
