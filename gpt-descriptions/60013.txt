Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of M. M. Mangasarian' by Mangasarian, M. M. (Mangasar Mugurditch) is a captivating collection of thought-provoking essays and speeches by the renowned author. This compilation delves into a wide range of topics, including religion, philosophy, ethics, and social issues. Through his eloquent and insightful writing, Mangasarian challenges conventional beliefs and encourages readers to question the status quo. With each piece, he invites us to explore the depths of our own thoughts and beliefs, sparking a desire for intellectual growth and self-discovery.

Genres: Essays, Speeches, Philosophy

Themes: Religion, Ethics, Social Issues, Intellectual Growth, Self-Discovery
