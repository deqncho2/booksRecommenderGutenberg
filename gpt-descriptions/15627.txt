Synopsis: In 'Verner's Pride' by Mrs. Henry Wood, a young woman named Lucy Verner finds herself caught between duty and desire when she is forced to make a difficult decision that will shape her future. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Lucy must confront the consequences of her choices and discover the true meaning of pride.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Victorian Literature

Themes: Love and Duty, Social Expectations, Personal Identity
