Synopsis: 
In 'Rambles on Railways' by Roney, Cusack P., Sir, embark on a captivating journey through the world of railways, as the author takes you on a series of delightful and insightful adventures. From the bustling city stations to the picturesque countryside, this book offers a unique perspective on the history, engineering marvels, and cultural significance of railways. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Roney explores the transformative power of trains, revealing the hidden stories behind their creation and the impact they have had on society. Whether you are a railway enthusiast or simply curious about the wonders of locomotion, 'Rambles on Railways' is sure to captivate and inspire.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, History

Themes: Transportation, Engineering, Cultural impact
