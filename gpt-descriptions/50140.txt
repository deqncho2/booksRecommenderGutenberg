Synopsis: 

In 'The Story of the Thirty-second Regiment, Massachusetts Infantry: Whence it came; where it went; what it saw, and what it did', Francis J. Parker takes us on a captivating journey through the experiences of the Thirty-second Regiment during the American Civil War. From their humble beginnings to their heroic battles, this book unveils the untold stories of these brave soldiers, revealing the true essence of their sacrifices and triumphs.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Sacrifice, Brotherhood
