Synopsis: In 'The Declaration of Independence: An Address' by Warren, Winslow, a renowned historian delves into the fascinating story behind one of the most influential documents in American history. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, the author uncovers the untold struggles, debates, and sacrifices that led to the creation of the Declaration of Independence. From the secret meetings of the Founding Fathers to the profound impact of this revolutionary document, this address offers a fresh perspective on the birth of a nation.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political Science

Themes: Revolution, Freedom, Democracy, Founding Fathers, American History
