Synopsis: In "The Psychical Researcher's Tale - The Sceptical Poltergeist: From "The New Decameron", Volume III," J.D. Beresford takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of the supernatural. Set in the early 20th century, the story follows a renowned psychical researcher who is determined to debunk the existence of poltergeists. However, when he is confronted with a series of inexplicable events in a seemingly ordinary household, he is forced to question his skepticism and confront the possibility that there may be more to the paranormal than he ever imagined.

Genres: Supernatural fiction, Mystery, Psychological thriller

Themes: Skepticism vs. Belief, Exploration of the Supernatural, Human Perception and Reality
