Synopsis: In 'Ghosts: A Domestic Tragedy in Three Acts', Henrik Ibsen delves into the dark secrets and hidden truths that haunt a seemingly idyllic family. As the story unfolds, the audience is drawn into a web of deceit, betrayal, and the consequences of societal expectations. With each act, the tension builds, revealing the devastating impact of the past on the present and the lengths people will go to protect their reputation. This gripping play explores the complexities of human relationships and the destructive power of societal norms.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Family secrets, societal expectations, betrayal, the consequences of the past
