Synopsis: In 'War Game' by Bryce Walton, a group of scientists invents a groundbreaking technology that allows them to simulate and predict the outcome of wars. As they delve deeper into their creation, they soon realize that their invention has the power to change the course of history. However, they must navigate the ethical dilemmas and personal sacrifices that come with wielding such immense power. With the fate of nations hanging in the balance, the scientists find themselves caught in a dangerous game where the line between simulation and reality becomes increasingly blurred.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Political

Themes: Ethics, Power, Consequences
