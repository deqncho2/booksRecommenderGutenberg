Synopsis: 
In "Personal Recollections of a Cavalryman: With Custer's Michigan Cavalry Brigade in the Civil War," James Harvey Kidd takes readers on a thrilling journey through his experiences as a cavalryman during the American Civil War. From the intense battles and daring charges to the camaraderie and hardships faced by the soldiers, Kidd's vivid and personal recollections provide a unique perspective on this pivotal period in history. As he recounts his time serving under General George Armstrong Custer, readers will be captivated by the bravery, sacrifice, and resilience of the men who fought alongside him.

Genres: Historical memoir, Military history, Biography

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Historical significance
