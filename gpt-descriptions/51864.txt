Synopsis: 
In 'Tales, Traditions and Romance of Border and Revolutionary Times', Edward Sylvester Ellis takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous era of the American Revolution and the untamed frontier. Through a collection of thrilling tales and vivid descriptions, Ellis paints a vivid picture of the challenges and triumphs faced by the brave men and women who shaped the destiny of a nation. From daring escapes and perilous battles to forbidden romances and unexpected alliances, this book offers a captivating blend of history, adventure, and romance that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Love and Sacrifice
