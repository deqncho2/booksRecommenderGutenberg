Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The Death Ship: A Strange Story', the mysterious and eerie events aboard the ghostly ship continue to unfold. As the crew members grapple with their own fears and the haunting presence that surrounds them, they are forced to confront the dark secrets that lie within the ship's history. With each passing day, the tension and suspense escalate, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly awaiting the chilling revelations that await them in the final volume.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Secrets, Haunting
