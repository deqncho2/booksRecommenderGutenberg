Synopsis: In "The Promotion of the Admiral, and Other Sea Comedies" by Roberts Morley, embark on a hilarious journey filled with eccentric characters, unexpected twists, and uproarious situations. From the misadventures of a bumbling admiral to the comical encounters of sailors at sea, this collection of sea comedies will keep you entertained from start to finish. Prepare to be swept away by the witty dialogue, slapstick humor, and clever plotlines that will leave you laughing out loud.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Miscommunication, Social hierarchy, Absurdity
