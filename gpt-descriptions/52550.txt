Synopsis: 

In 'A Short History of Christianity: Second Edition, Revised, With Additions', J.M. Robertson takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins and development of one of the world's most influential religions. From the early days of Jesus Christ and the formation of the early Christian communities, to the rise of the Roman Catholic Church and the Protestant Reformation, Robertson skillfully weaves together historical events, theological debates, and the impact of Christianity on society.

With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Robertson explores the diverse branches of Christianity, shedding light on the complex relationship between faith and power, the struggles for religious authority, and the profound transformations that have shaped the religion over the centuries. From the early Church Fathers to the challenges of modernity, this book offers a comprehensive and thought-provoking exploration of Christianity's rich and complex history.

Genres: History, Religion, Non-fiction
Themes: Religious development, Power and authority, Transformation
