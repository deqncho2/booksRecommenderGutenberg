Synopsis: In "The Brothers Van Eyck," Konody delves into the fascinating lives of the renowned Flemish painters, Jan and Hubert Van Eyck. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant art scene of the 15th century, this captivating biography unravels the mysteries surrounding the Van Eyck brothers' artistic techniques, their influential patrons, and the secrets hidden within their masterpieces. As the brothers navigate the complexities of their personal lives and the political turmoil of their time, readers are transported into a world of artistic brilliance, intrigue, and the pursuit of perfection.

Genres: Biography, Art History, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic genius, Renaissance culture, Secrets and symbolism in art
