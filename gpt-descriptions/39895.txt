Synopsis: In 'The Church of St. Bunco: A Drastic Treatment of a Copyrighted Religion-- Un-Christian Non-Science' by Clark, Gordon, a small town is turned upside down when a charismatic leader introduces a new religion that challenges traditional beliefs. As the townspeople become enthralled by the Church of St. Bunco, a group of skeptics begins to question the true intentions behind this copyrighted religion. With tensions rising and secrets being uncovered, the battle between faith and reason reaches a boiling point, forcing the community to confront their deepest fears and reevaluate their own beliefs.

Genres: Satire, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Religion, Faith vs. Reason, Manipulation
