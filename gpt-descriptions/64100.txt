Synopsis: In this captivating and beautifully illustrated book, the mysterious origins and enchanting legends surrounding Santa Claus, Kriss Kringle, and St. Nicholas are explored. Delve into the rich tapestry of folklore, history, and cultural traditions that have shaped the beloved figure we know today. From ancient myths to modern-day celebrations, this book unveils the captivating journey of Santa Claus through time and across continents, revealing surprising connections and heartwarming tales along the way.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Folklore

Themes: Legends and myths, Cultural traditions, Holiday spirit
