Synopsis: 
'Elementary Instruction in the Art of Illuminating and Missal Painting on Vellum: A Guide to Modern Illuminators' by Laurent de Lara, D. is a captivating and comprehensive guide that unveils the secrets of the ancient art of illuminating and missal painting. Filled with intricate techniques, step-by-step instructions, and stunning examples, this book takes readers on a journey through the history and practice of this exquisite craft. From the selection of materials to the delicate brushstrokes, de Lara's expertise shines through, making this guide an indispensable resource for both aspiring and experienced illuminators.

Genres: Art, Craft, History

Themes: Illumination, Painting techniques, Historical artistry
