Synopsis: 
In the small town of Hixon, Georgia, Abner Daniel, a young and ambitious lawyer, finds himself entangled in a web of scandal, secrets, and forbidden love. As he navigates the complexities of small-town politics and the dark underbelly of society, Abner must confront his own moral compass and make difficult choices that will shape his future. With vivid characters and a gripping narrative, 'Abner Daniel: A Novel' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the depths of human nature and the consequences of one man's actions.

Genres: Drama, Southern Gothic, Historical Fiction

Themes: Morality, Small-town politics, Forbidden love, Consequences of actions
