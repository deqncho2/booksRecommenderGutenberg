Synopsis: In Volume 15 of his memoirs, Jacques Casanova de Seingalt finds himself in the company of the renowned philosopher and writer, Voltaire. As they embark on a journey together, their encounters with influential figures and their discussions on love, philosophy, and the pursuit of happiness reveal the complexities of human nature and the power of intellectual discourse. Casanova's captivating storytelling takes readers on a thrilling adventure filled with wit, charm, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and Desire, Intellectualism, Human Nature
