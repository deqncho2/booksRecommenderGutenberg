Synopsis: 
In 'Te Tohunga: The ancient legends and traditions of the Maoris', author Wilhelm Dittmer delves into the rich mythology and cultural heritage of the Maori people. Through a collection of captivating legends and traditions, Dittmer takes readers on a journey through the ancient world of the Maoris, exploring their beliefs, rituals, and the extraordinary powers of the tohunga, the spiritual leaders of the Maori society. From tales of creation and the gods to stories of heroes and mythical creatures, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the Maori culture and their deep connection with the natural world.

Genres: Mythology, Cultural Studies, History

Themes: Maori culture, Mythology, Spirituality, Nature
