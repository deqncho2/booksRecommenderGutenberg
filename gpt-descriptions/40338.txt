Synopsis: In "The Further Adventures of Mr. Verdant Green, an Oxford Under-Graduate," we follow the hilarious and mischievous escapades of the lovable Oxford student, Mr. Verdant Green. From navigating the complexities of love and friendship to getting entangled in outrageous pranks and absurd situations, Mr. Green's journey through university life takes unexpected turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Comedy, Coming-of-age, Satire

Themes: University life, Friendship, Love and romance, Maturity, Social satire
