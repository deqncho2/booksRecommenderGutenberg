Synopsis: In the mystical land of Arz, a group of skilled anglers embark on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of the enchanted waters. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter mythical creatures, they must rely on their wit, courage, and the power of their fishing rods to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. With each catch, they unravel a deeper mystery that could change the fate of Arz forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Destiny, Magic
