Synopsis: In 'First in the Field: A Story of New South Wales' by George Manville Fenn, readers are transported to the vast and untamed lands of Australia. The story follows the adventures of a young protagonist who embarks on a journey to New South Wales, where he encounters a myriad of challenges and unexpected encounters. From dangerous wildlife to treacherous landscapes, the protagonist must navigate the harsh realities of the Australian wilderness while also uncovering the secrets of the land. As the story unfolds, readers are taken on a thrilling and captivating exploration of the early days of New South Wales, filled with suspense, danger, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Identity, Colonialism
