Synopsis:
In 'The Disowned - Volume 01', the reader is introduced to the enigmatic and charismatic protagonist, Ernest Maltravers. Born into a wealthy family, Ernest is disenchanted with the superficiality of his privileged life and yearns for something more meaningful. As he embarks on a journey of self-discovery, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, including the captivating Alice Darvil, who challenges his beliefs and ignites a passionate love affair. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century England, this gripping tale explores the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of personal fulfillment.

Genres: Romantic fiction, Bildungsroman, Historical fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Identity and self-discovery, Social class and privilege
