Synopsis: In "The Shadowy Third, and Other Stories," Ellen Anderson Gholson Glasgow presents a collection of captivating tales that delve into the complexities of human relationships and the hidden depths of the human psyche. From a mysterious presence that haunts a newlywed couple to the secrets and desires that lie beneath the surface of a seemingly perfect marriage, these stories explore the shadows that lurk within us all. With her keen insight and masterful storytelling, Glasgow takes readers on a journey through the intricacies of love, betrayal, and the haunting power of the past.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Love and Relationships, Secrets and Betrayal, Psychological Intrigue
