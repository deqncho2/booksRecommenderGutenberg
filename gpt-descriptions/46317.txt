Synopsis: In 'Ella Clinton; or, By Their Fruits Ye Shall Know Them', we follow the journey of Ella Clinton, a young woman who finds herself caught between the allure of wealth and the importance of moral character. As she navigates through the complexities of society, Ella must make difficult choices that will shape her future and reveal the true nature of those around her. With secrets, scandals, and unexpected twists, this captivating novel explores the consequences of one's actions and the power of personal integrity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Morality, Social Class, Personal Integrity
