Synopsis: In 'The Assault on Mount Everest, 1922', Bruce, C. G. takes readers on a thrilling expedition to conquer the world's highest peak. Set against the backdrop of the treacherous Himalayas, this gripping account follows a team of courageous climbers as they face unimaginable challenges and push the limits of human endurance. As they navigate through dangerous terrain and battle against the unforgiving elements, the climbers must confront their own fears and test the strength of their camaraderie. Will they succeed in reaching the summit or will Mount Everest prove to be an insurmountable foe?

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Historical

Themes: Perseverance, Exploration, Teamwork, Human resilience
