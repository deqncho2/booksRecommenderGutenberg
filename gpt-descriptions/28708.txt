Synopsis: 

'Practical Education, Volume I' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of two siblings, Emily and Henry, as they navigate the challenges and triumphs of their education. Set in the late 18th century, this novel explores the unconventional methods employed by their father, Mr. Stanley, to provide them with a well-rounded education. From hands-on experiences to moral lessons, the siblings embark on a series of adventures that shape their character and intellect. As they encounter various obstacles and make important choices, Emily and Henry learn valuable life lessons that extend far beyond the classroom. 

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Educational

Themes: Education, Morality, Personal growth
