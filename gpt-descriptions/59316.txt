Synopsis: 

'Computers on the Farm: Farm Uses for Computers, How to Select Software and Hardware, and Online Information Sources in Agriculture' by Deborah Takiff Smith is a comprehensive guide that explores the integration of technology in the agricultural industry. From the practical applications of computers on the farm to the selection of suitable software and hardware, this book provides valuable insights into harnessing the power of technology in agriculture. Additionally, it delves into the vast array of online information sources available to farmers, enabling them to stay updated with the latest advancements in the field. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Technology, Agriculture

Themes: Technology in agriculture, Farm management, Information sources
