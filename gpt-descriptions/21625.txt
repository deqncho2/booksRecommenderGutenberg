Synopsis: In 'Play the Game!' by Ruth Comfort Mitchell, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. As she embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the secrets of the map, Mary must navigate through a world of deception, danger, and unexpected romance. With each twist and turn, she must outsmart her adversaries and trust her instincts to survive. Will Mary be able to solve the puzzle and claim the treasure, or will she become a pawn in a much larger game?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Treasure hunting, Deception, Trust, Survival
