Synopsis: In 'Wilmshurst of the Frontier Force' by Percy F. Westerman, readers are taken on an exhilarating adventure through the rugged landscapes of the British Indian frontier. The story follows Lieutenant Ronald Wilmshurst, a brave and resourceful officer in the Frontier Force, as he faces treacherous enemies, navigates dangerous missions, and uncovers a web of intrigue that threatens the stability of the region. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of Wilmshurst and the Frontier Force.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Exploration, Conflict
