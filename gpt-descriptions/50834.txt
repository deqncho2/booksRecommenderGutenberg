Synopsis: In 'The Awakening' by Sharkey, Jack, a young woman named Emma finds herself trapped in a monotonous life, yearning for something more. When she stumbles upon a hidden world filled with magic and danger, Emma's life takes an unexpected turn. As she navigates through this enchanting realm, she discovers her own hidden powers and must make choices that will determine the fate of both worlds. With captivating twists and turns, 'The Awakening' is a thrilling tale of self-discovery and the power of embracing one's true potential.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Self-discovery, Magic, Choices, Dual Worlds
