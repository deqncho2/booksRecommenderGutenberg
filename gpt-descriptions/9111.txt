Synopsis: In 'The Bride of Dreams' by Frederik van Eeden, a young artist named Vincent embarks on a mysterious journey through his dreams, where he encounters a captivating woman who seems to exist only in his subconscious. As Vincent becomes increasingly obsessed with finding his dream bride in the waking world, he delves deeper into the realm of dreams, blurring the lines between reality and fantasy. With each dream encounter, Vincent's perception of love, desire, and the nature of existence is challenged, leading him on a thrilling and thought-provoking exploration of the human psyche.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Dreams, Love, Identity, Perception
