Synopsis: In the quaint village of New England, a series of mysterious events unfold, leaving the tight-knit community in a state of fear and uncertainty. As the villagers grapple with their own secrets and hidden desires, they must confront the darkness that lurks beneath the surface of their seemingly idyllic lives. With each passing day, tensions rise, alliances shift, and the true nature of their village is revealed. 'Under Fire: A Tale of New England Village Life' is a gripping and suspenseful exploration of the human psyche and the lengths people will go to protect their own. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Community, Identity
