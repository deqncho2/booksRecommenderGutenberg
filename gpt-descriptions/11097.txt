Synopsis: In 'Young Robin Hood' by George Manville Fenn, follow the thrilling adventures of a young boy named Robin as he navigates the treacherous forests of Sherwood. Raised by outlaws, Robin learns the ways of the forest and becomes a skilled archer. With his band of merry companions, he embarks on daring escapades, outwitting the corrupt Sheriff of Nottingham and defending the innocent. But as Robin's legend grows, he must face the ultimate test of loyalty and sacrifice. Will he become the legendary hero we all know? 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Justice, Coming of Age
