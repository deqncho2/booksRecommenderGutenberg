Synopsis: In "Over There with the Marines at Chateau Thierry," G. Harvey Ralphson takes readers on a thrilling journey alongside the brave Marines as they face the harrowing challenges of war. Set during World War I, this gripping narrative follows a group of young soldiers who find themselves thrust into the heart of the intense battle at Chateau Thierry. As they navigate the treacherous trenches, endure relentless enemy attacks, and witness the devastating consequences of war, the Marines must summon their courage and resilience to fight for their lives and the freedom of their country.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Action/Adventure

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, The horrors of war
