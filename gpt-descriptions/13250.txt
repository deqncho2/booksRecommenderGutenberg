Synopsis: In 'The Chums of Scranton High at Ice Hockey' by Ferguson, Donald, a group of friends from Scranton High School find themselves embarking on an exhilarating journey into the world of ice hockey. As they navigate the challenges of the sport, they uncover unexpected secrets and face intense rivalries that test their friendship and determination. With thrilling matches, surprising twists, and a dash of romance, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final buzzer.

Genres: Sports, Young Adult, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Competition, Coming of Age
