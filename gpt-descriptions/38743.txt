Synopsis: In "The Corner House Girls: How they moved to Milton, what they found, and what they did," the four adventurous Corner House girls embark on a thrilling journey as they relocate to the charming town of Milton. As they settle into their new home, they uncover a series of mysterious events that lead them on a quest to unravel the secrets of their new community. With their wit, courage, and determination, the Corner House girls find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of unexpected adventures, forging new friendships, and discovering the true meaning of home.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
