Synopsis: In 'A Feast for Serpents: Being the substance of a sermon, preached at the Obelisk Chapel, on Sunday evening, March 21, 1813', Reverend John Church delivers a captivating sermon that delves into the mysterious and dark world of serpents. As he explores the symbolism and significance of serpents in various religious and cultural contexts, he uncovers hidden truths and unveils a shocking revelation that will leave the reader questioning everything they thought they knew about these creatures. With vivid storytelling and thought-provoking insights, Church takes the audience on a journey that challenges their perceptions and forces them to confront their deepest fears.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious, Mystery

Themes: Symbolism, Perception, Fear
