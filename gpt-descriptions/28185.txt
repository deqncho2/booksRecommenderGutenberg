Synopsis: 
In the November 4, 1879 issue of Harper's Young People, readers are transported to a captivating world filled with adventure, mystery, and excitement. Through a collection of beautifully illustrated stories, this weekly publication takes young readers on thrilling journeys across different landscapes and time periods. From a daring escape from a treacherous pirate ship to a heart-pounding race against time to save a lost friend, each story is sure to keep readers on the edge of their seats. With its engaging narratives and stunning illustrations, Harper's Young People offers a captivating reading experience that will leave readers eagerly awaiting the next issue.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
