Synopsis: In 'Second Sight: A Study of Natural and Induced Clairvoyance' by Sepharial, embark on a captivating exploration of the mysterious world of clairvoyance. Delve into the depths of the human mind as the author unravels the secrets behind both natural and induced forms of this extraordinary ability. Through a combination of scientific analysis, historical anecdotes, and personal experiences, Sepharial takes readers on a thought-provoking journey that challenges conventional beliefs and offers a fresh perspective on the power of the mind. Prepare to be captivated by the enigmatic realm of clairvoyance and its potential to unlock hidden truths.

Genres: Non-fiction, Occult, Psychology

Themes: Clairvoyance, Mind Power, Perception, Supernatural Abilities
