Synopsis: 
In 'The American Therapist. Vol. II. No. 7. Jan. 15th, 1894: A Monthly Record of Modern Therapeutics, with Practical Suggestions Relating to the Clinical Applications of Drugs,' readers are transported to the fascinating world of 19th-century medicine. This gripping volume delves into the latest advancements in therapeutic treatments, offering practical suggestions for the clinical application of drugs. From innovative remedies to insightful case studies, this monthly record provides a captivating glimpse into the evolving field of modern therapeutics.

Genres: Medical literature, Historical non-fiction

Themes: Advancements in medicine, Clinical applications of drugs
