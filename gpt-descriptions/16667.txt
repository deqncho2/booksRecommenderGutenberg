Synopsis: In "Young Folks' History of Rome," Charlotte M. Yonge takes readers on an exciting journey through the captivating history of ancient Rome. From the legendary founding of the city by Romulus and Remus to the grandeur of the Roman Empire, this engaging narrative brings to life the triumphs, struggles, and remarkable individuals who shaped one of the greatest civilizations in history. Through vivid storytelling and fascinating anecdotes, Yonge transports readers to the heart of ancient Rome, where they will witness epic battles, political intrigue, and the rise and fall of emperors. This captivating history book is sure to captivate young readers and ignite their curiosity about the fascinating world of ancient Rome.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Children's Literature, Educational

Themes: History, Power and Politics, Heroism, Cultural Identity
