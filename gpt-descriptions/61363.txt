Synopsis: In 'The Universal Kinship', J. Howard Moore takes readers on a captivating journey through the animal kingdom, exploring the intricate connections and shared experiences between humans and animals. From the smallest insects to the largest mammals, Moore delves into the fascinating world of animal behavior, intelligence, and emotions, challenging conventional beliefs and shedding light on the profound bond that unites all living creatures. Through vivid storytelling and thought-provoking insights, this book invites readers to reconsider their relationship with the natural world and embrace a deeper understanding of our place within it.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Animal rights, Ethology, Interconnectedness of life
