Synopsis: 
'Boys and Girls Bookshelf (Vol 2 of 17): Folk-Lore, Fables, And Fairy Tales' is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that will transport readers to magical worlds filled with talking animals, mythical creatures, and extraordinary adventures. From classic folk tales like "Cinderella" and "Little Red Riding Hood" to lesser-known fables and legends, this volume offers a delightful mix of timeless narratives that will captivate both young and old. With each turn of the page, readers will be immersed in a world of imagination and wonder, where morals are taught, dreams come true, and the power of storytelling is celebrated.

Genres: Folklore, Fables, Fairy Tales

Themes: Imagination, Morality, Transformation
