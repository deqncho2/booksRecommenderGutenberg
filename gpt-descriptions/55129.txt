Synopsis: 
'Working my Way Around the World' is a captivating travel memoir that follows the incredible journey of Harry Alverson Franck as he embarks on a daring adventure to explore the globe. From the bustling streets of New York City to the remote corners of Africa and Asia, Franck's vivid storytelling takes readers on a thrilling expedition filled with unexpected encounters, daring escapades, and profound self-discovery. With a backpack on his shoulders and an insatiable curiosity in his heart, Franck immerses himself in diverse cultures, braving the unknown and embracing the beauty of the world. 

Genres: Travel, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Self-discovery
