Synopsis: 

In 'History of Early Steamboat Navigation on the Missouri River, Volume 1: Life and Adventures of Joseph La Barge', Hiram Martin Chittenden takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Joseph La Barge, a legendary figure in the early days of steamboat navigation on the Missouri River. From his humble beginnings as a cabin boy to becoming one of the most respected and skilled steamboat captains, La Barge's story is filled with thrilling adventures, dangerous encounters, and the relentless pursuit of progress in the untamed American West. As Chittenden delves into the challenges faced by La Barge and his fellow navigators, readers will be transported to a time when steamboats were the lifeline of commerce and exploration in the vast wilderness. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration and expansion, Perseverance and determination, Technological advancements, Frontier life
