Synopsis: In 'The Quest of Glory' by Marjorie Bowen, a young woman named Evelyn embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about her family's mysterious past. As she delves deeper into the secrets of her ancestors, she becomes entangled in a web of dark magic, ancient curses, and forbidden love. With each step closer to the truth, Evelyn must confront her own inner demons and make choices that will determine not only her own fate but also the fate of those she holds dear. Will she find the answers she seeks, or will the quest for glory lead her down a path of darkness?

Genres: Gothic fiction, Historical fiction, Mystery

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and sacrifice
