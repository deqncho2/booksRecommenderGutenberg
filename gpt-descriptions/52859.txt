Synopsis: 
In 'The Cholera Gazette, Vol. I. No. 5. Wednesday, August 8th, 1832.', a small town is struck by a mysterious and deadly disease, leaving its residents in a state of panic and uncertainty. As the town grapples with the outbreak, the Gazette becomes a vital source of information, revealing shocking secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to tear the community apart. With each edition, the Gazette uncovers more about the origins of the disease and the dark forces at play, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Disease outbreak, Conspiracy, Community survival
