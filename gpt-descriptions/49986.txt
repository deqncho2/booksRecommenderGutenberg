Synopsis: In 'War Medals and Their History', Steward, William Augustus takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of war medals. From ancient times to modern conflicts, this book delves into the stories behind these prestigious symbols of honor and valor. With vivid descriptions and intriguing anecdotes, Steward uncovers the secrets and significance of each medal, shedding light on the bravery and sacrifice of those who earned them. Whether you are a history enthusiast or simply curious about the untold tales behind these cherished decorations, 'War Medals and Their History' is sure to captivate and enlighten.

Genres: History, Military, Non-fiction

Themes: Honor, Valor, Sacrifice, Recognition
