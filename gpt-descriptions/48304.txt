Synopsis: In 'The Family at Misrule' by Ethel Turner, readers are introduced to the lively and mischievous Ralston family. Set in the charming Australian countryside, the story follows the adventures of the six Ralston siblings as they navigate the ups and downs of family life. From secret societies and daring escapades to unexpected friendships and heartwarming moments, this delightful tale captures the essence of childhood and the enduring bonds of family.

Genres: Children's literature, Family, Adventure

Themes: Sibling relationships, Coming of age, Friendship, Family dynamics
