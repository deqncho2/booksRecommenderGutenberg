Synopsis: 
'Great Hike; or, The Pride of the Khaki Troop' takes readers on an exhilarating adventure through the untamed wilderness. When a group of young scouts embarks on a challenging hike, they encounter unexpected obstacles and face their deepest fears. As they navigate treacherous terrain and test their limits, the bonds of friendship are forged and their true strength is revealed. Will they conquer the wilderness and emerge victorious, or will they succumb to the perils that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Survival

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resilience
