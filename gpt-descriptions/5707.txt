Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scout Aviators' by Durston, George, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey that takes them high above the clouds. When they stumble upon a mysterious aircraft hidden in the woods, they uncover a secret that leads them on a daring mission to protect their country. As they navigate through dangerous skies and face unexpected challenges, these young aviators must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and teamwork to save the day.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Patriotism
