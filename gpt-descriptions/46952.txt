Synopsis: 
In 'The Adventures of Old Man Coyote', follow the cunning and mischievous Old Man Coyote as he embarks on a series of thrilling escapades in the Green Forest. From outsmarting his fellow forest dwellers to narrowly escaping danger, Old Man Coyote's cunning and resourcefulness are put to the test. As he navigates through the challenges of the natural world, he teaches valuable lessons about survival, friendship, and the importance of wit. 

Genres: 
Children's literature, Animal fiction, Adventure

Themes: 
Survival, Friendship, Wit
