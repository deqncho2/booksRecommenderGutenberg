Synopsis: In 'Chains: A Play, in Four Acts' by Elizabeth Baker, the lives of four individuals become intertwined in a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and redemption. Set against the backdrop of a small coastal town, secrets are unveiled, relationships are tested, and the characters are forced to confront their pasts. As the story unfolds, the audience is taken on a rollercoaster of emotions, questioning the true nature of freedom and the lengths one will go to break free from the chains that bind them.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and sacrifice, the power of secrets, the search for identity
