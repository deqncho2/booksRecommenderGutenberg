Synopsis: In 'A Student in Arms: Second Series' by Donald Hankey, follow the captivating journey of a young student turned soldier as he navigates the trials and tribulations of war. Through his vivid and heartfelt accounts, experience the camaraderie, the fear, and the profound impact that war has on the human spirit. As he faces the harsh realities of battle, the protagonist's personal growth and transformation become a testament to the resilience of the human soul. 

Genres: War fiction, Memoir, Historical fiction

Themes: War and its effects, Personal growth and transformation, Camaraderie and friendship
