Synopsis: 
In 'The Modern Bicycle and Its Accessories', Schwalbach and Wilcox take readers on an exhilarating journey through the world of bicycles and their innovative accessories. From cutting-edge technology to stylish designs, this book explores the evolution of bicycles and the fascinating accessories that enhance the riding experience. With captivating anecdotes and stunning visuals, the authors delve into the intricate details of these two-wheeled wonders, leaving readers inspired to hit the road and discover the limitless possibilities of modern cycling.

Genres: Non-fiction, Technology, Design

Themes: Innovation, Adventure, Sustainability
