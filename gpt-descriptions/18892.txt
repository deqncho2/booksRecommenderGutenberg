Synopsis: 
In 'A Thoughtless Yes' by Helen H. Gardener, a young woman named Marion finds herself caught in a web of deceit and manipulation when she agrees to a seemingly innocent request. As she delves deeper into the consequences of her thoughtless decision, Marion must navigate a world filled with secrets, betrayal, and unexpected twists. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn into a suspenseful journey where the line between right and wrong becomes increasingly blurred.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Deception, Consequences of Actions, Morality
