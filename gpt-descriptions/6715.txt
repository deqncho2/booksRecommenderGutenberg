Synopsis: In 'Isobel: A Romance of the Northern Trail', James Oliver Curwood takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged wilderness of the Canadian North. The story follows Isobel, a young woman with a mysterious past, as she embarks on a perilous adventure filled with love, danger, and self-discovery. As Isobel navigates the treacherous landscapes and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, including the enigmatic Wabi and Mukoki, she must confront her own inner demons and uncover the truth about her identity. With breathtaking descriptions of the untamed wilderness and a gripping narrative, 'Isobel' is a thrilling romance that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
