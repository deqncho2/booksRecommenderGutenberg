Synopsis: 
'Field and Forest; Or, The Fortunes of a Farmer' follows the captivating journey of a young farmer named Frank, who embarks on a quest to transform his family's struggling farm into a prosperous enterprise. As Frank faces numerous challenges and unexpected adventures, he discovers the true meaning of perseverance, the power of nature, and the importance of community. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque countryside, this enthralling tale takes readers on a heartfelt exploration of the trials and triumphs of a farmer's life.

Genres: 
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Perseverance, Nature, Community
