Synopsis: In 'The Mistress of Bonaventure' by Harold Bindloss, a young woman named Muriel is thrust into a world of mystery and danger when she inherits a remote estate in the Canadian wilderness. As she navigates the treacherous landscape and uncovers the secrets of Bonaventure, Muriel finds herself entangled in a web of deceit, romance, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, she must confront her own past and make choices that will determine the fate of the estate and those who reside within it.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Identity, Love and Betrayal
