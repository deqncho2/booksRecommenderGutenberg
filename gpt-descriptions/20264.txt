Synopsis: In "Parsifal: Story and Analysis of Wagner's Great Opera," H.R. Haweis delves into the captivating world of Richard Wagner's renowned opera, "Parsifal." Through a detailed exploration of the story and analysis of its various elements, Haweis invites readers to uncover the secrets and mysteries that lie within this extraordinary masterpiece. From the enigmatic characters to the powerful symbolism, this book offers a fascinating journey into the heart of Wagner's opera, leaving readers eager to experience its magic for themselves.

Genres: Music, Opera, Analysis

Themes: Redemption, Spiritual Quest, Symbolism
