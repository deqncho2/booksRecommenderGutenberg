Synopsis: In 'A Guide to Stoicism' by Stock, St. George William Joseph, embark on a transformative journey through the ancient philosophy of Stoicism. Follow the footsteps of the great Stoic philosophers as they navigate the complexities of life, offering practical wisdom and timeless advice on how to find tranquility, cultivate resilience, and embrace the power of the present moment. With insightful anecdotes and thought-provoking reflections, this guide illuminates the path to living a more meaningful and fulfilling life.

Genres: Philosophy, Self-help, Non-fiction

Themes: Stoicism, Resilience, Mindfulness, Inner peace, Virtue
