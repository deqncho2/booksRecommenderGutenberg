Synopsis: In 'The Cuckoo in the Nest, v. 1/2' by Oliphant, Mrs., a seemingly idyllic family is disrupted when a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with them secrets and hidden agendas. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the once peaceful household becomes a battleground of deceit and betrayal. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning who can be trusted and what will become of this fractured family.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Family dynamics, Deception, Trust
