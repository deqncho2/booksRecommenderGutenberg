Synopsis: 

In "The Geologic Story of Glacier National Park," James L. Dyson takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient history and breathtaking landscapes of Glacier National Park. Through vivid descriptions and stunning photographs, Dyson unveils the fascinating geological processes that have shaped this majestic park over millions of years. From the formation of towering mountains to the carving of deep valleys by massive glaciers, the book unravels the secrets hidden within the park's rocks and landscapes. As readers delve into the geologic wonders of Glacier National Park, they will gain a deeper appreciation for the forces of nature that have created this awe-inspiring wilderness.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Geology, Natural History, Environmental Conservation
