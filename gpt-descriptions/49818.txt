Synopsis: 

In 'Animal Life and Intelligence', C. Lloyd Morgan takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of animals, exploring their behaviors, instincts, and remarkable intelligence. From the intricate communication systems of bees to the problem-solving abilities of dolphins, Morgan delves into the depths of animal cognition, challenging conventional beliefs and shedding light on the remarkable capabilities of our fellow creatures. Through captivating anecdotes and scientific research, this book offers a thought-provoking exploration of the complex and often surprising world of animal life and intelligence.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Animal behavior, Animal cognition, Intelligence, Communication, Evolution
