Synopsis: In 'Vera; Or, The Nihilists' by Oscar Wilde, a young Russian princess finds herself caught between her loyalty to her family and her growing attraction to a group of radical revolutionaries. As she delves deeper into their world, she must navigate the complexities of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of freedom. Set against the backdrop of political unrest in 19th-century Russia, this gripping tale explores the clash between tradition and progress, and the power of individual choice in shaping one's destiny.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Political Thriller

Themes: Love and Desire, Loyalty and Betrayal, Revolution and Social Change
