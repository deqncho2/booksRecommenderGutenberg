Synopsis: 
In 'No Hero' by E. W. Hornung, we follow the intriguing story of a man named Jack Darragh, who finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger. After a series of unexpected events, Jack is thrust into a world of espionage, where he must navigate treacherous alliances and face his own inner demons. As he delves deeper into the shadows, Jack must confront the question of what truly makes a hero and whether he has what it takes to become one.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Espionage, Identity, Morality
