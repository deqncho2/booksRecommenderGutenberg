Synopsis: In 'Uarda: a Romance of Ancient Egypt', Georg Ebers takes readers on a captivating journey to the enchanting world of ancient Egypt. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue, religious conflicts, and forbidden love, the story follows the life of Uarda, a young girl of mysterious origins. As she navigates the complexities of her identity and the tumultuous events of her time, Uarda becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances. With vivid descriptions and rich historical detail, Ebers weaves a tale that will transport readers to a bygone era, where love and destiny collide in the shadow of the pyramids.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Forbidden Love, Political Intrigue
