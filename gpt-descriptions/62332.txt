Synopsis: In "The Last Ninety Days of the War in North-Carolina," Cornelia Phillips Spencer takes us on a captivating journey through the final months of the Civil War in North Carolina. Through her vivid and personal accounts, we witness the struggles, sacrifices, and triumphs of both soldiers and civilians as they navigate the chaos and uncertainty of war. From the front lines to the home front, Spencer's narrative paints a poignant picture of a state torn apart by conflict, yet resilient in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, War literature

Themes: Resilience, Sacrifice, Human spirit, Homefront experiences, Civil War history
