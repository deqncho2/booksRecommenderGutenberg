Synopsis: 
In 'The Power-House' by John Buchan, a young lawyer named Edward Leithen finds himself entangled in a mysterious and dangerous web of international intrigue. When he stumbles upon a secret society with a sinister agenda, Leithen must navigate a treacherous world of espionage, betrayal, and hidden identities. As he races against time to uncover the truth, Leithen's own life becomes increasingly endangered, forcing him to confront his deepest fears and tap into his inner strength. With its gripping plot and unexpected twists, 'The Power-House' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Espionage, Identity, Courage
