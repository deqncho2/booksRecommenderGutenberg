Synopsis: 

In 'The Great North Road, the Old Mail Road to Scotland: London to York', Charles G. Harper takes readers on a captivating journey along one of England's most historic and iconic routes. From the bustling streets of London to the ancient city of York, this book delves into the rich history, fascinating stories, and hidden gems that lie along the Great North Road. Harper's vivid descriptions and meticulous research bring to life the landscapes, towns, and people that have shaped this renowned road over the centuries. 

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Heritage, Cultural significance
