Synopsis: In 'Warren Commission (08 of 26): Hearings Vol. VIII (of 15)', the United States Warren Commission delves deeper into the investigation surrounding the assassination of President John F. Kennedy. Through a series of hearings, witnesses are called to testify, shedding light on crucial details and raising new questions. As the Commission uncovers more evidence, the plot thickens, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth behind this historic event.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, True Crime

Themes: Assassination, Conspiracy, Government Investigation
