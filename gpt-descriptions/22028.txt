Synopsis: 

In 'The War Service of the 1/4 Royal Berkshire Regiment (T. F.)', author C. R. M. F. Cruttwell takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of a regiment during World War I. Through vivid and detailed accounts, Cruttwell paints a vivid picture of the bravery, sacrifice, and camaraderie displayed by the soldiers as they face the horrors of war. From the trenches of the Western Front to the battlefields of Gallipoli, this book offers a compelling and poignant narrative that will leave readers captivated.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: War, Bravery, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Human resilience
