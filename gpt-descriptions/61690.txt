Synopsis: In the picturesque countryside of Sussex, a young woman named Mary has her life turned upside down when she encounters a mysterious group of individuals known as the "Saints." Drawn into their world of secrets and spirituality, Mary embarks on a journey of self-discovery and transformation. As she delves deeper into the enigmatic lives of the Saints, she must confront her own desires, fears, and the complexities of human nature. Set against the backdrop of the idyllic English countryside, 'Saints in Sussex' is a captivating tale of love, faith, and the search for meaning.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Spiritual

Themes: Self-discovery, Transformation, Spirituality, Human nature
