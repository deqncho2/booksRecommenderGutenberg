Synopsis: 
"The Chronicle of Jocelin of Brakelond: A Picture of Monastic Life in the Days of Abbot Samson" takes readers on a captivating journey into the intriguing world of medieval monasticism. Through the eyes of Jocelin, a monk at the Abbey of Bury St. Edmunds, we witness the struggles, triumphs, and scandals that unfold within the walls of the abbey. From power struggles among the monks to encounters with royalty and even supernatural occurrences, this chronicle offers a fascinating glimpse into the daily life and inner workings of a medieval monastery.

Genres: Historical fiction, memoir, religious literature

Themes: Power and authority, faith and spirituality, community and brotherhood
