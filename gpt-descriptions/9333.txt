Synopsis: 
'Johnny Bear, and Other Stories from Lives of the Hunted' by Ernest Thompson Seton is a captivating collection of short stories that delve into the lives of various animals and their encounters with humans. From the heartwarming tale of Johnny Bear, a clever and mischievous bear who forms an unlikely bond with a young girl, to the thrilling adventures of a cunning fox named Lobo, these stories offer a unique perspective on the natural world and the complex relationships between humans and animals. Seton's vivid storytelling and deep understanding of animal behavior make this collection a must-read for nature enthusiasts and animal lovers alike.

Genres: 
Fiction, Short Stories, Nature Writing

Themes: 
Animal intelligence, Human-animal relationships, Nature conservation
