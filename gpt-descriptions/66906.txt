Synopsis: In 'Mårbacka' by Selma Lagerlöf, the reader is transported to the enchanting world of a Swedish manor house. Through the eyes of a young girl, we witness the joys and challenges of growing up in a privileged yet unpredictable environment. As the girl navigates the complexities of family dynamics, societal expectations, and the ever-changing landscape of Mårbacka, she discovers the power of resilience, love, and the enduring spirit of home.

Genres: Autobiographical fiction, Historical fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family, Identity, Social class, Resilience
