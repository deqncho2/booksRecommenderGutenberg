Synopsis: In "A New Graft on the Family Tree" by Pansy, a young woman named Ruth embarks on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected connections. When she receives a mysterious letter, Ruth's life takes an intriguing turn as she delves into her family's past and uncovers secrets that will challenge her perception of identity and love. As she navigates through the complexities of her newfound heritage, Ruth must confront the choices that have shaped her family's history and ultimately determine her own destiny.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and relationships
