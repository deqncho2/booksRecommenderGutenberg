Synopsis: 
In "The Outdoor Chums in the Forest; Or, Laying the Ghost of Oak Ridge," the adventurous group of friends embarks on a thrilling journey to the mysterious Oak Ridge. As they delve deeper into the forest, they encounter strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena that test their courage and friendship. Determined to uncover the truth, the Outdoor Chums must confront their fears and unravel the secrets hidden within the haunting woods.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
