Synopsis: 
'First Plays' by A. A. Milne is a captivating collection of four one-act plays that explore the complexities of human relationships and the unexpected twists of fate. In these delightful and thought-provoking plays, Milne masterfully weaves together humor, wit, and poignant moments to create a truly engaging experience. From a whimsical tale of mistaken identity to a heartwarming story of love and sacrifice, each play offers a unique and enchanting glimpse into the human experience. Prepare to be entertained, moved, and left pondering the intricacies of life through Milne's brilliant storytelling.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Love and relationships, Identity, Fate and chance
