Synopsis: In 'Warren Commission (07 of 26): Hearings Vol. VII (of 15)', the United States Warren Commission delves deeper into the investigation surrounding the assassination of President John F. Kennedy. Through a series of hearings, witnesses are called to testify, shedding light on various aspects of the case. As the commission uncovers new evidence and hears conflicting accounts, the mystery surrounding the assassination deepens, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, True Crime

Themes: Conspiracy, Government secrecy, Truth and justice
