Synopsis: 
In "Their Majesties as I Knew Them: Personal Reminiscences of the Kings and Queens of Europe," Xavier Paoli takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the lives of European monarchs. From the opulent courts of France to the mysterious castles of Eastern Europe, Paoli shares his intimate encounters and firsthand experiences with the most powerful rulers of the continent. Through captivating anecdotes and vivid descriptions, he unveils the hidden personalities, scandals, and intrigues that shaped the destinies of these regal figures. As Paoli delves into the private lives of these monarchs, readers will be enthralled by the untold stories and secrets that lie behind the grandeur of their public personas.

Genres: Biography, History, Memoir

Themes: Royalty, Power, Intrigue, Personalities
