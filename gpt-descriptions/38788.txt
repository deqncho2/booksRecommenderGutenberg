Synopsis: 

'Antigua and the Antiguans, Volume 1 (of 2): A full account of the colony and its inhabitants from the time of the Caribs to the present day' by Lanaghan, Mrs. takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and culture of the Caribbean island of Antigua. From the ancient Caribs to the present-day inhabitants, this comprehensive account delves into the rich tapestry of Antiguan society, exploring its triumphs, struggles, and unique traditions. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the author paints a vivid picture of this vibrant island, offering readers a deeper understanding of its people and their enduring spirit.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Cultural exploration

Themes: Caribbean history, Cultural identity, Colonialism, Resilience, Heritage preservation
