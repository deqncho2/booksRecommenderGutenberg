Synopsis: 
In "Roy Blakeley's Tangled Trail" by Percy Keese Fitzhugh, the beloved character Roy Blakeley finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a mysterious map. Alongside his loyal friends, Roy embarks on a journey filled with hidden clues, secret codes, and unexpected twists. As they navigate through treacherous terrain and encounter peculiar characters, the group must rely on their wit and resourcefulness to unravel the enigma of the tangled trail. Will Roy and his friends be able to solve the puzzle before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
