Synopsis: 

'Lancashire Folk-lore: Illustrative of the Superstitious Beliefs and Practices, Local Customs and Usages of the People of the County Palatine' takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich tapestry of Lancashire's folklore. Through a collection of intriguing tales, superstitions, and customs, authors Harland and Wilkinson delve into the mystical world of the county's inhabitants, revealing the fascinating beliefs and practices that have shaped their lives for generations. From ancient rituals to ghostly encounters, this book offers a captivating exploration of Lancashire's folklore, providing a glimpse into a world where the supernatural and the everyday intertwine.

Genres: Folklore, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Superstition, Local Customs, Belief Systems, Cultural Heritage
