Synopsis: In 'The Interloper' by Jacob, Violet, a young woman with a mysterious past, finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when she moves to a small town. As she tries to uncover the truth about her own identity, she becomes the target of a dangerous game played by those who will stop at nothing to protect their own interests. With each twist and turn, Violet must navigate the treacherous landscape of lies and betrayal, all while discovering her own strength and resilience.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal
