Synopsis: In 'A Copper Harvest; or, The Boys who Worked a Deserted Mine' by Self-made man, a group of adventurous boys stumble upon an abandoned mine in the heart of the desert. Ignoring the warnings of danger, they embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the secrets hidden within the depths of the mine. As they face unexpected challenges and encounter mysterious characters, their determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. Will they unearth a valuable treasure or find something even more precious along the way?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Resilience
