Synopsis: In the gripping novel 'Madame Delphine' by George Washington Cable, a mysterious woman arrives in New Orleans, captivating the city's elite with her beauty and charm. As her true identity and dark secrets slowly unravel, the lives of those around her become entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and scandal. Set against the backdrop of post-Civil War Louisiana, this captivating tale explores the complexities of race, class, and the power of secrets.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Social class, Race relations, Secrets
