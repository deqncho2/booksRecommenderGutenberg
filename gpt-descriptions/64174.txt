Synopsis: 
In 'Wireless Telegraph Construction For Amateurs' by Alfred Powell Morgan, embark on an electrifying journey into the world of wireless telegraphy. This captivating guide takes readers through the step-by-step process of constructing their own wireless telegraph apparatus, unraveling the mysteries of this groundbreaking technology. From the basics of electricity to the intricacies of antenna design, Morgan's expertise shines through as he demystifies the inner workings of wireless communication. With detailed diagrams and practical instructions, this book is a must-read for anyone fascinated by the wonders of early wireless technology.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Technology, DIY

Themes: 
Innovation, Communication
