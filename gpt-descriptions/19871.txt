Synopsis: 

'Serbia in Light and Darkness: With Preface by the Archbishop of Canterbury' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events of World War I, specifically focusing on the experiences of Serbia. Written by Nikolaj Velimirović, this book offers a unique perspective on the war, shedding light on the struggles, triumphs, and resilience of the Serbian people during this dark period in history. Through vivid storytelling and poignant anecdotes, Velimirović paints a vivid picture of the Serbian nation, its culture, and its unwavering spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical fiction, War literature, Memoir

Themes: Resilience, National identity, Human suffering, Faith and spirituality
