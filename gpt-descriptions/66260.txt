Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the captivating world of Londinium, as renowned architect and historian W.R. Lethaby takes you on a mesmerizing journey through its architecture and crafts. From the grandeur of Roman temples to the intricate details of medieval cathedrals, Lethaby unveils the hidden stories behind London's iconic structures. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, he delves into the craftsmanship and artistic genius that shaped the city's architectural heritage. Prepare to be enthralled by the secrets and wonders that lie within the walls of Londinium.

Genres: History, Architecture, Art

Themes: Architectural heritage, Craftsmanship, Historical significance
