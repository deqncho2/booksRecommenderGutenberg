Synopsis: In 'Through Magic Glasses and Other Lectures: A Sequel to The Fairyland of Science', Arabella B. Buckley takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders of the natural world. Through a series of engaging lectures, she explores the hidden secrets of plants, animals, and even the Earth itself. But what makes this sequel truly enchanting is the introduction of magic glasses that allow the wearer to see the world in a whole new light. With these glasses, readers will witness extraordinary phenomena and embark on extraordinary adventures, all while gaining a deeper understanding of the scientific principles that govern our world.

Genres: Science fiction, Fantasy, Educational

Themes: Wonder and curiosity, Science and exploration
