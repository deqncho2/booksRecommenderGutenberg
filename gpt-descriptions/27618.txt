Synopsis: In 'The End of a Coil' by Susan Warner, a young woman named Grace finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits a mysterious coil of hair. As she delves into the history behind this unusual heirloom, Grace uncovers a hidden world of love, betrayal, and unexpected connections that will challenge everything she thought she knew about her own identity. With each unraveling thread, the tension builds, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Family, Betrayal
