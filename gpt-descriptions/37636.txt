Synopsis: 

In "Charles Lewis Cocke, Founder of Hollins College" by Smith, William Robert Lee, embark on a captivating journey through the life of an extraordinary individual who defied societal norms and dedicated himself to the pursuit of education. This enthralling biography delves into the trials and triumphs of Charles Lewis Cocke, a visionary who founded Hollins College against all odds. From his humble beginnings to his relentless determination, Cocke's story is one of resilience, passion, and the unwavering belief in the power of education. 

Genres: Biography, History, Education

Themes: Perseverance, Education, Social Change
