Synopsis: 

In 'Hunting the Hun' by Belton, James and Odell, E. G., a thrilling and suspenseful tale unfolds as a group of skilled British spies embark on a dangerous mission during World War I. Set against the backdrop of the treacherous German-occupied territories, the story follows the team's relentless pursuit of a notorious German spy known as "The Hun." As they navigate through enemy lines, face deadly traps, and engage in heart-pounding encounters, the spies must rely on their wits, courage, and camaraderie to outsmart their cunning adversary. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leading to a climactic showdown that will test their loyalty and determination to the limit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: War, Espionage, Loyalty, Courage
