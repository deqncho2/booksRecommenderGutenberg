Synopsis:
'Dangerous Connections' is a captivating epistolary novel that delves into the scandalous lives of a group of aristocrats in 18th-century France. Through a series of letters, the reader is drawn into a web of deceit, manipulation, and forbidden desires. As the characters navigate their treacherous relationships, secrets are unveiled, alliances are formed, and hearts are broken. With each letter, the tension builds, leaving the reader eager to discover the ultimate consequences of these dangerous connections.

Genres:
Epistolary fiction, Historical fiction, Drama

Themes:
Love and desire, Betrayal and manipulation, Morality and social conventions
