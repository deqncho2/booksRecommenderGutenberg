Synopsis: In "Croatian Tales of Long Ago," Ivana Brlic-Mazuranic takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Croatian folklore. From mischievous fairies and cunning witches to brave heroes and mythical creatures, this collection of tales weaves together a tapestry of adventure, magic, and timeless wisdom. Each story transports readers to a different era, where they will encounter unforgettable characters and experience the triumph of good over evil. With its rich cultural heritage and vivid storytelling, "Croatian Tales of Long Ago" offers a delightful escape into a realm where imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Heroism, Magic, Cultural Heritage
