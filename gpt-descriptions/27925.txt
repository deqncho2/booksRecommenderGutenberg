Synopsis: 
In 'The Art of Disappearing' by Smith, John Talbot, a renowned magician named Ethan Vanish finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he is hired to perform at a secluded mansion. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the mansion and its eccentric inhabitants, Ethan discovers that not everything is as it seems. With illusions and deception at every turn, he must use his skills as a magician to uncover the truth and escape before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Illusion vs. Reality, Deception, Secrets and Hidden Identities
