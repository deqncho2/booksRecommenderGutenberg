Synopsis: In the fifth volume of the Warren Commission Hearings, the investigation into the assassination of President John F. Kennedy intensifies as key witnesses take the stand. From government officials to eyewitnesses, the hearings delve into the intricate details surrounding the tragic event, uncovering new evidence and raising thought-provoking questions. As the truth slowly unravels, tensions rise and the stakes become higher than ever before.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, True Crime

Themes: Conspiracy, Justice, Government Transparency
