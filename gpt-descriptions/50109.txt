Synopsis: In a small Austrian village, three young boys encounter a mysterious stranger who possesses an uncanny ability to manipulate reality. As they delve deeper into his enigmatic nature, they are confronted with profound questions about the nature of good and evil, the power of imagination, and the true meaning of existence. With each encounter, the stranger challenges their beliefs and forces them to confront the darker aspects of human nature. As the boys grapple with the stranger's influence, they are left questioning their own reality and the very fabric of the world around them.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Satire

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Reality vs. Illusion, Morality, Existentialism
