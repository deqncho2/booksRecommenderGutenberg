Synopsis: Nan Sherwood's Winter Holidays; Or, Rescuing the Runaways follows the adventurous Nan Sherwood as she embarks on a winter vacation filled with unexpected twists and turns. When Nan and her friends stumble upon a group of runaways in need of help, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling rescue mission that takes them on a thrilling journey through snowy landscapes and heart-pounding encounters. As they navigate the challenges of the winter season, Nan and her friends learn valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the importance of helping others.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Helping Others
