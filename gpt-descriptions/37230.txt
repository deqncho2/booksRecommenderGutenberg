Synopsis: In 'Frauds and Follies of the Fathers: A Review of the Worth of Their Testimony to the Four Gospels', J. M. Wheeler delves into the intriguing world of ancient religious texts and their credibility. With meticulous research and sharp analysis, Wheeler uncovers surprising revelations about the origins and authenticity of the four Gospels. As he unravels the web of historical context and religious bias, readers are taken on a thought-provoking journey that challenges long-held beliefs and sheds light on the complex nature of religious texts.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, Historical Analysis

Themes: Religious authenticity, Historical context, Bias and interpretation
