Synopsis: In "A Brief Narrative of the Fourth Tennessee Cavalry Regiment, Wheeler's Corps, Army of Tennessee," George B. Guild takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of a courageous cavalry regiment during the American Civil War. From the intense battles and daring escapades to the camaraderie and sacrifices made by these soldiers, Guild's narrative offers a vivid and gripping account of their harrowing adventures. As the Fourth Tennessee Cavalry Regiment faces the challenges of war, readers will be enthralled by the bravery and resilience displayed by these unsung heroes.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, War
