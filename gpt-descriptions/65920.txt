Synopsis: 
In the 'St. Nicholas Book of Plays & Operettas (Second Series)', readers will be transported into a world of enchanting performances and captivating stories. This collection features a delightful array of plays and operettas that will ignite the imagination and bring joy to both performers and audiences alike. From whimsical fairy tales to heartwarming holiday tales, each piece is filled with memorable characters, lively dialogue, and moments of laughter and emotion. Whether it's a magical journey through a hidden forest or a charming tale set in a bustling town, these plays and operettas offer a delightful escape into the world of theater.

Genres: 
Children's literature, Drama, Musical

Themes: 
Imagination, Friendship, Adventure, Love, Holiday spirit
