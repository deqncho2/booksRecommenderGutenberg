Synopsis: 
In 'An Amicable Controversy with a Jewish Rabbi, on The Messiah's Coming', John Ranicar Park engages in a thought-provoking dialogue with a Jewish Rabbi, delving into the prophecies of Zechariah and the coming of the Messiah. As they explore the scriptures, their discussion takes unexpected turns, challenging both their beliefs and opening up new perspectives on the Messiah's kingdom. With a fresh exposition of Zechariah, this book offers a captivating exploration of faith, prophecy, and the nature of the Messiah's reign.

Genres: Religious, Theological, Dialogue

Themes: Messianic prophecies, Interfaith dialogue, Faith exploration
