Synopsis: In "You Don't Make Wine Like the Greeks Did," David E. Fisher takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient world of winemaking. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Fisher uncovers the secrets of the Greeks' winemaking techniques, revealing a fascinating blend of science, artistry, and mythology. From the vineyards of ancient Greece to the bustling markets of Athens, this book offers a unique perspective on the history and cultural significance of wine. As Fisher delves into the past, he also explores the enduring legacy of Greek winemaking and its influence on modern practices. Prepare to be transported to a time when wine was more than just a beverage, but a symbol of civilization and a conduit to the divine.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Culinary

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Wine culture, Cultural heritage, Mythology
