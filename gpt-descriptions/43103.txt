Synopsis: 
"The System," As Uncovered by the San Francisco Graft Prosecution takes readers on a gripping journey through the underbelly of corruption and political scandal in early 20th century San Francisco. Investigative journalist Hichborn delves deep into the web of bribery, fraud, and manipulation that permeated the city's government, exposing the shocking extent to which the system was rigged. As the prosecution unfolds, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the true power players behind the scenes are revealed. This captivating account of the San Francisco graft prosecution will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the full extent of the corruption.

Genres: Non-fiction, True Crime, History

Themes: Corruption, Power, Justice
