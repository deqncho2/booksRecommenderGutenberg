Synopsis: In 'King Arthur's Socks and Other Village Plays' by Dell, Floyd, embark on a whimsical journey through a collection of charming and humorous village plays. From the misadventures of King Arthur's socks to the unexpected encounters of eccentric villagers, these delightful plays will transport you to a world filled with laughter, wit, and unexpected surprises. Each play offers a unique and entertaining story that will leave you eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what delightful twist awaits next.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Fiction

Themes: Humor, Community, Quirkiness
