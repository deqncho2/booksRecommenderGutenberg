Synopsis: In 'Edna Browning; $b or, the Leighton homestead', Mary Jane Holmes weaves a captivating tale of love, mystery, and family secrets. Set in the picturesque Leighton homestead, the story follows the life of Edna Browning, a young woman with a mysterious past. As Edna navigates the complexities of her relationships and uncovers the truth about her heritage, she finds herself torn between duty and desire. With unexpected twists and turns, this novel takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of true happiness.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Relationships, Identity and Heritage, Secrets and Betrayal
