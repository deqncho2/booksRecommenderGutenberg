Synopsis: In 'Gods and Heroes', Schmidt and Becker take readers on a captivating journey through ancient mythology, weaving together tales of gods, heroes, and epic adventures. From the mighty Zeus and his thunderbolts to the courageous Hercules and his legendary feats, this book brings to life the captivating stories of ancient Greece and Rome. With vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, the authors transport readers to a world filled with gods and goddesses, mythical creatures, and heroic quests. Prepare to be enthralled by the timeless tales of love, betrayal, and triumph that have captivated audiences for centuries.

Genres: Mythology, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Power and authority, Heroism, Fate and destiny
