Synopsis: In "A Little Princess: Being the whole story of Sara Crewe now told for the first time," we follow the captivating journey of Sara Crewe, a young girl who goes from riches to rags in a blink of an eye. Set in the enchanting Miss Minchin's Seminary for Young Ladies, this heartwarming tale explores the resilience and imagination of a remarkable young girl as she faces adversity with grace and kindness. With unexpected twists and turns, this story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of Sara Crewe.

Genres: Children's literature, Fiction, Historical fiction

Themes: Resilience, Imagination, Kindness, Friendship, Adversity
