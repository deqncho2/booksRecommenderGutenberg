Synopsis: 
'Bulfinch's Mythology: The Age of Fable; The Age of Chivalry; Legends of Charlemagne' is a captivating collection of timeless tales that transport readers into the realms of ancient mythology, medieval knights, and legendary heroes. From the enchanting stories of Greek and Roman gods to the epic adventures of King Arthur and his knights, and the heroic deeds of Charlemagne, this book weaves together a tapestry of myths, legends, and chivalrous tales that have captivated readers for generations. With its rich storytelling and vivid imagery, 'Bulfinch's Mythology' invites readers to embark on a journey through the ages, where gods, heroes, and knights come alive in a world of wonder and imagination.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Historical Fiction

Themes: Heroism, Fate and Destiny, Honor and Chivalry, Divine Intervention, Quests and Adventures
