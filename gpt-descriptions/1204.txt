Synopsis: In 'Cabin Fever' by B. M. Bower, a young woman named Mary finds herself trapped in a remote cabin during a fierce winter storm. As the days pass and the snow piles up, Mary's isolation becomes increasingly unbearable. Just when she thinks she can't take it anymore, a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a glimmer of hope and a dark secret that threatens to unravel everything. With tension mounting and danger lurking outside, Mary must navigate the treacherous path between survival and trust, all while battling her own inner demons.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Isolation, Survival, Trust, Secrets
