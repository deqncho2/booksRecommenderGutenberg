Synopsis: In 'The Story of Porcelain' by Sara Ware Bassett, a young woman named Ruth embarks on a journey that takes her from a small New England town to the bustling streets of New York City. Drawn into the world of porcelain manufacturing, Ruth discovers a hidden talent and a passion for creating exquisite works of art. As she navigates the challenges of love, ambition, and societal expectations, Ruth must find the strength to pursue her dreams and overcome the obstacles that stand in her way.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Pursuit of passion, Self-discovery, Breaking societal norms
