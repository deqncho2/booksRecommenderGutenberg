Synopsis: 
In 'Bee Keeping' by Benton Frank, we are introduced to a small coastal town where a mysterious newcomer, Lily, sets up a beekeeping business. As the townspeople become enchanted by her honey and the healing properties it possesses, they also find themselves drawn to Lily's enigmatic past. As secrets unravel and relationships are tested, the town is forced to confront their own desires and the power of nature's sweetest gift.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, the healing power of nature, the complexities of human relationships
