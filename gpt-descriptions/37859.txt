Synopsis: 
'The Call of the Mountains, and Other Poems' by James E. Pickering is a captivating collection of poems that takes readers on a journey through the beauty and grandeur of nature. From the majestic mountains to the serene lakes, each poem paints a vivid picture of the natural world, evoking a sense of awe and wonder. With lyrical verses and heartfelt emotions, the poems explore themes of love, longing, and the profound connection between humanity and the natural world. This collection is sure to transport readers to the breathtaking landscapes and leave them yearning for their own call to the mountains.

Genres: Poetry, Nature, Romance

Themes: Nature's beauty, Love and longing, Human connection with nature
