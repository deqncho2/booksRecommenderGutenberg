Synopsis: 

'Tea Leaves: Being a Collection of Letters and Documents relating to the shipment of Tea to the American Colonies in the year 1773, by the East India Tea Company' is a captivating historical account that delves into the events surrounding the Boston Tea Party. Through a collection of letters and documents, Francis S. Drake provides a unique perspective on the shipment of tea and the subsequent rebellion that forever changed American history. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Epistolary 

Themes: American Revolution, Colonial resistance, Political activism
