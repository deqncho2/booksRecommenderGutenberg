Synopsis: In 'Steamships and their story', E. Keble Chatterton takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating history of steamships. From the early experiments and inventions to the grand ocean liners of the 20th century, this book unveils the triumphs, challenges, and remarkable stories behind these revolutionary vessels. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Chatterton brings to life the pioneers, engineers, and adventurers who shaped the world of steamships. Discover the incredible feats of engineering, the daring voyages, and the impact these vessels had on global trade and travel. Whether you are a history enthusiast or simply curious about the evolution of maritime transportation, 'Steamships and their story' promises an enthralling exploration of a pivotal era in maritime history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Maritime

Themes: Technological advancements, Exploration, Human ingenuity
