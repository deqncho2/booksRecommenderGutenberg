Synopsis: In 'The Vicar's People' by George Manville Fenn, a young and ambitious vicar finds himself in a small village filled with mysterious secrets and hidden agendas. As he tries to bring the community together, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and long-held grudges that threaten to tear the village apart. With his faith and determination tested, the vicar must navigate through the darkness to restore peace and unity among the villagers.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Community, Faith
