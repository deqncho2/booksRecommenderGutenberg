Synopsis: 
In "Samuel Reynolds House of Siam, pioneer medical missionary, 1847-1876" by Feltus, George Haws, embark on a captivating journey through the life of Samuel Reynolds House, a remarkable medical missionary who ventured into the exotic and mysterious land of Siam. From his humble beginnings to his groundbreaking medical achievements, House's story is one of courage, resilience, and unwavering dedication to his mission. As he navigates the challenges of cultural differences, political unrest, and personal sacrifices, readers will be enthralled by the captivating narrative that unfolds, revealing the transformative power of compassion and the indomitable spirit of a true pioneer.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Missionary work, Cultural exploration, Medical advancements
