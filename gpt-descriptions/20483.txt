Synopsis: 

'Artillery Through the Ages: A Short Illustrated History of Cannon, Emphasizing Types Used in America' by Albert C. Manucy takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of cannon technology in America. From the early days of colonial settlements to the modern era, this book explores the various types of artillery used throughout American history, showcasing their impact on warfare and society. Through vivid illustrations and engaging narratives, Manucy brings to life the stories behind these powerful weapons, shedding light on their development, usage, and significance in shaping the nation's military strategies. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History, Illustrated Reference

Themes: Technological Advancement, Military Strategy, Societal Impact
