Synopsis: 
In 'The Somerset Coast' by Charles G. Harper, a mysterious disappearance sets off a chain of events that will uncover long-buried secrets and test the limits of friendship and loyalty. As the protagonist delves deeper into the enigma, they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, the tension builds, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the truth behind the haunting Somerset Coast.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Friendship and loyalty, Uncovering the truth
