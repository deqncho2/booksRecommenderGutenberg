Synopsis: In 'The Making of a Soul' by Kathlyn Rhodes, a young artist named Lily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. As she navigates through the complexities of life, love, and loss, Lily finds herself drawn to a mysterious antique shop and its enigmatic owner. Through a series of unexpected events and encounters, Lily's perception of reality is challenged, leading her on a transformative path towards understanding the true nature of her soul.

Genres: Fiction, Magical Realism, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Self-discovery, Spirituality, Love and Loss, Perception of Reality
