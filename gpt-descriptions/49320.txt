Synopsis: 
In 'Cadet Days: A Story of West Point' by Charles King, readers are transported to the prestigious United States Military Academy, where they follow the journey of a young cadet named Frank. As Frank navigates the challenges and triumphs of his time at West Point, he forms lasting friendships, faces intense rivalries, and discovers the true meaning of honor and loyalty. With its vivid descriptions of military life, thrilling competitions, and unexpected twists, this captivating novel offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of cadets and the sacrifices they make in pursuit of their dreams.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Honor, Friendship, Loyalty, Perseverance
