Synopsis: 
'Joseph Pennell's Pictures of the Wonder of Work' takes readers on a visual journey through the world, showcasing a series of drawings, etchings, and lithographs created by the talented artist between 1881 and 1915. With the addition of the artist's own impressions and notes, this collection offers a unique perspective on the wonders of various industries and the people who bring them to life. From bustling cityscapes to serene landscapes, Pennell's artwork captures the essence of work in all its forms, inviting readers to appreciate the beauty and significance of labor.

Genres: Art, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Work, Industry, Beauty of labor
