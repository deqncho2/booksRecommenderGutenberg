Synopsis: In 'The House on the Moor, v. 3/3' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), the mysterious secrets of a secluded mansion on the moor are finally revealed. As the protagonist delves deeper into the house's dark history, they uncover a web of deceit, betrayal, and supernatural occurrences that threaten to consume them. With each twist and turn, the tension builds, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the truth behind the house on the moor.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, the power of the supernatural, the struggle between good and evil
