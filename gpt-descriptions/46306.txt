Synopsis: In 'Knots Untied; Or, Ways and By-ways in the Hidden Life of American Detectives', George S. McWatters takes readers on a thrilling journey into the mysterious world of American detectives. Through a series of captivating stories, McWatters unveils the intricate web of crime and the relentless pursuit of justice. From high-stakes robberies to cunning con artists, this book delves into the hidden life of detectives as they navigate through the darkest corners of society. With each page, readers will be hooked by the suspenseful twists and turns, eagerly trying to unravel the knots that bind these captivating tales together.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Non-fiction

Themes: Crime and Justice, Investigation, Human Nature
