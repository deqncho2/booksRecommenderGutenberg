Synopsis: In 'The Edda, Volume 1: The Divine Mythology of the North', L. Winifred Faraday delves into the captivating world of Norse mythology, bringing to life the epic tales of gods, heroes, and mythical creatures. From the creation of the world to the final battle of Ragnarok, this volume explores the rich tapestry of Norse myths, including the adventures of Thor, Odin, Loki, and many others. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Faraday presents a comprehensive and enthralling introduction to the ancient myths that have inspired countless works of literature and art.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Romance

Themes: Gods and deities, Heroism, Fate and destiny
