Synopsis: In 'The Strange Adventures of Captain Dangerous, Vol. 3: Who was a sailor, a soldier, a merchant, a spy, a slave; among the moors...', George Augustus Sala takes readers on a thrilling journey through the life of Captain Dangerous. From his daring exploits on the high seas to his encounters with mysterious moors, this volume delves into the captain's multifaceted past. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and danger lurks at every turn. Brace yourself for an exhilarating adventure that will keep you on the edge of your seat!

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Survival, Betrayal
