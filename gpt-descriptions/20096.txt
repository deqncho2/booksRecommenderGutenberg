Synopsis: 
'Welsh Folk-Lore: a Collection of the Folk-Tales and Legends of North Wales' by Owen Elias is a captivating compilation of enchanting folk-tales and legends that have been passed down through generations in North Wales. From mythical creatures and magical encounters to heroic quests and tragic love stories, this collection immerses readers in the rich tapestry of Welsh folklore. Delve into the mystical world of dragons, fairies, and witches as you journey through the captivating landscapes of North Wales, where ancient traditions and supernatural beings intertwine.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Welsh culture and traditions, Supernatural beings, Heroism, Love and loss
