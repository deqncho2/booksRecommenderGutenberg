Synopsis: In 'The Stokesley Secret' by Charlotte M. Yonge, a young woman named Lucy discovers a hidden family secret that has been kept hidden for generations. As she delves deeper into her family's history, she uncovers a web of intrigue, betrayal, and forbidden love. With each revelation, Lucy's world is turned upside down, and she must navigate the complexities of her family's past while also grappling with her own identity and desires. Set against the backdrop of a quaint English village, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Forbidden love, Betrayal
