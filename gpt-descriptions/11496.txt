Synopsis: In 'Poetry' by Arthur Quiller-Couch, a young aspiring poet finds himself caught between the allure of fame and the true essence of his craft. As he navigates the literary world, he encounters a mysterious mentor who challenges his perception of what it means to be a poet. With each encounter, the protagonist is forced to confront his own desires and confront the question: What is the true purpose of poetry?

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Literary

Themes: Artistic integrity, Ambition, Identity, Mentorship
