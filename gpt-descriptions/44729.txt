Synopsis: In 'The Passenger Pigeon', a renowned ornithologist embarks on a captivating journey to uncover the mysterious disappearance of the once abundant and awe-inspiring passenger pigeon. As he delves into the past, he unravels a web of secrets, encounters unexpected allies, and confronts the devastating consequences of human actions. This gripping tale takes readers on an enthralling adventure that intertwines history, science, and the power of nature's resilience.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Environmental fiction

Themes: Extinction, Conservation, Human impact on nature
