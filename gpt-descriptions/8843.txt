Synopsis: In 'Weymouth New Testament in Modern Speech, 2 Timothy', Richard Francis Weymouth presents a contemporary translation of the second letter of Timothy, offering readers a fresh perspective on the teachings and wisdom of the apostle Paul. This powerful epistle delves into the challenges faced by early Christians, exploring themes of faith, perseverance, and the importance of passing on spiritual knowledge to future generations. Weymouth's modern language brings this ancient text to life, making it accessible and engaging for readers today.

Genres: Religious literature, Biblical translation, Christian non-fiction

Themes: Faith, Perseverance, Spiritual guidance, Passing on knowledge
