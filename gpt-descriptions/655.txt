Synopsis: 

'Life and Letters of Robert Browning' is a captivating biography that delves into the fascinating life and literary journey of the renowned poet, Robert Browning. Written by his wife, Sutherland Orr, this intimate account provides a unique perspective on Browning's personal experiences, creative process, and the profound impact he had on the world of poetry. Through a collection of letters, anecdotes, and insights, the reader is transported into the vibrant world of one of the most influential poets of the Victorian era. From his early struggles and triumphs to his enduring love story with Elizabeth Barrett Browning, this biography offers a captivating glimpse into the life of a literary genius.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Literary Criticism

Themes: Artistic expression, Love and relationships, Literary legacy
