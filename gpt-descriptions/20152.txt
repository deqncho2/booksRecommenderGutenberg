Synopsis: 
In 'The Winning Clue' by James Hay, a group of friends gather for a weekend getaway at a luxurious mansion. However, their peaceful retreat takes a thrilling turn when a mysterious murder occurs, leaving everyone as a potential suspect. As they navigate through a web of secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected alliances, the group must race against time to uncover the truth and identify the killer before they strike again. With each clue leading to more questions than answers, the tension rises, and the reader is left on the edge of their seat, eagerly trying to piece together the puzzle.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Trust, Investigation
