Synopsis: 
In 'Afghans' by Spool Cotton Co., embark on a captivating journey through the world of crochet as you discover a collection of stunning afghan patterns. From intricate designs inspired by nature to bold geometric patterns, this book offers a wide range of projects that will ignite your creativity and keep you hooked. Each pattern is accompanied by detailed instructions and helpful tips, making it accessible for both beginners and experienced crocheters. Whether you're looking to add a touch of warmth to your home or create a heartfelt gift for a loved one, 'Afghans' is a must-have resource that will inspire and delight.
