Synopsis: In 'Queen of the Blue World' by Basil Wells, a young woman named Lily discovers a hidden portal that transports her to a breathtaking underwater kingdom. As she navigates this enchanting realm, she becomes entangled in a dangerous power struggle between the ruling mermaid queen and a rebel faction. With her newfound abilities and the fate of the blue world hanging in the balance, Lily must choose between loyalty and her own destiny. 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration of hidden worlds, Power struggles, Self-discovery
