Synopsis: In 'Die, Shadow!' by Algis Budrys, a renowned scientist is haunted by a mysterious shadow that seems to follow him wherever he goes. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a web of secrets and conspiracies that threaten not only his own life but the fate of humanity itself. With time running out, he must confront his darkest fears and unravel the truth behind the relentless shadow before it consumes everything he holds dear.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Paranoia, Existentialism
