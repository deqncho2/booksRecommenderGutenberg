Synopsis: In the small town of Westville, a fierce battle erupts between two railway companies vying for control over the lucrative short line. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the townspeople find themselves caught in the crossfire of greed, corruption, and a fight for survival. With unexpected alliances forming and secrets unraveling, the Short Line War becomes a gripping tale of power, betrayal, and the lengths people will go to protect what they believe is rightfully theirs.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Western, Drama

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Corruption, Survival
