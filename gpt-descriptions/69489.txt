Synopsis: In the final installment of 'The Wellfields' trilogy, secrets long buried in the family's ancestral home are unearthed, threatening to tear the Wellfield family apart. As the siblings struggle to navigate their complicated relationships and confront their own demons, they must also confront the dark history that has haunted their family for generations. With the stakes higher than ever, the Wellfields must find a way to reconcile their past and forge a new future before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Family Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Redemption, Legacy
