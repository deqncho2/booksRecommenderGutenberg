Synopsis:
In 'Everybody's Lonesome: A True Fairy Story', Clara E. Laughlin takes readers on a captivating journey through a world where loneliness is a tangible force. The story follows a young girl named Lily who discovers a hidden realm where she encounters a cast of enchanting characters, each grappling with their own unique brand of loneliness. As Lily navigates this magical world, she learns valuable lessons about the power of connection and the importance of embracing one's true self. With its whimsical storytelling and heartfelt exploration of human emotions, this book is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres:
Fantasy, Fairy Tale, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
Loneliness, Connection, Self-Discovery
