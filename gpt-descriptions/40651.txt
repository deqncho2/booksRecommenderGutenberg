Synopsis: In 'The Hypocrite' by Thorne, Guy, a seemingly ordinary man named John leads a double life, hiding a dark secret that threatens to unravel his carefully constructed facade. As his past catches up with him, John finds himself entangled in a web of lies, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the complex world of this enigmatic character, questioning their own perceptions of truth and morality.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Morality
