Synopsis: In 'Marie: An Episode in the Life of the Late Allan Quatermain', the renowned adventurer Allan Quatermain finds himself entangled in a dangerous quest when he is approached by a mysterious woman named Marie. Drawn into a web of intrigue and treachery, Quatermain must navigate through perilous landscapes and face formidable adversaries to uncover the truth behind Marie's enigmatic past. As the stakes rise and the danger intensifies, Quatermain's loyalty and courage are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Betrayal, Redemption
