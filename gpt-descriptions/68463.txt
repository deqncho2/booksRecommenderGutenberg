Synopsis: In "The Salton Sea: An account of Harriman's fight with the Colorado River," George Kennan takes readers on a captivating journey through the dramatic battle between industrialist E.H. Harriman and the mighty Colorado River. Set against the backdrop of the early 20th century, this gripping narrative explores the audacious attempts to control and harness the power of nature, as Harriman's relentless pursuit of taming the river unfolds. From engineering feats to unexpected setbacks, the story delves into the high-stakes world of water management and the profound impact it has on both the environment and human lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Environmental Studies

Themes: Man vs. Nature, Environmental Conservation, Industrialization, Technological Advancements
