Synopsis: In 'Highways in Hiding' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist discovers a revolutionary invention that could change the course of humanity. However, his creation attracts the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess it. As the scientist goes on the run, he must navigate a treacherous world of espionage, danger, and unexpected allies, all while trying to protect his groundbreaking invention from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Technology and its impact on society, the pursuit of knowledge and its consequences, the struggle for freedom and survival.
