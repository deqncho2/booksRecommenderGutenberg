Synopsis: 
'Maps of Old London' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and hidden secrets of the city. Filled with beautifully illustrated maps, this book unveils the forgotten stories and lost landmarks that shaped London's past. From the bustling streets of medieval London to the grandeur of Victorian architecture, each map reveals a different era and invites readers to explore the city's vibrant tapestry of culture, politics, and intrigue. As readers delve into the pages of 'Maps of Old London', they will uncover the mysteries that lie beneath the surface of this iconic city, and gain a deeper understanding of its ever-evolving identity.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Art

Themes: Urban history, Cultural heritage, Architectural evolution
