Synopsis: 

In 'Twenty Years' Experience as a Ghost Hunter', O'Donnell, Elliott takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of paranormal investigations. With two decades of experience under his belt, the author shares captivating and bone-chilling tales of encounters with spirits, haunted locations, and unexplained phenomena. From abandoned asylums to ancient castles, O'Donnell, Elliott's firsthand accounts will leave readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the existence of the supernatural and the thin veil between the living and the dead.

Genres: Non-fiction, Paranormal, Memoir

Themes: Ghosts, Supernatural, Paranormal Investigations, Haunted Locations, Unexplained Phenomena
