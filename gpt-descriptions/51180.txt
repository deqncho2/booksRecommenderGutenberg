Synopsis: In "Santa Claus Gets His Wish: A Christmas Play in One Act For Young Children," join Santa Claus and his friends on a delightful adventure as they work together to make Christmas wishes come true. With a touch of magic and plenty of holiday cheer, this heartwarming play is sure to captivate young audiences and leave them filled with the spirit of Christmas.

Genres: Children's literature, Christmas, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Generosity, Believing in the magic of Christmas
