Synopsis: In "There Is Sorrow on the Sea" by Parker Gilbert, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger as she embarks on a perilous journey across the vast ocean. With secrets lurking beneath the surface and unexpected alliances forming, she must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the truth and secure her own survival. This gripping tale of adventure and suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Secrets
