Synopsis: In 'The Human Side of Animals', Dixon Royal takes readers on a captivating journey into the hidden depths of the animal kingdom. Through a series of remarkable anecdotes and scientific discoveries, Royal unveils the astonishing ways in which animals exhibit traits and behaviors that mirror our own. From the complex social dynamics of elephants to the remarkable problem-solving abilities of dolphins, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the human-like qualities found in various species. Prepare to be amazed as Royal delves into the captivating world of animal intelligence, emotions, and empathy, revealing the profound connections that exist between humans and the creatures that share our planet.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Animal intelligence, Emotional connections, Inter-species relationships
