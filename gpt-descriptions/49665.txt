Synopsis: In 'Drawing Rooms, Second Floor, and Attics: A Farce, in One Act' by John Maddison Morton, chaos ensues when a group of eccentric characters find themselves trapped in a labyrinthine mansion, desperately trying to navigate their way out. As they stumble upon secret passageways, hidden rooms, and unexpected encounters, the characters' true colors are revealed, leading to hilarious misunderstandings and uproarious situations. With each twist and turn, the audience is left wondering who will escape unscathed and who will be forever lost in the maze of this comedic farce.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Deception, Identity, Absurdity
