Synopsis: In 'History of the Anglo-Saxons, from the Earliest Period to the Norman Conquest: Second Edition', Thomas Miller takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and tumultuous history of the Anglo-Saxons. From their mysterious origins to their encounters with invading forces, this comprehensive account delves into the fascinating world of a people who shaped the destiny of England. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Miller brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and cultural developments of the Anglo-Saxons, leading up to the pivotal Norman Conquest. This second edition offers new insights and discoveries, making it an essential read for history enthusiasts and anyone seeking to understand the foundations of English history.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Anglo-Saxon culture, Invasion and conquest, Historical development
