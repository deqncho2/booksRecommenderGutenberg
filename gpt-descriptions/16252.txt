Synopsis: 
In 'Jan: A Dog and a Romance' by Dawson, A. J., a chance encounter between a lonely writer and an abandoned dog sets off a heartwarming and unexpected journey. As the writer takes in the dog, named Jan, their bond grows stronger, leading them both on an adventure filled with love, self-discovery, and the power of companionship. With each passing day, Jan teaches the writer valuable lessons about life, loyalty, and the importance of embracing second chances. Will this newfound connection be enough to heal their wounded hearts and bring them the happiness they both deserve?

Genres: Romance, Fiction, Animal Fiction

Themes: Love, Redemption, Healing, Companionship
