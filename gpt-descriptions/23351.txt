Synopsis:
"The Yacht Club; or, The Young Boat-Builder" by Optic, Oliver is a thrilling adventure that follows the journey of a young boat-builder named Paul Duncan. When Paul's father mysteriously disappears, he is determined to uncover the truth and sets out on a quest that takes him to the prestigious Yacht Club. As Paul navigates the challenges of building his own yacht and competing in high-stakes races, he discovers secrets that could change his life forever. With danger lurking at every turn, Paul must rely on his skills, courage, and the support of his newfound friends to unravel the mysteries that surround him.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family, Friendship, Perseverance, Discovery
