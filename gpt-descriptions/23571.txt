Synopsis: In 'General Max Shorter' by Neville Kris, a decorated military officer finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and betrayal when he is assigned to investigate a mysterious incident. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to shake the very foundations of the government he has sworn to protect. With his life on the line, General Max Shorter must navigate a treacherous landscape of power struggles and deceit, all while trying to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Political Fiction

Themes: Conspiracy, Betrayal, Power Struggles, Loyalty
