Synopsis: 
'Patriotic Song: A book of English verse' takes readers on a captivating journey through the patriotic poetry of the British Empire, spanning from the defeat of the Spanish Armada to the death of Queen Victoria. This anthology showcases the power of words in capturing the spirit, pride, and resilience of a nation. From epic battles to moments of triumph and loss, the verses within this collection evoke a sense of national identity and the enduring love for one's homeland. 

Genres: Poetry, Anthology, Historical

Themes: Patriotism, National Identity, Historical Events
