Synopsis: 
In 'The Shepherd of Banbury's Rules to Judge of the Changes of the Weather, Grounded on Forty Years' Experience', John Claridge shares his remarkable insights and observations on predicting the weather. Drawing from his extensive experience as a shepherd, Claridge unveils a fascinating collection of rules and signs that can help anyone become a skilled weather forecaster. From the behavior of animals to the appearance of the sky, he reveals the subtle clues that nature provides to anticipate changes in the weather. This captivating guide will leave readers astounded by the shepherd's uncanny ability to predict the unpredictable.

Genres: Non-fiction, Weather, Natural Sciences

Themes: Weather prediction, Nature's signs, Observational skills
