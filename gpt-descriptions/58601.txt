Synopsis: 
'Hiking Westward: Being the Story of Two Boys Whose Ambition Led Them to Face Privations and Hardships in Their Quest of a Home in the Great West' follows the journey of two young boys who embark on a daring adventure to find a new home in the vast and untamed American West. Faced with numerous challenges and obstacles, they must rely on their determination, resourcefulness, and the bond of friendship to overcome the hardships that await them. As they traverse through breathtaking landscapes and encounter a diverse cast of characters, their resilience and courage are put to the test. Will they find the home they seek, or will their dreams be shattered along the way?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Ambition, Friendship, Resilience, Exploration, Pursuit of a Dream
