Synopsis: In 'The Black Patch' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious murder takes place in a small English village, leaving the locals baffled and fearful. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, and the dark underbelly of the seemingly idyllic community is exposed. With a web of deceit and suspicion, the story takes unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking truth is revealed.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town secrets
