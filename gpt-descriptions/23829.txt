Synopsis: In 'Under the Rebel's Reign' by Neufeld, Charles, a young artist finds herself caught in the midst of a revolution, where her creativity becomes a powerful weapon against oppression. As she navigates the dangerous world of rebels and spies, she must confront her own fears and discover the true meaning of freedom. With unexpected alliances and heart-pounding twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Revolution, Artistic Expression, Freedom
