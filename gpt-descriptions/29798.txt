Synopsis: 
In 'Japan' by David Murray, a young American woman named Sarah embarks on a life-changing journey to Japan, a country steeped in ancient traditions and modern marvels. As she immerses herself in the vibrant culture, she encounters unexpected friendships, uncovers hidden secrets, and navigates the complexities of love and loss. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the serene temples of Kyoto, Sarah's adventure takes her on a captivating exploration of Japan's rich history, breathtaking landscapes, and the profound impact it has on her own identity.

Genres: Fiction, Travel, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Cultural immersion, Identity, Friendship, Love and loss
