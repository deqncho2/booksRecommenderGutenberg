Synopsis: In 'Darry the Life Saver; Or, The Heroes of the Coast' by Frank V. Webster, readers are taken on a thrilling journey along the treacherous coastlines where danger lurks at every turn. Darry, a young and courageous protagonist, finds himself facing perilous situations as he becomes a vital part of the coast's rescue team. With heart-pounding rescues, unexpected twists, and a cast of unforgettable characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Young Adult

Themes: Bravery, Heroism, Teamwork
