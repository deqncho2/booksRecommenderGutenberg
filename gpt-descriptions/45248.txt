Synopsis: In 'The Winepress' by Christine Beals, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn into a mysterious world when she inherits an old vineyard from her estranged grandmother. As she delves into the secrets of the vineyard, Emily uncovers a hidden society and a dark history that threatens to consume her. With the help of a charismatic winemaker and a group of unlikely allies, she must navigate a treacherous path filled with betrayal, danger, and forbidden love. As Emily's journey unfolds, she discovers that the power of the vineyard goes far beyond the production of wine, and that her own destiny is intricately intertwined with its ancient roots.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Family secrets, Redemption, Fate, Forbidden love
