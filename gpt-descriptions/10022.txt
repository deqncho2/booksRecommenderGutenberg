Synopsis: In "White Queen of the Cannibals: the Story of Mary Slessor of Calabar" by Bueltmann, A. J., readers are taken on a captivating journey through the life of Mary Slessor, a courageous and determined woman who defied societal norms and dedicated her life to helping the people of Calabar. From her humble beginnings in Scotland to her extraordinary adventures in Africa, Slessor's story is one of resilience, compassion, and unwavering faith. As she navigates the treacherous terrain of the Calabar region, facing dangers and cultural barriers, Slessor's unwavering commitment to justice and equality shines through. This gripping biography sheds light on the remarkable life of a woman who became a legend in her own time.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Courage, Compassion, Cultural Understanding
