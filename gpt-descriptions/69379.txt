Synopsis: In 'Prince Ragnal, and other holiday verses' by Eleanor C. Donnelly, embark on a delightful journey through a collection of enchanting holiday poems. From the whimsical adventures of Prince Ragnal to the heartwarming tales of Christmas cheer, this book offers a captivating blend of fantasy, magic, and festive spirit. Each verse transports readers to a world filled with wonder, where they will encounter mischievous elves, brave knights, and the true meaning of the holiday season. With its lyrical prose and imaginative storytelling, this collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Holiday

Themes: Magic, Adventure, Christmas spirit
