Synopsis: 
'Prison Poetry' by McKnight, Hiram Peck takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden world of incarceration. The story follows the life of a talented young poet who finds himself behind bars, where he discovers a newfound passion for writing. As he navigates the harsh realities of prison life, he uses his poetry as a means of escape, self-expression, and ultimately, redemption. With each verse, he delves into the depths of his emotions, relationships, and the power of words. 'Prison Poetry' is a gripping tale that explores the transformative power of art and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: 
Drama, Poetry, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Redemption, Self-expression, Resilience
