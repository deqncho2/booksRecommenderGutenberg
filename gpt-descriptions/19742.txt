Synopsis: In the enchanting Scottish Highlands, a young woman named Heather finds herself torn between two suitors, each with their own secrets and hidden agendas. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of love and betrayal, Heather must uncover the truth about her own identity and make a choice that will change her life forever.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Identity and self-discovery, Betrayal and deception
