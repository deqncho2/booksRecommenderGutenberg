Synopsis:
In "Frida; or, the lover's leap: a legend of the West Country," we are transported to the rugged landscapes of the West Country, where a haunting legend unfolds. The story follows Frida, a young woman whose life takes a dramatic turn when she falls in love with a mysterious stranger. As their forbidden romance blossoms, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and danger. With the stunning backdrop of the West Country, this captivating tale explores the lengths one will go to for love and the consequences that follow.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes:
Forbidden love, Betrayal, Secrets, Sacrifice
