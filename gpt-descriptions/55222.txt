Synopsis: In 'Misunderstood' by Florence Montgomery, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she becomes entangled with a mysterious stranger. As she delves deeper into his world, Emily must navigate the blurred lines between truth and lies, love and betrayal. With each twist and turn, she uncovers shocking revelations that challenge everything she thought she knew about herself and those around her. Will Emily be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Trust
