Synopsis: 

'Hymns for Christian Devotion: Especially Adapted to the Universalist Denomination' is a collection of hymns curated by Edwin Hubbell Chapin and John Greenleaf Adams. This compilation offers a diverse selection of hymns that are specifically tailored to the Universalist Denomination, providing a rich source of spiritual inspiration and guidance. From heartfelt expressions of faith to contemplative reflections on the nature of God, these hymns encompass a wide range of emotions and themes, inviting readers to deepen their connection with their faith and explore the beauty of Christian devotion.

Genres: Religious, Poetry, Hymnology

Themes: Faith, Worship, Universalism, Spiritual Reflection
