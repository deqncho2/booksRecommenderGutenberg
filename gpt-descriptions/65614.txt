Synopsis: In 'Mr. Togo: Maid of all Work' by Irwin Wallace, a seemingly ordinary man named Mr. Togo takes on the role of a maid, but there is more to him than meets the eye. As he navigates the challenges of his new job, he uncovers hidden secrets and becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling and suspenseful tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Intrigue
