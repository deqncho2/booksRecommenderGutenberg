Synopsis: In the thrilling novel 'The Black Phantom' by Leo E. Miller, a mysterious vigilante emerges in the crime-ridden city of New Haven. Known only as the Black Phantom, this enigmatic figure strikes fear into the hearts of criminals, leaving a trail of justice in his wake. As the city's corrupt underworld scrambles to uncover the identity of their new nemesis, a relentless detective becomes determined to bring the Black Phantom to justice. With each twist and turn, the line between hero and villain blurs, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the final revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Vigilantism, Corruption, Identity
