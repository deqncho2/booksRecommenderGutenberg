Synopsis: 

Step into the rich tapestry of Lancashire's history as Henry Fishwick takes you on a captivating journey through time. From the ancient Roman settlements to the bustling industrial revolution, 'A History of Lancashire' unveils the hidden stories and forgotten legends that have shaped this remarkable county. Delve into the lives of Lancashire's inhabitants, their triumphs and struggles, as you explore the vibrant landscapes and witness the evolution of a region steeped in heritage. Fishwick's meticulous research and vivid storytelling breathe life into the past, transporting readers to a world where battles were fought, industries flourished, and communities thrived. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Local History

Themes: Heritage, Community, Industrialization, Cultural Evolution
