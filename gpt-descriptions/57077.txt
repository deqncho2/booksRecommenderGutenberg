Synopsis: In 'The Old and the New Magic' by Henry Ridgely Evans, a renowned magician embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets of ancient mystical practices. As he delves deeper into the world of magic, he encounters a mysterious group of individuals who possess extraordinary powers. With each encounter, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between illusion and reality blurs. This captivating tale takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the realms of both old and new magic.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Illusion, Power, Mystery
