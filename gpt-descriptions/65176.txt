Synopsis: In 'Get Out of My Body!' by Tom W. Harris, a seemingly ordinary teenager named Max discovers a mysterious ability that allows him to switch bodies with other people. At first, he sees it as a thrilling adventure, but soon realizes that this power comes with unexpected consequences. As Max delves deeper into the secrets behind his newfound ability, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of deception and betrayal. With time running out, Max must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and reclaim control over his own body.

Genres: Young Adult, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Power, Morality
