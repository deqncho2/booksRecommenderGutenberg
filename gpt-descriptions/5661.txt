Synopsis: 
In 'A Son of the Gods, and A Horseman in the Sky', Ambrose Bierce takes readers on a journey through the complexities of war and the moral dilemmas faced by soldiers. The story follows two soldiers, one on the battlefield and the other in the sky, as they grapple with their duty, honor, and the consequences of their actions. As the narrative unfolds, unexpected twists and thought-provoking moments challenge the reader's perception of heroism and sacrifice.

Genres: 
War fiction, Historical fiction, Short story

Themes: 
War, Duty, Morality, Sacrifice
