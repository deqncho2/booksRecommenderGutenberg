Synopsis: In a post-apocalyptic England, where nature has reclaimed the land and mankind has regressed to a primitive state, a young man named Felix embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of the past. As he navigates through the wild and treacherous landscape, encountering both friend and foe, Felix must confront the harsh realities of survival and the enduring power of hope. 'After London; Or, Wild England' is a captivating tale of adventure and exploration, set in a world where civilization has crumbled and nature reigns supreme.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Dystopian

Themes: Post-apocalyptic society, Nature vs. Civilization, Survival
