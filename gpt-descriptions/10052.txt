Synopsis: In 'The Open Door, and the Portrait: Stories of the Seen and the Unseen,' Mrs. Oliphant takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of mysterious and supernatural tales. From a haunted house with a secret to a portrait that holds a chilling power, each story explores the boundaries between the visible and the invisible, the known and the unknown. With her masterful storytelling, Mrs. Oliphant weaves together suspense, intrigue, and the supernatural, leaving readers eager to uncover the secrets hidden within each tale.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural fiction, mystery

Themes: The supernatural, the power of the unseen, the boundaries between reality and the unknown, the mysteries of the human psyche
