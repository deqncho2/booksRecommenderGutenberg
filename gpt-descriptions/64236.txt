Synopsis: 

'Under Foch's Command: A Tale of the Americans in France' follows the thrilling journey of a group of American soldiers who find themselves under the command of the legendary General Ferdinand Foch during World War I. As they navigate the treacherous battlefields of France, they must not only face the horrors of war but also grapple with their own personal demons and forge unbreakable bonds of brotherhood. With danger lurking at every turn, the Americans must prove their mettle and fight for their lives, all while striving to make a difference in a war-torn world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Leadership
