Synopsis: In 'The Battle of Sempach' by Robert Walser, a small Swiss village becomes the backdrop for a gripping tale of honor, sacrifice, and unexpected heroism. As tensions rise between the ruling nobility and the common people, a group of unlikely allies must band together to defend their freedom and way of life. With vivid descriptions and a captivating narrative, Walser transports readers to the heart of a historic battle that will forever change the course of their beloved homeland.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Social class, Patriotism
