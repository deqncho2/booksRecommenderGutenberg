Synopsis: 

In 'Dr. Johnson's Works: Life, Poems, and Tales, Volume 1: The Works of Samuel Johnson, LL.D., in Nine Volumes', readers are invited into the captivating world of one of the most influential literary figures of the 18th century. This volume delves into the life, poems, and tales of Samuel Johnson, offering a glimpse into the mind of a brilliant writer and thinker. From his early struggles to his rise as a renowned author, Johnson's journey is filled with fascinating anecdotes, thought-provoking poetry, and captivating tales that will leave readers eager to explore the rest of his works.

Genres: Biography, Poetry, Fiction

Themes: Literary criticism, Morality, Human nature
