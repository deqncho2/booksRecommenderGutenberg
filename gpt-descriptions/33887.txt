Synopsis: 
'Fairy and Folk Tales of the Irish Peasantry' takes readers on a captivating journey into the enchanting world of Irish folklore. Filled with magical creatures, mischievous fairies, and ancient legends, this collection of tales transports readers to a time when the line between reality and fantasy was blurred. From encounters with shape-shifting witches to encounters with the legendary leprechauns, each story is a delightful blend of whimsy, mystery, and a touch of the supernatural. Through these tales, readers will discover the rich tapestry of Irish culture and the enduring power of storytelling.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Irish folklore, Supernatural beings, Oral tradition, Cultural heritage
