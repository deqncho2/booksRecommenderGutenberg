Synopsis: In 'Robin' by Frances Hodgson Burnett, a young girl named Robin is sent to live with her aunt in a quaint English village. As she explores her new surroundings, Robin discovers a hidden secret that leads her on a captivating journey of self-discovery and adventure. With the help of newfound friends, she unravels the mysteries of her past and learns the power of resilience and courage. 

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Resilience
