Synopsis: In "It Never Can Happen Again" by William De Morgan, a young man named Richard is thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when he inherits a peculiar object from his late uncle. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the object, Richard finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, he uncovers a hidden world that challenges his beliefs and forces him to question the very fabric of reality. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this captivating novel takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected surprises and profound revelations.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Love and betrayal, Perception of reality
