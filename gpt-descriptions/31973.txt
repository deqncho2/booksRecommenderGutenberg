Synopsis: In 'The Rhode Island Artillery at the First Battle of Bull Run', author J. Albert Monroe takes readers on a gripping journey through the chaos and bravery of one of the Civil War's most significant battles. Through the eyes of the Rhode Island Artillery, we witness the intensity and uncertainty of the battlefield, as well as the personal sacrifices made by these courageous soldiers. As the battle unfolds, unexpected alliances are formed, and the true nature of war is revealed. Will the Rhode Island Artillery prevail in the face of overwhelming odds? Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Action Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Camaraderie
