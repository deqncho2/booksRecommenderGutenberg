Synopsis: In "Micromegas," Voltaire takes readers on a captivating journey through the vastness of the universe, where two extraterrestrial beings embark on an extraordinary adventure. As they explore different planets and encounter various civilizations, they engage in thought-provoking conversations that challenge the very foundations of human knowledge and beliefs. With wit and satire, Voltaire invites readers to ponder the mysteries of existence and the limitations of human understanding.

Genres: Science fiction, philosophical fiction, satire

Themes: Exploration of the universe, the nature of knowledge, the limitations of human understanding, satire of societal norms
