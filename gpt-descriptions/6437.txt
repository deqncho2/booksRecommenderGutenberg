Synopsis: 
In 'The Splendid Spur: Being Memoirs of the Adventures of Mr. John Marvel, a Servant of His Late Majesty King Charles I, in the Years 1642-3', we follow the thrilling escapades of John Marvel, a loyal servant to King Charles I during the tumultuous years of 1642-3. As the English Civil War rages on, Marvel finds himself embroiled in a dangerous mission that takes him across the war-torn countryside, encountering treacherous enemies and unexpected allies along the way. With his unwavering loyalty and resourcefulness, Marvel becomes a key player in a high-stakes game of intrigue and survival, where his every move could determine the fate of the monarchy.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Intrigue, Survival
