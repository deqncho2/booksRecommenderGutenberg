Synopsis: 
'Yorkshire Ditties, Second Series: To which is added The Cream of Wit and Humour; from his Popular Writings' by John Hartley is a delightful collection of witty and humorous poems that capture the essence of Yorkshire life. From charming anecdotes to clever wordplay, this book takes readers on a journey through the unique dialect and culture of Yorkshire, offering a glimpse into the everyday experiences and idiosyncrasies of its people. With a mix of lightheartedness and astute observations, Hartley's verses will leave readers chuckling and nodding in recognition, as they uncover the hidden gems of wit and humor within the Yorkshire landscape.

Genres: Poetry, Humor, Regional Literature
Themes: Yorkshire culture, Everyday experiences, Wit and humor
