Synopsis: 
In 'The Big-Town Round-Up' by Raine, William MacLeod, a notorious outlaw finds himself caught in a web of deception and danger when he stumbles upon a small town with a dark secret. As he tries to uncover the truth, he becomes entangled in a thrilling game of cat and mouse, where the line between good and evil becomes blurred. With unexpected twists and a cast of intriguing characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Justice, Morality
