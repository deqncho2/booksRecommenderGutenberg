Synopsis: 
In "Fishing with a Worm" by Bliss Perry, a young boy named Tommy embarks on a summer adventure that takes him far beyond the familiar shores of his small town. With his trusty fishing rod and a bucket of worms, Tommy sets out to explore the mysteries of the nearby lake, encountering unexpected challenges and surprising friendships along the way. As he navigates the waters and learns the art of fishing, Tommy discovers that the true treasures lie not only in the depths of the lake but also within himself.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Nature

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Self-discovery
