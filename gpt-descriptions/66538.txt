Synopsis: In 'Revolt of the Outworlds' by Stephen Marlowe, a group of rebels from different planets unite to overthrow a tyrannical empire that has oppressed their worlds for centuries. As they navigate treacherous alliances, dangerous missions, and unexpected betrayals, they must confront their own inner demons and question the true meaning of freedom. With high-stakes action, complex characters, and a gripping plot, this thrilling space opera will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Space Opera, Adventure

Themes: Rebellion, Oppression, Freedom, Loyalty, Betrayal
