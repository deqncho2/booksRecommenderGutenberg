Synopsis: In "When Santiago Fell; or, The War Adventures of Two Chums" by Edward Stratemeyer, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside two close friends, Santiago and his chum. As war erupts, the two find themselves caught up in a series of dangerous and life-altering adventures. From the trenches to the front lines, they face the harsh realities of battle, testing their friendship and courage to the limits. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding moments, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, War, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival
