Synopsis: In 'The Homicidal Diary' by Peirce, Earl, a seemingly ordinary man discovers a hidden diary that reveals a chilling secret. As he delves deeper into its pages, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and danger, questioning his own sanity and the true nature of those around him. With each entry, the diary unveils a sinister plot that threatens to consume him entirely. As the lines between reality and fiction blur, the protagonist must confront his darkest fears and make a choice that will determine his fate. 

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Sanity, Deception, Betrayal
