Synopsis: 
In "The Modern Clock: A Study of Time Keeping Mechanism; Its Construction, Regulation and Repair," author Ward L. Goodrich takes readers on a fascinating journey into the intricate world of timekeeping mechanisms. From the inner workings of mechanical clocks to the precision of quartz and atomic clocks, this comprehensive study explores the construction, regulation, and repair of various timekeeping devices. Goodrich's meticulous attention to detail and engaging writing style make this book a captivating read for both clock enthusiasts and those curious about the history and mechanics of timekeeping.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Timekeeping, Mechanics, Precision, History of Clocks
