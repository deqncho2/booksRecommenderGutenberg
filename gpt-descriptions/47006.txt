Synopsis: In the Days of Queen Elizabeth takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous reign of one of England's most iconic monarchs. From political intrigue and religious conflicts to courtly romance and daring adventures, this historical novel immerses readers in the vibrant world of Queen Elizabeth I. Through the eyes of a young noblewoman, we witness the challenges and triumphs of a queen who defied expectations and shaped the destiny of a nation.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and politics, Love and loyalty, Gender and identity
