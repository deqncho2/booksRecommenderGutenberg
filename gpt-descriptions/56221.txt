Synopsis: In 'Backwater: Pilgrimage, Volume 2', the captivating sequel to the first volume, author Dorothy M. Richardson continues the enthralling journey of Miriam Henderson. Set in early 20th century England, Miriam navigates through the complexities of her personal and professional life, encountering new relationships, unexpected challenges, and profound self-discovery. As she delves deeper into her own thoughts and emotions, Miriam's pilgrimage becomes a captivating exploration of identity, independence, and the transformative power of introspection.

Genres: Modernist literature, coming-of-age, psychological fiction

Themes: Identity, self-discovery, personal growth, introspection
