Synopsis: In 'Fathers of Men' by E. W. Hornung, a young man named Jack Darragh finds himself caught between loyalty to his country and his love for a woman. As war looms on the horizon, Jack is faced with a difficult decision that will test his courage and honor. With vivid descriptions of adventure, romance, and the complexities of human nature, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Love, War
