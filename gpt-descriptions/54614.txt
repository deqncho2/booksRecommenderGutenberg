Synopsis: In 'The Land of Cockayne: A Novel' by Matilde Serao, readers are transported to the vibrant city of Naples, where the lives of three women intertwine in unexpected ways. As the city's social scene comes alive with lavish parties, scandalous affairs, and hidden secrets, the protagonists find themselves caught in a web of desire, ambition, and betrayal. With its vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this novel offers a mesmerizing exploration of the human condition and the pursuit of happiness in a world filled with temptation and indulgence.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Desire, Social Class, Happiness
