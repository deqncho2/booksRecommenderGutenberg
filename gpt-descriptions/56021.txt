Synopsis: 

In "Poisonous Snakes of Texas and First Aid Treatment of Their Bites: Bulletin No. 31," John E. Werler delves into the captivating world of venomous snakes that inhabit the vast landscapes of Texas. This comprehensive guide provides essential information on identifying and understanding the behavior of these dangerous reptiles, as well as offering crucial first aid treatment for snake bites. From the venomous rattlesnakes to the elusive copperheads, Werler's expertise and engaging writing style make this a must-read for anyone curious about the venomous serpents of Texas.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Guide, Reference

Themes: Wildlife, Survival, First Aid
