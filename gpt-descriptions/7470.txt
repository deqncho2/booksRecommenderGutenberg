Synopsis: 
'Castilian Days' by John Hay is a captivating collection of travel essays that takes readers on a vivid journey through the enchanting landscapes and vibrant culture of Spain. From the bustling streets of Madrid to the serene countryside of Andalusia, Hay's lyrical prose and keen observations paint a vivid picture of the country's rich history, passionate people, and timeless traditions. Through encounters with locals, explorations of historic sites, and indulgence in delicious cuisine, Hay immerses readers in the beauty and complexity of Spain, offering a unique perspective on this captivating country.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Cultural exploration, History, Identity, Tradition
