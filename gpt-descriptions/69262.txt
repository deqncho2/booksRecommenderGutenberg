Synopsis: 
In 'History for ready reference, Volume 4, Nicæa to Tunis', J.N. Larned takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period from the Council of Nicæa to the fall of Tunis. This volume delves into the rise and fall of empires, the clash of religions, and the struggles for power that shaped the course of history. From the Byzantine Empire to the rise of Islam, Larned's meticulous research and engaging storytelling bring to life the key events, influential figures, and epic battles that defined this era. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, this volume offers a comprehensive and enthralling exploration of a pivotal period in human history.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Empires and conquest, Religion and conflict, Power and politics
