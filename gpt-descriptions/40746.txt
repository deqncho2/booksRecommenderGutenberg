Synopsis: 
'First Impressions on a Tour upon the Continent: In the summer of 1818 through parts of France, Italy, Switzerland, the borders of Germany, and a part of French Flanders' takes readers on a captivating journey through various European countries. Baillie, Marianne shares her personal experiences and observations as she explores the landscapes, cultures, and people she encounters during her travels. From the bustling streets of France to the picturesque landscapes of Italy and Switzerland, the author provides vivid descriptions that transport readers to each location. Through her unique perspective, Baillie offers a glimpse into the beauty, diversity, and complexities of the European continent during the early 19th century.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Diversity, Personal Growth
