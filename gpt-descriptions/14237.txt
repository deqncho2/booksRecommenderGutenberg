Synopsis: In "The Christian Home," Samuel Philips delves into the intricate dynamics of the home, exploring its mission, duties, influences, habits, and responsibilities. With a focus on education, government, and discipline, Philips provides valuable insights into the role of parents and the choices they make for their children's marriages. The book also offers guidance on the selection of a suitable companion and the tests one should consider. Through a thoughtful examination of the Christian home, Philips offers readers a deeper understanding of the importance of this sacred institution.

Genres: Non-fiction, Christian literature, Family and relationships

Themes: Christian values, Parental responsibility, Marriage and companionship, Home education, Family dynamics
