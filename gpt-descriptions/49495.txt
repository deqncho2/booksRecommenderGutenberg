Synopsis: 
In "Recollections of Thomas D. Duncan, a Confederate Soldier," Thomas D. Duncan takes us on a captivating journey through his experiences as a soldier during the American Civil War. From the initial excitement of enlisting to the harsh realities of battle, Duncan's recollections provide a vivid and personal account of the war's impact on both individuals and the nation as a whole. Through his unique perspective, readers are transported to the front lines, witnessing the camaraderie, sacrifices, and horrors of war. As Duncan reflects on his time as a Confederate soldier, he offers a poignant exploration of loyalty, patriotism, and the complexities of war.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Military History
Themes: War, Patriotism, Loyalty, Human Resilience
