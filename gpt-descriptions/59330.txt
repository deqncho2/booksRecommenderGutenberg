Synopsis: In "Looking Further Forward: An Answer to Looking Backward," Richard Michaelis presents a captivating response to Edward Bellamy's influential utopian novel. Set in a future society where technology has transformed every aspect of life, Michaelis explores the consequences of a world driven by progress and the potential dangers that lie beneath its shiny surface. Through a thought-provoking narrative, the author challenges the reader to question the true nature of progress and the sacrifices that may come with it. As the protagonist navigates this futuristic world, they uncover startling truths that will leave readers questioning their own beliefs about the future. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: Progress and its consequences, Utopia vs. Dystopia, Technological advancements and their impact on society
