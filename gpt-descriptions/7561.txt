Synopsis: 
'Quotes and Images From Memoirs of Louis XV. and XVI.' is a captivating collection of quotes and images from the memoirs of Louis XV and XVI, as recorded by Du Hausset and Mme. Lamballe. Through their intimate accounts, the reader is transported to the opulent and tumultuous world of the French monarchy, gaining insight into the lives, thoughts, and experiences of these two kings. The book offers a unique perspective on the political intrigues, personal relationships, and historical events that shaped the reigns of Louis XV and XVI, providing a fascinating glimpse into the lives of these iconic figures.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, Memoir, Biography

Themes: 
Monarchy, Politics, Personal relationships, Historical events
