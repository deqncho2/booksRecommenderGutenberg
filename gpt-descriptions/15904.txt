Synopsis: In "The Rover Boys on the River; Or, The Search for the Missing Houseboat" by Edward Stratemeyer, the adventurous Rover brothers find themselves embarking on a thrilling quest to locate a mysteriously vanished houseboat. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter suspicious characters, and face unexpected challenges, the brothers must rely on their wit, courage, and teamwork to unravel the enigma and bring the missing houseboat back to safety. With each twist and turn, the Rovers' determination and resourcefulness are put to the test, making for an exhilarating and suspenseful journey.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Brotherhood, Exploration, Problem-solving
