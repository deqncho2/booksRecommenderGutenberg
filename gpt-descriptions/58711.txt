Synopsis: 
In 'Traced and Tracked; Or, Memoirs of a City Detective' by M'Govan, James, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the dangerous and mysterious world of a city detective. The story follows the protagonist as he unravels a series of perplexing crimes, navigating through the dark underbelly of the city and encountering a cast of intriguing characters along the way. As the detective delves deeper into the investigations, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Justice and morality, Deception and manipulation
