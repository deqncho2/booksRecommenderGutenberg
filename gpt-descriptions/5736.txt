Synopsis: 
In 'The Professional Aunt' by Mary C. E. Wemyss, a young woman named Emily finds herself thrust into the role of a professional aunt, caring for her sister's children while their parents are away. As she navigates the challenges of balancing her own dreams and desires with the responsibilities of being an aunt, Emily discovers the transformative power of love, family, and unexpected connections. With a cast of vibrant characters and a heartwarming storyline, this novel explores the complexities of familial relationships and the joys and sacrifices that come with being an aunt.

Genres: Fiction, Family Drama, Women's Fiction

Themes: Family, Love, Responsibility, Self-discovery
