Synopsis: In "Fighting in Cuban Waters; Or, Under Schley on the Brooklyn," we follow the thrilling adventures of a young naval officer during the Spanish-American War. As he serves aboard the USS Brooklyn, he finds himself in the midst of intense battles, daring rescues, and unexpected encounters. With danger lurking at every turn, our protagonist must navigate the treacherous waters of war while facing personal challenges that test his courage and loyalty. Will he emerge victorious and fulfill his duty to his country?

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Patriotism, Bravery, Loyalty
