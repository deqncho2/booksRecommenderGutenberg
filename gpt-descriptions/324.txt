Synopsis: In 'A Knight of the Cumberland' by John Fox, Jr., a young man named Lance Cleaverage finds himself torn between his duty to his family and his desire for adventure. Set in the rugged mountains of Kentucky, Lance embarks on a journey that will test his courage, loyalty, and honor. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters a cast of colorful characters, Lance must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. This gripping tale of love, sacrifice, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Duty and Responsibility, Identity and Self-discovery, Love and Sacrifice
