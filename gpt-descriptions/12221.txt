Synopsis:
In 'Beggars Bush: A Comedy', we are transported to a world of disguises, mistaken identities, and unexpected alliances. Set in the bustling city of London, the story follows a group of beggars who find themselves caught up in a web of love, deceit, and political intrigue. As they navigate their way through the treacherous streets, they must rely on their wit and cunning to survive. With a colorful cast of characters, including a cunning courtesan, a nobleman in disguise, and a bumbling constable, this comedic tale is filled with laughter, romance, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Deception, Social class, Love and desire
