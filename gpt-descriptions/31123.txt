Synopsis: In 'The Observers' by Vandenburg, G. L., a group of seemingly ordinary individuals discover they possess an extraordinary ability to observe events from the past. As they delve deeper into their newfound powers, they uncover a hidden world of secrets and conspiracies that threaten to unravel the fabric of reality itself. With each observation, they become entangled in a web of danger and must navigate the blurred lines between past and present to protect themselves and those they hold dear.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Time manipulation, Identity, Conspiracy
