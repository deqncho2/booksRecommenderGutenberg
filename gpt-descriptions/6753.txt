Synopsis: In 'Psmith in the City', the charismatic and unconventional Psmith finds himself embarking on a new adventure in the bustling world of finance. Joining the staff of a London bank, Psmith's wit and charm quickly make him a favorite among his colleagues, but his true motives remain a mystery. As he navigates the cutthroat world of business, Psmith's unique approach to life and work brings both hilarity and unexpected consequences. With his trademark humor and cleverness, Wodehouse takes readers on a delightful journey through the ups and downs of city life.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Identity, Workplace dynamics
