Synopsis: 
The Project Gutenberg Web Pages takes readers on a captivating journey through the digital realm of Project Gutenberg, a groundbreaking initiative that aims to make literature accessible to all. This collection of web pages unveils the fascinating history, challenges, and triumphs behind the world's oldest digital library. From the tireless efforts of volunteers to the vast array of literary treasures available, this immersive experience offers a glimpse into the transformative power of technology and the enduring love for literature that unites people across time and space.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Accessibility, Literature, Digital Revolution
