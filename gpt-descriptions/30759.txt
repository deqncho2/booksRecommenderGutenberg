Synopsis: 
In 'Exit Betty' by Grace Livingston Hill, Betty is a young woman who finds herself caught in a web of deceit and danger when she stumbles upon a mysterious secret. As she tries to uncover the truth, she must navigate through unexpected twists and turns, facing both love and betrayal along the way. With her faith and determination, Betty embarks on a thrilling journey that will test her courage and ultimately lead her to a life-changing decision.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Deception
