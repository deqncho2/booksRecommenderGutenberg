Synopsis: In 'This Simian World', Clarence Day takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating and often humorous world of our primate relatives. Through a series of witty and insightful observations, Day explores the similarities and differences between humans and monkeys, shedding light on the quirks and complexities of both species. From their social dynamics to their intelligence and even their sense of humor, Day's exploration of the simian world offers a fresh perspective on our own existence, leaving readers with a newfound appreciation for the intricate tapestry of life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Humor

Themes: Human nature, Animal behavior, Evolution
