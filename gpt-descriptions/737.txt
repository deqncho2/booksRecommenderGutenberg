Synopsis: The Bobbsey Twins, Nan and Bert, embark on a thrilling new adventure as they enter school for the first time. From making new friends to solving mysterious puzzles, the twins find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected events that test their courage and intelligence. As they navigate the challenges of school life, the Bobbsey Twins discover the importance of teamwork, loyalty, and the power of curiosity.

Genres: Children's literature, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Problem-solving, Education
