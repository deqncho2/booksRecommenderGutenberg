Synopsis: In 'Gospel Philosophy: Showing the Absurdities of Infidelity, and the Harmony of the Gospel with Science and History', Joseph Harvey Ward presents a thought-provoking exploration of the intersection between faith, reason, and the natural world. Through a captivating blend of scientific evidence, historical analysis, and philosophical insights, Ward challenges the skeptics and skeptics alike, inviting readers on a journey that unveils the surprising harmony between the Gospel and the realms of science and history. Prepare to have your beliefs challenged and your mind opened as Ward skillfully navigates the complexities of faith and reason, leaving readers with a renewed sense of wonder and a deeper understanding of the profound truths that lie at the heart of the Gospel.

Genres: Religious, Philosophy, Science, History

Themes: Faith and reason, Harmony between science and religion, Skepticism and belief
