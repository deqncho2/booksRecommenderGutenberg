Synopsis: In the small town of Camelia, strange occurrences begin to unfold when a mysterious newcomer arrives. As the townspeople become increasingly confounded by the newcomer's presence, secrets are unearthed and tensions rise. With each passing day, Camelia becomes a place of uncertainty and unease, leaving its residents questioning their own reality. As the truth slowly unravels, the town's fate hangs in the balance, and Camelia's inhabitants must confront their deepest fears and desires.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Small-town dynamics, Secrets
