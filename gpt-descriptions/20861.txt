Synopsis: In 'Friendship' by Hugh Black, a chance encounter between two strangers sparks an unexpected bond that transcends societal norms and challenges their own beliefs. As their friendship deepens, they navigate through the complexities of trust, loyalty, and the power of human connection. With each passing chapter, the reader is drawn into a captivating tale of friendship that explores the depths of the human heart and the transformative power of genuine companionship.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Contemporary

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Acceptance, Redemption
