Synopsis: In "The Hollow Needle; Further adventures of Arsène Lupin," the infamous gentleman thief, Arsène Lupin, finds himself entangled in a thrilling mystery involving a centuries-old secret and a hidden treasure. As Lupin races against time to solve the enigma, he must outwit his adversaries, navigate treacherous traps, and unravel the clues left behind by a cunning mastermind. With his quick wit, daring escapades, and unexpected alliances, Lupin takes readers on a heart-pounding journey filled with suspense, intrigue, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Crime

Themes: Secrets and hidden treasures, Cleverness and wit, Cat-and-mouse games
