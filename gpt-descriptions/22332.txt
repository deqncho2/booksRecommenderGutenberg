Synopsis: In 'Brain Twister', a brilliant scientist mysteriously disappears, leaving behind a trail of perplexing clues. As a government agent and a telepathic investigator team up to solve the enigma, they find themselves entangled in a web of mind-bending puzzles, dangerous adversaries, and a conspiracy that reaches the highest levels of power. With their lives on the line, they must unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Espionage, Telepathy, Government Conspiracy
