Synopsis:
In 'Caps and Capers: A Story of Boarding-School Life' by Gabrielle E. Jackson, readers are transported to the lively world of a boarding school where friendships are forged, secrets are uncovered, and adventures abound. Follow the spirited protagonist as she navigates the ups and downs of school life, facing challenges, and discovering the true meaning of loyalty and resilience. With a delightful blend of humor, mystery, and heartwarming moments, this captivating tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Young Adult, School Life

Themes: Friendship, Coming-of-age, Resilience, Adventure
