Synopsis: In 'The Bombardment of Reims' by Ferree Barr, a small French town becomes the unexpected battleground during World War I. As the citizens of Reims struggle to survive amidst the chaos and destruction, a young nurse finds herself caught in the crossfire, torn between duty and love. With the relentless bombardment threatening to shatter their lives, the characters must navigate the horrors of war while clinging to hope and resilience. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Romance

Themes: Survival, Sacrifice, Love and Loss, Resilience
