Synopsis: In "The Spirit of American Government: A Study Of The Constitution: Its Origin, Influence And Relation To Democracy," J. Allen Smith delves into the fascinating history and profound impact of the United States Constitution. Through meticulous research and insightful analysis, Smith explores the origins of the Constitution, its profound influence on American democracy, and its ongoing relevance in shaping the nation's governance. From the Founding Fathers' intentions to the challenges faced by modern-day democracy, this book offers a thought-provoking exploration of the Constitution's spirit and its enduring significance.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Political Science

Themes: Constitution, Democracy, Governance, American History, Political Philosophy
