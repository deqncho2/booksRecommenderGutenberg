Synopsis: 
'Within the Temple of Isis' by Belle M. Wagner is a captivating tale that follows the journey of a young archaeologist, Amelia, who stumbles upon an ancient Egyptian artifact that holds a mysterious power. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the artifact, Amelia finds herself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, facing unexpected challenges and uncovering long-lost secrets that could change the course of history. With each step closer to the truth, Amelia must confront her own fears and make choices that will determine the fate of not only herself but also the world around her.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Archaeology, Power and destiny
