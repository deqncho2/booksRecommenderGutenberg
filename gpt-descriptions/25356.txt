Synopsis: In 'Aunt Madge's Story' by May, Sophie, a young woman named Emily discovers a hidden diary belonging to her mysterious Aunt Madge. As she delves into the pages, she uncovers a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and family secrets that spans generations. With each revelation, Emily becomes more entangled in the past, questioning her own identity and the choices she must make in the present. As the story unfolds, the lines between past and present blur, leaving Emily to navigate a complex web of emotions and uncover the truth that has been buried for years.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and betrayal
