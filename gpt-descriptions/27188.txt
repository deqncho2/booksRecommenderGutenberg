Synopsis:
In 'The Wonder' by J. D. Beresford, a mysterious phenomenon grips the world, causing people to question their reality and the nature of existence itself. As the protagonist delves deeper into this enigma, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden truths that challenge everything they thought they knew. With each revelation, the line between what is real and what is imagined becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a mind-bending climax that will leave readers questioning their own perceptions.

Genres:
Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes:
Reality vs. Illusion, Identity, Perception
