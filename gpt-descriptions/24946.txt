Synopsis: 
'From a Cornish Window: A New Edition' by Quiller-Couch, Arthur takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of Cornwall. Through the eyes of the narrator, we are transported to the rugged cliffs, charming villages, and enchanting seascapes that make up this stunning region. As we peer through the window, we witness the lives and stories of the people who call Cornwall home, each with their own secrets, dreams, and desires. From tales of love and loss to mysteries waiting to be unraveled, this collection of stories offers a glimpse into the rich tapestry of life in Cornwall.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Regional Literature

Themes: Love, Loss, Community, Nature, Identity
