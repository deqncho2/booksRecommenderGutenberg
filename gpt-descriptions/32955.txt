Synopsis: In 'The Children of Westminster Abbey: Studies in English History', Rose Georgina Kingsley takes readers on a captivating journey through the corridors of Westminster Abbey, uncovering the fascinating stories of the children who lived within its walls. From royal princes and princesses to the children of abbey staff, each individual's life is brought to life with meticulous research and vivid storytelling. As we delve into their experiences, we witness the joys, sorrows, and secrets that shaped their lives, offering a unique perspective on English history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Social history

Themes: Childhood, Royalty, Social hierarchy, Family dynamics, Historical events
