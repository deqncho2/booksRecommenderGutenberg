Synopsis: 
In 'Ben Blair: The Story of a Plainsman' by Lillibridge, Will, follow the gripping tale of Ben Blair, a young man who finds himself torn between his love for the untamed wilderness and his desire for a settled life. As he navigates the challenges of the rugged plains, Ben must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape his future. With vivid descriptions of the American West and a cast of unforgettable characters, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Wilderness, Identity, Choices, Love and Sacrifice
