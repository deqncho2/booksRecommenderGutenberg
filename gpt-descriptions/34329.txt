Synopsis: In 'I Run with the Fox' by Mona Gould, a young woman named Sarah finds herself caught in a web of secrets and danger when she stumbles upon a mysterious fox in the woods near her home. As she delves deeper into the fox's world, Sarah uncovers a hidden society of shape-shifters and must navigate a treacherous path to protect herself and those she loves. With thrilling twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Survival
