Synopsis: In 'Making Over Martha' by Julie M. Lippmann, Martha, a shy and introverted woman, finds herself at a crossroads in her life. Feeling stuck in a monotonous routine, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and transformation. With the help of a quirky group of friends, Martha learns to step out of her comfort zone and embrace her true self. As she navigates through unexpected challenges and surprising adventures, Martha's life takes an exciting turn, leading her to discover the power of self-acceptance and the joy of living life to the fullest.

Genres: Contemporary Fiction, Women's Fiction, Romantic Comedy

Themes: Self-discovery, Transformation, Friendship, Embracing Change
