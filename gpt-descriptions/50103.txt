Synopsis: In 'The Dwindling Years' by Del Rey, Lester, a mysterious phenomenon begins to affect the aging population, causing them to rapidly lose their memories and abilities. As the world grapples with this unprecedented crisis, a young scientist named Emma embarks on a quest to uncover the truth behind the phenomenon and find a way to save her loved ones. Along the way, she encounters unexpected allies, dangerous adversaries, and uncovers dark secrets that challenge everything she thought she knew about the nature of time and memory. This gripping tale explores the fragility of the human mind and the lengths one will go to protect their memories and the ones they hold dear.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Aging, Memory, Identity, Sacrifice
