Synopsis: In "Hell on Ice: The Saga of the 'Jeannette'," Edward Ellsberg takes readers on a thrilling and harrowing journey to the Arctic. The story follows the ill-fated expedition of the USS Jeannette, a ship determined to reach the North Pole. As the crew battles treacherous ice, extreme weather conditions, and the constant threat of being trapped, the narrative unfolds with gripping intensity. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Ellsberg brings to life the incredible struggles and resilience of the crew, making this a captivating tale of survival against all odds.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Non-fiction, Survival

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Man vs Nature
