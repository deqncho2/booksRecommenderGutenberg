Synopsis: 

'Tales from Westminster Abbey Told to Children' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and enchanting legends surrounding one of the most iconic landmarks in England. Through a series of engaging stories, the authors bring to life the fascinating characters, events, and mysteries that have unfolded within the walls of Westminster Abbey. From kings and queens to poets and warriors, each tale offers a glimpse into the extraordinary lives and extraordinary moments that have shaped the Abbey's legacy. With a blend of history, folklore, and imagination, this book invites readers of all ages to discover the hidden treasures and untold stories of Westminster Abbey.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Children's Literature, Folklore

Themes: History, Legends, Heritage, Identity
