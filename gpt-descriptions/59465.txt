Synopsis: 

'Pugilistica: The History of British Boxing, Volume 1' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of British boxing. This meticulously researched volume delves into the lives of the most celebrated pugilists, providing full reports of their battles as chronicled in contemporary newspapers. With the inclusion of authentic portraits, personal anecdotes, and sketches of the principal patrons of the prize ring, this book offers a comprehensive history of the sport from its early days in the 18th century to the thrilling championship battle between King and Heenan in 1863.

Genres: Sports, History, Biography

Themes: Boxing, Courage, Determination, Social History
