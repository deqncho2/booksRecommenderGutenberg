Synopsis: 

'From Newton to Einstein: Changing Conceptions of the Universe' takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of scientific thought, exploring the groundbreaking discoveries that revolutionized our understanding of the cosmos. Benjamin Harrow delves into the lives and work of iconic figures such as Isaac Newton and Albert Einstein, unraveling the mysteries of gravity, relativity, and the nature of space and time. With vivid storytelling and accessible explanations, Harrow paints a vivid picture of the scientific revolution that reshaped our perception of the universe, leaving readers in awe of the brilliance and ingenuity of these visionary thinkers.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Scientific revolution, Evolution of ideas, Paradigm shifts
