Synopsis: In 'The Borough Treasurer' by J.S. Fletcher, a seemingly ordinary man named John Minute is unexpectedly thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when he inherits a vast fortune. As he delves into his newfound wealth, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and danger. With the help of a resourceful journalist, Minute must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and unexpected twists to uncover the truth behind his inheritance and protect his own life. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the enigma that lies within the Borough Treasurer's fortune.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Inheritance, Secrets, Deception, Danger
