Synopsis: 
In the small town of Bidwell, a group of young men find themselves caught in the whirlwind of change and uncertainty during the early 20th century. As they navigate the challenges of love, friendship, and societal expectations, their lives become intertwined in unexpected ways. From the bustling streets of Chicago to the quiet countryside, their individual journeys converge, revealing the complexities of human nature and the power of resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Identity, Masculinity, Social change, Individualism
