Synopsis: In 'The Spirit of Rome' by Lee, Vernon, a young archaeologist embarks on a thrilling journey through ancient Rome, uncovering hidden secrets and encountering mysterious figures along the way. As she delves deeper into the city's history, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger that could change her life forever. With each discovery, the line between past and present blurs, and she must navigate the treacherous paths of love, betrayal, and the enduring power of the Roman spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: History and its impact on the present, the allure of ancient civilizations, the resilience of the human spirit
