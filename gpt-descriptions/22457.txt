Synopsis: 
'The Verbalist: A Manual Devoted to Brief Discussions of the Right and the Wrong Use of Words and to Some Other Matters of Interest to Those Who Would Speak and Write with Propriety' is a comprehensive guide that explores the intricacies of language usage and provides valuable insights into the correct and incorrect use of words. Written by Alfred Ayres, this manual delves into various topics related to effective communication, offering practical advice and examples to help readers enhance their speaking and writing skills. From grammar and vocabulary to pronunciation and idiomatic expressions, Ayres covers a wide range of linguistic aspects, making this book an indispensable resource for anyone seeking to communicate with precision and propriety.

Genres: Non-fiction, Language and Linguistics, Reference

Themes: Language usage, Communication skills, Grammar and vocabulary
