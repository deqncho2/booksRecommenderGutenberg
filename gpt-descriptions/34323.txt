Synopsis: In 'The Samurai Strategy' by Thomas Hoover, a brilliant American businessman finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of corporate warfare in Japan. As he navigates the treacherous world of Japanese business and politics, he must rely on his wit, cunning, and knowledge of the ancient samurai code to survive. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Corporate espionage, Cultural clash, Survival
