Synopsis: 
In 'A Phantom Lover' by Lee, Vernon, a young woman named Janet is haunted by a mysterious and seductive presence that seems to be both real and otherworldly. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she uncovers a dark secret that threatens to consume her. With its eerie atmosphere and spine-chilling suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, mystery

Themes: Love and obsession, the blurred line between reality and the supernatural, the power of secrets
