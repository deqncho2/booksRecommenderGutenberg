Synopsis: 
Randy's Summer: A Story for Girls by Amy Brooks is a captivating coming-of-age tale that follows the adventures of Randy, a spirited young girl, as she navigates the ups and downs of a transformative summer. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque seaside town, Randy finds herself caught between new friendships, unexpected challenges, and the discovery of her own inner strength. As she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, Randy learns valuable lessons about love, loyalty, and the power of embracing one's true self. 

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Friendship, Resilience
