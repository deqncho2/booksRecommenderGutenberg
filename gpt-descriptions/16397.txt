Synopsis: In 'Larry Dexter's Great Search; Or, The Hunt for the Missing Millionaire', young Larry Dexter finds himself embarking on a thrilling adventure as he sets out to solve the mysterious disappearance of a wealthy businessman. With his quick wit and determination, Larry uncovers a web of secrets and unexpected twists that lead him on a chase across the city, encountering danger and intrigue at every turn. As he delves deeper into the case, Larry discovers that the missing millionaire's fate may be more connected to his own life than he ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Determination, Friendship, Secrets
