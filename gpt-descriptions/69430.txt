Synopsis: In the hauntingly beautiful city of Charleston, a grotesque legend unfolds as Madame Margot, a mysterious and enigmatic figure, weaves her web of secrets and deceit. Set against the backdrop of old Charleston, this gripping tale takes readers on a journey through the dark underbelly of the city, where nothing is as it seems. As the truth slowly unravels, the reader is left questioning the boundaries of love, loyalty, and the lengths one will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Southern Gothic

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity, Power
