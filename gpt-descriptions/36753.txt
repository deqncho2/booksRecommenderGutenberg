Synopsis: In "The Cavaliers of Virginia, vol. 2 of 2: or, The Recluse of Jamestown; An historical romance of the Old Dominion," William Alexander Caruthers takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous history of Virginia. Set in the 17th century, this historical romance follows the life of a recluse living in Jamestown, whose mysterious past and hidden secrets intertwine with the struggles and triumphs of the Cavaliers. As the story unfolds, the reader is immersed in a world of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the quest for redemption. Will the recluse's past catch up with him, or will he find solace and forgiveness in the arms of love?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Love and Sacrifice
