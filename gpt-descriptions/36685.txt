Synopsis: 
'National Rhymes of the Nursery' is a captivating collection of traditional nursery rhymes that takes readers on a delightful journey through the whimsical world of childhood. From familiar favorites to lesser-known gems, this anthology brings together a wide range of rhymes that are sure to enchant both young and old alike. With its charming illustrations and rhythmic verses, this book is a treasure trove of timeless tales that will transport readers to a world of imagination and wonder.

Genres: Children's Literature, Poetry, Anthology

Themes: Childhood, Imagination, Tradition
