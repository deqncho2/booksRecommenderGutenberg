Synopsis: In "At the Sign of the Golden Fleece," readers are transported to the vibrant world of Reformation Days, where a young girl named Ursula finds herself caught in the midst of religious and political turmoil. As she navigates the dangerous streets of Antwerp, Ursula becomes entangled in a web of secrets and conspiracies that could change the course of history. With the fate of her family and the future of her city hanging in the balance, Ursula must summon the courage to make difficult choices and challenge the established order. This gripping historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through a time of upheaval and transformation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Religious conflict, Courage, Loyalty, Power dynamics
