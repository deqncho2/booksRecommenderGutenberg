Synopsis: In 'The Vanishing Point' by Dawson, Coningsby, a renowned artist mysteriously disappears, leaving behind a trail of cryptic clues that lead his devoted wife on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth. As she delves deeper into her husband's enigmatic past, she discovers a world of secrets, deception, and hidden identities, where nothing is as it seems. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where her own life hangs in the balance. Will she be able to unravel the mystery and find her husband, or will she become lost in the vanishing point?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal, Obsession
