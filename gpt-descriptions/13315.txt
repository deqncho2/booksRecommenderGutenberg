Synopsis: In the days of Ina of Wessex, a young prince named Cadoc embarks on a journey that will test his courage, loyalty, and love. Set in the mystical land of Glastonbury, Cadoc finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden romance, and ancient secrets. As he navigates the treacherous path to his destiny, Cadoc must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape the future of his kingdom. With vivid descriptions of the West and its enchanting landscapes, "A Prince of Cornwall" is a captivating tale of adventure, passion, and the pursuit of one's true identity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Love, Political Intrigue
