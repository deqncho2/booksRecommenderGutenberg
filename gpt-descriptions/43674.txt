Synopsis: In 'The Cardinal Moth' by Fred M. White, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a priceless jewel, known as the Cardinal Moth, is stolen from a wealthy socialite's mansion. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate a dangerous game of cat and mouse to catch the cunning thief and recover the stolen treasure. Will he succeed before the Cardinal Moth disappears forever?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Intrigue, Betrayal, Pursuit of Justice
