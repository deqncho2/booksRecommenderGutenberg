Synopsis: In the heart of a post-apocalyptic world, a group of young warriors must navigate the treacherous streets of New York City to survive. Faced with rival gangs, corrupt police, and their own inner demons, they embark on a dangerous journey that will test their loyalty, courage, and resilience. As they fight for their lives, they discover the true meaning of brotherhood and the power of unity in the face of adversity.

Genres: Action, Thriller, Dystopian

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Brotherhood, Identity
