Synopsis:
In 'Frank's Ranche; Or, My Holiday in the Rockies: Being a Contribution to the Inquiry into What We Are to Do with Our Boys' by E. Marston, readers are taken on an exhilarating adventure to the majestic Rockies. The story follows Frank, a young boy who embarks on a holiday with his family, seeking solace and adventure in the untamed wilderness. As Frank explores the rugged landscapes, encounters with wildlife, and forms unexpected friendships, he discovers the transformative power of nature and the importance of self-discovery. This captivating tale offers a unique perspective on the challenges and opportunities that young boys face, ultimately posing the question of what society should do to nurture and guide them.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Nature
Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Nature's influence, Nurturing young boys
