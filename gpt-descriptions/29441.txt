Synopsis: 
'The Project Gutenberg Works of Plato: An Index' is a comprehensive collection of the works of the renowned Greek philosopher Plato. This index serves as a gateway to explore Plato's profound ideas and philosophical inquiries, covering a wide range of topics such as ethics, politics, metaphysics, and the nature of knowledge. Delve into the timeless wisdom of Plato's dialogues and immerse yourself in the intellectual journey of one of history's greatest thinkers.

Genres: Philosophy, Classics

Themes: Ethics, Politics, Metaphysics, Knowledge
