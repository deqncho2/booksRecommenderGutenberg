Synopsis: In 'The Myth in Marriage' by Alice Hubbard, a seemingly perfect couple's marriage is put to the test when a mysterious secret from the past resurfaces, threatening to unravel their lives. As they navigate through a web of lies, betrayal, and unexpected revelations, they must confront the myth of marital bliss and question the true meaning of love and commitment. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the complex dynamics of this captivating tale.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Marriage, Secrets, Betrayal, Identity, Redemption
