Synopsis: In 'In Search of Treasure' by Alger, Horatio, Jr., a young and ambitious protagonist embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him to uncharted territories in pursuit of a hidden treasure. As he faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected allies and adversaries, the reader is drawn into a captivating tale of danger, mystery, and the relentless pursuit of wealth. Will our protagonist's determination and resourcefulness be enough to uncover the treasure, or will he be outwitted by those who seek the same riches?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Greed, Friendship
