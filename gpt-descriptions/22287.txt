Synopsis: In "Smiles: A Rose of the Cumberlands," the reader is transported to the rugged and enchanting landscape of the Appalachian Mountains. The story revolves around the life of Smiles, a young woman with a captivating spirit and an indomitable will. As she navigates the challenges and triumphs of her existence, Smiles becomes a symbol of resilience and hope in a world filled with hardship and adversity. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel explores the power of love, the pursuit of dreams, and the enduring strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Appalachian Literature

Themes: Resilience, Love, Dreams, Adversity, Strength
