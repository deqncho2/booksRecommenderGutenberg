Synopsis: 
'Plays: Complete Edition, Including the Posthumous Plays' by Leo Tolstoy is a captivating collection of dramatic works that showcases the author's versatility and mastery of storytelling. From thought-provoking tragedies to lighthearted comedies, this anthology presents a diverse range of plays that delve into the complexities of human nature, relationships, and societal norms. Each play offers a unique and compelling narrative, filled with richly developed characters and thought-provoking dialogue. Tolstoy's exploration of love, morality, and the pursuit of truth will keep readers engrossed from beginning to end.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Comedy

Themes: Love, Morality, Truth, Human Nature, Relationships, Societal Norms
