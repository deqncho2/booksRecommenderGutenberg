Synopsis: In this captivating installment of the Chronicles of England, Scotland and Ireland, Holinshed delves into the intriguing life of Richard the Third, the third son of Richard Duke of Yorke and uncle to Edward the Fifth. As the plot unfolds, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through Richard's rise to power, his cunning strategies, and the web of political intrigue that surrounds him. With unexpected twists and turns, this book keeps readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the fate of Richard the Third.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Drama

Themes: Power and ambition, Loyalty and betrayal, Manipulation and deceit
