Synopsis: 

In 'How to Make and Set Traps: Including Hints on How to Trap Moles, Weasels, Otter, Rats, Squirrels and Birds; Also How to Cure Skins', Keene, J. Harrington shares a comprehensive guide on the art of trapping various animals. From moles to birds, this book provides valuable insights and techniques to successfully catch and trap these creatures. Additionally, it offers tips on how to cure skins, making it a valuable resource for those interested in preserving animal hides. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Outdoor and Survival, Hunting

Themes: Animal trapping, Wildlife management, Traditional skills
