Synopsis: 
In 'Literature for Children' by Lowe, Orton, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of children's literature. Delve into the pages of beloved classics and discover the magic that lies within these timeless tales. From whimsical adventures to heartwarming stories of friendship, this book explores the power of storytelling and its profound impact on young readers. Uncover the secrets behind the creation of these cherished stories and gain a deeper understanding of the art of writing for children. 

Genres: 
Literary Criticism, Children's Literature, Education

Themes: 
The power of storytelling, Imagination and creativity, The impact of literature on young readers
