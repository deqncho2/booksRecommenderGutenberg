Synopsis: In 'Bumper the White Rabbit and His Friends' by George Ethelbert Walsh, follow the enchanting adventures of Bumper, a curious and mischievous white rabbit, as he embarks on a journey filled with unexpected friendships, thrilling escapades, and heartwarming moments. From encountering a wise old owl to outsmarting a sly fox, Bumper's escapades will captivate readers of all ages, reminding us of the joy and wonder that can be found in the simplest of friendships and the beauty of the natural world.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Nature
