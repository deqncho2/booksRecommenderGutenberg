Synopsis: 
In the small town of Simsbury, Merton Gill dreams of becoming a famous movie star. With his naive charm and unwavering determination, he sets off to Hollywood, hoping to make it big in the glamorous world of cinema. However, Merton's journey takes an unexpected turn when he finds himself caught up in a series of hilarious misadventures, encountering eccentric characters and facing the harsh realities of the movie industry. As he navigates through the ups and downs of his newfound fame, Merton must learn valuable lessons about the true nature of success and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Comedy, Drama

Themes: Pursuit of fame and success, Identity and self-discovery
