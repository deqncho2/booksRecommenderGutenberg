Synopsis: 
In "Over the Santa Fé Trail, 1857" by William Barclay Napton, a group of diverse individuals embarks on a treacherous journey along the Santa Fé Trail in search of adventure, fortune, and a chance at a new life. As they navigate through the rugged landscapes, facing perilous encounters and unexpected alliances, their true characters are tested, and they must confront their deepest fears and desires. With danger lurking at every turn, the travelers must rely on their wits, resilience, and the bonds they form along the way to survive the unforgiving trail.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Exploration and Discovery, Survival, Identity and Transformation
