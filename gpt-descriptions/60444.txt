Synopsis: 
'From Billabong to London' follows the captivating journey of Norah Linton, a spirited young woman from the Australian outback, as she embarks on a thrilling adventure to the bustling city of London. Leaving behind her beloved billabong and the familiar landscapes of her homeland, Norah finds herself navigating the complexities of city life, encountering unexpected friendships, and facing the challenges of a rapidly changing world. As she immerses herself in the vibrant culture and society of London, Norah's journey becomes a transformative exploration of self-discovery, love, and the pursuit of her dreams.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Cultural exploration, Independence, Love and relationships
