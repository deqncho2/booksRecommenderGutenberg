Synopsis: In "A Wilful Ward" by Ruth Lamb, a headstrong young woman finds herself thrust into the world of high society when she becomes the ward of a wealthy and mysterious guardian. As she navigates the glittering ballrooms and treacherous social circles, she must learn to balance her own desires with the expectations of those around her. With secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Independence, Society's Expectations, Identity
