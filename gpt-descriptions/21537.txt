Synopsis: In 'Expansion and Conflict' by Dodd, William Edward, a young historian stumbles upon a hidden diary that reveals a shocking secret about a forgotten war. As he delves deeper into the past, he uncovers a web of intrigue, betrayal, and power struggles that threaten to rewrite history. With each revelation, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game where the line between truth and fiction blurs. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he must confront his own demons and make choices that will shape the future. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Power struggles, Personal redemption
