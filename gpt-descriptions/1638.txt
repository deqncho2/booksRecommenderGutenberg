Synopsis: In "The New Revelation," Arthur Conan Doyle takes readers on a thought-provoking journey that challenges conventional beliefs and explores the mysteries of the afterlife. Through a series of personal experiences and encounters, the author delves into the realms of spiritualism, mediumship, and the existence of a higher power. As he grapples with these profound questions, Doyle invites readers to question their own beliefs and consider the possibility of a new revelation.

Genres: Spiritualism, Non-fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Afterlife, Spiritualism, Religion, Existence, Belief
