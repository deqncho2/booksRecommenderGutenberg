Synopsis: 
In 'Aunt Jimmy's Will' by Mabel Osgood Wright, a young woman named Mary finds herself unexpectedly inheriting a vast fortune from her eccentric Aunt Jimmy. However, there is a catch: in order to claim her inheritance, Mary must embark on a mysterious journey that takes her to unexpected places and introduces her to a cast of intriguing characters. As Mary delves deeper into her aunt's past, she uncovers long-held family secrets and discovers the true meaning of love, loyalty, and self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Inheritance, Family Secrets, Self-discovery
