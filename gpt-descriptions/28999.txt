Synopsis:
In "Daisy's Aunt" by E. F. Benson, a young woman named Daisy finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when her eccentric Aunt arrives unexpectedly. As Daisy tries to uncover the truth behind her Aunt's secretive behavior, she becomes entangled in a series of unexpected events that will test her courage and determination. With twists and turns at every corner, Daisy's journey takes her on a thrilling adventure that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres:
Mystery, Suspense, Drama

Themes:
Family secrets, Identity, Deception
