Synopsis: In 'Lightnin', a small-town ne'er-do-well named Lightnin' Bill Jones finds himself in a series of hilarious misadventures that lead him to unexpected places and encounters. From his encounters with a charming young woman to his involvement in a high-stakes poker game, Lightnin' navigates through a whirlwind of comical situations that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Redemption, Love, Small-town life
