Synopsis: In 'Uncle Sam's Right Arm: A Patriotic Exercise' by Effa E. Preston, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught up in a whirlwind of patriotism and adventure during World War I. When she is unexpectedly recruited to join the war effort, Mary must navigate the challenges of being a woman in a male-dominated society while discovering her own strength and determination. As she embarks on a journey filled with danger, love, and sacrifice, Mary's loyalty to her country and her own personal growth are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Patriotism, Gender Roles, Personal Growth
