Synopsis: In 'Xerxes: Makers of History' by Abbott, Jacob, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of history's most intriguing figures, Xerxes, the powerful Persian king. From his rise to the throne to his ambitious military campaigns, this gripping narrative delves into the complexities of Xerxes' character, his struggles, and the impact he had on the ancient world. As the story unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the epic battles, political intrigues, and personal dilemmas that shaped Xerxes' reign, making this a truly captivating historical account.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Power and Ambition, Leadership, War and Conquest
