Synopsis: In "A Funeral Sermon Preach'd on the Decease of the Right Honourable the Lady Elizabeth Cutts," Reverend William Wigan delivers a moving sermon on the occasion of Lady Elizabeth Cutts' passing. With heartfelt words and profound reflections, Wigan pays tribute to her life, virtues, and the impact she had on those around her. Through his sermon, he offers solace and guidance to the grieving congregation, reminding them of the importance of faith and the eternal nature of the soul.

Genres: Sermon, Eulogy, Religious Literature

Themes: Death and mortality, Faith and spirituality, Legacy and remembrance
