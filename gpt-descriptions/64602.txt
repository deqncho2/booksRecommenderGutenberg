Synopsis: In 'Runaway' by Alfred Coppel, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of danger and intrigue when she stumbles upon a secret that could change the course of history. As she races against time to uncover the truth, she must navigate a treacherous world of espionage, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the stakes grow higher, and she must decide who she can trust before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Espionage

Themes: Secrets and deception, Trust and betrayal
