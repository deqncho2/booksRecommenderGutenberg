Synopsis: In 'The Boy Inventors' Flying Ship' by Bonner, Richard, a group of young inventors embark on a thrilling adventure when they create a remarkable flying ship. As they take to the skies, they encounter unexpected challenges and face dangerous adversaries, testing their courage and ingenuity. With their lives hanging in the balance, the boy inventors must rely on their friendship and resourcefulness to navigate through a series of exhilarating escapades.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Ingenuity, Courage
