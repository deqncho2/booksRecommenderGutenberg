Synopsis: In "Dorothy Harcourt's Secret," the long-awaited sequel to "A Deed Without a Name," we are once again immersed in the captivating world of Dorothy Harcourt. Set in the late 19th century, this thrilling tale follows Dorothy as she unravels a mysterious secret that threatens to upend her life and those around her. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Dorothy finds herself entangled in a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth alongside Dorothy.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery, Power and Manipulation
