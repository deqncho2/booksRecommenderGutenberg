Synopsis: 

In "The Path of Empire: A Chronicle of the United States as a World Power," Carl Russell Fish takes readers on a captivating journey through the rise of the United States as a global force. From the early days of colonization to the country's emergence as a world power, Fish delves into the pivotal moments, key figures, and significant events that shaped America's path to becoming a dominant force on the international stage. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, the author explores the nation's expansion, conflicts, and diplomatic endeavors, shedding light on the complex dynamics that propelled the United States into its role as a global power.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: American imperialism, Manifest Destiny, diplomacy, international relations, nation-building
