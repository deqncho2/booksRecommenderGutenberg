Synopsis: In 'The Eel' by Miriam Allen De Ford, a mysterious creature emerges from the depths of the ocean, captivating the attention of a small coastal town. As the townspeople become increasingly obsessed with the enigmatic eel, they find themselves drawn into a web of secrets, desires, and hidden agendas. With each passing day, the eel's presence begins to unravel the lives of the townsfolk, leading to unexpected consequences and a shocking revelation that will change everything.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Obsession, Secrets, Transformation, Human Nature
