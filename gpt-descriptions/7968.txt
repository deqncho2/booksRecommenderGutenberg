Synopsis: In 'Lying Prophets: A Novel' by Eden Phillpotts, a small village is shaken by the arrival of a mysterious prophet who claims to possess the power of divination. As his predictions start coming true, the villagers become enthralled by his words, but not everyone is convinced of his authenticity. Amidst the growing tension and skepticism, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of the prophet's intentions is revealed. This gripping tale explores the power of belief, the consequences of deception, and the fragile line between truth and lies.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Deception, Belief, Truth, Manipulation
