Synopsis: 
In 'Kidnapped' by Robert Louis Stevenson, a young and unsuspecting protagonist finds himself caught in a dangerous web of political intrigue and treachery. After being kidnapped and forced onto a ship, he embarks on a thrilling adventure across the rugged landscapes of Scotland, encountering both allies and enemies along the way. As he fights for his freedom and searches for justice, he must navigate through a world filled with secrets, betrayal, and the constant threat of danger. Will he be able to survive and reclaim his rightful place in society?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Justice, Survival
