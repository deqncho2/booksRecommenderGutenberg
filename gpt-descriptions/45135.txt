Synopsis: In "The Thick of the Fray at Zeebrugge, April 1918," Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a thrilling journey to the heart of a daring naval operation during World War I. Set against the backdrop of the treacherous waters of Zeebrugge, the story follows a group of courageous sailors as they embark on a perilous mission to disrupt German naval operations. With high-stakes action, intense battles, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Adventure

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Heroism
