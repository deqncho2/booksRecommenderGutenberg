Synopsis: In 'The Cab of the Sleeping Horse' by John Reed Scott, a mysterious cab driver takes passengers on a journey through the dark underbelly of a city plagued by corruption and secrets. As the passengers delve deeper into the night, they uncover a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the cab becomes a sanctuary and a battleground, where the fate of its occupants hangs in the balance.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Noir

Themes: Corruption, Secrets, Redemption
