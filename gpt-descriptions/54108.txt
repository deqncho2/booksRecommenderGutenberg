Synopsis: In 'Squire Arden; volume 1 of 3' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), readers are transported to the enchanting world of 19th-century England, where the young and ambitious Squire Arden embarks on a journey of self-discovery and love. As he navigates the complexities of society, Arden finds himself torn between duty and desire, facing unexpected challenges and encountering captivating characters along the way. With secrets lurking in the shadows and a web of intrigue unfolding, this captivating tale promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning each page to uncover the mysteries that lie ahead.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Society, Intrigue
