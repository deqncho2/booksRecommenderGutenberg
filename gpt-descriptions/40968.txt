Synopsis: In 'Desire No More' by Algis Budrys, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Richard Avery is on the verge of a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. However, his invention attracts the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess it. As Avery's life is turned upside down, he must navigate a treacherous world of intrigue, betrayal, and moral dilemmas, all while trying to protect his invention and those he holds dear. This gripping tale explores the consequences of scientific advancement and the lengths people will go to in order to fulfill their desires.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific ethics, Power and corruption, Sacrifice
