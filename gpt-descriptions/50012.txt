Synopsis: In 'A Duel in the Dark: An Original Farce, in One Act', chaos ensues when two gentlemen find themselves in a pitch-black room, mistakenly believing they are about to engage in a duel. With hilarious misunderstandings, mistaken identities, and unexpected encounters, this farce takes the audience on a wild ride of laughter and confusion.

Genres: Farce, Comedy

Themes: Miscommunication, Mistaken identity, Absurdity
