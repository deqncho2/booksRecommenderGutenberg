Synopsis: 

'A Narrative of the Life and Adventures of Venture, a Native of Africa, but Resident above Sixty Years in the United States of America, Related by Himself' is a captivating memoir that follows the extraordinary life of Venture Smith, an African man who spent over six decades in the United States. From his harrowing capture in Africa to his experiences as a slave and eventual freedom, Venture's story is one of resilience, determination, and the pursuit of liberty. Through his own words, we witness the hardships he endured, the relationships he formed, and the remarkable journey he undertook to secure his freedom and establish a life for himself and his family.

Genres: Memoir, Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Slavery, Freedom, Resilience, Identity, Family
