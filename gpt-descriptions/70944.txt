Synopsis: In 'Knock three-one-two' by Fredric Brown, a seemingly ordinary man named Ed Hunter finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he receives a phone call from a stranger who claims to be him. As the bizarre phone calls continue, Ed becomes entangled in a mind-bending game of identity, secrets, and a race against time to uncover the truth. With each call, the stakes rise higher, leading Ed down a path of suspense and unexpected twists that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Paranoia
