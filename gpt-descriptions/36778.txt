Synopsis: 
In "Twenty-Five Years in the Rifle Brigade," Surtees, William takes readers on an exhilarating journey through his experiences as a soldier in the renowned Rifle Brigade. From the battlefields of the Napoleonic Wars to the treacherous landscapes of the Iberian Peninsula, Surtees vividly recounts the trials and triumphs of his military career. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for storytelling, he offers a captivating glimpse into the life of a soldier, filled with camaraderie, danger, and the indomitable spirit of the men who fought alongside him.

Genres: Memoir, Military History, Adventure

Themes: War, Courage, Brotherhood, Survival
