Synopsis: In "A Letter to Sir Richard Ford and the Other Police Magistrates," Holloway exposes the dark underbelly of gaming and the corrupt practices of common informers. Through a series of shocking revelations, he unveils the extent of the prevalence of gambling and the nefarious tactics employed by those seeking to profit from it. As he delves deeper into this hidden world, Holloway uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and manipulation that threatens to unravel the very fabric of society. With each page, the reader is drawn further into the gripping narrative, eagerly awaiting the next revelation that will expose the truth behind the infamous practices that have plagued the city. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Social Commentary, Historical

Themes: Corruption, Gambling, Justice, Morality
