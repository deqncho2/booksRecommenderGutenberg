Synopsis: In 'The Mis-Rule of Three' by Florence Warden, a seemingly ordinary family is thrown into chaos when a mysterious stranger arrives at their doorstep, claiming to be a long-lost relative. As the stranger's true intentions become increasingly sinister, the family must unravel a web of secrets and lies to protect themselves and their loved ones. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Deception
