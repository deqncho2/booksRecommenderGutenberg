Synopsis: In 'Quatre contes de Prosper Mérimée', the reader is taken on a captivating journey through four distinct tales by the renowned French author Prosper Mérimée. From the mysterious encounters in a Spanish monastery to the tragic love affair in Corsica, each story is filled with suspense, passion, and unexpected twists. Whether it is the haunting tale of a supernatural being or the gripping narrative of a forbidden romance, Mérimée's storytelling prowess keeps the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets hidden within each narrative.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Historical fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and passion, Supernatural elements, Forbidden desires
