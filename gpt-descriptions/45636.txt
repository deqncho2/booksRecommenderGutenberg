Synopsis: In 'Stolen Idols' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a renowned art collector finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when a valuable artifact goes missing. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a world of deception, secret societies, and unexpected alliances. With danger lurking at every turn, he must race against time to recover the stolen idol and expose the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Art theft, Deception, Intrigue
