Synopsis: In 'Pattern' by Robert H. Rohrer, a renowned mathematician stumbles upon a mysterious pattern that has the potential to unlock the secrets of the universe. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic code, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between reality and illusion blurs. With his life on the line, he must race against time to decipher the pattern before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Mathematics, Perception, Conspiracy
