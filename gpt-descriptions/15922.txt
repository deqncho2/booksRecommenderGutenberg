Synopsis: In 'A Loose End and Other Stories' by S. Elizabeth Hall, prepare to be captivated by a collection of gripping tales that will leave you questioning the boundaries of reality. From a mysterious disappearance in a small town to a haunting encounter with a supernatural entity, each story takes unexpected twists and turns, keeping you on the edge of your seat until the very last page. With a blend of suspense, mystery, and a touch of the supernatural, this collection will leave you craving for more.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Supernatural

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Secrets and Hidden Truths
