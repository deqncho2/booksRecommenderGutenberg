Synopsis: 
In the midst of political turmoil and conspiracy, Julius Caesar, a powerful Roman leader, finds himself at the center of a web of betrayal and ambition. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the fate of Rome hangs in the balance. Shakespeare's masterful tragedy explores the consequences of power, the fragility of trust, and the complexities of honor and duty.

Genres: Tragedy, Historical Drama

Themes: Power and ambition, Betrayal, Loyalty, Honor and duty
