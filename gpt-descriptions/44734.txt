Synopsis: 

In "Training for the Trenches: A Practical Handbook Based upon Personal Experience During the First Two Years of the War in France," author Leslie Vickers takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing realities of World War I. Drawing from his own experiences as a soldier on the front lines, Vickers provides a comprehensive guide to the training and preparation necessary for survival in the trenches. From the physical demands of combat to the psychological toll of war, this handbook offers invaluable insights and practical advice for soldiers facing the brutalities of battle. Through vivid anecdotes and firsthand accounts, Vickers paints a vivid picture of the challenges and sacrifices endured by those who fought in the Great War.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military Memoir

Themes: War, Survival, Camaraderie, Sacrifice
