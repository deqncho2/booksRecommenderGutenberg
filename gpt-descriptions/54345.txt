Synopsis: 

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the lively debates that shaped the early years of the United States. In this captivating volume, the Abridgment of the Debates of Congress takes you on a journey through the years 1789 to 1856, offering a unique glimpse into the minds of the nation's most influential leaders. From heated discussions on the Constitution and the Bill of Rights to passionate debates on slavery and westward expansion, this collection of congressional debates provides a front-row seat to the birth and growth of a nation. Delve into the words that shaped history and witness the clash of ideas that defined the early American experience.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Political discourse, Government records

Themes: Democracy, Constitutional interpretation, Slavery, Manifest Destiny, Nation-building
