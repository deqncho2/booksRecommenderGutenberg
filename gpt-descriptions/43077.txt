Synopsis: In 'A Clerk of Oxford, and His Adventures in the Barons' War', we follow the journey of a young clerk named Hubert, who finds himself caught in the midst of the tumultuous Barons' War in medieval England. As he navigates through treacherous political alliances, secret missions, and unexpected friendships, Hubert's loyalty and courage are put to the ultimate test. With the fate of the kingdom hanging in the balance, Hubert must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the future of England.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Political Intrigue, Friendship, Identity
