Synopsis: In 'Weymouth New Testament in Modern Speech, 1 Corinthians', Richard Francis Weymouth presents a fresh and contemporary translation of the biblical book of 1 Corinthians. This powerful and thought-provoking work delves into the challenges faced by the early Christian community in Corinth, exploring themes of unity, love, and spiritual gifts. Through his modern language rendition, Weymouth brings to life the timeless wisdom and teachings of the Apostle Paul, captivating readers with his engaging and accessible interpretation.

Genres: Religious literature, Biblical translation, Christian non-fiction

Themes: Unity, Love, Spiritual gifts, Christian community
