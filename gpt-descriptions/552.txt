Synopsis: In 'The People That Time Forgot' by Edgar Rice Burroughs, a group of explorers find themselves stranded on a mysterious island where prehistoric creatures and a forgotten civilization await them. As they navigate the treacherous landscape, they must confront their own fears and adapt to survive in this thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Evolution, Human Nature
