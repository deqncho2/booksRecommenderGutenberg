Synopsis: In "Fruits of Culture," Tolstoy takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of a group of aristocrats who gather for a lavish dinner party. As the evening unfolds, secrets are revealed, passions ignite, and the characters are forced to confront the contradictions between their public personas and their true selves. With his keen observations and sharp wit, Tolstoy delves into the complexities of human nature, exploring the masks we wear and the consequences of living in a society driven by appearances.

Genres: Drama, Satire, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Hypocrisy, Social Class, Authenticity, Identity
