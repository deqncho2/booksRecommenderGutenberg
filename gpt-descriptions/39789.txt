Synopsis: 
'Nooks & Corners of Old New York' takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden gems and forgotten corners of the bustling city. Charles Hemstreet, a masterful storyteller, unveils the secrets and mysteries that lie within the historic streets, buildings, and neighborhoods of old New York. From the cobblestone lanes of Greenwich Village to the grand mansions of Fifth Avenue, Hemstreet's vivid descriptions and engaging narratives bring the city's past to life. As readers delve into the pages of this enchanting book, they will discover the untold stories of New York's vibrant history, encountering eccentric characters, architectural wonders, and unexpected surprises along the way.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Travelogue, Mystery

Themes: History, Urban Exploration, Hidden Stories
