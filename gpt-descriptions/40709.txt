Synopsis: In "Facts and Figures Concerning the Hoosac Tunnel," John J. Piper takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and construction of one of America's most ambitious engineering projects. From the treacherous challenges faced by the workers to the groundbreaking innovations that made the tunnel possible, this book unveils the untold stories and secrets hidden within the depths of the Hoosac Tunnel. As readers delve into the pages, they will be enthralled by the suspense, mystery, and sheer audacity that surrounded this monumental feat of engineering.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Engineering

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Innovation, Human Ingenuity
