Synopsis: In 'Famous Days in the Century of Invention', Fickett and Stone take readers on a captivating journey through history, exploring the most remarkable moments of the 19th century. From the invention of the telephone to the first flight of an airplane, this book delves into the lives of the brilliant minds behind these groundbreaking innovations. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors bring to life the struggles, triumphs, and pivotal moments that shaped the world we live in today.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Science

Themes: Innovation, Progress, Human Ingenuity
