Synopsis: 
The Letters of the Duke of Wellington to Miss J., 1834-1851: Edited, with Extracts from the Diary of the Latter offers a captivating glimpse into the private correspondence between the renowned Duke of Wellington and a mysterious Miss J. Spanning over seventeen years, these letters reveal a deep and complex relationship that evolves through time. As the Duke navigates the political landscape and Miss J. grapples with her own personal challenges, their letters become a testament to the power of connection and the complexities of love. With each letter, the reader is drawn deeper into their world, eagerly anticipating the next exchange and uncovering the secrets that lie within.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Epistolary Novel, Romance

Themes: Love and Relationships, Politics and Power, Personal Growth
