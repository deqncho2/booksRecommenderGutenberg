Synopsis: In 'Salve Venetia, gleanings from Venetian history; vol. II', F. Marion Crawford takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and mysterious history of Venice. From the opulent palaces of the Doges to the hidden canals and dark secrets of the city, this volume delves into the lives of the Venetian people and the events that shaped their extraordinary city. With tales of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the struggle for power, Crawford weaves a tapestry of captivating stories that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Power and politics, Love and betrayal, Secrets and deception
