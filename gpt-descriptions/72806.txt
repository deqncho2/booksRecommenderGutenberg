Synopsis: 
In "Itinerarium curiosum (centuria II)", William Stukeley takes readers on a captivating journey through Great Britain, uncovering the hidden antiquities and remarkable curiosities found in nature and art. Alongside this fascinating exploration, Stukeley includes the Itinerary of Richard of Cirencester, a monk of Westminster, shedding light on the author and his work. This book promises to be a thrilling and enlightening adventure, revealing the wonders and secrets of Great Britain's past.

Genres: 
Travelogue, History, Archaeology

Themes: 
Antiquities, Curiosities, Exploration, Historical Preservation
