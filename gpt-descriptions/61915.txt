Synopsis: In 'Effie Ogilvie: the story of a young life; vol. 2' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), we continue to follow the captivating journey of Effie Ogilvie as she navigates the complexities of love, family, and society. Set in the Victorian era, Effie finds herself torn between duty and desire, facing unexpected challenges that test her resilience and determination. As secrets unravel and relationships evolve, Effie must confront the consequences of her choices and discover the true meaning of happiness. This second volume of the series promises to delve deeper into Effie's world, captivating readers with its richly drawn characters and compelling narrative.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Social Expectations, Self-Discovery
