Synopsis: In 'Escape From Pluto' by William Oberfield, a group of astronauts find themselves stranded on the distant and mysterious planet of Pluto. As they struggle to survive in this harsh and unforgiving environment, they uncover a shocking secret that could change the course of human history. With time running out, they must find a way to escape and return to Earth before it's too late. This thrilling sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they join the characters in their desperate fight for survival.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Discovery
