Synopsis: In 'Rx' by Alan Edward Nourse, a brilliant young doctor finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger when he stumbles upon a mysterious drug that could change the course of medical history. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of the pharmaceutical industry, he must navigate treacherous alliances and face life-threatening challenges to uncover the truth. With lives hanging in the balance, he must decide how far he is willing to go to expose the shocking secrets hidden within the world of medicine.

Genres: Medical thriller, Suspense, Mystery

Themes: Ethics in medicine, Corruption, Pursuit of truth
