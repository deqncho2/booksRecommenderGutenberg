Synopsis: In "A Defence of Modern Spiritualism," Alfred Russel Wallace delves into the fascinating world of spiritualism, exploring the evidence and experiences that challenge conventional beliefs. Through a series of thought-provoking investigations and personal encounters, Wallace presents a compelling argument for the existence of a spiritual realm beyond our physical reality. As he navigates through the realms of mediumship, telepathy, and psychic phenomena, the reader is taken on a captivating journey that challenges preconceived notions and invites a deeper exploration of the mysteries of the universe.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Occult

Themes: Spiritualism, Paranormal phenomena, Scientific inquiry
