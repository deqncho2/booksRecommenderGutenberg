Synopsis:
In "Autobiography of Miss Cornelia Knight, lady companion to the Princess Charlotte of Wales," Ellis Cornelia Knight takes us on a captivating journey through her life as a lady companion to the Princess Charlotte of Wales. Through extracts from her journals and anecdote books, Knight offers a unique and intimate perspective on the royal court and the tumultuous events that unfolded during her time with the princess. From the opulent halls of Buckingham Palace to the political intrigues of the era, Knight's memoirs provide a fascinating glimpse into the world of the British monarchy and the challenges faced by those in its inner circle.

Genres:
Biography, Memoir, History

Themes:
Royal life, Friendship, Political intrigue
