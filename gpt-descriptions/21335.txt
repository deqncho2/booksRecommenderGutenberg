Synopsis: In the small, idyllic village of Lymstock, a series of anonymous poison-pen letters begins to circulate, spreading rumors and sowing discord among the residents. As the letters become increasingly malicious and their targets suffer the consequences, a young woman named Jerry Burton and her brother move to Lymstock to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. However, they soon find themselves caught up in the web of suspicion and intrigue, determined to uncover the identity of the mysterious letter writer before more harm is done. With the help of the enigmatic Miss Marple, they delve into the secrets and hidden motives of the villagers, unearthing a shocking truth that threatens to tear the community apart.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Small-town secrets, Rumors and gossip, Identity and deception
