Synopsis: 
In 'The Perpetual Curate' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), we follow the life of Frank Wentworth, a young and passionate clergyman who finds himself facing numerous challenges in his new role as a curate. From navigating the complexities of parish life to dealing with personal trials and temptations, Frank's journey is one of self-discovery, love, and the pursuit of his calling. As he grapples with the expectations of society and his own desires, Frank must find a way to balance his duty to his congregation with his own happiness. With a cast of intriguing characters and a captivating storyline, 'The Perpetual Curate' is a compelling exploration of faith, love, and the complexities of human nature.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Victorian Literature

Themes: Religion, Duty, Love, Self-discovery
