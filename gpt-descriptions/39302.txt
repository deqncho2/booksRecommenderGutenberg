Synopsis:
In the second volume of his captivating memoirs, Jacques Casanova de Seingalt takes us on a thrilling journey from Venice to the vibrant city of Paris, only to find himself entangled in a web of scandal and intrigue. As he navigates the treacherous world of the French aristocracy, Casanova's charm and wit are put to the ultimate test. From passionate love affairs to daring escapades, he must rely on his cunning and resourcefulness to survive the perils that await him. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Casanova's memoirs offer a fascinating glimpse into the decadent and dangerous world of 18th-century Europe.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Love and seduction, social status and class, survival and resilience
