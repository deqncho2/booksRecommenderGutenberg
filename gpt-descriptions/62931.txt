Synopsis:
In 'The Bubonic Plague' by Mitra, A., active 19th century, a mysterious and deadly disease sweeps through a medieval town, leaving its inhabitants in a state of panic and despair. As the plague spreads, the lives of the townspeople intertwine in unexpected ways, revealing the depths of human resilience and the fragility of society. Amidst the chaos and devastation, a group of unlikely heroes emerges, determined to find a cure and restore hope to their community. With each passing day, the stakes grow higher, and the race against time becomes a matter of life and death.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes:
Survival, Sacrifice, Community, Fear, Resilience
