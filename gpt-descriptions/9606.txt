Synopsis: 
In 'A Little Book of Western Verse', Eugene Field takes readers on a delightful journey through the enchanting landscapes of the American West. Through his whimsical and lyrical poetry, Field captures the essence of the wild frontier, painting vivid pictures of cowboys, pioneers, and the rugged beauty of the untamed wilderness. From humorous tales of mischievous prairie dogs to heartfelt odes to the vast open plains, this collection is a celebration of the spirit and charm of the Old West. 

Genres: 
Poetry, Western literature, Humor

Themes: 
Nature, Adventure, Nostalgia
