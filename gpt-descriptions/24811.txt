Synopsis: In 'Viking Tales' by Jennie Hall, embark on a thrilling journey through the captivating world of the Vikings. Follow the adventures of brave warriors, cunning explorers, and fierce shieldmaidens as they navigate treacherous seas, clash with rival clans, and uncover ancient secrets. From epic battles to mythical creatures, this collection of tales will transport you to a time of legendary heroes and legendary deeds.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mythology

Themes: Courage, Honor, Exploration, Myth and Legend
