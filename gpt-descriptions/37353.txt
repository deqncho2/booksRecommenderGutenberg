Synopsis: 

In 'Stories of Great Musicians' by Horne, Olive Brown and Scobey, Kathrine Lois, embark on a captivating journey through the lives of some of the most influential musicians in history. From the classical compositions of Bach and Mozart to the soulful melodies of Beethoven and Chopin, this book delves into the personal struggles, triumphs, and creative processes that shaped these musical geniuses. Discover the untold stories behind their iconic works and the profound impact they had on the world of music. 

Genres: Biography, Music, Non-fiction

Themes: Creativity, Perseverance, Legacy
