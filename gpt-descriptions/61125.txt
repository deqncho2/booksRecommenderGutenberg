Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Nineteenth, or the First Yorkshire North Riding Regiment of Foot', Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this remarkable regiment. From its inception in 1688 to its exploits up until 1848, Cannon delves into the regiment's triumphs, challenges, and the courageous individuals who shaped its history. This meticulously researched account offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of military history, shedding light on the regiment's pivotal role in various conflicts and campaigns. 

Genres: Military history, Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Military valor, Historical significance, Regimental pride
