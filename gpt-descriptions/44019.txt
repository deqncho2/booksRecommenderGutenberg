Synopsis: 

'A Course of Mechanical, Magnetical, Optical, Hydrostatical and Pneumatical Experiments' is a fascinating collection of experiments performed by Francis Hauksbee, accompanied by explanatory lectures delivered by William Whiston. This work delves into the realms of mechanics, magnetism, optics, hydrostatics, and pneumatics, presenting a series of captivating experiments that explore the fundamental principles of these scientific fields. From the manipulation of light to the study of air pressure, Hauksbee's experiments and Whiston's lectures offer a captivating journey into the wonders of the natural world.

Genres: Science, Experimental, Educational

Themes: Scientific inquiry, Natural phenomena, Exploration of physical principles
