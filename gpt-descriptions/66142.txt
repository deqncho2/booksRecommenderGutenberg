Synopsis: 

In 'Preferred Position' by Dave Dryfoos, a talented young baseball player named Jack finds himself caught between his dreams and the expectations of his family. As he navigates the pressures of high school, friendships, and first love, Jack must make difficult choices that will shape his future. With the stakes high and the competition fierce, Jack's journey becomes a captivating exploration of ambition, sacrifice, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Sports, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Family expectations, Identity, Sacrifice
