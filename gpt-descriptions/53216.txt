Synopsis: 
In 'An Essay on the Incubus, or Night-mare' by John Bond, M.D., a mysterious and unsettling phenomenon known as the incubus haunts the dreams of individuals, causing them great distress and fear. As the author delves into the origins and nature of this nocturnal terror, he uncovers a world of dark secrets and hidden desires. Through a combination of scientific analysis and personal anecdotes, Bond explores the psychological and physiological effects of the incubus, leaving readers captivated by the enigmatic nature of this haunting phenomenon.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Essay, Psychology

Themes: 
Dreams, Fear, Supernatural, Human Psychology
