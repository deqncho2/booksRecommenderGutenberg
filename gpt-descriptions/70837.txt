Synopsis: 
In "Thrice Wedded, but Only Once a Wife," we follow the tumultuous journey of a woman named Georgie Sheldon, who finds herself entangled in three marriages that bring her both joy and heartache. From her first marriage to a charming but troubled man, to her second marriage to a wealthy and influential figure, and finally to her third marriage to a mysterious stranger, Georgie's life is filled with unexpected twists and turns. As she navigates the complexities of love, betrayal, and self-discovery, Georgie must confront her own desires and make difficult choices that will shape her future. This gripping tale of love, passion, and resilience will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and relationships, self-discovery, societal expectations
