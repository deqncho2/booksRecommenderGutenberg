Synopsis: In "His Excellency [Son Exc. Eugène Rougon]" by Émile Zola, we are introduced to the ambitious and cunning Eugène Rougon, a man who will stop at nothing to climb the political ladder in 19th-century France. As he navigates the treacherous world of power and corruption, Rougon becomes entangled in a web of scandal, manipulation, and personal sacrifices. With his sights set on the highest office in the land, he must navigate through a world of political intrigue, secret alliances, and personal betrayals. Will Rougon's relentless pursuit of power lead to his ultimate downfall or will he emerge as the master manipulator he aspires to be?

Genres: Historical fiction, political drama, psychological thriller

Themes: Ambition, power, corruption, manipulation, loyalty, betrayal
