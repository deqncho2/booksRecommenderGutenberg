Synopsis: 

"The "City Guard": A History of Company "B" First Regiment Infantry, N. G. C. During the Sacramento Campaign, July 3 to 26, 1894" is a captivating historical account that delves into the experiences of Company B, a renowned infantry unit, during the intense Sacramento Campaign of 1894. This book not only provides a detailed narrative of the campaign itself but also offers a fascinating glimpse into the rich history of Company B, spanning from its formation in 1854 to the events leading up to the campaign. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Historical significance
