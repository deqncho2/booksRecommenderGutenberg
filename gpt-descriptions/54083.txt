Synopsis: 
In 'Sketch-Book of the North', George Eyre-Todd takes us on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich history of the Scottish Highlands. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, he paints a vivid picture of the region's rugged beauty, ancient castles, and mysterious folklore. From the bustling streets of Edinburgh to the remote islands of the Hebrides, Eyre-Todd's sketches transport us to a world of breathtaking landscapes and captivating tales. As he explores the land of his ancestors, he delves into the deep-rooted traditions and customs that have shaped the identity of the Scottish people. 

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, History

Themes: Scottish culture, Landscape appreciation, Historical exploration
