Synopsis: In 'The Story of a Great Delusion in a Series of Matter-of-Fact Chapters', White, William takes us on a captivating journey through the depths of a gripping delusion. With a masterful blend of suspense and intrigue, the author unravels the layers of this delusion, leaving readers questioning their own perceptions of reality. As the story unfolds, we are drawn into a world where truth and illusion become indistinguishable, and the consequences of this delusion become increasingly dire. With each chapter, White skillfully builds tension, keeping readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind this captivating delusion.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Perception vs. reality, Deception, Mental health
