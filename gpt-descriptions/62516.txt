Synopsis:
'Agatha's Aunt' by Harriet L. Smith is a captivating mystery novel that follows the adventures of Agatha, a young woman who unexpectedly inherits a large fortune from her mysterious aunt. As Agatha delves into her aunt's past, she uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that threatens her own life. With the help of a charming detective, Agatha must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects to uncover the truth behind her aunt's fortune and the danger that surrounds it.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes:
Inheritance, Secrets, Deception
