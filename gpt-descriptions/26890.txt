Synopsis: In 'The Rat Racket' by David H. Keller, a renowned scientist stumbles upon a mysterious underground organization that is using rats for a sinister purpose. As he delves deeper into the dark secrets of this rat racket, he finds himself entangled in a web of danger and deception. With the clock ticking, he must race against time to expose the truth and save humanity from an unimaginable threat.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Conspiracy, Scientific Ethics, Human Survival
