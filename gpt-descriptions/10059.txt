Synopsis: 
In 'Aunt Jane's Nieces on Vacation', the three charming nieces, Louise, Patsy, and Beth, embark on an exciting journey to a picturesque seaside resort. As they immerse themselves in the beauty of nature and the vibrant local culture, unexpected adventures and encounters await them. From mysterious strangers to thrilling escapades, the girls find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of excitement and intrigue. Will their vacation turn out to be the relaxing getaway they had hoped for, or will it lead them down a path they never could have imagined?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Independence
