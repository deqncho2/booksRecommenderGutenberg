Synopsis: 
"The Passing of the Storm, and Other Poems" by King, Alfred Castner is a collection of captivating and thought-provoking poems that explore the depths of human emotions and the beauty of nature. From the tumultuous storms of life to the serene moments of tranquility, these poems take the reader on a journey of self-reflection and appreciation for the world around them. With vivid imagery and lyrical language, King, Alfred Castner invites readers to delve into the complexities of the human experience and find solace in the power of words.

Genres: Poetry, Literary

Themes: Nature, Emotions, Self-reflection
