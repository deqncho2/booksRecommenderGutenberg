Synopsis: In "My New Curate" by Patrick Augustine Sheehan, a small Irish village is turned upside down when a charismatic and mysterious new curate arrives. As the villagers become captivated by his charm and enigmatic past, they soon discover that his presence brings both joy and turmoil to their lives. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, the story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth behind the curate's arrival.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Faith and spirituality, community dynamics, the power of secrets
