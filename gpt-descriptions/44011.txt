Synopsis: In 'Warren Commission (11 of 26): Hearings Vol. XI (of 15)', the United States Warren Commission delves deeper into the investigation surrounding the assassination of President John F. Kennedy. Through a series of hearings, witnesses are called to testify, shedding light on various aspects of the case. As the Commission uncovers new evidence and explores different angles, the plot thickens, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth behind this historic event.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, True Crime

Themes: Conspiracy, Government secrecy, Justice and accountability
