Synopsis: In 'A Study of Association in Insanity', Kent and Rosanoff delve into the mysterious world of mental illness, exploring the intricate connections between thoughts, emotions, and behavior. Through their groundbreaking research, they uncover surprising patterns and associations that challenge conventional understanding of insanity. As they navigate the labyrinthine corridors of the human mind, the authors offer a thought-provoking examination of the complexities of mental health and the potential for new insights into the nature of insanity.

Genres: Psychology, Non-fiction, Medical

Themes: Mental illness, Association, Human cognition
