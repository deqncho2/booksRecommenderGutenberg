Synopsis: In 'PRoblem' by Alan Edward Nourse, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Paul Cameron finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. As he races against time to protect his invention, he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. With his life on the line, Dr. Cameron must navigate a treacherous landscape of corporate greed, government conspiracies, and personal vendettas, all while trying to unravel the true nature of the 'PRoblem' he has uncovered.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific ethics, Corporate power, Government secrecy, Personal sacrifice
