Synopsis: In 'Motor Matt's Launch; or, A Friend in Need' by Stanley R. Matthews, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure alongside the young and resourceful Motor Matt. When a mysterious message leads him to a hidden treasure, Matt finds himself entangled in a dangerous race against time, facing treacherous villains and unexpected allies. With his quick thinking and mechanical skills, Motor Matt must navigate through perilous waters and outsmart his adversaries to uncover the truth behind the hidden treasure. Will he succeed in his mission and save the day?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Perseverance
