Synopsis: In the city of Midnight, where music is forbidden, a young girl named Mosca Mye finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when she becomes the unlikely accomplice of a mysterious wordsmith named Eponymous Clent. Together, they embark on a thrilling adventure filled with secrets, political turmoil, and a quest for freedom that will challenge everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Rebellion, Power of Words, Identity
