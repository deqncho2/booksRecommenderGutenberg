Synopsis: In 'Campaign of the Fourteenth Regiment New Jersey Volunteers', J. Newton Terrill takes readers on a gripping journey through the American Civil War, as experienced by the soldiers of the Fourteenth Regiment New Jersey Volunteers. From the chaos of battlefields to the camaraderie forged in the face of adversity, this captivating account offers a firsthand perspective of the sacrifices and triumphs of these brave men. As they navigate the horrors of war and the complexities of military strategy, readers will be drawn into the gripping narrative that unfolds, leaving them eager to discover the fate of these courageous soldiers.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Memoir

Themes: Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Resilience
