Synopsis: In 'Heir Apparent' by Alan Edward Nourse, a young girl named Giannine is immersed in a virtual reality game called Rasmussem Corporation's Heir Apparent. However, what starts as an exciting adventure quickly turns into a life-or-death situation when Giannine becomes trapped inside the game. With her every move being monitored by the game's creators, she must navigate through a series of challenging levels and outsmart her opponents in order to survive. As the stakes rise and the clock ticks, Giannine must rely on her wit, courage, and resourcefulness to find a way out of the game and back to reality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Young Adult, Adventure

Themes: Virtual reality, Survival, Identity, Technology
