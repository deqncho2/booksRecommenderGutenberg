Synopsis: In 'The Moon and the Sun' by James McKimmey, a brilliant scientist embarks on a daring expedition to a mysterious island in the 17th century. There, he discovers a captivating secret that challenges everything he knows about the world and ignites a dangerous battle for power and knowledge. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he must navigate treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of immortality. Will he uncover the truth or be consumed by the darkness that lurks beneath the surface?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Power, Love, Immortality
