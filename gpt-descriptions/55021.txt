Synopsis: 
In 'Through Swamp and Glade: A Tale of the Seminole War' by Munroe, Kirk, readers are transported to the untamed wilderness of Florida during the Seminole War. The story follows a young protagonist who finds himself caught between conflicting loyalties and embarks on a perilous journey through treacherous swamps and dense glades. As he navigates the dangers of war and the complexities of human relationships, he must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale immerses readers in a world of adventure, courage, and the struggle for survival.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Conflict, Survival
