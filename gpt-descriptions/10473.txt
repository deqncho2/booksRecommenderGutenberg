Synopsis: In 'The Heart of the Range' by William Patterson White, a young cowboy named Andy is faced with a life-altering decision when he discovers a hidden treasure in the heart of the Wyoming wilderness. As he embarks on a thrilling adventure to protect his newfound wealth, Andy must navigate the treacherous landscape of greed, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With danger lurking at every turn, he must rely on his wit, courage, and the bonds of friendship to survive. Will Andy's journey lead him to unimaginable riches or will it cost him everything he holds dear?

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Greed, Friendship, Survival
