Synopsis: In 'The Young Continentals at Monmouth', a group of brave young soldiers find themselves caught in the midst of the historic Battle of Monmouth during the American Revolutionary War. As they navigate the chaos and danger of the battlefield, they must rely on their courage, loyalty, and resourcefulness to survive. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival
