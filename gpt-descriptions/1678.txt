Synopsis:
In the small town of Gondreville, a mysterious event from the past resurfaces, captivating the attention of its inhabitants. As secrets are unveiled and hidden connections are revealed, the town becomes a hotbed of intrigue and suspicion. A group of individuals, each with their own motives and desires, find themselves entangled in a web of deception and betrayal. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they discover that the truth may be more shocking than they could have ever imagined.

Genres:
Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes:
Power and Corruption, Identity and Deception, Loyalty and Betrayal
