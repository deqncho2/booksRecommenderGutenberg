Synopsis: 
'A Year in a Yawl: A True Tale of the Adventures of Four Boys in a Thirty-foot Yawl' takes readers on an exhilarating journey as four young boys set sail on a daring adventure aboard a small yacht. From navigating treacherous waters to encountering unexpected challenges, the boys face thrilling escapades that test their courage and resilience. As they navigate the highs and lows of their voyage, they discover the true meaning of friendship and the transformative power of the sea.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Memoir

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resilience, Exploration
