Synopsis: In 'Michael and His Lost Angel: A Play in Five Acts', the enigmatic Michael finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and redemption. As he embarks on a journey to find his lost angel, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters who challenge his beliefs and test the limits of his faith. With each act, the plot thickens, unveiling secrets and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final curtain falls.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Redemption, Faith and spirituality
