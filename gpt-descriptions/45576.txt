Synopsis: In 'The Motor Boys Over the Rockies; Or, A Mystery of the Air' by Clarence Young, three adventurous friends embark on a thrilling journey across the majestic Rocky Mountains. When they stumble upon a perplexing mystery, their skills and courage are put to the test as they navigate treacherous terrain, encounter unexpected dangers, and uncover a secret that could change everything. With their trusty motor vehicle as their only ally, the Motor Boys must rely on their resourcefulness and determination to solve the enigma and emerge victorious.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Problem-solving
