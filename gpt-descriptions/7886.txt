Synopsis: In 'Steam, Steel and Electricity' by James W. Steele, a brilliant inventor embarks on a thrilling journey through a world where steam-powered contraptions, towering steel structures, and the wonders of electricity collide. As he navigates through a web of intrigue and danger, he must uncover the secrets behind a powerful new technology that could change the course of history. With unexpected allies and formidable adversaries, the inventor's quest becomes a race against time, filled with breathtaking discoveries and heart-pounding action.

Genres: Steampunk, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Innovation, Power, Exploration
