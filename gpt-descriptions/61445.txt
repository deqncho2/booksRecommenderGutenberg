Synopsis: In 'A House Divided Against Itself (Complete)' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a seemingly idyllic family is torn apart by a shocking revelation that threatens to unravel their lives. As secrets are exposed and loyalties are tested, the characters must confront the consequences of their actions and make difficult choices that will forever change their relationships. With its gripping narrative and complex characters, this novel explores the depths of human nature and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Family dynamics, Betrayal, Redemption, Morality
