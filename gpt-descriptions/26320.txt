Synopsis: In 'The Merry-Go-Round' by Van Vechten, Carl, we are introduced to a vibrant and eclectic group of characters who find themselves entangled in a web of scandal, secrets, and unexpected connections. Set against the backdrop of 1920s New York City, this novel takes us on a thrilling journey through the lives of artists, socialites, and bohemians, as they navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness. As the story unfolds, the reader is drawn into a world filled with glamour, intrigue, and the ever-present allure of the merry-go-round.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Class and Status, Art and Creativity, Identity and Authenticity
