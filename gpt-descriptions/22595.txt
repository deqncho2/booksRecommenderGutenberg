Synopsis: In 'At the Point of the Sword' by Hayens Herbert, a young and ambitious knight finds himself entangled in a treacherous web of political intrigue and personal vendettas. As he navigates the dangerous world of medieval warfare, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With vivid descriptions of epic battles, unexpected alliances, and a captivating cast of characters, this thrilling historical fiction takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous times of knights and chivalry.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Honor and loyalty, Betrayal, Redemption, Personal growth
