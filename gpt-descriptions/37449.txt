Synopsis: In 'Puppets at Large: Scenes and Subjects from Mr Punch's Show', Anstey takes readers on a delightful journey into the whimsical world of Mr Punch and his puppet companions. Through a series of captivating scenes and subjects, the author explores the mischievous antics, humorous dialogues, and unexpected twists that unfold during the puppet show. From Punch's encounters with various characters to the lively interactions between the puppets themselves, this collection of stories offers a charming and entertaining glimpse into the enchanting realm of puppetry.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Fantasy

Themes: Humor, Imagination, Performance, Tradition
