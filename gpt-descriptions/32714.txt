Synopsis: 

In the aftermath of the devastating Great Chelsea Fire of 1908, a mysterious souvenir book emerges, offering a glimpse into the charred remains of the burned district and the once-prominent buildings that stood tall. With thirty-four haunting views captured within its pages, this anonymous compilation takes readers on a visual journey through the aftermath of destruction, revealing the resilience of a community in the face of tragedy. As the reader delves into the pages, they are left to ponder the stories behind the scorched facades and the significance of the buildings that once shaped the landscape. 

Genres: Historical, Photography, Non-fiction

Themes: Tragedy, Resilience, Community
