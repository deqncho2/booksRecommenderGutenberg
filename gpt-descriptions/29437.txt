Synopsis: In 'The Martian Cabal' by Starzl, Roman Frederick, a group of astronauts embarks on a mission to colonize Mars, but they soon discover a hidden conspiracy that threatens not only their mission but the entire future of humanity. As they navigate the treacherous Martian landscape, they must unravel the secrets of the cabal and fight against time to save themselves and the fate of Earth. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping sci-fi thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Conspiracy, Survival, Exploration, Betrayal
