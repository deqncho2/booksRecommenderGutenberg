Synopsis: In 'The Boy Allies Under the Sea; Or, The Vanishing Submarines' by Clair W. Hayes, a thrilling adventure awaits as two young allies find themselves embarking on a dangerous mission to uncover the mystery behind the vanishing submarines. As they dive into the depths of the sea, they encounter treacherous obstacles, unexpected allies, and a sinister plot that could change the course of history. With their courage and resourcefulness, the boy allies must navigate through perilous waters to save the day and bring the truth to light.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Perseverance, Loyalty
