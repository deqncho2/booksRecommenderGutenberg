Synopsis: In "Under Sentence of Death; Or, a Criminal's Last Hours" by Victor Hugo, the reader is taken on a gripping journey through the final moments of a condemned criminal's life. As the clock ticks down, the narrative delves into the mind of the protagonist, exploring their thoughts, regrets, and hopes. With each passing hour, the tension builds, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, questioning the nature of justice and the complexities of human morality.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Drama, Crime

Themes: Death and mortality, Redemption, Morality and ethics
