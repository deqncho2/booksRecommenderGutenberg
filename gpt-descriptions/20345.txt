Synopsis: 
In 'Old Man Savarin, and Other Stories' by Thomson, Edward William, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing and thought-provoking tales. From the enigmatic life of an old man with a mysterious past to the unexpected consequences of a seemingly innocent decision, each story presents a unique and compelling narrative that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. With vivid descriptions and well-crafted characters, this collection explores the complexities of human nature, the power of choices, and the unexpected twists that life often brings.

Genres: 
Mystery, Drama, Fiction

Themes: 
Human nature, Choices, Unexpected consequences
