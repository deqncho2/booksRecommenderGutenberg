Synopsis: In 'The Legend of the Bleeding-heart' by Annie F. Johnston, a young girl named Dorothy discovers a hidden garden filled with mysterious flowers and a captivating legend. As she unravels the secrets of the garden, Dorothy embarks on a journey of self-discovery, friendship, and the power of love. With each step, she uncovers the enchanting tale behind the bleeding-heart flower and its connection to her own life. This heartwarming and magical story will transport readers to a world where legends come alive and the beauty of nature holds extraordinary power.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Love, Nature's Magic
