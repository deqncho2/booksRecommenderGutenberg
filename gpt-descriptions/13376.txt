Synopsis: 

'Our Legal Heritage, King AEthelbert, 596 to King George III, 1775' takes readers on a captivating journey through centuries of legal history, from the reign of King AEthelbert to the era of King George III. In this meticulously researched and engagingly written book, author S. A. Reilly delves into the fascinating evolution of legal systems, exploring the laws, customs, and landmark cases that shaped the legal landscape of England. From the establishment of the Anglo-Saxon legal code to the pivotal moments that laid the foundation for modern legal principles, this book offers a comprehensive and enthralling exploration of the legal heritage that has shaped our society.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Legal History

Themes: Legal evolution, Historical development, Influence of law on society
