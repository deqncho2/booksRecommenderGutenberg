Synopsis: 
In 'Freedom, Truth and Beauty: Sonnets' by Edward Doyle, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of sonnets that explore the depths of human emotions and experiences. Each sonnet is a window into the author's soul, delving into themes of love, loss, longing, and the pursuit of personal freedom. With lyrical and evocative language, Doyle weaves together a tapestry of emotions that will resonate with readers and leave them pondering the complexities of life and the power of poetic expression.

Genres: Poetry, Literature, Philosophy

Themes: Love, Loss, Freedom, Beauty, Human Emotions
