Synopsis: In 'After the Manner of Men' by Francis Lynde, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a dark secret that threatens to tear the community apart. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the townspeople must confront their own prejudices and fears in order to uncover the truth and restore peace. With its gripping plot and intriguing characters, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Drama

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Prejudice and judgment
