Synopsis: 

In 'Mexico, Aztec, Spanish and Republican, Vol. 2 of 2', Brantz Mayer takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history, diverse geography, complex politics, and vibrant society of Mexico. From the moment the Spaniards invaded the Aztec empire to the present day, Mayer provides a comprehensive account of the country's evolution, shedding light on the struggles, triumphs, and transformations that have shaped Mexico into what it is today.

Genres: Historical, Geographical, Political, Statistical, Social

Themes: Cultural assimilation, Colonialism, Independence, Nation-building, Social inequality
