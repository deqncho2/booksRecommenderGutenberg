Synopsis: In the dark and dangerous slums of London's East End, young Dicky Perrott navigates a treacherous world of poverty, crime, and violence. Born into the notorious Jago, a neighborhood plagued by squalor and despair, Dicky must find a way to survive amidst the constant struggle for survival. As he encounters a cast of unforgettable characters, including thieves, prostitutes, and gangsters, Dicky's journey becomes a gripping exploration of the human spirit and the lengths one will go to escape their circumstances.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Crime Fiction, Social Realism

Themes: Poverty, Survival, Morality, Social Inequality
