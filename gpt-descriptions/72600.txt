Synopsis: In the fifteenth century, a young trumpeter named Joseph Charnetski finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he and his family flee to the city of Krakow. As Joseph becomes entangled in the secrets of the alchemist's stone and the power struggles of the city, he must navigate a treacherous path to protect his loved ones and uncover the truth behind the ancient artifact. With its rich historical setting and thrilling plot, 'The Trumpeter of Krakow' takes readers on a captivating journey through medieval Poland.

Genres: Historical fiction, adventure, mystery

Themes: Courage, loyalty, the pursuit of knowledge, the power of family
