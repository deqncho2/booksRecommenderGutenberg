Synopsis: In the bustling streets of Piccadilly, a chance encounter between two strangers sets off a chain of events that will forever alter their lives. As secrets are unveiled and hidden desires come to the surface, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and ambition. With the backdrop of Victorian London, this captivating fragment of contemporary biography explores the complexities of human relationships and the consequences of one's choices.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Betrayal, Ambition, Victorian Society
