Synopsis: In 'The Fugitive' by Rabindranath Tagore, a young man named Amulya finds himself caught in a web of secrets and lies when he becomes entangled with a mysterious woman. As he embarks on a journey to uncover the truth, Amulya must confront his own identity and face the consequences of his choices. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, this gripping tale explores the complexities of love, betrayal, and the search for personal freedom.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Truth, Love, Betrayal, Freedom
