Synopsis: 
In "Prisoner of War" by Randall Garrett, a young soldier finds himself captured and imprisoned in a mysterious enemy camp during a brutal war. As he navigates the treacherous environment, he discovers a hidden network of prisoners who have developed their own unique ways of surviving and resisting their captors. With danger lurking at every turn, he must uncover the truth behind the camp's secrets and find a way to escape before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, War

Themes: Survival, Resistance, Betrayal
