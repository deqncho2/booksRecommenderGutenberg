Synopsis: In 'Notes Upon Indigo' by John L. Hayes, a mysterious journal is discovered, filled with cryptic entries and sketches that lead the reader on a captivating journey through the world of indigo dye. As the protagonist delves deeper into the secrets of this ancient pigment, they uncover a hidden history, encounter enigmatic characters, and become entangled in a web of intrigue that spans centuries. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn into a mesmerizing exploration of art, culture, and the power of color.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Art

Themes: Identity, Cultural Heritage, Artistic Expression
