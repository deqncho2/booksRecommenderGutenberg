Synopsis: In "With the Black Prince" by William O. Stoddard, a young American boy named Ned finds himself transported back in time to the 14th century, where he becomes entangled in the tumultuous events of the Hundred Years' War. As Ned navigates the treacherous world of medieval Europe, he forms an unlikely friendship with the legendary Black Prince, Edward of Woodstock. Together, they embark on a thrilling adventure filled with battles, political intrigue, and personal growth, as Ned learns the true meaning of honor, loyalty, and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Time Travel

Themes: Friendship, Honor, Loyalty, Identity, Coming of Age
