Synopsis: In 'A Connecticut Yankee in King Arthur's Court, Part 1,' a modern-day engineer named Hank Morgan finds himself transported back in time to the medieval kingdom of King Arthur. Armed with his knowledge of technology and science, Hank becomes determined to use his newfound position to bring about social and technological advancements in this ancient world. However, he soon realizes that his attempts to modernize the kingdom are met with resistance and skepticism from the superstitious and traditional society. As Hank navigates the challenges of this unfamiliar era, he must confront the clash between progress and tradition, and the consequences of wielding power and knowledge in a world unprepared for it.

Genres: Historical fiction, Satire, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Progress vs Tradition, Power and Knowledge
