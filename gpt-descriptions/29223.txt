Synopsis:
In "Ponteach; Or, The Savages of America," Robert Rogers takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of colonial America. Set during the French and Indian War, the story follows the charismatic Native American leader, Ponteach, as he navigates the complex web of alliances and betrayals between the indigenous tribes and the European settlers. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, Ponteach finds himself torn between his desire for peace and his duty to protect his people. With vivid descriptions of the rugged landscape and intense battles, this gripping tale explores the clash of cultures and the struggle for power in a land on the brink of change.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Cultural conflict, Power struggles, Loyalty, Identity
