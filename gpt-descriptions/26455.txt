Synopsis: 

'A History of Lumsden's Battery, C.S.A.' takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of the American Civil War. Written by George Little and James Robert Maxwell, this historical account delves into the remarkable story of Lumsden's Battery, a Confederate artillery unit that played a pivotal role in several major battles. From the intense battles of Shiloh and Chickamauga to the heart-wrenching Siege of Vicksburg, the battery's experiences are vividly brought to life, offering a unique perspective on the war. Through personal anecdotes, detailed descriptions, and strategic analysis, the authors paint a vivid picture of the soldiers' bravery, sacrifice, and unwavering loyalty to their cause. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History, Biography

Themes: Loyalty, Bravery, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Strategy
