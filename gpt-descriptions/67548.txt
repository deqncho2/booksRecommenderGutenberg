Synopsis: In the depths of a continental university library, a hidden manuscript reveals a captivating tale of a world where immortality reigns, but without the presence of a divine being. As the pages unfold, readers are transported to a realm where mortals have discovered the secret to eternal life, leading to a society filled with intrigue, power struggles, and philosophical debates. Through the eyes of a diverse cast of characters, this unpublished manuscript delves into the consequences of immortality, the search for meaning in a godless existence, and the eternal quest for knowledge and purpose.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Immortality, Power, Philosophy, Meaning of Life
