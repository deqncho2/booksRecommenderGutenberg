Synopsis: In "Jim Cummings; Or, The Great Adams Express Robbery" by A. Frank Pinkerton, follow the thrilling journey of Jim Cummings, a skilled detective, as he unravels the mystery behind a daring robbery that has left the nation in shock. As Cummings delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, leading him on a dangerous chase to catch the elusive culprits. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Cummings must rely on his wit, courage, and determination to bring justice to those responsible. Will he succeed in solving the crime and restoring order, or will he become entangled in a web of danger from which there is no escape?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Justice, Betrayal, Deception, Determination
