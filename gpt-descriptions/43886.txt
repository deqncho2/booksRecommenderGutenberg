Synopsis:
In this unique adaptation of John Bunyan's classic allegory, Samuel Phillips Day presents the timeless tale of Christian's journey to the Celestial City in words of only one syllable. Follow Christian as he encounters various obstacles and temptations, all while striving to stay on the narrow path towards salvation. With its simplified language, this version of Pilgrim's Progress offers a fresh and accessible take on a beloved literary masterpiece.

Genres:
Classic literature, Christian fiction, allegory

Themes:
Spiritual journey, faith, redemption, temptation, perseverance
