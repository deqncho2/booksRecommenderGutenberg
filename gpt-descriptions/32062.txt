Synopsis: In the second volume of "The Daltons; Or, Three Roads In Life," the captivating story of the Dalton family continues to unfold. As the siblings navigate their separate paths, they encounter unexpected challenges, dangerous encounters, and surprising alliances. With their lives intertwined by fate, the Daltons must confront their past, make difficult choices, and ultimately find their own destinies. This thrilling tale of love, betrayal, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Family bonds, Identity, Redemption
