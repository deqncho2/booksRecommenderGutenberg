Synopsis: 

Step back in time to the vibrant city of Birmingham in 1886, as the British Association for the Advancement of Science presents their meticulously crafted 'Handbook of Birmingham'. This captivating guide takes readers on a journey through the bustling streets, revealing the city's rich history, architectural marvels, and scientific achievements. From the grandeur of the Town Hall to the innovative factories that have propelled Birmingham to the forefront of industrialization, this handbook offers a fascinating glimpse into a city on the cusp of transformation. Delve into the pages of this remarkable handbook and discover the hidden gems, captivating stories, and remarkable individuals that have shaped Birmingham into the thriving metropolis it is today.

Genres: Historical, Travel, Reference

Themes: Urban development, Industrialization, Scientific progress
