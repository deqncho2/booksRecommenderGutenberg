Synopsis: 

'With the Flag in the Channel; or, The Adventures of Captain Gustavus Conyngham' by Barnes, James is a thrilling historical novel set during the American Revolutionary War. The story follows the daring exploits of Captain Gustavus Conyngham, a young and audacious American privateer. Conyngham embarks on a perilous mission to disrupt British shipping and challenge the mighty Royal Navy in the treacherous waters of the English Channel. As he navigates through dangerous encounters, captures enemy vessels, and outwits his pursuers, Captain Conyngham becomes a legendary figure in the annals of naval warfare. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Patriotism, Bravery, Naval Warfare
