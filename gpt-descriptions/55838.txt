Synopsis: 

'Signers of the Declaration: Historic Places Commemorating the Signing of the Declaration of Independence' takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the significant locations where the signers of the Declaration of Independence made history. From the hallowed halls of Independence Hall in Philadelphia to the charming streets of Williamsburg, Virginia, this book unveils the hidden stories and remarkable details behind each historic site. Through vivid descriptions and stunning visuals, readers will discover the rich tapestry of events that unfolded during this pivotal moment in American history, shedding light on the lives and legacies of the brave individuals who signed the Declaration. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: American Revolution, Founding Fathers, Historical Preservation
