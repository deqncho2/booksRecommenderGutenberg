Synopsis: In 'A Little Maid of Province Town', follow the captivating journey of Priscilla, a young girl living in the quaint town of Province. As she navigates the challenges of growing up in a close-knit community, Priscilla discovers a hidden secret that could change her life forever. With courage and determination, she embarks on a quest to uncover the truth, facing unexpected obstacles along the way. Will Priscilla's determination and resourcefulness be enough to unravel the mystery that lies within her beloved town?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Determination, Community, Secrets
