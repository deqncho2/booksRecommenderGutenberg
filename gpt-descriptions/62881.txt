Synopsis: In 'The Tank Corps' by Williams-Ellis, Clough/ Williams-Ellis, Amabel, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the world of armored warfare during World War I. The story follows a group of courageous tank crew members as they navigate the treacherous battlefields, facing not only the physical challenges of war but also the emotional toll it takes on their lives. As the tanks become their second home, the crew members forge unbreakable bonds and discover the true meaning of camaraderie and sacrifice. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale explores the triumphs and tragedies of those who fought in the Tank Corps.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Human resilience
