Synopsis: In 'Praying for Money' by Russell H. Conwell, a struggling young artist discovers a mysterious prayer that promises to bring him wealth and success. Skeptical yet desperate, he decides to give it a try, only to find himself caught in a web of unexpected consequences. As he delves deeper into the origins of the prayer, he uncovers a hidden world of ancient secrets and powerful forces that will challenge everything he thought he knew about faith, destiny, and the true nature of wealth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Ambition, Fate, Spirituality
