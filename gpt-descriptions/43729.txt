Synopsis: In "The Lost Pibroch, and other Sheiling Stories" by Neil Munro, readers are transported to the enchanting landscapes of the Scottish Highlands. This collection of captivating tales weaves together folklore, history, and the lives of the people who call these rugged lands home. From mysterious legends of lost music to heartwarming stories of love and resilience, Munro's vivid storytelling paints a vivid picture of a bygone era. Each story is a window into the rich tapestry of Highland life, where ancient traditions and modern challenges collide.

Genres: Historical fiction, folklore, short stories

Themes: Scottish culture, tradition, resilience, love, folklore
