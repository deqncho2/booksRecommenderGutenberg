Synopsis: In 'Spiritual Tales: Re-issue of the Shorter Stories of Fiona Macleod; Rearranged, with Additional Tales' by William Sharp, readers are transported to a mystical world where ancient Celtic legends and folklore come alive. This collection of shorter stories, beautifully rearranged and enhanced with additional tales, delves into the depths of the human spirit and explores the profound connection between nature, spirituality, and the supernatural. From ethereal landscapes to encounters with mythical beings, each story weaves a captivating tapestry of enchantment and wonder, leaving readers spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Supernatural

Themes: Spirituality, Nature, Mythology
