Synopsis: In "How The Raven Died: 1902, From "Wolfville Nights"," the small town of Wolfville is shaken by a mysterious death that leaves the community in shock. As the townspeople try to unravel the truth behind the tragedy, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and unexpected connections are revealed. With a cast of intriguing characters and a gripping plot, this story delves into the dark underbelly of a seemingly peaceful town, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Western, Thriller

Themes: Small-town secrets, Betrayal, Justice
