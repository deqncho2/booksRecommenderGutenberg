Synopsis: 

With Our Fighting Men: The story of their faith, courage, endurance in the Great War takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of soldiers during World War I. Through vivid storytelling and personal accounts, William E. Sellers delves into the lives of these brave men, exploring the depths of their faith, the unwavering courage they displayed, and the incredible endurance they demonstrated in the face of unimaginable challenges. From the trenches to the battlefields, this book offers a poignant and gripping portrayal of the human spirit amidst the horrors of war.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War literature, Biography

Themes: Faith, Courage, Endurance, Human resilience, Sacrifice
