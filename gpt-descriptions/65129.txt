Synopsis: In "How to Study Fiorillo," Edith Lynwood Winn presents a comprehensive and detailed analysis of how to effectively practice the renowned Fiorillo studies on the violin. Drawing from the teachings of representative modern violin playing, Winn provides invaluable insights and techniques to help violinists master these challenging studies. From fingerings and bowings to phrasing and interpretation, this book offers a wealth of practical advice and exercises to enhance technical proficiency and musicality. Whether you are a student, teacher, or professional violinist, "How to Study Fiorillo" is an indispensable guide that will revolutionize your approach to practicing and performing these influential studies.

Genres: Music Education, Instructional, Music Theory

Themes: Violin Technique, Practice Methods, Musical Interpretation
