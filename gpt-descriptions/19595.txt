Synopsis: In 'Essays on Various Subjects, Principally Designed for Young Ladies', Hannah More presents a collection of thought-provoking essays that explore the complexities of life, love, and society. Through her engaging and insightful writing, More delves into topics such as education, friendship, marriage, and the pursuit of happiness. With a blend of wit, wisdom, and practical advice, she offers guidance to young women navigating the challenges of their time. From the importance of cultivating virtue to the power of self-improvement, More's essays provide a captivating exploration of the human experience.

Genres: Non-fiction, Essay collection, Self-help

Themes: Education, Morality, Women's empowerment, Social norms
