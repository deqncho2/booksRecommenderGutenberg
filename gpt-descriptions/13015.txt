Synopsis: 
'Tales of Old Japan' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and enchanting folklore of Japan. From mythical creatures and supernatural beings to samurai warriors and forbidden love, this collection of stories offers a glimpse into the fascinating world of ancient Japan. With each tale, readers will be transported to a realm where honor, loyalty, and the power of destiny intertwine, leaving them spellbound until the very last page.

Genres: 
Folklore, Historical Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: 
Tradition and Modernity, Honor and Duty, Love and Sacrifice
