Synopsis: In 'Over the Wire' by Jones, Eugene, a young computer hacker named Alex finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse when he stumbles upon a mysterious encrypted message. As he delves deeper into the world of hacking, Alex becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and high-stakes cybercrime. With his life on the line, he must navigate the treacherous virtual landscape and outsmart his adversaries before it's too late.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Techno-thriller

Themes: Cybersecurity, Identity, Trust
