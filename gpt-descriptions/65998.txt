Synopsis: In 'The Witch's Head' by H. Rider Haggard, a young man named Leonard Outram finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he travels to Africa in search of his missing brother. As he delves deeper into the heart of the continent, he encounters a powerful sorceress known as the Witch of the Dawn, whose head holds a secret that could change the course of history. With his life on the line, Leonard must navigate treacherous landscapes, face ancient curses, and confront his own inner demons in order to uncover the truth and save his brother.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Supernatural, Brotherhood
