Synopsis: 
"The Humour of America: Selected, with an Introduction and Index of American Humorists" takes readers on a delightful journey through the rich tapestry of American humor. This collection features a diverse range of witty and clever writings from renowned American humorists, showcasing the unique and often satirical perspectives that have shaped the nation's comedic landscape. From hilarious anecdotes to sharp social commentary, these carefully selected pieces offer a glimpse into the heart and soul of American humor, capturing the essence of the nation's wit and laughter.

Genres: 
Humor, Anthology, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Satire, Social Commentary, American Identity
