Synopsis: In "The Parish Clerk" by P. H. Ditchfield, a small village is shaken by a mysterious event that leads to the discovery of a hidden secret. As the parish clerk, Mr. Barton finds himself at the center of the unfolding mystery, he must navigate through a web of intrigue, deception, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the truth becomes increasingly elusive. Will Mr. Barton be able to uncover the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Small-town intrigue, Power dynamics
