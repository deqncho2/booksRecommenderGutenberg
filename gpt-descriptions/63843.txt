Synopsis: In the small town of Silverbrook, a group of misfit teenagers stumble upon a hidden laboratory filled with strange and powerful substances. As they experiment with these mysterious metalloids, they soon discover that their newfound abilities come with a price. With their lives turned upside down, they must navigate the dangerous consequences of their actions while uncovering the truth behind the origins of these extraordinary elements.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Coming of age, Friendship, Consequences of power, Discovery
