Synopsis: In 'Is Slavery Sanctioned by the Bible?' author Isaac Allen delves into the controversial topic of slavery and its connection to biblical teachings. Through meticulous research and thought-provoking analysis, Allen explores the various interpretations of scripture and the moral implications surrounding this deeply divisive issue. With compelling arguments and historical context, this book challenges readers to reconsider their understanding of the Bible's stance on slavery and its relevance in modern society.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, History

Themes: Morality, Religion, Social Justice
