Synopsis: In 'The Secret Battleplane' by Percy F. Westerman, a young inventor stumbles upon a remarkable discovery that could change the course of warfare forever. As he embarks on a thrilling adventure to protect his invention from falling into the wrong hands, he finds himself caught in a web of danger, espionage, and international intrigue. With the fate of nations hanging in the balance, he must navigate treacherous waters and outsmart his enemies to ensure the secret battleplane remains a secret.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Invention and innovation, Espionage and secrecy, Patriotism and national security
