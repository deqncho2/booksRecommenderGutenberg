Synopsis: In "The Ascendancy of France, 1598-1715" by Henry Offley Wakeman, embark on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of French history. From the reign of Henry IV to the Sun King, Louis XIV, witness the rise of France as a dominant European power. Explore the political intrigues, military conquests, and cultural achievements that shaped the destiny of the nation. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Wakeman brings to life the captivating characters and events that defined this pivotal era in French history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Power and politics, Cultural transformation, Military conquest, Monarchy and absolutism
