Synopsis: In 'Clash of Arms: A Romance' by Bloundelle-Burton, John, a young and spirited woman finds herself caught in the midst of a tumultuous war. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of love, loyalty, and betrayal, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden agendas. With danger lurking at every turn, she must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and the fate of those she holds dear. This gripping tale of passion, intrigue, and sacrifice will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Betrayal, War and Conflict, Identity and Self-Discovery
