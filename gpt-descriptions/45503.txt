Synopsis: 

'Roster and Statistical Record of Company D, of the Eleventh Regiment Maine Infantry Volunteers: With a Sketch of Its Services in the War of the Rebellion' takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of Company D during the War of the Rebellion. This meticulously compiled record provides a comprehensive roster of the soldiers, along with their statistical information, offering a glimpse into the lives and sacrifices made by these brave individuals. Accompanied by a compelling sketch of the company's services, this book sheds light on the untold stories and heroic acts that unfolded on the battlefield.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Heroism
