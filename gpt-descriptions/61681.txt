Synopsis: In 'Grampa in Oz' by Ruth Plumly Thompson, a mischievous boy named Bud accidentally transports his grumpy grandfather to the magical land of Oz. As they embark on a whimsical adventure, they encounter peculiar creatures, enchanted objects, and unexpected challenges. With the help of their newfound friends, they must navigate through Oz's fantastical landscapes and outsmart the cunning villains who seek to exploit their presence. Will Grampa and Bud find a way back home, or will they discover that Oz has more to offer than they ever imagined?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Family bonds, Friendship, Imagination, Overcoming challenges
