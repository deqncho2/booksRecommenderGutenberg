Synopsis: In 'The Butterfly House' by Mary Eleanor Wilkins Freeman, a young woman named Eunice finds herself drawn to a mysterious house in her small New England town. As she becomes more entangled in the secrets and tragedies of the house's past, Eunice must confront her own fears and desires, leading to unexpected revelations and a haunting climax. This atmospheric and suspenseful tale explores the power of memory, the complexities of human relationships, and the haunting presence of the past.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, mystery

Themes: Memory, secrets, the supernatural, the power of the past
