Synopsis: In 'The Hunchback of Westminster' by William Le Queux, a mysterious and disfigured man lurks in the shadows of the political heart of London. As he navigates the corridors of power, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, espionage, and political intrigue. With his unique perspective and hidden talents, he uncovers a conspiracy that threatens to shake the foundations of the British government. As the stakes rise, he must confront his own demons and make a choice that could change the course of history.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power and corruption, Identity and appearance, Loyalty and betrayal
