Synopsis: In 'Puss in Boots, Jr., and the Good Gray Horse', follow the adventures of a clever and mischievous young cat named Puss as he teams up with a noble gray horse to outsmart a cunning villain. Together, they embark on a thrilling journey filled with danger, magic, and unexpected friendships. Will Puss and his trusty companion be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and save the day?

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Deception, Good vs. Evil
