Synopsis: In 'The Curse of Education' by Harold Edward Gorst, a young prodigy named Oliver finds himself trapped in a prestigious boarding school that hides a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the school's mysterious history, Oliver uncovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his own life but also the lives of his fellow students. With the help of a few unlikely allies, Oliver must navigate a treacherous web of deceit, betrayal, and supernatural forces to unravel the truth and break free from the curse that haunts the institution.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Education, Power, Secrets, Redemption
