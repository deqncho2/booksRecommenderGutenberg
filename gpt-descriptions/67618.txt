Synopsis: In 'Paying the Price; or, Nick Carter's Perilous Venture', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of deceit and treachery. When a wealthy businessman is found dead under mysterious circumstances, Carter is called upon to uncover the truth. As he delves deeper into the case, he discovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his own life but also the safety of the entire city. With time running out, Carter must navigate a perilous venture filled with unexpected twists and turns, risking everything to bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice
