Synopsis: 
In 'Camping' by Lockwine, Alexandra Agusta Guttman, a group of friends embarks on a seemingly ordinary camping trip in the picturesque wilderness. However, as they delve deeper into the woods, they discover that they are not alone. Strange occurrences and unexplainable phenomena begin to unfold, testing their friendships and pushing them to their limits. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the group must confront their darkest fears and fight for their survival in a battle against the unknown.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Fear, Mystery
