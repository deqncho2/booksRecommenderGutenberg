Synopsis: In 'Darius the Great: Makers of History' by Abbott, Jacob, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of history's most influential figures. Follow the rise of Darius, a young prince who defies all odds to become the mighty ruler of the Persian Empire. From his early struggles to his triumphs on the battlefield, this gripping tale explores the complexities of power, ambition, and the enduring legacy of a remarkable leader.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Leadership, Ambition, Legacy
