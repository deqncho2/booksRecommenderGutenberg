Synopsis: In 'The Home Mission' by Arthur, T. S. (Timothy Shay), a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey to fulfill a mission that will change her life forever. As she navigates through unexpected challenges and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Emily's determination and resilience are put to the test. With secrets to uncover and a higher purpose to fulfill, 'The Home Mission' takes readers on a captivating and heartwarming adventure that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Faith, Self-discovery
