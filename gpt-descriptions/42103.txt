Synopsis: 
'April Fools: A farce in one act for three male characters' by Chapman, W. F. is a hilarious and fast-paced comedy that revolves around three friends who find themselves caught up in a series of outrageous pranks on April Fools' Day. As the day unfolds, the pranks become increasingly elaborate and absurd, leading to a cascade of unexpected consequences. With mistaken identities, mischievous schemes, and uproarious misunderstandings, this one-act play guarantees non-stop laughter from start to finish.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Friendship, Deception, Humor
