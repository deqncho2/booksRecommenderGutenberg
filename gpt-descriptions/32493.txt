Synopsis: In 'Witch-Burning' by Mary Elizabeth Counselman, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious deaths that seem to be connected to an ancient curse. As the townspeople become increasingly fearful, a young woman named Sarah finds herself caught in the middle of the chaos, desperately searching for answers and trying to protect her loved ones. With its eerie atmosphere and spine-chilling suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Superstition, Power of Belief
