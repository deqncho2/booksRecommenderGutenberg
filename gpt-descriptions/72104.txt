Synopsis: In 'Interference: A Novel, Vol. 1', the lives of two seemingly unrelated individuals collide in a thrilling tale of mystery and suspense. As a series of unexplained events unfold, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of secrets and danger, while a renowned scientist becomes entangled in a conspiracy that threatens to unravel the very fabric of reality. With each passing chapter, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning pages to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic interference that binds these characters together.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Science Fiction

Themes: Identity, Conspiracy, Perception, Reality
