Synopsis: In the small Canadian village of Pontiac, a mysterious stranger named Valmond arrives, captivating the townspeople with his striking resemblance to Napoleon Bonaparte. As Valmond's influence grows, he becomes entangled in a web of love, loyalty, and political intrigue, forcing the villagers to question their own beliefs and desires. With the fate of Pontiac hanging in the balance, Valmond's true identity and intentions remain shrouded in mystery until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, political drama

Themes: Identity, power, love and loyalty, political manipulation
