Synopsis: In 'Songs of the Army of the Night' by Adams, Francis, a mysterious group of individuals known as the Army of the Night emerges, captivating the attention of the public with their enigmatic presence and haunting melodies. As their music spreads like wildfire, it becomes clear that their purpose goes beyond mere entertainment, leading to a thrilling exploration of the power of music and its ability to inspire and unite. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale delves into the depths of human emotions and the transformative nature of art.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Music, Power of Art, Identity, Transformation
