Synopsis: In 'Janus in Modern Life', Petrie takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the enigmatic figure of Janus and its relevance in the modern world. As the protagonist delves into the mysteries surrounding Janus, they uncover a web of secrets that intertwine past and present, leading to unexpected revelations and profound personal transformations. With its intricate plot and thought-provoking narrative, this book offers a unique perspective on the complexities of human nature and the eternal struggle between duality and unity.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Identity, Time, Dualism, Transformation
