Synopsis: 
In 'Tudor school-boy life: the dialogues of Juan Luis Vives', the reader is transported to the vibrant world of a Tudor school, where the young protagonist, Juan Luis Vives, navigates the challenges and adventures of his daily life. Through a series of engaging dialogues, Vives explores the complexities of friendship, the pursuit of knowledge, and the importance of moral values. As the story unfolds, the reader becomes immersed in the vivid descriptions of Tudor England, experiencing the joys and tribulations of school-boy life alongside Vives.

Genres: Historical fiction, Coming-of-age, Educational

Themes: Friendship, Education, Morality, Cultural exploration
