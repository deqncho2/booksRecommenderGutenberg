Synopsis: In 'The Legion of Lazarus' by Hamilton, Edmond, a group of scientists embarks on a daring mission to resurrect an ancient army of warriors. As they delve into the secrets of immortality, they soon discover that the consequences of their actions may be more terrifying than they ever imagined. With danger lurking at every turn, the fate of humanity hangs in the balance.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Immortality, Morality, Power
