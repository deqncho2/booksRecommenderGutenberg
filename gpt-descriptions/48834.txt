Synopsis: 

In "The Great Diamond Hoax: and Other Stirring Incidents in the Life of Asbury Harpending," Asbury Harpending takes readers on a thrilling journey through his adventurous life. From his involvement in a notorious diamond hoax that captivated the nation to his encounters with outlaws, gamblers, and even Native American tribes, Harpending's memoir is a captivating tale of deception, danger, and unexpected twists. As readers delve into the pages of this gripping narrative, they will be transported to a time of wild west frontier, where Harpending's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Memoir, Adventure, Historical

Themes: Deception, Survival, Frontier Life
