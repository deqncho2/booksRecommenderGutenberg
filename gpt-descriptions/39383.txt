Synopsis: In the enchanting city of Paris, Mademoiselle Blanche, a mysterious and captivating woman, arrives with a hidden past and a desire for reinvention. As she navigates the glamorous world of high society, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and forbidden love. With each twist and turn, Mademoiselle Blanche's true identity becomes increasingly elusive, leaving readers captivated by her enigmatic charm and eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Deception, Love and Betrayal
