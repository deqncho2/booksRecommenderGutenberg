Synopsis: In "The Y. M. C. A. Boys of Cliffwood; or, The Struggle for the Holwell Prize," author Henderley Brooks takes readers on an exciting journey with a group of young boys from the Y. M. C. A. as they face challenges and strive to win the prestigious Holwell Prize. Set in the vibrant town of Cliffwood, this captivating story follows the boys as they navigate friendship, competition, and personal growth, all while discovering the true meaning of teamwork and determination. As they face unexpected obstacles and forge unbreakable bonds, the boys learn valuable life lessons that will shape their futures forever.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Sports

Themes: Friendship, Competition, Personal growth, Teamwork
