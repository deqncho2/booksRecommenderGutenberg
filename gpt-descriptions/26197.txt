Synopsis: 
In 'The Nursery Rhyme Book', a mysterious old book is discovered in an attic, filled with enchanting and forgotten nursery rhymes. As a young girl delves into the pages, she is transported into a whimsical world where nursery rhymes come to life. With each rhyme she explores, she uncovers hidden secrets and embarks on thrilling adventures. But as she delves deeper, she realizes that the book holds a dark secret that could change her life forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Discovery, Secrets, Coming of Age
