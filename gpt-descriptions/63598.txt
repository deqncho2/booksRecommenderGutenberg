Synopsis: 
In 'The Essentials of Logic, Being Ten Lectures on Judgment and Inference', Bernard Bosanquet takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of logic. Through a series of ten thought-provoking lectures, Bosanquet explores the fundamental concepts of judgment and inference, unraveling the mysteries of logical reasoning and its applications in various fields. With a clear and engaging writing style, he presents complex ideas in a way that is accessible to both beginners and seasoned philosophers alike. As readers delve into the pages of this enlightening work, they will discover a wealth of insights that challenge their preconceptions and expand their understanding of the logical foundations that underpin our everyday thinking.

Genres: Philosophy, Logic, Non-fiction

Themes: Logical reasoning, Judgment, Inference, Critical thinking, Epistemology
