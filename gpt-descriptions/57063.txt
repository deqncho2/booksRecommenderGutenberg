Synopsis: 
In 'Stories of Symphonic Music: A Guide to the Meaning of Important Symphonies, Overtures, and Tone-poems from Beethoven to the Present Day', Lawrence Gilman takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of symphonic music. With his insightful analysis and vivid storytelling, Gilman unravels the hidden narratives and emotions behind some of the most iconic compositions in history. From Beethoven's revolutionary symphonies to the modern masterpieces of the present day, this guide offers a unique perspective on the profound impact of symphonic music on our lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Music, History

Themes: Music appreciation, Emotional expression, Artistic interpretation
