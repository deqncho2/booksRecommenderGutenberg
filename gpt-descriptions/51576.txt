Synopsis: In 'Shatter the Wall' by Sydney J. Van Scyoc, a young woman named Lys awakens from a deep sleep to find herself in a world vastly different from the one she knew. As she navigates this strange new reality, Lys discovers that she possesses a unique power that could either save or destroy everything she holds dear. With the help of unexpected allies, she embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about her origins and the secrets hidden behind the enigmatic Wall. As Lys delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding her existence, she must confront her own fears and make choices that will determine the fate of her world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Power, Discovery, Sacrifice
