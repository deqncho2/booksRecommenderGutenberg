Synopsis: In 'The Surgery of Ritual Circumcision' by Snowman, Jacob, a renowned surgeon finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he is asked to perform a highly secretive circumcision ritual. As he delves deeper into the ancient traditions and the hidden world surrounding this procedure, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel his own life and beliefs. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning the boundaries of faith, tradition, and the lengths one will go to protect them.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Tradition vs. Modernity, Secrets and Betrayal, Identity and Belief
