Synopsis: 
In 'Selections from the writings of ‘Abdu'l-Bahá', ʻAbdu'l-Bahá, the eldest son of Bahá'u'lláh, offers profound insights and guidance on various aspects of life, spirituality, and the human condition. Through a collection of his writings, he explores the nature of love, the importance of unity, and the role of religion in fostering peace and harmony. With eloquence and wisdom, ʻAbdu'l-Bahá's words inspire readers to reflect on their own beliefs and actions, and to strive for a more compassionate and just world.

Genres: Religious literature, Philosophy, Spirituality

Themes: Love, Unity, Peace, Spirituality, Human nature
