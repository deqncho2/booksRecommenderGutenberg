Synopsis:
'Can You Forgive Her?' by Anthony Trollope is a captivating novel that follows the lives of three women as they navigate love, marriage, and societal expectations. Alice Vavasor, a headstrong and independent woman, finds herself torn between two suitors, each representing different paths in life. As she grapples with her decision, her cousin, Lady Glencora, faces her own struggles within her marriage to a powerful politician. Meanwhile, the steadfast and loyal Arabella Greenow must confront the consequences of her own choices. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this novel delves into the complexities of relationships, the pressures of society, and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Victorian Literature
Themes: Love and Marriage, Independence and Choice, Society and Expectations
