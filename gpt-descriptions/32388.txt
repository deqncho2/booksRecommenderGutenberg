Synopsis: 
In 'The New Warden' by David G. Ritchie, a small, quiet town is shaken when a mysterious figure takes over as the warden of the local prison. As the new warden settles into his role, the townspeople begin to notice strange occurrences and unexplained events happening within the prison walls. As tensions rise and secrets are uncovered, the residents of the town find themselves drawn into a web of intrigue and danger that threatens to unravel their peaceful existence. With each passing day, the true nature of the new warden becomes more apparent, leaving the townspeople questioning who they can trust and what dark forces may be at play.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Power and corruption, Trust and betrayal, Small-town secrets
