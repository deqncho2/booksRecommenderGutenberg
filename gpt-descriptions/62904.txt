Synopsis: In "The Radio Boys with the Iceberg Patrol; Or, Making safe the ocean lanes" by Chapman, Allen, a group of adventurous radio enthusiasts find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey to the treacherous Arctic waters. As they join the Iceberg Patrol, their mission becomes clear: to ensure the safety of the ocean lanes and protect ships from the dangers lurking in the icy depths. With their radio skills and determination, the Radio Boys face perilous challenges, uncover hidden secrets, and navigate through the unforgiving Arctic landscape. Will they succeed in their mission and make it out alive?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Teamwork, Survival
