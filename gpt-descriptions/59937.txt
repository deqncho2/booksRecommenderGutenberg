Synopsis:
In 'The Yellow Poppy' by D. K. Broster, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey that takes her from the tranquil English countryside to the vibrant streets of Paris. Drawn into a world of art, passion, and intrigue, Mary finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden desires. As she navigates the complexities of love and loyalty, she must confront the consequences of her choices and the true nature of her own heart. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, 'The Yellow Poppy' is a mesmerizing tale that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes:
Love and Desire, Betrayal, Self-Discovery
