Synopsis: 
In 'The Care and Feeding of Children: A Catechism for the Use of Mothers and Children's Nurses', L. Emmett Holt provides a comprehensive guide for mothers and nurses on the proper care and nurturing of children. Through a series of questions and answers, Holt covers a wide range of topics including nutrition, hygiene, sleep, discipline, and the importance of play. With practical advice and insightful explanations, this book offers invaluable guidance to those responsible for the well-being of children.

Genres: Non-fiction, Parenting, Health

Themes: Childcare, Parenting, Health and Wellness
