Synopsis: In 'The Triumph of Hilary Blachland' by Mitford, Bertram, a young and ambitious artist named Hilary Blachland finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change the course of history. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where his artistic talents and quick thinking are put to the ultimate test. With each twist and turn, Hilary must navigate through a world of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, all while trying to uncover the truth that lies at the heart of the mystery. Will he be able to unravel the puzzle before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Artistic pursuit, Secrets and hidden truths, Betrayal and deception
