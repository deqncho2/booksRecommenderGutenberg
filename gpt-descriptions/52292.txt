Synopsis: 

In 'Report of the Committee of Fifteen: Read at the Cleveland Meeting of the Department of Superintendence, February 19-21, 1884, with the Debate', Draper, Harris, and Tarbell delve into a controversial topic that has long plagued the education system. Their report, presented at a pivotal meeting, sparks a heated debate among educators and administrators. As they explore the challenges and propose potential solutions, the reader is taken on a thought-provoking journey that questions the very foundation of education.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, History

Themes: Education reform, Curriculum development, Pedagogical approaches
