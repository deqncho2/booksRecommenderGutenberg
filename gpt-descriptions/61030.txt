Synopsis: 
In 'Technical School, Sioux Falls Army Air Field', Northwestern Bell Telephone Company takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of young men training at the Sioux Falls Army Air Field during World War II. Through vivid storytelling and personal accounts, the book delves into the challenges, triumphs, and camaraderie that these individuals encountered as they prepared for their crucial roles in the war effort. From the rigorous technical training to the emotional bonds formed amidst the backdrop of a nation at war, this book offers a compelling glimpse into a pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Friendship, Resilience
