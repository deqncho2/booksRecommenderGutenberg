Synopsis: In "The Story of Young Abraham Lincoln" by Wayne Whipple, embark on a captivating journey through the formative years of one of America's greatest leaders. Follow the humble beginnings of Abraham Lincoln as he navigates the challenges of poverty, loss, and self-discovery. From his early encounters with injustice to his unwavering determination to make a difference, witness the transformation of a young boy into a man who would shape the course of history. This inspiring tale sheds light on the resilience, compassion, and unwavering spirit that defined Abraham Lincoln's character.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Perseverance, Social Justice, Leadership
