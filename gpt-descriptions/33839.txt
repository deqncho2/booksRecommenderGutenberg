Synopsis: In 'Problem on Balak' by Roger D. Aycock, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case on the mysterious planet of Balak. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With time running out, the detective must navigate the treacherous landscape of Balak to solve the problem at hand, all while facing personal demons that threaten to unravel his own sanity.

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Betrayal, Identity, Psychological suspense
