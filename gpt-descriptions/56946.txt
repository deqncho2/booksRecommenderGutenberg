Synopsis: In 'St Nicotine of the Peace Pipe' by Edward Vincent Heward, a mysterious figure named Nicotine emerges from the shadows, offering a unique solution to the world's problems. As his influence grows, society becomes divided between those who embrace his teachings and those who resist. Amidst this turmoil, a young woman named Lily finds herself drawn into Nicotine's world, questioning her own beliefs and the true nature of peace. With secrets and revelations lurking around every corner, 'St Nicotine of the Peace Pipe' is a gripping tale that explores the power of addiction, the complexities of faith, and the search for inner peace.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Addiction, Faith, Identity, Power
