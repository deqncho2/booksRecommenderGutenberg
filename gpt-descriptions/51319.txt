Synopsis: 
'Woodcraft Boys at Sunset Island' follows a group of adventurous boys who embark on a thrilling summer camping trip to Sunset Island. As they navigate through the wilderness, they encounter mysterious clues, hidden treasures, and unexpected challenges that test their survival skills and friendship. With each new discovery, the boys uncover secrets that lead them deeper into the heart of the island, where danger and excitement await. Will they be able to unravel the island's mysteries and make it back home safely?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Self-discovery
