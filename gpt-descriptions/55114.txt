Synopsis: In 'Teresa of Watling Street: A Fantasia on Modern Themes' by Arnold Bennett, we are introduced to Teresa, a young woman with an extraordinary talent for impersonation. As she navigates the bustling streets of London, Teresa finds herself entangled in a web of intrigue and deception, assuming various identities to survive and thrive in a world filled with secrets. With each transformation, she uncovers hidden truths about herself and those around her, leading to unexpected consequences that challenge her perception of identity and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Self-discovery
