Synopsis: In 'American Dangers and Duties', Mayo, A. D. takes readers on a thrilling journey through the underbelly of American society, where danger lurks around every corner. As the protagonist navigates a treacherous web of deceit, corruption, and power struggles, they must confront their own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape the future of their nation. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this book will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Political Fiction, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Power, Morality, Patriotism
