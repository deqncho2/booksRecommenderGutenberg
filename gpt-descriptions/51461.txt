Synopsis: In 'A Pail of Air' by Leiber, Fritz, a young boy named Tim and his family find themselves living in a frozen world after a cataclysmic event. With their home buried deep underground, they must rely on a pail of air to survive. As they struggle to adapt to their new reality, Tim embarks on a journey of discovery, encountering both the wonders and dangers of this frozen world. Will he find a way to bring warmth and life back to their existence?

Genres: Science Fiction, Post-Apocalyptic, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Family, Adaptation
