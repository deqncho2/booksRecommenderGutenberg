Synopsis: In 'A Son of Mars, volume 1' by Griffiths, Arthur, follow the thrilling journey of a young man named Marcus as he navigates the treacherous world of ancient Rome. Born into a humble family, Marcus dreams of becoming a renowned gladiator and earning his place among the gods. However, his path is filled with unexpected challenges, dangerous rivalries, and a forbidden love that could shatter everything he holds dear. As Marcus fights for his dreams and battles against the forces that seek to destroy him, readers will be captivated by the gripping tale of courage, honor, and the pursuit of destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Ambition, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
