Synopsis: 

'Only an Ensign: A Tale of the Retreat from Cabul, Volume 1' by Grant, James is a gripping historical novel set during the disastrous retreat of British forces from Afghanistan in the 19th century. The story follows the journey of a young ensign, who finds himself caught in the midst of a treacherous and harrowing retreat, facing danger, betrayal, and the ultimate test of his courage and loyalty. As he navigates through the treacherous terrain and encounters various characters, the ensign's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Betrayal, Survival
