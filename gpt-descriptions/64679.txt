Synopsis: In the desolate and frozen wasteland of Antarctica, a group of scientists make a chilling discovery buried deep within the ice. As they unearth a mysterious alien creature, they soon realize that it has the ability to perfectly mimic any living being, including themselves. Paranoia and fear grip the team as they struggle to determine who among them is human and who is the deadly imposter. With their lives hanging in the balance, they must confront their own darkest fears and fight to survive against an enemy that could be anyone.

Genres: Science Fiction, Horror, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Paranoia, Survival
