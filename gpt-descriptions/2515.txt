Synopsis:
"Stepping Heavenward" is a captivating coming-of-age novel that follows the journey of Katherine, a young woman navigating the challenges and joys of life in the 19th century. Through her diary entries, we witness her struggles with self-doubt, her pursuit of personal growth, and her unwavering faith in the face of adversity. As Katherine grapples with societal expectations, love, and loss, readers are drawn into her world, rooting for her as she strives to find her true purpose and step ever closer to heaven.

Genres:
Fiction, Historical Fiction, Christian Fiction

Themes:
Self-discovery, Faith, Personal Growth, Love and Relationships
