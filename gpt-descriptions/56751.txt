Synopsis: In 'The Jolly Book of Boxcraft' by Beard and Patten, embark on a whimsical journey into the world of boxcrafting, where ordinary cardboard boxes are transformed into extraordinary creations. From castles to spaceships, the possibilities are endless as the authors guide readers through step-by-step instructions and imaginative ideas. Discover the joy of unleashing your creativity and the magic that can be found within the simplest of materials.

Genres: Craft, Children's, Non-fiction

Themes: Creativity, Imagination, Resourcefulness
