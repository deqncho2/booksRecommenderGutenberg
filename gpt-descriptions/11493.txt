Synopsis: In 'The Blotting Book' by E. F. Benson, a seemingly ordinary family is thrown into turmoil when a mysterious blot appears on a crucial document. As the consequences of this blot unfold, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the characters are forced to confront their deepest fears and desires. With a blend of suspense, intrigue, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Deception, Family Dynamics, Consequences of Actions
