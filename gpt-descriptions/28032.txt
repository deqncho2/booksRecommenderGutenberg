Synopsis: In 'Sprays of Shamrock' by Clinton Scollard, a young Irish immigrant named Maeve finds herself torn between her loyalty to her homeland and her desire for a better life in America. As she navigates the challenges of assimilation and the complexities of love, Maeve's journey becomes a captivating tale of resilience, sacrifice, and the enduring power of heritage.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Immigration, Cultural Heritage, Love and Sacrifice
