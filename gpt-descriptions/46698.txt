Synopsis: In 'Some Noble Sisters' by Edmund Lee, secrets and scandals unravel in the lives of three sisters from a prominent family in 19th century England. As they navigate the complexities of love, duty, and societal expectations, their intertwined stories take unexpected turns, leading to shocking revelations that threaten to shatter their carefully constructed lives. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this captivating novel explores the bonds of sisterhood and the lengths one will go to protect their family's reputation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Family, Love, Society, Secrets
