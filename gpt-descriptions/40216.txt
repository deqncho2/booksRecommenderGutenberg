Synopsis: 
In 'The Patriarchs: Being Meditations upon Enoch, Noah, Abraham, Isaac, Jacob, Joseph, Job; The Canticles, Heaven and Earth,' J. G. Bellett takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of biblical figures and the profound lessons they teach us. From the mysterious Enoch to the faithful Abraham, from the trials of Job to the poetic beauty of the Canticles, this book delves into the depths of human experience and spirituality. With insightful meditations and thought-provoking reflections, Bellett invites readers to explore the timeless wisdom and divine truths found within these ancient stories.

Genres: Spirituality, Biblical Studies, Meditation

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Divine Providence, Human Experience
