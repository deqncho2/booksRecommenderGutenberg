Synopsis: In "The Struggle between President Johnson and Congress over Reconstruction," Charles E. Chadsey delves into the intense power struggle that unfolded during the Reconstruction era in the United States. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Chadsey explores the clash between President Andrew Johnson and Congress as they grapple with the complex task of rebuilding a nation torn apart by the Civil War. As the battle for control over Reconstruction policies escalates, readers are taken on a gripping journey through political maneuvering, ideological clashes, and the fight for racial equality. This captivating narrative sheds light on a pivotal period in American history, revealing the high stakes and profound consequences of this epic struggle.

Genres: History, Political Science, Biography

Themes: Power struggle, Reconstruction, Racial equality, Political ideology
