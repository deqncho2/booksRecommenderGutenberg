Synopsis: In 'Set in Silver', a mysterious inheritance leads a group of friends on a thrilling adventure through the English countryside. As they uncover hidden secrets and encounter supernatural forces, they must unravel the enigma of an ancient artifact before it falls into the wrong hands. With danger lurking at every turn, the friends must rely on their wit and courage to solve the puzzle and protect their lives. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Friendship, Inheritance, Secrets, Supernatural forces
