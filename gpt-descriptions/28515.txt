Synopsis: In 'The Saracen: Land of the Infidel', a young Saracen warrior named Khalid finds himself torn between loyalty to his people and his growing fascination with the culture and beliefs of the infidels he encounters during a perilous journey. As he navigates the treacherous landscapes of medieval Europe, Khalid's encounters with knights, scholars, and mystics challenge his preconceived notions and force him to question his own identity and purpose. With danger lurking at every turn, Khalid must make difficult choices that will shape not only his own destiny but also the fate of two worlds.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Cultural Clash, Loyalty, Self-Discovery
