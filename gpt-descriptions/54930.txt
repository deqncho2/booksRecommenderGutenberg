Synopsis: In 'Little Snap the Postboy; Or, Working for Uncle Sam', follow the thrilling adventures of Snap, a young postboy who finds himself caught up in a dangerous mission for the government. As he delivers letters and packages, Snap uncovers a web of intrigue and must use his wit and courage to navigate through perilous situations. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies ahead for Little Snap.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Perseverance
