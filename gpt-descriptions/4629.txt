Synopsis: 
'Home Scenes and Home Influence; a series of tales and sketches' by Arthur, T. S. (Timothy Shay) takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of heartwarming and thought-provoking stories set in the comfort of home. From tales of love and sacrifice to sketches of everyday life, this book offers a glimpse into the joys and challenges of family, friendship, and the power of home. Each story presents a unique perspective, drawing readers in with its relatable characters and engaging narratives. As the pages turn, readers will find themselves immersed in a world where the bonds of home shape and influence the lives of those within it.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Domestic Fiction

Themes: Family, Love, Sacrifice, Friendship, Home, Everyday Life
