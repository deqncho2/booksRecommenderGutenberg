Synopsis: In the second volume of "Secret History of the Court of England," Lady Anne Hamilton delves into the intriguing and scandalous events that unfolded within the royal court from the reign of George III to the death of George IV. Among the many captivating stories, Lady Hamilton uncovers the mysterious circumstances surrounding the death of Princess Charlotte, shedding light on a dark and enigmatic chapter in British history. With meticulous research and a keen eye for detail, the author unveils the hidden secrets, power struggles, and personal dramas that shaped the court during this tumultuous period.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political intrigue

Themes: Royal scandals, Power dynamics, Historical mysteries
