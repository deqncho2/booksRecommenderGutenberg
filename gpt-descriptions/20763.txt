Synopsis: 

In "Carpentry for Boys: In a Simple Language, Including Chapters on Drawing, Laying Out Work, Designing and Architecture With 250 Original Illustrations," James Slough Zerbe takes readers on an engaging journey into the world of carpentry. This comprehensive guide, filled with 250 original illustrations, offers a simple language that makes it accessible to boys of all ages. From learning the basics of drawing and laying out work to exploring the intricacies of designing and architecture, Zerbe provides a wealth of knowledge and practical advice for aspiring carpenters. 

Genres: Non-fiction, How-to, Crafts

Themes: Carpentry, Skill-building, Creativity
