Synopsis: In 'The Pudding and Pastry Book' by Elizabeth Douglas, a young aspiring baker named Emma discovers an old recipe book hidden in her grandmother's attic. As she delves into the pages filled with mouthwatering desserts and delectable pastries, Emma embarks on a culinary journey that not only teaches her the art of baking but also uncovers a family secret that has been kept hidden for generations. With each recipe she tries, Emma unravels the stories behind the desserts and finds herself connecting with her heritage in unexpected ways. As she navigates the challenges of mastering the recipes and uncovering the truth, Emma's passion for baking grows, leading her to discover her own unique voice in the world of pastry.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Culinary

Themes: Family secrets, Heritage and identity, Passion for baking
