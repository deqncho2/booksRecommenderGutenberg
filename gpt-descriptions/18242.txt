Synopsis: 
'Behind the Arras: A Book of the Unseen' by Carman, Bliss is a captivating collection of mysterious and supernatural tales that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. From haunted houses to eerie encounters, this book delves into the hidden world beyond our perception, exploring the unexplained and the inexplicable. Each story takes readers on a thrilling journey, unraveling secrets and uncovering the unseen forces that shape our lives. Prepare to be enthralled by the unknown as you delve into the pages of 'Behind the Arras: A Book of the Unseen'.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Thriller

Themes: The supernatural, the unknown, hidden forces, secrets
