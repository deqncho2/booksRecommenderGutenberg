Synopsis: 
'A Journal of the First Voyage of Vasco da Gama 1497-1499' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through uncharted waters, as the intrepid explorer Vasco da Gama sets sail to find a new route to India. Through vivid descriptions and captivating accounts, the journal chronicles the challenges, triumphs, and encounters with unknown lands and peoples, offering a thrilling glimpse into the age of exploration.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, travelogue, adventure

Themes: Exploration, discovery, cultural encounters, perseverance
