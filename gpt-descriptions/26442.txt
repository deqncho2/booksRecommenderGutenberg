Synopsis: In 'True to His Home: A Tale of the Boyhood of Franklin' by Hezekiah Butterworth, readers are transported to the vibrant world of young Benjamin Franklin. Set in colonial Boston, this captivating tale follows the adventures of Franklin as he navigates the challenges of growing up in a bustling city. From his encounters with pirates to his experiments with electricity, Franklin's insatiable curiosity and determination to make a difference shine through. As he learns valuable life lessons and discovers his true calling, readers will be captivated by the inspiring journey of this remarkable young boy.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Curiosity, Determination, Self-discovery
