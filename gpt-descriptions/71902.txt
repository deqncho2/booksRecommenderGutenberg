Synopsis: In "Captain Cranberry: A Cape Cod comedy in three acts," readers are transported to the charming coastal town of Cape Cod, where they meet the eccentric and lovable Captain Cranberry. As the captain navigates through hilarious misadventures and encounters with quirky locals, a series of unexpected events unfolds, leading to uproarious laughter and heartwarming moments. This delightful comedy takes readers on a journey filled with laughter, love, and the unique charm of Cape Cod.

Genres: Comedy, Fiction, Humor

Themes: Humor, Community, Adventure
