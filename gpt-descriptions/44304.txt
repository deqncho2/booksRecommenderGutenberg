Synopsis: In 'The Deserter, and Other Stories: A Book of Two Wars', Frederic, Harold takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous landscapes of two different wars. From the gripping tale of a soldier's desertion to the haunting experiences of those left behind, each story delves into the complexities of war and its profound impact on individuals. With vivid descriptions and compelling narratives, this collection explores the depths of human emotions, the struggle for survival, and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: War fiction, Historical fiction, Short stories

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Loyalty, Survival, Human resilience
