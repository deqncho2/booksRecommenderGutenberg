Synopsis: In 'The Children on the Top Floor' by Nina Rhoades, a mysterious family moves into a small town, taking up residence in the old, abandoned house on the top floor. As the townspeople become increasingly curious about the family's origins and their peculiar behavior, a young girl named Lily befriends the children from the top floor. Together, they embark on a thrilling adventure that uncovers dark secrets and hidden truths about the town and its inhabitants. With each twist and turn, the story delves into the power of friendship, the importance of acceptance, and the courage to confront the unknown.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Identity, Courage
