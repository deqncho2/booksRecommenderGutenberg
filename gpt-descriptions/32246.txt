Synopsis: 
'Adventures and Reminiscences of a Volunteer; Or, A Drummer Boy from Maine' follows the captivating journey of a young drummer boy from Maine who volunteers to fight in the Civil War. Through his eyes, we witness the harsh realities of war, the camaraderie among soldiers, and the personal growth that comes with facing adversity. From the battlefields to the campfires, this memoir takes readers on a thrilling and emotional ride, offering a unique perspective on one of the most tumultuous periods in American history.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Coming of Age, Patriotism
