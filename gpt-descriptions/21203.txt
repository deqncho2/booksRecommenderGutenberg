Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Grandfather Mole' by Bailey, Arthur Scott, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the underground world of Grandfather Mole. As he navigates through dark tunnels and encounters various creatures, Grandfather Mole's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. With unexpected twists and turns, this enchanting tale will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what lies ahead for Grandfather Mole.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Perseverance, Courage, Exploration
