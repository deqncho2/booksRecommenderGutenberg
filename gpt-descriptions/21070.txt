Synopsis: In 'A Middy of the Slave Squadron: A West African Story', young midshipman Jack Rogers finds himself on a thrilling adventure as he joins the British Royal Navy's mission to combat the illegal slave trade along the West African coast. As Jack navigates the treacherous waters and encounters the harsh realities of the slave trade, he must grapple with his own beliefs and values. With danger lurking at every turn, Jack's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Morality, Identity, Courage, Social Justice
