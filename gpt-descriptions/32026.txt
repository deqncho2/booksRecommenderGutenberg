Synopsis: In a world where science and religion clash, a young man named Brion must navigate the complexities of a society that denies the existence of a certain element. As he embarks on a journey to uncover the truth, he discovers a hidden world that challenges everything he thought he knew. With his beliefs and the fate of his people hanging in the balance, Brion must make a choice that could change the course of their civilization forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Philosophical

Themes: Reality vs. Perception, Faith and Belief, Societal Constructs
