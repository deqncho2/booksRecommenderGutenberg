Synopsis: In 'The Gentleman: A Romance of the Sea' by Alfred Ollivant, a young man named Dick is thrust into a world of adventure and danger when he joins a crew of rough sailors on a treacherous voyage. As he navigates the perils of the sea, Dick must confront his own inner demons and discover the true meaning of honor and loyalty. With vivid descriptions of life at sea and a captivating cast of characters, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Identity, Morality
