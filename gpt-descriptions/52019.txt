Synopsis: In 'Ellen Levis: A Novel' by Singmaster, Elsie, readers are transported to the early 20th century, where they follow the captivating journey of Ellen Levis. Born into a wealthy family, Ellen's life takes an unexpected turn when she falls in love with a man from a different social class. As she navigates the complexities of love, family expectations, and societal norms, Ellen must make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing world, this novel explores themes of love, class, and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love, Social Class, Personal Freedom
