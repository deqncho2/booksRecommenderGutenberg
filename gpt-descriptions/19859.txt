Synopsis: In 'A Flat Iron for a Farthing; or, Some Passages in the Life of an only Son', we follow the captivating journey of a young boy named Dickie, who finds himself in a series of unexpected and extraordinary situations. From his encounters with a mysterious old woman to his adventures in the bustling streets of London, Dickie's life takes a thrilling turn as he learns valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the power of determination. This heartwarming tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies ahead for our courageous protagonist.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Perseverance, Self-discovery
