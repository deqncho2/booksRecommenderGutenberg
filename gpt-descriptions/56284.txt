Synopsis: In 'The Legend of Kupirri, or, The Red Kangaroo: An Aboriginal Tradition of the Port Lincoln Tribe', author W. A. Cawthorne delves into the captivating world of Aboriginal folklore. Set in the Port Lincoln Tribe, this enchanting tale follows the extraordinary journey of Kupirri, a red kangaroo with a mysterious power. As Kupirri embarks on a quest to protect his tribe from a looming threat, he encounters a series of trials and encounters that test his courage and determination. With vivid descriptions and rich cultural insights, this book offers a mesmerizing glimpse into the ancient traditions and beliefs of the Aboriginal people.

Genres: Folklore, Indigenous Literature, Adventure

Themes: Cultural heritage, Spirituality, Heroism
