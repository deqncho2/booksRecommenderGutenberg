Synopsis: In the city of Lankhmar, a young sorcerer's apprentice named Ningauble seeks to prove his worth by embarking on a perilous quest. Alongside his cunning and enigmatic mentor, Ningauble must navigate treacherous streets, encounter strange creatures, and face the ultimate test of his magical abilities. As the duo delves deeper into the mysteries of Lankhmar, they uncover a dark secret that threatens to unravel the very fabric of reality itself.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Coming of age, Power and responsibility, The nature of magic
