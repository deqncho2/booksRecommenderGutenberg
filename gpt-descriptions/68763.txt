Synopsis: In 'A Modern Exodus: A Novel' by Violet Guttenberg, a group of strangers find themselves thrown together in a desperate journey to escape a world on the brink of collapse. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and face unimaginable challenges, they must confront their own fears, forge unlikely alliances, and discover the strength within themselves to survive. With each step, they unravel the mysteries of their pasts and uncover the shocking truth about the future they are fighting for. This gripping tale of resilience and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Dystopian, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Identity, Redemption
