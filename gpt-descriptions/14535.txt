Synopsis: In 'A Christmas Sermon' by Robert Louis Stevenson, a mysterious and thought-provoking sermon is delivered on a snowy Christmas Eve. The speaker challenges conventional beliefs and encourages listeners to question their own perspectives on life, love, and the true meaning of Christmas. As the sermon unfolds, it takes unexpected turns, leaving the audience captivated and eager to explore the profound ideas presented.

Genres: Fiction, Short Story, Philosophy

Themes: Faith and Doubt, Morality, Redemption, Self-Reflection
