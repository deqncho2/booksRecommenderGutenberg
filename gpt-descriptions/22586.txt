Synopsis: In 'History of the Second Massachusetts Regiment of Infantry: Beverly Ford,' author Daniel Oakey takes readers on a gripping journey through the experiences of the brave soldiers of the Second Massachusetts Regiment during the intense battles at Beverly Ford. As the regiment faces the harsh realities of war, Oakey skillfully weaves together personal accounts, vivid descriptions, and historical context to create a captivating narrative that immerses readers in the heart-pounding action and emotional turmoil of the battlefield. With its meticulous attention to detail and powerful storytelling, this book offers a compelling exploration of the sacrifices, camaraderie, and resilience of the soldiers who fought in one of the most significant conflicts of the Civil War.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, War memoir

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Resilience
