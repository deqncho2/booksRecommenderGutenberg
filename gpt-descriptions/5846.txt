Synopsis: 
In 'The Adventures of Poor Mrs. Quack', follow the delightful and mischievous Mrs. Quack as she embarks on a series of unexpected adventures. From encounters with cunning hunters to daring escapes from dangerous situations, Mrs. Quack's journey is filled with excitement, humor, and heartwarming moments. Will she be able to outsmart her adversaries and find her way back home? Join Mrs. Quack on her thrilling escapades and discover the surprises that await her at every turn.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Courage, Survival, Friendship
