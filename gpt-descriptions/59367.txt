Synopsis: 

'Guatemala and Her People of To-day' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant land of Guatemala, exploring its rich history, development, and the unique customs and characteristics of its people. The book also delves into the neighboring countries of British Honduras and the Republic of Honduras, providing a comprehensive overview of Central America as a whole. With vivid descriptions and insightful references, the author paints a vivid picture of the region, enticing readers to discover the beauty and diversity of these fascinating countries.

Genres: Travel, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Cultural identity, Historical development, Regional exploration
