Synopsis: In 'A Troublesome Flock: A Mother Goose Play for Children', Elizabeth F. Guptill takes readers on a whimsical journey through the beloved world of Mother Goose. When Mother Goose's flock of mischievous nursery rhyme characters goes missing, chaos ensues as she embarks on a hilarious adventure to find them. From Humpty Dumpty's daring escape to Little Bo Peep's sheepish antics, this play is filled with laughter, surprises, and heartwarming moments that will captivate both children and adults alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Comedy, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Problem-solving
