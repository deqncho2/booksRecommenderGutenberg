Synopsis: In "Old Mortality, Complete" by Walter Scott, a young man named Henry Morton finds himself caught in the midst of political and religious turmoil during the 17th-century Scottish Covenanters' rebellion. As he navigates the dangerous landscape of divided loyalties, Henry becomes entangled in a forbidden love affair and is forced to make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With vivid historical detail and gripping storytelling, this novel explores the complexities of faith, loyalty, and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Political and religious conflict, Forbidden love, Loyalty, Identity
