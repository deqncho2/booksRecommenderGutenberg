Synopsis: In "The Lost Child" by Henry Kingsley, a young boy named Gilbert wanders into the enchanting Australian bush, captivated by its beauty and mystery. As he explores this unfamiliar world, he encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets and desires. Gilbert's journey becomes a transformative experience, filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. This gripping tale explores the power of nature, the complexities of human relationships, and the profound impact of self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Nature's allure, Identity and self-discovery, Human connection
