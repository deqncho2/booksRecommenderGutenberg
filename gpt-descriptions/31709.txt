Synopsis: 
In 'Our Southern Highlanders', Horace Kephart takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged and mysterious Appalachian Mountains. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Kephart introduces us to the unique culture, customs, and challenges faced by the resilient inhabitants of this remote region. From moonshiners and feuds to the beauty of the natural landscape, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a world that is both enchanting and harsh. As Kephart immerses himself in the lives of the Southern Highlanders, he uncovers a rich tapestry of stories and experiences that will leave readers eager to explore this captivating corner of America.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travelogue, Cultural Studies

Themes: Appalachian culture, Survival, Nature, Isolation, Resilience
