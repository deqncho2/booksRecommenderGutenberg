Synopsis: 
In 'Blackie, a Lost Cat: Her Many Adventures' by Richard Barnum, follow the captivating journey of Blackie, a curious and adventurous feline who finds herself lost in a bustling city. From encountering a mischievous gang of alley cats to befriending a kind-hearted homeless man, Blackie's escapades take her on a whirlwind of thrilling and heartwarming adventures. As she navigates through the challenges of survival and searches for a place to call home, Blackie's resilience and determination will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: 
Resilience, Friendship, Homelessness
