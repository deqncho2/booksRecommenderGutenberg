Synopsis: In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 98, January 25th, 1890', readers are transported to the vibrant and satirical world of Victorian England. This edition of the renowned magazine offers a collection of witty and humorous articles, cartoons, and social commentaries that provide a fascinating glimpse into the society of the time. From political satire to cultural observations, this volume promises to entertain and enlighten readers with its clever insights and cleverly crafted humor.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Social Commentary

Themes: Victorian society, Politics, Cultural observations
