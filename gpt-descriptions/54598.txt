Synopsis: In 'The Crimson Sign: A Narrative of the Adventures of Mr. Gervase Orme, Sometime Lieutenant in Mountjoy's Regiment of Foot', readers are transported to the tumultuous times of the English Civil War. The story follows the daring exploits of Mr. Gervase Orme, a former lieutenant in the army, as he navigates treacherous landscapes, encounters mysterious characters, and becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. As Orme's journey unfolds, secrets are revealed, loyalties are tested, and the true nature of courage is explored. Will Orme be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead, or will he succumb to the perils that surround him?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Courage, Intrigue
