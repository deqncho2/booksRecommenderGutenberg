Synopsis: 
'Myths That Every Child Should Know: A Selection Of The Classic Myths Of All Times For Young People' is a captivating collection of timeless myths from various cultures around the world. From the enchanting tales of Greek gods and goddesses to the heroic adventures of legendary figures, this book introduces young readers to the magical realm of mythology. Each myth is beautifully retold, transporting readers to ancient times filled with gods, monsters, and epic quests. With its engaging storytelling and vivid imagery, this collection is sure to ignite the imagination and curiosity of both children and adults alike.

Genres: Mythology, Children's Literature, Folklore

Themes: Heroism, Morality, Legends and Folklore
