Synopsis: In "Miss Muffet's Christmas Party" by Samuel McChord Crothers, a group of beloved nursery rhyme characters come together for a festive holiday celebration. As the night unfolds, unexpected events and delightful surprises ensue, bringing joy and laughter to all. This heartwarming tale captures the magic of Christmas and the power of friendship, leaving readers eagerly anticipating the outcome.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Holiday

Themes: Friendship, Celebration, Holiday Spirit
