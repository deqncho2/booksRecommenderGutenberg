Synopsis: In 'Aide Memoire' by Keith Laumer, a former intelligence agent finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger when he is unexpectedly thrust back into the world of espionage. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of double-crosses, hidden agendas, and deadly secrets. With his life on the line, he must rely on his wits and skills to survive and bring justice to those responsible. 

Genres: Thriller, Espionage, Mystery

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Intrigue
