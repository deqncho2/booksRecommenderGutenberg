Synopsis: In 'The Voice in Singing' by Emma Seiler, a young and talented singer named Lily discovers a mysterious power hidden within her voice. As she delves deeper into her gift, she uncovers a world of ancient secrets and hidden dangers. With the help of a enigmatic mentor, Lily must navigate the treacherous path of her newfound abilities while facing personal challenges and unexpected adversaries. This captivating tale of self-discovery and the power of music will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final note.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Self-discovery, Music, Power
