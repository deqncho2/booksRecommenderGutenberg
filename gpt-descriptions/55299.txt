Synopsis: 
In 'The Forum, October 1914', a group of diverse individuals find themselves unexpectedly brought together in the midst of a tumultuous time. As the world is on the brink of war, their lives intertwine in a captivating tale of love, loss, and the pursuit of truth. Secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the characters are forced to confront their deepest fears and desires. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, this gripping narrative explores the complexities of human nature and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and relationships, Identity and self-discovery, War and its impact, Secrets and deception
