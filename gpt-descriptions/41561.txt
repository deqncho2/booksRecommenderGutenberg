Synopsis: 

In 'Picturesque Pala: The Story of the Mission Chapel of San Antonio de Padua Connected with Mission San Luis Rey', George Wharton James takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and beauty of the Mission Chapel of San Antonio de Padua. With vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, James explores the significance of this mission chapel and its connection to Mission San Luis Rey. From the architectural marvels to the spiritual significance, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the rich heritage of Pala.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Architecture

Themes: History, Heritage, Spirituality
