Synopsis: In 'The Hermit Of ——— Street: 1898' by Anna Katharine Green, a mysterious recluse living on a quiet street becomes the center of attention when a series of strange events occur in his vicinity. As the townspeople grow increasingly curious about the hermit's past and the secrets he holds, a determined young woman sets out to uncover the truth. With each revelation, the line between reality and illusion blurs, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery
