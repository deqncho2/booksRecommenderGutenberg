Synopsis: 

In 'For Love of Country: A Story of Land and Sea in the Days of the Revolution', Cyrus Townsend Brady takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. Set against the backdrop of the Revolutionary War, this captivating tale follows the lives of two young men, one a patriot and the other a loyalist, as they navigate the treacherous waters of love, loyalty, and sacrifice. As the war rages on, their paths intertwine in unexpected ways, testing their convictions and forcing them to make difficult choices that will shape their destinies forever. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Brady paints a vivid picture of the challenges faced by those who fought for their beliefs during this pivotal period in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Love, Sacrifice
