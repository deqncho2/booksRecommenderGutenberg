Synopsis: In 'Missing at Marshlands: Arden Blake Mystery Series #3' by Cleo F. Garis, a small town is shaken when a prominent local resident mysteriously disappears. As amateur detective Arden Blake delves into the case, she uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that leads her to question everyone she thought she knew. With time running out, Arden must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and red herrings to uncover the truth and bring the missing person home. Will she be able to solve the puzzle before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town intrigue, Amateur detective
