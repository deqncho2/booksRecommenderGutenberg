Synopsis: In "Mary of Plymouth: A Story of the Pilgrim Settlement," James Otis takes readers on a captivating journey to the early days of the Pilgrim settlement in Plymouth. Through the eyes of young Mary, a courageous and determined girl, we witness the challenges, triumphs, and heartaches faced by the Pilgrims as they strive to build a new life in the New World. From encounters with Native Americans to the harsh realities of survival, Mary's story is a testament to the resilience and spirit of those who paved the way for future generations.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Perseverance, Cultural Encounter, Identity, Community
