Synopsis: 

'North Italian Folk: Sketches of Town and Country Life' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and enchanting world of North Italy. Through a series of vivid sketches, author Alice Vansittart Strettel Carr introduces us to a diverse cast of characters, from humble villagers to aristocratic nobles, each with their own unique stories and experiences. From the bustling streets of Milan to the tranquil countryside, Carr's evocative descriptions and keen observations paint a rich tapestry of life in this captivating region. 

Genres: Travelogue, Cultural Studies, Anthropology

Themes: Italian culture, Community, Social dynamics
