Synopsis: In 'A Dissertation upon Roast Pig', Charles Lamb takes us on a delightful culinary journey that explores the unexpected origins of a beloved dish. Through a whimsical and humorous narrative, Lamb uncovers the accidental discovery of roast pig and its profound impact on society. From a disastrous house fire to a mouthwatering feast, this essay unveils the surprising and mouthwatering history behind this delectable delicacy.

Genres: Essay, Satire, Humor

Themes: Food, Tradition, Serendipity
