Synopsis: In "One Hundred Proofs That the Earth Is Not a Globe," William Carpenter presents a thought-provoking exploration that challenges the widely accepted notion of a spherical Earth. Through a meticulous examination of various phenomena and scientific principles, Carpenter presents a compelling case that defies conventional wisdom. As he delves into topics such as gravity, perspective, and the curvature of the Earth, readers will find themselves questioning long-held beliefs and embarking on a journey of intellectual discovery. This captivating work invites readers to reconsider their understanding of the world we inhabit and invites them to explore alternative perspectives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Philosophy

Themes: Perception, Scientific inquiry, Paradigm shifts
