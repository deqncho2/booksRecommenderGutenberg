Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Worlds Within Worlds: The Story of Nuclear Energy', Isaac Asimov delves deeper into the fascinating world of atomic science. Exploring the concepts of mass and energy, the enigmatic neutron, and the intricate structure of the nucleus, Asimov takes readers on a captivating journey through the fundamental building blocks of the universe. With his signature clarity and enthusiasm, he unravels the mysteries of nuclear physics, shedding light on the incredible power and potential of atomic energy.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Nuclear physics, Energy, Scientific discovery
