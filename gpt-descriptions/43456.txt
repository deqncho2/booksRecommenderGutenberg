Synopsis: 

'Intimate China: The Chinese as I Have Seen Them' takes readers on a captivating journey through the author's personal experiences and encounters with the Chinese people. Mrs. Archibald Little provides a unique and intimate perspective on the Chinese culture, customs, and way of life, offering a vivid portrayal of the country and its people. From her observations of daily life in China to her interactions with individuals from various social backgrounds, the author paints a rich and nuanced picture of this fascinating nation. Through her engaging storytelling, readers will be transported to the heart of China, gaining a deeper understanding of its people and their traditions.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Cultural Studies

Themes: Chinese culture, Cross-cultural experiences, Personal observations, Social dynamics
