Synopsis: 
In 'The Banner Boy Scouts on a Tour; or, The Mystery of Rattlesnake Mountain' by George A. Warren, a group of adventurous boy scouts embarks on an exciting tour filled with unexpected twists and turns. As they explore the rugged terrain of Rattlesnake Mountain, they stumble upon a mysterious secret that puts their courage and resourcefulness to the test. With danger lurking in the shadows, the scouts must rely on their teamwork and determination to unravel the enigma and protect their fellow scouts. Will they be able to solve the mystery and emerge victorious from this thrilling adventure?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Teamwork, Exploration
