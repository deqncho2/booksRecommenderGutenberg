Synopsis: In 'The Bradys and the Girl Smuggler; Or, Working for the Custom House', the Brady family finds themselves caught up in a dangerous world of smuggling and intrigue. When a mysterious girl arrives in town, the Bradys are determined to uncover the truth behind her secretive past. As they delve deeper into the case, they become entangled in a web of deception and danger, risking everything to bring the truth to light. With thrilling twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Crime

Themes: Family, Justice, Loyalty
