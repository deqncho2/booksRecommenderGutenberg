Synopsis: In "Cosmic Symbolism," Sepharial delves into the enigmatic world of the Kabalists, exploring the profound connections between sound, form, and number. Through a captivating discussion and exposition, the author unravels the mysteries of cosmic symbolism, shedding light on the hidden principles that govern our universe. Prepare to embark on a journey of discovery, as Sepharial unveils the recondite and obscure points that lie at the heart of this ancient art.

Genres: Non-fiction, Esoteric, Occult

Themes: Symbolism, Mysticism, Numerology, Spiritualism
