Synopsis: In 'Voices from the Past' by Bartlett, Paul Alexander, a young archaeologist stumbles upon a hidden chamber in an ancient Egyptian tomb, unlocking a series of mysterious voices from the past. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the tomb, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between reality and the supernatural blurs. With each voice she hears, she uncovers a new piece of the puzzle, leading her on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the tomb's enigmatic past.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Archaeology, Ancient Egypt, Secrets and Mysteries, Supernatural Phenomena
