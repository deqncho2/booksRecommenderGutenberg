Synopsis: In 'Gerda in Sweden' by McDonald, Etta Blaisdell, follow the captivating journey of Gerda, a young girl who finds herself in a foreign land filled with mystery and adventure. As she navigates the enchanting landscapes of Sweden, Gerda encounters unexpected friendships, uncovers hidden secrets, and embarks on a quest that will change her life forever. With each turn of the page, readers will be drawn into Gerda's world, eagerly anticipating what lies ahead in this captivating tale.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Discovery, Self-discovery
