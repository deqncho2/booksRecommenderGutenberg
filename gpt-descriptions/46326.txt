Synopsis: 

'Brick and Marble in the Middle Ages: Notes of Tours in the North of Italy' takes readers on a captivating journey through the architectural wonders of medieval Italy. Written by George Edmund Street, this travelogue offers a vivid and immersive exploration of the region's rich history and stunning structures. From towering cathedrals to intricate palaces, Street's meticulous observations and insightful commentary bring these ancient marvels to life. As he delves into the intricate details of each building, the author uncovers hidden stories and secrets that have shaped the landscape of Italy. 

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Architecture, History, Cultural Heritage
