Synopsis: In "What Does History Teach?: Two Edinburgh Lectures," John Stuart Blackie takes his audience on a captivating journey through the annals of history. With his characteristic wit and wisdom, Blackie explores the lessons that can be gleaned from the past, shedding light on the triumphs and failures of civilizations, and offering thought-provoking insights into the nature of progress and human nature itself. Through engaging anecdotes and profound observations, Blackie challenges conventional wisdom and invites readers to reflect on the timeless wisdom that history has to offer.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Philosophy

Themes: Lessons from history, Progress, Human nature
