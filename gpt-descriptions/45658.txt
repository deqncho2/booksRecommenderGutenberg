Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Downs, a series of mysterious events unfold, leaving the residents puzzled and intrigued. When a local fisherman goes missing under strange circumstances, two unlikely companions, a retired detective and a curious young journalist, team up to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic occurrences. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the town, they find themselves entangled in a web of deception, hidden motives, and long-buried secrets. With each clue they uncover, the mystery deepens, leading them to question everything they thought they knew about Downs and its inhabitants. Will they be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town intrigue, Uncovering the truth
