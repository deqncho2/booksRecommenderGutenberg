Synopsis: In 'Kitty Alone: A Story of Three Fires', the second volume of the thrilling trilogy by S. Baring-Gould, the captivating tale of Kitty Alone continues. Set against the backdrop of the tumultuous times of the English Civil War, Kitty finds herself caught in a web of danger and intrigue as she navigates through the chaos of war and the complexities of love. As fires rage and secrets unravel, Kitty's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will she be able to survive the flames that threaten to consume her world?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Resilience and Survival, War and Conflict
