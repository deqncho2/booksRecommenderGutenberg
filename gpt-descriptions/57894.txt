Synopsis: 
In 'How to Do Mechanical Tricks: Containing Complete Instruction for Performing Over Sixty Ingenious Mechanical Tricks', Anderson, a master of mechanical illusions, unveils a captivating collection of over sixty mind-boggling tricks. From levitating objects to disappearing acts, this book provides step-by-step instructions on how to perform these mesmerizing mechanical feats. As readers delve into the world of mechanical magic, they will be enthralled by the intricate mechanisms and clever techniques behind each trick. With detailed illustrations and clear explanations, Anderson invites readers to unlock the secrets of these ingenious mechanical wonders and become masters of illusion themselves.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Hobbies & Crafts, Magic

Themes: 
Ingenuity, Illusion, Mastery
