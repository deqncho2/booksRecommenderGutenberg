Synopsis: In 'Picturesque London' by Fitzgerald, Percy, a young artist named Edward embarks on a journey through the vibrant streets of London, capturing the city's essence through his sketches. As he delves deeper into the heart of the metropolis, he encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own stories to tell. From the bustling markets to the hidden alleyways, Edward's artistic vision unveils the hidden beauty and secrets of London, leading him on a captivating exploration of the city's soul.

Genres: Fiction, Historical, Art

Themes: Artistic expression, Urban exploration, Human connection
