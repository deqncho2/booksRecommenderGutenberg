Synopsis: 
In 'Six Women' by Victoria Cross, a group of seemingly ordinary women find themselves unexpectedly connected through a series of mysterious events. As their lives intertwine, secrets are unveiled, alliances are formed, and the true nature of their individual strengths is revealed. With each woman facing her own personal challenges, they must navigate through a web of intrigue and danger, ultimately discovering the power of unity and resilience. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic bond that binds these six women together.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Female empowerment, Secrets and revelations, Resilience and strength
