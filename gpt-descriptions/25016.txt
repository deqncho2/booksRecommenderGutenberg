Synopsis: In the remote Welsh countryside, a young man stumbles upon an ancient house with a dark and mysterious past. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the house, he becomes entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and encounters with otherworldly beings. With each revelation, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leading him to question his own sanity and the true nature of the house.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, supernatural

Themes: The duality of human nature, the power of the subconscious mind, the thin veil between the seen and unseen, the allure of the unknown
