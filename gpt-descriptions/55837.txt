Synopsis: In 'The Rainbow Bridge' by Frances Margaret Fox, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her mysterious past. As she delves deeper into her family history, she discovers a hidden world filled with secrets, magic, and unexpected connections. With each revelation, Emily's perception of reality is challenged, leading her on a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and reshape her understanding of herself and the world around her.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Family, Discovery
