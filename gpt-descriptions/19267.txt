Synopsis: 

'Century of Light' by Bahá'í International Community is a captivating exploration of the transformative power of religion and its impact on society throughout the 20th century. Through a thought-provoking narrative, the book delves into the struggles, triumphs, and profound changes that have shaped the world over the course of a hundred years. From the rise of new spiritual movements to the challenges faced by established religions, 'Century of Light' offers a compelling journey through the evolution of faith and its role in shaping our collective destiny.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Religion

Themes: Religion and spirituality, Social change, Global transformation
