Synopsis: In 'A Simpleton' by Charles Reade, a young man named John discovers a hidden talent that sets him on a remarkable journey. As he navigates through various challenges and encounters intriguing characters, John's simple nature becomes his greatest strength. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the power of innocence and the triumph of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Innocence, Self-discovery, Resilience
