Synopsis: In 'The Enchiridion', Epictetus, a Stoic philosopher, offers a concise and practical guide to living a virtuous life. Through a series of insightful and thought-provoking teachings, he explores the nature of human existence, the importance of self-discipline, and the power of our own perceptions. With a focus on personal responsibility and the pursuit of inner tranquility, Epictetus provides invaluable wisdom that transcends time and remains relevant to this day.

Genres: Philosophy, Self-help, Classics

Themes: Stoicism, Virtue, Self-discipline, Perception, Personal responsibility
