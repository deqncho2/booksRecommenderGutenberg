Synopsis: In 'The Problem Makers' by Robert Hoskins, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious and disruptive events that leave the community in chaos. As the townspeople struggle to maintain order, a group of unlikely heroes emerges to uncover the truth behind these disturbances. With a blend of humor, suspense, and unexpected twists, this thrilling novel takes readers on a wild ride as they join the characters in their quest to restore peace to their town.

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Adventure

Themes: Community, Heroism, Uncovering the truth
