Synopsis: In 'A People's Man' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a charismatic and enigmatic man named Richard Storm enters the lives of the wealthy and influential, captivating them with his charm and mysterious past. As Storm's true intentions remain hidden, a web of secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances unfolds, leaving the reader questioning who can be trusted and what lies beneath the surface of this captivating character. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Power, Identity
