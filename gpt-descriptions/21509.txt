Synopsis: In "Rowena & Harold: A Romance in Rhyme of an Olden Time, of Hastyngs and Normanhurst," William Stephen Pryer takes readers on a captivating journey back to a bygone era. Set in the enchanting world of Hastyngs and Normanhurst, this poetic romance follows the intertwined lives of Rowena and Harold. As their paths cross and their destinies become entwined, their love story unfolds against a backdrop of medieval castles, chivalry, and courtly intrigue. With lyrical verses and vivid imagery, Pryer paints a vivid picture of a time long past, where love and honor are put to the ultimate test. 

Genres: Poetry, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love and Romance, Honor and Chivalry, Medieval Life and Society
