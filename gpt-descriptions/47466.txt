Synopsis: 

In "Edinburgh: Painted by John Fulleylove; described by Rosaline Masson," readers are transported to the enchanting city of Edinburgh through the vivid descriptions of Rosaline Masson and the beautiful paintings of John Fulleylove. From the bustling streets of the Old Town to the grandeur of the Edinburgh Castle, this book captures the essence and charm of the Scottish capital. Masson's lyrical prose and Fulleylove's stunning artwork bring to life the rich history, architectural wonders, and natural beauty of Edinburgh, making it a must-read for anyone who has ever been captivated by this remarkable city.

Genres: Travel, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: City exploration, Scottish history, Architectural beauty
