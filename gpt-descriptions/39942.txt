Synopsis: In "Botticelli" by Henry Bryan Binns, a young art historian stumbles upon a mysterious painting that leads her on a captivating journey through Renaissance Italy. As she delves deeper into the secrets behind the artwork, she uncovers a web of intrigue, betrayal, and forbidden love that spans centuries. With each revelation, she becomes more entangled in the enigmatic world of Sandro Botticelli, the renowned artist whose masterpieces hold the key to a hidden truth. As she races against time to unravel the mystery, she must confront her own past and make choices that will forever change her life.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Art and creativity, the power of secrets, the enduring impact of history
