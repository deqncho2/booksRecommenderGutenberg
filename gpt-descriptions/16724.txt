Synopsis:
'The Campaign of 1760 in Canada: A Narrative Attributed to Chevalier Johnstone' is a gripping historical account that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the events of the campaign in Canada during the year 1760. The narrative, attributed to Chevalier Johnstone, offers a firsthand perspective on the military strategies, battles, and political intrigues that unfolded during this pivotal period. As the story unfolds, readers will be captivated by the bravery and cunning of the soldiers, the challenges they faced, and the unexpected twists and turns that shaped the outcome of the campaign.

Genres:
Historical fiction, Military history, Adventure

Themes:
War and conflict, Leadership and strategy, Loyalty and betrayal
