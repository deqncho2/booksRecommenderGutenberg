Synopsis: In 'The Red House Mystery: The Piccadilly Novels' by Duchess, a charming and enigmatic detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing murder case when a wealthy man is found dead in his country estate. As the investigation unfolds, secrets, motives, and unexpected twists emerge, keeping readers on the edge of their seats. With a cast of intriguing characters and a cleverly crafted plot, this gripping mystery takes readers on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the Red House murder.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Justice, Secrets
