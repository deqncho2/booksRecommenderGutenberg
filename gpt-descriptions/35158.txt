Synopsis: In 'Verdi: The Story of the Little Boy who Loved the Hand Organ' by Tapper, Thomas, readers are transported to the vibrant streets of Italy, where a young boy named Verdi discovers a deep passion for music. As Verdi's love for the hand organ grows, he embarks on a remarkable journey filled with unexpected challenges and heartwarming encounters. This enchanting tale explores the power of music to inspire, connect, and transform lives, leaving readers captivated by Verdi's extraordinary journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Children's Literature, Historical Fiction, Musical

Themes: Passion for Music, Self-Discovery, Resilience
