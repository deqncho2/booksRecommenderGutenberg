Synopsis:
In 'Disenchantment' by Montague, C. E., a young woman named Alice finds herself trapped in a world where magic and reality collide. As she navigates through a mysterious realm filled with enchanting creatures and dangerous secrets, Alice must uncover the truth about her own identity and the dark forces that threaten to consume her. With the help of unlikely allies and her own inner strength, she embarks on a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and challenge everything she thought she knew.

Genres:
Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes:
Identity, Power, Redemption
