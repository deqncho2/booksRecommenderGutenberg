Synopsis: In 'Oklahoma Sunshine' by Miller, Freeman Edwin, a young woman named Lily embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's mysterious past. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the sun-soaked landscapes of Oklahoma, she discovers a web of intrigue, love, and betrayal that will challenge everything she thought she knew. With each revelation, Lily's determination grows, leading her on a path that will forever change her life and the lives of those around her.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Redemption
