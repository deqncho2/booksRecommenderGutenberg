Synopsis: In 'Under the Liberty Tree: A Story of The 'Boston Massacre' by James Otis, readers are transported to the tumultuous streets of Boston in the 1770s. As tensions rise between the American colonists and the British soldiers stationed in the city, a young boy named Johnny finds himself caught in the middle of the brewing conflict. Through Johnny's eyes, we witness the events leading up to the infamous Boston Massacre and the profound impact it has on the people of Boston. With danger lurking around every corner, Johnny must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and fight for justice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Courage, Justice
