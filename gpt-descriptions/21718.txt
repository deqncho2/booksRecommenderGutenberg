Synopsis: In 'The Big Otter' by R. M. Ballantyne, a young boy named Ralph finds himself on a thrilling adventure when he encounters a mysterious creature in the Scottish Highlands. As Ralph delves deeper into the secrets of the otter, he uncovers a hidden world filled with danger, friendship, and unexpected discoveries. With each twist and turn, Ralph's courage and determination are put to the test, leading him to uncover the truth behind the big otter and its significance to his own life.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Nature's wonders
