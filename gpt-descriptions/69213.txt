Synopsis: In 'The 13th Juror' by Leslie Waltham, a high-profile murder trial takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious woman appears claiming to be the 13th juror. As the trial unfolds, the woman's presence begins to unravel the truth behind the crime, leading to shocking revelations and a race against time to uncover the real culprit. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping legal thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Justice, Truth, Deception
