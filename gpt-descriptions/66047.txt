Synopsis: In "The Continent of the Future: Africa and Its Wonderful Development: Exploration, Gold Mining, Trade, Missions and Elevation," author Coppinger takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse and vibrant continent of Africa. From the unexplored territories to the bustling gold mines, the book delves into the rich history, remarkable trade routes, and the transformative power of missions in Africa. With vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, Coppinger unveils the untold stories of exploration, the allure of gold, the intricacies of trade, and the profound impact of missions on the continent. This book is a captivating exploration of Africa's past, present, and future, offering readers a deeper understanding of its remarkable development.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Exploration

Themes: African history, Exploration, Trade, Missions, Development
