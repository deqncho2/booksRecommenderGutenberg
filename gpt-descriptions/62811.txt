Synopsis: 

'Our Western Hills: How to reach them; And the Views from their Summits: By a Glasgow Pedestrian' takes readers on a captivating journey through the breathtaking landscapes of the Western Hills surrounding Glasgow. Written by an anonymous author, this guidebook not only provides detailed instructions on how to reach these majestic summits but also offers vivid descriptions of the awe-inspiring views that await those who venture to the top. From hidden trails to hidden gems, this book is a treasure trove of knowledge for both seasoned hikers and curious explorers alike.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Adventure

Themes: Nature exploration, Outdoor activities, Scottish landscapes
