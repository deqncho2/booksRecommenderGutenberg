Synopsis: In 'Common Denominator' by John D. MacDonald, a renowned mathematician finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is approached by a beautiful woman with a perplexing problem. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he discovers that the solution lies not only in numbers but also in the dark corners of human nature. With his intellect and intuition put to the test, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it consumes him.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Mathematics, Deception, Human nature
