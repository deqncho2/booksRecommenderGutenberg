Synopsis: 
In "Eminent Victorians," Lytton Strachey presents a captivating and unconventional exploration of four influential figures from the Victorian era. Through his witty and incisive prose, Strachey delves into the lives of Cardinal Manning, Florence Nightingale, Thomas Arnold, and General Gordon, shedding light on their achievements, contradictions, and the societal forces that shaped them. From the religious fervor of Manning to the relentless determination of Nightingale, Strachey's biographical sketches offer a fresh perspective on these iconic individuals, challenging the traditional notions of heroism and virtue.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Heroism, Morality, Social Change, Victorian Society
