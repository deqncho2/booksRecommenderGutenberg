Synopsis:
In the glamorous and mysterious world of late 19th-century Paris, two sisters find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and desires. As maids in the opulent mansions of the city's elite, they witness the hidden lives of their employers, while harboring their own forbidden dreams. But when a shocking event occurs, their lives take an unexpected turn, leading them down a dangerous path of deception and betrayal. As they navigate the treacherous waters of love, power, and identity, the sisters must confront the dark underbelly of paradise and make choices that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Class struggle, Identity, Forbidden desires, Deception
