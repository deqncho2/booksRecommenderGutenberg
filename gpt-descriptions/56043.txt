Synopsis: 
In the third edition of "Rock-climbing in the English Lake District," Owen Glynne Jones takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the stunning landscapes and challenging rock faces of the Lake District. With detailed descriptions, expert advice, and captivating anecdotes, this guidebook offers a comprehensive and thrilling exploration of the region's most iconic climbing routes. From the towering crags of Scafell Pike to the hidden gems of Borrowdale, readers will discover the sheer beauty and adrenaline-pumping excitement of rock-climbing in this breathtaking part of England.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Travel, Sports

Themes: 
Adventure, Nature, Skill development
