Synopsis: 
In 'Daughters of the Puritans: A Group of Brief Biographies', Seth Curtis Beach delves into the lives of remarkable women who defied societal norms and left an indelible mark on American history. From the courageous abolitionist Harriet Beecher Stowe to the pioneering educator Mary Lyon, these biographies offer a captivating glimpse into the lives of these extraordinary women. Beach's vivid storytelling and meticulous research bring to life the struggles, triumphs, and enduring legacies of these daughters of the Puritans. 

Genres: Biography, History, Women's Studies

Themes: Feminism, Activism, Education, Religion
