Synopsis: In 'Sacred Mysteries Among the Mayas and the Quiches, 11,500 Years Ago', Augustus Le Plongeon takes readers on a captivating journey back in time to explore the ancient sacred mysteries of the Mayas and the Quiches. Drawing intriguing connections to the sacred mysteries of Egypt, Greece, Chaldea, and India, Le Plongeon delves into the enigmatic world of Free Masonry in times anterior to the Temple of Solomon. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, he unveils a hidden history that challenges conventional beliefs and offers a fresh perspective on the ancient civilizations of the Americas and beyond.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Archaeology, Ancient Mysteries

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Sacred mysteries, Cross-cultural connections, Alternative history
