Synopsis:
In the dark and treacherous alleys of 17th century Venice, a tale of love, betrayal, and political intrigue unfolds. Jaffier, a nobleman torn between loyalty and his heart's desires, finds himself entangled in a dangerous plot against the ruling class. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the fate of Venice hangs in the balance, and Jaffier must make a choice that will forever alter his life and the lives of those he holds dear.

Genres:
Tragedy, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty and Betrayal, Power and Corruption
