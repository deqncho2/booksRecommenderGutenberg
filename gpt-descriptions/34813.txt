Synopsis: In 'Caravans By Night: A Romance of India' by Harry Hervey, a young American woman finds herself captivated by the exotic allure of India during the early 20th century. Drawn into a world of opulent palaces, vibrant bazaars, and mysterious caravans, she embarks on a journey that will test her courage, challenge her beliefs, and ultimately lead her to discover a love she never thought possible. As she navigates the complexities of Indian society and encounters a cast of unforgettable characters, she must confront her own desires and confront the clash between tradition and modernity. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Cultural exploration, Love and desire, Identity and self-discovery
