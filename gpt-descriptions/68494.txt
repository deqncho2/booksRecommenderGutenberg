Synopsis: Embark on a breathtaking journey through the awe-inspiring Shasta route, as Southern Pacific Company's guide book takes you from the vibrant city of San Francisco, California, to the enchanting landscapes of Portland, Oregon. Discover the hidden gems and natural wonders that await you along this road of a thousand wonders, as you traverse majestic mountains, traverse sparkling rivers, and witness the beauty of untouched wilderness. Immerse yourself in the rich history, captivating stories, and fascinating anecdotes that bring this scenic route to life, making it an unforgettable adventure for all who dare to explore its grandeur.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Adventure

Themes: Natural beauty, History, Exploration
