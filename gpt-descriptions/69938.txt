Synopsis: In "Told in the Twilight," F. E. Weatherly takes readers on a captivating journey through a world where reality and imagination intertwine. As the sun sets and darkness falls, a mysterious storyteller emerges, weaving enchanting tales that blur the line between dreams and reality. From magical creatures to haunting mysteries, each story holds a hidden truth that will leave readers questioning what is real and what lies in the shadows. Prepare to be spellbound as you delve into the twilight and discover the secrets that lie within.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Imagination, Perception, Truth, Mystery
