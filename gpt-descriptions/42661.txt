Synopsis: In 'The Golden Bough: A Study in Magic and Religion', James George Frazer takes readers on a captivating journey through the realms of ancient beliefs and rituals. This third edition, Volume 09 of 12, delves into the fascinating world of magic and religion, exploring the intricate connections between myth, folklore, and the human psyche. Frazer's meticulous research and compelling storytelling unveil the hidden threads that bind diverse cultures and civilizations together, shedding light on the universal human quest for meaning and transcendence. From ancient rites to modern superstitions, this volume offers a thought-provoking exploration of the mystical and the sacred.

Genres: Anthropology, Mythology, History

Themes: Magic, Religion, Ritual, Cultural Beliefs, Human Psychology
