Synopsis: In the midst of World War I, a young American woman finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and forbidden love in the heart of Paris. As the city becomes a playground for spies and secret agents, she must navigate the treacherous landscape of war-torn Europe while grappling with her own desires and loyalties. With danger lurking at every turn, she must make choices that will test her courage and ultimately determine her fate.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Love and betrayal, the impact of war on individuals, the struggle for personal freedom and identity
