Synopsis: 
In 'Animal Behaviour' by C. Lloyd Morgan, a renowned zoologist embarks on a captivating journey into the intricate world of animal psychology. As he delves into the minds of various creatures, from primates to insects, he uncovers astonishing insights that challenge our understanding of the animal kingdom. Through vivid anecdotes and scientific observations, Morgan explores the complex behaviors, emotions, and intelligence exhibited by animals, ultimately leading to a profound reflection on the nature of consciousness itself. This thought-provoking exploration will leave readers captivated by the wonders of the animal world and questioning the boundaries between humans and other species.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: 
Animal psychology, Consciousness, Intelligence, Behavior
