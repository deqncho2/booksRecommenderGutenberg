Synopsis: In this thought-provoking dissertation, Reidenbach delves into the complex and often controversial concept of patriotism. Through a critical analysis, he challenges conventional notions and explores the ethical implications of this deeply ingrained sentiment. With meticulous research and compelling arguments, Reidenbach invites readers to question their own understanding of patriotism and its impact on society. 

Genres: Academic, Philosophy, Political Science

Themes: Patriotism, Ethics, Nationalism, Identity, Morality
