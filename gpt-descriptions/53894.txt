Synopsis: In 'The Peep of Day', a young girl named Lucy embarks on a journey of self-discovery and faith as she navigates the challenges of growing up in a world filled with both darkness and light. Through her encounters with various characters and situations, Lucy learns valuable lessons about love, forgiveness, and the power of hope. As she peeks into the complexities of life, Lucy's innocence and curiosity lead her on a path of enlightenment and understanding.

Genres: Children's literature, Christian fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Moral growth
