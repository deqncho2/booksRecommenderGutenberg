Synopsis: In 'The X Bar X boys on the ranch' by Ferris, James Cody, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey when they stumble upon a hidden treasure map. As they navigate the rugged terrain of the ranch, they encounter unexpected challenges, mysterious characters, and must rely on their wit and courage to unravel the secrets of the map. With danger lurking around every corner, the X Bar X boys must work together to protect their newfound treasure and uncover the truth behind its origins.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Western

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Courage, Treasure Hunting
