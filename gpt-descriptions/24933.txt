Synopsis: In 'The Man Who Knew' by Wallace Edgar, a mysterious man with a dark past finds himself entangled in a web of crime and deception. As he navigates the dangerous underworld of London, he must rely on his wit and knowledge to uncover the truth and protect those he cares about. With unexpected twists and a relentless pursuit of justice, this thrilling mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Justice
