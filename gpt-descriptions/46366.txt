Synopsis: 

In 'Down the Ice, and Other Winter Sports Stories' by Harold M. Sherman, readers are taken on an exhilarating journey through a collection of captivating winter sports tales. From the adrenaline-fueled world of ice hockey to the graceful artistry of figure skating, this book immerses readers in the thrilling and sometimes treacherous world of winter sports. 

Follow the gripping stories of determined athletes as they face intense competition, overcome personal challenges, and strive for greatness on the ice. From the underdog team fighting for victory against all odds to the talented figure skater pushing the boundaries of their sport, each story is filled with heart-pounding action and inspiring moments of triumph.

Genres: Sports Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Perseverance, Teamwork, Overcoming Challenges, Pursuit of Excellence
