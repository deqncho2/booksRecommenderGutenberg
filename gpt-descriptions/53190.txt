Synopsis: 
'Absurd Ditties' by Farrow, G. E. is a collection of whimsical and humorous poems that will transport readers to a world of delightful absurdity. From talking animals to peculiar characters, each poem presents a unique and entertaining tale that will leave readers chuckling and pondering the peculiarities of life. With its clever wordplay and unexpected twists, this collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages and tickle their funny bones.

Genres: Poetry, Humor, Fantasy

Themes: Absurdity, Humor, Imagination
