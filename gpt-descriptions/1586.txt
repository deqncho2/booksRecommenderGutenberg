Synopsis: 
In 'Man and Wife' by Wilkie Collins, a young couple's marriage is put to the ultimate test when a scandalous secret threatens to tear them apart. As the truth unravels, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the stakes are raised, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the shocking truth that lies at the heart of this gripping tale.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Victorian Literature

Themes: Marriage, Trust, Secrecy, Social Expectations
