Synopsis: In "The Man with the Broken Ear" by Edmond About, a mysterious man with a peculiar deformity arrives in a small French town, capturing the attention and curiosity of the locals. As they delve into his past, they uncover a web of secrets, political intrigue, and unexpected connections that will challenge their perceptions and change their lives forever. With each revelation, the plot thickens, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Political Thriller

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Power, Redemption
