Synopsis: In the small town of Cottonwoods, a mysterious stranger arrives, shrouded in secrecy and danger. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the locals, a thrilling tale of love, betrayal, and redemption unfolds. With his past catching up to him, the mysterious rider must confront his demons and make a choice that will change everything.

Genres: Western, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Identity, Sacrifice
