Synopsis: In 'The Natural Cure of Consumption, Constipation, Bright's Disease, Neuralgia, Rheumatism, "Colds" (Fevers), Etc.', author C. E. Page delves into the origins of sickness and provides valuable insights on how to prevent various ailments. This health manual offers a comprehensive guide to understanding and treating conditions such as consumption, constipation, Bright's disease, neuralgia, rheumatism, and even common colds. With a focus on natural remedies, this book presents a wealth of knowledge that can help readers take control of their health and well-being.

Genres: Health, Self-help, Medical

Themes: Natural remedies, Disease prevention, Holistic health
