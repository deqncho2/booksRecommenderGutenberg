Synopsis: In 'The Day of the Beast' by Zane Grey, a young man named John Shefford embarks on a perilous journey to the wild and lawless land of Utah, where he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and revenge. As he navigates the treacherous terrain and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Shefford finds himself torn between his own desires and the pursuit of justice. With danger lurking at every turn, he must confront his inner demons and make choices that will shape his destiny forever.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Redemption, Honor, Love and Sacrifice
