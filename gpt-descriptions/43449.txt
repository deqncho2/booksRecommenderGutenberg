Synopsis: In "Mrs. Severn: A Novel, Vol. 1 (of 3)" by Mary Elizabeth Carter, we are introduced to the intriguing world of Mrs. Severn, a woman of mystery and charm. Set in the 19th century, the story follows the life of Mrs. Severn as she navigates the complexities of society, love, and secrets. As her past begins to catch up with her, Mrs. Severn finds herself torn between her desire for a new life and the ghosts that haunt her. With captivating characters and a web of hidden truths, this novel takes readers on a journey of suspense and self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Society, Love, Redemption
