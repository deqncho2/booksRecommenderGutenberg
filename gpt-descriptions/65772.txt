Synopsis: In 'Once Upon a Monbeast...', a small town is plagued by a mysterious creature that emerges from the depths of the forest, terrorizing its inhabitants. As fear and chaos spread, a group of unlikely heroes must come together to unravel the secrets of the monbeast and find a way to save their town. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Overcoming Fear
