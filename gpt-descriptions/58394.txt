Synopsis: 

'Anatomy of the Cat' takes readers on a captivating journey into the intricate world of feline anatomy. Written by renowned scientists Jennings, H. S. and Reighard, Jacob, this book delves deep into the inner workings of our beloved feline companions. From the mesmerizing structure of their bones to the fascinating complexity of their organs, this comprehensive exploration sheds light on the remarkable design of cats. Through vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, readers will gain a newfound appreciation for the elegance and functionality of the feline body.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Biology

Themes: Animal anatomy, Comparative anatomy, Zoology
