Synopsis: 
In 'His Unknown Wife' by Tracy, Louis, a successful businessman wakes up one morning to find a woman in his bed claiming to be his wife. The problem is, he has no recollection of ever getting married. As he delves into the mystery of his unknown wife, he uncovers a web of secrets and lies that threaten to unravel his entire life. With each revelation, he becomes more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means facing the consequences of his own actions. As the lines between reality and deception blur, he must confront his past and make a choice that will change everything.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Romance

Themes: Identity, Deception, Redemption
