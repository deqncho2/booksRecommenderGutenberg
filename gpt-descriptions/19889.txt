Synopsis: In 'Naughty Miss Bunny: A Story for Little Children' by Clara Mulholland, follow the mischievous adventures of a playful bunny who just can't seem to stay out of trouble. From sneaking into the garden to causing chaos at tea parties, Miss Bunny's antics will keep young readers entertained and guessing what she'll do next. With charming illustrations and a delightful storyline, this book is sure to capture the imagination of children and leave them eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book

Themes: Playfulness, Curiosity, Consequences
