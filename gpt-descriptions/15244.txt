Synopsis: In 'Lewie; Or, The Bended Twig' by Sarah H. Bradford, a young boy named Lewie embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience in the face of adversity. Set in the backdrop of the American Civil War, Lewie's life takes an unexpected turn when he is separated from his family and forced to navigate the harsh realities of war-torn America. As he encounters various challenges and encounters with both friends and foes, Lewie's determination and courage are put to the test. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way and find his place in a world turned upside down?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Resilience, Identity, Family, War
