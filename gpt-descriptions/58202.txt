Synopsis: In 'A Reaping' by Benson, E. F., a young couple moves into a charming country cottage, unaware of the dark secrets that lie within its walls. As they settle into their new home, they begin to experience strange occurrences and eerie encounters that gradually escalate into a terrifying battle for survival. With each passing day, the couple unravels the chilling history of the cottage and its previous inhabitants, leading them to question their own sanity and the true nature of the evil that lurks within. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they must confront their deepest fears and make a desperate attempt to break free from the clutches of the malevolent force that threatens to consume them.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Hauntings, Psychological terror, Dark secrets
