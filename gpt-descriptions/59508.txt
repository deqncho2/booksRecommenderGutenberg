Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of the Brothers Grimm' is a comprehensive collection of the timeless fairy tales and folklore written by the legendary Brothers Grimm, Jacob and Wilhelm. This anthology includes beloved stories such as "Cinderella," "Snow White," and "Hansel and Gretel," as well as lesser-known tales that will captivate readers of all ages. Dive into a world of enchantment, where magical creatures, brave heroes, and cunning villains come to life in these captivating narratives. Whether you are revisiting childhood favorites or discovering these tales for the first time, this collection is sure to transport you to a realm of wonder and imagination.

Genres: Fairy tales, Folklore, Children's literature
Themes: Magic, Morality, Transformation, Good vs. Evil
