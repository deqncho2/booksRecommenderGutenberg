Synopsis: In 'At the Queen's Mercy' by Mabel Fuller Blodgett, a young woman finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue when she becomes a lady-in-waiting to the Queen. As she navigates the treacherous world of court politics, she must uncover the truth behind a mysterious disappearance while also grappling with her own desires and ambitions. With danger lurking around every corner, she must decide whom she can trust and how far she is willing to go to protect herself and those she loves.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Power and manipulation, loyalty and betrayal, identity and self-discovery
