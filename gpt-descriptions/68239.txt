Synopsis: 

In "Personality of Plants" by Dixon, Royal and Fitch, Franklyn Everett, a groundbreaking discovery leads a team of scientists on a thrilling journey into the hidden world of plants. As they delve deeper into their research, they uncover astonishing secrets that challenge everything we thought we knew about the natural world. From the mysterious communication between plants to their surprising ability to adapt and survive, this captivating exploration will leave readers in awe of the incredible personalities that lie within the plant kingdom.

Genres: Science, Nature, Non-fiction

Themes: Botany, Communication, Adaptation, Discovery
