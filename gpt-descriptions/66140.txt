Synopsis: In 'Test Problem' by Alan J. Ramm, a brilliant mathematician named Dr. Emily Collins is presented with an intriguing challenge that could change the course of scientific discovery. As she delves deeper into the enigmatic problem, she uncovers a web of secrets and hidden agendas that threaten not only her career but also her life. With time running out, Dr. Collins must navigate a treacherous path of deception and betrayal to solve the test problem and protect the truth from those who would stop at nothing to keep it hidden.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Intellectual pursuit, Conspiracy, Sacrifice
