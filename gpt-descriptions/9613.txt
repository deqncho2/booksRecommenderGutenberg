Synopsis: 
In 'The Young Buglers' by G. A. Henty, two young brothers find themselves caught up in the midst of the Anglo-Zulu War in 19th century South Africa. As they join the British army as buglers, they embark on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, bravery, and unexpected friendships. From the treacherous battlefields to the heart-pounding encounters with the Zulu warriors, the brothers must navigate the chaos of war while staying true to their duty and loyalty. Will they survive the brutal conflict and prove themselves as courageous buglers?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Brotherhood, Coming of Age
