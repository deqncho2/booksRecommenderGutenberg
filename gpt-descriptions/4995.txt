Synopsis: In "True to Himself; Or, Roger Strong's Struggle for Place" by Edward Stratemeyer, we follow the journey of Roger Strong, a young man determined to find his place in the world. From his humble beginnings to facing unexpected challenges, Roger must navigate the complexities of society, relationships, and personal identity. As he grapples with his own ambitions and the expectations of others, Roger's resilience and determination are put to the test. Will he stay true to himself or succumb to the pressures around him?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Bildungsroman

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Society's Expectations
