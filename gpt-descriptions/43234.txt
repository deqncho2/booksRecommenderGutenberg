Synopsis: In 'Bright Ideas: A Record of Invention and Misinvention' by Strang Herbert, embark on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of human ingenuity and its occasional missteps. From groundbreaking inventions that revolutionized society to bizarre and ill-conceived contraptions, this book unveils a collection of remarkable stories that will leave you in awe and disbelief. Delve into the minds of inventors who dared to dream big, exploring their triumphs and failures, and discover the unexpected consequences that arise when bright ideas meet the complexities of reality. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Invention, Innovation, Human Ingenuity, Failure, Consequences
