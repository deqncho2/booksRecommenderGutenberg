Synopsis: 
"The Circus Boys on the Mississippi; Or, Afloat with the Big Show on the Big River" follows the thrilling adventures of Phil Forrest and his friends as they join a traveling circus on a journey down the mighty Mississippi River. From daring performances under the big top to unexpected encounters along the riverbanks, the young circus boys find themselves in a series of exhilarating and sometimes dangerous situations. As they navigate the challenges of life on the river, they learn valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the power of following their dreams.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Pursuit of Dreams
