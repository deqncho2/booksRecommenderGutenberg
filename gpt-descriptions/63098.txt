Synopsis: 

In 'The Horse's Friend: The Only Practical Method of Educating the Horse and Eradicating Vicious Habits', author O. S. Pratt presents a comprehensive guide to horse training and care, offering valuable recipes, instructions in farriery, horse-shoeing, and the latest rules of trotting. The book also includes a record of fast horses up to 1876, showcasing the remarkable achievements of these magnificent creatures. With a focus on practicality and eradicating vicious habits, Pratt's work provides a wealth of knowledge for horse enthusiasts and professionals alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Care, Sports

Themes: Horse training, Animal behavior, Equestrian sports, Practical horsemanship
