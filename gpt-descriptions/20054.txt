Synopsis: In 'The Mermaid: A Love Tale' by Lily Dougall, a young artist named David discovers a mysterious mermaid washed ashore on a remote beach. As he nurses her back to health, an unexpected bond forms between them, leading to a captivating and forbidden love affair. However, their relationship is soon tested by societal norms, personal sacrifices, and the secrets that lie beneath the surface. This enchanting tale explores the depths of love, the complexities of human connection, and the power of embracing the unknown.

Genres: Romance, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Identity, Sacrifice, Acceptance
