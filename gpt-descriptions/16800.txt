Synopsis: In 'The Secret of a Happy Home', Marion Harland takes readers on a captivating journey into the lives of the Hamilton family. Set in the late 19th century, this heartwarming novel explores the challenges and triumphs faced by the Hamiltons as they navigate the complexities of family dynamics, societal expectations, and personal growth. As secrets are unveiled and relationships tested, readers will be drawn into a tale of love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of true happiness. 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Domestic Fiction

Themes: Family, Love, Sacrifice, Happiness
