Synopsis: In 'The Irish on the Somme: Being a Second Series of 'The Irish at the Front',' Michael MacDonagh takes us back to the harrowing battlefields of World War I, where the Irish soldiers face unimaginable challenges and sacrifices. Through vivid and poignant storytelling, MacDonagh explores the experiences of these brave men, their camaraderie, and the impact of war on their lives. From the trenches to the front lines, this gripping account sheds light on the untold stories of the Irish soldiers who fought on the Somme.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Literature, Memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Loss and Grief
