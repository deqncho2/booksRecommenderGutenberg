Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's Triumph; Or, The Disappearance of Felicia', Frank Merriwell, the popular and talented athlete, finds himself embroiled in a mysterious case when his friend Felicia suddenly goes missing. Determined to uncover the truth, Frank delves into a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected twists that will test his courage and resourcefulness. As he races against time to rescue Felicia, Frank must confront his own fears and rely on his skills to unravel the truth behind her disappearance.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Determination, Loyalty
