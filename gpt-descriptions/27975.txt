Synopsis: 
'Delineations of the Ox Tribe: The Natural History of Bulls, Bisons, and Buffaloes' takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse world of bovines. From the majestic bulls to the powerful bison and the enigmatic buffalo, this book explores the known species and remarkable variations within the Bos genus. Through vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, George Vasey delves into the fascinating characteristics, habitats, and behaviors of these magnificent creatures, shedding light on their evolutionary history and their significance to human culture. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Zoology

Themes: Animal diversity, Evolution, Human-animal relationship
