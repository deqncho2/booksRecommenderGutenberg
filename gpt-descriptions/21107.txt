Synopsis: 
"On Board the Esmeralda: Martin Leigh's Log - A Sea Story" follows the thrilling adventures of Martin Leigh, a young sailor who finds himself aboard the Esmeralda, a ship bound for unknown waters. As the crew embarks on a perilous journey, they encounter treacherous storms, mysterious islands, and encounters with other ships that test their courage and resilience. Through Leigh's log entries, readers are immersed in the vivid world of the sea, filled with danger, camaraderie, and the pursuit of treasure. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Nautical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Loyalty, Treasure Hunting
