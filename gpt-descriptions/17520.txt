Synopsis:
'Letters of the Right Honourable Lady M—y W—y M—e: Written during Her Travels in Europe, Asia and Africa to Persons of Distinction, Men of Letters, &c. in Different Parts of Europe' is a collection of letters written by Lady Mary Wortley Montagu during her extensive travels across Europe, Asia, and Africa. In these letters, Lady Montagu shares her experiences, observations, and encounters with various individuals of distinction and men of letters. Through her vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, she offers a unique perspective on the cultures, customs, and societies she encounters during her journeys.

Genres:
Travelogue, Epistolary

Themes:
Cultural exploration, Enlightenment, Gender roles and expectations
