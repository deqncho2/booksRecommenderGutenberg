Synopsis: In 'Sweet Rocket' by Mary Johnston, a young woman named Judith embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience in the midst of the American Civil War. As she navigates the chaos and uncertainty of the war-torn South, Judith's life becomes intertwined with a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets and desires. From the opulent mansions of Virginia to the battlefields of Gettysburg, this captivating tale explores the power of love, loyalty, and the indomitable human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Identity and Self-discovery, Resilience and Survival
