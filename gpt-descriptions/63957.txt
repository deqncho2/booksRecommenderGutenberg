Synopsis: In the gripping historical novel, 'The Salem Belle: A Tale of 1692' by Ebenezer Wheelwright, readers are transported to the infamous Salem witch trials. Set in the 17th century, the story follows the life of a young woman named Isabella, who finds herself caught in the midst of the hysteria and paranoia that grips the town. As accusations fly and lives hang in the balance, Isabella must navigate the treacherous waters of fear, betrayal, and the search for truth. With its richly detailed setting and complex characters, this captivating tale explores the dark depths of human nature and the consequences of unchecked power.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Hysteria, Betrayal, Power, Truth
