Synopsis: In 'A Poached Peerage' by William Magnay, Sir, a mysterious disappearance of a prominent member of the British aristocracy sets off a thrilling investigation. As the search for the missing peer intensifies, a web of secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances is unraveled, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. This gripping tale of intrigue and suspense takes readers on a journey through the upper echelons of society, where nothing is as it seems and everyone has something to hide.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Power, Social Class
