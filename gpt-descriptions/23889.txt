Synopsis: In 'Charley de Milo' by Laurence M. Janifer, a mysterious and enigmatic woman named Charley de Milo enters the life of a struggling writer, turning his world upside down. As the writer becomes increasingly infatuated with Charley, he discovers that she is not who she appears to be, and her true identity holds a dark secret. As their relationship deepens, the writer is drawn into a dangerous web of intrigue and deception, where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning the true nature of Charley de Milo and the consequences of the writer's obsession.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Identity, Obsession, Deception
