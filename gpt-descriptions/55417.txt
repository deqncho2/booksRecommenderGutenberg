Synopsis: In 'The Gentleman Who Vanished: A Psychological Phantasy' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious disappearance sets off a chain of events that will challenge the boundaries of reality and the depths of the human mind. As the protagonist delves into the enigma, they are drawn into a world where dreams and illusions intertwine, blurring the line between what is real and what is imagined. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning the nature of identity, perception, and the power of the subconscious mind.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Perception, Reality vs. Illusion, Subconscious Mind
