Synopsis: 
In "Mr. Prohack" by Arnold Bennett, Arthur Prohack, a middle-aged civil servant, suddenly finds himself in possession of an enormous fortune. As he navigates the challenges and temptations that come with newfound wealth, Prohack's unconventional approach to life leads him on a series of unexpected adventures. From managing his eccentric family to outsmarting the financial world, Prohack's journey is filled with humor, wit, and a touch of romance. Will he be able to maintain his integrity and find true happiness amidst the chaos?

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Romance

Themes: Wealth and its consequences, Morality and integrity, Family dynamics, Pursuit of happiness
