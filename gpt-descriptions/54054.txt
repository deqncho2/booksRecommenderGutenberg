Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Twelfth, or the East Suffolk, Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment from 1685 to 1847. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, Cannon delves into the regiment's rich history, shedding light on the battles fought, the heroes who emerged, and the challenges they faced. This meticulously researched account offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the soldiers who served in the Twelfth Regiment, providing a comprehensive narrative that will leave readers eager to uncover the untold stories and remarkable feats of this esteemed military unit.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Military service, Regimental history, Heroism, Warfare
