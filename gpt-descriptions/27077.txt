Synopsis: 
In 'Dangers of the Trail in 1865: A Narrative of Actual Events', Charles E. Young takes readers on a gripping journey through the treacherous trails of 1865. Set against the backdrop of the American West, this thrilling narrative follows a group of pioneers as they face perilous encounters with outlaws, Native American tribes, and the unforgiving wilderness. As they navigate through dangerous territories, the pioneers must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and the bonds they form along the way to survive. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the suspenseful and heart-pounding adventures that await these brave souls.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Survival, Courage, Camaraderie, Exploration
