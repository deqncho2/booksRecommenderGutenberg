Synopsis: 

In 'Textiles, for Commercial, Industrial, and Domestic Arts Schools', author William H. Dooley takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of textiles. This comprehensive guide is not only a valuable resource for students studying textiles, but also for professionals in the wholesale and retail dry goods, wool, cotton, and dressmaker's trades. 

Dooley's expertise shines through as he explores various aspects of textiles, from their production and characteristics to their application in different industries. Through detailed descriptions and illustrations, readers will gain a deeper understanding of the materials, techniques, and processes involved in creating textiles. 

Whether you are a student, a professional, or simply curious about the world of textiles, this book is sure to captivate your interest and expand your knowledge in this fascinating field.

Genres: Educational, Reference, Textbook

Themes: Textile production, Material characteristics, Industrial applications, Trade industries
