Synopsis: In 'The Princess Galva: A Romance' by David Whitelaw, a young princess finds herself torn between duty and desire as she navigates a treacherous world of political intrigue, forbidden love, and personal sacrifice. As she grapples with her own identity and the expectations placed upon her, she must make choices that will shape not only her own destiny but also the fate of her kingdom. With captivating twists and turns, this enchanting tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within the palace walls.

Genres: Romance, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Duty and sacrifice, Identity and self-discovery, Love and betrayal
