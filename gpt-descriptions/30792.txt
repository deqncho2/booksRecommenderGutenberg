Synopsis: 

'Lavengro: The Scholar, The Gypsy, The Priest' follows the captivating journey of a young scholar named Lavengro, who finds himself entangled in a world of adventure, mystery, and self-discovery. As he encounters a diverse cast of characters, including gypsies, priests, and highwaymen, Lavengro's life takes unexpected turns, leading him to question his own identity and purpose. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century England, this enthralling tale explores the complexities of human nature, the clash of cultures, and the pursuit of knowledge and freedom.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Bildungsroman

Themes: Identity, Cultural diversity, Quest for knowledge, Freedom
