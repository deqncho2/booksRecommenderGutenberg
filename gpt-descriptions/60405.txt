Synopsis: In "The Story of the Grail and the Passing of Arthur," Howard Pyle weaves a captivating tale of chivalry, adventure, and the pursuit of a sacred relic. Set in the legendary realm of King Arthur and his Knights of the Round Table, the story follows the noble Sir Galahad as he embarks on a perilous quest to find the Holy Grail. Along the way, he encounters mystical beings, faces moral dilemmas, and must prove his worthiness to fulfill his destiny. As the story unfolds, the fate of Arthur's kingdom hangs in the balance, and the characters are tested in their loyalty, honor, and devotion to the ideals of knighthood.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Chivalry, Quest for Meaning, Loyalty, Honor, Destiny
