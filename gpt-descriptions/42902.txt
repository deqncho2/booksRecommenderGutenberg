Synopsis: In 'Young Blood' by E. W. Hornung, a young man named Jack is drawn into a world of adventure and danger when he becomes entangled with a group of notorious criminals. As he navigates the treacherous underworld of London, Jack must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to survive. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what fate has in store for Jack.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Coming of age, Morality, Survival
