Synopsis: 
"The Collector's Whatnot" is a comprehensive compendium that delves into the fascinating world of antique collecting. Written by Tarkington, Booth, Roberts, Kenneth Lewis, and Kahler, Hugh MacNair, this manual provides a wealth of information and advice on all aspects of collecting antiques, from ancient artifacts to more contemporary pieces. Whether you are a seasoned collector or just starting out, this book offers valuable insights, tips, and resources to enhance your knowledge and passion for antiques.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Collectibles

Themes: Antique collecting, History, Preservation
