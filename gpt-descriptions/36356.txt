Synopsis: In "Life of John Keats: His Life and Poetry, His Friends, Critics and After-Fame" by Colvin, Sidney, delve into the captivating world of one of the greatest Romantic poets, John Keats. This biography takes readers on a journey through Keats' life, exploring his personal struggles, his relationships with friends and critics, and the enduring legacy he left behind. Discover the man behind the poetry, his triumphs and tribulations, and the profound impact he had on the literary world.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Poetry

Themes: Romanticism, Artistic Expression, Legacy
