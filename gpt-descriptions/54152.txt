Synopsis: 

Step into the world of mid-20th century American art as the Library of Congress unveils a captivating catalog of copyright renewals from 1951 to 1959. This meticulously curated collection showcases a diverse range of artwork, from stunning paintings and sculptures to intricate illustrations and designs. Delve into the creative minds of talented artists who sought to protect their original works during this transformative era. With each page turned, you'll uncover hidden gems and forgotten masterpieces, offering a glimpse into the artistic landscape of post-war America. 

Genres: Art, Reference

Themes: Creativity, Intellectual Property
