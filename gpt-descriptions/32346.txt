Synopsis: In 'Keep Your Shape' by Robert Sheckley, a man wakes up one day to find that he has transformed into a completely different person. As he navigates through a world where everyone's shape is constantly changing, he must uncover the truth behind this bizarre phenomenon and find a way to regain control over his own identity. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking scenarios, this gripping tale explores the limits of personal identity and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Transformation, Reality vs. Perception
