Synopsis: In "The Spirit of American Sculpture," Adeline Adams takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of American sculpture, exploring the lives and works of renowned artists who have shaped the nation's artistic landscape. From the grandeur of monumental sculptures to the delicate intricacies of smaller pieces, Adams delves into the stories behind these masterpieces, revealing the passion, struggles, and triumphs of the artists who brought them to life. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the evolution of American sculpture and the enduring spirit that continues to inspire generations.

Genres: Art History, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, American identity, Creativity and innovation
