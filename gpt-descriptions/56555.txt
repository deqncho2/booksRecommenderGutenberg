Synopsis: In 'The Magical Mimics in Oz' by Snow, Jack, a mysterious group of shape-shifting creatures known as the Mimics arrive in the magical land of Oz, causing chaos and confusion among its inhabitants. As Dorothy and her friends embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the Mimics' arrival, they must confront their own fears and discover the power of friendship and self-belief. With unexpected twists and enchanting encounters, this captivating tale takes readers on a journey through the whimsical world of Oz, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Self-belief, Identity
