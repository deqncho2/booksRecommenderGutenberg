Synopsis: In the remote countryside of Arkham, a strange and otherworldly phenomenon occurs when a mysterious meteorite crashes onto a farm, emitting an eerie and indescribable color. As the color spreads and infects the land, the lives of the Gardner family and the entire community are irrevocably changed. As they struggle to comprehend the inexplicable and horrifying events that unfold, they are faced with a malevolent force that threatens their sanity and existence.

Genres: Horror, Science Fiction, Cosmic Horror

Themes: The unknown and incomprehensible, the fragility of human perception and sanity, the destructive power of the supernatural, the insignificance of humanity in the face of cosmic forces.
