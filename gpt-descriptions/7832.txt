Synopsis: In 'The Deluge' by David Graham Phillips, a young woman named Margaret finds herself caught in a whirlwind of scandal and intrigue when she becomes entangled with a powerful and influential man. As she navigates the treacherous waters of high society, Margaret must confront her own desires and ambitions, while facing the consequences of her choices. With secrets lurking around every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Power and corruption, love and betrayal, societal expectations
