Synopsis:
In the small town of Somerset, a mysterious event unfolds, shrouding the community in a web of secrets and intrigue. As the townspeople grapple with the aftermath, they soon discover that nothing is as it seems, and everyone has something to hide. From the enigmatic newcomer with a dark past to the long-buried secrets of the town's founding families, Somerset becomes a hotbed of tension and suspicion. As the truth slowly unravels, the residents of Somerset must confront their own demons and face the consequences of their actions. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Small-town dynamics, Redemption
