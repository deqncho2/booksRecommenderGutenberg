Synopsis: In 'The Flying Mercury' by Eleanor M. Ingram, a mysterious statue known as the Flying Mercury becomes the center of attention in a small town. As the townspeople become obsessed with the statue's supposed magical powers, a series of strange events unfold, leading to unexpected consequences. With elements of mystery, romance, and supernatural intrigue, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Supernatural

Themes: Obsession, Power, Fate
