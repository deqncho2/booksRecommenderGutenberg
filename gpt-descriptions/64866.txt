Synopsis: 

In 'Narrative of an Expedition Through the Upper Mississippi to Itasca Lake, the Actual Source of This River', Henry Rowe Schoolcraft takes readers on a thrilling journey through the uncharted wilderness of the Upper Mississippi. Accompanied by a team of intrepid explorers, Schoolcraft embarks on an exploratory trip, navigating the treacherous St. Croix and Burntwood (or Broule) Rivers. As they venture deeper into the unknown, the expedition faces numerous challenges and encounters with Native American tribes, all while striving to uncover the elusive source of the mighty Mississippi River. 

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Wilderness, Native American Culture
