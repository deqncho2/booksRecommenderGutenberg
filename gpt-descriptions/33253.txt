Synopsis: 
In 'Gala-Day Luncheons: A Little Book of Suggestions', Caroline French Benton invites readers into a world of culinary creativity and elegance. With her delightful collection of luncheon recipes, she offers a tantalizing glimpse into the art of hosting and entertaining. From delectable appetizers to mouthwatering main courses and exquisite desserts, Benton's suggestions are sure to inspire even the most discerning palates. Whether you are planning a festive gathering or simply seeking to elevate your everyday meals, this book is a treasure trove of ideas that will leave you hungry for more.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink, Entertaining

Themes: Culinary creativity, Hosting and entertaining, Gourmet cuisine
