Synopsis: In a world on the brink of change, a group of Neanderthals struggle to survive amidst the encroaching presence of a new species. As they navigate the complexities of their environment, their primitive instincts clash with the emerging intelligence of the Homo sapiens. Through their eyes, we witness the collision of two worlds and the profound impact it has on their existence.

Genres: Historical fiction, Anthropological fiction, Adventure

Themes: Evolution, Survival, Clash of cultures, Loss of innocence
