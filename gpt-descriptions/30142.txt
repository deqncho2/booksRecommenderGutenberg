Synopsis: 
After a terrorist attack rocks San Francisco, seventeen-year-old Marcus is caught in the wrong place at the wrong time. When he and his friends are wrongfully detained and interrogated by the Department of Homeland Security, Marcus becomes determined to fight back against the oppressive surveillance state that has taken over his city. With his tech-savvy skills and a group of like-minded individuals, Marcus embarks on a dangerous mission to expose the truth and reclaim their freedom.

Genres: 
Science Fiction, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: 
Civil liberties, Government surveillance, Rebellion, Technology, Freedom
