Synopsis: In 'Five Little Peppers Midway', the lovable Pepper family embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes them to a bustling carnival. As they navigate through the colorful attractions and encounter a cast of intriguing characters, the Peppers find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected events. With their trademark charm and resourcefulness, the Peppers must rely on their wits and teamwork to overcome challenges and unravel mysteries. Will they be able to navigate the midway and uncover the secrets hidden within? Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Mystery Themes: Family bonds, Resilience, Discovery
