Synopsis: In the mysterious world of the Infra-Medians, a group of individuals with extraordinary abilities, a young woman named Lily discovers her own unique power. As she navigates through a hidden society filled with secrets and danger, Lily must uncover the truth about her past and the true nature of the Infra-Medians. With unexpected alliances and thrilling twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Power, Secrets, Betrayal
