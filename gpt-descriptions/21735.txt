Synopsis: In 'The Floating Light of the Goodwin Sands', a young sailor named Jack is drawn into a mysterious and treacherous adventure when he encounters a strange floating light on the treacherous Goodwin Sands. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the light, Jack finds himself facing danger, betrayal, and the power of the supernatural. With his courage and resourcefulness tested to the limit, Jack must navigate through a web of intrigue and uncover the truth behind the enigmatic floating light.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Courage, Betrayal, Supernatural forces, Intrigue
