Synopsis: Join Jack London on an exhilarating adventure as he sets sail on his custom-built yacht, the Snark, with his wife and a small crew. From the turquoise waters of the South Pacific to the remote islands of the Solomon Archipelago, London's voyage takes unexpected turns, encountering treacherous storms, dangerous wildlife, and encounters with fascinating indigenous cultures. As the Snark navigates through uncharted waters, London's captivating storytelling brings to life the thrill and challenges of a once-in-a-lifetime journey.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Cultural encounters
