Synopsis: In the enchanting novel 'Aylwin' by Theodore Watts-Dunton, a young poet named Felix Aylwin finds himself captivated by the mysterious beauty of a woman he encounters in the countryside. As he delves deeper into her world, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden love, and haunting pasts. Set against the backdrop of the Victorian era, this tale weaves together elements of romance, mystery, and tragedy, taking readers on a journey through the complexities of human emotions and the power of fate.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Secrets, Fate, Victorian society
