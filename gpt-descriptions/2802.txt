Synopsis: 
In the small town of Southern California, Ramona, a spirited and imaginative young girl, embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, Ramona navigates the challenges of growing up in a changing world, where her mixed heritage and unconventional family dynamics make her feel like an outsider. As she encounters love, loss, and the complexities of societal expectations, Ramona's indomitable spirit shines through, inspiring those around her and leaving an indelible mark on her community.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: 
Identity, Belonging, Resilience, Cultural Heritage
