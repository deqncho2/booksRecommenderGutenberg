Synopsis: 
In 'The Affair at the Semiramis Hotel', a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he is called upon to investigate a peculiar incident at a luxurious hotel. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a series of unexpected twists and turns, leading him to question the motives and identities of those involved. With danger lurking around every corner, the detective must use his wit and intuition to solve the puzzle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Betrayal
