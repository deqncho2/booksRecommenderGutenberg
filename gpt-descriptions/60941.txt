Synopsis: In 'Jack Straw in Mexico: How the Engineers Defended the Great Hydro-Electric Plant', author Crump, Irving takes readers on a thrilling journey to Mexico, where a group of engineers find themselves facing a dangerous threat to a vital hydro-electric plant. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected challenges, the engineers must use their skills and ingenuity to protect the plant and the community it serves. With high stakes and adrenaline-pumping action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Action

Themes: Engineering, Teamwork, Survival
