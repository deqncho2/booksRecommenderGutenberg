Synopsis: 

"The Life of George Stephenson and of his Son Robert Stephenson" is a captivating biography that delves into the extraordinary lives of two pioneering figures in the history of locomotives. Samuel Smiles takes readers on a fascinating journey, chronicling the humble beginnings, relentless determination, and groundbreaking achievements of George and Robert Stephenson. From their struggles and triumphs to their pivotal role in revolutionizing transportation, this book offers a compelling narrative that will leave readers in awe of their remarkable contributions.

Genres: Biography, History, Engineering

Themes: Perseverance, Innovation, Industrial Revolution
