Synopsis: In 'Know Thy Neighbor' by Elisabeth R. Lewis, a seemingly idyllic suburban neighborhood is shaken when a mysterious new family moves in next door. As the residents become increasingly curious about their enigmatic neighbors, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel the peaceful facade of their community. Tensions rise, alliances are tested, and the true nature of trust and loyalty is put to the ultimate test. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the dark underbelly of this seemingly perfect neighborhood, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Trust and betrayal, Community dynamics
