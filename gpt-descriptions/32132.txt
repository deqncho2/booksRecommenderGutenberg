Synopsis: In "Long Live the King!" by Boothby, Guy, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, claiming to be the rightful king of a distant land. As the townspeople become captivated by his charisma and regal presence, they are drawn into a web of intrigue and deception. With secrets lurking beneath the surface, the stranger's true identity and intentions remain shrouded in mystery, leaving the reader questioning who they can trust and what lies ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Power, Deception
