Synopsis: 
'Korean Tales' is a captivating collection of stories that takes readers on a journey into the enchanting world of Korean folklore. Translated by Horace Newton Allen, these tales are filled with mythical creatures, magical encounters, and moral lessons that will leave readers spellbound. From cunning foxes and mischievous goblins to brave heroes and wise old men, each story offers a unique glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of Korea. Accompanied by introductory chapters that provide a vivid description of the country, this book is a delightful exploration of a land steeped in tradition and wonder.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural

Themes: Mythology, Morality, Cultural Heritage
