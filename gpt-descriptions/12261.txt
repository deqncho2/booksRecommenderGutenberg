Synopsis: In 'Balder the Beautiful, Volume I.: A Study in Magic and Religion: the Golden Bough, Part VII., The; Fire-Festivals of Europe and the Doctrine of the External Soul', James George Frazer delves into the captivating world of magic and religion. Exploring the ancient fire festivals of Europe and the intriguing concept of the external soul, this volume takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the mystical realms of human belief and ritual. As Frazer uncovers the significance of these age-old traditions, he unveils a tapestry of fascinating cultural practices and their profound impact on society. 

Genres: Anthropology, Mythology, Religious Studies

Themes: Magic, Religion, Ritual, Cultural Traditions
