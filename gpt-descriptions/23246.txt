Synopsis: 

In 'Mistress Anne' by Bailey, Temple, a young woman named Anne finds herself caught in a web of secrets and forbidden desires. As she navigates the treacherous waters of love and betrayal, Anne discovers that the path to happiness is paved with unexpected twists and turns. With each page, the reader is drawn deeper into Anne's world, eagerly anticipating the shocking revelations that lie ahead.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Forbidden love, Betrayal, Secrets, Self-discovery
