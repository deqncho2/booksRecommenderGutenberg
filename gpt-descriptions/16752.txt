Synopsis: In 'Caste' by Fraser, William Alexander, a young archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the key to a long-lost civilization. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and a race against time to uncover the truth before it falls into the wrong hands. With each discovery, the protagonist's life is put at risk, and he must navigate treacherous paths to protect the artifact and those he holds dear. This thrilling adventure takes readers on a captivating journey through history, mythology, and the human desire for power.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Archaeology, Ancient Civilizations, Betrayal, Power
