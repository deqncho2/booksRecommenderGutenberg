Synopsis: 

In "Piracy off the Florida Coast and Elsewhere," Samuel A. Green takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous waters of piracy in the 19th century. From the notorious Blackbeard to lesser-known but equally ruthless pirates, Green delves into the captivating tales of these seafaring criminals and their daring exploits. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the author paints a vivid picture of the dangerous world of piracy, filled with high-stakes battles, hidden treasures, and unexpected alliances. Brace yourself for an exhilarating adventure that will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Adventure, Maritime History

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Exploration and Adventure, Legends and Mythology
