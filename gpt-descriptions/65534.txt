Synopsis: In 'Slaughter on Dornell IV', two renowned science fiction authors, Garrett and Silverberg, join forces to deliver a gripping tale set on a distant planet. As a team of intergalactic investigators arrives on Dornell IV, they are confronted with a perplexing mystery surrounding a series of brutal murders. With time running out and tensions rising, they must navigate a treacherous landscape of political intrigue, alien cultures, and hidden agendas to uncover the truth before more lives are lost.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of alien worlds, Crime and investigation, Political manipulation
