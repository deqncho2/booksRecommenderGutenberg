Synopsis:
In 'Mike and Psmith', two young men find themselves thrown together at a prestigious English boarding school. Mike, a talented cricketer, is struggling to fit in and find his place among the upper-class students. Psmith, on the other hand, is a charming and eccentric character who effortlessly navigates the social hierarchy. Together, they embark on a series of misadventures, from outwitting the school authorities to pursuing their own unique interests. As their friendship deepens, they discover that they have more in common than they initially thought, and their escapades take them on a journey of self-discovery and camaraderie.

Genres:
Comedy, Coming-of-age, School story

Themes:
Friendship, Identity, Social class
