Synopsis: In 'Picture Bride' by Joseph Samachson, a young woman named Mei Ling leaves her home in China to travel to America as a picture bride, entering into an arranged marriage with a man she has never met. As she navigates the challenges of a new country and a new life, Mei Ling discovers unexpected friendships, faces discrimination, and finds herself torn between tradition and her own desires. With vivid descriptions and emotional depth, this captivating novel explores the struggles and triumphs of an immigrant experience, as Mei Ling's journey unfolds against the backdrop of early 20th century America.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Cultural Fiction

Themes: Immigration, Identity, Tradition, Love and Marriage
