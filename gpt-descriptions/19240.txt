Synopsis: 
'Bahá'í Prayers' is a collection of powerful and inspiring prayers revealed by Bahá'u'lláh, the Báb, and ‘Abdu'l-Bahá. These prayers touch the depths of the human soul, offering solace, guidance, and a connection to the divine. From prayers of gratitude and supplication to those seeking strength and healing, this collection encompasses a wide range of spiritual expressions. Each prayer is a profound invitation to commune with the divine and find inner peace and purpose.

Genres: Religious, Spiritual, Poetry

Themes: Faith, Unity, Guidance, Gratitude, Healing, Connection with the Divine
