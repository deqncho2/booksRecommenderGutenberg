Synopsis: In "The Man Who Ended War" by Godfrey Hollis, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to bring an end to all wars. As he races against time to share his findings with the world, he faces numerous obstacles and must confront the ethical implications of his invention. With suspenseful twists and thought-provoking dilemmas, this captivating novel explores the power of technology and the complexities of human nature.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Peace and Conflict, Ethics, Technological Advancements
