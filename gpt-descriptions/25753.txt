Synopsis: In 'Radio Boys Loyalty; Or, Bill Brown Listens In', the Radio Boys find themselves caught up in a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon a secret code while experimenting with their radio equipment. As they delve deeper into the code's meaning, they uncover a dangerous plot that puts their loyalty and courage to the test. With their radio skills and determination, the Radio Boys embark on a mission to protect their community and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Courage, Technology
