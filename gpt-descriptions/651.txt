Synopsis: 
'Phantasmagoria and Other Poems' by Lewis Carroll is a collection of whimsical and imaginative poems that take readers on a journey through a world of fantastical creatures, nonsensical situations, and clever wordplay. From a ghostly encounter with a skeptical narrator to a conversation between a father and his son about the nature of time, these poems are filled with Carroll's signature wit and charm. Each poem presents a unique and enchanting story, inviting readers to explore the boundaries of reality and imagination.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: 
Imagination, Nonsense, Wordplay
