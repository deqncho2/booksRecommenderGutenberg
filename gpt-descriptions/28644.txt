Synopsis: In 'Beyond the Door' by Philip K. Dick, a seemingly ordinary household object becomes the source of inexplicable and terrifying occurrences. As a family's lives are turned upside down by the mysterious powers of this object, they must confront the boundaries of reality and question their own sanity. With each passing day, the line between the real and the supernatural blurs, leading to a gripping and mind-bending exploration of the unknown.

Genres: Science Fiction, Horror, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Paranormal Phenomena, Psychological Unraveling
