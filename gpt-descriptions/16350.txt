Synopsis: In 'Curiosities of Literature, Vol. 2', Isaac Disraeli takes readers on a captivating journey through the lesser-known corners of literary history. From forgotten authors to peculiar publishing practices, this volume delves into the intriguing stories behind famous works and the curious characters who shaped the literary world. With a blend of wit, insight, and meticulous research, Disraeli uncovers hidden gems and sheds light on the mysteries that surround literature. Prepare to be enthralled by the unexpected and discover a whole new dimension to the books you thought you knew.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, History

Themes: Literary history, Forgotten authors, Publishing practices, Curiosities of the literary world
