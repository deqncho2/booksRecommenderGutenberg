Synopsis: In 'Romulus: Makers of History' by Abbott, Jacob, embark on a captivating journey through ancient Rome as we follow the life of Romulus, the legendary founder of the city. From his humble beginnings to his rise as a powerful leader, this gripping narrative explores the challenges, triumphs, and sacrifices that shaped Romulus into a legendary figure. With political intrigue, epic battles, and personal struggles, this historical tale will transport readers to a time of myth and legend, where the fate of an empire hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Leadership, Power, Destiny, Sacrifice
