Synopsis: In 'Derelicts of Uranus' by J. Harvey Haggard, a group of space explorers embarks on a perilous mission to the mysterious planet Uranus. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter strange creatures, they uncover a shocking secret that could change the fate of humanity forever. With their lives hanging in the balance, the crew must confront their deepest fears and make unimaginable sacrifices to ensure the survival of their species.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Sacrifice, Discovery
