Synopsis: In 'The Dark Flower' by John Galsworthy, a young artist named Mark Lennan finds himself torn between his passion for painting and his desire for love. As he navigates the complexities of relationships and societal expectations, Mark must confront the consequences of his choices and the true nature of his own desires. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century England, this captivating novel explores the depths of human emotions and the pursuit of personal fulfillment.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Art and creativity, Society and expectations
