Synopsis: In 'The Blue Lights: A Detective Story', Detective John Dene finds himself embroiled in a thrilling investigation when a mysterious series of blue lights begins to appear across London. As he delves deeper into the case, Dene uncovers a web of intrigue, secrets, and danger that will test his skills and determination to the limit. With each clue leading him closer to the truth, the detective must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while racing against time to solve the puzzle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Detective work, Intrigue, Danger, Secrets
