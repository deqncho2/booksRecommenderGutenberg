Synopsis: 
'Emblems of Mortality; representing, in upwards of fifty cuts, death seizing all ranks and degrees of people' is a captivating and thought-provoking book that explores the universal theme of mortality through a series of powerful illustrations. Each of the fifty cuts depicts death in various forms, capturing its relentless grip on individuals from all walks of life. From kings and queens to peasants and children, no one is spared from the inevitable embrace of death. This haunting and visually striking collection serves as a poignant reminder of the fragility of life and the equalizing force of death.

Genres: Art, Philosophy, Symbolism

Themes: Mortality, Equality, Transience
