Synopsis: In 'The Debs Decision' by Nearing, Scott, four best friends find themselves caught in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected alliances as they navigate the treacherous world of high society debutante balls. With their futures hanging in the balance, the girls must make a decision that could change their lives forever. Will they choose loyalty or self-preservation? And can they trust anyone, even each other, when the stakes are this high?

Genres: Young Adult, Contemporary Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Social Class, Betrayal
