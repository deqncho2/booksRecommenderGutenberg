Synopsis: In 'The Motor Boys Bound for Home; or, Ned, Bob and Jerry on the Wrecked Troopship' by Clarence Young, the adventurous trio finds themselves in a thrilling and dangerous situation when they stumble upon a wrecked troopship. As they navigate through treacherous waters and encounter unexpected obstacles, the Motor Boys must rely on their wit, courage, and mechanical skills to survive and make it back home. Will they be able to overcome the perils that await them? Find out in this action-packed tale of friendship, resilience, and the power of ingenuity.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Resourcefulness
