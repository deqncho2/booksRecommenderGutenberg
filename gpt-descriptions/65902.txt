Synopsis: In 'Tomorrow the World!' by Robert W. Krepps, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the course of humanity forever. As the world grapples with the implications of this discovery, a thrilling race against time ensues, filled with unexpected twists and turns. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, the characters must confront their own fears and make difficult choices that will shape the future of humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Scientific advancement, Ethical dilemmas, Human potential
