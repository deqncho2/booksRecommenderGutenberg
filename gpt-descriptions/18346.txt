Synopsis: In the futuristic world of 'Null-ABC', a young man named Johnny has been assigned a seemingly mundane job at the Null-ABC office, where he is responsible for sorting and delivering packages. However, Johnny soon discovers that there is more to his job than meets the eye. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the Null-ABC system, he uncovers a dangerous secret that could change the course of society forever. With his life on the line, Johnny must navigate a web of intrigue and deception, all while trying to uncover the truth and protect those he cares about.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Technology and its impact on society, Conspiracy, Identity and self-discovery
