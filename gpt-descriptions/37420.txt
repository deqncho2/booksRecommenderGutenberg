Synopsis: In 'Paint Technology and Tests', Gardner delves into the fascinating world of paint, uncovering its secrets and exploring the various tests used to evaluate its quality. From the chemistry behind paint formulations to the techniques used to assess its durability and performance, this book offers a comprehensive guide for both professionals and enthusiasts alike. With engaging anecdotes and practical advice, Gardner takes readers on a journey through the colorful history and science of paint, revealing the intricate details that make it an essential part of our everyday lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Technology

Themes: Chemistry, Material Science, Quality Assessment
