Synopsis: In "Confessions of an Inquiring Spirit and Some Miscellaneous Pieces," Samuel Taylor Coleridge takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through his personal reflections on religion, philosophy, and the nature of faith. Through a series of letters, Coleridge explores his own doubts, questions, and struggles with religious doctrine, inviting readers to join him in a profound exploration of the human spirit and its search for truth.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Religious Studies

Themes: Faith and doubt, Religious skepticism, Philosophy of religion, Personal introspection
