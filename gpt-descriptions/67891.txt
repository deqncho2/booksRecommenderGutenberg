Synopsis: 
In 'The Two Great Canyons: Excerpts From Letters Written on a Western Journey', Cyrenus Cole takes readers on a captivating adventure through the awe-inspiring landscapes of the American West. Through a series of vividly descriptive letters, Cole shares his experiences exploring the majestic canyons, immersing readers in the breathtaking beauty and untamed wilderness of the region. From encounters with Native American tribes to heart-stopping encounters with wildlife, this collection of letters offers a unique glimpse into the wonders and challenges of the Western frontier. 

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Nature Writing

Themes: Exploration, Natural Beauty, Cultural Encounters
