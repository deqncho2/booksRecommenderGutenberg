Synopsis: 
'Have faith in Massachusetts; 2d ed.: A Collection of Speeches and Messages' by Calvin Coolidge is a captivating compilation of powerful speeches and messages delivered by the former President of the United States. This collection takes readers on a journey through Coolidge's inspiring words, offering a unique perspective on the challenges and triumphs of his time. From addressing economic issues to discussing the importance of individual liberty, Coolidge's speeches resonate with timeless wisdom and a deep belief in the American spirit. This book is a testament to the power of leadership and the enduring values that shape a nation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political speeches, Historical

Themes: Leadership, American values, Economic policy, Individual liberty
