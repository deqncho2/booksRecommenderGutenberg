Synopsis: 
In 'The Legacy of Cain' by Wilkie Collins, a mysterious murder takes place, setting off a chain of events that will unravel a web of secrets and deceit. As the investigation unfolds, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of society, where nothing is as it seems and everyone has something to hide. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Crime and punishment, Betrayal, Revenge, Identity
