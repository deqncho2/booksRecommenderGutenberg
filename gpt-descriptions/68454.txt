Synopsis: 
In "Whale hunting with gun and camera," Roy Chapman Andrews takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of shore-whaling, sharing his firsthand experiences and encounters with these majestic creatures. From the chilling Arctic waters to the tropical seas, Andrews provides a captivating account of the modern whaling industry, shedding light on the habits and behaviors of whales. Through his vivid storytelling, he paints a vivid picture of the exhilarating and dangerous world of hunting whales, both with guns and cameras. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, Natural History

Themes: Conservation, Exploration, Human-animal relationship
