Synopsis: In "Roland Yorke: A Sequel to 'The Channings'," the reader is transported back to the captivating world of the Channing family. This sequel follows the life of Roland Yorke, a young man who finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, love, and ambition. As Roland navigates the complexities of his relationships and strives to achieve his dreams, he must confront the consequences of his choices and the unexpected twists that fate has in store for him. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this novel promises to keep readers eagerly turning the pages until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Family dynamics, Ambition, Love and relationships
