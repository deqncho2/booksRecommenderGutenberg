Synopsis: 
In 'Winning the Wilderness' by Margaret Hill McCarter, a young woman named Mary embarks on a thrilling adventure to the untamed wilderness of Kansas in the late 1800s. Determined to claim her own piece of land and build a life of independence, Mary faces countless challenges and dangers along the way. From battling harsh weather conditions to encountering hostile wildlife and even navigating the complexities of love, Mary's journey is a captivating tale of resilience, courage, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Independence, Survival, Love, Perseverance
