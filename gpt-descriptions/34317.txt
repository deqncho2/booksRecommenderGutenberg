Synopsis: In 'Caribbee' by Thomas Hoover, a young American journalist finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger as he investigates a mysterious disappearance in the Caribbean. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a hidden world of political corruption, ancient secrets, and high-stakes power struggles. With his life on the line, he must navigate treacherous waters and confront his own demons to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Corruption, Power, Secrets
