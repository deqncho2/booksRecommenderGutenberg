Synopsis: In "1914" by John Oxenham, a young man named Dick Thornton finds himself caught up in the tumultuous events of World War I. As he navigates the horrors of the battlefield, he also discovers unexpected love and friendship amidst the chaos. With gripping intensity, the story explores the sacrifices, courage, and resilience of individuals during a time of great upheaval and uncertainty.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Romance

Themes: War, Love, Friendship, Sacrifice, Resilience
