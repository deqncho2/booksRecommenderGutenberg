Synopsis: In "What Dreams May Come," Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton takes readers on a captivating journey through the afterlife, exploring the depths of love, loss, and the power of the human spirit. When a tragic event separates a devoted couple, they find themselves in different realms of existence. As the husband embarks on a perilous quest to reunite with his beloved wife, he discovers the astonishing landscapes and challenges that await him in the afterlife. With breathtaking descriptions and thought-provoking encounters, Atherton weaves a tale that delves into the mysteries of the soul and the eternal bonds that transcend even death.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Spiritual Fiction

Themes: Love, Loss, Redemption, Life after Death, Spiritual Awakening
