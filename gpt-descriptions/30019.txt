Synopsis: In 'Navy Day' by Harry Harrison, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of international intrigue when he stumbles upon a secret that could change the course of history. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where his every move is watched and his life hangs in the balance. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Espionage, Conspiracy, Betrayal
