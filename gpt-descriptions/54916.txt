Synopsis: In 'A Prince to Order' by Wayne Charles Stokes, a young prince finds himself thrust into a world of political intrigue and forbidden love when he is chosen to be the next ruler of his kingdom. As he navigates the treacherous waters of courtly life, he must confront his own identity and make difficult choices that will shape the future of his people. With secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Identity, Power, Love and Sacrifice
