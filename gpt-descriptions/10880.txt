Synopsis: In 'Teddy's Button' by Amy Le Feuvre, a young boy named Teddy embarks on a thrilling adventure that leads him to a mysterious button. Little does he know that this button holds the power to change his life forever. As Teddy grapples with the decision of whether to press the button or not, he is confronted with unexpected challenges and learns valuable lessons about courage, integrity, and the consequences of his choices. This captivating tale takes readers on a journey filled with suspense, moral dilemmas, and the transformative power of a single decision.

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature, Christian Fiction

Themes: Morality, Decision-making, Consequences, Courage
