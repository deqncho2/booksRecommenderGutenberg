Synopsis:
Richard Hannay, a bored and disillusioned man, finds himself caught up in a web of international intrigue after a chance encounter with a mysterious stranger. Accused of a crime he did not commit, Hannay becomes a fugitive, relentlessly pursued by both the police and a sinister organization. With his life on the line, he embarks on a thrilling journey across the Scottish countryside, desperately trying to uncover the truth and clear his name. As he races against time, Hannay must rely on his wits, courage, and resourcefulness to survive and expose a conspiracy that could have far-reaching consequences.

Genres:
Thriller, Mystery, Adventure

Themes:
Espionage, Identity, Survival
