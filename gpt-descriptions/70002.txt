Synopsis: In "The Eternal Savage" by Edgar Rice Burroughs, a young man named Waldo Emerson Smith-Jones embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him from the bustling streets of New York City to the uncharted depths of a mysterious island. After a shipwreck leaves him stranded, Waldo must navigate a treacherous jungle filled with dangerous creatures and hostile tribes. As he fights for survival, he discovers a hidden civilization and encounters a beautiful woman who will forever change his life. With action, romance, and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Love and Sacrifice
