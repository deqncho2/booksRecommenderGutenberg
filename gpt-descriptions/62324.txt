Synopsis: In 'Grifters' Asteroid', a group of skilled con artists find themselves embroiled in a high-stakes heist when they discover a valuable secret hidden within an asteroid. As they navigate a dangerous web of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, the fate of their lives and the future of the galaxy hang in the balance.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Identity, Morality, Survival
