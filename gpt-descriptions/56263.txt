Synopsis: In "How to be a man: a book for boys, containing useful hints on the formation of character," Newcomb, Harvey presents a captivating guide that explores the essential qualities and skills necessary for young boys to become honorable and successful men. Through a series of practical advice, insightful anecdotes, and thought-provoking exercises, the book delves into various aspects of character development, including self-discipline, integrity, perseverance, and empathy. With its engaging narrative and relatable examples, this book serves as a valuable resource for boys seeking to navigate the challenges of adolescence and grow into responsible adults.

Genres: Self-help, Education, Non-fiction

Themes: Character development, Personal growth, Ethics, Responsibility
