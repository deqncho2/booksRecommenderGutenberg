Synopsis: 

'Egypt of the Pharaohs and of the Khedivé' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and enchanting landscapes of ancient Egypt. Written by F. Barham Zincke, this book offers a vivid exploration of the land of the pharaohs, from the awe-inspiring pyramids to the bustling cities along the Nile. With meticulous attention to detail, Zincke paints a vibrant picture of the daily lives, customs, and beliefs of the ancient Egyptians, while also delving into the intriguing era of the Khedivé. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be transported back in time, immersing themselves in the wonders and mysteries of this extraordinary civilization.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Cultural heritage, Exploration
