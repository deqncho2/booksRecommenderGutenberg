Synopsis: 
In the Carquinez Woods is a gripping mystery that follows a young couple, George and Grace, as they embark on a romantic getaway in the secluded Carquinez Woods. However, their idyllic retreat takes a sinister turn when they stumble upon a mysterious crime scene. As they become entangled in a web of secrets and danger, George and Grace must navigate through a series of unexpected twists and turns to uncover the truth and ensure their own survival.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Survival, Uncovering the truth
