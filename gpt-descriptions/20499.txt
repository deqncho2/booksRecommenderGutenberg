Synopsis: 
In 'Afloat; or, Adventures on Watery Trails' by Douglas, Alan, Captain, embark on a thrilling journey across treacherous waters as a group of adventurers set sail on a daring expedition. From encountering mysterious creatures to facing unexpected challenges, their resilience and resourcefulness are put to the test. As they navigate through uncharted territories, the crew must rely on their wits and teamwork to survive the perils that lie ahead. With each twist and turn, the story unfolds with gripping suspense, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies beyond the next wave.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Camaraderie
