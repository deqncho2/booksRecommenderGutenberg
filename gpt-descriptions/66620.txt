Synopsis: 
In the bustling streets of 16th-century Rome, a young and talented artist named Ascanio finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love. Ascanio, a protege of the renowned artist Benvenuto Cellini, becomes entangled in a dangerous plot that could change the course of history. With his artistic skills and cunning mind, Ascanio must navigate the treacherous world of power, betrayal, and passion, all while trying to protect his own secrets and the woman he loves. Ascanio's journey takes him from the opulent palaces of Rome to the dark underbelly of the city, where he must make choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of those around him.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Art and creativity, Love and desire, Power and politics, Loyalty and betrayal
