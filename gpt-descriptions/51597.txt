Synopsis: In 'Gourmet' by Lang, Allen Kim, a renowned chef finds himself entangled in a mysterious culinary competition that pushes the boundaries of taste and morality. As he delves deeper into the world of gastronomy, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to consume him and everything he holds dear. With each dish he creates, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning the true nature of culinary art.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Obsession, Morality, Artistic Expression
