Synopsis: In 'The Prince and the Pauper, Part 2' by Mark Twain, the thrilling sequel to the beloved classic, the lives of the prince and the pauper continue to intertwine in unexpected ways. As they navigate the treacherous world of Tudor England, filled with political intrigue and danger, their identities become increasingly entangled, leading to a series of gripping adventures and heart-wrenching dilemmas. With their fates hanging in the balance, the prince and the pauper must confront their own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape their destinies forever.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Social class, Power and corruption
