Synopsis: 
In "Ruth Fielding at the War Front; or, The Hunt for the Lost Soldier" by Alice B. Emerson, Ruth Fielding finds herself embarking on a thrilling adventure amidst the chaos of World War I. When a soldier goes missing, Ruth is determined to uncover the truth and bring him back safely. As she navigates the dangers of the war front, Ruth's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Determination, Loyalty
