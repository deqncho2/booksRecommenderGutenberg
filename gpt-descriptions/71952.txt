Synopsis: In "The Young Master of Hyson Hall" by Frank R. Stockton, readers are introduced to a young boy named Jack, who unexpectedly inherits a grand estate called Hyson Hall. As Jack navigates his new life as the master of the hall, he encounters a series of mysterious events and encounters that challenge his courage and wit. With the help of his loyal friends and a touch of adventure, Jack must unravel the secrets of Hyson Hall and prove himself worthy of his newfound position.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Identity, Legacy
