Synopsis: 

'Under the Chilian Flag: A Tale of War between Chili and Peru' by Collingwood, Harry is a gripping historical novel set during the 19th century. The story follows the adventures of a young British sailor, Jack Bracebridge, who finds himself caught in the midst of a fierce conflict between Chile and Peru. As Jack navigates the treacherous waters of war, he must confront personal challenges, forge unlikely alliances, and face the harsh realities of battle. With vivid descriptions of naval warfare, political intrigue, and the resilience of the human spirit, this thrilling tale takes readers on an unforgettable journey through a tumultuous period in South American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Survival, Identity
