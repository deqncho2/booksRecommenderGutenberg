Synopsis: 
In "Marjorie Dean, High School Senior" by Josephine Chase, readers are introduced to Marjorie Dean, a determined and ambitious young woman who is about to embark on her final year of high school. As Marjorie navigates the challenges of her senior year, she finds herself facing unexpected obstacles, forming new friendships, and discovering the true meaning of loyalty and perseverance. With her dreams and aspirations on the line, Marjorie must learn to balance her personal life, academic pursuits, and the ever-changing dynamics of her social circle. Will she be able to overcome the hurdles that come her way and achieve her goals?

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Coming-of-Age, School Life

Themes: Ambition, Friendship, Perseverance, Self-discovery
