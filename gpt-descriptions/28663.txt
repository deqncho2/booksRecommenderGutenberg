Synopsis:
In "The Ranger; Or, The Fugitives of the Border," Edward Sylvester Ellis takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed American frontier. Set against the backdrop of the early 19th century, this gripping tale follows the adventures of a young ranger named Henry Ware. When Henry's family is tragically killed by a band of ruthless outlaws, he embarks on a quest for justice and redemption. Along the way, he encounters treacherous landscapes, encounters with Native American tribes, and unexpected alliances. As Henry's determination grows, so does the danger he faces, leading to a heart-pounding climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Historical Fiction
Themes: Justice, Survival, Redemption
