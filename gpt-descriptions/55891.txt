Synopsis: In 'The Family on Wheels', the Oxley family embarks on an extraordinary adventure that takes them on a thrilling journey across the country in their trusty old caravan. From encountering mysterious strangers to facing unexpected challenges, the family's bond is tested as they navigate through the unknown. With each new destination, they uncover secrets that will change their lives forever, leading them to question the true meaning of family and the importance of embracing the unexpected.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Family

Themes: Family bonds, Adventure, Self-discovery
