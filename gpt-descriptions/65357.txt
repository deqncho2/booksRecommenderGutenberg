Synopsis: In "Ismael; an oriental tale. With other poems," Edward Bulwer Lytton takes readers on a captivating journey through the exotic and mysterious Orient. The story follows Ismael, a young man who embarks on a quest to uncover the truth about his origins and find his place in the world. Along the way, he encounters enchanting landscapes, encounters with intriguing characters, and experiences that challenge his perceptions of love, identity, and destiny. Through a collection of beautifully crafted poems, Lytton weaves together a tapestry of emotions, desires, and philosophical musings that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Oriental fiction, Poetry

Themes: Identity, Love, Destiny
