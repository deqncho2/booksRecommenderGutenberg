Synopsis: In 'Tales of Troy: Ulysses, the Sacker of Cities' by Lang, Andrew, embark on an epic journey alongside the cunning and resourceful Ulysses as he navigates the treacherous world of ancient Greece. From the legendary Trojan War to encounters with mythical creatures and gods, Ulysses' courage and wit are put to the ultimate test. As he faces countless trials and tribulations, readers will be captivated by the thrilling adventures and unexpected twists that await him at every turn.

Genres: Adventure, Mythology, Historical Fiction

Themes: Heroism, Fate and Destiny, Loyalty
