Synopsis: In 'The Corner House Girls Snowbound: How They Went Away, What They Discovered, and How It Ended', the Corner House girls find themselves trapped in a snowstorm, leading to a series of unexpected events and thrilling discoveries. As they navigate through the challenges of being snowbound, the girls uncover secrets and embark on a journey that will test their courage and resilience. Will they be able to overcome the obstacles and find their way back home? This captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the mysteries that lie within the snowbound house.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Discovery
