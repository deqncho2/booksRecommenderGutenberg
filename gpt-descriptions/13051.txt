Synopsis: 
In 'Sketches of Japanese Manners and Customs', embark on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Japanese culture, as seen through the eyes of the observant and insightful author, J. M. W. Silver. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the serene beauty of Kyoto, this collection of vivid sketches offers a glimpse into the intricacies of Japanese society, its customs, traditions, and the fascinating nuances that make it truly unique. Through his keen observations and engaging storytelling, Silver paints a vivid picture of a country that seamlessly blends ancient traditions with modernity, leaving readers enchanted and eager to explore more.

Genres: Travelogue, Cultural Exploration, Non-fiction

Themes: Japanese culture, Tradition and Modernity, Observations and Reflections
