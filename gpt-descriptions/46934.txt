Synopsis: In 'Flower Children: The Little Cousins of the Field and Garden', Elizabeth Gordon takes readers on a whimsical journey through the enchanting world of flowers. Through captivating stories and beautiful illustrations, she introduces us to a delightful cast of characters - the flower children. These charming beings, each representing a different flower, come to life and share their unique personalities and adventures. From the mischievous Pansy to the graceful Lily, and the courageous Rose to the shy Violet, the flower children teach us valuable lessons about friendship, kindness, and the beauty of nature. This enchanting book is sure to captivate readers of all ages and ignite a love for flowers and their magical world.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Picture Book

Themes: Nature, Friendship, Kindness, Imagination
