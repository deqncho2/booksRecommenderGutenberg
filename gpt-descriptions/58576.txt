Synopsis: 
In 'Fifty Years a Detective: 35 Real Detective Stories', author Thomas Furlong takes readers on a thrilling journey through his remarkable career as a detective. From solving high-profile murder cases to uncovering hidden secrets, Furlong shares captivating tales that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each story, Furlong delves into the intricate details of his investigations, showcasing his keen eye for clues and his relentless pursuit of justice. As readers follow Furlong's footsteps, they will be immersed in a world of suspense, mystery, and unexpected twists that will leave them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, True Crime, Memoir

Themes: Crime-solving, Justice, Dedication
