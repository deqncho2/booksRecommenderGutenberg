Synopsis: In 'Who Was Lost and Is Found: A Novel' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a mysterious disappearance sets off a chain of events that will test the bonds of family, love, and loyalty. When a young woman vanishes without a trace, her loved ones are left grappling with unanswered questions and the haunting possibility of never finding her. As the search intensifies, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and unexpected connections are made. This gripping tale explores the depths of human emotions and the resilience of the human spirit, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Family, Love, Loyalty, Secrets, Resilience
