Synopsis: In "Wesblock, the autobiography of an automaton," Harry McDonald takes us on a captivating journey through the life of Wesblock, a remarkable automaton with a unique perspective on the world. From his creation in a small workshop to his encounters with humans and other automata, Wesblock's story is filled with unexpected twists and turns. As he navigates the complexities of existence, Wesblock grapples with questions of identity, purpose, and the nature of consciousness. This thought-provoking autobiography offers a fascinating exploration of what it means to be alive and the blurred boundaries between man and machine.

Genres: Science Fiction, Autobiography, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Consciousness, Artificial Intelligence, Existentialism
