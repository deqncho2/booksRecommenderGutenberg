Synopsis: 
"Smiles" is a captivating collection of recitations for girls, written by Alice Lewis Richards. This delightful book takes readers on a journey through various heartwarming and thought-provoking stories, each filled with laughter, tears, and moments of inspiration. From tales of friendship and family bonds to stories of courage and self-discovery, "Smiles" offers a diverse range of recitations that will leave readers both entertained and touched. Whether it's a humorous anecdote or a poignant reflection, this book is sure to bring a smile to your face and warmth to your heart.

Genres: Poetry, Short Stories, Inspirational

Themes: Friendship, Family, Courage, Self-discovery
