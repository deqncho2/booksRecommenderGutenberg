Synopsis: In 'Science and the Criminal', Mitchell, C. Ainsworth delves into the fascinating world of forensic science and its role in solving crimes. Through captivating real-life cases, the author explores the intricate relationship between science and the criminal mind, unraveling the secrets hidden within crime scenes and shedding light on the methods used to catch perpetrators. From fingerprint analysis to bloodstain pattern interpretation, this gripping book takes readers on a thrilling journey through the cutting-edge techniques that have revolutionized the field of criminal investigation.

Genres: True Crime, Non-fiction, Science

Themes: Forensic Science, Criminal Investigation, Justice
