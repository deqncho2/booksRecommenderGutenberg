Synopsis: In 'Christian Mystery: A Chinese Tale, Found in the Portfolio of a Portuguese Friar' by Anonymous, readers are transported to 17th century China, where a Portuguese friar uncovers a perplexing mystery that intertwines faith, culture, and intrigue. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he encounters a cast of captivating characters and uncovers secrets that challenge his own beliefs. With each twist and turn, the friar's quest for truth becomes increasingly perilous, leading to a stunning revelation that will leave readers questioning the boundaries of faith and the power of redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Religious Fiction

Themes: Faith and Doubt, Cultural Clash, Redemption, Secrets and Truth
