Synopsis: In this intriguing guide, Lewis M. Terman presents a condensed version of the Stanford revision of the Binet-Simon intelligence tests, offering readers a fascinating insight into the measurement of human intelligence. With a focus on practical application, Terman provides valuable tools and techniques for assessing intelligence, making this guide an essential resource for educators, psychologists, and anyone interested in understanding the complexities of the human mind.

Genres: Educational, Psychology, Reference

Themes: Intelligence assessment, Cognitive abilities, Psychological testing
