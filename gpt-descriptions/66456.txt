Synopsis: In 'Stellar Vengeance' by Freeman, Frank, a brilliant scientist discovers a hidden secret that could change the fate of the entire galaxy. As he embarks on a perilous journey through uncharted territories, he must confront powerful enemies and make unimaginable sacrifices to protect the truth. With breathtaking action and mind-bending twists, this gripping sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Action, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Betrayal, Redemption
