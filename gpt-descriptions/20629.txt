Synopsis: 
In "Torchy As A Pa" by Ford, Sewell, the lovable and mischievous Torchy finds himself unexpectedly thrust into the role of a father figure. As he navigates the challenges of parenthood, Torchy's quick wit and unconventional methods lead to hilarious and heartwarming adventures. From diaper disasters to bedtime battles, Torchy's journey as a pa is filled with laughter, love, and unexpected life lessons.

Genres: Comedy, Family, Coming-of-age

Themes: Parenthood, Responsibility, Growth
