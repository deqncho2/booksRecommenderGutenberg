Synopsis: In "The Woodcraft Girls at Camp" by Lillian Elizabeth Roy, a group of adventurous young girls embark on a thrilling summer camp experience filled with mystery and excitement. As they navigate the challenges of the wilderness, they uncover hidden secrets and form unbreakable bonds. With each passing day, the girls learn valuable life lessons and discover the true meaning of friendship and resilience. This captivating story will transport readers into a world of outdoor adventures and unforgettable memories.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Exploration
