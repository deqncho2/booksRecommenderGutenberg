Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Tom Kitten' by Beatrix Potter, readers are introduced to mischievous Tom Kitten and his two sisters, Moppet and Mittens. The three kittens are preparing for a visit from their mother's friends, but Tom's playful nature leads him into a series of comical mishaps. As the chaos unfolds, the kittens must find a way to fix the mess before their guests arrive. Will they succeed in saving the day?

Genres: Children's literature, Animal fiction, Picture book

Themes: Mischief, Responsibility, Family bonds
