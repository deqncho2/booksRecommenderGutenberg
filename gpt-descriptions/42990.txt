Synopsis: 
In "In Unfamiliar England: A Record of a Seven Thousand Mile Tour by Motor of the Unfrequented Nooks and Corners, and the Shrines of Especial Interest, in England; With Incursions into Scotland and Ireland," Thomas Dowler Murphy takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the lesser-known and enchanting parts of England, Scotland, and Ireland. With his trusty motor as his companion, Murphy explores the hidden gems, unexplored corners, and sacred sites that are often overlooked by tourists. From picturesque villages to ancient ruins, from breathtaking landscapes to mystical shrines, this travelogue offers a captivating glimpse into the rich history, culture, and natural beauty of these lands. As Murphy immerses himself in the local traditions and encounters fascinating characters along the way, readers are transported to a world of discovery, adventure, and wonder.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, History

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Natural Beauty
