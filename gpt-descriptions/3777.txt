Synopsis: In "Tom Swift and His Electric Rifle; Or, Daring Adventures in Elephant Land," Tom Swift embarks on a thrilling expedition to Africa, where he encounters dangerous wildlife and faces unexpected challenges. Armed with his innovative electric rifle, Tom must navigate through treacherous terrain and outsmart cunning adversaries to protect both himself and the majestic elephants he has come to study. As he delves deeper into the heart of Elephant Land, Tom's courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test, leading to a series of electrifying adventures that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Conservation, Ingenuity
