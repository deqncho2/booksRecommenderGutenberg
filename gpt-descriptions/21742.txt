Synopsis:
'Jarwin and Cuffy' by R. M. Ballantyne is an adventurous tale set in the Caribbean. The story follows two young boys, Jarwin and Cuffy, who find themselves shipwrecked on a deserted island. As they navigate their way through the challenges of survival, they encounter dangerous animals, treacherous pirates, and unexpected allies. With their resourcefulness and determination, Jarwin and Cuffy embark on a thrilling journey that will test their courage and friendship.

Genres:
Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes:
Survival, Friendship, Courage
