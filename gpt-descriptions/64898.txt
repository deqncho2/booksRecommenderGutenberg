Synopsis: In 'Judgments in Vacation' by Sir Edward Abbott Parry, a renowned judge takes a break from his courtroom duties and embarks on a series of intriguing adventures during his vacation. From solving a mysterious murder case to uncovering hidden secrets in a quaint countryside village, the judge's sharp intellect and keen observation skills are put to the test. As he navigates through a web of deception and uncovers the truth, he finds himself entangled in a thrilling journey that challenges his own beliefs and principles.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Legal Fiction

Themes: Justice, Truth, Morality, Perception
