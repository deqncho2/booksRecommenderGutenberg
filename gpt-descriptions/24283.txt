Synopsis: 
In 'Down the River; Or, Buck Bradford and His Tyrants' by Optic, Oliver, readers are taken on an exhilarating journey alongside Buck Bradford, a young and courageous protagonist. Buck finds himself facing a series of challenges and encounters as he embarks on a thrilling adventure down the river. From treacherous rapids to unexpected friendships, Buck's resilience and determination are put to the test. As he navigates through the twists and turns of the river, Buck learns valuable lessons about loyalty, bravery, and the true meaning of freedom.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Freedom
