Synopsis: 
Jennie Baxter, a determined and ambitious journalist, finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when she stumbles upon a mysterious conspiracy that reaches the highest levels of power. As she delves deeper into her investigation, she uncovers shocking secrets that threaten not only her career but also her life. With each revelation, Jennie becomes more entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where trust is a luxury she can't afford. Will she be able to expose the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Journalism, Corruption, Betrayal, Power
