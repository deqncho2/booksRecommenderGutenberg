Synopsis: In 'A Study of Fairy Tales' by Laura Fry Kready, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of fairy tales. Delve into the origins, symbolism, and cultural significance of these timeless stories, as the author unravels the secrets hidden within their pages. From the magical realms of Cinderella and Sleeping Beauty to the dark depths of Little Red Riding Hood and Hansel and Gretel, this book offers a thought-provoking exploration of the power and enduring allure of fairy tales.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Folklore, Cultural Studies

Themes: Symbolism, Cultural Significance, Power of Storytelling
