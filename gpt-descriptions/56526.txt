Synopsis: 
In 'How the Garden Grew' by Maryon, Maud, a young woman named Lily embarks on a journey of self-discovery when she inherits a neglected garden from her late grandmother. As she delves into the secrets of the garden, Lily uncovers a hidden world filled with enchantment and mystery. With the help of new friends and unexpected allies, she must navigate through challenges and overcome her own fears to restore the garden to its former glory. Along the way, Lily learns valuable lessons about love, resilience, and the power of nature.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Nature, Friendship, Resilience
