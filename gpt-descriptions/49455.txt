Synopsis: In 'The Mentor: Guynemer, The Wingèd Sword of France', the reader is transported to the thrilling world of aerial combat during World War I. This captivating volume delves into the life and exploits of Georges Guynemer, a legendary French fighter pilot. As the war rages on, Guynemer's courage and skill in the skies become the stuff of legend, earning him the title of "The Wingèd Sword of France." Through vivid storytelling and stunning visuals, this installment of The Mentor series offers a captivating glimpse into the daring and dangerous world of early aviation.

Genres: Historical, Biography, War

Themes: Heroism, Courage, Sacrifice, Aviation
