Synopsis: In 'The Imitation of Earth' by Stamers, James, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Evelyn Hartman makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything humanity knows about the origins of life. As she delves deeper into her research, she uncovers a hidden truth that could change the course of human history. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, Dr. Hartman must navigate a web of deception, betrayal, and moral dilemmas to protect her discovery and ensure the survival of humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific ethics, Human identity, Existentialism
