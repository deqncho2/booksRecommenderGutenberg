Synopsis:
In 'The Three Brothers; vol. 1/3' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), three brothers find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries as they navigate the complexities of family, love, and ambition. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, the eldest brother, Arthur, struggles to maintain his family's reputation while facing unexpected challenges. Meanwhile, the middle brother, Edward, grapples with his own desires and the consequences of his actions. And the youngest brother, Walter, embarks on a journey of self-discovery that leads him down a path he never could have imagined. As their individual stories intertwine, the brothers must confront their pasts and make choices that will shape their futures. With its richly drawn characters and captivating plot, 'The Three Brothers; vol. 1/3' is a compelling tale of love, betrayal, and the enduring bonds of family.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Family, Love, Ambition, Secrets, Identity
