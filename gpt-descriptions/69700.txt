Synopsis: In "The Case-Book of Sherlock Holmes," the brilliant detective Sherlock Holmes and his loyal companion Dr. John Watson embark on a series of perplexing and thrilling investigations. From a mysterious disappearance on a train to a deadly curse haunting an aristocratic family, Holmes must use his unparalleled deductive skills to unravel the truth behind each baffling case. As the duo delves deeper into the dark underbelly of Victorian London, they encounter a wide array of intriguing characters and face unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Crime Fiction

Themes: Deduction, Crime and Punishment, Victorian Society
