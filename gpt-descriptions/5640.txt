Synopsis: 
In 'Báró Podmaniczky Pál és a norvég Biblia: Elbeszélés a 18 nyelvü nagyapáról 77 nyelven és rovásírással', author Ilona Kutas takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Baron Podmaniczky Pál and his encounter with the Norwegian Bible. This unique narrative explores the fascinating story of an extraordinary man and his quest to unravel the mysteries of language and culture. With 77 languages and various writing systems, the book delves into the intricacies of communication and the power of words. As the plot unfolds, readers will be drawn into a world of discovery, adventure, and the profound connections that language can forge.

Genres: Historical fiction, Linguistic exploration, Adventure

Themes: Language and communication, Cultural diversity, Identity and heritage
