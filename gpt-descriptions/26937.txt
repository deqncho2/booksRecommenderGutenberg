Synopsis: 
'A Syllabus of Kentucky Folk-Songs' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich musical heritage of Kentucky. Combs and Shearin meticulously compile a collection of traditional folk songs, revealing the diverse cultural tapestry woven by the people of Kentucky. From haunting ballads to lively dance tunes, this anthology showcases the raw emotions and timeless stories that have been passed down through generations. As readers delve into the melodies and lyrics, they will be transported to a world where the past and present intertwine, and where the power of music connects people across time and space.

Genres: Folklore, Music, Cultural Studies

Themes: Cultural heritage, Oral tradition, Community
