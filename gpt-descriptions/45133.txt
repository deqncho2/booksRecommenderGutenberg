Synopsis: In 'The Helpers' by Francis Lynde, a young lawyer named John Strickland finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a secret society known as "The Helpers." As he delves deeper into their world, he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his own life but also the fate of the nation. With the help of an enigmatic woman and a group of unlikely allies, Strickland must navigate a treacherous path filled with deception, betrayal, and unexpected twists. Will he be able to unravel the truth and expose the true intentions of The Helpers before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secret societies, Conspiracy, Redemption
