Synopsis: In 'Summer Days' by George M. Baker, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and forbidden love during a picturesque summer in a small coastal town. As she navigates the complexities of family dynamics and societal expectations, Emily discovers that the idyllic facade of her surroundings hides a multitude of hidden desires and buried truths. With each passing day, the heat of the summer intensifies, mirroring the simmering tensions and unspoken passions that threaten to unravel the lives of those around her. Will Emily succumb to the allure of forbidden love or will she find the strength to forge her own path amidst the sweltering heat of the season?

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Forbidden love, Family secrets, Societal expectations
