Synopsis: 
In 'Dialogues of the Dead', two prominent figures from the 18th century, George Lyttelton and Mrs. Elizabeth Montagu, engage in a captivating and thought-provoking conversation beyond the grave. As they discuss various topics, from politics and literature to love and morality, their witty and insightful dialogue offers a unique perspective on the world they left behind. Through their exchange, the reader is invited to reflect on the complexities of human nature and the enduring relevance of their ideas.

Genres: 
Philosophical fiction, Epistolary novel, Historical fiction

Themes: 
Mortality, Human nature, Enlightenment ideals
