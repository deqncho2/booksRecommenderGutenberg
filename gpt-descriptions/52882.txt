Synopsis: 
"The poems of Heine; Complete: Translated into the original metres; with a sketch of his life" is a captivating collection of poetry by the renowned German poet, Heinrich Heine. This anthology presents a comprehensive selection of Heine's poems, translated into their original meters, allowing readers to experience the beauty and rhythm of his words. Accompanied by a fascinating sketch of his life, this book offers a glimpse into the mind of a brilliant poet whose works continue to resonate with readers across generations.

Genres: Poetry, Biography

Themes: Love and longing, Nature and the sublime, Social and political commentary
