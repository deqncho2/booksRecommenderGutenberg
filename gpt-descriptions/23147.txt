Synopsis: In 'Untechnological Employment' by Clinton, Ed M., a young woman named Lily finds herself in a world where technology has been stripped away, leaving society to rely on traditional methods of work and communication. As she navigates this new reality, Lily discovers a hidden network of individuals who have embraced this untechnological lifestyle, each with their own unique skills and secrets. With danger lurking around every corner, Lily must learn to adapt and trust in order to survive in this captivating tale of resilience and human connection.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Adventure

Themes: Technology, Survival, Community
