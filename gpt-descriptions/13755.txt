Synopsis: 
'How to See the British Museum in Four Visits' takes readers on an intriguing journey through one of the world's most renowned museums. With the help of the witty and knowledgeable author, readers are guided through the museum's vast collection, uncovering hidden treasures and fascinating stories along the way. Through four carefully planned visits, readers will gain a deeper understanding of the museum's history, its diverse exhibits, and the significance of each artifact. This engaging guide is a must-read for anyone seeking to explore the British Museum's wonders and unravel the mysteries behind its captivating displays.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, History

Themes: Art and culture, History and heritage, Museum exploration
