Synopsis: In 'A Walk through Leicester: being a Guide to Strangers' by Susannah Watts, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the vibrant city of Leicester. As the author guides us through its streets, she unveils hidden secrets, historical landmarks, and intriguing anecdotes that bring the city's rich past to life. From the bustling markets to the grand architecture, readers will be enthralled by the vivid descriptions and fascinating tales that make Leicester a truly unique destination. 

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Local culture, Heritage
