Synopsis: 
In 'Greener Than You Think' by Moore, Ward, a struggling writer stumbles upon a mysterious substance that has the power to transform the world. As she becomes entangled in a web of corporate greed, ecological disaster, and personal ambition, she must navigate the consequences of her actions and confront the ethical dilemmas that arise. With unexpected twists and a dark sense of humor, this gripping tale explores the unforeseen consequences of human interference with nature.

Genres: Science Fiction, Satire, Environmental Fiction

Themes: Environmentalism, Corporate Power, Moral Responsibility
