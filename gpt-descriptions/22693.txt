Synopsis: 
In 'A Book of Myths' by Jean Lang, embark on a captivating journey through the realms of ancient mythology. From the enchanting tales of Greek gods and goddesses to the heroic exploits of Norse warriors, this collection of myths and legends will transport you to a world of magic, adventure, and timeless wisdom. Discover the origins of legendary heroes, encounter mythical creatures, and witness epic battles that shape the destiny of gods and mortals alike. With vivid storytelling and rich imagery, Lang brings these ancient tales to life, captivating readers of all ages and leaving them yearning for more.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Gods and Mortals, Legends and Lore
