Synopsis: In 'Latent Image' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious photograph that challenges everything he knows about reality. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between truth and illusion blurs. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of perception and the power of the human mind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Perception, Reality vs. Illusion, Scientific Discovery
