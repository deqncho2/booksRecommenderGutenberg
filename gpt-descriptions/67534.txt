Synopsis: In 'The Black Star: A School Story for Boys' by Andrew H. Walpole, a group of boys at a prestigious boarding school stumble upon a mysterious secret hidden within the school's ancient walls. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling adventure that tests their courage, friendship, and loyalty. With each clue they uncover, the boys become entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, leading them to question everything they thought they knew about their school and themselves. Will they be able to solve the puzzle before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Loyalty, Discovery
