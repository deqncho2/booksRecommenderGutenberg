Synopsis: 
In 'The Mercer Boys at Woodcrest', the three adventurous Mercer brothers find themselves embroiled in a mysterious and thrilling series of events when they arrive at the prestigious Woodcrest School. As they navigate the unfamiliar territory of their new boarding school, they uncover a web of secrets and danger that threatens not only their own lives but also the reputation of the school. With their quick thinking and resourcefulness, the Mercer boys must unravel the truth behind the strange occurrences before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Discovery
