Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's False Friend; Or, An Investment in Human Nature' by Standish, Burt L., Frank Merriwell, the beloved hero, finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal. When a supposed friend turns out to be anything but, Frank must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear. As the stakes rise and the danger intensifies, Frank's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to outsmart his false friend and emerge victorious, or will he fall victim to the cunning schemes that threaten to destroy him?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Betrayal, Trust, Resilience
