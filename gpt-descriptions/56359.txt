Synopsis: In 'A Dark Chapter from New Zealand History' by Hawthorne, James, a gripping tale unfolds as a young journalist stumbles upon a long-buried secret that threatens to unravel the very fabric of society. Set against the backdrop of New Zealand's haunting landscapes, the protagonist embarks on a dangerous journey to uncover the truth, facing treacherous individuals and unexpected alliances along the way. As the past collides with the present, the reader is taken on a thrilling ride filled with mystery, suspense, and the enduring power of human resilience.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Identity and Heritage, Consequences of the Past
