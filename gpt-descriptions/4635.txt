Synopsis: In "Tom Swift and His Great Searchlight; or, on the border for Uncle Sam," Tom Swift embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him to the borderlands, where he must utilize his ingenious inventions to aid Uncle Sam. When a mysterious threat looms over the nation, Tom's searchlight becomes a crucial tool in uncovering the truth and protecting his country. As danger escalates, Tom's resourcefulness and determination are put to the test, leading to a high-stakes race against time.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Patriotism, Invention and Innovation, National Security
