Synopsis: In "The Heritage of Unrest" by Gwendolen Overton, a young woman named Amelia finds herself caught in a web of family secrets and hidden desires when she inherits a mysterious ancestral estate. As she delves deeper into the history of her family, Amelia uncovers a haunting past that intertwines with her own present, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and forbidden love. With each revelation, Amelia becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deceit and betrayal, where the line between truth and fiction blurs. Will she be able to unravel the enigma of her heritage before it consumes her?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Forbidden love, Betrayal
