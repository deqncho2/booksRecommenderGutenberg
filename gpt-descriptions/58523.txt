Synopsis: In "Reminiscences of Peace and War" by Sara Agnes Rice Pryor, the reader is transported to the tumultuous times of the American Civil War. Through the eyes of the author, we witness the gripping experiences of a woman living in the heart of the conflict. From the bustling streets of New York City to the battlefields of Virginia, Pryor's vivid storytelling captures the essence of a nation divided and the personal sacrifices made by those caught in the crossfire. With a blend of historical accuracy and heartfelt emotion, this memoir offers a unique perspective on one of the most defining periods in American history.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Love and Loss, Patriotism, Resilience, Gender Roles
