Synopsis: In 'Easy come, easy go' by Edwin L. Sabin, a young man named Jack finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. With the promise of unimaginable riches, Jack embarks on a perilous journey, facing treacherous landscapes, cunning adversaries, and unexpected allies. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Jack discovers that the true value of the treasure may lie in something far more precious than gold. Will he be able to overcome the challenges and uncover the ultimate secret hidden within the map?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Treasure hunting, Perseverance, Friendship
