Synopsis: 
In 'On the Equator', Harry De Windt takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of Africa. As he embarks on a daring expedition, De Windt encounters treacherous landscapes, encounters with dangerous wildlife, and unexpected encounters with local tribes. With vivid descriptions and a captivating narrative, this travelogue offers a unique perspective on the challenges and wonders of exploring the equatorial region.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural encounters
