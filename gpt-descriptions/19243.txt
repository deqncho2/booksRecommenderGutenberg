Synopsis: In 'The Advent of Divine Justice', Shoghi Effendi takes readers on a thought-provoking journey that explores the challenges faced by humanity in the modern world. Through a series of powerful letters, he addresses the pressing issues of racial prejudice, moral decay, and the erosion of spiritual values. With a captivating blend of wisdom and urgency, Effendi offers profound insights and guidance on how to navigate these turbulent times and build a more just and harmonious society.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious, Social Commentary

Themes: Racial prejudice, Moral decay, Spiritual values, Social justice, Unity
