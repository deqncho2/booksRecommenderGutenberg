Synopsis: In 'An Anglo-American Alliance: A Serio-Comic Romance and Forecast of the Future', Casparian, Gregory takes readers on a thrilling journey through a world where the United States and Great Britain form an unexpected alliance. As the story unfolds, readers are introduced to a cast of eccentric characters, embarking on a series of misadventures that blend humor, romance, and political intrigue. With unexpected twists and turns, this novel offers a unique perspective on the future of these two nations and the challenges they face.

Genres: Satire, Romance, Political Fiction

Themes: International relations, Cultural differences, Power dynamics
