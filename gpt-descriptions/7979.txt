Synopsis: 
In 'Jean-Christophe, Volume I' by Romain Rolland, we are introduced to the titular character, a passionate and talented musician who embarks on a journey of self-discovery and artistic expression. Set against the backdrop of late 19th-century Europe, Jean-Christophe's story takes us through his struggles, triumphs, and encounters with a diverse cast of characters. From his humble beginnings in a small German town to his pursuit of success in Paris, Jean-Christophe's unwavering dedication to his art and his unwavering spirit make for a captivating and inspiring tale.

Genres: 
Historical fiction, Bildungsroman, Music

Themes: 
Artistic expression, Individualism, Social and political change
