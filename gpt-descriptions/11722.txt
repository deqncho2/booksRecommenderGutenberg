Synopsis: 
In 'First Lessons in Geography: Or, Introduction to "Youth's Manual of Geography"', James Monteith takes young readers on an exciting journey around the world, exploring different continents, countries, and cultures. Through vivid descriptions and captivating illustrations, this educational book introduces readers to the wonders of geography, from the highest mountains to the deepest oceans. Join the author as he uncovers fascinating facts about various regions, landmarks, and natural phenomena, making learning an adventure that will ignite curiosity and expand young minds.

Genres: Educational, Children's Literature, Geography

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Diversity, Natural Landmarks, Curiosity
