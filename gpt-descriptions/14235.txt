Synopsis: 
In "Más vale maña que fuerza: Proverbio en un acto; with notes, exercises, and vocabulary" by Manuel Tamayo y Baus, the reader is transported to a small village in Spain, where a series of mysterious events unfold. The story revolves around a group of intriguing characters who find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. As the plot thickens, the reader is kept on the edge of their seat, trying to unravel the truth behind the village's dark past and the motivations of its inhabitants. With its clever twists and turns, this captivating play explores the power of wit and cunning over brute force, leaving the reader questioning the true nature of strength.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Power dynamics, Morality
