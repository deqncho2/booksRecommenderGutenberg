Synopsis: 
'Farm Ballads' by Carleton, Will is a collection of heartfelt and vividly descriptive poems that transport readers to the rural landscapes of America. Through the use of lyrical verses, Carleton captures the joys, struggles, and everyday experiences of farmers, painting a vivid picture of their lives. From tales of love and loss to humorous anecdotes and reflections on nature, these ballads offer a glimpse into the beauty and hardships of rural life. 

Genres: Poetry, Regional Literature, American Literature

Themes: Rural life, Nature, Love and Loss, Hardship, Humor
