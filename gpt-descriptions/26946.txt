Synopsis: In 'The Defence of Stonington (Connecticut) Against a British Squadron, August 9th to 12th, 1814', author J. Hammond Trumbull takes readers on a thrilling journey back to a pivotal moment in American history. Set during the War of 1812, this gripping account unveils the harrowing events that unfolded when a small coastal town found itself under attack by a formidable British squadron. Trumbull's vivid storytelling and meticulous research bring to life the bravery, resilience, and resourcefulness of the townspeople as they valiantly defend their homes and way of life against overwhelming odds. As the battle rages on, readers will be captivated by the courage and determination displayed by both sides, making this a truly unforgettable tale of heroism and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, War memoir

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Resilience, Community, Conflict
