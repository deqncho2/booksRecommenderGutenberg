Synopsis: Join the adventurous journey of the Land-Yacht "Wanderer" as it embarks on a thrilling 1,300-mile cruise across unknown territories. Gordon Stables takes readers on a captivating ride, filled with unexpected encounters, breathtaking landscapes, and the challenges of navigating uncharted paths. As the Land-Yacht "Wanderer" ventures into the unknown, the story unfolds with excitement, danger, and the triumph of human spirit. 

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Adventure, Resilience, Discovery
