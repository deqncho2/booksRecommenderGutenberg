Synopsis: In 'The Valley of the Giants' by Peter B. Kyne, a young engineer named Bryce Cardigan inherits a vast timber empire in Northern California. As he navigates the treacherous world of business and battles against ruthless competitors, Cardigan finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and revenge. With the fate of his family's legacy hanging in the balance, Cardigan must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to protect what is rightfully his.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Drama

Themes: Power and greed, Love and loyalty
