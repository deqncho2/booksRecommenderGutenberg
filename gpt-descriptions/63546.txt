Synopsis: In 'The Undefeated' by J. C. Snaith, a mysterious and enigmatic stranger arrives in a small English village, captivating the locals with his charm and charisma. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the villagers, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of the undefeated is revealed. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of power and the lengths one will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Power, Secrets, Identity
