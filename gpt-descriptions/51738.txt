Synopsis: In 'Julian Mortimer: A Brave Boy's Struggle for Home and Fortune' by Castlemon, Harry, follow the captivating journey of Julian Mortimer, a young boy who finds himself thrust into a world of adventure and adversity. From his humble beginnings to his quest for fortune, Julian's determination and bravery will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As he faces numerous challenges and encounters unexpected allies and enemies, Julian's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the test. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles in his path and find his true home and fortune?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Perseverance, Identity, Family
