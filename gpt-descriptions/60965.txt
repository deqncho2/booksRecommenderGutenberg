Synopsis: In 'A Righted Wrong: A Novel. Volume 2 (of 3)' by Edmund Yates, the gripping tale continues as the protagonist, haunted by a dark secret, embarks on a dangerous journey to uncover the truth. As the plot thickens, unexpected alliances are formed, and shocking revelations come to light, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat. With each page turned, the tension builds, leading to a climactic confrontation that will change everything. This second volume of the trilogy promises to captivate readers with its intricate plot twists and compelling characters.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Redemption, Justice
