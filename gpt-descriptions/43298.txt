Synopsis: In 'The Riddle and the Ring; or, Won by Nerve' by MacLaren, Gordon, a young man named Jack finds himself entangled in a mysterious and dangerous adventure when he stumbles upon an ancient riddle and a peculiar ring. As he delves deeper into the enigma, Jack must rely on his wit, courage, and nerve to unravel the secrets hidden within the riddle and the ring. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale takes readers on a suspenseful journey filled with intrigue, suspense, and the power of human determination.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Quest for knowledge, Bravery in the face of danger
