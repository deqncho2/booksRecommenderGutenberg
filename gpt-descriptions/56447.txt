Synopsis: In 'The Tunnel: Pilgrimage, Volume 4', Dorothy M. Richardson takes readers on a captivating journey through the mind of her protagonist, Miriam Henderson. As Miriam navigates the complexities of her relationships and grapples with her own identity, she embarks on a pilgrimage of self-discovery. Through vivid and introspective prose, Richardson delves into the depths of Miriam's thoughts and emotions, revealing the profound impact of her experiences. As Miriam continues her journey, readers are drawn into a world of introspection, self-reflection, and the search for meaning.

Genres: Modernist literature, psychological fiction, stream of consciousness

Themes: Identity, self-discovery, relationships, introspection
