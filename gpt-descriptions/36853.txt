Synopsis: 
In 'A Very Naughty Girl' by L. T. Meade, readers are introduced to a young woman named Muriel. Despite her innocent appearance, Muriel possesses a mischievous and rebellious spirit that often leads her into trouble. As she navigates through various social circles and encounters unexpected situations, Muriel's audacious nature becomes both a source of amusement and concern for those around her. With her quick wit and fearless demeanor, Muriel's escapades are sure to captivate readers and keep them guessing what she will do next.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Rebellion, Independence, Consequences
