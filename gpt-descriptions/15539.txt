Synopsis: In this captivating issue of 'The Great Round World and What Is Going On In It', readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the latest news and events of May 13, 1897. From the mysterious disappearance of a famous explorer to the discovery of a hidden treasure, this weekly magazine for boys and girls is filled with fascinating stories that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each turn of the page, young readers will uncover a world of adventure, intrigue, and discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, Discovery
