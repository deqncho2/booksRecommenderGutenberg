Synopsis: 
'A Lute of Jade: Selections from the Classical Poets of China' is a captivating collection of ancient Chinese poetry, carefully curated and translated by L. Cranmer-Byng. This anthology takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the rich tapestry of Chinese culture, exploring themes of love, nature, spirituality, and the human experience. From the lyrical verses of Li Bai to the introspective musings of Du Fu, these timeless poems offer profound insights into the beauty and complexity of life. With its elegant prose and evocative imagery, 'A Lute of Jade' invites readers to immerse themselves in the enchanting world of classical Chinese poetry.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology, Translation
Themes: Love, Nature, Spirituality, Human Experience
