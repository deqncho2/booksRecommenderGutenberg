Synopsis: In "Oscar Wilde, His Life and Confessions. Volume 1 (of 2)" by Harris, Frank, delve into the captivating life of the renowned playwright and wit, Oscar Wilde. This intimate biography takes readers on a journey through Wilde's fascinating personal and professional experiences, shedding light on his flamboyant personality, his scandalous relationships, and his brilliant literary achievements. From his rise to fame as a celebrated writer to his tragic downfall and imprisonment, this volume offers a compelling exploration of the complex and enigmatic figure that was Oscar Wilde.

Genres: Biography, Non-fiction, Literary Criticism

Themes: Artistic genius, Social conventions, Sexuality, Scandal, Society's judgment
