Synopsis: 
'Eight Cousins' by Louisa May Alcott follows the story of Rose Campbell, a young girl who finds herself in the care of her bachelor uncles after the death of her parents. As Rose navigates her new life, she is surrounded by her eight male cousins, each with their own unique personalities and interests. Through her interactions with her cousins, Rose learns valuable life lessons, discovers her own passions, and experiences the joys and challenges of growing up. 

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Family, Fiction

Themes: 
Family, Friendship, Self-discovery
