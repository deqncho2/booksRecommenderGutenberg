Synopsis: In 'The Boy in the Bush' by Richard Rowe, a young boy mysteriously appears in the Australian outback, sparking curiosity and intrigue among the locals. As they try to uncover the truth behind his arrival, they are drawn into a captivating tale of adventure, secrets, and unexpected connections. With each revelation, the boy's presence becomes more enigmatic, leaving everyone wondering about his origins and the impact he will have on their lives.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Belonging, Discovery
