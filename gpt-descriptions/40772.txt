Synopsis: In 'Human Animals' by Frank Hamel, a mysterious event causes the inhabitants of a small town to lose their ability to sleep. As the sleepless nights take their toll, the community descends into chaos, revealing the true nature of humanity. With a diverse cast of characters, including a troubled doctor, a sleep researcher, and a young girl with a secret, this gripping novel explores the boundaries of sanity and the lengths people will go to survive. As the town's inhabitants grapple with their newfound insomnia, they must confront their deepest fears and desires, leading to unexpected alliances and shocking revelations. 

Genres: Psychological thriller, dystopian fiction, mystery

Themes: Human nature, survival, the fragility of sanity
