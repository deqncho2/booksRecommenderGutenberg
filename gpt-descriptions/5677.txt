Synopsis: 
In the small town of Leesville, Jimmie Higgins, a passionate and idealistic young man, finds himself caught in the midst of a tumultuous labor movement. As he becomes more involved in the fight for workers' rights, Jimmie's life takes unexpected turns, leading him to question his own beliefs and confront the harsh realities of class struggle. With his unwavering determination, Jimmie becomes a symbol of hope for the oppressed, but also faces personal sacrifices that challenge his commitment to the cause.

Genres: Political fiction, Social realism

Themes: Labor rights, Social inequality, Idealism vs. pragmatism
