Synopsis: In 'The Honour of the Flag' by William Clark Russell, a young sailor named Jack Rogers finds himself caught in a dangerous web of intrigue and betrayal aboard a British warship. As he navigates the treacherous waters of loyalty and duty, Jack must confront his own moral compass and make choices that will test his courage and honor. With vivid descriptions of life at sea and a gripping plot filled with unexpected twists, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Naval Fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Betrayal, Morality
