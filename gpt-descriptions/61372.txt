Synopsis: 
In 'The Cruise of the Catalpa: A Poem' by John Breslin, embark on a thrilling journey across the vast ocean as a group of courageous men set out to rescue prisoners from a remote island. Filled with danger, suspense, and unexpected twists, this epic poem will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning each page to discover the fate of these daring adventurers.

Genres: 
Epic Poetry, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Courage, Freedom, Redemption
