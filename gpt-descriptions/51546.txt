Synopsis: 
In 'Handyman' by Banta, Frank, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious incidents that leave the community on edge. When a young handyman arrives in town, he becomes entangled in the web of secrets and lies that shroud the town's dark past. As he delves deeper into the mysteries, he uncovers shocking truths that threaten to unravel the fabric of the entire community. With danger lurking around every corner, the handyman must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and bring justice to the town.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Justice and truth
