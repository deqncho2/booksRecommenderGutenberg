Synopsis: In "Tom Swift and His Big Tunnel; Or, The Hidden City of the Andes," Tom Swift embarks on a thrilling adventure to the Andes Mountains, where he discovers a hidden city with ancient secrets. With his ingenious inventions and unwavering determination, Tom must overcome treacherous obstacles and outsmart dangerous adversaries to uncover the mysteries that lie within the depths of the Andes. This action-packed tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats as they join Tom Swift on his quest for discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Invention, Ancient Civilizations
