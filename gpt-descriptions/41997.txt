Synopsis: In "The Light Keepers: A Story of the United States Light-house Service" by James Otis, readers are transported to the rugged coastlines of 19th-century America, where they will follow the captivating journey of young Ben, who dreams of becoming a light keeper like his father. As Ben navigates the challenges and dangers of life on the lighthouse, he discovers secrets that will change his perception of the world around him. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding moments, this historical adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the mysteries hidden within the walls of the lighthouse.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Family, Perseverance, Discovery
