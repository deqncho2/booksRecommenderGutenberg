Synopsis: In "An Englishman Looks at the World," H.G. Wells fearlessly delves into a wide range of contemporary topics, offering his unrestrained and thought-provoking remarks. From politics and education to religion and social issues, Wells presents a captivating exploration of the world around him, challenging conventional wisdom and sparking lively debates. With his characteristic wit and insight, he invites readers to question their own beliefs and consider alternative perspectives, making this collection of essays a captivating journey through the mind of one of the greatest thinkers of his time.

Genres: Non-fiction, Essay collection, Social commentary

Themes: Politics, Education, Religion, Social issues, Critical thinking
