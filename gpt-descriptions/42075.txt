Synopsis:
Step into the scandalous world of 18th-century London as 'Harris's List of Covent Garden Ladies: or Man of Pleasure's Kalendar for the Year, 1788' takes you on a captivating journey through the secret lives of the city's most alluring courtesans. This anonymous work unveils the hidden desires, seductive charms, and intriguing stories of these women, providing a tantalizing glimpse into a world of pleasure, power, and forbidden love. From the elegant to the audacious, each lady's tale is a captivating blend of passion, danger, and the pursuit of freedom. As you delve into the pages of this provocative guide, prepare to be enthralled by the secrets and scandals that lie behind the closed doors of Covent Garden.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Erotica, Social Commentary

Themes:
Sexuality, Power dynamics, Gender roles, Social class
