Synopsis: In 'X Y Z: A Detective Story' by Anna Katharine Green, a brilliant detective is faced with a perplexing case involving a mysterious murder, a missing artifact, and a web of deceit. As the detective delves deeper into the investigation, they uncover a series of unexpected twists and turns that lead them to question everything they thought they knew. With time running out, the detective must unravel the truth before more lives are lost and the secrets buried in the shadows are revealed.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
