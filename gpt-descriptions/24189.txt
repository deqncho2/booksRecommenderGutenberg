Synopsis: In 'Something Will Turn Up' by David Mason, a down-on-his-luck protagonist finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden artifact. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the object, he becomes entangled in a thrilling race against time, facing unexpected allies and formidable adversaries along the way. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices that could alter the course of his life forever. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Fate and destiny, Redemption, Trust and betrayal
