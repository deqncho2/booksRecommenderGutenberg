Synopsis: In 'Excuse Me!' by Rupert Hughes, a chance encounter between a young woman and a mysterious stranger sets off a chain of events that will test their courage, challenge their beliefs, and ultimately lead them on a thrilling journey of self-discovery. As they navigate through a web of secrets and deception, they must confront their pasts and make difficult choices that will determine their future. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Trust, Fate
