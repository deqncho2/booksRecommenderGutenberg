Synopsis: 
In 'The Childerbridge Mystery' by Boothby, Guy, a young and ambitious lawyer named Hugh Moneylaw finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when he is summoned to the eerie and isolated village of Childerbridge. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the village, he uncovers a web of deceit, hidden identities, and long-buried scandals. With each revelation, Hugh becomes more determined to uncover the truth, even if it means putting his own life at risk. As the mystery unravels, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey filled with suspense, unexpected twists, and a race against time to solve the enigma of Childerbridge.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Secrets, Justice
