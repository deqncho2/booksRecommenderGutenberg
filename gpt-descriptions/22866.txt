Synopsis: In "An Ounce of Cure" by Alan Edward Nourse, a young girl's life takes an unexpected turn when she experiences heartbreak for the first time. As she tries to cope with the pain, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, leading her to a surprising solution that will forever change her perspective on life and love.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Romance

Themes: Adolescence, Heartbreak, Resilience, Self-discovery
