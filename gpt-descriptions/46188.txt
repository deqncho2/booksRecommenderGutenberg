Synopsis: Elsie's Young Folks in Peace and War follows the adventures of Elsie and her friends as they navigate through both peaceful and tumultuous times. From joyful family gatherings to the challenges of war, Elsie's resilience and determination are put to the test. As she faces personal trials and witnesses the impact of war on her loved ones, Elsie's unwavering spirit shines through, inspiring those around her. This captivating tale takes readers on a journey filled with love, courage, and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Family Drama

Themes: Resilience, Love, Patriotism, Family Bonds
