Synopsis: Elsie at Home follows the captivating journey of Elsie, a young woman who finds herself torn between duty and desire as she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations. Set against the backdrop of a charming countryside estate, Elsie's world is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger enters her life, leading her on a path of self-discovery and unexpected revelations. As she grapples with her own desires and the pressures of society, Elsie must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Identity and Self-Discovery, Society and Expectations
