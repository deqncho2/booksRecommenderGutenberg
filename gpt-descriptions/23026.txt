Synopsis: In 'The Phantom of the River' by Edward Sylvester Ellis, a young boy named Ralph finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he encounters a mysterious figure known as the Phantom of the River. As Ralph delves deeper into the secrets of the river and its phantom, he uncovers a web of danger, intrigue, and hidden treasures. With his courage and determination, Ralph must navigate treacherous waters and outsmart cunning adversaries to unravel the truth behind the enigmatic Phantom of the River.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Secrets and Hidden Treasures
