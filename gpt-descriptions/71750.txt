Synopsis: 
'Leaves from a middy's log' follows the thrilling adventures of a young naval officer as he embarks on a perilous journey across the high seas. From encounters with pirates to navigating treacherous storms, the protagonist's log entries provide a captivating glimpse into the life of a sailor during the 19th century. As the middy faces numerous challenges and tests his mettle, readers are taken on a thrilling voyage filled with danger, excitement, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Maritime

Themes: Coming of age, Courage, Survival
