Synopsis: In 'A Secret of the Lebombo' by Bertram Mitford, a young Englishman embarks on a thrilling adventure in the untamed wilderness of South Africa. Drawn by the allure of hidden treasure and the mystery surrounding an ancient tribe, he finds himself entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and forbidden love. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the Lebombo, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, face formidable foes, and uncover the truth that could change everything. This gripping tale of exploration and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Treasure Hunting, Forbidden Love, Betrayal
