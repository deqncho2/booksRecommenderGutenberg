Synopsis: 
'Understood Betsy' by Dorothy Canfield Fisher is a heartwarming and transformative coming-of-age story that follows the journey of a young girl named Betsy. After living a sheltered and overprotected life, Betsy is suddenly thrust into a new environment that challenges her in unexpected ways. As she navigates through the ups and downs of her new experiences, Betsy discovers her own strength, resilience, and the power of self-discovery. With endearing characters and a captivating narrative, 'Understood Betsy' takes readers on a delightful and inspiring adventure.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Resilience, Independence, Growth
