Synopsis: In 'The Eagles Gather' by Joseph E. Kelleam, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious events that threaten to tear the community apart. As the townspeople struggle to uncover the truth behind these unsettling occurrences, they soon realize that their own secrets and hidden desires may be the key to solving the enigma. With tensions rising and trust waning, the residents must come together to confront their darkest fears and unravel the web of deceit that has entangled their lives.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Community dynamics, Human nature
