Synopsis: In 'The Living Mummy' by Pratt, Ambrose, a renowned archaeologist uncovers an ancient tomb in Egypt, unleashing a curse that transforms him into a living mummy. As he struggles to find a way to reverse the curse, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and danger, facing ancient evils and supernatural forces that threaten his very existence. With time running out, he must navigate treacherous paths and make unimaginable sacrifices to break free from the curse and save himself and those he holds dear.

Genres: Horror, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Ancient curses, Redemption, Sacrifice
