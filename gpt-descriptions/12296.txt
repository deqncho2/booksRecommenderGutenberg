Synopsis: 

In "Camps and Trails in China: A Narrative of Exploration, Adventure, and Sport in Little-Known China," Roy Chapman Andrews and Yvette Borup Andrews take readers on an exhilarating journey through the uncharted landscapes of China. From the rugged mountains to the dense forests, the authors recount their thrilling experiences of exploration, encountering unique wildlife, and engaging in daring sporting activities. As they navigate through the unknown, they uncover hidden treasures and encounter unexpected challenges that test their resilience and determination. This captivating narrative offers a fascinating glimpse into the lesser-known regions of China, filled with adventure, discovery, and the spirit of exploration.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Wildlife, Cultural Encounter
