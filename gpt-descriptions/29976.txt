Synopsis: In 'Weak on Square Roots' by Burton Russell, a brilliant mathematician finds himself entangled in a mysterious world of mathematical secrets and hidden codes. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he discovers that the answers lie not only in the realm of numbers but also in the depths of his own mind. With each revelation, the line between reality and imagination blurs, leading him on a thrilling journey where the pursuit of knowledge becomes a dangerous obsession.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Mathematical Fiction

Themes: Obsession, Reality vs. Imagination, Mathematical Enigmas
