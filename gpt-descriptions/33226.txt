Synopsis: 
In 'No Moss; Or, The Career of a Rolling Stone' by Castlemon, Harry, we follow the captivating journey of a restless protagonist who refuses to settle down. From one adventure to another, the protagonist's life is filled with unexpected twists and turns, as they navigate through various professions and encounters with intriguing characters. As the story unfolds, readers will be drawn into a world of constant movement and excitement, wondering where the rolling stone will finally come to rest.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Restlessness, Identity, Self-discovery
