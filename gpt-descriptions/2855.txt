Synopsis:
'Elissa; Or, The Doom of Zimbabwe' is a captivating adventure novel by H. Rider Haggard that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the mysterious and ancient land of Zimbabwe. The story follows the protagonist, Leonard Outram, as he embarks on a perilous quest to uncover the secrets of a lost civilization and rescue the woman he loves. As Outram delves deeper into the heart of Zimbabwe, he encounters treacherous landscapes, dangerous adversaries, and unimagined wonders, all while facing his own inner demons. With its blend of action, romance, and exploration, 'Elissa; Or, The Doom of Zimbabwe' promises an enthralling tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres:
Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes:
Exploration, Love and Sacrifice, Ancient Civilizations
