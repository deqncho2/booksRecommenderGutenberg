Synopsis: 
In the third edition of 'Wakeman's Handbook of Irish Antiquities', author W. F. Wakeman takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and mysterious world of ancient Ireland. From the enigmatic stone circles of the Boyne Valley to the haunting ruins of medieval castles, this comprehensive guide unveils the secrets and legends hidden within Ireland's ancient sites. With vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, Wakeman's handbook offers a fascinating exploration of the country's archaeological wonders, shedding light on the customs, beliefs, and daily lives of its ancient inhabitants. Whether you are a history enthusiast, a lover of Irish culture, or simply seeking to uncover the hidden stories of the past, this book is sure to ignite your curiosity and transport you to a bygone era.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Archaeology

Themes: Irish history, Ancient civilizations, Archaeological exploration
