Synopsis: 
'The Russian Story Book: Containing tales from the song-cycles of Kiev and Novgorod and other early sources' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and enchanting tales of ancient Russia. From heroic warriors and cunning witches to magical creatures and forbidden love, this collection of stories offers a glimpse into the vibrant tapestry of Russian mythology and tradition. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will be transported to a world where legends come alive and where the power of storytelling holds the key to understanding the soul of a nation.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Magic, Love, Tradition
