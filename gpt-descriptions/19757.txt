Synopsis: 

In "Return Loads' to Increase Transport Resources by Avoiding Waste of Empty Vehicle Running," the United States Council of National Defense's Highways Transport Committee presents a compelling exploration of a crucial aspect of transportation logistics. This insightful work delves into the concept of return loads, which aims to optimize transport resources by eliminating the wasteful practice of empty vehicle running. By examining the economic and environmental implications of this issue, the committee offers practical solutions to enhance efficiency and reduce unnecessary costs in the transportation industry. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Transportation, Logistics

Themes: Resource optimization, Efficiency, Economic impact, Environmental sustainability
