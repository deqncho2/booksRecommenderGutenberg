Synopsis: 

In 'How Jerusalem Was Won: Being the Record of Allenby's Campaign in Palestine', Massey takes readers on a gripping journey through the remarkable military campaign led by General Allenby during World War I. Through vivid descriptions and firsthand accounts, Massey paints a vivid picture of the challenges faced by the British forces as they strive to capture the holy city of Jerusalem from the Ottoman Empire. From the scorching deserts to the treacherous mountain passes, the reader is immersed in the intense battles and strategic maneuvers that ultimately lead to the historic victory. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, War memoir

Themes: Courage and resilience in the face of adversity, the significance of historical landmarks, the impact of war on individuals and societies
