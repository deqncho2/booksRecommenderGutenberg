Synopsis: In the December 1885 issue of Lippincott's Magazine, readers are transported to a captivating world filled with mystery, romance, and intrigue. From the bustling streets of London to the exotic landscapes of India, this collection of stories takes readers on a thrilling journey through various captivating narratives. From a young woman's quest for justice to a forbidden love affair that defies societal norms, each tale is sure to leave readers eagerly turning the pages, craving for more.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Justice, Forbidden Love, Society and Norms
