Synopsis: In "Ticonderoga: A Story of Early Frontier Life in the Mohawk Valley," James, G. P. R. takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of colonial America. Set during the French and Indian War, the story follows the adventures of a young Englishman named Lionel Lincoln, who finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous conflict between rival nations and Native American tribes. As Lionel navigates the treacherous landscapes and encounters both friend and foe, he must confront his own identity and make choices that will shape his destiny. Filled with action, suspense, and unexpected twists, this historical novel paints a vivid picture of life on the frontier and the struggles faced by those who dared to venture into the unknown.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Identity, Survival, Conflict, Exploration
