Synopsis: In 'How to Marry Well' by Duchess, a young woman named Elizabeth finds herself thrust into the world of high society and must navigate the treacherous waters of courtship and marriage. With the help of her witty and unconventional grandmother, Elizabeth learns the art of strategic matchmaking, all while discovering the true meaning of love and happiness. As she encounters a cast of colorful characters and faces unexpected challenges, Elizabeth's journey to find a suitable partner becomes a delightful and captivating adventure.

Genres: Romance, Comedy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love, Social Status, Family Dynamics, Self-discovery
