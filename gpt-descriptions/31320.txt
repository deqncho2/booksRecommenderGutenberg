Synopsis: In 'The Wooing of Calvin Parks', a young woman named Ruth embarks on a journey to win the heart of the elusive and enigmatic Calvin Parks. As she navigates through unexpected twists and turns, Ruth discovers the power of love, self-discovery, and the importance of taking risks. Will she be able to capture Calvin's heart, or will her efforts be in vain?

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Love, Self-discovery, Taking risks
