Synopsis: In 'The Boy With the U. S. Survey' by Rolt-Wheeler, Francis, we follow the thrilling adventures of young Jack, who joins a team of surveyors exploring the vast and untamed American West. As they navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter Native American tribes, and face unexpected dangers, Jack's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test. Along the way, he learns valuable lessons about friendship, perseverance, and the importance of preserving the natural world. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Nature Conservation
