Synopsis: In the idyllic English countryside, a group of eccentric characters gather at the vibrant Crome Yellow estate for a summer retreat. Among them is Denis Stone, a young poet who finds himself entangled in a web of love, intellectual debates, and societal expectations. As the days unfold, secrets are revealed, passions ignite, and the tranquil setting becomes a backdrop for personal and philosophical exploration. With wit and satire, Aldous Huxley paints a vivid portrait of a society on the brink of change, where tradition clashes with modernity, and individuals grapple with their desires and aspirations.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and desire, Intellectualism and creativity, Society and tradition
