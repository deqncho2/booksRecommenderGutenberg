Synopsis: 
In 'George in Camp; or, Life on the Plains' by Castlemon, Harry, young George is thrilled to join his older brother on a journey to the wild and untamed American frontier. As they venture into the vast plains, George encounters thrilling adventures, dangerous encounters, and unexpected friendships. From facing hostile Native American tribes to navigating treacherous landscapes, George's courage and resilience are put to the test. Will he be able to survive the challenges of life on the plains and prove himself in this rugged and unforgiving environment?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Survival, Brotherhood, Exploration
