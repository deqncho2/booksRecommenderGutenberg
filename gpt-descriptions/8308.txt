Synopsis: In the Book of Ruth, a young widow named Ruth finds herself in a foreign land, facing a future of uncertainty. Determined to support her mother-in-law, Naomi, she embarks on a journey that leads her to unexpected encounters and life-changing decisions. As Ruth's path intertwines with that of a wealthy landowner named Boaz, their lives become entangled in a tale of love, loyalty, and redemption. Set against the backdrop of ancient Israel, this captivating story explores the power of faith, the resilience of the human spirit, and the transformative nature of love.

Genres: Biblical fiction, Historical fiction, Romance

Themes: Faith, Loyalty, Redemption, Love, Resilience
