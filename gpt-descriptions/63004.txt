Synopsis: 

In '10 Cakes Husbands Like Best: From Spry's Recipe Round-up', the Lever Brothers Company presents a delectable collection of cake recipes that are sure to captivate both bakers and cake enthusiasts alike. From classic favorites to innovative creations, this recipe round-up offers a delightful array of cakes that are bound to please even the most discerning husbands. With easy-to-follow instructions and helpful tips, this book is a must-have for anyone looking to impress their loved ones with mouthwatering homemade cakes.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink

Themes: Baking, Culinary Delights, Domestic Bliss
