Synopsis: 
Marjorie Dean, High School Sophomore follows the journey of Marjorie Dean, a bright and ambitious young girl who enters her sophomore year of high school with high hopes and dreams. As she navigates the challenges of school, friendships, and personal growth, Marjorie finds herself facing unexpected obstacles and discovering hidden strengths within herself. With the support of her friends and mentors, she learns valuable life lessons and begins to shape her own identity in the midst of a changing world.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Young Adult, Fiction

Themes: 
Identity, Friendship, Personal growth, Resilience
