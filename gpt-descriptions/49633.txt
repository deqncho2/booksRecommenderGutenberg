Synopsis: 

Step back in time to the captivating era of Queen Elizabeth I, as renowned historian John Smith takes you on a thrilling journey through the Age of Elizabeth. From the moment she ascended to the throne in 1547, Elizabeth's reign was marked by political intrigue, religious turmoil, and the rise of England as a global power. Smith delves into the fascinating details of Elizabeth's life, exploring her relationships, her struggles, and the pivotal events that shaped her reign. From the Spanish Armada to the exploration of the New World, this book paints a vivid picture of a remarkable era that forever changed the course of history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Power and politics, Religion and religious conflict, Exploration and discovery
