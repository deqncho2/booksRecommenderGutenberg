Synopsis: In 'The Golden Chimney: A Boy's Mine' by Elizabeth Gerberding, readers are transported to a small mining town where twelve-year-old Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure. When Jack discovers a hidden map leading to a legendary treasure, he must navigate treacherous tunnels, solve cryptic puzzles, and outsmart dangerous foes to uncover the truth behind the town's mysterious past. With the fate of the entire community at stake, Jack's determination and bravery are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to unlock the secrets of the Golden Chimney before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Discovery
