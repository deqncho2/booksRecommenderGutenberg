Synopsis: In 'Fashion and Famine' by Ann S. Stephens, a young woman named Edith finds herself caught between the allure of high society fashion and the harsh realities of a devastating famine. As she navigates the glamorous world of fashion, Edith becomes increasingly aware of the stark contrast between the opulence she enjoys and the suffering of those around her. With her heart torn between two worlds, Edith must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge her perception of beauty and compassion.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Class divide, Morality, Self-discovery
