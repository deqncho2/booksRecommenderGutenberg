Synopsis: In 'A Question of Courage' by Jesse F. Bone, a young boy named Andy embarks on a thrilling adventure that tests his bravery and determination. When he stumbles upon a mysterious artifact, he is transported to a fantastical world filled with danger and magic. With the help of newfound friends, Andy must navigate treacherous landscapes, face formidable foes, and uncover the truth behind his extraordinary journey. As he confronts his fears and discovers his inner strength, Andy learns valuable lessons about courage, friendship, and the power of believing in oneself.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Self-discovery
