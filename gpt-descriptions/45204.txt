Synopsis: 
In 'The Putumayo, the Devil's Paradise: Travels in the Peruvian Amazon Region and an Account of the Atrocities Committed upon the Indians Therein', author W. E. Hardenburg takes readers on a gripping journey deep into the heart of the Peruvian Amazon. Through vivid descriptions and harrowing encounters, he uncovers a dark and sinister world where unimaginable atrocities are committed against the indigenous people. As he delves deeper into the region, the author uncovers shocking secrets and exposes the true nature of the Devil's Paradise.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travelogue, History

Themes: Exploration, Colonialism, Human Rights, Indigenous Culture
