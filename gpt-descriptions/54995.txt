Synopsis: In 'Uncle Wiggily's Fortune' by Howard Roger Garis, follow the adventures of Uncle Wiggily Longears, a lovable rabbit with a knack for finding hidden treasures. When Uncle Wiggily stumbles upon a mysterious map, he embarks on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. Along the way, he encounters a colorful cast of characters, including mischievous animals and cunning villains, all vying for the same fortune. Will Uncle Wiggily's wit and charm be enough to outsmart his adversaries and claim the treasure for himself?

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Perseverance
