Synopsis: In "The Case of Oscar Slater," Arthur Conan Doyle delves into a gripping true crime story that unfolds in the bustling streets of Glasgow. When a wealthy elderly woman is brutally murdered in her own home, suspicion falls upon a German immigrant named Oscar Slater. As the evidence against Slater begins to crumble under scrutiny, a relentless pursuit for justice ensues, revealing shocking twists and turns that challenge the very foundations of the legal system.

Genres: Mystery, True Crime, Historical

Themes: Justice and injustice, Prejudice and discrimination
