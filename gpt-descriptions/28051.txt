Synopsis: In "Caesar Rodney's Ride" by Henry Fisk Carlton, a thrilling historical tale unfolds as a young man named Caesar Rodney embarks on a daring mission that could change the course of history. Set against the backdrop of the American Revolution, this gripping narrative follows Rodney's perilous journey through treacherous terrain and dangerous encounters, as he races against time to deliver a crucial message. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome of Rodney's courageous ride.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Bravery, Loyalty
