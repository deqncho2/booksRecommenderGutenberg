Synopsis: In 'Heart: A Social Novel' by Tupper, Martin Farquhar, we are introduced to the intriguing world of Victorian society, where the lives of the wealthy and the working class collide. The story follows the journey of a young woman named Lucy, who finds herself torn between her duty to her family and her desire for independence. As she navigates through the complexities of love, societal expectations, and personal growth, Lucy's choices will have far-reaching consequences that will challenge the very fabric of her world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Class division, Women's rights, Love and duty
