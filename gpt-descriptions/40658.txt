Synopsis: In 'With God in the Yellowstone', Alma White takes readers on a captivating journey through the awe-inspiring landscapes of Yellowstone National Park. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, White explores the spiritual connection between nature and the divine, revealing the profound impact that the park's natural wonders have on the human soul. As she encounters breathtaking geysers, majestic wildlife, and serene forests, White's experiences in Yellowstone become a testament to the power of God's creation and the importance of preserving and cherishing the natural world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travelogue, Spiritual

Themes: Nature's beauty and spirituality, Environmental conservation, Personal connection with the divine
