Synopsis: In 'Time Enough at Last' by Venable, Lyn, the story follows a bookish and introverted bank teller named Henry Bemis, who is constantly yearning for more time to indulge in his passion for reading. One fateful day, an unexpected event grants him the ultimate wish, but as he revels in his newfound solitude, he soon discovers that there may be a price to pay for his solitary existence. This gripping tale explores the delicate balance between solitude and companionship, and the consequences of unchecked desires.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Solitude, Escapism, Consequences of Desires
