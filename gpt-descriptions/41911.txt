Synopsis: 

'The Australian Army Medical Corps in Egypt: An Illustrated and Detailed Account of the Early Organisation and Work of the Australian Medical Units in Egypt in 1914-1915' takes readers on a captivating journey through the crucial early days of the Australian Army Medical Corps in Egypt during World War I. Through vivid illustrations and meticulous details, the book explores the challenges, triumphs, and sacrifices of the Australian medical units as they navigate the harsh realities of war. From the establishment of medical facilities to the tireless efforts of doctors and nurses, this account offers a compelling insight into the vital role played by the Australian Army Medical Corps in Egypt.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, War memoir

Themes: War and its impact, Medical advancements and challenges, Heroism and sacrifice
