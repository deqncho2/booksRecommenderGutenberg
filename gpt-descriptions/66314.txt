Synopsis: 

In 'Beware the Star Gods' by Byrne, S. J., a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the key to unlocking a long-lost civilization's secrets. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the artifact, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where ancient prophecies and cosmic forces collide. With time running out, he must navigate treacherous paths and make difficult choices to protect the world from the wrath of the enigmatic Star Gods.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Cosmic forces, Prophecy, Sacrifice
