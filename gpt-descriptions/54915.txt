Synopsis: In the untamed wilderness of early 19th-century Indiana, young Balser Brent embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, courage, and the awe-inspiring presence of bears. As he navigates the treacherous forests and encounters these formidable creatures, Balser's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the test. With each encounter, he learns valuable lessons about survival, friendship, and the indomitable spirit of the human heart.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Wilderness survival, Courage, Friendship, Nature's power
