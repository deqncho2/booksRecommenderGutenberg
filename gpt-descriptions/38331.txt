Synopsis: In 'Frédérique, vol. 1' by Paul de Kock, readers are introduced to the charming and enigmatic Frédérique, a young woman who captivates everyone she meets. Set in 19th century Paris, the story follows Frédérique's journey as she navigates the complexities of love, society, and personal identity. As she becomes entangled in a web of romantic entanglements and scandalous secrets, Frédérique's true nature and motivations remain a mystery, leaving readers eager to uncover the truth behind her captivating allure.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Love and Desire, Identity and Self-discovery, Society and Scandal
