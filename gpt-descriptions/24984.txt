Synopsis: In "Little Gidding and its inmates in the Time of King Charles I.: with an account of the Harmonies," author John Edward Acland takes us on a captivating journey to the secluded village of Little Gidding during the tumultuous reign of King Charles I. Through vivid descriptions and intriguing anecdotes, Acland introduces us to the diverse and enigmatic inhabitants of this small community, each with their own secrets and desires. As we delve deeper into the lives of these individuals, we uncover a web of hidden harmonies that bind them together, leading to unexpected revelations and profound transformations. Acland's narrative skillfully intertwines historical events with personal stories, creating a rich tapestry of love, betrayal, and redemption that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Community, Secrets, Transformation, Redemption
