Synopsis: In 'The Black Eagle Mystery' by Geraldine Bonner, a young woman named Ruth embarks on a thrilling adventure when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter that leads her to a hidden treasure. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the past, Ruth finds herself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where she must rely on her wit and courage to uncover the truth. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Treasure hunting, Secrets and hidden identities
