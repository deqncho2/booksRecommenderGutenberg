Synopsis: 
In "Tall Tales from Texas," Mody C. Boatright takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore and larger-than-life characters of the Lone Star State. From the legendary cowboy Pecos Bill to the mischievous trickster Brer Rabbit, these tall tales are filled with humor, adventure, and a touch of the supernatural. With each story, readers will be transported to a world where the boundaries of reality are stretched, and the imagination knows no limits. Whether it's a talking animal or a heroic feat, these tales will leave readers both entertained and in awe of the colorful tapestry of Texas folklore.

Genres: Folklore, Fiction, Humor

Themes: Heroism, Imagination, Supernatural, Cultural Identity
