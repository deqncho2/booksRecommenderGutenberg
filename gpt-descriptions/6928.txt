Synopsis: In "Who Wrote the Bible? : a Book for the People," Gladden, Washington takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins of the Bible, unraveling the mysteries surrounding its authorship. With meticulous research and thought-provoking analysis, the author delves into the historical, cultural, and religious contexts that shaped the sacred text. As readers explore the various theories and controversies surrounding the Bible's creation, they are invited to question their own beliefs and gain a deeper understanding of the complex tapestry that is the Bible.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, History

Themes: Authorship, Religious Interpretation, Historical Context, Faith and Belief
