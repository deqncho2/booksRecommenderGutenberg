Synopsis: In "The Young Bridge-Tender; or, Ralph Nelson's Upward Struggle" by Edward Stratemeyer, readers are introduced to Ralph Nelson, a young and determined bridge-tender who dreams of a better life. As he navigates the challenges of his job and the expectations of his family, Ralph finds himself caught between duty and his own aspirations. With the support of unexpected allies and the discovery of a hidden secret, Ralph embarks on a journey that will test his courage and resilience. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles in his path and achieve his dreams?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Family dynamics, Social class
