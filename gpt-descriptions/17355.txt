Synopsis: In 'The Runaway Skyscraper' by Murray Leinster, a group of people find themselves trapped in a skyscraper that suddenly breaks free from its foundations and starts hurtling through the city. As they struggle to survive and find a way to stop the runaway building, they uncover a shocking secret that could change the course of humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Technology gone awry, Survival, Human ingenuity
