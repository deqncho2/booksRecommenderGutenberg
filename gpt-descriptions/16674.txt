Synopsis: In the small town of Palomar, a young man named John Cardigan inherits his father's lumber empire and becomes determined to revive it. Facing numerous challenges and betrayals, John must navigate the treacherous world of business while also dealing with personal tragedies and a love triangle that threatens to tear his world apart. Set against the backdrop of the majestic redwood forests of California, 'The Pride of Palomar' is a gripping tale of ambition, love, and redemption that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Love, Redemption
