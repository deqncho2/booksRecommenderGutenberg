Synopsis: In 'Sunrise' by William Black, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small coastal village, captivating the locals with his enigmatic charm and intriguing past. As the villagers become entangled in his web of secrets, their lives are forever changed, leading to unexpected alliances, forbidden love, and a shocking revelation that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, the power of love, the impact of the past on the present
