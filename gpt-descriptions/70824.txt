Synopsis: In "The World-Mover" by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. As he delves deeper into his invention, he realizes the immense power it holds and the potential consequences it may unleash. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path of moral dilemmas, personal sacrifices, and unforeseen challenges. This gripping tale explores the boundaries of scientific progress and the ethical implications that arise when playing with the very fabric of reality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Scientific advancement, Morality, Consequences of power
