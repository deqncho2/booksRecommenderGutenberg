Synopsis: In 'Radio Boys in the Secret Service; Or, Cast Away on an Iceberg' by Duffield, J. W., a group of adventurous radio boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling mission that takes them to the treacherous Arctic. As they navigate through icy landscapes and encounter mysterious characters, they uncover a secret that could have far-reaching consequences. With their radio skills and determination, the boys must use all their wits to survive and protect what they hold dear.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
