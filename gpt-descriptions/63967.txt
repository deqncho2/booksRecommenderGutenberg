Synopsis: In 'The Timeless Ones' by Frank Belknap Long, a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the key to a mysterious civilization lost in time. As he delves deeper into the secrets of this enigmatic society, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where the boundaries between past and present blur. With each revelation, he uncovers a truth that challenges his understanding of history and the very nature of humanity itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Ancient civilizations, Human nature
