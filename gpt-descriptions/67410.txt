Synopsis: In 'Our Winnie, and The Little Match Girl' by Everett-Green, Evelyn, two heartwarming tales intertwine to create a captivating story. Follow the journey of Winnie, a young girl with a mysterious past, as she navigates the challenges of life in a bustling city. Alongside her, discover the poignant tale of the Little Match Girl, a character from a beloved fairy tale, as she faces the harsh realities of poverty and loneliness. As their paths cross, these two girls find solace and strength in each other, reminding us of the power of friendship and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Social Inequality
