Synopsis:
'Tales of St. Austin's' takes readers on a hilarious journey through the misadventures of the students and staff at St. Austin's, a fictional English public school. From eccentric professors to mischievous students, this collection of interconnected stories is filled with uproarious situations and witty dialogue that will keep readers laughing from start to finish. Whether it's a cricket match gone awry or a prank that spirals out of control, the characters of St. Austin's find themselves in one comical predicament after another. 

Genres:
Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes:
School life, Coming of age, Mischievousness
