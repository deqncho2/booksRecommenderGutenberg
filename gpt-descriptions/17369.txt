Synopsis: 

"The Fifth Leicestershire: A Record of the 1/5th Battalion the Leicestershire Regiment, T.F., During the War, 1914-1919" by John David Hills is a gripping historical account that takes readers on a journey through the experiences of the 1/5th Battalion during World War I. 

From the initial excitement and patriotism that led these men to enlist, to the harsh realities of trench warfare and the devastating toll it took on their lives, this book offers a vivid and poignant portrayal of the battalion's struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices. Through personal anecdotes, letters, and official records, the author brings to life the camaraderie, bravery, and resilience of these soldiers as they faced unimaginable challenges on the front lines.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Memoir

Themes: War and its impact on individuals and communities, Brotherhood and camaraderie in the face of adversity, Sacrifice and heroism, The human experience in times of conflict
