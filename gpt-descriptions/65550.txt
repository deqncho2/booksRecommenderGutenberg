Synopsis: In 'The Mosquito Fleet' by Keating, Bern, a group of unlikely heroes embarks on a thrilling adventure to save their coastal town from a mysterious threat. As they navigate treacherous waters and face unexpected challenges, they must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and the power of friendship to overcome the looming danger. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Heroism, Friendship, Perseverance
