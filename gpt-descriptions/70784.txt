Synopsis: In 'Money and trade considered: With a proposal for supplying the nation with money,' John Law presents a thought-provoking exploration of the intricate relationship between money and trade. Through his innovative proposal, Law challenges conventional notions and offers a fresh perspective on how a nation can effectively manage its monetary system. With a captivating blend of economic analysis and practical solutions, this work promises to engage readers in a stimulating journey of discovery.

Genres: Economics, Finance, Political Science

Themes: Monetary systems, Trade, Economic policy
