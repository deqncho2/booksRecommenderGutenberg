Synopsis: In 'The Pilgrim Story: Being largely a compilation from the documents of Governor Bradford and Governor Winslow, severally and in collaboration; together with a list of Mayflower passengers,' author William Franklin Atwood delves into the captivating history of the Pilgrims who sailed aboard the Mayflower to the New World. Drawing from the firsthand accounts of Governor Bradford and Governor Winslow, Atwood weaves together a compelling narrative that explores the challenges, triumphs, and sacrifices of these brave individuals. From their perilous journey across the Atlantic to their establishment of Plymouth Colony, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the Mayflower passengers and the impact they had on American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Perseverance, Religious freedom, Exploration, Colonialism
