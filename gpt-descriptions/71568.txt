Synopsis: In "The Show Must Go On" by Henry Slesar, a renowned theater director finds himself in a perplexing situation when his lead actor suddenly falls ill on the opening night of a highly anticipated play. With no understudy available, the director must make a daring decision that could either save the show or lead to its ultimate downfall. As the tension rises and the clock ticks, the director's determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test, revealing unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Perseverance, Creativity, Unexpected Challenges
