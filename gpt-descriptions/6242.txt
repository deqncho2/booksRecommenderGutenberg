Synopsis: In 'Parables of a Province' by Gilbert Parker, a young journalist named Gaston Belward embarks on a journey to a remote Canadian province, seeking to uncover the truth behind a mysterious family secret. As he delves deeper into the lives of the locals, Gaston becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and political intrigue. With each parable he uncovers, Gaston's perception of the world is challenged, forcing him to question his own beliefs and confront the consequences of his actions. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Morality, Redemption
