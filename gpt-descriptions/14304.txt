Synopsis: 
'The Tale of Peter Rabbit' follows the mischievous adventures of a young rabbit named Peter, who can't resist the temptation to explore Mr. McGregor's garden. Despite his mother's warnings, Peter finds himself in a world of danger and excitement as he tries to outsmart the cunning farmer. Will Peter be able to escape unscathed, or will his curiosity lead him into trouble?

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Curiosity, Consequences of disobedience, Nature
