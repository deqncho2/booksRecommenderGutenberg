Synopsis: 

In 'Manx Fairy Tales' by Sophia Morrison, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting folklore of the Isle of Man. Delve into a world where mythical creatures, mischievous fairies, and ancient legends come to life. From the mysterious Fairy Bridge to the haunting tales of the Buggane, these stories will transport you to a realm where magic and reality intertwine. Prepare to be spellbound by the captivating narratives that have been passed down through generations, revealing the rich cultural heritage of the Isle of Man.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Magic, Legends, Cultural Heritage
