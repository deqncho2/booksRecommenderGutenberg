Synopsis: In the roaring 1920s, Jay Gatsby, a mysterious millionaire, throws extravagant parties at his opulent mansion in Long Island. As the enigmatic Gatsby becomes the talk of the town, his neighbor, Nick Carraway, is drawn into his world of wealth, glamour, and secrets. Through Nick's eyes, we witness the captivating and tragic story of Gatsby's relentless pursuit of the American Dream and his unrequited love for the beautiful Daisy Buchanan. Set against the backdrop of the Jazz Age, this timeless tale explores themes of love, wealth, illusion, and the corrupting power of the American Dream.

Genres: Literary fiction, Historical fiction, Tragedy

Themes: The American Dream, Love and Desire, Wealth and Class, Illusion and Reality
