Synopsis: In this captivating volume, Nathan Drake delves into the life and times of the legendary playwright, William Shakespeare. Offering a comprehensive biography, insightful critiques of his works, a fresh chronology of his plays, an exploration of the enigmatic sonnets, and a vivid portrayal of the customs, superstitions, and elegant literature of the Elizabethan era, Drake paints a vivid picture of Shakespeare's world. From the bustling streets of London to the grand theaters of the time, readers will be transported to a fascinating era that shaped one of the greatest literary figures in history.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, History

Themes: Shakespearean era, Elizabethan society, Literary analysis, Cultural and historical context
