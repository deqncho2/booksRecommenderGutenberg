Synopsis: 
'Deck and Port' takes readers on an exciting journey aboard the United States frigate Congress as it sails from the East Coast to California. Through vivid sketches, the author provides glimpses of the exotic ports of Rio Janeiro, Valparaiso, Lima, Honolulu, and San Francisco. The narrative is filled with intriguing incidents and adventures that unfold during the cruise, keeping readers engaged and eager to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural encounters, Naval life
