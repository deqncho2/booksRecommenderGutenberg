Synopsis: In 'The Beast-Jewel of Mars' by Thiessen, V. E., a team of explorers embarks on a perilous mission to uncover the secrets of Mars. As they delve deeper into the mysterious planet, they encounter a creature unlike anything they have ever seen before. With their lives hanging in the balance, they must navigate treacherous landscapes and face unexpected challenges, all while trying to unravel the enigma of the beast-jewel. This thrilling sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the truth that lies hidden within the red planet.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Discovery
