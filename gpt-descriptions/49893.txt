Synopsis: In 'The Mentor: Great American Inventors, Vol. 1, Num. 29, Serial No. 29', author Bruce H. Addington takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and achievements of some of America's most brilliant inventors. From their humble beginnings to their groundbreaking inventions, this volume delves into the fascinating stories of these visionaries, revealing the challenges they faced and the impact they made on society. With vivid storytelling and insightful anecdotes, Addington brings to life the remarkable individuals who shaped the course of American innovation.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Innovation, Perseverance, American Dream
