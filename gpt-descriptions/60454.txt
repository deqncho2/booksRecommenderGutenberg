Synopsis: 
'Choyce Drollery: Songs and Sonnets: Being a Collection of Divers Excellent Pieces of Poetry, of Several Eminent Authors' is a captivating anthology that brings together a diverse range of exceptional poetry from various renowned authors. From heartfelt sonnets to enchanting songs, this collection offers a delightful exploration of the beauty and power of language. Each piece is a unique gem, showcasing the talent and creativity of the featured authors. With its rich tapestry of emotions and themes, this anthology promises to transport readers to different worlds and leave them with a renewed appreciation for the art of poetry.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology

Themes: Love, Nature, Emotions, Beauty, Human Experience
