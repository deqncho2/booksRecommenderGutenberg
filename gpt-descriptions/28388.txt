Synopsis: In 'The Wreck on the Andamans' by Joseph Darvall, a group of strangers find themselves stranded on a remote island after a catastrophic shipwreck. As they struggle to survive in this unforgiving environment, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to unexpected alliances and betrayals. With danger lurking at every turn, the survivors must confront their own demons and make difficult choices that will determine their fate. This gripping tale of survival and human nature will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Human nature, Betrayal
