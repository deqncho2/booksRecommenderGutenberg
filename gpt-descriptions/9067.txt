Synopsis: In "Robert Browning: How to Know Him," William Lyon Phelps takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and works of the renowned poet Robert Browning. Through insightful analysis and engaging anecdotes, Phelps delves into Browning's complex personality, his unique writing style, and the profound impact he had on the literary world. From Browning's early struggles to his eventual rise to fame, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the man behind the poetry, shedding light on the inspirations and motivations that shaped his remarkable body of work.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, Love and relationships, Individuality, Literary influence
