Synopsis: In 'The Strange Likeness' by Harriet Pyne Grove, a young woman named Emily discovers an uncanny resemblance between herself and a mysterious stranger. As she delves deeper into the stranger's life, Emily becomes entangled in a web of secrets and deception that threatens to unravel her own identity. With each twist and turn, the lines between reality and illusion blur, leaving Emily questioning her own sanity and the true nature of the strange likeness.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Perception, Sanity
