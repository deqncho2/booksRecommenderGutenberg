Synopsis: In 'True Stories about Dogs and Cats', Follen, Eliza Lee Cabot takes readers on a heartwarming journey through a collection of captivating tales centered around our beloved furry friends. From heartwrenching rescues to unexpected friendships, these stories will leave readers laughing, crying, and feeling a renewed appreciation for the unconditional love and loyalty that dogs and cats bring into our lives.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Stories, Memoir

Themes: Love and loyalty, Human-animal bond, Resilience, Compassion
