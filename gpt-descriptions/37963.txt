Synopsis: In 'Miss Arnott's Marriage' by Richard Marsh, a young woman named Miss Arnott finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she agrees to marry a mysterious stranger. As she delves deeper into her husband's past, she uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to unravel their marriage and her own sanity. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of love and trust.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Marriage and relationships, Identity and self-discovery
