Synopsis: In 'Nerves and Common Sense' by Annie Payson Call, a young woman named Mary finds herself trapped in a cycle of anxiety and exhaustion. Desperate for a solution, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, seeking to understand the connection between her nerves and her common sense. As Mary delves deeper into her own mind and body, she uncovers surprising truths about the power of perception and the importance of balance. With the help of insightful guidance, she learns to navigate the challenges of modern life and find a path towards inner peace and well-being.

Genres: Self-help, Psychology, Personal Development

Themes: Mental health, Stress management, Self-awareness, Mind-body connection
