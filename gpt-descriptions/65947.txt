Synopsis: In 'The Color of His Boots' by W. C. Tuttle, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small western town, catching the attention of the locals with his unique choice of footwear. As the townspeople become increasingly curious about the man and his colorful boots, they soon discover that his arrival is not a mere coincidence. With secrets and hidden motives at play, the stranger's boots become a symbol of intrigue and danger, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Small-town dynamics
