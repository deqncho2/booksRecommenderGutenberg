Synopsis: In "Recollections of the Civil War" by Maud E. Morrow, the author takes us on a captivating journey through her personal experiences during one of the most tumultuous periods in American history. Through vivid and heartfelt recollections, Morrow provides a unique perspective on the Civil War, offering a glimpse into the lives of ordinary individuals caught in the midst of extraordinary circumstances. From the chaos of battlefields to the struggles on the home front, this memoir paints a poignant and gripping portrait of a nation divided.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, resilience and survival, the human experience in times of conflict
