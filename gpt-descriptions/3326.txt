Synopsis: In 'The Well-Beloved: A Sketch of a Temperament', Thomas Hardy takes us on a captivating journey through the life of Jocelyn Pierston, a sculptor who becomes infatuated with a mysterious woman. As he searches for perfection in love, he finds himself entangled in a web of desire, obsession, and the ever-changing nature of beauty. Set against the backdrop of the stunning Isle of Slingers, this novel explores the complexities of human relationships and the elusive nature of true love.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Psychological

Themes: Love and desire, Beauty and perfection, Obsession and infatuation, Change and impermanence
