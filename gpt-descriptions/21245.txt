Synopsis: 
In 'Three Boys in the Wild North Land' by Young, Egerton Ryerson, three adventurous boys set out on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of the North Land. As they navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter wild animals, and face unexpected challenges, their bond of friendship is tested to its limits. With each passing day, they discover the true meaning of courage, resilience, and the power of the human spirit. Will they conquer the wild and find their way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Survival

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Resilience
