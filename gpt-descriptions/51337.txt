Synopsis: In 'The Man Outside' by Evelyn E. Smith, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious stranger arrives, claiming to be from another world. As the townspeople grapple with their fear and suspicion, they must confront their own prejudices and question what it truly means to be human. With secrets and revelations lurking around every corner, this gripping tale explores the boundaries of identity and the power of acceptance.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Prejudice, Acceptance
