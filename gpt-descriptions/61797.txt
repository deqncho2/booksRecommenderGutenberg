Synopsis: In 'The Planet That Time Forgot' by Donald A. Wollheim, a group of explorers find themselves stranded on a mysterious and dangerous island that seems to exist outside of time. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter prehistoric creatures, they must unravel the secrets of this enigmatic place in order to survive and find a way back home. With each twist and turn, the story keeps readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what lies around the next corner and what mysteries the island holds.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Time, Evolution
