Synopsis: The Bobbsey Twins embark on a thrilling adventure when they spend their summer vacation on a houseboat. As they explore the river, they stumble upon a mysterious secret that leads them on a quest to uncover the truth. With their keen detective skills and unwavering determination, the Bobbsey Twins find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of suspense and danger. Will they be able to solve the puzzle before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Family, Friendship, Curiosity, Problem-solving
