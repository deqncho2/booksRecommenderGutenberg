Synopsis: In "Outcast of the Stars" by Robert Silverberg, a young man named Kinnall Darival finds himself thrust into a dangerous mission to save his planet from an impending invasion. As the last hope for his people, Kinnall must navigate treacherous alliances, face formidable enemies, and confront his own inner demons. With the fate of his world hanging in the balance, Kinnall's journey takes him across the vastness of space, where he discovers the true meaning of sacrifice, loyalty, and the power of redemption.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Space Opera

Themes: Heroism, Identity, Redemption, Survival
