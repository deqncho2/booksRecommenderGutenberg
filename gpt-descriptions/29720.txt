Synopsis: In 'Hall of Mirrors' by Fredric Brown, a small town is shaken by a series of brutal murders that seem to have no connection. As the local police struggle to make sense of the baffling crimes, a journalist and a detective team up to uncover the truth behind the killings. As they delve deeper into the dark underbelly of the town, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected twists that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Small-town secrets, Psychological manipulation
