Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's Nobility; Or, The Tragedy of the Ocean Tramp' by Standish, Burt L., readers are taken on a thrilling journey aboard a mysterious ocean tramp. Frank Merriwell, a young and courageous adventurer, finds himself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue as he uncovers the dark secrets hidden within the ship's walls. With his quick wit and unwavering determination, Frank must navigate treacherous waters and face unexpected adversaries to unravel the tragic mysteries that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Betrayal, Redemption
