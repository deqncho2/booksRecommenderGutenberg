Synopsis: 
In 'The Trail of the White Mule' by B. M. Bower, a young woman named Mary Hope finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she inherits a ranch in the rugged Montana wilderness. As she navigates the treacherous landscape and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Mary must uncover the truth behind her inheritance and confront the secrets that threaten to unravel her newfound life. With its gripping plot and vivid descriptions, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Inheritance, Identity, Survival
