Synopsis: 

'Indian Legends from the land of Al-ay-ek-sa' by Harriet Rossiter takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Indian folklore. From enchanting tales of mythical creatures to heroic legends of brave warriors, this collection immerses readers in a world of magic, adventure, and wisdom. As the stories unfold, readers will be transported to a land where gods and goddesses, spirits and demons, coexist with mortals, shaping their destinies and teaching valuable lessons along the way. Each legend is beautifully woven with vibrant imagery and lyrical prose, leaving readers spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Mythical creatures, Heroism, Wisdom, Fate, Cultural heritage
