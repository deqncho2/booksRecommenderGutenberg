Synopsis: In 'The Golgotha Dancers' by Manly Wade Wellman, a small Appalachian town becomes the stage for a mysterious and terrifying phenomenon. As the townspeople gather for their annual festival, they unwittingly unleash a force that threatens to consume them all. With the arrival of a stranger who possesses ancient knowledge, the battle between good and evil takes a chilling turn. As the dancers of Golgotha emerge from the shadows, the fate of the town hangs in the balance.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Ancient Forces, Small Town Secrets
