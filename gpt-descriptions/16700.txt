Synopsis: 
"The Ancient Church: Its History, Doctrine, Worship, and Constitution" takes readers on a captivating journey through the early years of Christianity, exploring the origins, beliefs, rituals, and structure of the ancient church. From the early apostolic period to the emergence of the Roman Catholic Church, this book delves into the fascinating world of the early Christian community, shedding light on its struggles, triumphs, and enduring legacy. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, William Dool Killen paints a vivid picture of the ancient church, offering readers a deeper understanding of its profound impact on the development of Christianity.

Genres: History, Religion, Non-fiction

Themes: Early Christianity, Church History, Religious Doctrine, Worship Practices, Organizational Structure
