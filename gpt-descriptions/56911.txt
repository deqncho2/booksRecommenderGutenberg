Synopsis:
'The New Century Standard Letter-Writer: Business, Family and Social Correspondence, Love-Letters, Etiquette, Synonyms, Legal Forms, Etc.' by Alfred B. Chambers is a comprehensive guidebook that offers a wealth of knowledge and practical advice on various forms of written communication. From crafting professional business letters to expressing heartfelt emotions in love letters, this book covers it all. It also delves into the intricacies of proper etiquette, provides synonyms to enhance one's vocabulary, and includes legal forms for various purposes. With its extensive range of topics, this letter-writing manual is an indispensable resource for anyone seeking to improve their written communication skills.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Self-help

Themes: Communication, Etiquette, Language, Writing
