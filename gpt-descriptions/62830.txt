Synopsis: 
In 'The Young Section-Hand' by Stevenson, Burton Egbert, we follow the gripping journey of a young man named Tom as he embarks on a new job as a section-hand on a railroad. As he navigates the challenges and dangers of his work, Tom finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the secrets that lie within the railroad.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Coming of age, Perseverance, Deception
