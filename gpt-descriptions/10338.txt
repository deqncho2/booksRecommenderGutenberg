Synopsis: 
'With the Turks in Palestine' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of the Middle East during World War I. Aaronsohn, a daring and resourceful spy, finds himself caught between the clashing forces of the Ottoman Empire and the British Army. As he navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters unexpected allies and enemies, Aaronsohn's mission becomes a race against time to secure vital intelligence that could change the course of the war. With danger lurking at every turn, the fate of nations hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical fiction, espionage, war

Themes: Courage, loyalty, sacrifice, the impact of war on individuals and nations
