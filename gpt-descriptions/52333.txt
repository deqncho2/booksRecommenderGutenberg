Synopsis: 
In 'A Day at Camp Killkare; Or, Aunt Jane and the Campfire Girls', readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as Aunt Jane and the Campfire Girls embark on a day filled with mystery and excitement at Camp Killkare. As they explore the beautiful surroundings, they stumble upon a hidden secret that leads them on a quest to uncover the truth. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
