Synopsis:
"The Head of Kay's" is a delightful and humorous novel that takes place in a prestigious English boarding school. When a mischievous new student named Farnie arrives, he quickly becomes the center of attention, causing chaos and hilarity among the staff and students. As Farnie's pranks escalate, the school's headmaster, Mr. Kay, finds himself facing an unprecedented challenge in maintaining order and discipline. With witty dialogue, eccentric characters, and unexpected twists, this story will keep readers entertained from start to finish.

Genres:
Comedy, Satire, Coming-of-age

Themes:
School life, Pranks and mischief, Authority and discipline
