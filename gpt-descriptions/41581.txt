Synopsis: In 'Amazing Grace, Who Proves That Virtue Has Its Silver Lining' by Kate Trimble Sharber, readers are introduced to the captivating story of Grace, a young woman whose life takes an unexpected turn. Set in the early 1900s, Grace's journey is filled with love, loss, and the pursuit of her dreams. As she navigates through the challenges of her time, Grace's unwavering virtue and resilience become her greatest strengths. This heartwarming tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what fate has in store for Grace.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Virtue, Resilience, Love, Pursuit of Dreams
