Synopsis: 
In 'The Country House (with Designs)', the reader is transported to the enchanting world of a grand country estate, where secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances unfold. As the inhabitants of the house navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations, they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue that threatens to unravel their carefully constructed lives. With stunning descriptions of the lush countryside and captivating characters, this novel takes readers on a journey filled with passion, betrayal, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Class, Family Dynamics, Betrayal, Ambition
