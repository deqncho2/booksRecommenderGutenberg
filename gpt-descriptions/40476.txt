Synopsis: 
'Children of the Dawn: Old Tales of Greece' takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient myths and legends of Greece. From the heroic adventures of gods and goddesses to the enchanting tales of mythical creatures, this collection of stories brings the rich tapestry of Greek mythology to life. Delve into the world of gods and mortals, where love, betrayal, and destiny intertwine in extraordinary ways. With vivid storytelling and vibrant imagery, Elsie Finnimore Buckley invites readers to rediscover the timeless magic of these ancient tales.

Genres: Mythology, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Love and Betrayal, Power and Deception
