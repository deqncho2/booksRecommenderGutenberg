Synopsis: 
In 'Half-hours with the Highwaymen - Vol 2: Picturesque Biographies and Traditions of the "Knights of the Road"', Charles G. Harper delves into the captivating world of highwaymen, exploring their intriguing lives and the legends that surround them. From the notorious Dick Turpin to the lesser-known figures of the road, Harper paints vivid portraits of these daring outlaws and the adventures they embarked upon. With thrilling tales of daring robberies, narrow escapes, and the pursuit of justice, this volume offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of these infamous figures from history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Crime and punishment, Legends and folklore, Heroism
