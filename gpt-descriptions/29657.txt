Synopsis: In 'Mixed Faces' by Norton Roy, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and mystery when he is called to investigate a series of bizarre murders in a small, seemingly idyllic town. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel the very fabric of the community. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of lies and hidden agendas, all while trying to stay one step ahead of a cunning and elusive killer. As the body count rises, the detective realizes that the truth may be more sinister than he ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Small-town dynamics
