Synopsis: In 'The White Mail' by Cy Warman, a young man named Jack Rawn finds himself caught in a dangerous web of deception and betrayal when he becomes entangled with a mysterious group known as the White Mail. As Jack delves deeper into their world, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with secrets, hidden identities, and unexpected alliances. With his life on the line, Jack must uncover the truth behind the White Mail before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
