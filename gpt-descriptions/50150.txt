Synopsis: 
In 'The Devil is an Ass' by Ben Jonson, chaos ensues when a cunning devil named Pug disguises himself as a human in order to wreak havoc on the unsuspecting citizens of London. As Pug navigates the city's corrupt society, he encounters a colorful cast of characters who are all entangled in their own web of deceit and desires. With his devilish wit and charm, Pug manipulates these individuals, leading them down a path of hilarious and disastrous consequences. As the plot unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and the true nature of humanity is put to the test. 

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Drama

Themes: Deception, Morality, Human Nature
