Synopsis: 
In 'Magic, Stage Illusions and Scientific Diversions, Including Trick Photography,' Albert A. Hopkins takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of magic and illusion. From mind-boggling tricks to mind-reading techniques, Hopkins reveals the secrets behind some of the most astonishing performances ever witnessed. With detailed explanations and step-by-step instructions, this book offers a fascinating insight into the art of deception and the mastery of illusion. Prepare to be amazed as Hopkins unveils the hidden techniques that have enthralled audiences for centuries.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Magic, Photography

Themes: 
Deception, Entertainment, Creativity
