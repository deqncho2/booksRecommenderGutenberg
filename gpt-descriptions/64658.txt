Synopsis: In 'When Kohonnes Screamed' by Gardner F. Fox, a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds a mysterious power. As he delves deeper into its origins, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where the line between reality and myth begins to blur. With each discovery, the stakes rise, and he must race against time to unlock the artifact's secrets before it falls into the wrong hands. This thrilling adventure takes readers on a journey through ancient civilizations, supernatural forces, and the power of human curiosity.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Archaeology, Mythology, Power and its consequences
