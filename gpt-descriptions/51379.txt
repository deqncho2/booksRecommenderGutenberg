Synopsis: In 'The Music Master of Babylon' by Edgar Pangborn, a renowned musician finds himself transported to an ancient city where music holds a mysterious power. As he navigates this unfamiliar world, he must uncover the secrets of the city's musical traditions and confront the enigmatic ruler who controls its fate. With captivating melodies, unexpected alliances, and a quest for harmony, this tale takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through time and sound.

Genres: Fantasy, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Music, Power, Identity, Cultural Preservation
