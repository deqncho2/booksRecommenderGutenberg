Synopsis: In the dystopian city of Los Angeles, two teenagers from opposite sides of society find themselves entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse. June, a prodigy of the Republic's elite military, is determined to bring down the most notorious criminal, known as Day, who has become a symbol of rebellion against the oppressive regime. As their paths cross, secrets are unveiled, loyalties are tested, and the line between right and wrong becomes blurred. With thrilling action and unexpected twists, 'Legend' takes readers on a heart-pounding journey through a divided society where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Young Adult, Dystopian, Science Fiction

Themes: Power and corruption, love and sacrifice, identity and loyalty
