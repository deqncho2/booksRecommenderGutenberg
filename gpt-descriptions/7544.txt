Synopsis: 
'Quotes and Images From the Works of John Galsworthy' is a captivating collection of excerpts and imagery from the renowned author's literary masterpieces. This compilation offers a glimpse into Galsworthy's profound insights on human nature, society, and the complexities of life. Through poignant quotes and evocative images, readers are transported into the rich tapestry of Galsworthy's storytelling, leaving them eager to explore his works in their entirety.

Genres: 
Literary Fiction, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes: 
Human nature, Society, Morality, Class divide, Justice
