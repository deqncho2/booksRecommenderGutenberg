Synopsis: In 'Familiar Talks on Science: World-Building and Life; Earth, Air and Water,' Elisha Gray takes readers on a captivating journey through the wonders of the natural world. From the formation of planets to the intricate web of life on Earth, Gray's engaging and accessible style brings complex scientific concepts to life. Exploring the mysteries of the universe, the author delves into the elements that shape our planet, including the fascinating properties of earth, air, and water. With a blend of scientific knowledge and storytelling, this book offers a captivating exploration of the world around us.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Education

Themes: Nature, Scientific exploration, Environmentalism
