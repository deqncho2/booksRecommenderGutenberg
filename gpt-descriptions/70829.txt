Synopsis: In 'For the good of the team' by Ralph Henry Barbour, a talented young athlete finds himself torn between personal ambition and the greater good of his team. As he navigates the challenges of competition, friendship, and loyalty, he must make difficult choices that will shape not only his own future, but also the fate of his teammates. With high stakes and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the sacrifices one must make for the sake of a common goal.

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Teamwork, Sacrifice, Ambition, Friendship
