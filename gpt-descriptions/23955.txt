Synopsis: In 'Chicken Little Jane' by Ritchie, Lily Munsell, follow the captivating journey of Jane, a young girl with an extraordinary ability to predict disasters. As she navigates through a world that doubts her abilities, Jane must find the courage to embrace her unique gift and prove that sometimes, the smallest voices can make the biggest impact. With unexpected twists and heartfelt moments, this story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what fate has in store for Chicken Little Jane.

Genres: Children's Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Believing in oneself, Overcoming adversity, Embracing uniqueness
