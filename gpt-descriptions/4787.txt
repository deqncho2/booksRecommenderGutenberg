Synopsis: 
In 'The Story of Julia Page' by Kathleen Thompson Norris, readers are introduced to Julia Page, a young woman who seemingly has it all - beauty, wealth, and a loving family. However, as she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations, Julia's life takes unexpected turns that challenge her beliefs and force her to confront her own desires and ambitions. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century America, this captivating novel explores the choices Julia must make to find her true path in life.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, societal expectations, self-discovery
