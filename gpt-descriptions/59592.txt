Synopsis: 

In 'The Siwash, Their Life, Legends, and Tales: Puget Sound and Pacific Northwest', J.A. Costello takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich cultural heritage of the Siwash people. Through a collection of fascinating legends and tales, Costello unveils the mystical world of the Puget Sound and Pacific Northwest tribes, offering a glimpse into their unique way of life and the profound wisdom embedded in their stories. From mythical creatures and heroic deeds to ancient rituals and spiritual beliefs, this book immerses readers in a world where nature and humanity intertwine in extraordinary ways. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Folklore, Anthropology

Themes: Indigenous culture, Mythology, Oral tradition, Connection with nature
