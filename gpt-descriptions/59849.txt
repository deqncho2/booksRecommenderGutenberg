Synopsis: 
In 'Filthy Rich' by Sheinbaum, Fred, a young and ambitious journalist named Jamie Johnson is given the opportunity of a lifetime when she is assigned to cover the glamorous and scandalous lives of the ultra-wealthy elite. As she delves deeper into this world of opulence and excess, Jamie uncovers shocking secrets and uncensored truths that could shatter the lives of those involved. With her career and personal life hanging in the balance, Jamie must navigate a treacherous web of power, greed, and deception, all while trying to maintain her own integrity.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Wealth and privilege, Morality and ethics, Ambition and success
