Synopsis: 
In 'The Bail Jumper' by Robert J. C. Stead, a seasoned bounty hunter finds himself entangled in a high-stakes chase when he is assigned to track down a notorious bail jumper. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances that will test his skills and determination to the limit. With each twist and turn, the hunter becomes the hunted, and the line between justice and revenge blurs. Will he be able to bring the fugitive to justice, or will he become another casualty in this thrilling game of cat and mouse?

Genres: 
Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: 
Justice, Revenge, Deception
