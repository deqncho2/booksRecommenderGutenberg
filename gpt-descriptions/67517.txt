Synopsis: 

In 'Apes and Monkeys: Their Life and Language', Richard Lynch Garner takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of primates. Through his firsthand experiences and extensive research, Garner unveils the astonishing abilities and complex social structures of these intelligent creatures. From the intricate communication systems of apes to the playful antics of monkeys, this book offers a captivating exploration of their lives, shedding light on their remarkable similarities to humans. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Nature

Themes: Animal intelligence, Communication, Social behavior
