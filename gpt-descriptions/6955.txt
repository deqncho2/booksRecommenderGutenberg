Synopsis: 
In "The Prince and Betty," a young American woman named Betty Silver meets a charming European prince while working as a secretary at a New York hotel. When the prince's plans to marry an American heiress are jeopardized, Betty finds herself caught up in a whirlwind of mistaken identities, romantic entanglements, and hilarious misunderstandings. As she navigates the world of high society and love, Betty must decide whether to follow her heart or succumb to the pressures of society.

Genres: Comedy, Romance

Themes: Love and relationships, Social class and status
