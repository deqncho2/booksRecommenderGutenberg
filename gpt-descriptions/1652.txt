Synopsis: 

"The Survivors of the Chancellor: Diary of J.R. Kazallon, Passenger" by Jules Verne is a gripping tale that follows the harrowing journey of a group of passengers aboard the ill-fated Chancellor ship. The story unfolds through the diary entries of J.R. Kazallon, a young man who finds himself trapped on a vessel plagued by disaster and despair. As the ship battles treacherous storms, dwindling supplies, and mounting tensions among the passengers, Kazallon's diary becomes a haunting account of survival and the depths of human nature.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Survival, Human Nature, Desperation, Morality
