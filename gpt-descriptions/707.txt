Synopsis: 
In 'Raffles: Further Adventures of the Amateur Cracksman', the charming and enigmatic gentleman thief, A.J. Raffles, returns for another thrilling escapade. This time, Raffles finds himself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue as he takes on a daring heist that could make or break his reputation. With his quick wit, cunning, and audacious plans, Raffles must outsmart both the authorities and his criminal rivals to come out on top. As the stakes rise and the tension mounts, readers will be captivated by the exhilarating twists and turns that keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Morality, Identity, Deception
