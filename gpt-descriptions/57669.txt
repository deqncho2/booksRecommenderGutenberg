Synopsis: 
In 'The Problem of Cell 13' by Jacques Futrelle, a renowned detective finds himself facing an extraordinary challenge when a brilliant criminal mastermind is locked in a seemingly impenetrable prison cell. As the days pass, the detective must use his wits and ingenuity to solve the perplexing mystery of how the criminal plans to escape, before it's too late. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling and unexpected conclusion.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Intelligence, Ingenuity, Escape
