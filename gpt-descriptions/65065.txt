Synopsis: 
'Base-ball Ballads' by Rice, Grantland is a collection of captivating and lyrical poems that celebrate the spirit and excitement of America's favorite pastime. From the crack of the bat to the roar of the crowd, Rice's verses transport readers to the heart of the baseball diamond, capturing the triumphs, defeats, and unforgettable moments that make the game so beloved. With vivid imagery and a deep love for the sport, these ballads pay homage to the players, the teams, and the timeless magic of baseball.

Genres: Poetry, Sports, American Literature

Themes: Baseball, Sportsmanship, Nostalgia, Passion, Community
