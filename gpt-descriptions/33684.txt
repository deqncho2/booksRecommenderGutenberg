Synopsis: In 'On the Trail of Deserters; A Phenomenal Capture' by Carter, Robert Goldthwaite, a relentless investigator embarks on a thrilling pursuit to bring deserters to justice. As he delves deeper into the shadows of betrayal and deception, he uncovers a web of secrets that will test his determination and courage. With each step closer to the truth, the stakes rise, leading to a breathtaking climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Betrayal, Justice, Perseverance
