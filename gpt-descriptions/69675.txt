Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Allworth Abbey, secrets and scandals lurk behind every elegant facade. When a mysterious stranger arrives at the abbey, the lives of its inhabitants are forever changed. As forbidden love, betrayal, and revenge intertwine, the true nature of Allworth Abbey is revealed, leaving readers captivated until the very last page.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Deception and betrayal, Family secrets, Social class and hierarchy
