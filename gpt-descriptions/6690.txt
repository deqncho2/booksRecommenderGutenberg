Synopsis: In the heart of Tanner's Lane, a quiet English village, a revolution is brewing. When a mysterious stranger arrives, the lives of the villagers are turned upside down. Secrets are unveiled, loyalties are tested, and the bonds of friendship are pushed to their limits. As tensions rise and the revolution gains momentum, the residents of Tanner's Lane must decide where their allegiances truly lie. Will they embrace change or cling to the familiar? The Revolution in Tanner's Lane is a gripping tale of intrigue, betrayal, and the power of unity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Revolution, Social Change, Loyalty, Friendship
