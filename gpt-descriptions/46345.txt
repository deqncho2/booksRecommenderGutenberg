Synopsis:
In 'Sir Quixote of the Moors: Being some account of an episode in the life of the Sieur de Rohaine', John Buchan takes us on a thrilling adventure set in the rugged landscapes of Scotland. The story follows the enigmatic Sieur de Rohaine, a man with a mysterious past and a penchant for chivalry. When he stumbles upon a hidden treasure and becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, he must navigate through treacherous moors, encounter dangerous foes, and confront his own inner demons. As the plot unfolds, the reader is drawn into a world of daring escapades, unexpected alliances, and the timeless struggle between honor and temptation.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Chivalry, Identity, Redemption
