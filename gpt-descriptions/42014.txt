Synopsis: 
In 'Meg of Mystery Mountain' by Grace May North, a young girl named Meg finds herself on a thrilling adventure when she stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. Determined to uncover the secrets of Mystery Mountain, Meg embarks on a journey filled with danger, unexpected allies, and mysterious encounters. As she delves deeper into the mountain's secrets, Meg discovers that the treasure she seeks may not be what she initially expected. Will Meg's courage and resourcefulness be enough to overcome the challenges that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Friendship, Self-discovery
