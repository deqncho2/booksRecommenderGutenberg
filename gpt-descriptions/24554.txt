Synopsis: 

In 'Down South; or, Yacht Adventure in Florida' by Optic, Oliver, a group of adventurous young friends embark on an exciting journey to the sunny state of Florida. Their destination is a luxurious yacht, where they hope to experience the thrill of sailing and explore the breathtaking beauty of the Florida coast. However, their idyllic vacation takes an unexpected turn when they stumble upon a mysterious secret that leads them into a world of danger and intrigue. As they navigate through treacherous waters and encounter unexpected obstacles, the group must rely on their wits and teamwork to unravel the truth and protect themselves from those who would do anything to keep the secret hidden.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Courage, Secrets
