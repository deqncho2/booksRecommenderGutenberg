Synopsis:
In the backdrop of British colonial India, 'A Passage to India' follows the journey of Dr. Aziz, a young Indian Muslim physician, and his encounters with the British expatriate community. When Aziz befriends two Englishwomen, Mrs. Moore and her young friend Adela, their interactions lead to a series of events that challenge the cultural and racial tensions of the time. As the story unfolds, the characters navigate through misunderstandings, prejudices, and the complexities of human connection, ultimately leading to a dramatic incident that will forever change their lives.

Genres:
Literary Fiction, Historical Fiction, Colonial Literature

Themes:
Colonialism, Racism, Cultural Misunderstandings, Friendship, Identity
