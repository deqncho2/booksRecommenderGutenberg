Synopsis: In 'The Cruise of the Little Dipper, and Other Fairy Tales', Susanne K. Langer takes readers on a whimsical journey through a collection of enchanting fairy tales. From a magical cruise aboard a tiny boat to encounters with mischievous fairies and talking animals, each story is filled with delightful surprises and imaginative adventures. These tales transport readers to a world where anything is possible, where dreams come true, and where the power of imagination reigns supreme.

Genres: Fantasy, Fairy Tales, Children's Literature

Themes: Imagination, Adventure, Magic, Transformation
