Synopsis: 
In 'Grace Harlowe's Overland Riders on the Great American Desert', Grace and her friends embark on an exhilarating adventure across the vast and treacherous Great American Desert. As they navigate through dangerous terrain, encounter mysterious strangers, and face unexpected challenges, the Overland Riders must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. Will they conquer the desert and emerge victorious, or will the harsh landscape prove to be their ultimate test?

Genres: Adventure, Western, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Perseverance
