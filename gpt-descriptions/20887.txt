Synopsis: In this thought-provoking collection of essays, John Morley delves into the life and death of Mr Mill, a prominent figure whose passing leaves a void in the intellectual world. Through an exploration of Mr Mill's autobiography, Morley uncovers the fascinating journey of a man who dedicated his life to the pursuit of knowledge and the advancement of society. With insightful analysis and captivating anecdotes, Morley sheds light on the legacy and impact of this remarkable individual.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Essay

Themes: Intellectualism, Legacy, Personal Growth
