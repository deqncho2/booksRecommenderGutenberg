Synopsis: 

In 'The Cornish Coast (South), and the Isles of Scilly', Charles G. Harper takes readers on a captivating journey along the stunning coastline of Cornwall and the enchanting Isles of Scilly. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Harper explores the hidden gems, historical landmarks, and natural wonders that make this region a true paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers. From picturesque fishing villages to dramatic cliffs and secluded beaches, the author unveils the rich tapestry of landscapes and the unique charm of the local communities. As readers delve into the pages of this travelogue, they will be transported to a world of breathtaking beauty and discover the secrets that lie within the Cornish Coast and the Isles of Scilly.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Natural beauty, Cultural heritage, Exploration
