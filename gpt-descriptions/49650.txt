Synopsis:
'Arrah Neil; or, Times of Old' by James, G. P. R. takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous times of 17th century Ireland. The story follows the life of Arrah Neil, a young and spirited woman who finds herself caught in the midst of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the struggle for power. As Arrah navigates through a world filled with danger and uncertainty, she must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and the fate of those she holds dear. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling characters, this novel promises an enthralling tale of love, loyalty, and resilience.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes:
Love and Sacrifice, Political Intrigue, Power Struggles
