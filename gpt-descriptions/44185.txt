Synopsis: In 'The Fighting Starkleys; or, The Test of Courage' by Theodore Goodridge Roberts, the Starkley siblings find themselves embroiled in a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a mysterious map that leads to hidden treasure. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter dangerous foes, the Starkleys must summon all their courage and resourcefulness to overcome the challenges that lie ahead. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Family, Exploration
