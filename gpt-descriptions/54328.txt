Synopsis: 
'York: A Sketch-Book' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the historic city of York, England. Through the eyes of the talented artist Gordon Home, the book presents a collection of stunning sketches that capture the essence and charm of this ancient city. From its iconic landmarks and picturesque streets to its hidden corners and bustling markets, each sketch unveils a unique aspect of York's rich history and vibrant culture. With intricate details and a keen eye for beauty, Home's sketches transport readers to the heart of York, inviting them to explore its enchanting atmosphere and discover its hidden treasures.

Genres: 
Art, Travel, History

Themes: 
Cityscapes, Cultural Heritage, Architectural Beauty
