Synopsis: 

In 'Thirty Canadian V.Cs., 23d April 1915 to 30th March 1918', Theodore Goodridge Roberts, along with the Canadian War Records Office, Stuart Martin, and Robin Richards, presents a captivating collection of stories that highlight the extraordinary bravery and heroism of thirty Canadian soldiers during the period of April 1915 to March 1918. From the trenches of World War I to the battlefields of Europe, these accounts shed light on the remarkable acts of valor and sacrifice displayed by these individuals in the face of unimaginable adversity. 

Genres: Military history, Biography, War literature

Themes: Bravery, Heroism, Sacrifice, World War I
