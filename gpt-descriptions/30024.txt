Synopsis: In 'The Fisher-Boy Urashima', a young fisherman named Urashima Taro embarks on a captivating adventure that takes him to a mysterious underwater realm. As he delves deeper into this enchanting world, he discovers secrets that challenge his perception of reality and the passage of time. This timeless tale weaves together elements of fantasy, folklore, and adventure, captivating readers with its mesmerizing narrative and unexpected twists.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Perception of reality, Passage of time
