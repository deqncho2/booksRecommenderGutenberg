Synopsis: 
In 'Why Marry?' by Jesse Lynch Williams, a group of friends gather for a lively discussion on the institution of marriage. As they delve into the complexities of love, commitment, and societal expectations, they find themselves questioning the true purpose of marriage and the pursuit of happiness. With witty dialogue and thought-provoking arguments, this play explores the timeless question of whether marriage is a necessity or a choice, and challenges conventional notions of what it means to find fulfillment in life.

Genres: Drama, Comedy

Themes: Marriage, Love, Happiness, Societal Expectations
