Synopsis: In "Married Love: A New Contribution to the Solution of Sex Difficulties," Marie Carmichael Stopes delves into the intimate and often misunderstood realm of marital relationships. With a compassionate and insightful approach, she explores the complexities of sexual difficulties that couples may encounter, offering practical advice and guidance to enhance their connection. Through personal anecdotes and scientific research, Stopes sheds light on the importance of communication, understanding, and mutual satisfaction in the pursuit of a fulfilling and harmonious marriage.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Psychology

Themes: Marriage, Intimacy, Communication, Sexual satisfaction, Relationships
