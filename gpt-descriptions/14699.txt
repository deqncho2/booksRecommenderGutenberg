Synopsis: In 'Shakespeare Study Programs; The Comedies', Clarke and Porter delve into the enchanting world of Shakespeare's comedic plays, offering readers a comprehensive analysis and exploration of these timeless works. From mistaken identities and witty wordplay to love triangles and hilarious misunderstandings, this book takes readers on a journey through the delightful and often absurd world of Shakespearean comedy. With insightful commentary and in-depth character studies, the authors shed light on the themes of love, deception, and the power of laughter that permeate these plays. Whether you're a Shakespeare enthusiast or a newcomer to his works, this book is sure to captivate and entertain.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Drama, Theatre Studies

Themes: Love, Deception, Laughter, Identity
