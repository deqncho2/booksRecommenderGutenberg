Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Booth Tarkington' by Booth Tarkington is a comprehensive collection of the renowned American author's works. This index provides a convenient guide to explore Tarkington's literary universe, encompassing a wide range of captivating stories and characters. From his Pulitzer Prize-winning novel "The Magnificent Ambersons" to the delightful tales of the mischievous Penrod, this collection offers a glimpse into Tarkington's mastery of storytelling and his ability to capture the essence of American life during the early 20th century.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Satire, Drama, Humor

Themes: Social change, Class dynamics, Family relationships, Youthful innocence, Nostalgia
