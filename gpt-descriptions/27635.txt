Synopsis: In 'The Canterbury Puzzles, and Other Curious Problems', Henry Ernest Dudeney presents a collection of mind-bending puzzles and intriguing problems that will captivate and challenge readers. From mathematical conundrums to logical riddles, this book takes readers on a journey through a world of perplexing enigmas and clever solutions. With each puzzle, Dudeney invites readers to exercise their problem-solving skills and think outside the box, providing hours of entertainment and mental stimulation. Whether you are a puzzle enthusiast or simply enjoy a good mental workout, 'The Canterbury Puzzles, and Other Curious Problems' is sure to leave you both fascinated and eager to unravel the mysteries within.

Genres: Puzzle, Mathematics, Logic

Themes: Problem-solving, Critical thinking, Creativity
