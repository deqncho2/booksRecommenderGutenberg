Synopsis: In 'The Invisible Man: A Grotesque Romance', a mysterious stranger arrives in a small English village, wrapped in bandages and hiding a dark secret. As the villagers become increasingly suspicious of his strange behavior, they soon discover that his ability to turn invisible is just the beginning of his terrifying powers. With a thrilling blend of suspense and horror, this classic tale explores the consequences of unchecked power and the thin line between science and madness.

Genres: Science fiction, Horror, Thriller

Themes: Power and its consequences, Identity and perception, The dangers of scientific experimentation
