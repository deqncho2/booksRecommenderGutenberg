Synopsis: In "As Gold in the Furnace: A College Story," follow the journey of a young college student named Mark as he navigates the challenges of academia, friendships, and self-discovery. As Mark immerses himself in the vibrant college atmosphere, he encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own ambitions and secrets. Through a series of unexpected events, Mark finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and must confront his own inner demons. This gripping tale explores the complexities of college life, the pursuit of personal growth, and the transformative power of friendship.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Ambition, Friendship, Self-discovery
