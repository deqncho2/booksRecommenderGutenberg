Synopsis: 

'Old Continental Towns' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting streets and hidden corners of historic European towns. From the picturesque canals of Bruges to the vibrant markets of Florence, Gallichan's vivid descriptions transport us to a bygone era, where each town holds its own unique charm and secrets. Through his keen observations and historical anecdotes, the author unveils the rich tapestry of these old-world destinations, inviting readers to wander through cobblestone streets, explore ancient architecture, and immerse themselves in the captivating stories that lie within these towns' walls.

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: European history, Architecture, Cultural heritage
