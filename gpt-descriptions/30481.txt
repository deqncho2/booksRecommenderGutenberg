Synopsis: In 'Ioläus: The man that was a ghost' by James Allan Mackereth, a mysterious man named Ioläus appears in a small town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic presence. As the townspeople become increasingly intrigued by Ioläus, they uncover a haunting secret that threatens to unravel their lives. With elements of mystery and suspense, this gripping tale explores the blurred lines between reality and the supernatural, leaving readers questioning the nature of existence itself.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Perception, Existence
