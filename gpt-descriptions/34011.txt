Synopsis: In 'The Development of Rates of Postage: An Historical and Analytical Study', A. D. Smith takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating evolution of postal rates. From ancient civilizations to modern times, Smith delves into the intricate details of how postage rates have shaped societies and influenced communication. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, this book uncovers the hidden stories behind the seemingly mundane world of postage. Discover the surprising connections between postal rates and economic development, political systems, and even cultural exchange. Prepare to be enthralled by this unique exploration of an often overlooked aspect of history.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Cultural Studies

Themes: Communication, Economic Development, Cultural Exchange
