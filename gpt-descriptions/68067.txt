Synopsis: In 'Frank Reade, Jr., with his new steam horse in the great American desert: or, The sandy trail of death', the brilliant inventor Frank Reade, Jr. embarks on a thrilling adventure across the treacherous American desert. Equipped with his latest invention, a powerful steam horse, Frank Reade, Jr. faces deadly challenges and encounters mysterious adversaries as he navigates the unforgiving terrain. With danger lurking at every turn, will Frank Reade, Jr. and his steam horse conquer the sandy trail of death?

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Western

Themes: Invention and Innovation, Survival, Exploration
