Synopsis: In 'How to Read the Crystal; or, Crystal and Seer' by Sepharial, embark on a captivating journey into the mystical world of crystal gazing and clairvoyance. Follow the protagonist as they delve into the secrets of the crystal, unlocking its power to reveal hidden truths and foresee the future. As they navigate the realms of the supernatural, they encounter unexpected challenges and encounter fascinating characters who possess their own unique abilities. With each revelation, the line between reality and the ethereal becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader spellbound until the very end.

Genres: Occult fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Divination, Supernatural abilities, Perception of reality
