Synopsis: In 'The Tory Maid' by Stimpson, Herbert Baird, a young woman named Mary Hamilton finds herself caught in the midst of the American Revolution. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, Mary must navigate a dangerous world of espionage, romance, and betrayal. With her life hanging in the balance, she must make difficult choices that will shape the course of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Loyalty, Patriotism, Sacrifice, Identity
