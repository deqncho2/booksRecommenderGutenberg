Synopsis: In the small town of Hanbury, Lady Ludlow rules with an iron fist, upholding the traditions and hierarchy of the aristocracy. But when a young woman named Miss Galindo arrives, she challenges Lady Ludlow's beliefs and brings about a series of events that will test the boundaries of class, love, and loyalty. As secrets are revealed and lives are changed, the town of Hanbury will never be the same again.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, social commentary

Themes: Class hierarchy, societal expectations, love and loyalty, women's roles in society
