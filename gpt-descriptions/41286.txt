Synopsis:
In the charming town of Carlingford, Miss Lucilla Marjoribanks, a young and ambitious woman, returns home after completing her education. Determined to make her mark on society, she sets out to transform the town's social scene and become its undisputed queen. With her wit, intelligence, and unwavering confidence, Miss Marjoribanks navigates the complexities of matchmaking, politics, and family dynamics, all while maintaining her own independence and pursuing her personal goals. As she orchestrates various social events and manipulates the people around her, Miss Marjoribanks discovers that her journey to success may not be as straightforward as she initially thought. Will she achieve her dreams or will her ambitions lead her down unexpected paths?

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Comedy

Themes: Ambition, Social Climbing, Gender Roles, Independence
