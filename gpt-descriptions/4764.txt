Synopsis: 
'A Family Man: in three acts' by John Galsworthy is a gripping play that delves into the complexities of family dynamics and the sacrifices one must make for their loved ones. The story revolves around the life of a successful businessman, Robert Cokeson, who finds himself torn between his professional ambitions and the needs of his family. As he navigates the challenges of his career and the demands of his loved ones, Robert must confront the consequences of his choices and ultimately discover what truly matters in life.

Genres: Drama, Family, Tragedy

Themes: Family relationships, Sacrifice, Ambition, Priorities
