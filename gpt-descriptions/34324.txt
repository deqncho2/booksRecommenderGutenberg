Synopsis: In 'Zen Culture' by Thomas Hoover, embark on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Zen philosophy, art, and history. From the serene gardens of Kyoto to the bustling streets of Tokyo, this immersive exploration unveils the profound influence of Zen on Japanese culture. Delve into the enigmatic world of Zen masters, samurai warriors, and tea ceremonies, as the author unravels the secrets of Zen's timeless wisdom and its impact on the modern world. Prepare to be enchanted by the beauty, tranquility, and profound insights that Zen Culture has to offer.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, History, Travel

Themes: Zen philosophy, Japanese culture, Art and aesthetics, Meditation, Mindfulness
