Synopsis: 
In 'The Boy Volunteers with the British Artillery' by Kenneth Ward, readers are taken on an exhilarating journey through the eyes of a young boy who joins the British Artillery during a time of war. From the moment he enlists, the boy finds himself thrust into a world of danger, camaraderie, and unexpected heroism. As he navigates the harsh realities of war, he forms unbreakable bonds with his fellow soldiers and learns valuable lessons about courage, sacrifice, and the true meaning of patriotism. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Coming of age, Friendship, Patriotism, Courage, Sacrifice
