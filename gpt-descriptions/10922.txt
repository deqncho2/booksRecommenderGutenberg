Synopsis: 
In 'Young Lives' by Le Gallienne, Richard, we are introduced to a group of young individuals who are navigating the complexities of life, love, and self-discovery in the vibrant city of New York. As they embark on their respective journeys, their paths intertwine, leading to unexpected connections and profound revelations. Through their experiences, the novel explores the exhilaration and heartache of youth, the pursuit of dreams, and the transformative power of human connection.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Youth, Identity, Love, Ambition, Friendship
