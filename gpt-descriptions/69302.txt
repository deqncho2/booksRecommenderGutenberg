Synopsis: 
'Land of Play: Verses, Rhymes, Stories' takes readers on a whimsical journey through a magical world filled with enchanting characters, captivating adventures, and delightful surprises. From talking animals to mischievous fairies, each page is brimming with imaginative tales that will transport readers of all ages to a place where anything is possible. With its lyrical verses, charming rhymes, and heartwarming stories, this book is sure to ignite the imagination and leave readers longing for more.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Poetry

Themes: Imagination, Adventure, Friendship, Magic
