Synopsis: In 'The White Eagle of Poland' by E.F. Benson, a young Englishman finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when he inherits a dilapidated castle in Poland. As he delves into the history of his newfound property, he uncovers a centuries-old secret that could change the course of his own destiny. With the help of a captivating Polish woman, he embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him deep into the heart of Poland's past, where legends and reality intertwine in unexpected ways.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Heritage and Identity, Love and Sacrifice, Secrets and Betrayal
