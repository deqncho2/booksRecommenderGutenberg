Synopsis: In 'The Klygha' by H. B. Fyfe, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Richard Hargraves embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that could change the course of humanity. However, his creation, the Klygha, possesses an unexpected and dangerous ability that threatens to unravel everything he has worked for. As Dr. Hargraves races against time to understand and control the Klygha, he finds himself caught in a web of moral dilemmas and unforeseen consequences that challenge his own humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Artificial Intelligence, Human Nature
