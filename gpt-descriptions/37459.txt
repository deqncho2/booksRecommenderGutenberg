Synopsis: Polly, a young and adventurous girl, finds herself in the bustling city of New York. As she navigates the vibrant streets, she encounters a cast of colorful characters and experiences the excitement and challenges of urban life. From unexpected friendships to thrilling escapades, Polly's journey in the Big Apple becomes a transformative adventure that will leave readers captivated.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Self-discovery
