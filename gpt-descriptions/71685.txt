Synopsis: 

In "It might have been worse: A Motor Trip from Coast to Coast," Beatrice Larned Massey takes readers on an exhilarating journey across the United States. Join her and her husband as they embark on a daring adventure, encountering unexpected challenges, breathtaking landscapes, and colorful characters along the way. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene beauty of the Grand Canyon, this travel memoir captures the essence of the American spirit and the wonders that await those who dare to explore.

Genres: Travel, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Resilience, Cultural diversity
