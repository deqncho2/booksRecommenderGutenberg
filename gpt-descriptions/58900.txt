Synopsis: In 'Where Animals Talk: West African Folk Lore Tales', Robert Hamill Nassau takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and enchanting world of West African folklore. From cunning tortoises to wise elephants, this collection of tales brings to life the vibrant characters and magical landscapes of traditional African storytelling. Each story is filled with suspense, humor, and valuable life lessons, as animals and humans interact in extraordinary ways. Through these captivating narratives, Nassau invites readers to explore the depths of human nature, the power of wisdom, and the importance of harmony with nature. 

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural

Themes: Wisdom, Nature, Human Nature
