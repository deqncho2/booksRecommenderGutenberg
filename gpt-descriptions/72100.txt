Synopsis: 

In "Geological facts; or, the crust of the earth, what it is, and what are its uses," author W. G. Barrett takes readers on a captivating journey deep into the Earth's crust, uncovering fascinating geological facts and exploring the practical applications of this knowledge. From the formation of rocks and minerals to the forces that shape our landscapes, Barrett presents a wealth of information that will leave readers in awe of the Earth's incredible history and its impact on our daily lives. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Geology

Themes: Earth's formation, Geological processes, Practical applications of geology
