Synopsis: In 'Hopalong Cassidy's Rustler Round-Up; Or, Bar-20', Hopalong Cassidy finds himself caught in a web of deception and danger as he tries to uncover the truth behind a series of cattle rustlings. With his trusty companions by his side, Hopalong must navigate through treacherous terrain and face off against a cunning adversary who will stop at nothing to protect his illicit operation. As the stakes rise and the tension mounts, Hopalong's quick wit and sharpshooting skills are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to outsmart the rustlers and bring them to justice, or will he become their next victim?

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Justice, Loyalty, Deception
