Synopsis: 

In 'Survey of the High Roads of England and Wales. Part the First.: Comprising the counties of Kent, Surrey, Sussex, Hants, Wilts, Dorset, Somerset, Devon, and Cornwall', Edward S. Mogg takes readers on an enchanting journey through the picturesque landscapes of southern England. This meticulously detailed guidebook provides a comprehensive overview of the high roads, offering invaluable insights into the historical landmarks, charming towns, and breathtaking natural beauty that await travelers along the way. From the rolling hills of Kent to the rugged coastlines of Cornwall, Mogg's vivid descriptions and practical advice make this book an indispensable companion for anyone seeking to explore the hidden gems of England and Wales.

Genres: Travel Guide, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Heritage, Natural Beauty
