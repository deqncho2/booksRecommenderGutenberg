Synopsis: In 'Making His Way; Or, Frank Courtney's Struggle Upward', we follow the journey of Frank Courtney, a young and ambitious protagonist who is determined to rise above his humble beginnings. Faced with numerous challenges and setbacks, Frank must navigate the treacherous world of business and society, all while staying true to his values and aspirations. Will Frank be able to overcome the obstacles in his path and achieve the success he so desperately desires?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman, Realistic fiction

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Social mobility, Integrity
