Synopsis: In 'Dangerous Ground; or, The Rival Detectives' by Lynch, Lawrence L., two brilliant detectives find themselves entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse. As they race against time to solve a perplexing case, their rivalry intensifies, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With danger lurking at every corner, the detectives must navigate treacherous ground to uncover the truth and outsmart their cunning adversary.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Rivalry, Deception, Danger, Perseverance
