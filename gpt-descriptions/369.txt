Synopsis: In 'The Outlaw of Torn', a mysterious and feared figure known as the Outlaw wreaks havoc in medieval England, challenging the authority of the king and leaving a trail of chaos in his wake. As the Outlaw's true identity remains shrouded in secrecy, a young noblewoman finds herself drawn to him, torn between her loyalty to her family and her growing fascination with this enigmatic figure. With political intrigue, forbidden love, and a quest for justice, 'The Outlaw of Torn' takes readers on a thrilling journey through a turbulent and dangerous world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Justice
