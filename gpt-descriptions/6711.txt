Synopsis: In "Philip Dru: Administrator; A Story of Tomorrow, 1920-1935," Edward Mandell House takes readers on a captivating journey into a future world where a charismatic leader rises to power and transforms the United States. As the story unfolds, we witness the protagonist, Philip Dru, navigate through political intrigue, social unrest, and personal challenges, all while striving to create a utopian society. With its gripping narrative and thought-provoking vision of the future, this novel offers a compelling exploration of power, revolution, and the potential for change.

Genres: Political fiction, Dystopian fiction, Science fiction

Themes: Leadership, Social change, Utopia, Revolution
