Synopsis: 
In "The Madman: His Parables and Poems," Kahlil Gibran takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the mind of a madman. Through a collection of parables and poems, the madman challenges societal norms, questions the nature of truth, and explores the depths of human existence. With his unconventional wisdom and poetic language, the madman invites readers to question their own perceptions and embrace the beauty of madness.

Genres: Poetry, Philosophy, Fiction

Themes: Individuality, Truth, Madness, Society, Existentialism
