Synopsis: In 'King of Ranleigh: A School Story', follow the thrilling adventures of a young boy named Jack Vance as he navigates the challenges and mysteries of his new boarding school. From secret societies to hidden treasures, Jack finds himself caught up in a web of intrigue and danger. With the help of his friends, he must unravel the mysteries of Ranleigh and prove himself worthy of the title 'King'. Will Jack succeed in his quest, or will the secrets of Ranleigh remain forever hidden?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, School

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Courage, Discovery
