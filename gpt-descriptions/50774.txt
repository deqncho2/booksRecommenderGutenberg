Synopsis: In 'Contagion' by Katherine MacLean, a deadly virus is unleashed, spreading rapidly and causing chaos across the globe. As scientists race against time to find a cure, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate through a world on the brink of collapse, facing not only the physical threat of the virus but also the moral dilemmas that arise in times of crisis. With suspenseful twists and unexpected alliances, 'Contagion' explores the resilience of the human spirit and the lengths people will go to survive.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Morality, Human Resilience
