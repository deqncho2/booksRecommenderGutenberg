Synopsis: 
In 'The Two Vanrevels' by Booth Tarkington, the small town of Kings Port is abuzz with gossip and scandal when a mysterious stranger arrives, claiming to be the long-lost son of a prominent family. As the town's secrets unravel, a forbidden love affair and a shocking betrayal threaten to tear apart the community. With its captivating characters and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the complexities of identity, loyalty, and the power of secrets.

Genres: 
Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: 
Identity, Loyalty, Secrets
