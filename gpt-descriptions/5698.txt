Synopsis: In "Fanny's First Play" by Bernard Shaw, a young woman named Fanny is about to have her first play performed. However, the play takes an unexpected turn when the characters come to life and challenge societal norms and conventions. As Fanny's play unfolds, it becomes a witty and thought-provoking exploration of love, marriage, and the role of women in society. With clever dialogue and sharp satire, Shaw invites the audience to question the status quo and consider alternative perspectives.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Satire

Themes: Love, Marriage, Gender roles, Social conventions
