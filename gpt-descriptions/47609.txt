Synopsis: 
'Nursery Comedies: Twelve Tiny Plays for Children' by Florence Bell is a delightful collection of short plays that will captivate young readers. Each play presents a unique and humorous story, filled with lively characters and imaginative settings. From mischievous fairies to talking animals, these plays are sure to entertain and engage children's imaginations. Whether it's a magical adventure or a comical mishap, each play offers a delightful and entertaining experience for young readers.

Genres: Children's Literature, Comedy, Fantasy

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Adventure
