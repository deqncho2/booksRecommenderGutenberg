Synopsis: 
In 'The Story of a Governess' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), we follow the life of a young governess named Lucy, who finds herself thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when she takes up a position in a grand country estate. As she navigates the complexities of her new role, Lucy becomes entangled in the secrets and hidden desires of the household's inhabitants. With each passing day, she uncovers shocking revelations that challenge her perception of love, loyalty, and the true nature of those around her. As the tension builds, Lucy must decide whether to remain a passive observer or take matters into her own hands, risking everything she holds dear.

Genres: 
Gothic Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Drama

Themes: 
Deception, Power dynamics, Identity, Morality
