Synopsis: 
In 'Selections from Modern Poets: Made by J. C. Squire', readers are taken on a captivating journey through the minds and hearts of some of the most talented poets of the modern era. This anthology carefully curates a diverse collection of poems that explore the depths of human emotions, from love and loss to hope and despair. Each poem is a window into a different world, offering unique perspectives and evoking powerful imagery that will leave readers spellbound. With its masterful selection and arrangement, this book invites readers to immerse themselves in the beauty and power of modern poetry.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology

Themes: Love, Loss, Hope, Despair, Human Emotions
