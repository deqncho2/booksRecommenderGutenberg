Synopsis: In 'Stories of Useful Inventions' by S. E. Forman, embark on a captivating journey through time and discover the fascinating stories behind some of the world's most ingenious inventions. From the creation of the telephone to the development of the light bulb, each chapter unveils the trials, triumphs, and unexpected twists that led to these groundbreaking innovations. Delve into the lives of inventors and witness their relentless pursuit of knowledge and their unwavering determination to change the world. With vivid storytelling and intriguing anecdotes, this book will leave you inspired by the power of human ingenuity and the impact of these inventions on society.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Biography

Themes: Innovation, Persistence, Impact of Inventions
