Synopsis: In 'Prince or Chauffeur? A Story of Newport' by Perry, Lawrence, a chance encounter between a wealthy socialite and a humble chauffeur sets off a chain of events that will challenge societal norms and ignite a forbidden romance. Set against the backdrop of the opulent Newport, this captivating tale explores the complexities of love, class, and the pursuit of happiness in a world where appearances are everything.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Social class, Forbidden love, Identity, Society's expectations
