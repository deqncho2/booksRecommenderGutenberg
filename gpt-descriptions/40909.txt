Synopsis: In 'Anthony Trent, Master Criminal' by Martyn, Wyndham, follow the thrilling adventures of Anthony Trent, a master of disguise and a criminal genius. With his cunning mind and impeccable skills, Trent embarks on a series of daring heists, leaving the police baffled and the public in awe. But as he delves deeper into the world of crime, Trent finds himself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where his own secrets may be his downfall. Will he be able to outsmart his pursuers and maintain his double life as a respected member of society? Prepare for a gripping tale of deception, suspense, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Morality, Deception
