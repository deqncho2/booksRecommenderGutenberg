Synopsis: 
'The Modern Housewife or, Ménagère' is a comprehensive guidebook written by Alexis Soyer, offering nearly one thousand recipes for the economic and judicious preparation of every meal of the day. It goes beyond just cooking, providing instructions for managing the nursery, sick room, and various aspects of family management. With its wealth of practical advice and detailed directions, this book is an indispensable resource for any modern housewife seeking to excel in her role.

Genres: Cookbook, Household Management Guide

Themes: Culinary expertise, Household organization, Family management
