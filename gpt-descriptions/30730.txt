Synopsis: 
"The Hound of Heaven" by Francis Thompson is a captivating and thought-provoking poem that follows the journey of a desperate soul pursued relentlessly by an unseen force. As the protagonist tries to escape, he is pursued by a relentless hound, representing an overwhelming and divine presence. Through vivid imagery and powerful metaphors, the poem explores the themes of spiritual longing, the pursuit of happiness, and the inescapable nature of divine love.

Genres: Poetry, Spiritual, Allegory

Themes: Spiritual longing, Pursuit of happiness, Divine love
