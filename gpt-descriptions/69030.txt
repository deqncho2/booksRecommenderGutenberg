Synopsis: In "The Girl in the Crowd" by Albert Payson Terhune, a young woman named Marion finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she witnesses a crime during a crowded event. As she becomes entangled in the investigation, Marion must navigate a world of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With her life on the line, she must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Crime, Deception, Survival
