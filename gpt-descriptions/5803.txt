Synopsis: 
In "Not that it Matters" by A. A. Milne, a collection of humorous essays, the reader is taken on a delightful journey through the author's witty observations and musings on various topics. From the absurdity of modern life to the quirks of human behavior, Milne's sharp wit and clever writing style will keep readers entertained and laughing throughout. With each essay offering a unique perspective and a touch of whimsy, this collection is a delightful escape into the world of Milne's imagination.

Genres: Humor, Essays

Themes: Absurdity of modern life, Human behavior, Whimsical observations
