Synopsis: 

In 'Siam: The Land of the White Elephant as It Was and Is', George B. Bacon takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting kingdom of Siam. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Bacon paints a mesmerizing picture of the country's rich history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking landscapes. From the bustling streets of Bangkok to the serene temples hidden in the mountains, readers will be transported to a world filled with exotic beauty and ancient traditions. As Bacon delves into the customs, beliefs, and daily life of the Siamese people, he uncovers fascinating stories and encounters with royalty, monks, and ordinary citizens. This captivating travelogue offers a unique glimpse into a land that is both mysterious and alluring, leaving readers yearning to explore the wonders of Siam for themselves.

Genres: Travelogue, History, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Diversity, Tradition, Beauty of Nature
