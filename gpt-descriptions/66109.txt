Synopsis: In the captivating 'Young Engineer's Guide' by Rohan, J. V., readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside a brilliant young engineer named Alex. Faced with a series of complex challenges, Alex must navigate the intricate world of engineering, where innovation and problem-solving skills are put to the ultimate test. As he delves deeper into his work, Alex discovers that the path to success is not always straightforward, and he must confront unexpected obstacles that push him to his limits. With a blend of suspense, intrigue, and a touch of romance, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within the world of engineering.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Innovation, Problem-solving, Perseverance
