Synopsis: In "The Cliff Climbers: A Sequel to "The Plant Hunters,"" the adventurous siblings, Harry and Kate, embark on a thrilling expedition to the treacherous cliffs of South America. As they navigate through dangerous terrain and encounter mysterious creatures, they must rely on their wit and courage to uncover the secrets hidden within the cliffs. With breathtaking landscapes and heart-pounding adventures, this sequel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies beyond the precipice.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Sibling Bond, Discovery
