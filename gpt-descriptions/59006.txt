Synopsis: In 'Pogonologia; Or, A Philosophical and Historical Essay on Beards', Jacques-Antoine Dulaure takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of beards. From ancient civilizations to modern times, Dulaure explores the cultural, social, and even political significance of facial hair. Delving into the symbolism, rituals, and myths surrounding beards, this thought-provoking essay sheds light on the profound impact that this seemingly simple feature has had on human history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Identity, Symbolism, Social Constructs
