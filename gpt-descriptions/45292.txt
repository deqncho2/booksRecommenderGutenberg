Synopsis: 
In 'The Placid Pug, and Other Rhymes' by Douglas, Alfred Bruce, readers are taken on a whimsical journey through a collection of delightful rhymes. From mischievous cats to adventurous dogs, and from magical creatures to everyday objects with a twist, this book is filled with charming tales that will captivate both young and old. Each rhyme is beautifully crafted, with vivid imagery and clever wordplay that will leave readers smiling and eager for more. Whether it's a tale of friendship, a lesson in bravery, or a simple celebration of life's little joys, 'The Placid Pug, and Other Rhymes' is a delightful read that will enchant readers of all ages.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Adventure, Imagination, Joy
