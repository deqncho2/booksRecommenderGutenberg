Synopsis: In 'Proper Pride: A Novel. Volume 3', the captivating conclusion to the series by B. M. Croker, the lives of two strong-willed women, Lady Jane and her niece, Lady Betty, take unexpected turns as they navigate the complexities of love, society, and personal growth. As secrets are revealed and alliances tested, the reader is drawn into a world of intrigue and passion, where the characters must confront their own desires and the consequences of their actions. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this novel will keep readers eagerly turning the pages until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Society and Class, Personal Growth
