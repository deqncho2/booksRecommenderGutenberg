Synopsis: 

'Lineage, Life and Labors of José Rizal, Philippine Patriot' by Craig, Austin is a captivating biography that delves into the extraordinary life of José Rizal, a prominent figure in Philippine history. From his humble beginnings to his remarkable achievements, this book uncovers the untold story of a true patriot and national hero. 

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Nationalism, Identity, Colonialism, Revolution
