Synopsis: 
In 'The Diamond Pin' by Wells, Carolyn, a mysterious diamond pin becomes the center of attention when it goes missing during a glamorous society event. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and unexpected twists keep the reader guessing until the very end. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot filled with suspense, this thrilling mystery will leave you on the edge of your seat.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Wealth and Status
