Synopsis: In the distant future, a group of colonists on the planet of Mount Lookitthat discover a secret that could change their lives forever. As they navigate a society ruled by a powerful elite, they must decide whether to use this newfound knowledge for their own gain or for the betterment of all. With high stakes and unexpected twists, 'A Gift from Earth' takes readers on a thrilling journey of morality, power, and the consequences of our choices.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Morality, Power, Choice, Social Inequality
