Synopsis: In 'What Peace Means' by Henry Van Dyke, a young artist embarks on a journey to discover the true meaning of peace. Along the way, he encounters unexpected challenges and encounters that test his understanding of the world. Through his experiences, he begins to unravel the complexities of peace and its profound impact on humanity. This thought-provoking tale explores the depths of the human spirit and the transformative power of finding inner peace.

Genres: Fiction, Inspirational, Philosophical

Themes: Peace, Self-discovery, Human nature
