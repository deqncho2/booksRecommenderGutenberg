Synopsis: 
In 'Accidental Flight' by Wallace, F. L., a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of danger and intrigue when he stumbles upon a mysterious package. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
