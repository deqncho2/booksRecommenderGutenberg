Synopsis: 
'Scenes and Legends of the North of Scotland: or, The Traditional History of Cromarty' by Hugh Miller is a captivating collection of stories that takes readers on a journey through the enchanting landscapes and rich folklore of the Scottish Highlands. From ancient legends and mythical creatures to tales of love, betrayal, and redemption, this book weaves together a tapestry of captivating narratives that will transport readers to a world of mystery and wonder. As the stories unfold, the reader will uncover the hidden depths of Cromarty's history and the enduring power of its traditions.

Genres: 
Fiction, Historical Fiction, Folklore

Themes: 
Tradition, Mythology, History, Love and Betrayal
