Synopsis: Marjorie Dean, a young and ambitious woman, finds herself embarking on a thrilling journey as she enters Hamilton Arms, a prestigious boarding school. Surrounded by a diverse group of classmates, Marjorie must navigate the challenges of friendship, academic pressures, and unexpected mysteries that unfold within the walls of the school. As she strives to excel in her studies and uncover the secrets hidden at Hamilton Arms, Marjorie's determination and resilience are put to the test, leading her on a path of self-discovery and personal growth.

Genres: Young Adult, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Ambition, Academic Pressure, Self-Discovery
