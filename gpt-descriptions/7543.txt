Synopsis: 
'Quotes and Images From The Tales and Novels of Jean de La Fontaine' is a collection of thought-provoking quotes and captivating images from the renowned works of Jean de La Fontaine. This compilation showcases the wit, wisdom, and timeless charm of La Fontaine's fables and novels, offering readers a glimpse into the rich tapestry of his storytelling. From the mischievous antics of animals to the foibles of human nature, these quotes and images invite readers to reflect on the complexities of life and the moral lessons hidden within La Fontaine's tales.

Genres: 
Literary Fiction, Fables, Poetry

Themes: 
Morality, Human Nature, Animal Behavior, Satire
