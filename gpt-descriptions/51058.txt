Synopsis: 
In 'Motorcycle, Solo (Harley-Davidson Model WLA)', the United States War Department takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the eyes of a fearless soldier and his trusty Harley-Davidson Model WLA motorcycle. As the soldier navigates treacherous terrains and faces unexpected challenges, the story unfolds with heart-pounding action and unexpected twists. This gripping narrative offers a unique perspective on the role of motorcycles in wartime and the indomitable spirit of those who ride them.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: 
Courage, Camaraderie, Resilience
