Synopsis: In 'The Spider and the Fly; or, An Undesired Love' by Charles Garvice, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of deceit and danger when she becomes the object of an unwanted affection. As she navigates the treacherous path of love, she must unravel the mysteries surrounding her admirer and make choices that could have dire consequences. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering who to trust and what lies beneath the surface.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Love and obsession, Deception and betrayal
