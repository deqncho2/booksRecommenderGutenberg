Synopsis: In 'The Water Eater' by Winston K. Marks, a small coastal town is plagued by a mysterious creature that lurks in the depths of the ocean, preying on unsuspecting victims. As the town's residents become increasingly terrified, a group of unlikely heroes must band together to uncover the truth behind the creature's existence and find a way to stop it before it claims more lives. With suspenseful twists and heart-pounding action, 'The Water Eater' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Horror, Mystery

Themes: Fear, Survival, Community
