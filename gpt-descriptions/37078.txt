Synopsis: In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 93., October 22, 1887', readers are transported to the vibrant and satirical world of Victorian England. Through a collection of witty and humorous articles, cartoons, and poems, this volume offers a delightful glimpse into the social and political issues of the time. From comical observations on fashion trends to clever commentaries on the latest scandals, this edition of Punch is sure to entertain and enlighten readers with its sharp wit and clever insights.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Periodical

Themes: Social commentary, Victorian society, Political satire
