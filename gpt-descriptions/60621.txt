Synopsis: In 'Boys' Make-at-Home Things', brothers Jack and Tom embark on a thrilling adventure as they discover a hidden treasure map in their grandfather's attic. With their creativity and resourcefulness, they set out to build ingenious contraptions and solve puzzles, leading them on a quest filled with excitement, danger, and unexpected twists. As they navigate through secret passages and unravel the mysteries of the map, the boys learn valuable lessons about teamwork, perseverance, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Creativity, Problem-solving, Family bonds, Exploration
