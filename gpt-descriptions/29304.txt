Synopsis: In the Days of Drake takes readers on a thrilling journey back to the time of the famous English explorer, Sir Francis Drake. Set against the backdrop of the Elizabethan era, this captivating historical novel follows the adventures of a young man who becomes entangled in a dangerous mission alongside Drake and his crew. As they sail the treacherous seas, facing pirates, political intrigue, and the unknown, the protagonist must navigate a world of secrets and betrayal. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this tale of courage and loyalty will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Loyalty, Betrayal
