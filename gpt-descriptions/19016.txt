Synopsis: 
In 'Dave Porter at Star Ranch; Or, The Cowboy's Secret' by Stratemeyer, Edward, we follow the thrilling adventures of Dave Porter as he embarks on a journey to Star Ranch. Dave's arrival at the ranch is met with mystery and intrigue, as he discovers a secret that could change everything. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the ranch, Dave must navigate the treacherous world of cowboys and uncover the truth before it's too late. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Western

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Coming of age, Courage and bravery
