Synopsis: 
'Letters of Franz Liszt -- Volume 1: from Paris to Rome: Years of Travel as a Virtuoso' is a collection of personal letters written by the renowned composer and pianist, Franz Liszt. Spanning his time from Paris to Rome, the letters provide a captivating glimpse into the life of a virtuoso musician during the 19th century. Liszt's letters reveal his encounters with fellow artists, his thoughts on music and art, and his reflections on the cities he visits. Through his vivid descriptions and intimate musings, readers are transported to a world of artistic passion, cultural exploration, and the inner workings of a musical genius.

Genres: Biography, Music, Travelogue

Themes: Artistic expression, Cultural exploration, Personal growth
