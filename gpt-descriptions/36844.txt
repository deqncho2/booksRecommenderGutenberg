Synopsis: 
In "In New England Fields and Woods" by Robinson, Rowland Evans, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey to the picturesque landscapes of New England. As she explores the enchanting fields and woods, she encounters a series of unexpected events and encounters that challenge her perception of the world around her. With each new experience, Emily's understanding of nature, love, and the human spirit deepens, leading her on a transformative path of self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Nature, Love, Self-discovery
