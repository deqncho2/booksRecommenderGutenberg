Synopsis: In 'Oliver Twist, Vol. 1 (of 3)' by Charles Dickens, we follow the journey of young Oliver, an orphan who endures a harsh and unjust upbringing in a workhouse. After escaping to London, Oliver finds himself caught up in a world of crime and corruption, encountering a colorful cast of characters along the way. As he navigates the treacherous streets, Oliver's innocence and resilience are put to the test, leading him to uncover shocking secrets about his own past.

Genres: Classic Literature, Victorian Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: Poverty and Social Injustice, Morality and Redemption, the Power of Goodness
