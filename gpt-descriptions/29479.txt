Synopsis: In the rugged and untamed landscape of early Montana, a mysterious group known as the Night Riders emerges, wreaking havoc and instilling fear among the settlers. As tensions rise and danger looms, a young rancher finds himself caught in the middle of a deadly conflict, torn between loyalty and justice. With the fate of his community hanging in the balance, he must navigate treacherous alliances, face his own demons, and uncover the truth behind the Night Riders before it's too late.

Genres: Western, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Justice, Loyalty, Survival, Identity
