Synopsis: In 'The Hermit of Mars' by Bartholomew, Stephen, a renowned astronaut embarks on a solo mission to Mars, only to mysteriously disappear without a trace. Years later, a young scientist stumbles upon a hidden message that leads her on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the astronaut's disappearance and the enigmatic hermit who resides on the red planet. As she delves deeper into the secrets of Mars, she discovers that the answers she seeks may hold the key to humanity's future.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Isolation, Discovery, Survival
