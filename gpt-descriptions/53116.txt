Synopsis: In 'Right Guard Grant' by Ralph Henry Barbour, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside Grant Adams, a talented young athlete who finds himself caught up in a web of mystery and danger. As Grant's skills on the football field attract attention, he becomes entangled in a series of unexpected events that test his courage and determination. With his future hanging in the balance, Grant must navigate through a world of secrets and deceit, where the stakes are higher than he could have ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Sports, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Determination, Deception
