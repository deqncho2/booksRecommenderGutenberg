Synopsis: In 'The Stolen Aeroplane; or, How Bud Wilson Made Good', young Bud Wilson finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a stolen aeroplane. Determined to prove himself and make a difference, Bud embarks on a daring mission to recover the aircraft and bring the culprits to justice. As he faces numerous challenges and unexpected twists, Bud's courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will he succeed in his mission and prove that he has what it takes to make a difference?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Determination, Coming of Age
