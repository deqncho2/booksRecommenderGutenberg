Synopsis: In 'Delusion; or, The Witch of New England', a young woman named Mary is accused of witchcraft in the midst of the Salem witch trials. As she fights to prove her innocence, she uncovers dark secrets and faces unimaginable challenges that test her faith and resilience. With the town consumed by fear and hysteria, Mary must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and save herself from a tragic fate.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Witchcraft, Innocence, Fear, Justice
