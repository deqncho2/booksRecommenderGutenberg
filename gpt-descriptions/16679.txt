Synopsis: In "The history of England, from the accession of Henry III. to the death of Edward III.," historian T. F. Tout takes readers on a captivating journey through a tumultuous period in English history. From political intrigue and power struggles to epic battles and royal scandals, this book delves into the fascinating lives of the monarchs and the events that shaped the nation. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Tout brings to life the complex dynamics of medieval England, offering a fresh perspective on this pivotal era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Power and politics, Royal succession, War and conflict, Social and economic changes
