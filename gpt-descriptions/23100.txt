Synopsis: In 'A Budget of Paradoxes, Volume I', Augustus De Morgan takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of mathematics, unveiling a collection of intriguing and mind-boggling paradoxes. From the perplexing paradoxes of infinity to the enigmatic puzzles of logic, De Morgan presents a series of thought-provoking anecdotes and mathematical conundrums that challenge our understanding of the universe. With wit and intellectual prowess, he explores the paradoxical nature of numbers, shapes, and concepts, leaving readers both astounded and fascinated by the mysteries of mathematics.

Genres: Non-fiction, Mathematics, Philosophy

Themes: Paradoxes, Infinity, Logic, Mathematical Puzzles, Intellectual Curiosity
