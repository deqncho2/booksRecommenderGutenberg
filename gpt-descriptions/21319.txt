Synopsis: 
In 'Three Boys; Or, The Chiefs of the Clan Mackhai' by George Manville Fenn, three young boys find themselves embroiled in a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a hidden treasure map. As they set out on a perilous journey across the rugged Scottish Highlands, they encounter treacherous landscapes, cunning adversaries, and unexpected allies. With their courage and resourcefulness tested at every turn, the boys must rely on their wits and friendship to unravel the secrets of their ancestral clan and claim their rightful inheritance.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Heritage, Treasure Hunting
