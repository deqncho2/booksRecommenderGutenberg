Synopsis: In 'The Tragedy of Ida Noble' by William Clark Russell, a young woman named Ida Noble finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deceit when she becomes entangled with a mysterious stranger. As her life takes a dark and unexpected turn, Ida must navigate through a series of shocking revelations that threaten to unravel her world. With its gripping plot and intriguing characters, this tragic tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
