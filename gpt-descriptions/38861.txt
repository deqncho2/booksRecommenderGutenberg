Synopsis: In 'My Lords of Strogue, Vol. 1 (of 3): A Chronicle of Ireland, from the Convention to the Union', author Lewis Wingfield takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period of Irish history from the Convention of 1782 to the Act of Union in 1800. Through the eyes of the powerful Strogue family, the reader is immersed in a world of political intrigue, family secrets, and forbidden love. As the Strogues navigate the treacherous landscape of Irish politics, they must confront their own demons and make choices that will shape the destiny of their nation. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling characters, this first volume sets the stage for an epic saga that will leave readers eagerly anticipating the next installment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Romance

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Heritage
