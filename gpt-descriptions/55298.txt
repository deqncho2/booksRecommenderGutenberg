Synopsis: In 'Her Husband's Purse' by Helen Reimensnyder Martin, a young woman named Mary finds herself in a peculiar situation when she discovers her husband's secret. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she uncovers a world of hidden desires, unexpected alliances, and shocking revelations. With her own life at stake, Mary must navigate a web of deceit and betrayal, questioning everything she thought she knew about love and loyalty.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Drama

Themes: Secrets and deception, Trust and betrayal
