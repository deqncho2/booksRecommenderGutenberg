Synopsis: In 'Ziska: The Problem of a Wicked Soul' by Marie Corelli, a mysterious and captivating woman named Ziska enters the lives of a group of friends, bringing with her an air of darkness and intrigue. As her presence begins to unravel their lives, secrets are revealed, passions are ignited, and the boundaries between love and obsession blur. With elements of romance, mystery, and the supernatural, this gripping tale explores the depths of the human soul and the consequences of unchecked desires.

Genres: Gothic fiction, romance, supernatural

Themes: Love and obsession, the power of secrets, the duality of human nature
