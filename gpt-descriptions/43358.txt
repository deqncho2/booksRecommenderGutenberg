Synopsis: 
In 'Modern Flirtations: A Novel' by Catherine Sinclair, follow the captivating journey of Amelia, a young woman navigating the complexities of love, desire, and societal expectations in the bustling city of London. As Amelia finds herself entangled in a web of flirtations and romantic encounters, she must confront her own desires and make choices that will shape her future. With a cast of intriguing characters and unexpected twists, this novel explores the thrilling and sometimes treacherous world of modern courtship.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, self-discovery
