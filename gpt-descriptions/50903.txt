Synopsis: 
In "Sleight of Hand: A Practical Manual of Legerdemain for Amateurs & Others," Edwin Thomas Sachs takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of magic and illusion. This comprehensive guide provides step-by-step instructions for mastering a wide range of mesmerizing tricks, from card manipulations to mind-boggling illusions. With detailed explanations and illustrations, Sachs unveils the secrets behind the art of deception, allowing readers to amaze and astonish their friends and audiences. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced magician, this enchanting manual is sure to unlock the secrets of sleight of hand and leave you spellbound.

Genres: Non-fiction, Magic, How-to

Themes: Deception, Performance, Skill acquisition
