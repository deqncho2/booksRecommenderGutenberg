Synopsis: 
In 'Come and Find Me' by Elizabeth Robins, a young woman named Rachel embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her past after receiving a mysterious letter. As she delves deeper into her investigation, Rachel discovers a web of secrets and lies that threaten to unravel her world. With each clue she uncovers, Rachel becomes more determined to find the answers she seeks, even if it means confronting the darkest corners of her own history. As the suspense builds, readers will be captivated by the twists and turns of Rachel's quest for the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Betrayal
