Synopsis: In 'Stories of the Saints by Candle-Light', Vera C. Barclay takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of various saints, shedding light on their extraordinary experiences and unwavering faith. From the humble beginnings of Saint Francis of Assisi to the courageous acts of Saint Joan of Arc, each story is beautifully woven with vivid imagery and heartfelt emotions. As the candle flickers, illuminating the pages, readers will be inspired by the saints' remarkable devotion and the profound impact they had on the world.

Genres: Biography, Religious, Historical

Themes: Faith, Devotion, Sacrifice, Inspiration
