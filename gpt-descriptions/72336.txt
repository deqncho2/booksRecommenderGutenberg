Synopsis: 
"The Romance of the Animal World: Interesting Descriptions of the Strange & Curious in Natural History" takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating and often mysterious world of animals. Edmund Selous presents a collection of intriguing anecdotes and captivating stories about the peculiar behaviors, unique adaptations, and extraordinary characteristics of various creatures. From the cunning strategies of predators to the remarkable survival instincts of prey, Selous delves into the captivating realm of the animal kingdom, offering readers a glimpse into the awe-inspiring wonders of nature.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Animal Science

Themes: Animal behavior, Adaptation, Survival, Biodiversity
