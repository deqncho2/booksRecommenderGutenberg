Synopsis: In 'Christopher and the Clockmakers' by Sara Ware Bassett, a young boy named Christopher discovers a hidden world of magic and adventure when he stumbles upon an old clock shop. As he befriends the eccentric clockmakers and learns their ancient craft, Christopher becomes entangled in a race against time to save the shop from a mysterious threat. With the help of his newfound friends and the power of imagination, Christopher embarks on a thrilling journey that will test his courage and unlock the secrets of the clocks.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Time
