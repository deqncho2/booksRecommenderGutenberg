Synopsis: In "With Perry on Lake Erie: A Tale of 1812" by James Otis, readers are transported back to the War of 1812, where they embark on a thrilling adventure alongside young Jack Somers. As Jack joins the American naval forces under the command of Commodore Oliver Hazard Perry, he finds himself in the midst of intense battles, facing the perils of war and the challenges of leadership. With danger lurking at every turn, Jack must summon courage and resilience to navigate the treacherous waters of Lake Erie and play a crucial role in the outcome of the war.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Leadership, Patriotism
