Synopsis: In 'Ahead of the Army' by William O. Stoddard, a young soldier finds himself thrust into the heart of the Civil War, serving as a scout for the Union Army. As he navigates the treacherous landscapes and encounters unexpected dangers, he must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to stay one step ahead of the enemy. With vivid descriptions and gripping action, this thrilling historical novel takes readers on a captivating journey through the chaos and uncertainty of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Survival, Loyalty, Coming of Age
