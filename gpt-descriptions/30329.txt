Synopsis: 
In the bustling city of New York, detective Jack Black Eyes finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and corruption as he investigates a series of mysterious murders. With each clue leading him deeper into the dark underbelly of the city, Black Eyes must navigate through a labyrinth of secrets and danger to uncover the truth. As the body count rises and the stakes become higher, he must confront his own demons and make a choice that could change everything.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Corruption, Redemption, Identity
