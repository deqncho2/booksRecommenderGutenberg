Synopsis: In 'Observations on the Oleum Jecoris Aselli, or Cod-liver Oil: Its nature, properties, mode of preparation, &c.', Savory delves into the intriguing world of cod-liver oil, exploring its mysterious nature, unique properties, and the intricate process of its preparation. Through meticulous observations, the author uncovers fascinating insights about this enigmatic substance, captivating readers with its hidden secrets and potential benefits. 

Genres: Medical literature, Scientific research, Health and wellness

Themes: Natural remedies, Pharmaceutical discoveries
