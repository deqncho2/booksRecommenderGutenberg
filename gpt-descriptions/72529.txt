Synopsis: 

Discover the captivating story behind Vermont's iconic maple sugar industry in this enthralling historical account by the Vermont Maple Sugar Makers Association. From the early days of Native American traditions to the modern techniques employed by today's skilled artisans, this book takes readers on a journey through time, exploring the challenges, triumphs, and enduring legacy of Vermont's maple sugar industry. Delve into the rich history, cultural significance, and economic impact of this beloved tradition, as you uncover the untold stories of the hardworking individuals who have shaped this sweet and cherished industry.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Tradition, Agriculture, Economic Impact, Cultural Heritage
