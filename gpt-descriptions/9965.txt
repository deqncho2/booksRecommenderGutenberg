Synopsis: In 'An Enemy to the King: From the Recently Discovered Memoirs of the Sieur de la Tournoire', we are transported to 17th century France, where the young and enigmatic Sieur de la Tournoire finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of political intrigue and personal vendettas. As he navigates the treacherous world of courtly life, he must make difficult choices that will not only determine his own fate but also the destiny of the kingdom. With its captivating blend of suspense, romance, and historical detail, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Power, Revenge
