Synopsis: In 'The Ridin' Kid from Powder River' by Henry Herbert Knibbs, a young cowboy named Jim Lacy finds himself caught in a web of danger and intrigue when he stumbles upon a secret that could change the course of his life forever. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters a cast of colorful characters, Jim must rely on his wit, courage, and skill with a gun to survive. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Coming of age, Survival, Loyalty
