Synopsis: In 'The Camp Fire Girls in Glorious France' by Margaret Vandercook, a group of adventurous young girls embark on a thrilling journey to France during World War I. As they navigate through the war-torn country, they encounter danger, bravery, and unexpected friendships. With their Camp Fire training and unwavering spirit, the girls find themselves playing a crucial role in the war effort, proving that even in the midst of chaos, hope and courage can prevail.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resilience, War Effort
