Synopsis: 

'American Indian Fairy Tales' by W. T. Larned is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that transport readers into the rich and mystical world of Native American folklore. From the depths of the Great Plains to the ancient forests of the Pacific Northwest, these tales are filled with magical creatures, brave heroes, and powerful spirits. Journey alongside the cunning Coyote, witness the wisdom of the wise old Owl, and discover the secrets of the sacred Medicine Wheel. Each story is a window into the vibrant tapestry of Native American culture, offering a glimpse into their beliefs, traditions, and the timeless lessons they hold.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural

Themes: Spirituality, Nature, Wisdom, Trickery, Heroism
