Synopsis: In "Adventures of the runaway rocking chair" by Howard Roger Garis, a seemingly ordinary rocking chair takes on a life of its own, embarking on a series of unexpected adventures. From a bustling city to a mysterious forest, the runaway rocking chair encounters a cast of quirky characters and finds itself in extraordinary situations. As the chair's escapades unfold, readers are taken on a whimsical journey filled with humor, suspense, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Unexpected journeys
