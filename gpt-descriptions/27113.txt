Synopsis: 

'Narrative of a Voyage to India; of a Shipwreck on board the Lady Castlereagh; and a Description of New South Wales' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the treacherous seas, a harrowing shipwreck, and the captivating landscapes of New South Wales. Written by W. B. Cramp, this gripping narrative follows the author's personal experiences as he embarks on a voyage to India, only to find himself facing the perils of a shipwreck aboard the Lady Castlereagh. From the chaos and despair of the wreck, Cramp emerges as a survivor, providing vivid descriptions of the unexplored beauty and challenges of New South Wales. 

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Memoir

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Resilience
