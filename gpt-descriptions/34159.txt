Synopsis: In 'The A B C of Drag Hunting' by Newton, Grace Clarke, a group of friends embarks on an exhilarating adventure through the English countryside, following the scent of excitement and mystery. As they navigate the challenges of drag hunting, they uncover hidden secrets and forge unbreakable bonds. With each twist and turn, the friends discover the true meaning of loyalty, courage, and the thrill of the chase.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Friendship

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Friendship, Adventure
