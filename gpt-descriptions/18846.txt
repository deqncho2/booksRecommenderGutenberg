Synopsis: On the mysterious planet of Khatka, a team of explorers led by Dane Thorson find themselves entangled in a web of ancient rituals and dark magic. As they delve deeper into the secrets of this enigmatic world, they must confront their own fears and navigate the treacherous landscape of voodoo. With danger lurking at every turn, the fate of both the planet and its inhabitants hangs in the balance.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Ancient Mysteries, Supernatural Powers
