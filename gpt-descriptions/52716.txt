Synopsis: In "The Business of Being a Housewife: A Manual to Promote Household Efficiency and Economy," Jean Prescott takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of managing a household with finesse and resourcefulness. Through practical tips, insightful anecdotes, and a touch of humor, Prescott unveils the secrets to running a home like a well-oiled machine. From organizing daily routines to budgeting wisely, this manual offers invaluable advice for anyone seeking to enhance their domestic skills and create a harmonious home environment. With a delightful blend of wit and wisdom, Prescott empowers readers to embrace the art of being a housewife and transform their homes into havens of efficiency and economy.

Genres: Self-help, Home and Garden, Lifestyle

Themes: Household management, Efficiency, Budgeting, Organization, Domestic skills
