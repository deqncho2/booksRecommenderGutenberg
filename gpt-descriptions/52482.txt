Synopsis: 

'Rules to be observed by masters and pilots of vessels, arriving at the port of Philadelphia' is a comprehensive guide established by the health law and regulations of the Board of Health in Philadelphia. This work provides essential instructions and guidelines for masters and pilots of vessels arriving at the port, ensuring the health and safety of the city's inhabitants. The annexed supplement to the health law, passed on the 2nd day of April, 1821, further enhances the regulations and addresses any additional concerns. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Health and Safety, Maritime Regulations

Themes: Public Health, Maritime Safety, Legal Compliance
