Synopsis: 
The Aeneid of Virgil is an epic poem that follows the journey of Aeneas, a Trojan hero, as he navigates through war, love, and destiny. After the fall of Troy, Aeneas embarks on a perilous voyage across the Mediterranean, facing numerous challenges and encountering both mortal and divine beings. Along the way, he must confront his own inner struggles and make difficult choices that will shape the future of his people. With its vivid imagery, captivating storytelling, and profound exploration of human emotions, The Aeneid of Virgil is a timeless masterpiece that delves into the complexities of fate, honor, and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: Epic poetry, Mythology, Historical fiction

Themes: Fate and destiny, Heroism, Love and passion, War and its consequences, Loyalty and duty
