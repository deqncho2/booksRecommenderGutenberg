Synopsis: In 'The Drunkard' by Thorne, Guy, we follow the tumultuous journey of a man named John, whose life takes a drastic turn after a fateful encounter. As he spirals into the depths of alcoholism, John's relationships, career, and sanity are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this story explores the dark and destructive consequences of addiction, while also delving into themes of redemption, self-discovery, and the power of second chances.

Genres: Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Addiction, Redemption, Self-destruction, Second chances
