Synopsis: In 'The Boy Allies with Haig in Flanders; Or, the Fighting Canadians of Vimy Ridge', author Clair W. Hayes takes readers on an exhilarating journey alongside two young American boys who find themselves in the midst of the intense battles of World War I. As they join forces with the courageous Canadian soldiers, they must navigate the treacherous trenches, face deadly enemies, and uncover a secret mission that could change the course of the war. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must summon all their bravery and resourcefulness to survive and make a difference in the face of unimaginable adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Sacrifice, Heroism
