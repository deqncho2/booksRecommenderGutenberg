Synopsis: In 'Bob Dexter and the Storm Mountain Mystery; or, The Secret of the Log Cabin' by Willard F. Baker, a young detective named Bob Dexter finds himself embroiled in a perplexing case when he stumbles upon a mysterious log cabin hidden deep in the mountains. As Bob delves deeper into the secrets of the cabin, he uncovers a web of intrigue and danger that threatens to unravel the peaceful town. With his quick wit and determination, Bob must navigate through a series of twists and turns to solve the Storm Mountain Mystery and bring justice to those involved.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Courage and perseverance, Justice and truth
