Synopsis: In 'The Knitting Book' by Riego de la Branchardière, Eléonore, a young woman with a passion for knitting, embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling streets of Paris to the picturesque countryside of France. As she delves deeper into the world of knitting, she discovers a hidden community of artisans who possess extraordinary skills and secrets. Along the way, Eléonore encounters unexpected friendships, uncovers family mysteries, and learns valuable life lessons that will forever change her perspective on love, creativity, and the power of handmade crafts.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Women's Fiction

Themes: Creativity, Community, Self-discovery, Family, Love
