Synopsis: In "The Little Lady of the Big House," Jack London takes readers on a captivating journey into the complexities of love, marriage, and desire. Set against the backdrop of a grand estate in California, the story follows the lives of Dick and Paula, a couple whose marriage is tested by their own desires and the presence of a mysterious visitor. As their relationship becomes entangled in a web of passion and longing, London explores the depths of human emotions and the consequences of pursuing forbidden desires.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Love and desire, Marriage and fidelity, Temptation and consequences
