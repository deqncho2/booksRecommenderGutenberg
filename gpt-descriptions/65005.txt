Synopsis: 
Step back in time to the year 1907 and explore the fascinating world of motor-cars and motor business wagons with "An Illustrated Directory of the Specifications of All Domestic and Foreign Motor-cars and Motor Business Wagons, Gasoline, Steam and Electric, Sold in This Country, 1907." This comprehensive directory provides a detailed look at the specifications of various motor vehicles, including gasoline, steam, and electric models, sold in the United States during that era. From elegant domestic cars to innovative foreign imports, this directory offers a glimpse into the diverse and rapidly evolving world of early 20th-century automobiles. 

Genres: 
Historical reference, Automotive guide

Themes: 
Automotive industry, Technological advancements
