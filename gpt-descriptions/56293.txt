Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Tenth, or the North Lincolnshire, Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of the regiment from 1685 to 1847. From the regiment's humble beginnings to its involvement in significant historical events, Cannon's meticulous account unveils the triumphs, challenges, and sacrifices of the brave soldiers who served in the Tenth Regiment. With vivid descriptions and compelling narratives, this historical record offers a fascinating glimpse into the regiment's role in shaping the course of history.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Military service, Regimental history, Historical events
