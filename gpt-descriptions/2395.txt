Synopsis: In "The Golden Fleece and the Heroes Who Lived Before Achilles," Padraic Colum takes readers on an epic journey through ancient Greek mythology. Follow the adventures of Jason and the Argonauts as they set sail on a perilous quest to find the legendary Golden Fleece. Along the way, they encounter mythical creatures, face treacherous challenges, and forge unbreakable bonds of friendship. This thrilling tale of heroism, bravery, and the pursuit of glory will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Mythology, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Friendship, Quest for Glory
