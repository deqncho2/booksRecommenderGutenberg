Synopsis: In 'The Gate of Remembrance: The Story of the Psychological Experiment which Resulted in the Discovery of the Edgar Chapel at Glastonbury', Frederick Bligh Bond takes readers on a captivating journey into the realm of psychological experimentation and historical discovery. Set in the mystical town of Glastonbury, the story follows Bond's groundbreaking experiment that leads to an astonishing revelation about the hidden secrets of the Edgar Chapel. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the past, Bond's quest for truth becomes intertwined with his own personal journey of self-discovery, ultimately challenging the boundaries of science, history, and spirituality.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: History and Memory, Spiritual Quest, Psychological Exploration
