Synopsis: In 'The Tangled Skein' by Baroness Orczy, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young woman finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deceit. Set against the backdrop of the French Revolution, the story follows the protagonist as she navigates through dangerous political intrigues, unexpected alliances, and a love that could jeopardize everything. As the plot thickens, the reader is drawn deeper into a world of suspense and uncertainty, where nothing is as it seems. Will the truth be unraveled, or will the tangled skein of lies remain forever entwined?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Deception, Loyalty, Love and Sacrifice
