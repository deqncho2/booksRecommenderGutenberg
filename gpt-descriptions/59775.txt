Synopsis: In 'Alf's Button' by W. A. Darlington, a young boy named Alf discovers a mysterious button that grants him a remarkable power. As he embarks on a series of thrilling adventures, Alf must navigate the consequences of his newfound ability and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets behind Alf's button.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Power and responsibility, Fate and destiny
