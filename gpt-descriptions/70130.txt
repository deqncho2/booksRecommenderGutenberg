Synopsis: 
In "To Panama and back: The record of an experience," Henry T. Byford takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the vibrant landscapes and captivating cultures of Panama. As he embarks on this adventure, Byford encounters unexpected challenges, forms unlikely friendships, and discovers the true essence of exploration. From traversing dense rainforests to navigating bustling city streets, the author's vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes transport readers to the heart of Panama's rich history and natural wonders. With each page, the anticipation builds, leaving readers eager to uncover what lies ahead on this unforgettable expedition.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Personal growth
