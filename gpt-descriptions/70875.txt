Synopsis: 
Arrowsmith follows the journey of Martin Arrowsmith, a young and ambitious medical student who dreams of making groundbreaking discoveries in the field of science. As he navigates through various challenges and temptations, Martin's unwavering dedication to his profession is put to the test. From his humble beginnings in a small Midwestern town to his encounters with influential figures in the medical world, Martin's pursuit of scientific truth becomes a thrilling and thought-provoking adventure.

Genres: 
Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Ambition, Integrity, Scientific Progress
