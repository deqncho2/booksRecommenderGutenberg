Synopsis: In 'Under Sail' by Lincoln Colcord, embark on a thrilling maritime adventure as the author recounts his experiences aboard a majestic sailing ship. From battling treacherous storms to encountering exotic cultures, this captivating memoir takes readers on a voyage filled with danger, camaraderie, and the awe-inspiring power of the open sea.

Genres: Memoir, Adventure, Maritime

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Camaraderie, Nature's Power
