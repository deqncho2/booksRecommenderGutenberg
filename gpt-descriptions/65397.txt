Synopsis: 

'Anthony Wayne, 1745-1796' is a captivating biography that delves into the life of one of America's most influential military leaders. From his humble beginnings to his rise as a prominent figure in the Revolutionary War, this book explores the triumphs and challenges faced by Anthony Wayne. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the Public Library of Fort Wayne and Allen County, along with author Randolph C. Downes, paints a vivid portrait of a man who played a crucial role in shaping the nation's history. 

Genres: Biography, History, Military

Themes: Leadership, Patriotism, American Revolution
