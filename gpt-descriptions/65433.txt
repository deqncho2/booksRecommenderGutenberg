Synopsis: In "Billy Whiskers at Home" by Frances Trego Montgomery, follow the adventures of Billy Whiskers, a mischievous and clever goat, as he finds himself in a new home filled with unexpected challenges and exciting escapades. From encounters with other animals to daring escapades, Billy Whiskers navigates his way through various obstacles, all while bringing joy and laughter to those around him. This heartwarming tale will captivate readers with its delightful characters and humorous situations, making it a perfect choice for animal lovers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Animal Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Adaptability
