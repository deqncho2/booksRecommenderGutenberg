Synopsis: 

'Studying the short-story' is a comprehensive collection that brings together sixteen timeless short-story classics, accompanied by insightful introductions, detailed notes, and a groundbreaking laboratory study method. This innovative approach allows readers to delve into each story individually, providing a unique and enriching experience for both college students and avid readers alike. From the haunting tales of Edgar Allan Poe to the thought-provoking narratives of Anton Chekhov, this anthology offers a diverse range of stories that captivate and challenge the imagination. With its meticulous analysis and interactive study method, 'Studying the short-story' is an indispensable resource for anyone seeking a deeper understanding of the art of storytelling.

Genres: Literary Fiction, Anthology, Educational

Themes: Human nature, Morality, Psychological exploration
