Synopsis: In 'Cinderella Story' by Lang, Allen Kim, a young woman named Ella finds herself trapped in a life of servitude under her cruel stepmother and stepsisters. However, when an unexpected opportunity arises, Ella's life takes an enchanting turn. With the help of a mysterious benefactor, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, love, and the pursuit of her dreams. Will Ella's fairy tale come true, or will the forces working against her keep her from finding her happily ever after?

Genres: Romance, Fantasy, Young Adult

Themes: Overcoming adversity, Self-empowerment, Love and destiny
