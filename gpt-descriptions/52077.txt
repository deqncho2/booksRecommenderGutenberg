Synopsis: In 'Corinne; or, Italy', the captivating and enigmatic Corinne, a talented poetess, captures the hearts of all who encounter her during her travels through Italy. As she navigates the complexities of love, art, and society, Corinne's passionate spirit and unconventional behavior challenge the norms of her time. Set against the backdrop of the stunning Italian landscape, this novel explores the clash between reason and emotion, the pursuit of artistic expression, and the constraints placed upon women in society.

Genres: Romantic novel, Bildungsroman, Historical fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Artistic expression, Gender roles and societal expectations
