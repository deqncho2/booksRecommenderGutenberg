Synopsis: In 'The Fate of Felix Brand' by Kelly, Florence Finch, a renowned detective, is called upon to solve the mysterious disappearance of a wealthy businessman, Felix Brand. As she delves deeper into the case, Florence uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that lead her on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of society. With each clue she uncovers, Florence becomes more entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. Will she be able to unravel the truth behind Felix Brand's fate before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
