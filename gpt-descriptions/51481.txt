Synopsis: In 'Flying Machines Today', Ennis takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of modern aviation. From sleek commercial jets to cutting-edge military aircraft, this book explores the incredible advancements and innovations that have revolutionized the way we fly. With stunning visuals and insightful narratives, Ennis delves into the intricate mechanics, engineering marvels, and the awe-inspiring capabilities of these flying machines. Whether you're an aviation enthusiast or simply curious about the wonders of flight, this book will leave you in awe of the incredible feats achieved by today's aircraft.

Genres: Non-fiction, Technology, Aviation

Themes: Advancements in technology, Engineering marvels, Human achievement
