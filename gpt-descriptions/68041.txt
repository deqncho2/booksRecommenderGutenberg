Synopsis: In 'The West Point Rivals: or, Mark Mallory's Stratagem' by Sinclair, Upton, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the intense rivalry between two cadets at West Point Military Academy. Mark Mallory, a determined and resourceful young man, devises a cunning plan to outsmart his rival and secure his place at the top. As the story unfolds, unexpected twists and turns keep the reader on the edge of their seat, wondering who will emerge victorious in this high-stakes battle of wits.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Rivalry, Ambition, Strategy, Perseverance
