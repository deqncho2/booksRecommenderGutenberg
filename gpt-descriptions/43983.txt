Synopsis: In 'Wanted: A Cook: Domestic Dialogues' by Dale, Alan, a seemingly ordinary household is turned upside down when a mysterious advertisement for a cook sets off a chain of unexpected events. As the family interviews a series of eccentric candidates, secrets are unveiled, tensions rise, and the true nature of the household is revealed. With a touch of humor and a dash of suspense, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Drama

Themes: Family dynamics, Secrets and revelations, Unpredictability
