Synopsis: 

'Encyclopedia of Needlework' by Thérèse de Dillmont is a comprehensive guide that unveils the intricate world of needlework, offering a treasure trove of techniques, patterns, and inspiration. From delicate embroidery to intricate lacework, this encyclopedia delves into the artistry and craftsmanship of needlework, providing step-by-step instructions and beautiful illustrations to ignite the reader's creativity. Whether you are a seasoned needlework enthusiast or a beginner eager to explore this timeless craft, this book is a must-have resource that will transport you into a world of beauty, skill, and endless possibilities.

Genres: Craft, Reference, Art

Themes: Creativity, Tradition, Skill
