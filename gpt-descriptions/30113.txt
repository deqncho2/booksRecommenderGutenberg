Synopsis: In 'The One-Way Trail: A story of the cattle country', author Ridgwell Cullum takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rugged and dangerous world of the American West. When a young cowboy named Jim Goad finds himself entangled in a web of treachery and revenge, he must navigate the perils of the one-way trail, where danger lurks at every turn. As Jim fights to survive and protect those he cares about, he discovers the true meaning of loyalty and the price one must pay for justice.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Loyalty, Justice, Survival
