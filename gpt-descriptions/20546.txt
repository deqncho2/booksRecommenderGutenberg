Synopsis: In 'The Hand in the Dark' by Arthur J. Rees, a renowned detective is called upon to solve a perplexing murder case that takes place in a secluded mansion. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected twists that keep the reader guessing until the very end. With a gripping narrative and a cast of intriguing characters, this thrilling mystery will keep you on the edge of your seat.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Intrigue
