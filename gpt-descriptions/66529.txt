Synopsis: In 'You Don't Walk Alone' by Robinson, Frank M., a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and danger when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter hidden in her deceased grandmother's belongings. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Emily becomes entangled in a thrilling journey that takes her across continents, testing her courage and resilience. With each step, she uncovers shocking revelations that challenge her perception of family, love, and the power of forgiveness. Will Emily be able to unravel the truth before it consumes her?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Redemption
