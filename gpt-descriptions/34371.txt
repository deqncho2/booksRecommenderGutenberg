Synopsis: 

In 'Pliocene and Pleistocene Records of Fossil Turtles from Western Kansas and Oklahoma', Edwin C. Galbreath takes readers on a fascinating journey through time, uncovering the ancient secrets of the region's prehistoric turtles. Through meticulous research and detailed analysis, Galbreath presents a comprehensive study of the fossilized remains found in Western Kansas and Oklahoma, shedding light on the evolution and diversity of these ancient reptiles. From the discovery of new species to the examination of their habitats and behaviors, this book offers a captivating exploration of the rich paleontological history of the area.

Genres: Paleontology, Natural History, Scientific Research

Themes: Evolution, Biodiversity, Prehistoric Life
