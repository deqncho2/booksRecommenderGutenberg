Synopsis: In 'The Wizard's Son, Vol. 1', Mrs. Oliphant weaves a captivating tale of mystery and magic that follows the life of a young boy named Harry. After the sudden disappearance of his father, Harry discovers a hidden world of sorcery and enchantment, where he must navigate treacherous alliances and face dangerous adversaries. As he delves deeper into his father's secret past, Harry uncovers shocking truths that will forever change his destiny. With each page, the reader is drawn into a spellbinding adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Coming of age, Family bonds, Power and responsibility
