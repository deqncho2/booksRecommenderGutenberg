Synopsis: In 'Kathie's Soldiers' by Amanda M. Douglas, Kathie, a spirited young woman, finds herself caught in the midst of the American Civil War. As she navigates the challenges and dangers of the war-torn country, she forms unexpected connections with a group of soldiers who become her loyal companions. Together, they embark on a journey of courage, sacrifice, and resilience, as they strive to protect what they hold dear amidst the chaos of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Love, Resilience, Sacrifice
