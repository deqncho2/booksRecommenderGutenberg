Synopsis: In "Gondola Days" by Francis Hopkinson Smith, the enchanting city of Venice becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of love, mystery, and adventure. As the story unfolds, a young American artist finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue, navigating the labyrinthine canals and hidden passageways of the city. With each stroke of his brush, he uncovers a world of forbidden romance, ancient legends, and a long-lost masterpiece that holds the key to a centuries-old mystery. As he delves deeper into the heart of Venice, he must confront his own desires and confront the consequences of his actions. Will he unlock the secrets of the past and find redemption, or will he be forever lost in the shadows of the gondola-filled waterways?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Art and Creativity, Secrets and Intrigue
