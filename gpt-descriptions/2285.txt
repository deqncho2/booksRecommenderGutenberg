Synopsis: In 'Ridgway of Montana: A story of to-day, in which the hero is also the villain', William MacLeod Raine weaves a captivating tale set in the rugged landscapes of Montana. The story follows the enigmatic character of Ridgway, a man with a mysterious past and a complex moral compass. As the plot unfolds, Ridgway's actions blur the line between hero and villain, leaving readers questioning their own perceptions of right and wrong. With a backdrop of treacherous mountains, wild frontier towns, and a cast of intriguing characters, this gripping novel explores the depths of human nature and the consequences of one's choices.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Morality, Identity, Redemption
