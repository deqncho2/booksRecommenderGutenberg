Synopsis: In "Murder in the Maze" by J. J. Connington, a renowned detective is called to investigate a perplexing murder that takes place within the confines of a seemingly impenetrable maze. As the detective delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected alliances, all while navigating the intricate twists and turns of the maze. With each revelation, the detective must race against time to solve the murder before the killer strikes again.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Puzzle-solving
