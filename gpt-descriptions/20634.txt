Synopsis: In "The Sleeping Bard; Or, Visions of the World, Death, and Hell," Wynne Ellis takes readers on a captivating journey through the mystical and haunting landscapes of Wales. Through a series of vivid and eerie visions, the Sleeping Bard reveals profound insights into the human condition, the nature of life and death, and the depths of the underworld. As the protagonist delves deeper into these otherworldly realms, the lines between reality and fantasy blur, leaving readers questioning the very fabric of existence.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, philosophical

Themes: Life and death, the afterlife, the human condition, the power of imagination
