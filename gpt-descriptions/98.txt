Synopsis: In the midst of the French Revolution, two men from different backgrounds find their lives intertwined in a tale of love, sacrifice, and redemption. As the chaos of the revolution unfolds, secrets are revealed, loyalties are tested, and the characters are forced to confront the darkness within themselves. Set against the backdrop of London and Paris, this gripping story explores the power of love and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Sacrifice, Redemption, Social Injustice, Resilience
