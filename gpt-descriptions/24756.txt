Synopsis: In "The Story of General Gordon" by Jean Lang, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the life of one of the most enigmatic and heroic figures in British history. From his early days as a young officer to his remarkable exploits in far-flung corners of the world, General Gordon's story is one of courage, adventure, and unwavering dedication to his principles. As the narrative unfolds, readers are immersed in a world of political intrigue, military strategy, and personal sacrifice, as General Gordon navigates the complexities of colonialism, religious conflict, and the pursuit of justice. With vivid descriptions and compelling storytelling, Lang brings to life the extraordinary tale of a man who became a legend.

Genres: Biography, History, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Duty, Colonialism, Religion, Justice
