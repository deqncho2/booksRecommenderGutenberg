Synopsis: In 'A Digit of the Moon: A Hindoo Love Story', the reader is transported to the vibrant and mystical world of India, where a young Englishman finds himself captivated by the enchanting beauty of an Indian princess. As their paths intertwine, they embark on a journey filled with forbidden love, cultural clashes, and the pursuit of destiny. Amidst the backdrop of ancient traditions and modern desires, the story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, leaving the reader yearning for more.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Cultural Fiction

Themes: Forbidden love, Cultural clashes, Fate and destiny
