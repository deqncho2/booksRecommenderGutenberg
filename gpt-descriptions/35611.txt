Synopsis: In 'The Great Return' by Arthur Machen, a mysterious event occurs that brings about a profound change in the world. As people begin to experience strange and inexplicable phenomena, a group of individuals embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind this extraordinary occurrence. Their quest takes them through a series of eerie and unsettling encounters, leading them to question the nature of reality and the boundaries of human perception. With each revelation, they are drawn deeper into a web of ancient secrets and hidden knowledge, ultimately confronting a truth that challenges everything they thought they knew.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Perception, Ancient Knowledge
