Synopsis: In "The Surrender of Santiago: An Account of the Historic Surrender of Santiago to General Shafter, July 17, 1898," Frank Norris takes readers on a gripping journey through the intense events surrounding the surrender of Santiago during the Spanish-American War. Through vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Norris brings to life the bravery, strategy, and sacrifices made by both American and Spanish forces during this pivotal moment in history. As the tension builds and the fate of Santiago hangs in the balance, readers will be captivated by the unfolding drama and the profound impact it had on the course of the war.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Military

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Strategy, Historical significance
