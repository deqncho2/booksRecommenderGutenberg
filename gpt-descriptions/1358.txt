Synopsis: 

'Enoch Arden, &c.' by Alfred Tennyson, Baron is a captivating narrative poem that follows the life of Enoch Arden, a humble sailor who embarks on a perilous journey. Set against the backdrop of a small coastal village, the story delves into the complexities of love, friendship, and the indomitable human spirit. As Enoch faces unimaginable challenges and makes heart-wrenching sacrifices, the reader is drawn into a tale of resilience and the pursuit of happiness. 

Genres: Narrative poem, Romantic literature, Tragedy

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Friendship, Resilience, Pursuit of happiness
