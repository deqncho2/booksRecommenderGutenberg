Synopsis: In 'Dealing in Futures: A Play in Three Acts' by Harold Brighouse, the lives of a group of ambitious individuals intertwine as they navigate the world of finance and the pursuit of wealth. Set against the backdrop of a bustling stock exchange, the play delves into the high-stakes world of futures trading, where fortunes can be made or lost in an instant. As the characters grapple with their own desires, secrets, and moral dilemmas, the play explores the lengths people will go to achieve success and the consequences that follow. With sharp dialogue and unexpected twists, 'Dealing in Futures' offers a captivating glimpse into the complex dynamics of ambition and greed.

Genres: Drama, Comedy

Themes: Ambition, Wealth, Morality, Consequences
