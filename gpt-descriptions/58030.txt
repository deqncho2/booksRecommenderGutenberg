Synopsis: 

In 'Warren Commission (15 of 26): Hearings Vol. XV (of 15)', the United States Warren Commission delves deep into the final set of hearings, uncovering shocking revelations and unexpected twists. As the investigation reaches its climax, the truth behind the assassination of President John F. Kennedy begins to emerge, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat. With each witness testimony and expert analysis, the Commission uncovers a web of conspiracy and deceit that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew about this historic event.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, True Crime

Themes: Conspiracy, Government cover-ups, Assassination, Truth and justice
