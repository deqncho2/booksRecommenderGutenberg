Synopsis: In "A Peep Into the Past," Sir Max Beerbohm takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the lives and personalities of historical figures in a unique and humorous way. Through his witty and insightful observations, Beerbohm offers a fresh perspective on well-known historical events and sheds light on the private lives of famous individuals. From kings and queens to artists and writers, this collection of essays provides a delightful glimpse into the past, inviting readers to see history in a whole new light.

Genres: Historical fiction, Satire, Biography

Themes: History, Human nature, Fame and celebrity
