Synopsis: In 'The Seventh Order' by Jerry Sohl, a group of scientists embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that could change the course of human evolution. As they delve deeper into their research, they uncover a shocking secret that threatens not only their project but also the very fabric of society. With their lives on the line, they must navigate a treacherous web of intrigue, betrayal, and moral dilemmas, all while racing against time to prevent a catastrophe of unimaginable proportions. This gripping sci-fi thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of scientific progress and the consequences of playing god.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Human evolution, Ethical dilemmas, Consequences of scientific advancement
