Synopsis: In "The Riflemen of the Ohio: A Story of the Early Days along 'The Beautiful River'," Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed wilderness of the Ohio River Valley during the early days of American settlement. The story follows a group of courageous frontiersmen known as the Riflemen, as they navigate the treacherous landscape, face dangerous encounters with Native American tribes, and strive to protect their families and secure their future in this new land. As tensions rise and conflicts escalate, the Riflemen must rely on their skills, resourcefulness, and unwavering determination to survive and shape the destiny of the Ohio River Valley.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Exploration and Settlement, Conflict and Survival, Loyalty and Brotherhood
