Synopsis: 
In "Rollo in London" by Abbott, Jacob, young Rollo embarks on an exciting adventure as he explores the bustling city of London. From visiting famous landmarks to encountering intriguing characters, Rollo's journey is filled with unexpected twists and turns. As he navigates the city's vibrant streets, Rollo learns valuable lessons about friendship, independence, and the importance of embracing new experiences.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Independence, Cultural immersion
