Synopsis: In "The Young Visiters or, Mr. Salteena's Plan," we are introduced to the charming and eccentric world of Mr. Alfred Salteena, a middle-aged man with dreams of becoming a gentleman. When he meets the young and beautiful Ethel Monticue, he sees an opportunity to elevate his social status. As Mr. Salteena navigates the complexities of high society, he finds himself entangled in a series of humorous and unexpected events that challenge his notions of love, friendship, and self-worth.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Romance

Themes: Social class, Love and relationships, Self-discovery
