Synopsis: In 'The Heroic Age' by H. Munro, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself thrust into extraordinary circumstances when he is unexpectedly called upon to save the day. With wit, humor, and a touch of satire, Munro takes readers on a thrilling journey through a world where heroes are made, not born. As our protagonist navigates treacherous obstacles and faces unexpected challenges, the true nature of heroism is explored in a way that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Satire, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Heroism, Unexpected bravery, Satirical critique of societal norms
