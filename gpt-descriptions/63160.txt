Synopsis: In 'Forest Glen; or, The Mohawk's Friendship' by Elijah Kellogg, a young boy named Forest embarks on a thrilling adventure in the untamed wilderness of America. As Forest encounters the Mohawk tribe, he forms an unexpected friendship that will test his courage and loyalty. With danger lurking at every turn, Forest must navigate through treacherous landscapes and face formidable challenges to protect his newfound companions. Will Forest's friendship with the Mohawk tribe withstand the trials they face together?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Cultural Understanding
