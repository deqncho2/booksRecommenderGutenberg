Synopsis: 

In "Rescuing the Czar: Two authentic diaries arranged and translated," James P. Smythe presents a gripping account of a daring rescue mission that unfolds through the pages of two remarkable diaries. Set against the backdrop of the Russian Revolution, the diaries reveal the harrowing journey of a group of individuals determined to save the last Russian emperor, Nicholas II, and his family from their impending doom. As the rescue mission unfolds, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the fate of the Romanovs hangs in the balance. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Sacrifice, Redemption, Survival
