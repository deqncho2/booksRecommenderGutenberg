Synopsis: In the bustling city of Chicago, a group of engineers uncovers a hidden world of untapped potential within the scrap industry. As they delve deeper into this unexplored territory, they find themselves entangled in a web of mystery, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each discovery, their pursuit of profits becomes a thrilling adventure that challenges their skills, ethics, and the very fabric of their lives. Will they be able to navigate this treacherous landscape and emerge victorious, or will the allure of wealth lead them astray?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Ambition, Morality
