Synopsis: 
'Quaint Gleanings from Ancient Poetry: A Collection of Curious Poetical Compositions of the XVIth, XVIIth, and XVIIIth Centuries' by Goldsmid, Edmund is a captivating anthology that takes readers on a journey through the enchanting world of poetry from the 16th to the 18th centuries. This collection brings together a diverse range of curious and lesser-known poetic compositions, offering a glimpse into the rich tapestry of literary history. From sonnets to ballads, each poem is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered, evoking emotions and painting vivid pictures of the past. Delve into the forgotten verses of the past and uncover the beauty and power of these timeless works.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology, Historical Literature
Themes: Literary history, Timelessness, Emotion, Artistic expression
