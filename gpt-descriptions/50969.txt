Synopsis: In 'Big Ancestor' by Wallace, F. L., a young archaeologist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to unlocking a forgotten civilization. As she delves deeper into her research, she uncovers a hidden world filled with ancient secrets and unexpected dangers. With time running out, she must navigate treacherous landscapes and outsmart ruthless adversaries to protect the truth from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Legacy, Power of Knowledge
