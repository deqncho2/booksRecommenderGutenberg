Synopsis: 
In 'Roast Beef, Medium: The Business Adventures of Emma McChesney', we follow the captivating journey of Emma McChesney, a smart and ambitious woman who defies societal norms to become a successful businesswoman in the early 20th century. As she navigates the male-dominated world of advertising and sales, Emma faces numerous challenges and triumphs, all while balancing her personal life and motherhood. With her wit, determination, and unconventional methods, Emma proves that she is a force to be reckoned with in the business world.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Women's Fiction

Themes: Gender equality, Ambition, Motherhood, Success, Breaking societal norms
