Synopsis: 

Joseph Pennell's Pictures of War Work in America is a captivating collection of lithographs that provide a unique glimpse into the world of munition works during wartime. With the permission and authority of the United States government, Pennell's reproductions showcase the immense scale and intricate details of these industrial sites. Accompanied by the artist's insightful notes and introduction, this collection offers a powerful visual narrative of the vital role played by these facilities in supporting the war effort. 

Genres: Art, History, War

Themes: Industrialization, Patriotism, War effort
