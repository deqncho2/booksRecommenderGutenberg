Synopsis: In 'Colville of the Guards, Volume 2 (of 3)' by Grant, James, the captivating tale continues as Captain Colville finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal vendettas. As he navigates the treacherous world of the British Guards, Colville must uncover the truth behind a mysterious assassination plot that threatens to destabilize the nation. With danger lurking at every turn, he must rely on his wit, courage, and unwavering loyalty to protect those he holds dear. Will Colville be able to outsmart his enemies and bring justice to those responsible? Find out in this thrilling second volume of the Guards series.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Intrigue, Justice
