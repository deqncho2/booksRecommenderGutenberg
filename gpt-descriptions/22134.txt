Synopsis: 

'Gathering Jewels: The Secret of a Beautiful Life: In Memoriam of Mr. & Mrs. James Knowles. Selected from Their Diaries.' takes readers on an intimate journey through the lives of Mr. and Mrs. James Knowles, as revealed through their personal diaries. This captivating collection of entries unveils the hidden secrets, joys, and struggles of a couple deeply committed to living a beautiful life. As readers delve into the pages of their diaries, they will uncover a world filled with love, passion, and the pursuit of true happiness. 

Genres: Memoir, Biography, Self-help

Themes: Love, Happiness, Personal Growth
