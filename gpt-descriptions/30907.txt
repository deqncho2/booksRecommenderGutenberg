Synopsis: In "Rosemary in Search of a Father," Rosemary, a young and spirited girl, embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her father's mysterious disappearance. With the help of her loyal friends and a series of intriguing clues, Rosemary delves into a world filled with secrets, hidden identities, and unexpected revelations. As she unravels the truth, Rosemary discovers the power of love, resilience, and the importance of family bonds.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Family, Friendship, Resilience
