Synopsis: 
'Caudebec in America: A Record of the Descendants of Jacques Caudebec 1700 to 1920' is a captivating genealogical journey that traces the lineage of the Caudebec family over two centuries. From their humble beginnings in France to their migration to America, the book delves into the lives, triumphs, and struggles of the Caudebec descendants. Through meticulous research and personal anecdotes, the author paints a vivid picture of the family's history, revealing surprising connections, hidden secrets, and the enduring legacy of Jacques Caudebec. 

Genres: Genealogy, Historical Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Family heritage, Immigration, Identity, Legacy
