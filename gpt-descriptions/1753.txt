Synopsis: In "Swan Song" by Anton Chekhov, we are introduced to a renowned actor who finds himself alone in a dressing room, reflecting on his life and career. As he prepares to give his final performance, he contemplates the nature of art, the passage of time, and the sacrifices made for success. Through his introspection, the actor reveals the complexities of the human condition and the enduring power of the stage.

Genres: Drama, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Art and its significance, Aging and mortality, Reflection on life and regrets
