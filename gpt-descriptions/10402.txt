Synopsis: In 'A Man and His Money' by Frederic Stewart Isham, a wealthy businessman finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal when he becomes the target of a cunning con artist. As the protagonist fights to protect his fortune and reputation, he must navigate a treacherous world where nothing is as it seems. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the lengths one man will go to protect what is rightfully his.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Greed, Trust
