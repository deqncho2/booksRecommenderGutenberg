Synopsis: In 'Christian's Mistake' by Dinah Maria Mulock Craik, a young man named Christian finds himself torn between duty and desire when he falls in love with a woman who is already engaged to another. As he grapples with his emotions and the consequences of his actions, Christian must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and the pursuit of happiness. Will he make the right choice or succumb to his own desires?

Genres: Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Morality and ethics
