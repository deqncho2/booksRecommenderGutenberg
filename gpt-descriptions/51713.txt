Synopsis: In 'Metamorphosis' by Charles V. De Vet, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the course of humanity. However, his experiment takes an unexpected turn, leading to a series of bizarre and unsettling events. As the scientist grapples with the consequences of his creation, he must confront the dark side of scientific progress and the ethical dilemmas it presents. With a gripping narrative and thought-provoking twists, 'Metamorphosis' explores the boundaries of science and the human condition.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Scientific progress, Ethics, Identity, Consequences
