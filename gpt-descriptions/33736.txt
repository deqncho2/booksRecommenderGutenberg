Synopsis: In 'The Tempering' by Charles Neville Buck, a young man named John finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he inherits a family estate in the English countryside. As he delves into the secrets of his ancestors, he uncovers a hidden world of ancient rituals, forbidden love, and a chilling prophecy that could change his life forever. With each revelation, John's courage and determination are tested, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family legacy, Secrets and hidden truths, Love and sacrifice, Redemption
