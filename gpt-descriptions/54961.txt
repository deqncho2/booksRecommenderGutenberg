Synopsis: In 'How a Farthing Made a Fortune; or "Honesty is the best policy"', a young orphan named Lucy finds herself in a precarious situation when she discovers a valuable farthing coin. As she navigates the challenges of her new life, Lucy's honesty and integrity are put to the test. Will this small coin lead her to a fortune or bring her nothing but trouble?

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Moral tale

Themes: Honesty, Integrity, Perseverance, Morality
