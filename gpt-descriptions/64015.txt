Synopsis: 
In 'Last Call' by Bryce Walton, a mysterious phone call sets off a chain of events that plunges a struggling writer into a world of danger and intrigue. As he delves deeper into the secrets behind the call, he becomes entangled in a web of deception, where nothing is as it seems. With his life on the line, he must uncover the truth before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Danger
