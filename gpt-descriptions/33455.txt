Synopsis: 
'To Cuba and Back' follows the thrilling journey of Dana, a young journalist, who embarks on a dangerous mission to uncover the truth behind a mysterious disappearance in Cuba. As she delves deeper into the island's secrets, she becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden romance, and life-threatening situations. With each twist and turn, Dana must navigate the treacherous landscape of Cuba, risking everything to uncover the truth and make it back home alive.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Political intrigue, Forbidden love, Survival
