Synopsis: In 'The Rocketeers Have Shaggy Ears' by Bennett, Keith, a group of young rocketeers stumble upon a mysterious secret that leads them on an exhilarating adventure through time and space. As they navigate through thrilling challenges and unexpected encounters, they must unravel the truth behind their shaggy-eared companions and the extraordinary powers they possess. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie within the rocketeers' shaggy ears.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Discovery, Courage
