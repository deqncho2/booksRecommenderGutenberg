Synopsis: In 'Andy at Yale: Or, The Great Quadrangle Mystery', Andy, a bright and curious student, finds himself entangled in a perplexing mystery that unfolds within the prestigious walls of Yale University. As he delves deeper into the enigma, Andy uncovers a web of secrets, hidden passages, and unexpected alliances, all while navigating the challenges of college life. With his wit and determination, Andy must unravel the truth behind the Great Quadrangle Mystery before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Friendship and loyalty, Coming of age
