Synopsis: 

In 'Researches on Cellulose, 1895-1900', Bevan and Cross delve into the fascinating world of cellulose, uncovering its secrets and exploring its potential applications. Through meticulous research and experimentation, they unravel the mysteries surrounding this versatile compound, shedding light on its chemical properties and structural characteristics. As they delve deeper into their investigations, they make groundbreaking discoveries that have the potential to revolutionize various industries. However, their journey is not without challenges, as they encounter unexpected obstacles and face skepticism from their peers. Will their perseverance and determination be enough to overcome these hurdles and unlock the full potential of cellulose?

Genres: Scientific research, Non-fiction

Themes: Scientific discovery, Innovation, Persistence
