Synopsis: In the small village of Hilton, a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a sense of unease and curiosity among the villagers. As the stranger becomes entangled in the lives of the villagers, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of the harvest is revealed. With its atmospheric setting and intriguing characters, 'Harvest' takes readers on a suspenseful journey that explores the dark undercurrents of a seemingly idyllic community.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Community dynamics, Nature and its power
