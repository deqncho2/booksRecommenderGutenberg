Synopsis: In "Tom Taylor at West Point: or, The Old Army Officer's Secret" by Frank V. Webster, readers are introduced to Tom Taylor, a young and ambitious cadet at the prestigious West Point Military Academy. As Tom navigates the challenges of military training, he stumbles upon a mysterious secret that has been hidden for years within the academy's walls. With his determination and resourcefulness, Tom sets out on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth, risking his own reputation and safety in the process. This captivating tale of adventure, loyalty, and courage will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Tom's quest for answers.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Discovery
