Synopsis: 
'Letters to an Unknown' by Mérimée, Prosper is a gripping epistolary novel that follows the correspondence between two strangers, revealing a mysterious and captivating story. The letters, filled with intrigue and emotion, gradually unveil a web of secrets, hidden identities, and unexpected connections. As the characters navigate love, betrayal, and the pursuit of truth, the reader is drawn into a world of suspense and uncertainty, eagerly anticipating the next revelation.

Genres: 
Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Identity, Deception, Fate
