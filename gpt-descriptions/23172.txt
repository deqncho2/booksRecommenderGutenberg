Synopsis: In the small town of Tecumseh, a horrifying and inexplicable event occurs, leaving the residents in a state of terror and confusion. As the townspeople struggle to comprehend the nature of the unseen menace, a determined journalist sets out to uncover the truth behind the damnable thing that haunts their lives. With each revelation, the boundaries between reality and the supernatural blur, leading to a chilling climax that will leave readers questioning the very fabric of existence.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Perception of reality, Fear of the unknown, Powerlessness in the face of the supernatural
