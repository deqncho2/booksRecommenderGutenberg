Synopsis: In 'Tower's Little Primer, for the youngest class in primary school' by Anna E. Tower, young readers are taken on a delightful journey through the whimsical world of learning. Join Tower, a wise and playful character, as she introduces the youngest students to the wonders of language, numbers, and the joy of discovery. Through colorful illustrations and engaging activities, Tower's Primer sparks curiosity and encourages young minds to explore the magic of education.

Genres: Children's Literature, Educational

Themes: Learning, Curiosity, Imagination
