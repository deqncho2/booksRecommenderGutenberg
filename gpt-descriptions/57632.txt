Synopsis: In 'The Legend of the Lincoln Imp' by H. J. Kesson, a mysterious and mischievous creature wreaks havoc in the historic city of Lincoln. As the imp's pranks escalate, a young girl named Lily finds herself drawn into a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth behind the imp's origins and its connection to the city's ancient legends. With danger lurking at every turn, Lily must navigate a world of magic, secrets, and unexpected friendships to save Lincoln from the imp's chaos.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Legends and folklore, Friendship, Courage
