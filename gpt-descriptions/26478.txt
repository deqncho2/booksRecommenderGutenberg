Synopsis: In 'The Wallypug in London' by G. E. Farrow, follow the whimsical adventures of the Wallypug, a peculiar creature from a faraway land, as he finds himself in the bustling city of London. From encounters with eccentric characters to unexpected challenges, the Wallypug's journey takes him on a delightful and humorous exploration of the city's wonders. Will the Wallypug be able to navigate the unfamiliar cityscape and find his way back home?

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Friendship
