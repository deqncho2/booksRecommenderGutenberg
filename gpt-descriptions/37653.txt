Synopsis: In 'Sentiment, Inc.' by Anderson, Poul, a world where emotions can be bought and sold, a young man named Peter Graham finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of corporate espionage. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of Sentiment, Inc., Peter discovers shocking secrets that could change the course of humanity forever. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit, manipulation, and forbidden love, all while questioning the true nature of emotions and the power they hold.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Dystopian

Themes: Corporate control, Ethics of emotion manipulation, Identity and self-discovery
