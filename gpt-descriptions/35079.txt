Synopsis: In 'The Rustle of Silk' by Hamilton, Cosmo, a mysterious and captivating woman named Arabella arrives in a small English village, turning the lives of its inhabitants upside down. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, Arabella's presence becomes increasingly enigmatic, leaving everyone questioning her true intentions. With a blend of suspense, romance, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Identity, Betrayal
