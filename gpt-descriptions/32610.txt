Synopsis: In 'The Long Arm' by Franz Nabl, a mysterious and enigmatic figure known as "The Long Arm" emerges, captivating the attention of a small town. As the townspeople become increasingly fascinated by this elusive character, they are drawn into a web of secrets, desires, and hidden motives. The Long Arm's presence begins to unravel the lives of the townsfolk, exposing their vulnerabilities and forcing them to confront their own inner demons. With each passing day, the tension escalates, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the true nature of human nature.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Human Nature
