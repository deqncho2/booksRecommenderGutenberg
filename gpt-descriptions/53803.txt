Synopsis: 

In 'Report of a special committee of the City Council, on the improvement of the Fire Department: and also, on the best means of obtaining an additional supply of water for fire purposes, for the city of Halifax, N.S.', the City Council of Halifax forms a special committee to address the pressing issues surrounding the improvement of the Fire Department and the need for an additional water supply for fire purposes. As the committee delves into their investigation, they uncover surprising findings and propose innovative solutions that could potentially revolutionize the city's fire safety measures. This report provides a comprehensive analysis of the challenges faced by the Fire Department and offers a glimpse into the committee's recommendations for a safer and more efficient firefighting system.

Genres: Non-fiction, Government report

Themes: Fire safety, Urban infrastructure, Public administration
