Synopsis: In 'Collecting Old Glass, English and Irish', Sir James Henry Yoxall takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of antique glassware. From the elegant Georgian pieces to the intricate Irish designs, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the history, craftsmanship, and beauty of old glass. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Yoxall invites readers to discover the hidden stories behind each unique piece, unraveling the secrets of their creation and the lives they have touched. Whether you are a seasoned collector or simply fascinated by the artistry of the past, this book will ignite your curiosity and inspire you to delve into the enchanting world of antique glass.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Antiques

Themes: History, Craftsmanship, Beauty, Collecting, Artistry
