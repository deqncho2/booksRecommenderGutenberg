Synopsis: In the small town of Maplewood, a mysterious inn appears seemingly out of nowhere, drawing in curious visitors from far and wide. The Squirrel Inn, with its peculiar charm and enigmatic owner, holds secrets that will challenge the perceptions of reality and test the limits of human imagination. As the guests delve deeper into the inn's mysteries, they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue, where nothing is as it seems and every twist and turn leads to unexpected revelations. With each passing day, the boundaries between dreams and reality blur, leaving the guests questioning their own sanity and the true nature of the Squirrel Inn.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Perception, Reality vs. Illusion, Identity, Secrets
