Synopsis: 
In 'The Life of a Foxhound' by John Mills, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the eyes of a courageous and loyal foxhound named Jasper. From his early days as a mischievous pup to his training as a skilled hunter, Jasper's life is filled with adventure, danger, and unexpected friendships. As he navigates the treacherous world of fox hunting, Jasper must confront his own instincts and question the morality of his actions. With vivid descriptions of the English countryside and heart-pounding chase scenes, this captivating tale will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Jasper's every move.

Genres: Adventure, Animal Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Loyalty, Morality, Nature vs. Nurture
