Synopsis: 

'The Detection of Forgery: A Practical Handbook for the Use of Bankers, Solicitors, Magistrates' Clerks, and All Handling Suspected Documents' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the intricate world of forgery detection. Written by Blackburn and Caddell, this handbook provides invaluable insights and techniques for professionals who deal with suspected fraudulent documents. From analyzing handwriting and signatures to examining paper and ink, the authors equip readers with the knowledge and tools necessary to uncover the truth behind suspected forgeries. With real-life examples and practical advice, this book is an essential resource for anyone involved in the field of document examination.

Genres: Non-fiction, Crime, Reference

Themes: Forgery detection, Document examination, Fraud prevention
