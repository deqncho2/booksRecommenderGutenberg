Synopsis: 

In 'Immortal Youth: A Study in the Will to Create' by Lucien Price, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the life of a brilliant artist named Adrian. As the story unfolds, we witness Adrian's relentless pursuit of artistic perfection and his unyielding desire to leave a lasting legacy. From his humble beginnings to his rise to fame, the novel delves into the depths of Adrian's creative process, exploring the sacrifices he makes and the challenges he faces along the way. With each page, readers are drawn deeper into the enigmatic world of Adrian, where passion, obsession, and the quest for immortality collide.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Psychological

Themes: Artistic ambition, Legacy, Sacrifice, Obsession
