Synopsis: 
In the enchanting city of Naples, a young and beautiful woman named Fiammetta finds herself caught in a web of love, desire, and betrayal. As she navigates the complexities of courtly life, Fiammetta becomes entangled in a passionate affair that threatens to unravel her world. Set against the backdrop of 14th-century Italy, 'La Fiammetta' is a captivating tale of love, lust, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: 
Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: 
Love and Desire, Betrayal, Social Status, Gender Roles
