Synopsis: 
In "I Will Repay" by Baroness Orczy, a mysterious and vengeful woman named Juliette Marny arrives in Paris seeking justice for her family's tragic past. As she navigates the treacherous world of the French Revolution, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With her determination and cunning, Juliette sets out to fulfill her promise of retribution, but at what cost?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Revenge, Justice, Loyalty, Sacrifice
