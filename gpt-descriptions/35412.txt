Synopsis: In "My Unknown Chum: 'Aguecheek'" by Charles Bullard Fairbanks, a chance encounter between two strangers leads to an unexpected friendship that takes them on a thrilling journey through the bustling streets of New York City. As they navigate the complexities of their own lives, they find solace and support in each other, uncovering hidden truths and discovering the power of true companionship. With twists and turns at every corner, this captivating tale explores the depths of human connection and the transformative power of friendship.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Self-discovery
