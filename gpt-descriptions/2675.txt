Synopsis: 
In 'Burlesques' by William Makepeace Thackeray, readers are taken on a delightful journey through a collection of satirical and humorous stories. From the misadventures of a bumbling knight to the absurdities of high society, Thackeray's wit and cleverness shine through in these entertaining tales. With each story, the author playfully pokes fun at various aspects of Victorian society, offering a lighthearted and entertaining critique of the times.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Fiction

Themes: Satire of Victorian society, Social commentary, Irony
