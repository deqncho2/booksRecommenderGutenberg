Synopsis: In 'The Tyranny of Weakness' by Charles Neville Buck, a chance encounter between a wealthy American businessman and a mysterious European woman sets off a chain of events that will test their strength, resilience, and the power of love. As their lives become intertwined, they must navigate through secrets, betrayal, and the complexities of their own desires. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century society, this gripping tale explores the lengths one will go to protect what they hold dear, and the sacrifices they are willing to make in the face of adversity.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Secrets and betrayal, Society and class
