Synopsis: 

'Fanny, the Flower-Girl; or, Honesty Rewarded. To Which are Added Other Tales' by Bunbury, Selina is a captivating collection of tales that will transport readers into a world of adventure, virtue, and unexpected twists. The main story revolves around Fanny, a young flower-girl with a heart full of honesty and a determination to do what is right. As Fanny navigates through various challenges and encounters, she learns valuable lessons about the power of integrity and the rewards that come with it. Alongside Fanny's tale, the book also presents other intriguing stories that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. 

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Moral Tale

Themes: Honesty, Integrity, Virtue, Rewards of Goodness
