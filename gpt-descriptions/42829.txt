Synopsis: In 'In Quest of Gold; Or, Under the Whanga Falls' by St. Johnston, Alfred, a group of adventurous young men embark on a perilous journey to the remote Whanga Falls in search of a legendary treasure. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter unexpected dangers, their determination and camaraderie are put to the ultimate test. With each step closer to their goal, they uncover secrets that challenge their beliefs and push them to their limits. Will they find the elusive gold, or will their quest lead them to something far more valuable?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance, Self-discovery
