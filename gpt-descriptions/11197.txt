Synopsis: In "Bambi" by Marjorie Benton Cooke, readers are transported to the enchanting world of the forest, where a young deer named Bambi embarks on a journey of self-discovery. Through his encounters with various woodland creatures, Bambi learns about the beauty and harshness of nature, the importance of friendship, and the fragility of life. As he navigates the challenges of growing up, Bambi's innocence and resilience are tested, ultimately shaping him into a courageous and compassionate leader. 

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Animal fiction

Themes: Nature, Friendship, Survival, Growth
