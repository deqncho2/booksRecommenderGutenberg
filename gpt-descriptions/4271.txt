Synopsis: In 'A Modern Telemachus' by Charlotte M. Yonge, a young man named Philip May lands himself in a perplexing situation when he discovers a hidden secret about his family's past. As he embarks on a journey to uncover the truth, he encounters unexpected allies and adversaries, leading him to question his own identity and purpose. With each revelation, Philip's courage and determination are put to the test, as he navigates through a web of deceit and betrayal. Will he be able to reconcile his newfound knowledge with his own sense of self? This gripping tale of self-discovery and personal growth will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Family, Betrayal, Truth
