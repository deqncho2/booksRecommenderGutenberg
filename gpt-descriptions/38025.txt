Synopsis: 
'Fables for Children, Stories for Children, Natural Science Stories, Popular Education, Decembrists, Moral Tales' is a collection of captivating and thought-provoking tales written by Leo Tolstoy. From fables that teach valuable life lessons to stories that explore the wonders of the natural world, this collection offers a diverse range of narratives that will engage readers of all ages. With themes ranging from morality and education to social justice and the power of storytelling, Tolstoy's work invites readers to reflect on the complexities of human nature and the world around them.

Genres: Fables, Children's Stories, Natural Science, Popular Education, Historical Fiction

Themes: Morality, Education, Social Justice, Human Nature, Power of Storytelling
