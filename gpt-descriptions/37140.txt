Synopsis: In 'A Proposal Under Difficulties: A Farce' by John Kendrick Bangs, a young man finds himself in a series of comical and absurd situations as he attempts to propose to the woman he loves. From mistaken identities to outrageous misunderstandings, this farcical tale takes readers on a hilarious journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will our protagonist overcome the difficulties and finally succeed in his proposal?

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Satire

Themes: Love and romance, Miscommunication, Social conventions
