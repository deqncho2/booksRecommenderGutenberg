Synopsis: 
In "Back at School with the Tucker Twins" by Speed, Nell, the Tucker twins, Max and Molly, are back for another exciting adventure as they navigate the challenges of starting a new school year. From making new friends to facing unexpected obstacles, the twins find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of mystery and intrigue. As they unravel the secrets of their new school, they discover that things are not always as they seem, and their determination to uncover the truth leads them on a thrilling journey that will test their courage and resilience.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Middle-grade Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Problem-solving, Trust
