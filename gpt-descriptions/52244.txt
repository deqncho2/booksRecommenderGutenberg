Synopsis: 

In "Mail Carrying Railways Underpaid," the Committee on Railway Mail Pay delves into a captivating investigation that uncovers a shocking truth about the compensation of mail-carrying railways. As the committee delves deeper into their research, they uncover a web of deceit, corruption, and hidden agendas that threaten to disrupt the entire postal system. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the committee must navigate a treacherous path to expose the truth and fight for justice. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Non-fiction

Themes: Corruption, Justice, Government Accountability
