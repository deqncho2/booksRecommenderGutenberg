Synopsis: In 'The Woollen Dress' by Bordeaux, Henry, a mysterious and enchanting woollen dress becomes the centerpiece of a small town's obsession. As the dress weaves its way through the lives of various characters, secrets are unveiled, desires are awakened, and lives are forever changed. This captivating tale explores the power of clothing to transform and influence individuals, while delving into the complexities of human relationships and the hidden desires that lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Transformation, Secrets, Desire, Human Relationships
