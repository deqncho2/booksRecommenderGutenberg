Synopsis: In 'Ted and the Telephone' by Sara Ware Bassett, a young boy named Ted discovers a mysterious telephone hidden in his attic. As he begins to explore its capabilities, he becomes entangled in a thrilling adventure that takes him to unexpected places and introduces him to fascinating characters. With each call he makes, Ted uncovers secrets and uncovers a hidden world that will change his life forever. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Curiosity, Discovery, Friendship, Coming of Age
