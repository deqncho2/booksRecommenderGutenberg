Synopsis: In 'The Origin of the Mound Builders' by Alfred Oscar Coffin, a group of archaeologists embarks on a thrilling expedition to unravel the mysteries surrounding the ancient Mound Builders of North America. As they delve deeper into the secrets hidden within these enigmatic structures, they uncover a web of intrigue, ancient rituals, and a lost civilization that challenges everything they thought they knew. With each discovery, the team becomes entangled in a race against time, facing danger and deception at every turn. Will they be able to unlock the truth behind the Mound Builders before it's too late?

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Archaeology, Ancient civilizations, Exploration, Secrets and mysteries
