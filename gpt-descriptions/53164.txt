Synopsis: In 'The Standard Bearer' by S. R. Crockett, a young man named Hugh Ritson finds himself caught in a web of secrets and intrigue as he becomes the standard bearer for a mysterious group. As he delves deeper into their world, he uncovers a hidden history that could change everything he thought he knew. With danger lurking at every turn, Hugh must navigate treacherous alliances and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Identity, Power
