Synopsis: In "The White Ladies of Worcester: A Romance of the Twelfth Century," Florence L. Barclay takes readers on a captivating journey back to the medieval era. Set in the enchanting city of Worcester, the story follows the lives of two remarkable women, Lady Elinor de Montfort and Lady Isabel de Clare. As their paths intertwine, secrets, forbidden love, and treacherous plots unfold, testing their loyalty and challenging their destinies. Amidst the backdrop of political turmoil and religious conflict, the White Ladies of Worcester must navigate the complexities of power, honor, and sacrifice, ultimately shaping the course of their lives and the fate of those around them.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Loyalty and Honor, Power and Politics
