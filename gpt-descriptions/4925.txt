Synopsis: In 'The Age of Fable' by Bulfinch, Thomas, embark on a captivating journey through the realms of Greek and Roman mythology. Delve into the enchanting tales of gods, goddesses, heroes, and mythical creatures, as they shape the destinies of mortals and weave intricate webs of love, betrayal, and adventure. From the epic battles of the Trojan War to the tragic love story of Orpheus and Eurydice, this timeless collection of myths and legends will transport you to a world where gods walk among men and the extraordinary becomes reality.

Genres: Mythology, Fantasy, Classics

Themes: Power of the gods, Heroism, Love and tragedy, Fate and destiny
