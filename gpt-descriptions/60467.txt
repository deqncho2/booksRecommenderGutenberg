Synopsis: In 'A Question of Identity' by Frank Riley, a renowned psychologist is faced with a perplexing case that challenges his own beliefs and forces him to question the very essence of identity. When a mysterious patient arrives at his clinic claiming to be someone he cannot possibly be, the psychologist embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth, delving into the realms of memory, perception, and the intricacies of the human mind. As he unravels the enigma, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and deception, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers questioning their own sense of self.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Memory, Perception, Deception
