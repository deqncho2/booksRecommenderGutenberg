Synopsis: In 'Taxonomic Status of Some Mice of The Peromyscus boylii Group in Eastern Mexico, With Description of a New Subspecies', Alvarez and Ticul delve into the fascinating world of mice taxonomy in Eastern Mexico. Through meticulous research and observation, they uncover the existence of a previously unknown subspecies, adding a new layer of complexity to the already diverse Peromyscus boylii group. As they navigate the intricacies of classification and identification, the authors shed light on the rich biodiversity of the region and the importance of understanding the subtle variations within species. 

Genres: Scientific research, Zoology, Taxonomy

Themes: Biodiversity, Species classification, Ecological diversity
