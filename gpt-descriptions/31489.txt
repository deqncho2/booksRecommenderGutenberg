Synopsis: In 'A Mad Love' by Charlotte M. Brame, a young woman named Maud finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she falls in love with a mysterious and enigmatic man. As their relationship deepens, Maud becomes entangled in a dangerous game of love and betrayal, where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, the boundaries between sanity and madness blur, leaving Maud questioning her own perception of reality. Will she uncover the truth before it's too late, or will her mad love consume her completely?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Love and obsession, Deception and secrets, Sanity and madness
