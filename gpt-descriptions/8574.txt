Synopsis: In "Racketty-Packetty House, as Told by Queen Crosspatch," Frances Hodgson Burnett takes readers on a whimsical journey into the secret lives of dolls. When a neglected doll named Tidy Castle is brought into the Racketty-Packetty House, the once harmonious doll community is thrown into chaos. Queen Crosspatch, the wise and no-nonsense ruler of the dolls, must navigate through jealousy, friendship, and the power of belief to restore order and happiness to their tiny world.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Resilience
