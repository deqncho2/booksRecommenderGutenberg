Synopsis: In 'Lion Ben of Elm Island' by Kellogg, Elijah, a young boy named Ben experiences a life-changing event that sets him on a thrilling adventure. As he navigates through the challenges and mysteries of Elm Island, Ben discovers hidden secrets and forms unexpected friendships. With courage and determination, he embarks on a quest that will test his strength and character, leading him to uncover the truth about his own identity.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Courage
