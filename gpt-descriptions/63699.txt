Synopsis: In "Madame Roland: A Biographical Study," Ida M. Tarbell delves into the captivating life of Madame Roland, a remarkable woman who played a pivotal role in the French Revolution. From her humble beginnings to her influential position in the political and intellectual circles of her time, Tarbell paints a vivid portrait of a woman who defied societal norms and fought passionately for her beliefs. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, Tarbell brings to life the tumultuous era of the French Revolution and the indomitable spirit of Madame Roland.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Women's empowerment, Political activism, Revolution, Intellectualism
