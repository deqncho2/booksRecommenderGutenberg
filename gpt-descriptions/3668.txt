Synopsis: 
In 'The Albert N'Yanza, Great Basin of the Nile, And Explorations of the Nile Sources', Sir Samuel White Baker takes readers on a thrilling adventure through uncharted territories in search of the source of the Nile River. Join Baker as he navigates treacherous landscapes, encounters dangerous wildlife, and faces numerous challenges in his quest to uncover one of the world's greatest geographical mysteries. This captivating account of exploration and discovery will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Baker's every step in his remarkable journey.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Perseverance, Nature
