Synopsis: 

'Quotes and Images from Memoirs of the Court of St. Cloud' takes readers on a captivating journey through the opulent and scandalous world of the French court during the reign of Napoleon Bonaparte. Through a collection of intriguing quotes and vivid images, Goldsmith, Lewis offers a tantalizing glimpse into the lives of the powerful and influential figures who resided within the court's gilded walls. From political maneuverings and secret affairs to lavish parties and extravagant fashion, this book immerses readers in a world of intrigue, ambition, and betrayal. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Memoir

Themes: Power and politics, Intrigue and scandal, Ambition and betrayal
