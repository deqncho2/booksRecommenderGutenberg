Synopsis: In 'Waste: A Tragedy, In Four Acts', the reader is transported to the grandeur of a decaying country estate, where secrets lurk behind every closed door. As the story unfolds, a web of scandal, betrayal, and political intrigue begins to unravel, revealing the dark underbelly of society. Amidst the crumbling walls and hidden desires, the characters are forced to confront their own personal demons, leading to a devastating climax that will leave readers questioning the true nature of waste.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Corruption, Morality, Power, Deception
