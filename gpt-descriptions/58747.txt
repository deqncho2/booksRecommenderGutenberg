Synopsis: 

In 'Holcomb, Fitz, and Peate: Three 19th Century American Telescope Makers', embark on a captivating journey through the lives of three remarkable individuals who revolutionized the world of astronomy. Set against the backdrop of the rapidly changing 19th century America, this enthralling tale unveils the extraordinary stories of Holcomb, Fitz, and Peate, three brilliant minds driven by their passion for the stars. As they navigate the challenges of their time, from technological advancements to personal struggles, their paths intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to groundbreaking discoveries and unforeseen consequences. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Science

Themes: Ambition, Innovation, Pursuit of Knowledge, Personal Sacrifice
