Synopsis: In 'Freaks on the Fells: Three Months' Rustication', a group of adventurous young boys embark on a thrilling journey to the rugged fells of England. As they navigate through treacherous terrain and encounter mysterious characters, they uncover a hidden secret that will test their courage and friendship. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must rely on their wits and resourcefulness to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
