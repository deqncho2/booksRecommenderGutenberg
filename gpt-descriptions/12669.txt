Synopsis: 
In 'Marriage' by Susan Ferrier, we are introduced to a charming and witty young woman named Julia, who finds herself caught in the midst of a society obsessed with wealth and status. As she navigates the treacherous waters of courtship and marriage, Julia must confront the expectations placed upon her by her family and society, while also grappling with her own desires and ambitions. With a cast of colorful characters and a healthy dose of humor, 'Marriage' takes readers on a delightful journey through the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations.

Genres: 
Romance, Comedy, Satire

Themes: 
Love and marriage, Social expectations, Gender roles
