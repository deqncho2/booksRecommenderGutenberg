Synopsis: 

In 'Canada' by Willson, Beckles, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey that takes her across the vast and diverse landscapes of Canada. As she travels from the bustling city of Toronto to the serene beauty of the Rocky Mountains, Emily encounters a cast of intriguing characters and experiences the rich tapestry of Canadian culture. Along the way, she discovers secrets about her own family history that will forever change her perspective on life. 

Genres: Fiction, Travel, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Exploration, Family, Cultural diversity
