Synopsis: In 'The Captives' by Hugh Walpole, a young woman named Teresa is drawn into a mysterious and captivating world when she becomes the governess to the children of a wealthy family. As she delves deeper into their lives, she uncovers dark secrets and hidden desires that threaten to consume them all. With its haunting atmosphere and complex characters, this gripping tale explores the boundaries of love, loyalty, and the lengths one will go to protect those they care about.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, mystery

Themes: Secrets and deception, forbidden desires, the power of obsession, the consequences of one's actions
