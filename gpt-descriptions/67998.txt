Synopsis: In 'Beam Pirate' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist discovers a revolutionary technology that could change the course of interstellar travel forever. However, when his invention falls into the wrong hands, a daring and resourceful pirate must navigate a dangerous galaxy to reclaim it. As the stakes rise and the chase intensifies, the line between hero and villain becomes blurred, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Space Opera

Themes: Technology and its consequences, Morality and ethics in a futuristic society, The pursuit of power and its corrupting influence
