Synopsis: 
'Patriotic Plays and Pageants for Young People' by Mackay, Constance D'Arcy is a collection of captivating and inspiring plays and pageants designed to instill a sense of patriotism and pride in young readers. From historical reenactments to imaginative tales, this collection offers a variety of engaging stories that celebrate the spirit of patriotism. Whether it's a play set during the American Revolution or a pageant honoring national heroes, each piece in this collection is sure to captivate young minds and ignite their love for their country.

Genres: 
Drama, Historical Fiction, Education

Themes: 
Patriotism, National Identity, History, Heroism
