Synopsis: 
In 'Art' by Bell, Clive, three friends find their long-standing friendship tested when one of them purchases a controversial piece of modern art. As they engage in a heated debate about the nature of art and its value, their differing perspectives on aesthetics and personal relationships come to the forefront, leading to unexpected revelations and a reevaluation of their own identities.

Genres: Drama, Comedy

Themes: Art appreciation, Friendship, Identity
