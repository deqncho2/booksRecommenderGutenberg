Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the rich history of Llangollen and its surrounding area in this captivating book by W.T. Simpson. Delve into the secrets and stories that have shaped this picturesque Welsh town, as you embark on a fascinating circuit of approximately seven miles. From ancient legends and mythical tales to the rise and fall of influential figures, this historical journey will transport you through the ages, unveiling the hidden treasures and untold narratives of Llangollen's past. 

Genres: History, Travel, Local Interest

Themes: Welsh history, Legends and folklore, Local heritage
