Synopsis: In "Old Love Stories Retold," Le Gallienne takes us on a captivating journey through time, reimagining classic tales of love and passion. From the enchanting romance of Romeo and Juliet to the tragic love affair of Tristan and Isolde, these timeless stories are given a fresh and modern twist. As the characters navigate the complexities of desire, betrayal, and sacrifice, Le Gallienne's masterful storytelling transports us to a world where love knows no boundaries. Prepare to be swept away by these captivating retellings that will leave you longing for more.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love, Passion, Betrayal, Sacrifice
