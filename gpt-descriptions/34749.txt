Synopsis: In 'The Girls of Central High on Track and Field: Or, The Champions of the School League' by Morrison, Gertrude W., a group of determined and talented girls from Central High School come together to form a track and field team. As they face various challenges and obstacles, they must learn to work as a team, push their limits, and overcome their own personal struggles. With their eyes set on becoming the champions of the school league, these girls embark on a thrilling journey of self-discovery, friendship, and athletic excellence.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Sports Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Teamwork, Perseverance, Friendship, Self-Discovery
