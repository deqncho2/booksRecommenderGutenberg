Synopsis: In "The Southern Soldier Boy: A Thousand Shots for the Confederacy," James Carson Elliott takes readers on a gripping journey through the Civil War from the perspective of a young Confederate soldier. As the war unfolds, the protagonist faces the harsh realities of battle, the bonds of brotherhood, and the sacrifices made for a cause he believes in. With vivid descriptions and emotional depth, this novel explores the personal struggles and triumphs of a soldier caught in the midst of a nation divided.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Identity
