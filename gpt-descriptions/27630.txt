Synopsis: In 'A Princess in Calico' by Edith Ferguson Black, a young woman named Margaret finds herself caught between two worlds when she is unexpectedly thrust into a life of royalty. As she navigates the complexities of her new role, Margaret must confront the challenges of love, duty, and the pursuit of personal happiness. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, this captivating tale explores the transformative power of self-discovery and the choices we make in the face of societal expectations.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Love and Relationships, Social Expectations
