Synopsis: In 'Turandot: The Chinese Sphinx' by Friedrich Schiller, a mysterious and captivating princess named Turandot challenges her suitors with three riddles. Failure to answer correctly results in a deadly consequence. As a brave prince falls in love with her, he embarks on a perilous journey to win her heart, facing the enigmatic and dangerous world of ancient China. This thrilling tale of love, power, and sacrifice will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Desire, Power and Control, Sacrifice and Redemption
