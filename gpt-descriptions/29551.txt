Synopsis: 
'Told by the Northmen: Stories from the Eddas and Sagas' takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich mythology and epic tales of the Norse world. Ethel Mary Wilmot-Buxton skillfully weaves together a collection of enchanting stories, transporting readers to a time of gods, heroes, and legendary battles. From the creation of the world to the exploits of mighty warriors, this book offers a glimpse into the fascinating world of Norse mythology and the heroic sagas that have captivated audiences for centuries.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Epic

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Gods and Mortals, Honor, Adventure
