Synopsis: In 'Equality' by Edward Bellamy, a young man named Julian West wakes up in the year 2000 after being in a deep sleep for over a century. As he explores this new world, he discovers a society that has achieved true equality, where wealth, power, and opportunities are shared by all. However, as Julian delves deeper into this seemingly perfect society, he begins to question the cost of such equality and the sacrifices that were made to achieve it. With thought-provoking twists and turns, 'Equality' takes readers on a captivating journey through a future that challenges our understanding of fairness and the human condition.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Utopian Fiction

Themes: Equality, Social Justice, Human Nature, Utopia vs. Dystopia
