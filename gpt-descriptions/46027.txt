Synopsis: 
In 'The Khaki Boys at Camp Sterling; Or, Training for the Big Fight in France' by Josephine Chase, three young friends find themselves at Camp Sterling, where they undergo rigorous training to prepare for the impending war in France. As they navigate the challenges of military life, they form unbreakable bonds and discover the true meaning of bravery and sacrifice. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, this captivating story follows the Khaki Boys on their journey from ordinary civilians to courageous soldiers.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Friendship, Patriotism, Coming of Age
