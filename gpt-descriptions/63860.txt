Synopsis: 
In 'Signal Red' by Guth, Henry, a seemingly ordinary train ride takes a thrilling turn when a mysterious passenger boards the train. As the journey unfolds, the protagonist finds themselves entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected twists. With each passing moment, the tension escalates, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly anticipating the next revelation.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Intrigue, Deception, Unpredictability
