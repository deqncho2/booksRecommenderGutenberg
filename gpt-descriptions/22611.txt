Synopsis: In 'The Fox and the Geese; and The Wonderful History of Henny-Penny' by Anonymous, two charming and whimsical tales unfold. In the first story, a cunning fox devises a clever plan to outsmart a group of unsuspecting geese. In the second story, a courageous hen named Henny-Penny embarks on a thrilling adventure that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. These delightful tales are filled with suspense, humor, and unexpected twists that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fables, Folklore

Themes: Deception, Bravery, Consequences
