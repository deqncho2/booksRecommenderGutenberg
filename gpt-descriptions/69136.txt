Synopsis: In the small, picturesque town of Piper's Glen, a mysterious force begins to draw people in, captivating them with its enchanting allure. As the townsfolk become increasingly entranced, a group of unlikely friends must uncover the truth behind this irresistible power before it consumes them all. With secrets lurking around every corner and a sense of impending danger, they embark on a thrilling journey that will test their courage and loyalty. Will they be able to resist the seductive pull of Piper's Glen, or will they too fall under its spell?

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Power of temptation, Friendship, Uncovering secrets
