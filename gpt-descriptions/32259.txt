Synopsis: In "Modest Remarks upon the Bishop of London's Letter Concerning the Late Earthquakes," an anonymous author challenges the views expressed by the Bishop of London regarding the cause and significance of recent earthquakes. Through a series of thought-provoking arguments and counterarguments, the author delves into the nature of these natural disasters, questioning their divine origins and exploring alternative explanations. As the debate unfolds, readers are invited to question their own beliefs and consider the implications of these seismic events. 

Genres: Essay, Religious Critique, Philosophical Inquiry

Themes: Religion and Science, Divine Intervention, Skepticism, Natural Disasters
