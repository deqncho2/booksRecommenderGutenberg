Synopsis: 
'Aurora Leigh' by Elizabeth Barrett Browning is a captivating narrative poem that follows the life of a young woman named Aurora Leigh. Raised in Italy by her aunt, Aurora is determined to pursue her passion for writing and become a successful poet. However, her journey is filled with obstacles, including societal expectations, love, and the pursuit of her own identity. As she navigates through the complexities of her personal and professional life, Aurora must confront her own beliefs and find her voice in a world that often silences women.

Genres: 
Epic Poetry, Victorian Literature, Feminist Literature

Themes: 
Identity, Artistic Expression, Gender Roles, Love and Relationships
