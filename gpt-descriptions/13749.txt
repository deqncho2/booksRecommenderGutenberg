Synopsis: In 'Around the World on a Bicycle - Volume II: From Teheran To Yokohama', Thomas Stevens continues his remarkable journey across continents, encountering breathtaking landscapes, diverse cultures, and thrilling adventures. From traversing treacherous mountain passes to navigating bustling cities, Stevens takes readers on an exhilarating ride through Persia, India, and Japan. As he faces unexpected challenges and forms unlikely friendships, Stevens's determination and resilience shine through, making this travelogue an inspiring tale of human spirit and exploration.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Diversity, Perseverance
