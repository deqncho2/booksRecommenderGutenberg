Synopsis: In 'The Venus Trap' by Evelyn E. Smith, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Helen Larch discovers a groundbreaking formula that could revolutionize the world. However, her invention attracts the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess it. As Dr. Larch finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, she must navigate a treacherous path to protect her creation and ensure its potential is used for the greater good.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific discovery, Power struggles, Ethics
