Synopsis: 

From School to Battle-field: A Story of the War Days takes readers on a gripping journey through the eyes of a young protagonist as he transitions from the familiar halls of his school to the chaotic battlefields of war. Charles King masterfully weaves a tale of courage, sacrifice, and the harsh realities faced by soldiers during this tumultuous time. As the protagonist navigates the horrors of war, readers are drawn into a world of intense emotions, unexpected friendships, and the indomitable human spirit. Will he be able to survive the trials that await him, or will the war claim yet another victim?

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Friendship, Survival
