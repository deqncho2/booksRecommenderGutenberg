Synopsis: In the small town of Old Chester, a charismatic preacher named John Ward arrives, captivating the community with his powerful sermons and magnetic personality. As he gains a devoted following, his influence begins to unravel the lives of those around him, exposing hidden secrets and testing the limits of faith and loyalty. Told through the eyes of various townspeople, this gripping novel explores the complex dynamics of a close-knit community and the profound impact one man can have on the lives of many.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Religion, Power, Morality, Community, Identity
