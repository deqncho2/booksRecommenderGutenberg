Synopsis: 

In 'Legendary Islands of the Atlantic: A Study of Medieval Geography', William Henry Babcock takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious and enchanting world of medieval legends and myths surrounding the Atlantic Ocean. Delving into ancient texts and maps, Babcock explores the tales of legendary islands that have fascinated explorers, scholars, and dreamers for centuries. From the mythical land of Atlantis to the fabled island of Avalon, this book uncovers the hidden stories and rich folklore that have shaped our understanding of the Atlantic's mythical past. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Mythology

Themes: Exploration, Mythical Lands, Medieval Geography
