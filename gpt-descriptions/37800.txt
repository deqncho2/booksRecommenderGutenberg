Synopsis: In 'Girl Scouts at Dandelion Camp' by Lillian Elizabeth Roy, a group of adventurous Girl Scouts embark on a summer camp adventure filled with mystery and unexpected challenges. As they navigate through the enchanting wilderness, they uncover secrets that test their courage, teamwork, and resilience. With each new discovery, the girls learn valuable life lessons and forge lifelong friendships, making this summer camp experience one they will never forget.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Teamwork, Self-discovery
