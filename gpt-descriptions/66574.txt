Synopsis: In 'A Matter of Ethics' by R. R. Winterbotham, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the world. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that challenges his moral compass and forces him to confront the consequences of his actions. As he grapples with the ethical dilemma, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. This gripping tale of moral ambiguity will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries of science and the choices we make.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Morality, Scientific Responsibility
