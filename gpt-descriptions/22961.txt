Synopsis:
In 'Nell, of Shorne Mills; or, One Heart's Burden' by Charles Garvice, readers are introduced to Nell, a young woman living in the quaint village of Shorne Mills. Nell's life takes an unexpected turn when she finds herself burdened with a secret that could change everything she knows. As she navigates the challenges of love, betrayal, and societal expectations, Nell must summon the strength to face her past and forge her own path towards happiness. With captivating twists and turns, this novel explores the complexities of human relationships and the power of resilience.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Secrets and redemption, Overcoming adversity
