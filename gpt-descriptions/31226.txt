Synopsis: In 'Rulers of India: Albuquerque' by H. Morse Stephens, embark on a captivating journey through the life of Afonso de Albuquerque, a Portuguese nobleman who became one of the most influential figures in the history of India. From his daring exploits on the high seas to his strategic brilliance on land, Albuquerque's relentless pursuit of power and his unwavering determination to establish Portuguese dominance in the Indian Ocean will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As he navigates treacherous political landscapes and encounters formidable adversaries, Albuquerque's complex character and his impact on the course of history will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Power and ambition, Exploration and conquest, Cultural clash, Leadership and strategy
