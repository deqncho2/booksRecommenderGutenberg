Synopsis: 
'The Fables of Aesop: Selected, Told Anew, and Their History Traced' takes readers on a captivating journey through the timeless and enchanting world of Aesop's fables. This collection presents a selection of Aesop's most beloved tales, retold in a fresh and engaging manner, while also delving into the fascinating history behind these enduring stories. From clever animals to wise moral lessons, each fable offers a glimpse into the complexities of human nature and the universal truths that have resonated throughout the ages.

Genres: 
Fiction, Fables, Classic Literature

Themes: 
Morality, Wisdom, Human Nature
