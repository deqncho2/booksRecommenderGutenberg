Synopsis: In "The Shadow of Ashlydyat" by Henry Wood, Mrs., a young woman named Lucy is drawn to the mysterious and eerie Ashlydyat House. As she becomes entangled in the secrets and dark history of the house, Lucy must navigate a web of deception, betrayal, and supernatural occurrences. With each revelation, the shadows of Ashlydyat grow deeper, leaving Lucy questioning her own sanity and the true nature of the house's haunting presence.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Madness and psychological turmoil, Supernatural forces and hauntings
