Synopsis: 
When the Bunker family visits their cousin Tom's house, they never expected to stumble upon a mysterious secret hidden within the old mansion. As the six little Bunkers embark on a thrilling adventure, they must unravel clues, solve puzzles, and outsmart unexpected foes to uncover the truth. With each twist and turn, the Bunkers find themselves closer to a shocking revelation that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Family bonds, Curiosity and exploration, Problem-solving
