Synopsis: Set in 1930s Shanghai, 'The White Countess' follows the story of a blind former diplomat who seeks solace in a glamorous nightclub, where he encounters a mysterious Russian countess. As their lives intertwine, they navigate the complexities of love, loss, and redemption in a city on the brink of chaos.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, drama

Themes: Redemption, resilience, the power of human connection
