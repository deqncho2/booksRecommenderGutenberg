Synopsis: In 'Holidays at Roselands: A Sequel to Elsie Dinsmore' by Martha Finley, readers are transported back to the enchanting world of Elsie Dinsmore. Set during the holiday season, Elsie finds herself surrounded by family and friends at her beloved Roselands estate. However, as the festivities unfold, unexpected challenges arise, testing Elsie's strength and resilience. With heartwarming moments and surprising twists, this sequel promises to captivate readers as they follow Elsie's journey through love, loss, and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Christian Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Family, Love, Faith, Forgiveness
