Synopsis: In "Janet Hardy in Hollywood" by Wheeler, Ruthe S., aspiring actress Janet Hardy finds herself thrust into the glitz and glamour of Hollywood in the 1950s. With dreams of stardom, she navigates the cutthroat world of auditions, studio politics, and the constant pressure to conform. As she encounters both allies and adversaries, Janet must learn to navigate the treacherous waters of fame while staying true to herself.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Pursuit of dreams, Identity, Conformity, Ambition
