Synopsis: In 'The 125th Regiment, Illinois Volunteer Infantry: Attention Battalion!' by Robert M. Rogers, readers are transported back to the American Civil War, where they follow the journey of a group of soldiers from Illinois as they join the Union Army. Through vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, the author brings to life the challenges, triumphs, and camaraderie experienced by these brave men on the battlefield. As the regiment faces the harsh realities of war, they must navigate through personal sacrifices, loyalty, and the ever-present specter of death. With each page, readers are immersed in the gripping narrative, witnessing the resilience and determination of these soldiers as they fight for their beliefs and for each other.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Military History

Themes: Patriotism, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Survival
