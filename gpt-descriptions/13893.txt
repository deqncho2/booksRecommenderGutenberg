Synopsis: 

'Compilation of the Messages and Papers of the Presidents: William McKinley; Messages, Proclamations, and Executive Orders Relating to the Spanish-American War' is a historical collection of documents that provides a unique insight into the events surrounding the Spanish-American War during President William McKinley's administration. This compilation includes a wide range of messages, proclamations, and executive orders that shed light on the political, military, and diplomatic aspects of the conflict. From the initial tensions to the decisive moments that shaped the outcome, readers will be captivated by the firsthand accounts and official communications that shaped this pivotal moment in American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, political documents, war history

Themes: War and diplomacy, American imperialism, presidential leadership
