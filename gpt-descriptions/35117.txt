Synopsis: 
In 'Lord Tony's Wife: An Adventure of the Scarlet Pimpernel', the enigmatic Scarlet Pimpernel returns in a thrilling tale of love, intrigue, and danger. As Lord Tony embarks on a passionate romance with a captivating woman, he soon discovers that she holds a secret that could jeopardize everything he holds dear. With the fate of their love and the safety of the Scarlet Pimpernel's mission hanging in the balance, Lord Tony must navigate a treacherous web of deception and betrayal. Will he be able to protect his wife and the cause he fights for, or will their world come crashing down around them?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty and Betrayal, Heroism and Bravery
