Synopsis: In 'The Dinner Club', a group of friends gather regularly for dinner, engaging in lively conversations and sharing their personal stories. As the evenings progress, secrets are unveiled, tensions rise, and unexpected alliances form. With each meeting, the members of the dinner club find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and deception, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Betrayal, Trust, Secrets, Friendship, Morality
