Synopsis: In 'Out of the Air' by Inez Haynes Gillmore, a young woman named Doris finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a secret society that possesses a remarkable power. As she delves deeper into this hidden world, Doris must navigate treacherous alliances, unexpected betrayals, and her own growing abilities. With her life on the line, she must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Secrets and hidden societies, Power and its consequences, Self-discovery
