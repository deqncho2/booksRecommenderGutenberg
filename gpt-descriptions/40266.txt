Synopsis: 
'The Punster's Pocket-book: or, the Art of Punning Enlarged' is a delightful collection of puns and wordplay, brought to life with charming illustrations. This witty and entertaining book will have readers chuckling and groaning in equal measure as they explore the clever and often unexpected ways in which language can be twisted and turned. From clever one-liners to elaborate pun-filled stories, this pocket-book is a treasure trove of linguistic amusement that will leave readers both impressed and amused.

Genres: Humor, Wordplay, Illustration

Themes: Language, Wit, Entertainment
