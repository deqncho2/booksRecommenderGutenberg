Synopsis: 

In 'Witchcraft and Superstitious Record in the South-Western District of Scotland', J. Maxwell Wood delves into the mysterious and chilling world of witchcraft and superstition in a specific region of Scotland. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Wood uncovers a hidden history of dark rituals, eerie legends, and the enduring belief in supernatural forces that have shaped the lives of the people in this area for centuries. From tales of witches casting spells to encounters with malevolent spirits, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a realm where reality and the supernatural intertwine.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Occult

Themes: Witchcraft, Superstition, Folklore, Scottish History
