Synopsis: 

In 'Flaming June' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, a young woman named June finds herself at a crossroads in life. Torn between societal expectations and her own desires, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. As June navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and family, she must confront the challenges that come with following her heart. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant and changing society, 'Flaming June' is a captivating tale that explores the choices we make and the consequences they bring.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Society, Personal growth
