Synopsis: In 'The Eighth Year: A Vital Problem of Married Life' by Gibbs, Philip, a seemingly perfect marriage is put to the test as a couple faces a challenging dilemma in their eighth year together. As secrets are unveiled and tensions rise, the couple must navigate through a web of emotions, trust, and self-discovery. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the complexities of married life and the choices we make when faced with difficult situations.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Marriage, Trust, Secrets, Self-discovery
