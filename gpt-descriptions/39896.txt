Synopsis: In 'The Girl Next Door' by Augusta Huiell Seaman, a young girl named Jean moves into a new neighborhood and befriends her mysterious neighbor, Joyce. As they embark on a thrilling adventure together, they uncover hidden secrets and unexpected dangers lurking in their seemingly ordinary surroundings. With each clue they uncover, Jean and Joyce find themselves drawn deeper into a web of intrigue and suspense, where nothing is as it seems. Will they be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Discovery
