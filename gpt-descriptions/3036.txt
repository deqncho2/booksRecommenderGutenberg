Synopsis: In "The Railroad Builders: A Chronicle of the Welding of the States," John Moody takes readers on a captivating journey through the remarkable history of the American railroad system. From the early days of steam locomotives to the monumental challenges faced in connecting the vast expanse of the United States, Moody's narrative is filled with fascinating anecdotes, larger-than-life characters, and the relentless pursuit of progress. As the story unfolds, readers will witness the triumphs and tragedies that shaped the nation, as well as the profound impact of the railroad on American society and the transformation of the American West. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Progress and innovation, Manifest Destiny, Industrialization, American expansion
