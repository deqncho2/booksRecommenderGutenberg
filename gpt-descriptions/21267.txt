Synopsis: 

'Our American Holidays: Lincoln's Birthday' offers a captivating collection of essays, orations, poems, fiction, and even Lincoln's own writings, providing readers with a comprehensive view of one of America's most iconic figures. Delve into the profound insights and perspectives shared by renowned authors and thinkers, as they explore the multifaceted character of Abraham Lincoln. From heartfelt tributes to thought-provoking analyses, this anthology presents a rich tapestry of Lincoln's life, achievements, and enduring legacy. Discover the man behind the legend, as this book invites readers to embark on a captivating journey through the pages of history.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Anthology

Themes: Leadership, Legacy, American History
