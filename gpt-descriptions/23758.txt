Synopsis: In 'Work and Win; Or, Noddy Newman on a Cruise' by Optic, Oliver, we follow the adventurous journey of Noddy Newman as he embarks on a thrilling cruise. Noddy, a hardworking and determined young man, finds himself facing unexpected challenges and opportunities while aboard the ship. As he navigates through various encounters and tests his skills, Noddy's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the test. With a mix of excitement, mystery, and personal growth, this captivating tale takes readers on a voyage filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Hard work, Resilience, Personal growth
