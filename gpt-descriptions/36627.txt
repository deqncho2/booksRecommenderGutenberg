Synopsis: In "How the Bible was Invented: A Lecture Delivered Before the Independent Religious Society," Mangasarian delves into the origins and development of the Bible, challenging traditional beliefs and shedding light on the historical context surrounding its creation. With thought-provoking insights and meticulous research, the author explores the complex process that led to the compilation of this influential religious text, unraveling the layers of myth and legend that have shaped its narrative. Through a captivating lecture, Mangasarian invites readers to question long-held assumptions and embark on a journey of intellectual discovery.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Religious Studies

Themes: Religious history, Biblical interpretation, Historical context, Critical thinking
