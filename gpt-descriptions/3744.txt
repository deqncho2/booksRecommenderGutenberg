Synopsis: In 'The Trial; Or, More Links of the Daisy Chain', Charlotte M. Yonge weaves a captivating tale of mystery and intrigue. The story follows a young woman named Ethel May, who finds herself entangled in a perplexing trial that threatens to unravel the secrets of her past. As the trial unfolds, Ethel must navigate a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, all while trying to uncover the truth and protect her loved ones. With each twist and turn, the reader is kept on the edge of their seat, eagerly awaiting the shocking revelations that lie ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Justice and injustice, Family secrets, Loyalty and betrayal
