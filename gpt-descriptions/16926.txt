Synopsis: In 'Skookum Chuck Fables: Bits of History, Through the Microscope', author R. D. Cumming takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the hidden stories and intriguing events that have shaped the world as we know it. From ancient civilizations to modern-day mysteries, this collection of fables offers a unique perspective on history, delving into the untold tales that often go unnoticed. With a keen eye for detail and a touch of whimsy, Cumming invites readers to see the world through a microscope, uncovering the fascinating secrets that lie beneath the surface. Prepare to be enthralled by these captivating stories that will leave you questioning everything you thought you knew about history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Hidden Histories, Perspective, Curiosity
