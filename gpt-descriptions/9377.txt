Synopsis: 

London Pride, Or, When the World Was Younger takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling streets of Victorian London. The story follows the lives of two young women, Lucy and Phoebe, as they navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. As their paths intertwine with a cast of intriguing characters, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human desires is explored. With its vivid descriptions and compelling narrative, this novel offers a glimpse into a bygone era, where passion and ambition collide in the heart of the city.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Desire, Ambition and Society, Secrets and Betrayal
