Synopsis: In 'The Red Bicycle' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young man named Jack embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind a stolen bicycle. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Jack finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the story takes on a life of its own, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Betrayal, Identity, Redemption
