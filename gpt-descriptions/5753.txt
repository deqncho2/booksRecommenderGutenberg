Synopsis: In "St. George and St. Michael" by George MacDonald, a young Englishman finds himself caught in the midst of the Jacobite Rebellion in 18th century Scotland. As he navigates the treacherous political landscape, he becomes entangled in a forbidden love affair and is forced to question his loyalties. With danger lurking at every turn, he must choose between duty and desire, and face the consequences of his decisions.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Love, Political Intrigue
