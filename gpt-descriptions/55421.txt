Synopsis: In 'Secrets of the Bosphorus' by Henry Morgenthau, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the mysterious and enchanting city of Istanbul. As an American ambassador, Morgenthau finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, espionage, and forbidden love. With the backdrop of World War I and the crumbling Ottoman Empire, he must navigate treacherous waters to uncover the secrets hidden within the ancient city. From the opulent palaces to the bustling markets, Morgenthau's quest for truth will lead him to unexpected allies and dangerous adversaries, ultimately revealing the hidden depths of the Bosphorus.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Political intrigue, Espionage, Forbidden love, Cultural exploration
