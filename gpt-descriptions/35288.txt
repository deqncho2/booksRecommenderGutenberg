Synopsis: 
In "Two Years in Oregon" by Nash Wallis, a young woman named Emma embarks on a life-changing journey to the rugged landscapes of Oregon. Leaving behind her comfortable life, she finds herself immersed in a world of untamed wilderness, unpredictable weather, and a community of intriguing characters. As Emma navigates the challenges of survival and self-discovery, she uncovers secrets that will test her resilience and force her to confront her deepest fears. With each passing season, Emma's connection to the land and its people grows stronger, leading her on a path of personal growth and unexpected love.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Connection to nature
