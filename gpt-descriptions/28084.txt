Synopsis: In "Malcolm Sage, Detective," the enigmatic and brilliant detective Malcolm Sage is called upon to solve a series of perplexing and seemingly unsolvable crimes. From a baffling murder in a locked room to a daring jewel heist, Sage's deductive skills and unconventional methods are put to the test as he unravels the truth behind each case. With a cast of intriguing characters and unexpected twists, this thrilling detective novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deduction, Crime-solving, Unconventional methods
