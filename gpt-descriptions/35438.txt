Synopsis: In 'The Three Devils: Luther's, Milton's, and Goethe's; With Other Essays', David Masson delves into the lives and works of three influential figures: Martin Luther, John Milton, and Johann Wolfgang von Goethe. Through a series of captivating essays, Masson explores the profound impact these individuals had on literature, religion, and society. From Luther's revolutionary ideas that sparked the Protestant Reformation, to Milton's epic poem that challenged traditional religious beliefs, and Goethe's exploration of human nature and the pursuit of knowledge, this book offers a fascinating journey through the minds of these literary giants. With insightful analysis and engaging storytelling, Masson invites readers to discover the complex and enduring legacies of Luther, Milton, and Goethe.

Genres: Literary criticism, Biography, History

Themes: Religion, Literature, Human nature, Knowledge
