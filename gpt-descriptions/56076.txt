Synopsis:
In 'Travels in Southern Europe and the Levant, 1810-1817: The Journal of C. R. Cockerell, R.A.', embark on a captivating journey through the vibrant landscapes of Southern Europe and the Levant. Join renowned architect C. R. Cockerell as he documents his awe-inspiring encounters with ancient ruins, breathtaking architecture, and the rich cultural tapestry of the region. From the bustling streets of Athens to the serene shores of the Mediterranean, Cockerell's journal offers a vivid and intimate glimpse into the wonders he encountered during his seven-year expedition. 

Genres:
Travelogue, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes:
Exploration, Architecture, Cultural Heritage
