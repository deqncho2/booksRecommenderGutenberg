Synopsis: In 'Snarled Identities; Or, A Desperate Tangle' by Carter, Nicholas, a web of deceit and mistaken identities unfolds as a group of strangers find themselves entangled in a dangerous game of secrets and lies. As they struggle to uncover the truth, the line between friend and foe becomes blurred, leading to unexpected alliances and shocking revelations. With each twist and turn, the characters must confront their own identities and question the trust they place in others. Will they be able to untangle the snarled web before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Trust
