Synopsis: In 'The Half-Brothers' by Elizabeth Gaskell, a family is torn apart by a tragic event that leads to a deep-seated resentment between two brothers. As they navigate their complicated relationship, secrets are unveiled, and the true nature of their bond is tested. Set against the backdrop of the English countryside, this poignant tale explores the complexities of family, love, and forgiveness.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Gothic

Themes: Family dynamics, Sibling rivalry, Secrets and betrayal, Forgiveness
