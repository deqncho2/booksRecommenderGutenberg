Synopsis: 
In 'Pariah Planet' by Murray Leinster, a group of space explorers find themselves stranded on a mysterious planet that seems to be devoid of any intelligent life. However, they soon discover that they are not alone, as they encounter a strange and hostile species that challenges their survival skills and forces them to question their own humanity. As they navigate the treacherous landscape and face unexpected dangers, the explorers must confront their own fears and prejudices in order to find a way back home.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Prejudice, Identity
