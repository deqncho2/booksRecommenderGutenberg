Synopsis: In 'Wet Magic' by E. Nesbit, two siblings, Paul and Jane, stumble upon a hidden world of enchantment when they discover a magical pool in their garden. As they embark on a thrilling adventure, they encounter talking animals, mysterious creatures, and a powerful sorceress who holds the key to their destiny. With danger lurking at every turn, Paul and Jane must navigate this fantastical realm and uncover the secrets of wet magic before it's too late.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Magic and enchantment, Sibling bonds, Courage and bravery
