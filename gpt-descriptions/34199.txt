Synopsis: 

'Peeps Into China; Or, The Missionary's Children' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and mysterious land of China. The story revolves around a group of missionary children who find themselves immersed in the rich culture and traditions of the country. As they navigate their way through various adventures and encounters, they gain a deeper understanding of the Chinese people and their way of life. From exploring ancient temples to witnessing breathtaking landscapes, the children's experiences in China are both eye-opening and heartwarming. 

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Cross-cultural understanding, Exploration, Friendship, Family, Adventure
