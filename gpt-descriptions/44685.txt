Synopsis: 
In 'Story of the Bible Animals', J. G. Wood takes readers on a captivating journey through the pages of the Scriptures, exploring the fascinating world of animals mentioned in the Old and New Testament. From the mighty lion to the humble sparrow, Wood delves into the habits, uses, and symbolism of each creature, shedding light on the passages in which they are referenced. With vivid descriptions and insightful explanations, this book offers a unique perspective on the animal kingdom as it intertwines with biblical narratives.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Religious, Natural History

Themes: 
Animals in the Bible, Symbolism, Biblical Interpretation
