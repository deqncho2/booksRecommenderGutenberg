Synopsis:
In 'Air Monster' by Edwin Green, a mysterious creature terrorizes a small town, leaving the residents in a state of fear and confusion. As the creature's attacks become more frequent and deadly, a group of unlikely heroes must come together to uncover the truth behind the monster's origins and find a way to stop it. With thrilling action, unexpected twists, and a race against time, 'Air Monster' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes:
Fear, Survival, Heroism
