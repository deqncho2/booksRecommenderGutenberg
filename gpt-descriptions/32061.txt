Synopsis: In "The Daltons; Or, Three Roads In Life," Charles James Lever takes readers on a thrilling journey through the lives of three brothers who find themselves on divergent paths. From the bustling streets of Dublin to the battlefields of Europe, the Daltons navigate love, ambition, and the pursuit of their dreams. As their individual stories unfold, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the bonds of family are put to the ultimate test. With its captivating characters and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover the fate of the Daltons.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Family Drama

Themes: Family bonds, Ambition, Love and relationships, Secrets and revelations
