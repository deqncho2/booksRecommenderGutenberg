Synopsis: In 'Samba: A Story of the Rubber Slaves of the Congo' by Herbert Strang, readers are transported to the heart of the Congo, where the gripping tale of Samba unfolds. Samba, a young Congolese boy, finds himself torn between his desire for freedom and his duty to his family. As he navigates the treacherous rubber plantations, he encounters a cast of characters who will shape his destiny. From the ruthless rubber barons to the courageous rebels fighting for justice, Samba's journey is one of resilience, hope, and the pursuit of a better future.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Oppression, Freedom, Family, Resilience
