Synopsis: In the captivating novel "Evelyn Byrd" by George Cary Eggleston, readers are transported to the enchanting world of Virginia in the 18th century. The story revolves around the spirited and independent-minded Evelyn Byrd, a young woman who defies societal expectations and embarks on a journey of self-discovery. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and personal growth, Evelyn finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue that threatens to unravel her world. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this historical tale takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the heart of colonial America.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Independence, Identity, Love and Relationships, Society and Expectations
