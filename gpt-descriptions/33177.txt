Synopsis: 

In 'Narrative of the March of Co. A, Engineers from Fort Leavenworth, Kansas, to Fort Bridger, Utah, and Return, May 6 to October 3, 1858' by Seville, William P., readers are taken on an extraordinary journey through the rugged landscapes of the American West. This gripping narrative follows the experiences of Co. A, Engineers as they embark on a challenging expedition from Fort Leavenworth, Kansas, to Fort Bridger, Utah, and back. Through vivid descriptions and firsthand accounts, the author paints a vivid picture of the hardships, triumphs, and encounters with Native American tribes that the company faces along the way. As the march unfolds, readers are immersed in the untamed beauty of the frontier and the resilience of the men who navigate it.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural encounters
