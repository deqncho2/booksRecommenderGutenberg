Synopsis: In 'The Heart of Pinocchio: New Adventures of the Celebrated Little Puppet' by Nipote, Collodi, we are transported back to the enchanting world of Pinocchio, the beloved wooden puppet. This time, Pinocchio embarks on a series of thrilling and whimsical adventures that take him to unexpected places and introduce him to a cast of colorful characters. As he navigates through these escapades, Pinocchio learns valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the true meaning of being human. Will Pinocchio's heart guide him towards becoming a real boy? Dive into this captivating tale that will leave you spellbound and eager to discover the secrets hidden within the heart of Pinocchio.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Identity, Coming of Age
