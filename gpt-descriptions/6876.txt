Synopsis: 
In 'The British Association's Visit to Montreal, 1884: Letters', Clara, Lady Rayleigh takes readers on a captivating journey through her correspondence during the British Association's visit to Montreal in 1884. Through her vivid descriptions and personal insights, she offers a unique perspective on the cultural, scientific, and social events that unfolded during this historic visit. From encounters with influential figures to the exploration of Montreal's vibrant cityscape, Lady Rayleigh's letters provide a fascinating glimpse into a pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Epistolary

Themes: Cultural exchange, Scientific progress, Social dynamics
