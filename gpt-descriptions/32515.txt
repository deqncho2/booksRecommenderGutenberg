Synopsis: 
'The Maner of the Tryumphe of Caleys and Bulleyn and The Noble Tryumphant Coronacyon of Quene Anne, Wyfe unto the Most Noble Kynge Henry VIII' takes readers on a captivating journey through the grand triumphs and coronation of Queen Anne, wife to King Henry VIII. Set against the backdrop of the historical events surrounding the capture of Caleys and Bulleyn, this work immerses readers in a world of political intrigue, royal celebrations, and the complexities of power. As the story unfolds, readers will witness the triumphs and challenges faced by Queen Anne, and the impact her reign has on the kingdom.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Biography

Themes: Power and politics, Royal celebrations and ceremonies, Gender roles and expectations
