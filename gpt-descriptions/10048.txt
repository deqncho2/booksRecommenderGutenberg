Synopsis: 
In 'Billie Bradley and Her Inheritance; Or, The Queer Homestead at Cherry Corners', Billie Bradley finds herself unexpectedly inheriting a homestead in a small town called Cherry Corners. As she explores her new surroundings, she uncovers a series of mysterious events and encounters some peculiar characters. Determined to solve the mysteries and make the homestead her own, Billie embarks on an adventure that will test her courage and resourcefulness.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Inheritance, Courage, Exploration
