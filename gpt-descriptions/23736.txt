Synopsis: In "The Dew of Their Youth" by S.R. Crockett, a young man named Alan Fairford finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change the course of his life forever. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he must navigate treacherous alliances, face unexpected adversaries, and confront his own inner demons. Set against the backdrop of a small Scottish town, this gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Coming of age, Moral dilemmas
