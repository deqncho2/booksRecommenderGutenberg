Synopsis: 
In "Modern Magic: A Practical Treatise on the Art of Conjuring," Professor Hoffmann takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of magic and illusion. From mind-boggling card tricks to astonishing stage performances, this book unveils the secrets behind some of the most mesmerizing acts ever witnessed. With detailed explanations and step-by-step instructions, Hoffmann invites readers to explore the inner workings of magic, providing a fascinating glimpse into the art of conjuring. Prepare to be amazed as you delve into the realm of Modern Magic!

Genres: Non-fiction, Entertainment, How-to

Themes: Illusion, Performance, Skill
