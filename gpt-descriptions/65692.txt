Synopsis: 

'Progress and Achievements of the Colored People' is a captivating handbook that delves into the remarkable advancements made by colored Americans throughout history. Written by Miller and Gay, this book explores the past accomplishments of colored individuals, their present-day opportunities, and offers a glimpse into the future for further developments. With an emphasis on self-improvement and the pursuit of greater success, this work serves as a guide for readers to navigate the triumphant era that lies ahead.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Self-help

Themes: Racial progress, Self-improvement, Success
