Synopsis: 
In 'Angelica' by Elisabeth Sanxay Holding, a young woman named Rachel finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she becomes entangled with the enigmatic Angelica. As Rachel delves deeper into Angelica's world, she uncovers dark secrets and must confront her own fears and desires. With its gripping suspense and unexpected twists, 'Angelica' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Power dynamics
