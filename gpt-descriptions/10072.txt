Synopsis: 

'English Housewifry: Exemplified in above Four Hundred and Fifty Receipts Giving Directions for most Parts of Cookery' by Elizabeth Moxon is a captivating culinary guide that takes readers on a journey through the art of English cooking. With over four hundred and fifty recipes, this book provides detailed instructions and insights into various aspects of cookery, offering a glimpse into the rich traditions and flavors of English cuisine. From hearty stews to delicate pastries, Moxon's collection of recipes showcases the diverse range of dishes that were popular during her time. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will discover the secrets behind creating delectable meals that were enjoyed by households across England. 

Genres: Cookbook, Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: Culinary traditions, English cuisine, Cooking techniques
