Synopsis: In 'Classic Myths', Mary Catherine Judd takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient world, exploring the timeless tales of gods, heroes, and mythical creatures. From the epic adventures of Hercules to the tragic love story of Orpheus and Eurydice, Judd brings these classic myths to life with vivid storytelling and rich imagery. Delve into the realms of Greek, Roman, and Norse mythology, and discover the profound lessons and moral dilemmas that lie within these age-old stories. With each page, readers will be transported to a world of wonder and imagination, where gods and mortals collide in extraordinary ways.

Genres: Mythology, Fantasy, Folklore

Themes: Heroism, Love and Loss, Fate and Destiny, Morality and Ethics
