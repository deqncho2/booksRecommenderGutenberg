Synopsis: In the picturesque setting of medieval Switzerland, a tale unfolds that combines political intrigue, personal sacrifice, and the indomitable spirit of freedom. 'Wilhelm Tell' follows the journey of a skilled archer who becomes an emblem of resistance against an oppressive regime. As the tension escalates, Tell finds himself torn between his duty as a father and his duty to his country, leading to a climactic showdown that will determine the fate of an entire nation.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Political Thriller

Themes: Freedom and Resistance, Patriotism, Individual vs. State, Family and Sacrifice
