Synopsis: In 'The Wanderer; or, Female Difficulties', we follow the journey of a young woman named Juliet, who finds herself in a precarious situation after the death of her guardian. With limited options and facing societal constraints, she embarks on a quest for independence and self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of 18th-century England, Juliet encounters a cast of intriguing characters, navigates complex relationships, and grapples with the challenges of being a woman in a patriarchal society. As she strives to find her place in the world, Juliet's resilience and determination are put to the test, leading to unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers captivated.

Genres: Historical fiction, coming-of-age, romance

Themes: Gender roles, societal expectations, self-discovery, independence
