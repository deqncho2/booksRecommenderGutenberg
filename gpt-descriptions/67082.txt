Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Strikes Oil; or, Uncovering More Than a Murder', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he investigates a murder that leads him to an unexpected discovery. As he delves deeper into the case, Carter uncovers a sinister plot that goes far beyond a simple homicide, involving secrets, greed, and a valuable resource. With his keen intellect and relentless determination, Nick Carter must navigate treacherous waters to bring the culprits to justice and protect those in danger.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Detective work, Greed, Deception, Justice
