Synopsis: In 'A Mock Idyl' by Percy Ross, a seemingly idyllic small town is disrupted by the arrival of a mysterious stranger. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of secrets and hidden desires, the line between reality and illusion begins to blur. With its captivating characters and unexpected twists, this gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of a seemingly perfect community.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town secrets
