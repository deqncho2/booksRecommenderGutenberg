Synopsis: In 'A Transmutation of Muddles' by Fyfe, H. B., a young artist named Oliver finds himself entangled in a mysterious world of illusions and secrets when he stumbles upon an ancient artifact. As he delves deeper into this enigmatic realm, Oliver must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, where nothing is as it seems. With the help of a peculiar group of allies, he must unravel the truth behind the artifact and confront his own inner demons before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Perception vs. Reality, Identity, Redemption
