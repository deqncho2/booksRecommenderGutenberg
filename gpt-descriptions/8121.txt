Synopsis: In the small town of Norway, a widow named Mrs. Alving is haunted by the secrets of her past. As she struggles to protect her son from the sins of his father, the ghosts of her choices begin to emerge, threatening to unravel her carefully constructed facade. With the arrival of an old family friend, the truth becomes impossible to ignore, leading to a gripping exploration of morality, societal expectations, and the consequences of one's actions.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Morality, Family, Society, Secrets
