Synopsis: In 'Europa's Fairy Book' by Joseph Jacobs, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of European folklore. From mischievous fairies to cunning witches, this collection of timeless tales will transport readers to a realm filled with magic, adventure, and unexpected twists. Discover the secrets of a hidden kingdom, witness the power of true love, and encounter extraordinary creatures that will ignite your imagination. With each story, Jacobs weaves a tapestry of wonder and mystery, leaving readers spellbound until the very last page.

Genres: Fairy tales, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Magic, Adventure, Love, Transformation
