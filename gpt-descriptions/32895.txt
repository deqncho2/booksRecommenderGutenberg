Synopsis: In 'A Veldt Vendetta' by Mitford, Bertram, a wealthy family moves into a luxurious high-tech home that seems to fulfill their every desire. However, as the children become increasingly obsessed with the virtual reality nursery, their parents begin to question the consequences of this advanced technology. As tensions rise and strange occurrences unfold, the family finds themselves caught in a chilling battle against the very creation they once cherished.

Genres: Science Fiction, Psychological Thriller, Horror

Themes: Technology's impact on human relationships, the dangers of excessive escapism, the blurred lines between reality and illusion.
