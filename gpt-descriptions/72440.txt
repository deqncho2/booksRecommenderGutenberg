Synopsis: In 'The Smart Ones' by Jack Sharkey, a group of brilliant but socially awkward scientists find themselves caught in a web of mystery and danger when their groundbreaking invention attracts the attention of powerful forces. As they navigate through unexpected twists and turns, they must rely on their intellect and unconventional skills to outsmart their adversaries and protect their creation. With a blend of suspense, humor, and unexpected alliances, this thrilling story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Intelligence, Friendship, Innovation
