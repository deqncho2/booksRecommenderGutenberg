Synopsis: 
'Portraits of the Nineties' by Raymond, E. T. is a captivating collection of short stories that delves into the lives of various individuals living in the tumultuous decade of the 1990s. From struggling artists to disillusioned corporate workers, each story offers a unique glimpse into the hopes, dreams, and challenges faced by these characters. As the narratives unfold, readers are taken on a rollercoaster ride of emotions, exploring themes of love, loss, identity, and the search for meaning in a rapidly changing world.

Genres: Fiction, Contemporary, Short Stories

Themes: Love, Loss, Identity, Change, Society
