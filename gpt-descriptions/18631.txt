Synopsis: In 'The Lady of Fort St. John' by Mary Hartwell Catherwood, a young woman named Marguerite finds herself in the midst of a mysterious and dangerous adventure when she travels to the remote Fort St. John in the Canadian wilderness. As she navigates the treacherous landscape and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Marguerite becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden motives. With her courage and determination, she must unravel the truth and confront the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Identity, Deception
