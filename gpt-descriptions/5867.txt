Synopsis: In 'Rataplan, a Rogue Elephant; and Other Stories' by Velvin, Ellen, embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales. From the misadventures of a mischievous elephant named Rataplan to the mysterious encounters of a young girl with magical creatures, these stories will transport readers to a world filled with wonder, excitement, and unexpected twists. Each story is a delightful blend of imagination, humor, and heart, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover what awaits next.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Courage, Discovery
