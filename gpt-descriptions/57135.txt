Synopsis: 
In 'Guide to Historic Plymouth: Localities and Objects of Interest', author A. S. Burbank takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of Plymouth. From the iconic landmarks to hidden gems, this guide unveils the secrets and stories that have shaped this historic town. With vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, Burbank paints a vivid picture of Plymouth's past, transporting readers back in time to witness the triumphs and struggles of its inhabitants. Whether exploring the cobblestone streets or delving into the mysteries of Plymouth's oldest buildings, this guide is a must-read for history enthusiasts and curious travelers alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: History, Heritage, Exploration
