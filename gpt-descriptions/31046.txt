Synopsis: In 'A New Pocket Mouse (Genus Perognathus) from Kansas', E. Lendell Cockrum takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of a newly discovered species of pocket mouse. Set in the heartland of Kansas, this scientific exploration unveils the fascinating characteristics and behaviors of this elusive creature, shedding light on its unique adaptations and ecological significance. As the narrative unfolds, readers are immersed in the thrill of discovery and the wonders of the natural world, as Cockrum's meticulous research and vivid descriptions bring this tiny creature to life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Science

Themes: Biodiversity, Species Discovery, Ecological Adaptations
