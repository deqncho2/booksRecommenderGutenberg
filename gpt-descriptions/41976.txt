Synopsis: In 'Mpuke, Our Little African Cousin', we follow the adventures of Mpuke, a young African boy living in a village in the heart of Africa. From his encounters with wild animals to his experiences with tribal customs and traditions, Mpuke's journey is filled with excitement, discovery, and valuable life lessons. As he navigates the challenges of his unique environment, Mpuke's resilience and curiosity shine through, offering readers a captivating glimpse into African culture and the beauty of the natural world.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Cultural Fiction

Themes: African culture, Wildlife, Exploration, Resilience, Friendship
