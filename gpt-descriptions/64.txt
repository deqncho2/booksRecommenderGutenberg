Synopsis: In 'The Gods of Mars' by Edgar Rice Burroughs, the thrilling sequel to 'A Princess of Mars,' John Carter finds himself transported back to the mysterious and dangerous planet of Barsoom. As he embarks on a perilous journey to rescue his beloved Dejah Thoris, Carter encounters treacherous enemies, uncovers ancient secrets, and discovers a hidden civilization ruled by powerful and vengeful gods. With his strength and courage tested to the limit, Carter must navigate through a world filled with deadly creatures and deadly politics, all while fighting for his own survival and the fate of Barsoom itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Heroism, Love and Sacrifice, Exploration of Other Worlds, Power and Corruption
