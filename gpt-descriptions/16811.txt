Synopsis: In 'Gurre Kamilaroi: or, Kamilaroi Sayings', William Ridley takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich cultural heritage of the Kamilaroi people, an Indigenous Australian group. Through a collection of sayings, proverbs, and anecdotes, Ridley offers a unique glimpse into the wisdom, traditions, and beliefs of this ancient civilization. From tales of creation to insights on human nature, this book immerses readers in the vibrant world of the Kamilaroi, providing a profound understanding of their way of life.

Genres: Cultural anthropology, Indigenous studies, Ethnography

Themes: Indigenous culture, Oral tradition, Wisdom and knowledge, Cultural preservation
