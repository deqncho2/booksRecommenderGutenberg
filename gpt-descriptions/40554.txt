Synopsis: 
'Journal of a Horticultural Tour through Germany, Belgium, and part of France, in the Autumn of 1835: To which is added, a Catalogue of the different Species of Cacteæ in the Gardens at Woburn Abbey' takes readers on a captivating journey through the stunning landscapes of Germany, Belgium, and France. Written by James Forbes, this travelogue provides a vivid account of his horticultural exploration, offering a unique perspective on the botanical wonders he encounters. Alongside his observations, Forbes includes a comprehensive catalogue of the various species of Cacteæ found in the renowned gardens of Woburn Abbey. 

Genres: Travelogue, Horticulture, Botany
Themes: Exploration, Nature, Horticultural Diversity
