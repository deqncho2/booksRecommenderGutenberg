Synopsis: 

In "Speeches and Addresses of H. R. H. the Prince of Wales: 1863-1888," we are granted a captivating glimpse into the eloquence and charisma of Edward VII, the future King of Great Britain. This collection brings together a selection of his most memorable speeches and addresses, spanning a period of twenty-five years. From grand ceremonial occasions to intimate gatherings, the Prince of Wales' words resonate with passion, wisdom, and a deep understanding of the issues of his time. As we delve into this remarkable anthology, we witness the evolution of a young prince into a statesman, and gain insight into the challenges and triumphs that shaped his reign.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Speeches

Themes: Leadership, Diplomacy, Historical events, Social issues
