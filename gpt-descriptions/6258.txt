Synopsis: In the third volume of the captivating Donovan Pasha series, the enigmatic British officer finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal dilemmas in the vibrant land of Egypt. As Donovan navigates the complexities of Egyptian society, he encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets and motivations. From the opulent palaces of the ruling elite to the bustling streets of Cairo, Donovan's journey takes him on a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will he be able to unravel the mysteries that surround him and find his own path amidst the chaos of Egypt?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Colonialism, Identity, Power dynamics
