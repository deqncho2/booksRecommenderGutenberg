Synopsis: In 'The Phantoms of the Foot-Bridge, and Other Stories', Charles Egbert Craddock takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of mysterious and haunting tales. From the eerie encounters on a foot-bridge to the secrets hidden within a forgotten graveyard, each story delves into the realm of the supernatural, leaving readers on the edge of their seats. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, Craddock weaves a web of suspense and intrigue that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Suspense

Themes: Ghostly encounters, Secrets and hidden truths, The power of the unknown
