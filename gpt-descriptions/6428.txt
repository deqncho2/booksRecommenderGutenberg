Synopsis: 
In "The Surgeon's Daughter" by Walter Scott, a young surgeon finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is sent on a secret mission to India. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, he becomes embroiled in a plot that could have far-reaching consequences. With twists and turns at every corner, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Betrayal
