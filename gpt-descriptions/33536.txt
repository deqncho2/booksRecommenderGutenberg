Synopsis: In "A Report of Major Hart's Case, of Rice-Frauds, near Seringapatam," the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the intricate world of rice-frauds in colonial India. Major Hart finds himself embroiled in a web of deceit and corruption as he uncovers a shocking conspiracy that threatens to destabilize the region. With danger lurking at every turn, Major Hart must navigate treacherous waters to expose the truth and bring the perpetrators to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Crime

Themes: Corruption, Deception, Justice
