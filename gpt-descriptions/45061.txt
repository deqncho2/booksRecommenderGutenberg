Synopsis: In 'The Call of the East: A Romance of Far Formosa', the reader is transported to the exotic and mysterious island of Formosa (modern-day Taiwan) in the early 20th century. The story follows the adventures of a young British doctor, Dr. Alan Sinclair, who finds himself irresistibly drawn to the allure of the East. As he navigates the complex cultural and political landscape of Formosa, he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and danger. With vivid descriptions of the island's breathtaking landscapes, vibrant traditions, and the clash between Western and Eastern values, this captivating romance takes readers on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and forbidden desires.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Cultural exploration, Forbidden love, Identity, Colonialism
