Synopsis: In "Finding the Lost Treasure" by Helen M. Persons, a group of adventurous friends stumble upon an ancient map that leads them on a thrilling quest for hidden riches. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter unexpected obstacles, they must rely on their wit, courage, and teamwork to unravel the mysteries of the past and uncover the long-lost treasure. With each step closer to their goal, the stakes rise higher, and the friends find themselves facing not only external challenges but also their own inner demons. Will they succeed in their quest and find the treasure that has eluded others for centuries?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Perseverance, Self-discovery
