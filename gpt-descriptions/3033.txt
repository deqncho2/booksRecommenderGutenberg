Synopsis: 
"The Passing of the Frontier: A Chronicle of the Old West" takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed landscapes and tumultuous times of the American frontier. From the thrilling tales of cowboys and outlaws to the struggles of Native American tribes, this chronicle paints a vivid picture of a bygone era. As the frontier gradually gives way to civilization, the characters must confront the challenges of progress and the loss of their way of life. With its rich historical detail and compelling narratives, this book offers a captivating exploration of the Old West.

Genres: Historical fiction, Western

Themes: Exploration and settlement of the American West, clash of cultures, the fading of the frontier
