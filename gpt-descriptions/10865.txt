Synopsis: In 'Play-Making: A Manual of Craftsmanship', Archer, William takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of playwriting. Through insightful analysis and practical advice, Archer delves into the art of crafting compelling and thought-provoking plays. From the intricacies of character development to the nuances of dialogue and structure, this manual serves as an indispensable guide for aspiring playwrights and theater enthusiasts alike. With examples from renowned playwrights and a wealth of knowledge, 'Play-Making' offers a fascinating exploration of the creative process behind the magic of theater.

Genres: Non-fiction, Writing, Theater

Themes: Playwriting, Craftsmanship, Creativity, Character Development
