Synopsis: 
In 'On Love' by Stendhal, we follow the journey of a young man named Julien Sorel, who is torn between his ambitions and his desires. Set in 19th-century France, this captivating novel explores the complexities of love, power, and social class. As Julien navigates through a web of forbidden relationships and societal expectations, he must confront the consequences of his choices and the true nature of his own heart. With its rich character development and intricate plot, 'On Love' is a compelling tale that delves into the depths of human emotions and the complexities of romantic relationships.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Love and desire, Ambition and social climbing, Forbidden relationships, Societal expectations
