Synopsis: 
In 'Passage to Planet X' by Hasse, Henry, a group of intrepid explorers embarks on a perilous journey to a mysterious planet located in a distant galaxy. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter strange creatures, they must confront their deepest fears and unravel the enigmatic secrets of Planet X. With each step closer to their destination, the stakes rise higher, and the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred. Will they survive the challenges that await them on this extraordinary voyage?

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Identity, Perception
