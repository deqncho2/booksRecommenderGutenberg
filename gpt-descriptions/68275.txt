Synopsis: In 'The War of the Carolinas' by Nicholson Meredith, a small coastal town becomes the unexpected battleground for a fierce conflict that threatens to tear apart families and test the limits of loyalty. As tensions rise and secrets are unveiled, the residents find themselves caught in a web of betrayal, love, and sacrifice. With each passing day, the war intensifies, leaving the characters to question their own beliefs and values. Will they be able to find peace amidst the chaos, or will the war consume them all?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Conflict, Loyalty, Betrayal, Love, Sacrifice
