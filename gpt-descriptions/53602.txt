Synopsis: In 'A Taxicab Tangle; or, The Mission of the Motor Boys: Brave and Bold Weekly No. 362', the Motor Boys find themselves caught up in a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a mysterious taxicab with a hidden secret. As they delve deeper into the tangled web of intrigue, danger lurks around every corner, testing their courage and resourcefulness. With their quick thinking and unwavering determination, the Motor Boys must navigate through a series of unexpected twists and turns to uncover the truth and save the day.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Courage, Resourcefulness, Intrigue
