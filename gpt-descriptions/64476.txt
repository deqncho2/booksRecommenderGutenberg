Synopsis: 

'Panama and the Canal in Picture and Prose' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant landscapes and rich history of Panama, while also delving into the awe-inspiring engineering marvel that is the Panama Canal. Through a combination of vivid imagery and engaging storytelling, author Willis J. Abbot paints a vivid picture of this remarkable region, from its lush rainforests and exotic wildlife to the bustling cities and bustling waterways. As readers explore the pages of this book, they will be transported to a world of adventure, discovery, and the incredible human ingenuity that made the Panama Canal possible.

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Engineering, Cultural Heritage
