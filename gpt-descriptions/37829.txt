Synopsis: In 'Little Man's family = Diné yázhí ba'áłchíní : pre-primer', follow the heartwarming journey of Little Man as he introduces readers to his loving Diné (Navajo) family. Through vibrant illustrations and simple text, this pre-primer explores the daily activities, traditions, and values that make Little Man's family unique. From storytelling around the fire to harvesting corn, readers will be captivated by the rich cultural heritage and strong bond that Little Man shares with his family.

Genres: Children's Literature, Cultural Diversity, Family

Themes: Cultural Identity, Family Traditions, Love and Togetherness
