Synopsis: 
'Yuletide entertainments: Christmas recitations, monologues, drills, tableaux, motion songs, exercises, dialogues and plays' by Ellen M. Willard is a delightful collection of festive performances that will bring joy and merriment to any holiday gathering. From heartwarming recitations to lively dialogues and captivating plays, this book offers a wide range of entertainment options for the Christmas season. Whether you're looking to entertain family and friends or organize a community event, these carefully curated pieces are sure to enchant and engage audiences of all ages. Get ready to immerse yourself in the spirit of Christmas with this charming compilation of Yuletide entertainments.

Genres: Drama, Poetry, Performance

Themes: Christmas, Festivity, Community, Joy
