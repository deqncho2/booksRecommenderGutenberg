Synopsis: 
In 'In Queer Street' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious murder takes place in the heart of London, sending shockwaves through the city's upper-class society. As Detective Jack Tarling delves into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden desires that threaten to unravel the very fabric of Victorian society. With each twist and turn, Tarling finds himself drawn deeper into a world where appearances can be deceiving and where the truth may be more dangerous than he ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Social Class, Sexuality
