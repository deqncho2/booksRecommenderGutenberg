Synopsis: In 'The Star of Satan' by Hasse, Henry, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient artifact rumored to possess unimaginable power. As he delves deeper into the mysteries surrounding the artifact, he becomes entangled in a web of deception, betrayal, and supernatural forces that threaten to consume him. With time running out, Henry must navigate treacherous paths and face his darkest fears to prevent the artifact from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Power and its consequences, Betrayal, Redemption
