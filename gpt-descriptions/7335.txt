Synopsis: In 'Jack Harkaway and His Son's Escape from the Brigands of Greece', readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as Jack Harkaway and his son find themselves entangled in the treacherous world of Greek brigands. With danger lurking at every turn, the father-son duo must rely on their wits, courage, and resourcefulness to outsmart their captors and secure their freedom. As they navigate through the rugged landscapes of Greece, facing perilous challenges and unexpected allies, the Harkaways' resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will they manage to escape the clutches of the brigands and make it back home unscathed?

Genres: Adventure, Action, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Survival, Family bonds
