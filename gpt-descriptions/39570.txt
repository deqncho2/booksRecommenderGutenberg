Synopsis: 
Polly's First Year at Boarding School follows the journey of a young girl named Polly as she navigates the challenges and adventures of her first year at a prestigious boarding school. From making new friends and facing homesickness to discovering hidden secrets and unraveling mysteries, Polly's experiences at the school are filled with excitement, laughter, and unexpected twists. As she grows and learns, Polly discovers the true meaning of friendship, the importance of perseverance, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: 
Children's Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Mystery

Themes: 
Friendship, Independence, Self-Discovery
