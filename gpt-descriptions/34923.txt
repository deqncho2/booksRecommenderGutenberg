Synopsis: In "Breaking with the Past; Or, Catholic Principles Abandoned at the Reformation," Gasquet takes readers on a captivating journey through history, exploring the profound changes that occurred during the Reformation. With meticulous research and compelling storytelling, he unveils the dramatic events and decisions that led to the abandonment of Catholic principles. From the political intrigues to the religious debates, this book sheds light on the complex forces that shaped the religious landscape of Europe. Gasquet's exploration of this pivotal period in history will leave readers questioning the consequences of such a profound rupture.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Religious history, Cultural analysis

Themes: Reformation, Catholicism, Religious conflict, Historical change
