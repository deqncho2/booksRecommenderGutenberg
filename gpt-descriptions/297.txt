Synopsis: 
In 'The Flirt' by Booth Tarkington, a charming and enigmatic young woman named Cora Madison captivates the hearts of many men in her small town. With her irresistible allure and playful nature, she effortlessly draws suitors into her web of flirtation. As the story unfolds, the consequences of Cora's actions become increasingly apparent, revealing the complexities of love, desire, and the power dynamics between men and women. Tarkington masterfully explores the depths of human emotions and the consequences of one's actions in this captivating tale.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Power dynamics, Consequences of actions
