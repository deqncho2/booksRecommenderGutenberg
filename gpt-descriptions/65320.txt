Synopsis: In 'Kibun Daizin; Or, From Shark-Boy to Merchant Prince' by Murai, Gensai, embark on an exhilarating journey through the life of a young boy named Kibun, who rises from humble beginnings as a shark-boy to become a prominent merchant prince. Set against the backdrop of a vibrant and ever-changing world, Kibun's story is filled with unexpected twists, daring adventures, and profound personal growth. As he navigates the treacherous waters of commerce and power, Kibun must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. This captivating tale explores the transformative power of ambition, resilience, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Resilience, Personal growth, Pursuit of dreams
