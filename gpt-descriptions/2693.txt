Synopsis: In the heart of 19th century Edinburgh, a loyal little Skye Terrier named Bobby captures the attention and affection of the entire city. When his beloved master passes away, Bobby's unwavering devotion leads him on an extraordinary journey, defying all odds and touching the lives of those he encounters along the way. This heartwarming tale of loyalty, friendship, and resilience will leave readers captivated by Bobby's remarkable story.

Genres: Historical fiction, Animal fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Friendship, Grief, Perseverance
