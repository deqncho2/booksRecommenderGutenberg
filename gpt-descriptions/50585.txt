Synopsis: In 'A Thousand Degrees Below Zero' by Murray Leinster, a group of scientists embarks on a groundbreaking experiment to control the weather. However, their ambitious project takes an unexpected turn when they inadvertently trigger a catastrophic event that plunges the world into a frozen wasteland. As they struggle to survive in this new ice age, they must confront not only the harsh elements but also the consequences of their own actions. With time running out, they race against the clock to find a solution that could save humanity from extinction.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Environmental disaster, Scientific ethics, Survival
