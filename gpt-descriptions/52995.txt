Synopsis: In 'Spaceman on a Spree' by Reynolds, Mack, follow the thrilling journey of an astronaut who embarks on a reckless adventure across the galaxy. Fueled by a desire for freedom and a taste for danger, he finds himself entangled in a web of interstellar intrigue and unexpected encounters. As he navigates through treacherous space, he must confront his own demons and make choices that will shape the fate of the universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Consequences
