Synopsis: In 'Charge! A Story of Briton and Boer' by George Manville Fenn, readers are transported to the tumultuous times of the Boer War. The story follows the adventures of a young British soldier, who finds himself caught in the midst of the conflict between the British Empire and the Boer Republics. As he navigates the treacherous landscapes of South Africa, he must confront the harsh realities of war, forge unlikely alliances, and discover the true meaning of courage and loyalty. With vivid descriptions and gripping action, this historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through a pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Conflict, Survival
