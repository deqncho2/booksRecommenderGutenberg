Synopsis: In the third installment of Felix Dahn's captivating historical saga, 'A Struggle for Rome', the fate of Rome hangs in the balance as political intrigue, treachery, and forbidden love intertwine. As the Roman Empire teeters on the brink of collapse, a young warrior must navigate the dangerous world of power-hungry nobles, secret societies, and a mysterious prophecy that could change everything. With the fate of Rome and his own heart at stake, he must make impossible choices that will shape the destiny of an empire.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Thriller

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Forbidden love, Destiny
