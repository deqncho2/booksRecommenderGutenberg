Synopsis: In 'What a Colored Man Should Do to Vote' by Anonymous, a young African American man finds himself caught in the midst of a pivotal moment in American history. As he navigates the complex web of racial discrimination and political turmoil, he embarks on a journey that challenges his beliefs, tests his courage, and forces him to confront the harsh realities of the world around him. With the fate of his community hanging in the balance, he must make a decision that could change the course of his life forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Political Drama

Themes: Racial inequality, Civil rights, Identity, Social justice
