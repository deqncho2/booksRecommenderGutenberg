Synopsis: In "Nelson the Newsboy; Or, Afloat in New York," we follow the adventures of young Nelson, a determined and resourceful newsboy struggling to make a living on the bustling streets of New York City. As he navigates the challenges of his daily life, Nelson finds himself caught up in a web of mystery and intrigue that will test his courage and resilience. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the vibrant and dangerous world of 19th-century New York.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Social inequality
