Synopsis: In "Russian Roulette: Russia's Economy in Putin's Era," Vaknin, Samuel takes readers on a captivating journey through the complex economic landscape of modern Russia under the leadership of Vladimir Putin. With meticulous research and insightful analysis, Vaknin delves into the intricate web of political maneuvering, corruption, and power struggles that have shaped Russia's economy during Putin's reign. From the rise of oligarchs to the impact of international sanctions, the book uncovers the hidden truths behind Russia's economic rollercoaster ride. As the stakes get higher and the risks become more pronounced, Vaknin paints a vivid picture of a country playing a dangerous game of economic Russian roulette.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political Economy, International Relations

Themes: Power and Politics, Economic Development, Corruption, Globalization
