Synopsis: 
In 'Dwell Deep; or, Hilda Thorn's Life Story' by Amy Le Feuvre, readers are introduced to Hilda Thorn, a young woman with a mysterious past and a hidden talent. As Hilda navigates the challenges of life, she discovers the power of forgiveness, the importance of self-discovery, and the beauty of embracing one's true identity. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale takes readers on a journey of love, redemption, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Forgiveness, Self-discovery, Identity
