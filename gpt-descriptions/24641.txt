Synopsis: In "Martin of Nitendi"; and The River of Dreams: 1901, Louis Becke takes readers on a captivating journey to the remote islands of the Pacific. The story follows Martin, a young sailor who finds himself entangled in a series of thrilling adventures, encountering treacherous waters, mysterious islands, and encounters with indigenous cultures. As Martin navigates through the perils of the Pacific, he discovers the power of dreams and the transformative nature of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Dreams and Imagination
