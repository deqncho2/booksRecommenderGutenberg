Synopsis: In 'Tangled Trails: A Western Detective Story', a mysterious murder in the Wild West sets off a thrilling chain of events as a determined detective unravels a web of secrets and deceit. With danger lurking at every turn, the detective must navigate through a treacherous landscape of cowboys, outlaws, and hidden motives to uncover the truth. As the stakes rise and the body count increases, the detective's own past becomes entangled in the case, adding a personal dimension to the race against time. Will justice prevail in this gripping tale of crime and redemption?

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Justice, Redemption, Betrayal
