Synopsis: In 'The Bondwoman' by Marah Ellis Ryan, a young woman named Liza finds herself torn between love and duty as she navigates the complexities of the American Civil War. Set against the backdrop of the Deep South, Liza's journey takes her from the opulent mansions of the wealthy to the harsh realities of life as a bondwoman. As she grapples with her own identity and the bonds that hold her captive, Liza must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the society she lives in.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Identity, Love and Sacrifice, Social Injustice
