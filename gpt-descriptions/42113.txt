Synopsis: In 'The First Capture; or, Hauling Down the Flag of England' by Castlemon, Harry, a young and adventurous protagonist finds himself caught in the midst of a thrilling naval battle during the War of 1812. As he embarks on a dangerous journey aboard a privateer ship, he must navigate treacherous waters, face enemy forces, and make difficult choices that will test his courage and loyalty. With the fate of his crew and his country hanging in the balance, the young protagonist must summon all his strength and resourcefulness to achieve victory.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Patriotism
