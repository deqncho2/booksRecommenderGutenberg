Synopsis:
'Under the Star-Spangled Banner: A Tale of the Spanish-American War' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Spanish-American War. Set against the backdrop of the late 19th century, the story follows a young American soldier named Jack Somers as he embarks on a dangerous mission to the heart of enemy territory. As Jack faces the perils of war, he must confront his own fears and discover the true meaning of courage and patriotism. With vivid descriptions of battlefields, political intrigue, and personal sacrifices, this gripping tale captures the essence of a pivotal moment in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Sacrifice, Coming of Age
