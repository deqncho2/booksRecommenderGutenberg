Synopsis: 

In 'Terrestrial and Celestial Globes Volume 1: Their History and Construction Including a Consideration of their Value as Aids in the Study of Geography and Astronomy', Edward Luther Stevenson takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of globes. From their ancient origins to their intricate construction, this volume delves into the rich history and significance of these celestial and terrestrial spheres. With detailed explanations and illustrations, Stevenson explores the value of globes as essential tools for studying geography and astronomy, shedding light on their importance in understanding the world and the cosmos.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Astronomy

Themes: History of globes, Construction of globes, Importance of globes in geography and astronomy
