Synopsis: In 'Self Help; with Illustrations of Conduct and Perseverance', Samuel Smiles takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of various individuals who have overcome adversity and achieved success. Through inspiring anecdotes and practical advice, Smiles explores the power of determination, resilience, and self-improvement. From humble beginnings to remarkable accomplishments, this book offers a collection of stories that will leave readers motivated and eager to embark on their own path to personal growth.

Genres: Self-help, Biography, Motivational

Themes: Perseverance, Self-improvement, Success, Overcoming adversity
