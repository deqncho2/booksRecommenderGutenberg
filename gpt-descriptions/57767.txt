Synopsis: In 'The Andalusian Knitting and Netting Book' by Riego de la Branchardière, Eléonore, a young woman with a passion for knitting and netting, embarks on a journey that takes her from the vibrant streets of Andalusia to the opulent courts of Europe. As she navigates through love, loss, and the pursuit of her craft, Eléonore discovers the power of creativity and the resilience of the human spirit. With each stitch, she weaves together a tapestry of hope and determination that transcends borders and generations.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Women's Fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, Love and loss, Female empowerment
