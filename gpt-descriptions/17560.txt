Synopsis: In 'The Adventures of Ann: Stories of Colonial Times' by Mary Eleanor Wilkins Freeman, readers are transported to the captivating world of colonial America through the eyes of a spirited young girl named Ann. As she navigates the challenges and wonders of her time, Ann encounters mysterious strangers, uncovers hidden secrets, and embarks on thrilling adventures that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. From encounters with Native Americans to encounters with the supernatural, Ann's journey is filled with excitement, danger, and unexpected twists that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Coming of Age, Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Independence
