Synopsis: 
'The Golden Wheel Dream-book and Fortune-teller' is a comprehensive guide to fortune-telling and dream interpretation, offering a wealth of knowledge on various divination methods. From deciphering dreams and lucky numbers to using cards, dice, and dominoes for fortune-telling, this book covers it all. It also delves into the art of palmistry, face reading, and other techniques to predict future events and understand people's temperaments. Additionally, it provides insights into finding valuable resources through divination and offers tips on love charms and fortune-telling on special occasions. With a complete language and signification of flowers, this book is a treasure trove of mystical knowledge.

Genres: Occult, Divination, Reference
Themes: Fortune-telling, Dream interpretation, Divination methods, Personal insight, Love charms
