Synopsis: In 'Aunt Jo's Scrap Bag, Volume 2: Shawl-Straps', Louisa May Alcott takes readers on a delightful journey through Europe with four young women. Join them as they navigate the challenges and adventures of traveling abroad, encountering new cultures, making new friends, and discovering themselves along the way. From the bustling streets of Paris to the picturesque landscapes of Switzerland, this collection of stories is filled with humor, romance, and the timeless spirit of adventure.

Genres: Fiction, Travelogue, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Self-discovery
