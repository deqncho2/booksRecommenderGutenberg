Synopsis: In 'Precaution: A Novel', a young American inherits an English estate and finds himself entangled in a web of societal expectations, romantic enticements, and family secrets. As he navigates the unfamiliar world of English aristocracy, he must confront his own prejudices and make difficult choices that will shape his future. With its captivating characters and intricate plot, this novel explores the clash between American and European cultures, the complexities of love and marriage, and the pursuit of personal identity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Cultural clash, Identity, Love and marriage, Social expectations
