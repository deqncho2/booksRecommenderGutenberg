Synopsis: 
'Russian Fairy Tales: A Choice Collection of Muscovite Folk-lore' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Russian folklore. This collection presents a diverse array of magical tales filled with mythical creatures, brave heroes, and cunning villains. From the adventures of a clever fox to the trials of a young prince, each story is a testament to the rich storytelling tradition of Russia. With its vivid imagery and timeless themes, this collection is sure to transport readers to a realm where anything is possible.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Heroism, Magic, Wisdom
