Synopsis: In "The Curse of Yig," a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious deaths that seem to be connected to an ancient Native American legend. As the townspeople grapple with their fear and superstitions, a young couple sets out to uncover the truth behind the curse, leading them on a chilling journey into the heart of darkness.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Ancient legends, Fear and superstition, Exploration of the unknown
