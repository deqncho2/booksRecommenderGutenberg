Synopsis: In 'Eastern Standard Tribe', Art Berry finds himself caught between two worlds - the physical world and the virtual world. As a member of the Eastern Standard Tribe, a secret society that operates on a different time zone, Art's loyalty is tested when he is recruited by a rival tribe. With his relationships and sanity at stake, Art must navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and betrayal in a world where time zones dictate allegiances.

Genres: Science Fiction, Cyberpunk, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Technology, Relationships
