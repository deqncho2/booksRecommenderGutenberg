Synopsis: In 'Motor Boat Boys on the St. Lawrence: Or, Solving the Mystery of the Thousand Islands', the adventurous trio of Jack, Andy, and Mark find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey along the picturesque St. Lawrence River. As they navigate the enchanting Thousand Islands, they stumble upon a perplexing mystery that needs solving. With their trusty motorboat as their vessel, the boys delve into a world of hidden secrets, unexpected dangers, and unexpected allies. Will they be able to unravel the mystery that lies within the Thousand Islands?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Problem-solving
