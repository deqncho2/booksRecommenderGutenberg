Synopsis: In 'The Secret of Wyvern Towers' by T. W. Speight, a young woman named Alice finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she inherits a grand estate known as Wyvern Towers. As she delves into the secrets of the past, she uncovers a hidden world of dark secrets, hidden passages, and a chilling presence that lurks within the walls. With the help of a mysterious stranger, Alice must navigate the treacherous corridors of Wyvern Towers to uncover the truth before it consumes her. 

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the power of the past, the supernatural, the struggle between good and evil
