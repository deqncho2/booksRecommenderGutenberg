Synopsis: In "Esther: a book for girls" by Rosa Nouchette Carey, we are introduced to Esther, a young girl who finds herself navigating the complexities of life and love. Set in the late 19th century, Esther's journey takes her from the idyllic countryside to the bustling city, where she encounters unexpected challenges and discovers the power of resilience and self-discovery. As she grapples with societal expectations and personal desires, Esther's story unfolds with a delicate balance of heartwarming moments and poignant revelations. This captivating tale explores the themes of love, identity, and the pursuit of happiness, making it a must-read for anyone seeking a heartfelt coming-of-age story.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Historical

Themes: Love, Identity, Self-Discovery
