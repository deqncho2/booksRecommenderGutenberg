Synopsis: In 'Man to Man' by Gregory Jackson, a chance encounter between two strangers on a train leads to a gripping and unexpected journey of self-discovery. As they delve into each other's lives, secrets are unveiled, and the boundaries between truth and deception blur. With every twist and turn, the reader is left questioning the nature of identity and the power of human connection.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Deception, Human Connection
