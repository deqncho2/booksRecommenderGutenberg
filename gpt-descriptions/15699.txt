Synopsis: 

'A Handbook of the Boer War: With General Map of South Africa and 18 Sketch Maps and Plans' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the intricacies of the Boer War, providing readers with a detailed understanding of the conflict. Through the use of maps and plans, the book offers a visual representation of the war's key locations and battles, allowing readers to immerse themselves in the historical context. From the strategies employed by both sides to the challenges faced by soldiers on the ground, this handbook offers a captivating exploration of one of the most significant conflicts in South African history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Reference

Themes: War and conflict, Colonialism, Strategy and tactics
