Synopsis: 
In 'A Record of Buddhistic Kingdoms', the Chinese monk Fâ-hien embarks on a remarkable journey through India and Ceylon in search of the sacred Buddhist books of discipline. Facing treacherous terrains, encountering diverse cultures, and overcoming numerous obstacles, Fâ-hien's travels offer a captivating account of his quest for spiritual enlightenment and the preservation of ancient Buddhist teachings.

Genres: 
Travelogue, Religious literature, Historical account

Themes: 
Spiritual enlightenment, Cultural exploration, Preservation of ancient teachings
