Synopsis:
In "Westminster" by Walter Besant, a young and ambitious journalist named Richard West embarks on a thrilling journey through the political and social landscape of Victorian London. As he delves deeper into the heart of Westminster, he uncovers a web of secrets, corruption, and power struggles that threaten to shake the very foundations of the city. With his determination and wit, Richard becomes entangled in a dangerous game of politics, where alliances are fragile and betrayal lurks around every corner. As he navigates through the treacherous world of Westminster, Richard must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will shape not only his own destiny but also the future of the nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Political Thriller

Themes: Power and Corruption, Ambition, Loyalty, Social Class
