Synopsis: 
'Sleep-Book: Some of the Poetry of Slumber' takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the ethereal realm of dreams and the mysteries of sleep. This collection of poetry delves into the enchanting landscapes of the subconscious mind, exploring the delicate balance between reality and fantasy. Each poem is a lyrical exploration of the various facets of sleep, from the peaceful embrace of slumber to the haunting depths of nightmares. With vivid imagery and evocative language, this book invites readers to immerse themselves in the enigmatic world of dreams.

Genres: Poetry, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Sleep, Dreams, Subconscious mind, Reality vs. Fantasy
