Synopsis: 

In "Ten Months in a German Raider: A prisoner of war aboard the Wolf," John Stanley Cameron takes us on a gripping journey through his harrowing experience as a prisoner of war aboard the German raider, Wolf. From the moment his ship is captured, Cameron finds himself thrust into a world of uncertainty, danger, and survival. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the South Atlantic, he must outwit his captors, endure grueling conditions, and confront the constant threat of enemy attacks. With each passing day, Cameron's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the test, as he forms unexpected alliances and faces unimaginable challenges. Will he be able to escape the clutches of the Wolf and make it back home?

Genres: Memoir, War, Adventure

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Camaraderie, Conflict
