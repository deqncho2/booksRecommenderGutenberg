Synopsis: In 'Corleone: A Tale of Sicily', Francis Marion Crawford takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged landscapes and intricate society of Sicily. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows the lives of two young men, Giovanni Corleone and his best friend, Salvatore Falcone. Born into different social classes, their friendship is tested as they navigate the treacherous world of power, honor, and betrayal. As they become entangled in the web of the Mafia, their choices will shape their destinies and the future of their beloved Sicily.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Crime, Drama

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Power, Honor, Betrayal
