Synopsis: In 'The Marvellous History of the Shadowless Man, and The Cold Heart', two captivating tales intertwine to explore the extraordinary and the supernatural. One story follows a man who mysteriously loses his shadow and embarks on a quest to regain it, encountering strange beings and facing unexpected challenges along the way. The other tale delves into the life of a young man who makes a fateful deal with a sinister figure, leading him down a path of greed and transformation. These enchanting narratives will transport readers to a world where magic and reality collide, leaving them spellbound until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Fairy Tale, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Identity, Transformation, Morality, Greed
