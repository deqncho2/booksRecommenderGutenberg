Synopsis: In 'The Rhymer' by Allan McAulay, a mysterious poet emerges in a small Scottish village, captivating the townsfolk with his enchanting verses. As his words weave a spell over the community, secrets are unveiled, relationships are tested, and the power of poetry becomes a force to be reckoned with. With each rhyme, the Rhymer's influence grows, leaving the villagers questioning the boundaries between reality and imagination. As tensions rise and the lines blur, the true nature of the Rhymer's intentions becomes a haunting mystery that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Poetry

Themes: Power of words, Identity, Perception
