Synopsis: In 'Ten Dollars Enough: Keeping House Well on Ten Dollars a Week: How It Has Been Done; How It May Be Done Again', Catherine Owen takes us on a captivating journey into the world of frugal living. Through her personal experiences and practical advice, she reveals the secrets of maintaining a comfortable household on a meager budget. From creative meal planning to resourceful homemaking, Owen's insightful anecdotes and clever strategies will inspire readers to rethink their spending habits and embrace a simpler, more fulfilling way of life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Self-help, Memoir

Themes: Frugality, Budgeting, Resourcefulness, Simple living
