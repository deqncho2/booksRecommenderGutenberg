Synopsis: In 'Philosophical Letters: or, modest Reflections upon some Opinions in Natural Philosophy', the brilliant and unconventional Duchess of Newcastle, Margaret Cavendish, takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through the realms of natural philosophy. Through a series of letters, she explores various philosophical opinions and challenges traditional notions, offering fresh perspectives on topics such as motion, matter, and the nature of the universe. With her unique blend of wit and intellect, the Duchess invites readers to question their own beliefs and embark on a philosophical adventure unlike any other.

Genres: Philosophy, Epistolary, Non-fiction

Themes: Natural philosophy, Scientific inquiry, Critique of traditional beliefs
