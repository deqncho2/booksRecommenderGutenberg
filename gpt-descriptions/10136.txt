Synopsis: 
In 'The Book of Household Management', Mrs. Beeton takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of running a household in Victorian England. From managing servants and organizing social events to preparing delicious meals and maintaining a well-kept home, this comprehensive guide offers invaluable advice and practical tips for every aspect of domestic life. With its engaging anecdotes, detailed instructions, and charming illustrations, this book is not only a practical manual but also a delightful exploration of the Victorian era's domestic customs and traditions.

Genres: Non-fiction, Cookbook, Domestic Guide

Themes: Domesticity, Victorian lifestyle, Household management
