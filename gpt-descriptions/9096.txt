Synopsis: In 'Weighed and Wanting' by George MacDonald, a young woman named Annie Anderson finds herself torn between the allure of wealth and the pursuit of true happiness. As she navigates the complexities of society and the expectations placed upon her, Annie must confront the consequences of her choices and discover what truly matters in life. Set against the backdrop of Victorian England, this captivating novel explores the themes of materialism, love, and the search for meaning.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: Materialism, Happiness, Self-discovery
