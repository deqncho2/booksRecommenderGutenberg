Synopsis: In 'Garrity's Annuities' by David Mason, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Garrity, offering its residents a chance at financial security through a unique annuity program. As the townspeople eagerly sign up, they soon discover that the annuities come with a price far greater than they could have imagined. With secrets, suspense, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Greed, Deception, Consequences
