Synopsis: In 'Menhardoc' by George Manville Fenn, a young boy named Menhardoc finds himself caught in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden secret in his family's ancestral home. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Menhardoc uncovers a web of intrigue, danger, and long-held family secrets that will test his courage and determination. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth alongside Menhardoc.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Courage, Determination
