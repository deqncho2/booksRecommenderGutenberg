Synopsis: In 'The Haunted Fountain: A Judy Bolton Mystery' by Margaret Sutton, Judy Bolton finds herself entangled in a perplexing case when she stumbles upon a mysterious fountain with a haunting secret. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Judy uncovers a web of deceit, hidden identities, and long-buried secrets that threaten to unravel the peaceful town of Farringdon. With her sharp wit and determination, Judy must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects to solve the haunting mystery of the fountain before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Young Adult Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Deception and betrayal
