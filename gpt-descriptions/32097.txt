Synopsis: In the 11th edition of the renowned Encyclopaedia Britannica, Volume 7, Slice 3 takes readers on a captivating journey through the topics of "Convention" to "Copyright." This volume delves into the fascinating world of international agreements, diplomatic protocols, and the evolution of copyright laws. From the intricacies of diplomatic negotiations to the historical development of copyright protection, this volume offers a comprehensive exploration of these crucial aspects of human society. With its rich and detailed entries, readers will uncover the hidden stories behind the conventions that shape our world and gain a deeper understanding of the importance of copyright in fostering creativity and protecting intellectual property.

Genres: Reference, Non-fiction

Themes: International relations, Diplomacy, Intellectual property, Legal frameworks
