Synopsis: In 'A King's Daughter: A Tragedy in Verse' by John Masefield, a captivating tale unfolds as a king's daughter finds herself entangled in a web of love, power, and betrayal. Set against a backdrop of royal courts and political intrigue, the story follows the young princess as she navigates the complexities of her position and the consequences of her choices. With poetic language and dramatic twists, this tragedy in verse explores the depths of human emotions and the price of ambition.

Genres: Tragedy, Poetry, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Desire, Power and Politics, Betrayal and Consequences
