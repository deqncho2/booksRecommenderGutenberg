Synopsis: 
In 'Angling Sketches', Lang, Andrew takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of fishing, sharing his personal experiences and encounters with various fish species. From the tranquil rivers of Scotland to the wild waters of Norway, Lang's vivid descriptions and humorous anecdotes bring the art of angling to life. As he navigates the challenges and triumphs of the sport, readers will be enthralled by the unexpected twists and turns that unfold along the way.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Memoir, Sports

Themes: 
Nature, Adventure, Reflection, Humor
