Synopsis: In the second volume of "A Lady's Tour in Corsica," Gertrude Forde takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged landscapes and vibrant culture of Corsica. As she explores the island's hidden corners and encounters its diverse inhabitants, Forde's vivid descriptions and keen observations transport us to a world of breathtaking beauty and intriguing customs. From ancient ruins to bustling markets, from encounters with bandits to encounters with local legends, this travelogue is a thrilling adventure that will leave readers yearning to discover Corsica for themselves.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Natural beauty, Local customs
