Synopsis: In 'The Orange-Yellow Diamond' by Fletcher, J.S., a renowned detective is called upon to solve the mysterious disappearance of a priceless diamond during a glamorous society event. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances, leading him on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth and recover the precious gem. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Wealth and Power
