Synopsis: In 'The Origin of the Werewolf Superstition', Caroline Taylor Stewart delves into the mysterious and chilling history behind the age-old belief in werewolves. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, she uncovers ancient legends, historical accounts, and cultural beliefs that have shaped this enduring superstition. From the dark forests of Europe to the remote villages of Africa, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey to unravel the truth behind the werewolf myth.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Folklore

Themes: Mythology, Supernatural, Cultural Beliefs
