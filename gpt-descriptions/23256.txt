Synopsis: In "The King Of Beaver, and Beaver Lights: From "Mackinac And Lake Stories", 1899" by Mary Hartwell Catherwood, a mysterious figure known as the King of Beaver arrives in a small lakeside community, captivating the locals with his enigmatic presence. As the townspeople become increasingly intrigued by the King's peculiar behavior and his connection to the mesmerizing Beaver Lights, they find themselves drawn into a world of intrigue and wonder. This captivating tale explores the power of nature, the allure of the unknown, and the transformative impact of unexpected encounters.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Supernatural

Themes: Intrigue, Nature's Power, Transformation
