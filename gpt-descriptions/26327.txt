Synopsis:
In Casa Braccio, Volumes 1 and 2, F. Marion Crawford takes readers on a captivating journey through the grandeur and secrets of an ancient Italian palace. Set in Rome during the late 19th century, the story follows the lives of two American siblings, Paul and Sylvia Landry, who find themselves entangled in a web of mystery, romance, and intrigue within the walls of Casa Braccio. As they navigate the labyrinthine corridors and encounter a cast of enigmatic characters, the siblings uncover dark family secrets and face unexpected challenges that test their courage and resilience. With its vivid descriptions, gripping plot twists, and a touch of the supernatural, Casa Braccio is a mesmerizing tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Love and betrayal, Supernatural elements
