Synopsis: In 'To the Fore with the Tanks!', Percy F. Westerman takes readers on an exhilarating journey into the world of armored warfare. Set during World War I, the story follows a group of young men who find themselves at the forefront of a revolutionary military technology: the tank. As they navigate the challenges of battle, camaraderie, and personal growth, the protagonists must confront their fears and prove their mettle in the face of adversity. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale explores the triumphs and tribulations of those who dared to take the tanks to the fore.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Technological Advancement
