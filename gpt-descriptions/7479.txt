Synopsis: In 'Dorothy Dainty at Glenmore' by Amy Brooks, young Dorothy finds herself embarking on a thrilling adventure when she visits her aunt and uncle's grand estate, Glenmore. Surrounded by beautiful landscapes and intriguing characters, Dorothy's curiosity leads her to uncover hidden secrets and unexpected challenges. As she navigates through the enchanting Glenmore, Dorothy's courage and determination are put to the test, ultimately shaping her into a stronger and more resilient young girl.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Curiosity, Courage, Resilience
