Synopsis: In 'The Carnivore' by Katherine MacLean, a renowned scientist embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that blurs the line between human and animal. As the boundaries of morality and scientific progress are pushed to their limits, the protagonist finds themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning the true nature of humanity and the consequences of tampering with the natural order.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Morality, Scientific Ethics, Identity
