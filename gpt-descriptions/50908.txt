Synopsis: In 'A Relic of the Revolution' by Charles Herbert, a young historian stumbles upon a mysterious artifact from the American Revolution that holds a dark secret. As he delves deeper into its origins, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger, where the past and present collide in unexpected ways. With each revelation, the stakes rise, and he must race against time to uncover the truth before it consumes him.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, the power of the past, the consequences of uncovering the truth
