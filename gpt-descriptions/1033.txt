Synopsis: 

In "Rose o' the River" by Kate Douglas Smith Wiggin, we are introduced to Rose Wiley, a spirited and imaginative young girl who finds herself caught between the worlds of poverty and privilege. When circumstances force Rose to leave her humble home and move to a grand estate, she must navigate the complexities of social class, friendship, and love. As Rose embarks on a journey of self-discovery, she learns valuable lessons about the true meaning of happiness and the power of following one's heart.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Social class, Friendship, Love, Self-discovery
