Synopsis: In "Pollyanna Grows Up," we reunite with the beloved character Pollyanna Whittier as she embarks on a new chapter of her life. Now a young woman, Pollyanna faces unexpected challenges and heartbreaks that test her unwavering optimism. As she navigates through the complexities of adulthood, Pollyanna's infectious positivity becomes a beacon of hope for those around her, reminding us of the power of resilience and finding joy in the face of adversity.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Inspirational

Themes: Optimism, Resilience, Growth, Finding joy in difficult times
