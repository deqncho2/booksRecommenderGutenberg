Synopsis: In 'An Engagement of Convenience: A Novel' by Zangwill, Louis, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she agrees to a marriage of convenience. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, she discovers unexpected alliances, hidden motives, and a love that defies societal expectations. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a world where appearances can be deceiving and true happiness may require breaking free from the constraints of convention.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Marriage, Identity and Authenticity, Society and Expectations
