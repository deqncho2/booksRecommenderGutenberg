Synopsis: 

In 'The Illustrated Horse Doctor: Being an accurate and detailed account of the various diseases to which the equine race are subjected', Mayhew takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of equine health. Through vivid descriptions and illustrations, he explores the numerous diseases that afflict horses, providing valuable insights into their causes, symptoms, and treatments. From common ailments to more obscure conditions, Mayhew's expertise shines through as he delves into the intricacies of equine medicine. This comprehensive guide is a must-read for horse enthusiasts, veterinarians, and anyone seeking a deeper understanding of the health challenges faced by these majestic creatures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Veterinary Medicine, Animal Health

Themes: Equine health, Veterinary care, Disease prevention, Animal welfare
