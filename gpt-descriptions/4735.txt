Synopsis: In the picturesque Ozark Mountains, a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing with him a haunting secret that has plagued the community for years. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the locals, their hidden desires, regrets, and aspirations are brought to the surface. Through the power of forgiveness and redemption, the bonds of love and friendship are tested, forever changing the lives of those involved.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Forgiveness, Community, Secrets, Love
