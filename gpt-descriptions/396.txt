Synopsis: In "The Lady, or the Tiger?" by Frank R. Stockton, a young man finds himself in a precarious situation when he is forced to choose between two doors. Behind one door awaits a ferocious tiger, while behind the other door awaits a beautiful lady. The young man's fate lies in the hands of a princess who holds the secret to the doors. Will love or death be his ultimate destiny?

Genres: Short story, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and jealousy, Fate and decision-making
