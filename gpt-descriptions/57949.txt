Synopsis: In 'Were You Ever a Child?' by Dell, Floyd, a mysterious event forces a group of adults to confront their forgotten childhood memories, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and redemption. As they delve into their past, they uncover hidden secrets and face the consequences of their actions, ultimately questioning the nature of innocence and the power of forgiveness. This captivating novel explores the complexities of human nature and the enduring impact of childhood experiences.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Memory, Identity, Redemption, Forgiveness
