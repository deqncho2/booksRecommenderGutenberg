Synopsis: In 'One Man's Poison' by Robert Sheckley, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he stumbles upon a mysterious substance that grants him extraordinary abilities. As he grapples with the consequences of his newfound powers, he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where nothing is as it seems and trust is a luxury he can't afford. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous world where his every move could be his last.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power and its consequences, Identity and self-discovery, Deception and manipulation
