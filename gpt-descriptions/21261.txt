Synopsis: In "The Attempted Assassination of ex-President Theodore Roosevelt," Bloodgood, Wheeler P./ Cochems, Henry F. (Henry Frederick)/ Remey, Oliver E. take readers on a thrilling journey into the heart of a conspiracy to assassinate the beloved ex-President Theodore Roosevelt. Set in the early 20th century, this gripping true story follows the relentless pursuit of justice as Roosevelt's life hangs in the balance. With unexpected twists and turns, the authors paint a vivid picture of the political landscape and the dangerous forces at play. As the plot unfolds, readers will be captivated by the courage and determination of those who risk everything to protect their former leader.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, True crime, Political thriller

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and sacrifice, Justice and redemption
