Synopsis: In "When You Don't Know Where to Turn: A Self-Diagnosing Guide to Counseling and Therapy," author Steven J. Bartlett takes readers on a transformative journey of self-discovery and empowerment. Through a unique blend of personal anecdotes, expert advice, and practical exercises, Bartlett offers a comprehensive guide to navigating the complex world of counseling and therapy. With a focus on self-diagnosis and understanding one's own needs, this book provides invaluable tools for individuals seeking guidance and support in their mental health journey. As readers delve into the pages, they will uncover a wealth of knowledge and insights that will help them make informed decisions about their mental well-being. Genres: Self-help, Psychology, Mental Health. Themes: Self-discovery, Empowerment, Mental Health Awareness.
