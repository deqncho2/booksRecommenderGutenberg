Synopsis: 

In 'Christie Redfern's Troubles' by Margaret M. Robertson, readers are introduced to Christie Redfern, a young woman who finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue. When Christie's life takes an unexpected turn, she is forced to confront her past and navigate a series of challenges that threaten to unravel her world. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding her, Christie must make difficult choices that will determine her future. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind Christie Redfern's troubles.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Redemption
