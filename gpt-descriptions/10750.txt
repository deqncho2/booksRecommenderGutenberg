Synopsis: 
'The Miracle, and Other Poems' by Virna Sheard is a collection of captivating and thought-provoking poems that explore the depths of human emotions and experiences. From the enchantment of love to the anguish of loss, Sheard's words paint vivid pictures and evoke powerful emotions. Each poem is a journey, taking the reader on a rollercoaster of feelings, from joy and hope to despair and longing. With lyrical language and poignant imagery, Sheard delves into the complexities of life, offering glimpses of beauty and moments of profound reflection. This collection is a testament to the power of poetry to touch the soul and illuminate the human experience.

Genres: Poetry, Literary

Themes: Love, Loss, Emotions, Human Experience
