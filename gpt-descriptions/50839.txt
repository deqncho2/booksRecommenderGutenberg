Synopsis:
In "The Chronicles of Enguerrand de Monstrelet, Vol. 01," the reader is transported to a tumultuous period in French history, where the houses of Orleans and Burgundy engage in brutal civil wars. The narrative unfolds with gripping accounts of the English occupation of Paris and Normandy, their eventual expulsion, and the occurrence of other remarkable events in France and beyond. Enguerrand de Monstrelet, a contemporary chronicler, provides a vivid and detailed portrayal of the political and military conflicts that shaped the kingdom of France during this time.

Genres:
Historical non-fiction, Chronicle, Memoir

Themes:
War and conflict, Political intrigue, National identity
