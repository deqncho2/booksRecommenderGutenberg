Synopsis: 
In 'Daddy's Girl' by L. T. Meade, a young woman named Mabel finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she receives a cryptic letter from her estranged father. As she delves into her family's dark secrets, Mabel uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to shatter her world. With danger lurking around every corner, she must navigate through a labyrinth of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth about her father's past and protect her own future.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity
