Synopsis: In 'The Forest Monster of Oz', a mysterious creature emerges from the depths of the enchanted forest, wreaking havoc on the peaceful land of Oz. As the inhabitants of Oz band together to uncover the truth behind the forest monster, they embark on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, magic, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of Oz hanging in the balance, they must confront their deepest fears and confront the darkness that lies within the heart of the forest.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Redemption
