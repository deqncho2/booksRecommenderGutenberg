Synopsis: 

The Bible, Douay-Rheims, Book 01: Genesis: The Challoner Revision takes readers on a captivating journey through the beginning of time, exploring the creation of the world, the origins of humanity, and the early stories of faith and redemption. From the awe-inspiring account of the seven days of creation to the trials and tribulations faced by the first human beings, this book delves into the profound mysteries of life, love, and the divine. With its rich prose and timeless wisdom, Genesis invites readers to ponder the complexities of existence and the enduring power of faith.

Genres: Religious, Historical, Mythology

Themes: Creation, Faith, Redemption, Human Nature
