Synopsis: In 'The Children's Story of Westminster Abbey', author G. E. Troutbeck takes young readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and enchanting legends surrounding one of the most iconic landmarks in London. From the coronations of kings and queens to the mysterious tales of the Abbey's resident ghosts, this delightful book unveils the secrets and wonders hidden within the ancient walls of Westminster Abbey. Through vivid storytelling and beautiful illustrations, children will discover the Abbey's role in shaping British history and its enduring significance as a place of worship and remembrance.

Genres: Children's Literature, History, Non-fiction

Themes: History, Heritage, Legends, Spirituality, British Monarchy
