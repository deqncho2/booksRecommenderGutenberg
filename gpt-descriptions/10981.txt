Synopsis: 
'Child's New Story Book;: Or, Tales and Dialogues for Little Folks' is a delightful collection of stories and dialogues that will captivate young readers. Filled with charming characters and imaginative adventures, this book takes children on a journey through various tales that are both entertaining and educational. From talking animals to magical encounters, each story is designed to engage young minds and spark their imagination. With its engaging narratives and lively dialogues, this book is sure to become a beloved favorite for children everywhere.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Imagination, Morality, Education
