Synopsis: In 'Peeps at Postage Stamps', Stanley C. Johnson takes readers on a fascinating journey through the world of postage stamps, uncovering the hidden stories and secrets behind these tiny pieces of art. From the first stamps ever issued to the most rare and valuable ones, Johnson explores the historical, cultural, and artistic significance of these miniature masterpieces. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, he reveals the surprising tales behind famous stamps and introduces readers to the passionate collectors who dedicate their lives to this hobby. Whether you're a stamp enthusiast or simply curious about the world of philately, this book will open your eyes to the captivating world of postage stamps.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Art

Themes: Philately, Cultural Significance, Artistic Expression
