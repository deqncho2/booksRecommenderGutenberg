Synopsis: In 'Money Island' by Howell, Andrew Jackson, a group of strangers find themselves stranded on a mysterious island with a hidden treasure waiting to be discovered. As they navigate the treacherous landscape and encounter unexpected challenges, they must work together to unravel the island's secrets and outsmart their own greed. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Greed, Survival, Trust
