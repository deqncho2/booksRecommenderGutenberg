Synopsis: 

'Memoir of John Howe Peyton: in sketches by his contemporaries, together with some of his public and private letters, etc., also a sketch of Ann M. Peyton' is a captivating biographical work that delves into the life of John Howe Peyton, a prominent figure of his time. Through sketches by his contemporaries and a collection of his public and private letters, this memoir offers a unique glimpse into the personal and professional life of Peyton. Additionally, the book includes a sketch of Ann M. Peyton, providing further insight into the lives of this remarkable couple. 

Genres: Biography, Letters, Historical

Themes: Personal and professional achievements, Relationships and partnerships
