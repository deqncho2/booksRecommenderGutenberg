Synopsis: In 'A Parody Outline of History', Donald Ogden Stewart takes readers on a hilarious journey through American historical events, reimagining them as they would be narrated by some of America's most iconic contemporary authors. From the discovery of America to the Revolutionary War and beyond, Stewart's irreverent treatment of these events will have readers laughing out loud as they see history through a whole new lens.

Genres: Satire, Parody, Historical Fiction

Themes: Humor, American History, Literary Critique
