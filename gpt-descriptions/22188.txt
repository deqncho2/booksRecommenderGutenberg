Synopsis: 
In 'The Comedies of Terence: Literally Translated into English Prose, with Notes,' Terence takes us on a delightful journey through his collection of comedic plays. From mistaken identities to clever schemes, this compilation showcases the wit and humor of ancient Roman theater. Each play presents a unique story filled with love, friendship, and the complexities of human relationships. Terence's masterful storytelling and sharp dialogue will keep readers entertained and engaged from start to finish.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Roman Literature

Themes: Love and romance, Friendship, Identity, Deception, Social class
