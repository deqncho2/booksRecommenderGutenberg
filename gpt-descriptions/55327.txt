Synopsis: 
'Over the Border: A Romance' by Barr, Robert is a captivating tale that takes readers on a thrilling journey across borders. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows the lives of two individuals from different worlds who find themselves irresistibly drawn to each other. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and face numerous obstacles, their love is put to the ultimate test. Filled with suspense, adventure, and unexpected twists, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Forbidden love, Identity, Courage
