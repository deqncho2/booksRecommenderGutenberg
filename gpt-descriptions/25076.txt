Synopsis: 
In 'Between the Lines' by Cable, Boyd, a mysterious book transports a young girl into a captivating world where the characters come to life. As she delves deeper into this enchanting realm, she discovers secrets that challenge her perception of reality and blur the line between fiction and truth. With the help of a charming prince from the story, she embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the hidden mysteries that lie between the lines.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Imagination, Identity, Perception, Reality
