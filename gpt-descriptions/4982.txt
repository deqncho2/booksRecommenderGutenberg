Synopsis: In 'A Rock in the Baltic' by Robert Barr, a young journalist named Frank Mildmay finds himself embroiled in a thrilling adventure when he is assigned to cover a mysterious event on a remote island in the Baltic Sea. As he delves deeper into the island's secrets, Frank uncovers a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected romance. With his life on the line, he must navigate treacherous waters and outwit cunning adversaries to uncover the truth hidden within the rock. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Intrigue, Survival, Love and Betrayal
