Synopsis: In 'Echoes of the War' by J.M. Barrie, a collection of short stories takes readers on a journey through the aftermath of war, exploring the profound impact it has on individuals and society. From soldiers returning home to the struggles of those left behind, these poignant tales delve into the complexities of human emotions and the lasting echoes of conflict.

Genres: Fiction, War, Drama

Themes: Post-war trauma, Loss and grief, Resilience, Love and sacrifice
