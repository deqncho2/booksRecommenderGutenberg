Synopsis: 
In 'Tom Tufton's Travels' by Everett-Green, Evelyn, follow the thrilling adventures of young Tom Tufton as he embarks on a journey that takes him to far-off lands and introduces him to fascinating cultures. From encounters with pirates to unexpected friendships, Tom's travels are filled with danger, excitement, and unexpected twists. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and faces numerous challenges, Tom discovers the true meaning of courage, resilience, and the importance of embracing new experiences.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Self-discovery
