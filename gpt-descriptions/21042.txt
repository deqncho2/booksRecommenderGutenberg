Synopsis: In 'Roger Ingleton, Minor' by Reed, Talbot Baines, we follow the captivating journey of a young boy named Roger Ingleton, who finds himself thrust into a world of mystery and adventure. As Roger navigates through unexpected twists and turns, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters and uncovers secrets that will change his life forever. With each page, the reader is drawn deeper into Roger's quest, eagerly anticipating the next revelation and the ultimate resolution.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Courage, Discovery
