Synopsis: In 'The Forest' by Stewart Edward White, a group of diverse individuals find themselves drawn together in the enchanting wilderness of the Pacific Northwest. As they navigate the challenges of survival and the mysteries lurking within the forest, they discover that the true test lies not only in their ability to conquer nature, but also in their ability to conquer themselves. With danger lurking at every turn, the characters must confront their deepest fears and desires, ultimately revealing the transformative power of the untamed wilderness.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Nature's power and beauty, self-discovery, the human psyche, the struggle between civilization and the wild.
