Synopsis: 
In 'The Politeness of Princes, and Other School Stories', P. G. Wodehouse takes us back to the world of British boarding schools, where mischief, camaraderie, and unexpected adventures abound. From the misadventures of a young prince trying to fit in with his classmates to the hilarious escapades of a group of friends determined to outwit their strict headmaster, these school stories are filled with laughter, wit, and the timeless charm that Wodehouse is known for. Prepare to be transported to a world of pranks, friendships, and the triumph of the underdog in this delightful collection of tales.

Genres: Humor, Fiction, School Stories

Themes: Friendship, Mischief, Coming of Age
