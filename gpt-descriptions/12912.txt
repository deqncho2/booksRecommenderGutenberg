Synopsis: 
In 'The Price of Love' by Arnold Bennett, a young and ambitious artist named Denry Machin finds himself entangled in a web of love, desire, and societal expectations. As he navigates the complexities of relationships and the pursuit of success, Denry must confront the consequences of his choices and the sacrifices he is willing to make. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century England, this captivating tale explores the price one must pay for love and the lengths one will go to achieve their dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Ambition and success, Societal expectations
