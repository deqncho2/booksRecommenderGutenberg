Synopsis: In 'Mother West Wind 'Why' Stories', join the lovable characters of the Green Forest as they embark on a series of delightful adventures filled with curiosity and wonder. Follow Peter Rabbit, Jimmy Skunk, and their friends as they seek answers to the many questions that arise in their animal world. From unraveling the mysteries of nature to discovering the importance of friendship, this collection of charming tales will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Animal fiction, Adventure

Themes: Curiosity, Friendship, Nature, Learning
