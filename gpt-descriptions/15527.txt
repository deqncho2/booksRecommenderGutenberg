Synopsis: In 'Captivity' by Leonora Eyles, a young woman finds herself trapped in a mysterious and sinister world after accepting a seemingly innocent invitation. As she navigates through a labyrinth of secrets and deception, she must uncover the truth behind her captivity and fight for her freedom. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Survival
