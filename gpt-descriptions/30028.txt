Synopsis: In 'The Award of Justice; Or, Told in the Rockies: A Pen Picture of the West', Anna Maynard Barbour takes readers on a thrilling journey to the rugged landscapes of the American West. The story follows a young protagonist who finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger as she navigates the untamed wilderness. With unexpected twists and turns, the narrative explores the complexities of justice, honor, and the pursuit of truth in a land where survival is paramount. 

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Justice, Honor, Survival
