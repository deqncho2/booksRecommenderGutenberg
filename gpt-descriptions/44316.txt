Synopsis: In "Kobo: A Story of the Russo-Japanese War," Strang takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Russo-Japanese War. Set against the backdrop of this historical conflict, the story follows Kobo, a young Japanese boy who finds himself caught up in the chaos and violence of war. As Kobo navigates the treacherous landscapes of battlefields and encounters both friend and foe, he must confront his own fears and discover the true meaning of courage. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale explores the human spirit's resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Coming of Age
