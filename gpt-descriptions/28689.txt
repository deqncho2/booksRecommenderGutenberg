Synopsis:
In the small village of Glenoro, Duncan Polite is known as the watchman, a man of integrity and dedication. As the story unfolds, we follow Duncan's life and the challenges he faces, from his humble beginnings to his rise as a respected member of the community. Along the way, we witness his unwavering commitment to his family, his unwavering sense of duty, and his unwavering faith in the face of adversity. Through Duncan's experiences, we are reminded of the power of kindness, the importance of community, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Family Drama, Inspirational

Themes:
Integrity, Duty, Family, Community, Faith
