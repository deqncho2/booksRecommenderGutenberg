Synopsis: 
In 'Bobby Blake at Rockledge School; or, Winning the Medal of Honor' by Frank A. Warner, readers are introduced to Bobby Blake, a young and determined student who embarks on a thrilling adventure at Rockledge School. As Bobby navigates the challenges of school life, he finds himself caught up in a mysterious series of events that test his courage and integrity. With the help of his friends, Bobby must unravel the secrets of Rockledge School and prove himself worthy of the prestigious Medal of Honor. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the truth behind the school's hidden past.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Integrity
