Synopsis: 
In 'The Caxtons: A Family Picture - Volume 01', the reader is introduced to the eccentric and endearing Caxton family. The story revolves around Austin Caxton, a scholar and writer, who is determined to uncover the secrets of an ancient manuscript. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery, romance, and unexpected encounters. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century England, this captivating tale takes the reader on a journey filled with humor, intrigue, and the complexities of family dynamics.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Family, Love, Pursuit of Knowledge, Identity
