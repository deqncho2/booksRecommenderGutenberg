Synopsis: In 'The Boys of '98' by Otis, James, a group of young boys find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure during the summer of 1898. As they stumble upon a hidden treasure map, they set out on a quest that takes them through mysterious caves, dangerous encounters, and unexpected friendships. Along the way, they learn valuable lessons about bravery, loyalty, and the power of teamwork. Will they be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and uncover the long-lost treasure? Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age. Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery.
