Synopsis: In "The Etiquette of Engagement and Marriage," Devereux, G. R. M. takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of courtship, marriage, and the grandeur of wedding ceremonies. This comprehensive guide provides a fascinating glimpse into the world of Victorian-era customs and traditions surrounding love and matrimony. From the delicate art of courtship to the elaborate arrangements of a wedding, the author offers a wealth of knowledge and advice, ensuring that readers are well-equipped to navigate the complexities of engagement and marriage with grace and elegance.

Genres: Non-fiction, Etiquette, History

Themes: Courtship, Marriage, Victorian customs, Wedding traditions, Etiquette
