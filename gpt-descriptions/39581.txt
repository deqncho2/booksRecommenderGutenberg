Synopsis: In "Life of Frederick Courtenay Selous, D.S.O., Capt. 25th Royal Fusiliers," John Guille Millais takes readers on a captivating journey through the extraordinary life of Frederick Courtenay Selous. From his early adventures in Africa to his remarkable achievements as a soldier, explorer, and naturalist, Selous's story is one of courage, resilience, and a deep connection with the natural world. As readers delve into the pages of this biography, they will be enthralled by Selous's encounters with dangerous wildlife, his expeditions into uncharted territories, and his unwavering dedication to conservation. Millais paints a vivid portrait of a man whose life was filled with thrilling escapades and profound insights, leaving readers inspired by Selous's indomitable spirit and his lasting impact on the world.

Genres: Biography, Adventure, Natural History

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Conservation
