Synopsis: 
In "Jean Craig in New York" by Kay Lyttleton, Jean Craig, a young and ambitious woman, embarks on an exciting adventure in the bustling city of New York. Leaving behind her small town life, Jean finds herself navigating the fast-paced world of fashion, friendship, and romance. As she immerses herself in the vibrant culture of the city, Jean discovers that New York holds both opportunities and challenges that will test her determination and resilience. With her dreams on the line, Jean must learn to navigate the complexities of love, career, and self-discovery in the city that never sleeps.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Romance, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: 
Ambition, Self-discovery, Love, Friendship
