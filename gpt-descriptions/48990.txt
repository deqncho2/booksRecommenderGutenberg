Synopsis: In 'Old-Dad' by Eleanor Hallowell Abbott, a young woman named Mary finds herself unexpectedly responsible for her aging father, who has always been a bit eccentric. As she navigates the challenges of caring for him, Mary discovers a hidden side to her father's personality and embarks on a journey of self-discovery and understanding. With humor and heart, this touching story explores the complexities of family dynamics and the power of unconditional love.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Family

Themes: Aging, Family relationships, Self-discovery
