Synopsis: In 'The Boat Club; or, The Bunkers of Rippleton' by Optic, Oliver, a group of adventurous young boys form a secret boat club and embark on thrilling escapades along the river. As they navigate the challenges of their secret society, they encounter mysterious characters, uncover hidden secrets, and face unexpected dangers. With their friendship and resourcefulness tested, the boys must rely on their wits and courage to overcome the obstacles that come their way.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Courage, Secrets
