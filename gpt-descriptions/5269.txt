Synopsis: 
In 'Driven Back to Eden' by Edward Payson Roe, a young man named Philip is forced to leave his comfortable city life and return to his childhood home in the countryside. As he navigates the challenges of rural living, he discovers a hidden world of beauty, love, and self-discovery. Along the way, he encounters a mysterious woman who holds the key to his past and future. With each step, Philip is driven back to the simplicity and purity of Eden, where he must confront his own desires and find his true purpose in life.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Nature, Self-discovery, Redemption
