Synopsis: In 'Dragon's Blood' by Henry Milner Rideout, a young man named John is thrust into a world of adventure and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. With the help of a mysterious guide, he embarks on a perilous journey to find the legendary dragon's blood, a substance rumored to possess incredible powers. As John faces treacherous landscapes, encounters with mythical creatures, and unexpected allies, he must navigate the fine line between good and evil, ultimately discovering the true nature of courage and sacrifice.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Young Adult

Themes: Quest for identity, Power of friendship, Bravery in the face of adversity
