Synopsis: 
In 'The Racer Boys; Or, The Mystery of the Wreck' by Clarence Young, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves caught up in a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon a mysterious wreck in the woods. As they delve deeper into the secrets surrounding the wreck, they uncover a web of intrigue and danger that puts their courage and friendship to the test. With their racing skills and quick thinking, the boys must navigate through unexpected twists and turns to unravel the truth behind the wreck and bring justice to those involved.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Secrets
