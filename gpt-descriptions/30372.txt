Synopsis: In 'Robert Coverdale's Struggle' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Robert, a young man who dreams of a better life beyond his humble beginnings. Determined to rise above his circumstances, Robert faces numerous challenges and setbacks as he navigates the complexities of society. Along his journey, he encounters unexpected friendships, tests his own resilience, and learns valuable lessons about the true meaning of success. Will Robert's determination and perseverance be enough to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman, Adventure

Themes: Social mobility, Perseverance, Friendship, Self-discovery
