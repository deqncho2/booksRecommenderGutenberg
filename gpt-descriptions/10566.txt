Synopsis: In 'The Bequest: Ship's Company, Part 6', the crew of the tramp steamer, the "Flamingo," find themselves embroiled in a mysterious and dangerous situation when they stumble upon a hidden treasure map. As they set sail on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of the map, they encounter treacherous waters, cunning adversaries, and unexpected alliances. With their lives hanging in the balance, the crew must navigate through a web of deceit and betrayal to claim the long-lost treasure that could change their fortunes forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Treasure hunting, Survival, Loyalty, Betrayal
