Synopsis: In 'The Celebrity at Home' by Violet Hunt, a renowned celebrity finds herself caught in a web of scandal and intrigue when a mysterious stranger enters her life. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, the line between reality and illusion becomes blurred, leaving the reader questioning who can be trusted and what lies beneath the glamorous facade. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this thrilling novel takes readers on a journey through the dark side of fame and the consequences of living in the public eye.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Celebrity culture, Identity, Deception, Betrayal
