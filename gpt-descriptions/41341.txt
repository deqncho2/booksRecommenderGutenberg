Synopsis: In the final volume of the captivating Mount Royal series, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the characters' lives are forever changed. As the wealthy and influential characters navigate the treacherous waters of love, power, and betrayal, a shocking revelation threatens to tear their world apart. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Power and Influence, Secrets and Revelations
