Synopsis: In "Vesty of the Basins" by Sarah Pratt McLean Greene, a young woman named Vesty finds herself caught between two worlds - the bustling city life of New York and the serene beauty of the rural countryside. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Vesty must make difficult choices that will shape her future. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel explores the timeless themes of self-discovery, the clash of cultures, and the power of following one's heart.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Love and Relationships, Social Expectations
