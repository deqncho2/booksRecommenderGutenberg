Synopsis: In the remote wilderness of Keewatin, a small community is shaken by a mysterious murder that sends shockwaves through the tight-knit town. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of the residents is revealed. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the dark and twisted world of Murder Point, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Small-town secrets, Betrayal, Justice, Survival
