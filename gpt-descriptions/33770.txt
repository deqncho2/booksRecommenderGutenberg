Synopsis: 
Prepare to be captivated by the irreverent and audacious verses of 'Impertinent Poems' by Edmund Vance Cooke. This collection of witty and satirical poems takes aim at the follies and foibles of society, delivering biting social commentary with a mischievous flair. From humorous observations on love and relationships to sharp critiques of politics and human nature, Cooke's poems will leave you both laughing and pondering the absurdities of life. With a delightful blend of clever wordplay and insightful observations, 'Impertinent Poems' is a delightful journey through the lighter side of poetry.

Genres: 
Satire, Humor, Poetry

Themes: 
Social commentary, Human nature, Love and relationships
