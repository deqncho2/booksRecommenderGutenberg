Synopsis: 
In "Hidden Treasure: The Story of a Chore Boy Who Made the Old Farm Pay," we follow the journey of a young chore boy named John Thomas Simpson as he discovers a hidden secret on the old farm he works on. As he unravels the mystery, he finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure that will change his life forever. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth alongside John Thomas.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Discovery, Perseverance, Self-discovery
