Synopsis: 

In "The History of the Indian Revolt and of the Expeditions to Persia, China and Japan, 1856-7-8" by George Dodd, embark on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events of the Indian Revolt and the subsequent expeditions to Persia, China, and Japan. This gripping historical account takes readers on a captivating exploration of the political upheaval, military campaigns, and cultural encounters that shaped these regions during the mid-19th century. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Travelogue

Themes: Rebellion and resistance, Colonialism and imperialism, Cross-cultural encounters
