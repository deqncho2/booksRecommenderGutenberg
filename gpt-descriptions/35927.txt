Synopsis: In 'A Romance of Toronto (Founded on Fact): A Novel', Annie G. Savigny takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling city of Toronto in the late 19th century. The story follows the lives of two young individuals from different social backgrounds, whose paths unexpectedly cross, leading to a whirlwind romance that challenges societal norms and tests the boundaries of love. As their relationship blossoms amidst the backdrop of a rapidly changing city, they must navigate the complexities of class, family expectations, and personal ambitions. With vivid descriptions of Toronto's landmarks and a cast of intriguing characters, this novel offers a compelling exploration of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness in a rapidly evolving urban landscape.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Relationships, Social Class, Ambition, City Life
