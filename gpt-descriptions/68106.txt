Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 152, August 7, 1915: The Forced Crime; or, Nick Carter's Brazen Clew,' renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he is forced to commit a crime against his will. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Carter uncovers a brazen clue that leads him on a thrilling chase to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Justice, Determination
