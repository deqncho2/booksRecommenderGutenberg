Synopsis: In "Peace on Earth, Good-will to Dogs" by Eleanor Hallowell Abbott, a charming and heartwarming tale unfolds as a young woman finds herself unexpectedly surrounded by a delightful pack of dogs. As she navigates the challenges and joys of caring for these furry companions, she discovers the transformative power of love, loyalty, and the unconditional friendship that dogs bring into our lives.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Animal Fiction

Themes: Love, Loyalty, Companionship, Animal Welfare
