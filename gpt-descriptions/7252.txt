Synopsis: In "The Women Who Came in the Mayflower," Annie Russell Marble takes us on a captivating journey back to the early days of America, focusing on the remarkable women who were part of the historic Mayflower voyage. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Marble brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and untold stories of these courageous women who played a pivotal role in shaping the New World. From their arduous journey across the Atlantic to their encounters with Native Americans and the challenges of building a new society, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the lives of these extraordinary women.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Women's history

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Exploration, Gender roles, Cultural encounters
