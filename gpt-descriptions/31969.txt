Synopsis: 

'Eleven days in the militia during the war of the rebellion: A journal of the 'Emergency' campaign of 1862' by Richards, Louis is a captivating firsthand account of a soldier's experience during a crucial period of the American Civil War. Through the pages of his journal, the author takes us on a gripping journey, immersing us in the chaos, camaraderie, and courage that defined the 'Emergency' campaign. From the tense anticipation before battle to the harrowing moments on the front lines, Richards vividly captures the raw emotions and sacrifices of those who fought for their beliefs. This poignant and riveting journal offers a unique perspective on the realities of war and the indomitable spirit of those who faced it.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Memoir, Military History

Themes: War, Patriotism, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Resilience
