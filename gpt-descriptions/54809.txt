Synopsis: 

'Warwick, Leamington & Kenilworth: A Sketch-Book' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the picturesque towns of Warwick, Leamington, and Kenilworth. Through a collection of beautifully illustrated sketches, the book unveils the hidden gems, architectural wonders, and historical landmarks that define these charming English towns. From the grandeur of Warwick Castle to the elegant gardens of Leamington Spa, and the medieval ruins of Kenilworth Castle, this sketch-book offers a unique perspective on the rich heritage and cultural tapestry of these three interconnected towns. 

Genres: Travel, Art, History

Themes: Heritage, Architecture, Local Culture
