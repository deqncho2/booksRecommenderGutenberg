Synopsis: In 'Yellow Star: A Story of East and West', Elaine Goodale Eastman takes us on a captivating journey through the contrasting worlds of the East and the West. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows the life of a young woman named Yellow Star, who finds herself torn between her Native American heritage and the allure of the Western world. As she navigates the complexities of cultural identity, love, and societal expectations, Yellow Star's story unfolds with both heart-wrenching moments and unexpected triumphs. This poignant tale explores the clash of traditions, the search for belonging, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Cultural Heritage

Themes: Cultural Identity, Love and Relationships, Tradition and Modernity
