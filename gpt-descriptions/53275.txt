Synopsis: The Every-day Book and Table Book, v. 1 (of 3) takes readers on a captivating journey through the past and present, exploring the rich tapestry of popular amusements, sports, pastimes, ceremonies, manners, customs, and events that have shaped each of the three hundred and sixty-five days of the year. From ancient traditions to contemporary practices, this book serves as a comprehensive history of the year, month, and seasons, offering a perpetual key to the almanac. Delve into the fascinating world of daily life, uncovering the hidden stories and intriguing details behind the rituals and festivities that have marked our calendars for centuries.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Reference

Themes: Cultural traditions, Daily life, Timekeeping
