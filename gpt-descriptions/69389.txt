Synopsis: In 'The Arctic regions and the northern whale-fishery', Scoresby, William takes us on a thrilling journey through the treacherous Arctic regions, where he explores the captivating world of the northern whale-fishery. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding encounters, Scoresby's account of his adventures in the icy wilderness will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the secrets and dangers that lie within this unforgiving environment.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Exploration

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Man versus nature, Survival
