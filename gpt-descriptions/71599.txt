Synopsis: In 'Kitty's Enemy: $b or, the Boy Next Door,' Kitty finds herself drawn to the mysterious new boy who moves in next door. As their paths intertwine, Kitty discovers that appearances can be deceiving and that sometimes, the people we least expect can become our greatest allies or enemies.
