Synopsis: 

In "Alexander the Great: Makers of History" by Abbott, Jacob, embark on an extraordinary journey through the life of one of history's most legendary figures. This captivating biography delves into the remarkable achievements and tumultuous adventures of Alexander the Great, a young and ambitious ruler who conquered vast territories, shaped empires, and left an indelible mark on the world. From his early days as a prince to his awe-inspiring military campaigns, this book unveils the captivating story of a man driven by an insatiable thirst for power and glory. 

Genres: Biography, History, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Leadership, Conquest, Legacy
