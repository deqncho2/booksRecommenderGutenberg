Synopsis: 

In "In League with Israel: A Tale of the Chattanooga Conference," Annie F. Johnston takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of a group of young women attending a conference in Chattanooga. As they navigate the challenges of friendship, love, and personal growth, they find themselves unexpectedly drawn into a world of international intrigue and political secrets. With their loyalty and courage tested, the girls must make difficult choices that will not only impact their own lives but also the fate of nations.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Love, Loyalty, Political Intrigue, Personal Growth
