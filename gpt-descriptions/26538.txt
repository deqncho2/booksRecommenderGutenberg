Synopsis: 
In 'Madge Morton's Victory' by Amy D. V. Chalmers, a young and determined Madge Morton finds herself caught up in a thrilling adventure when she stumbles upon a mysterious secret. As she unravels the clues and faces unexpected challenges, Madge must rely on her wit and courage to overcome the obstacles in her path. With danger lurking around every corner, Madge's journey takes her to unexpected places and introduces her to a cast of intriguing characters. Will Madge be able to uncover the truth and achieve her ultimate victory?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Determination, Discovery
