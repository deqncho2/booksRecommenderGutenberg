Synopsis: In 'Attila: A Romance. Vol. I.', James, G. P. R. takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous world of ancient Rome and the fearsome Hunnic Empire. Amidst political intrigue, forbidden love, and epic battles, the fate of nations hangs in the balance as the legendary warrior Attila rises to power. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the boundaries of loyalty and honor are pushed to their limits. Brace yourself for a thrilling adventure that will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and ambition, Love and sacrifice, Loyalty and betrayal
