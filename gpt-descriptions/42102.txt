Synopsis: 
In 'The Boy Scouts' Mountain Camp' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of young Boy Scouts embark on an exciting adventure in the rugged wilderness. As they navigate through treacherous terrain, face unexpected challenges, and encounter mysterious creatures, the boys must rely on their skills, teamwork, and determination to survive. With each passing day, they uncover secrets that will test their courage and change their lives forever. This thrilling tale of friendship, bravery, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Self-discovery
