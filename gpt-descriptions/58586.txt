Synopsis: 

In 'Bird Houses, Baths and Feeding Shelters: How to Make and Where to Place Them', author Edmund J. Sawyer takes readers on an enchanting journey into the world of birds and their habitats. This comprehensive guide provides step-by-step instructions on creating beautiful and functional bird houses, baths, and feeding shelters, while also offering valuable insights into the ideal locations for placing them. From simple designs to more intricate structures, Sawyer's expertise and passion for avian conservation shine through, making this book a must-read for both novice and experienced bird enthusiasts.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, DIY

Themes: Bird conservation, Habitat creation, Wildlife appreciation
