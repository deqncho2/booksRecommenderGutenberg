Synopsis: In 'Ye Lyttle Salem Maide: A Story of Witchcraft', Pauline Bradford Mackie takes us back to the infamous Salem witch trials through the eyes of a young girl named Dorothy. As accusations of witchcraft spread like wildfire, Dorothy finds herself caught in a web of fear, suspicion, and betrayal. With her life hanging in the balance, she must navigate the treacherous path of survival while uncovering the truth behind the hysteria that grips the town. This gripping historical novel delves into the dark and haunting world of witchcraft, offering a fresh perspective on one of America's most chilling chapters.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Witchcraft, Hysteria, Betrayal, Survival
