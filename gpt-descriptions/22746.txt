Synopsis: 

In 'The Copy/South Dossier: Issues in the economics, politics, and ideology of copyright in the global South,' readers are taken on a thought-provoking journey through the complex world of copyright in the global South. This captivating work delves into the economic, political, and ideological aspects of copyright, shedding light on the challenges faced by countries outside the traditional centers of power. Through a collection of insightful essays and case studies, the book explores the impact of copyright on creativity, innovation, and cultural expression in the global South, offering a fresh perspective on the dynamics of intellectual property in a rapidly changing world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Cultural Studies, Intellectual Property

Themes: Copyright, Global South, Economics, Politics, Ideology, Creativity, Cultural Expression
