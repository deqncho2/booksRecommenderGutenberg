Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Major Monkey' by Bailey, Arthur Scott, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure through the eyes of Major Monkey, a mischievous and clever primate with a knack for getting into trouble. When a mysterious artifact is stolen from the local museum, Major Monkey finds himself caught up in a dangerous quest to retrieve it. Along the way, he encounters a colorful cast of characters, navigates treacherous landscapes, and uncovers shocking secrets that will challenge everything he thought he knew. With heart-pounding action and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Redemption
