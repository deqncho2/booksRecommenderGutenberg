Synopsis: 

'Byzantine Churches in Constantinople: Their History and Architecture' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and stunning architecture of the Byzantine churches in Constantinople. Written by a team of esteemed scholars, including George Walter, Arthur Henderson, Ramsay Traquair, and Alexander Van Millingen, this book delves into the intricate details and fascinating stories behind these magnificent structures. From the iconic Hagia Sophia to lesser-known gems, the authors explore the evolution of Byzantine architecture, the cultural significance of these churches, and the challenges they faced throughout history. Through vivid descriptions and beautiful illustrations, this book offers a comprehensive and engaging exploration of the Byzantine churches in Constantinople.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Architecture

Themes: Byzantine history, Architecture and design, Cultural heritage
