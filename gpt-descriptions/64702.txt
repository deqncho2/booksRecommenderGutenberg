Synopsis: In 'Ultimatum' by Roger D. Aycock, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Jonathan Reed finds himself caught in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse when he receives a mysterious ultimatum that threatens the lives of millions. As he races against time to uncover the truth behind the ultimatum, Reed must navigate a web of deception, betrayal, and hidden agendas, all while trying to outsmart a cunning adversary who seems to always be one step ahead. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, Reed must make difficult choices and confront his own demons in order to save humanity from an unimaginable disaster.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Ethics, Power and Control, Redemption
