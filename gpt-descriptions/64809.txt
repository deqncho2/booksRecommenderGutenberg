Synopsis: 
'An Adventure' follows the thrilling journey of two women, Eleanor and Charlotte, as they embark on a mysterious and otherworldly adventure. While visiting the Palace of Versailles, they find themselves inexplicably transported back in time to the era of Marie Antoinette. As they navigate the treacherous court of the French queen, they must unravel the secrets of their time-travel and find a way back home. Filled with suspense, intrigue, and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, Time travel, Mystery

Themes: Adventure, Friendship, Identity, Historical events
