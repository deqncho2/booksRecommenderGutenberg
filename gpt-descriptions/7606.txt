Synopsis:
In 'Zicci: A Tale — Volume 01', the enigmatic and mysterious Zicci arrives in Naples, captivating the locals with his extraordinary abilities and enigmatic presence. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the city's inhabitants, including the beautiful Viola Pisani and the ambitious Prince di —, Zicci's true nature and purpose remain shrouded in secrecy. With elements of romance, suspense, and the supernatural, this tale takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden depths of human desires and the power of fate.

Genres:
Gothic fiction, supernatural, romance

Themes:
Mystery, fate, power, desire
