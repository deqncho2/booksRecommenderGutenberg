Synopsis: In 'Boon, The Mind of the Race, The Wild Asses of the Devil, and The Last Trump;: Being a First Selection from the Literary Remains of George Boon, Appropriate to the Times' by H. G. Wells, readers are introduced to the enigmatic character of George Boon, whose literary remains offer a fascinating glimpse into his mind. Through a collection of writings, the reader is taken on a thought-provoking journey that explores the complexities of human nature, the struggles of society, and the looming uncertainties of the future. As Boon's words unfold, the reader is left pondering the profound questions raised and the implications they hold for our own time.

Genres: Fiction, Science Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: Human nature, Society, Future, Philosophy
