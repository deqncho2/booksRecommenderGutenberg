Synopsis: In "Bi-sexual Love; The Homosexual Neurosis," Wilhelm Stekel delves into the complex and often misunderstood world of bisexuality. Through a series of case studies and psychological analysis, Stekel explores the inner struggles and societal pressures faced by individuals who experience attraction to both genders. With a compassionate and insightful approach, the author challenges conventional notions of sexuality and offers a thought-provoking exploration of the human psyche.

Genres: Psychology, LGBTQ+ Studies, Non-fiction

Themes: Bisexuality, Sexual Identity, Society's Perception of Sexuality, Psychological Analysis
