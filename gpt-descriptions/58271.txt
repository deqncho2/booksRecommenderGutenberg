Synopsis: In "The Tower of London" by William Benham, a gripping tale unfolds within the ancient walls of one of England's most notorious landmarks. As secrets are unearthed and dark forces converge, a young historian finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger. With the fate of the nation hanging in the balance, she must navigate treacherous paths and face unimaginable challenges to uncover the truth hidden within the Tower's shadowy past.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal, History and its impact
