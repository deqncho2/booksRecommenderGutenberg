Synopsis: In "The Critics Versus Shakspere: A Brief for the Defendant," Francis Asbury Smith presents a compelling defense of the renowned playwright against the criticisms and doubts surrounding his identity and authorship. Through meticulous research and persuasive arguments, Smith challenges the skeptics and sheds light on the true genius behind the works attributed to William Shakspere. As he delves into the historical context and literary analysis, the author unravels a captivating narrative that will leave readers questioning everything they thought they knew about Shakespeare.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, Historical Analysis

Themes: Authorship, Identity, Literary Legacy
