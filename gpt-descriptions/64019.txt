Synopsis: In 'A Fine Day for Dying' by John Martin, a retired detective is drawn back into the world of crime when a mysterious murder takes place in his small town. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that threatens to unravel everything he thought he knew. With time running out, he must navigate the treacherous path of truth and lies to bring the killer to justice, all while confronting his own demons from the past.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Justice
