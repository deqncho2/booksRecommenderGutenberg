Synopsis: In "The first New Testament printed in English," embark on a captivating journey through history as you witness the groundbreaking translation of the Bible into English for the very first time. Follow the tireless efforts of a courageous scholar who risks everything to bring the sacred text to the masses, facing opposition, controversy, and even persecution along the way. As the pages of this remarkable tale unfold, discover the profound impact this translation has on society, religion, and the course of English literature. Prepare to be enthralled by the triumphs and tribulations of those who dared to challenge the status quo and forever change the way we engage with the Word of God.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Religious Literature

Themes: Translation and Interpretation, Religious Freedom, Cultural Transformation
