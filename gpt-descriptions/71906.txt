Synopsis: In 'The adopted son: Illustrations of the Lord's Prayer' by A. L. O. E., a young boy named Ernest embarks on a journey of self-discovery and faith as he navigates the challenges of life. Through a series of captivating and heartwarming events, Ernest learns the power of forgiveness, the importance of trust, and the true meaning of family. As he grows closer to understanding the Lord's Prayer, Ernest's life is forever transformed, and he discovers the incredible love and grace that can be found in the most unexpected places.

Genres: Christian fiction, coming-of-age, family

Themes: Faith, forgiveness, family, self-discovery
