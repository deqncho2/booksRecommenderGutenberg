Synopsis: In "The Man Who Saw the Future" by Edmond Hamilton, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious device that grants him the ability to see into the future. As he delves deeper into the visions, he becomes consumed by the knowledge of what lies ahead, leading him on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth and alter the course of destiny. With each glimpse into the future, the scientist must grapple with the consequences of his newfound power and the ethical dilemmas it presents. As the line between fate and free will blurs, the story takes unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Time travel, Fate vs. free will, Ethical dilemmas
