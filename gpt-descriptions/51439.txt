Synopsis: In "Taxidermy without a Teacher: Comprising a Complete Manual of Instruction for Preparing and Preserving Birds, Animals and Fishes," author Walter Porter Manton takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of taxidermy. This comprehensive manual provides step-by-step instructions and techniques for preparing and preserving various creatures, from birds to animals and fishes. Manton's expertise and passion for the art of taxidermy shine through as he shares his knowledge and secrets, making this book an invaluable resource for both beginners and experienced taxidermists alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, How-to, Reference

Themes: Artistry, Preservation, Natural History
