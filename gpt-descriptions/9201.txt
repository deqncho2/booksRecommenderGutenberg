Synopsis: In "Sunday at Home," Hawthorne takes readers on a journey through the mind of a young boy as he explores the mysteries and wonders of his family's ancestral home. As he delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the house, he uncovers a world filled with enchantment, darkness, and unexpected revelations. This captivating tale invites readers to join the young protagonist on a Sunday adventure that will leave them questioning the boundaries between reality and imagination.

Genres: Gothic fiction, supernatural, mystery

Themes: Exploration of the human psyche, the power of imagination, the blurred line between reality and fantasy
