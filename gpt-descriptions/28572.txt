Synopsis: 
'Letters on an Elk Hunt' is a captivating collection of letters written by Elinore Pruitt Stewart, a courageous and independent woman who embarks on an extraordinary adventure in the early 20th century. Leaving behind her life in Denver, Elinore moves to the remote Wyoming wilderness to become a homesteader. Through her heartfelt and vivid letters, she shares her experiences of surviving the harsh conditions, encountering wild animals, and forming unexpected friendships with the locals. As Elinore's story unfolds, readers are transported to a time and place where determination, resilience, and the beauty of nature intertwine.

Genres: 
Memoir, Adventure, Historical

Themes: 
Independence, Survival, Nature, Community
