Synopsis: 
'Poems 1918-21, Including Three Portraits and Four Cantos' by Ezra Pound is a collection of poetry that takes readers on a captivating journey through the post-World War I era. Through vivid imagery and powerful language, Pound explores various themes such as love, loss, and the complexities of human nature. The collection includes three portraits, which offer intimate glimpses into the lives of individuals, and four cantos, which delve into historical and mythological subjects. With its evocative verses and thought-provoking narratives, this collection invites readers to immerse themselves in the beauty and depth of Pound's poetic world.

Genres: Poetry, Modernist literature

Themes: Love, Loss, Human nature, History, Mythology
