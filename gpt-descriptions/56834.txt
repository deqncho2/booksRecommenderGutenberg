Synopsis: In 'The Polly Page Yacht Club', a group of adventurous young girls embark on a thrilling summer adventure that takes them beyond their wildest dreams. Polly Page and her friends find themselves caught up in a mysterious treasure hunt, leading them to uncover hidden secrets and face unexpected challenges. As they navigate the high seas and encounter new friends and foes, the girls learn the importance of teamwork, courage, and the power of friendship. Will they be able to solve the puzzle and claim the treasure before it falls into the wrong hands?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Teamwork, Discovery
