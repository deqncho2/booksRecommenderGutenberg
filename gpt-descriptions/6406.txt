Synopsis: 
In the remote Scottish Highlands, a young nobleman seeks refuge in a mysterious monastery after a series of tragic events. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the monastery, he uncovers a web of intrigue, forbidden love, and dark forces that threaten to consume him. With each passing day, the line between reality and illusion becomes blurred, and he must confront his own demons to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Historical fiction, Mystery

Themes: Love and desire, Religion and spirituality, Madness and sanity
