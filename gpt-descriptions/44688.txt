Synopsis: 

In 'To and Through Nebraska' by Fulton, Frances I. Sims, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey through the vast and untamed landscapes of Nebraska. As she travels, she encounters a diverse cast of characters, each with their own secrets and desires. From the bustling city streets to the quiet solitude of the prairies, Emily's adventure takes her on a transformative path of self-discovery and unexpected love. With every step she takes, the mysteries of Nebraska unravel, revealing a world filled with both beauty and darkness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration of the American West, Coming-of-Age, Identity, Love and Relationships
