Synopsis: In 'The Automobile Girls at Chicago; Or, Winning Out Against Heavy Odds', Laura Dent Crane takes readers on an exhilarating journey with a group of young women who find themselves in the bustling city of Chicago. As they navigate the challenges of the big city, they uncover a web of secrets and face unexpected obstacles that test their determination and resilience. With their passion for adventure and their unwavering friendship, the Automobile Girls embark on a thrilling quest that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Overcoming adversity
