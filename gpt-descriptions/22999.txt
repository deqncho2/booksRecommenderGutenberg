Synopsis: In 'The Ffolliots of Redmarley' by L. Allen Harker, a mysterious family with a dark past moves into the quaint village of Redmarley, stirring up curiosity and suspicion among the locals. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, the Ffolliots find themselves entangled in a web of scandal, love, and betrayal that threatens to tear their family apart. With a cast of intriguing characters and a gripping plot, this captivating novel will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Small-town gossip
