Synopsis: In 'The Duchess of Dublin: A Farce', the charming and mischievous Duchess of Dublin finds herself entangled in a web of mistaken identities, secret affairs, and hilarious misunderstandings. As she navigates through a series of comical situations, her quick wit and cunning nature are put to the test. With a cast of eccentric characters and a plot filled with unexpected twists, this farce promises to keep readers laughing from beginning to end.

Genres: Farce, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Identity, Deception, Social Class
