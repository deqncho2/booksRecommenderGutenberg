Synopsis: 

In "Scotland Yard: The methods and organisation of the Metropolitan Police," author George Dilnot takes readers on a captivating journey into the inner workings of one of the world's most renowned police forces. Through meticulous research and firsthand accounts, Dilnot unveils the secrets behind Scotland Yard's investigative techniques, its organizational structure, and the challenges faced by its officers. From high-profile murder cases to the pursuit of notorious criminals, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the thrilling world of crime-solving in the heart of London. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Crime, History

Themes: Law enforcement, Criminal investigation, Organizational structure
