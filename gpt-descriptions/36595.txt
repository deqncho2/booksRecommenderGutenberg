Synopsis: In "Stranger Than Fiction: Being Tales from the Byways of Ghosts and Folk-lore," Mary L. Lewes takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious and supernatural. From haunted houses to eerie encounters with spirits, this collection of tales delves into the unexplained and the uncanny. Each story is filled with suspense, leaving readers on the edge of their seats as they navigate the realms of ghosts and folklore. Prepare to be enthralled by these spine-chilling narratives that blur the line between reality and the supernatural.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural Fiction, Folklore

Themes: Ghosts, Mystery, Legends, Supernatural Phenomena
