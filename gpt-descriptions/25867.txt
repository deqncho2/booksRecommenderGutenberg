Synopsis: In 'The Tragic Bride' by Francis Brett Young, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of secrets and forbidden desires as she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations. Set against the backdrop of a picturesque English village, Mary's journey takes unexpected turns, leading her to question the true nature of happiness and the sacrifices one must make for it. As the story unfolds, the reader is drawn into a captivating tale of passion, betrayal, and the devastating consequences of choices made in the name of love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Society and Expectations, Sacrifice and Consequences
