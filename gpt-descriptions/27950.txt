Synopsis: In 'The Rhodesian' by Gertrude Page, a young woman named Elizabeth finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she inherits a sprawling estate in colonial Rhodesia. As she delves into the secrets of her family's past, Elizabeth uncovers a hidden world of forbidden love, political intrigue, and dark family secrets. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game where trust is scarce and betrayal lurks around every corner. As Elizabeth navigates the treacherous landscape of her inheritance, she must confront her own identity and make choices that will shape her future. 

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Betrayal, Love and sacrifice
