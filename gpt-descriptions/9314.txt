Synopsis: In 'The Calling of Dan Matthews' by Harold Bell Wright, a young minister named Dan Matthews arrives in a small town filled with corruption, hypocrisy, and moral decay. As he strives to bring about change and inspire the community, he faces numerous challenges and personal struggles. With his unwavering faith and determination, Dan must navigate through the complexities of love, faith, and the pursuit of justice, all while discovering his own true calling.

Genres: Fiction, Christian literature, Social commentary

Themes: Faith and spirituality, Moral integrity, Social reform, Love and relationships
