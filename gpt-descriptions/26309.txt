Synopsis: In 'The High Calling' by Charles M. Sheldon, a young and ambitious lawyer named John Grayson finds himself torn between his career aspirations and his desire to make a positive impact on society. When he is unexpectedly thrust into a high-profile case involving a controversial social issue, John must navigate the complexities of the legal system while grappling with his own moral compass. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers shocking truths that challenge his beliefs and force him to confront the true meaning of justice and the sacrifices required to achieve it.

Genres: Legal thriller, drama, social commentary

Themes: Morality, justice, personal sacrifice, societal impact
