Synopsis: In the small frontier town of Dusty Creek, a mysterious murder rocks the tight-knit community. As the local sheriff investigates, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that lead him down a treacherous path. With each clue he uncovers, the sheriff realizes that the truth may be more elusive than he ever imagined, and the danger may be closer to home than he ever anticipated.

Genres: Mystery, Western, Thriller

Themes: Small-town secrets, Justice and morality, Betrayal and loyalty
