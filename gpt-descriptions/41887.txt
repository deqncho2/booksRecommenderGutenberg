Synopsis: In "Filippo Lippi" by Paul G. Konody, delve into the captivating life of the renowned Italian painter, Filippo Lippi. From his humble beginnings as an orphaned child to his rise as a celebrated artist, this biography takes readers on a journey through Lippi's tumultuous life, filled with scandal, passion, and artistic brilliance. Uncover the secrets behind his most famous works and the fascinating relationships that shaped his career. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Konody paints a vivid portrait of a complex and enigmatic artist, leaving readers eager to explore Lippi's masterpieces for themselves.

Genres: Biography, Art History

Themes: Artistic genius, Scandal and controversy, Personal relationships
