Synopsis: In 'The Quiet Hotel: A Farcical Sketch in One Act', the eccentric guests of a seemingly ordinary hotel find themselves entangled in a series of hilarious misadventures. From mistaken identities to absurd misunderstandings, this fast-paced comedy takes the audience on a whirlwind journey through the corridors of chaos. As the characters navigate their way through a maze of comedic situations, the true nature of the hotel and its inhabitants is gradually revealed, leaving the audience in stitches until the very end.

Genres: Comedy, Farce

Themes: Mistaken identity, Absurdity, Chaos
