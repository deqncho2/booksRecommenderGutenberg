Synopsis: 

'Nights: Rome, Venice, in the Aesthetic Eighties; London, Paris, in the Fighting Nineties' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant cities of Rome, Venice, London, and Paris during two distinct decades. Through the eyes of the protagonist, we witness the allure and decadence of the Aesthetic Eighties, where art, beauty, and passion intertwine in the streets of Rome and Venice. Then, we are transported to the tumultuous Fighting Nineties, where the cities of London and Paris become the backdrop for political upheaval, social change, and the struggle for women's rights. 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Art and Aesthetics, Decadence, Political and Social Change, Women's Rights
