Synopsis: At the Back of the North Wind follows the extraordinary adventures of a young boy named Diamond, who forms an unlikely friendship with the enigmatic North Wind. As Diamond embarks on a series of magical journeys, he discovers a world filled with wonder, danger, and profound lessons about life and love. Through his encounters with fantastical creatures and mysterious events, Diamond's perspective on the world is forever transformed, leading him to question the nature of good and evil, the power of faith, and the importance of embracing the beauty and mystery of the unknown.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Morality, Loss, Redemption
