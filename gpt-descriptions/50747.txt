Synopsis: In 'The Expositor's Bible: The Book of the Twelve Prophets, Vol. 2: Commonly Called the Minor', George Adam Smith delves into the lesser-known prophets of the Old Testament, exploring their messages and significance. From the poetic words of Hosea to the fiery prophecies of Malachi, this volume offers a comprehensive analysis of the twelve minor prophets, shedding light on their historical context and timeless relevance. Smith's insightful commentary invites readers to uncover the profound wisdom and spiritual insights contained within these often overlooked books of the Bible.

Genres: Biblical commentary, religious studies, theology

Themes: Prophecy, divine judgment, repentance, social justice, faithfulness to God
