Synopsis: 
'Peak and Prairie: From a Colorado Sketch-book' takes readers on a captivating journey through the stunning landscapes of Colorado, as seen through the eyes of Anna Fuller. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt observations, Fuller paints a vivid picture of the rugged peaks, vast prairies, and the people who call this land home. From encounters with Native American tribes to the challenges faced by early settlers, this book offers a unique perspective on the history, culture, and natural beauty of Colorado.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Nature and wilderness, Cultural diversity, Exploration and adventure
