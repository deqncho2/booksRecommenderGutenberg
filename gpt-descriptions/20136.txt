Synopsis: 

The Seventeenth Highland Light Infantry (Glasgow Chamber of Commerce Battalion): Record of War Service, 1914-1918 takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of a unique group of soldiers during World War I. This meticulously documented account unveils the untold stories of the brave men who served in the Glasgow Chamber of Commerce Battalion, shedding light on their triumphs, sacrifices, and the indomitable spirit that carried them through the horrors of war. From the trenches of the Western Front to the battlefields of Gallipoli, this book offers a poignant and gripping portrayal of the battalion's remarkable contributions to the war effort.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Resilience
