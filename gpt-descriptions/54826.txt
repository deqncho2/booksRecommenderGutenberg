Synopsis: 
In 'The Mystery at Camp Lenape' by Carl Saxon, a group of friends embarks on a thrilling summer adventure at Camp Lenape. However, their excitement quickly turns to intrigue when they stumble upon a hidden treasure map. As they follow the clues, they uncover a web of secrets and unexpected twists that lead them to a mysterious and dangerous discovery. With time running out, the friends must work together to solve the puzzle and unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Secrets, Courage
