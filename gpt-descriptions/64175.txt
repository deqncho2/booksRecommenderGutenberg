Synopsis: In 'Distress Signal' by Ross Rocklynne, a mysterious signal from deep space sets off a thrilling chain of events as a group of scientists and explorers embark on a dangerous mission to uncover its origin. As they venture into the unknown, they encounter mind-bending phenomena and face life-threatening challenges that push the boundaries of their understanding. With time running out, they must unravel the secrets behind the distress signal before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Survival, the Unknown, Scientific Discovery
