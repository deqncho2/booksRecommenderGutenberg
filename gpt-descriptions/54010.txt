Synopsis: In 'The Younger Sister: A Novel, Vol. I.', we are introduced to the charming and spirited character of Clara, the younger sister of a wealthy family. As she navigates the complexities of society and the expectations placed upon her, Clara finds herself torn between duty and desire. With secrets, scandals, and unexpected twists, this captivating novel takes readers on a journey of love, betrayal, and self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Family dynamics, societal expectations, love and passion, personal growth
