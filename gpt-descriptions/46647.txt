Synopsis: 
In "True Tales of the Weird: a record of personal experiences of the supernatural," Sidney Dickinson takes readers on a spine-chilling journey through a collection of eerie and unexplained encounters. From encounters with ghostly apparitions to inexplicable phenomena, this book delves into the realm of the supernatural, leaving readers questioning the boundaries of reality. As each story unfolds, the author's vivid descriptions and attention to detail draw readers into a world where the line between the natural and the supernatural becomes blurred. Prepare to be captivated by these true tales that will leave you questioning the unknown.

Genres: Supernatural, Horror, Non-fiction

Themes: Supernatural experiences, Ghostly encounters, Unexplained phenomena
