Synopsis: In 'The North Shore Mystery' by Fletcher, Henry, a small coastal town is shaken by a series of unexplained disappearances. As the local detective delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets and hidden motives that lead him to question the trustworthiness of everyone around him. With each twist and turn, the mystery deepens, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the North Shore's dark secrets.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town secrets
