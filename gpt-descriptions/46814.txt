Synopsis:
In 'Gypsy Flight: A Mystery Story for Girls' by Roy J. Snell, a group of adventurous young girls stumble upon a hidden secret during their summer vacation. When they encounter a mysterious gypsy woman and a series of strange occurrences, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling mystery that takes them on a journey filled with danger and intrigue. As they unravel the clues and face unexpected challenges, the girls must rely on their courage and resourcefulness to solve the puzzle and uncover the truth. Will they be able to unravel the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
