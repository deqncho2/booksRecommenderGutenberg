Synopsis: 

In "The development of British landscape painting in water-colours," Finberg and Taylor take readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of a unique art form. From the early pioneers to the modern masters, this book explores the rich history and techniques of British landscape painting in watercolours. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, the authors delve into the artists' inspirations, their experimentation with light and color, and the challenges they faced in capturing the essence of nature on paper. As the narrative unfolds, readers will discover the fascinating stories behind iconic paintings and gain a deeper appreciation for the artists' dedication and innovation. 

Genres: Art History, Non-fiction

Themes: Landscape painting, British art, Evolution of artistic techniques
