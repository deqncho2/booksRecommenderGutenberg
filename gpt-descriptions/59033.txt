Synopsis: 

'Popular Romances of the West of England, Second Series: or, The Drolls, Traditions, and Superstitions of Old Cornwall' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Cornwall, filled with drolls, traditions, and superstitions. This collection of popular romances delves into the rich folklore and magical tales of the region, transporting readers to a realm where mythical creatures, ancient legends, and supernatural occurrences intertwine. From the haunting tales of ghostly apparitions to the heartwarming stories of love and bravery, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the mystical heritage of Cornwall.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Mythology, Tradition, Supernatural, Love, Bravery
