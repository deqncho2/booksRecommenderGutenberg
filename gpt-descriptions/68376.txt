Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 120, December 26, 1914: An uncanny revenge; or, Nick Carter and the mind murderer,' renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving a mysterious mind murderer. As the body count rises and the killer's methods become increasingly bizarre, Carter must rely on his keen intellect and deductive skills to unravel the twisted motives behind this uncanny revenge. With danger lurking at every turn, Nick Carter races against time to stop the mind murderer before more lives are lost.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Revenge, Psychological manipulation
