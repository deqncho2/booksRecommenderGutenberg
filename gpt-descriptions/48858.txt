Synopsis: In "The Expositor's Bible: The Epistle of St Paul to the Romans," H. C. G. Moule delves into the profound and thought-provoking letter written by the apostle Paul to the Romans. Moule's insightful commentary explores the intricate theological concepts and practical applications found within this epistle, shedding light on the timeless truths that continue to shape Christian faith and understanding. With a keen eye for detail and a deep appreciation for the richness of Paul's words, Moule invites readers on a journey of discovery, uncovering the transformative power of God's grace and the profound implications of salvation through faith.

Genres: Biblical commentary, Christian literature, Theology

Themes: Salvation, Grace, Faith, Justification, Sin, Redemption
