Synopsis: In 'The Lost Dahlia' by Mitford, Mary Russell, a renowned detective, is called upon to solve a perplexing case involving a missing heiress, a mysterious dahlia flower, and a web of secrets. As she delves deeper into the investigation, Russell uncovers a world of hidden desires, betrayal, and unexpected connections. With each clue she unravels, the stakes become higher, leading her on a thrilling journey that will test her wit and determination. Will Russell be able to solve the puzzle and find the lost dahlia before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery
