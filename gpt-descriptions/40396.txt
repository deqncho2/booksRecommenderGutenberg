Synopsis: In 'Jack Harvey's Adventures; or, The Rival Campers Among the Oyster Pirates' by Smith, Ruel Perley, young Jack Harvey finds himself caught in a thrilling and dangerous adventure when he stumbles upon a group of oyster pirates. As he becomes entangled in their world, Jack must navigate treacherous waters, outsmart rival campers, and uncover the secrets hidden within the pirate's cove. With his quick wit and determination, Jack's journey takes him on a thrilling ride filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
