Synopsis: In 'Conspiracy on Callisto' by Frederik Pohl, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Victor Hunt discovers a shocking secret hidden beneath the icy surface of Jupiter's moon, Callisto. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of corporate greed, political intrigue, and personal vendettas. With his life on the line, Dr. Hunt must navigate a treacherous landscape of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth and expose the conspiracy that could change the fate of humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration of outer space, Corporate corruption, Political manipulation, Personal sacrifice
