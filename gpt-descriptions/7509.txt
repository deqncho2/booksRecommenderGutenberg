Synopsis: 

The Logbooks of the Lady Nelson: With the journal of her first commander Lieutenant James Grant takes readers on a captivating journey through the logbooks and journal entries of the Lady Nelson, a historic ship that played a significant role in the exploration of Australia. As readers delve into the pages of this remarkable collection, they will uncover tales of adventure, discovery, and the challenges faced by the crew as they navigated uncharted waters. From encounters with indigenous peoples to the perils of treacherous storms, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the life of a ship and its crew during a pivotal period in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Exploration, Maritime

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Cultural encounters, Perseverance, Historical significance
