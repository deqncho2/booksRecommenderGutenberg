Synopsis: In Volume 4 of 'Clarissa Harlowe; or the history of a young lady', the captivating story of Clarissa Harlowe continues to unfold as she faces new challenges and heart-wrenching decisions. As her relationships with those around her become increasingly complex, Clarissa must navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and societal expectations. Will she find the strength to stay true to herself, or will she succumb to the pressures of her circumstances? This volume delves deeper into the emotional turmoil and moral dilemmas that Clarissa faces, leaving readers eagerly anticipating the next chapter of her journey.

Genres: Epistolary novel, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and desire, Morality and virtue, Social expectations and constraints
