Synopsis: In 'By Advice of Counsel' by Arthur Cheney Train, a young lawyer finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he takes on a seemingly routine case. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he uncovers a shocking conspiracy that threatens not only his career but also his life. With the help of a resourceful detective and a mysterious woman, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice. This gripping legal thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they unravel the secrets hidden within the legal system.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Betrayal, Justice
