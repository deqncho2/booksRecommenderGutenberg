Synopsis: 
In 'A History of Southern Utah and Its National Parks (Revised)', Angus M. Woodbury takes readers on an enthralling journey through the captivating landscapes and rich history of Southern Utah. From the ancient Native American civilizations that once thrived in the region to the awe-inspiring geological wonders that have made it a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, this revised edition offers a comprehensive exploration of the area's past and present. With vivid descriptions and fascinating anecdotes, Woodbury paints a vivid picture of the struggles and triumphs that have shaped Southern Utah into the remarkable destination it is today.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: Natural beauty, Cultural heritage, Conservation
