Synopsis: In 'Si Klegg, Book 3: Si and Shorty Meet Mr. Rosenbaum, the Spy, Who Relates His Adventures' by John McElroy, Si and Shorty find themselves in the midst of a thrilling encounter with Mr. Rosenbaum, a mysterious spy. As Mr. Rosenbaum shares his captivating adventures, Si and Shorty become entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. With their quick wit and resourcefulness, the duo must navigate through unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to uncover the truth behind Mr. Rosenbaum's secrets. This action-packed story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Espionage, Loyalty, Deception
