Synopsis: In "Ben Hadden; or, Do Right Whatever Comes Of It" by William Henry Giles Kingston, follow the captivating journey of young Ben Hadden as he navigates the challenges and adventures that life throws his way. From his humble beginnings in a small English village to his encounters with pirates and daring escapades on the high seas, Ben's unwavering determination to always do what is right sets him apart. As he faces numerous trials and tribulations, Ben's character is tested, and he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. This thrilling tale of courage, honor, and self-discovery will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Morality, Integrity, Resilience, Self-discovery
