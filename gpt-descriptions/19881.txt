Synopsis: 
In "Bell's Cathedrals: The Cathedral Church of Carlisle: A Description of Its Fabric and A Brief History of the Episcopal See," C. King Eley takes readers on a captivating journey through the magnificent Cathedral Church of Carlisle. From its awe-inspiring architecture to its rich historical significance, Eley delves into the intricate details of this iconic structure, providing a vivid description of its fabric and an engaging account of the Episcopal See. As readers explore the nooks and crannies of the cathedral, they will uncover fascinating stories and discover the profound impact this place of worship has had on the community throughout the ages.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Architecture

Themes: Religious heritage, Cultural significance, Architectural marvels
