Synopsis: Oblomov, a young Russian nobleman, lives a life of indolence and inertia, rarely leaving his bed or his apartment. He is content to spend his days in a state of dreamy idleness, avoiding any form of responsibility or action. However, when a series of events disrupts his tranquil existence, Oblomov is forced to confront the consequences of his lethargy and make a choice that will determine the course of his life.

Genres: Literary fiction, Satire, Psychological novel

Themes: Indolence and inertia, Social critique, Personal transformation
