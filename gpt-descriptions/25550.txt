Synopsis: In 'The Defiant Agents' by Andre Norton, a group of young people are sent on a dangerous mission to a distant planet, where they must navigate treacherous landscapes and face unknown dangers. As they struggle to survive, they discover ancient secrets that could change the fate of humanity forever. With their lives on the line, they must find the strength to defy the odds and fight for their future.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Coming of Age
