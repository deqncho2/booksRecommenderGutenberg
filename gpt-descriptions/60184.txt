Synopsis: In "The Story of King Arthur and his Knights," Howard Pyle weaves a captivating tale of chivalry, adventure, and destiny. Follow the legendary King Arthur and his valiant knights as they embark on quests, face formidable foes, and strive to uphold the ideals of honor and justice. From the enchanting Lady of the Lake to the treacherous Morgan le Fay, this timeless tale explores the triumphs and tribulations of Arthur's reign, revealing the true measure of a noble heart.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Destiny, Loyalty
