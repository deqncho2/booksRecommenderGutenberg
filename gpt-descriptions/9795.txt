Synopsis: In 'The Four Faces: A Mystery' by William Le Queux, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious woman with four different identities seeks his help. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and danger that spans across continents. With each face revealing a new layer of the puzzle, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of intrigue to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Intrigue
