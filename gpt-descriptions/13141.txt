Synopsis: 
In 'The Princess Priscilla's Fortnight', the spirited and independent Princess Priscilla embarks on a journey to a small English village, where she encounters a cast of eccentric characters and finds herself caught up in a whirlwind of unexpected adventures. From mistaken identities to romantic entanglements, Priscilla's fortnight proves to be anything but ordinary. With wit, charm, and a touch of whimsy, Elizabeth Von Arnim weaves a delightful tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Independence, Identity, Love, Adventure
