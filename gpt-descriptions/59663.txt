Synopsis: 

'Notes on Diseases of Swine, Sheep, Poultry and the Dog: Cause, Symptoms and Treatments' by Korinek, Charles James is a comprehensive guide that delves into the world of animal diseases, providing valuable insights into their causes, symptoms, and treatments. From the mysterious ailments affecting swine to the common afflictions of sheep, poultry, and dogs, this book offers a wealth of knowledge for both veterinarians and animal enthusiasts alike. With a focus on practical solutions and evidence-based approaches, Korinek's work serves as an indispensable resource for anyone seeking to understand and combat animal diseases.

Genres: Non-fiction, Veterinary Medicine, Animal Health

Themes: Animal health, Disease prevention, Veterinary care
