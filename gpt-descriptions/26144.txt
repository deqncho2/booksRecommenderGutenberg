Synopsis: In 'The Unicorn from the Stars and Other Plays', Yeats and Gregory transport readers to a world of enchantment and mystery through a collection of captivating plays. From the tale of a mythical creature seeking its true identity to the exploration of love, power, and the supernatural, these plays delve into the depths of human emotions and the complexities of the human experience. Each play offers a unique and thought-provoking journey, leaving readers spellbound and eager for more.

Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Supernatural

Themes: Identity, Love, Power, Mythology, Transformation
