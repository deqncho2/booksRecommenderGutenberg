Synopsis: In 'Custer's Last Shot; or, The Boy Trailer of the Little Horn', Colonel Travers takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed American West. Set against the backdrop of the infamous Battle of Little Bighorn, the story follows a young boy named Trailer who finds himself caught in the midst of the conflict. As the battle rages on, Trailer must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to survive the chaos and make sense of the devastating events unfolding around him. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale explores the complexities of war, the resilience of the human spirit, and the enduring legacy of one of history's most iconic battles.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: War, Survival, Coming of Age, Heroism
