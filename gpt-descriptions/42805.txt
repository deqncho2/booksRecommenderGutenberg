Synopsis: In 'The History of Little Jack, a Foundling' by Thomas Day, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the life of an abandoned child named Jack. From his humble beginnings as an orphan to his unexpected encounters with various individuals, Jack's story is filled with heart-wrenching moments, unexpected twists, and profound lessons. As he navigates the challenges of his circumstances, Jack's resilience and determination shine through, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets of his past and the possibilities of his future.

Genres: Historical fiction, coming-of-age, adventure

Themes: Identity, resilience, the power of love and friendship
