Synopsis: In "Miss Ashton's New Pupil: A School Girl's Story" by Sarah Stuart Robbins, a young girl named Lucy embarks on a new chapter of her life as she enters a prestigious boarding school. As she navigates the challenges of fitting in and making friends, Lucy discovers secrets and mysteries that lie within the school's walls. With the help of her newfound friends, she sets out on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth, all while learning valuable lessons about friendship, loyalty, and the power of perseverance.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Secrets, Adventure
