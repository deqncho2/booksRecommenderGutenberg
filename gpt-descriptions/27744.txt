Synopsis: In "Remarks on Clarissa," Fielding, Sarah offers a captivating analysis of Samuel Richardson's acclaimed novel, "Clarissa." Through her insightful commentary, she delves into the complex characters, their motivations, and the moral dilemmas they face. With a keen eye for detail, Fielding, Sarah explores the themes of love, virtue, and societal expectations, shedding new light on this beloved literary masterpiece.

Genres: Literary criticism, analysis, commentary

Themes: Love, virtue, societal expectations
