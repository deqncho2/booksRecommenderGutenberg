Synopsis: 
In 'Linda Carlton's Ocean Flight' by Edith Lavell, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as Linda Carlton, a courageous and determined young woman, embarks on a daring journey across the vast ocean. Facing numerous challenges and unexpected encounters, Linda's resilience and resourcefulness are put to the test. As she navigates through treacherous waters and encounters mysterious characters, the story unfolds with suspense and excitement, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Courage, Determination, Exploration
