Synopsis: 
'Alone on an Island' by William Henry Giles Kingston is a thrilling adventure novel that follows the harrowing journey of a young castaway stranded on a deserted island. As the protagonist battles against the elements and faces numerous challenges, the story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, keeping readers on the edge of their seats. Will the castaway find a way to survive and escape the island, or will they be forever alone in this unforgiving wilderness?

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Coming-of-age

Themes: Resilience, Self-discovery, Man vs. Nature
