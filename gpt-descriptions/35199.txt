Synopsis: In 'The Torn Bible; Or, Hubert's Best Friend' by Alice Somerton, a young boy named Hubert discovers a mysterious torn Bible hidden in his attic. As he delves into its pages, he uncovers a captivating tale of adventure, friendship, and a secret that could change his life forever. With each turn of the page, Hubert becomes more entangled in the story, blurring the lines between reality and fiction. As he navigates through the twists and turns of the torn Bible, Hubert must confront his own fears and make choices that will shape his destiny. This enchanting tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth hidden within the pages of the torn Bible.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Coming of Age
