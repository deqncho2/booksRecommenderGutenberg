Synopsis: In "The Guest Rites" by Robert Silverberg, a renowned anthropologist embarks on a daring expedition to a remote planet inhabited by a mysterious alien race. As he delves deeper into their culture, he becomes entangled in a web of ancient rituals and forbidden knowledge that could have profound consequences for both him and the alien civilization. With each encounter, he uncovers shocking secrets that challenge his understanding of humanity and the universe itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Cultural exploration, Forbidden knowledge, Identity, Morality
