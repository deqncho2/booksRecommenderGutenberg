Synopsis: In 'The Altar Steps' by Compton MacKenzie, a young man named Richard Gunn is torn between his desire to become a priest and his growing infatuation with a captivating woman. Set in the early 20th century, this gripping novel explores the complexities of faith, love, and the struggle to find one's true calling. As Richard navigates the challenges of his spiritual journey, he must confront the consequences of his choices and the conflicts that arise from his divided heart.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Faith and doubt, Love and desire, Identity and self-discovery
