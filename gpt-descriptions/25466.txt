Synopsis: In 'Little Bobtail; or, The Wreck of the Penobscot,' we follow the thrilling adventures of young Bobtail as he finds himself caught in a treacherous storm at sea. As the Penobscot battles against the raging elements, Bobtail must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to survive. With danger lurking at every turn, will Bobtail be able to overcome the perils of the deep and make it out alive?

Genres: Adventure, Action, Survival

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Survival
