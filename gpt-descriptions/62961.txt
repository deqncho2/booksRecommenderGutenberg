Synopsis: In 'The Tyranny of Shams' by Joseph McCabe, a renowned psychologist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and deception when he is called upon to evaluate a patient with a peculiar condition. As he delves deeper into the patient's mind, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens to unravel his own sanity. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning what is real and what is merely a figment of the imagination.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Perception vs. reality, Identity, Mental health
