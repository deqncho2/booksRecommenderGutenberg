Synopsis: In 'Cynthia Wakeham's Money' by Anna Katharine Green, a wealthy heiress mysteriously disappears, leaving behind a substantial fortune. As her family and friends search for answers, they uncover a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the mystery, questioning the motives of those closest to Cynthia and wondering what truly happened to her. This gripping tale of intrigue and suspense will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Crime

Themes: Wealth and power, Family dynamics, Deception and betrayal
