Synopsis: In 'The Rat-Pit' by Patrick MacGill, follow the gripping tale of a young Irishman named Johnnie who finds himself trapped in the dark underbelly of Glasgow's slums. As he navigates the treacherous world of poverty, crime, and despair, Johnnie's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to escape the clutches of the rat-pit and find a way to a better life?

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Social Realism

Themes: Poverty, Survival, Social Injustice
