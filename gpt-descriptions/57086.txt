Synopsis: In 'A Strange World: A Novel. Volume 1 (of 3)', the reader is transported to a mysterious and captivating world where secrets lurk around every corner. The story follows the life of a young woman named Evelyn, who finds herself entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. As she navigates through a society filled with enigmatic characters and hidden agendas, Evelyn must uncover the truth behind the strange occurrences that surround her. With each twist and turn, the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, leaving the reader questioning what is real and what is merely illusion.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery, Perception vs. reality
