Synopsis: 
The Children's Book of Thanksgiving Stories is a heartwarming collection of tales that will transport young readers into the spirit of gratitude and celebration. From the adventures of a mischievous turkey to the heartwarming encounters between diverse characters, each story captures the essence of Thanksgiving in its own unique way. Through these enchanting narratives, children will learn the importance of friendship, family, and giving thanks, while also discovering the joy of coming together to celebrate this special holiday.

Genres: Children's literature, Fiction, Holiday

Themes: Gratitude, Friendship, Family, Celebration
