Synopsis: In "Henry Horn's X-Ray Eye Glasses" by Dwight V. Swain, a young boy named Henry discovers a pair of extraordinary glasses that grant him the ability to see through objects and people. As Henry explores the world with his newfound power, he uncovers a hidden world of secrets and mysteries that he never could have imagined. However, as he delves deeper into this extraordinary ability, Henry soon realizes that there are consequences to seeing what others cannot. With danger lurking around every corner, Henry must navigate a treacherous path to protect himself and those he cares about.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Power and responsibility, Curiosity and exploration, Secrets and hidden truths
