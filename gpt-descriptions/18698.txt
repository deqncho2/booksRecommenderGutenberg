Synopsis: In 'The Recitation' by George Herbert Betts, a mysterious recitation takes place in a small town, captivating the residents and leaving them questioning their own beliefs and desires. As the recitation unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the boundaries between reality and illusion blur. With each passing moment, the townspeople become entangled in a web of intrigue and self-discovery, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Truth vs. Perception, Morality, Human Nature
