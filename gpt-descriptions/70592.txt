Synopsis: In the second volume of "Elizabeth Montagu, the queen of the bluestockings," readers delve into the captivating correspondence of Elizabeth Montagu, a prominent figure in 18th-century British society. Spanning from 1720 to 1761, this collection of letters offers a fascinating glimpse into Montagu's relationships, intellectual pursuits, and the social and cultural landscape of the time. As Montagu navigates the complexities of her personal life and engages in lively discussions with renowned thinkers and writers, readers are transported to a world of wit, intellect, and societal change.

Genres: Biography, Epistolary, Historical

Themes: Intellectualism, Gender roles, Enlightenment ideals, Social dynamics
