Synopsis: In "ABC of the Steel Square and Its Uses," Fred T. Hodgson takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of carpentry and construction. Through clear and concise explanations, accompanied by detailed illustrations, Hodgson unveils the secrets of the steel square, a versatile tool that holds the key to countless possibilities in the realm of woodworking. From measuring and marking to complex calculations and intricate designs, this comprehensive guide equips both beginners and seasoned craftsmen with the knowledge and skills to master the art of carpentry. Whether you're a DIY enthusiast or a professional builder, this book will unlock a whole new level of precision and creativity in your woodworking projects.

Genres: Non-fiction, DIY, Carpentry

Themes: Woodworking, Construction techniques, Precision craftsmanship
