Synopsis: In 'Bring the Jubilee' by Moore, Ward, the protagonist, Hodge Backmaker, finds himself transported to an alternate version of history where the Confederacy won the American Civil War. As Hodge navigates this new reality, he becomes entangled in a secret society's quest to alter the course of history once again. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, Hodge must confront the consequences of tampering with the past and the power of individual agency.

Genres: Science Fiction, Alternate History, Dystopian Fiction

Themes: Time Travel, Historical Consequences, Individual Agency
