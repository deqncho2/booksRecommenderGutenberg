Synopsis: In "Defending the Island: A story of Bar Harbor in 1758" by James Otis, readers are transported to the captivating world of 18th-century Bar Harbor. Set during the French and Indian War, this historical fiction novel follows the lives of the island's inhabitants as they face the imminent threat of invasion. As tensions rise and danger looms, the characters must navigate treacherous waters, forge unlikely alliances, and make sacrifices in order to protect their beloved island. With vivid descriptions and compelling storytelling, Otis paints a vivid picture of a community united in the face of adversity, showcasing the resilience and courage of ordinary people in extraordinary circumstances.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Community, Resilience, Sacrifice
