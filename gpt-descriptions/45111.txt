Synopsis: In "The Anglo-Saxon Century and the Unification of the English-Speaking People," Dos Passos takes readers on a captivating journey through history, exploring the rise of the English-speaking world and its profound impact on global affairs. From the early days of the Anglo-Saxon tribes to the modern era, Dos Passos weaves together a rich tapestry of events, personalities, and cultural shifts that shaped the English-speaking nations. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, he delves into the triumphs and struggles of these nations, revealing the forces that propelled them to greatness and the challenges they faced along the way. This thought-provoking work offers a fresh perspective on the historical forces that have shaped our world today.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Cultural identity, Global influence, Historical legacy
