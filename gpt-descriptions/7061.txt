Synopsis: In "A March on London: Being a Story of Wat Tyler's Insurrection," G. A. Henty takes us back to 14th-century England, where a young nobleman finds himself caught up in the tumultuous events of the Peasants' Revolt. As the rebellion led by the charismatic Wat Tyler gains momentum, our protagonist must navigate treacherous political alliances, face personal challenges, and make difficult choices that will shape the fate of the nation. With vivid historical detail and gripping action, this thrilling adventure brings to life a pivotal moment in English history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Rebellion, Social Injustice, Loyalty, Leadership
