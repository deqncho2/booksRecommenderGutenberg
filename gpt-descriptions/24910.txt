Synopsis: In 'The Last Woman' by Beeckman Ross, a mysterious virus has wiped out the entire female population, leaving only one woman alive. As society struggles to adapt to this unprecedented situation, the last woman finds herself at the center of a power struggle between those who see her as a symbol of hope and those who view her as a threat. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, she must navigate a treacherous world where survival and love become intertwined in unexpected ways.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Gender, Power, Survival, Love
