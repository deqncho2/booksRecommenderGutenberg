Synopsis: 

In "The Present State of Hayti (Saint Domingo) with Remarks on its Agriculture, Commerce, Laws, Religion, Finances, and Population," James Franklin takes readers on a captivating journey to the Caribbean island of Hayti. As a merchant, Franklin provides a unique perspective on the island's various aspects, offering intriguing insights into its agriculture, commerce, laws, religion, finances, and population. Through his vivid descriptions and astute observations, Franklin paints a vivid picture of the island's present state, shedding light on its vibrant culture and complex societal dynamics. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Travelogue, Historical

Themes: Colonialism, Cultural diversity, Economic development, Social dynamics
