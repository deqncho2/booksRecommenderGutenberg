Synopsis: 
"The Soldier Boy; or, Tom Somers in the Army: A Story of the Great Rebellion" follows the journey of Tom Somers, a young and courageous boy who enlists in the army during the Great Rebellion. As he navigates the challenges and dangers of war, Tom's character is tested, and he must find strength and resilience within himself. Along the way, he forms deep bonds with his fellow soldiers and experiences the harsh realities of battle. This gripping tale explores the sacrifices and bravery of those who fought during a tumultuous time in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Friendship, Resilience
