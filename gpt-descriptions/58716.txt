Synopsis: 
"The Day of Doom; Or, A Poetical Description of the Great and Last Judgment: With Other Poems" by Michael Wigglesworth is a captivating and thought-provoking work that delves into the final judgment of humanity. Through vivid and powerful poetry, the author explores the consequences of one's actions and the ultimate fate that awaits us all. This collection of poems takes readers on a journey through the realms of heaven and hell, painting a haunting picture of the afterlife and the moral choices that shape our destiny. Prepare to be enthralled by Wigglesworth's masterful storytelling and profound insights into the human condition.

Genres: Religious poetry, Apocalyptic literature, Puritan literature
Themes: Judgment, Salvation, Sin, Redemption
