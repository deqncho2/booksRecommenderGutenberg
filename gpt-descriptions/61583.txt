Synopsis: 

In 'Daybreak in Turkey: Second Edition', James L. Barton takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and enigmatic country of Turkey. With vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, Barton explores the rich history, diverse culture, and breathtaking landscapes that make Turkey a truly unique destination. From the bustling streets of Istanbul to the ancient ruins of Ephesus, readers will be immersed in the beauty and complexity of this fascinating country. As Barton delves into the intricacies of Turkish society and its people, he uncovers hidden gems and untold stories that will leave readers yearning to experience Turkey for themselves.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Cultural exploration, Adventure, Discovery
