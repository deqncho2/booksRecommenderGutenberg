Synopsis: In 'Merry's Book of Puzzles' by J. N. Stearns, readers are invited to join Merry on an enchanting journey through a world of mind-bending puzzles and riddles. As Merry delves deeper into the mysterious puzzles, she uncovers hidden secrets and embarks on a thrilling adventure that will challenge her wit and determination. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the cleverness and complexity of the puzzles, eagerly trying to solve them alongside Merry. But as the stakes rise and the puzzles become more intricate, will Merry be able to unravel the ultimate mystery that lies at the heart of the book?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Puzzle

Themes: Curiosity, Problem-solving, Discovery
