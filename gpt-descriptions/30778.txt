Synopsis: In 'Rhymes and Meters: A Practical Manual for Versifiers' by Winslow Horatio, embark on a captivating journey into the world of poetry and learn the secrets behind crafting beautiful verses. This practical manual serves as a comprehensive guide, offering invaluable techniques and insights to aspiring poets and seasoned versifiers alike. From the intricacies of rhyme schemes to the art of meter, Winslow Horatio delves into the nuances of poetic composition, providing step-by-step instructions and inspiring examples. With its engaging prose and enlightening exercises, this book is a must-read for anyone seeking to unlock their poetic potential.

Genres: Non-fiction, Poetry, Writing Guide

Themes: Poetry composition, Rhyme and meter techniques, Artistic expression
