Synopsis: 
'Fifteen Thousand Useful Phrases' is a comprehensive handbook that offers a vast collection of expressions, similes, and terms to enhance speech and writing in the English language. Written by Grenville Kleiser, this practical guide is designed to improve the vocabulary and communication skills of readers, writers, and speakers alike. With its wide range of literary, commercial, conversational, and oratorical terms, this book serves as an invaluable resource for anyone seeking to embellish their speech and literature. 

Genres: Reference, Language, Communication

Themes: Vocabulary enhancement, Communication skills, Literary embellishment
