Synopsis: 
'The City of Dreadful Night' by James Thomson is a haunting and atmospheric poem that takes readers on a journey through a desolate city plagued by darkness and despair. The narrator, tormented by his own existential crisis, wanders through the city's gloomy streets, encountering various characters who embody the city's bleakness. As the night unfolds, the poem delves into themes of isolation, nihilism, and the search for meaning in a world devoid of hope.

Genres: Poetry, Dark Romanticism, Symbolism

Themes: Isolation, Nihilism, Despair, Existentialism
