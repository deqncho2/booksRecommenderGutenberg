Synopsis: In 'The Girl from Arizona' by Nina Rhoades, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she discovers a hidden secret about her past. As she embarks on a journey to uncover the truth, she becomes entangled with a group of enigmatic individuals who may hold the key to her identity. With each revelation, Emily's world is turned upside down, forcing her to confront her own fears and question everything she thought she knew. As the stakes rise and the danger intensifies, Emily must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to unravel the truth behind her origins.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
