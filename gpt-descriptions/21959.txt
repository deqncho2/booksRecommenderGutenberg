Synopsis:
'Letters from a Self-Made Merchant to His Son' is a collection of humorous and insightful letters written by John Graham, a successful pork-packer in Chicago, to his son Pierrepont. Through these letters, John imparts his wisdom and life lessons to his son, offering advice on various topics such as business, relationships, and personal growth. The letters provide a unique perspective on the challenges and triumphs of a self-made man, while also showcasing the endearing and humorous relationship between father and son.

Genres:
Epistolary fiction, Humor, Business

Themes:
Success and ambition, Family and relationships, Personal growth
