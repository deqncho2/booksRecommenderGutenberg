Synopsis:
In 'A Mere Chance: A Novel. Vol. 1' by Cambridge, Ada, we are introduced to the intriguing world of Lady Evelyn, a young woman with a mysterious past and an uncertain future. Set in the backdrop of Victorian England, Lady Evelyn finds herself caught in a web of secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances. As she navigates through the treacherous waters of high society, she must confront her own desires and ambitions, all while trying to uncover the truth about her own identity. With each turn of the page, readers will be captivated by the intricate plot twists and the complex characters that populate this enthralling tale.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Society, Ambition
