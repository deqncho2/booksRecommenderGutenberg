Synopsis: In the remote coastal town of Port Haven, a mysterious shipwreck washes ashore, bringing with it a dark secret that threatens to unravel the lives of its inhabitants. As the townspeople grapple with the enigmatic presence, a young woman named Amelia embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind the shipwreck and the haunting melodies that echo from the depths of the sea. With each step closer to the truth, Amelia finds herself entangled in a web of ancient legends, forbidden love, and the power of forgiveness. 'Where Deep Seas Moan' is a captivating tale of mystery and redemption that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, the power of music, redemption and forgiveness
