Synopsis: In "Children of the Lens" by E.E. Smith, a group of extraordinary siblings with incredible mental powers must confront a powerful alien race that threatens the entire galaxy. As they navigate through treacherous alliances and dangerous missions, the siblings discover shocking truths about their own origins and the true nature of their powers. With the fate of the universe hanging in the balance, they must harness their unique abilities and face their greatest challenge yet.

Genres: Science Fiction, Space Opera, Adventure

Themes: Family bonds, Evolution, Power and responsibility
