Synopsis: In 'The Dop Doctor' by Dehan, Richard, a brilliant doctor finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is unexpectedly thrust into the world of illegal drugs. As he navigates through a treacherous underworld, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that could have far-reaching consequences. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Morality, Redemption, Consequences
