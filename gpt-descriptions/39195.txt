Synopsis: 

'Legends of Gods and Ghosts (Hawaiian Mythology): Collected and Translated from the Hawaiian' takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich and mystical world of Hawaiian mythology. Through a collection of enchanting tales, author W. D. Westervelt unveils the ancient legends of gods and ghosts that have shaped the cultural fabric of Hawaii. From epic battles between deities to encounters with mischievous spirits, these stories offer a glimpse into a realm where the supernatural intertwines with everyday life. As readers delve into the depths of Hawaiian mythology, they will be transported to a world filled with awe-inspiring legends and timeless wisdom.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Cultural Studies

Themes: Supernatural beings, Cultural heritage, Oral tradition, Spiritual beliefs
