Synopsis: 
"The Shadow On The Dial, and Other Essays: 1909" is a collection of thought-provoking essays by Ambrose Bierce that delve into various aspects of society, culture, and human nature. From exploring the complexities of time and its influence on our lives to dissecting the illusions of power and the consequences of war, Bierce's essays offer a unique perspective on the world around us. With his characteristic wit and sharp observations, he challenges conventional wisdom and invites readers to question their own beliefs and assumptions. 

Genres: Essay collection, Non-fiction, Satire

Themes: Time, Society, Power, War, Human nature
