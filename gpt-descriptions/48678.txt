Synopsis: 

In 'Dutch Bulbs and Gardens' by Silberrad, Una L./ Lyall, Sophie, a young woman named Emma finds herself captivated by the enchanting world of Dutch bulbs and gardens. Drawn into a mesmerizing journey, she embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets behind these vibrant and mysterious flowers. As Emma delves deeper into the history and cultivation of Dutch bulbs, she discovers a hidden world filled with passion, beauty, and unexpected connections. Along the way, she encounters a cast of intriguing characters who share their knowledge and stories, leading her to question her own perceptions of love, growth, and the power of nature. 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Love, Nature, Growth, History, Personal Transformation
