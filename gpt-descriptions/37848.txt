Synopsis: 
'Old English Chronicles' is a captivating historical fiction novel that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous times of early medieval England. Set against the backdrop of warring kingdoms, political intrigue, and the rise of Christianity, this epic tale follows the lives of several interconnected characters as they navigate the treacherous waters of power, love, and loyalty. From battles on the battlefield to secret alliances in the shadows, the story weaves together a tapestry of adventure, romance, and betrayal that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Religion, Love and Betrayal
