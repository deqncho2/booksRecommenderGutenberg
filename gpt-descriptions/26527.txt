Synopsis: In the remote mountains of Kentucky, a young woman named Judith struggles to find her place in a world filled with poverty, violence, and the constant threat of feuds. As she navigates the challenges of her harsh environment, Judith's determination and resilience are put to the test. With her fiery spirit and unwavering love for her family, she becomes a symbol of hope and strength for her community. But when a devastating tragedy strikes, Judith must summon all her courage to protect those she holds dear and fight for justice in a world that seems determined to keep her down.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Resilience, Family, Social Injustice, Survival
