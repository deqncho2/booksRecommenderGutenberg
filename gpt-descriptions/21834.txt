Synopsis: In 'The Black Colonel' by James Milne, a mysterious and enigmatic figure known as the Black Colonel arrives in a small town, captivating the locals with his charm and charisma. As the townspeople become increasingly fascinated by the Colonel, they soon discover that there is more to him than meets the eye. Secrets, hidden agendas, and unexpected twists unfold, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Power
