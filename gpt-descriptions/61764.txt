Synopsis: In 'Tennyson', G. K. Chesterton and Richard Garnett delve into the life and works of the renowned poet Alfred Lord Tennyson. Through their insightful analysis, they explore the enigmatic nature of Tennyson's poetry, his unique perspective on the world, and the profound impact he had on the Victorian era. This captivating biography offers a fascinating glimpse into the mind of one of the greatest poets of all time.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Poetry, Victorian Era, Artistic Expression
