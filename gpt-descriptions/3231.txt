Synopsis: In 'The Rifle and the Hound in Ceylon', Sir Samuel White Baker takes us on an exhilarating adventure through the untamed wilderness of Ceylon. Set in the 19th century, this gripping tale follows the exploits of a fearless hunter and his loyal hound as they navigate treacherous terrain, encounter dangerous wildlife, and face unexpected challenges. As they embark on their quest, they uncover secrets that will test their courage and resilience, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Loyalty, Man vs. Nature
