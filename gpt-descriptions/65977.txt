Synopsis: In "Time Grabber" by Gordon R. Dickson, a brilliant scientist invents a device that can manipulate time, allowing him to travel to different eras and alter history. However, when the device falls into the wrong hands, it becomes a dangerous weapon that threatens the very fabric of time itself. As the scientist races against time to stop the malevolent forces, he must confront the ethical implications of his creation and make a choice that could change everything.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Time Travel

Themes: Ethics, Consequences of Technology, Power and Responsibility
