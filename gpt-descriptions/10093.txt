Synopsis: In 'Gutta-Percha Willie: The Working Genius' by George MacDonald, follow the extraordinary journey of young Willie, a boy with a unique talent for invention and problem-solving. Set in the bustling streets of Victorian London, Willie's remarkable abilities catch the attention of a wealthy benefactor who offers him an opportunity that could change his life forever. As Willie navigates the challenges and triumphs of his newfound success, he must also confront the complexities of friendship, loyalty, and the true meaning of genius. 

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Ingenuity, Ambition, Friendship, Social Class
