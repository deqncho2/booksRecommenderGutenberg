Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of William Butler Yeats' is a comprehensive collection of the literary works of the renowned Irish poet and playwright, William Butler Yeats. This anthology showcases Yeats' diverse range of writings, including poetry, plays, essays, and autobiographical works. From his early lyrical poems to his later mystical and symbolic works, this index provides a captivating journey through Yeats' profound exploration of love, mythology, Irish folklore, and the complexities of the human condition.

Genres: Poetry, Drama, Essays, Autobiography

Themes: Love, Mythology, Irish folklore, Human condition
