Synopsis: In 'Dawn of the Demigods' by Raymond Z. Gallun, a group of ordinary individuals suddenly find themselves bestowed with extraordinary powers, transforming them into demigods. As they navigate their newfound abilities and grapple with the consequences of their actions, they must confront a looming threat that could plunge the world into chaos. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, these demigods must learn to harness their powers and unite against a formidable enemy, all while questioning the nature of their own humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Action

Themes: Power and Responsibility, Identity and Self-Discovery, Good vs. Evil
