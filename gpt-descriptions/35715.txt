Synopsis: In 'The Early History of the Colonial Post-Office' by Mary Emma Woolley, embark on a captivating journey through time to uncover the fascinating origins of the colonial post-office system. Delve into the challenges faced by early settlers as they sought to establish a reliable means of communication across vast distances. From daring postal riders to the establishment of post offices in remote corners of the New World, this historical account unveils the untold stories behind the development of a crucial institution that shaped the course of American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Communication, Exploration, Colonial America
