Synopsis: 

'Legends & Romances of Brittany' by Lewis Spence is a captivating collection of enchanting tales that transport readers to the mystical land of Brittany. From ancient legends of heroic knights and mythical creatures to hauntingly beautiful romances, this book weaves together a tapestry of folklore and imagination. Discover the secrets of the ancient druids, encounter the legendary King Arthur and his knights, and delve into the passionate love stories that have been passed down through generations. With each turn of the page, readers will be immersed in a world of magic, adventure, and timeless tales that will leave them longing for more.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Mythology, Chivalry, Love and Destiny
