Synopsis: In 'The Headless Horseman: A Strange Tale of Texas' by Reid, Mayne, a young man named Maurice Gerald finds himself entangled in a series of mysterious events after encountering a headless horseman on the Texan plains. As he delves deeper into the enigma, Maurice uncovers a web of secrets, danger, and supernatural occurrences that will test his courage and unravel the truth behind the haunting legend.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Legends and folklore, Courage and bravery, Supernatural phenomena
