Synopsis: 

In "Story of the automobile: Its history and development from 1760 to 1917: With an analysis of the standing and prospects of the automobile industry," H. L. Barber takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the evolution of the automobile. From its humble beginnings in the 18th century to its rapid advancements in the early 20th century, Barber delves into the fascinating stories, innovations, and challenges that shaped the automobile industry. With meticulous research and captivating storytelling, this book offers a comprehensive analysis of the standing and prospects of the automobile industry, leaving readers with a newfound appreciation for the impact of this revolutionary invention.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Innovation, Industrialization, Progress, Economic Impact
