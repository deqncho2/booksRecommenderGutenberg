Synopsis: In "The Radio Girls on the program: $b or, Singing and reciting at the sending station" by Margaret Penrose, we follow the captivating journey of a group of talented young women who find themselves at the forefront of the exciting world of radio broadcasting. As they navigate the challenges and triumphs of working in a male-dominated industry, they uncover a web of secrets and mysteries that could change the course of their lives forever. With their voices reaching millions of listeners, the radio girls must use their wit, determination, and friendship to unravel the truth and make their mark in this thrilling tale of adventure and intrigue.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Women's Fiction

Themes: Gender equality, Friendship, Pursuit of dreams, Secrets and deception
