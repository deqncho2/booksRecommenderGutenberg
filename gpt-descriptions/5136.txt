Synopsis: 
In 'Around the World on a Bicycle - Volume I: From San Francisco to Teheran', Thomas Stevens takes readers on an exhilarating journey across continents, as he embarks on an audacious adventure to become the first person to circumnavigate the globe on a bicycle. From the bustling streets of San Francisco to the rugged terrains of Asia, Stevens encounters breathtaking landscapes, encounters with diverse cultures, and unexpected challenges that test his determination and resilience. As he pedals through vast deserts, treacherous mountains, and bustling cities, Stevens offers a captivating account of his extraordinary expedition, filled with thrilling encounters, humorous anecdotes, and profound reflections on the human spirit.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Cultural Exchange
