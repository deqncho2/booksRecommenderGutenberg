Synopsis: In 'The Carpenter's Daughter', a young woman named Ruth finds herself torn between duty and desire when she falls in love with a man from a different social class. As she navigates the challenges of societal expectations and personal aspirations, Ruth must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge the norms of her time.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Social class, Love and sacrifice, Gender roles, Self-discovery
