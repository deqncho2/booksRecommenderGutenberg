Synopsis: In 'A Bad Day for Vermin' by Keith Laumer, a seemingly ordinary day takes a bizarre turn when a man discovers an unexpected ability to communicate with insects. As he navigates this newfound power, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the smallest creatures hold the key to a much larger mystery. With his life on the line, he must unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Communication, Power, Intrigue
