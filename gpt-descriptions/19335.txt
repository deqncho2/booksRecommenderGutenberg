Synopsis: In 'Oscar the Detective; Or, Dudie Dunne, The Exquisite Detective' by Old Sleuth, readers are introduced to the enigmatic and cunning detective, Oscar. When a perplexing case involving a missing heiress and a mysterious secret society emerges, Oscar is called upon to unravel the truth. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected alliances. With his sharp intellect and unyielding determination, Oscar must navigate a treacherous world of crime and deception to bring justice to those who deserve it. Will he succeed in solving the case, or will he become entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Detective work, Deception, Justice
