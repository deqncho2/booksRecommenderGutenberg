Synopsis: In 'The Romance of Names' by Ernest Weekley, embark on a captivating linguistic journey that unveils the hidden stories behind the names we use every day. From the origins of common names to the fascinating tales behind historical figures and places, this book delves into the rich tapestry of human language and its connection to our collective history. With a blend of wit, curiosity, and meticulous research, Weekley invites readers to explore the captivating world of names and discover the surprising narratives they hold.

Genres: Non-fiction, Linguistics, History

Themes: Language and culture, Etymology, Historical significance of names
