Synopsis:
In the bustling city of The Hague, Eline Vere, a young and beautiful woman, finds herself trapped in a world of societal expectations and personal desires. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and her own inner turmoil, Eline becomes entangled in a web of passion, longing, and self-destruction. Will she find the happiness she so desperately seeks, or will her own demons consume her?

Genres:
Realistic fiction, Psychological fiction, Romance

Themes:
Societal expectations, Love and desire, Self-destruction, Inner turmoil
