Synopsis: 

In "Helping Himself; Or, Grant Thornton's Ambition" by Horatio Alger, Jr., we follow the journey of Grant Thornton, a young and determined protagonist who is determined to rise above his humble beginnings. Grant's ambition leads him to leave his small town and venture into the bustling city, where he encounters numerous challenges and opportunities. With his unwavering determination and resourcefulness, Grant navigates through the trials and tribulations of urban life, all while striving to achieve his dreams. Will Grant's ambition be enough to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Rags-to-riches

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Social mobility
