Synopsis: In 'A Practical Physiology: A Text-Book for Higher Schools', Albert F. Blaisdell takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate workings of the human body. With a blend of scientific knowledge and engaging storytelling, this book delves into the fascinating world of physiology, exploring the mysteries of the human body's functions, systems, and interactions. From the beating of the heart to the complexities of the nervous system, Blaisdell's practical approach and vivid descriptions bring the subject to life, making it accessible and captivating for readers of all ages.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Education

Themes: Human anatomy, Physiology, Scientific exploration
