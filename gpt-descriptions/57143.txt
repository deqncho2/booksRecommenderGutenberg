Synopsis: 

In 'History of Greece, Volume 02 (of 12)', George Grote continues his captivating exploration of ancient Greece, delving into the tumultuous period from the Persian Wars to the rise of Athens as a dominant power. As the city-states grapple with external threats and internal conflicts, Grote skillfully weaves together the stories of legendary figures such as Themistocles, Aristides, and Pausanias, offering a vivid account of their triumphs and struggles. From the epic battles of Marathon and Thermopylae to the political intrigues of the Delian League, this volume immerses readers in the rich tapestry of Greek history, shedding light on the complex dynamics that shaped the destiny of this remarkable civilization.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient Greece, Persian Wars, Athenian democracy, Leadership, War and conflict, Political intrigue
