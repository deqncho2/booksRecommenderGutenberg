Synopsis: In the mysterious world of 'Poetry of the Supernatural', a young poet discovers a hidden collection of poems that possess an otherworldly power. As she delves deeper into the verses, she becomes entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and encounters with enigmatic beings. With each poem she unravels, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leading her on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the ancient verses and their connection to her own existence.

Genres: Supernatural, Mystery, Poetry

Themes: The power of words, the thin veil between reality and the supernatural, self-discovery
