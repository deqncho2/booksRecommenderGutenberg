Synopsis: In "Aunt Caroline's Dixieland Recipes," Emma and William McKinney invite readers into the heart of the American South, where they uncover a treasure trove of mouthwatering recipes passed down through generations. From savory dishes like gumbo and jambalaya to delectable desserts like pecan pie and peach cobbler, this culinary journey through Dixieland is a celebration of the rich flavors and traditions that define Southern cuisine. Alongside the recipes, the authors share anecdotes and stories that bring the vibrant culture and history of the region to life. Get ready to savor the tastes of the South and embark on a culinary adventure that will leave you hungry for more.

Genres: Cookbook, Food and Drink, Southern Cooking

Themes: Southern culture, Family traditions, Culinary heritage
