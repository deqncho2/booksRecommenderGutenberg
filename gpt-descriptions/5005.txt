Synopsis: 
'Journal of an Overland Expedition in Australia: From Moreton Bay to Port Essington, a distance of upwards of 3000 miles, during the years 1844-1845' follows the remarkable journey of Ludwig Leichhardt, an adventurous explorer, as he embarks on a treacherous expedition across the vast and untamed Australian continent. Facing numerous challenges and dangers, Leichhardt and his team navigate through harsh landscapes, encounter indigenous tribes, and battle against the harsh elements. This gripping account offers a firsthand perspective of the hardships and triumphs experienced during this daring exploration, captivating readers with its vivid descriptions and captivating narrative.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural Encounter
