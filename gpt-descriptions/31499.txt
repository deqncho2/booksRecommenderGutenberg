Synopsis: In "A Campfire Girl's Happiness" by Jane L. Stewart, follow the captivating journey of a young girl named Betty, who finds herself in a world of adventure and self-discovery during a summer camp. As she navigates through the challenges and joys of camp life, Betty forms deep friendships, uncovers hidden talents, and learns valuable life lessons that will shape her future. With each campfire tale and outdoor escapade, Betty's happiness blossoms, leaving readers eager to join her in this heartwarming coming-of-age story.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Friendship

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Adventure, Personal growth
