Synopsis: In "Threads gathered up: A sequel to Virgie's Inheritance," Georgie Sheldon takes readers on a captivating journey as the lives of the characters from "Virgie's Inheritance" continue to intertwine. Secrets are revealed, unexpected alliances are formed, and the threads of their lives are gathered up, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Redemption, Love and forgiveness
