Synopsis: In this captivating lecture, John N. Luff takes us on a journey through the fascinating world of philately, revealing the hidden stories and secrets behind postage stamps. From the origins of stamp collecting to the intricate details that make each stamp unique, Luff explores the art, history, and cultural significance of this beloved hobby. Prepare to be enthralled by the tales of rare stamps, famous collectors, and the unexpected connections that can be found within the pages of a stamp album.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Hobbies

Themes: Philately, Collecting, Cultural Significance
