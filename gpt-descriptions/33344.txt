Synopsis: 
In "Narrative of Mr. John Dodge during his Captivity at Detroit," John Dodge recounts his harrowing experiences as a captive in Detroit during the early 19th century. From his capture by Native Americans to his eventual escape, Dodge's narrative takes readers on a gripping journey filled with danger, resilience, and unexpected alliances. As he navigates the complexities of captivity, Dodge's story sheds light on the clash of cultures, the human will to survive, and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: 
Cultural Conflict, Survival, Resilience, Hope
