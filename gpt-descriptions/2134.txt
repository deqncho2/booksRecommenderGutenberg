Synopsis: In 'Utopia of Usurers and Other Essays', G.K. Chesterton takes readers on a thought-provoking journey through a collection of essays that challenge the prevailing notions of progress, capitalism, and modernity. With his characteristic wit and insight, Chesterton explores the paradoxes and pitfalls of a society obsessed with efficiency, materialism, and the pursuit of profit. From critiquing the dehumanizing effects of industrialization to questioning the true meaning of progress, these essays offer a refreshing and often contrarian perspective on the modern world. 

Genres: Essay, Social Criticism, Philosophy

Themes: Capitalism, Progress, Materialism, Industrialization, Humanism
