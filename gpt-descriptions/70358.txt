Synopsis:
Bunny Brown and his sister Sue are in for an exciting adventure when they discover a talented trick dog named Wango. As they train Wango to perform amazing tricks, the siblings find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected events that test their courage and determination. With their new furry friend by their side, Bunny and Sue embark on a journey filled with laughter, friendship, and heartwarming surprises.

Genres:
Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes:
Friendship, Courage, Determination
