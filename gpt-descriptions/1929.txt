Synopsis: In the high society of 18th-century London, a group of wealthy and influential individuals indulge in scandalous gossip, deceit, and manipulation. Amidst the intricate web of rumors and secrets, the true characters of these socialites are revealed, as they navigate love, loyalty, and the consequences of their actions. With witty dialogue and sharp satire, "The School for Scandal" takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricacies of human nature and the allure of scandal.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Drama

Themes: Deception, Appearance vs. Reality, Social Criticism
