Synopsis: In the midst of the bustling city of London, a devastating plague and a catastrophic fire threaten to consume everything in their path. Against this backdrop, a tale unfolds within the walls of the ancient and majestic Old Saint Paul's Cathedral. Filled with mystery, romance, and intrigue, the lives of a diverse cast of characters intertwine as they navigate the chaos and uncertainty of these harrowing times. From the noble to the destitute, from the righteous to the corrupt, their stories converge in a gripping narrative that explores the resilience of the human spirit and the power of love and redemption.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Gothic Fiction, Romance

Themes: Survival, Faith, Love, Social Hierarchy, Redemption
