Synopsis: In 'John de Lancaster: a novel; vol. III.', Richard Cumberland takes readers on a thrilling journey through the life of the enigmatic protagonist, John de Lancaster. Set against the backdrop of 18th-century England, this captivating novel delves into the complex web of secrets, scandals, and forbidden love that surrounds John's life. As he navigates through the treacherous world of high society, John must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. With its intricate plot twists and vivid characters, 'John de Lancaster: a novel; vol. III.' promises to keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, Forbidden love, Identity and self-discovery
