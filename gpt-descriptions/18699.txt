Synopsis: In 'The Moving Picture Girls at Sea: or, A Pictured Shipwreck That Became Real' by Laura Lee Hope, the adventurous and talented Moving Picture Girls find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey at sea. As they set sail, they stumble upon a mysterious shipwreck that holds more secrets than they could have ever imagined. With danger lurking around every corner, the girls must use their wit and courage to uncover the truth and save the day. Will they be able to solve the enigma before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
