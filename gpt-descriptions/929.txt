Synopsis: In 'The Real Cyberpunk Fakebook', Nagel, Bart/ Sirius, R. U./ St. Jude take readers on a mind-bending journey through a dystopian future where virtual reality and artificial intelligence blur the lines between what is real and what is simulated. As the protagonist delves deeper into the dark underbelly of this cyberpunk world, they uncover a conspiracy that threatens to unravel the very fabric of their existence. With high-stakes action, thought-provoking philosophical questions, and a cast of unforgettable characters, this book will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Cyberpunk, Science Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Virtual reality, Artificial intelligence, Identity, Conspiracy
