Synopsis: 
'Curiosities of Science, Past and Present: A Book for Old and Young' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of scientific discoveries and inventions. From ancient times to the present day, author John Timbs explores the curious and often surprising stories behind some of the most significant scientific breakthroughs. Delving into the realms of physics, chemistry, biology, and more, this book uncovers the hidden wonders of the scientific world, revealing the ingenuity and curiosity of the human mind. With a blend of historical anecdotes, intriguing facts, and thought-provoking insights, 'Curiosities of Science, Past and Present' is sure to captivate readers of all ages and ignite a sense of wonder about the mysteries of the universe.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Scientific discovery, Human curiosity, Historical significance
