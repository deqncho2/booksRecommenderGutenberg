Synopsis: In 'Foxglove Manor: A Novel, Volume 1 (of 3)' by Buchanan, Robert Williams, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn to the mysterious and enchanting Foxglove Manor. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the manor, she uncovers a dark history and a haunting presence that threatens her sanity. With each page, the suspense builds, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Foxglove Manor.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Supernatural elements, Psychological suspense
