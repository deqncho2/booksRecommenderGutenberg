Synopsis: 

'Durham: A Sketch-Book' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the historic city of Durham, England. Through the intricate and detailed sketches of Robert J. S. Bertram, the essence and charm of Durham come to life on the pages. From the iconic Durham Cathedral to the winding River Wear, each sketch captures the unique architecture, landscapes, and hidden corners of this enchanting city. Bertram's artistic talent and attention to detail transport readers into the heart of Durham, inviting them to explore its rich history and immerse themselves in its timeless beauty.

Genres: Art, Travel, History

Themes: Architecture, Heritage, Beauty of Nature
