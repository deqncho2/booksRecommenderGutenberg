Synopsis: In 'The Story of My Mind; Or, How I Became a Rationalist', Mangasarian takes readers on a captivating journey through his personal transformation from a devout believer to a staunch rationalist. Through thought-provoking anecdotes and introspection, he explores the power of reason and the pursuit of truth, challenging long-held beliefs and societal norms along the way. As he navigates the complexities of faith, philosophy, and science, readers are invited to question their own beliefs and embark on a quest for intellectual liberation.

Genres: Autobiography, Philosophy, Memoir

Themes: Rationalism, Skepticism, Intellectual Freedom
