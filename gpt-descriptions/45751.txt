Synopsis: In 'Nellie's Housekeeping: Little Sunbeams Series', Nellie embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience as she navigates the challenges of housekeeping in a bustling city. With her unwavering determination and infectious optimism, Nellie's story unfolds with heartwarming moments, unexpected friendships, and valuable life lessons. As she faces both triumphs and setbacks, readers will be captivated by Nellie's unwavering spirit and her ability to find joy in the simplest of tasks.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Inspirational

Themes: Resilience, Friendship, Self-discovery
