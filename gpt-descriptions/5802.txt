Synopsis: In 'A Sketch of the Causes, Operations and Results of the San Francisco Vigilance Committee of 1856', Stephen Palfrey Webb delves into the gripping true story of a secret organization that emerged during a time of lawlessness and corruption in San Francisco. As the city is plagued by crime and a failing justice system, a group of ordinary citizens takes matters into their own hands, forming a clandestine committee to restore order and seek justice. Webb's account unveils the inner workings of this vigilante group, their controversial methods, and the profound impact they had on the city's history. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, True crime, Political history

Themes: Vigilantism, Justice, Corruption, Civic responsibility
