Synopsis: In 'The Street of Precious Pearls' by Nora Waln, a young American woman finds herself immersed in the vibrant and mysterious world of 1930s Shanghai. As she navigates the bustling streets and encounters a diverse cast of characters, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal. With the backdrop of a city on the brink of change, she must confront her own desires and the consequences of her choices. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Cultural exploration, Identity, Forbidden love, Social change
