Synopsis: 
'Anting-Anting Stories, and Other Strange Tales of the Filipinos' by Kayme Sargent is a collection of captivating and mysterious stories that delve into the supernatural world of the Philippines. From encounters with mythical creatures to the power of ancient talismans, each tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rich folklore and traditions of the Filipino people. As the characters navigate through the realms of magic and superstition, they must confront their deepest fears and desires, ultimately discovering the true nature of their own humanity.

Genres: 
Folklore, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: 
Power of belief, Cultural identity, Human nature
