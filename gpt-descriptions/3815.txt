Synopsis: In "Rolling Stones" by Henry, O., a group of adventurous siblings embarks on a thrilling journey through space, encountering strange creatures and facing unexpected challenges. As they navigate the vast unknown, they learn valuable lessons about teamwork, resilience, and the power of family bonds. This captivating tale will transport readers to a world filled with wonder and excitement.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Family, Resilience
