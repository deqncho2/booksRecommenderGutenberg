Synopsis: In 'The Sapphire Signet' by Augusta Huiell Seaman, a young girl named Doris stumbles upon a mysterious sapphire signet ring that holds a secret from the past. As she delves deeper into the history of the ring, Doris finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, where she must unravel the clues left behind by a long-lost relative. With the help of her friends, Doris embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes her from New York City to a remote island, where the truth behind the sapphire signet is finally revealed.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Treasure hunting, Friendship, Identity
