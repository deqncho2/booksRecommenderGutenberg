Synopsis: In 'The Prodigals and Their Inheritance; Complete' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a family is torn apart by secrets, betrayal, and the pursuit of wealth. As the prodigal children return to claim their inheritance, they are faced with unexpected challenges that force them to confront their past and make difficult choices. With a web of complex relationships and a captivating narrative, this novel delves into the depths of human nature and the consequences of greed and ambition.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Family Saga

Themes: Family dynamics, Betrayal, Inheritance, Redemption, Morality
