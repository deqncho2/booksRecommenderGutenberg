Synopsis: In 'New Ideas for Work and Play: What a Girl Can Make and Do', Adelia B. Beard and Lina Beard present a captivating collection of creative projects and activities for young girls. From building a treehouse to making homemade toys, this book is filled with inspiring ideas that will spark the imagination and encourage girls to explore their own abilities. With step-by-step instructions and charming illustrations, the authors empower girls to embrace their curiosity and discover the joy of hands-on learning.

Genres: Children's Literature, Crafts and Hobbies, Self-Empowerment

Themes: Creativity, Empowerment, Skill-building
