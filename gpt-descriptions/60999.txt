Synopsis: In 'The Seeder' by Max Williams, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the course of humanity. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger, forcing him to question his own morality and the consequences of his creation. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to protect his groundbreaking invention from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Consequences
