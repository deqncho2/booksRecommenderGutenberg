Synopsis: In 'The Ghost of One Man Coulee' by B. M. Bower, a young cowboy named Chip Bennett finds himself entangled in a mysterious and haunting tale when he stumbles upon an abandoned ranch in the desolate Montana wilderness. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the ranch, Chip becomes haunted by a ghostly presence that seems determined to reveal a long-buried truth. With danger lurking at every turn, Chip must unravel the enigma of One Man Coulee before it consumes him entirely.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Redemption, Justice, the Power of the Past
