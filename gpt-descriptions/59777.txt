Synopsis: In the isolated town of Veazie, Maine, a gripping drama unfolds as a mysterious illness strikes the household of the wealthy Jordan family. As the family members become increasingly desperate to find a cure, tensions rise and dark secrets are revealed. With the threat of death looming over them, the characters must confront their pasts and make difficult choices that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Family dynamics, Secrets and lies, Morality, Sacrifice
