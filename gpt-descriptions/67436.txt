Synopsis: In 'Autumn Leaves' by Barton, Ardelia Maria Cotton, a young woman named Lily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and lies when she returns to her childhood home after a mysterious tragedy. As she delves deeper into her family's history, she uncovers shocking revelations that challenge everything she thought she knew about her loved ones. With each twist and turn, Lily must navigate the complexities of love, betrayal, and forgiveness, all while trying to unravel the truth behind the haunting autumn leaves that seem to hold the key to her past.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Family

Themes: Secrets and lies, Family dynamics, Identity, Redemption
