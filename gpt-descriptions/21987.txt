Synopsis: In 'The Village Pulpit, Volume II. Trinity to Advent', Sabine Baring-Gould presents a collection of 66 short sermons and sermon outlines, designed to guide and inspire readers through the Christian year. Each sermon provides a thought-provoking exploration of the Scriptures, offering insights and reflections on the Sundays and holy days from Trinity to Advent. Baring-Gould's eloquent and engaging style captivates the reader, inviting them to delve deeper into their faith and contemplate the teachings of the Christian tradition.

Genres: Religious literature, Sermons, Christian theology

Themes: Christian spirituality, Biblical interpretation, Liturgical year, Faith and devotion
