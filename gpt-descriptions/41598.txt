Synopsis: 
'Bypaths in Dixie: Folk Tales of the South' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Southern folklore. Sarah Johnson Cocke weaves together a collection of enchanting and often mysterious tales that have been passed down through generations. From haunted plantations to magical creatures, these stories transport readers to a world where the line between reality and myth blurs. Each tale is a window into the unique culture and traditions of the South, offering a glimpse into the region's history and the resilience of its people.

Genres: 
Folklore, Southern literature, Mystery

Themes: 
Oral tradition, Supernatural, Cultural heritage
