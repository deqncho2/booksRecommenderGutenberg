Synopsis: 
In the Heart of the Vosges and Other Sketches by a "Devious Traveller" takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes of the Vosges region. Through a series of vivid sketches, the author, Betham-Edwards, introduces us to a cast of intriguing characters and their enthralling stories. From the enchanting forests and charming villages to the hidden secrets and unexpected encounters, this collection of sketches offers a delightful exploration of the Vosges and the people who call it home.

Genres: Travelogue, Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Cultural encounters, Natural beauty
