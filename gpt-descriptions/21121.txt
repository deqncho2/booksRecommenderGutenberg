Synopsis: In 'A Houseful of Girls' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, a lively and unconventional family finds themselves facing unexpected challenges when their father's business takes a downturn. With their comfortable lifestyle at stake, the girls must navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations, all while striving to maintain their unique sense of self. As they face adversity together, the bonds between the sisters grow stronger, revealing the power of resilience and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family dynamics, Resilience, Identity, Love and friendship
