Synopsis: In 'Hearts and Masks' by Harold MacGrath, a mysterious and captivating woman named Zara steals the hearts of men in high society, leaving them enchanted and longing for more. As she navigates the glamorous world of the elite, Zara's true identity and motives remain shrouded in secrecy. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale explores the power of love, deception, and the masks we wear to protect our hearts.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Love, Society
