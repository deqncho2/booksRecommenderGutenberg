Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 158, September 18, 1915: The blue veil; or, Nick Carter's torn trail,' renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a mysterious blue veil becomes the key to a trail of secrets and danger. As Carter delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden identities that threaten to unravel everything he thought he knew. With time running out, Carter must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Identity, Justice
