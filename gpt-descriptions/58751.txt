Synopsis: In 'The Friendly Five: A Story' by Mary C. Hungerford, a group of five childhood friends reunite after many years apart, each carrying their own secrets and burdens. As they navigate through the complexities of adulthood, their bond is tested, and they must confront the past and make difficult choices that will shape their futures. With unexpected twists and heartfelt moments, this captivating tale explores the power of friendship, the weight of secrets, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Redemption, Self-discovery
