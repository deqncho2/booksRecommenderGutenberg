Synopsis: In 'The Son of His Mother' by Clara Viebig, a young man named Paul finds himself torn between his love for his mother and his desire for independence. As he navigates the complexities of family dynamics and societal expectations, Paul must confront the consequences of his choices and the impact they have on those around him. Set against the backdrop of a small German town in the early 20th century, this gripping tale explores the complexities of love, duty, and the search for personal identity.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Family relationships, Independence, Identity
