Synopsis: 

Step into the fascinating world of English architecture as J. Alfred Gotch takes you on a journey through time, exploring the growth and evolution of the English house from 1100 to 1800. From humble beginnings to grand estates, this short history delves into the intricate details and stories behind the architectural development that shaped the English landscape. Discover the secrets hidden within the walls, the influences of different eras, and the innovative designs that emerged over the centuries. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Architecture

Themes: Architectural development, Cultural influences, Evolution of design
