Synopsis: 

'Children's Literature: A Textbook of Sources for Teachers and Teacher-Training Classes' takes readers on an enchanting journey through the world of children's literature. This comprehensive textbook, written by Clippinger and Curry, serves as a valuable resource for teachers and aspiring educators, providing a wealth of information on the diverse range of children's literature available. From classic fairy tales to contemporary stories, this book explores the power of storytelling and its impact on young minds. Through insightful analysis and practical advice, the authors delve into the importance of selecting age-appropriate literature, fostering a love for reading, and promoting critical thinking skills. With its engaging content and practical approach, this textbook is an indispensable guide for educators seeking to inspire and educate the next generation of readers.

Genres: Education, Literature, Reference

Themes: Children's literature, Teaching, Storytelling, Reading promotion
