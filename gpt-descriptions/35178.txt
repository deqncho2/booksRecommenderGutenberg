Synopsis: In the small town of Ashford, young Ben Pepper finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change everything. As he delves deeper into the enigma, Ben must navigate through a labyrinth of clues, unexpected alliances, and treacherous adversaries. With time running out, Ben must summon all his courage and wit to unravel the truth before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Courage and resilience, Friendship and loyalty
