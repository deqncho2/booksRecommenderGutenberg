Synopsis: In 'The Seventh Man' by Max Brand, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small western town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic presence. As the townspeople become entangled in his web of secrets and hidden agendas, they soon discover that his arrival is just the beginning of a thrilling and dangerous journey that will test their loyalties and push them to their limits. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
