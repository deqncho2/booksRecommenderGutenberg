Synopsis: In 'The Fire People' by Ray Cummings, a young scientist stumbles upon a hidden civilization deep within the Earth's core. As he delves deeper into their society, he uncovers a shocking secret that could change the fate of humanity forever. With thrilling adventures, unexpected alliances, and a race against time, this captivating science fiction novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Secrets
