Synopsis: In the small town of Willowbrook, a mysterious grave captures the attention of the townspeople. The grave belongs to Solon Regh, a man with a dark past and a tragic fate. As the townspeople delve into the secrets surrounding Solon's life and death, they uncover a web of deceit, betrayal, and supernatural occurrences that threaten to unravel their own lives. With each revelation, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leaving the townspeople questioning their own sanity and the true nature of Solon Regh's grave.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Identity and Perception, Supernatural Phenomena
