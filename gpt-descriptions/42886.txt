Synopsis: In 'The Blue Dragon: A Tale of Recent Adventure in China' by Munroe, Kirk, a young adventurer finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger as he embarks on a journey through the heart of China. With the enigmatic Blue Dragon as his guide, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter ancient legends, and confront a powerful secret society. As he delves deeper into the unknown, he discovers that the fate of an entire nation rests in his hands. Will he be able to unravel the truth and save China from impending doom?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Identity, Power and Corruption
