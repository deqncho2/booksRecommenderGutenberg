Synopsis: 

In the heart of the prestigious Oxford University, a young and ambitious student named James finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries. As he delves deeper into the hidden history of the university, he uncovers a dark and dangerous world that threatens to consume him. With the help of a brilliant but enigmatic professor, James must navigate through treacherous alliances, ancient rituals, and a sinister plot that could change the course of history. As the stakes rise, James must confront his own demons and make choices that will determine not only his future but the fate of Oxford itself.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Ambition and power, Loyalty and betrayal
