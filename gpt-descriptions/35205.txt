Synopsis: In 'Who?' by Elizabeth Kent, a renowned scientist wakes up in a hospital bed with no memory of who he is or how he got there. As he embarks on a journey to uncover his true identity, he discovers a web of secrets, lies, and a dangerous conspiracy that threatens not only his life but the fate of humanity. With each clue he uncovers, he becomes more entangled in a world where nothing is as it seems, and the line between reality and illusion blurs. As he races against time to unravel the truth, he must confront his own past and question the very nature of his existence.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Identity, Memory, Conspiracy, Reality vs. Illusion
