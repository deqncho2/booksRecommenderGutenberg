Synopsis: In 'Meeting of the Board' by Alan Edward Nourse, a group of influential individuals gather for a secret meeting that will determine the fate of humanity. As tensions rise and hidden agendas come to light, the board members must navigate a treacherous web of power, deceit, and moral dilemmas. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, the stakes couldn't be higher.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Political Fiction

Themes: Power struggles, Morality, Conspiracy
