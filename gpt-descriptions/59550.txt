Synopsis: 

In 'Cancer: Its Cause and Treatment, Volume 1 (of 2)', Lucius Duncan Bulkley delves into the mysterious world of cancer, unraveling its enigmatic origins and exploring groundbreaking treatments. Through meticulous research and compelling anecdotes, Bulkley presents a comprehensive examination of this devastating disease, shedding light on its multifaceted nature and the various factors that contribute to its development. From the historical context to the latest scientific advancements, this volume offers a captivating exploration of cancer that will leave readers both informed and intrigued.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Science

Themes: Cancer research, Medical advancements, Disease etiology
