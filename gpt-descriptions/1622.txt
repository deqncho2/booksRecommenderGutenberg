Synopsis: 

Valeria Woodville, a young and spirited woman, embarks on a quest to uncover the truth behind her husband's mysterious past. Determined to prove his innocence, she delves into a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden identities. As Valeria unravels the enigma, she finds herself entangled in a thrilling journey that challenges her perception of love, trust, and the law.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Justice, Betrayal, Female Empowerment
