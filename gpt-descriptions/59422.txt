Synopsis: 

In 'Wisdom's Daughter: The Life and Love Story of She-Who-Must-be-Obeyed', H. Rider Haggard takes readers on a captivating journey through ancient Egypt. The story follows the life of a young woman named Ayesha, who possesses extraordinary beauty and wisdom. As she navigates the complexities of power, love, and immortality, Ayesha's fate becomes intertwined with that of a British explorer named Leo Vincey. Together, they embark on a thrilling adventure that will test their beliefs, challenge their desires, and reveal the true nature of their destinies.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Power and its consequences, Love and obsession, Immortality and mortality
