Synopsis: 
In "Between Whiles" by Helen Hunt Jackson, a young woman named Kate embarks on a journey to the picturesque mountains of Colorado, seeking solace and a fresh start after a devastating loss. As she immerses herself in the breathtaking landscapes and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Kate's path becomes intertwined with a mysterious stranger who holds a secret that could change her life forever. With each passing day, Kate finds herself torn between the allure of the unknown and the comfort of familiarity, leading her to question her own desires and the true meaning of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Grief and healing, Self-discovery, Nature's transformative power
