Synopsis: In 'Beyond' by Hubbard, Henry Seward, a young scientist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of the universe. As he delves deeper into its enigmatic powers, he finds himself embarking on a thrilling journey that takes him beyond the boundaries of known reality. With each discovery, he becomes entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where the line between science and the supernatural blurs. As he races against time to unravel the artifact's true purpose, he must confront his own beliefs and confront the unimaginable. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the power of knowledge, the intersection of science and spirituality
