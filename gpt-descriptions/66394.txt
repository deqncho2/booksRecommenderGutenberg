Synopsis: In 'The Dangerous Scarecrow' by Carl Jacobi, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious and gruesome deaths. As the body count rises, the townspeople become increasingly terrified, suspecting that a malevolent force is at work. Amidst the chaos, a young farmer named Ethan discovers a peculiar scarecrow in his field, seemingly innocent but harboring a dark secret. With the help of a skeptical detective, Ethan embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind the scarecrow and put an end to the deadly curse that has befallen their town.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Fear of the unknown, Supernatural forces, Small-town secrets
