Synopsis: In 'A Little Window' by Jean M. Snyder, a mysterious antique shop holds the key to a young woman's past, leading her on a captivating journey of self-discovery and unexpected connections. As she delves into the secrets hidden within the shop's walls, she uncovers a world of enchantment and possibility that will forever change her life. With each turn of the page, readers will be drawn deeper into the intricate web of the protagonist's past, present, and future, eagerly anticipating the revelations that lie just beyond the little window.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Identity, Fate, Magic
