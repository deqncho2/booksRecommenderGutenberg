Synopsis: In 'King Cole' by John Masefield, a young man named Cole embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him from the bustling streets of London to the mysterious depths of the ocean. As he navigates through treacherous waters and encounters fantastical creatures, Cole discovers a hidden world filled with ancient secrets and unexpected allies. With danger lurking at every turn, he must summon his courage and wit to fulfill his destiny and save both the land and sea from an impending catastrophe.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Heroism, Exploration, Fate, Redemption
