Synopsis: In 'Kept for the Master's Use' by Frances Ridley Havergal, a young woman embarks on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual growth as she learns to surrender her life completely to the will of her Master. Through a series of encounters and experiences, she discovers the transformative power of being wholly devoted to God and the joy that comes from living a life of purpose and obedience. As she navigates the challenges and blessings that come her way, she finds herself drawn closer to her Master and discovers the true meaning of surrender and service.

Genres: Christian literature, spiritual growth, self-help

Themes: Surrender, obedience, purpose, spiritual growth, devotion
