Synopsis: In 'For Love and Life; vol. 2 of 2' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), the lives of two young women, Lucy and Edith, intertwine as they navigate the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations. As their paths diverge and converge, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and unexpected twists challenge their notions of loyalty and sacrifice. With vivid characters and a captivating narrative, this novel explores the power of love and the choices we make in the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Family dynamics, Social expectations
