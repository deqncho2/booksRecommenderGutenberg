Synopsis:
In 'The Continental Classics, Volume XVIII., Mystery Tales', readers are treated to a collection of captivating stories by renowned authors Feodor Mikhailovitch Dostoyevsky, Jörgen Wilhelm Bergsöe, and Bernhard Severin Ingemann. Each tale delves into the realm of mystery, weaving intricate plots that keep readers guessing until the very end. From psychological suspense to supernatural elements, these stories explore the depths of human nature and the enigmatic forces that shape our lives. Prepare to be enthralled by the masterful storytelling and unexpected twists that await within this volume.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Supernatural

Themes: Human nature, Psychological exploration, Intrigue
