Synopsis: In 'How to Tell the Birds from the Flowers: A Manual of Flornithology for Beginners', Robert Williams Wood presents a whimsical and humorous guide that challenges readers to distinguish between birds and flowers. Through clever wordplay and imaginative illustrations, Wood takes readers on a delightful journey where the line between the natural world and human perception becomes delightfully blurred. This unique manual will entertain and educate readers of all ages, inviting them to see the world around them in a whole new light.

Genres: Humor, Non-fiction, Nature

Themes: Perception, Humor, Nature appreciation
