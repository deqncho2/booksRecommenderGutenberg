Synopsis: In the second volume of "Bismarck: Some Secret Pages of His History," Dr. Moritz Busch continues to share his personal diary entries from his twenty-five years of close association with the renowned Chancellor, Otto von Bismarck. As the reader delves into the pages of this captivating diary, they are taken on a journey through the political and personal life of one of the most influential figures in European history. With intriguing anecdotes, behind-the-scenes revelations, and intimate observations, Busch provides a unique perspective on Bismarck's character, his strategies, and the events that shaped his era. This volume offers further insight into the complex personality of Bismarck and the significant role he played in shaping the destiny of Germany and Europe.

Genres: Biography, History, Memoir

Themes: Politics, Power, Diplomacy, Leadership
