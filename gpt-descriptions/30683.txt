Synopsis: In 'The Unthinking Destroyer' by Phillips, Rog, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but the very fabric of society. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal to protect his invention and prevent it from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Consequences
