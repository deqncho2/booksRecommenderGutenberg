Synopsis: In 'The Devil's Asteroid' by Wellman, Manly Wade, a group of scientists embarks on a perilous mission to investigate a mysterious asteroid hurtling towards Earth. As they venture into the unknown, they encounter strange phenomena and face unimaginable dangers that challenge their beliefs and push the boundaries of science. With time running out, they must unravel the asteroid's secrets before it unleashes catastrophic consequences on humanity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the limits of human knowledge, the power of scientific discovery
