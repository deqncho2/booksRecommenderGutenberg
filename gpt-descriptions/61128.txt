Synopsis: In 'Seven-Day Terror' by R. A. Lafferty, a small town is suddenly plagued by a series of bizarre and inexplicable events that escalate with each passing day. As the townspeople struggle to make sense of the chaos, they must confront their deepest fears and unravel the mysteries that threaten to consume them all. With a blend of suspense, humor, and the supernatural, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fear, Community, Mystery
