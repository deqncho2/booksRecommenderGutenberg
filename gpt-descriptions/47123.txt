Synopsis: In 'Agnes Strickland's Queens of England, Vol. 3: Abridged and Fully Illustrated', the remarkable lives of the queens of England continue to unfold in vivid detail. From the captivating reign of Queen Elizabeth I to the tragic fate of Mary, Queen of Scots, this volume delves into the complex relationships, political intrigues, and personal struggles that shaped these iconic women. With stunning illustrations bringing their stories to life, readers will be enthralled by the triumphs and tribulations of these influential queens.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and politics, Gender and identity, Royal lineage and succession
