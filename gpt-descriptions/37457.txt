Synopsis: 

In 'Canada: Its Postage Stamps and Postal Stationery', Clifton A. Howes takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and intricate design of Canada's postage stamps and postal stationery. From the earliest issues to the modern era, this book delves into the fascinating stories behind each stamp, exploring the cultural, historical, and artistic significance they hold. With stunning visuals and insightful commentary, Howes uncovers the hidden gems and rare finds that make the world of Canadian philately truly remarkable. Whether you are a seasoned stamp collector or simply curious about the world of postal history, this book is sure to captivate and inspire.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Art

Themes: Philately, Canadian history, Cultural significance, Artistic expression
