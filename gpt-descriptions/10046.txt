Synopsis: In "Salute to Adventurers" by John Buchan, a young man named Andrew Garvald finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and danger as he embarks on a journey to the New World. Set in the 17th century, this thrilling historical adventure follows Garvald's quest for justice and redemption, as he navigates treacherous waters, encounters mysterious figures, and faces the ultimate test of his courage and loyalty.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Redemption, Political Intrigue
