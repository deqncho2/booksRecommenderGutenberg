Synopsis: In "The Pictorial Field-Book of the Revolution, Vol. 2," Benson John Lossing takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the history, biography, scenery, relics, and traditions of the War for Independence. Through vivid illustrations and engaging storytelling, Lossing brings to life the people, places, and events that shaped the birth of a nation. From the battles and military strategies to the personal stories of key figures, this volume offers a unique perspective on the American Revolution, providing readers with a deeper understanding of the sacrifices and triumphs that led to the birth of the United States.

Genres: History, Biography, Art

Themes: American Revolution, Patriotism, Historical Preservation
