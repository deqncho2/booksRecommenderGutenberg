Synopsis: 
In "Little Rivers: A Book of Essays in Profitable Idleness," Van Dyke takes readers on a captivating journey through the serene and picturesque landscapes of nature. With his eloquent prose and keen observations, he invites us to join him in exploring the hidden wonders of rivers, forests, and mountains. From the enchanting tales of trout fishing to the contemplation of life's deeper meanings, Van Dyke's essays offer a delightful escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Prepare to be transported to a world where time slows down, and the beauty of nature becomes a source of inspiration and solace.

Genres: Essay, Nature Writing, Travelogue

Themes: Nature appreciation, Reflection on life, Escapism
