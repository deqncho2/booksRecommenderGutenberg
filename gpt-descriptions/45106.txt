Synopsis: In "The Life of Francis Thompson," Meynell, Everard delves into the captivating and enigmatic life of the renowned poet Francis Thompson. From his humble beginnings to his struggles with addiction and his eventual rise to literary fame, this biography unveils the extraordinary journey of a man whose words touched the hearts of many. With vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, Meynell, Everard paints a vivid portrait of Thompson's tumultuous life, exploring the depths of his creativity and the demons that haunted him. This compelling narrative takes readers on a captivating exploration of one man's quest for redemption and the enduring power of art.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, Memoir

Themes: Addiction, Redemption, Artistic Expression
