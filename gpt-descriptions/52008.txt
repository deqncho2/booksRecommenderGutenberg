Synopsis: 

In "Dante Rossetti and the Pre-Raphaelite Movement" by Esther Wood, embark on a captivating journey through the life and art of one of the most influential figures in the Victorian art world. Discover the secrets behind Dante Rossetti's mesmerizing paintings and delve into the scandalous love affairs that shaped his artistic vision. From his passionate relationships with his muses to his rebellious stance against the conventional art establishment, this book unveils the untold stories that lie beneath Rossetti's masterpieces. 

Genres: Biography, Art History, Historical Non-Fiction

Themes: Love and Desire, Artistic Rebellion, Victorian Society
