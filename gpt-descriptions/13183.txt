Synopsis: In the Days of Chivalry: A Tale of the Times of the Black Prince takes readers on a captivating journey through medieval England, where honor, bravery, and love intertwine. Follow the adventures of young Sir Lionel, a knight-in-training, as he navigates the treacherous world of knights, castles, and courtly love. From thrilling jousts to dangerous quests, Sir Lionel's path is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As he strives to prove himself worthy of knighthood, he must confront his own fears and face the challenges that come with being a knight in the age of chivalry.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Chivalry, Honor, Bravery, Love
