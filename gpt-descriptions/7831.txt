Synopsis: In "When London Burned: a Story of Restoration Times and the Great Fire," G. A. Henty takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous period of Restoration England. Set against the backdrop of the Great Fire of London in 1666, the story follows the adventures of a young protagonist who finds himself caught up in the chaos and danger that engulfed the city. As the flames consume London, our hero must navigate treacherous streets, encounter historical figures, and face personal challenges that will test his courage and resilience. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, Henty brings to life the devastating fire and its aftermath, offering a captivating glimpse into a pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Resilience, Survival, Historical Events, Coming of Age
