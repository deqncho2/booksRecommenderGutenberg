Synopsis: 

In "Stonehenge, Today and Yesterday" by Frank Stevens, embark on a captivating journey through time and space as the author unravels the mysteries surrounding one of the world's most enigmatic ancient structures. From the awe-inspiring grandeur of Stonehenge's present-day existence to the enigmatic secrets hidden within its ancient past, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the monument's enduring allure. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Stevens delves into the historical, archaeological, and cultural significance of Stonehenge, shedding light on its construction, purpose, and the profound impact it has had on human imagination throughout the ages. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Archaeology

Themes: Ancient mysteries, Human fascination with the past, Cultural heritage
