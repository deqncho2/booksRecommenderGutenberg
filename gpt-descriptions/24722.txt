Synopsis: 

'Letters from Port Royal: Written at the Time of the Civil War (1862-1868)' is a collection of poignant and intimate letters that offer a unique perspective on the American Civil War. Set in Port Royal, South Carolina, during the years of conflict, these letters provide a vivid and personal account of the experiences and struggles faced by both soldiers and civilians alike. Through the heartfelt correspondence, the reader is transported to a time of great turmoil and witnesses the resilience, courage, and humanity of those caught in the midst of war.

Genres: Historical fiction, Epistolary novel, War literature

Themes: War and its impact on individuals and communities, resilience and survival, the human spirit in times of adversity
