Synopsis: In 'The Great Days of the Garden District, and the Old City of Lafayette', Samuel and Martha Ann Brett take us on a captivating journey through the vibrant history and enchanting streets of the Garden District and Old City of Lafayette. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, they unveil the secrets, scandals, and triumphs of this iconic neighborhood, painting a vivid picture of the past that will leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travel, Biography

Themes: History, Architecture, Culture, Community
