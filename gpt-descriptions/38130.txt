Synopsis: 

'Legends of Loudoun: An account of the history and homes of a border county of Virginia's Northern Neck' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and enchanting homes of Loudoun County, Virginia. From the early settlers and Native American tribes to the grand estates and hidden treasures, this book unveils the untold stories and legends that have shaped the county's identity. With vivid descriptions and intriguing anecdotes, the author delves into the lives of the people who have called Loudoun County home, painting a vivid picture of the county's past and present.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Local history, Architecture

Themes: History, Heritage, Community, Architecture, Cultural identity
