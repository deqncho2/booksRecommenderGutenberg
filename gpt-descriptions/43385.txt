Synopsis: 

Step into the world of Macmillan & Co.'s Catalogue, September 1874, where a treasure trove of captivating works in Belles Lettres awaits. This meticulously curated collection features an array of poetry, fiction, and more, promising to transport readers to enchanting realms and ignite their imagination. From the lyrical verses of renowned poets to the gripping narratives woven by talented storytellers, this catalogue offers a diverse range of literary gems that are sure to captivate and inspire.

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Belles Lettres

Themes: Imagination, Creativity, Exploration, Human experience
