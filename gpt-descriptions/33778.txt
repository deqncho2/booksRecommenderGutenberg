Synopsis: Set in the late 19th century, 'The Patriot (Piccolo Mondo Antico)' follows the life of a young woman named Luisa, who finds herself torn between two men from different social classes. As she navigates the complexities of love, societal expectations, and personal growth, Luisa becomes entangled in a web of secrets and political turmoil that will test her loyalty and resilience. With vivid descriptions of the Italian countryside and a cast of compelling characters, this captivating novel explores the timeless themes of love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and passion, Social class and inequality, Political unrest, Personal growth and self-discovery
