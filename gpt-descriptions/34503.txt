Synopsis: In 'The Green Book; Or, Freedom Under the Snow: A Novel' by Jókai, Mór, a young Hungarian nobleman finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love during the tumultuous times of the Hungarian Revolution of 1848. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters a cast of captivating characters, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and test his loyalty. This gripping tale of adventure, romance, and sacrifice will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Revolution, Love, Loyalty, Political intrigue
