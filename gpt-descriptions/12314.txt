Synopsis: 
In "Ashton-Kirk, Investigator" by John T. McIntyre, readers are introduced to the brilliant and enigmatic detective, Ashton-Kirk. When a mysterious murder takes place in a small town, Ashton-Kirk is called upon to unravel the perplexing web of clues and motives. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a series of interconnected secrets that lead him on a thrilling journey filled with danger, suspense, and unexpected twists. With his sharp intellect and keen observation skills, Ashton-Kirk must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit and deception to uncover the truth and bring the culprit to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Intrigue, Deduction, Uncovering Secrets
