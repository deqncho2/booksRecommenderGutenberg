Synopsis: 

'Lives of Boulton and Watt' takes readers on a captivating journey into the lives of two remarkable individuals, Matthew Boulton and James Watt, who revolutionized the world with their invention of the steam engine. Samuel Smiles delves into the original Soho manuscripts to uncover the untold stories behind their partnership, shedding light on their struggles, triumphs, and the immense impact they had on the Industrial Revolution. From their humble beginnings to their rise as pioneers of engineering, this book unveils the fascinating history of the steam engine and its profound influence on society.

Genres: Biography, History, Engineering

Themes: Innovation, Entrepreneurship, Industrial Revolution
