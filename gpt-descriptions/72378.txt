Synopsis: 
In "The Sorcerer's Stone" by Grimshaw, Beatrice, a young orphan named Harry Potter discovers that he is a wizard and is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. As he embarks on his magical journey, Harry uncovers a mysterious plot involving a powerful sorcerer's stone that could grant immortality. With the help of his new friends Ron and Hermione, Harry must navigate through challenges, face dangerous creatures, and unravel the secrets of the stone before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Good vs. Evil
