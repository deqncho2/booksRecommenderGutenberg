Synopsis: 
When Mr. John Scott Eccles receives an invitation to visit Wisteria Lodge, he expects nothing more than a pleasant weekend in the countryside. However, upon his arrival, he finds himself embroiled in a mysterious and sinister affair. Eccles soon discovers that the lodge holds dark secrets and that his host, Mr. Garcia, is not who he appears to be. As the plot thickens, Eccles becomes entangled in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Identity, Betrayal
