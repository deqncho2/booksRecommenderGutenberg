Synopsis: 

'Cardiff: A Sketch-Book' takes readers on a captivating visual journey through the vibrant city of Cardiff, Wales. Through the intricate and detailed sketches of Douglas S. Andrews, the reader is transported to the bustling streets, historic landmarks, and hidden corners of this enchanting city. From the iconic Cardiff Castle to the lively Cardiff Bay, each sketch captures the essence and charm of Cardiff, inviting the reader to explore its rich history and diverse culture. With every turn of the page, Andrews's sketches unveil the city's unique character, showcasing its architectural beauty, natural landscapes, and the everyday life of its inhabitants. This sketch-book is not only a celebration of Cardiff's beauty but also a testament to the artist's deep connection and love for the city.

Genres: Art, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: City exploration, Cultural heritage, Architectural beauty
