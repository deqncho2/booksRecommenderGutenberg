Synopsis: In the small town of Margenes, a mysterious newcomer named Lavinia arrives, captivating the townspeople with her enigmatic charm. As the residents become entangled in her web, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of Margenes is revealed. With its atmospheric setting and a cast of intriguing characters, 'The Margenes' is a gripping tale of suspense and deception that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Identity and self-discovery
