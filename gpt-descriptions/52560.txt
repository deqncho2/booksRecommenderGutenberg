Synopsis: In 'The Fortune of the Landrays' by Kester Vaughan, a mysterious inheritance sets off a chain of events that unravels the secrets of the Landray family. As the siblings navigate through hidden treasures, long-lost love letters, and unexpected alliances, they must confront their own desires and confrontations with the past. With each revelation, the Landrays are forced to question their identities and the true meaning of family.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Family Drama

Themes: Inheritance, Identity, Family Secrets
