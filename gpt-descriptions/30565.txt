Synopsis: In 'The Art of Lecturing: Revised Edition', Arthur M. Lewis takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of lecturing, offering invaluable insights and practical advice for both novice and experienced speakers. With a blend of personal anecdotes, expert analysis, and engaging examples, Lewis explores the art of captivating an audience, structuring a compelling lecture, and delivering it with confidence and impact. From the power of storytelling to the importance of body language, this book is a comprehensive guide that will inspire and empower anyone seeking to master the art of lecturing.

Genres: Non-fiction, Education, Communication

Themes: Public speaking, Effective communication, Engaging an audience, Teaching and learning
