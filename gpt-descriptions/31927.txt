Synopsis: In 'The Portal of Dreams' by Charles Neville Buck, a young woman named Emily discovers a mysterious portal that allows her to enter the realm of dreams. As she delves deeper into this enchanting world, she encounters fantastical creatures, uncovers hidden truths, and faces unexpected challenges. With each journey, Emily's perception of reality becomes blurred, and she must navigate the thin line between dreams and waking life. As she unravels the secrets of the portal, she realizes that her actions in the dream realm have consequences in the real world, leading her on a thrilling and perilous adventure.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Dreams and reality, Identity and self-discovery, Consequences of actions
