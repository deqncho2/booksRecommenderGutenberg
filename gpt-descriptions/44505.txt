Synopsis: 
'Across America by Motor-cycle' follows the thrilling journey of a young adventurer as he embarks on a daring cross-country trip on his motorcycle. From the bustling streets of New York City to the vast landscapes of the American West, he encounters a myriad of fascinating characters, faces unexpected challenges, and discovers the true essence of the American spirit. With each mile traveled, he unravels the mysteries of the open road and learns valuable lessons about life, love, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Freedom
