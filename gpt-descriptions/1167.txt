Synopsis: 
In 'A Strange Disappearance' by Anna Katharine Green, a young girl mysteriously vanishes from her home, leaving behind a perplexing trail of clues. As the investigation unfolds, a web of secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected connections is revealed, leading to a shocking conclusion that will keep readers guessing until the very end. With a blend of suspense, intrigue, and clever detective work, this gripping mystery will captivate readers from start to finish.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Disappearance, Secrets, Investigation, Deception
