Synopsis: In 'The Holladay Case: A Tale' by Stevenson, Burton Egbert, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing mystery when a wealthy businessman is found dead under suspicious circumstances. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and red herrings to uncover the truth behind the Holladay case.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice
