Synopsis: In the quaint English village of Littlegreen, a series of anonymous poison-pen letters begins to circulate, spreading scandal and suspicion among the residents. Jerry Burton, a young war veteran recovering from an injury, moves to Littlegreen with his sister Joanna seeking a peaceful retreat. However, they soon find themselves entangled in the web of deceit and dark secrets that the letters have unleashed. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they uncover shocking truths that threaten to tear the village apart. With their own lives at stake, Jerry and Joanna must race against time to unmask the anonymous letter writer before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Small-town secrets, Deception, Revenge, Redemption
