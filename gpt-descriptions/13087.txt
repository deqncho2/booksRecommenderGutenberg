Synopsis: 
In 'Sammie and Susie Littletail' by Howard Roger Garis, follow the adorable adventures of two young rabbit siblings, Sammie and Susie Littletail. From exploring the meadow to encountering new friends, the Littletails embark on a series of delightful escapades that will captivate readers of all ages. As they navigate the challenges of growing up, Sammie and Susie learn valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the importance of family. Join these lovable bunnies on their heartwarming journey filled with excitement, laughter, and a touch of mischief.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Animal Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Family, Coming of Age
