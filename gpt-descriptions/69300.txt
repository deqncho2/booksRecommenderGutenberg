Synopsis: 

In "Korea and her neighbors: A narrative of travel, with an account of the recent vicissitudes and present position of the country," Isabella L. Bird takes readers on an enthralling journey through the mysterious and captivating land of Korea. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Bird paints a vivid picture of the country's landscapes, people, and customs, while also delving into the tumultuous history and current state of affairs.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Exploration and Adventure, Cultural Identity, Historical Context, Socio-political Dynamics
