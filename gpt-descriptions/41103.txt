Synopsis: 

In 'Waterways of Westward Expansion - The Ohio River and its Tributaries', Archer Butler Hulbert takes readers on a captivating journey through the untamed wilderness of the Ohio River and its tributaries. From the early days of exploration to the bustling era of steamboats and trade, Hulbert delves into the rich history and remarkable stories that shaped the region. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, he uncovers the challenges faced by pioneers, the conflicts with Native American tribes, and the transformative impact of these waterways on the growth of the United States. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Travelogue

Themes: Exploration, Manifest Destiny, Native American relations, Industrialization
