Synopsis: In 'A Reversible Santa Claus' by Nicholson Meredith, a mysterious man named Santa Claus arrives in a small town, bringing joy and wonder to its residents. However, there is something unique about this Santa Claus - he has the ability to reverse time. As the townspeople experience the magic of the holiday season in reverse, they are forced to confront their past mistakes and rediscover the true meaning of Christmas. This heartwarming tale explores the power of forgiveness, redemption, and the importance of cherishing every moment.

Genres: Fantasy, Magical Realism, Holiday Fiction

Themes: Forgiveness, Redemption, Time, Christmas Spirit
