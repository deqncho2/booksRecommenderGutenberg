Synopsis: In 'The Five Giants', Daniel P. Kidder takes readers on a thrilling journey through a world where ancient legends and modern technology collide. As a group of unlikely heroes embarks on a quest to uncover the truth behind the mysterious giants that have suddenly appeared, they find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each step they take, the stakes grow higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. Will they be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Legends and mythology, the power of friendship, the consequences of secrets
