Synopsis: 

In 'Great Opera Stories: Taken from Original Sources in Old German', Millicent Schwab Bender takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of opera. Drawing from original German sources, Bender weaves together a collection of fascinating stories that serve as the inspiration behind some of the greatest operas ever composed. From tragic love affairs to epic battles, each tale is filled with passion, drama, and the timeless melodies that have captivated audiences for centuries. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Music, Drama

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Fate and Destiny, Human Emotions and Relationships
