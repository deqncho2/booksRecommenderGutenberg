Synopsis: In 'The Castlecourt Diamond Mystery' by Geraldine Bonner, a priceless diamond mysteriously disappears from a grand estate, setting off a thrilling investigation filled with unexpected twists and turns. As the search for the missing gem unfolds, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of the characters is revealed. With a cast of intriguing suspects and a race against time, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Crime

Themes: Deception, Greed, Betrayal
