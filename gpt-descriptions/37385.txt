Synopsis: 

Embryology: The Beginnings of Life takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate world of embryonic development. Written by renowned biologist Gerald R. Leighton, this book delves into the awe-inspiring process of how life begins, exploring the mysteries and wonders of embryology. From the formation of the first cells to the development of complex organs, Leighton unravels the secrets behind the creation of life, presenting a fascinating narrative that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Science, Non-fiction, Biology

Themes: Embryonic development, Life creation, Biological processes
