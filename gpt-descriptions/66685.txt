Synopsis: In 'The Tunnel Under the Channel' by Whiteside, Thomas, a brilliant engineer embarks on a daring project to connect two countries through an ambitious tunnel under the English Channel. As he faces numerous challenges and obstacles, including political opposition and technical setbacks, the fate of this groundbreaking endeavor hangs in the balance. With high stakes and a race against time, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering if the tunnel will ever become a reality.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Innovation, Perseverance
