Synopsis: In "Carlyle's Laugh, and Other Surprises" by Thomas Wentworth Higginson, prepare to be captivated by a collection of intriguing and unexpected tales. From a mysterious laugh that haunts a renowned philosopher to a surprising encounter with a famous poet, these stories will keep you on the edge of your seat, eagerly turning the pages to uncover each surprising twist and turn. With Higginson's masterful storytelling and his ability to blend reality with the supernatural, this collection promises to leave readers both delighted and astounded.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Surprise, Mystery, Human Nature
