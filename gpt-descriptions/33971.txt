Synopsis: In the remote Australian outback, a group of friends stumble upon an abandoned gold mine rumored to be cursed. As they delve deeper into the treacherous tunnels, they uncover dark secrets and encounter supernatural forces that test their friendships and sanity. With danger lurking at every turn, they must confront their deepest fears and unravel the mysteries of Witching Hill before it consumes them all.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Adventure

Themes: Supernatural, Friendship, Exploration
