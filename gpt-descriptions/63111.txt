Synopsis: In the final volume of 'The Grenadier Guards in the Great War of 1914-1918', author Frederick Ponsonby takes readers on a gripping journey through the last years of the war. From the intense battles on the Western Front to the dramatic events leading up to the Armistice, this book offers a vivid and detailed account of the Grenadier Guards' experiences. With firsthand testimonies, historical analysis, and captivating storytelling, Ponsonby brings to life the bravery, sacrifice, and camaraderie of these remarkable soldiers.

Genres: Military history, Non-fiction, War memoir

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Historical events
