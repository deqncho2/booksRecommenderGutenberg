Synopsis: 

In 'U.S. Marine Operations in Korea, 1950-1953, Volume 1 (of 5): The Pusan Perimeter', authors Nicholas A. Canzona and Lynn Montross, along with the United States Marine Corps, take readers on a gripping journey through one of the most critical periods of the Korean War. Focusing on the intense battle for the Pusan Perimeter, this volume provides a detailed account of the Marines' heroic efforts to hold their ground against overwhelming odds. With vivid descriptions and firsthand accounts, the book offers a captivating narrative that brings to life the bravery, sacrifice, and resilience of the Marines during this pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Military History, War, Non-fiction

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Sacrifice, Heroism
