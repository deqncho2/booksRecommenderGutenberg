Synopsis: 
'The Book of Elves and Fairies for Story-Telling and Reading Aloud and for the Children's Own Reading' by Frances Jenkins Olcott is a delightful collection of enchanting tales that will transport readers into a world of magic and wonder. From mischievous elves to graceful fairies, this book is filled with captivating stories that will captivate both children and adults alike. Each story is beautifully written and perfect for reading aloud or for children to enjoy on their own. Get ready to embark on a magical journey through the realms of elves and fairies!

Genres: Fantasy, Children's Literature, Folklore

Themes: Magic, Imagination, Adventure, Morality
