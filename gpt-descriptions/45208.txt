Synopsis: In 'The Flight of the Silver Ship: Around the World Aboard a Giant Dirigible' by Hugh McAlister, embark on a thrilling adventure as a group of daring explorers set off on a remarkable journey around the world aboard a massive airship. As they navigate through treacherous skies, encounter mysterious lands, and face unexpected challenges, the crew must rely on their ingenuity and teamwork to survive. With breathtaking descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Courage, Survival
