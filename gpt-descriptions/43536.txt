Synopsis: In 'None so Deaf As Those Who Won't Hear: A Comedietta in One Act', the eccentric Mr. Mufflewump finds himself in a series of hilarious misunderstandings when he refuses to acknowledge the simplest of truths. As his stubbornness leads to a cascade of comedic situations, the audience is taken on a delightful journey filled with wit, charm, and uproarious laughter.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Communication, Stubbornness, Misunderstandings
