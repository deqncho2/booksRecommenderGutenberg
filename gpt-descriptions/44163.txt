Synopsis: 
'London in the Sixties (with a few digressions)' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and transformative era of 1960s London. Through a series of interconnected stories and digressions, the book explores the lives of various characters who find themselves swept up in the cultural revolution of the time. From aspiring musicians and fashion icons to political activists and bohemian artists, the narrative delves into the intertwining lives and experiences of these individuals, painting a vivid portrait of a city on the brink of change. With its richly detailed descriptions and compelling storytelling, this book offers a nostalgic and immersive glimpse into a pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Coming-of-age, Literary fiction

Themes: Cultural revolution, Identity, Social change, Artistic expression
