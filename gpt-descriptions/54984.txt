Synopsis: In "A Serious Proposal to the Ladies," Mary Astell presents a thought-provoking and unconventional argument aimed at women, urging them to consider their own empowerment and intellectual growth. Divided into two parts, Astell challenges societal norms and encourages women to seek education, independence, and a voice in a world dominated by men. With a blend of wit, logic, and persuasive rhetoric, Astell's proposal offers a fresh perspective on the role of women in society, leaving readers intrigued and eager to explore her ideas further.

Genres: Non-fiction, Feminist literature, Philosophy

Themes: Women's empowerment, Gender equality, Education, Independence, Societal norms
