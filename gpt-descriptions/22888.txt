Synopsis: 

'Under the Window: Pictures & Rhymes for Children' by Kate Greenaway is a delightful collection of charming illustrations and whimsical rhymes that will captivate both children and adults alike. This beautifully illustrated book takes readers on a journey through the world of childhood, filled with playful scenes, endearing characters, and imaginative storytelling. From the joys of outdoor play to the wonders of nature, each page is a visual feast that sparks the imagination and invites readers to explore the magic of childhood.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book, Poetry

Themes: Childhood, Imagination, Nature, Playfulness
