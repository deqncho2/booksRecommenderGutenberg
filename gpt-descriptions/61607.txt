Synopsis: "The Flu" takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of influenza, exploring its impact on U.S. America, Europe, and even the remote islands of Hawaii. From the devastating pandemics that swept through cities to the scientific breakthroughs that revolutionized our understanding of the virus, Mouritz, A. A. St. M. delves into the fascinating stories behind this notorious disease. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the author uncovers the untold tales of those who fought against the flu, revealing the resilience of humanity in the face of a relentless enemy.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Medical

Themes: Disease and its impact, Scientific advancements, Human resilience
