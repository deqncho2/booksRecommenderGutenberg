Synopsis: 

In 'The Elder Brother: The Works of Francis Beaumont and John Fletcher (Volume 2 of 10)', the collaborative genius of Francis Beaumont, John Fletcher, and Philip Massinger shines through in this captivating play. Set in the vibrant city of London, the story follows the lives of two brothers, one virtuous and the other reckless, as they navigate love, loyalty, and the pursuit of power. As their paths intertwine with a cast of intriguing characters, including a cunning courtesan and a mysterious nobleman, the brothers find themselves entangled in a web of deceit and betrayal. With its witty dialogue, complex relationships, and unexpected twists, 'The Elder Brother' is a thrilling tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Tragedy

Themes: Love and desire, Loyalty and betrayal, Power and ambition
