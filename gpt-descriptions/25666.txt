Synopsis: 
In 'The Boy Tar' by Reid, Mayne, follow the thrilling adventures of a young boy named Jack, who finds himself aboard a ship bound for the treacherous seas. As he navigates the challenges of life at sea, Jack must prove his worth and face unexpected dangers that test his courage and resilience. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this captivating tale will transport readers into a world of high-stakes adventure and the indomitable spirit of a young hero.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Exploration
