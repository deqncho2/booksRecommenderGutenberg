Synopsis: 
'PoPHILO: Popular Philosophy' by Dom is a captivating exploration of the complexities of human existence through the lens of popular philosophy. The story follows a group of diverse characters who find themselves drawn together by their shared fascination with philosophical ideas. As they navigate the challenges of their own lives, they embark on a thought-provoking journey that delves into the nature of reality, the meaning of life, and the pursuit of happiness. With engaging dialogue and unexpected twists, this novel invites readers to ponder profound questions and contemplate the intricacies of the human experience.

Genres: Fiction, Philosophy, Contemporary

Themes: Existentialism, Identity, Morality, Happiness
