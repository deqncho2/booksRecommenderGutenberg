Synopsis: 
"Glimpses of Bengal: Selected from the letters of Sir Rabindranath Tagore, 1885 to 1895" offers a captivating glimpse into the vibrant and diverse world of Bengal through the personal letters of the renowned poet and philosopher, Rabindranath Tagore. Spanning a decade of his life, this collection takes readers on a journey through the enchanting landscapes, rich cultural traditions, and profound insights that shaped Tagore's artistic and intellectual development. From his reflections on nature and spirituality to his observations on social issues and the struggles of his time, these letters provide a unique window into the mind of one of India's most celebrated literary figures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Letters, Biography

Themes: Nature, Spirituality, Cultural Identity, Social Issues
