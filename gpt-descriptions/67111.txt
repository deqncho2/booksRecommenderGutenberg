Synopsis: In 'The Sacred Tree: Being the Second Part of 'The Tale of Genji',' Murasaki Shikibu continues the captivating story of Genji, a nobleman whose life is filled with love, intrigue, and tragedy. As Genji navigates the complexities of court life, he finds himself entangled in a web of forbidden desires, secret affairs, and political rivalries. With vivid descriptions and poetic prose, Shikibu paints a vivid picture of the Heian period in Japan, immersing readers in a world of beauty, passion, and heartbreak.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, drama

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, power and politics, beauty and aesthetics
