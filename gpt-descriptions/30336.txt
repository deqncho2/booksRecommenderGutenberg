Synopsis: 

'Hours in a Library, Volume 2: New Edition, with Additions' by Leslie Stephen is a captivating collection of essays that takes readers on a literary journey through various topics and authors. From exploring the works of Shakespeare and Milton to delving into the lives of famous writers like Samuel Johnson and Jonathan Swift, Stephen's insightful analysis and engaging writing style make this volume a true treasure trove for book lovers. With each essay offering a unique perspective and shedding light on the intricacies of literature, readers will find themselves immersed in a world of intellectual exploration and discovery.

Genres: Literary criticism, essay collection, biography

Themes: Literature, authorship, intellectual curiosity
