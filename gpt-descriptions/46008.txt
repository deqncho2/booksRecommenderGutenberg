Synopsis: 
In 'The Room with the Tassels' by Wells, Carolyn, a young woman named Patty Fairfield finds herself drawn into a mysterious and eerie world when she inherits an old mansion from her late aunt. As she explores the mansion, she discovers a hidden room with tassels that seem to hold a secret power. With the help of her friends, Patty embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the room and its enigmatic tassels, leading her to unexpected revelations and danger.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Supernatural elements, Female empowerment
