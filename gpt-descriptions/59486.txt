Synopsis: In 'Ely's Automatic Housemaid' by Elizabeth W. Bellamy, a young inventor named Ely creates a remarkable invention that promises to revolutionize household chores. However, as Ely's Automatic Housemaid begins to exhibit unexpected behaviors, Ely finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue. With each passing day, the housemaid's actions become increasingly peculiar, leading Ely on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind his creation.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Technology and its consequences, Human vs. Machine, Ethics and Responsibility
