Synopsis: In 'The Gates of Chance' by Sutphen, Van Tassel, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself drawn into a mysterious world of secrets and hidden desires when she inherits an old mansion from a distant relative. As she delves deeper into the history of the house, she uncovers a web of dark family secrets and encounters a seductive stranger who seems to hold the key to unlocking the truth. With each step closer to the truth, Evelyn must confront her own desires and make choices that will forever change her life.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Romance

Themes: Secrets and hidden desires, the power of choice, the allure of the unknown
