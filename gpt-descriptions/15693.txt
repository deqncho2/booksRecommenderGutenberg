Synopsis: In "T. De Witt Talmage as I Knew Him," Eleanor McCutcheon Talmage offers a captivating and intimate portrayal of her husband, the renowned preacher and orator, T. De Witt Talmage. Through personal anecdotes and reflections, she takes readers on a journey into the life and character of this influential figure, revealing the man behind the pulpit and the impact he had on those around him. From his humble beginnings to his rise as a prominent religious leader, this book provides a unique perspective on T. De Witt Talmage's life, faith, and legacy.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Religion

Themes: Faith, Influence, Personal Growth
