Synopsis: 
'Amiens Before and During the War' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant city of Amiens, France, both before and during the tumultuous period of war. Through the eyes of its diverse inhabitants, the story unfolds, revealing the intertwining lives of individuals from different backgrounds and social classes. As the war looms closer, tensions rise, secrets are unveiled, and the characters are forced to confront their deepest fears and desires. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling characters, this novel offers a poignant exploration of love, loss, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and Relationships, Social Class, Identity, Resilience
