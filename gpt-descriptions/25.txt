Synopsis: 

The 1991 CIA World Factbook is a captivating and eye-opening reference guide that takes readers on a global journey, providing a comprehensive overview of the world's countries. From the smallest island nations to the largest superpowers, this meticulously researched book offers a wealth of information on each nation's geography, population, economy, government, and much more. Delve into the fascinating details of diverse cultures, historical backgrounds, and geopolitical landscapes, as the CIA presents an unparalleled glimpse into the world as it stood in 1991. 

Genres: Reference, Non-fiction, Geography

Themes: Global diversity, International relations, Cultural exploration
