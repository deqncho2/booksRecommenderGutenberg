Synopsis: 
In "Jack the runaway; or, On the road with a circus" by Frank V. Webster, we follow the thrilling adventures of Jack, a young boy who finds himself escaping the confines of his ordinary life to join a traveling circus. As Jack embarks on this extraordinary journey, he encounters a colorful cast of characters, faces unexpected challenges, and discovers the true meaning of friendship and self-discovery. With each new town and performance, Jack's courage and determination are put to the test, leading him on a path of excitement, danger, and personal growth.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Adventure, Friendship, Self-discovery
