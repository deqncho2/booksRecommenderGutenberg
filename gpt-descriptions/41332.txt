Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Ten Thousand a-Year', the captivating story of Arthur Effingham continues as he navigates the complexities of his newfound wealth and the challenges that come with it. As Arthur becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, he must confront the consequences of his choices and the true nature of those around him. With his fortune at stake, Arthur's journey takes him on a thrilling ride filled with suspense, romance, and the pursuit of justice. Will he be able to maintain his integrity and find happiness amidst the chaos?

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Wealth and its consequences, Deception and betrayal, Morality and integrity
