Synopsis: In "Sarah Dillard's Ride: A Story of the Carolinas in 1780" by James Otis, readers are transported to the tumultuous times of the American Revolution. Set in the Carolinas during 1780, the story follows young Sarah Dillard as she embarks on a daring journey that will test her courage and resilience. As the war rages on, Sarah finds herself caught in the middle of the conflict, facing danger and uncertainty at every turn. With her determination and resourcefulness, she becomes an unexpected hero, making a significant impact on the outcome of the war. This gripping historical tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of Sarah and the Carolinas during this pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Patriotism, Coming of Age
