Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a young and adventurous girl, finds herself caught up in a mysterious and thrilling adventure when a valuable pearl necklace goes missing. As she delves into the world of gypsies, Ruth must navigate through secrets, danger, and unexpected twists to uncover the truth and recover the precious necklace. With her determination and resourcefulness, Ruth proves that she is more than capable of solving the mystery and facing the challenges that come her way.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Coming of age, Independence, Courage, Identity
