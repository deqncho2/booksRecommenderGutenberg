Synopsis: 
In 'Davy and the Goblin: What Followed Reading 'Alice's Adventures in Wonderland'', young Davy finds himself embarking on a whimsical and fantastical journey after reading Lewis Carroll's famous tale. As he falls asleep, Davy is transported to a world filled with mischievous goblins, talking animals, and peculiar characters. With the help of his newfound friend, the Goblin, Davy must navigate through a series of enchanting and sometimes dangerous encounters, all while trying to find his way back home. This delightful and imaginative story will captivate readers as they join Davy on his extraordinary adventure.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Coming of Age
