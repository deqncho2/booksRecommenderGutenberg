Synopsis: 

'American Architecture: Studies' by Schuyler, Montgomery takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and diverse styles of American architecture. From the iconic skyscrapers of New York City to the charming colonial houses of New England, this book delves into the fascinating stories behind some of the nation's most renowned architectural masterpieces. Through vivid descriptions and stunning visuals, the authors explore the influences, innovations, and cultural significance of these architectural wonders, shedding light on the evolution of American design and the individuals who shaped it. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Architecture, History

Themes: American identity, Cultural heritage, Architectural innovation
