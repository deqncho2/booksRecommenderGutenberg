Synopsis: In 'Boarding Party' by Robert F. Young, a group of astronauts embarks on a dangerous mission to investigate a mysterious alien spacecraft that has appeared near Earth. As they board the vessel, they soon discover that they are not alone, and the true nature of their mission becomes increasingly perilous. With unexpected twists and intense action, 'Boarding Party' takes readers on a thrilling journey into the unknown.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Alien Encounters
