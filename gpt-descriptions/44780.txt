Synopsis: In 'Bessie at the Sea-Side' by Joanna H. Mathews, young Bessie embarks on a thrilling adventure during her summer vacation at the sea-side. As she explores the sandy beaches, encounters fascinating creatures, and makes unexpected friends, Bessie's imagination takes flight, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and wonder. With each passing day, Bessie's experiences at the sea-side shape her perspective on life and leave an indelible mark on her heart.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Imagination, Exploration, Friendship, Self-Discovery
