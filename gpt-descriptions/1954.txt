Synopsis: 
Colonel Chabert, a war hero presumed dead during the Battle of Eylau, miraculously resurfaces years later in Paris. However, he finds himself in a world that has moved on without him, as his wife has remarried and inherited his fortune. Determined to reclaim his identity and wealth, Colonel Chabert embarks on a legal battle that uncovers the dark underbelly of society and tests the limits of justice.

Genres: 
Historical fiction, Legal drama, Psychological thriller

Themes: 
Identity, Betrayal, Justice, Social class
