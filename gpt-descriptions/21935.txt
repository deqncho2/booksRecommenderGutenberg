Synopsis: In "Prince Prigio: From His Own Fairy Book," Lang takes readers on a captivating journey into a world of enchantment and adventure. Prince Prigio, a young prince with a unique gift, finds himself facing extraordinary challenges and embarking on a quest to prove his worth. With the help of magical beings, he must navigate treacherous obstacles and confront his own inner demons. This enthralling tale is filled with unexpected twists and turns, as Prince Prigio discovers the true meaning of courage, love, and self-discovery.

Genres: Fantasy, Fairy Tale, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Self-acceptance, Magic
