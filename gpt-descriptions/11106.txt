Synopsis: In the secluded village of Cobhurst, a mysterious girl arrives, captivating the attention of the townspeople. As her presence begins to unravel the secrets of the village, a web of intrigue and romance unfolds, leading to unexpected consequences for all involved. With its blend of mystery, romance, and suspense, 'The Girl at Cobhurst' takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden depths of a small community.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Love and relationships, Small-town dynamics
