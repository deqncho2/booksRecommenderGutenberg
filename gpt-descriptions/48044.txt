Synopsis: 
In 'Facts in Jingles' by Winifred Sackville Stoner, a young woman named Alice embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment as she navigates the challenges of love, loss, and societal expectations. Through a series of unexpected events, Alice finds herself questioning the truths she once held dear and reevaluating her own identity. As she delves deeper into her own psyche, she uncovers a hidden world of secrets and revelations that will forever change her perspective on life. With its captivating storytelling and thought-provoking narrative, 'Facts in Jingles' takes readers on a mesmerizing journey of personal growth and the pursuit of truth.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Love and loss, Identity, Societal expectations
