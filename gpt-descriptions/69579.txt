Synopsis: In 'Adventures of the Comte de la Muette during the Reign of Terror' by Capes, Bernard, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the French Revolution. The story follows the enigmatic Comte de la Muette, a nobleman forced to navigate the treacherous landscape of the Reign of Terror. As he becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue, secret societies, and forbidden love, the Comte must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to survive. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a world of danger, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. Will the Comte de la Muette be able to outmaneuver his enemies and protect those he holds dear?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Revolution, Loyalty, Survival, Love and Betrayal
