Synopsis: In 'The Automobile Girls Along the Hudson; Or, Fighting Fire in Sleepy Hollow', the spirited and adventurous Automobile Girls find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery while exploring the picturesque Hudson River Valley. As they navigate treacherous roads, encounter suspicious characters, and face unexpected dangers, the girls must rely on their wit, courage, and resourcefulness to unravel the secrets of Sleepy Hollow. With their trusty automobiles as their only allies, the girls embark on a heart-pounding journey that will test their friendship and reveal the true power of determination.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
