Synopsis: In "The Coxswain's Bride; also, Jack Frost and Sons; and, A Double Rescue" by R. M. Ballantyne, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the lives of a brave coxswain, a mischievous Jack Frost, and a daring rescue mission. Set against the backdrop of the sea, this captivating tale intertwines the lives of these characters in unexpected ways, leading to heart-pounding adventures and unexpected twists. As the story unfolds, the reader is immersed in a world of danger, courage, and the enduring power of love.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Bravery, Love, Perseverance, Family
