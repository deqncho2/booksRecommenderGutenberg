Synopsis: In 'The Harlot's Progress' by Cibber and 'The Rake's Progress' by Theophilus, we follow the tumultuous lives of two individuals as they navigate the treacherous paths of vice and indulgence. Through a series of captivating events, we witness the consequences of their choices and the harrowing consequences that await them. These works delve into the dark underbelly of society, exploring themes of moral decay, redemption, and the destructive power of temptation.

Genres: Drama, Satire, Moral Tale

Themes: Moral decay, Redemption, Temptation, Consequences of vice
