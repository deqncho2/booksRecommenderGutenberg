Synopsis:
In 'Telepathy and the Subliminal Self' by Mason, R. Osgood, a renowned psychologist delves into the mysterious world of telepathy and explores the hidden depths of the human mind. Through a series of captivating case studies and scientific experiments, the author uncovers the astonishing power of the subliminal self and its potential to connect minds beyond the boundaries of time and space. As the boundaries between reality and the supernatural blur, readers are taken on a thought-provoking journey that challenges their understanding of human consciousness and the true nature of communication.

Genres:
Psychology, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes:
Telepathy, Subconscious Mind, Human Consciousness, Supernatural Phenomena
