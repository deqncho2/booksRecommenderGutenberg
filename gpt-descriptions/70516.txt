Synopsis: 
In "Now we are six" by A. A. Milne, readers are transported into the enchanting world of childhood through a collection of delightful poems. Join Christopher Robin and his beloved friends, including Winnie-the-Pooh, Piglet, and Eeyore, as they embark on whimsical adventures, explore the wonders of imagination, and navigate the complexities of growing up. From the joy of blowing out birthday candles to the bittersweet realization that one is no longer a child, this heartwarming collection captures the essence of innocence, friendship, and the timeless magic of being six years old.

Genres: 
Children's literature, Poetry, Fantasy

Themes: 
Childhood, Imagination, Friendship, Growing up
