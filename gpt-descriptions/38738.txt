Synopsis: 

'American Military Insignia, 1800-1851' by J. Duncan Campbell is a captivating exploration of the visual symbols that adorned the uniforms of the American military during the early 19th century. From the intricate designs of the infantry to the distinguished emblems of the cavalry, this book delves into the rich history and evolution of military insignia during a transformative period in American history. Through detailed illustrations and insightful analysis, Campbell takes readers on a journey through the significance and symbolism behind these badges of honor, shedding light on the stories and traditions they represent.

Genres: History, Military, Art

Themes: Symbolism, Identity, Historical Evolution
