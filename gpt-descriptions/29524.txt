Synopsis: In 'The Masked Bridal' by Sheldon, Georgie, Mrs., a mysterious masked figure appears at a lavish wedding, captivating the guests with her enigmatic allure. As the night unfolds, secrets are unveiled, passions ignite, and the line between fantasy and reality becomes blurred. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a world of intrigue and forbidden desires, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Forbidden Love
