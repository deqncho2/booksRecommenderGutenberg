Synopsis: In 'The Wolf's Long Howl' by Waterloo, Stanley, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances, leaving the community in a state of fear and suspicion. As the local sheriff investigates the unsettling events, he uncovers a web of dark secrets and hidden motives that threaten to tear the town apart. With each passing day, the tension rises, and the sheriff must confront his own demons while racing against time to solve the chilling mystery that haunts the town. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Psychological suspense
