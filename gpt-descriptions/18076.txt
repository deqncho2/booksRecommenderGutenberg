Synopsis: In 'The Boy Trapper' by Castlemon, Harry, a young boy named Frank embarks on a thrilling adventure in the untamed wilderness. When his family moves to a remote area, Frank discovers his passion for trapping animals and becomes an expert trapper. However, his skills are put to the test when he encounters a mysterious and dangerous creature that threatens not only his newfound passion but also his very life. As Frank navigates the treacherous terrain and faces unexpected challenges, he must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to survive and protect those he cares about.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Coming-of-age

Themes: Survival, Courage, Wilderness exploration
