Synopsis: "Those Holy Fields. Palestine, Illustrated by Pen and Pencil" takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and historical sites of Palestine. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, the author, Samuel Manning, paints a vivid picture of this sacred land, its people, and their customs. From the bustling streets of Jerusalem to the serene shores of the Sea of Galilee, Manning's narrative transports readers to a world filled with ancient wonders and timeless beauty. As the author delves into the rich history and religious significance of the region, readers are left captivated by the allure and mystique of Palestine.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction, Art

Themes: Cultural heritage, Spirituality, Historical significance
