Synopsis: In 'A Matter of Importance' by Murray Leinster, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. As he races against time to protect his invention from falling into the wrong hands, he finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and unexpected alliances. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous landscape where trust is scarce and betrayal lurks at every corner.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific advancement, Ethics, Trust and betrayal
