Synopsis: In 'War and the Weird', a gripping collaboration between R. Thurston Hopkins and Forbes Phillips, the boundaries between reality and the supernatural are blurred as a group of soldiers find themselves facing not only the horrors of war but also the eerie and inexplicable. As they navigate the treacherous landscapes of World War I, they encounter strange phenomena and encounter beings that defy explanation. With each encounter, their understanding of the world is challenged, leading them to question the very nature of reality and the true extent of the horrors they face. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Supernatural, War

Themes: The impact of war on individuals and society, the exploration of the supernatural, the fragility of reality.
