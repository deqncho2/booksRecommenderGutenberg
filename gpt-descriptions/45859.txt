Synopsis: 
'Patrañas; or, Spanish Stories, Legendary and Traditional' by Rachel Harriette Busk is a captivating collection of enchanting tales that transport readers to the rich and vibrant world of Spanish folklore. From mythical creatures to legendary heroes, each story is filled with intrigue, adventure, and a touch of magic. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will be captivated by the colorful characters, the vivid descriptions of Spanish landscapes, and the timeless themes that resonate throughout the tales. Whether it's a story of love and sacrifice or a battle between good and evil, 'Patrañas' offers a delightful journey into the heart of Spanish storytelling.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Good vs. Evil, Myth and Legend
