Synopsis: In the depths of Victorian London, a sinister experiment is conducted that unveils a hidden world of ancient gods and forbidden knowledge. As the boundaries between reality and the supernatural blur, a series of mysterious events unfold, leaving the characters questioning their sanity and the very fabric of existence. With its eerie atmosphere and chilling revelations, "The Great God Pan" takes readers on a haunting journey into the realms of the unknown.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: The duality of human nature, the pursuit of forbidden knowledge, the thin line between reality and the supernatural, the consequences of meddling with ancient powers.
