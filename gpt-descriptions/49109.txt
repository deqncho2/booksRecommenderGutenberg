Synopsis: In "John Sherman; and, Dhoya" by W.B. Yeats, the reader is transported to a world where reality and fantasy intertwine. John Sherman, a young man with a mysterious past, finds himself caught between two worlds as he embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about his identity. Along the way, he encounters Dhoya, a captivating and enigmatic woman who holds the key to his destiny. As their paths converge, John must navigate through a series of surreal and magical encounters, challenging his perceptions of love, loyalty, and the nature of existence itself.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Love, Reality vs. Illusion
