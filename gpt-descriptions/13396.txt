Synopsis: In the picturesque town of Sweetapple Cove, a mysterious newcomer arrives, stirring up curiosity and intrigue among the townsfolk. As secrets unravel and relationships are tested, the idyllic facade of the town begins to crumble, revealing a dark underbelly that threatens to tear the community apart. With its charming setting and a cast of captivating characters, 'Sweetapple Cove' takes readers on a thrilling journey of love, betrayal, and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Small-town secrets, Identity, Redemption
