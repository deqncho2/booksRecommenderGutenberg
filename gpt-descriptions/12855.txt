Synopsis: In "Elizabethan Sea-Dogs: A Chronicle of Drake and His Companions," William Wood takes readers on a thrilling journey through the daring exploits of the legendary Sir Francis Drake and his fellow adventurers. From their audacious raids on Spanish ships to their perilous circumnavigation of the globe, this captivating chronicle brings to life the exhilarating era of Elizabethan sea-dogs. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Wood paints a vivid picture of the high-stakes world of piracy, exploration, and international intrigue that defined this remarkable period in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Courage, Nationalism, Power dynamics
