Synopsis: In the remote Pacific island of Ponape, a group of explorers stumble upon a mysterious pool that holds an ancient secret. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they find themselves entangled in a web of supernatural forces and forbidden knowledge. With each step, they uncover a world beyond imagination, where ancient civilizations clash with modern science and the boundaries of reality blur. As they strive to unlock the secrets of the Moon Pool, they must confront their own fears and desires, for the power it holds could either save humanity or bring about its ultimate destruction.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Supernatural, Forbidden Knowledge
