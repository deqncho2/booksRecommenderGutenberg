Synopsis: In 'Making His Mark' by Alger, Horatio, Jr., follow the journey of a young and ambitious protagonist as he navigates the challenges of the bustling city, striving to achieve his dreams. Faced with adversity and unexpected turns, he must summon his determination and resourcefulness to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way. This captivating tale explores the pursuit of success, the power of resilience, and the transformative impact of seizing opportunities.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Resilience, Opportunity
