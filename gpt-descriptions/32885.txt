Synopsis: In 'Circle of Flight' by Richard Stockham, a young woman named Emma finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she discovers a hidden secret about her family's past. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Emma becomes entangled in a thrilling adventure that takes her across continents and through time, where she must confront her own fears and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Courage, Redemption
