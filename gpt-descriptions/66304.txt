Synopsis: In 'The Norse King's Bridal: Translations from the Danish and old Norse, with original ballads', E. M. Smith-Dampier takes readers on a captivating journey through ancient Scandinavian folklore. This collection of translated tales and original ballads delves into the enchanting world of Norse mythology, where gods, giants, and heroes collide in epic battles and forbidden romances. From the treacherous depths of the sea to the majestic halls of Valhalla, these stories transport readers to a time of bravery, magic, and destiny. With each page, the reader becomes immersed in a rich tapestry of adventure, love, and the eternal struggle between good and evil.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Poetry

Themes: Heroism, Fate, Love and Sacrifice
