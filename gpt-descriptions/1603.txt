Synopsis: In 'The Blue Flower', a young artist named Fritz embarks on a journey of self-discovery and love in 18th-century Germany. Inspired by his encounters with nature, philosophy, and the enigmatic character of a young woman, Fritz's pursuit of the elusive blue flower becomes a metaphor for his quest for beauty, meaning, and the essence of life itself.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Bildungsroman

Themes: Love and longing, Nature and spirituality, Art and creativity, Quest for meaning
