Synopsis: In "History of the Discovery of the Northwest by John Nicolet in 1634: With a Sketch of his Life," Consul Willshire Butterfield takes readers on a captivating journey back to the 17th century. This enthralling historical account follows the intrepid explorer John Nicolet as he embarks on a daring expedition to the uncharted lands of the Northwest. As Nicolet encounters indigenous peoples, navigates treacherous terrain, and uncovers ancient mysteries, readers are transported to a time of exploration and discovery. Alongside the thrilling narrative, Butterfield provides a fascinating glimpse into Nicolet's life, shedding light on the man behind the legend. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Indigenous cultures, Adventure
