Synopsis:
In "Benton of the Royal Mounted: A Tale of the Royal Northwest Mounted Police," Ralph S. Kendall takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the rugged and untamed Canadian wilderness. The story follows the adventures of Benton, a courageous and resourceful member of the Royal Northwest Mounted Police, as he navigates treacherous landscapes, confronts dangerous outlaws, and unravels a web of mystery and intrigue. As Benton's loyalty to his duty and dedication to justice are put to the test, he must rely on his wits and bravery to protect the innocent and uphold the law in the face of overwhelming odds.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Justice, Wilderness survival
