Synopsis: 
In 'The Guilty River' by Wilkie Collins, a young woman named Grace Roseberry finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception when she assumes a false identity to escape her troubled past. As she becomes entwined in a complex love triangle, her actions set off a chain of events that lead to unexpected consequences. With suspenseful twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the depths of human nature and the consequences of our choices.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Love and Betrayal
