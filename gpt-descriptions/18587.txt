Synopsis: In 'The Chums of Scranton High: Or, Hugh Morgan's Uphill Fight' by Ferguson, Donald, readers are introduced to Hugh Morgan, a determined and resilient young man who faces numerous challenges as he navigates his high school years. From academic struggles to personal conflicts, Hugh must find the strength to overcome adversity and prove his worth to himself and those around him. As he forms deep friendships and discovers unexpected allies, Hugh's uphill battle becomes a journey of self-discovery and triumph.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Coming-of-Age, School Drama

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Self-Identity
