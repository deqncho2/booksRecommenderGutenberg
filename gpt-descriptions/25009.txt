Synopsis: 
'The World's Greatest Books — Volume 14 — Philosophy and Economics' is a captivating collection of thought-provoking works that delve into the realms of philosophy and economics. From the timeless wisdom of ancient Greek philosophers to the groundbreaking theories of modern economists, this volume offers a diverse range of perspectives on the fundamental questions of human existence and the complexities of economic systems. Explore the depths of human thought and gain a deeper understanding of the world we live in through this enlightening compilation.

Genres: Philosophy, Economics, Non-fiction

Themes: Existentialism, Ethics, Political Economy, Rationality, Human Nature
