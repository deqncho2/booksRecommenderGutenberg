Synopsis: In 'The House of Dreams-Come-True' by Margaret Pedler, a young woman named Joan finds herself drawn to a mysterious house that holds the promise of making dreams come true. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the house, she uncovers a web of hidden desires, forbidden love, and unexpected consequences. With each dream fulfilled, Joan must confront the true nature of her own desires and the price she is willing to pay for them.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Desire, Forbidden Love, Consequences
