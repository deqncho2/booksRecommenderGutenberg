Synopsis: 

In 'Historical Record of the Eighteenth, or the Royal Irish Regiment of Foot', Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and remarkable services of the regiment from 1684 to 1848. This meticulously researched account delves into the regiment's origins, its involvement in significant historical events, and the brave individuals who served within its ranks. From battles fought on European soil to expeditions across the globe, Cannon's narrative paints a vivid picture of the regiment's trials, triumphs, and enduring legacy.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Courage, Historical legacy
