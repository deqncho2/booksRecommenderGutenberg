Synopsis: In 'Metal Spinning', Painter and Tuells take readers on a captivating journey into the world of an ancient art form that combines craftsmanship, creativity, and precision. Through vivid storytelling and insightful anecdotes, they explore the secrets and techniques behind metal spinning, revealing the hidden beauty and complexity of this often overlooked craft. From the humble beginnings of metal spinning to its modern-day applications, the authors delve into the lives of the artisans who have dedicated their lives to perfecting this mesmerizing art. As readers immerse themselves in the pages, they will discover the fascinating history, the challenges faced, and the remarkable innovations that have shaped the world of metal spinning.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, Craftsmanship

Themes: Creativity, Tradition, Mastery
