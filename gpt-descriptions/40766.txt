Synopsis: In 'Nationalism' by Rabindranath Tagore, embark on a thought-provoking journey that challenges conventional notions of patriotism and explores the complexities of identity and belonging. Through a series of insightful essays, Tagore delves into the dangers of narrow-minded nationalism and advocates for a more inclusive and harmonious world. With his eloquent prose and profound observations, Tagore invites readers to question the boundaries that divide us and to embrace a broader understanding of humanity.

Genres: Essay, Non-fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Nationalism, Identity, Patriotism, Unity, Globalism
