Synopsis: In this captivating autobiography, Andrew Carnegie, a self-made industrialist and philanthropist, takes readers on a remarkable journey through his life, from his humble beginnings as a Scottish immigrant to his rise as one of the wealthiest men in America. With vivid storytelling and insightful reflections, Carnegie shares his experiences in the steel industry, his encounters with influential figures such as Thomas Edison and Abraham Lincoln, and his unwavering commitment to giving back to society. This inspiring tale of ambition, success, and the pursuit of a greater purpose will leave readers both enthralled and inspired.

Genres: Autobiography, Business, History

Themes: Ambition, Success, Philanthropy, Social Responsibility
