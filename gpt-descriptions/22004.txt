Synopsis: 
In 'The Genius' by Margaret Horton Potter, a brilliant but reclusive artist named Emily finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she receives a mysterious package containing a cryptic message. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Emily discovers a hidden world of secrets, art forgery, and unexpected alliances. With her unique artistic abilities and a determination to uncover the truth, Emily embarks on a thrilling journey that will challenge her perceptions of art, identity, and the power of creativity.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Art Fiction

Themes: Artistic genius, Identity, Deception, Power of creativity
