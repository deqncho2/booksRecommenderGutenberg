Synopsis: In "The New Year's Carol" by Johanna Spyri, a young girl named Verena finds herself caught between the traditions of her family and the allure of the outside world. As she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, Verena encounters unexpected friendships, challenges societal norms, and learns the power of following her own dreams. Set against the backdrop of a quaint Swiss village, this heartwarming tale explores the complexities of family, the pursuit of happiness, and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Family

Themes: Tradition vs. Modernity, Self-discovery, Individuality
