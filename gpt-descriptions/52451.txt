Synopsis: 
'Living Bayonets: A Record of the Last Push' by Dawson, Coningsby is a gripping and intense account of the final offensive push during a war. Through vivid and visceral storytelling, the author takes readers on a harrowing journey alongside a group of soldiers as they face the brutal realities of combat, camaraderie, and the indomitable human spirit. As the soldiers navigate the chaos and horrors of war, they are forced to confront their own fears, question their beliefs, and find strength in the bonds they forge with one another. This powerful narrative captures the essence of the human experience in the face of adversity and the sacrifices made in the pursuit of victory.

Genres: War fiction, Historical fiction, Action

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Survival
