Synopsis: In 'Wager of Battle: A Tale of Saxon Slavery in Sherwood Forest' by Herbert, Henry William, a young Saxon nobleman finds himself thrust into a world of treachery and injustice when he is captured and enslaved by the Normans. As he struggles to survive in the depths of Sherwood Forest, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of wits and battles against the oppressive forces that seek to keep him and his people in chains. With the help of a band of outlaws led by the legendary figure of Robin Hood, he embarks on a thrilling journey of courage, loyalty, and the fight for freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Oppression, Freedom, Loyalty, Justice
