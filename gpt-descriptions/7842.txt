Synopsis: In 'The Rise of Iskander' by Benjamin Disraeli, a young and ambitious protagonist embarks on a journey that takes him from humble beginnings to the heights of power and influence. Set against a backdrop of political intrigue, romance, and adventure, this gripping tale follows Iskander's relentless pursuit of his dreams, as he navigates treacherous waters and faces numerous challenges along the way. With unexpected twists and turns, the story unfolds with a sense of anticipation, leaving readers eager to discover the fate of Iskander and the world he inhabits.

Genres: Historical fiction, political thriller, romance

Themes: Ambition, power, love, betrayal
