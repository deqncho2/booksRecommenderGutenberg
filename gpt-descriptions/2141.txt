Synopsis: In 'Strictly Business: More Stories of the Four Million', Henry, O. takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of ordinary people facing extraordinary circumstances. From a struggling artist to a cunning conman, each story delves into the complexities of ambition, love, and the pursuit of success in the bustling city. With his signature wit and insightful storytelling, Henry, O. paints a vivid picture of the human condition, leaving readers eager to uncover the outcomes of these intriguing tales.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Ambition, Love, Success, Human Condition
