Synopsis: In "The Historical Christ," Conybeare delves into the controversial views of prominent scholars J.M. Robertson, A. Drews, and W.B. Smith regarding the existence of the historical Jesus. Through a meticulous investigation, he challenges their theories and presents a compelling argument that will leave readers questioning their own beliefs about the life of Christ.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, Historical Analysis

Themes: Historical Jesus, Biblical Criticism, Religious Scholarship
