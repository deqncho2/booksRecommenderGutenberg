Synopsis: In 'An Artist in Crime' by Ottolengui, Rodrigues, a renowned artist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and deception when a valuable painting goes missing. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he discovers a world of art forgery, secret identities, and unexpected alliances. With his artistic skills and keen eye for detail, he must navigate the treacherous art underworld to uncover the truth and protect his own reputation. This thrilling tale of crime and artistry will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Art forgery, Identity, Deception
