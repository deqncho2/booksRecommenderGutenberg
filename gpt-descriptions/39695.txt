Synopsis: In 'The Story of Switzerland', Stead and Hug take readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and cultural tapestry of Switzerland. From the majestic Swiss Alps to the bustling cities, this book unveils the secrets and stories that have shaped the nation. Through vivid storytelling and fascinating anecdotes, the authors delve into the struggles, triumphs, and unique characteristics that define Switzerland. From the legendary William Tell to the birth of the Red Cross, this book offers a captivating exploration of a country that has become a symbol of neutrality, innovation, and natural beauty.

Genres: History, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: National identity, Cultural heritage, Historical events, Natural beauty
