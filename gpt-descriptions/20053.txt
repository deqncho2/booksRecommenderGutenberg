Synopsis: The Rover Boys in the Air; Or, From College Campus to the Clouds takes readers on an exhilarating adventure with the three Rover brothers as they embark on a daring journey that will test their courage and ingenuity. When a mysterious airship appears on the horizon, the boys find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of intrigue and danger. As they navigate through treacherous skies and encounter unexpected allies and foes, the Rover boys must rely on their wits and resourcefulness to unravel the secrets of the airship and save the day. This thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies ahead for the intrepid Rover brothers.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Ingenuity, Friendship, Exploration
