Synopsis: 
In 'Baboo Jabberjee, B.A.', we follow the hilarious misadventures of Baboo Jabberjee, a young Indian man who travels to England to pursue his education. However, his journey is far from smooth as he encounters a series of comical and absurd situations that challenge his understanding of British culture and society. From mistaken identities to cultural clashes, Baboo's experiences provide a satirical commentary on the clash between Eastern and Western values, as well as the complexities of identity and assimilation.

Genres: 
Satire, Comedy, Fiction

Themes: 
Cultural clash, Identity, Assimilation
