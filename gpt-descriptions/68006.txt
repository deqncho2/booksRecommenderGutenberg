Synopsis: 
In 'Recoil' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. However, his invention attracts the attention of powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess it. As the scientist fights to protect his creation, he is thrust into a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with intrigue, betrayal, and unexpected alliances.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Technological advancement, Power struggles, Morality
