Synopsis: In 'The Death of the Gods: (Christ and Antichrist, 1 of 3)' by Dmitry Sergeyevich Merezhkovsky, a captivating tale unfolds as the clash between Christ and Antichrist looms over humanity. Set in a tumultuous era of religious and political upheaval, the story follows the lives of two extraordinary individuals who embody these opposing forces. As their paths intertwine, the fate of the world hangs in the balance, leading to a climactic confrontation that will forever alter the course of history.

Genres: Historical fiction, philosophical fiction, religious fiction

Themes: Good vs. evil, faith and doubt, the nature of divinity, the power of belief, the struggle for salvation
