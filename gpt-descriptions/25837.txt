Synopsis: 
In the small, idyllic town of Mayville, strange occurrences begin to unfold when a mysterious newcomer, May, arrives. As the townspeople become captivated by her enigmatic charm, they soon find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected twists. With each passing day, the madness within Mayville intensifies, leaving everyone questioning their own sanity and the true nature of May's intentions. As the truth slowly unravels, the residents of Mayville must confront their deepest fears and darkest desires, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Madness, Small-town Secrets
