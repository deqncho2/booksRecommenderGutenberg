Synopsis: 
'The Irish Nuns at Ypres: An Episode of the War' by Columban, M., Dame is a gripping historical novel set during World War I. The story follows a group of Irish nuns who find themselves in the midst of the devastating Battle of Ypres. As the war rages on, the nuns must navigate the horrors of the battlefield while also grappling with their own personal struggles and the challenges of their faith. Amidst the chaos and destruction, they find strength, resilience, and unexpected connections that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Religious Fiction

Themes: Faith and spirituality, resilience in the face of adversity, the impact of war on individuals and communities, the power of human connection.
