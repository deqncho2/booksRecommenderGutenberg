Synopsis: In 'Combed Out' by F. A. Voigt, a young woman named Alice finds herself caught in the midst of a dangerous conspiracy during World War II. As she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and lies, Alice must navigate the treacherous landscape of espionage and betrayal to uncover the truth. With her life on the line, she must rely on her wit and courage to outsmart her enemies and protect those she holds dear. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Espionage, Betrayal, Courage, Survival
