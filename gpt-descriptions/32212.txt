Synopsis: 

In 'Clean Break' by Roger D. Aycock, a seemingly ordinary man named Jack finds himself caught in a web of deceit and danger when he stumbles upon a mysterious briefcase. As he delves deeper into the secrets hidden within, Jack becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse, where trust is a luxury he cannot afford. With his life on the line, Jack must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic briefcase.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
