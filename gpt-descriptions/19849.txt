Synopsis: In 'Flag and Fleet: How the British Navy Won the Freedom of the Seas', author William Wood takes readers on a thrilling journey through the history of the British Navy, revealing the untold stories of the brave men and women who fought to secure the freedom of the seas. From epic battles to secret missions, this captivating narrative explores the strategic genius, technological advancements, and sheer determination that propelled the British Navy to become the dominant force on the world's oceans. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Wood brings to life the triumphs and sacrifices of those who shaped naval warfare and ultimately secured Britain's maritime supremacy.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Maritime history

Themes: Naval warfare, National identity, Technological advancements, Leadership, Sacrifice
