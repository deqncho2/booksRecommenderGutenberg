Synopsis: In 'The Baby's Own Aesop', Aesop's timeless fables are brought to life through enchanting illustrations by Walter Crane. This beautifully illustrated collection introduces young readers to classic tales such as "The Tortoise and the Hare" and "The Boy Who Cried Wolf," teaching them valuable life lessons in a captivating and accessible way. Each fable is accompanied by Crane's intricate artwork, making this book a delightful and educational experience for children and adults alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fables, Picture Book

Themes: Morality, Wisdom, Life Lessons
