Synopsis: In 'The Cruise of the Dazzler', a young boy named Joe Bronson finds himself caught in a thrilling adventure on the high seas. When he joins a secret society of sailors, he must navigate treacherous waters, face dangerous adversaries, and uncover the mysteries of the Dazzler. With his courage and determination, Joe's journey becomes a test of character and a quest for freedom.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Independence, Social class
