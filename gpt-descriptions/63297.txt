Synopsis: In "The Brother of a Hero" by Ralph Henry Barbour, we follow the story of Jack, a young man who idolizes his older brother, Tom, a star athlete and war hero. However, when tragedy strikes and Tom's life takes an unexpected turn, Jack is forced to confront his own fears and insecurities. As he navigates the challenges of growing up and finding his own identity, Jack learns valuable lessons about courage, resilience, and the true meaning of heroism.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Family

Themes: Heroism, Identity, Resilience, Brotherhood
