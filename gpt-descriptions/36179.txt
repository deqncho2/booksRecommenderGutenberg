Synopsis: The Rover Boys on a Tour; or, Last Days at Brill College takes readers on an exciting journey with the adventurous Rover brothers as they embark on a tour during their final days at Brill College. Join Tom, Sam, and Dick Rover as they encounter unexpected challenges, unravel mysteries, and face dangerous situations that test their courage and loyalty. As they navigate through thrilling escapades, the brothers must rely on their wit and resourcefulness to overcome obstacles and protect their friends. With their bond as brothers and their determination to do what is right, the Rovers prove that no adventure is too daunting for them to conquer.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Brotherhood, Courage, Loyalty
