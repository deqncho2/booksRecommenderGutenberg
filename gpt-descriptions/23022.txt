Synopsis: In 'Red Rose and Tiger Lily; Or, In a Wider World' by L. T. Meade, two young girls from different backgrounds find themselves thrown together in a thrilling adventure. Red Rose, a spirited and independent orphan, and Tiger Lily, a sheltered and privileged heiress, must navigate the complexities of friendship, loyalty, and the pursuit of their dreams. As they journey through a world filled with secrets, danger, and unexpected twists, they discover the true meaning of courage and the power of sisterhood. Will their bond be strong enough to overcome the challenges they face? Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-Age. Themes: Friendship, Identity, Resilience.
