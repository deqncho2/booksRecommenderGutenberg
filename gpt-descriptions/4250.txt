Synopsis: In 'Imperial Purple' by Edgar Saltus, a captivating tale unfolds as the lives of two ambitious men, one a Roman Emperor and the other a Persian King, intertwine in a battle for power, love, and immortality. Set against the backdrop of ancient Rome and Persia, this historical fiction takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with political intrigue, forbidden romance, and the pursuit of eternal glory. As the empires clash and destinies collide, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the true nature of power is revealed. Will these men be consumed by their insatiable thirst for dominance, or will they find redemption in the face of their own mortality?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Power, Ambition, Love, Mortality
