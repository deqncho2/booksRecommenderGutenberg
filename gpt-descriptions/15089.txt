Synopsis: 
In 'The Deserter' by Richard Harding Davis, a young soldier finds himself torn between duty and his own moral compass during the heat of battle. As the war rages on, he must make a life-altering decision that will test his loyalty, honor, and the true meaning of bravery. With vivid descriptions and intense emotions, this gripping tale explores the complexities of war and the internal struggles of those caught in its midst.

Genres: War fiction, Historical fiction, Adventure

Themes: Duty and loyalty, Morality and ethics, Bravery and sacrifice
