Synopsis: In 'An Account of the Escape of Six Federal Soldiers from Prison at Danville, Va.', author W.H. Newlin takes readers on a thrilling journey as he recounts the daring escape of six Union soldiers from a Confederate prison during the American Civil War. Through vivid descriptions and heart-pounding moments, the author paints a vivid picture of the soldiers' harrowing escape, their resourcefulness, and the risks they take to regain their freedom. As the story unfolds, readers will be captivated by the soldiers' determination and the obstacles they face, making this a gripping tale of courage and resilience.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Freedom, Survival
