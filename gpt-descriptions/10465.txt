Synopsis: In "The Outdoor Girls of Deepdale; Or, camping and tramping for fun and health," four adventurous young women embark on a thrilling journey filled with mystery, friendship, and outdoor exploration. As they set out on a camping trip, they stumble upon a hidden secret that leads them on an unexpected adventure. With their resourcefulness and determination, the Outdoor Girls navigate through challenges, uncovering clues, and forming unbreakable bonds along the way. Will they solve the mystery and make it back home safely?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Independence, Problem-solving
