Synopsis: 

'Blue Bonnet in Boston; or, Boarding-School Days at Miss North's' follows the adventures of Blue Bonnet, a spirited young girl from Texas, as she embarks on a new chapter of her life at a prestigious boarding school in Boston. Blue Bonnet must navigate the challenges of fitting in with her classmates, adjusting to the strict rules of the school, and discovering her own identity in a new environment. Along the way, she forms unexpected friendships, encounters exciting opportunities, and learns valuable life lessons that shape her journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Young Adult, Fiction

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Self-discovery, Adaptation
