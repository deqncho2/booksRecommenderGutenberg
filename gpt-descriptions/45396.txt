Synopsis: 

'From the Cape to Cairo: The First Traverse of Africa from South to North' takes readers on an extraordinary adventure across the vast and untamed continent of Africa. Ewart Scott Grogan and Arthur H. Sharp embark on a daring expedition, facing treacherous landscapes, hostile tribes, and unpredictable wildlife as they strive to become the first to traverse the entire length of Africa, from the Cape of Good Hope to Cairo. Their journey is filled with thrilling encounters, heart-stopping moments, and unexpected friendships, providing a captivating account of their remarkable feat.

Genres: Adventure, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Cultural encounters
