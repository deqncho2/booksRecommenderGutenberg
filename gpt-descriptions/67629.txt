Synopsis: 
'An Illustrated Dictionary of Words used in Art and Archaeology' is a comprehensive guide that takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate world of art and archaeology. Written by John W. Mollett, this dictionary delves into the meanings and origins of terms frequently used in various artistic disciplines, including architecture, sculpture, painting, pottery, and more. With its beautifully illustrated pages, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the language and concepts that shape the artistic and archaeological realms, making it an indispensable resource for both enthusiasts and professionals alike.

Genres: Reference, Art, Archaeology

Themes: Artistic terminology, Historical context, Cultural significance
