Synopsis: In 'Too Old for Dolls: A Novel' by Ludovici, Anthony M., we follow the journey of Emma, a young woman who finds herself trapped in a world where reality and fantasy intertwine. As she navigates through the complexities of adulthood, Emma discovers a hidden realm where her childhood dolls come to life, revealing secrets and challenges she never anticipated. With each encounter, Emma is forced to confront her past, face her fears, and ultimately find her true self. This captivating tale explores the blurred boundaries between imagination and reality, and the transformative power of embracing one's inner child.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Imagination, Identity, Growing up, Self-discovery
