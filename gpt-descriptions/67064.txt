Synopsis: In "The Character and Happiness of Them That Die in the Lord," William Dealtry delivers a powerful sermon in honor of the late Rev. John Owen. Through his words, Dealtry explores the profound impact of a life lived in devotion to the Lord and the ultimate happiness that awaits those who die in His grace. With heartfelt anecdotes and biblical references, the sermon delves into the character traits and virtues that define those who have found salvation, offering a glimpse into the transformative power of faith and the eternal joy that awaits believers.

Genres: Sermon, Religious Literature

Themes: Faith, Salvation, Eternal Happiness
