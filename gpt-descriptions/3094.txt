Synopsis: In 'Red Eve' by H. Rider Haggard, a young woman named Eve is thrust into a world of danger and intrigue when she becomes entangled in a web of political conspiracies and forbidden love. Set against the backdrop of medieval England, Eve must navigate treacherous alliances and face her own inner demons as she fights for her survival and the ones she holds dear. With unexpected twists and a captivating romance, 'Red Eve' is a thrilling tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Political Intrigue, Female Empowerment
