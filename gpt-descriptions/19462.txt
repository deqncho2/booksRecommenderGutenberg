Synopsis: In 'The Price' by Lynde Francis, a renowned art dealer is presented with a mysterious and valuable painting that could change his life forever. As he delves into the origins of the artwork, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and danger. With each step closer to the truth, he must confront his own past and make a decision that could have far-reaching consequences. This gripping tale of art, obsession, and redemption will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Art, Identity, Morality
