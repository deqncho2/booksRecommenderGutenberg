Synopsis: 
In 'Initials Only' by Anna Katharine Green, a mysterious and wealthy woman named Mrs. Leroy desires to uncover the truth behind a tragic event that occurred years ago. She enlists the help of a young lawyer, Everett Raymond, who becomes entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden identities. As Everett delves deeper into the case, he discovers that the truth may be more dangerous than he ever imagined, and that the past has a way of catching up with those who try to bury it.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Identity, Justice
