Synopsis: In 'The Girl from Alsace: A Romance of the Great War, Originally Published under the Title of Little Comrade', we follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Marie, who finds herself caught in the midst of the tumultuous events of World War I. Hailing from the picturesque region of Alsace, Marie's life takes an unexpected turn when she becomes entangled with a group of soldiers from opposing sides. As she navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and survival, Marie's strength and resilience are put to the ultimate test. This gripping tale of war, sacrifice, and the indomitable human spirit will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Resilience, Conflict and Loyalty
