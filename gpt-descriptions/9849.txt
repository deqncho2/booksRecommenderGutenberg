Synopsis: In 'The Brown Mask' by Percy James Brebner, a mysterious figure known as the Brown Mask emerges in London, wreaking havoc and instilling fear in the hearts of the city's residents. As the police struggle to catch the elusive criminal, a young journalist becomes entangled in the dangerous game of cat and mouse, determined to uncover the truth behind the Brown Mask's identity. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Identity, Deception, Justice
