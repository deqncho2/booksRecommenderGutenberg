Synopsis:
'Beechcroft at Rockstone' by Charlotte M. Yonge is a captivating novel that follows the lives of the May family as they navigate the challenges and joys of their country estate, Beechcroft. The story unfolds through the eyes of various family members, each with their own unique struggles and desires. As the May family faces unexpected events and encounters, they must confront their own fears, make difficult choices, and ultimately find their place in the world. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, 'Beechcroft at Rockstone' is a compelling tale of love, loss, and the enduring power of family bonds.

Genres:
Family drama, Historical fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Family dynamics, Identity, Duty and responsibility
