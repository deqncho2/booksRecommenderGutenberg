Synopsis: 
In this captivating volume, delve into the brilliant mind of Alexander Hamilton through his extensive correspondence and political writings. This collection offers a unique insight into Hamilton's thoughts and ideas, showcasing his influential role in shaping the early years of the United States. From his passionate defense of the Constitution to his astute observations on foreign policy, Hamilton's words resonate with timeless relevance. Discover the man behind the Federalist Papers as you explore his diverse range of writings, both civil and military, that shaped the course of American history.

Genres: 
Biography, Political Science, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: 
Founding Fathers, American Revolution, Constitutional Law, Nation Building
