Synopsis: In 'A Dark Night's Work' by Elizabeth Gaskell, a mysterious and haunting secret threatens to unravel the lives of two siblings. As the truth slowly emerges, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of deception, guilt, and the consequences of their actions. With each passing night, the darkness deepens, and the siblings must confront the terrifying consequences of their past choices.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Guilt, Secrets, Redemption, Consequences of Actions
