Synopsis: In 'A Legend of Goat Island' by Peter A. Porter, a mysterious island off the coast becomes the setting for a thrilling adventure. When a group of friends stumble upon the island's dark secret, they are drawn into a dangerous quest that will test their courage and loyalty. As they uncover the island's hidden history, they must confront their deepest fears and make difficult choices that will determine their fate. With its blend of mystery, suspense, and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Secrets
