Synopsis: 
In 'The Defence of Duffer's Drift', a young officer finds himself in command of a small group of soldiers defending a seemingly insignificant outpost. As he faces a series of unexpected challenges and deadly encounters, he must quickly learn from his mistakes and adapt his tactics to survive. With each failed defense, he gains valuable insights that shape his approach to the next battle. Will he be able to outsmart the enemy and successfully defend Duffer's Drift?

Genres: Military fiction, War fiction, Tactical fiction

Themes: Leadership, Tactical decision-making, Adaptability, Learning from mistakes
