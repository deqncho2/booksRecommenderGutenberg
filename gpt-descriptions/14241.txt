Synopsis: 
In 'More English Fairy Tales', readers will embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of English folklore. This collection of timeless tales features a mischievous imp who wreaks havoc, a brave young girl who outwits a cunning witch, and a clever blacksmith who encounters a mysterious creature. From magical creatures to daring adventures, these stories will transport readers to a realm where anything is possible. Prepare to be spellbound by the whimsical and imaginative tales that have been passed down through generations.

Genres: 
Fairy tales, folklore, fantasy

Themes: 
Magic, bravery, trickery, transformation
