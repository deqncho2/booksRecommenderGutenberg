Synopsis: In 'The Soul Stealers' by Chester S. Geier, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances that leave the community in a state of fear and paranoia. As the townspeople struggle to uncover the truth behind these unsettling events, they soon realize that they are facing an ancient evil that feeds on the souls of the living. With time running out and the stakes higher than ever, a group of unlikely heroes must come together to confront the malevolent force and save their town from eternal darkness.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Sacrifice, Community
