Synopsis: 

In 'Allied Cookery: British, French, Italian, Belgian, Russian', embark on a culinary journey through the flavors of World War I. This unique cookbook brings together a collection of recipes from five different countries, each representing a different Allied nation during the war. From hearty British stews to delicate French pastries, from rich Italian pasta dishes to comforting Belgian soups, and from traditional Russian delicacies to inventive wartime substitutions, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the diverse cuisines that sustained the soldiers and civilians during a time of conflict. 

Genres: Cookbook, History, Culinary

Themes: World War I, International Cuisine, Food and Culture
