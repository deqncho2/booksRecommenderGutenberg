Synopsis: In 'Poppy's Presents', a young girl named Poppy discovers a mysterious box in her attic that holds the power to grant her every wish. As she embarks on a magical adventure, Poppy learns valuable lessons about the true meaning of happiness and the importance of appreciating what she already has. With each wish, Poppy's world becomes more enchanting, but also more complicated. Will she be able to find a balance between her desires and the happiness that comes from within?

Genres: Children's Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Gratitude, Self-discovery, Contentment
