Synopsis:
'The Lighthouse' by R. M. Ballantyne is a thrilling adventure set in the treacherous world of the high seas. The story follows a group of young sailors who find themselves stranded on a remote island after a shipwreck. As they struggle to survive in this harsh and unforgiving environment, they stumble upon a mysterious lighthouse that holds secrets beyond their wildest imagination. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must unravel the enigma of the lighthouse and confront their deepest fears to find a way back home.

Genres:
Adventure, Mystery, Survival

Themes:
Courage, Friendship, Exploration
