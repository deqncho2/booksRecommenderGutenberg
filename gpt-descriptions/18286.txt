Synopsis: In the small town of Old Church, Virginia, a young woman named Sue is torn between her duty to her family and her desire for independence. When a charming miller arrives in town, Sue's life takes an unexpected turn, leading her on a journey of self-discovery, love, and the pursuit of her dreams. Set against the backdrop of the post-Civil War South, this captivating novel explores the complexities of societal expectations, the power of personal choice, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, coming-of-age

Themes: Duty vs. independence, societal expectations, self-discovery, love, resilience
