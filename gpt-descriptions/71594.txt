Synopsis: In "Uncle Wiggily on the Farm," Uncle Wiggily embarks on an exciting adventure as he sets out to hunt for eggs. Little does he know that his search will lead to unexpected surprises and cause quite a commotion. Meanwhile, the mischievous froggie boys, Bully and Bawly, find themselves in a series of amusing escapades. As the story unfolds, Uncle Wiggily also lends a helping hand to Jane, assisting her with the daunting task of house cleaning. This delightful tale is filled with laughter, excitement, and heartwarming moments that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Helping others, Overcoming challenges
