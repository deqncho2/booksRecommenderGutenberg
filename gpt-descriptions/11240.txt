Synopsis: In 'The Apartment Next Door' by Johnston, William, a young woman named Mary is intrigued by her new neighbor, Mr. Ransom, who lives in the apartment next door. As Mary becomes more acquainted with Mr. Ransom, she discovers that he leads a mysterious and unconventional life. Drawn into his world, Mary finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and unexpected events that will challenge her perception of reality and test her own limits.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Perception, Secrets, Intrigue
