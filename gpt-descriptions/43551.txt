Synopsis: In the small coastal town of St. Piran, a mysterious herd of white horses appears on the beach, captivating the locals and sparking a series of extraordinary events. As the townspeople delve into the origins and significance of these ethereal creatures, they uncover long-buried secrets and confront their own personal demons. With its enchanting blend of folklore, mystery, and human drama, 'The White Horses' takes readers on a mesmerizing journey that explores the power of belief, the complexities of love, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Drama

Themes: Belief, Secrets, Redemption, Love, Resilience
