Synopsis: In 'The Great Green Diamond; Or, Thief Against Thief' by Stark, Inspector, a notorious thief sets his sights on the legendary Great Green Diamond, a priceless gem rumored to possess mystical powers. However, he soon finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse with a cunning detective determined to bring him to justice. As the stakes rise and the chase intensifies, both thief and detective must rely on their wits and skills to outsmart each other in a thrilling battle of wits.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Redemption, Pursuit of Power
