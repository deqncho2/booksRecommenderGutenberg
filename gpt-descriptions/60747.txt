Synopsis: In 'The Little Red Bag' by Jerry Sohl, a mysterious red bag falls into the hands of a struggling artist, leading him on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As he delves deeper into the bag's secrets, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where nothing is as it seems. With each revelation, the artist's life is forever changed, forcing him to confront his own desires and the consequences of his actions. This gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic little red bag.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Morality, Fate
