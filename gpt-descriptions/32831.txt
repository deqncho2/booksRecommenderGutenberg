Synopsis: 
In 'The Lost Door' by Dorothy Quick, a young woman named Emily discovers a mysterious door hidden within the walls of her family's ancestral home. As she ventures through the door, she finds herself transported to a captivating world filled with enchantment and danger. With each visit, Emily uncovers secrets about her family's past and her own identity, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of truth. As she navigates this hidden realm, Emily must confront her fears, make difficult choices, and ultimately decide where she truly belongs.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Family secrets, Identity, Courage
