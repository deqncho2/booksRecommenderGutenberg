Synopsis: 
In "The Book-Hunter in London: Historical and Other Studies of Collectors and Collecting," author W. Roberts takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of book collecting in London. From the bustling streets of the city to the hidden corners of rare bookshops, Roberts introduces us to a diverse cast of passionate collectors and their fascinating stories. As we delve into the lives of these book-hunters, we uncover hidden treasures, uncover long-lost manuscripts, and witness the thrill of the chase. With each chapter, Roberts paints a vivid picture of the vibrant book-collecting scene in London, leaving readers eager to explore this captivating world for themselves.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Biography

Themes: Book collecting, Obsession, History, Literary treasures
