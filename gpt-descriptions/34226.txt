Synopsis: 

"The Iron Division, National Guard of Pennsylvania, in the World War" is an authentic and comprehensive narrative that chronicles the remarkable and heroic endeavors of the 28th division during the world's greatest war. From the gallant deeds to the glorious achievements, this book delves into the gripping and awe-inspiring experiences of the soldiers who fought under the banner of the Iron Division. 

Genres: Historical, Military, Non-fiction

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, War Efforts
