Synopsis: In 'Magic and Fetishism', Alfred C. Haddon takes readers on a captivating journey into the fascinating world of ancient beliefs and rituals. Through his meticulous research and vivid storytelling, Haddon explores the mysterious practices of magic and fetishism across various cultures and time periods. From the enchanting rituals of indigenous tribes to the mystical powers attributed to objects and symbols, this book unveils the hidden realms of human spirituality and the profound impact they have on our lives.

Genres: Anthropology, Cultural Studies, History

Themes: Belief Systems, Rituals and Ceremonies, Supernatural Powers, Cultural Identity
