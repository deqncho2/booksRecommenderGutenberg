Synopsis: In 'Jack North's Treasure Hunt; Or, Daring Adventures in South America' by Rockwood, Roy, young Jack North embarks on a thrilling journey to South America in search of hidden treasure. As he faces dangerous obstacles and encounters mysterious characters, Jack's determination and resourcefulness are put to the test. With each step closer to the treasure, the stakes rise higher, leading to an exhilarating climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Discovery
