Synopsis: 
"On the Plantation: A Story of a Georgia Boy's Adventures during the War" by Joel Chandler Harris takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of a young boy named Johnny. Set during the tumultuous times of the Civil War, Johnny's life on a Georgia plantation is forever changed as he embarks on a series of thrilling adventures. From encounters with Union soldiers to unexpected friendships, Johnny's experiences shed light on the complexities of war and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: War and its impact on individuals and communities, Friendship and loyalty, Resilience and personal growth
