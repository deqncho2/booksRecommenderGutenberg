Synopsis: In 'The Teaching of Jesus' by Jackson, George, a young professor embarks on a journey to uncover the profound teachings of Jesus Christ. As he delves into ancient texts and engages with diverse perspectives, he discovers a transformative message that challenges his own beliefs and forces him to question the very essence of his faith. With each revelation, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and personal growth, ultimately leading him to a life-altering decision that will shape his future forever.

Genres: Fiction, Religious, Mystery

Themes: Faith and doubt, personal transformation, the power of teachings
