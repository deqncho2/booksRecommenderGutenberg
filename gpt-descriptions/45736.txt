Synopsis: 
'Ballades and Rondeaus, Chants Royal, Sestinas, Villanelles, etc.' is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through various poetic forms. From the enchanting ballades and rondeaus to the intricate sestinas and villanelles, this anthology showcases the beauty and versatility of these poetic structures. Each poem is a unique exploration of emotions, experiences, and the human condition, offering a rich tapestry of imagery and language that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres:
Poetry, Anthology

Themes:
Emotions, Human experience, Language and imagery
