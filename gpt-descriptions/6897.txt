Synopsis: In 'The Little Savage' by Frederick Marryat, a young boy finds himself stranded on a deserted island after a shipwreck. Alone and forced to fend for himself, he must navigate the challenges of survival and discover the secrets of the island. As he adapts to his new environment, he encounters unexpected friendships and uncovers hidden truths that will change his life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Survival

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Friendship, Nature
