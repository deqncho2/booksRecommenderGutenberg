Synopsis: 

'Half-Hours with Great Story-Tellers: Artemus Ward, George Macdonald, Max Adeler, Samuel Lover, and Others' takes readers on a captivating journey through the imaginative and enchanting worlds created by some of the greatest storytellers of their time. From the witty and humorous tales of Artemus Ward to the fantastical and thought-provoking narratives of George Macdonald, this collection offers a diverse range of stories that will leave readers spellbound. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to different eras and settings, encountering unforgettable characters and experiencing a myriad of emotions. Whether it's the whimsical adventures of Max Adeler or the heartfelt romances of Samuel Lover, this anthology promises to entertain and inspire readers with its rich tapestry of storytelling brilliance.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Anthology

Themes: Imagination, Humor, Fantasy, Love, Adventure
