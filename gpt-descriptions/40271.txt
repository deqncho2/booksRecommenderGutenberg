Synopsis: 

In "London Before the Conquest," W. R. Lethaby takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient history of London, unearthing the secrets and mysteries that lie beneath the bustling metropolis we know today. From the earliest settlements to the rise of Roman influence, Lethaby paints a vivid picture of a city steeped in rich heritage and forgotten tales. Through meticulous research and engaging storytelling, he reveals the hidden layers of London's past, shedding light on its origins, architecture, and the diverse communities that once thrived within its walls. As readers delve into the depths of this historical treasure trove, they will be transported back in time, discovering a London that is both familiar and yet entirely different from the city they know today.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Archaeology, Urban History

Themes: Urban development, Cultural heritage, Historical preservation
