Synopsis:
"The life and times of the Rev. Samuel Wesley: Rector of Epworth and father of the Revs. John and Charles Wesley, the founders of the Methodists" is a captivating biography that delves into the extraordinary life of Samuel Wesley, a prominent figure in the religious landscape of 18th-century England. From his humble beginnings as a rector in the small town of Epworth to his influential role as the father of two renowned Methodist leaders, this book uncovers the trials, triumphs, and untold stories of a man whose legacy shaped the course of religious history.

Genres:
Biography, History, Religion

Themes:
Family, Faith, Religious Reform, Legacy
