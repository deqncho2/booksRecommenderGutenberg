Synopsis: In "Barbara Hale: A Doctor's Daughter" by Lilian Garis, readers are introduced to Barbara, a young woman who is determined to follow in her father's footsteps and become a doctor. However, her dreams are challenged when she falls in love with a man who has a different vision for her future. As Barbara navigates the complexities of love, family expectations, and her own desires, she must make difficult choices that will shape her life and career.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Family drama

Themes: Pursuing one's passion, Love and sacrifice, Family expectations
