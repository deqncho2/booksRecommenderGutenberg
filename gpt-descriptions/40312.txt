Synopsis: In "The Intoxicated Ghost, and other stories" by Arlo Bates, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing and mysterious tales. From a ghostly encounter with a twist to a haunting love story set in the depths of the sea, each story presents a unique and unexpected narrative that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. With a blend of suspense, supernatural elements, and thought-provoking twists, this collection promises to keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Romance

Themes: Ghostly encounters, Love and loss, Unexpected twists
