Synopsis: 
'Arohanui: Letters from Shoghi Effendi to New Zealand' is a captivating collection of letters written by Shoghi Effendi, the Guardian of the Bahá'í Faith, to the Bahá'í community in New Zealand. These heartfelt and inspiring letters offer a unique glimpse into the spiritual guidance and wisdom of Shoghi Effendi, as he addresses various challenges and opportunities faced by the Bahá'í community in New Zealand. Through his eloquent words, Shoghi Effendi encourages unity, perseverance, and the pursuit of spiritual growth, while also shedding light on the global vision of the Bahá'í Faith. This collection of letters serves as a testament to the enduring power of faith and the transformative impact of Shoghi Effendi's leadership.

Genres: Spirituality, Religion, Letters

Themes: Faith, Unity, Spiritual Guidance, Leadership
