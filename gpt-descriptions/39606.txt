Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Old Cornwall, England, a young boy named Ande Trembath embarks on a thrilling adventure that will test his courage and reveal the secrets of his family's past. As he delves into the mysteries of his ancestors, Ande discovers a hidden world filled with ancient legends, magical creatures, and a destiny that he never could have imagined. With danger lurking at every turn, Ande must navigate treacherous landscapes and face formidable foes to uncover the truth and fulfill his extraordinary destiny.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family legacy, Coming of age, Mythology, Identity
