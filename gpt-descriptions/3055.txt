Synopsis: In 'The Wood Beyond the World' by William Morris, a young man named Golden Walter embarks on a perilous journey to a mysterious land beyond his own. There, he encounters enchanting landscapes, treacherous adversaries, and a captivating woman who holds the key to his destiny. As Golden Walter navigates this fantastical realm, he must confront his deepest fears and make choices that will shape his fate forever.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Quest for self-discovery, Love and desire, Good versus evil
