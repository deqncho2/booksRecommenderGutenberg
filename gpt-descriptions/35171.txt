Synopsis: 
In the aftermath of the Trojan War, the once-proud city of Troy lies in ruins, and its women are left to face the devastating consequences. "The Trojan Women of Euripides" delves into the lives of the women who have lost everything - their husbands, their children, and their homeland. Through their poignant and powerful voices, we witness their resilience, their grief, and their unwavering determination to survive amidst the horrors of war. As they navigate the treacherous aftermath, the women confront the harsh realities of fate, the cruelty of conquerors, and the enduring strength of the human spirit.

Genres: 
Tragedy, Drama

Themes: 
War and its aftermath, Loss and grief, Resilience and survival
