Synopsis: In "What Luck! A Study in Opposites" by Abbie Farwell Brown, two unlikely friends, Lucy and Max, find themselves in a series of humorous and adventurous situations. Lucy, a cautious and organized girl, is paired with Max, a spontaneous and carefree boy, for a school project. As they navigate their differences, they discover that their contrasting personalities might just be the perfect recipe for unexpected fun and friendship. From unexpected encounters with animals to daring escapades, Lucy and Max's journey is filled with laughter, surprises, and valuable life lessons.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Acceptance, Embracing Differences
