Synopsis: In 'Three Acres and Liberty' by Hall, Bolton, a young couple embarks on a daring adventure to create their own self-sufficient farm in the English countryside. As they face numerous challenges and unexpected obstacles, they discover the true meaning of freedom and the power of the land. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, this book takes readers on a journey of determination, resilience, and the pursuit of a simpler life.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Self-sufficiency, Freedom, Resilience
