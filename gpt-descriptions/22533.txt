Synopsis: 

'Life of Schamyl: And Narrative of the Circassian War of Independence Against Russia' is a captivating historical account that delves into the life of Schamyl, a legendary figure who led the Circassian people in their struggle for independence against the Russian Empire. Set against the backdrop of the breathtaking Caucasus Mountains, this gripping narrative takes readers on a journey through the tumultuous events of the Circassian War, revealing the courage, resilience, and determination of a nation fighting for its freedom. As the story unfolds, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the fate of an entire people hangs in the balance. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, War

Themes: Independence, Resistance, Identity, Sacrifice
