Synopsis:
Step into the enchanting world of Russian Folk-Tales, a collection of captivating stories compiled by Afanas'ev, A. N. (Aleksandr Nikolaevich). Delve into the rich tapestry of Russian folklore as you encounter magical creatures, brave heroes, and cunning villains. From the whimsical adventures of talking animals to the haunting tales of supernatural beings, these folk-tales will transport you to a realm where imagination knows no bounds. Prepare to be enthralled by the timeless wisdom, moral lessons, and the enduring power of storytelling that have been passed down through generations. 

Genres:
Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes:
Magic, Morality, Heroism, Supernatural
