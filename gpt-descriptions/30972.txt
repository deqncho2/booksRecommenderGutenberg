Synopsis: In 'Take the Reason Prisoner' by John J. McGuire, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Elizabeth Ashby is on the verge of a groundbreaking discovery that could change the world. However, when her research attracts the attention of a powerful and mysterious organization, she finds herself caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse. As she races against time to protect her work and uncover the truth, Dr. Ashby must confront her own past and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of humanity.

Genres: Thriller, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Scientific ethics, Conspiracy, Redemption
