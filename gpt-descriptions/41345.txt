Synopsis: 

In "Windsor Castle" by Thomas Edward, readers are transported to the majestic world of one of England's most iconic landmarks. Set against the backdrop of the historic Windsor Castle, this captivating tale weaves together the lives of its inhabitants, from the royal family to the dedicated staff who serve them. As secrets unravel and unexpected events unfold within the castle's walls, the reader is taken on a journey filled with intrigue, romance, and the timeless allure of a place steeped in history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Power, Love, Intrigue
