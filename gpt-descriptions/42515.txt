Synopsis: 
In 'The Book of the Ladies: Illustrious Dames: The Reign and Amours of the Bourbon Régime,' Brantôme takes us on a captivating journey through the scandalous lives of the influential women of the Bourbon dynasty. From queens and mistresses to noblewomen and courtesans, this book unveils the hidden desires, political intrigues, and passionate affairs that shaped the French court during this tumultuous era. Brantôme's vivid storytelling and intimate knowledge of these remarkable ladies will leave readers enthralled, as they discover the untold stories behind the glamorous façade of the Bourbon Régime.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and influence, Love and desire, Political intrigue
