Synopsis: 

'Our Legal Heritage: 600-1776 King Aethelbert - King George III' by Reilly, S. A. takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of English law from the reign of King Aethelbert to King George III. This meticulously researched and engagingly written book explores the fascinating legal developments, landmark cases, and influential figures that shaped the legal system of England over the course of nearly twelve centuries. From the establishment of common law to the struggles for justice and the rise of constitutional principles, this book offers a comprehensive and enlightening exploration of the legal heritage that continues to influence modern legal systems.

Genres: Legal history, Non-fiction

Themes: Evolution of law, Landmark cases, Influence of legal systems
