Synopsis: 
In 'Beeton's Book of Needlework', Mrs. Beeton takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of needlework, offering a comprehensive guide to various techniques and projects. From intricate embroidery and delicate lacework to practical dressmaking and home decoration, this book is a treasure trove of inspiration and instruction. With detailed illustrations and step-by-step instructions, Mrs. Beeton's expertise and passion for needlework shine through, making this a must-have for both beginners and experienced crafters alike.

Genres: Craft, Non-fiction, Reference

Themes: Creativity, Skill-building, Home-making
