Synopsis: In 'Heresy: Its Utility And Morality. A Plea And A Justification', Charles Bradlaugh challenges conventional beliefs and explores the controversial topic of heresy. Through thought-provoking arguments and compelling examples, he delves into the utility and morality of heretical ideas, urging readers to question established dogmas and embrace intellectual freedom. This captivating work challenges societal norms and encourages readers to reconsider their own beliefs.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Religion

Themes: Intellectual freedom, Challenging dogma, Morality, Societal norms
