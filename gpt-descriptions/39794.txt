Synopsis: In 'King-Errant' by Flora Annie Steel, a young prince finds himself thrust into a world of adventure and self-discovery when he is forced to flee his kingdom. With danger lurking at every turn, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter mysterious characters, and confront his own inner demons. As he embarks on a quest to reclaim his throne, he learns valuable lessons about courage, loyalty, and the true meaning of leadership.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Power, Redemption
