Synopsis: 
In 'The Substitute Prisoner' by Marcin, Max, a young man named Alex finds himself caught in a web of deceit and danger when he agrees to take the place of a mysterious prisoner. As he navigates the treacherous world of crime and corruption, Alex must unravel the truth behind his own identity and the secrets that surround him. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the shocking truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Corruption
