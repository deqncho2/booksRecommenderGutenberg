Synopsis: In 'Total Recall' by Sternig, Larry, a man named Douglas Quaid finds himself haunted by vivid dreams of a life he can't remember. Desperate for answers, he decides to undergo a memory implant procedure that will allow him to experience a thrilling adventure as a secret agent. However, as the line between reality and fantasy blurs, Quaid soon discovers a dangerous conspiracy that threatens not only his own life but the fate of an entire planet. With his identity in question and his survival at stake, Quaid must navigate a treacherous world where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Action

Themes: Identity, Reality vs. Illusion, Conspiracy
