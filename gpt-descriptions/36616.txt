Synopsis: 

'Project Gutenberg 4 July 1971 - 4 July 2011: Album' by Marie Lebert takes readers on a captivating journey through four decades of digital revolution. Through a collection of thought-provoking essays, interviews, and anecdotes, Lebert explores the profound impact of Project Gutenberg, the world's oldest digital library, on the way we consume and share knowledge. From the early days of digitization to the present, this album offers a unique perspective on the evolution of literature, technology, and the human experience in the digital age.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Digital revolution, Knowledge sharing, Evolution of literature, Impact of technology
