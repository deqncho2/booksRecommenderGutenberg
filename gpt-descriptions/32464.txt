Synopsis: In 'A Supplication for the Beggars' by Fish, Simon, a mysterious beggar arrives in a small town, captivating the locals with his enigmatic presence. As the beggar's true intentions slowly unravel, the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, the beggar's influence grows stronger, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of truth and the power of perception.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Deception, Identity, Power dynamics
