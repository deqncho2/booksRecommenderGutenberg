Synopsis: In 'The Trail of the Seneca' by James A. Braden, a young archaeologist embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient Native American tribe. As he delves deeper into their history, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each step along the trail, he unravels a hidden world filled with ancient artifacts, cryptic symbols, and a long-lost civilization that holds the key to a powerful secret. As the stakes rise, he must navigate treacherous landscapes and face formidable adversaries to protect the truth from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Archaeology, Native American culture, Secrets and hidden knowledge
