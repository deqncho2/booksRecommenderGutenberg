Synopsis:
In the glamorous and opulent world of New York's high society, Lily Bart, a beautiful and intelligent young woman, finds herself caught in a web of social expectations and financial constraints. As she navigates through a series of lavish parties, elegant balls, and secret rendezvous, Lily must make difficult choices that will determine her fate. Will she succumb to the pressures of society and secure her place among the elite, or will she defy convention and follow her own path to happiness?

Genres:
Literary Fiction, Social Commentary, Tragedy

Themes:
Societal Expectations, Wealth and Class, Gender Roles, Love and Marriage
