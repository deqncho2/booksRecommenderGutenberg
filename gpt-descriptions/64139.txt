Synopsis:
In "Memoirs of a Country Doll. Written by Herself," we are introduced to a charming and spirited doll named Mary. Through her captivating narrative, Mary takes us on a journey from her creation in a small countryside workshop to her adventures in the bustling city. Along the way, she encounters a diverse cast of characters, both human and doll, and experiences love, loss, and the bittersweet realities of life. With her unique perspective and insightful observations, Mary offers a poignant and enchanting tale that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes:
Identity, Resilience, Friendship, Love, Loss
