Synopsis: 
In 'The Egoist: A Comedy in Narrative', we are introduced to Sir Willoughby Patterne, a man whose ego knows no bounds. As he navigates the world of courtship and marriage, his self-absorption and relentless pursuit of his own desires lead to a series of humorous and often disastrous situations. With a cast of eccentric characters and witty dialogue, this novel takes us on a journey filled with love, deception, and the consequences of unchecked vanity.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Romance

Themes: Egoism, Love and Marriage, Social Expectations
