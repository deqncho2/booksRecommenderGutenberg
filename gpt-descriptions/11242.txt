Synopsis: In "The Life of Michelangelo Buonarroti" by Symonds, John Addington, delve into the captivating world of one of the greatest artists of all time. This biography takes readers on a journey through the tumultuous life of Michelangelo, from his humble beginnings as a young artist in Florence to his rise as a renowned sculptor, painter, and architect in Renaissance Italy. Witness the struggles, triumphs, and inner turmoil of a genius who pushed the boundaries of art and left an indelible mark on the world.

Genres: Biography, Art History, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic genius, Passion and dedication, Renaissance culture, Struggles and triumphs
