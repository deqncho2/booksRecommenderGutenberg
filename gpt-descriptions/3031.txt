Synopsis: 
'Wild Animals I Have Known' by Ernest Thompson Seton is a captivating collection of true stories that takes readers on a thrilling journey into the untamed world of wildlife. From the cunning and resourceful coyote to the majestic and noble grizzly bear, Seton's vivid and intimate encounters with these wild creatures will leave readers spellbound. Through his keen observations and deep understanding of animal behavior, Seton paints a vivid portrait of the natural world, revealing the complex and often surprising lives of these fascinating creatures. Each story is filled with adventure, heartache, and moments of profound connection between man and beast, offering a unique glimpse into the hidden lives of wild animals.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature writing, Adventure

Themes: Animal behavior, Human-animal connection, Nature's beauty and power
