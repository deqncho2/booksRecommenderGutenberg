Synopsis: In 'The Pioneer Boys of the Yellowstone; or, Lost in the Land of Wonders', a group of adventurous boys embarks on a thrilling journey to the mesmerizing Yellowstone National Park. As they explore the wonders of this untamed wilderness, they encounter unexpected challenges and face the perils of nature. Lost in this captivating land, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness, courage, and teamwork to find their way back home. Will they conquer the dangers that lie ahead and make it out of the Land of Wonders?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Survival
