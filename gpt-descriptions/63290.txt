Synopsis: In the second volume of "The Life and Times of Queen Victoria," author Robert Wilson delves deeper into the captivating story of one of the most influential monarchs in British history. As Queen Victoria navigates the complexities of her reign, readers are taken on a journey through the triumphs and challenges she faces, both on the throne and in her personal life. From political intrigue and international conflicts to her enduring love for Prince Albert, this volume offers a rich and immersive exploration of the remarkable life of Queen Victoria.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Monarchy, Power and Politics, Love and Relationships
