Synopsis: In 'The Knights Templars' by Addison, C. G., a young historian stumbles upon a hidden manuscript that reveals a shocking secret about the legendary Knights Templar. As he delves deeper into their mysterious history, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of conspiracy, betrayal, and ancient relics. With his life on the line, he must decipher the truth before it falls into the wrong hands, forever altering the course of history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Secret societies, Conspiracy, History and its impact
