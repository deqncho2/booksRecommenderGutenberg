Synopsis: 
In 'The Philistine: A Periodical of Protest (Vol. I, No. 1, June 1895)', readers are introduced to a captivating collection of essays, stories, and illustrations that challenge societal norms and provoke thought. This inaugural issue of the periodical presents a diverse range of topics, from the exploration of art and literature to the examination of social conventions and the pursuit of personal freedom. With its sharp wit and unapologetic critique, 'The Philistine' promises to captivate readers and ignite their curiosity about the world around them.

Genres: Satire, Social Commentary, Literary Magazine

Themes: Nonconformity, Individualism, Critique of Society
