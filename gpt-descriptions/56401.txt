Synopsis: 
In 'Fairy Tales from Gold Lands: Second Series' by May Wentworth, embark on a captivating journey through a magical realm filled with enchanting creatures, brave heroes, and treacherous villains. From the depths of the ocean to the highest mountaintops, these timeless tales will transport readers to a world where dreams come true and the impossible becomes possible. Prepare to be spellbound by the whimsical adventures that unfold, as love, courage, and the power of imagination intertwine to create a tapestry of wonder and awe.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Fairy Tales

Themes: Love, Courage, Imagination
