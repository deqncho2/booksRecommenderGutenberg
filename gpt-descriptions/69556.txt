Synopsis: In "Golden Rules of Medical Evidence," Stanley B. Atkinson takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate world of medical evidence. With a blend of real-life cases, expert analysis, and thought-provoking insights, Atkinson explores the crucial role that evidence plays in the field of medicine. From dissecting the complexities of clinical trials to unraveling the mysteries of statistical analysis, this book offers a fascinating exploration of how medical evidence shapes our understanding of health and influences medical decision-making. Through engaging storytelling and meticulous research, Atkinson sheds light on the golden rules that underpin the credibility and reliability of medical evidence, leaving readers with a newfound appreciation for the importance of evidence-based medicine.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Science

Themes: Evidence-based medicine, Clinical trials, Statistical analysis, Credibility, Reliability
