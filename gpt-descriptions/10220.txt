Synopsis: In "Daddy Takes Us Skating" by Howard Roger Garis, a family embarks on a thrilling adventure on the ice. Follow the lively children as they glide, twirl, and laugh alongside their loving father, creating unforgettable memories on the frozen pond. But as the day unfolds, unexpected challenges arise, testing their resilience and the strength of their familial bond. Will they overcome these obstacles and continue their joyous escapade? Find out in this heartwarming tale that captures the essence of family, love, and the exhilaration of winter sports.

Genres: Children's Literature, Family, Adventure

Themes: Family bonding, Resilience, Joy of outdoor activities
