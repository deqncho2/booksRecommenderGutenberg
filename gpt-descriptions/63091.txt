Synopsis: 

In 'The Craft of Athenian Pottery: An Investigation of the Technique of Black-Figured and Red-Figured Athenian Vases', Gisela M. A. Richter takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of ancient Athenian pottery. Through meticulous research and analysis, Richter unveils the secrets behind the creation of the exquisite black-figured and red-figured vases that have fascinated art enthusiasts for centuries. From the selection of clay to the intricate painting techniques, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the craftsmanship and artistry that went into producing these masterpieces. 

Genres: Art History, Archaeology, Craftsmanship

Themes: Ancient Greek art, Pottery techniques, Artistic craftsmanship
