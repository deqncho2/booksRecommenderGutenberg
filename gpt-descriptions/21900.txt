Synopsis: 

In 'The Principles of Breeding: or, Glimpses at the Physiological Laws involved in the Reproduction and Improvement of Domestic Animals', author S. L. Goodale delves into the fascinating world of animal breeding, uncovering the hidden secrets and intricate laws that govern the reproduction and enhancement of our beloved domestic creatures. Through a captivating exploration of the physiological processes and principles at play, Goodale offers readers a glimpse into the remarkable world of breeding, where science and nature intertwine to shape the future of our animal companions.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Agriculture

Themes: Animal reproduction, Genetics, Selective breeding, Animal husbandry
