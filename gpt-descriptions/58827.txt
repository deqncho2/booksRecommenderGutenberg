Synopsis: In 'Wedding Day' by Winston K. Marks, a seemingly perfect wedding day takes a dark turn when a mysterious event threatens to unravel the lives of the bride and groom. As secrets are revealed and tensions rise, the couple must confront their past and make difficult choices that will determine the course of their future. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this suspenseful tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Consequences of choices, Love and betrayal
