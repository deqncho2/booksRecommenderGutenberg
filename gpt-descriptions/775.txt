Synopsis: In "When the Sleeper Wakes," a man falls into a deep sleep and wakes up in a future world where he is the richest man alive. As he navigates this unfamiliar society, he discovers the dark secrets behind his wealth and the true cost of power. With thrilling twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the consequences of unchecked capitalism and the struggle for individual freedom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Wealth and Power, Social Inequality, Freedom and Oppression
