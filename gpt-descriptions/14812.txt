Synopsis: 
'A Midsummer Drive Through the Pyrenees' follows the journey of a group of friends as they embark on a road trip through the stunning landscapes of the Pyrenees mountains. As they navigate the winding roads and encounter unexpected adventures, their bonds are tested and secrets are revealed, leading to a transformative summer they will never forget. 

Genres: 
Adventure, Travel, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Friendship, Self-discovery, Nature's beauty
