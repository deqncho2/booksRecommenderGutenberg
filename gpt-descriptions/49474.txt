Synopsis: 

'Pedal and Path: Across the Continent Awheel and Afoot' takes readers on an exhilarating adventure as George B. Thayer embarks on a remarkable journey across the continent, both on his bicycle and on foot. From bustling cities to serene countryside, Thayer encounters a myriad of landscapes, cultures, and unexpected encounters that test his physical and mental endurance. As he navigates through the challenges and triumphs of his expedition, Thayer's narrative paints a vivid picture of the beauty and diversity of the American landscape, while also delving into the personal growth and self-discovery that comes with such an extraordinary undertaking.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Nature, Perseverance
