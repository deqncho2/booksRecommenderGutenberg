Synopsis: 
In 'Robin' by Bruce, Mary Grant, a young woman named Robin finds herself caught in a web of secrets and danger when she stumbles upon a mysterious artifact. As she delves deeper into the artifact's history, Robin becomes entangled in a thrilling adventure that takes her across different time periods and forces her to confront her own identity. With each revelation, Robin must navigate treacherous alliances and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of not only herself but also the world around her.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Time, Secrets
