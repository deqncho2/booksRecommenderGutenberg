Synopsis: In 'Stand Fast, Craig-Royston! (Volume II)' by William Black, the story continues to unfold in the picturesque Scottish village of Craig-Royston. As the lives of the villagers intertwine, secrets are revealed, love is tested, and unexpected alliances are formed. Amidst the breathtaking landscapes and the charming characters, a gripping tale of loyalty, sacrifice, and redemption unfolds, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the fate of Craig-Royston.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Sacrifice, Redemption, Secrets
