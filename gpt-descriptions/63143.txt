Synopsis: In 'A Broken Bond; Or, The Man Without Morals' by Carter, Nicholas, a mysterious man with a dark past finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and revenge. As he navigates through a treacherous world filled with dangerous alliances and hidden agendas, he must confront his own demons and make choices that will test the limits of his morality. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Betrayal, Redemption
