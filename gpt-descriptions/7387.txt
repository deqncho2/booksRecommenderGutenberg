Synopsis:
In "Grisly Grisell; Or, The Laidly Lady of Whitburn: A Tale of the Wars of the Roses," Charlotte M. Yonge weaves a captivating tale set during the tumultuous Wars of the Roses. The story follows the life of Grisell, a young woman cursed with a terrible affliction that has transformed her into a fearsome creature. As she navigates the treacherous world of politics and power, Grisell must confront her own inner demons and find a way to break the curse that has plagued her. With its gripping narrative and rich historical backdrop, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through love, betrayal, and redemption.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Power and Politics
