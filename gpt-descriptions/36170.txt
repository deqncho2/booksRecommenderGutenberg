Synopsis: In 'The Son of his Father' by Cullum, Ridgwell, a young man finds himself torn between loyalty to his family and his own sense of justice when he becomes entangled in a dangerous feud. As he navigates the treacherous world of the Wild West, he must confront his own identity and make difficult choices that will shape his future. With thrilling action, unexpected twists, and complex characters, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Family loyalty, Justice, Identity
