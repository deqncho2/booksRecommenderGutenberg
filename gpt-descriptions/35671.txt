Synopsis: In 'The Messenger' by Elizabeth Robins, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she stumbles upon a hidden message that could change the course of history. As she delves deeper into the dangerous world of espionage, Mary must navigate treacherous alliances, unexpected betrayals, and her own inner demons. With her life on the line, she becomes a reluctant messenger, racing against time to deliver the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Espionage, Betrayal, Identity, Courage
