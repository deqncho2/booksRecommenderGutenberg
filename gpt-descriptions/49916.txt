Synopsis:
In 'The Tenth Man: A Tragic Comedy in Three Acts', a wealthy man finds himself in a life-or-death situation during World War II. To save his own life, he must make a difficult and morally challenging decision that will have far-reaching consequences. As the story unfolds, unexpected twists and turns keep the reader on the edge of their seat, questioning the true nature of sacrifice and the limits of human compassion.

Genres:
Tragic Comedy, Drama, War

Themes:
Morality, Sacrifice, Human Compassion
