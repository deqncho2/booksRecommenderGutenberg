Synopsis:
In 'Mooswa & Others of the Boundaries' by William Alexander Fraser, embark on a thrilling adventure through the untamed wilderness of the Canadian North. Follow the captivating story of Mooswa, a young Cree Indian, as he navigates the challenges of survival, encounters with dangerous animals, and unexpected friendships. Alongside Mooswa, meet a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own unique tales to tell. As the boundaries between cultures and landscapes blur, secrets are revealed, and the true meaning of courage and loyalty is put to the test. Prepare to be captivated by this enthralling journey into the heart of the Canadian wilderness.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Cultural Identity
