Synopsis: In "Shakespeare and the Stage," Maurice Jonas takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of William Shakespeare and his theatrical genius. With a comprehensive list of theatrical terms used by Shakespeare, arranged in alphabetical order, and accompanied by enlightening explanatory notes, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the Bard's works and the intricacies of the stage during his time. From the vibrant characters to the dramatic plot twists, readers will gain a deeper understanding of Shakespeare's plays and poems, uncovering the secrets behind his timeless artistry.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Theater, Reference

Themes: Shakespearean Drama, Theatrical Techniques, Language and Wordplay
