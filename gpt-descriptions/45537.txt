Synopsis: 
Interrupted by Pansy is a captivating novel that follows the life of a young woman named Marian, whose world is turned upside down when she is faced with unexpected challenges and heartbreak. As she navigates through the trials and tribulations of life, Marian finds solace in her faith and discovers the power of forgiveness and redemption. With its compelling characters and thought-provoking storyline, Interrupted is a gripping tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Christian literature, Coming-of-age

Themes: Faith, Forgiveness, Redemption, Overcoming adversity
