Synopsis: 
'Ruthless Rhymes for Heartless Homes' is a collection of darkly humorous and wickedly clever poems by Harry Graham. From mischievous children to conniving spouses, Graham's verses explore the twisted and often hilarious aspects of human nature. With a touch of macabre and a dash of satire, these rhymes will leave readers chuckling and questioning their own morality.

Genres: Poetry, Dark Comedy, Satire

Themes: Human nature, Morality, Satire
