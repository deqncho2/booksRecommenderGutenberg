Synopsis: 
"The Hidden Words of Bahá'u'lláh" is a profound and enlightening collection of spiritual teachings and guidance, written by Bahá'u'lláh, the founder of the Bahá'í Faith. This timeless work offers a unique and transformative perspective on various aspects of human existence, exploring themes of love, unity, detachment, and the search for truth. Through its concise and poetic verses, the book invites readers to embark on a personal journey of self-discovery and spiritual growth, unveiling hidden truths that have the power to inspire and uplift the soul.

Genres: Spiritual, Philosophy, Poetry

Themes: Love, Unity, Detachment, Truth, Spiritual Growth
