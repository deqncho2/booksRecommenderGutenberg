Synopsis: In 'Banked Fires' by Savi, E. W., a young woman named Evelyn finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she is unexpectedly thrust into a world of intrigue and danger. As she navigates through a labyrinth of hidden motives and unexpected alliances, Evelyn must uncover the truth before it's too late. With its gripping plot and complex characters, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Secrets
