Synopsis: In 'The Secret of Divine Civilization', ʻAbdu'l-Bahá presents a compelling exploration of the principles and teachings necessary for the advancement and progress of society. Through a thought-provoking analysis of historical events and societal challenges, he unveils a transformative vision for the betterment of humanity. This enlightening work offers profound insights into the essential elements that can lead to the flourishing of civilization, inspiring readers to reflect on their role in shaping a brighter future.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Social Sciences

Themes: Social progress, Education, Unity, Moral development
