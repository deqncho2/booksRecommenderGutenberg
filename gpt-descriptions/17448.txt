Synopsis: In 'The Auld Doctor and other Poems and Songs in Scots', Rorie, David takes readers on a captivating journey through the Scottish countryside, exploring the lives and experiences of its inhabitants. From the humorous anecdotes of the mischievous Auld Doctor to the heartfelt songs of love and loss, this collection of poems and songs in Scots showcases the rich cultural heritage of Scotland. With vivid imagery and a lyrical writing style, Rorie, David invites readers to immerse themselves in the beauty and charm of the Scottish landscape, while delving into the complexities of human emotions and relationships.

Genres: Poetry, Songwriting, Scottish Literature

Themes: Scottish culture, Nature, Love and Loss, Humor, Human emotions and relationships
