Synopsis: In 'Freedom' by Reynolds Mack, a young woman named Lily finds herself trapped in a dystopian society where personal freedom is a distant memory. As she embarks on a dangerous journey to uncover the truth about her world, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances. With each step she takes towards freedom, Lily discovers the power of hope and the resilience of the human spirit. Will she be able to break free from the chains that bind her and ignite a revolution?

Genres: Dystopian, Science Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Freedom, Oppression, Rebellion, Identity
