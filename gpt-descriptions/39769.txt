Synopsis: 
In "Ghosts I Have Seen, and Other Psychic Experiences," Violet Tweedale takes readers on a captivating journey through her encounters with the supernatural. From eerie apparitions to inexplicable premonitions, Tweedale shares her personal experiences with ghosts and other psychic phenomena, leaving readers spellbound and questioning the boundaries of reality. As she delves into the mysterious realm of the paranormal, Tweedale's vivid descriptions and compelling narratives will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to uncover the secrets that lie beyond the veil.

Genres: Paranormal, Memoir, Occult

Themes: Supernatural encounters, Psychic phenomena, Exploration of the unknown
