Synopsis: In 'The Story of Leather' by Sara Ware Bassett, a young woman named Leather finds herself caught between two worlds - the bustling city of New York and the tranquil countryside of Vermont. As she navigates the challenges of love, family secrets, and personal growth, Leather must make difficult choices that will shape her future. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, this captivating novel explores the complexities of identity and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Love and Relationships, Personal Growth
