Synopsis: In 'Mystic London; or, Phases of occult life in the metropolis', Charles Maurice Davies takes readers on a captivating journey through the hidden realms of London. From secret societies to mysterious rituals, this book unveils the enigmatic underbelly of the city, where the supernatural and the mundane collide. As the protagonist delves deeper into the occult, they encounter a cast of intriguing characters and uncover dark secrets that threaten to consume them. With each turn of the page, the reader is drawn into a world of magic, intrigue, and the unknown, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Occult Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Occultism, Secret Societies, Supernatural, Mystery, Intrigue
