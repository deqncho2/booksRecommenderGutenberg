Synopsis: In 'A Captured Santa Claus' by Thomas Nelson Page, a group of mischievous children devise a cunning plan to capture Santa Claus on Christmas Eve. As they set their trap, they soon discover that Santa is not the jolly old man they expected, but a magical being with a heartwarming secret. Filled with enchantment and unexpected twists, this delightful tale takes readers on a captivating journey that will leave them believing in the magic of Christmas.

Genres: Fantasy, Children's Literature

Themes: Christmas spirit, Belief in magic, Kindness
