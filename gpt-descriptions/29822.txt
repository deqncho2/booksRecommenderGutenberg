Synopsis: In 'Rescue Squad' by Thomas J. O'Hara, a group of dedicated individuals form an unconventional rescue squad, determined to save lives and make a difference in their community. As they face numerous challenges and personal struggles, their bond grows stronger, and they discover the true meaning of heroism. With heart-pounding action and unexpected twists, this gripping novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Action, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Heroism, Teamwork, Sacrifice
