Synopsis: In 'The Lost Ten Tribes, and 1882' by Joseph Wild, a mysterious artifact is discovered, leading a group of adventurers on a thrilling quest to uncover the truth behind the lost tribes of Israel. As they delve deeper into ancient legends and hidden secrets, they find themselves entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where the fate of these lost tribes hangs in the balance. With each step closer to the truth, they must confront their own beliefs and face the consequences of their actions. Will they succeed in unraveling the mystery or will they become lost themselves?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Faith, Exploration
