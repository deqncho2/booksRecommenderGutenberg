Synopsis: 
"Oh, Well, You Know How Women Are!" is a delightful and humorous collection of short stories that explores the complexities and idiosyncrasies of women in early 20th-century America. From mischievous pranks to unexpected adventures, the stories follow a diverse cast of female characters as they navigate the challenges of love, friendship, and societal expectations. With wit and charm, Mary Roberts Rinehart and Irvin S. Cobb present a series of entertaining anecdotes that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what antics these women will get up to next.

Genres: Humor, Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Gender roles, Relationships, Social expectations
