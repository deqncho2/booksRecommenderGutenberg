Synopsis: In 'Lays and Legends (Second Series)', Edith Nesbit takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales filled with magic, mystery, and adventure. From ancient myths to medieval legends, each story weaves a spellbinding narrative that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. With her signature storytelling style, Nesbit transports readers to different worlds and introduces them to a cast of memorable characters, each facing their own trials and triumphs. From the depths of the sea to the heights of the sky, these tales will ignite the imagination and leave readers yearning for more.

Genres: Fantasy, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Heroism, Fate, Transformation
