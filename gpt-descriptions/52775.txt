Synopsis: 
In 'Ellis's Primary Physiology; Or, Good Health for Boys and Girls', Edward Sylvester Ellis takes young readers on an engaging journey through the fascinating world of the human body. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Ellis explores various aspects of the human anatomy, from the intricate workings of the heart and lungs to the importance of proper nutrition and exercise. With a blend of scientific knowledge and relatable storytelling, this book offers a delightful and educational experience that will leave readers eager to learn more about their own bodies.

Genres: Educational, Non-fiction, Children's Literature

Themes: Health and Wellness, Anatomy and Physiology, Self-care
