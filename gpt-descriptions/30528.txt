Synopsis: In 'The Day of the Dog' by Horne, Anderson, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious and gruesome dog attacks. As the community becomes increasingly fearful, a young veterinarian named Dr. Sarah Collins takes it upon herself to uncover the truth behind these horrifying incidents. With the help of a skeptical detective, she delves into a dark and twisted world where nothing is as it seems. As the danger escalates, Dr. Collins must confront her own fears and unravel the secrets hidden within her town before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Fear, Secrets, Courage
