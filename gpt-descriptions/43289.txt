Synopsis:
'Hänsel and Gretel: A Fairy Opera in Three Acts' is a captivating and enchanting opera that follows the adventures of two siblings, Hänsel and Gretel, as they find themselves lost in a mysterious forest. As they navigate through the magical and treacherous world, they encounter a mischievous witch who has a sinister plan. With the help of unexpected allies, the siblings must use their wit and bravery to outsmart the witch and find their way back home. Filled with beautiful music and delightful characters, this opera is a heartwarming tale of family, courage, and the power of love.

Genres: Opera, Fairy tale, Music

Themes: Family, Bravery, Love
