Synopsis: 
'Randy of the River; Or, The Adventures of a Young Deckhand' follows the thrilling journey of Randy Thompson, a young and ambitious deckhand who dreams of a better life. When Randy's father passes away, he is left with no choice but to take on the responsibilities of supporting his family. Determined to make a name for himself, Randy embarks on a series of daring adventures along the river, facing numerous challenges and encountering a cast of colorful characters. From dangerous encounters with river pirates to unexpected acts of heroism, Randy's resilience and determination are put to the test. Will he be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in his way and achieve his dreams?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Family, Social mobility
