Synopsis: In 'Ghost Beyond the Gate' by Mildred A. Wirt, a young girl named Penny Parker finds herself embroiled in a mysterious and haunting adventure when she stumbles upon an old, abandoned house. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the house, Penny uncovers a ghostly presence that seems to be desperately trying to communicate with her. With the help of her quick thinking and determination, Penny must unravel the enigma surrounding the ghost and the house before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

Themes: Ghosts, Secrets, Courage
