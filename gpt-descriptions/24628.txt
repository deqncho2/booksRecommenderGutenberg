Synopsis: 
In 'The Tale of Jimmy Rabbit: Sleepy-Time Tales' by Bailey, Arthur Scott, follow the mischievous adventures of Jimmy Rabbit as he explores the enchanting world of the forest. From encounters with talking animals to unexpected friendships, Jimmy Rabbit's escapades will captivate readers of all ages. As the sun sets and bedtime approaches, join Jimmy Rabbit on his journey of discovery and imagination.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Nature
