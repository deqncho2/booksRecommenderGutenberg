Synopsis: 
"Unto Caesar" by Emmuska Orczy, Baroness, is a thrilling historical novel set in ancient Rome. The story follows a young Roman noblewoman, who finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and danger when she becomes entangled with a powerful and enigmatic figure. As she navigates the treacherous world of Roman politics, she must make difficult choices that will not only determine her own fate but also have far-reaching consequences for the empire itself.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Power and Politics, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Self-discovery
