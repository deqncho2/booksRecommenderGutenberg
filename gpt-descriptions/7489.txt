Synopsis: 

In "Through Central Borneo; an Account of Two Years' Travel in the Land of Head-Hunters Between the Years 1913 and 1917," Carl Lumholtz takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the heart of Borneo, a land shrouded in mystery and inhabited by fearsome head-hunters. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Lumholtz recounts his thrilling adventures, encountering diverse indigenous tribes, navigating treacherous rivers, and delving into the untouched depths of the rainforest. As he immerses himself in the customs and traditions of the local people, Lumholtz unveils the hidden wonders of Central Borneo, offering a unique perspective on a land few have ever explored.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Anthropology

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Survival
