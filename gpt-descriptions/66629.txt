Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Sixty-first, or the South Gloucestershire Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment from 1758 to 1844. From the regiment's humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, Cannon delves into the triumphs and challenges faced by the soldiers who served in this esteemed unit. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the author paints a vivid picture of the regiment's role in various conflicts and campaigns, offering a fascinating glimpse into the lives of these brave men.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Military service, Regimental history, War and conflict
