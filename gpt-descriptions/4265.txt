Synopsis: 
'Heroes Every Child Should Know' is a captivating collection of timeless tales that introduces young readers to a diverse array of legendary heroes from various cultures and historical periods. From the valiant exploits of King Arthur and the noble deeds of Joan of Arc, to the daring adventures of Robin Hood and the cunning strategies of Odysseus, this anthology brings together stories of courage, honor, and resilience that will inspire and captivate readers of all ages. Through these tales, readers will embark on thrilling journeys filled with epic battles, extraordinary feats, and profound lessons that will leave a lasting impression.

Genres: 
Folklore, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Heroism, Courage, Resilience, Honor
