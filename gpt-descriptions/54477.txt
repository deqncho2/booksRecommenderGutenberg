Synopsis: In 'The Story of Live Dolls' by Josephine Scribner Gates, a mysterious dollmaker creates lifelike dolls that captivate the hearts of children and adults alike. As the dolls become increasingly popular, their enchanting presence begins to blur the line between fantasy and reality, leading to unexpected consequences. This captivating tale explores the power of imagination and the delicate balance between dreams and the real world.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Fiction

Themes: Imagination, Reality, Magic, Childhood
