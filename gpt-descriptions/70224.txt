Synopsis: In 'The Chariot of the Sun', a captivating fantasy by Roger Pocock, readers are transported to a world where ancient myths and legends come to life. The story follows a young protagonist who embarks on a thrilling quest to retrieve the stolen chariot of the sun, a powerful artifact that holds the fate of the world in its hands. As the protagonist faces formidable challenges and encounters mythical creatures, they must navigate a treacherous journey filled with danger, magic, and unexpected alliances. With vivid descriptions and a richly imagined world, 'The Chariot of the Sun' is a spellbinding adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mythology

Themes: Hero's Journey, Good vs. Evil, Mythical Quest
