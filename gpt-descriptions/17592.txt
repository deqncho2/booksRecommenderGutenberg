Synopsis: 

In 'A Narrative of the Expedition to Dongola and Sennaar', George Bethune English takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of Africa. As an American serving under Ismael Pasha, the commander of the expedition, English provides a captivating firsthand account of the challenges, triumphs, and discoveries encountered during this daring mission. From the treacherous landscapes to the encounters with local tribes, the narrative is filled with suspense, cultural insights, and unexpected twists. As the expedition pushes deeper into unknown territories, the reader is left eagerly turning the pages, wondering what lies ahead for this intrepid group of explorers.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Survival
