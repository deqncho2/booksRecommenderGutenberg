Synopsis: In 'The Fascinating Boston: How to Dance and How to Teach the Popular New Social Favorite', Alfonso Josephs Sheafe takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant world of Boston's social scene. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Sheafe explores the art of dancing the Boston, a popular social dance of the time, while also providing valuable insights on how to teach this captivating dance to others. As readers delve into the pages of this book, they will discover the secrets behind mastering the Boston's intricate steps and graceful movements, as well as the etiquette and social dynamics that accompany it. With its blend of historical context, practical advice, and captivating storytelling, 'The Fascinating Boston' is a must-read for dance enthusiasts and history buffs alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Dance

Themes: Social dancing, Teaching, Etiquette, Cultural history
