Synopsis: In 'Lady Cassandra' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, a young woman named Cassandra finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and duty, Cassandra must make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set in the early 20th century, this captivating novel explores the challenges faced by women in a rapidly changing world, and the power of following one's heart.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Gender Roles and Expectations, Self-Discovery
