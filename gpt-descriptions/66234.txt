Synopsis: In 'The Abergeldie Winter Book' by Riego de la Branchardière, Eléonore, readers are transported to the enchanting world of Abergeldie, a small village nestled in the Scottish Highlands. As winter descends upon the village, Eléonore, a young woman with a mysterious past, arrives seeking refuge. Drawn to her enigmatic presence, the villagers are captivated by her charm and beauty. However, as the winter unfolds, secrets begin to unravel, and the true nature of Eléonore's arrival becomes apparent. With the backdrop of snow-covered landscapes and the warmth of the village community, this tale weaves together love, betrayal, and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets, Identity, Redemption
