Synopsis: In 'Psycho-Phone Messages' by Grierson, Francis, a mysterious series of voice messages begins to haunt the protagonist, leaving them questioning their sanity and the reality around them. As they delve deeper into the origins of these messages, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel their life. Gripping and suspenseful, this psychological thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Perception, Deception
