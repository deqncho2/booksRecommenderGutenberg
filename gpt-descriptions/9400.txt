Synopsis: 
'A Select Collection of Old English Plays, Volume 02' is a captivating anthology that transports readers back in time to the rich and vibrant world of Old English theater. This volume features a collection of intriguing plays, each with its own unique story and characters. From tales of love and betrayal to stories of revenge and redemption, these plays offer a fascinating glimpse into the dramatic and theatrical traditions of the past. With its vivid language and compelling narratives, this anthology is sure to captivate readers and leave them eager to explore more of the Old English theatrical world.

Genres: Drama, Historical Fiction, Literature

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Revenge and Redemption, Theatrical Traditions
