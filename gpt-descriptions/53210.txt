Synopsis: 

In 'Canterbury' by W. Teignmouth Shore, a mysterious stranger arrives in the quaint town of Canterbury, setting off a chain of events that will unravel the secrets hidden within its ancient walls. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of intrigue and deception, they must confront their own pasts and face the consequences of their actions. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the dark underbelly of Canterbury, where nothing is as it seems and danger lurks around every corner.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Redemption, Small-town dynamics
