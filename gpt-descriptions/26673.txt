Synopsis: 
'At the Sign of the Jack O'Lantern' by Reed, Myrtle is a captivating mystery novel set in the quaint village of Old Chester. When a mysterious stranger arrives in town, the lives of the villagers are turned upside down. Secrets are unearthed, suspicions arise, and the residents find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and deception. As the tension mounts, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth behind the stranger's arrival and the secrets that lie hidden in the shadows of Old Chester.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town dynamics, Uncovering the truth
