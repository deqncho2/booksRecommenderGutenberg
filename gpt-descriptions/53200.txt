Synopsis: In 'The Camp Fire Girls by the Blue Lagoon' by Margaret Vandercook, a group of adventurous young girls embark on a thrilling summer camp experience filled with mystery and unexpected challenges. As they navigate the enchanting Blue Lagoon, they uncover secrets that test their courage, friendship, and resourcefulness. With each twist and turn, the girls must rely on their Camp Fire training and their own determination to overcome obstacles and solve the mysteries that surround them. Will they succeed in unraveling the secrets of the Blue Lagoon and emerge stronger than ever?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
