Synopsis: In 'Minos of Sardanes' by Charles B. Stilson, a mysterious artifact is discovered in the ruins of an ancient civilization, leading a team of archaeologists on a thrilling adventure across time and space. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the artifact, they uncover a hidden world filled with danger, ancient prophecies, and a powerful enemy determined to possess its unimaginable power. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, the team must navigate treacherous landscapes and face unimaginable challenges to unlock the artifact's true potential.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Time travel, Ancient civilizations, Power and its consequences
