Synopsis: In 'Bert Wilson at Panama' by J. W. Duffield, follow the thrilling adventures of young Bert Wilson as he embarks on a journey to the Panama Canal. Amidst the dangerous jungles, treacherous waters, and mysterious encounters, Bert finds himself caught up in a web of intrigue and suspense. With his quick thinking and resourcefulness, he must navigate through unexpected challenges and uncover hidden secrets that could change the course of history. Will Bert Wilson emerge victorious in this thrilling escapade?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Courage, Discovery
