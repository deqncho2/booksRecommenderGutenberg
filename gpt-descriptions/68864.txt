Synopsis: 

In 'Ramsey & Carmick, contract.: Letter from the Postmaster General transmitting copy of a conditional mail contract; also copies of correspondence relative to the same', a mysterious mail contract comes under scrutiny, revealing a web of intrigue and hidden agendas. As the Postmaster General delves into the correspondence surrounding the contract, secrets are unveiled and unexpected alliances are formed. With each letter, the reader is drawn deeper into a world of deception and suspense, where the truth may be more elusive than it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Power struggles, Corruption
