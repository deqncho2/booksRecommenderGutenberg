Synopsis: In 'Quarantine' by George O. Smith, a mysterious and deadly disease sweeps across the world, forcing governments to take drastic measures. As the protagonist, Dr. John Smith, races against time to find a cure, he uncovers a shocking truth that could change everything. With suspenseful twists and unexpected revelations, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Epidemic, Survival, Scientific Discovery
