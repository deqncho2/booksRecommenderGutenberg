Synopsis: In a future where technology controls every aspect of life, a man from the past becomes the key to unraveling a catastrophic event that could alter the course of history. As he struggles to adapt to this advanced society, his unpredictable nature and unique abilities make him a variable that could either save or destroy the world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Time Travel

Themes: Technology's impact on society, the unpredictability of human nature, the consequences of altering history
