Synopsis: In 'The Stowaway' by Alvin Heiner, a young and adventurous teenager named Max finds himself caught up in a thrilling and dangerous journey when he accidentally becomes a stowaway on a mysterious ship. As Max navigates through treacherous waters and encounters a crew with hidden motives, he must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to survive. Along the way, he uncovers secrets that could change the course of history forever. Will Max be able to outsmart his captors and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Survival, Discovery
