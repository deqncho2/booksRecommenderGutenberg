Synopsis: 
In 'Foundling on Venus' by De Courcy, Dorothy/ De Courcy, John, a young orphan named Lily finds herself transported to the mysterious planet of Venus. As she navigates this strange new world, Lily discovers hidden secrets and encounters extraordinary beings that challenge her perception of reality. With the help of newfound friends, she embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth about her origins and the destiny that awaits her.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Exploration, Friendship, Coming of Age
