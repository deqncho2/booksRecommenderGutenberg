Synopsis: 

In 'Brief Records of the Independent Church at Beccles, Suffolk', author S. Wilton Rix takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of nonconformity in the East Anglian counties. Focusing on the Independent Church at Beccles, Rix provides biographical notices of its ministers and offers a fascinating account of the rise of nonconformity in the region. From the struggles faced by dissenters to the triumphs of religious freedom, this book unveils the untold stories of those who dared to challenge the established norms. 

Genres: Historical, Religious, Biographical

Themes: Nonconformity, Religious Freedom, Dissent
