Synopsis: In "The Angel Adjutant of 'Twice Born Men'", Minnie Lindsay Rowell takes us on a captivating journey through the life of a remarkable woman who dedicates herself to helping those in need. From the bustling streets of London to the trenches of World War I, this inspiring true story follows the Angel Adjutant as she fearlessly fights for justice, compassion, and the redemption of lost souls. With her unwavering faith and unwavering determination, she becomes a beacon of hope in a world torn apart by war and despair.

Genres: Biography, Historical, Inspirational

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Social Justice
