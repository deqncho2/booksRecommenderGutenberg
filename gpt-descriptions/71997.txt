Synopsis: In "From Ploughshare to Pulpit: A Tale of the Battle of Life," Gordon Stables takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a young man who transitions from a humble farmer to a prominent figure in the church. This enthralling tale explores the challenges, triumphs, and personal growth that the protagonist experiences as he navigates the complexities of life, love, and faith. With unexpected twists and heartfelt moments, this story will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover the ultimate destination of the protagonist's journey.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Religious

Themes: Personal growth, Faith, Love, Life's challenges
