Synopsis: In "Love's Bitterest Cup" by Emma Dorothy Eliza Nevitte Southworth, a young woman finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is forced into a loveless marriage. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, she discovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel everything she holds dear. With passion, betrayal, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Love and sacrifice, societal expectations, hidden truths
