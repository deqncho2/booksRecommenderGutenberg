Synopsis: In 'The Slim Princess' by George Ade, a young woman named Patricia O'Rourke finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires when she is unexpectedly thrust into the world of high society. As she navigates the complexities of love, wealth, and social status, Patricia must discover her true identity and make choices that will shape her future. With humor and wit, Ade takes readers on a delightful journey through the challenges and triumphs of a young woman's quest for self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Identity, Social expectations, Love and relationships
