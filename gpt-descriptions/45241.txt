Synopsis: 

'Popular British Ballads, Ancient and Modern, Vol. 1 (of 4)' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of British ballads. From haunting tales of love and loss to thrilling stories of adventure and betrayal, this collection brings together a diverse range of ballads that have been passed down through generations. Each ballad is a window into the past, offering a glimpse into the lives and experiences of the people who once sang them. With its lyrical storytelling and timeless themes, this volume is sure to enchant readers and transport them to a world of folklore and tradition.

Genres: Folklore, Poetry, Music

Themes: Love and Loss, Adventure and Betrayal, Tradition and Folklore
