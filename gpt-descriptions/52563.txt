Synopsis: In 'The Hand of the Mighty, and Other Stories' by Kester Vaughan, prepare to be transported to a world where ordinary lives collide with extraordinary circumstances. From a mysterious hand that grants unimaginable power to a young girl's encounter with a mythical creature, these captivating tales will keep you on the edge of your seat. As the characters navigate through love, loss, and the unknown, they are forced to confront their deepest fears and make life-altering choices. With each story offering a unique twist and unexpected turns, 'The Hand of the Mighty, and Other Stories' is a collection that will leave you questioning the boundaries of reality and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Power and its consequences, Fate and destiny, Courage and resilience
