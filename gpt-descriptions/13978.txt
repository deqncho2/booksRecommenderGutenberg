Synopsis: In "The Horse Shoe: The True Legend of St. Dunstan and the Devil, Showing How the Horse-Shoe Came to Be a Charm against Witchcraft," Edward G. Flight takes readers on a captivating journey into the origins of the horse-shoe as a powerful talisman against evil. Set in medieval times, the story follows the legendary encounter between St. Dunstan and the Devil, revealing the fascinating circumstances that led to the horse-shoe becoming a symbol of protection against witchcraft. As the tale unfolds, secrets are unveiled, and the true power of faith and belief is put to the test.

Genres: Historical fiction, Folklore, Supernatural

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Faith and Belief, Symbolism
