Synopsis: 

'American Thumb-prints: Mettle of Our Men and Women' by Kate Stephens takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of extraordinary individuals who have left an indelible mark on American history. From the bustling streets of New York City to the vast landscapes of the Wild West, this collection of stories unveils the untold tales of courage, resilience, and determination that have shaped the nation. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Stephens brings to life the triumphs and tribulations of these unsung heroes, revealing the true mettle of the American spirit.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Biography, Anthology

Themes: American History, Heroism, Resilience, Identity
