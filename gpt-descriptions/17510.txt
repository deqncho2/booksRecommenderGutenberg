Synopsis: In "When the Yule Log Burns: A Christmas Story" by Leona Dalrymple, a young woman named Mary Jane finds herself caught in a web of mystery and romance during the holiday season. As she navigates through unexpected twists and turns, she discovers the true meaning of love and the power of forgiveness. Set against the backdrop of a charming small town, this heartwarming tale will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Holiday

Themes: Love, Forgiveness, Redemption
