Synopsis: In 'Color Blind' by Charles A. Stearns, a renowned artist mysteriously loses his ability to see colors, plunging him into a world of darkness and despair. As he struggles to cope with his new reality, he embarks on a journey of self-discovery and redemption, uncovering hidden truths about himself and the power of perception. With the help of an enigmatic woman who sees the world differently, he begins to question the nature of art, beauty, and the true meaning of life.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Perception, Identity, Redemption, Artistic Expression
