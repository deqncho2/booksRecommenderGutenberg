Synopsis: In 'Contraband: A Tale of Modern Smugglers' by Spencer, Erle, follow the thrilling journey of a former smuggler who is forced back into the dangerous world he thought he had left behind. When his family's safety is at stake, he must navigate treacherous waters, outsmart ruthless criminals, and confront his own dark past. With high-stakes action, unexpected twists, and a race against time, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Redemption, Family, Morality
