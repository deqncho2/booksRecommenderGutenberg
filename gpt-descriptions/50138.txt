Synopsis: In 'Doomsday Eve' by Robert Moore Williams, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a shocking discovery that could change the fate of humanity forever. As he races against time to unravel the truth, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger, where the line between good and evil blurs. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must make difficult choices that will determine the future of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Apocalyptic, Ethics, Conspiracy
