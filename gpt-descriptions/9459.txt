Synopsis: In 'Indian Legends of Vancouver Island', Alfred Carmichael takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and enchanting world of Indigenous legends from Vancouver Island. From mythical creatures and supernatural beings to heroic figures and cautionary tales, this collection of stories weaves together a tapestry of ancient wisdom and cultural heritage. As readers delve into the pages, they will be transported to a realm where nature and spirituality intertwine, where the past and present coexist, and where the power of storytelling holds the key to understanding the mysteries of life. 

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Indigenous Literature

Themes: Cultural heritage, Spirituality, Nature, Oral tradition
