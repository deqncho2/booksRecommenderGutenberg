Synopsis: In 'Billy Whiskers Out for Fun' by Frances Trego Montgomery, follow the mischievous adventures of Billy Whiskers, a clever and curious goat who always finds himself in the most unexpected situations. From causing chaos at a circus to outsmarting a group of misbehaving children, Billy Whiskers' playful antics will keep readers entertained and eager to see what he gets up to next.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Curiosity, Mischief, Problem-solving
