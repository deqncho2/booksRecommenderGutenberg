Synopsis: 
In 'The Three Impostors', a group of friends stumble upon a mysterious manuscript that leads them on a dark and twisted journey through the underbelly of Victorian London. As they delve deeper into the secrets hidden within the manuscript, they encounter a series of bizarre and enigmatic characters, each with their own hidden agenda. With each encounter, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning who can be trusted and what is truly real.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Madness, Supernatural, Victorian society
