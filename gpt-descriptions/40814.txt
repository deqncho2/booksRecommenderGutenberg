Synopsis:
'Ruth Hall: A Domestic Tale of the Present Time' follows the life of Ruth Hall, a young woman who faces numerous challenges and hardships as she navigates the complexities of society. From her struggles as a single mother to her pursuit of a writing career, Ruth's journey is filled with triumphs and setbacks, love and loss, and the constant battle to find her voice in a world that often dismisses her. Through her experiences, Ruth Hall explores the themes of gender inequality, the power of perseverance, and the importance of self-expression.

Genres:
Domestic fiction, Realistic fiction, Feminist literature

Themes:
Gender inequality, Perseverance, Self-expression
