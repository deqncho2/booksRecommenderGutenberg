Synopsis: 
Step into the enchanting world of Mother Goose as Charles Perrault takes you on a captivating journey through a collection of timeless fairy tales. From the mischievous adventures of Puss in Boots to the magical transformation of Cinderella, these beloved stories will transport you to a realm where animals talk, wishes come true, and love conquers all. Discover the origins of classic tales that have captured the hearts of readers for centuries, and let your imagination soar as you delve into the enchanting world of Mother Goose.

Genres: Fairy tales, Children's literature, Fantasy

Themes: Magic, Morality, Transformation, Love, Good vs. Evil
