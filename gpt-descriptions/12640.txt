Synopsis: In the gripping true story of the 'Trial of Mary Blandy', a young woman finds herself at the center of a scandalous murder case that captivated 18th-century England. Accused of poisoning her own father, Mary's trial becomes a spectacle of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of justice. As the courtroom drama unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the truth becomes a matter of life and death.

Genres: Historical fiction, Crime, Legal drama

Themes: Love and obsession, Betrayal, Justice and morality
