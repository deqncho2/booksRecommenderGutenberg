Synopsis: In 'The Elves of Mount Fern' by Katherine Creighton, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden world of magical creatures living in the depths of Mount Fern. As she befriends the elves and learns about their ancient traditions, Lily becomes entangled in a thrilling adventure to save their homeland from an impending danger. With the fate of the elves hanging in the balance, Lily must summon her courage and uncover her own hidden powers to protect her newfound friends and their enchanted realm.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovering hidden potential
