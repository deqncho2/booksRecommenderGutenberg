Synopsis: In 'Hiwa: A Tale of Ancient Hawaii', Edmund P. Dole takes readers on a captivating journey to the enchanting world of ancient Hawaii. Set against the backdrop of lush landscapes and vibrant traditions, this enthralling tale follows the life of Hiwa, a young warrior with a mysterious past. As Hiwa embarks on a perilous quest to uncover his true identity, he must navigate treacherous alliances, face formidable adversaries, and confront the secrets that lie within his own heart. With its richly woven narrative and vivid descriptions, 'Hiwa' immerses readers in a world of adventure, love, and self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Heritage, Courage, Love, Coming-of-age
