Synopsis: In the second volume of 'Only an Ensign: A Tale of the Retreat from Cabul,' the gripping story continues as Ensign Edmund O'Connor finds himself caught in the midst of the treacherous retreat from Cabul. With danger lurking at every turn, O'Connor must navigate through enemy territory, facing harrowing challenges and making difficult choices that will test his courage and loyalty. As the retreat becomes increasingly desperate, the young ensign's determination to survive and protect his comrades is put to the ultimate test. Will he be able to overcome the odds and make it out alive?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival
