Synopsis: 

In "The Heart of the Alleghanies; or, Western North Carolina," Grosscup and Zeigler take readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes and vibrant communities of Western North Carolina. Through vivid descriptions and engaging narratives, the authors explore the region's rich history, its unique cultural heritage, and the untamed beauty of the Alleghany Mountains. From the bustling cities to the remote mountain villages, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the people who call this region home. As the story unfolds, readers will discover the hidden secrets, unexpected adventures, and enduring spirit that make Western North Carolina a truly remarkable place.

Genres: Travelogue, Historical Fiction, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Nature and Wilderness, Community and Identity, History and Heritage
