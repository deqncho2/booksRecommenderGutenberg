Synopsis: 
'Uncle Josh's Punkin Centre Stories' by Stewart, Cal is a collection of humorous and heartwarming tales set in the fictional town of Punkin Centre. Through the eyes of Uncle Josh, a lovable and eccentric character, readers are taken on a delightful journey filled with misadventures, quirky townsfolk, and unexpected surprises. From comical encounters with animals to hilarious misunderstandings, each story is a testament to the power of laughter and the joy of small-town life. 

Genres: Humor, Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Humor, Community, Friendship, Rural Life
