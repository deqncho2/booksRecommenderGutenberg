Synopsis:
In 'Frank Merriwell's Prosperity; or, Toil Has Its Reward' by Standish, Burt L., we follow the adventures of Frank Merriwell, a young and ambitious protagonist who is determined to achieve success and prosperity. Facing numerous challenges and obstacles, Frank must navigate the treacherous world of business, where loyalty and betrayal often go hand in hand. As he strives to make his mark in the business world, Frank's integrity and determination are put to the test, forcing him to make difficult choices that will shape his future. Will Frank be able to overcome the odds and find the prosperity he seeks, or will he be consumed by the cutthroat nature of the business world?

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Integrity, Loyalty, Success, Betrayal
