Synopsis: In 'Pinocchio in Africa' by Eugenio Cherubini, the beloved wooden puppet embarks on a thrilling adventure across the vast continent of Africa. Accompanied by his loyal friend, the wise Cricket, Pinocchio encounters a myriad of captivating characters and faces numerous challenges that test his bravery and determination. From the bustling markets of Marrakech to the majestic landscapes of the Serengeti, Pinocchio's journey takes him deep into the heart of Africa, where he learns valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the importance of embracing one's true self.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Courage
