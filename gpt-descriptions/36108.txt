Synopsis: In the final volume of the Trevethlan series, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the fate of the Cornish estate hangs in the balance. As the characters navigate through love, betrayal, and redemption, they must confront their pasts and make difficult choices that will shape their future. With its captivating storytelling and richly developed characters, Trevethlan: A Cornish Story. Volume 3 (of 3) takes readers on an emotional journey filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Family, Identity, Redemption, Betrayal
