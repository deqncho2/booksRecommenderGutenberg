Synopsis: In "Samantha at the St. Louis Exposition" by Marietta Holley, follow the spirited and witty Samantha as she embarks on a thrilling adventure to the St. Louis Exposition. With her unique perspective and sharp observations, Samantha encounters a myriad of fascinating characters and experiences the wonders of the exposition. From the grand exhibits to the bustling streets, Samantha's journey is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Humor

Themes: Adventure, Exploration, Social Commentary
