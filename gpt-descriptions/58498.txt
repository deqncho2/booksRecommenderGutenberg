Synopsis: 
In "The Copernicus of Antiquity (Aristarchus of Samos)" by Thomas Little Heath, embark on a captivating journey through the life and discoveries of Aristarchus, an ancient Greek astronomer who challenged the prevailing beliefs of his time. From his humble beginnings on the island of Samos to his groundbreaking theories about the cosmos, follow Aristarchus as he dares to question the established order and revolutionize our understanding of the universe. This gripping biography unveils the struggles, triumphs, and enduring legacy of a visionary thinker who paved the way for future scientific revolutions.

Genres: Biography, History, Science

Themes: Scientific revolution, Challenging established beliefs, Legacy of ancient thinkers
