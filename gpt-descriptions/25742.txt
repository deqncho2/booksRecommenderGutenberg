Synopsis: In 'Hostage: A Terran Empire Story' by Wilson, Ann, a young diplomat finds herself caught in a dangerous game of political intrigue when she becomes the unexpected hostage of an alien civilization. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of interstellar relations, she must rely on her wit, courage, and unexpected alliances to survive. With each passing moment, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. Will she be able to outmaneuver her captors and secure her freedom, or will she become a pawn in a much larger game?

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Political intrigue, Survival, Diplomacy
