Synopsis: In 'Making Good on Private Duty: Practical Hints to Graduate Nurses', Harriet Camp Lounsbery takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of private duty nursing. Through her insightful and practical advice, Lounsbery offers a unique perspective on the challenges and rewards faced by graduate nurses in their pursuit of excellence. From navigating complex medical cases to building strong relationships with patients and their families, this book provides invaluable guidance for those entering the field. With a blend of personal anecdotes and professional expertise, Lounsbery's work is a must-read for anyone interested in the world of private duty nursing.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical, Self-help

Themes: Nursing, Healthcare, Professional Development
