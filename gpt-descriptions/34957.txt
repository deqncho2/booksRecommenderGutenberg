Synopsis: In the picturesque region of Alsace, a young girl named Fanny grows up amidst the beauty and turmoil of the early 20th century. As she navigates the complexities of family, love, and war, Fanny's journey intertwines with the lives of those around her, revealing the resilience and strength of the people of Alsace. With its captivating storytelling and vivid descriptions, "The Children of Alsace" takes readers on an emotional and unforgettable journey through a tumultuous period in history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Coming-of-age, Family drama

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Love and loss, War and its impact
