Synopsis: In 'The Doctor's Wife: A Novel', M. E. Braddon weaves a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and the consequences of one woman's desperate choices. When a young and beautiful woman marries a respected doctor, she believes she has found the perfect life. However, as secrets unravel and dark truths come to light, she is forced to confront the devastating consequences of her actions. Set against the backdrop of Victorian society, this gripping novel explores the complexities of human relationships and the lengths one will go to protect their own happiness.

Genres: Victorian Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Morality and Consequences, Society and Class
