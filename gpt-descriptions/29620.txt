Synopsis: In 'Sorry: Wrong Dimension' by Rocklynne, Ross, a brilliant scientist accidentally creates a device that allows him to travel to alternate dimensions. However, each dimension he visits seems to be more bizarre and dangerous than the last. As Ross desperately tries to find his way back home, he encounters strange creatures, mind-bending landscapes, and unexpected allies. With each dimension presenting new challenges and mysteries, Ross must navigate a treacherous journey through the multiverse, all while questioning the nature of reality itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Multiverse exploration, Identity, Perception of reality
