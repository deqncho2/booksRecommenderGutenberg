Synopsis: In 'The Crystal Ball: A Mystery Story for Girls', a group of adventurous young girls stumble upon a mysterious crystal ball that holds the power to reveal secrets and predict the future. As they delve deeper into its enigmatic abilities, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling mystery that involves hidden treasures, suspicious characters, and unexpected twists. With their friendship and courage tested, the girls must use their wits to unravel the secrets of the crystal ball and solve the perplexing puzzle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Secrets, Discovery
