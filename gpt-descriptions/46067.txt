Synopsis: In 'How To Ski and How Not To' by Caulfeild, Vivian, embark on an exhilarating journey through the world of skiing as the author shares their personal experiences, mishaps, and triumphs on the slopes. From hilarious anecdotes to valuable tips, this book is a delightful blend of humor and practical advice that will leave readers eager to hit the slopes themselves. Whether you're a seasoned skier or a complete novice, this entertaining guide is sure to captivate and inspire.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Memoir

Themes: Adventure, Perseverance, Humor
