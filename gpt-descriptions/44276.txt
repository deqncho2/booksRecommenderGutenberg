Synopsis: 
'The Toilet of Flora: or, A collection of the most simple and approved methods of preparing baths, essences, pomatums, powders, perfumes, and sweet-scented waters. With receipts for cosmetics of every kind, that can smooth and brighten the skin, give force to beauty, and take off the appearance of old age and decay' is a captivating guidebook written by Pierre-Joseph Buc'hoz. This enchanting work delves into the secrets of beauty and rejuvenation, offering a plethora of recipes and techniques to enhance one's appearance. From luxurious baths to fragrant perfumes, Buc'hoz provides a comprehensive collection of beauty rituals that promise to restore youthfulness and radiance. With a focus on natural ingredients and proven methods, this book is a treasure trove for those seeking to enhance their beauty and defy the signs of aging.

Genres: Non-fiction, Beauty and Cosmetics, Self-help

Themes: Beauty, Aging, Self-care
