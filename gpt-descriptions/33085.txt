Synopsis: In 'The Admirable Bashville', Shaw presents a captivating tale of Cashel Byron, a young man with an unconventional profession. As the story unfolds, Cashel finds himself torn between love and ambition, navigating the complexities of society and the allure of fame. With wit and sharp social commentary, Shaw explores the world of prize fighting and the sacrifices one must make to pursue their passions.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Love and ambition, Society and class, Sacrifice and passion
