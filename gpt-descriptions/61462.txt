Synopsis: 

In "Standard Methods for the Examination of Water and Sewage," the American Chemical Society, American Public Health Association, and Association of Official Agricultural Chemists come together to provide a comprehensive guide for analyzing and understanding the quality of water and sewage. This essential reference book offers a wealth of knowledge and techniques for professionals in the field, ensuring the safety and purity of our water resources. From testing methods to data interpretation, this guide equips readers with the tools to assess and address potential risks, making it an indispensable resource for anyone involved in water and sewage analysis.

Genres: Reference, Science, Environmental

Themes: Water quality, Public health, Analytical techniques
