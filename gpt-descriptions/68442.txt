Synopsis: In "Deportation, its meaning and menace: Last message to the people of America," Emma Goldman and Alexander Berkman delve into the dark reality of deportation and its implications for the American people. Through their powerful and thought-provoking words, they shed light on the oppressive nature of deportation and the devastating impact it has on individuals and society as a whole. With a sense of urgency, Goldman and Berkman deliver a heartfelt message to the American people, urging them to confront the menace of deportation and fight for justice and compassion.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political, Social Commentary

Themes: Immigration, Oppression, Justice, Activism
