Synopsis: In 'Exile' by H. B. Fyfe, a brilliant scientist finds himself stranded on a desolate planet, forced to confront the mysteries of an ancient civilization and the dark secrets it holds. As he battles against the harsh environment and his own inner demons, he must unravel the enigma of his exile and discover the truth that could change everything. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, 'Exile' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the depths of isolation and the power of redemption.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Isolation, Redemption, Exploration
