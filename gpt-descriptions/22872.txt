Synopsis: In "Susan Clegg and a Man in the House" by Anne Warner, Susan Clegg finds herself in a delightful yet perplexing situation when a mysterious man unexpectedly moves into her boarding house. As Susan's curiosity grows, she embarks on a journey filled with humor, romance, and unexpected twists. With her wit and charm, Susan navigates the complexities of this new relationship, uncovering secrets and discovering the true meaning of love and friendship.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Love and relationships, Friendship, Mystery
