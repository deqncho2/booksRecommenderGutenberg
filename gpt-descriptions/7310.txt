Synopsis: 
In 'Mr. Pim Passes By: A Comedy in Three Acts', chaos ensues when a seemingly ordinary visit from Mr. Pim turns the lives of the Marden family upside down. As secrets are revealed and unexpected connections are made, the characters find themselves questioning their own identities and the nature of truth. With witty dialogue and clever plot twists, this delightful comedy explores the complexities of human relationships and the unpredictable nature of fate.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Farce

Themes: Identity, Truth, Relationships
