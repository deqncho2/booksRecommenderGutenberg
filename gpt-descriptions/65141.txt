Synopsis: In 'The Vicious Delinquents' by Mark Reinsberg, a group of troubled teenagers find themselves entangled in a dangerous game that pushes the boundaries of their morality. As they navigate through a web of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, they must confront their own demons and make choices that will determine their fate. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the delinquents' actions.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: Morality, Identity, Consequences
