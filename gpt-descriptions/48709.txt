Synopsis: 

'Information for the Guidance of Field Men and Cooperators of the Bureau of Biological Survey Engaged in the Control of Injurious Rodents and Predatory Animals' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the fascinating world of managing and controlling harmful rodents and predatory animals. Written by Stanley Paul Young and Paul G. Redington, this informative work provides valuable insights and practical advice for field men and cooperators involved in this crucial task. From identifying different species to implementing effective control methods, the authors cover a wide range of topics, offering a wealth of knowledge and expertise to combat these troublesome creatures. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Field Guide, Wildlife Management

Themes: Pest control, Wildlife conservation, Animal behavior
