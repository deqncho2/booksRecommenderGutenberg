Synopsis: In 'The Geneva Protocol' by David Hunter Miller, a gripping tale of international intrigue unfolds as a young diplomat finds himself entangled in a web of political conspiracy and high-stakes negotiations. Set against the backdrop of post-World War I Europe, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous landscape of secret alliances, hidden agendas, and personal sacrifices. As tensions rise and the fate of nations hangs in the balance, the protagonist must uncover the truth and make impossible choices that will shape the course of history.

Genres: Political thriller, Historical fiction, Suspense

Themes: Diplomacy, Betrayal, Power struggles, Moral dilemmas
