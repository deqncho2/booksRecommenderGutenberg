Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Wellmouth, a mysterious and enigmatic sea captain named Cap'n Eri arrives, bringing with him a sense of adventure and intrigue. As the townspeople become captivated by his tales of the sea, they soon discover that there is more to Cap'n Eri than meets the eye. With his unconventional ways and a hidden past, Cap'n Eri's presence begins to unravel the secrets and desires of the townsfolk, leading to unexpected consequences and a journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Community
