Synopsis: In 'Hagar' by Mary Johnston, a young woman named Hagar finds herself caught between duty and desire in the midst of the American Revolution. As she navigates the treacherous waters of love, loyalty, and betrayal, Hagar must make choices that will shape her destiny and challenge the conventions of her time. With vivid historical detail and a captivating narrative, 'Hagar' explores the complexities of personal freedom and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Independence and Identity, Social Expectations
