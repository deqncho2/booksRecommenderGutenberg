Synopsis: 

'English Coins and Tokens, with a Chapter on Greek and Roman Coins' takes readers on a fascinating journey through the history of English currency, exploring the intricate details and stories behind various coins and tokens. From ancient Greek and Roman coins to the coins used in medieval England and beyond, this book delves into the evolution of currency and its significance in different eras. With detailed descriptions and beautiful illustrations, the authors provide a comprehensive overview of the coins' designs, materials, and historical context, shedding light on the economic, social, and cultural aspects of each period. Whether you are a numismatist, a history enthusiast, or simply curious about the origins of money, this book offers a captivating exploration of the world of English coins and tokens.

Genres: History, Numismatics, Archaeology

Themes: Currency and economic systems, Historical artifacts and their significance, Cultural and social history
