Synopsis: 
'The Runaway Donkey, and Other Rhymes for Children' by Emilie Poulsson is a delightful collection of whimsical and enchanting rhymes that will captivate young readers. From mischievous donkeys to playful kittens, each rhyme tells a unique and engaging story that will leave children eagerly turning the pages. With colorful illustrations that bring the characters to life, this collection is sure to spark the imagination and bring joy to both children and adults alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Poetry, Picture Book

Themes: Adventure, Friendship, Imagination
