Synopsis: In 'The Story of the Three Little Pigs', Brooke L. Leslie presents a classic tale with a twist. Follow the adventures of three little pigs as they face a formidable adversary and must rely on their wits and resourcefulness to survive. With unexpected challenges and surprising outcomes, this timeless story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what fate awaits the three little pigs.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fairy Tale, Adventure

Themes: Resilience, Problem-solving, Consequences
