Synopsis: 

In "Unknown to History: A Story of the Captivity of Mary of Scotland," Charlotte M. Yonge takes readers on a captivating journey into the life of Mary, Queen of Scots, during her tumultuous captivity. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue and power struggles, this historical novel delves into the hidden aspects of Mary's life, shedding light on her resilience, courage, and unwavering spirit. As secrets are unveiled and alliances are tested, readers will be drawn into a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and the indomitable strength of a queen.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biographical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Betrayal, Resilience and Determination
