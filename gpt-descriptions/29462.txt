Synopsis: In "The House Under the Sea: A Romance" by Pemberton, Max, a young artist named Hugh finds himself drawn to a mysterious house that seems to defy the laws of nature. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the house, he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and supernatural forces. With each discovery, Hugh's perception of reality is challenged, leading him on a thrilling journey that will test his courage and unravel the enigma of the house under the sea.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Supernatural

Themes: Love and obsession, Perception of reality, Secrets and hidden truths
