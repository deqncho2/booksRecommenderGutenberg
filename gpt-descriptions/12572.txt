Synopsis: 
'The World's Greatest Books — Volume 10 — Lives and Letters' is a captivating collection that delves into the lives and correspondences of some of the most influential figures in history. From the intimate letters of great thinkers to the biographies of remarkable individuals, this volume offers a fascinating glimpse into the personal lives and inner thoughts of these extraordinary individuals. Discover the untold stories, hidden emotions, and profound insights that shaped the course of history.

Genres: Biography, Autobiography, Letters, Non-fiction

Themes: Personal growth, Human connection, Historical significance
