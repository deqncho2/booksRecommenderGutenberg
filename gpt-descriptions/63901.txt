Synopsis: In "The Duke's Daughter; and, The Fugitives; vol. 1/3" by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), we follow the captivating story of Lady Edith, the daughter of a powerful duke, who finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and danger. When a mysterious group of fugitives seeks refuge in her family's estate, Lady Edith's life takes an unexpected turn as she becomes embroiled in their plight. As she navigates the treacherous waters of society and uncovers shocking truths, Lady Edith must decide where her loyalties lie and how far she is willing to go to protect those she cares about.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Social class, Loyalty, Secrets
