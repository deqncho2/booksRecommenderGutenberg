Synopsis: 

'Price/Cost Indexes from 1875 to 1989; Estimated to 2010' by Hart, Michael takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical evolution of price and cost indexes over a span of more than a century. From the industrial revolution to the modern era, this meticulously researched work provides a fascinating exploration of how economic factors have shaped our world. Delving into the intricacies of inflation, market fluctuations, and economic trends, Hart's comprehensive analysis offers valuable insights into the past, present, and potential future of global economies. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Economic History, Data Analysis

Themes: Economic fluctuations, Inflation, Historical trends
