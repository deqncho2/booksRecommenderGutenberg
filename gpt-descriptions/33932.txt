Synopsis: 

'Company B, 307th Infantry: Its history, honor roll, company roster, Sept., 1917, May, 1919' by Klausner, Julius is a captivating historical account that delves into the experiences of Company B, 307th Infantry during the period of September 1917 to May 1919. Through meticulous research and personal narratives, the book unveils the untold stories of the brave men who served in this company, highlighting their triumphs, sacrifices, and the challenges they faced on the battlefield. From the camaraderie forged in the trenches to the harrowing moments of combat, this book offers a vivid and immersive portrayal of the soldiers' journey during World War I.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Patriotism, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Resilience
