Synopsis: In 'Grey Roses' by Henry Harland, a young American artist named Hugh Treffinger finds himself captivated by the enigmatic beauty of a woman he encounters in Paris. As he becomes entangled in a web of passion, secrets, and art, Hugh must navigate the complexities of love and desire in a city known for its romantic allure. With vivid descriptions and a touch of mystery, 'Grey Roses' takes readers on a journey through the bohemian streets of Paris, where art and love intertwine in unexpected ways.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and desire, Art and creativity, Secrets and deception
