Synopsis: In 'Red Riding Hood' by Lydia L. A. Very, a young girl named Red embarks on a perilous journey through a dark and enchanted forest to deliver a basket of goodies to her grandmother. However, she soon discovers that the forest is filled with mysterious creatures and dangerous secrets that threaten her mission and her very life. As Red navigates through the treacherous woods, she must rely on her wit, courage, and unexpected allies to unravel the truth and save her grandmother from a sinister fate.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Coming of age, Good vs. Evil, Identity and self-discovery
