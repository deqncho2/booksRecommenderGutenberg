Synopsis: 

In 'Agriculture for Beginners: Revised Edition', Burkett, Hill, and Stevens take readers on an enlightening journey into the world of agriculture, offering a comprehensive guide for both novices and seasoned farmers alike. This revised edition delves into the fundamental principles of farming, exploring topics such as soil management, crop cultivation, livestock care, and sustainable practices. Through engaging anecdotes and practical advice, the authors provide invaluable insights into the intricate workings of the agricultural industry, empowering readers to make informed decisions and achieve success in their own farming endeavors. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Agriculture, Education

Themes: Sustainable farming, Soil management, Livestock care, Crop cultivation, Agricultural practices
