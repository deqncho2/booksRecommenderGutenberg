Synopsis: In the midst of a zombie apocalypse, a group of strangers find themselves trapped in a remote farmhouse, fighting for their lives against the relentless undead. As tensions rise and the threat grows, they must confront not only the horrors outside but also the darkness within themselves. With its gripping suspense and shocking twists, 'Night of the Living Dead' takes readers on a terrifying journey that will leave them questioning their own survival instincts.

Genres: Horror, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Survival, Fear, Human Nature
