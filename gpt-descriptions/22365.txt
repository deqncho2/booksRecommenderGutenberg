Synopsis: 

'Little By Little; or, The Cruise of the Flyaway' by Oliver Optic is an exciting adventure novel that follows the thrilling journey of a group of young friends aboard the Flyaway, a small yacht. As they set sail on their summer vacation, they encounter unexpected challenges and dangers that test their courage and resourcefulness. From treacherous storms to encounters with pirates, the young adventurers must rely on their wits and teamwork to navigate through the perils of the open sea. Along the way, they also discover the importance of friendship, loyalty, and perseverance. 

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Perseverance, Teamwork
