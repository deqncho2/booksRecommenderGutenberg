Synopsis: In 'The History of Little King Pippin', readers are taken on a captivating journey through the adventures of a young king and the misfortunes that befall four naughty boys. As the story unfolds, the fate of these boys becomes entwined with the miraculous rescue of Master Harry Harmless by a mysterious little white horse. With its intriguing title and thrilling narrative, this book promises to transport readers into a world of wonder and excitement.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Morality, Consequences of actions, Redemption
