Synopsis: 

In 'Campaigns of a Non-Combatant, and His Romaunt Abroad During the War', George Alfred Townsend takes readers on a captivating journey through the American Civil War. Through the eyes of a non-combatant, the author provides a unique perspective on the conflict, offering a thrilling account of his experiences and adventures during this tumultuous time. From witnessing battles and encounters with famous generals to encountering the harsh realities of war, Townsend's narrative is filled with vivid descriptions and compelling anecdotes that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: War, Courage, Survival, Human Resilience
