Synopsis:
In 'Frank Merriwell's Athletes; Or, The Boys Who Won' by Standish, Burt L., readers are introduced to Frank Merriwell, a talented and charismatic young athlete who becomes the center of attention at Fardale Academy. As Frank and his friends face various challenges and obstacles, they must rely on their determination, teamwork, and sportsmanship to overcome adversity. From thrilling athletic competitions to unexpected friendships, this captivating story follows Frank and his companions as they strive to prove their worth both on and off the field.

Genres:
Sports fiction, Young adult fiction, Adventure fiction

Themes:
Sportsmanship, Friendship, Perseverance, Teamwork
