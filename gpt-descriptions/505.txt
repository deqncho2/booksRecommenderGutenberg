Synopsis: In "History of the Warfare of Science with Theology in Christendom," Andrew Dickson White takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous relationship between science and theology in the context of Christendom. From ancient times to the modern era, White explores the clashes, controversies, and breakthroughs that have shaped this complex dynamic. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, he unveils the fascinating battles fought between scientific progress and religious dogma, shedding light on the profound impact these conflicts have had on society and the advancement of knowledge.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science, Religion

Themes: Conflict between science and religion, Evolution of scientific thought, Influence of theology on scientific progress, Societal implications of scientific discoveries, Intellectual freedom and censorship
