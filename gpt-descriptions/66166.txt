Synopsis:
In 'The Wounded Name' by D. K. Broster, a young woman named Mary is thrust into a world of intrigue and danger when she becomes entangled in a mysterious family curse. As she delves deeper into the secrets of her ancestral home, she uncovers a web of dark secrets and forbidden love that spans generations. With her own life at stake, Mary must navigate treacherous alliances and confront her own inner demons in order to break free from the curse that threatens to consume her.

Genres:
Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes:
Family secrets, Forbidden love, Identity, Redemption
