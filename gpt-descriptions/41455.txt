Synopsis: In "The Lady in the Car" by William Le Queux, a young woman named Marjorie Holt finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she borrows her employer's car for a weekend getaway. As she embarks on a solitary journey along the French Riviera, Marjorie becomes entangled in a series of inexplicable events that lead her to question her own sanity. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and illusion blurs, leaving Marjorie and the reader questioning who can be trusted and what secrets lie hidden within the lady in the car.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Identity, Perception, Deception
