Synopsis: In 'Out of Russia' by Marriott, Crittenden, a young Russian woman finds herself caught in the midst of political turmoil and espionage as she desperately tries to escape her war-torn country. With danger lurking at every turn, she must rely on her wit, courage, and unexpected alliances to navigate a treacherous journey to freedom. As secrets unravel and loyalties are tested, the gripping narrative takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the shadows of Cold War-era Russia.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Survival, Betrayal, Identity, Resilience
