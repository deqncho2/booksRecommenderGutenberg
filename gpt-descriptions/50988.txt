Synopsis: 
In 'Bodyguard' by H. L. Gold, a renowned scientist is on the brink of a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity forever. However, his life is suddenly in grave danger, and he must rely on an unlikely bodyguard to protect him at all costs. As they navigate a treacherous world filled with deceit and betrayal, they uncover a web of secrets that could have catastrophic consequences. With time running out, they must outsmart their enemies and stay one step ahead in a deadly game of cat and mouse.

Genres: 
Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: 
Technology and its impact on society, Trust and loyalty, Survival
