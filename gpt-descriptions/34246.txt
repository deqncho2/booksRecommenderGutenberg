Synopsis: In 'Of High Descent' by George Manville Fenn, a young man named Frank is unexpectedly thrust into a world of mystery and danger when he discovers a hidden family secret. As he delves deeper into his ancestry, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden treasures that will test his courage and determination. With the help of a loyal friend, Frank embarks on a thrilling adventure that will challenge his beliefs and change his life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Courage, Identity
