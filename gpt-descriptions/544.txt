Synopsis: In "Anne's House of Dreams," Lucy Maud Montgomery takes readers on a captivating journey as Anne Shirley embarks on a new chapter of her life. Set in the picturesque Prince Edward Island, Anne and her beloved Gilbert Blythe begin their married life in a quaint seaside house. As they settle into their new home, they encounter a colorful cast of characters, each with their own stories and secrets. From heartwarming friendships to unexpected tragedies, Anne's House of Dreams is a tale of love, loss, and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love and marriage, Friendship, Overcoming adversity, Finding one's place in the world
