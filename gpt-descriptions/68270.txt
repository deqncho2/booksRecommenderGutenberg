Synopsis: In 'A History of the 17th Lancers (Duke of Cambridge's Own)', Sir John William Fortescue takes readers on a captivating journey through the illustrious history of one of the most renowned cavalry regiments in British military history. From their early days as a small troop of horsemen to their pivotal role in major conflicts, this book delves into the triumphs, tragedies, and extraordinary tales that shaped the 17th Lancers. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Fortescue brings to life the bravery, camaraderie, and unwavering spirit of these gallant soldiers, painting a vivid picture of their exploits on the battlefield and the impact they had on the course of history.

Genres: Military history, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Courage, Honor, Military strategy, Camaraderie, Historical significance
