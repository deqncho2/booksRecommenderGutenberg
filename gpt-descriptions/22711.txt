Synopsis: In 'A Love Story Reversed: 1898' by Bellamy, Edward, a young woman finds herself transported back in time to the year 1898, where she becomes entangled in a passionate and forbidden love affair. As she navigates the complexities of this era, she must confront societal expectations, personal desires, and the consequences of altering the course of history. With each twist and turn, the story unveils secrets and surprises that will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Time Travel

Themes: Love and Desire, Societal Expectations, Consequences of Actions
