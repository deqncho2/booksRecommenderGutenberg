Synopsis: In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 98, June 21 1890', readers are transported to the vibrant and satirical world of Victorian England. This edition of the renowned magazine showcases a collection of witty and humorous articles, cartoons, and social commentaries that cleverly dissect the absurdities of society, politics, and everyday life. From comical observations on fashion trends to biting critiques of the upper class, this volume offers a delightful and thought-provoking glimpse into the Victorian era.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Social Commentary

Themes: Society, Politics, Everyday Life
