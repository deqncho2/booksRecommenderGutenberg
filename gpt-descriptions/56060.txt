Synopsis: In 'Presidential Problems' by Cleveland, Grover, the 22nd and 24th President of the United States, Grover Cleveland, finds himself facing a series of unexpected challenges during his time in office. From political scandals to international crises, Cleveland must navigate treacherous waters to protect the nation and preserve his legacy. As the pressure mounts, he must make difficult decisions that will shape the course of history. Will Cleveland rise to the occasion or crumble under the weight of these presidential problems?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Biographical

Themes: Leadership, Integrity, Political Turmoil
