Synopsis: In 'The Dinner Year-Book' by Marion Harland, readers are invited into the world of a group of friends who gather regularly for dinner parties. Each chapter unveils a new dinner party, complete with delectable menus, lively conversations, and unexpected twists. As the year progresses, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of friendship is explored. With mouthwatering descriptions of food and captivating storytelling, 'The Dinner Year-Book' takes readers on a delightful journey through the joys and complexities of shared meals and the bonds they create.

Genres: Fiction, Food and Drink, Friendship

Themes: Friendship, Secrets, Relationships, Food, Community
