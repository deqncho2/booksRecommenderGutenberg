Synopsis: 
In 'The Overcoming Life, and Other Sermons', Dwight Lyman Moody delivers a collection of powerful and inspiring sermons that delve into the depths of the human spirit and offer guidance on how to overcome life's challenges. From exploring the nature of faith and the importance of prayer to discussing the significance of forgiveness and the pursuit of holiness, Moody's words resonate with a timeless wisdom that speaks directly to the hearts of readers. With his characteristic passion and clarity, Moody encourages readers to embrace a life of faith, hope, and perseverance, reminding them that true victory lies in overcoming the obstacles that come their way.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Christian literature, Sermons

Themes: 
Faith, Prayer, Forgiveness, Holiness, Perseverance
