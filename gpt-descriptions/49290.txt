Synopsis: In 'The Year Nine: A Tale of the Tyrol' by Manning, Anne, a young woman named Hilda finds herself caught in the midst of a tumultuous period in the Tyrol region. As war looms and political tensions rise, Hilda must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and sacrifice. With vivid descriptions of the Tyrolean landscape and a cast of compelling characters, this gripping tale explores the resilience of the human spirit in the face of adversity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and Relationships, Political Turmoil, Resilience
