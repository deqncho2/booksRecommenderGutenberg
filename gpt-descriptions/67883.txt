Synopsis: In 'The End of Elfintown' by Jane Barlow, a mysterious event threatens to destroy the enchanting world of Elfintown, leaving its inhabitants in a desperate race against time to save their beloved home. As the clock ticks, a young elf named Lily embarks on a perilous journey, encountering unexpected allies and facing unimaginable challenges along the way. With the fate of Elfintown hanging in the balance, Lily must summon all her courage and resourcefulness to uncover the truth and find a way to preserve the magic before it's too late.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Preservation of nature, Courage, Friendship, Overcoming adversity
