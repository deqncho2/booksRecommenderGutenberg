Synopsis: 
In 'Motor Tours in the West Country', Mrs. Stawell takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the picturesque landscapes of the West Country. With vivid descriptions and charming anecdotes, she invites us to join her on a series of thrilling motor tours, exploring hidden gems and uncovering the rich history and folklore of the region. From quaint villages to breathtaking coastlines, this travelogue promises to ignite the wanderlust in every reader, offering a delightful blend of adventure, discovery, and the joy of the open road.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, History, Culture, Natural beauty
