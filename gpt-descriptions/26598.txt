Synopsis: In 'Seasoning of Wood' by J.B. Wagner, a mysterious antique shop holds a secret that will transport a young woman into a world of enchantment and danger. As she delves deeper into the shop's hidden history, she uncovers a connection to her own past that she never could have imagined. With each step she takes, the line between reality and fantasy becomes blurred, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and unexpected revelations.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Fate, Magic
