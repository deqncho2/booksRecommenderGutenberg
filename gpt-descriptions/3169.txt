Synopsis: In 'The Pursuit of the House-Boat', a group of eccentric characters find themselves embarking on a hilarious and adventurous journey aboard a floating house. As they navigate through unexpected twists and turns, they encounter mythical creatures, engage in witty banter, and face the challenges of love and friendship. This whimsical tale is filled with delightful surprises that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Humor, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Love, Imagination
