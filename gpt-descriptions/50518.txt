Synopsis:
In "Gowrie; or, the King's Plot," James, G. P. R. takes us on a thrilling journey through the treacherous world of political intrigue and conspiracy. Set in 17th century Scotland, the story follows the young and ambitious Earl of Gowrie as he becomes entangled in a dangerous plot against the King. As Gowrie navigates a web of secrets, betrayals, and hidden agendas, he must make difficult choices that will determine not only his own fate but also the destiny of an entire nation. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, "Gowrie; or, the King's Plot" will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, Political thriller, Suspense

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Deception, Patriotism
