Synopsis: 
In 'Soap-Bubble Stories: For Children', Fanny Barry takes young readers on a whimsical journey through a world of enchanting soap bubbles. Each bubble holds a unique story, filled with colorful characters and magical adventures. From a mischievous bubble that leads a young girl on a thrilling chase, to a bubble that reveals the secret language of animals, these delightful tales will captivate the imagination of children and adults alike. With its charming illustrations and imaginative storytelling, this collection of soap-bubble stories is sure to bring joy and wonder to readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Wonder, Exploration
