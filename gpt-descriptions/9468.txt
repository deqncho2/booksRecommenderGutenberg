Synopsis: In 'Anna St. Ives' by Holcroft, Thomas, a young woman named Anna finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she is unexpectedly thrust into a world of secrets and hidden identities. As she navigates through a series of unexpected events and encounters, Anna must unravel the truth behind her own past and the people around her, all while trying to protect her own life. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the ultimate truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
