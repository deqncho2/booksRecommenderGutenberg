Synopsis: 

In 'Chance in Chains: A Story of Monte Carlo' by Thorne, Guy, we are transported to the glamorous and mysterious world of Monte Carlo, where fortunes are made and lost in the blink of an eye. The story follows a captivating protagonist who finds themselves entangled in a web of high-stakes gambling, secret alliances, and dangerous love affairs. As the tension rises and the stakes get higher, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the opulent casinos and hidden backrooms of this iconic city. With unexpected twists and turns at every corner, 'Chance in Chains' keeps the reader on the edge of their seat, wondering who will come out on top in this game of chance.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Gambling, Deception, Power
