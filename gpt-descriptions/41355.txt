Synopsis: In the midst of the American Civil War, young and spirited Mademoiselle Victorine Ravenel finds herself torn between her Southern roots and her growing loyalty to the Union cause. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, Victorine's journey takes her from the opulent plantations of Louisiana to the war-torn streets of New York City. Will she find the strength to challenge her beliefs and embrace a new perspective, or will she remain trapped in the confines of her divided loyalties?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Love, Societal Expectations
