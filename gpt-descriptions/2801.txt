Synopsis: In 'The Commonwealth of Oceana' by James Harrington, a visionary political philosopher presents a captivating tale set in a future society where power is distributed among the citizens based on their land ownership. As the protagonist navigates the intricacies of this unique system, they uncover hidden truths and face unexpected challenges that threaten the stability of the Commonwealth. With its thought-provoking exploration of governance and the consequences of political structures, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through a world where power and democracy collide.

Genres: Political fiction, Utopian fiction, Historical fiction

Themes: Power and governance, Democracy, Social equality
