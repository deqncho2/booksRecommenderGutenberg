Synopsis: 
'Practical Rules for the Management of a Locomotive Engine: in the Station, on the Road, and in cases of Accident' takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of locomotive engines. Written by Charles Hutton Gregory, this comprehensive guide provides invaluable insights into the operation and maintenance of these powerful machines. From the bustling station to the treacherous road, and even in the face of unexpected accidents, Gregory's practical rules offer a fascinating glimpse into the challenges and triumphs of managing a locomotive engine.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Technical Manual, Transportation

Themes: 
Engineering, Safety, Efficiency
