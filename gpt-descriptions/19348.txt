Synopsis: In the heart of the Mississippi River, a group of unlikely companions forms an extraordinary bond that will test their loyalty, challenge societal norms, and ignite a powerful revolution. Gideon, a charismatic leader, gathers a diverse band of individuals, each with their own secrets and desires, to fight against the injustices of the time. As they navigate the treacherous waters of the Mississippi, they will face danger, love, and the ultimate quest for freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Social justice, Friendship, Identity, Rebellion
