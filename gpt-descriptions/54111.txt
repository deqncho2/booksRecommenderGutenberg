Synopsis: 
In 'On the Iron at Big Cloud' by Frank L. Packard, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Big Cloud, bringing with him a dark secret and a dangerous past. As the townspeople become entangled in his web of deception, they must confront their own hidden desires and face the consequences of their actions. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Consequences, Hidden Desires
