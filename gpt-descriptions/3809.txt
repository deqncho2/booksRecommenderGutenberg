Synopsis: In 'The Master of the World' by Jules Verne, a series of mysterious events unfold as a strange and powerful machine wreaks havoc across the United States. As government officials and investigators race against time to uncover the truth behind this enigmatic force, they find themselves entangled in a thrilling adventure that will test their courage and challenge their understanding of the world. With each twist and turn, the mystery deepens, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the secrets behind the mastermind controlling this extraordinary machine.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Technology and its impact on society, Power and control, Exploration and discovery
