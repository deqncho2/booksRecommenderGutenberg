Synopsis: In 'A Question' by Georg Ebers, a young scholar named Hiram is faced with a perplexing mystery that takes him on a journey through ancient Egypt. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the past, he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and political intrigue. With danger lurking at every turn, Hiram must uncover the truth before it consumes him and those he holds dear.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Secrets and mysteries, Love and betrayal
