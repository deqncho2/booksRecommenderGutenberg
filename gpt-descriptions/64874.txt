Synopsis: In 'Space Blackout' by Carson Sam, a routine mission to repair a malfunctioning satellite takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious phenomenon plunges the crew into darkness. With their communication systems down and no way to navigate, they must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to survive the eerie and treacherous void of space. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the crew must confront their deepest fears and unravel the truth behind the blackout before it consumes them all.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Isolation, Trust, Discovery
