Synopsis: In the heart of a bustling metropolis, a series of gruesome murders sends shockwaves through the city. As the body count rises, Detective Sarah Collins finds herself caught in a deadly game of cat and mouse with a cunning and elusive killer. With time running out, she must navigate the dark underbelly of the city, uncovering secrets and facing her own demons, in order to stop the relentless murderer before they strike again.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Serial killers, Urban crime, Psychological suspense
