Synopsis: In 'A Historical Account of Useful Inventions and Scientific Discoveries: Being a manual of instruction and entertainment,' Grant, George takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the fascinating world of inventions and scientific breakthroughs. From ancient civilizations to the modern era, this book unveils the ingenious minds behind revolutionary creations that have shaped our lives. Delving into the stories behind these innovations, Grant offers a blend of historical facts, anecdotes, and captivating narratives that will leave readers in awe of human ingenuity. Whether it's the invention of the printing press, the discovery of electricity, or the development of the internet, this book provides a comprehensive and engaging account of the pivotal moments in human history that have propelled us forward.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Human ingenuity, Progress and innovation, Historical significance
