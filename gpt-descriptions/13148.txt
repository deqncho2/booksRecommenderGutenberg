Synopsis: In "Peter Simple; and, The Three Cutters," we follow the adventures of Peter Simple, a young midshipman in the Royal Navy, as he navigates the treacherous waters of life at sea. From encounters with pirates and smugglers to battles against enemy ships, Peter's journey is filled with danger, excitement, and unexpected twists. Along the way, he forms lasting friendships, falls in love, and learns valuable lessons about honor, loyalty, and the true meaning of courage.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Naval Fiction

Themes: Coming of Age, Friendship, Loyalty, Honor, Courage
