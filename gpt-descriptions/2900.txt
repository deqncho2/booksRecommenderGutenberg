Synopsis: In 'The Age of Invention: A Chronicle of Mechanical Conquest', Thompson, Holland takes readers on a captivating journey through the remarkable era of invention and innovation. From the steam engine to the telegraph, and from the sewing machine to the telephone, this book explores the brilliant minds and groundbreaking creations that shaped the world as we know it. Filled with fascinating anecdotes and historical insights, it unveils the triumphs and challenges faced by inventors as they revolutionized industries and transformed society. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, 'The Age of Invention' offers a captivating exploration of humanity's relentless pursuit of progress.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Innovation, Progress, Technological Advancement
