Synopsis: In 'Time Payment' by Sylvia Jacobs, a young woman named Emily discovers a mysterious antique pocket watch that has the power to manipulate time. As she delves deeper into its secrets, she becomes entangled in a thrilling adventure that spans different eras and challenges her perception of reality. With each tick of the watch, Emily must navigate the consequences of altering time and confront the moral dilemmas that arise. As she races against the clock, she uncovers the true nature of the watch and the profound impact it can have on her life and the world around her.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Time manipulation, Morality, Perception of reality
