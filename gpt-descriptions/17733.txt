Synopsis: In "The Black Douglas" by S.R. Crockett, a young Scottish nobleman finds himself caught in a web of treachery and betrayal as he seeks to reclaim his family's honor and protect his loved ones. Set against the backdrop of medieval Scotland, this gripping tale follows the protagonist's journey from a life of privilege to one of exile and danger. With his courage and cunning, he must navigate through political intrigue, battles, and personal sacrifices to fulfill his destiny and restore his family's legacy.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Honor, Loyalty, Betrayal, Redemption
