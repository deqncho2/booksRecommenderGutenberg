Synopsis: 
'The Works of Thomas Hood; Vol. 01 (of 11): Comic and Serious, in Prose and Verse, With All the Original Illustrations' by Thomas Hood is a collection of humorous and thought-provoking writings that will captivate readers with its wit and charm. From clever satires to poignant poems, this volume showcases the diverse talents of Thomas Hood, a master of both comedy and tragedy. With its original illustrations, this book offers a delightful journey through the imaginative and often surprising world of Hood's literary creations.

Genres: Humor, Satire, Poetry

Themes: Comedy, Tragedy, Social Critique
