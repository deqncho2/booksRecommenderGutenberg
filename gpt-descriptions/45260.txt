Synopsis: 
In 'Doris Force at Locked Gates; Or, Saving a Mysterious Fortune' by Julia K. Duncan, readers are introduced to the fearless and resourceful Doris Force. When Doris receives a cryptic message about a hidden fortune, she embarks on a thrilling adventure to unlock the secrets behind locked gates. As she delves deeper into the mystery, Doris must navigate treacherous obstacles, solve perplexing riddles, and outsmart cunning adversaries. With her unwavering determination and quick thinking, Doris is determined to uncover the truth and save the mysterious fortune before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Discovery
