Synopsis: 
'Rustic Chivalry (Cavalleria Rusticana): Melodrama in One Act' is a gripping tale set in a small Sicilian village, where love, betrayal, and honor collide. The story follows the lives of several characters, each entangled in a web of passion and secrets. As tensions rise and emotions run high, the village becomes a stage for intense drama, where love and revenge take center stage. With unexpected twists and heart-wrenching moments, this one-act melodrama will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Tragedy

Themes: Love and desire, Betrayal and revenge, Honor and duty
