Synopsis: In 'Palissy the Huguenot Potter: A True Tale', follow the remarkable journey of Bernard Palissy, a talented potter and devout Huguenot during the tumultuous times of 16th-century France. As he faces persecution for his religious beliefs, Palissy's unwavering determination to create his masterpiece leads him on a path filled with sacrifice, danger, and unexpected discoveries. This captivating true tale explores the power of art, the resilience of the human spirit, and the enduring pursuit of one's passions.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Persecution, Artistic Passion, Religious Freedom
