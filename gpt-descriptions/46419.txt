Synopsis: 
In 'Shorter Bible Plays' by Rita Benton, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of condensed biblical stories. From the dramatic tale of David and Goliath to the inspiring story of Moses parting the Red Sea, Benton skillfully brings these timeless narratives to life in a concise and engaging manner. Each play is filled with vivid characters, powerful dialogue, and moments of both triumph and despair. Whether you are a devout believer or simply curious about these iconic biblical events, 'Shorter Bible Plays' offers a fresh and accessible perspective on these age-old stories.

Genres: Drama, Religious, Historical

Themes: Faith, Redemption, Morality
