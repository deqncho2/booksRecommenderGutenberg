Synopsis: In "Penelope Brandling: A Tale of the Welsh coast in the Eighteenth Century," we are transported to the rugged and enchanting Welsh coast during the 18th century. Penelope Brandling, a young woman with a mysterious past, finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal as she navigates the treacherous waters of her ancestral home. As she unravels the truth about her family and the dark forces that threaten to destroy everything she holds dear, Penelope must summon her courage and resilience to protect her heritage and forge her own destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Family, Secrets, Love, Betrayal
