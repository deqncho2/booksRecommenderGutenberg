Synopsis: 
In "Sand and Canvas: Narrative of adventures in Egypt with a sojourn among the artists in Rome," Samuel Bevan takes readers on a captivating journey through the exotic landscapes of Egypt and the vibrant art scene of Rome. Through his vivid descriptions and personal experiences, Bevan immerses us in the awe-inspiring wonders of ancient Egypt, from the majestic pyramids to the bustling streets of Cairo. Along the way, he encounters a diverse cast of characters, including fellow artists, local guides, and intriguing individuals who challenge his perceptions of art and life. As Bevan delves deeper into the artistic community in Rome, he discovers the power of creativity and the transformative nature of self-expression. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to a world of adventure, beauty, and artistic inspiration.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Art

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Artistic expression, Self-discovery
