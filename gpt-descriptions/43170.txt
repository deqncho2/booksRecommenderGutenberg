Synopsis: 

'Prentice Hugh' by Frances Mary Peard is a captivating coming-of-age novel set in the late 19th century. The story follows the life of Hugh, a young boy who finds himself torn between his desire for adventure and the responsibilities that come with his family's expectations. As he navigates the challenges of adolescence, Hugh encounters unexpected friendships, faces moral dilemmas, and discovers the true meaning of loyalty. With its richly drawn characters and vivid descriptions, 'Prentice Hugh' takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of one's own path in life.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Family, Duty, Friendship
