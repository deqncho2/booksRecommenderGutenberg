Synopsis: In 'The Missing Formula: Madge Sterling Series, #1', follow the brilliant and resourceful Madge Sterling as she embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover a mysterious formula that holds the key to a groundbreaking scientific discovery. As Madge delves deeper into the enigma, she finds herself entangled in a web of danger, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With time running out, Madge must use her intellect and determination to solve the puzzle and protect the formula from falling into the wrong hands. Will she succeed in her mission, or will the consequences be too dire to bear?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Science Fiction

Themes: Scientific discovery, Intrigue, Courage
