Synopsis: In "Temporal Power: A Study in Supremacy," Marie Corelli takes readers on a captivating journey through the corridors of power, where political intrigue, ambition, and manipulation reign supreme. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows the lives of two influential figures, a charismatic statesman and a mysterious woman, whose paths intertwine in unexpected ways. As their destinies unfold, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the true nature of power is revealed. With its gripping narrative and thought-provoking exploration of human nature, "Temporal Power" offers a mesmerizing tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Drama, Mystery

Themes: Power, Ambition, Manipulation, Secrets, Human Nature
