Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Seventy-Third Regiment," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of the regiment, from its humble beginnings as the Second Battalion of the Forty-Second Royal Highlanders in 1780 to its remarkable achievements up until 1851. With vivid descriptions and meticulous attention to detail, Cannon delves into the regiment's triumphs, challenges, and the courageous individuals who shaped its history. From battles fought on distant shores to the camaraderie forged in the face of adversity, this historical record offers a compelling narrative that will leave readers eager to uncover the untold stories of this remarkable regiment.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Resilience, Camaraderie
