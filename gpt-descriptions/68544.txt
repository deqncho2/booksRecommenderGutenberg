Synopsis: In "St. Cuthbert's Tower" by Florence Warden, a mysterious tower stands as a haunting presence in the lives of those who encounter it. As secrets unravel and dark forces come into play, a young woman finds herself drawn into a web of intrigue and danger. With each twist and turn, the tower's secrets become more entangled with her own past, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Supernatural, Redemption
