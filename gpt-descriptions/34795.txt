Synopsis: In "The Golden Triangle: The Return of Arsène Lupin" by Maurice Leblanc, the infamous gentleman thief, Arsène Lupin, makes a thrilling comeback as he finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. When a priceless artifact known as the Golden Triangle goes missing, Lupin is drawn into a high-stakes game of cat and mouse with a cunning adversary. As he navigates through a labyrinth of clues and hidden secrets, Lupin must outwit his opponents and reclaim the Golden Triangle before it falls into the wrong hands. With its clever twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Lupin's every move.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Intrigue, Deception, Redemption
