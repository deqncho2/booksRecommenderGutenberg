Synopsis:
In "Naomi: $b or the last days of Jerusalem" by Webb, J. B., Mrs. (Annie), readers are transported to the ancient city of Jerusalem during its final days before destruction. The story revolves around Naomi, a young woman caught in the midst of political turmoil and religious conflict. As the city faces imminent doom, Naomi finds herself torn between love, loyalty, and survival. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, the novel takes readers on a gripping journey through the chaos and uncertainty of a city on the brink of collapse.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Political unrest, Religious conflict, Love and loyalty, Survival
