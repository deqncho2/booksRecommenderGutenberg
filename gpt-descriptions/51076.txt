Synopsis: In 'Aaron Rodd, Diviner' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious and enigmatic man named Aaron Rodd possesses an extraordinary ability to predict the future. As his reputation as a diviner spreads, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where his unique gift becomes both a blessing and a curse. With his life on the line, Aaron must navigate through a world of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, all while trying to uncover the truth behind his own powers.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Supernatural abilities, Fate and destiny, Deception and manipulation
