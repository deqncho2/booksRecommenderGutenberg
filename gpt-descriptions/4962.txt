Synopsis: In 'The Story of Germ Life', Conn takes readers on a captivating journey into the microscopic world of germs, revealing their astonishing abilities and the impact they have on our daily lives. From the fascinating history of germ discovery to the intricate mechanisms of their survival and reproduction, this book unveils the hidden secrets of these tiny organisms that shape our health and environment. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Conn paints a vivid picture of the complex and often surprising world of germ life.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Microbiology

Themes: Microscopic life, Disease, Health, Environmental impact
