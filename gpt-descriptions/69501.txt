Synopsis: In 'At the Fall of Montreal; or, A Soldier Boy's Final Victory' by Edward Stratemeyer, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the final days of the French and Indian War. Follow the courageous soldier boy as he faces danger, treachery, and unexpected alliances in the heart of the Canadian wilderness. With the fate of nations hanging in the balance, will he be able to achieve his ultimate victory?

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival
