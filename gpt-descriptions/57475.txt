Synopsis: 

'Experience of a Confederate States Prisoner: Being an Ephemeris Regularly Kept by an Officer of the Confederate States Army' takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of a Confederate soldier who becomes a prisoner of war. Through his vivid and poignant entries, we witness the harsh realities, emotional turmoil, and unexpected moments of camaraderie that he experiences while confined in enemy territory. As the officer's journal unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of humanity is explored in the most challenging of circumstances.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Memoir

Themes: Survival, Loyalty, Humanity, Resilience
