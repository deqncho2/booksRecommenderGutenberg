Synopsis: 
These Charming People is a delightful and witty novel that follows the lives of a group of friends, including Shelmerdene, Lord Tarlyon, Mr. Michael Wagstaffe, Mr. Ralph Wyndham Trevor, and others. Set in the early 20th century, the story takes readers on a journey through the fortunes, follies, adventures, gallantries, and general activities of these charming individuals. From romantic entanglements to social gatherings, the characters navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and society with humor and grace. As their lives intertwine, secrets are revealed, alliances are formed, and unexpected twists and turns keep the reader engaged until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Love and relationships, Society and class, Friendship, Intrigue and secrets
