Synopsis: In 'Captain Carey; or, Fighting the Indians at Pine Ridge', Lionel Lounsberry takes readers on a thrilling journey to the American West, where Captain Carey finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous conflict with the Native American tribes at Pine Ridge. As he navigates the treacherous terrain and faces the challenges of war, Captain Carey must confront his own beliefs and prejudices, ultimately questioning the true nature of heroism and the cost of victory.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Western

Themes: Conflict and reconciliation, Identity and self-discovery, Prejudice and stereotypes
