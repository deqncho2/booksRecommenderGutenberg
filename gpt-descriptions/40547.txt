Synopsis:
In "The Rival Campers Afloat; or, The Prize Yacht Viking," a group of adventurous young campers find themselves embarking on a thrilling maritime journey. When a prestigious yacht race is announced, the campers eagerly set out to build their own boat, the Viking, to compete against their rivals. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter unexpected challenges, and face fierce competition, the campers must rely on their teamwork, resourcefulness, and determination to achieve their goal. Will the Viking prove to be a worthy contender and lead the campers to victory?

Genres:
Adventure, Young Adult, Sports

Themes:
Teamwork, Competition, Resourcefulness
