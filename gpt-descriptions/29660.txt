Synopsis: 
In "Three Years in the Federal Cavalry," Willard W. Glazier takes readers on a thrilling and captivating journey through his experiences as a soldier in the Union Army during the American Civil War. From the intense battles and daring escapades to the camaraderie and hardships faced by the cavalry, Glazier's vivid and detailed account offers a unique perspective on the war that shaped a nation. As he shares his personal anecdotes and encounters with notable figures, readers will be transported to the front lines, feeling the adrenaline and tension of each moment. With its gripping narrative and historical significance, this memoir is a must-read for anyone interested in the Civil War and the brave men who fought in it.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Military History
Themes: War, Courage, Brotherhood, Survival
