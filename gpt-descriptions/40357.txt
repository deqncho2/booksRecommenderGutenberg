Synopsis: 
Embark on an exhilarating adventure through the vibrant landscapes of Spain as Harry Alverson Franck takes you on a four-month journey on foot. From the bustling streets of Madrid to the tranquil countryside of Andalusia, Franck immerses himself in the rich culture, history, and people of this captivating country. Encounter unexpected encounters, breathtaking scenery, and the challenges of traveling by foot, as Franck's vivid descriptions transport you to the heart of Spain. 

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, Personal growth
