Synopsis: In the small town of Blackwood, a series of gruesome murders has left the community in a state of fear and paranoia. As the bodies continue to pile up, suspicion falls on a mysterious newcomer, who seems to possess an uncanny connection to the moon. Is he the key to solving the murders, or is there something far more sinister at play? 'Lycanthropus' is a gripping thriller that delves into the dark depths of human nature and the primal instincts that lie within us all.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Fear, Supernatural, Morality
