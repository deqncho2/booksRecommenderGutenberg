Synopsis: In 'Fortune's My Foe: A Romance' by Bloundelle-Burton, John, a young woman named Maud finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits a mysterious fortune. As she navigates the treacherous waters of high society, Maud must unravel the truth behind her newfound wealth and confront the unexpected dangers that come with it. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the pages.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Wealth and its consequences, Deception and betrayal, Love and sacrifice
