Synopsis: In 'The Eulogies of Howard: A Vision', Hayley William takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and mind of Howard, a man grappling with his own mortality. As Howard reflects on his past, present, and uncertain future, his eulogies become a poignant exploration of love, loss, and the search for meaning. With lyrical prose and a touch of magical realism, this novel delves into the depths of human emotions and the complexities of existence.

Genres: Literary Fiction, Magical Realism, Psychological Drama

Themes: Mortality, Love, Loss, Identity, Existentialism
