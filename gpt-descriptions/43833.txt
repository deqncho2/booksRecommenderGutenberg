Synopsis: In 'Our Little Japanese Cousin', readers are transported to the enchanting world of Japan through the eyes of a young Japanese girl named Kiku. Join Kiku as she takes you on a captivating journey filled with cultural traditions, beautiful landscapes, and heartwarming friendships. Experience the wonders of Japanese life, from tea ceremonies and cherry blossom festivals to sumo wrestling and traditional clothing. Discover the joys and challenges of growing up in a different culture, as Kiku learns valuable lessons about family, loyalty, and the importance of embracing one's heritage.

Genres: Children's Literature, Cultural Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Cultural identity, Family values, Friendship, Tradition
