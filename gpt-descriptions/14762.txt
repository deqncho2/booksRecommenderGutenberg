Synopsis: 
In 'Now or Never; Or, The Adventures of Bobby Bright: A Story for Young Folks' by Optic, Oliver, readers are introduced to the spirited and curious Bobby Bright. When Bobby's father is called away on business, Bobby seizes the opportunity to embark on an exciting adventure. Joined by his loyal friends, Bobby sets off on a journey filled with unexpected challenges, thrilling encounters, and valuable life lessons. As Bobby navigates through various escapades, he learns the importance of courage, friendship, and perseverance. Will Bobby's determination and quick thinking lead him to triumph in the face of adversity?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Children's literature

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Perseverance
