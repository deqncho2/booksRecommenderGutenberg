Synopsis: In 'Pele and Hiiaka: A Myth From Hawaii' by Emerson, Nathaniel Bright, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Hawaiian mythology. Follow the adventures of the fiery goddess Pele and her sister Hiiaka as they navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter mystical creatures, and face unexpected challenges. As the sisters embark on a quest to save their family, they must confront their own inner demons and discover the true power of love and loyalty. This mesmerizing tale will transport readers to a realm where ancient legends come to life, weaving a tapestry of magic, courage, and the enduring bond between siblings.

Genres: Mythology, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Family, Love, Loyalty, Transformation
