Synopsis: In 'Witness' by George H. Smith, a young man named David finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when he becomes the sole witness to a mysterious crime. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he discovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel his own life. With his every move being watched, David must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect himself from those who will stop at nothing to keep it hidden.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Truth and deception, Identity and self-discovery, Power and corruption
