Synopsis: 

'Trial of Pedro de Zulueta, jun., on a Charge of Slave Trading' is a gripping account of a high-profile trial that took place in London in 1843. The book provides a full report of the trial, which spanned three days at the Central Criminal Court, also known as the Old Bailey. The trial centers around Pedro de Zulueta, a man accused of engaging in the illegal trade of slaves under the 5 Geo. IV, cap. 113. As the trial unfolds, the reader is taken on a suspenseful journey through the courtroom, witnessing the arguments, testimonies, and the ultimate verdict. 

Genres: Legal drama, Historical non-fiction

Themes: Slavery, Justice, Morality
