Synopsis: In this captivating lecture, Thomas Roscoe Rede Stebbing explores the fascinating intersection of Darwinism and the Noachian Flood. Delivered before the Torquay Natural History Society, Stebbing delves into the controversial theories surrounding the origins of life and the biblical account of the great flood. With thought-provoking insights and compelling evidence, he challenges conventional beliefs and offers a fresh perspective on the relationship between science and religion.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Evolution, Creationism, Biblical interpretation
