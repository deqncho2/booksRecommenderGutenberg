Synopsis: In 'The Girl Scouts Rally; or, Rosanna Wins' by Katherine Keene Galt, a group of determined Girl Scouts embark on an exciting adventure that tests their courage, teamwork, and resourcefulness. As they face unexpected challenges and uncover hidden secrets, one scout named Rosanna emerges as a true leader, inspiring her fellow scouts to overcome obstacles and achieve their goals. This heartwarming tale of friendship, empowerment, and triumph will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Leadership, Empowerment, Teamwork
