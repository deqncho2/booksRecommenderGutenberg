Synopsis: 

In 'King Arthur in History and Legend' by William Lewis Jones, embark on a captivating journey through the enigmatic world of King Arthur. Delve into the historical accounts and mythical tales that have shaped the legendary figure of Arthur, from his humble beginnings to his rise as the fabled King of Camelot. Uncover the mysteries surrounding his famous sword, Excalibur, and the Knights of the Round Table who fought alongside him. With meticulous research and compelling storytelling, Jones explores the blurred lines between history and myth, leaving readers spellbound by the enduring allure of King Arthur.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mythology, Biography

Themes: Legends and Mythology, Heroism, Chivalry, Power and Leadership
