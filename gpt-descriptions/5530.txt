Synopsis: In 'A Thorny Path - Volume 01' by Georg Ebers, we follow the captivating journey of a young Egyptian woman named Hatasu. Set in ancient Egypt, Hatasu's life takes an unexpected turn when she is chosen to become the wife of Pharaoh Thutmose II. As she navigates the treacherous world of palace politics and power struggles, Hatasu must find her own path amidst the thorny challenges that lie ahead. With secrets, betrayals, and forbidden love lurking in the shadows, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the mysteries that lie within the ancient Egyptian court.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Intrigue

Themes: Power and Politics, Identity and Self-discovery, Forbidden Love
