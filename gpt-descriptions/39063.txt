Synopsis: In "The Motor Girls in the Mountains; or, The Gypsy Girl's Secret" by Margaret Penrose, the adventurous and resourceful Motor Girls embark on a thrilling journey to the mountains. When they encounter a mysterious gypsy girl with a hidden secret, their curiosity is piqued, and they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. As they navigate treacherous paths and face unexpected challenges, the Motor Girls must rely on their wit and determination to unravel the gypsy girl's secret and bring justice to those involved.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Secrets, Justice
