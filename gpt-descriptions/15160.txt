Synopsis: 

In 'On Commando' by Van Warmelo, Dietlof, the protagonist, finds himself thrust into the heart of the Boer War, fighting for his homeland against the British forces. As he navigates the treacherous landscapes and faces the harsh realities of war, Dietlof's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the trials and tribulations of a soldier's life during a tumultuous time in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Survival, Sacrifice
