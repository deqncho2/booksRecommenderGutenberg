Synopsis: In "The 13th Immortal" by Robert Silverberg, a renowned scientist discovers a way to achieve immortality, but with a twist. Each time a person undergoes the process, they are reborn with all their memories intact, but in a different body and time period. As the 13th immortal, the protagonist embarks on a thrilling journey through history, encountering famous figures and witnessing pivotal moments. However, the consequences of immortality and the search for true purpose become increasingly complex, leading to unexpected revelations and moral dilemmas.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Historical Fiction

Themes: Immortality, Identity, Ethics, Purpose
