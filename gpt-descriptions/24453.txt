Synopsis: 
In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, Vol. 146, June 17, 1914', readers are transported to the vibrant and satirical world of early 20th-century London. This volume of the iconic magazine presents a collection of witty and humorous illustrations, articles, and cartoons that provide a sharp commentary on the social, political, and cultural landscape of the time. From clever observations on fashion and etiquette to cleverly crafted political satire, this edition of Punch offers a delightful and insightful glimpse into the world of early 20th-century London.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Magazine

Themes: Social commentary, Politics, Culture
