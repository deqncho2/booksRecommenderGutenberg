Synopsis: In 'The Addicts' by Joseph Samachson, a group of seemingly ordinary individuals find themselves entangled in a mysterious and dangerous game. As they navigate through a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected alliances, they must confront their own addictions and face the consequences of their choices. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning who can be trusted and what is truly at stake.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Addiction, Deception, Identity
