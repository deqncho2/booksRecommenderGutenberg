Synopsis: In "Florence Nightingale: A Biography" by Annie Matheson, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the life of one of history's most influential figures. From her privileged upbringing to her groundbreaking work as a nurse during the Crimean War, Florence Nightingale's story is one of determination, compassion, and resilience. Matheson delves into Nightingale's personal struggles, her tireless efforts to improve healthcare, and her unwavering commitment to social reform. This biography offers a fascinating exploration of Nightingale's remarkable life and the lasting impact she had on the field of nursing.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Determination, Compassion, Social Reform
