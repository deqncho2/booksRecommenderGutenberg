Synopsis:
'Ayala's Angel' by Anthony Trollope is a captivating novel that follows the life of Ayala Dormer, a young and spirited woman who finds herself torn between love and societal expectations. When Ayala's family faces financial difficulties, she is forced to leave her comfortable life in the countryside and move to London. There, she encounters a series of romantic entanglements and unexpected encounters that challenge her beliefs and desires. As Ayala navigates the complexities of love, she must confront the choices she must make and the consequences they may bring.

Genres:
Romance, Victorian literature, Social commentary

Themes:
Love and desire, societal expectations, personal freedom
