Synopsis: 

'From Ocean to Ocean: Across a Continent on a Bicycle: An Account of a Solitary Ride From Adelaide to Port Darwin' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the vast and diverse landscapes of Australia. Jerome J. Murif, an adventurous cyclist, embarks on a daring solo ride from the southern city of Adelaide to the northern port of Darwin, covering thousands of kilometers across the continent. As he pedals through rugged terrains, encounters unpredictable weather conditions, and faces unexpected challenges, Murif's captivating narrative offers a unique perspective on the beauty, hardships, and resilience of the Australian outback. 

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Nature's Majesty
