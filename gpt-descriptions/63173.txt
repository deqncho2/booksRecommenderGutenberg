Synopsis: In "The Son of Columbus," Molly Elliot Seawell takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a young man named Christopher Columbus, who happens to be the direct descendant of the famous explorer. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows Christopher's quest to uncover the truth about his family's legacy and his own place in the world. As he navigates through love, ambition, and the complexities of his heritage, Christopher's journey becomes a thrilling exploration of identity, discovery, and the enduring power of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Legacy, Family, Exploration, Ambition
