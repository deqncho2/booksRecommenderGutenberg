Synopsis: In 'The First Afghan War' by Morris, Mowbray, a group of British soldiers find themselves embroiled in a treacherous conflict in the rugged and unforgiving terrain of Afghanistan. As they navigate the complexities of war, they must confront not only the enemy forces but also their own fears, doubts, and loyalties. With danger lurking at every turn, the soldiers must rely on their courage and resilience to survive in a land that seems determined to swallow them whole.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Survival, Betrayal, Identity
