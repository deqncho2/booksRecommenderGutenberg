Synopsis: 
'Letters of Peregrine Pickle' is an epistolary novel that follows the misadventures of the eponymous character as he navigates through a series of humorous and often scandalous encounters. Peregrine, a young and impulsive gentleman, embarks on a journey filled with love affairs, duels, and encounters with eccentric characters. Through a collection of letters, the reader is taken on a rollicking ride as Peregrine's escapades unfold, revealing a world of wit, satire, and social commentary.

Genres: 
Satire, Comedy, Epistolary Fiction

Themes: 
Love and Relationships, Social Critique, Coming of Age
