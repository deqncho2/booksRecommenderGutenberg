Synopsis: In "For the Sake of the School" by Angela Brazil, a group of spirited students at a prestigious boarding school find themselves facing unexpected challenges and thrilling adventures. As they navigate the complexities of friendship, loyalty, and personal growth, they uncover hidden secrets that could change the course of their lives forever. With a mix of mystery, romance, and a dash of mischief, this captivating tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages until the very end.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Personal Growth, Secrets
