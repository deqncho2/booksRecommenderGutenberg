Synopsis: In "From Boniface to Bank Burglar; Or, The Price of Persecution," we follow the gripping journey of a successful business man whose life takes an unexpected turn due to a grave miscarriage of justice. As he is wrongly accused and persecuted, he is forced to confront the dark underbelly of society and make choices that will forever alter his destiny. This thrilling tale explores the transformation of an ordinary man into a notorious bank looter, delving into the depths of human nature and the consequences of societal injustice.

Genres: Crime, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Persecution, Injustice, Transformation
