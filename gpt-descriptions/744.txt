Synopsis: In 'The Golden Mean', Bonnell and Nemiroff take readers on a thrilling journey through the world of mathematics, where a brilliant mathematician discovers a hidden pattern that could change the course of history. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where his life and the fate of the world hang in the balance. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Mathematical Fiction

Themes: Mathematics, Discovery, Consequences
