Synopsis:
In "Janet of the Dunes," we follow the captivating journey of Janet, a young girl who finds herself living a simple life on the sandy shores of Cape Cod. As she navigates the challenges of her unconventional upbringing, Janet's encounters with nature, love, and the mysteries of the dunes shape her into a resilient and independent woman. With vivid descriptions of the coastal landscape and a cast of intriguing characters, this coming-of-age tale will transport readers to a world of adventure and self-discovery.

Genres:
Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes:
Nature, Independence, Self-discovery
