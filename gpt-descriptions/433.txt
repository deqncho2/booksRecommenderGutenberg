Synopsis: In 'The Conflict' by David Graham Phillips, a young journalist uncovers a web of corruption and deceit that reaches the highest levels of power. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of society, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between truth and lies becomes increasingly blurred. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of political intrigue and personal betrayal, all while trying to expose the truth that could shatter the foundations of society.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Political Fiction

Themes: Corruption, Power, Truth vs. Deception
