Synopsis: In 'The White Road of Mystery: The Note-Book of an American Ambulancier', follow the gripping and mysterious journey of an American ambulancier as he navigates the treacherous battlefields of World War I. Filled with unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling narrative takes readers on a suspenseful exploration of the human spirit amidst the chaos of war. As the ambulancier uncovers a series of enigmatic clues, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets that could change the course of history. With danger lurking at every turn, the ambulancier must rely on his wit and courage to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, War

Themes: Courage, Secrets, Survival
