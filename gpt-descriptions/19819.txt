Synopsis:
In Milton's Comus, a young woman named Lady is separated from her brothers in a dark forest. As she wanders alone, she encounters a mysterious and seductive figure named Comus, who offers her a tempting proposition. With her virtue at stake, Lady must navigate a treacherous world of illusions, enchantments, and moral dilemmas. Will she succumb to the allure of Comus or find the strength to resist his charms?

Genres:
Drama, Poetry, Fantasy

Themes:
Morality, Temptation, Power of Virtue
