Synopsis: In "Army Life in a Black Regiment," Thomas Wentworth Higginson takes readers on a captivating journey through his experiences as the commander of the first federally authorized African American regiment during the American Civil War. From the challenges of training and organizing the troops to the camaraderie and resilience displayed on the battlefield, Higginson provides a unique and intimate perspective on the courage and determination of these soldiers fighting for their freedom.

Genres: Memoir, History, Military

Themes: Racial equality, Courage, Resilience, Brotherhood, Leadership
