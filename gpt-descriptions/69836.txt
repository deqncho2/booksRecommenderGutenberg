Synopsis: 

Join adventurer Elihu Stewart as he embarks on a thrilling journey down the Mackenzie River and up the Yukon in the year 1906. In this captivating travelogue, Stewart vividly recounts his encounters with the untamed wilderness, treacherous rapids, and the fascinating characters he meets along the way. From the breathtaking landscapes to the heart-pounding adventures, this gripping tale will transport readers to a time when the Canadian North was still largely unexplored. 

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Nature's Majesty
