Synopsis: 
In "New Year's Day (The 'Seventies)" by Edith Wharton, a group of friends gathers at a luxurious country estate to celebrate the arrival of the new year. As the night unfolds, secrets are revealed, tensions rise, and unexpected events take place, leading to a dramatic climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats. With vivid descriptions and a keen eye for human nature, Wharton explores the complexities of relationships, the masks people wear, and the consequences of past actions. 

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Relationships, Deception, Consequences
