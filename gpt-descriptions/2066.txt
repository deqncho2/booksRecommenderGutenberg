Synopsis: 

In the thrilling novel "Wildfire" by Zane Grey, a young cowboy finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous range war that threatens to consume the entire town. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and faces off against ruthless adversaries, he must also confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of those he holds dear. With vivid descriptions of the untamed West and a gripping narrative, "Wildfire" will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Redemption
