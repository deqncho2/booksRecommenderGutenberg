Synopsis: 
In 'And Gone Tomorrow' by Andrew J. Offutt, a small town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances that have left the community in a state of fear and paranoia. As the townspeople struggle to uncover the truth behind these unsettling events, they soon realize that there may be more to the disappearances than meets the eye. With each passing day, the tension rises, and the line between reality and nightmare becomes increasingly blurred. As the secrets of the town unravel, the residents must confront their darkest fears and confront the sinister forces that threaten to consume them all.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Fear, Paranoia, Secrets, Supernatural
