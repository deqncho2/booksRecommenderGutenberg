Synopsis: 

In "The Sword of Deborah: First-hand impressions of the British Women's Army in France," Jesse F. Tennyson takes us on a captivating journey through the experiences of the British Women's Army during World War I. Through the eyes of the protagonist, we witness the challenges, triumphs, and sacrifices of these courageous women as they navigate the frontlines and fight for their place in a male-dominated world. From the camaraderie forged in the trenches to the heart-wrenching moments of loss, this gripping account offers a unique perspective on the often overlooked role of women in war.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Women's fiction

Themes: Gender roles, Empowerment, Sacrifice, Camaraderie
