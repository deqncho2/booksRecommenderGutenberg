Synopsis: 
'American War Ballads and Lyrics, Volume 2' is a captivating collection of songs and ballads that chronicles the tumultuous history of America, from the Colonial Wars to the Civil War. This volume delves into the stirring tales of the Revolution, the War of 1812-15, the conflict with Mexico, and the heart-wrenching events of the Civil War. Through powerful and evocative lyrics, the book transports readers to the battlefields, capturing the bravery, sacrifice, and emotions of those who fought for their country. From patriotic anthems to haunting laments, this anthology offers a poignant and immersive journey through America's defining conflicts.

Genres: Poetry, History, Music
Themes: Patriotism, War, Sacrifice, National Identity
