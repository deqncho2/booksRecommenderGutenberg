Synopsis: 
In the Sixties takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous decade that defined a generation. Through the eyes of the protagonist, we witness the rise of counterculture, the civil rights movement, and the Vietnam War, all while navigating personal relationships and self-discovery. As the world undergoes radical transformations, the protagonist must confront their own beliefs and values, ultimately leading to a profound exploration of identity and the power of collective action.

Genres: Historical fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Identity, Social change, Rebellion, Love and relationships
