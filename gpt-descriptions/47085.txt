Synopsis:
In the second volume of "Cradock Nowell: A Tale of the New Forest," the story continues to unfold with captivating twists and turns. Cradock, the young protagonist, finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love. As he navigates the treacherous paths of the New Forest, he encounters mysterious characters and faces unexpected challenges that test his courage and resilience. With each page, the plot thickens, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic events that unfold in this enthralling tale.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes:
Forbidden love, Secrets and deception, Coming of age
