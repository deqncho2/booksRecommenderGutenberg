Synopsis: In 'Our Own Set: A Novel' by Ossip Schubin, readers are transported to the vibrant social scene of late 19th-century Vienna. The story revolves around a group of young artists, musicians, and intellectuals who form a close-knit circle, navigating the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. As their lives intertwine, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the characters find themselves caught in a web of passion and intrigue. With its richly drawn characters and evocative setting, this novel offers a captivating exploration of the human condition and the pursuit of personal and artistic fulfillment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Artistic Expression, Social Conventions, Friendship and Betrayal
