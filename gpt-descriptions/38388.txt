Synopsis:
In 'A Bride from the Bush' by E. W. Hornung, a young Englishman finds himself in the vast and untamed Australian outback, where he becomes captivated by the enigmatic beauty of a local woman. As he delves deeper into her world, he uncovers a web of secrets and hidden desires that threaten to unravel their lives. With the harsh landscape as their backdrop, their passionate and forbidden love story unfolds, leading to unexpected consequences that will test their loyalty and resilience.

Genres:
Romance, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes:
Forbidden love, Cultural clash, Identity, Survival
