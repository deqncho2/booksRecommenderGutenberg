Synopsis: In 'The Cruise of the Sally D' by Otis, James, a group of adventurous young friends embark on a thrilling sailing expedition aboard the Sally D, a magnificent yacht. As they set sail, they encounter mysterious islands, treacherous storms, and unexpected dangers that test their courage and friendship. With each new challenge, they uncover hidden secrets and embark on a quest that will change their lives forever. Will they be able to navigate the perils of the sea and unravel the mysteries that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
