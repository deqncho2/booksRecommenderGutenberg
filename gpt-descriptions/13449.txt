Synopsis: In 'The Plain Man and His Wife' by Arnold Bennett, a seemingly ordinary man and his wife find themselves caught in a web of unexpected events that challenge their beliefs, test their resilience, and force them to confront the complexities of life. As they navigate through various trials and tribulations, their love and determination are put to the ultimate test, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and transformation. With its intriguing twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the profound impact of ordinary lives and the extraordinary power of human connection.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and relationships, Self-discovery, Resilience, Social class
