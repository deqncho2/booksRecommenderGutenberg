Synopsis: In "Beneath an Umbrella," Hawthorne weaves a captivating tale that follows the lives of two individuals who find themselves unexpectedly connected by a mysterious umbrella. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the characters are faced with choices that will forever alter their paths. With its intriguing twists and turns, this story explores the complexities of human nature and the power of fate.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Fate and Destiny, Secrets and Identity, Connection and Interdependence
