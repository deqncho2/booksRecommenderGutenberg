Synopsis: In 'Under Greek Skies' by Ioulia D. Dragoume, a young woman embarks on a journey to Greece, seeking solace and a fresh start. As she immerses herself in the vibrant culture, she discovers hidden secrets, encounters unexpected love, and unravels the mysteries of her own past. With breathtaking descriptions of the Greek landscapes and a captivating narrative, this novel takes readers on a captivating adventure that will leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Travel

Themes: Self-discovery, Cultural exploration, Love and relationships
