Synopsis: 
In 'Buddhism in the Modern World', Kenneth J. Saunders takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of Buddhism in contemporary society. From its ancient origins to its relevance in the present day, this book explores the ways in which Buddhism has adapted and thrived amidst the complexities of the modern world. Through insightful analysis and engaging anecdotes, Saunders delves into the various ways in which Buddhism has influenced and been influenced by globalization, technology, and social change. This thought-provoking exploration sheds light on the enduring appeal of Buddhism and its ability to provide guidance and solace in an ever-changing world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Religion

Themes: Adaptation, Globalization, Technology, Social Change, Spirituality
