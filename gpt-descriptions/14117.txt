Synopsis: In 'Wanted, a Young Woman to Do Housework: Business principles applied to housework', author C. Hélène Barker takes a fresh and unconventional approach to the mundane task of housework. Through a captivating narrative, the book explores how applying business principles can transform the way we approach and manage household chores. With wit and insight, Barker offers practical strategies and innovative ideas that challenge traditional notions of housework, making it an engaging and empowering read for anyone seeking to revolutionize their domestic routine.

Genres: Self-help, Non-fiction, Lifestyle

Themes: Productivity, Time management, Gender roles, Empowerment
