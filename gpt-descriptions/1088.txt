Synopsis: In "Rolf in the Woods" by Ernest Thompson Seton, a young boy named Rolf finds himself lost in the vast wilderness. As he navigates through the challenges of survival, he encounters various animals and learns valuable lessons about nature and self-reliance. With each encounter, Rolf's understanding of the natural world deepens, leading him on a transformative journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Nature

Themes: Wilderness survival, Animal behavior, Self-discovery
