Synopsis: 
In 'Gypsy Sorcery and Fortune Telling', Charles Godfrey Leland takes readers on a captivating journey into the mystical world of gypsy magic and divination. Through a collection of incantations, medical magic, and enchanting tales, Leland explores the secrets of gypsy sorcery and the art of fortune telling. From love spells to curses, from palmistry to tarot reading, this book unveils the ancient practices and beliefs of the Romani people, offering a fascinating glimpse into their rich cultural heritage. Prepare to be enchanted by the captivating stories and intriguing rituals that lie within the pages of this mystical guide.

Genres: Occult, Folklore, Non-fiction

Themes: Magic, Divination, Cultural Heritage
