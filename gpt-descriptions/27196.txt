Synopsis: In 'Cheap Postage' by Joshua Leavitt, a young woman named Emily discovers a mysterious package in her mailbox that sets off a chain of events she never could have imagined. As she delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the package, Emily finds herself entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred. With each twist and turn, she must confront her own fears and question the true nature of the world around her. 'Cheap Postage' is a gripping psychological thriller that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the shocking truth.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Perception vs. reality, Identity, Deception
