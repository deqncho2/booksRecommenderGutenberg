Synopsis: In 'Joe Carson's Weapon' by Adams, James R., a retired detective finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse when a mysterious weapon falls into his possession. As he delves deeper into the weapon's origins, he uncovers a web of conspiracy and deceit that threatens not only his own life but the safety of the entire city. With time running out, Joe Carson must navigate a treacherous landscape of corruption and betrayal to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Conspiracy, Betrayal, Redemption
