Synopsis: In 'The Boys' Book of Model Aeroplanes: How to Build and Fly Them: With the Story of the Evolution of the Flying Machine', Francis A. Collins takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the history of aviation. From the early pioneers of flight to the cutting-edge technology of the time, this book provides a comprehensive guide to building and flying model aeroplanes. With detailed instructions and captivating anecdotes, Collins inspires young readers to explore the wonders of flight and ignite their passion for aviation.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, How-to

Themes: Aviation, Exploration, Ingenuity
