Synopsis: In 'Little Citizens: The Humours of School Life' by Kelly, Myra, readers are transported into the lively and unpredictable world of a school filled with curious and mischievous children. Through a series of humorous and heartwarming anecdotes, the author captures the essence of school life, from the hilarious antics of the students to the challenges faced by teachers. From unexpected friendships to unforgettable pranks, this delightful book offers a glimpse into the joys and trials of growing up in a school environment.

Genres: Humor, Children's Literature, Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Childhood, Education, Coming of Age
