Synopsis: In 'Your Affectionate Godmother' by Glyn, Elinor, a young woman named Alice finds herself caught in a web of secrets and intrigue when she becomes the ward of a mysterious godmother. As Alice delves deeper into her godmother's world, she uncovers a hidden society filled with forbidden desires, dangerous alliances, and unexpected love. With her own life at stake, Alice must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and find her own place in this captivating tale of love, betrayal, and self-discovery.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Forbidden love, Personal identity and self-discovery
