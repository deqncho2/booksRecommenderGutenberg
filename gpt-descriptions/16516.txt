Synopsis: In "Walker's Appeal, with a Brief Sketch of His Life: And Also Garnet's Address to the Slaves of the United States of America," Henry Highland Garnet and David Walker present powerful and provocative writings that challenge the institution of slavery in the United States. Through their impassioned words, they call upon enslaved individuals to rise up against their oppressors and fight for their freedom. This work provides a glimpse into the lives of these influential figures and their tireless efforts to advocate for the rights of African Americans. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Political literature, Historical document

Themes: Slavery, Resistance, Civil rights, Empowerment
