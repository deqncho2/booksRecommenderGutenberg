Synopsis: In 'St. George's Cross; Or, England Above All', H. G. Keene takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of England, where a young protagonist finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and danger. As he navigates through a world of secret societies, espionage, and hidden agendas, he must uncover the truth and protect his country from a sinister plot that threatens to tear it apart. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Betrayal, National Identity
