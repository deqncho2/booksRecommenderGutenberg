Synopsis: 
In 'De Tribus Impostoribus, A. D. 1230: The Three Impostors', a mysterious French manuscript from 1716 is brought to light, revealing a shocking and controversial secret that has been hidden for centuries. Translated with meticulous notes and comments, this work delves into the dark world of deceit and manipulation, exploring the lives of three enigmatic impostors who have left an indelible mark on history. As the reader unravels the intricate web of lies and deception, they are confronted with a profound question: can we truly trust those who claim to be our leaders and saviors? Prepare to be captivated by this gripping tale of intrigue and skepticism.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Deception, Skepticism, Power
