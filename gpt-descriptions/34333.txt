Synopsis: In 'Captain Ted: A Boy's Adventures Among Hiding Slackers in the Great Georgia Swamp' by Pendleton, Louis, young Ted embarks on a thrilling journey through the mysterious Great Georgia Swamp. When he stumbles upon a group of hiding slackers, Ted's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test as he navigates treacherous terrain and encounters unexpected allies and foes. With danger lurking at every turn, Ted must rely on his wits and determination to uncover the secrets hidden within the swamp and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Discovery
