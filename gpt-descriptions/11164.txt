Synopsis: In 'Rough and Tumble Engineering' by James H. Maggard, a brilliant engineer finds himself caught in a web of corporate espionage and high-stakes engineering challenges. As he navigates through a world of cutthroat competition and dangerous secrets, he must rely on his ingenuity and resourcefulness to survive. With thrilling action sequences and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Techno-thriller

Themes: Corporate espionage, Engineering challenges, Survival
