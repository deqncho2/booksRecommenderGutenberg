Synopsis: In 'The Road to Providence' by Maria Thompson Daviess, a young woman named Mary Ware embarks on a journey to the small town of Providence, where she hopes to find love, adventure, and a sense of belonging. Along the way, she encounters a cast of colorful characters, navigates unexpected challenges, and discovers the true meaning of home. As Mary's story unfolds, readers are drawn into a world filled with humor, romance, and the timeless quest for self-discovery.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Love and relationships, Identity and self-discovery, Community and belonging
