Synopsis: In 'Adrift in New York: Tom and Florence Braving the World' by Horatio Alger Jr., two young siblings, Tom and Florence, find themselves alone in the bustling city of New York after their father's sudden death. With no money or family to turn to, they must navigate the treacherous streets, encountering both kind strangers and dangerous villains along the way. As they struggle to survive, they learn valuable lessons about friendship, perseverance, and the true meaning of family.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Social class, Family
