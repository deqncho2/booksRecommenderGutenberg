Synopsis: 
In "A Harum-Scarum Schoolgirl" by Angela Brazil, we are introduced to the spirited and mischievous Patty Fairfax, a young girl who finds herself in one adventure after another at her boarding school. From secret societies to daring escapades, Patty's lively personality and knack for trouble make her a captivating character to follow. As she navigates friendships, rivalries, and the challenges of school life, Patty's escapades will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering what she will get up to next.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, School story

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Resilience
