Synopsis: 
"The Boats of the "Glen Carrig"" follows the harrowing adventures of the survivors of the shipwrecked vessel, Glen Carrig, as they navigate through strange and perilous places on Earth. After their ship strikes a hidden rock in the unknown seas to the Southward, the crew finds themselves in a series of bizarre and otherworldly encounters. John Winterstraw recounts these extraordinary events to his son, James Winterstraw, in the year 1757, and the account is meticulously transcribed into a manuscript. 

Genres: 
Adventure, Horror, Fantasy

Themes: 
Survival, Exploration, the Unknown, Man vs. Nature
