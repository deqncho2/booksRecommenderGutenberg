Synopsis: 

'A German Pompadour: Being the Extraordinary History of Wilhelmine van Grävenitz, Landhofmeisterin of Wirtemberg' delves into the captivating life of Wilhelmine van Grävenitz, a woman who defied societal norms and rose to power in 18th century Germany. From her humble beginnings as a maid to becoming the influential mistress of Duke Eberhard Ludwig, Wilhelmine's journey is filled with scandal, ambition, and intrigue. As she navigates the treacherous world of court politics and power struggles, Wilhelmine's determination and cunning make her a force to be reckoned with. 

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Drama

Themes: Power and ambition, Gender roles and societal expectations, Love and desire
