Synopsis: In 'A Woodland Queen ('Reine des Bois') — Complete', André Theuriet takes us on a captivating journey through the enchanting forests of France. The story revolves around a mysterious young woman named Yolande, who possesses an ethereal beauty and an uncanny connection with nature. As Yolande's life intertwines with the lives of those around her, secrets are unveiled, love blossoms, and the power of the natural world becomes a force to be reckoned with. With vivid descriptions and a touch of magic, this tale explores the delicate balance between humanity and the wilderness, leaving readers spellbound until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Nature, Beauty, Secrets, Love
