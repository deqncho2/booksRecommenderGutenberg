Synopsis: In the small town of Mawm, a group of men find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected alliances. As their lives intertwine, they must confront their pasts and make difficult choices that will shape their futures. With a mysterious newcomer stirring up trouble and a long-buried secret threatening to unravel everything, the men of Mawm must navigate a treacherous path to redemption and forgiveness.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and lies, Redemption, Forgiveness
