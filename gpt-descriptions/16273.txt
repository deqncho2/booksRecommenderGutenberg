Synopsis: 

In 'The Manual of Heraldry; Fifth Edition: Being a Concise Description of the Several Terms Used, and Containing a Dictionary of Every Designation in the Science', an anonymous author takes readers on a captivating journey into the intricate world of heraldry. This comprehensive guide unveils the secrets behind the symbols, colors, and designs that have adorned coats of arms for centuries. From the majestic lions to the enigmatic unicorns, this book unravels the hidden meanings and historical significance behind each emblem, providing a fascinating insight into the art of heraldry.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, History

Themes: Symbolism, History, Art
