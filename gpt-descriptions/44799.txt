Synopsis: 
'Benjamin Disraeli, the Earl of Beaconsfield, K.G.: Cartoons from "Punch" 1843-1878' is a captivating collection of political cartoons that chronicles the life and career of Benjamin Disraeli, one of the most influential figures in British politics during the 19th century. Through the witty and satirical illustrations from the renowned magazine "Punch," readers are taken on a visual journey that explores Disraeli's rise to power, his political strategies, and his interactions with other prominent figures of the time. This collection offers a unique perspective on Disraeli's life, providing both historical insight and entertainment.

Genres: Political satire, Biography, Historical

Themes: Politics, Power, Satire, Social commentary
