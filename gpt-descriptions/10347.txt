Synopsis: In 'Free from School' by Rahul Alvares, embark on a captivating journey that challenges the conventional notions of education. Follow the author as he explores alternative learning environments, from the lush forests of Goa to the bustling streets of Mumbai. Through his experiences, Alvares delves into the transformative power of self-directed education and the profound impact it can have on one's personal growth and understanding of the world. This thought-provoking memoir will inspire readers to question the traditional education system and consider the possibilities of a more liberated approach to learning.

Genres: Memoir, Education, Travel

Themes: Alternative education, Self-directed learning, Personal growth, Challenging societal norms
