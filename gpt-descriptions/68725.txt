Synopsis: In 'Snap: A legend of the Lone Mountain' by Phillipps-Wolley, Clive, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young man named Snap embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind a legendary creature lurking in the depths of the Lone Mountain. As Snap delves deeper into the secrets of this mythical being, he finds himself entangled in a web of danger, adventure, and unexpected alliances. With each step, Snap's courage and determination are tested, leading him to discover the true nature of friendship and the power of self-discovery. This gripping narrative will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the mysteries that lie within the Lone Mountain.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Self-discovery
