Synopsis: 
'An Old Man's Love' by Anthony Trollope tells the story of an elderly man named William Whittlestaff who finds himself torn between duty and desire when he falls in love with a young woman. As he grapples with his own insecurities and societal expectations, Whittlestaff must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and the pursuit of happiness. Set against the backdrop of a small English village, this novel explores the timeless themes of age, love, and the choices we make in the pursuit of our hearts' desires.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Victorian Literature

Themes: 
Love, Duty, Age, Society
