Synopsis: In 'A Knyght Ther Was' by Robert F. Young, a young squire named Thomas embarks on a perilous journey to prove his worthiness and fulfill his destiny. As he faces treacherous challenges and encounters mysterious characters, Thomas must navigate a world filled with magic, danger, and the ever-present shadow of a legendary knight. With his courage and determination tested at every turn, Thomas must discover the true meaning of knighthood and the power that lies within himself.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Heroism, Identity, Destiny
