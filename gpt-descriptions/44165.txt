Synopsis: In "Soldier Rigdale: How He Sailed in the Mayflower and How He Served Miles Standish," we follow the captivating journey of Soldier Rigdale, a young man who embarks on the historic voyage aboard the Mayflower. As the ship sails towards the unknown shores of America, Soldier Rigdale finds himself caught in the midst of a tumultuous love triangle and becomes entangled in the conflicts and challenges faced by the Pilgrims. With the enigmatic Miles Standish as his commander, Soldier Rigdale must navigate the treacherous waters of loyalty, honor, and survival in this gripping tale of adventure and sacrifice.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Love and Desire, Loyalty, Survival
