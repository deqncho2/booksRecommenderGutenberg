Synopsis: 
In "Spanish Highways and Byways," Katharine Lee Bates takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of Spain. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, she explores the hidden gems and lesser-known corners of this vibrant country. From the bustling streets of Madrid to the picturesque villages of Andalusia, Bates delves into the rich history, diverse culture, and breathtaking beauty that make Spain a truly captivating destination. As she immerses herself in the local customs and traditions, she encounters fascinating characters and experiences unforgettable moments that will leave readers yearning to embark on their own Spanish adventure.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Cultural immersion, History, Beauty of nature
