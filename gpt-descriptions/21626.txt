Synopsis: 
'Adrift in the Wilds; Or, The Adventures of Two Shipwrecked Boys' by Edward Sylvester Ellis is a thrilling adventure novel that follows the harrowing journey of two young boys who find themselves stranded on a deserted island after a shipwreck. As they struggle to survive in the untamed wilderness, they encounter dangerous animals, face treacherous terrain, and must rely on their resourcefulness and courage to overcome the numerous challenges that come their way. With each passing day, their bond grows stronger, and they discover the true meaning of friendship and resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Coming-of-age

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Resilience
