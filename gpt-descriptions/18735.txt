Synopsis: In 'The Little Red Hen: An Old English Folk Tale', a determined little hen embarks on a journey to fulfill her dreams, facing numerous challenges along the way. With her unwavering determination and resourcefulness, she teaches valuable lessons about hard work, perseverance, and the importance of collaboration. This timeless tale captivates readers with its engaging narrative and delightful characters, leaving them inspired and eager to discover the hen's ultimate triumph.

Genres: Children's literature, Folklore, Fable

Themes: Hard work, Perseverance, Collaboration
