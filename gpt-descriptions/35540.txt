Synopsis: In 'The Great White Army' by Max Pemberton, a renowned surgeon finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is called to assist with a top-secret medical mission in a remote and treacherous location. As he delves deeper into the mission, he uncovers shocking secrets and faces unexpected adversaries, forcing him to question his own beliefs and confront his darkest fears. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the surgeon must navigate a perilous path to uncover the truth and save lives.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Redemption, Morality, Survival
