Synopsis: In 'Mammon and Co.' by E. F. Benson, a young and ambitious stockbroker named Paul Pleydell finds himself caught in a web of financial intrigue and personal drama. As he navigates the cutthroat world of high finance, Paul becomes entangled with a mysterious woman who holds the key to a scandal that could bring down the entire financial empire. With his reputation and livelihood on the line, Paul must make difficult choices that will test his loyalty, integrity, and ultimately, his own humanity.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Betrayal
