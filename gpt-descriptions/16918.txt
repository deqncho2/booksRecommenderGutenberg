Synopsis: In the small town of Shatemuc, a young woman named Margaret is torn between her duty to her family and her desire for independence. As she navigates the complexities of love, societal expectations, and personal growth, Margaret finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and betrayals that threaten to unravel the very fabric of her world. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque Hills of the Shatemuc, this captivating novel explores the timeless themes of love, loyalty, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Family, Independence, Love, Betrayal
