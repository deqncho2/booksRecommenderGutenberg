Synopsis: In "Charge It': Keeping Up With Harry" by Irving Bacheller, readers are introduced to Harry, a charismatic and ambitious young man who is determined to make a name for himself in the bustling city of New York. With his charm and wit, Harry navigates the world of high society, rubbing shoulders with the elite and indulging in the luxuries of the time. However, as Harry's desires grow and his spending habits spiral out of control, he finds himself entangled in a web of financial troubles and questionable decisions. Will Harry be able to maintain his extravagant lifestyle, or will he be forced to face the consequences of his reckless choices?

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Comedy

Themes: Ambition, Materialism, Consequences of Excess
