Synopsis: In 'The Flame' by Gabriele D'Annunzio, a young Italian nobleman finds himself irresistibly drawn to a mysterious woman who embodies both beauty and danger. As their passionate affair unfolds, he becomes entangled in a web of desire, obsession, and betrayal that will test the limits of his own morality. Set against the backdrop of late 19th-century Italy, this captivating novel explores the complexities of love, power, and the destructive forces that can consume a person's soul.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Love and desire, Obsession, Betrayal, Morality, Power dynamics
