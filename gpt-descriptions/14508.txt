Synopsis: 
In 'The Christmas Dinner' by Knapp, Shepherd, a seemingly ordinary family gathering takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious guest arrives uninvited. As the evening unfolds, secrets are revealed, tensions rise, and the true meaning of family is put to the test. With twists and turns at every corner, this heartwarming tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Family

Themes: Family dynamics, Secrets and revelations, The importance of forgiveness
