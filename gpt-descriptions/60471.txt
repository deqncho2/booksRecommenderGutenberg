Synopsis: 
"The Bustan of Sadi: Translated from the Persian with an introduction" takes readers on a captivating journey through the wisdom and wit of the renowned Persian poet, Sadi. This collection of beautifully crafted stories, anecdotes, and verses offers a glimpse into the human experience, exploring themes of love, morality, and the pursuit of happiness. From tales of kings and beggars to reflections on the nature of life, Sadi's words resonate with timeless wisdom, inviting readers to reflect on their own lives and find inspiration in the power of storytelling.

Genres: Poetry, Prose, Philosophy

Themes: Love, Morality, Happiness, Human Experience
