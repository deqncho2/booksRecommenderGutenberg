Synopsis: In 'The Definite Object: A Romance of New York' by Farnol, Jeffery, a young Englishman named Peter embarks on a journey to New York City, where he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and romance. Drawn to a captivating woman named Phyllis, Peter finds himself caught between her and a dangerous suitor. As he navigates the treacherous world of high society, Peter must uncover the truth behind Phyllis' enigmatic past and protect her from impending danger. With its gripping plot and unexpected twists, this novel takes readers on a thrilling ride through the bustling streets of New York.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and obsession, Social class and society, Identity and self-discovery
