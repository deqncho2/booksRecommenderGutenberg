Synopsis: In "The Truth about Jesus: Is He a Myth?" Mangasarian delves into the controversial topic of the historical existence of Jesus. With meticulous research and thought-provoking analysis, he challenges the traditional narrative surrounding Jesus and presents a compelling argument that challenges long-held beliefs. This thought-provoking exploration will leave readers questioning their own understanding of one of history's most influential figures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Religious Studies

Themes: Historical accuracy, Religious beliefs, Critical thinking
