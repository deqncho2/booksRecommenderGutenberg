Synopsis: In 'The Dawn of a To-morrow', a young woman named Victoria embarks on a journey of self-discovery and transformation after a life-altering event. As she navigates through the challenges of her new reality, Victoria encounters unexpected friendships, explores the depths of her own strength, and learns the power of embracing change. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century England, this captivating novel takes readers on a poignant and inspiring journey of hope, resilience, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Self-discovery, Transformation, Friendship, Resilience, Embracing change
