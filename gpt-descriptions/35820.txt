Synopsis: In 'Granny's Wonderful Chair & Its Tales of Fairy Times', a young girl named Rosy discovers a magical chair that transports her to enchanting worlds filled with talking animals, mischievous fairies, and brave knights. Each time she sits in the chair, Granny's stories come to life, captivating Rosy and the reader with their whimsy and adventure. From a daring rescue mission to a magical feast, Rosy's journey through Fairy Times is a delightful escape into a world of imagination and wonder.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Fairy Tales

Themes: Imagination, Adventure, Magic, Family
