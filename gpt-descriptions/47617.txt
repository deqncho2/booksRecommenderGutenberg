Synopsis: 

'Edinburgh Under Sir Walter Scott' takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting city of Edinburgh during the time of the renowned Scottish author, Sir Walter Scott. Fyfe's vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes bring to life the bustling streets, grand architecture, and vibrant culture of this iconic city. From the majestic Edinburgh Castle to the hidden closes and taverns, the book unveils the secrets and stories that lie within the city's walls. As readers delve into the pages, they will discover the intertwining of fiction and reality, as Scott's literary works are seamlessly woven into the fabric of Edinburgh's history. 

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Travelogue, Biography

Themes: Scottish Literature, City Life, Historical Preservation
