Synopsis: In "The Black Cat: A Play in Three Acts" by Todhunter, John, a mysterious black cat becomes the catalyst for a series of unexpected events in the lives of a group of friends. As the cat's presence grows more ominous, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the characters are forced to confront their deepest fears. With each act, the tension escalates, leading to a gripping climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Guilt, Fate, Redemption, Psychological turmoil
