Synopsis: 
In 'A Jolly by Josh', readers are introduced to Josh, a young and imaginative boy who embarks on a thrilling adventure when he discovers a mysterious object in his backyard. As he unravels the secrets behind this extraordinary find, Josh finds himself transported to a whimsical world filled with enchanting creatures and unexpected challenges. With the help of newfound friends, Josh must navigate through this magical realm, facing both danger and wonder, in order to uncover the truth and save the day. 

Genres: 
Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: 
Imagination, Friendship, Courage
