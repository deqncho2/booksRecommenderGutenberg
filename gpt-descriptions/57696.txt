Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Charles A. Eastman' is a comprehensive collection of the works of Charles A. Eastman, a prominent Native American author and activist. This index provides a gateway to explore Eastman's writings, which encompass a wide range of topics including Native American history, culture, spirituality, and the challenges faced by indigenous communities. From his captivating narratives to his insightful essays, this collection offers a glimpse into the rich and diverse perspectives of Native American life. Whether you are a history enthusiast, a literature lover, or simply curious about Native American heritage, this index is a treasure trove waiting to be discovered.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Biography, Native American Studies

Themes: Native American culture, Indigenous rights, Identity, Assimilation, Spirituality
