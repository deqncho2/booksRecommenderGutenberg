Synopsis: In 'Security' by Anderson, Poul, we follow the gripping story of a young man named Conn Maxwell who finds himself thrust into a dangerous and mysterious world. After accepting a job as a security officer on a distant planet, Conn soon discovers that the seemingly peaceful colony harbors dark secrets and hidden agendas. As he delves deeper into the enigma surrounding the planet's inhabitants, Conn must navigate treacherous alliances, deadly encounters, and a web of deceit that threatens not only his own life but the fate of the entire colony. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Betrayal, Identity, Survival
