Synopsis: In "My Experiences in a Lunatic Asylum: By a Sane Patient," Herman Charles Merivale takes us on a gripping journey through the mysterious and unsettling world of a mental institution. Through his vivid and chilling accounts, the author unveils the dark secrets and disturbing practices that occur within the asylum's walls. As the reader delves deeper into the narrative, they are left questioning the boundaries between sanity and madness, and the true nature of the human mind.

Genres: Memoir, Psychological Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Sanity vs. Insanity, Institutionalization, Human Psychology
