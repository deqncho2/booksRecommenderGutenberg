Synopsis: In 'Andrew Golding: A Tale of the Great Plague' by Anne E. Keeling, readers are transported to 17th century London, a city ravaged by the deadly Great Plague. Amidst the chaos and despair, we follow the journey of Andrew Golding, a young apothecary's apprentice, as he navigates the treacherous streets and faces unimaginable challenges. As the plague spreads its grip on the city, Andrew's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test, leading him on a path that will forever change his life and the lives of those around him.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Survival, Sacrifice, Resilience, Community
