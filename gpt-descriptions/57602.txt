Synopsis: 
In "My German Prisons: Being the Experiences of an Officer During Two and a Half Years as a Prisoner of War," Horace Gray Gilliland takes us on a gripping journey through his harrowing experiences as a prisoner of war during World War I. From the moment he is captured by German forces, Gilliland's resilience and determination to survive are put to the ultimate test. Through vivid and poignant storytelling, he recounts the physical and psychological challenges he faced, the friendships he forged, and the moments of hope that kept him going. As he navigates the brutal conditions of various German prisons, Gilliland's story becomes a testament to the indomitable human spirit and the power of resilience in the face of adversity.

Genres: Memoir, War, History

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Friendship, Human Spirit
