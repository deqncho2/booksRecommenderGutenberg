Synopsis: In the enchanting setting of the Upper Rhine, a young trumpeter named Werner falls deeply in love with a beautiful countess named Margaretha. However, their love is challenged by societal expectations, class differences, and the interference of others. As Werner strives to win Margaretha's heart, he must navigate a world filled with passion, betrayal, and the pursuit of true love.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Social class and prejudice, Pursuit of happiness
