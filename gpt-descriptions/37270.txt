Synopsis: In 'The City in the Clouds' by Thorne, Guy, a young adventurer named Alex embarks on a thrilling journey to uncover the secrets of a hidden city floating high above the clouds. As he navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters mysterious creatures, Alex must unravel the enigmatic history of the city and confront his own inner demons. With each step closer to the truth, he becomes entangled in a web of ancient prophecies and dangerous alliances, leading to a climactic battle that will determine the fate of the city and its inhabitants.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Destiny, Redemption
