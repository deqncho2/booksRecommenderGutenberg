Synopsis: 
In 'A Woman's Journey through the Philippines: On a Cable Ship that Linked Together the Strange Lands Seen En Route', Florence Kimball Russel takes readers on a captivating adventure through the enchanting landscapes of the Philippines. Join her as she embarks on a cable ship, exploring the diverse and mysterious lands that lie along the way. From bustling cities to remote islands, Russel's vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes paint a vivid picture of the country's rich culture, breathtaking scenery, and the fascinating people she encounters. This travelogue is a captivating exploration of the Philippines, offering readers a unique perspective on this captivating country.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Cultural exploration, Personal growth, Connection with nature
