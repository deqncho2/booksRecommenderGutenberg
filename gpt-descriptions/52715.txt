Synopsis: In 'A Woman's War: A Novel' by Deeping, Warwick, we are transported to the tumultuous years of World War I, where we follow the journey of a young woman named Helen, who finds herself thrust into the heart of the conflict. As the war rages on, Helen's life takes unexpected turns, leading her to face unimaginable challenges and make difficult choices that will shape her future. With vivid storytelling and compelling characters, this novel explores the resilience, courage, and sacrifices of women during wartime, offering a captivating glimpse into a world forever changed by the ravages of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Drama

Themes: Women's experiences in war, Resilience, Sacrifice
