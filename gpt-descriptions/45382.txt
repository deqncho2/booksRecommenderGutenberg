Synopsis: In "The Blue and the Gray; Or, The Civil War as Seen by a Boy: A Story of Patriotism and Adventure in Our War for the Union," Annie Randall White takes readers on a captivating journey through the eyes of a young boy during the American Civil War. As the war unfolds, the boy finds himself caught between the conflicting loyalties of his family and friends, leading him to make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. Filled with heart-pounding adventures, unexpected alliances, and the harsh realities of war, this gripping tale explores the true meaning of patriotism and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Morality, Identity, Courage
