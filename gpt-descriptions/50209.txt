Synopsis: 
In 'The Mystery of the Sycamore' by Wells, Carolyn, a young woman named Pennington Wise finds herself entangled in a perplexing case when a wealthy socialite is found dead under mysterious circumstances. As Wise delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that lead him to question everyone's innocence. With each clue he uncovers, the puzzle becomes more complex, and the danger intensifies. Will Wise be able to solve the mystery before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Intrigue
