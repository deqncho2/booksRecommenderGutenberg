Synopsis: 
In the midst of the Civil War, a young boy named Chadwick Buford finds himself torn between loyalty to his family and his duty to his country. When he is forced to make a life-altering decision, Chadwick embarks on a journey that will test his courage, honor, and resilience. As he navigates the treacherous landscapes of war-torn Kentucky, Chadwick encounters a cast of unforgettable characters who shape his understanding of love, sacrifice, and the true meaning of home. Will he find redemption and peace amidst the chaos of war?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Duty, Courage, Identity, Redemption
