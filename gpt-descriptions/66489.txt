Synopsis: In 'Our Lady of Darkness' by Bernard Capes, a renowned writer moves into a mysterious apartment building in San Francisco, only to discover a hidden world of supernatural occurrences and dark secrets. As he delves deeper into the building's history, he becomes entangled in a web of occult forces that threaten his sanity and his very existence. With each eerie encounter, the line between reality and fantasy blurs, leaving the reader questioning what is truly lurking in the shadows.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: The power of the supernatural, the thin line between reality and imagination, the exploration of hidden worlds
