Synopsis: 
In 'On the Sweeny Wire' by Charles R. Barnes, a small town is shaken by a series of mysterious events that seem to be connected to an old legend. As the townspeople become increasingly fearful, a young journalist named Sarah Sweeny takes it upon herself to uncover the truth behind the legend and the strange occurrences. With each step closer to the truth, Sarah finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. As the tension rises, she must navigate through a maze of deception and betrayal to uncover the shocking truth that lies beneath the surface of her seemingly peaceful town.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Legends and folklore, Small-town secrets, Investigative journalism
