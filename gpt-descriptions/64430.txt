Synopsis: In 'Josie O'Gorman and the Meddlesome Major' by Emma Speed Sampson, Josie O'Gorman finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a secret plot involving a notorious major. With her quick wit and determination, Josie must navigate through a world of deception and intrigue to uncover the truth and protect those she holds dear.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Justice
