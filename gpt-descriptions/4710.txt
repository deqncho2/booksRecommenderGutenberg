Synopsis: In "The Story of Electricity," Munro, John takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating history and groundbreaking discoveries that have shaped the world of electricity. From the ancient Greeks' early experiments to the modern-day marvels of technology, this book unveils the incredible stories behind the pioneers who harnessed the power of electricity and transformed the way we live. With engaging anecdotes and vivid descriptions, Munro brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and unexpected consequences that accompanied each breakthrough, making this a must-read for anyone curious about the electrifying tale of humanity's relationship with this powerful force.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Innovation, Progress, Impact of Technology
