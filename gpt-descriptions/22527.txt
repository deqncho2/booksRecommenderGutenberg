Synopsis: In 'Beyond the Vanishing Point' by Ray Cummings, a brilliant scientist invents a device that allows him to travel through time and space. However, when he accidentally becomes trapped in a future world, he must navigate a dangerous and unfamiliar landscape filled with advanced technology, strange creatures, and a mysterious society. As he desperately searches for a way back home, he uncovers shocking secrets that could change the course of history forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Time travel, Exploration, Survival, Societal evolution
