Synopsis: In 'The Wonder-Child: An Australian Story' by Ethel Turner, a young girl named Dorothea possesses an extraordinary talent that captivates everyone around her. As she navigates the challenges of growing up in the Australian outback, Dorothea's remarkable abilities become both a blessing and a burden. With her unique gift, she must confront societal expectations, personal identity, and the complexities of fame. This enchanting tale explores the power of individuality and the pursuit of one's true purpose.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Individuality, Identity, Talent, Society
