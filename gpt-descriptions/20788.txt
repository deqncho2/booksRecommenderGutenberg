Synopsis: In "Storm Over Warlock" by Andre Norton, Shann Lantee finds himself stranded on the desolate planet of Warlock, where he must navigate treacherous landscapes and face deadly creatures in order to survive. As he uncovers the secrets of this mysterious world, Shann becomes entangled in a battle for power and must rely on his wits and courage to overcome the forces that seek to destroy him.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Power Struggles
