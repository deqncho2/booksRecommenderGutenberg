Synopsis: In 'The Adventure Girls at Happiness House' by Blank, Clair, a group of adventurous girls find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey when they stumble upon a mysterious old house. As they explore its hidden secrets, they uncover a series of clues that lead them on a quest filled with danger, friendship, and unexpected twists. With each step they take, the girls must rely on their courage and resourcefulness to unravel the enigma surrounding Happiness House and ultimately discover the truth that lies within its walls.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
