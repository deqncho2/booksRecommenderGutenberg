Synopsis: 

In 'Mental Defectives and Sexual Offenders: Report of the Committee of Inquiry Appointed by the Hon. Sir Maui Pomare, K.B.E., C.M.G., Minister of Health,' the Committee of Inquiry delves into the complex and controversial issues surrounding mental defectives and sexual offenders in New Zealand. Through meticulous research and analysis, the committee uncovers shocking truths and presents thought-provoking recommendations that challenge societal norms and perceptions. This groundbreaking report sheds light on the dark corners of society, raising important questions about the treatment and rehabilitation of individuals deemed mentally defective or sexual offenders.

Genres: Non-fiction, Social Sciences, Criminology

Themes: Mental health, Criminal justice, Rehabilitation, Social stigma
