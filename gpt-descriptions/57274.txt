Synopsis: In 'My Queen: A Weekly Journal for Young Women. Issue 2, October 6, 1900: Marion Marlowe's Courage; or, A Brave Girl's Struggle for Life and Honor' by Sheldon, Lurana, readers are introduced to Marion Marlowe, a young woman who finds herself in a perilous situation that tests her courage and determination. As she navigates through a series of challenges, Marion's fight for life and honor becomes a captivating tale of bravery and resilience.

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Honor, Perseverance
