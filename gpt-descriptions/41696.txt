Synopsis: In 'The Feasts of Autolycus: The Diary of a Greedy Woman', we are introduced to a captivating and unapologetically greedy woman who takes us on a mouthwatering journey through the culinary delights of Europe. From the bustling markets of Paris to the charming cafes of Rome, she indulges in decadent feasts, tantalizing her taste buds and satisfying her insatiable appetite. But as she explores the world of gastronomy, she also discovers the deeper connections between food, culture, and personal fulfillment. With vivid descriptions and a delightful sense of humor, this diary invites readers to savor every page and reflect on their own relationship with food.

Genres: Memoir, Food Writing, Travelogue

Themes: Greed, Culinary Exploration, Self-Discovery
