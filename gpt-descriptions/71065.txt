Synopsis: In "The Hyena" by Robert E. Howard, a mysterious and enigmatic figure known as the Hyena emerges from the shadows, wreaking havoc and leaving a trail of bloodshed in his wake. As the authorities struggle to apprehend this elusive and ruthless criminal, a determined detective becomes obsessed with bringing him to justice. However, the Hyena's true identity and motives remain shrouded in darkness, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful cat-and-mouse chase that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Identity, Obsession, Justice
