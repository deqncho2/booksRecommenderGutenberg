Synopsis: 

In the enchanting town of Buxton, a hidden secret lies within its medicinal waters. As the protagonist delves into the history and mysteries surrounding this unique phenomenon, they uncover a web of intrigue, romance, and unexpected connections. From the captivating tales of those who have been healed by the waters to the dark forces that seek to exploit their power, Buxton becomes a place where past and present collide in a thrilling and unpredictable way. With each revelation, the protagonist's journey becomes more perilous, leading them to question their own beliefs and confront the true nature of the town's miraculous waters.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Healing, Secrets, Fate, Power
