Synopsis: In "The Automobile Girls in the Berkshires; Or, The Ghost of Lost Man's Trail," the adventurous and resourceful Automobile Girls embark on a thrilling journey to the picturesque Berkshires. As they explore the scenic trails and encounter mysterious happenings, they find themselves entangled in a captivating mystery that will test their courage and detective skills. With their trusty automobiles as their companions, the girls must unravel the secrets of Lost Man's Trail and confront the ghostly presence that haunts the area. Will they be able to solve the mystery and bring peace to the Berkshires?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration, Mystery-solving
