Synopsis: In 'The Next Logical Step' by Ben Bova, a brilliant scientist named Sam Gunn embarks on a daring mission to explore the mysteries of the universe. With his unconventional methods and unyielding determination, Sam faces numerous challenges and unexpected encounters that push the boundaries of human knowledge and test the limits of his own capabilities. As he ventures further into the unknown, Sam must confront the consequences of his actions and make difficult choices that could alter the course of humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Human Potential, Ethical Dilemmas
