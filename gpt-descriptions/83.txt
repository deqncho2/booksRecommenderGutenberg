Synopsis: 
'From the Earth to the Moon; and, Round the Moon' by Jules Verne takes readers on an exhilarating journey as a group of ambitious individuals set out to accomplish an extraordinary feat: to travel to the moon. Filled with scientific ingenuity, daring adventures, and unexpected twists, this captivating tale explores the challenges and triumphs of mankind's quest to conquer the unknown depths of space.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Human Ingenuity, Ambition, Scientific Progress
