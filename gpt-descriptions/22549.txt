Synopsis: In 'Space Prison' by Tom Godwin, a group of humans find themselves trapped on an alien planet, forced to survive in a hostile environment. As they struggle to adapt and escape, they uncover the shocking truth about their captors and the fate that awaits them. With their lives hanging in the balance, they must find a way to outsmart their alien oppressors and reclaim their freedom.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Survival

Themes: Alien abduction, Survival instincts, Human resilience
