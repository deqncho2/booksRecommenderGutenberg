Synopsis: In the small town of Oakwood, a mysterious disappearance takes place at the renowned Arnold Hall. As the townspeople grapple with the unsettling event, a group of curious teenagers embarks on a thrilling investigation to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic vanishing. With each clue they uncover, they find themselves drawn deeper into a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected connections. As the stakes rise and tensions mount, the young sleuths must race against time to solve the mystery before it consumes them all.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and deception, Friendship and loyalty, Coming of age
