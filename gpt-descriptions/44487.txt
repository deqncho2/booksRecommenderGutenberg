Synopsis: 

In 'Captured at Tripoli: A Tale of Adventure' by Percy F. Westerman, young Tom Burnaby finds himself caught in a thrilling and perilous journey when his ship is attacked by pirates off the coast of Tripoli. Separated from his crew, Tom must navigate the treacherous world of the Barbary pirates, facing danger at every turn as he strives to survive and find a way back home. Along the way, he encounters unexpected allies, uncovers hidden secrets, and discovers the true meaning of courage and resilience.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Bravery, Coming of Age
