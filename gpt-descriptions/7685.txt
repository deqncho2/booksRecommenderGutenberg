Synopsis: 
In 'Lucretia - Volume 01', the reader is transported to the vibrant city of Rome during the 17th century. The story revolves around the enigmatic and captivating character of Lucretia, a young woman with a mysterious past and an undeniable allure. As she navigates the intricate web of Roman society, Lucretia becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power, love, and betrayal. With vivid descriptions of the opulent palaces, secret societies, and political intrigues, this gripping tale keeps the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Power, Love, Betrayal, Society, Secrets
