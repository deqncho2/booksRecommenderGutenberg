Synopsis: In "Adventures of a Telegraph Boy; or, "Number 91," we follow the thrilling journey of a young telegraph boy named Joe, who finds himself caught up in a web of mystery and danger. As he delivers messages across the bustling city, Joe stumbles upon a secret that could change his life forever. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Determination, Friendship, Overcoming adversity
