Synopsis: In 'The Cat of Bubastes: A Tale of Ancient Egypt', young prince Amuba finds himself captured by the Egyptians and forced into slavery. As he navigates the treacherous world of ancient Egypt, he befriends a high priest's son and together they embark on a thrilling adventure to uncover a hidden secret that could change the course of history. Filled with political intrigue, ancient rituals, and daring escapades, this captivating tale transports readers to the fascinating world of ancient Egypt.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Cultural Identity, Coming of Age
