Synopsis: In "The Vertigo Hook" by Richard Ashby, a renowned architect finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he receives a cryptic message from an old friend. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he discovers a hidden world of secrets, betrayal, and a sinister plot that threatens to shatter his reality. With his life hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, where nothing is as it seems. Will he uncover the truth before it's too late, or will he become another victim of the vertigo hook?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Perception
