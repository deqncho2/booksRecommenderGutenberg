Synopsis: 
In the remote and mysterious Poison Island, a group of adventurers embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets hidden within its treacherous shores. As they navigate through dense jungles, encounter dangerous creatures, and face unexpected challenges, they soon realize that the island holds more than just physical dangers. With each step, they are drawn deeper into a web of intrigue and deception, where trust becomes a scarce commodity. Will they be able to unravel the island's mysteries and escape with their lives?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Betrayal, Secrets
