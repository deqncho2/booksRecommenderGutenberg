Synopsis: In 'The Fairy Godmother-in-law', Oliver Herford weaves a delightful tale of a young woman who unexpectedly finds herself with a rather unconventional fairy godmother. As their relationship unfolds, the protagonist is taken on a whimsical journey filled with magic, humor, and unexpected twists. With the help of her newfound ally, she navigates the challenges of love, self-discovery, and the pursuit of happiness. This enchanting story will captivate readers with its charm and leave them eagerly turning the pages to uncover the delightful surprises that lie ahead.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Magic, Transformation, Love and Relationships
