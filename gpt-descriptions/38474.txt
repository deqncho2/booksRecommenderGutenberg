Synopsis: In 'Loaded Dice' by Clark, Ellery H., a high-stakes game of chance takes an unexpected turn when a mysterious stranger enters the scene. As the tension rises and the stakes get higher, the players find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and danger. With each roll of the dice, the characters' true motivations are revealed, leading to a thrilling climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Trust, Gambling
