Synopsis: In "A Narrative of the Death of Captain James Cook," David Samwell recounts the gripping and tragic events surrounding the demise of the renowned explorer. Through his firsthand account as a ship's surgeon aboard the HMS Resolution, Samwell takes readers on a harrowing journey filled with perilous encounters, cultural clashes, and the ultimate tragedy that befalls Captain Cook. As the narrative unfolds, the reader becomes immersed in the treacherous world of exploration, witnessing the complexities of colonial encounters and the devastating consequences they can bring.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Biography

Themes: Exploration, Colonialism, Cultural clashes, Tragedy
