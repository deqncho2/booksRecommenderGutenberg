Synopsis: 
In the small town of Willowbrook, high school student Emma discovers a hidden talent for spelling that sets her on a path she never expected. As she navigates the pressures of school, friendships, and family, Emma finds herself drawn into the competitive world of spelling bees. With the support of her quirky group of friends, she must overcome personal obstacles and face unexpected challenges to prove that she has what it takes to become the ultimate speller.

Genres: Young Adult, Coming-of-Age, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Self-discovery, Competition
