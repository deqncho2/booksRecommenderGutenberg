Synopsis:
In "Malvina of Brittany" by Jerome K. Jerome, a young woman named Malvina finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love in medieval Brittany. As she navigates the treacherous world of courtly politics and battles against societal expectations, Malvina must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and the fate of her beloved homeland. With its captivating blend of romance, adventure, and suspense, this historical novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through a tumultuous era.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes:
Love and Sacrifice, Power and Politics, Identity and Independence
