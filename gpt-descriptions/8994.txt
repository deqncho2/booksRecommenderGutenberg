Synopsis: 
"What Katy Did" is a heartwarming coming-of-age story that follows the adventures of Katy Carr, a spirited and imaginative young girl. Despite her mischievous nature, Katy's life takes an unexpected turn that challenges her resilience and forces her to discover the true meaning of strength and perseverance. Through a series of trials and triumphs, Katy embarks on a journey of self-discovery, learning valuable life lessons along the way.

Genres: 
Children's literature, Fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: 
Resilience, Self-discovery, Perseverance
