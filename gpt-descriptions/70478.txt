Synopsis: Randolph Carter, a dreamer and seeker of forgotten knowledge, discovers a mysterious silver key that unlocks a hidden realm beyond the boundaries of time and space. As he embarks on a perilous journey through strange dimensions, Carter must confront his deepest fears and face the enigmatic beings that dwell in the realms of the unknown. With each turn of the key, the line between reality and fantasy blurs, leading Carter to question the nature of existence itself.

Genres: Horror, Fantasy, Science Fiction

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Identity and self-discovery, The limits of human perception, The fragility of reality
