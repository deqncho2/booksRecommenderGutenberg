Synopsis: 
In 'Rest Harrow: A Comedy of Resolution' by Maurice Hewlett, a young man named Richard Calmady finds himself torn between his love for a beautiful woman and his duty to his family. As he navigates the complexities of his relationships, he is faced with unexpected challenges and must make difficult choices that will shape his future. Set against the backdrop of the English countryside, this captivating tale explores the themes of love, duty, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: 
Romance, Drama, Comedy

Themes: 
Love, Duty, Family, Choices
