Synopsis: 
In 'Double Harness' by Anthony Hope, a young woman named Joan is determined to marry for love, not convenience or social status. However, her plans take an unexpected turn when she meets a charming and enigmatic man who seems to hold the key to her heart's desires. As their relationship develops, Joan finds herself torn between her own desires and the expectations of society. With secrets, intrigue, and a touch of scandal, 'Double Harness' is a captivating tale of love, ambition, and the complexities of navigating societal expectations.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Love and marriage, societal expectations, personal ambition
