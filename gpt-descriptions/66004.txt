Synopsis: 

'Marie Corelli: The Writer and the Woman' delves into the fascinating life of Marie Corelli, a renowned British novelist of the late 19th and early 20th centuries. This captivating biography explores the enigmatic persona of Corelli, unveiling the secrets behind her immense success and the controversies that surrounded her. From her humble beginnings to her rise as one of the most popular authors of her time, the book uncovers the trials and triumphs that shaped Corelli's career and personal life. With a blend of meticulous research and engaging storytelling, the authors shed light on the complex woman behind the pen, leaving readers eager to discover the untold stories that lie within the pages of Corelli's life.

Genres: Biography, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Literary success, Controversy, Personal struggles
