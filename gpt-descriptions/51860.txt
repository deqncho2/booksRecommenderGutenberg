Synopsis: 
'A Book of the Riviera' takes readers on a captivating journey along the stunning French Riviera, exploring its picturesque landscapes, charming towns, and fascinating history. From the glamorous city of Nice to the quaint village of Menton, the author delves into the region's rich cultural heritage, uncovering hidden gems and sharing intriguing anecdotes. With vivid descriptions and engaging storytelling, this book offers a delightful and immersive experience of the Riviera's allure, making it a must-read for travel enthusiasts and history buffs alike.

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Cultural heritage, Exploration, Beauty of nature
