Synopsis: In 'The Greatest Plague of Life: or, the Adventures of a Lady in Search of a Good Servant,' the reader is taken on a humorous and insightful journey as a lady navigates the challenges of finding a reliable domestic servant. From encounters with eccentric characters to unexpected twists and turns, this book offers a delightful exploration of the trials and tribulations of domestic life.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Satire

Themes: Domesticity, Social class, Human nature
