Synopsis: 

In "Scaramouche: A Romance of the French Revolution," Rafael Sabatini takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous era of the French Revolution. The story follows the life of André-Louis Moreau, a young lawyer who finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, love, and revenge. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of revolutionary France, André-Louis must assume various identities, including that of the charismatic and enigmatic Scaramouche, a renowned actor and master swordsman. With his quick wit and cunning, André-Louis becomes a symbol of hope for the oppressed, challenging the corrupt aristocracy and fighting for justice. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Revolution, Identity, Love, Revenge
