Synopsis: In "My Lady Clancarty," Mary Imlay Taylor presents the captivating true story of the Earl of Clancarty and Lady Elizabeth Spencer. Set in 17th century England, this historical romance follows the forbidden love between a nobleman and a spirited young woman. As their passionate affair unfolds, they must navigate the treacherous waters of society's expectations and face the consequences of their actions. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling characters, this tale of love and sacrifice will keep readers enthralled until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Forbidden Love, Society's Expectations, Sacrifice
