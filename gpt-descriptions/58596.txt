Synopsis: 
'Character Sketches of Romance, Fiction, and the Drama, Vol. 2: A Revised American Edition of the Reader's Handbook' by Brewer, Ebenezer Cobham is a captivating collection of character sketches from various literary works. From the enigmatic heroes and heroines to the cunning villains and sidekicks, this volume delves into the depths of their personalities, motivations, and relationships. Each sketch provides a glimpse into the intricate web of emotions and actions that shape these unforgettable characters, leaving readers eager to explore the original works they come from.

Genres: Literary Criticism, Reference, Non-fiction

Themes: Character analysis, Literature appreciation
