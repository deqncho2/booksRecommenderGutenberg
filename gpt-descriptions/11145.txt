Synopsis: 
In "The Discovery of Yellowstone Park: Journal of the Washburn Expedition to the Yellowstone and Firehole Rivers in the Year 1870," Nathaniel Pitt Langford takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the uncharted wilderness of Yellowstone. Join Langford and the Washburn Expedition as they navigate treacherous terrain, encounter breathtaking natural wonders, and face unexpected dangers. Through vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Langford brings to life the awe-inspiring beauty and untamed wilderness of Yellowstone, forever changing our understanding of the American West.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Natural Beauty, Preservation
