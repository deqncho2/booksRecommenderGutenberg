Synopsis: 
In 'Phyllis: A Twin' by Dorothy Whitehill, the story revolves around Phyllis, a young woman who discovers she has a twin sister she never knew existed. As Phyllis delves into her newfound family history, she uncovers a web of secrets and mysteries that threaten to unravel her own identity. With each revelation, Phyllis becomes entangled in a dangerous game of deception and betrayal, where the truth may be more elusive than she ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Family secrets, Deception
