Synopsis: In 'The Redemption of Freetown' by Sheldon, Charles M., a small coastal town is plagued by a dark secret that has haunted its residents for generations. When a mysterious stranger arrives, he becomes determined to uncover the truth and bring justice to the town. As he delves deeper into the town's history, he uncovers a web of lies, betrayal, and redemption that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Justice and redemption, the power of forgiveness
