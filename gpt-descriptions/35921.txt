Synopsis: In the aftermath of a devastating cyclone that strikes Louisville, Kentucky in 1890, the city is left in ruins and its residents must grapple with the overwhelming destruction and loss. As the community comes together to rebuild their lives and their city, they face numerous challenges and discover unexpected sources of strength and resilience. Amidst the chaos and despair, stories of hope, courage, and the indomitable human spirit emerge, painting a vivid picture of a city determined to rise from the ashes.

Genres: Historical fiction, Natural disaster, Drama

Themes: Resilience, Community, Rebuilding, Loss and grief, Human spirit
