Synopsis: In Camp with L Company, Second Regiment New Jersey Volunteer Infantry takes readers on an immersive journey into the life of a soldier during the American Civil War. Through the eyes of George W. Petty, a member of the Second Regiment, this captivating memoir provides a firsthand account of the daily struggles, camaraderie, and moments of bravery experienced by the soldiers on the front lines. From the intense battles to the mundane routines of camp life, Petty's vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes offer a unique perspective on the realities of war.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: War and its impact on individuals, Brotherhood and camaraderie in the face of adversity, The human experience in times of conflict
