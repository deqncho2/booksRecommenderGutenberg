Synopsis: 
In 'A Text-Book of Precious Stones for Jewelers and the Gem-Loving Public', Frank Bertram Wade takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of precious stones. From the mesmerizing allure of diamonds to the vibrant hues of emeralds and sapphires, Wade delves into the fascinating history, properties, and characteristics of each gemstone. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, this book serves as a comprehensive guide for both jewelers and gem enthusiasts, offering valuable insights into the art of gemstone identification, cutting, and setting. Whether you are a professional jeweler or simply a lover of all things sparkly, Wade's text-book is sure to captivate and inspire.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Gemology

Themes: Gemstones, Jewelry, History, Artistry
