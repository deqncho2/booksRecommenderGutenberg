Synopsis: In 'The Pawns Count' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious and enigmatic woman named Madame Koluchy arrives in London, capturing the attention of both the British intelligence and criminal underworld. As her true identity and motives remain shrouded in secrecy, a thrilling game of cat and mouse ensues, with unexpected alliances and dangerous betrayals. With high stakes and a web of intrigue, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: Deception, Power struggles, Identity
