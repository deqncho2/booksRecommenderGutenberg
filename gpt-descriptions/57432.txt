Synopsis: 
Join Mattie Crawford on an extraordinary journey as she embarks on a daring adventure through the heart of Central America, spreading the message of the Gospel. Traveling on the back of a mule, she encounters breathtaking landscapes, treacherous obstacles, and diverse cultures, all while facing personal challenges and discovering the true power of faith. As she navigates through the region's vibrant cities, dense jungles, and remote villages, Mattie's unwavering determination and unwavering belief in the transformative power of the Gospel will inspire readers to reflect on their own spiritual journey.

Genres: Adventure, Memoir, Religious

Themes: Faith, Exploration, Cultural diversity
