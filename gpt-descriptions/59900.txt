Synopsis: In 'The Mud Larks' by Garstin, Crosbie, a group of young orphans find themselves embarking on a thrilling adventure in the treacherous world of Victorian London. As they navigate the grimy streets and encounter a cast of colorful characters, they uncover a hidden secret that could change their lives forever. With danger lurking at every corner, the orphans must rely on their wit, courage, and the bonds of friendship to survive and unravel the mystery that lies before them.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Survival, Secrets, Coming of Age
