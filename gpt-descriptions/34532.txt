Synopsis: 

In 'A Synopsis of the American Bats of the Genus Pipistrellus', Hall and Dalquest delve into the fascinating world of these elusive creatures, providing a comprehensive overview of their taxonomy, distribution, and behavior. From the tiny Pipistrellus bats found in the United States to their counterparts across the Americas, this synopsis offers a captivating exploration of their unique characteristics and ecological significance. With detailed descriptions and stunning illustrations, the authors shed light on the mysterious lives of these nocturnal creatures, leaving readers intrigued by the diversity and complexity of the Pipistrellus genus.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Zoology

Themes: Biodiversity, Animal Behavior, Conservation
