Synopsis: 
In "Seventeen Years in the Underworld" by Scott Wellington, a young man named Ethan finds himself thrust into the dangerous and mysterious world of organized crime. After a chance encounter with a notorious gang leader, Ethan becomes entangled in a web of deceit, violence, and betrayal. As he navigates the treacherous underworld, Ethan must confront his own morality and make choices that will determine his fate. With each passing year, the stakes grow higher, and Ethan's journey becomes a thrilling and heart-pounding race against time.

Genres: Crime fiction, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Betrayal, Redemption
