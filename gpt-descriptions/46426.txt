Synopsis:
In "Ruskin Relics" by W. G. Collingwood, a young artist named Alan embarks on a journey to uncover the hidden secrets of a mysterious collection of relics left behind by the renowned art critic John Ruskin. As Alan delves deeper into the relics, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, encountering unexpected allies and dangerous adversaries along the way. With each relic he uncovers, Alan's perception of art, beauty, and the human condition is challenged, leading him to question the very essence of his own artistic identity. As he races against time to unlock the truth behind Ruskin's relics, Alan must confront his own demons and make a choice that will forever change his life.

Genres:
Mystery, Historical Fiction, Art

Themes:
Artistic identity, Beauty, Truth, Legacy
