Synopsis: 

'Automobile Biographies' takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and achievements of the brilliant minds behind the invention and development of self-propelled vehicles. From the early pioneers to the modern innovators, this book delves into the fascinating stories of those who have shaped the automobile industry as we know it today. With a blend of historical accounts and personal anecdotes, Lyman Horace Weeks paints a vivid picture of the challenges, triumphs, and groundbreaking discoveries that have revolutionized transportation. Prepare to be inspired by the remarkable individuals who have left an indelible mark on the common roads.

Genres: Biography, History, Technology

Themes: Innovation, Persistence, Impact of Invention
