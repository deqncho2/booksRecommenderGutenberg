Synopsis: In the enchanting city of Rome, four young artists embark on a journey that will forever change their lives. Miriam, Hilda, Kenyon, and Donatello find themselves entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when they encounter a mysterious figure known as the Faun. As their paths intertwine, secrets are revealed, passions are ignited, and the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur. Set against the backdrop of ancient ruins and hidden treasures, 'The Marble Faun' is a captivating tale of love, guilt, and the pursuit of identity.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Sin and guilt, Art and creativity
