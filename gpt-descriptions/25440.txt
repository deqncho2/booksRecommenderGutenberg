Synopsis: In 'An Undivided Union', two young friends find themselves caught up in the midst of the American Civil War. As they navigate the dangers and challenges of war, they must confront their own beliefs and loyalties, testing the strength of their friendship. With thrilling adventures and unexpected twists, this story takes readers on a journey through a divided nation, where courage and sacrifice become the ultimate test of character.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Identity, War, Courage
