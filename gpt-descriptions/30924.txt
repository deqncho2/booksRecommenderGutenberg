Synopsis: In 'Thirty-Seven Days of Peril: from Scribner's Monthly Vol III Nov. 1871', Truman Everts recounts his harrowing journey through the treacherous wilderness of Yellowstone National Park. After becoming separated from his expedition, Everts faces a series of life-threatening challenges, including starvation, freezing temperatures, and encounters with dangerous wildlife. As he battles against the elements and his own dwindling hope, Everts must summon every ounce of courage and resourcefulness to survive. This gripping tale of survival and resilience will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of Truman Everts.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Survival

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Man vs. Nature
