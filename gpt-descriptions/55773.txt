Synopsis: In "Mrs. Maybrick's Own Story: My Fifteen Lost Years," Florence Elizabeth Maybrick takes us on a captivating journey through her tumultuous life. From her idyllic marriage to her husband, James, to the shocking events that led to her imprisonment, Florence's gripping narrative unveils a web of secrets, betrayal, and injustice. As she fights to reclaim her freedom and clear her name, the truth behind her fifteen lost years slowly unravels, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Autobiography, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and betrayal, Justice and injustice, Women's rights, Redemption
