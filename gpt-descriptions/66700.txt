Synopsis: In 'A Cigarette Clew; Or, "Salted" For a Million' by Carter, Nicholas (House name), a mysterious cigarette leads a group of unlikely companions on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, deception, and unexpected twists. As they follow the clues left behind, they uncover a web of secrets that could change their lives forever. With each step closer to the truth, the stakes get higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. Will they solve the puzzle before it's too late, or will they become pawns in a much larger game?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Deception, Loyalty, Redemption
