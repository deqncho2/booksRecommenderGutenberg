Synopsis: In 'One Day At Arle' by Frances Hodgson Burnett, a young girl named Lesley finds herself transported to a picturesque French village where she embarks on a journey of self-discovery. As Lesley explores the enchanting landscapes and encounters a cast of colorful characters, she learns valuable lessons about friendship, love, and the power of embracing one's true self. With each passing day, Lesley's perspective on life transforms, leading her to uncover hidden secrets and unexpected connections that will forever change her world.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Love, Identity
