Synopsis: 
'Amid the High Hills' by Hugh Fraser, Sir, takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rugged and mysterious Scottish Highlands. When a renowned archaeologist discovers an ancient artifact that holds the key to a long-lost treasure, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of deception, betrayal, and hidden agendas. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the high hills, he must navigate treacherous landscapes and face unexpected adversaries, all while racing against time to uncover the truth before it falls into the wrong hands. This gripping tale of adventure and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Treasure hunting, Scottish folklore, Betrayal, Secrets
