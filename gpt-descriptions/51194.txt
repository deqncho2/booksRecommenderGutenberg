Synopsis: In 'Made to Measure' by William Campbell Gault, a renowned detective is faced with a perplexing case when a wealthy businessman is found dead in his luxurious mansion. As the detective delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives that lead him down a dangerous path. With each clue he uncovers, the detective realizes that nothing is as it seems, and the truth may be more elusive than he ever imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Power dynamics
