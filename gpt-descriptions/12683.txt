Synopsis: Christine, a young woman with a mysterious past, finds herself in a secluded castle in Germany. As she navigates the complex relationships and secrets of the castle's inhabitants, she becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and hidden desires. With each twist and turn, Christine's true identity and the dark secrets of the castle are slowly revealed, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers breathless.

Genres: Gothic fiction, mystery, romance

Themes: Identity, secrets, love and betrayal
