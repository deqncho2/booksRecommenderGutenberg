Synopsis: In 'Don't Shoot' by Robert Zacks, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of corruption and deceit when he is assigned to investigate a high-profile murder case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel the very fabric of society. With his own life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and bring justice to those who deserve it.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Corruption, Justice, Betrayal
