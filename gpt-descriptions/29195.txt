Synopsis: In "It's All Yours" by Sam Merwin, a mysterious inheritance leads a struggling artist on a thrilling journey through time and space. When a peculiar antique shop owner bequeaths him an extraordinary object, the artist discovers its power to transport him to different eras and dimensions. As he navigates through perilous adventures and encounters enigmatic characters, he must confront his own fears and desires. With each leap, the artist unravels the secrets of the object and the true nature of his inheritance, leading to a mind-bending revelation that will forever change his perception of reality.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Identity, Perception of reality
