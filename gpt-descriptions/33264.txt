Synopsis: 
In 'Living Up to Billy' by Elizabeth Cooper, a small town is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger named Billy arrives. As the townspeople become infatuated with Billy's charm and charisma, they soon discover that he has a dark secret that threatens to unravel their lives. Told through the eyes of multiple characters, this gripping tale explores the lengths people will go to in order to fit in and the consequences of living a life built on lies.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Identity, Deception, Conformity, Betrayal
