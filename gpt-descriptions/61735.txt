Synopsis: In "Uncle Wiggily Goes Swimming; Or, How the Frog Boys Surprised the Fox," join the lovable Uncle Wiggily Longears on a delightful adventure as he embarks on a swimming expedition with his animal friends. However, things take an unexpected turn when the mischievous Frog Boys devise a clever plan to outsmart the cunning Fox. Filled with humor, excitement, and unexpected twists, this charming tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Friendship, Resourcefulness, Overcoming challenges
