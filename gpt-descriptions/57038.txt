Synopsis: In 'The Monomaniac (La bête humaine)' by Émile Zola, we follow the gripping story of a railway worker named Jacques Lantier, who harbors a dark secret that threatens to consume him. As he navigates the treacherous world of trains and their passengers, Lantier becomes entangled in a web of desire, obsession, and violence. With each passing chapter, the tension builds, and the reader is left wondering how far Lantier's monomania will take him and what consequences it will have for those around him.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Crime fiction, Naturalism

Themes: Obsession, Fate and determinism, Violence, Human nature
