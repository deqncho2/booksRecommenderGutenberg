Synopsis: In 'Succession in the Presidency of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints', B.H. Roberts delves into the intriguing and often controversial topic of leadership succession within the LDS Church. Through meticulous research and insightful analysis, Roberts explores the historical events, complex dynamics, and personal struggles that have shaped the process of choosing a new president. As he uncovers the intricate web of power, influence, and spiritual guidance, readers will be captivated by the high-stakes drama and the profound impact it has on the faith and future of the Church.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Religious Studies

Themes: Leadership, Succession, Faith, Power, Influence
