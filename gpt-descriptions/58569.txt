Synopsis: 
Beyond the Horizon is a captivating play by Eugene O'Neill that delves into the lives of two brothers, Robert and Andrew Mayo, as they navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness. Set against the backdrop of a rural New England farm, the story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, exploring the sacrifices one must make in order to follow their dreams. As the brothers face difficult choices and confront their own desires, the play takes the audience on an emotional journey filled with love, loss, and the eternal search for fulfillment.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Love, Ambition, Sacrifice, Fulfillment
