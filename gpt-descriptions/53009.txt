Synopsis: In 'Patience Sparhawk and Her Times: A Novel' by Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton, readers are transported to the vibrant world of 19th-century San Francisco. Patience Sparhawk, a young and ambitious woman, finds herself navigating the complexities of love, power, and societal expectations in a rapidly changing city. As she strives to carve her own path, Patience becomes entangled in a web of secrets and scandals that threaten to unravel everything she holds dear. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the highs and lows of Patience's life, revealing the hidden truths that lie beneath the surface of society.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Ambition, Love and Relationships, Social Expectations, Power Dynamics
