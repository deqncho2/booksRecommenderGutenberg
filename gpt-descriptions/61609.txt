Synopsis: In 'The Centaurians: a novel' by Biagi, a group of explorers embarks on a thrilling journey to the mysterious planet Centauri. As they navigate through uncharted territories and encounter strange beings, they are faced with a series of mind-bending challenges that test their courage and resilience. Amidst the breathtaking landscapes and captivating adventures, the explorers also grapple with their own inner demons and desires, leading to unexpected twists and turns. This gripping tale of exploration, discovery, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Human Nature
