Synopsis: In 'The Engadine' by Musson, Spencer C., a mysterious murder takes place in the picturesque Engadine Valley, setting off a chain of events that will unravel the secrets of a tight-knit community. As Detective Inspector John Sinclair delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of deceit, hidden motives, and unexpected alliances. With the stunning Swiss Alps as a backdrop, Sinclair must navigate through a labyrinth of lies to uncover the truth before more lives are lost.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town secrets
