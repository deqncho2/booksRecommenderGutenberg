Synopsis: In 'Beyond the Ultra-Violet' by Frank M. Robinson, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything we know about the universe. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the cosmos, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and conspiracies that could alter the course of humanity forever. With mind-bending twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping sci-fi thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Scientific exploration, Conspiracy, Human potential
