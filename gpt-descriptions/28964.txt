Synopsis: 
In "Crumps", The Plain Story of a Canadian Who Went, we follow the captivating journey of a young Canadian man who embarks on a life-altering adventure. From the bustling streets of Montreal to the battlefields of Europe during World War I, the protagonist's experiences are both heart-wrenching and inspiring. As he navigates the horrors of war and the complexities of love, readers will be drawn into a tale that explores the resilience of the human spirit and the profound impact of one person's choices.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Resilience, Love, Identity, Sacrifice
