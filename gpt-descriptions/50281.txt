Synopsis: In "Diego Pinzon and the Fearful Voyage He Took Into the Unknown Ocean A.D. 1492" by Coryell, John Russell, embark on a thrilling historical adventure that follows the courageous Diego Pinzon as he sets sail into uncharted waters. Faced with treacherous challenges and unknown dangers, Diego's journey becomes a test of his resilience, determination, and unwavering belief in the power of exploration. As he navigates through the vast ocean, encountering both friend and foe, Diego's voyage becomes a pivotal moment in history, forever changing the course of human exploration.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Courage, Discovery, Identity
