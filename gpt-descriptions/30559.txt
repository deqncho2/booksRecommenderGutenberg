Synopsis: 
In "Eighth Reader," a young girl named Lily discovers an old book that holds a mysterious power. As she delves into its pages, she is transported to different worlds and times, encountering extraordinary characters and facing unexpected challenges. With each adventure, Lily uncovers secrets about herself and the true nature of the book, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and the power of imagination.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Imagination, Power of books
