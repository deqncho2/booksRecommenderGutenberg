Synopsis: 

'Legends from River & Mountain' is a captivating collection of enchanting tales that transport readers to a world where mythical creatures and magical landscapes come to life. From the depths of the river to the peaks of the mountains, these legends weave together stories of love, bravery, and the enduring power of nature. Each tale is filled with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets hidden within. With vivid imagery and lyrical prose, Sylva and Strettell invite readers on a mesmerizing journey through a realm where legends are born and dreams take flight.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Nature, Love, Bravery, Mythology
