Synopsis: In 'The Flag-Raising' by Kate Douglas Smith Wiggin, a small New England town is thrown into turmoil when a mysterious stranger arrives with a secret mission. As the townspeople grapple with their own fears and prejudices, they must come together to face a common enemy and protect their way of life. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the power of community, the complexities of human nature, and the strength found in unity.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Community, Prejudice, Unity
