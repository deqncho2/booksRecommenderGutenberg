Synopsis: In Those Days: The Story of an Old Man takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of an elderly man named Judah Steinberg. From his humble beginnings in a small village to his experiences during World War II, Judah's story is one of resilience, love, and the enduring power of the human spirit. As he reflects on his past, the reader is transported to a world filled with heartache, joy, and the complexities of life. Through his vivid storytelling, Judah's tale becomes a testament to the strength of the human soul and the importance of cherishing every moment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Resilience, Love, Identity, Memory
