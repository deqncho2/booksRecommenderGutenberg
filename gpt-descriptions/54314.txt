Synopsis: In 'Bruges: A Sketch-Book' by Joseph Pike, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting city of Bruges. Through vivid descriptions and beautiful illustrations, the author takes us on a visual and literary exploration of the city's hidden corners, historic landmarks, and charming streets. From the bustling market squares to the tranquil canals, Pike's sketches and accompanying narratives transport readers to a place where time seems to stand still. Discover the secrets of Bruges, its rich history, and the unique atmosphere that makes it a true gem of Europe.

Genres: Travel, Art, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, History, Cultural Heritage
