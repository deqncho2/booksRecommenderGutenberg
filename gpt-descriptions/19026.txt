Synopsis: 
In 'The Boss of the Lazy Y' by Charles Alden Seltzer, a mysterious stranger arrives at the Lazy Y Ranch, bringing with him a dark past and a hidden agenda. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the ranch's owner, Jack Allen, finds himself caught in a web of deceit and danger. With the fate of the Lazy Y hanging in the balance, Jack must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will determine the future of the ranch and those he holds dear.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Redemption, Betrayal, Justice
