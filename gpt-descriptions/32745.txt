Synopsis: In 'The Unlearned' by Raymond F. Jones, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Jonathan Grant embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that could change the course of human evolution. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only his own life but the future of humanity itself. With time running out, Dr. Grant must navigate a treacherous web of conspiracy and betrayal to protect his discovery and ensure the survival of the human race.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Human evolution, Scientific ethics, Conspiracy
