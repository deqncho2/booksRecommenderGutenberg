Synopsis: 

In "Sandwich Glass: A Technical Book for Collectors" by Lenore Wheeler Williams, readers are taken on a fascinating journey into the world of Sandwich glass, a renowned type of glassware produced in the early 19th century. Through detailed descriptions and captivating illustrations, Williams delves into the intricate techniques and processes used in creating these exquisite pieces, shedding light on the craftsmanship and artistry behind each item. From the delicate patterns to the vibrant colors, this technical book offers a comprehensive exploration of Sandwich glass, making it a must-read for collectors and enthusiasts alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Art, History

Themes: Glassmaking, Craftsmanship, Collecting
