Synopsis: In the third volume of "History of the Reformation in Europe in the Time of Calvin," Merle d'Aubigné takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events of the Reformation. From the rise of Calvin and his influence in Geneva to the spread of Protestantism across Europe, this book delves into the political, religious, and social upheavals that shaped the era. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, d'Aubigné brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and controversies of this pivotal period in European history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Religious studies

Themes: Reformation, Religious conflict, Political upheaval, Intellectual revolution
