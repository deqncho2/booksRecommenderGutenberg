Synopsis: In 'The Men Who Wrought' by Cullum Ridgwell, a group of unlikely heroes embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious artifact that holds the power to change the course of history. As they navigate treacherous landscapes and encounter dangerous adversaries, they must confront their own inner demons and make sacrifices that will test the limits of their courage and loyalty. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Action, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Heroism, Friendship, Sacrifice
