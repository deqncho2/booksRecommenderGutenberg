Synopsis: In the fifteenth issue of 'The Great Round World and What Is Going On In It', readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the latest news and events from around the world. From the mysterious disappearance of a famous explorer to the discovery of a hidden treasure, this magazine for boys and girls is filled with captivating stories that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each turn of the page, young readers will uncover fascinating tales of adventure, intrigue, and discovery, making this issue a must-read for anyone seeking excitement and knowledge.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, Discovery
