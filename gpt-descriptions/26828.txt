Synopsis: In 'A Red Wallflower' by Susan Warner, a young woman named Lucy finds herself caught between societal expectations and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and family, Lucy must confront the consequences of her choices and discover her own path to happiness. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century England, this captivating novel explores the power of self-discovery and the courage it takes to defy convention.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Love and Relationships, Societal Expectations
