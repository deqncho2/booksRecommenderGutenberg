Synopsis: 

In 'Lancashire Humour' by Thomas Newbigging, readers are transported to the charming and witty world of Lancashire, where laughter is the order of the day. Through a collection of humorous anecdotes, jokes, and tales, Newbigging showcases the unique and delightful sense of humor that permeates the region. From hilarious encounters with eccentric characters to comical mishaps and misunderstandings, this book is a delightful exploration of the lighter side of life in Lancashire. 

Genres: Humor, Regional Literature

Themes: Comedy, Cultural Identity, Everyday Life
