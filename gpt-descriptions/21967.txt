Synopsis: 
'Athaliah: A Tragedy: Intended for Reading Only, Translated Into English Blank Verse, From Racine (A. Gombert's Edition, 1825)' by Jean Racine, translated by J. Donkersley, is a gripping tragedy that explores the dark and treacherous world of power, religion, and ambition. Set in ancient Israel, the story revolves around the ruthless Queen Athaliah, who stops at nothing to maintain her grip on the throne. As political intrigue and personal vendettas unfold, the fate of the kingdom hangs in the balance, leading to a climactic confrontation that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Power, Ambition, Religion, Betrayal
