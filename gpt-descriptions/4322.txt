Synopsis: 
In 'My Three Days in Gilead', Elmer U. Hoenshel takes readers on a captivating journey through a mysterious and enchanting town called Gilead. When the protagonist, a curious traveler, stumbles upon this hidden gem, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, strange occurrences, and unexpected encounters. As he delves deeper into the heart of Gilead, he uncovers a dark history and a community grappling with its own demons. With each passing day, the traveler's perception of reality becomes increasingly blurred, leaving him questioning his own sanity. Will he be able to unravel the enigma of Gilead before it consumes him?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Secrets, Community
