Synopsis: In "Wagner as I Knew Him," Ferdinand Praeger takes us on a captivating journey through his personal encounters with the legendary composer Richard Wagner. Praeger's vivid and intimate recollections provide a unique perspective on Wagner's life, his creative process, and the tumultuous world of 19th-century music. From their initial meeting in Dresden to their later encounters in Paris and London, Praeger unveils the complex and enigmatic personality of Wagner, shedding light on his genius, his controversies, and the profound impact he had on the world of opera. Through Praeger's engaging narrative, readers will gain a deeper understanding of the man behind the music and the lasting legacy he left behind.

Genres: Biography, Music, Memoir

Themes: Genius and creativity, Artistic influence, Personal relationships
