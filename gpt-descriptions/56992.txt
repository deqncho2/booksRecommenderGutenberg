Synopsis: In Stephen H. Branch's Alligator, Vol. 1 no. 14, July 24, 1858, readers are transported to the vibrant world of 19th-century America, where a captivating tale unfolds. Amidst the backdrop of political turmoil and social change, the protagonist embarks on a journey that will challenge their beliefs and ignite a desire for justice. As secrets unravel and alliances are tested, the reader is left eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover the truth that lies beneath the surface.

Genres: Historical fiction, political thriller, mystery

Themes: Power struggles, social justice, personal transformation
