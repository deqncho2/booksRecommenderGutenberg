Synopsis: In "The White Indian Boy: The Story of Uncle Nick Among the Shoshones," we are taken on a captivating journey through the life of Elijah Nicholas Wilson, a white boy who was adopted by the Shoshone tribe. From his early encounters with the Native Americans to his assimilation into their culture, Uncle Nick's story is filled with adventure, friendship, and a deep appreciation for the natural world. As he navigates the complexities of his dual identity, readers will be enthralled by the unique perspective he brings to both the white and Native American worlds.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Cultural Studies

Themes: Identity, Cultural assimilation, Friendship, Nature and spirituality
