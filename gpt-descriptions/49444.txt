Synopsis: 

In "Five Years in Texas: Or, What you did not hear during the war from January 1861 to January 1866," Thomas North takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous years of the American Civil War. Through his vivid narrative, North shares his personal experiences, travels, and observations during this pivotal period in history, shedding light on the untold stories and hidden perspectives that were often overlooked.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, memoir, travelogue

Themes: American Civil War, personal experiences, untold stories, hidden perspectives
