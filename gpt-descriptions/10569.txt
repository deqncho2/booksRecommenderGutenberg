Synopsis: In 'Skilled Assistance: Ship's Company, Part 9', the crew of the tramp steamer, the Astrid, find themselves in a perilous situation when they encounter a mysterious stowaway on board. As tensions rise and suspicions grow, the crew must navigate through treacherous waters and confront their own secrets to ensure their survival. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling installment of the Ship's Company series will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Survival
