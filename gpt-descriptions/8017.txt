Synopsis: 

In the seventeenth book of the King James version of the Bible, we are introduced to Esther, a young and beautiful Jewish woman who finds herself thrust into a position of power and influence. Set in the Persian Empire during the reign of King Ahasuerus, Esther's story takes us on a captivating journey filled with political intrigue, danger, and unexpected twists. As she navigates the treacherous waters of palace life, Esther must make difficult choices that will not only impact her own fate but also the destiny of her people. With her courage and cunning, she becomes a symbol of hope and resilience, proving that even the most unlikely heroes can change the course of history.

Genres: Historical fiction, religious literature, drama

Themes: Courage, faith, identity, power, redemption
