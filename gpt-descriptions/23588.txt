Synopsis: In 'A Filbert Is a Nut' by Raphael, Rick, a seemingly ordinary man, embarks on a journey that takes him to unexpected places and introduces him to a cast of eccentric characters. As he navigates through a series of bizarre and humorous situations, Rick begins to question the nature of reality and his own existence. With each twist and turn, the story delves deeper into themes of identity, perception, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Surrealism

Themes: Identity, Perception, Imagination
