Synopsis: In 'The Profiteers' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges in the world of finance, manipulating the stock market and amassing a fortune overnight. As the authorities scramble to uncover the identity of this elusive mastermind, a young journalist finds herself entangled in a dangerous game of power and deception. With the stakes rising and the truth becoming increasingly elusive, she must navigate a treacherous world of greed and ambition to expose the profiteers and protect her own life.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Financial manipulation, Power and corruption
