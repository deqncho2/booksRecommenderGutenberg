Synopsis: In 'The Influence of Buddhism on Primitive Christianity', Lillie, Arthur delves into the fascinating connections between two ancient religions that have shaped the world as we know it. Through meticulous research and thought-provoking analysis, Lillie uncovers surprising parallels and shared philosophies between Buddhism and Christianity, shedding light on the potential cross-pollination of ideas that occurred during their early development. As the author explores the historical context and cultural exchange between these belief systems, readers are invited to embark on a captivating journey of discovery, challenging their preconceptions and deepening their understanding of the profound impact these religions have had on humanity.

Genres: Religious Studies, Comparative Religion, History

Themes: Religious syncretism, Cultural exchange, Spiritual evolution
