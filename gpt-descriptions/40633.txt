Synopsis: 

In "In Northern Mists: Arctic Exploration in Early Times," Fridtjof Nansen takes us on a captivating journey through the history of Arctic exploration. From the ancient myths and legends that inspired early explorers to the daring expeditions of the Vikings and the intrepid search for the Northwest Passage, Nansen delves into the fascinating tales of those who braved the treacherous Arctic landscapes. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, he paints a vivid picture of the challenges faced by these explorers and the profound impact their discoveries had on our understanding of the world. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Human resilience, Nature's power and beauty
