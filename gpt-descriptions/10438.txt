Synopsis: In 'Up the Hill and Over' by Isabel Ecclestone Mackay, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey that takes her from the bustling city to the tranquil countryside. As she navigates through unexpected challenges and encounters intriguing characters, Mary discovers the transformative power of self-discovery and the importance of embracing life's uncertainties. This captivating tale explores the complexities of human relationships, the pursuit of personal growth, and the beauty found in embracing the unknown.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Adventure

Themes: Self-discovery, Transformation, Embracing uncertainty
