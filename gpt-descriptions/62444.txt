Synopsis: In 'The Preservation of the Exterior of Wooden Buildings', Gardner and Cushman delve into the secrets of maintaining the timeless beauty and structural integrity of wooden buildings. Through a combination of historical research, practical advice, and expert insights, they uncover the hidden techniques used by craftsmen throughout the ages to protect these architectural treasures. From the importance of proper maintenance to the art of selecting the right materials, this book offers a comprehensive guide to preserving the exterior of wooden buildings, ensuring their longevity for generations to come.

Genres: Non-fiction, Architecture, Preservation

Themes: Historical preservation, Architectural craftsmanship, Building maintenance
