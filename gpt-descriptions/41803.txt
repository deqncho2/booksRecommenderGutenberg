Synopsis: In 'Joan of the Sword Hand' by S.R. Crockett, a young woman named Joan finds herself thrust into a world of danger and adventure when she discovers a hidden talent for sword fighting. As she navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters mysterious characters, Joan must uncover the truth about her past and embrace her destiny as a warrior. With thrilling action and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Courage, Fate
