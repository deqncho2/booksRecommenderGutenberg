Synopsis: 
In 'Home is Where You Left It' by Stephen Marlowe, a renowned archaeologist, Dr. Amelia Hartley, embarks on a thrilling expedition to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization. As she delves deeper into the mysteries of the past, she becomes entangled in a web of danger, betrayal, and unexpected romance. With each discovery, Amelia's world is turned upside down, forcing her to question everything she thought she knew about herself and the world around her. As the stakes rise, she must navigate treacherous landscapes and confront her own demons to unravel the truth that lies buried beneath the sands of time.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
