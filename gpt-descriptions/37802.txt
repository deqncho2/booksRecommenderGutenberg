Synopsis: In the second volume of "The Heritage of the Kurts," Bjørnstjerne Bjørnson continues the captivating saga of the Kurt family, delving deeper into their complex relationships and the challenges they face. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, passions ignite, and the characters are forced to confront their pasts and make difficult choices that will shape their futures. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this volume takes readers on an emotional journey filled with love, betrayal, and redemption.

Genres: Historical fiction, family drama, romance

Themes: Family legacy, identity, love and sacrifice
