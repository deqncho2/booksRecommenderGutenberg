Synopsis: In the midst of the decadent and tyrannical reign of Emperor Nero, a forbidden love story unfolds between a Roman military officer and a beautiful Christian woman. As their paths intertwine, they must navigate the treacherous political landscape, religious persecution, and their own personal struggles. With vivid descriptions of ancient Rome and its opulence, this gripping historical novel explores the clash between power and morality, love and duty, and the enduring strength of faith.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Religious persecution, Corruption and power, Faith and spirituality
