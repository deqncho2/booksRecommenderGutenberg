Synopsis: In 'Clocks' by Jerome K. Jerome, a seemingly ordinary man named John Biddle embarks on a journey that takes him through time and space, encountering peculiar characters and mind-bending situations. As he navigates this surreal world, John must unravel the mysteries of the clocks that seem to hold the key to his existence. With humor and wit, Jerome K. Jerome crafts a captivating tale that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Time, Identity, Perception
