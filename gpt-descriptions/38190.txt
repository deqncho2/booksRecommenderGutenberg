Synopsis:
In "Cornwall," Sabine Baring-Gould takes us on a captivating journey through the rugged landscapes and rich history of the enchanting county of Cornwall. From ancient legends and folklore to tales of smugglers and shipwrecks, this book delves into the fascinating stories that have shaped Cornwall's identity. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Baring-Gould paints a vivid picture of this unique region, capturing the essence of its people, traditions, and natural beauty.

Genres:
Travelogue, History, Folklore

Themes:
Identity, Tradition, Natural Beauty
