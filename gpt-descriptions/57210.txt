Synopsis: In 'The Substitute Millionaire', a young man named Jack is unexpectedly thrust into a world of wealth and luxury when he is mistaken for a millionaire. As he navigates this new lifestyle, Jack finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, romance, and danger. With his true identity at stake, Jack must unravel the secrets of the millionaire's life while trying to protect his own. Will he be able to maintain the charade or will the truth come crashing down around him?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Identity, Deception, Wealth and Power
