Synopsis: 
In 'Little Journeys to the Homes of the Great - Volume 11: Little Journeys to the Homes of Great Businessmen', Elbert Hubbard takes readers on a captivating journey into the lives and homes of some of the most influential businessmen in history. From the opulent mansions of Andrew Carnegie to the humble abode of Benjamin Franklin, Hubbard delves into the personal stories, struggles, and triumphs of these remarkable individuals. Through vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, he paints a vivid picture of the environments that shaped these great businessmen and offers valuable lessons on success, perseverance, and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Biography, History, Self-help

Themes: Success, Entrepreneurship, Personal development
