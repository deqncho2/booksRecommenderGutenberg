Synopsis: In 'Daily Training', a mysterious and enigmatic figure arrives in a small English village, captivating the attention of the locals with his peculiar daily routine. As the villagers become increasingly intrigued by his unconventional practices, they find themselves drawn into a world of unexpected revelations and hidden secrets. With each passing day, the boundaries between reality and illusion blur, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of truth and the power of perception.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Perception, Deception, Truth
