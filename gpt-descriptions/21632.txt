Synopsis: In 'Fame and Fortune; or, The Progress of Richard Hunter' by Horatio Alger, Jr., follow the captivating journey of Richard Hunter, a young man determined to rise above his humble beginnings and achieve success. From his small hometown to the bustling city, Richard faces numerous challenges and encounters a cast of intriguing characters who shape his path. Will he be able to overcome adversity and fulfill his dreams of fame and fortune?

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Rags-to-riches

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Social mobility
