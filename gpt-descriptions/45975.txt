Synopsis: In "The Little Lame Prince and His Travelling Cloak," a young prince finds himself confined to a wheelchair after a tragic accident. Despite his physical limitations, he embarks on a magical journey with the help of a special cloak that grants him the ability to travel through time and space. Along the way, he encounters enchanting creatures, faces unexpected challenges, and discovers the true power of courage and kindness. This heartwarming tale explores the transformative power of imagination and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Overcoming adversity, Imagination, Kindness, Resilience
