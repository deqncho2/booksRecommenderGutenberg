Synopsis: In 'Exile from Space' by Judith Merril, a group of astronauts embarks on a mission to explore a distant planet, only to find themselves stranded and cut off from Earth. As they struggle to survive in this alien environment, they uncover shocking secrets that challenge their understanding of humanity's place in the universe. With danger lurking at every turn, the astronauts must confront their own fears and make difficult choices that will determine their fate. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Identity, Morality
