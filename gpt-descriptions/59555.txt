Synopsis: 

In "Poison-ivy, Poison-oak and Poison Sumac: Identification, Precautions and Eradication," authors Crooks and Kephart delve into the fascinating world of these notorious plants that have plagued humanity for centuries. Through a comprehensive exploration of their identification, precautions, and eradication methods, the authors provide readers with essential knowledge to protect themselves and their surroundings from these toxic plants. With vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, this book serves as a valuable guide for outdoor enthusiasts, gardeners, and anyone seeking to navigate the wilderness safely. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, Nature

Themes: Plant identification, Outdoor safety, Environmental awareness
