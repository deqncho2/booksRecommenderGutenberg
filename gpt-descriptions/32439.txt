Synopsis: In 'The Doctor, his Wife, and the Clock' by Anna Katharine Green, a renowned doctor and his wife find themselves entangled in a mysterious case when a peculiar clock appears in their home. As they delve deeper into the origins and purpose of the clock, they uncover a web of secrets and danger that threatens their very lives. With each tick of the clock, the couple must race against time to unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and deception, Time and its significance
