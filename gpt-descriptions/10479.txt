Synopsis: 

In 'Our Churches and Chapels: Their Parsons, Priests, & Congregations: Being a Critical and Historical Account of Every Place of Worship in Preston' by Atticus, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the religious landscape of Preston. Atticus delves into the rich history and intricate details of each church and chapel, providing a critical and historical account that unveils the fascinating stories behind these sacred spaces. From the charismatic parsons to the devoted congregations, the author paints a vivid picture of the diverse religious community that has shaped the town. As the pages turn, readers will find themselves immersed in the captivating tales of faith, devotion, and the ever-evolving nature of religious practices in Preston.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Religious Studies

Themes: Religion, Community, History, Faith, Tradition
