Synopsis:
'The Wild Tribes of Davao District, Mindanao: The R. F. Cummings Philippine Expedition' takes readers on a captivating journey into the unexplored depths of the Davao District in Mindanao. Join anthropologist Fay-Cooper Cole as he recounts his thrilling encounters with the indigenous tribes that inhabit this remote region. From the fierce Bagobo warriors to the enigmatic Mandaya people, Cole's expedition unveils the rich cultural tapestry and untamed beauty of these wild tribes. As he delves deeper into their customs, rituals, and beliefs, Cole uncovers a world that is both fascinating and mysterious, challenging his own preconceptions and expanding his understanding of humanity. This enthralling account of adventure and discovery will leave readers yearning to explore the uncharted territories of the human spirit.

Genres: Anthropology, Adventure, Travelogue

Themes: Indigenous cultures, Exploration, Cultural diversity
