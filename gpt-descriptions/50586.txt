Synopsis: 
"Billy" Sunday, the Man and His Message: With his own words which have won thousands for Christ is a captivating biography that delves into the life and impact of one of America's most influential evangelists, Billy Sunday. Through the use of Sunday's own words, author William T. Ellis takes readers on a journey through Sunday's humble beginnings, his rise to fame as a professional baseball player, and his transformation into a powerful preacher who touched the lives of thousands. This gripping account explores the challenges and triumphs that shaped Sunday's life, revealing the unwavering dedication and passion that fueled his mission to bring people to Christ.

Genres: Biography, Christian Literature, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Faith and Redemption, Personal Transformation, Evangelism
