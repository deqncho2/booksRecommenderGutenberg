Synopsis: In 'Beyond' by John Galsworthy, a young woman named Daphne Wing falls in love with a man who seems to be the embodiment of her dreams. However, as their relationship unfolds, she discovers a dark secret that threatens to shatter her world. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century England, this gripping tale explores the complexities of love, desire, and the lengths one will go to protect their own happiness.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and betrayal, societal expectations, the pursuit of happiness
