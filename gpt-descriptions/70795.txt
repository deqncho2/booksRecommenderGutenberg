Synopsis: In 'A Forgotten Prince of Wales' by Curties, Henry, a captivating tale unfolds as a long-lost secret threatens to unravel the history of the British monarchy. Set against the backdrop of the early 20th century, this gripping historical mystery follows a determined historian who stumbles upon a hidden truth that could change the course of history. As she delves deeper into the enigma surrounding a forgotten prince, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, deception, and danger. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the race to uncover the truth becomes a thrilling journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Power, History
