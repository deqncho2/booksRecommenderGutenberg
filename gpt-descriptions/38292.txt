Synopsis: In 'A Witch of the Hills, v. 2 [of 2]' by Florence Warden, the mysterious and enchanting world of witchcraft continues to unfold as the protagonist, a young woman named Mavis, delves deeper into her newfound powers. As she navigates the treacherous path of love, betrayal, and self-discovery, Mavis must confront the dark forces that threaten to consume her and the ones she holds dear. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Witchcraft, Power, Love and Betrayal
