Synopsis: In "Egypt and Its Monuments," Hichens takes readers on a captivating journey through the ancient wonders of Egypt. From the majestic pyramids of Giza to the enigmatic Sphinx, he delves into the rich history and mythology surrounding these iconic monuments. As he explores the hidden secrets and legends of Egypt, Hichens paints a vivid picture of a civilization that continues to fascinate and mystify the world.

Genres: Travelogue, History, Archaeology

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Mythology, Cultural heritage
