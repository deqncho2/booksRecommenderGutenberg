Synopsis: In 'The Carved Cupboard' by Amy Le Feuvre, a young girl named Rosamund discovers a hidden secret within an old cupboard that takes her on a captivating journey of self-discovery and adventure. As she unravels the mysteries behind the carved cupboard, Rosamund encounters unexpected friendships, faces challenges, and learns valuable life lessons along the way. This enchanting tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the magical cupboard.

Genres: Children's Literature, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Courage, Mystery
