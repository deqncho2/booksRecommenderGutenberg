Synopsis: 
In 'Locomotive Engine Running and Management' by Sinclair, Angus, embark on a thrilling journey through the world of steam locomotives. Follow the protagonist as they navigate the challenges of operating these powerful machines, from mastering the intricate controls to troubleshooting unexpected issues. As they delve deeper into the inner workings of locomotives, they uncover a hidden secret that could change the course of their career forever. With danger lurking around every corner, the protagonist must use their expertise and quick thinking to overcome obstacles and save the day. 

Genres: 
Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Exploration of technology, Perseverance, Discovery
