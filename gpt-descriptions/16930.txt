Synopsis: In 'A Short History of Russia' by Mary Platt Parmele, embark on a captivating journey through the tumultuous and enigmatic history of Russia. From the rise of the medieval Rus' to the grandeur of the Romanov dynasty, this concise yet comprehensive account unveils the fascinating tales of power struggles, cultural shifts, and remarkable individuals that have shaped the nation. Delve into the mysteries of Ivan the Terrible, witness the sweeping reforms of Peter the Great, and experience the revolutionary fervor that ignited the Russian Revolution. With vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, Parmele paints a vivid picture of a nation that has both dazzled and confounded the world.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Power and politics, Cultural transformation, Revolution and upheaval
