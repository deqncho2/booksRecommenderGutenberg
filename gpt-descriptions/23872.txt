Synopsis: In 'The Professional Approach', Harness and Thomas take readers on a thrilling journey into the world of high-stakes corporate espionage. When a brilliant young analyst uncovers a dangerous conspiracy within his own company, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit, betrayal, and danger to expose the truth. As the stakes escalate and the danger intensifies, he must rely on his wits, skills, and a few unexpected allies to stay one step ahead of those who will stop at nothing to protect their secrets.

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Corporate Fiction

Themes: Corporate espionage, Betrayal, Deception, Power struggle
