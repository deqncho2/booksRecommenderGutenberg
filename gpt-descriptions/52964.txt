Synopsis: 
In 'Patty's Motor Car' by Wells, Carolyn, Patty Fairfield, a spirited and independent young woman, finds herself caught up in a thrilling adventure when she acquires her very own motor car. With her friends by her side, Patty embarks on a series of exhilarating escapades, encountering unexpected challenges and discovering the true meaning of friendship and courage. As she navigates the roads and encounters various characters, Patty's motor car becomes a symbol of freedom and empowerment, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Independence, Friendship, Self-discovery
