Synopsis: In "The House of Egremont," Molly Elliot Seawell takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of the aristocratic Egremont family. Set in the late 19th century, the novel explores the intricate dynamics of love, power, and secrets within the opulent walls of Egremont Manor. As the family's dark past resurfaces, the protagonist, Lady Mary Egremont, finds herself torn between loyalty to her family and her own desires. With scandalous affairs, hidden motives, and unexpected twists, this gripping tale unravels the mysteries that lie within the House of Egremont.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Family secrets, Love and betrayal, Social class, Power dynamics
