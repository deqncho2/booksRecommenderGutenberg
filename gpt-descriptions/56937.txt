Synopsis: 
'Christmas on the Briny, The Innocents Abroad: Or, A Holiday Trip to the Abrolhos Islands' takes readers on a captivating journey to the Abrolhos Islands during the Christmas season. Join the protagonist as they embark on an adventure filled with mystery, unexpected encounters, and the magic of the holiday spirit. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, friendships are formed, and the true meaning of Christmas is discovered. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Holiday

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Redemption, Christmas Spirit
