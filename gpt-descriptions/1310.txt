Synopsis: 
'The Annals of the Parish: Or, the Chronicle of Dalmailing During the Ministry of the Rev. Micah Balwhidder' is a captivating novel that takes readers on a journey through the life of a Scottish parish in the late 18th century. Written in the form of a diary, the book follows the experiences and observations of Reverend Micah Balwhidder as he navigates the challenges and triumphs of his ministry. From the joys and sorrows of his parishioners to the political and social changes sweeping through the country, Balwhidder's diary provides a unique and intimate perspective on the lives of the people he serves.

Genres: Historical fiction, Epistolary novel

Themes: Religion, Community, Social change
