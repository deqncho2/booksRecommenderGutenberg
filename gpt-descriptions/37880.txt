Synopsis: 
In the 11th edition of the renowned Encyclopaedia Britannica, Volume 12, Slice 2 takes readers on a captivating journey from "Gloss" to "Gordon, Charles George." This volume delves into a wide range of fascinating topics, from the intricacies of language and linguistics to the life and accomplishments of the influential British general, Charles George Gordon. With meticulous attention to detail and a wealth of knowledge, this edition offers a comprehensive exploration of diverse subjects, providing readers with a treasure trove of information and insights.

Genres: Reference, Non-fiction
