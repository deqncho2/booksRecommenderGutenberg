Synopsis: In the small town of Hixon, a mysterious stranger named Drag Harlan arrives, stirring up trouble and challenging the status quo. With his rugged charm and enigmatic past, Drag becomes a source of fascination and fear for the townspeople. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the town is forced to confront its own dark underbelly. With danger lurking around every corner, Drag Harlan's presence becomes a catalyst for unexpected and thrilling events that will forever change the lives of those in Hixon.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Justice
