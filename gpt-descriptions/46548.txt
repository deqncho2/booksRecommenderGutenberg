Synopsis: In 'Children of the Dear Cotswolds' by L. Allen Harker, a mysterious family secret is unearthed when two siblings stumble upon a hidden diary in their ancestral home. As they delve deeper into the past, they uncover a web of dark secrets, forbidden love, and a haunting presence that refuses to be forgotten. With each revelation, the siblings find themselves drawn into a world of supernatural forces and must confront their own fears to protect their family's legacy. This gripping tale of mystery and the supernatural will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Forbidden love, Supernatural forces
