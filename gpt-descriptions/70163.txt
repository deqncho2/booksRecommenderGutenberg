Synopsis: In 'Millbank; $b or, Roger Irving's ward', Mary Jane Holmes weaves a captivating tale of a young woman named Millbank who finds herself under the guardianship of the enigmatic Roger Irving. As Millbank navigates the complexities of her new life, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, romance, and unexpected twists. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century America, this novel explores the power dynamics between individuals from different social classes and the transformative journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Social class, Identity, Love and betrayal
