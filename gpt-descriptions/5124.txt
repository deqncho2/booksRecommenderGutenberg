Synopsis:
In "Henrietta's Wish; Or, Domineering" by Charlotte M. Yonge, readers are introduced to Henrietta, a young woman with a strong will and a desire for independence. However, her wish for freedom clashes with the domineering nature of those around her, leading to a series of unexpected events and challenges. As Henrietta navigates through societal expectations and personal desires, she must confront the consequences of her choices and find a way to reconcile her own happiness with the expectations of others.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Independence, Society's expectations, Personal growth
