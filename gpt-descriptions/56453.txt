Synopsis: In 'The Egregious English', a young Englishman named Edward embarks on a journey to a remote island in the South Pacific, where he encounters a mysterious tribe with a unique language and culture. As Edward immerses himself in their society, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden love, and a quest for self-discovery. With each passing day, he unravels the enigmatic history of the tribe and the profound impact it has on his own identity. This captivating tale explores the power of language, the complexities of cultural assimilation, and the transformative nature of human connection.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Language and communication, Cultural identity, Forbidden love, Self-discovery
