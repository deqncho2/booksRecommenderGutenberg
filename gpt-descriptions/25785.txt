Synopsis: In 'The Proud Prince' by Justin H. McCarthy, a young prince finds himself torn between duty and desire when he falls in love with a commoner. As he navigates the treacherous waters of court politics and societal expectations, he must make a choice that could change the course of his life and the kingdom forever. With secrets, betrayals, and forbidden love, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Power and Responsibility, Class and Society
