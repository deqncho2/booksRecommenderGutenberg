Synopsis: In 'The Mayor of Troy' by Arthur Quiller-Couch, a small coastal town is thrown into turmoil when a mysterious stranger arrives, claiming to be the rightful heir to the town's mayoralty. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of deception, loyalty, and betrayal. With its intriguing characters and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Power, Community
