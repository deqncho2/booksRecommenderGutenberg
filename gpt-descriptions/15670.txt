Synopsis: In 'The Secret Chamber at Chad' by Evelyn Everett-Green, a young girl named Maud finds herself drawn into a mysterious adventure when she discovers a hidden chamber in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the chamber, Maud uncovers a web of intrigue, ancient legends, and unexpected connections that will challenge her courage and change her life forever. With each revelation, the stakes grow higher, and Maud must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those she loves. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the secrets of the secret chamber.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Courage, Identity, Heritage
