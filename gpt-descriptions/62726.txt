Synopsis: In 'Rollo's Philosophy [Fire]' by Abbott, Jacob, young Rollo embarks on a captivating journey of discovery as he explores the fascinating world of fire. From the mesmerizing flames of a campfire to the destructive power of a wildfire, Rollo learns about the various forms and uses of fire, while also uncovering its potential dangers. Through engaging anecdotes and thought-provoking discussions, Rollo's adventures will ignite the reader's curiosity and leave them eager to learn more about this elemental force. 

Genres: Children's Literature, Educational, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Science, Safety
