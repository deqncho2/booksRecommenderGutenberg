Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Fyodor Dostoevsky' is a comprehensive collection of the renowned Russian author's works, showcasing his unparalleled talent for delving into the depths of the human psyche. From the gripping psychological exploration of crime and punishment to the intricate portrayal of love, redemption, and existentialism, this compilation offers a captivating journey through Dostoevsky's literary masterpieces. Whether it's the haunting tale of an ex-convict seeking salvation or the profound examination of moral dilemmas, each story in this collection immerses readers in a world of complex characters and thought-provoking narratives.

Genres: Fiction, Psychological Thriller, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Crime and Punishment, Redemption, Existentialism, Morality, Love and Relationships
