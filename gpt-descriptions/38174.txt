Synopsis: 
'Vidyāpati: Bangīya padābali; songs of the love of Rādhā and Krishna' by Vidyāpati Thākura is a mesmerizing collection of lyrical poems that transport readers to the enchanting world of divine love. Through the verses, the author beautifully weaves together the passionate and eternal love story of Rādhā and Krishna, exploring the depths of their emotions and the complexities of their relationship. Each poem is a delicate tapestry of emotions, evoking a range of feelings from longing and desire to devotion and surrender. As readers delve into the verses, they will be captivated by the ethereal beauty of the language and the profound insights into the nature of love and spirituality.

Genres: Poetry, Romance, Spiritual

Themes: Divine love, Longing, Devotion, Surrender
