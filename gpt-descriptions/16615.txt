Synopsis: 
'By Still Waters: Lyrical Poems Old and New' by George William Russell is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a journey through the depths of human emotions and the wonders of nature. From the enchanting melodies of ancient myths to the poignant reflections on love and loss, Russell's lyrical verses transport readers to a realm where beauty and truth intertwine. With each poem, the author invites us to pause, to listen, and to be moved by the profound wisdom and timeless truths that lie within the stillness of our souls.

Genres: Poetry, Lyrical, Mythology

Themes: Nature, Love, Loss, Spirituality
