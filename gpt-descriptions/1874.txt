Synopsis:
When their father mysteriously disappears, three siblings, Roberta, Peter, and Phyllis, are forced to leave their comfortable life in London and move to a small cottage near a railway station. As they adjust to their new surroundings, they befriend the station master and his family, and embark on a series of heartwarming adventures. Along the way, they uncover secrets, face challenges, and learn the true meaning of friendship and family. With the railway as their backdrop, the children's lives are forever changed as they discover the power of resilience, love, and the importance of standing up for what is right.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Family

Themes: Family bonds, Resilience, Friendship, Coming of age
