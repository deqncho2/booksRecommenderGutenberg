Synopsis: In 'Josiah Allen's Wife as a P. A. and P. I.: Samantha at the Centennial', the spirited and witty Samantha embarks on a new adventure as a personal assistant and private investigator. Set against the backdrop of the Centennial Exhibition, Samantha's sharp observations and unconventional methods shed light on the injustices and errors that plague society. With her unique perspective, she strives to guide her husband Josiah and others towards the path of virtue and happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Humor, Mystery

Themes: Social justice, Women's empowerment, Morality
