Synopsis: In 'A Noble Queen: A Romance of Indian History (Volume 1 of 3)', Taylor Meadows takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and tumultuous world of ancient India. Set against the backdrop of political intrigue, cultural clashes, and forbidden love, this historical romance follows the life of a remarkable queen whose strength and determination defy societal norms. As she navigates the treacherous waters of power and love, the queen's fate becomes intertwined with the destiny of her kingdom, leading to a thrilling tale of passion, sacrifice, and the enduring spirit of a noble queen.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Sacrifice, Gender Roles and Society
