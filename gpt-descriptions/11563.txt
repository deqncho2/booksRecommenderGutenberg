Synopsis: 
'Morning Bells; Or, Waking Thoughts for Little Ones' by Frances Ridley Havergal is a delightful collection of short poems and prayers that will captivate young readers. Each page is filled with charming verses that explore the wonders of nature, the joys of childhood, and the importance of gratitude and kindness. From the first rays of sunlight to the twinkling stars at night, this enchanting book will awaken the imagination and inspire young minds to embrace the beauty of the world around them.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: Nature, Gratitude, Childhood, Imagination
