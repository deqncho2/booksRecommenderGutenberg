Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Toroni, a mysterious series of events unfolds when a renowned detective, No. 13, arrives to investigate a perplexing case. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the town, he uncovers a web of deceit, hidden motives, and unexpected alliances. With each clue he uncovers, No. 13 becomes entangled in a complex puzzle that challenges his wit and determination. As the tension rises, the line between truth and deception blurs, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eagerly awaiting the shocking conclusion.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Unraveling the Truth
