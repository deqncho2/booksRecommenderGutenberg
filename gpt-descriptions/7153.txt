Synopsis: 
In 'Elder Conklin and Other Stories' by Frank Harris, readers are taken on a captivating journey through a collection of short stories that explore the complexities of human nature. From the tale of a young man's struggle with his religious upbringing to the unexpected consequences of a forbidden love affair, each story delves into the depths of human emotions and desires. With vivid storytelling and thought-provoking narratives, Harris invites readers to question societal norms and reflect on the choices we make in life. 

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Human nature, Religion, Love and desire
