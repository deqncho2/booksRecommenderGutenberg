Synopsis: In the small town of Milby, three compelling stories intertwine, revealing the hidden struggles and passions of the local clergy. From the enigmatic Reverend Amos Barton, whose faith is tested by personal tragedy, to the spirited Miss Janet Dempster, haunted by a dark secret, and the wise and compassionate Mr. Gilfil, who finds himself torn between duty and desire, these scenes of clerical life offer a captivating exploration of human nature, love, and redemption.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Faith and doubt, Love and desire, Redemption, Social class, Morality
