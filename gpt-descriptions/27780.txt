Synopsis:
When young Jim Hawkins discovers a mysterious map leading to buried treasure, he embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him to a remote island. Joined by a crew of eccentric pirates, Jim must navigate treacherous waters, face dangerous foes, and uncover the secrets of the legendary Captain Flint. As the stakes rise and alliances shift, Jim finds himself torn between loyalty and survival in a quest for riches that will test his courage and cunning to the limit.

Genres:
Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes:
The allure of treasure and its corrupting influence, the duality of human nature, the power of friendship and loyalty, the journey from innocence to experience
