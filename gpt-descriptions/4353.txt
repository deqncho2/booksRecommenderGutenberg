Synopsis: In 'Five Thousand an Hour: How Johnny Gamble Won the Heiress', George Randolph Chester takes readers on a thrilling journey through the high-stakes world of gambling and romance. Johnny Gamble, a charismatic and cunning gambler, finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of wits as he sets out to win the heart of a wealthy heiress. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the lengths one man will go to for love and fortune.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Gambling, Love, Wealth, Deception
