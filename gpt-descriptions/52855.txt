Synopsis: In 'The Star-Sent Knaves' by Keith Laumer, a group of interstellar con artists find themselves in a high-stakes game of deception and survival. When they stumble upon a mysterious planet with a hidden secret, they must navigate treacherous alliances and outwit their enemies to uncover the truth. As the stakes rise and the danger intensifies, the line between friend and foe becomes blurred, leaving the reader guessing until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Deception, Survival, Loyalty
