Synopsis: In "Papa knows best" by Umphrey Wallace, a small town is shaken by a mysterious event that leaves the community divided and secrets buried. As the protagonist, a young woman named Lily, delves deeper into the truth, she uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected connections that challenge her perception of family and loyalty. With each revelation, Lily must navigate the blurred lines between right and wrong, ultimately questioning who she can trust and how far she is willing to go to protect those she loves.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Family Drama

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Trust, Morality
