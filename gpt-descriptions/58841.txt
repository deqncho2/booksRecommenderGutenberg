Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The Holyhead Road: The Mail-coach Road to Dublin', Charles G. Harper takes readers on a captivating journey along the historic mail-coach road from London to Dublin. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Harper explores the fascinating landscapes, towns, and landmarks that line this iconic route. From encountering eccentric characters to uncovering hidden histories, this book offers a delightful blend of travelogue, history, and adventure that will transport readers back in time to the golden age of mail-coach travel.

Genres: Travelogue, History, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Transportation, Cultural Heritage
