Synopsis: In 'My Lady of the North' by Parrish, Randall, a young man named David finds himself captivated by a mysterious woman he encounters in the remote wilderness of the North. Drawn to her enigmatic charm and beauty, David embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets of her past and the truth behind her presence in this desolate land. As their paths intertwine, David becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and the haunting allure of the unknown. Will he be able to unravel the enigma that is his Lady of the North?

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and passion, Identity and self-discovery, Secrets and hidden pasts
