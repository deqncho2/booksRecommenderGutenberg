Synopsis: In 'A Victor of Salamis' by William Stearns Davis, a young Athenian soldier finds himself caught in the midst of the epic Battle of Salamis, a pivotal moment in ancient Greek history. As he fights for his life and witnesses the horrors of war, he is faced with difficult choices that will test his loyalty, honor, and courage. Through his journey, the reader is transported to the heart of ancient Greece, experiencing the intensity of battle and the complexities of human nature.

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Loyalty, Morality, the Brutality of War
