Synopsis: In 'The Ocean Wireless Boys on the Atlantic' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of adventurous young men embark on a thrilling journey across the Atlantic Ocean. As they navigate the treacherous waters, they encounter mysterious signals, dangerous storms, and unexpected allies. With their skills in wireless telegraphy, they uncover a hidden world of secrets and intrigue that could change the course of history. Will they be able to overcome the challenges and unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Technology, Courage
