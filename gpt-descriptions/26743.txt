Synopsis: In the small town of Putnam, a mysterious tradition has been passed down through generations, shrouded in secrecy and whispered rumors. When young Emma Putnam discovers a hidden diary that unveils the truth behind the tradition, she becomes determined to break free from its grip and challenge the norms of her community. As she delves deeper into the dark history of her family, Emma must navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and sacrifice, ultimately questioning the very foundations of her identity.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Tradition vs. Individuality, Family Secrets, Self-discovery
