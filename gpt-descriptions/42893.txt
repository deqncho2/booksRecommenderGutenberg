Synopsis: In 'The Guardians of the Columbia: Mount Hood, Mount Adams and Mount St. Helens', author John H. Williams takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the majestic landscapes of the Pacific Northwest. As the story unfolds, a group of unlikely heroes find themselves drawn together by a shared connection to these awe-inspiring mountains. With Mount Hood, Mount Adams, and Mount St. Helens as the backdrop, they must navigate treacherous terrain, face unexpected challenges, and confront their own inner demons. As they strive to protect these natural wonders, they discover that the true power lies not only in the mountains themselves, but also in the bonds of friendship and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Environmental Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Conservation, Friendship, Overcoming Adversity
