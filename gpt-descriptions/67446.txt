Synopsis: 

In 'Modern Whaling & Bear-Hunting', author Burn Murdoch takes readers on a thrilling journey into the dangerous and captivating world of present-day whaling and bear hunting. With vivid descriptions and up-to-date information, Murdoch explores the use of advanced tools and techniques employed in various parts of the world for these perilous pursuits. From the vast Arctic regions to the treacherous seas, this book unveils the challenges faced by hunters as they navigate through icy waters and confront formidable creatures. Prepare to be enthralled by the gripping accounts of these daring adventures and the remarkable individuals who undertake them.

Genres: Non-fiction, Adventure, Natural History

Themes: Man vs. Nature, Conservation, Exploration
