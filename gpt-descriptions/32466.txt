Synopsis: The Wouldbegoods follows the misadventures of a group of children who are determined to be good, but somehow always end up causing chaos and mayhem instead. From their attempts at gardening to their disastrous attempts at helping others, their good intentions often lead to hilarious and unpredictable outcomes. As they navigate through a series of misadventures, the children learn valuable lessons about friendship, responsibility, and the importance of thinking before acting.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Responsibility, Consequences of actions
