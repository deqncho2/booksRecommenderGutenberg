Synopsis: 
In 'Wild Life on the Rockies' by Mills, Enos A., embark on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of the Rocky Mountains. Join the author as he recounts his thrilling encounters with majestic wildlife, from grizzly bears and mountain lions to elusive wolves and graceful elk. Through vivid descriptions and heart-pounding anecdotes, Mills paints a captivating portrait of the rugged beauty and inherent dangers of life in the Rockies. As he navigates the treacherous terrain and faces the challenges of survival, readers will be transported to a world where nature reigns supreme and every moment is a test of strength and resilience.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Nature Writing

Themes: Wildlife conservation, Man vs. Nature, Survival
