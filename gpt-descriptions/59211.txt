Synopsis: In 'The Marriage of Elinor' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman named Elinor finds herself caught between duty and desire when she is forced into an arranged marriage. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, Elinor discovers unexpected allies and faces difficult choices that will shape her future. With its compelling characters and intricate plot, this novel explores the complexities of love, marriage, and societal expectations in Victorian England.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Historical

Themes: Love and duty, societal expectations, personal growth
