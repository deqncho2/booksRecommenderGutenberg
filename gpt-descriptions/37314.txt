Synopsis: In 'The Bradys After a Chinese Princess; Or, The Yellow Fiends of 'Frisco', the famous detective duo, the Bradys, find themselves embroiled in a thrilling adventure when they are tasked with protecting a Chinese princess from a dangerous group of criminals known as the Yellow Fiends. As they navigate the treacherous streets of San Francisco's Chinatown, the Bradys must unravel a web of deception, face deadly encounters, and uncover the truth behind the princess's mysterious past. Will they be able to outsmart the Yellow Fiends and ensure the princess's safety?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Crime

Themes: Crime and justice, Cultural clashes, Loyalty and betrayal
