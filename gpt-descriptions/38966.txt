Synopsis: 

'The Manifestation of the Miraculous Medal: A Brief Account of Its History and of the Establishment of the Association' takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins and significance of the Miraculous Medal. This concise account delves into the historical events surrounding the manifestation of the medal and the subsequent establishment of the Association dedicated to its promotion. From the mysterious apparitions to the profound impact it has had on countless lives, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the Miraculous Medal's enduring power and relevance.

Genres: Religious, Historical, Inspirational

Themes: Faith, Divine Intervention, Devotion, Miracles
