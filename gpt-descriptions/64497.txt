Synopsis: In 'Murderer's Base' by William J. Brittain, a small town is shaken by a series of mysterious deaths that seem to be connected. As the local police struggle to uncover the truth, a young detective with a troubled past is brought in to assist. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets and deceit that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Secrets, Deception, Redemption
