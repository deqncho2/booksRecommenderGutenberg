Synopsis: In 'Tom Pagdin, Pirate' by Brady, E. J., embark on a thrilling adventure as Tom Pagdin, a young sailor, finds himself caught in the dangerous world of piracy. From treacherous sea battles to hidden treasures, Tom must navigate the high seas and face formidable foes to survive. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Action

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Redemption
