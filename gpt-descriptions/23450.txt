Synopsis: 
'The Book of Ornamental Alphabets, Ancient and Medieval, from the Eighth Century: With Numerals, including Gothic; Church Text, Large and Small; German Arabesque; Initials for Illumination; Monograms, Crosses, &c.' is a captivating exploration of the intricate and beautiful world of ornamental alphabets. Delamotte takes readers on a visual journey through time, showcasing a wide range of alphabets and decorative elements from ancient and medieval periods. From Gothic to German Arabesque, this book offers a stunning collection of ornamental designs that will captivate and inspire artists, calligraphers, and history enthusiasts alike.

Genres: Art, Calligraphy, History
Themes: Ornamental design, Alphabets, Historical aesthetics
