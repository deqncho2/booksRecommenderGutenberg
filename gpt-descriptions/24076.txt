Synopsis: 

'Vegetable Dyes: Being a Book of Recipes and Other Information Useful to the Dyer' by Ethel Mairet is a captivating guide that delves into the world of natural dyes and their enchanting possibilities. With a wealth of recipes and insightful information, this book takes readers on a journey through the vibrant and diverse realm of vegetable dyes, revealing the secrets behind their creation and application. From the rich hues of madder root to the delicate shades of indigo, Mairet's expertise and passion shine through as she explores the art of dyeing with plants. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Craft, Reference

Themes: Natural dyes, Sustainability, Artistic expression
