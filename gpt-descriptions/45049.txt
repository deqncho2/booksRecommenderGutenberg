Synopsis: In 'My First Mission' by George Q. Cannon, a young protagonist embarks on a thrilling and dangerous adventure that takes them to uncharted territories. As they navigate through treacherous obstacles and encounter unexpected allies and enemies, they must rely on their wit, courage, and determination to fulfill their mission. With each twist and turn, the reader is left on the edge of their seat, eagerly anticipating the outcome of this captivating journey.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Perseverance
