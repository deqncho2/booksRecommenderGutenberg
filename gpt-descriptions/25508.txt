Synopsis: In the heart of London's bustling Strand district, a young woman named Hetty finds herself caught up in a web of mystery and intrigue. As she navigates the labyrinthine streets and encounters a cast of colorful characters, Hetty becomes entangled in a dangerous secret that could change her life forever. With each twist and turn, she must unravel the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, urban life, social class
