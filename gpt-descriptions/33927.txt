Synopsis: In "Not Quite Eighteen" by Susan Coolidge, we follow the adventures of a spirited young girl named Susy, who is on the cusp of adulthood. From her mischievous escapades at school to her encounters with intriguing characters, Susy navigates the challenges and joys of growing up in the late 19th century. With her wit, curiosity, and determination, she learns valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Adolescence, Identity, Independence, Friendship, Self-discovery
