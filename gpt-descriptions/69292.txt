Synopsis: In 'A Matter of Size' by Samuel Mines, a seemingly ordinary man named Jack discovers a mysterious device that grants him the ability to alter his physical size at will. As he explores the possibilities and consequences of this newfound power, Jack finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, where his choices could have far-reaching implications. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the true nature of power and the limits of human potential.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power, Identity, Morality
