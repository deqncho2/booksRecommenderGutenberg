Synopsis: 

In "Furniture of the Olden Time" by Frances Clary Morse, embark on a captivating journey through the history of furniture, where each piece holds a story waiting to be unveiled. From intricately carved chairs to ornate cabinets, this book takes readers on a visual and informative exploration of the craftsmanship and artistry that defined furniture in the past. Discover the secrets behind the designs, the materials used, and the cultural influences that shaped these timeless pieces. With vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Morse brings the furniture of the olden time to life, inviting readers to appreciate the beauty and significance of these historical treasures.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Art

Themes: Furniture craftsmanship, Historical significance, Cultural influences
