Synopsis: 
In 'Just Folks', Edgar A. Guest takes readers on a heartfelt journey through the lives of ordinary people, capturing the essence of their struggles, joys, and triumphs. Through his poignant and relatable poetry, Guest paints vivid portraits of everyday individuals, from the hardworking laborer to the devoted mother, the ambitious dreamer to the wise elder. With his signature blend of wit, wisdom, and compassion, Guest invites readers to reflect on the beauty and resilience found in the lives of "just folks."

Genres: Poetry, Inspirational, Slice of Life

Themes: Everyday life, Resilience, Human connection
