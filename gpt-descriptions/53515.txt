Synopsis: In 'The Mystery Boys and Captain Kidd's Message' by Powell, Van, a group of adventurous boys stumble upon a hidden treasure map that leads them on a thrilling quest. As they follow the clues left by the notorious pirate Captain Kidd, they encounter dangerous obstacles and unexpected allies. With time running out, the Mystery Boys must decipher the cryptic message and outsmart their rivals to uncover the long-lost treasure. Will they succeed in their daring adventure?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Treasure Hunting
