Synopsis: In 'The phantom of Bogue Holauba: 1911' by Charles Egbert Craddock, a small town in the Appalachian Mountains becomes the setting for a mysterious and haunting presence. As the townspeople grapple with unexplained occurrences and eerie sightings, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey to uncover the truth behind the phantom that haunts Bogue Holauba. With secrets, suspense, and a touch of the supernatural, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets, Fear, Supernatural phenomena
