Synopsis: In 'Carnac's Folly, Volume 3' by Gilbert Parker, the captivating story continues as the enigmatic Carnac faces new challenges and unexpected revelations. As he navigates through a web of political intrigue, personal conflicts, and romantic entanglements, Carnac's true identity becomes increasingly elusive. With his reputation on the line and his heart torn between loyalty and desire, Carnac must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny. Will he uncover the truth behind his past and find redemption, or will he succumb to the folly that threatens to consume him?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Love and Betrayal
