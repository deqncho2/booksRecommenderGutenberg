Synopsis: In "Overruled" by Bernard Shaw, two couples find themselves entangled in a hilarious and thought-provoking love quadrangle. As they navigate their desires and societal expectations, their relationships become a battleground of wit and charm. With unexpected twists and clever dialogue, Shaw challenges conventional notions of love, fidelity, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Comedy, Drama, Satire

Themes: Love, Marriage, Morality, Social Expectations
