Synopsis: In 'The Duke's Sweetheart: A Romance' by Richard Dowling, a spirited young woman finds herself caught in a whirlwind of passion and intrigue when she captures the attention of a powerful duke. As their worlds collide, secrets are revealed, hearts are tested, and the boundaries of society are challenged. With its captivating blend of forbidden love, hidden desires, and unexpected twists, this enchanting tale will keep readers eagerly turning the pages until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Social class, Identity, Betrayal
