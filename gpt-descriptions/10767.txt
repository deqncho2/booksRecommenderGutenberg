Synopsis:
In "Courage" by J. M. Barrie, a young man named David finds himself caught between duty and desire when he is faced with a life-altering decision. As he navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and personal growth, David must summon the courage to follow his heart, even if it means defying societal expectations. Set against the backdrop of early 20th-century England, this captivating tale explores the transformative power of bravery and the pursuit of true happiness.

Genres:
Drama, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Courage, Love, Duty, Personal growth
