Synopsis: In 'Type: A Primer of Information About the Mechanical Features of Printing Types', Alexander A. Stewart takes readers on a fascinating journey into the world of printing types. From the intricate details of their sizes and font schemes to the captivating process of their manufacture, this book unveils the secrets behind the mechanical features that bring words to life on the page. With a blend of historical context and technical expertise, Stewart's primer offers a captivating exploration of the art and science of typography.

Genres: Non-fiction, Reference, History

Themes: Typography, Printing, Design
