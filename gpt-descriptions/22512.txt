Synopsis: In "The Stutterer" by Reuben Merliss, we are introduced to a young man named David who struggles with a debilitating stutter. Despite his speech impediment, David possesses a brilliant mind and a deep desire to connect with others. As he navigates through life, he encounters various challenges and opportunities that force him to confront his fears and find his voice. Along the way, he discovers the power of love, friendship, and self-acceptance, ultimately leading him on a journey of personal growth and transformation.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Romance

Themes: Overcoming adversity, Self-discovery, Communication, Acceptance
