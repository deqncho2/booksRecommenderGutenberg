Synopsis: In 'The Power of Darkness' by Leo Tolstoy, a seemingly idyllic rural family is torn apart by a web of secrets, lies, and forbidden desires. As the darkness of human nature takes hold, the characters are forced to confront their own moral dilemmas and face the consequences of their actions. This gripping tale explores the depths of human depravity and the struggle between good and evil, leaving readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Morality, Sin, Family, Redemption
