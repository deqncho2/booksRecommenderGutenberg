Synopsis: In "The Borghesi Astronomical Clock in the Museum of History and Technology: Contributions from the Museum of History and Technology, Paper 35," Silvio A. Bedini takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate world of a remarkable astronomical clock. This clock, created by the Borghesi family, not only tells time but also reveals the wonders of the universe. As Bedini explores the clock's history, construction, and significance, he unveils a fascinating blend of art, science, and technology that will leave readers in awe of human ingenuity.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Art and Science, Human Ingenuity, Historical Significance
