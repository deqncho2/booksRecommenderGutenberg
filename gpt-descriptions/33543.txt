Synopsis: In 'The Amphibians and Reptiles of Michoacán, México', Duellman takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse and enchanting world of amphibians and reptiles found in the beautiful region of Michoacán, México. From the vibrant colors of the poison dart frogs to the majestic movements of the snakes, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the fascinating creatures that inhabit this Mexican state. With stunning photographs and detailed descriptions, Duellman provides a window into the unique ecosystems and habitats that these animals call home. Whether you are a nature enthusiast, a herpetology lover, or simply curious about the wonders of the natural world, this book will leave you captivated and inspired.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Science

Themes: Biodiversity, Conservation, Natural History
