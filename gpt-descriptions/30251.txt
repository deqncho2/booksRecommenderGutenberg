Synopsis: 

In 'Disqualified' by Fontenay, Charles L., a brilliant scientist finds himself at the center of a high-stakes conspiracy that threatens to unravel the fabric of society. Driven by a relentless pursuit of truth, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden agendas to uncover the shocking secrets that lie beneath the surface. As the tension escalates, the protagonist's life is turned upside down, forcing him to question everything he thought he knew and confront the dark forces that seek to silence him. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a world of intrigue and danger, where the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Conspiracy, Truth vs. Deception, Power Dynamics
