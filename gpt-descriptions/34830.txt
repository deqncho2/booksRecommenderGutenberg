Synopsis: 
In 'Little Frankie and his Mother' by Leslie, Madeline, readers are introduced to a heartwarming tale of a young boy named Frankie and his loving mother. Set in a small, idyllic town, the story follows Frankie as he embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience. Through a series of unexpected events, Frankie and his mother find themselves facing challenges that test their bond and strength. As they navigate through adversity, they learn valuable lessons about love, courage, and the power of family. This captivating narrative explores the unbreakable connection between a mother and her child, and the transformative power of unconditional love.

Genres: Fiction, Family, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love, Resilience, Family bonds
