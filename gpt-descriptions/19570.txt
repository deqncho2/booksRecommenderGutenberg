Synopsis: 

'Van Dyck: A Collection of Fifteen Pictures and a Portrait of the Painter with Introduction and Interpretation' by Estelle M. Hurll is a captivating exploration of the renowned Flemish painter, Anthony van Dyck. Through a collection of fifteen carefully selected pictures and a portrait of the painter himself, Hurll delves into the life and artistry of this influential figure. With insightful interpretations and a compelling introduction, the book offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of van Dyck and his masterpieces.

Genres: Art History, Biography, Interpretation

Themes: Artistic genius, Portrait painting, Historical context
