Synopsis: In 'Caprice' by Ronald Firbank, readers are transported to a world of eccentric characters and scandalous affairs. Set in a luxurious European hotel, the story follows a group of aristocrats, artists, and socialites as they navigate a web of love, jealousy, and hidden desires. As the characters engage in witty banter and engage in outrageous behavior, the reader is drawn into a captivating and decadent world where appearances are deceiving and nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Social class and hierarchy, Deception and appearances
