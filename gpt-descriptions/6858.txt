Synopsis: Grace Harlowe's Second Year at Overton College follows the adventures of Grace Harlowe and her friends as they navigate their second year at college. From solving mysteries to facing personal challenges, Grace and her friends find themselves caught up in a whirlwind of excitement and growth. As they strive to balance their academic pursuits with their social lives, they discover the true meaning of friendship, loyalty, and perseverance. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to uncover what lies ahead for Grace and her friends.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Friendship, Personal Growth, Academic Pursuits, Loyalty, Perseverance
