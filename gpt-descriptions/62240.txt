Synopsis: 
'A History of Chester Cathedral: with biographical notices of the Bishops and Deans' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of Chester Cathedral, delving into the lives of the influential bishops and deans who have shaped its legacy. From its humble beginnings as a Saxon church to its transformation into a magnificent Gothic masterpiece, this book unveils the secrets, scandals, and triumphs that have unfolded within the cathedral's hallowed walls. With meticulous research and vivid storytelling, author John Hicklin paints a vivid picture of the cathedral's evolution, offering a fascinating glimpse into the lives of the individuals who have left an indelible mark on this iconic landmark.

Genres: History, Biography, Architecture

Themes: Religious institutions, Cultural heritage, Architectural evolution
