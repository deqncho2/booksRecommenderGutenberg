Synopsis: In 'The Rough Road' by William John Locke, a young artist named Philip Ostrander embarks on a journey that takes him from the bustling streets of Paris to the enchanting landscapes of rural France. Along the way, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own secrets and desires. As Philip navigates the twists and turns of his own personal and artistic growth, he must confront the challenges of love, ambition, and the pursuit of one's true self. This captivating tale explores the complexities of human relationships and the transformative power of self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Artistic expression, Identity, Love and relationships
