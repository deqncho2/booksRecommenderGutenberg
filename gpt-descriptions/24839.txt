Synopsis: In "The Blonde Lady: Being a Record of the Duel of Wits between Arsène Lupin and the English Detective," the infamous gentleman thief, Arsène Lupin, finds himself entangled in a thrilling battle of intelligence and cunning with an English detective. As Lupin sets his sights on a valuable treasure, the detective becomes determined to outsmart him at every turn. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following the clash between these two formidable adversaries.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Adventure

Themes: Duel of wits, Cunning and intelligence, Treasure hunting
