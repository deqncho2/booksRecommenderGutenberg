Synopsis: In 'Molly, the Drummer Boy: A Story of the Revolution', follow the captivating journey of Molly, a young girl who disguises herself as a boy to join the Continental Army during the American Revolution. As she navigates the challenges and dangers of war, Molly's courage and determination shine through, inspiring those around her and leaving a lasting impact on the fight for independence.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Identity, Patriotism, Gender Roles
