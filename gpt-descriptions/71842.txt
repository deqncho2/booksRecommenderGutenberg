Synopsis: 

In the second volume of 'A history of art in Chaldæa & Assyria', Chipiez and Perrot take readers on a captivating journey through the ancient civilizations of Chaldæa and Assyria. Delving into the rich artistic heritage of these ancient cultures, the authors explore the intricate details of their architecture, sculpture, and decorative arts. From the majestic palaces of Nineveh to the awe-inspiring reliefs of the Assyrian kings, this volume offers a comprehensive and visually stunning exploration of the artistry that flourished in these ancient lands. 

Genres: Art History, Archaeology, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Artistic expression, Cultural heritage
