Synopsis: In 'The Abbatial Crosier; or, Bonaik and Septimine', the reader is transported to a medieval abbey where the lives of the nuns are disrupted by the arrival of a mysterious young woman. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the abbess must navigate the delicate balance between duty and desire. With its rich historical setting and complex characters, this tale of love, power, and faith will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Power struggles, Forbidden love, Religious devotion
