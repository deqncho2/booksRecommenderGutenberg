Synopsis: 

'Out To Win: The Story of America in France' by Dawson, Coningsby takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous events of World War I. Set against the backdrop of France, this gripping narrative follows a group of American soldiers as they navigate the horrors of war, forming deep bonds and facing unimaginable challenges. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, loyalties are tested, and the characters are forced to confront their own humanity in the face of adversity. With its vivid descriptions and compelling storytelling, this book offers a unique perspective on the American experience in France during a pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Drama

Themes: Friendship, Sacrifice, Identity, Courage, Survival
