Synopsis: In 'Old Gold: The Cruise of the "Jason" Brig', a group of adventurers embarks on a perilous journey to uncover a hidden treasure. Led by the fearless Captain Robur, they set sail on the mysterious "Jason" brig, facing treacherous waters, dangerous encounters, and unexpected twists. As they delve deeper into their quest, the crew must confront their own fears and desires, testing their loyalty and determination. Will they succeed in their quest for old gold, or will the secrets of the past remain buried forever?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Treasure hunting, Exploration, Loyalty, Survival
