Synopsis: 

'Famous Adventures and Prison Escapes of the Civil War' is a gripping collection of true stories that chronicles the daring exploits and harrowing escapes of soldiers during the American Civil War. From thrilling accounts of audacious prison breaks to heart-pounding adventures behind enemy lines, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the extraordinary courage and resilience displayed by individuals in the face of immense danger. With each page, readers will be transported to a time of turmoil and conflict, where the will to survive and fight for freedom knows no bounds.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, War

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Survival, Freedom
