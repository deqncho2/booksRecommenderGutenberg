Synopsis: 
In the small town of Clover, a mysterious event takes place that changes the lives of its residents forever. As secrets unravel and tensions rise, a group of unlikely friends must come together to uncover the truth behind the enigma that has gripped their community. With each twist and turn, the lines between reality and fantasy blur, leading them on a thrilling journey that will test their courage and challenge their beliefs. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fiction

Themes: Secrets and lies, Friendship, Perception of reality
