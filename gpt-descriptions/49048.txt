Synopsis: 

'Two Centuries of New Milford Connecticut' takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of a small town, as it celebrates its bi-centennial in 1907. Filled with historical articles, reminiscences, and vivid descriptions of the festivities, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the founding and development of New Milford. From the struggles of the early settlers to the growth of industry and the vibrant community that emerged, the book paints a vivid picture of the town's rich past. 

Genres: Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: History, Community, Progress
