Synopsis: In 'The Auto Boys' Quest' by James A. Braden, a group of adventurous friends stumble upon a mysterious map that leads them on an exhilarating journey filled with danger, secrets, and unexpected twists. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter enigmatic characters, the boys must rely on their wit, courage, and teamwork to unravel the clues and uncover the truth behind the map's origins. With each step closer to their goal, they find themselves entangled in a web of intrigue that could change their lives forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Discovery
