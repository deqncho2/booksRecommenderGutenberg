Synopsis: In "Lake of Fire" by Frank Belknap Long, a small New England town becomes the setting for a series of inexplicable and terrifying events. As the residents grapple with the growing sense of unease, they soon discover that an ancient evil has awakened, threatening to consume everything in its path. With the town's very existence at stake, a group of unlikely heroes must confront their deepest fears and uncover the truth behind the malevolent force that has been unleashed upon them.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Fear, Sacrifice
