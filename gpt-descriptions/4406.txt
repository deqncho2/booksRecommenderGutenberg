Synopsis: In 'The Ordeal of Richard Feverel - Volume 1', George Meredith takes us on a captivating journey through the tumultuous life of Richard Feverel, a young man caught between the expectations of society and the desires of his heart. Raised by a strict father who believes in the power of reason, Richard is determined to follow a path of rationality in matters of love. However, when he meets the enchanting Lucy Desborough, his carefully constructed world begins to unravel. As Richard navigates the complexities of love, passion, and societal pressures, he is faced with a series of challenges that will test his beliefs and force him to confront the true nature of his desires.

Genres: Bildungsroman, Romance, Satire

Themes: Love and desire, societal expectations, the conflict between reason and emotion
