Synopsis: 
'Songs of the Ridings' by F. W. Moorman is a captivating collection of traditional folk songs from the Yorkshire Dales, filled with tales of love, loss, and the resilience of the people. From haunting ballads to lively drinking songs, this anthology takes readers on a lyrical journey through the rich cultural heritage of the region. As each song unfolds, the reader is transported to a bygone era, where the beauty of the Yorkshire countryside intertwines with the raw emotions of its inhabitants. With its evocative storytelling and melodic verses, 'Songs of the Ridings' is a must-read for anyone seeking to immerse themselves in the enchanting world of Yorkshire's musical traditions.

Genres: Folklore, Poetry, Music

Themes: Love, Loss, Resilience, Cultural Heritage
