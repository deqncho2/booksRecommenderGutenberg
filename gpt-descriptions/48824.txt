Synopsis: In 'Countess Vera; or, The Oath of Vengeance' by Miller, Alex. McVeigh, Mrs., a young countess finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and betrayal as she seeks to avenge the tragic loss of her family. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Russia, Countess Vera must navigate the treacherous world of high society while unraveling the truth behind her family's demise. As she delves deeper into the dark underbelly of her aristocratic world, she discovers shocking revelations that challenge her beliefs and force her to question her own desires for revenge. With danger lurking at every turn, Countess Vera must decide whether to uphold her oath of vengeance or find a path to forgiveness and redemption.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Revenge, Betrayal, Identity, Redemption
