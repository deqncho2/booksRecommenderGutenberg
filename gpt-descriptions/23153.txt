Synopsis: 

In 'The Big Bounce' by Walter S. Tevis, we are introduced to a charismatic and cunning protagonist named Jack Ryan. Jack finds himself caught in a web of deceit and danger when he becomes entangled in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse. As he navigates through a world of con artists, femme fatales, and unexpected twists, Jack must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to survive. With every move he makes, the tension rises, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. 

Genres: Crime fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Survival
