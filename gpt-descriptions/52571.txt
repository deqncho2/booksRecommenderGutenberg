Synopsis: In 'Evolution' by James A. S. Watson, a groundbreaking scientific discovery sets off a chain of events that challenges everything we thought we knew about the origins of life. As a team of researchers delves deeper into the mysteries of evolution, they uncover a hidden truth that could change the course of humanity forever. With thrilling twists and thought-provoking revelations, this gripping novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the realms of science and the human condition.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Evolution, Human Nature, Scientific Ethics
