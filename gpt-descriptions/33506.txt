Synopsis: In "The Death-Blow to Spiritualism: Being the True Story of the Fox Sisters," Reuben Briggs Davenport delves into the captivating and controversial world of spiritualism through the lens of the famous Fox Sisters. As the sisters gain fame for their supposed ability to communicate with the dead, Davenport sets out to uncover the truth behind their mysterious powers. Through a series of shocking revelations and unexpected twists, the author challenges the very foundation of spiritualism, leaving readers questioning the nature of the supernatural.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Mystery

Themes: Deception, Skepticism, Supernatural phenomena
