Synopsis: In 'Barbarians' by Robert W. Chambers, a young artist named Howard Milmarsh finds himself drawn into a mysterious and dangerous world when he becomes infatuated with a captivating woman. As he delves deeper into her world, he uncovers a hidden society filled with secrets, forbidden desires, and ancient rituals. Howard must navigate a treacherous path, balancing his own desires with the dark forces that threaten to consume him.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Psychological thriller

Themes: Forbidden love, the duality of human nature, the power of obsession, the allure of the unknown
