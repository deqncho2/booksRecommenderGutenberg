Synopsis: In 'Return to Pleasure Island' by Cory Doctorow, a group of childhood friends reunite after many years to revisit the mysterious and enchanting Pleasure Island. As they delve into the island's secrets, they uncover a hidden world filled with danger, adventure, and unexpected twists. With their bonds tested and their pasts haunting them, they must navigate through a thrilling journey that will challenge their perceptions of reality and the power of friendship.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Science Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Nostalgia, Identity, Exploration
