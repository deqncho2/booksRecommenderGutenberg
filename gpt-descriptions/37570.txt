Synopsis: In "Secret History of the Court of England," Lady Anne Hamilton delves into the intriguing and scandalous events that unfolded within the English royal court during the reigns of George III and George IV. Volume 1 of this captivating two-part series uncovers the hidden secrets and untold stories surrounding the court, including the mysterious death of Princess Charlotte. Lady Hamilton's meticulous research and insider knowledge shed light on the clandestine affairs, political intrigues, and personal dramas that shaped the course of history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political intrigue

Themes: Royal scandals, Power struggles, Courtly politics
