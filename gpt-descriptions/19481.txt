Synopsis: 
"Everyman," with other interludes, including eight miracle plays, is a captivating and thought-provoking collection of medieval English drama. This anthology features the renowned morality play "Everyman," which follows the journey of a character named Everyman as he faces the ultimate reckoning. Alongside this powerful play, the collection includes eight miracle plays, each presenting a unique biblical story with a touch of humor and spectacle. From the fall of Lucifer to the resurrection of Christ, these interludes offer a fascinating glimpse into the medieval mindset and the moral dilemmas faced by humanity. Prepare to be enthralled by the timeless themes and vivid characters that populate this extraordinary collection.

Genres: Drama, Morality Play, Medieval Literature
Themes: Morality, Death, Redemption, Spirituality, Human Nature
