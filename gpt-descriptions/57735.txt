Synopsis: 

'Kelion Franklin Peddicord of Quirk's Scouts, Morgan's Kentucky Cavalry, C. S. A.' is a captivating historical fiction novel that follows the life of Kelion Franklin Peddicord, a young soldier who finds himself caught in the midst of the American Civil War. As he navigates the treacherous battlefields and forms unexpected alliances, Kelion's loyalty and courage are put to the ultimate test. With vivid descriptions and compelling storytelling, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through the war-torn landscapes of Kentucky.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Survival
