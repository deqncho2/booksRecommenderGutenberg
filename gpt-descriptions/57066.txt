Synopsis: In the small town of Maplewood, a mysterious corner house stands as a symbol of intrigue and secrets. When a young journalist, Alice, arrives to investigate the house's dark history, she becomes entangled in a web of hidden pasts, forbidden love, and long-buried secrets. As she delves deeper into the house's enigmatic past, Alice discovers that the truth may be more dangerous than she ever imagined. With each revelation, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leaving Alice questioning her own sanity and the true nature of the corner house.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets, Forbidden Love, Supernatural Phenomena
