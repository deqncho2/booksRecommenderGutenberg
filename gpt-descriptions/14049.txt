Synopsis: In 'The Pointing Man: A Burmese Mystery' by Marjorie Douie, a young British woman named Alice finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and danger while living in colonial Burma. When she stumbles upon a mysterious statue known as the Pointing Man, she becomes determined to uncover its hidden meaning and the dark forces that surround it. As Alice delves deeper into the secrets of the Pointing Man, she must navigate treacherous alliances, cultural clashes, and the haunting presence of the Burmese jungle. With each step closer to the truth, Alice realizes that the answers she seeks may come at a great personal cost.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Colonialism, Cultural Identity, Secrets and Betrayal
