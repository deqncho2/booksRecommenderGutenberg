Synopsis: In "When the Word is Given...: A Report on Elijah Muhammad, Malcolm X, and the Black Muslim World," Louis E. Lomax delves into the lives and ideologies of two influential figures in the Black Muslim movement: Elijah Muhammad and Malcolm X. Through extensive research and interviews, Lomax uncovers the complex dynamics within the Nation of Islam and the impact these leaders had on the Black community during the civil rights era. From their early lives to their rise within the movement, Lomax provides a compelling narrative that explores the tensions, controversies, and ultimate transformations that shaped the Black Muslim world.

Genres: Biography, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Identity, Religion, Activism, Leadership
