Synopsis: In 'The Pilots of Pomona: A Story of the Orkney Islands', author Robert Leighton takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged and mysterious Orkney Islands. Set in the 19th century, the story follows the lives of a group of courageous pilots who navigate treacherous waters to guide ships safely to shore. Amidst the dangerous storms and hidden secrets of the islands, the pilots find themselves entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of justice. As they face personal and professional challenges, their loyalty and determination are put to the ultimate test. With vivid descriptions of the stunning landscapes and a cast of compelling characters, 'The Pilots of Pomona' is a gripping tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Love, Betrayal, Justice
