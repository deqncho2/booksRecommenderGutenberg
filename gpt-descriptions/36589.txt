Synopsis: 
In "The Dover Road: Annals of an Ancient Turnpike," Charles G. Harper takes readers on a captivating journey along the historic Dover Road, a renowned turnpike that has witnessed countless tales of adventure, romance, and mystery. Through vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, Harper unveils the hidden stories and secrets that lie within the road's ancient stones, captivating readers with tales of highwaymen, ghostly encounters, and unexpected twists of fate. As travelers traverse the Dover Road, they will be transported to a bygone era, where every mile holds a new surprise and every bend in the road reveals a new chapter in history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Travelogue, Mystery

Themes: Adventure, Romance, History, Mystery
