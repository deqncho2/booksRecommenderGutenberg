Synopsis: In 'A Woman's Burden: A Novel' by Hume, Fergus, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits a mysterious estate from a distant relative. As she delves into the history of the estate, Emily uncovers a hidden world of forbidden love, family rivalries, and long-buried secrets that threaten to unravel her own life. With each revelation, Emily must navigate treacherous waters and make difficult choices that will ultimately determine her fate. This gripping tale of love, betrayal, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Forbidden love, Family dynamics, Self-discovery
