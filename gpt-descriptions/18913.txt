Synopsis: 
In 'Hardy Perennials and Old Fashioned Flowers', author J. G. Wood takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of plants, unveiling the most desirable species for borders, rockeries, and shrubberies. With vivid descriptions and insightful tips, Wood introduces a wide range of stunning flowers that will leave readers eager to transform their own gardens into breathtaking landscapes. From delicate blooms to robust perennials, this book is a treasure trove of knowledge for both novice and experienced gardeners alike.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Gardening, Reference

Themes: 
Botany, Horticulture, Garden Design
