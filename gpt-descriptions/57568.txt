Synopsis:
'Peter' by E. F. Benson is a gripping psychological thriller that follows the life of Peter, a seemingly ordinary man with a dark secret. As the story unfolds, Peter's past begins to catch up with him, leading to a series of unexpected and chilling events. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into Peter's complex and haunting world, questioning the true nature of identity and the lengths one will go to protect their secrets.

Genres:
Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes:
Identity, Secrets, Consequences
