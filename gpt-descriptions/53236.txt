Synopsis: In 'A Thanksgiving Dream: A One Act Play for Primary Children', a group of young students embark on a magical journey that takes them back in time to the very first Thanksgiving. As they interact with historical figures and experience the hardships and joys of the Pilgrims and Native Americans, they learn valuable lessons about gratitude, friendship, and the true meaning of Thanksgiving.

Genres: Children's Literature, Historical Fiction, Fantasy

Themes: Gratitude, Friendship, Cultural Understanding, History
