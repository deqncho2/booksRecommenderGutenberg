Synopsis: In 'The Story of Chautauqua' by Jesse Lyman Hurlbut, embark on a captivating journey through the history of Chautauqua, a small town that became a cultural and educational phenomenon. From its humble beginnings as a Sunday school assembly to its transformation into a renowned center for learning and entertainment, this book unveils the fascinating stories of the people and events that shaped Chautauqua's legacy. Discover the secrets behind its success and the impact it had on American society, as well as the challenges it faced along the way. Prepare to be inspired by the remarkable individuals who made Chautauqua a beacon of enlightenment and a testament to the power of education and community.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Cultural history

Themes: Education, Community, Cultural enrichment, Social progress
