Synopsis: 
'Within the Pale: The True Story of Anti-Semitic Persecution in Russia' by Michael Davitt takes readers on a gripping journey through the dark underbelly of late 19th-century Russia. This powerful non-fiction work delves into the harrowing experiences of Jewish communities living within the Pale of Settlement, where they faced relentless discrimination, violence, and persecution. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Davitt exposes the deep-rooted anti-Semitism that plagued Russian society, shedding light on the struggles and resilience of a marginalized community fighting for survival.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Social Justice

Themes: Anti-Semitism, Discrimination, Persecution, Resilience, Social Injustice
