Synopsis: In the small town of Salem, a young woman named Dulcibel finds herself caught in the midst of a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of justice. As accusations of witchcraft spread like wildfire, Dulcibel must navigate the dangerous waters of suspicion and fear, all while trying to protect her own secrets. With the fate of her loved ones hanging in the balance, Dulcibel must summon the strength to face the darkness that threatens to consume them all.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Witchcraft, Love and Betrayal, Justice and Innocence
