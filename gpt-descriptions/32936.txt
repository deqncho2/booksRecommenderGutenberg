Synopsis: In 'An Unknown Lover' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, a young woman named Muriel finds herself caught between two suitors, each with their own secrets and hidden agendas. As she navigates the complexities of love and loyalty, Muriel must unravel the mysteries surrounding her unknown lover and make a choice that will shape her future. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the power of secrets and the consequences of following one's heart.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Love and betrayal, Identity and deception
