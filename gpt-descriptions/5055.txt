Synopsis: 
'Plays: First Series' by John Galsworthy is a captivating collection of four thought-provoking plays that delve into the complexities of human relationships and societal norms. From the struggles of a young woman caught between duty and love, to the clash between tradition and progress in a small village, each play presents a unique and compelling story that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. Galsworthy's masterful storytelling and insightful exploration of human nature make this collection a must-read for fans of drama and social commentary.

Genres: Drama, Social Commentary

Themes: Love and Duty, Tradition vs. Progress
