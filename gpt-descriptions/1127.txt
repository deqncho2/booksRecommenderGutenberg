Synopsis: In the city of Venice, a powerful and respected Moorish general named Othello falls deeply in love with a beautiful and virtuous woman named Desdemona. However, their love is soon tested when Othello's ensign, Iago, begins to sow seeds of doubt and jealousy in Othello's mind. As Iago's manipulations intensify, Othello's once strong and noble character begins to crumble, leading to a tragic chain of events that will forever change the lives of those involved.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama

Themes: Jealousy, Betrayal, Racism, Love, Appearance vs. Reality
