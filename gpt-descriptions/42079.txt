Synopsis: In 'Mari, Our Little Norwegian Cousin', readers are transported to the beautiful country of Norway, where they meet Mari, a young girl with a heart full of curiosity and a spirit of adventure. Join Mari as she embarks on a journey filled with cultural traditions, breathtaking landscapes, and unexpected friendships. From exploring the fjords to celebrating St. John's Day, Mari's experiences will captivate readers and leave them yearning for their own Norwegian adventure.

Genres: Children's Literature, Cultural Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Cultural exploration, Friendship, Tradition
