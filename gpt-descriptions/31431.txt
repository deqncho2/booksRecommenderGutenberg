Synopsis: 
Step into a world of enchantment and wonder as Charles Perrault weaves together a collection of captivating old-time stories. From the mischievous adventures of a clever cat to the magical transformation of a humble pumpkin, these timeless tales will transport you to a realm where animals talk, wishes come true, and love conquers all. Discover the secrets hidden within these pages and let your imagination soar as you embark on a journey through the realms of fantasy and imagination.

Genres: Fairy tales, Fantasy, Children's literature

Themes: Magic, Morality, Transformation, Love, Adventure
