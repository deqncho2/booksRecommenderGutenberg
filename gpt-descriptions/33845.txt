Synopsis: In 'The Lightning Conductor: The Strange Adventures of a Motor-Car', Alice Muriel and Charles Norris Williamson take readers on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. The story revolves around a remarkable motor-car named "The Lightning Conductor" and its eccentric owner, Mr. Larkins. As the car embarks on a series of extraordinary adventures, it becomes clear that there is more to this vehicle than meets the eye. From mysterious encounters to daring escapades, the authors weave a captivating tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Ingenuity, Unpredictability
