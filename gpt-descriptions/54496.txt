Synopsis: In 'The Dreadnought Boys in Home Waters' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of adventurous young men find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey aboard a powerful battleship. As they navigate the treacherous waters of their home country, they encounter unexpected challenges and face dangerous adversaries. With their courage and resourcefulness, the Dreadnought Boys must overcome these obstacles to protect their nation and uncover a hidden secret that could change the course of history.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Patriotism, Friendship, Courage, Loyalty
