Synopsis: In "The Rover Boys in Southern Waters; or, The Deserted Steam Yacht," the adventurous Rover brothers find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey aboard a mysterious steam yacht. As they navigate the treacherous waters of the South, they uncover a series of puzzling clues that lead them to a deserted island. With danger lurking around every corner, the Rovers must use their wits and bravery to unravel the secrets of the steam yacht and solve the enigma of the deserted island.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Brotherhood, Courage
