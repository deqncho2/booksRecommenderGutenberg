Synopsis: In "The Red Book of Heroes" by Mrs. Lang, embark on a captivating journey through the tales of legendary heroes from various cultures and times. From the valiant exploits of ancient warriors to the cunning tricks of clever tricksters, this collection of stories will transport readers to a world of adventure, courage, and wisdom. Discover the extraordinary feats of heroes like Hercules, Robin Hood, and King Arthur, as well as lesser-known but equally fascinating figures. Each tale is filled with thrilling action, unexpected twists, and valuable lessons that will leave readers inspired and eager for more.

Genres: Mythology, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Heroism, Courage, Wisdom, Morality
