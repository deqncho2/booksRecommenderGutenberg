Synopsis: In 'A Treatise on the Crime of Onan: Illustrated with a Variety of Cases, Together with the Method of Cure', Tissot delves into the controversial topic of self-pleasure and its consequences. Through a collection of intriguing cases, he explores the physical, mental, and moral implications of this act, providing a thought-provoking analysis that challenges societal norms and offers a potential path to redemption.

Genres: Non-fiction, Medical literature, Ethics

Themes: Morality, Sexual health, Personal responsibility
