Synopsis: In "The Yellow Face," a seemingly ordinary suburban couple is thrown into turmoil when a mysterious yellow face appears in their lives. As they delve deeper into the truth behind this enigmatic presence, they are forced to confront their own assumptions and prejudices, leading to unexpected revelations and a profound examination of love, trust, and the complexities of human relationships.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological thriller, Domestic drama

Themes: Identity, Perception, Prejudice, Trust, Love
