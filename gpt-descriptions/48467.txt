Synopsis: 
In the prestigious Oakdale Academy, a group of boys find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, rivalries, and unexpected friendships. As they navigate the challenges of adolescence, they uncover hidden truths that threaten to shatter their world. From clandestine meetings to daring escapades, the boys of Oakdale Academy must confront their fears and confront the consequences of their actions. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the ultimate truth.

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Friendship, Betrayal, Identity, Consequences
