Synopsis: 
In 'Zoe; Or, Some Day: A Novel' by May Leonard, readers are introduced to Zoe, a young woman with an extraordinary ability that sets her apart from others. As she navigates through life, Zoe's unique gift becomes both a blessing and a curse, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. Along the way, she encounters unexpected challenges, forms deep connections with others, and learns the true power of her own potential. This captivating novel explores the complexities of identity, the pursuit of dreams, and the transformative nature of love.

Genres: 
Fiction, Coming-of-age, Magical realism

Themes: 
Identity, Self-discovery, Personal growth, Dreams, Love
