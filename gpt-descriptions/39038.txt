Synopsis: In 'One dialogue, or Colloquye of Erasmus (entituled Diuersoria)', Erasmus presents a captivating conversation between two characters, exploring various topics of interest. Through their dialogue, the reader is taken on a thought-provoking journey, delving into the realms of education, religion, and social customs. The characters engage in a lively exchange of ideas, challenging conventional wisdom and shedding light on the complexities of human nature. This translation from Latin to English allows the learned to offer their judgment before the translator proceeds with the rest of the work.

Genres: Dialogue, Satire, Educational

Themes: Education, Religion, Social Customs, Human Nature
