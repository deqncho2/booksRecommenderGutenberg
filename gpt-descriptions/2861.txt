Synopsis: In "The Sleuth of St. James's Square," a renowned detective is faced with a perplexing case that takes him deep into the heart of London's elite society. When a wealthy aristocrat is found dead under mysterious circumstances, the detective must navigate a web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives to uncover the truth. As he delves into the dark underbelly of high society, he discovers that even the most respectable individuals can harbor deadly secrets. With each twist and turn, the detective must rely on his keen intellect and astute observations to solve the puzzle and bring the culprit to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Class divide, Intrigue
