Synopsis: 

In 'Uncle Sam's Boys as Lieutenants; or, Serving Old Glory as Line Officers', author H. Irving Hancock takes readers on an exhilarating journey alongside a group of young men who have just been promoted to the rank of lieutenants in the United States Army. As they navigate the challenges and responsibilities of their new positions, these brave soldiers find themselves thrust into the heart of a dangerous conflict that tests their courage, loyalty, and leadership skills. From intense battles to unexpected alliances, the story unfolds with gripping suspense, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of these young lieutenants and the impact they will have on the outcome of the war.

Genres: Adventure, War, Historical Fiction

Themes: Patriotism, Duty, Camaraderie, Leadership
