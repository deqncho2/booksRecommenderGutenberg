Synopsis: 
In "A Year Among the Trees: or, The Woods and By-Ways of New England," Flagg, Wilson takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of New England. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, the author explores the hidden wonders of the region's forests, revealing the beauty and mysteries that lie within. From the changing seasons to encounters with wildlife, Flagg's observations offer a unique perspective on the natural world, inviting readers to appreciate the tranquility and harmony found in the woods and by-ways of New England.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature Writing, Travelogue

Themes: Nature appreciation, Environmentalism, Connection with the natural world
