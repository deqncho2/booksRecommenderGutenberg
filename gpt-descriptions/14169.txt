Synopsis: Ethel Hollister is back for another thrilling summer as a Campfire Girl, filled with new adventures, challenges, and unexpected friendships. As Ethel navigates the joys and trials of camp life, she discovers the power of teamwork, the importance of embracing diversity, and the true meaning of friendship. With each passing day, Ethel's confidence grows, and she learns valuable life lessons that will shape her future. Join Ethel on her unforgettable journey as she embarks on her second summer as a Campfire Girl.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Self-discovery
