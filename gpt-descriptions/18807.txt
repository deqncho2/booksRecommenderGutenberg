Synopsis: In "He Walked Around the Horses" by H. Beam Piper, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, bringing with him an air of intrigue and danger. As the townspeople become increasingly curious about his true identity, they uncover a web of secrets and hidden agendas that could change their lives forever. With suspenseful twists and unexpected revelations, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Western

Themes: Identity, Deception, Small-town dynamics
