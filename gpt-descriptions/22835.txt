Synopsis: In "The London Visitor" by Mary Russell Mitford, a mysterious stranger arrives in London, captivating the attention of the city's inhabitants. As the visitor's true identity remains shrouded in secrecy, the lives of those who encounter them are forever changed. From the bustling streets to the elegant drawing rooms, this captivating tale explores the intertwining lives of London's diverse residents and the unexpected connections that bind them together.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Social class, Human connection
