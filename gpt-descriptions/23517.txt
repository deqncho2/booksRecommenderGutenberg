Synopsis: In the heart of New York City's tenements, a young girl named Maggie finds herself caught between the harsh realities of poverty and the glimmers of hope that surround her. As she navigates the crowded streets and crowded apartments, she encounters a mysterious figure known as the Angel of the Tenement, who offers her a chance at a better life. But as Maggie delves deeper into the secrets of the Angel, she must confront the sacrifices she is willing to make for her own survival and the well-being of those she loves.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Poverty, Hope, Sacrifice, Survival
