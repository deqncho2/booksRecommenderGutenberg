Synopsis: In 'Music in Medicine' by Sidney Licht, a renowned neurologist embarks on a groundbreaking journey to explore the profound impact of music on the human brain. As he delves into the fascinating world of music therapy, he uncovers unexpected connections between melodies and healing, leading to a series of remarkable discoveries that challenge conventional medical practices. With captivating anecdotes and scientific insights, this book takes readers on an enlightening exploration of the transformative power of music in the realm of medicine.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Music

Themes: Music therapy, Neurology, Healing, Scientific exploration
