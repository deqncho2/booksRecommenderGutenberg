Synopsis: In 'Diary of an Enlisted Man' by Van Alstyne, Lawrence, we follow the gripping and emotional journey of a young soldier as he navigates the harsh realities of war. Through the pages of his diary, we witness the intense camaraderie, the heart-wrenching losses, and the profound personal growth that comes with serving on the front lines. As the soldier's experiences unfold, we are drawn into a world of sacrifice, resilience, and the indomitable human spirit. This poignant and thought-provoking narrative offers a unique perspective on the true cost of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Literature, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Personal Growth
