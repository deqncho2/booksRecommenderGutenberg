Synopsis: 

'Dictionary of Battles: From the Earliest Date to the Present Time' by Thomas Benfield Harbottle is an enthralling and comprehensive reference book that takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of warfare. This meticulously researched work provides a detailed account of numerous battles, from ancient times to the present day, offering a wealth of information on the strategies, tactics, and key figures involved in each conflict. From epic clashes that shaped the course of nations to lesser-known skirmishes that had significant consequences, this dictionary brings to life the thrilling and often brutal nature of warfare throughout history. Whether you are a history enthusiast, a military buff, or simply curious about the human experience in times of conflict, this book is sure to captivate and educate.

Genres: Reference, History, Military

Themes: Warfare, Strategy, Historical Events
