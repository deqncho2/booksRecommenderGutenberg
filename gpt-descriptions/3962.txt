Synopsis: 
In 'A Romance of Youth — Complete' by François Coppée, we are introduced to a young artist named André, who finds himself captivated by the beauty and charm of a mysterious woman he encounters on the streets of Paris. As he becomes increasingly infatuated with her, André embarks on a journey of self-discovery, navigating the complexities of love, passion, and the pursuit of artistic fulfillment. Set against the backdrop of bohemian Paris in the late 19th century, this captivating tale explores the transformative power of youthful romance and the sacrifices one is willing to make for the sake of love.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Historical fiction

Themes: Love and infatuation, Artistic pursuit, Self-discovery
