Synopsis: In 'Penguin Persons & Peppermints' by Eaton, Walter Prichard, a mysterious invitation leads a group of strangers to a secluded island where they are confronted with a series of perplexing puzzles and enigmatic challenges. As they navigate through the island's secrets, they discover that their lives are intertwined in unexpected ways, and that the true purpose of their gathering goes far beyond mere entertainment. With each twist and turn, the characters must confront their own fears and desires, ultimately leading them to question the nature of reality itself.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Fate, Connection
