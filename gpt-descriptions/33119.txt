Synopsis: 

In "Battery E in France: 149th Field Artillery, Rainbow (42nd) Division" by Frederic Richard Kilner, readers are transported to the battlefields of World War I. Through the eyes of the soldiers of Battery E, a unit of the 149th Field Artillery in the renowned Rainbow Division, the author vividly depicts the harrowing experiences, camaraderie, and sacrifices made by these brave men during their time in France. As they face the horrors of war, the soldiers of Battery E must navigate the complexities of military life, forge unbreakable bonds, and confront their own fears and doubts. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction

Themes: War, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Courage
