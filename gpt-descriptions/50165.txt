Synopsis: In 'The Flying Machine Boys on Duty; Or, The Clue Above the Clouds' by Frank Walton, a group of adventurous young aviators find themselves embarking on a thrilling mission that takes them high above the clouds. As they navigate through dangerous skies and encounter unexpected challenges, they must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and teamwork to uncover a mysterious clue that holds the key to a hidden secret. With their flying skills put to the test, the boys embark on an exhilarating journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
