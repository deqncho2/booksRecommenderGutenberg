Synopsis: 
'Aphorisms and Reflections from the Works of T. H. Huxley' is a collection of thought-provoking and insightful writings by the renowned scientist and philosopher, Thomas Henry Huxley. This compilation offers a glimpse into Huxley's brilliant mind as he explores various topics such as science, religion, ethics, and the nature of knowledge. Through concise and powerful aphorisms, Huxley challenges conventional wisdom and encourages readers to question their beliefs, ultimately leading them on a journey of intellectual discovery.

Genres: Philosophy, Science, Non-fiction

Themes: Knowledge and skepticism, Science and religion, Ethics and morality
