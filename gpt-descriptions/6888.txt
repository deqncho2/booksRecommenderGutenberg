Synopsis: In 'History of the Gatling Gun Detachment, Fifth Army Corps, at Santiago: With a Few Unvarnished Truths Concerning that Expedition', John H. Parker takes us on a thrilling journey through the intense battles and harrowing experiences of the Gatling Gun Detachment during the Santiago campaign. From the scorching heat of the Cuban jungles to the heart-pounding moments on the front lines, Parker's vivid storytelling brings to life the bravery and resilience of these soldiers as they face the challenges of war. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping account will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the untold truths of this historic expedition.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History, Memoir

Themes: War and Conflict, Bravery and Resilience, Historical Accuracy
