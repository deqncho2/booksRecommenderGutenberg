Synopsis: In "The Riddle of the Rangeland" by Parkhill Forbes, a young rancher named Jake finds himself entangled in a mysterious series of events when his cattle start disappearing without a trace. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances that threaten not only his livelihood but also his life. With time running out, Jake must navigate the treacherous rangeland and solve the riddle before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Western, Thriller

Themes: Betrayal, Survival, Justice
