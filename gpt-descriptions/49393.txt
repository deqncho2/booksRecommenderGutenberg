Synopsis: In 'A History of the War of 1812-15 Between the United States and Great Britain,' Johnson, Rossiter takes readers on a captivating journey through one of the most pivotal conflicts in American history. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, the author delves into the political intrigues, military strategies, and personal stories that shaped this war. From the burning of Washington, D.C., to the iconic Battle of New Orleans, this book offers a comprehensive account of the events that unfolded during this tumultuous period. Through vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, Johnson, Rossiter brings to life the heroes, villains, and ordinary individuals who played a role in this epic struggle. 

Genres: History, Military History, Non-fiction

Themes: National identity, Diplomacy, Military strategy, Patriotism, Conflict and its consequences
