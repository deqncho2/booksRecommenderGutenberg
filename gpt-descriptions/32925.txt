Synopsis: In 'The King's Assegai: A Matabili Story' by Mitford, Bertram, we are transported to the heart of Africa, where a young Englishman finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger. As he becomes entangled in the power struggles of the Matabili tribe, he must navigate treacherous alliances, face deadly adversaries, and uncover the secrets that lie hidden within the kingdom. With vivid descriptions of the African landscape and a gripping narrative, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Power struggles, Cultural clashes, Exploration of African history and culture
