Synopsis: In 'Summer' by Dallas Lore Sharp, a small New England town becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of mystery and intrigue. As the summer heat intensifies, secrets begin to unravel, and the residents find themselves caught in a web of unexpected events. With each passing day, the tension builds, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Small-town dynamics, Unpredictability
