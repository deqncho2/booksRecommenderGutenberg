Synopsis: 
In 'The Adventures of a Freshman' by Jesse Lynch Williams, follow the captivating journey of a young college freshman as he navigates the thrilling and often unpredictable world of university life. From the excitement of newfound freedom to the challenges of academic pressure and social expectations, our protagonist's experiences will keep readers eagerly turning the pages. As he encounters a colorful cast of characters, gets involved in various escapades, and learns valuable life lessons, the freshman's story unfolds with humor, heart, and a touch of nostalgia. 

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Comedy, Drama

Themes: 
Identity, Friendship, Self-discovery
