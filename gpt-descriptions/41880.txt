Synopsis: In the enchanting world of Wild Folk, a young girl named Ophelia discovers a hidden realm filled with magical creatures and embarks on a thrilling adventure to save it from an ancient evil. Alongside her newfound friends, Ophelia must navigate treacherous landscapes, unravel ancient mysteries, and harness her own inner strength to protect the wild and preserve the balance between humans and nature. With captivating storytelling and vivid imagery, Wild Folk takes readers on a mesmerizing journey that will leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Nature conservation, Friendship, Self-discovery
