Synopsis: 

In 'Garcia the Centenarian and His Times: Being a Memoir of Manuel Garcia's Life and Labours for the Advancement of Music and Science', author Malcolm Sterling takes us on a captivating journey through the extraordinary life of Manuel Garcia, a centenarian who dedicated his life to the pursuit of music and science. From his humble beginnings to his remarkable achievements, this memoir unveils the untold story of a man whose contributions shaped the world of music and science in ways we could never have imagined.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Music

Themes: Perseverance, Innovation, Legacy
