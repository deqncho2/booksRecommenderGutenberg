Synopsis: In the midst of the picturesque village of Holmfirth, a devastating flood strikes, leaving behind a trail of destruction and heartbreak. Amidst the chaos, a young foundling named Tom Pinder emerges, his mysterious past shrouded in secrecy. As he navigates the aftermath of the flood, Tom finds himself entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal, all while searching for his true identity. With the village in turmoil and the truth waiting to be uncovered, Tom's journey becomes a captivating tale of resilience, redemption, and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Secrets, Love, Betrayal
