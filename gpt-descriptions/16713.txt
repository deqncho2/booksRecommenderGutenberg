Synopsis: 
Prepare to be captivated by the mind-bending puzzles and perplexing riddles that await you in 'Amusements in Mathematics' by Henry Ernest Dudeney. This collection of mathematical brain teasers will challenge your logical thinking and test your problem-solving skills to the limit. From cunning chess problems to intricate number puzzles, each page offers a new and exciting challenge that will leave you both frustrated and exhilarated. With a delightful blend of wit and ingenuity, Dudeney invites you to embark on a journey of intellectual amusement that will keep you guessing until the very end.

Genres: Puzzle, Mathematics, Entertainment

Themes: Logical thinking, Problem-solving, Intellectual amusement
