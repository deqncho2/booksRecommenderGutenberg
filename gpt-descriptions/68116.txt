Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 150, July 24, 1915: The House of Fear; or, Nick Carter's Counterstroke,' renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he receives a cryptic message from an old friend. As he delves deeper into the case, Carter discovers a sinister plot involving a haunted house, a secret society, and a deadly game of cat and mouse. With his quick wit and unparalleled detective skills, Carter must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and confront his most formidable adversary yet.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Detective Fiction

Themes: Crime-solving, Danger, Deception, Courage
