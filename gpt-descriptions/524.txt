Synopsis:
Ann Veronica is a young, intelligent, and independent woman living in Edwardian England. Frustrated by the limitations placed upon her by society, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and rebellion. As she navigates the complexities of love, politics, and personal freedom, Ann Veronica finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires. With a cast of vibrant characters and a captivating narrative, this modern love story explores the challenges faced by women in a time of social change and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres:
Fiction, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes:
Gender roles, Individualism, Women's rights, Love and relationships
