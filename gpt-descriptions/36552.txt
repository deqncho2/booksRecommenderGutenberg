Synopsis:
In "York Minster" by Arthur P. Purey-Cust, a captivating tale unfolds within the grandeur of one of England's most iconic cathedrals. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows a young architect named Edward as he embarks on a journey to restore the magnificent York Minster to its former glory. As Edward delves deeper into the secrets and mysteries hidden within the cathedral's ancient walls, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, romance, and danger. With each discovery, Edward's perception of the world around him is challenged, leading him to question his own beliefs and the true purpose of his mission. As he unravels the enigmatic history of York Minster, Edward must confront his own demons and make choices that will shape not only his own destiny but also the fate of the cathedral itself.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Architecture, History, Secrets, Redemption
