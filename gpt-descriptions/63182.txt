Synopsis: In 'Pat the Lighthouse Boy' by Evelyn Everett-Green, a young boy named Pat finds himself drawn to the mysterious lighthouse that overlooks his coastal village. As he embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets hidden within its walls, Pat discovers a world of adventure, friendship, and unexpected twists. With each step he takes, Pat's courage and determination are tested, leading him to uncover truths that will change his life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Discovery
