Synopsis: 
In "Religion in Earnest: A Memorial of Mrs. Mary Lyth, of York," the author, John Lyth, presents a captivating account of the life and spiritual journey of Mrs. Mary Lyth. Through a series of personal anecdotes and reflections, the book delves into the profound impact of Mrs. Lyth's unwavering faith and her dedication to spreading the message of Christianity. As the narrative unfolds, readers are drawn into a world of devotion, perseverance, and the transformative power of religious conviction. 

Genres: Biography, Memoir, Religious Literature

Themes: Faith, Devotion, Spiritual Transformation
