Synopsis: In 'The Seven Purposes: An Experience in Psychic Phenomena', Margaret Cameron takes readers on a captivating journey into the realm of psychic phenomena. When she unexpectedly finds herself connected to a group of individuals with extraordinary abilities, Margaret embarks on a quest to uncover the seven purposes that guide their unique powers. As she delves deeper into this mysterious world, Margaret's own perceptions and beliefs are challenged, leading her to question the very nature of reality. With each revelation, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden agendas, where the line between the physical and the metaphysical blurs. This thought-provoking and suspenseful exploration of psychic phenomena will leave readers questioning the boundaries of human potential.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Psychological

Themes: Psychic abilities, Perception, Reality
