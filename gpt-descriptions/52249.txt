Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Third, or Prince of Wales' Regiment of Dragoon Guards," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment. From its inception in 1685 to its exploits up until 1838, Cannon delves into the regiment's rich history, chronicling its triumphs, challenges, and the remarkable individuals who shaped its legacy. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the world of the Third Dragoon Guards, shedding light on their pivotal role in various conflicts and their enduring impact on military history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Military valor, Regimental pride, Historical legacy
