Synopsis: 

In 'Five Months on a German Raider: Being the Adventures of an Englishman Captured by the 'Wolf'', Frederic George Trayes takes readers on a thrilling journey through his harrowing experiences as a captive on a German raider during World War I. From the moment he is captured, Trayes finds himself immersed in a world of danger, espionage, and survival. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the Atlantic, he must constantly outwit his captors and find ways to communicate with the outside world. With each passing day, the tension rises, and Trayes must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to stay alive. Will he manage to escape the clutches of the 'Wolf' and make it back home?

Genres: Adventure, Memoir, Historical

Themes: Survival, Espionage, War
