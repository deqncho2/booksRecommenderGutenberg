Synopsis: In 'Arethusa' by F. Marion Crawford, a young American artist named Paul Griggs finds himself captivated by the mysterious beauty of a woman he encounters in Rome. As he becomes increasingly obsessed with her, he embarks on a journey that takes him from the bustling streets of the Eternal City to the tranquil landscapes of the Italian countryside. Along the way, he uncovers secrets and encounters unexpected challenges that test his determination and love. This gripping tale explores the power of passion, the complexities of human relationships, and the pursuit of one's desires.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Obsession, Identity, Love and Desire
