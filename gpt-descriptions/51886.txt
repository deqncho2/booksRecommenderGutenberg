Synopsis: In the small town of Caxton, a mysterious event takes place that leaves the community in shock and confusion. As the townspeople grapple with the aftermath, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of human resilience is revealed. McDermott's gripping tale explores the depths of human emotions and the power of community in the face of adversity.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and lies, Community, Resilience
