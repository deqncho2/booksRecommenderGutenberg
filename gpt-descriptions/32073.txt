Synopsis: In 'The Regular Brigade of the Fourteenth Army Corps, the Army of the Cumberland, in the Battle of Stone River, or Murfreesboro', Tennessee', Frederick Phisterer takes readers on a gripping journey through the intense Battle of Stone River during the American Civil War. Through vivid descriptions and firsthand accounts, Phisterer brings to life the bravery and sacrifices of the Regular Brigade as they face the horrors of war and fight for their cause.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, War memoir

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, The horrors of war
