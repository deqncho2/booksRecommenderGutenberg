Synopsis: 
"The Siege of Kimberley: Its Humorous and Social Side; Anglo-Boer War (1899-1902); Eighteen Weeks in Eighteen Chapters" by Phelan, T. takes readers on a captivating journey through the events of the Anglo-Boer War, specifically focusing on the siege of Kimberley. Through a unique blend of humor and social commentary, the author provides an engaging account of the experiences and challenges faced by the people of Kimberley during this tumultuous time. With each chapter representing a week of the siege, the narrative unfolds with a mix of wit, anecdotes, and insightful observations, offering a fresh perspective on the war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Satire

Themes: Resilience, Community, Absurdity of War
