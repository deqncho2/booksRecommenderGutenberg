Synopsis: In 'Translations from the German (Vol 3 of 3): Tales by Musæus, Tieck, Richter', Thomas Carlyle presents a collection of captivating tales by renowned German authors. From the enchanting folklore of Musæus to the imaginative narratives of Tieck and the whimsical stories of Jean Paul, this volume takes readers on a journey through the rich tapestry of German literature. Each tale is filled with mystery, romance, and unexpected twists, transporting readers to fantastical worlds and exploring the depths of human emotions. 

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Romance

Themes: Folklore, Imagination, Love, Human Nature
