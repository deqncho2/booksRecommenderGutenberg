Synopsis: In 'Rich Living' by Cathal, Michael, a young woman named Emma finds herself thrust into a world of opulence and luxury when she unexpectedly inherits a vast fortune. As she navigates this new lifestyle, Emma becomes entangled in a web of secrets, deceit, and dangerous alliances. With her newfound wealth comes a price she never anticipated, forcing her to question the true meaning of happiness and the lengths she is willing to go to maintain her rich living.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Wealth and its consequences, Identity and self-discovery, Morality and ethics
