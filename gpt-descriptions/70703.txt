Synopsis: In "Christmas Builders" by Jefferson, Charles Edward, a group of unlikely friends come together to save their beloved community center from being demolished. With the clock ticking and the holiday season in full swing, they embark on a heartwarming and humorous journey filled with unexpected challenges and surprising discoveries. As they work together to restore the center, they not only find the true meaning of Christmas but also learn valuable lessons about friendship, perseverance, and the power of community.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Drama

Themes: Friendship, Community, Perseverance, Christmas Spirit
