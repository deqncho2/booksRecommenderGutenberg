Synopsis: In 'Why Lincoln Laughed' by Russell H. Conwell, delve into the intriguing world of Abraham Lincoln as he navigates the challenges of his presidency with a unique sense of humor. This captivating book explores the untold stories behind Lincoln's laughter, revealing the unexpected role it played in his leadership and the impact it had on those around him. As you journey through the pages, you'll uncover the fascinating anecdotes and witty remarks that shaped Lincoln's character, shedding light on the man behind the iconic figure. Prepare to be captivated by the enigmatic laughter of one of America's greatest leaders.

Genres: Biography, History, Humor

Themes: Leadership, Humor, Character Development
