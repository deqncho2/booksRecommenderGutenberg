Synopsis: In 'The Blockade Runners' by Jules Verne, a young American adventurer finds himself caught in the midst of the American Civil War. With his daring spirit and thirst for adventure, he becomes entangled in a dangerous mission to break through the Union blockade and deliver vital supplies to the Confederacy. As he navigates treacherous waters and encounters unexpected allies and enemies, the protagonist must confront the moral dilemmas of war and the lengths one will go to for their beliefs.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Morality, Loyalty, Conflict
