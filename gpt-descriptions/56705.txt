Synopsis: 
In "Mrs. Essington: The Romance of a House-party," the charming Mrs. Essington invites a group of diverse and intriguing guests to her lavish country estate for a weekend of revelry and romance. As the guests arrive, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and unexpected connections are formed. Amidst the backdrop of luxurious parties, scandalous affairs, and hidden desires, the characters navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations. With each passing day, the house-party becomes a melting pot of passion, intrigue, and unexpected twists that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the ultimate outcome.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Love and desire, Social expectations, Secrets and revelations
