Synopsis: In 'My Attainment of the Pole', Frederick Albert Cook takes readers on a thrilling journey to the unexplored boreal center, documenting the groundbreaking expedition that achieved this remarkable feat between 1907 and 1909. As the story unfolds, Cook's account becomes entangled in a polar controversy, leaving readers questioning the truth behind his claims. This gripping narrative will captivate readers as they delve into the mysteries of the polar regions and the controversies that surround them.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Truth and Deception
