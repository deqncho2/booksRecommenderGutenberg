Synopsis: In 'Space Nomads: Meteorites in Sky, Field, and Laboratory', LaPaz and Lincoln LaPaz take readers on an exhilarating journey through the world of meteorites. From the vastness of space to the depths of the Earth, they explore the mysteries and wonders of these celestial rocks. With captivating anecdotes and scientific expertise, the authors delve into the thrilling adventures of hunting, studying, and analyzing meteorites, uncovering secrets that have been hidden for millions of years. Prepare to be amazed by the extraordinary tales of these space nomads and the incredible insights they provide into our universe.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Adventure

Themes: Astronomy, Geology, Exploration
