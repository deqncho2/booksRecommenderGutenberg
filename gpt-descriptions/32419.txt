Synopsis: 
'The Constant Couple; Or, A Trip to the Jubilee: A Comedy, in Five Acts' by George Farquhar is a delightful and witty play that follows the adventures of Sir Harry Wildair, a charming and audacious gentleman, as he embarks on a journey to the Jubilee celebration. Along the way, he encounters a variety of colorful characters, including his love interest, Lady Lurewell, and his mischievous friend, Colonel Standard. As the plot unfolds, secrets are revealed, disguises are donned, and hilarious misunderstandings ensue, all leading to a climactic and uproarious finale. 

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and desire, Deception and disguise, Social class and status
