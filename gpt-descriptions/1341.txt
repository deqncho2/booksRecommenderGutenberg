Synopsis: In 'The Altruist in Politics' by Benjamin N. Cardozo, a brilliant and idealistic politician finds himself torn between his personal convictions and the harsh realities of the political world. As he navigates the treacherous waters of power and influence, he must confront the ethical dilemmas that arise when one's altruistic intentions clash with the compromises demanded by politics. With captivating characters and thought-provoking twists, this gripping tale explores the complexities of morality and the sacrifices one must make in the pursuit of a better society.

Genres: Political fiction, Drama, Moral dilemma

Themes: Altruism, Politics, Ethics, Compromise
