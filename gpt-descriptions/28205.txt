Synopsis: In "Washington Crossing the Delaware" by Henry Fisk Carlton, a gripping historical fiction novel, the reader is transported to the pivotal moment in American history when General George Washington and his troops embark on a daring mission to cross the icy Delaware River. As the story unfolds, the reader is immersed in the challenges, sacrifices, and triumphs of the soldiers who fought for the birth of a nation. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel brings to life the untold stories behind one of the most iconic events in American history.

Genres: Historical fiction, War, Adventure

Themes: Patriotism, Leadership, Sacrifice, Perseverance
