Synopsis: In the small New England town of Pembroke, secrets and scandals lurk beneath the surface of a seemingly idyllic community. As the lives of its residents intertwine, a mysterious newcomer arrives, stirring up old wounds and igniting forbidden desires. With its richly drawn characters and captivating storytelling, 'Pembroke: A Novel' delves into the complexities of human relationships, the power of secrets, and the consequences of hidden desires.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Small-town dynamics, Secrets and scandals, Forbidden desires
