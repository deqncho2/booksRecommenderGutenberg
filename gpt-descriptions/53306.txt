Synopsis: 
In 'Little Prudy's Captain Horace' by Sophie May, young Prudy embarks on a thrilling adventure when she meets the charming Captain Horace. As they set sail on a mysterious voyage, Prudy discovers hidden treasures, encounters dangerous pirates, and learns valuable life lessons along the way. With its delightful blend of adventure, humor, and heartwarming moments, this enchanting tale will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Adventure, Children's Literature, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
