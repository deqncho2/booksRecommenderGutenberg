Synopsis: 

'Masterpieces of Mystery in Four Volumes: Detective Stories' is a captivating collection of thrilling tales that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. From the mind of renowned author, Joseph Lewis French, this anthology brings together a diverse range of detective stories that delve into the darkest corners of human nature. Each story presents a unique mystery, filled with unexpected twists and turns, as brilliant detectives unravel perplexing cases and uncover hidden truths. From locked room mysteries to intricate puzzles, this collection offers a mesmerizing journey through the world of crime and investigation. 

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Human nature, Deception, Justice, Puzzle-solving
