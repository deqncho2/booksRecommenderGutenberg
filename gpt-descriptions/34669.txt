Synopsis: 
In 'Lost Pond' by Abbott, Henry, a young woman named Lily finds herself mysteriously drawn to a secluded pond hidden deep within the forest. As she explores its enchanting surroundings, she uncovers a series of strange occurrences that defy explanation. With each visit, Lily becomes more entangled in the pond's secrets, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and uncovering the truth behind its mystical powers. As she delves deeper into the pond's mysteries, Lily must confront her own fears and desires, ultimately questioning the boundaries between reality and imagination.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Self-discovery, Nature's power, Blurring of reality and fantasy
