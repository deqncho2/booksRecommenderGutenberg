Synopsis: 
In 'My Day: Reminiscences of a Long Life', Sara Agnes Rice Pryor takes us on a captivating journey through her extraordinary life, filled with remarkable experiences and encounters with influential figures of her time. From her childhood in the antebellum South to her involvement in the Civil War and her later years as a prominent writer and socialite, Pryor's memoir offers a unique perspective on the tumultuous events that shaped American history. With vivid storytelling and heartfelt reflections, she provides a glimpse into a bygone era, revealing the triumphs and challenges of a woman who defied societal expectations and left an indelible mark on her world.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction, Biography

Themes: Personal resilience, Historical events and their impact, Gender roles and societal expectations
