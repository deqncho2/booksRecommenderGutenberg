Synopsis: 
"At the Back of the North Wind" by George MacDonald is a captivating tale that follows the adventures of a young boy named Diamond. One night, Diamond encounters the mystical North Wind, who takes him on extraordinary journeys to enchanted lands. As Diamond navigates through both the real and fantastical worlds, he learns valuable lessons about life, love, and the power of the human spirit. This enchanting story is filled with magical encounters, heartwarming moments, and thought-provoking insights that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: The power of imagination, the beauty of innocence, the interconnectedness of life
