Synopsis: 
In 'Between You and Me' by Harry Lauder, Sir, a renowned actor finds himself caught in a web of secrets and deception when he becomes entangled with a mysterious woman. As he delves deeper into her enigmatic past, he uncovers shocking truths that threaten to unravel his own life. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate a treacherous path of love, betrayal, and redemption, ultimately questioning the very nature of trust and loyalty.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Deception, Identity, Redemption
