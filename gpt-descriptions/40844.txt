Synopsis: In 'The Diva's Ruby' by F. Marion Crawford, a renowned opera singer receives a mysterious ruby as a gift, setting off a chain of events that lead her on a thrilling journey through the glamorous world of the opera, filled with secrets, romance, and danger. As she delves deeper into the origins of the ruby, she uncovers a web of intrigue that threatens not only her career but also her life. With the help of a dashing detective, she must navigate the treacherous path of deception and betrayal to uncover the truth behind the precious gem.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Ambition and fame, Love and sacrifice
