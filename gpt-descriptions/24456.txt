Synopsis: In "Fathers of Biology" by Charles McRae, embark on a captivating journey through the lives of some of the greatest minds in the field of biology. From ancient Greece to the Renaissance, this book unveils the remarkable stories of these visionary thinkers who revolutionized our understanding of life itself. Discover the struggles, triumphs, and groundbreaking discoveries that shaped the course of scientific history, as these brilliant individuals challenged conventional wisdom and paved the way for modern biology. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, History

Themes: Scientific discovery, Intellectual curiosity, Legacy
