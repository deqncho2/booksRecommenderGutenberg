Synopsis: 

In "Poultry diseases: Causes, symptoms and treatment, with notes on post-mortem examinations," E. Jocelyn Wortley delves into the fascinating world of poultry diseases, providing a comprehensive guide to understanding their causes, identifying symptoms, and implementing effective treatments. This invaluable resource also includes insightful notes on post-mortem examinations, shedding light on the importance of understanding the underlying causes of poultry diseases. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Veterinary Medicine, Animal Health

Themes: Disease prevention, Animal welfare, Veterinary diagnostics
