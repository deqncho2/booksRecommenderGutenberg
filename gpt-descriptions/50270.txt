Synopsis: In 'The Delectable Mountains' by Arthur Colton, a young woman named Mary embarks on a journey to the mysterious Delectable Mountains, a place rumored to hold the key to eternal happiness. As she navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters enigmatic characters, Mary's perception of reality is challenged, leading her to question the true nature of happiness and the sacrifices one must make to attain it. With each step closer to the mountains, Mary's understanding of herself and the world around her is transformed, culminating in a breathtaking revelation that will leave readers pondering the meaning of true fulfillment.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Self-discovery, Perception of reality, Pursuit of happiness
