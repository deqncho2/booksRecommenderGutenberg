Synopsis: 
In 'A Lieutenant at Eighteen' by Optic, Oliver, we follow the journey of a young protagonist who unexpectedly finds himself thrust into the world of military service at a tender age. As he navigates the challenges and responsibilities of his new role, he must confront the harsh realities of war, forge new friendships, and discover his own strength and courage. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the transformative power of adversity and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: War, Friendship, Resilience
