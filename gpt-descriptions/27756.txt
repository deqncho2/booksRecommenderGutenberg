Synopsis: In 'Revenge' by Arthur Porges, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of deceit and betrayal after a chance encounter with a mysterious stranger. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of his own past, he becomes consumed by a thirst for vengeance that threatens to consume him entirely. With each twist and turn, the line between justice and revenge blurs, leaving the reader questioning the true nature of right and wrong.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Revenge, Betrayal, Morality
