Synopsis: 

'Alfgar the Dane or the Second Chronicle of Aescendune: A Tale of the Days of Edmund Ironside' by A. D. Crake is a gripping historical novel set in the tumultuous era of Edmund Ironside. The story follows Alfgar, a young Dane who finds himself torn between his loyalty to his Viking heritage and his growing love for the people of Aescendune. As war looms and alliances shift, Alfgar must navigate treacherous waters to protect those he holds dear and ultimately discover where his true allegiance lies.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
