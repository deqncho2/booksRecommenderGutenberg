Synopsis: In 'The Standard Household-Effect Company', a young man named Tom Corey finds himself caught between his desire for success and his longing for love. When he joins a company that specializes in selling household goods, he becomes entangled in a web of ambition, deceit, and unexpected romance. As Tom navigates the cutthroat world of business, he must confront his own values and make difficult choices that will shape his future. With its captivating characters and intricate plot, this novel explores the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Realistic fiction, Romance, Satire

Themes: Ambition, Love, Morality, Social class
