Synopsis: 

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the rich history of Jamaica with 'Historic Jamaica' by Frank Cundall. This captivating book takes readers on a journey through the island's past, uncovering hidden stories, forgotten landmarks, and the vibrant tapestry of Jamaican culture. From the colonial era to the struggles for independence, Cundall's meticulous research and vivid descriptions bring the history of Jamaica to life. Discover the untold tales of influential figures, explore the architectural wonders of the past, and delve into the social and political dynamics that have shaped the nation. 'Historic Jamaica' is a captivating exploration of a nation's heritage that will leave readers yearning to learn more.

Genres: History, Non-fiction, Travel

Themes: Jamaican history, Cultural heritage, Architecture, Colonialism, Independence
