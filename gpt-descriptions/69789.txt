Synopsis: In 'The Fellowship of the Frog' by Wallace, Edgar, a notorious criminal mastermind known as The Frog is wreaking havoc in London, leaving a trail of mystery and destruction in his wake. As Detective Inspector Elk and his team race against time to catch The Frog, they find themselves entangled in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, the true identity of The Frog becomes more elusive, and the stakes higher than ever before. Will Elk and his team be able to outsmart this cunning adversary, or will The Frog's reign of terror continue?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Identity, Justice
