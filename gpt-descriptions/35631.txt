Synopsis: In 'A Letter on Shakspere's Authorship of The Two Noble Kinsmen', Spalding delves into the mysterious origins of one of Shakespeare's lesser-known plays, questioning whether the Bard was truly the sole author. Through meticulous analysis of the play's style and themes, Spalding uncovers intriguing clues that challenge the traditional attribution. As he unravels the enigma surrounding Shakespeare's authorship, the reader is taken on a captivating journey of literary investigation and discovery.

Genres: Literary criticism, Shakespearean studies, Historical analysis

Themes: Authorship, Shakespearean canon, Literary analysis
