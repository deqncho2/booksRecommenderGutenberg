Synopsis: In the small village of Marbeck, a mysterious inn stands as a haven for those seeking refuge from their troubled pasts. As the innkeeper, Mr. Marbeck, welcomes a diverse group of guests, each with their own secrets and desires, the tranquil facade of the inn begins to unravel. With the arrival of a charismatic stranger, tensions rise and long-buried secrets threaten to be exposed, forcing the guests to confront their pasts and make choices that will forever alter their lives.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, Redemption, Identity, Consequences of one's actions
