Synopsis: 
In 'A Collection of Ballads' by Lang, Andrew, readers are transported to a world of enchanting tales and captivating narratives. This collection brings together a diverse range of ballads, each with its own unique story and lyrical beauty. From ancient legends to haunting love stories, the ballads explore themes of heroism, tragedy, and the power of human emotions. With vivid imagery and melodic verses, Lang weaves a tapestry of emotions that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Poetry, Folklore, Fantasy

Themes: Love, Heroism, Tragedy, Emotions
