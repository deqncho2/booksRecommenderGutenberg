Synopsis: In 'St. Clair's Defeat', the Public Library of Fort Wayne and Allen County takes readers on a captivating journey through a little-known historical event. Set in the late 18th century, this gripping narrative unveils the true story of a military expedition gone awry, led by General Arthur St. Clair. As the pages turn, readers will be immersed in the harrowing events that unfolded during this fateful battle, filled with unexpected twists and turns. With meticulous research and vivid storytelling, the Public Library of Fort Wayne and Allen County brings to life the bravery, sacrifice, and resilience of those involved in this forgotten chapter of American history.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History, American History

Themes: Courage, Leadership, Historical Perspectives
