Synopsis: 

"The Religion of the Samurai: A Study of Zen Philosophy and Discipline in China and Japan" takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of Zen philosophy and the disciplined way of life followed by the samurai in China and Japan. Through insightful analysis and historical anecdotes, Nukariya, Kaiten explores the profound teachings of Zen and its impact on the lives of these revered warriors. From the origins of Zen in China to its development and practice in Japan, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the spiritual and philosophical aspects of the samurai's way of life.

Genres: Philosophy, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Zen philosophy, Samurai culture, Discipline, Spiritual enlightenment
