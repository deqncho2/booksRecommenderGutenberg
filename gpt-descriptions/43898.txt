Synopsis: In "Florence Nightingale, the Angel of the Crimea: A Story for Young People," Laura Elizabeth Howe Richards brings to life the inspiring story of Florence Nightingale, a remarkable woman who defied societal expectations and dedicated her life to nursing. Set against the backdrop of the Crimean War, this captivating biography follows Florence's journey from a privileged upbringing to her transformative experiences on the battlefield. As she faces numerous challenges and overcomes adversity, Florence's unwavering determination and compassion shine through, making her a true hero and a beacon of hope for generations to come.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Compassion, Gender Roles, Social Change
