Synopsis: In 'The Man with a Secret: A Novel' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious man arrives in a small English village, harboring a hidden past and a secret that could shatter the lives of those around him. As the townspeople become increasingly curious about his true identity, they uncover a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected connections that will keep readers guessing until the very end. With its gripping plot and complex characters, this thrilling mystery will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the truth.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal
