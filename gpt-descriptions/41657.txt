Synopsis: 
In "To Geyserland: Union Pacific-Oregon Short Line Railroads to the Yellowstone National Park," author Edward F. Colborn takes readers on a captivating journey through the scenic landscapes and thrilling adventures of the Yellowstone National Park. Through vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, Colborn explores the fascinating history of the Union Pacific-Oregon Short Line Railroads and their role in opening up this natural wonder to the world. From the awe-inspiring geysers to the majestic wildlife, readers will be transported to a world of natural beauty and excitement.

Genres: Travel, History, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Conservation, Natural Wonders
