Synopsis: In 'The Hall and the Grange: A Novel' by Marshall, Archibald, a mysterious inheritance leads a young woman on a journey to uncover the secrets of her family's past. As she delves deeper into the history of the Hall and the Grange, she becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and hidden treasures. With each revelation, she must confront the ghosts of the past and make choices that will shape her future. This captivating tale of mystery and romance will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and betrayal
