Synopsis: In 'Trial and Triumph' by Frances Ellen Watkins Harper, a young woman named Virginia embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience amidst the challenges of the Civil War era. As she navigates through personal trials and societal expectations, Virginia's determination and strength are tested, leading her to unexpected triumphs and revelations. This captivating novel explores the complexities of love, identity, and the pursuit of freedom in a time of great turmoil.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Romance

Themes: Resilience, Self-discovery, Love, Identity, Freedom
