Synopsis: In 'The Dream-God, or, A Singular Evolvement of Thought in Sleep' by Cuningham, John, a young artist finds himself trapped in a mesmerizing world where dreams and reality intertwine. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic realm of his own mind, he uncovers hidden truths and encounters extraordinary beings that challenge his perception of existence. With each dream, the artist's journey becomes more captivating and perilous, blurring the line between imagination and actuality. This mesmerizing tale will transport readers to a realm where the boundaries of consciousness are pushed to their limits.

Genres: Fantasy, Psychological Fiction, Surrealism

Themes: Dreams and Reality, Perception, Identity, Exploration of the Subconscious
