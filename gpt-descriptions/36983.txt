Synopsis:
"The Life of Mr. Richard Savage: Who was Condemn'd with Mr. James Gregory, the last Sessions at the Old Baily, for the Murder of Mr. James Sinclair, at Robinson's Coffee-house at Charing-Cross" is a gripping true story that delves into the scandalous life of Richard Savage, a man accused of a heinous crime. Set in 18th century London, this captivating narrative explores the events leading up to the murder and the subsequent trial that shocked society. As the layers of Savage's complex personality are peeled away, the reader is left questioning the nature of truth, justice, and the human capacity for both good and evil.

Genres:
Biography, True Crime, Historical

Themes:
Justice and Injustice, Morality, Society and Class
