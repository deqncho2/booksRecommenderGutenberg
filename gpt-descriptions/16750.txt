Synopsis: In 'The Colored Regulars in the United States Army', author T. G. Steward delves into the untold stories of African American soldiers who served in the United States Army during the Civil War and beyond. Through vivid accounts and personal narratives, Steward sheds light on the struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices of these brave men, offering a unique perspective on a pivotal period in American history. From the battlefields to the barracks, this captivating book unveils the remarkable contributions and resilience of the Colored Regulars, challenging conventional narratives and inspiring readers to reconsider the true heroes of the past.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History, African American Studies

Themes: Racial Equality, Heroism, Identity, Resilience
