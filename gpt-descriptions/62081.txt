Synopsis: In 'The Timber Pirate' by Charles Christopher Jenkins, a young and ambitious timber cruiser finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of deception and treachery in the rugged wilderness of the Pacific Northwest. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the timber industry, he must confront his own moral compass and make choices that will determine not only his own fate but also the future of the timber trade. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering who can be trusted and who is truly a timber pirate.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Morality, Deception, Survival
