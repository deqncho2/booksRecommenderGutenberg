Synopsis: 
In 'On Revenues' by Xenophon, a renowned philosopher and historian, a young ruler embarks on a journey to discover the secrets of managing his kingdom's finances. As he seeks advice from experienced statesmen and explores various economic strategies, he uncovers surprising insights that challenge conventional wisdom. With engaging anecdotes and thought-provoking discussions, Xenophon offers a captivating exploration of the intricate relationship between wealth, power, and the well-being of a nation.

Genres: Philosophy, Political Science, Economics

Themes: Financial management, Leadership, Wealth and power
