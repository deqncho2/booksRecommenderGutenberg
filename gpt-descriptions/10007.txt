Synopsis: In the remote and eerie Styrian countryside, a young woman named Laura finds herself drawn into a mysterious and captivating friendship with the enigmatic Carmilla. As their bond deepens, Laura begins to uncover unsettling secrets about her new companion, leading her on a thrilling journey of discovery and danger. Filled with Gothic atmosphere and suspense, "Carmilla" is a haunting tale that explores the complexities of desire, obsession, and the blurred boundaries between love and fear.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Horror, Mystery

Themes: Forbidden love, Female sexuality, Identity, Supernatural elements
