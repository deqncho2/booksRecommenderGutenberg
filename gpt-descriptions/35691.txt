Synopsis: 
In 'The Late Tenant' by Tracy, Louis, a young couple moves into their dream apartment, only to discover a mysterious secret hidden within its walls. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, danger, and supernatural occurrences. With each passing day, the couple's sanity is tested, and they must uncover the truth before it consumes them entirely.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Psychological suspense, Haunting and the supernatural
