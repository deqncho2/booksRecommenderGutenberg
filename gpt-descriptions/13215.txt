Synopsis:
'Edwy the Fair or the First Chronicle of Aescendune: A Tale of the Days of Saint Dunstan' is a captivating historical novel set in the days of Saint Dunstan. The story follows the life of Edwy, a young nobleman who finds himself caught in the midst of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the struggle for power. As Edwy navigates the treacherous world of medieval England, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of his people. With its rich historical backdrop and compelling characters, this tale of adventure and romance will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes:
Power and Politics, Forbidden Love, Personal Sacrifice
