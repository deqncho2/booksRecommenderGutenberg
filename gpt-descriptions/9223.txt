Synopsis: In "Fire Worship," Hawthorne takes us on a mysterious journey to a secluded village where an enigmatic group of individuals engage in a peculiar form of worship. As the protagonist delves deeper into their rituals, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and desires that threaten to consume him. With each revelation, the line between devotion and obsession blurs, leaving the reader captivated by the allure of the unknown.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, mystery

Themes: Religion and spirituality, the power of obsession, the human desire for transcendence
