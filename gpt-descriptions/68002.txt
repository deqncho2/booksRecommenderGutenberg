Synopsis:
In 'Identity' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist named Dr. John Smith makes a groundbreaking discovery that has the potential to change the course of humanity. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he begins to question his own identity and the consequences of his creation. As the line between reality and illusion blurs, Dr. Smith finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, where the stakes are higher than he could have ever imagined.

Genres:
Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes:
Identity, Ethics, Reality vs. Illusion
