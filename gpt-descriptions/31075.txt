Synopsis: 

In "In The Ranks: From the Wilderness to Appomattox Court House: The War, as Seen and Experienced by a Private Soldier in the Army of the Potomac," R. E. McBride takes readers on an extraordinary journey through the American Civil War. Through the eyes of a private soldier in the Army of the Potomac, the reader is immersed in the harrowing experiences, intense battles, and personal sacrifices that shaped the course of history. From the chaos of the Wilderness to the decisive moments at Appomattox Court House, this gripping narrative offers a unique perspective on the war that tore a nation apart.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Memoir

Themes: Courage, Sacrifice, Brotherhood, Survival
