Synopsis: In 'A Trace of Memory' by Keith Laumer, a man wakes up in a hospital bed with no memory of who he is or how he got there. As he embarks on a journey to uncover his identity, he discovers a world filled with danger, conspiracy, and mind-bending technology. With each clue he uncovers, he becomes more entangled in a web of secrets that could change the course of humanity. Will he be able to piece together his past before it's too late?

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Memory, Conspiracy
