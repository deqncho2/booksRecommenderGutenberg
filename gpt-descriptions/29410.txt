Synopsis: In 'The End of Time' by West, Wallace, a mysterious phenomenon begins to disrupt the flow of time, causing chaos and confusion among the inhabitants of a small town. As the days become increasingly unpredictable, a group of unlikely heroes must unravel the secrets behind this temporal anomaly and find a way to restore order before it's too late. With mind-bending twists and heart-pounding suspense, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Time, Identity, Reality
