Synopsis:
In the second volume of 'The Manchester and Glasgow Road: This Way to Gretna Green', Charles G. Harper takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical route connecting Manchester and Glasgow. Filled with intriguing anecdotes, vivid descriptions, and fascinating characters, this book offers a unique perspective on the road and the stories it holds. From tales of highwaymen and smugglers to accounts of famous travelers and notable landmarks, Harper's narrative paints a vivid picture of the road's rich history and the adventures that took place along its path. Whether you are a history enthusiast, a lover of travel literature, or simply curious about the hidden stories of the past, this book will transport you to a bygone era and leave you yearning for more.

Genres: Travel literature, History, Non-fiction
Themes: Historical journeys, Adventure, Hidden stories
