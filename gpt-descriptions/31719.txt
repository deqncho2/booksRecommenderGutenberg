Synopsis: In 'Madge Morton's Trust' by Amy D. V. Chalmers, a young girl named Madge finds herself caught up in a mysterious inheritance that leads her on a thrilling adventure. As she unravels the secrets of her newfound trust, Madge must navigate a web of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, Madge's courage and determination are put to the test, as she discovers the true meaning of trust and loyalty.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Trust, Loyalty, Deception, Courage
