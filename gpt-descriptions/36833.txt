Synopsis: In 'The Camp Fire Girls at Onoway House; Or, The Magic Garden', a group of adventurous Camp Fire Girls embark on a thrilling journey to Onoway House, a mysterious estate with a magical garden. As they explore the enchanting grounds, they encounter unexpected challenges and uncover secrets that test their courage and friendship. With each new discovery, the girls are drawn deeper into the captivating world of Onoway House, where they must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to overcome obstacles and unlock the garden's true power.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
