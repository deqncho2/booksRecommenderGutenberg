Synopsis: In "The Garotters" by William Dean Howells, a young man named Bartley Hubbard finds himself caught in a dangerous situation when he stumbles upon a secret society known as the Garotters. As he becomes entangled in their web of intrigue and violence, Bartley must navigate a treacherous path to protect himself and those he cares about. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this thrilling story will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secret societies, Betrayal, Survival
