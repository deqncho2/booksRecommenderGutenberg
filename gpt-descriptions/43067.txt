Synopsis: In the Hands of the Cave-Dwellers takes readers on a thrilling adventure through the treacherous caves of the Pyrenees. When a group of explorers becomes trapped deep underground, they must rely on their wits and courage to survive. As they navigate the dark and dangerous tunnels, they encounter mysterious cave-dwellers who may hold the key to their escape. With danger lurking at every turn, the explorers must unravel the secrets of the caves and find a way back to the surface before it's too late.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Courage
