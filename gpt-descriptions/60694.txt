Synopsis: In 'Virgin Ground' by Rosel George Brown, a young woman named Lavinia is thrust into a dangerous and mysterious world when she inherits a vast estate in the remote wilderness. As she navigates the treacherous landscape and encounters a cast of eccentric characters, Lavinia must uncover the secrets of her inheritance while facing unexpected challenges that test her resilience and determination. With its blend of suspense, adventure, and unexpected twists, 'Virgin Ground' will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the mysteries that lie within the untamed wilderness.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Inheritance, Survival, Secrets
