Synopsis: 
In 'The Preliminaries, and Other Stories', Comer, Cornelia A. P. takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking and emotionally charged tales. From the mysterious disappearance of a young girl to the unexpected consequences of a forbidden love affair, each story delves into the complexities of human relationships and the secrets that lie beneath the surface. With vivid storytelling and rich character development, this collection explores the depths of human nature, leaving readers questioning their own perceptions of truth and morality.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Mystery

Themes: Secrets, Relationships, Morality
