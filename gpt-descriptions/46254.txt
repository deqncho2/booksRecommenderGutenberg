Synopsis:
In 'Mrs. Hale's Receipts for the Million', Sarah Josepha Buell Hale presents a treasure trove of over four thousand practical and creative recipes, tips, and instructions for various aspects of everyday life. From cooking and cleaning to gardening and home remedies, this comprehensive guide offers a wealth of knowledge to enhance the useful, ornamental, and domestic arts. Whether you seek to impress with elegant dishes, beautify your surroundings, or find remedies for common ailments, Mrs. Hale's receipts are sure to captivate and inspire readers with their timeless wisdom and practicality.

Genres:
Cookbook, Household Guide, Self-help

Themes:
Domesticity, Practicality, Creativity
