Synopsis: In 'Big Game Shooting, volume 2 (of 2)', Phillipps-Wolley takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the untamed wilderness of Africa and India. Join him as he recounts his thrilling encounters with majestic elephants, cunning leopards, and ferocious lions, all while navigating the challenges and dangers of the hunt. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this volume is a captivating exploration of the world of big game hunting.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Wildlife, Survival
