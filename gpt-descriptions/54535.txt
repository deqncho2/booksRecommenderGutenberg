Synopsis: In 'The Changed Valentines, and Other Plays for St. Valentine's Day', Elizabeth F. Guptill presents a collection of delightful and heartwarming plays that explore the many facets of love and romance. From unexpected twists to humorous misunderstandings, these plays capture the essence of Valentine's Day with their charming characters and captivating stories. Whether it's a tale of secret admirers or a comedic clash of Cupid's arrows, each play offers a unique and entertaining take on the joys and challenges of love. 

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Love, Relationships, Fate, Humor
