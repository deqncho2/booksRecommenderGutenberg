Synopsis: 

'Curiosities of Great Britain: England and Wales Delineated' is an extensive 11-volume set that takes readers on a captivating journey through the historical, entertaining, and commercial aspects of England and Wales. Written by Thomas Dugdale, this meticulously arranged collection offers a comprehensive exploration of the two countries, alphabetically organized for easy reference. From ancient landmarks and architectural marvels to intriguing anecdotes and fascinating trivia, each volume is filled with captivating stories and hidden gems waiting to be discovered. 

Genres: Historical, Travel, Reference

Themes: British history, Cultural heritage, Architectural wonders, Local legends, Curiosities and oddities
