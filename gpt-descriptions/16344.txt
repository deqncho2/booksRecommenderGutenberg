Synopsis: 
"The Waif of the 'Cynthia'" follows the thrilling journey of a young girl who finds herself aboard a mysterious ship, the Cynthia, after a tragic event. As she navigates the treacherous waters of the sea, she encounters a diverse cast of characters and uncovers secrets that could change her life forever. With danger lurking at every turn, the waif must summon her courage and resourcefulness to survive the perils of the open ocean.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Survival, Identity, Resilience
