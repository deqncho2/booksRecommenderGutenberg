Synopsis: In "The Gadget Had a Ghost" by Murray Leinster, a brilliant inventor creates a revolutionary gadget that has the potential to change the world. However, he soon discovers that his invention is haunted by a mysterious presence that threatens to unravel everything he has worked for. As he delves deeper into the origins of the ghostly phenomenon, he must confront his own fears and make a choice that could have far-reaching consequences. This gripping tale explores the boundaries of science and the power of the unknown, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Invention, Supernatural, Fear, Consequences
