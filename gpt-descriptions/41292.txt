Synopsis: 

'Every Man His Own Doctor: The Cold Water, Tepid Water, and Friction-Cure, as Applicable to Every Disease to Which the Human Frame Is Subject, and Also to the Cure of Disease in Horses and Cattle' by Claridge, R. T. is a comprehensive guide that explores the power of water and friction as a cure for various diseases. This enlightening book delves into the practical applications of cold water, tepid water, and friction-cure, providing readers with valuable insights on how to treat ailments in both humans and animals. With a focus on self-care and natural remedies, Claridge presents a wealth of knowledge that empowers individuals to take control of their health and well-being.

Genres: Non-fiction, Health and Wellness, Self-help

Themes: Natural Remedies, Self-care, Holistic Healing
