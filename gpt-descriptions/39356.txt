Synopsis: In "The Political Doctrines of Sun Yat-sen: An Exposition of the San Min Chu I," Linebarger delves into the profound political ideologies of Sun Yat-sen, the founding father of modern China. Through a meticulous analysis of Sun Yat-sen's seminal work, the San Min Chu I, Linebarger unveils the visionary ideas that shaped the political landscape of China during a time of great transformation. This insightful exposition explores the principles of nationalism, democracy, and people's livelihood, shedding light on Sun Yat-sen's quest for a new China and his enduring impact on the nation's history.

Genres: Political theory, Historical analysis, Biography

Themes: Nationalism, Democracy, Social reform, Political philosophy
