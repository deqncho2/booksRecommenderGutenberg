Synopsis: In 'Stories Pictures Tell. Book 3', Carpenter takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of intriguing and enigmatic images. Each picture holds a hidden story, waiting to be unraveled by the reader's imagination. From mysterious landscapes to haunting portraits, these visual tales will transport you to different worlds and challenge your perception of reality. As you delve into the pages, you'll find yourself captivated by the power of visual storytelling and the endless possibilities it holds.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Art

Themes: Imagination, Perception, Storytelling
