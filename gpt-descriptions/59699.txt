Synopsis: 

Step aboard the U.S.S. Leviathan, a legendary cruiser and transport vessel that has witnessed the triumphs and tragedies of the United States Atlantic fleet. In this captivating historical account, compiled from the ship's log and data gathered by the dedicated U.S.S. Leviathan History Committee, embark on a journey through time as you uncover the untold stories and remarkable events that have shaped the ship's legacy. From daring naval battles to secret missions, from the camaraderie of the crew to the challenges faced on the high seas, this gripping narrative offers a unique perspective on the U.S.S. Leviathan's pivotal role in American history.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Naval Warfare, Patriotism, Heroism
