Synopsis: 
'Life on the Farm; in Amusing Rhyme' takes readers on a delightful journey through the daily adventures and mischievous escapades of the animals on a charming farm. From the clever antics of the mischievous goats to the heartwarming friendships formed between unlikely pairs, this book offers a whimsical and entertaining glimpse into the lively world of farm life. With its witty rhymes and endearing characters, this book is sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Poetry, Humor

Themes: Friendship, Adventure, Animal Behavior
