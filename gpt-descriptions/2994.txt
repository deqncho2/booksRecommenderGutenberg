Synopsis: In 'A Spirit in Prison' by Hichens, Robert, we follow the captivating journey of a young woman named Mary O'Neil, who finds herself trapped in a suffocating marriage. As she yearns for freedom and self-discovery, Mary embarks on a daring adventure that takes her from the bustling streets of London to the enchanting landscapes of Egypt. Along the way, she encounters mysterious characters, experiences forbidden desires, and confronts the complexities of her own identity. This gripping tale explores the depths of human emotions, the pursuit of personal liberation, and the transformative power of love.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Self-discovery, Freedom, Identity, Forbidden desires, Love
