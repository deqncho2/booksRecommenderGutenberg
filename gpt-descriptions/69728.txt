Synopsis: 
'Authors at Home: Personal and biographical sketches of well-known American writers' takes readers on an intimate journey into the lives of some of the most celebrated literary figures in American history. Through captivating anecdotes and insightful observations, this book offers a unique glimpse into the private worlds of these renowned authors, shedding light on their inspirations, struggles, and the environments that shaped their literary masterpieces. From the cozy study of a reclusive poet to the bustling streets that inspired a groundbreaking novelist, each chapter unveils the hidden stories behind the words that have captivated generations of readers. 

Genres: Biography, Non-fiction, Literary Criticism

Themes: Personal lives of authors, Literary inspiration, Writing process
