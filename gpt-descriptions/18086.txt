Synopsis: In "A Dozen Ways Of Love" by L. Dougall, a young woman named Lily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and love, exploring twelve different relationships that shape her life. From passionate romances to unexpected connections, Lily navigates the complexities of love, learning valuable lessons along the way. As she encounters various individuals who challenge her beliefs and desires, Lily must confront her own vulnerabilities and make choices that will ultimately define her path. This captivating novel takes readers on a heartfelt exploration of love in its many forms, leaving them eager to uncover the twists and turns that lie ahead.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Contemporary Fiction

Themes: Love, Self-discovery, Relationships, Identity
