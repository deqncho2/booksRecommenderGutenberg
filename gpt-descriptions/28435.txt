Synopsis: In 'The Cryptogram: A Novel' by De Mille, James, a young boy named Jed becomes entangled in a mysterious and dangerous game when he stumbles upon a cryptogram that holds a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the puzzle, Jed finds himself caught in a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden agendas. With each clue he uncovers, the stakes rise higher, and Jed must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before it's too late. This gripping and suspenseful novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the cryptogram's enigma.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Betrayal and deception
