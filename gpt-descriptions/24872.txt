Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Master Meadow Mouse' by Arthur Scott Bailey, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure through the meadows as they follow the courageous and clever Master Meadow Mouse. Facing numerous challenges and dangers, Master Meadow Mouse must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to navigate the treacherous world around him. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies ahead for our brave protagonist.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Courage, Survival, Ingenuity
