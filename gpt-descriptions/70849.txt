Synopsis:
In "Rodeo" by W. C. Tuttle, a young cowboy named Jim is determined to prove himself in the thrilling world of rodeo. With his trusty horse, he embarks on a journey filled with danger, competition, and unexpected friendships. As Jim faces the challenges of the rodeo circuit, he must confront his own fears and discover what truly matters to him. Will he find success and fulfillment in the rodeo arena, or will he be forced to confront the harsh realities of his chosen path?

Genres:
Western, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes:
Courage, Identity, Pursuit of dreams
