Synopsis:
In the second volume of 'Waverley; Or, 'Tis Sixty Years Since', the captivating tale continues as the protagonist, Edward Waverley, finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love. Set against the backdrop of the Jacobite uprising in 18th-century Scotland, Waverley's loyalty is tested as he navigates the treacherous paths of war, friendship, and honor. As he becomes embroiled in the conflict between the English and the Highland clans, Waverley must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and the fate of those he holds dear.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes:
Loyalty, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
