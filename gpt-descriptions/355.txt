Synopsis: In 'The Parasite: A Story' by Arthur Conan Doyle, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young man finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and deception. As he navigates through the dark underbelly of society, he becomes increasingly aware of the parasitic nature of those around him. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping narrative explores the lengths people will go to in order to survive and thrive in a world where appearances can be deceiving.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Survival, Social Commentary
