Synopsis: 
'The Quest of the Silver Fleece: A Novel' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rural South, where a young African American man named Zora embarks on a quest for love, justice, and self-discovery. As Zora navigates the complexities of race, class, and identity, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and power struggles that threaten to tear apart the community he holds dear. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel explores the challenges and triumphs of an individual's pursuit for freedom and equality in a divided society.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Romance, Social Commentary

Themes: 
Race, Identity, Love, Social Injustice, Power Struggles
