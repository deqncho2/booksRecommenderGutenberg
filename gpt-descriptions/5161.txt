Synopsis: In 'The Treasure' by Selma Lagerlöf, a young woman named Gösta Berling finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries when he becomes the tutor at Ekeby, a grand manor in Sweden. As he delves deeper into the lives of the eccentric inhabitants, Gösta discovers a hidden treasure that holds the power to change everything. With love, betrayal, and redemption at stake, he must navigate a world filled with unexpected twists and turns, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, mystery

Themes: Redemption, the power of love, the search for identity
