Synopsis: 
In 'The Leak' by Jacques Futrelle, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when a seemingly harmless leak in a government building leads to a series of unexpected events. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a conspiracy that threatens not only his own life but also the security of the entire nation. With time running out, he must navigate through a labyrinth of deceit and betrayal to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Conspiracy, Betrayal, Government secrecy
