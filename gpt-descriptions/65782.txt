Synopsis: In "Her Christmas at the Hermitage: A Tale About Rachel and Andrew Jackson," Helen Topping Miller takes readers on a captivating journey into the lives of Rachel and Andrew Jackson during the holiday season. Set in the historic Hermitage, the story unfolds as Rachel prepares for a special Christmas celebration, filled with love, joy, and unexpected surprises. As the couple navigates the challenges of their time, their enduring bond and the spirit of the season shine through, creating a heartwarming tale that captures the essence of Christmas.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Holiday

Themes: Love, Resilience, Tradition, Family
