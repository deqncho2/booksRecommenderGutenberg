Synopsis: In 'The Sorcery Club' by O'Donnell, Elliott, a group of friends stumble upon a mysterious club that promises to grant their deepest desires through the power of sorcery. As they delve deeper into the club's secrets, they soon realize that the price for their desires may be higher than they ever imagined. With danger lurking at every turn, they must navigate a treacherous world of magic, betrayal, and dark forces that threaten to consume them all.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Power and its consequences, Friendship and loyalty, The allure of the supernatural
