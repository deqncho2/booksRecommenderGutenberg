Synopsis: In 'An Uninvited Member: A Play for Girls in Two Scenes', a group of young girls gather for a secret club meeting, only to discover an unexpected guest who challenges their friendship and loyalty. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the girls must navigate the complexities of trust and acceptance in order to find a resolution. This captivating play explores the dynamics of friendship and the power of inclusion, leaving the audience on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Drama, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Acceptance, Secrets
