Synopsis: In "Tracks of a Rolling Stone," we follow the captivating journey of a young man named Coke as he embarks on a series of adventures across continents, encountering a diverse array of characters and experiencing both triumphs and heartbreaks. From the bustling streets of London to the wild landscapes of America and the exotic wonders of India, Coke's life becomes a tapestry of love, loss, and self-discovery. As he navigates through the highs and lows of his extraordinary existence, readers will be enthralled by the unexpected twists and turns that shape his destiny.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Exploration of identity, Love and relationships, Quest for meaning and purpose
