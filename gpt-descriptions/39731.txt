Synopsis: In 'The Marriage of Esther' by Boothby, Guy, a young woman named Esther finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she agrees to an arranged marriage. As she delves deeper into her new husband's mysterious past, Esther uncovers a dangerous world of intrigue and betrayal that threatens to consume her. With her life hanging in the balance, Esther must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those she loves.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Identity and self-discovery, Deception and betrayal
