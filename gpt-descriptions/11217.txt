Synopsis: In the small town of Freeport, a young woman named Prue Marsh is haunted by vivid and prophetic visions that she cannot explain. As she struggles to understand the meaning behind these visions, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, love, and betrayal that will challenge her beliefs and change the course of her life forever. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century America, "The Visioning" is a captivating tale of self-discovery, the power of intuition, and the complexities of human relationships.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Psychological

Themes: Intuition, Identity, Secrets, Love, Betrayal
