Synopsis: 

In 'Eminent doctors: Their lives and their work; Vol. 2 of 2', author G. T. Bettany delves into the fascinating lives and groundbreaking contributions of some of history's most renowned medical practitioners. From the ancient world to the modern era, this captivating volume uncovers the untold stories behind the medical pioneers who revolutionized the field of medicine. Through vivid narratives and meticulous research, readers will embark on a journey through time, exploring the triumphs, struggles, and extraordinary achievements of these eminent doctors. 

Genres: Biography, History, Medicine

Themes: Medical advancements, Human perseverance, Historical context
