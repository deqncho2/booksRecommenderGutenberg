Synopsis: In 'The Prodigal Returns' by Lilian Staveley, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey of self-discovery and redemption as she returns to her childhood home after years of absence. As she reconnects with her estranged family and confronts the secrets that tore them apart, Emily must navigate the complexities of love, forgiveness, and the power of second chances. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this captivating novel explores the depths of human emotions and the transformative power of homecoming.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Family

Themes: Redemption, Forgiveness, Family dynamics, Self-discovery
