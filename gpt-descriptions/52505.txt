Synopsis: In 'Daughters of Belgravia; vol. 2 of 3' by Fraser, Alexander, Mrs., the captivating story continues as the lives of two young women from different backgrounds intertwine in the glamorous world of 19th century London. As secrets are revealed and unexpected alliances are formed, the characters navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. With each page, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of intrigue and romance, eagerly anticipating the next twist in this enthralling tale.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social class, Family dynamics, Love and relationships
