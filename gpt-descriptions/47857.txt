Synopsis: 

In 'Adrift in the Arctic Ice Pack: from the history of the first U.S. Grinnell Expedition in search of Sir John Franklin' by Elisha Kent Kane, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the treacherous Arctic landscape. Set in the mid-19th century, the story follows the courageous crew of the U.S. Grinnell Expedition as they embark on a perilous mission to find the renowned explorer Sir John Franklin, who mysteriously disappeared while searching for the Northwest Passage. As they navigate through freezing temperatures, treacherous ice floes, and encounters with indigenous peoples, the crew faces unimaginable challenges that test their resilience and determination. Will they uncover the truth behind Franklin's disappearance, or will they too become lost in the unforgiving Arctic wilderness?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Exploration

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Perseverance, Discovery
