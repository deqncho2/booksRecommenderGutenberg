Synopsis: 
In Bram Stoker's iconic novel, 'Dracula', a young solicitor named Jonathan Harker travels to Transylvania to finalize a real estate transaction with the mysterious Count Dracula. As Harker delves deeper into the eerie castle, he begins to uncover the dark secrets and supernatural powers possessed by the enigmatic Count. Meanwhile, back in England, a group of friends and allies become entangled in a battle against the ancient evil that Dracula represents. With its gripping narrative and chilling atmosphere, 'Dracula' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the realms of horror and suspense.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Horror, Epistolary novel

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Sexuality, Immortality, Fear of the Unknown
