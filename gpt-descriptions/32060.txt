Synopsis: 
Confessions Of Con Cregan, the Irish Gil Blas is a thrilling and humorous novel that follows the adventures of Con Cregan, a young Irishman with a knack for mischief and a talent for storytelling. From his humble beginnings in the slums of Dublin to his encounters with high society in London, Con's life is a rollercoaster of escapades, love affairs, and unexpected twists. With his quick wit and charm, Con navigates through a world filled with colorful characters, dangerous situations, and surprising revelations. As he strives to find his place in society and uncover the truth about his own identity, Con's journey takes him on a wild ride that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Comedy

Themes: Identity, Social Class, Coming of Age
