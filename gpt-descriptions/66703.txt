Synopsis: 
'The Story of a Boulder; or, Gleanings from the Note-book of a Field Geologist' follows the journey of a passionate field geologist as he uncovers the remarkable history and secrets hidden within a seemingly ordinary boulder. As he delves deeper into the boulder's past, he unravels a captivating tale that spans millions of years, taking the reader on a thrilling adventure through time and geological wonders. With each revelation, the geologist's fascination grows, leading him to question the very essence of our planet's existence.

Genres:
Adventure, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes:
The passage of time, the wonders of geology, the mysteries of the Earth
