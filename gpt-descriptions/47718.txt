Synopsis: 
In 'Mr. Punch at Home: The Comic Side of Domestic Life', readers are invited into the uproarious world of Mr. Punch, a mischievous and irreverent character who navigates the everyday chaos of domestic life with his signature wit and charm. From hilarious mishaps in the kitchen to comical encounters with nosy neighbors, this collection of humorous vignettes offers a delightful and satirical exploration of the joys and challenges of family life. With its clever wordplay and clever illustrations, 'Mr. Punch at Home' is sure to leave readers laughing out loud and eagerly turning the pages to see what hilarious antics Mr. Punch will get up to next.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Graphic Novel

Themes: Domestic life, Family dynamics, Humor
