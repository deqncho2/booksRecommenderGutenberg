Synopsis: In "Mollie's Substitute Husband" by Max McConn, a young woman named Mollie finds herself in a desperate situation when her husband suddenly disappears. With no one to turn to, she makes a bold decision to hire a substitute husband to maintain appearances and protect her reputation. As Mollie navigates the complexities of her new arrangement, unexpected emotions and secrets begin to unravel, leading her on a path of self-discovery and forbidden love. This captivating tale explores the lengths one woman will go to protect her image and the consequences that come with living a lie.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Identity, Deception, Love and Sacrifice
