Synopsis: In the remote town of Eagle's, a group of strangers find themselves trapped together during a fierce snowstorm. As they struggle to survive the harsh conditions, tensions rise and secrets are revealed, leading to unexpected alliances and heart-wrenching betrayals. With each passing day, the snowbound residents must confront their deepest fears and confront the haunting past that brought them to this isolated place. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Isolation, Survival, Redemption, Secrets
