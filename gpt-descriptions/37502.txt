Synopsis: 

In 'Climbing in The British Isles, Vol. 2 - Wales and Ireland', authors Hart and Haskett Smith take readers on an exhilarating journey through the stunning landscapes of Wales and Ireland, uncovering hidden gems and challenging climbs. From the rugged peaks of Snowdonia to the dramatic cliffs of the Irish coast, this guidebook offers a comprehensive exploration of the best climbing spots in these regions. With detailed route descriptions, maps, and breathtaking photographs, the authors provide both experienced climbers and adventurous beginners with the tools they need to conquer these awe-inspiring heights.

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, Sports

Themes: Adventure, Exploration, Nature, Outdoor pursuits
