Synopsis: In 'Captain Dieppe' by Hope, Anthony, a mysterious and enigmatic captain arrives in the coastal town of Dieppe, capturing the attention and curiosity of the locals. As the captain's past is slowly revealed, the townspeople find themselves drawn into a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. With its gripping narrative and intriguing characters, 'Captain Dieppe' takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with suspense and unexpected twists.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
