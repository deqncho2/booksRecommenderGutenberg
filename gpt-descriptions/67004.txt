Synopsis: In 'The Young Continentals at Lexington' by McIntyre, John T., a group of young patriots find themselves caught up in the chaos and danger of the American Revolution. As the British army marches towards Lexington, these brave young individuals must navigate treacherous paths, face unexpected challenges, and make difficult choices that will shape their futures. With their lives on the line, they must summon courage, loyalty, and resilience to fight for their freedom and the birth of a new nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Sacrifice, Independence
