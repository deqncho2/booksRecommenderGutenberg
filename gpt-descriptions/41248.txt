Synopsis: 

'With the Zionists in Gallipoli' takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing events of World War I, as seen through the eyes of a young Jewish soldier. Amidst the chaos and brutality of the Gallipoli campaign, the protagonist finds himself torn between his duty to his country and his unwavering commitment to the Zionist cause. As he navigates the treacherous landscapes of war, he must confront not only the physical challenges but also the internal struggles that arise from his dual identity. With vivid descriptions and heart-wrenching moments, this captivating tale explores the sacrifices, loyalties, and resilience of those caught in the midst of a historic conflict.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Jewish Literature
Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Sacrifice, Conflict
