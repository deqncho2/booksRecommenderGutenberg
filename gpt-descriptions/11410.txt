Synopsis: 

In 'Wanderings in Wessex: An Exploration of the Southern Realm from Itchen to Otter', author Edric Holmes takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of Wessex. With vivid descriptions and a keen eye for detail, Holmes delves into the hidden corners and forgotten paths of this ancient region, uncovering its rich history, folklore, and natural wonders. From the rolling hills of Hampshire to the rugged coastline of Dorset, the author's wanderings lead us to discover the secrets and stories that lie within the very fabric of Wessex. As we follow Holmes on his explorations, we encounter mysterious ruins, encounter eccentric locals, and witness the timeless beauty of this captivating realm.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Cultural Heritage
