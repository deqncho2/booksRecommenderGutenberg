Synopsis: 
In 'Awakening' by Walton, Bryce, a young woman named Emma finds herself trapped in a mysterious and hauntingly beautiful world after a tragic accident. As she navigates through this ethereal realm, she discovers that she possesses a unique power that could change the fate of both worlds. With the help of enigmatic allies and facing formidable adversaries, Emma embarks on a thrilling journey of self-discovery, uncovering secrets that will challenge everything she thought she knew about herself and the world around her.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Power, Redemption
