Synopsis: In "Mrs. Pendleton's Four-in-hand" by Gertrude Franklin Horn Atherton, a spirited and unconventional widow finds herself entangled in a web of scandal and intrigue when she becomes the target of a mysterious blackmailer. As she navigates the treacherous waters of high society, Mrs. Pendleton must rely on her wit, charm, and a dash of audacity to uncover the truth and protect her reputation. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Scandal, Society, Identity, Power
