Synopsis: In "Skulls in the Stars" by Robert E. Howard, a renowned scientist becomes entangled in a deadly game of wits with a sinister sorcerer. As he delves deeper into the dark arts, he discovers a horrifying secret that threatens not only his own life but the very fabric of reality itself. With pulse-pounding action and spine-chilling suspense, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Horror, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Forbidden Knowledge, Supernatural Forces
