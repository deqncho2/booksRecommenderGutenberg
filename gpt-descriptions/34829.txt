Synopsis: In 'The Sick-a-Bed Lady: And Also Hickory Dock, The Very Tired Girl, The Happy-Day, Something That Happened in October, The Amateur Lover, Heart of The City, The Pink Sash, Woman's Only Business' by Eleanor Hallowell Abbott, readers are introduced to a collection of charming and heartwarming short stories. From a sick woman who discovers unexpected joy to a tired girl seeking solace, and a mysterious event in October that changes lives, each tale offers a glimpse into the lives of ordinary people facing extraordinary circumstances. With themes of resilience, love, and the power of human connection, this collection is sure to captivate readers with its delightful storytelling.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Resilience, Love, Human Connection
