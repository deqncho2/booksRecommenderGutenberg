Synopsis: In 'The Four Roads' by Sheila Kaye-Smith, a young woman named Mary finds herself torn between the expectations of her family and her own desires for independence. Set in the picturesque countryside of Sussex, England, Mary embarks on a journey that takes her down four different paths, each leading her to new experiences, challenges, and unexpected encounters. As she navigates through love, loss, and self-discovery, Mary must confront the choices she has made and ultimately decide which road will lead her to true happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Identity, Freedom, Self-discovery
