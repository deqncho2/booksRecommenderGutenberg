Synopsis: The Bobbsey Twins, Nan and Bert, embark on a thrilling new adventure as they enter school for the first time. From making new friends to solving mysterious puzzles, the twins find themselves caught up in a series of unexpected events that test their courage and intelligence. As they navigate the challenges of school life, the Bobbsey Twins discover that there is more to their classmates and teachers than meets the eye. With their trademark curiosity and resourcefulness, they uncover secrets that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Curiosity, Problem-solving
