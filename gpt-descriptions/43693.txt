Synopsis: 

In "Summary Narrative of an Exploratory Expedition to the Sources of the Mississippi River, in 1820: Resumed and Completed, by the Discovery of its Origin in Itasca Lake, in 1832," Henry Rowe Schoolcraft takes readers on a captivating journey through the uncharted wilderness of North America. Set in the early 19th century, this narrative follows Schoolcraft and his team as they embark on a daring expedition to uncover the elusive source of the mighty Mississippi River. Facing treacherous landscapes, encounters with Native American tribes, and the constant threat of the unknown, Schoolcraft's account offers a thrilling and enlightening exploration of the unexplored heartland of America.

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Nature, Native American culture, American identity
