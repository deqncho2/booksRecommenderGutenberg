Synopsis: In "Scarlet and Hyssop: A Novel" by E. F. Benson, a seemingly idyllic English village is disrupted by the arrival of a mysterious stranger. As secrets are unveiled and hidden desires come to the surface, the lives of the villagers are forever changed. With a touch of romance, suspense, and a dash of the supernatural, this captivating novel explores the dark undercurrents that lie beneath the surface of a seemingly peaceful community.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Supernatural fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Forbidden desires, Small-town dynamics, The power of the supernatural
