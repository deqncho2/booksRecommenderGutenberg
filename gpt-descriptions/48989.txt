Synopsis: In 'Tinman' by Tom Gallon, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, capturing the attention and curiosity of its inhabitants. As the stranger's true identity and purpose slowly unravel, the town becomes embroiled in a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected connections. With each revelation, the reader is drawn deeper into a gripping tale of love, betrayal, and the lengths people will go to protect their own. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Betrayal
