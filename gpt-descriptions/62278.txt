Synopsis: In 'A Son of Courage' by Archie P. McKishnie, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the life of a young man named Ethan, who finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous conflict. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters unexpected allies and enemies, Ethan must summon his inner strength and make difficult choices that will test his courage and shape his destiny. With its gripping narrative and unpredictable twists, this book will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind Ethan's extraordinary journey.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Identity, Morality
