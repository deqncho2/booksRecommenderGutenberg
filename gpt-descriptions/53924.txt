Synopsis: 
'Abroad and at Home; Practical Hints for Tourists' is a captivating travel guide written by Phillips Morris. Filled with insightful tips and advice, this book takes readers on a journey through various destinations, offering practical suggestions and recommendations to enhance their travel experiences. From navigating unfamiliar cities to immersing oneself in local culture, this guide is a valuable companion for both seasoned travelers and those embarking on their first adventure.

Genres: Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Travel tips, Cultural immersion, City exploration
