Synopsis: 
In 'The Book of Dreams and Ghosts' by Lang, Andrew, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the realms of dreams and the supernatural. This collection of eerie tales and haunting encounters will leave you spellbound as you delve into the mysterious and inexplicable. From ghostly apparitions to prophetic dreams, each story is a mesmerizing exploration of the unknown, filled with suspense and intrigue. Prepare to be enthralled by these chilling tales that will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very last page.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Horror, Mystery

Themes: 
Supernatural, Dreams, Fear, Fate
