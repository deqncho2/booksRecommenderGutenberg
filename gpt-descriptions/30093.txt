Synopsis: In 'The Shepherd of the North' by Richard Aumerle Maher, a young shepherd named David embarks on a journey that takes him from the rugged landscapes of the Scottish Highlands to the bustling streets of Victorian London. As he navigates through the challenges of love, loss, and self-discovery, David's life becomes intertwined with a mysterious secret that could change everything he knows. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this captivating novel explores the power of resilience, the complexities of human relationships, and the enduring strength of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Resilience, Love and Loss, Self-Discovery
