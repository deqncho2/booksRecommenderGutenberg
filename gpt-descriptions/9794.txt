Synopsis: In the heart of a bustling city, a young girl named Calvary Alley navigates the challenges of poverty and adversity with unwavering determination. As she encounters a cast of colorful characters, including a charismatic street preacher and a mysterious stranger, Calvary's resilience is put to the test. With her indomitable spirit and a desire for a better life, she embarks on a journey that will change her life forever.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Poverty, Resilience, Hope, Social Inequality
