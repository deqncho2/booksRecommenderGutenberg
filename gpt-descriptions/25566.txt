Synopsis: 
Step into the world of the Northern Nut Growers Association as they gather for their fifteenth annual meeting in New York City in 1924. Delve into the fascinating discussions and presentations on various nut-growing topics, from the cultivation of rare and exotic nut trees to the latest advancements in nut processing and marketing. Discover the passion and expertise of these dedicated nut enthusiasts as they share their knowledge and experiences, offering valuable insights into the world of nut cultivation and its potential for economic growth. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Horticulture, Agriculture

Themes: Nut cultivation, Horticultural advancements, Economic potential
