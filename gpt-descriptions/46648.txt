Synopsis: In "An Old English Home and Its Dependencies," Baring-Gould takes readers on a captivating journey through the history and secrets of a charming old English home. From the hidden passages and mysterious artifacts to the eccentric inhabitants and their intriguing stories, this book unravels the fascinating tapestry of the house and its dependencies. As the author delves into the past, he uncovers a web of family secrets, scandals, and long-lost treasures that will keep readers engrossed until the very end.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Gothic literature

Themes: Family secrets, History and heritage, Intrigue and mystery
