Synopsis: In 'The suppressed Gospels and Epistles of the original New Testament of Jesus the Christ, Volume 2, the Protevanglion', William Wake delves into the hidden texts that were excluded from the traditional New Testament. This captivating volume uncovers the fascinating stories and teachings that were once considered too controversial or unconventional. From the infancy of Jesus to his early years, this book sheds light on the lesser-known aspects of his life and ministry. As the secrets of these suppressed gospels are unveiled, readers are taken on a thought-provoking journey that challenges traditional beliefs and invites a deeper understanding of Jesus' message.

Genres: Religious literature, Historical fiction, Biblical studies

Themes: Hidden knowledge, Alternative perspectives, Spiritual exploration
