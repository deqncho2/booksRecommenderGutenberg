Synopsis: In 'The Good Neighbors' by Edgar Pangborn, a small town is shaken by the arrival of a mysterious family who seem too perfect to be true. As the townspeople become increasingly fascinated by their new neighbors, they soon discover that there is more to this family than meets the eye. Secrets, hidden agendas, and unexpected alliances emerge, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful exploration of the dark side of human nature.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Deception, Community, Morality, Identity
