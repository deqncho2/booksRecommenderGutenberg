Synopsis: In 'Snow-White; or, The House in the Wood', Laura Elizabeth Howe Richards weaves a captivating tale of a young girl named Snow-White who finds herself in a mysterious house deep in the woods. As Snow-White navigates the enchanting rooms and encounters peculiar characters, she unravels the secrets of the house and discovers her own inner strength. Filled with magic, adventure, and unexpected twists, this story will transport readers to a world where imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Fairy tale, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Self-discovery, Courage, Magic
