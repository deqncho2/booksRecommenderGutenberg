Synopsis: In 'Home-made Electrical Apparatus', Alfred Powell Morgan takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of DIY electrical inventions. From simple contraptions to more complex devices, Morgan provides step-by-step instructions and insightful anecdotes that will leave readers inspired and eager to embark on their own electrical projects. With a mix of historical context, practical advice, and a touch of humor, this book is a must-read for anyone curious about the wonders of homemade electrical apparatus.

Genres: Non-fiction, DIY, Technology

Themes: Creativity, Ingenuity, Practicality
