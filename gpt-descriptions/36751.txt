Synopsis: 

'Engraving for Illustration: Historical and Practical Notes' by Joseph Kirkbride takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of engraving. From its rich historical origins to its practical applications in the modern era, this book delves into the intricate techniques and artistic mastery behind this timeless art form. Through vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Kirkbride explores the evolution of engraving, revealing the secrets behind its enduring appeal and its profound impact on the world of illustration. 

Genres: Art, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic craftsmanship, Historical significance, Evolution of illustration
