Synopsis: In 'Thy Name Is Woman' by Walton Bryce, a young woman named Emily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious letter hidden in her deceased grandmother's belongings. As she delves deeper into her family's history, Emily uncovers a shocking truth that will challenge everything she thought she knew about her own identity. With danger lurking around every corner, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those she loves. This gripping tale of suspense and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Betrayal
