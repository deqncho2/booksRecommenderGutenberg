Synopsis: In 'A Singular Metamorphosis' by Skiles, May Evelyn, a young woman named Alice finds herself caught in a mysterious and enchanting world where reality and fantasy intertwine. As she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, Alice encounters peculiar characters and faces unexpected challenges that force her to question her own identity and the nature of the world around her. With each twist and turn, Alice's perception of herself and her place in the universe undergoes a profound transformation, leading her to uncover secrets that will forever change her life.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Perception, Self-discovery
