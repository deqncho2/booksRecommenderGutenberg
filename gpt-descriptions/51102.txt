Synopsis: In 'The Sentimentalists' by Murray Leinster, a group of scientists embark on a groundbreaking experiment that allows them to tap into the emotions and memories of others. As they delve deeper into the human psyche, they uncover a dark secret that threatens to unravel their own sanity and the very fabric of their reality. With suspenseful twists and thought-provoking revelations, this gripping novel explores the boundaries of science and the complexities of human emotions.

Genres: Science Fiction, Psychological Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Memory, Identity, Ethics, Perception
