Synopsis: In 'His Official Fiancée' by Berta Ruck, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of deception when she agrees to pose as the fiancée of a wealthy aristocrat. As she navigates the glamorous world of high society, she must confront her own desires and the consequences of her actions. With secrets, romance, and unexpected twists, this captivating novel will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Deception, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
