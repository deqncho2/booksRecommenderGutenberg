Synopsis: In "Legends of the Rhine" by Wilhelm Ruland, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of the Rhine River. This collection of mythical tales and folklore will transport you to a world where knights, castles, and supernatural beings come to life. From the mysterious Lorelei to the legendary Drachenfels, each story unveils the hidden secrets and legends that have shaped the Rhine's rich history. Prepare to be mesmerized by the beauty and intrigue of these timeless tales that have captivated generations.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Mythology, Adventure, Love and Loss
