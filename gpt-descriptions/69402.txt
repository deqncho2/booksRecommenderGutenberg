Synopsis: 
In "Twice round the clock; or, The hours of the day and night in London," George Augustus Sala takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling streets of London, exploring the city's vibrant and diverse characters and their activities during each hour of the day and night. From the early morning risers to the nocturnal creatures, Sala's vivid descriptions and keen observations paint a vivid picture of the city's pulse and its inhabitants' lives. As the clock ticks, readers are immersed in a world filled with intrigue, humor, and unexpected encounters, making each hour a delightful adventure.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Travelogue, Social commentary

Themes: 
Urban life, Time, Diversity, Human behavior
