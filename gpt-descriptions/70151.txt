Synopsis: In "Myra: The Child of Adoption: A Romance of Real Life," Ann S. Stephens weaves a captivating tale of love, mystery, and the complexities of identity. The story follows Myra, a young woman who discovers that she is not who she thought she was, setting her on a journey to uncover her true origins. As she delves into her past, Myra encounters unexpected twists and turns, encountering both love and heartbreak along the way. With its intriguing plot and compelling characters, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the secrets that lie within Myra's past.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Identity, Family, Love, Secrets
