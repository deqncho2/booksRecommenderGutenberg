Synopsis: 
In 'Tom Slade with the Colors', Tom Slade, a young and adventurous boy, finds himself caught up in the excitement and danger of World War I. Leaving behind his familiar scouting life, Tom joins the army and embarks on a journey that will test his courage, loyalty, and resourcefulness. As he faces the challenges of war, Tom must navigate through the chaos and uncertainty, forming new friendships and discovering the true meaning of bravery along the way.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Friendship, Coming of Age
