Synopsis: 
In 'Historic Events of Colonial Days', Rupert Sargent Holland takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating and often overlooked events of colonial America. From the early settlements of Jamestown and Plymouth to the tumultuous years leading up to the American Revolution, this book delves into the untold stories and lesser-known figures that shaped the course of history. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Holland brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and conflicts that defined the colonial era, offering a fresh perspective on a pivotal period in American history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, American history

Themes: Exploration and settlement, Colonial society and culture, Political and social change, Conflict and revolution
