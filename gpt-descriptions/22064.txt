Synopsis: In the small coastal village of Storm Country, Tess Durbeyfield, a spirited and independent young woman, finds herself caught between the rigid social hierarchy and her own desires for a better life. When a wealthy stranger arrives in town, Tess's world is turned upside down as she navigates love, betrayal, and the pursuit of her dreams. As she fights against the constraints of her circumstances, Tess discovers the power of resilience and the strength to challenge the status quo.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Social class, Love and desire, Female empowerment
