Synopsis: In 'Virgil' by Collins, W. Lucas, a young man named Virgil finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden artifact with incredible powers. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the artifact, Virgil becomes entangled in a centuries-old battle between ancient forces. With his life on the line, Virgil must navigate treacherous alliances and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Power of ancient artifacts, Hidden secrets, Battle between good and evil
