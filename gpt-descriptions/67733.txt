Synopsis: In 'A Girton Girl' by Annie Edwards, we follow the journey of a young woman named Alice as she defies societal expectations and enrolls in Girton College, one of the first women's colleges in England. Set in the late 19th century, Alice faces numerous challenges and prejudices as she strives to prove herself academically and navigate the complexities of love and friendship. As she fights for her place in a male-dominated world, Alice's determination and resilience are put to the test, leading to unexpected discoveries and personal growth.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Women's Literature

Themes: Gender Equality, Education, Identity, Love and Friendship
