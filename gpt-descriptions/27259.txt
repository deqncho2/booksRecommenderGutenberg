Synopsis: In 'A Soldier in the Philippines' by Freeman, Needom N., follow the gripping journey of a young soldier deployed to the Philippines during a time of political unrest and cultural clashes. As he navigates the complexities of war, he finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and self-discovery. With vivid descriptions and heart-wrenching moments, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the soldier's fate.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War, Romance

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Sacrifice
