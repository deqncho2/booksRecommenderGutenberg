Synopsis: In 'Love Story' by Cox, Irving E., two individuals from different worlds find themselves irresistibly drawn to each other. Oliver, a wealthy Harvard student, and Jenny, a working-class musician, embark on a passionate and unconventional love affair that challenges societal norms and tests the boundaries of their own personal ambitions. As their relationship unfolds, they navigate through the complexities of love, sacrifice, and the pursuit of happiness, ultimately leading them to confront a heart-wrenching reality that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Romance, Drama

Themes: Love, Class differences, Sacrifice
