Synopsis: In 'The Airship Boys in the Great War; or, The Rescue of Bob Russell' by Cass, De Lysle F., a group of adventurous young men find themselves caught in the midst of World War I as they embark on a daring mission to save their friend, Bob Russell. With their trusty airship, they navigate through treacherous skies, encountering enemy forces, and facing unexpected challenges. As they race against time, the Airship Boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and unwavering friendship to complete their mission and bring Bob home safely.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Loyalty, Survival
