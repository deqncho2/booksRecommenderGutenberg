Synopsis: 
In Shakespeare's Richard II, a young and inexperienced king ascends to the throne, only to find himself caught in a web of political intrigue and power struggles. As Richard grapples with his own flaws and the consequences of his actions, the kingdom is plunged into chaos, leading to a gripping tale of betrayal, loyalty, and the fragility of power.

Genres: 
Tragedy, Historical Drama

Themes: 
Power and its corrupting influence, the nature of kingship, loyalty and betrayal, the consequences of one's actions
