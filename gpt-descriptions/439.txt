Synopsis: In "The Vital Message" by Arthur Conan Doyle, a renowned detective turns his attention to a different kind of mystery - the enigma of life after death. As he delves into the realms of spiritualism and the occult, he encounters a series of intriguing characters and uncovers a shocking secret that challenges his beliefs and forces him to question the very nature of existence. With his signature wit and keen observation, Doyle takes readers on a thought-provoking journey that explores the boundaries between science and spirituality, skepticism and faith.

Genres: Mystery, Spiritualism, Occult

Themes: Life after death, Skepticism vs. faith, Science vs. spirituality
