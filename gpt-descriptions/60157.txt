Synopsis: 

"On the Plains with Custer" is an enthralling historical account that takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous years of 1866-1876. The story follows Boy Bugler Ned Fletcher, who serves under the legendary Chief with the Yellow Hair, General George Armstrong Custer. As part of the Fighting Seventh Cavalry, Ned witnesses firsthand the pivotal role they play in securing the territories of Pioneer Kansas, Nebraska, and Dakota for white civilization. Amidst the backdrop of intense battles and the clash of cultures, the book explores the bravery, sacrifice, and complexities of the individuals involved in shaping the American West.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Western

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Clash of Cultures, Manifest Destiny, Exploration
