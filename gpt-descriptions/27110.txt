Synopsis: In 'The Eternal Wall' by Raymond Z. Gallun, a group of scientists embarks on a perilous journey to a distant planet, where they discover a mysterious and ancient structure that holds the key to humanity's survival. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the enigmatic wall, they must confront their own fears and make unimaginable sacrifices to protect the future of mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Sacrifice, Humanity's Potential
