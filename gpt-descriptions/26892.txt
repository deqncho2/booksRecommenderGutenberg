Synopsis: In 'Chasing an Iron Horse; Or, A Boy's Adventures in the Civil War' by Edward Robins, readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous times of the Civil War. The story follows a young boy named Henry, who finds himself caught up in the chaos and danger of the war. As he embarks on a mission to find his missing father, Henry encounters unexpected allies, treacherous enemies, and experiences that will shape him forever. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale explores the resilience and courage of a young boy amidst the backdrop of one of America's most defining moments.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Family, Friendship, War
