Synopsis: In 'The Supplies for the Confederate Army: How They Were Obtained in Europe and How Paid For,' Caleb Huse delves into the intriguing world of international politics and covert operations during the American Civil War. This captivating non-fiction work uncovers the clandestine efforts made by the Confederacy to acquire crucial supplies from Europe, shedding light on the intricate web of negotiations, financial arrangements, and secret missions that took place. As Huse unveils the intricate details of these operations, readers will be drawn into a world of intrigue, espionage, and high-stakes diplomacy, where the fate of a nation hangs in the balance.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Military

Themes: International politics, Covert operations, Diplomacy, Espionage
