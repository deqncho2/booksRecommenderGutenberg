Synopsis: 

In 'Titian: a collection of fifteen pictures and a portrait of the painter', Estelle M. Hurll takes readers on a captivating journey through the life and art of the renowned Italian painter, Titian. Through vivid descriptions and stunning illustrations, Hurll delves into the masterpieces created by Titian, exploring the emotions, stories, and techniques behind each painting. From mythological scenes to religious works, the collection showcases the breadth and depth of Titian's talent, leaving readers in awe of his artistic genius. 

Genres: Art History, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, Renaissance culture, Mastery of technique
