Synopsis: 
In "The Boy Scouts at the Canadian Border" by John Henry Goldfrap, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon a suspicious smuggling operation near the Canadian border. As they delve deeper into the dangerous world of smugglers, the young scouts must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and bravery to uncover the truth and protect their community. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding action, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
