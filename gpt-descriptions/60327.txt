Synopsis: In 'The Green Mirror: A Quiet Story' by Hugh Walpole, a young woman named Mary encounters a mysterious mirror that holds a captivating secret. As she delves deeper into its enchanting powers, Mary becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where reality and illusion blur. With each reflection, she uncovers hidden truths about herself and those around her, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of perception and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological thriller, Gothic fiction

Themes: Identity, Perception, Illusion, Self-discovery
