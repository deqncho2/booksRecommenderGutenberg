Synopsis: In 'The Oppressed English' by Hay, Ian, a young linguistics professor stumbles upon a hidden manuscript that challenges everything he thought he knew about the English language. As he delves deeper into the mysterious origins of the manuscript, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and conspiracies that threaten to unravel the very fabric of society. With each revelation, he must confront his own beliefs and navigate a treacherous path to protect the truth from those who seek to suppress it.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Language and power, Hidden knowledge, Conspiracy
