Synopsis: 

In 'Royal Edinburgh: Her Saints, Kings, Prophets and Poets', Mrs. Oliphant takes us on a captivating journey through the rich history and enchanting tales of the iconic city of Edinburgh. From the ancient legends of saints and kings to the profound wisdom of prophets and the lyrical beauty of poets, this book unveils the hidden treasures and untold stories that have shaped the city's identity. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the author paints a vivid picture of Edinburgh's past, intertwining fact and folklore to create a mesmerizing tapestry of the city's cultural heritage. As we delve into the lives of the remarkable individuals who have left their mark on Edinburgh, we are transported to a world of intrigue, passion, and inspiration.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travel literature, Biography

Themes: History, Culture, Identity, Legends, Literature
