Synopsis: In the small town of Riverboro, young Rebecca Mary Randall is known for her wild imagination and adventurous spirit. When a mysterious stranger arrives in town, Rebecca Mary's life takes an unexpected turn. As she navigates through secrets, friendships, and the complexities of growing up, Rebecca Mary discovers the power of love, loyalty, and the importance of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Mystery, Fiction

Themes: Imagination, Friendship, Identity, Secrets
