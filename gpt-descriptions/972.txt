Synopsis: 
'The Devil's Dictionary' by Ambrose Bierce is a satirical lexicon that offers witty and sardonic definitions of various words. Through a collection of humorous and thought-provoking entries, Bierce takes a sharp and critical look at human nature, society, and the absurdities of everyday life. From politics and religion to love and death, this book provides a unique and entertaining perspective on the complexities of language and the human condition.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Dictionary

Themes: Critique of society, Human nature, Language and communication
