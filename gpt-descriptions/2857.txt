Synopsis: In 'A Yellow God: An Idol of Africa', H. Rider Haggard takes readers on a thrilling adventure deep into the heart of Africa. The story follows a young Englishman named Alan Vernon who finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he becomes obsessed with a legendary African idol. As he delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the idol, Alan's journey takes him through treacherous landscapes, encounters with enigmatic characters, and a series of unexpected twists and turns. This gripping tale explores the dark allure of ancient legends and the lengths one man will go to uncover the truth.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Obsession, Cultural Clash
