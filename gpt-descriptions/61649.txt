Synopsis: 

Pugilistica: The History of British Boxing, Volume 2 (of 3) takes readers on a thrilling journey through the fascinating world of British boxing. This volume focuses on the period from 1719 to 1863, providing detailed accounts of the lives and battles of the most celebrated pugilists of the time. Through full reports from contemporary newspapers, readers will experience the intensity and drama of these historic fights, accompanied by authentic portraits, personal anecdotes, and sketches of the influential figures who shaped the sport. From the early days of Fig and Broughton to the epic championship battle between King and Heenan in December 1863, this volume offers a comprehensive and captivating history of the ring.

Genres: Sports, History, Biography

Themes: Courage, Determination, Legacy, Society and Culture
