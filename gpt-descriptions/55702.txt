Synopsis: 
In 'Day by Day with the Russian Army, 1914-15', Bernard Pares takes readers on an immersive journey through the tumultuous events of World War I, offering a unique perspective from within the Russian Army. Through vivid and personal accounts, Pares provides a gripping narrative that captures the daily struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices of the soldiers on the Eastern Front. From the chaos of battlefields to the camaraderie among comrades, this book offers a compelling glimpse into the harsh realities of war and the indomitable spirit of those who fought.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War memoir

Themes: War, camaraderie, sacrifice, resilience
