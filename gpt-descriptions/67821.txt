Synopsis: In "Life of Emanuel Swedenborg," author William White delves into the fascinating life of the enigmatic Swedish scientist, philosopher, and theologian. From his early achievements in the fields of mathematics and engineering to his later spiritual awakening and profound mystical experiences, Swedenborg's journey is one of intellectual curiosity, spiritual exploration, and profound transformation. White provides a captivating account of Swedenborg's life, shedding light on his influential writings and their impact on both philosophy and theology.

Genres: Biography, Philosophy, Theology

Themes: Intellectual curiosity, Spiritual exploration, Transformation
