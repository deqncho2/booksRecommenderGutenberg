Synopsis: 
In 'Just David' by Eleanor H. Porter, we are introduced to a young boy named David who lives a secluded life in the mountains with his father. David possesses an extraordinary talent for playing the violin, but his father forbids him from sharing his gift with the world. When circumstances force David to leave his isolated home and enter the bustling city, he must navigate a world filled with new experiences, friendships, and challenges. As David's musical abilities capture the attention of those around him, he must decide whether to embrace his talent or continue to live in secrecy. 

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, Music

Themes: 
Individuality, Pursuit of Passion, Self-Discovery
