Synopsis: In 'The Golden Web' by E. Phillips Oppenheim, a young man named Felix is drawn into a world of mystery and intrigue when he becomes entangled with a secretive group known as "The Golden Web." As Felix delves deeper into their web of secrets, he discovers a hidden world of power, betrayal, and forbidden love. With danger lurking at every turn, Felix must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear. Will he be able to untangle himself from the Golden Web before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, Power and manipulation, Forbidden love
