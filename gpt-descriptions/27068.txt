Synopsis:
'The Dead Command: From the Spanish Los Muertos Mandan' by Blasco Ibáñez, Vicente is a gripping and haunting tale set during the Spanish Civil War. The story follows a young soldier named Julio, who finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and personal vendettas. As he navigates through the chaos of war, Julio becomes entangled with a mysterious group known as "The Dead Command," whose motives and actions are shrouded in secrecy. With danger lurking at every turn, Julio must uncover the truth behind this enigmatic organization while facing his own inner demons.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Mystery

Themes:
Power and Corruption, Loyalty and Betrayal, Identity and Self-discovery
