Synopsis: 
'Four in Camp: A Story of Summer Adventures in the New Hampshire Woods' follows the thrilling escapades of four young boys as they embark on a summer camping trip in the picturesque New Hampshire woods. As they navigate through the wilderness, they encounter unexpected challenges, forge deep friendships, and uncover hidden secrets that will test their courage and resourcefulness. With each passing day, the boys learn valuable life lessons and discover the true meaning of adventure and self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Outdoor

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Self-discovery
