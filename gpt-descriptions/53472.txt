Synopsis: 
Step into the mesmerizing world of 'Myers' Grand Hippodrome', a thrilling tale that takes readers on a captivating journey through the enigmatic and dazzling world of the circus. Set in the early 1900s, this mysterious story follows a young woman named Amelia who finds herself drawn into the secrets and wonders of the renowned circus. As she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, Amelia must navigate the treacherous paths of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of her own identity. With each turn of the page, the reader is transported to a world filled with breathtaking performances, hidden agendas, and unexpected twists that will leave them yearning for more.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Secrets, Love, Ambition
