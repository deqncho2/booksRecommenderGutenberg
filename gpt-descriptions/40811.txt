Synopsis: In 'Expositor's Bible: The Gospel of Matthew', John Monro Gibson takes readers on a captivating journey through the Gospel of Matthew, unraveling the profound teachings and life of Jesus Christ. With insightful commentary and engaging storytelling, Gibson explores the historical and cultural context of the Gospel, shedding light on the significance of each event and parable. From the birth of Jesus to his crucifixion and resurrection, this book offers a fresh perspective on the life-changing message of Matthew's Gospel, leaving readers inspired and eager to delve deeper into the teachings of Jesus.

Genres: Biblical commentary, Religious literature, Christian non-fiction

Themes: The life and teachings of Jesus, Salvation and redemption, The Kingdom of God, Faith and discipleship
