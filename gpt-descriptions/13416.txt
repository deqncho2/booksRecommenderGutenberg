Synopsis: In 'The Darling and Other Stories', Anton Chekhov presents a collection of captivating tales that delve into the complexities of human nature and the intricacies of relationships. From the story of a woman who finds her identity through her relationships with men, to a man's struggle with his own mortality, each narrative offers a unique exploration of the human condition. With Chekhov's signature blend of wit, insight, and emotional depth, these stories will leave readers pondering the complexities of life and love.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Identity, Relationships, Mortality
