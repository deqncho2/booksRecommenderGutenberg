Synopsis: 
In 'Corduroy' by Ruth Comfort Mitchell, a young woman named Cordelia finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires. Set in the early 20th century, Cordelia embarks on a journey of self-discovery, challenging the norms of her time and seeking her own path to happiness. As she navigates through love, loss, and personal growth, Cordelia's choices have far-reaching consequences that will leave readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Independence, Love and Sacrifice
