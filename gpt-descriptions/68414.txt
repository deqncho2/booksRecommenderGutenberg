Synopsis: In "The tryal of Mr. Daniel Sutton, for the high crime of preserving the lives of His Majesty's liege subjects, by means of inoculation," Daniel Sutton finds himself on trial for a crime that seems unthinkable - saving lives. As he faces the court, he must defend his controversial method of inoculation against a society that fears the unknown. With the lives of countless people hanging in the balance, the trial becomes a battle between tradition and progress, as Sutton fights for the right to protect his fellow citizens.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Legal Drama, Medical Thriller

Themes: Innovation vs. Tradition, Ethics in Medicine, Societal Fear and Prejudice
