Synopsis:
In "Royalty Restored; Or, London Under Charles II" by J. Fitzgerald Molloy, readers are transported to the vibrant and tumultuous era of Restoration England. Set against the backdrop of the reign of Charles II, this captivating historical novel follows the lives of a diverse cast of characters as they navigate the treacherous world of court intrigue, political machinations, and forbidden love. From the glittering halls of Whitehall Palace to the seedy underbelly of London's streets, the story unfolds with gripping twists and turns, revealing the hidden secrets and scandals that threaten to upend the monarchy. As the city teeters on the brink of chaos, the characters must make choices that will shape their destinies and determine the fate of the nation. With its richly detailed setting and compelling characters, "Royalty Restored; Or, London Under Charles II" offers a captivating glimpse into a fascinating period of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Power and Politics, Love and Betrayal, Social Class and Identity
