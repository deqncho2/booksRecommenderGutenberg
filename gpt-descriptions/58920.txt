Synopsis: 
Step into the captivating world of Horatio Alger, Jr., a prolific American author known for his rags-to-riches stories that have inspired generations. This collection brings together a diverse range of Alger's works, showcasing his talent for crafting tales of determination, perseverance, and the triumph of the human spirit. From the bustling streets of New York City to the untamed wilderness of the American West, Alger's characters navigate through adversity, facing challenges that test their character and resolve. With each story, Alger weaves a tapestry of hope, ambition, and the pursuit of success, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover the fate of his endearing protagonists.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Rags-to-riches, Perseverance, Morality, Social mobility
