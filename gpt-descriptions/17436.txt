Synopsis: In 'The Queen's Cup' by G. A. Henty, a young Englishman finds himself embroiled in a thrilling adventure when he is unexpectedly chosen to compete in a prestigious horse race. As he navigates the treacherous world of horse racing, he must overcome numerous obstacles and face dangerous adversaries. With his determination and courage, he strives to prove himself and secure victory in the ultimate test of skill and bravery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Sports

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Honor, Friendship
