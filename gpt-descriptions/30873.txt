Synopsis: In 'His Lordship's Leopard: A Truthful Narration of Some Impossible Facts' by David Dwight Wells, a mysterious leopard appears in the English countryside, causing chaos and confusion among the locals. As the townspeople try to unravel the enigma behind the leopard's presence, they uncover a web of secrets and unexpected connections that challenge their perception of reality. With a blend of humor, suspense, and a touch of the supernatural, this captivating tale takes readers on a thrilling journey filled with twists and turns.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Comedy

Themes: Perception of reality, Secrets and hidden connections
