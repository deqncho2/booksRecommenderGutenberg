Synopsis: 

In "In Eastern Seas: Or, the Commission of H.M.S. 'Iron Duke,' flag-ship in China, 1878-83," author J. J. Smith takes readers on a captivating journey through the exotic and mysterious Eastern Seas. Set aboard the renowned H.M.S. 'Iron Duke,' this thrilling narrative follows the ship's commission in China from 1878 to 1883. As the crew navigates treacherous waters, encounters with pirates, and diplomatic challenges, they find themselves immersed in a world of intrigue, danger, and cultural exploration. With vivid descriptions and compelling storytelling, Smith paints a vivid picture of life at sea during this pivotal period in history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Maritime

Themes: Exploration, Diplomacy, Cultural Encounter, Survival
