Synopsis: In the midst of the American Revolution, a young patriot named Horse-Shoe Robinson finds himself caught in a dangerous web of intrigue and betrayal. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of the Tory Ascendency, Horse-Shoe must rely on his wit, courage, and the loyalty of his fellow patriots to survive. With the fate of the revolution hanging in the balance, Horse-Shoe's actions will have far-reaching consequences that could change the course of history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Political Thriller

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Courage, Revolution
