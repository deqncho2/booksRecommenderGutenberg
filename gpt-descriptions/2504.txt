Synopsis: 
In 'Some Cities and San Francisco, and Resurgam', Bancroft, Hubert Howe takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant cities of the West Coast. From the bustling streets of San Francisco to the lesser-known corners of the region, this book offers a vivid exploration of the unique character and history of these cities. Through Bancroft's keen observations and engaging storytelling, readers will discover the hidden stories, remarkable individuals, and fascinating events that have shaped the growth and development of these urban landscapes.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, Travelogue, Biography

Themes: 
Urban development, Cultural diversity, Historical significance
