Synopsis: In "The Floral Symbolism of the Great Masters" by Elizabeth Haig, embark on a captivating journey through the world of art and flowers. Delve into the hidden meanings and symbolism behind the exquisite floral arrangements depicted in the works of renowned artists. From the vibrant tulips of Van Gogh to the delicate roses of Renoir, this book unveils the secrets and stories that lie within each brushstroke. As you explore the pages, you will discover a fascinating connection between nature, art, and the human experience, leaving you with a newfound appreciation for the beauty that surrounds us.

Genres: Art, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Symbolism, Nature, Aesthetics
