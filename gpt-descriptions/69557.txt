Synopsis: In "A Definition of Social Work: A Thesis in Sociology," Alice S. Cheyney delves into the complex world of social work, exploring its multifaceted nature and the impact it has on individuals and communities. Through a thought-provoking analysis, Cheyney uncovers the underlying principles and values that guide social work practice, shedding light on the challenges and rewards faced by those in this vital profession. With compelling insights and real-life examples, this thesis offers a fresh perspective on the transformative power of social work, leaving readers eager to explore the intricacies of this essential field.

Genres: Sociology, Academic, Research

Themes: Social justice, Community empowerment, Ethical practice
