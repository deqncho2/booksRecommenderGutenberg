Synopsis: 
In the small town of Las Palmas, a mysterious stranger arrives with a dark past and a hidden agenda. As he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and deceit, the townspeople find themselves caught in a thrilling game of cat and mouse. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the truth becomes more elusive. Will the stranger's true identity be revealed before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Western

Themes: Identity, Deception, Redemption
