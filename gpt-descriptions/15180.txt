Synopsis: 
In 'The Honorable Percival' by Alice Caldwell Hegan Rice, a young lawyer named Percival is determined to make a name for himself in the legal world. However, his ambitions are put to the test when he takes on a case that challenges his moral compass and forces him to confront the dark side of justice. As Percival delves deeper into the case, he finds himself entangled in a web of deceit, corruption, and personal sacrifice. Will he be able to navigate the treacherous waters of the legal system and maintain his integrity, or will he succumb to the pressures of power and ambition?

Genres: Legal fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Morality, Corruption, Ambition, Justice
