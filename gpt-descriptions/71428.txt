Synopsis: 
'Rings for the Finger' takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the fascinating world of rings from their earliest origins to the present day. George Frederick Kunz delves into the intricate details of ring making, materials used, and the rich history and symbolism behind these cherished pieces of jewelry. From rings of affection and love to those of engagement, wedding, commemoration, and mourning, this book offers a comprehensive exploration of the various types and purposes of rings throughout history. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, Kunz invites readers to discover the hidden stories and cultural significance behind these timeless treasures.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Archaeology

Themes: Jewelry, Symbolism, Cultural History
