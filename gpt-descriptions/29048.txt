Synopsis: 

"The Fabric of Civilization: A Short Survey of the Cotton Industry in the United States" takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating history and impact of the cotton industry in America. From its humble beginnings to its transformation into a global powerhouse, this book delves into the intricate web of economic, social, and cultural threads that have shaped the fabric of American society. Through vivid storytelling and insightful analysis, the Guaranty Trust Company of New York unveils the untold stories of the individuals, innovations, and challenges that have defined this influential industry. 

Genres: Historical, Economic, Cultural

Themes: Industrialization, Innovation, Social Impact
