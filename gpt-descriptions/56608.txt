Synopsis: In 'Songs Unsung' by Morris Lewis, a renowned musician mysteriously disappears, leaving behind a trail of unanswered questions and unfinished melodies. As his loved ones embark on a journey to uncover the truth, they are drawn into a world of secrets, betrayal, and the power of music. With each revelation, the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur, leading to a breathtaking climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of art and the depths of human emotion.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Obsession, Artistic Expression, Family Dynamics
