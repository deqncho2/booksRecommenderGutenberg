Synopsis: In 'Out Like a Light', a brilliant scientist invents a device that allows people to experience their dreams as vividly as reality. However, when the device falls into the wrong hands, it becomes a dangerous tool that blurs the line between dreams and reality, leading to unexpected consequences. As the protagonist races against time to reclaim the device, they must confront their own deepest fears and desires, navigating a thrilling and mind-bending journey through the realm of dreams.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Power and Responsibility, Exploration of the Subconscious Mind
