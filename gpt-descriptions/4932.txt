Synopsis: In 'A Knight of the White Cross: A Tale of the Siege of Rhodes,' follow the thrilling adventures of young Cuthbert as he joins the Knights of St. John during the epic siege of Rhodes. Amidst the clash of swords and the roar of cannons, Cuthbert must prove his worth as a knight, facing treacherous enemies, navigating political intrigue, and discovering the true meaning of honor and loyalty. Will he emerge victorious and fulfill his destiny as a knight of the White Cross?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Chivalry, Loyalty, Courage, Honor
