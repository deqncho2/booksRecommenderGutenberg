Synopsis: 
In 'Lanagan, Amateur Detective' by Edward H. Hurlbut, readers are introduced to the clever and resourceful protagonist, Lanagan, who finds himself entangled in a mysterious case of theft and deception. When a valuable artifact goes missing from a local museum, Lanagan's curiosity and sharp instincts lead him on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he encounters a web of secrets, unexpected allies, and dangerous adversaries. With each clue he uncovers, Lanagan becomes more determined to solve the case and bring the culprits to justice. Will his amateur detective skills be enough to crack the case and restore order?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Crime

Themes: Curiosity, Justice, Deception
