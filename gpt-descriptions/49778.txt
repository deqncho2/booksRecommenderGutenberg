Synopsis: 
In 'The Profligate: A Play in Four Acts', we are introduced to a young man named Leonard Charteris, who seems to have it all - wealth, charm, and a promising future. However, his reckless behavior and insatiable desire for pleasure threaten to unravel his seemingly perfect life. As Leonard navigates through a web of deceit, love, and betrayal, the consequences of his actions become increasingly dire. Will he be able to redeem himself and find true happiness, or will his profligate ways lead to his ultimate downfall?

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Romance

Themes: Morality, Consequences of Actions, Redemption
