Synopsis: 

In "Charles Duran: Or, The Career of a Bad Boy; By the author of "The Waldos," readers are introduced to the captivating and enigmatic character of Charles Duran. From his troubled childhood to his rebellious teenage years, this gripping narrative follows Charles as he navigates a world filled with mischief, danger, and unexpected twists. As the story unfolds, readers will be drawn into Charles' complex web of relationships, secrets, and personal growth, leaving them eager to uncover the truth behind his intriguing persona.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Consequences of Actions
