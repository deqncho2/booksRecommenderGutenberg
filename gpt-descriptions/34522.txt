Synopsis: In 'The Secret of the League: The Story of a Social War' by Bramah, Ernest, a mysterious secret society known as the League emerges, threatening to disrupt the delicate balance of society. As the protagonist delves deeper into the secrets of the League, he uncovers a web of intrigue, conspiracy, and hidden agendas. With danger lurking at every turn, he must navigate a treacherous world where loyalties are tested and the truth is elusive. This gripping tale of suspense and mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the enigma of the League.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secret societies, Conspiracy, Power struggle
