Synopsis: In this intriguing anonymous account, the Magistrate of the Office of Health in Venice unveils the extraordinary measures and precautions taken to safeguard public health. From the mysterious rituals performed by the city's officials to the ingenious methods employed to combat deadly diseases, this book offers a captivating glimpse into the hidden world of Venice's health authorities. As the Magistrate's narrative unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the secrets and strategies that have preserved the well-being of the city's inhabitants for centuries.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Medical Literature, Travelogue

Themes: Public Health, Disease Prevention, Historical Practices
