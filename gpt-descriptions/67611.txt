Synopsis: In 'The Old Card' by Pertwee, Roland, a mysterious old card is discovered, setting off a chain of events that leads the protagonist on a thrilling journey through time and space. As the secrets of the card unfold, the reader is taken on a captivating adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns. Will the protagonist be able to unlock the true power of the old card before it falls into the wrong hands?

Genres: Mystery, Science Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Time travel, Destiny, Power
