Synopsis: In 'Unwise Child' by Randall Garrett, a young boy named Jimmy is not your average child. With an IQ of 187, he possesses an incredible intellect that surpasses even the most brilliant minds of his time. When a group of scientists discovers Jimmy's extraordinary abilities, they recruit him to help solve a perplexing mystery that threatens the entire solar system. As Jimmy navigates through dangerous situations and encounters various alien species, he must rely on his exceptional intelligence to outwit his adversaries and save humanity from impending disaster.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Intelligence, Problem-solving, Human potential
