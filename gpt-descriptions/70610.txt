Synopsis: In 'The Grandfathers' War' by Leinster Murray, a young man discovers a hidden box of letters that reveal a secret about his family's past. As he delves deeper into the letters, he uncovers a captivating tale of love, sacrifice, and the devastating impact of war. With each letter, he becomes more entangled in the lives of his ancestors, leading him on a journey of self-discovery and understanding. As he uncovers the truth, he must confront his own beliefs and make choices that will shape his future.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Family Drama, Mystery

Themes: War, Family, Identity, Legacy
