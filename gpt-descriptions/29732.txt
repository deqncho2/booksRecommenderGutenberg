Synopsis: In "The Old Arm-Chair" by Eliza Cook, a mysterious antique armchair becomes the centerpiece of a young woman's life, leading her on a captivating journey of discovery and self-reflection. As she unravels the chair's enigmatic past, she finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and unexpected connections that challenge her perception of reality. This gripping tale explores the power of objects to hold memories and the transformative nature of uncovering hidden truths.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Memory, Secrets, Perception
