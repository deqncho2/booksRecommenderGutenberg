Synopsis:
In the historical novel "Domitia" by S. Baring-Gould, the reader is transported to ancient Rome, where the captivating and enigmatic Domitia Longina resides. As the wife of the future Emperor Domitian, Domitia's life is filled with intrigue, power struggles, and forbidden desires. Through her eyes, we witness the complex dynamics of Roman society, the treacherous politics of the imperial court, and the personal sacrifices one must make to survive in a world of ambition and betrayal. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, "Domitia" takes us on a thrilling journey through the heart of ancient Rome, where love, loyalty, and ambition collide.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Power and Ambition, Forbidden Love, Betrayal, Survival in a Corrupt Society
