Synopsis: In "Christie's Old Organ: or, Home, Sweet Home" by Mrs. O. F. Walton, readers are introduced to Christie, a young boy who finds solace in playing an old organ. When tragedy strikes, Christie's life takes an unexpected turn, leading him on a journey of self-discovery and redemption. This heartwarming tale explores the power of music, the importance of home, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Children's literature, Christian fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Music, Home, Redemption, Resilience
