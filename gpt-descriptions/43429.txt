Synopsis: 

In 'Cats: Their Points and Characteristics: With Curiosities of Cat Life, and a Chapter on Feline Ailments', Gordon Stables takes readers on a captivating journey into the mysterious world of cats. From their unique physical attributes to their intriguing behaviors, this book delves into the fascinating aspects of feline life. With a special focus on the various ailments that can affect our beloved feline companions, Stables provides valuable insights and advice for cat owners. Prepare to be enthralled by the enchanting tales and curious facts that unravel the secrets of these enigmatic creatures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Animal Care, Reference

Themes: Cats, Animal behavior, Pet care, Health and wellness
