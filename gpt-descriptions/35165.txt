Synopsis: In 'The Crooked Stick; Or, Pollie's Probation' by Boldrewood, Rolf, we follow the captivating journey of Pollie, a young woman who finds herself caught between duty and desire. Set in the rugged Australian outback, Pollie's life takes an unexpected turn when she is sent to live with her strict aunt and uncle. As she navigates the challenges of her new environment, she discovers a hidden world of secrets, forbidden love, and the power of self-discovery. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this novel explores the complexities of family, societal expectations, and the pursuit of personal happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Family, Love, Identity, Social Expectations
