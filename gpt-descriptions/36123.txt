Synopsis:
In the heart of San Francisco during the late 1800s, a mysterious and exclusive club known as The Comstock Club holds its members captive with its dark secrets and hidden agendas. When a young journalist named Henry Sinclair infiltrates the club, he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, power struggles, and forbidden desires. As he delves deeper into the club's twisted world, Henry must navigate treacherous alliances and confront his own demons in order to uncover the truth and save himself from the clutches of The Comstock Club.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Power and corruption, Secrets and deception, Identity and self-discovery
