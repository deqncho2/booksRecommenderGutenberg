Synopsis: 

In 'Pictures and Problems from London Police Courts', Thomas Holmes takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling streets of Victorian London, delving into the intriguing and often shocking cases that pass through the city's police courts. From petty thefts to scandalous affairs, Holmes presents a collection of real-life stories that will leave readers both enthralled and astounded. With vivid descriptions and a keen eye for detail, he brings to life the characters and events that unfold within the walls of these courts, offering a fascinating glimpse into the darker side of London society. 

Genres: Non-fiction, True Crime, Historical

Themes: Crime and Justice, Social Issues, Victorian Society
