Synopsis: In "A Military Genius: Life of Anna Ella Carroll of Maryland," Sarah Ellen Blackwell delves into the extraordinary life of Anna Ella Carroll, a remarkable woman who defied societal norms and became a military strategist during the American Civil War. From her early years as a political prodigy to her pivotal role in shaping military strategies, Carroll's story is one of resilience, determination, and unwavering patriotism. As Blackwell uncovers the hidden truths and accomplishments of this unsung hero, readers will be captivated by Carroll's brilliance and the impact she had on the course of history.

Genres: Biography, History, Military

Themes: Gender roles and expectations, Patriotism, Political influence, Women's empowerment
