Synopsis: In 'The King of Arcadia' by Lynde, Francis, a young man named Jack embarks on a thrilling adventure when he discovers a hidden world beyond his imagination. As he navigates through the enchanting land of Arcadia, Jack encounters mythical creatures, uncovers ancient secrets, and faces dangerous challenges that test his courage and determination. With the fate of Arcadia hanging in the balance, Jack must embrace his destiny as the chosen one and confront the ultimate evil that threatens to destroy everything he holds dear.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Hero's Journey, Destiny, Good vs. Evil
