Synopsis: In 'Ruby: A Story of the Australian Bush', Molly E. Jamieson takes readers on a captivating journey through the rugged and untamed Australian bush. The story revolves around Ruby, a young woman with a mysterious past, who finds herself entangled in a web of secrets, love, and adventure. As she navigates the harsh landscapes and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Ruby's resilience and determination are put to the test. With vivid descriptions and unexpected twists, this tale of self-discovery and survival will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Identity, Wilderness, Love and Loss
