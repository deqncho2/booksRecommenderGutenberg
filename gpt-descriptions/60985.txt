Synopsis: In 'Misinforming a Nation' by Wright, Willard Huntington, a renowned journalist uncovers a shocking conspiracy that threatens to manipulate the entire nation's perception of reality. As he delves deeper into the web of deceit, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of hidden agendas, powerful figures, and dangerous secrets. With the truth hanging in the balance, he races against time to expose the truth and save the nation from being misled. 

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Political Fiction

Themes: Media manipulation, Government secrecy, Truth and deception
