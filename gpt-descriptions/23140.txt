Synopsis: In 'The Death Shot: A Story Retold' by Reid, Mayne, a mysterious and deadly game of chance unfolds, captivating the reader with its thrilling twists and turns. As the protagonist finds himself entangled in a high-stakes wager, he must navigate a treacherous world where every move could be his last. With danger lurking at every corner, the story takes unexpected and thrilling directions, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Fate and destiny, Morality and ethics
