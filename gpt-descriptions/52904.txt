Synopsis: In 'Ventures Into Verse', H. L. Mencken presents a collection of forgotten and neglected poems, rescued from the depths of old files and given a new lease on life. From ballads to odes, rondeaux to roundels, this anthology showcases a diverse range of poetic forms and styles. Each poem is a hidden gem, waiting to be discovered and appreciated by modern readers. With wit, humor, and a touch of irreverence, Mencken invites us to delve into the potters' field of old files and unearth the treasures within. 

Genres: Poetry, Anthology

Themes: Rediscovery, Literary Resurrection
