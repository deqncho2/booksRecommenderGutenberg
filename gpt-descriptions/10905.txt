Synopsis: 
Phantom Fortune, a Novel by M. E. Braddon, takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of Isabel Gilbert. After a sudden twist of fate, Isabel finds herself thrust into a world of wealth and privilege, but soon discovers that her newfound fortune comes with a dark secret. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, Isabel must unravel the mysteries surrounding her inheritance and confront the ghosts of her past. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Victorian Literature

Themes: Wealth and Privilege, Secrets and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
