Synopsis: In "The Tallants of Barton, vol. 1 (of 3): A tale of fortune and finance," Joseph Hatton weaves a captivating story that follows the lives of the Tallant family as they navigate the intricate world of finance and the pursuit of wealth. Set in the bustling city of London during the 19th century, the Tallants find themselves entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances. As they strive to rise above their humble beginnings, they encounter love, betrayal, and the relentless pursuit of fortune. With each twist and turn, the Tallants must confront their own desires and ambitions, ultimately questioning the true cost of success.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Wealth, Social Class, Love, Betrayal
