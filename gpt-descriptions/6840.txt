Synopsis: In the charming village of Riseholme, the self-proclaimed queen, Lucia, reigns supreme over the social scene. With her impeccable taste, cunning wit, and relentless ambition, Lucia orchestrates a series of delightful and humorous events that keep the village on its toes. However, when a rival queen arrives, the battle for power and influence reaches new heights, leading to unexpected consequences and hilarious misunderstandings. Set in the early 20th century, 'Queen Lucia' is a delightful and witty tale of social intrigue and the pursuit of status in a small English village.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Fiction

Themes: Social hierarchy, Ambition, Power struggles
