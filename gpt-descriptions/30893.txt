Synopsis: In 'The Little Book of the Flag' by Eva March Tappan, embark on a captivating journey through the history and symbolism of flags. From ancient civilizations to modern nations, this concise yet enlightening book explores the power and significance behind these colorful emblems. Discover the stories of bravery, unity, and identity that flags have represented throughout time, and gain a deeper appreciation for the flags that fly high above us. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Reference

Themes: Symbolism, Identity, Nationalism
