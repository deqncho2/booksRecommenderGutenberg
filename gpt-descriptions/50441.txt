Synopsis: In 'Master of Life and Death' by Robert Silverberg, a brilliant scientist discovers a groundbreaking technology that allows him to control life and death. As he delves deeper into his newfound power, he becomes entangled in a web of moral dilemmas and personal consequences. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must confront the limits of his own humanity and make a choice that will change everything.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Ethics, Power, Identity, Mortality
