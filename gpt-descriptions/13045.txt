Synopsis: In 'War-Time Financial Problems' by Withers, Hartley, a young couple finds themselves caught in the midst of financial turmoil during a time of war. As they navigate the challenges of limited resources and uncertain futures, they must make difficult decisions that will shape their lives forever. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale explores the sacrifices and resilience of individuals facing adversity in a time of crisis.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, drama

Themes: Financial struggles, war, love, resilience
