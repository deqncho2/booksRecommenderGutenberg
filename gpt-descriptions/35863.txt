Synopsis: In 'The Pocahontas-John Smith Story' by Edmunds, Pocahontas Wight, embark on a captivating journey through the untold story of Pocahontas and John Smith. Set against the backdrop of the early colonization of America, this gripping tale explores the unlikely friendship that blossoms between a Native American princess and an English explorer. As their worlds collide, secrets, danger, and forbidden love intertwine, leading to a series of events that will forever change their lives. Prepare to be swept away by this enchanting and poignant tale of two individuals from different worlds, united by destiny.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Cultural clash, Friendship, Love and sacrifice
