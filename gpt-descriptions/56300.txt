Synopsis: 
'Low Tide on Grand Pré: A Book of Lyrics' by Carman, Bliss takes readers on a poetic journey through the enchanting landscapes of Grand Pré, a small village in Nova Scotia. Through vivid and evocative language, the author paints a mesmerizing picture of the natural beauty and the ebb and flow of life in this coastal community. From the gentle lapping of the tides to the whispers of the wind, Carman, Bliss captures the essence of Grand Pré in a collection of lyrical verses that explore the depths of human emotions and the timeless connection between nature and the human spirit.

Genres: Poetry, Nature Writing, Canadian Literature

Themes: Nature, Beauty, Transience, Human Emotions, Connection with the Natural World
