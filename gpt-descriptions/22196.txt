Synopsis: 

'Little Miss Grouch: A Narrative Based on the Log of Alexander Forsyth Smith's Maiden Transatlantic Voyage' takes readers on a thrilling journey across the Atlantic Ocean. The story follows the adventures of Alexander Forsyth Smith, a young sailor who finds himself aboard the ship 'Little Miss Grouch' on its maiden voyage. As the ship battles treacherous storms, encounters mysterious passengers, and faces unexpected challenges, Alexander's courage and determination are put to the test. With each passing day, secrets unravel, alliances form, and the true nature of the 'Little Miss Grouch' is revealed, leading to a climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Discovery
