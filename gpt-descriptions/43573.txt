Synopsis: In 'Savage Island: An Account of a Sojourn in Niué and Tonga', Basil Thomson takes readers on a captivating journey to the remote islands of Niué and Tonga. Through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, Thomson immerses us in the fascinating cultures, traditions, and landscapes of these South Pacific paradises. From encounters with fierce warriors to witnessing ancient rituals, the author's enthralling narrative paints a vivid picture of life on these savage islands. As Thomson navigates the challenges and wonders of his sojourn, readers will be captivated by the untamed beauty and mysterious allure of Niué and Tonga.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Anthropology

Themes: Cultural exploration, Survival, Nature's power
