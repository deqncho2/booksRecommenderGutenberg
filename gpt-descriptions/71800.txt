Synopsis: In 'Formula for Murder' by Milton A. Rothman, a brilliant chemist finds himself entangled in a web of deceit and danger when he stumbles upon a deadly formula that could change the course of history. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he becomes the target of ruthless individuals willing to do anything to possess the formula. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous world of espionage and betrayal, where even the closest allies may have hidden agendas. Will he be able to outsmart his enemies and protect the formula, or will he become another victim in this deadly game?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Science and technology, Espionage, Betrayal
