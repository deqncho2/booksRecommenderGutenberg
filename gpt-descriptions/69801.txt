Synopsis: 
"The dread Apache: That early-day scourge of the Southwest" by Freeman, Merrill Pingree is a gripping historical account that delves into the life and exploits of one of the most feared figures in the American Southwest. This captivating narrative takes readers on a thrilling journey through the untamed frontier, where the Apache warrior's cunning tactics and relentless pursuit of freedom leave an indelible mark on the region's history. From daring raids to epic battles, this book unveils the true nature of the Apache and the impact they had on the Southwest.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Native American history, Frontier life, Conflict and survival
