Synopsis: In "The Prince of the House of David," a young woman named Miriam finds herself caught in a web of love, betrayal, and destiny as she becomes entangled with a mysterious prince from a distant land. Set in biblical times, this captivating tale follows Miriam's journey as she navigates the complexities of faith, loyalty, and the power of true love.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Faith and spirituality, Destiny and fate
