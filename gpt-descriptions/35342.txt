Synopsis: 
"The Romance of a Poor Young Man: A Drama Adapted from the French of Octave Feuillet" follows the captivating journey of a destitute young man as he navigates the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century France, the story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, as the protagonist's determination to rise above his circumstances is tested by the allure of wealth and the allure of forbidden love. As he grapples with his own desires and the pressures of society, the young man must make difficult choices that will shape his future and reveal the true nature of his character.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Class and Ambition, Morality and Ethics
