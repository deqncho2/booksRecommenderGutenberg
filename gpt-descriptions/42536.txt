Synopsis:
In the remote village of Yonder, a young woman named Jane arrives to take up a teaching position. As she settles into her new role, she becomes intrigued by the mysterious history and secrets that seem to shroud the village. Jane's curiosity leads her on a journey of discovery, uncovering hidden truths and unearthing long-buried secrets that will challenge her perception of reality. As she delves deeper into the enigma of Yonder, Jane finds herself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and the supernatural, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres:
Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Supernatural

Themes:
Secrets and hidden histories, Perception vs. reality, Love and betrayal
