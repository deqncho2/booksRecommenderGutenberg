Synopsis: In the bustling city of Melbourne, a wealthy man is found dead in a hansom cab, and the mystery surrounding his murder unfolds through the eyes of two unlikely investigators. As they delve deeper into the case, they uncover a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice, Class Divide
