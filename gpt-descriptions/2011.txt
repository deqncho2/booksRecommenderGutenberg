Synopsis:
'Rudder Grange' by Frank R. Stockton is a delightful and humorous tale that follows the adventures of a newlywed couple, the Hubbards, as they embark on a unique and unconventional living arrangement. Seeking a change of pace from city life, the Hubbards decide to move into a dilapidated old houseboat named Rudder Grange. As they settle into their new floating home, they encounter a cast of eccentric characters and find themselves in a series of comical and unexpected situations. From navigating the challenges of boat life to dealing with peculiar neighbors, the Hubbards' journey on Rudder Grange is filled with laughter, romance, and a touch of mystery.

Genres:
Humor, Romance, Adventure

Themes:
New beginnings, Unconventional living, Adaptation to change
