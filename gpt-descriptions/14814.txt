Synopsis: In 'The Tale of Jemima Puddle-Duck', a naive and determined duck named Jemima embarks on a perilous adventure to fulfill her dream of becoming a mother. Along the way, she encounters a charming gentleman who offers to help her, but things may not be as they seem. Will Jemima's determination lead her to happiness or danger?

Genres: Children's literature, Animal fiction, Adventure

Themes: Determination, Trust, Deception
