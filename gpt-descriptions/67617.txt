Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 147, July 3, 1915: On Death's Trail; or, Nick Carter's Strangest Case', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing mystery when a series of bizarre deaths occur in quick succession. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Carter uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that lead him on a thrilling chase to catch a cunning and elusive killer. With time running out, Nick Carter must rely on his sharp intellect and unparalleled detective skills to solve his strangest case yet.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Pursuit of Justice
