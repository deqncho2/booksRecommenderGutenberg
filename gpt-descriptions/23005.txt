Synopsis: In "Eli: First published in the 'Century Magazine'," Heman White Chaplin takes us on a captivating journey through the life of Eli, a young man with a mysterious past. Set in the late 19th century, Eli's story unfolds against the backdrop of a rapidly changing America. From his humble beginnings in a small New England town to his encounters with enigmatic characters and unexpected adventures, Eli's path is filled with both triumphs and tribulations. As the layers of Eli's past are gradually peeled away, the reader is left wondering about the true nature of his identity and the secrets that lie hidden within him.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Self-discovery
