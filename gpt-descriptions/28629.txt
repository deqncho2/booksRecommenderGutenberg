Synopsis: 
In 'Phoebe, Junior' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), we are introduced to the charming and spirited Phoebe Beecham, a young woman who finds herself navigating the complexities of society and love in Victorian England. As Phoebe embarks on a journey of self-discovery, she encounters a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own secrets and desires. With wit and grace, Phoebe must navigate the treacherous waters of romance, family expectations, and societal norms, all while staying true to herself. Will she find the happiness and fulfillment she seeks, or will she be forced to conform to the expectations of others?

Genres: 
Romance, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Identity, Love and Relationships, Society and Conformity
