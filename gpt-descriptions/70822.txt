Synopsis: In the second volume of 'The Tallants of Barton', the captivating tale of fortune and finance continues to unfold. The Tallant family finds themselves embroiled in a web of intrigue and deception as they navigate the treacherous world of high society and business. With unexpected alliances, hidden secrets, and a relentless pursuit of wealth, the Tallants must confront the consequences of their choices and face the challenges that threaten to tear them apart. As their fortunes rise and fall, the family's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover the fate of the Tallants of Barton.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Wealth and Ambition, Family Dynamics, Society and Class
