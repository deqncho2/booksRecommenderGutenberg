Synopsis: In "The Rivet in Grandfather's Neck: A Comedy of Limitations," James Branch Cabell takes readers on a whimsical journey through the life of Felix Kennaston, a man who finds himself trapped in the confines of his own imagination. As Kennaston navigates the complexities of love, art, and societal expectations, he grapples with the limitations imposed upon him by both himself and the world around him. With a touch of satire and a dash of humor, Cabell weaves a tale that explores the boundaries of human existence and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Fantasy

Themes: Imagination, Self-expression, Societal constraints
