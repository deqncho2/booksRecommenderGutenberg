Synopsis: In 'Tibby', delve into a captivating world where psychic forces and telepathy intertwine, following the extraordinary journey of a young girl named Tibby. As Tibby discovers her unique abilities, she is thrust into a realm of mystery and danger, where she must navigate the complexities of her powers and the secrets they hold. With unexpected twists and turns, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning each page to uncover the truth behind Tibby's extraordinary gifts.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Psychic abilities, Identity, Coming of age
