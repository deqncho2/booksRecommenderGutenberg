Synopsis: In 'Reluctant Genius' by Henry Slesar, a brilliant but reclusive scientist is forced to confront his past when a mysterious woman enters his life. As their paths intertwine, secrets are revealed and the boundaries of reality are tested. With elements of suspense and psychological intrigue, this captivating novel explores the power of memory, the nature of identity, and the consequences of unchecked ambition.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Science Fiction

Themes: Memory, Identity, Ambition
