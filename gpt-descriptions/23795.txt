Synopsis: In "Katie Robertson: A Girl's Story of Factory Life," we follow the journey of Katie, a young girl who finds herself thrust into the harsh realities of factory work during the Industrial Revolution. As she navigates the dangerous and grueling conditions of the factory, Katie's determination and resilience are put to the test. Along the way, she forms unexpected friendships and discovers the power of unity and standing up for what is right. Will Katie be able to overcome the challenges she faces and find a better life for herself?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Social Commentary

Themes: Industrialization, Child Labor, Friendship, Resilience, Social Justice
