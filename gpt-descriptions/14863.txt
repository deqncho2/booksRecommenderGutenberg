Synopsis: In 'The Tinder-Box' by Maria Thompson Daviess, a young woman named Polly is unexpectedly thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when she discovers a hidden secret in her family's ancestral home. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Polly finds herself entangled in a web of romance, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, she must navigate the treacherous path of love and loyalty, all while trying to uncover the truth behind the tinder-box. 

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Love and loyalty, Intrigue and suspense
