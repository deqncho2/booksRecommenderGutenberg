Synopsis: 
'Four Short Plays' by Florence Eveleen Eleanore Olliffe, Lady Bell, is a collection of four captivating and thought-provoking plays that delve into the complexities of human relationships and the depths of the human psyche. Each play presents a unique and compelling story, exploring themes of love, betrayal, redemption, and the pursuit of happiness. From the intimate struggles of a married couple to the haunting consequences of a secret affair, these plays offer a glimpse into the intricacies of human emotions and the consequences of our actions. With richly drawn characters and powerful dialogue, 'Four Short Plays' takes readers on a journey of self-discovery and introspection, leaving them pondering the complexities of the human experience.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Love, Betrayal, Redemption, Human Relationships, Consequences of Actions
