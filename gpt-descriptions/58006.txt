Synopsis: In "The Glorious Law-Giver, and the Obedient Subject," Reverend John Church delivers a powerful sermon on the duty of all believers to commemorate the death of the Redeemer in the Lord's Supper. With a focus on the authority of Christ, the nature of the ordinance, and the description of those who have the right to partake, Church explores the significance of this sacred act and its profound impact on the lives of believers.

Genres: Sermon, Religious Literature

Themes: Commemoration, Redemption, Authority, Obedience
