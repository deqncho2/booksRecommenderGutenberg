Synopsis: In 'Side Show Studies' by Metcalfe, Francis, step right up and enter the captivating world of the circus! This collection of short stories takes readers on a thrilling journey through the lives of the performers, revealing the hidden depths behind the dazzling spectacle. From the mysterious fortune teller to the fearless tightrope walker, each character's story unfolds with unexpected twists and turns, exploring the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of dreams. Prepare to be mesmerized by the enchanting tales that lie beneath the surface of the big top.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Magical Realism

Themes: Identity, Sacrifice, Illusion, Acceptance
