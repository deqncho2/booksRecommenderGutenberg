Synopsis: 
In 'The Man Who Lost Himself', a wealthy Englishman named Everard Dominey embarks on a journey to Africa, but a series of unexpected events leads to his disappearance. When he finally returns to England, he finds himself in a baffling situation - everyone believes he is someone else. As he tries to unravel the mystery and reclaim his identity, he is faced with a web of deceit, betrayal, and danger. With his life hanging in the balance, Dominey must navigate through a world where nothing is as it seems, and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal
