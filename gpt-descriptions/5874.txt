Synopsis: 
In 'Dawn' by Eleanor H. Porter, a young woman named Dawn finds herself at a crossroads in life after a series of unexpected events. With her world turned upside down, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, facing challenges and making life-altering choices along the way. As Dawn navigates through love, loss, and the pursuit of happiness, readers will be captivated by her resilience and determination to find her true path.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Love and Loss
