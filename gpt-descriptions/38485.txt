Synopsis: 

In 'Ancient Pagan and Modern Christian Symbolism', authors Inman and Newton delve into the fascinating world of ancient pagan and modern Christian symbolism, exploring the hidden meanings behind various symbols and their significance in different cultures. From the enigmatic Baal worship to the mysterious Assyrian sacred "grove," this book uncovers the secrets of these ancient symbols and their connections to modern Christian practices. Through a captivating essay, the authors shed light on the intriguing world of symbolism, offering readers a deeper understanding of the rich tapestry of religious and cultural traditions.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, Symbolism

Themes: Symbolism, Religion, Cultural Traditions
