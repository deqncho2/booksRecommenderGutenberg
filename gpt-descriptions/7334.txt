Synopsis: 

In 'With Buller in Natal, Or, a Born Leader' by G. A. Henty, readers are transported to the tumultuous times of the Anglo-Zulu War in 1879. The story follows the adventures of a young Englishman named Ralph Conway, who finds himself caught up in the conflict while on a journey to South Africa. As Ralph becomes entangled in the war, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, face formidable enemies, and prove himself as a true leader. With his courage and resourcefulness, Ralph rises to the occasion, earning the respect of his comrades and the admiration of his superiors. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Leadership, Courage, Conflict, Loyalty
