Synopsis: In "Madam Constantia: The Romance of a Prisoner of War in the Revolution (South Carolina)" by Jefferson Carter, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the American Revolution. The story revolves around a captivating prisoner of war named Constantia, whose life takes an unexpected turn when she finds herself entangled in a web of love, danger, and intrigue. As she navigates the treacherous landscape of war-torn South Carolina, Constantia must make difficult choices that will shape her destiny and challenge her notions of loyalty and sacrifice. This gripping tale of romance and resilience will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the pages of "Madam Constantia."

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty, Resilience, War and Conflict
