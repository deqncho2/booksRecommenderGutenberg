Synopsis: 
'A Legend of Old Persia and Other Poems' by Tennyson, A. B. S. takes readers on a captivating journey through ancient Persia, where legends come to life and emotions run deep. This collection of poems weaves together tales of love, honor, and sacrifice, transporting readers to a world of enchantment and wonder. From the epic story of a heroic prince to the tender musings of a lovelorn poet, each poem in this collection is a window into the rich tapestry of Persian culture and mythology. With vivid imagery and lyrical language, Tennyson, A. B. S. invites readers to immerse themselves in the beauty and complexity of Old Persia.

Genres: Poetry, Historical Fiction, Mythology

Themes: Love, Honor, Sacrifice, Mythology, Cultural Identity
