Synopsis: In 'Tattine' by Ruth Ogden, a group of adventurous children embark on a summer filled with mystery and excitement. When they stumble upon a hidden treasure map, they set off on a thrilling quest that takes them through secret passages, encounters with peculiar characters, and unexpected twists. As they unravel the clues, the children learn valuable lessons about friendship, bravery, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Bravery, Imagination
