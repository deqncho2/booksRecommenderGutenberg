Synopsis: 
'From Immigrant to Inventor' is a captivating memoir that chronicles the extraordinary journey of Michael Pupin, a Serbian immigrant who overcame immense challenges to become one of America's most celebrated inventors. From his humble beginnings in a small village to his groundbreaking discoveries in the field of telecommunications, Pupin's story is one of resilience, determination, and the pursuit of the American dream. As he navigates the complexities of a new country, Pupin's relentless pursuit of knowledge and his unwavering belief in the power of education propel him towards a remarkable destiny. This inspiring tale of triumph against all odds will leave readers in awe of Pupin's indomitable spirit and his lasting impact on the world.

Genres: Biography, Memoir, History

Themes: Immigration, Perseverance, Education, Innovation
