Synopsis: In the final installment of the "Far above rubies" series, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and the characters find themselves facing unexpected challenges. As the story unfolds, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the lives of the protagonists, who must navigate love, betrayal, and the pursuit of their dreams. With each twist and turn, the stakes are raised, leaving the reader eagerly turning the pages to discover how it all concludes.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Betrayal and redemption, Pursuit of dreams
