Synopsis: In 'The Man Without a Conscience; Or, From Rogue to Convict' by Carter, Nicholas (House name), follow the gripping tale of a man whose life takes a dark turn when he loses his moral compass. Set in the seedy underbelly of a bustling city, this thrilling novel explores the transformation of a once-charming rogue into a hardened convict. As the protagonist navigates a treacherous world of crime and punishment, readers will be captivated by the twists and turns that lead him down a path of redemption or destruction. 

Genres: Crime fiction, Thriller, Psychological drama

Themes: Morality, Redemption, Consequences of one's actions
