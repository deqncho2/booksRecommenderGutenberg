Synopsis: 
'Cousin Phillis' by Elizabeth Gaskell is a captivating novella that follows the story of Paul Manning, a young engineer who moves to the countryside to work on a railway project. There, he becomes acquainted with his cousin Phillis, a gentle and innocent young woman. As Paul spends more time with Phillis, he finds himself drawn to her charm and intelligence. However, their blossoming relationship is soon tested by unforeseen circumstances, leading to a poignant exploration of love, loss, and the complexities of human emotions.

Genres: 
Romance, Coming-of-age, Victorian Literature

Themes: 
Love and desire, Innocence and experience, Social class
