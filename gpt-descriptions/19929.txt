Synopsis: In 'Cad Metti, The Female Detective Strategist; Or, Dudie Dunne Again in the Field' by Old Sleuth, the brilliant detective Cad Metti returns to solve a perplexing case alongside her trusted partner Dudie Dunne. This time, they find themselves entangled in a web of deceit and danger as they investigate a series of mysterious disappearances in the city. With their sharp wit and cunning strategies, Cad and Dudie must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects to uncover the truth before it's too late. Will they be able to outsmart the cunning mastermind behind it all? Prepare for a thrilling adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Thriller

Themes: Crime-solving, Female Empowerment, Deception
