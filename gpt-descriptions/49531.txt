Synopsis: In 'A Man Obsessed' by Alan Edward Nourse, a brilliant scientist becomes consumed by a dangerous obsession that threatens to unravel his life and those around him. As he delves deeper into his fixation, he must confront the consequences of his actions and the moral dilemmas that arise. This gripping tale explores the dark side of human nature and the lengths one man will go to in pursuit of his desires.

Genres: Science Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Obsession, Morality, Consequences
