Synopsis: 
'Practical Boat-Sailing: A Concise and Simple Treatise' by Frazar Douglas is a captivating guide that takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the art of boat-sailing. With a perfect blend of technical expertise and storytelling, this book offers invaluable insights into the world of sailing, providing practical advice and tips for both novice and experienced sailors. From mastering the basics of navigation to understanding the intricacies of wind and weather, Douglas's engaging narrative style keeps readers hooked as they embark on a thrilling adventure on the open seas.

Genres: Non-fiction, Sports, Adventure

Themes: Seamanship, Navigation, Skill Development
