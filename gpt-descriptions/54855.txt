Synopsis: In 'The New Abelard: A Romance, Volume 1', Buchanan takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of two star-crossed lovers, Adrian Earnshaw and Hilda Tregellis. Set in the picturesque landscapes of Cornwall, this enthralling romance explores the forbidden love between a brilliant young poet and a beautiful, enigmatic woman. As their paths intertwine, secrets, betrayal, and societal expectations threaten to tear them apart. Will their love conquer all, or will fate conspire against them?

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, societal expectations, betrayal, fate
