Synopsis: 
'Early Woodcut Initials: Containing over Thirteen Hundred Reproductions of Ornamental Letters of the Fifteenth and Sixteenth Centuries' by Oscar Jennings is a captivating exploration of the intricate and visually stunning world of ornamental letters from the 15th and 16th centuries. This book showcases over thirteen hundred reproductions of woodcut initials, offering a glimpse into the artistic craftsmanship and creativity of the time. From delicate floral motifs to bold and elaborate designs, these ornamental letters transport readers to a bygone era of exquisite artistry and attention to detail. 

Genres: Art, History, Design

Themes: Ornamentation, Typography, Historical Artistry
