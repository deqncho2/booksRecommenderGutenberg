Synopsis: 

'Account of an expedition to the interior of New Holland' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the unexplored lands of Australia. Written by Richard Whately and Mary, Lady Fox, this captivating work recounts their extraordinary expedition into the heart of New Holland, filled with encounters with indigenous peoples, breathtaking landscapes, and unexpected challenges. As they delve deeper into the unknown, the explorers uncover secrets that will forever change their understanding of this mysterious land. 

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Encounter, Discovery
