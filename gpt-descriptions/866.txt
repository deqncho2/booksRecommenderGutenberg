Synopsis: In 'The Cost of Kindness' by Jerome K. Jerome, a seemingly ordinary man finds himself caught in a web of unexpected events after a simple act of kindness. As he delves deeper into the consequences of his actions, he discovers a hidden world of secrets, danger, and personal transformation. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate the blurred lines between right and wrong, ultimately questioning the true cost of kindness.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Morality, Consequences of Actions, Redemption
