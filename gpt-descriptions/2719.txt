Synopsis: 
In 'Greville Fane' by Henry James, a renowned author named Greville Fane finds himself in a peculiar situation when a young woman claims to be his daughter from a past affair. As Fane grapples with the truth of her claim, he is forced to confront his own past and the consequences of his actions. The story takes unexpected twists and turns, exploring themes of identity, family secrets, and the complexities of human relationships.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Drama

Themes: Identity, Family, Secrets, Consequences, Relationships
