Synopsis: In 'An Old Story of My Farming Days Vol. 2 (of 3).: (Ut Mine Stromtid)' by Fritz Reuter, the protagonist continues to navigate the challenges and joys of rural life in the 19th century. As he faces personal and professional setbacks, he finds solace in the beauty of nature and the resilience of the human spirit. With vivid descriptions and heartfelt anecdotes, this volume delves deeper into the protagonist's journey, offering a captivating glimpse into the trials and triumphs of farming life.

Genres: Historical fiction, Autobiographical novel, Rural literature

Themes: Resilience, Nature, Rural life, Personal growth
