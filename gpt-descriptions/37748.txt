Synopsis: In 'How to Collect a Doctor Bill' by Davis, Franklyn Pierre, a young woman finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and deceit when she takes on the task of collecting an unpaid doctor bill. As she delves deeper into the case, she uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to unravel her own life. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must navigate a dangerous game of cat and mouse, all while trying to collect what is rightfully owed. Will she succeed in her mission, or will the truth prove to be more elusive than she ever imagined?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Justice, Redemption
