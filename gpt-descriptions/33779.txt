Synopsis: 

In 'Majesty: A Novel' by Louis Couperus, we are transported to the opulent world of European royalty. The story revolves around the enigmatic Queen Sophie, who finds herself torn between her duty as a monarch and her desire for personal happiness. As she navigates the treacherous waters of power, love, and betrayal, the reader is drawn into a captivating tale of intrigue and passion. With vivid descriptions and complex characters, Couperus paints a mesmerizing portrait of a queen struggling to maintain her majesty in a world filled with secrets and hidden agendas.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Power and Responsibility, Love and Sacrifice, Betrayal and Loyalty
