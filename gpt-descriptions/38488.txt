Synopsis: 
'Folk-Tales of Bengal' by Day, Lal Behari is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that transport readers to the mystical world of Bengal. From talking animals and magical creatures to brave heroes and cunning villains, these folk tales are filled with adventure, wisdom, and a touch of the supernatural. Each story is beautifully woven with vivid imagery and rich cultural traditions, offering a glimpse into the folklore and oral traditions of Bengal. As readers delve into these tales, they will be captivated by the timeless themes of love, bravery, morality, and the power of storytelling that resonate throughout the collection.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural

Themes: Love, Bravery, Morality, Power of Storytelling
