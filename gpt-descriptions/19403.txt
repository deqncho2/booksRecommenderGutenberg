Synopsis: In the small town of Kent, a seemingly innocent game of bridge takes a sinister turn when one of the players is found dead. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, motives are questioned, and the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. With each twist and turn, the reader is left guessing who could be behind the murder, and what dark secrets lie beneath the surface of this seemingly peaceful community.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town secrets
