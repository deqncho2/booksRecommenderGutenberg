Synopsis: 

In "Traditions of Edinburgh" by Robert Chambers, embark on a captivating journey through the rich history and enchanting tales of the Scottish capital. From the mysterious legends surrounding the ancient castle to the eerie stories of haunted closes, this book unveils the hidden secrets and forgotten traditions that have shaped Edinburgh into the captivating city it is today. Delve into the lives of the city's inhabitants, both past and present, as you explore the dark and intriguing corners of Edinburgh's past. 

Genres: Historical fiction, folklore, travelogue

Themes: History, folklore, urban legends, cultural heritage
