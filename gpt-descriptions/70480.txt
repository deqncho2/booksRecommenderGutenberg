Synopsis: 
In "Old Cape Cod: The Land, The Men, The Sea," Mary Rogers Bangs takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Cape Cod. Through vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, she unveils the rich history, unique landscapes, and the resilient spirit of the people who call this place home. From the picturesque lighthouses to the bustling fishing industry, from the charming coastal towns to the treacherous storms that shape the lives of its inhabitants, this book paints a vivid portrait of a place that is both timeless and ever-changing. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: History and heritage, Nature and environment, Community and resilience
