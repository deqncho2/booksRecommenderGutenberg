Synopsis: In 'Rescue Dog of the High Pass' by Jim Kjelgaard, a young boy named Link Stevens embarks on a thrilling adventure in the rugged mountains of the High Pass. When a devastating storm strikes, Link's courage is put to the test as he must rely on the help of a loyal and intelligent rescue dog to save lives and overcome the treacherous conditions. As they navigate through dangerous terrain and face unexpected challenges, Link and his canine companion forge an unbreakable bond that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Adventure, Action, Animal Fiction

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Survival
