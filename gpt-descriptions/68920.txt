Synopsis: In 'Captain Balaam of the 'Cormorant', and other sea comedies', Roberts Morley takes readers on a hilarious and adventurous journey through the high seas. Join Captain Balaam and his eccentric crew as they navigate treacherous waters, encounter peculiar characters, and find themselves in uproarious situations. From mischievous pirates to bumbling sailors, this collection of sea comedies is filled with witty dialogue, unexpected twists, and laugh-out-loud moments that will keep readers hooked until the very last page.

Genres: Comedy, Adventure, Maritime Fiction

Themes: Humor, Friendship, Exploration
