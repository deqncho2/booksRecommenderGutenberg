Synopsis: In "The League of the Scarlet Pimpernel," the enigmatic hero known as the Scarlet Pimpernel returns to France during the height of the French Revolution, risking his life to save innocent lives from the guillotine. As the French government intensifies its efforts to capture him, the Scarlet Pimpernel must outwit his enemies and navigate a treacherous web of deception, danger, and romance. With each daring rescue, the stakes grow higher, and the fate of countless lives hangs in the balance.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Heroism, Sacrifice, Love, Loyalty, Revolution
