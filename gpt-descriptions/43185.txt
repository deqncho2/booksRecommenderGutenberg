Synopsis: 
'By Force of Impulse: A Drama in Five Acts' by Harry V. Vogt is a gripping tale that follows the lives of a group of individuals whose paths intertwine in unexpected ways. Set against the backdrop of a bustling city, the story delves into the complexities of human nature, exploring the depths of ambition, love, and betrayal. As the characters navigate through their desires and confront the consequences of their actions, the tension rises, leading to a climactic finale that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Ambition, Love, Betrayal
