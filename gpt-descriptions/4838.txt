Synopsis: In "History of the United Netherlands from the Death of William the Silent to the Twelve Year's Truce, 1584-85a," John Lothrop Motley takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous period following the assassination of William the Silent. Amidst political intrigue, religious conflicts, and military campaigns, Motley paints a vivid picture of the struggle for independence in the Netherlands. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for storytelling, he brings to life the key figures and events that shaped this pivotal era in Dutch history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Political history

Themes: Independence and resistance, Religious conflict, Political intrigue, Leadership and statesmanship
