Synopsis: In "The Orphan; Or, The Unhappy Marriage," the reader is transported to a world of forbidden love, betrayal, and heart-wrenching tragedy. Set in the 17th century, the story revolves around Monimia, a young and innocent orphan who finds herself caught in the midst of a tumultuous and ill-fated marriage. As secrets unravel and passions ignite, the characters are faced with impossible choices that lead to devastating consequences. With its gripping plot and complex characters, this tragedy explores the depths of human emotions and the consequences of societal constraints.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama, Romance

Themes: Forbidden love, Betrayal, Fate, Morality, Society's expectations
