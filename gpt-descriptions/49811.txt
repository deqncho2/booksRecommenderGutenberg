Synopsis: In 'The Mentor: The Conquest of the Poles, Serial No. 37', Robert E. Peary takes us on a thrilling adventure to the uncharted territories of the North and South Poles. Join him as he faces treacherous conditions, encounters indigenous cultures, and battles against the forces of nature in his relentless pursuit of conquering these icy frontiers. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding moments, this gripping account will leave readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of Peary and his team.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Exploration

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Perseverance, Cultural encounters
