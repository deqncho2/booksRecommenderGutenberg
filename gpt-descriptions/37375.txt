Synopsis: In 'Legends of the North: The Guidman O' Inglismill and The Fairy Bride' by Patrick Buchan, a young man named Jamie finds himself entangled in a mysterious world of folklore and enchantment. When he encounters a captivating fairy bride, his life takes an unexpected turn as he becomes embroiled in a battle between ancient forces. As Jamie delves deeper into the legends of the North, he must navigate treacherous landscapes and face perilous challenges to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Myth and legend, Love and sacrifice, Coming of age
