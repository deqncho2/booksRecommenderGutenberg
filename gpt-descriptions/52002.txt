Synopsis: In the thrilling conclusion of "Mitchelhurst Place: A Novel," secrets are unraveled, alliances are tested, and the fate of the estate hangs in the balance. As the mysterious events surrounding the disappearance of Lady Mitchelhurst continue to haunt the inhabitants, the truth slowly emerges, revealing shocking revelations and unexpected connections. With danger lurking around every corner, the characters must navigate a treacherous web of deceit, betrayal, and forbidden love, leading to a climactic showdown that will determine the future of Mitchelhurst Place.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Forbidden love, Identity, Redemption
