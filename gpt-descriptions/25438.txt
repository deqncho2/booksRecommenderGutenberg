Synopsis: In 'The Airlords of Han' by Philip Francis Nowlan, the world is under the oppressive rule of the Han, a technologically advanced civilization from another planet. As the Han's domination spreads, a group of rebels led by Anthony Rogers, a man from the past who has been awakened from suspended animation, must fight against the tyrannical overlords. With thrilling aerial battles, daring escapes, and unexpected alliances, this action-packed science fiction adventure explores the struggle for freedom and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Dystopian

Themes: Oppression, Rebellion, Technology, Survival
