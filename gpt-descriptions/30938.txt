Synopsis: 
Polly's Senior Year at Boarding School takes readers on a captivating journey through the ups and downs of a young girl's final year at a prestigious boarding school. As Polly navigates the challenges of academics, friendships, and self-discovery, she finds herself caught in a web of secrets and unexpected twists that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With each page turn, the story unravels the mysteries surrounding Polly's past and the choices she must make for her future. Will she find the courage to confront her fears and embrace her true self? This coming-of-age tale is a rollercoaster of emotions, filled with heartwarming moments, unexpected surprises, and the power of resilience.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Mystery, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Self-discovery, Friendship, Secrets, Resilience
