Synopsis: In "The Little Colonel's Chum: Mary Ware," Mary Ware, a spirited and independent young woman, finds herself in a new town, facing challenges and making unexpected friendships. As she navigates through the ups and downs of her new life, Mary's determination and kindness shine through, leaving a lasting impact on those around her. With her infectious spirit and unwavering loyalty, Mary becomes a beloved chum to the Little Colonel and her friends, bringing joy and adventure to their lives.

Genres: Children's literature, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Friendship, Independence, Resilience
