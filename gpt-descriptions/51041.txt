Synopsis: In 'The Horses of the Sahara and the Manners of the Desert', Daumas takes us on a captivating journey through the vast and mysterious Sahara desert. Through his vivid descriptions and personal experiences, he introduces us to the magnificent horses that roam the desert and the unique customs and traditions of the desert dwellers. From thrilling desert chases to encounters with nomadic tribes, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a world few have experienced.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Cultural Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Horseback Riding, Cultural Traditions, Survival in Harsh Environments
