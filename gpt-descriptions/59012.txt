Synopsis: 
In 'Young Jack Harkaway Fighting the Pirates of the Red Sea', readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as young Jack Harkaway finds himself caught in the treacherous world of pirates. Set against the backdrop of the Red Sea, Jack must navigate dangerous waters, outsmart cunning adversaries, and uncover hidden secrets. With his quick wit and fearless spirit, Jack becomes an unlikely hero, facing perilous challenges and unexpected twists at every turn. Will he be able to outmaneuver the pirates and save the day?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
