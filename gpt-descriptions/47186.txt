Synopsis: In 'Model Women' by Anderson, William, a young aspiring model named Emma finds herself thrust into the glamorous and cutthroat world of high fashion. As she navigates the treacherous waters of the industry, she must learn to balance her dreams with the harsh realities of the business. With unexpected twists and turns, Emma's journey takes her from the runways of New York to the fashion capitals of the world, where she discovers the true cost of success and the power of staying true to oneself.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Identity, Beauty standards, Self-discovery
