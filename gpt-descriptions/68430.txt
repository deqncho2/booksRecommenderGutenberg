Synopsis: 
'Isola; or, The disinherited: A revolt for woman and all the disinherited' by Dixie, Florence, Lady is a gripping and thought-provoking novel that follows the journey of Isola, a young woman who defies societal norms and embarks on a quest for justice and equality. Set in the backdrop of a patriarchal society, Isola challenges the oppressive systems that confine women and the disenfranchised. As she navigates through various obstacles and encounters a diverse cast of characters, Isola's determination and resilience inspire a revolution that seeks to liberate not only women but all those who have been marginalized and oppressed. 

Genres: Fiction, Feminist Literature, Social Commentary

Themes: Gender Equality, Social Justice, Rebellion against Oppression
