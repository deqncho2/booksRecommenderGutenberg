Synopsis: In the small village of Eldridge, a group of unlikely heroes embarks on a perilous quest to slay the fearsome dragons that have plagued their land for centuries. Led by the enigmatic and battle-hardened warrior, Sir Roland, this diverse band of misfits must overcome their own personal demons and forge an unbreakable bond in order to face the ultimate challenge. As they journey through treacherous landscapes and encounter mythical creatures, they discover that the true meaning of bravery lies not in the act of slaying dragons, but in the courage to confront their own inner dragons. 

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Action

Themes: Bravery, Friendship, Overcoming adversity
