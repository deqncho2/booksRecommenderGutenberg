Synopsis: 

In 'Mount Everest, the Reconnaissance, 1921', three daring explorers embark on a treacherous journey to conquer the world's highest peak. Led by the fearless George Mallory, the team faces extreme weather conditions, dangerous terrain, and the constant threat of avalanches. As they push the limits of human endurance, they must confront their own fears and doubts, driven by an insatiable desire to reach the summit. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding moments, this gripping account takes readers on an unforgettable adventure that will leave them breathless.

Genres: Adventure, Non-fiction, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Perseverance, Human ambition
