Synopsis: In 'Jane Allen, Center' by Edith Bancroft, readers are introduced to Jane Allen, a young woman with a passion for basketball. As she enters high school, Jane faces numerous challenges and obstacles that test her determination and resilience. Alongside her friends and teammates, Jane navigates the complexities of friendship, rivalry, and personal growth, all while striving to become the best center on the basketball court. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story explores the triumphs and tribulations of Jane's journey towards achieving her dreams.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Sports Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Self-discovery
