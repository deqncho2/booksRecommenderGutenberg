Synopsis: 

"The Overland Route to the Road of a Thousand Wonders" takes readers on a captivating journey along the Union Pacific and Southern Pacific Railroads, spanning the magnificent distance from Omaha to San Francisco. This remarkable 1,800-mile expedition unveils a world where the bison and the Indian once reigned, offering a glimpse into the rich history and breathtaking landscapes of the American West. 

Genres: Historical, Travel, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Manifest Destiny, Cultural Encounter
