Synopsis: In 'The Tale of the Pie and the Patty Pan', Beatrix Potter takes readers on a delightful adventure filled with mystery and mischief. When a well-mannered cat named Ribby invites her friend Duchess to tea, things take an unexpected turn as a pie and a patty pan become the center of a comical mix-up. As the two friends navigate their way through misunderstandings and surprises, readers are treated to a charming tale that will keep them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Hospitality, Communication
