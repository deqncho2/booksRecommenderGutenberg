Synopsis: In 'What Inhabits Me?' by Robert Moore Williams, a renowned scientist embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that delves into the mysteries of the human mind. As he delves deeper into his research, he discovers a hidden realm within his own consciousness, where he encounters unimaginable beings and uncovers a dark secret that threatens to consume him. With his sanity hanging by a thread, he must navigate the treacherous depths of his own mind to find the truth and save himself from the forces that seek to control him.

Genres: Science Fiction, Psychological Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the human mind, Identity, Reality vs. Illusion
