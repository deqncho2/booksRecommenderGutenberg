Synopsis: In "Dorothy on a House Boat" by Raymond, Evelyn, a young woman named Dorothy embarks on a thrilling adventure aboard a houseboat. As she navigates the waters, she encounters mysterious characters, uncovers hidden secrets, and finds herself entangled in a web of intrigue. With each twist and turn, Dorothy's courage and resourcefulness are put to the test, leading her to unexpected discoveries and life-changing experiences. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind Dorothy's extraordinary journey.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Exploration, Self-discovery, Resilience
