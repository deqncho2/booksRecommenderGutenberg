Synopsis: 
In 'Peter Jameson: A Modern Romance' by Frankau, Gilbert, we are introduced to Peter Jameson, a charismatic and enigmatic young man who captivates everyone he meets. From his humble beginnings in a small English village, Peter embarks on a journey that takes him through the glamorous world of high society, where he becomes entangled in a web of love, ambition, and betrayal. As he navigates the complexities of relationships and the pursuit of his dreams, Peter's true character is revealed, leaving readers questioning the nature of love and the sacrifices one is willing to make for it.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Love, Ambition, Betrayal, Identity
