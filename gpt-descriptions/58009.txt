Synopsis: In 'Russian Portraits' by Sheridan Clare, a young American artist finds herself immersed in the vibrant and enigmatic world of St. Petersburg, Russia. As she navigates the complexities of love, friendship, and artistic expression, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue that could change her life forever. With each portrait she paints, she uncovers hidden truths about herself and those around her, leading her on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Artistic expression, Identity, Betrayal, Cultural exploration
