Synopsis: 
In 'A Little Pilgrim: Stories of the Seen and the Unseen', Mrs. Oliphant takes readers on a captivating journey through a series of enchanting tales. Follow the adventures of a young pilgrim as she encounters mysterious beings and explores the realms of the seen and the unseen. From encounters with mischievous fairies to encounters with lost souls, each story unveils a new layer of the supernatural world. With each page turned, readers will be drawn deeper into the magical and thought-provoking narratives that explore the boundaries between reality and the unknown.

Genres: 
Fantasy, Supernatural, Short Stories

Themes: 
Exploration of the supernatural, Perception of reality, Existence of otherworldly beings
