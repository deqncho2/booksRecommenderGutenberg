Synopsis: In 'Bird of Paradise' by Ada Leverson, a young woman named Cicely is swept away by the allure of a mysterious and charming stranger. As their relationship unfolds, Cicely finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and desires, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and forbidden love. Set against the backdrop of the vibrant and decadent society of early 20th century London, this captivating novel explores the complexities of passion, identity, and the consequences of following one's heart.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Self-discovery, Society and class
