Synopsis: 

In "Historical record of the Sixty-Seventh, or the South Hampshire Regiment: Containing an account of the formation of the regiment in 1758, and of its subsequent services to 1849," Richard Cannon takes us on a captivating journey through the rich history of the South Hampshire Regiment. From its humble beginnings in 1758 to its remarkable achievements up until 1849, this book unveils the untold stories of bravery, sacrifice, and resilience that shaped the regiment's legacy. Cannon's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life the battles, campaigns, and pivotal moments that defined the regiment, making this a must-read for history enthusiasts and military buffs alike.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Bravery, Sacrifice, Resilience, Military Strategy, Regimental History
