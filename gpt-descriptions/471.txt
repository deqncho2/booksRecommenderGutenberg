Synopsis:
In the remote and hauntingly beautiful Scottish countryside, a tragic love story unfolds. The Bride of Lammermoor follows the forbidden romance between Lucy Ashton, a young and innocent noblewoman, and Edgar Ravenswood, a brooding and passionate Scottish lord. As their love blossoms amidst family feuds and political turmoil, dark forces conspire to tear them apart. Secrets, betrayal, and a curse from the past threaten to unravel their lives, leading to a heart-wrenching climax that will leave readers breathless.

Genres:
Gothic fiction, Historical fiction, Romance

Themes:
Forbidden love, Family feuds, Fate and destiny
