Synopsis: 

In "Marie Bashkirtseff (From Childhood to Girlhood)" by Marie Bashkirtseff, we are taken on a captivating journey through the early years of a remarkable young woman. From her idyllic childhood in Ukraine to her blossoming adolescence in Paris, Marie's story is one of ambition, passion, and the pursuit of artistic greatness. Through her vivid and introspective narration, we witness the challenges she faces as a female artist in a male-dominated world, the complexities of her relationships, and the sacrifices she must make to follow her dreams. As we delve into Marie's world, we are left wondering how she will navigate the obstacles that lie ahead and whether she will ultimately achieve the recognition she so desperately desires.

Genres: Autobiography, Coming-of-age, Historical

Themes: Artistic ambition, Gender inequality, Self-discovery
