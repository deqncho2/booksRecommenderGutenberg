Synopsis: In 'Studies in Occultism; A Series of Reprints from the Writings of H. P. Blavatsky: No. 1: Practical Occultism—Occultism versus the Occult; Arts—The Blessings of Publicity', Helena Petrovna Blavatsky delves into the mysterious world of occultism, exploring the clash between true occult knowledge and the superficial occult practices that have gained popularity. Through thought-provoking essays, Blavatsky challenges the reader to question the true nature of the occult and its potential impact on society. This captivating work offers a unique perspective on the hidden forces that shape our world.

Genres: Occultism, Philosophy, Non-fiction

Themes: Occult knowledge, Spiritual enlightenment, Society and its beliefs
