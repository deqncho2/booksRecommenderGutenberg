Synopsis: 

In 'Cornish Worthies: Sketches of Some Eminent Cornish Men and Families, Volume 2', Walter H. Tregellas delves into the lives of remarkable individuals and influential families from Cornwall. From renowned artists and writers to pioneering engineers and politicians, this collection of sketches offers a captivating glimpse into the rich history and achievements of the Cornish people. With vivid anecdotes and engaging storytelling, Tregellas paints a vivid picture of these extraordinary figures, their accomplishments, and the impact they had on their communities and beyond.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Cornish heritage, Achievement, Influence, Community
