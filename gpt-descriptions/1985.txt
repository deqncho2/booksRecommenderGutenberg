Synopsis: 
In 'Men's Wives' by William Makepeace Thackeray, the lives of two couples become intertwined in a tale of love, deception, and societal expectations. As secrets are revealed and relationships are tested, the characters navigate the complexities of marriage and the consequences of their actions. With a touch of humor and a keen observation of human nature, Thackeray explores the intricacies of married life and the choices we make in the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Victorian Literature

Themes: Marriage, Deception, Social Expectations, Love, Happiness
