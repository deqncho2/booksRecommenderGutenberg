Synopsis: In 'The Gun-Brand' by James B. Hendryx, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small western town, carrying a reputation as deadly as the gun he wears. As tensions rise and secrets are revealed, the townspeople find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger. With each passing day, the stranger's true intentions become more elusive, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat, eager to uncover the truth behind the gun-brand.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Justice
