Synopsis:
In "Liza; Or, 'A Nest of Nobles'", Ivan Turgenev takes us on a captivating journey through the lives of the Russian nobility. The story revolves around the charming and enigmatic Liza, a young woman who finds herself caught between the constraints of society and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love, loyalty, and societal expectations, Liza's world becomes a battleground for passion and duty, leading to unexpected consequences that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Psychological Fiction

Themes:
Love and Desire, Social Class, Individual Freedom
