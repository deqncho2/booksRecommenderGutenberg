Synopsis: Step into the enchanting world of Old Salem as Mary Harrod Northend takes you on a captivating journey through the historic doorways that hold the secrets of this charming town. From grand mansions to humble cottages, each doorway tells a unique story, revealing the lives and experiences of the people who once called this place home. With vivid descriptions and stunning photographs, 'Historic Doorways of Old Salem' invites you to unlock the past and discover the hidden treasures behind these fascinating entrances.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Architecture

Themes: Historical preservation, Cultural heritage, Architectural significance
