Synopsis: 

'Gettysburg: Stories of the Red Harvest and the Aftermath' by Elsie Singmaster is a captivating collection of short stories set during and after the historic Battle of Gettysburg. Through the eyes of various characters, both Union and Confederate, Singmaster weaves a tapestry of human experiences, exploring the profound impact of war on individuals and communities. From soldiers on the front lines to civilians caught in the crossfire, these stories offer a poignant and intimate glimpse into the harrowing realities of one of the bloodiest battles in American history. 

Genres: Historical fiction, War fiction, Short stories

Themes: War and its consequences, Human resilience, Sacrifice, Community
