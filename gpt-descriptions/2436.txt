Synopsis: In 'The Marriages' by Henry James, readers are introduced to a young American woman named Bessie Alden who finds herself caught in a web of complex relationships and societal expectations. As she navigates the world of high society in Europe, Bessie becomes entangled in a series of marriages, each with its own set of secrets and hidden motives. With her innocence and naivety tested, Bessie must confront the consequences of her choices and the true nature of love and loyalty.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and marriage, societal expectations, deception and secrets, innocence and experience
