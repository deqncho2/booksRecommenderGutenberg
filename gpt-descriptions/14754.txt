Synopsis: 
In 'An Illustrated History of Ireland from AD 400 to 1800', Mary Francis Cusack takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich and tumultuous history of Ireland. From the arrival of the Celts to the struggles against Viking invasions, Norman conquests, and English colonization, this beautifully illustrated book brings to life the key events, figures, and cultural developments that shaped Ireland over a span of four centuries. Cusack's vivid storytelling and meticulous research provide a comprehensive and engaging account of Ireland's past, offering readers a deeper understanding of the country's complex heritage.

Genres: History, Non-fiction, Art

Themes: Irish history, Cultural identity, Colonialism, Resistance, Political and social upheaval
