Synopsis: In 'A Rambler's Lease' by Torrey Bradford, a young woman named Amelia embarks on a journey to a remote island, seeking solace and a fresh start. However, she soon discovers that the island holds dark secrets and mysterious inhabitants who challenge her perception of reality. As Amelia delves deeper into the island's history, she becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, forcing her to confront her own past and make choices that will determine her future.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Secrets, Perception
