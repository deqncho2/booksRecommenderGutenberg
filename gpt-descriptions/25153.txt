Synopsis: 
'Tales of a Wayside Inn' by Henry Wadsworth Longfellow is a collection of interconnected stories told by a group of travelers staying at an inn. Each traveler takes turns sharing their own unique tale, ranging from tales of love and adventure to tales of mystery and the supernatural. As the stories unfold, the characters reveal their deepest desires, fears, and secrets, creating a captivating tapestry of human experiences. 

Genres: Poetry, Fiction, Anthology

Themes: Love, Adventure, Mystery, Supernatural, Human Experience
