Synopsis: In "A Treatise Upon the Law of Copyright," MacGillivray delves into the intricate world of copyright law, exploring its application in the United Kingdom, the Dominions of the Crown, and the United States of America. This comprehensive work provides a detailed analysis of the legal framework surrounding copyright, including Acts of Parliament, international conventions, orders in council, treasury minute, and acts of Congress. With its wealth of information and extensive appendix, this treatise serves as an invaluable resource for anyone seeking a deeper understanding of copyright law and its implications.

Genres: Legal reference, Non-fiction

Themes: Copyright law, Intellectual property, Legal frameworks
