Synopsis: In 'Distributive Justice: The Right and Wrong of Our Present Distribution of Wealth', John A. Ryan delves into the complex and controversial topic of wealth distribution in society. Through a thought-provoking exploration of economic systems, social structures, and moral principles, Ryan challenges readers to question the fairness and ethics behind our current distribution of wealth. With compelling arguments and insightful analysis, the book offers a fresh perspective on the economic disparities that exist and the potential consequences they may have on society. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Economics

Themes: Wealth inequality, Social justice, Morality, Economic systems
