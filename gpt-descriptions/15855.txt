Synopsis: 
In 'The Man from Home', a wealthy American businessman and his daughter find themselves caught up in a web of intrigue and romance while traveling through Europe. When they encounter a charming and enigmatic stranger, their lives take an unexpected turn as they become entangled in a dangerous game of secrets and hidden identities. As they navigate through the glamorous world of European high society, they must unravel the truth and make choices that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Deception, Love and Relationships
