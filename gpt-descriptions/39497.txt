Synopsis: In 'A Breeze from the Woods, 2nd Ed.', author W. C. Bartlett takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious and enchanting world of the woods. As the protagonist embarks on a quest to uncover the secrets hidden within the ancient trees, they encounter a series of unexpected events and encounters that challenge their perception of reality. With each step deeper into the woods, the line between imagination and truth becomes increasingly blurred, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the enigma that lies within.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Nature's mysteries, Perception vs. reality
