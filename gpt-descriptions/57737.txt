Synopsis: In 'A Dead Reckoning' by T. W. Speight, a mysterious murder takes place in a small coastal town, leaving the residents baffled and fearful. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, and the line between truth and deception becomes blurred. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into a web of intrigue, where nothing is as it seems and everyone is a suspect. Will the truth be uncovered before more lives are lost?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Small-town dynamics
