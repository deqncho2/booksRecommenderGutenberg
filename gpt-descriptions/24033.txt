Synopsis: In 'Dave Porter in the Gold Fields; Or, The Search for the Landslide Mine' by Edward Stratemeyer, young Dave Porter embarks on an exciting adventure to the gold fields of California. Determined to uncover the mystery surrounding the Landslide Mine, Dave faces numerous challenges and encounters both friends and foes along the way. With danger lurking at every turn, Dave must rely on his wit, courage, and resourcefulness to navigate the treacherous gold fields and unravel the secrets of the elusive mine.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Perseverance
