Synopsis: In 'The Ivory Trail' by Talbot Mundy, a mysterious adventurer named King is on a quest to uncover the secrets of an ancient civilization hidden deep within the African jungle. With danger lurking at every turn, King must navigate treacherous landscapes, outsmart cunning adversaries, and decipher cryptic clues to unlock the truth. As he delves deeper into the heart of darkness, King's journey becomes a thrilling blend of adventure, mystery, and exploration that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Ancient Civilizations, Survival
