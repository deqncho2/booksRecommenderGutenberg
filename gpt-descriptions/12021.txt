Synopsis: 
In 'Adopting an Abandoned Farm' by Sanborn, Kate, a young couple stumbles upon a dilapidated farm in the countryside and decides to take on the challenge of restoring it to its former glory. As they delve into the history of the farm, they uncover a series of mysterious events that took place there, leading them on a captivating journey of discovery and self-discovery. With each step they take towards revitalizing the farm, they find themselves drawn deeper into a web of secrets and unexpected connections, ultimately transforming not only the land but also their own lives.

Genres: 
Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Rebirth, Identity, Family Legacy
