Synopsis: In 'The Unjust Steward; or, The Minister's Debt' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young minister finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and debts when he inherits a mysterious estate. As he delves deeper into the history of the property, he uncovers a shocking truth that threatens to unravel his own life and reputation. With the help of an enigmatic steward, he must navigate a treacherous path to redemption and justice, all while facing the consequences of his own actions.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Redemption, Morality, Secrets and Betrayal
