Synopsis: In "Sarita, the Carlist" by Arthur W. Marchmont, a young woman named Sarita finds herself caught in the midst of political turmoil and forbidden love in 19th century Spain. As the daughter of a Carlist leader, Sarita is torn between her loyalty to her family and her growing affection for a man from the opposing side. With danger lurking at every turn, Sarita must navigate a treacherous path filled with secrets, betrayal, and the struggle for freedom. Will she be able to find her own voice and forge her own destiny in a world torn apart by war?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Political Drama

Themes: Loyalty, Forbidden Love, Political Conflict, Personal Identity
