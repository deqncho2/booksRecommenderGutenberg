Synopsis: 
In 'Motor Boat Boys on the Great Lakes; or, Exploring the Mystic Isle of Mackinac' by Louis Arundel, a group of adventurous young boys embark on a thrilling journey across the Great Lakes. When they stumble upon the enigmatic Mystic Isle of Mackinac, they find themselves caught up in a series of mysterious events that test their courage and resourcefulness. As they navigate treacherous waters, encounter dangerous wildlife, and uncover hidden secrets, the Motor Boat Boys must rely on their wits and teamwork to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Bravery
