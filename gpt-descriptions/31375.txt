Synopsis: 
In 'Under False Pretences: A Novel' by Adeline Sergeant, a seemingly ordinary woman finds herself entangled in a web of deception and danger when she agrees to help a mysterious stranger. As secrets unravel and identities are questioned, she must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth, all while trying to protect her own life. With unexpected twists and a gripping narrative, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Danger
