Synopsis: 
In 'The Alps' by Lunn, Arnold, a group of adventurers embarks on a treacherous journey through the majestic and unforgiving Alps. As they navigate through breathtaking landscapes and face numerous challenges, they must confront their deepest fears and push themselves to the limits of human endurance. With danger lurking at every turn, the group's bonds are tested, and they discover the true power of friendship and resilience. 

Genres: Adventure, Drama, Survival

Themes: Exploration, Courage, Friendship, Overcoming adversity
