Synopsis: In "The Baritone's Parish; or, 'All Things to All Men'" by James M. Ludlow, a talented baritone finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and intrigue when he accepts a position as the music director at a small parish. As he delves deeper into the lives of the parishioners, he uncovers hidden desires, forbidden love, and a mysterious past that threatens to unravel the very fabric of the community. With his voice as his only weapon, the baritone must navigate the complexities of human nature and confront the darkness that lurks beneath the surface of this seemingly idyllic parish.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and desire, Identity and self-discovery
