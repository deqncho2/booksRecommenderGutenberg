Synopsis: In "The Bicyclers and Three Other Farces," John Kendrick Bangs presents a collection of hilarious and absurd short plays that satirize various aspects of society. From a group of bicyclists who take their hobby a bit too seriously to a man who finds himself in a peculiar predicament with his own reflection, these farces explore the comical and often nonsensical situations that arise in everyday life. With witty dialogue and clever plot twists, Bangs invites readers to laugh along as he pokes fun at human foibles and societal conventions.

Genres: Comedy, Satire, Farce

Themes: Social satire, Absurdity, Human folly
