Synopsis: In "To The Work! To The Work! Exhortations to Christians," Dwight Lyman Moody delivers a powerful call to action for Christians to actively engage in their faith. Through personal anecdotes and biblical teachings, Moody challenges readers to embrace their role in spreading the Gospel and making a difference in the world. With a passionate and persuasive voice, he encourages believers to overcome obstacles, prioritize their spiritual growth, and live out their faith in practical ways. This book serves as a rallying cry for Christians to rise up and fulfill their purpose in the Kingdom of God.

Genres: Christian literature, Non-fiction, Inspirational

Themes: Christian discipleship, Evangelism, Spiritual growth, Purpose in life
