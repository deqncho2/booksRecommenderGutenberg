Synopsis: 
'Tales from the Hindu Dramatists' by Dutta, R. N. is a captivating collection of stories that takes readers on a journey through the rich and vibrant world of Hindu drama. From tales of love and betrayal to stories of gods and demons, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the diverse range of narratives found in Hindu literature. Each story is filled with vivid characters, intricate plots, and thought-provoking themes that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. Whether it's a tale of divine intervention or a story of human triumph, 'Tales from the Hindu Dramatists' is sure to captivate and enchant readers with its timeless storytelling.

Genres: Drama, Mythology, Fiction

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Divine Intervention, Human Triumph, Morality, Fate
