Synopsis: 
'Folk-Lore of West and Mid-Wales' by Jonathan Ceredig Davies takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Welsh folklore. From ancient legends of mythical creatures to enchanting tales of love and betrayal, this collection of stories immerses readers in the magical world of West and Mid-Wales. As readers delve into the pages, they will encounter mysterious witches, mischievous fairies, and heroic figures who navigate the blurred lines between reality and fantasy. With each story, Davies weaves a spellbinding narrative that transports readers to a bygone era, where the supernatural and the everyday intertwine in captivating ways.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: Welsh culture and traditions, Supernatural beings, Legends and myths
