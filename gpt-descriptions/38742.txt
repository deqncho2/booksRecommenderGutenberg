Synopsis: In "The Corner House Girls Under Canvas: How they reached Pleasant Cove and what happened afterward," the Corner House girls embark on an exciting adventure to Pleasant Cove. As they set up camp and explore the beautiful surroundings, unexpected events unfold, leading to thrilling encounters and mysterious discoveries. This captivating story takes readers on a journey filled with friendship, courage, and the joy of embracing new experiences.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Resilience
