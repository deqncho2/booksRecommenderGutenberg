Synopsis: In "Two New Pocket Gophers from Wyoming and Colorado," Hall and Montague take readers on an intriguing journey into the world of these elusive underground creatures. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, they unveil the discovery of two previously unknown species, shedding light on their unique characteristics and habitats. As the authors delve into the fascinating world of pocket gophers, readers will be captivated by the unexpected twists and turns that unfold, leaving them eager to learn more about these enigmatic creatures.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Zoology

Themes: Biodiversity, Species Discovery, Wildlife Conservation
