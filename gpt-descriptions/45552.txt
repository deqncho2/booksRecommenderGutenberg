Synopsis: In "The Old Market-Cart" by F. Burge Griswold, a mysterious old market-cart appears in a small town, captivating the curiosity of its inhabitants. As the cart's origins and purpose remain shrouded in secrecy, the townspeople find themselves drawn into a web of intrigue and unexpected connections. With each encounter, the cart reveals hidden truths and challenges the characters' perceptions of themselves and their community. As the story unfolds, the old market-cart becomes a catalyst for self-discovery, transformation, and the exploration of the human spirit.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Magical Realism

Themes: Identity, Community, Transformation, Perception
