Synopsis: In 'The Iron Furrow' by George C. Shedd, a young farmer named John embarks on a journey to reclaim his family's land, which has been taken over by a powerful and corrupt corporation. As he battles against the odds, John discovers hidden secrets about his family's past and uncovers a web of deceit that stretches far beyond his own struggles. With the fate of his community hanging in the balance, John must navigate treacherous alliances and confront his own inner demons to fight for justice and reclaim what is rightfully his.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Land ownership, Corruption, Family legacy, Justice
