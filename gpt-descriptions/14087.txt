Synopsis: In 'The Jungle Girl' by Casserly, Gordon, a young woman finds herself stranded in the heart of the untamed jungle after a tragic accident. As she navigates through the treacherous wilderness, she encounters mysterious creatures, ancient ruins, and a hidden civilization. With danger lurking at every turn, she must rely on her wits and resilience to survive and uncover the secrets of the jungle.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Discovery
