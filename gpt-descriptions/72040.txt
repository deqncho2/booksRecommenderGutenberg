Synopsis: In "Dorothy Dale's Engagement" by Margaret Penrose, the spirited and adventurous Dorothy finds herself caught up in a whirlwind of unexpected events when she becomes engaged. As she navigates the challenges and mysteries that arise, Dorothy's loyalty, bravery, and quick thinking are put to the test. With her friends by her side, she embarks on a thrilling journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets that lie ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Friendship, Love, Courage, Loyalty
