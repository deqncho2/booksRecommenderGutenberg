Synopsis: In 'The American Prisoner' by Eden Phillpotts, a gripping tale unfolds as an unexpected turn of events leads an American traveler to find himself imprisoned in a remote English village. As he navigates the peculiarities of his captors and the secrets they hold, he becomes entangled in a web of mystery and suspense. With each passing day, the protagonist must unravel the truth behind his confinement and find a way to escape before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Freedom, Deception
