Synopsis: In 'The Cosmic Bluff' by Reynolds, Mack, a brilliant astrophysicist stumbles upon a mysterious signal from outer space that could change the course of humanity. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she finds herself caught in a web of deception, danger, and cosmic secrets that challenge everything she thought she knew about the universe. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, she must navigate a treacherous game of wits and uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Conspiracy, Human resilience
