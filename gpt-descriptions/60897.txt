Synopsis: In 'The Non-Electronic Bug' by Mittleman, E., a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious insect that possesses extraordinary abilities. As he delves deeper into the bug's secrets, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between science and magic blurs. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous world of hidden agendas and unexpected alliances to uncover the truth behind the bug's existence.

Genres: Science fiction, mystery, thriller

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, the power of nature, the ethics of scientific discovery
