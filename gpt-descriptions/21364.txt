Synopsis: In 'The Rajah of Dah' by George Manville Fenn, a young English boy named Dick Roylance finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he is taken captive by a mysterious Rajah in the exotic land of Dah. As Dick tries to navigate the treacherous world of palace intrigue and dangerous escapades, he must rely on his wit and courage to survive and find a way back home. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will transport readers to a vividly depicted world of danger, suspense, and unexpected alliances.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of cultural differences, Coming of age, Loyalty and friendship
