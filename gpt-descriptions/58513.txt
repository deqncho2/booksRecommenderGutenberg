Synopsis: 
In 'Mary Louise Adopts a Soldier', Mary Louise Burrows, a spirited and compassionate young girl, finds herself caught up in the turmoil of World War I. Determined to make a difference, she takes it upon herself to adopt a soldier and support him throughout the war. As Mary Louise navigates the challenges of war-time life and forms a unique bond with her soldier, she learns valuable lessons about love, sacrifice, and the power of friendship.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Historical Fiction, War Fiction

Themes: 
Love and Compassion, Patriotism, Friendship, Sacrifice
