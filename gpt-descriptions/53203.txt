Synopsis: 

In 'Complete Treatise on the mare and foal at the time of delivery, with illustrations: Also on cows and calves, with stallion and mare, when diseased by Gonorrhea (clap) or Pox, also Diarrhea and Costiveness in Colts,' Conrad Mitchell delves into the intricate world of equine health and reproduction. This comprehensive guide provides valuable insights into the delicate process of delivering a foal, as well as addressing common ailments that can affect horses and cows. With detailed illustrations accompanying the text, Mitchell offers a wealth of knowledge on topics ranging from the impact of diseases like Gonorrhea and Pox on stallions and mares to the management of diarrhea and constipation in young horses. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Veterinary Medicine, Animal Husbandry

Themes: Equine health, Reproduction, Disease management
