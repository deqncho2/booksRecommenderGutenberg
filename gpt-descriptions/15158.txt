Synopsis: 

In Time of Emergency: A Citizen's Handbook on Nuclear Attack, Natural Disasters (1968) is a comprehensive guide that equips readers with essential knowledge and practical advice to navigate through times of crisis. This handbook, published by the United States Office of Civil Defense, covers a wide range of topics including nuclear attack preparedness, survival techniques, and strategies for coping with natural disasters. With a focus on empowering citizens to take control of their own safety, this handbook provides invaluable information on emergency planning, communication, and resource management. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Survival Guide, Reference

Themes: Emergency Preparedness, Self-Sufficiency, Community Resilience
