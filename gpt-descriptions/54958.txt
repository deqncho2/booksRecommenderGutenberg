Synopsis: 
'Among the An-ko-me-nums, or Flathead Tribes of Indians of the Pacific Coast' takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich cultural tapestry of the Flathead tribes. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Thomas Crosby unveils the fascinating traditions, rituals, and beliefs of these indigenous peoples. As readers delve into the lives of the An-ko-me-nums, they will encounter gripping tales of bravery, spiritual encounters, and the enduring resilience of a community shaped by their environment. This immersive exploration of the Flathead tribes offers a unique perspective on the history and heritage of the Pacific Coast's indigenous peoples.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Anthropology

Themes: Indigenous culture, Native American history, Cultural preservation
