Synopsis: In this captivating address, Honorable William C. Redfield, Secretary of Commerce, delivers a powerful speech at the Conference of Regional Chairmen of the Highways Transport Committee Council of National Defense. With a focus on the crucial role of transportation in national defense, Redfield explores the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead. Through his insightful analysis and compelling arguments, he sheds light on the importance of efficient transportation systems and their impact on the nation's security and economic prosperity.

Genres: Speech, Government, Transportation

Themes: National defense, Transportation infrastructure, Economic impact
