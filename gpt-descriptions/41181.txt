Synopsis: In "The Great Strike on the "Q"," John A. Hall takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous events surrounding a major strike on the "Q" railroad. As tensions rise and the strike escalates, the lives of the workers, their families, and the company executives become intertwined in a web of conflict, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Hall paints a vivid picture of the struggle for justice and the human cost of industrial unrest. Will the strike succeed in bringing about change, or will it be crushed under the weight of corporate power?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Labor rights, Social justice, Power dynamics
