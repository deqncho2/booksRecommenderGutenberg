Synopsis: In "To Be "Talked About" Use Cookie 'n' Cracker Cookin'" by Elliott, Jeanette Hindman/ Festen, Alfred, a mysterious cookbook falls into the hands of a young woman named Emily, leading her on a culinary adventure that takes her to unexpected places. As she delves into the recipes, she discovers that each one holds a secret message, guiding her towards a hidden treasure. Along the way, Emily encounters a cast of eccentric characters, each with their own motives and desires. With the help of her newfound friends, she must decipher the clues within the recipes and navigate a world filled with danger and intrigue. Will Emily uncover the truth behind the cookbook and find the treasure before it falls into the wrong hands?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Cooking

Themes: Secrets, Friendship, Discovery
