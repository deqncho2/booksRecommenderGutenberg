Synopsis: 
In the dark and violent world of ancient Rome, a renowned general returns home from war, only to find himself entangled in a web of revenge, betrayal, and unspeakable horrors. As the body count rises and the boundaries of morality blur, Titus Andronicus is pushed to the brink of madness, forced to make unimaginable choices that will forever alter the course of his life and the fate of his family.

Genres: Tragedy, Drama, Revenge

Themes: Revenge, Violence, Power, Honor, Madness
