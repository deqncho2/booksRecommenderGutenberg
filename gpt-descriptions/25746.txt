Synopsis: In 'New Year's Wake: A Terran Empire story' by Wilson, Ann, a young archaeologist named Amelia discovers a mysterious artifact that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of an ancient civilization. As she delves deeper into the artifact's origins, Amelia finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, where she must navigate treacherous alliances and face her own inner demons. With the fate of the Terran Empire hanging in the balance, Amelia must uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Power
