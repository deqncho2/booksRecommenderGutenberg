Synopsis: 
"Up the Country: Letters Written to Her Sister from the Upper Provinces of India" is a captivating collection of letters written by Emily Eden during her time in the Upper Provinces of India. Through her vivid and engaging correspondence, Eden takes the reader on a journey filled with fascinating encounters, humorous anecdotes, and insightful observations about the people, culture, and landscapes of India. From the bustling streets of Calcutta to the tranquil hills of Simla, Eden's letters provide a unique and intimate glimpse into the colonial life of the 19th century.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Colonialism, Cultural Exploration, Personal Growth
