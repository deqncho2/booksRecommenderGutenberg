Synopsis: In 'With Cochrane the Dauntless', follow the thrilling adventures of young Ned, who joins the Royal Navy during the Napoleonic Wars. As he serves under the legendary Admiral Cochrane, Ned finds himself embroiled in daring naval battles, secret missions, and encounters with pirates. With his courage and resourcefulness tested to the limit, Ned must navigate the treacherous waters of war and prove himself worthy of the Dauntless. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Naval Warfare, Heroism
