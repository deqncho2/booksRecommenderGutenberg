Synopsis: In 'Colonel Crockett's Co-operative Christmas' by Hughes, Rupert, a group of unlikely companions find themselves stranded in a remote cabin during a snowstorm on Christmas Eve. As they come together to make the best of their situation, they embark on a heartwarming and humorous adventure filled with unexpected twists and turns. With each passing moment, they discover the true meaning of friendship, love, and the spirit of Christmas.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Holiday

Themes: Friendship, Love, Christmas spirit
