Synopsis: In 'Practical Pointers for Patentees' by Cresee, Franklin Allison, embark on a captivating journey through the world of patents as the author shares invaluable insights and practical advice for inventors. From navigating the complex patent application process to protecting intellectual property, this guide offers a wealth of knowledge to help patentees succeed. With engaging anecdotes and real-life examples, the book provides a comprehensive understanding of the patent landscape, making it an essential resource for aspiring inventors and seasoned professionals alike.

Genres: Non-fiction, Business, Self-help

Themes: Patents, Intellectual property, Invention, Entrepreneurship
