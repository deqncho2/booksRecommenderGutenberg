Synopsis: In "The Preservation of Antiquities: A Handbook for Curators," Friedrich Rathgen takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of curating and preserving ancient artifacts. From the mysterious tombs of Egypt to the crumbling ruins of ancient Rome, Rathgen shares his expertise and insights on the delicate art of conserving these precious treasures. With a blend of historical anecdotes, practical advice, and scientific knowledge, this handbook offers a fascinating exploration of the challenges faced by curators in their quest to protect and showcase our cultural heritage.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Art

Themes: Cultural preservation, Conservation, Historical significance
