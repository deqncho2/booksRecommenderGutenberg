Synopsis: 
In "The Outdoor Girls in a Winter Camp: Or, Glorious Days on Skates and Ice Boats" by Laura Lee Hope, the adventurous Outdoor Girls embark on a thrilling winter camp filled with exciting activities on skates and ice boats. As they navigate the frozen landscapes, they encounter unexpected challenges and form new friendships. With their determination and resourcefulness, the girls prove that winter can be a time of exhilarating adventure and unforgettable memories.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Outdoor activities, Winter adventures
