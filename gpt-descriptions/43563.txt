Synopsis: In 'Bits of Blarney' by Mackenzie, R. Shelton, a young woman named Fiona embarks on a journey to Ireland, where she discovers a hidden family secret that leads her on a quest to uncover the truth about her heritage. As she delves deeper into her family's history, Fiona encounters mysterious clues, enchanting landscapes, and unexpected allies, all while navigating the complexities of love and identity. This captivating tale of self-discovery and adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the secrets that lie within the emerald isle.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Heritage, Love, Adventure
