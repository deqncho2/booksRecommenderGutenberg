Synopsis: In the small village of Westways, a mysterious event sets off a chain of events that will forever change the lives of its inhabitants. As secrets are unveiled and relationships tested, the village becomes a microcosm of human nature, showcasing the complexities of love, betrayal, and redemption. With its richly developed characters and intricate plot, 'Westways: A Village Chronicle' takes readers on a captivating journey through the triumphs and tragedies of a close-knit community.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Community, Secrets, Human Nature
