Synopsis: In 'This Marrying' by Margaret Culkin Banning, a young woman named Anne finds herself caught in a whirlwind of societal expectations and personal desires as she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and self-discovery. Set in the early 20th century, Anne's journey takes her through a series of unexpected twists and turns, challenging her notions of tradition, independence, and the true meaning of happiness. As she grapples with the choices before her, Anne must confront the pressures of conformity and the courage it takes to forge her own path.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Marriage, Gender Roles, Self-Identity, Societal Expectations
