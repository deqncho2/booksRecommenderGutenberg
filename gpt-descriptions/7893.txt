Synopsis: 
In 'Hide and Seek' by Collins, Wilkie, a young woman named Valeria is caught in a web of mystery and deception when she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of hide and seek. As she delves deeper into the secrets of those around her, Valeria must navigate a treacherous world where nothing is as it seems. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and Valeria must rely on her wit and courage to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
