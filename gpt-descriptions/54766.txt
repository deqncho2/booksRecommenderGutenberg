Synopsis: In 'The Traitor: A Story of the Fall of the Invisible Empire', Thomas Dixon Jr. takes readers on a gripping journey through the dark underbelly of a secret society. Set against the backdrop of post-Civil War America, this thrilling novel follows the life of a man torn between loyalty and justice. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the Invisible Empire, he must confront his own beliefs and make a choice that will change his life forever.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Justice, Identity, Morality
