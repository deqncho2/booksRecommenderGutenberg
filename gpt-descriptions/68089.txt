Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 151, July 31, 1915: The Mystery of the Crossed Needles; or Nick Carter and the Yellow Tong', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving crossed needles and a mysterious criminal organization known as the Yellow Tong. As Carter delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of deceit, danger, and hidden motives that threaten to unravel everything he thought he knew. With time running out, Nick Carter must use his unparalleled deductive skills to solve the puzzle and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Criminal organizations, Detective work
