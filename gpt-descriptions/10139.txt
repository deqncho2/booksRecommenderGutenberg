Synopsis: In 'The Faith of the Millions (2nd series)', George Tyrrell takes readers on a captivating exploration of the diverse religious beliefs and practices that shape the lives of millions around the world. Through vivid anecdotes and thought-provoking analysis, Tyrrell delves into the complexities of faith, examining the ways in which it both unites and divides societies. From the rituals of ancient civilizations to the modern expressions of faith, this book offers a fascinating journey through the rich tapestry of human spirituality.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, Anthropology

Themes: Faith, Religion, Spirituality, Cultural Diversity
