Synopsis: 

'Vikings of the Pacific: The Adventures of the Explorers who Came from the West, Eastward' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the uncharted waters of the Pacific Ocean. Agnes C. Laut delves into the mysterious tales of ancient seafarers who ventured from the West, defying the unknown to explore the vast expanse of the East. From the legendary Polynesians to the enigmatic Vikings, this captivating narrative uncovers the untold stories of these intrepid explorers and their remarkable encounters with distant lands and cultures. As the secrets of their voyages unfold, readers are transported to a time of daring exploration, where the Pacific Ocean becomes a stage for epic adventures and extraordinary discoveries.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Exchange, Human Curiosity
