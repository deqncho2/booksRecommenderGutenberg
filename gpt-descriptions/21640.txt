Synopsis: In 'A Bid for Fortune; Or, Dr. Nikola's Vendetta' by Boothby, Guy, a young man named Richard Hatteras finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he inherits a vast fortune from a distant relative. As he delves deeper into his newfound wealth, he becomes the target of a sinister and enigmatic figure known as Dr. Nikola. With his life on the line, Richard must navigate a treacherous world of secrets, deception, and unexpected alliances to uncover the truth behind his inheritance and survive the vendetta of Dr. Nikola.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Greed, Revenge, Identity
