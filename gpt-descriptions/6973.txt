Synopsis: In 'The Boy Aviators' Polar Dash; or, Facing Death in the Antarctic' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of young aviators embark on a daring expedition to the treacherous Antarctic. As they face the harsh elements and unexpected challenges, they must confront their deepest fears and push the limits of their courage. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must rely on their skills, resourcefulness, and unwavering determination to survive. Will they conquer the unforgiving Antarctic and make it back home alive?

Genres: Adventure, Action, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Survival, Exploration
