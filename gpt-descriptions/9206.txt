Synopsis: In "The Toll Gatherer's Day," Hawthorne takes us on a mysterious journey through the life of a toll gatherer, whose solitary existence is disrupted by a peculiar encounter. As the toll gatherer grapples with the enigmatic events that unfold, the reader is drawn into a world where reality and imagination intertwine, leaving us questioning the boundaries of perception and the power of the human mind.

Genres: Gothic fiction, psychological thriller, mystery

Themes: Isolation, perception, the supernatural, the power of the mind
