Synopsis: 
In 'Dramatized Rhythm Plays: Mother Goose and Traditional', John N. Richards presents a collection of captivating and lively plays that bring beloved Mother Goose nursery rhymes and traditional tales to life on the stage. From Humpty Dumpty's daring adventure to Little Red Riding Hood's encounter with the Big Bad Wolf, these dramatized rhythm plays offer a fresh and entertaining twist on familiar stories. With colorful characters, catchy songs, and engaging dialogue, this collection is sure to delight both young and old alike.

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Drama, Musical

Themes: 
Imagination, Adventure, Morality
