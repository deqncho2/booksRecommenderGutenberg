Synopsis: In 'Freezing a Mother-in-Law; or, Suspended Animation: A farce in one act', chaos ensues when a desperate son-in-law attempts to freeze his overbearing mother-in-law in order to gain some peace and quiet. However, his plan takes an unexpected turn, leading to a hilarious series of misunderstandings and absurd situations that will keep readers laughing until the very end.

Genres: Farce, Comedy

Themes: Family dynamics, Marriage, Absurdity
