Synopsis: In "Clara Maynard; Or, The True and the False: A Tale of the Times" by William Henry Giles Kingston, readers are introduced to Clara, a young woman who finds herself caught between the allure of wealth and the importance of staying true to oneself. Set in the tumultuous times of the 19th century, Clara's journey takes her through a world of deception, secrets, and unexpected twists. As she navigates the complexities of society, Clara must make difficult choices that will shape her future and challenge her understanding of truth and falsehood.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Morality, Social Class, Deception
