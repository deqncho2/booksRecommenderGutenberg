Synopsis: In 'My Miscellanies, Vol. 2 (of 2)', Wilkie Collins takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of his lesser-known works. From intriguing short stories to thought-provoking essays, this volume showcases Collins' versatility as a writer and his ability to captivate readers with his unique storytelling style. Delve into the mysterious and the macabre, as well as the humorous and the heartwarming, as Collins explores a wide range of themes and emotions. With each turn of the page, readers will be transported to different worlds and immersed in the rich imagination of one of the greatest Victorian authors.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Essays

Themes: Mystery, Suspense, Love, Social Commentary
