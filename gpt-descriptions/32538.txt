Synopsis: In 'The Tower of Dago' by Jókai, Mór, a young artist named Gabor finds himself drawn to a mysterious tower that holds a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the tower's history, he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and supernatural forces. With each step he takes, Gabor uncovers shocking revelations that challenge his perception of reality and test his courage. Will he be able to unravel the enigma of the tower before it consumes him?

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and obsession, Secrets and hidden truths, Supernatural elements
