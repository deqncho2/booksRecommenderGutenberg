Synopsis: In 'An A. B. C. of every-day people: $b Good, bad & indifferent', author G. E. Farrow takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of ordinary individuals. From the virtuous to the wicked, and those who fall somewhere in between, Farrow presents a collection of character sketches that offer a unique glimpse into the complexities of human nature. With each letter of the alphabet, the author introduces a new person and their story, weaving together a tapestry of diverse personalities and experiences. Through wit, humor, and keen observation, Farrow invites readers to reflect on the intricacies of human behavior and the choices we make in our everyday lives.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Character Studies

Themes: Human nature, Morality, Everyday life
