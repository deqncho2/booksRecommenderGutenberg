Synopsis: In "You Can't Scare Me!" by Farrell, Henry, a group of friends embarks on a thrilling adventure when they stumble upon a mysterious haunted house in their neighborhood. As they dare each other to enter, they soon discover that the house holds secrets far beyond their imagination. With spine-tingling encounters and unexpected twists, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Overcoming fears
