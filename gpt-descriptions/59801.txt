Synopsis: 
'Cartoons by Sir John Tenniel, Selected from the Pages of "Punch"' is a captivating collection of illustrations by the renowned artist Sir John Tenniel, known for his iconic illustrations in Lewis Carroll's "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland." This compilation showcases Tenniel's wit and artistic brilliance through a series of satirical and humorous cartoons that were originally published in the pages of the famous British magazine "Punch." From political commentary to social satire, these cartoons offer a delightful and thought-provoking glimpse into the Victorian era. 

Genres: 
Satire, Humor, Art

Themes: 
Political commentary, Social satire, Victorian society
