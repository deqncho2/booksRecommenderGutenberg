Synopsis: 
In 'Merkland; or, Self Sacrifice' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman named Lucy finds herself torn between duty and desire when she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and forbidden love. Set against the backdrop of a grand estate in Scotland, Lucy must navigate the complexities of family loyalty, societal expectations, and the consequences of her own choices. As she delves deeper into the mysteries of Merkland, Lucy discovers that self-sacrifice may be the only path to true happiness.

Genres: 
Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Duty vs. Desire, Family Loyalty, Forbidden Love, Secrets and Betrayal
