Synopsis: In 'Invader From Infinity' by George A. Whittington, a brilliant scientist accidentally creates a device that allows him to travel to parallel universes. However, his experiment takes an unexpected turn when he finds himself trapped in a world ruled by a ruthless dictator. As he navigates this dangerous new reality, he must uncover the truth behind his predicament and find a way back home before it's too late. Filled with thrilling twists and turns, this sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Parallel universes, Dictatorship, Survival
