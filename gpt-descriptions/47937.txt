Synopsis: 
In "How to Judge Architecture: A Popular Guide to the Appreciation of Buildings," Sturgis Russell takes readers on an enlightening journey through the world of architecture, offering a captivating exploration of the principles and elements that make buildings truly remarkable. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, Russell unveils the secrets behind architectural masterpieces, guiding readers to develop a discerning eye and a deeper appreciation for the built environment. From ancient wonders to modern marvels, this book is a captivating companion for anyone seeking to understand and judge architecture with a fresh perspective.

Genres: Non-fiction, Architecture, Art

Themes: Appreciation of beauty, Architectural analysis, Aesthetics
