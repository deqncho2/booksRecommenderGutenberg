Synopsis: In 'She Buildeth Her House' by Comfort, Will Levington, a young woman embarks on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment as she defies societal expectations and builds a life of her own. Set in the early 20th century, this captivating novel follows her struggles, triumphs, and the unconventional choices she makes, challenging the norms of her time. As she navigates through love, career, and personal growth, readers will be drawn into a world where strength, resilience, and determination prevail.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Women's Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Independence, Gender Roles, Self-empowerment
