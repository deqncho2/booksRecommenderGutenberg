Synopsis: 
In "Rope" by Hall, Holworthy, two friends commit a heinous crime and then proceed to host a dinner party, using a rather unconventional item to serve as a conversation starter. As the evening unfolds, tension rises and secrets are revealed, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Morality, Guilt, Manipulation
