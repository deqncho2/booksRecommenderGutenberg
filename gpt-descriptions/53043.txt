Synopsis:
'Extracts from the Diary of William C. Lobenstine, December 31, 1851-1858' takes readers on a captivating journey through the personal diary entries of William C. Lobenstine, a man living in the mid-19th century. As the pages unfold, readers are immersed in Lobenstine's world, witnessing his joys, sorrows, and the profound impact of historical events on his life. From his encounters with notable figures to his reflections on love, loss, and the changing society around him, Lobenstine's diary offers a unique perspective on a pivotal era in American history.

Genres:
Historical fiction, Memoir, Epistolary

Themes:
Personal growth, Love and relationships, Historical events, Social change
