Synopsis: 
'Newfoundland Verse' by E.J. Pratt is a captivating collection of poetry that takes readers on a lyrical journey through the rugged landscapes and rich cultural heritage of Newfoundland. From the crashing waves of the Atlantic to the vibrant characters that inhabit the island, Pratt's verses paint a vivid picture of this unique corner of the world. Through his evocative language and powerful imagery, the poet explores themes of nature, identity, and the enduring spirit of Newfoundlanders. 

Genres: Poetry, Canadian Literature, Regional Literature

Themes: Nature, Identity, Resilience
