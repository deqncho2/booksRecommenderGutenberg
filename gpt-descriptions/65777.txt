Synopsis: In 'Treve' by Albert Payson Terhune, a young man named Bruce Duncan finds himself in possession of a mysterious and powerful object that has the ability to grant wishes. As he navigates the consequences of his newfound power, Bruce must make difficult choices and face unexpected challenges. With each wish, the stakes become higher, leading to a thrilling and thought-provoking journey that explores the limits of human desire and the consequences of our actions.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Power and its consequences, Morality and decision-making
