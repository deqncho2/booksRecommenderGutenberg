Synopsis: In "The Black Cat" (Vol. I, No. 3, December 1895), a mysterious and chilling tale unfolds as a man finds himself inexplicably drawn to a peculiar black cat that enters his life. As the bond between them deepens, the cat's presence begins to have a profound and unsettling effect on the man's psyche, leading him down a dark and sinister path. With each passing moment, the line between reality and madness becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader captivated by the haunting and suspenseful narrative.

Genres: Horror, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Guilt, Madness, Supernatural, Animal Symbolism
