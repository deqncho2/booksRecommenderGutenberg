Synopsis: In the heart of London, a seemingly impossible crime takes place when a man is found dead inside a locked room with no weapon and no apparent way for the murderer to escape. As the police struggle to unravel the mystery, a brilliant and eccentric detective steps in to solve the perplexing case. With a web of secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected twists, 'The Big Bow Mystery' will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking truth is revealed.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Crime and Justice, Deception and Illusion
