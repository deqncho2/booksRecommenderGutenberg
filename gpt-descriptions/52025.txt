Synopsis: In 'Motor Matt's Make-and-Break; or, Advancing the Spark of Friendship' by Stanley R. Matthews, readers are taken on an exhilarating journey alongside the young and resourceful Motor Matt. When a mysterious message leads him to a hidden treasure, Matt finds himself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue. With his trusty motorcycle and quick thinking, he must navigate through treacherous obstacles and outsmart cunning adversaries to uncover the truth. As the stakes rise, Motor Matt's determination and loyalty are put to the ultimate test, making this a thrilling adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Perseverance
