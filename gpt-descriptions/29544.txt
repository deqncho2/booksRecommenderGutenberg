Synopsis: In 'Jolly Sally Pendleton; Or, the Wife Who Was Not a Wife', the charming and vivacious Sally Pendleton finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception. As she navigates the complexities of her relationships, Sally must confront the consequences of her choices and discover the true meaning of love and loyalty. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres: Romance, Drama, Mystery

Themes: Love and betrayal, Identity and self-discovery
