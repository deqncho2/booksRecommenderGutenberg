Synopsis: In 'The Ranger Boys Outwit the Timber Thieves' by Claude A. LaBelle, a group of adventurous young rangers find themselves embroiled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse with a gang of timber thieves. As they navigate the treacherous wilderness, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness, teamwork, and quick thinking to outsmart the cunning criminals. With thrilling action, unexpected twists, and a race against time, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Environmental Conservation
