Synopsis: In 'The Flying Girl' by L. Frank Baum, a young girl named Louise Merrick discovers a hidden talent for flying and becomes a sensation in the world of aviation. As she navigates the challenges and triumphs of her newfound fame, Louise's journey takes her to unexpected places and introduces her to a cast of colorful characters. With her courage and determination, she inspires others to follow their dreams and defy societal expectations. 

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Courage, Breaking societal norms
