Synopsis: 

In 'Fatigue Study: The Elimination of Humanity's Greatest Unnecessary Waste: A First Step in Motion Study', renowned efficiency experts Lillian Moller Gilbreth and Frank B. Gilbreth delve into the fascinating world of human fatigue and its impact on productivity. Through their meticulous research and innovative approach, they uncover the hidden causes of fatigue and propose groundbreaking solutions to eliminate this wasteful phenomenon. With captivating anecdotes and real-life examples, the Gilbreths shed light on the untapped potential of human energy and offer a compelling vision for a more efficient and productive future.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, Self-help

Themes: Efficiency, Productivity, Human potential
