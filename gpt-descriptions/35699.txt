Synopsis: 
'Fontainbleau; a comic opera. In three acts' by John O'Keeffe is a delightful and humorous tale set in the enchanting town of Fontainbleau. The story follows a group of colorful characters as they navigate love, mistaken identities, and hilarious misunderstandings. With lively music and witty dialogue, this comic opera takes the audience on a whimsical journey filled with laughter and romance.

Genres: 
Comic opera, Musical, Comedy

Themes: 
Love and romance, Mistaken identities, Humor
