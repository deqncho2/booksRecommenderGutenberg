Synopsis: 

In "Florence and Northern Tuscany with Genoa," author Edward Hutton takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting landscapes of Italy. With the help of William Parkinson's stunning illustrations in colour and sixteen other illustrations, Hutton paints a vivid picture of the rich history, art, and architecture found in Florence and its surrounding regions. From the iconic landmarks of Florence to the hidden gems of Northern Tuscany and the vibrant city of Genoa, this second edition guidebook offers a comprehensive exploration of these captivating destinations. 

Genres: Travel, Art, History

Themes: Italian Renaissance, Cultural Heritage, Architectural Marvels
