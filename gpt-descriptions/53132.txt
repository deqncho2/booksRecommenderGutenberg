Synopsis: In 'The Night of the Trolls' by Keith Laumer, a small town is suddenly plagued by a series of bizarre and terrifying events. As the residents struggle to understand the source of these disturbances, they soon discover that they are facing a threat unlike anything they have ever encountered before. With the town's fate hanging in the balance, a group of unlikely heroes must band together to confront the ancient evil that has been awakened. Filled with suspense, mystery, and unexpected twists, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Horror, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Good vs. Evil, Courage, Community
