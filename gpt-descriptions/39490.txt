Synopsis: In "A Lad of Grit: A Story of Adventure on Land and Sea in Restoration Times," follow the thrilling journey of a young protagonist as he navigates through treacherous waters and dangerous lands during the Restoration era. From daring escapes to encounters with pirates, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover what lies ahead for our courageous hero.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Exploration, Loyalty
