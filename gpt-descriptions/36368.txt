Synopsis: In 'If Any Man Sin' by Cody, H. A., a small town is shaken by a mysterious murder that threatens to expose long-buried secrets. As the investigation unfolds, the residents find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the tension rises, and the true nature of the town's inhabitants is brought to light. Will justice prevail, or will the darkness that lurks within prevail?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Betrayal, Small-town dynamics
