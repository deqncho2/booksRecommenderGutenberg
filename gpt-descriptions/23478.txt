Synopsis: In 'Self-Denial; or, Alice Wood, and Her Missionary Society' by American Sunday-School Union, follow the captivating journey of Alice Wood as she embarks on a mission to make a difference in the world. Faced with challenges and obstacles, Alice's determination and selflessness inspire those around her, leading to unexpected outcomes and heartwarming moments. This uplifting tale explores the power of self-denial and the transformative impact of one person's dedication to a greater cause.

Genres: Fiction, Christian literature, Inspirational

Themes: Selflessness, Missionary work, Empathy, Personal growth
