Synopsis: In 'The Black Box: A Tale of Monmouth's Rebellion' by Cooke, W. Bourne, a young woman named Elizabeth finds herself caught in the midst of the tumultuous events of Monmouth's Rebellion in 17th century England. As she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and intrigue, Elizabeth must navigate treacherous alliances and make difficult choices that will determine her fate. With the fate of the rebellion hanging in the balance, Elizabeth's actions could have far-reaching consequences for both herself and those she holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Courage, Love, Political Intrigue
