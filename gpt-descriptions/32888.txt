Synopsis: 

In 'Paris, From the Earliest Period to the Present Day; Volume 2', author William Walton takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of the City of Light. From the grandeur of the medieval era to the tumultuous events of the French Revolution, this volume delves into the fascinating stories and hidden secrets that have shaped Paris into the iconic city it is today. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, Walton paints a vivid picture of the city's architectural marvels, cultural landmarks, and the vibrant lives of its inhabitants. From the opulent court of Louis XIV to the bohemian atmosphere of Montmartre, readers will be transported through time as they explore the captivating tapestry of Parisian history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travelogue

Themes: History, Architecture, Culture, Society
