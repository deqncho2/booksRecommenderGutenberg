Synopsis: In 'Thy Sea is Great, Our Boats are Small, and Other Hymns of To-Day', Henry Van Dyke takes readers on a poetic journey that explores the beauty and challenges of life. Through his heartfelt hymns, Van Dyke delves into the depths of human emotions, the wonders of nature, and the power of faith. With each verse, he invites readers to reflect on their own experiences and find solace in the midst of life's storms. This collection of hymns is a testament to the enduring power of hope and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Poetry, Inspirational, Spiritual

Themes: Faith, Nature, Human Emotions, Hope
