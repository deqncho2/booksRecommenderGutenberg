Synopsis: 
'The Tiger Hunter' follows the journey of Sami Malik, a young Indian immigrant who moves to America in pursuit of the American Dream. Armed with his engineering degree and a heart full of ambition, Sami faces a series of comical and heartwarming challenges as he navigates through a new country, a new job, and a new love interest. With the support of his quirky group of friends, Sami embarks on a hilarious and inspiring adventure that will make you laugh, cry, and cheer for his triumphs.

Genres: Comedy, Drama

Themes: Immigration, Identity, Pursuit of Dreams
