Synopsis: 

'Yorkshire Oddities, Incidents, and Strange Events' by Baring-Gould, S. is a captivating collection of bizarre and mysterious tales set in the enchanting region of Yorkshire. From eerie ghostly encounters to peculiar local customs, this book delves into the peculiarities and eccentricities that make Yorkshire truly unique. Prepare to be enthralled by accounts of haunted castles, unexplained phenomena, and extraordinary characters that will leave you questioning the boundaries of reality. 

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Folklore

Themes: Strange occurrences, Local legends, Supernatural phenomena
