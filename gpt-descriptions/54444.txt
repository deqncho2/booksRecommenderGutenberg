Synopsis: In "The Real Captain Kidd: A Vindication," Dalton, Cornelius Neale delves into the mysterious life of the infamous pirate, Captain William Kidd. Through meticulous research and compelling evidence, the author challenges the prevailing narrative surrounding Kidd's actions and motives. Uncovering hidden truths and shedding light on the complexities of Kidd's character, this captivating account takes readers on a thrilling journey through the high seas of piracy and the enigmatic life of a man who has been both vilified and romanticized.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Truth and perception, Justice and injustice, Legacy and reputation
