Synopsis: 
'A Bayard From Bengal: Being some account of the Magnificent and Spanking Career of Chunder Bindabun Bhosh, I.C.S.' is a humorous and adventurous novel that follows the extraordinary life of Chunder Bindabun Bhosh, an ambitious and resourceful Indian civil servant. From his humble beginnings in Bengal to his rise through the ranks of the Indian Civil Service, Chunder encounters a series of comical and unexpected situations that test his wit and cunning. As he navigates the complexities of British colonial rule and Indian society, Chunder's escapades take him from the bustling streets of Calcutta to the opulent palaces of Rajasthan, all while he strives to maintain his reputation and achieve his ambitions.

Genres: Humor, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Colonialism, Cultural Identity
