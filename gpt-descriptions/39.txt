Synopsis: In 'Hitchhiker's Guide to the Internet' by Krol, Ed, a young computer programmer stumbles upon a mysterious website that promises to reveal the secrets of the digital world. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic realm of the internet, he encounters eccentric characters, uncovers hidden truths, and embarks on a thrilling adventure that challenges his perception of reality. With wit, humor, and a touch of surrealism, this captivating tale explores the vastness of cyberspace and the profound impact it has on our lives.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Technology, Identity, Perception
