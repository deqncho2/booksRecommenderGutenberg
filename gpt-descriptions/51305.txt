Synopsis: 

In 'Confidence Game' by Jim Harmon, a charismatic con artist named Max is on a mission to pull off the ultimate heist. With his quick wit, charm, and a team of skilled accomplices, Max sets out to deceive and manipulate his way into a fortune. As the stakes rise and the risks become greater, Max finds himself entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where trust is a luxury and betrayal is always lurking around the corner. With unexpected twists and turns, 'Confidence Game' is a thrilling tale of deception, greed, and the lengths people will go to for power and wealth.

Genres: Thriller, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Trust, Greed
