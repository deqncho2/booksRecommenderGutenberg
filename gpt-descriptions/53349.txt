Synopsis: In 'Our Cavalry' by Michael Frederic Rimington, a group of young soldiers find themselves thrust into the chaos of war as they join the cavalry during a critical period in history. As they navigate the treacherous battlefields and face the harsh realities of combat, they must confront their own fears, forge unbreakable bonds, and discover the true meaning of courage. With vivid descriptions and heart-wrenching moments, this gripping tale takes readers on an unforgettable journey through the trials and triumphs of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Sacrifice, Identity
