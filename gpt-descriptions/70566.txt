Synopsis: In 'The Collected Works of Henrik Ibsen, Vol. 07 (of 11)', Ibsen takes us on a journey through a series of captivating plays that delve into the complexities of human relationships and societal norms. From the struggles of a young woman trapped in a loveless marriage to the exploration of the consequences of societal expectations, this collection offers a thought-provoking exploration of the human condition. With each play, Ibsen masterfully crafts characters that challenge the status quo and confront the consequences of their actions, leaving readers questioning their own beliefs and values.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy, Realism

Themes: Marriage and relationships, societal expectations, individual freedom, gender roles, morality
