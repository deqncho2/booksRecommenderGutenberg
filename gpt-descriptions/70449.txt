Synopsis: In 'Bluffer's Luck' by W. C. Tuttle, a down-on-his-luck cowboy named Jim Galloway finds himself caught in a web of deception and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden fortune. As he tries to outwit a ruthless gang of outlaws, Jim must rely on his quick thinking and resourcefulness to survive. With unexpected twists and a thrilling chase across the Wild West, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Deception, Redemption
