Synopsis: In 'The Wizard's Daughter, and Other Stories' by Margaret Collier Graham, embark on a captivating journey through a collection of enchanting tales. From a young girl with a mysterious heritage to a forbidden love affair, these stories will transport you to worlds filled with magic, secrets, and unexpected twists. Each narrative unveils a unique and compelling story, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover what lies beyond the next turn. With vivid imagery and rich storytelling, Graham weaves together a tapestry of emotions and experiences that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Love, Fate, Secrets
