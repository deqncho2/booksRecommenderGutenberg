Synopsis: In 'Janet Hardy in Radio City' by Wheeler, Ruthe S., follow the captivating journey of Janet Hardy, a young woman with big dreams, as she navigates the bustling world of Radio City in the 1940s. From her humble beginnings as a small-town girl to her rise as a talented performer, Janet encounters unexpected challenges, thrilling opportunities, and a whirlwind of romance. As she strives to make her mark in the glamorous world of entertainment, Janet must confront her own fears and insecurities, discovering the true power of determination and self-belief along the way.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Pursuing dreams, Overcoming obstacles, Self-discovery
