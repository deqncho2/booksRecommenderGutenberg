Synopsis: In 'Amusement Only' by Richard Marsh, a mysterious and alluring fortune-telling machine appears in a small seaside town, captivating the locals with its uncanny accuracy. As the machine's predictions become increasingly accurate and personal, it begins to reveal dark secrets and hidden desires, leading to unexpected consequences for those who dare to consult it. With each revelation, the line between reality and illusion blurs, leaving the characters questioning their own sanity and the true nature of the machine. This gripping tale of mystery and psychological suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fate and Destiny, Deception and Illusion, Human Desires and Obsessions
