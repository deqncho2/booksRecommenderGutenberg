Synopsis: Ruth Fielding, a spirited and determined college girl, finds herself embarking on a thrilling adventure in the wild and untamed land of gold. As she takes on the challenges of the rugged terrain and encounters mysterious characters, Ruth discovers her own strength and resilience. With her friends by her side, she navigates through unexpected twists and turns, uncovering secrets that could change everything. Will Ruth's courage and determination lead her to success, or will she be faced with insurmountable obstacles?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Self-discovery
