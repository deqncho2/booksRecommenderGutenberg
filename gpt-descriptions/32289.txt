Synopsis: In 'An American Girl Abroad' by Trafton, Adeline, follow the captivating journey of Amelia, a young American woman who embarks on a life-changing adventure across Europe. Leaving behind her familiar surroundings, Amelia immerses herself in the rich cultures, breathtaking landscapes, and unexpected encounters that await her. As she navigates through the complexities of new friendships, love, and self-discovery, Amelia's experiences abroad will challenge her perceptions, broaden her horizons, and ultimately shape her into the person she was always meant to be.

Genres: Travel, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Cultural immersion
