Synopsis: 
In 'Odd' by Amy Le Feuvre, a young girl named Amy finds herself in a peculiar situation when she is sent to live with her distant relatives in a mysterious old house. As she explores the house and its surroundings, Amy discovers hidden secrets and encounters strange occurrences that challenge her perception of reality. With the help of an unexpected friend, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and unravels the enigma that lies within the walls of the house. 

Genres: 
Mystery, Fantasy, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Identity, Perception, Friendship
