Synopsis: In 'The Piskey-Purse: Legends and Tales of North Cornwall', Tregarthen Enys weaves a captivating collection of enchanting stories set in the mystical landscapes of North Cornwall. From mischievous piskey folk to ancient legends of lost treasures, each tale takes readers on a magical journey through the rich folklore of the region. As the stories unfold, secrets are revealed, and the line between reality and fantasy becomes delightfully blurred. With its vivid imagery and captivating storytelling, this collection is sure to transport readers to a world where legends come alive.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Short Stories

Themes: Mythology, Magic, Cornwall's Cultural Heritage
