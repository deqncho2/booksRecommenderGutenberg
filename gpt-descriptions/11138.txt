Synopsis: In 'Comic History of England' by Bill Nye, embark on a hilarious journey through the rich and often absurd history of England. From the eccentricities of the monarchy to the peculiarities of British culture, Nye's witty and satirical commentary will keep readers entertained and laughing throughout. Discover the surprising and comical events that shaped England's past, all presented with Nye's signature humor and wit.

Genres: Humor, History, Satire

Themes: British history, Monarchy, Cultural eccentricities
