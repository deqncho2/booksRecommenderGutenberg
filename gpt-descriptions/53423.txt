Synopsis: 

In 'Better Than Men' by Hawkins, Rush C., a group of women find themselves at a crossroads in their lives, each facing unique challenges and searching for fulfillment. When they come together to form an unlikely alliance, they discover the power of female friendship and the strength that lies within them. As they navigate through personal and professional obstacles, their bond grows stronger, leading them on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment. 

Genres: Women's Fiction, Contemporary Fiction, Friendship

Themes: Female empowerment, self-discovery, friendship, overcoming challenges
