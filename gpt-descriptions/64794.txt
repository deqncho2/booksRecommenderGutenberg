Synopsis: 
In 'Betsy Baker! or, Too Attentive by Half; A Farce, in One Act', the charming and vivacious Betsy Baker finds herself at the center of a hilarious comedy of errors. As suitors vie for her attention, misunderstandings and mistaken identities abound, leading to uproarious situations and unexpected revelations. With witty dialogue and clever plot twists, this one-act farce promises an evening of laughter and entertainment.

Genres: Farce, Comedy

Themes: Love and courtship, Mistaken identity, Social conventions
