Synopsis: 
'Philipp Reis: Inventor of the Telephone: A Biographical Sketch' by Thompson, Silvanus P. delves into the fascinating life of Philipp Reis, the lesser-known inventor of the telephone. This biographical sketch takes readers on a captivating journey through Reis' struggles, triumphs, and the groundbreaking invention that would revolutionize communication. From his humble beginnings to his relentless pursuit of innovation, this book uncovers the untold story of a brilliant mind and the impact of his invention on the world.

Genres: Biography, History, Science

Themes: Innovation, Persistence, Communication
