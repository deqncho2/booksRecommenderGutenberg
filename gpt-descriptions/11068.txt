Synopsis: In "The Spirit of the Age; Or, Contemporary Portraits," Hazlitt presents a collection of insightful and captivating portraits of the leading figures of his time. From poets and politicians to actors and philosophers, Hazlitt delves into the essence of each individual, exploring their character, achievements, and impact on society. Through his sharp observations and eloquent prose, he offers a unique perspective on the spirit of the age, shedding light on the cultural and intellectual landscape of the early 19th century.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Individuality, Influence, Cultural Analysis
