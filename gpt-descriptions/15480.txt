Synopsis: 

In the June 1915 edition of the New York Times Current History, delve into the gripping and tumultuous events that unfolded during the months of April to September 1915, amidst the chaos of the European War. This volume presents a comprehensive collection of articles, reports, and analyses that provide a vivid and detailed account of the ongoing conflict, offering a unique perspective on the war's progression and its impact on various nations involved. From the frontlines to the diplomatic negotiations, from the military strategies to the humanitarian crises, this edition of the New York Times Current History paints a vivid picture of a world in turmoil, capturing the essence of a pivotal period in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, War journalism, Periodical

Themes: World War I, International diplomacy, Military strategy, Humanitarian crises
