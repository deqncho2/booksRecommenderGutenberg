Synopsis:
In the remote Austrian Alps, a young woman named Wally defies societal expectations and embraces her wild spirit. Known as the Vulture Maiden, she possesses an uncanny connection with the majestic birds that soar above the mountains. As Wally navigates the challenges of love, family, and her own identity, she must confront the prejudices and limitations imposed upon her by a conservative society. Set against the breathtaking backdrop of the Alps, 'The Vulture Maiden' is a captivating tale of courage, independence, and the pursuit of freedom.

Genres:
Romance, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Gender roles, Nature, Individualism
