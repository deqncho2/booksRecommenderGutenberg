Synopsis: 
'Old Melbourne Memories: Second Edition, Revised' takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant streets of Melbourne in the late 19th century. Through the eyes of the renowned Australian author, Boldrewood, we are transported to a time of bustling markets, grand architecture, and colorful characters. From the opulent ballrooms of the elite to the gritty laneways of the working class, this collection of nostalgic vignettes paints a vivid picture of a city on the cusp of transformation. With each story, Boldrewood masterfully captures the essence of a bygone era, evoking a sense of nostalgia and wonder that will leave readers yearning for the Melbourne of old.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Anthology

Themes: Nostalgia, Urban Transformation, Social Class
