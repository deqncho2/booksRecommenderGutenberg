Synopsis: In "Abraham Lincoln: The Practical Mystic," Grierson, Francis delves into the enigmatic life of one of America's most revered presidents. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Grierson uncovers the lesser-known aspects of Lincoln's character, revealing a man who possessed a deep connection to the spiritual realm. From his early years in a log cabin to his tumultuous presidency during the Civil War, Lincoln's journey is intertwined with his profound spiritual experiences and his ability to tap into a higher power. Grierson's exploration of Lincoln's practical mysticism offers a fresh perspective on the man behind the legend, shedding light on the extraordinary forces that shaped his leadership and ultimately changed the course of history.

Genres: Biography, History, Spiritual

Themes: Leadership, Spirituality, Personal Growth
