Synopsis: In 'Juju' by Leinster, Murray, a renowned archaeologist embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the secrets of an ancient African civilization. As he delves deeper into the mysteries of the past, he becomes entangled in a web of dark forces and supernatural occurrences. With his life hanging in the balance, he must navigate treacherous landscapes and confront his own beliefs to unravel the truth that lies hidden beneath the surface.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Exploration of ancient civilizations, the clash between science and superstition, the power of belief
