Synopsis: In 'The Story of the Rock', a group of adventurous young boys embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the secrets hidden within a mysterious rock formation. As they delve deeper into the rock's history, they encounter unexpected challenges and face the test of their friendship. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must rely on their courage and resourcefulness to unravel the enigma of the rock and its significance. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Perseverance
