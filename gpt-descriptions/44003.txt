Synopsis: In 'Warren Commission (03 of 26): Hearings Vol. III (of 15)', the United States Warren Commission delves deeper into the investigation surrounding the assassination of President John F. Kennedy. Through a series of hearings, witnesses are called to testify, shedding light on the events leading up to that fateful day in Dallas. As the commission uncovers new evidence and explores various theories, the truth becomes increasingly elusive, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets hidden within the pages.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, True Crime

Themes: Assassination, Conspiracy, Government Investigation
