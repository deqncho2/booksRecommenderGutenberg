Synopsis: 

"A Military Dictionary and Gazetteer" is a comprehensive reference book that delves into the world of military terminology, historical accounts of North American Indians and ancient warlike tribes, as well as notable battles throughout history. It also provides a concise explanation of heraldry terms and their significance, along with valuable geographical information. Compiled from the best authorities of all nations, this work serves as an invaluable resource for military enthusiasts, historians, and anyone seeking a deeper understanding of military terminology and historical events.

Genres: Reference, History, Military

Themes: Military terminology, Historical accounts, War, Heraldry, Geography
