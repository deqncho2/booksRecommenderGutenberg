Synopsis: In 'The Master-Girl' by Hilliers, Ashton, a young woman named Emily finds herself entangled in a mysterious world of secrets and hidden identities when she takes a job as a governess in a grand estate. As she delves deeper into the lives of the enigmatic family she works for, Emily uncovers a web of deceit, forbidden love, and a dark past that threatens to consume them all. With each revelation, Emily must navigate treacherous waters, questioning her own sanity and the true nature of those around her. Will she uncover the truth before it's too late, or will she become just another victim of the master's game?

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Deception, Forbidden Love, Sanity
