Synopsis: 

In the second volume of "The life and times of George Villiers, duke of Buckingham," the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the intriguing life of one of England's most influential figures. From his rise to power as a favorite of King James I to his controversial relationships and political maneuverings, this volume delves deeper into the complex character of George Villiers. With original and authentic sources, the author paints a vivid portrait of a man who was both admired and despised, exploring the scandals, ambitions, and triumphs that shaped his life. As Buckingham navigates the treacherous waters of court politics and personal relationships, the reader is left wondering what lies ahead for this enigmatic figure.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Power and influence, Court politics, Personal relationships, Ambition
