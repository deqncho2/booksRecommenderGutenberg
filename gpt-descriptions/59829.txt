Synopsis: In 'The Queer Folk of Fife: Tales from the Kingdom' by Pryde, David, readers are transported to the enchanting region of Fife, Scotland, where a collection of captivating and mysterious tales unfold. From ancient legends to modern-day encounters, this book delves into the lives of the queer folk who inhabit this magical kingdom. As the stories intertwine, readers will be drawn into a world filled with folklore, romance, and unexpected twists, leaving them eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the pages.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, LGBTQ+

Themes: Folklore, Identity, Love and Relationships, Mystery
