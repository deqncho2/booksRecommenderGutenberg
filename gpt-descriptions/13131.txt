Synopsis: In the remote village of Rylstone, a dark secret lurks beneath the moonlit nights. As the townspeople fall victim to a series of gruesome attacks, suspicion and fear grip the community. Amidst the chaos, a young woman named White Fell finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. With the help of a brave and enigmatic stranger, she embarks on a treacherous journey to uncover the truth behind the terrifying creature that haunts their midst. As the moon waxes and wanes, the line between human and beast blurs, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Gothic fiction, horror, mystery

Themes: Transformation, identity, fear, the supernatural
