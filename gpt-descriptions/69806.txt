Synopsis: In 'The Circe of the Deserts' by Henry-Bordeaux, Paule, a young Frenchwoman named Jeanne embarks on a journey to the deserts of North Africa, where she becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue. Drawn into the captivating world of a powerful desert queen, Jeanne must navigate the treacherous sands of love, betrayal, and self-discovery. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the desert, Jeanne's life is forever transformed, leading her on a path she never could have imagined.

Genres: Adventure, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration of cultural identity, Forbidden love, Female empowerment
