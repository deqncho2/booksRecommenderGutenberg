Synopsis: In the enchanting ballad 'Ermeline', a young woman named Ermeline finds herself caught between the allure of the mysterious forest and the expectations of her village. As she embarks on a journey of self-discovery, Ermeline encounters magical creatures, faces dangerous trials, and uncovers long-hidden secrets that will forever change her life. With lyrical prose and captivating imagery, 'Ermeline' weaves a tale of adventure, love, and the power of embracing one's true nature.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Self-discovery, Nature, Identity
