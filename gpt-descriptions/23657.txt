Synopsis: In 'That Sweet Little Old Lady', a seemingly innocent elderly woman named Mrs. Phipps moves into a quiet suburban neighborhood. However, her charming demeanor hides a dark secret that begins to unravel when strange occurrences start happening around her. As the neighbors become increasingly suspicious, they embark on a thrilling journey to uncover the truth about this sweet little old lady.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Secrets, Trust
