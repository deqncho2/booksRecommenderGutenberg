Synopsis: 

'Green Spring Farm, Fairfax County, Virginia' takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of a remarkable place. From its origins as a colonial plantation to its transformation into a thriving community, this book unveils the untold stories and hidden secrets of Green Spring Farm. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors delve into the lives of the people who shaped the land, the conflicts that unfolded, and the enduring legacy that remains. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be transported to a world filled with intrigue, triumph, and the ever-present echoes of the past.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Local history

Themes: Land ownership and development, Colonial America, Community building, Historical preservation
