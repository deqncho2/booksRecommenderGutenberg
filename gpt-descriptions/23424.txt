Synopsis: In 'The Flag of My Country = Shikéyah Bidah Na'at'a'í', author Cecil S. King takes readers on a captivating journey through the life of a Navajo code talker during World War II. Through the eyes of the protagonist, we witness the challenges, sacrifices, and triumphs of a young Native American man as he navigates the complexities of war and his cultural identity. As the code talker's story unfolds, readers are immersed in a tale of bravery, resilience, and the power of language.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Cultural Fiction

Themes: Identity, Heritage, Language, War, Bravery
