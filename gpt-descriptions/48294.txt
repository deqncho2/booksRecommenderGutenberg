Synopsis: In "Sir Christopher: A Romance of a Maryland Manor in 1644," Maud Wilder Goodwin takes readers on a captivating journey to colonial Maryland, where the lives of Sir Christopher, a young English nobleman, and the inhabitants of a Maryland manor intertwine in unexpected ways. As Sir Christopher navigates the challenges of his new life in the New World, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden love, and political intrigue. With vivid descriptions and rich historical detail, Goodwin paints a vivid picture of a bygone era, where loyalty, honor, and the pursuit of freedom collide in a tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery, Power and Politics
