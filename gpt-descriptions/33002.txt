Synopsis: In 'The Shoemaker's Apron: A Second Book of Czechoslovak Fairy Tales and Folk Tales', Fillmore Parker takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of Czechoslovakia. From mischievous spirits and talking animals to brave heroes and cunning witches, this collection of fairy tales and folk tales is filled with magical adventures and unexpected twists. Each story transports readers to a different time and place, where they will encounter both familiar and unfamiliar characters, all with their own unique challenges and desires. With its rich storytelling and vivid imagery, 'The Shoemaker's Apron' is a delightful exploration of the Czechoslovakian folklore that will leave readers spellbound.

Genres: Fairy tales, Folk tales, Fantasy

Themes: Magic, Heroism, Transformation, Morality
