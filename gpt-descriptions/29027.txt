Synopsis: In 'Spawn of the Comet' by Rich H. Thompson, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious comet passes overhead, leaving behind a trail of bizarre occurrences. As the townspeople grapple with the unexplainable events, they must confront their deepest fears and secrets, leading to a thrilling and suspenseful journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Supernatural phenomena, Small-town secrets, Fear and paranoia
