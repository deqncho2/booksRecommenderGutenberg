Synopsis: In 'Cord and Creese' by James De Mille, a young man named Cord sets out on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind a mysterious artifact known as the Creese. As he navigates treacherous landscapes and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Cord becomes entangled in a web of secrets and ancient legends. With each step closer to the truth, he finds himself questioning his own identity and the true nature of power. This thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as Cord's quest takes unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Power, Secrets
