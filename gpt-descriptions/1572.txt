Synopsis: In Plato's 'Timaeus', a group of philosophers gather to discuss the nature of the universe and the creation of the world. Through a captivating dialogue, they explore the concepts of time, space, and the existence of a divine craftsman. As they delve into the mysteries of the cosmos, they uncover profound insights that challenge their understanding of reality and the purpose of life.

Genres: Philosophy, Dialogue, Ancient Literature

Themes: Cosmology, Creation, Time, Divinity, Metaphysics
