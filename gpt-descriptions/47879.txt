Synopsis: In the April 1900 edition of 'The Brochure Series of Architectural Illustration,' readers are transported to the enchanting world of the Petit Trianon in Versailles, France. This volume showcases the exquisite beauty and intricate details of English carved fireplaces, adding a touch of elegance to any space. Through stunning illustrations and insightful commentary, readers are invited to explore the grandeur of this architectural gem and discover the timeless allure of these remarkable fireplaces.

Genres: Architecture, Art, Design

Themes: Beauty of architectural details, Historical preservation, Interior design
