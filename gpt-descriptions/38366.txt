Synopsis: In 'A New Subspecies of Ground Squirrel (Spermophilus spilosoma) from Tamaulipas, Mexico', Alvarez Ticul takes readers on a captivating journey through the discovery of a previously unknown subspecies of ground squirrel. Set in the picturesque region of Tamaulipas, Mexico, this scientific exploration unveils the fascinating world of wildlife and the tireless efforts of researchers to uncover hidden secrets. As the story unfolds, readers are immersed in the thrill of scientific discovery, the challenges faced by field researchers, and the intricate web of ecological relationships that shape our natural world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Scientific literature, Natural history

Themes: Biodiversity, Conservation, Scientific exploration
