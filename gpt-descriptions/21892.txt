Synopsis: In 'At the Time Appointed' by A. Maynard Barbour, a mysterious murder takes place in a small New England town, leaving the community in shock and suspicion. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the lives of the townspeople become intertwined in unexpected ways. With each twist and turn, the reader is drawn deeper into the web of intrigue, wondering who can be trusted and what dark secrets lie beneath the surface. This gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town dynamics, Trust and betrayal
