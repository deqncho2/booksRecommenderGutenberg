Synopsis: 
'Project Gutenberg Newsletters 1999: Thirteen Letters: December 1998 to December 1999' by Hart, Michael takes readers on a captivating journey through a year's worth of newsletters from Project Gutenberg, an online library of free eBooks. Each monthly installment offers a unique blend of literary insights, technological advancements, and the passionate dedication of volunteers. From discussions on the future of digital publishing to the challenges of preserving and sharing knowledge, this collection provides a fascinating glimpse into the world of digital literature and the tireless efforts behind its creation.

Genres: Non-fiction, Technology, Literature

Themes: Digital publishing, Preservation of knowledge, Volunteerism
