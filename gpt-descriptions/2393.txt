Synopsis: 
In 'His Dog' by Albert Payson Terhune, a young man named Larry is faced with a difficult decision when he inherits a loyal and intelligent collie named Bruce. As Larry navigates the challenges of his own life, he discovers the remarkable bond that can form between a man and his dog. With heartwarming moments and unexpected twists, 'His Dog' explores the profound impact that animals can have on our lives and the power of unconditional love.

Genres: 
Fiction, Animal Fiction, Adventure

Themes: 
Loyalty, Friendship, Unconditional Love
