Synopsis: In "The Emerald of Catherine the Great" by Belloc, Hilaire, a renowned art historian and a cunning thief find themselves entangled in a high-stakes race to possess a legendary emerald once owned by the powerful Catherine the Great. As they navigate through treacherous landscapes and encounter unexpected allies and enemies, the true value of the emerald becomes more than just a precious gem. With secrets, betrayals, and a touch of romance, this thrilling adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Art and its significance, the pursuit of power, loyalty and betrayal
