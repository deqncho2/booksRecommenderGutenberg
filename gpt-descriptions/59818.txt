Synopsis:
In the small village of Markenmore, a mysterious murder takes place, leaving the residents baffled and fearful. Inspector Hanaud, a renowned detective, is called in to solve the perplexing case. As he delves deeper into the secrets and hidden motives of the villagers, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and long-held grudges. With each twist and turn, the tension rises, and the truth becomes more elusive. Will Inspector Hanaud be able to unravel the Markenmore Mystery and bring the killer to justice?

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town Secrets
