Synopsis: In "Laura Secord, the heroine of 1812: A Drama; and Other Poems," Sarah Anne Curzon brings to life the remarkable story of Laura Secord, a courageous woman who played a pivotal role during the War of 1812. Through a captivating blend of drama and poetry, the author delves into the trials and triumphs of Laura Secord as she embarks on a dangerous journey to warn the British forces of an impending attack by the Americans. As the plot unfolds, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure filled with bravery, sacrifice, and the indomitable spirit of a true heroine.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Poetry

Themes: Heroism, Patriotism, Sacrifice, Women's empowerment
