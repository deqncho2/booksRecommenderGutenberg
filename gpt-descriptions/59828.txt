Synopsis: In the dark and mysterious streets of Fleet Street, a string of disappearances has left the city in fear. As the residents whisper about the infamous barber and his sinister accomplice, a young sailor named Sweeney Todd finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and revenge. With each victim, the barber's thirst for blood grows stronger, and Sweeney Todd must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth before he becomes the next victim.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Revenge, Madness, Deception
