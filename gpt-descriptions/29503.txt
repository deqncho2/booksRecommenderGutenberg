Synopsis: In 'The Hated' by Frederik Pohl, a mysterious and powerful alien race known as the Hated has arrived on Earth, bringing with them advanced technology and a chilling ultimatum. As humanity grapples with the implications of their arrival, a group of unlikely heroes must navigate a treacherous landscape of political intrigue, personal sacrifice, and moral dilemmas. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, they must confront their own prejudices and find a way to unite against a common enemy. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Political Drama

Themes: Alien invasion, Prejudice, Power dynamics, Survival
