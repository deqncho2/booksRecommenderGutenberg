Synopsis: In 'Historical Parallels, vol. 1 (of 3)', Arthur Thomas Malkin takes readers on a captivating journey through time, exploring the intriguing connections between different historical periods. From ancient civilizations to medieval Europe and beyond, Malkin uncovers surprising parallels that shed new light on the human experience. As he weaves together stories of power, love, and betrayal, readers will be enthralled by the intricate web of history that binds us all.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Non-fiction, Comparative History

Themes: Human nature, Power dynamics, Love and betrayal, Historical interconnectedness
