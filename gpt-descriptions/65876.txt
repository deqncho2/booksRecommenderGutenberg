Synopsis: 
In 'Theft' by Venable, Bill, a renowned art thief finds himself caught in a high-stakes game of cat and mouse when he is tasked with stealing a priceless artifact from a heavily guarded museum. As he navigates through a web of deception and danger, he must outsmart both the authorities and a rival thief who will stop at nothing to claim the artifact for themselves. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, 'Theft' takes readers on a thrilling journey into the world of art heists and the lengths one man will go to protect his secrets.

Genres: Crime, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
