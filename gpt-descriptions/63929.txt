Synopsis: 

In 'Christian Schools and Scholars: or, Sketches of Education from the Christian Era to the Council of Trent', Augusta Theodosia Drane takes readers on a captivating journey through the history of education within the Christian world. From the early days of Christianity to the pivotal Council of Trent, Drane explores the development of Christian schools and the influential scholars who shaped them. Through vivid sketches and engaging anecdotes, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the evolution of education and its profound impact on the Christian faith.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Education, Religious Studies

Themes: History of education, Christian scholarship, Influence of scholars, Evolution of Christian schools
