Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Dunnet Landing, a young writer named Sylvia spends her summer exploring the unique and captivating world of the local inhabitants. Through her encounters with the resilient and wise women of the community, Sylvia uncovers the hidden stories and deep connections that bind them together. As she delves into their lives, she discovers the beauty and complexity of human relationships, the power of nature, and the importance of finding one's place in the world.

Genres: Fiction, Regionalism, Realism

Themes: Community, Female Empowerment, Nature, Identity
