Synopsis: In the charming English countryside, a young woman named Anna finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries when she becomes the governess to a wealthy family. As she delves deeper into the family's history, Anna uncovers dark secrets that threaten to unravel their idyllic facade. With her wit and determination, she must navigate through a labyrinth of deception and betrayal to protect those she cares about. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Family Dynamics
