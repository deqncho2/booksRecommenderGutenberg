Synopsis: 
When the Tucker family embarks on a much-needed vacation, they never could have anticipated the wild adventures that await them. Join the mischievous Tucker twins, Max and Mia, as they navigate through unexpected challenges, encounter new friends, and uncover a hidden treasure that will change their lives forever. From thrilling escapades to heartwarming moments, this vacation will be one they'll never forget.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Family

Themes: Sibling bonds, Discovery, Adventure
