Synopsis: 
"The Old Man in the Corner" is a collection of intriguing mysteries that revolve around a peculiar character who sits in the corner of a London tea shop, solving crimes through his astute observations and deductive reasoning. As he spins his tales to a young journalist, he delves into unsolved cases, shedding light on the darkest corners of human nature and leaving the reader captivated by the enigmatic nature of the crimes.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Crime and Justice, Human Nature, Deductive Reasoning
