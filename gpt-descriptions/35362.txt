Synopsis: 
In 'Billy Barcroft, R.N.A.S.: A Story of the Great War', Percy F. Westerman takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the skies of World War I. The story follows Billy Barcroft, a young and ambitious pilot in the Royal Naval Air Service, as he faces the perils of aerial combat and the challenges of life on the front lines. From thrilling dogfights to heart-wrenching moments of loss, Billy's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. As the war rages on, he must navigate the complexities of friendship, loyalty, and the devastating impact of war on both individuals and nations. Will Billy's unwavering spirit and resilience be enough to carry him through the chaos and destruction of the Great War?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Loss, Resilience
