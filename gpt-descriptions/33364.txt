Synopsis: 

In 'A New Subspecies of the Fruit-eating Bat, Sturnira ludovici, From Western Mexico', Jones and Phillips take us on a captivating scientific journey deep into the heart of Western Mexico. Through meticulous research and stunning illustrations, they unveil the discovery of a previously unknown subspecies of fruit-eating bat, Sturnira ludovici. As they delve into the bat's unique characteristics, habitat, and behavior, a fascinating world of biodiversity and ecological significance unfolds before our eyes. 

Genres: Scientific research, Natural history, Zoology

Themes: Biodiversity, Ecological significance
