Synopsis: 
'Mink Trapping: A Book of Instruction Giving Many Methods of Trapping: A Valuable Book for Trappers' is a comprehensive guide that delves into the art of trapping minks, offering a plethora of techniques and strategies to successfully capture these elusive creatures. Written by A. R. Harding, this instructional book provides valuable insights and practical advice for trappers of all levels, ensuring an engaging and informative read.

Genres: Non-fiction, Outdoor and Wildlife, Instructional

Themes: Trapping techniques, Wildlife conservation, Outdoor skills
