Synopsis: In "The Rover Boys on the Ocean; Or, A chase for a fortune," the adventurous Rover brothers embark on a thrilling journey across the high seas. When they stumble upon a mysterious clue that leads to a hidden treasure, they find themselves caught in a dangerous chase filled with unexpected twists and turns. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter cunning adversaries, the Rover boys must rely on their wit, courage, and brotherly bond to outsmart their enemies and claim the fortune that awaits them.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Brotherhood, Courage, Treasure Hunting
