Synopsis: 

In 'Canadian Fairy Tales' by Cyrus MacMillan, readers are transported to a magical world where the enchanting folklore of Canada comes to life. From the mystical creatures that inhabit the vast wilderness to the captivating legends passed down through generations, this collection of fairy tales offers a delightful and captivating journey through the rich cultural heritage of Canada. Each story is filled with adventure, mystery, and a touch of whimsy, as readers encounter talking animals, brave heroes, and cunning tricksters. Whether it's a tale of a mischievous beaver, a courageous young girl, or a mythical creature guarding a hidden treasure, these enchanting stories are sure to captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Fairy Tales, Folklore, Children's Literature

Themes: Canadian culture, Nature and wildlife, Heroism, Folklore and legends
