Synopsis: 
In 'Alice Wilde: The Raftsman's Daughter. A Forest Romance' by Victor, Metta Victoria Fuller, readers are transported to the enchanting world of Alice Wilde, a spirited young woman living in the heart of a dense forest. Raised by her father, a skilled raftsman, Alice possesses an adventurous spirit and an unwavering love for nature. However, her life takes an unexpected turn when she encounters a mysterious stranger who awakens a deep longing within her. As their paths intertwine, Alice finds herself torn between her duty to her family and her desire for a life filled with passion and freedom. Will she choose the familiar or embark on a thrilling journey into the unknown?

Genres: Romance, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Love and longing, Nature and wilderness, Self-discovery
