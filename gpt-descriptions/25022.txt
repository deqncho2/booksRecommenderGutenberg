Synopsis: 
'Narrative of the shipwreck of the brig Betsey, of Wiscasset, Maine, and murder of five of her crew, by pirates, on the coast of Cuba, Dec. 1824.' is a gripping true story that recounts the harrowing events of a shipwreck and the subsequent murder of five crew members by pirates off the coast of Cuba. Written by Daniel Collins, this narrative takes readers on a thrilling journey as they uncover the shocking details of the tragedy and the desperate fight for survival in the face of ruthless criminals.

Genres: 
Adventure, True Crime, Historical

Themes: 
Survival, Crime and Punishment
