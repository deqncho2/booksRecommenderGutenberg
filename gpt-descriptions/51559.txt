Synopsis: In 'The Fledgling' by Nordhoff, Charles, a young man named Harvey Cheyne finds himself stranded on a fishing schooner after falling overboard from a luxury ocean liner. Forced to adapt to a life of hard work and camaraderie, Harvey's journey becomes a transformative experience that challenges his privileged upbringing and shapes his character. As he navigates the harsh realities of life at sea, Harvey must confront his own weaknesses and discover the true meaning of resilience and self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Transformation, Resilience, Social class, Self-discovery
