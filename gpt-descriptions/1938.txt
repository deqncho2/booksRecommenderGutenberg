Synopsis: 
Resurrection by Leo Tolstoy is a captivating novel that follows the life of a wealthy nobleman, Nekhlyudov, who finds himself on a transformative journey after serving on a jury for a murder trial. Filled with moral dilemmas, complex relationships, and profound introspection, Nekhlyudov's path intertwines with that of a young woman, leading him to confront his own past and question the societal norms of his time. As he seeks redemption and meaning, the story delves into the depths of human nature, exploring themes of justice, love, and the search for spiritual awakening.

Genres: 
Literary Fiction, Historical Fiction, Legal Drama

Themes: 
Redemption, Justice, Love, Social Critique, Spiritual Awakening
