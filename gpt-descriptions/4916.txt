Synopsis: 
In the small coastal town of Sea Haven, secrets run deep and danger lurks beneath the surface. When a mysterious stranger arrives, the lives of three women become intertwined in unexpected ways. As they navigate the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and redemption, they must confront their pasts and face the truth that threatens to drown them all.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Redemption
