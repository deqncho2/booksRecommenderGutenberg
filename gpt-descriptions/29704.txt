Synopsis: 
'Masterpieces of Mystery in Four Volumes: Riddle Stories' is a captivating collection of intriguing and mind-bending tales that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. From puzzling crimes to enigmatic puzzles, each story presents a unique challenge for both the characters and the readers. As the mysteries unfold, readers will find themselves engrossed in a world of suspense, trying to unravel the secrets hidden within each riddle. With unexpected twists and clever solutions, this collection promises to deliver an exhilarating reading experience that will leave readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Riddles, Puzzles, Suspense, Crime-solving
