Synopsis: In 'The Crucifixion of Philip Strong' by Charles M. Sheldon, a small-town pastor named Philip Strong finds himself at the center of a controversial trial that challenges his faith and tests the limits of his convictions. As the community becomes divided and tensions rise, Philip must navigate the complexities of justice, forgiveness, and the power of redemption. This gripping tale explores the profound impact of one man's choices on the lives of those around him, leaving readers questioning their own beliefs and the true meaning of sacrifice.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Religious

Themes: Faith, Justice, Redemption, Morality
