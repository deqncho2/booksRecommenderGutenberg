Synopsis: In 'Peace Manoeuvres' by Davis, Richard Harding, a renowned diplomat finds himself entangled in a web of international intrigue and political gamesmanship. As he navigates through a delicate peace negotiation, he must uncover hidden agendas, decipher cryptic messages, and outmaneuver his adversaries to ensure the success of the mission. With high stakes and unexpected twists, this gripping tale keeps readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Thriller, Political Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Diplomacy, Power Struggles, Deception
