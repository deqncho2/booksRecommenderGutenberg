Synopsis: In 'Hidden Hand' by Emma Dorothy Eliza Nevitte Southworth, a young woman named Capitola finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she is taken in by a wealthy and eccentric old man. As she navigates through a series of unexpected events and encounters, Capitola must rely on her wit and resourcefulness to uncover the truth behind the hidden hand that seems to control her fate. With secrets, romance, and danger lurking around every corner, this captivating novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Power, Deception
