Synopsis: 

In 'Piano Tuning: A Simple and Accurate Method for Amateurs', author J. Cree Fischer takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of piano tuning. With a unique blend of technical expertise and engaging storytelling, Fischer unveils a method that allows even amateurs to achieve professional-level results. As readers delve into the intricacies of this art form, they will discover the secrets behind the harmonious symphony of notes that emanate from a well-tuned piano. From the delicate adjustments of the strings to the precise manipulation of the hammers, Fischer's step-by-step instructions provide a comprehensive guide for anyone seeking to unlock the true potential of their instrument. 

Genres: Non-fiction, How-to, Music

Themes: Music appreciation, Skill development, Artistic craftsmanship
