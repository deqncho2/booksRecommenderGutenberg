Synopsis: In "The Girl Scouts at Miss Allen's School" by Edith Lavell, a group of adventurous Girl Scouts find themselves embarking on a thrilling journey when they attend a prestigious boarding school. As they navigate the challenges of school life, they uncover a mysterious secret that leads them on a quest filled with danger and excitement. With their determination and teamwork, the Girl Scouts must unravel the truth and protect what they hold dear.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
