Synopsis: In 'The Fire Trumpet: A Romance of the Cape Frontier' by Mitford, Bertram, a young Englishman named Hugh Wynne finds himself caught in the midst of the turbulent Cape Frontier during the 19th century. As he navigates the treacherous landscape, he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and adventure. With the backdrop of the stunning African wilderness, Hugh must confront his own desires and confront the challenges of a land on the brink of change.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Betrayal, Cultural Conflict, Wilderness and Survival
