Synopsis: In 'The Young Engineers on the Gulf: Or, The Dread Mystery of the Million Dollar Breakwater', a group of talented young engineers find themselves embroiled in a thrilling and dangerous adventure as they attempt to solve the perplexing mystery surrounding a valuable breakwater project. With their skills and determination, they must navigate treacherous waters, face unexpected obstacles, and uncover hidden secrets that could change everything. Will they be able to unravel the enigma and save the day?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Problem-solving, Teamwork, Perseverance
