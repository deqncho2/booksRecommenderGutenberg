Synopsis: Janice Day, a curious and adventurous young girl, finds herself in the enchanting world of Poketown, a place where imagination comes to life. As she explores this magical realm, Janice encounters a cast of colorful characters and embarks on a thrilling quest to uncover the secrets of Poketown. With each step she takes, Janice discovers that the boundaries between reality and fantasy are not as clear-cut as they seem, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and wonder.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Imagination, Exploration, Self-discovery
