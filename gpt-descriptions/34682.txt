Synopsis: In 'The Secret Pact' by Mildred A. Wirt, a group of adventurous friends stumble upon a mysterious hidden room in an old mansion. Inside, they discover a hidden message that leads them on a thrilling quest filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected twists. As they unravel the secrets of the past, they must confront their own fears and make a pact that will change their lives forever.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Secrets, Discovery
