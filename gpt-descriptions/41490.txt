Synopsis: In 'The Decadent: Being the Gospel of Inaction', Ralph Adams Cram takes readers on a captivating journey through the curious characteristics of the ultimate years. Through a captivating romance form, the author explores the diverse causes behind the decadence of this era, offering intriguing reflections that will leave readers pondering the state of society. 

Genres: Romance, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Decadence, Society, Reflection, Inaction
