Synopsis: In 'Shavings: A Novel' by Joseph Crosby Lincoln, a small New England town becomes the backdrop for a captivating tale of mystery and intrigue. When a series of peculiar events unfold, the lives of the townspeople are forever changed. As secrets are revealed and unexpected connections are made, the reader is drawn into a web of suspense that keeps them guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Small-town secrets, Intrigue, Redemption
