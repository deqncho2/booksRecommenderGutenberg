Synopsis: In 'The City of Numbered Days' by Lynde, Francis, a young artist named Gabriel finds himself drawn into a mysterious world where time is both a precious commodity and a dangerous force. As he navigates through a city where every day is numbered, Gabriel must uncover the truth behind the enigmatic figures known as the Timekeepers and their hidden agenda. With the help of a captivating woman named Isabella, Gabriel embarks on a thrilling journey that challenges his perception of reality and tests the limits of his own existence.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Time, Identity, Perception, Power
