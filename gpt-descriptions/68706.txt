Synopsis: 
Step into the world of modern house-plans as Samuel Burrage Reed takes you on a captivating journey through a wide range of village and country residences. From affordable options starting at two hundred and fifty dollars to luxurious designs reaching up to eight thousand dollars, this book offers a treasure trove of architectural inspiration for everyone. With detailed illustrations and insightful descriptions, Reed presents a diverse collection of house-plans that cater to various tastes and budgets. Whether you dream of a cozy cottage or a grand estate, this book is sure to ignite your imagination and provide practical guidance for creating your ideal home.

Genres: Architecture, Home Improvement, Design

Themes: Affordable housing, Architectural diversity, Home customization
