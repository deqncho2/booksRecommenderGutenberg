Synopsis: 
'Plays: Second Series' by John Galsworthy is a captivating collection of four thought-provoking plays that delve into the complexities of human relationships and societal issues. In these plays, Galsworthy masterfully explores themes of love, power, justice, and the struggle between tradition and progress. From the poignant tale of a forbidden love affair to the gripping story of a man's fight for justice, each play in this series offers a unique and compelling narrative that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Drama, Social Realism, Tragedy

Themes: Love, Power, Justice, Tradition vs. Progress
