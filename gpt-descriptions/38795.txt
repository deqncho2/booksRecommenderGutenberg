Synopsis: In "With Drake on the Spanish Main" by Herbert Strang, embark on a thrilling adventure set in the 16th century, where young Ned Hearne finds himself caught up in the dangerous world of piracy and treasure hunting. Join Ned as he sails the treacherous seas alongside the infamous Sir Francis Drake, encountering fierce battles, hidden treasures, and unexpected alliances. As Ned's courage and loyalty are put to the test, he must navigate a world of deceit and danger, where the line between friend and foe becomes blurred. Will Ned survive the perils of the Spanish Main and emerge victorious?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Treasure Hunting, Friendship
