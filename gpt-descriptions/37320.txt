Synopsis:
In 'Tiny Luttrell' by E. W. Hornung, a young woman named Tiny finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she becomes entangled with a notorious criminal. As she navigates the treacherous world of crime and deception, Tiny must rely on her wit and resourcefulness to survive. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres:
Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes:
Deception, Survival
