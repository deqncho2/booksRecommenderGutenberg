Synopsis: In 'The Coming' by J. C. Snaith, a small English village is thrown into turmoil when a mysterious stranger arrives, claiming to have knowledge of a life-altering event that will soon occur. As the villagers grapple with their own fears and desires, they must confront the possibility of an impending catastrophe that could change their lives forever. With secrets, suspense, and a touch of the supernatural, 'The Coming' takes readers on a gripping journey of anticipation and uncertainty.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Supernatural

Themes: Fear of the unknown, Human nature, Fate vs. free will
