Synopsis: 

'Point Park, Lookout Mountain and Chattanooga Battlefields: Chickamauga and Chattanooga National Military Park, Georgia and Tennessee' takes readers on a captivating journey through the historic battlefields of Chickamauga and Chattanooga. This immersive guide, crafted by the United States National Park Service, offers a vivid exploration of the landscapes, events, and individuals that shaped these pivotal moments in American history. From the strategic maneuvers of military leaders to the personal stories of soldiers and civilians, this book provides a compelling account of the battles that unfolded in these hallowed grounds. With detailed maps, photographs, and engaging narratives, readers will gain a deeper understanding of the sacrifices made and the lasting impact of these conflicts.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Travel guide, Military history

Themes: War and conflict, Leadership, Commemoration and remembrance
