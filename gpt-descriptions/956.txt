Synopsis: In 'Tik-Tok of Oz', join Dorothy and her friends as they embark on a thrilling adventure to rescue the Royal Family of Ev from the clutches of the evil Nome King. Along the way, they encounter enchanting creatures, magical lands, and a mechanical man named Tik-Tok who proves to be an invaluable ally. As they navigate through treacherous obstacles and face unexpected challenges, the group must rely on their wit, courage, and friendship to overcome the perils that lie ahead.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Loyalty, Good vs. Evil
