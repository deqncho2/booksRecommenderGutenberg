Synopsis: In this captivating issue of 'The Great Round World and What Is Going On In It', readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the latest news and events from around the world. From the mysterious disappearance of a famous explorer to the discovery of a hidden treasure, this magazine keeps its young audience on the edge of their seats with its engaging stories and fascinating articles. With each turn of the page, readers are transported to different corners of the globe, uncovering secrets and unraveling mysteries. Get ready for an adventure-filled reading experience that will leave you wanting more!

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Discovery, Curiosity
