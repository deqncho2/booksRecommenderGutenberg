Synopsis: 
In "The Benson Murder Case" by S. S. Van Dine, the brilliant detective Philo Vance is called upon to solve a perplexing murder case that has left the police baffled. When a wealthy businessman is found dead in his study, with no apparent motive or evidence, Vance must navigate through a web of deceit, secrets, and unexpected twists to uncover the truth. As he delves deeper into the investigation, Vance encounters a cast of intriguing characters, each with their own motives and alibis. With his sharp intellect and keen observation skills, Vance must unravel the complex puzzle before the killer strikes again.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Crime Fiction

Themes: Crime and Justice, Deception and Betrayal, Intellectualism
