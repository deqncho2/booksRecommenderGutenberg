Synopsis: In 'The Train Boy' by Alger, Horatio, Jr., follow the captivating journey of a young boy named Frank who finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure after a chance encounter with a mysterious stranger on a train. As Frank's life takes an unexpected turn, he must navigate through a world filled with secrets, danger, and unexpected friendships. Will Frank's determination and resourcefulness be enough to overcome the challenges he faces? Prepare to be swept away in this gripping tale of courage, resilience, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Courage, Resilience, Self-discovery
