Synopsis: In "The Life and Teaching of Karl Marx" by Max Beer, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the tumultuous life of one of history's most influential thinkers. From Marx's early years in Germany to his revolutionary ideas that would shape the course of history, Beer delves into the personal struggles, intellectual breakthroughs, and political battles that defined Marx's life. With meticulous research and engaging storytelling, this biography offers a fascinating exploration of the man behind the ideology, shedding light on the complexities and contradictions that made Karl Marx a figure of both admiration and controversy.

Genres: Biography, History, Political Science

Themes: Marxism, Socialism, Revolution, Class Struggle
