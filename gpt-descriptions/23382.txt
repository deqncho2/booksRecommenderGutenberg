Synopsis: In 'Crown and Sceptre: A West Country Story' by George Manville Fenn, a young orphan named Dick is thrust into a world of mystery and adventure when he discovers a hidden secret within the walls of an old inn. As he unravels the truth behind the inn's past, Dick finds himself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue, where his courage and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Set against the backdrop of the picturesque West Country, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within the Crown and Sceptre inn.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden histories, Courage and resilience, Coming of age
