Synopsis: In 'Vashti; Or, Until Death Us Do Part', Augusta J. Evans weaves a captivating tale of love, betrayal, and redemption. Set in the antebellum South, the story follows the life of Vashti, a young woman whose world is turned upside down when she discovers a devastating secret about her husband. As she navigates the complexities of her marriage and the societal expectations placed upon her, Vashti must confront her own desires and make choices that will shape her future. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this novel explores the depths of human emotions and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Gender Roles, Redemption
