Synopsis: In "The Young Continentals at Trenton" by John T. McIntyre, a group of young patriots find themselves caught in the midst of the American Revolution. As they join General George Washington's army, they embark on a dangerous mission to turn the tide of the war. Facing treacherous conditions, unexpected alliances, and the harsh realities of war, these young heroes must summon their courage and resourcefulness to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Bravery, Friendship, Sacrifice
