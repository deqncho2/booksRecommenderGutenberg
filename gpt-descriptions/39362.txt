Synopsis: In 'Punch, or the London Charivari, October 28th 1893', readers are transported to the vibrant and satirical world of Victorian England. Through a collection of witty and humorous articles, cartoons, and social commentaries, this edition of Punch offers a delightful glimpse into the absurdities and eccentricities of the era. From political satire to social observations, this publication captures the essence of Victorian society with its sharp wit and clever insights.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Periodical

Themes: Victorian society, Politics, Social commentary
