Synopsis: 
In 'Three Thousand Dollars' by Anna Katharine Green, a young woman named Violet Strange finds herself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when she is hired to solve a perplexing case involving a missing necklace. As she delves deeper into the investigation, Violet discovers that there is much more at stake than just a piece of jewelry. With her sharp wit and determination, she must navigate through a labyrinth of secrets and deceit to uncover the truth, all while facing unexpected dangers and unexpected allies.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Female empowerment, Justice and truth
