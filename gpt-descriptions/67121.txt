Synopsis: In 'Polaris and the Goddess Glorian' by Charles B. Stilson, a young astronomer named Polaris embarks on a thrilling journey through time and space after discovering a mysterious artifact. As he travels to distant galaxies and encounters otherworldly beings, Polaris must unravel the secrets of the artifact and confront his own destiny. With breathtaking imagery and a captivating narrative, this sci-fi adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Fantasy

Themes: Exploration, Destiny, Time and Space
