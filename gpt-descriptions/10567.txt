Synopsis: In 'The Guardian Angel: Ship's Company, Part 7,' the crew of the tramp steamer S.S. Guardian Angel find themselves embroiled in a mysterious and dangerous situation while docked in a foreign port. As they navigate through a web of intrigue and deception, they must rely on their wits and teamwork to uncover the truth and protect their ship. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling installment of the Ship's Company series will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Survival
