Synopsis: In 'Heart and Science: A Story of the Present Time' by Wilkie Collins, a renowned surgeon becomes entangled in a web of mystery and intrigue when a series of unexplained deaths occur in his vicinity. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers shocking secrets that challenge his beliefs and force him to confront the dark side of scientific progress. With a cast of complex characters and a gripping plot, this novel explores the ethical dilemmas and moral consequences of scientific advancements in the Victorian era.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic fiction, Social commentary

Themes: Science and morality, the pursuit of knowledge, the dangers of unchecked ambition, societal expectations and gender roles
