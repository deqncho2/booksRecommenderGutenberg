Synopsis: In "My Leper Friends," Alice M. Hayes takes us on a captivating journey into her personal work among lepers in India. Through vivid storytelling, she offers a unique glimpse into the daily lives of these individuals, sharing their struggles, triumphs, and the profound impact they have on her own life. As Hayes immerses herself in their world, she uncovers the resilience, strength, and humanity that exist within the leper community, challenging societal stigmas and inspiring readers to see beyond the disease. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Cultural Studies

Themes: Empathy, Resilience, Social Stigma, Cross-cultural Understanding
