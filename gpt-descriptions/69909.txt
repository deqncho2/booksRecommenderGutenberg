Synopsis: 

Step into the fascinating world of cork as the Armstrong Cork Company takes you on a captivating journey through the origin, manufacturing processes, and diverse applications of this remarkable material. Discover the secrets behind the creation of cork and explore its countless uses in today's world. From its humble beginnings to its extraordinary versatility, this book unveils the untold story of cork, leaving readers intrigued and eager to learn more.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Innovation, Sustainability, Material Science
