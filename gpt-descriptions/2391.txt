Synopsis: 
In 'Bruce' by Albert Payson Terhune, a young and spirited collie named Bruce finds himself torn between his loyalty to his human family and his instinctual desire to roam free. As he navigates the challenges of life in the countryside, Bruce encounters a variety of characters, both human and animal, who shape his understanding of the world. With his intelligence and determination, Bruce embarks on a journey of self-discovery, ultimately discovering the true meaning of loyalty and the power of unconditional love.

Genres: 
Adventure, Animal Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
Loyalty, Freedom, Self-discovery, Unconditional Love
