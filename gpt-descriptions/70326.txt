Synopsis: In 'The Art of Preserving Health: A Poem', Armstrong takes readers on a captivating journey through the realms of medicine, nature, and the human body. Through vivid imagery and lyrical verses, the poet explores the delicate balance between physical and mental well-being, offering insightful observations on the importance of maintaining one's health. From the bustling streets of London to the serene landscapes of the countryside, Armstrong's poetic narrative unveils the secrets to a harmonious existence, enticing readers to embark on a quest for vitality and longevity.

Genres: Poetry, Health and Wellness, Nature Writing

Themes: Health, Balance, Nature, Mind-Body Connection
