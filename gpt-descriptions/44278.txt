Synopsis: In 'From the Earth to the Moon, Direct in Ninety-Seven Hours and Twenty Minutes: and a Trip Round It', Jules Verne takes readers on an exhilarating journey as a group of ambitious individuals set out to accomplish the seemingly impossible: to travel to the moon. Filled with scientific ingenuity, daring adventures, and unexpected twists, this captivating tale explores the challenges and triumphs of human exploration beyond Earth's boundaries.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Speculative Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Human Ingenuity, Ambition, Scientific Progress
