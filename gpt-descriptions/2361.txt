Synopsis: In 'Why Go to College? An Address', Alice Freeman Palmer delivers a thought-provoking speech that challenges conventional wisdom and explores the true purpose of higher education. With eloquence and passion, she delves into the transformative power of knowledge, the importance of personal growth, and the societal benefits of an educated citizenry. Through her compelling arguments, Palmer inspires readers to question their assumptions and consider the profound impact that college can have on individuals and society as a whole.

Genres: Non-fiction, Speech, Education

Themes: Higher education, Personal growth, Societal impact
