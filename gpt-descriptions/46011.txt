Synopsis: In 'The Moving Picture Boys and the Flood; Or, Perilous Days on the Mississippi' by Victor Appleton, a group of adventurous boys find themselves caught in the midst of a devastating flood along the mighty Mississippi River. As they navigate the treacherous waters, they must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to survive the perils that await them. Along the way, they encounter unexpected challenges, uncover hidden secrets, and discover the true meaning of courage. Will they be able to overcome the forces of nature and make it out alive?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Resilience
