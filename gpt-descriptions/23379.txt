Synopsis: In 'Old Mr. Wiley' by La Spina, Greye, a young artist, moves into a mysterious old house to find inspiration for her paintings. However, she soon discovers that the house holds dark secrets and is haunted by the ghost of its former owner, Mr. Wiley. As Greye delves deeper into the house's history, she becomes entangled in a web of supernatural occurrences and must uncover the truth before it consumes her.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Supernatural

Themes: Haunting, Secrets, Artistic Inspiration
