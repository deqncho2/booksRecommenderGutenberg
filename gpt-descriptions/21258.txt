Synopsis: In 'Devil-Worship in France; or, The Question of Lucifer', Arthur Edward Waite delves into the mysterious world of occult practices and secret societies. Set in France, the story follows a young protagonist who becomes entangled in a web of devil-worship, dark rituals, and hidden truths. As he uncovers the secrets of Luciferian worship, he must confront his own beliefs and navigate the dangerous consequences that come with challenging the established order. With its gripping narrative and atmospheric setting, this book will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the boundaries between good and evil.

Genres: Occult fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Occultism, Secret societies, Good vs. evil
