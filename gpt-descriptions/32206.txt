Synopsis: 

'The Utah Batteries: A History' is a captivating historical account that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the muster-in, sea voyage, battles, skirmishes, and barrack life of the Utah batteries during a significant period in American history. Written by Charles Rendell Mabey, this comprehensive book also delves into the biographies of the officers and provides muster-out rolls, offering a complete picture of the brave soldiers who served.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, War experiences
