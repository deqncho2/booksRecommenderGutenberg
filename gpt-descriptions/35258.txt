Synopsis: 

In 'Contagious Abortion of Cows' by MacNeal, Ward J., a small rural community is struck by a mysterious and devastating disease that threatens the livelihood of its farmers. As the epidemic spreads, panic ensues, and the local veterinarian, Dr. Sarah Thompson, finds herself at the center of the crisis. With time running out and the fate of the community hanging in the balance, Dr. Thompson must race against the clock to uncover the cause of the outbreak and find a cure before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Medical Thriller, Drama

Themes: Disease outbreak, Community resilience, Scientific investigation
