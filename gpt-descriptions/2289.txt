Synopsis: In the small town of Rosmersholm, a former clergyman named John Rosmer finds himself torn between his past and his present as he grapples with guilt, love, and the weight of societal expectations. As he becomes entangled in a complex web of relationships with his free-thinking companion Rebecca West and his conservative brother-in-law Kroll, Rosmer's beliefs and values are put to the ultimate test. Secrets are unveiled, alliances are formed, and the characters are forced to confront their own desires and the consequences of their actions. Set against a backdrop of political unrest and social change, "Rosmersholm" is a gripping exploration of morality, duty, and the struggle for personal liberation.

Genres: Drama, Tragedy

Themes: Morality, Guilt, Love, Social Change
