Synopsis: In the enchanting world of ancient Japan, a young samurai named Hiroshi finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden village plagued by a centuries-old curse. As he delves deeper into the secrets of Koshiu, Hiroshi must navigate treacherous alliances, face supernatural adversaries, and confront his own inner demons. With the fate of the village hanging in the balance, Hiroshi must summon all his courage and skill to break the curse and save those he holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Honor and duty, the power of belief, the clash between tradition and progress
