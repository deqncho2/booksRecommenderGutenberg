Synopsis: In 'The Pothunters', a group of mischievous schoolboys at Linwood House School find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mystery when a valuable trophy goes missing. As they navigate the world of schoolboy pranks, secret societies, and amateur detective work, they uncover a web of intrigue that leads them on an unexpected adventure. With humor, wit, and a touch of mischief, P.G. Wodehouse takes readers on a delightful journey through the escapades of these young boys.

Genres: Mystery, Humor, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Adventure, School life
