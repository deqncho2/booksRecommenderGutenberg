Synopsis:
In 'History of the Wars, Books III and IV: The Vandalic War' by Procopius, the reader is transported to the tumultuous era of the 6th century, where the mighty Vandals have established a powerful kingdom in North Africa. As the Roman Empire struggles to regain its former glory, the narrative follows the gripping account of the Byzantine general Belisarius and his valiant efforts to reclaim the lost territories from the Vandals. Amidst treacherous alliances, epic battles, and political intrigue, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the heart of a war-torn empire, where the fate of nations hangs in the balance.

Genres:
Historical fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes:
War and conquest, Political intrigue, Loyalty and betrayal
