Synopsis: In 'Winner Take All' by Larry Evans, a brilliant chess prodigy finds himself caught in a high-stakes game of strategy and deception. As he navigates the cutthroat world of professional chess, he must confront his own inner demons and make difficult choices that will determine his fate. With intense rivalries, unexpected alliances, and a gripping climax, this thrilling novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last move.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Psychological Drama

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Identity, Redemption
