Synopsis: In 'The Story of Valentine and His Brother' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), two brothers find themselves entangled in a web of secrets and mysteries that threaten to tear their lives apart. As they navigate through a world filled with hidden motives and unexpected alliances, they must confront their own demons and make choices that will shape their futures. With a backdrop of intrigue and suspense, this captivating tale explores the complexities of family bonds and the power of love.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Family

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, Family dynamics, Love and sacrifice
