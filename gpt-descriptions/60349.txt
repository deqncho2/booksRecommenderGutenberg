Synopsis: In "An Account of the Late Intended Insurrection among a Portion of the Blacks of this City," Charleston (S.C.) takes us on a gripping journey through a city on the brink of chaos. As tensions rise and the threat of rebellion looms, the author unveils a shocking tale of conspiracy and defiance that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. With vivid descriptions and a relentless pace, this account sheds light on a little-known chapter of history that will leave readers questioning the boundaries of power and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, True crime, Political thriller

Themes: Rebellion, Oppression, Power dynamics
