Synopsis: In "The Oyster" by Peer, a mysterious oyster with a hidden secret becomes the center of attention in a small coastal town. As the townspeople become increasingly obsessed with the oyster, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to a series of shocking revelations. This gripping tale explores the depths of human desire, the consequences of obsession, and the power of secrets waiting to be uncovered.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Obsession, Secrets, Human Nature
