Synopsis: In 'Men Without a World' by Joseph Farrell, a group of scientists embarks on a groundbreaking mission to explore a distant planet. However, their journey takes an unexpected turn when they encounter a mysterious phenomenon that challenges their understanding of reality. As they struggle to navigate this strange new world, the scientists must confront their own fears and desires, ultimately questioning the very nature of existence itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Reality vs. Illusion
