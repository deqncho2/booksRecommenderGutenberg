Synopsis: In 'The Heart of Una Sackville', the captivating and enigmatic Una Sackville finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires. Set in the early 20th century, this compelling novel follows Una's journey as she navigates the complexities of love, passion, and self-discovery. As secrets unravel and relationships are tested, Una must confront the choices that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Identity, Love and Desire, Society and Conformity
