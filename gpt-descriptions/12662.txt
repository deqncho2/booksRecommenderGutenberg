Synopsis: Four Girls at Chautauqua follows the captivating journey of four young women who find themselves at the renowned Chautauqua Institution. As they navigate the stimulating lectures, engaging discussions, and unexpected encounters, their lives intertwine in ways they never could have imagined. Each girl faces personal challenges and discovers the power of friendship, love, and self-discovery amidst the backdrop of this vibrant intellectual community. With secrets to uncover and dreams to pursue, their summer at Chautauqua becomes a transformative experience that will shape their futures forever.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Intellectual growth, Love, Secrets
