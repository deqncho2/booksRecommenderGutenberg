Synopsis: In the second volume of "Life of Mozart," Otto Jahn delves deeper into the extraordinary life of the musical genius, Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart. This captivating biography takes readers on a journey through Mozart's travels, his relationships with fellow composers and musicians, and the challenges he faced in his personal and professional life. Jahn's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life the vibrant world of 18th-century Europe, where Mozart's talent and ambition flourished. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be enthralled by the triumphs and tribulations that shaped Mozart's career, ultimately leading to his enduring legacy as one of the greatest composers of all time.

Genres: Biography, Music History, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Genius and Creativity, Artistic Pursuit, Personal Struggles, Musical Legacy
