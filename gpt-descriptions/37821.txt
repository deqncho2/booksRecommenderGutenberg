Synopsis: In 'The Woman Who Vowed (The Demetrian)' by Harding Ellison, a mysterious woman with a troubled past finds herself entangled in a dangerous game of power and secrets. As she navigates a treacherous world filled with deceit and betrayal, she must uncover the truth behind her own identity and the dark forces that threaten her existence. With unexpected allies and formidable enemies, she embarks on a thrilling journey that will test her resolve and challenge everything she thought she knew.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Fantasy

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Power
