Synopsis: In 'Film Truth; November, 1920' by Anonymous, a young filmmaker stumbles upon a mysterious film reel that holds a dark secret. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic world of the film, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where reality and fiction blur. With each frame, he uncovers a truth that challenges his perception of the world and forces him to confront his own demons. As the line between the film and his own life begins to fade, he must race against time to unravel the truth before it consumes him entirely.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Identity, Obsession
