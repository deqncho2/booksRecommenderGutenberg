Synopsis:
In "Josephine: Makers of History," John S. C. Abbott takes us on a captivating journey through the life of one of history's most intriguing women. From her humble beginnings in Martinique to her rise as the Empress of France, Josephine's story is one of resilience, ambition, and love. As we delve into her world, we witness her navigate the treacherous political landscape of the French Revolution, her passionate romances, and her unwavering determination to secure her place in history. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, Abbott brings Josephine's captivating tale to life, leaving readers enthralled by her indomitable spirit and the mark she left on the world.

Genres: Biography, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Love, Resilience
