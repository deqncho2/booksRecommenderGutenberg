Synopsis: In 'Timeline: A Terran Empire timeline' by Wilson, Ann, a group of scientists stumble upon a groundbreaking discovery that allows them to travel through time. As they navigate different eras, they encounter unexpected challenges and must confront the consequences of altering history. With each jump, they uncover secrets that could reshape the future of the Terran Empire. This thrilling adventure explores the complexities of time travel and the moral dilemmas that arise when tampering with the past.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Adventure

Themes: Time manipulation, Consequences of actions, Ethical dilemmas
