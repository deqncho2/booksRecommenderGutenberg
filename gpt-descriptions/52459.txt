Synopsis: In "Saint Abe and His Seven Wives: A Tale of Salt Lake City," Buchanan takes us on a captivating journey through the intriguing world of polygamy in 19th-century Utah. The story revolves around the enigmatic figure of Saint Abe, a charismatic leader with seven wives, each with her own unique story and struggles. As we delve into the lives of these women, we witness the complexities of their relationships, the challenges they face, and the sacrifices they make in the name of faith and love. Set against the backdrop of Salt Lake City, this tale is filled with secrets, forbidden desires, and the eternal quest for personal freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Polygamy, Religion, Women's Rights, Love and Sacrifice
