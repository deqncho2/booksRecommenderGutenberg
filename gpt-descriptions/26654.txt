Synopsis:
In the enchanting world of Neverland, a mischievous boy named Peter Pan leads a group of lost boys, fairies, and pirates on thrilling adventures. When Peter visits the Darling family in London, he takes Wendy, John, and Michael on a magical journey to his fantastical realm, where they encounter mermaids, Indians, and the notorious Captain Hook. As they navigate through danger and wonder, the children discover the true meaning of friendship, bravery, and the power of imagination.

Genres:
Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes:
Imagination, Friendship, Growing up, Identity
