Synopsis: In 'The Flying Machine Boys on Secret Service; Or, The Capture in the Air' by Frank Walton, a group of adventurous young aviators find themselves embroiled in a thrilling mission that takes them high above the clouds. As they navigate treacherous skies and encounter unexpected dangers, the boys must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and the power of their flying machines to uncover a hidden secret. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise, and the boys must race against time to complete their mission and save the day. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
