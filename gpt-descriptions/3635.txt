Synopsis: In 'Mother: A Story' by Kathleen Thompson Norris, a young woman named Margaret finds herself torn between her duty as a daughter and her desire for independence. As she navigates the complexities of love, marriage, and motherhood, Margaret must confront societal expectations and her own personal ambitions. Set against the backdrop of early 20th century America, this captivating novel explores the sacrifices and challenges faced by women in their pursuit of happiness and fulfillment.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Women's Literature

Themes: Gender roles, Independence, Sacrifice, Love, Motherhood
