Synopsis: 
"The Woman Who Toils: Being the Experiences of Two Gentlewomen as Factory Girls" follows the captivating journey of two upper-class women who voluntarily immerse themselves in the harsh reality of factory work. As they navigate the grueling conditions and face the challenges of their new lives, they gain a profound understanding of the struggles faced by the working class. Through their experiences, they shed light on the social injustices and inequalities prevalent in industrial society, ultimately questioning the stark divide between the privileged and the working class.

Genres: 
Social commentary, historical fiction, women's literature

Themes: 
Social inequality, class divide, women's rights, empathy, societal change
