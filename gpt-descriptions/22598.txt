Synopsis: 
"The Story of the First Trans-Continental Railroad: Its Projectors, Construction and History" by William Francis Bailey takes readers on a captivating journey through the remarkable tale of the construction of the first trans-continental railroad in the United States. From the ambitious visionaries who conceived the project to the challenges faced during its construction, this book delves into the fascinating details of this monumental feat of engineering. With vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, Bailey brings to life the struggles, triumphs, and sacrifices of the individuals who made this groundbreaking achievement possible.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Engineering

Themes: Ambition, Innovation, Perseverance, Progress
