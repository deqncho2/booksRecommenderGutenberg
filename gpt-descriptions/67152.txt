Synopsis: 
'Ballads and Other Poems: Fourth Edition, Revised' by George Lansing Raymond is a captivating collection of poems that takes readers on a journey through various emotions and experiences. From tales of love and loss to reflections on nature and the human condition, Raymond's words paint vivid pictures and evoke powerful emotions. This revised edition showcases the author's mastery of language and his ability to capture the essence of life in its many forms. Whether exploring the depths of despair or celebrating the joys of existence, these poems will leave readers moved and inspired.

Genres: Poetry, Literature, Classic

Themes: Love, Nature, Human experience, Emotions
