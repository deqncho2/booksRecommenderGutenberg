Synopsis: 

'From Dartmouth to the Dardanelles: A Midshipman's Log' follows the thrilling journey of a young midshipman as he embarks on a naval adventure during World War I. From the prestigious Dartmouth Naval College to the treacherous waters of the Dardanelles, the protagonist faces numerous challenges and tests his mettle in the face of danger. As he navigates through the perils of war, the midshipman must confront his own fears, forge new friendships, and make difficult decisions that will shape his future. This gripping tale offers a vivid portrayal of life at sea and the sacrifices made by those who serve their country.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Coming of Age, Courage, Camaraderie, Sacrifice
