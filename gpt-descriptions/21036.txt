Synopsis: In 'My Friend Smith: A Story of School and City Life', the protagonist, a young boy named Tom, forms an unlikely friendship with a mysterious and enigmatic classmate named Smith. As their bond grows, Tom is drawn into a world of adventure, danger, and secrets that will test their friendship to its limits. Set against the backdrop of both the bustling city and the confines of school life, this captivating tale explores the complexities of friendship, loyalty, and the power of trust.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Trust, Loyalty
