Synopsis: In 'The Headswoman' by Kenneth Grahame, a young woman named Helena is unexpectedly thrust into a position of power as the executioner for a secret society. As she carries out her grim duties, Helena grapples with the moral implications of her role and the consequences it has on her own life. Set against the backdrop of a mysterious and clandestine organization, this gripping tale explores the complexities of duty, justice, and the choices we make in the face of power.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Morality, Power, Identity
