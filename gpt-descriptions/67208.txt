Synopsis: In 'The Light Machine' by Cummings, Ray, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Amelia Collins invents a groundbreaking device that has the power to change the world as we know it. However, as she delves deeper into the mysteries of her creation, she uncovers a dark secret that threatens not only her own life but the fate of humanity. With time running out, Dr. Collins must navigate a treacherous web of deception and betrayal to protect her invention and those she holds dear.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics of scientific advancement, the consequences of playing god, the power of knowledge and discovery
