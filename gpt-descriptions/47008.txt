Synopsis: In 'With the Allies to Pekin: A Tale of the Relief of the Legations', George Alfred Henty takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of China during the Boxer Rebellion. The story follows a young British officer, Roger, who finds himself caught up in the chaos and danger of the siege of the foreign legations in Pekin. With the help of his loyal Chinese friend, Roger embarks on a daring mission to bring relief to the trapped diplomats and their families. Along the way, they face treacherous enemies, navigate through hostile territory, and encounter unexpected allies. Will they succeed in their mission and save the legations from certain doom?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Friendship, Cultural Clash
