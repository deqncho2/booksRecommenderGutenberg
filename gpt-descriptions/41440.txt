Synopsis: 
'Poppea of the Post-Office' by Mabel Osgood Wright is a delightful and heartwarming novel that follows the life of Poppea, a young woman who works at the post-office in a small New England town. Poppea's days are filled with the mundane tasks of sorting mail and dealing with the eccentricities of the townspeople. However, her life takes an unexpected turn when she receives a mysterious letter that leads her on a journey of self-discovery, love, and unexpected adventures. As Poppea navigates through the challenges and surprises that come her way, she learns valuable lessons about friendship, resilience, and the power of following one's dreams.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Love, Friendship, Resilience, Pursuing dreams
