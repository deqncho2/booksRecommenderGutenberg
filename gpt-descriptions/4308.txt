Synopsis: In 'The Town Traveller' by George Gissing, we follow the life of Egremont, a young man who finds himself torn between the allure of the city and the simplicity of the countryside. As he navigates the bustling streets of London and the tranquil landscapes of rural England, Egremont encounters a cast of intriguing characters who challenge his beliefs and shape his destiny. With vivid descriptions and a captivating narrative, Gissing explores the complexities of human nature and the eternal struggle between ambition and contentment.

Genres: Fiction, Victorian Literature, Social Realism

Themes: Urban vs Rural, Ambition, Identity, Social Class
