Synopsis: In 'The Heiress of Wyvern Court' by Emilie Searchfield, a young woman named Amelia finds herself thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when she inherits her family's ancestral home, Wyvern Court. As she delves into the secrets of her family's past, Amelia uncovers a web of lies, betrayal, and forbidden love that spans generations. With the help of a charming and enigmatic stranger, she must navigate the treacherous waters of high society and uncover the truth before it destroys everything she holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Forbidden love, Identity
