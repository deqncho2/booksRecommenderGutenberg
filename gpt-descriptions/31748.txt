Synopsis: 
In 'Pemrose Lorry, Camp Fire Girl' by Isabel Hornibrook, readers are introduced to the spirited and adventurous Pemrose Lorry. When Pemrose's family moves to a new town, she finds solace and excitement in joining the Camp Fire Girls. As she navigates the challenges of camp life, Pemrose discovers the power of friendship, the importance of teamwork, and the joy of embracing the great outdoors. Along the way, she encounters unexpected obstacles and learns valuable life lessons that shape her into a resilient and compassionate young woman.

Genres: 
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Young Adult

Themes: 
Friendship, Resilience, Nature, Personal Growth
