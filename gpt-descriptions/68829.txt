Synopsis: In 'Mistake inside' by James Blish, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. However, as he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden truth that threatens to unravel everything he thought he knew. With time running out, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and betrayal to protect his discovery and save the world from the consequences of his own creation.

Genres: Science fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Consequences of scientific advancement, Betrayal
