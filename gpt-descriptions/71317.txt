Synopsis: In "The Day's Journey" by Netta Syrett, a young woman named Mary embarks on a transformative journey that takes her through the bustling streets of London. As she navigates the city's diverse neighborhoods, encounters various characters, and experiences unexpected events, Mary's perceptions of herself and the world around her are challenged. This captivating novel explores the complexities of identity, the power of self-discovery, and the profound impact of one day's journey on a person's life.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Urban Literature

Themes: Identity, Self-discovery, Transformation, Urban life
