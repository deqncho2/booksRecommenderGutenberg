Synopsis: In 'A Terrible Tomboy' by Angela Brazil, follow the spirited and unconventional adventures of a young girl named Marjorie as she navigates the challenges of school life and defies societal expectations. From her mischievous pranks to her determination to prove herself, Marjorie's journey is filled with laughter, friendship, and unexpected twists. As she challenges the norms of her time, readers will be captivated by her daring spirit and the lessons she learns along the way.

Genres: Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Gender Roles, Individuality, Friendship, Self-Discovery
