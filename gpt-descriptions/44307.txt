Synopsis: In 'A. D. 2000' by Fuller, Alvarado M., a mysterious artifact is discovered in the ruins of an ancient civilization, leading a team of archaeologists on a thrilling journey through time and space. As they unravel the secrets of this enigmatic object, they are faced with unexpected dangers and mind-bending revelations that challenge their understanding of history and the nature of humanity. This gripping tale of adventure and discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind the artifact and its implications for the future.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Time travel, Ancient civilizations, Human evolution
