Synopsis: In "Richard Vandermarck: A Novel" by Miriam Coles Harris, readers are transported to the bustling streets of New York City in the late 19th century. The story revolves around the enigmatic Richard Vandermarck, a wealthy and influential man with a mysterious past. As the narrative unfolds, secrets are unveiled, scandals are exposed, and the true nature of Vandermarck's character is revealed. With a cast of intriguing characters and a plot filled with twists and turns, this captivating novel explores the complexities of power, ambition, and the consequences of one's actions.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Wealth and Power, Identity and Self-discovery, Morality and Consequences
