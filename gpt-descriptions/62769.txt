Synopsis: In "The Radio Boys with the Forest Rangers; Or, The great fire on Spruce Mountain" by Chapman, Allen, a group of adventurous boys find themselves caught in the midst of a raging forest fire. Armed with their trusty radios, they join forces with the brave forest rangers to battle the flames and save lives. As they face danger and uncertainty, the boys must rely on their resourcefulness and teamwork to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Survival
