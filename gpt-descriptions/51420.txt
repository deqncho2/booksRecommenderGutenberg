Synopsis: In 'License to Steal' by Louis Newman, a brilliant and audacious thief named Max is recruited by a secret organization to carry out a series of high-stakes heists. As Max delves deeper into the world of organized crime, he must navigate a treacherous web of deceit, betrayal, and danger. With each daring theft, Max's skills are put to the ultimate test, and he soon realizes that the line between right and wrong is not as clear as he once thought. As the stakes rise and the danger intensifies, Max must decide where his loyalties truly lie and whether he can escape the clutches of a world that thrives on chaos and deception.

Genres: Thriller, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Morality, Loyalty, Deception
