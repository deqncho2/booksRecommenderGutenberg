Synopsis: 
'Our Profession and Other Poems' by Jared Barhite is a captivating collection of poetry that delves into the complexities of human emotions and experiences. Through vivid imagery and thought-provoking verses, the poems explore themes of love, loss, self-discovery, and the pursuit of one's true passion. From the depths of despair to the heights of joy, the poet takes readers on a journey through the intricacies of life, leaving them with a renewed appreciation for the power of words and the beauty of the human spirit.

Genres: Poetry, Contemporary Literature

Themes: Love, Loss, Self-discovery, Passion, Human emotions
