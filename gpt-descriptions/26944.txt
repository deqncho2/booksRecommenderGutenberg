Synopsis: In 'The Magic Soap Bubble' by Cory, David, a young boy named Max discovers a mysterious soap bubble that grants him extraordinary powers. As Max embarks on a thrilling adventure, he must navigate the challenges of using his newfound abilities responsibly and face the consequences of his choices. With unexpected twists and turns, this enchanting tale will captivate readers and leave them eagerly turning the pages to uncover the secrets hidden within the magical bubble.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Power and responsibility, Self-discovery, Consequences of actions
