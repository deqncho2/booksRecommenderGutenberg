Synopsis: In 'Lanier of the Cavalry; or, A Week's Arrest' by Charles King, readers are transported to the thrilling world of the cavalry, where Lieutenant Lanier finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. When he is unexpectedly arrested and confined to his quarters for a week, Lanier must uncover the truth behind the accusations against him. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he discovers shocking secrets that could not only clear his name but also expose a sinister plot that threatens the entire cavalry. With time running out, Lanier must navigate treacherous alliances and face unexpected adversaries to unravel the truth and restore his honor.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Honor, Betrayal, Loyalty, Justice
