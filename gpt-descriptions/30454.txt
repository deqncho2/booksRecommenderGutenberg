Synopsis: 

In "Blind Spot" by Jones, Bascom, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a series of seemingly unrelated events begin to converge. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and hidden motives that challenge his own perceptions and force him to confront his own blind spots. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate through a labyrinth of deception to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Perception, Deception, Identity
