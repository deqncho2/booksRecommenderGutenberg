Synopsis:
'The Escaping Club' by A. J. Evans is a gripping memoir that takes readers on a thrilling journey through the daring escapes of British prisoners of war during World War I. From the heart-pounding moments of planning to the adrenaline-fueled execution, this true account unveils the incredible ingenuity and resilience of these brave men as they defy the odds and outwit their captors. With each escape attempt, the stakes rise higher, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover if freedom will finally be achieved. 

Genres:
Memoir, History, War

Themes:
Courage, Resilience, Ingenuity, Survival
