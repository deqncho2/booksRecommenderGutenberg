Synopsis: 

Join the thrilling adventure of a cruise in the Schooner "Terrapin" as it sails through the captivating Andaman and Nicobar Islands. In this enthralling narrative, C. Boden Kloss takes readers on a journey filled with mysterious encounters, breathtaking landscapes, and unexpected discoveries. From encounters with indigenous tribes to encounters with dangerous wildlife, the crew of the "Terrapin" faces numerous challenges that test their courage and resilience. As they navigate through the uncharted waters of the Andamans and Nicobars, the crew unravels the secrets of these remote islands, uncovering hidden treasures and encountering ancient civilizations. 

Genres: Adventure, Travelogue, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Cultural Encounter
