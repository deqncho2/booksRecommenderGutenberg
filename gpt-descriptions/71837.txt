Synopsis: 
Step into the captivating world of European zoological gardens as Charles Victor Alexander Peel takes you on an enthralling journey through their rich history and remarkable features. From the majestic menageries of ancient civilizations to the modern-day marvels of conservation, this book unveils the secrets and wonders of these extraordinary institutions. Discover the fascinating stories behind the creation of iconic zoos, encounter the exotic creatures that have graced their enclosures, and delve into the tireless efforts of dedicated individuals who have shaped the course of zoological gardens. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Peel paints a vivid picture of the evolution of these remarkable spaces, leaving readers eager to explore the hidden treasures of Europe's zoological gardens.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Travel

Themes: Zoology, Conservation, Cultural Heritage
