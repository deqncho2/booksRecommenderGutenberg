Synopsis: 
In 'Yorkshire' by Home, Gordon, a young woman named Emily finds herself drawn to the enchanting landscapes and rich history of the Yorkshire Dales. As she delves deeper into the region's secrets, she uncovers a hidden world of ancient legends, mysterious disappearances, and a love story that transcends time. With each step she takes, Emily becomes entangled in a web of intrigue that challenges her perceptions of reality and forces her to confront her own past. As she unravels the truth, she must make a choice that will forever change her life and the lives of those around her.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Love, History, Mythology
