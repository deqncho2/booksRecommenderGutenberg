Synopsis: In 'A Short Treatise on Head Wear, Ancient and Modern', Anne C. Goater takes readers on a fascinating journey through the history of headwear, exploring the diverse styles and cultural significance of hats throughout the ages. From the extravagant headdresses of ancient civilizations to the iconic hats worn by historical figures, Goater delves into the intriguing stories behind these fashion statements. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, this book offers a unique perspective on the evolution of headwear and its enduring impact on society.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Fashion

Themes: Cultural significance, Fashion evolution
