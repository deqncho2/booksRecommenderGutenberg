Synopsis:
"A Voyage Round the World: Being an account of a remarkable enterprize, begun in the year 1719, chiefly to cruise on the Spaniards in the great South ocean" is a captivating historical narrative that takes readers on an extraordinary journey. Written by William Betagh, this book recounts a daring expedition that sets sail in pursuit of the Spanish fleet in the vast South Pacific. The story unfolds through the eyes of those involved, providing a firsthand account of the challenges, triumphs, and unexpected encounters they face during their voyage. With testimonies from numerous participants and supported by evidence from the owners, this gripping tale offers a true and authentic depiction of the entire affair.

Genres:
Historical Non-fiction, Adventure, Maritime

Themes:
Exploration, Naval Warfare, Colonialism, Survival
