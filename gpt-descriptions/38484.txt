Synopsis: In the second volume of "Abraham Lincoln: The True Story of a Great Life," authors Herndon and Weik delve deeper into the remarkable life of one of America's most iconic presidents. This captivating biography uncovers lesser-known aspects of Lincoln's personal and political journey, shedding light on his early years, his rise to power, and the challenges he faced during his presidency. Through meticulous research and vivid storytelling, the authors paint a vivid portrait of Lincoln's character, his unwavering determination, and his enduring legacy. From his humble beginnings to his pivotal role in shaping the nation's history, this volume offers a comprehensive and intimate look into the life of a truly extraordinary leader.

Genres: Biography, History

Themes: Leadership, Perseverance, Legacy
