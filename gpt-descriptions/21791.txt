Synopsis: 
In 'The Aeroplane Speaks. Fifth Edition', author H. Barber takes readers on a thrilling journey through the world of aviation. This comprehensive guide explores the inner workings of airplanes, from their design and construction to their navigation and operation. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Barber delves into the fascinating history of flight, highlighting the pioneers who pushed the boundaries of what was once thought impossible. From the exhilarating experience of takeoff to the awe-inspiring views from above, this book offers a unique perspective on the wonders of aviation.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Technology

Themes: Aviation, Exploration, Innovation
