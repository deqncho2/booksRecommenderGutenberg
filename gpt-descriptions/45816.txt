Synopsis: In 'The House on the Moor, v. 1/3' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman named Lucy embarks on a journey to a mysterious house on the moor, where she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden pasts. As she delves deeper into the house's history, Lucy discovers that the inhabitants are not what they seem, and that the house holds dark and haunting secrets that threaten to consume her. With each twist and turn, Lucy must navigate the treacherous landscape of the house on the moor, unsure of who to trust and what lies beneath the surface.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Secrets, Identity, Betrayal, Supernatural, Isolation
