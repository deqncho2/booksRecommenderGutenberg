Synopsis: In the small town of Primrose, a mysterious woman named Miss Primrose arrives, captivating the townspeople with her enigmatic charm and unconventional ways. As she becomes entangled in the lives of the locals, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the town's traditional values are challenged. With her unique perspective, Miss Primrose brings a breath of fresh air to Primrose, but will her presence ultimately bring about transformation or chaos?

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Individuality, Tradition, Community
