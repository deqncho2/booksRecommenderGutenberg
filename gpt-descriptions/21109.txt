Synopsis: In 'Big Game: A Story for Girls' by Mrs. George de Horne Vaizey, a group of adventurous young girls embark on a thrilling journey that takes them deep into the heart of the African wilderness. As they navigate through dangerous terrain and encounter wild animals, the girls must rely on their courage, resourcefulness, and friendship to overcome the challenges they face. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies ahead for these brave girls.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Fiction

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration, Survival
