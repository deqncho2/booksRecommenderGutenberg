Synopsis: In 'True Blue' by William Henry Giles Kingston, a young sailor named Jack is thrust into a thrilling adventure when he is falsely accused of a crime he did not commit. Determined to clear his name, Jack embarks on a perilous journey across the high seas, encountering treacherous pirates, unexpected allies, and uncovering a hidden secret that could change his life forever. As he fights for justice and redemption, Jack's courage and resilience are put to the ultimate test in this gripping tale of honor, loyalty, and the power of the human spirit.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Justice, Redemption, Friendship, Perseverance
