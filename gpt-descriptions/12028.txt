Synopsis: In 'The Uttermost Farthing: A Savant's Vendetta', a brilliant and enigmatic savant named Paul Savaron embarks on a relentless quest for justice and revenge. As he delves into a web of deceit, corruption, and murder, Savaron's genius mind becomes both his greatest asset and his most dangerous weapon. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Justice, Revenge, Deception, Genius
