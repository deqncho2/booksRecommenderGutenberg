Synopsis: In 'The Airship Boys' Ocean Flyer; Or, New York to London in Twelve Hours', follow the thrilling adventure of three young friends who embark on a daring journey across the Atlantic Ocean in a revolutionary airship. As they face treacherous weather, unexpected challenges, and encounters with dangerous adversaries, their determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will they succeed in their mission to make history and complete the incredible feat of traveling from New York to London in just twelve hours?

Genres: Adventure, Young Adult, Historical Fiction

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Innovation, Exploration
