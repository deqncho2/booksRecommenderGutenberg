Synopsis: In 'Follow My Leader: The Boys of Templeton' by Reed, Talbot Baines, a young boy named Jimmy is tragically blinded in an accident. As he struggles to adapt to his new life, he discovers a newfound sense of independence and courage through an unexpected friendship with a guide dog named Leader. Together, they embark on a remarkable journey of self-discovery, facing challenges and triumphs that will inspire readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Overcoming adversity, Friendship, Independence, Resilience
