Synopsis: In 'The Frogs of Mars' by Aycock, Roger D., a team of scientists embarks on a groundbreaking mission to Mars, only to discover an unexpected and perplexing phenomenon: frogs. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they uncover secrets that challenge their understanding of life and the universe, leading to a thrilling and thought-provoking adventure that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Extraterrestrial Life, Scientific Discovery
