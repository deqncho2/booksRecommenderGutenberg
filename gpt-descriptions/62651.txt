Synopsis: 

In 'Morristown National Historical Park, a Military Capital of the American Revolution', author Melvin J. Weig takes readers on a captivating journey through one of the most significant locations in American history. This meticulously researched book delves into the events that unfolded in Morristown, New Jersey during the American Revolution, revealing the pivotal role it played as a military capital. From the harsh winter encampments to the strategic decisions made by General George Washington, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the challenges faced by the Continental Army and the resilience of those who fought for independence.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Military

Themes: American Revolution, Military Strategy, Historical Preservation
