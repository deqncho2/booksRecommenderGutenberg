Synopsis: In 'The Crime of the Boulevard' by Jules Claretie, a shocking crime takes place on a bustling boulevard in Paris, sending ripples of fear and intrigue through the city. As the investigation unfolds, the reader is drawn into a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected twists, where nothing is as it seems. With each revelation, the tension mounts, and the reader is left guessing until the very end. 

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice
