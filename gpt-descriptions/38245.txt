Synopsis: 
'Atlantic Classics, Second Series' is a captivating collection of short stories that will transport readers to different worlds, each filled with its own unique set of characters and circumstances. From the depths of the ocean to the far reaches of outer space, these tales explore the depths of human emotions, the complexities of relationships, and the power of the human spirit. With unexpected twists and thought-provoking narratives, this anthology will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what lies beyond the next story.

Genres: Fiction, Science Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Human emotions, Relationships, Exploration, Resilience
