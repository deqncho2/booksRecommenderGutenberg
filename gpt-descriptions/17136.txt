Synopsis: 

"Selected Official Documents of the South African Republic and Great Britain: A Documentary Perspective Of The Causes Of The War In South Africa" offers a captivating exploration of the events leading up to the war in South Africa through a collection of official documents. This thought-provoking work delves into the political and diplomatic exchanges between the South African Republic and Great Britain, shedding light on the complex circumstances that ultimately ignited the conflict. As readers delve into these historical documents, they will uncover the intricate web of motivations, tensions, and power dynamics that shaped this pivotal moment in history.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, political analysis, diplomatic history

Themes: Colonialism, nationalism, power struggles, diplomacy, war
