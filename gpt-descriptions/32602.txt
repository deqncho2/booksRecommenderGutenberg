Synopsis: In "What Every Singer Should Know," Millie Ryan takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of singing, filled with unexpected twists and turns. As aspiring singer Lily embarks on a quest to discover her true voice, she encounters a mysterious mentor who challenges her beliefs and pushes her to new heights. Along the way, Lily must confront her fears, navigate the complexities of the music industry, and learn valuable lessons about the power of self-expression. This gripping tale will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to uncover the secrets that lie within Lily's journey.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Self-discovery, Pursuit of passion, Overcoming obstacles
