Synopsis: In the enchanting setting of Sicily, 'A Sicilian Romance' follows the story of two young sisters, Julia and Emilia, who find themselves entangled in a web of mystery, secrets, and forbidden love. As they navigate through the dark corridors of a crumbling castle, they uncover a haunting past that threatens to consume them. With its eerie atmosphere and suspenseful plot, this gothic novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, as they unravel the secrets hidden within the ancient walls of the castle.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Forbidden love, Secrets and deception, Supernatural elements, Female agency
