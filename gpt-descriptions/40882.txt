Synopsis: In the small town of Treby Magna, a young and enigmatic radical named Felix Holt arrives, challenging the established social order and stirring up the passions of the townspeople. As he becomes entangled in a web of love, politics, and personal struggles, Felix's presence sets off a chain of events that will forever change the lives of those around him. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, "Felix Holt, the Radical" is a captivating tale of love, justice, and the power of individual conviction.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Fiction, Romance

Themes: Social justice, Political reform, Love and relationships, Class struggle
