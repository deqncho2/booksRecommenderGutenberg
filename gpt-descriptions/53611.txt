Synopsis:
In 'Goslings' by J. D. Beresford, a seemingly idyllic English village is disrupted by the arrival of a mysterious family with unconventional beliefs and practices. As the villagers become increasingly intrigued by the newcomers, tensions rise and secrets are unveiled, leading to unexpected consequences that will forever change the lives of everyone involved.

Genres:
Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes:
Social conformity, Outsiders, Family dynamics, Secrets and lies
