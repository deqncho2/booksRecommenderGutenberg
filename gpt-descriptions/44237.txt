Synopsis: In "A Family of Noblemen" by Mikhail Evgrafovich Saltykov, a seemingly idyllic noble family is depicted through the eyes of a young boy named Alexey. As he grows up, Alexey begins to uncover the dark secrets and hypocrisy that lie beneath the surface of his family's aristocratic facade. Through a series of satirical and often humorous events, Saltykov explores the complexities of social hierarchy, the decay of the Russian nobility, and the struggle for individual identity in a rigid society.

Genres: Satire, Fiction, Historical

Themes: Social hierarchy, Hypocrisy, Decay of the nobility, Individual identity
