Synopsis: In 'Frank of Freedom Hill' by Samuel A. Derieux, a mysterious stranger named Frank arrives in the small town of Freedom Hill, bringing with him a dark secret from his past. As the townspeople become captivated by Frank's enigmatic charm, they soon discover that his presence will challenge their notions of trust, loyalty, and the true meaning of freedom. With each passing day, the tension in Freedom Hill builds, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of justice and the lengths one will go to protect their own. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Trust, Loyalty, Justice, Freedom
