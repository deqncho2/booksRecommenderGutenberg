Synopsis: In 'Uarda: a Romance of Ancient Egypt - Volume 02', the captivating story continues as the young Egyptian girl, Uarda, finds herself caught between her love for the noble prince Rameses and her loyalty to her own people. As political tensions rise and secrets are unveiled, Uarda's fate becomes intertwined with the destiny of Egypt itself. With vivid descriptions of ancient Egyptian life and a gripping narrative, this volume takes readers on a thrilling journey through love, betrayal, and the struggle for power in the ancient world.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty, Power and Politics
