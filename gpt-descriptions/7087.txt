Synopsis:
In the remote wilderness of Umbagog, a young trapper named Gaut Gurley finds himself entangled in a thrilling adventure that will test his courage and survival skills. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters dangerous foes, Gaut must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to overcome the perils that lie ahead. Along the way, he forms unexpected alliances and discovers secrets that will forever change his perception of the world around him. 'Gaut Gurley; Or, the Trappers of Umbagog: A Tale of Border Life' is a captivating tale of adventure and self-discovery that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Wilderness Fiction

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Coming of Age
