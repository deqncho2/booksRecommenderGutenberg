Synopsis: In "Buffalo Bill's Bold Play; Or, The Tiger of the Hills" by Ingraham, Prentiss, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure through the untamed American West. The story follows the legendary Buffalo Bill as he embarks on a daring mission to confront a notorious outlaw known as the Tiger of the Hills. As the two formidable forces clash, a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances unfolds, keeping readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Action

Themes: Heroism, Justice, Redemption
