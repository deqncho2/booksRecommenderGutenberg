Synopsis: In 'Dragons and Cherry Blossoms' by Alice A. Parmelee Morris, a young woman named Sakura finds herself caught in a world where ancient legends and modern reality collide. When she discovers a hidden portal to a realm filled with mythical creatures, Sakura embarks on a thrilling adventure to uncover the truth about her own heritage and the secrets that lie within her family. As she navigates through a land of dragons, cherry blossoms, and enchanting landscapes, Sakura must confront her fears, embrace her destiny, and learn the power of love and friendship.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Identity, Heritage, Mythology, Coming of Age
