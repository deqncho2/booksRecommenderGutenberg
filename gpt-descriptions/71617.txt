Synopsis: 
In 'Bonnie May' by Louis Dodge, a young woman named Bonnie May finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret in her small town. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Bonnie May must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to protect her loved ones and uncover the truth. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and Bonnie May must summon her courage and resourcefulness to survive the perilous journey that lies ahead.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Courage and resilience
