Synopsis: In 'Rhymes of a Roughneck' by Frank J. Cotter, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the life of a roughneck, exploring the highs and lows of his experiences in the oil fields. Through vivid and powerful poetry, Cotter delves into the gritty realities of this dangerous profession, painting a vivid picture of the roughneck's world. From the thrill of striking oil to the heart-wrenching tragedies that occur, this collection of poems offers a raw and unfiltered glimpse into a life filled with both triumph and hardship.

Genres: Poetry, Memoir, Realism

Themes: Hard work and perseverance, the beauty and danger of the oil industry, the human spirit in challenging circumstances
