Synopsis: 
In 'Betty Lee, Senior' by Grove, Harriet Pyne, we are introduced to Betty Lee, a spirited and independent senior who finds herself embarking on a journey of self-discovery and unexpected adventures. When Betty's quiet life takes an unexpected turn, she is forced to confront her past and confront the challenges that come with aging. As she navigates through new friendships, unexpected romance, and the complexities of family dynamics, Betty learns valuable lessons about resilience, love, and the power of embracing change. 

Genres: Fiction, Contemporary, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Aging, Self-discovery, Family dynamics, Resilience
