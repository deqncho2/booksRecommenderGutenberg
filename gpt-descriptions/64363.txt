Synopsis: In 'The Little Pets of Arkkhan' by Bill Garson, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden world of magical creatures living in her own backyard. As she befriends these enchanting creatures, she becomes entangled in a thrilling adventure to save their home from a dark and mysterious threat. With the help of her newfound friends, Lily must navigate through danger and uncover the secrets of Arkkhan before it's too late. This captivating tale will transport readers to a world filled with wonder, friendship, and the power of imagination.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Imagination, Conservation
