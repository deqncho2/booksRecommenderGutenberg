Synopsis: 
In 'Held for Orders: Being Stories of Railroad Life', Frank H. Spearman takes readers on a thrilling journey through the world of railroad workers. From the bustling train yards to the treacherous tracks, this collection of stories delves into the lives of those who dedicate themselves to the iron horse. Through the eyes of various characters, readers will witness the dangers, triumphs, and sacrifices that come with working on the railroad. From high-speed chases to unexpected encounters, each story offers a unique glimpse into the captivating and often unpredictable world of railroad life.

Genres: 
Adventure, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Perseverance, Camaraderie, Danger
