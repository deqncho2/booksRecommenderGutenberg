Synopsis: 
'Parlour Magic' is a captivating mystery novel that follows the story of a renowned magician, Victor Sinclair, who finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and illusions when a mysterious death occurs during one of his performances. As Victor delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a world of deception, hidden motives, and unexpected alliances, all while trying to protect his own reputation and uncover the truth behind the tragedy. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Illusion, Identity
