Synopsis: In a future society where individuality is suppressed and conformity is enforced, a mathematician named D-503 finds himself torn between his loyalty to the totalitarian state and his growing desire for freedom. As he becomes involved with a mysterious woman named I-330, D-503's perception of the world begins to unravel, leading him on a journey of self-discovery and rebellion against the oppressive regime.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian Fiction, Political Fiction

Themes: Totalitarianism, Individuality, Freedom, Conformity, Rebellion
