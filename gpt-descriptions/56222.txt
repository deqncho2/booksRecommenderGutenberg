Synopsis: In 'Bertha's Christmas Vision: An Autumn Sheaf' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Bertha, a young girl with a vivid imagination and a heart full of dreams. As the holiday season approaches, Bertha finds herself caught between the harsh realities of her family's financial struggles and the enchanting world she creates in her mind. Through a series of unexpected events, Bertha's Christmas takes an unexpected turn, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and the power of hope. This heartwarming tale explores the magic of the holiday season and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Holiday

Themes: Imagination, Family, Hope, Resilience
