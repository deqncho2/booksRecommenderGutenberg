Synopsis: In "A Week at Waterloo in 1815: Lady De Lancey's Narrative," Magdalene, Lady De Lancey, shares her gripping account of how she courageously cared for her husband, Colonel Sir William Howe De Lancey, after he was mortally wounded in the historic Battle of Waterloo. Set against the backdrop of one of the most significant battles in history, Lady De Lancey's narrative takes readers on an emotional journey as she navigates the chaos and devastation of war, offering a unique perspective on the human cost of conflict.

Genres: Historical fiction, Memoir, War

Themes: Love and sacrifice, Resilience in the face of adversity, The impact of war on individuals and families
