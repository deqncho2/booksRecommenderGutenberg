Synopsis: In 'The Nostalgia Gene' by Hutchins, Roy, a brilliant scientist discovers a gene that unlocks the ability to relive cherished memories from the past. As the world becomes obsessed with this newfound power, a young woman named Emma finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger, uncovering a dark secret that threatens to unravel the very fabric of society. With the clock ticking, Emma must navigate a treacherous path to protect the truth and preserve the essence of what it means to be human.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Memory, Identity, Ethics, Power
