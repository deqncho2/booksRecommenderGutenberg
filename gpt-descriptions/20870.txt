Synopsis: In 'The Motor Girls Through New England; or, Held by the Gypsies' by Margaret Penrose, the adventurous and resourceful Motor Girls embark on a thrilling journey through the picturesque landscapes of New England. When they encounter a group of mysterious gypsies, they find themselves caught up in a web of intrigue and danger. With their quick thinking and determination, the Motor Girls must navigate through unexpected twists and turns to uncover the truth and ensure their own safety.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
