Synopsis: In 'Hard Pressed' by Fred M. White, a renowned journalist finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a secret society with a sinister agenda. As he delves deeper into the dark underbelly of the city, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, risking everything to uncover the truth. With his life on the line, he must outsmart his adversaries and expose the shocking secrets hidden in plain sight.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Conspiracy, Journalism, Deception
