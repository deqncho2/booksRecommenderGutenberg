Synopsis: In "Kwasa the cliff dweller" by Katharine Atherton Grimes, a young woman named Kwasa finds herself torn between two worlds when she discovers a hidden community living on the edge of a cliff. As she delves deeper into their way of life, she uncovers secrets that challenge her perception of reality and force her to question her own identity. With danger lurking around every corner, Kwasa must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and find her place in this mysterious society.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Belonging, Secrets, Discovery
