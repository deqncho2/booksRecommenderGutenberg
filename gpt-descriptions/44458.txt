Synopsis: In 'Tools and materials illustrating the Japanese method of colour-printing', Edward Fairbrother Strange takes readers on a captivating journey into the intricate world of Japanese colour-printing. Through vivid descriptions and detailed illustrations, Strange unveils the secrets behind this ancient art form, exploring the tools and materials used by Japanese craftsmen to create stunning prints. From delicate brushes to vibrant pigments, readers will discover the meticulous techniques employed to produce these exquisite works of art. As Strange delves into the history and cultural significance of colour-printing in Japan, readers will be enthralled by the beauty and precision of this captivating craft.

Genres: Art, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Traditional craftsmanship, Japanese art and culture, Visual aesthetics
