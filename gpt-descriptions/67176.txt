Synopsis: In 'Legend Land, Vol. 4', George Basil Barham takes readers on a captivating journey through the enchanting tales of the western parts of Britain. From ancient legends to mystical creatures, this collection of stories serves as a gateway to a world filled with magic, adventure, and wonder. As readers delve into the pages, they will encounter heroes and heroines, mythical creatures, and the timeless battle between good and evil. Each tale is intricately woven, transporting readers to a realm where legends come to life and the imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Fantasy, Folklore, Adventure

Themes: Mythology, Heroism, Good vs. Evil, Magic, Adventure
