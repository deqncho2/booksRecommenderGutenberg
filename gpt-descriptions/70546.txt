Synopsis: In 'Parodies of the works of English & American authors, vol. IV', prepare to embark on a hilarious journey through the literary world as renowned authors become the subjects of clever and witty parodies. From the classics to contemporary works, this collection offers a delightful twist on beloved stories, characters, and writing styles. Get ready to laugh out loud as familiar tales are turned upside down and iconic authors are playfully mocked. Genres: Parody, Satire, Humor. Themes: Literary criticism, Subversion of expectations.
