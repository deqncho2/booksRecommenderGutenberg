Synopsis: In 'The Mystery Boys and the Secret of the Golden Sun' by Powell, Van, a group of adventurous friends stumble upon a hidden treasure map that leads them on a thrilling quest. As they follow the clues, they encounter dangerous obstacles, ancient riddles, and unexpected allies. With time running out, the Mystery Boys must unravel the mystery of the Golden Sun before it falls into the wrong hands. Will they succeed in their mission and unlock the secrets of the past?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
