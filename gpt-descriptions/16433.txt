Synopsis: In 'The Gay Cockade' by Bailey, Temple, a young woman named Cecily finds herself caught in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love during the French Revolution. As she navigates the treacherous world of spies and revolutionaries, Cecily must make difficult choices that could change the course of history. With danger lurking at every turn, she discovers the power of loyalty, sacrifice, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Thriller

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Political Intrigue, Resilience
