Synopsis: In 'Martyr' by Alan Edward Nourse, a brilliant young doctor finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger when he uncovers a shocking secret about a new medical breakthrough. As he races against time to expose the truth, he becomes a target for those who will stop at nothing to protect their interests. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path filled with moral dilemmas and unexpected allies, all while trying to save lives and preserve his own sanity.

Genres: Medical thriller, Suspense, Mystery

Themes: Ethics in medicine, Corruption, Sacrifice
