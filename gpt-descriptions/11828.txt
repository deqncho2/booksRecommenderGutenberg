Synopsis: 

Step into the world of U.S. Copyright Renewals, 1963 July - December, a captivating compilation by the Library of Congress. Copyright Office that unveils a treasure trove of forgotten literary gems. Delve into the pages of this remarkable collection and discover a diverse array of works that have shaped American culture. From gripping novels to thought-provoking essays, this anthology offers a glimpse into the creative minds of the past. Explore the forgotten stories, poems, and plays that have stood the test of time, waiting to be rediscovered by a new generation of readers.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literature, History

Themes: Cultural heritage, Intellectual property, Literary preservation
