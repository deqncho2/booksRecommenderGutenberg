Synopsis: In 'The Torch-Bearers: A Satirical Comedy in Three Acts' by George Kelly, a group of amateur actors come together to put on a play, but their lack of talent and egos clash in hilarious and unexpected ways. As the production spirals into chaos, the characters must confront their own delusions and the absurdity of their ambitions. With witty dialogue and clever satire, this play explores the comical side of human vanity and the pursuit of artistic excellence.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Theatre

Themes: Vanity, Ambition, Artistic Delusions
