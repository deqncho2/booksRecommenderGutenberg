Synopsis: In 'An Essay Upon Projects', Daniel Defoe takes readers on a captivating journey through a multitude of innovative ideas and proposals that aim to improve various aspects of society. From economic reforms to social advancements, Defoe's thought-provoking exploration delves into the realm of possibilities, offering a glimpse into a world of endless potential.

Genres: Non-fiction, Satire, Social commentary

Themes: Social improvement, Innovation, Critique of societal norms
