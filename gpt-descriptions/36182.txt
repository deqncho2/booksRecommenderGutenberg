Synopsis: In 'The Whale House of the Chilkat' by George Thornton Emmons, a young anthropologist embarks on a journey to the remote Chilkat village in Alaska, where he becomes immersed in the rich cultural traditions and legends of the indigenous Tlingit people. As he delves deeper into their history, he uncovers a hidden secret that has been guarded for generations, leading him on a thrilling quest to unravel the mysteries of the ancient Whale House. With each revelation, he finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the past and present collide in unexpected ways.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Cultural heritage, Identity, Tradition, Secrets
