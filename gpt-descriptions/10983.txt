Synopsis: 

'The Young Captives: A Narrative of the Shipwreck and Suffering of John and William Doyley' follows the harrowing journey of two brothers, John and William Doyley, who find themselves shipwrecked on a remote island. Stranded and alone, they must navigate the treacherous terrain, face unexpected dangers, and confront their own inner demons. As they struggle to survive, the brothers discover the true strength of their bond and the resilience of the human spirit. 

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Historical Fiction

Themes: Brotherhood, Resilience, Survival, Coming of Age
