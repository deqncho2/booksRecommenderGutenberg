Synopsis: 
'Little Willie' by Field, Eugene is a delightful collection of humorous and heartwarming poems that follow the mischievous adventures of a young boy named Willie. From his hilarious encounters with animals to his imaginative escapades, Willie's antics will keep readers of all ages entertained and laughing. 

Genres: 
Children's Literature, Poetry, Humor

Themes: 
Childhood, Imagination, Adventure, Humor
