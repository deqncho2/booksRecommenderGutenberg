Synopsis: 

In "The Isles of Scilly: Their story, their folk & their flowers," Jessie Mothersole takes readers on a captivating journey to the enchanting Isles of Scilly. Through vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, she unveils the rich history, fascinating folklore, and breathtaking beauty of this hidden gem in the Atlantic Ocean. From the ancient tales of mermaids and shipwrecks to the vibrant tapestry of wildflowers that paint the landscape, Mothersole's narrative transports readers to a world where myth and reality intertwine. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Travel, History, Folklore

Themes: History, Folklore, Nature, Beauty, Mythology
