Synopsis: In 'Luck and Pluck; or, John Oakley's Inheritance' by Horatio Alger, Jr., follow the thrilling journey of John Oakley, a young man who unexpectedly inherits a fortune. As John navigates the challenges and temptations that come with newfound wealth, he must learn to distinguish between true friends and those who seek to take advantage of him. With determination and resourcefulness, John sets out to prove that success is not solely dependent on luck, but also on one's ability to seize opportunities and make wise choices. Will John be able to maintain his integrity and find true happiness amidst the complexities of his inheritance?

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Rags-to-riches

Themes: Wealth and its consequences, Friendship and loyalty, Self-discovery and personal growth
