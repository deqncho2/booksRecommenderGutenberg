Synopsis: 

'Quotes and Images from Christopher Columbus' by Young, Filson is a captivating collection that delves into the mind of the renowned explorer, Christopher Columbus. Through a compilation of his most profound quotes and stunning images, this book offers a unique glimpse into the thoughts, experiences, and discoveries of one of history's most influential figures. From his initial dreams of exploration to his encounters with new lands and cultures, readers will be transported on a captivating journey alongside Columbus, gaining a deeper understanding of his motivations, struggles, and triumphs. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Exploration

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Ambition and determination, Cultural encounters
