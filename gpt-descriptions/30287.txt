Synopsis: 

In 'Modern Americans: A Biographical School Reader for the Upper Grades', authors Owen and Sanford present a captivating collection of biographies that delve into the lives of remarkable individuals who have shaped the course of American history. From influential politicians and groundbreaking scientists to visionary artists and courageous activists, this book offers a diverse range of stories that will inspire and captivate young readers. Through vivid narratives and engaging anecdotes, the lives of these modern Americans come to life, providing a deeper understanding of their achievements and the impact they have had on society. 

Genres: Biography, History, Education

Themes: Achievement, Inspiration, Impact, American History
