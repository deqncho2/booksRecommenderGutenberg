Synopsis: 

'Mount Rushmore National Memorial: A monument commemorating the conception, preservation, and growth of the great American republic' takes readers on a captivating journey through the creation and significance of one of America's most iconic landmarks. From the visionary minds behind the project to the challenges faced during its construction, this book unveils the remarkable story behind Mount Rushmore National Memorial. Through vivid descriptions and historical anecdotes, readers will discover the profound symbolism and enduring legacy of this monumental tribute to the American spirit.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Biography

Themes: American patriotism, National identity, Artistic expression, Historical preservation
