Synopsis: In 'Yermah the Dorado: The story of a lost race' by Frona Eunice Wait, embark on a thrilling adventure to a hidden world where ancient civilizations, mystical powers, and a lost race await. When a young explorer stumbles upon a mysterious map, he sets off on a quest to uncover the truth behind the legendary city of Yermah, said to hold unimaginable treasures and secrets. As he delves deeper into this enigmatic realm, he encounters perilous challenges, unexpected allies, and a destiny that could change the course of history. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to unravel the mysteries of Yermah.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Ancient civilizations, Destiny, Power
