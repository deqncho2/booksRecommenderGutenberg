Synopsis: In "Abraham Lincoln" by James Russell Lowell, embark on a captivating journey through the life of one of America's most iconic figures. This insightful biography delves into the personal and political struggles faced by Abraham Lincoln, from his humble beginnings to his rise as the 16th President of the United States. Lowell's vivid storytelling paints a vivid picture of Lincoln's determination, resilience, and unwavering commitment to justice, making this a must-read for history enthusiasts and those seeking inspiration from a true American hero.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Leadership, Perseverance, Equality, American History
