Synopsis: In "The Story of the Red Cross as told to The Little Colonel," Annie F. Johnston takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins and heroic efforts of the Red Cross. As the Little Colonel learns about the organization's founding and its mission to provide aid and support during times of crisis, she becomes inspired to make a difference in her own community. Alongside the Little Colonel, readers will discover the incredible stories of compassion, bravery, and resilience that have shaped the Red Cross into the renowned humanitarian organization it is today.

Genres: Historical fiction, Children's literature

Themes: Compassion, Humanitarianism, Empathy, Community service
