Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Driftwood, secrets simmer beneath the surface, threatening to ignite a chain of events that will forever change the lives of its inhabitants. When a mysterious fire engulfs the historic Driftwood Inn, the lives of three women become intertwined in unexpected ways. As they navigate the aftermath of the fire, they must confront their own pasts, face their deepest fears, and find the strength to rebuild their lives amidst the ashes. With each page, the mystery deepens, and the bonds of friendship and love are tested in this gripping tale of resilience and redemption.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Romance

Themes: Secrets and betrayal, resilience, friendship, redemption
