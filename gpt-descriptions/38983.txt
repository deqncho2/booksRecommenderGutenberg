Synopsis: In "The Camp Fire Girls Solve a Mystery; Or, The Christmas Adventure at Carver House," a group of resourceful and adventurous Camp Fire Girls find themselves embroiled in a puzzling mystery during their Christmas vacation at Carver House. As they uncover clues and unravel secrets, the girls must use their wit and teamwork to solve the enigma and bring justice to those involved. With the holiday season as the backdrop, this thrilling tale takes readers on a captivating journey filled with suspense, friendship, and the spirit of Christmas.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Problem-solving, Holiday Spirit
