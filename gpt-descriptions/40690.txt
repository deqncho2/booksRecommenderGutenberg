Synopsis: In 'Roger Kyffin's Ward' by William Henry Giles Kingston, a young orphan named Roger Kyffin finds himself under the guardianship of a mysterious and eccentric old man. As Roger delves deeper into his guardian's past, he uncovers a hidden world of adventure, danger, and secrets. With the help of newfound friends, Roger embarks on a thrilling journey that will test his courage and reveal his true destiny.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Discovery
