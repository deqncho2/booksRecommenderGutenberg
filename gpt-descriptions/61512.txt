Synopsis: In 'The One-Eyed Fairies' by Hanley, Georgia Eldridge, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden world of magical creatures living in her backyard. When she befriends a group of one-eyed fairies, she embarks on a thrilling adventure to save their kingdom from an ancient curse. As Lily delves deeper into this enchanting realm, she uncovers secrets that challenge her perception of reality and the power of friendship. With danger lurking at every turn, Lily must summon her courage and wit to protect her newfound friends and restore harmony to the fairy kingdom.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Middle Grade

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Perception of Reality
