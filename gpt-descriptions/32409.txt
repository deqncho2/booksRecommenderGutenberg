Synopsis: In 'Seeds of Pine' by Emily F. Murphy, a young woman named Lily embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about her family's mysterious past. As she delves deeper into her ancestral history, she discovers a hidden world filled with secrets, betrayal, and unexpected connections. With each revelation, Lily's perception of her own identity is challenged, leading her on a thrilling adventure that will test her courage and resilience.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Identity, Heritage, Secrets, Betrayal
