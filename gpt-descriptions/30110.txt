Synopsis: In 'Name and Fame: A Novel' by Sergeant, Adeline, a young woman named Adeline embarks on a journey of self-discovery and ambition in Victorian England. Faced with societal expectations and limited opportunities, Adeline must navigate the complexities of love, friendship, and the pursuit of success. As she strives to make a name for herself in a world dominated by men, Adeline's determination and resilience are put to the test. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating novel explores the sacrifices one must make to achieve both name and fame.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Gender roles, Ambition, Identity, Society's expectations
