Synopsis: In 'The White Flag' by Gene Stratton-Porter, a young woman named Linda embarks on a journey of self-discovery and resilience amidst the backdrop of the beautiful Limberlost Swamp. As she navigates the challenges of love, loss, and societal expectations, Linda's determination to find her own path leads her to unexpected places and tests her strength in ways she never imagined.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Romance, Historical Fiction

Themes: Self-discovery, Resilience, Love and Loss
