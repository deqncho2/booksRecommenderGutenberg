Synopsis: 

'Highways and Byways in Sussex' takes readers on a captivating journey through the picturesque landscapes and charming towns of Sussex, England. Written by Edward Verrall Lucas, this travelogue invites readers to explore the hidden gems, historical landmarks, and local customs of this enchanting region. From the bustling streets of Brighton to the tranquil beauty of the South Downs, Lucas paints a vivid and immersive portrait of Sussex, capturing the essence of its unique character and captivating the reader with his delightful anecdotes and observations.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, History

Themes: Exploration, Local culture, Historical landmarks, Natural beauty
