Synopsis: 
'At Good Old Siwash' by Fitch, George is a humorous and satirical novel set in the fictional Siwash College, a small-town American university. The story follows the misadventures of Professor Siwash, a bumbling and eccentric academic, as he navigates the absurdities of campus life. From outlandish faculty meetings to student pranks, Siwash College becomes a playground for hilarious and unexpected situations. As the plot unfolds, readers are taken on a rollicking journey filled with witty dialogue, comical characters, and a series of laugh-out-loud moments. 

Genres: 
Humor, Satire, Fiction

Themes: 
Academic life, Absurdity, Social commentary
