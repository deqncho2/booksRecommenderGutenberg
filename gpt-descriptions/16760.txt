Synopsis: In this installment of the Chronicles of England, Scotland and Ireland, we delve into the fascinating life of Stephan, the Earl of Bullongne. From his noble lineage to his remarkable military exploits, Stephan's story is one of courage, ambition, and the pursuit of power. As he navigates the treacherous political landscape of medieval England, Stephan's loyalty is tested, alliances are forged, and enemies are made. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Holinshed brings to life the thrilling adventures of this enigmatic historical figure.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Adventure

Themes: Power and ambition, Loyalty and betrayal, Medieval politics
