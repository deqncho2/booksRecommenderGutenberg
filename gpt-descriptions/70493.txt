Synopsis: In "The Prince of Wales and other famous Americans," embark on a thrilling journey through time as a group of extraordinary individuals from different eras find themselves mysteriously connected. From the enigmatic Prince of Wales to iconic American figures, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to a series of captivating adventures that will keep readers on the edge of their seats. As secrets are unveiled and destinies collide, this gripping tale explores the power of fate, the complexities of identity, and the enduring impact of historical figures on the present day.

Genres: Historical fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Fate and destiny, Identity, Historical significance
