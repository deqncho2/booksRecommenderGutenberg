Synopsis: In 'The Collected Works of Henrik Ibsen, Vol. 09 (of 11)', Ibsen takes us on a captivating journey through a series of interconnected stories that explore the complexities of human relationships and the consequences of societal expectations. From the struggles of individuals trapped in oppressive marriages to the clash between tradition and progress, this collection delves into the depths of the human psyche, challenging our perceptions and leaving us questioning the very fabric of our existence.

Genres: Drama, Realism, Tragedy

Themes: Marriage and its constraints, Gender roles and expectations, Individual freedom and societal norms, Morality and ethics, The search for identity
