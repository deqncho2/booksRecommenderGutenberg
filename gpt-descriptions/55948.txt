Synopsis: 
'The Abysmal Brute' by Jack London is a gripping tale that follows the life of Pat Glendon, a young boxer with a mysterious past. As Pat rises through the ranks of the boxing world, he becomes entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and personal redemption. With each fight, Pat's strength and determination are tested, forcing him to confront his inner demons and make difficult choices that will shape his future. This thrilling story explores the dark underbelly of the boxing world and delves into the complexities of human nature.

Genres: 
Sports fiction, Adventure, Drama

Themes: 
Identity, Redemption, Love and Betrayal
