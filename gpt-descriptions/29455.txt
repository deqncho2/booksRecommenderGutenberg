Synopsis: 
In 'Invasion' by Murray Leinster, an unexplained phenomenon occurs, causing the entire population of Earth to fall into a deep sleep. When they awaken, they find themselves in a world completely transformed, with mysterious alien beings now in control. As a small group of survivors band together to uncover the truth behind the invasion, they must navigate a dangerous landscape filled with unknown dangers and unexpected allies. With time running out, they must find a way to reclaim their planet and restore humanity's place in the universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Alien invasion, Survival, Exploration, Identity
