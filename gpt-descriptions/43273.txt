Synopsis: In 'Zen Buddhism, and Its Relation to Art', Arthur Waley delves into the profound connection between Zen Buddhism and the world of art. Through captivating anecdotes and insightful analysis, Waley explores how Zen philosophy has influenced and shaped various forms of artistic expression, from painting and calligraphy to poetry and tea ceremonies. This thought-provoking exploration invites readers to contemplate the transformative power of Zen and its impact on the creation and appreciation of art.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Art

Themes: Zen Buddhism, Artistic expression, Spiritual enlightenment, Aesthetics
