Synopsis: 
'Georgian Folk Tales' is a captivating collection of enchanting stories that transport readers to the rich and vibrant world of Georgian folklore. From mischievous spirits and cunning animals to brave heroes and magical objects, these tales are filled with adventure, humor, and wisdom. Each story unveils a unique aspect of Georgian culture, offering a glimpse into the country's traditions, beliefs, and values. As readers delve into this mesmerizing collection, they will be captivated by the timeless charm and universal themes that resonate throughout these folk tales.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Tradition, Morality, Courage, Wisdom
