Synopsis: In 'A Crime of the Under-seas' by Boothby, Guy, a renowned detective finds himself embroiled in a thrilling mystery when a valuable artifact is stolen from a sunken ship. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden motives that lead him on a dangerous journey beneath the waves. With time running out, the detective must navigate treacherous waters and outsmart cunning adversaries to solve the crime and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Crime and investigation, Betrayal, Underwater exploration
