Synopsis: In 'When Day is Done' by Edgar A. Guest, a young woman named Emily finds herself at a crossroads in life, torn between her dreams and the expectations of society. As she navigates the challenges of love, family, and personal growth, Emily must make difficult choices that will shape her future. With heartfelt emotions and unexpected twists, this captivating novel explores the complexities of self-discovery and the power of following one's own path.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Independence, Love and Relationships
