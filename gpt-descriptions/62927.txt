Synopsis: In 'The Jugglers: A Story' by Molly Elliot Seawell, a young woman finds herself caught between duty and desire as she navigates the complex world of high society in Washington, D.C. With secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances, she must learn to juggle the expectations of others while staying true to herself. As the stakes rise and tensions mount, she must make difficult choices that will shape her future and the lives of those around her.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Identity, Society, Love and Sacrifice
