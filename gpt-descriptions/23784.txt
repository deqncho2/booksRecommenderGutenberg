Synopsis: In "The History of Sir Richard Calmady: A Romance" by Lucas Malet, readers are transported to the intriguing world of Sir Richard Calmady, a man with a mysterious past and a complex present. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, love is tested, and the boundaries of societal expectations are pushed to their limits. With vivid descriptions and captivating characters, this novel takes readers on a journey through the depths of human emotions and the complexities of personal identity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Love and Relationships, Society and Expectations
