Synopsis: In 'The Slanderer: 1901' by Anton Chekhov, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious slanderer begins spreading malicious rumors about its residents. As the rumors escalate, friendships are tested, secrets are revealed, and the town's social fabric starts to unravel. With a web of deceit and suspicion woven throughout, the story explores the destructive power of gossip and the consequences it has on individuals and the community as a whole.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Gossip, Rumors, Betrayal, Community, Trust
