Synopsis: 
In 'An Englishwoman in Angora' by Grace Ellison, a spirited young woman named Mary embarks on a journey to the exotic city of Angora, Turkey. Drawn by the allure of adventure and cultural exploration, Mary finds herself immersed in a world of vibrant bazaars, opulent palaces, and captivating traditions. As she navigates the complexities of a foreign land, Mary encounters unexpected friendships, dangerous intrigues, and a love that defies societal norms. With each step she takes, Mary's perception of herself and the world around her is transformed, leading her to question her own identity and the true meaning of freedom.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Travelogue

Themes: Cultural exploration, Identity, Love and relationships
