Synopsis: In 'The Asses of Balaam' by Randall Garrett, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving a series of mysterious disappearances. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each clue leading him closer to the truth, the detective must navigate a treacherous path filled with danger and betrayal. Will he be able to solve the case before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Crime

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
