Synopsis: In 'Woman and Womanhood: A Search for Principles', Caleb Williams Saleeby delves into the complex and multifaceted nature of womanhood, exploring the various roles and expectations placed upon women in society. Through a thought-provoking analysis, Saleeby challenges conventional notions and seeks to uncover the true essence of womanhood, offering a fresh perspective on the subject. This captivating work invites readers on a journey of self-discovery and enlightenment, as they navigate the intricate tapestry of femininity and its significance in the world.

Genres: Non-fiction, Philosophy, Gender Studies

Themes: Womanhood, Gender Roles, Femininity, Society, Identity
