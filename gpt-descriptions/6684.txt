Synopsis:
Jimmy Pitt, a young American living in London, finds himself in a financial predicament. In order to solve his money troubles, he takes on a job as a secretary to a wealthy Englishman. However, his new position comes with a set of unexpected challenges and hilarious misadventures. As Jimmy navigates the eccentricities of the upper class, he becomes entangled in a web of romantic entanglements and mistaken identities. With his quick wit and resourcefulness, Jimmy must find a way to untangle the mess he finds himself in and secure his uneasy money.

Genres:
Humor, Satire, Romance

Themes:
Social class, Wealth and status, Love and relationships
