Synopsis: In "Edison's Conquest of Mars," Garrett Putman Serviss takes readers on an exhilarating journey to the Red Planet, where Thomas Edison and a group of intrepid adventurers embark on a daring mission to save Earth from a menacing Martian invasion. Filled with thrilling encounters, ingenious inventions, and epic battles, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Steampunk

Themes: Exploration, Innovation, Survival
