Synopsis: In 'The Lady of the Decoration' by Frances Little, we follow the captivating journey of a young American woman who finds herself in Japan as a missionary during the early 20th century. Through her vivid and heartfelt letters, we witness her struggles, triumphs, and the profound impact of her experiences on her life and perspective. As she navigates cultural differences, personal growth, and unexpected challenges, the reader is drawn into a world of self-discovery and the power of human connection.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Epistolary Novel, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Cultural exploration, Identity, Resilience, Empathy
