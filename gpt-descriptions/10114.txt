Synopsis: 
In 'The Great Events by Famous Historians, Volume 02: (From the Rise of Greece to the Christian Era)', readers are taken on a captivating journey through the pivotal moments that shaped the ancient world. From the grandeur of ancient Greece to the rise and fall of mighty empires, this volume delves into the triumphs, conflicts, and cultural transformations that defined an era. Through the eyes of renowned historians, the reader will witness the birth of democracy, the epic battles of Alexander the Great, the rise of the Roman Empire, and the emergence of Christianity. This enthralling collection of historical accounts offers a vivid and comprehensive exploration of the ancient world, transporting readers to a time of great upheaval and extraordinary achievements.

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Power and conquest, Cultural transformation
