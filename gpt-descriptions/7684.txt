Synopsis: In "Harold: the Last of the Saxon Kings," Edward Bulwer Lytton takes us back to 11th century England, a time of political intrigue, power struggles, and forbidden love. The story follows the life of Harold, the last Saxon king, as he navigates the treacherous world of medieval politics, battles against Norman invaders, and grapples with his own personal desires. As Harold's fate intertwines with that of his kingdom, the reader is taken on a captivating journey filled with passion, betrayal, and the ultimate fight for freedom.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power and politics, Love and desire, Loyalty and betrayal
