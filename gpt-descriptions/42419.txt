Synopsis: 

In 'The True History of the State Prisoner, commonly called the Iron Mask: Extracted from Documents in the French Archives', George Agar Ellis delves into the mysterious tale of the enigmatic prisoner known as the Iron Mask. Drawing from authentic documents found in the French Archives, Ellis uncovers a captivating story that has intrigued historians and readers for centuries. As the narrative unfolds, secrets, political intrigue, and hidden identities are revealed, leaving the reader spellbound and eager to uncover the truth behind the Iron Mask's identity.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Biography

Themes: Identity, Power, Conspiracy
