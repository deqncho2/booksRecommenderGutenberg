Synopsis: In "An Essay to the Restoring of our Decayed Trade," Joseph Trevers delves into the dark underbelly of society, exposing the hidden world of smugglers, lawyers, and corrupt officers. Through vivid descriptions and shocking revelations, Trevers uncovers the intricate web of fraud and deceit that plagues our trade system. As the reader delves deeper into this captivating narrative, they will be left questioning the integrity of those in power and the true cost of our decaying trade.

Genres: Non-fiction, Political, Historical

Themes: Corruption, Trade, Society, Power
