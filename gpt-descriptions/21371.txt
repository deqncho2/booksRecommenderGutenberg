Synopsis: In 'Our Soldier Boy' by George Manville Fenn, a young boy named Dick is determined to follow in his father's footsteps and become a soldier. Against his mother's wishes, he enlists in the army and embarks on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, camaraderie, and unexpected twists. As Dick faces the harsh realities of war, he must confront his own fears and discover the true meaning of bravery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Sacrifice
