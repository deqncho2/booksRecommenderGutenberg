Synopsis: 
In 'The First Discovery of Australia and New Guinea: Being The Narrative of Portuguese and Spanish Discoveries in the Australasian Regions, between the Years 1492-1606, with Descriptions of their Old Charts,' George Collingridge takes readers on a captivating journey through the unexplored lands of Australia and New Guinea. Drawing from historical accounts and ancient charts, Collingridge unveils the remarkable tales of Portuguese and Spanish explorers who ventured into the unknown between 1492 and 1606. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, the author sheds light on the early encounters, discoveries, and charting of these distant regions, offering a fascinating glimpse into the exploration of the Australasian territories.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Exploration, Adventure

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Cultural encounters, Cartography
