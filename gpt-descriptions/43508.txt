Synopsis: In 'The Eddy: A Novel of To-day', we are transported to the bustling streets of New York City, where we follow the captivating story of Eddie, a young and ambitious musician. As he navigates the vibrant jazz scene, Eddie finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and the pursuit of his dreams. With the city as its backdrop, this gripping novel takes us on a journey filled with passion, secrets, and the relentless pursuit of artistic expression.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Music

Themes: Ambition, Love, Betrayal, Artistic Expression
