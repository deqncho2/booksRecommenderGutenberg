Synopsis: In 'The Club of Masks' by Allen Upward, a mysterious secret society known as the Club of Masks holds clandestine meetings in the heart of London. As the protagonist delves deeper into this enigmatic world, he uncovers a web of intrigue, deception, and hidden identities. With each revelation, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning their own perceptions.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Deception, Power, Perception
