Synopsis: In 'Coronation Rites' by Reginald Maxwell Woolley, a young historian stumbles upon a hidden manuscript that reveals a shocking secret about the ancient coronation rituals of a mysterious civilization. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic world of these rites, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue, danger, and forbidden knowledge that could change the course of history forever. With each revelation, the historian's own beliefs and understanding of power, tradition, and the human condition are challenged, leading him on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and enlightenment.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Power and its consequences, Tradition and its significance, Forbidden knowledge and its allure
