Synopsis: In 'The Meaning of Truth', James, William takes readers on a captivating exploration of the nature of truth itself. Through a series of thought-provoking essays, James delves into the complexities of truth, challenging conventional notions and inviting readers to question their own beliefs. With a blend of philosophical insight and personal anecdotes, this work offers a fresh perspective on the elusive concept of truth, leaving readers with a renewed sense of curiosity and a deeper understanding of the world around them.

Genres: Philosophy, Non-fiction

Themes: Truth, Perception, Belief, Pragmatism
