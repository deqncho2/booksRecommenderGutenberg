Synopsis: 
In 'Quarter-Back Bates' by Ralph Henry Barbour, follow the thrilling journey of Jack Bates, a talented young athlete who finds himself thrust into the world of college football. As he navigates the challenges of being a freshman quarterback, Jack must prove himself both on and off the field, facing intense rivalries, unexpected friendships, and the pressures of living up to his own potential. With each game, Jack's determination and resilience are put to the test, leading to a climactic showdown that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Sports, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Identity
