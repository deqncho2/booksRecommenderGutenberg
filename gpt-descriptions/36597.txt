Synopsis: 
In 'Three in Norway, by Two of Them', two adventurous friends embark on a thrilling journey through the stunning landscapes of Norway. As they navigate the fjords, mountains, and charming villages, they encounter unexpected challenges and forge unforgettable memories. From daring outdoor activities to cultural encounters, their experiences in Norway will leave readers captivated and yearning for their own Nordic adventure.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Cultural immersion
