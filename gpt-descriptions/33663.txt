Synopsis: In "The Origin and Development of the Quantum Theory," Max Planck takes readers on a captivating journey through the revolutionary world of quantum physics. From his groundbreaking discovery of the quantum of action to the subsequent development of the quantum theory, Planck unveils the fascinating story behind one of the most profound scientific revolutions of the 20th century. With a blend of scientific rigor and personal anecdotes, this book offers a captivating exploration of the mysterious and counterintuitive nature of the quantum realm.

Genres: Non-fiction, Science, History

Themes: Scientific discovery, Paradigm shift, Intellectual curiosity
