Synopsis: 

'Stories of W.W. Jacobs: An Index to All Volumes and Stories' is a comprehensive collection that brings together the captivating tales of renowned author W.W. Jacobs. From eerie ghost stories to humorous anecdotes, this index provides a tantalizing glimpse into the diverse and imaginative world created by Jacobs. Each story promises to transport readers to different times and places, filled with intriguing characters and unexpected twists. Whether it's a chilling encounter with the supernatural or a lighthearted exploration of human nature, this collection offers a treasure trove of unforgettable narratives that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Mystery, Horror, Humor, Adventure

Themes: Supernatural, Human Nature, Fate, Irony
