Synopsis: In 'We Girls: a Home Story' by Whitney, A. D. T., follow the lives of four sisters as they navigate the challenges and joys of growing up in a loving and supportive family. From their daily adventures to their dreams and aspirations, this heartwarming tale explores the bonds of sisterhood and the power of love and friendship. As the girls face various trials and tribulations, they learn valuable lessons about resilience, loyalty, and the importance of staying true to oneself. 

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Family

Themes: Sisterhood, Love, Friendship, Growth, Resilience
