Synopsis: In 'Creating Capital: Money-making as an aim in business', Frederick L. Lipman delves into the fascinating world of business and explores the art of making money. Through captivating anecdotes and insightful analysis, Lipman uncovers the secrets behind successful entrepreneurship and the strategies employed by individuals to amass wealth. From the early days of capitalism to the modern era, this book offers a thought-provoking exploration of the pursuit of financial success and the impact it has on society.

Genres: Business, Economics, Non-fiction

Themes: Wealth creation, Entrepreneurship, Capitalism
