Synopsis: In 'The Master of Stair' by Marjorie Bowen, a mysterious and enigmatic figure emerges as the mastermind behind the political intrigues and power struggles of 17th century Scotland. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the fate of a nation hangs in the balance. With its gripping narrative and complex characters, this historical thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power, Betrayal, Loyalty, Political Intrigue
