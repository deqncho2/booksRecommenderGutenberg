Synopsis: In 'The Expert Maid-Servant' by Christine Terhune Herrick, a young woman named Mary finds herself in a precarious situation when she takes on the role of a maid-servant in a wealthy household. As she navigates the complexities of her new position, Mary must confront unexpected challenges and make difficult choices that will shape her future. With secrets lurking behind closed doors and a web of intrigue unfolding, this captivating tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Social class, Deception, Independence
