Synopsis: 
In 'Survival' by Basil Wells, a group of strangers find themselves stranded on a remote island after a catastrophic event. As they struggle to adapt to their new surroundings, tensions rise and alliances are formed. With limited resources and the constant threat of danger, they must confront their deepest fears and make difficult choices in order to survive. As the days turn into weeks, the survivors are forced to confront not only the harsh realities of their situation, but also the darker aspects of human nature. Will they be able to overcome their differences and work together, or will their own instincts for self-preservation tear them apart?

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Survival

Themes: Human nature, Survival instincts, Moral dilemmas
