Synopsis:
In 'Tony the Tramp; Or, Right is Might' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Tony, a young boy who finds himself living on the streets of New York City. Despite his difficult circumstances, Tony possesses a strong sense of justice and a determination to make a better life for himself. As he navigates the challenges of street life, Tony encounters both friends and foes, and must rely on his wit and resourcefulness to overcome adversity. This captivating tale follows Tony's journey as he strives to prove that doing what is right can lead to a brighter future.

Genres:
Adventure, Coming-of-age, Social Commentary

Themes:
Justice, Perseverance, Morality
