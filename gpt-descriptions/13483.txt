Synopsis: In 'The Drama' by Irving, Henry, Sir, a young playwright finds himself entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and ambition as he navigates the cutthroat world of the theater. With his dreams hanging in the balance, he must confront the dark secrets and hidden agendas that threaten to destroy everything he holds dear. As the drama unfolds both on and off the stage, the protagonist must make difficult choices that will shape his future and reveal the true nature of those around him.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Love, Deception
