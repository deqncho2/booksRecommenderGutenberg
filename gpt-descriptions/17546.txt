Synopsis: In 'The Lion of Saint Mark: A Story of Venice in the Fourteenth Century', follow the thrilling adventures of young Venetian, Frank, as he navigates the treacherous waters of medieval Venice. Amidst political intrigue, pirate attacks, and the looming threat of war, Frank must prove his loyalty to his city and protect its honor. With vivid descriptions of the bustling streets, grand palaces, and majestic gondolas, this captivating historical novel transports readers to the enchanting world of Venice during the fourteenth century.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Political Intrigue, Coming of Age
