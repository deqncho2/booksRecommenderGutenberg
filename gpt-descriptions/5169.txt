Synopsis: In 'Hardscrabble; or, the fall of Chicago: a tale of Indian warfare', Major Richardson takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous events surrounding the fall of Chicago during the War of 1812. As the city becomes a battleground between Native American tribes and American settlers, the lives of a diverse cast of characters intertwine in a desperate struggle for survival. Amidst the chaos and violence, love, loyalty, and sacrifice emerge as powerful forces that shape the destiny of individuals and the fate of a city.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Conflict and Survival, Love and Sacrifice, Cultural Clash
