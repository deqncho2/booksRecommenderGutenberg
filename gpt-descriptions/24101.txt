Synopsis: In 'Egocentric Orbit' by Cory, John, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Alexei is faced with a life-altering decision when he discovers a mysterious anomaly in space. As he delves deeper into the unknown, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets and must confront the consequences of his actions. With high-stakes tension and thought-provoking twists, this gripping sci-fi thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Ethical dilemmas, Consequences of scientific discovery
