Synopsis: In 'Tiger Cat' by David H. Keller, a renowned scientist embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that blurs the line between man and beast. As he delves deeper into his research, he discovers the extraordinary abilities of his feline subject, leading to unforeseen consequences that challenge the very essence of humanity. With suspenseful twists and thought-provoking revelations, this captivating tale explores the boundaries of scientific exploration and the ethical dilemmas that arise when tampering with nature's secrets.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Human-animal relationships, Ethical dilemmas, Scientific experimentation
