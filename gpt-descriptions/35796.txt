Synopsis: 
In 'I Walked in Arden' by Jack Randall Crawford, a young woman named Emily embarks on a journey to the mysterious and enchanting forest of Arden. As she explores the depths of the forest, she encounters a series of captivating and otherworldly experiences that challenge her perception of reality. With each step she takes, Emily becomes entangled in a web of secrets, magic, and unexpected connections that will forever change her life. 

Genres: 
Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: 
Exploration of the unknown, Transformation, Connection between nature and humanity
