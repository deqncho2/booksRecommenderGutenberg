Synopsis: In 'Fritz to the Front, or, the Ventriloquist Scamp-Hunter', young Fritz embarks on a thrilling adventure as he sets out to uncover the truth behind a mysterious ventriloquist and his mischievous puppets. As Fritz delves deeper into the world of ventriloquism, he finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and danger, where nothing is as it seems. With his quick wit and determination, Fritz must navigate through a series of unexpected twists and turns to solve the enigma and bring justice to those involved. Will he succeed in his quest or become another victim of the ventriloquist's sinister game?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Identity, Courage
