Synopsis: In 'Coralie: Everyday Life Library No. 2' by Charlotte M. Brame, readers are introduced to Coralie, a young woman whose life takes an unexpected turn when she is faced with a series of mysterious events. As she navigates through a web of secrets and hidden truths, Coralie finds herself entangled in a world of intrigue and danger. With each twist and turn, the reader is left wondering who can be trusted and what lies beneath the surface of Coralie's seemingly ordinary life.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, Trust and betrayal
