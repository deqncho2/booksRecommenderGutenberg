Synopsis: In 'Mates at Billabong' by Mary Grant Bruce, a group of adventurous friends embark on a thrilling journey to the Australian outback. As they navigate the rugged terrain and encounter unexpected challenges, their bond is tested and strengthened. With breathtaking landscapes and heart-pounding moments, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Australian literature

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Exploration
