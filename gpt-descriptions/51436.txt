Synopsis: In 'Bullet with His Name' by Leiber, Fritz, a down-on-his-luck private investigator finds himself caught in a web of deceit and danger when he takes on a seemingly routine missing persons case. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a dark underworld filled with corruption, betrayal, and a mysterious figure known only as "The Bullet." With his own life on the line, the detective must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the truth and bring justice to those who deserve it.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Noir

Themes: Corruption, Betrayal, Redemption
