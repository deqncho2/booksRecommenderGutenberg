Synopsis: In "California Romantic and Resourceful: A plea for the Collection, Preservation and Diffusion of Information Relating to Pacific Coast History," John Francis Davis takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of the Pacific Coast. From the early Spanish explorers to the Gold Rush era and beyond, Davis weaves together a tapestry of stories that highlight the unique charm and resourcefulness of California. Through his passionate plea for the collection, preservation, and diffusion of historical information, Davis invites readers to delve into the captivating past of the Pacific Coast and discover the untold tales that shaped this remarkable region.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, California history, Cultural studies

Themes: History preservation, Cultural heritage, Exploration and discovery
