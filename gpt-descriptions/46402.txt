Synopsis: In 'Harry Blount, the Detective; Or, The Martin Mystery Solved' by Flanagan, T. J., readers are introduced to the brilliant and enigmatic detective, Harry Blount. When a wealthy businessman, Mr. Martin, is found dead under mysterious circumstances, Blount is called upon to unravel the truth behind his untimely demise. As Blount delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected connections that lead him on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of society. With each clue he uncovers, Blount finds himself drawn further into a complex and dangerous game, where nothing is as it seems. As the tension rises and the stakes become higher, Blount must rely on his keen intellect and unwavering determination to solve the Martin mystery and bring the culprits to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Detective work, Deception, Intrigue
