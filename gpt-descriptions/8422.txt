Synopsis: In "Moral Deliberations in Modern Cinema," Vaknin, Samuel takes readers on a captivating journey through the complex moral landscapes depicted in contemporary films. From thought-provoking dramas to mind-bending thrillers, Vaknin explores the ethical dilemmas faced by characters and the profound impact these choices have on their lives. Through insightful analysis and engaging storytelling, this book delves into the moral gray areas that challenge our beliefs and force us to question our own values. Prepare to be captivated by the intricate web of moral deliberations that unfold on the silver screen.

Genres: Film analysis, Philosophy, Cultural studies

Themes: Morality, Ethics, Decision-making, Values, Consequences
