Synopsis: In 'More Mittens; with The Doll's Wedding and Other Stories: Being the third book of the series' by Fanny, Aunt, readers are taken on a delightful journey through a collection of charming and heartwarming tales. From the adventures of Mittens the kitten to the enchanting Doll's Wedding, this book is filled with captivating stories that will leave readers eagerly turning the pages. Each story is filled with love, friendship, and the joy of discovery, making it a perfect read for both children and adults alike.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Friendship, Love, Adventure, Imagination
