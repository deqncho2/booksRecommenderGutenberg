Synopsis: In "Luke's Wife" by Evelyn R. Garratt, a seemingly ordinary woman named Luke's wife finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when her husband mysteriously disappears. As she delves deeper into his disappearance, she uncovers shocking truths about her husband's past and the dark forces that threaten their marriage. With each revelation, she must confront her own fears and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of their relationship. This gripping tale of love, betrayal, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Marriage and relationships, Identity and self-discovery
