Synopsis:
'Heartsease; Or, The Brother's Wife' by Charlotte M. Yonge is a captivating tale that follows the lives of two brothers, Guy and Philip Morville, and the woman who comes between them. Set in the picturesque English countryside, the story unfolds as the brothers navigate the complexities of love, loyalty, and family secrets. As their paths intertwine with that of the enigmatic Edith, a woman of great beauty and mystery, the bonds of brotherhood are tested, and the consequences of their choices reverberate through their lives. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, 'Heartsease' is a compelling exploration of the human heart and the power of love.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Love and Desire, Family Relationships, Loyalty, Secrets and Betrayal
