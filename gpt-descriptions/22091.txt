Synopsis: 
'The Best Short Stories of 1920, and the Yearbook of the American Short Story' is a captivating collection of diverse and thought-provoking tales that will transport readers to different worlds and evoke a range of emotions. From tales of love and loss to stories of mystery and adventure, this anthology showcases the finest examples of storytelling from the year 1920. Each story is a unique journey, filled with vivid characters and unexpected twists, ensuring that readers will be enthralled from beginning to end.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories, Anthology

Themes: Love, Loss, Mystery, Adventure, Human Nature
