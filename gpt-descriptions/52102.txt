Synopsis: 

'Across the Salt Seas: A Romance of the War of Succession' takes readers on a thrilling journey through treacherous waters and political intrigue. Set during the War of Succession, the story follows the lives of two individuals from different worlds who find themselves bound together by fate. As they navigate the perils of war and the complexities of love, they must make difficult choices that will shape their destinies and the outcome of the conflict. With its captivating blend of adventure, romance, and suspense, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Loyalty and Betrayal, Power and Ambition
