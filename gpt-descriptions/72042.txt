Synopsis: In 'The Corning Egg Farm', Edward Corning takes us on a captivating journey through the trials and triumphs of his family's egg farm. From the humble beginnings of a small operation to the challenges of scaling up production, Corning's personal anecdotes and insights provide a fascinating glimpse into the world of egg farming. As he navigates the complexities of the industry, Corning's determination and passion for his craft shine through, making this a truly inspiring tale of perseverance and the pursuit of one's dreams.

Genres: Memoir, Non-fiction, Agriculture

Themes: Entrepreneurship, Family, Agriculture, Perseverance
