Synopsis: In "Cease Firing" by Mary Johnston, a young woman named Barbara finds herself caught in the midst of the American Civil War. As she navigates the chaos and destruction, she becomes entangled in a forbidden love affair that challenges her loyalty and forces her to question the true meaning of honor and sacrifice. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, this gripping historical novel explores the complexities of war and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, War

Themes: Love and Loyalty, Honor and Sacrifice, Resilience in the Face of Adversity
