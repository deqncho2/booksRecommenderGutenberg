Synopsis: In "Amy Foster" by Joseph Conrad, a young doctor named Kennedy finds himself in a small coastal town where he encounters a mysterious and isolated woman named Amy Foster. As their relationship develops, Kennedy becomes fascinated by Amy's past and the secrets she holds. Set against the backdrop of the harsh and unforgiving natural world, this haunting tale explores themes of isolation, prejudice, and the complexities of human connection.

Genres: Fiction, Short Story, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Isolation, Prejudice, Human Connection
