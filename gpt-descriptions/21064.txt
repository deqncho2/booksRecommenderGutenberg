Synopsis: In 'A Middy in Command: A Tale of the Slave Squadron', young midshipman Jack Rogers finds himself thrust into a dangerous mission aboard a British warship patrolling the African coast to intercept slave ships. As he navigates the treacherous waters of the Atlantic, Jack must confront the horrors of the slave trade, face the challenges of command, and grapple with his own moral compass. With thrilling naval battles, unexpected alliances, and a quest for justice, this gripping tale takes readers on an unforgettable journey through the dark underbelly of the 19th-century slave trade.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Naval Fiction

Themes: Morality, Duty, Justice, Coming of Age
