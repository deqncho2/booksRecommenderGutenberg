Synopsis: In 'What Not: A Prophetic Comedy' by Rose Macaulay, a brilliant scientist named Kitty Grammont finds herself at the center of a government initiative to control the population through a controversial new technology. As she navigates the complexities of love, power, and societal expectations, Kitty must confront the ethical implications of her work and the consequences it may have on humanity. With a touch of humor and a dash of prophecy, this thought-provoking novel explores the limits of science and the importance of individuality in a world obsessed with conformity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Satire, Dystopian Fiction

Themes: Individuality, Ethics, Government Control, Love and Relationships
