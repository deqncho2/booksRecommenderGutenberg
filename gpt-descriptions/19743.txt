Synopsis: The Outdoor Chums at Cabin Point; Or, The Golden Cup Mystery takes readers on an exciting adventure with a group of friends as they embark on a camping trip to Cabin Point. Little do they know that their peaceful getaway will soon turn into a thrilling mystery involving a valuable golden cup. As they navigate through clues and unexpected twists, the Outdoor Chums must use their wit and teamwork to unravel the secrets hidden within the woods. Will they be able to solve the mystery and uncover the truth behind the golden cup?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Exploration
