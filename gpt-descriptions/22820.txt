Synopsis: In 'The Crooked House' by Brandon Fleming, a young woman named Alice inherits a mysterious old house from her estranged grandmother. As she begins to uncover the secrets hidden within its crooked walls, Alice finds herself drawn into a web of deception, betrayal, and dark family history. With each revelation, the house seems to twist and turn, mirroring the twisted relationships and hidden agendas of its inhabitants. As Alice delves deeper into the house's past, she must confront her own demons and make a choice that could change her life forever.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Identity, Psychological suspense
