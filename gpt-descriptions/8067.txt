Synopsis: 
In 'The Boy Scouts on Sturgeon Island; or, Marooned Among the Game-fish Poachers', a group of adventurous Boy Scouts find themselves on a remote island known for its abundance of game-fish. However, their excitement quickly turns to danger when they stumble upon a group of ruthless poachers. Marooned and outnumbered, the Boy Scouts must use their skills, resourcefulness, and teamwork to outsmart the poachers and find a way to escape the island.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Survival, Teamwork, Conservation
