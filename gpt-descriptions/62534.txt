Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the bustling streets of Chester in the year 1858 through the eyes of Hugh Roberts. In this captivating guide, Roberts takes readers on a vivid journey through the city, uncovering its hidden gems, historical landmarks, and vibrant culture. From the majestic Chester Cathedral to the charming rows of shops, every corner of this ancient city comes to life in Roberts' detailed descriptions. As readers navigate the winding streets and immerse themselves in the rich history, they will discover the secrets and stories that make Chester a truly remarkable place. 

Genres: Travel, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Historical preservation, Cultural heritage, Architectural beauty
