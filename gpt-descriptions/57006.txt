Synopsis: In 'The Freaks of Mayfair' by E. F. Benson, a group of eccentric characters residing in the exclusive Mayfair district of London find themselves entangled in a series of peculiar and mysterious events. From a renowned artist with a dark secret to a wealthy socialite with a penchant for the occult, each character's hidden desires and peculiarities are gradually revealed as they navigate a web of intrigue and deception. As the story unfolds, the boundaries between reality and illusion blur, leading to unexpected consequences that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden desires, Illusion vs. reality, Intrigue and deception
