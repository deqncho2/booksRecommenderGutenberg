Synopsis: In "Samantha Among the Colored Folks: My Ideas on the Race Problem," Marietta Holley takes readers on a thought-provoking journey as Samantha, a witty and observant woman, explores the complexities of the race problem in America. Through her encounters with individuals from different backgrounds, Samantha gains a deeper understanding of the challenges faced by people of color and offers her unique perspective on how to bridge the divide. With humor and empathy, Holley's narrative sheds light on the racial tensions of the time and encourages readers to question their own beliefs and biases.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Social Commentary

Themes: Race relations, Empathy, Social justice
