Synopsis: 

Prepare to be captivated by the chilling tales of 'Some Haunted Houses of England & Wales' as O'Donnell, Elliott takes you on a spine-tingling journey through the most haunted dwellings in these ancient lands. From eerie manor houses to ghostly castles, this collection of true stories will leave you questioning the boundaries between the living and the dead. Discover the secrets that lie within these haunted houses and the terrifying encounters experienced by those who dare to enter. Brace yourself for a hauntingly unforgettable exploration of the supernatural.

Genres: Non-fiction, Paranormal, History

Themes: Hauntings, Ghosts, Supernatural phenomena, History, Folklore
