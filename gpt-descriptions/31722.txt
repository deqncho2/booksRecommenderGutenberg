Synopsis: In 'The Corner House Girls in a Play: How they rehearsed, how they acted, and what the play brought in', the Corner House Girls find themselves caught up in the excitement of putting on a play. As they navigate the challenges of rehearsing and preparing for their roles, unexpected twists and turns keep them on their toes. Will they be able to pull off a successful performance? And what surprises await them when the curtains finally rise?

Genres: Children's literature, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Creativity, Perseverance
