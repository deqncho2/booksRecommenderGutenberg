Synopsis: In 'The Wishing-Stone Stories' by Thornton W. Burgess, a magical stone grants wishes to the animals of the Green Forest. As the animals discover the power of the wishing-stone, they embark on exciting adventures and learn valuable lessons along the way. From mischievous pranks to unexpected consequences, the animals must navigate the challenges that arise from their newfound abilities. With each wish, they uncover the true meaning of friendship, responsibility, and the importance of appreciating what they already have. 

Genres: Children's literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Responsibility, Gratitude
