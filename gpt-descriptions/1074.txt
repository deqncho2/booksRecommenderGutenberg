Synopsis: In "The Sea-Wolf," a thrilling adventure unfolds as Humphrey van Weyden, a refined literary critic, finds himself shipwrecked and rescued by a mysterious and enigmatic sea captain named Wolf Larsen. As Humphrey becomes entangled in the brutal and lawless world of the Ghost, Larsen's ship, he must navigate the treacherous waters of survival, power struggles, and the complexities of human nature. With danger lurking at every turn, Humphrey's journey becomes a gripping exploration of the human spirit and the lengths one will go to survive.

Genres: Adventure, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Survival, Power, Morality, Human Nature
