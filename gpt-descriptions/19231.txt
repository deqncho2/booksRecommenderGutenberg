Synopsis: In "The Mummy and Miss Nitocris: A Phantasy of the Fourth Dimension," George Chetwynd Griffith takes readers on a captivating journey through time and space. The story revolves around the discovery of an ancient Egyptian mummy and the mysterious connection it holds with a modern-day woman named Miss Nitocris. As the narrative unfolds, secrets from the past intertwine with the present, blurring the boundaries between reality and the supernatural. With elements of suspense, romance, and the occult, this phantasmagorical tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the enigmatic truth that lies within the fourth dimension.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Time travel, Ancient civilizations, Fate and destiny
