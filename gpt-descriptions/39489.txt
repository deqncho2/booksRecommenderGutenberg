Synopsis: 

In 'With Beatty off Jutland: A Romance of the Great Sea Fight', Percy F. Westerman takes readers on a thrilling journey through the tumultuous waters of World War I. Set against the backdrop of the historic Battle of Jutland, this gripping tale follows the adventures of a young naval officer, Lieutenant Ronald Standish. As Standish finds himself thrust into the heart of one of the most significant naval battles in history, he must navigate not only the treacherous seas but also the complexities of love and loyalty. With danger lurking at every turn, Standish's courage and determination are put to the ultimate test. Will he emerge victorious, or will the merciless tides of war claim him?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Love, Conflict
