Synopsis: In 'The Yellow Frigate; or, The Three Sisters' by Grant, James, a mysterious yellow frigate sets sail, captivating the imaginations of all who encounter it. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the lives of three sisters become intertwined with the enigmatic vessel. With its blend of adventure, romance, and intrigue, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the yellow frigate.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Secrets and Betrayal, Sisterhood and Family, Fate and Destiny
