Synopsis: 
In 'A History of the Growth of the Steam-Engine', Robert Henry Thurston takes readers on a captivating journey through the evolution of one of the most influential inventions in human history. From its humble beginnings as a simple device to its revolutionary impact on transportation and industry, Thurston delves into the fascinating stories of the brilliant minds behind the steam-engine's development. With vivid descriptions and engaging anecdotes, he explores the challenges, triumphs, and controversies that shaped this remarkable machine, captivating readers with every turn of the page.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Engineering

Themes: Technological advancement, Innovation, Industrial Revolution
