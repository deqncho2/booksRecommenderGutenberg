Synopsis: 
'From the Oak to the Olive: A Plain record of a Pleasant Journey' takes readers on a captivating travelogue as Julia Ward Howe embarks on a delightful journey across Europe. Through her vivid descriptions and insightful observations, she paints a vivid picture of the landscapes, cultures, and people she encounters along the way. From the grandeur of ancient oaks to the serenity of olive groves, Howe's journey becomes a metaphorical exploration of personal growth and transformation. As she immerses herself in new experiences and encounters unexpected challenges, the reader is invited to join her in discovering the beauty and diversity of the world.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, Cultural diversity, Personal growth
