Synopsis: 

In 'Crayon Portraiture: Complete Instructions for Making Crayon Portraits on Crayon Paper and on Platinum, Silver and Bromide Enlargements', Jerome A. Barhydt takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of crayon portraiture. This comprehensive guide provides step-by-step instructions and techniques for creating stunning crayon portraits on various mediums, including crayon paper and platinum, silver, and bromide enlargements. From selecting the right materials to mastering shading and blending techniques, Barhydt's expertise shines through as he shares his secrets for capturing the essence and personality of his subjects. 

Genres: Art, Instructional, Non-fiction

Themes: Creativity, Technique, Portrait Art
