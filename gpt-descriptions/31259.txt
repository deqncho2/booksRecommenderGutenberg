Synopsis: In "The Boy With the U. S. Life-Savers" by Rolt-Wheeler, Francis, follow the thrilling journey of young Jack, who finds himself amidst the treacherous waters of the Atlantic Ocean. As a member of the U.S. Life-Saving Service, Jack must face the dangers of shipwrecks, fierce storms, and the relentless power of the sea. With bravery and determination, he embarks on daring rescue missions, risking his own life to save others. Will Jack's courage be enough to overcome the perils that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Bravery, Heroism, Survival
