Synopsis:
In 'Motor Matt's Clue; or, The Phantom Auto', readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside the young and resourceful Motor Matt as he unravels the mystery behind a phantom automobile that seems to appear and disappear at will. As Matt delves deeper into the enigma, he finds himself entangled in a web of danger, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With his quick thinking and remarkable driving skills, Motor Matt must navigate treacherous paths and outsmart cunning adversaries to uncover the truth behind the phantom auto.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Investigation, Perseverance, Deception
