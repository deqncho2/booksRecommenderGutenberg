Synopsis: In 'A Secret of the Sea: A Novel. Vol. 1 (of 3)' by T. W. Speight, a mysterious shipwreck sets off a chain of events that will unravel the secrets of a small coastal town. As the townspeople grapple with the enigma surrounding the ship and its crew, a young woman finds herself drawn into a web of intrigue and danger. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the truth becomes more elusive. This gripping tale of mystery and suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the hidden secrets lurking beneath the waves.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Small-town intrigue, Unraveling the past
