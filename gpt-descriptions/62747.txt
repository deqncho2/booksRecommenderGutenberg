Synopsis: 
In 'Two American Boys in the War Zone' by Green, Levi Worthington, two young boys find themselves thrust into the heart of a war-torn country. As they navigate the dangers and uncertainties of the war zone, they form an unlikely bond that will test their courage and resilience. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this gripping tale takes readers on a thrilling journey through the chaos of war, where the strength of friendship becomes their only hope for survival.

Genres: Adventure, War, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival
