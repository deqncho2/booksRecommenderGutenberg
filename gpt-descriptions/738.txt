Synopsis: In "The Puzzle of Dickens's Last Plot," Lang, Andrew delves into the mysterious and enigmatic final plot of Charles Dickens's last unfinished novel. With a captivating blend of literary analysis and detective work, Lang unravels the secrets behind Dickens's intentions and the possible outcomes of the story. As he explores the intricate web of characters and events, Lang invites readers to join him on a thrilling journey of discovery, shedding light on the untold conclusion of one of literature's greatest authors.

Genres: Literary analysis, Mystery, Biography

Themes: Unfinished works, Authorial intent, Literary puzzles
