Synopsis: 
In 'The Handy Horse-book: or Practical Instructions in Driving, Riding, and the General Care and Management of Horses', Maurice Hartland Mahon presents a comprehensive guide that equips both novice and experienced horse enthusiasts with essential knowledge and techniques. From mastering the art of riding to understanding the intricacies of horse care, this fourth edition offers invaluable insights and practical advice. With a focus on safety, efficiency, and the bond between horse and rider, Mahon's expertise shines through, making this book an indispensable resource for anyone passionate about horses.

Genres: Non-fiction, Equestrian, Reference

Themes: Horse care, Horsemanship, Equestrian skills, Animal welfare
