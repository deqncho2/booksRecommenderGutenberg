Synopsis: In the Year of Jubilee follows the life of Nancy Lord, a young woman living in the late 19th century who finds herself caught between the constraints of societal expectations and her own desires for independence. As she navigates through the challenges of love, family, and social class, Nancy must confront the harsh realities of a world that often denies women agency and freedom. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society, this novel explores the complexities of gender roles and the struggle for personal fulfillment in a time of great social upheaval.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Social Commentary

Themes: Gender roles, societal expectations, women's rights, social class, personal fulfillment
