Synopsis: In 'Paul and His Dog, v.2', Paul de Kock takes readers on a delightful journey filled with humor, adventure, and unexpected twists. This captivating novel follows the misadventures of Paul, a charming young man, and his loyal canine companion. From their escapades in the bustling streets of Paris to their encounters with eccentric characters, Paul and his dog navigate a world full of surprises and challenges. As their bond grows stronger, they learn valuable lessons about friendship, loyalty, and the power of unconditional love. 

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Love, Adventure
