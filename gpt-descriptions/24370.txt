Synopsis: In 'Mercenary' by Reynolds, Mack, a former special forces operative finds himself caught in a web of conspiracy and danger when he is hired for a seemingly routine mission. As he delves deeper into the assignment, he uncovers a dark secret that could change the course of history. With his skills and survival instincts put to the ultimate test, he must navigate a treacherous world of betrayal and deceit to uncover the truth and protect those he holds dear.

Genres: Thriller, Action, Suspense

Themes: Conspiracy, Betrayal, Redemption
