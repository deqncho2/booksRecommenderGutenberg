Synopsis: In 'Munchausen XX' by Worfel, W. G., a renowned psychiatrist finds himself entangled in a web of deception and manipulation when a mysterious patient with an uncanny ability to fabricate stories enters his practice. As the psychiatrist delves deeper into the patient's mind, he begins to question his own sanity and the boundaries between reality and fiction blur. With each twist and turn, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through the labyrinth of the human mind, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Identity, Perception
