Synopsis: In "Jest 'Fore Christmas," a mischievous young boy named Sandy embarks on a hilarious adventure on Christmas Eve, causing chaos and laughter along the way. In "The Night Before Christmas," a family is visited by a magical figure on Christmas Eve, bringing joy and wonder to their home. These heartwarming and enchanting Christmas stories capture the spirit of the holiday season and will leave readers filled with joy and anticipation.

Genres: Children's literature, Christmas fiction, Poetry

Themes: Christmas spirit, Family, Joy, Magic
