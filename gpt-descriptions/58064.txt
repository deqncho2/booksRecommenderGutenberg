Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Grant Allen' is a comprehensive collection of the works of Grant Allen, a prolific writer known for his diverse range of subjects and captivating storytelling. This index provides a convenient guide to explore Allen's literary world, encompassing various genres and themes. From thrilling mysteries to thought-provoking scientific essays, this compilation promises to transport readers into a world of imagination and intellectual exploration.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Science Fiction, Non-fiction, Essays, Short Stories, Romance, Adventure, Historical Fiction, Fantasy, Humor.

Themes: Exploration of human nature, Scientific inquiry, Social commentary, Adventure and discovery, Love and romance, Historical events and settings, Satire and humor.
