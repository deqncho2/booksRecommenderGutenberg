Synopsis: In 'The Coming of Lugh: A Celtic Wonder-Tale Retold', Ella Young weaves a captivating tale that transports readers to ancient Ireland, where the realms of gods and mortals intertwine. As the young hero Lugh embarks on a perilous journey, he discovers his true destiny and the power that lies within him. With enchanting prose and vivid imagery, Young brings to life a world of magic, mythical creatures, and epic battles, leaving readers spellbound until the very end.

Genres: Fantasy, Mythology, Adventure

Themes: Destiny, Power, Identity, Mythical Beings
