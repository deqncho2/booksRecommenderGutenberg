Synopsis: In 'The Talking Thrush, and Other Tales from India', Rouse and Crooke present a collection of enchanting folk tales that transport readers to the vibrant and mystical world of India. From talking animals to magical objects, these captivating stories are filled with adventure, wisdom, and unexpected twists. Each tale offers a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of India, exploring themes of bravery, love, morality, and the power of storytelling. 

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Bravery, Love, Morality, Power of Storytelling
