Synopsis: 
'A Collection of Latin Maxims and Phrases Literally Translated: Intended for the Use of Students for All Legal Examinations' is a comprehensive compilation of Latin maxims and phrases, meticulously translated into English. This invaluable resource serves as a guide for students studying law, providing them with a deeper understanding of the Latin language's influence on legal terminology and principles. From concise legal maxims to complex phrases, this collection offers a wealth of knowledge that will pique the interest of both aspiring lawyers and language enthusiasts alike.

Genres: Reference, Language, Legal Studies

Themes: Latin language, Legal terminology, Education
