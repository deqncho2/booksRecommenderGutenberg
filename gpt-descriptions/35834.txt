Synopsis: In 'The Dust of Conflict' by Harold Bindloss, a young engineer named Ralph Luttrell finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he is sent to a remote mining town in the Canadian wilderness. As he navigates the treacherous landscape and uncovers the secrets hidden within the town, Ralph must confront his own past and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of those around him. With its gripping narrative and atmospheric setting, this thrilling tale of adventure and suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Exploration, Redemption, Moral dilemmas
