Synopsis: In 'The Invention of a New Religion' by Basil Hall Chamberlain, a brilliant and enigmatic professor embarks on a daring experiment to create a religion from scratch. As he delves into ancient texts, explores mystical practices, and challenges societal norms, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger. With each step closer to his goal, the professor must confront his own beliefs and the consequences of his actions, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of faith and the power of human imagination.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Philosophical Fiction

Themes: Religion, Belief, Morality, Identity
