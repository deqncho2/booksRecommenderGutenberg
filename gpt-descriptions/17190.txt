Synopsis: 
'Greek and Roman Ghost Stories' by Collison-Morley and Lacy is a captivating collection of eerie tales that transport readers to the ancient world. From vengeful spirits haunting the living to encounters with the supernatural in the realm of the dead, this anthology delves into the chilling and mysterious realm of Greek and Roman ghost stories. Each story is filled with suspense, supernatural elements, and the exploration of the human psyche when faced with the unknown. Prepare to be enthralled by these haunting tales that have stood the test of time.

Genres: 
Horror, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: 
Supernatural, Fear, Mortality
