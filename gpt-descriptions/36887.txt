Synopsis: In 'The Story of Nefrekepta, from a Demotic Papyrus' by Murray, Gilbert, we are transported to ancient Egypt, where a young scribe named Nefrekepta embarks on a perilous journey that will test his courage, loyalty, and determination. As he navigates through treacherous landscapes and encounters mysterious characters, Nefrekepta's fate becomes intertwined with the fate of his kingdom. With secrets to uncover and a destiny to fulfill, Nefrekepta's adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the outcome.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Coming of Age, Loyalty, Fate and Destiny
