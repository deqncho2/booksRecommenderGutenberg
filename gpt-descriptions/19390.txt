Synopsis: In 'Baby Pitcher's Trials: Little Pitcher Stories' by May, Carrie L., follow the captivating journey of a young pitcher named Baby as she faces a series of trials and challenges on and off the baseball field. From overcoming self-doubt to navigating the complexities of friendship and competition, Baby's determination and resilience shine through as she strives to prove herself in a male-dominated sport. With each trial she faces, Baby learns valuable lessons about perseverance, teamwork, and the power of believing in oneself. This heartwarming collection of stories will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, rooting for Baby every step of the way.

Genres: Children's Literature, Sports Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Gender Equality
