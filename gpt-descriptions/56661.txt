Synopsis: In 'Hacking Through Belgium' by Edmund Dane, embark on a thrilling journey through the heart of World War II as a group of unlikely heroes find themselves caught in a web of espionage, danger, and betrayal. Set against the backdrop of the German occupation of Belgium, this gripping tale follows a team of skilled hackers who must navigate treacherous terrain, outsmart the enemy, and uncover a secret that could change the course of the war. As they delve deeper into the heart of darkness, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. Will they succeed in their mission, or will they become pawns in a deadly game of cat and mouse?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Thriller, Espionage

Themes: War, Resistance, Betrayal, Courage
