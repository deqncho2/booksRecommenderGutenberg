Synopsis: In 'The Lion of the North: A Tale of the Times of Gustavus Adolphus,' follow the thrilling adventures of young Malcolm Graheme as he joins the Swedish army during the tumultuous Thirty Years' War. From epic battles to daring escapes, Malcolm's loyalty and bravery are put to the test as he fights alongside the legendary King Gustavus Adolphus. With political intrigue, personal sacrifices, and unexpected alliances, this historical tale will transport readers to a time of great upheaval and heroism.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, War

Themes: Loyalty, Bravery, Political Intrigue, Heroism
