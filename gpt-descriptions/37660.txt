Synopsis: 
'Of All Things' by Benchley, Robert is a collection of humorous essays that will leave readers in stitches. From the absurdity of everyday life to the comical mishaps of the author himself, Benchley's witty observations and clever anecdotes will keep readers entertained from start to finish. Whether he's navigating the perils of travel or finding humor in the most mundane situations, Benchley's unique perspective and sharp sense of humor make 'Of All Things' a delightful read.

Genres: Humor, Essays

Themes: Everyday absurdity, Comical mishaps
