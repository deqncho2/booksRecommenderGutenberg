Synopsis: In 'Stormy, Misty's Foal' by Marguerite Henry, a young girl named Molly longs for a special connection with horses. When a wild mare named Misty gives birth to a spirited foal named Stormy, Molly's dream of having a horse of her own becomes a possibility. But as Stormy grows, Molly must face unexpected challenges and make difficult decisions that will test her love for horses and her determination to protect them.

Genres: Children's literature, Animal fiction, Adventure

Themes: Friendship, Responsibility, Love for animals
