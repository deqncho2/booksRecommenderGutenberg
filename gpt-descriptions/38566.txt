Synopsis: In 'The Roman Poets of the Republic, 3rd edition', author W. Y. Sellar takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant world of ancient Rome's poets. From the passionate verses of Catullus to the epic tales of Virgil, this book delves into the lives and works of these influential literary figures. As Sellar explores the political and social context in which these poets thrived, he uncovers the hidden stories behind their verses, revealing a tapestry of love, war, and the pursuit of power. With vivid descriptions and insightful analysis, 'The Roman Poets of the Republic' offers a fascinating glimpse into the poetic brilliance of a bygone era.

Genres: Literary Criticism, History, Biography

Themes: Poetry, Roman Republic, Love, War, Power
