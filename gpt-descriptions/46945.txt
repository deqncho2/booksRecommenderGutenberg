Synopsis: In "Rich Man, Poor Man" by Foster Maximilian, the lives of two brothers, separated by circumstances, intertwine in a captivating tale of ambition, love, and betrayal. As Rudy and Tom navigate their divergent paths, one rising to wealth and power while the other struggles to survive, their choices and the consequences they face will keep readers on the edge of their seats. Set against the backdrop of a changing America, this gripping story explores the complexities of family, the pursuit of success, and the enduring bonds that can both unite and divide us.

Genres: Drama, Family Saga, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Ambition, Family, Social Mobility, Love and Betrayal
