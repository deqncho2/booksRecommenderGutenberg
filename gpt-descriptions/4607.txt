Synopsis: In 'Love Me Little, Love Me Long' by Charles Reade, a young woman named Lucy Fountain finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deceit as she navigates the complexities of love and society. Set in Victorian England, this gripping tale follows Lucy's journey as she uncovers shocking truths about her own identity and the people around her. With unexpected twists and turns, this novel explores the power of love, the consequences of deception, and the lengths one will go to protect their secrets.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Deception, Love and Betrayal
