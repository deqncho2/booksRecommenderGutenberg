Synopsis: 

Step back in time and explore the captivating history of Madeley, a small town nestled in the heart of England. In this enthralling book, John Randall takes readers on a journey through the fascinating stories of Ironbridge, Coalbrookdale, and Coalport, three iconic locations that played a pivotal role in shaping the Industrial Revolution. From the birth of the iron industry to the rise of the canal system, from the struggles of the working class to the triumphs of innovation, this meticulously researched account unveils the secrets and triumphs of a bygone era. With vivid descriptions and captivating anecdotes, Randall brings to life the characters and events that have left an indelible mark on Madeley's rich heritage. 

Genres: History, Non-fiction

Themes: Industrial Revolution, Social History, Innovation
