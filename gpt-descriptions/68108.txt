Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 155, August 28, 1915: The Gordon Elopement; or, Nick Carter's Three of a Kind,' renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a perplexing case involving a high-stakes elopement. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Carter uncovers a web of deceit, danger, and unexpected alliances. With time running out, he must rely on his sharp intellect and resourcefulness to solve the puzzle and bring justice to those involved.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
