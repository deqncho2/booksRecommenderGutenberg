Synopsis: In 'Geordie's Tryst: A Tale of Scottish Life' by Janet Milne Rae, readers are transported to the enchanting Scottish countryside, where Geordie, a young and spirited protagonist, embarks on a journey that will test his courage, loyalty, and love. Set against the backdrop of a close-knit community, Geordie's life takes an unexpected turn when he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, forbidden desires, and the pursuit of personal freedom. As he navigates the complexities of his relationships and grapples with the consequences of his choices, Geordie must confront his own identity and the true meaning of love. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, 'Geordie's Tryst' is a compelling tale that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Identity, Love and Desire, Community and Belonging
