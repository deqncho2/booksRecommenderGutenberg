Synopsis: 
In "Tea, its history and mystery" by Joseph M. Walsh, embark on a captivating journey through time and culture to uncover the secrets behind one of the world's most beloved beverages. From ancient legends and mythical origins to the global trade routes that shaped its destiny, this book delves into the fascinating history of tea, revealing surprising anecdotes and untold stories that will leave readers thirsting for more. As the author unravels the mysteries surrounding tea, he also explores its profound impact on society, politics, and even spirituality. Prepare to be enchanted by the aromatic allure of tea and discover the hidden depths of this timeless elixir.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Tradition, Globalization, Rituals, Cultural Exchange
