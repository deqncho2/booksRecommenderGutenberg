Synopsis: In the second volume of "Mary Seaham: A Novel," the captivating story continues as Mary Seaham navigates the complexities of love, family, and society. Set in the 19th century, this enthralling tale follows Mary's journey as she faces unexpected challenges, encounters new acquaintances, and grapples with the consequences of her choices. As secrets unravel and relationships evolve, readers will be drawn into a world of intrigue, passion, and self-discovery.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Relationships, Social Expectations, Personal Growth
