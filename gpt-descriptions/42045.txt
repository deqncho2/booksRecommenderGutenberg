Synopsis: 
In 'The Curate in Charge' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young and ambitious curate finds himself unexpectedly thrust into a position of authority and responsibility. As he navigates the challenges of his new role, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and conflicting loyalties within his parish. With the fate of his community hanging in the balance, the curate must confront his own doubts and fears while striving to bring about justice and redemption.

Genres:
Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes:
Power and authority, Morality and ethics, Redemption
