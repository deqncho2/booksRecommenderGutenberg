Synopsis: In 'The Story of a New York House', follow the captivating journey of a historic house in New York City as it witnesses the rise and fall of generations, secrets, and scandals. From its construction in the 19th century to its present-day inhabitants, this house holds the key to a multitude of intertwined lives and the mysteries that lie within its walls. As the narrative unfolds, readers will be drawn into a web of love, betrayal, and redemption, all set against the backdrop of the ever-changing city. 

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Family, Legacy, Secrets, Urban Life
