Synopsis: In 'Mousey: $b or, Cousin Robert's treasure', readers are taken on a thrilling adventure alongside Mousey, a young girl with a knack for solving mysteries. When Mousey's cousin Robert unexpectedly passes away, he leaves behind a cryptic clue that leads Mousey and her friends on a quest to uncover a hidden treasure. As they navigate through secret passages, decipher riddles, and encounter unexpected obstacles, Mousey's determination and quick thinking are put to the test. Will Mousey and her friends be able to unravel the mystery and find the treasure before it falls into the wrong hands?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-solving
