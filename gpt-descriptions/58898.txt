Synopsis: 
Join Charles Harper on an unforgettable journey as he embarks on a summer tramp from London to the Land's End. From bustling city streets to picturesque countryside, Harper's adventure takes him through charming villages, breathtaking landscapes, and encounters with fascinating characters along the way. As he immerses himself in the beauty and diversity of the English countryside, Harper's journey becomes not only a physical exploration but also a personal and introspective one. With each step, he discovers the transformative power of nature, the importance of human connection, and the profound impact of embarking on a journey of self-discovery.

Genres: Travelogue, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Nature, Self-discovery, Human connection
