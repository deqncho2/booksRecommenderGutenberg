Synopsis: In 'A London Baby: The Story of King Roy' by Meade, L. T., readers are transported to the bustling streets of London, where a remarkable baby named Roy is born. As Roy grows up, he discovers that he possesses an extraordinary gift that sets him apart from others. With the help of his loyal friends, Roy embarks on a thrilling adventure that takes him through the vibrant city, encountering unexpected challenges and uncovering secrets that will change his life forever.

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Self-discovery
