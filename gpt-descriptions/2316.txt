Synopsis: In the small town of Smithville, a young man named John Gray struggles to find his place in the world amidst the challenges of love, ambition, and societal expectations. As he navigates the complexities of life, John becomes entangled in a web of secrets and desires that threaten to unravel the very fabric of his existence. With the backdrop of a close-knit community and the haunting allure of the unknown, 'The Choir Invisible' takes readers on a captivating journey of self-discovery and the pursuit of truth.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Love, Social expectations, Secrets
