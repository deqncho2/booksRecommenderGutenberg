Synopsis: In 'The Europeans' by Henry James, two European siblings, Eugenia and Felix, arrive in America and disrupt the lives of their American cousins, the Wentworths. As the Europeans navigate the unfamiliar American society, their presence brings about a clash of cultures and a series of unexpected events. With their charm, wit, and unconventional behavior, Eugenia and Felix challenge the norms and expectations of the American society they encounter, leaving the Wentworths both intrigued and unsettled. As the story unfolds, the characters find themselves caught between the allure of the Old World and the possibilities of the New World, leading to a captivating exploration of love, identity, and the complexities of cultural exchange.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Comedy of Manners

Themes: Cultural clash, Identity, Love and relationships
