Synopsis: In 'Mary' by Bjørnstjerne Bjørnson, a young woman named Mary finds herself torn between societal expectations and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and personal freedom, Mary must make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of a small Norwegian village, this captivating novel explores the struggles and triumphs of a woman determined to forge her own path.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-age

Themes: Individualism, Gender roles, Self-discovery
