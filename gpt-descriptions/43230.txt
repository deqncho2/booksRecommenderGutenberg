Synopsis: In "Johnny Longbow" by Roy J. Snell, a young boy named Johnny discovers a hidden talent for archery during a summer vacation in the wilderness. As he hones his skills, he stumbles upon a mysterious secret that leads him on an adventure filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected allies. With his newfound abilities and determination, Johnny must navigate through treacherous obstacles to uncover the truth and protect those he cares about. This thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to see how Johnny's journey unfolds.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Discovery, Courage, Friendship
