Synopsis: 
'Old Deccan Days; or, Hindoo Fairy Legends Current in Southern India' is a captivating collection of enchanting tales that transport readers to the mystical world of Southern India. From magical creatures and mythical beings to brave heroes and treacherous villains, these ancient legends are filled with adventure, love, and the eternal struggle between good and evil. Each story unveils a new realm of imagination, where gods and mortals coexist, and where the boundaries between reality and fantasy blur. With its rich cultural backdrop and vivid storytelling, this book promises to captivate readers of all ages and take them on an unforgettable journey through the realms of Hindoo fairy legends.

Genres: 
Folklore, Fantasy, Mythology

Themes: 
Adventure, Good vs. Evil, Love and Sacrifice
