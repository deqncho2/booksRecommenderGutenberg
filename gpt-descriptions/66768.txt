Synopsis: In the small coastal town of Port Jefferson, strange occurrences begin to unfold as the ocean mysteriously starts to rise, threatening to engulf the entire community. As the townspeople scramble to find answers and solutions, they uncover a shocking truth that will challenge their beliefs and force them to confront their deepest fears. With time running out, they must come together and make difficult choices to save their town from being swallowed by the relentless sea.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Environmental disaster, Human resilience, Community unity
