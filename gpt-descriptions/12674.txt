Synopsis: In "Cock Lane and Common-Sense" by Lang, Andrew, a seemingly ordinary haunting in a London house takes a captivating twist when skepticism clashes with the supernatural. As the story unfolds, the reader is drawn into a web of intrigue, deception, and unexpected revelations. With each turn of the page, the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning what is truly haunting the house on Cock Lane.

Genres: Mystery, Supernatural, Historical

Themes: Skepticism vs. Belief, Deception, Truth and Perception
