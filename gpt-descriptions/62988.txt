Synopsis: The Complete Manual of Catholic Piety is a comprehensive guide that offers a selection of fervent prayers, pious reflections, and solid instructions, tailored to suit every stage of life. This invaluable resource also includes a supplementary section filled with excellent and approved devotions, along with the Epistles and Gospels for all the Sundays and festivals of the year. Written by William Gahan, this manual serves as a spiritual companion, providing guidance and inspiration for Catholics seeking to deepen their faith and live a devout life.

Genres: Religious, Devotional, Spiritual

Themes: Catholicism, Prayer, Devotion, Spiritual Growth
