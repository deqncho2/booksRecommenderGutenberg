Synopsis: 

In Volume 08 of "The History of England from the First Invasion by the Romans to the Accession of King George the Fifth," Belloc and Lingard delve into a pivotal period of English history. This volume covers the reigns of Henry IV and Henry V, two kings who left an indelible mark on the nation. From political intrigue and religious conflicts to military conquests and the rise of English nationalism, this volume offers a captivating account of a transformative era in England's past.

Genres: History, Biography, Non-fiction

Themes: Power struggles, Religious tensions, National identity
