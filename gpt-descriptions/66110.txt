Synopsis: In 'A Son of Ishmael: A Novel' by L. T. Meade, a young man named Cyril is thrust into a world of mystery and intrigue when he receives a cryptic message from his estranged father. As Cyril embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about his family's past, he becomes entangled in a web of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, Cyril's perception of his own identity is challenged, leading him to question his place in the world and the true nature of his heritage. Set against the backdrop of a rapidly changing society, this gripping novel explores the complexities of family, loyalty, and the search for personal identity.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Family, Identity, Betrayal, Secrets
