Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the First, or Royal Regiment of Foot," Richard Cannon takes readers on a captivating journey through the origins and remarkable exploits of one of the oldest regiments in the British Army. From its inception during the reign of King James VI of Scotland to its valiant services up until 1846, this historical record unveils the triumphs, challenges, and sacrifices of the regiment's soldiers. Cannon's meticulous research and vivid storytelling bring to life the battles, campaigns, and pivotal moments that shaped the regiment's legacy, making this a must-read for history enthusiasts and military buffs alike.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Military valor, Historical legacy, National identity
