Synopsis: 

In 'A Historical Survey of the Customs, Habits, & Present State of the Gypsies', John Hoyland takes readers on a captivating journey through the mysterious world of the Romani people. Delving into their rich history, unique customs, and present-day circumstances, Hoyland sheds light on a community that has long been misunderstood and marginalized. From their nomadic lifestyle to their vibrant traditions, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the Gypsies' way of life, providing a glimpse into their struggles, triumphs, and enduring resilience. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Cultural Studies

Themes: Cultural identity, Marginalization, Resilience
