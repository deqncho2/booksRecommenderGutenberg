Synopsis: 
Join J. Harris Knowles on an exhilarating journey as he takes readers on a thrilling flight across the United States in the spring of 1898. In "A Flight in Spring: In the car Lucania from New York to the Pacific coast and back, during April and May, 1898," Knowles recounts his daring adventure, sharing captivating anecdotes and vivid descriptions of the landscapes, people, and challenges he encounters along the way. From the bustling streets of New York to the untamed beauty of the Pacific coast, this gripping travelogue will transport readers to a time when aviation was still in its infancy and the vastness of America was waiting to be explored.

Genres: Travelogue, Adventure, Historical

Themes: Exploration, Technology and Progress, Natural Beauty
