Synopsis: In "Shotgun Gold" by W. C. Tuttle, a mysterious stranger arrives in the small town of Dry Gulch, bringing with him a deadly secret and a thirst for revenge. As the townspeople become entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and greed, they must confront their own dark pasts and make choices that will determine their fate. With its gripping plot twists and unforgettable characters, this thrilling Western will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final showdown.

Genres: Western, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Revenge, Betrayal, Redemption
