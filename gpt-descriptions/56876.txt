Synopsis: In 'New Amazonia: A Foretaste of the Future', Mrs. George Corbett takes readers on a captivating journey to a utopian society where women rule and men are subservient. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows a young Englishman who stumbles upon the hidden island of New Amazonia, where he must navigate the unfamiliar dynamics of a matriarchal society. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the women who inhabit this unique world, he begins to question his own beliefs and societal norms. With its thought-provoking exploration of gender roles and power dynamics, 'New Amazonia' offers a fascinating glimpse into an alternative future.

Genres: Utopian fiction, Science fiction, Feminist literature

Themes: Gender roles, Power dynamics, Utopian societies
