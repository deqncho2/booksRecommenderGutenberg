Synopsis: 
"The Diary of a Nobody" is a humorous and satirical novel that follows the daily life and misadventures of Charles Pooter, a middle-class clerk living in Victorian London. Through his diary entries, we witness Pooter's attempts to navigate the challenges of social status, family dynamics, and the absurdities of everyday life. From his comical encounters with eccentric friends and neighbors to his humorous mishaps and aspirations for recognition, Pooter's diary provides a delightful and entertaining glimpse into the ordinary yet extraordinary life of an average man.

Genres: 
Satire, Comedy, Fiction

Themes: 
Social class, Identity, Everyday absurdities
