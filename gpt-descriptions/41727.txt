Synopsis: 

'The Traditional Games of England, Scotland, and Ireland' is a captivating volume that delves into the rich heritage of traditional games and pastimes from these three nations. Written by Alice Bertha Gomme, this book takes readers on a journey through the enchanting world of ancient games, accompanied by tunes, singing-rhymes, and detailed methods of playing. From well-known games like hopscotch and marbles to lesser-known gems, this volume uncovers a treasure trove of forgotten traditions and brings them back to life. With vivid descriptions and historical context, Gomme invites readers to explore the origins, rules, and variations of these games, providing a fascinating glimpse into the cultural tapestry of England, Scotland, and Ireland.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Folklore

Themes: Cultural heritage, Traditional games, Folk traditions
