Synopsis: In 'The Secret of the Silver Car: Further Adventures of Anthony Trent, Master Criminal', the enigmatic and cunning Anthony Trent finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a silver car with a secret compartment. As he delves deeper into the car's hidden secrets, Trent becomes embroiled in a thrilling chase that takes him across Europe, encountering dangerous criminals and unexpected allies along the way. With his quick wit and unparalleled skills, Trent must outsmart his adversaries and uncover the truth behind the silver car before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Redemption
