Synopsis: 
'On Our Selection' by Rudd, Steele is a heartwarming and humorous tale that follows the trials and triumphs of a pioneering Australian family. Set in the late 19th century, the story revolves around Dad Rudd and his resourceful wife, Mother Rudd, as they navigate the challenges of rural life, battling droughts, bushfires, and the whims of nature. With their unique blend of resilience, wit, and determination, the Rudd family's adventures will keep readers captivated as they witness their struggles, joys, and the indomitable spirit that binds them together.

Genres: 
Comedy, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Family bonds, Resilience, Survival
