Synopsis: In "We Were There at the Oklahoma Land Run," author Jim Kjelgaard takes readers on a thrilling journey back to the late 1800s, where a group of determined settlers participate in the historic Oklahoma Land Run. Through the eyes of two young brothers, the story unfolds with heart-pounding excitement as they face numerous challenges and obstacles in their quest for a new life on the untamed frontier. As the race to claim land begins, the brothers must navigate treacherous terrain, outsmart rival settlers, and confront their own fears and doubts. Will they succeed in staking their claim and building a future in this wild and unpredictable land?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Perseverance, Courage, Frontier Life, Family Bonds
