Synopsis: In "Mirrors of Moscow" by Louise Stevens Bryant, a young American woman finds herself entangled in a web of intrigue and danger when she travels to Moscow in the midst of the Russian Revolution. As she navigates the treacherous political landscape and encounters mysterious figures, she must confront her own beliefs and make choices that could have far-reaching consequences. With its captivating blend of historical fiction and suspense, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through a tumultuous period in Russian history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Revolution, Identity, Betrayal, Survival
