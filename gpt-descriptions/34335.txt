Synopsis: In 'The William Henry Letters' by Abby Morton Diaz, a series of mysterious letters written by a young boy named William Henry captivate the attention of a small New England town. As the townspeople delve into the enigmatic messages, they uncover a web of secrets, hidden pasts, and unexpected connections that will forever change their lives. With each letter, the intrigue deepens, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Epistolary

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Community and interconnectedness
