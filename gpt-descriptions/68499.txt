Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 143, June 5, 1915: The sultan's pearls; or, Nick Carter's Porto Rico trail', renowned detective Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a thrilling adventure when he is tasked with recovering a valuable set of pearls stolen from a sultan. As he follows the trail that leads him to the exotic island of Porto Rico, Carter must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter dangerous adversaries, and unravel a web of deceit to solve the mystery and retrieve the precious pearls.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Crime

Themes: Pursuit of justice, Deception and betrayal
