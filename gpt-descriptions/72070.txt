Synopsis: In 'Floor of Heaven' by T. D. Hamm, a young archaeologist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to an ancient secret. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she becomes entangled in a dangerous web of intrigue, betrayal, and hidden agendas. With each step closer to the truth, she uncovers shocking revelations that challenge everything she thought she knew about history and the world. As the stakes rise, she must race against time to unravel the puzzle before it falls into the wrong hands, risking not only her own life but the fate of humanity itself.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Ancient secrets, Betrayal, Conspiracy, History, Quest for truth
