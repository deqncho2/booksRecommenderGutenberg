Synopsis: In the Tail of the Peacock takes readers on a captivating journey through the vibrant and mysterious world of Istanbul in the early 20th century. Isabel Savory, an adventurous young woman, finds herself entangled in a web of intrigue and danger as she becomes embroiled in a quest for a hidden treasure. Along the way, she encounters a cast of fascinating characters, navigates the labyrinthine streets of the city, and unravels the secrets of the past. With its richly descriptive prose and thrilling plot twists, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Secrets, Cultural Heritage
