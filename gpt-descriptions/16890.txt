Synopsis: 
In the small coastal town of Polperro, Hetty Wesley, a young and spirited woman, finds herself caught between the expectations of society and her own desires. As she navigates the complexities of love, family, and personal freedom, Hetty must confront the consequences of her choices and the secrets that threaten to unravel her world. Set against the backdrop of 19th-century Cornwall, 'Hetty Wesley' is a captivating tale of passion, sacrifice, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and Desire, Social Expectations, Personal Freedom
