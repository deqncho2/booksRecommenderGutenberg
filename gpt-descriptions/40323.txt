Synopsis: 
In the April 1876 issue of The Celtic Magazine, immerse yourself in a captivating collection of literature, history, antiquities, folk lore, traditions, and the social and material interests of the Celt at home and abroad. Delve into the rich tapestry of Celtic culture as you explore the pages filled with enchanting tales, insightful historical accounts, and fascinating glimpses into the lives of the Celts. From ancient legends to contemporary issues, this monthly periodical offers a comprehensive exploration of the Celtic world, captivating readers with its diverse range of topics and engaging storytelling.

Genres: 
Literary Magazine, Historical Magazine, Folklore Magazine

Themes: 
Celtic culture, Literature, History, Antiquities, Folklore, Traditions, Social interests, Material interests
