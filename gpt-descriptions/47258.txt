Synopsis: 

'American Inventions and Inventors' takes readers on a captivating journey through the fascinating world of American innovation. From the early days of the nation to the present, authors Mowry and Mowry delve into the stories behind some of the most groundbreaking inventions and the brilliant minds that brought them to life. From the invention of the telephone to the creation of the internet, this book uncovers the remarkable ingenuity and determination that shaped America's technological landscape. Through vivid storytelling and meticulous research, the authors shed light on the triumphs, failures, and untold stories of the inventors who forever changed the course of history.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Biography

Themes: Innovation, Ingenuity, American History, Technological Advancement
