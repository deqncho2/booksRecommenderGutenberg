Synopsis:
In the second volume of Glenarvon, the captivating and scandalous tale continues as the protagonist, Lady Calantha, finds herself entangled in a web of forbidden love, political intrigue, and personal vendettas. As she navigates the treacherous waters of society, Lady Calantha must confront her own desires and the consequences of her actions. With secrets and betrayals lurking around every corner, this gripping novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth.

Genres:
Romance, Gothic Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Love and passion, Betrayal, Power and politics
