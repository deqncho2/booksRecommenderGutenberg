Synopsis: In 'The Peacemaker' by Alfred Coppel, a seasoned diplomat finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous international crisis. As tensions rise and the threat of war looms, he must navigate treacherous political waters and uncover the truth behind a mysterious conspiracy. With lives hanging in the balance, he races against time to prevent catastrophe and bring about peace. 

Genres: Political thriller, Suspense, Mystery

Themes: Diplomacy, International relations, Conspiracy
