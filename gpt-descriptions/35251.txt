Synopsis: In 'Under a Charm: A Novel. Vol. I' by Werner, E., a young woman named Amelia finds herself drawn into a mysterious world of enchantment and danger when she discovers an ancient artifact. As she delves deeper into the secrets surrounding the charm, Amelia must navigate treacherous alliances, uncover hidden truths, and confront her own inner demons. With each step she takes, the line between reality and fantasy blurs, leaving her questioning everything she thought she knew. This captivating tale of magic and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth behind the charm.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Magic, Identity, Betrayal, Self-discovery
