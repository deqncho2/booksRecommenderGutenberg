Synopsis: In 'I, Executioner', a gripping collaboration between Terry Carr and Ted White, a young man finds himself thrust into the role of executioner in a dystopian society where death is a form of entertainment. As he carries out his grim duties, he begins to question the morality of his actions and the true nature of justice. With each execution, he delves deeper into a world of corruption and secrets, leading him to a shocking revelation that will challenge everything he thought he knew.

Genres: Science Fiction, Dystopian, Thriller

Themes: Morality, Justice, Corruption
