Synopsis: In 'The Dare Boys in Virginia' by Cox, Stephen Angus Douglas, a group of adventurous young boys find themselves caught up in the midst of the American Civil War. As they embark on a daring mission to deliver a vital message, they encounter danger, intrigue, and unexpected allies. With their courage and resourcefulness tested to the limit, the Dare Boys must navigate through enemy lines and face the harsh realities of war, all while striving to fulfill their mission and make it back home alive.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, War

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Loyalty, Survival
