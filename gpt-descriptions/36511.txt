Synopsis: In 'The Weight of the Crown' by Fred M. White, a gripping tale unfolds as a young prince finds himself thrust into a world of power, intrigue, and danger. As he navigates the treacherous waters of royalty, he must confront the dark secrets that lie within the palace walls and make choices that will shape the destiny of his kingdom. With unexpected twists and a cast of compelling characters, this thrilling novel explores the sacrifices one must make when burdened with the weight of the crown.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Power and Responsibility, Betrayal, Identity and Self-Discovery
