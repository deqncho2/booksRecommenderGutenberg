Synopsis: In 'Dave Porter's Great Search; Or, The Perils of a Young Civil Engineer' by Edward Stratemeyer, we follow the thrilling journey of Dave Porter, a young civil engineer, as he embarks on a quest that will test his courage and determination. When a mysterious letter arrives, Dave finds himself entangled in a dangerous web of secrets and hidden treasures. With his loyal friends by his side, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter unexpected allies and foes, and unravel the clues that will lead him to a life-changing discovery. Will Dave's engineering skills be enough to overcome the perils that await him? Join him on this gripping adventure that will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration, Pursuit of Knowledge
