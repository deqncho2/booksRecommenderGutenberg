Synopsis: In 'A Middle English Vocabulary, Designed for use with Sisam's Fourteenth Century Verse & Prose,' J.R.R. Tolkien takes readers on a linguistic journey through the enchanting world of Middle English. This comprehensive vocabulary guide serves as a key to unlock the beauty and richness of the language used in the fourteenth-century verse and prose. With Tolkien's expertise and passion for philology, readers will delve into the intricacies of Middle English, discovering the hidden meanings and nuances behind the words. From Chaucer to Gower, this invaluable resource allows readers to fully appreciate the literary treasures of the past and gain a deeper understanding of the historical context in which they were written.

Genres: Linguistics, Philology, Literary Criticism

Themes: Language and its evolution, Historical context, Literary analysis
