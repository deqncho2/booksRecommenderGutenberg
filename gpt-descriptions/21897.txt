Synopsis: 
'An Incident on Route 12' by James H. Schmitz is a gripping and suspenseful thriller that follows the life of Sarah, a young woman who finds herself caught in a dangerous situation while driving on Route 12. As she encounters a mysterious stranger on the road, her life takes an unexpected turn, leading her on a thrilling journey of survival and self-discovery. With each twist and turn, Sarah must confront her deepest fears and make difficult choices that will determine her fate. 

Genres: Thriller, Suspense, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Identity, Fear
