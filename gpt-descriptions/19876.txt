Synopsis: 
In 'On the Fringe of the Great Fight', George Gallie Nasmith takes us on a gripping journey through the tumultuous events of World War I. Through the eyes of a young soldier, we witness the horrors of the trenches, the camaraderie among comrades, and the indomitable spirit that emerges amidst the chaos. As the protagonist faces the harsh realities of war, he is forced to confront his own fears and question the meaning of sacrifice. With vivid descriptions and emotional depth, this novel offers a poignant exploration of the human experience during one of history's most devastating conflicts.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War, Sacrifice, Camaraderie, Fear, Resilience
