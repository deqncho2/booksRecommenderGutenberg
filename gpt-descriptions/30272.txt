Synopsis: 
'Very Short Stories and Verses For Children' by Clifford, W. K., Mrs. is a delightful collection of whimsical tales and playful verses that will captivate young readers. From mischievous animals to magical adventures, each story and verse is filled with imagination and charm. Whether it's a clever fox outsmarting his friends or a mischievous fairy causing mischief, these short tales are sure to entertain and inspire young minds. With colorful illustrations and engaging language, this collection is perfect for bedtime reading or for children to enjoy on their own.

Genres: Children's Literature, Short Stories, Poetry

Themes: Imagination, Adventure, Playfulness
