Synopsis: In 'An Old Meerschaum: From Coals Of Fire And Other Stories, Volume II. (of III.)' by David Christie Murray, a mysterious old meerschaum pipe holds the power to transport its owner through time and space, leading to unexpected encounters and life-altering experiences. As the protagonist delves deeper into the pipe's secrets, they must confront their own desires and confront the consequences of their actions. This captivating collection of stories explores the profound impact of choices and the interconnectedness of past, present, and future.

Genres: Fiction, Fantasy, Time Travel

Themes: Time, Fate, Consequences, Personal Transformation
