Synopsis: 
In the November 1895 issue of 'The Philistine: A Periodical of Protest', readers are treated to a thought-provoking collection of essays, stories, and illustrations that challenge societal norms and ignite a spirit of rebellion. From scathing critiques of the art world to poignant reflections on the human condition, this volume captivates with its bold and unapologetic voice. Delve into the pages of 'The Philistine' and discover a world where dissent is celebrated, conformity is questioned, and the power of individuality reigns supreme.

Genres: 
Satire, Social Commentary, Literary Magazine

Themes: 
Nonconformity, Individualism, Critique of Society
