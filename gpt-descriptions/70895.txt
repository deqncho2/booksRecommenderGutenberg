Synopsis: In "Notes on Witchcraft," George Lyman Kittredge delves into the mysterious world of witchcraft, exploring its origins, practices, and the beliefs surrounding it. Through a meticulous examination of historical records and folklore, Kittredge uncovers the fascinating and often chilling tales of witches and their alleged powers. From the infamous Salem witch trials to the ancient practices of Europe, this captivating work sheds light on the dark and enigmatic realm of witchcraft.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Occult

Themes: Witchcraft, Folklore, Supernatural, History
