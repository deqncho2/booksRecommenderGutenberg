Synopsis: 
Torchy, Private Sec. follows the adventures of Torchy Blane, a smart and sassy private secretary who finds herself caught up in a dangerous web of mystery and intrigue. When her boss goes missing, Torchy takes it upon herself to uncover the truth, leading her on a thrilling journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. With her quick wit and determination, Torchy must navigate through a world of deception and danger to solve the case and save the day.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Comedy

Themes: Female empowerment, Determination, Loyalty
