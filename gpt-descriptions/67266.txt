Synopsis: 
In 'Dogs Always Know' by Elisabeth Sanxay Holding, a seemingly ordinary woman finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a secret that could change her life forever. As she delves deeper into the enigma, she realizes that her beloved canine companion may hold the key to unlocking the truth. With her life hanging in the balance, she must navigate a treacherous path of deception and betrayal, all while relying on her intuition and the unwavering loyalty of her four-legged friend.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Trust, Loyalty, Perception
