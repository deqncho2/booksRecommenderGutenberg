Synopsis: In 'The Boy Scouts Under Fire in Mexico' by Goldfrap, John Henry, a group of adventurous Boy Scouts find themselves embroiled in a dangerous mission across the border. As they navigate the treacherous landscapes of Mexico, they encounter unexpected allies and face formidable enemies, testing their courage and resourcefulness to the limit. With their loyalty and determination, the Boy Scouts must overcome numerous obstacles and unravel a mystery that could have far-reaching consequences. Will they emerge victorious or succumb to the perils that await them?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Exploration
