Synopsis: 
In "Such Things Are: A Play, in Five Acts" by Inchbald, Mrs., a mysterious stranger arrives in a small village, causing a stir among the townspeople. As secrets are unveiled and relationships are tested, the characters find themselves entangled in a web of love, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With each act, the tension builds, leading to a climactic finale that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Love and passion, Betrayal, Secrets and deception
