Synopsis: In 'The Death Crystal' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious crystal with the power to predict the exact moment of a person's death. As he delves deeper into its secrets, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of conspiracy and must race against time to uncover the truth before it's too late. This gripping sci-fi thriller will keep readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the nature of fate and the consequences of knowing one's own destiny.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Fate, Consequences, Conspiracy
