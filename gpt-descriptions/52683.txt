Synopsis: In 'The Mystery Boys and the Inca Gold' by Powell, Van, a group of adventurous friends stumble upon a hidden treasure map that leads them on a thrilling journey through the heart of the Peruvian jungle. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter mysterious obstacles, the boys must rely on their wits and teamwork to unravel the secrets of the ancient Inca civilization and uncover the legendary Inca gold. With danger lurking at every turn, the Mystery Boys find themselves caught in a race against time to claim the treasure before it falls into the wrong hands.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Ancient Civilizations, Treasure Hunting
