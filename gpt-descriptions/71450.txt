Synopsis: 
In 'My Heart's in the Highlands' by Amy Le Feuvre, a young woman named Jean finds herself torn between duty and desire when she is unexpectedly sent to the Scottish Highlands. As she navigates the rugged landscape and encounters a cast of intriguing characters, Jean's heart is awakened to new possibilities and she must confront the choices that will shape her future. With its captivating setting and heartfelt emotions, this story explores the transformative power of love and the pursuit of one's true passions.

Genres: Romance, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Duty vs Desire, Self-discovery, Love and Sacrifice
