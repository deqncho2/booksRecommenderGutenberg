Synopsis: In the midst of religious turmoil during the Elizabethan era, 'Come Rack! Come Rope!' follows the lives of two young lovers, Robin and Margaret, as they navigate the dangerous world of Catholic persecution in England. As their faith is tested and their loyalty to each other is put to the ultimate test, they find themselves caught in a web of intrigue, betrayal, and sacrifice. Will their love and devotion be enough to withstand the relentless persecution they face?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Religious persecution, Loyalty, Sacrifice
