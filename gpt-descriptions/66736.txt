Synopsis: In 'The Butterflies and Moths of Teneriffe' by Holt White, A. E., a passionate entomologist embarks on a captivating journey to the enchanting island of Teneriffe, where he becomes immersed in the vibrant world of butterflies and moths. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers surprising secrets and encounters unexpected challenges that test his dedication to the study of these delicate creatures. With vivid descriptions and intriguing discoveries, this book takes readers on a mesmerizing exploration of the intricate beauty and mysterious allure of Teneriffe's butterfly and moth population.

Genres: Non-fiction, Natural History, Travelogue

Themes: Nature's wonders, Scientific exploration, Biodiversity
