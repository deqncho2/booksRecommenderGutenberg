Synopsis: 
'Gold-Seeking on the Dalton Trail' follows the thrilling adventures of two young boys from New England who embark on a daring journey to Alaska and the Northwest Territory in search of gold. As they navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter wild animals, and face the challenges of the harsh wilderness, their determination and resilience are put to the ultimate test. With danger lurking at every turn, the boys must rely on their wits and resourcefulness to survive and achieve their dreams of striking it rich. This gripping tale of courage, friendship, and the pursuit of fortune will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Friendship, Pursuit of Wealth
