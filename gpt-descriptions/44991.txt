Synopsis: 
In "Lily Norris' Enemy" by Joanna H. Mathews, the story revolves around Lily Norris, a young woman who finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and deception. When a mysterious stranger enters her life, Lily's world is turned upside down, and she must navigate through a dangerous game of cat and mouse. As she uncovers the truth about her enemy, Lily discovers shocking revelations that challenge her perception of trust and loyalty. With her life on the line, Lily must confront her enemy head-on and fight for her survival.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Identity, Survival
