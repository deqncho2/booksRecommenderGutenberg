Synopsis: In 'A Good Samaritan' by Mary Raymond Shipman Andrews, a chance encounter between a wealthy young woman and a destitute man on a train leads to a life-altering decision. As their paths intertwine, the boundaries of social class and personal sacrifice are tested, revealing the true nature of compassion and the power of human connection. This gripping tale explores the unexpected consequences of a single act of kindness and the transformative potential it holds.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Short Story

Themes: Compassion, Social class, Sacrifice
