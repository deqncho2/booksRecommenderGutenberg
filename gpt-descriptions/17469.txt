Synopsis: In 'Berry and Co.' by Yates, Dornford, a young man named Berry Pleydell finds himself entangled in a mysterious and dangerous world when he takes a job at a seemingly ordinary London office. As he delves deeper into the company's affairs, Berry uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With his life on the line, Berry must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity
