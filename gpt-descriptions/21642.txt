Synopsis: 
In 'Lady Cadogan's Illustrated Games of Solitaire or Patience: New Revised Edition, including American Games', Adelaide, Lady Cadogan presents a captivating collection of solitaire games that will keep readers entertained for hours. This revised edition includes a variety of American games, adding a fresh twist to the classic pastime. With detailed illustrations and clear instructions, Lady Cadogan guides readers through each game, providing strategies and tips along the way. Whether you're a seasoned solitaire player or a beginner looking to learn, this book offers a delightful and challenging selection of games to enjoy.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Games, Reference

Themes: 
Entertainment, Strategy, Mental Stimulation
