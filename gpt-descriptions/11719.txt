Synopsis: In 'Kincaid's Battery' by George Washington Cable, a young soldier named Kincaid finds himself caught in the midst of the American Civil War. As he joins a battery of artillery, he must navigate the chaos and brutality of battle, while also grappling with his own personal demons. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Cable takes readers on a gripping journey through the harrowing experiences of war, exploring the bonds of brotherhood and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: War, Brotherhood, Identity, Resilience
