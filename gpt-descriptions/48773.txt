Synopsis: 
In 'Calvary: A Novel' by Octave Mirbeau, a small French village becomes the stage for a gripping tale of sin, redemption, and the complexities of human nature. When a respected priest receives a death threat during confession, he embarks on a profound journey that forces him to confront the darkest corners of his community. As he navigates through a web of secrets, scandals, and moral dilemmas, the priest's faith is tested, and he must grapple with the blurred lines between good and evil. Mirbeau's masterful storytelling delves into the depths of human depravity and the power of forgiveness, leaving readers captivated until the very last page.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Sin and redemption, Morality, Hypocrisy, Faith and doubt
