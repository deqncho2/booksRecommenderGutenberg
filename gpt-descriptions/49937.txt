Synopsis: In 'Motor Matt's Defiance; or, Around the Horn' by Stanley R. Matthews, readers are taken on a thrilling journey as Motor Matt, a young and daring adventurer, sets out on a daring mission to prove his mettle. With danger lurking at every turn, Motor Matt must navigate treacherous waters, face formidable foes, and overcome seemingly insurmountable obstacles. As he races against time, his determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test. Will Motor Matt's defiance be enough to conquer the challenges that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Action, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Perseverance, Coming of Age
