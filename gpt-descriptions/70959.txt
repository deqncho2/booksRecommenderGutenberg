Synopsis: 
In 'Ikom Folk Stories from Southern Nigeria', Dayrell takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich folklore of the Ikom people. From talking animals and mythical creatures to brave heroes and cunning tricksters, these enchanting tales are sure to captivate readers of all ages. As the stories unfold, readers will be transported to a world filled with adventure, magic, and moral lessons that have been passed down through generations. With each story, Dayrell weaves a tapestry of culture, tradition, and the timeless power of storytelling.

Genres: Folklore, Fantasy, Cultural

Themes: Tradition, Morality, Cultural Identity
