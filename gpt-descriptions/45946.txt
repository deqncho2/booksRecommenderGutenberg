Synopsis: 
In 'A-B-C of Gardening' by Eben E. Rexford, embark on a captivating journey through the world of gardening as the author shares his wealth of knowledge and passion for plants. From the basics of soil preparation to the intricacies of plant care, this book serves as a comprehensive guide for both novice and experienced gardeners alike. With practical tips, insightful anecdotes, and beautiful illustrations, Rexford's love for gardening shines through, inspiring readers to cultivate their own green oasis and discover the joy of nurturing nature.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gardening, Reference

Themes: Gardening techniques, Plant care, Horticultural knowledge
