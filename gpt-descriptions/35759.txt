Synopsis: In 'Security Risk' by Clinton, Ed M., a brilliant computer hacker finds himself caught in a dangerous game of cat and mouse when he inadvertently uncovers a top-secret government conspiracy. As he races against time to expose the truth, he becomes entangled in a web of deception, betrayal, and high-stakes espionage. With his life on the line, he must navigate through a maze of hidden agendas and unexpected alliances, all while trying to stay one step ahead of those who will stop at nothing to protect their secrets.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Techno-thriller

Themes: Government conspiracy, Cybersecurity, Betrayal, Deception
