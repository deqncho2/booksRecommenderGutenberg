Synopsis: In 'The Home at Greylock', a young woman named Lucy travels to the enchanting Greylock estate to work as a governess for two mysterious children. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the house, Lucy discovers a hidden world filled with magic, danger, and a dark family history. With the help of a charming stranger, she must unravel the mysteries of Greylock before it consumes her and the children she has come to care for.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Magic and enchantment, Identity and self-discovery
