Synopsis: In 'The Coming Race', a young explorer stumbles upon a hidden underground civilization with advanced technology and a mysterious power source. As he becomes entangled in their society, he uncovers dark secrets and faces a choice that could change the fate of both worlds. This thrilling novel explores the clash between progress and tradition, the dangers of unchecked power, and the potential consequences of human curiosity.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Utopian Fiction

Themes: Technological advancement, Power and control, Exploration and discovery
