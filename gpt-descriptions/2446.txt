Synopsis: In a small coastal town, Dr. Thomas Stockmann discovers a dangerous truth that threatens the economic prosperity of the community. As he tries to expose the truth, he faces opposition from powerful figures who prioritize their own interests over the well-being of the people. Driven by his unwavering conviction, he becomes an enemy of the very society he seeks to protect.

Genres: Drama, Political, Social Commentary

Themes: Truth and Integrity, Individual vs. Society, Corruption and Power, Morality and Consequences
