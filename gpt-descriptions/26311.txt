Synopsis: In 'Choice Cookery', Catherine Owen takes readers on a culinary adventure like no other. From mouthwatering recipes to unexpected twists, this cookbook will leave you hungry for more. With a dash of humor and a pinch of creativity, Owen invites you to explore the art of cooking with a delightful twist. Get ready to embark on a gastronomic journey that will tantalize your taste buds and ignite your imagination.

Genres: Cookbook, Humor, Creative Nonfiction

Themes: Culinary exploration, Creativity in cooking
