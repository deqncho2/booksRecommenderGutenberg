Synopsis: In 'The Rumble and the Roar' by Bartholomew, Stephen, a young woman named Lily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden society of extraordinary individuals with unique abilities. As she delves deeper into this mysterious world, Lily must navigate treacherous alliances, uncover the truth about her own past, and ultimately decide where her loyalties lie. With thrilling twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Power, Betrayal
