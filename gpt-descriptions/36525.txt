Synopsis: 
In "Notes on Recent Researches in Electricity and Magnetism: Intended as a Sequel to Professor Clerk-Maxwell's Treatise on Electricity and Magnetism," J.J. Thomson delves into the fascinating world of electricity and magnetism, building upon the groundbreaking work of Professor Clerk-Maxwell. Through a series of insightful observations and experiments, Thomson uncovers new discoveries and sheds light on the intricate relationship between these two fundamental forces of nature. From the exploration of electromagnetic waves to the understanding of electric currents, this sequel offers a captivating journey into the realm of electricity and magnetism.

Genres: 
Science, Non-fiction

Themes: 
Electromagnetism, Scientific discovery
