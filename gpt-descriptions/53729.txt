Synopsis: 
In "In Partnership: Studies in story-telling," Bunner and Matthews take readers on a captivating journey through the art of storytelling. Through a collection of thought-provoking essays and analyses, they delve into the intricacies of narrative techniques, character development, and the power of storytelling itself. From exploring the impact of dialogue to dissecting the essence of a well-crafted plot, this book offers invaluable insights into the craft of storytelling that will leave readers inspired and eager to embark on their own creative endeavors.

Genres: Literary criticism, Non-fiction, Writing

Themes: Storytelling, Narrative techniques, Character development, Creative writing
