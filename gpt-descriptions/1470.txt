Synopsis: In "The Fall of the Moghul Empire of Hindustan," Keene takes readers on a captivating journey through the tumultuous decline of one of history's most powerful empires. Set against the backdrop of 18th-century India, this gripping narrative follows the lives of key figures who shaped the destiny of the Moghul Empire. As political intrigue, treachery, and external threats mount, the empire's foundations begin to crumble, leading to a dramatic climax that forever alters the course of Hindustan's history.

Genres: Historical fiction, Drama, Adventure

Themes: Power and corruption, Loyalty and betrayal, Cultural clashes, Historical legacy
