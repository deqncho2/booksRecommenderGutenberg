Synopsis: In the ancient city of Carthage, a young priestess named Salammbo becomes entangled in a dangerous web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and the thirst for power. As she navigates the treacherous world of war and betrayal, Salammbo finds herself torn between her duty to her people and her own desires. With vivid descriptions of opulent palaces, epic battles, and mystical rituals, 'Salammbo' is a captivating tale that explores the depths of human passion and the consequences of unchecked ambition.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Power and politics, Forbidden love, Loyalty and betrayal
