Synopsis: 
'Officer 666' is a thrilling and humorous mystery novel that follows the misadventures of a bumbling police officer who finds himself caught up in a web of crime and mistaken identity. When Officer 666 is assigned to protect a wealthy heiress, he unwittingly becomes entangled in a dangerous plot that could cost him his job and even his life. With a mix of slapstick comedy and suspenseful twists, this fast-paced story will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Comedy, Crime

Themes: Mistaken identity, Crime and investigation, Humor
