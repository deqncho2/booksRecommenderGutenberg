Synopsis: In the small, picturesque town of Pleasantville, a series of mysterious deaths occur, leaving the community in shock and fear. As the local police struggle to solve the cases, a group of unlikely amateur detectives, each with their own secrets and motives, band together to uncover the truth behind the happy homicides. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, The darkness beneath a seemingly idyllic facade
