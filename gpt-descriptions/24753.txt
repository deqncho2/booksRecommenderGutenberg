Synopsis: In 'Who Are Happiest? and Other Stories', Arthur, T. S. takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking tales. From the struggles of a young artist to the complexities of love and the pursuit of happiness, each story delves into the depths of human emotions and the choices we make. With unexpected twists and poignant moments, this collection will leave readers pondering the true meaning of happiness and the paths we take to find it.

Genres: Fiction, Short Stories

Themes: Happiness, Love, Choices, Human Emotions
