Synopsis: In 'Boy Scouts in the Canal Zone; Or, The Plot Against Uncle Sam', a group of adventurous Boy Scouts find themselves embroiled in a dangerous conspiracy while on a trip to the Panama Canal Zone. As they navigate through treacherous jungles and encounter mysterious characters, they uncover a plot that threatens the security of their country. With their resourcefulness and bravery, the Boy Scouts must work together to foil the sinister plan and protect Uncle Sam.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Patriotism, Courage, Teamwork
