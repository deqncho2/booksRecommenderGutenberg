Synopsis: In 'His Master's Voice' by Randall Garrett, a brilliant scientist is tasked with decoding a mysterious message from outer space. As he delves deeper into the enigmatic transmission, he uncovers a web of secrets, conspiracies, and unexpected consequences that could change the course of humanity forever. With time running out, he must navigate the treacherous waters of politics, science, and personal ambition to unlock the truth behind the message and its implications for mankind.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Communication, Extraterrestrial Life, Power Dynamics, Ethics
