Synopsis: In 'Carnival' by MacKenzie, Compton, a small town is transformed when a mysterious carnival arrives, bringing with it a sense of wonder and excitement. As the townspeople indulge in the carnival's attractions, they soon discover that there is more to this spectacle than meets the eye. Secrets, desires, and hidden agendas begin to unravel, leading to unexpected consequences that will forever change the lives of those involved. With a cast of intriguing characters and a captivating atmosphere, 'Carnival' takes readers on a thrilling journey through the dark underbelly of a seemingly innocent event.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Temptation, Consequences
