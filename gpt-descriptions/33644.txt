Synopsis: In 'The Secret of the Ninth Planet' by Donald A. Wollheim, a group of young scientists embarks on a thrilling space adventure to uncover the mysteries of the ninth planet in our solar system. As they journey through the vastness of space, they encounter strange phenomena and encounter a race against time to prevent a catastrophic event that could change the fate of humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Scientific Discovery, Friendship, Survival
