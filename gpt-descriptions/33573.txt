Synopsis: In 'The Progressionists, and Angela,' Conrad von Bolanden weaves a captivating tale that follows the lives of a group of individuals known as the Progressionists, and the enigmatic Angela who becomes entangled in their world. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, alliances are tested, and the characters are forced to confront their own desires and ambitions. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping narrative explores the complexities of human nature and the power of choice.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Drama

Themes: Identity, Betrayal, Redemption
