Synopsis: In 'The Lucky Seventh' by Ralph Henry Barbour, a group of young boys from different backgrounds come together to form a baseball team. As they navigate the challenges of adolescence and the pressures of competition, they discover the true meaning of friendship, loyalty, and the power of teamwork. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, this coming-of-age story will keep readers on the edge of their seats, rooting for the underdogs until the very end.

Genres: Sports fiction, Coming-of-age, Friendship

Themes: Teamwork, Friendship, Adolescence, Overcoming challenges
