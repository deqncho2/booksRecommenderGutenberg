Synopsis: In the eerie moors of Dartmoor, a curse haunts the Baskerville family, claiming the lives of its heirs. When Sir Charles Baskerville is found dead under mysterious circumstances, the legendary detective Sherlock Holmes and his loyal companion Dr. John Watson are called upon to solve the chilling case. As they delve into the secrets of the Baskerville estate, they encounter a terrifying supernatural hound that seems to be the embodiment of the curse. With danger lurking at every turn, Holmes and Watson must unravel the truth behind the curse before it claims its next victim.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Gothic Fiction

Themes: The supernatural, the power of fear, the conflict between reason and superstition
