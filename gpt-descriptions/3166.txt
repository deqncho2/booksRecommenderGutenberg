Synopsis:
In the small village of Greshamsbury, secrets, scandals, and social hierarchies intertwine as the lives of the aristocratic Gresham family and the humble Doctor Thorne become entangled. As the Greshams face financial ruin and struggle to maintain their social standing, the steadfast Doctor Thorne finds himself torn between loyalty to his friends and the pursuit of love. With a cast of vibrant characters, including a spirited young woman and a charming but reckless heir, 'Doctor Thorne' is a captivating tale of love, ambition, and the clash between tradition and progress.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Social Commentary

Themes:
Social class, Love and marriage, Morality, Family dynamics
