Synopsis: In 'The Eye of Zeitoon' by Talbot Mundy, a young American engineer finds himself caught in the midst of a dangerous adventure in the rugged mountains of Armenia. Drawn into a web of intrigue and ancient secrets, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter mysterious characters, and uncover the truth behind the legendary Eye of Zeitoon. As he delves deeper into the heart of the conflict, he discovers that the fate of an entire nation may rest in his hands.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Courage, Betrayal, Cultural Identity, Redemption
