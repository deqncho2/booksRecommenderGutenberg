Synopsis: In 'Supply and Demand' by Henderson, Hubert Douglas, Sir, a brilliant economist finds himself caught in a web of intrigue and deception when he stumbles upon a secret that could disrupt the global economy. As he races against time to uncover the truth, he must navigate through a dangerous world of power, greed, and hidden agendas. With each twist and turn, the stakes rise higher, and the line between friend and foe becomes increasingly blurred. Will he be able to expose the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Economics, Power, Deception
