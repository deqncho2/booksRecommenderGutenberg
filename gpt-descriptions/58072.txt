Synopsis: In this thought-provoking sermon, James Henry Alexander Gwyther explores the crucial role of unity within the Church for the conversion of the world. Preached before the Directors and Friends of the London Missionary Society, Gwyther delves into the power of collective action and the impact it can have on spreading the message of Christianity. With compelling arguments and insightful anecdotes, he challenges his audience to consider the significance of unity in their mission to bring about global transformation.

Genres: Sermon, Religious Literature

Themes: Unity, Conversion, Missionary Work
