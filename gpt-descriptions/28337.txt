Synopsis: In 'Hushed Up! A Mystery of London' by William Le Queux, a young journalist named Hugh Moneylaw stumbles upon a perplexing secret that leads him into a web of intrigue and danger in the heart of London. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he uncovers a sinister plot that threatens the very fabric of society. With his life on the line, Hugh must navigate the treacherous underworld of the city, where nothing is as it seems and trust is a luxury he cannot afford. Will he be able to unravel the truth before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Intrigue, Conspiracy, Betrayal
