Synopsis: 

In "Helena Brett's Career" by Coke, Desmond, we are introduced to the ambitious and determined Helena Brett, a young woman who embarks on a journey to establish herself in the male-dominated world of business. As she navigates through various challenges and encounters unexpected opportunities, Helena's career takes her on a thrilling and unpredictable path. With her intelligence, resilience, and unwavering determination, Helena strives to overcome societal expectations and prove her worth in a world that constantly underestimates her.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Ambition, Gender equality, Resilience, Societal expectations
