Synopsis: In 'How It Feels to Be Fifty' by Butler, Ellis Parker, we follow the life of a middle-aged man who finds himself at a crossroads as he reaches the milestone of turning fifty. As he navigates the challenges and joys of this new phase, he reflects on his past, contemplates his future, and discovers unexpected truths about himself and the world around him. With humor, wit, and a touch of nostalgia, this poignant tale explores the complexities of aging, the pursuit of happiness, and the power of self-discovery.

Genres: Fiction, Comedy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Aging, Self-reflection, Life transitions, Personal growth
