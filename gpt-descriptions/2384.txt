Synopsis: In 'The Deliverance: A Romance of the Virginia Tobacco Fields', Ellen Anderson Gholson Glasgow takes us on a captivating journey through the lush tobacco fields of Virginia. The story follows the lives of two young women, Dorinda Oakley and Janet Ruffin, who find themselves entangled in a web of love, desire, and societal expectations. As they navigate the complexities of their relationships and the constraints of their time, they must confront their own desires and the consequences of their choices. Set against the backdrop of the changing South, this novel explores the power dynamics, social conventions, and personal struggles that shape the lives of its characters.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Southern Literature

Themes: Love and Desire, Gender Roles, Social Expectations, Personal Freedom
