Synopsis: In Stephen H. Branch's Alligator, Vol. 1 no. 23, September 25, 1858, a mysterious event unfolds in a small town, leaving its residents bewildered and on edge. As the townspeople grapple with the aftermath, secrets are unearthed, alliances are tested, and the true nature of human behavior is revealed. With its gripping narrative and unexpected twists, this captivating work will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Human nature, Small-town dynamics
