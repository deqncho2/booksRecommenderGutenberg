Synopsis: 
In 'Debussy's Pelléas et Mélisande: A Guide to the Opera with Musical Examples from the Score', Lawrence Gilman takes readers on a captivating journey through one of the most enchanting and mysterious operas of all time. With insightful analysis and musical examples, Gilman explores the intricate web of emotions, relationships, and secrets that unfold in Debussy's masterpiece. From the ethereal melodies to the hauntingly beautiful libretto, this guide offers a deep dive into the world of Pelléas et Mélisande, leaving readers eager to experience the opera's spellbinding magic for themselves.

Genres: Music, Opera, Music Theory

Themes: Love and desire, Fate and destiny, Secrets and hidden truths
