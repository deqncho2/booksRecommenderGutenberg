Synopsis: 
'Penrod and Sam' by Booth Tarkington is a delightful coming-of-age story that follows the misadventures of two mischievous boys, Penrod Schofield and Sam Williams, as they navigate the challenges of childhood in early 20th century America. From their hilarious pranks to their attempts at impressing girls, Penrod and Sam's escapades will keep readers entertained and nostalgic for their own youthful adventures.

Genres: 
Children's literature, Humor, Fiction

Themes: 
Friendship, Innocence, Growing up
