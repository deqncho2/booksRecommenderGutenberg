Synopsis: 

In 'Best Laid Schemes' by Nicholson Meredith, a seemingly perfect life takes a dark turn when a young woman's carefully constructed plans begin to unravel. As secrets are revealed and unexpected events unfold, the protagonist finds herself caught in a web of deceit and danger. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning who can be trusted and what lies beneath the surface of this gripping tale.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Suspense

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Identity, Consequences
