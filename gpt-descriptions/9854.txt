Synopsis: 
In 'Frank Roscoe's Secret; Or, the Darewell Chums in the Woods' by Chapman, Allen, a group of adventurous friends stumble upon a hidden secret during their exploration of the woods. As they uncover clues and face unexpected challenges, they find themselves entangled in a thrilling mystery that could change their lives forever. With danger lurking around every corner, the Darewell Chums must rely on their friendship and resourcefulness to unravel the truth and protect what they hold dear.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Friendship

Themes: Friendship, Exploration, Secrets, Courage
