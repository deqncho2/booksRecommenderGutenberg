Synopsis: In 'The Accumulation of Capital', Rosa Luxemburg takes readers on a captivating journey through the intricate web of global capitalism. Through her insightful analysis, Luxemburg explores the mechanisms that drive the expansion of capital and the consequences it has on societies around the world. As she delves into the dynamics of imperialism, trade, and finance, Luxemburg uncovers the hidden forces that shape the modern world. With a keen eye for detail and a powerful voice, Luxemburg challenges conventional economic theories and offers a fresh perspective on the complex interplay between capitalism and imperialism.

Genres: Political theory, Economic analysis, Historical critique

Themes: Capitalism, Imperialism, Globalization, Exploitation, Economic crises
