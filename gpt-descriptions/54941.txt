Synopsis: In 'Monica: A Novel, Volume 2 (of 3)' by Evelyn Everett-Green, the captivating story continues as Monica, a young woman with a mysterious past, finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and unexpected alliances. As she navigates the treacherous waters of love, betrayal, and redemption, Monica's resilience and determination are put to the ultimate test. With each turn of the page, the plot thickens, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Monica's enigmatic past and the choices she must make to secure her future.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Redemption, Love and Betrayal
