Synopsis: 

In 'Off Course' by Reynolds, Mack, a young woman named Lily finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she stumbles upon a mysterious journal. As she delves deeper into its pages, she becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, unsure of who she can trust. With each twist and turn, Lily's life takes an unexpected detour, leading her down a path she never could have imagined.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Betrayal, Identity, Trust
