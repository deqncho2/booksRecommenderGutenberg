Synopsis: In "Make your own hats" by Martin, Gene Allen, a young aspiring fashion designer named Lily discovers a hidden treasure trove of vintage hat-making materials in her grandmother's attic. With the help of her quirky friends, Lily embarks on a creative journey to revive the lost art of hat-making and bring her unique designs to life. As she delves deeper into the world of millinery, Lily uncovers family secrets and learns valuable lessons about self-expression, following her dreams, and the power of embracing one's own individuality.

Genres: Contemporary Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Fashion

Themes: Self-expression, Creativity, Family, Identity
