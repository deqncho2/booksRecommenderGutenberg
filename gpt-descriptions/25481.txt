Synopsis: In 'Little Grandfather' by May, Sophie, a young girl named Lily discovers a hidden world within her grandfather's antique pocket watch. As she embarks on a magical adventure, she encounters talking animals, enchanted forests, and a mysterious figure who holds the key to her family's past. With each step, Lily unravels the secrets of her lineage and learns the true power of love and courage. This captivating tale will transport readers to a whimsical realm where imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Family, Identity, Courage, Magic
