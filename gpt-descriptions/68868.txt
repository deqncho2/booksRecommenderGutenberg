Synopsis: In 'Bulldog' by Max Brand, a mysterious gunslinger with a reputation for being unbeatable arrives in a small western town. As he becomes entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and revenge, the town's residents soon realize that this stranger may be their only hope for survival. With his quick draw and unwavering determination, the gunslinger must navigate through a dangerous landscape where loyalty is scarce and danger lurks around every corner. As the tension rises and secrets are revealed, the ultimate showdown becomes inevitable, leaving the reader on the edge of their seat until the very last page.

Genres: Western, Action, Mystery

Themes: Redemption, Justice, Betrayal
