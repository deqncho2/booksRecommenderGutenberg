Synopsis: 
'The Visions of the Sleeping Bard' by Wynne, Ellis is a captivating collection of Welsh folklore and mythology. The book takes readers on a mesmerizing journey through the dreams and visions of a sleeping bard, revealing tales of ancient heroes, magical creatures, and mystical landscapes. As the bard's visions unfold, the reader is transported to a world filled with enchantment, adventure, and the power of storytelling. With each story, the line between reality and fantasy becomes blurred, leaving the reader questioning the true nature of the bard's visions and their significance in the modern world.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Fantasy

Themes: Dreams and visions, Welsh culture and heritage, the power of storytelling
