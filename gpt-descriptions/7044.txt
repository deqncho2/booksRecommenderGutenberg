Synopsis: 

"In the Courts of Memory, 1858-1875; from Contemporary Letters" takes readers on a captivating journey through the intriguing world of European high society during the mid-19th century. Through a collection of contemporary letters, Lillie de Hegermann-Lindencrone unveils a web of scandal, secrets, and political intrigue that unfolds within the opulent courts of Europe. As the reader delves into the lives of the aristocracy, they are drawn into a world of forbidden love, power struggles, and the struggle for personal identity. With each turn of the page, the reader becomes entangled in a captivating narrative that reveals the hidden desires and ambitions of the elite, leaving them yearning for more.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Epistolary Novel, Romance

Themes: Love and Desire, Power and Politics, Identity and Self-Discovery
