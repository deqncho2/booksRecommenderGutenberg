Synopsis: In 'The King's Sons' by George Manville Fenn, a young prince finds himself caught in a web of treachery and danger when he uncovers a dark secret about his own family. As he navigates through a world of political intrigue and deceit, he must rely on his wit and courage to protect himself and those he loves. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding suspense, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Betrayal, Loyalty, Power
