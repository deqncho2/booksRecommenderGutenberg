Synopsis: In "The suppressed Gospels and Epistles of the original New Testament of Jesus the Christ, Volume 1, Mary," author William Wake delves into the lesser-known texts that were excluded from the traditional New Testament. Focusing on the figure of Mary, the mother of Jesus, this volume uncovers a wealth of intriguing stories and teachings that have long been suppressed. From her early life to her role in Jesus' ministry and beyond, this book sheds light on the enigmatic and often misunderstood figure of Mary, offering a fresh perspective on her significance in the Christian narrative.

Genres: Religious, Historical, Non-fiction

Themes: Biblical interpretation, Feminine spirituality, Hidden teachings
