Synopsis: In 'Between Friends' by Robert W. Chambers, two childhood friends, Hugh and Jack, find themselves reunited after years apart. As they navigate the complexities of adulthood, their bond is tested by secrets, betrayals, and a shared love interest. Set against the backdrop of New York City's vibrant social scene, this gripping tale explores the power of friendship and the consequences of choices made in the pursuit of love and success.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Friendship, Betrayal, Love, Ambition
