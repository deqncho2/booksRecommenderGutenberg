Synopsis: In 'Citadel of Faith' by Shoghi Effendi, a captivating tale unfolds as a young protagonist embarks on a transformative journey through the intricate tapestry of the Bahá'í Faith. Set against a backdrop of historical events and spiritual revelations, the story delves into the protagonist's quest for truth, love, and purpose, leading to unexpected encounters and profound insights. As the protagonist navigates the challenges of faith and the complexities of human nature, the narrative weaves together elements of mystery, adventure, and self-discovery, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to uncover the ultimate destination of this spiritual odyssey.

Genres: Fiction, Historical, Spiritual

Themes: Faith, Truth, Love, Purpose, Self-discovery
