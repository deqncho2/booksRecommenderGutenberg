Synopsis: In 'The Delinquent (Vol. IV, No. 3, March 1914)', a mysterious figure emerges from the shadows, wreaking havoc on the unsuspecting residents of a small town. As the townspeople grapple with fear and uncertainty, a determined detective sets out to uncover the truth behind the delinquent's actions. With each twist and turn, the story delves deeper into the dark secrets that bind the community, leading to a shocking revelation that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Crime and punishment, Secrets and deception
