Synopsis: In 'At the Black Rocks' by Edward A. Rand, a small coastal town is plagued by a series of mysterious disappearances. As the community becomes increasingly fearful, a young journalist sets out to uncover the truth behind these unsettling events. With each revelation, the journalist delves deeper into a dark and enigmatic world, where secrets lurk beneath the surface and danger awaits at every turn. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Fear, Investigation
