Synopsis: In the post-apocalyptic world of 'Dead World', a group of survivors must navigate through a desolate landscape filled with danger and uncertainty. As they struggle to find safety and meaning in their new reality, they uncover dark secrets that threaten to unravel their already fragile existence. With each step they take, the line between life and death becomes increasingly blurred, forcing them to confront their deepest fears and question what it truly means to be alive.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Horror

Themes: Survival, Morality, Identity
