Synopsis: In "Abraham Lincoln: The People's Leader in the Struggle for National Existence," George Haven Putnam delves into the life and leadership of one of America's most iconic figures. Through meticulous research and captivating storytelling, Putnam paints a vivid portrait of Abraham Lincoln, exploring his rise from humble beginnings to becoming the President who guided the nation through its most challenging times. From his early political career to his pivotal role in the Civil War, this book offers a compelling narrative that showcases Lincoln's unwavering commitment to preserving the Union and his unwavering dedication to the American people.

Genres: Biography, History, Politics

Themes: Leadership, National Identity, Civil War, American History
