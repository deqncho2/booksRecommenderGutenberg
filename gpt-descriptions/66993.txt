Synopsis: 

In "Historical Record of the Seventy-Second Regiment, or the Duke of Albany's Own Highlanders," Richard Cannon takes us on a captivating journey through the formation and subsequent services of this renowned regiment from 1778 to 1848. From its humble beginnings to its remarkable achievements, Cannon delves into the triumphs, challenges, and sacrifices of the soldiers who proudly served under the Duke of Albany's banner. With vivid descriptions and meticulous research, this historical record offers a fascinating glimpse into the regiment's battles, campaigns, and the individuals who shaped its legacy.

Genres: Historical Non-fiction, Military History

Themes: Patriotism, Loyalty, Bravery, Military Strategy, Camaraderie
