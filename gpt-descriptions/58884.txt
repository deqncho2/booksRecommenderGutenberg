Synopsis: 
In the small English village of Ramridge, the Osbornes, a seemingly ordinary family, lead a quiet and uneventful life. However, when a mysterious stranger arrives in town, their lives take an unexpected turn. Secrets are revealed, tensions rise, and the Osbornes find themselves caught in a web of intrigue and danger. As the truth unfolds, the family must confront their past and make choices that will forever change their future.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Family Drama

Themes: Secrets and deception, Family bonds, Consequences of the past
