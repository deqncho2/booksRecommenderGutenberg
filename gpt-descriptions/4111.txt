Synopsis: In the fourth volume of "Christopher Columbus and the New World of His Discovery," Filson Young takes readers on a captivating journey through the later years of Columbus' life and his continued exploration of the New World. From his encounters with indigenous peoples to his struggles with political intrigue and personal challenges, this book delves into the complex and often controversial legacy of the renowned explorer. As Columbus faces new obstacles and navigates uncharted territories, readers will be enthralled by the triumphs and tribulations that shape his extraordinary story.

Genres: Historical biography, Adventure, Exploration

Themes: Exploration and discovery, Cultural encounters, Legacy and historical impact
