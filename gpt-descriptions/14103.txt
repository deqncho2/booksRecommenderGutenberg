Synopsis: 
In 'Prose Fancies (Second Series)', Richard Le Gallienne takes readers on a captivating journey through a collection of imaginative and thought-provoking essays. From exploring the enchanting world of dreams to contemplating the beauty of nature, Le Gallienne's prose transports readers to a realm where reality and fantasy intertwine. With his eloquent and lyrical writing style, he delves into the depths of human emotions, pondering the mysteries of love, longing, and the passage of time. Each essay is a unique exploration of the human experience, inviting readers to reflect on their own lives and the profound beauty that surrounds them.

Genres: Essay, Literary Fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Imagination, Nature, Love, Time, Human Experience
