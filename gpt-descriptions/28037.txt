Synopsis: In "In Doublet and Hose: A Story for Girls" by Lucy Foster Madison, a young girl named Alice finds herself transported back in time to the Elizabethan era. As she navigates this unfamiliar world, she must disguise herself as a boy and join a traveling theater troupe. Along the way, she encounters adventure, romance, and the challenges of living a double life. Will Alice be able to maintain her secret identity and find her way back home?

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Identity, Gender roles, Time travel
