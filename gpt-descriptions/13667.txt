Synopsis: 
'Bog-Myrtle and Peat: Tales Chiefly of Galloway Gathered from the Years 1889 to 1895' is a collection of captivating stories set in the enchanting region of Galloway. Written by S. R. Crockett, this book takes readers on a journey through the rugged landscapes and rich folklore of the area. From tales of love and loss to stories of adventure and mystery, each narrative is intricately woven with vivid descriptions and memorable characters. As the pages turn, readers will find themselves immersed in the timeless charm and captivating allure of Galloway.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Folklore

Themes: Love and Loss, Adventure, Mystery, Folklore and Legends
