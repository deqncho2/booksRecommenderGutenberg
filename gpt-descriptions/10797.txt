Synopsis: In 'The Wits and Beaux of Society. Volume 2', the authors take readers on a captivating journey through the glamorous and scandalous world of high society in the 18th century. Through a series of witty and satirical sketches, they introduce us to a colorful cast of characters, from charming rakes and fashionable ladies to eccentric artists and ambitious social climbers. As the stories unfold, secrets are revealed, reputations are at stake, and love and betrayal intertwine in unexpected ways. With its sharp observations and clever humor, this volume offers a delightful glimpse into the intrigues and follies of the upper echelons of society.

Genres: Satire, Comedy, Historical Fiction

Themes: Social class, Love and relationships, Morality, Satire of societal norms
