Synopsis: In "Miss Pat at School" by Ginther Pemberton, follow the captivating journey of Miss Pat as she navigates the challenges and triumphs of her first year as a teacher. From unruly students to unexpected friendships, Miss Pat's determination and passion for education shine through as she strives to make a difference in the lives of her students. With heartwarming moments and unexpected twists, this story will keep readers eagerly turning the pages, wondering what lies ahead for Miss Pat and her students.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Education

Themes: Perseverance, Friendship, Empathy, Growth
