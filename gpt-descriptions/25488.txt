Synopsis: In 'The Grocery Man And Peck's Bad Boy: Peck's Bad Boy and His Pa, No. 2 - 1883', George W. Peck takes readers on a hilarious and mischievous adventure with the lovable troublemaker, Peck's Bad Boy, and his long-suffering father. When the mischievous boy encounters the local grocery man, chaos ensues as he unleashes his pranks and antics, leaving his poor pa to deal with the consequences. From practical jokes to outrageous schemes, this delightful tale will keep readers laughing and eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Humor, Fiction

Themes: Family dynamics, Mischief and pranks
