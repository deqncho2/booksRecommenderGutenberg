Synopsis: In 'My First Campaign' by Grant, Joseph W., a young and ambitious political novice finds himself thrust into the cutthroat world of election campaigns. As he navigates the treacherous waters of dirty politics, scandalous secrets, and unexpected alliances, he must learn to balance his ideals with the harsh realities of the political landscape. With each twist and turn, he discovers that winning isn't just about votes, but also about the sacrifices one must make to achieve their goals.

Genres: Political thriller, coming-of-age, drama

Themes: Ambition, morality, power, corruption
