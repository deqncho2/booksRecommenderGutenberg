Synopsis: In 'Motor Matt's Mandarin; or, Turning a Trick for Tsan Ti' by Stanley R. Matthews, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as Motor Matt, the young and daring automobile enthusiast, finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger. When a wealthy Mandarin's prized possession is stolen, Motor Matt is called upon to solve the perplexing case and recover the stolen item. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he encounters unexpected allies and formidable adversaries, leading him on a race against time to uncover the truth and outsmart the cunning criminals. With his quick thinking, resourcefulness, and unwavering determination, Motor Matt proves that he is not just a skilled driver, but also a formidable detective.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Crime-solving, Courage, Friendship
