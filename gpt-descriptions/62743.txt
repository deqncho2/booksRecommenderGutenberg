Synopsis: 
In 'Grace Harlowe's Overland Riders at Circle O Ranch', Grace and her friends embark on an exciting adventure as they join a cattle drive at the Circle O Ranch. As they navigate the rugged terrain and face various challenges, they uncover a mysterious secret that puts their lives in danger. With their courage and determination, the Overland Riders must unravel the truth and protect themselves before it's too late.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Discovery
