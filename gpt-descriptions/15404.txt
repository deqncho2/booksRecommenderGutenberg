Synopsis: In this captivating issue of 'The Great Round World and What Is Going On In It', readers are taken on a thrilling journey through the latest news and events of 1897. From mysterious disappearances to groundbreaking scientific discoveries, this weekly magazine for boys and girls offers a fascinating blend of adventure, knowledge, and entertainment. Delve into the pages of this volume to uncover the secrets of the world and be swept away by the excitement of the unknown.

Genres: Historical, Adventure, Educational

Themes: Exploration, Curiosity, Discovery
