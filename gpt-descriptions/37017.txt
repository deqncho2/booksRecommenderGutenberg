Synopsis: In 'The Comforts of Home' by Ralph Bergengren, a seemingly ordinary house becomes the setting for a series of mysterious and unsettling events. As the protagonist delves deeper into the secrets hidden within the walls, they uncover a dark history that threatens to consume them. With each twist and turn, the line between reality and illusion blurs, leaving the reader questioning what is truly lurking in the comforts of home.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Perception vs. reality
