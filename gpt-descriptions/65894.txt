Synopsis: 
In 'Beyond the Law' by Gregory Jackson, a former detective turned vigilante finds himself entangled in a web of corruption and deceit when he takes justice into his own hands. As he delves deeper into the criminal underworld, he uncovers shocking secrets that threaten to destroy everything he holds dear. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous path where the line between right and wrong becomes blurred, and the consequences of his actions become increasingly dire.

Genres: Crime, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Vigilantism, Corruption, Morality
