Synopsis:
'Camilla; or, A Picture of Youth' by Fanny Burney is a captivating novel that follows the life of the young and spirited Camilla Tyrold. Set in the late 18th century, Camilla finds herself navigating the complexities of love, friendship, and societal expectations. As she encounters various suitors and faces numerous challenges, Camilla must make difficult choices that will shape her future. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this novel takes readers on a journey filled with romance, intrigue, and unexpected twists.

Genres:
Romance, Coming-of-age, Historical fiction

Themes:
Love and relationships, Social class and expectations, Personal growth and self-discovery
