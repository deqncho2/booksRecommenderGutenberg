Synopsis: In the small town of Pine Needles, secrets are buried deep within the community, waiting to be unearthed. When a mysterious newcomer arrives, the town's residents find themselves caught in a web of deception, betrayal, and unexpected connections. As the truth slowly unravels, the lives of these individuals become intertwined in ways they never could have imagined, leading to shocking revelations and life-altering decisions.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Thriller

Themes: Secrets and lies, Small-town dynamics, Redemption, Identity
