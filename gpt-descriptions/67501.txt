Synopsis: 
In 'Adolescence' by Paget, Stephen, we are introduced to a group of teenagers navigating the complexities of growing up in a small town. As they grapple with the challenges of identity, friendship, and first love, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways. Secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the characters are forced to confront the harsh realities of adulthood looming on the horizon. With its compelling characters and poignant storytelling, 'Adolescence' takes readers on an emotional journey through the tumultuous years of adolescence.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Drama, Romance

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Love, Coming-of-age
