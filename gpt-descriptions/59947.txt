Synopsis: 
Embark on an exciting journey through history and culture with the 'Hubbell Trading Post National Historic Site: Junior Ranger Workbook'. Join the Junior Ranger team as they explore the fascinating world of the Hubbell Trading Post, a place where Native American traditions and the Old West collide. Through engaging activities and interactive challenges, readers will uncover the secrets of this historic site, from its origins as a trading post to its significance in preserving Native American art and culture. Discover the stories of the people who lived and worked at the trading post, and learn about the unique items that were traded and sold. With each page, readers will be transported back in time, immersing themselves in the rich heritage of the American Southwest. 

Genres: Educational, Historical, Activity Book

Themes: Native American culture, History, Preservation
