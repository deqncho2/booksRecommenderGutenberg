Synopsis: In 'Ardath: The Story of a Dead Self' by Marie Corelli, a mysterious and captivating tale unfolds as a young man embarks on a journey of self-discovery. Drawn into a world of ancient secrets and hidden truths, he encounters a mesmerizing woman who holds the key to his past and future. As he delves deeper into the enigma surrounding Ardath, he must confront his own inner demons and make choices that will shape his destiny. With elements of romance, mystery, and the supernatural, this gripping novel takes readers on a thrilling and thought-provoking adventure.

Genres: Gothic fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Self-discovery, Fate and destiny, Supernatural forces
