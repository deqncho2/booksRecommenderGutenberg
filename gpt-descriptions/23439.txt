Synopsis: In 'Attention Saint Patrick' by Leinster, Murray, a young archaeologist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to an ancient secret. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes entangled in a dangerous race against time, facing unexpected adversaries and uncovering a truth that could change history forever. With thrilling twists and turns, this gripping adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Archaeology, Ancient Secrets, Pursuit of Knowledge
