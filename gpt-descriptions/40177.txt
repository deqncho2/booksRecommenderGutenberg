Synopsis: In the small town of Carterville, four sisters navigate the complexities of love, family, and secrets that threaten to tear them apart. As they each face their own personal challenges, they must come together to uncover the truth and protect their bond. With unexpected twists and heartwarming moments, 'The Carter Girls' is a captivating tale of sisterhood and resilience that will keep readers hooked until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Women's Fiction, Family Drama

Themes: Sisterhood, Love, Secrets, Resilience
