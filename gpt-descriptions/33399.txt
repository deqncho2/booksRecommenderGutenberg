Synopsis: 
In 'A Romance in Transit' by Lynde Francis, a chance encounter on a train sets off a whirlwind romance that transcends time and space. As the two strangers navigate the complexities of their own lives, they find solace and passion in each other's company, creating a bond that defies logic and reason. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale explores the power of love and the transformative nature of human connection.

Genres: Romance, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Love, Fate, Serendipity, Identity
