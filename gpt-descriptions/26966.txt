Synopsis: 
In 'A Place in the Sun' by Stephen Marlowe, a young and ambitious journalist, Tom, finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change the course of history. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he becomes the target of powerful forces determined to keep the truth buried. With his life on the line, Tom must navigate a treacherous path, uncovering shocking revelations and facing unexpected allies and enemies along the way. This gripping tale of suspense and intrigue will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, Power and corruption
