Synopsis: In 'Mrs. Leary's Cow: A Legend of Chicago', C. C. Hine takes us back to the infamous Great Chicago Fire of 1871. Through the eyes of a young girl named Maggie, we witness the chaos and devastation that ensues when a simple act of carelessness leads to a city in flames. As the fire rages on, Maggie finds herself caught up in a thrilling adventure, where she must navigate the burning streets, encounter unexpected allies, and confront the true power of community in the face of disaster. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover the fate of Mrs. Leary's cow and the city of Chicago.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Resilience, Community, Survival
