Synopsis: 

'Jap Herron: A Novel Written from the Ouija Board' is a captivating and enigmatic literary work that delves into the mysterious realm of the supernatural. The story revolves around a deceased author who communicates with the living through a Ouija board, revealing a tale that challenges the boundaries of life and death. As the narrative unfolds, the reader is drawn into a world where the line between reality and the afterlife becomes blurred, leaving them questioning the nature of existence itself. 

Genres: Supernatural fiction, Mystery, Literary fiction

Themes: Life and death, Communication with the afterlife, Existentialism
