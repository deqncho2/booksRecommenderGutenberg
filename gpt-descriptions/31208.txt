Synopsis: In 'Collectivum' by Mike Lewis, a mysterious organization known as the Collectivum offers individuals the chance to escape their mundane lives and join a hidden world of extraordinary abilities. As the protagonist delves deeper into this enigmatic society, they uncover dark secrets and face dangerous challenges that test their loyalty and reshape their understanding of reality. With thrilling twists and a captivating narrative, 'Collectivum' takes readers on a thrilling journey through a hidden world where nothing is as it seems.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Power, Betrayal
