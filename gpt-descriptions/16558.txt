Synopsis: 
'From the Ranks' by Charles King is a captivating military novel that follows the journey of a young soldier named Jack, who rises through the ranks of the army during the late 19th century. From his humble beginnings as a lowly private, Jack faces numerous challenges, battles, and personal sacrifices as he strives to prove his worth and earn the respect of his comrades. Along the way, he encounters love, friendship, and betrayal, all set against the backdrop of a nation on the brink of change. As Jack's story unfolds, readers are taken on a thrilling and emotional ride, filled with unexpected twists and turns.

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: 
Ambition, Loyalty, Honor, Coming of Age
