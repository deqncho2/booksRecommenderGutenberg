Synopsis: In 'The Man Who Rose Again' by Joseph Hocking, a mysterious man emerges from the shadows, claiming to be someone who was thought to be dead. As he navigates through a web of secrets and deception, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and revenge. With each twist and turn, the reader is left questioning the true identity and motives of this enigmatic figure. Will he be able to rise again and reclaim what is rightfully his, or will he be consumed by the darkness that surrounds him?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Betrayal
