Synopsis: 

In 'Progress Report', a renowned scientist embarks on a groundbreaking experiment that blurs the line between human and machine. As he delves deeper into his creation, he becomes entangled in a web of moral dilemmas and personal consequences that threaten to unravel his entire existence. With each passing page, the reader is drawn into a world where the boundaries of science and ethics are pushed to their limits, leaving them questioning the very nature of humanity.

Genres: Science fiction, thriller, philosophical fiction

Themes: Artificial intelligence, ethics, identity
