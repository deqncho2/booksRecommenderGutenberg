Synopsis: 
In "Fred Fenton on the Track; Or, The Athletes of Riverport School" by Chapman, Allen, follow the thrilling journey of Fred Fenton as he navigates the competitive world of track and field at Riverport School. As Fred trains alongside his fellow athletes, he faces numerous challenges and unexpected twists that test his determination and resilience. With friendships forged, rivalries ignited, and secrets unveiled, Fred's journey on the track becomes more than just a race to victory. Will Fred be able to overcome the obstacles in his path and emerge as a true champion?

Genres: 
Sports, Young Adult, Adventure

Themes: 
Determination, Friendship, Competition
