Synopsis: In 'The True Story of My Parliamentary Struggle', Charles Bradlaugh takes us on a captivating journey through his relentless battle for political representation. From his humble beginnings to becoming a prominent figure in the British Parliament, Bradlaugh's story is one of unwavering determination, controversy, and the pursuit of justice. As he faces numerous obstacles and confronts societal norms, the reader is left eagerly turning the pages to discover the outcome of his extraordinary struggle.

Genres: Autobiography, Political Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Political activism, Social justice, Personal resilience
