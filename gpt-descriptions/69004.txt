Synopsis: In 'The Trevor Case' by Natalie Sumner Lincoln, a renowned detective is faced with a perplexing mystery when a wealthy businessman is found dead under suspicious circumstances. As she delves deeper into the case, she uncovers a web of secrets, lies, and unexpected connections that lead her down a dangerous path. With time running out, Detective Lincoln must navigate through a maze of deceit to uncover the truth and bring the perpetrator to justice.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Justice
