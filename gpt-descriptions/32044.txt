Synopsis: 
In 'Jimmy Kirkland and the Plot for a Pennant' by Hugh S. Fullerton, a young and talented baseball player named Jimmy Kirkland finds himself caught in a web of deceit and conspiracy when he joins a struggling minor league team. As he strives to prove his worth and help his team win the pennant, Jimmy uncovers a sinister plot that threatens not only his career but also the integrity of the game itself. With the clock ticking, Jimmy must navigate through a maze of secrets, betrayal, and unexpected alliances to expose the truth and save the sport he loves.

Genres: Sports Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Ambition, Integrity, Betrayal, Redemption
