Synopsis: In 'Her Lord and Master' by Martha Morton, a young woman finds herself caught in a web of secrets and desires when she becomes entangled with a mysterious and enigmatic man. As their relationship deepens, she discovers that her new lover holds a dark past and a hidden agenda, forcing her to question her own desires and the true nature of love. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, wondering who truly holds the power in this dangerous game of passion and control.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Power dynamics, Secrets and deception, Forbidden love
