Synopsis: In 'The Velvet Glove' by Henry Seton Merriman, a young Englishman finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love while on a journey through Eastern Europe. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of power struggles and secret societies, he must make difficult choices that will shape his destiny and challenge his beliefs. With its gripping plot and richly drawn characters, this captivating novel takes readers on a thrilling adventure filled with mystery, romance, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Romance

Themes: Power and Corruption, Identity and Self-discovery, Love and Sacrifice
