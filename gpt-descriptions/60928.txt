Synopsis: In 'The Contact Point' by Jack Sharkey, a renowned scientist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of the universe. As he delves deeper into its origins, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of conspiracy and intrigue, where the line between reality and fiction blurs. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must race against time to uncover the truth before it's too late.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Exploration of the unknown, Conspiracy, Human curiosity and ambition
