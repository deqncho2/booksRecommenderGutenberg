Synopsis: 
'Narrative and Legendary Poems, Complete: Volume I of The Works of John Greenleaf Whittier' is a captivating collection of poems by renowned poet John Greenleaf Whittier. This volume takes readers on a journey through various narratives and legends, each filled with vivid imagery and powerful storytelling. From tales of love and loss to historical events and mythical figures, Whittier's poems transport readers to different worlds and evoke a range of emotions. With his lyrical and evocative language, Whittier weaves together stories that will leave readers spellbound and eager for more.

Genres: Poetry, Narrative, Legend

Themes: Love, Loss, History, Mythology
