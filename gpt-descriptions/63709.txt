Synopsis: In 'Prisoner of the Brain-Mistress' by Walton Bryce, a brilliant neuroscientist named Dr. Evelyn Hartman finds herself trapped in a mysterious and dangerous experiment within her own mind. As she navigates through a labyrinth of memories, illusions, and hidden truths, she must confront her deepest fears and unravel the enigma that holds her captive. With time running out, Dr. Hartman must rely on her intellect and resilience to escape the clutches of the enigmatic Brain-Mistress and regain control of her own consciousness.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Science fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Perception, Reality vs. Illusion
