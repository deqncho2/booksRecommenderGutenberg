Synopsis: 
'Index of the Project Gutenberg Works of Louisa M. Alcott' is a comprehensive collection of the works of Louisa May Alcott, one of the most beloved American authors of the 19th century. This index includes a wide range of Alcott's writings, from her famous novel 'Little Women' to lesser-known works such as 'A Garland for Girls' and 'An Old-Fashioned Thanksgiving.' With a diverse array of stories, essays, and poems, this collection offers a glimpse into Alcott's remarkable talent and her ability to capture the essence of human emotions and experiences.

Genres: Fiction, Non-fiction, Children's literature, Poetry

Themes: Family, Coming of age, Women's rights, Social issues, Love and friendship
