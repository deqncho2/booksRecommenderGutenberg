Synopsis: In 'Bombs Awry' by George O. Smith, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking invention that could change the course of warfare forever. However, when his creation falls into the wrong hands, chaos ensues as the line between good and evil becomes blurred. As the protagonist races against time to prevent disaster, unexpected alliances are formed and shocking secrets are revealed, leading to a thrilling climax that will leave readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Action

Themes: Technology and its consequences, Morality and ethics in warfare, Unlikely alliances
