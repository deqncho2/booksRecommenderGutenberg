Synopsis: In 'The Co-opolitan: A Story of the Co-operative Commonwealth of Idaho' by Forbush, Zebina, we are transported to a future where the state of Idaho has transformed into a remarkable society known as the Co-operative Commonwealth. As we follow the protagonist's journey through this utopian world, we encounter a captivating blend of political intrigue, social exploration, and unexpected romance. With each turn of the page, we are drawn deeper into the mysteries and complexities of this extraordinary society, leaving us eager to uncover the secrets that lie beneath the surface.

Genres: Utopian fiction, Political fiction, Romance

Themes: Utopia, Socialism, Equality, Community, Love
