Synopsis: 
When Lady Frances Carfax mysteriously vanishes while traveling through Europe, her concerned friends turn to the brilliant detective Sherlock Holmes for help. As Holmes delves into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and danger that leads him on a thrilling chase across the continent. With each clue he uncovers, Holmes gets closer to unraveling the truth behind Lady Frances Carfax's disappearance, but the clock is ticking, and the stakes are higher than ever.

Genres: Mystery, Detective Fiction, Crime Fiction

Themes: Disappearance, Deception, Investigation, Loyalty
