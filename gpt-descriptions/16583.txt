Synopsis: In 'The Yoke: A Romance of the Days when the Lord Redeemed the Children of Israel from the Bondage of Egypt' by Elizabeth Miller, embark on a captivating journey through ancient Egypt as a young Hebrew slave named Miriam finds herself caught between her loyalty to her people and her forbidden love for an Egyptian prince. As the Israelites endure the harshness of slavery, Miriam's courage and determination are put to the test, leading her on a path of self-discovery, sacrifice, and ultimately, redemption. This enthralling tale weaves together themes of love, faith, and the enduring power of hope.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Religious Fiction

Themes: Love and Sacrifice, Faith and Redemption, Freedom and Liberation
