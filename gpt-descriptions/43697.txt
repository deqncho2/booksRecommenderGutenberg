Synopsis: In "Nelly's First Schooldays" by Josephine Franklin, a young girl named Nelly embarks on her first day of school, filled with excitement and nervousness. As she navigates the unfamiliar environment, she encounters new friends, unexpected challenges, and valuable life lessons. This heartwarming tale explores the joys and anxieties of starting school, capturing the essence of childhood innocence and growth.

Genres: Children's Literature, Coming-of-Age, School Story

Themes: Friendship, Adaptation, Self-Discovery
