Synopsis: In 'The Electric Man: Being the One Act Version of the Three Act Farcical Comedy of the Same Name' by Hannan, Charles, the audience is taken on a hilarious journey filled with mistaken identities, absurd situations, and uproarious misunderstandings. The story revolves around a peculiar inventor who creates a device that grants him the ability to generate electricity. As the inventor's invention becomes the center of attention, chaos ensues, leading to a series of comical events that will keep the audience laughing from start to finish.

Genres: Comedy, Farce, Theatre

Themes: Invention and Innovation, Identity and Deception
