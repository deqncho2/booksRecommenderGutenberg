Synopsis: In 'St. Andrews Ghost Stories: Fourth Edition', William Thomas Linskill takes readers on a chilling journey through the haunted streets of St. Andrews, Scotland. From eerie apparitions to mysterious disappearances, this collection of ghostly tales will leave readers on the edge of their seats, questioning the thin veil between the living and the dead. As the stories unfold, secrets are revealed, and the dark history of this ancient town comes to life. Prepare to be captivated by the spine-tingling encounters with the supernatural that lurk within the shadows of St. Andrews.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural, Mystery

Themes: Ghosts, Hauntings, History, Secrets
