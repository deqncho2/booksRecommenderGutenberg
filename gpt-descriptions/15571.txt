Synopsis: 
In "Mary Cary: 'Frequently Martha'" by Kate Langley Bosher, we follow the captivating journey of Mary Cary, a young girl with a heart full of dreams and a spirit that refuses to be tamed. Raised in an orphanage, Mary's life takes an unexpected turn when she is adopted by a wealthy family. As she navigates the complexities of her new life, Mary's infectious optimism and unwavering determination lead her to discover the true meaning of family, love, and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Family, Identity, Social class, Resilience
