Synopsis: 
'Playful Poems' is a delightful collection of whimsical and imaginative verses that will transport readers to a world of laughter and wonder. From mischievous animals to magical adventures, each poem is a playful exploration of the joys and curiosities of life. With clever wordplay and vivid imagery, this collection is sure to captivate readers of all ages and leave them with a smile on their face.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature, Humor

Themes: Playfulness, Imagination, Joy, Curiosity
