Synopsis: 
'An Etymological Dictionary of the Scottish Language' is a comprehensive and fascinating exploration of the origins and meanings of words in the Scottish language. Written by John Jamieson, this dictionary delves into the various senses of words, drawing upon the authority of renowned writers and the titles of their works. By tracing the etymology of these words back to their original sources, Jamieson provides readers with a captivating journey through the linguistic history of Scotland.

Genres: 
Non-fiction, Linguistics, Reference

Themes: 
Language, Etymology, Scottish culture
