Synopsis: In 'Alas! A Novel' by Rhoda Broughton, a young woman named Alas discovers a hidden secret that turns her world upside down. As she navigates through a web of deceit, love, and betrayal, Alas must confront her own desires and make choices that will shape her future. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey of self-discovery and the complexities of human relationships.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Drama

Themes: Secrets and deception, Love and relationships, Self-discovery
