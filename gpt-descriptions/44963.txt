Synopsis: In 'For Faith and Freedom' by Walter Besant, a young man named Richard is thrust into a world of political intrigue and religious conflict in 16th century England. As he navigates through dangerous alliances and secret societies, Richard must make difficult choices that will test his loyalty and challenge his beliefs. With the fate of his country hanging in the balance, Richard's journey becomes a thrilling adventure filled with suspense, romance, and unexpected twists.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Religious Conflict, Political Intrigue
