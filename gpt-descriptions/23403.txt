Synopsis: 

In 'Special Report on Diseases of the Horse', a team of renowned experts in veterinary medicine and animal health come together to provide a comprehensive and insightful examination of various diseases that afflict horses. From common ailments to rare conditions, this report delves into the causes, symptoms, and treatments of these diseases, offering invaluable knowledge to horse owners, veterinarians, and anyone interested in equine health. With a focus on prevention and management, the report also explores the impact of these diseases on the equine industry and the measures taken to control their spread. 

Genres: Non-fiction, Veterinary Medicine, Animal Health

Themes: Equine health, Disease prevention, Veterinary care, Animal industry
