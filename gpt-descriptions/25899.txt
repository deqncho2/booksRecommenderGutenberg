Synopsis: In 'The Prodigal Father' by J. Storer Clouston, a wealthy and eccentric father disappears, leaving his family in a state of confusion and financial uncertainty. As his children embark on a quest to find him, they uncover a web of secrets, hidden identities, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the family's bonds are tested, and they must confront their own flaws and desires. This gripping tale of mystery and family dynamics will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Family Drama, Suspense

Themes: Family relationships, Identity, Secrets and deception
