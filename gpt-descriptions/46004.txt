Synopsis: In 'Neighbors Unknown' by Charles G. D. Roberts, a small, peaceful town is suddenly plagued by a series of mysterious events that disrupt the tranquility of its residents. As the townspeople grapple with the unknown, they must confront their deepest fears and unravel the secrets hidden within their seemingly ordinary lives. With each passing day, the tension rises, and the line between reality and illusion becomes blurred. As the truth slowly emerges, the town's inhabitants find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and unexpected connections that will forever change their lives.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the fragility of perception, the impact of the unknown on human behavior
