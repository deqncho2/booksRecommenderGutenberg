Synopsis: In 'Lob Lie-By-The-Fire, The Brownies and Other Tales', Juliana Horatia Ewing weaves a collection of enchanting and whimsical tales that transport readers to a world of magical creatures and extraordinary adventures. From the mischievous brownies who bring both chaos and joy, to the heartwarming tale of Lob Lie-By-The-Fire, a loyal and wise old dog, these stories captivate with their imaginative storytelling and endearing characters. Each tale is filled with unexpected twists and turns, leaving readers eagerly turning the pages to discover what awaits them next. 

Genres: Fantasy, Children's Literature, Short Stories

Themes: Magic, Friendship, Loyalty, Adventure
