Synopsis: In 'A Visit to Newfoundland' by Mary Lydia Branch, a young woman embarks on a journey to the rugged and mysterious island of Newfoundland. As she explores the breathtaking landscapes and encounters the warm-hearted locals, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets and hidden histories. With each passing day, she unravels the enigmatic past of this captivating place, leading her to question her own identity and purpose. This gripping tale of discovery and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth that lies beneath the surface of Newfoundland.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Exploration
