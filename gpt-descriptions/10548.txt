Synopsis: 
In the small coastal town of Polperro, a mysterious family known as the Westcotes arrives, bringing with them an air of intrigue and secrets. As the townspeople become increasingly fascinated by the enigmatic newcomers, they soon discover that the Westcotes' presence is not without consequences. Told through the eyes of a young local girl, this captivating tale explores the complexities of human nature, the power of perception, and the hidden depths that lie within us all.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Secrets, Perception, Identity, Community
