Synopsis: In 'The Executioner' by Frank Riley, a renowned executioner finds himself torn between his duty and his conscience when he is assigned to carry out a controversial execution. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers shocking secrets that challenge his beliefs and force him to question the justice system he has faithfully served. With time running out, he must make a difficult decision that could have far-reaching consequences. This gripping and thought-provoking novel explores the complexities of morality, justice, and the human capacity for compassion.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Legal Drama

Themes: Morality, Justice, Conscience, Redemption
