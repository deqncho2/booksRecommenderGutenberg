Synopsis: 

'Roster of Company I, 24th Regiment, Massachusetts Volunteers' is a captivating historical document that provides a detailed account of the soldiers who served in Company I during the American Civil War. Compiled by Amory, Charles Bean, Armstrong, James, and DeLane, Nelson H., this roster offers a unique glimpse into the lives and experiences of these brave men who fought for their country. From their enlistment to their battles on the front lines, this book paints a vivid picture of the sacrifices and camaraderie that defined their service. 

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history

Themes: Patriotism, Sacrifice, Brotherhood
