Synopsis: In the heart of New York City, a mysterious and captivating painting known as "The Blue Venus" emerges, captivating the attention of art enthusiasts and collectors alike. As the painting's allure grows, it becomes the center of a thrilling and dangerous game, involving a diverse cast of characters, from art dealers to thieves, all vying for possession of this enigmatic masterpiece. With secrets, betrayals, and unexpected twists, "The Blue Venus" takes readers on a thrilling journey through the art world's underbelly, where the line between beauty and obsession becomes blurred.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Art Fiction

Themes: Artistic obsession, Deception, Power dynamics
