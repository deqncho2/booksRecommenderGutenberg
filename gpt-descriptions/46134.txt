Synopsis: In "Garden Cities of To-Morrow," Sir Ebenezer Howard presents a visionary blueprint for the future of urban planning. This second edition of his groundbreaking work, "To-Morrow: a Peaceful Path to Real Reform," delves into the concept of garden cities, self-contained communities that harmoniously blend the benefits of urban and rural living. Howard's captivating exploration takes readers on a journey through his innovative ideas, offering a compelling vision of a more sustainable and equitable society.

Genres: Non-fiction, Urban Planning, Utopian Literature

Themes: Urbanization, Sustainable Development, Social Equality, Community Building
