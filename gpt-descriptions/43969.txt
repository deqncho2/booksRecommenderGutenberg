Synopsis: 

'With the Ulster Division in France: A Story of the 11th Battalion Royal Irish Rifles (South Antrim Volunteers), From Bordon to Thiepval' takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of the 11th Battalion Royal Irish Rifles during World War I. The story follows the battalion from their training grounds in Bordon, England, to the harrowing battlefields of Thiepval in France. Through the eyes of the soldiers, the reader witnesses the camaraderie, bravery, and sacrifices made by these men as they face the horrors of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Military History

Themes: Patriotism, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Courage, The Impact of War on Individuals and Communities
