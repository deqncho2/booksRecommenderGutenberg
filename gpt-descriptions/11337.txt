Synopsis: In 'Cowmen and Rustlers: A Story of the Wyoming Cattle Ranges', Edward Sylvester Ellis takes readers on a thrilling journey through the rugged and lawless Wyoming cattle ranges. Set in the late 19th century, the story follows a group of cowmen who must navigate the treacherous landscape while facing off against ruthless rustlers. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, the cowmen find themselves entangled in a dangerous game of survival and justice. With vivid descriptions of the untamed wilderness and gripping action sequences, this novel immerses readers in the wild west, where honor, greed, and justice collide.

Genres: Western, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Honor, Justice, Survival, Loyalty
