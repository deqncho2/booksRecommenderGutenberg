Synopsis: In the thrilling conclusion of "A Secret of the Sea: A Novel," the secrets of the mysterious sea captain are finally revealed, leading to a heart-pounding chase across treacherous waters. As the protagonists navigate through danger and deception, they uncover a hidden world of intrigue and betrayal that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Betrayal, Redemption
