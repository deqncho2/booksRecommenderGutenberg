Synopsis: In 'Moment of Truth' by Basil Wells, a renowned scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything he thought he knew about the world. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between truth and deception becomes increasingly blurred. With his reputation and even his life at stake, he must navigate a treacherous path to uncover the ultimate truth that could change the course of humanity forever.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Scientific exploration, Deception, Consequences of knowledge
