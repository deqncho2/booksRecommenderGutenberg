Synopsis: In 'The Collector's Handbook to Keramics of the Renaissance and Modern Periods', Chaffers, William takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of ceramics, from the Renaissance to the modern era. With meticulous attention to detail, Chaffers explores the exquisite craftsmanship, intricate designs, and historical significance of these remarkable pieces. From delicate porcelain to vibrant majolica, the book unveils the secrets behind the creation and evolution of ceramics, offering invaluable insights for collectors and enthusiasts alike. With stunning illustrations and expert analysis, this handbook is a treasure trove of knowledge for anyone fascinated by the artistry and beauty of ceramics.

Genres: Art, History, Collectibles

Themes: Craftsmanship, Design, Historical Significance
