Synopsis: 

In 'The English Rogue: Described in the Life of Meriton Latroon, a Witty Extravagant', we follow the captivating journey of Meriton Latroon, a charming and audacious character who navigates through the vibrant and treacherous world of 17th century England. From his humble beginnings to his rise as a cunning and resourceful rogue, Meriton's adventures take him through various social circles, encountering both the wealthy elite and the criminal underbelly of society. With his quick wit and audacious nature, Meriton finds himself entangled in a web of love, deceit, and danger, as he strives to survive and thrive in a world filled with both opportunity and peril.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Picaresque

Themes: Identity, Morality, Social Class, Survival
