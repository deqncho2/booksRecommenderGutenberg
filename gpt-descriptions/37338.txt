Synopsis: 
In 'The Crime Doctor' by E. W. Hornung, a renowned doctor finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he becomes entangled with a notorious criminal. As he delves deeper into the criminal underworld, he must navigate a dangerous game of cat and mouse, where the line between right and wrong becomes increasingly blurred. With his reputation and life on the line, the doctor must use his intellect and medical expertise to outsmart his adversaries and uncover the truth. 

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Morality, Redemption, Deception
