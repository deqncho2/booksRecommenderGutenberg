Synopsis: In 'Wanted: A Husband. A Novel' by Adams, Samuel Hopkins, we are introduced to the charming and independent Miss Lydia Orr, who finds herself in a predicament. With her inheritance at stake, she must find a suitable husband within a year. Determined to maintain her freedom, Lydia embarks on a journey filled with unexpected encounters, romantic entanglements, and a quest for true love. As she navigates the complexities of society and her own desires, Lydia discovers that sometimes the most unexpected paths lead to the greatest happiness.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Comedy

Themes: Love and Marriage, Independence and Freedom, Society and Expectations
