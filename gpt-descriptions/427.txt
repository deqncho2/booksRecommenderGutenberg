Synopsis: In 'The Great War Syndicate' by Frank R. Stockton, a group of wealthy individuals forms a secret society with a unique plan to end all wars. They embark on a daring mission to bring about peace by using their vast resources and cunning strategies. As they navigate through political intrigue, international conflicts, and unexpected obstacles, the fate of nations hangs in the balance. Will their audacious plan succeed, or will it lead to unforeseen consequences?

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Political Thriller

Themes: War and Peace, Power and Influence, Societal Change
