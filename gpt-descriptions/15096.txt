Synopsis: 
In 'My Life In The South', Jacob Stroyer takes us on a captivating journey through his experiences as a former slave in the American South. From his childhood on a plantation to his eventual escape to freedom, Stroyer's powerful narrative sheds light on the harsh realities of slavery, the resilience of the human spirit, and the pursuit of liberty against all odds. Through his vivid storytelling, Stroyer offers a unique perspective on the complexities of race, identity, and the enduring quest for justice.

Genres:
Autobiography, Historical Non-fiction, Memoir

Themes:
Slavery, Freedom, Resilience, Identity, Justice
