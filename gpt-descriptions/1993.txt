Synopsis:
'Told After Supper' is a collection of humorous and spooky ghost stories that are shared among a group of friends during a Christmas gathering. As the night progresses, the tales become increasingly bizarre and absurd, leaving the reader both amused and intrigued. From haunted houses to mischievous spirits, each story is filled with unexpected twists and turns that will keep you guessing until the very end.

Genres:
Humor, Horror, Short Stories

Themes:
Supernatural, Christmas, Friendship, Mystery
