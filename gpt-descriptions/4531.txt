Synopsis: In 'The Secret Passage' by Fergus Hume, a mysterious murder takes place in a grand country estate, setting off a chain of events that will unravel dark secrets and hidden desires. As the investigation unfolds, the reader is taken on a thrilling journey through secret passages, unexpected alliances, and shocking revelations. With each twist and turn, the tension builds, leaving the reader eager to uncover the truth behind the murder and the secrets that lie within the walls of the estate.

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Gothic Fiction

Themes: Secrets and deception, Betrayal, Revenge, Identity
