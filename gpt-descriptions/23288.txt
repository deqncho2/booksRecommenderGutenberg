Synopsis: In 'Little Mary: Or, The Picture-Book' by Cecil, Sabina, follow the enchanting journey of a young girl named Mary as she embarks on a whimsical adventure through the pages of her picture book. As Mary delves deeper into the magical world within the illustrations, she discovers that the characters and stories come to life, leading her on a quest to uncover the secrets hidden within the pages. With each turn of the page, Mary's imagination takes flight, blurring the lines between reality and fantasy. This captivating tale will transport readers of all ages into a realm where imagination knows no bounds.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Exploration, Magic
