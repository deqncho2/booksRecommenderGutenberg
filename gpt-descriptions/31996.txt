Synopsis: In "Is There a Santa Claus?" Jacob A. Riis takes readers on a heartwarming journey that explores the enduring magic and belief in Santa Claus. Through a series of captivating anecdotes and personal experiences, Riis delves into the question of whether Santa Claus truly exists, unraveling the essence of the beloved figure and the impact it has on the lives of children and adults alike. With a blend of nostalgia, humor, and thought-provoking insights, Riis invites readers to rediscover the joy and wonder of the holiday season.

Genres: Non-fiction, Memoir, Holiday

Themes: Belief, Imagination, Childhood, Tradition
