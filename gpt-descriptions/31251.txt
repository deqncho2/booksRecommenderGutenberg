Synopsis: In "The Book of Pears and Plums; With Chapters on Cherries and Mulberries" by Edward Bartrum, readers are taken on a captivating journey through the world of these delectable fruits. From the rich history and origins of pears and plums to the fascinating varieties and cultivation techniques, this book is a treasure trove of knowledge for fruit enthusiasts. With additional chapters on cherries and mulberries, the author delves into the unique characteristics and uses of these fruits, providing a comprehensive guide for both gardeners and connoisseurs. Filled with beautiful illustrations and insightful anecdotes, this book is a delightful exploration of the diverse and delicious world of pears, plums, cherries, and mulberries.

Genres: Non-fiction, Gardening, Food and Drink

Themes: Horticulture, Culinary exploration, Botanical history
