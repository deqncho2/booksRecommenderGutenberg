Synopsis: In the near future, a man wakes up in a hospital with no memory of who he is or how he got there. As he begins to piece together his past, he discovers a world filled with secrets, conspiracies, and a powerful organization known as the Citadel. With his life on the line, he must navigate a treacherous landscape of deception and danger to uncover the truth about his identity and the true nature of the Citadel.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Identity, Memory, Conspiracy, Power
