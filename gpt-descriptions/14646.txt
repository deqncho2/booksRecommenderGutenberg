Synopsis:
'Christopher and Columbus' by Von Arnim, Elizabeth is a captivating novel that follows the lives of two young women, Christopher and Columbus, as they embark on a journey of self-discovery and adventure. Set in the early 20th century, the story takes us from the bustling streets of London to the picturesque landscapes of the English countryside. As the two friends navigate the complexities of love, family, and societal expectations, they find themselves questioning their own desires and aspirations. With wit, charm, and a touch of whimsy, this enchanting tale explores the transformative power of friendship and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Friendship, Self-discovery, Love, Society
