Synopsis: 
Step back in time to the vibrant city of Tacoma in 1904, as author Louis W. Pratt takes you on a captivating journey through the electric streets and bustling waterfront. From the grand architecture to the lively entertainment scene, immerse yourself in the rich history and captivating stories of this electrifying city on the Pacific Coast. Discover the secrets, triumphs, and challenges that shaped Tacoma into the remarkable metropolis it is today.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Local history

Themes: Urban development, Technological advancements, Cultural transformation
