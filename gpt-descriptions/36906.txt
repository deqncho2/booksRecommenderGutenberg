Synopsis: 
In "Marjorie Dean, College Senior" by Josephine Chase, we follow the captivating journey of Marjorie Dean as she navigates her final year of college. Faced with the challenges of academic pressure, social expectations, and personal growth, Marjorie finds herself at a crossroads, torn between her dreams and the responsibilities that come with adulthood. As she grapples with love, friendship, and the pursuit of her passions, Marjorie must summon the strength to make difficult decisions that will shape her future. Will she find the courage to follow her heart and embrace the unknown, or will she succumb to the pressures of conformity? 

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Romance, Drama

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Independence, Sacrifice
