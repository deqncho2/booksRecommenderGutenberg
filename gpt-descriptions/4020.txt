Synopsis: 
In "Arcadian Adventures with the Idle Rich," Stephen Leacock takes readers on a hilarious and satirical journey through the lives of the wealthy elite. Through a series of witty and absurd anecdotes, Leacock exposes the frivolous and often ridiculous pursuits of the idle rich, from their extravagant parties to their misguided attempts at philanthropy. With his sharp wit and keen observations, Leacock offers a scathing critique of the upper class while providing a delightful and entertaining read.

Genres: Satire, Humor, Fiction

Themes: Wealth and privilege, Social class, Materialism, Critique of society
