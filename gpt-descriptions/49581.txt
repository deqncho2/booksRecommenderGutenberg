Synopsis: In "The Cathedral Church of Oxford: A description of its fabric and a brief history of the Episcopal see," Percy Dearmer takes readers on a captivating journey through the magnificent Cathedral Church of Oxford. From its awe-inspiring architecture to its rich historical significance, Dearmer delves into the intricate details of this iconic religious landmark. As he unveils the secrets hidden within its walls, readers will be transported to a world of grandeur and spirituality, discovering the profound impact this cathedral has had on the Episcopal see. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Architecture

Themes: Religious heritage, Architectural marvels, Historical significance
