Synopsis:
In "Antonina; Or, The Fall of Rome" by Wilkie Collins, the reader is transported to ancient Rome, where the city is on the brink of collapse. Amidst the chaos, a young woman named Antonina finds herself caught in a web of secrets, betrayal, and forbidden love. As the city crumbles around her, Antonina must navigate treacherous alliances and uncover the truth about her own identity. With its vivid historical setting and gripping narrative, this novel takes readers on a thrilling journey through the twilight of an empire.

Genres:
Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes:
Betrayal, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
