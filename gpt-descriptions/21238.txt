Synopsis: 
In 'The Castaways' by Reid, Mayne, a group of strangers find themselves stranded on a remote island after a catastrophic event. As they struggle to survive and find a way back home, they must confront their own personal demons and form unlikely alliances. With danger lurking around every corner, they must rely on their wits and resilience to overcome the challenges that the island presents. But as secrets are revealed and tensions rise, they soon realize that their greatest threat might not be the island itself. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Redemption, Trust, Human Nature
