Synopsis: 
In 'The Right Time' by John Berryman, a renowned author finds himself caught in a web of mystery and intrigue when he receives a cryptic message from a stranger. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a hidden world of secrets, lies, and unexpected connections that threaten to unravel his own life. With each twist and turn, the protagonist must confront his own demons and make choices that will determine his fate. This gripping tale of suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Identity, Redemption, Consequences of Actions
