Synopsis: In 'Marriage as a Trade' by Hamilton, Cicely, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of societal expectations and familial pressures as she navigates the world of marriage. With her heart torn between duty and desire, Mary must make a choice that will shape her future and challenge the conventions of her time. As she delves into the complexities of love, sacrifice, and self-discovery, Mary's journey becomes a captivating exploration of the true meaning of marriage.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Society and Expectations, Love and Desire, Self-Identity
