Synopsis: In 'A Christmas Prayer' by James W. Foley, a small town is faced with a devastating tragedy that threatens to dampen their Christmas spirit. As the community comes together to support one another, a mysterious stranger arrives, bringing hope and healing in unexpected ways. With the power of faith and love, this heartwarming tale explores the true meaning of Christmas and the miracles that can happen when people unite.

Genres: Fiction, Holiday, Inspirational

Themes: Faith, Community, Redemption, Miracles
