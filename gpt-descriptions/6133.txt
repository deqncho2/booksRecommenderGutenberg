Synopsis: 
In 'The Extraordinary Adventures of Arsène Lupin, Gentleman-Burglar', the enigmatic and charming Arsène Lupin takes center stage as he embarks on a series of audacious heists that leave the police and his victims equally perplexed. From stealing priceless jewels to outwitting the most cunning detectives, Lupin's exploits take him across Europe and into the hearts of readers. But as his reputation grows, so does the determination of those who seek to bring him to justice. Will Lupin's wit and cunning be enough to outsmart his pursuers, or will his extraordinary adventures come to an end?

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Crime

Themes: Identity, Morality, Cat-and-mouse game
