Synopsis: In 'Elizabeth Ann's Houseboat', a young girl named Elizabeth Ann embarks on a thrilling adventure when she discovers a hidden houseboat on the river near her home. With the help of her friends, she sets out to uncover the secrets of the houseboat and the mysterious past it holds. As Elizabeth Ann delves deeper into the houseboat's history, she uncovers unexpected connections to her own family and learns valuable lessons about friendship, courage, and the power of discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Family, Identity, Exploration
