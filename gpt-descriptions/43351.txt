Synopsis: 
In 'A Chain of Evidence' by Wells, Carolyn, a renowned detective, Fleming Stone, is called upon to solve a perplexing murder case that seems to have no logical explanation. As Stone delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected connections that will keep readers guessing until the very end. With each new clue, the tension rises, and the truth becomes more elusive, leading to a thrilling and unexpected conclusion.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Justice, Perception
