Synopsis: 

"A Bible School Manual: Studies in the Book of Revelation" by Stephen Alexander Hunter is a comprehensive guide that offers an introduction, analysis, and notes on the Book of Revelation. It presents a concise interpretation of the text, focusing on the symbolic view, while also referencing various authorities and mentioning other interpretations. The manual includes the text of the American revised version, edited in paragraphs, making it easily accessible for Bible students.

Genres: Religious literature, Biblical studies, Reference guide

Themes: Biblical interpretation, Symbolism, Eschatology
