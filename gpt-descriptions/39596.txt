Synopsis:
In "Georgina of the Rainbows" by Annie F. Johnston, readers are introduced to the enchanting world of Georgina, a young girl with a special connection to rainbows. When Georgina discovers a hidden secret about her family, she embarks on a magical journey that takes her to extraordinary places and introduces her to fascinating characters. As she unravels the mysteries of her past, Georgina learns valuable lessons about love, friendship, and the power of embracing one's true self.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Family, Self-discovery
