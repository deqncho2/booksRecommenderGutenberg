Synopsis: 

In "Fishes of the Wakarusa River in Kansas," Deacon and Metcalf take readers on an immersive journey through the diverse aquatic world of the Wakarusa River. From the elusive and colorful species that inhabit its depths to the intricate ecosystems that sustain them, this book offers a captivating exploration of the river's fish population. Through vivid descriptions and stunning photographs, the authors shed light on the unique characteristics and behaviors of each species, revealing a hidden world of beauty and complexity beneath the river's surface.

Genres: Non-fiction, Nature, Field Guide

Themes: Biodiversity, Conservation, Environmental Awareness
