Synopsis: In 'Halima And The Scorpions: 1905' by Hichens, Robert, readers are transported to the vibrant and mysterious world of Egypt in the early 20th century. Halima, a young and courageous woman, finds herself entangled in a web of secrets and danger when she becomes involved with a group of enigmatic scorpion hunters. As she delves deeper into their world, Halima discovers shocking truths that challenge her beliefs and force her to confront her own identity. With its richly atmospheric setting and compelling characters, this captivating tale of love, betrayal, and self-discovery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Cultural Exploration
