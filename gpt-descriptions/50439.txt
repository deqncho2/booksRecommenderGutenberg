Synopsis: 
'English Eccentrics and Eccentricities' by Timbs, John is a captivating collection of true stories that delves into the lives of some of the most peculiar individuals in English history. From eccentric inventors and artists to eccentric aristocrats and scholars, this book takes readers on a fascinating journey through the eccentricities of the English. With tales of bizarre habits, unconventional lifestyles, and peculiar obsessions, Timbs paints a vivid portrait of these extraordinary characters, leaving readers both amused and intrigued.

Genres: Non-fiction, Biography, History

Themes: Eccentricity, Individuality, British culture
