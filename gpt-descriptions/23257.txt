Synopsis: In 'Clair de Lune: A Play in Two Acts and Six Scenes' by Michael Strange, a mysterious and enchanting woman named Luna enters the lives of a group of friends, forever altering their perceptions of love, friendship, and the power of the human spirit. As the characters navigate through their own personal struggles and desires, Luna's presence becomes a catalyst for self-discovery and transformation. With poetic dialogue and captivating imagery, this play explores the complexities of human relationships and the profound impact one person can have on the lives of others.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Magical Realism

Themes: Love, Friendship, Self-discovery, Transformation
