Synopsis: In 'The Penalty' by Morris, Gouverneur, a young man named Austin Stoneman finds himself caught in a web of political intrigue and personal vendettas in post-Civil War America. As he navigates the treacherous world of power and corruption, Austin must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will shape the future of the nation. With unexpected twists and a cast of complex characters, 'The Penalty' is a gripping tale of ambition, loyalty, and the price of redemption.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Drama

Themes: Power and Corruption, Redemption, Personal Sacrifice
