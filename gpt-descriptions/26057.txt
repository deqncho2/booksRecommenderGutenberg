Synopsis:
In the small town of Marjorie, a mysterious event occurs that shakes the community to its core. As the townspeople grapple with the aftermath, secrets are unearthed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of human resilience is revealed. Told through the eyes of multiple characters, this gripping novel explores the complexities of love, loss, and the power of redemption.

Genres:
Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller

Themes:
Secrets and their consequences, Community and its impact, Resilience and redemption
