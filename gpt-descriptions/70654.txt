Synopsis: 

In 'Submerged forests' by Reid, Clement, a mysterious discovery in the depths of the ocean sets off a thrilling adventure that intertwines the realms of science, history, and mythology. As a team of marine archaeologists delves into the secrets hidden beneath the waves, they uncover a forgotten world that holds the key to an ancient civilization's enigmatic past. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, and the protagonists find themselves entangled in a race against time to protect the truth from those who would exploit it for their own gain. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Ancient civilizations, Preservation of knowledge
