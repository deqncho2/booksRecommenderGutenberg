Synopsis: In 'Handbook to the new Gold-fields', R. M. Ballantyne takes readers on a thrilling adventure to the untamed gold-fields of the American West. Join our protagonist as he navigates treacherous landscapes, encounters dangerous outlaws, and faces the ultimate test of survival. With vivid descriptions and heart-pounding action, this handbook is a must-read for anyone seeking fortune and excitement in the wild frontier.

Genres: Adventure, Western, Historical Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Greed, Survival
