Synopsis: 

'Life of Henriette Sontag, Countess de Rossi' takes readers on a captivating journey through the extraordinary life of a renowned opera singer. Through the eyes of various notable figures, including composers, writers, and even a prince, this book offers intriguing sketches that shed light on the fascinating personality and talent of Henriette Sontag. From her humble beginnings to her rise to fame, the reader will be enthralled by the twists and turns of her life, as well as the impact she had on those around her.

Genres: Biography, Music, Historical

Themes: Ambition, Artistic Expression, Love and Relationships
