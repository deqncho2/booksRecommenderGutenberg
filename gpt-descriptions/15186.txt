Synopsis: 
'Folk-Lore and Legends: Scandinavian' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich tapestry of Scandinavian folklore and mythology. From the enchanting tales of mythical creatures and magical beings to the heroic sagas of legendary warriors, this collection unveils the hidden depths of Scandinavian culture and imagination. Delve into the realms of gods and goddesses, explore the mysteries of the natural world, and witness the timeless battles between good and evil. With each story, readers will be transported to a realm where ancient traditions and fantastical adventures intertwine, leaving them spellbound and yearning for more.

Genres: Folklore, Mythology, Fantasy

Themes: Scandinavian culture, Mythical creatures, Heroism, Good vs. Evil
