Synopsis: In "The Last Space Ship" by Murray Leinster, a group of astronauts embarks on a perilous mission to save humanity from an impending catastrophe. As they journey through the vastness of space, they encounter unexpected challenges and face the ultimate test of their courage and resourcefulness. With time running out, they must rely on their ingenuity and teamwork to ensure the survival of the human race.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Human Ingenuity
