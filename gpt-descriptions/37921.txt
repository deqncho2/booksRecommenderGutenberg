Synopsis: 

"The Bath Road: History, Fashion, & Frivolity on an Old Highway" takes readers on a captivating journey through time along one of England's most iconic roads. Charles G. Harper delves into the rich history, vibrant fashion, and delightful frivolities that have unfolded along the Bath Road, revealing a tapestry of stories that will transport readers to a bygone era. From the grandeur of Georgian architecture to the scandalous escapades of the Regency period, this book offers a fascinating exploration of the people, places, and events that have shaped the road's legacy. With vivid descriptions and charming anecdotes, Harper paints a vivid picture of the road's allure, inviting readers to immerse themselves in the captivating world of the Bath Road.

Genres: History, Travel, Non-fiction

Themes: Historical exploration, Cultural heritage, Social customs
