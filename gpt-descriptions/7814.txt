Synopsis: In 'Daybreak: A Romance of an Old World', the reader is transported to a captivating and mysterious realm where a young man named Julian embarks on a journey that will forever change his life. As he navigates through a world filled with secrets, forbidden love, and ancient prophecies, Julian finds himself torn between duty and desire, and must make choices that will shape the destiny of an entire civilization. With its richly imagined setting and compelling characters, this novel weaves together elements of adventure, romance, and intrigue, leaving the reader eagerly turning the pages to uncover the truth that lies at the heart of this enchanting tale.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Destiny, Love, Sacrifice, Power
