Synopsis: In "Mr. Durant of Salt Lake City, 'That Mormon'", we follow the intriguing journey of Mr. Durant, a charismatic and enigmatic figure who arrives in Salt Lake City, Utah. As he becomes entangled in the lives of the locals, his true intentions and identity remain shrouded in mystery. With secrets, scandals, and unexpected alliances, this captivating tale explores the depths of human nature and the power of belief.

Genres: Mystery, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Identity, Faith, Deception, Community
