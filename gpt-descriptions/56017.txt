Synopsis: In 'The Black Barque: A Tales of the Pirate Slave-Ship Gentle Hand on Her Last African Cruise', author T. Jenkins Hains takes readers on a thrilling journey aboard a notorious pirate slave-ship. Set during the final African cruise of the ship Gentle Hand, the story follows a diverse cast of characters as they navigate the treacherous waters of the Atlantic Ocean. From the heart-stopping encounters with rival pirates to the harrowing experiences of the enslaved individuals on board, this gripping tale explores the dark underbelly of the pirate trade and the resilience of those caught in its clutches.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Slavery, Piracy, Survival
