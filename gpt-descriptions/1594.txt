Synopsis: In 'Essays in Little' by Lang, Andrew, embark on a captivating journey through a collection of thought-provoking essays that explore a wide range of topics. From the mysteries of ancient civilizations to the enigmatic nature of dreams, Lang's insightful observations and witty anecdotes will keep readers engaged and eager for more. Delve into the realms of literature, history, and philosophy as the author offers unique perspectives on life's complexities, leaving readers with a renewed sense of curiosity and a deeper understanding of the world around them.

Genres: Essay, Non-fiction, Philosophy

Themes: Curiosity, Knowledge, Human Nature
