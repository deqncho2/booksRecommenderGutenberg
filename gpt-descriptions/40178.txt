Synopsis: The Carter Girls, a group of adventurous sisters, find themselves intrigued by their new neighbors who seem to have a mysterious secret. As they embark on a journey to uncover the truth, they stumble upon unexpected twists and turns that challenge their courage and loyalty. With each clue they uncover, the girls become entangled in a web of intrigue that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Curiosity, Friendship, Family, Secrets
