Synopsis: 
In "The Landlord at Lion's Head," we are introduced to Marcia Gaylord, a young and ambitious writer who moves to Boston with her husband, Bartley Hubbard, in pursuit of literary success. However, their dreams are soon shattered as they find themselves caught in a web of scandal, betrayal, and moral dilemmas. As Marcia navigates the treacherous waters of the literary world, she must confront her own desires and the consequences of her choices. Set against the backdrop of late 19th-century Boston, this captivating novel explores the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of artistic fulfillment.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Betrayal, Morality, Artistic Pursuit
