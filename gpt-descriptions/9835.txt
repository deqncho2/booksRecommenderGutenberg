Synopsis: 
In "Martin Conisby's Vengeance" by Farnol, Jeffery, a young Englishman named Martin Conisby finds himself betrayed and abandoned on a remote island in the Caribbean. Determined to seek justice and reclaim his rightful place, he embarks on a perilous journey filled with treacherous pirates, unexpected alliances, and a quest for revenge that will test his courage and resilience. As Martin navigates the dangerous waters of the Caribbean, he must confront his own inner demons and discover the true meaning of loyalty and honor.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Revenge, Betrayal, Redemption, Courage
