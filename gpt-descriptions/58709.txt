Synopsis: In 'When Scout Meets Scout; or, The Aeroplane Spy' by H. L. Sayler, a thrilling adventure unfolds as two young scouts find themselves caught up in a dangerous game of espionage. When a mysterious aeroplane spy threatens their community, the scouts must use their skills, resourcefulness, and teamwork to uncover the truth and protect their loved ones. As they navigate through treacherous obstacles and face unexpected challenges, the young protagonists embark on a heart-pounding journey that will test their courage and loyalty. Will they be able to outsmart the cunning spy and save the day?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Loyalty, Teamwork, Espionage
