Synopsis: In 'Calling World-4 of Kithgol' by H. B. Fyfe, a group of scientists stumble upon a mysterious portal that transports them to a parallel world filled with advanced technology and strange creatures. As they navigate this new world, they must unravel its secrets and find a way back home, all while facing unexpected challenges and uncovering the dark truths that lie beneath the surface. With each twist and turn, the characters are forced to confront their own beliefs and question the nature of reality itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Reality vs. Perception
