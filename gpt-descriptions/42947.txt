Synopsis: 
'Little Ann, and Other Poems' is a collection of charming and heartwarming poems written by Ann and Jane Taylor. This delightful compilation takes readers on a journey through various tales, including the story of Little Ann, a loyal dog who embarks on a courageous adventure. Each poem is filled with vivid imagery, memorable characters, and thought-provoking messages that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Poetry, Children's Literature

Themes: Loyalty, Courage, Friendship, Nature, Morality
