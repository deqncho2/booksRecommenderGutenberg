Synopsis: In 'Her Serene Highness: A Novel', David Graham Phillips takes readers on a captivating journey through the glamorous world of European royalty. The story revolves around a young American woman who unexpectedly finds herself thrust into the aristocratic circles of a small European principality. As she navigates the complexities of courtly life, she becomes entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and forbidden love. With vivid descriptions and intriguing characters, this novel explores the clash between tradition and modernity, the pursuit of personal freedom, and the sacrifices one must make for love.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Social class, Identity, Love and sacrifice
