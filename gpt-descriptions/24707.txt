Synopsis: 
In 'By Proxy' by Garrett Randall, a brilliant scientist named Dr. Evelyn Hartman finds herself caught in a web of intrigue and danger when she becomes the target of a shadowy organization. As she races against time to uncover the truth, she must navigate a world of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances. With her life on the line, Dr. Hartman must rely on her intellect and resourcefulness to outwit her enemies and protect her groundbreaking research.

Genres: Thriller, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Conspiracy, Identity, Ethics
