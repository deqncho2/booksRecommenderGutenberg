Synopsis: In 'Invaders of the Forbidden Moon' by Raymond Z. Gallun, a team of scientists embarks on a perilous mission to explore a mysterious moon that holds the key to humanity's survival. As they uncover ancient secrets and encounter hostile alien beings, they must navigate treacherous landscapes and face unimaginable dangers. With time running out, the fate of Earth hangs in the balance, and the team must summon all their courage and ingenuity to save humanity from annihilation.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Thriller

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Alien Encounters
