Synopsis: 
In 'Shrubs for Kansas Landscapes', Gary L. McDaniel takes readers on a captivating journey through the diverse world of shrubs, revealing their hidden beauty and practicality in Kansas landscapes. With stunning photographs and expert advice, McDaniel explores the unique characteristics and benefits of various shrub species, offering valuable insights on their growth habits, maintenance, and landscaping applications. From vibrant flowering shrubs to hardy evergreens, this book is a comprehensive guide that will inspire both novice and experienced gardeners to transform their outdoor spaces into stunning, sustainable havens.

Genres: Gardening, Landscaping, Horticulture

Themes: Plant selection, Landscape design, Sustainable gardening
