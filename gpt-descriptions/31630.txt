Synopsis: In 'Hand-Loom Weaving: A Manual for School and Home', author Mattie Phipps Todd takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of hand-loom weaving. Through detailed instructions and insightful anecdotes, Todd unveils the artistry and craftsmanship behind this ancient textile technique. From selecting the perfect fibers to mastering intricate patterns, this manual serves as a comprehensive guide for both beginners and experienced weavers. With its engaging storytelling and practical advice, 'Hand-Loom Weaving' invites readers to discover the joy of creating beautiful and unique fabrics by hand.

Genres: Non-fiction, Craft, Art

Themes: Creativity, Tradition, Skill development
