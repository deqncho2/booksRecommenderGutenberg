Synopsis: 
"The Philatelic Index" is a comprehensive alphabetical list of articles from renowned English-language philatelic magazines up until 1904. Written by William A. R. Jex Long, this invaluable reference guide offers a treasure trove of information for stamp collectors and enthusiasts. From rare stamps to historical anecdotes, this index promises to captivate readers with its vast collection of philatelic knowledge.

Genres: Reference, Collectibles, Hobbies

Themes: Philately, Stamp collecting, Historical trivia
