Synopsis: In "King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table" by Sir Thomas Malory, embark on a captivating journey into the legendary world of King Arthur and his valiant knights. This epic tale follows the rise of Arthur as he pulls the sword from the stone, becomes the rightful king of Camelot, and establishes the noble Knights of the Round Table. From the enchanting Lady of the Lake to the treacherous sorceress Morgan le Fay, the story is filled with magical encounters, epic battles, and timeless quests for honor and chivalry. As Arthur and his knights face formidable challenges, they must navigate the complexities of loyalty, love, and the pursuit of a just kingdom. 

Genres: Arthurian legend, Historical fiction, Adventure

Themes: Chivalry, Loyalty, Destiny, Love, Justice
