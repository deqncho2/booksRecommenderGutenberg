Synopsis: 
In 'The Luck of the Vails: A Novel' by E. F. Benson, the Vail family finds themselves at the center of a mysterious and thrilling series of events. When a long-lost relative unexpectedly arrives, their lives are turned upside down as secrets from the past are unearthed. As the Vails navigate through a web of deception, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, they must rely on their wits and a stroke of luck to unravel the truth and secure their future.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Family Drama

Themes: Family secrets, Betrayal, Fate and luck
