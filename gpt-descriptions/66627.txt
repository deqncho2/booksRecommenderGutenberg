Synopsis: In 'The Cat's Paw' by Natalie Sumner Lincoln, a young woman named Patricia finds herself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when she inherits a peculiar antique shop from her late uncle. As she delves deeper into the secrets of the shop, Patricia discovers a hidden world of supernatural creatures, ancient artifacts, and a sinister plot that threatens not only her own life but the fate of humanity itself. With the help of a charming and enigmatic stranger, she must navigate treacherous alliances and unravel the truth before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, the power of knowledge, the battle between good and evil
