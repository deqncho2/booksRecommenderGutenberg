Synopsis: 
In 'The Circular Study' by Anna Katharine Green, a renowned detective is called to investigate a perplexing case involving a mysterious death in a secluded mansion. As the detective delves deeper into the investigation, they uncover a web of secrets, hidden motives, and unexpected connections among the eccentric inhabitants of the house. With each twist and turn, the detective must navigate through a complex puzzle of clues and red herrings to uncover the truth behind the enigmatic death. 

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Psychological Suspense
