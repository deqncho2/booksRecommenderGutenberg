Synopsis: 
In 'Thirty Years Since; or, The Ruined Family. A Tale', the lives of two families become intertwined in a web of secrets, betrayal, and tragedy. Set against the backdrop of a changing society, this gripping tale explores the consequences of past actions and the power of forgiveness. As the characters navigate through love, loss, and redemption, they are forced to confront the haunting ghosts of their pasts. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: 
Gothic fiction, Historical fiction, Family drama

Themes: 
Secrets and their consequences, Betrayal, Redemption, Forgiveness
