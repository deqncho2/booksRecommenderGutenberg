Synopsis: 
In "Mike Flannery On Duty and Off" by Butler, Ellis Parker, readers are introduced to the charming and witty character of Mike Flannery, a hardworking and dedicated employee of the railroad. As the story unfolds, Flannery finds himself caught in a series of humorous and unpredictable situations, both on and off duty. From dealing with eccentric passengers to navigating the complexities of love, Flannery's adventures are sure to keep readers entertained and eager to discover what lies around the next corner.

Genres: Humor, Fiction

Themes: Work ethic, Love and relationships
