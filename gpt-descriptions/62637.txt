Synopsis: In 'The Princess Sonia' by Julia Magruder, a young princess finds herself torn between duty and desire when she falls in love with a commoner. As she navigates the treacherous waters of royal expectations and societal norms, she must make a choice that could change her life forever. With secrets, betrayals, and unexpected twists, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Romance, Historical Fiction, Drama

Themes: Love and duty, societal expectations, forbidden love
