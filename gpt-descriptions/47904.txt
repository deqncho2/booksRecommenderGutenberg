Synopsis: In the July 1900 edition of 'The Brochure Series of Architectural Illustration,' readers are transported to the enchanting House of Jacques Coeur in Bourges. This captivating volume showcases the intricate Gothic carved woodwork that adorns the house, revealing the remarkable craftsmanship and artistic vision of the era. Through detailed illustrations and insightful commentary, readers are invited to explore the rich history and architectural beauty of this hidden gem.

Genres: Architecture, Art, History

Themes: Gothic architecture, craftsmanship, historical preservation
