Synopsis: In 'By Right of Purchase' by Harold Bindloss, a young man named Ralph discovers a hidden family secret that leads him on a thrilling journey across the rugged Canadian wilderness. As he delves deeper into his family's past, Ralph becomes entangled in a web of mystery, danger, and unexpected romance. With each step, he uncovers shocking revelations that challenge his perception of his own identity and test his courage to the limit. Set against the backdrop of breathtaking landscapes and filled with suspenseful twists, this captivating novel takes readers on an unforgettable adventure of self-discovery and the pursuit of truth.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Courage
