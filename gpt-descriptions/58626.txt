Synopsis: In "North Woods Manhunt (A Sugar Creek Gang Story)" by Hutchens, Paul, the Sugar Creek Gang embarks on an exciting adventure when they stumble upon a mysterious cabin in the North Woods. As they investigate the cabin's secrets, they find themselves caught up in a thrilling manhunt, facing danger and unexpected twists at every turn. With their friendship and resourcefulness tested, the gang must rely on their wits and courage to solve the puzzle and bring justice to the North Woods.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Children's Literature

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Problem-Solving
