Synopsis: In 'The Cave Boy of the Age of Stone' by Margaret A. McIntyre, follow the thrilling journey of a young cave boy named Kael as he navigates the treacherous world of the Stone Age. Faced with dangerous predators, rival tribes, and the mysteries of his own past, Kael must summon his courage and resourcefulness to survive. Along the way, he discovers ancient secrets that could change his tribe's destiny forever. Will Kael be able to overcome the challenges that lie ahead and fulfill his true potential?

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Survival, Identity, Prehistoric Life
