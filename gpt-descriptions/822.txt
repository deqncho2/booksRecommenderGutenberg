Synopsis: In 'The Tarn of Eternity' by Tymon Frank, a mysterious artifact is discovered in the depths of an ancient forest, setting off a chain of events that will challenge the boundaries of time and reality. As a group of unlikely heroes embarks on a perilous journey to unlock the secrets of the tarn, they find themselves entangled in a web of ancient prophecies, hidden powers, and a battle between light and darkness that spans across centuries. With each step closer to the truth, they must confront their own fears and make unimaginable sacrifices to protect the very fabric of existence.

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Destiny, Sacrifice, Good vs. Evil
