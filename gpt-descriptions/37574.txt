Synopsis: In 'Great Inventions and Discoveries' by Piercy, Willis Duff, embark on a captivating journey through time and witness the remarkable ingenuity of humanity. From the ancient wonders of the world to groundbreaking scientific breakthroughs, this book unveils the stories behind some of the most influential inventions and discoveries in history. Delve into the minds of brilliant inventors and explorers as they push the boundaries of knowledge and reshape the world as we know it. Prepare to be inspired by the triumphs and failures that have shaped our modern civilization.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Innovation, Human Ingenuity, Progress
