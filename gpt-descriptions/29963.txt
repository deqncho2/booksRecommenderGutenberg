Synopsis: In 'Goodbye, Dead Man!' by Tom W. Harris, a small town is shaken when a mysterious dead body is discovered in the local cemetery. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, and the residents find themselves caught in a web of deception and betrayal. With each twist and turn, the tension escalates, leaving the reader guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets, Betrayal, Deception
