Synopsis: In 'Zeritsky's Law' by Ann Warren Griffith, a brilliant lawyer named Sarah Zeritsky finds herself entangled in a high-stakes legal battle when she takes on a controversial case involving a powerful corporation. As she delves deeper into the case, Sarah uncovers shocking secrets and faces dangerous adversaries who will stop at nothing to protect their interests. With her career and personal life on the line, Sarah must navigate a treacherous web of deceit and corruption to seek justice and expose the truth.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Corruption, Justice, Power struggle
