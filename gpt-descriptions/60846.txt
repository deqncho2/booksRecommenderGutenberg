Synopsis: In 'A Great Day for the Irish' by Alice L. Hopf, a small town in Ireland is turned upside down when a mysterious stranger arrives with a secret that could change everything. As the townspeople grapple with their own personal struggles, they must come together to confront the challenges that lie ahead. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating story will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Drama, Fiction

Themes: Identity, Community, Secrets
