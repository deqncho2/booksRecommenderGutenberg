Synopsis: In 'The Missing Link' by Edward Dyson, a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that challenges everything we know about human evolution. As he delves deeper into the secrets of this enigmatic discovery, he becomes entangled in a web of conspiracy and danger. With each revelation, the line between science and mythology blurs, leading him on a thrilling quest to uncover the truth behind the missing link.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Science Fiction

Themes: Evolution, Conspiracy, Identity
