Synopsis: In the small coastal village of Moonfleet, young John Trenchard finds himself entangled in a thrilling adventure when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that leads him on a dangerous quest for treasure. As he delves deeper into the mystery, John must navigate treacherous smugglers, ancient legends, and the haunting presence of a ghostly figure. Set against the backdrop of 18th-century England, 'Moonfleet' is a captivating tale of friendship, loyalty, and the pursuit of redemption.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Redemption
