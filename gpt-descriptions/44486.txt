Synopsis: In 'Dodo: A Detail of the Day. Volumes 1 and 2', we are introduced to the charming and vivacious character of Dodo, a young woman who finds herself at the center of a whirlwind of social events and romantic entanglements. Set in the early 20th century, this delightful tale takes us on a journey through the glamorous world of high society, where Dodo's wit and unconventional behavior captivate those around her. As she navigates the complexities of love and friendship, Dodo's infectious spirit brings both laughter and heartache to those who cross her path.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Comedy

Themes: Love and relationships, Social conventions, Individuality
