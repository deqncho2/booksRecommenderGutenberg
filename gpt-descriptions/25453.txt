Synopsis: In 'How the Fairy Violet Lost and Won Her Wings' by Ker, Marianne L. B., follow the enchanting journey of Violet, a young fairy who finds herself stripped of her wings and banished from her magical realm. Determined to regain her wings and return home, Violet embarks on a perilous adventure filled with unexpected allies, dangerous foes, and profound self-discovery. As she navigates through a world of mystery and wonder, Violet must confront her deepest fears and uncover the true power that lies within her. Will she be able to overcome the obstacles in her path and reclaim her wings, or will she forever be grounded in a world that is not her own?

Genres: Fantasy, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, Resilience, Self-discovery
