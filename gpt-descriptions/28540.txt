Synopsis: 
In "Bibliomania; or Book-Madness: A Bibliographical Romance," we follow the eccentric adventures of a passionate bibliophile named Dibdin. Obsessed with collecting rare books, Dibdin embarks on a quest that takes him through the enchanting world of bookshops, libraries, and literary societies. Along the way, he encounters a colorful cast of characters, including fellow book enthusiasts, cunning booksellers, and even a mysterious book thief. As Dibdin's obsession deepens, he finds himself entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between reality and fiction becomes blurred. Will Dibdin's love for books lead him to enlightenment or madness?

Genres: 
Romance, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: 
Obsession, Love for Literature, Identity
