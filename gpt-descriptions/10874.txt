Synopsis: In 'At Sunwich Port, Part 4', the story takes an unexpected turn as the eccentric Captain Trimblett finds himself in a peculiar situation involving a mysterious package. As the townspeople of Sunwich Port become increasingly curious about the contents of the package, Captain Trimblett's reputation hangs in the balance. With humor and wit, W.W. Jacobs weaves a tale of intrigue and suspense that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Humor, Adventure

Themes: Reputation, Curiosity, Deception
