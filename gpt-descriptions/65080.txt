Synopsis: 
In 'Dick Rodney; or, The Adventures of an Eton Boy' by Grant, James, we follow the thrilling escapades of young Dick Rodney, a spirited and mischievous student at Eton College. From secret societies and daring pranks to unexpected friendships and dangerous encounters, Dick's journey takes him through the corridors of power and the hidden underbelly of society. As he navigates the challenges of school life and uncovers hidden truths, Dick's character is tested, and he must make choices that will shape his future. With its blend of adventure, mystery, and coming-of-age themes, this captivating novel offers a glimpse into the world of an Eton boy and the transformative power of self-discovery.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Morality, Self-discovery
