Synopsis: 
In the second volume of "Sheppard Lee, Written by Himself," the captivating tale of Sheppard Lee's extraordinary adventures continues. Lee, a man who possesses the unique ability to inhabit the bodies of others, embarks on a series of thrilling escapades that take him from one unexpected situation to another. From experiencing life as a wealthy aristocrat to becoming a sailor on a perilous voyage, Lee's journey is filled with both exhilarating highs and devastating lows. As he navigates through various identities, Lee grapples with questions of identity, morality, and the true nature of human existence. Will he ever find his true self amidst the ever-changing personas he inhabits?

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Satire

Themes: Identity, Morality, Existentialism
