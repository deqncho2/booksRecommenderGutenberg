Synopsis: In 'An Irish Cousin; vol. 2/2', the captivating sequel to the first volume, the story continues to follow the adventures of the spirited and unconventional heroine, Miss Jane Barlow. Set against the backdrop of the enchanting Irish countryside, Jane finds herself entangled in a web of family secrets, forbidden love, and unexpected alliances. As she navigates through the complexities of her relationships and confronts the challenges of her own identity, Jane's journey takes her on a thrilling and emotional rollercoaster that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages until the very end.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Family secrets, Identity, Love and relationships
