Synopsis: In "Stevenson's Shrine: The Record of a Pilgrimage," Laura Stubbs takes readers on a captivating journey as she retraces the footsteps of the renowned author Robert Louis Stevenson. From the bustling streets of Edinburgh to the tranquil landscapes of the South Seas, Stubbs immerses herself in Stevenson's life and works, uncovering hidden stories and encountering unexpected connections along the way. Through vivid descriptions and personal reflections, she invites readers to join her pilgrimage and discover the enduring legacy of one of literature's most beloved figures.

Genres: Travelogue, Biography, Literary Criticism

Themes: Literary exploration, Personal connection, Legacy
