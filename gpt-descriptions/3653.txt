Synopsis: In 'The Guns of Bull Run: A Story of the Civil War's Eve', Joseph A. Altsheler takes readers on a gripping journey through the tumultuous events leading up to the American Civil War. Set against the backdrop of the Bull Run campaign, the story follows Harry Kenton, a young Kentuckian who finds himself torn between his loyalty to his home state and his desire for a united nation. As Harry navigates the complexities of war, he must confront his own fears, forge unlikely alliances, and face the harsh realities of battle. With vivid descriptions and compelling characters, Altsheler paints a vivid picture of the Civil War's early days, capturing the spirit of a nation on the brink of division.

Genres: Historical Fiction, War Fiction, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Patriotism, Conflict, Bravery, Identity
