Synopsis: In 'The Edge of the Knife' by H. Beam Piper, a brilliant scientist discovers a mysterious artifact that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of time travel. As he delves deeper into its enigmatic powers, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse, pursued by powerful forces who will stop at nothing to possess the artifact. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate a treacherous path, where every decision could alter the course of history.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Time travel, Power and control, Ethical dilemmas
