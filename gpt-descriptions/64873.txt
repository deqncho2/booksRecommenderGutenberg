Synopsis: 
In the distant future, a team of scientists embarks on a perilous mission to explore a mysterious ice planet located on the outer edges of the galaxy. As they navigate treacherous terrain and encounter unexpected dangers, they uncover a shocking secret that could change the course of humanity forever. With time running out, they must confront their own fears and make impossible choices in order to survive the unforgiving cold of the ice planet.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Morality, Sacrifice
