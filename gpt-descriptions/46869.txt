Synopsis: In 'The Royal Institution: Its Founder and First Professors' by Jones, Bence, embark on a captivating journey through the birth of a groundbreaking institution and the brilliant minds that shaped it. From the visionary founder's audacious dreams to the eccentric personalities of the first professors, this enthralling account unveils the secrets behind the Royal Institution's rise to prominence. Delve into the world of scientific discovery, captivating experiments, and the clash of intellects that defined this extraordinary era.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Biography, Science

Themes: Scientific innovation, Intellectual rivalry, Institutional development
