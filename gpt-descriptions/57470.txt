Synopsis: In 'Captured by Apes; or, How Philip Garland Became King of Apeland' by Prentice, Harry, readers are taken on a thrilling adventure as Philip Garland finds himself stranded in a mysterious land inhabited by intelligent apes. As he navigates this strange world, Philip must confront his own fears and prejudices, forming unexpected alliances and discovering the true meaning of leadership. With danger lurking at every turn, will Philip be able to survive and find his way back home?

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Coming-of-age

Themes: Survival, Identity, Prejudice, Leadership
