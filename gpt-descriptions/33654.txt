Synopsis: In 'The Old English Herbals', Eleanour Sinclair Rohde takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history and folklore surrounding traditional English herbs. From the mystical properties of lavender to the healing powers of chamomile, Rohde delves into the fascinating stories and uses of these ancient plants. With vivid descriptions and enchanting illustrations, this book offers a delightful exploration of the cultural significance and practical applications of herbs in Old English society.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Folklore

Themes: Herbalism, Folk medicine, Cultural traditions
