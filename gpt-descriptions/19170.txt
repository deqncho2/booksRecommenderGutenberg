Synopsis: 

'Primavera: Poems by Four Authors' is a captivating collection of poetry written by four talented authors: Binyon, Laurence; Cripps, Arthur Shearly; Ghose, Manmohan; and Phillips, Stephen. Each poet brings their unique voice and perspective to the pages, creating a tapestry of emotions and imagery that will leave readers spellbound. From the enchanting beauty of nature to the depths of human emotions, these poems explore the intricacies of life, love, and the human experience. With lyrical verses and evocative language, 'Primavera' takes readers on a poetic journey that will resonate long after the last page is turned.

Genres: Poetry, Anthology

Themes: Nature, Love, Human Experience
