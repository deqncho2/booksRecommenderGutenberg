Synopsis: In 'Adolescents Only' by Cox, Irving E., a group of teenagers find themselves trapped in a mysterious and isolated boarding school, where they must navigate a series of mind-bending challenges and uncover the dark secrets that lie within. As they struggle to survive and escape, they form unlikely alliances and discover hidden strengths within themselves. With each twist and turn, the teenagers are forced to confront their deepest fears and question the true nature of their reality. Will they be able to unravel the enigma that surrounds them before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Young Adult

Themes: Identity, Friendship, Survival, Coming of Age
