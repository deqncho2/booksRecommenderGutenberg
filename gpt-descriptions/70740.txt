Synopsis: In 'The Dalrymples' by Agnes Giberne, a mysterious family moves into a small English village, capturing the attention and curiosity of the locals. As the story unfolds, secrets from the past are gradually revealed, leading to unexpected twists and turns. With elements of suspense, romance, and a touch of the supernatural, this captivating tale explores the complexities of human relationships and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Supernatural

Themes: Secrets and their consequences, Redemption, Love and forgiveness
