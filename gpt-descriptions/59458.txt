Synopsis: In 'The Earthman' by Cox, Irving E., a young astronaut named Mark finds himself stranded on an alien planet after a catastrophic space mission. As he navigates this strange and hostile world, Mark encounters a mysterious indigenous species and forms an unlikely bond with one of them. Together, they embark on a perilous journey to uncover the truth about the planet's history and the secrets it holds. Filled with suspense, adventure, and unexpected twists, 'The Earthman' takes readers on an enthralling exploration of survival, friendship, and the resilience of the human spirit.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Exploration, Identity
