Synopsis: In 'The Turn of the Tide: The Story of How Margaret Solved Her Problem', Margaret finds herself facing a difficult situation that threatens to upend her life. With determination and resourcefulness, she embarks on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth, navigating through unexpected twists and turns. As she confronts her problem head-on, Margaret learns valuable lessons about resilience, courage, and the power of inner strength. Will she be able to overcome the odds and find a way to turn the tide in her favor?

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Drama

Themes: Resilience, Self-discovery, Personal growth
