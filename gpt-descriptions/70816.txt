Synopsis: In 'Lost on Venus' by Edgar Rice Burroughs, Carson Napier, an adventurous Earthman, finds himself stranded on the mysterious planet of Venus. As he explores the treacherous jungles and encounters strange creatures, Carson becomes entangled in a web of political intrigue and forbidden love. With danger lurking at every turn, Carson must navigate the perilous landscape and uncover the secrets of this enigmatic world.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Survival, Forbidden Love
