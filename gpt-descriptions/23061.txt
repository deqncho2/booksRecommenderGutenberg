Synopsis: In 'The Dead Are Silent: 1907' by Arthur Schnitzler, a renowned Viennese doctor finds himself entangled in a web of secrets and desires when he attends a mysterious masked ball. As the night unfolds, he becomes captivated by a beautiful woman who seems to hold the key to his deepest desires. However, as the doctor delves deeper into the enigmatic world of the masked ball, he discovers that nothing is as it seems, and the line between reality and illusion becomes increasingly blurred. With each revelation, the doctor is forced to confront his own desires and the consequences of his actions, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning the nature of identity and the power of the subconscious mind.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Desire, Identity, Illusion, Subconscious mind
