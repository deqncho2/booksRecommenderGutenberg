Synopsis: In 'The White Shield' by Reed, Myrtle, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she inherits a mysterious white shield. As she delves deeper into its history, she uncovers a hidden world of ancient legends, forbidden love, and a powerful curse that threatens to consume her. With danger lurking at every turn, Mary must navigate treacherous alliances and make difficult choices to break the curse and protect those she holds dear. Will she be able to unravel the truth behind the white shield before it's too late?

Genres: Mystery, Romance, Fantasy

Themes: Secrets and deception, Forbidden love, Fate and destiny
