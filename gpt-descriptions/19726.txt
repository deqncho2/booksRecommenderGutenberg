Synopsis: In "The Door Through Space" by Marion Zimmer Bradley, a young woman named Kennon embarks on a dangerous mission to a distant planet. Tasked with uncovering the truth behind a mysterious alien race, she must navigate treacherous landscapes, encounter strange beings, and confront her own inner demons. As Kennon delves deeper into the secrets of this enigmatic world, she discovers that the key to her mission lies in unlocking the power of the ancient doorways scattered across the planet. With her life hanging in the balance, Kennon must make a choice that will not only determine her own fate but also the fate of the entire universe.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Power, Redemption
