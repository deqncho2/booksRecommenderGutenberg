Synopsis: In 'Academica' by Cicero, Marcus Tullius, a group of friends embarks on a philosophical journey that challenges their beliefs and forces them to question the nature of truth. As they engage in lively debates and explore different schools of thought, they encounter unexpected twists and revelations that keep them on the edge of their seats. This thought-provoking work delves into the depths of human knowledge and the pursuit of wisdom, leaving readers captivated by its intellectual intrigue.

Genres: Philosophy, Fiction, Dialogue

Themes: Truth, Knowledge, Skepticism, Wisdom
