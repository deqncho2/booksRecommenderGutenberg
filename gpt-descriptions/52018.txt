Synopsis: In "Tilda Jane: An Orphan in Search of a Home," young Tilda Jane finds herself alone in the world after the death of her parents. Determined to find a place where she truly belongs, she embarks on a journey filled with unexpected encounters, heartwarming friendships, and challenging obstacles. From bustling cities to serene countryside, Tilda Jane's quest takes her on a captivating adventure that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Adventure, Coming-of-age, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Belonging, Resilience
