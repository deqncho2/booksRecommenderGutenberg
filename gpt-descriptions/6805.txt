Synopsis: In 'The Mill Mystery' by Anna Katharine Green, a small New England town is shaken by a mysterious death at the local mill. As the investigation unfolds, secrets are unearthed, and the residents find themselves entangled in a web of deceit, betrayal, and hidden motives. With a brilliant detective at the helm, the truth behind the mill mystery slowly comes to light, revealing shocking revelations that will keep readers guessing until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Crime Fiction, Detective Fiction

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Small-town Secrets
