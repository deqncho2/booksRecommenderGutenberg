Synopsis: 
In 'Motor Matt's Short Circuit; or, The Mahout's Vow' by Stanley R. Matthews, readers are taken on a thrilling journey alongside the adventurous Motor Matt. When a mysterious message leads him to the exotic land of India, Matt finds himself entangled in a web of danger and intrigue. With his quick wit and mechanical skills, he must navigate treacherous landscapes, outsmart cunning adversaries, and uncover the truth behind a puzzling vow. As the stakes rise, Motor Matt's determination and resourcefulness are put to the ultimate test, making this a gripping tale that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Loyalty
