Synopsis: In 'Bones and I: $b or, the skeleton at home' by G. J. Whyte-Melville, a young man named Jack finds himself in possession of a mysterious skeleton, which he affectionately names Bones. As Jack navigates the challenges of his everyday life, Bones becomes an unexpected companion, offering guidance and insight into the world around them. Together, they embark on a series of adventures that blur the lines between reality and imagination, leading Jack to question the true nature of his newfound friend and the secrets that lie within Bones' ancient bones.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Fantasy

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Perception of Reality
