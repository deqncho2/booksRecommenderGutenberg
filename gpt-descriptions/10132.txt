Synopsis: In 'The Sowers' by Henry Seton Merriman, a young Englishman named John Stirling finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal vendettas while living in a small Balkan country. As he navigates the treacherous landscape of power struggles and secret societies, John becomes torn between his loyalty to his new friends and his own sense of justice. With danger lurking at every turn, he must make difficult choices that will shape not only his own destiny but also the fate of an entire nation.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Political Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Power, Justice
