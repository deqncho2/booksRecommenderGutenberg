Synopsis: 
In 'Public Speaking' by Clarence Stratton, a renowned public speaker mysteriously disappears just days before a crucial speech that could change the course of history. As his loyal assistant delves into the enigmatic world of public speaking, she uncovers a web of secrets, rivalries, and hidden agendas that threaten to unravel everything she thought she knew. With time running out, she must navigate through a labyrinth of deception and betrayal to find the truth and save her mentor's reputation. This gripping tale of intrigue and suspense will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the final page.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Power of persuasion, Trust and betrayal
