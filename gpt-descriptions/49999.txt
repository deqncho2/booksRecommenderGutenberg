Synopsis: In 'Eminent Authors of the Nineteenth Century: Literary Portraits', Georg Brandes takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives and minds of some of the most influential writers of the 1800s. From the passionate romantics to the introspective realists, Brandes delves into the personal struggles, creative processes, and societal impact of these eminent authors. Through vivid and insightful portrayals, he unveils the hidden depths and complexities that shaped their literary masterpieces, offering a unique perspective on their genius and the era they inhabited.

Genres: Biography, Literary Criticism, History

Themes: Artistic expression, Individualism, Social change, Romanticism, Realism
