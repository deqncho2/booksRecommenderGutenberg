Synopsis: In the enchanting tale of 'Rose Cottage', a young woman named Emily finds herself inheriting a mysterious cottage in the English countryside. As she delves into the secrets of the cottage's past, she uncovers a hidden world of love, loss, and unexpected connections. With each discovery, Emily's own life begins to intertwine with the lives of those who came before her, leading her on a journey of self-discovery and the power of forgiveness.

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Family secrets, Love and loss, Redemption, Connection between past and present
