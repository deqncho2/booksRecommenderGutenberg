Synopsis: 
Step into the world of Methuen & Co. Ltd. with their latest selection of books published in September 1911. From thrilling adventures to captivating romances, this collection offers a diverse range of stories that will transport readers to different times and places. Discover tales of love and betrayal, mysteries waiting to be unraveled, and journeys that will leave you breathless. With each turn of the page, you'll find yourself immersed in the vivid imaginations of talented authors, eagerly awaiting what lies ahead.

Genres: Fiction, Adventure, Romance, Mystery, Historical

Themes: Love and Betrayal, Exploration, Intrigue, Time and Place
