Synopsis: In "Of the Capture of Ticonderoga: His Captivity and Treatment by the British," Ethan Allen recounts his gripping experience of being captured by the British during the American Revolutionary War. From the moment of his capture to his subsequent imprisonment, Allen takes readers on a suspenseful journey filled with unexpected twists and turns. As he endures the harsh treatment by his captors, Allen's resilience and determination shine through, making this a captivating tale of survival and resilience.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Memoir, War literature

Themes: Patriotism, Resilience, Survival
