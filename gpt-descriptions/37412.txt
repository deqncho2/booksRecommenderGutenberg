Synopsis: In 'The Empty Sack' by Basil King, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small town, carrying an empty sack that seems to hold a secret. As the townspeople become increasingly curious about the contents of the sack, they are drawn into a web of intrigue and unexpected consequences. With each passing day, the stranger's presence becomes more enigmatic, leaving the townsfolk questioning their own desires and the true nature of the empty sack.

Genres: Mystery, Psychological Thriller, Literary Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden truths, the power of perception, the human desire for meaning and fulfillment
