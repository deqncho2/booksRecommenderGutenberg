Synopsis: In "Historic Doubts on the Life and Reign of King Richard the Third," Horace Walpole delves into the controversial history surrounding the infamous King Richard III. Through meticulous research and a keen eye for detail, Walpole challenges the traditional narrative, raising thought-provoking doubts about Richard's character and actions. As he unravels the mysteries surrounding this enigmatic figure, the reader is taken on a captivating journey through the annals of history, questioning long-held beliefs and uncovering hidden truths.

Genres: Historical fiction, Biography, Mystery

Themes: Historical revisionism, Power and ambition, Perception versus reality
