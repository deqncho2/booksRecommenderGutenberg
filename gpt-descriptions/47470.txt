Synopsis:
In the small English village of Blackstable, a young and beautiful woman named Bertha Ley leads a seemingly idyllic life. However, her world is turned upside down when she falls in love with a charming and enigmatic artist named Edward Craddock. As their passionate affair unfolds, Bertha must confront the complexities of love, desire, and the limitations of societal expectations. Set against the backdrop of late 19th-century England, "Mrs. Craddock" is a captivating exploration of the human heart and the choices we make in the pursuit of happiness.

Genres:
Romance, Drama, Historical Fiction

Themes:
Love and desire, societal expectations, personal fulfillment
