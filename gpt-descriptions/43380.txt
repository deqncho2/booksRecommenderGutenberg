Synopsis: In 'Aunt Jane' by Jennette Lee, a young woman named Mary finds herself caught between the traditional expectations of her family and her own desire for independence. When her unconventional Aunt Jane comes to visit, Mary's world is turned upside down as she is exposed to new ideas and possibilities. As Mary navigates the complexities of love, societal norms, and personal growth, she must decide whether to follow the path set out for her or forge her own way.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Independence, Tradition, Self-discovery
