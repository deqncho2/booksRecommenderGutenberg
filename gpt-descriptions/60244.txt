Synopsis:
In 'Larry Dexter, Reporter; Or, Strange Adventures in a Great City' by Howard Roger Garis, readers are introduced to Larry Dexter, a young and ambitious reporter who finds himself entangled in a web of mysterious events in the bustling city. As Larry delves deeper into his investigations, he uncovers a series of strange occurrences that lead him on a thrilling journey filled with danger, suspense, and unexpected twists. With his determination and quick thinking, Larry must navigate through the dark underbelly of the city, facing both physical and moral challenges, in order to uncover the truth behind these strange adventures.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Ambition, Morality, Truth-seeking
