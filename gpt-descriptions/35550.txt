Synopsis: In 'Archimedes' by Thomas Little Heath, embark on a captivating journey through the life and mind of one of history's greatest mathematicians. This enthralling biography delves into the brilliant mind of Archimedes, exploring his groundbreaking discoveries, his struggles against adversity, and the profound impact he had on the world. From his ingenious inventions to his quest for knowledge, this book unveils the remarkable story of a true genius.

Genres: Biography, History, Mathematics

Themes: Genius and creativity, Scientific discovery, Persistence and resilience
