Synopsis: In 'Conversation: What to Say and How to Say it', Mary Greer Conklin takes readers on a captivating journey through the art of conversation. With insightful anecdotes and practical advice, she explores the power of words and the impact they can have on our relationships and personal growth. From mastering small talk to navigating difficult discussions, this book offers valuable guidance on how to communicate effectively and authentically. Whether you're an introvert looking to enhance your social skills or simply seeking to deepen your connections with others, Conklin's expertise will inspire you to embrace the transformative potential of conversation.

Genres: Self-help, Communication, Psychology

Themes: Effective communication, Social skills, Personal growth
