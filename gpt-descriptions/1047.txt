Synopsis:
In "The New Machiavelli," H.G. Wells takes us on a captivating journey through the life of Richard Remington, a man who finds himself at a crossroads after a scandalous affair threatens to ruin his career and personal life. Seeking solace and redemption, Remington embarks on a quest for self-discovery, encountering a mysterious mentor who introduces him to a world of power, politics, and manipulation. As Remington delves deeper into this new realm, he must grapple with the moral implications of his actions and confront the consequences of his choices. With its gripping narrative and thought-provoking exploration of human nature, "The New Machiavelli" is a compelling tale that will leave readers questioning the boundaries of ambition and the price of success.

Genres: Fiction, Political Thriller, Psychological Drama

Themes: Power, Morality, Ambition, Redemption
