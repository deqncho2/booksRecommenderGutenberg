Synopsis: In 'Rupert's Ambition' by Alger, Horatio, Jr., follow the captivating journey of Rupert, a young and ambitious protagonist who dreams of a better life. Faced with adversity and setbacks, Rupert must navigate a world filled with unexpected challenges and make difficult choices that will shape his future. As he strives to achieve his goals, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters and experiences both triumph and heartbreak. Will Rupert's determination and resilience be enough to overcome the obstacles in his path? Find out in this gripping tale of ambition, perseverance, and the pursuit of dreams.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Drama

Themes: Ambition, Perseverance, Social mobility
