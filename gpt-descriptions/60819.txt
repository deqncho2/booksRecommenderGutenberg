Synopsis: 
"The Lathe & Its Uses" is a comprehensive guide written by James Lukin that delves into the art of turning wood and metal. This instructional book not only provides detailed instructions on the techniques of turning, but also explores the most modern appliances for embellishing both plane and curved surfaces. In addition, Lukin introduces an entirely novel form of lathe for eccentric and rose engine turning, as well as a lathe and planing machine combined. With valuable information and insights, this book is a must-read for anyone interested in mastering the art of turning.

Genres: 
Instructional, Crafts, Woodworking

Themes: 
Artistic craftsmanship, Innovation in machinery
