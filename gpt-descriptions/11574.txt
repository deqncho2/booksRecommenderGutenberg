Synopsis: In 'Master Skylark: A Story of Shakspere's Time', young Nick Attwood finds himself caught up in a thrilling adventure when he is mistaken for a boy actor and becomes part of Shakespeare's company. As he navigates the world of the theater and encounters the famous playwright himself, Nick's life takes an unexpected turn filled with danger, secrets, and the magic of the stage. Will he be able to keep his true identity hidden and fulfill his dreams? Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-Age. Themes: Identity, Dreams and Ambitions, The Power of Theater.
