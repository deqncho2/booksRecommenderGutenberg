Synopsis: In 'The Young Engineers in Arizona; or, Laying Tracks on the Man-killer Quicksand' by H. Irving Hancock, a group of young engineers embarks on a thrilling adventure in the treacherous Arizona desert. As they work to lay tracks for a new railroad, they encounter dangerous quicksand that threatens their progress and their lives. With their skills and determination, the young engineers must navigate the deadly quicksand and overcome various obstacles to complete their mission. Will they succeed in their endeavor or will the unforgiving desert claim their lives?

Genres: Adventure, Action, Young Adult

Themes: Perseverance, Teamwork, Survival
