Synopsis: In 'The Big Idea' by Cummings, Ray, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a groundbreaking discovery that could change the course of humanity. As he delves deeper into his research, he becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, where the line between right and wrong blurs. With the fate of the world hanging in the balance, he must navigate through a maze of secrets and betrayal to protect his invention from falling into the wrong hands.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Ethics, Power, Consequences
