Synopsis: In the third volume of "What Will He Do with It?" the reader is taken deeper into the intricate lives of the characters as their destinies intertwine and secrets are revealed. Amidst the backdrop of political turmoil and personal struggles, the protagonist faces a pivotal decision that will shape his future and the lives of those around him. With unexpected twists and turns, this volume delves into the complexities of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness.

Genres: Historical fiction, romance, drama

Themes: Love and relationships, personal growth, societal expectations, political intrigue
