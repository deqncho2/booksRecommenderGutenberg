Synopsis: 
In "Rambles Beyond Railways; or, Notes in Cornwall taken A-foot," Collins, Wilkie takes readers on an enchanting journey through the picturesque landscapes of Cornwall. Through his vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, he invites us to join him as he explores the hidden gems and untrodden paths of this beautiful region. From ancient ruins to breathtaking coastlines, Collins paints a vivid picture of Cornwall's natural beauty and rich history, while also delving into the lives and stories of the people he encounters along the way. This delightful travelogue is a testament to the joy of wandering off the beaten path and discovering the wonders that lie beyond the railways.

Genres: Travelogue, Non-fiction, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Nature, History, Cultural encounters
