Synopsis: In "Witchcraft of New England Explained by Modern Spiritualism," Putnam Allen delves into the mysterious world of witchcraft that once plagued the region. Through a combination of historical research and spiritual insights, the author uncovers the hidden truths behind the witch trials and the supernatural occurrences that have long fascinated and haunted New England. From Salem to the remote villages, this captivating exploration sheds light on the dark secrets and unexplained phenomena that continue to bewitch the imagination.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Occult

Themes: Witchcraft, Supernatural, History, Belief Systems
