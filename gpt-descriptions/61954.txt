Synopsis: In 'The Lady's Walk' by Oliphant, Mrs. (Margaret), a young woman named Lucy embarks on a mysterious journey through a secluded woodland path. As she delves deeper into the enchanting forest, Lucy encounters a series of strange and captivating events that challenge her perception of reality. With each step, she unravels the secrets hidden within the Lady's Walk, leading her to question her own identity and the true nature of the world around her.

Genres: Mystery, Gothic Fiction, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Perception, Mystery, Supernatural elements
