Synopsis: In 'The Lance of Kanana: A Story of Arabia', follow the thrilling adventures of a young Englishman named Hugh as he embarks on a perilous journey through the mysterious and enchanting land of Arabia. Drawn into a web of intrigue and danger, Hugh finds himself entangled in a quest for a legendary artifact that holds the power to change the fate of nations. With treacherous enemies lurking at every turn and the fate of his newfound love hanging in the balance, Hugh must summon all his courage and resourcefulness to navigate the treacherous sands of Arabia and uncover the truth behind the Lance of Kanana.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Quest for Power, Love and Sacrifice
