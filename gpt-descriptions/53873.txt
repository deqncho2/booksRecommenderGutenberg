Synopsis: 

In "Old Coaching Days: Some Incidents in the Life of Moses James Nobbs, the last of the Mail Coach Guards," we are transported back to a bygone era when mail coaches ruled the roads. Moses James Nobbs, the last surviving mail coach guard, shares his captivating and often humorous experiences from his time on the road. From daring encounters with highwaymen to unexpected friendships formed along the way, Nobbs takes us on a thrilling journey through the golden age of coaching.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Memoir, Adventure

Themes: Nostalgia, Courage, Camaraderie, Change
