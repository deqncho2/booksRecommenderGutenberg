Synopsis: In "The Heart of the Rose" by Mabel Anne McKee, a young woman named Rose finds herself caught in a web of mystery and romance when she inherits a beautiful estate from a distant relative. As she delves into the secrets of the house, she uncovers a hidden world filled with enchantment and danger. With the help of a charming stranger, Rose must navigate through treacherous alliances and ancient curses to uncover the truth about her family's past and her own destiny. This captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to unravel the mysteries that lie within the heart of the rose.

Genres: Fantasy, Romance, Mystery

Themes: Family secrets, Destiny, Love and sacrifice
