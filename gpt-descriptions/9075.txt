Synopsis: Rico and Wiseli is a captivating tale that follows the lives of two young orphans, Rico and Wiseli, as they navigate the challenges of their circumstances. Set in the picturesque Swiss Alps, this heartwarming story takes readers on a journey filled with unexpected friendships, resilience, and the power of hope. As Rico and Wiseli face various trials and tribulations, their unwavering spirits and determination to find happiness will leave readers eagerly turning the pages, eager to discover what lies ahead for these endearing characters.

Genres: Children's literature, Fiction, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Resilience, Hope, Overcoming adversity
