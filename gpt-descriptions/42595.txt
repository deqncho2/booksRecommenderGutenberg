Synopsis: In 'The Last Call: A Romance (Vol. 1 of 3)' by Dowling, Richard, we are introduced to the enigmatic world of a small coastal town where secrets and desires intertwine. When a mysterious stranger named Gabriel arrives at the local bar, he captivates the attention of the townspeople, especially Emma, a young woman with a troubled past. As their paths cross, a forbidden romance begins to blossom, leading them on a journey of self-discovery, passion, and the unraveling of long-held secrets. With each encounter, the stakes grow higher, and the town's dark underbelly threatens to expose their forbidden love. Will Emma and Gabriel be able to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way, or will their romance be doomed from the start?

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Drama

Themes: Forbidden love, Secrets, Redemption
