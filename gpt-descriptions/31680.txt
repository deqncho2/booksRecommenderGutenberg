Synopsis: In 'Generals Help Themselves' by M. C. Pease, a retired general finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue and personal vendettas when he is unexpectedly called back into service. As he navigates the treacherous world of military politics, he must confront his own demons and make difficult choices that will determine the fate of his country. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Political thriller, Military fiction, Suspense

Themes: Power struggles, Loyalty, Redemption
