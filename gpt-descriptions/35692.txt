Synopsis: 

"The History of Company A, Second Illinois Cavalry" is a captivating historical account that delves into the experiences and adventures of a courageous cavalry unit during the American Civil War. Written by Fletcher, D. H. and Fletcher, Samuel H., this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through the battles, hardships, and triumphs of Company A. From their initial formation and training to their involvement in pivotal conflicts, the narrative vividly portrays the camaraderie, bravery, and sacrifices of these soldiers as they navigate the tumultuous landscape of war.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Military History, Biography

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Resilience
