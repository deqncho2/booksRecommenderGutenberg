Synopsis: 

'Narrative of Henry Box Brown: Who Escaped from Slavery Enclosed in a Box 3 Feet Long and 2 Wide' is a captivating memoir that tells the extraordinary true story of Henry Box Brown, a man who ingeniously escaped from slavery by mailing himself in a small box. 

This gripping narrative takes readers on a suspenseful journey as they follow Henry's harrowing escape, experiencing the physical and emotional challenges he faces along the way. From the depths of despair to the heights of hope, Henry's determination and resourcefulness shine through, making this a tale of resilience and triumph against all odds.

Genres: Memoir, Historical Non-fiction

Themes: Slavery, Freedom, Resilience, Human Spirit
