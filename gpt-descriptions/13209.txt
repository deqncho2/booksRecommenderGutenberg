Synopsis: In 'The Second Violin' by Grace S. Richmond, a talented musician finds herself torn between her passion for music and the expectations of society. As she navigates the challenges of love, ambition, and self-discovery, she must make difficult choices that will shape her future. Set against the backdrop of the early 20th century, this captivating novel explores the complexities of following one's dreams and the sacrifices that come with it.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Pursuit of passion, societal expectations, self-discovery
