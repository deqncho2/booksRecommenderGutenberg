Synopsis: In "A Queen of Nine Days," a young and ambitious queen ascends to the throne, only to face a tumultuous reign filled with political intrigue, betrayal, and heart-wrenching decisions. As she navigates the treacherous world of power, she must grapple with her own desires and the expectations placed upon her. With her reign hanging by a thread, she must summon the strength to fight for her kingdom and her own survival.

Genres: Historical fiction, drama, romance

Themes: Power struggles, loyalty, sacrifice, identity
