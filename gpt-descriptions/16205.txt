Synopsis: 
In 'The Physiology of Marriage, Complete', Balzac delves into the intricate and often scandalous world of marriage. Through a series of interconnected stories, he explores the secrets, desires, and power dynamics that shape the relationships between husbands and wives. From the struggles of newlyweds to the temptations of extramarital affairs, Balzac uncovers the hidden truths behind the institution of marriage, revealing the complexities and contradictions that lie beneath its surface.

Genres: Fiction, Satire, Comedy

Themes: Marriage, Love, Power dynamics, Gender roles, Infidelity
