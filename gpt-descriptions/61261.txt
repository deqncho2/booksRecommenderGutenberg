Synopsis: In "It's Fun to Sew a Cotton School Dress," Johnson, Theta takes readers on a delightful journey into the world of sewing as a young girl named Lily embarks on a special project. With the help of her grandmother, Lily learns the art of sewing and discovers the joy of creating her very own cotton school dress. As she navigates through the ups and downs of the sewing process, Lily not only gains valuable skills but also learns important life lessons about patience, perseverance, and the power of self-expression. This heartwarming tale is filled with colorful illustrations and relatable moments that will captivate readers of all ages.

Genres: Children's Literature, Picture Book

Themes: Creativity, Self-expression, Learning and Growth
