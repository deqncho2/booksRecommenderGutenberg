Synopsis: 
'Four-Day Planet' by H. Beam Piper is a thrilling science fiction novel set on the harsh and dangerous planet of Fenris. The story follows a young journalist named Floyd, who finds himself caught in the middle of a deadly conflict between the ruthless corporations exploiting the planet's resources and the rebellious colonists fighting for their rights. As Floyd uncovers shocking secrets and faces life-threatening challenges, he must navigate the treacherous landscape of Fenris to expose the truth and protect the lives of those he cares about.

Genres: Science Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Corporate greed, Colonialism, Rebellion, Journalism
