Synopsis: In 'Dorothy's Double. Volume 2 (of 3)' by G. A. Henty, the captivating story continues as Dorothy and her look-alike cousin, Kate, find themselves embroiled in a dangerous adventure. As they navigate treacherous waters and encounter mysterious characters, the girls must rely on their wit and courage to unravel a web of secrets and protect their loved ones. With unexpected twists and heart-pounding moments, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what fate has in store for Dorothy and Kate.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Historical Fiction

Themes: Identity, Loyalty, Courage
