Synopsis: 
In 'The Count of Monte Cristo', a young and ambitious sailor named Edmond Dantès finds himself betrayed and imprisoned on false charges. After years of captivity, he manages to escape and discovers a hidden treasure that grants him immense wealth and power. Under the guise of the enigmatic Count of Monte Cristo, he sets out on a mission of revenge against those who wronged him, using his newfound resources to manipulate their lives and bring them to ruin. As he navigates the complex web of deceit and vengeance, Dantès must confront the moral implications of his actions and question the true nature of justice.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Revenge, Betrayal, Justice, Morality
