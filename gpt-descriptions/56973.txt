Synopsis: In 'Adrift in the City; or, Oliver Conrad's Plucky Fight' by Horatio Alger, Jr., readers are introduced to Oliver Conrad, a young man who finds himself alone and struggling to survive in the bustling city. With determination and resourcefulness, Oliver embarks on a journey filled with unexpected encounters, challenges, and opportunities. As he navigates the urban landscape, Oliver's plucky spirit and unwavering resolve are put to the test, leading him to discover the true meaning of resilience and the power of self-belief.

Genres: Coming-of-age, Adventure, Fiction

Themes: Perseverance, Self-reliance, Social mobility
