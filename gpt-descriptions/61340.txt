Synopsis: In 'The Art of Story-Telling' by Marie L. Shedlock, embark on a captivating journey through the enchanting world of storytelling. Follow the author as she delves into the secrets and techniques that make stories come alive, exploring the power of imagination and the transformative impact of a well-told tale. From ancient myths to modern narratives, Shedlock unravels the threads that bind us to the stories we love, revealing the timeless magic that lies within the art of storytelling.

Genres: Non-fiction, Literary Criticism, Writing

Themes: Imagination, Transformation, Power of Stories
