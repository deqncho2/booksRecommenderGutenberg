Synopsis: 
In "Counsel for the Defense" by Leroy Scott, a brilliant defense attorney finds himself caught in a high-stakes murder trial that challenges his skills and ethics. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers shocking secrets and faces a moral dilemma that could jeopardize everything he holds dear. With the clock ticking and the pressure mounting, he must navigate a treacherous legal landscape to uncover the truth and save his client's life.

Genres: Legal thriller, Mystery, Suspense

Themes: Justice and morality, Loyalty and betrayal
