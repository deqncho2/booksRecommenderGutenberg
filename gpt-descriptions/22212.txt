Synopsis: In 'The Paternoster Ruby' by Charles Edmonds Walk, a renowned detective is called upon to solve the mysterious disappearance of a priceless ruby necklace. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and unexpected alliances. With each twist and turn, Walk must navigate through a labyrinth of clues and suspects, all while racing against time to recover the precious gem and bring the culprits to justice. This thrilling mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly following Walk's every move as he unravels the truth behind the Paternoster Ruby.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Justice
