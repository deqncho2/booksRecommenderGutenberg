Synopsis: In "The satellite-keeper's daughter" by Mark Reinsberg, a young girl named Lily finds herself caught between two worlds - the isolated, mysterious satellite station where her father works, and the bustling city where she longs to belong. As she uncovers secrets about her family's past and the true purpose of the satellite station, Lily must navigate a dangerous journey of self-discovery and face the consequences of her choices. With a blend of adventure, mystery, and a touch of science fiction, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Young Adult, Science Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Identity, Family, Secrets, Coming of Age
