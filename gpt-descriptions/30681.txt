Synopsis: In 'Nanny Merry: or, What Made the Difference?' a young orphan named Lucy finds herself in the care of a mysterious and unconventional nanny. As Nanny Merry introduces Lucy to a world of wonder and imagination, the young girl's life takes an unexpected turn. With secrets to uncover and lessons to learn, Lucy embarks on a transformative journey that will forever change her perspective on life and love.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Mystery

Themes: Imagination, Transformation, Unconventional wisdom
