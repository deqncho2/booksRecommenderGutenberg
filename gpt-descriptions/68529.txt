Synopsis: In 'Firegod' by Algis Budrys, a renowned scientist is tasked with investigating a mysterious phenomenon that threatens to engulf the world in flames. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he uncovers a web of ancient secrets, dangerous alliances, and a power that could reshape the very fabric of reality. With time running out, he must navigate treacherous paths and confront his own inner demons to prevent the cataclysmic destruction that awaits. 

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Mystery

Themes: Power and its consequences, the pursuit of knowledge, the nature of reality
