Synopsis: In 'The Fortunes of Philippa: A School Story', we follow the journey of Philippa, a young girl who finds herself in a new school filled with mystery and intrigue. As she navigates the challenges of friendship, academic pressures, and the secrets that lie within the school's walls, Philippa discovers her own strength and resilience. With unexpected twists and turns, this captivating tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the truth behind Philippa's extraordinary journey.

Genres: Mystery, Coming-of-age, School

Themes: Friendship, Identity, Resilience
