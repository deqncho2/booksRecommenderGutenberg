Synopsis: In 'The Camp Fire Girls Behind the Lines' by Margaret Vandercook, a group of adventurous and resourceful Camp Fire Girls find themselves caught up in the midst of World War I. As they navigate the dangers and challenges of war-torn Europe, they must rely on their courage, ingenuity, and the bonds of friendship to overcome the obstacles that stand in their way. With their determination and unwavering spirit, these young girls prove that even in the darkest of times, hope and resilience can prevail.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Resilience, Coming of Age
