Synopsis: In 'White Fire' by Roy J. Snell, a young adventurer named Jack Darrow finds himself caught in a thrilling mystery when he stumbles upon a hidden treasure map. With danger lurking at every turn, Jack embarks on a perilous journey through treacherous landscapes, encountering enigmatic characters and facing unexpected challenges. As he delves deeper into the mystery, Jack must unravel the secrets of the past and confront the white-hot flames of danger that threaten to consume him.

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Treasure hunting, Survival, Courage, Betrayal
