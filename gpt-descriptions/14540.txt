Synopsis: 
In 'When William Came', Saki takes us on a thrilling journey to a world turned upside down. Set in an alternate reality, the story unfolds in a Britain occupied by a foreign power. As the nation grapples with the consequences of this invasion, we follow the lives of various characters who navigate the complexities of loyalty, resistance, and the loss of personal freedoms. Amidst the political turmoil, Saki weaves a tale of suspense and intrigue, leaving readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the fate of this transformed society.

Genres: 
Dystopian fiction, Political thriller, Satire

Themes: 
Occupation, Patriotism, Resistance, Loss of freedom
