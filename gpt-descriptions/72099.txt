Synopsis: In "Citizen or Subject?" by Francis X. Hennessy, a gripping tale unfolds as a young woman finds herself torn between her duty as a citizen and her desire for personal freedom. Set in a dystopian society where individuality is suppressed, she embarks on a dangerous journey to challenge the oppressive regime and discover her true identity. As she navigates through a web of secrets and betrayals, the reader is taken on a thrilling ride filled with suspense and unexpected twists.

Genres: Dystopian fiction, Thriller, Science fiction

Themes: Identity, Freedom, Resistance, Power dynamics
