Synopsis: In 'The Lady of the Ice: A Novel' by De Mille, James, a mysterious woman named Eliza arrives in a small town, captivating the locals with her beauty and enigmatic presence. As the townspeople become increasingly infatuated with her, they uncover a web of secrets and dark pasts that threaten to unravel their lives. With each passing day, Eliza's true nature becomes more elusive, leaving the townspeople questioning their own sanity and the depths of their desires. This gripping tale of obsession, deception, and the power of attraction will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Psychological Fiction

Themes: Obsession, Deception, Identity
