Synopsis: 

In 'The Story of Troy', the legendary city of Troy becomes the backdrop for an epic tale of love, war, and destiny. As the Trojan War looms, the lives of gods and mortals intertwine, setting the stage for a conflict that will shape the course of history. From the valiant Achilles to the cunning Odysseus, from the beautiful Helen to the wise Priam, this gripping narrative explores the triumphs and tragedies of those caught in the midst of a war that will test their loyalty, courage, and humanity. 

Genres: Epic, Historical Fiction, Mythology

Themes: Fate and Free Will, Honor and Glory, Love and Betrayal
