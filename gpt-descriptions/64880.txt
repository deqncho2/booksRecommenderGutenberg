Synopsis: In 'The Facts of Life' by P. Schuyler Miller, a brilliant scientist makes a groundbreaking discovery that challenges everything we know about the nature of reality. As he delves deeper into his research, he uncovers a hidden world of secrets and conspiracies that threaten to unravel his sanity and endanger the lives of those around him. With each revelation, the line between science and the supernatural becomes increasingly blurred, leaving the reader questioning what is real and what is merely a product of the human imagination.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Thriller

Themes: Reality vs. Illusion, Scientific Exploration, Conspiracy
