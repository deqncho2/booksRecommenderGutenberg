Synopsis: In "Prize Ship" by Philip K. Dick, a group of space explorers stumble upon a mysterious abandoned ship drifting in the vastness of space. As they investigate the vessel, they uncover a series of perplexing and unsettling discoveries that challenge their understanding of reality and their own identities. With each revelation, the crew becomes entangled in a web of intrigue and danger, leading them to question the nature of existence itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Mystery, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Identity, Reality vs. Illusion, Exploration of the Unknown
