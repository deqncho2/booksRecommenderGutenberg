Synopsis: 
Step back in time and immerse yourself in the ancient world of Babylon and Assyria as you explore their fascinating laws, contracts, and letters. In this captivating collection, C. H. W. Johns takes you on a journey through the legal and administrative systems of these ancient civilizations, revealing the intricacies of their social, economic, and political structures. From the detailed contracts that governed trade and commerce to the personal letters that offer glimpses into the daily lives of individuals, this book offers a unique window into the past. Discover the ancient codes of justice, the complexities of land ownership, and the art of negotiation in this captivating exploration of Babylonian and Assyrian legal documents.

Genres: History, Archaeology, Legal Studies

Themes: Ancient civilizations, Legal systems, Social structures, Economic practices
