Synopsis: In 'Frank Merriwell's Cruise', Frank and his friends embark on an exciting adventure aboard a luxurious yacht. As they set sail, they encounter mysterious characters, face unexpected challenges, and uncover hidden secrets that test their courage and loyalty. With danger lurking around every corner, Frank must rely on his wit and determination to navigate through treacherous waters and protect his friends. Will they be able to unravel the mysteries that surround them and make it safely back to shore?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Loyalty
