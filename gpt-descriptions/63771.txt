Synopsis: In "The Clay Industries, Including the Fictile & Ceramic Arts on the Banks of the Severn," John Randall takes us on a captivating journey through the history of pottery and porcelain in the Shropshire district. From the early use of local clays to the development of intricate ceramic arts, this book unveils the fascinating stories behind the clay industries that flourished on the banks of the Severn. With vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, Randall paints a vivid picture of the craftsmen, their techniques, and the cultural significance of these industries. Delve into the world of clay and ceramics, and discover the rich heritage that shaped this region.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Art history, Local history

Themes: Industrial development, Craftsmanship, Cultural heritage
