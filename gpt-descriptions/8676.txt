Synopsis: 
In 'The Heavenly Twins' by Sarah Grand, readers are introduced to the captivating lives of Evadne and Ursula, identical twins who couldn't be more different. Evadne, the rebellious and free-spirited sister, challenges societal norms and fights for women's rights, while Ursula, the more conventional and reserved twin, struggles to find her own voice. Set against the backdrop of late 19th-century England, their intertwined stories explore love, passion, and the complexities of female identity. As the twins navigate their way through a world filled with secrets, scandals, and unexpected twists, readers will be drawn into a tale that challenges traditional notions of femininity and explores the power of sisterhood.

Genres: Fiction, Historical Fiction, Feminist Literature

Themes: Gender roles, Women's rights, Identity, Sisterhood
