Synopsis: 
In the remote countryside of England, a group of friends embarks on a seemingly innocent adventure to explore an abandoned house. Little do they know, this house holds a dark secret that will test their sanity and challenge their beliefs. As they delve deeper into the mysteries of the house, they find themselves trapped in a web of supernatural occurrences and malevolent forces that threaten to consume them. With each passing day, their grip on reality weakens, and they must confront their deepest fears to survive the horrors that await them within the walls of the damned house.

Genres: Horror, Supernatural, Psychological Thriller

Themes: Fear, Sanity, Supernatural forces, Psychological torment
