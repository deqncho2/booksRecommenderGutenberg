Synopsis: In 'An Apostate: Nawin of Thais' by Sills, Steven David Justin, readers are introduced to Nawin, a young man who embarks on a journey of self-discovery and rebellion against societal norms. Set in the vibrant city of Bangkok, Nawin's life takes an unexpected turn when he encounters a mysterious woman who challenges his beliefs and pushes him to question his place in the world. As Nawin navigates through the complexities of love, spirituality, and personal freedom, he must confront the consequences of his choices and decide whether to conform or forge his own path.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Romance

Themes: Identity, Rebellion, Love, Spirituality
