Synopsis: 
In the fourth annual meeting of the Northern Nut Growers Association, held in Washington D.C. in 1913, a diverse group of horticulturists, botanists, and enthusiasts gather to discuss the cultivation and promotion of nut trees in the northern regions. As they exchange knowledge, share experiences, and present their latest research, the attendees delve into the fascinating world of nut cultivation, exploring innovative techniques, new varieties, and the potential economic benefits. Amidst the lively discussions and debates, unexpected discoveries and challenges arise, leaving the participants inspired and eager to continue their pursuit of nut-growing excellence.

Genres: Non-fiction, Horticulture, Botany

Themes: Nut cultivation, Horticultural innovation, Economic potential
