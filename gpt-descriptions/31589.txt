Synopsis:
In 'The Blue Ghost Mystery: A Rick Brant Science-Adventure Story', Rick Brant and his friend Scotty embark on an exhilarating journey filled with intrigue and danger. When a mysterious blue ghost ship appears off the coast of Spindrift Island, the boys find themselves caught up in a thrilling quest to uncover the truth behind its haunting presence. As they delve deeper into the enigma, they encounter secret codes, hidden treasures, and a web of deceit that puts their lives at risk. With their scientific knowledge and unwavering determination, Rick and Scotty must unravel the mystery before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Science Fiction

Themes: Exploration, Friendship, Problem-solving
