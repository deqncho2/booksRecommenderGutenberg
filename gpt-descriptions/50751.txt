Synopsis: In 'The Teacup Club' by Elisa Armstrong Bengough, a group of four women find solace and support in their weekly teacup club meetings. As they navigate the challenges of love, loss, and friendship, their lives intertwine in unexpected ways, leading to profound personal transformations. Secrets are revealed, bonds are tested, and the power of female connection is celebrated in this heartwarming tale of resilience and self-discovery.

Genres: Women's Fiction, Contemporary Fiction, Romance

Themes: Friendship, Healing, Self-Discovery, Resilience
