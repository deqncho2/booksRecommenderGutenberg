Synopsis: In 'Tyrants of Time' by Stephen Marlowe, a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon an ancient artifact that holds the power to manipulate time itself. As he delves deeper into its mysteries, he becomes entangled in a dangerous game of power and greed, where the line between past, present, and future blurs. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, he must navigate treacherous alliances and face unimaginable challenges to protect the fabric of time itself.

Genres: Science Fiction, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Time manipulation, Power and greed, Moral dilemmas
