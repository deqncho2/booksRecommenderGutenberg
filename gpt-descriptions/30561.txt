Synopsis: 

In 'His Last Week: The Story of the Passion and Resurrection of Jesus in the Words of the Four Gospels', authors Barton, Soares, and Strong bring to life the final week of Jesus Christ through the words of the four Gospels. This captivating retelling delves into the events leading up to Jesus' crucifixion, his resurrection, and the profound impact it had on his disciples and followers. Through a harmonious blend of scriptural passages, the authors paint a vivid picture of the emotional turmoil, spiritual significance, and ultimate triumph of Jesus' last days on Earth.

Genres: Religious, Historical, Spiritual

Themes: Faith, Sacrifice, Redemption, Divine Love
