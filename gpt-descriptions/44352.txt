Synopsis:
In 'The Shadow Passes: A Mystery Story for Boys' by Roy J. Snell, a group of adventurous boys find themselves caught up in a thrilling mystery when they stumble upon a hidden cave during a camping trip. As they delve deeper into the secrets of the cave, they uncover a series of puzzling clues that lead them on a dangerous journey filled with suspense, intrigue, and unexpected twists. With their friendship and resourcefulness put to the test, the boys must unravel the mystery before it's too late. 

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Exploration
