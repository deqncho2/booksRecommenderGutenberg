Synopsis: In 'Middy and Ensign' by George Manville Fenn, two young boys find themselves caught up in a thrilling adventure on the high seas. Middy, a resourceful and courageous sailor, and Ensign, a brave and determined officer, must navigate treacherous waters, face dangerous enemies, and uncover a hidden secret that could change the course of history. As they embark on their perilous journey, the boys learn the true meaning of loyalty, friendship, and the importance of standing up for what is right.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Friendship, Loyalty, Coming of Age
