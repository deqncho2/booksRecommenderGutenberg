Synopsis: In 'The Sign of the Stranger' by William Le Queux, a mysterious stranger arrives in a small English village, arousing suspicion and curiosity among the locals. As his presence becomes increasingly enigmatic, the villagers find themselves drawn into a web of secrets, danger, and unexpected alliances. With each passing day, the stranger's true intentions become more elusive, leaving everyone wondering who he really is and what he wants. As tensions rise and the truth slowly unravels, the village is forced to confront its own dark past and face the consequences of their actions.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Small-town secrets
