Synopsis: 
'Four Short Stories By Emile Zola' is a collection of gripping tales that delve into the dark underbelly of human nature. In the first story, a young woman's life takes a dramatic turn when she becomes entangled in a scandalous affair. The second story follows a struggling artist who finds himself caught in a web of deceit and betrayal. In the third story, a wealthy family's secrets threaten to destroy their carefully constructed facade. And finally, the fourth story explores the consequences of a forbidden love that transcends societal boundaries. Each story is filled with suspense, passion, and moral dilemmas that will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Realism

Themes: Love and desire, social class and inequality, morality and ethics
