Synopsis: In the midst of religious and political turmoil in 16th century France, the captivating and audacious Marguerite de Valois finds herself at the center of a dangerous game of power, love, and betrayal. As the daughter of a powerful king, she must navigate the treacherous waters of court intrigue, while also grappling with her own desires and ambitions. With her intelligence and charm, Marguerite becomes a key player in the tumultuous events that unfold, ultimately shaping the destiny of a nation.

Genres: Historical fiction, Romance, Adventure

Themes: Power struggles, Love and passion, Betrayal, Political intrigue
