Synopsis: In 'The Great Thames Barrage' by Thomas Walter Barber, a mysterious and ambitious project takes shape along the iconic River Thames, capturing the attention of the nation. As the construction progresses, secrets and hidden agendas begin to unravel, leading to a thrilling race against time to uncover the truth. With unexpected twists and turns, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Ambition, Deception, Power
