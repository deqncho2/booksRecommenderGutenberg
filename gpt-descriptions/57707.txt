Synopsis: In 'The Great War of 189-', Forbes, Murray, Colomb, Lowe, Maude, Maurice, and Scudamore come together to present a gripping forecast of a war that could change the course of history. Through their expert analysis and predictions, they paint a vivid picture of the potential conflicts, alliances, and strategies that may shape the world in the near future. As tensions rise and nations prepare for battle, the authors delve into the intricate web of political intrigue, military advancements, and the human cost of war. With their collective expertise, they offer a thought-provoking glimpse into a world on the brink of chaos.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Military Strategy, Political Analysis

Themes: War, Geopolitics, Human Cost
