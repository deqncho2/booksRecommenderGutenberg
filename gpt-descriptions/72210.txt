Synopsis: In 'Interference: A Novel, Vol. 3 (of 3)', the gripping conclusion to the thrilling series by B. M. Croker, secrets are unveiled, alliances are tested, and unexpected twists take center stage. As the lives of the characters intertwine, a web of deceit and manipulation begins to unravel, leaving readers on the edge of their seats. With danger lurking around every corner, the stakes are higher than ever, and the truth becomes a precious commodity that must be fought for. Will the characters find redemption or succumb to the interference that threatens to destroy everything they hold dear?

Genres: Mystery, Suspense, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Betrayal, Redemption
