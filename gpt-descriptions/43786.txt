Synopsis: In 'A Gentleman-at-Arms: Being Passages in the Life of Sir Christopher Rudd, Knight', we follow the captivating journey of Sir Christopher Rudd, a valiant knight who finds himself entangled in a web of political intrigue, forbidden love, and dangerous quests. From the grand halls of the royal court to the treacherous battlefields, Sir Christopher's loyalty and honor are put to the ultimate test as he navigates through a world filled with secrets, betrayals, and unexpected alliances. As his life becomes increasingly entwined with the fate of the kingdom, Sir Christopher must confront his own demons and make choices that will shape not only his own destiny but also the future of those he holds dear.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Loyalty, Honor, Betrayal, Love, Identity
