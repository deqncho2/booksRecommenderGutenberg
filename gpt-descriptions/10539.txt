Synopsis: In the Riding-School; Chats with Esmeralda takes readers on a captivating journey into the world of horse riding through a series of intriguing conversations with the enigmatic Esmeralda. As the protagonist delves deeper into the art of horsemanship, they uncover hidden secrets and unexpected revelations that challenge their perception of both horses and themselves. With each chat, the reader is drawn further into a mesmerizing tale that explores the profound connection between humans and animals, and the transformative power of passion and dedication.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Coming-of-age

Themes: Human-animal connection, Self-discovery, Passion and dedication
