Synopsis: In 'A Dominie in Doubt' by Alexander Sutherland Neill, a dedicated schoolmaster finds himself questioning his own beliefs and principles when faced with a challenging student. As he navigates the complexities of education, morality, and personal growth, the dominie's journey takes unexpected turns that will keep readers captivated until the very end.

Genres: Fiction, Coming-of-age, Psychological

Themes: Education, Morality, Self-discovery
