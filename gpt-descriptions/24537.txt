Synopsis: In "History of the Confederate Powder Works," George Washington Rains takes us on a captivating journey through the creation and operation of one of the most significant munitions factories in the American Civil War. From its humble beginnings to its pivotal role in supplying the Confederate Army, Rains unveils the untold stories of the men and women who worked tirelessly to produce the ammunition that shaped the outcome of the war. With vivid descriptions and compelling anecdotes, this book sheds light on the ingenuity, challenges, and sacrifices that went into the creation of this crucial Confederate establishment.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, Military history, Biography

Themes: Industrialization, War efforts, Civil War history
