Synopsis: In 'Far to Seek: A Romance of England and India', Maud Diver weaves a captivating tale that spans two continents and explores the complexities of love, duty, and cultural identity. Set against the backdrop of colonial India and Edwardian England, the story follows the lives of two individuals from vastly different backgrounds who find themselves irresistibly drawn to each other. As they navigate the challenges of societal expectations, family obligations, and personal desires, their journey takes them on a path filled with passion, sacrifice, and unexpected twists. This enthralling romance delves into the clash of cultures, the search for identity, and the power of love to transcend boundaries.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Cultural Identity, Love and Desire, Duty and Obligation
