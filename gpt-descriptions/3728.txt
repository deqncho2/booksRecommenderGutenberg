Synopsis: 
The Getting of Wisdom by Henry Handel Richardson is a captivating coming-of-age novel set in late 19th century Australia. The story follows the journey of Laura Rambotham, a young girl from a rural background, as she navigates her way through a prestigious boarding school in Melbourne. As Laura encounters the challenges of fitting in with her classmates, dealing with strict teachers, and discovering her own identity, she learns valuable lessons about friendship, love, and the complexities of social hierarchy. With its vivid portrayal of the school environment and the emotional turmoil of adolescence, this novel offers a compelling exploration of the universal themes of self-discovery and the pursuit of wisdom.

Genres: 
Coming-of-age, Bildungsroman, Historical fiction

Themes: 
Self-discovery, Identity, Social hierarchy, Education
