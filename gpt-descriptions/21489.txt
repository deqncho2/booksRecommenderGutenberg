Synopsis: 
In 'The Secret of the Island' by Jules Verne, a group of castaways find themselves stranded on a mysterious island after a shipwreck. As they explore their new surroundings, they stumble upon a hidden message that leads them on a thrilling adventure filled with danger, intrigue, and unexpected discoveries. With the help of their ingenuity and resourcefulness, the castaways must unravel the secrets of the island and find a way to escape. But as they delve deeper into the island's mysteries, they soon realize that their survival may depend on more than just their wits. 

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Science Fiction

Themes: Survival, Exploration, Ingenuity, Friendship
