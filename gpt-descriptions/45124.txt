Synopsis: 

'Great Facts: A Popular History and Description of the Most Remarkable Inventions During the Present Century' takes readers on an exhilarating journey through the groundbreaking inventions that have shaped the world we live in today. From the awe-inspiring wonders of technology to the ingenious creations of human ingenuity, this book unveils the remarkable stories behind these inventions, captivating readers with tales of innovation, perseverance, and the boundless potential of the human mind. 

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Innovation, Human Ingenuity, Technological Advancement
