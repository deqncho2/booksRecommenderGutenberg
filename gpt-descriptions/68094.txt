Synopsis: In 'Nick Carter Stories No. 149, July 17, 1915: A Network of Crime; or, Nick Carter's Tangled Skein,' Nick Carter finds himself entangled in a complex web of criminal activities. As he investigates a series of seemingly unrelated crimes, he soon discovers a hidden network that connects them all. With his keen intellect and relentless determination, Nick must unravel the intricate threads of this criminal network before it claims more victims.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Detective

Themes: Crime and Justice, Investigation, Intrigue
