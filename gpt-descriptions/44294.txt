Synopsis: In 'The Phantom Violin: A Mystery Story for Girls' by Roy J. Snell, a young girl named Betty finds herself caught up in a thrilling mystery when she discovers a hidden violin in her attic. As she delves deeper into the instrument's history, Betty uncovers a web of secrets and intrigue that leads her on a dangerous journey to uncover the truth. With the help of her friends, Betty must navigate through clues, suspicious characters, and unexpected twists to solve the mystery of the phantom violin before it's too late.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Secrets and hidden pasts, Friendship and teamwork, Pursuit of truth
