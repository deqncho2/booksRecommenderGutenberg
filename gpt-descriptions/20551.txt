Synopsis: In 'The White Invaders' by Cummings, Ray, a remote island in the Pacific becomes the unexpected battleground for a group of mysterious white invaders and the indigenous people who must defend their home. As tensions rise and alliances are tested, the island's fate hangs in the balance, leading to a thrilling and thought-provoking exploration of power, identity, and the clash of cultures.

Genres: Adventure, Science Fiction, Historical Fiction

Themes: Colonialism, Cultural Conflict, Survival
