Synopsis: 
In 'A Bachelor's Comedy' by Buckrose, J. E., a young bachelor finds himself entangled in a series of hilarious and unexpected events when he agrees to help a friend with a peculiar favor. As he navigates through misunderstandings, mistaken identities, and a cast of eccentric characters, our protagonist's life takes a comical turn that will keep readers eagerly turning the pages.

Genres: Comedy, Romance, Fiction

Themes: Love and relationships, Friendship, Humor
