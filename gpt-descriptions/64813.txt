Synopsis: In 'The Lightning's Course' by John Victor Peterson, a small town is struck by a series of mysterious lightning storms that leave the residents bewildered and fearful. As the storms intensify, a young scientist named Emma embarks on a quest to uncover the truth behind these electrifying phenomena. With the help of an enigmatic stranger, she delves into a world of ancient legends, hidden secrets, and a race against time to save her town from the wrath of nature's fury. As Emma's journey unfolds, she discovers that the lightning holds a power far greater than anyone could have imagined, and that the fate of her community rests in her hands.

Genres: Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Power of nature, Quest for knowledge, Community resilience
