Synopsis: In "Rootabaga Pigeons" by Carl Sandburg, embark on a whimsical journey through the fantastical world of Rootabaga Country, where pigeons have peculiar powers and adventures await at every turn. From a pigeon who can turn invisible to a pigeon who can speak multiple languages, these extraordinary birds will captivate readers with their extraordinary abilities and endearing personalities. As the stories unfold, readers will be transported to a place where imagination knows no bounds and where the unexpected becomes the norm.

Genres: Children's Literature, Fantasy, Adventure

Themes: Imagination, Individuality, Friendship
