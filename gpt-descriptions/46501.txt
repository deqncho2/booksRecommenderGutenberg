Synopsis: 
In 'Some Zulu Customs and Folk-lore', Samuelson, L. H. takes readers on a captivating journey into the rich cultural traditions and enchanting folklore of the Zulu people. From the intriguing rituals surrounding birth and marriage to the mesmerizing tales of mythical creatures and ancestral spirits, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into a world filled with ancient customs and beliefs. Through vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, Samuelson invites readers to explore the vibrant tapestry of Zulu culture, where tradition and folklore intertwine to create a captivating and mystical realm.

Genres: 
Cultural Studies, Folklore, Anthropology

Themes: 
Cultural traditions, Mythology, Belief systems
