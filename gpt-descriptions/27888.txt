Synopsis: 
In 'Making People Happy' by Buchanan Thompson, a mysterious woman named Lily arrives in a small town, bringing with her an extraordinary ability to bring joy and happiness to everyone she encounters. As the townspeople become enamored with Lily's magical presence, they soon realize that her power comes with unexpected consequences. As the story unfolds, secrets are revealed, relationships are tested, and the true nature of happiness is explored in this captivating tale.

Genres: Fiction, Magical Realism, Mystery

Themes: Happiness, Identity, Consequences
