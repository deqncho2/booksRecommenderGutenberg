Synopsis: 
'Collins' Illustrated Guide to London and Neighbourhood' takes readers on a captivating journey through the bustling metropolis, providing a concise description of its most intriguing places of interest. From iconic landmarks to hidden gems, this guide offers valuable information on how to access these sites, including details on railways, omnibuses, steamers, and more. Written by an anonymous author, this comprehensive guidebook promises to unveil the secrets of London, making it an essential companion for both locals and tourists alike.

Genres: Travel, Guidebook, Non-fiction

Themes: Exploration, History, Transportation
