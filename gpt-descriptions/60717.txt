Synopsis: 
In "Practical School Discipline: Introductory Course," author Ray Coppock takes readers on a captivating journey through the world of school discipline. With a blend of insightful anecdotes, practical strategies, and thought-provoking analysis, this book offers a fresh perspective on managing student behavior in the classroom. From addressing disruptive behavior to fostering a positive learning environment, Coppock provides educators with invaluable tools and techniques to create a harmonious and effective educational setting. As readers delve into the pages of this engaging work, they will discover a wealth of knowledge and inspiration that will transform their approach to discipline and ultimately enhance the educational experience for both teachers and students.

Genres: Education, Self-help, Non-fiction

Themes: Classroom management, Behavior modification, Effective teaching strategies
