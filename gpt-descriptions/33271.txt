Synopsis: In 'The Riddle of the Night' by Thomas W. Hanshew, a renowned detective finds himself entangled in a perplexing case when a wealthy socialite is found dead under mysterious circumstances. As he delves deeper into the investigation, he uncovers a web of secrets, deceit, and hidden motives that lead him down a dark and twisted path. With each clue he uncovers, the detective becomes more determined to solve the riddle of the night and bring the truth to light.

Genres: Mystery, Crime, Thriller

Themes: Deception, Intrigue, Justice
