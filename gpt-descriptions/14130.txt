Synopsis: The Outdoor Chums embark on an exhilarating adventure as they set out to rescue a group of lost balloonists in the vast and treacherous Gulf. With their unwavering determination and resourcefulness, the Chums face numerous challenges and unexpected twists, pushing their limits to the extreme. Will they be able to locate the missing balloonists and bring them back to safety?

Genres: Adventure, Mystery, Action

Themes: Friendship, Courage, Survival
