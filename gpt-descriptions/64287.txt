Synopsis: In 'Progress in the Household' by Lucy Maynard Salmon, a seemingly ordinary household is transformed by a series of unexpected events. As the family navigates through challenges and changes, secrets are unveiled, relationships are tested, and the true nature of progress is questioned. With a blend of humor, heartache, and surprising twists, this captivating novel explores the complexities of family dynamics and the pursuit of a better life.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Family

Themes: Family dynamics, Change, Secrets, Progress
