Synopsis: In 'Nothing to Eat', two friends find themselves in a peculiar situation when they are left with nothing to eat in a small village. As they embark on a quest to satisfy their hunger, they encounter a series of comical and unexpected events that test their resourcefulness and friendship. Will they be able to find a solution to their predicament, or will their hunger get the best of them?

Genres: Humor, Adventure, Satire

Themes: Friendship, Resourcefulness, Social Commentary
