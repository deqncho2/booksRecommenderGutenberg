Synopsis: In 'The Novel on the Tram' by Pérez Galdós, Benito, a chance encounter on a tram leads to a captivating story of love, betrayal, and redemption. As the passengers share their personal tales, secrets are unveiled, and lives are forever changed. With each stop along the way, the narrative becomes more enthralling, leaving the reader eager to discover the ultimate destination of these interconnected lives.

Genres: Fiction, Drama, Romance

Themes: Love and passion, Betrayal and deceit, Redemption and forgiveness
