Synopsis: 
In 'The Cavalry General' by Xenophon, a young and ambitious soldier named Xenophon finds himself thrust into a position of leadership as he is appointed as a cavalry general. With little experience and facing numerous challenges, Xenophon must navigate the treacherous world of warfare, politics, and personal rivalries. As he strives to prove himself and lead his troops to victory, he must confront his own fears and insecurities, while also grappling with the moral dilemmas that arise in times of war. Will Xenophon rise to the occasion and become the leader he aspires to be, or will he succumb to the pressures and obstacles that stand in his way?

Genres: Historical fiction, Military fiction, Adventure

Themes: Leadership, Ambition, Courage, Morality, Personal growth
