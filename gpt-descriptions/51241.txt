Synopsis: In 'Bridge Crossing' by Dave Dryfoos, a small town is thrown into chaos when a mysterious bridge appears overnight, connecting their world to an unknown realm. As the townspeople venture across the bridge, they discover a world filled with strange creatures, hidden dangers, and unexpected revelations. With each crossing, the line between reality and fantasy blurs, leading to a thrilling exploration of the human psyche and the power of imagination.

Genres: Fantasy, Mystery, Adventure

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Perception
