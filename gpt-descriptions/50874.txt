Synopsis: 
Step into the world of the seventeenth century and embark on a delightful journey through the pages of "Humour, Wit, & Satire of the Seventeenth Century." This captivating collection of literary gems will transport you to a time of wit, charm, and biting satire. From the clever banter of courtly love to the sharp social commentary hidden within humorous tales, this anthology showcases the brilliance and artistry of the era's most renowned writers. Lose yourself in the whimsical adventures, witty dialogues, and clever wordplay that will leave you both entertained and enlightened.

Genres: 
Satire, Comedy, Historical Fiction

Themes: 
Social commentary, Human nature, Irony
