Synopsis: In 'A Mere Accident' by George Moore, a chance encounter between two strangers on a train sets off a chain of events that will challenge their beliefs, ignite forbidden desires, and ultimately lead them down a path of self-discovery. As their lives become increasingly intertwined, they must navigate the complexities of love, morality, and societal expectations, all while grappling with the consequences of their choices. With unexpected twists and a captivating narrative, 'A Mere Accident' explores the power of fate and the transformative nature of human connection.

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Fate, Love, Morality, Self-discovery
