Synopsis: In 'The Missing Merchantman' by Collingwood, Harry, a seemingly routine voyage takes a thrilling turn when a merchant ship mysteriously disappears without a trace. As a team of investigators delves into the enigma, they uncover a web of secrets, treachery, and unexpected alliances. With each revelation, the stakes rise higher, leading to a heart-pounding climax that will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Suspense

Themes: Intrigue, Betrayal, Perseverance
