Synopsis: In "Ismailia" by Samuel White Baker, a young Englishman named Harold is thrust into an extraordinary adventure when he joins the Egyptian army during the turbulent times of the Suez Canal construction. As he navigates the treacherous landscapes of the Nile, Harold encounters a captivating cast of characters, including a mysterious woman who holds the key to his destiny. Amidst political intrigue, ancient secrets, and unexpected alliances, Harold's journey becomes a thrilling quest for love, self-discovery, and the pursuit of a hidden treasure that could change the course of history.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Romance

Themes: Exploration, Identity, Love and Sacrifice
