Synopsis: 

'Battling with Waves and Lawyers' is a gripping and captivating true story that delves into the treacherous world of the deep sea and the complex legal battles that ensue. Set in Australasia, this book takes readers on a thrilling journey through perilous waters and into the heart of a groundbreaking shipping case that unfolds in the supreme courts. Filled with suspense, drama, and unexpected twists, this authentic record of real-life events will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to uncover the outcome of this high-stakes legal battle.

Genres: Non-fiction, Legal Thriller, Maritime Adventure

Themes: Perilous journeys, Legal battles, Justice and fairness
