Synopsis: In 'The Fire-Gods: A Tale of the Congo' by Charles Gilson, a young explorer embarks on a treacherous journey deep into the heart of the Congo, where he encounters a mysterious tribe with a dark secret. As he delves deeper into their world, he becomes entangled in a web of ancient rituals, forbidden love, and a battle for power that could ignite the entire region. With danger lurking at every turn, the protagonist must navigate the complexities of this unknown land and confront the fire-gods that hold the fate of the Congo in their hands.

Genres: Adventure, Historical Fiction, Mystery

Themes: Exploration, Cultural Clash, Supernatural Forces
