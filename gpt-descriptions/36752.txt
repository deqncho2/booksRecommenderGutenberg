Synopsis: In 'Capturing a Locomotive: A History of Secret Service in the Late War,' William Pittenger takes readers on a thrilling journey through the heart of the American Civil War. This gripping true story follows a daring group of Union soldiers as they embark on a dangerous mission to capture a Confederate locomotive deep behind enemy lines. Filled with suspense, action, and unexpected twists, Pittenger's account of this audacious operation will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Historical non-fiction, war, adventure

Themes: Courage, espionage, sacrifice, determination
