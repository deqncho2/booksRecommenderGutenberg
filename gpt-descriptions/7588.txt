Synopsis: In the third volume of "The Caxtons: A Family Picture," the Caxton family continues to navigate the complexities of love, ambition, and societal expectations. As the young protagonist, Pisistratus, embarks on a journey to pursue his dreams, he encounters unexpected challenges and encounters that test his character and resilience. Meanwhile, the other members of the Caxton family face their own trials and tribulations, leading to a series of captivating and emotionally charged events. With its richly drawn characters and intricate plot, this volume delves deeper into the complexities of human relationships and the pursuit of personal fulfillment.

Genres: Fiction, Family Drama, Coming-of-Age

Themes: Love and Relationships, Ambition, Identity, Society and Class
