Synopsis: In 'Dick Merriwell's Fighting Chance; Or, The Split in the Varsity' by Standish, Burt L., Dick Merriwell, the talented athlete and all-around good guy, finds himself facing a challenging situation when a rift forms within the varsity team. As tensions rise and loyalties are tested, Dick must navigate the complexities of friendship, competition, and personal growth. With unexpected twists and high-stakes conflicts, this gripping tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eagerly turning the pages to discover the outcome.

Genres: Sports, Adventure, Young Adult

Themes: Friendship, Teamwork, Integrity, Overcoming adversity
