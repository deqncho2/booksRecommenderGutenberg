Synopsis: 
'Early American Plays, 1714-1830: A Compilation of the Titles of Plays and Dramatic Poems Written by Authors Born in or Residing in North America Previous to 1830' by Oscar Wegelin is a comprehensive collection of the titles of plays and dramatic poems written by American authors during the early years of the nation. This compilation provides a glimpse into the rich theatrical history of North America, showcasing the creativity and talent of the playwrights of that era. From tragedies to comedies, historical dramas to satirical works, this book offers a diverse range of titles that reflect the cultural and social landscape of early America.

Genres: Drama, Comedy, Tragedy, Satire, Historical Drama, Poems

Themes: American identity, Social issues, Historical events, Satire of societal norms
