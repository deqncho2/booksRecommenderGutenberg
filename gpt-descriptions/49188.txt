Synopsis:
In "Daisy Herself" by Will E. Ingersoll, a young woman named Daisy finds herself caught in a web of mystery and danger when she stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change her life forever. As she delves deeper into the enigma, Daisy must navigate a treacherous path filled with unexpected twists and turns, all while trying to uncover the truth and protect those she loves. With each revelation, Daisy's courage and determination are put to the test, leading her on a thrilling journey that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Secrets and hidden identities, Courage and resilience
