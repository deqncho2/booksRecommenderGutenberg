Synopsis: 

In 'Days on the Road: Crossing the Plains in 1865', Sarah Raymond Herndon takes us on a captivating journey through the American West during a time of great turmoil. Set in 1865, just after the end of the Civil War, the story follows Sarah and her family as they embark on a treacherous journey across the plains, facing numerous challenges and dangers along the way. From encounters with Native American tribes to the harsh realities of life on the road, this gripping narrative offers a vivid portrayal of the hardships and triumphs experienced by pioneers during this pivotal period in American history.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Memoir

Themes: Survival, Resilience, Family, Exploration, Cultural Encounter
