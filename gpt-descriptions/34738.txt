Synopsis: In 'The Ivory Gate, a new edition' by Walter Besant, a mysterious and enchanting young woman named Hester is thrust into the bustling streets of Victorian London. As she navigates the treacherous world of high society, Hester becomes entangled in a web of secrets, scandals, and forbidden love. With each step closer to the truth, she discovers that the ivory gate she seeks holds the key to her own destiny. Will Hester unlock the gate and find the answers she seeks, or will the secrets of the past remain forever hidden?

Genres: Historical Fiction, Mystery, Romance

Themes: Identity, Secrets, Love and Betrayal
