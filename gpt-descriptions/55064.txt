Synopsis: 

'An Abstract of the Proceedings of the Select Committee of the House of Commons, Appointed Session, 1849, to Inquire Into the Contract Packet Service' takes readers on a captivating journey through the investigation of the Contract Packet Service, specifically focusing on the Peninsular and Oriental Steam Navigation Company. This gripping account delves into the inner workings of the company, uncovering secrets, controversies, and the intricate web of relationships that shape the world of maritime contracts. As the committee delves deeper into their inquiry, shocking revelations and unexpected twists emerge, leaving readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Non-fiction, Historical, Political

Themes: Corruption, Power dynamics, Accountability
