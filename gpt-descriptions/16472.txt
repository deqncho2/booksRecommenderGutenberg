Synopsis: In 'Through Forest and Fire: Wild-Woods Series No. 1', follow the thrilling journey of a young adventurer named Jack as he embarks on a perilous quest through a mysterious forest. Faced with treacherous landscapes, mythical creatures, and unexpected allies, Jack must navigate through the heart of the wilderness to uncover the truth behind an ancient legend. With danger lurking at every turn, will Jack's determination and resourcefulness be enough to overcome the challenges that lie ahead?

Genres: Adventure, Fantasy, Young Adult

Themes: Courage, Exploration, Mythology
