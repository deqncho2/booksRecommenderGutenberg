Synopsis: In 'The Prisoner of the Mill; or, Captain Hayward's "Body Guard"', a young man named Captain Hayward finds himself entangled in a web of mystery and danger when he becomes the bodyguard of a wealthy mill owner's daughter. As he delves deeper into the secrets of the mill and its inhabitants, Captain Hayward must navigate treacherous waters and confront his own past in order to protect the woman he has come to care for. With unexpected twists and turns, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Mystery, Adventure, Romance

Themes: Secrets and deception, Loyalty and sacrifice
