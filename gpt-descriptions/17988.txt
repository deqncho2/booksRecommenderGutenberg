Synopsis: 
Grace Harlowe's First Year at Overton College follows the journey of Grace Harlowe as she enters college and navigates the challenges and adventures that come with it. From forming new friendships and facing academic pressures to dealing with unexpected obstacles, Grace's first year at Overton College is filled with excitement, growth, and self-discovery. As she learns to balance her studies, extracurricular activities, and personal life, Grace finds herself facing unexpected dilemmas that test her resilience and determination. With the support of her friends and her own determination, Grace strives to make the most of her first year at Overton College and embrace the opportunities that come her way.

Genres: Young Adult Fiction, Coming-of-Age, School/College Drama

Themes: Friendship, Personal Growth, Academic Challenges, Resilience, Self-Discovery
