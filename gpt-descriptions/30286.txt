Synopsis: In 'The Phantom Lover' by Ruby M. Ayres, a young woman named Evelyn finds herself drawn into a mysterious and passionate romance with a man who seems to exist only in her dreams. As their connection deepens, Evelyn becomes determined to uncover the truth behind her phantom lover's identity and the secrets that surround him. With each encounter, she is swept away by their intense connection, but as she delves deeper into the enigma, she begins to question her own reality. Will Evelyn be able to unravel the mystery of her phantom lover, or will she be forever haunted by the allure of a love that may only exist in her imagination?

Genres: Romance, Mystery, Paranormal

Themes: Love and obsession, Identity and self-discovery
