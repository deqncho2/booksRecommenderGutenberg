Synopsis: In 'Limbo' by Aldous Huxley, a young man named Anthony Beavis embarks on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. As he navigates through the complexities of love, art, and society, he finds himself caught between the allure of material success and the pursuit of a deeper meaning in life. Set against the backdrop of post-World War I London, this thought-provoking novel explores the inner conflicts and existential dilemmas faced by the protagonist, ultimately leading him to question the nature of reality and the purpose of human existence.

Genres: Literary fiction, philosophical fiction, coming-of-age

Themes: Identity, spirituality, the search for meaning, societal expectations, the conflict between materialism and idealism
