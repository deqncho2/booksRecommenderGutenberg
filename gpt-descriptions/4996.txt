Synopsis: 
In the heart of London, a mysterious murder takes place on a seemingly ordinary train. As the passengers become suspects, a young man finds himself entangled in a web of deception, danger, and unexpected alliances. With time running out, he must unravel the truth before the killer strikes again, all while trapped in a house that holds dark secrets and hidden identities. 

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Identity, Deception, Betrayal, Crime
