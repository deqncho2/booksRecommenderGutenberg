Synopsis: In the bustling streets of Jerusalem, Tor, a young boy, navigates a world filled with poverty, danger, and unexpected friendships. Tor's journey takes him from the depths of despair to the heights of hope as he discovers the power of resilience and the importance of finding one's place in the world. With vivid descriptions and captivating storytelling, 'Tor, a Street Boy of Jerusalem' immerses readers in a tale of survival, love, and the enduring spirit of a young boy determined to overcome the odds.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Coming-of-Age, Adventure

Themes: Poverty, Resilience, Friendship, Identity
