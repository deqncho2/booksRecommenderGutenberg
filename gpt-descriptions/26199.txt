Synopsis: In 'Fables of John Gay (Somewhat Altered)', John Benson Rose takes the timeless fables of John Gay and gives them a modern twist. From cunning foxes to wise owls, these fables come alive with a contemporary edge, offering a fresh perspective on age-old moral lessons. With clever wit and a touch of humor, Rose invites readers to explore the familiar tales in a whole new light, making them relevant and thought-provoking for today's audience.

Genres: Fiction, Fables, Satire

Themes: Morality, Human Nature, Social Critique
