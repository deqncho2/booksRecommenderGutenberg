Synopsis: In '!Tention: A Story of Boy-Life during the Peninsular War' by George Manville Fenn, readers are transported to the tumultuous era of the Peninsular War. The story revolves around a young boy named Tom, who finds himself caught up in the chaos and danger of war. As Tom navigates through the perils of battle, he encounters both friends and foes, experiencing the harsh realities of war firsthand. With vivid descriptions and gripping storytelling, this novel offers a captivating glimpse into the life of a young boy amidst the backdrop of a historic conflict.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: War, Friendship, Courage, Survival
