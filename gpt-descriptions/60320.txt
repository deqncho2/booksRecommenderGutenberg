Synopsis: In 'A Plea for Monogamy' by Lay, Wilfrid, a renowned psychologist finds himself caught in a web of desire, temptation, and moral dilemmas when he becomes entangled in a passionate affair with a mysterious woman. As he grapples with the consequences of his actions, he is forced to confront the true meaning of love, loyalty, and the complexities of human relationships. This gripping tale explores the depths of human emotions and the consequences of straying from the path of monogamy.

Genres: Psychological fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love, Temptation, Morality, Loyalty, Human relationships
