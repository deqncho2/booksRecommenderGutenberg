Synopsis: In "The History of Company C, Seventh Regiment, O.V.I," Theodore Wilder takes readers on a captivating journey through the experiences of a courageous group of soldiers during the American Civil War. From their initial enlistment to their harrowing battles and the bonds they form along the way, this gripping narrative offers a vivid portrayal of the realities faced by Company C. As they navigate the horrors of war and the complexities of military life, the soldiers' resilience and camaraderie shine through, leaving readers eager to uncover the fate of these brave men.

Genres: Historical fiction, War literature, Military history

Themes: Courage, Brotherhood, Sacrifice, Survival
