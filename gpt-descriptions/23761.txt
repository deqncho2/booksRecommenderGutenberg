Synopsis: 
In 'Little Journeys to the Homes of the Great, Volume 7: Little Journeys to the Homes of Eminent Orators', Elbert Hubbard takes readers on a captivating journey into the lives and homes of some of history's most influential orators. From the eloquent speeches of Demosthenes to the passionate orations of Patrick Henry, Hubbard delves into the personal stories and environments that shaped these great speakers. Through vivid descriptions and insightful anecdotes, he brings to life the homes and surroundings that nurtured their powerful voices, offering a unique glimpse into the lives of these remarkable individuals.

Genres: Biography, History, Non-fiction

Themes: Oratory, Influence, Personal Development
