Synopsis: 

In "Model Aeroplanes: The Building of Model Monoplanes, Biplanes, etc., Together with a Chapter on Building a Model Airship," Frederick James Camm takes readers on an exhilarating journey into the world of model aviation. With detailed instructions and expert guidance, Camm explores the art of constructing model monoplanes, biplanes, and even airships. From the intricacies of design to the delicate balance of aerodynamics, this book offers a comprehensive and captivating exploration of the craft.

Genres: Non-fiction, Hobby, Crafts

Themes: Model aviation, Engineering, Creativity
