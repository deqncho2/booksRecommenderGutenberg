Synopsis: In 'The Furnace of Gold' by Philip Verrill Mighels, a young man named John finds himself caught in a web of mystery and danger when he stumbles upon a hidden secret that could change the course of history. As he delves deeper into the enigma, he must navigate treacherous alliances, face unexpected adversaries, and confront his own inner demons. With each step closer to the truth, John realizes that the stakes are higher than he ever imagined, and the consequences of his actions could be catastrophic. Will he be able to unravel the puzzle before it consumes him?

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Themes: Secrets and hidden knowledge, Betrayal and loyalty, Personal growth and self-discovery
