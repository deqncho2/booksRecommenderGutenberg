Synopsis: In 'The Einstein See-Saw' by Miles J. Breuer, a brilliant scientist stumbles upon a mysterious device that allows him to travel through time. As he embarks on a thrilling journey through different eras, he discovers shocking secrets about the nature of reality and the consequences of tampering with the fabric of time. With each leap, the stakes become higher, and the scientist must confront the ethical dilemmas that arise from his newfound power. This gripping tale of scientific discovery and its moral implications will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Genres: Science Fiction, Time Travel, Thriller

Themes: Ethics, Reality, Consequences
