Synopsis: 
In 'Wanda, Vol. 3 (of 3)' by Ouida, the captivating conclusion to the series, the enigmatic and alluring Wanda continues to navigate the treacherous waters of high society. As her secrets unravel and her past catches up with her, Wanda finds herself torn between love and duty, facing impossible choices that will determine her fate. With scandal, betrayal, and unexpected alliances, this thrilling tale will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very last page.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Identity, Society, Love and Sacrifice
