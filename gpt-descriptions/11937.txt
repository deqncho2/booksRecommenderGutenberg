Synopsis: In "The Symbolism of Freemasonry," Albert Gallatin Mackey delves into the mysterious world of Freemasonry, unraveling its hidden meanings and exploring its rich symbolism. From ancient legends to modern rituals, Mackey takes readers on a captivating journey through the secret society, shedding light on its science, philosophy, and the profound significance of its symbols. With meticulous research and insightful analysis, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the enigmatic world of Freemasonry, leaving readers intrigued and eager to uncover its secrets.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Philosophy

Themes: Symbolism, Secret societies, Enlightenment, Esoteric knowledge
