Synopsis:
'Arden of Feversham' is a gripping and suspenseful drama that delves into the dark depths of human nature. Set in 16th-century England, the story follows the seemingly ordinary life of Thomas Arden, a wealthy businessman. However, behind closed doors, a web of deceit, betrayal, and murder begins to unravel, leading to a shocking and unexpected climax. As the tension builds, the audience is left questioning the true nature of love, loyalty, and the lengths people will go to protect their own interests.

Genres:
Drama, Crime, Suspense

Themes:
Deceit, Betrayal, Love, Loyalty
