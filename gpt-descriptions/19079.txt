Synopsis: In 'The Adventures of Lightfoot the Deer', follow the captivating journey of Lightfoot, a young and curious deer, as he navigates the dangers and wonders of the Green Forest. From thrilling encounters with cunning hunters to unexpected friendships with fellow forest creatures, Lightfoot's determination and bravery are put to the test. As he strives to protect his family and find his place in the wild, Lightfoot's adventures will keep readers on the edge of their seats, eager to discover what lies around the next corner.

Genres: Children's literature, Adventure, Animal fiction

Themes: Survival, Friendship, Coming of age
