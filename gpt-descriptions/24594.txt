Synopsis: In "Letter to the Reverend Mr. Cary," George Bethune English offers a thought-provoking response to a review of his work, "The Grounds of Christianity Examined by Comparing the New Testament to the Old." Through a series of insightful remarks, English challenges the reviewer's arguments and delves into the complexities of biblical interpretation. As the letter unfolds, the reader is drawn into a captivating intellectual discourse that explores the foundations of Christianity and the relationship between the Old and New Testaments.

Genres: Non-fiction, Religious Studies, Theology

Themes: Biblical interpretation, Christianity, Comparative religion
