Synopsis: 

In 'Don't Look Now' by Rubin, Leonard, John and Laura Baxter, a grieving couple, travel to Venice in hopes of finding solace after the tragic loss of their daughter. However, their journey takes an unexpected turn when they encounter two mysterious sisters who claim to have psychic abilities. As the couple becomes entangled in a series of eerie events and unexplained occurrences, they find themselves questioning their own sanity and the true nature of the sisters' predictions. With each passing moment, the line between reality and the supernatural blurs, leading to a shocking climax that will leave readers questioning what they thought they knew.

Genres: Psychological thriller, Mystery, Supernatural

Themes: Grief and loss, Perception vs. reality, Supernatural phenomena
