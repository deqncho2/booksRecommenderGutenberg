Synopsis: 
In 'Up for Renewal' by Daniel, Lucius, we follow the life of Allison, a successful magazine editor who seems to have it all together. However, when she is unexpectedly fired from her job, Allison's world is turned upside down. Desperate to regain control of her life, she embarks on a year-long journey of self-discovery and reinvention. Along the way, she encounters a colorful cast of characters, navigates the complexities of love and friendship, and learns valuable lessons about the importance of embracing change and finding one's true passion.

Genres: Fiction, Chick Lit, Contemporary

Themes: Self-discovery, Reinvention, Resilience, Friendship, Love
