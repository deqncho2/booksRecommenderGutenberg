Synopsis: In 'The Red Symbol' by John Ironside, a renowned archaeologist stumbles upon a mysterious artifact that holds the key to an ancient secret. As he delves deeper into the artifact's origins, he becomes entangled in a dangerous web of conspiracy and betrayal. With time running out, he must decipher the meaning behind the red symbol before it falls into the wrong hands, risking the fate of humanity itself.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Adventure

Themes: Ancient secrets, Conspiracy, Betrayal
