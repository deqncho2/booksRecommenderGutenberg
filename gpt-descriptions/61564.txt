Synopsis: 
In 'Attitude' by Clement, Hal, a young and ambitious journalist named Emma finds herself caught in a web of secrets and deception when she is assigned to investigate a mysterious and influential businessman. As she delves deeper into the case, Emma uncovers shocking truths that not only challenge her professional integrity but also put her life in danger. With time running out, she must navigate a treacherous path of lies and betrayal to uncover the ultimate truth.

Genres: Mystery, Thriller, Suspense

Themes: Deception, Ambition, Betrayal
