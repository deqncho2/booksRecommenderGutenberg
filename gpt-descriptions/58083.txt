Synopsis:
In the seventh volume of "The Chronicles of Enguerrand de Monstrelet," readers are transported to a tumultuous period in French history. The narrative delves into the brutal civil wars between the houses of Orleans and Burgundy, which ravaged the kingdom. The book vividly recounts the gripping struggle for power, the possession of Paris and Normandy by the English, their eventual expulsion, and the various significant events that unfolded not only in France but also in other countries. Enguerrand de Monstrelet's meticulous account offers a captivating glimpse into the political intrigues, military conflicts, and the resilience of the people during this chaotic era.

Genres:
Historical non-fiction, Chronicle, Memoir

Themes:
Power struggles, War and conflict, Political intrigue, Resilience
