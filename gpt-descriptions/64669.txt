Synopsis: In 'Philip Rollo; or, the Scottish Musketeers, Vol. 1 (of 2)' by Grant, James, readers are transported to 17th century Scotland, where young Philip Rollo finds himself caught up in a dangerous web of political intrigue and personal vendettas. As he joins the ranks of the Scottish Musketeers, Philip must navigate treacherous alliances, face formidable enemies, and uncover long-buried secrets that could change the course of history. With swashbuckling action, unexpected twists, and a captivating cast of characters, this thrilling historical adventure will keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Genres: Historical Fiction, Adventure, Mystery

Themes: Loyalty, Betrayal, Honor, Identity
