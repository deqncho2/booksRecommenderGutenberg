Synopsis: In this captivating volume, 'The World's Greatest Books — Volume 05 — Fiction' takes readers on a thrilling journey through a collection of timeless stories. From the mysterious and haunting tales of Edgar Allan Poe to the enchanting and whimsical adventures of Lewis Carroll, this volume offers a diverse range of fiction that will leave readers spellbound. Delve into the depths of human nature, explore the complexities of love and loss, and experience the power of imagination through the pages of these extraordinary works.

Genres: Fiction, Mystery, Fantasy, Adventure, Gothic

Themes: Human nature, Love and loss, Imagination, Mystery, Adventure
