Synopsis: In 'Two Little Confederates' by Thomas Nelson Page, two young boys find themselves caught up in the turmoil of the American Civil War. As the war rages on, they embark on a series of thrilling adventures, navigating the dangers of enemy soldiers, secret missions, and unexpected friendships. Set against the backdrop of the Confederate South, this captivating tale follows the boys' journey of growth, resilience, and the enduring power of friendship.

Genres: Historical fiction, Adventure, Coming-of-age

Themes: Friendship, Loyalty, Resilience, War
