Synopsis: In 'Sea and Shore: A Sequel to "Miriam's Memoirs"', Catherine A. Warfield takes readers on a captivating journey through the lives of Miriam and her companions as they navigate the unpredictable waters of love, loss, and self-discovery. Set against the backdrop of the enchanting sea and picturesque shores, this sequel delves deeper into the complexities of relationships, secrets, and the pursuit of personal fulfillment. As Miriam's world expands, new characters and unexpected twists emerge, keeping readers on the edge of their seats. Will Miriam find the answers she seeks, or will the tides of fate wash away her dreams?

Genres: Fiction, Romance, Drama

Themes: Love and relationships, Self-discovery, Secrets and revelations
