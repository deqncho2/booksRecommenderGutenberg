Synopsis: In the icy wilderness of the Yukon, a group of men embark on a treacherous journey to find gold, facing the harsh realities of nature and the depths of their own humanity. As they battle against the unforgiving elements, they encounter unexpected challenges and form unlikely alliances, testing their resilience and determination. Amidst the frozen landscape, the characters are forced to confront their deepest fears and desires, ultimately revealing the true nature of their souls.

Genres: Adventure, Survival, Historical Fiction

Themes: Man vs. Nature, Human Resilience, Moral Ambiguity
