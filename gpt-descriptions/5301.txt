Synopsis: 
In 'The Imperialist' by Sara Jeannette Duncan, we follow the story of Lorne Murchison, a young Canadian with big dreams and ambitions. Set in the late 19th century, Lorne finds himself caught between his desire for success and his loyalty to his homeland. As he navigates the complex world of politics, business, and love, Lorne's journey takes him from the small town of Elgin to the bustling city of London. Along the way, he encounters a cast of intriguing characters and faces numerous challenges that test his principles and shape his destiny. 

Genres: 
Historical Fiction, Political Fiction, Coming-of-Age

Themes: 
National identity, Ambition, Loyalty, Love and Relationships
