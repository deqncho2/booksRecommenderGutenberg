Synopsis: In 'Twelve Preludes for the Pianoforte Op. 25: I. Prelude in F Major' by N. Louise Wright, a captivating musical journey unfolds as the delicate notes of the piano transport the reader to a world of enchantment and emotion. This Prelude in F Major, the first of a series of twelve, sets the stage for a mesmerizing exploration of melody and harmony. As the music flows, the listener is drawn into a realm where beauty and passion intertwine, leaving them yearning for more.

Genres: Classical music, Romantic music, Solo piano

Themes: Musical expression, Emotion, Artistic exploration
