Synopsis: 
In 'Historic Inventions' by Rupert Sargent Holland, embark on a captivating journey through time as the author delves into the fascinating stories behind some of the world's most groundbreaking inventions. From the creation of the printing press to the invention of the telephone, Holland uncovers the trials, triumphs, and unexpected twists that accompanied these revolutionary discoveries. With vivid storytelling and meticulous research, 'Historic Inventions' offers a captivating exploration of the human ingenuity that has shaped our world.

Genres: Non-fiction, History, Science

Themes: Innovation, Human Ingenuity, Technological Advancement
