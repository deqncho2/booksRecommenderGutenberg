Synopsis: 
'Collections of the Minnesota Historical Society for the Year 1867' takes readers on a captivating journey through the rich history of Minnesota during a pivotal year. Through a collection of historical documents, personal accounts, and insightful essays, this book offers a fascinating glimpse into the state's past, shedding light on the struggles, triumphs, and untold stories of its people. From the early settlers and Native American tribes to the political and social developments that shaped the region, this compilation paints a vivid picture of Minnesota's vibrant heritage.

Genres: 
Historical non-fiction, Anthology, Research

Themes: 
Minnesota history, Cultural heritage, Social and political developments
