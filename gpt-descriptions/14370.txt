Synopsis: In "Zanetto; and Cavalleria Rusticana," two captivating tales intertwine to explore the complexities of love, desire, and the human condition. In "Zanetto," a wandering minstrel encounters a mysterious woman who challenges his perception of beauty and unveils the depths of his own emotions. Meanwhile, in "Cavalleria Rusticana," a small Sicilian village becomes the stage for a passionate love triangle that ignites intense emotions and tragic consequences. These two stories, filled with longing, betrayal, and redemption, will transport readers to a world of raw emotions and profound introspection.

Genres: Drama, Romance, Opera

Themes: Love and desire, Beauty and perception, Betrayal and redemption, Human emotions and relationships
